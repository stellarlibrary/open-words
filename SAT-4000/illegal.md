# 🚫 illegal

🔊 /ɪˈliːɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Contrary to or forbidden by law; not authorized by official rules or regulations
**中文：** adj. 不合法的；非法的；违法的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unlawful（非法的）
- illicit（违法的）
- prohibited（被禁止的）
- criminal（犯罪的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **illegal activity / illegal substance / illegal trade / deemed illegal / declared illegal**

👉 Example: The court ruled that the surveillance program was illegal because it violated citizens' constitutional rights to privacy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The regulatory agency imposed substantial fines on companies found to be engaged in **illegal** dumping of toxic waste into nearby waterways.
   监管机构对被发现向附近水道非法倾倒有毒废物的公司处以巨额罚款。

2. International organizations have struggled for decades to curtail the **illegal** trafficking of endangered wildlife across national borders.
   国际组织数十年来一直在努力遏制跨国非法贩卖濒危野生动物的行为。

3. The journalist's investigation revealed that several government officials had accepted **illegal** donations to fund their recent reelection campaigns.
   记者的调查揭示，数名政府官员接受了非法捐款来资助他们最近的竞选连任活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "il-legal" — the prefix "il-" means "not," so illegal simply means "not legal," against the law.
把"illegal"拆成"il（不）+ legal（合法的）"——就是不合法的，即违反法律的。
