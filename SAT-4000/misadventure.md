# 😰 misadventure

🔊 /ˌmɪsədˈventʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An unfortunate incident or mishap; an unlucky event or series of events
**中文：** n. 灾难；不幸遭遇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mishap（不幸事故）
- misfortune（厄运）
- calamity（灾祸）
- setback（挫折）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **death by misadventure / a series of misadventures / youthful misadventure / travel misadventure**

👉 Example: The expedition was plagued by one misadventure after another from the very start.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coroner recorded a verdict of death by **misadventure** after concluding that the incident was accidental rather than intentional.
   验尸官在认定该事件属于意外而非故意后，做出了因意外事故致死的裁决。

2. Her memoir recounts a series of comical **misadventures** encountered during a solo backpacking journey through rural Southeast Asia.
   她的回忆录讲述了独自背包穿越东南亚农村时遇到的一系列滑稽的不幸遭遇。

3. The company's ambitious overseas expansion turned into a costly **misadventure** due to inadequate market research and poor planning.
   由于市场调研不足和规划不当，该公司雄心勃勃的海外扩张变成了一场代价高昂的灾难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-adventure" — an adventure that goes wrong, a "missed" adventure where things turn unfortunate.
把"misadventure"拆成"mis（错误的）+ adventure（冒险）"——一次出了差错的冒险，变成了不幸的遭遇。
