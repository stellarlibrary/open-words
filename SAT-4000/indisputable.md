# ✅ indisputable

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unable to be challenged, denied, or disputed; unquestionable; beyond doubt or argument
**中文：** adj. 无争论之余地的；明白的；不容置疑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- undeniable（不可否认的）
- irrefutable（不可反驳的）
- incontrovertible（无可辩驳的）
- unquestionable（无可置疑的）
- conclusive（决定性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indisputable fact / indisputable evidence / indisputable truth / indisputable proof**

👉 Example: The DNA evidence provided indisputable proof that the defendant had been present at the scene of the crime.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeological findings provided **indisputable** evidence that the settlement had been continuously inhabited for over three thousand years.
   考古发现提供了无可争辩的证据，表明该定居点已被连续居住了三千多年。

2. It is an **indisputable** fact that rising carbon emissions have contributed significantly to global temperature increases over recent decades.
   不可否认的事实是，不断增加的碳排放在近几十年来对全球气温上升做出了重大贡献。

3. The mathematician's elegant proof established the theorem as an **indisputable** cornerstone of modern algebraic number theory.
   这位数学家优雅的证明将该定理确立为现代代数数论中不可争议的基石。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-disputable" — not disputable — something so clear and obvious that nobody can dispute or argue against it.
把"indisputable"想成"in-disputable"——不可争辩的——某事如此清楚明白，以至于没有人能够争辩或反驳。
