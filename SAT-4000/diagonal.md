# 📐 diagonal

🔊 /daɪˈæɡənəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extending from one corner to the opposite corner; slanting. (n.) A straight line joining two opposite corners of a rectangle or polygon
**中文：** adj. 斜的；对角线的 n. 对角线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oblique（斜的）
- slanting（倾斜的）
- crosswise（交叉的）
- angled（成角度的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diagonal line / diagonal pattern / diagonal direction / draw a diagonal**

👉 Example: The architect incorporated diagonal lines into the facade to create a sense of dynamic movement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The designer arranged the tiles in a **diagonal** pattern to add visual interest to the otherwise plain floor.
   设计师将瓷砖以对角线图案排列，为原本单调的地板增添了视觉趣味。

2. A **diagonal** line drawn across the rectangle divides it into two congruent right triangles.
   在矩形上画一条对角线将其分成两个全等的直角三角形。

3. The hikers followed a **diagonal** path across the steep hillside rather than ascending directly upward.
   徒步者沿着陡峭山坡的斜线路径行走，而不是直接向上攀登。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diagonal" as "dia-" (across) + "gon" (angle) — a line that goes across from angle to angle, connecting opposite corners.
把"diagonal"想成"dia-"（穿过）+"gon"（角）——一条从一个角穿到另一个角的线，连接对角。
