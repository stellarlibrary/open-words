# 🏛️ archaeology

🔊 /ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The scientific study of human history and cultures through the analysis of physical remains, artifacts, and monuments
**中文：** n. 考古学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- antiquarianism（古物研究）
- paleontology（古生物学）
- excavation science（发掘科学）
- prehistory studies（史前研究）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study archaeology / underwater archaeology / a degree in archaeology / archaeological site**

👉 Example: She decided to pursue a degree in archaeology after visiting the ruins of Pompeii.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern **archaeology** relies heavily on advanced technologies such as carbon dating and satellite imaging for analysis.
   现代考古学在很大程度上依赖碳定年法和卫星成像等先进技术进行分析。

2. The professor's lecture on **archaeology** revealed how ancient civilizations adapted their agricultural practices to climate changes.
   教授关于考古学的讲座揭示了古代文明如何调整其农业实践以适应气候变化。

3. Underwater **archaeology** has uncovered numerous shipwrecks that provide invaluable insights into ancient maritime trade routes.
   水下考古学发现了许多沉船，为研究古代海上贸易路线提供了宝贵的线索。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "archaeo-logy" — "archaeo" means ancient (from Greek arkhaios), and "-logy" means the study of. Archaeology is literally the study of ancient things.
把"archaeology"拆成"archaeo（古代的）+ logy（学科）"——考古学就是字面意义上研究古代事物的学科。
