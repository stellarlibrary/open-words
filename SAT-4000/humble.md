# 🙇 humble

🔊 /ˈhʌmbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a modest opinion of oneself; of low social or economic rank; plain and simple. (v.) To make someone feel less important or proud
**中文：** adj. 谦逊的；简陋的；卑微的 v. 使谦恭；降低；贬抑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modest（谦虚的）
- meek（温顺的）
- unassuming（不张扬的）
- lowly（卑微的）
- humiliate（使丢脸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **humble beginnings / humble opinion / humble abode / humble oneself**

👉 Example: Despite her fame, the actress remained humble and always credited her success to her team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished professor remained remarkably **humble** despite receiving numerous international awards throughout her career.
   这位杰出的教授尽管在整个职业生涯中获得了众多国际奖项，仍然非常谦逊。

2. The unprecedented defeat served to **humble** the arrogant general, who had previously dismissed all strategic criticism.
   这场空前的失败使那位傲慢的将军变得谦恭，他此前一直无视所有战略批评。

3. Many successful entrepreneurs share stories of their **humble** beginnings to inspire the next generation of innovators.
   许多成功的企业家分享他们卑微出身的故事，以激励下一代创新者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humble" as "hum + ble" — someone who just hums quietly (hum) instead of boasting is a humble person.
把"humble"想成"hum + ble"——一个只是轻声哼唱(hum)而不炫耀的人，就是一个谦逊的人。
