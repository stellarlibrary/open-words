# 🤲 outstretch

🔊 /ˌaʊtˈstrɛtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To extend or stretch out to full length; to spread or reach out beyond the usual limit
**中文：** v. 伸出，伸展；拉长；伸展得超出范围

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extend（伸展）
- stretch（拉伸）
- spread（展开）
- reach out（伸出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outstretched arms / outstretched hands / outstretched wings**

👉 Example: The child ran toward her mother with outstretched arms after months of separation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador greeted the visiting delegates with **outstretched** hands as a gesture of diplomatic goodwill and cooperation.
   大使伸出双手迎接来访代表，以示外交善意与合作。

2. The eagle soared above the canyon with its magnificent wings fully **outstretched** against the clear morning sky.
   雄鹰在峡谷上空翱翔，壮丽的双翼在晴朗的晨空中完全展开。

3. The statue depicted a robed figure with arms **outstretched** toward the horizon, symbolizing hope and liberation.
   雕像描绘了一个身着长袍的人物，双臂向地平线伸展，象征着希望与解放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out-stretch" as "stretching outward" — you stretch your arms out as far as they can go.
把"outstretch"想成"向外stretch"——把手臂尽力向外伸展。
