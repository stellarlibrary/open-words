# 🌗 penumbra

🔊 /pɪˈnʌmbrə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The partially shaded outer region of a shadow cast by an opaque object; a surrounding area of lesser intensity or uncertainty
**中文：** n. 半影；模糊地带

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- partial shadow（半影）
- fringe（边缘）
- periphery（周边）
- twilight zone（模糊地带）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **penumbra of a shadow / penumbra of doubt / in the penumbra / penumbra region**

👉 Example: The moon passed through the penumbra of the Earth before entering the full shadow during the eclipse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During the lunar eclipse, observers noted that the moon first entered the **penumbra** before gradually moving into the darker central shadow.
   在月食期间，观察者注意到月球首先进入半影区，然后逐渐移入较暗的中心阴影。

2. The constitutional scholar argued that privacy rights exist within the **penumbra** of several amendments to the Bill of Rights.
   这位宪法学者认为，隐私权存在于《权利法案》若干修正案的模糊地带中。

3. The artist skillfully rendered the **penumbra** surrounding each object in the still life, creating a remarkably realistic sense of depth.
   这位艺术家巧妙地描绘了静物画中每个物体周围的半影，营造出极为逼真的深度感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pen-umbra" — "pen" (almost) + "umbra" (Latin for shadow). A penumbra is an "almost shadow," the partial shadow at the edge of full darkness.
把"penumbra"想成"pen（几乎）+ umbra（拉丁语：影子）"——"几乎是影子"，即完全黑暗边缘的半影。
