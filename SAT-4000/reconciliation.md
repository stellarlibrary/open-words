# 🤝 reconciliation

🔊 /ˌrɛkənsɪliˈeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The restoration of friendly relations; the act of making opposing views, beliefs, or accounts consistent or compatible with each other
**中文：** n. 和解；调和；和谐一致；甘愿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rapprochement（和解）
- conciliation（调解）
- resolution（解决）
- harmonization（协调）
- reunion（重聚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reconciliation between / reconciliation process / national reconciliation / reconciliation of differences**

👉 Example: The reconciliation between the two rival factions brought lasting peace to the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **reconciliation** between the warring nations required years of careful diplomatic negotiation and significant mutual concessions from both sides.
   交战国之间的和解需要数年的缜密外交谈判以及双方做出重大的相互让步。

2. Financial auditors performed a thorough **reconciliation** of the company's accounts to identify discrepancies between reported and actual revenues.
   财务审计员对公司账目进行了彻底的核对，以查明报告收入与实际收入之间的差异。

3. The truth and **reconciliation** commission was established to address historical injustices and promote national healing after the conflict.
   真相与和解委员会成立，旨在解决历史不公并促进冲突后的国家愈合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-council-iation" as "sitting in council again" — opposing parties come back to the council table to resolve their differences.
把"reconciliation"想成"re-council-iation（重新开会）"——对立双方重新回到会议桌前解决分歧。
