# 💪 overpower

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To defeat or overcome with superior strength; to overwhelm; to make helpless or ineffective
**中文：** v. 压倒；制服；使无法忍受；供给过强的力量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overwhelm（压倒）
- subdue（制服）
- vanquish（征服）
- overcome（克服）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overpower an opponent / overpowering smell / overpowering force / overpowered by emotion**

👉 Example: The overpowering scent of perfume in the elevator made several passengers feel dizzy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The security guards managed to **overpower** the intruder before he could gain access to the restricted area of the facility.
   保安人员在入侵者进入设施的限制区域之前成功将其制服。

2. An **overpowering** sense of grief consumed the entire community following the unexpected loss of their beloved civic leader.
   在他们敬爱的市民领袖意外去世后，一种压倒性的悲伤笼罩了整个社区。

3. The smaller nation's forces were quickly **overpowered** by the invading army, which possessed vastly superior weaponry and numbers.
   这个小国的军队很快被拥有远超其武器装备和兵力的入侵军队所压倒。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-power" — to have "power over" someone, using so much force that they cannot resist or fight back.
把"overpower"想成拥有"凌驾于某人之上的力量"——使用如此大的力量使对方无法抵抗或反击。
