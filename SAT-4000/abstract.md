# 💭 abstract

🔊 /ˈæbstrækt/ (n., adj.) /æbˈstrækt/ (v.)<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A summary or condensed version of a text. (adj.) Existing in thought or as an idea but not having physical existence; theoretical. (v.) To extract or summarize
**中文：** n. 摘要，概要，抽象 adj. 抽象的；深奥的；理论的 v. 摘要，提炼，提取

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- theoretical（理论的）
- conceptual（概念的）
- summary（摘要）
- intangible（无形的）
- extract（提取）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abstract concept / abstract art / abstract of a paper / abstract thinking / in the abstract**

👉 Example: The professor asked each student to write a brief abstract summarizing their research paper.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Young children often struggle to grasp **abstract** concepts such as justice, freedom, and equality.
   幼儿通常难以理解诸如正义、自由和平等这样的抽象概念。

2. The researcher submitted a detailed **abstract** of her dissertation to the academic conference committee.
   研究人员向学术会议委员会提交了一份详细的论文摘要。

3. He attempted to **abstract** the key findings from hundreds of pages of experimental data.
   他试图从数百页的实验数据中提炼出关键发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abstract" as "abs-TRACT" — to TRACT (pull) something away from reality, leaving only the idea behind.
把"abstract"想成"abs-TRACT"——把某物从现实中拉(tract)出来，只留下抽象的概念。
