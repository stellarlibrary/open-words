# 📃 procedure

🔊 /prəˈsiːdʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An established or official way of doing something; a series of actions conducted in a certain order or manner
**中文：** n. 程序，手续；步骤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- process（过程）
- method（方法）
- protocol（规程）
- routine（常规）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **standard procedure / follow procedure / surgical procedure / legal procedure**

👉 Example: All employees must follow the established safety procedure when operating heavy machinery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital implemented a new sterilization **procedure** to minimize the risk of post-operative infections among surgical patients.
   医院实施了一项新的消毒程序，以最大限度地减少手术患者术后感染的风险。

2. The committee reviewed the existing **procedure** for approving research grants and proposed several modifications to improve efficiency.
   委员会审查了现有的研究资助审批程序，并提出了几项提高效率的修改建议。

3. Students were required to follow the laboratory **procedure** precisely to ensure the accuracy and reproducibility of experimental results.
   学生被要求严格遵循实验室操作程序，以确保实验结果的准确性和可重复性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "procedure" as "pro-ceed-ure" — the proper way to proceed through a series of steps to accomplish a task.
把"procedure"想成"pro-ceed-ure"——按照一系列步骤正确推进以完成任务的方式。
