# 🔢 amount

🔊 /əˈmaʊnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To total or add up to; to be equivalent to. (n.) A quantity or sum of something.
**中文：** (v.) 总计，合计；相当于 (n.) 数量；总额

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quantity（数量）
- sum（总数）
- total（总计）
- aggregate（合计）
- volume（量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amount to, amount of, large/small amount, total amount, significant amount**
👉 Example: The expenses amount to far more than the original budget allowed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The total **amount** of funding allocated to scientific research has increased substantially this fiscal year.
   本财年分配给科学研究的资金总额已大幅增加。

2. His repeated absences **amount** to a deliberate disregard for the institution's attendance policies.
   他反复缺席相当于故意无视该机构的出勤政策。

3. A considerable **amount** of evidence was presented during the trial to establish the defendant's culpability.
   审判期间提交了大量证据以确定被告的罪责。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a mount" of things piling up — the higher the mount, the greater the amount.
想象一"座山"的东西堆积起来——山越高，数量（amount）越大。
