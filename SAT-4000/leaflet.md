# 🍃 leaflet

🔊 /ˈliːflɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small, flat leaf or leaflike part of a compound leaf; a printed sheet of paper containing information, distributed for promotional or informational purposes
**中文：** n. 小叶；嫩叶；传单；活页

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pamphlet（小册子）
- brochure（宣传手册）
- flyer（传单）
- handout（散发材料）
- circular（通告）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **distribute leaflets / hand out leaflets / information leaflet / promotional leaflet**

👉 Example: Volunteers distributed leaflets throughout the neighborhood to raise awareness about the upcoming community meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The environmental group printed thousands of **leaflets** to inform residents about the proposed construction near the nature reserve.
   环保组织印刷了数千份传单，告知居民关于在自然保护区附近拟建项目的信息。

2. A botanist explained that each **leaflet** of the compound leaf performs photosynthesis independently, contributing to overall plant growth.
   植物学家解释说，复叶的每片小叶独立进行光合作用，为植物的整体生长做出贡献。

3. The health department issued a detailed **leaflet** outlining preventive measures citizens should follow during the influenza outbreak.
   卫生部门发布了一份详细的传单，概述了市民在流感爆发期间应遵循的预防措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "leaflet" as "leaf + let (small)" — a small leaf, or by extension, a small sheet of paper like a leaf falling from a tree.
把"leaflet"想成"leaf（叶子）+ let（小的）"——一片小叶子，引申为像树叶飘落一样散发的小纸片。
