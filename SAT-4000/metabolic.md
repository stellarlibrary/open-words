# 🔬 metabolic

🔊 /ˌmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or involving metabolism, the chemical processes that occur within a living organism to maintain life, including the conversion of food into energy.
**中文：** adj. 变化的；新陈代谢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- physiological（生理的）
- biochemical（生物化学的）
- catabolic（分解代谢的）
- anabolic（合成代谢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **metabolic rate / metabolic process / metabolic disorder / metabolic pathway**

👉 Example: Regular exercise can significantly increase your metabolic rate and improve overall health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers discovered that the new drug targets a specific **metabolic** pathway involved in the development of type 2 diabetes.
   研究人员发现这种新药物针对与2型糖尿病发展相关的特定代谢途径。

2. The patient was diagnosed with a rare **metabolic** disorder that prevents the body from properly processing certain amino acids.
   该患者被诊断出一种罕见的代谢紊乱，导致身体无法正常处理某些氨基酸。

3. Studies have shown that prolonged sleep deprivation can significantly disrupt **metabolic** functions and increase the risk of obesity.
   研究表明，长期睡眠不足会严重扰乱代谢功能并增加肥胖风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meta-bolic" as "meta (change) + bolic (throw)" — metabolism is about throwing and changing chemicals around inside your body to produce energy.
把"metabolic"想成"meta（变化）+ bolic（投掷）"——新陈代谢就是在体内投掷和转化化学物质以产生能量。
