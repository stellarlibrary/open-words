# ☣️ hazardous

🔊 /ˈhæzərdəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Risky; dangerous; involving or exposing one to risk of loss or harm
**中文：** adj. 危险的，冒险的；碰运气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dangerous（危险的）
- perilous（险恶的）
- risky（冒险的）
- treacherous（充满危险的）
- precarious（不安全的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hazardous waste / hazardous materials / hazardous conditions / hazardous to health**

👉 Example: Workers handling hazardous materials must wear proper protective equipment at all times.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The factory was fined for illegally dumping **hazardous** waste into the river, contaminating the drinking water supply.
   该工厂因非法向河流倾倒危险废物、污染饮用水源而被罚款。

2. Prolonged exposure to **hazardous** chemicals without adequate ventilation can lead to severe respiratory illness over time.
   长期暴露在危险化学品中而没有足够的通风，可能会导致严重的呼吸系统疾病。

3. The rescue team faced **hazardous** conditions as they navigated through the collapsed structure to locate survivors.
   救援队在穿过倒塌建筑物寻找幸存者时面临着危险的条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hazard + ous" — full of hazard, meaning full of danger and risk at every turn.
把"hazardous"想成"hazard（危险）+ ous（充满的）"——充满危险，到处都有风险。
