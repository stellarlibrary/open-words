# 😂 risible

🔊 /ˈrɪzɪbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Deserving or inviting ridicule; laughable or ludicrous
**中文：** adj. 可笑的，滑稽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- laughable（可笑的）
- ludicrous（荒唐的）
- absurd（荒谬的）
- comical（滑稽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **risible claim / risible attempt / risible notion / utterly risible**

👉 Example: The defendant's risible explanation failed to convince even the most sympathetic jurors in the courtroom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics dismissed the proposal as **risible**, arguing that it lacked any foundation in economic reality or sound reasoning.
   批评者认为这项提案可笑至极，缺乏任何经济现实或合理推理的基础。

2. The author's **risible** depiction of aristocratic manners served as a biting satire of upper-class pretensions.
   作者对贵族礼仪的滑稽描写是对上层阶级虚伪做作的辛辣讽刺。

3. His **risible** attempt to justify the discrepancy only deepened the committee's suspicion of financial misconduct.
   他为差异所做的可笑辩解只加深了委员会对财务不当行为的怀疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the Latin root "risus" (laughter) — something risible makes you want to laugh, usually because it is absurd.
把"risible"联想到拉丁语词根"risus"（笑）——可笑的事物让你忍不住发笑，通常因为它很荒谬。
