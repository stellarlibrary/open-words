# 🏛️ buttress

🔊 /ˈbʌtrəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A projecting support built against a wall; something that supports or strengthens; (v.) to support, reinforce, or prop up
**中文：** n. 扶壁；拱壁；支撑物 v. 支持；以扶壁支撑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reinforce（加强）
- bolster（支持）
- support（支撑）
- prop up（撑起）
- strengthen（巩固）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **buttress an argument / buttress a claim / buttress against / flying buttress**

👉 Example: The lawyer presented additional evidence to buttress her argument before the skeptical jury.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee introduced new statistical data to **buttress** its recommendation for increased funding in public education.
   委员会引入了新的统计数据来支持其增加公共教育拨款的建议。

2. Medieval architects designed elaborate flying **buttresses** to distribute the weight of cathedral walls and soaring vaulted ceilings.
   中世纪建筑师设计了精巧的飞扶壁来分散大教堂墙壁和高耸拱顶的重量。

3. Recent archaeological discoveries **buttress** the theory that ancient civilizations engaged in long-distance maritime trade across oceans.
   最近的考古发现支持了古代文明进行跨洋远距离海上贸易的理论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "butt-ress" — like pressing your butt against a wall to hold it up. A buttress is a structure that presses against a wall to support it.
想想"butt-ress"——就像用屁股（butt）顶住墙壁来撑住它。Buttress就是一种紧贴墙壁以支撑它的结构。
