# 🚫 renunciation

🔊 /rɪˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The formal act of giving up a right, claim, possession, or association; a declaration of abandonment
**中文：** n. 放弃；脱离关系；拒绝承认

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abandonment（放弃）
- relinquishment（放弃）
- rejection（拒绝）
- abdication（退位）
- disavowal（否认）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **renunciation of rights / renunciation of citizenship / renunciation of violence / formal renunciation**

👉 Example: The treaty required a complete renunciation of territorial claims by both parties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The monk's **renunciation** of worldly possessions demonstrated his profound commitment to a life of spiritual devotion.
   这位僧侣对世俗财产的放弃体现了他对精神信仰生活的深切承诺。

2. The government demanded a formal **renunciation** of violence from all armed groups before any peace negotiations could begin.
   政府要求所有武装团体在和平谈判开始前正式宣布放弃暴力。

3. Her **renunciation** of dual citizenship was a legal prerequisite for assuming the high-ranking governmental position.
   她放弃双重国籍是担任这一高级政府职位的法律前提。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-NUNCI-ation" as "re-ANNOUNCE-ation" — you announce again that you are giving something up formally.
把"renunciation"想成"re-ANNOUNCE-ation"——你再次宣布正式放弃某样东西。
