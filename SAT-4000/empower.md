# 💪 empower

🔊 /ɪmˈpaʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To give someone the authority or power to do something; to make someone stronger and more confident
**中文：** v. 授权，允许；使能够

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- authorize（授权）
- enable（使能够）
- entitle（赋予权利）
- strengthen（增强）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **empower someone to do something / empower communities / empower individuals / feel empowered**

👉 Example: The new legislation was designed to empower citizens to participate more actively in local governance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's mission is to **empower** underprivileged youth through education, mentorship, and access to resources.
   该组织的使命是通过教育、指导和资源获取来赋能弱势青年。

2. The board of directors voted to **empower** the committee chairperson to negotiate contracts on behalf of the institution.
   董事会投票授权委员会主席代表机构进行合同谈判。

3. Access to quality education can **empower** entire communities to break the persistent cycle of poverty and inequality.
   获得优质教育可以使整个社区有能力打破持续的贫困和不平等循环。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "em-power" as putting "power" into someone — you fill them with the power they need to act and succeed.
把"empower"想成把"power（力量）"注入某人——你给他们注入行动和成功所需的力量。
