# 💵 stipend

🔊 /ˈstaɪpɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A fixed regular sum paid as a salary or allowance, especially to a student or trainee
**中文：** n. 薪金，定期生活津贴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- allowance（津贴）
- salary（薪水）
- remuneration（报酬）
- grant（助学金）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **monthly stipend / receive a stipend / living stipend / research stipend**

👉 Example: Graduate students typically receive a modest stipend to cover basic living expenses during their studies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university offered a generous **stipend** to attract talented doctoral candidates from around the world to its program.
   该大学提供了丰厚的津贴，以吸引世界各地有才华的博士候选人加入其项目。

2. Her monthly **stipend** as a research assistant barely covered rent and groceries in the expensive metropolitan area.
   她作为研究助理的每月津贴勉强够支付这个昂贵都市区的房租和日常食品。

3. The foundation provides an annual **stipend** to emerging artists so they can focus entirely on their creative work.
   该基金会向新兴艺术家提供年度津贴，使他们能够全身心投入创作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sti-PEND" — "pend" sounds like "pending payment," a stipend is a regular pending payment you receive.
把"stipend"想成"sti-PEND"——"pend"听起来像"pending payment（待付款）"，津贴就是你定期收到的固定付款。
