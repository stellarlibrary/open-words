# 🌿 luxuriant

🔊 /lʌɡˈʒʊəriənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Rich and profuse in growth; lush and abundant; elaborate or extravagant in style
**中文：** 繁茂的；丰富的；肥沃的；奢华的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lush（茂盛的）
- abundant（丰富的）
- profuse（大量的）
- verdant（翠绿的）
- opulent（华丽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **luxuriant vegetation / luxuriant growth / luxuriant hair / luxuriant foliage**

👉 Example: The tropical island was covered with luxuriant vegetation that thrived in the warm, humid climate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanical garden featured **luxuriant** tropical plants that created a dense canopy of vivid green above the winding pathways.
   这座植物园拥有繁茂的热带植物，在蜿蜒的小径上方形成了一片浓密的翠绿树荫。

2. After months of consistent rainfall, the once-barren hillside transformed into a **luxuriant** landscape of wildflowers and tall grasses.
   经过数月持续降雨，曾经荒芜的山坡变成了一片野花和高草繁茂的美丽景观。

3. The author's **luxuriant** prose style, rich with elaborate imagery and detail, captivated readers throughout the literary world.
   这位作者华丽的散文风格，充满精致的意象和细节，吸引了整个文学界的读者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "luxuri-ant" — like a luxury garden full of ants because there is so much lush, abundant growth everywhere.
把"luxuriant"想成"luxury（奢华）+ ant（蚂蚁）"——像一个奢华的花园里到处都是蚂蚁，因为到处都是茂盛丰富的植被。
