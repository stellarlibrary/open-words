# 🌿 herbivore

🔊 /ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An animal that feeds primarily on plants, grasses, and other vegetation
**中文：** n. 食草动物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plant-eater（食草动物）
- grazer（食草动物）
- folivore（食叶动物）
- browser（食嫩叶动物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strict herbivore / large herbivore / herbivore species / herbivore population**

👉 Example: The African savanna is home to numerous large herbivores, including elephants and zebras.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **herbivore** population in the national park has increased significantly since the reintroduction of native grassland species.
   自从重新引入本地草原物种以来，国家公园中食草动物的数量显著增加。

2. Unlike carnivores, a **herbivore** possesses specialized digestive systems capable of breaking down cellulose found in plant material.
   与肉食动物不同，食草动物拥有能够分解植物材料中纤维素的特殊消化系统。

3. Researchers discovered that this ancient **herbivore** thrived in wetland environments during the late Cretaceous period.
   研究人员发现这种古代食草动物在白垩纪晚期的湿地环境中繁盛生长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "herbi-vore" as "herb" (草本植物) + "vore" (吃) — an animal that eats herbs and plants.
把"herbivore"拆成"herb"（草本植物）+"vore"（吃）——一种吃草本植物的动物。
