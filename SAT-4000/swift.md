# 🏃 swift

🔊 /swɪft/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, adverb | 形容词, 名词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A type of bird (brown swift). (adj.) Fast; rapid; agile; immediate. (adv.) Swiftly
**中文：** adj. 快速的，敏捷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rapid（迅速的）
- fleet（快速的）
- nimble（敏捷的）
- prompt（立刻的）
- hasty（迅速的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **swift action / swift response / swift justice / swift current / swift recovery**

👉 Example: The government promised swift action to address the growing concerns about public safety.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee's **swift** response to the crisis prevented further economic damage and restored public confidence in the institution.
   委员会对危机的迅速反应防止了进一步的经济损失，并恢复了公众对该机构的信心。

2. The predator's **swift** movements made it nearly impossible for the prey to escape across the open savanna.
   捕食者敏捷的动作使猎物几乎不可能在开阔的草原上逃脱。

3. Historians credit the general's **swift** decision-making with securing a decisive victory against a numerically superior opposing force.
   历史学家认为这位将军迅速的决策确保了对数量占优的敌军取得决定性胜利。
