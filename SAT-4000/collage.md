# 🎨 collage

🔊 /kəˈlɑːʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An artistic composition made by gluing various materials such as photographs, fabric, and paper onto a surface; a combination of diverse elements
**中文：** n. 抽象拼贴画；拼贴艺术；各种事物的组合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- montage（蒙太奇）
- assemblage（组合体）
- mosaic（镶嵌画）
- patchwork（拼凑物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a collage / photo collage / collage of images / digital collage**

👉 Example: The artist created a striking collage from old newspaper clippings and vintage photographs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's newest exhibit features an enormous **collage** composed of recycled materials gathered from urban environments across the continent.
   博物馆最新的展览展出了一幅巨大的拼贴画，由从整个大陆的城市环境中收集的回收材料制成。

2. Her memoir reads like a vivid **collage** of memories, weaving together childhood recollections with philosophical reflections.
   她的回忆录读起来像一幅生动的记忆拼贴画，将童年回忆与哲学思考交织在一起。

3. Students were instructed to assemble a **collage** representing their cultural heritage using photographs, fabrics, and handwritten notes.
   学生们被要求用照片、织物和手写笔记组装一幅代表其文化传统的拼贴画。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "collage" as "coll-age" — a collection of things glued ("colle" means glue in French) together to form art.
把"collage"想成来自法语"colle"（胶水）——用胶水把各种东西粘在一起形成的艺术作品。
