# ❓ unaccountable

🔊 /ˌʌnəˈkaʊntəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unable to be explained or understood; not required to justify actions or decisions; mysterious
**中文：** adj. 无法解释的；莫名其妙的；无责任的；不受约束的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inexplicable（无法解释的）
- mysterious（神秘的）
- unanswerble（不可问责的）
- baffling（令人困惑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unaccountable behavior / unaccountable fear / unaccountable to anyone / remain unaccountable**

👉 Example: The committee expressed concern that the agency remained largely unaccountable to the public it served.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. An **unaccountable** sense of dread overcame the researcher as she reviewed the anomalous data from the experiment.
   一种莫名其妙的恐惧感笼罩了这位研究人员，当她审查实验中的异常数据时。

2. Critics argued that the regulatory body was effectively **unaccountable**, operating without any meaningful oversight or transparency.
   批评者认为该监管机构实际上不受问责，在没有任何实质性监督或透明度的情况下运作。

3. The **unaccountable** disappearance of several key documents raised serious questions about the integrity of the investigation.
   几份关键文件莫名其妙地消失，引发了对调查公正性的严重质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-account-able" — something you cannot account for, something that defies explanation or responsibility.
把"unaccountable"拆分为"un（不能）+ account（解释）+ able（能够的）"——无法解释的，或不需要对任何人负责的。
