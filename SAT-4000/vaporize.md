# 🌫️ vaporize

🔊 /ˈveɪpəraɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To convert or be converted into vapor; to cause a substance to change from a liquid or solid into a gaseous state
**中文：** v. （使）蒸发；（使）汽化；（使）气化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- evaporate（蒸发）
- volatilize（挥发）
- sublimate（升华）
- dissipate（消散）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vaporize water / vaporize instantly / vaporize a substance / vaporize under heat / vaporize at high temperatures**

👉 Example: The intense laser beam can vaporize metal within a fraction of a second.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The extreme heat generated by the volcanic eruption was sufficient to **vaporize** entire bodies of water in the surrounding crater lakes.
   火山爆发产生的极端高温足以使周围火山口湖中的整个水体蒸发殆尽。

2. Scientists used a high-powered laser to **vaporize** rock samples and analyze their elemental composition through spectroscopic methods.
   科学家使用高功率激光蒸发岩石样本，并通过光谱方法分析其元素组成。

3. When heated beyond its boiling point, the chemical compound will **vaporize** and release fumes that require proper ventilation in the laboratory.
   当加热超过沸点时，该化合物将蒸发并释放出需要实验室适当通风的烟气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vaporize" as "vapor + ize" — to make into vapor. Picture a puddle of water vanishing into thin air on a scorching day.
把"vaporize"想成"vapor（蒸气）+ ize（使…化）"——使变成蒸气。想象一滩水在酷热的天气里消失在空气中。
