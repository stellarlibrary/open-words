# 😴 lackadaisical

🔊 /ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lacking enthusiasm and determination; carelessly lazy; showing a lack of interest or effort
**中文：** adj. 懒洋洋的；无精打采的；伤感的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- listless（无精打采的）
- lethargic（昏昏欲睡的）
- languid（懒散的）
- indifferent（漠不关心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a lackadaisical attitude / lackadaisical approach / lackadaisical performance / lackadaisical effort**

👉 Example: The supervisor criticized the intern's lackadaisical approach to meeting deadlines and completing assigned tasks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor expressed frustration with the students' **lackadaisical** attitude toward submitting their research papers on time.
   教授对学生们在按时提交研究论文方面懒洋洋的态度表示不满。

2. A **lackadaisical** approach to laboratory safety protocols can result in serious accidents and costly equipment damage.
   对实验室安全规程采取漫不经心的态度可能导致严重事故和昂贵的设备损坏。

3. The team's **lackadaisical** performance during the first quarter prompted management to implement stricter accountability measures.
   团队在第一季度无精打采的表现促使管理层实施了更严格的问责措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lackadaisical" as "lack a daisy call" — someone so lazy they lack the energy even to call out to a daisy in the garden.
把"lackadaisical"想成"lack a daisy call"——一个人懒到连对花园里的雏菊打个招呼的精力都没有。
