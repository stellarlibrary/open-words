# ⛵ becalm

🔊 /bɪˈkɑːm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make calm or still; to deprive a sailing ship of wind, causing it to be unable to move
**中文：** v. 使安静；使停滞；因无风而使停航

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- still（使平静）
- soothe（抚慰）
- pacify（使安静）
- immobilize（使停滞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **becalmed at sea / becalmed ship / becalmed in negotiations / completely becalmed**

👉 Example: The fleet was becalmed for three days in the tropical waters with no wind in sight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sailing vessel was **becalmed** in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean, leaving the anxious crew stranded without provisions.
   这艘帆船在大西洋中部因无风而停航，焦虑的船员们在没有补给的情况下被困。

2. Progress on the legislative reform has been **becalmed** by persistent disagreements among the coalition's leading members.
   立法改革的进展因联盟主要成员之间持续的分歧而陷入停滞。

3. The therapist's gentle words gradually **becalmed** the distressed patient, restoring a sense of tranquility to the room.
   治疗师温和的话语逐渐使焦虑的病人平静下来，为房间恢复了一种宁静感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be + calm" — to becalm is literally to make something be calm, whether it is a person or a windless sea.
把"becalm"拆成"be + calm"——使某物变得平静，无论是人还是无风的海面。
