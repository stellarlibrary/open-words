# 🎯 acute

🔊 /əˈkjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very serious or severe; having a rapid onset and short course (of illness); keenly perceptive or intellectually sharp; intense.
**中文：** 严重的，急性的；敏锐的；激烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- severe（严重的）
- intense（强烈的）
- keen（敏锐的）
- sharp（尖锐的）
- critical（危急的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acute pain/illness/shortage/crisis**, **acute awareness/sense**, **acute angle (geometry)**

👉 Example: The region faces an acute shortage of clean drinking water during the prolonged drought.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient was admitted to the hospital with **acute** respiratory distress requiring immediate medical intervention.
   患者因急性呼吸窘迫被送入医院，需要立即进行医疗干预。

2. Her **acute** observation skills allowed her to detect subtle inconsistencies in the financial reports.
   她敏锐的观察能力使她能够发现财务报告中细微的不一致之处。

3. The country experienced an **acute** economic crisis that led to widespread unemployment and social unrest.
   该国经历了一场严重的经济危机，导致了大范围的失业和社会动荡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of an "acute angle" — it is sharp and pointed, just like acute pain is sharp or an acute mind is sharp.
想想"acute angle（锐角）"——它是尖锐的，就像acute疼痛是剧烈的，acute头脑是敏锐的，一切都指向"尖锐"。
