# 🧪 lactose

🔊 /ˈlæktoʊs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sugar naturally present in milk, composed of glucose and galactose; also called milk sugar
**中文：** n. 乳糖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- milk sugar（乳糖）
- disaccharide（二糖）
- galactose compound（半乳糖化合物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lactose intolerance / lactose-free / contain lactose / digest lactose**

👉 Example: Many adults around the world are unable to properly digest lactose found in dairy products.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Approximately seventy percent of the global population experiences some degree of **lactose** intolerance after early childhood.
   全球大约百分之七十的人口在幼儿期过后会出现一定程度的乳糖不耐受。

2. The biochemistry students analyzed the molecular structure of **lactose** during their laboratory session on carbohydrate chemistry.
   生物化学专业的学生在碳水化合物化学实验课上分析了乳糖的分子结构。

3. Food manufacturers now offer a wide variety of **lactose**-free alternatives to accommodate consumers with dietary sensitivities.
   食品制造商现在提供各种各样的无乳糖替代品，以满足有饮食敏感性的消费者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lact-" from Latin "lac" meaning milk, plus "-ose" the suffix for sugars — lactose is literally "milk sugar."
把"lactose"拆成"lact（乳）"和"-ose（糖的后缀）"——乳糖就是"牛奶中的糖"。
