# 🛡️ interposition

🔊 /ˌɪntərpəˈzɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of placing or inserting something between other things; the act of intervening or coming between; interference or mediation
**中文：** n. 干涉，介入；插入，放入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intervention（干预）
- mediation（调解）
- insertion（插入）
- interference（干涉）
- intercession（斡旋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **interposition of authority / interposition of a barrier / interposition of objections / state interposition**

👉 Example: The interposition of federal authority prevented the state government from enforcing the unconstitutional statute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **interposition** of a neutral mediator proved essential in resolving the prolonged labor dispute between the union and management.
   中立调解人的介入对于解决工会与管理层之间旷日持久的劳资纠纷至关重要。

2. Historians studied the doctrine of **interposition**, whereby states claimed the right to oppose federal actions they deemed unconstitutional.
   历史学家研究了州权介入学说，即各州声称有权反对他们认为违宪的联邦行为。

3. The **interposition** of a protective shield between the reactor and the control room ensured the safety of all personnel on site.
   在反应堆和控制室之间插入防护屏障确保了现场所有人员的安全。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-POSITION" — placing something into a position between two other things, which is exactly what interposition means.
把"interposition"想成"inter-POSITION"——将某物放置到两个其他事物之间的位置，这正是interposition的含义。
