# 🎙️ narrate

🔊 /ˈnæreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To give a spoken or written account of events; to provide a commentary or voiceover for a film, broadcast, or performance
**中文：** v. 叙述；讲述；给……作旁白

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recount（叙述）
- relate（讲述）
- describe（描述）
- chronicle（记述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **narrate a story / narrate a documentary / narrate events / narrate in first person**

👉 Example: The acclaimed actor was invited to narrate the award-winning documentary about marine conservation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author chose to **narrate** the novel from the perspective of an unreliable protagonist to heighten the sense of suspense.
   作者选择从一个不可靠的主人公的视角来**叙述**这部小说，以增强悬念感。

2. A distinguished historian was commissioned to **narrate** the documentary series chronicling the nation's civil rights movement.
   一位杰出的历史学家受邀为记录该国民权运动的纪录片系列**作旁白**。

3. The witness was asked to carefully **narrate** the sequence of events that preceded the unprecedented security breach.
   证人被要求仔细**叙述**这次史无前例的安全漏洞发生之前的事件经过。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "narrate" as "narrative" without the ending — to narrate is to create a narrative, telling the story aloud.
把"narrate"想成去掉词尾的"narrative"——narrate就是创造叙事，大声讲述故事。
