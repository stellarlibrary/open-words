# 🔥 combustible

🔊 /kəmˈbʌs.tɪ.bəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Capable of catching fire and burning easily; easily aroused or excited. (n.) A substance that catches fire and burns readily.

**中文：** （形容词）易燃的；容易激动的。（名词）可燃物；容易着火并燃烧的物质。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- flammable（易燃的）
- inflammable（易燃的）
- ignitable（可点燃的）
- volatile（挥发性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** "combustible materials / combustible mixture / highly combustible" — used in both literal (fire-related) and figurative (emotionally volatile) contexts.

**Example:** Workers must store all combustible materials in properly ventilated areas away from sources of ignition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The safety inspector warned that storing **combustible** materials near the furnace posed a serious risk of fire to the entire facility.
   安全检查员警告说，在火炉附近存放**易燃**材料对整个设施构成严重的火灾风险。

2. The **combustible** political atmosphere in the region made diplomatic intervention both urgent and extraordinarily difficult to execute.
   该地区**一触即发的**政治氛围使得外交干预既紧迫又极难执行。

3. The chemist classified each substance as either **combustible** or non-combustible before determining appropriate laboratory storage protocols.
   化学家在确定适当的实验室存储规程之前，将每种物质分类为**可燃的**或不可燃的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Combust" (to burn) + "-ible" (able to) — literally "able to burn." Think of "combustion engine" — an engine that burns fuel.

**中文：** "Combust"（燃烧）+ "-ible"（能够）——字面意思是"能够燃烧的"。想想"combustion engine"（内燃机）——一种燃烧燃料的发动机，就能记住"易燃的"。
