# 💪 potent

🔊 /ˈpoʊtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having great power, influence, or effect; strongly persuasive or convincing
**中文：** adj. 有效的；强有力的，有权势的；有说服力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- powerful（强有力的）
- influential（有影响力的）
- compelling（令人信服的）
- formidable（强大的）
- effective（有效的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **potent force / potent argument / potent drug / potent symbol / potent reminder**

👉 Example: The scientist discovered a potent compound capable of neutralizing the virus within hours.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor presented a **potent** argument that challenged the foundational assumptions of conventional economic theory.
   教授提出了一个有力的论点，挑战了传统经济理论的基本假设。

2. This newly developed medication proved to be far more **potent** than any previously available treatment for the disease.
   这种新开发的药物被证明比以前任何可用的疾病治疗方法都更加有效。

3. The activist's speech served as a **potent** reminder of the injustices still prevalent in modern democratic societies.
   这位活动家的演讲有力地提醒人们现代民主社会中仍然普遍存在的不公正现象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "potent" as sharing the root with "potential" — something potent has the power and potential to make a strong impact.
把"potent"想成和"potential（潜力）"共享词根——有potent特质的事物拥有产生强大影响的力量和潜力。
