# 🏆 competitive

🔊 /kəmˈpetɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or characterized by competition; having a strong desire to compete or succeed; as good as or better than others
**中文：** adj. 竞争的；有竞争力的；比赛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rival（竞争的）
- aggressive（进取的）
- ambitious（有雄心的）
- combative（好斗的）
- cutthroat（激烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **competitive advantage / competitive market / competitive price / highly competitive / competitive edge**

👉 Example: The firm maintained a competitive edge by investing heavily in research and innovation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technology sector remains fiercely **competitive**, with companies constantly striving to outpace rivals through innovation and strategic acquisitions.
   科技行业仍然竞争激烈，各公司不断努力通过创新和战略收购超越竞争对手。

2. Universities must offer **competitive** salaries and research funding to attract and retain world-class scholars in their departments.
   大学必须提供有竞争力的薪资和研究经费，以吸引和留住各部门的世界级学者。

3. Her inherently **competitive** nature drove her to excel in every academic pursuit, from undergraduate studies through doctoral research.
   她天生的竞争天性驱使她在每一项学术追求中都力求卓越，从本科学习到博士研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "competitive" as "compete + itive" — having the quality of competing, always striving to be the best in any contest or market.
把"competitive"想成"compete（竞争）+ itive（具有…性质的）"——具有竞争性质的，总是在任何比赛或市场中力争最好。
