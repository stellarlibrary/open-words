# 🏛️ mansion

🔊 /ˈmænʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large, impressive house; an official residence of a dignitary or wealthy person
**中文：** n. 大厦；宅邸；官邸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- estate（庄园）
- manor（庄园宅第）
- villa（别墅）
- palace（宫殿）
- residence（住所）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stately mansion / mansion house / governor's mansion / sprawling mansion**

👉 Example: The governor hosted the annual charity gala at the historic mansion overlooking the lake.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Victorian **mansion** on the hilltop had been meticulously restored to reflect its original nineteenth-century architectural grandeur.
   山顶上的维多利亚式宅邸经过精心修复，重现了其十九世纪原有的建筑宏伟风貌。

2. After amassing considerable wealth, the industrialist commissioned architects to design a sprawling **mansion** with over forty rooms.
   在积累了可观的财富之后，这位实业家委托建筑师设计了一座拥有四十多个房间的大型宅邸。

3. The historic **mansion** now serves as a museum, offering guided tours that highlight the region's colonial heritage and culture.
   这座历史悠久的大厦现在用作博物馆，提供导览讲解该地区的殖民遗产和文化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "man-sion" as a house so big that a "man" needs a whole "zone" (sion sounds like zone) to live in — a massive, grand residence.
把"mansion"想成房子大到一个"man"（人）需要一整个"zone"（sion听起来像zone，区域）来居住——一座巨大宏伟的住所。
