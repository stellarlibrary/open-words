# 🎯 assassinate

🔊 /əˈsæsɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To murder a prominent or important person, especially for political or ideological reasons.
**中文：** v. 暗杀；行刺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- murder（谋杀）
- execute（处决）
- eliminate（消灭）
- slay（杀害）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assassinate** exclusively for the deliberate killing of important figures such as political leaders, heads of state, or public personalities. It implies premeditation and often political motivation.

👉 Example: The rebels plotted to assassinate the dictator during the annual military parade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The conspirators devised an elaborate scheme to **assassinate** the president during a formal state banquet.
   密谋者制定了一项精心策划的计划，企图在正式国宴期间暗杀总统。

2. Several attempts to **assassinate** the reformist leader failed because of his vigilant and well-trained security team.
   由于这位改革派领导人警惕且训练有素的安保团队，数次暗杀企图均告失败。

3. The intelligence agency uncovered a foreign plot to **assassinate** the ambassador stationed in the capital city.
   情报机构揭露了一个暗杀驻首都大使的外国阴谋。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assassinate" as "assassin + ate" — the assassin "ate" (consumed) the life of an important person. The extra "-ate" turns the noun into an action verb.
把"assassinate"想成"assassin + ate"——刺客"吞噬"了一位重要人物的生命。多出的"-ate"把名词变成了动词。
