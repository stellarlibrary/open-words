# ⛓️ trammel

🔊 /ˈtræməl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To restrict or restrain someone's freedom of action. (n.) A constraint or impediment to free action; a type of fishing net
**中文：** v. 束缚；拘束；限制 n. 拘束；束缚物；限制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restrain（限制）
- shackle（束缚）
- hinder（阻碍）
- impede（妨碍）
- constrain（约束）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **trammel freedom / trammel progress / trammels of convention / free from trammels**

👉 Example: The artist refused to be trammeled by the rigid conventions of traditional academic painting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Excessive bureaucratic regulations continue to **trammel** innovation and prevent small businesses from competing effectively.
   过多的官僚法规继续**束缚**创新，阻止小企业有效竞争。

2. The philosopher argued that outdated social **trammels** prevent individuals from achieving their full intellectual potential.
   这位哲学家认为过时的社会**束缚**阻碍了个人充分发挥其智力潜能。

3. The new administration pledged to remove the legal **trammels** that had long hindered sustainable economic development.
   新政府承诺消除长期以来阻碍可持续经济发展的法律**桎梏**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trammel" as "trap + hammel" — like a trap that hampers your movement, a trammel restricts your freedom.
把"trammel"想成"trap（陷阱）+ hamper（妨碍）"——就像一个阻碍你行动的陷阱，trammel限制你的自由。
