# 🌊 agitate

🔊 /ˈædʒ.ɪ.teɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To shake or stir vigorously; to disturb, excite, or provoke someone emotionally or politically.
**中文：** 摇动；骚动；使…激动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disturb（扰乱）
- provoke（激怒）
- stir（搅动）
- unsettle（使不安）
- rouse（唤起）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **agitate for/against, agitate the public, agitate a solution**
👉 Example: The activists continued to agitate for meaningful reforms in the education system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The controversial proposal began to **agitate** the residents, who organized protests across the entire district.
   这项有争议的提案开始激怒居民，他们在整个地区组织了抗议活动。

2. Please do not **agitate** the chemical mixture, as any disturbance could trigger a dangerous reaction.
   请不要摇动化学混合物，因为任何扰动都可能引发危险反应。

3. Opposition leaders sought to **agitate** the public against the government's newly proposed taxation policies.
   反对派领导人试图煽动公众反对政府新提出的税收政策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a washing machine on "agitate" cycle — it shakes and stirs everything vigorously inside.
想象洗衣机的"搅动"模式——它在里面剧烈地摇动和搅拌一切。
