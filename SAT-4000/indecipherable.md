# 🔐 indecipherable

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈsaɪfərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Impossible to read, interpret, or understand; not able to be decoded or made sense of
**中文：** adj. 无法解释的；破译不出的；难辨认的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- illegible（难以辨认的）
- unintelligible（无法理解的）
- incomprehensible（不可理解的）
- cryptic（神秘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indecipherable handwriting / indecipherable code / virtually indecipherable / indecipherable symbols**

👉 Example: The ancient tablet was covered in indecipherable symbols that baffled even the most experienced linguists.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **indecipherable** handwriting on the exam papers caused considerable confusion among the teaching assistants during grading.
   教授在试卷上难以辨认的笔迹在批改过程中给助教们造成了相当大的困惑。

2. Despite decades of scholarly effort, the encrypted manuscript remained **indecipherable**, its secrets locked away from the modern world.
   尽管经过数十年的学术努力，这份加密手稿仍然无法破译，其秘密与现代世界隔绝。

3. The witness provided testimony filled with **indecipherable** jargon that neither the jury nor the judge could adequately comprehend.
   证人提供的证词充满了难以理解的术语，陪审团和法官都无法充分理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-decipher-able" — "in" means "not" and "decipher" means "to decode," so indecipherable means something that cannot be deciphered or decoded.
把"indecipherable"拆开——"in"表示"不能"，"decipher"表示"破译"，所以indecipherable就是无法破译的意思。
