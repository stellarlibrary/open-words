# 🧬 hereditary

🔊 /həˈredɪteri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Passed down from parent to offspring through genes; inherited by legal succession or tradition
**中文：** adj. 遗传的；世袭的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inherited（继承的）
- genetic（遗传的）
- ancestral（祖传的）
- inborn（天生的）
- congenital（先天的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hereditary disease / hereditary trait / hereditary monarchy / hereditary title**

👉 Example: The researchers confirmed that the condition is hereditary and can be traced back several generations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medical studies have confirmed that certain forms of cancer are **hereditary** and may be passed through specific gene mutations.
   医学研究已证实某些形式的癌症是遗传性的，可能通过特定的基因突变传递。

2. The **hereditary** monarchy was abolished after the revolution, and a democratic republic was established in its place.
   世袭君主制在革命后被废除，取而代之的是民主共和国。

3. Genetic counselors help families understand the risks associated with **hereditary** conditions before making reproductive decisions.
   遗传咨询师帮助家庭在做出生育决定之前了解与遗传疾病相关的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hereditary" as containing "heir" — an heir inherits things passed down, just as hereditary traits are passed down.
把"hereditary"看成包含"heir"（继承人）——继承人继承传递下来的东西，正如遗传特征代代相传。
