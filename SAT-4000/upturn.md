# 📈 upturn

🔊 /ˈʌptɜːrn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An improvement or upward trend, especially in economic conditions. (v.) To turn upward or upside down
**中文：** n. 情况好转，上升趋势 v. 使转朝上，翻转

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- improvement（改善）
- recovery（复苏）
- upswing（上升）
- revival（振兴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an upturn in / economic upturn / market upturn / sharp upturn**

👉 Example: Economists predicted a significant upturn in the housing market by the end of the fiscal year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation experienced a welcome **upturn** in employment rates following the implementation of new fiscal stimulus measures.
   在实施新的财政刺激措施后，该国就业率出现了令人欣喜的好转。

2. Analysts attribute the recent **upturn** in consumer confidence to declining inflation and stabilizing interest rates.
   分析人士将近期消费者信心的回升归因于通胀下降和利率趋于稳定。

3. The farmer **upturned** the soil with a plow to prepare the fields for the spring planting season.
   农民用犁翻转土壤，为春季播种季节做准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "up-turn" as things literally "turning up" — when the economy turns upward, conditions improve.
把"upturn"想成事物"向上翻转"——当经济向上转时，情况就好转了。
