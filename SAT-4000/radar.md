# 📡 radar

🔊 /ˈreɪdɑːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A system for detecting the presence, direction, distance, and speed of objects by sending out pulses of radio waves; figuratively, one's awareness or attention.
**中文：** n. 雷达；无线电探测器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detection system（探测系统）
- tracking system（追踪系统）
- sonar（声呐）
- sensor（传感器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on the radar / off the radar / under the radar / radar screen / radar detection**

👉 Example: The emerging technology had barely appeared on the radar of most industry analysts before it disrupted the market.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advanced **radar** technology enabled meteorologists to track the hurricane's trajectory with unprecedented precision and accuracy.
   先进的雷达技术使气象学家能够以前所未有的精度和准确性追踪飓风的轨迹。

2. The small startup managed to operate under the **radar** until its innovative product attracted attention from major investors.
   这家小型初创公司设法在雷达之下运营，直到其创新产品引起了主要投资者的关注。

3. Military vessels rely on sophisticated **radar** systems to identify potential threats across vast expanses of open ocean.
   军事舰艇依靠精密的雷达系统来识别广阔公海上的潜在威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "RADAR" as an acronym: "RAdio Detection And Ranging" — it sends out radio waves and listens for echoes to find objects.
把"RADAR"想成首字母缩写："RAdio Detection And Ranging（无线电探测与测距）"——它发出无线电波并监听回声来发现物体。
