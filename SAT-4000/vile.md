# 🤢 vile

🔊 /vaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely unpleasant; morally bad; wicked; of very poor quality or low value
**中文：** adj. 卑鄙的，可耻的，恶劣的；坏透的；肮脏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- despicable（卑鄙的）
- contemptible（可鄙的）
- wretched（恶劣的）
- abominable（可恶的）
- loathsome（令人厌恶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vile act / vile behavior / vile language / vile taste / vile temper / vile creature**

👉 Example: The dictator's vile treatment of political prisoners drew condemnation from the international community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist exposed the **vile** conditions in which migrant workers were forced to live on the industrial compound.
   记者揭露了外来务工人员被迫在工业园区中忍受的恶劣生活条件。

2. Throughout history, societies have struggled to punish those who commit **vile** acts against the most vulnerable populations.
   纵观历史，社会一直在努力惩罚那些对最弱势群体犯下卑鄙行为的人。

3. The defendant showed no remorse for the **vile** slander he had spread about his former business partner's reputation.
   被告对他散布的关于前商业伙伴名誉的恶毒诽谤毫无悔意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vile" as rhyming with "bile" — bile is the bitter, disgusting fluid in your stomach, just as vile things make you feel sick.
把"vile"想成和"bile"（胆汁）押韵——胆汁是胃里苦涩恶心的液体，就像卑鄙的事物让你感到不适。
