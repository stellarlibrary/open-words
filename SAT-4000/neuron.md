# 🧠 neuron

🔊 /ˈnjʊərɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A specialized cell that transmits nerve impulses; the basic functional unit of the nervous system
**中文：** n. 神经元；神经单位

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nerve cell（神经细胞）
- neural cell（神经元细胞）
- brain cell（脑细胞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **motor neuron / sensory neuron / neuron firing / mirror neuron / neuron pathway**

👉 Example: Each neuron in the brain can form thousands of connections with other neurons through synaptic junctions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The human brain contains approximately eighty-six billion **neurons**, each capable of forming thousands of synaptic connections simultaneously.
   人脑包含大约八百六十亿个神经元，每个神经元能够同时形成数千个突触连接。

2. Damage to motor **neurons** in the spinal cord can result in permanent loss of voluntary movement in the affected limbs.
   脊髓中运动神经元的损伤可能导致受影响肢体永久丧失自主运动能力。

3. Scientists have developed techniques to observe individual **neurons** firing in real time, revealing unprecedented details about brain function.
   科学家已开发出实时观察单个神经元放电的技术，揭示了关于大脑功能的前所未有的细节。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neuron" as "neuro" (nerve) + "on" (unit) — the basic ON-switch unit of the nervous system that fires electrical signals.
把"neuron"想成"neuro"(神经) + "on"(开)——神经系统中发射电信号的基本"开关"单元。
