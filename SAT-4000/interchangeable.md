# 🔄 interchangeable

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being exchanged or substituted for one another without affecting function or meaning
**中文：** adj. 可互换的；可交换的；可交替的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exchangeable（可交换的）
- substitutable（可替代的）
- equivalent（等价的）
- convertible（可转换的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **interchangeable parts / interchangeable with / virtually interchangeable / interchangeable terms**

👉 Example: In casual conversation, the terms "lawyer" and "attorney" are often used as interchangeable expressions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manufacturer designed the components to be **interchangeable** across all models, significantly reducing production costs and repair complexity.
   制造商将零部件设计为可在所有型号之间互换，大大降低了生产成本和维修复杂度。

2. Although the two words appear similar, they are not entirely **interchangeable** because each carries distinct connotations in academic writing.
   虽然这两个词看起来相似，但它们并非完全可互换，因为在学术写作中各自带有不同的含义。

3. The standardization of **interchangeable** parts during the Industrial Revolution transformed manufacturing efficiency and enabled mass production worldwide.
   工业革命期间可互换零件的标准化改变了制造效率，并使全球大规模生产成为可能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-change-able" — literally "able to change between," meaning two things can swap places and still work the same way.
把"interchangeable"想成"able(能够) + interchange(互换)"——能够互相交换位置，即可互换的。
