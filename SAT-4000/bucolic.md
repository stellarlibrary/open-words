# 🌾 bucolic

🔊 /bjuːˈkɒlɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside and rural life; pastoral. (n.) A pastoral poem or the genre of pastoral poetry
**中文：** adj. 牧歌的；牧羊的；田园风味的 n. 田园诗；农夫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pastoral（田园的）
- rustic（乡村的）
- rural（农村的）
- idyllic（田园诗般的）
- agrarian（农业的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bucolic landscape / bucolic setting / bucolic charm / bucolic scene**

👉 Example: The artist captured the bucolic charm of the rolling green hills dotted with grazing sheep.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The retired professor sought a **bucolic** existence in the countryside, far removed from the noise and pressures of urban life.
   这位退休教授在乡村寻求田园般的生活，远离城市生活的喧嚣和压力。

2. The novel's opening chapter paints a **bucolic** scene of wheat fields stretching toward distant mountains under a cloudless sky.
   小说的开篇描绘了一幅田园风光：麦田在万里无云的天空下延伸至远山。

3. Despite its **bucolic** appearance, the farming community faced serious economic challenges due to declining agricultural subsidies.
   尽管这个农业社区外表呈现田园风光，但由于农业补贴减少，它面临着严重的经济挑战。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bu-COL-ic" — "COL" sounds like "col" in "colonial" farmland; picture a beautiful colonial-era farm with cows and green pastures.
把"bucolic"想成"bu-COL-ic"——"COL"听起来像"colonial（殖民地的）"中的"col"；想象一个美丽的殖民时代农场，有牛群和绿色牧场。
