# 👴 avuncular

🔊 /əˈvʌŋkjələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Resembling or characteristic of an uncle, especially in being kind, friendly, and tolerant toward younger or less experienced people; having a warm, benevolent demeanor.

**中文：** 伯父的；慈祥的。像叔伯一样的，尤其指对年轻人或经验较少的人表现出和蔼、友善和宽容；具有温暖、仁慈的风度。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- benevolent （仁慈的）
- kindly （和蔼的）
- genial （亲切的）
- paternal （父亲般的）
- warm-hearted （热心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Avuncular" derives from the Latin "avunculus" meaning "maternal uncle." It is used to describe a manner or personality rather than an actual family relationship. It typically conveys positive qualities of warmth and gentle guidance.

"avuncular"源自拉丁语"avunculus"，意为"舅舅"。它用于描述一种态度或个性，而非实际的家庭关系。它通常传达温暖和温和引导的积极品质。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **avuncular** manner put his nervous students at ease during their first research presentations.
   *这位教授**慈祥的**态度让紧张的学生们在第一次做研究报告时放松了下来。*

2. With his **avuncular** smile and patient demeanor, the mentor guided countless young professionals through difficult transitions.
   *凭借**慈祥的**微笑和耐心的态度，这位导师引导了无数年轻专业人士度过了困难的转型期。*

3. The ambassador was known for his **avuncular** warmth, which made foreign dignitaries feel genuinely welcomed and respected.
   *这位大使以其**慈祥的**热情而闻名，这使外国政要感到真正受到欢迎和尊重。*

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Avuncular" contains "uncle" hidden inside — think "a-VUNC-ular" where "VUNC" sounds like "UNC" (uncle). An avuncular person acts like a kind, wise uncle.

"avuncular"里藏着"uncle"——想想"a-VUNC-ular"，其中"VUNC"听起来像"UNC"（uncle，叔叔）。一个avuncular的人就像一位和蔼、睿智的叔伯。
