# 🌱 tyro

🔊 /ˈtaɪroʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A beginner or novice; someone who is new to a subject, skill, or activity
**中文：** n. 新手；生手；初学者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- novice（新手）
- beginner（初学者）
- neophyte（新入门者）
- apprentice（学徒）
- greenhorn（生手）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a mere tyro / tyro in the field / tyro at something / political tyro**

👉 Example: Though a tyro in diplomacy, she handled the negotiations with remarkable composure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite being a **tyro** in the world of finance, the young analyst quickly demonstrated an exceptional aptitude for market analysis.
   尽管在金融领域是个新手，这位年轻分析师很快展现出卓越的市场分析能力。

2. The seasoned professor enjoyed mentoring **tyros** who brought fresh perspectives and enthusiasm to the research laboratory.
   这位资深教授喜欢指导那些为研究实验室带来新视角和热情的初学者。

3. Even the most accomplished experts were once **tyros** who had to master the fundamentals before advancing to complex theories.
   即使是最有成就的专家，也曾经是必须先掌握基础知识才能进入复杂理论的新手。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tyro" as "try + o" — someone who is just starting to try things out, a beginner giving it their first attempt.
把"tyro"想成"try（尝试）+ o"——一个刚刚开始尝试事物的人，一个初次尝试的初学者。
