# 🔄 convert

🔊 /kənˈvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To change the form, character, or function of something; to cause someone to adopt a different belief or religion. (n.) A person who has changed their beliefs or religion
**中文：** v. 使转变；转换；使改变信仰 n. 皈依者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transform（转变）
- change（改变）
- switch（转换）
- adapt（改造）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convert into / convert from...to / convert to a belief / convert energy / a recent convert**

👉 Example: The engineers devised a system to convert solar energy into electricity for the entire campus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city plans to **convert** the abandoned industrial warehouse into a modern community center with recreational and educational facilities.
   该市计划将这座废弃的工业仓库转变为一个拥有休闲和教育设施的现代社区中心。

2. Researchers have developed a more efficient method to **convert** carbon dioxide emissions into useful synthetic fuels for transportation.
   研究人员开发了一种更高效的方法，将二氧化碳排放转化为可用于交通运输的合成燃料。

3. As a passionate **convert** to the environmental movement, she dedicated her career to promoting sustainable agricultural practices worldwide.
   作为环保运动的热忱皈依者，她将毕生事业献给了在全球推广可持续农业实践。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-vert" as "completely turn" — the Latin root "vertere" means to turn, so convert means to turn something completely into something else.
把"convert"想成"completely turn"——拉丁词根"vertere"意为转动，所以convert意为将某物彻底转变为另一物。
