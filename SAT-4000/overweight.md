# ⚖️ overweight

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈweɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, verb | 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Above a normal or desirable weight; exceeding the expected weight. (n.) Excess weight. (v.) To burden with too much weight
**中文：** adj. 超重的；过重的 n. 超重 vt. 使负担过重

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obese（肥胖的）
- heavy（沉重的）
- corpulent（肥胖的）
- portly（肥胖的）
- excess weight（超重）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overweight luggage / overweight patients / slightly overweight / clinically overweight**

👉 Example: The airline charged an additional fee because the passenger's suitcase was significantly overweight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent studies indicate that **overweight** individuals face a significantly higher risk of developing cardiovascular disease and type-two diabetes.
   近期研究表明，超重人群患心血管疾病和二型糖尿病的风险明显更高。

2. The shipping company refused to transport the container because it was **overweight** and exceeded the maximum allowable load limit.
   航运公司拒绝运输该集装箱，因为它超重并超过了最大允许载重限制。

3. Public health campaigns aim to reduce the number of **overweight** children through improved nutrition education and physical activity programs.
   公共卫生运动旨在通过改善营养教育和体育活动计划来减少超重儿童的数量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-weight" as literally "over the weight" limit — the scale tips past the acceptable mark, signaling excess.
把"overweight"想成"over（超过）+weight（重量）"——体重秤超过了可接受的标志，意味着超重。
