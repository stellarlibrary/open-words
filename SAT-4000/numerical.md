# 🔢 numerical

🔊 /njuːˈmerɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or expressed as a number or numbers; involving or measured in numbers
**中文：** adj. 数值的；数字的；用数字表示的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- numeric（数字的）
- quantitative（定量的）
- arithmetical（算术的）
- statistical（统计的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **numerical order / numerical value / numerical data / numerical analysis / numerical superiority**

👉 Example: Please arrange the entries in numerical order from smallest to largest.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher organized all experimental results in strict **numerical** order to facilitate systematic comparison across multiple trials.
   研究人员将所有实验结果按严格的数字顺序排列，以便于多次试验之间的系统比较。

2. Despite their **numerical** superiority on the battlefield, the invading forces lacked the strategic coordination necessary for victory.
   尽管入侵部队在战场上拥有数量优势，但缺乏取胜所必需的战略协调。

3. Advanced **numerical** modeling techniques enabled scientists to simulate climate patterns with unprecedented accuracy and precision.
   先进的数值建模技术使科学家能够以前所未有的准确性和精度模拟气候模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "numerical" as "numer-ical" — directly from "number," anything that is numerical is simply related to numbers.
把"numerical"想成"numer-ical"——直接来自"number（数字）"，任何数值的东西都和数字有关。
