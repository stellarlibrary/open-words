# 🌨️ hail

🔊 /heɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Pellets of frozen rain that fall from cumulonimbus clouds. (v.) To fall as hail; to greet or acclaim enthusiastically
**中文：** n. 冰雹 v. 下雹；向……欢呼；欢迎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acclaim（欢呼）
- greet（迎接）
- salute（致敬）
- shower（如雨般降下）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hail a taxi / hail from / hail as a hero / a hail of bullets**

👉 Example: Critics hailed the young novelist as the most promising literary voice of her generation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audience rose to **hail** the distinguished conductor after his breathtaking interpretation of the symphony.
   观众起立向这位杰出的指挥家致敬，赞叹他对交响曲的精彩诠释。

2. A sudden **hail** of criticism descended upon the administration following the release of the controversial report.
   在这份有争议的报告发布后，一阵猛烈的批评如冰雹般降临到政府头上。

3. Scholars **hail** this archaeological discovery as one of the most significant findings of the twenty-first century.
   学者们称赞这一考古发现是二十一世纪最重要的发现之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hail" as calling out "hail!" to a king — you are greeting and cheering for someone important, just like hailstones loudly announce a storm.
把"hail"想象成对国王高呼"万岁！"——你在向重要人物欢呼致敬，就像冰雹大声宣告暴风雨的来临。
