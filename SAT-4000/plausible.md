# 🎭 plausible

🔊 /ˈplɔːzɪbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Seeming reasonable or probable; appearing truthful or believable, sometimes deceptively so
**中文：** adj. 貌似可信的，花言巧语的；貌似真实的，貌似有理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- credible（可信的）
- believable（可信赖的）
- convincing（令人信服的）
- feasible（可行的）
- specious（似是而非的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plausible explanation / plausible excuse / plausible argument / sound plausible**

👉 Example: The suspect offered a plausible explanation, but the detective remained unconvinced by his story.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher presented a **plausible** hypothesis that could explain the unexpected decline in migratory bird populations.
   研究人员提出了一个貌似合理的假说，可以解释候鸟数量意外下降的原因。

2. Although his alibi sounded **plausible** at first, further investigation revealed several critical inconsistencies in his account.
   虽然他的不在场证明起初听起来貌似可信，但进一步调查揭示了他陈述中的几个关键矛盾。

3. The committee demanded more than a **plausible** theory; they required concrete evidence before approving the project.
   委员会要求的不仅仅是一个貌似有理的理论；他们在批准项目前需要确凿的证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plausible" as "applause-ible" — an idea that sounds good enough to earn applause, even if it might not be entirely true.
把"plausible"想成"applause-ible"——一个听起来好到能赢得掌声的想法，即使它可能并不完全正确。
