# 📋 instance

🔊 /ˈɪnstəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An example or single occurrence of something; a particular case or situation. (v.) To cite as an example
**中文：** n. 实例；情况；建议 v. 举为例

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- example（例子）
- case（情况）
- occurrence（事件）
- illustration（例证）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **for instance / in this instance / in the first instance / at the instance of**

👉 Example: Several instances of academic dishonesty were reported to the disciplinary committee during the semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. This particular **instance** of regulatory failure demonstrates the urgent need for comprehensive reform of the oversight framework.
   这一监管失败的具体实例表明，迫切需要对监督框架进行全面改革。

2. The researcher cited numerous **instances** in which environmental factors significantly influenced the outcome of the genetic study.
   研究人员引用了许多环境因素显著影响基因研究结果的实例。

3. In every known **instance**, the experimental drug proved more effective than the conventional treatment prescribed to control groups.
   在每一个已知的案例中，实验药物都比对照组使用的常规治疗更有效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "instance" as "in-stance" — standing in a specific position, representing one particular case among many.
把"instance"想成"in-stance"——站在一个特定的位置上，代表众多案例中的某一个。
