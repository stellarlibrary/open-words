# 🚫 repudiate

🔊 /rɪˈpjuːdieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To refuse to accept or be associated with; to deny the truth or validity of; to reject with disapproval or condemnation
**中文：** 拒绝接受；否定；批判；与……断绝关系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reject（拒绝）
- renounce（放弃）
- disavow（否认）
- disown（与……断绝关系）
- deny（否定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **repudiate a claim / repudiate a debt / repudiate an allegation / repudiate a treaty**

👉 Example: The senator moved swiftly to repudiate the controversial remarks attributed to her campaign.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government chose to **repudiate** the treaty after concluding that its terms were fundamentally unfair to developing nations.
   政府在认定条约条款对发展中国家根本不公平后选择否定该条约。

2. The scholar publicly **repudiated** the earlier findings, presenting compelling new evidence that contradicted the original research conclusions.
   这位学者公开否定了早期的研究发现，并提出了有力的新证据来反驳最初的研究结论。

3. Several board members voted to **repudiate** the proposed merger, citing significant financial risks and regulatory concerns.
   几位董事会成员投票拒绝拟议的合并，理由是存在重大财务风险和监管方面的担忧。

把"repudiate"想成"re-pud-iate"——想象你说"呃，布丁！"然后把它推开，完全拒绝它。
