# 🫁 abdomen

🔊 /ˈæbdəmən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The part of the body between the thorax and the pelvis; the belly or stomach area
**中文：** n. 腹部；下腹；腹腔

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- belly（腹部）
- stomach（胃部）
- midsection（腹部中段）
- gut（肠道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lower abdomen / abdominal pain / abdomen examination / muscles of the abdomen**

👉 Example: The patient reported persistent pain in the lower abdomen that worsened after eating.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon made a small incision in the patient's **abdomen** to perform the minimally invasive laparoscopic procedure.
   外科医生在患者腹部做了一个小切口，以进行微创腹腔镜手术。

2. In insects, the **abdomen** is the posterior section of the body that contains the digestive and reproductive organs.
   在昆虫中，腹部是身体的后段，包含消化和生殖器官。

3. Emergency physicians examined her **abdomen** carefully to rule out appendicitis as the cause of the acute pain.
   急诊医生仔细检查了她的腹部，以排除阑尾炎作为急性疼痛的原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abdomen" as "ab-dome-n" — your abdomen is shaped like a dome covering your internal organs.
把"abdomen"想成"ab-dome-n"——你的腹部就像一个圆顶，覆盖着你的内脏器官。
