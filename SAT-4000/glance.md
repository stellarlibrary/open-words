# 👀 glance

🔊 /ɡlæns/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A brief or hurried look. (v.) To take a quick or brief look at something
**中文：** n. 一瞥；一滑；闪光 v. 扫视

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- peek（偷看）
- glimpse（瞥见）
- scan（扫视）
- skim（浏览）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **at first glance / glance at / glance over / a quick glance / a sideways glance**

👉 Example: At first glance, the proposal appeared reasonable, but closer analysis revealed serious flaws.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. At first **glance**, the financial report appeared favorable, but a thorough review exposed several critical accounting irregularities.
   乍一看，这份财务报告似乎不错，但仔细审查后发现了几处严重的会计违规。

2. The editor could **glance** at a manuscript and immediately identify structural weaknesses that less experienced readers would overlook.
   这位编辑只需扫视一下稿件，就能立即发现经验不足的读者会忽略的结构性缺陷。

3. She cast a nervous **glance** toward the examination board before delivering the final portion of her doctoral defense.
   她在进行博士答辩的最后部分之前，紧张地向考试委员会瞥了一眼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glance" as "gl-ance (dance)" — your eyes dance quickly across something when you glance at it.
把"glance"想成"gl-ance（dance，跳舞）"——当你glance的时候，你的眼睛像跳舞一样快速掠过某物。
