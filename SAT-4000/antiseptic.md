# 🧴 antiseptic

🔊 /ˌæntɪˈsɛptɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Preventing the growth of disease-causing microorganisms; relating to or denoting substances that prevent infection. (n.) A substance that prevents or inhibits the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms.

**中文：** (adj.) 防腐的；抗菌的。防止致病微生物生长的。(n.) 防腐剂；抗菌剂。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- disinfectant（消毒剂）
- germicidal（杀菌的）
- antibacterial（抗菌的）
- sterile（无菌的）
- sanitizing（消毒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antiseptic solution, antiseptic cream, antiseptic wipe, antiseptic properties, apply antiseptic

**Example:** The nurse applied an **antiseptic** solution to the wound to prevent any bacterial infection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon insisted that all instruments be treated with a powerful **antiseptic** before the operation commenced.
   外科医生坚持在手术开始前用强效防腐剂处理所有器械。

2. Modern **antiseptic** techniques have dramatically reduced the incidence of post-operative infections in hospitals worldwide.
   现代抗菌技术已大幅降低了全球医院术后感染的发生率。

3. The laboratory maintained an almost **antiseptic** environment to ensure the integrity of sensitive biological experiments.
   实验室保持了近乎无菌的环境，以确保敏感生物实验的完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "anti-" (against) + "septic" (infection/decay). Antiseptic works against sepsis — it fights germs and prevents infection.

联想："anti-"（反对）+ "septic"（感染/腐败）。antiseptic 就是对抗感染的——抵抗细菌、防止感染。
