# 🌾 cereal

🔊 /ˈsɪəriəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A grain used for food, such as wheat, oats, or corn; a breakfast food made from processed grain (adj.) Of or relating to grain or cereal plants
**中文：** n. 谷类；谷物；谷类食品 adj. 谷类的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grain（谷物）
- crop（庄稼）
- corn（谷类）
- wheat（小麦）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cereal crop / cereal production / breakfast cereal / cereal grain / cereal farming**

👉 Example: Global cereal production has increased steadily over the past decade due to advances in agricultural technology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural report indicates that **cereal** yields in the northern provinces exceeded expectations following favorable weather conditions.
   农业报告指出，在有利的天气条件下，北方各省的谷物产量超出了预期。

2. Nutritionists recommend choosing whole-grain **cereals** over highly processed varieties to maintain a balanced and healthful diet.
   营养学家建议选择全谷物谷类食品而非高度加工的品种，以保持均衡健康的饮食。

3. Ancient civilizations depended heavily on **cereal** cultivation, which enabled the establishment of permanent agricultural settlements.
   古代文明严重依赖谷物种植，这使得建立永久性农业定居点成为可能。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Ceres," the Roman goddess of agriculture and harvest — "cereal" comes from her name, representing the grains she protects.
想想"Ceres（谷神星/刻瑞斯）"，罗马农业和丰收女神——"cereal"源于她的名字，代表她守护的谷物。
