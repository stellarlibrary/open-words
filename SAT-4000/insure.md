# 🔒 insure

🔊 /ɪnˈʃʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To guarantee or make certain; to arrange for financial compensation in the event of damage, loss, illness, or death
**中文：** v. 确保，保证；给…保险

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guarantee（保证）
- ensure（确保）
- underwrite（承保）
- protect（保护）
- indemnify（赔偿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insure against / insure a property / insure for a sum / fully insured**

👉 Example: Homeowners should insure their property against natural disasters such as floods and earthquakes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company decided to **insure** all overseas shipments against potential damage during international transit operations.
   公司决定为所有海外货物投保，以防在国际运输过程中可能遭受的损坏。

2. Financial advisors strongly recommend that young families **insure** their primary income earner with adequate life coverage.
   理财顾问强烈建议年轻家庭为主要收入来源者购买充足的人寿保险。

3. The museum chose to **insure** the rare painting for twelve million dollars before transporting it to the exhibition.
   博物馆在将这幅珍贵画作运往展览之前，选择为其投保一千两百万美元。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insure" as "in-sure" — to make something "sure" by putting a safety net "in" place, so you are protected if something goes wrong.
把"insure"想成"in（放入）+ sure（确定）"——通过设置安全网使事情"确定"，这样出了问题你也有保障。
