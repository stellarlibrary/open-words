# 🔊 sound

🔊 /saʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective, adverb | 动词, 名词, 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To auscultate; to measure depth; to cause to produce a sound; to probe; to proclaim. (n.) A noise, voice, or tone produced; a strait; the range of hearing. (adj.) Healthy; reasonable; reliable; thorough. (adv.) Thoroughly, completely
**中文：** n. 声

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resonance（共鸣）
- probe（探测）
- reliable（可靠的）
- thorough（彻底的）
- wholesome（健全的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sound advice / sound judgment / sound asleep / sound out**

👉 Example: The doctor used a stethoscope to sound the patient's chest for any abnormalities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician decided to **sound** the patient's lungs carefully before making a preliminary diagnosis of the condition.
   医生在对病情做出初步诊断之前，决定仔细听诊患者的肺部。

2. Her argument was remarkably **sound**, grounded in empirical evidence and supported by decades of rigorous academic research.
   她的论点非常合理，以实证证据为基础，并得到了数十年严谨学术研究的支持。

3. The sailors attempted to **sound** the depth of the harbor before guiding the vessel safely through the narrow channel.
   水手们试图在引导船只安全通过狭窄航道之前测量港口的深度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sound" as something you hear (noun) that helps you "sound out" (verb) whether something is "sound" (adjective, meaning solid and reliable).
把"sound"想成你听到的声音（名词），帮助你"探测"（动词）某事是否"健全可靠"（形容词）。
