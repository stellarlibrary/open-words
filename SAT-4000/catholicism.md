# ⛪ Catholicism

🔊 /kəˈθɒlɪsɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The faith, practice, and system of governance of the Roman Catholic Church, one of the largest branches of Christianity
**中文：** n. 天主教；天主教义、信仰及组织

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- Roman Catholicism（罗马天主教）
- Catholic faith（天主教信仰）
- papism（教皇制度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convert to Catholicism / practice Catholicism / the teachings of Catholicism / influence of Catholicism**

👉 Example: The historian examined the profound influence of Catholicism on medieval European art and architecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Catholicism** played a central role in shaping the political and cultural institutions of Western Europe throughout the medieval period.
   天主教在整个中世纪时期对塑造西欧的政治和文化制度发挥了核心作用。

2. The scholar's dissertation explored how **Catholicism** influenced colonial governance and indigenous communities in Latin America over three centuries.
   这位学者的论文探讨了天主教如何在三个世纪中影响拉丁美洲的殖民治理和原住民社区。

3. After years of personal reflection, she decided to embrace **Catholicism** and began preparing for the rites of initiation.
   经过多年的个人反思，她决定皈依天主教，并开始为入教仪式做准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Catholicism" as "catholic" (meaning universal) plus "-ism" — a universal system of belief and worship centered on the Roman Catholic Church.
把"Catholicism"想成"catholic"（意为普遍的）加"-ism"——一个以罗马天主教会为中心的普遍信仰和崇拜体系。
