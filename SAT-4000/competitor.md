# 🥊 competitor

🔊 /kəmˈpetɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person or organization that competes against others, especially in business or sport; a rival
**中文：** n. 竞争者；对手

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rival（对手）
- opponent（对手）
- contender（竞争者）
- adversary（敌手）
- challenger（挑战者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **main competitor / fierce competitor / competitor in the market / outperform competitors / competitor analysis**

👉 Example: The company conducted a thorough competitor analysis before launching its new product line.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation identified its primary **competitor** and devised a comprehensive strategy to capture a larger share of the market.
   该公司确定了其主要竞争对手，并制定了一项全面战略以夺取更大的市场份额。

2. Every serious **competitor** in the international science olympiad spent months preparing under the guidance of experienced mentors.
   每一位参加国际科学奥林匹克竞赛的认真竞争者都在经验丰富的导师指导下花了数月准备。

3. Rather than viewing each **competitor** as a threat, the startup chose to study their innovations and adapt successful approaches.
   这家初创公司没有将每个竞争对手视为威胁，而是选择研究他们的创新并采纳成功的方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "competitor" as "compete + itor (one who)" — one who competes, a person striving alongside you for the same prize or goal.
把"competitor"想成"compete（竞争）+ itor（做…的人）"——竞争的人，与你一起争夺同一奖品或目标的人。
