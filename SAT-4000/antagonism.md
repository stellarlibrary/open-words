# ⚔️ antagonism

🔊 /ænˈtæɡənɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Active hostility or opposition between people, groups, or ideas; a deep-seated feeling of ill will or enmity
**中文：** 对抗；敌对；敌意；对立

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hostility（敌意）
- enmity（仇恨）
- animosity（敌意）
- opposition（对立）
- antipathy（反感）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mutual antagonism / racial antagonism / antagonism between / antagonism toward / deep antagonism**

👉 Example: The antagonism between the two political factions has made legislative compromise virtually impossible.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The longstanding **antagonism** between the neighboring countries has hindered diplomatic negotiations and regional economic cooperation for decades.
   两个邻国之间长期的敌对关系数十年来一直阻碍着外交谈判和区域经济合作。

2. Her open **antagonism** toward the proposed merger alienated several board members who had initially supported the restructuring plan.
   她对拟议合并的公开敌意疏远了几位最初支持重组计划的董事会成员。

3. The professor's lecture explored the philosophical **antagonism** between rationalism and empiricism throughout the history of Western thought.
   教授的讲座探讨了西方思想史上理性主义和经验主义之间的哲学对立。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ant-agon-ism" as "anti- (against) + agon (struggle)" — antagonism is the state of struggling against someone or something.
把"antagonism"想成"anti-（反对）+ agon（斗争）"——antagonism就是与某人或某事对抗斗争的状态。
