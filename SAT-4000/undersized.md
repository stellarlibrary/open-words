# 📏 undersized

🔊 /ˌʌndərˈsaɪzd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Smaller than the usual, expected, or standard size; not large enough
**中文：** adj. 较一般为小的，不够大的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diminutive（小的）
- stunted（发育不良的）
- compact（小型的）
- miniature（微型的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undersized portions / undersized equipment / undersized clothing / undersized fish**

👉 Example: The inspector noted that the undersized bolts posed a serious structural risk to the entire bridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Regulations require that all **undersized** fish be returned to the water immediately to preserve the breeding population.
   法规要求将所有体型过小的鱼立即放回水中，以保护繁殖种群。

2. The architect criticized the **undersized** windows, arguing that they would fail to provide adequate ventilation and light.
   建筑师批评了过小的窗户，认为它们无法提供足够的通风和采光。

3. Many **undersized** classrooms in rural districts struggle to accommodate the growing number of enrolled students each year.
   农村地区许多面积不足的教室难以容纳每年不断增长的入学学生人数。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "under-sized" as being under the expected size — something that falls short of the standard measurement.
把"undersized"想成在预期尺寸"之下"——某物未达到标准尺寸。
