# 😱 frantic

🔊 /ˈfræntɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wild or distraught with fear, anxiety, or other emotion; conducted in a hurried and chaotic way
**中文：** 狂乱的，疯狂的；焦急万分的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frenzied（疯狂的）
- desperate（绝望的）
- panicked（恐慌的）
- hysterical（歇斯底里的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frantic search / frantic pace / frantic efforts / frantic with worry**

👉 Example: The parents made a frantic search of the neighborhood after their child went missing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rescue team launched a **frantic** effort to locate survivors trapped beneath the rubble of the collapsed building.
   救援队疯狂地努力寻找被困在倒塌建筑废墟下的幸存者。

2. As the deadline approached, the researchers worked at a **frantic** pace to compile their data and finalize the report.
   随着截止日期的临近，研究人员以疯狂的速度整理数据并完成报告。

3. The stock market crash triggered **frantic** selling as investors desperately attempted to minimize their financial losses.
   股市崩盘引发了疯狂的抛售，投资者拼命试图将经济损失降到最低。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frantic" as "fran-tic" — like a frantic tick of the clock when time is running out and panic sets in.
把"frantic"想成"fran-tic"——像时间耗尽时时钟疯狂地滴答作响，恐慌随之而来。
