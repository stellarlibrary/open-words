# 🚧 hamper

🔊 /ˈhæmpər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To hinder or impede the movement or progress of. (n.) A large basket with a lid, used for carrying food
**中文：** v. 妨碍；阻碍；束缚 n. 食盒；大篮子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hinder（阻碍）
- impede（妨碍）
- obstruct（阻挡）
- encumber（拖累）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hamper progress / hamper efforts / hamper growth / hampered by**

👉 Example: Bureaucratic regulations continue to hamper the development of small businesses in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Severe weather conditions **hampered** the rescue operations, forcing emergency teams to delay their efforts by several hours.
   恶劣的天气条件妨碍了救援行动，迫使应急小组将工作推迟了数小时。

2. Insufficient funding has consistently **hampered** the university's ability to attract and retain world-class researchers and faculty.
   资金不足一直阻碍着该大学吸引和留住世界级研究人员和教师的能力。

3. Political instability continues to **hamper** economic reform efforts that are essential for the nation's long-term prosperity.
   政治不稳定继续阻碍着对国家长期繁荣至关重要的经济改革努力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hamper" as trying to run with a heavy hamper (basket) in your arms — the basket hampers your movement and slows you down.
把"hamper"想象成抱着一个沉重的篮子跑步——这个篮子妨碍了你的行动，让你慢下来。
