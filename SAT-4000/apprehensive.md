# 😟 apprehensive

🔊 /ˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Anxious or fearful that something bad or unpleasant will happen; also, quick to understand or perceive.

**中文：** 忧虑的；不安的；担心的；敏悟的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- anxious（焦虑的）
- uneasy（不安的）
- worried（担心的）
- fearful（恐惧的）
- nervous（紧张的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** apprehensive about, apprehensive of, grow apprehensive, feel apprehensive, increasingly apprehensive

**Example:** She was apprehensive about the outcome of the final examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students grew **apprehensive** as the date of the national examination drew closer each day.
   （随着全国考试日期日益临近，学生们变得越来越忧虑。）

2. Many investors remain **apprehensive** about committing capital during periods of significant market volatility.
   （许多投资者在市场剧烈波动期间仍对投入资金感到不安。）

3. Though **apprehensive** at first, she delivered her speech with remarkable confidence before the audience.
   （尽管起初感到忧虑，她还是在观众面前以非凡的自信发表了演讲。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apprehensive" shares the root "apprehend," which can mean to seize or arrest — imagine being seized by worry and fear.

"Apprehensive" 与 "apprehend"（逮捕、抓住）同根——想象自己被忧虑和恐惧"抓住"了。
