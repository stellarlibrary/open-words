# 😨 appalling

🔊 /əˈpɔːlɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adj. 可怕的；令人震惊的*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Causing shock or dismay; extremely bad or dreadful; inspiring horror or revulsion.

**中文：** 可怕的；令人震惊的；骇人听闻的；极其糟糕的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- dreadful 可怕的
- horrifying 令人恐惧的
- shocking 令人震惊的
- atrocious 残暴的
- ghastly 骇人的

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** appalling conditions, appalling behavior, appalling state, appalling lack of, appalling news

**Example:** "Appalling" is stronger than "bad" or "unpleasant." It often describes conditions, behavior, or situations that provoke moral outrage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed **appalling** conditions in the factory where workers labored without basic safety equipment.
   调查揭露了工厂里**骇人听闻的**工作条件，工人在没有基本安全设备的情况下劳动。

2. The committee expressed concern over the **appalling** decline in educational standards across the region.
   委员会对该地区教育水平**令人震惊的**下降表示关切。

3. Witnesses described the aftermath of the explosion as an **appalling** scene of widespread destruction and chaos.
   目击者将爆炸后的景象描述为一片**可怕的**大范围破坏与混乱。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Appalling" = "appall" + "-ing." If something appalls you (fills you with horror), then it is appalling.

词形联想：appalling = appall（使惊骇）+ -ing（令人……的）——令人惊骇的事物就是 appalling。
