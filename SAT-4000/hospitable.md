# 🏠 hospitable

🔊 /hɒˈspɪtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Friendly and welcoming to guests or strangers; (of an environment) pleasant and favorable for living or growth
**中文：** adj. 好客的；招待周到的；宜人的；易接受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- welcoming（热情的）
- cordial（诚恳的）
- gracious（亲切的）
- accommodating（乐于助人的）
- receptive（乐于接受的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hospitable environment / hospitable to visitors / hospitable climate / hospitable host**

👉 Example: The region's hospitable climate makes it ideal for cultivating a wide variety of crops.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The local community proved remarkably **hospitable**, offering shelter and provisions to the displaced families without hesitation.
   当地社区表现得非常好客，毫不犹豫地为流离失所的家庭提供住所和物资。

2. Scientists have long debated whether Mars could ever become **hospitable** enough to support permanent human colonization.
   科学家们长期以来一直在争论火星是否能变得足够宜居以支持人类永久定居。

3. The university fosters a **hospitable** atmosphere where scholars from diverse backgrounds collaborate freely on interdisciplinary research.
   该大学营造了友好的学术氛围，让来自不同背景的学者自由合作开展跨学科研究。

---

"hospitable"与"hospital"（医院）和"hospitality"（款待）同根——都与关怀和欢迎他人有关。
