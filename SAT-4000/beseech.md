# 🙏 beseech

🔊 /bɪˈsiːtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To implore urgently or fervently; to beg earnestly for something
**中文：** v. 恳求，哀求；乞求，急切地要求得到

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- implore（恳求）
- entreat（恳请）
- supplicate（哀求）
- plead（乞求）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beseech someone to do something / beseech someone for something**

👉 Example: The refugees beseeched the authorities to grant them asylum and safe passage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat **beseeched** the warring factions to consider a ceasefire that would spare innocent civilian lives.
   外交官恳求交战各方考虑停火，以挽救无辜平民的生命。

2. She **beseeched** the committee to reconsider their decision, presenting compelling evidence in her formal appeal.
   她恳求委员会重新考虑他们的决定，并在正式申诉中提出了令人信服的证据。

3. The villagers **beseeched** the governor for emergency relief after devastating floods destroyed their homes and crops.
   毁灭性的洪水摧毁了村民的房屋和庄稼后，村民们恳求省长提供紧急救济。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-SEECH" as "be-SPEECH" — making a speech to beg someone. When you beseech, you plead with heartfelt words.
想想"be-SEECH"像"be-SPEECH"——发表一番恳求的演说。当你beseech时，你用真挚的话语在乞求。
