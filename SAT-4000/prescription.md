# 💊 prescription

🔊 /prɪˈskrɪpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A doctor's written instruction for medicine; a rule, direction, or authoritative recommendation. (adj.) Available only with a doctor's written order
**中文：** n. 药方；处方；医生开的药；指示；规定；法规；惯例 adj. 凭处方方可购买的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- directive（指示）
- instruction（指令）
- formula（药方）
- recommendation（建议）
- decree（法令）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **write a prescription / fill a prescription / prescription medication / by prescription only**

👉 Example: The physician wrote a prescription for antibiotics to treat the patient's persistent bacterial infection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor's **prescription** for the chronic condition included both medication and a structured physical therapy regimen over six months.
   医生对这种慢性病的处方包括药物治疗和为期六个月的结构化物理治疗方案。

2. Many economists offered their **prescription** for reviving the struggling economy, though few agreed on the most effective approach.
   许多经济学家提出了振兴低迷经济的药方，但很少有人就最有效的方法达成一致。

3. The regulatory board established a strict **prescription** governing the conduct of clinical trials to ensure participant safety and data integrity.
   监管委员会制定了严格的规定，规范临床试验的开展，以确保参与者安全和数据完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-script-ion" as "something scripted before you take action" — whether it is medicine or rules, it is written guidance you follow.
把"prescription"想成"pre-script-ion"——在你行动之前写好的"剧本"，无论是药方还是规定，都是你要遵循的书面指导。
