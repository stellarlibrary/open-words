# 📚 secondary

🔊 /ˈsɛkənˌdɛri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Second in order; of medium quality; subordinate; of intermediate level. (n.) A deputy; a representative
**中文：** adj. 第二的；间接的，非原始的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- subordinate（次要的）
- auxiliary（辅助的）
- subsidiary（附属的）
- inferior（较低的）
- ancillary（从属的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **secondary importance / secondary source / secondary education / secondary role**

👉 Example: The researcher relied on secondary sources to supplement the primary data collected in the field.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee considered environmental concerns to be of **secondary** importance compared to the urgent economic crisis.
   委员会认为与紧迫的经济危机相比，环境问题属于次要地位。

2. Historians must carefully distinguish between primary documents and **secondary** accounts when evaluating the reliability of evidence.
   历史学家在评估证据的可靠性时必须仔细区分原始文献和间接记述。

3. She accepted a **secondary** role in the organization, content to support the director rather than seek prominence.
   她接受了组织中的辅助角色，乐于支持主任而非寻求显赫地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "second-ary" — it literally contains "second," meaning it comes after the first and is therefore not the primary or most important.
把"secondary"想成包含"second（第二）"——它排在第一之后，因此不是首要的或最重要的。
