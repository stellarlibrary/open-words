# 💪 hardihood

🔊 /ˈhɑːrdihʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Boldness and daring; resolute courage; audacity or impudence
**中文：** n. 刚毅；大胆；鲁莽；厚颜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- audacity（大胆）
- boldness（勇敢）
- fortitude（刚毅）
- temerity（鲁莽）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have the hardihood to / show great hardihood / with remarkable hardihood / the hardihood of**

👉 Example: Few had the hardihood to challenge the chairman's authority during the contentious board meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young explorer demonstrated extraordinary **hardihood** by venturing alone into uncharted territory despite repeated warnings of danger.
   这位年轻的探险家不顾反复的危险警告，独自冒险进入未知领域，展现了非凡的刚毅。

2. It required considerable **hardihood** for the junior analyst to openly contradict the findings of the department's most senior researcher.
   初级分析师公开反驳部门最资深研究员的发现需要相当大的胆量。

3. The settlers of the frontier era are often remembered for their remarkable **hardihood** in facing harsh winters and hostile conditions.
   边疆时代的定居者常因在严冬和恶劣环境中展现出的非凡刚毅而被人铭记。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hardihood" as "hardy + hood" — the hood (state) of being hardy, meaning tough, bold, and courageous enough to face anything.
把"hardihood"想成"hardy + hood"——处于hardy（强壮、勇敢）的状态，意味着足够坚强、大胆和勇敢去面对一切。
