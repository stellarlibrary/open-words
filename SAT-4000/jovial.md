# 😊 jovial

🔊 /ˈdʒoʊviəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Cheerful and friendly in disposition; markedly good-humored and full of hearty warmth
**中文：** adj. 天性快活的；愉快的；和蔼可亲的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cheerful（快活的）
- genial（和蔼的）
- convivial（欢乐的）
- affable（和蔼可亲的）
- buoyant（乐观的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jovial manner / jovial personality / jovial host / jovial disposition**

👉 Example: The jovial host greeted every guest with a warm handshake and a genuine smile.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **jovial** personality helped ease diplomatic tensions and foster productive dialogue between the delegations.
   大使愉快的个性有助于缓解外交紧张局势，并促进代表团之间的建设性对话。

2. Despite facing considerable financial difficulties, the shopkeeper remained **jovial** and greeted each customer with sincere warmth.
   尽管面临相当大的经济困难，店主依然快活，以真诚的热情迎接每位顾客。

3. The **jovial** atmosphere at the annual faculty dinner encouraged even the most reserved professors to share personal anecdotes.
   年度教师晚宴上愉快的氛围鼓励了即使是最内向的教授也分享个人轶事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jovial" as coming from "Jove" (Jupiter), the king of Roman gods — associated with merriment and good fortune.
把"jovial"想成来自"Jove"（朱庇特），罗马众神之王——与欢乐和好运联系在一起。
