# 🎭 avocation

🔊 /ˌævəˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A hobby, secondary occupation, or activity pursued in addition to one's main profession, usually for enjoyment rather than financial gain.

**中文：** 副业；业余爱好。指在主要职业之外从事的活动或兼业，通常是为了乐趣而非经济收益。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- hobby （爱好）
- pastime （消遣）
- sideline （副业）
- diversion （消遣活动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Do not confuse "avocation" with "vocation." A "vocation" is your main calling or profession, while an "avocation" is a side interest or hobby. The prefix "a-" suggests something apart from your primary vocation.

不要将"avocation"与"vocation"混淆。"vocation"是你的主要职业或使命，而"avocation"是副业或业余爱好。前缀"a-"暗示它是与主要职业不同的事物。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Although she practiced law by day, her true **avocation** was painting landscapes inspired by the countryside.
   *虽然她白天从事法律工作，但她真正的**业余爱好**是绘制以乡村为灵感的风景画。*

2. He regarded woodworking as merely an **avocation**, yet his handcrafted furniture rivaled that of professional artisans.
   *他把木工仅仅视为一种**副业**，但他手工制作的家具可以与专业工匠的作品媲美。*

3. Many accomplished scientists have pursued music as an **avocation** that complements their analytical work remarkably well.
   *许多有成就的科学家将音乐作为一种**业余爱好**，它与他们的分析工作形成了极好的互补。*

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"A-vocation" = "away from your vocation." Your vocation is your main job; your avocation is what you do AWAY from your main job, just for fun.

"a-vocation" = "远离你的职业"。你的vocation是主业；你的avocation是你在主业之外做的事情，纯粹为了乐趣。
