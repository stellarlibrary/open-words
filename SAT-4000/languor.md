# 💤 languor

🔊 /ˈlæŋɡər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of physical or mental weariness; lack of energy or vitality; a dreamy, relaxed quality
**中文：** n. 身心疲惫；无精打采；柔情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lethargy（倦怠）
- torpor（麻木）
- listlessness（无精打采）
- weariness（疲倦）
- lassitude（无力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a sense of languor / summer languor / overcome by languor / tropical languor**

👉 Example: A pleasant languor settled over the guests as they reclined on the terrace after the lavish dinner.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tropical heat induced a pervasive **languor** that made even the simplest physical tasks feel extraordinarily burdensome.
   热带的高温引发了一种弥漫的倦怠感，使得即使最简单的体力活动都显得格外沉重。

2. After weeks of relentless studying, a deep **languor** overtook the students as the final examination period finally concluded.
   经过数周不间断的学习，当期末考试终于结束时，一种深深的疲倦笼罩了学生们。

3. The poet captured the **languor** of a slow autumn afternoon with vivid imagery of falling leaves and fading sunlight.
   诗人用落叶和渐逝阳光的生动意象捕捉了一个慵懒秋日午后的倦怠之感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "languor" sounding like "linger" — when you linger too long doing nothing, you fall into a state of languor and weariness.
"languor"听起来像"linger（逗留）"——当你无所事事地逗留太久，就会陷入一种倦怠和疲惫的状态。
