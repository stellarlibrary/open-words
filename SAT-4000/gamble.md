# 🎲 gamble

🔊 /ˈɡæmbl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To play games of chance for money; to take a risky action in the hope of a desired result. (n.) An act of gambling; a risky undertaking
**中文：** v. 赌博；孤注一掷；冒险 n. 赌博；冒险；碰运气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wager（打赌）
- bet（赌注）
- risk（冒险）
- speculate（投机）
- venture（冒险尝试）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gamble on / gamble away / take a gamble / a calculated gamble**

👉 Example: Investing in that startup was a calculated gamble that ultimately paid off.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation decided to **gamble** on an untested technology, hoping it would give them a decisive competitive advantage.
   该公司决定在一项未经验证的技术上冒险，希望能获得决定性的竞争优势。

2. Many economists argued that the fiscal stimulus was a dangerous **gamble** that could lead to unsustainable national debt.
   许多经济学家认为，财政刺激是一场危险的赌博，可能导致不可持续的国家债务。

3. The general chose to **gamble** everything on a single bold offensive rather than endure a prolonged and costly siege.
   将军选择把一切赌在一次大胆的进攻上，而不是忍受漫长而代价高昂的围困。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gamble" as "game + able" — you are able to play a game of chance, but the outcome is never guaranteed.
把"gamble"想成"game + able"——你能参加一场碰运气的游戏，但结果永远无法保证。
