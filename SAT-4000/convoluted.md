# 🌀 convoluted

🔊 /ˈkɒnvəluːtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely complex and difficult to follow; intricately twisted or coiled in form
**中文：** adj. 复杂的；费解的；旋绕的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intricate（错综复杂的）
- complicated（复杂的）
- tortuous（曲折的）
- labyrinthine（迷宫般的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convoluted argument / convoluted plot / convoluted explanation / convoluted process / convoluted logic**

👉 Example: The professor's convoluted explanation left most of the students more confused than before the lecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bureaucratic process for obtaining a research permit was so **convoluted** that many applicants abandoned their efforts entirely.
   获得研究许可证的官僚流程如此复杂，以至于许多申请人完全放弃了努力。

2. Critics argued that the novelist's **convoluted** narrative structure made the central themes nearly impossible for average readers to grasp.
   评论家认为这位小说家费解的叙事结构使得普通读者几乎无法理解其核心主题。

3. The defense attorney presented a **convoluted** legal argument that even experienced judges found difficult to follow during the proceedings.
   辩护律师提出了一个复杂的法律论点，即使是经验丰富的法官在诉讼过程中也难以理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-voluted" as "completely revolving" — like a path that keeps revolving and twisting, making it impossible to follow directly.
把"convoluted"想成"completely revolving"——就像一条不断旋转和扭曲的路径，让人无法直接跟随。
