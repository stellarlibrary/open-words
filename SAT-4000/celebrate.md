# 🎉 celebrate

🔊 /ˈselɪbreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To observe a notable occasion with festivities; to perform a religious ceremony; to praise or honor publicly
**中文：** v. 庆祝，祝贺；举行（宗教仪式）；赞美，颂扬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commemorate（纪念）
- honor（尊敬）
- extol（赞美）
- observe（庆祝）
- acclaim（喝彩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **celebrate an anniversary / celebrate a victory / celebrate diversity / celebrate achievements**

👉 Example: The university held a grand ceremony to celebrate the achievements of its distinguished alumni.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Communities around the world **celebrate** cultural traditions that have been passed down through countless generations of ancestors.
   世界各地的社区庆祝代代相传的文化传统。

2. The institution organized a formal gala to **celebrate** the groundbreaking research contributions of its most accomplished faculty members.
   该机构组织了一场正式晚宴，以表彰其最杰出教职人员的开创性研究贡献。

3. Literary critics often **celebrate** works that challenge conventional narratives and offer fresh perspectives on the human condition.
   文学评论家经常赞美那些挑战传统叙事并对人类处境提供全新视角的作品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "celebrate" as "celeb-rate" — you rate someone as a "celeb" (celebrity) by celebrating their accomplishments.
把"celebrate"想成"celeb-rate"——你通过庆祝某人的成就来将他们评为"celeb（名人）"。
