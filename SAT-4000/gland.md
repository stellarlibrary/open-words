# 🔬 gland

🔊 /ɡlænd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An organ in the body that produces and releases chemical substances such as hormones or enzymes for specific functions
**中文：** n. 腺，腺体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- organ（器官）
- secretory organ（分泌器官）
- endocrine gland（内分泌腺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sweat gland / thyroid gland / pituitary gland / adrenal gland / swollen gland**

👉 Example: The thyroid gland plays a crucial role in regulating the body's metabolic rate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pituitary **gland** is often called the master gland because it controls the activity of most other endocrine organs.
   垂体腺常被称为主腺，因为它控制着大多数其他内分泌器官的活动。

2. Researchers identified a previously unknown **gland** in the human throat that may have significant implications for cancer treatment.
   研究人员在人类喉部发现了一个此前未知的腺体，这可能对癌症治疗具有重要意义。

3. Chronic stress can cause the adrenal **gland** to overproduce cortisol, leading to a range of adverse health consequences.
   长期压力会导致肾上腺过度分泌皮质醇，从而引发一系列不良的健康后果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gland" as "g-land" — a little land (territory) in your body where special chemicals are produced and exported.
把"gland"想成"g-land（土地）"——身体里的一小片领地，专门生产和输出特殊的化学物质。
