# ⏩ acceleration

🔊 /əkˌsɛləˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of increasing speed or rate; in physics, the rate of change of velocity per unit of time.
**中文：** 加速；促进；加速度。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quickening（加速）
- hastening（加快）
- speedup（提速）
- advancement（推进）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acceleration of growth**, **rate of acceleration**, **acceleration due to gravity**

👉 Example: The acceleration of urbanization has transformed many rural communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **acceleration** of technological innovation has profoundly reshaped the global economy.
   技术创新的加速深刻地重塑了全球经济。

2. In physics, **acceleration** due to gravity is approximately 9.8 meters per second squared.
   在物理学中，重力加速度约为每秒平方9.8米。

3. The sudden **acceleration** of the vehicle caused the passengers to lurch forward in their seats.
   车辆的突然加速使乘客在座位上猛地向前倾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Acceleration" = "accelerate" + "-tion" (noun form). Just add the noun ending to the verb meaning "speed up."
"Acceleration" = "accelerate"（加速）+ "-tion"（名词后缀），把动词变成名词就是加速的过程。
