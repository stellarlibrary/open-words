# ❓ unknown

🔊 /ʌnˈnoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not known or familiar; not identified or established. (n.) An unknown person, thing, or quantity
**中文：** adj. 未知的；不出名的 n. 未知数；未知的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unfamiliar（不熟悉的）
- unidentified（未确认的）
- obscure（模糊的）
- anonymous（匿名的）
- mysterious（神秘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unknown territory / unknown quantity / unknown origin / unknown variable / the great unknown**

👉 Example: The explorers ventured into unknown territory, uncertain of what challenges they would encounter ahead.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cause of the mysterious illness remained **unknown** despite months of thorough investigation by leading medical researchers and epidemiologists.
   尽管顶尖医学研究人员和流行病学家进行了数月的深入调查，这种神秘疾病的病因仍然未知。

2. Mathematicians often use the variable x to represent an **unknown** quantity that must be determined through systematic calculation and analysis.
   数学家们常用变量x来表示必须通过系统计算和分析来确定的未知数。

3. The painting was attributed to an **unknown** artist of the Renaissance period, yet its extraordinary craftsmanship rivaled established masters.
   这幅画被认为出自文艺复兴时期一位不知名的画家之手，但其精湛技艺可与知名大师媲美。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-known" as simply "not known" — the prefix "un-" negates "known," giving you something that has not yet been discovered or identified.
把"unknown"想成"un（不、未）+ known（知道的）"——不被知道的，即未知的。
