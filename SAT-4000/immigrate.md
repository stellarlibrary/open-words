# 🌍 immigrate

🔊 /ˈɪmɪɡreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To come to live permanently in a foreign country; to move into a new country or region for settlement.
**中文：** v. 移居入境；移入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- migrate（迁移）
- relocate（迁居）
- resettle（重新定居）
- move abroad（移居海外）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **immigrate to a country / immigrate from a region / immigrate legally / immigrate permanently**

👉 Example: Her grandparents decided to immigrate to Canada in search of better economic opportunities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Thousands of skilled professionals **immigrate** to developed nations each year in pursuit of advanced career opportunities.
   每年有数千名专业人才移居发达国家，以寻求更好的职业发展机会。

2. The policy reform made it significantly easier for qualified applicants to **immigrate** to the country through official channels.
   政策改革使符合条件的申请人通过官方渠道移民该国变得更加容易。

3. His family chose to **immigrate** to Australia during the postwar period, seeking stability and renewed hope for the future.
   他的家人在战后时期选择移民澳大利亚，以寻求稳定和对未来的新希望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-migrate" as "in + migrate" — to migrate into a new country, as opposed to emigrate (out).
把"immigrate"想成"im（进入）+ migrate（迁移）"——迁入一个新国家，与emigrate（迁出）相对。
