# ⚖️ suspension

🔊 /səˈspɛnʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of suspending; a state of being suspended in a fluid; a temporary removal from duty or position.
**中文：** n. 吊；悬浮；悬浮液；暂停，中止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- postponement（推迟）
- interruption（中断）
- moratorium（暂停）
- deferment（延期）
- cessation（停止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suspension of disbelief / suspension bridge / suspension from school / in suspension**

👉 Example: The principal announced a three-day suspension for students involved in the altercation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The athlete received a two-year **suspension** from competition after testing positive for prohibited performance-enhancing substances.
   这名运动员在禁用兴奋剂检测呈阳性后被禁赛两年。

2. The **suspension** of trade negotiations between the two countries raised concerns among international economic analysts.
   两国之间贸易谈判的中止引起了国际经济分析师的关注。

3. In chemistry, a **suspension** consists of fine particles dispersed throughout a liquid that will eventually settle over time.
   在化学中，悬浮液由分散在液体中的细小颗粒组成，最终会随时间沉降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suspension" as the noun form of "suspend" — something hanging in mid-air or a pause that keeps things on hold.
把"suspension"想成"suspend"的名词形式——某物悬在半空中，或者让事情搁置的暂停。
