# 🦴 anatomy

🔊 /əˈnætəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of science concerned with the bodily structure of organisms; a detailed analysis or examination of something
**中文：** 解剖；解剖学；剖析

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dissection（解剖）
- structure（结构）
- analysis（分析）
- examination（剖析）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **human anatomy / anatomy of a crisis / study anatomy / anatomy class**

👉 Example: The professor's lecture provided a thorough anatomy of the financial crisis that shook global markets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medical students spend their first year mastering the **anatomy** of the human body through rigorous coursework and dissection.
   医学生在第一年通过严格的课程和解剖实践来掌握人体解剖学。

2. The journalist published a compelling **anatomy** of the scandal that exposed systemic corruption within the corporation.
   记者发表了一篇对丑闻的深度剖析，揭露了公司内部的系统性腐败。

3. Understanding the **anatomy** of a persuasive argument is essential for anyone studying rhetoric or formal debate.
   理解有说服力论证的结构对于任何学习修辞学或正式辩论的人来说都是至关重要的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ana-tomy" as "ana" (up) + "tomy" (cutting) — cutting something open to study its internal structure.
把"anatomy"拆成"ana"（向上）+"tomy"（切割）——切开某物来研究其内部结构。
