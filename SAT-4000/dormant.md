# 🌙 dormant

🔊 /ˈdɔːrmənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In a state of rest or inactivity; temporarily inactive but capable of being reactivated
**中文：** adj. 休眠的；冬眠的；静止的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inactive（不活跃的）
- latent（潜在的）
- quiescent（静止的）
- hibernating（冬眠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dormant volcano / dormant account / lie dormant / dormant seed**

👉 Example: The virus can lie dormant in the body for years before symptoms appear.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Mount Vesuvius remained **dormant** for decades before its catastrophic eruption devastated the surrounding communities.
   维苏威火山在其灾难性喷发摧毁周围社区之前已经休眠了数十年。

2. The researchers discovered that certain genes remain **dormant** until specific environmental triggers activate their expression.
   研究人员发现，某些基因在特定环境因素激活其表达之前一直处于休眠状态。

3. The company's **dormant** patent portfolio was revived when new technology made the inventions commercially viable.
   当新技术使这些发明具有商业可行性时，该公司休眠的专利组合被重新激活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dormant" as "dorm + ant" — an ant sleeping in a dorm, completely inactive and resting.
把"dormant"想成"dorm + ant"——一只在宿舍里睡觉的蚂蚁，完全不活动地休息着。
