# 📗 comprehensible

🔊 /ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be understood; intelligible; clear enough to be grasped by the mind
**中文：** adj. 可理解的；易于了解的；能领悟的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- understandable（可理解的）
- intelligible（易懂的）
- lucid（清晰的）
- accessible（易接近的）
- clear（清楚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **easily comprehensible / readily comprehensible / comprehensible to someone / barely comprehensible**

👉 Example: The professor's lecture was comprehensible even to students with no prior background in the subject.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A well-written scientific paper should present its findings in a manner that is **comprehensible** to readers beyond the immediate field of study.
   一篇写得好的科学论文应以超出直接研究领域的读者也能理解的方式呈现其发现。

2. The translator worked diligently to render the ancient philosophical text into modern English that was **comprehensible** to contemporary audiences.
   翻译者努力将这部古代哲学文本译成当代读者能够理解的现代英语。

3. Without adequate visual aids and clear explanations, the complex statistical analysis would not have been **comprehensible** to the jury members.
   如果没有充分的视觉辅助和清晰的解释，陪审团成员将无法理解这项复杂的统计分析。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comprehensible" as "comprehend + ible" — if something is "able to be comprehended," it is comprehensible.
把"comprehensible"想成"comprehend + ible"——如果某事物"能够被理解"，那它就是comprehensible（可理解的）。
