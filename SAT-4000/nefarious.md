# 😈 nefarious

🔊 /nɪˈfɛriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wicked, villainous, or criminal in nature; extremely evil or immoral
**中文：** adj. 邪恶的；穷凶极恶的；不法的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wicked（邪恶的）
- villainous（恶毒的）
- heinous（极其恶劣的）
- sinister（阴险的）
- iniquitous（不公正的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nefarious scheme / nefarious activities / nefarious purposes / nefarious plot**

👉 Example: The investigation revealed the corporation's nefarious scheme to defraud thousands of investors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective uncovered a **nefarious** plot to embezzle millions of dollars from the municipal pension fund.
   侦探揭露了一个从市政养老基金中挪用数百万美元的邪恶阴谋。

2. Authorities arrested several individuals involved in **nefarious** activities including fraud, bribery, and money laundering.
   当局逮捕了几名参与欺诈、贿赂和洗钱等不法活动的个人。

3. The novel's antagonist devised a **nefarious** plan to seize control of the government through systematic corruption.
   小说中的反派设计了一个邪恶的计划，通过系统性腐败来夺取政府控制权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ne-FARI-ous" as containing "fari" like "nefarious fairy" — imagine an evil fairy doing wicked things instead of granting wishes.
把"nefarious"想成包含一个"邪恶的仙女（nefarious fairy）"——想象一个邪恶的仙女做坏事而不是实现愿望。
