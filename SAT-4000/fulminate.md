# ⚡ fulminate

🔊 /ˈfʌlmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To explode violently; to issue a thunderous verbal attack or denunciation. (n.) A salt of fulminic acid, used as a detonator
**中文：** v. 爆炸；电闪；怒喝；以严词谴责 n. 雷酸盐

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- denounce（谴责）
- declaim（抨击）
- rail（痛斥）
- detonate（引爆）
- inveigh（猛烈抨击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fulminate against / fulminate over / fulminate about injustice / fulminate in protest**

👉 Example: The senator fulminated against the proposed legislation, calling it a betrayal of public trust.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial **fulminated** against government corruption, demanding immediate accountability from every elected official involved.
   社论严厉谴责政府腐败，要求每一位涉事民选官员立即承担责任。

2. Environmental activists continued to **fulminate** over the corporation's decision to dump industrial waste into the river.
   环保活动人士继续猛烈抨击该公司将工业废物倾倒入河流的决定。

3. Mercury **fulminate** was historically employed as a primary explosive in detonators due to its extreme sensitivity to impact.
   雷酸汞因其对撞击的极高敏感性，历史上曾被用作雷管中的起爆药。

把"fulminate"想成"full（满的）+ dynamite（炸药）"——充满爆炸性的能量，无论是物理引爆还是言语怒火。
