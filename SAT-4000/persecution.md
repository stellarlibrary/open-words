# 😣 persecution

🔊 /ˌpɜːrsɪˈkjuːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Hostility and ill-treatment, especially on the basis of ethnicity, religion, or political beliefs; persistent annoyance or harassment
**中文：** n. 迫害；烦扰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oppression（压迫）
- harassment（骚扰）
- victimization（迫害）
- tyranny（暴政）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **religious persecution / political persecution / persecution of minorities / face persecution**

👉 Example: Many refugees flee their homeland to escape persecution based on their religious beliefs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian documented the systematic **persecution** of intellectuals during the totalitarian regime's decades-long rule.
   这位历史学家记录了极权政权数十年统治期间对知识分子的系统性迫害。

2. Entire communities sought asylum abroad after enduring relentless **persecution** for their ethnic and cultural identity.
   整个社区在因民族和文化身份遭受无情迫害后纷纷到国外寻求庇护。

3. International law guarantees protection to individuals who can demonstrate a well-founded fear of **persecution** in their country.
   国际法保障能够证明在本国有充分理由恐惧遭受迫害的个人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "persecut-ion" as someone being "pursued and cut" — chased and harmed repeatedly, which captures the essence of persecution.
把"persecution"想成被人"追赶并伤害"——反复被追逐和伤害，这正是迫害的本质。
