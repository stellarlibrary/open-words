# 🔗 coherent

🔊 /kəʊˈhɪərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Logically connected and consistent; clear and easy to understand; forming a unified whole
**中文：** adj. 连贯的；一致的；明了的；清晰的；黏在一起的；条理清楚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- logical（合逻辑的）
- consistent（一致的）
- lucid（清晰的）
- articulate（表达清楚的）
- orderly（有条理的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coherent argument / coherent strategy / coherent narrative / coherent explanation**

👉 Example: The professor insisted that every thesis must present a coherent argument supported by credible evidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the complexity of the subject matter, the lecturer delivered a remarkably **coherent** explanation that all students could follow.
   尽管主题复杂，讲师还是做出了非常清晰连贯的解释，所有学生都能理解。

2. The administration failed to articulate a **coherent** policy on immigration, leading to widespread confusion among government agencies.
   政府未能阐明一项连贯的移民政策，导致各政府机构之间普遍存在困惑。

3. A well-written essay requires **coherent** paragraphs that transition smoothly from one idea to the next throughout the text.
   一篇好的文章需要段落连贯，从一个观点到下一个观点过渡顺畅。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "coherent" as "co-here-ent" — ideas that "co-here" (stick together here) form a logical, unified whole that makes sense.
把"coherent"想成"co（共同）-here（在这里）-ent"——各个想法"粘在一起"，形成一个有逻辑的、统一的整体。
