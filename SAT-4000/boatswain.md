# ⚓ boatswain

🔊 /ˈboʊsən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A petty officer on a ship responsible for the maintenance of the hull and rigging, and for supervising the crew's work on deck.
**中文：** n. 水手长（负责船体维护和甲板工作的船员长官）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bosun（水手长）
- petty officer（下级军官）
- deck officer（甲板军官）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the boatswain's whistle / boatswain's mate / boatswain's chair / boatswain's call**

👉 Example: The boatswain blew his whistle to summon the crew to their stations on deck.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **boatswain** directed the crew to secure all rigging before the approaching storm could inflict damage upon the vessel.
   水手长指挥船员在即将来临的风暴对船只造成损害之前固定好所有索具。

2. An experienced **boatswain** is indispensable on any large sailing vessel, ensuring that daily maintenance proceeds according to schedule.
   一位经验丰富的水手长在任何大型帆船上都是不可或缺的，确保日常维护按计划进行。

3. The captain relied heavily on the **boatswain** to maintain discipline and operational efficiency among the deck crew.
   船长十分依赖水手长来维持甲板船员的纪律和工作效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "boat-swain" as the "swain (servant) of the boat" — the person who takes care of the boat and its crew, like a devoted caretaker of the ship.
把"boatswain"想成"boat（船）的swain（仆人）"——照顾船只和船员的人，就像船的忠实管家。
