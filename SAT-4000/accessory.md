# 👜 accessory

🔊 /əkˈsɛsəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A supplementary item or fitting; an accomplice in a crime. (adj.) Additional; aiding in a secondary capacity.
**中文：** (n.) 配件，附件；从犯，帮凶。(adj.) 副的，附属的；同谋的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supplement（补充物）
- attachment（附件）
- accomplice（同谋）
- adjunct（附属物）
- addition（附加物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accessory to + crime/murder** (legal); **fashion/phone accessory** (object)

👉 Example: She was charged as an accessory to the robbery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant was convicted as an **accessory** to fraud for knowingly concealing critical evidence.
   被告因故意隐瞒关键证据而被判定为欺诈案的从犯。

2. A well-chosen **accessory** can elevate a simple outfit into something remarkably sophisticated.
   一件精心挑选的配饰可以将简单的着装提升为非常精致的造型。

3. The report identified several **accessory** factors that contributed to the structural failure of the bridge.
   报告指出了导致桥梁结构失效的几个辅助因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
An "accessory" gives extra "access" to style or to a crime — it always plays a supporting role.
accessory 可以联想到"辅助进入（access）"——它总是起辅助作用，无论是配饰还是从犯。
