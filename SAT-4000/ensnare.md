# 🪤 ensnare

🔊 /ɪnˈsneər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To catch in or as in a trap; to lure into a compromising or dangerous situation
**中文：** v. 诱捕；诱入陷阱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- entrap（诱捕）
- snare（设陷阱）
- entangle（使纠缠）
- lure（引诱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ensnare in a trap / ensnare the unsuspecting / ensnared by debt / ensnared in a web of lies**

👉 Example: The elaborate scheme was designed to ensnare investors who lacked financial expertise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The fraudulent investment scheme managed to **ensnare** hundreds of retirees who had entrusted their life savings to the firm.
   这个欺诈性投资计划成功诱骗了数百名将毕生积蓄托付给该公司的退休人员。

2. Investigators set up an elaborate undercover operation specifically designed to **ensnare** the notorious international smuggling ring.
   调查人员专门设计了一项精密的卧底行动来诱捕这个臭名昭著的国际走私团伙。

3. The persuasive rhetoric of extremist propaganda can **ensnare** vulnerable individuals who are searching for a sense of belonging.
   极端主义宣传的蛊惑性言辞能够诱惑那些正在寻找归属感的脆弱个体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-snare" as putting someone "into a snare" — a snare is a trap, so ensnare means to catch someone as if in a trap.
把"ensnare"想成把某人"放入snare（陷阱）"中——snare是捕兽夹，所以ensnare就是像用陷阱一样抓住某人。
