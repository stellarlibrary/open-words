# 💡 semiconductor

🔊 /ˌsɛmikənˈdʌktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A solid substance that has conductivity between that of an insulator and a conductor, used in electronic devices
**中文：** n. 半导体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chip（芯片）
- microchip（微芯片）
- transistor（晶体管）
- integrated circuit（集成电路）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **semiconductor industry / semiconductor chip / semiconductor manufacturing / semiconductor device**

👉 Example: The global shortage of semiconductors disrupted automobile production across multiple continents for over a year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern **semiconductor** technology has enabled the production of processors containing billions of transistors on a single chip.
   现代半导体技术使得在单个芯片上集成数十亿晶体管的处理器成为可能。

2. The government announced a substantial investment in domestic **semiconductor** manufacturing to reduce dependence on foreign supply chains.
   政府宣布对国内半导体制造业进行大量投资，以减少对外国供应链的依赖。

3. Researchers at the laboratory developed a novel **semiconductor** material that operates efficiently at significantly higher temperatures than silicon.
   实验室的研究人员开发了一种新型半导体材料，可在比硅高得多的温度下高效运行。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "semi-conductor" as "semi" (half) + "conductor" — it half-conducts electricity, sitting between a full conductor and an insulator.
把"semiconductor"拆成"semi"（半）+"conductor"（导体）——它只能部分导电，介于导体和绝缘体之间。
