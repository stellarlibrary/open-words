# 💔 alienate

🔊 /ˈeɪ.li.ə.neɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause someone to feel isolated or estranged; to turn away in feeling or affection; to transfer ownership of property or rights.
**中文：** 使疏远，离间；让与，转让（财产或权利）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- estrange（使疏远）
- isolate（孤立）
- disaffect（使不满）
- distance（使疏远）
- transfer（转让）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alienate someone from, alienate supporters, alienate friends, alienate affection**
👉 Example: His dismissive remarks alienated many of his former supporters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician's controversial policies gradually **alienated** a significant portion of his electoral base.
   这位政治家有争议的政策逐渐疏远了他很大一部分选民基础。

2. Her persistent refusal to compromise **alienated** her colleagues and left her professionally isolated.
   她一直拒绝妥协，这使她与同事疏远，在职业上变得孤立。

3. The corporation's decision to relocate overseas **alienated** the local community that had supported it for decades.
   该公司决定将业务迁往海外，这疏远了数十年来一直支持它的当地社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alienate" makes someone feel like an "alien" — a stranger or outsider in their own group.
"alienate"让某人感觉像个"alien（外人）"——在自己的群体中变成陌生人或局外人。
