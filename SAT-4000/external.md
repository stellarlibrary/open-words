# 🔲 external

🔊 /ɪkˈstɜːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or situated on the outside; coming from outside a particular entity; for outward application. (n.) The outward features or aspect of something
**中文：** adj. 外部的；表面的；外用的 n. 外部；外观

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outer（外部的）
- outside（外面的）
- extrinsic（外在的）
- superficial（表面的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **external factors / external pressure / external affairs / for external use only / external environment**

👉 Example: The committee examined how external economic factors contributed to the decline in domestic manufacturing output.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization hired an **external** auditor to conduct an independent review of its financial records and accounting practices.
   该组织聘请了一位外部审计师对其财务记录和会计操作进行独立审查。

2. The medication is labeled strictly for **external** use and should never be ingested or applied to open wounds.
   该药物标明仅供外用，绝不能口服或涂于开放性伤口上。

3. Scholars argue that **external** influences from neighboring civilizations significantly shaped the cultural development of the ancient empire.
   学者们认为，来自邻近文明的外部影响极大地塑造了这一古代帝国的文化发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extern-al" — "extern" means outside (like an extern who works outside their home institution), so external means relating to the outside.
把"external"想成"extern(外部的)+al"——"extern"意为外面的，所以external就是外部的、外面的。
