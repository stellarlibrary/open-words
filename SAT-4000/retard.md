# 🐌 retard

🔊 /rɪˈtɑːrd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To slow down the development or progress of something; to delay, hinder, or impede
**中文：** v. 延迟；阻止；妨碍；使减速

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- delay（延迟）
- impede（妨碍）
- hinder（阻碍）
- decelerate（减速）
- obstruct（阻塞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retard growth / retard progress / retard the spread of / retard development**

👉 Example: Excessive regulation can retard economic growth and discourage foreign investment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged drought conditions significantly **retard** the growth of crops and threaten the livelihoods of agricultural communities.
   长期干旱条件严重阻碍了农作物的生长，并威胁到农业社区的生计。

2. The introduction of bureaucratic obstacles will inevitably **retard** the implementation of much-needed educational reforms nationwide.
   官僚障碍的引入将不可避免地延迟全国急需的教育改革的实施。

3. Chemical additives are commonly used to **retard** the deterioration of perishable materials during long-distance transportation.
   化学添加剂通常用于延缓易腐材料在长途运输过程中的变质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-TARD" — "tard" comes from Latin "tardus" meaning "slow" — to make something slow again or keep it slow.
把"retard"想成"re-TARD"——"tard"源自拉丁语"tardus"，意为"慢的"——使某物减速或保持缓慢。
