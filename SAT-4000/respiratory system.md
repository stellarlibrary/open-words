# 🫁 respiratory system

🔊 /ˈrɛspərəˌtɔːri ˈsɪstəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun phrase | 名词短语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The system of organs and structures responsible for gas exchange in the body, including the lungs, trachea, bronchi, and diaphragm
**中文：** n. 呼吸系统

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pulmonary system（肺部系统）
- respiratory tract（呼吸道）
- airways（气道）
- breathing apparatus（呼吸器官）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **upper/lower respiratory system / respiratory system infection / damage to the respiratory system / respiratory system function**

👉 Example: Chronic exposure to pollutants can severely compromise the respiratory system over time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The human **respiratory system** consists of the nasal passages, trachea, bronchi, and lungs working in coordinated harmony.
   人体呼吸系统由鼻腔、气管、支气管和肺协调配合组成。

2. Prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants can cause irreversible damage to the **respiratory system** of factory workers.
   长期接触工业污染物会对工厂工人的呼吸系统造成不可逆转的损害。

3. The biology lecture examined how the **respiratory system** facilitates the exchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide at the alveolar level.
   生物课讲解了呼吸系统如何在肺泡层面促进氧气和二氧化碳的交换。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "respiratory system" as "the system that re-inspires" — it keeps bringing fresh air (inspiration) into your body to keep you alive.
把"respiratory system"想成"重新吸入的系统"——它不断将新鲜空气（吸气）带入你的身体以维持生命。
