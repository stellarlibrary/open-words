# 🤢 queasy

🔊 /ˈkwiːzi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Feeling nauseous or experiencing an unsettled stomach; causing or prone to feelings of unease or discomfort
**中文：** adj. 令人作呕的；恶心的；不安的；忧虑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nauseous（恶心的）
- squeamish（易恶心的）
- uneasy（不安的）
- sick（不舒服的）
- bilious（胆汁质的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel queasy / queasy stomach / queasy about / a queasy feeling**

👉 Example: The turbulent flight made several passengers feel queasy throughout the entire journey.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The graphic descriptions of the surgical procedure made even the most experienced medical students feel **queasy**.
   对手术过程的详细描述甚至让最有经验的医学生都感到恶心。

2. She felt **queasy** about accepting the promotion, knowing it had been offered under ethically questionable circumstances.
   她对接受这次晋升感到不安，因为她知道这是在道德上有问题的情况下提出的。

3. The volatile motion of the small fishing vessel left most of the inexperienced crew members feeling profoundly **queasy**.
   小渔船的剧烈颠簸使大多数缺乏经验的船员感到非常恶心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "queasy" as "quea-sea" — being on a rough sea makes your stomach feel uneasy and sick.
把"queasy"想成"quea-sea（海）"——在波涛汹涌的大海上会让你的胃感到不适和恶心。
