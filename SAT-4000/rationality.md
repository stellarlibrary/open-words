# 💡 rationality

🔊 /ˌræʃəˈnælɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being based on or agreeable to reason; the ability to think and make decisions logically
**中文：** n. 合理性；合理的行动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reasonableness（合理性）
- logic（逻辑性）
- soundness（合理）
- lucidity（清晰）
- coherence（连贯性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **question someone's rationality / economic rationality / bounded rationality / principle of rationality**

👉 Example: The professor questioned the rationality of the proposed economic model during the seminar.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics began to question the **rationality** of the government's decision to invest billions in an unproven technology.
   批评者开始质疑政府投资数十亿于一项未经验证的技术的合理性。

2. Behavioral economists argue that human **rationality** is bounded by cognitive limitations, incomplete information, and time constraints.
   行为经济学家认为，人类的理性受到认知局限、信息不完整和时间限制的约束。

3. The **rationality** of the scientific method lies in its reliance on evidence, reproducibility, and systematic logical analysis.
   科学方法的合理性在于其对证据、可重复性和系统逻辑分析的依赖。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rational-ity" — adding "-ity" to "rational" gives you the noun form, the state of being rational, like a quality you measure.
把"rationality"想成"rational（理性的）+ ity（性质）"——理性的性质就是合理性。
