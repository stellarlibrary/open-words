# 🌍 wherever

🔊 /wɛrˈɛvər/<br>
🏷️ *adverb, conjunction | 副词, 连词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) In or to whatever place; no matter where. (conj.) In every case or situation in which; regardless of where
**中文：** adv. 无论什么地方；究竟在哪里 conj. 无论在哪里；无论什么情况下

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- anyplace（任何地方）
- everywhere（到处）
- no matter where（无论哪里）
- regardless of location（不论地点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wherever possible / wherever you go / wherever necessary / wherever it may be**

👉 Example: The organization pledged to provide humanitarian aid wherever it was most urgently needed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new policy requires that sustainable practices be implemented **wherever** feasible across all departments of the institution.
   新政策要求在该机构所有部门中尽可能实施可持续做法。

2. **Wherever** the research team traveled, they encountered communities eager to participate in the public health initiative.
   无论研究团队走到哪里，他们都遇到了渴望参与公共卫生计划的社区。

3. The professor encouraged her students to seek knowledge **wherever** it could be found, whether in libraries, laboratories, or fieldwork.
   这位教授鼓励她的学生到任何能找到知识的地方去探寻，无论是图书馆、实验室还是实地考察。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wherever" as "where + ever" — it does not matter where, it applies in every possible place or situation.
把"wherever"想成"where（哪里）+ ever（任何）"——不管在哪里，它适用于每一个可能的地点或情况。
