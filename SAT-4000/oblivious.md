# 😶 oblivious

🔊 /əˈblɪviəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not aware of or not concerned about what is happening around one; forgetful; lacking conscious awareness
**中文：** adj. 遗忘的；健忘的；不注意的；不知道的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unaware（不知道的）
- ignorant（不了解的）
- heedless（不注意的）
- unmindful（不留心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oblivious to / oblivious of** — always followed by a preposition indicating what one is unaware of

👉 Example: She was completely oblivious to the chaos unfolding around her.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students remained **oblivious** to the approaching deadline, continuing their leisurely discussions without any sense of urgency.
   学生们对即将到来的截止日期浑然不觉，继续悠闲地讨论着，毫无紧迫感。

2. He walked through the crowded marketplace entirely **oblivious** of the pickpocket who had been following him for several blocks.
   他穿过拥挤的市场，完全没有注意到已经跟踪他好几个街区的扒手。

3. The corporation appeared **oblivious** to the environmental damage its operations were causing in the surrounding communities.
   该公司似乎对其运营给周边社区造成的环境损害毫无察觉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oblivious" as related to "oblivion" — if you are in oblivion, you are oblivious, completely unaware of everything.
把"oblivious"和"oblivion"联系起来——如果你身处遗忘之中，你就是oblivious的，对一切浑然不知。
