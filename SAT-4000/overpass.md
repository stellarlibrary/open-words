# 🌉 overpass

🔊 /ˈoʊvərpæs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A bridge or roadway that crosses over another road or railway. (v.) To surpass; to exceed; to disregard
**中文：** n. 过街天桥；立交桥 v. 超越；胜过；忽略

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bridge（桥梁）
- flyover（立交桥）
- viaduct（高架桥）
- surpass（超越）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **highway overpass / pedestrian overpass / overpass the highway / cross the overpass**

👉 Example: The new pedestrian overpass allows commuters to safely cross the busy eight-lane highway.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The highway **overpass** collapsed during the earthquake, disrupting transportation routes and stranding thousands of commuters for several hours.
   高速公路立交桥在地震中坍塌，交通路线中断，数千名通勤者被困数小时。

2. Engineers designed a modern **overpass** to alleviate the chronic traffic congestion that had plagued the intersection for decades.
   工程师们设计了一座现代化的立交桥，以缓解困扰该路口数十年的长期交通拥堵问题。

3. The city council approved funding for a new pedestrian **overpass** connecting the residential neighborhood to the commercial district safely.
   市议会批准了一座新的人行天桥的资金，将住宅区与商业区安全地连接起来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-pass" — a structure that lets you "pass over" a road or obstacle, going above rather than through it.
把"overpass"想成让你"从上方通过"马路或障碍物的建筑——从上面走而不是穿过去。
