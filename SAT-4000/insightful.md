# 🔍 insightful

🔊 /ˈɪnˌsaɪtfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing an accurate and deep understanding; perceptive and discerning
**中文：** adj. 有深刻见解的；富有洞察力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perceptive（有洞察力的）
- discerning（有辨别力的）
- astute（精明的）
- penetrating（深刻的）
- shrewd（敏锐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insightful analysis / insightful comment / insightful observation / insightful perspective**

👉 Example: The critic offered an insightful analysis of the novel's complex narrative structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist's **insightful** reporting revealed the hidden connections between corporate lobbying and environmental policy decisions.
   这位记者富有洞察力的报道揭示了企业游说与环境政策决定之间的隐秘联系。

2. Students praised the professor for consistently delivering **insightful** lectures that challenged their fundamental assumptions about history.
   学生们称赞这位教授始终如一地发表富有深刻见解的讲座，挑战了他们对历史的基本假设。

3. Her **insightful** commentary on the economic crisis helped millions of viewers understand the underlying causes of the recession.
   她对经济危机富有洞察力的评论帮助数百万观众理解了衰退的根本原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insight + ful" — full of insight, meaning someone who is brimming with deep understanding and keen perception.
把"insightful"想成"insight + ful"——充满洞察力，形容一个人满是深刻理解和敏锐感知。
