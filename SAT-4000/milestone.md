# 🏆 milestone

🔊 /ˈmaɪlstoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A stone set up beside a road to mark distance; a significant event or turning point in history or in a person's life.
**中文：** n. 里程碑，里程标；重要事件，转折点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- landmark（里程碑）
- turning point（转折点）
- benchmark（基准）
- breakthrough（突破）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach a milestone / mark a milestone / major milestone / important milestone in**

👉 Example: The discovery of penicillin was a major milestone in the history of modern medicine.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The successful launch of the satellite marked a significant **milestone** in the nation's ambitious space exploration program.
   卫星的成功发射标志着该国雄心勃勃的太空探索计划中的一个重要里程碑。

2. Graduating from university represented a personal **milestone** that she had worked toward for over four dedicated years.
   大学毕业是她为之奋斗了四年多的个人里程碑。

3. The peace treaty was widely regarded as a historic **milestone** in resolving decades of conflict between the two nations.
   该和平条约被广泛视为解决两国数十年冲突的历史性里程碑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mile-stone" as a literal stone marking each mile on a road — each stone represents progress, just as milestones in life mark important achievements.
把"milestone"想成路上每一英里处的标记石——每块石头代表进步，正如人生里程碑标记着重要成就。
