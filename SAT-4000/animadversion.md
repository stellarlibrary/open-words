# 🗣️ animadversion

🔊 /ˌænɪmædˈvɜːrʒən/<br>
🏷️ *n. criticism; censure; remark | 名词：批评；非难；评语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A critical or censorious remark; strong disapproval or harsh criticism directed at someone or something.
**中文：** 批评性或指责性的评语；对某人或某事的强烈不满或严厉批评。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- criticism（批评）
- censure（谴责）
- reproach（责备）
- rebuke（斥责）
- stricture（苛评）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **animadversion on/upon (a subject), harsh animadversion, public animadversion**
👉 Example: His animadversion upon the new policy reflected deep dissatisfaction among the committee members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial contained a sharp **animadversion** upon the government's failure to address the growing economic inequality.
   这篇社论包含了对政府未能解决日益加剧的经济不平等问题的尖锐批评。

2. Her **animadversion** on the director's artistic choices provoked a heated debate among the film critics present.
   她对导演艺术选择的批评在在场的影评人中引发了激烈的辩论。

3. The professor's **animadversion** regarding the student's careless methodology served as a stern warning to the entire class.
   教授对学生粗心方法论的批评对全班起到了严厉的警示作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "anima" (mind/spirit) + "version" (turning) — turning your mind against something, i.e., criticizing it.
想象"anima"（心灵/精神）+ "version"（转向）——将你的心灵转向反对某事，即批评它。
