# ⚖️ inequity

🔊 /ɪnˈekwɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Lack of fairness or justice; an unjust or unfair act or condition
**中文：** n. 不公平；不公正

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- injustice（不公正）
- unfairness（不公平）
- bias（偏见）
- partiality（偏袒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social inequity / racial inequity / address inequity / systemic inequity / glaring inequity**

👉 Example: The community organized protests against the inequity embedded in the criminal justice system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary exposed the profound **inequity** in healthcare access between wealthy and impoverished neighborhoods.
   这部纪录片揭露了富裕社区和贫困社区之间在医疗服务获取方面的严重**不公平**。

2. Civil rights advocates have long fought against the structural **inequity** that perpetuates discrimination in hiring practices.
   民权倡导者长期以来一直在与招聘实践中延续歧视的结构性**不公正**作斗争。

3. The judge acknowledged the fundamental **inequity** of applying different sentencing standards based on socioeconomic status.
   法官承认，根据社会经济地位适用不同量刑标准是根本性的**不公平**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-equity" as "no equity" — without equity (fairness), you have inequity, a situation that is fundamentally unjust.
把"inequity"想成"in-equity"，即"没有公正"——没有equity（公正），就有了inequity（不公平）。
