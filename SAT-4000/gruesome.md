# 👻 gruesome

🔊 /ˈɡruːsəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing repulsion or horror; extremely unpleasant and shocking, especially in connection with death or injury
**中文：** adj. 可怕的；可憎的；令人厌恶的；阴森的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grisly（恐怖的）
- ghastly（骇人的）
- horrific（可怕的）
- macabre（恐怖的）
- revolting（令人作呕的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gruesome discovery / gruesome details / gruesome scene / gruesome murder / gruesome reminder**

👉 Example: The news report included gruesome details about the accident that left viewers deeply unsettled.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum exhibit presented a **gruesome** yet historically accurate depiction of medieval surgical practices and medical instruments.
   博物馆展览呈现了中世纪外科手术和医疗器械令人毛骨悚然但符合历史事实的描绘。

2. Investigators described the scene as the most **gruesome** they had encountered in over two decades of professional law enforcement.
   调查人员称这是他们从事执法工作二十多年来遇到的最可怕的现场。

3. The novel's **gruesome** imagery served as a deliberate artistic choice to convey the true horrors of wartime atrocities.
   小说中令人恐惧的意象是一种刻意的艺术选择，用以传达战时暴行的真实恐怖。
