# 💥 havoc

🔊 /ˈhævək/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Widespread destruction; great confusion or disorder; devastating damage
**中文：** n. 大破坏；浩劫；混乱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- devastation（毁灭）
- destruction（破坏）
- chaos（混乱）
- ravage（蹂躏）
- turmoil（动荡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wreak havoc / cause havoc / play havoc with / create havoc**

👉 Example: The unexpected policy change wreaked havoc on the financial markets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hurricane wreaked **havoc** across the coastal region, displacing thousands of residents and destroying critical infrastructure.
   飓风在沿海地区造成了巨大破坏，导致数千居民流离失所，关键基础设施被摧毁。

2. Cyberattacks have the potential to cause widespread **havoc** in essential systems such as banking and healthcare networks.
   网络攻击有可能在银行和医疗网络等重要系统中造成大范围的破坏。

3. The invasive species played **havoc** with the local ecosystem, threatening the survival of several native plant populations.
   入侵物种对当地生态系统造成了严重破坏，威胁到了几种本地植物种群的生存。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "havoc" as "have-wreck" — to have a wreck, meaning total destruction and chaos everywhere.
把"havoc"想成"have-wreck（遇到残骸）"——到处都是破坏和混乱。
