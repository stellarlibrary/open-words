# 🚫 inhibit

🔊 /ɪnˈhɪbɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To restrain, hinder, or prevent an action or process; to prohibit or forbid
**中文：** v. 抑制；禁止；阻止，妨碍

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restrain（抑制）
- suppress（压制）
- hinder（阻碍）
- impede（妨碍）
- curb（遏制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inhibit growth / inhibit development / inhibit the ability to / inhibit expression**

👉 Example: Excessive regulation can inhibit economic growth in developing regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new medication was designed to **inhibit** the reproduction of harmful bacteria within the patient's bloodstream.
   这种新药旨在抑制患者血液中有害细菌的繁殖。

2. Fear of public criticism may **inhibit** researchers from publishing unconventional findings in academic journals.
   对公众批评的恐惧可能会阻止研究人员在学术期刊上发表非传统的研究成果。

3. Cold temperatures significantly **inhibit** the metabolic processes of most organisms living in polar environments.
   低温显著抑制了大多数生活在极地环境中的生物体的代谢过程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-hibit" as "in habit" — when something gets in the way of your habit, it inhibits you from doing it.
把"inhibit"想成"in habit"——当某事阻碍了你的习惯，它就抑制了你去做这件事。
