# 🤫 introvert

🔊 /ˈɪntrəvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who tends to turn inward mentally, preferring solitude and quiet environments. (v.) To turn inward; to direct one's thoughts toward oneself
**中文：** n. 性格内向的人 v. 使内向；向内弯曲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- loner（独来独往的人）
- homebody（宅家的人）
- recluse（隐士）
- wallflower（不爱社交的人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a natural introvert / introverted personality / introvert at heart / self-described introvert**

👉 Example: As a self-described introvert, she preferred small gatherings to large social events.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The famous author was a committed **introvert** who found creative inspiration in long periods of solitary contemplation.
   这位著名作家是一个坚定的内向者，在长时间的独处沉思中找到了创作灵感。

2. Many successful leaders are **introvert**s who recharge their energy through quiet reflection rather than social interaction.
   许多成功的领导者是内向的人，他们通过安静的反思而非社交互动来恢复精力。

3. Research suggests that an **introvert** may excel in roles requiring deep focus, analytical thinking, and independent work.
   研究表明，内向的人可能在需要深度专注、分析思维和独立工作的岗位上表现出色。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "introvert" as "intro-vert" — "intro" means inward and "vert" means to turn, so an introvert turns inward toward their own thoughts.
把"introvert"想成"intro（向内）-vert（转）"——向内转向自己的思想世界，即内向的人。
