# 📐 hypotenuse

🔊 /haɪˈpɑːtənuːs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle
**中文：** n. 直角三角形的斜边

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- longest side（最长边）
- diagonal（对角线）
- oblique side（斜边）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the hypotenuse of a right triangle / calculate the hypotenuse / the length of the hypotenuse / Pythagorean theorem and hypotenuse**

👉 Example: According to the Pythagorean theorem, the square of the hypotenuse equals the sum of the squares of the other two sides.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematics instructor asked students to calculate the length of the **hypotenuse** using the Pythagorean theorem during the examination.
   数学老师要求学生在考试中使用勾股定理计算斜边的长度。

2. In trigonometry, the sine of an angle is defined as the ratio of the opposite side to the **hypotenuse** of a right triangle.
   在三角学中，一个角的正弦被定义为直角三角形中对边与斜边的比值。

3. Ancient Egyptian builders reportedly used ropes to verify that the **hypotenuse** of triangular structures conformed to precise mathematical proportions.
   据报道，古埃及建筑工人使用绳索来验证三角形结构的斜边是否符合精确的数学比例。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hypo-" (under) + "tenuse" (stretch) — the hypotenuse is the side that "stretches under" the right angle, connecting the two legs of the triangle.
把"hypotenuse"想成"hypo-"（在下面）+"tenuse"（伸展）——斜边是"在直角下方伸展"的那条边，连接三角形的两条直角边。
