# 🦹 villainous

🔊 /ˈvɪlənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to, constituting, or guilty of wicked or criminal behavior; extremely wicked or evil
**中文：** adj. 恶毒的，缺德的；罪恶的；恶棍的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wicked（邪恶的）
- nefarious（罪恶的）
- diabolical（恶魔般的）
- heinous（极恶的）
- iniquitous（不公正的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **villainous scheme / villainous character / villainous plot / villainous deeds / villainous conduct**

👉 Example: The prosecutor described the defendant's villainous scheme to defraud elderly investors of their savings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novel's most compelling character was the **villainous** mastermind whose elaborate plans consistently outwitted law enforcement authorities.
   这部小说最引人注目的角色是那个精心策划始终能智胜执法部门的恶毒主谋。

2. Historians have debated whether the emperor's **villainous** reputation was deserved or merely the result of hostile propaganda.
   历史学家一直在争论这位皇帝的恶毒名声是否属实，还是仅仅是敌对宣传的结果。

3. The committee uncovered a **villainous** conspiracy to manipulate financial markets for the personal enrichment of corporate executives.
   委员会揭露了一个操纵金融市场以使企业高管个人牟利的罪恶阴谋。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "villainous" as "villain" + "-ous" — full of villain qualities, just like the classic evil characters in stories and films.
把"villainous"想成"villain"（恶棍）+"-ous"——充满恶棍特质的，就像故事和电影中经典的反派角色。
