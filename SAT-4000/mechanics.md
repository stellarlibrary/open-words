# 🔧 mechanics

🔊 /məˈkænɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of physics dealing with motion and forces; the practical aspects of how something works or is done; the technical workings of a system
**中文：** n. 力学；机械学；技术；结构

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dynamics（动力学）
- workings（运作方式）
- mechanism（机制）
- engineering（工程学）
- operation（操作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the mechanics of / quantum mechanics / fluid mechanics / game mechanics**

👉 Example: Understanding the mechanics of cellular division is fundamental to the study of molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students of physics must first master classical **mechanics** before advancing to more complex theories such as relativity.
   物理学学生必须先掌握经典力学，然后才能进阶学习相对论等更复杂的理论。

2. The author spent considerable time explaining the **mechanics** of the electoral process to help readers understand democratic governance.
   作者花了大量时间解释选举过程的运作机制，以帮助读者理解民主治理。

3. Researchers in quantum **mechanics** have proposed new models that challenge previously accepted interpretations of subatomic behavior.
   量子力学研究人员提出了新模型，挑战了此前被接受的亚原子行为解释。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mechanics" as what a "mechanic" studies — the inner workings of machines, or more broadly, the science of how things move and function.
把"mechanics"想成"mechanic"研究的东西——机器的内部运作，或更广泛地说，是关于事物如何运动和运作的科学。
