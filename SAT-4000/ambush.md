# 🪖 ambush

🔊 /ˈæmbʊʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A surprise attack made from a concealed position. (v.) To make a surprise attack on someone from a hidden location
**中文：** n. 埋伏，伏击 v. 埋伏，伏击

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trap（陷阱）
- waylay（伏击）
- surprise attack（突然袭击）
- assail（攻击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **set up an ambush / lie in ambush / ambush attack / ambush the enemy**

👉 Example: The patrol was ordered to lie in ambush along the narrow mountain pass until dawn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The resistance fighters planned to **ambush** the supply convoy as it passed through the densely forested mountain road.
   抵抗战士计划在补给车队经过茂密的山间林道时对其进行伏击。

2. The unsuspecting travelers walked directly into an **ambush** that had been carefully prepared by the bandits days in advance.
   毫无防备的旅行者直接走进了土匪数日前精心准备的埋伏之中。

3. Investigators discovered that the journalist had been **ambushed** outside her residence by individuals seeking to suppress the story.
   调查人员发现，这名记者在住所外遭到了试图压制报道的人员的伏击。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "am-BUSH" — imagine enemies hiding in a BUSH, waiting to jump out and attack when you walk past.
把"ambush"想成"am-BUSH"——想象敌人藏在灌木丛（bush）里，等你走过时跳出来攻击。
