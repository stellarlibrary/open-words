# 💡 luminescent

🔊 /ˌluːmɪˈnɛsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Emitting light not caused by heat; producing cold light through chemical, biological, or physical processes
**中文：** 发冷光的；冷光的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- glowing（发光的）
- phosphorescent（磷光的）
- fluorescent（荧光的）
- radiant（辐射的）
- shimmering（微光闪烁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **luminescent glow / luminescent organisms / luminescent material / luminescent display**

👉 Example: The luminescent plankton turned the ocean surface into a breathtaking display of blue light.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The deep-sea expedition discovered several **luminescent** organisms that produced an eerie blue glow in total darkness.
   深海探险发现了几种在完全黑暗中发出诡异蓝色冷光的发光生物。

2. Scientists developed a new **luminescent** coating that could make road signs visible without requiring any external power source.
   科学家研发了一种新的冷光涂层，可以使路标在不需要任何外部电源的情况下可见。

3. The **luminescent** properties of certain minerals have proven invaluable in medical imaging and diagnostic technology applications.
   某些矿物的冷光特性在医学成像和诊断技术应用中已被证明具有极高价值。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lumin-escent" as "light-becoming" — something that becomes its own source of cool light, like a firefly.
把"luminescent"想成"lumin（光）+ escent（变成）"——某物变成自身冷光源，就像萤火虫一样。
