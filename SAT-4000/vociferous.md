# 📣 vociferous

🔊 /voʊˈsɪfərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Making or characterized by a loud outcry; noisy and insistent
**中文：** adj. 大声叫的；喊叫的，喧嚷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clamorous（吵闹的）
- boisterous（喧闹的）
- strident（刺耳的）
- vehement（激烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vociferous opposition / vociferous critics / vociferous demands / vociferous support**

👉 Example: The proposal met with vociferous opposition from environmental advocacy groups.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new policy provoked **vociferous** debate among legislators who held deeply opposing views on the matter.
   这项新政策在持截然相反观点的立法者之间引发了激烈的辩论。

2. Despite **vociferous** objections from the faculty, the university administration proceeded with its restructuring plan.
   尽管教职员工强烈反对，大学管理层仍继续推进其重组计划。

3. The **vociferous** crowd outside the courthouse made it difficult for attorneys to conduct proceedings inside.
   法院外喧嚷的人群使律师们难以在内部进行诉讼程序。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voci-" (voice) + "-ferous" (bearing) — bearing a loud voice, someone who carries their voice forcefully.
把"vociferous"想成"voci-"（声音）+"-ferous"（携带的）——携带着响亮的声音，大声喧嚷的人。
