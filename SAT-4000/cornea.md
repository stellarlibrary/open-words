# 👁️ cornea

🔊 /ˈkɔːrniə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The transparent front part of the eye that covers the iris, pupil, and anterior chamber, responsible for refracting light
**中文：** n. 角膜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eye membrane（眼膜）
- ocular surface（眼表面）
- transparent coat（透明膜层）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cornea transplant / cornea damage / cornea surgery / artificial cornea**

👉 Example: The ophthalmologist examined the patient's cornea for signs of infection or abrasion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon performed a delicate **cornea** transplant that successfully restored the patient's vision after years of progressive deterioration.
   外科医生进行了一次精细的角膜移植手术，成功恢复了患者多年来逐渐恶化的视力。

2. Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation can cause significant damage to the **cornea** and impair long-term visual acuity.
   长时间暴露在紫外线辐射下会对角膜造成严重损伤，并影响长期视力。

3. Researchers developed a bioengineered **cornea** using collagen proteins that could potentially eliminate the shortage of donor tissue worldwide.
   研究人员利用胶原蛋白开发了一种生物工程角膜，有望消除全球供体组织短缺的问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cornea" as the "corner" window of your eye — it is the clear front surface through which light first enters.
把"cornea"想成眼睛的"corner"（角落）窗户——它是光线首先进入的透明前表面。
