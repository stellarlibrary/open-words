# 🎤 elocution

🔊 /ˌɛləˈkjuːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The skill of clear and expressive speech, especially of distinct pronunciation and articulation; the art of public speaking
**中文：** n. 朗诵法；演说法；演说术；雄辩术

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oratory（演说术）
- diction（措辞）
- articulation（发音清晰）
- rhetoric（修辞学）
- declamation（朗诵）

---

Use with: **elocution lessons / elocution skills / elocution teacher / study elocution / practice elocution**

👉 Example: The aspiring politician enrolled in elocution classes to refine her public speaking abilities.

   维多利亚时代的教育者非常重视演说术，将其视为完善的古典教育中不可或缺的组成部分。
