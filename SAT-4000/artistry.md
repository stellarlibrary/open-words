# 🖼️ artistry

🔊 /ˈɑːrtɪstri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Creative skill or ability; the quality of being artistic or the exceptional craftsmanship in a creative endeavor.

**中文：** 艺术性；艺术技巧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- craftsmanship 工艺
- mastery 精通
- virtuosity 精湛技艺
- finesse 精巧

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Artistry" emphasizes the skill and technique behind creative work, not just the result. It is used to praise the high level of ability demonstrated in any field, from painting to cooking to sports.

"artistry" 强调创作背后的技巧和技术，而不仅仅是结果。它用于赞扬在任何领域中展现的高水平能力，从绘画到烹饪再到体育。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sheer **artistry** of the violinist's performance left the entire concert hall in breathless silence.
   小提琴家演奏中的纯粹艺术性让整个音乐厅陷入屏息的寂静。

2. Critics praised the **artistry** displayed in every scene of the award-winning film directed by her.
   评论家赞扬了她执导的这部获奖影片中每一个场景所展现的艺术技巧。

3. The chef's remarkable **artistry** transformed simple ingredients into a visually stunning and delicious masterpiece.
   这位厨师非凡的艺术技巧将简单的食材变成了视觉惊艳且美味的杰作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Artist" + "-ry" (noun suffix indicating quality) — the quality or skill that makes someone a true artist.

"Artist"（艺术家）+ "-ry"（表示性质的名词后缀）——使某人成为真正艺术家的技艺或品质。
