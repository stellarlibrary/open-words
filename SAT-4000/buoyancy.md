# 🎈 buoyancy

🔊 /ˈbɔɪənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ability or tendency to float in water or air; a cheerful and optimistic disposition; resilience
**中文：** n. 浮力；轻快；轻松的心情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lightness（轻盈）
- cheerfulness（快活）
- resilience（恢复力）
- optimism（乐观）
- vivacity（活泼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **buoyancy of spirit / natural buoyancy / market buoyancy / buoyancy force / emotional buoyancy**

👉 Example: The buoyancy of the life jacket kept the swimmer safely afloat in the rough waters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist demonstrated the principle of **buoyancy** by showing how objects of varying densities behave when submerged in water.
   物理学家通过展示不同密度的物体浸入水中时的表现来演示浮力原理。

2. Her natural **buoyancy** of spirit helped her recover quickly from the setbacks that would have discouraged most of her peers.
   她天生的乐观精神帮助她迅速从挫折中恢复，而这些挫折会让大多数同龄人灰心丧气。

3. Economists attributed the **buoyancy** of the housing market to sustained low interest rates and strong consumer confidence levels.
   经济学家将房地产市场的活跃归因于持续的低利率和强劲的消费者信心水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "buoy" + "-ancy" — a buoy floats on water, so buoyancy is the quality of floating or staying uplifted.
把"buoyancy"想成"buoy（浮标）+ -ancy（性质）"——浮标漂浮在水面上，所以buoyancy就是漂浮或保持向上的特性。
