# 🧬 helix

🔊 /ˈhiːlɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A three-dimensional spiral curve; an object or structure with a spiral shape, such as a coiled spring or DNA strand
**中文：** n. 螺旋；螺旋状物；耳轮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spiral（螺旋）
- coil（线圈）
- corkscrew（螺旋形）
- whorl（涡旋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **double helix / alpha helix / helix structure / helix shape**

👉 Example: Watson and Crick famously discovered the double helix structure of DNA in 1953.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The discovery of the double **helix** structure of DNA fundamentally transformed the fields of biology and modern genetics.
   DNA双螺旋结构的发现从根本上改变了生物学和现代遗传学领域。

2. The architect designed the museum staircase in a graceful **helix** that ascended through the center of the glass atrium.
   建筑师将博物馆楼梯设计成一个优雅的螺旋形，穿过玻璃中庭的中心向上延伸。

3. Protein folding often produces an alpha **helix** configuration that is essential for maintaining proper biological function.
   蛋白质折叠通常产生一种对维持正常生物功能至关重要的α螺旋构型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "helix" as related to "helicopter" — both involve spinning or spiral motion, like the rotors of a helicopter tracing a helix.
把"helix"与"helicopter（直升机）"联系起来——两者都涉及旋转或螺旋运动，就像直升机旋翼描绘出的螺旋形。
