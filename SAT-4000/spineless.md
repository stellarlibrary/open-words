# 🪱 spineless

🔊 /ˈspaɪnləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking courage or determination; having no spine or backbone; cowardly and weak-willed
**中文：** adj. 无脊椎的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cowardly（胆小的）
- timid（怯懦的）
- invertebrate（无脊椎的）
- weak-willed（意志薄弱的）
- gutless（没有胆量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spineless creature / spineless politician / spineless coward / spineless response**

👉 Example: The committee criticized the director for his spineless refusal to confront the ethical violations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator was condemned as **spineless** for refusing to vote against the deeply unpopular and unjust legislation.
   这位参议员因拒绝对这项极不受欢迎且不公正的立法投反对票而被谴责为懦弱。

2. Many **spineless** invertebrates, such as jellyfish and worms, play essential roles in marine ecosystems worldwide.
   许多无脊椎动物，如水母和蠕虫，在全球海洋生态系统中扮演着重要角色。

3. His **spineless** capitulation to the demands of the opposition surprised even his most loyal political supporters.
   他对反对派要求的懦弱屈服甚至让他最忠实的政治支持者也感到惊讶。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spineless" as "spine-less" — without a spine, you cannot stand upright, symbolizing a lack of courage or backbone.
把"spineless"想成"spine-less"——没有脊椎就无法直立，象征着缺乏勇气和骨气。
