# ⛪ abbess

🔊 /ˈæbɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A woman who is the head of an abbey of nuns; the female superior of a convent
**中文：** n. 女修道院院长；女庵主持

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mother superior（女修道院院长）
- prioress（女副院长）
- convent head（修女院领导）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the abbess of the convent / elected abbess / served as abbess**

👉 Example: The abbess governed the convent with both spiritual wisdom and practical administrative skill.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **abbess** addressed the assembly of nuns, urging them to maintain their devotion during difficult times.
   女修道院院长向修女们发表讲话，敦促她们在困难时期坚守信仰。

2. Medieval records show that the **abbess** wielded considerable political influence in the surrounding region.
   中世纪的记录表明女修道院院长在周围地区拥有相当大的政治影响力。

3. Upon the former leader's passing, a new **abbess** was elected unanimously by the senior members of the order.
   前任领导去世后，修会的资深成员一致选出了新的女修道院院长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abbess" as the female version of "abbot" — both lead an abbey, but the abbess leads nuns.
把"abbess"想成"abbot"的女性版——两者都领导修道院，但abbess领导的是修女。
