# 📨 submit

🔊 /səbˈmɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To yield or surrender to the authority or will of another; to present for consideration or judgment
**中文：** v. 使服从，屈服；呈送，提交

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- yield（屈服）
- surrender（投降）
- present（呈交）
- propose（提出）
- comply（遵从）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **submit a report / submit to authority / submit an application / submit a proposal**

👉 Example: All applicants must submit their completed forms and supporting documents before the official deadline expires.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team was required to **submit** their findings to the peer-reviewed journal no later than the end of the fiscal quarter.
   研究团队被要求在本财政季度结束前将研究成果提交给同行评审期刊。

2. The conquered nation had no choice but to **submit** to the demands imposed by the occupying military forces after the ceasefire agreement.
   战败国在停火协议后别无选择，只能屈服于占领军强加的要求。

3. Students who fail to **submit** their dissertations by the designated deadline will not be eligible for graduation in the current academic term.
   未能在指定截止日期前提交论文的学生将没有资格在本学期毕业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-mit" as "sending (mittere) from below (sub)" — you send something upward to a higher authority, either yielding to them or handing in your work.
把"submit"想成"从下面（sub）发送（mittere）"——你向上级发送东西，要么向他们屈服，要么递交你的工作。
