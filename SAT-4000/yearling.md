# 🐴 yearling

🔊 /ˈjɪrlɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An animal that is one year old or in its second year. (adj.) Being one year old
**中文：** n. 一岁的家畜；满一岁的动物 adj. 一岁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- colt（小马驹）
- foal（幼驹）
- calf（小牛）
- juvenile（幼畜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **yearling colt / yearling heifer / yearling sale / a fine yearling**

👉 Example: The rancher purchased a healthy yearling at the annual livestock auction last spring.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The breeder carefully evaluated each **yearling** before deciding which horses to enter in the upcoming auction season.
   育马师在决定哪些马参加即将到来的拍卖季之前，仔细评估了每匹一岁马。

2. A promising **yearling** from the champion bloodline fetched an extraordinary price at the thoroughbred sale in Kentucky.
   一匹来自冠军血统的优秀一岁马在肯塔基纯种马拍卖会上拍出了惊人的价格。

3. The veterinarian recommended a specialized diet for the **yearling** cattle to ensure optimal growth during their critical development stage.
   兽医为一岁牛犊推荐了专门的饲料，以确保它们在关键发育阶段的最佳生长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "year-ling" as "year" + "ling" (a small or young one) — a young animal that has lived for one year.
把"yearling"想成"year"（一年）+"ling"（小的、年幼的）——活了一年的幼小动物。
