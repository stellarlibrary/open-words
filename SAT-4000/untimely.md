# ⏰ untimely

🔊 /ʌnˈtaɪmli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Happening at a bad or inappropriate time; premature; occurring before the expected or proper time
**中文：** 不合时宜的；过早的；不适时的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- premature（过早的）
- inopportune（不合时宜的）
- ill-timed（不合时机的）
- unseasonable（不合时令的）
- early（提前的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **untimely death / untimely end / untimely demise / untimely interruption**

👉 Example: The celebrated author's untimely death at the age of thirty-seven shocked the entire literary community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **untimely** frost destroyed much of the region's agricultural harvest, causing significant economic losses for local farmers.
   这场不合时令的霜冻摧毁了该地区大部分农业收成，给当地农民造成了严重的经济损失。

2. His **untimely** resignation from the cabinet left a critical leadership vacuum during the nation's most pressing diplomatic crisis.
   他过早辞去内阁职务，在国家最紧迫的外交危机期间留下了关键的领导真空。

3. The **untimely** interruption of the keynote address by a fire alarm caused considerable frustration among conference attendees.
   火灾警报对主旨演讲的不合时宜的打断引起了与会者相当大的不满。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-time-ly" as "not at the right time" — something untimely happens when the timing is wrong, too early, or inappropriate.
把"untimely"想成"un（不）+ time（时间）+ ly"——不合时宜的事情发生在时机不对、过早或不恰当的时候。
