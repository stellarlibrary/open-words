# 🎭 hypocrite

🔊 /ˈhɪpəkrɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who pretends to have virtues, moral beliefs, or principles that they do not actually possess or practice
**中文：** n. 伪君子；伪善者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fraud（骗子）
- pretender（伪装者）
- deceiver（欺骗者）
- phony（虚伪的人）
- dissembler（伪装者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a total hypocrite / call someone a hypocrite / expose a hypocrite / be branded a hypocrite**

👉 Example: The public called him a hypocrite after he was caught violating the very regulations he had championed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The activist was labeled a **hypocrite** when it was discovered that her personal lifestyle contradicted every principle she promoted.
   当人们发现这位活动家的个人生活方式与她倡导的每一条原则相矛盾时，她被贴上了伪君子的标签。

2. History often reveals that those who preach moral superiority most loudly are frequently the greatest **hypocrites** of their era.
   历史常常揭示，那些最大声宣扬道德优越感的人往往是其时代最大的伪善者。

3. The professor argued that anyone who demands honesty from others while practicing deception is fundamentally a **hypocrite**.
   这位教授认为，任何要求他人诚实却自己行骗的人本质上都是伪君子。

想象一只河马（hippo）批评其他动物太重，而自己却体型庞大——这就是伪君子（hypocrite）！
