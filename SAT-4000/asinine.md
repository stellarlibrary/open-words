# 🫏 asinine

🔊 /ˈæsɪnaɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely stupid or foolish; of or resembling a donkey
**中文：** 驴的；愚蠢的，固执的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- foolish（愚蠢的）
- idiotic（白痴的）
- absurd（荒谬的）
- inane（愚蠢的）
- fatuous（愚昧的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **asinine remark / asinine behavior / asinine decision / asinine question**

👉 Example: The committee dismissed his asinine proposal without any further deliberation or discussion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's **asinine** comments during the debate alienated many voters who had previously supported his campaign.
   那位参议员在辩论中愚蠢的言论疏远了许多此前支持他竞选的选民。

2. It would be **asinine** to invest the company's entire budget in a single untested venture with no guarantees.
   将公司全部预算投入一个没有保障的未经测试的项目是极其愚蠢的。

3. The critic described the film's plot as **asinine**, noting that its logic defied even the most generous interpretation.
   评论家将这部电影的情节描述为愚蠢至极，指出其逻辑经不起最宽容的解读。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Asinine" comes from Latin "asinus" meaning "donkey." Think: acting like an ass — stubborn and foolish.
"Asinine"源自拉丁语"asinus"（驴）。联想：像驴一样行事——固执又愚蠢。
