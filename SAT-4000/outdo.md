# 🏆 outdo

🔊 /ˌaʊtˈduː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To surpass or exceed someone in performance, achievement, or quality
**中文：** v. 超过；胜过

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surpass（超越）
- exceed（超过）
- outperform（胜过）
- eclipse（使黯然失色）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outdo oneself / outdo a rival / outdo the competition / not to be outdone**

👉 Example: Not to be outdone by her colleague, she submitted an even more ambitious project proposal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young pianist managed to **outdo** all previous competitors with her extraordinary technical skill and artistry.
   这位年轻的钢琴家凭借非凡的技巧和艺术性超越了所有先前的竞争者。

2. Each year the festival organizers strive to **outdo** themselves by creating more spectacular and innovative events.
   每年节日组织者都努力超越自己，创造更加壮观和创新的活动。

3. The company's latest innovation clearly **outdid** the efforts of its competitors in both design and functionality.
   该公司最新的创新在设计和功能上明显胜过了竞争对手的努力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out + do" — to do more or better than others, to go beyond what others have done.
把"outdo"拆成"out（超出）+ do（做）"——比别人做得更多更好，超越他人所做的事。
