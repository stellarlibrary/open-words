# 🌿 fern

🔊 /fɜːrn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A flowerless, seedless plant that has feathery or leafy fronds and reproduces by means of spores; a member of the class Polypodiopsida
**中文：** n. 蕨；蕨类植物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bracken（欧洲蕨）
- frond plant（叶状植物）
- pteridophyte（蕨类植物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tree fern / fern frond / fern species / maidenhair fern / grow ferns / fern forest**

👉 Example: The shady garden was filled with lush ferns that thrived in the cool, moist environment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Ancient **fern** fossils discovered in the coal deposits provide crucial evidence about the lush vegetation of the Carboniferous period.
   在煤矿沉积物中发现的古蕨类化石提供了关于石炭纪茂盛植被的重要证据。

2. The botanist catalogued over thirty distinct species of **fern** growing within the protected boundaries of the tropical rainforest reserve.
   植物学家编录了在热带雨林保护区范围内生长的三十多种不同蕨类植物。

3. Unlike flowering plants, the **fern** reproduces through spores rather than seeds, representing an ancient evolutionary lineage of plant life.
   与开花植物不同，蕨类植物通过孢子而非种子繁殖，代表了植物生命的古老进化谱系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fern" as the plant you see in "ferny" forests — dark, moist woodlands where these ancient leafy plants unfurl their distinctive curled fronds.
把"fern"和"ferny forests（蕨林）"联系起来——在阴暗潮湿的林地中，这些古老的叶状植物展开它们独特的卷曲叶片。
