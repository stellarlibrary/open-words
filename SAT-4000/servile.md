# ⛓️ servile

🔊 /ˈsɜːrvaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Excessively submissive or obedient; of or relating to a slave or slavery; lacking independence or originality
**中文：** adj. 奴隶的；卑屈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- subservient（卑躬屈膝的）
- obsequious（谄媚的）
- slavish（奴性的）
- sycophantic（阿谀奉承的）
- fawning（讨好的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **servile attitude / servile obedience / servile flattery / servile behavior / servile imitation**

👉 Example: His servile attitude toward authority figures made his colleagues uncomfortable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The courtiers displayed a **servile** devotion to the monarch, agreeing with every decree regardless of its merit.
   朝臣们对君主表现出卑屈的忠诚，不论法令优劣都表示赞同。

2. The critic dismissed the artist's work as a **servile** imitation of the great masters, lacking any original vision.
   评论家将这位艺术家的作品斥为对大师们的奴性模仿，缺乏任何原创视野。

3. A truly democratic society must reject **servile** compliance and instead encourage citizens to think critically about governance.
   一个真正的民主社会必须摒弃卑屈的顺从，转而鼓励公民批判性地思考治理问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "servile" as "serve + ile" — someone who serves in a submissive, slave-like manner, always bowing and obeying without question.
把"servile"想成"serve + ile"——一个以卑屈、奴隶般的方式服务的人，总是点头哈腰、毫无质疑地服从。
