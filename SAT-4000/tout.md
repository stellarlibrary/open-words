# 📢 tout

🔊 /taʊt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To promote or advertise aggressively; to solicit business or support persistently. (n.) A person who solicits business or sells tickets at inflated prices
**中文：** v. 兜售；吹捧；极力推荐 n. 兜售者；票贩子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- promote（推广）
- advertise（宣传）
- peddle（兜售）
- hawk（叫卖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tout something as / tout the benefits / widely touted / tout for business**

👉 Example: The company aggressively touted its new product as a revolutionary breakthrough in renewable energy technology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Pharmaceutical representatives frequently **tout** the advantages of their medications to physicians at medical conferences.
   制药代表经常在医学会议上向医生们**吹捧**他们药物的优势。

2. The candidate was **touted** as the most qualified leader to guide the organization through its challenging restructuring period.
   这位候选人被**吹捧**为在充满挑战的重组时期引领该组织的最合适领导人。

3. Street vendors attempted to **tout** counterfeit luxury goods to unsuspecting tourists near the popular landmark.
   街头小贩试图在著名地标附近向毫无戒心的游客**兜售**假冒奢侈品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tout" as "shout out" — when you tout something, you shout about it to everyone, promoting it loudly.
把"tout"想成"shout out（大声喊出）"——当你tout某物时，你大声向所有人宣传它。
