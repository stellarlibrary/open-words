# 💡 solution

🔊 /səˈluːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A means of solving a problem or dealing with a difficult situation; a liquid mixture in which a substance has been dissolved; the process of dissolving; an answer to a question
**中文：** n. 溶解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- answer（答案）
- resolution（解决）
- mixture（混合物）
- remedy（补救方法）
- dissolution（溶解）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solution to a problem / aqueous solution / find a solution / solution set**

👉 Example: The committee proposed a practical solution to the budgetary constraints affecting the research department.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist prepared a saline **solution** by dissolving precisely measured sodium chloride into distilled water at room temperature.
   化学家通过在室温下将精确称量的氯化钠溶解到蒸馏水中来配制盐溶液。

2. After months of deliberation, the advisory board finally reached a viable **solution** to the persistent infrastructure challenges.
   经过数月的审议，顾问委员会终于对持续存在的基础设施挑战找到了可行的解决方案。

3. The **solution** of the complex equation required advanced mathematical techniques that extended beyond the standard curriculum.
   这道复杂方程的解答需要超出标准课程范围的高等数学技巧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solution" as "solve + ution" — the result of solving, whether it is a problem resolved or a substance dissolved.
把"solution"想成"solve + ution"——解决的结果，无论是问题被解决还是物质被溶解。
