# 🕳️ abyss

🔊 /əˈbɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An extremely deep or seemingly bottomless chasm; a profound void or state of despair.
**中文：** 深渊；无底洞；深邃的空间；绝望的境地。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chasm（深渊）
- void（虚空）
- gulf（鸿沟）
- depths（深处）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the abyss of**, **stare into the abyss**, **on the edge of an abyss**

👉 Example: The nation stood on the edge of an economic abyss.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher warned that society was descending into an **abyss** of moral decay.
   哲学家警告说，社会正在堕入道德沦丧的深渊。

2. Between the two political factions lay an **abyss** of irreconcilable differences.
   两个政治派系之间横亘着一道不可调和的鸿沟。

3. She gazed into the **abyss** of uncertainty, unsure of what the future held.
   她凝视着不确定的深渊，不知道未来会怎样。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a + byss" — imagine "a bus" falling into a bottomless pit, an abyss so deep the bus vanishes.
想象"一辆巴士"（a bus）掉进了无底洞，深不见底，就是 abyss（深渊）。
