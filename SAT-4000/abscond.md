# 🏃 abscond

🔊 /əbˈskɒnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To leave hurriedly and secretly, typically to escape from custody or avoid arrest or detection
**中文：** v. 潜逃，逃匿；避债

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flee（逃走）
- escape（逃脱）
- bolt（逃跑）
- decamp（溜走）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abscond with funds / abscond from custody / abscond with the money / abscond before trial**

👉 Example: The treasurer absconded with nearly half a million dollars from the charity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The suspect **absconded** from the country just hours before authorities issued a formal arrest warrant.
   嫌疑人在当局发出正式逮捕令前数小时就潜逃出境了。

2. Investigators discovered that the accountant had **absconded** with millions in embezzled corporate funds.
   调查人员发现该会计携数百万挪用的公司资金潜逃了。

3. The prisoner **absconded** during a routine transfer between facilities, prompting a nationwide manhunt immediately.
   囚犯在设施间的例行转移中逃跑，立即引发了全国范围的搜捕。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abscond" as "ab-s-GONE-d" — the person is gone! They secretly left and cannot be found.
把"abscond"想成"abs-GONE-d"——那个人已经消失了（gone）！他们偷偷离开，无处可寻。
