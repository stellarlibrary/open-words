# 🏆 glorious

🔊 /ˈɡlɔːriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or deserving great admiration, praise, or distinction; magnificently beautiful or splendid
**中文：** adj. 光荣的；壮丽的，辉煌的；极好的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- magnificent（壮丽的）
- splendid（辉煌的）
- illustrious（杰出的）
- resplendent（华丽的）
- sublime（崇高的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glorious victory / glorious achievement / glorious sunset / glorious past**

👉 Example: The nation celebrated the glorious achievement of sending its first astronaut into orbit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The empire's **glorious** era of expansion eventually gave way to decades of internal conflict and gradual territorial decline.
   帝国辉煌的扩张时代最终让位于数十年的内部冲突和领土逐步缩减。

2. Hikers who reached the summit were rewarded with a **glorious** panoramic view stretching across three mountain ranges.
   到达山顶的徒步者得到了一幅壮丽的全景，可以俯瞰三条山脉。

3. The orchestra delivered a **glorious** performance of the symphony that earned a prolonged standing ovation from the audience.
   管弦乐团奉献了一场辉煌的交响乐演出，赢得了观众长时间的起立鼓掌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glorious" as "glory + ous" — full of glory, like a shining trophy that everyone admires.
把"glorious"想成"glory（荣耀）+ ous"——充满荣耀的，就像一座闪闪发光、人人钦羡的奖杯。
