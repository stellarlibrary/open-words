# 🔄 involve

🔊 /ɪnˈvɑːlv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To include as a necessary part or result; to cause to participate or become entangled; to engross or absorb
**中文：** vt. 包含；牵涉；使卷入；使陷于；潜心于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- entail（牵涉）
- include（包含）
- implicate（使牵连）
- engage（使参与）
- encompass（包含）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **involve risk / involve someone in / get involved / be involved with / deeply involved**

👉 Example: The research project will involve collaboration between scientists from multiple universities and disciplines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that the scandal would **involve** several high-ranking officials who had concealed financial irregularities.
   调查显示，这一丑闻将牵涉到几位隐瞒财务违规行为的高级官员。

2. Effective urban planning must **involve** careful consideration of environmental, economic, and social factors to achieve sustainability.
   有效的城市规划必须包含对环境、经济和社会因素的仔细考量，以实现可持续发展。

3. The professor encouraged students to **involve** themselves actively in community service projects throughout the academic semester.
   教授鼓励学生在整个学期中积极参与社区服务项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-VOLVE" — "volve" comes from Latin "volvere" meaning "to roll," so to involve is to roll someone or something INTO a situation.
把"involve"想成"in-VOLVE"——"volve"来自拉丁语"volvere"意为"滚动"，所以involve就是把某人或某事卷入一个情境中。
