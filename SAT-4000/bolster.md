# 💪 bolster

🔊 /ˈboʊlstər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A long, firm pillow or cushion used for support. (v.) To support, strengthen, or prop up something.
**中文：** n. 长枕；垫子；支持物 v. 支持；支撑；加强

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reinforce（加强）
- buttress（支撑）
- fortify（巩固）
- sustain（维持）
- prop up（支撑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bolster confidence / bolster the economy / bolster an argument / bolster support / bolster morale**

👉 Example: The new evidence served to bolster the prosecution's already compelling case against the defendant.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The central bank introduced a series of fiscal measures designed to **bolster** the weakening national economy during the recession.
   央行推出了一系列旨在提振衰退期间疲软的国民经济的财政措施。

2. Additional research findings **bolstered** the scientist's original hypothesis, convincing even the most skeptical members of the review panel.
   额外的研究成果支持了科学家最初的假设，说服了评审小组中最持怀疑态度的成员。

3. The commander sought to **bolster** troop morale by personally visiting each forward outpost during the prolonged military campaign.
   指挥官在漫长的军事行动中亲自走访每个前沿哨所，以鼓舞士气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bolster" as a "bolster pillow" that props you up — just as a cushion supports your body, to "bolster" something means to give it the support it needs to stay strong.
把"bolster"想成一个"支撑枕头"——就像垫子支撑你的身体一样，"bolster"意味着给某物提供所需的支持使其保持强大。
