# 🏔️ height

🔊 /haɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The measurement of how tall something or someone is; an elevated area of land; the peak or most intense point of something
**中文：** n. 高度；身高；高地；顶点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- altitude（海拔）
- elevation（高度）
- summit（顶点）
- peak（巅峰）
- stature（身高）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the height of fame / the height of summer / at a great height / reach new heights**

👉 Example: The Renaissance is often regarded as the height of artistic achievement in European history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. At the **height** of the Roman Empire, its territory extended across three continents and encompassed millions of people.
   在罗马帝国的鼎盛时期，其领土横跨三大洲，涵盖了数百万人口。

2. The research balloon ascended to a **height** of thirty thousand meters to collect atmospheric data for the study.
   研究气球上升到三万米的高度，以收集用于研究的大气数据。

3. Critics argue that the economic crisis reached its **height** during the fourth quarter when unemployment rates soared dramatically.
   评论家认为经济危机在第四季度达到了顶峰，当时失业率急剧飙升。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "height" as "high" with a twist — it measures how HIGH something goes, whether physically or figuratively.
把"height"想成"high（高）"的变体——它衡量某物有多高，无论是物理上还是比喻意义上。
