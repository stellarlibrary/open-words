# 💢 spasmodic

🔊 /spæzˈmɒdɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring or done in brief, irregular bursts; relating to or caused by a spasm or spasms; intermittent and unpredictable
**中文：** adj. 痉挛的；间歇性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intermittent（间歇的）
- sporadic（偶发的）
- fitful（断断续续的）
- convulsive（痉挛的）
- erratic（不稳定的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spasmodic efforts / spasmodic movements / spasmodic bursts / spasmodic contractions**

👉 Example: His spasmodic attempts at studying produced inconsistent results throughout the academic semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient experienced **spasmodic** contractions in her lower limbs, prompting the neurologist to order a comprehensive diagnostic evaluation.
   患者下肢出现痉挛性收缩，促使神经科医生下令进行全面的诊断评估。

2. The committee's **spasmodic** efforts to address the housing crisis failed to produce any coherent or lasting policy solutions.
   委员会为解决住房危机所做的断断续续的努力未能产生任何连贯或持久的政策解决方案。

3. Progress on the construction project was **spasmodic** at best, with weeks of inactivity followed by sudden bursts of labor.
   建设项目的进展充其量是间歇性的，数周的停工之后突然爆发一阵劳动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spasmodic" as "spasm" + "odd" + "ic" — like odd spasms that come and go unpredictably, never following a steady pattern.
把"spasmodic"想成"spasm"（痉挛）+"odd"（奇怪的）+"ic"——像是不可预测地时来时去的奇怪痉挛，从不遵循稳定的模式。
