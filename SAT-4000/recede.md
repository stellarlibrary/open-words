# 🌊 recede

🔊 /rɪˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To move back or away from a previous position; to gradually diminish or fade; to slope backward
**中文：** v. 后退；减弱；渐渐远去；向后倾斜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- retreat（撤退）
- withdraw（退缩）
- diminish（减弱）
- ebb（退潮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recede from view / recede into the distance / floodwaters recede / hairline recedes**

👉 Example: As the ship sailed away, the coastline gradually receded from view.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After three weeks of devastating floods, the waters finally began to **recede**, revealing the extent of structural damage.
   经过三周的毁灭性洪水后，水位终于开始消退，露出了建筑损毁的程度。

2. Public enthusiasm for the reform initiative started to **recede** as economic conditions continued to deteriorate throughout the region.
   随着该地区经济状况持续恶化，公众对改革倡议的热情开始减退。

3. The mountain peaks slowly **receded** into the mist as the train carried passengers through the winding valley.
   当列车载着乘客穿越蜿蜒的山谷时，山峰慢慢消失在薄雾之中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-cede" as "go back" — "cede" relates to "cede" (yield/go), so recede means to go back or pull away.
把"recede"想成"re-（向后）+ cede（走）"——向后走，即后退、消退。
