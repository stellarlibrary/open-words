# 🔍 clarity

🔊 /ˈklærɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being clear, coherent, and easily understood; the quality of transparency or purity
**中文：** n. 清楚；明晰；透明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lucidity（清晰）
- precision（精确）
- transparency（透明）
- coherence（连贯性）
- distinctness（清楚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clarity of thought / clarity of expression / lack clarity / achieve clarity / with great clarity**

👉 Example: The essay was praised for its remarkable clarity of argument and elegant prose style.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **clarity** of the professor's explanation enabled even the most inexperienced students to grasp the complex theoretical framework.
   教授清晰的解释使即使最缺乏经验的学生也能理解这一复杂的理论框架。

2. Environmental regulations must be written with sufficient **clarity** to prevent misinterpretation and ensure consistent enforcement across jurisdictions.
   环境法规必须写得足够清晰，以防止误读并确保各辖区的一致执行。

3. After weeks of deliberation, the committee finally reached **clarity** on the procedural steps required for institutional reform.
   经过数周的审议，委员会终于明确了机构改革所需的程序步骤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clar-ity" — the state of being "clear" (clar-), like a crystal-clear lake where you can see all the way to the bottom.
把"clarity"想成"清楚"（clar-）的状态——就像一个清澈见底的湖泊，一眼就能看到底部。
