# 🚧 stymie

🔊 /ˈstaɪmi/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To obstruct or thwart; to hinder or prevent progress. (n.) A situation where one's ball blocks another on the green
**中文：** v. 从中作梗，完全妨碍

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thwart（阻挠）
- obstruct（妨碍）
- hinder（阻碍）
- impede（阻止）
- frustrate（挫败）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stymie efforts / stymie progress / stymie plans / stymied by bureaucracy**

👉 Example: Bureaucratic red tape continued to stymie the committee's efforts to implement meaningful reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Political disagreements between the two factions effectively **stymied** all legislative progress on the critical infrastructure bill.
   两个派系之间的政治分歧有效地阻碍了关键基础设施法案的所有立法进展。

2. The lack of adequate funding continued to **stymie** the research team's attempts to complete the groundbreaking clinical trial.
   资金不足持续妨碍研究团队完成这项突破性临床试验的努力。

3. Investigators were **stymied** by the suspect's refusal to cooperate, which significantly delayed the resolution of the case.
   嫌疑人拒绝合作使调查人员受阻，大大推迟了案件的解决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stymie" as "sty + me" — imagine being stuck in a pig sty with no way out, completely blocked and unable to make progress.
把"stymie"想成"sty + me"——想象自己被困在猪圈里无路可走，完全被阻挡，无法前进。
