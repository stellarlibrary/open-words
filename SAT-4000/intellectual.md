# 🧠 intellectual

🔊 /ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the intellect or mental abilities; requiring use of the mind. (n.) A person who engages in deep thinking, study, or academic pursuits
**中文：** adj. 智力的；聪明的；理智的 n. 知识分子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cerebral（智力的）
- scholarly（学术的）
- erudite（博学的）
- academic（学术的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intellectual curiosity / intellectual capacity / intellectual property / public intellectual**

👉 Example: Her intellectual curiosity drove her to explore topics far beyond the standard curriculum.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was recognized as a leading **intellectual** whose writings influenced public policy discussions across the continent.
   这位教授被公认为杰出的知识分子，其著作影响了整个大陆的公共政策讨论。

2. Engaging in **intellectual** debate requires the ability to consider multiple perspectives without resorting to personal attacks.
   参与理性辩论需要能够考虑多种观点而不诉诸人身攻击。

3. The university fosters an **intellectual** environment where students are encouraged to challenge conventional assumptions and theories.
   该大学营造了一种学术环境，鼓励学生挑战传统假设和理论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intellect-ual" — someone full of "intellect," a person whose mind is always working and learning.
把"intellectual"想成"充满intellect（智力）的人"——一个头脑始终在思考和学习的人。
