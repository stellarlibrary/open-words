# 🎨 aesthetic

🔊 /ɛsˈθɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to beauty, the appreciation of beauty, or good taste in art and design.
**中文：** 美的；美学的；审美的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- artistic（艺术的）
- elegant（优雅的）
- tasteful（有品味的）
- refined（精致的）
- beautiful（美丽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **value, appeal, sense, judgment, pleasure**
👉 Example: The gallery curator prioritized the aesthetic value of each selected artwork.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect achieved a remarkable balance between functional requirements and **aesthetic** appeal in the building's design.
   建筑师在建筑设计中实现了功能需求与审美吸引力之间的卓越平衡。

2. Her **aesthetic** sensibility was shaped by years of studying classical art and Renaissance sculpture.
   她的审美感受力是通过多年研究古典艺术和文艺复兴雕塑塑造而成的。

3. The committee evaluated each proposal based on its **aesthetic** merit as well as its structural integrity.
   委员会根据每项提案的美学价值及其结构完整性进行评估。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "aesthetic" as sharing a root with "anesthesia" — both come from Greek "aisthesis" (feeling); aesthetic relates to the feeling of beauty.
将"aesthetic"与"anesthesia"联系起来——两者都源于希腊语"aisthesis"（感觉）；aesthetic与对美的感受有关。
