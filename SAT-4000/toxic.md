# ☠️ toxic

🔊 /ˈtɑːksɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Containing or being poisonous material; capable of causing serious harm or death; extremely harmful or unpleasant
**中文：** 有毒的；有害的；引起中毒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- poisonous（有毒的）
- venomous（有毒液的）
- noxious（有害的）
- hazardous（危险的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **toxic waste / toxic substance / toxic environment / toxic relationship / toxic chemicals**

👉 Example: Prolonged exposure to toxic industrial chemicals can have severe consequences for workers' long-term health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental scientists discovered **toxic** contaminants in the groundwater supply that posed a serious risk to public health.
   环境科学家在地下水中发现了对公众健康构成严重威胁的**有毒**污染物。

2. The committee determined that the **toxic** workplace culture had contributed significantly to high employee turnover rates.
   委员会认定，**有害的**职场文化在很大程度上导致了员工高离职率。

3. Regulations require manufacturers to clearly label all products containing **toxic** materials before distributing them to retailers.
   法规要求制造商在将含有**有毒**材料的产品分销给零售商之前必须明确标注。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "toxic" as coming from "toxin" — both share the root "tox" meaning poison, so toxic means full of poison.
把"toxic"和"toxin（毒素）"联系起来——两者共享词根"tox"意为毒，所以toxic就是充满毒的。
