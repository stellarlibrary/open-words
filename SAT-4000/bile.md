# 😤 bile

🔊 /baɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A bitter greenish-brown fluid secreted by the liver to aid digestion; (figurative) anger, irritability, or bad temper
**中文：** 胆汁；愤怒；暴躁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gall（胆汁；怨恨）
- bitterness（苦涩；怨恨）
- rancor（怨恨）
- spleen（怒气）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bile duct / full of bile / rise of bile / bile and venom / spew bile**

👉 Example: The critic's review was filled with bile, attacking the author's work with unnecessary hostility and contempt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The liver produces **bile** that is stored in the gallbladder and released into the small intestine to facilitate fat digestion.
   肝脏产生胆汁，储存在胆囊中，释放到小肠以促进脂肪消化。

2. His resignation letter was laced with **bile** and resentment, reflecting years of frustration with the corporate management structure.
   他的辞职信充满了愤怒和怨恨，反映了多年来对公司管理结构的不满。

3. The surgeon identified a blockage in the **bile** duct that had caused the patient severe abdominal pain for several weeks.
   外科医生发现了胆管中的堵塞，这一堵塞已导致患者数周的严重腹痛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bile" as something bitter — both the bitter fluid in your body and the bitter anger you might feel. Bitter bile makes you boil.
把"bile"想成苦涩的东西——既是体内苦涩的液体，也是你可能感受到的苦涩愤怒。苦涩的胆汁让人沸腾。
