# 🏛️ grandeur

🔊 /ˈɡrændʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Splendor and impressiveness, especially of appearance or style; the quality of being grand or magnificent
**中文：** n. 壮丽；庄严；宏伟；壮观

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- magnificence（壮丽）
- splendor（辉煌）
- majesty（威严）
- nobility（高贵）
- sublimity（崇高）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grandeur of / faded grandeur / natural grandeur / delusions of grandeur**

👉 Example: The grandeur of the mountain range left even the most seasoned travelers speechless with awe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **grandeur** of the cathedral's vaulted ceilings and stained glass windows continues to inspire architects and visitors alike.
   大教堂拱形天花板和彩色玻璃窗的壮丽至今仍令建筑师和游客深受启发。

2. Despite its faded **grandeur**, the abandoned palace still offers compelling evidence of the empire's former wealth and cultural influence.
   尽管昔日的辉煌已经褪色，这座废弃的宫殿仍然提供了帝国昔日财富和文化影响力的有力证据。

3. The philosopher argued that true **grandeur** lies not in material possessions but in the cultivation of moral virtue and wisdom.
   这位哲学家认为，真正的崇高不在于物质财富，而在于道德品质和智慧的培养。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grandeur" as "grand + eur" — the suffix "-eur" makes it a noun, so it is literally the quality of being grand, like a grand palace or grand mountain.
把"grandeur"想成"grand + eur"——后缀"-eur"使它成为名词，字面意思就是宏伟的品质，如宏伟的宫殿或壮丽的山脉。
