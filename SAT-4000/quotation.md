# 📜 quotation

🔊 /kwoʊˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A phrase or passage repeated by someone other than the original author; a formal statement of the price of goods or services
**中文：** n. 引用语；引证；报价单

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- citation（引用）
- excerpt（摘录）
- passage（段落）
- estimate（报价）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **direct quotation / famous quotation / quotation marks / quotation from / price quotation**

👉 Example: The essay opened with a powerful quotation from a renowned philosopher to establish its central theme.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar included a lengthy **quotation** from the original manuscript to support her controversial interpretation of the ancient text.
   学者引用了原始手稿中的一大段引文来支持她对这篇古代文本的争议性解读。

2. The contractor provided a detailed **quotation** for the renovation project, itemizing every material cost and labor expense involved.
   承包商为翻修项目提供了一份详细的报价单，逐项列出了所有材料成本和人工费用。

3. His inspiring speech concluded with a memorable **quotation** attributed to Abraham Lincoln about the enduring nature of democratic values.
   他鼓舞人心的演讲以一句据称出自亚伯拉罕·林肯关于民主价值持久性的名言作为结尾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quotation" as "quote + ation" — the act of quoting someone's words, or quoting a price for a transaction.
把"quotation"想成"quote + ation"——引用某人话语的行为，或为交易报价的行为。
