# 🎭 protagonist

🔊 /proʊˈtæɡənɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The leading character or one of the major characters in a story, play, or film; a chief advocate or champion of a cause or movement
**中文：** n. 领导者；主角；积极参加者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hero（主角）
- lead character（主要人物）
- champion（拥护者）
- advocate（倡导者）
- principal（主要角色）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **main protagonist / protagonist of the story / protagonist in the debate / protagonist of reform**

👉 Example: The protagonist of the novel struggles with moral dilemmas that mirror contemporary societal conflicts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The film's **protagonist** undergoes a profound moral transformation as she confronts the ethical consequences of her earlier decisions.
   影片的主角在面对她早期决定的道德后果时经历了深刻的道德转变。

2. As the chief **protagonist** of educational reform, the senator championed legislation to increase funding for underserved rural schools.
   作为教育改革的主要倡导者，这位参议员推动立法增加对服务不足的农村学校的拨款。

3. Literary scholars have debated whether the **protagonist** of the classic novel represents the author's own conflicted political ideals.
   文学学者们一直在争论这部经典小说的主人公是否代表了作者本人矛盾的政治理想。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proto-AGONIST" — "proto" means first, and "agonist" relates to a contestant; the protagonist is the FIRST and most important character.
把"protagonist"想成"proto-AGONIST"——"proto"意为第一，"agonist"与竞争者有关；protagonist就是第一位、最重要的角色。
