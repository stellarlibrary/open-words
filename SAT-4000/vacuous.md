# 💨 vacuous

🔊 /ˈvækjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing a lack of thought or intelligence; empty; purposeless
**中文：** adj. 空的；空虚的；空洞的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- empty（空洞的）
- mindless（愚蠢的）
- inane（空洞的）
- vapid（乏味的）
- blank（茫然的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vacuous expression / vacuous stare / vacuous remark / vacuous smile**

👉 Example: The interviewer was dismayed by the candidate's vacuous responses to serious policy questions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The critic dismissed the film as a **vacuous** spectacle that relied entirely on visual effects rather than meaningful storytelling.
   评论家将这部电影斥为空洞的视觉奇观，完全依赖视觉效果而非有意义的叙事。

2. Her **vacuous** expression during the lecture suggested that she had not comprehended any of the complex material presented.
   她在讲座期间茫然的表情表明她没有理解所呈现的任何复杂内容。

3. The editorial board criticized the **vacuous** promises made by candidates who offered no concrete plans for economic reform.
   编辑委员会批评了候选人们空洞的承诺，他们没有提出任何具体的经济改革计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vacuous" as "vacuum-ous" — like a vacuum, a vacuous mind is completely empty with nothing inside.
把"vacuous"想成"vacuum-ous"——就像真空一样，vacuous的头脑里面完全是空的，什么也没有。
