# 🚶 ambulate

🔊 /ˈæmbjəleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To walk from place to place; to move about on foot, especially as a form of exercise or recovery
**中文：** 走动；步行；移动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- walk（步行）
- stroll（漫步）
- perambulate（巡行）
- traverse（穿越）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ambulate independently / ambulate with assistance / able to ambulate / ambulate freely**

👉 Example: The physician encouraged the post-surgical patient to ambulate as soon as possible to promote recovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After weeks of physical therapy, the patient was finally able to **ambulate** without the aid of a walker or crutches.
   经过数周的物理治疗，患者终于能够在不借助助行器或拐杖的情况下行走。

2. The nursing staff assisted the elderly resident to **ambulate** through the hallway each morning as part of her daily routine.
   护理人员每天早晨协助这位老年住户在走廊里步行，作为她日常活动的一部分。

3. The rehabilitation program required patients to **ambulate** for at least twenty minutes each day to regain their strength.
   康复计划要求患者每天至少步行二十分钟以恢复体力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ambulate" sharing a root with "ambulance" — both come from Latin "ambulare" meaning to walk; an ambulance helps those who cannot ambulate.
把"ambulate"和"ambulance"（救护车）联系起来——都来自拉丁语"ambulare"（行走）；救护车帮助那些无法行走的人。
