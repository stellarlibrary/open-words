# 🎓 graduate

🔊 /ˈɡrædʒueɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To confer or receive an academic degree; to complete a course of study. (n.) A person who has received an academic degree; also, a graduated measuring vessel
**中文：** v. 授予…学位；毕业 n. 毕业生；量筒，量杯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alumnus（校友）
- complete（完成）
- finish（结束）
- qualify（取得资格）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **graduate from / graduate with honors / graduate school / college graduate**

👉 Example: She plans to graduate from the engineering program with highest distinction next spring.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students who **graduate** with advanced degrees in data science are increasingly sought after by major technology corporations worldwide.
   获得数据科学高级学位的毕业生越来越受到全球主要科技公司的追捧。

2. The university will **graduate** its largest class in institutional history during the commencement ceremony scheduled for this June.
   该大学将在定于今年六月举行的毕业典礼上为校史上最大的一届学生授予学位。

3. As a recent **graduate** of the doctoral program, he secured a prestigious research fellowship at the national laboratory.
   作为博士项目的应届毕业生，他在国家实验室获得了一项享有盛誉的研究奖学金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "graduate" as sharing the root "grad" (step) — to graduate is to step up to the next level of your academic or professional journey.
把"graduate"和词根"grad"（步）联系起来——毕业就是迈上学业或职业旅程的下一个台阶。
