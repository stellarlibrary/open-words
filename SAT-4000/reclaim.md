# ♻️ reclaim

🔊 /rɪˈkleɪm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To retrieve or recover something previously lost or given; to bring waste land or land under water into a condition suitable for use; to reform a person
**中文：** v. 要求归还；收回；开垦；回收再利用；改造某人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recover（恢复）
- retrieve（取回）
- rehabilitate（修复）
- salvage（挽救）
- restore（恢复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reclaim land / reclaim materials / reclaim one's rights / reclaim lost territory**

👉 Example: The Netherlands has a long history of reclaiming land from the sea through innovative engineering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental engineers developed a comprehensive plan to **reclaim** the contaminated industrial site for future residential development.
   环境工程师制定了一项全面计划，将受污染的工业用地开垦为未来的住宅开发区。

2. The indigenous community launched a legal campaign to **reclaim** ancestral lands that had been seized during the colonial era.
   原住民社区发起了一场法律运动，要求归还在殖民时期被夺取的祖传土地。

3. Modern recycling facilities can **reclaim** valuable metals from discarded electronic devices, significantly reducing environmental waste and pollution.
   现代回收设施可以从废弃电子设备中回收有价值的金属，大大减少环境废物和污染。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-claim" as "claim again" — you are making a claim to take back something that was once yours, whether it is land, materials, or rights.
把"reclaim"拆成"re-（再次）+ claim（声称拥有）"——再次声称拥有，即取回曾经属于你的东西，无论是土地、材料还是权利。
