# 🤝 encounter

🔊 /ɪnˈkaʊntər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To unexpectedly meet or come upon; to face or experience something difficult. (n.) An unexpected or casual meeting
**中文：** v. 遭遇，邂逅 n. 遭遇，相遇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confront（面对）
- come across（偶遇）
- meet（碰到）
- run into（碰见）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **encounter difficulties / encounter resistance / a chance encounter / a close encounter**

👉 Example: Researchers may encounter unexpected obstacles when conducting fieldwork in remote and unfamiliar regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The expedition team **encountered** severe weather conditions that forced them to delay their ascent of the mountain summit.
   探险队遭遇了恶劣的天气条件，迫使他们推迟了向山顶的攀登。

2. A chance **encounter** with a distinguished professor at the conference ultimately changed the trajectory of her academic career.
   在会议上与一位杰出教授的偶然相遇最终改变了她学术生涯的轨迹。

3. Students frequently **encounter** complex ethical dilemmas in their study of biomedical research and clinical practice.
   学生在生物医学研究和临床实践的学习中经常遇到复杂的伦理困境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-counter" as running into someone "at the counter" — an unexpected meeting that happens by chance at a shop counter.
把"encounter"想成在"counter（柜台）"前碰到某人——在商店柜台前偶然发生的意外相遇。
