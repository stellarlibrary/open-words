# 😣 vexation

🔊 /vɛkˈseɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being annoyed or frustrated; something that causes annoyance or distress
**中文：** n. 恼怒；烦恼；苦恼的原因

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritation（恼怒）
- annoyance（烦恼）
- frustration（挫败感）
- exasperation（愤怒）
- aggravation（加剧的烦恼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **source of vexation / great vexation / cause vexation / to one's vexation**

👉 Example: To her great vexation, the flight was delayed for the third consecutive time that week.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The repeated bureaucratic obstacles became a source of considerable **vexation** for the researchers applying for government grants.
   反复出现的官僚障碍成为申请政府资助的研究人员相当**烦恼**的根源。

2. She could not conceal her **vexation** when the committee dismissed her meticulously prepared proposal without adequate deliberation.
   当委员会未经充分讨论就否决了她精心准备的提案时，她无法掩饰自己的**恼怒**。

3. The constant noise from the nearby construction site was a daily **vexation** that disrupted the students' concentration during examinations.
   附近建筑工地持续不断的噪音是每天的**烦恼**之源，在考试期间扰乱了学生们的注意力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vexation" as "vex + action" — the action of being vexed, the state of feeling thoroughly annoyed.
把"vexation"想成"vex（烦恼）+ action（行为）"——被烦恼的行为，即彻底恼怒的状态。
