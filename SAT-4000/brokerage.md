# 💼 brokerage

🔊 /ˈbroʊkərɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A fee or commission charged by a broker for services; the business or establishment of a broker
**中文：** n. 佣金；回扣；中间人业务；经纪公司

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commission（佣金）
- fee（费用）
- intermediary（中介）
- agency（代理）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brokerage firm / brokerage fee / brokerage account / brokerage house**

👉 Example: The brokerage firm handled all transactions related to the acquisition of foreign assets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investment **brokerage** managed portfolios worth billions of dollars for institutional and private clients alike.
   该投资经纪公司为机构和私人客户管理着价值数十亿美元的投资组合。

2. Investors should carefully compare **brokerage** fees before selecting a firm to handle their stock transactions.
   投资者在选择处理股票交易的公司之前，应仔细比较经纪佣金。

3. The **brokerage** facilitated negotiations between the two multinational corporations seeking a strategic partnership agreement.
   该经纪公司促成了两家寻求战略合作协议的跨国公司之间的谈判。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "broker + age" — the "age" (era/business) of the broker, referring to everything a broker does and earns.
把"brokerage"想成"broker（经纪人）+ age（时代/业务）"——经纪人的业务时代，指经纪人所做的一切和赚取的佣金。
