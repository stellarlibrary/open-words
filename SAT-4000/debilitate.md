# 🩺 debilitate

🔊 /dɪˈbɪlɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make weak or feeble; to impair the strength or vitality of
**中文：** v. 使衰弱；使虚弱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- weaken（使虚弱）
- enfeeble（使衰弱）
- incapacitate（使无能力）
- enervate（使无力）
- undermine（削弱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **debilitating disease / debilitating injury / debilitate the body / debilitating effect**

👉 Example: The prolonged illness debilitated the patient to the point where she could barely walk.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chronic autoimmune disorder gradually **debilitated** the young athlete, forcing her to withdraw from competitive sports entirely.
   这种慢性自身免疫疾病逐渐使这位年轻运动员衰弱，迫使她完全退出竞技体育。

2. Years of economic sanctions have **debilitated** the nation's infrastructure and severely limited access to essential medical supplies.
   多年的经济制裁已经削弱了该国的基础设施，并严重限制了基本医疗物资的获取。

3. The tropical fever can **debilitate** even the healthiest individuals, leaving them bedridden for weeks without proper treatment.
   这种热带热病甚至能使最健康的人虚弱不堪，若不适当治疗将卧床数周。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-ability-ate" — to remove someone's ability, making them weak and unable to function normally.
把"debilitate"想成"de-ability-ate"——去掉某人的能力，使其变得虚弱无法正常运作。
