# 🔢 integer

🔊 /ˈɪntɪdʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A whole number that is not a fraction; a complete entity or thing regarded as a unified whole
**中文：** n. 整数；整体；完整的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- whole number（整数）
- digit（数字）
- numeral（数字符号）
- whole（整体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **positive integer / negative integer / integer value / integer variable**

👉 Example: The function only accepts positive integers as valid input parameters in the algorithm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In mathematics, an **integer** includes all positive and negative whole numbers as well as zero on the number line.
   在数学中，整数包括数轴上所有的正整数、负整数以及零。

2. The programming language requires developers to declare whether a variable stores an **integer** or a floating-point number.
   该编程语言要求开发者声明变量存储的是整数还是浮点数。

3. The professor explained that dividing one **integer** by another does not always yield an **integer** as the result.
   教授解释说，一个整数除以另一个整数并不总是能得到一个整数作为结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "integer" as related to "integral" and "integrity" — all from Latin "integer" meaning "whole, complete." An integer is a whole number, not broken into fractions.
把"integer"想成与"integral（完整的）"和"integrity（完整性）"相关——都来自拉丁语"integer"，意为"完整的"。整数就是完整的数，没有被分成分数。
