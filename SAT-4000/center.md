# 🎯 center

🔊 /ˈsɛntər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The middle point or part of something; a place or group of buildings where a specified activity is concentrated (v.) To place in or at the center; to focus or concentrate (adj.) Of or at the center
**中文：** n. 中心；中央；圆心 v. 居中；集中 adj. 中央的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- middle（中间）
- core（核心）
- hub（枢纽）
- focus（焦点）
- nucleus（中心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **center of attention / center on / center around / research center / city center**

👉 Example: The debate centered on the ethical implications of emerging artificial intelligence technologies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new cultural **center** will serve as a hub for artistic expression and community engagement in the downtown district.
   新的文化中心将成为市中心地区艺术表达和社区参与的枢纽。

2. The professor urged students to **center** their analysis on primary sources rather than relying solely on secondary interpretations.
   教授敦促学生将分析集中在一手资料上，而非仅仅依赖二手解读。

3. Located at the geometric **center** of the campus, the library functions as the intellectual heart of the university.
   图书馆坐落于校园的几何中心，是大学的学术核心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "center" sharing its root with "central" — both point to the very middle, the focal point of everything around it.
想想"center"和"central（中央的）"同根——两者都指向正中间，即周围一切的焦点。
