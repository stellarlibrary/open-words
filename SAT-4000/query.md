# ❓ query

🔊 /ˈkwɪəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A question, especially one addressed to an official or organization. (v.) To ask a question or express doubt about something
**中文：** n. 疑问，质问；查询 v. 询问；对……表示疑问

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- question（问题）
- inquiry（询问）
- interrogation（质询）
- doubt（疑问）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **raise a query / query a decision / database query / query about**

👉 Example: The journalist raised a query about the accuracy of the government's official statistics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student submitted a **query** to the registrar's office regarding the discrepancy in her transcript records.
   该学生向教务处提交了一份关于其成绩单记录差异的查询。

2. Several board members chose to **query** the validity of the financial projections presented during the annual meeting.
   几位董事会成员选择质疑年会上提出的财务预测的有效性。

3. The database administrator optimized the search **query** to retrieve relevant records more efficiently from the system.
   数据库管理员优化了搜索查询，以便更高效地从系统中检索相关记录。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "query" as related to "quest" — both involve seeking answers, with a query being a specific question on your quest for knowledge.
把"query"想成与"quest（探索）"相关——两者都涉及寻找答案，query就是你在探索知识过程中提出的具体问题。
