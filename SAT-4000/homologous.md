# 🧬 homologous

🔊 /həˈmɒləɡəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having the same relation, relative position, or structure; corresponding in origin, structure, or function
**中文：** adj. 相应的；同源的；类似的；一致的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corresponding（对应的）
- analogous（类似的）
- equivalent（等同的）
- parallel（平行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **homologous structures / homologous pairs / homologous chromosomes / homologous series**

👉 Example: The bat's wing and the human arm are homologous structures derived from a common ancestor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Biologists identified **homologous** structures in the forelimbs of mammals, suggesting a shared evolutionary ancestor.
   生物学家在哺乳动物的前肢中发现了同源结构，表明它们有共同的进化祖先。

2. The two proteins were found to be **homologous**, sharing over ninety percent of their amino acid sequences.
   研究发现这两种蛋白质是同源的，它们的氨基酸序列有超过百分之九十的相似性。

3. During meiosis, **homologous** chromosomes pair up and exchange genetic material through a process called crossing over.
   在减数分裂过程中，同源染色体配对并通过称为交叉互换的过程交换遗传物质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homo-" (same) + "-logous" (relation) — things that share the same origin or correspond to each other structurally.
把"homologous"拆成"homo-"（相同）+"logous"（关系）——指在起源或结构上相互对应的事物。
