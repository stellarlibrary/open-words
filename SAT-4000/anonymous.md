# 🎭 anonymous

🔊 /əˈnɑːnɪməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not identified by name; of unknown authorship or origin; lacking distinctive features or individuality
**中文：** 匿名的；无名的；没有特色的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unnamed（未署名的）
- unidentified（身份不明的）
- nameless（无名的）
- incognito（隐姓埋名的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **anonymous donor / anonymous source / anonymous letter / remain anonymous / anonymous tip**

👉 Example: The journalist refused to reveal the identity of the anonymous source who provided the classified documents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. An **anonymous** benefactor donated ten million dollars to the university's scholarship fund without requesting any public recognition.
   一位匿名捐助者向该大学的奖学金基金捐赠了一千万美元，且未要求任何公开表彰。

2. The whistleblower chose to remain **anonymous** out of legitimate concern for personal safety and professional retaliation.
   举报人出于对人身安全和职业报复的合理担忧，选择保持匿名。

3. The police received an **anonymous** tip that ultimately led to the arrest of the suspect in the long-unsolved case.
   警方收到一个匿名举报，最终导致了这起长期未破案件中嫌疑人的逮捕。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "anonymous" as "a-no-nym-ous" where "nym" means name (like synonym, pseudonym). Anonymous literally means "without a name."
把"anonymous"想成"a-no-nym-ous"，其中"nym"表示名字（如synonym同义词、pseudonym假名）。Anonymous字面意思就是"没有名字的"。
