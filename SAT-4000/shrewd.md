# 🦊 shrewd

🔊 /ʃruːd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having sharp powers of judgment; astute and clever, especially in practical matters. (n.) A shrewd or clever person
**中文：** adj. 敏捷的，机灵的，精明的，伶俐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astute（精明的）
- perceptive（敏锐的）
- cunning（狡猾的）
- sagacious（睿智的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shrewd businessman / shrewd observer / shrewd decision / shrewd judgment**

👉 Example: The shrewd investor anticipated the market downturn and adjusted her portfolio accordingly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **shrewd** negotiator secured a deal that was far more favorable than anyone on the opposing side had anticipated.
   这位精明的谈判者达成了一项远比对方预期更为有利的协议。

2. Her **shrewd** observation about the statistical anomaly led the research team to uncover a fundamental flaw in the study.
   她对统计异常的敏锐观察使研究团队发现了该研究中的一个根本性缺陷。

3. A **shrewd** understanding of consumer behavior allowed the company to dominate the market despite fierce competition.
   对消费者行为的精明理解使该公司在激烈的竞争中占据了市场主导地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shrewd" as related to a "shrew" — the tiny animal that survives through quick, clever instincts despite its small size.
把"shrewd"和"shrew（鼩鼱）"联系起来——这种小动物虽然体型很小，却靠敏捷机智的本能生存下来。
