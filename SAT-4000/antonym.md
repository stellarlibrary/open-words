# 🔀 antonym

🔊 /ˈæntənɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A word that has the opposite meaning of another word; a term that expresses a concept contrary to that of another term in the same language.

**中文：** 反义词。与另一个词意义相反的词。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- opposite（反义词）
- converse（相反的词）
- contrary（相反）
- reverse（反面）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antonym of, antonym pair, direct antonym, find the antonym, antonym and synonym

**Example:** The word "generous" is an **antonym** of "stingy," as they express opposite qualities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students were instructed to identify the precise **antonym** for each vocabulary term listed on the examination sheet.
   学生们被要求找出试卷上所列每个词汇的准确反义词。

2. Understanding **antonyms** is essential for developing nuanced comprehension and effective use of the English language.
   理解反义词对于培养细致的理解力和有效地使用英语至关重要。

3. The linguist explained that certain words possess no true **antonym** because their meanings are not inherently binary.
   语言学家解释说，某些词没有真正的反义词，因为它们的含义本质上不是二元对立的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "ant-" (anti-, against) + "-onym" (name/word). An antonym is a word that is against or opposite to another word. Compare with "synonym" (syn- = same + -onym = word).

联想："ant-"（anti-，相反）+ "-onym"（名称/词）。antonym 就是与另一个词意思相反的词。对比 "synonym"（syn- = 相同 + -onym = 词，即同义词）。
