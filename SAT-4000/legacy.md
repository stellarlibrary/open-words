# 🏛️ legacy

🔊 /ˈlɛɡəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Money or property left to someone in a will; something handed down from an ancestor or predecessor; a lasting impact or contribution.
**中文：** n. 遗赠的财物，遗产；（前人留下的）遗产，影响

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inheritance（继承物）
- bequest（遗赠）
- heritage（遗产）
- endowment（捐赠）
- birthright（与生俱来的权利）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **leave a legacy / cultural legacy / legacy of war / legacy system / lasting legacy**

👉 Example: The philanthropist's generous legacy funded scholarships for underprivileged students for generations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned scientist left behind an intellectual **legacy** that continues to shape modern research in molecular biology.
   这位著名科学家留下的学术遗产继续影响着现代分子生物学的研究。

2. Her grandmother's **legacy** included a substantial trust fund and a collection of rare nineteenth-century first editions.
   她祖母的遗产包括一笔可观的信托基金和一批珍贵的十九世纪初版书籍。

3. The colonial **legacy** of that nation profoundly influenced its legal institutions, economic structures, and social hierarchies for decades.
   那个国家的殖民遗产在数十年间深刻影响了其法律制度、经济结构和社会等级。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "legacy" as "leg-a-cy" — something that has legs and keeps walking forward through generations, carrying the past into the future.
把"legacy"想成"leg-a-cy"——有"腿"的东西，能一代代地走下去，把过去带入未来。
