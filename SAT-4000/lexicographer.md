# 📚 lexicographer

🔊 /ˌlɛksɪˈkɒɡrəfər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who compiles, writes, or edits dictionaries; a specialist in lexicography
**中文：** n. 词典编纂者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dictionary compiler（词典编纂者）
- linguist（语言学家）
- etymologist（词源学家）
- philologist（语文学家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **noted lexicographer / work as a lexicographer / chief lexicographer / lexicographer of renown**

👉 Example: Samuel Johnson is perhaps the most celebrated lexicographer in the history of the English language.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **lexicographer** dedicated over two decades to compiling a comprehensive dictionary of regional dialects.
   这位著名的词典编纂者花了二十多年时间编纂一部全面的地方方言词典。

2. Modern **lexicographers** rely on vast digital corpora to track how word meanings evolve across different contexts.
   现代词典编纂者依靠庞大的数字语料库来追踪词义在不同语境中的演变。

3. The **lexicographer** argued that prescriptive approaches to language fail to capture how people actually use words daily.
   这位词典编纂者认为，规范性的语言方法无法反映人们在日常生活中实际使用词汇的方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lexicographer" as "lexicon + grapher" — a person who writes (graphs) the lexicon (word collection), i.e., a dictionary maker.
把"lexicographer"拆分为"lexicon（词汇）+ grapher（书写者）"——书写词汇集的人，即词典编纂者。
