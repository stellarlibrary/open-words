# 🤷 fallible

🔊 /ˈfæləbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of making mistakes or being wrong; liable to error or inaccuracy
**中文：** adj. 易犯错误的；不可靠的；可能犯错的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imperfect（不完美的）
- errant（易犯错的）
- unreliable（不可靠的）
- flawed（有缺陷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fallible human / fallible judgment / fallible memory / fallible system**

👉 Example: Even the most experienced scientists acknowledge that their methods are fallible.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge reminded the jury that eyewitness testimony is **fallible** and should be weighed carefully against corroborating evidence.
   法官提醒陪审团，目击者证词是不可靠的，应与佐证证据仔细权衡。

2. All technological systems are inherently **fallible**, which is precisely why redundant safety mechanisms must be implemented.
   所有技术系统本质上都是易出错的，这正是必须实施冗余安全机制的原因。

3. Recognizing that human memory is **fallible**, the researchers employed rigorous documentation protocols throughout the clinical trial.
   鉴于人类记忆容易出错，研究人员在整个临床试验中采用了严格的记录规程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fall-ible" — someone who is able to "fall" into mistakes, just as we all stumble from time to time.
把"fallible"想成"fall（跌倒）+ ible（能够的）"——能够跌倒犯错的，就像我们每个人都会时不时犯错一样。
