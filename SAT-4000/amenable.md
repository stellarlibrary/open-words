# 🤝 amenable

🔊 /əˈmiːnəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Willing to comply or agree; open to suggestion or influence; answerable or accountable.
**中文：** 顺从的，服从的；有义务的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compliant（顺从的）
- agreeable（乐意的）
- cooperative（合作的）
- receptive（善于接受的）
- tractable（温顺的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amenable to + noun/gerund (suggestions, change, reason, persuasion)**
👉 Example: The committee members were amenable to revising the original proposal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new director proved remarkably **amenable** to incorporating feedback from junior staff members.
   新主管非常乐于采纳初级员工的反馈意见。

2. Citizens who are **amenable** to civic engagement tend to contribute positively to democratic governance.
   愿意参与公民事务的市民往往能对民主治理做出积极贡献。

3. The defendant was **amenable** to the jurisdiction of the federal court throughout the legal proceedings.
   在整个法律程序中，被告服从联邦法院的管辖权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amenable" sounds like "a MEN able" — imagine a group of able men who are always willing to help and agree.
"Amenable"听起来像"a MEN able"——想象一群有能力的人总是愿意帮忙和同意。
