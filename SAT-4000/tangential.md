# 🔀 tangential

🔊 /tænˈdʒenʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or moving along a tangent; merely touching or slightly connected; diverging from a main topic. (n.) A tangent or tangent line
**中文：** adj. 切线的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- peripheral（外围的）
- digressive（离题的）
- irrelevant（不相关的）
- incidental（附带的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tangential relationship / tangential remark / tangential to the issue / tangential force**

👉 Example: His comments were tangential to the discussion and did not contribute to the main argument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The reviewer criticized the author for including **tangential** details that distracted readers from the central narrative.
   评论家批评作者加入了离题的细节，分散了读者对核心叙事的注意力。

2. While her research was only **tangential** to the primary investigation, it provided unexpected and valuable insights.
   虽然她的研究与主要调查仅有间接关系，但提供了意想不到的宝贵见解。

3. The committee dismissed the **tangential** objections and focused exclusively on the substantive policy proposals before them.
   委员会驳回了那些离题的反对意见，专注于审议面前的实质性政策提案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tangential" as "tangent + ial" — like a tangent line that barely touches the circle, tangential remarks barely touch the main point.
把"tangential"想成"tangent + ial"——就像切线只轻轻触碰圆一样，tangential的言论也只是轻轻擦过主题。
