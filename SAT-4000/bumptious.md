# 😤 bumptious

🔊 /ˈbʌmpʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Self-assertive or proud to an annoying degree; offensively conceited or presumptuous
**中文：** adj. 傲慢的；盲目自大的；瞎自夸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arrogant（傲慢的）
- conceited（自负的）
- presumptuous（专横的）
- pompous（自大的）
- overbearing（专横的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bumptious attitude / bumptious manner / bumptious official / bumptious young man**

👉 Example: His bumptious manner at the meeting alienated colleagues who might otherwise have supported his proposal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bumptious** new manager immediately began issuing directives without consulting any of the experienced senior staff members.
   那位傲慢的新经理立即开始下达指令，完全没有征询任何经验丰富的高级员工。

2. Her **bumptious** behavior at the academic conference drew sharp criticism from several distinguished scholars in the audience.
   她在学术会议上的自大行为引起了在场几位著名学者的尖锐批评。

3. The **bumptious** diplomat offended his foreign counterparts by dismissing their proposals without offering any reasoned justification.
   那位傲慢的外交官不加任何合理解释就否定了外国同行的提案，令对方十分不满。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bump" + "-tious" — someone who bumps into others rudely, pushing ahead with arrogant self-importance.
把"bumptious"想成"bump（碰撞）+ -tious"——一个粗鲁地碰撞别人、傲慢自大地往前挤的人。
