# 💨 aerate

🔊 /ˈɛr.eɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To introduce air or gas into a substance, especially a liquid or soil.
**中文：** 充气；让空气进入；使通气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ventilate（通风）
- oxygenate（充氧）
- freshen（使清新）
- air out（通风换气）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aerate + the soil / the water / the lawn / the wine**

👉 Example: Gardeners aerate the soil to promote healthy root growth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Landscapers recommend that homeowners **aerate** their lawns annually to improve nutrient absorption.
   园艺师建议房主每年给草坪通气以改善营养吸收。

2. The winemaker carefully **aerated** the vintage to enhance its complex flavor profile before serving.
   酿酒师在上酒前仔细地给这瓶陈酿充气，以增强其复杂的风味。

3. Aquatic systems must **aerate** the water continuously to maintain adequate oxygen levels for marine life.
   水产系统必须持续给水充气，以维持海洋生物所需的充足氧气水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aerate" starts with "aer-" from Latin "aer" (air) — to aerate is simply to add air into something.
联想记忆：aerate 以 aer-（空气）开头，aerate 就是"往里面加空气"，即充气、通气。
