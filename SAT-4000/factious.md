# 💥 factious

🔊 /ˈfækʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Inclined to cause internal dissension or form factions; promoting disorder or conflict within a group
**中文：** 好捣乱的；好搞派系的；闹派别的；制造不和的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- divisive（引起分裂的）
- seditious（煽动性的）
- contentious（好争论的）
- disruptive（破坏性的）
- mutinous（叛变的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **factious behavior / factious members / factious elements / factious disputes**

👉 Example: The factious members of the council repeatedly disrupted proceedings with heated personal attacks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee chair struggled to maintain order as **factious** delegates launched bitter attacks against one another over policy disagreements.
   委员会主席努力维持秩序，因为好斗的代表们因政策分歧而互相发动激烈攻击。

2. Historians note that **factious** rivalries among the nobility ultimately weakened the kingdom and left it vulnerable to foreign invasion.
   历史学家指出，贵族之间的派系争斗最终削弱了王国，使其容易受到外国入侵。

3. The organization expelled several **factious** members whose persistent agitation threatened to undermine the unity of the entire coalition.
   该组织开除了几名好搞派系的成员，他们持续不断的煽动活动威胁到了整个联盟的团结。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "factious" as "faction" + "-ous" — someone factious is full of faction-making tendencies, always stirring up divisions.
把"factious"想成"faction"(派系) + "-ous"(充满的)——一个factious的人充满了搞派系的倾向，总是在制造分裂。
