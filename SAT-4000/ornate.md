# 🏛️ ornate

🔊 /ɔːrˈneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Made in an intricate shape or decorated with complex patterns; elaborately adorned; (of literary style) using unusual words and complex constructions
**中文：** adj. 装饰的，华丽的；绚丽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elaborate（精心制作的）
- lavish（奢华的）
- embellished（装饰过的）
- florid（华丽的）
- ostentatious（炫耀的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ornate decoration / ornate style / ornate architecture / ornate prose / ornate design**

👉 Example: The cathedral's ornate ceiling depicted biblical scenes in extraordinary detail and vivid color.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The palace featured **ornate** carvings and gilded moldings that reflected the extravagant tastes of the eighteenth-century aristocracy.
   宫殿以华丽的雕刻和镀金线脚为特色，反映了十八世纪贵族奢华的品味。

2. Many literary scholars consider his **ornate** prose style excessively complex, making it challenging for modern readers to appreciate fully.
   许多文学学者认为他绚丽的散文风格过于复杂，使现代读者难以充分欣赏。

3. The jeweler crafted an **ornate** necklace adorned with sapphires and diamonds, which was later auctioned for a considerable sum.
   珠宝商制作了一条镶嵌蓝宝石和钻石的华丽项链，后来被拍卖出了可观的价格。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ornate" as "orn-ate" — it comes from "ornament" and means something that has been heavily "ornamented" or decorated to an elaborate degree.
把"ornate"想成"orn-ate"——它来自"ornament"（装饰品），意味着某物被过度"装饰"到精致华丽的程度。
