# 🌀 irrational

🔊 /ɪˈræʃənl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not logical or reasonable; lacking sound judgment. (n.) An irrational number, a number that cannot be expressed as a simple fraction
**中文：** adj. 不合理的；无理性的；荒谬的 n. 无理数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unreasonable（不合理的）
- illogical（不合逻辑的）
- absurd（荒谬的）
- senseless（无意义的）
- preposterous（荒唐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irrational fear / irrational behavior / irrational belief / irrational number**

👉 Example: His irrational fear of public speaking prevented him from presenting his groundbreaking research at the conference.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economist argued that **irrational** consumer behavior during market panics often exacerbates the severity of financial crises.
   经济学家认为，市场恐慌期间消费者的非理性行为往往加剧了金融危机的严重性。

2. It would be entirely **irrational** to abandon a well-supported hypothesis based solely on a single inconclusive experimental trial.
   仅凭一次不确定的实验就放弃一个有充分支持的假设是完全不合理的。

3. Mathematicians proved that the square root of two is an **irrational** number that cannot be expressed as a finite decimal.
   数学家证明了二的平方根是一个无法表示为有限小数的无理数。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irrational" as "ir-rational" — the prefix "ir-" means "not," so irrational literally means "not rational" or without reason.
把"irrational"想成"ir-rational"——前缀"ir-"意为"不"，所以irrational字面意思是"不理性的"或没有道理的。
