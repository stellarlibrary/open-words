# 💪 resilience

🔊 /rɪˈzɪliəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability to recover quickly from difficulties or setbacks; the capacity of a material to spring back into shape
**中文：** n. 弹回，有弹力，恢复力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- toughness（韧性）
- elasticity（弹性）
- adaptability（适应力）
- fortitude（坚韧）
- perseverance（毅力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show / demonstrate / build resilience / resilience in the face of adversity / emotional resilience**

👉 Example: The community showed remarkable resilience in rebuilding their homes after the devastating earthquake.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Psychologists have long studied the factors that contribute to emotional **resilience** in children who grow up in challenging environments.
   心理学家长期以来一直在研究促使在困难环境中成长的儿童具备情感恢复力的因素。

2. The **resilience** of the local economy surprised analysts who had predicted a prolonged period of stagnation following the crisis.
   当地经济的恢复力令分析师们感到意外，他们曾预测危机后会出现长期停滞。

3. Engineers tested the **resilience** of the new polymer material by subjecting it to extreme temperatures and repeated mechanical stress.
   工程师们通过将新型聚合物材料置于极端温度和反复机械应力下来测试其弹性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "resilience" as "re-silence" — after all the noise and chaos, you find silence again, bouncing back to peace.
把"resilience"想成"re-silence"——在所有的喧嚣和混乱之后，你重新找回了安静，恢复了平静。
