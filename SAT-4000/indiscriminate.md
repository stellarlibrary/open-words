# 🎯 indiscriminate

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Done at random or without careful judgment; not making distinctions; lacking selectivity or discrimination
**中文：** adj. 任意的；无差别的；不加分析的；不加选择的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- random（随意的）
- haphazard（偶然的）
- wholesale（大规模的）
- unselective（不加选择的）
- sweeping（笼统的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indiscriminate use of / indiscriminate attack / indiscriminate killing / indiscriminate in**

👉 Example: The indiscriminate use of pesticides has severely damaged local ecosystems and contaminated water supplies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. International law prohibits the **indiscriminate** bombing of civilian areas during armed conflicts between sovereign nations.
   国际法禁止在主权国家之间的武装冲突中对平民地区进行无差别轰炸。

2. The **indiscriminate** application of antibiotics in livestock farming has accelerated the emergence of drug-resistant bacterial strains.
   在畜牧业中不加选择地使用抗生素加速了耐药细菌菌株的出现。

3. Critics condemned the corporation's **indiscriminate** harvesting of old-growth forests without regard for environmental or ecological consequences.
   批评者谴责该公司不加区分地砍伐原始森林，完全不顾环境和生态后果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-discriminate" — not discriminating — someone who does not discriminate treats everything the same, without careful selection.
把"indiscriminate"想成"in-discriminate"——不加区分——一个不加区分的人对一切都同等对待，没有仔细选择。
