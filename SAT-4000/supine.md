# 😴 supine

🔊 /suːˈpaɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lying face upward; showing a lazy or passive lack of resistance; with the palm upward
**中文：** adj. 仰卧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recumbent（斜躺的）
- prostrate（俯卧的）
- passive（被动的）
- indolent（懒散的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supine position / lie supine / supine attitude / supine acceptance**

👉 Example: The patient was instructed to remain in a supine position during the examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor asked the patient to lie **supine** on the examination table so that she could assess the abdominal area.
   医生要求病人仰卧在检查台上，以便她能评估腹部区域。

2. The editorial criticized the legislature for its **supine** acceptance of policies that clearly undermined public welfare.
   社论批评立法机关懒散地接受了明显损害公共福利的政策。

3. Archaeologists discovered the ancient remains arranged in a **supine** posture, suggesting specific burial customs of that civilization.
   考古学家发现古代遗骸呈仰卧姿势排列，表明该文明有特定的丧葬习俗。

把"supine"想成"su-PINE"——想象仰卧在一棵松树下，懒洋洋地仰望天空。
