# 💅 primp

🔊 /prɪmp/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To spend time dressing or grooming oneself with elaborate care; to make minor adjustments to one's appearance
**中文：** v. 精心打扮；装饰；细心打扮；过分装饰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- preen（精心打扮）
- groom（梳妆）
- adorn（装饰）
- spruce up（打扮整齐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **primp in front of the mirror / primp for hours / primp and preen / primp oneself**

👉 Example: She spent nearly an hour primping in front of the mirror before the formal reception began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The actress spent hours **primping** in her dressing room before stepping onto the stage for the opening night performance.
   这位女演员在首演之夜登台前，在化妆间里花了数小时精心打扮。

2. He was known to **primp** meticulously before every business meeting, ensuring that every detail of his appearance was flawless.
   他以在每次商务会议前精心打扮而闻名，确保自己外表的每个细节都无可挑剔。

3. The teenagers gathered in the restroom to **primp** and adjust their outfits before the highly anticipated school dance began.
   青少年们聚集在洗手间里精心打扮并调整着装，等待万众期待的校园舞会开始。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "primp" as "prim" + "p" — to make yourself prim and proper, fussing over every little detail of your appearance.
把"primp"想成"prim（整洁的）"+"p"——让自己变得整洁得体，对外表的每个小细节都精心打理。
