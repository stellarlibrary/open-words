# 🎭 artifice

🔊 /ˈɑːrtɪfɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Clever or cunning devices or expedients, especially as used to trick or deceive others; trickery or ingenuity.

**中文：** 诡计；欺骗；巧妙的办法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- trickery 诡计
- cunning 狡猾
- stratagem 策略
- deception 欺骗
- ruse 计谋

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Artifice" carries a dual meaning: it can refer to clever skill or ingenuity, but more often implies deceit or cunning manipulation. Context determines whether the connotation is positive or negative.

"artifice" 有双重含义：可以指巧妙的技巧或创造力，但更常暗示欺骗或狡猾的操纵。语境决定其褒贬色彩。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician relied on **artifice** rather than genuine policy to win the voters' support.
   这位政客依靠诡计而非真正的政策来赢得选民的支持。

2. Behind her gracious smile lay an elaborate **artifice** designed to conceal her true intentions completely.
   在她优雅的微笑背后隐藏着一个精心设计的骗局，旨在完全掩饰她的真实意图。

3. The magician's greatest **artifice** was making the entire audience believe the impossible had truly occurred.
   这位魔术师最高超的技巧是让全场观众相信不可能的事真的发生了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "arti-" (artificial) + "-fice" (make) — something artificially made up, like a trick or scheme.

将 "artifice" 联想为 "arti-"（人造的）+ "-fice"（制造）——人为编造的东西，即诡计或骗术。
