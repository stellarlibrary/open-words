# 🔥 sear

🔊 /sɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective, noun | 动词, 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To burn or scorch with intense heat; to cause to wither. (adj.) Dried up; withered. (n.) A brand or mark left by burning
**中文：** v. 烤焦，使枯萎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scorch（烧焦）
- char（烧焦）
- wither（枯萎）
- parch（烤干）
- singe（烧灼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sear the meat / seared into memory / searing heat / searing pain**

👉 Example: The chef used a cast-iron skillet to sear the steak at extremely high temperature for a perfect crust.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unprecedented drought continued to **sear** the agricultural landscape, devastating crops and threatening the region's food supply.
   史无前例的干旱继续烤焦农业用地，摧毁庄稼并威胁该地区的粮食供应。

2. The traumatic images of the disaster were permanently **seared** into the memories of the rescue workers who responded.
   灾难的惨烈画面永久地烙印在前来救援的工作人员的记忆中。

3. Intense ultraviolet radiation from prolonged sun exposure can **sear** unprotected skin and significantly increase the risk of melanoma.
   长时间暴露在阳光下的强烈紫外线辐射会灼伤未受保护的皮肤，并显著增加黑色素瘤的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sear" as sounding like "seer" — a seer gazes into fire, and fire sears everything it touches, leaving burns and withered remains.
把"sear"联想到发音类似的"seer"（预言家）——预言家凝视火焰，而火焰会烤焦它接触到的一切，留下灼烧和枯萎的痕迹。
