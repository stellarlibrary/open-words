# ⚠️ negligent

🔊 /ˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Failing to take proper care; characterized by a lack of attention or caution in fulfilling one's duties
**中文：** adj. 疏忽的；粗心大意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- careless（粗心的）
- inattentive（不注意的）
- remiss（怠慢的）
- lax（松懈的）
- derelict（失职的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **negligent behavior / negligent in duties / negligent driver / grossly negligent**

👉 Example: The supervisor was found negligent in enforcing workplace safety regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation concluded that the engineer had been **negligent** in conducting routine inspections of the structural components.
   调查得出结论，该工程师在对结构部件进行例行检查时存在疏忽。

2. Parents who are persistently **negligent** in providing adequate care for their children may face serious legal consequences.
   持续疏忽未给子女提供足够照顾的父母可能面临严重的法律后果。

3. The auditor identified several instances where management had been **negligent** in adhering to established financial protocols.
   审计员发现了管理层在遵守既定财务规程方面存在疏忽的多个实例。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "negligent" as "neglect" + "ent" — a negligent person is one who neglects what they should be doing.
把"negligent"想成"neglect（忽视）"+"ent"——疏忽的人就是忽视自己应该做的事的人。
