# 🏟️ amphitheater

🔊 /ˈæmfɪˌθiːətər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An open circular or oval building with a central space surrounded by tiers of seats, used for spectacles or performances.
**中文：** 竞技场；圆形露天剧场

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arena（竞技场）
- coliseum（大型竞技场）
- stadium（体育场）
- theater（剧场）
- auditorium（礼堂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Roman amphitheater, outdoor amphitheater, ancient amphitheater, natural amphitheater**
👉 Example: The ancient amphitheater could accommodate over twenty thousand spectators.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Roman **amphitheater** at Verona still hosts magnificent opera performances during the summer festival season.
   维罗纳的罗马圆形剧场在夏季音乐节期间仍然举办壮观的歌剧演出。

2. Archaeologists unearthed the remains of a Greek **amphitheater** that had been buried beneath volcanic ash for centuries.
   考古学家发掘出了一座被火山灰掩埋了几个世纪的希腊圆形剧场遗迹。

3. The university constructed a modern **amphitheater** on campus for outdoor lectures and cultural performances throughout the year.
   大学在校园内建造了一座现代露天剧场，供全年举办户外讲座和文化演出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amphi-" (around) + "theater" = a theater built all around a central stage, forming a circular venue.
"Amphi-"（围绕）+ "theater"（剧场）= 围绕中央舞台建造的剧场，形成圆形场地。
