# 💎 lavish

🔊 /ˈlævɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely generous or extravagant; given in abundance. (v.) To give or bestow in generous or extravagant quantities
**中文：** adj. 非常大方的；过分丰富的；浪费的 v. 浪费；慷慨给予

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extravagant（奢侈的）
- opulent（豪华的）
- profuse（丰富的）
- generous（慷慨的）
- sumptuous（奢华的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lavish praise on / lavish attention on / lavish lifestyle / lavish party / lavish gifts**

👉 Example: The host lavished attention on every guest, ensuring that each person felt welcome and valued.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The billionaire hosted a **lavish** gala at his estate, complete with gourmet cuisine and world-renowned entertainment.
   这位亿万富翁在他的庄园举办了一场奢华的晚会，配备了美食佳肴和世界知名的娱乐表演。

2. Critics argued that the government should not **lavish** taxpayer funds on programs that produce no measurable results.
   批评者认为政府不应该将纳税人的资金浪费在没有产生可衡量成效的项目上。

3. The mentor **lavished** encouragement upon her students, inspiring them to pursue ambitious academic and professional goals.
   这位导师慷慨地给予学生们鼓励，激励他们追求远大的学术和职业目标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lavish" as "lav-ish" — like a "lava-ish" flow of abundance, pouring out generously like volcanic lava overflows.
把"lavish"想成"lav-ish"——像"lava（岩浆）"般涌出的丰盛，像火山岩浆般慷慨地倾泻而出。
