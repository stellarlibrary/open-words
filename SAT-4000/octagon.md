# 🛑 octagon

🔊 /ˈɑːktəɡɑːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A polygon with eight sides and eight angles
**中文：** n. 八边形，八角形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eight-sided figure（八边形）
- polygon（多边形）
- octangle（八角形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regular octagon / shaped like an octagon / octagon ring / stop sign octagon**

👉 Example: The architect incorporated an octagon into the building's floor plan to create a unique central atrium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The standard stop sign is designed in the shape of a regular **octagon** to ensure universal recognition by drivers.
   标准的停车标志被设计成正八边形，以确保驾驶员能普遍识别。

2. The mathematician demonstrated how to calculate the interior angles of an **octagon** using a simple geometric formula.
   这位数学家演示了如何用一个简单的几何公式来计算八边形的内角。

3. The ancient fortress was built in the form of an **octagon**, providing strategic defensive advantages from all directions.
   这座古老的堡垒被建成八边形，从各个方向提供了战略防御优势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "octa-gon" — "octa" means eight (like an octopus has eight arms), and "gon" means angle, so an octagon has eight angles.
把"octagon"想成"octa-gon"——"octa"表示八（就像章鱼有八条腿），"gon"表示角，所以octagon就是八角形。
