# 🤸 supple

🔊 /ˈsʌpəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Bending easily without breaking; flexible; compliant. (v.) To make soft or flexible; to make compliant
**中文：** v. 使柔软，使顺从

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flexible（柔韧的）
- pliable（易弯曲的）
- limber（柔软的）
- resilient（有弹性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supple leather / supple body / supple mind / supple movements**

👉 Example: Years of yoga practice had made her body remarkably supple and agile.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artisan selected only the most **supple** leather to craft handbags that would be both durable and elegant.
   工匠只挑选最柔软的皮革来制作既耐用又优雅的手提包。

2. A truly effective leader must maintain a **supple** mind, adapting strategies swiftly to meet evolving challenges.
   一个真正有效的领导者必须保持灵活的头脑，迅速调整策略以应对不断变化的挑战。

3. The dancer's **supple** movements across the stage captivated the audience and earned unanimous praise from the judges.
   舞者在舞台上柔软的动作吸引了观众，并赢得了评委的一致好评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "supple" as "SUP + ple(ase)" — when you are supple, you can bend to please, moving flexibly in any direction.
把"supple"想成"SUP + ple(ase)"——当你是柔软的，你可以弯曲以取悦，灵活地向任何方向移动。
