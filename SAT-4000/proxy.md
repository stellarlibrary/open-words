# 🤝 proxy

🔊 /ˈprɑːksi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person authorized to act on behalf of another; a document granting such authority; a substitute or stand-in
**中文：** n. 代理人；委托书；代用品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- representative（代表）
- delegate（代表人）
- surrogate（替代者）
- substitute（替代品）
- agent（代理人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **by proxy / proxy vote / proxy server / act as a proxy / proxy for something**

👉 Example: Shareholders who cannot attend the meeting may vote by proxy through an authorized representative.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board allowed absent members to submit their decisions by **proxy**, ensuring that every voice was represented in the vote.
   董事会允许缺席成员通过代理人提交决定，确保每个人的意见都在投票中得到体现。

2. Economists often use consumer spending as a **proxy** for measuring the overall health and vitality of a national economy.
   经济学家经常使用消费者支出作为衡量国民经济整体健康和活力的替代指标。

3. In the absence of direct evidence, researchers relied on tree ring data as a **proxy** for historical climate conditions.
   在缺乏直接证据的情况下，研究人员依赖树木年轮数据作为历史气候条件的替代指标。

---

把"proxy"想成"pro + X"——一个标有X的专业替身，代替你行事的人。
