# 🗣️ patter

🔊 /ˈpætər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Rapid continuous talk; the sound of quick light taps. (v.) To talk rapidly or glibly; to make a series of quick light tapping sounds
**中文：** n. 行话；轻快脚步声；急速拍打声 v. 喋喋不休；急速地说；发出急速轻拍声

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chatter（喋喋不休）
- prattle（唠叨）
- babble（絮叨）
- tap（轻拍）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **patter of rain / patter of footsteps / sales patter / patter on the roof / patter away**

👉 Example: The gentle patter of rain on the windowpane created a soothing atmosphere for reading.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The salesman launched into his well-rehearsed **patter** about the product's unique features before the customer could raise any objections.
   推销员在顾客提出任何异议之前，就开始了他精心排练的关于产品独特功能的滔滔不绝的推销话术。

2. The soft **patter** of raindrops against the library windows provided a calming backdrop for the students preparing for final examinations.
   雨滴轻柔地拍打图书馆窗户的声音，为准备期末考试的学生们提供了宁静的背景音。

3. Small feet began to **patter** across the wooden floor as the children rushed excitedly toward the elaborately decorated holiday tree.
   当孩子们兴奋地冲向精心装饰的节日树时，小脚丫开始在木地板上发出轻快的脚步声。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "patter" as the "pat-pat-pat" sound — like rain pattering on a roof or feet pattering on the floor.
把"patter"想成"pat-pat-pat"的声音——就像雨滴拍打屋顶或脚步轻拍地板的声音。
