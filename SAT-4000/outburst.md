# 🌋 outburst

🔊 /ˈaʊtbɜːrst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sudden and violent release of emotion, energy, or physical force; an eruption
**中文：** n. （火山、情感等的）爆发；破裂；突发

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eruption（爆发）
- explosion（爆发）
- flare-up（突然发作）
- surge（涌发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outburst of anger / outburst of emotion / outburst of violence / emotional outburst**

👉 Example: His sudden outburst of anger during the meeting shocked everyone in the conference room.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's emotional **outburst** during the debate drew widespread media attention and public scrutiny.
   这位参议员在辩论中的情绪爆发引起了媒体的广泛关注和公众的审视。

2. Scientists recorded an unprecedented **outburst** of seismic activity near the volcanic region last month.
   科学家们上个月在火山地区附近记录到了一次前所未有的地震活动爆发。

3. The teacher's patience finally gave way to an **outburst** of frustration after repeated disruptions in class.
   在课堂上多次被打断后，老师的耐心终于让位于一阵沮丧的爆发。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out + burst" — something that bursts outward, like a volcano erupting or emotions breaking free.
把"outburst"拆成"out（向外）+ burst（爆裂）"——某种东西向外爆裂，就像火山喷发或情绪释放。
