# 😇 beatific

🔊 /ˌbiːəˈtɪfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Imparting or expressing supreme bliss, happiness, or blessedness; having a saintly or divinely serene quality
**中文：** adj. 幸福的；祝福的；快乐的；极乐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blissful（极乐的）
- serene（宁静的）
- rapturous（狂喜的）
- angelic（天使般的）
- ecstatic（欣喜若狂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beatific smile / beatific expression / beatific vision / beatific glow / beatific calm**

👉 Example: The monk sat in meditation with a beatific smile that radiated profound inner peace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The portrait captured the saint's **beatific** expression, conveying an otherworldly sense of peace and spiritual transcendence.
   这幅肖像画捕捉了圣人极乐的表情，传达出一种超凡脱俗的宁静和精神超越感。

2. After years of spiritual practice, the elderly nun maintained a **beatific** calm that deeply impressed all who encountered her.
   经过多年的精神修行，这位年迈的修女保持着一种极乐的平静，深深打动了所有遇到她的人。

3. The audience watched in silence as the pianist played with a **beatific** serenity that transformed the concert hall entirely.
   观众静静地看着钢琴家以一种极乐的安详演奏，完全改变了音乐厅的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "beatific" as "bea-TIFIC" — like "beautiful" + "terrific" combined, describing something so wonderfully blissful it seems divine.
把"beatific"想成"bea-TIFIC"——像"beautiful"（美丽的）和"terrific"（极好的）的结合，形容某种美好至极、如神圣般的幸福。
