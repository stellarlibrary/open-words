# 🌾 starch

🔊 /stɑːrtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A white, odorless carbohydrate found in foods; stiffness or formality in manner. (v.) To stiffen fabric with starch
**中文：** n. 淀粉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carbohydrate（碳水化合物）
- rigidity（刻板）
- formality（生硬）
- stiffen（使变硬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **starch a shirt / starch content / remove the starch / full of starch**

👉 Example: The laundry service will starch your dress shirts to give them a crisp, professional appearance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nutritionist advised her patients to reduce their intake of **starch** in order to maintain balanced blood sugar levels.
   营养师建议她的患者减少淀粉摄入量，以维持血糖水平的平衡。

2. His manner carried a certain **starch** that made casual conversation with colleagues feel awkward and overly formal.
   他的举止带有一种刻板，使得与同事的随意交谈显得尴尬而过于正式。

3. Before the diplomatic reception, the valet carefully **starched** the ambassador's collar to ensure an immaculate presentation.
   在外交招待会之前，男仆仔细地给大使的衣领上浆，以确保外表无可挑剔。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "starch" as "star + ch" — a star chef always uses starch to make food thick and clothing stiff.
把"starch"想成"star + ch"——明星厨师总是用淀粉让食物变稠，用浆水让衣服变挺。
