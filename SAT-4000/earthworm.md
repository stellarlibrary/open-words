# 🪱 earthworm

🔊 /ˈɜːrθwɜːrm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A burrowing annelid worm that lives in soil, important for aerating and enriching the earth through its tunneling activity
**中文：** n. 蚯蚓

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nightcrawler（夜行蚯蚓）
- angleworm（钓饵蚯蚓）
- annelid（环节动物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **earthworm population / earthworm activity / earthworm castings / earthworm species**

👉 Example: Earthworm activity is a reliable indicator of healthy, nutrient-rich soil in agricultural fields.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biologist studied **earthworm** populations across different soil types to evaluate the overall health of the ecosystem.
   生物学家研究了不同土壤类型中的蚯蚓种群，以评估生态系统的整体健康状况。

2. **Earthworm** castings serve as a natural and highly effective fertilizer that enriches garden soil with essential nutrients.
   蚯蚓粪便是一种天然且高效的肥料，能为花园土壤补充必需的营养物质。

3. Agricultural researchers confirmed that **earthworm** activity significantly improves soil aeration and facilitates water drainage in farmland.
   农业研究人员证实，蚯蚓的活动显著改善了农田的土壤通气性并促进了水分排放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "earth-worm" literally — it is a worm that lives in the earth, tunneling through soil all day long.
把"earthworm"直接拆分——"earth（土地）"+"worm（虫）"，就是生活在泥土里的虫子。
