# 💤 dormancy

🔊 /ˈdɔːrmənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A state of biological rest or inactivity in which growth and development are temporarily suspended
**中文：** n. 休眠；冬眠；休眠状态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hibernation（冬眠）
- inactivity（不活动）
- latency（潜伏）
- quiescence（静止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **period of dormancy / enter dormancy / break dormancy / winter dormancy**

👉 Example: Many plant seeds require a period of dormancy before they can germinate in spring.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The volcano had been in a state of **dormancy** for centuries before scientists detected renewed seismic activity.
   这座火山在科学家检测到新的地震活动之前已处于休眠状态数百年。

2. Certain bacterial spores can survive prolonged **dormancy** and reactivate when environmental conditions become favorable again.
   某些细菌孢子能够在长期休眠后存活，并在环境条件再次有利时重新激活。

3. The economic **dormancy** of the region persisted until new infrastructure investments stimulated growth and development.
   该地区的经济沉寂一直持续到新的基础设施投资刺激了增长和发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dormancy" as related to "dormitory" — a place where people sleep, just as dormancy is a state of sleep.
把"dormancy"联想到"dormitory（宿舍）"——一个人们睡觉的地方，正如dormancy是一种休眠状态。
