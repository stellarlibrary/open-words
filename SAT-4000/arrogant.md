# 👑 arrogant

🔊 /ˈærəɡənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having an exaggerated sense of one's own importance or abilities; overbearingly proud and disdainful
**中文：** adj. 自大的，傲慢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- haughty（高傲的）
- conceited（自负的）
- pompous（自大的）
- supercilious（目中无人的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arrogant attitude / arrogant behavior / arrogant tone / arrogant assumption**

👉 Example: His arrogant demeanor alienated many of his colleagues and subordinates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manager's **arrogant** refusal to consider alternative viewpoints ultimately led to the failure of the entire project.
   经理傲慢地拒绝考虑其他观点，最终导致了整个项目的失败。

2. Despite her remarkable achievements, she remained humble and never displayed an **arrogant** attitude toward her peers.
   尽管她取得了卓越的成就，她始终保持谦逊，从未对同事表现出傲慢的态度。

3. The diplomat's **arrogant** remarks during the summit strained relations between the two nations for several months afterward.
   这位外交官在峰会上的傲慢言论使两国关系在之后数月内持续紧张。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arrogant" containing "arrow" — an arrogant person acts as though they are a sharp arrow, above everyone else and always pointing upward.
"arrogant"中有"arrow（箭）"——傲慢的人表现得像一支尖箭，总是高高在上，目中无人。
