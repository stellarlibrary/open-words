# 👑 viceroy

🔊 /ˈvaɪsrɔɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A ruler exercising authority in a colony on behalf of a sovereign; also a North American butterfly resembling the monarch
**中文：** n. 总督；（北美）一种黑色蝴蝶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- governor（总督）
- regent（摄政者）
- proconsul（地方总督）
- administrator（行政长官）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **viceroy of a colony / appointed as viceroy / serve as viceroy / the viceroy's decree**

👉 Example: The viceroy governed the distant colony with absolute authority granted by the crown.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **viceroy** administered the colonial territory for decades, implementing policies that reflected the interests of the empire.
   总督管理殖民地领土数十年，实施了反映帝国利益的政策。

2. Upon his appointment as **viceroy**, he pledged to maintain order and promote economic development throughout the province.
   被任命为总督后，他承诺在整个省份维持秩序并促进经济发展。

3. The **viceroy** butterfly has evolved to mimic the monarch's coloring as a remarkable defense against predators.
   总督蝴蝶进化出模仿帝王蝶颜色的能力，作为一种卓越的防御手段来对抗捕食者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vice-roy" as "vice" (deputy) + "roy" (king, from French "roi") — a deputy king who rules on behalf of the real monarch.
把"viceroy"想成"vice"（副）+"roy"（国王，源自法语roi）——代替国王统治的副王，即总督。
