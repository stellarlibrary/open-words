# 🦁 ferocious

🔊 /fəˈroʊʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Savagely fierce, cruel, or violent; extremely intense or severe
**中文：** adj. 残忍的；凶猛的；惊人的；十分强烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fierce（凶猛的）
- savage（残暴的）
- brutal（残忍的）
- vicious（恶毒的）
- intense（强烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ferocious beast / ferocious attack / ferocious competition / ferocious storm / ferocious appetite**

👉 Example: The ferocious competition among technology firms has driven rapid innovation in artificial intelligence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ferocious** storm battered the coastal town for three days, destroying infrastructure and displacing hundreds of residents from their homes.
   这场猛烈的风暴连续三天袭击了沿海小镇，摧毁了基础设施，迫使数百名居民离开家园。

2. Critics described the debate as **ferocious**, with both candidates launching personal attacks that shocked the national television audience.
   评论人士形容这场辩论异常激烈，两位候选人的人身攻击震惊了全国电视观众。

3. The **ferocious** determination of the research team ultimately led to a groundbreaking discovery in the treatment of autoimmune diseases.
   研究团队惊人的决心最终促成了自身免疫疾病治疗方面的突破性发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ferocious" as "fero-cious" — "fero" sounds like "pharaoh," and imagine a ferocious pharaoh ruling with savage, iron-fisted power.
把"ferocious"想成"fero-cious"——"fero"听起来像"pharaoh（法老）"，想象一个凶猛的法老以残暴的铁腕统治。
