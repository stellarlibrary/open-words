# ⚖️ proportion

🔊 /prəˈpɔːrʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A part, share, or number considered in comparative relation to a whole; the correct or harmonious relationship of parts. (v.) To adjust in size or amount relative to something else
**中文：** n. 比例；占比；部分；均衡 v. 使成比例；使均衡

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ratio（比率）
- percentage（百分比）
- balance（均衡）
- fraction（部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in proportion to / out of proportion / a large proportion of / sense of proportion**

👉 Example: A significant proportion of the budget was allocated to improving public transportation infrastructure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A growing **proportion** of university graduates are choosing to pursue careers in technology rather than traditional professional fields.
   越来越大比例的大学毕业生选择从事科技行业，而非传统的专业领域。

2. The architect carefully **proportioned** each element of the building's facade to achieve visual harmony and classical elegance.
   建筑师仔细地调整了建筑立面每个元素的比例，以实现视觉和谐与古典优雅。

3. Critics argued that the media coverage was blown out of **proportion**, exaggerating a minor incident into a national controversy.
   批评者认为媒体报道被夸大了，将一个小事件夸张成了全国性争议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proportion" as "pro-portion" — a professional portion, meaning everything is measured and balanced correctly.
把"proportion"想成"pro-portion"——专业的份额，意味着一切都被正确地衡量和平衡。
