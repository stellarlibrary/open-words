# 💪 recover

🔊 /rɪˈkʌvər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To return to a normal state of health, mind, or strength; to regain possession or control of something lost; to compensate for a loss
**中文：** v. 恢复；弥补；重新获得

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regain（重新获得）
- recuperate（恢复）
- reclaim（收回）
- retrieve（取回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recover from an illness / recover lost data / recover damages / fully recover**

👉 Example: The patient is expected to fully recover from surgery within the next six weeks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The national economy began to **recover** steadily after the government implemented a series of targeted fiscal stimulus measures.
   在政府实施了一系列有针对性的财政刺激措施后，国民经济开始稳步复苏。

2. Archaeologists managed to **recover** several rare artifacts from the excavation site that had been buried for centuries.
   考古学家成功地从挖掘现场找回了几件被掩埋了数百年的珍稀文物。

3. The plaintiff sought to **recover** substantial financial damages through the civil lawsuit filed against the negligent corporation.
   原告通过对该过失公司提起的民事诉讼寻求弥补巨额经济损失。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-cover" as "covering yourself again" — when you recover, you regain what was lost and cover the gap back up.
把"recover"想成"re-cover"——重新覆盖，也就是把失去的东西重新找回来，填补空缺。
