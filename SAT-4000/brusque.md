# 😤 brusque

🔊 /brʌsk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Abrupt or offhand in speech or manner; blunt and curt to the point of rudeness
**中文：** adj. 唐突的；直率的；无礼的；粗暴的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- curt（简短无礼的）
- blunt（直言不讳的）
- terse（简洁生硬的）
- abrupt（唐突的）
- gruff（粗鲁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brusque manner / brusque reply / brusque tone / brusque dismissal**

👉 Example: His brusque manner of speaking often offended colleagues who were unaccustomed to such directness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The receptionist's **brusque** response to the patient's inquiry drew a formal complaint from the hospital administration board.
   接待员对患者询问的粗鲁回答引起了医院管理委员会的正式投诉。

2. Although the professor was known for her **brusque** demeanor, her students recognized the genuine concern behind her blunt advice.
   虽然这位教授以唐突的举止著称，但她的学生们能感受到她直率建议背后的真切关怀。

3. Diplomatic negotiations nearly collapsed when the ambassador delivered a **brusque** rejection of the proposed ceasefire agreement terms.
   当大使对提议的停火协议条款给出无礼的拒绝时，外交谈判几乎崩溃。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brusque" sounding like "brisk" — someone so brisk and hasty in conversation that they come across as rude and curt.
把"brusque"想成听起来像"brisk（急促的）"——某人说话如此急促匆忙，以至于给人粗鲁无礼的感觉。
