# 😡 infuriate

🔊 /ɪnˈfjʊəriˌeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make someone extremely angry or furious. (adj.) Filled with intense rage
**中文：** v. 激怒；使大怒 adj. 狂怒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enrage（激怒）
- exasperate（使恼怒）
- incense（使愤怒）
- provoke（激怒）
- madden（使发狂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infuriate someone / infuriated by / infuriating behavior / absolutely infuriating**

👉 Example: His dismissive attitude continued to infuriate his colleagues during the meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician's evasive responses during the debate served only to **infuriate** an already frustrated electorate.
   那位政客在辩论中闪烁其词的回答只会让本已沮丧的选民更加愤怒。

2. Repeated delays and broken promises from the contractor began to **infuriate** the homeowners waiting for renovations.
   承包商多次延误和违背承诺开始激怒等待装修的业主们。

3. Nothing could **infuriate** the professor more than students who plagiarized their research papers without any remorse.
   没有什么比学生毫无悔意地抄袭研究论文更能激怒那位教授了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-FURY-ate" — it puts FURY into someone, making them absolutely furious.
把"infuriate"想成"in + fury（狂怒）+ ate"——把狂怒注入某人体内，使其暴怒。
