# 🌫️ indistinct

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not clear or sharply defined; hazy, vague, or blurred in form, outline, or sound
**中文：** adj. 模糊的；不清楚的；朦胧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vague（模糊的）
- blurry（模糊不清的）
- hazy（朦胧的）
- unclear（不清楚的）
- faint（微弱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indistinct memory / indistinct outline / indistinct murmur / become indistinct**

👉 Example: The distant mountains appeared as indistinct shapes through the heavy morning fog.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness could only provide an **indistinct** description of the suspect, having observed the incident from a considerable distance.
   目击者只能提供对嫌疑人模糊的描述，因为他是从相当远的距离观察到事件的。

2. As the old photograph faded over the decades, the faces of the family members became increasingly **indistinct** and unrecognizable.
   随着这张老照片在几十年间逐渐褪色，家庭成员的面孔变得越来越模糊、无法辨认。

3. The radio transmission was **indistinct** due to atmospheric interference, making it impossible to decipher the pilot's emergency message.
   由于大气干扰，无线电传输变得模糊不清，使人无法辨别飞行员的紧急信息。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-distinct" — not distinct — like looking at something through fog where all the distinct edges and details disappear.
把"indistinct"想成"in-distinct"——不清晰的——就像透过雾气看东西，所有清晰的边缘和细节都消失了。
