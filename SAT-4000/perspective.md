# 🔭 perspective

🔊 /pərˈspɛktɪv/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular attitude toward or way of regarding something; a point of view; the art of representing three-dimensional objects on a flat surface. (adj.) Of or relating to the technique of perspective
**中文：** n. 观点；远景；透视图 adj. 透视的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- viewpoint（观点）
- outlook（看法）
- standpoint（立场）
- vantage point（有利位置）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **from the perspective of / gain perspective / put into perspective / historical perspective**

👉 Example: Traveling abroad often gives people a fresh perspective on their own culture and values.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary presented the conflict from the **perspective** of civilians who had endured decades of displacement and hardship.
   这部纪录片从经历了数十年流离失所和苦难的平民的视角呈现了这场冲突。

2. Students of art history must master the principles of linear **perspective** to fully appreciate Renaissance painting techniques.
   艺术史专业的学生必须掌握线性透视原理，才能充分领会文艺复兴时期的绘画技法。

3. Placing current economic challenges in historical **perspective** reveals that societies have overcome far greater adversities before.
   将当前的经济挑战放在历史的视角下审视，可以发现社会以前克服过大得多的困难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-SPECT-ive" — "spect" means to look (like spectacle), so perspective is how you "look through" at something from your unique angle.
把"perspective"想成"per-SPECT-ive"——"spect"意为看（如spectacle），所以perspective就是你从独特角度"透过"某物来观察。
