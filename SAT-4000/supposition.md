# 💭 supposition

🔊 /ˌsʌpəˈzɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A belief or assumption based on reasoning rather than proof; conjecture or speculation
**中文：** n. 假定，想象；推测，推想

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assumption（假设）
- conjecture（推测）
- hypothesis（假说）
- presumption（推定）
- speculation（猜测）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mere supposition / based on supposition / supposition that / wild supposition**

👉 Example: His argument rested on the supposition that all participants would cooperate willingly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective's theory was based entirely on **supposition** rather than any concrete physical evidence found at the scene.
   侦探的理论完全建立在推测之上，而非在现场发现的任何具体物证。

2. It is a common **supposition** that increased funding alone will resolve the deep-rooted problems within public education systems.
   人们普遍假定，仅靠增加资金就能解决公共教育体系中根深蒂固的问题。

3. The researcher cautioned against drawing conclusions from **supposition** when empirical data could readily be obtained through experimentation.
   研究人员告诫不要在可以通过实验轻易获取经验数据时依赖推测得出结论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "supposition" as "suppose + position" — it is the position you take when you merely suppose something without proof.
把"supposition"想成"suppose + position"——这是你在没有证据、仅凭假设时所持的立场。
