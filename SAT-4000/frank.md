# 💬 frank

🔊 /fræŋk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Open, honest, and direct in speech or writing, especially when dealing with unpleasant matters
**中文：** 坦白的，直率的；老实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- candid（坦率的）
- forthright（直率的）
- straightforward（直截了当的）
- outspoken（直言不讳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frank discussion / frank admission / to be frank / frank assessment**

👉 Example: To be frank, the company's financial situation is far worse than anyone had anticipated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador offered a **frank** assessment of the deteriorating diplomatic relations between the two neighboring countries.
   大使对两个邻国之间不断恶化的外交关系作出了坦率的评估。

2. During the interview, the candidate was remarkably **frank** about the failures she had encountered in her previous role.
   在面试中，这位候选人对她在之前职位上遇到的失败非常坦率。

3. The professor encouraged **frank** debate among the students, believing that honest disagreement fostered intellectual growth.
   教授鼓励学生之间坦率辩论，相信真诚的分歧能促进智识成长。

---

Think of a person named Frank who always tells the truth — he is frank by nature, never hiding his honest opinions.
想象一个叫Frank的人总是说真话——他天性坦率，从不隐藏自己真实的想法。
