# ⚔️ autocratic

🔊 /ˌɔːtəˈkrætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or characteristic of a ruler with absolute power; domineering and controlling.
**中文：** adj. 专制的；独裁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- despotic（专制的）
- tyrannical（暴虐的）
- dictatorial（独裁的）
- authoritarian（威权的）
- domineering（专横的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **autocratic rule / autocratic leader / autocratic style / autocratic government**

👉 Example: His autocratic management style discouraged creative thinking among the team members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **autocratic** approach to classroom management left students feeling intimidated and reluctant to participate in discussions.
   这位教授专制的课堂管理方式让学生们感到害怕，不愿参与课堂讨论。

2. Several nations have transitioned from **autocratic** regimes to democratic systems through persistent civic engagement and reform.
   一些国家通过持续的公民参与和改革，从专制政权过渡到了民主制度。

3. The committee criticized the director's **autocratic** decision to restructure the entire department without seeking any employee input.
   委员会批评了主任在未征求任何员工意见的情况下独裁式地决定重组整个部门。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Autocratic" shares its root with "automatic" — an autocratic leader acts automatically, without asking anyone else.
"Autocratic"与"automatic"有相同的词根——一个专制的领导者自动做决定，不征求任何人的意见。
