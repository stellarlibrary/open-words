# 🐄 moo

🔊 /muː/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make the characteristic deep vocal sound of a cow. (n.) The deep vocal sound made by a cow
**中文：** v. 发出哞哞声；鸣叫 n. 牛叫声

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- low（哞哞叫）
- bellow（吼叫）
- bawl（大声叫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **moo loudly / moo softly / a long moo / the moo of a cow**

👉 Example: The cattle began to moo restlessly as the approaching storm darkened the sky.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cows in the distant pasture began to **moo** plaintively as the farmer arrived with their evening feed.
   远处牧场上的奶牛在农夫带着晚间饲料到来时开始哀切地哞哞叫。

2. A solitary **moo** echoed across the valley, signaling the herd's presence beyond the ridge.
   一声孤独的牛叫声在山谷中回荡，表明牛群就在山脊的另一边。

3. Researchers recorded the frequency at which dairy cows **moo** to study their communication patterns and stress levels.
   研究人员记录了奶牛哞哞叫的频率，以研究它们的沟通模式和压力水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the sound itself — "moo" is an onomatopoeia that directly imitates the sound a cow makes, so the spelling mirrors the sound.
"moo"就是拟声词，直接模仿牛的叫声，拼写和声音完全一致，听到就能记住。
