# 💊 alleviate

🔊 /əˈliːvieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make suffering, a problem, or a burden less severe; to relieve or reduce
**中文：** 减轻；缓和；缓解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relieve（减轻）
- mitigate（缓和）
- ease（缓解）
- diminish（减少）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alleviate pain / alleviate suffering / alleviate poverty / alleviate concerns**

👉 Example: The new medication was designed to alleviate chronic pain without causing severe side effects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government introduced several economic reforms intended to **alleviate** the financial burden on low-income families across the region.
   政府推出了几项经济改革，旨在减轻该地区低收入家庭的经济负担。

2. Volunteers distributed food and medical supplies to **alleviate** the suffering of refugees displaced by the ongoing armed conflict.
   志愿者分发了食物和医疗用品，以缓解因持续武装冲突而流离失所的难民的痛苦。

3. The therapist recommended several strategies to **alleviate** the patient's anxiety before the upcoming surgical procedure next month.
   治疗师推荐了几种策略来减轻患者在下个月即将进行的外科手术前的焦虑。

---
