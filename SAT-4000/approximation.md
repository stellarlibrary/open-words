# 🎯 approximation

🔊 /əˌprɑːksɪˈmeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A value or quantity that is nearly but not exactly correct; the act or process of coming close to something in quality or character.

**中文：** 近似法；近似值；接近；大约的数额。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- estimate（估计值）
- rough calculation（粗略计算）
- semblance（相似）
- likeness（近似）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** close approximation, rough approximation, good approximation, approximation of, approximation to

**Example:** This figure is only an approximation; the actual cost may differ considerably.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer's initial **approximation** proved remarkably close to the final measurements obtained through precise instruments.
   （工程师最初的近似值与通过精密仪器获得的最终测量结果非常接近。）

2. A rough **approximation** of the population suggests that the region now houses over two million residents.
   （对人口的粗略估算表明，该地区现在拥有超过两百万居民。）

3. The model provides a useful **approximation** of climate behavior, though it cannot predict every variable perfectly.
   （该模型提供了气候行为的有用近似，尽管它无法完美预测每个变量。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Approximation" = "approximate" + "-tion" (noun form). An approximation is a result you get when you approximate — you get close, but not exact.

"Approximation" = "approximate"（近似的）+ "-tion"（名词后缀）。近似值就是你近似计算时得到的结果——接近但不精确。
