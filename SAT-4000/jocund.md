# 😄 jocund

🔊 /ˈdʒɒkənd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Cheerful and lighthearted; full of high spirits and lively merriment
**中文：** adj. 快活的；高兴的；欢乐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cheerful（快活的）
- merry（欢乐的）
- jovial（愉快的）
- lighthearted（轻松愉快的）
- buoyant（乐观的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jocund mood / jocund spirit / jocund atmosphere / jocund laughter**

👉 Example: The jocund atmosphere of the harvest celebration lifted the spirits of the entire village.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **jocund** demeanor during lectures made even the most complex topics seem approachable and engaging.
   教授在讲课时快活的态度使得即使是最复杂的话题也显得平易近人、引人入胜。

2. A **jocund** crowd gathered in the town square to celebrate the long-awaited return of the diplomatic envoy.
   一群欢乐的人聚集在城镇广场上，庆祝期待已久的外交使节归来。

3. Despite the mounting challenges ahead, she maintained a **jocund** spirit that inspired confidence among her colleagues.
   尽管前方挑战日益严峻，她依然保持着快活的精神，鼓舞了同事们的信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jocund" as "joke-und" — someone who is always joking around is naturally cheerful and jocund.
把"jocund"想成"joke-und"——一个总是开玩笑的人自然是快活的、高兴的。
