# ⬆️ vertical

🔊 /ˈvɜːrtɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Perpendicular to the horizon; upright; extending straight up and down. (n.) A vertical line, plane, or position
**中文：** adj. 垂直的，竖的；头顶的 n. 垂线，垂直面

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- upright（竖直的）
- perpendicular（垂直的）
- erect（直立的）
- plumb（垂直的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vertical line / vertical axis / vertical integration / vertical position**

👉 Example: The engineers ensured the pillar was perfectly vertical before pouring the concrete foundation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist observed that the rock strata had shifted from a horizontal to a nearly **vertical** orientation over millennia.
   地质学家观察到，岩层在数千年间从水平方向转变为近乎**垂直**的方向。

2. The company pursued a strategy of **vertical** integration by acquiring both its raw material suppliers and retail distributors.
   该公司通过收购其原材料供应商和零售分销商，采取了**垂直**整合战略。

3. The architect designed the tower with bold **vertical** lines to create an impression of soaring height and elegance.
   建筑师用大胆的**垂直**线条设计了这座塔，以营造出高耸优雅的印象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vertical" as containing "vert" (from Latin "vertere," to turn) — turned upward, standing straight up.
把"vertical"想成包含"vert"（来自拉丁语"vertere"，意为"转"）——向上转，笔直站立。
