# 🧴 solvent

🔊 /ˈsɒlvənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the power to dissolve other substances; able to pay all debts. (n.) A liquid capable of dissolving other substances; a means of solving a problem
**中文：** adj. 有溶解力的，溶剂的；有偿付能力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dissolvent（溶剂）
- diluent（稀释剂）
- medium（介质）
- financially sound（财务健全的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **organic solvent / solvent extraction / remain solvent / solvent properties**

👉 Example: The laboratory technician selected an appropriate solvent to dissolve the organic compound for analysis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The industrial chemist used a powerful organic **solvent** to extract the target compounds from the raw plant material effectively.
   工业化学家使用强效有机溶剂从原始植物材料中有效地提取目标化合物。

2. Despite the economic downturn, the corporation managed to remain **solvent** by implementing rigorous cost-reduction measures throughout operations.
   尽管经济低迷，该公司通过在整个运营中实施严格的成本削减措施，设法保持了偿付能力。

3. Water is often called the universal **solvent** because it can dissolve a greater variety of substances than any other liquid.
   水通常被称为万能溶剂，因为它能溶解的物质种类比任何其他液体都多。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solvent" as "solve + ent" — a substance that solves the problem of dissolution, or a company that can solve its debts.
把"solvent"想成"solve + ent"——一种能解决溶解问题的物质，或者一家能偿还债务的公司。
