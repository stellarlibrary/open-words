# ⚔️ spartan

🔊 /ˈspɑːrtən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to Sparta; rigorously self-disciplined or austere; simple and frugal in lifestyle. (n.) A citizen of Sparta; a person of great courage and endurance
**中文：** n. 斯巴达人；强悍勇敢、结实强壮的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- austere（朴素的）
- frugal（节俭的）
- ascetic（苦行的）
- stoic（坚忍的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spartan lifestyle / spartan conditions / spartan discipline / spartan quarters**

👉 Example: The military cadets endured spartan living conditions during their rigorous six-month training program.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The monastery maintained a deliberately **spartan** environment, with bare walls and simple wooden furnishings in every chamber.
   修道院刻意保持斯巴达式的简朴环境，每个房间都是光秃秃的墙壁和简单的木制家具。

2. Her **spartan** approach to personal finance allowed her to accumulate substantial savings well before her planned retirement date.
   她斯巴达式的个人理财方式使她在计划退休日期之前就积累了可观的储蓄。

3. The soldiers demonstrated a **spartan** resolve during the prolonged siege, refusing to surrender despite dwindling supplies and reinforcements.
   士兵们在漫长的围困中展现了斯巴达式的坚毅，尽管补给和增援日益减少仍拒绝投降。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the warriors of ancient Sparta, famous for their extreme discipline and simple living — anything "spartan" is stripped down to bare essentials.
想想古代斯巴达的勇士，以极端的纪律和简朴的生活闻名——任何"spartan"的东西都被精简到最基本的状态。
