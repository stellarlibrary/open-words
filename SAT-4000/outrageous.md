# 🤯 outrageous

🔊 /aʊtˈreɪdʒəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Shockingly bad or excessive; wildly violent or cruel; going far beyond what is reasonable or acceptable
**中文：** adj. 粗暴的；可恶的；令人吃惊的；蛮横的；无耻的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scandalous（可耻的）
- appalling（骇人的）
- atrocious（残暴的）
- monstrous（极其恶劣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outrageous behavior / outrageous price / outrageous claim / absolutely outrageous**

👉 Example: The committee condemned the outrageous treatment of prisoners held in the detention facility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney argued that the **outrageous** sentence imposed by the judge was disproportionate to the offense committed.
   辩护律师辩称法官判处的过分刑罚与所犯罪行不成比例。

2. Consumers protested the **outrageous** prices charged by pharmaceutical companies for essential life-saving medications.
   消费者抗议制药公司对必需的救命药物收取的离谱价格。

3. The journalist exposed the **outrageous** working conditions endured by factory laborers in the developing region.
   记者揭露了发展中地区工厂工人所忍受的恶劣工作条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "outrageous" as "causing outrage" — something so extreme or shocking that it triggers fury in everyone.
把"outrageous"想成"引发outrage的"——某事极端到令所有人震惊愤怒。
