# 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 acquainted

🔊 /əˈkweɪntɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having knowledge or experience of something; knowing someone personally but not closely
**中文：** 熟识的；知晓的；有知识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- familiar（熟悉的）
- informed（知情的）
- versed（精通的）
- conversant（了解的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **become acquainted with / get acquainted / well acquainted / fully acquainted**

👉 Example: The new manager quickly became acquainted with the team's workflow and project deadlines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. All participants must become fully **acquainted** with the laboratory safety procedures before conducting any experiments.
   所有参与者在进行任何实验之前必须完全熟悉实验室安全程序。

2. Although they had been **acquainted** for several years, their relationship remained purely professional and formal.
   尽管他们相识多年，但他们的关系始终是纯粹的职业性和正式的。

3. The researcher was well **acquainted** with the latest developments in artificial intelligence and machine learning.
   这位研究人员对人工智能和机器学习的最新进展非常了解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "acquainted" as "a-quaint-ed" — you have visited a quaint place before, so you are familiar with it.
把"acquainted"想成"a-quaint-ed"——你以前去过一个古雅的地方，所以你对它很熟悉。
