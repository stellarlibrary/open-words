# ⚖️ violation

🔊 /ˌvaɪəˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of breaking or failing to comply with a rule, law, or agreement; an infringement or transgression of rights
**中文：** 违反；违背；妨碍；侵害；亵渎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- infringement（侵犯）
- breach（违反）
- transgression（越轨）
- contravention（违反）
- infraction（违规）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **violation of law / human rights violation / in violation of / traffic violation**

👉 Example: The factory was cited for multiple violations of environmental protection regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international tribunal documented systematic **violations** of human rights committed during the prolonged armed conflict.
   国际法庭记录了在长期武装冲突期间所犯的系统性侵犯人权行为。

2. Any **violation** of the confidentiality agreement will result in immediate termination of employment and possible legal action.
   任何违反保密协议的行为都将导致立即解除雇佣关系并可能面临法律诉讼。

3. The investigation revealed that the corporation had been operating in **violation** of federal trade regulations for several years.
   调查显示该公司多年来一直在违反联邦贸易法规的情况下运营。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "violation" as "violent action" against the rules — when you violate something, you forcefully break through its boundaries.
把"violation"想成对规则的"violent action"（暴力行为）——当你违反某事时，你强行突破了它的界限。
