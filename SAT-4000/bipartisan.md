# 🤝 bipartisan

🔊 /ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Involving or supported by two political parties; relating to cooperation between two major parties
**中文：** adj. 两党的；两党连立的；代表两党的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nonpartisan（无党派的）
- bipartite（双方的）
- collaborative（合作的）
- cross-party（跨党派的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bipartisan support / bipartisan effort / bipartisan agreement / bipartisan consensus**

👉 Example: The new infrastructure bill received strong bipartisan support from both sides of the aisle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator urged her colleagues to adopt a **bipartisan** approach to healthcare reform in the national interest.
   这位参议员敦促同僚们以两党合作的方式推进医疗改革，以维护国家利益。

2. Achieving **bipartisan** consensus on immigration policy has proven exceptionally difficult in recent legislative sessions.
   在最近的立法会议中，就移民政策达成两党共识已被证明极其困难。

3. The committee issued a **bipartisan** report recommending substantial increases in federal funding for scientific research.
   该委员会发布了一份两党联合报告，建议大幅增加联邦科学研究经费。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "partisan" (party supporter) — two party supporters coming together in agreement.
把"bipartisan"拆成"bi-"（二）+"partisan"（党派支持者）——两个党派的支持者走到一起达成共识。
