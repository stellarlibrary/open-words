# ⚠️ imprudent

🔊 /ɪmˈpruːdənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not showing care for the consequences of an action; rash; lacking discretion or good judgment
**中文：** adj. 不谨慎的；轻率的；鲁莽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rash（轻率的）
- reckless（鲁莽的）
- unwise（不明智的）
- injudicious（欠考虑的）
- foolhardy（有勇无谋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imprudent decision / imprudent investment / imprudent behavior / fiscally imprudent**

👉 Example: It would be imprudent to invest all of your savings in a single volatile stock.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board criticized the executive's **imprudent** allocation of capital, which exposed the company to significant financial risk.
   董事会批评了该高管不审慎的资本配置，这使公司面临重大财务风险。

2. Historians regard the general's **imprudent** decision to advance without reinforcements as the turning point of the entire campaign.
   历史学家认为这位将军在没有增援的情况下轻率推进的决定是整个战役的转折点。

3. The student's **imprudent** remarks during the seminar offended several distinguished scholars and undermined her academic credibility.
   这位学生在研讨会上轻率的言论冒犯了几位著名学者，并损害了她的学术信誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imprudent" as "im + prudent" — the opposite of prudent (careful and wise), meaning careless and rash.
把"imprudent"想成"im（否定前缀）+ prudent（谨慎的）"——谨慎的反面，即轻率的、鲁莽的。
