# 🕵️ furtive

🔊 /ˈfɜːrtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Attempting to avoid notice or attention, typically because of guilt or a belief that discovery would lead to trouble; secretive and sly
**中文：** adj. 鬼鬼祟祟的，秘密的；偷偷摸摸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stealthy（隐秘的）
- sneaky（偷偷摸摸的）
- surreptitious（秘密的）
- clandestine（暗中的）
- covert（隐蔽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **furtive glance / furtive behavior / furtive movement / furtive whisper**

👉 Example: The student cast a furtive glance at her neighbor's paper during the examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective noticed the suspect casting **furtive** glances toward the emergency exit throughout the entire interview.
   侦探注意到嫌疑人在整个审讯过程中不断偷偷地瞥向紧急出口。

2. His **furtive** behavior at the restricted archives aroused the suspicion of the security personnel stationed nearby.
   他在限制区档案室的鬼鬼祟祟的行为引起了附近驻守保安人员的怀疑。

3. The spy exchanged **furtive** whispers with her contact in the crowded marketplace to avoid drawing any unwanted attention.
   间谍在拥挤的市场上与联络人偷偷低声交谈，以避免引起不必要的注意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "furtive" as related to "furt" (like a ferret) — a ferret sneaks around furtively, always darting and hiding.
把"furtive"联想到"furt"（像雪貂ferret）——雪貂总是鬼鬼祟祟地溜来溜去，不断窜动和躲藏。
