# 🐟 flounder

🔊 /ˈflaʊndər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To struggle clumsily; to proceed with great difficulty; to act in a confused or aimless manner. (n.) A type of flatfish; the act of struggling
**中文：** v. 挣扎；折腾；错乱地做事；困难地前行 n. 比目鱼；挣扎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- struggle（挣扎）
- fumble（笨拙地做）
- falter（蹒跚）
- stumble（绊跌）
- blunder（犯错）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **flounder in / flounder around / flounder through / left to flounder / flounder helplessly**

👉 Example: Without a clear business plan, the new company began to flounder within its first six months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inexperienced speaker began to **flounder** when audience members posed challenging questions about the methodology of the study.
   当听众提出关于研究方法论的挑战性问题时，这位缺乏经验的演讲者开始语无伦次。

2. Several developing economies continued to **flounder** despite receiving substantial international aid and comprehensive policy reform packages.
   尽管获得了大量国际援助和全面的政策改革方案，一些发展中经济体仍然持续挣扎。

3. The rescue team watched the swimmer **flounder** in the turbulent current before deploying the emergency flotation device toward him.
   救援队看着游泳者在湍急的水流中挣扎，随后向他投放了紧急漂浮装置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "flounder" as "found-er" — when you cannot find (found) your way, you flounder and struggle, just like a fish flopping on land.
把"flounder"想成"flound（拍打）+ er"——想象一条比目鱼在陆地上拍打挣扎，手足无措的样子。
