# 🎭 levity

🔊 /ˈlɛvɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Humor or lack of seriousness, especially during a serious occasion; lightness of mind or behavior; fickleness
**中文：** n. 轻率；轻浮；多变；不稳定

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frivolity（轻浮）
- flippancy（轻率）
- lightheartedness（轻松愉快）
- triviality（琐碎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a moment of levity / inappropriate levity / inject levity / treat with levity**

👉 Example: The chairman's unexpected joke provided a welcome moment of levity during the tense negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge reprimanded the attorney for displaying **levity** during the proceedings of such a grave criminal trial.
   法官训斥了律师在如此严重的刑事审判过程中表现出的轻浮态度。

2. A brief moment of **levity** helped ease the tension among delegates at the contentious international summit.
   片刻的轻松帮助缓解了在激烈的国际峰会上代表们之间的紧张气氛。

3. The philosopher cautioned against treating matters of ethical significance with **levity** or casual indifference.
   这位哲学家告诫人们不要以轻率或漫不经心的态度对待具有伦理意义的事务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "levity" as "lev-ity" from "levitate" — lightness that makes things float upward, the opposite of gravity.
把"levity"想成来自"levitate（漂浮）"——一种使事物向上飘浮的轻盈，是"gravity（严肃/重力）"的反义词。
