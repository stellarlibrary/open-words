# 🌫️ diffusion

🔊 /dɪˈfjuːʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The spreading or scattering of something widely; in science, the movement of particles from an area of higher concentration to lower concentration
**中文：** n. 扩散，传播；漫射

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dispersion（散布）
- dissemination（传播）
- spreading（扩展）
- propagation（传播）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diffusion of knowledge / cultural diffusion / diffusion process / rate of diffusion**

👉 Example: The diffusion of new technologies across developing nations has accelerated rapidly in recent decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **diffusion** of democratic ideals throughout the region was a gradual process spanning several tumultuous decades of change.
   民主理想在该地区的传播是一个跨越数十年动荡变革的渐进过程。

2. In chemistry, **diffusion** occurs when molecules move from regions of high concentration to regions of lower concentration naturally.
   在化学中，扩散是指分子从高浓度区域自然地向低浓度区域移动。

3. Scholars have studied the **diffusion** of agricultural innovations to understand how traditional farming communities adopt modern scientific practices.
   学者们研究了农业创新的传播，以了解传统农业社区如何采用现代科学实践。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dif-fusion" as "spreading in different fusions" — things fuse and spread out in different directions, like perfume diffusing through air.
把"diffusion"想成"向不同方向融合扩散"——事物向不同方向融合和扩展，就像香水在空气中扩散一样。
