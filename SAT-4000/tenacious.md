# 💪 tenacious

🔊 /tɪˈneɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Tending to keep a firm hold of something; persistent and determined; not easily dispelled or discouraged
**中文：** adj. 顽强的；坚韧的；固执的；紧握的；黏着力强的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- persistent（坚持不懈的）
- resolute（坚决的）
- dogged（顽强的）
- unyielding（不屈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tenacious grip / tenacious pursuit / tenacious defender / tenacious spirit / tenacious determination**

👉 Example: Her tenacious pursuit of justice inspired an entire generation of civil rights advocates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **tenacious** investigative journalist spent three years uncovering the corruption scandal that implicated several senior government officials.
   这位顽强的调查记者花了三年时间揭露了牵涉数名高级政府官员的腐败丑闻。

2. Despite repeated failures in the laboratory, the **tenacious** researcher refused to abandon her groundbreaking experiment on gene therapy.
   尽管在实验室中屡次失败，这位坚韧的研究人员拒绝放弃她在基因治疗方面的开创性实验。

3. The small but **tenacious** army held its defensive position against overwhelming odds for nearly two exhausting weeks.
   这支规模虽小但顽强的军队在压倒性的劣势下坚守防御阵地近两周。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tenac-ious" from Latin "tenax" (holding fast) — like a tenant who refuses to leave, someone tenacious holds on tightly.
把"tenacious"想成来自拉丁语"tenax（紧握）"——就像一个拒绝搬走的租户，顽强的人紧紧抓住不放。
