# 🎰 profligate

🔊 /ˈprɒflɪɡət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Recklessly extravagant or wasteful; shamelessly immoral or dissolute. (n.) A person given to wildly extravagant or dissolute behavior
**中文：** adj. 放荡的，不检点的；恣意挥霍的 n. 放荡者；挥霍者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extravagant（挥霍的）
- wasteful（浪费的）
- dissolute（放荡的）
- prodigal（挥金如土的）
- debauched（堕落的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **profligate spending / profligate lifestyle / profligate use of / profligate waste / profligate behavior**

👉 Example: The senator criticized the profligate spending habits of the administration during the fiscal hearing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation's **profligate** expenditure on luxury retreats drew sharp criticism from shareholders concerned about declining quarterly revenues.
   该公司在豪华度假上的挥霍性支出招致了担心季度收入下降的股东们的尖锐批评。

2. Environmental activists condemned the **profligate** consumption of natural resources that had accelerated the degradation of fragile ecosystems.
   环保活动人士谴责对自然资源的挥霍性消耗，这加速了脆弱生态系统的退化。

3. The biography revealed him to be a **profligate** who squandered his considerable family fortune on gambling and reckless speculation.
   传记揭示他是一个将大量家族财产挥霍在赌博和鲁莽投机上的放荡者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "profligate" as "profit-less gate" — someone so wasteful that all their money flows through a gate with no profit left.
把"profligate"想成"profit（利润）+ less + gate（大门）"——一个人挥霍无度，所有钱都从大门流走，没有剩下任何利润。
