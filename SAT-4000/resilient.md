# 🌱 resilient

🔊 /rɪˈzɪliənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to recover quickly from misfortune or change; able to spring back into shape after being bent or stretched
**中文：** adj. 弹回的，有回弹力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hardy（坚强的）
- adaptable（适应性强的）
- flexible（灵活的）
- tough（坚韧的）
- buoyant（有活力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resilient to / resilient in the face of / remarkably resilient / highly resilient / resilient economy**

👉 Example: Children are often more resilient than adults expect, adapting quickly to new circumstances.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite facing numerous personal setbacks, she proved to be remarkably **resilient** and continued to pursue her academic research with vigor.
   尽管遭遇了众多个人挫折，她展现出了非凡的韧性，继续充满活力地从事学术研究。

2. The **resilient** infrastructure of the city allowed essential services to resume within hours of the severe flooding event.
   该城市具有韧性的基础设施使得基本服务在严重洪灾发生后数小时内恢复运行。

3. Ecologists noted that the coral reef ecosystem was surprisingly **resilient** after conservation measures were implemented along the coastline.
   生态学家注意到，在沿海地区实施保护措施后，珊瑚礁生态系统的恢复力出乎意料地强。

---

把"resilient"想成"re-silly-ent"——就像一个人在困难之后能重新欢笑，从艰难时期中恢复过来。
