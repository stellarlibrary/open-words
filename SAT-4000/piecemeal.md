# 🧩 piecemeal

🔊 /ˈpiːsmiːl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done or made in separate stages rather than all at once; gradual and unsystematic. (adv.) In a gradual or fragmented manner; piece by piece
**中文：** adj. 零碎的；逐渐的 adv. 零碎地；逐个地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gradual（逐渐的）
- fragmentary（碎片的）
- incremental（递增的）
- bit-by-bit（一点一点的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **piecemeal approach / piecemeal fashion / piecemeal reform / done piecemeal**

👉 Example: The company adopted a piecemeal approach to restructuring, addressing one department at a time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argued that the government's **piecemeal** approach to healthcare reform failed to address the systemic problems in the industry.
   批评者认为政府零碎的医疗改革方式未能解决该行业的系统性问题。

2. The restoration of the historic building was carried out **piecemeal** over a period of nearly fifteen years.
   这座历史建筑的修复工作在近十五年的时间里逐步完成。

3. Rather than implementing a comprehensive strategy, the committee introduced changes in a **piecemeal** fashion that confused stakeholders.
   委员会没有实施全面战略，而是以零碎的方式引入变化，令利益相关者感到困惑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "piece-meal" as eating a meal "one piece at a time" — doing something piecemeal means handling it bit by bit, not all at once.
把"piecemeal"想成一顿饭"一块一块地吃"——零碎地做某事就是一点一点地处理，而不是一次完成。
