# 💤 soporific

🔊 /ˌsɑːpəˈrɪfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A drug or agent that induces sleep. (adj.) Tending to induce drowsiness or sleep
**中文：** adj. 催眠的，想睡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sedative（镇静剂）
- hypnotic（催眠的）
- sleep-inducing（催眠的）
- drowsy（昏昏欲睡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **soporific effect / soporific drug / soporific speech / soporific warmth / soporific quality**

👉 Example: The soporific effect of the medication made it difficult for the patient to remain alert during the day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The lecturer's monotonous and **soporific** delivery caused more than half the audience to struggle visibly against falling asleep.
   演讲者单调催眠的表达方式导致超过一半的听众明显地挣扎着不让自己睡着。

2. Physicians prescribed a mild **soporific** to help the patient overcome persistent insomnia that had lasted for several weeks.
   医生开了一种温和的催眠剂来帮助患者克服持续数周的顽固失眠症。

3. The **soporific** warmth of the sunlit reading room gradually lulled every student into a peaceful and unintended afternoon nap.
   阳光照射的阅览室里催眠般的温暖逐渐使每个学生不由自主地进入了一场宁静的午睡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sopor-" (Latin for deep sleep) + "-ific" (making) — soporific literally means "sleep-making," something that puts you to sleep.
把"sopor-"（拉丁语意为深度睡眠）+"−ific"（制造）联系起来——soporific字面意思是"制造睡眠的"，即让你入睡的东西。
