# 💊 overdose

🔊 /ˈoʊvərdoʊs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An excessive and dangerous dose of a drug or medication. (v.) To take an excessively large dose of a drug; to give too large a dose to
**中文：** n. 药量过多 v. 配药过量；使过分沉溺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excess dose（过量剂量）
- overmedicate（过度用药）
- surplus（过量）
- overindulge（过度沉溺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **drug overdose / fatal overdose / overdose on something / accidental overdose**

👉 Example: Emergency responders were trained to recognize the warning signs of an opioid overdose.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital reported a significant increase in patients admitted for accidental **overdose** of prescription painkillers last year.
   医院报告去年因意外过量服用处方止痛药而入院的患者显著增加。

2. Public health officials launched a campaign to educate communities about how to prevent and respond to drug **overdoses**.
   公共卫生官员发起了一项运动，教育社区如何预防和应对药物过量。

3. The physician cautioned that even common medications can cause a dangerous **overdose** if taken beyond the recommended dosage.
   医生警告说，即使是常见药物，如果超出推荐剂量服用也会导致危险的药物过量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-dose" as "a dose that goes over the limit" — too much of a medicine becomes poison.
把"overdose"想成"dose over了"——超过限度的剂量，药就变成了毒。
