# 🏦 redeem

🔊 /rɪˈdiːm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To buy back; to make up for past wrongs; to exchange for something of value; to fulfill a promise or pledge
**中文：** v. 赎回；挽回；兑换；履行；补偿；恢复

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reclaim（收回）
- salvage（挽救）
- atone（赎罪）
- compensate（补偿）
- recover（恢复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **redeem a coupon / redeem oneself / redeem a pledge / redeem one's reputation**

👉 Example: The athlete worked tirelessly to redeem his reputation after the doping scandal tarnished his career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation offered generous compensation packages to **redeem** its public image following the environmental contamination scandal.
   该公司提供了丰厚的赔偿方案，以在环境污染丑闻后挽回其公众形象。

2. Customers may **redeem** their accumulated loyalty points for airline tickets, hotel stays, or retail merchandise credits.
   客户可以将累积的忠诚积分兑换为机票、酒店住宿或零售商品抵扣。

3. The final chapter of the novel allows the protagonist to **redeem** himself through a profound act of personal sacrifice.
   小说的最后一章让主人公通过一次深刻的自我牺牲行为来救赎自己。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-deem" — "re" means again and "deem" means to consider, so to redeem is to make others consider you worthy again.
把"redeem"想成"re(重新) + deem(认为)"——让别人重新认为你有价值，即赎回、挽回。
