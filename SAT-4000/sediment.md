# 🏔️ sediment

🔊 /ˈsɛdɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Deposited material; precipitate
**中文：** n. 沉淀物，沉积物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deposit（沉积物）
- residue（残渣）
- precipitate（沉淀物）
- dregs（沉渣）
- silt（淤泥）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **layers of sediment / sediment buildup / sediment deposits / river sediment**

👉 Example: Geologists analyzed the layers of sediment to determine the age and composition of the rock formations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Over millions of years, accumulated **sediment** at the bottom of the ancient sea gradually transformed into solid limestone.
   经过数百万年，古老海底积累的沉积物逐渐转变为坚固的石灰岩。

2. The heavy rainfall caused excessive **sediment** to flow into the reservoir, threatening the water supply and filtration system.
   暴雨导致大量沉积物流入水库，威胁到供水和过滤系统。

3. Researchers examined the **sediment** cores extracted from the lake bed to reconstruct the region's paleoclimatic history.
   研究人员检查了从湖床提取的沉积物岩芯，以重建该地区的古气候历史。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sedi-ment" — "sed" from Latin "sedere" (to sit/settle). Sediment is material that settles and sits at the bottom.
把"sediment"想成"sed"来自拉丁语"sedere（坐下/沉降）"——沉积物就是沉降并停留在底部的物质。
