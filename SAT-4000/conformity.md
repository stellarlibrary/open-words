# 🤝 conformity

🔊 /kənˈfɔːrmɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Compliance with standards, rules, or norms; behavior in accordance with socially accepted conventions or expectations
**中文：** n. 一致，符合；遵从，顺从

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compliance（遵从）
- accordance（一致）
- adherence（遵守）
- obedience（服从）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in conformity with / conformity to rules / social conformity / pressure to conformity**

👉 Example: The regulations require strict conformity with international safety standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The school's rigid emphasis on **conformity** often stifled the creative expression and independent thinking of its students.
   学校对一致性的严格强调常常压制了学生的创造性表达和独立思考。

2. In **conformity** with the new environmental regulations, the factory installed advanced filtration systems to reduce emissions.
   为了符合新的环保法规，工厂安装了先进的过滤系统以减少排放。

3. Sociologists have long studied how social **conformity** influences individual decision-making in group settings and institutions.
   社会学家长期以来研究社会从众心理如何在群体环境和机构中影响个人决策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-FORM-ity" — everyone molding into the same FORM, following the same shape and pattern.
把"conformity"想成"con-FORM-ity"——每个人都塑造成相同的形式（FORM），遵循相同的模式。
