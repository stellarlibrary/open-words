# 🗡️ murderous

🔊 /ˈmɜːrdərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of or intent on murder; extremely violent or savage; informal: extremely difficult or unpleasant
**中文：** adj. 杀人的，残忍的；凶残的；蓄意谋杀的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- homicidal（杀人的）
- lethal（致命的）
- savage（凶残的）
- brutal（残暴的）
- ferocious（凶猛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **murderous intent / murderous rage / murderous attack / murderous look**

👉 Example: The detective identified the suspect based on evidence of murderous intent found at the crime scene.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary examined the **murderous** regime that systematically persecuted political dissidents throughout the twentieth century.
   这部纪录片审视了整个二十世纪系统迫害政治异见人士的凶残政权。

2. Witnesses described the assailant's **murderous** expression as he advanced toward the building with a weapon drawn.
   目击者描述了袭击者手持武器向建筑物逼近时那凶残的表情。

3. The hiking expedition encountered **murderous** weather conditions that forced the team to abandon their ascent of the summit.
   徒步探险队遭遇了极其恶劣的天气条件，迫使团队放弃了登顶计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "murder-ous" as "full of murder" — the suffix "-ous" means "full of," so murderous literally means full of murderous intent or violence.
把"murderous"想成"murder（谋杀）+ -ous（充满的）"——后缀"-ous"表示"充满"，所以murderous字面意思就是充满杀意或暴力的。
