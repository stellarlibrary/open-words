# 📦 amply

🔊 /ˈæmpli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In a way that is more than adequate; sufficiently or abundantly.
**中文：** 充足地；广大地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundantly（丰富地）
- sufficiently（充分地）
- generously（慷慨地）
- plentifully（大量地）
- richly（丰厚地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amply demonstrated, amply rewarded, amply supplied, amply justified, amply provided**
👉 Example: The research findings amply demonstrated the effectiveness of the new treatment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship fund was **amply** endowed by generous donors who valued the pursuit of academic excellence.
   奖学金基金由重视追求学术卓越的慷慨捐赠者充足地资助。

2. Her qualifications **amply** justified the committee's decision to appoint her as the new department chairperson.
   她的资历充分证明了委员会任命她为新系主任的决定是正确的。

3. The expedition was **amply** supplied with provisions to sustain the crew throughout the arduous six-month voyage.
   探险队配备了充足的补给，以维持船员度过艰苦的六个月航程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amply" is simply "ample" turned into an adverb — if you have ample food, you are amply fed.
"Amply"就是"ample"变成的副词——如果你有充足的（ample）食物，你就被充足地（amply）喂饱了。
