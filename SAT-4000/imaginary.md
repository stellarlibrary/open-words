# 🦄 imaginary

🔊 /ɪˈmædʒɪneri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Existing only in the imagination; not real or based on fact; fictitious
**中文：** adj. 假想的，想象的，虚构的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fictitious（虚构的）
- fictional（小说般的）
- unreal（不真实的）
- fabricated（编造的）
- hypothetical（假设的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imaginary friend / imaginary world / imaginary line / imaginary number / imaginary threat**

👉 Example: The child spent hours conversing with her imaginary friend in the garden behind the house.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novelist constructed an elaborate **imaginary** world complete with its own languages, governments, and complex social hierarchies.
   这位小说家构建了一个精心设计的虚构世界，拥有自己的语言、政府和复杂的社会等级制度。

2. Critics argued that the administration was responding to an **imaginary** crisis rather than addressing the genuine concerns of citizens.
   批评者认为政府是在应对一场假想的危机，而非解决公民真正关心的问题。

3. In mathematics, **imaginary** numbers play a crucial role in solving equations that have no real number solutions.
   在数学中，虚数在求解没有实数解的方程中起着至关重要的作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imaginary" as "imagine + ary" — something that exists only because you imagine it, not because it is truly there.
把"imaginary"想成"imagine（想象）+ ary"——只因你想象而存在的东西，并非真实存在。
