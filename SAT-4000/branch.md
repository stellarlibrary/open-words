# 🌿 branch

🔊 /bræntʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A part of a tree that grows out from the trunk; a division or subdivision of a larger entity. (v.) To divide into separate parts; to diverge
**中文：** n. 树枝；分枝；分部；支流；支路 v. 分支；出现分歧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- limb（树枝）
- offshoot（分支）
- division（部门）
- tributary（支流）
- diverge（分叉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **branch out / branch office / branch of government / branch off from**

👉 Example: The company decided to branch out into international markets to diversify its revenue streams.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The local library opened a new **branch** in the suburban district to serve the growing population of young families.
   当地图书馆在郊区新开了一个分馆，以服务日益增长的年轻家庭群体。

2. After a decade in finance, she decided to **branch** out into technology consulting to broaden her professional expertise.
   在金融行业工作十年后，她决定拓展到科技咨询领域，以扩大自己的专业技能。

3. The river's northern **branch** flows through a densely forested valley before merging with the main waterway near the coast.
   这条河流的北部支流穿过一片茂密的森林山谷，然后在海岸附近汇入主要水道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a tree branch literally splitting off from the trunk — just as a branch office or branch road splits off from the main body.
想象一根树枝从树干上分出来——就像分公司或支路从主体中分离出来一样。
