# 👑 autarchy

🔊 /ˈɔːtɑːrki/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Absolute rule or power by a single person; a system of government in which one person has unlimited authority; autocracy or despotism.
**中文：** 专制，独裁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- autocracy（独裁政治）
- despotism（专制）
- tyranny（暴政）
- absolutism（专制主义）
- dictatorship（独裁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political autarchy**, **regime of autarchy**, **autarchy of the ruler**. Do not confuse with "autarky" (economic self-sufficiency), which is a different word.

👉 Example: The nation suffered under decades of autarchy before the people finally demanded democratic reforms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian argued that the emperor's **autarchy** ultimately weakened the empire by suppressing all forms of dissent.
   历史学家认为，皇帝的专制最终通过压制一切异议而削弱了帝国。

2. Citizens who had lived under **autarchy** for generations struggled to adapt to the responsibilities of democratic governance.
   在专制统治下生活了几代人的公民难以适应民主治理的责任。

3. The political philosopher warned that unchecked executive power could gradually transform a republic into an **autarchy**.
   这位政治哲学家警告说，不受约束的行政权力可能会逐渐将共和国变为专制政体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Auto-" means "self" and "-archy" means "rule." So autarchy literally means "self-rule" — one person ruling all by themselves. Think: AUTOcrat + monARCHY = autarchy.
"Auto-"意为"自己"，"-archy"意为"统治"。所以autarchy字面意思是"自我统治"——一个人独自统治一切。联想：AUTOcrat（独裁者）+ monARCHY（君主制）= autarchy。
