# 🌬️ exhale

🔊 /eksˈheɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To breathe out air or vapor from the lungs; to give off or emit gas, vapor, or an aroma
**中文：** v. 呼气；发出；发散；使蒸发

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- breathe out（呼出）
- expire（呼气）
- emit（发出）
- expel（排出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exhale slowly / exhale deeply / exhale air / exhale smoke / exhale a breath**

👉 Example: The yoga instructor reminded the class to exhale slowly through pursed lips.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor instructed the patient to **exhale** completely before the chest radiograph was taken for diagnostic purposes.
   医生指示患者在拍摄胸部X光片进行诊断之前完全呼气。

2. After hours of tense negotiation, the diplomat finally **exhaled** with relief when both parties signed the agreement.
   经过数小时紧张的谈判，当双方签署协议时，这位外交官终于如释重负地呼了口气。

3. The volcanic vents continuously **exhale** sulfurous gases that contribute to the unique microclimate of the surrounding region.
   火山口持续散发出含硫气体，形成了周围地区独特的微气候。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-hale" — "ex" means out, and "hale" sounds like "inhale" minus the "in," so exhale means to breathe out.
把"exhale"想成"ex-hale"——"ex"意为向外，"hale"听起来像去掉"in"的"inhale"，所以exhale意为向外呼气。
