# 🌟 prominent

🔊 /ˈprɑːmɪnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Standing out so as to be easily seen; important and well-known; projecting from a surface
**中文：** adj. 突起的；凸起的；杰出的；著名的；显著的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distinguished（杰出的）
- notable（显著的）
- eminent（卓越的）
- conspicuous（显眼的）
- renowned（著名的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prominent figure / prominent role / prominent feature / prominent position / prominent member**

👉 Example: Several prominent scholars contributed essays to the interdisciplinary volume on modern ethics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **prominent** historian delivered a keynote address at the international conference on postcolonial studies and cultural identity.
   这位著名历史学家在关于后殖民研究和文化认同的国际会议上发表了主旨演讲。

2. A **prominent** cliff formation along the coastline served as a natural navigational landmark for sailors throughout the centuries.
   海岸线上一处突出的悬崖地貌几个世纪以来一直是水手们天然的导航地标。

3. Climate policy has assumed a more **prominent** role in national elections as voters increasingly prioritize environmental sustainability.
   随着选民越来越重视环境可持续性，气候政策在国家选举中扮演了更加突出的角色。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "PROMIN-ent" as "pro-MINE" — something that is prominently "mine" stands out and is easily noticed by everyone.
把"prominent"想成"pro-MINE"——某样突出地属于"我的"东西会脱颖而出，让所有人都注意到。
