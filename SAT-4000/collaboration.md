# 🤝 collaboration

🔊 /kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of working together with one or more people to achieve a shared goal; treasonable cooperation with an enemy
**中文：** n. 合作；协作；勾结；通敌

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cooperation（合作）
- partnership（伙伴关系）
- teamwork（团队合作）
- alliance（联盟）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in collaboration with / close collaboration / international collaboration / collaboration between**

👉 Example: The novel was written in collaboration with a renowned historian who provided invaluable archival research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The breakthrough in medical science was achieved through close **collaboration** between university researchers and pharmaceutical companies.
   这项医学科学突破是通过大学研究人员与制药公司的密切合作实现的。

2. International **collaboration** among space agencies has enabled the construction and continuous operation of the orbital station.
   各航天机构之间的国际合作使轨道空间站的建设和持续运行成为可能。

3. His wartime **collaboration** with the occupying regime resulted in a lengthy prison sentence after the country was liberated.
   他在战时与占领政权的勾结导致国家解放后被判处长期监禁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "collaboration" as "co-labor-ation" — the state of laboring together, combining efforts for a shared purpose.
把"collaboration"想成"co-labor-ation"——共同劳动的状态，为共同目标联合努力。
