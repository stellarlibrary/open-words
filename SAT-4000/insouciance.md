# 🎈 insouciance

🔊 /ɪnˈsuːsiəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A casual lack of concern; carefree indifference to matters that might otherwise cause worry or anxiety
**中文：** n. 无忧无虑；漫不经心；满不在乎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nonchalance（冷淡）
- indifference（漠不关心）
- unconcern（不在乎）
- carefreeness（无忧无虑）
- apathy（冷漠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with insouciance / air of insouciance / youthful insouciance / characteristic insouciance**

👉 Example: She faced the daunting challenge with remarkable insouciance, as though nothing could disturb her calm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young aristocrat's **insouciance** toward financial matters eventually led to the complete depletion of the family fortune.
   这位年轻贵族对财务问题的漫不经心最终导致了家族财产的完全耗尽。

2. Her **insouciance** in the face of mounting academic pressures impressed some classmates but deeply concerned her advisors.
   她面对日益增长的学业压力时的满不在乎给一些同学留下了深刻印象，但也深深地让她的导师们感到担忧。

3. The novelist captured the **insouciance** of youth with vivid prose that evoked a carefree summer untouched by responsibility.
   这位小说家用生动的散文捕捉了青春的无忧无虑，唤起了一个不受责任束缚的悠闲夏天。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in + souci (French for worry) + ance" — without worry, a state of having no concerns at all.
把"insouciance"想成"in + souci（法语中'担忧'的意思）+ ance"——没有担忧，一种完全无忧无虑的状态。
