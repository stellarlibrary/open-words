# 🦁 carnivore

🔊 /ˈkɑːrnɪvɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An animal that feeds on flesh; an organism that derives its energy and nutrient requirements primarily from animal tissue
**中文：** n. 食肉动物；食虫植物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- predator（捕食者）
- meat-eater（食肉者）
- hunter（猎食者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **apex carnivore / obligate carnivore / large carnivore / top carnivore / carnivore diet**

👉 Example: The lion is a well-known apex carnivore in the African savanna ecosystem.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The introduction of a large **carnivore** into the national park dramatically altered the balance of the local food chain.
   将大型食肉动物引入国家公园极大地改变了当地食物链的平衡。

2. Biologists classify the domestic cat as an obligate **carnivore** because it requires nutrients found exclusively in animal tissue.
   生物学家将家猫归类为专性食肉动物，因为它需要仅存在于动物组织中的营养物质。

3. Conservation efforts to protect endangered **carnivores** have yielded measurable increases in several declining predator populations worldwide.
   保护濒危食肉动物的努力已在全球多个减少的捕食者种群中取得了可衡量的增长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carni-" meaning "flesh" and "-vore" meaning "to devour" — a carnivore is literally a flesh-devourer, an animal that eats meat to survive.
把"carni-"想成"肉"，"-vore"想成"吞食"——carnivore字面意思就是"吞食肉的动物"，即以肉为食来生存的动物。
