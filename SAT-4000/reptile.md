# 🦎 reptile

🔊 /ˈreptaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A cold-blooded vertebrate animal of a class that includes snakes, lizards, crocodiles, and turtles. (adj.) Of or relating to reptiles
**中文：** n. 爬行动物 adj. 爬虫类的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- serpent（蛇类）
- lizard（蜥蜴）
- cold-blooded animal（冷血动物）
- creeping creature（爬行生物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reptile species / reptile habitat / reptile house / giant reptile**

👉 Example: The zoo's reptile house features over forty species of snakes and lizards from around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Fossil evidence indicates that the ancient **reptile** thrived in tropical climates long before the rise of modern mammals.
   化石证据表明，这种古代爬行动物在现代哺乳动物出现之前很久就在热带气候中繁盛。

2. The documentary examined how each **reptile** species has evolved unique adaptations to survive in harsh desert environments.
   这部纪录片研究了每种爬行动物如何进化出独特的适应能力以在恶劣的沙漠环境中生存。

3. Conservation efforts have successfully increased the population of this endangered **reptile** across several protected island habitats.
   保护工作成功地在多个受保护的岛屿栖息地增加了这种濒危爬行动物的数量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reptile" as "rept-ile" from Latin "reptilis" meaning "creeping" — these animals creep and crawl along the ground.
把"reptile"想成来自拉丁语"reptilis"，意为"爬行的"——这些动物在地面上匍匐爬行。
