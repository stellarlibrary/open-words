# 🔀 convergence

🔊 /kənˈvɜːrdʒəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process or state of coming together from different directions to meet at a single point; the merging of distinct elements
**中文：** n. 收敛；会聚，集合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merging（合并）
- intersection（交汇）
- confluence（汇合）
- unification（统一）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convergence of ideas / convergence of technology / point of convergence / convergence between**

👉 Example: The convergence of multiple scientific disciplines led to groundbreaking discoveries in biotechnology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **convergence** of artificial intelligence and healthcare has created unprecedented opportunities for early disease detection and diagnosis.
   人工智能与医疗保健的融合为早期疾病检测和诊断创造了前所未有的机会。

2. Scholars have noted a remarkable **convergence** of political ideologies among younger voters who prioritize environmental sustainability above all.
   学者们注意到年轻选民在政治意识形态上出现了显著的趋同，他们将环境可持续性放在首位。

3. The **convergence** of three major rivers at this geographic point has sustained a thriving agricultural community for several centuries.
   三条主要河流在此地理位置的汇聚数百年来维系了一个繁荣的农业社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-verge-nce" as "come together to verge on the same point" — different paths all verging toward one destination.
把"convergence"想成"come together to verge on the same point"——不同的路径都趋向同一个目的地。
