# 🎓 pedantic

🔊 /pɪˈdæntɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Excessively concerned with minor details or rules; making an ostentatious display of learning
**中文：** adj. 迂腐的；学究式的；卖弄学问的；过分拘泥于细节的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dogmatic（教条的）
- bookish（书呆子气的）
- punctilious（一丝不苟的）
- doctrinaire（教条主义的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pedantic about details / pedantic approach / pedantic tone / pedantic insistence**

👉 Example: His pedantic insistence on following every minor rule frustrated his more creative colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **pedantic** lectures focused so heavily on trivial details that students often lost sight of the broader argument.
   这位教授学究式的讲座过于关注琐碎细节，以至于学生们常常忽略了更宏观的论点。

2. Critics dismissed the author's writing style as excessively **pedantic**, noting its overreliance on obscure technical terminology.
   批评家们认为这位作者的写作风格过于迂腐，指出其过度依赖晦涩的专业术语。

3. Rather than offering practical solutions, the committee engaged in **pedantic** debates over the precise wording of each clause.
   委员会没有提出实际的解决方案，而是对每个条款的精确措辞进行了学究式的辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ped-antic" as "ped(al)-antic" — someone who pedals through every tiny detail like an antic performance, overdoing it absurdly.
把"pedantic"想成"pedal+antic"——像杂耍表演一样踩着每一个细节不放，过分到荒唐的地步。
