# 📢 raucous

🔊 /ˈrɔːkəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Making or constituting a disturbingly harsh and loud noise; hoarse or rough-sounding
**中文：** adj. 沙哑的；刺耳的；粗声的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hoarse（沙哑的）
- strident（刺耳的）
- harsh（刺耳的）
- grating（刺耳的）
- boisterous（喧闹的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **raucous laughter / raucous crowd / raucous voice / raucous applause / raucous noise**

👉 Example: The raucous cheering from the stadium could be heard several blocks away from the arena.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **raucous** debate in the legislative chamber drew sharp criticism from observers who expected more decorum and civility.
   立法会议厅里喧闹的辩论引起了期望更多礼仪和文明的观察者的尖锐批评。

2. A flock of crows filled the morning air with their **raucous** calls, disrupting the tranquility of the peaceful countryside.
   一群乌鸦用它们刺耳的叫声充满了清晨的空气，打破了宁静乡村的安详。

3. The professor struggled to deliver her lecture over the **raucous** commotion emanating from the construction site outside.
   教授在外面建筑工地传来的刺耳嘈杂声中艰难地进行着她的讲座。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "raucous" sounding like "raw-cuss" — someone cursing in a raw, harsh voice makes a raucous noise.
把"raucous"想成"raw（生硬的）+ cuss（骂）"——用粗声粗气骂人就是刺耳的声音。
