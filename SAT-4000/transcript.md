# 📄 transcript

🔊 /ˈtrænskrɪpt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A written or printed copy of material originally presented in another medium; an official record of a student's academic courses and grades
**中文：** 成绩单；抄本；文字记录

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- record（记录）
- copy（副本）
- documentation（文件）
- written account（书面记录）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **official transcript / academic transcript / court transcript / transcript of proceedings**

👉 Example: Applicants must submit an official transcript from each college or university they have attended.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The admissions committee requires an official **transcript** from every accredited institution where the applicant has completed coursework.
   招生委员会要求申请人提交所有就读过的认可院校的正式成绩单。

2. Journalists obtained the full **transcript** of the congressional hearing and published key excerpts revealing previously undisclosed details.
   记者获得了国会听证会的完整文字记录，并发表了揭示此前未公开细节的关键摘录。

3. The professor compared the original audio recording with the written **transcript** to verify the accuracy of the quoted statements.
   教授将原始录音与书面记录进行比对，以验证所引用陈述的准确性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tran-script" as "transferred script" — a transcript is speech or grades transferred into a written script.
把"transcript"想成"trans（转移）+ script（文字）"——成绩单或文字记录就是把信息转移成书面文字。
