# 🧪 reagent

🔊 /riˈeɪdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A substance used in a chemical reaction to detect, measure, examine, or produce other substances
**中文：** n. 试剂；反应物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reactant（反应物）
- chemical agent（化学试剂）
- catalyst（催化剂）
- substance（物质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chemical reagent / laboratory reagent / reagent grade / add a reagent / reagent bottle**

👉 Example: The laboratory technician carefully measured the reagent before adding it to the experimental solution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist added a specialized **reagent** to the solution, which immediately turned bright blue, confirming the presence of copper ions.
   化学家向溶液中加入了一种专用试剂，溶液立即变成亮蓝色，证实了铜离子的存在。

2. Researchers discovered that a newly synthesized **reagent** could detect trace amounts of contaminants far more accurately than existing methods.
   研究人员发现一种新合成的试剂能够比现有方法更准确地检测出微量污染物。

3. Students were instructed to handle the volatile **reagent** with extreme caution, as improper storage could lead to hazardous decomposition.
   学生们被要求极其小心地处理这种易挥发的试剂，因为不当存储可能导致危险的分解反应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reagent" as "re-agent" — an agent that re-acts with other chemicals; it is the active agent that triggers a chemical reaction.
把"reagent"想成"re-agent（再次行动的物质）"——它是与其他化学物质发生反应的活性物质，是触发化学反应的"代理人"。
