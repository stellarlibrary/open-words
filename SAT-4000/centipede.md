# 🐛 centipede

🔊 /ˈsɛntɪˌpiːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A predatory arthropod with a flattened elongated body composed of many segments, most of which bear a pair of legs
**中文：** n. 蜈蚣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- myriapod（多足类动物）
- arthropod（节肢动物）
- chilopod（唇足纲动物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **giant centipede / house centipede / centipede bite / centipede species**

👉 Example: The biologist discovered a rare centipede species previously unknown to science during the tropical expedition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team identified a new **centipede** species in the cave system, adding to the region's documented biodiversity.
   研究团队在洞穴系统中发现了一种新的蜈蚣物种，丰富了该地区的生物多样性记录。

2. Despite their fearsome appearance, most **centipedes** are beneficial predators that help control pest insect populations in gardens.
   尽管外表吓人，大多数蜈蚣是有益的捕食者，有助于控制花园里的害虫数量。

3. Ancient texts describe the **centipede** as a symbol of perseverance, owing to its remarkable ability to navigate difficult terrain.
   古代文献将蜈蚣描述为坚韧的象征，因为它具有在复杂地形中穿行的非凡能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "centi-" (hundred) + "-pede" (foot, as in pedestrian) — the creature with seemingly a hundred feet.
想想"centi-（百）"加"-pede（脚，如pedestrian行人）"——那种看起来有一百只脚的生物。
