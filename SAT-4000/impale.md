# 🗡️ impale

🔊 /ɪmˈpeɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To pierce or transfix with a sharp instrument; to pin or fix upon something pointed
**中文：** v. 刺穿；钉住

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pierce（刺穿）
- skewer（串住）
- stab（刺）
- transfix（贯穿）
- puncture（穿刺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impale on a spike / impale on a stake / impale with a spear / impale through**

👉 Example: The medieval warrior was nearly impaled on the enemy's lance during the fierce cavalry charge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists discovered ancient remains that showed evidence of victims who had been **impaled** on sharpened wooden stakes.
   考古学家发现了古代遗骸，证据显示受害者曾被尖锐的木桩刺穿。

2. The fallen tree branch **impaled** the roof of the vehicle, narrowly missing the passengers seated inside the car.
   倒下的树枝刺穿了车辆的车顶，差点击中车内的乘客。

3. In his historical novel, the author vividly described how captives were **impaled** as a gruesome warning to potential rebels.
   在他的历史小说中，作者生动地描述了俘虏如何被刺穿示众以警告潜在的反叛者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-pale" as "in-pole" — imagine being stuck on a pole, which is essentially what impale means: to be pierced through.
把"impale"想成"im-pale（插在杆上）"——想象被钉在杆子上，这就是impale的本意：被刺穿。
