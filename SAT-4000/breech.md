# 🎯 breech

🔊 /briːtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The lower rear part of the body; the buttocks; the back part of a firearm barrel behind the bore. (v.) To clothe with breeches; to fit a gun with a breech
**中文：** n. 臀部；后膛 vt. 给……穿上裤子；给……装上炮尾

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- buttocks（臀部）
- rear（后部）
- rump（臀部）
- hindquarters（后臀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **breech birth / breech-loading / breech of a cannon / breech presentation**

👉 Example: The physician confirmed a breech presentation and recommended a carefully planned cesarean delivery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The obstetrician detected a **breech** presentation during the ultrasound examination and recommended scheduling a cesarean section procedure.
   产科医生在超声检查中发现了臀位胎位，建议安排剖宫产手术。

2. The artillery crew inspected the **breech** of the cannon thoroughly before loading the next round of heavy ammunition into position.
   炮兵小组在装填下一发重型弹药之前，仔细检查了大炮的后膛。

3. The introduction of **breech**-loading rifles in the nineteenth century revolutionized infantry tactics and transformed modern battlefield warfare.
   十九世纪后膛装填步枪的引入彻底改变了步兵战术，改变了现代战场作战方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "breech" as "bree-ch" where "ch" sounds like the back end — the breech is always at the back, whether of a body or a gun.
把"breech"想成"后端"——无论是身体还是枪械，breech总是指后面的部分。
