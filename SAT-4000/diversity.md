# 🌈 diversity

🔊 /daɪˈvɜːrsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being diverse; a range of different things; variety in composition, character, or background
**中文：** n. 多样性；差异

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- variety（多样性）
- heterogeneity（异质性）
- multiplicity（多样）
- assortment（各种各样）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cultural diversity / diversity of opinion / biodiversity / promote diversity**

👉 Example: The organization launched a new initiative to promote diversity and inclusion across all departments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **diversity** of perspectives within the research team contributed significantly to the development of more innovative and effective solutions.
   研究团队内部观点的多样性对开发更具创新性和有效性的解决方案做出了重大贡献。

2. Preserving biological **diversity** is essential for maintaining the ecological balance upon which all living organisms ultimately depend.
   保护生物多样性对于维持所有生物最终赖以生存的生态平衡至关重要。

3. The city's cultural **diversity** is reflected in its wide array of restaurants, festivals, and artistic traditions from around the world.
   这座城市的文化多样性体现在其来自世界各地的各种餐厅、节日和艺术传统中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "divers-ity" — the quality of having "divers" (various) kinds, like a city with people from every corner of the world.
把"diversity"想成"divers(各种各样的)-ity(性质)"——拥有各种各样事物的特性，就像一座汇聚了来自世界各地人们的城市。
