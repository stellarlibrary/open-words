# 🧠 cerebellum

🔊 /ˌserəˈbeləm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The part of the brain located at the back of the skull, responsible for coordinating voluntary movements, balance, and motor learning
**中文：** n. 小脑（位于颅骨后部，负责协调运动、平衡和运动学习）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hindbrain（后脑）
- little brain（小脑）
- metencephalon（后脑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cerebellum damage / cerebellum function / cerebellum controls / cerebellar cortex**

👉 Example: The cerebellum plays a crucial role in maintaining balance and coordinating complex movements.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent neurological research has demonstrated that the **cerebellum** plays a far greater role in cognitive processing than previously assumed.
   近期的神经学研究表明，小脑在认知处理中所起的作用远比之前假设的要大。

2. Damage to the **cerebellum** can result in severe impairments to motor coordination, balance, and voluntary muscle movements.
   小脑损伤可能导致运动协调、平衡和自主肌肉运动的严重障碍。

3. The surgeon carefully navigated around the **cerebellum** during the delicate procedure to remove the posterior fossa tumor.
   外科医生在精细手术中小心地绕过小脑，以切除后颅窝肿瘤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cerebellum" as "cerebr- (brain) + -ellum (little)" — it literally means "little brain" in Latin, which describes its smaller size compared to the cerebrum.
把"cerebellum"想成"cerebr-（脑）+ -ellum（小的）"——它在拉丁语中的字面意思是"小脑"，因为它比大脑（cerebrum）小。
