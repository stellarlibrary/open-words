# 💎 exclusive

🔊 /ɪkˈskluːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Restricted to a particular person, group, or area; not shared; excluding others from participation; high-end and selective
**中文：** 专有的；独占的；排他的；除外的；高档的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sole（唯一的）
- select（精选的）
- restricted（受限的）
- elite（精英的）
- private（私有的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exclusive rights / exclusive access / exclusive club / mutually exclusive / exclusive interview**

👉 Example: The journalist secured an exclusive interview with the reclusive Nobel laureate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The publishing house obtained **exclusive** rights to distribute the acclaimed author's complete works throughout the European market.
   这家出版社获得了在整个欧洲市场分销这位著名作家全部作品的独家版权。

2. These two hypotheses are not mutually **exclusive** and may, in fact, operate simultaneously to produce the observed phenomenon.
   这两个假设并非相互排斥，实际上可能同时起作用以产生观察到的现象。

3. The **exclusive** resort caters only to affluent clientele willing to pay premium rates for unparalleled privacy and luxury.
   这个高档度假村仅面向愿意支付高价以享受无与伦比的隐私和奢华的富裕客户。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-clusive" — it "closes" (cludes) others out (ex), keeping something only for a select few.
把"exclusive"想成"ex-clusive"——它把其他人关在（cludes）外面（ex），只为少数精选者保留。
