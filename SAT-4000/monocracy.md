# 🏛️ monocracy

🔊 /mɒˈnɒkrəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A system of government in which one person has absolute and unchecked power; autocratic rule by a single individual
**中文：** n. 独裁政治；独裁统治

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- autocracy（专制政治）
- dictatorship（独裁统治）
- tyranny（暴政）
- despotism（专制主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **establish a monocracy / rule under monocracy / transition from monocracy / oppose monocracy**

👉 Example: The nation suffered under a monocracy for decades before democratic reforms were finally introduced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Political analysts warned that the concentration of executive power was gradually transforming the republic into a **monocracy**.
   政治分析人士警告说，行政权力的集中正在逐步将共和国转变为独裁政体。

2. The **monocracy** suppressed all forms of dissent, leaving citizens with no legal means to challenge governmental authority.
   独裁政权压制一切形式的异议，使公民没有合法手段挑战政府权威。

3. Historians often compare the ancient **monocracy** of that empire with modern authoritarian regimes found across several continents.
   历史学家经常将该帝国古代的独裁政治与现代几个大洲上的威权政权进行比较。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mono" (one) + "cracy" (rule) — monocracy literally means "rule by one," a government controlled by a single person.
把"monocracy"拆成"mono"（一个）+"cracy"（统治）——独裁政治字面意思是"一个人的统治"。
