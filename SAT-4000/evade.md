# 🏃 evade

🔊 /ɪˈveɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To escape or avoid something, especially by cleverness or trickery; to circumvent or sidestep
**中文：** v. 逃避；规避；逃脱；躲避

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- avoid（避免）
- elude（逃避）
- dodge（躲避）
- circumvent（规避）
- sidestep（回避）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **evade taxes / evade responsibility / evade the question / evade capture**

👉 Example: The suspect managed to evade capture for months by constantly changing locations and using false identities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation attempted to **evade** federal tax regulations by establishing offshore accounts in multiple jurisdictions simultaneously.
   该公司试图通过同时在多个司法管辖区设立离岸账户来规避联邦税收法规。

2. When pressed by journalists, the senator continued to **evade** direct questions about the allocation of campaign funds.
   当记者追问时，那位参议员继续回避关于竞选资金分配的直接问题。

3. The invasive species has managed to **evade** every eradication effort implemented by environmental authorities over the past decade.
   这种入侵物种成功逃避了环境部门在过去十年中实施的所有根除行动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-vade" as "e" (out) + "vade" (go, from Latin vadere) — to go out of the way, to escape from something.
把"evade"想成"e"（出去）+ "vade"（走，源自拉丁语vadere）——走开，逃离某事物。
