# 🌾 field

🔊 /fiːld/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A broad area of study or activity; an open area of land used for farming or pasture; a wide expanse of open ground
**中文：** n. 领域；牧场；旷野；场

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- domain（领域）
- discipline（学科）
- meadow（草地）
- realm（范围）
- pasture（牧场）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **field of study / field of expertise / in the field / open field / field research**

👉 Example: Her research in the field of molecular biology has earned international recognition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor has published extensively in the **field** of comparative literature over the past two decades.
   这位教授在过去二十年中在比较文学领域发表了大量著作。

2. Researchers conducted a comprehensive **field** study to observe the migratory patterns of arctic waterfowl.
   研究人员进行了一项全面的实地研究，以观察北极水禽的迁徙模式。

3. The vast **field** stretched endlessly toward the horizon, covered with golden wheat ready for harvest.
   广阔的田野向地平线无尽延伸，覆盖着准备收割的金色麦穗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "field" as a wide open space — whether it is a physical meadow or an intellectual domain, it represents a broad area to explore.
把"field"想象成一片广阔的空间——无论是实际的草地还是知识的领域，它都代表着一个可以探索的广大区域。
