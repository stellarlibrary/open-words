# 👁️ obtrusive

🔊 /əbˈtruːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Noticeable in an unwelcome or intrusive way; tending to push oneself or one's ideas forward
**中文：** adj. 突出的；强迫的；冒失的；引人注意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conspicuous（显眼的）
- intrusive（侵入的）
- pushy（爱出风头的）
- prominent（突出的）
- noticeable（显眼的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obtrusive presence / obtrusive behavior / obtrusive advertising / overly obtrusive**

👉 Example: The obtrusive surveillance cameras made visitors feel that their privacy was constantly being violated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect designed the security features to be functional yet not **obtrusive**, blending seamlessly with the building's aesthetic.
   建筑师将安全设施设计得既实用又不突兀，与建筑的美学风格无缝融合。

2. Critics argued that the **obtrusive** product placements in the film detracted significantly from the integrity of the narrative.
   评论家认为电影中过于突出的产品植入严重损害了叙事的完整性。

3. The new employee's **obtrusive** manner of inserting himself into every conversation quickly irritated his more reserved colleagues.
   那位新员工强行插入每一次谈话的冒失举止很快就惹恼了他较为含蓄的同事们。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-TRUSIVE" as related to "obtrude" — something that obtrudes is obtrusive, sticking out where it is not wanted.
把"obtrusive"想成和"obtrude（强加）"相关——强行突出的东西就是obtrusive的，出现在不受欢迎的地方。
