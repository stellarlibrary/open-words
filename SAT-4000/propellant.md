# 🔥 propellant

🔊 /prəˈpɛlənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance used to propel or push something forward, especially fuel for rockets or gas in aerosol cans. (adj.) Capable of propelling
**中文：** n. 推进物；推进燃料；发射火药 adj. 推进的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fuel（燃料）
- thrust agent（推力剂）
- propulsion（推进力）
- accelerant（助燃剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rocket propellant / solid propellant / liquid propellant / propellant charge**

👉 Example: Engineers carefully loaded the liquid propellant into the rocket's fuel tanks before the launch countdown began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. NASA scientists developed a new type of **propellant** that significantly increases fuel efficiency for deep space exploration missions.
   NASA科学家开发了一种新型推进剂，大幅提高了深空探测任务的燃料效率。

2. The environmental agency banned certain aerosol **propellants** because they were found to contribute to ozone layer depletion.
   环境机构禁止了某些气雾剂推进剂，因为它们被发现会导致臭氧层损耗。

3. Solid **propellant** motors are preferred for military applications due to their reliability and ease of long-term storage.
   固体推进剂发动机因其可靠性和易于长期储存而在军事应用中受到青睐。
