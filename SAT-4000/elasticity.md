# 🔬 elasticity

🔊 /ˌiːlæˈstɪsəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ability of an object or material to resume its normal shape after being stretched or compressed; flexibility or adaptability
**中文：** n. 弹性；弹力；灵活性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flexibility（柔韧性）
- resilience（回弹力）
- suppleness（柔软）
- pliability（可塑性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elasticity of demand / elasticity of supply / price elasticity / lose elasticity**

👉 Example: The elasticity of the rubber band allows it to stretch without breaking under moderate force.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **elasticity** of human skin decreases significantly with age, leading to the formation of wrinkles and sagging tissue.
   人类皮肤的弹性随年龄增长而显著下降，导致皱纹和组织松弛的形成。

2. Economists measure the price **elasticity** of demand to determine how consumer purchasing behavior responds to price changes.
   经济学家通过测量需求的价格弹性来确定消费者购买行为对价格变化的反应。

3. The new polymer material demonstrated remarkable **elasticity**, returning to its original shape after being compressed by fifty percent.
   这种新型聚合物材料展现出卓越的弹性，在被压缩百分之五十后能恢复原始形状。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elastic" + "-ity" — like an elastic band that always snaps back to its original shape, elasticity is the quality of bouncing back.
把"elasticity"想成"elastic（弹性的）"加"-ity"——就像橡皮筋总能弹回原形，弹性就是恢复原状的特质。
