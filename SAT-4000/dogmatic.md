# 📜 dogmatic

🔊 /dɔːɡˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inclined to lay down principles as undeniably true; asserting opinions in an arrogant or authoritative manner
**中文：** adj. 教条的；武断的；独断的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- authoritarian（专断的）
- opinionated（固执己见的）
- doctrinaire（教条主义的）
- peremptory（专横的）
- imperious（专横的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dogmatic belief / dogmatic approach / dogmatic stance / dogmatic assertion**

👉 Example: The professor's dogmatic approach to the subject left no room for intellectual debate among the students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee criticized the director's **dogmatic** insistence on outdated procedures that hindered organizational progress and innovation.
   委员会批评了主任对过时程序的教条坚持，这阻碍了组织的进步和创新。

2. A truly open-minded scholar avoids **dogmatic** thinking and remains receptive to evidence that challenges established theories.
   一位真正开明的学者会避免教条式思维，并始终接受挑战既定理论的证据。

3. His **dogmatic** refusal to consider alternative solutions ultimately resulted in the failure of the entire project.
   他武断地拒绝考虑替代方案，最终导致了整个项目的失败。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dogma + tic" — someone with a "tic" for dogma, compulsively sticking to rigid beliefs without questioning them.
想象"dogma + tic"——一个对教条有"强迫症"的人，不加质疑地固守僵化的信念。
