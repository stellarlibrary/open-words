# 💪 recuperate

🔊 /rɪˈkuːpəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To recover from illness, exhaustion, or loss; to regain health, strength, or composure
**中文：** v. 恢复；复原；挽回损失

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recover（恢复）
- convalesce（康复）
- rally（振作）
- heal（痊愈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recuperate from an illness / recuperate losses / recuperate one's strength / recuperate fully**

👉 Example: After the major surgery, she needed several weeks to recuperate at home before returning to work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The executive took a three-month leave of absence to **recuperate** from the severe burnout caused by relentless workloads.
   这位高管休了三个月的假，以从繁重工作负荷造成的严重倦怠中恢复。

2. The national economy required nearly a decade to fully **recuperate** from the devastating effects of the financial crisis.
   国家经济花了近十年时间才从金融危机的毁灭性影响中完全恢复。

3. Doctors recommended that the patient **recuperate** in a warm climate to accelerate the healing of her respiratory condition.
   医生建议患者在温暖的气候中疗养，以加速她呼吸系统疾病的康复。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-cuper-ate" — "re" means again and "cuper" sounds like "cooperate," so you cooperate with your body again to get well.
把"recuperate"想成"re(重新) + cuper(恢复) + ate"——重新恢复健康，即康复。
