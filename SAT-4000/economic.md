# 💹 economic

🔊 /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to economics or the economy; concerned with the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services
**中文：** adj. 经济的；经济学的；有利可图的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- financial（金融的）
- fiscal（财政的）
- monetary（货币的）
- commercial（商业的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **economic growth / economic policy / economic crisis / economic development / economic impact**

👉 Example: The government introduced a series of economic reforms to stimulate long-term sustainable growth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation experienced a prolonged period of **economic** stagnation following the global financial crisis of the previous decade.
   该国在上一个十年的全球金融危机后经历了长期的经济停滞。

2. Scholars continue to debate whether **economic** globalization benefits developing nations or exacerbates existing inequalities among them.
   学者们继续争论经济全球化究竟有利于发展中国家还是加剧了它们之间的不平等。

3. The committee released a comprehensive report analyzing the **economic** consequences of proposed environmental regulations on industry.
   委员会发布了一份综合报告，分析拟议环境法规对工业的经济影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eco-nomic" as "eco" (household, from Greek oikos) + "nomic" (management) — managing the household of a nation's resources.
把"economic"想成"eco"（家庭，源自希腊语oikos）+"nomic"（管理）——管理一个国家的资源家庭。
