# 🦊 wile

🔊 /waɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A trick or stratagem intended to deceive or ensnare; cunning deception. (v.) To lure or entice
**中文：** n. 诡计，阴谋，欺骗 v. 欺骗，诱骗；消遣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stratagem（计谋）
- ruse（诡计）
- trick（花招）
- artifice（诡计）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feminine wiles / use one's wiles / wiles and tricks / wile away the hours**

👉 Example: The cunning diplomat used all her wiles to secure a favorable trade agreement for her nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The protagonist employed clever **wiles** to outwit the antagonist and escape the elaborate trap set in the abandoned warehouse.
   主人公运用巧妙的诡计智胜了反派，逃脱了在废弃仓库中设置的精密陷阱。

2. Political analysts warned that voters must remain vigilant against the **wiles** of candidates who make unrealistic campaign promises.
   政治分析人士警告选民必须对那些做出不切实际竞选承诺的候选人的诡计保持警惕。

3. Through subtle **wiles** and persuasive rhetoric, the negotiator managed to convince both parties to accept the compromise agreement.
   通过巧妙的手段和有说服力的言辞，谈判者设法说服双方接受了妥协协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wile" as in "Wile E. Coyote" from cartoons — he is always scheming up wiles to catch the Road Runner.
把"wile"想成卡通里的"Wile E. Coyote（怀尔狼）"——他总是想出各种诡计来抓走鹃。
