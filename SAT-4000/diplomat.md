# 🤝 diplomat

🔊 /ˈdɪpləmæt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An official representing a country abroad; a person skilled in managing sensitive negotiations tactfully
**中文：** n. 外交家，外交官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- envoy（使节）
- ambassador（大使）
- emissary（特使）
- negotiator（谈判者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **career diplomat / seasoned diplomat / skilled diplomat / diplomat stationed in**

👉 Example: The seasoned diplomat brokered a ceasefire between the two warring factions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **diplomat** was dispatched to the conflict zone to negotiate the terms of a lasting peace agreement.
   这位外交官被派往冲突地区，就持久和平协议的条款进行谈判。

2. As a trained **diplomat**, she possessed the rare ability to mediate disputes without offending either party involved.
   作为一名训练有素的外交官，她拥有在不冒犯任何一方的情况下调解纠纷的罕见能力。

3. The retiring **diplomat** reflected on three decades of service representing his nation across multiple continents.
   这位即将退休的外交官回顾了自己在多个大洲代表国家服务的三十年。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diploma-t" — a person who holds a "diploma" in the art of negotiation and international relations.
把"diplomat"想成拿着"外交文凭(diploma)"的人——在谈判和国际关系方面有专业训练的人。
