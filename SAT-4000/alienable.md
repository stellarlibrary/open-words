# 📜 alienable

🔊 /ˈeɪ.li.ə.nə.bəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of being transferred, sold, or conveyed to another owner, especially regarding property or rights.
**中文：** 可让与的，可转让的（尤指财产或权利）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transferable（可转让的）
- conveyable（可转让的）
- assignable（可分配的）
- negotiable（可协商转让的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alienable rights, alienable property, alienable assets, alienable interests**
👉 Example: The estate's alienable assets were divided among the beneficiaries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Under the terms of the contract, the intellectual property was deemed **alienable** and could be sold freely.
   根据合同条款，该知识产权被视为可转让的，可以自由出售。

2. The court ruled that the disputed land was **alienable** and therefore subject to commercial transaction.
   法院裁定争议土地是可转让的，因此可以进行商业交易。

3. Unlike fundamental human rights, certain statutory entitlements are considered **alienable** by legal scholars.
   与基本人权不同，法学学者认为某些法定权利是可以转让的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alienable" = "alien" + "able" — property that is "able" to become "alien" (belong to someone else).
"alienable" = "alien（他人的）" + "able（能够的）"——能够变成"他人的"财产，即可转让的。
