# 💍 betrothal

🔊 /bɪˈtrɒθəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A formal agreement or promise to marry; the act of becoming engaged
**中文：** 订婚；婚约

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engagement（订婚）
- espousal（婚约）
- troth（誓约）
- pledge（承诺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **formal betrothal / announce a betrothal / betrothal ceremony / betrothal agreement**

👉 Example: The betrothal was announced at a lavish ceremony attended by both families.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **betrothal** of the prince to the foreign dignitary's daughter strengthened diplomatic relations between the two kingdoms.
   王子与外国显贵之女的订婚加强了两国之间的外交关系。

2. In many historical societies, a formal **betrothal** carried nearly the same legal weight as the marriage itself.
   在许多历史社会中，正式的婚约几乎与婚姻本身具有同等的法律效力。

3. The sudden dissolution of the **betrothal** caused considerable scandal among the aristocratic families of the region.
   婚约的突然解除在该地区的贵族家庭中引起了相当大的丑闻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-troth-al" — "troth" means a pledge of faithfulness, so a betrothal is the act of pledging your troth to marry someone.
把"betrothal"拆分为"be-troth-al"——"troth"意为忠诚的誓言，所以betrothal就是向某人许下结婚誓言的行为。
