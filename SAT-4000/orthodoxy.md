# 📜 orthodoxy

🔊 /ˈɔːrθədɑːksi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of conforming to established doctrine, belief, or practice; an authorized or generally accepted theory or practice.
**中文：** n. 正统；正教；正统说法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conformity（遵从）
- convention（惯例）
- doctrine（教义）
- tradition（传统）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **religious orthodoxy / challenge orthodoxy / political orthodoxy / defy orthodoxy**

👉 Example: The researcher's groundbreaking findings challenged the prevailing orthodoxy in the field of genetics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher devoted his career to questioning the **orthodoxy** that had dominated Western thought for centuries.
   这位哲学家毕生致力于质疑统治西方思想数百年的正统观念。

2. Political **orthodoxy** within the party made it difficult for reformers to propose innovative policy solutions.
   党内的政治正统使改革者难以提出创新的政策方案。

3. The new evidence threatened to overturn the scientific **orthodoxy** regarding the origins of the ancient civilization.
   新证据有可能推翻关于这一古代文明起源的科学正统说法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "orthodoxy" as "orthodox" + "-y" (a state) — the state of being orthodox, the condition of holding correct or accepted beliefs.
把"orthodoxy"想成"orthodox"（正统的）+"-y"（状态）——处于正统的状态，持有正确或公认信仰的状态。
