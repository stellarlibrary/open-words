# 📋 duplicate

🔊 /ˈdjuːplɪkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective | 动词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make an exact copy of something. (n.) An identical copy or reproduction. (adj.) Exactly like something else; copied
**中文：** v. 复制；复写 n. 副本；复制品 adj. 复制的；完全相同的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- replicate（复制）
- copy（拷贝）
- reproduce（再生产）
- clone（克隆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **duplicate a key / duplicate copy / in duplicate / duplicate effort**

👉 Example: Please submit the application form in duplicate so we can retain a copy for our records.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician was instructed to **duplicate** the experiment under identical conditions to verify the original findings.
   实验室技术员被要求在相同条件下复制实验，以验证最初的发现。

2. The museum displayed a **duplicate** of the ancient manuscript because the original was far too fragile for public exhibition.
   博物馆展出了这份古代手稿的复制品，因为原件太脆弱了，不适合公开展出。

3. Management asked the departments to coordinate their efforts carefully to avoid **duplicate** work and unnecessary expenditure of resources.
   管理层要求各部门仔细协调工作，以避免重复劳动和不必要的资源浪费。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "duplicate" as "du-plic-ate" — "du" sounds like "two" and "plic" relates to "fold," so it means to make a second fold or copy of something.
把"duplicate"想成"du-plic-ate"——"du"听起来像"two"（二），"plic"与"fold"（折叠）有关，所以意味着制作某物的第二份副本。
