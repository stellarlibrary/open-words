# 🐣 callow

🔊 /ˈkæloʊ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Young and inexperienced; immature; (of a young bird) lacking feathers
**中文：** adj. 年轻而无经验的；不成熟的；未生羽毛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inexperienced（缺乏经验的）
- immature（不成熟的）
- naive（天真的）
- green（稚嫩的）
- unseasoned（未经历练的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **callow youth / callow recruit / callow inexperience / callow young man**

👉 Example: The callow recruit quickly learned that real combat was nothing like the training exercises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **callow** intern made several avoidable errors during his first week, revealing his lack of practical experience.
   这位稚嫩的实习生在第一周犯了几个本可避免的错误，暴露了他缺乏实践经验。

2. Despite her **callow** appearance, the young diplomat proved remarkably adept at navigating complex international negotiations.
   尽管看起来年轻稚嫩，这位年轻的外交官在处理复杂的国际谈判中表现得异常老练。

3. His **callow** enthusiasm for the project was admirable, though seasoned colleagues warned him of unforeseen challenges ahead.
   他对这个项目稚嫩的热情令人钦佩，尽管经验丰富的同事警告他前方有不可预见的挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "callow" as "cal-low" — someone with "low" experience, like a baby bird still too young to fly with "low" feathers.
把"callow"想成"cal-low"——经验"低"（low）的人，就像一只羽毛尚未丰满、还不能飞的幼鸟。
