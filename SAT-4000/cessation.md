# 🛑 cessation

🔊 /seˈseɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of ceasing; a complete stop or discontinuation of an activity, process, or state
**中文：** n. 停止；中止；中断

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- termination（终止）
- discontinuation（中断）
- halt（暂停）
- suspension（暂停）
- stoppage（停顿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cessation of hostilities / cessation of operations / smoking cessation / cessation agreement**

👉 Example: The two nations agreed upon an immediate cessation of hostilities along the disputed border.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The peace treaty mandated an immediate **cessation** of all military operations within the contested territories along the northern frontier.
   和平条约要求在北部边境争议领土内立即停止一切军事行动。

2. Public health campaigns promoting smoking **cessation** have significantly reduced tobacco-related mortality rates over the past two decades.
   推动戒烟的公共健康运动在过去二十年中显著降低了与烟草相关的死亡率。

3. The abrupt **cessation** of federal funding forced several research institutions to suspend their ongoing climate change studies indefinitely.
   联邦资金的突然中断迫使几家研究机构无限期暂停正在进行的气候变化研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cessation" as "cease + -ation" — it is the noun form of "cease," meaning the act of stopping completely.
把"cessation"想成"cease（停止）+ -ation（行为）"——它是"cease"的名词形式，意思是完全停止的行为。
