# 🔬 paradigm

🔊 /ˈpærəˌdaɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A typical example or pattern of something; a model or framework for understanding
**中文：** n. 范例；典范；词形变化表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- model（模型）
- exemplar（典范）
- prototype（原型）
- framework（框架）
- standard（标准）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **paradigm shift / new paradigm / dominant paradigm / paradigm of**

👉 Example: The discovery of DNA represented a paradigm shift in the field of molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientific community experienced a fundamental **paradigm** shift when quantum mechanics challenged the principles of classical physics.
   当量子力学挑战了经典物理学的原理时，科学界经历了一次根本性的范式转变。

2. This innovative approach has become the prevailing **paradigm** for addressing complex environmental sustainability challenges worldwide.
   这种创新方法已成为全球应对复杂环境可持续性挑战的主流范式。

3. The professor urged her students to question the established **paradigm** rather than accepting conventional assumptions without scrutiny.
   教授敦促学生质疑既定范式，而不是不加审视地接受传统假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "para-digm" as "para" (beside) + a "dig" into a "model" — you dig beside a model to find the perfect example.
把"paradigm"想成"para"（旁边）+ "dig"（挖掘）——在模型旁边挖掘，找到完美的范例。
