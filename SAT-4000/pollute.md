# 🏭 pollute

🔊 /pəˈluːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To contaminate or make impure, especially by introducing harmful or undesirable substances into the environment; to corrupt or defile
**中文：** v. 弄脏；污染；玷污；败坏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contaminate（污染）
- taint（玷污）
- defile（弄脏）
- corrupt（败坏）
- foul（弄脏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pollute the environment / pollute the water / pollute the air / pollute young minds**

👉 Example: Industrial waste continues to pollute rivers and threaten the health of surrounding communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Unregulated factories along the river have continued to **pollute** the waterways with toxic chemicals and industrial effluent.
   沿河未受监管的工厂持续用有毒化学物质和工业废水污染水道。

2. Critics argued that sensationalist media coverage could **pollute** public discourse and erode trust in legitimate journalistic institutions.
   批评人士认为，耸人听闻的媒体报道可能败坏公共话语并削弱对正规新闻机构的信任。

3. The environmental agency imposed heavy fines on corporations found to have **polluted** protected wetlands with hazardous waste materials.
   环保机构对被发现用危险废物污染受保护湿地的企业处以巨额罚款。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pollute" as "poll-ute" — imagine pouring pollution into a pool until it becomes utterly unusable and dirty.
把"pollute"想成往"pool（水池）"里倒污物——直到它变得完全不能使用，又脏又臭。
