# 🎵 choral

🔊 /ˈkɔːrəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or sung by a choir or chorus. (n.) A hymn or psalm sung to a traditional or harmonized melody
**中文：** adj. 合唱的；合唱队的 n. 赞美诗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vocal（声乐的）
- harmonic（和声的）
- melodic（旋律的）
- hymnal（赞美诗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **choral music / choral performance / choral arrangement / choral society / choral work**

👉 Example: The choral performance at the cathedral left the audience deeply moved by its sublime harmonies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's **choral** ensemble performed a magnificent rendition of Handel's Messiah at the annual winter concert.
   该大学的**合唱**团在年度冬季音乐会上精彩演绎了亨德尔的《弥赛亚》。

2. Several distinguished composers contributed original **choral** works to the festival celebrating three centuries of sacred music.
   几位杰出的作曲家为庆祝三个世纪宗教音乐的节日贡献了原创**合唱**作品。

3. The **choral** tradition at this institution dates back to the seventeenth century and remains a source of great pride.
   该机构的**合唱**传统可追溯至十七世纪，至今仍是巨大的骄傲来源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "choral" as "choir + al" — it is the adjective form of choir, describing anything related to group singing.
把"choral"想成"choir + al"——它是choir的形容词形式，描述与合唱相关的一切。
