# 🍀 propitious

🔊 /prəˈpɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Giving or indicating a good chance of success; favorable; presenting advantageous conditions
**中文：** adj. 适合的；吉利的；顺利的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- auspicious（吉利的）
- favorable（有利的）
- opportune（适时的）
- fortunate（幸运的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **propitious moment / propitious conditions / propitious time / propitious for success**

👉 Example: The calm weather provided propitious conditions for the explorers to begin their mountain ascent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economic recovery created a **propitious** environment for entrepreneurs seeking to launch innovative technology startups in the region.
   经济复苏为寻求在该地区创办创新技术初创企业的企业家创造了有利的环境。

2. Diplomats waited for a **propitious** moment to introduce the peace proposal, knowing that timing was absolutely critical.
   外交官们等待一个合适的时机提出和平提案，因为他们知道时机至关重要。

3. The **propitious** alignment of funding and public support made this the ideal year to undertake the ambitious infrastructure project.
   资金和公众支持的有利结合使今年成为开展这项宏大基础设施项目的理想年份。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "propitious" as "pro-piteous" reversed — instead of piteous (pitiful), things are going in your favor, pro-you.
把"propitious"想成"pro"（有利）加上"itious"——一切都对你有利，形势大好。
