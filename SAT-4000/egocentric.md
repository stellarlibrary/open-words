# 🪞 egocentric

🔊 /ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Thinking only of oneself; centered on one's own interests without regard for others. (n.) A self-centered person
**中文：** adj. 自我中心的；利己的 n. 利己主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- self-centered（以自我为中心的）
- narcissistic（自恋的）
- selfish（自私的）
- self-absorbed（自我沉迷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **egocentric behavior / egocentric perspective / egocentric worldview / egocentric individual**

👉 Example: The psychologist noted that egocentric thinking is a normal stage in early childhood cognitive development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The executive's **egocentric** leadership style alienated colleagues and ultimately led to a sharp decline in team morale.
   这位高管以自我为中心的领导风格疏远了同事，最终导致团队士气急剧下降。

2. Developmental psychologists observe that children gradually outgrow **egocentric** thinking as they acquire perspective-taking abilities.
   发展心理学家观察到，随着儿童获得换位思考能力，他们会逐渐摆脱自我中心的思维。

3. Critics dismissed the memoir as an **egocentric** narrative that lacked genuine reflection on the author's broader social impact.
   评论家们认为这本回忆录是一部以自我为中心的叙述，缺乏对作者更广泛社会影响的真正反思。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EGO-centric" — your ego is at the center of everything; an egocentric person places themselves at the center of the world.
把"egocentric"想成"EGO-centric"——你的自我（ego）处于一切的中心；一个egocentric的人把自己置于世界的中心。
