# 📟 obsolescent

🔊 /ˌɑːbsəˈlesənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In the process of becoming outdated or obsolete; gradually falling into disuse
**中文：** adj. 荒废的；即将过时的；逐渐被废弃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outdating（正在过时的）
- declining（衰落的）
- aging（老化的）
- outmoded（过时的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obsolescent technology / obsolescent equipment / becoming obsolescent / obsolescent practices**

👉 Example: The obsolescent machinery will need to be replaced within the next few years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company invested heavily in upgrading its **obsolescent** manufacturing equipment before competitors gained a decisive technological advantage.
   该公司大力投资升级其即将过时的生产设备，以防竞争对手获得决定性的技术优势。

2. Many educators argue that traditional lecture-based teaching methods are becoming increasingly **obsolescent** in the age of digital learning.
   许多教育工作者认为，在数字化学习时代，传统的讲授式教学方法正变得越来越过时。

3. The government report identified several **obsolescent** regulations that no longer served their original purpose and required comprehensive revision.
   政府报告指出了几项逐渐过时的法规，它们已不再服务于最初的目的，需要全面修订。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "obsolescent" as "obsolete +escent (becoming)" — the suffix "-escent" means "becoming," so obsolescent means in the process of becoming obsolete.
把"obsolescent"想成"obsolete + -escent（正在变成）"——后缀"-escent"表示"正在变成"，所以obsolescent意味着正在变得过时。
