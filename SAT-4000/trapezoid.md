# 📐 trapezoid

🔊 /ˈtræpɪzɔɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A quadrilateral with exactly one pair of parallel sides. (adj.) Having the shape of a trapezoid
**中文：** n. 梯形 adj. 梯形的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trapezium（梯形，英式用法）
- quadrilateral（四边形）
- polygon（多边形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **trapezoid shape / area of a trapezoid / trapezoid rule / isosceles trapezoid**

👉 Example: The area of a trapezoid is calculated by averaging the two parallel sides and multiplying by the height.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geometry instructor demonstrated how to calculate the area of a **trapezoid** using the standard formula involving its parallel sides.
   几何老师演示了如何使用涉及平行边的标准公式来计算梯形的面积。

2. The architect designed the building's footprint as an irregular **trapezoid** to maximize the use of the oddly shaped lot.
   建筑师将建筑的平面设计为不规则梯形，以最大限度地利用这块形状奇特的地块。

3. In anatomy, the **trapezoid** bone is a small carpal bone located in the wrist between the trapezium and capitate.
   在解剖学中，小多角骨是位于腕部大多角骨和头状骨之间的一块小腕骨。
