# 📚 perusal

🔊 /pəˈruːzəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of reading or examining something carefully and thoroughly; close or attentive reading
**中文：** n. 熟读；精读

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examination（审查）
- scrutiny（细查）
- inspection（检查）
- review（审阅）
- study（研究）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **for your perusal / careful perusal / upon perusal / perusal of the document / close perusal**

👉 Example: The contract has been forwarded to legal counsel for thorough perusal before signing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A careful **perusal** of the historical archives revealed previously overlooked correspondence between the two prominent political figures.
   对历史档案的仔细阅读揭示了这两位杰出政治人物之间此前被忽视的通信。

2. The editor recommended a thorough **perusal** of the manuscript before submitting it to the peer review committee.
   编辑建议在提交给同行评审委员会之前对手稿进行一次彻底的精读。

3. Upon **perusal** of the revised regulations, the compliance officers identified several provisions requiring immediate operational adjustments.
   在仔细阅读修订后的法规后，合规官员发现了几项需要立即进行运营调整的条款。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perusal" as the noun form of "peruse" — a perusal is what you do when you sit down to peruse a document with full attention.
把"perusal"想成"peruse（精读）"的名词形式——perusal就是你坐下来全神贯注地精读一份文件时所做的事。
