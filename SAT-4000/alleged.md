# 🕵 alleged

🔊 /əˈlɛdʒd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Said or claimed to be the case, but not yet proven or confirmed
**中文：** 所谓的；声称的；被断言的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supposed（假定的）
- purported（据称的）
- claimed（声称的）
- reputed（据说的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alleged crime / alleged perpetrator / alleged misconduct / alleged fraud**

👉 Example: The newspaper reported on the alleged corruption scandal involving several senior officials.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **alleged** perpetrator was apprehended by authorities shortly after the incident was reported to the police.
   据称的肇事者在事件被报告给警方后不久便被当局逮捕。

2. Several witnesses came forward to testify against the **alleged** fraud scheme that had deceived hundreds of investors.
   数名证人站出来指证这一据称的欺诈计划，该计划欺骗了数百名投资者。

3. The company denied all involvement in the **alleged** violation of environmental regulations cited by federal inspectors.
   该公司否认参与了联邦检查人员所指控的违反环境法规的行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alleged" as "all-edged" — the claim has edges but no solid center, meaning it is not yet proven.
把"alleged"想成"all-edged"——这个说法只有边缘却没有实心，意味着尚未被证实。
