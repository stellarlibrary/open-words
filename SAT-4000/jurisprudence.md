# 📚 jurisprudence

🔊 /ˌdʒʊrɪsˈpruːdəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The theory, philosophy, or science of law; a body or system of laws and legal principles
**中文：** n. 法学；法律体系；法学及其分支

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- legal theory（法学理论）
- law（法律）
- legal philosophy（法律哲学）
- legal science（法律科学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **medical jurisprudence / comparative jurisprudence / school of jurisprudence / constitutional jurisprudence**

👉 Example: The course in jurisprudence examined how legal principles evolve in response to societal change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The landmark ruling significantly altered the nation's **jurisprudence** on privacy rights in the digital age.
   这一里程碑式的裁决在数字时代极大地改变了该国关于隐私权的法学理论。

2. Students of **jurisprudence** must grapple with fundamental questions about the nature and purpose of legal systems.
   法学学生必须深入思考关于法律体系的本质和目的等根本性问题。

3. Her scholarly contributions to feminist **jurisprudence** have profoundly influenced contemporary debates about gender equality under the law.
   她对女性主义法学的学术贡献深刻地影响了当代关于法律下性别平等的辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "juris" (law) + "prudence" (wisdom) — jurisprudence is the wisdom of the law, the deep study of legal principles.
拆分记忆："juris"（法律）+ "prudence"（智慧）——jurisprudence就是法律的智慧，即对法律原则的深入研究。
