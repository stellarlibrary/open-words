# 🚀 oxidizer

🔊 /ˈɑːksɪdaɪzər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A substance that provides oxygen or accepts electrons in a chemical reaction, enabling or accelerating combustion or oxidation
**中文：** n. 氧化剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oxidizing agent（氧化剂）
- oxidant（氧化剂）
- reagent（试剂）
- combustion agent（助燃剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chemical oxidizer / powerful oxidizer / liquid oxidizer / rocket oxidizer / oxidizer tank**

👉 Example: The rocket engine requires a carefully measured supply of oxidizer to sustain combustion in the vacuum of space.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Liquid oxygen serves as the primary **oxidizer** in many modern rocket propulsion systems used for both commercial and military launches.
   液态氧是许多现代火箭推进系统中的主要氧化剂，用于商业和军事发射。

2. The safety inspector warned that storing a powerful **oxidizer** near flammable materials could create an extremely dangerous fire hazard.
   安全检查员警告说，将强氧化剂存放在易燃材料附近可能会造成极其危险的火灾隐患。

3. Researchers synthesized a novel **oxidizer** compound that significantly improved the efficiency of solid-fuel combustion in experimental engines.
   研究人员合成了一种新型氧化剂化合物，显著提高了实验发动机中固体燃料燃烧的效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oxidiz-er" as "the thing that oxidizes" — an oxidizer is the agent or substance that causes oxidation to happen.
把"oxidizer"想成"进行oxidize（氧化）的东西"——氧化剂就是引起氧化反应发生的物质。
