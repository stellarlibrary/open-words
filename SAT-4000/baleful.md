# 😈 baleful

🔊 /ˈbeɪlfʊl/<br>
🏷️ *adj. | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Threatening harm; menacing; having a harmful or destructive influence; filled with malice or ill intent.

**中文：** 恶意的；有害的；充满敌意的；凶兆的，预示不祥的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- menacing（威胁的）
- malevolent（恶意的）
- sinister（阴险的）
- ominous（不祥的）
- pernicious（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Baleful" is a formal, literary word often used to describe looks, glances, influences, or atmospheres that convey threat or evil. A "baleful stare" is a classic collocation. It is stronger than merely "angry" — it implies genuine menace or destructive intent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dictator cast a **baleful** glare at the journalist who dared to question his authority during the press conference.
   那位独裁者向在新闻发布会上敢于质疑其权威的记者投去了**恶意的**一瞥。

2. The **baleful** influence of unchecked industrial pollution has devastated fragile ecosystems along the entire coastal region.
   不受控制的工业污染的**有害**影响已经摧毁了整个沿海地区脆弱的生态系统。

3. Villagers regarded the abandoned manor with suspicion, believing its **baleful** presence brought misfortune to the surrounding farmland.
   村民们怀疑地看着那座废弃的庄园，认为它**不祥的**存在给周围的农田带来了厄运。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Bale" (evil, calamity) + "ful" (full of) = "baleful" means literally "full of evil." Think: a "baleful" look is a look full of bad intentions.

"Bale（灾祸）"+"ful（充满）"="baleful"字面意思是"充满邪恶的"。联想：一个"baleful"的眼神就是一个充满恶意的眼神。
