# 🍬 saccharine

🔊 /ˈsækəraɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Excessively sweet or sentimental; containing or resembling sugar. (n.) An artificial sweetener
**中文：** adj. 糖的，似糖的；产糖的，含糖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sugary（甜腻的）
- cloying（过分甜蜜的）
- sentimental（多愁善感的）
- mawkish（感伤的）
- syrupy（甜得发腻的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **saccharine smile / saccharine sentimentality / saccharine tone / saccharine sweetness**

👉 Example: Critics dismissed the film as saccharine and overly sentimental, lacking any genuine emotional depth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician's **saccharine** speech about national unity struck many listeners as insincere and transparently manipulative.
   这位政治家关于国家团结的甜言蜜语让许多听众觉得虚伪且明显具有操纵性。

2. Beneath her **saccharine** demeanor lay a shrewd and calculating mind that few of her colleagues ever suspected.
   在她甜蜜的外表之下隐藏着一个精明且善于算计的头脑，她的同事们很少察觉。

3. The literary critic condemned the novel's **saccharine** ending as a betrayal of the otherwise complex narrative themes.
   文学评论家谴责这部小说过于甜腻的结局是对其本来复杂叙事主题的背叛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "saccharine" as related to "saccharide" (sugar molecule) — something so sweet it becomes artificial and excessive.
把"saccharine"联想到"saccharide"（糖类分子）——甜到过头就变成了虚假和过分。
