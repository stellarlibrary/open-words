# 💔 compunction

🔊 /kəmˈpʌŋkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A feeling of guilt or moral scruple that prevents or follows the doing of something bad; remorse or deep regret
**中文：** n. 良心的谴责；后悔；悔恨；内疚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- remorse（悔恨）
- regret（后悔）
- guilt（内疚）
- scruple（顾虑）
- contrition（悔过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **without compunction / feel no compunction / have compunction about / a pang of compunction**

👉 Example: The ruthless executive fired dozens of loyal employees without the slightest compunction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dictator showed no **compunction** about silencing journalists who dared to criticize government policies in their publications.
   这位独裁者对压制那些在出版物中敢于批评政府政策的记者毫无愧疚。

2. She felt a sudden pang of **compunction** after realizing that her harsh words had deeply hurt her closest friend.
   当她意识到自己尖刻的话深深伤害了最亲密的朋友时，她突然感到一阵内疚。

3. The corporation continued to pollute the river without **compunction**, ignoring repeated warnings from environmental protection agencies.
   该公司毫无悔意地继续污染河流，无视环保机构的反复警告。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-PUNCH-tion" — your conscience delivers a punch to your gut when you feel compunction.
把"compunction"想成"com-PUNCH（拳击）-tion"——当你感到内疚时，良心就像一拳打在你的胃上。
