# 🔁 recurrent

🔊 /rɪˈkʌrənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Occurring often or repeatedly; happening again and again, especially at regular intervals
**中文：** adj. 复发的；周期性的；经常发生的；循环的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recurring（反复出现的）
- periodic（周期性的）
- frequent（频繁的）
- repetitive（重复的）
- chronic（长期的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recurrent theme / recurrent problem / recurrent episodes / recurrent pattern**

👉 Example: The recurrent flooding in the coastal region prompted the government to invest in improved drainage infrastructure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **recurrent** disruptions to the supply chain exposed fundamental vulnerabilities in the company's global logistics network.
   供应链的反复中断暴露了该公司全球物流网络的根本脆弱性。

2. Scholars have identified a **recurrent** motif of exile and return in the literary tradition of postcolonial nations.
   学者们在后殖民国家的文学传统中发现了流放与回归这一反复出现的主题。

3. The patient was hospitalized due to **recurrent** episodes of cardiac arrhythmia that failed to respond to standard medication.
   患者因反复发作的心律不齐住院治疗，常规药物对此无效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "recur-rent" — something that keeps "recurring" like paying "rent" every month, it comes back regularly.
把"recurrent"想成"recur(再发生) + rent(租金)"——像每月要交的租金一样，反复发生的。
