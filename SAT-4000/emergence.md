# 🌱 emergence

🔊 /ɪˈmɜːrdʒəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of coming into being or becoming prominent; the act of appearing or coming forth from a concealed or undeveloped state
**中文：** n. 出现；浮现；发生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appearance（出现）
- arising（产生）
- development（发展）
- manifestation（显现）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the emergence of / emergence as a power / emergence of new technologies / rapid emergence**

👉 Example: The emergence of social media has fundamentally transformed how information is disseminated globally.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **emergence** of antibiotic-resistant bacteria has become one of the most pressing public health challenges of the modern era.
   抗生素耐药细菌的出现已成为现代最紧迫的公共卫生挑战之一。

2. Historians have studied the **emergence** of democratic institutions in ancient Athens as a foundational model for modern governance systems.
   历史学家研究了古雅典民主制度的出现，将其作为现代治理体系的基础模型。

3. The rapid **emergence** of artificial intelligence technologies has prompted urgent debates about regulation, ethics, and workforce displacement.
   人工智能技术的迅速出现引发了关于监管、伦理和劳动力替代的紧迫讨论。

---

把"emergence"想成"EMERGE的名词"——就像蝴蝶从茧中破茧而出（emergence），进入开放的世界。
