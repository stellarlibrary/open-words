# ⚖️ excess

🔊 /ɪkˈsɛs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An amount that is more than necessary, permitted, or desirable; overabundance. (adj.) More than what is normal or required; extra
**中文：** n. 过量；过剩；超额；无节制 adj. 过量的；额外的；附加的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surplus（过剩）
- overabundance（过多）
- superfluity（多余）
- glut（过量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in excess of / excess baggage / excess weight / to excess**

👉 Example: Spending in excess of one's income inevitably leads to significant financial difficulties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The factory produced goods in **excess** of market demand, resulting in substantial warehouse storage costs for the company.
   工厂生产的商品超过了市场需求，导致公司承担了大量仓储费用。

2. Medical researchers have confirmed that **excess** consumption of processed sugar significantly increases the risk of chronic diseases.
   医学研究人员已证实，过量摄入加工糖会显著增加患慢性疾病的风险。

3. The government imposed a tax on **excess** profits earned by corporations that benefited disproportionately from the economic crisis.
   政府对在经济危机中获得不成比例利益的企业的超额利润征收了税款。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-cess" as "exit access" — when too much tries to access, the extra must exit, leaving only the excess behind.
把"excess"想成"exit access"——当太多试图进入时，多余的必须离开，留下的就是过量的部分。
