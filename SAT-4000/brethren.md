# 🤝 brethren

🔊 /ˈbrɛðrɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Fellow members of a group, society, or religious community; brothers in a collective or spiritual sense
**中文：** n. 弟兄们；教友们；同胞；同党，会友

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brothers（兄弟们）
- comrades（同志们）
- fellows（同伴们）
- kinsmen（同胞们）
- congregation（会众）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fellow brethren / brethren in faith / our brethren / brethren of the order**

👉 Example: The pastor addressed the assembled brethren with a message of unity and shared purpose.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The monastery's **brethren** devoted themselves to a life of prayer, scholarship, and charitable service within the surrounding community.
   修道院的教友们献身于祈祷、学术研究以及在周围社区的慈善服务。

2. He called upon his professional **brethren** to unite against the proposed legislation that threatened their collective livelihoods and rights.
   他呼吁他的同行们团结起来，反对这项威胁他们集体生计和权利的拟议法案。

3. The ancient guild required all **brethren** to uphold strict ethical standards and contribute to the welfare of fellow members.
   这个古老的行会要求所有会友遵守严格的道德标准，并为其他成员的福祉做出贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brethren" as an old-fashioned plural of "brother" — your brethren are your brothers in spirit, faith, or community.
把"brethren"想成"brother"的古老复数形式——你的brethren就是精神上、信仰上或社区中的兄弟们。
