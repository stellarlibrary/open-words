# 🍞 knead

🔊 /niːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To work and press dough or clay with the hands; to massage or manipulate with a pressing motion
**中文：** v. 揉合，揉捏；按摩；捏制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- massage（按摩）
- mold（塑造）
- work（揉制）
- manipulate（捏弄）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **knead the dough / knead clay / knead muscles / knead into shape**

👉 Example: The baker instructed his students to knead the dough for at least ten minutes until it became smooth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artisan carefully **kneaded** the clay for several minutes before shaping it into an elegant ceramic vessel.
   工匠仔细地揉捏了几分钟黏土，然后将其塑造成一个精美的陶瓷器皿。

2. Physical therapists often **knead** the muscles surrounding an injury to promote circulation and accelerate the healing process.
   物理治疗师经常按摩伤口周围的肌肉以促进血液循环并加速愈合过程。

3. The experienced chef demonstrated how to **knead** the pasta dough with firm, rhythmic strokes until it reached proper consistency.
   经验丰富的厨师演示了如何用有力而有节奏的动作揉面团，直到达到适当的稠度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "knead" as sounding like "need" — you need to knead the dough before you can bake bread.
把"knead"想成发音像"need"（需要）——你需要（need）揉（knead）面团才能烤面包。
