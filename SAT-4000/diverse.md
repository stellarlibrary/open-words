# 🌍 diverse

🔊 /daɪˈvɜːrs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing a great deal of variety; very different from one another; including people of many different backgrounds
**中文：** adj. 不同的；多种多样的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- varied（各种各样的）
- heterogeneous（异质的）
- multifarious（多种多样的）
- eclectic（多元的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diverse population / diverse perspectives / culturally diverse / diverse range of**

👉 Example: The university prides itself on maintaining a diverse student body representing over sixty nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **diverse** workforce brings together a wide range of perspectives that can drive innovation and improve organizational performance.
   多元化的劳动力汇集了广泛的视角，可以推动创新并提升组织绩效。

2. The rainforest ecosystem supports an incredibly **diverse** array of species, many of which have not yet been formally classified.
   雨林生态系统支撑着极其多样的物种，其中许多尚未被正式分类。

3. Scholars emphasize that reading **diverse** literary works cultivates empathy and broadens one's understanding of different cultural traditions.
   学者们强调，阅读多样化的文学作品能培养共情能力并拓宽对不同文化传统的理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "di-verse" as "two verses" — like a poem with many different verses, each one unique and varied.
把"diverse"想成"di(多)-verse(诗节)"——就像一首有许多不同诗节的诗，每一节都独特而多样。
