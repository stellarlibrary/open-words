# 👃 odorous

🔊 /ˈoʊdərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a distinctive smell; emitting an odor, which may be pleasant or unpleasant
**中文：** adj. 有气味的；香的；难闻的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- smelly（有气味的）
- scented（有香味的）
- pungent（刺鼻的）
- aromatic（芳香的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **odorous gases / odorous substances / odorous waste / odorous emissions**

👉 Example: The factory released odorous emissions that prompted complaints from residents in the surrounding neighborhoods.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental inspectors traced the **odorous** fumes to a chemical plant that had been operating without proper emission controls.
   环境检查员追踪到有气味的烟雾来自一家未采取适当排放控制措施的化工厂。

2. The **odorous** herbs hanging from the ceiling of the medieval kitchen served both culinary and medicinal purposes for the household.
   挂在中世纪厨房天花板上的芳香草药兼具烹饪和药用功能。

3. Scientists developed a new filtration system capable of neutralizing **odorous** compounds released during the industrial manufacturing process.
   科学家开发了一种新的过滤系统，能够中和工业制造过程中释放的有气味化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "odor + ous" — simply "full of odor." If something is odorous, it is full of smell, whether good or bad.
把"odorous"想成"odor + ous"——简单理解为"充满气味的"。如果某物是odorous的，它就充满了气味，无论好坏。
