# 🐌 sluggish

🔊 /ˈslʌɡɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Economically stagnant; slow to respond; moving slowly; lazy. (n.) A sluggish market condition
**中文：** adj. 懒散的；反应迟钝的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lethargic（无精打采的）
- torpid（迟缓的）
- listless（倦怠的）
- languid（懒洋洋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sluggish economy / sluggish response / sluggish growth / sluggish pace**

👉 Example: The sluggish economy forced many businesses to downsize their operations significantly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation's **sluggish** economic recovery left millions of workers struggling to find stable employment opportunities.
   国家经济复苏迟缓，数百万工人难以找到稳定的就业机会。

2. After the heavy meal, he felt **sluggish** and found it difficult to concentrate on the afternoon lecture.
   饱餐一顿后，他感到迟钝，发现很难集中注意力听下午的讲座。

3. The committee expressed concern over the **sluggish** progress of the infrastructure project in the rural district.
   委员会对该农村地区基础设施项目进展缓慢表示担忧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slug-gish" as resembling a "slug" — a slug is a slow-moving creature, so sluggish means slow and lazy.
把"sluggish"想成像"slug（蛞蝓）"——蛞蝓是一种行动缓慢的生物，所以sluggish意思是迟缓和懒散。
