# 🌱 fertile

🔊 /ˈfɜːrtəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Producing or capable of producing abundant vegetation or crops; able to conceive young; rich and inventive
**中文：** adj. 肥沃的，富饶的；能生育的；丰富的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fruitful（富有成效的）
- productive（多产的）
- fecund（肥沃的）
- prolific（丰富的）
- rich（富饶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fertile land / fertile soil / fertile imagination / fertile ground for**

👉 Example: The river delta provides fertile ground for agriculture and economic development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fertile** valley along the river basin has sustained agricultural communities for thousands of years.
   这条河流域的肥沃山谷数千年来一直养育着农业社区。

2. Her **fertile** imagination allowed her to craft elaborate fictional worlds that captivated millions of readers.
   她丰富的想象力使她能够创造出迷住数百万读者的精心构建的虚构世界。

3. Scientists discovered that the volcanic ash had rendered the previously barren landscape remarkably **fertile** and productive.
   科学家发现火山灰使之前贫瘠的土地变得异常肥沃且高产。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fertile" as containing "fert" — like "ferment," where things grow and bubble with life and productivity.
把"fertile"想成包含"fert"——就像"ferment"（发酵），事物在其中生长、充满生机和产出力。
