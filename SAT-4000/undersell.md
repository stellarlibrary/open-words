# 💰 undersell

🔊 /ˌʌndərˈsɛl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To sell goods at a lower price than a competitor; to promote or present something in a restrained or inadequate way
**中文：** v. 抛售；以低于市价售出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- undercut（削价竞争）
- undercharge（少收费）
- undervalue（低估价值）
- discount（打折出售）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undersell competitors / undersell the market / undersell oneself / undersell a product**

👉 Example: The new retailer managed to undersell every competitor in the region by streamlining its supply chain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Foreign manufacturers often **undersell** domestic producers by exploiting lower labor costs and fewer regulatory constraints.
   外国制造商往往利用较低的劳动力成本和较少的监管限制来以低于国内生产商的价格销售。

2. She consistently **undersells** her qualifications during interviews, which undermines her chances of securing prestigious positions.
   她在面试中总是低估自己的资历，这削弱了她获得名望职位的机会。

3. The company's strategy to **undersell** the competition ultimately eroded its profit margins and threatened long-term viability.
   该公司以低价竞争对手的策略最终侵蚀了其利润率，威胁到了长期生存能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "under-sell" as selling under the normal price — you go below the market rate to beat the competition.
把"undersell"想成在正常价格"之下"出售——你以低于市场价的价格来击败竞争对手。
