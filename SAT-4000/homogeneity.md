# 🔲 homogeneity

🔊 /ˌhɒmədʒəˈniːɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality or state of being all the same or of the same kind; uniformity in composition or character
**中文：** n. 同质；同种；均匀性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- uniformity（一致性）
- sameness（相同）
- consistency（一致性）
- similarity（相似性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cultural homogeneity / genetic homogeneity / lack of homogeneity / degree of homogeneity**

👉 Example: The researchers observed a surprising degree of homogeneity among the samples collected from different regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argue that globalization threatens cultural diversity by promoting **homogeneity** in consumer habits and social values worldwide.
   批评者认为全球化通过在全球范围内推动消费习惯和社会价值观的同质化来威胁文化多样性。

2. The **homogeneity** of the experimental group ensured that observed differences could be attributed solely to the treatment variable.
   实验组的同质性确保了观察到的差异可以完全归因于处理变量。

3. Urban planners warned that excessive **homogeneity** in architectural design would produce neighborhoods lacking character and vitality.
   城市规划者警告说，建筑设计中过度的同质化将产生缺乏特色和活力的社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homogeneity" as "homo(same) + gene + ity" — having the same genes means everything is uniform and identical.
把"homogeneity"想成"homo（相同）+ gene（基因）+ ity"——拥有相同的基因意味着一切都是均匀一致的。
