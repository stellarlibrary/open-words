# 🏰 recapture

🔊 /ˌriːˈkæptʃər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To capture again; to recover by force; to experience again. (n.) The act of recapturing or the state of being recaptured
**中文：** v. 夺回；拿回；再体验 n. 夺回；取回

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recover（恢复）
- retrieve（取回）
- regain（重新获得）
- retake（夺回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recapture a city / recapture the spirit / recapture a feeling / recapture an escapee**

👉 Example: The author tried to recapture the magic of her childhood summers in her latest memoir.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Allied forces launched a decisive offensive to **recapture** the strategic port city from the occupying army.
   盟军发动了一场决定性的攻势，从占领军手中夺回了这座战略港口城市。

2. The documentary attempts to **recapture** the excitement and optimism that characterized the early years of space exploration.
   这部纪录片试图再现太空探索早期那种令人兴奋和乐观的氛围。

3. After the prisoner escaped, authorities mobilized extensive resources to **recapture** him before he could cross the border.
   囚犯逃脱后，当局调动了大量资源，争取在他越境之前将其重新抓获。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-capture" as "capture again" — the prefix "re-" means again, so you are seizing or experiencing something for a second time.
把"recapture"拆成"re-（再次）+ capture（捕获）"——再次捕获，即夺回或重新体验。
