# 💪 redoubtable

🔊 /rɪˈdaʊtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Formidable, especially as an opponent; inspiring fear or awe; worthy of respect
**中文：** adj. 可怕的；令人敬畏的；值得尊敬的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- formidable（令人敬畏的）
- fearsome（可怕的）
- imposing（令人印象深刻的）
- daunting（令人生畏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **redoubtable opponent / redoubtable warrior / redoubtable figure / redoubtable reputation**

👉 Example: The redoubtable professor was known for both her brilliance and her exacting standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **redoubtable** general led his forces to victory in every major campaign throughout the decade-long conflict.
   这位令人敬畏的将军在长达十年的冲突中带领部队在每场重大战役中取得胜利。

2. Few attorneys dared to challenge the **redoubtable** prosecutor, whose courtroom record remained virtually unblemished.
   很少有律师敢于挑战这位令人敬畏的检察官，他的法庭记录几乎无瑕。

3. The small nation proved to be a **redoubtable** adversary, repelling invasion attempts by far larger neighboring states.
   这个小国证明了自己是一个可怕的对手，击退了邻国更大规模的入侵企图。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-doubt-able" as someone who makes you "doubt" yourself again and again — so formidable that you repeatedly question whether you can compete.
把"redoubtable"想成让你"re-doubt"（反复怀疑）的人——如此令人敬畏，以至于你反复质疑自己能否与之抗衡。
