# ✏️ delineate

🔊 /dɪˈlɪnieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To describe or portray something precisely; to indicate the exact position of a border or boundary; to draw or trace the outline of
**中文：** v. 描绘；描写；画轮廓

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- depict（描绘）
- outline（勾勒）
- describe（描述）
- portray（刻画）
- define（界定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **delineate boundaries / delineate a plan / clearly delineate / delineate the differences / delineate roles**

👉 Example: The contract must clearly delineate the responsibilities of each party involved in the partnership.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect used detailed blueprints to **delineate** the precise dimensions and structural layout of the proposed building.
   建筑师使用详细的蓝图来描绘拟建建筑的精确尺寸和结构布局。

2. The treaty was designed to **delineate** the territorial boundaries between the two nations and prevent future border disputes.
   该条约旨在划定两国之间的领土边界，防止未来的边境争端。

3. The professor asked students to **delineate** the key differences between classical and contemporary approaches to economic theory.
   教授要求学生描述古典经济理论与当代经济理论之间的主要区别。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-line-ate" as "de- + line + -ate" — to draw a line around something, outlining its shape or defining its boundaries.
把"delineate"想成"de- + line（线）+ -ate"——在某物周围画一条线，勾勒其轮廓或界定其边界。
