# 🎭 reveal

🔊 /rɪˈviːl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make known something that was previously secret or hidden; to display or show openly
**中文：** v. 显示；透露；揭露

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disclose（揭露）
- divulge（泄露）
- expose（暴露）
- unveil（揭示）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reveal the truth / reveal a secret / reveal details / reveal information**

👉 Example: The investigation revealed a network of corruption that extended to the highest levels of government.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The declassified documents **revealed** the full extent of the government's covert surveillance operations during the Cold War era.
   解密文件揭露了冷战时期政府秘密监控行动的全部范围。

2. Recent archaeological excavations **revealed** the remains of an ancient civilization previously unknown to modern historical scholarship.
   最近的考古发掘揭示了一个此前不为现代历史学界所知的古代文明遗迹。

3. The journalist's thorough investigation **revealed** systematic financial irregularities within the organization's senior leadership over several years.
   记者的深入调查揭露了该组织高层领导多年来的系统性财务违规行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-veal" as removing a veil — when you lift the veil, what was hidden is now revealed for all to see.
把"reveal"想成"掀开面纱"——当你揭开面纱时，隐藏的东西就展现在所有人面前了。
