# ⚖️ condone

🔊 /kənˈdoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To accept, overlook, or forgive behavior that is considered wrong or offensive; to tacitly approve by not objecting.

**中文：** 宽恕；赦免；纵容；默许。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- overlook（忽视）
- forgive（原谅）
- pardon（赦免）
- excuse（原谅）
- tolerate（容忍）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** condone violence / condone behavior / cannot condone

**Example:** The institution does not **condone** any form of academic dishonesty among its students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The administration made it clear that it would not **condone** any violations of the established code of conduct.
   管理层明确表示不会纵容任何违反既定行为准则的行为。

2. By remaining silent on the matter, the committee appeared to **condone** the unethical practices within the organization.
   委员会在此事上保持沉默，似乎在默许组织内部的不道德做法。

3. No responsible government should **condone** the use of excessive force against civilians during peaceful demonstrations.
   任何负责任的政府都不应容忍在和平示威期间对平民使用过度武力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Con-" (fully) + "done" — once something is fully done, you let it go and forgive it, you condone it.

**中文：** 联想 "con-"（完全）+ "done"（完成）——事情已经完全做了，你就放过它、原谅它，即"宽恕、纵容"。
