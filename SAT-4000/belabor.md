# 🔨 belabor

🔊 /bɪˈleɪbər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To attack verbally; to explain or discuss excessively; to beat severely
**中文：** v. 痛打；抨击；过度说明；反复讨论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overemphasize（过分强调）
- dwell on（反复讨论）
- berate（严厉斥责）
- thrash（痛打）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **belabor the point / belabor the issue / belabor the obvious / belabor an argument**

👉 Example: There is no need to belabor the point; I believe the committee fully understands the proposal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor tended to **belabor** minor theoretical distinctions that most students found unnecessarily confusing and tedious.
   这位教授倾向于过度阐述细小的理论差异，大多数学生觉得这既令人困惑又乏味。

2. Critics accused the author of **belaboring** her central thesis across three hundred pages of repetitive argumentation.
   批评者指责这位作者在三百页重复的论证中过度阐述她的核心论点。

3. I will not **belabor** the obvious, but the data clearly indicate that our current strategy requires immediate revision.
   我不会反复强调显而易见的事实，但数据清楚地表明我们当前的策略需要立即修正。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-labor" — to labor over something excessively, working a point so hard that it becomes tiresome.
把"belabor"想成"be-labor"——对某事过度费力，把一个观点反复说到令人厌烦。
