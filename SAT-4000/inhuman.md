# 😈 inhuman

🔊 /ɪnˈhjuːmən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking human qualities of compassion and mercy; extremely cruel or brutal; not human in nature
**中文：** adj. 残忍的；野蛮的；无人性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brutal（残忍的）
- barbarous（野蛮的）
- merciless（无情的）
- savage（凶残的）
- heartless（冷酷无情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inhuman treatment / inhuman conditions / inhuman cruelty / inhuman act**

👉 Example: The prisoners endured inhuman conditions that violated basic international standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international tribunal condemned the **inhuman** treatment of civilians during the prolonged armed conflict in the region.
   国际法庭谴责了该地区长期武装冲突期间对平民的非人道待遇。

2. Workers in the factory were subjected to **inhuman** conditions, laboring sixteen hours daily without adequate rest or nourishment.
   工厂里的工人遭受着非人的工作条件，每天劳动十六个小时，没有充足的休息或食物。

3. The dictator's regime was notorious for its **inhuman** policies that systematically denied citizens their fundamental rights and freedoms.
   该独裁者的政权因其系统性剥夺公民基本权利和自由的残忍政策而臭名昭著。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-human" as "not human" — behavior so cruel that it seems to lack any trace of human decency or compassion.
把"inhuman"想成"in（不是）+ human（人类的）"——行为如此残忍，似乎毫无人性的体面或同情。
