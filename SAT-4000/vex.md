# 😤 vex

🔊 /vɛks/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To annoy, frustrate, or worry someone; to cause distress or agitation
**中文：** v. 使烦恼；使恼怒；使困惑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritate（激怒）
- annoy（使烦恼）
- exasperate（使恼怒）
- agitate（使不安）
- perplex（使困惑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vexed by / vexed question / vex someone's patience / sorely vexed**

👉 Example: The unresolved border dispute continued to vex diplomats from both nations for decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The persistent software glitches continued to **vex** the engineering team despite numerous attempts at debugging the system.
   尽管多次尝试调试系统，持续不断的软件故障仍然令工程团队**烦恼**不已。

2. The philosopher's paradox has **vexed** scholars for centuries, as no universally accepted solution has yet been proposed.
   这个哲学悖论已经使学者们**困惑**了几个世纪，因为至今尚未提出普遍接受的解决方案。

3. It **vexed** the committee members that the proposed budget contained several unexplained discrepancies in projected revenue figures.
   拟议预算中预计收入数字存在多处无法解释的差异，这令委员会成员们**恼怒**。
