# 🕳️ absence

🔊 /ˈæbsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being away from a place or person; the nonexistence or lack of something
**中文：** n. 不在场，缺席；缺乏，没有

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lack（缺乏）
- nonattendance（缺席）
- deficiency（不足）
- void（空缺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **absence from / in the absence of / absence of evidence / leave of absence / conspicuous absence**

👉 Example: In the absence of clear regulations, companies often set their own standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged **absence** of the chairman raised serious concerns among the board of directors.
   董事长长期缺席引起了董事会成员的严重关切。

2. In the **absence** of reliable data, policymakers were forced to rely on outdated projections.
   在缺乏可靠数据的情况下，决策者被迫依赖过时的预测。

3. Her unexpected **absence** from the annual conference was widely interpreted as a sign of internal disagreement.
   她意外缺席年度会议被广泛解读为内部分歧的信号。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Absence" comes from "ab-" (away) + "sense" (being). When someone is absent, their being is away — they are simply not there.
"Absence"来自"ab-"（远离）+"sense"（存在）。当某人absent时，他们的存在远离了——他们就是不在那里。
