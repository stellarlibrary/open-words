# 🩹 adhesion

🔊 /ədˈhiː.ʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The action or process of sticking to a surface or object; steady or faithful attachment to a person, cause, or belief; support
**中文：** n. 粘附，粘着；支持，拥护；固定

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attachment（附着）
- bonding（粘合）
- cohesion（凝聚）
- sticking（粘着）
- allegiance（忠诚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adhesion to a surface / adhesion between layers / cell adhesion / adhesion to a cause / strong adhesion**

👉 Example: The engineer tested the adhesion of the new coating to ensure it could withstand harsh conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory conducted rigorous tests to measure the **adhesion** of the polymer coating to the aluminum substrate.
   实验室进行了严格的测试以测量聚合物涂层对铝基板的粘附力。

2. Poor **adhesion** between the two materials caused the laminate to separate under moderate mechanical stress.
   两种材料之间粘附力不足导致层压板在中等机械应力下发生分离。

3. The party secured the **adhesion** of several influential leaders, which significantly bolstered its political standing.
   该党获得了几位有影响力的领导人的支持，这大大增强了其政治地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adhesion" as "ad-HE-sion" — like "adhesive" without the "ve." Just as adhesive tape sticks things together, adhesion is the state or process of sticking firmly.
把"adhesion"想成"ad-HE-sion"——类似"adhesive"（粘合剂）去掉"ve"。就像胶带把东西粘在一起，adhesion就是牢固粘附的状态或过程。
