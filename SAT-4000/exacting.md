# ⚙️ exacting

🔊 /ɪɡˈzæktɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Making great demands on one's skill, attention, or resources; very strict and demanding high standards
**中文：** adj. 苛求的，严格的；吃力的，需付出极大耐心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- demanding（苛求的）
- rigorous（严格的）
- stringent（严厉的）
- meticulous（细致的）
- arduous（艰巨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exacting standards / exacting requirements / exacting work / exacting taskmaster**

👉 Example: The professor was known for her exacting standards, accepting nothing less than academic excellence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The restoration of Renaissance paintings is an **exacting** process that demands years of specialized training and extraordinary patience.
   修复文艺复兴时期画作是一项苛求的工作，需要多年的专业训练和非凡的耐心。

2. The pharmaceutical industry operates under **exacting** regulatory standards designed to ensure the safety and efficacy of all medications.
   制药行业在严格的监管标准下运营，旨在确保所有药物的安全性和有效性。

3. Only the most dedicated musicians can meet the **exacting** technical demands required to perform at internationally renowned concert venues.
   只有最敬业的音乐家才能满足在国际知名音乐厅演出所需的严格技术要求。
