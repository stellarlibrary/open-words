# 😤 discourteous

🔊 /dɪsˈkɜːrtiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing rudeness and a lack of consideration for others; impolite in manner or behavior
**中文：** adj. 失礼的，无礼貌的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rude（粗鲁的）
- impolite（不礼貌的）
- uncivil（无礼的）
- disrespectful（不敬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **discourteous behavior / discourteous remark / discourteous to someone**

👉 Example: It would be discourteous to leave the dinner party without thanking the host.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members found his **discourteous** interruptions during the formal proceedings to be highly unprofessional and disruptive.
   委员会成员认为他在正式会议期间的无礼打断极不专业且具有破坏性。

2. Several customers filed complaints about the **discourteous** treatment they received from the front desk staff at the hotel.
   几位顾客对酒店前台工作人员的无礼对待提出了投诉。

3. The diplomat's **discourteous** response to the ambassador's proposal nearly derailed the entire negotiation process between the two nations.
   该外交官对大使提案的失礼回应几乎使两国之间的整个谈判进程脱轨。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-courteous" — the prefix "dis-" negates "courteous" (有礼貌的), so discourteous literally means "not courteous."
把"discourteous"拆成"dis-courteous"——前缀"dis-"否定了"courteous"（有礼貌的），所以字面意思就是"没有礼貌的"。
