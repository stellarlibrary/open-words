# 🌟 forte

🔊 /fɔːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person's strong suit or area of particular talent and skill. (adj.) In music, played loudly
**中文：** n. 长处；特长；专长 adj. 强音的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strength（强项）
- specialty（专长）
- talent（才能）
- strong suit（拿手好戏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **not one's forte / a particular forte / public speaking is her forte / patience is not his forte**

👉 Example: Creative problem-solving has always been her greatest forte in the workplace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Diplomatic negotiation was widely regarded as the ambassador's greatest **forte**, having resolved numerous international disputes throughout her career.
   外交谈判被普遍认为是这位大使最大的特长，她在职业生涯中解决了众多国际争端。

2. Although the professor excelled in theoretical research, classroom instruction was admittedly not his **forte** among academic responsibilities.
   虽然这位教授在理论研究方面表现出色，但课堂教学确实不是他在学术职责中的强项。

3. Data analysis proved to be the young consultant's **forte**, enabling the firm to identify critical inefficiencies in its operations.
   数据分析被证明是这位年轻顾问的特长，使公司能够发现其运营中的关键低效之处。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "forte" as related to "fort" (fortress) — your forte is the area where you are strongest, like a well-defended fortress that cannot be defeated.
把"forte"想成与"fort（堡垒）"有关——你的forte就是你最强的领域，就像一座坚不可摧的堡垒。
