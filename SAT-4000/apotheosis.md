# ✨ apotheosis

🔊 /əˌpɒθiˈoʊsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *n. 神化；崇拜；典范*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The elevation of someone or something to divine status; the highest point of development or the perfect example of a quality or type.

**中文：** 神化；奉为神明；事物发展的顶峰或某种品质的典范。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- deification 神化
- glorification 美化；颂扬
- pinnacle 顶峰
- epitome 典范
- exaltation 提升；崇高

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** the apotheosis of something, reach one's apotheosis, represent the apotheosis, cultural apotheosis

**Example:** "Apotheosis" is used both literally (raising to godhood) and figuratively (the perfect example or highest achievement).

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cathedral's ceiling fresco depicted the **apotheosis** of the emperor ascending among the gods.
   大教堂天顶壁画描绘了皇帝**封神**升入众神之列的场景。

2. Many scholars consider this symphony to be the **apotheosis** of the Romantic musical tradition.
   许多学者认为这部交响曲是浪漫主义音乐传统的**巅峰之作**。

3. The athlete's flawless performance represented the **apotheosis** of years of rigorous and disciplined training.
   这位运动员完美的表现是多年严格训练的**最高体现**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Break it down: "apo-" (from) + "theo" (god) + "-sis" (process) — the process of making someone into a god.

拆解记忆：apo-（从……）+ theo（神）+ -sis（过程）——把某人变成神的过程，即"神化"。
