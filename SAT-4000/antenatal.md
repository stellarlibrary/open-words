# 🤰 antenatal

🔊 /ˌæn.tiˈneɪ.təl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Relating to the period before birth; occurring or existing before delivery of a child; prenatal.

**中文：** 产前的；出生前的；胎儿期的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- prenatal（产前的）
- prebirth（出生前的）
- gestational（妊娠期的）
- intrauterine（子宫内的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antenatal care, antenatal clinic, antenatal screening, antenatal classes, antenatal check-up

**Example:** Regular **antenatal** visits are essential for monitoring the health of both the mother and the developing child.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government launched a comprehensive **antenatal** care program to reduce infant mortality rates in rural communities.
   政府启动了一项全面的产前护理计划，以降低农村社区的婴儿死亡率。

2. Expectant mothers were encouraged to attend **antenatal** classes in order to prepare for labor and early childcare.
   准妈妈们被鼓励参加产前课程，以便为分娩和早期育儿做准备。

3. The research demonstrated that adequate **antenatal** nutrition significantly improves developmental outcomes for newborn infants.
   研究表明，充足的产前营养能显著改善新生儿的发育状况。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Ante-" (before) + "natal" (birth, from Latin *natalis*) = before birth. Think of "antenatal" as the time **before** the baby is born — natal relates to birth, as in "nativity." 前缀 ante-（之前）+ natal（出生的，源自拉丁语 natalis）= 出生之前的。natal 与出生有关，如 nativity（诞生）。
