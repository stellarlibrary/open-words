# 🌸 vernal

🔊 /ˈvɜːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or occurring in the spring; suggestive of youth and freshness
**中文：** adj. 春天的；和煦的；青春的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- springlike（春天般的）
- fresh（清新的）
- youthful（青春的）
- rejuvenating（焕发活力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vernal equinox / vernal bloom / vernal breeze / vernal season**

👉 Example: The vernal equinox marks the official beginning of spring in the Northern Hemisphere.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **vernal** equinox has been celebrated by diverse cultures throughout history as a symbol of renewal and agricultural rebirth.
   春分自古以来就被不同文化视为更新和农业复苏的象征加以庆祝。

2. Botanists documented the **vernal** wildflowers that emerged briefly each year before the dense forest canopy blocked the sunlight.
   植物学家记录了每年在茂密的森林树冠遮挡阳光之前短暂出现的春季野花。

3. The composer's symphony evoked a **vernal** atmosphere, with light melodies suggesting the warmth and optimism of early spring.
   作曲家的交响曲唤起了春天的氛围，轻快的旋律暗示着早春的温暖与乐观。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vernal" — it comes from Latin "ver" meaning spring. Connect it to the "vernal equinox," the most common phrase where you encounter this word.
把"vernal"想成来自拉丁语"ver"意为春天。将其与"vernal equinox"（春分）联系起来，这是你最常遇到这个词的短语。
