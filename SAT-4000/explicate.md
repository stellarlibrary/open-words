# 📚 explicate

🔊 /ˈɛksplɪˌkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To analyze and develop an idea or principle in detail; to explain or interpret something thoroughly
**中文：** v. 说明，解释；阐明，分析

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elucidate（阐明）
- clarify（澄清）
- interpret（解释）
- expound（详述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **explicate a text / explicate a theory / explicate the meaning / explicate a concept**

👉 Example: The professor asked her students to explicate the underlying themes of the novel in their essays.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literary critic attempted to **explicate** the complex symbolism woven throughout the author's most celebrated novel.
   这位文学评论家试图阐明贯穿作者最著名小说的复杂象征手法。

2. During the seminar, the philosopher took great care to **explicate** each premise of his argument before drawing conclusions.
   在研讨会上，这位哲学家在得出结论之前仔细地说明了他论证的每一个前提。

3. The teacher asked students to **explicate** the poem line by line, revealing the deeper meanings hidden beneath its surface.
   老师要求学生逐行解释这首诗，揭示其表面之下隐藏的深层含义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-plic-ate" as "unfold the plica (fold)" — like unfolding layers of a complex idea to make it clear and understandable.
把"explicate"想成"展开褶皱（plica）"——就像展开一个复杂想法的层层面纱，使其变得清晰易懂。
