# 🌀 commingle

🔊 /kəˈmɪŋ.ɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To mix or blend together; to combine or merge different elements into one
**中文：** v. 混合；掺和

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blend（混合）
- intermingle（混合）
- merge（合并）
- mingle（混合）
- amalgamate（融合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commingle funds / commingle assets / commingle with / commingle cultures**

👉 Example: Financial regulations strictly prohibit brokers from commingling client funds with their own accounts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The attorney was disbarred for illegally **commingling** client trust funds with his personal business accounts.
   这位律师因非法将客户信托资金与其个人商业账户混合而被取消了执业资格。

2. Over centuries of trade, the cultures along the ancient Silk Road **commingled** to produce a rich tapestry of traditions.
   经过几个世纪的贸易往来，古丝绸之路沿线的文化相互交融，形成了丰富多彩的传统。

3. The rivers **commingled** at the confluence, their distinct waters merging into a single powerful current flowing southward.
   河流在汇合处混合在一起，各自不同的水流汇聚成一股向南流去的强大水流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-MINGLE" — "com" means together and "mingle" means to mix, so commingle literally means to mix together.
把"commingle"想成"com-MINGLE"——"com"意为一起，"mingle"意为混合，所以commingle字面意思就是混合在一起。
