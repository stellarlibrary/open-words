# 👑 influential

🔊 /ˌɪnfluˈɛnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having great influence on someone or something; powerful and important. (n.) A person who has great influence
**中文：** adj. 有影响的；有权势的 n. 有影响力的人物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- powerful（有权力的）
- prominent（杰出的）
- authoritative（权威的）
- significant（重要的）
- impactful（有影响的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **influential figure / influential in / highly influential / influential work / influential role**

👉 Example: Darwin remains one of the most influential figures in the history of biological science.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **influential** research on climate change shaped government policies and international environmental agreements for decades.
   这位教授关于气候变化的有影响力的研究在数十年间塑造了政府政策和国际环境协议。

2. Several **influential** members of the board opposed the merger, ultimately persuading the majority to vote against the proposed deal.
   董事会中几位有影响力的成员反对合并，最终说服大多数人投票否决了拟议交易。

3. Her debut novel proved remarkably **influential**, inspiring an entire generation of writers to explore themes of identity and belonging.
   她的处女作小说极具影响力，激励了一整代作家去探索身份认同和归属感的主题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "influence" + "ial" — someone who has the power to "flow into" (influence) other people's decisions and actions.
把"influential"想成"influence（影响）" + "ial"——拥有"流入"（influence源自拉丁语"流入"）他人决策和行动的力量。
