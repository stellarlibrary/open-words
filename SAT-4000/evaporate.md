# 💨 evaporate

🔊 /ɪˈvæpəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To change from liquid into vapor; to disappear or cause to disappear gradually
**中文：** v. 使蒸发；使脱水；使消失

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vaporize（蒸发）
- dissipate（消散）
- vanish（消失）
- disappear（消失）
- dry up（干涸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **evaporate quickly / evaporate into thin air / hopes evaporate / moisture evaporates**

👉 Example: Public enthusiasm for the project seemed to evaporate once the full costs were announced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. As global temperatures continue to rise, freshwater reserves in arid regions **evaporate** at an increasingly alarming rate each summer.
   随着全球气温持续上升，干旱地区的淡水储备每年夏天以越来越惊人的速度蒸发。

2. The initial optimism surrounding the peace negotiations began to **evaporate** when neither side demonstrated willingness to make meaningful concessions.
   当双方都没有表现出做出实质性让步的意愿时，围绕和平谈判的最初乐观情绪开始消散。

3. The chemist heated the solution carefully, allowing the solvent to **evaporate** and leave behind a concentrated crystalline residue.
   化学家小心地加热溶液，让溶剂蒸发，留下浓缩的结晶残留物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-vapor-ate" — the word "vapor" is hidden inside, reminding you that liquid turns into vapor and disappears.
把"evaporate"想成"e-vapor-ate"——单词中藏着"vapor"（蒸汽），提醒你液体变成蒸汽并消失。
