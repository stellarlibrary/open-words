# 🚀 impetus

🔊 /ˈɪmpɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The force or energy with which something moves; a driving force or stimulus that encourages something to happen or develop
**中文：** n. 动力；促进；冲力；推动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- momentum（动力）
- stimulus（刺激）
- catalyst（催化剂）
- incentive（激励）
- impulsion（推动力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **give impetus to / provide the impetus for / the main impetus behind / fresh impetus**

👉 Example: The discovery of new evidence gave fresh impetus to the long-stalled criminal investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The successful launch of the satellite provided significant **impetus** for increased government investment in the national space exploration program.
   卫星的成功发射为政府加大对国家太空探索计划的投资提供了重要推动力。

2. The economic crisis served as the primary **impetus** behind sweeping regulatory reforms in the global financial services industry.
   经济危机成为全球金融服务业进行全面监管改革的主要推动力。

3. Her groundbreaking research gave new **impetus** to the development of renewable energy technologies that could benefit future generations.
   她开创性的研究为可再生能源技术的发展注入了新的动力，将惠及后代。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impetus" as "I'm put us in motion" — impetus is the force that puts things into motion, driving them forward with energy.
把"impetus"想成"推我们前进的力量"——impetus就是推动事物前进的动力和冲力。
