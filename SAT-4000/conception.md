# 🌱 conception

🔊 /kənˈsɛpʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of becoming pregnant; an abstract idea or notion; the forming of a plan or idea in the mind
**中文：** n. 怀孕；概念；观念；设想

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- notion（观念）
- idea（想法）
- perception（认知）
- understanding（理解）
- concept（概念）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conception of / have no conception of / from conception / popular conception / original conception**

👉 Example: The architect's original conception of the building underwent numerous revisions during the planning phase.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher challenged the traditional **conception** of justice by introducing a framework rooted in communal responsibility.
   这位哲学家通过引入一个以集体责任为基础的框架，挑战了传统的正义观念。

2. From **conception** to completion, the ambitious infrastructure project required more than fifteen years of sustained effort.
   从构想到完成，这项宏大的基础设施项目需要十五年以上的持续努力。

3. Many citizens have no clear **conception** of how complex monetary policy decisions affect their daily economic lives.
   许多公民对复杂的货币政策决定如何影响他们的日常经济生活没有清晰的概念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "conception" as the "birth" of something — whether it is the birth of a baby or the birth of an idea in the mind.
把"conception"想成某事物的"诞生"——无论是婴儿的诞生还是头脑中一个想法的诞生。
