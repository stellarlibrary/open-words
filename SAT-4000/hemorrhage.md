# 🩹 hemorrhage

🔊 /ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An excessive or uncontrollable flow of blood from ruptured blood vessels. (v.) To bleed heavily or lose something rapidly and uncontrollably
**中文：** n. 出血 v. 大量出血；大量丧失

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bleeding（出血）
- blood loss（失血）
- haemorrhage（出血，英式拼写）
- exsanguination（放血）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **internal hemorrhage / cerebral hemorrhage / hemorrhage blood / hemorrhage money (figurative)**

👉 Example: The surgeon worked urgently to control the hemorrhage before the patient lost too much blood.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The trauma patient was rushed to surgery after physicians detected a severe internal **hemorrhage** caused by the accident.
   外伤患者在医生检测到事故导致的严重内出血后被紧急送往手术室。

2. The corporation continued to **hemorrhage** revenue throughout the fiscal quarter due to declining consumer confidence and weak sales.
   由于消费者信心下降和销售疲软，该公司在整个财季持续大量流失收入。

3. Medical researchers developed a new treatment capable of stopping cerebral **hemorrhage** within minutes of initial application.
   医学研究人员开发了一种新疗法，能够在初次使用后几分钟内止住脑出血。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "hemo" means blood and "rrhage" means bursting forth — a hemorrhage is blood bursting out of the body uncontrollably.
拆分来看："hemo"表示血液，"rrhage"表示涌出——出血就是血液不受控制地从体内涌出。
