# 🔊 sonar

🔊 /ˈsoʊnɑːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A system using transmitted and reflected sound waves to detect and locate submerged objects
**中文：** n. 声纳

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- echolocation（回声定位）
- sound navigation（声波导航）
- acoustic detection（声学探测）
- echo ranging（回声测距）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sonar equipment / sonar system / sonar detection / sonar signals / active sonar**

👉 Example: The submarine relied on sonar to navigate the dark depths of the ocean floor safely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Marine biologists employed advanced **sonar** technology to map the topography of the previously unexplored ocean trench.
   海洋生物学家使用先进的声纳技术来绘制此前未被探索的海沟地形图。

2. The naval vessel activated its **sonar** system to detect potential underwater threats lurking beneath the surface of the strait.
   军舰启动了声纳系统以探测潜伏在海峡水面下的潜在水下威胁。

3. Researchers discovered that excessive **sonar** use in coastal waters may disrupt the migratory patterns of certain whale species.
   研究人员发现，在沿海水域过度使用声纳可能会干扰某些鲸鱼物种的迁徙模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"SONAR" is an acronym for "SOund NAvigation and Ranging" — it uses sound waves to navigate and measure distance underwater.
"SONAR"是"SOund NAvigation and Ranging"（声音导航与测距）的首字母缩写——它利用声波在水下导航和测距。
