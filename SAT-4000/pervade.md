# 🌫️ pervade

🔊 /pərˈveɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To spread throughout; to be present in every part of something
**中文：** v. 遍及；弥漫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permeate（渗透）
- saturate（浸透）
- suffuse（弥漫）
- infiltrate（渗入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pervade the atmosphere / pervade society / pervade every aspect of**

👉 Example: A sense of unease pervaded the entire community after the unexpected announcement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A feeling of optimism **pervaded** the organization after the new leadership announced its ambitious reform agenda.
   新领导层宣布雄心勃勃的改革议程后，一种乐观情绪弥漫了整个组织。

2. The aroma of freshly baked bread **pervaded** every corridor of the ancient monastery during the morning hours.
   清晨时分，新鲜出炉的面包香气弥漫在古老修道院的每条走廊里。

3. Corruption had **pervaded** the institutional framework to such a degree that meaningful reform seemed nearly impossible.
   腐败已经渗透到制度框架中，以至于有意义的改革似乎几乎不可能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-vade" as "per" (through) + "vade" (go) — something that goes through everything, spreading everywhere.
把"pervade"想成"per"（通过）+"vade"（走）——某种东西穿过一切，到处蔓延。
