# 💰 frugal

🔊 /ˈfruːɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Sparing or economical with regard to money or food; simple and plain, costing little
**中文：** 节俭的；朴素的；花钱少的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thrifty（节俭的）
- economical（经济的）
- prudent（审慎的）
- sparing（节省的）
- parsimonious（吝啬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frugal lifestyle / frugal spending / frugal meal / frugal habits**

👉 Example: Her frugal habits during college allowed her to graduate without any student loan debt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **frugal** entrepreneur reinvested every saved dollar back into the business rather than indulging in personal luxuries.
   这位节俭的企业家将每一笔省下的钱重新投入到企业中，而不是沉溺于个人奢侈品。

2. Despite their considerable wealth, the couple maintained a remarkably **frugal** lifestyle that surprised many of their affluent peers.
   尽管拥有可观的财富，这对夫妇保持着一种非常节俭的生活方式，令许多富裕的同龄人感到惊讶。

3. A **frugal** approach to resource management proved essential for the survival of the small community during the prolonged drought.
   在持久干旱期间，节俭的资源管理方法对这个小社区的生存至关重要。

---

把"frugal"想成"fruit（水果）+ gal（女孩）"——一个节俭的女孩只买简单的水果而不是昂贵的饭菜来省钱。
