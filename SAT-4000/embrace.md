# 🤗 embrace

🔊 /ɪmˈbreɪs/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To hold someone closely in one's arms; to accept or support willingly; to include or contain. (n.) An act of holding someone closely
**中文：** v. 拥抱；信奉；包含 n. 拥抱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hug（拥抱）
- adopt（采纳）
- encompass（包含）
- welcome（欣然接受）
- espouse（信奉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **embrace change / embrace an opportunity / embrace diversity / warm embrace**

👉 Example: Progressive companies are quick to embrace new technologies that enhance productivity and innovation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university encouraged students to **embrace** diverse perspectives and engage in open intellectual discourse across disciplines.
   该大学鼓励学生拥抱多元视角并跨学科参与开放的学术讨论。

2. After years of resistance, the industry finally chose to **embrace** sustainable practices as both ethical and economically viable.
   经过多年的抵制，该行业最终选择信奉可持续发展实践，认为其既合乎道德又具经济可行性。

3. The two leaders met in a symbolic **embrace** that signaled the beginning of a new era of diplomatic cooperation.
   两位领导人以象征性的拥抱会面，标志着外交合作新时代的开始。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "em-BRACE" — you BRACE yourself to hold someone tightly, wrapping your arms around them in a warm embrace.
把"embrace"想成"em-BRACE"——你支撑（BRACE）自己去紧紧抱住某人，用双臂温暖地环抱他们。
