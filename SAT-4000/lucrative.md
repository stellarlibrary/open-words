# 💵 lucrative

🔊 /ˈluːkrətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Producing a great deal of profit; financially rewarding or profitable
**中文：** adj. 有利可图的，赚钱的；获利的；合算的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- profitable（有利润的）
- rewarding（有回报的）
- gainful（有收益的）
- remunerative（有报酬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lucrative business / lucrative deal / lucrative career / lucrative contract / lucrative market**

👉 Example: The technology sector continues to offer some of the most lucrative career opportunities for graduates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company secured a **lucrative** contract with the government to supply vaccines to hospitals across the entire nation.
   这家制药公司与政府签订了一份利润丰厚的合同，向全国各地的医院供应疫苗。

2. Many graduates are attracted to investment banking because it is widely regarded as one of the most **lucrative** professions available.
   许多毕业生被投资银行业所吸引，因为它被广泛认为是最赚钱的职业之一。

3. What began as a modest hobby eventually transformed into a remarkably **lucrative** enterprise generating millions in annual revenue.
   最初只是一项不起眼的爱好，最终变成了一项年收入数百万的极为赚钱的企业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lucr-ative" — "lucr" comes from Latin "lucrum" meaning "profit," so lucrative means something that brings profit.
把"lucrative"想成"lucr（利润）+ ative"——"lucr"来自拉丁语"lucrum（利润）"，所以lucrative就是带来利润的。
