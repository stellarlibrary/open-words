# 😤 presumptuous

🔊 /prɪˈzʌmptʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Overstepping due bounds of propriety or courtesy; taking liberties; excessively bold or forward
**中文：** adj. 专横的；放肆的；冒昧的；自以为是的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arrogant（傲慢的）
- audacious（放肆的）
- impertinent（无礼的）
- overbearing（专横的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **presumptuous to / presumptuous of someone / presumptuous behavior / seem presumptuous**

👉 Example: It would be presumptuous of me to offer advice when I have so little experience in the field.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The junior employee was considered **presumptuous** for suggesting sweeping changes to the company's long-established corporate strategy.
   这位初级员工因建议对公司长期确立的企业战略进行大刀阔斧的改革而被认为过于冒昧。

2. Critics argued that it was **presumptuous** of the author to claim definitive authority on a subject still debated by scholars.
   批评人士认为，该作者在一个学者们仍在争论的话题上声称拥有最终权威，实属自以为是。

3. She apologized for being **presumptuous** but felt compelled to address the ethical concerns raised by the proposed legislation.
   她为自己的冒昧道歉，但觉得不得不提出该拟议立法引发的伦理问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "presume + tu + ous" — someone who "presumes too much" about their own importance, acting as if they have more authority than they do.
把"presumptuous"想成"presume too much"——一个人对自己的重要性"推定过多"，表现得好像自己比实际更有权威。
