# 👊 peremptory

🔊 /pəˈrɛmptəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Insisting on immediate attention or obedience, especially in a brusquely imperious way; not open to appeal or challenge
**中文：** adj. 专横的；断然的；强制的；不容置疑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imperious（专横的）
- dictatorial（独裁的）
- authoritative（权威的）
- dogmatic（武断的）
- overbearing（傲慢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peremptory tone / peremptory command / peremptory challenge / peremptory manner**

👉 Example: The judge issued a peremptory ruling that left no room for further debate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manager's **peremptory** demand for immediate compliance alienated many of the senior staff members in the department.
   经理专横地要求立即服从，疏远了部门中许多资深员工。

2. In a **peremptory** tone, the professor dismissed the student's objection without offering any substantive explanation or justification.
   教授以断然的语气驳回了学生的异议，没有提供任何实质性的解释或理由。

3. The committee rejected the **peremptory** directive from the chairman, insisting that all proposals deserve thorough and fair consideration.
   委员会拒绝了主席的强制指令，坚持所有提案都应得到全面公正的审议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peremptory" as "pre-empty" — someone who pre-emptively empties all discussion, leaving no room for others to speak.
把"peremptory"想成"pre-empty"——一个先发制人地清空所有讨论的人，不给别人说话的余地。
