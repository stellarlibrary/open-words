# 🦅 jingoism

🔊 /ˈdʒɪŋɡoʊɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extreme patriotism, especially in the form of aggressive or warlike foreign policy; chauvinistic nationalism
**中文：** n. 沙文主义；侵略主义；武力外交政策

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chauvinism（沙文主义）
- nationalism（民族主义）
- militarism（军国主义）
- xenophobia（仇外心理）
- hawkishness（鹰派立场）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blind jingoism / rise of jingoism / jingoism in politics / fueled by jingoism**

👉 Example: The editorial warned that unchecked jingoism could lead the nation into unnecessary military conflicts abroad.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians argue that **jingoism** in the late 19th century played a decisive role in pushing several European powers toward imperial expansion.
   历史学家认为，19世纪末的沙文主义在推动几个欧洲大国走向帝国扩张方面起了决定性作用。

2. The professor cautioned students against confusing genuine patriotism with **jingoism**, which advocates aggression rather than constructive diplomacy.
   教授告诫学生不要将真正的爱国主义与沙文主义混为一谈，后者主张侵略而非建设性外交。

3. Critics accused the political leader of exploiting **jingoism** to distract the public from pressing domestic economic challenges.
   批评人士指责这位政治领导人利用沙文主义来转移公众对紧迫国内经济问题的注意力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jingoism" as "jingo-ism" — from the British exclamation "By Jingo!" used in a patriotic war song, so jingoism means extreme war-hungry patriotism.
把"jingoism"想成"jingo-ism"——源自英国感叹语"By Jingo!"，出现在一首爱国战歌中，所以jingoism意味着极端好战的爱国主义。
