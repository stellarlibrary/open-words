# 🔬 ovum

🔊 /ˈoʊvəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A mature female reproductive cell; an egg cell that can be fertilized to produce an embryo
**中文：** n. 卵；卵子；卵细胞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- egg cell（卵细胞）
- oocyte（卵母细胞）
- gamete（配子）
- reproductive cell（生殖细胞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fertilized ovum / human ovum / release an ovum / ovum implantation**

👉 Example: The fertilized ovum implants itself in the uterine wall within approximately one week after conception.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During each menstrual cycle, the ovary typically releases a single **ovum** that travels through the fallopian tube toward the uterus.
   在每个月经周期中，卵巢通常释放一个卵子，通过输卵管移向子宫。

2. In vitro fertilization involves extracting an **ovum** from the patient and combining it with sperm in a controlled laboratory environment.
   体外受精包括从患者体内提取卵子并在受控的实验室环境中将其与精子结合。

3. The biologist examined the **ovum** under a high-powered microscope to assess its viability for the reproductive research study.
   生物学家在高倍显微镜下检查卵子，以评估其在生殖研究中的可行性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ovum" as related to "oval" — an egg is oval-shaped, and an ovum is the biological term for an egg cell.
把"ovum"和"oval（椭圆形）"联系起来——鸡蛋是椭圆形的，而ovum就是卵细胞的生物学术语。
