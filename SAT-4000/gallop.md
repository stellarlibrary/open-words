# 🐎 gallop

🔊 /ˈɡæləp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The fastest pace of a horse or other quadruped; a rapid rate of progression. (v.) To run at a gallop; to move or progress swiftly
**中文：** n. 疾驰；飞奔；飞速 v. 飞驰；飞奔；疾驰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sprint（冲刺）
- dash（猛冲）
- bolt（飞奔）
- race（疾跑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gallop across / gallop through / at a gallop / gallop ahead**

👉 Example: The horse broke into a gallop as soon as the rider loosened the reins.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Inflation continued to **gallop** unchecked throughout the decade, eroding the purchasing power of ordinary citizens.
   通货膨胀在整个十年间持续飞速增长，侵蚀了普通公民的购买力。

2. The cavalry regiment began to **gallop** across the open plain, raising clouds of dust in their urgent advance.
   骑兵团开始在开阔的平原上疾驰，在紧急推进中扬起了阵阵尘土。

3. Technological innovation proceeds at such a **gallop** that regulatory frameworks often struggle to keep pace.
   技术创新以飞速推进，监管框架往往难以跟上其步伐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gallop" as the sound of hooves — "ga-llop, ga-llop" — rhythmically pounding the ground as a horse runs at full speed.
把"gallop"想象成马蹄声——"ga-llop, ga-llop"——有节奏地敲击地面，就像马在全速奔跑。
