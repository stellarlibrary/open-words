# 🎤 oration

🔊 /ɔːˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A formal speech, especially one given on a ceremonial occasion; an eloquent public address
**中文：** n. 演说；致辞；叙述法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- speech（演讲）
- address（致辞）
- discourse（论述）
- lecture（讲演）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliver an oration / funeral oration / public oration / eloquent oration**

👉 Example: The senator delivered a powerful oration on civil rights that moved the entire audience to applause.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The valedictorian's **oration** at the graduation ceremony eloquently captured the shared experiences and aspirations of the graduating class.
   毕业典礼上毕业生代表的演说雄辩地记录了毕业班的共同经历和愿望。

2. Lincoln's Gettysburg Address remains one of the most celebrated **orations** in the history of American political rhetoric.
   林肯的葛底斯堡演说仍然是美国政治修辞史上最著名的演讲之一。

3. The professor's **oration** on the ethical implications of artificial intelligence provoked a vigorous debate among the conference attendees.
   教授关于人工智能伦理影响的演说在与会者之间引发了激烈的辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oration" as "oral-ation" — an "oral" presentation or speech delivered formally to an audience.
把"oration"想成"oral-ation"——一种正式"口头的（oral）"演讲，面向听众发表。
