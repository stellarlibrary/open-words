# 🎩 urbane

🔊 /ɜːrˈbeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Suave, courteous, and refined in manner; showing worldly elegance and sophistication
**中文：** adj. 彬彬有礼的，温文尔雅的，文雅的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- suave（温文尔雅的）
- sophisticated（老练的）
- refined（优雅的）
- courteous（彬彬有礼的）
- polished（有教养的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **urbane manner / urbane charm / urbane sophistication / urbane gentleman**

👉 Example: His urbane demeanor and eloquent speech made him a natural choice for the diplomatic post.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **urbane** manner and cultural sensitivity enabled him to navigate complex international negotiations with ease.
   大使温文尔雅的举止和文化敏感性使他能够轻松驾驭复杂的国际谈判。

2. Despite his humble origins, the professor cultivated an **urbane** persona that impressed colleagues at prestigious academic conferences.
   尽管出身卑微，这位教授培养了一种温文尔雅的气质，在知名学术会议上给同事留下了深刻印象。

3. The novel's protagonist is an **urbane** intellectual whose refined wit conceals a deeply troubled and restless inner life.
   这部小说的主人公是一位温文尔雅的知识分子，其优雅的机智掩盖了内心深处的不安与挣扎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "urbane" as related to "urban" — a person from the city who is polished, sophisticated, and well-mannered.
把"urbane"和"urban"（城市的）联系起来——来自城市的人通常举止优雅、温文尔雅。
