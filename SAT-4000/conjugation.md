# 🔗 conjugation

🔊 /ˌkɒndʒʊˈɡeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The variation of the form of a verb to express tense, mood, person, or number; the act of joining or combining together.
**中文：** n. 动词的词形变化；结合，配合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inflection（词形变化）
- combination（结合）
- coupling（配合）
- union（联合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verb conjugation / conjugation pattern / conjugation table / learn conjugation**

👉 Example: Students must memorize the conjugation of irregular verbs before advancing to more complex grammatical structures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor devoted an entire lecture to the **conjugation** of Latin verbs, emphasizing their irregular forms and patterns.
   教授用一整节课讲解拉丁语动词的词形变化，强调其不规则形式和模式。

2. Understanding verb **conjugation** is essential for mastering any foreign language, particularly in formal written communication.
   理解动词的词形变化对于掌握任何外语至关重要，尤其在正式书面交流中。

3. The **conjugation** of these two bacterial cells resulted in the transfer of genetic material between the organisms.
   这两个细菌细胞的结合导致了遗传物质在有机体之间的转移。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-jug-ation" as "joining (con) together like a jug collects water" — conjugation brings different verb forms together in one system.
把"conjugation"想成"像水壶(jug)汇集水一样连接(con)在一起"——词形变化将不同的动词形式汇集在一个系统中。
