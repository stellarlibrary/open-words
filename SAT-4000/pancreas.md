# 🫁 pancreas

🔊 /ˈpæŋkriəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large gland behind the stomach that secretes digestive enzymes and produces insulin to regulate blood sugar
**中文：** n. 胰腺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sweetbread（胰脏，烹饪用语）
- pancreatic gland（胰腺）
- digestive gland（消化腺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pancreas function / inflammation of the pancreas / pancreas transplant / artificial pancreas**

👉 Example: The pancreas plays a vital role in both the digestive and endocrine systems of the human body.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Chronic alcohol consumption can severely damage the **pancreas**, leading to a painful and debilitating condition known as pancreatitis.
   长期饮酒会严重损害胰腺，导致一种称为胰腺炎的痛苦且使人衰弱的疾病。

2. Researchers developed an artificial **pancreas** that continuously monitors glucose levels and delivers insulin automatically to diabetic patients.
   研究人员开发了一种人工胰腺，能持续监测血糖水平并自动为糖尿病患者输送胰岛素。

3. The surgeon carefully examined the **pancreas** during the operation to determine whether the tumor had spread to adjacent organs.
   外科医生在手术中仔细检查了胰腺，以确定肿瘤是否已扩散到邻近器官。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pan-creas" — "pan" like a frying pan and "creas" like grease; the pancreas produces digestive juices that help break down greasy foods.
把"pancreas"想成"pan-creas"——"pan"像平底锅，"creas"像油脂；胰腺产生消化液来帮助分解油腻食物。
