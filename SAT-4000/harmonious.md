# 🎶 harmonious

🔊 /hɑːrˈmoʊniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Forming a pleasing or consistent whole; free from disagreement or dissent; tuneful and melodious
**中文：** adj. 和谐的，和睦的；协调的；相称的；融洽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concordant（一致的）
- compatible（协调的）
- amicable（和睦的）
- melodious（悦耳的）
- congruent（和谐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **harmonious relationship / harmonious blend / harmonious coexistence / harmonious balance**

👉 Example: The architecture creates a harmonious blend of traditional and modern design elements.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee worked toward a **harmonious** resolution that would satisfy all parties involved in the dispute.
   委员会致力于达成一项和谐的解决方案，以满足争端中所有相关方的需求。

2. A **harmonious** balance between economic development and environmental protection remains a critical policy objective.
   经济发展与环境保护之间的和谐平衡仍然是一项关键的政策目标。

3. The diverse instruments combined to produce a remarkably **harmonious** sound that captivated the entire audience.
   各种不同的乐器结合在一起，产生了非常和谐的声音，吸引了全场观众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "harmony + ous" — full of harmony, like musical notes blending together in perfect agreement.
把"harmonious"想成"harmony（和谐）+ ous（充满的）"——充满和谐，就像音符完美地融合在一起。
