# 🌸 ephemeral

🔊 /ɪˈfemərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting for a very short time; transitory; short-lived
**中文：** adj. 短暂的；朝生暮死的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transient（短暂的）
- fleeting（转瞬即逝的）
- momentary（瞬间的）
- evanescent（消逝的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ephemeral beauty / ephemeral pleasure / ephemeral nature / ephemeral existence**

👉 Example: The ephemeral beauty of cherry blossoms reminds us to appreciate the present moment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist's sand sculptures were deliberately **ephemeral**, designed to be washed away by the next incoming tide.
   这位艺术家的沙雕刻意是**短暂的**，被设计成会被下一次涨潮冲走。

2. Social media trends are inherently **ephemeral**, often capturing public attention for mere days before fading into obscurity.
   社交媒体趋势本质上是**短暂的**，通常只能吸引公众注意几天便消失无踪。

3. The committee warned that the economic recovery might prove **ephemeral** without sustained investment in infrastructure and education.
   委员会警告说，如果没有对基础设施和教育的持续投资，经济复苏可能只是**昙花一现**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ephemeral" as related to "E-FEM-eral" — like a femme (woman) in a fairy tale who appears briefly and vanishes at dawn.
把"ephemeral"联想到希腊语"ephemeros"（只活一天的）——像蜉蝣一样朝生暮死，极其短暂。
