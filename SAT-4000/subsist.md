# 🌾 subsist

🔊 /səbˈsɪst/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To maintain or support oneself at a minimal level; to exist; to remain in force
**中文：** v. 生存，生活；供养

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- survive（存活）
- exist（存在）
- endure（持续）
- persist（坚持）
- sustain（维持）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subsist on / subsist by / barely subsist / subsist at a minimal level**

👉 Example: During the famine, entire communities had to subsist on whatever roots and grains they could gather.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many rural families in developing nations **subsist** on less than two dollars per day and lack basic necessities.
   发展中国家的许多农村家庭每天靠不到两美元生存，缺乏基本生活必需品。

2. The explorers were forced to **subsist** on dried provisions and rainwater during their arduous journey through the wilderness.
   探险者们在穿越荒野的艰苦旅途中被迫靠干粮和雨水维生。

3. Ancient philosophical traditions continue to **subsist** in modern thought, influencing contemporary debates on ethics and morality.
   古代哲学传统在现代思想中继续存在，影响着当代关于伦理和道德的辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-sist" as "existing below" — to subsist is to exist at a basic, minimal level, just barely getting by.
把"subsist"想成"sub"（在下面）+"sist"（存在）——以最基本、最低限度的水平存在，勉强度日。
