# 🏆 consummate

🔊 /ˈkɑːnsəmət (adj.), ˈkɑːnsəmeɪt (v.)/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Supremely skilled; complete or perfect in every respect. (v.) To bring to completion or fulfillment; to make perfect
**中文：** adj. 至上的，完美的，圆满的 v. 完成；使达到极点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perfect（完美的）
- supreme（至上的）
- accomplished（精通的）
- fulfill（实现）
- complete（完成）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **consummate skill / consummate professional / consummate a deal / consummate an agreement**

👉 Example: The ambassador was a consummate diplomat who navigated international crises with remarkable poise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The violinist delivered a **consummate** performance that left the audience in stunned silence before erupting into prolonged applause.
   这位小提琴家奉献了一场完美的演出，观众先是惊叹得鸦雀无声，随后爆发出持久的掌声。

2. After months of intense negotiation, the two corporations finally **consummated** the merger that reshaped the entire telecommunications industry.
   经过数月的激烈谈判，两家公司终于完成了重塑整个电信行业的合并。

3. She is widely regarded as a **consummate** scholar whose publications have profoundly influenced contemporary literary criticism and theory.
   她被广泛认为是一位造诣极高的学者，其出版物深刻影响了当代文学批评和理论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-SUMMA-te" — "summa" means highest in Latin, so consummate means reaching the highest level of perfection.
把"consummate"想成"con-SUMMA-te"——"summa"在拉丁语中意为最高，所以consummate意味着达到最高的完美境界。
