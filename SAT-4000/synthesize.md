# 🔬 synthesize

🔊 /ˈsɪnθəsaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To combine separate elements into a coherent whole; to produce by chemical or biochemical synthesis
**中文：** v. 综合；合成

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- combine（结合）
- integrate（整合）
- merge（合并）
- unify（统一）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **synthesize information / synthesize data / synthesize a compound / synthesize ideas**

👉 Example: Students must learn to synthesize information from multiple sources when writing research papers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The analyst was able to **synthesize** vast amounts of data into a concise and actionable intelligence report.
   分析师能够将大量数据综合成一份简明且可操作的情报报告。

2. Chemists have developed new methods to **synthesize** complex organic molecules more efficiently in the laboratory.
   化学家们开发了新方法，以便在实验室中更高效地合成复杂的有机分子。

3. The committee attempted to **synthesize** the diverse viewpoints of all stakeholders before drafting the final proposal.
   委员会在起草最终提案之前，试图综合所有利益相关者的不同观点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "synthesize" as "synth" (together) + "ize" (to make) — to make things come together into one.
把"synthesize"想成"synth"（一起）+"ize"（使）——使事物汇聚在一起成为一体。
