# 😬 grimace

🔊 /ˈɡrɪməs/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make a twisted or distorted facial expression, typically of pain or disgust. (n.) A contorted facial expression indicating pain, disgust, or disapproval
**中文：** v. 扮鬼脸；作怪相 n. 鬼脸；怪相

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scowl（怒容）
- frown（皱眉）
- sneer（冷笑）
- wince（畏缩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grimace in pain / grimace at / grimace of disgust / a painful grimace / grimace with displeasure**

👉 Example: The patient grimaced in pain as the doctor carefully examined the injured shoulder.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The child **grimaced** at the bitter taste of the medicine, refusing to swallow the remaining dose without protest.
   孩子因药的苦味做了个鬼脸，不愿乖乖吞下剩余的药量。

2. A visible **grimace** crossed the senator's face when the journalist raised an uncomfortable question about the scandal.
   当记者提出关于丑闻的令人不安的问题时，参议员脸上露出了明显的不悦表情。

3. She tried to suppress a **grimace** as she read the scathing review of her recently published academic paper.
   她在阅读对她最近发表的学术论文的尖锐评论时，努力克制住想做鬼脸的冲动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grimace" as "grim + face" — a grim face is exactly what a grimace looks like, a twisted expression of pain or displeasure.
把"grimace"想成"grim + face"——一张阴沉的脸正是鬼脸的样子，表示痛苦或不快的扭曲表情。
