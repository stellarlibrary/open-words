# ✅ admissible

🔊 /ədˈmɪsəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Acceptable or valid, especially as evidence in a court of law; worthy of being allowed or permitted.
**中文：** 可容许的；可采纳的；可接受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acceptable（可接受的）
- permissible（允许的）
- allowable（容许的）
- legitimate（合法的）
- valid（有效的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **admissible evidence**, **admissible in court**, **legally admissible**

👉 Example: The judge ruled that the recording was admissible as evidence in the trial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney argued that the coerced confession should not be considered **admissible** in court.
   辩护律师辩称，被迫供词不应被视为法庭上可采纳的证据。

2. Only data collected through approved methodologies will be deemed **admissible** for the research review.
   只有通过认可方法收集的数据才会被认为可用于研究审查。

3. The committee established clear guidelines defining what constitutes **admissible** documentation for the application.
   委员会制定了明确的指南，规定申请中何种文件为可接受的材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "admit" + "-ible" — something that can be admitted or allowed in, especially into a courtroom.
联想"admit"（准许进入）+"-ible"（可以的）——可以被准许进入的，尤其是可以被法庭采纳的。
