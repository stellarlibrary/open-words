# 🧠 acumen

🔊 /əˈkjuː.mən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ability to make good judgments and quick decisions, especially in a particular domain; keen insight and shrewdness.
**中文：** 聪明，敏锐

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shrewdness（精明）
- insight（洞察力）
- astuteness（机敏）
- perspicacity（敏锐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **business/political/financial acumen**, **demonstrate/show acumen**, **sharp acumen**

👉 Example: Her remarkable business acumen helped transform a small startup into a global enterprise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investor's financial **acumen** enabled him to identify profitable opportunities that others had overlooked.
   这位投资者的财务敏锐性使他能够发现其他人忽略的盈利机会。

2. Political **acumen** is essential for any leader who hopes to navigate complex diplomatic negotiations successfully.
   对于任何希望成功驾驭复杂外交谈判的领导人来说，政治敏锐性是必不可少的。

3. She demonstrated exceptional **acumen** in identifying market trends long before her competitors recognized them.
   她在竞争对手之前很久就展现出了识别市场趋势的卓越洞察力。

---
