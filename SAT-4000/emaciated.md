# 😞 emaciated

🔊 /ɪˈmeɪʃieɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Abnormally thin or weak, especially due to illness, starvation, or severe lack of nourishment
**中文：** adj. 瘦弱的；憔悴的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gaunt（憔悴的）
- skeletal（骨瘦如柴的）
- wasted（消瘦的）
- haggard（枯槁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emaciated body / emaciated figure / emaciated appearance / look emaciated / severely emaciated**

👉 Example: The rescued hostages appeared emaciated after months of captivity without proper food.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The humanitarian workers were deeply disturbed by the **emaciated** children they encountered in the drought-stricken refugee camps.
   人道主义工作者对他们在遭受干旱的难民营中遇到的瘦弱儿童深感不安。

2. After a prolonged battle with the illness, the once-robust athlete had become visibly **emaciated** and could barely walk unassisted.
   在与疾病的长期斗争之后，这位曾经健壮的运动员已明显消瘦，几乎无法独自行走。

3. The **emaciated** stray animals found near the abandoned factory were immediately taken to a veterinary clinic for urgent care.
   在废弃工厂附近发现的瘦弱流浪动物被立即送往兽医诊所进行紧急治疗。

把"emaciated"想成"e-MACE-iated"——想象一个人被锤击了太多次，以至于消瘦得只剩皮包骨头。
