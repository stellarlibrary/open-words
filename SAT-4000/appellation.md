# 🏷️ appellation

🔊 /ˌæpəˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A name, title, or designation by which a person, place, or thing is known or called
**中文：** 称呼；名称；命名

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- designation（称号）
- title（头衔）
- name（名称）
- moniker（绰号）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **earn an appellation / bear the appellation / a formal appellation / a regional appellation**

👉 Example: The city earned the appellation "The Eternal City" due to its enduring cultural significance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist earned the **appellation** "Father of Modern Physics" for his groundbreaking contributions to quantum theory.
   这位科学家因其对量子理论的开创性贡献而获得"现代物理学之父"的称号。

2. The wine's prestigious **appellation** guarantees that it was produced in a specific region under strict quality regulations.
   这款葡萄酒的知名产区名称保证了它是在严格质量规定下于特定地区酿造的。

3. Many indigenous communities prefer their traditional **appellation** over the names imposed by colonial administrators centuries ago.
   许多原住民社区更喜欢使用他们的传统称呼，而非几个世纪前殖民管理者强加的名称。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "appellation" as "a-pell-ation" — linked to "appeal," because a name or title is what people use to appeal to or call someone.
把"appellation"想成与"appeal"（呼唤）相关——名称或头衔就是人们用来称呼某人的方式。
