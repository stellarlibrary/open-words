# 🌐 multiplicity

🔊 /ˌmʌltɪˈplɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large number or great variety; the quality or state of being multiple or various
**中文：** n. 多样性；多重性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- variety（多样性）
- diversity（多元性）
- plurality（多数）
- abundance（丰富）
- profusion（大量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a multiplicity of factors / a multiplicity of views / a multiplicity of roles / a multiplicity of causes**

👉 Example: The committee had to consider a multiplicity of perspectives before reaching a final consensus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **multiplicity** of factors, including economic instability and political unrest, contributed to the dramatic decline in foreign investment.
   包括经济不稳定和政治动荡在内的多种因素导致了外国投资的急剧下降。

2. The professor emphasized that a **multiplicity** of interpretations can coexist within a single literary text without contradiction.
   教授强调，在一部文学作品中可以同时存在多种解读而不相互矛盾。

3. Modern professionals often navigate a **multiplicity** of roles, balancing career obligations with personal and civic responsibilities.
   现代职业人士常常需要应对多重角色，在职业义务与个人及公民责任之间取得平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "multiplicity" as "MULTI-plic-ity" — the state of having multiple layers or aspects, like a city (city) with many diverse neighborhoods.
把"multiplicity"想成"MULTI-plic-ity"——拥有多层面或多方面的状态，就像一座拥有许多不同社区的城市(city)。
