# 💔 remorse

🔊 /rɪˈmɔːrs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Deep regret or guilt for a wrong committed; a gnawing distress arising from a sense of having done something morally wrong
**中文：** n. 懊悔；自责；同情，怜悯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regret（后悔）
- contrition（悔恨）
- penitence（忏悔）
- guilt（愧疚）
- compunction（内疚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel remorse / show remorse / without remorse / filled with remorse / a sense of remorse**

👉 Example: The defendant expressed genuine remorse for his actions during the sentencing hearing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The convicted official expressed deep **remorse** for the corruption that had eroded public trust in the institution he once led.
   这位被定罪的官员对曾经侵蚀他所领导机构公众信任的腐败行为表示深深的懊悔。

2. She was overcome with **remorse** after realizing that her careless remarks had caused irreparable damage to a valued friendship.
   当她意识到自己轻率的言论对一段珍贵的友谊造成了无法弥补的伤害后，她充满了自责。

3. The novel explores the crushing weight of **remorse** carried by soldiers who survived while their comrades perished in battle.
   这部小说探讨了幸存士兵在战友阵亡后所承受的沉重懊悔之情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remorse" as "re-morse" (morse code) — the guilt keeps sending repeated signals in your mind, like an SOS you cannot silence.
把"remorse"想成"re-morse"（摩尔斯电码）——愧疚不断在你脑海中发出重复的信号，像一个无法消除的求救信号。
