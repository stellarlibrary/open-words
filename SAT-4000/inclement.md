# 🌧️ inclement

🔊 /ɪnˈklɛmənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) (Of weather) unpleasantly cold or wet; severe or harsh in nature.
**中文：** adj. 险恶的；气候严酷的；狂风暴雨的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harsh（严酷的）
- severe（恶劣的）
- stormy（暴风雨的）
- tempestuous（狂暴的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inclement weather / inclement conditions / due to inclement weather**

👉 Example: The outdoor ceremony was postponed indefinitely due to inclement weather conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university announced that all outdoor athletic events would be canceled owing to the **inclement** weather forecast for the weekend.
   大学宣布，由于周末恶劣的天气预报，所有户外体育赛事将被取消。

2. Researchers studying arctic ecosystems must endure **inclement** conditions that would deter most ordinary field investigators.
   研究北极生态系统的研究人员必须忍受大多数普通野外调查员望而却步的严酷条件。

3. Despite the **inclement** skies and biting winds, the determined volunteers continued distributing supplies to the displaced families.
   尽管天空阴沉、寒风刺骨，坚定的志愿者们仍继续向流离失所的家庭分发物资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CLEMENT" — "clement" means mild and merciful. Adding "in-" reverses it: inclement weather is NOT mild — it is harsh and merciless.
把"inclement"想成"in-CLEMENT"——"clement"意为温和的、仁慈的。加上"in-"就反义了：inclement天气一点都不温和——它严酷无情。
