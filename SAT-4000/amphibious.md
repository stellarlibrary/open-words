# 🐸 amphibious

🔊 /æmˈfɪbiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to, living in, or suited for both land and water; involving military operations launched from the sea.
**中文：** 两栖的，水陆两用的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dual-terrain（双地形的）
- aquatic-terrestrial（水陆的）
- versatile（多用途的）
- adaptable（适应性强的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amphibious assault, amphibious vehicle, amphibious operation, amphibious landing, amphibious creature**
👉 Example: The military deployed amphibious vehicles to cross the river during the exercise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **amphibious** assault on the enemy coastline was meticulously planned over several months by the commanders.
   指挥官们花了几个月时间精心策划了对敌方海岸线的两栖突击。

2. Frogs are **amphibious** creatures that spend their early development in water before transitioning to land.
   青蛙是两栖动物，它们在早期发育阶段生活在水中，然后过渡到陆地。

3. The engineering team designed an **amphibious** craft capable of traversing both marshlands and open waterways efficiently.
   工程团队设计了一种能够高效穿越沼泽地和开阔水道的水陆两用船。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amphi-" (both sides) + "bios" (life) = life on both sides, living in water and on land.
"Amphi-"（两边）+ "bios"（生命）= 两边的生命，即水陆两栖的。
