# 🎭 ironic

🔊 /aɪˈrɒnɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Using or characterized by irony; happening in a way that is opposite to what is expected and often amusing as a result
**中文：** adj. 讽刺的；反话的；具有讽刺意味的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sarcastic（讽刺的）
- sardonic（嘲讽的）
- satirical（讽刺性的）
- paradoxical（矛盾的）
- wry（挖苦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ironic twist / ironic remark / ironic tone / it is ironic that...**

👉 Example: It is ironic that the fire station itself burned down while all the firefighters were away on a call.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. It was deeply **ironic** that the renowned communication professor struggled to convey his own ideas clearly to the faculty.
   颇具讽刺意味的是，这位著名的传播学教授却难以向同事清楚地传达自己的想法。

2. The **ironic** outcome of the energy conservation campaign was a significant increase in overall electricity consumption.
   这场节能运动的讽刺性结果是总体电力消耗的显著增加。

3. Critics noted the **ironic** contrast between the politician's public advocacy for transparency and his private concealment of records.
   评论家注意到这位政治家公开倡导透明与私下隐瞒记录之间的讽刺性对比。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ironic" as "iron-ic" — imagine an iron mask that shows one expression while hiding the opposite feeling underneath.
把"ironic"想成"iron-ic"——想象一个铁面具，表面展示一种表情，而下面却隐藏着相反的感受。
