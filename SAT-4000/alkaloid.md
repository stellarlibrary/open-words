# 🌿 alkaloid

🔊 /ˈæl.kə.lɔɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A class of naturally occurring organic compounds that contain nitrogen, are typically derived from plants, and often have significant pharmacological effects.
**中文：** 生物碱；植物碱基。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plant base（植物碱）
- organic compound（有机化合物）
- phytochemical（植物化学物质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plant alkaloid, alkaloid content, alkaloid compound, toxic alkaloid, alkaloid extraction**
👉 Example: Caffeine is one of the most widely consumed alkaloids in the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmacologist isolated a potent **alkaloid** from the bark of the tropical tree for further medicinal study.
   药理学家从热带树木的树皮中分离出一种强效生物碱，以供进一步药物研究。

2. Many **alkaloids** found in nature serve as chemical defenses that protect plants against herbivorous predators.
   自然界中许多生物碱充当化学防御物质，保护植物免受草食性捕食者的侵害。

3. The toxicology report confirmed that the poisoning was caused by an **alkaloid** present in the ingested berries.
   毒理学报告证实，中毒是由所食浆果中含有的一种生物碱引起的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alkaloid" = "alkali" + "-oid" (resembling) — these compounds "resemble alkali" because they are basic (alkaline) in nature.
"alkaloid" = "alkali（碱）" + "-oid（类似的）"——这些化合物"类似碱"，因为它们本质上呈碱性。
