# 🔢 apiece

🔊 /əˈpiːs/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** For each one; to, by, or from each individual person or item in a group; used to indicate an equal distribution or amount per unit.

**中文：** 每人；每个

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- each（每个）
- per person（每人）
- individually（各自地）
- respectively（分别地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **cost ... apiece**, **score ... apiece**, **receive ... apiece**, **worth ... apiece**, always placed **after** the quantity it modifies.

> The rare manuscripts were valued at ten thousand dollars **apiece**.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The handcrafted ceramic vases were priced at two hundred dollars **apiece** at the exhibition.
   手工制作的陶瓷花瓶在展览上每件标价两百美元。

2. The two competing teams scored three goals **apiece** before the match entered overtime proceedings.
   两支对阵球队在比赛进入加时赛前各攻入三球。

3. Each member of the delegation received a commemorative medallion and certificate **apiece** at the ceremony.
   代表团的每位成员在仪式上各获得了一枚纪念章和一份证书。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Break it down: "a" + "piece." Think of giving **a piece** to each person — everyone gets one **apiece**.

拆分记忆：a（一个）+ piece（份）。想象给每个人发"一份"——每人一份就是 apiece（每人/每个）。
