# 📣 propaganda

🔊 /ˌprɒpəˈɡændə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Information, especially of a biased or misleading nature, used to promote a political cause or point of view
**中文：** n. 宣传；（有偏向性的）宣传材料

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disinformation（虚假信息）
- indoctrination（灌输）
- publicity（宣传）
- agitation（煽动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spread propaganda / propaganda campaign / propaganda machine / political propaganda**

👉 Example: The regime relied heavily on propaganda to maintain its grip on power.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians argue that wartime **propaganda** significantly influenced public opinion and bolstered support for military campaigns abroad.
   历史学家认为，战时宣传极大地影响了公众舆论，并增强了对海外军事行动的支持。

2. The authoritarian government used state-controlled media as a vehicle for **propaganda** to suppress dissenting political voices.
   该专制政府利用国家控制的媒体作为宣传工具来压制不同的政治声音。

3. Critical thinking skills are essential for identifying **propaganda** disguised as legitimate journalism in the modern information landscape.
   在现代信息环境中，批判性思维技能对于识别伪装成合法新闻的宣传至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "propaganda" as "proper agenda" — it is information crafted to push a proper agenda onto the public.
把"propaganda"想成"proper agenda"——它是为了向公众推行特定议程而精心制作的信息。
