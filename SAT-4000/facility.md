# 🏢 facility

🔊 /fəˈsɪlɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A building, service, or piece of equipment provided for a particular purpose; also, a natural ability to do something well and easily
**中文：** 设施；设备；容易；灵巧；天赋

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- establishment（机构）
- installation（设施）
- aptitude（天资）
- proficiency（熟练）
- amenity（便利设施）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **research facility / medical facility / facility for languages / facility with numbers**

👉 Example: The new research facility will house state-of-the-art laboratories for biomedical studies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government allocated substantial funding to construct a advanced manufacturing **facility** dedicated to renewable energy technology.
   政府拨出大量资金建造一座专门用于可再生能源技术的先进制造设施。

2. Her remarkable **facility** with foreign languages enabled her to serve as the lead interpreter at international summit meetings.
   她出色的语言天赋使她能够在国际峰会上担任首席翻译。

3. The medical **facility** was equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic instruments that significantly improved patient treatment outcomes nationwide.
   该医疗设施配备了尖端的诊断仪器，显著改善了全国范围内的患者治疗效果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "facility" as a place that makes things "facile" (easy) — a facility provides the tools and space to accomplish tasks with ease.
把"facility"想成一个让事情变得"facile"(容易)的地方——设施提供了轻松完成任务所需的工具和空间。
