# 🙅 ingrate

🔊 /ˈɪnɡreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An ungrateful person who fails to show appreciation for kindness received. (adj.) Ungrateful; showing no thanks
**中文：** n. 忘恩负义的人 adj. 忘恩的；不知恩的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ungrateful person（不知感恩的人）
- thankless wretch（不知好歹的人）
- unappreciative（不领情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a complete ingrate / such an ingrate / an ungrateful ingrate / labeled as an ingrate**

👉 Example: After all the support she provided, he proved himself to be nothing but an ingrate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The benefactor felt deeply hurt when the scholarship recipient proved to be an **ingrate** who never acknowledged the gift.
   当奖学金获得者被证明是个忘恩负义之人，从未对这份馈赠表示感谢时，施恩者深感受伤。

2. History often remembers those who bite the hand that feeds them as nothing more than contemptible **ingrates**.
   历史往往将那些恩将仇报的人记为令人鄙视的忘恩负义者。

3. The mentor warned that acting like an **ingrate** would eventually alienate the very allies who had supported his career.
   导师警告说，表现得忘恩负义最终会疏远那些曾支持他事业的盟友。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-GRATE" — the prefix "in-" means "not," and "grate" resembles "grateful," so an ingrate is someone who is NOT grateful.
把"ingrate"想成"in（不）+ grate（像grateful感恩的）"——ingrate就是不感恩的人。
