# 🦊 guile

🔊 /ɡaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Sly or cunning intelligence; the use of deceit or trickery to achieve one's ends
**中文：** n. 狡猾；诡计；欺诈；奸诈

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cunning（狡猾）
- deceit（欺骗）
- craftiness（诡诈）
- duplicity（口是心非）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **use guile / employ guile / full of guile / political guile**

👉 Example: The diplomat relied more on guile than on force to achieve favorable terms during the negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The con artist used remarkable **guile** to deceive even the most cautious investors into parting with their savings.
   那个骗子用非凡的狡猾手段欺骗了最谨慎的投资者，使他们交出了积蓄。

2. Through sheer **guile** and strategic maneuvering, the politician outmaneuvered his opponents in the contentious legislative debate.
   凭借十足的狡诈和战略运作，这位政治家在激烈的立法辩论中击败了对手。

3. The ancient myth portrays the fox as a creature of extraordinary **guile**, capable of outwitting far stronger animals.
   这则古老的神话将狐狸描绘为一种极其狡猾的动物，能够智胜比它强壮得多的动物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "guile" as sounding like "wile" — both words suggest crafty tricks and sly deception.
把"guile"想成听起来像"wile"（诡计）——两个词都暗示狡猾的伎俩和诡诈的欺骗。
