# 🧼 saponaceous

🔊 /ˌsæpəˈneɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Resembling or having the qualities of soap; smooth and slippery in manner; glib
**中文：** adj. 圆滑的；肥皂似的；口齿伶俐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- slippery（圆滑的）
- unctuous（油嘴滑舌的）
- glib（口齿伶俐的）
- soapy（肥皂似的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **saponaceous manner / saponaceous charm / saponaceous texture / saponaceous reply**

👉 Example: His saponaceous manner made it difficult to pin him down on any specific commitment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **saponaceous** rhetoric allowed him to evade direct questions without offending any of the delegates present.
   这位外交官圆滑的措辞使他能够回避直接问题，同时不冒犯在场的任何代表。

2. Critics described the candidate's **saponaceous** personality as charming yet fundamentally untrustworthy in matters of policy.
   批评者形容这位候选人圆滑的个性虽然迷人，但在政策问题上根本不值得信赖。

3. The **saponaceous** quality of the mineral made it feel smooth and slippery, much like a bar of wet soap.
   这种矿物肥皂似的质地使它摸起来光滑而滑腻，很像一块湿肥皂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sapon-aceous" — "sapon" comes from Latin "sapo" meaning soap. A saponaceous person is as slippery as a bar of soap — you can never quite get a grip on them.
把"saponaceous"想成"sapon（肥皂）+ aceous（具有…性质的）"——一个圆滑的人就像一块肥皂一样滑溜，你永远抓不住他。
