# 🌊 serene

🔊 /səˈriːn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, verb | 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Calm, peaceful, and untroubled; clear and bright. (n.) A calm or tranquil state. (v.) To make calm or peaceful
**中文：** adj. 安定的，没有风波的；沉着的，沉静的，安详的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tranquil（宁静的）
- placid（平静的）
- peaceful（和平的）
- composed（沉着的）
- unruffled（镇定的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **serene expression / serene atmosphere / serene landscape / remain serene**

👉 Example: Despite the chaos around her, she maintained a serene composure that impressed everyone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat maintained a **serene** demeanor throughout the heated negotiations, never revealing any sign of frustration.
   这位外交官在激烈的谈判过程中始终保持安详的风度，从未表露出任何沮丧的迹象。

2. The monastery was nestled in a **serene** valley surrounded by ancient forests and gently flowing streams.
   修道院坐落在一个宁静的山谷中，四周环绕着古老的森林和缓缓流淌的溪流。

3. Her **serene** confidence during the crisis inspired the entire team to remain calm and focused on solutions.
   她在危机中表现出的沉静自信激励了整个团队保持冷静并专注于解决方案。

把"serene"想成"se-REEN"——想象电影银幕上一个宁静的画面，像镜面湖一样完美地平静。
