# 🌈 iridescent

🔊 /ˌɪrɪˈdesənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles; shimmering with rainbow-like colors
**中文：** adj. 彩虹色的；闪光的；色彩斑斓的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- opalescent（乳白色的；闪彩虹光的）
- shimmering（闪烁的）
- lustrous（有光泽的）
- prismatic（棱镜般的）
- pearlescent（珠光的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **iridescent sheen / iridescent glow / iridescent feathers / iridescent surface / iridescent colors**

👉 Example: The hummingbird's iridescent feathers shifted from emerald green to sapphire blue as it turned in the sunlight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **iridescent** wings of the butterfly displayed a stunning array of colors that shifted with every subtle movement.
   蝴蝶彩虹般闪烁的翅膀展现出一系列令人惊叹的色彩，随着每次细微的移动而变化。

2. Scientists studied the **iridescent** properties of certain minerals to develop advanced optical coatings for modern technology applications.
   科学家研究了某些矿物的虹彩特性，以开发用于现代技术应用的先进光学涂层。

3. The oil spill left an **iridescent** film on the surface of the water, revealing the contamination's disturbing yet vivid presence.
   石油泄漏在水面上留下了一层彩虹色的薄膜，揭示了污染令人不安却又鲜明的存在。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "IRIS-descent" — "iris" is the Greek goddess of the rainbow, so iridescent means having rainbow-like, shifting colors descending across a surface.
把"iridescent"想成"IRIS-descent"——"iris"是希腊彩虹女神，所以iridescent就是有着像彩虹般变幻的色彩在表面流转。
