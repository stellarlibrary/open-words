# 📋 verification

🔊 /ˌvɛrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of establishing the truth, accuracy, or validity of something; confirmation through evidence or proof
**中文：** n. 确认；查证；核实

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confirmation（确认）
- validation（验证）
- authentication（鉴定）
- corroboration（证实）
- substantiation（证明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **identity verification / verification process / verification of results / require verification / independent verification**

👉 Example: The bank requires two-step verification before granting access to sensitive account information.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory conducted independent **verification** of the experimental results before the findings were submitted for peer review.
   实验室在将研究结果提交同行评审之前，对实验结果进行了独立核实。

2. Government agencies now mandate rigorous identity **verification** procedures to prevent fraudulent access to classified information systems.
   政府机构现在要求严格的身份验证程序，以防止对机密信息系统的欺诈性访问。

3. The archaeological team sought **verification** from multiple carbon-dating laboratories to confirm the artifact's estimated historical age.
   考古队向多个碳测年实验室寻求验证，以确认该文物的估计历史年代。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "verification" as "veri-fic-ation" — "veri" from "verity" (truth) plus "-fication" (making), so it means the act of making something true or confirmed.
把"verification"想成"veri-fic-ation"——"veri"来自"verity"（真实）加上"-fication"（制作），意为确认某事为真的行为。
