# 🏔️ elevated

🔊 /ˈɛləˌveɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Raised to a higher level or position; lofty in character, tone, or moral quality; dignified
**中文：** adj. 提高的；高尚的；严肃的；高架的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lofty（崇高的）
- exalted（高贵的）
- dignified（庄严的）
- raised（提高的）
- noble（高尚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elevated level / elevated tone / elevated status / elevated language / elevated position**

👉 Example: The professor maintained an elevated tone throughout the academic lecture on moral philosophy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient's **elevated** blood pressure prompted the physician to recommend immediate lifestyle changes and medication adjustments.
   患者血压升高促使医生建议立即改变生活方式并调整药物。

2. Her **elevated** sense of responsibility made her the ideal candidate for leading the international humanitarian relief effort.
   她崇高的责任感使她成为领导国际人道主义救援工作的理想人选。

3. The ambassador delivered his address in an **elevated** style that conveyed both gravitas and sincere diplomatic respect.
   大使以庄重的风格发表了讲话，既传达了严肃感又表达了真诚的外交尊重。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elevated" as standing on an "elevated" platform — you are physically higher, and figuratively, your status or quality is also higher.
把"elevated"想成站在一个"高架"平台上——你在物理上更高，而在比喻意义上，你的地位或品质也更高。
