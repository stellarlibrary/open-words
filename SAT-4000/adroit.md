# 🎯 adroit

🔊 /əˈdrɔɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Clever or skillful in using the hands or mind; resourceful and dexterous
**中文：** adj. 敏捷的；灵巧的；熟练的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deft（灵巧的）
- skillful（熟练的）
- dexterous（敏捷的）
- nimble（灵活的）
- proficient（精通的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adroit at / adroit in / adroit handling / politically adroit**

👉 Example: The diplomat was adroit at navigating complex international negotiations with grace and precision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon's **adroit** hands performed the delicate procedure with remarkable precision and unwavering confidence.
   这位外科医生灵巧的双手以非凡的精准和坚定的信心完成了这项精密手术。

2. Her **adroit** management of the financial crisis saved the corporation from what seemed like inevitable bankruptcy.
   她对金融危机的熟练应对使公司免于了看似不可避免的破产。

3. The lawyer proved exceptionally **adroit** at cross-examining witnesses and exposing inconsistencies in their testimonies.
   这位律师在交叉盘问证人和揭露其证词矛盾方面表现得极为娴熟。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adroit" as coming from French "à droit" (to the right) — a right-handed person is typically more skillful with their dominant hand.
"adroit"源自法语"à droit"（向右），右手通常是惯用手，更加灵巧——联想为"右手般熟练"。
