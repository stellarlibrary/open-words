# 🎣 angle

🔊 /ˈæŋɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *v. to fish; to scheme for | 动词：钓鱼；谋取 · n. angle; perspective | 名词：角度，角，方面*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fish with a hook and line; to try to obtain something by indirect or artful means. (n.) The space between two intersecting lines; a point of view or perspective.
**中文：** （动词）用钩和线钓鱼；用间接或巧妙的手段谋取某物。（名词）两条相交线之间的空间；观点或角度。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fish（钓鱼）
- scheme（谋划）
- perspective（视角）
- viewpoint（观点）
- slant（倾斜；角度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **angle for (a promotion/compliment), from every angle, at an angle**
👉 Example: She tried to angle for a better position by flattering her supervisor constantly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician attempted to **angle** for public sympathy by recounting his humble origins during the debate.
   那位政客试图在辩论中通过讲述自己卑微的出身来谋取公众的同情。

2. We must examine this complex issue from every possible **angle** before reaching a final decision.
   在做出最终决定之前，我们必须从每一个可能的角度审视这个复杂的问题。

3. He spent the entire afternoon **angling** in the serene lake behind the old country estate.
   他整个下午都在那座古老乡间庄园后面宁静的湖中钓鱼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of an angler casting a line at just the right "angle" to catch a fish — both meanings share the idea of positioning carefully to get what you want.
想象一个钓鱼者以恰到好处的"角度"抛出鱼线来钓鱼——两个意思都包含了巧妙定位以获取所需之物的概念。
