# 🧑 stripling

🔊 /ˈstrɪplɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A young man or boy; an adolescent who has not yet fully matured
**中文：** n. 年轻人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- youth（青年）
- adolescent（青少年）
- youngster（年轻人）
- lad（小伙子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a mere stripling / young stripling / ambitious stripling / from stripling to statesman**

👉 Example: The general dismissed the new recruit as a mere stripling unfit for the rigors of command.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. As a **stripling** of only seventeen, he demonstrated remarkable composure and leadership during the crisis at the academy.
   作为一个年仅十七岁的年轻人，他在学院的危机中表现出了非凡的镇定和领导力。

2. The seasoned diplomat recalled his days as a **stripling** entering the foreign service with boundless ambition and naivety.
   这位经验丰富的外交官回忆起自己年轻时怀着无限抱负和天真进入外交部门的日子。

3. Many a **stripling** from the countryside traveled to the capital seeking education and opportunities unavailable in rural provinces.
   许多来自乡村的年轻人前往首都寻求在偏远省份无法获得的教育和机会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "strip-ling" as a "little strip" — a young sapling that is still thin like a strip, not yet grown into a full tree.
把"stripling"想成"小细条"——一棵还像细条一样纤细的幼苗，尚未长成一棵大树。
