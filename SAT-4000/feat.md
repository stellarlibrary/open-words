# 🏆 feat

🔊 /fiːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An achievement that requires great courage, skill, or strength; a remarkable accomplishment or deed
**中文：** n. 功绩；伟业；壮举；技艺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- achievement（成就）
- accomplishment（功绩）
- exploit（壮举）
- deed（事迹）
- triumph（胜利）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remarkable feat / feat of engineering / feat of strength / no mean feat / extraordinary feat**

👉 Example: Completing the transcontinental railway was a remarkable feat of nineteenth-century engineering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Landing a spacecraft on Mars was widely regarded as one of the greatest **feat**s of modern engineering and scientific innovation.
   将航天器降落在火星上被广泛认为是现代工程和科学创新最伟大的壮举之一。

2. Translating the entire ancient manuscript within a single year was no mean **feat**, given its archaic and obscure language.
   在一年内翻译完整部古代手稿绝非易事，因为其语言古老而晦涩。

3. The athlete's **feat** of breaking three world records in one competition earned her universal admiration and international acclaim.
   这位运动员在一场比赛中打破三项世界纪录的壮举为她赢得了普遍的钦佩和国际赞誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "feat" as related to "feet" — great feats require you to stand on your own two feet and accomplish something extraordinary.
把"feat"联想到"feet（脚）"——伟大的壮举需要你靠自己的双脚站立，完成非凡的成就。
