# ⚠️ injurious

🔊 /ɪnˈdʒʊəriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing or likely to cause damage or harm; hurtful; defamatory or slanderous
**中文：** adj. 有害的；造成伤害的；诽谤的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmful（有害的）
- detrimental（不利的）
- damaging（破坏性的）
- deleterious（有害的）
- pernicious（有毒害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **injurious to health / injurious effects / injurious substances / injurious to reputation**

👉 Example: Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation is injurious to human skin and eyes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court ruled that the factory's emissions were **injurious** to the health of residents living within a five-mile radius.
   法院裁定该工厂的排放物对五英里范围内居民的健康有害。

2. Researchers have demonstrated that prolonged sedentary behavior is profoundly **injurious** to cardiovascular health and overall well-being.
   研究人员已证明，长时间久坐不动对心血管健康和整体健康极其有害。

3. The publication of such **injurious** statements without factual basis constituted defamation under existing national media law.
   在没有事实依据的情况下发表这种有害的声明，依据现行国家媒体法构成诽谤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "injurious" as "injury + ous" — something that has the quality of causing injury is injurious.
把"injurious"想成"injury（伤害）+ ous（形容词后缀）"——具有造成伤害特性的东西就是injurious。
