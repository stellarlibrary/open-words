# 🚧 quarantine

🔊 /ˈkwɒrəntiːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A period of isolation imposed to prevent the spread of disease. (v.) To place in isolation to prevent contamination or the spread of infectious disease
**中文：** n. 检疫期；隔离；封锁 v. 检疫；隔离

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- isolation（隔离）
- seclusion（隔绝）
- segregation（隔离）
- confinement（禁闭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quarantine period / under quarantine / quarantine measures / quarantine restrictions**

👉 Example: The passengers were placed under quarantine after a contagious illness was detected aboard the ship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Health officials decided to **quarantine** the entire building after several residents tested positive for the infectious disease.
   卫生官员在多名居民检测出传染病阳性后决定对整栋大楼进行隔离。

2. The mandatory **quarantine** period required all arriving travelers to remain in designated facilities for fourteen days.
   强制隔离期要求所有入境旅客在指定设施中停留十四天。

3. Agricultural authorities **quarantined** the imported livestock to ensure that no foreign pathogens entered the domestic population.
   农业当局对进口牲畜进行了检疫，以确保没有外来病原体进入本国种群。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quarantine" as coming from "quaranta" (Italian for forty) — historically, ships were isolated for forty days to prevent plague.
把"quarantine"想成来自"quaranta"（意大利语的四十）——历史上船只被隔离四十天以防止瘟疫传播。
