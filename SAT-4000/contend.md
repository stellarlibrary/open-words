# ⚔️ contend

🔊 /kənˈtɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To struggle or compete against difficulties or opponents; to assert or maintain a position in an argument
**中文：** v. 竞争；奋斗；争论；主张

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compete（竞争）
- assert（主张）
- argue（争论）
- struggle（奋斗）
- maintain（坚持认为）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contend with / contend for / contend that / have to contend with**

👉 Example: The researchers contend that their findings challenge long-held assumptions about human evolution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Several qualified candidates will **contend** for the prestigious scholarship that funds three years of doctoral research abroad.
   几位合格的候选人将角逐这项资助三年海外博士研究的著名奖学金。

2. The defense attorney **contended** that the evidence presented by the prosecution was entirely circumstantial and legally insufficient.
   辩护律师主张控方提出的证据完全是间接的，在法律上不充分。

3. Small businesses must **contend** with rising operational costs while simultaneously adapting to rapidly evolving market conditions.
   小型企业必须应对不断上升的运营成本，同时适应快速变化的市场条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-TEND" — when you "tend" to something "con" (together with) others, you are competing and struggling alongside them.
把"contend"想成"con（一起）+ tend（趋向）"——大家一起朝同一个目标趋向，就形成了竞争和争论。
