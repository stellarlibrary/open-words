# 😨 cower

🔊 /ˈkaʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To crouch down or shrink back in fear; to draw away trembling, as from something threatening or painful
**中文：** v. 退缩；畏缩；蜷缩

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cringe（畏缩）
- flinch（退缩）
- shrink（缩回）
- recoil（退缩）
- quail（胆怯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cower in fear / cower before someone / cower in the corner / cower behind something**

👉 Example: The puppy cowered in the corner whenever thunder rumbled across the darkened sky.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The villagers **cowered** in their homes as the invading army marched through the streets with terrifying precision.
   村民们在侵略军以可怕的精准度行进在街道上时，蜷缩在家中瑟瑟发抖。

2. Rather than **cower** before the tyrant's threats, the ambassador stood firm and delivered her response with dignity.
   大使没有在暴君的威胁面前退缩，而是坚定地站着，有尊严地作出了回应。

3. The accused **cowered** visibly when the prosecutor presented the damning evidence that dismantled his entire defense.
   当检察官出示了摧毁他整个辩护的有力证据时，被告明显地畏缩了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cow-er" as someone acting like a scared cow — hunching down and trembling with fear.
把"cower"想成"cow-er"——像一头受惊的牛一样弓着身子、因恐惧而颤抖。
