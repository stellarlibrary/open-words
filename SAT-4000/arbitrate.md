# 🤝 arbitrate

🔊 /ˈɑːrbɪtreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To act as a neutral judge in a dispute; to settle a disagreement between two or more parties through formal mediation
**中文：** v. 仲裁；公断

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mediate（调解）
- adjudicate（裁决）
- negotiate（协商）
- settle（解决）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arbitrate a dispute / arbitrate between parties / arbitrate a conflict / agree to arbitrate**

👉 Example: A retired judge was called upon to arbitrate the labor dispute between the union and management.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international tribunal was established to **arbitrate** border disputes between the two neighboring nations diplomatically and fairly.
   该国际法庭的设立旨在以外交方式公正地仲裁两个邻国之间的边界争端。

2. Both companies agreed to **arbitrate** rather than pursue costly litigation that could damage their reputations in the industry.
   两家公司同意通过仲裁而非诉讼来解决问题，以避免可能损害其行业声誉的高昂费用。

3. She was frequently asked to **arbitrate** disagreements among colleagues because of her reputation for impartiality and sound judgment.
   由于她以公正和判断力著称，同事们经常请她仲裁分歧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arbitrate" as what an "arbiter" does — the verb form means to act as a judge and settle disputes between conflicting parties.
把"arbitrate"想成"arbiter"（仲裁者）的动作——这个动词的意思就是充当裁判，解决对立双方之间的争端。
