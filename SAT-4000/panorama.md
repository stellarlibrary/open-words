# 🏔️ panorama

🔊 /ˌpænəˈræmə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An unbroken view of the whole region surrounding an observer; a complete survey or presentation of a subject or sequence of events
**中文：** n. 全景；全貌；全景画；概论；综述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vista（远景）
- overview（概览）
- landscape（风景）
- spectacle（壮观景象）
- survey（综述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **panorama of / sweeping panorama / stunning panorama / panorama view / panorama of history**

👉 Example: From the summit, the hikers enjoyed a breathtaking panorama of snow-capped mountains stretching to the horizon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The observation deck offered visitors a stunning **panorama** of the sprawling metropolitan skyline and the shimmering river below.
   观景台为游客提供了城市广阔天际线和下方波光粼粼的河流的壮丽全景。

2. The historian's comprehensive book presents a vivid **panorama** of social and political transformations throughout the twentieth century.
   这位历史学家的综合著作呈现了二十世纪社会和政治变革的生动全貌。

3. The museum exhibition provided a remarkable **panorama** of artistic movements that shaped Western culture from the Renaissance onward.
   博物馆展览展示了从文艺复兴至今塑造西方文化的艺术运动的精彩全貌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pan-orama" — "pan" means all and "orama" means view; a panorama is a view of ALL your surroundings at once.
想想"pan-orama"——"pan"意为"全部"，"orama"意为"景色"；panorama就是一次看到所有周围景色的全景。
