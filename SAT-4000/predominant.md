# 👑 predominant

🔊 /prɪˈdɒmɪnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the greatest influence, importance, or authority; most common or conspicuous; prevailing
**中文：** adj. 主要的；卓越的；支配的；有力的；占优势的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dominant（占支配地位的）
- prevailing（盛行的）
- principal（主要的）
- paramount（首要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **predominant role / predominant feature / predominant view / predominant influence**

👉 Example: Agriculture remains the predominant industry in many developing regions around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **predominant** theory among scholars suggests that climate change was a key factor in the civilization's decline.
   学者们的主流理论认为，气候变化是该文明衰落的关键因素。

2. English has become the **predominant** language of international scientific research and academic publishing.
   英语已成为国际科学研究和学术出版的主要语言。

3. The architect's **predominant** concern was ensuring that the structure could withstand seismic activity in the region.
   这位建筑师最主要的关注点是确保该建筑能够承受该地区的地震活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-dominant" as "the one that dominates before all others" — the chief force that stands above the rest.
把"predominant"想成"pre（在……之前）+ dominant（支配的）"——在所有其他事物之前占据支配地位的力量。
