# 🌈 variegated

🔊 /ˈvɛəriəˌɡeɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Exhibiting different colors, especially in irregular patches or streaks; having great variety or diversity
**中文：** adj. 杂色的；斑驳的；多样化的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- multicolored（多彩的）
- mottled（斑驳的）
- diverse（多样的）
- dappled（斑点的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **variegated leaves / variegated plumage / variegated landscape / variegated experience**

👉 Example: The garden featured variegated hostas with striking patterns of green and white on each leaf.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanist cultivated **variegated** plants whose leaves displayed intricate patterns of cream, green, and gold throughout the growing season.
   这位植物学家培育了杂色植物，其叶片在整个生长季节呈现出奶油色、绿色和金色的精致图案。

2. The author's **variegated** career spanned journalism, diplomacy, and academia, giving her a remarkably broad intellectual perspective.
   这位作者多样化的职业生涯涵盖了新闻、外交和学术领域，赋予了她非常宽广的学术视野。

3. Sunlight filtered through the canopy and cast a **variegated** mosaic of light and shadow across the forest floor below.
   阳光透过树冠，在下方的森林地面上投射出一幅斑驳的光影图案。

把"variegated"想成"varied + gated"——一个围起来的花园，里面每块地都种着各种各样色彩缤纷的植物。
