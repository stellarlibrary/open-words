# 🔧 braze

🔊 /breɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To solder or join with brass or a similar alloy; to make or coat with brass. (n.) The process of brazing or a brazed joint
**中文：** v. 用黄铜镀或制造；铜焊 n. 铜焊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- solder（焊接）
- weld（熔接）
- fuse（熔合）
- bond（粘合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **braze a joint / braze together / braze with copper alloy / brazed connection**

👉 Example: The technician carefully brazed the copper pipes to ensure a leak-proof connection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The skilled metalworker used a torch to carefully **braze** the two steel components together with a silver-copper alloy.
   这位熟练的金属工人用焊枪小心地将两个钢部件用银铜合金铜焊在一起。

2. Engineers recommended that workers **braze** rather than weld the delicate instrument fittings to prevent structural damage from heat.
   工程师建议工人对精密仪器配件进行铜焊而非熔焊，以防止热量造成结构损坏。

3. The artisan learned to **braze** ornamental fixtures in the traditional manner, preserving techniques passed down through generations.
   这位工匠学会了用传统方式铜焊装饰性五金件，保留了代代相传的技艺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "braze" as "brass + blaze" — you use a blaze of fire to join metals with brass, which is brazing.
把"braze"想成"brass + blaze"——用火焰（blaze）把金属用黄铜（brass）连接起来，就是铜焊。
