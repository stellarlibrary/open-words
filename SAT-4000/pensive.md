# 💭 pensive

🔊 /ˈpensɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Engaged in deep or serious thought, often with a tinge of sadness; quietly reflective or melancholy
**中文：** adj. 沉思的；忧郁的；悲伤的，哀愁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contemplative（沉思的）
- reflective（深思的）
- meditative（冥想的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- wistful（惆怅的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pensive mood / pensive expression / pensive silence / look pensive / pensive gaze**

👉 Example: She sat by the window with a pensive expression, lost in memories of years gone by.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist adopted a **pensive** demeanor as she contemplated the deeper meaning behind her latest abstract composition.
   这位艺术家在思考她最新抽象作品背后更深层含义时，表现出一种沉思的神态。

2. After receiving the unexpected news, he grew unusually **pensive** and withdrew into a prolonged period of quiet reflection.
   在收到这个意外消息后，他变得异常沉思，进入了一段长时间的安静反思。

3. The **pensive** tone of the author's final chapter suggested an underlying sadness about the inevitable passage of time.
   作者最后一章沉思的基调暗示了对时间不可阻挡流逝的深层悲伤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pens-ive" — a person holding a "pen" in a thoughtful, reflective state, pondering what to write next.
把"pensive"想成"pens（笔）+ ive"——手握笔沉思着该写什么的人，就是沉思的状态。
