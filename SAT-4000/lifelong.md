# 🌟 lifelong

🔊 /ˈlaɪflɒŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting or remaining throughout the entire course of a person's life
**中文：** adj. 终身的，毕生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lasting（持久的）
- enduring（持续的）
- permanent（永久的）
- perpetual（永恒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lifelong learning / lifelong friend / lifelong commitment / lifelong dream**

👉 Example: She pursued her lifelong dream of becoming a concert pianist with unwavering dedication.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor emphasized the importance of **lifelong** learning as a means of adapting to the rapidly changing demands of modern society.
   教授强调了终身学习作为适应现代社会快速变化需求的手段的重要性。

2. Their **lifelong** friendship began in elementary school and endured through decades of geographical separation and personal challenges.
   他们终生的友谊始于小学，历经数十年的地理分隔和个人挑战依然坚固。

3. The scientist dedicated her **lifelong** career to developing sustainable agricultural practices that could alleviate global food insecurity.
   这位科学家毕生致力于开发可以缓解全球粮食不安全问题的可持续农业实践。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lifelong" as "life" + "long" — something that lasts as long as your entire life, from beginning to end.
把"lifelong"想成"life"（生命）+"long"（长久的）——某事持续你整个生命那么长，从始至终。
