# 🌿 slender

🔊 /ˈslɛndər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Gracefully thin; slim in build; small or inadequate in amount or extent
**中文：** adj. 修长的，苗条的；微小的，微薄的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- slim（苗条的）
- thin（纤细的）
- slight（微小的）
- meager（微薄的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **slender figure / slender margin / slender chance / slender resources**

👉 Example: The candidate won the election by a slender margin of only a few hundred votes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dancer's **slender** frame moved with extraordinary grace across the illuminated stage during the performance.
   这位舞者修长的身姿在演出中以非凡的优雅在灯光舞台上移动。

2. The researchers operated on a **slender** budget that barely covered essential laboratory supplies and equipment.
   研究人员依靠微薄的预算运作，仅够支付基本的实验室用品和设备。

3. Only a **slender** hope remained that the diplomatic negotiations would yield a lasting peace agreement.
   只剩下微小的希望，期待外交谈判能达成持久的和平协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slender" as "s-lend-er" — someone so thin they could slip through a narrow lender's window to borrow money.
把"slender"想成"s-lend-er"——一个人纤细到可以从窄窄的借贷窗口滑过去借钱。
