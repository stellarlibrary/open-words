# 🍃 naturalistic

🔊 /ˌnætʃərəˈlɪstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Imitating or based on nature; relating to naturalism in art, literature, or philosophy
**中文：** adj. 自然的；自然主义的；博物学的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- realistic（写实的）
- lifelike（逼真的）
- representational（再现的）
- true-to-life（栩栩如生的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **naturalistic style / naturalistic approach / naturalistic observation / naturalistic setting**

👉 Example: The psychologist employed a naturalistic observation method to study children at play.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The painter adopted a **naturalistic** style that captured every subtle detail of the rural landscape with remarkable accuracy.
   这位画家采用了自然主义风格，以惊人的准确度捕捉了乡村景观的每一个细微之处。

2. Researchers conducted a **naturalistic** study by observing animal behavior in the wild rather than in controlled laboratory conditions.
   研究人员通过在野外而非受控实验室条件下观察动物行为，开展了一项自然观察研究。

3. The novel's **naturalistic** portrayal of urban poverty shocked readers who were unaccustomed to such unflinching literary realism.
   这部小说对城市贫困的自然主义描写震惊了不习惯如此直白的文学写实主义的读者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "natural" + "-istic" — having the quality of being natural, depicting things as they truly appear in nature.
把"naturalistic"拆成"natural"加"-istic"——具有自然的特质，描绘事物在自然中的真实面貌。
