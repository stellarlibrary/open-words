# ✨ prepossessing

🔊 /ˌpriːpəˈzɛsɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Attractive or appealing in appearance; creating a favorable impression at first sight
**中文：** adj. 给人好感的；有魅力的；引人注意的；讨人喜欢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attractive（有魅力的）
- appealing（吸引人的）
- charming（迷人的）
- engaging（讨人喜欢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a prepossessing appearance / prepossessing manner / prepossessing smile / not the most prepossessing**

👉 Example: The candidate made a prepossessing impression during the interview with her confident demeanor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new professor had a **prepossessing** manner that immediately put the anxious graduate students at ease during orientation.
   这位新教授有一种令人好感的风度，让紧张的研究生们在迎新时立刻放松了下来。

2. Although the building's exterior was hardly **prepossessing**, the interior revealed an elegant and meticulously designed workspace.
   尽管这栋建筑的外观并不起眼，但内部展现出优雅且精心设计的工作空间。

3. Her **prepossessing** smile and articulate speech convinced the committee to approve the ambitious research proposal unanimously.
   她讨人喜欢的微笑和清晰的表达说服了委员会一致通过这项雄心勃勃的研究提案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-possess-ing" as "possessing charm before you even speak" — someone who wins you over at first glance.
把"prepossessing"想成"pre-possess-ing"——还没开口就"占有"了你的好感，一眼就让人喜欢的人。
