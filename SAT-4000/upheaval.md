# 🌋 upheaval

🔊 /ʌpˈhiːvəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A violent or sudden change or disruption; a great disturbance or upward geological displacement
**中文：** n. 剧变；动乱；隆起；大变动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- turmoil（动荡）
- disruption（扰乱）
- revolution（变革）
- convulsion（剧变）
- cataclysm（大灾变）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political upheaval / social upheaval / economic upheaval / period of upheaval**

👉 Example: The country experienced tremendous political upheaval following the sudden resignation of its president.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The industrial revolution caused widespread social **upheaval** as millions of workers migrated from rural communities to urban centers.
   工业革命引发了广泛的社会剧变，数百万工人从农村社区迁移到城市中心。

2. Geologists attributed the dramatic landscape formations to ancient tectonic **upheaval** that occurred millions of years in the past.
   地质学家将这些壮观的地貌归因于数百万年前发生的古老地壳隆起运动。

3. The sudden economic **upheaval** forced policymakers to implement unprecedented fiscal measures to stabilize the national currency.
   突如其来的经济剧变迫使决策者实施前所未有的财政措施来稳定本国货币。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "up-heave-al" — things being heaved upward violently, like the ground rising during an earthquake, causing total chaos.
把"upheaval"想成"向上举起"——事物被猛烈地向上推起，就像地震中地面隆起，造成一片混乱。
