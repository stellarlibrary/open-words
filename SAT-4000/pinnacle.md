# 🏔️ pinnacle

🔊 /ˈpɪnəkl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The highest point of development or achievement; a small pointed turret or spire on a building; a peak or pointed summit
**中文：** n. 高峰；极点；小尖塔；尖峰；山顶；顶点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apex（顶点）
- zenith（顶峰）
- summit（山顶）
- acme（极点）
- peak（高峰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pinnacle of success / pinnacle of one's career / reach the pinnacle / at the pinnacle**

👉 Example: After decades of dedication, the scientist finally reached the pinnacle of her career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The athlete considered winning the Olympic gold medal to be the **pinnacle** of her distinguished sporting career.
   这位运动员认为赢得奥运金牌是她杰出体育生涯的顶峰。

2. The ancient cathedral's **pinnacle** rose majestically above the surrounding rooftops, visible from miles across the countryside.
   这座古老大教堂的尖塔巍然耸立于周围屋顶之上，数英里外的乡间都能望见。

3. Many historians regard the Renaissance as the **pinnacle** of artistic and intellectual achievement in Western civilization.
   许多历史学家将文艺复兴视为西方文明中艺术和知识成就的巅峰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pin-nacle" as a "pin on a nacelle" — a sharp pin pointing upward to the very top, representing the highest point.
把"pinnacle"想成"pin（针）+ nacle"——一根朝上的针指向最高点，代表顶峰和极点。
