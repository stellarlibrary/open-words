# 😰 predicament

🔊 /prɪˈdɪkəmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A difficult, unpleasant, or embarrassing situation from which there is no easy escape
**中文：** n. 窘况；困境；尴尬的处境

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dilemma（困境）
- quandary（困惑）
- plight（困境）
- bind（窘境）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in a predicament / a difficult predicament / face a predicament / escape a predicament**

👉 Example: The company found itself in a serious predicament after the scandal became public knowledge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation faced a dire **predicament** as rising inflation and unemployment simultaneously threatened economic stability.
   这个国家面临着严峻的困境，因为通货膨胀和失业率同时上升，威胁着经济稳定。

2. Without adequate funding or institutional support, the researchers found themselves in an unprecedented **predicament** regarding their project.
   由于缺乏充足的资金和机构支持，研究人员发现自己在项目上陷入了前所未有的困境。

3. The diplomat's **predicament** deepened when both allied nations demanded conflicting commitments on the trade agreement.
   当两个盟国在贸易协定上提出相互矛盾的要求时，这位外交官的困境进一步加深了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "predicament" as "predict-a-mess" — if you could predict the mess, you might have avoided the predicament altogether.
把"predicament"想成"predict（预测）+ a mess（一团糟）"——如果你能预测到这团糟，也许就能完全避免这个困境。
