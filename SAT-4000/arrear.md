# 💰 arrear

🔊 /əˈrɪr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Money that is owed and should have been paid earlier; an unpaid or overdue debt
**中文：** n. 欠款，逾期债款

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- debt（债务）
- liability（负债）
- delinquency（拖欠）
- outstanding payment（未付款项）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in arrears / fall into arrears / pay off arrears / rent arrears**

👉 Example: The company fell into arrears on its loan payments during the economic downturn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tenant accumulated several months of rent **arrears** and now faces the possibility of formal eviction proceedings.
   该租户累积了数月的租金欠款，现在面临被正式驱逐的可能性。

2. The government introduced a new program to help struggling homeowners settle their mortgage **arrears** systematically.
   政府推出了一项新计划，帮助陷入困境的房主系统性地清偿抵押贷款欠款。

3. Employees were advised that salary **arrears** resulting from the payroll error would be corrected in the next cycle.
   员工被告知因工资系统错误导致的薪资欠款将在下一个周期内予以纠正。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arrear" as "a rear" — the payment is lagging behind, stuck at the rear, meaning it is overdue.
把"arrear"想成"a rear（后面）"——付款落在了后面，意味着已经逾期了。
