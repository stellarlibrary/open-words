# 🤝 empathetic

🔊 /ˌempəˈθetɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another; characterized by empathy
**中文：** adj. 移情作用的；有同感的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compassionate（有同情心的）
- sympathetic（同情的）
- understanding（理解的）
- sensitive（敏感的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **empathetic listener / empathetic response / empathetic toward / deeply empathetic**

👉 Example: An empathetic leader understands the concerns of team members and responds with genuine care and consideration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Effective therapists must be genuinely **empathetic** to establish the trust necessary for productive and meaningful counseling sessions.
   有效的治疗师必须真正具有同理心，才能建立起有成效且有意义的咨询所需的信任。

2. The teacher's **empathetic** approach to struggling students significantly improved classroom engagement and overall academic performance throughout the year.
   这位老师对学习困难学生采取的同理心方法显著提高了全年的课堂参与度和整体学业表现。

3. Research indicates that individuals who are naturally **empathetic** tend to form stronger social bonds and maintain healthier long-term relationships.
   研究表明，天生具有同理心的人往往能形成更强的社会纽带并维持更健康的长期关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "empathetic" as "em-PATH-etic" — you walk along someone else's "path" to feel what they feel.
把"empathetic"想成"em-PATH（路径）-etic"——你沿着别人的路走，去感受他们的感受。
