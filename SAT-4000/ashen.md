# 😰 ashen

🔊 /ˈæʃən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very pale, typically as a result of shock, fear, or illness; resembling ashes in color
**中文：** 灰色的；苍白的（常因惊恐、疾病等）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pallid（苍白的）
- wan（苍白的）
- pale（苍白的）
- ashy（灰白的）
- blanched（发白的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ashen face / ashen complexion / turn ashen / look ashen**

👉 Example: Her face turned ashen when she heard the devastating news about the accident.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness turned **ashen** upon recounting the terrifying details of the crime she had observed unfold.
   证人在讲述她所目睹的犯罪恐怖细节时面色变得苍白。

2. After the long illness, his **ashen** complexion revealed the severity of his condition to all visiting relatives.
   长期患病后，他苍白的面色向所有来访的亲属揭示了他病情的严重性。

3. The survivors emerged from the wreckage with **ashen** faces, visibly shaken by the magnitude of the disaster.
   幸存者从废墟中走出，面色灰白，明显被灾难的规模所震撼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ashen" as "ash-en" — the color of ash is pale gray, just like a face drained of blood from fear or shock.
把"ashen"想成"ash-en"——灰烬的颜色是灰白色，就像因恐惧或震惊而失去血色的脸。
