# 📚 assiduity

🔊 /ˌæsɪˈdjuːɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Constant and careful attention to what one is doing; persistent diligence and industriousness
**中文：** 勤勉；刻苦；殷勤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diligence（勤勉）
- industriousness（勤奋）
- perseverance（坚持不懈）
- devotion（专注）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with great assiduity / demonstrate assiduity / assiduity in one's work**

👉 Example: Her assiduity in mastering the subject was evident from the meticulous notes she kept.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher's remarkable **assiduity** in collecting data over two decades led to a groundbreaking scientific discovery.
   这位研究人员二十年来收集数据的非凡勤勉促成了一项突破性的科学发现。

2. His **assiduity** in practicing the violin every morning eventually earned him a seat in the national orchestra.
   他每天早晨勤勉练习小提琴，最终为他赢得了国家管弦乐团的席位。

3. The committee praised the intern's **assiduity** and recommended her for a permanent position within the organization.
   委员会赞扬了这位实习生的勤奋，并推荐她在该机构获得一个正式职位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assiduity" as "a-SIT-do-it-y" — someone who sits down and does it persistently, with tireless dedication.
把"assiduity"想成"坐下来做到底"——一个坐下来坚持不懈、孜孜不倦地完成任务的人。
