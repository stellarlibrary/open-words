# 🍞 carbohydrate

🔊 /ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An organic compound consisting of carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen, found in foods such as sugars, starches, and cellulose; a major source of energy in the diet
**中文：** n. 碳水化合物；糖类

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sugar（糖）
- starch（淀粉）
- saccharide（糖类）
- glucose（葡萄糖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complex carbohydrate / simple carbohydrate / carbohydrate intake / rich in carbohydrates / low-carbohydrate diet**

👉 Example: Nutritionists recommend balancing carbohydrate intake with adequate protein and healthy fats.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Athletes often increase their **carbohydrate** consumption before competitions to maximize glycogen stores and sustain peak performance.
   运动员通常在比赛前增加碳水化合物的摄入，以最大化糖原储备并维持巅峰表现。

2. The research demonstrated that complex **carbohydrates** provide more sustained energy than their simple refined counterparts.
   研究表明，复杂碳水化合物比简单精制的碳水化合物提供更持久的能量。

3. Excessive restriction of **carbohydrates** in the diet may lead to fatigue, cognitive impairment, and nutritional deficiencies.
   饮食中过度限制碳水化合物可能导致疲劳、认知障碍和营养不良。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carbohydrate" as "carbon + hydrate" — it literally means carbon combined with water (hydrogen and oxygen), which describes its chemical structure.
把"carbohydrate"想成"carbon（碳）+ hydrate（水合物）"——它字面意思就是碳与水（氢和氧）的结合，描述了其化学结构。
