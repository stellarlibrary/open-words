# ✅ assent

🔊 /əˈsent/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To agree to or approve of something, especially after careful consideration. (n.) The expression of agreement or approval.
**中文：** v. 同意；赞成 n. 同意

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agree（同意）
- consent（允许）
- approve（批准）
- concur（赞同）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assent** in formal contexts to express agreement. Do not confuse it with "ascent" (climbing upward). Common collocations include "give one's assent" and "assent to a proposal."

👉 Example: The board members gave their assent to the merger after lengthy deliberations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The monarch must give royal **assent** before any legislation passed by Parliament can officially become law.
   任何经议会通过的立法在正式成为法律之前，必须获得君主的御准。

2. After reviewing the detailed proposal, the director chose to **assent** to the new partnership agreement without reservation.
   在审阅了详细提案后，主任毫无保留地同意了新的合作协议。

3. The committee members nodded in **assent** when the chairman proposed extending the project deadline by two weeks.
   当主席提议将项目截止日期延长两周时，委员会成员们点头表示同意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Assent" contains "sent" — imagine sending a message that says "Yes, I agree!" When you assent, you send your approval.
"assent"包含"sent（发送）"——想象发送一条写着"是的，我同意！"的信息。当你assent时，你就是在发送你的同意。
