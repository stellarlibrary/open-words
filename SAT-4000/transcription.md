# 🖊️ transcription

🔊 /trænˈskrɪpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The action or process of transcribing something; a written or printed representation of something
**中文：** 抄写；转录；转写本

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recording（记录）
- copying（抄写）
- transliteration（音译）
- notation（记谱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **phonetic transcription / transcription of data / transcription service / transcription error**

👉 Example: The phonetic transcription of each word is provided in the dictionary entry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **transcription** of the president's address contained several errors that were later corrected by the official communications office.
   总统讲话的文字记录中有几处错误，后来由官方通讯办公室进行了更正。

2. In molecular biology, **transcription** refers to the process by which genetic information is copied from DNA to messenger RNA.
   在分子生物学中，转录是指将遗传信息从DNA复制到信使RNA的过程。

3. The linguist provided a detailed phonetic **transcription** of the endangered dialect before its last native speakers passed away.
   语言学家在这种濒危方言的最后几位母语使用者去世前提供了详细的语音转写。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transcription" as "the action of transcribing" — just add "-tion" to "transcribe" to get the noun form describing the process.
把"transcription"想成"transcribe（转录）+ tion（动作）"——在动词后加"-tion"就得到了描述这个过程的名词形式。
