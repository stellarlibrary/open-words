# 🎭 travesty

🔊 /ˈtrævəsti/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A grotesque or debased imitation or likeness; a mockery. (v.) To make a travesty of; to parody or distort
**中文：** n. 歪曲；滑稽作品；拙劣的模仿 v. 歪曲；滑稽地模仿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mockery（嘲弄）
- parody（拙劣的模仿）
- caricature（讽刺画）
- distortion（歪曲）
- farce（闹剧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a travesty of justice / a travesty of the truth / make a travesty of**

👉 Example: The biased trial was widely condemned as a travesty of justice by legal scholars.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argued that the hastily conducted investigation was a **travesty** of due process and constitutional rights.
   批评者认为这场仓促进行的调查是对正当程序和宪法权利的歪曲。

2. The unauthorized adaptation **travestied** the original masterpiece, reducing its profound themes to shallow entertainment.
   这部未经授权的改编歪曲了原作，将其深刻的主题沦为肤浅的娱乐。

3. Many observers regarded the election as a complete **travesty**, citing widespread irregularities and suppression of dissent.
   许多观察人士认为这次选举完全是一场闹剧，并指出了普遍的违规行为和对异见的压制。
