# 🔢 arithmetic

🔊 /əˈrɪθmətɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of mathematics dealing with basic operations of numbers, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
**中文：** 算术；算法；四则运算

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- computation（计算）
- calculation（运算）
- mathematics（数学）
- reckoning（估算）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **basic arithmetic / mental arithmetic / arithmetic skills / arithmetic operations**

👉 Example: Mastery of basic arithmetic is essential before students advance to algebra and higher mathematics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accountant discovered a critical error in the **arithmetic** that had caused a significant discrepancy in the quarterly financial report.
   会计发现了**算术**中的一个关键错误，该错误导致了季度财务报告中的重大差异。

2. Elementary school curricula emphasize **arithmetic** proficiency as the foundational skill upon which all advanced mathematical reasoning is built.
   小学课程强调**算术**能力是所有高级数学推理赖以建立的基础技能。

3. The politician's economic proposal failed basic **arithmetic** scrutiny when independent analysts examined the projected budget figures closely.
   当独立分析师仔细审查预算数据后，这位政治家的经济提案经不起基本的**算术**审查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arithmetic" from Greek "arithmos" (number) — it is literally the science of numbers and basic counting operations.
"arithmetic"源自希腊语"arithmos"（数字）——它本质上就是关于数字和基本计算运算的学科。
