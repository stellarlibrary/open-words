# 🙏 plea

🔊 /pliː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An earnest request or appeal; a formal statement in a court of law; an excuse or pretext
**中文：** n. 恳求，请求；辩解，辩护；借口，托辞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appeal（呼吁）
- entreaty（恳求）
- petition（请愿）
- supplication（祈求）
- defense（辩护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a plea / plea for help / plea of guilty / plea bargain / emotional plea**

👉 Example: The defendant entered a plea of not guilty and requested a trial by jury.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador made an impassioned **plea** for international cooperation to address the escalating humanitarian crisis abroad.
   大使为应对不断升级的海外人道主义危机发出了恳切的国际合作请求。

2. Despite her repeated **plea** for leniency, the judge imposed the maximum sentence permitted under the existing legislation.
   尽管她一再恳求从宽处理，法官仍然依据现行法律判处了最高刑罚。

3. The organization issued a public **plea** urging citizens to donate essential supplies for communities devastated by the flood.
   该组织发出公开呼吁，敦促市民为遭受洪灾的社区捐赠必要的物资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plea" as "please" without the "se" — when you say "please," you are making a plea, begging someone to help you.
把"plea"想成"please（请）"去掉"se"——当你说"please"时，你就是在发出恳求，请求某人帮助你。
