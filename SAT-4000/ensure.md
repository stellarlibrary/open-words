# 🔒 ensure

🔊 /ɪnˈʃʊr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make certain that something will happen or be the case; to guarantee or secure
**中文：** v. 保证；确保

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guarantee（保证）
- secure（确保）
- assure（使确信）
- safeguard（保障）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ensure safety / ensure success / ensure compliance / ensure that...**

👉 Example: The new regulations were designed to ensure the safety of workers in hazardous environments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee implemented stringent protocols to **ensure** that all research data remained accurate and confidential.
   委员会实施了严格的规程，以确保所有研究数据保持准确且保密。

2. Proper funding is essential to **ensure** the long-term viability of public education programs across the region.
   充足的资金对于确保该地区公共教育项目的长期可行性至关重要。

3. The engineering team conducted multiple inspections to **ensure** structural integrity before the bridge opened to traffic.
   工程团队在桥梁通车前进行了多次检查，以确保其结构完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-sure" as "make sure" — adding "en-" intensifies the action of being sure about something.
把"ensure"想成"en（使）+ sure（确定的）"——使某事变得确定，即确保。
