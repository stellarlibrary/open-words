# 🔥 rampant

🔊 /ˈræmpənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Flourishing or spreading unchecked; widespread and difficult to control; violent or extravagant in action
**中文：** adj. 猖獗的；蔓延的；狂暴的；奔放的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- widespread（蔓延的）
- unchecked（不受控制的）
- prevalent（盛行的）
- unrestrained（猖獗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rampant corruption / rampant inflation / rampant growth / run rampant**

👉 Example: Misinformation ran rampant on social media platforms during the election season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Rampant** deforestation in tropical regions has accelerated biodiversity loss and contributed significantly to global climate change.
   热带地区猖獗的森林砍伐加速了生物多样性的丧失，并对全球气候变化产生了重大影响。

2. The committee's report revealed that **rampant** corruption within the agency had undermined public trust for over a decade.
   委员会的报告揭示，该机构内部猖獗的腐败已经破坏了公众信任超过十年。

3. Invasive weeds grew **rampant** across the abandoned agricultural fields, choking out the few remaining native species in the area.
   入侵性杂草在废弃的农田中蔓延生长，扼杀了该地区仅存的少数本地物种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rampant" as "ramp + ant" — imagine ants racing up a ramp, spreading everywhere uncontrollably, impossible to stop.
把"rampant"想成"ramp + ant"——想象蚂蚁沿着斜坡奔跑，不可控制地到处蔓延，无法阻止。
