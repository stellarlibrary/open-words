# 🎯 intentional

🔊 /ɪnˈtɛnʃənl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done on purpose; deliberate and planned rather than accidental or spontaneous
**中文：** adj. 有意图的；故意的；蓄意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deliberate（故意的）
- premeditated（预谋的）
- calculated（蓄意的）
- purposeful（有目的的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intentional act / intentional harm / intentional design / intentional about something**

👉 Example: The architect's intentional use of natural light created a warm and inviting atmosphere throughout the building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court determined that the destruction of evidence was **intentional** and not merely the result of administrative negligence.
   法院认定销毁证据是故意行为，而非仅仅是行政疏忽的结果。

2. Her **intentional** decision to study abroad broadened her understanding of different cultures and global economic systems.
   她有意选择出国留学，拓宽了她对不同文化和全球经济体系的理解。

3. The researchers confirmed that the omission of critical data from the published report was **intentional** rather than accidental.
   研究人员证实，已发表报告中遗漏关键数据是故意为之，而非意外。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intention-al" — if you have an "intention" before you act, then your action is intentional, meaning it was done on purpose.
把"intentional"想成"intention(意图) + al"——如果你在行动之前有"意图"，那么你的行为就是intentional，即故意的。
