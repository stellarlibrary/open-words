# 📋 compulsory

🔊 /kəmˈpʌlsəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Required by law or a rule; obligatory; mandatory
**中文：** adj. 强制的；义务的；必修的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mandatory（强制的）
- obligatory（必须的）
- required（必需的）
- enforced（被强制执行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **compulsory education / compulsory attendance / compulsory course / compulsory service**

👉 Example: In many countries, compulsory education requires children to attend school until the age of sixteen.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university introduced a **compulsory** ethics course for all students enrolled in the business administration program.
   大学为所有工商管理专业的学生引入了一门必修的伦理课程。

2. Military service remains **compulsory** for male citizens in several nations, typically beginning at the age of eighteen.
   在一些国家，服兵役对男性公民仍然是义务性的，通常从十八岁开始。

3. The new workplace regulation makes annual safety training **compulsory** for every employee regardless of their department or position.
   新的工作场所法规要求每位员工无论部门或职位都必须参加年度安全培训。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compel" + "sory" — compulsory things compel you to do them; you have no choice.
把"compulsory"想成"compel（强迫）+ sory"——义务的事会强迫你去做，你别无选择。
