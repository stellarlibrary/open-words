# 🔁 persistent

🔊 /pərˈsɪstənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Continuing firmly in a course of action despite difficulty or opposition; lasting for a long time or recurring frequently
**中文：** adj. 固执的，坚持的；持久稳固的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tenacious（顽强的）
- relentless（不懈的）
- steadfast（坚定的）
- enduring（持久的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **persistent effort / persistent problem / persistent rumor / persistent pain**

👉 Example: The persistent drought forced farmers to adopt entirely new methods of irrigation and crop management.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Through **persistent** effort and unwavering dedication, the research team finally achieved a breakthrough in renewable energy storage.
   通过坚持不懈的努力和坚定的奉献，研究团队终于在可再生能源储存方面取得了突破。

2. The **persistent** inequality in educational access remains one of the most pressing challenges facing developing nations today.
   教育机会方面的持续不平等仍然是当今发展中国家面临的最紧迫挑战之一。

3. Despite facing **persistent** criticism from skeptics, the architect refused to compromise on her innovative design philosophy.
   尽管面临怀疑者的持续批评，这位建筑师拒绝在她的创新设计理念上妥协。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-SIST-ent" — someone who keeps "insisting" per (through) every obstacle, stubbornly refusing to stop.
把"persistent"想成"per-SIST-ent"——一个在每一个障碍中都坚持"insisting"的人，固执地拒绝停下来。
