# 🕊️ chaste

🔊 /tʃeɪst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Morally pure; virtuous; abstaining from sexual activity; simple and unadorned in style or design
**中文：** adj. 纯洁的；贞洁的；有道德的；朴素的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pure（纯洁的）
- virtuous（有道德的）
- modest（朴素的）
- austere（简朴的）
- innocent（纯真的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chaste life / chaste design / remain chaste / chaste elegance / chaste and simple**

👉 Example: The architect favored a chaste design that emphasized clean lines and simplicity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The monastery required its members to maintain a **chaste** lifestyle devoted entirely to spiritual contemplation and scholarly pursuits.
   修道院要求其成员保持纯洁的生活方式，完全致力于精神沉思和学术追求。

2. The **chaste** elegance of the neoclassical building stood in stark contrast to the ornate baroque structures surrounding it.
   这座新古典主义建筑朴素的典雅与周围华丽的巴洛克建筑形成了鲜明的对比。

3. Literary critics praised the author's **chaste** prose style for conveying profound emotions through remarkably restrained and simple language.
   文学评论家赞扬了作者朴素的散文风格，用极其克制和简洁的语言传达了深刻情感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chaste" sounding like "chased" — someone so pure that temptation is chased away from them.
把"chaste"的发音想成"chased（被赶走的）"——一个如此纯洁的人，诱惑都被赶走了。
