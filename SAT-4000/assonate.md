# 🎤 assonate

🔊 /ˈæsəneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To correspond in vowel sounds; to form an approximate rhyme by matching vowel sounds while consonants differ
**中文：** 音相谐，成为准押韵

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmonize（使和谐）
- rhyme（押韵）
- resonate（共鸣）
- correspond（相呼应）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **assonate with / words that assonate / assonate in verse / assonate through vowel sounds**

👉 Example: In Spanish poetry, it is common for line endings to assonate rather than fully rhyme.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The words "lake" and "fade" **assonate** because they share the same long vowel sound despite different consonant endings.
   "lake"和"fade"音相谐，因为它们共享相同的长元音，尽管辅音结尾不同。

2. Medieval troubadours frequently allowed their verse endings to **assonate** rather than insisting on exact and perfect rhyme.
   中世纪的游吟诗人经常让他们的诗句结尾音相谐，而不是坚持精确完美的押韵。

3. The poet deliberately chose words that **assonate** to produce a subtle musical quality throughout the entire composition.
   诗人故意选择谐音的词语，以在整首作品中产生一种微妙的音乐感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assonate" as "a-SONG-ate" — words that assonate sing together like a song, matching their vowel melodies.
把"assonate"想成"像歌曲（song）一样"——谐音的词语像歌曲一样一起吟唱，元音旋律相互呼应。
