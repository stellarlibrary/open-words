# 😤 pique

🔊 /piːk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To stimulate or provoke; to wound someone's pride or self-respect. (n.) A feeling of irritation or resentment resulting from wounded pride
**中文：** v. 刺激；激怒；伤害自尊心 n. 生气；愠怒；不满

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritate（激怒）
- provoke（刺激）
- resentment（愠怒）
- annoyance（恼怒）
- stimulate（激发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pique one's interest / pique one's curiosity / in a fit of pique / pique someone's pride**

👉 Example: The mysterious letter piqued her curiosity, compelling her to investigate further.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's provocative lecture on quantum mechanics **piqued** the students' intellectual curiosity and inspired lively classroom debate.
   教授关于量子力学的发人深省的讲座激发了学生们的求知欲，并引发了热烈的课堂辩论。

2. In a fit of **pique**, the diplomat abruptly withdrew from the negotiations after his proposal was publicly dismissed.
   这位外交官在一阵愠怒之下，于其提案被公开驳回后突然退出了谈判。

3. The documentary's striking opening scene was carefully designed to **pique** the audience's interest in the subject matter.
   这部纪录片引人注目的开场精心设计，旨在激发观众对这一主题的兴趣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pique" sounding like "peek" — something that piques your interest makes you want to take a peek and learn more.
把"pique"想成发音类似"peek（偷看）"——激起你兴趣的事情会让你想偷看一眼，了解更多。
