# 🛁 ablution

🔊 /əˈbluːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of washing or cleansing the body, especially as a religious ritual; (pl.) the facilities for washing
**中文：** n. 洗礼；洗澡（常用复数）；斋戒沐浴；清洗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- washing（清洗）
- cleansing（净化）
- purification（净化）
- bathing（沐浴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perform ablutions / ritual ablution / morning ablutions / ablution block**

👉 Example: The monks performed their morning ablutions before gathering in the temple for prayer.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Before entering the sacred temple, worshippers must perform ritual **ablutions** at the fountain outside.
   在进入神圣的寺庙之前，信徒必须在外面的喷泉处进行仪式性的沐浴。

2. The soldiers lined up at the **ablution** block each morning for their daily wash before inspection.
   士兵们每天早晨在盥洗区排队进行日常清洗，然后接受检查。

3. In many Islamic traditions, **ablution** is a mandatory purification rite performed before each daily prayer.
   在许多伊斯兰传统中，沐浴是每次日常祈祷前必须进行的净化仪式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Split it: "ab-" (away) + "lution" (from Latin "luere," to wash) — washing away impurities.
拆分记忆："ab-"（离开）+ "lution"（源自拉丁语"luere"，洗）——洗去污秽。
