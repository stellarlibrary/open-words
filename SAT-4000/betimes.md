# ⏰ betimes

🔊 /bɪˈtaɪmz/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) In good time; early; before the usual or expected time; promptly
**中文：** adv. 及时，准时；早

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- early（早地）
- promptly（及时地）
- punctually（准时地）
- seasonably（适时地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rise betimes / arrive betimes / act betimes**

👉 Example: The prudent merchant arrived betimes at the harbor to secure the finest goods before his competitors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diligent scholar rose **betimes** each morning to complete his research before the university library became crowded.
   这位勤奋的学者每天早早起床，以便在大学图书馆变得拥挤之前完成研究。

2. Had the authorities acted **betimes**, the devastating consequences of the financial crisis might have been substantially mitigated.
   如果当局及时采取行动，金融危机的毁灭性后果可能会大幅减轻。

3. The ambassador counseled the delegation to prepare **betimes**, lest they find themselves unprepared for the critical negotiations.
   大使建议代表团及早准备，以免在关键谈判中措手不及。
