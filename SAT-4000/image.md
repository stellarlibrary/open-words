# 🖼️ image

🔊 /ˈɪmɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A visual representation or likeness of something; a mental picture or impression; a public perception of a person or organization
**中文：** n. 影像；想象；肖像；形象

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- picture（图片）
- likeness（肖像）
- representation（再现）
- impression（印象）
- depiction（描绘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public image / mirror image / mental image / project an image / image of**

👉 Example: The corporation invested heavily in campaigns to improve its public image after the environmental scandal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The satellite captured a remarkably detailed **image** of the hurricane's eye as it approached the southeastern coastline.
   卫星在飓风逼近东南海岸时拍摄到了一张极其详细的飓风眼影像。

2. The candidate worked diligently to craft a trustworthy **image** that would resonate with undecided voters before the election.
   这位候选人在选举前努力塑造一个值得信赖的形象，以引起未决选民的共鸣。

3. The poet's vivid language created a powerful **image** of solitude and longing that lingered in the reader's mind.
   诗人生动的语言创造了一种强烈的孤独和渴望的意象，久久萦绕在读者心中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "image" as coming from Latin "imago" meaning a copy or likeness — an image is what you see reflected or represented.
把"image"想成来自拉丁语"imago"，意为复制品或肖像——image就是你看到的反映或再现。
