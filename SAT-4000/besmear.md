# 🖌️ besmear

🔊 /bɪˈsmɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To smear over; to soil or make dirty; to sully or tarnish
**中文：** v. 弄脏，涂抹；玷污

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- smear（涂抹）
- daub（涂抹）
- soil（弄脏）
- sully（玷污）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **besmear with / besmear something on something**

👉 Example: The vandals besmeared the ancient monument with crude paint, outraging the local community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The protesters **besmeared** the walls of the government building with slogans demanding immediate political reform.
   抗议者在政府大楼的墙壁上涂满了要求立即政治改革的标语。

2. His once sterling reputation was **besmeared** by unfounded allegations that circulated widely through the press.
   他曾经良好的声誉被通过媒体广泛传播的无端指控所玷污。

3. The child **besmeared** the entire canvas with bright colors, creating a vivid but chaotic abstract composition.
   孩子用鲜艳的颜色涂满了整块画布，创造出一幅生动但混乱的抽象作品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-SMEAR" — it literally contains "smear." To besmear is to smear something all over, making it dirty.
想想"be-SMEAR"——它字面上包含"smear"（涂抹）。Besmear就是把东西涂得到处都是，弄脏。
