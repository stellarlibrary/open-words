# 🌍 amity

🔊 /ˈæmɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A friendly and peaceful relationship between people or nations; mutual understanding and goodwill.
**中文：** 友好；亲善关系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- friendship（友谊）
- goodwill（善意）
- harmony（和谐）
- concord（和睦）
- rapport（融洽关系）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **between nations, international amity, spirit of amity, promote amity, treaty of amity**
👉 Example: The treaty of amity strengthened diplomatic bonds between the two countries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The signing of the bilateral accord fostered lasting **amity** between the two historically rival nations.
   双边协议的签署促进了两个历史宿敌国家之间的持久友好关系。

2. The organization dedicated itself to promoting **amity** among diverse cultural and religious communities worldwide.
   该组织致力于促进全球不同文化和宗教社区之间的友好关系。

3. A spirit of **amity** prevailed at the international summit, enabling delegates to reach meaningful compromises.
   国际峰会上弥漫着友好的气氛，使代表们得以达成有意义的妥协。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amity" shares its root with "ami" (French for "friend") — amity is the state of being friends at a grand scale.
"Amity"与法语"ami"（朋友）同根——amity就是大规模的友谊状态。
