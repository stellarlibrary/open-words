# 🦀 appendage

🔊 /əˈpɛndɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A thing that is added or attached to something larger or more important; a limb or other projecting body part of an organism
**中文：** 附加物；附属肢体；附属器官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attachment（附属物）
- extension（延伸部分）
- limb（肢体）
- adjunct（附加物）
- protuberance（突出物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bodily appendage / a mere appendage / an unnecessary appendage / external appendage**

👉 Example: The crab uses its front appendages to grasp food and defend itself from predators.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The insect's delicate **appendages** allow it to navigate complex terrain and detect subtle vibrations in its environment.
   这种昆虫精巧的附肢使它能够在复杂地形中行动并感知环境中的细微振动。

2. Critics dismissed the new department as a mere **appendage** of the existing bureaucracy with no real authority.
   批评者认为这个新部门不过是现有官僚机构的附属品，没有实际权力。

3. The biologist studied how certain marine organisms regenerate lost **appendages** within remarkably short periods of time.
   这位生物学家研究了某些海洋生物如何在极短时间内再生失去的附肢。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "appendage" as "append + age" — something that has been "appended" or attached, like an extra part hanging off a body or structure.
把"appendage"想成"append（附加）+ age"——被"附加上去"的东西，就像挂在身体或结构上的额外部分。
