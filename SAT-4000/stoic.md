# 🗿 stoic

🔊 /ˈstoʊɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who endures pain or hardship without showing emotion. (adj.) Enduring difficulty without complaint; impassive
**中文：** n. 坚忍克己的人；不以苦乐为意的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impassive（无动于衷的）
- unemotional（不动感情的）
- phlegmatic（冷静的）
- forbearing（忍耐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stoic demeanor / stoic acceptance / remain stoic / stoic in the face of adversity**

👉 Example: Despite the devastating loss, she maintained a stoic composure throughout the entire memorial service.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The veteran soldier remained remarkably **stoic** as he recounted the harrowing experiences of combat during the lengthy interview.
   那位老兵在漫长的采访中讲述惊心动魄的战斗经历时始终保持着非凡的坚忍。

2. Her **stoic** acceptance of the terminal diagnosis impressed the medical staff, who admired her extraordinary inner strength.
   她对绝症诊断的坚忍接受给医护人员留下了深刻印象，他们钦佩她非凡的内在力量。

3. The community's **stoic** response to the natural disaster demonstrated remarkable resilience and an unwavering collective determination to rebuild.
   社区对自然灾害的坚忍回应展现了非凡的韧性和坚定不移的集体重建决心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stoic" as "stone-ic" — like a stone, a stoic person shows no emotion and remains firm no matter what happens.
把"stoic"想成"stone-ic"——像石头一样，坚忍的人无论发生什么都不动声色，坚定不移。
