# 🌀 anticyclone

🔊 /ˌæntɪˈsaɪkloʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A weather system characterized by high atmospheric pressure, in which winds spiral outward in a clockwise direction in the Northern Hemisphere, typically bringing calm, clear conditions.

**中文：** 反气旋，高气压（一种以高气压为特征的天气系统，在北半球风以顺时针方向向外旋转，通常带来晴朗平静的天气）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- high-pressure system 高压系统
- high-pressure area 高气压区
- ridge of high pressure 高压脊

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **anticyclone conditions**, **blocking anticyclone**, **an anticyclone settles over (a region)**, **subtropical anticyclone**. Used primarily in meteorological and scientific contexts.

> Example: A persistent **anticyclone** over the region brought weeks of unusually dry weather.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The meteorologist explained that a large **anticyclone** was responsible for the prolonged heatwave across the continent.
   气象学家解释说，一个大型反气旋是整个大陆持续热浪的原因。

2. A blocking **anticyclone** prevented rain-bearing fronts from reaching the drought-stricken agricultural areas for weeks.
   一个阻塞反气旋数周内阻止了携带降雨的锋面到达遭受干旱的农业区。

3. As the **anticyclone** gradually weakened, forecasters predicted the return of cooler temperatures and scattered showers.
   随着反气旋逐渐减弱，预报员预测气温将回落并出现零星阵雨。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anti-" (opposite) + "cyclone" (a low-pressure storm). An **anticyclone** is the opposite of a cyclone — instead of low pressure and storms, it brings high pressure and clear skies.

"anti-"（相反）+"cyclone"（气旋，低气压风暴）。**Anticyclone** 是气旋的反面——不是低气压和暴风雨，而是带来高气压和晴朗的天空。
