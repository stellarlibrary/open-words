# 🕊️ placate

🔊 /ˈpleɪkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make someone less angry or hostile; to soothe, pacify, or appease
**中文：** v. 安抚；抚慰；怀柔；使和解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appease（安抚）
- pacify（平息）
- mollify（缓和）
- conciliate（调解）
- soothe（抚慰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **placate the crowd / placate angry customers / placate critics / attempt to placate**

👉 Example: The manager offered a full refund in an attempt to placate the dissatisfied customer.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government introduced several new social programs in a strategic effort to **placate** the increasingly restless and dissatisfied populace.
   政府推出了几项新的社会计划，以战略性努力安抚日益不安和不满的民众。

2. No amount of diplomatic concession could **placate** the opposition leader, who continued to demand the minister's immediate resignation.
   再多的外交让步也无法安抚这位反对派领袖，他继续要求部长立即辞职。

3. The teacher attempted to **placate** the upset student by calmly explaining the reasoning behind the unexpectedly low grade.
   老师试图通过平静地解释出乎意料的低分背后的理由来安抚这位不安的学生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plac-ate" containing "place" — to placate someone is to put them back in a calm, peaceful place emotionally.
把"placate"想成包含"place（地方）"——安抚某人就是在情感上把他们带回一个平静安宁的地方。
