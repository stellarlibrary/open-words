# 🩸 hemoglobin

🔊 /ˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A red protein in blood cells that carries oxygen from the lungs to tissues throughout the body and returns carbon dioxide
**中文：** n. 血红蛋白

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- Hb（血红蛋白缩写）
- haemoglobin（血红蛋白，英式拼写）
- red pigment（红色色素）
- oxygen carrier（氧载体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hemoglobin level / hemoglobin count / hemoglobin concentration / low hemoglobin**

👉 Example: The doctor ordered a blood test to check whether the patient's hemoglobin levels were within normal range.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Patients with iron deficiency anemia often exhibit dangerously low **hemoglobin** levels that require immediate medical intervention.
   缺铁性贫血患者通常表现出需要立即医疗干预的危险低水平血红蛋白。

2. The research team discovered a genetic mutation that affects **hemoglobin** structure and impairs oxygen transport in the bloodstream.
   研究团队发现了一种影响血红蛋白结构并损害血液中氧气运输的基因突变。

3. High-altitude athletes naturally develop elevated **hemoglobin** concentrations as their bodies adapt to reduced atmospheric oxygen levels.
   高海拔运动员在身体适应大气氧含量降低的过程中，自然会产生较高的血红蛋白浓度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "hemo" means blood (as in hemorrhage) and "globin" means a globe-shaped protein — hemoglobin is a globe-shaped blood protein.
拆分来看："hemo"表示血液（如hemorrhage出血），"globin"表示球形蛋白——血红蛋白就是球形的血液蛋白。
