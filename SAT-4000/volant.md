# 🕊 volant

🔊 /ˈvoʊlənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of flying; moving quickly or nimbly; swift and agile
**中文：** adj. 会飞的；迅速的，敏捷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flying（飞行的）
- swift（迅速的）
- nimble（敏捷的）
- agile（灵活的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **volant creatures / volant species / volant movement**

👉 Example: The volant squirrel glided effortlessly from one towering tree to the next.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Biologists classified the newly discovered species as **volant**, noting its remarkably well-developed wing membranes.
   生物学家将这一新发现的物种归类为会飞的，并注意到其非常发达的翼膜。

2. The **volant** dancer moved across the stage with such grace that she appeared to defy gravity.
   那位敏捷的舞者在舞台上优雅地移动，仿佛在抵抗地心引力。

3. Evolutionary pressures favored **volant** organisms in the archipelago, where flight provided essential access to resources.
   在那个群岛上，进化压力有利于会飞的生物，因为飞行提供了获取资源的重要途径。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vol-" from Latin "volare" (to fly), like "volleyball" flying through the air — volant means capable of flight or swift movement.
把"volant"联想到拉丁语"volare"（飞）——就像排球（volleyball）在空中飞行一样，volant表示会飞的或迅速的。
