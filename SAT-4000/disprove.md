# ❌ disprove

🔊 /ˌdɪsˈpruːv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To prove that something is false or incorrect; to refute
**中文：** v. 反驳；证明……不成立；证明……是虚假的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refute（反驳）
- debunk（揭穿）
- invalidate（使无效）
- contradict（驳斥）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disprove a theory / disprove a claim / disprove an argument / disprove a hypothesis**

👉 Example: A single counterexample is sufficient to disprove a universal claim in formal logic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory results conclusively **disproved** the hypothesis that the compound could serve as an effective catalyst.
   实验室结果明确证明了该化合物可以作为有效催化剂的假设不成立。

2. Subsequent investigations **disproved** the defendant's alibi, revealing significant inconsistencies in the testimony provided.
   后续调查推翻了被告的不在场证明，揭示了所提供证词中的重大不一致之处。

3. Galileo's astronomical observations helped **disprove** the geocentric model that had dominated scientific thought for centuries.
   伽利略的天文观测帮助推翻了统治科学思想数百年的地心说模型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-prove" as "dis" (opposite) + "prove" — to do the opposite of proving something, which means showing it is false.
把"disprove"想成"dis"（相反）+"prove"（证明）——做与证明相反的事，即证明某事是错误的。
