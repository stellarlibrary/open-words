# 🎩 suave

🔊 /swɑːv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Smooth and gentle in manner; charming, polished, and sophisticated
**中文：** adj. 温和的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- urbane（文雅的）
- debonair（温文尔雅的）
- sophisticated（老练的）
- refined（优雅的）
- polished（有教养的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suave manner / suave demeanor / suave gentleman / suave and charming**

👉 Example: His suave demeanor and eloquent speech made a lasting impression on everyone at the diplomatic reception.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **suave** manner and impeccable diplomatic skills enabled him to navigate even the most delicate negotiations successfully.
   这位大使温文尔雅的举止和无可挑剔的外交技巧使他能够成功应对最微妙的谈判。

2. Despite his **suave** exterior, the executive harbored deeply unconventional ideas about corporate governance and organizational reform.
   尽管外表温和文雅，这位高管对公司治理和组织改革持有非常不同寻常的理念。

3. The professor's **suave** articulation of complex theories made even the most challenging lectures accessible and engaging for students.
   教授对复杂理论温和流畅的阐述使最具挑战性的讲座都变得通俗易懂且引人入胜。

---

把"suave"想成发音类似"smooth"——一个温文尔雅的人在社交场合中如丝绸般顺滑自如。
