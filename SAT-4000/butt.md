# 🎯 butt

🔊 /bʌt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The buttocks; a cigarette end; an object of ridicule; a target mound; (v.) to strike or push with the head; to collide
**中文：** n. 屁股；烟头；笑柄；靶垛 v. 以头抵撞；碰撞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- target（目标）
- laughingstock（笑柄）
- ram（撞击）
- bump（碰撞）
- stub（烟蒂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the butt of a joke / butt heads / butt against / butt in**

👉 Example: He was always the butt of his colleagues' jokes, yet he maintained his composure admirably.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new employee quickly became the **butt** of every office joke, though no malice was intended by his peers.
   新员工很快成了办公室里每个玩笑的笑柄，尽管同事们并无恶意。

2. The two rival executives continued to **butt** heads over the proposed merger throughout the contentious board meeting.
   两位竞争对手高管在整个激烈的董事会议上就拟议合并持续争执。

3. The mountain goat lowered its horns and began to **butt** forcefully against the wooden fence surrounding the enclosure.
   山羊低下犄角，开始用力顶撞围栏周围的木栅栏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "butt" as hitting something with the blunt end — a goat butts with its head, and a cigarette butt is the blunt end left over.
想想"butt"就是用钝的一端撞击——山羊用头顶撞（butt），而cigarette butt就是香烟剩下的钝端（烟头）。
