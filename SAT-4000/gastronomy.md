# 🍽️ gastronomy

🔊 /ɡæˈstrɒnəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The art or science of good eating; the practice or study of cooking and serving fine food
**中文：** n. 烹饪法；美食法；美食学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cuisine（烹饪）
- culinary arts（烹饪艺术）
- cookery（烹饪术）
- epicureanism（美食主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **molecular gastronomy / French gastronomy / the art of gastronomy / gastronomy festival**

👉 Example: The university now offers an advanced course in gastronomy that combines chemistry with culinary technique.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city has become a global capital of **gastronomy**, attracting chefs and food enthusiasts from every continent each year.
   这座城市已成为全球美食之都，每年吸引来自各大洲的厨师和美食爱好者。

2. Molecular **gastronomy** applies scientific principles to the preparation of food, transforming traditional dishes into innovative creations.
   分子美食学将科学原理应用于食物制备，将传统菜肴转化为创新之作。

3. The professor argued that **gastronomy** reflects cultural identity and should be studied as seriously as literature or history.
   这位教授认为美食学反映了文化认同，应该像文学或历史一样被认真研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gastro-nomy" — "gastro" means stomach (as in gastric), and "-nomy" means laws or rules, so gastronomy is the "rules of the stomach," the science of fine eating.
把"gastronomy"拆分为"gastro-nomy"——"gastro"意为胃（如gastric胃的），"-nomy"意为法则，所以gastronomy就是"胃的法则"，即美食学。
