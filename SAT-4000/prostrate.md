# 🙇 prostrate

🔊 /ˈprɑːstreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lying face down on the ground, especially in submission or worship; completely overcome or exhausted. (v.) To throw oneself flat on the ground; to reduce to extreme weakness or helplessness
**中文：** adj. 俯卧的；拜倒的；降伏的 v. 使屈服；将……弄倒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prone（俯卧的）
- supine（仰卧的）
- overwhelm（使屈服）
- flatten（击倒）
- subjugate（征服）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prostrate oneself before / prostrate with grief / lie prostrate / prostrate on the ground**

👉 Example: The worshippers prostrated themselves before the altar in a solemn act of devotion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defeated general lay **prostrate** before the conquering emperor, pleading for mercy on behalf of his surviving soldiers.
   战败的将军俯伏在征服者皇帝面前，为他幸存的士兵请求宽恕。

2. The devastating earthquake left the entire coastal region **prostrate**, with critical infrastructure completely destroyed beyond immediate repair.
   毁灭性的地震使整个沿海地区陷入瘫痪，关键基础设施被彻底摧毁，无法立即修复。

3. Grief-stricken mourners **prostrated** themselves at the memorial, overwhelmed by the magnitude of the tragedy that had befallen their community.
   悲痛欲绝的哀悼者们在纪念碑前伏地痛哭，被降临在他们社区的巨大悲剧所压倒。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-STRAIGHT" — you lie STRAIGHT down flat on the ground, completely prostrate in submission or exhaustion.
把"prostrate"想成"pro-STRAIGHT"——你直直地（straight）趴倒在地上，完全俯伏表示臣服或精疲力竭。
