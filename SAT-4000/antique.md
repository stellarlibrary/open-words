# 🏛️ antique

🔊 /ænˈtiːk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Belonging to or made in an earlier period; old and often valuable. (n.) A collectible object such as a piece of furniture or work of art that has a high value because of its considerable age.

**中文：** (adj.) 古老的；年代久远的。(n.) 古董，古玩。因年代久远而具有较高价值的收藏品。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- vintage（古典的）
- ancient（古老的）
- relic（遗物）
- heirloom（传家宝）
- artifact（文物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antique furniture, antique dealer, antique shop, antique collection, genuine antique

**Example:** The family inherited a magnificent **antique** clock that had been crafted in the early eighteenth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **antique** porcelain vase, dating from the Ming Dynasty, was sold at auction for an extraordinary sum.
   这只明朝时期的古董瓷瓶在拍卖会上以惊人的价格成交。

2. Collectors from around the world gathered at the exhibition to admire the **antique** silverware and jewelry.
   来自世界各地的收藏家齐聚展览会，欣赏这些古董银器和珠宝。

3. The restoration specialist carefully preserved the **antique** wooden desk while reinforcing its weakened structural joints.
   修复专家在加固其老化的结构接头的同时，小心翼翼地保护了这张古老的木质书桌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

The word "antique" shares its root with "antiquity" (古代). Picture a beautiful old object sitting in a museum — it is an antique, something ancient and treasured.

联想："antique" 与 "antiquity"（古代）同根。想象一件精美的古老物品陈列在博物馆中——那就是 antique，古老而珍贵的东西。
