# 📻 commentary

🔊 /ˈkɒm.ən.tər.i/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An expression of opinions or explanations about an event or situation; a series of explanatory notes or annotations on a text
**中文：** n. 评论；注释；解说词

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- analysis（分析）
- critique（评论）
- annotation（注释）
- exposition（阐述）
- narration（解说）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **provide commentary / running commentary / social commentary / commentary on the text**

👉 Example: The documentary offered a compelling commentary on the effects of urbanization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar published an extensive **commentary** on the ancient philosophical text that illuminated its most obscure passages.
   这位学者发表了一篇关于古代哲学文本的详尽注释，阐明了其中最晦涩的段落。

2. The journalist's sharp **commentary** on political corruption prompted widespread public debate and calls for institutional reform.
   这位记者对政治腐败的犀利评论引发了广泛的公众辩论和制度改革的呼声。

3. The broadcaster delivered a lively running **commentary** throughout the championship match that captivated millions of listeners.
   解说员在整场冠军赛中进行了生动的实况解说，吸引了数百万听众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comment-ary" — it is built on "comment," and a commentary is an extended, detailed comment or series of comments on a subject.
把"commentary"想成"comment-ary"——它建立在"comment（评论）"之上，commentary就是对某一主题的详细、深入的评论或解说。
