# 🐦 canary

🔊 /kəˈnɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small bright yellow songbird native to the Canary Islands, often kept as a pet; a pale yellow color resembling the bird's plumage.
**中文：** n. 金丝雀；淡黄色

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- songbird（鸣禽）
- finch（雀类）
- yellow（黄色）
- warbler（莺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **canary in a coal mine / canary yellow / pet canary / sing like a canary**

👉 Example: The sudden decline in bee populations served as a canary in the coal mine for ecological collapse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Miners historically used a **canary** in coal mines as an early warning system for detecting toxic gases underground.
   矿工们历史上在煤矿中使用金丝雀作为检测地下有毒气体的预警系统。

2. The economist argued that rising unemployment rates were a **canary** in the coal mine for a looming recession.
   那位经济学家认为，不断上升的失业率是经济衰退迫在眉睫的预警信号。

3. She painted the nursery walls in a soft **canary** hue that brought a warm and cheerful atmosphere to the room.
   她把育婴室的墙壁涂成柔和的淡黄色，为房间带来了温暖而愉快的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "canary" as the bright yellow bird from the "Canary Islands" — picture a small golden bird singing in a cage, and you will remember both the bird and the color.
把"canary"联想为来自"加那利群岛（Canary Islands）"的明黄色小鸟——想象一只金色小鸟在笼中歌唱，你就能记住这个词的鸟和颜色两层含义。
