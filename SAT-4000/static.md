# ⚡ static

🔊 /ˈstætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking in movement, action, or change; relating to or producing stationary electric charges. (n.) Static electricity; crackling or hissing interference
**中文：** adj. 静态的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stationary（静止的）
- unchanging（不变的）
- fixed（固定的）
- immobile（不动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **static electricity / static state / remain static / static analysis**

👉 Example: The population of the rural town has remained largely static over the past two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers observed that the chemical compound remained **static** under controlled laboratory conditions for the entire duration of the experiment.
   研究人员观察到，在整个实验期间，该化合物在受控的实验室条件下保持静态。

2. Critics argued that the nation's education policy had become dangerously **static** and failed to address modern challenges.
   批评者认为，该国的教育政策已变得危险地僵化，未能应对现代挑战。

3. The buildup of **static** on the synthetic fabric caused a mild electrical discharge when she touched the metal railing.
   合成织物上积聚的静电在她触碰金属栏杆时产生了轻微的放电。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "static" as "stay-tic" — something that stays in place and does not move, like a statue standing still.
把"static"想成"stay-tic"——停留不动的东西，就像一座静止不动的雕像。
