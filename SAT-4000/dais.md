# 🎤 dais

🔊 /ˈdeɪɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A low platform or raised floor for a speaker, lecturer, or honored guests
**中文：** n. 台；讲台

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- platform（平台）
- podium（讲台）
- rostrum（演讲台）
- stage（舞台）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on the dais / step onto the dais / raised dais / speaker's dais**

👉 Example: The keynote speaker ascended the dais to deliver her opening remarks at the conference.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor stepped onto the **dais** at the front of the auditorium and addressed the assembled graduates.
   教授走上礼堂前方的讲台，向在场的毕业生们发表讲话。

2. A wooden **dais** had been erected in the town square for the mayor's annual address to the community.
   市镇广场上搭建了一个木制讲台，供市长向社区发表年度讲话。

3. The panel of distinguished scholars sat upon the **dais**, each prepared to present their latest research findings.
   杰出学者组成的小组坐在讲台上，每人都准备好展示他们最新的研究成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dais" as "day-is" — the day is here for you to stand on the raised platform and give your speech.
把"dais"想成"day-is"——这一天到了，你要站上讲台发表演讲。
