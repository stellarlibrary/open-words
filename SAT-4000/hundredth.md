# 💯 hundredth

🔊 /ˈhʌndrədθ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Coming at number one hundred in a series; being one of a hundred equal parts. (n.) One of a hundred equal parts; the ordinal form of one hundred
**中文：** adj. 第一百的 n. 百分之一；第一百个

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- centesimal（百分之一的）
- one percent（百分之一）
- 100th（第一百）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the hundredth anniversary / one hundredth of / the hundredth time / hundredth of a second**

👉 Example: The university celebrated its hundredth anniversary with a grand ceremony attended by thousands of alumni.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The institution commemorated its **hundredth** anniversary by establishing a generous scholarship fund for underprivileged students nationwide.
   该机构通过设立面向全国贫困学生的慷慨奖学金基金来纪念其一百周年。

2. The Olympic sprinter won the gold medal by merely a **hundredth** of a second, stunning spectators around the world.
   这位奥运短跑选手仅以百分之一秒的优势赢得金牌，震惊了全世界的观众。

3. For the **hundredth** time, the committee reviewed the proposal and finally reached a unanimous decision to approve the project.
   委员会第一百次审查了该提案，最终达成一致决定批准该项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hundredth" as "hundred + th" — simply adding the ordinal suffix "-th" to "hundred," just like "fourth" or "fifth."
把"hundredth"想成"hundred + th"——只需在"hundred"后加上序数后缀"-th"，就像"fourth"或"fifth"一样。
