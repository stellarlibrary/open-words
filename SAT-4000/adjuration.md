# 🙏 adjuration

🔊 /ˌædʒʊˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A solemn or earnest appeal; an urgent entreaty or command, often invoking something sacred.
**中文：** 严令；恳请；立誓

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- entreaty（恳求）
- plea（请求）
- supplication（祈求）
- exhortation（劝告）
- appeal（呼吁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adjuration to do something**, **solemn adjuration**, **earnest adjuration**

👉 Example: The judge's adjuration to the jury emphasized the gravity of their decision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The leader's **adjuration** to maintain unity resonated deeply with every member of the assembly.
   领导人关于维护团结的恳请在每位与会成员心中引起了深深的共鸣。

2. Despite her repeated **adjuration**, the committee refused to reconsider its controversial ruling.
   尽管她反复恳请，委员会仍拒绝重新考虑其有争议的裁决。

3. The priest delivered a solemn **adjuration** urging the congregation to uphold their moral obligations.
   牧师发出庄严的告诫，敦促会众履行其道德义务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adjure" + "-ation" — the act of solemnly urging someone, like swearing ("jure" relates to oath/law).
联想"adjure"（恳请）+"-ation"（名词后缀）——庄严地敦促某人的行为，"jure"与誓言/法律有关。
