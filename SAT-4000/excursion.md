# 🚌 excursion

🔊 /ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A short journey or trip, especially one taken for leisure or pleasure; a deviation or digression from a main topic or course
**中文：** 短途旅行；游览；远足；偏移；离题

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outing（郊游）
- expedition（远征）
- jaunt（短途旅行）
- trip（旅行）
- digression（离题）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **go on an excursion / a day excursion / excursion to / guided excursion / brief excursion**

👉 Example: The class went on a guided excursion to the botanical garden to study native plant species.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university organized an educational **excursion** to the archaeological site where students could observe ongoing excavation work firsthand.
   大学组织了一次前往考古遗址的教育性短途旅行，学生们可以亲眼观察正在进行的挖掘工作。

2. The author's brief **excursion** into political commentary in the third chapter disrupted the otherwise cohesive narrative structure.
   作者在第三章中对政治评论的短暂离题打破了原本连贯的叙事结构。

3. Weekend **excursions** to the countryside provided a restorative escape from the relentless pressures of urban professional life.
   周末前往乡间的短途旅行为摆脱城市职业生活的不断压力提供了恢复性的放松。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-cursion" as "ex-course" — you go off your regular course for a short trip, then return to your normal routine.
把"excursion"想成"ex-course"——你离开你的常规路线去短途旅行，然后回到正常的日常安排。
