# ✨ sheer

🔊 /ʃɪr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb, verb, noun | 形容词, 副词, 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Absolute; transparent and thin; very steep. (adv.) Completely; steeply. (v.) To deviate from a course. (n.) A deviation; a thin fabric
**中文：** adj. 全然的，纯粹的；绝对的，彻底的；极其薄的，透明的；峻峭的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- utter（完全的）
- absolute（绝对的）
- steep（陡峭的）
- transparent（透明的）
- sheer（纯粹的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sheer determination / sheer luck / sheer volume / sheer cliff / sheer fabric**

👉 Example: It was by sheer determination that the team managed to complete the project before the deadline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **sheer** magnitude of the environmental crisis demands immediate and coordinated action from all nations.
   环境危机的严重程度之大，要求所有国家立即采取协调一致的行动。

2. Climbers faced a **sheer** rock face that rose nearly five hundred meters above the valley floor.
   攀登者面对一面几乎从谷底垂直升起五百米的陡峭岩壁。

3. Through **sheer** perseverance and intellectual rigor, the scientist finally proved the theorem that had eluded researchers for decades.
   凭借纯粹的毅力和严谨的思维，这位科学家终于证明了困扰研究人员数十年的定理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sheer" as something so extreme it has nothing mixed in — sheer cliff (purely vertical), sheer fabric (purely transparent), sheer luck (purely chance).
把"sheer"想成极端纯粹的事物——sheer cliff（纯粹的垂直），sheer fabric（纯粹的透明），sheer luck（纯粹的运气）。
