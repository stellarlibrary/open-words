# 🔧 salvageable

🔊 /ˈsælvɪdʒəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being salvaged, rescued, or recovered; able to be saved from loss or destruction
**中文：** adj. 可抢救的；可打捞的；可挽救的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recoverable（可恢复的）
- retrievable（可取回的）
- redeemable（可挽回的）
- reclaimable（可收回的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **still salvageable / barely salvageable / salvageable materials / salvageable relationship**

👉 Example: The engineers determined that much of the damaged machinery was still salvageable after the flood.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the earthquake, inspectors assessed which structures were **salvageable** and which required complete demolition and rebuilding.
   地震过后，检查人员评估了哪些建筑可以抢救，哪些需要完全拆除重建。

2. The mediator believed the partnership was still **salvageable** if both parties agreed to renegotiate the terms of their contract.
   调解人认为，如果双方同意重新谈判合同条款，这种合作关系仍然是可以挽救的。

3. Only a small portion of the water-damaged archives proved **salvageable** despite the conservators' extensive restoration efforts.
   尽管修复人员做出了大量努力，只有一小部分被水损坏的档案被证明是可以抢救的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "salvageable" as "salvage + able" — if something is salvageable, you are able to salvage it, meaning it can still be saved.
把"salvageable"想成"salvage（抢救）+ able（能够的）"——如果某物是salvageable的，你就能够抢救它，意味着它仍可以被拯救。
