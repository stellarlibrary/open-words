# 🎨 renaissance

🔊 /ˈrenəsɑːns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A revival or renewed interest in something; the period of European cultural and artistic rebirth from the 14th to 17th century
**中文：** n. 复兴，复活，新生；文艺复兴，文艺复兴时期

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revival（复兴）
- rebirth（新生）
- resurgence（复苏）
- renewal（更新）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cultural renaissance / renaissance of something / renaissance man / urban renaissance**

👉 Example: The city experienced a remarkable cultural renaissance after decades of economic stagnation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **renaissance** of classical music in the region attracted prominent composers and performers from across the continent.
   该地区古典音乐的复兴吸引了来自各地的著名作曲家和演奏家。

2. Historians consider the Italian **Renaissance** a pivotal era that fundamentally transformed European art, science, and philosophy.
   历史学家认为意大利文艺复兴是从根本上改变了欧洲艺术、科学和哲学的关键时代。

3. The small coastal town is currently undergoing an economic **renaissance** driven by technology startups and creative industries.
   这个沿海小镇目前正经历由科技初创企业和创意产业推动的经济复兴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-NAISS-ance" as "re-NASCENT" — being born again, a rebirth of culture and knowledge.
把"renaissance"想成"re-NASCENT"——再次诞生，文化和知识的重生。
