# 💎 pithy

🔊 /ˈpɪθi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Concise and forcefully expressive; (of a plant stem) containing pith or spongy tissue
**中文：** adj. 简洁有力的；精练的；有髓的；多髓的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- succinct（简洁的）
- concise（精练的）
- terse（简短的）
- laconic（言简意赅的）
- cogent（有说服力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pithy remark / pithy comment / pithy summary / pithy observation / pithy saying**

👉 Example: The columnist was known for her pithy observations about contemporary politics and culture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editor praised the journalist for crafting a **pithy** summary that conveyed the essence of the report in just two sentences.
   编辑称赞这位记者撰写了一篇精练的摘要，仅用两句话就传达了报告的要点。

2. Benjamin Franklin's **pithy** aphorisms, such as "time is money," continue to resonate with readers across many generations.
   本杰明·富兰克林简洁有力的格言，如"时间就是金钱"，至今仍在一代又一代读者中引起共鸣。

3. A truly **pithy** argument can be far more persuasive than a lengthy discourse packed with redundant supporting evidence.
   一个真正简洁有力的论点可能比充斥着冗余论据的冗长论述更具说服力。

---

把"pith"想成水果的果芯——简洁有力的语言剥去外皮，直达意义的核心。
