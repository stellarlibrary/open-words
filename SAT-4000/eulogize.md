# 🌟 eulogize

🔊 /ˈjuːlədʒaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To praise highly in speech or writing; to deliver a eulogy for someone
**中文：** v. 颂扬；称赞；赞美

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- praise（赞美）
- extol（颂扬）
- laud（赞扬）
- commend（称赞）
- glorify（美化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eulogize someone / eulogize the achievements / eulogize the virtues of**

👉 Example: The professor was invited to eulogize the contributions of the late Nobel laureate at the memorial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The minister stood before the congregation to **eulogize** the beloved community leader who had served for decades.
   牧师站在会众面前颂扬那位服务了数十年的深受爱戴的社区领袖。

2. Critics often **eulogize** the author's early works while dismissing her later experimental novels as overly ambitious.
   评论家常常称赞这位作家的早期作品，却认为她后来的实验性小说过于野心勃勃。

3. The documentary seeks to **eulogize** the sacrifices made by frontline workers during the unprecedented global health crisis.
   这部纪录片旨在颂扬前线工作者在空前的全球健康危机中所做的牺牲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eu-log-ize" as "eu" (good, from Greek) + "log" (words) — using good words to praise someone.
把"eulogize"想成"eu"（好的，源自希腊语）+ "log"（话语）——用美好的话语来赞美某人。
