# 🧂 brine

🔊 /braɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Water strongly saturated with salt; seawater; a saline solution used for preserving food. (v.) To treat or preserve with brine
**中文：** n. 卤水；盐水；海水 v. 用浓盐水处理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- saltwater（盐水）
- saline（盐水的）
- pickle（腌制液）
- seawater（海水）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brine solution / soak in brine / brine the turkey / brine shrimp / salt brine**

👉 Example: The chef recommended soaking the chicken in brine overnight to enhance its flavor and moisture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Ancient civilizations used **brine** as a primary method of preserving meat and vegetables during the long winter months.
   古代文明使用盐水作为在漫长冬季保存肉类和蔬菜的主要方法。

2. The laboratory technician prepared a concentrated **brine** solution to test the corrosion resistance of various metal alloys.
   实验室技术人员配制了浓盐水溶液，以测试各种金属合金的耐腐蚀性。

3. Culinary experts advise cooks to **brine** poultry for several hours before roasting to achieve optimal tenderness and flavor.
   烹饪专家建议厨师在烘烤前将家禽用盐水浸泡数小时，以达到最佳的嫩度和风味。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brine" as "br-ine" — the "ine" sounds like "in the sea," where brine naturally comes from.
把"brine"想成"br-ine"——"ine"听起来像"in the sea"（在海里），盐水正是来自大海。
