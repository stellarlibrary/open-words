# 🎯 coincide

🔊 /ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To occur at the same time or place; to be in agreement or harmony; to correspond exactly
**中文：** v. 一致；符合；同时发生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- correspond（一致）
- concur（同时发生）
- accord（符合）
- align（吻合）
- synchronize（同步）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coincide with / happen to coincide / coincide in time / views coincide**

👉 Example: The opening ceremony was scheduled to coincide with the anniversary of the institution's founding.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The release of the economic report was timed to **coincide** with the annual shareholders' meeting held at corporate headquarters.
   经济报告的发布时间被安排在公司总部举行的年度股东大会同期。

2. Her research findings **coincided** with those of an independent laboratory, thereby strengthening the validity of both studies considerably.
   她的研究结果与一个独立实验室的结果一致，从而大大增强了两项研究的有效性。

3. The interests of the environmental activists and local farmers **coincided** on the issue of preserving the region's natural watershed.
   环保活动人士和当地农民在保护该地区天然分水岭的问题上利益一致。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "coincide" as "co-in-cide" — "co" (together) + "in" (in) + "cide" (fall), meaning things that "fall together" into the same place or time.
把"coincide"想成"co（共同）-in（在）-cide（落下）"——意思是事物"一起落在"同一个地点或时间。
