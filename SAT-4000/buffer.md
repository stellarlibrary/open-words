# 🛡️ buffer

🔊 /ˈbʌfər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person or thing that serves to reduce shock, friction, or danger between two forces; a temporary data storage area in computing. (v.) To lessen or absorb the impact of something
**中文：** n. 缓冲区；缓冲器，减震器 v. 缓冲；减轻冲击

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cushion（缓冲物）
- shield（屏障）
- safeguard（保障）
- intermediary（中间人）
- absorber（减震器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **buffer zone / buffer against / buffer state / buffer solution / act as a buffer**

👉 Example: The demilitarized zone served as a buffer between the two hostile nations along the contested border.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The central bank maintained substantial foreign reserves to **buffer** the national economy against sudden fluctuations in global markets.
   中央银行保持大量外汇储备，以缓冲全球市场突然波动对国民经济的影响。

2. The neutral country historically served as a diplomatic **buffer** between the two rival superpowers throughout the Cold War era.
   在整个冷战时期，该中立国历来充当两个对立超级大国之间的外交缓冲。

3. Engineers designed an advanced **buffer** system to protect the delicate laboratory instruments from harmful seismic vibrations underground.
   工程师设计了一套先进的缓冲系统，以保护精密实验室仪器免受地下有害地震振动的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "buff-er" — something that "buffs" or softens the blow, like a padded bumper absorbing impact between two objects.
把"buffer"想成"buff（打磨/减缓）+ er"——像一个软垫保险杠一样，吸收两个物体之间的冲击力。
