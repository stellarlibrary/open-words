# ⚔️ centurion

🔊 /sɛnˈtjʊəriən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A commander of a century (a unit of approximately one hundred soldiers) in the ancient Roman army
**中文：** n. 百夫长；百人队队长

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commander（指挥官）
- captain（队长）
- officer（军官）
- legion leader（军团领袖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Roman centurion / centurion's command / centurion of the legion / centurion rank**

👉 Example: The centurion led his hundred soldiers with unwavering discipline through the harsh mountain campaign.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **centurion** was responsible for training, disciplining, and leading approximately one hundred legionaries into battle.
   百夫长负责训练、管理和率领大约一百名军团士兵投入战斗。

2. Archaeological evidence suggests that Roman **centurions** wielded significant authority and were well compensated for their service.
   考古证据表明，罗马百夫长拥有重大权力，并因其服役获得丰厚报酬。

3. The historian described the **centurion** as the backbone of the Roman military, essential to maintaining order and morale.
   历史学家将百夫长描述为罗马军队的中坚力量，对于维持秩序和士气至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "centuri-" (hundred, as in century) + "-on" (person) — the person in charge of a hundred soldiers.
想想"centuri-（百，如century世纪）"加"-on（人）"——掌管一百名士兵的人。
