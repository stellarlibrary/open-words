# 🚫 abstain

🔊 /əbˈsteɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something; to refrain deliberately
**中文：** v. 自制；放弃；避免；禁绝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refrain（克制）
- forgo（放弃）
- renounce（弃绝）
- desist（停止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abstain from alcohol / abstain from voting / abstain from eating / choose to abstain**

👉 Example: Several delegates chose to abstain from the controversial vote on the new policy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor advised the patient to **abstain** from consuming sugary foods during the recovery period.
   医生建议患者在恢复期间避免食用含糖食品。

2. Three members of the committee decided to **abstain** from voting on the proposed amendment.
   委员会的三名成员决定在拟议修正案的投票中弃权。

3. He vowed to **abstain** from all social media for an entire month to improve his productivity.
   他发誓整整一个月不使用任何社交媒体，以提高自己的工作效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abstain" as "abs-TAIN" — you CONTAIN your abs (yourself), holding back from temptation.
把"abstain"想成"abs-tain"——你约束(contain)自己，抵挡住诱惑不去做某事。
