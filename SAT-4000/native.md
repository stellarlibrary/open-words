# 🌿 native

🔊 /ˈneɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Belonging to a place by birth; innate or inherent. (n.) A person born in a specified place; an original inhabitant
**中文：** adj. 本国的；土著的；天然的；与生俱来的 n. 本地人；土产；当地居民

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indigenous（土著的）
- innate（天生的）
- local（本地的）
- inherent（固有的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **native speaker / native land / native to / go native / native language**

👉 Example: The koala is native to Australia and cannot be found in the wild elsewhere.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biologist confirmed that the plant species is **native** to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia.
   生物学家确认该植物物种原产于东南亚的热带森林。

2. As a **native** of the region, she possessed an intimate knowledge of its geography, customs, and oral traditions.
   作为该地区的本地人，她对其地理、风俗和口头传统有着深入的了解。

3. His **native** intelligence allowed him to grasp complex mathematical concepts without any formal academic training.
   他与生俱来的智慧使他无需任何正式学术训练便能理解复杂的数学概念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "native" as sharing a root with "nature" — what is native to you is natural, born with you or belonging to your birthplace.
把"native"和"nature"联系起来——native的东西是自然的，是与你一起出生的或属于你出生地的。
