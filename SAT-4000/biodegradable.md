# ♻️ biodegradable

🔊 /ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms, thereby avoiding pollution
**中文：** 生物可降解的；能被生物分解的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decomposable（可分解的）
- compostable（可堆肥的）
- degradable（可降解的）
- eco-friendly（环保的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **biodegradable packaging / biodegradable materials / biodegradable waste / biodegradable plastic / fully biodegradable**

👉 Example: Consumers are increasingly choosing biodegradable packaging to reduce their environmental footprint.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The municipality mandated that all single-use food containers sold within city limits must be made from **biodegradable** materials.
   该市规定在市区范围内销售的所有一次性食品容器必须由生物可降解材料制成。

2. Scientists developed a **biodegradable** polymer that breaks down completely within six months under normal composting conditions.
   科学家们开发了一种在正常堆肥条件下六个月内可完全分解的生物可降解聚合物。

3. The shift toward **biodegradable** alternatives has significantly reduced the volume of persistent plastic waste in coastal ecosystems.
   向生物可降解替代品的转变已显著减少了沿海生态系统中持久性塑料废物的数量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bio-" (life) + "degrade" (break down) + "-able" (capable of) — biodegradable means life can break it down naturally.
把"biodegradable"拆分为"bio-"（生命）+"degrade"（降解）+"-able"（能够）——意思是生物能够将其自然分解。
