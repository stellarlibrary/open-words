# 🔙 retrograde

🔊 /ˈrɛtrəˌɡreɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Moving backward; declining or deteriorating. (v.) To move backward; to revert to an earlier or inferior condition
**中文：** adj. 倒退的；退化的 v. 逆行；倒退

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regressive（退步的）
- backward（向后的）
- deteriorating（退化的）
- declining（衰退的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retrograde motion / retrograde step / retrograde policy / Mercury retrograde**

👉 Example: Critics labeled the proposed education reforms as a retrograde step that would undermine decades of progress.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Astronomers observed the planet's apparent **retrograde** motion as it seemed to move westward against the backdrop of distant stars.
   天文学家观察到这颗行星的视逆行运动，它似乎在遥远恒星的背景下向西移动。

2. Many historians regard the abolition of civil liberties during that era as a profoundly **retrograde** development in democratic governance.
   许多历史学家认为那个时代废除公民自由是民主治理中一次严重的倒退。

3. The committee warned that reducing funding for scientific research would cause the nation's technological capabilities to **retrograde** significantly.
   委员会警告说，削减科研经费将导致国家的技术能力大幅倒退。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "retro-grade" as getting a grade that sends you back — a failing grade that makes you go backward instead of forward.
把"retrograde"想成"让你倒退的成绩"——一个不及格的分数让你后退而不是前进。
