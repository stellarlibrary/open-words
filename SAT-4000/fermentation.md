# 🧪 fermentation

🔊 /ˌfɜːrmɛnˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A chemical process in which organic substances are broken down by enzymes, bacteria, or yeast, especially the conversion of sugars to alcohol or acids; a state of agitation or excitement
**中文：** n. 发酵

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brewing（酿造）
- leavening（发酵）
- agitation（骚动）
- effervescence（起泡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fermentation process / alcoholic fermentation / undergo fermentation / fermentation of ideas / natural fermentation**

👉 Example: The fermentation of grape juice into wine requires careful temperature control and precise timing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fermentation** process used in producing traditional sourdough bread relies on naturally occurring wild yeast and lactic acid bacteria.
   制作传统酸面包所用的发酵过程依赖于天然存在的野生酵母和乳酸菌。

2. Scientists studied the **fermentation** of agricultural waste as a sustainable method for generating biofuel in developing regions.
   科学家研究了农业废料的发酵作为在发展中地区生产生物燃料的可持续方法。

3. The intellectual **fermentation** of the Enlightenment era produced revolutionary ideas that fundamentally transformed European political thought.
   启蒙时代的思想发酵产生了从根本上改变欧洲政治思想的革命性理念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fermentation" as "ferment + ation" — when things ferment, they bubble and transform, just like ideas fermenting in your mind.
把"fermentation"拆成"ferment（发酵）+ ation"——东西发酵时会冒泡和转化，就像想法在脑中酝酿一样。
