# 🧱 density

🔊 /ˈdensɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The degree of compactness of a substance; the quantity of mass per unit volume; the quality of being closely packed together
**中文：** n. 密度；稠密；浓度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compactness（紧密）
- concentration（浓度）
- thickness（厚度）
- solidity（密实）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **population density / high density / low density / density of a material / bone density**

👉 Example: The population density of urban areas presents unique challenges for city planners and architects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **density** of Earth's inner core is estimated to be approximately thirteen times greater than that of water at the surface.
   地球内核的密度估计约为地表水密度的十三倍。

2. Rapid urbanization has dramatically increased the population **density** of many developing cities, straining infrastructure and public services beyond capacity.
   快速城市化大幅提高了许多发展中城市的人口密度，使基础设施和公共服务不堪重负。

3. Scientists measured the **density** of the newly synthesized polymer to determine whether it would be suitable for lightweight aerospace applications.
   科学家们测量了新合成聚合物的密度，以确定其是否适合轻量化航空航天应用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dense-ity" as "how dense something is" — density measures how tightly packed the matter is within a given space.
把"density"想成"dense（稠密的）+ ity（名词后缀）"——density衡量的是在给定空间内物质的紧密程度。
