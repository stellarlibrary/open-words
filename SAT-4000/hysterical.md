# 😱 hysterical

🔊 /hɪˈsterɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Affected by or deriving from wildly uncontrolled emotion; extremely funny or amusing (informal)
**中文：** adj. 歇斯底里的；情绪异常激动的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frantic（疯狂的）
- overwrought（过度紧张的）
- frenzied（狂乱的）
- delirious（神志不清的）
- uncontrollable（无法控制的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hysterical laughter / become hysterical / hysterical reaction / nearly hysterical / hysterical crying**

👉 Example: The crowd became nearly hysterical when the fire alarm sounded during the packed evening performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness became **hysterical** on the stand, sobbing uncontrollably as she recounted the traumatic events of that evening.
   证人在证人席上变得歇斯底里，在讲述那晚的创伤性事件时不可控制地哭泣。

2. The psychologist noted that **hysterical** responses to stress often mask deeper emotional issues that require professional intervention.
   心理学家指出，对压力的歇斯底里反应往往掩盖了需要专业干预的更深层情感问题。

3. Although the satirical film was intended to be **hysterical**, many critics found its humor shallow and its social commentary unconvincing.
   尽管这部讽刺电影意在令人捧腹大笑，但许多评论家认为其幽默肤浅，社会评论缺乏说服力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hyster-" (from Greek "hystera," meaning womb) + "-ical" — in ancient times, emotional outbursts were wrongly attributed to the womb, giving us "hysterical."
"hyster-"来自希腊语"hystera"（子宫）+"-ical"——古代人错误地将情绪失控归因于子宫，由此产生了"hysterical"一词。
