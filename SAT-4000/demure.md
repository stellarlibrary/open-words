# 🌸 demure

🔊 /dɪˈmjʊr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Reserved, modest, and shy in manner or behavior; affectedly modest or coy
**中文：** adj. 端庄的；娴静的；假装矜持的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modest（谦虚的）
- reserved（矜持的）
- coy（腼腆的）
- unassuming（谦逊的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **demure manner / demure smile / demure appearance / demure young woman**

👉 Example: She gave a demure smile when the professor praised her outstanding research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate maintained a **demure** composure throughout the interview, carefully choosing her words with quiet confidence.
   这位候选人在整个面试过程中保持端庄的姿态，以沉静的自信谨慎地选择措辞。

2. Despite her **demure** exterior, she possessed a fierce determination that surprised everyone in the boardroom.
   尽管她外表娴静，但她内心的坚定决心让会议室里的每个人都感到惊讶。

3. Victorian society expected women to adopt a **demure** disposition, discouraging any outward display of strong emotion.
   维多利亚时代的社会要求女性保持端庄矜持的举止，不鼓励任何强烈情感的外在表现。

---

把"demure"想成"the mural（壁画）"——壁画安静地挂在墙上，沉静端庄，就像一个矜持的人。
