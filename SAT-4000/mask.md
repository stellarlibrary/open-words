# 🎭 mask

🔊 /mæsk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A covering for the face used for protection, disguise, or concealment. (v.) To conceal or disguise something; to cover with a mask
**中文：** n. 面具；口罩；掩饰 v. 掩饰；戴面具

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disguise（伪装）
- conceal（隐藏）
- camouflage（掩饰）
- veil（面纱；掩盖）
- cloak（遮掩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mask one's feelings / mask the truth / wear a mask / behind a mask / mask the odor**

👉 Example: Her cheerful demeanor could not fully mask the anxiety she felt about the upcoming examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's calm expression served to **mask** the deep frustration he felt during the prolonged and difficult negotiations.
   这位外交官平静的表情掩饰了他在漫长而艰难的谈判中感到的深深挫败。

2. Researchers discovered that certain organisms use chemical compounds to **mask** their scent from potential predators in the wild.
   研究人员发现某些生物利用化学物质来掩盖自身气味，以躲避野外的潜在捕食者。

3. The elaborate **mask** worn during the ceremonial performance symbolized the duality of human nature in that ancient culture.
   典礼表演中佩戴的精致面具象征着那个古代文化中人性的二元性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mask" as "m-ask" — when someone wears a mask, you must ask "Who are you?" because their true identity is hidden.
把"mask"想成"m-ask"——当一个人戴着面具时，你必须问"你是谁？"因为他们的真实身份被隐藏了。
