# 🚫 barring

🔊 /ˈbɑːrɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *prep. | 介词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Except for; unless there is or are; not including.

**中文：** 除非；不包括；除……之外。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- except for （除了）
- excluding （不包括）
- unless （除非）
- apart from （除……之外）
- save for （除了）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Barring" is a formal preposition typically placed at the beginning of a clause or phrase. It introduces an exception or condition. Common collocations include "barring any unforeseen circumstances" and "barring accidents." It derives from the verb "bar," meaning to prevent or exclude.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Barring** any unexpected delays, the construction project should be completed by next December.
   中文：除非出现意外延误，该建设项目应于明年十二月前完工。

2. **Barring** a few minor revisions, the committee approved the entire proposal without further debate.
   中文：除了几处小修改外，委员会未经进一步辩论便批准了整个提案。

3. The team is confident of victory, **barring** injuries to their most valuable starting players.
   中文：除非主力球员受伤，否则该队对胜利充满信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of a "bar" across a doorway — it blocks things from entering. "Barring" means blocking something from being included, i.e., "excluding" or "unless."

联想记忆：想象门口有一根"bar"（横杆）——它阻止事物进入。"Barring"意为将某事物挡在外面，即"不包括"或"除非"。
