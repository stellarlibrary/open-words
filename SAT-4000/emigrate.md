# ✈️ emigrate

🔊 /ˈemɪɡreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To leave one's own country in order to settle permanently in another
**中文：** v. 移居外国；迁居

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- migrate（迁移）
- relocate（迁居）
- resettle（重新定居）
- expatriate（移居国外）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emigrate from / emigrate to / emigrate abroad / decide to emigrate**

👉 Example: Thousands of families chose to emigrate from their homeland in search of greater economic opportunities abroad.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many skilled professionals chose to **emigrate** from the country during the economic downturn to seek better career prospects.
   许多技术人才在经济低迷期间选择移居国外，以寻求更好的职业前景。

2. Her grandparents decided to **emigrate** from Eastern Europe in the early twentieth century to escape political persecution.
   她的祖父母在二十世纪初决定从东欧移居他国，以逃避政治迫害。

3. The government introduced new incentives to discourage young graduates from choosing to **emigrate** after completing their education.
   政府推出了新的激励措施，以阻止年轻毕业生在完成学业后选择移居国外。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the "e" in "emigrate" as "exit" — you exit your home country to live somewhere else.
把"emigrate"中的"e"想成"exit（离开）"——你离开自己的祖国去别处生活。
