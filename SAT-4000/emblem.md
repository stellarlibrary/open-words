# 🛡️ emblem

🔊 /ˈɛmbləm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A heraldic device, symbol, or figure used to represent a quality, concept, organization, or nation
**中文：** n. 象征；徽章；符号

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- symbol（象征）
- insignia（徽章）
- badge（标志）
- crest（纹章）
- icon（图标）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **national emblem / emblem of peace / emblem of hope / serve as an emblem**

👉 Example: The olive branch has long served as an emblem of peace and diplomatic reconciliation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dove remains a widely recognized **emblem** of peace and has been used in political imagery for centuries.
   鸽子仍然是广泛认可的和平象征，几个世纪以来一直用于政治形象中。

2. The university adopted a new **emblem** featuring an open book and a torch to represent knowledge and enlightenment.
   该大学采用了新的徽章，以一本打开的书和一支火炬代表知识与启蒙。

3. The bald eagle serves as the national **emblem** of the United States, symbolizing strength, freedom, and sovereignty.
   白头鹰是美国的国家象征，代表着力量、自由和主权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EMBLEM" as "EM-BLEM" — an emblem is a mark or blemish on a shield that identifies a group or nation.
把"emblem"想成"EM-BLEM"——徽章就是盾牌上标识一个团体或国家的标记。
