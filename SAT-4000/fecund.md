# 🌱 fecund

🔊 /ˈfekənd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Highly fertile and productive; capable of producing an abundance of offspring, vegetation, or creative work
**中文：** adj. 生殖力强的；多产的；丰饶的；肥沃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fertile（肥沃的）
- prolific（多产的）
- fruitful（富有成效的）
- productive（高产的）
- abundant（丰富的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fecund imagination / fecund soil / fecund mind / fecund period / fecund environment**

👉 Example: The river delta's fecund soil supported thriving agricultural communities for thousands of years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fecund** valley produced enough grain each season to sustain the entire surrounding population through the harshest winter months.
   这片肥沃的山谷每季生产的粮食足以供养周围全部人口度过最严酷的冬季。

2. Her **fecund** imagination allowed her to compose dozens of symphonies that remain celebrated masterpieces of classical music today.
   她丰富的想象力使她创作了数十部交响曲，这些作品至今仍是古典音乐中备受推崇的杰作。

3. Biologists discovered that the **fecund** species could produce over a thousand offspring annually, rapidly colonizing new aquatic habitats.
   生物学家发现这种多产的物种每年能繁殖一千多个后代，迅速占据新的水生栖息地。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fecund" as sounding like "second" — nature gives a second, third, and hundredth yield because the soil is so fecund and productive.
把"fecund"的发音联想到"second（第二）"——大自然不断给出第二次、第三次、第一百次产出，因为土壤如此肥沃多产。
