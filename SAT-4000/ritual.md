# 🕯️ ritual

🔊 /ˈrɪtʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A ceremony or series of acts regularly repeated in a set manner. (adj.) Relating to or done as a rite or ritual
**中文：** adj. 典礼的，仪式的 n. 典礼，仪式；礼节

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ceremony（典礼）
- rite（仪式）
- custom（习俗）
- observance（遵守惯例）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ritual practice / ritual sacrifice / morning ritual / perform a ritual**

👉 Example: The ancient ritual had been performed by the tribe for centuries without any alteration to its steps.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Anthropologists documented the elaborate **ritual** that marked the community's annual harvest celebration and thanksgiving ceremony.
   人类学家记录了这个社区年度丰收庆祝和感恩仪式中的精心典礼。

2. Her morning **ritual** of reading academic journals over coffee had become an indispensable part of her daily routine.
   她每天早晨边喝咖啡边阅读学术期刊的仪式已成为日常生活中不可或缺的一部分。

3. The **ritual** significance of the ceremony was lost on younger generations who viewed it merely as an outdated tradition.
   这个仪式的礼节意义对年轻一代已不复存在，他们仅视其为过时的传统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rite" + "-ual" — a ritual is something done as a rite, a repeated ceremonial act performed in a fixed order.
把"ritual"想成"rite（仪式）+ -ual"——ritual就是作为仪式进行的活动，按固定顺序重复的典礼行为。
