# :performing_arts: repertory

:speaker: /ˈrɛpərˌtɔːri/<br>
:label: *noun | 名词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A stock of plays, dances, or pieces that a company or performer knows and is prepared to perform; a repository or storehouse
**中文：** n. 保留剧目汇演；全部剧目；储备；仓库

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- repertoire（全部剧目）
- collection（收藏）
- repository（储藏室）
- stockpile（储备）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **repertory theater / repertory company / in repertory / a repertory of skills**

:point_right: Example: The repertory theater rotates several classic productions throughout its annual performance season.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The acclaimed **repertory** company performed a different Shakespeare play each evening throughout the entire summer festival season.
   这家著名的保留剧目剧团在整个夏季艺术节期间每晚演出一部不同的莎士比亚戏剧。

2. Her extensive **repertory** of classical piano compositions allowed her to accept performance invitations on remarkably short notice.
   她广泛的古典钢琴曲目储备使她能够在极短的通知时间内接受演出邀请。

3. The museum serves as a **repertory** of cultural artifacts spanning three centuries of the region's rich artistic heritage.
   该博物馆是一个文化艺术品的宝库，涵盖了该地区三个世纪丰富的艺术遗产。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "repertory" as "re-PERT-ory" — a place where performers keep their "expert" collection of plays ready to go.
把"repertory"想成"re-PERT-ory"——一个表演者保存他们"拿手（expert）"剧目合集的地方，随时准备上演。
