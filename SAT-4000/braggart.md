# 🦚 braggart

🔊 /ˈbræɡərt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who boasts excessively about achievements or possessions. (adj.) Boastful; given to bragging
**中文：** n. 吹牛者；好自夸者；大言者 adj. 吹牛的；自夸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boaster（吹嘘者）
- show-off（爱出风头的人）
- blowhard（吹牛大王）
- windbag（空话连篇的人）
- swaggerer（大摇大摆的人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insufferable braggart / notorious braggart / a braggart who... / braggart behavior**

👉 Example: No one enjoyed dining with the braggart, who dominated every conversation with tales of his own greatness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The office **braggart** claimed sole credit for the project's success, ignoring the contributions of the entire team.
   办公室里的吹牛者将项目成功的功劳全部揽到自己身上，无视了整个团队的贡献。

2. In classical literature, the **braggart** soldier is a recurring archetype used to satirize vanity and false courage.
   在古典文学中，自夸的士兵是一个反复出现的原型，用来讽刺虚荣和虚假的勇气。

3. She soon recognized him as a **braggart** whose extravagant promises rarely translated into meaningful or tangible results.
   她很快认出他是一个吹牛者，其夸张的承诺很少转化为有意义或切实的成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bragg-art" — a person who has made an art form out of bragging, perfecting the skill of boasting about themselves.
把"braggart"想成"bragg（吹牛）+ art（艺术）"——一个把吹牛变成了一门艺术的人。
