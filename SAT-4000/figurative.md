# 🎭 figurative

🔊 /ˈfɪɡjərətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Using figures of speech; metaphorical rather than literal; richly decorative or ornamental in expression
**中文：** adj. 比喻的；修饰丰富的；形容多的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- metaphorical（隐喻的）
- symbolic（象征的）
- allegorical（寓言的）
- ornamental（装饰性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **figurative language / figurative meaning / figurative expression / in a figurative sense**

👉 Example: The poet employed figurative language to convey emotions that literal words could not capture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's use of **figurative** language transforms ordinary descriptions into vivid and evocative imagery throughout the novel.
   作者对比喻语言的运用使整部小说中的普通描写变得生动而富有感染力。

2. When she said the news was a "bombshell," she was speaking in a **figurative** sense rather than a literal one.
   当她说这个消息是一颗"炸弹"时，她是在用比喻的意义而非字面意义来表达。

3. Art critics distinguish between **figurative** painting, which represents recognizable forms, and purely abstract composition.
   艺术评论家区分了表现可辨识形象的具象绘画和纯粹的抽象构图。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "figurative" as containing "figure" — a figure of speech uses creative images and comparisons, not plain literal meaning.
把"figurative"想成包含"figure"——修辞手法使用创造性的意象和比较，而非简单的字面意义。
