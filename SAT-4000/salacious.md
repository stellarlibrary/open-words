# 🚫 salacious

🔊 /səˈleɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lustful; characterized by obscene or indecent content; appealing to sexual desire
**中文：** adj. 猥亵的，好色的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obscene（猥亵的）
- lewd（淫荡的）
- prurient（好色的）
- lascivious（好色的）
- indecent（下流的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salacious gossip / salacious content / salacious details / salacious remarks**

👉 Example: The tabloid newspaper was widely criticized for publishing salacious details about the celebrity's private life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee voted to ban the novel from school libraries, citing its **salacious** content as inappropriate for young readers.
   委员会投票禁止该小说进入学校图书馆，认为其猥亵内容不适合年轻读者。

2. Critics argued that the media's fixation on **salacious** gossip distracted the public from genuinely important political issues.
   批评者认为媒体对猥亵八卦的关注分散了公众对真正重要的政治议题的注意力。

3. The defense attorney objected to the introduction of **salacious** testimony that bore no relevance to the charges in question.
   辩护律师反对引入与指控无关的猥亵证词。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sala-cious" as sounding like "sala-cious" where "sala" sounds like "salad" gone bad — something that started innocent but turned indecent.
把"salacious"想成"sala（像salad沙拉）+ cious"——就像一盘变质的沙拉，原本无害的东西变得令人不适，引申为猥亵的、下流的。
