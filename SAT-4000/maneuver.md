# 🎯 maneuver

🔊 /məˈnuːvər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A carefully planned movement or action; a military training exercise. (v.) To move skillfully or manipulate cleverly into a desired position
**中文：** n. 机动；演习；策略 v. 机动；演习；调遣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stratagem（策略）
- tactic（战术）
- navigate（操纵）
- manipulate（巧妙操控）
- ploy（手段）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maneuver through / political maneuver / military maneuver / maneuver into position**

👉 Example: The diplomat had to maneuver carefully through the complex negotiations to secure a favorable agreement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The admiral ordered a series of tactical **maneuvers** designed to outflank the enemy fleet before dawn broke over the horizon.
   海军上将下令进行一系列战术机动，旨在黎明前包抄敌方舰队。

2. She skillfully **maneuvered** through the corporate hierarchy, earning promotions through both competence and strategic relationship building.
   她巧妙地在公司层级中周旋，凭借能力和战略性的人际关系建设获得了晋升。

3. The pilot had to **maneuver** the aircraft through severe turbulence while maintaining communication with the control tower below.
   飞行员不得不在严重的气流颠簸中操控飞机，同时与下方的控制塔保持通信。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "man-UV-er" as a "man" making a "U-V" shaped turn — a skilled, deliberate movement to change direction or position.
把"maneuver"想成一个"man"（人）做出"U-V"形转弯——一个熟练的、刻意的动作来改变方向或位置。
