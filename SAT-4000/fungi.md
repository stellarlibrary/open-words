# 🍄 fungi

🔊 /ˈfʌŋɡaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A kingdom of organisms including mushrooms, yeasts, and molds that obtain nutrients by decomposing organic matter; plural of fungus
**中文：** n. 真菌；菌类；蘑菇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mushrooms（蘑菇）
- molds（霉菌）
- yeasts（酵母菌）
- toadstools（毒蘑菇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **parasitic fungi / beneficial fungi / fungi species / fungi growth / kingdom of fungi**

👉 Example: Certain fungi form symbiotic relationships with plant roots, enhancing nutrient absorption from the soil.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers discovered that specific **fungi** in forest soil networks facilitate chemical communication between neighboring trees and plants.
   研究人员发现，森林土壤网络中的特定真菌促进了相邻树木和植物之间的化学交流。

2. The spread of pathogenic **fungi** across agricultural regions has caused significant crop losses and threatened global food security.
   致病真菌在农业地区的蔓延造成了严重的作物损失，并威胁到全球粮食安全。

3. Biologists classify **fungi** as a separate kingdom because they absorb nutrients externally rather than producing food through photosynthesis.
   生物学家将真菌归为一个独立的界，因为它们从外部吸收营养而非通过光合作用制造食物。

Think of "fungi" as "fun guy" — a mushroom walks into a party because he's a "fun guy" (fungi) everyone wants around.
把"fungi"想成"fun guy（有趣的家伙）"——一个蘑菇走进派对，因为他是一个人人都想与之相处的"fun guy（fungi）"。
