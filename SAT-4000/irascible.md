# 😤 irascible

🔊 /ɪˈræsəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing a tendency to be easily angered; quick-tempered and irritable
**中文：** adj. 易怒的；暴躁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritable（易怒的）
- choleric（暴躁的）
- cantankerous（脾气坏的）
- petulant（任性暴躁的）
- testy（易怒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irascible temperament / irascible old man / irascible nature / increasingly irascible**

👉 Example: The irascible professor was known to snap at students who arrived even a minute late to his lectures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **irascible** committee chairman frequently interrupted speakers and dismissed their proposals with undisguised contempt and impatience.
   这位暴躁的委员会主席经常打断发言者，并带着毫不掩饰的蔑视和不耐烦否定他们的提案。

2. Despite his **irascible** reputation, the renowned surgeon was deeply respected for his extraordinary skill and dedication to patients.
   尽管以脾气暴躁闻名，这位著名外科医生因其非凡的技术和对患者的奉献而深受尊敬。

3. The novelist created an **irascible** protagonist whose volatile temperament both alienated and fascinated the people around him.
   这位小说家塑造了一个易怒的主人公，其反复无常的性情既疏远又吸引着周围的人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "IRAS-cible" — "iras" sounds like "irate" (angry), so an irascible person is someone who easily becomes irate.
把"irascible"想成"IRAS-cible"——"iras"听起来像"irate"（愤怒的），所以irascible的人就是容易变得愤怒的人。
