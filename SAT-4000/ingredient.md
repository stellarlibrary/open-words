# 🧪 ingredient

🔊 /ɪnˈɡriːdiənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Any of the foods or substances that are combined to make a particular dish; a component or element of something
**中文：** n. 原料；要素；组成部分；成分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- component（组成部分）
- element（要素）
- constituent（成分）
- factor（因素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **key ingredient / essential ingredient / active ingredient / secret ingredient / ingredient list**

👉 Example: Trust is a key ingredient in building any successful long-term professional relationship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Effective communication is widely regarded as a vital **ingredient** in the success of any collaborative research endeavor.
   有效的沟通被广泛认为是任何合作研究成功的关键要素。

2. The pharmaceutical company refused to disclose the active **ingredient** in its bestselling medication due to trade secret protections.
   由于商业机密保护，该制药公司拒绝公开其畅销药物的活性成分。

3. Perseverance and intellectual curiosity are essential **ingredients** for achieving meaningful breakthroughs in scientific research and discovery.
   毅力和求知欲是在科学研究与发现中取得重大突破的关键要素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-GREDI-ent" — things that "go in" (Latin ingredi = to go into) to make up a whole, like ingredients going into a recipe.
把"ingredient"想成"in + gredi（进入）+ ent"——进入整体的东西，就像配料进入菜谱一样。
