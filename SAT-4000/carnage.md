# 🩸 carnage

🔊 /ˈkɑːrnɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The killing of a large number of people; great slaughter or massacre, especially in war or conflict
**中文：** n. 大屠杀；残杀；大量流血

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- massacre（大屠杀）
- slaughter（屠杀）
- bloodshed（流血事件）
- butchery（残杀）
- havoc（浩劫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **widespread carnage / scenes of carnage / carnage on the battlefield / utter carnage**

👉 Example: The battlefield was a scene of utter carnage after the prolonged siege.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary depicted the horrific **carnage** that resulted from the prolonged civil war in vivid and disturbing detail.
   这部纪录片以生动而令人不安的细节描绘了长期内战造成的可怕大屠杀。

2. International observers called for an immediate ceasefire to halt the **carnage** inflicted upon the civilian population.
   国际观察人士呼吁立即停火，以制止对平民造成的残杀。

3. The historian's account of the medieval battle revealed the sheer **carnage** that characterized warfare before modern conventions existed.
   这位历史学家对中世纪战役的叙述揭示了在现代战争公约出现之前战争中的惨烈屠杀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carn-" from Latin "carnis" meaning "flesh" — carnage literally means the destruction of flesh, a scene of brutal killing and bloodshed.
把"carn-"想成拉丁语"carnis"（肉体）——carnage字面意思是肉体的毁灭，形容残酷杀戮和血流成河的场景。
