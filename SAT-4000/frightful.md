# 😱 frightful

🔊 /ˈfraɪtfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Causing fear or dread; extremely unpleasant or shocking; very great in extent or degree
**中文：** 可怕的；惊人的；非常的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dreadful（可怕的）
- terrible（糟糕的）
- horrifying（令人恐惧的）
- appalling（骇人的）
- ghastly（恐怖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frightful accident / frightful mess / frightful noise / frightful weather**

👉 Example: The frightful storm caused widespread destruction across the entire coastal region overnight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary depicted the **frightful** conditions endured by prisoners during the prolonged and devastating wartime conflict.
   这部纪录片描绘了囚犯在漫长而毁灭性的战时冲突中所忍受的可怕条件。

2. A **frightful** explosion rocked the chemical plant, forcing the immediate evacuation of thousands of nearby residents.
   一场可怕的爆炸震动了化工厂，迫使附近数千名居民立即疏散。

3. The committee discovered a **frightful** amount of waste in the budget after conducting a thorough financial audit.
   委员会在进行全面财务审计后发现预算中存在惊人的浪费。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fright + ful" — something so full of fright that it makes everyone terrified or shocked.
把"frightful"想成"fright（恐惧）+ ful（充满）"——充满恐惧的事物让每个人都感到害怕或震惊。
