# 🏔️ crest

🔊 /krɛst/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The top of a wave, mountain, or hill; a plume or tuft on the head of a bird or animal. (v.) To reach the top of something
**中文：** n. 波峰；冠；山顶 v. 到达绝顶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- summit（顶峰）
- peak（山顶）
- apex（最高点）
- crown（冠）
- ridge（山脊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crest of a wave / crest a hill / reach the crest / riding the crest**

👉 Example: The hikers paused to admire the view when they finally crested the mountain ridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers observed that the flood waters would **crest** by midnight, threatening nearby residential communities.
   研究人员观察到洪水将在午夜达到最高水位，威胁着附近的居民社区。

2. A magnificent golden **crest** adorned the head of the rare tropical bird discovered in the remote jungle.
   一顶华丽的金色冠羽装饰着在偏远丛林中发现的珍稀热带鸟类的头部。

3. After months of rising prices, economists predicted that inflation would soon **crest** and begin to decline.
   经过数月的价格上涨，经济学家预测通货膨胀将很快见顶并开始下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crest" as the highest point — like the crest of toothpaste squeezed to the top of the tube, just like the brand Crest.
把"crest"想成最高点——就像牙膏被挤到管子顶部，正如牙膏品牌"佳洁士"（Crest）。
