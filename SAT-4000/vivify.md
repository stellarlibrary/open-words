# 🌱 vivify

🔊 /ˈvɪvɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To give life or vitality to; to enliven or animate; to make more lively or vivid
**中文：** v. 给予生气，使复生；使生动，使活跃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enliven（使活跃）
- animate（赋予生命）
- invigorate（使充满活力）
- revitalize（使恢复生机）
- energize（激发活力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vivify a narrative / vivify the imagination / vivify a performance / vivify the spirit**

👉 Example: The director's bold staging choices helped to vivify the classic play for a modern audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The introduction of interactive exhibits helped to **vivify** the museum's permanent collection and attract a younger demographic.
   互动展品的引入有助于使博物馆的永久馆藏更加生动，并吸引更年轻的人群。

2. A skilled historian can **vivify** centuries-old events by weaving together personal narratives with carefully researched factual accounts.
   一位技艺精湛的历史学家可以通过将个人叙事与精心研究的事实记录交织在一起，使数百年前的事件栩栩如生。

3. The composer sought to **vivify** the symphony's final movement with unexpected rhythmic shifts and unconventional instrumentation choices.
   作曲家试图通过意想不到的节奏变化和非常规的配器选择来使交响曲的最后乐章更加生动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vivify" as "vivi + fy" — "vivi" means life (like in "vivisection") and "-fy" means to make, so vivify means to make alive.
把"vivify"想成"vivi（生命）+ fy（使……）"——使之有生命力，使之生动起来。
