# 🏠 occupant

🔊 /ˈɑːkjəpənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who resides in or occupies a particular place; one who holds possession of something
**中文：** n. 居住者；占有者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resident（居民）
- inhabitant（居住者）
- tenant（租户）
- dweller（居住者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **occupant of a building / occupant of a vehicle / sole occupant / current occupant**

👉 Example: The occupant of the apartment had lived there for over a decade before deciding to relocate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The previous **occupant** of this office left behind several documents that contained sensitive financial information.
   这间办公室的前任使用者留下了几份包含敏感财务信息的文件。

2. Emergency responders confirmed that all **occupants** of the building had been safely evacuated before the fire spread.
   应急人员确认大楼内所有居住者在火势蔓延之前已被安全疏散。

3. The legal dispute centered on whether the **occupant** had the right to modify the property without consent.
   法律纠纷的焦点在于占有者是否有权未经同意就对房产进行改造。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "occupant" as "occupy + ant" — imagine an ant that occupies a space in a house, stubbornly residing there.
把"occupant"想成"occupy + ant"——想象一只蚂蚁占据了房子里的一个空间，顽固地住在那里。
