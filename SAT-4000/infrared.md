# 🌡️ infrared

🔊 /ˌɪnfrəˈrɛd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths longer than visible red light. (adj.) Of or relating to infrared radiation
**中文：** n. 红外线 adj. 红外线的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thermal radiation（热辐射）
- heat radiation（热射线）
- IR radiation（红外辐射）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infrared light / infrared sensor / infrared camera / infrared spectrum / infrared radiation**

👉 Example: The security system uses infrared sensors to detect movement in complete darkness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Astronomers use **infrared** telescopes to observe celestial objects that are obscured by interstellar dust in the visible spectrum.
   天文学家使用红外望远镜观测在可见光谱中被星际尘埃遮蔽的天体。

2. The rescue team employed **infrared** imaging technology to locate survivors trapped beneath the rubble of the collapsed building.
   救援队使用红外成像技术定位被困在倒塌建筑废墟下的幸存者。

3. Modern remote controls transmit signals using **infrared** light, which is invisible to the human eye but detectable by electronic receivers.
   现代遥控器使用红外光传输信号，这种光人眼不可见，但可被电子接收器检测到。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "infra-" (below) + "red" — infrared light is BELOW red on the visible spectrum, with wavelengths too long for our eyes to see.
把"infrared"想成"infra-（低于）" + "red（红色）"——红外线在可见光谱中位于红色之下，波长太长，我们的眼睛看不到。
