# 🩸 capillary

🔊 /ˈkæpɪˌlɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A very fine, narrow tube, especially the smallest blood vessels connecting arteries to veins. (adj.) Relating to or resembling a hair-thin tube; of or relating to capillaries.
**中文：** n. 毛细管；毛细血管 adj. 毛细管的；毛状的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- microvessel（微血管）
- fine tube（细管）
- hairlike vessel（毛状管）
- tubule（细管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **capillary action / capillary tube / capillary bed / capillary network / capillary blood**

👉 Example: Water rises through narrow spaces by capillary action, defying gravity through surface tension.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Oxygen diffuses from the blood in the **capillaries** into surrounding tissues, sustaining cellular respiration throughout the body.
   氧气从毛细血管中的血液扩散到周围组织，维持着全身的细胞呼吸。

2. The physicist demonstrated **capillary** action by placing a thin glass tube into a beaker of colored water.
   物理学家通过将一根细玻璃管放入一杯有色水中来演示毛细现象。

3. Damage to the **capillary** walls can result in fluid leakage, causing swelling and inflammation in affected areas.
   毛细血管壁的损伤可能导致液体渗漏，引起受影响区域的肿胀和炎症。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "capillary" from Latin "capillus" meaning "hair" — capillaries are as thin as a single strand of hair.
"capillary"源自拉丁语"capillus（毛发）"——毛细管细如一根头发丝。
