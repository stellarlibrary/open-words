# 🙇 supplicate

🔊 /ˈsʌplɪkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To ask or beg for something earnestly or humbly; to make a humble petition
**中文：** v. 恳求，哀求，恳请

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beseech（恳求）
- implore（哀求）
- entreat（恳请）
- plead（请求）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supplicate for mercy / supplicate the gods / supplicate humbly / supplicate before someone**

👉 Example: The prisoners supplicated for clemency before the judge delivered the final sentence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The villagers gathered at the temple to **supplicate** the gods for rain after months of devastating and relentless drought.
   村民们在寺庙聚集，在数月毁灭性的持续干旱之后恳求神灵降雨。

2. The ambassador was forced to **supplicate** before the foreign court, requesting the release of citizens detained abroad.
   大使被迫在外国宫廷前恳请，要求释放在海外被拘留的公民。

3. Rather than **supplicate** for assistance, the determined scholar resolved to find a solution through her own research efforts.
   这位意志坚定的学者没有恳求帮助，而是决心通过自己的研究努力找到解决方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suppli-cate" as "supply + kate" — imagine begging Kate to supply what you need, earnestly pleading for her help.
把"supplicate"想成"supply + kate"——想象恳求凯特提供你需要的东西，真诚地请求她的帮助。
