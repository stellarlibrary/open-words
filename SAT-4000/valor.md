# ⚔️ valor

🔊 /ˈvælər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Great courage or bravery, especially in the face of danger or battle
**中文：** n. 英勇；勇猛；勇气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bravery（勇敢）
- courage（勇气）
- heroism（英雄气概）
- gallantry（英勇）
- intrepidity（无畏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acts of valor / display valor / medal of valor / military valor / great valor**

👉 Example: The soldier was awarded a medal for his extraordinary valor during the rescue mission.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The firefighter received a commendation for **valor** after rescuing three children from the engulfed building despite life-threatening conditions.
   这位消防员因在危及生命的条件下从被大火吞噬的建筑中救出三名儿童而获得英勇表彰。

2. Historical accounts celebrate the **valor** of the outnumbered defenders who held the fortress against relentless siege for over forty days.
   历史记载赞颂了寡不敌众的守军英勇守卫堡垒、抵抗持续四十多天猛烈围攻的壮举。

3. The novel explores how ordinary individuals can summon remarkable **valor** when confronted with moral crises that challenge their deepest convictions.
   这部小说探讨了普通人在面对挑战其最深信念的道德危机时如何展现非凡的勇气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "valor" as related to "value" — true valor is valued by all because it takes immense courage to face danger.
把"valor"想成与"value"（价值）相关——真正的英勇被所有人珍视，因为面对危险需要巨大的勇气。
