# 💗 bosom

🔊 /ˈbʊzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The chest or breast; the heart or inner feelings; an intimate embrace. (v.) To embrace; to conceal in one's heart. (adj.) Close; intimate; cherished
**中文：** n. 胸；胸怀；内心；乳房；内部 v. 怀抱；把…藏在心中 adj. 知心的；亲密的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- breast（胸部）
- heart（内心）
- chest（胸膛）
- embrace（怀抱）
- intimate（亲密的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bosom friend / in the bosom of / bosom companion / bosom of the family**

👉 Example: She held the secret close to her bosom, unwilling to share it with anyone outside her trusted circle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. He returned to the **bosom** of his family after years of military service abroad, grateful for their unwavering support.
   在海外服役多年后，他回到了家人的怀抱，感激他们始终如一的支持。

2. The two scholars had been **bosom** friends since their university days, sharing both intellectual pursuits and personal confidences.
   这两位学者自大学时代起就是知心好友，分享学术追求和个人心事。

3. She **bosomed** the painful memories of her childhood, rarely allowing them to surface in everyday conversation with others.
   她将童年的痛苦回忆深藏心中，很少让它们在与他人的日常交谈中浮现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bosom" as where you keep things closest — your chest holds your heart, so "bosom" represents both the physical chest and your innermost feelings.
把"bosom"想成你最贴近的地方——胸膛里藏着心脏，所以"bosom"既代表身体的胸部，也代表你最深处的情感。
