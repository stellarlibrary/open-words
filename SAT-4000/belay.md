# 🧗 belay

🔊 /bɪˈleɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To secure a rope by winding it around a cleat or pin; to stop or cease (nautical/climbing term)
**中文：** v. 用绳索拴住；把缆绳拴在系索栓上；中止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- secure（固定）
- fasten（拴紧）
- anchor（锚定）
- halt（停止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **belay a rope / belay that order / belay a climber / on belay**

👉 Example: The experienced climber called out "on belay" to signal that the rope was properly secured.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The instructor demonstrated how to properly **belay** a partner during the advanced rock-climbing certification course.
   教练在高级攀岩认证课程中演示了如何正确地为搭档做绳索保护。

2. The captain ordered the crew to **belay** the previous command and prepare for an immediate change of course.
   船长命令船员中止先前的指令，并准备立即改变航向。

3. Before ascending the cliff face, each climber verified that their harness was correctly attached to the **belay** device.
   在攀登悬崖面之前，每位攀岩者都确认其安全带已正确连接到保护装置上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-lay" — to lay a rope around something to hold it fast, securing it in place for safety.
把"belay"想成"be-lay"——把绳子绕在某物上系牢固定，确保安全。
