# 💪 overcome

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To succeed in dealing with a problem or difficulty; to defeat or prevail over an opponent or obstacle
**中文：** v. 克服；战胜；胜过；征服

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conquer（征服）
- surmount（克服）
- prevail（战胜）
- defeat（击败）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overcome obstacles / overcome fear / overcome challenges / overcome adversity / be overcome with emotion**

👉 Example: The team worked tirelessly to overcome the technical difficulties that had delayed the project.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Through sustained effort and determination, the community managed to **overcome** the devastating effects of the natural disaster.
   通过持续的努力和决心，社区成功克服了自然灾害的毁灭性影响。

2. The researcher had to **overcome** significant funding obstacles before she could proceed with her groundbreaking clinical trial.
   这位研究人员必须克服重大的资金障碍，才能继续进行她开创性的临床试验。

3. The survivors were visibly **overcome** with emotion during the memorial ceremony honoring the victims of the tragedy.
   在纪念悲剧遇难者的仪式上，幸存者们明显被情感所淹没。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-come" as "coming over the top" — you climb over the wall of difficulty and come out on the other side.
把"overcome"想成"come over"——你翻越困难的高墙，从另一面走出来。
