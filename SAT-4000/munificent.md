# 💎 munificent

🔊 /mjuːˈnɪfɪsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely generous; characterized by great liberality in giving, especially of money or gifts
**中文：** adj. 慷慨的；丰厚的；宽宏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- generous（慷慨的）
- lavish（大方的）
- bountiful（丰厚的）
- philanthropic（乐善好施的）
- liberal（慷慨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **munificent gift / munificent patron / munificent donation / munificent gesture**

👉 Example: The foundation made a munificent contribution to support disaster relief efforts in the affected region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **munificent** endowment from the billionaire enabled the research institute to pursue groundbreaking studies in renewable energy.
   这位亿万富翁的慷慨捐赠使研究所能够开展可再生能源方面的开创性研究。

2. Critics praised the government's **munificent** allocation of funds toward public education and infrastructure improvement programs.
   评论家称赞了政府向公共教育和基础设施改善项目慷慨拨款的举措。

3. The retiring professor received a **munificent** farewell gift from colleagues who deeply appreciated her decades of dedicated service.
   即将退休的教授收到了同事们赠送的丰厚告别礼物，以感谢她数十年来的敬业奉献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "munificent" as "money-ficent" — like "magnificent" but with money; a munificent person is magnificently generous.
把"munificent"想成"money-ficent"——像"magnificent（宏伟的）"但与金钱有关；一个munificent的人在给予上十分宏大慷慨。
