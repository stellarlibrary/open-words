# 🤝 alliance

🔊 /əˈlaɪəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A union or association formed for mutual benefit, especially between countries, organizations, or individuals
**中文：** 联盟；联合；联姻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coalition（联合体）
- confederation（联邦）
- partnership（合作关系）
- pact（协定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **form an alliance / strategic alliance / political alliance / alliance between/with**

👉 Example: The two nations formed a strategic alliance to strengthen their military and economic cooperation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly established **alliance** between the three countries aimed to promote regional stability and mutual economic development.
   三国之间新建立的联盟旨在促进地区稳定和共同经济发展。

2. Several technology firms announced a groundbreaking **alliance** to develop open-source artificial intelligence tools for global educational institutions.
   几家科技公司宣布了一项开创性的联盟，为全球教育机构开发开源人工智能工具。

3. The fragile **alliance** between the political parties collapsed after irreconcilable disagreements over the proposed tax reform legislation emerged.
   各政党之间脆弱的联盟在就拟议的税收改革立法出现不可调和的分歧后瓦解了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alliance" as "all-I-ance" — when "all" stand together as "I" in "ance" (a dance), they form a united front.
把"alliance"想成"all-I-ance"——当"所有人"像"我"一样在"ance"（舞蹈）中站在一起时，他们就形成了统一战线。
