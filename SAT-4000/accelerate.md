# 🚀 accelerate

🔊 /əkˈsɛləˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To increase in speed or rate; to cause something to happen sooner or progress faster.
**中文：** 加速；促进；使加快。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hasten（加速）
- expedite（加快）
- quicken（使加快）
- advance（推进）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accelerate the process**, **accelerate growth**, **accelerate the pace of**

👉 Example: The new policy is designed to accelerate economic recovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The introduction of new technology **accelerated** the pace of industrial development significantly.
   新技术的引进大大加快了工业发展的步伐。

2. Researchers discovered that rising temperatures **accelerate** the melting of polar ice caps.
   研究人员发现，气温升高加速了极地冰盖的融化。

3. The government implemented reforms to **accelerate** growth in the renewable energy sector.
   政府实施了改革，以加速可再生能源领域的增长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the "accelerator" pedal in a car — pressing it makes the car accelerate and go faster.
想想汽车里的"油门"（accelerator）——踩下去车就会 accelerate（加速）。
