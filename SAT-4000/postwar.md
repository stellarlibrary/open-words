# 🕊️ postwar

🔊 /ˈpoʊstˌwɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring or existing after a war, especially after World War II. (n.) The period following a war
**中文：** adj. 战后的 n. 战后时期

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aftermath（战后时期）
- post-conflict（冲突后的）
- peacetime（和平时期的）
- reconstruction-era（重建时期的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **postwar era / postwar reconstruction / postwar economy / postwar period**

👉 Example: The postwar economic boom transformed the nation into a global industrial powerhouse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **postwar** reconstruction of Europe required unprecedented levels of international cooperation and financial assistance.
   战后欧洲的重建需要前所未有的国际合作和财政援助。

2. Scholars have extensively studied the **postwar** social transformations that reshaped cultural norms across the continent.
   学者们广泛研究了重塑整个大陆文化规范的战后社会变革。

3. The museum's exhibit chronicled the **postwar** migration patterns that fundamentally altered the demographic landscape of the region.
   博物馆的展览记录了从根本上改变该地区人口格局的战后移民模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "post-war" as "after (post) the war" — it simply describes the time period or conditions that come after a war ends.
把"postwar"想成"post（之后）+ war（战争）"——它简单地描述战争结束后的时期或状况。
