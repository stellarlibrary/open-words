# ⏰ urgency

🔊 /ˈɜːrdʒənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality or state of requiring immediate action or attention; an earnest and pressing insistence
**中文：** n. 紧急，紧迫；催促

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pressing need（迫切需要）
- exigency（紧急状况）
- imperativeness（紧迫性）
- gravity（严重性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a sense of urgency / with urgency / the urgency of / great urgency**

👉 Example: The director spoke with urgency about the need for immediate reforms to the healthcare system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The climate report conveyed a profound sense of **urgency** regarding the need to reduce global carbon emissions within this decade.
   气候报告传达了一种深刻的紧迫感，强调必须在这十年内减少全球碳排放。

2. Medical professionals emphasized the **urgency** of distributing vaccines to vulnerable populations before the winter outbreak season.
   医学专家强调在冬季疫情暴发前向脆弱人群分发疫苗的紧迫性。

3. The ambassador's tone reflected considerable **urgency** as she appealed for international cooperation to resolve the humanitarian crisis.
   大使在呼吁国际合作解决人道主义危机时，语气中透露出相当的紧迫感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "urgency" as something that "urges" you to act now — when there is urgency, you feel urged to respond immediately.
把"urgency"想成某种"催促"（urge）你立刻行动的东西——当有紧迫感时，你会感到被催促立即回应。
