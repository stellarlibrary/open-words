# 🔍 dilate

🔊 /daɪˈleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make or become wider, larger, or more open; to expand in size; to speak or write at length about a topic
**中文：** v. 扩大；膨胀；详述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expand（扩张）
- widen（加宽）
- enlarge（扩大）
- elaborate（详述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dilate on a topic / pupils dilate / blood vessels dilate / dilate with excitement**

👉 Example: The doctor observed that the patient's pupils began to dilate in response to the dim lighting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ophthalmologist used special drops to **dilate** the patient's pupils before conducting a thorough retinal examination.
   眼科医生在进行彻底的视网膜检查之前，使用特殊药水来扩大患者的瞳孔。

2. During the lecture, the professor chose to **dilate** upon the philosophical implications of quantum mechanics for nearly an hour.
   在讲座中，教授选择用将近一个小时的时间详述量子力学的哲学含义。

3. Intense physical exercise causes blood vessels to **dilate**, thereby increasing circulation and delivering more oxygen to active muscles.
   剧烈的体育锻炼会使血管扩张，从而增加血液循环，向活跃的肌肉输送更多氧气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dilate" as "die-LATE" — when you are late, your eyes dilate wide with panic as you realize the time.
把"dilate"想成"die-LATE"——当你迟到时，你意识到时间后眼睛惊恐地睁大（扩张）。
