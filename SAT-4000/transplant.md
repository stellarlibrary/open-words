# 🌱 transplant

🔊 /trænzˈplænt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To move or transfer something to another place or situation; to surgically transfer an organ or tissue. (n.) An operation in which an organ or tissue is transplanted; the organ itself
**中文：** v. 移植；迁移 n. 移植；移植的器官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relocate（迁移）
- transfer（转移）
- graft（嫁接）
- implant（植入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **organ transplant / transplant a seedling / heart transplant / transplant patients**

👉 Example: The surgeon successfully performed a kidney transplant that saved the patient's life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medical team performed a groundbreaking heart **transplant** that gave the critically ill patient a renewed chance at life.
   医疗团队进行了一场开创性的心脏移植手术，给了这位危重患者重获新生的机会。

2. Gardeners must carefully **transplant** seedlings into nutrient-rich soil to ensure healthy root development during the growing season.
   园丁必须小心地将幼苗移植到营养丰富的土壤中，以确保在生长季节根系健康发育。

3. After the war, thousands of displaced families were **transplanted** to new settlements where they struggled to rebuild their lives.
   战后，数千个流离失所的家庭被迁移到新的定居点，在那里他们艰难地重建生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-plant" as "planting across" — uprooting something and planting it in a completely new location, whether a seedling or an organ.
把"transplant"想成"trans（跨越）+ plant（种植）"——将某物连根拔起，种植到全新的位置，无论是幼苗还是器官。
