# 🍂 auburn

🔊 /ˈɔːbərn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A reddish-brown color. (adj.) Of a reddish-brown color, especially used to describe hair
**中文：** n. 赤褐色 adj. 赤褐色的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chestnut（栗色的）
- russet（红褐色的）
- copper（铜色的）
- reddish-brown（红棕色的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **auburn hair / auburn locks / auburn curls / deep auburn / rich auburn**

👉 Example: Her striking auburn hair cascaded over her shoulders, catching the warm afternoon sunlight beautifully.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The portrait depicted a noblewoman with flowing **auburn** hair and penetrating green eyes that captivated every viewer.
   这幅肖像画描绘了一位拥有飘逸赤褐色头发和深邃绿色眼睛的贵妇，令每位观者着迷。

2. As autumn arrived, the forest canopy transformed into magnificent shades of **auburn**, gold, and crimson.
   随着秋天的到来，森林的树冠变成了壮丽的赤褐色、金色和深红色。

3. She decided to dye her hair a rich **auburn** shade to complement her warm complexion and hazel eyes.
   她决定将头发染成浓郁的赤褐色，以衬托她温暖的肤色和淡褐色的眼睛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "auburn" as "autumn-burn" — the warm, reddish-brown color of leaves burned by the autumn sun.
把"auburn"想成"autumn-burn"——被秋日阳光"灼烧"后树叶呈现的温暖赤褐色。
