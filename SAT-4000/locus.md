# 📍 locus

🔊 /ˈloʊkəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular position, point, or place; in genetics, the specific location of a gene on a chromosome; in mathematics, a set of points satisfying a condition
**中文：** n. 轨迹；地点，所在地；基因座

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- location（位置）
- site（地点）
- position（方位）
- center（中心）
- focal point（焦点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **locus of control / locus of power / gene locus / geometric locus**

👉 Example: Psychologists study locus of control to understand whether individuals attribute outcomes to internal or external factors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The capital city has long served as the **locus** of political power and cultural influence within the nation.
   首都长期以来一直是国家政治权力和文化影响力的中心所在。

2. Geneticists identified the specific **locus** on chromosome seven that is associated with increased susceptibility to the disease.
   遗传学家确定了第七号染色体上与该疾病易感性增加相关的特定基因座。

3. The research university became the **locus** of innovation, attracting scholars and entrepreneurs from around the entire world.
   这所研究型大学成为创新中心，吸引了来自全世界的学者和企业家。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "locus" as related to "locate" — both share the Latin root "locus" meaning place, the exact spot where something is found.
把"locus"和"locate（定位）"联系起来——两者共享拉丁语词根"locus"，意为地点，某物所在的确切位置。
