# 🐕 tractable

🔊 /ˈtræktəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easy to control, manage, or deal with; docile and obedient; (of a problem) capable of being solved
**中文：** 易于管教的；易驾驭的；温顺的；（问题）易处理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- docile（温顺的）
- compliant（顺从的）
- manageable（易管理的）
- amenable（顺从的）
- obedient（服从的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tractable problem / tractable animal / tractable student / more tractable / prove tractable**

👉 Example: The committee found the budget dispute to be more tractable than initially anticipated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young horse proved surprisingly **tractable** and responded well to the experienced trainer's gentle methods of instruction.
   这匹小马出奇地**温顺**，对经验丰富的训练师温和的教导方法反应良好。

2. Advances in computing have rendered previously intractable mathematical problems far more **tractable** for modern researchers.
   计算技术的进步使以前难以解决的数学问题对现代研究人员来说变得更加**易处理**。

3. The diplomat's patient approach made the opposing faction considerably more **tractable** during the prolonged peace negotiations.
   外交官耐心的方式使对立派系在漫长的和平谈判中变得更加**易于沟通**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tractable" as "tract-able" — from "tract" meaning to pull or draw, something tractable can be easily pulled along or led.
把"tractable"想成"tract（拉）+ able（能够的）"——能够被轻易牵引的，即温顺的、易管教的。
