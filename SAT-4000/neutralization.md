# ⚗️ neutralization

🔊 /ˌnjuːtrələˈzeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of making something neutral; in chemistry, the reaction of an acid with a base to form water and a salt
**中文：** n. 中和；中和作用；中立状态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- counteraction（抵消）
- nullification（使无效）
- cancellation（取消）
- equalization（均衡化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acid-base neutralization / neutralization reaction / neutralization of a threat / chemical neutralization**

👉 Example: The neutralization of the toxic spill required several teams of trained hazardous-materials specialists.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **neutralization** of the acid with sodium hydroxide produced a harmless salt solution in the laboratory experiment.
   在实验室实验中，用氢氧化钠中和酸产生了无害的盐溶液。

2. Military strategists focused on the **neutralization** of enemy radar installations before launching the main aerial offensive.
   军事战略家在发动主要空中攻势之前专注于摧毁敌方雷达设施。

3. The **neutralization** of political opposition through propaganda became a central tactic of the authoritarian regime.
   通过宣传消除政治反对派成为该专制政权的核心策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neutral" + "-ization" — the process of making something neutral, like mixing an acid and a base until they cancel each other out.
把"neutralization"想成"neutral（中性的）"加"-ization（过程）"——就像把酸和碱混合直到它们相互抵消。
