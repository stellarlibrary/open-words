# 🏚️ frowzy

🔊 /ˈfraʊzi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Musty, stale, or stuffy; slovenly or unkempt in appearance; having an unpleasant smell
**中文：** 不整洁的；有臭味的；霉臭的；邋遢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- musty（发霉的）
- slovenly（邋遢的）
- unkempt（不整洁的）
- stuffy（闷的）
- disheveled（蓬乱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frowzy room / frowzy clothes / frowzy smell / frowzy appearance**

👉 Example: The frowzy apartment had clearly not been cleaned or ventilated in several months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inspector noted the **frowzy** condition of the dormitory and immediately ordered a comprehensive cleaning and sanitation effort.
   检查员注意到宿舍不整洁的状况，并立即下令进行全面的清洁和消毒工作。

2. A **frowzy** odor permeated the abandoned warehouse, suggesting years of neglect and accumulated decay within its walls.
   一股霉臭的气味弥漫在废弃的仓库中，表明多年的疏忽和墙壁内积累的腐朽。

3. The once-elegant hotel had become **frowzy** and dilapidated after decades without proper maintenance or renovation investment.
   这家曾经优雅的酒店在数十年没有适当维护或翻新投资后变得邋遢破旧。

Think of "frowzy" as "frown + cozy gone wrong" — a place that makes you frown because it is the opposite of cozy: messy and smelly.
把"frowzy"想成"frown（皱眉）+ cozy（舒适）变质"——一个让你皱眉的地方，因为它与舒适相反：又脏又臭。
