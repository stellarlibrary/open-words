# 🌿 algae

🔊 /ˈældʒiː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Simple, non-flowering aquatic organisms that photosynthesize, ranging from single-celled forms to large seaweeds
**中文：** 藻类；海藻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- seaweed（海藻）
- plankton（浮游生物）
- kelp（巨藻）
- phytoplankton（浮游植物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **algae bloom / algae growth / blue-green algae / algae-based / toxic algae**
👉 Example: The excessive algae bloom in the lake threatened the survival of native fish populations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists are investigating whether **algae** can serve as a sustainable and efficient source of biofuel for future generations.
   科学家们正在研究藻类是否可以作为未来几代人可持续且高效的生物燃料来源。

2. The rapid proliferation of toxic **algae** in the reservoir prompted authorities to issue a public health advisory.
   水库中有毒藻类的快速繁殖促使当局发布了公共卫生警告。

3. Researchers discovered that certain species of **algae** absorb carbon dioxide far more effectively than terrestrial plant life.
   研究人员发现某些藻类物种吸收二氧化碳的效率远高于陆地植物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Algae" sounds like "all green" — picture a pond covered entirely in green slime, and you will remember algae are green aquatic organisms.
"Algae"听起来像"all green"（全绿）——想象一个完全被绿色黏液覆盖的池塘，你就会记住藻类是绿色的水生生物。
