# 👶 natal

🔊 /ˈneɪtl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to birth; associated with the place or circumstances of one's birth
**中文：** adj. 出生的；诞生的；分娩的；出生地的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- birth-related（与出生有关的）
- native（出生地的）
- congenital（先天的）
- neonatal（新生儿的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natal chart / natal care / natal day / pre-natal / post-natal**

👉 Example: The hospital has expanded its natal care unit to accommodate the growing number of expectant mothers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologist returned to her **natal** village to conduct fieldwork on the traditional customs that shaped her early childhood.
   这位人类学家回到她的**出生**村庄，对塑造她童年的传统习俗进行田野调查。

2. Comprehensive pre-**natal** and post-**natal** care programs have significantly reduced infant mortality rates in developing regions.
   全面的产前和产后护理项目显著降低了发展中地区的婴儿死亡率。

3. Astrologers construct a **natal** chart based on the precise time and location of an individual's birth to interpret personality traits.
   占星师根据个人出生的精确时间和地点绘制**出生**星盘来解读性格特征。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "natal" as sharing its root with "nation" and "native" — all from Latin "natus" meaning "born." Your natal place is where you were born.
把"natal"想成和"nation"（国家）、"native"（本地的）同根——都来自拉丁语"natus"，意为"出生"。你的natal之地就是你出生的地方。
