# ⚡ stimulate

🔊 /ˈstɪmjəˌleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To excite; to encourage; to invigorate
**中文：** v. 刺激，使兴奋；激励，鼓舞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- encourage（鼓励）
- invigorate（激发）
- arouse（唤起）
- motivate（激励）
- spur（促进）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stimulate growth / stimulate the economy / stimulate interest / stimulate discussion**

👉 Example: The professor used thought-provoking questions to stimulate lively classroom discussion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The central bank lowered interest rates to **stimulate** economic growth and encourage consumer spending during the downturn.
   央行降低利率以刺激经济增长，并在经济低迷期间鼓励消费者支出。

2. Innovative teaching methods can **stimulate** students' intellectual curiosity and foster a lifelong passion for independent learning.
   创新的教学方法可以激发学生的求知欲，培养终身独立学习的热情。

3. The research grant was designed to **stimulate** collaboration between universities and private industry in developing renewable energy solutions.
   该研究资助旨在激励大学和私营企业在开发可再生能源解决方案方面的合作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stimulate" as "steam + late" — like adding steam to an engine that is running late, giving it a boost of energy.
把"stimulate"想成"steam（蒸汽）+ late（迟到）"——就像给一台运行迟缓的引擎加蒸汽，给它一股能量推动。
