# 🖨️ cartridge

🔊 /ˈkɑːrtrɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A container holding a supply of material, such as ammunition for a firearm, ink for a printer, or film for a camera
**中文：** n. 弹药筒；打印机的墨盒；暗盒；笔芯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- capsule（胶囊）
- canister（小罐）
- cassette（盒式装置）
- container（容器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ink cartridge / cartridge case / rifle cartridge / replace a cartridge**

👉 Example: The technician replaced the ink cartridge in the office printer before the deadline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forensic team recovered a spent **cartridge** casing from the scene, which proved essential to the criminal investigation.
   法医团队从现场回收了一枚用过的弹壳，这对刑事调查至关重要。

2. Please ensure you install the correct toner **cartridge** before attempting to print the quarterly financial report.
   请确保在打印季度财务报告之前安装正确的碳粉墨盒。

3. The museum displayed an antique rifle alongside its original brass **cartridge** collection from the nineteenth century.
   博物馆展出了一支古董步枪及其十九世纪的原装黄铜弹药筒收藏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cart-ridge" as a small "cart" carrying supplies along a "ridge" — a cartridge is a small container that carries material inside it.
把"cartridge"想成一辆小推车（cart）沿着山脊（ridge）运送物资——弹药筒/墨盒就是一个承载材料的小容器。
