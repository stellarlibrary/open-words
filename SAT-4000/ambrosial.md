# 🍯 ambrosial

🔊 /æmˈbroʊʒəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Exceptionally pleasing to taste or smell; worthy of the gods; divinely delicious or fragrant
**中文：** 特别美味的；芬香的；美味佳肴般的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- delectable（美味的）
- divine（极好的）
- fragrant（芬芳的）
- luscious（甘美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ambrosial flavor / ambrosial aroma / ambrosial feast / ambrosial fragrance**

👉 Example: The ambrosial aroma of freshly baked pastries drifted through the entire neighborhood.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chef prepared an **ambrosial** dessert that left every guest at the banquet requesting a second serving.
   厨师准备了一道特别美味的甜点，让宴会上的每位客人都要求再来一份。

2. The garden was filled with the **ambrosial** fragrance of jasmine and roses blooming in the warm evening air.
   花园里弥漫着茉莉和玫瑰在温暖夜风中绽放的芬芳气息。

3. Ancient poets described the food of the gods as **ambrosial**, a nectar so exquisite that no mortal could resist.
   古代诗人将众神的食物描述为美味无比，是一种精致到凡人无法抗拒的琼浆玉液。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ambrosial" as coming from "ambrosia" — the legendary food of the Greek gods, so divine it grants immortality.
把"ambrosial"和"ambrosia"（仙馔）联系起来——希腊众神的传说食物，美味到能赐予永生。
