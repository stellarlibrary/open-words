# 🐢 gradual

🔊 /ˈɡrædʒuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Taking place or progressing slowly or by degrees; not sudden or steep
**中文：** adj. 逐渐的，逐步的；平缓的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incremental（递增的）
- progressive（逐步的）
- steady（稳步的）
- slow（缓慢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gradual change / gradual improvement / gradual decline / gradual transition**

👉 Example: The gradual shift in public opinion eventually led to significant legislative reforms across the nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **gradual** erosion of the coastal cliffs over centuries has dramatically reshaped the geography of the entire southern peninsula.
   数百年来海岸悬崖的逐渐侵蚀极大地重塑了整个南部半岛的地理面貌。

2. Economists predict a **gradual** recovery in the housing market as employment rates continue to improve throughout the fiscal year.
   经济学家预测，随着就业率在整个财政年度持续改善，房地产市场将逐步复苏。

3. Through **gradual** exposure therapy, the patient learned to manage her severe anxiety in previously overwhelming social situations.
   通过逐步暴露疗法，该患者学会了在以前令人难以承受的社交场合中控制严重的焦虑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gradual" as related to "grade" — like walking up a gentle grade or slope, progress happens one small step at a time.
把"gradual"和"grade"（坡度）联系起来——就像走上一个平缓的坡道，进步是一步一步慢慢来的。
