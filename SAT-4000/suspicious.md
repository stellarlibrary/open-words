# 🕵️ suspicious

🔊 /səˈspɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing a feeling of distrust; having the impression that something is wrong; inclined to suspect.
**中文：** adj. 可疑的，令人怀疑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dubious（可疑的）
- questionable（有问题的）
- distrustful（不信任的）
- skeptical（怀疑的）
- wary（警惕的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suspicious behavior / suspicious of someone / look suspicious / suspicious circumstances / highly suspicious**

👉 Example: The guards became suspicious when they noticed the visitor repeatedly photographing restricted areas.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bank teller reported the **suspicious** transaction to authorities after noticing an unusual pattern of large withdrawals.
   银行出纳在注意到大额取款的异常模式后，向当局报告了这笔可疑交易。

2. Residents grew increasingly **suspicious** of the construction company's intentions after it refused to disclose its environmental impact report.
   在建筑公司拒绝公开其环境影响报告后，居民们对其意图越来越怀疑。

3. The **suspicious** package found in the lobby prompted an immediate evacuation of the entire government office building.
   在大厅发现的可疑包裹导致整栋政府办公楼立即进行了疏散。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suspicious" as the adjective of "suspicion" — when something looks "sus," you feel doubt and want to investigate further.
把"suspicious"想成"suspicion"的形容词形式——当某物看起来"可疑"（sus），你会感到怀疑并想进一步调查。
