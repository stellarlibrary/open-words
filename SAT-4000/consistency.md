# 🔄 consistency

🔊 /kənˈsɪstənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being uniform, steady, or compatible over time; the degree of firmness or thickness of a substance
**中文：** n. 一致性，前后一致；稠度；相容性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- uniformity（一致性）
- coherence（连贯性）
- regularity（规律性）
- steadiness（稳定性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **consistency in / consistency of / maintain consistency / lack of consistency**

👉 Example: The key to academic success lies in the consistency of one's daily study habits.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher emphasized the importance of **consistency** in data collection methods to ensure the validity of experimental results.
   研究人员强调了数据收集方法一致性的重要性，以确保实验结果的有效性。

2. Critics noted a troubling lack of **consistency** between the administration's public statements and its actual policy decisions.
   批评人士指出，政府的公开声明与其实际政策决定之间令人担忧地缺乏一致性。

3. The chef insisted on achieving the perfect **consistency** of the sauce before proceeding to the next stage of preparation.
   厨师坚持要将酱汁调到完美的稠度后，才进入下一步的准备工作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "consist-ency" — what something "consists" of stays the same, showing steady and reliable uniformity.
把"consistency"想成"consist（组成）+ ency"——组成保持不变，体现出稳定可靠的一致性。
