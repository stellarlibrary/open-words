# 📎 addendum

🔊 /əˈden.dəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An item of additional material added at the end of a book or other publication; a supplement or appendix
**中文：** n. 附录，附件；补遗；附加物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appendix（附录）
- supplement（补编）
- attachment（附件）
- postscript（附言）
- additive（附加物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **addendum to a contract / addendum to a report / issue an addendum / include an addendum**

👉 Example: The publisher released an addendum to correct several factual errors in the original edition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legal team prepared an **addendum** to the contract that addressed all newly negotiated terms.
   法律团队准备了一份合同附录，涵盖了所有新谈判的条款。

2. An **addendum** was appended to the dissertation to include data collected after the initial submission.
   论文中附加了一份补遗，以包含初次提交后收集的数据。

3. The committee issued an **addendum** clarifying the revised eligibility criteria for grant applicants.
   委员会发布了一份附录，澄清了资助申请人修订后的资格标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "addendum" as "add-end-um" — something you ADD at the END. It comes from Latin "addere" meaning to add, so an addendum is literally something to be added.
把"addendum"想成"add-end-um"——在末尾ADD（添加）的东西。它源自拉丁语"addere"，意为添加，所以addendum就是要被添加的内容。
