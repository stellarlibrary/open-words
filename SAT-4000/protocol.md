# 📜 protocol

🔊 /ˈproʊtəˌkɑːl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A set of rules governing formal occasions, diplomatic etiquette, or the procedures for data exchange; a formal agreement or treaty draft
**中文：** n. 协议；草案；礼仪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- convention（惯例）
- procedure（程序）
- etiquette（礼仪）
- agreement（协议）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **follow protocol / breach of protocol / diplomatic protocol / security protocol**

👉 Example: All diplomatic staff must follow strict protocol when addressing foreign heads of state.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team established a rigorous **protocol** to ensure the validity and reproducibility of their experimental findings.
   研究团队制定了严格的协议以确保其实验结果的有效性和可重复性。

2. Violating established **protocol** during the summit caused a significant diplomatic incident between the two nations.
   在峰会期间违反既定礼仪引发了两国之间一场严重的外交事件。

3. The hospital implemented a new emergency **protocol** requiring all staff to complete mandatory training within thirty days.
   医院实施了一项新的紧急方案，要求所有员工在三十天内完成强制培训。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proto-col" as "first column" — the first column of rules you must follow before anything else happens.
把"protocol"想成"proto（最初的）+ col（栏目）"——最初的规则栏目，即必须首先遵守的协议和礼仪。
