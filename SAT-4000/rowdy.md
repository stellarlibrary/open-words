# 🗣️ rowdy

🔊 /ˈraʊdi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Noisy and disorderly; rough and unruly. (n.) A rough, disorderly person; a troublemaker
**中文：** n. 无赖，凶暴的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boisterous（喧闹的）
- unruly（难以控制的）
- disorderly（混乱的）
- raucous（嘈杂的）
- turbulent（骚动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rowdy behavior / rowdy crowd / rowdy party / get rowdy**

👉 Example: The security guards were called to manage the rowdy spectators who disrupted the ceremony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor refused to continue the lecture until the **rowdy** students in the back of the hall settled down.
   教授拒绝继续讲课，直到大厅后排吵闹的学生安静下来。

2. Authorities deployed additional police officers to control the **rowdy** demonstrators who had gathered outside the courthouse.
   当局部署了额外的警力来控制聚集在法院外面的粗暴示威者。

3. What began as a peaceful celebration gradually turned into a **rowdy** affair as the night progressed and tempers flared.
   原本平和的庆祝活动随着夜幕深沉和情绪激动而逐渐变成了一场喧闹的事件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rowdy" as "row + dy" — a "row" is a quarrel or commotion, so a rowdy person is someone who causes rows.
把"rowdy"想成"row（争吵）+ dy"——row是争吵或骚动的意思，所以rowdy就是制造争吵的人。
