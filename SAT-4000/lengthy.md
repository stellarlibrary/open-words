# 🕰️ lengthy

🔊 /ˈlɛŋθi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lasting a long time; excessively long or tedious; verbose or wordy in expression
**中文：** adj. 漫长的，冗长的；啰唆的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prolonged（持久的）
- protracted（拖长的）
- verbose（冗长的）
- tedious（乏味的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lengthy process / lengthy discussion / lengthy delay / lengthy document**

👉 Example: The lengthy negotiations between the two nations finally culminated in a historic trade agreement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After a **lengthy** deliberation, the jury returned a unanimous verdict of not guilty on all charges.
   经过漫长的审议，陪审团对所有指控作出了一致的无罪裁决。

2. The professor's **lengthy** lecture on quantum mechanics left many students feeling exhausted and overwhelmed.
   教授关于量子力学的冗长讲座让许多学生感到疲惫不堪。

3. Approval of the new pharmaceutical drug required a **lengthy** review process by the regulatory authorities.
   新药的批准需要监管机构进行漫长的审查程序。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lengthy" as "length + y" — something that has too much length, dragging on and on.
把"lengthy"想成"length（长度）+ y"——某件事太长了，拖得没完没了。
