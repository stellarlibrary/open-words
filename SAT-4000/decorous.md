# 🎩 decorous

🔊 /ˈdekərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Characterized by propriety, politeness, and good taste in behavior or appearance; dignified and proper
**中文：** adj. 有礼貌的，高雅的；端正的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proper（得体的）
- dignified（庄重的）
- refined（优雅的）
- courteous（有礼貌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **decorous behavior / decorous manner / decorous appearance / decorous conduct**

👉 Example: Guests at the formal banquet maintained a decorous demeanor throughout the entire evening.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **decorous** conduct at the diplomatic reception earned widespread admiration from foreign dignitaries and officials.
   大使在外交招待会上得体的举止赢得了外国政要和官员的广泛赞赏。

2. The students were reminded to maintain a **decorous** appearance and respectful attitude during the official graduation ceremony.
   学生们被提醒在正式毕业典礼上保持端庄的仪表和尊重的态度。

3. Despite the heated debate, the panel members remained **decorous** and addressed each opposing argument with civility and restraint.
   尽管辩论激烈，小组成员仍保持有礼的态度，以文明和克制回应每一个反对意见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "decorous" as related to "decor" — just as good decor makes a room beautiful, decorous behavior makes a person dignified and elegant.
把"decorous"想成与"decor（装饰）"相关——就像好的装饰让房间变得美丽，得体的行为让一个人显得高雅而有尊严。
