# ⚠️ abusive

🔊 /əˈbjuːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Using harsh, insulting language; involving the cruel mistreatment of someone; characterized by improper or excessive use
**中文：** adj. 辱骂的；滥用的；虐待的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insulting（侮辱的）
- offensive（冒犯的）
- cruel（残忍的）
- exploitative（剥削的）
- harsh（严厉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abusive language / abusive behavior / abusive relationship / abusive practices / verbally abusive**

👉 Example: The organization launched a campaign to raise awareness about abusive working conditions in factories.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court issued a restraining order against the man after his former partner reported years of **abusive** behavior.
   在其前伴侣举报了多年的虐待行为后，法院对该男子发出了限制令。

2. Regulators intervened to stop the company's **abusive** pricing practices that exploited vulnerable consumers unfairly.
   监管机构介入，制止了该公司不公平地剥削弱势消费者的滥用定价行为。

3. The referee ejected the player from the match for directing **abusive** language at the officiating team.
   裁判因该球员对裁判组使用辱骂性语言而将其驱逐出比赛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abusive" as "ab-USE-ive" — when someone AB-USES power or words, their behavior becomes abusive.
把"abusive"想成"ab-USE-ive"——当某人滥用(abuse)权力或言语时，他们的行为就是abusive的。
