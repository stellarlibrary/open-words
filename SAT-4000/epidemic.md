# 🦠 epidemic

🔊 /ˌepɪˈdemɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Widespread among a community at a particular time. (n.) A widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community
**中文：** adj. 流行性的 n. 流行病；传染病

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outbreak（爆发）
- plague（瘟疫）
- pandemic（大流行病）
- contagion（传染）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **epidemic proportions / epidemic outbreak / epidemic disease / contain an epidemic**

👉 Example: The opioid crisis has reached epidemic proportions in many rural communities across the country.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public health officials worked tirelessly to contain the **epidemic** before it could spread to neighboring provinces and urban centers.
   公共卫生官员不知疲倦地工作，以在**疫情**蔓延到邻近省份和城市中心之前加以控制。

2. The **epidemic** of misinformation on social media platforms poses a serious threat to democratic institutions and public trust.
   社交媒体平台上虚假信息的**泛滥**对民主制度和公众信任构成了严重威胁。

3. Historians have documented how the cholera **epidemic** of the nineteenth century fundamentally transformed urban sanitation and public health policy.
   历史学家记录了十九世纪霍乱**疫情**如何从根本上改变了城市卫生和公共卫生政策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EPI-DEMIC" as "epi (upon) + demos (people)" — something that falls upon the people, spreading widely.
把"epidemic"拆分为"epi（在...之上）+ demos（人民）"——落在人民身上的东西，即广泛传播的疾病。
