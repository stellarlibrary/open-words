# 😤 rile

🔊 /raɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To irritate or annoy; to stir up or make turbid
**中文：** v. 搅混；惹怒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritate（激怒）
- provoke（挑衅）
- aggravate（使恼火）
- vex（使烦恼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rile someone up / rile the crowd / easily riled / get riled**

👉 Example: His dismissive comments were enough to rile even the most composed members of the committee.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's inflammatory remarks were clearly intended to **rile** the opposition and provoke a heated debate.
   那位参议员的煽动性言论显然意在激怒反对派并引发激烈辩论。

2. Nothing could **rile** the seasoned diplomat, whose composure remained unshaken throughout the tense negotiations.
   没有什么能激怒这位经验丰富的外交官，他在紧张的谈判中始终保持镇定。

3. The controversial editorial was designed to **rile** readers and generate widespread discussion across social media platforms.
   这篇有争议的社论旨在激怒读者，并在社交媒体平台上引发广泛讨论。

---

Think of "rile" as rhyming with "file" — imagine someone messing up your carefully organized files, which would certainly rile you up.
把"rile"想成和"file"押韵——想象有人弄乱了你精心整理的文件，这肯定会惹怒你。
