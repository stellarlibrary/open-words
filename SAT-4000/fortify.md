# 🛡️ fortify

🔊 /ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To strengthen or reinforce a place, structure, or position; to invigorate or encourage mentally or physically
**中文：** v. 加强；增强；在……设要塞；在……建防御工事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strengthen（加强）
- reinforce（增强）
- bolster（支撑）
- shore up（支持）
- brace（加固）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fortify a position / fortify oneself / fortify with vitamins / fortified city / fortified wine**

👉 Example: The army moved quickly to fortify the border against potential incursions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government allocated substantial funds to **fortify** the coastal defenses before the approaching hurricane season began.
   政府拨出大量资金在即将到来的飓风季节开始之前加固沿海防御设施。

2. Many cereal manufacturers **fortify** their products with essential vitamins and minerals to address common nutritional deficiencies.
   许多谷物制造商在其产品中添加必需的维生素和矿物质，以解决常见的营养缺乏问题。

3. She sought to **fortify** her argument with extensive empirical data gathered from multiple independent longitudinal studies.
   她试图用从多项独立纵向研究中收集的大量实证数据来加强她的论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fort-ify" as making something into a "fort" — a strong, defended place. To fortify is to make something as strong as a fort.
把"fortify"想成把某物变成"fort"（堡垒）——一个坚固的、有防御的地方。加强就是让某物像堡垒一样坚固。
