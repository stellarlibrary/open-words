# 🌐 sphere

🔊 /sfɪr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A round three-dimensional geometric shape; a field or area of activity, interest, or influence. (v.) To enclose in a sphere; to form into a sphere. (adj.) Spherical in form.
**中文：** n. 球体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- globe（球体）
- orb（球）
- domain（领域）
- realm（范围）
- field（领域）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sphere of influence / public sphere / sphere of activity / celestial sphere / outside one's sphere**

👉 Example: International diplomacy often involves negotiations over competing spheres of influence among major powers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that education falls within the **sphere** of public responsibility rather than private enterprise alone.
   这位哲学家认为教育属于公共责任的范围，而非仅仅是私人企业的事务。

2. The Earth is not a perfect **sphere** but rather an oblate spheroid that is slightly flattened at the poles.
   地球并非一个完美的球体，而是一个在两极略扁的扁球体。

3. Her expertise in molecular biology extended well beyond the traditional **sphere** of academic research into practical applications.
   她在分子生物学方面的专业知识远远超出了传统学术研究的范围，延伸到了实际应用领域。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sphere" as the shape of the atmosphere — the "atmo-sphere" is literally the air sphere surrounding the Earth.
把"sphere"联想到"atmosphere"（大气层）——大气层字面意思就是包围地球的空气球体。
