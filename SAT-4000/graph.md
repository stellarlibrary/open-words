# 📊 graph

🔊 /ɡræf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A diagram showing the relation between variable quantities, typically plotted along two axes
**中文：** n. 图表；曲线图

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chart（图表）
- diagram（图解）
- plot（曲线图）
- figure（图形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bar graph / line graph / plot a graph / graph shows / according to the graph**

👉 Example: The graph clearly illustrated the steady decline in unemployment rates over the past decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **graph** presented in the research paper demonstrated a strong positive correlation between education levels and income.
   研究论文中的图表显示了教育水平与收入之间的强正相关关系。

2. Students were asked to construct a **graph** representing the population growth trends observed across three continents.
   学生们被要求绘制一张图表，表示在三个大洲观察到的人口增长趋势。

3. According to the **graph**, global temperatures have risen significantly since the beginning of the industrial revolution.
   根据该曲线图，自工业革命开始以来，全球气温已显著上升。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "graph" as related to "graphic" — both come from the Greek "graphein" meaning "to write or draw," so a graph is something drawn to display data.
把"graph"和"graphic"联系起来——两者都源自希腊语"graphein"，意为"书写或绘画"，所以graph就是用图画来展示数据。
