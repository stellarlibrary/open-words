# 🌿 swamp

🔊 /swɑːmp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A marsh; wetland. (v.) To flood or sink; to overwhelm with difficulties
**中文：** n. 沼泽，沼地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- marsh（沼泽）
- bog（泥沼）
- wetland（湿地）
- overwhelm（淹没）
- inundate（使泛滥）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **swamp with requests / swamped by work / drain the swamp / swamp land**

👉 Example: The customer service department was swamped with complaints after the product recall was announced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The relentless floodwaters threatened to **swamp** the coastal villages, forcing thousands of residents to evacuate immediately.
   无情的洪水威胁着沿海村庄，迫使数千名居民立即撤离。

2. Ecologists discovered several rare amphibian species thriving in the protected **swamp** adjacent to the national forest reserve.
   生态学家在国家森林保护区旁边的受保护沼泽中发现了几种珍稀两栖动物。

3. The administration was **swamped** by an unprecedented volume of applications, causing significant delays in the review process.
   行政部门被空前数量的申请所淹没，导致审查过程严重延迟。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "swamp" — water that "swamps" the land, covering everything like a soggy, waterlogged trap.
想想"swamp"——水"淹没"了土地，像一个湿透的水潭一样覆盖一切。
