# 🏠 habitable

🔊 /ˈhæbɪtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Suitable or fit for living in; capable of supporting human habitation
**中文：** adj. 可居住的；适于居住的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- livable（宜居的）
- inhabitable（可居住的）
- occupiable（可占用的）
- residential（适合居住的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **habitable zone / habitable conditions / habitable planet / barely habitable**

👉 Example: Scientists search for planets within the habitable zone where liquid water could potentially exist.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Astronomers have identified several exoplanets within the **habitable** zone that may possess conditions favorable for life.
   天文学家已在宜居带内发现了几颗可能拥有有利于生命存在条件的系外行星。

2. After extensive renovation, the previously condemned building was finally deemed **habitable** by the city inspection authorities.
   经过大规模翻新后，这座先前被判定为危房的建筑终于被市检查部门认定为可居住。

3. Climate change threatens to render entire coastal regions barely **habitable** within the coming decades due to rising sea levels.
   由于海平面上升，气候变化可能在未来几十年内使整个沿海地区几乎不宜居住。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "habitable" as "habit + able" — a place where you are able to form habits of daily living, meaning you can live there.
把"habitable"想成"habit（习惯）+ able（能够）"——一个你能养成日常生活习惯的地方，意味着你可以住在那里。
