# 🎩 propriety

🔊 /prəˈpraɪəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being proper or appropriate; conformity to accepted standards of behavior or morals; the rules of correct social behavior
**中文：** n. 适当，正当，得体；礼节，礼仪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decorum（礼仪）
- decency（得体）
- etiquette（礼节）
- correctness（正确性）
- seemliness（合宜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sense of propriety / breach of propriety / observe propriety / with propriety**

👉 Example: The diplomat always conducted herself with the utmost propriety during formal state events.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board questioned whether the chairman's remarks violated basic standards of **propriety** expected in professional settings.
   董事会质疑主席的言论是否违反了专业场合中应有的基本行为规范。

2. Victorian society placed enormous emphasis on **propriety**, dictating strict rules about dress, speech, and social conduct.
   维多利亚时代的社会极其重视礼仪，对着装、言谈和社交行为制定了严格的规则。

3. The professor reminded students that academic **propriety** requires proper citation of all sources used in research papers.
   教授提醒学生，学术规范要求在研究论文中正确引用所有参考来源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "propriety" as "proper-iety" — it's about being PROPER in behavior and following social rules of decency.
把"propriety"想成"proper-iety"——它关于行为的得体与合宜，遵循社会礼仪规范。
