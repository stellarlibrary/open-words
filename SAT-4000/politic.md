# 🎯 politic

🔊 /ˈpɑːlɪtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing good judgment; shrewd and tactful in dealing with others; prudent and expedient in practical matters
**中文：** adj. 精明的；有策略的；审慎的；狡猾的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shrewd（精明的）
- prudent（审慎的）
- tactful（得体的）
- diplomatic（圆滑的）
- judicious（明智的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **body politic / politic decision / politic move / it would be politic to**

👉 Example: It would be politic to wait until tensions subside before raising the contentious issue again.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador made the **politic** decision to postpone the announcement until both parties had reached a preliminary agreement.
   大使做出了精明的决定，将公告推迟到双方达成初步协议之后。

2. It was hardly **politic** of the senator to criticize her own party's platform during a nationally televised prime-time debate.
   参议员在全国直播的黄金时段辩论中批评自己党的纲领实在不够明智。

3. A **politic** leader understands when to compromise and when to stand firm on matters of fundamental principle and policy.
   一位精明的领导者懂得何时妥协，何时在基本原则和政策问题上坚持立场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "politic" as being skilled in "politics" — a politic person knows how to navigate tricky situations with shrewdness and tact.
把"politic"想成擅长"politics（政治）"的人——精明的人知道如何机智地应对棘手局面。
