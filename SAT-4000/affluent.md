# 🏛️ affluent

🔊 /ˈæfluənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a great deal of wealth; abundant in supply. (n.) A tributary stream; a wealthy person.
**中文：** (adj.) 富裕的；丰富的 (n.) 支流；富人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wealthy（富有的）
- prosperous（繁荣的）
- opulent（奢华的）
- well-to-do（富足的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **affluent neighborhood, affluent society, affluent families, affluent lifestyle**
👉 Example: The affluent neighborhood featured grand estates and meticulously maintained gardens.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The survey revealed that **affluent** households spend considerably more on education than average-income families.
   调查显示，**affluent**（富裕的）家庭在教育上的支出远高于中等收入家庭。

2. Many **affluent** donors contributed generously to the foundation's charitable programs throughout the fiscal year.
   许多富裕的捐赠者在整个财年中慷慨地资助了该基金会的慈善项目。

3. The city's most **affluent** district attracted international investors seeking premium commercial real estate opportunities.
   该市最富裕的地区吸引了寻求优质商业地产机会的国际投资者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Affluent" shares its root with "affluence" — think of wealth flowing ("flu" from Latin "fluere") abundantly toward someone.
"Affluent"与"affluence"同根——想象财富源源不断地流向（"flu"来自拉丁语"fluere"，意为流动）某人。
