# 🔗 transmission

🔊 /trænzˈmɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act or process of sending or passing something from one place, person, or thing to another; a device in a vehicle that transmits power from the engine to the wheels; a broadcast
**中文：** n. 传动装置；传递；播送；传输

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conveyance（传送）
- transfer（传递）
- broadcast（播送）
- dissemination（传播）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transmission of disease / data transmission / automatic transmission / transmission line**

👉 Example: The transmission of information across networks has fundamentally reshaped modern commerce.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **transmission** of infectious diseases can be significantly reduced through proper sanitation and public health measures.
   传染病的传播可以通过适当的卫生设施和公共卫生措施大幅减少。

2. Engineers redesigned the vehicle's **transmission** system to improve fuel efficiency and reduce mechanical wear over time.
   工程师重新设计了车辆的传动系统，以提高燃油效率并减少长期机械磨损。

3. The live **transmission** of the presidential debate attracted an audience of over fifty million viewers nationwide.
   总统辩论的现场转播吸引了全国超过五千万观众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-mission" as "a mission to transfer across" — something being sent across from one place to another.
把"transmission"想成"trans（跨越）+ mission（任务）"——一项跨越传输的任务。
