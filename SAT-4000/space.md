# 🚀 space

🔊 /speɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A continuous area or expanse that is free or unoccupied; the universe beyond the earth's atmosphere; the distance between two points. (v.) To position items at intervals; to leave gaps between
**中文：** n. 空间

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expanse（广阔空间）
- room（空间）
- cosmos（宇宙）
- interval（间隔）
- gap（间距）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outer space / space out / personal space / office space / breathing space**

👉 Example: The architect designed the building to maximize open space and allow natural light to fill every room.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international collaboration to explore outer **space** has yielded remarkable discoveries about the formation of distant galaxies.
   探索外太空的国际合作已经产生了关于遥远星系形成的重大发现。

2. Urban planners emphasized the importance of preserving green **space** within densely populated metropolitan areas for public well-being.
   城市规划者强调了在人口密集的大都市区域保留绿色空间对公众福祉的重要性。

3. The typographer carefully **spaced** each letter to ensure optimal readability and visual harmony throughout the printed document.
   排版师仔细地间隔每个字母，以确保整个印刷文件具有最佳的可读性和视觉和谐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "space" as the "place" with an "s" — every place needs space, from a room in your house to the vast cosmos above.
把"space"想成带"s"的"place"——每个地方都需要空间，从你家的房间到头顶广阔的宇宙。
