# 🔬 experiment

🔊 /ɪkˈspɛrɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A scientific test or trial designed to discover or demonstrate something. (v.) To try something new; to conduct a systematic investigation
**中文：** v. 尝试；进行实验 n. 实验，试验

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trial（试验）
- test（测试）
- investigation（调查）
- exploration（探索）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conduct an experiment / experiment with / a controlled experiment / experiment on**

👉 Example: The biology students were eager to experiment with different variables in the laboratory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers designed a rigorous **experiment** to test the efficacy of the new pharmaceutical compound on patients.
   研究人员设计了一项严格的实验来测试这种新药物化合物对患者的疗效。

2. The professor encouraged her students to **experiment** with unconventional methods rather than relying on established procedures.
   教授鼓励学生们尝试非常规的方法，而不是依赖既定的程序。

3. A landmark **experiment** conducted at the university conclusively demonstrated the relationship between sleep deprivation and cognitive decline.
   该大学进行的一项具有里程碑意义的实验确凿地证明了睡眠不足与认知能力下降之间的关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "experi-ment" as containing "experience" — an experiment is a way to gain experience through systematic testing and observation.
把"experiment"想成包含"experience"——实验是一种通过系统测试和观察来获取经验的方式。
