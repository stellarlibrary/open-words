# 🔍 authenticity

🔊 /ˌɔːθɛnˈtɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being authentic, genuine, or true to its origin; the degree to which something is reliable, trustworthy, or accurately represents what it claims to be.
**中文：** 真实性，确实性；可靠性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genuineness（真实性）
- legitimacy（合法性）
- validity（有效性）
- veracity（真实）
- credibility（可信度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **authenticity of a document/claim**, **question the authenticity**, **verify/confirm authenticity**, **cultural authenticity**

👉 Example: Experts were called upon to verify the authenticity of the ancient scroll before the auction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forensic laboratory employed advanced carbon dating techniques to determine the **authenticity** of the archaeological artifacts.
   法医实验室采用先进的碳定年技术来确定考古文物的真实性。

2. Critics questioned the **authenticity** of the memoir after several key claims were contradicted by historical evidence.
   在几项关键说法被历史证据反驳后，评论家们质疑了这本回忆录的真实性。

3. The brand built its global reputation on the **authenticity** of its craftsmanship and commitment to traditional methods.
   该品牌凭借其工艺的真实性和对传统方法的坚持建立了全球声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Authenticity" = "authentic" + "-ity" (the state of being). If you can prove the author is real, you prove authenticity. Think of a detective verifying whether a painting is the real deal.
"Authenticity"="authentic"+"ity"（……的状态）。如果你能证明作者是真的，你就证明了authenticity。想象一位侦探在验证一幅画是否为真迹。
