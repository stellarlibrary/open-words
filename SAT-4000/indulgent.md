# 🍬 indulgent

🔊 /ɪnˈdʌldʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing a tendency to be overly generous or lenient; willing to allow excessive pleasure or satisfaction
**中文：** adj. 放纵的；宽容的；纵容的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lenient（宽容的）
- permissive（放任的）
- tolerant（容忍的）
- doting（溺爱的）
- forbearing（忍耐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indulgent parent / indulgent smile / indulgent attitude / self-indulgent behavior**

👉 Example: The indulgent grandmother never refused any of her grandchildren's requests, no matter how unreasonable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argued that the **indulgent** parenting style prevalent among affluent families contributed to rising entitlement among younger generations.
   批评者认为，富裕家庭中盛行的纵容型教养方式导致了年轻一代日益增长的特权意识。

2. The professor adopted an **indulgent** tone when addressing the student's naive but earnest questions about advanced quantum mechanics.
   教授在回答学生关于高等量子力学幼稚但真诚的问题时，采用了宽容的语气。

3. Her **indulgent** spending habits eventually depleted the family savings and necessitated a complete restructuring of their financial plan.
   她放纵的消费习惯最终耗尽了家庭积蓄，迫使他们对财务计划进行全面重组。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "INDULGE-nt" as someone who loves to "indulge" — an indulgent person freely gives in to desires, either their own or others'.
把"indulgent"想成喜欢"indulge(纵容)"的人——一个纵容的人总是轻易满足欲望，无论是自己的还是别人的。
