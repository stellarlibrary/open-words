# 🏛️ incontrovertible

🔊 /ˌɪnkɒntrəˈvɜːtɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not able to be denied, questioned, or disputed; absolutely certain and undeniable
**中文：** adj. 无可争议的；无疑的；明白的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indisputable（不容置疑的）
- irrefutable（无可辩驳的）
- undeniable（不可否认的）
- unquestionable（毋庸置疑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incontrovertible evidence / incontrovertible fact / incontrovertible proof / incontrovertible truth**

👉 Example: The prosecution presented incontrovertible evidence that left the jury with no room for reasonable doubt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The DNA analysis provided **incontrovertible** proof of the suspect's presence at the scene of the crime.
   DNA分析提供了嫌疑人在犯罪现场出现的无可争议的证据。

2. Climate scientists assert that the data supporting global temperature increases is now **incontrovertible** and beyond dispute.
   气候科学家断言，支持全球气温上升的数据现在已无可争议且不容置疑。

3. The archaeological discovery offered **incontrovertible** evidence that the ancient civilization had developed advanced metallurgical techniques.
   这一考古发现提供了无可辩驳的证据，证明该古代文明已经发展出先进的冶金技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-controvert-ible" as "not able to be controverted" — a controversy requires two sides, but this evidence is so strong nobody can argue against it.
把"incontrovertible"拆分为"in（不能）+ controvert（反驳）+ ible（能够）"——不能被反驳的，就是无可争议的。
