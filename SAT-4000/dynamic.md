# ⚡ dynamic

🔊 /daɪˈnæmɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Characterized by constant change, activity, or progress; relating to forces that produce motion. (n.) A force or process that stimulates change or progress
**中文：** adj. 动态的；动力的；充满活力的 n. 动态；动力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- energetic（精力充沛的）
- vigorous（充满活力的）
- active（活跃的）
- forceful（有力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dynamic environment / dynamic personality / group dynamic / power dynamic**

👉 Example: The dynamic nature of the technology sector demands continuous adaptation and innovation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **dynamic** teaching style captivated students and transformed the lecture hall into an engaging learning environment.
   教授充满活力的教学风格吸引了学生，将讲堂变成了一个引人入胜的学习环境。

2. Understanding the **dynamic** between supply and demand is essential for anyone pursuing a career in economics.
   理解供需之间的动态关系对于任何从事经济学职业的人来说都至关重要。

3. The region's **dynamic** economy attracted substantial foreign investment and fostered rapid technological development over the decade.
   该地区充满活力的经济吸引了大量外国投资，并在十年间促进了快速的技术发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dynamic" as related to "dynamite" — both come from the Greek "dynamis" meaning power, full of explosive energy and force.
把"dynamic"和"dynamite（炸药）"联系起来——两者都来自希腊语"dynamis"（力量），充满爆发性的能量和力量。
