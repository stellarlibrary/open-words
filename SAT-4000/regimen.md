# 💪 regimen

🔊 /ˈredʒɪmən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A prescribed course of medical treatment, diet, or exercise for the promotion or restoration of health; a systematic plan or routine.
**中文：** n. 养生法；生活规则；养生之道

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- routine（日常规律）
- program（方案）
- schedule（计划安排）
- discipline（训练方法）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strict regimen / daily regimen / exercise regimen / health regimen / skincare regimen**

👉 Example: The doctor prescribed a strict regimen of medication and physical therapy for the patient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician recommended a rigorous dietary **regimen** to help the patient manage chronic inflammation and reduce symptoms.
   医生建议了一套严格的饮食养生方案，以帮助患者控制慢性炎症并减轻症状。

2. Olympic athletes typically follow an intensive training **regimen** that demands extraordinary physical discipline and mental resilience.
   奥林匹克运动员通常遵循一套高强度的训练方案，这要求非凡的身体自律和精神韧性。

3. Adhering to a consistent skincare **regimen** can significantly improve complexion and slow the visible signs of premature aging.
   坚持一套稳定的护肤养生法可以显著改善肤色，并延缓过早衰老的明显迹象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "regimen" as "regime + n" — like a personal "regime" or structured system you follow for your health and daily habits.
把"regimen"想成"regime + n"——就像一个你为健康和日常习惯而遵循的个人"制度"或结构化体系。
