# 🚫 intolerable

🔊 /ɪnˈtɑːlərəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unable to be endured; too extreme, painful, or difficult to bear; exceeding the limits of what is acceptable
**中文：** adj. 无法忍受的；难耐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unbearable（难以忍受的）
- insufferable（不可容忍的）
- unendurable（忍受不了的）
- excruciating（极度痛苦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intolerable conditions / intolerable pain / intolerable burden / find something intolerable**

👉 Example: The workers declared the unsafe conditions at the factory to be intolerable and demanded immediate reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugees fled their homeland because the political persecution and economic hardship had become absolutely **intolerable** for ordinary citizens.
   难民逃离了祖国，因为政治迫害和经济困难对普通公民来说已变得完全无法忍受。

2. The committee found the level of corruption within the organization to be **intolerable** and recommended sweeping institutional reforms immediately.
   委员会认为该组织内部的腐败程度已不可容忍，并建议立即进行全面的制度改革。

3. Living near the construction site became **intolerable** due to the relentless noise and vibrations that persisted well into the evening hours.
   由于持续不断的噪音和震动一直延续到深夜，住在工地附近变得令人难以忍受。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-tolerable" — the prefix "in-" means "not," and "tolerable" means bearable. So intolerable literally means "not bearable."
把"intolerable"想成"in-"（不）+ "tolerable"（可忍受的）——不可忍受的，字面意思就是"无法承受的"。
