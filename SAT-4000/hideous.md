# 😱 hideous

🔊 /ˈhɪdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely ugly or unpleasant; causing horror or disgust; shockingly dreadful or revolting
**中文：** adj. 可怕的；丑恶的；骇人听闻的；令人厌恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grotesque（怪诞的）
- horrifying（令人恐惧的）
- repulsive（令人厌恶的）
- ghastly（可怕的）
- monstrous（骇人的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hideous crime / hideous appearance / hideous sight / hideous truth / hideous mistake**

👉 Example: The investigators described the scene of the crime as absolutely hideous and deeply disturbing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary exposed the **hideous** conditions inside the factory, prompting immediate government investigations and sweeping regulatory reforms.
   这部纪录片揭露了工厂内部骇人听闻的条件，促使政府立即展开调查并进行全面的监管改革。

2. Critics universally condemned the **hideous** architectural design, arguing that it clashed violently with the historic character of the surrounding neighborhood.
   评论家们一致谴责这一丑陋的建筑设计，认为它与周围社区的历史特色严重冲突。

3. The witness provided a **hideous** account of the disaster that left the entire courtroom audience visibly shaken and profoundly disturbed.
   目击者对灾难的骇人叙述让整个法庭旁听人员明显震惊且深感不安。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hide" + "ous" — something so ugly you want to hide from it; it makes you want to look away immediately.
把"hideous"想成"hide"（躲藏）+"ous"——丑到你想躲起来不看，让你想立刻移开目光。
