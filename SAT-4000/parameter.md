# 📊 parameter

🔊 /pəˈræmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A measurable factor or variable that defines a system or sets the conditions of its operation; a limit or boundary
**中文：** n. 参数；系数；参量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- variable（变量）
- criterion（标准）
- guideline（准则）
- specification（规格）
- boundary（界限）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **set parameters / within the parameters / key parameter / parameter of / adjust the parameters**

👉 Example: The engineers had to work within the strict parameters established by the safety regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team carefully defined the **parameters** of the study to ensure that the experimental results would be both valid and reproducible.
   研究团队仔细界定了研究的参数，以确保实验结果既有效又可重复。

2. Budget constraints serve as the primary **parameter** that determines the scope and feasibility of most large-scale public infrastructure projects.
   预算限制是决定大多数大型公共基础设施项目范围和可行性的主要参数。

3. The software developer adjusted several critical **parameters** in the algorithm to improve its accuracy in predicting consumer behavior patterns.
   软件开发人员调整了算法中几个关键参数，以提高其预测消费者行为模式的准确性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "para-meter" as a "meter" that measures what is "para" (beside) — it measures the boundaries and limits that define a system.
把"parameter"想成一个测量"para"（旁边）的"meter"（仪表）——它测量定义系统的边界和限制。
