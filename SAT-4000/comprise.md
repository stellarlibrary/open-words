# 🧱 comprise

🔊 /kəmˈpraɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To consist of; to be made up of; to include or contain as parts of a whole
**中文：** v. 包含；包括；由……组成；构成

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consist of（由……组成）
- include（包括）
- contain（包含）
- encompass（涵盖）
- constitute（构成）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **comprise of (informal) / is comprised of / comprises several parts**

👉 Example: The committee comprises twelve members drawn from various academic departments across the university.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthology **comprises** thirty essays written by leading scholars in the field of contemporary philosophy.
   这本文集包含三十篇由当代哲学领域顶尖学者撰写的论文。

2. Women now **comprise** nearly half of the workforce in many industrialized nations around the world.
   女性现在在全球许多工业化国家中占据了近一半的劳动力。

3. The national park **comprises** diverse ecosystems, including wetlands, forests, and alpine meadows stretching across vast territory.
   该国家公园包含多种生态系统，包括湿地、森林和绵延广阔领土的高山草甸。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-prize" — the whole com(es with) a prize of many parts. A whole comprises its parts.
把"comprise"想成"com（共同）+ prise（拿取）"——把各部分共同拿到一起就组成了整体。
