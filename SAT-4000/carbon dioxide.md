# 🌍 carbon dioxide

🔊 /ˌkɑːrbən daɪˈɒksaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, odorless gas (CO₂) produced by respiration, combustion, and organic decomposition; a key greenhouse gas in Earth's atmosphere
**中文：** n. 二氧化碳

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- CO₂（二氧化碳）
- carbonic acid gas（碳酸气）
- greenhouse gas（温室气体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **carbon dioxide emissions / carbon dioxide levels / reduce carbon dioxide / release carbon dioxide / atmospheric carbon dioxide**

👉 Example: Rising levels of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere are a primary driver of global climate change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists have confirmed that atmospheric **carbon dioxide** concentrations have reached unprecedented levels in recorded geological history.
   科学家已确认大气中二氧化碳浓度已达到有记录的地质历史中前所未有的水平。

2. Photosynthesis enables plants to absorb **carbon dioxide** and convert it into oxygen and organic compounds essential for life.
   光合作用使植物能够吸收二氧化碳并将其转化为氧气和生命所必需的有机化合物。

3. International agreements aim to limit **carbon dioxide** emissions through coordinated regulatory frameworks and renewable energy investments.
   国际协议旨在通过协调的监管框架和可再生能源投资来限制二氧化碳排放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carbon dioxide" as "carbon + di-oxide" — "di" means two, so it is one carbon atom bonded with two oxygen atoms (CO₂).
把"carbon dioxide"想成"carbon（碳）+ di(两个)-oxide（氧化物）"——"di"表示二，即一个碳原子与两个氧原子结合（CO₂）。
