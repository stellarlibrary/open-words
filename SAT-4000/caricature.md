# 🎭 caricature

🔊 /ˈkærɪkətʃʊr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A picture, description, or imitation of a person in which certain striking characteristics are exaggerated for comic or grotesque effect. (v.) To make or give a comically exaggerated representation of someone
**中文：** n. 漫画；讽刺画；夸张的描绘 v. 画漫画讽刺；夸张地描绘

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- parody（模仿讽刺）
- satire（讽刺）
- lampoon（讽刺文）
- burlesque（滑稽模仿）
- travesty（歪曲的模仿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a caricature of someone / caricature an opponent / political caricature / crude caricature**

👉 Example: The editorial cartoon presented a cruel caricature of the newly elected mayor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political cartoonist drew a biting **caricature** of the senator that exaggerated his prominent features to devastating effect.
   这位政治漫画家画了一幅尖刻的参议员讽刺画，夸张地刻画了他的突出特征，效果极具冲击力。

2. Critics argued that the novel's antagonist was merely a shallow **caricature** rather than a fully realized literary character.
   评论家认为这部小说的反派角色只是一个肤浅的漫画式形象，而非一个丰满的文学人物。

3. The satirical newspaper routinely **caricatured** public officials to expose their hypocrisy and moral contradictions.
   这份讽刺报纸经常以漫画手法讽刺公职人员，以揭露他们的虚伪和道德矛盾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "caricature" as "carry a feature" — a caricature carries and exaggerates a person's most noticeable feature to make it look funny or absurd.
把"caricature"想成"carry a feature"（携带一个特征）——讽刺画把一个人最显著的特征放大夸张，使其看起来滑稽荒诞。
