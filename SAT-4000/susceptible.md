# 🎯 susceptible

🔊 /səˈsɛptəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Easily influenced or affected; capable of being moved emotionally; admitting of. (n.) A person prone to illness.
**中文：** adj. 敏感的，易受感动的，易受影响的，易感染的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vulnerable（脆弱的）
- prone（易于…的）
- impressionable（易受影响的）
- sensitive（敏感的）
- receptive（易接受的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **susceptible to disease / susceptible to influence / susceptible to flattery / highly susceptible**

👉 Example: Young children are particularly susceptible to respiratory infections during the winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Elderly patients are especially **susceptible** to complications from influenza and should receive vaccinations annually.
   老年患者特别容易出现流感并发症，应每年接种疫苗。

2. The coastal region remains highly **susceptible** to flooding due to its low elevation and inadequate drainage systems.
   由于海拔低和排水系统不完善，该沿海地区仍然极易遭受洪水侵袭。

3. Adolescents are often **susceptible** to peer pressure, which can significantly shape their behavioral choices and attitudes.
   青少年往往容易受到同伴压力的影响，这会显著塑造他们的行为选择和态度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "susceptible" as "sus-cept-ible" — able to "accept" (cept) things easily, making you open to influence or infection.
把"susceptible"想成"sus-cept-ible"——容易"接受"（cept）事物，使你容易受到影响或感染。
