# 🗽 autonomy

🔊 /ɔːˈtɒnəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The right or condition of self-government; the ability to make one's own decisions without external control.
**中文：** n. 自治，自治权；自主权

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- independence（独立）
- self-governance（自治）
- sovereignty（主权）
- self-determination（自决）
- freedom（自由）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grant autonomy / personal autonomy / regional autonomy / respect someone's autonomy**

👉 Example: The province demanded greater autonomy in managing its own economic and cultural affairs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The treaty granted the region full **autonomy** over its domestic policies while maintaining federal control of national defense.
   该条约授予该地区对其国内政策的完全自治权，同时保留联邦对国防的控制。

2. Psychologists emphasize that respecting a patient's **autonomy** is essential to building trust in the therapeutic relationship.
   心理学家强调，尊重患者的自主权对于建立治疗关系中的信任至关重要。

3. The university values academic **autonomy**, allowing professors to design their own curricula without administrative interference or oversight.
   该大学重视学术自主权，允许教授在没有行政干预或监督的情况下自行设计课程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Autonomy" = "auto" (self) + "nomy" (law/management) — self-management, the power to rule yourself.
"Autonomy"="auto"（自己）+"nomy"（法律/管理）——自我管理，自己统治自己的权力。
