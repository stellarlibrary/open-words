# 🔍 epitomize

🔊 /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To be a perfect example of a quality or type; to summarize or condense
**中文：** v. 摘要；概括；成为缩影；成为典型

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- embody（体现）
- exemplify（是…的典范）
- represent（代表）
- typify（是…的典型）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **epitomize the spirit of / epitomize excellence / epitomize the values of**

👉 Example: Her career achievements epitomize the spirit of perseverance and determination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's lifelong commitment to research and teaching **epitomizes** the highest ideals of academic excellence.
   这位教授毕生致力于研究和教学，是学术卓越最高理想的典型代表。

2. The historic accord between the two nations **epitomized** the possibility of diplomatic resolution even amid deep conflict.
   两国之间的历史性协议是即使在深刻冲突中也能通过外交解决问题的典范。

3. This innovative technology **epitomizes** the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence in contemporary scientific research.
   这项创新技术是当代科学研究中人工智能快速发展的缩影。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "epitomize" as "epitome + ize" — to make something into an epitome, to become the perfect example of something.
把"epitomize"想成"epitome + ize"——将某物变成缩影，成为某事物的完美典型。
