# 🌹 ruddy

🔊 /ˈrʌdi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb, verb | 形容词, 副词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Red or reddish; having a healthy reddish complexion. (adv.) Extremely; very. (v.) To make or become red
**中文：** adj. 红色的；红润的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rosy（红润的）
- flushed（发红的）
- reddish（微红的）
- florid（红润的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ruddy complexion / ruddy cheeks / ruddy glow / ruddy face**

👉 Example: The farmer's ruddy complexion was a testament to years of working outdoors in the sun and wind.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the brisk morning walk through the countryside, her cheeks displayed a healthy **ruddy** glow that radiated vitality.
   在乡间清晨快走之后，她的脸颊呈现出健康的红润光泽，散发着活力。

2. The artist carefully blended warm pigments to capture the **ruddy** hue of the autumn leaves along the riverbank.
   艺术家仔细调和暖色颜料，以捕捉河岸边秋叶的红色调。

3. The old sailor's **ruddy** face, weathered by decades of exposure to ocean winds, told stories of countless voyages.
   那位老水手红润而饱经风霜的脸庞，诉说着无数次航行的故事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ruddy" as "red + buddy" — your buddy's face turns red and ruddy after exercising in the cold.
把"ruddy"想成"red（红色）+ buddy（伙伴）"——你的伙伴在寒冷中锻炼后脸变得红润。
