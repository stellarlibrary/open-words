# 🧪 transmute

🔊 /trænzˈmjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To change in form, nature, or substance; to transform something into a different and often superior state
**中文：** v. 使变形；使变质；使变化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transform（转变）
- convert（转化）
- metamorphose（变态；变形）
- alter（改变）
- transfigure（使变形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transmute into / transmute base metals into gold / transmute experience into wisdom**

👉 Example: Medieval alchemists devoted their lives to attempting to transmute lead into gold.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist possessed a remarkable ability to **transmute** ordinary materials into extraordinary works of contemporary sculpture.
   这位艺术家拥有将普通材料转化为非凡当代雕塑作品的卓越能力。

2. Great literature has the power to **transmute** personal suffering into universal insights that resonate across generations.
   伟大的文学有能力将个人痛苦转化为跨越世代引起共鸣的普遍洞见。

3. Nuclear reactors **transmute** radioactive isotopes through controlled fission processes that release tremendous amounts of energy.
   核反应堆通过可控裂变过程使放射性同位素发生嬗变，释放出巨大的能量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-mute" as "mutate across" — something changes so completely that it crosses over into a new form entirely.
把"transmute"想成"trans（跨越）+ mute（变化）"——某物彻底改变，完全跨入一种新形态。
