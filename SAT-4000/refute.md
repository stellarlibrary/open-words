# ⚔️ refute

🔊 /rɪˈfjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To prove that a statement, theory, or charge is wrong or false; to deny or contradict with evidence.
**中文：** v. 反驳；驳斥；驳倒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disprove（反驳）
- rebut（驳斥）
- contradict（反驳）
- debunk（揭穿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **refute an argument / refute a claim / refute allegations / refute a theory**

👉 Example: The defense attorney worked tirelessly to refute the prosecution's key allegations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist published compelling data to **refute** the widely held theory that the phenomenon was caused by climate change.
   这位科学家发表了令人信服的数据，以驳斥该现象由气候变化引起的这一广泛观点。

2. Despite numerous attempts to **refute** his conclusions, the researcher's findings have consistently withstood rigorous peer review.
   尽管多次尝试反驳他的结论，该研究人员的发现始终经受住了严格的同行评审。

3. The spokesperson issued a detailed statement to **refute** the allegations of financial misconduct within the organization.
   发言人发表了一份详细声明，驳斥有关该组织内部财务不当行为的指控。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "refute" as "re-FUTE" — imagine "fighting back" (fute sounds like "fight") to prove someone wrong again and again.
把"refute"想成"re-FUTE"——想象"反击"（fute听起来像fight），一次又一次地证明对方是错的。
