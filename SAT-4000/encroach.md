# 🚧 encroach

🔊 /ɪnˈkroʊtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To gradually intrude on the territory, rights, or possessions of another; to advance beyond proper limits
**中文：** v. 蚕食，侵占

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intrude（侵入）
- infringe（侵犯）
- trespass（侵占）
- impinge（侵犯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **encroach on / encroach upon / encroach into / gradually encroach**

👉 Example: Urban development continues to encroach on the remaining natural habitats surrounding the city.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Suburban sprawl has steadily **encroached** upon the agricultural land that once sustained the region's farming communities for generations.
   郊区扩张不断蚕食曾经维系该地区几代农业社区的农地。

2. Critics warned that the proposed surveillance measures would dangerously **encroach** on the fundamental civil liberties of ordinary citizens.
   批评者警告说，拟议的监控措施将危险地侵犯普通公民的基本公民自由。

3. The rising sea levels continue to **encroach** upon coastal villages, displacing thousands of residents and threatening critical infrastructure.
   不断上升的海平面继续侵蚀沿海村庄，迫使数千居民流离失所并威胁重要基础设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-croach" as a "cockroach" creeping into your space — just as cockroaches slowly invade, to encroach is to creep into someone else's territory.
把"encroach"想成"cockroach（蟑螂）"爬进你的空间——就像蟑螂慢慢入侵一样，encroach就是悄悄侵入别人的领地。
