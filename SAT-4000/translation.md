# 🌐 translation

🔊 /trænzˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of converting text or speech from one language into another; the resulting translated text. In mathematics, movement of a figure without rotation.
**中文：** n. 翻译；译文；译本。（数学）平移。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- interpretation（口译；解释）
- rendering（翻译；表现）
- conversion（转换）
- version（译本）
- paraphrase（意译）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **literal translation / free translation / translation of a text / lose something in translation / translation studies**

👉 Example: Much of the poem's emotional nuance was inevitably lost in translation from Japanese to English.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **translation** of ancient Greek philosophical texts into Arabic during the medieval period profoundly influenced Islamic intellectual tradition.
   中世纪时期将古希腊哲学文本翻译成阿拉伯语深刻影响了伊斯兰学术传统。

2. Students of mathematics learn that a geometric **translation** moves every point of a figure the same distance in the same direction.
   数学专业的学生学到，几何平移将图形的每个点沿同一方向移动相同的距离。

3. The acclaimed **translation** of the novel preserved both the cultural authenticity and the literary elegance of the original work.
   这部小说广受好评的译本保留了原作的文化真实性和文学优雅。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "translation" as "trans (across) + lation (carrying)" — carrying meaning across from one language to another, or carrying a shape across space.
把"translation"想成"跨越（trans）+ 搬运（lation）"——将意义从一种语言搬运到另一种语言，或将图形在空间中搬移。
