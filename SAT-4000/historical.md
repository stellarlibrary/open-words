# 📜 historical

🔊 /hɪˈstɒrɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or based on history; concerning past events or the study of the past
**中文：** adj. 历史的；史学的；有关历史的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- past（过去的）
- bygone（过去的）
- archival（档案的）
- chronicled（被记录的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **historical records / historical context / historical significance / historical evidence**

👉 Example: The professor emphasized the importance of examining historical context before drawing conclusions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scholars must consult **historical** documents and primary sources to construct an accurate account of medieval governance.
   学者必须查阅历史文献和原始资料，以构建对中世纪治理的准确描述。

2. The museum's new exhibit presents **historical** artifacts that illuminate the daily lives of ordinary citizens in ancient Rome.
   博物馆的新展览展出了揭示古罗马普通市民日常生活的历史文物。

3. A thorough understanding of **historical** precedents is essential for legislators crafting policies on constitutional reform.
   对历史先例的透彻理解对于起草宪法改革政策的立法者至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "historical" as "history + cal(endar)" — events written on the calendar of history, recording what happened in the past.
把"historical"想成"history + cal(endar)"（历史+日历）——写在历史日历上的事件，记录着过去发生的一切。
