# 🥶 frigid

🔊 /ˈfrɪdʒɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely cold in temperature; lacking warmth of feeling; stiff and formal in manner
**中文：** 寒冷的；严寒的；冷淡的；冷漠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- freezing（极冷的）
- icy（冰冷的）
- glacial（冰川般冷淡的）
- frosty（冷淡的）
- aloof（冷漠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frigid temperatures / frigid climate / frigid reception / frigid atmosphere**

👉 Example: The ambassador received a frigid reception from the host nation following the diplomatic dispute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The explorers endured **frigid** temperatures that plunged well below minus forty degrees during their Arctic expedition.
   探险家们在北极探险期间忍受着骤降至零下四十多度的严寒温度。

2. Her **frigid** response to the heartfelt apology made it clear that reconciliation would not come easily.
   她对真诚道歉的冷淡回应表明和解不会轻易到来。

3. The **frigid** winds sweeping across the barren plateau forced the research team to suspend all outdoor activities.
   席卷荒凉高原的寒风迫使研究团队暂停所有户外活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frig-id" as related to "fridge" — a fridge is cold, and frigid means extremely cold or emotionally icy.
把"frigid"联想到"fridge（冰箱）"——冰箱是冷的，frigid就是极度寒冷或情感上冷漠的意思。
