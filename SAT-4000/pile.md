# 📚 pile

🔊 /paɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A heap of things laid or lying one on top of another; a large amount; a tall building or group of buildings. (v.) To place things on top of one another; to accumulate
**中文：** n. 堆；大量；建筑群；电池组 v. 累积；打桩于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- heap（堆）
- stack（叠）
- mound（土堆）
- accumulation（积累）
- mass（大量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pile up / pile of papers / pile on / compost pile / pile into**

👉 Example: Unfinished reports continued to pile up on the manager's desk throughout the busy quarter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A massive **pile** of documents awaited the legal team as they prepared for the complex international litigation case.
   一大堆文件等待着法律团队，因为他们正在准备这桩复杂的国际诉讼案件。

2. The construction workers drove steel **piles** deep into the riverbed to form a stable foundation for the bridge.
   建筑工人将钢桩深深打入河床，为桥梁构建稳固的基础。

3. Evidence continued to **pile** up against the corporation, ultimately compelling regulators to launch a formal investigation.
   针对该公司的证据不断累积，最终迫使监管机构启动了正式调查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pile" as things "piling" on top of each other like a tower — one thing after another stacking up into a heap.
想象东西像塔一样一个接一个地"堆叠"起来——这就是pile（堆积）。
