# 🎬 projector

🔊 /prəˈdʒɛktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A device that projects an image or light onto a surface; a person who plans or promotes something
**中文：** n. 投影仪；放映机；探照灯；设计者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- display device（显示设备）
- film projector（电影放映机）
- overhead projector（高架投影仪）
- beamer（投影机）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **video projector / projector screen / digital projector / set up a projector / projector lamp**

👉 Example: The professor connected the laptop to the projector before the lecture began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The classroom **projector** displayed the presentation slides clearly, allowing every student in the auditorium to follow along.
   教室里的投影仪清晰地展示了演示幻灯片，使礼堂里的每位学生都能跟上进度。

2. Early cinema relied on mechanical **projectors** that fed celluloid film past a bright lamp to create moving images.
   早期电影依赖于机械放映机，将赛璐珞胶片从明亮的灯光前送过以产生动态影像。

3. The architect used a digital **projector** to present the three-dimensional building model to the review committee.
   建筑师使用数字投影仪向评审委员会展示了三维建筑模型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "project-or" — the device or person that "projects" images, light, or ideas outward for others to see.
把"projector"想成"project（投射）+ or（者/器）"——向外"投射"图像、光线或想法的设备或人。
