# 🕵️ clandestine

🔊 /klænˈdɛstɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit or unauthorized
**中文：** adj. 秘密的；私下的；偷偷摸摸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- covert（隐蔽的）
- surreptitious（偷偷摸摸的）
- furtive（鬼鬼祟祟的）
- stealthy（秘密的）
- undercover（秘密进行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clandestine operation / clandestine meeting / clandestine affair / clandestine activity**

👉 Example: The intelligence agency conducted a clandestine operation to gather information behind enemy lines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomats held **clandestine** meetings in neutral territory to negotiate terms without attracting unwanted media attention.
   外交官们在中立地区举行秘密会议，在不引起不必要媒体关注的情况下进行谈判。

2. Investigators uncovered a **clandestine** network of smugglers who had been operating across international borders for decades.
   调查人员发现了一个已在国际边境运作数十年的秘密走私网络。

3. The researcher's **clandestine** correspondence with foreign scholars was eventually discovered by the authorities during the audit.
   这位研究者与外国学者的秘密通信最终在审计期间被当局发现。
