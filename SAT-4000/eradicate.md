# 🔥 eradicate

🔊 /ɪˈrædɪkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To completely destroy or eliminate something; to pull up by the roots
**中文：** v. 根除；根绝；灭绝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eliminate（消除）
- exterminate（灭绝）
- abolish（废除）
- annihilate（消灭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eradicate disease / eradicate poverty / eradicate corruption / eradicate completely**

👉 Example: The global health initiative aims to eradicate malaria in developing nations within the next decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. International organizations have worked for decades to **eradicate** polio, and their sustained efforts have nearly eliminated the disease entirely.
   国际组织数十年来一直致力于根除脊髓灰质炎，其持续的努力几乎完全消灭了这种疾病。

2. The new policy was specifically designed to **eradicate** systemic corruption that had plagued the institution for over a generation.
   这项新政策专门旨在根除困扰该机构一代多人的系统性腐败。

3. Scientists believe that emerging gene-editing technologies may eventually **eradicate** certain hereditary diseases from the human population altogether.
   科学家们相信，新兴的基因编辑技术最终可能从人类群体中彻底根除某些遗传性疾病。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-radic-ate" as pulling out the "radix" (Latin for root) — to tear something out completely by its roots.
把"eradicate"想成拔出"radix（拉丁语中的'根'）"——将某物连根彻底拔除。
