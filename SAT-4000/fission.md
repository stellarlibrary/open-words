# ☢️ fission

🔊 /ˈfɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The splitting of an atomic nucleus into two or more parts, releasing enormous energy; division or splitting into parts
**中文：** n. 裂变；分裂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- splitting（分裂）
- division（分裂）
- fragmentation（碎裂）
- cleavage（裂开）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nuclear fission / fission reaction / fission products / binary fission / undergo fission**

👉 Example: Nuclear fission of uranium-235 releases a tremendous amount of energy that can be harnessed for electricity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The discovery of nuclear **fission** in 1938 fundamentally transformed both the scientific community and the geopolitical landscape.
   1938年核裂变的发现从根本上改变了科学界和地缘政治格局。

2. In biological contexts, binary **fission** is the primary method by which single-celled organisms reproduce and multiply rapidly.
   在生物学中，二分裂是单细胞生物快速繁殖和增殖的主要方式。

3. The controlled **fission** reaction within the reactor core generates sufficient thermal energy to power an entire metropolitan area.
   反应堆堆芯内受控的裂变反应产生的热能足以为整个大都市地区供电。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fission" as "fissure" — both involve splitting apart. A fissure is a crack, and fission is the splitting of an atom.
把"fission"和"fissure（裂缝）"联系起来——两者都涉及裂开。裂缝是一条裂口，而裂变是原子的分裂。
