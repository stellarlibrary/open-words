# 🛠️ alteration

🔊 /ˌɔːltəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act or process of changing or modifying something; a change or modification made to something.
**中文：** 修改；改变；变更

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modification（修改）
- revision（修订）
- amendment（修正）
- adjustment（调整）
- transformation（转变）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make an alteration, undergo alteration, minor alteration, structural alteration, alteration to/in**
👉 Example: The committee approved several alterations to the original proposal before granting final approval.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **alteration** of the company's strategic plan required approval from every member of the board of directors.
   公司战略计划的变更需要董事会全体成员的批准。

2. Significant **alterations** to the historical building were prohibited under the city's heritage preservation ordinance.
   根据城市遗产保护条例，禁止对这座历史建筑进行重大改动。

3. The scientist documented each **alteration** in the specimen's cellular structure throughout the duration of the experiment.
   科学家在整个实验过程中记录了标本细胞结构的每一次变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alter + ation" — simply the noun form of "alter." When you alter something, the result is an alteration.
把 alteration 看作 "alter（改变）+ ation（名词后缀）"——改变的行为或结果就是 alteration。
