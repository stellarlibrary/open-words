# 🧪 alcohol

🔊 /ˈælkəhɒl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless volatile flammable liquid (ethanol) produced by fermentation of sugars; intoxicating drinks containing this substance
**中文：** 酒精；乙醇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ethanol（乙醇）
- spirits（烈酒）
- liquor（酒）
- intoxicant（致醉物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alcohol consumption / alcohol abuse / blood alcohol level / rubbing alcohol**
👉 Example: The study examined the long-term effects of excessive alcohol consumption on cardiovascular health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public health officials have emphasized the importance of reducing **alcohol** consumption to lower the risk of chronic disease.
   公共卫生官员强调了减少酒精摄入以降低慢性病风险的重要性。

2. The laboratory technician used **alcohol** as a solvent to dissolve the organic compound during the chemical analysis.
   实验室技术人员在化学分析过程中使用酒精作为溶剂来溶解有机化合物。

3. Several nations have implemented strict regulations governing the sale and distribution of **alcohol** to minors.
   一些国家已实施严格法规来管理向未成年人销售和分销酒精的行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alcohol" comes from Arabic "al-kuhl" meaning "the kohl" (a fine powder), later referring to any distilled essence.
"Alcohol"源自阿拉伯语"al-kuhl"，意为"眼影粉"（一种细粉），后来指任何蒸馏提取的精华。
