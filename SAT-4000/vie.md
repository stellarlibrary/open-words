# 🏆 vie

🔊 /vaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To compete eagerly with someone in order to do or achieve something
**中文：** v. 竞争；争夺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compete（竞争）
- contend（争夺）
- rival（与…竞争）
- struggle（奋争）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vie for attention / vie for power / vie with each other / vie for a position / vie for the title**

👉 Example: Several qualified candidates are vying for the prestigious scholarship offered by the university.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two pharmaceutical companies **vied** fiercely for the exclusive contract to supply vaccines to the federal government.
   两家制药公司激烈竞争向联邦政府独家供应疫苗的合同。

2. Young scholars from across the country **vie** for limited research grants that fund groundbreaking scientific investigations.
   来自全国各地的年轻学者竞争有限的研究资助，以支持开创性的科学研究。

3. Multiple cities are currently **vying** to host the international summit, each presenting elaborate development proposals.
   多个城市目前正在竞争举办国际峰会，各自提出了精心设计的发展方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vie" as a short, punchy word — like a quick jab in a boxing match, competitors vie by throwing fast strikes to win.
把"vie"想成一个简短有力的词——就像拳击比赛中的快速出拳，竞争者通过快速出击来争夺胜利。
