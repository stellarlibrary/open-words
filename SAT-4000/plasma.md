# 🔬 plasma

🔊 /ˈplæzmə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The fourth state of matter consisting of ionized gas; the colorless fluid part of blood in which cells are suspended
**中文：** n. 等离子体；血浆；深绿玉髓

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ionized gas（电离气体）
- blood fluid（血液液体）
- protoplasm（原生质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blood plasma / plasma physics / plasma screen / plasma membrane**

👉 Example: The research team studied the behavior of plasma at extremely high temperatures inside the reactor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists at the fusion laboratory successfully heated **plasma** to over one hundred million degrees for a sustained experimental period.
   聚变实验室的科学家成功地将等离子体加热到超过一亿度并维持了一段实验时间。

2. Blood banks collect and store **plasma** separately from other components because it contains vital proteins essential for treating trauma patients.
   血库将血浆与其他成分分开收集和储存，因为它含有治疗创伤患者所必需的重要蛋白质。

3. The **plasma** state of matter is the most abundant form in the visible universe, comprising the majority of stars and nebulae.
   等离子体态是可见宇宙中最丰富的物质形态，构成了大多数恒星和星云。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plasma" as "plas-ma" — matter (ma) that is plastic (plas) and fluid, flowing freely whether as ionized gas in stars or liquid in blood.
把"plasma"想成"plas-ma"——一种可塑的（plas）、流动的物质（ma），无论是恒星中的电离气体还是血液中的液体，都自由流动。
