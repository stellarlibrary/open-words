# 🤡 ridiculous

🔊 /rɪˈdɪkjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deserving or inviting mockery; absurd, preposterous, or laughably unreasonable
**中文：** adj. 荒谬的，可笑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absurd（荒谬的）
- preposterous（荒唐的）
- ludicrous（可笑的）
- laughable（可笑的）
- farcical（滑稽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **absolutely ridiculous / look ridiculous / a ridiculous idea / ridiculous amount of / ridiculous claim**

👉 Example: The defendant's explanation was so ridiculous that even the judge struggled to maintain composure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee dismissed the proposal as **ridiculous**, arguing that it lacked empirical evidence and relied entirely on unfounded speculation.
   委员会认为该提案荒谬可笑，认为它缺乏实证依据，完全依赖毫无根据的推测。

2. It would be **ridiculous** to suggest that a single policy change could resolve the deeply entrenched systemic inequality overnight.
   认为仅凭一项政策变化就能在一夜之间解决根深蒂固的系统性不平等是荒谬的。

3. The scientist found it **ridiculous** that peer reviewers rejected her well-documented study without providing any substantive criticism whatsoever.
   这位科学家觉得荒唐的是，同行评审者在没有提供任何实质性批评的情况下驳回了她论据充分的研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ridiculous" as "rid-ICK-ulous" — something so absurd it makes you say "ick!" and want to rid yourself of the idea.
把"ridiculous"想成"rid-ICK-ulous"——某事荒谬到让你说"呃！"并想要摆脱这个想法。
