# 🚪 secede

🔊 /sɪˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To withdraw formally from membership in a political union, alliance, or organization
**中文：** v. 脱离，分离

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- withdraw（退出）
- separate（分离）
- break away（脱离）
- defect（叛离）
- disaffiliate（脱离关系）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **secede from a union / secede from a federation / secede from the organization / right to secede**

👉 Example: Several southern states attempted to secede from the Union, precipitating the American Civil War.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The province threatened to **secede** from the federation unless the central government granted it significantly greater legislative autonomy.
   该省威胁要脱离联邦，除非中央政府给予其更大的立法自主权。

2. International law does not universally recognize the unilateral right of a region to **secede** from an established sovereign nation.
   国际法并不普遍承认一个地区从已建立的主权国家单方面脱离的权利。

3. The dissident faction voted overwhelmingly to **secede** from the parent organization and establish an independent political movement.
   持不同意见的派别以压倒性多数投票决定脱离母组织，建立一个独立的政治运动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "secede" as "se-cede" where "se" means apart and "cede" means to go — literally to go apart, to separate from a larger group.
把"secede"拆成"se（分开）+ cede（走）"——字面意思就是走开、分离，即从一个较大的群体中脱离出去。
