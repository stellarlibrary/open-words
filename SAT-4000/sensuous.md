# 🎨 sensuous

🔊 /ˈsenʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or affecting the senses rather than the intellect; appealing to aesthetic appreciation
**中文：** adj. 感觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aesthetic（美感的）
- lush（华美的）
- sumptuous（感官丰富的）
- luxuriant（丰盛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sensuous beauty / sensuous imagery / sensuous delight / sensuous prose / sensuous music**

👉 Example: The composer crafted a sensuous melody that stirred deep emotional responses in every listener.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The gallery featured paintings of **sensuous** landscapes that captured the interplay of light and color with remarkable delicacy.
   画廊展出了感官丰富的风景画，以非凡的细腻手法捕捉了光影与色彩的交融。

2. Milton coined the word **sensuous** to describe aesthetic pleasure without the morally negative connotations of its counterpart.
   弥尔顿创造了"sensuous"一词来描述美感愉悦，以避免其对应词的道德负面含义。

3. The **sensuous** quality of silk fabric against the skin has made it a prized material throughout centuries of textile history.
   丝绸织物贴肤时的感官品质使其在数个世纪的纺织历史中一直是珍贵的材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sensuous" as "sense + uous (beautiful)" — it is about the beauty perceived through the senses, an aesthetic experience.
把"sensuous"想成"sense + uous（美丽的）"——它关乎通过感官感知到的美，是一种审美体验。
