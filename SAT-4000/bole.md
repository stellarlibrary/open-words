# 🌳 bole

🔊 /boʊl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The trunk of a tree, especially the lower part below the main branches. Also refers to a type of reddish-brown clay.
**中文：** n. 树干，树身；红玄武土（一种红褐色黏土）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trunk（树干）
- stem（茎干）
- stalk（茎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the bole of a tree / thick bole / massive bole / gnarled bole / bole of an oak**

👉 Example: The woodpecker hammered rhythmically against the rough bole of the ancient oak.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanist measured the circumference of the enormous **bole** to estimate the age of the centuries-old redwood tree.
   植物学家测量了巨大树干的周长，以估算这棵数百年红杉树的年龄。

2. Moss and lichen covered the weathered **bole** of the elm, giving it an appearance of timeless natural grandeur.
   苔藓和地衣覆盖着榆树饱经风霜的树干，赋予它一种历久弥新的自然壮美。

3. The artist used red **bole** as an underlayer for the gold leaf, following the traditional gilding techniques of Renaissance craftsmen.
   艺术家使用红色黏土作为金箔的底层，遵循文艺复兴时期工匠的传统镀金技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bole" rhyming with "pole" — a tree trunk stands upright like a pole, and "bole" is simply the formal word for that sturdy, vertical trunk.
把"bole"和"pole（杆）"联系起来——树干像杆子一样直立，而"bole"就是指那个坚固的、垂直的树干。
