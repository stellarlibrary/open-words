# ⚡ ohm

🔊 /oʊm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The SI unit of electrical resistance, equal to the resistance through which a current of one ampere flows when a potential difference of one volt is applied
**中文：** n. 欧姆（电阻单位）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unit of resistance（电阻单位）
- impedance unit（阻抗单位）
- resistance measure（电阻度量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **measured in ohms / resistance of X ohms / ohm meter / ohm rating**

👉 Example: The circuit requires a resistor with a value of at least two hundred ohms to function safely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer calculated that the circuit needed a resistor of exactly fifty **ohms** to regulate the current properly.
   工程师计算出电路需要恰好五十欧姆的电阻器来正确调节电流。

2. According to Ohm's Law, the voltage across a conductor is directly proportional to the current multiplied by the **ohm** value.
   根据欧姆定律，导体两端的电压与电流乘以欧姆值成正比。

3. The technician discovered that the faulty component had an unexpectedly high resistance of several thousand **ohms**.
   技术人员发现故障组件的电阻出乎意料地高达数千欧姆。
