# 💊 apothecary

🔊 /əˈpɒθɪkəri/<br>
🏷️ *n. 药剂师；药材商*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A person who prepared and sold medicines and drugs; an early form of a pharmacist, historically also offering medical advice.

**中文：** 药剂师；药材商；旧时配制和出售药物的人，也常提供医疗建议。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- pharmacist 药剂师
- druggist 药商
- chemist 药剂师（英式）
- dispenser 配药师

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** the local apothecary, apothecary shop, apothecary's remedy, village apothecary

**Example:** "Apothecary" is somewhat archaic; in modern English, "pharmacist" is the standard term. It often appears in historical or literary contexts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **apothecary** carefully measured the herbs before preparing the prescribed remedy for the patient.
   **药剂师**在为病人配制药方前仔细称量了草药。

2. In medieval towns, the **apothecary** served as both a pharmacist and an informal medical advisor.
   在中世纪的城镇里，**药材商**既是药剂师，也是非正式的医疗顾问。

3. Shakespeare famously depicted an impoverished **apothecary** who sold poison to Romeo in desperation.
   莎士比亚笔下有一位穷困潦倒的**药剂师**，在绝望中将毒药卖给了罗密欧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "apothecary" as related to "apotheca" (Latin for storehouse) — the apothecary kept a storehouse of medicines.

联想记忆：apothecary 源自拉丁语"apotheca"（仓库）——药剂师就是管理药物仓库的人。
