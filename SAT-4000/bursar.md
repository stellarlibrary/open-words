# 💼 bursar

🔊 /ˈbɜːrsər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A treasurer or financial officer, especially of a college, university, or school
**中文：** n. 财务主管；会计员（尤指学校的）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- treasurer（财务主管）
- comptroller（审计官）
- accountant（会计）
- cashier（出纳）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **college bursar / bursar's office / university bursar / school bursar**

👉 Example: Students must visit the bursar's office to resolve outstanding tuition balances before registering for courses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bursar** informed the board of trustees that the university's endowment had grown substantially over the preceding fiscal year.
   财务主管告知董事会，大学的捐赠基金在上一财年大幅增长。

2. All tuition payments and financial aid disbursements are processed through the **bursar's** office located on the first floor.
   所有学费缴纳和助学金发放均通过位于一楼的财务处办理。

3. The newly appointed **bursar** implemented stricter auditing procedures to ensure greater transparency in the institution's financial operations.
   新任命的财务主管实施了更严格的审计程序，以确保机构财务运作更加透明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bursar" and "purse" — they share the Latin root "bursa" (purse), so a bursar is the person who manages the institution's purse.
想到"bursar"和"purse"（钱包）——它们共享拉丁语词根"bursa"（钱包），所以bursar就是管理机构钱包的人。
