# ⚡ denounce

🔊 /dɪˈnaʊns/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To publicly declare to be wrong or evil; to condemn openly; to formally accuse or inform against
**中文：** vt. 谴责；公开指责；告发；公然抨击

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- condemn（谴责）
- censure（严厉批评）
- decry（公开反对）
- castigate（严惩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **denounce violence / denounce corruption / denounce someone as / publicly denounce**

👉 Example: World leaders gathered to denounce the use of chemical weapons against civilian populations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international community moved swiftly to **denounce** the authoritarian regime's systematic suppression of free speech and independent journalism.
   国际社会迅速采取行动谴责该威权政权对言论自由和独立新闻业的系统性压制。

2. Several prominent scientists **denounced** the publication as fraudulent after discovering significant data manipulation in the research methodology.
   几位著名科学家在发现研究方法中存在严重数据操纵后，公开谴责该出版物为欺诈性作品。

3. The council voted unanimously to **denounce** the proposed development plan, citing irreversible environmental damage to protected wetland habitats.
   委员会一致投票谴责拟议的开发计划，理由是会对受保护的湿地栖息地造成不可逆的环境破坏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-nounce" as "announce against" — when you denounce, you publicly announce your opposition to something.
把"denounce"想成"announce against（反对宣布）"——当你denounce时，你就是在公开宣布你的反对立场。
