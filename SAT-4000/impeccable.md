# ✨ impeccable

🔊 /ɪmˈpɛkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Without fault or error; flawless in quality, execution, or character
**中文：** adj. 无瑕疵的；没有缺点的；完美的；无懈可击的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flawless（完美的）
- faultless（无过失的）
- perfect（完美的）
- exemplary（模范的）
- irreproachable（无可指摘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impeccable taste / impeccable manners / impeccable record / impeccable timing**

👉 Example: The ambassador was known for her impeccable manners and flawless command of diplomatic protocol.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate's **impeccable** academic record and extensive volunteer experience made her the ideal choice for the scholarship.
   这位候选人完美的学术记录和丰富的志愿服务经验使她成为奖学金的理想人选。

2. The restaurant earned its reputation through **impeccable** service and consistently outstanding cuisine prepared by world-class chefs.
   这家餐厅通过无可挑剔的服务和世界级厨师持续烹制的杰出美食赢得了声誉。

3. His **impeccable** logic and thorough preparation allowed him to dismantle every counterargument presented during the formal debate.
   他无懈可击的逻辑和充分的准备使他能够在正式辩论中驳倒每一个反对论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-pecc-able" — "im" means not, "pecc" comes from Latin "peccare" (to sin), so impeccable literally means "not able to sin," hence without fault.
把"impeccable"拆成"im-pecc-able"——"im"表示"不"，"pecc"来自拉丁语"peccare（犯罪）"，所以impeccable字面意思是"不会犯错的"，即完美的。
