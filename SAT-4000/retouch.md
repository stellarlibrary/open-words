# 🖌️ retouch

🔊 /riːˈtʌtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of making minor improvements or corrections. (v.) To improve or modify by making small changes, especially to a photograph or painting
**中文：** n. 润饰；修整部分 v. 润饰；修整

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- touch up（修饰）
- refine（精炼）
- polish（润色）
- enhance（增强）
- revise（修改）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retouch a photograph / retouch a painting / digitally retouch / retouch imperfections**

👉 Example: The photographer spent hours retouching the portrait to remove minor blemishes and adjust the lighting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Professional editors routinely **retouch** magazine cover photographs to meet the publication's exacting aesthetic standards and guidelines.
   专业编辑通常会润饰杂志封面照片，以达到出版物严格的审美标准和要求。

2. The art conservator was hired to carefully **retouch** the damaged sections of the centuries-old Renaissance masterpiece.
   艺术修复师被聘请来仔细修整这幅数百年历史的文艺复兴杰作的受损部分。

3. Critics argue that the excessive **retouch** of advertising images promotes unrealistic beauty standards among impressionable young audiences.
   批评者认为，广告图片的过度修饰在易受影响的年轻受众中传播了不切实际的审美标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-TOUCH" — literally to "touch again" — to go back and touch up small details to make something look better.
把"retouch"想成"re-TOUCH"——字面意思是"再次触碰"——回过头来修整小细节，使其看起来更好。
