# 🐢 torpid

🔊 /ˈtɔːrpɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Mentally or physically inactive; sluggish; in a state of hibernation or dormancy; showing little interest or enthusiasm
**中文：** 迟钝的；麻痹的；不活泼的；蛰伏的；懒散的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lethargic（昏睡的）
- sluggish（迟缓的）
- dormant（休眠的）
- listless（无精打采的）
- inert（惰性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **torpid state / torpid mind / torpid economy / become torpid / remain torpid**

👉 Example: The reptile lay torpid in the shade, conserving energy during the hottest part of the day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the heavy midday meal, the students grew **torpid** and struggled to maintain their concentration during the afternoon lecture.
   丰盛的午餐后，学生们变得昏昏沉沉，在下午的讲座中难以集中注意力。

2. The bear remained in a **torpid** condition throughout the winter, its metabolic rate reduced to a fraction of normal levels.
   这只熊整个冬天都处于蛰伏状态，其新陈代谢率降至正常水平的一小部分。

3. Critics argued that the **torpid** response of the regulatory agency allowed the financial crisis to escalate beyond recovery.
   批评人士认为，监管机构迟钝的反应使金融危机恶化到了无法挽回的地步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "torpid" as related to "torpedo" — a torpedo lies still and dormant before it launches, just as something torpid is inactive and sluggish.
把"torpid"和"torpedo（鱼雷）"联系起来——鱼雷在发射前静止不动，就像torpid的事物是不活跃和迟缓的。
