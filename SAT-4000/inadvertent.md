# 😅 inadvertent

🔊 /ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning; unintentional; caused by a lack of attention or care
**中文：** adj. 疏忽的；不注意的；无意中做的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unintentional（无意的）
- accidental（偶然的）
- careless（粗心的）
- unwitting（不知不觉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inadvertent error / inadvertent disclosure / inadvertent consequence / inadvertent omission**

👉 Example: The inadvertent omission of key data led to a flawed analysis of the experimental results.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher's **inadvertent** inclusion of corrupted data compromised the validity of the entire longitudinal study.
   研究人员无意中纳入了损坏的数据，损害了整个纵向研究的有效性。

2. An **inadvertent** disclosure of classified documents prompted a thorough review of the agency's security protocols.
   一次无意的机密文件泄露促使该机构对安全协议进行了全面审查。

3. The diplomat's **inadvertent** remark during the press conference created a minor international controversy among allied nations.
   这位外交官在新闻发布会上的无心之言在盟国之间引发了一场小规模的国际争议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-ad-vert-ent" — you failed to "ad-vert" (turn your attention toward) something, so the mistake was inadvertent.
把"inadvertent"想成"没有'advert'（注意到）"——你没有把注意力转向某事，所以这个错误是无意中犯的。
