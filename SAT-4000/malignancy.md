# ☠️ malignancy

🔊 /məˈlɪɡnənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state or quality of being malignant; a cancerous tumor or growth; intense ill will or malevolence
**中文：** n. 恶性（肿瘤等）；恶意；恶毒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- malevolence（恶意）
- virulence（恶毒）
- hostility（敌意）
- cancer（癌症）
- wickedness（邪恶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **detect a malignancy / malignancy of spirit / signs of malignancy / treat a malignancy**

👉 Example: The biopsy confirmed the presence of a malignancy that required immediate surgical intervention.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Early screening programs have proven instrumental in detecting **malignancy** before it progresses to an advanced and untreatable stage.
   早期筛查项目已被证明在恶性肿瘤发展到晚期不可治疗阶段之前发现它方面起着关键作用。

2. The critic described the regime's propaganda machine as a **malignancy** that had corrupted public discourse for entire generations.
   评论家将该政权的宣传机器描述为一种恶性毒瘤，腐蚀了几代人的公共话语。

3. Oncologists recommended an aggressive treatment protocol after discovering that the **malignancy** had begun to spread to surrounding tissues.
   肿瘤学家在发现恶性肿瘤已开始向周围组织扩散后，建议采用积极的治疗方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "malignancy" as "malign" + "-ancy" (state of) — the state of being malign, whether a harmful tumor in the body or harmful intent in the heart.
把"malignancy"想成"malign"（恶性的）+"-ancy"（状态）——处于恶性状态，无论是体内的有害肿瘤还是内心的恶意。
