# 🏛️ caste

🔊 /kæst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A hereditary social class or system of rigid social stratification, especially as found in Hindu society; social standing or rank
**中文：** n. （印度社会中的）种姓；社会地位；世袭阶级

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- class（阶级）
- rank（等级）
- stratum（社会阶层）
- hierarchy（等级制度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **caste system / caste discrimination / upper caste / lower caste / caste hierarchy**

👉 Example: The rigid caste system in traditional societies often determined one's occupation from birth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scholars have extensively studied how the **caste** system influenced economic mobility and social relations in premodern India.
   学者们广泛研究了种姓制度如何影响了前现代印度的经济流动性和社会关系。

2. Constitutional reforms sought to abolish discrimination based on **caste** and to promote equality among all citizens.
   宪法改革旨在废除基于种姓的歧视，并促进所有公民之间的平等。

3. The anthropologist observed that **caste** distinctions persisted informally even after legal protections were formally enacted.
   人类学家观察到，即使在正式颁布法律保护之后，种姓的区分仍以非正式方式持续存在。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "caste" sounding like "cast" — once you are "cast" into a role by birth, you are fixed in your social position, just like a caste.
"caste"听起来像"cast"（铸造）——一出生就被"铸造"进一个角色，你的社会地位就固定了，这就是种姓。
