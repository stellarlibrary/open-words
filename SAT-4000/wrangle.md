# 🗣️ wrangle

🔊 /ˈræŋɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A prolonged dispute or argument. (v.) To argue or dispute in a noisy or angry way
**中文：** n. 争论；吵嘴 v. 争论；争吵；口角

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quarrel（争吵）
- bicker（拌嘴）
- squabble（口角）
- dispute（争论）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wrangle over / wrangle with / wrangle about / a bitter wrangle**

👉 Example: The two senators continued to wrangle over the proposed budget amendment well into the night.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members **wrangled** for hours over the allocation of funds before reaching a reluctant compromise.
   委员会成员们就资金分配争论了数小时，最终才勉强达成妥协。

2. A prolonged legal **wrangle** between the two corporations delayed the merger by nearly eighteen months.
   两家公司之间旷日持久的法律纠纷使合并推迟了将近十八个月。

3. Diplomats from both nations **wrangled** over the terms of the treaty, each side refusing to concede ground.
   两国外交官就条约条款争论不休，双方都不愿作出让步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wrangle" as "wring + tangle" — people wringing their hands while tangled in a heated argument.
把"wrangle"想成"wring（拧）+ tangle（纠缠）"——人们在激烈的争论中纠缠不清，焦虑地搓着双手。
