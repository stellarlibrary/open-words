# 🦁 predation

🔊 /prɪˈdeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of preying on other organisms for food; the practice of plundering or exploiting others
**中文：** n. 捕食；掠夺；掠食

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hunting（捕猎）
- plundering（掠夺）
- preying（捕食）
- exploitation（剥削）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **predation on / predation by / risk of predation / predation pressure**

👉 Example: The zebra's stripes may serve as a defense mechanism to reduce predation by large carnivores.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study revealed that **predation** by invasive species had significantly reduced the native bird population on the island.
   研究表明，外来物种的捕食已大幅减少了岛上本地鸟类的数量。

2. Small mammals have evolved nocturnal behaviors as an adaptive strategy to minimize the risk of **predation**.
   小型哺乳动物进化出了夜间活动习性，作为一种减少被捕食风险的适应性策略。

3. The ecologist's research focused on the complex relationship between **predation** and population dynamics in marine ecosystems.
   这位生态学家的研究聚焦于海洋生态系统中捕食与种群动态之间的复杂关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "predation" as "predator in action" — it describes what a predator does when it hunts and consumes its prey.
把"predation"想成"predator（捕食者）in action（行动中）"——描述捕食者猎杀和捕食猎物的行为。
