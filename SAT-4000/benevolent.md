# 😊 benevolent

🔊 /bəˈnɛvələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Well-meaning and kindly; showing goodwill; characterized by charitable intentions or generous spirit
**中文：** adj. 仁慈的；慈善的；亲切的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- kind（善良的）
- charitable（慈善的）
- compassionate（有同情心的）
- generous（慷慨的）
- philanthropic（博爱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **benevolent ruler / benevolent smile / benevolent act / benevolent organization**

👉 Example: The benevolent monarch was celebrated for his generous policies that improved the welfare of all citizens.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **benevolent** organization distributed food and medical supplies to thousands of displaced families each year.
   这个慈善组织每年向数千个流离失所的家庭分发食物和医疗物资。

2. Her **benevolent** nature compelled her to volunteer at the shelter every weekend despite her demanding schedule.
   她仁慈的天性驱使她在繁忙的日程中仍每个周末去收容所做志愿者。

3. A truly **benevolent** leader prioritizes the well-being of the people above personal ambition or political gain.
   一位真正仁慈的领导者将人民的福祉置于个人野心或政治利益之上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "benevolent" as "bene (good) + volent (wishing)" — someone who wishes good things for others is benevolent.
把"benevolent"想成"bene（好的）+ volent（希望的）"——一个希望他人好的人就是仁慈的。
