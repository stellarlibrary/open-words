# 🏛️ abbey

🔊 /ˈæbi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A monastery or convent, especially one headed by an abbot or abbess; a large church associated with such a community
**中文：** n. 大修道院；大教堂；修道院中全体修士或修女

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- monastery（修道院）
- convent（女修道院）
- priory（小修道院）
- cloister（隐修院）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Westminster Abbey / the ruins of the abbey / founded an abbey / abbey church**

👉 Example: Tourists visit Westminster Abbey each year to admire its magnificent Gothic architecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient **abbey** has stood atop the hill for nearly eight centuries, surviving wars and natural disasters.
   这座古老的大修道院矗立在山顶近八个世纪，经历了战争和自然灾害。

2. Archaeologists discovered valuable manuscripts hidden beneath the ruins of the medieval **abbey** last summer.
   考古学家去年夏天在中世纪大修道院的废墟下发现了珍贵的手稿。

3. The **abbey** served as both a spiritual retreat and a center of learning throughout the Middle Ages.
   这座大修道院在整个中世纪既是灵修之所，也是学术中心。

---

想想"abbey"和著名的"Westminster Abbey"（威斯敏斯特大教堂）——一座僧侣或修女生活和礼拜的宏伟宗教建筑。
