# 😤 temerity

🔊 /tɪˈmɛrɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Excessive confidence or boldness; audacity; reckless daring
**中文：** n. 鲁莽；冒失；蛮勇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- audacity（大胆）
- boldness（勇气）
- rashness（鲁莽）
- impudence（无礼）
- recklessness（轻率）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have the temerity to / sheer temerity / display temerity / remarkable temerity**

👉 Example: Few employees had the temerity to challenge the director's decision during the meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The junior associate had the **temerity** to contradict the senior partner's legal strategy in front of the entire firm.
   这位初级合伙人竟然鲁莽地在整个事务所面前反驳了高级合伙人的法律策略。

2. Critics admired the journalist's **temerity** in publishing the exposé despite receiving numerous threats from powerful interests.
   评论家们钦佩这位记者的胆大，尽管收到了来自强大利益集团的众多威胁，仍然发表了这篇揭露报道。

3. It takes considerable **temerity** to question established scientific paradigms, yet such challenges often drive transformative discoveries.
   质疑既定的科学范式需要相当的胆量，但这样的挑战往往推动变革性的发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temerity" as related to "temere" (Latin for "blindly/rashly") — acting blindly without considering consequences, like a reckless daredevil.
把"temerity"想成与拉丁语"temere"（盲目地/鲁莽地）有关——不考虑后果地盲目行事，像一个鲁莽的冒险者。
