# 🌊 influx

🔊 /ˈɪnflʌks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An arrival or entry of large numbers of people or things; a flowing in, especially of a river into a larger body of water
**中文：** n. 流入；汇集；河流的汇集处

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inflow（流入）
- inrush（涌入）
- invasion（涌入）
- flood（洪流）
- surge（激增）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **influx of immigrants / influx of capital / influx of tourists / massive influx**

👉 Example: The sudden influx of refugees placed enormous strain on the region's limited resources.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **influx** of foreign investment transformed the once-stagnant economy into one of the fastest-growing markets in the developing world.
   外国投资的涌入将这个曾经停滞的经济体转变为发展中国家增长最快的市场之一。

2. City planners struggled to manage the massive **influx** of residents migrating from rural areas in search of better employment.
   城市规划者努力应对从农村地区迁来寻找更好就业机会的大量居民涌入。

3. The annual **influx** of tourists during the summer months generates substantial revenue but also creates significant environmental concerns.
   每年夏季游客的大量涌入带来了可观的收入，但也引发了严重的环境问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in" + "flux" (flow) — a flux or flow coming IN, like a river of people or resources pouring into a place.
把"influx"想成"in（进入）" + "flux（流动）"——涌入的潮流，如同人流或资源洪流涌入一个地方。
