# 🎨 amateur

🔊 /ˈæmətʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who engages in an activity for pleasure rather than for professional gain; a person lacking expertise. (adj.) Done by or characteristic of a non-professional
**中文：** n. 爱好者；业余爱好者 adj. 业余的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- novice（新手）
- hobbyist（业余爱好者）
- dilettante（半吊子）
- nonprofessional（非专业人士）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amateur photographer / amateur athlete / amateur hour / amateur status**

👉 Example: The amateur photographer captured a stunning image that rivaled the work of seasoned professionals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **amateur** astronomer discovered a previously unknown comet using a modest telescope set up in her suburban backyard observatory.
   这位业余天文学家使用架设在她郊区后院天文台中的一台普通望远镜发现了一颗此前未知的彗星。

2. Despite being an **amateur** in the field of ceramics, his work demonstrated extraordinary artistic sensibility and technical precision.
   尽管在陶瓷领域只是一名业余爱好者，他的作品却展现出非凡的艺术敏感性和技术精确度。

3. The competition welcomed both professional and **amateur** participants, fostering a collaborative environment that encouraged creative experimentation among all entrants.
   比赛欢迎专业和业余参赛者，营造了一个鼓励所有参赛者进行创意实验的协作环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "amateur" from French "amour" (love) — an amateur does something for the love of it, not for money or professional status.
"Amateur"源自法语"amour"（爱）——业余爱好者做某事是出于热爱，而非为了金钱或专业地位。
