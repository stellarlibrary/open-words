# 🎭 personality

🔊 /ˌpɜːrsəˈnælɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The combination of characteristics or qualities that form an individual's distinctive character; a famous or notable person
**中文：** n. 个性；品格；名人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- character（性格）
- temperament（气质）
- disposition（性情）
- identity（身份）
- celebrity（名人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strong personality / personality trait / personality disorder / television personality**

👉 Example: Her dynamic personality made her an effective leader in both corporate and community settings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Psychologists have long debated whether **personality** is primarily shaped by genetic inheritance or environmental influences during childhood.
   心理学家长期以来一直在争论个性是主要由遗传还是由童年时期的环境影响所塑造。

2. The charismatic television **personality** leveraged her public platform to advocate for educational reform across the country.
   这位具有魅力的电视名人利用她的公共平台在全国范围内倡导教育改革。

3. Effective leaders often possess a commanding **personality** that inspires confidence and motivates teams to achieve ambitious goals.
   高效的领导者通常具有令人信服的个性，能激发信心并激励团队实现远大目标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "personal-ity" — the quality that makes you uniquely "personal," the traits that define who you are as a person.
把"personality"想成"personal-ity"——使你独特地成为"个人"的品质，定义你作为一个人的特征。
