# 🦊 trickery

🔊 /ˈtrɪkəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The practice of deception; the use of tricks or stratagems to cheat or mislead others.
**中文：** n. 欺骗；诡计；花招

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deceit（欺骗）
- fraud（诈骗）
- chicanery（诡辩）
- subterfuge（诡计）
- duplicity（口是心非）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resort to trickery / political trickery / by trickery / an act of trickery**

👉 Example: The kingdom was seized not by military force but through political trickery and carefully orchestrated betrayal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that the company had relied on financial **trickery** to inflate its earnings reports for consecutive quarters.
   调查显示，该公司连续多个季度依赖财务欺骗手段来虚增收益报告。

2. Ancient myths frequently depict heroes who overcome powerful adversaries through cleverness and **trickery** rather than brute strength.
   古代神话经常描绘英雄通过智慧和诡计而非蛮力战胜强大对手的故事。

3. The election results were later challenged on the grounds that widespread **trickery** had undermined the integrity of the process.
   选举结果后来受到质疑，理由是大范围的欺骗行为破坏了选举过程的公正性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trickery" as "trick" + "-ery" (a place or practice) — it is the practice of performing tricks to deceive others.
把"trickery"拆分为"trick"加上"-ery"（表示行为或场所）——即实施诡计来欺骗他人的行为。
