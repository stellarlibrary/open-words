# 🏔️ immense

🔊 /ɪˈmɛns/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely large or great in size, scale, or degree; vast and boundless.
**中文：** adj. 巨大的；广大的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enormous（巨大的）
- vast（广阔的）
- colossal（庞大的）
- tremendous（极大的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **immense pressure / immense wealth / immense importance / immense satisfaction**

👉 Example: The project required an immense amount of funding before construction could begin.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers faced **immense** challenges in developing a vaccine within such an extraordinarily compressed time frame.
   研究人员在如此紧迫的时间内开发疫苗面临着巨大的挑战。

2. The discovery of ancient artifacts beneath the cathedral holds **immense** significance for our understanding of medieval civilization.
   在大教堂下方发现的古代文物对我们理解中世纪文明具有重大意义。

3. She felt **immense** gratitude toward the scholarship committee whose generous funding made her education abroad possible.
   她对奖学金委员会深怀感激，正是他们的慷慨资助使她的海外求学成为可能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-mense" as "impossible to measure" — something so large that it cannot be measured easily.
把"immense"想成"im（不能）+ mense（测量）"——大到无法测量，所以是巨大的。
