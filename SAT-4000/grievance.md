# 📋 grievance

🔊 /ˈɡriːvəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A real or perceived cause for complaint; a feeling of resentment over something believed to be wrong or unfair
**中文：** n. 不满；不平；委屈；冤情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- complaint（抱怨）
- injustice（不公）
- resentment（怨恨）
- objection（异议）
- gripe（不满）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **file a grievance / air a grievance / legitimate grievance / grievance against / grievance procedure**

👉 Example: The employees filed a formal grievance against the company regarding unsafe working conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The workers presented their **grievance** to management, demanding fair compensation and improved safety standards in the factory.
   工人们向管理层提出了不满，要求获得公平的薪酬和改善工厂的安全标准。

2. Historical **grievances** between the two nations continued to hinder diplomatic negotiations and undermine prospects for lasting peace.
   两国之间的历史积怨继续阻碍着外交谈判，并削弱了实现持久和平的前景。

3. The student council established a formal **grievance** procedure to address complaints about academic policies fairly and transparently.
   学生会建立了正式的申诉程序，以公正透明地处理有关学术政策的投诉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grievance" as "grieve + ance" — something that makes you grieve or feel wronged, a cause for complaint.
把"grievance"想成"grieve + ance"——让你感到悲伤或受到不公正待遇的事情，即抱怨的原因。
