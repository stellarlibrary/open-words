# 🏺 archaeologist

🔊 /ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who studies human history and prehistory through the excavation of artifacts, structures, and other physical remains
**中文：** n. 考古学家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excavator（发掘者）
- antiquarian（古物研究者）
- historian（历史学家）
- paleontologist（古生物学家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a team of archaeologists / archaeologist discovered / archaeologist unearthed / lead archaeologist**

👉 Example: The archaeologist carefully unearthed pottery fragments dating back to the Bronze Age.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **archaeologist** spent decades excavating ancient tombs in the Nile Valley to study Egyptian burial customs.
   这位著名的考古学家花了数十年在尼罗河谷发掘古墓，以研究埃及的丧葬习俗。

2. A team of **archaeologists** discovered remarkably preserved Roman mosaics beneath a farmer's field in southern England.
   一支考古学家团队在英格兰南部一位农民的田地下发现了保存极为完好的罗马马赛克。

3. The young **archaeologist** published her groundbreaking findings on prehistoric settlements in a prestigious academic journal.
   这位年轻的考古学家在一本权威学术期刊上发表了她关于史前聚落的突破性发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "archaeo-logist" — "archaeo" means ancient, and "-logist" means a specialist, so an archaeologist is a specialist in ancient things.
把"archaeologist"拆成"archaeo（古代的）+ logist（专家）"——考古学家就是研究古代事物的专家。
