# 🌫️ befog

🔊 /bɪˈfɒɡ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To envelop in fog; to make obscure or confused; to bewilder or muddle
**中文：** v. 罩入雾中；使朦胧；使模糊；使困惑，使迷惑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obscure（使模糊）
- confuse（使困惑）
- bewilder（使迷惑）
- muddle（使混乱）
- cloud（使模糊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **befog the issue / befogged mind / befogged by complexity / befog the truth**

👉 Example: The politician's evasive answers only served to befog the issue rather than clarify it.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dense legal jargon in the contract was deliberately designed to **befog** the signatories and conceal unfavorable terms.
   合同中晦涩的法律术语是刻意设计来迷惑签署者并隐藏不利条款的。

2. Years of excessive alcohol consumption had gradually **befogged** his once brilliant mind, impairing his capacity for critical thought.
   多年过度饮酒逐渐使他曾经聪明的头脑变得昏沉，损害了他的批判性思维能力。

3. The coastal village was completely **befogged** by a thick maritime mist that reduced visibility to mere meters.
   沿海村庄完全被浓厚的海雾笼罩，能见度降到了仅仅几米。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be + fog" — to befog is to wrap something in fog, making it impossible to see or think clearly.
把"befog"拆成"be + fog（雾）"——就是把某物笼罩在雾中，使人看不清或想不明白。
