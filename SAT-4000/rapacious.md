# 🦅 rapacious

🔊 /rəˈpeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Aggressively greedy or grasping; inclined to seize by force; predatory
**中文：** adj. 贪婪的；掠夺的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- voracious（贪婪的）
- avaricious（贪财的）
- predatory（掠夺的）
- greedy（贪心的）
- grasping（贪得无厌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rapacious greed / rapacious appetite / rapacious landlord / rapacious corporation**

👉 Example: The rapacious landlord raised rents far beyond what tenants could reasonably afford.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **rapacious** corporation exploited natural resources without regard for environmental sustainability or local communities.
   那家贪婪的公司肆意开采自然资源，全然不顾环境的可持续性和当地社区的利益。

2. Historians described the invaders as **rapacious** warriors who plundered every village they encountered along the frontier.
   历史学家将入侵者描述为贪婪的掠夺者，他们劫掠了沿边境遇到的每一个村庄。

3. Her **rapacious** appetite for power led her to undermine colleagues and manipulate institutional procedures for personal gain.
   她对权力的贪婪渴望驱使她暗中破坏同事并操纵制度程序以谋取私利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rapacious" as related to "raptor" — a bird of prey that snatches its victims with greedy, grasping claws.
把"rapacious"和"raptor"（猛禽）联系起来——猛禽用贪婪的爪子猛扑并抓住猎物。
