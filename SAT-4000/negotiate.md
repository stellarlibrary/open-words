# 🤝 negotiate

🔊 /nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To discuss terms in order to reach an agreement; to successfully navigate or overcome an obstacle or difficulty
**中文：** v. 谈判；商议；交涉；越过

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bargain（讨价还价）
- mediate（调解）
- deliberate（商议）
- confer（协商）
- arbitrate（仲裁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **negotiate a deal / negotiate terms / negotiate a settlement / negotiate a contract**

👉 Example: The union representatives met with management to negotiate better working conditions for employees.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two nations agreed to **negotiate** a comprehensive trade agreement that would benefit their respective economies.
   两国同意就一项有利于双方经济的全面贸易协定进行谈判。

2. The experienced diplomat was able to **negotiate** a ceasefire between the rival factions after weeks of intense discussion.
   这位经验丰富的外交官经过数周激烈讨论后成功在敌对派别之间谈判达成停火协议。

3. The driver skillfully **negotiated** the treacherous mountain road despite heavy rainfall and significantly reduced visibility conditions.
   尽管暴雨导致能见度大幅降低，司机仍熟练地驾车通过了危险的山路。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nego-TI-ate" as "no ego, TI(e) it" — to negotiate well, set aside your ego and work to tie an agreement together.
把"negotiate"想成"no ego, tie it（放下自尊，达成协议）"——要谈判成功，就要放下自尊，共同达成协议。
