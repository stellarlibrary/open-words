# 🌍 cosmopolitan

🔊 /ˌkɒzməˈpɒlɪtən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having worldwide scope or relevance; sophisticated and cultured. (n.) A person who has lived in and is familiar with many countries
**中文：** adj. 世界性的；世界主义的 n. 四海为家者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- worldly（世故的）
- global（全球的）
- sophisticated（老练的）
- urbane（彬彬有礼的）
- international（国际的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cosmopolitan city / cosmopolitan outlook / cosmopolitan society / cosmopolitan atmosphere**

👉 Example: New York is often described as one of the most cosmopolitan cities in the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university attracts students from over sixty countries, creating a truly **cosmopolitan** academic environment on campus.
   该大学吸引了来自六十多个国家的学生，在校园里营造出真正的国际化学术环境。

2. Her **cosmopolitan** upbringing in several European capitals gave her a remarkably broad perspective on cultural diversity.
   她在欧洲几个首都的国际化成长经历赋予了她对文化多样性非凡的广阔视野。

3. The conference fostered a **cosmopolitan** exchange of ideas among scholars representing diverse intellectual traditions worldwide.
   这次会议促进了代表全球不同学术传统的学者之间的国际化思想交流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cosmo-politan" as "cosmos + politan (citizen)" — a citizen of the cosmos, someone who belongs to the whole world.
把"cosmopolitan"想成"cosmos（宇宙）+ politan（公民）"——宇宙的公民，属于整个世界的人。
