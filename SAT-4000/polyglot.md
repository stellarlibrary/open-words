# 🌍 polyglot

🔊 /ˈpɑːliɡlɑːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Knowing or using several languages. (n.) A person who speaks or writes several languages fluently
**中文：** adj. 通晓多种语言的；多种语言的 n. 精通数国语言的人；数种文字的对照本

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- multilingual（多语言的）
- linguist（语言学家）
- bilingual（双语的）
- cosmopolitan（世界性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a true polyglot / polyglot community / polyglot skills / polyglot edition**

👉 Example: As a polyglot, she effortlessly switched between five languages during the international summit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **polyglot** diplomat was invaluable during multilateral negotiations, translating nuances that automated systems frequently missed entirely.
   这位通晓多种语言的外交官在多边谈判中极具价值，能翻译自动系统经常完全遗漏的细微差别。

2. Growing up in a **polyglot** household, the children naturally acquired fluency in three languages before entering formal schooling.
   在多语言家庭中长大，孩子们在接受正规教育之前就自然地掌握了三种语言的流利表达。

3. The university library housed a rare **polyglot** Bible containing parallel translations in Hebrew, Greek, Latin, and Aramaic.
   大学图书馆收藏了一本珍贵的多语对照圣经，包含希伯来语、希腊语、拉丁语和阿拉姆语的对照翻译。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "poly" (many) + "glot" (tongue/language) — a polyglot has "many tongues," someone who can speak in many languages.
把"polyglot"拆成"poly（多）"+"glot（舌头/语言）"——一个有"很多舌头"的人，能说多种语言。
