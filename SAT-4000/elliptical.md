# 🔵 elliptical

🔊 /ɪˈlɪptɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the shape of an ellipse; oval; (of speech or writing) using or involving the omission of words; deliberately obscure or ambiguous
**中文：** adj. 椭圆的；省略的；晦涩的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oval（椭圆的）
- oblique（间接的）
- ambiguous（含糊的）
- cryptic（隐晦的）
- terse（简洁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elliptical orbit / elliptical shape / elliptical speech / elliptical construction / elliptical sentence**

👉 Example: The poet's elliptical style requires careful reading to uncover the deeper layers of meaning.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The planets in our solar system travel along **elliptical** orbits around the sun rather than perfectly circular paths.
   我们太阳系中的行星沿着椭圆形轨道围绕太阳运行，而非完美的圆形轨道。

2. The novelist's **elliptical** prose style deliberately omits key details, requiring readers to infer meaning from subtle contextual clues.
   这位小说家省略式的散文风格故意省略关键细节，要求读者从微妙的上下文线索中推断含义。

3. Her **elliptical** response to the journalist's question left the audience uncertain about her actual position on the issue.
   她对记者提问的含糊回答让观众对她在这个问题上的实际立场感到不确定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elliptical" as related to "ellipsis" (...) — an ellipsis omits words, and an elliptical sentence omits parts, just as an ellipse is a circle with parts "squeezed" away.
把"elliptical"和"ellipsis（省略号……）"联系起来——省略号省略了词语，椭圆句省略了部分内容，就像椭圆是被"挤压"掉部分的圆。
