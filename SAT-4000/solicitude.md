# 🤗 solicitude

🔊 /səˈlɪsɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of anxiety or concern; eager attentiveness; care or concern for someone or something
**中文：** n. 关怀

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concern（关心）
- anxiety（焦虑）
- attentiveness（关注）
- care（关怀）
- worry（担忧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solicitude for / solicitude about / show solicitude / maternal solicitude**

👉 Example: The physician's solicitude for her patients extended well beyond routine clinical obligations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The director expressed deep **solicitude** for the welfare of displaced communities affected by the recent environmental disaster.
   主任对近期环境灾害中受影响的流离失所社区的福祉表示了深切的关怀。

2. Her constant **solicitude** about the accuracy of the data reflected her unwavering commitment to scientific integrity.
   她对数据准确性的持续关注反映了她对科学诚信的坚定承诺。

3. The mentor's **solicitude** for his students' academic development inspired them to pursue excellence in their respective fields.
   导师对学生学术发展的关怀激励他们在各自的领域追求卓越。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solicitude" as "solicit + attitude" — an attitude of actively seeking to help and care for others.
把"solicitude"想成"solicit + attitude"——一种主动寻求帮助和关怀他人的态度。
