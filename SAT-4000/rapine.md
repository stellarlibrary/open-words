# ⚔️ rapine

🔊 /ˈræpɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The violent seizure of someone's property; plunder or pillage
**中文：** n. 掠夺；劫掠

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plunder（掠夺）
- pillage（劫掠）
- looting（抢劫）
- spoliation（抢夺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acts of rapine / rapine and destruction / rapine and pillage / widespread rapine**

👉 Example: The conquered territories suffered years of rapine and devastation at the hands of occupying forces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chronicles documented widespread **rapine** and destruction that followed the collapse of the central government's authority.
   编年史记录了中央政府权威崩溃后随之而来的大规模掠夺和破坏。

2. Scholars argue that acts of **rapine** during wartime have been a persistent feature of armed conflict throughout recorded history.
   学者们认为，战时的劫掠行为是有记载的历史中武装冲突的持久特征。

3. The museum exhibit depicted scenes of **rapine** committed by colonial powers against indigenous populations during the imperial era.
   博物馆展览描绘了殖民列强在帝国时代对原住民犯下的掠夺场景。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rapine" as "rap + pine" — imagine someone rapping (seizing) all the pine trees: violent plunder of resources.
把"rapine"想成"rap（猛击）+ pine（松树）"——想象有人猛力夺走所有松树：暴力掠夺资源。
