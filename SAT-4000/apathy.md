# 😑 apathy

🔊 /ˈæp.ə.θi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Lack of interest, enthusiasm, or concern; a state of indifference or emotional detachment from matters that typically engage others.

**中文：** 冷漠，无兴趣，漠不关心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- indifference（冷漠）
- unconcern（漠不关心）
- lethargy（无精打采）
- passivity（被动）
- disinterest（不感兴趣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **voter apathy**, **public apathy**, **apathy toward/about** (something), **overcome apathy**, **widespread apathy**, **sense of apathy**.

> The campaign sought to combat the growing **apathy** among eligible voters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Widespread **apathy** among citizens allowed the corrupt administration to operate without meaningful opposition.
   公民中普遍的冷漠使腐败政府得以在没有实质性反对的情况下运作。

2. The professor lamented the intellectual **apathy** that had permeated the once distinguished academic department.
   教授哀叹曾经杰出的学术部门弥漫着的学术冷漠。

3. Overcoming public **apathy** remains the greatest challenge for organizations advocating environmental reform.
   克服公众的冷漠仍然是倡导环境改革的组织面临的最大挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"A-" (without) + "-pathy" (feeling, from Greek *pathos*). **Apathy** = without feeling. Compare with "sympathy" (feeling with someone) and "empathy" (feeling into someone).

词根记忆：a-（没有）+ -pathy（感情，源自希腊语 pathos）。apathy = 没有感情。与 sympathy（同情）和 empathy（共情）对比记忆。
