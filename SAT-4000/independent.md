# 🗽 independent

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Free from outside control or influence; self-governing; not relying on others. (n.) A person who is not affiliated with any political party
**中文：** adj. 独立的；自主的；无党派的 n. 独立自主者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- autonomous（自治的）
- self-reliant（自力更生的）
- sovereign（主权的）
- self-sufficient（自给自足的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **independent of / independent thinking / independent variable / fiercely independent**

👉 Example: The commission must remain independent of political influence to ensure fair and impartial investigations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly **independent** nation faced the enormous challenge of establishing functional governmental institutions from the ground up.
   这个新独立的国家面临着从零开始建立功能性政府机构的巨大挑战。

2. The researcher insisted on conducting an **independent** analysis of the data to verify the conclusions presented in the original study.
   研究人员坚持对数据进行独立分析，以验证原始研究中提出的结论。

3. Growing up in a rural community taught her to be fiercely **independent** and resourceful when confronting unexpected obstacles.
   在农村社区长大教会了她在面对意外障碍时要极其独立且足智多谋。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-depend-ent" — someone who is NOT dependent on others, standing firmly on their own two feet without external support.
把"independent"想成"不depend（依赖）的"——一个不依赖他人的人，靠自己的双脚稳稳站立，不需要外部支持。
