# 💎 flaunt

🔊 /flɔːnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To display ostentatiously or boastfully; to show off something in order to impress others. (n.) The act of flaunting
**中文：** v. 炫耀；夸耀；飘扬 n. 炫耀

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- show off（炫耀）
- parade（夸示）
- brandish（炫示）
- boast（吹嘘）
- exhibit（展示）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **flaunt one's wealth / flaunt one's success / flaunt it / flaunt convention**

👉 Example: He liked to flaunt his expensive watch at every social gathering he attended.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The billionaire would constantly **flaunt** his extravagant lifestyle on social media, attracting both admiration and widespread criticism.
   这位亿万富翁不断在社交媒体上炫耀其奢华的生活方式，既引来了羡慕也招致了广泛的批评。

2. Critics argued that the corporation chose to **flaunt** its profits rather than invest meaningfully in community development programs.
   批评人士认为，该公司选择炫耀其利润，而非有意义地投资社区发展项目。

3. The peacock seemed to **flaunt** its magnificent plumage, spreading its iridescent feathers in a spectacular chromatic display.
   孔雀似乎在炫耀其华丽的羽毛，展开五彩斑斓的翎羽呈现出壮观的色彩展示。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "flaunt" as "fla-unt" — like an aunt who always flashes her jewelry at family reunions to show off.
把"flaunt"想成"fla-aunt"——就像一位在家庭聚会上总爱展示珠宝炫耀的阿姨（aunt）。
