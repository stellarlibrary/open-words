# 🛡️ insulate

🔊 /ˈɪnsjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To isolate or separate from surroundings; to protect from heat, cold, electricity, or sound by surrounding with non-conducting material
**中文：** v. 隔离，使孤立；使绝缘，使隔热

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- isolate（隔离）
- shield（屏蔽）
- protect（保护）
- segregate（隔开）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insulate from / insulate against / insulate a building / insulate wires**

👉 Example: The thick walls help insulate the building from extreme temperatures throughout the year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The contractor recommended using fiberglass panels to **insulate** the attic and reduce overall heating costs significantly.
   承包商建议使用玻璃纤维板来隔热阁楼，从而大幅降低整体供暖成本。

2. Wealthy communities often **insulate** themselves from the economic hardships that affect the broader population.
   富裕社区往往将自己与影响更广泛人群的经济困难隔离开来。

3. Engineers must properly **insulate** electrical wiring to prevent short circuits and ensure workplace safety standards.
   工程师必须正确地对电线进行绝缘处理，以防止短路并确保工作场所的安全标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insulate" as "in-sula-te" — "insula" means "island" in Latin. An island is surrounded by water, isolated from the mainland, just as insulation surrounds and separates.
把"insulate"想成"insula（岛屿）"——岛屿被水包围，与大陆隔离，就像绝缘材料包裹并隔离物体一样。
