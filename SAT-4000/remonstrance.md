# 📢 remonstrance

🔊 /rɪˈmɒnstrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A forceful protest or objection; an earnest presentation of reasons for opposition or complaint
**中文：** n. 抗议；规劝；谏书

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- protest（抗议）
- objection（反对）
- grievance（不满）
- expostulation（规劝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a remonstrance against / file a remonstrance / formal remonstrance / despite remonstrance**

👉 Example: The citizens delivered a formal remonstrance against the proposed tax increase to the legislature.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The faculty submitted a written **remonstrance** to the university administration protesting the sudden reduction of research funding allocations.
   教职人员向大学管理层提交了一份书面抗议书，抗议研究经费拨款的突然削减。

2. Despite repeated **remonstrance** from environmental organizations, the government proceeded with the construction of the coastal pipeline.
   尽管环保组织多次抗议，政府仍然继续推进沿海管道的建设。

3. The ambassador's formal **remonstrance** regarding the trade restrictions was presented before the international diplomatic assembly last week.
   大使就贸易限制提出的正式抗议书已于上周在国际外交大会上提交。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remonstrance" as "re-monster-ance" — when something monstrous happens, you protest and raise a remonstrance.
把"remonstrance"想成"re-monster-ance"——当发生可怕的事情时，你会抗议并提出异议。
