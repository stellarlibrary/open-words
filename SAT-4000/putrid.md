# 🤢 putrid

🔊 /ˈpjuːtrɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) In a state of decay or decomposition; producing a foul smell; extremely unpleasant or disgusting
**中文：** adj. 腐烂的；腐败的；令人厌恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rotten（腐烂的）
- decayed（腐朽的）
- fetid（恶臭的）
- rancid（腐臭的）
- foul（恶心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **putrid smell / putrid flesh / putrid odor / putrid conditions / putrid taste**

👉 Example: The putrid smell emanating from the abandoned warehouse prompted residents to alert the authorities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Health inspectors immediately shut down the restaurant after discovering **putrid** meat stored in an unrefrigerated room.
   卫生检查员在发现未冷藏房间中存放的腐烂肉类后，立即关闭了这家餐厅。

2. The **putrid** stench of the polluted river could be detected from several blocks away during the summer months.
   在夏季的几个月里，几个街区外就能闻到受污染河流发出的恶臭。

3. Archaeologists carefully extracted the **putrid** remains from the burial site while wearing protective equipment and respirators.
   考古学家戴着防护设备和呼吸器，小心翼翼地从墓地中提取腐烂的遗骸。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "put-rid" as "put + rid" — you want to get rid of something putrid because it is so rotten and disgusting you cannot stand it.
把"putrid"想成"put + rid（摆脱）"——你想摆脱腐烂的东西，因为它太恶心了，让人无法忍受。
