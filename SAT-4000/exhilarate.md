# 🎉 exhilarate

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make someone feel very happy, animated, or elated; to enliven or invigorate
**中文：** v. 使高兴；使振奋；使愉快

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thrill（使兴奋）
- elate（使高兴）
- invigorate（使充满活力）
- animate（使振奋）
- stimulate（刺激）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exhilarate the crowd / exhilarated by the experience / exhilarating adventure / feel exhilarated**

👉 Example: The breathtaking mountain scenery exhilarated every member of the expedition team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orchestra's powerful performance **exhilarated** the audience, leaving them in a state of profound emotional uplift.
   管弦乐团强有力的演奏使观众振奋不已，让他们处于一种深刻的情感升华之中。

2. Scientific breakthroughs have the capacity to **exhilarate** researchers and renew their commitment to further discovery and inquiry.
   科学突破有能力使研究人员振奋，并重新激发他们对进一步发现和探索的热情。

3. The prospect of exploring uncharted territory **exhilarated** the young adventurers, who prepared eagerly for their departure.
   探索未知领域的前景使年轻的冒险者们兴奋不已，他们热切地为出发做准备。

---

把"exhilarate"想成"ex-HILAR-ate"——其中包含"hilar"，像"hilarious"（滑稽的），让你大笑并感到极度开心。
