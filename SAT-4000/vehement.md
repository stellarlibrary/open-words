# 🔥 vehement

🔊 /ˈviːəmənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing strong feeling; forceful, passionate, or intense in expression or conviction
**中文：** adj. 激烈的，猛烈的；热烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fervent（热烈的）
- passionate（激情的）
- fierce（猛烈的）
- ardent（热切的）
- intense（强烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vehement opposition / vehement denial / vehement protest / vehement criticism**

👉 Example: The senator voiced vehement opposition to the proposed budget cuts affecting public education.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members expressed **vehement** disagreement with the proposed policy changes during the heated legislative session.
   委员会成员在激烈的立法会议上对拟议的政策变更表达了强烈的反对。

2. Despite **vehement** protests from environmental groups, the corporation proceeded with its plans to construct the industrial facility.
   尽管环保团体提出了激烈的抗议，该公司仍继续推进其建造工业设施的计划。

3. The scholar delivered a **vehement** defense of academic freedom, arguing that intellectual inquiry must remain unrestricted.
   这位学者对学术自由进行了热烈的辩护，认为学术探究必须保持不受限制。

---

把"vehement"想成"VEry HEated MENTal"——某人的精神状态因某事而非常激烈和强烈。
