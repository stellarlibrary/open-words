# 🌐 ubiquitous

🔊 /juːˈbɪkwɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Present, appearing, or found everywhere; seeming to exist or be encountered at every turn
**中文：** adj. 普遍存在的；无所不在的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- omnipresent（无所不在的）
- pervasive（弥漫的）
- universal（普遍的）
- prevalent（盛行的）
- widespread（广泛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ubiquitous presence / ubiquitous in modern life / ubiquitous technology / become ubiquitous**

👉 Example: Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society, transforming how people communicate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ubiquitous** presence of surveillance cameras in urban centers has sparked an intense debate about privacy and civil liberties.
   城市中心无处不在的监控摄像头引发了关于隐私和公民自由的激烈辩论。

2. Plastic pollution has become so **ubiquitous** that microplastics have been detected in the most remote oceanic and polar regions.
   塑料污染已变得如此普遍，以至于在最偏远的海洋和极地地区都检测到了微塑料。

3. The researcher noted that social media platforms are now **ubiquitous** tools for political mobilization across diverse demographics.
   研究人员指出，社交媒体平台现在已成为跨不同人群进行政治动员的无处不在的工具。

---

Think of "ubiquitous" as "you-be-quit-us" — no matter where you go, you cannot quit encountering it because it is everywhere.
把"ubiquitous"想成"you-be-quit-us"——无论你走到哪里，你都无法避开它，因为它无处不在。
