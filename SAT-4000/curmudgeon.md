# 😤 curmudgeon

🔊 /kərˈmʌdʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A bad-tempered, difficult, or cantankerous person, especially an older one
**中文：** n. 脾气坏的人，乖戾的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grouch（爱发牢骚的人）
- crank（怪人）
- grumbler（抱怨者）
- sourpuss（阴沉的人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **old curmudgeon / lovable curmudgeon / neighborhood curmudgeon / a bit of a curmudgeon**

👉 Example: Despite his reputation as a curmudgeon, the elderly professor genuinely cared about his students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literary critic was widely regarded as a **curmudgeon** whose harsh reviews intimidated even the most established authors.
   这位文学评论家被普遍认为是一个脾气乖戾的人，他尖锐的评论甚至让最知名的作家也感到畏惧。

2. Beneath the exterior of a **curmudgeon**, the retired judge possessed a generous spirit that he rarely displayed in public.
   在这位退休法官乖戾的外表之下，隐藏着一颗慷慨的心，只是他很少在公众面前展示。

3. The village **curmudgeon** complained loudly at every town meeting about proposed changes to the community's longstanding traditions.
   村里那个脾气坏的人在每次镇会议上都大声抱怨对社区长期传统的拟议变更。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cur-mudgeon" as "cur (angry dog) + mudgeon (grudge)" — like an angry dog holding a grudge, always grumpy and irritable.
把"curmudgeon"想成"cur（恶犬）+ mudgeon（怨恨）"——像一只怀恨在心的恶犬，总是脾气暴躁、易怒。
