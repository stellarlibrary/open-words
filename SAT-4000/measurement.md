# 📏 measurement

🔊 /ˈmeʒərmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act or process of measuring; a figure, extent, or amount obtained by measuring; a system of measuring
**中文：** n. 测量；度量；尺寸；量度制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dimension（尺寸）
- quantification（量化）
- assessment（评估）
- calibration（校准）
- gauge（测量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **take measurements / accurate measurement / measurement of / measurement system / unit of measurement**

👉 Example: Precise measurement of atmospheric conditions is essential for reliable weather forecasting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect took careful **measurements** of the site before drafting the blueprints for the proposed commercial building.
   建筑师在为拟建商业大楼绘制蓝图之前，仔细测量了场地的尺寸。

2. Advances in quantum **measurement** techniques have enabled scientists to observe phenomena previously beyond the limits of detection.
   量子测量技术的进步使科学家能够观测到以前超出检测极限的现象。

3. The **measurement** of student performance through standardized testing remains a subject of considerable debate among educators.
   通过标准化考试来衡量学生表现仍然是教育工作者间持续争论的话题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "measure + ment" — the suffix "-ment" turns the verb "measure" into a noun, representing the result or process of measuring something.
把"measurement"拆成"measure + ment"——后缀"-ment"将动词"measure"变为名词，表示测量的过程或结果。
