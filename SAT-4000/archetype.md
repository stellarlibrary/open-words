# 🎭 archetype

🔊 /ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An original model or pattern from which all things of the same kind are copied or based; a universally recognized symbol or character type
**中文：** n. 原型；典型

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prototype（原型）
- model（模型）
- paradigm（范例）
- exemplar（典范）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the archetype of / a classic archetype / Jungian archetype / archetypal hero / cultural archetype**

👉 Example: The hero's journey is a classic archetype found in mythology across every human civilization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cunning trickster is an **archetype** that appears consistently in the folklore and mythology of diverse cultures.
   狡猾的骗子是一种在不同文化的民间传说和神话中反复出现的原型。

2. Literary scholars regard Odysseus as the **archetype** of the resourceful wanderer who overcomes impossible obstacles through cunning.
   文学学者将奥德修斯视为足智多谋的流浪者原型，他凭借智慧克服了不可能的障碍。

3. The psychologist Carl Jung proposed that universal **archetypes** reside deep within the collective unconscious of all humanity.
   心理学家卡尔·荣格提出，普遍的原型深藏于全人类的集体无意识之中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arche-type" — "arche" means original or first (as in "archaic"), and "type" means a kind. An archetype is the first type, the original model.
把"archetype"拆成"arche（最初的）+ type（类型）"——原型就是最初的类型，是一切同类事物的模板。
