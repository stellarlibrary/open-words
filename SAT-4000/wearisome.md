# 😩 wearisome

🔊 /ˈwɪrisəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing one to feel tired or bored; tediously dull or monotonous
**中文：** adj. 使疲倦的；使厌倦的；乏味的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tedious（乏味的）
- tiresome（令人厌倦的）
- monotonous（单调的）
- dreary（沉闷的）
- laborious（费力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wearisome task / wearisome journey / wearisome process / wearisome routine**

👉 Example: The wearisome negotiations dragged on for months without producing any meaningful progress.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students found the professor's **wearisome** lectures on procedural regulations almost impossible to endure throughout the semester.
   学生们发现教授关于程序规定的乏味讲座几乎让人整个学期都难以忍受。

2. After a **wearisome** day of bureaucratic paperwork, the diplomat longed for a quiet evening of rest and reflection.
   经过一天令人疲惫的官僚文书工作后，这位外交官渴望一个安静的休息和反思的夜晚。

3. The committee acknowledged that the **wearisome** review process needed to be streamlined to improve overall institutional efficiency.
   委员会承认，令人厌倦的审查流程需要精简以提高整体机构效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wearisome" as "weary + some" — something that gives you weariness, making you feel tired and bored.
把"wearisome"想成"weary（疲倦的）+ some"——某种让你感到疲倦的事物，使你感到厌倦和无聊。
