# 💘 amorous

🔊 /ˈæmərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing, feeling, or relating to romantic or sexual love or desire.
**中文：** 多情的；恋爱的；热情的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- passionate（热情的）
- romantic（浪漫的）
- enamored（迷恋的）
- affectionate（深情的）
- ardent（热烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glances, feelings, advances, attention, encounters**
👉 Example: He cast amorous glances at her across the crowded ballroom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet composed **amorous** verses dedicated to his beloved muse throughout the spring season.
   诗人整个春天都在为他心爱的缪斯创作多情的诗篇。

2. Her **amorous** disposition made her the subject of considerable admiration among her acquaintances.
   她多情的性格使她在熟人中受到相当多的仰慕。

3. The novel depicted the **amorous** entanglements of aristocratic families during the eighteenth century.
   这部小说描绘了十八世纪贵族家庭之间的恋爱纠葛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "amor" (Latin for love) + "-ous" (full of) = full of love, amorous.
想想拉丁语"amor"（爱）加上"-ous"（充满），就是"充满爱的"，即多情的。
