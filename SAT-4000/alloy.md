# 🔩 alloy

🔊 /ˈælɔɪ/ (n.) /əˈlɔɪ/ (v.)<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A metal made by combining two or more metallic elements. (v.) To mix metals together to form an alloy
**中文：** n. 合金 v. 使成合金；掺混

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blend（混合物）
- mixture（混合）
- amalgam（汞合金；混合物）
- compound（化合物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **metal alloy / steel alloy / alloy of copper and tin / alloyed with**

👉 Example: Bronze is an alloy composed primarily of copper and tin, valued for its durability.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers developed a new titanium **alloy** that offers superior strength while remaining significantly lighter than conventional structural steel.
   工程师们开发了一种新的钛合金，在保持比传统结构钢轻得多的同时提供了卓越的强度。

2. The jeweler chose to **alloy** gold with a small percentage of copper to increase the metal's hardness and overall durability.
   珠宝商选择将黄金与少量铜合金化，以增加金属的硬度和整体耐用性。

3. Modern aircraft rely heavily on lightweight aluminum **alloys** to achieve optimal fuel efficiency without compromising structural integrity or safety.
   现代飞机在很大程度上依赖轻质铝合金，以在不影响结构完整性或安全性的情况下实现最佳燃油效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alloy" as "all-oy" — when "all" metals join together, they form "oy," a new combined substance.
把"alloy"想成"all-oy"——当"所有"金属结合在一起时，它们就形成了"oy"，一种新的混合物质。
