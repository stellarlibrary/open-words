# 🎯 precise

🔊 /prɪˈsaɪs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Marked by exactness and accuracy of expression or detail; strictly correct or defined.
**中文：** adj. 精确的；准确的；明确的；严格的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exact（精确的）
- accurate（准确的）
- meticulous（一丝不苟的）
- specific（具体的）
- definite（明确的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **precise measurements / precise instructions / to be precise / at that precise moment**

👉 Example: The engineer required precise measurements before approving the final design specifications for the bridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon's **precise** movements during the delicate operation ensured that no surrounding tissue was damaged.
   外科医生在这场精细手术中精确的动作确保了周围组织没有受到损伤。

2. The witness was unable to provide a **precise** account of the events due to the chaos and confusion at the scene.
   由于现场的混乱和慌张，证人无法提供对事件的精确描述。

3. To be **precise**, the experiment yielded a success rate of ninety-seven point three percent across all controlled trials.
   准确地说，该实验在所有对照试验中的成功率为百分之九十七点三。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-CISE" — like using scissors ("cise" from Latin "caedere," to cut) to cut exactly on the line, with no room for error.
把"precise"想成"pre（预先）+ cise（切割）"——像用剪刀精确地沿线剪裁，没有任何误差。
