# 🤝 cohesive

🔊 /kəʊˈhiːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Characterized by forming a united, well-integrated whole; tending to stick together or cohere
**中文：** adj. 凝聚的；有结合力的；紧密结合的；黏着的；黏合性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unified（统一的）
- integrated（整合的）
- connected（紧密联系的）
- consolidated（巩固的）
- tenacious（黏性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cohesive unit / cohesive force / cohesive team / cohesive structure**

👉 Example: The department functioned as a cohesive unit, with every member contributing to the shared objectives.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editor praised the manuscript for presenting a **cohesive** narrative that seamlessly wove together multiple complex storylines.
   编辑赞扬了这部手稿，因为它呈现了一个连贯的叙事，将多条复杂的故事线无缝地编织在一起。

2. A **cohesive** organizational culture enables companies to respond more effectively to external challenges and market disruptions.
   一种有凝聚力的组织文化使公司能够更有效地应对外部挑战和市场波动。

3. The research team developed a **cohesive** framework that integrated findings from biology, chemistry, and environmental science disciplines.
   研究团队开发了一个紧密结合的框架，整合了生物学、化学和环境科学学科的研究成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cohesive" as "co-hesive" — like "adhesive" (glue) with "co" (together), something cohesive acts like glue that holds parts together as a unified whole.
把"cohesive"想成"co（共同）-hesive（粘合的）"——就像带有"co（共同）"的"adhesive（胶水）"，有凝聚力的东西像胶水一样把各部分粘合成一个整体。
