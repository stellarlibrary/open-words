# 🏆 prowess

🔊 /ˈpraʊəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Great skill or expertise in a particular area; exceptional bravery or valor, especially in combat
**中文：** n. 英勇；超凡技术；勇猛；非凡的能力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expertise（专长）
- mastery（精通）
- valor（英勇）
- skill（技能）
- proficiency（熟练）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **athletic prowess / military prowess / intellectual prowess / prowess in something**

👉 Example: The young athlete demonstrated remarkable prowess on the field during the championship finals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her academic **prowess** earned her a prestigious scholarship to one of the most competitive universities in the nation.
   她卓越的学术能力为她赢得了全国最具竞争力的大学之一的著名奖学金。

2. The general's strategic **prowess** proved decisive in turning the tide of the prolonged military campaign.
   将军卓越的战略才能在扭转持久军事行动的局势中起到了决定性作用。

3. Critics praised the novelist's literary **prowess**, noting her exceptional ability to weave complex narratives with elegance.
   评论家称赞这位小说家的文学造诣，指出她以优雅方式编织复杂叙事的非凡能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prowess" as "pro + ESS" — a professional with the essence of skill, someone who is truly a "pro" at what they do.
把"prowess"想成"pro + ess"——一个拥有技能精髓的专业人士，一个真正的"行家"。
