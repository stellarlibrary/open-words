# 😤 peevish

🔊 /ˈpiːvɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easily irritated or annoyed, especially over trivial matters; bad-tempered and querulous
**中文：** adj. 易怒的；暴躁的；脾气坏的；爱发牢骚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritable（易怒的）
- petulant（任性的）
- querulous（爱抱怨的）
- cantankerous（脾气暴躁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peevish tone / peevish complaint / peevish mood / peevish remark**

👉 Example: The child grew peevish after missing his afternoon nap and refused to cooperate with anyone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manager's **peevish** response to a reasonable question revealed the mounting pressure he was experiencing from senior leadership.
   经理对一个合理问题暴躁的回应揭示了他正承受着来自高层领导的巨大压力。

2. Lack of adequate sleep can make even the most patient individuals **peevish** and prone to unnecessary conflicts at work.
   睡眠不足甚至会让最有耐心的人变得暴躁，容易在工作中产生不必要的冲突。

3. The novelist portrayed the aging patriarch as increasingly **peevish**, alienating family members with his constant complaints and criticisms.
   小说家将这位年迈的家长描绘得越来越暴躁，他不断的抱怨和批评疏远了家人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peev-ish" as related to "peeve" — a pet peeve is something that annoys you, so peevish means easily peeved or annoyed.
把"peevish"想成和"peeve（恼怒）"相关——pet peeve是让你恼火的事，所以peevish就是容易恼怒的。
