# ✅ right

🔊 /raɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb, noun, adverb | 形容词, 动词, 名词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Correct; straight; on the right side. (v.) To restore to a normal or upright position. (n.) That which is correct or just; the right-hand side. (adv.) Correctly; properly; completely
**中文：** adj. 直的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- correct（正确的）
- proper（适当的）
- straight（直的）
- just（公正的）
- rectify（纠正）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **right angle / right away / right a wrong / right to vote / the right direction**

👉 Example: The captain managed to right the vessel after it nearly capsized in the turbulent storm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineers worked tirelessly to **right** the structural imbalance before the building sustained any further irreversible damage from settling.
   工程师们不懈努力以纠正结构失衡，防止建筑因沉降遭受更多不可逆的损害。

2. Citizens have every **right** to demand transparency from their elected officials regarding the allocation of public financial resources.
   公民完全有权要求民选官员对公共财政资源的分配保持透明。

3. The new policy aims to **right** decades of systemic injustice by establishing equitable access to education and healthcare services.
   新政策旨在通过建立公平的教育和医疗服务途径来纠正数十年的系统性不公正。

Think of "right" as "write without the W" — when you write correctly, you get it right, and the line goes straight across the page.
把"right"想成"write去掉W"——当你正确书写时，你就做对了，线条笔直地横穿页面。
