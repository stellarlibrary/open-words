# 🧠 theoretical

🔊 /ˌθɪəˈretɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Based on or involving theory rather than practical application; existing only in theory, not proven or tested.
**中文：** adj. 理论的；理论上的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hypothetical（假设的）
- abstract（抽象的）
- speculative（推测的）
- conceptual（概念的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **theoretical framework / theoretical approach / purely theoretical / theoretical basis**

👉 Example: The distinction between theoretical knowledge and practical experience is crucial in scientific education.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research paper presented a **theoretical** framework for understanding the relationship between economic inequality and social unrest.
   该研究论文提出了一个理解经济不平等与社会动荡之间关系的理论框架。

2. While the concept remains purely **theoretical**, scientists believe it could revolutionize quantum computing within the next decade.
   虽然这一概念仍然纯粹是理论上的，但科学家们相信它可能在未来十年内革新量子计算。

3. Her **theoretical** contributions to astrophysics earned widespread recognition despite the difficulty of obtaining experimental verification.
   她对天体物理学的理论贡献获得了广泛认可，尽管实验验证很难获得。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "theoretical" as "theory + ical" — something that belongs to the world of theory, not yet touching reality.
把"theoretical"想成"theory + ical"——属于理论世界的东西，尚未触及现实。
