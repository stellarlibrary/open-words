# 🏗️ underlie

🔊 /ˌʌndərˈlaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To lie or exist beneath; to be the basis or foundation of something; to form the fundamental cause or reason
**中文：** 成为……的基础；位于……之下；构成……的根本原因

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- undergird（加强……的基础）
- support（支撑）
- underpin（巩固）
- form the basis of（构成……的基础）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **underlie a theory / underlie a problem / underlie a decision / principles that underlie**

👉 Example: Several fundamental assumptions underlie the entire framework of modern economic theory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Complex social and economic factors **underlie** the persistent achievement gap observed between students from different demographic backgrounds.
   复杂的社会和经济因素构成了不同人口背景学生之间持续存在的成绩差距的根本原因。

2. The geological survey revealed that massive limestone formations **underlie** the entire region, explaining the prevalence of caves and sinkholes.
   地质调查显示，大量石灰岩层位于整个地区之下，解释了洞穴和天坑的普遍存在。

3. Researchers sought to identify the biological mechanisms that **underlie** the development of resistance to commonly prescribed antibiotic medications.
   研究人员试图确定导致对常用抗生素产生耐药性的生物学机制。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "under-lie" — something that LIES UNDER the surface; the hidden foundation that supports everything above it.
把"underlie"想成"under（在……下面）+ lie（位于）"——位于表面之下的东西，是支撑上面一切的隐藏基础。
