# 📚 encyclopedia

🔊 /ɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A comprehensive reference work containing articles on a wide range of subjects or on numerous aspects of a particular field
**中文：** n. 百科全书

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compendium（概要）
- reference work（参考著作）
- almanac（年鉴）
- lexicon（词典）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an encyclopedia of knowledge / encyclopedia entry / consult an encyclopedia / a walking encyclopedia**

👉 Example: The professor was regarded as a walking encyclopedia of medieval European history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly published **encyclopedia** contains over fifty thousand entries covering every major scientific discipline known to scholars.
   这部新出版的百科全书包含五万多条目，涵盖了学者们所知的每一个主要科学学科。

2. Researchers frequently consult the online **encyclopedia** to verify historical facts before including them in academic publications.
   研究人员经常查阅在线百科全书，以在将历史事实纳入学术出版物之前加以核实。

3. The eighteen-volume **encyclopedia** served as the primary reference source for generations of students and intellectual inquirers.
   这套十八卷的百科全书曾是几代学生和求知者的主要参考来源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "encyclo-pedia" as "encircle + education" — it encircles all areas of knowledge and education in one complete work.
把"encyclopedia"想成"encircle（环绕）+ education（教育）"——它将所有知识和教育领域环绕在一部完整的著作中。
