# ✅ corroborate

🔊 /kəˈrɑːbəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To confirm or give support to a statement, theory, or finding; to make more certain
**中文：** v. 证实；确证；使坚固

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confirm（确认）
- verify（核实）
- substantiate（证实）
- validate（验证）
- authenticate（鉴定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **corroborate a story / corroborate evidence / corroborate testimony / corroborate findings**

👉 Example: The detective sought additional witnesses to corroborate the suspect's alibi.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly discovered documents **corroborate** the theory that the ancient civilization had extensive trade networks across the continent.
   新发现的文件证实了该古代文明拥有遍布大陆的广泛贸易网络的理论。

2. Several independent witnesses were able to **corroborate** the defendant's account of events on the night in question.
   几位独立证人能够证实被告对案发当晚事件的陈述。

3. The laboratory results **corroborate** the initial hypothesis, providing strong empirical evidence for the proposed mechanism of action.
   实验室结果证实了最初的假说，为所提出的作用机制提供了有力的实证依据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cor-rob-orate" as "co-" (together) + "roborate" (strengthen) — multiple sources come together to strengthen a claim.
把"corroborate"想成"co-"（一起）加上"roborate"（加强）——多个来源汇聚在一起来加强一个说法。
