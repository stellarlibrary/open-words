# 😤 irritable

🔊 /ˈɪrɪtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easily annoyed or made angry; having a tendency to become upset over minor things
**中文：** 易怒的；急躁的；过敏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irascible（易怒的）
- testy（暴躁的）
- petulant（任性的）
- cantankerous（脾气坏的）
- peevish（易怒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irritable mood / irritable temperament / irritable bowel syndrome / become irritable**

👉 Example: Lack of sleep made the students increasingly irritable during the final examination period.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged isolation during the quarantine period left many residents feeling anxious, restless, and profoundly **irritable**.
   隔离期间长时间的隔绝使许多居民感到焦虑、不安和极度易怒。

2. Chronic pain sufferers often become **irritable** when their condition is dismissed or inadequately treated by medical professionals.
   慢性疼痛患者在其病情被医疗专业人员忽视或治疗不当时往往变得易怒。

3. The committee chairman grew visibly **irritable** as delegates continued to raise objections to the proposed amendments without offering alternatives.
   随着代表们不断对提议的修正案提出反对意见却不提供替代方案，委员会主席明显变得烦躁起来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irritable" as "irritate + able" — someone who is able to be irritated very easily, like a short fuse ready to blow.
把"irritable"想成"irritate（激怒）+ -able（容易……的）"——容易被激怒的人，就像一根随时会爆的短导火索。
