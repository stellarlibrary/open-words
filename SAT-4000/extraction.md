# 🧪 extraction

🔊 /ɪkˈstrækʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act or process of removing or obtaining something from a source; one's ethnic origin or descent
**中文：** n. 取出；抽出；拔出；萃取；抽出物；出身

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- removal（取出）
- withdrawal（抽取）
- separation（分离）
- derivation（起源）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **data extraction / oil extraction / tooth extraction / of French extraction**

👉 Example: The dentist performed a routine extraction of the impacted wisdom tooth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **extraction** of natural resources from developing nations has long been a subject of ethical debate among scholars.
   从发展中国家提取自然资源长期以来一直是学者们道德争论的话题。

2. Advanced chemical **extraction** techniques allow scientists to isolate valuable compounds from previously inaccessible plant materials.
   先进的化学萃取技术使科学家能够从以前无法获取的植物材料中分离出有价值的化合物。

3. The historian discovered that the renowned diplomat was of Italian **extraction**, tracing his ancestry back to Renaissance Florence.
   这位历史学家发现，这位著名外交官是意大利血统，其祖先可追溯到文艺复兴时期的佛罗伦萨。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extraction" as "ex- (out) + tract (pull) + ion" — the action of pulling something out from its source.
把"extraction"想成"ex-（出来）+ tract（拉）+ ion"——从来源中拉出某物的动作。
