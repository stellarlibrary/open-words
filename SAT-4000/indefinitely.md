# ♾️ indefinitely

🔊 /ɪnˈdɛfɪnɪtli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** For an unlimited or unspecified period of time; in a vague or unclear manner without definite limits
**中文：** adv. 无限期地；不确定地；模糊地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- endlessly（无尽地）
- perpetually（永久地）
- vaguely（模糊地）
- unlimitedly（无限地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **postponed indefinitely / continue indefinitely / suspended indefinitely / delayed indefinitely**

👉 Example: The construction project was postponed indefinitely due to a severe shortage of funding.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university announced that all in-person classes would be suspended **indefinitely** until public health conditions improved significantly.
   大学宣布所有面授课程将无限期暂停，直到公共卫生状况显著改善。

2. Scientists warn that certain pollutants can persist **indefinitely** in the environment, posing long-term risks to ecological systems.
   科学家警告说，某些污染物可以在环境中无限期存在，对生态系统构成长期风险。

3. The diplomatic negotiations were adjourned **indefinitely** after both sides failed to reach a compromise on the territorial dispute.
   在双方未能就领土争端达成妥协后，外交谈判被无限期休会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-definite-ly" — without any definite end, like a road stretching on with no visible destination or stopping point.
把"indefinitely"想成"没有definite（确定的）尽头"——就像一条看不到目的地或终点的路一直延伸下去。
