# 🔪 cull

🔊 /kʌl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To select carefully from a large quantity; to gather or collect; to remove or eliminate the inferior members of a group. (n.) An animal removed from a group
**中文：** v. 精选；采集；剔除 n. 剔出的动物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- select（挑选）
- glean（采集）
- eliminate（剔除）
- weed out（淘汰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cull from / cull information / cull the herd / cull the weak**

👉 Example: The editor had to cull the best essays from over three hundred submissions for the anthology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher spent months **culling** relevant data from thousands of archived documents in the national library.
   研究人员花了数月时间从国家图书馆的数千份档案文件中精选相关数据。

2. Wildlife authorities decided to **cull** the deer population to prevent overgrazing and ecological damage in the region.
   野生动物管理部门决定剔除部分鹿群，以防止该地区过度放牧和生态破坏。

3. The admissions committee carefully **culled** the most promising applications from an exceptionally competitive pool of candidates.
   招生委员会从竞争异常激烈的候选人中仔细精选出最有前途的申请。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cull" as "cut + pull" — you cut out and pull away the unwanted items to keep only the best.
把"cull"想成"cut + pull"——你剪除并拉走不需要的东西，只保留最好的。
