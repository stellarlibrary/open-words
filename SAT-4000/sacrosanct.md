# 🛡️ sacrosanct

🔊 /ˈsækroʊˌsæŋkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Regarded as too important or valuable to be interfered with; inviolable and supremely sacred
**中文：** adj. 不可侵犯的；极神圣的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inviolable（不可侵犯的）
- hallowed（神圣的）
- sacred（神圣的）
- untouchable（不可触碰的）
- sanctified（神圣化的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sacrosanct right / sacrosanct principle / sacrosanct tradition / regarded as sacrosanct**

👉 Example: Freedom of speech is considered sacrosanct in democratic societies and must be vigilantly protected.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The constitutional right to a fair trial remains **sacrosanct** even during periods of national emergency or civil unrest.
   即使在国家紧急状态或社会动荡时期，宪法赋予的公正审判权仍然是神圣不可侵犯的。

2. For generations, the family's Sunday dinner tradition was treated as **sacrosanct** and no one dared to suggest canceling it.
   几代人以来，这个家庭的周日晚餐传统被视为神圣不可侵犯的，没有人敢提议取消。

3. The professor maintained that academic freedom is **sacrosanct** and must never be compromised by political interference.
   这位教授坚持认为学术自由是极其神圣的，绝不能因政治干预而受到损害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sacro-sanct" — double sacred! "Sacro" means sacred and "sanct" also means holy, so it is doubly sacred and untouchable.
把"sacrosanct"拆解为"sacro（神圣的）+sanct（圣洁的）"——双重神圣！所以是极其神圣、不可侵犯的。
