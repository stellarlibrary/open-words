# 🧩 complement

🔊 /ˈkɒmplɪment/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Something that completes or makes perfect; a grammatical complement; the full number or quantity. (v.) To complete or enhance by providing what is lacking
**中文：** n. 补语；补足物 v. 补足，补充

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supplement（补充）
- counterpart（对应物）
- companion（配对物）
- complete（使完整）
- enhance（增强）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complement each other / a perfect complement to / complement the flavor / full complement of**

👉 Example: The bold red wine served as a perfect complement to the rich chocolate dessert.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect designed the modern addition to **complement** the historic building's original character rather than compete with it.
   建筑师设计了现代化的扩建部分，以补充历史建筑的原始特色，而不是与之竞争。

2. A full **complement** of security personnel was deployed throughout the venue to ensure the safety of all conference attendees.
   场馆各处部署了全部安保人员，以确保所有与会者的安全。

3. The professor explained that a subject **complement** follows a linking verb and provides additional information about the sentence's subject.
   教授解释说，主语补语跟在连系动词之后，提供关于句子主语的附加信息。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "complEment" with an E as "complEte" — a complement completes something, making it whole and perfect.
把"complement"中的E想成"complete"——补足物使某物完整，使其完美。
