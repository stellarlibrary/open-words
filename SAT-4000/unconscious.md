# 💤 unconscious

🔊 /ʌnˈkɒnʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not awake or aware of one's surroundings; done without conscious thought or intention
**中文：** 无意识的；失去知觉的；不自觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unaware（未察觉的）
- insensible（无知觉的）
- oblivious（不知道的）
- involuntary（无意的）
- unwitting（不知情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unconscious bias / unconscious mind / fall unconscious / knock unconscious / unconscious of**

👉 Example: The patient was found unconscious on the floor and was immediately rushed to the emergency room.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent studies suggest that **unconscious** biases can significantly influence hiring decisions even among well-intentioned and experienced managers.
   最近的研究表明，无意识的偏见可以显著影响招聘决策，即使是善意且经验丰富的管理者也不例外。

2. The boxer was knocked **unconscious** in the third round by a devastating combination of powerful and precisely targeted punches.
   拳击手在第三回合被一连串强有力且精准的组合拳击昏。

3. Many of our daily habits are driven by **unconscious** mental processes that operate well below the threshold of deliberate awareness.
   我们许多日常习惯是由无意识的心理过程驱动的，这些过程远低于主动意识的门槛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-conscious" — the prefix "un" negates "conscious" (aware), so you are either physically knocked out or mentally unaware of something.
把"unconscious"想成"un（不）+ conscious（有意识的）"——失去意识，要么身体上昏迷，要么心理上不自觉。
