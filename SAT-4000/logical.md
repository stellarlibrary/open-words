# 🧠 logical

🔊 /ˈlɒdʒɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Based on or in accordance with the principles of logic; reasonable and sensible; of or relating to logic as a discipline
**中文：** adj. 合逻辑的，合理的；逻辑学的；符合逻辑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rational（理性的）
- reasonable（合理的）
- coherent（连贯的）
- sound（合理的）
- analytical（分析的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **logical conclusion / logical reasoning / logical step / logical argument / logical thinking**

👉 Example: The detective followed a logical sequence of deductions to identify the suspect.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor presented a **logical** framework for analyzing the complex ethical dilemmas facing modern biotechnology researchers.
   教授提出了一个合乎逻辑的框架，用于分析现代生物技术研究人员面临的复杂伦理困境。

2. It is entirely **logical** to assume that increased investment in education will yield substantial long-term economic benefits.
   完全合乎逻辑地认为，增加教育投资将带来可观的长期经济效益。

3. Her argument was so **logical** and well-structured that even her most vocal critics found it difficult to refute.
   她的论点如此合乎逻辑且结构严密，即使是最激烈的批评者也难以反驳。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "logic-al" — something full of "logic" is naturally logical, following clear and orderly reasoning.
把"logical"想成"充满logic（逻辑）的"——充满逻辑的事物自然是合理的、有条理的。
