# 🏆 unparalleled

🔊 /ʌnˈpærəlɛld/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having no parallel or equal; exceptional, unprecedented, surpassing all others
**中文：** 无比的；空前未有的；无与伦比的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unmatched（无与伦比的）
- incomparable（无可比拟的）
- unprecedented（空前的）
- peerless（无双的）
- supreme（至高的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unparalleled success / unparalleled beauty / unparalleled achievement / unparalleled in history**

👉 Example: The athlete's unparalleled performance at the championship shattered three world records in a single evening.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's collection of ancient artifacts is **unparalleled** in scope and historical significance among institutions worldwide.
   该博物馆的古代文物收藏在全球各机构中，其范围和历史意义都是无与伦比的。

2. The economic growth experienced during that decade was **unparalleled**, transforming the nation from a developing economy into a global powerhouse.
   那十年间经历的经济增长是空前的，将该国从发展中经济体转变为全球强国。

3. Her **unparalleled** dedication to scientific research earned her the highest honors in her field and the admiration of peers.
   她对科学研究无与伦比的奉献精神为她赢得了该领域的最高荣誉和同行的钦佩。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-parallel-ed" as "nothing runs parallel to it" — if nothing can stand alongside it as an equal, it must be the absolute best.
把"unparalleled"想成"un（没有）+ parallel（平行的）+ ed"——没有任何事物能与之并驾齐驱，即无与伦比的。
