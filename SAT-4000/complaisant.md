# 🎩 complaisant

🔊 /kəmˈpleɪzənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Willing to please others; courteously obliging; showing a desire to make others comfortable or satisfied
**中文：** adj. 彬彬有礼的；顺从的；殷勤的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obliging（乐于助人的）
- accommodating（随和的）
- deferential（恭敬的）
- agreeable（和蔼的）
- courteous（有礼貌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complaisant manner / complaisant host / complaisant attitude / excessively complaisant**

👉 Example: The complaisant waiter anticipated every need of the demanding guests without hesitation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **complaisant** butler attended to every request with impeccable grace, ensuring that all visitors felt thoroughly welcomed.
   这位殷勤的管家以无可挑剔的优雅满足每一个请求，确保所有来访者都感到宾至如归。

2. A **complaisant** disposition may be admirable in social settings, but it can become a liability in competitive negotiations.
   在社交场合中，殷勤随和的性格可能令人钦佩，但在竞争性谈判中可能成为一种不利因素。

3. The senator's **complaisant** response to corporate lobbyists raised serious concerns about potential conflicts of interest in government.
   这位参议员对企业说客的顺从回应引发了对政府中潜在利益冲突的严重关切。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-PLAIS-ant" as "one who is pleasant to comply" — a complaisant person pleasantly agrees and obliges others.
把"complaisant"想成"pleasant to comply"——一个彬彬有礼的人愉快地顺从并取悦他人。
