# 😈 atrocious

🔊 /əˈtroʊʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely wicked, cruel, or brutal; shockingly bad or tasteless
**中文：** adj. 凶恶的，残暴的；糟糕透顶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- heinous（极恶的）
- abominable（可憎的）
- monstrous（骇人的）
- barbarous（野蛮的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **atrocious behavior / atrocious crime / atrocious conditions / atrocious weather**

👉 Example: The journalist documented the atrocious conditions inside the overcrowded detention facility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tribunal condemned the dictator for committing **atrocious** crimes against the civilian population during the conflict.
   法庭谴责该独裁者在冲突期间对平民犯下的残暴罪行。

2. Critics unanimously agreed that the film was **atrocious**, lacking coherent plot and competent performances alike.
   评论家一致认为这部电影糟糕透顶，既缺乏连贯的情节也缺少合格的表演。

3. The refugees described **atrocious** living conditions in the camps, with insufficient food, water, and medical care.
   难民们描述了营地中恶劣的生活条件，食物、水和医疗都严重不足。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "atrocious" as containing "a-TROC-ious" — link "TROC" to "trophy of cruelty," something so cruel it is shockingly terrible.
把"atrocious"想成包含"a-TROC-ious"——将"TROC"联想为"残酷的奖杯"，形容某事残忍得令人震惊。
