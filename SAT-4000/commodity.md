# 📦 commodity

🔊 /kəˈmɑːdəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold; a useful or valuable thing; an article of trade or commerce
**中文：** n. 商品，货物；日用品；有价值的物品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- goods（商品）
- merchandise（货物）
- product（产品）
- article（物品）
- staple（主要商品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commodity prices / commodity market / a scarce commodity / trade in commodities**

👉 Example: Clean water has become an increasingly precious commodity in many arid regions of the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Global **commodity** prices fluctuated dramatically throughout the year due to geopolitical tensions and disruptions in the supply chain.
   由于地缘政治紧张局势和供应链中断，全球商品价格全年剧烈波动。

2. In the modern digital economy, personal data has emerged as a highly valuable **commodity** traded among corporations worldwide.
   在现代数字经济中，个人数据已成为全球企业之间交易的高价值商品。

3. The nation's economy depended heavily on the export of agricultural **commodities** such as coffee, cocoa, and raw cotton.
   该国经济严重依赖咖啡、可可和原棉等农产品的出口。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "commodity" as "common-ity" — something so common and useful that everyone trades it, like basic goods in a marketplace.
把"commodity"想成"common-ity"——如此常见和有用的东西，人人都在交易，就像市场上的基本商品。
