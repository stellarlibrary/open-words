# 🌿 therapeutic

🔊 /ˌθerəˈpjuːtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to the healing of disease or injury; having a beneficial effect on the body or mind; conducive to well-being.
**中文：** adj. 治疗的；有益于健康的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- curative（治愈的）
- healing（治疗的）
- remedial（补救的）
- restorative（恢复性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **therapeutic effect / therapeutic approach / therapeutic value / therapeutic intervention**

👉 Example: Many patients find that regular exercise has a profoundly therapeutic effect on both anxiety and depression.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **therapeutic** benefits of mindfulness meditation have been extensively documented in peer-reviewed psychological and medical research.
   正念冥想的治疗益处已在同行评审的心理学和医学研究中被广泛记录。

2. Art programs in hospitals serve a **therapeutic** purpose by helping patients cope with stress and emotional trauma during recovery.
   医院中的艺术项目通过帮助患者在康复期间应对压力和情感创伤来发挥治疗作用。

3. The physician recommended a **therapeutic** dosage of the medication that would alleviate symptoms without causing significant adverse effects.
   医生建议使用治疗剂量的药物，既能缓解症状又不会造成明显的副作用。

---

Think of "therapeutic" as "therapy + eutic" — anything related to therapy that makes you feel better and heals you.
把"therapeutic"想成"therapy + eutic"——任何与治疗相关的、让你感觉更好并治愈你的东西。
