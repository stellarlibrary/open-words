# 🔬 granular

🔊 /ˈɡrænjʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Resembling or consisting of small grains or particles; detailed and precise in texture or analysis
**中文：** adj. 颗粒的，粒状的；由小粒而成的；细致入微的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grainy（有颗粒的）
- coarse（粗糙的）
- particulate（微粒的）
- detailed（细致的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **granular data / granular analysis / granular detail / granular texture / granular level**

👉 Example: The report provides a granular breakdown of expenditures across every department within the organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team required more **granular** data to identify the specific environmental factors contributing to the observed population decline.
   研究团队需要更细致的数据来确定导致观察到的种群数量下降的具体环境因素。

2. Under the microscope, the mineral sample revealed a distinctly **granular** structure composed of tightly packed crystalline particles.
   在显微镜下，矿物样本显示出由紧密排列的结晶颗粒组成的明显粒状结构。

3. Modern analytics platforms enable businesses to achieve a **granular** understanding of consumer behavior across multiple digital channels.
   现代分析平台使企业能够在多个数字渠道上对消费者行为获得细致入微的了解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "granular" as coming from "granule" (small grain) — just as sugar granules are tiny particles, granular means broken down into fine, detailed pieces.
把"granular"和"granule"（小颗粒）联系起来——就像糖粒是微小的颗粒一样，granular意味着分解成细小、精细的部分。
