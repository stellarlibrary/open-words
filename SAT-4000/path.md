# 🛤️ path

🔊 /pæθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A way or track laid down for walking; a course of action or direction of movement; a trajectory
**中文：** n. 路程；道路；小路；轨道

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- route（路线）
- trail（小径）
- course（路线）
- trajectory（轨道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **career path / path to success / follow a path / clear a path / path of destruction**

👉 Example: The hikers followed a narrow path through the dense forest to reach the mountain summit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee recommended a clear **path** forward for regulatory reform that would balance economic growth with environmental protection.
   委员会建议了一条明确的监管改革前进路径，以平衡经济增长与环境保护。

2. The asteroid's projected **path** indicated that it would pass within a remarkably close distance of Earth's orbital trajectory.
   这颗小行星的预测轨道表明，它将在距地球轨道极近的距离内通过。

3. Many graduates struggle to identify a meaningful career **path** that aligns with both their personal values and professional aspirations.
   许多毕业生难以找到一条既符合个人价值观又符合职业抱负的有意义的职业道路。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "path" as the way you "pass through" — a path is the route you take to get from one place to another.
把"path"想成你"pass through（经过）"的方式——路径是你从一个地方到另一个地方的路线。
