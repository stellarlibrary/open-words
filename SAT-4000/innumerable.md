# ✨ innumerable

🔊 /ɪˈnjuːmərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Too many to be counted; extremely numerous
**中文：** 无数的，数不清的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- countless（无数的）
- myriad（无数的）
- numberless（数不清的）
- incalculable（不可计量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **innumerable occasions / innumerable benefits / innumerable challenges / innumerable examples**

👉 Example: The library contained innumerable volumes spanning centuries of accumulated knowledge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist faced **innumerable** obstacles before finally achieving a breakthrough in her groundbreaking cancer research.
   这位科学家在最终取得突破性的癌症研究成果之前，面临了无数的障碍。

2. There are **innumerable** factors that contribute to the economic disparity observed between urban and rural communities.
   导致城乡社区之间经济差距的因素数不胜数。

3. The ancient manuscript referenced **innumerable** civilizations that had risen and fallen throughout the course of recorded history.
   这份古代手稿提及了有史以来兴衰更替的无数文明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-numer-able" — "in" (not) + "numer" (number) + "able." Something that is not numberable — too many to count.
把"innumerable"想成"in（不）+ numer（数字）+ able（能够）"——无法计数的，多到数不清。
