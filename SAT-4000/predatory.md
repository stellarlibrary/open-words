# 🦅 predatory

🔊 /ˈpredətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or characterized by plundering or exploitation; living by preying on other organisms
**中文：** adj. 掠夺的；掠夺成性的；食肉的；捕食生物的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rapacious（贪婪的）
- exploitative（剥削的）
- carnivorous（食肉的）
- voracious（贪食的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **predatory behavior / predatory lending / predatory animals / predatory instincts**

👉 Example: Consumer protection agencies have warned the public about predatory lending practices targeting vulnerable borrowers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary exposed the **predatory** business practices of corporations that exploit workers in developing nations.
   这部纪录片揭露了企业剥削发展中国家工人的掠夺性商业行为。

2. Hawks and eagles are **predatory** birds that rely on exceptional eyesight to locate and capture their prey.
   鹰和雕是依靠卓越视力来定位和捕获猎物的食肉鸟类。

3. Regulators introduced new legislation to combat **predatory** pricing strategies that undermine fair market competition.
   监管机构推出了新立法，以打击破坏公平市场竞争的掠夺性定价策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "predatory" as "predator-y" — having the qualities of a predator, always hunting and seizing what belongs to others.
把"predatory"想成"predator（捕食者）+ y（具有……特征的）"——具有捕食者的特征，总是猎取和掠夺他人的东西。
