# 📏 discipline

🔊 /ˈdɪsəplɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A branch of knowledge or field of study; the practice of training people to obey rules. (v.) To train someone to follow rules or a code of behavior
**中文：** n. 学科；纪律 v. 训练

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- training（训练）
- regulation（规范）
- self-control（自制力）
- field（学科）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **academic discipline / strict discipline / discipline oneself / lack of discipline**

👉 Example: Maintaining discipline in daily study habits is essential for success on standardized examinations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university offers programs across multiple **disciplines**, including the natural sciences, humanities, and engineering fields.
   该大学提供多个学科的课程，包括自然科学、人文学科和工程领域。

2. Parents must **discipline** their children with consistency and fairness rather than relying on harsh or arbitrary punishment.
   父母在管教孩子时必须保持一致和公平，而不是依赖严厉或随意的惩罚。

3. The athlete credited her remarkable success to years of rigorous **discipline** and unwavering commitment to her training regimen.
   这位运动员将她的卓越成就归功于多年严格的纪律和对训练计划的坚定执行。
