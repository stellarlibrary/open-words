# 🔗 adhere

🔊 /ədˈhɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To stick firmly to a surface or substance; to believe in and follow a rule, principle, or set of instructions faithfully
**中文：** v. 粘着，附着；坚持，遵守；追随，忠于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cling（紧附）
- stick（粘住）
- comply（遵从）
- abide（遵守）
- conform（服从）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adhere to rules / adhere to principles / adhere to a plan / adhere to standards / strictly adhere**

👉 Example: All participants must adhere to the ethical guidelines established by the research committee.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization requires all members to **adhere** strictly to the code of conduct outlined in the charter.
   该组织要求所有成员严格遵守章程中规定的行为准则。

2. The adhesive was designed to **adhere** to both metal and glass surfaces under extreme temperature conditions.
   这种粘合剂被设计成在极端温度条件下能粘附在金属和玻璃表面上。

3. Nations that fail to **adhere** to the treaty provisions may face significant diplomatic and economic sanctions.
   未能遵守条约条款的国家可能面临重大的外交和经济制裁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adhere" as "ad-HERE" — sticking right HERE, not moving away. From Latin "adhaerere" meaning to stick to. Whether physical glue or moral principles, to adhere is to hold fast.
把"adhere"想成"ad-HERE"——粘在HERE（这里），不离开。源自拉丁语"adhaerere"，意为粘附。无论是物理上的胶水还是道德原则，adhere都是紧紧依附的意思。
