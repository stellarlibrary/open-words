# ⚖️ penalty

🔊 /ˈpenəlti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A punishment imposed for breaking a law, rule, or agreement; a fine or other form of sanction
**中文：** n. 罚款，罚金；处罚，惩罚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- punishment（惩罚）
- sanction（制裁）
- fine（罚款）
- forfeit（罚金）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pay a penalty / impose a penalty / penalty for / death penalty / penalty clause**

👉 Example: The company faced a severe penalty for violating environmental regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court imposed a substantial financial **penalty** on the corporation for its repeated violations of federal trade laws.
   法院对该公司反复违反联邦贸易法的行为处以了巨额罚款。

2. Students who submit their assignments after the deadline will incur a significant **penalty** on their final grade.
   在截止日期后提交作业的学生将受到期末成绩的大幅扣分处罚。

3. The new legislation introduces a harsh **penalty** for those convicted of engaging in insider trading activities.
   新立法对被判从事内幕交易活动的人引入了严厉的处罚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "penal-ty" — "penal" relates to punishment, and "-ty" makes it a noun. A penalty is the price you pay for breaking the rules.
把"penalty"想成"penal（刑罚的）+ ty"——与惩罚相关的名词，就是违规后要付出的代价。
