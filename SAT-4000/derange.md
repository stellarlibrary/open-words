# 🌀 derange

🔊 /dɪˈreɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make someone insane; to throw into disorder or confusion; to disturb the normal functioning of
**中文：** v. 使精神错乱；扰乱；使混乱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unsettle（使不安）
- unbalance（使失衡）
- disrupt（扰乱）
- disorder（使紊乱）
- madden（使发狂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **derange the mind / deranged behavior / emotionally deranged / derange a system**

👉 Example: The prolonged exposure to extreme stress can derange even the most stable and composed individual.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Investigators concluded that prolonged solitary confinement had severely **deranged** the prisoner's mental faculties beyond recovery.
   调查人员得出结论，长期的单独监禁已严重损害了囚犯的心智能力，无法恢复。

2. The unexpected power surge threatened to **derange** the laboratory's delicate instruments and corrupt months of collected data.
   突如其来的电涌威胁着实验室精密仪器的正常运作，并可能毁坏数月收集的数据。

3. Historians noted that the king's increasingly **deranged** decrees ultimately led to a widespread revolt among the populace.
   历史学家指出，国王越来越错乱的法令最终导致了民众的大规模起义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-range" as "out of range" — when something goes out of its normal range or order, it becomes deranged and chaotic.
把"derange"想成"de（脱离）+ range（范围）"——当某物脱离正常范围或秩序时，就变得错乱混乱了。
