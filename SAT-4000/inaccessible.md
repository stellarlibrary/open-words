# 🚧 inaccessible

🔊 /ˌɪnækˈsɛsəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Difficult or impossible to reach, approach, or understand; not readily available or obtainable
**中文：** adj. 难达到的；难接近的；难见到的；不可接近的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unreachable（无法到达的）
- unapproachable（难以接近的）
- remote（偏远的）
- impenetrable（不可进入的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inaccessible to someone / inaccessible area / inaccessible terrain / remain inaccessible**

👉 Example: The mountain village remains inaccessible to most travelers during the harsh winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient ruins are **inaccessible** to researchers because the surrounding terrain is dangerously unstable and overgrown.
   这些古代遗迹对研究人员来说难以到达，因为周围地形极不稳定且杂草丛生。

2. Many scholarly databases remain **inaccessible** to students who lack institutional subscriptions or proper academic credentials.
   许多学术数据库对于缺乏机构订阅或适当学术资格的学生来说仍然无法访问。

3. The CEO's reclusive lifestyle has made her virtually **inaccessible** to journalists seeking interviews about the company's future.
   这位首席执行官隐居的生活方式使她几乎无法被寻求采访公司未来的记者接触到。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-access-ible" as "in (not) + access + ible (able)" — not able to be accessed, something you simply cannot reach or get to.
把"inaccessible"拆成"in（不）+ access（进入）+ ible（能够）"——不能够进入的，指你根本无法到达或接触到的东西。
