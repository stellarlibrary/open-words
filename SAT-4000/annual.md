# 📅 annual

🔊 /ˈænjuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring once every year; relating to a period of one year. (n.) A publication issued once a year; a plant that completes its life cycle in one year
**中文：** adj. 年度的；每年的 n. 年刊；一年生植物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- yearly（每年的）
- perennial（多年的，反义参考）
- periodic（周期性的）
- recurring（反复出现的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **annual report / annual meeting / annual income / annual subscription / annual review**

👉 Example: The company publishes its annual report every March to disclose financial performance to shareholders.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board of directors convenes its **annual** meeting in December to review fiscal performance and strategic priorities.
   董事会每年十二月召开年度会议，审查财务表现和战略重点。

2. Marigolds are popular **annual** plants that gardeners must replant each spring after the last frost has passed.
   万寿菊是受欢迎的一年生植物，园丁必须在最后一次霜冻过后每年春天重新种植。

3. The organization's **annual** fundraising gala has consistently generated significant contributions for educational programs nationwide.
   该组织的年度筹款晚会一直为全国范围内的教育项目带来大量捐款。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "annual" as containing "annu-" from Latin "annus" meaning year. Anything annual happens once per annus (year).
把"annual"想成包含拉丁语"annus"（年）——annual就是每年一次的意思。
