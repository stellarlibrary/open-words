# 🧴 epidermis

🔊 /ˌepɪˈdɜːrmɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The outermost layer of skin; the surface layer of cells covering an organism
**中文：** n. 上皮，表皮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outer skin（外皮）
- cuticle（角质层）
- skin layer（皮肤层）
- integument（外皮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **epidermis layer / damage the epidermis / epidermis cells / outer epidermis**

👉 Example: Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation can severely damage the cells of the epidermis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **epidermis** serves as the body's primary barrier against pathogens, chemicals, and harmful ultraviolet radiation from the sun.
   **表皮**是人体抵御病原体、化学物质和有害紫外线辐射的主要屏障。

2. Under the microscope, the researchers observed significant changes in the **epidermis** of patients exposed to industrial pollutants.
   在显微镜下，研究人员观察到暴露于工业污染物的患者**表皮**发生了显著变化。

3. The plant's **epidermis** contains specialized guard cells that regulate gas exchange and water loss through tiny pores called stomata.
   植物的**表皮**含有特化的保卫细胞，通过称为气孔的微小孔隙调节气体交换和水分散失。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EPI-DERMIS" as "epi (upon) + dermis (skin)" — the layer that sits upon the skin, the outermost covering.
把"epidermis"拆分为"epi（在...之上）+ dermis（真皮）"——在真皮之上的那层，即最外层的表皮。
