# 🪨 petrify

🔊 /ˈpetrɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To turn organic matter into stone through mineral replacement; to paralyze with fear or astonishment
**中文：** v. 使石化；使僵硬；使麻木；使惊呆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- terrify（使恐惧）
- paralyze（使瘫痪）
- fossilize（使石化）
- stun（使震惊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **petrified with fear / petrified wood / petrified of heights / absolutely petrified**

👉 Example: The children were petrified when they heard strange noises coming from the abandoned building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sudden appearance of the predator **petrified** the herd, leaving them motionless for several agonizing seconds.
   捕食者的突然出现使兽群惊呆了，让它们在令人痛苦的几秒钟内一动不动。

2. Over millions of years, the ancient forest had **petrified** into magnificent stone formations now studied by geologists.
   经过数百万年，这片古老的森林已经石化成壮观的石层，如今被地质学家研究。

3. The witness was so **petrified** by the intensity of the cross-examination that she could barely articulate her responses.
   证人被交叉质询的强度吓得目瞪口呆，几乎无法清楚地表达她的回答。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "petr-ify" — "petr" comes from Greek "petra" (stone), so to petrify means to turn to stone, either literally or figuratively from fear.
把"petrify"想成"petr"（来自希腊语"petra"，石头）+"ify"（使）——使变成石头，无论是字面上的石化还是比喻被吓得僵住。
