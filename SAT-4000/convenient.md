# 🛋️ convenient

🔊 /kənˈviːniənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Fitting in well with a person's needs, activities, or plans; easily accessible and suited to one's comfort
**中文：** adj. 方便的；适当的；近便的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- handy（方便的）
- accessible（容易获得的）
- practical（实用的）
- suitable（合适的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convenient for / convenient to / convenient location / convenient time / at your convenience**

👉 Example: The library is in a convenient location, just a short walk from the main campus building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new public transit system provides a **convenient** alternative to driving for thousands of daily commuters in the region.
   新的公共交通系统为该地区数千名日常通勤者提供了一种方便的驾车替代方案。

2. Online databases have made it remarkably **convenient** for researchers to access scholarly articles and historical archives remotely.
   在线数据库使研究人员远程访问学术文章和历史档案变得非常方便。

3. The professor scheduled office hours at a **convenient** time so that both morning and afternoon students could attend easily.
   教授将办公时间安排在方便的时段，以便上午和下午的学生都能轻松参加。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-venient" as "come-with-ease" — something convenient comes to you with ease, requiring minimal effort.
把"convenient"想成"come-with-ease"——方便的事物轻松而来，几乎不需要费力。
