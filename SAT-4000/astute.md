# 🦊 astute

🔊 /əˈstjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations and turn them to one's advantage; shrewd and perceptive.
**中文：** 机敏的；精明的；狡猾的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shrewd（精明的）
- perceptive（敏锐的）
- sagacious（睿智的）
- canny（精明的）
- discerning（有洞察力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **astute observer/businessman/politician/judgment/analysis/move**

👉 Example: An astute investor knows when to hold and when to sell based on careful market analysis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **astute** negotiator recognized the subtle shift in tone and adjusted her strategy immediately.
   这位机敏的谈判者察觉到了语气的微妙变化，并立即调整了她的策略。

2. His **astute** observations about consumer behavior helped the company launch a highly successful product line.
   他对消费者行为的敏锐观察帮助公司推出了一条非常成功的产品线。

3. Only an **astute** reader would notice the foreshadowing hidden within the novel's seemingly ordinary opening chapter.
   只有机敏的读者才会注意到小说看似平凡的开篇章节中隐藏的伏笔。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ASTUTE" sounds like "a STUD" — picture a sharp, clever person who is a stud at reading people and situations.
"ASTUTE"听起来像"a STUD（高手）"——想象一个精明的人，是洞察人心和局势的高手。
