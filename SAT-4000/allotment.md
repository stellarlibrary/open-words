# 📋 allotment

🔊 /əˈlɒtmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of distributing or apportioning something; a share or portion that has been assigned
**中文：** 分配；分配物；份额

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- allocation（分配）
- portion（部分）
- share（份额）
- quota（配额）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **allotment of resources / budget allotment / land allotment / allotment of time**

👉 Example: Each department received a fair allotment of the company's total operating budget.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **allotment** of government funds to public healthcare was increased significantly following the nationwide legislative reform initiative.
   在全国性立法改革倡议之后，政府对公共医疗保健的拨款大幅增加。

2. Workers received their monthly **allotment** of vacation days in accordance with the provisions outlined in the collective bargaining agreement.
   工人们根据集体谈判协议中的条款获得了每月的休假天数分配。

3. The committee is responsible for overseeing the equitable **allotment** of scholarship funds to deserving students from disadvantaged backgrounds.
   委员会负责监督向来自弱势背景的优秀学生公平分配奖学金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "allotment" as "allot-meant" — it is what was "meant" to be "allotted," the result of distributing shares.
把"allotment"想成"allot-meant"——它是"注定"要被"分配"的东西，即分配份额的结果。
