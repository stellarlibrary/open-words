# 🧪 centiliter

🔊 /ˈsɛntɪˌliːtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A metric unit of volume equal to one hundredth of a liter, approximately 0.34 fluid ounces
**中文：** n. 厘升；毫升（一升的百分之一）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cL（厘升缩写）
- small measure（小量度）
- metric unit（公制单位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **measured in centiliters / a centiliter of liquid / centiliter capacity / centiliter bottle**

👉 Example: The laboratory technician carefully measured exactly five centiliters of the reagent for the experiment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The recipe specifies twenty **centiliters** of cream, which must be measured precisely to achieve the desired consistency.
   食谱规定了二十厘升的奶油，必须精确计量以达到所需的稠度。

2. European beverage labels typically express volume in **centiliters**, whereas American labels predominantly use fluid ounces.
   欧洲饮料标签通常以厘升表示容量，而美国标签则主要使用液量盎司。

3. The pharmacist dispensed exactly three **centiliters** of the medicinal solution into a sterile graduated container.
   药剂师将恰好三厘升的药液分装到无菌量筒中。

想想"centi-（百分之一，如centimeter厘米）"加"liter（升）"——一升的百分之一，一个微小但精确的计量单位。
