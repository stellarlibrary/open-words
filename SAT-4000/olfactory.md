# 👃 olfactory

🔊 /ɑːlˈfæktəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the sense of smell. (n.) An organ or structure involved in the sense of smell
**中文：** adj. 嗅觉的；味道的 n. 嗅觉器官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nasal（鼻的）
- aromatic（芳香的）
- scent-related（与气味相关的）
- smell-based（基于嗅觉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **olfactory nerve / olfactory bulb / olfactory system / olfactory receptors / olfactory sensation**

👉 Example: The researchers studied the olfactory receptors to understand how humans detect thousands of distinct odors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **olfactory** system in humans can distinguish among thousands of different chemical compounds in the environment.
   人类的嗅觉系统可以区分环境中数千种不同的化学物质。

2. Damage to the **olfactory** nerve may result in a partial or complete loss of the ability to perceive smells.
   嗅觉神经的损伤可能导致部分或完全丧失感知气味的能力。

3. The scientist published a groundbreaking study on how **olfactory** cues influence memory formation and emotional responses.
   这位科学家发表了一项开创性研究，探讨嗅觉线索如何影响记忆形成和情绪反应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ol-factory" as an "old factory" — imagine walking past an old factory and the strong smells hitting your nose.
把"olfactory"想成"old factory（老工厂）"——想象经过一家老工厂时，浓烈的气味扑鼻而来。
