# 🕊️ liberal

🔊 /ˈlɪbərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Open to new ideas and willing to respect different opinions; generous in giving. (n.) A person who holds liberal political or social views
**中文：** adj. 慷慨的，大方的；自由的，思想开明的 n. 自由主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- generous（慷慨的）
- progressive（进步的）
- open-minded（思想开明的）
- tolerant（宽容的）
- bountiful（大方的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **liberal arts / liberal education / liberal with (money/praise) / liberal democracy**

👉 Example: She was always liberal with her time, volunteering extensively in the local community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university offers a **liberal** arts education that encourages students to explore diverse disciplines before choosing a major.
   该大学提供文科教育，鼓励学生在选择专业之前探索不同的学科。

2. The philanthropist was remarkably **liberal** with his donations, funding scholarships for hundreds of underprivileged students each year.
   这位慈善家在捐赠方面非常慷慨，每年为数百名贫困学生提供奖学金资助。

3. A **liberal** interpretation of the constitution allows the judiciary to adapt legal principles to contemporary societal challenges effectively.
   对宪法的宽泛解释使司法机构能够有效地将法律原则适用于当代社会挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liberal" as coming from "liber" (Latin for "free") — a liberal person is free in thought and generous in spirit.
把"liberal"想成源于"liber"（拉丁语"自由"）——一个liberal的人思想自由，精神慷慨。
