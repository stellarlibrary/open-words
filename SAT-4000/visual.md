# 👀 visual

🔊 /ˈvɪʒuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or used in seeing; perceived by the eye; creating a vivid mental image
**中文：** adj. 视觉的，视力的；栩栩如生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- optical（光学的）
- visible（可见的）
- graphic（图形的）
- pictorial（图画的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **visual aid / visual effect / visual representation / visual learner / visual arts**

👉 Example: The teacher used visual aids to help students comprehend the complex scientific concepts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's latest exhibition employs stunning **visual** effects to immerse visitors in the experience of ancient civilizations.
   博物馆最新的展览运用了令人惊叹的视觉效果，让参观者沉浸在古代文明的体验中。

2. Researchers found that **visual** learners retain information more effectively when material is presented through diagrams and charts.
   研究人员发现，当材料以图表和图示呈现时，视觉型学习者能更有效地记住信息。

3. The architect created a detailed **visual** representation of the proposed building to present to the city planning committee.
   建筑师制作了拟建建筑的详细视觉呈现，以向城市规划委员会展示。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "visual" as sharing the root "vis-" (to see) with "vision" — anything visual is related to what you can see with your eyes.
把"visual"想成与"vision"（视觉）共享词根"vis-"（看）——任何visual的事物都与你眼睛能看到的东西相关。
