# 👆 pointer

🔊 /ˈpɔɪntər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A device or instrument used to indicate position on a display or scale; a rod used for pointing at things; a helpful hint or piece of advice.
**中文：** n. 指针；指示器；教鞭；暗示

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indicator（指示器）
- needle（指针）
- hint（暗示）
- clue（线索）
- suggestion（建议）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laser pointer / a useful pointer / mouse pointer / give someone a pointer**

👉 Example: The experienced mentor offered several practical pointers on how to improve the research proposal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pointer** on the pressure gauge moved steadily upward, indicating that the system was approaching its maximum operating capacity.
   压力表上的**指针**稳步上升，表明系统正接近其最大运行容量。

2. The senior analyst provided several valuable **pointers** that helped the junior team refine their quarterly financial forecast.
   高级分析师提供了几条宝贵的**建议**，帮助初级团队完善了季度财务预测。

3. The instructor used a long wooden **pointer** to highlight important geographical features displayed on the large classroom wall map.
   教师用一根长木**教鞭**指出教室墙上大地图上显示的重要地理特征。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pointer" as "point" + "er" — the one who points, whether it is a needle on a dial or a person giving you direction.
把"pointer"想成"point"（指向）+"er"（者）——指向的东西，无论是表盘上的指针还是给你指引方向的人。
