# 💡 innovate

🔊 /ˈɪnəveɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To introduce new ideas, methods, or products; to make changes in something established
**中文：** 创新；改革；革新

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revolutionize（彻底改变）
- modernize（现代化）
- pioneer（开创）
- transform（变革）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **innovate in a field / innovate by doing something / continue to innovate / strive to innovate**

👉 Example: Companies that fail to innovate risk losing their competitive advantage in the marketplace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technology firm continues to **innovate** by developing artificial intelligence solutions for complex medical diagnoses.
   该科技公司通过开发用于复杂医学诊断的人工智能解决方案来持续创新。

2. Universities must **innovate** in their teaching methodologies to prepare students for the rapidly evolving job market.
   大学必须在教学方法上进行创新，以帮助学生应对快速变化的就业市场。

3. The architect sought to **innovate** by integrating sustainable materials into the design of large-scale urban structures.
   这位建筑师试图通过将可持续材料融入大型城市建筑设计来进行革新。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-nov-ate" — "nov" means "new" (like "novel"). To innovate is to bring in something new.
把"innovate"想成"in-nov-ate"——"nov"意为"新的"（如"novel"）。创新就是引入新事物。
