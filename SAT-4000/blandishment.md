# 🍯 blandishment

🔊 /ˈblændɪʃmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Flattering or pleasing statements or actions used to persuade someone gently; coaxing words
**中文：** n. 奉承；谄媚；哄诱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flattery（谄媚）
- cajolery（哄骗）
- sweet talk（甜言蜜语）
- enticement（诱惑）
- coaxing（哄诱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resist blandishments / use blandishments / immune to blandishments / offer blandishments** (often used in plural form)

👉 Example: The diplomat remained unmoved by the blandishments of the opposing negotiators.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the lobbyist's persistent **blandishments**, the legislator refused to alter her vote on the environmental bill.
   尽管说客不断地花言巧语，这位立法者仍拒绝改变她在环保法案上的投票。

2. The corporation's **blandishments** of generous bonuses and promotions failed to retain its most talented engineers.
   公司以丰厚奖金和晋升机会进行的哄诱未能留住其最有才华的工程师。

3. History reveals that many rulers have succumbed to the **blandishments** of courtiers seeking personal political advantage.
   历史表明，许多统治者都屈服于谋求个人政治利益的朝臣们的奉承之下。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bland-ish-ment" — making something seem "bland" (harmless) through sweet words to lure someone in.
把"blandishment"想成用"bland"（温和的）话语来消除对方的戒心，从而达到哄诱的目的。
