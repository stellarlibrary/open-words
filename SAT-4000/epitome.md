# 🌟 epitome

🔊 /ɪˈpɪtəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A perfect example or representation of a quality or type; a summary or condensed account
**中文：** n. 缩影；摘要；象征；典型

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- embodiment（化身）
- personification（典型）
- quintessence（精髓）
- exemplar（模范）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the epitome of elegance / the epitome of success / the epitome of virtue**

👉 Example: Her grace under pressure made her the epitome of professionalism in the workplace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient library of Alexandria was widely regarded as the **epitome** of accumulated human knowledge and scholarship.
   古代亚历山大图书馆被广泛认为是人类知识和学术积累的缩影。

2. His selfless dedication to humanitarian causes made him the **epitome** of compassion and moral integrity.
   他对人道主义事业的无私奉献使他成为同情心和道德正直的典型。

3. The newly constructed museum serves as the **epitome** of modern architectural innovation and sustainable design principles.
   新建的博物馆是现代建筑创新和可持续设计理念的缩影。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "epitome" as "epi-tome" — "epi" means upon/above, and "tome" means a book. The epitome is the summary that sits above the whole book.
把"epitome"想成"epi-tome"——"epi"意为在上面，"tome"意为书。epitome就是凌驾于整本书之上的摘要和精华。
