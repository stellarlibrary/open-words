# 📢 vociferate

🔊 /voʊˈsɪfəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cry out loudly; to shout or exclaim with great vehemence
**中文：** v. 喊叫；大叫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shout（呐喊）
- bellow（吼叫）
- clamor（叫嚷）
- exclaim（大声说）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vociferate against / vociferate demands / vociferate in protest**

👉 Example: The crowd began to vociferate against the unjust ruling handed down by the court.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The protesters gathered outside the parliament to **vociferate** their demands for immediate constitutional reform.
   抗议者聚集在议会外大声疾呼，要求立即进行宪法改革。

2. The senator began to **vociferate** against the proposed legislation, insisting it would harm working families.
   那位参议员开始大声反对拟议的立法，坚称它将损害工薪家庭的利益。

3. Witnesses reported that the defendant stood up and **vociferated** his innocence throughout the entire trial proceedings.
   目击者称被告在整个审判过程中一直站起来大声宣称自己无罪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voci-" (voice) + "fer" (carry) — to carry your voice loudly, to shout out for all to hear.
把"vociferate"拆成"voci-"（声音）+"fer"（传递）——把你的声音大声传出去，大叫让所有人听到。
