# 🌸 vivacious

🔊 /vɪˈveɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Attractively lively and animated; full of spirit and enthusiasm
**中文：** adj. 活泼的；快活的；有生气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lively（活泼的）
- animated（生气勃勃的）
- spirited（精神饱满的）
- exuberant（充满活力的）
- bubbly（开朗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vivacious personality / vivacious woman / vivacious smile / vivacious charm**

👉 Example: Her vivacious personality made her the center of attention at every social gathering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **vivacious** young diplomat captivated the audience with her eloquent speech and infectious enthusiasm for reform.
   这位活泼的年轻外交官以其雄辩的演讲和对改革的感染力十足的热情吸引了听众。

2. Despite the somber occasion, her **vivacious** spirit lifted the mood and brought unexpected warmth to the memorial service.
   尽管场合庄重，她活泼的精神提升了气氛，为追悼会带来了意想不到的温暖。

3. The director sought a **vivacious** lead actress who could embody the character's boundless energy and irrepressible optimism.
   导演寻找一位活泼的女主角，能够体现角色无限的精力和不可抑制的乐观精神。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vivacious" as containing "viva" (meaning "life" in Latin) — a vivacious person is bursting with life and energy.
把"vivacious"想成包含"viva"（拉丁语中意为"生命"）——活泼的人充满了生命力和活力。
