# ⚖️ accusatory

🔊 /əˈkjuːzətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Indicating or suggesting that one believes a person has done something wrong.
**中文：** 非难的，指责的；控诉的，控告的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- condemning（谴责的）
- reproachful（责备的）
- censorious（挑剔的）
- denunciatory（谴责的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accusatory tone / glance / finger / remark / look**

👉 Example: She pointed an accusatory finger at the suspect during the hearing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosecutor adopted an **accusatory** tone while questioning the defendant about the missing evidence.
   检察官在询问被告有关失踪证据时，采用了指责的语气。

2. Her **accusatory** glance made him realize that she already knew the truth about his deception.
   她指责的目光让他意识到她已经知道了他欺骗的真相。

3. The committee issued an **accusatory** report that held several officials responsible for the financial scandal.
   委员会发布了一份控诉性报告，追究了数名官员在金融丑闻中的责任。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "accuse" + "-atory" — it describes the manner of someone who is accusing another person.
"accusatory"由"accuse（控告）"加上形容词后缀"-atory"构成，表示"带有指控意味的"。
