# 🏔️ gigantic

🔊 /dʒaɪˈɡæntɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely large; enormous in size, extent, or degree
**中文：** adj. 巨大的，庞大的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enormous（巨大的）
- colossal（庞大的）
- immense（极大的）
- massive（大规模的）
- tremendous（巨大的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gigantic effort / gigantic scale / gigantic structure / gigantic leap**

👉 Example: The construction crew faced a gigantic challenge when building the bridge across the canyon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists uncovered a **gigantic** stone temple buried beneath centuries of sediment and dense tropical vegetation.
   考古学家发掘出一座被数百年沉积物和茂密热带植被掩埋的巨大石庙。

2. Completing the infrastructure project required a **gigantic** investment of both financial resources and skilled human capital.
   完成该基础设施项目需要巨大的资金投入和大量熟练的人力资源。

3. The corporation's **gigantic** influence over global trade policy has drawn scrutiny from international regulatory agencies.
   该公司对全球贸易政策的巨大影响力引起了国际监管机构的审查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gigantic" as coming from "giant" — something gigantic is as big as a giant, overwhelmingly large.
把"gigantic"想成来自"giant（巨人）"——像巨人一样大的东西就是gigantic。
