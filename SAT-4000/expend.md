# 💸 expend

🔊 /ɪkˈspɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To spend or use up resources such as money, time, or energy; to consume entirely
**中文：** v. 花费；消耗；用光；耗尽

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consume（消耗）
- deplete（耗尽）
- utilize（使用）
- disburse（支出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **expend energy / expend resources / expend effort / expend funds**

👉 Example: The committee chose to expend its remaining budget on infrastructure improvements.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization decided to **expend** a significant portion of its annual budget on advanced research initiatives.
   该组织决定将其年度预算的很大一部分花费在先进的研究项目上。

2. Athletes must learn to **expend** their energy wisely throughout the competition to avoid premature exhaustion.
   运动员必须学会在比赛中合理地消耗体力，以避免过早疲劳。

3. The nation continued to **expend** vast resources on defense, leaving little funding for social welfare programs.
   国家继续在国防上耗费大量资源，几乎没有留下用于社会福利项目的资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-pend" as "exit + spend" — money exits your pocket when you spend it, so to expend is to use something up completely.
把"expend"想成"exit + spend"——钱从你口袋里花出去了，所以expend就是把东西彻底用光。
