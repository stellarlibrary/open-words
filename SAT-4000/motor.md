# 🚗 motor

🔊 /ˈmoʊtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A machine that converts energy into mechanical motion; an automobile. (adj.) Relating to or driven by a motor. (v.) To travel by automobile
**中文：** n. 发动机，马达；汽车 adj. 汽车的；机动的 v. 乘汽车

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engine（发动机）
- machine（机器）
- drive（驾驶）
- automobile（汽车）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **motor vehicle / motor skills / motor function / motor industry**

👉 Example: The development of fine motor skills is essential for children learning to write and draw.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The electric **motor** in the new hybrid vehicle operates with remarkable efficiency and minimal environmental impact.
   新型混合动力汽车中的电动机运行效率极高，对环境影响极小。

2. Neurologists observed that the patient's **motor** functions had deteriorated significantly following the traumatic brain injury.
   神经科医生观察到，患者在遭受创伤性脑损伤后，运动功能显著退化。

3. They decided to **motor** through the countryside, stopping at several historic villages along the picturesque route.
   他们决定驱车穿越乡间，沿着风景如画的路线在几个历史悠久的村庄停留。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "motor" as related to "motion" — a motor creates motion, powering movement in machines and vehicles.
把"motor"联想到"motion（运动）"——马达创造运动，为机器和车辆提供动力。
