# 😄 nickname

🔊 /ˈnɪkneɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An informal name for a person, sometimes based on a characteristic or abbreviation. (v.) To give an informal name to someone
**中文：** n. 绰号；昵称 v. 给……取绰号；叫错名字

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alias（别名）
- moniker（绰号）
- sobriquet（雅号）
- pet name（昵称）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **earn a nickname / go by a nickname / give someone a nickname / known by the nickname**

👉 Example: His teammates gave him the nickname "Lightning" because of his extraordinary speed on the field.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city of Chicago earned the **nickname** "The Windy City" due to its notoriously strong lakefront breezes.
   芝加哥因其著名的湖滨强风而获得了"风城"的绰号。

2. Classmates began to **nickname** the unusually tall student "Tower" during his very first week at the new school.
   同学们在这位异常高大的学生到新学校的第一周就开始叫他"高塔"。

3. The explorer's daring expeditions earned him the **nickname** "The Pathfinder" among historians and fellow adventurers alike.
   这位探险家大胆的远征使他在历史学家和同行冒险家中获得了"开路先锋"的绰号。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nick" + "name" — a name that nicks or cuts short the original, giving a quick, informal version of someone's identity.
把"nickname"想成"nick（切割）"加"name（名字）"——一个把原名"切短"的名字，是对某人身份的快速非正式称呼。
