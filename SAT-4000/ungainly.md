# 🦒 ungainly

🔊 /ʌnˈɡeɪnli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Clumsy, awkward, or lacking grace in movement or appearance
**中文：** 笨拙的；不雅的；不优美的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clumsy（笨拙的）
- awkward（不灵活的）
- graceless（不优雅的）
- lumbering（笨重的）
- gawky（呆笨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ungainly movements / ungainly gait / ungainly posture / ungainly figure**

👉 Example: The young colt moved with ungainly strides before gradually developing a more graceful gallop.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The adolescent boy made an **ungainly** attempt to climb the fence, stumbling twice before finally clearing the obstacle.
   那个少年笨拙地试图翻越围栏，绊倒了两次才终于翻过障碍物。

2. Despite its **ungainly** appearance on land, the albatross becomes remarkably elegant once it takes flight over the ocean.
   尽管信天翁在陆地上外形笨拙，但一旦飞越海洋便显得格外优雅。

3. The dancer overcame her naturally **ungainly** build through years of rigorous training and unwavering disciplined practice.
   那位舞者通过多年严格训练和坚定不移的刻苦练习，克服了天生笨拙的体型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-gain-ly" as "unable to gain grace" — someone who cannot gain gracefulness in their movements.
把"ungainly"想成"un（不能）+ gain（获得）+ ly"——无法获得优雅，即笨拙的、不雅的。
