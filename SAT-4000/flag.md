# 🚩 flag

🔊 /flæɡ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To mark for attention; to become weak, tired, or less enthusiastic; to droop or wilt. (n.) A piece of cloth used as a symbol or signal; a banner
**中文：** v. 标记；衰退；枯萎；变弱；疲乏 n. 国旗；旗帜；标志

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wane（衰退）
- weaken（变弱）
- banner（旗帜）
- signal（信号）
- mark（标记）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **flag down / flag an issue / flag as important / spirits flag / energy flags**

👉 Example: The editor flagged several inconsistencies in the manuscript that required immediate revision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The automated system will **flag** any transaction exceeding ten thousand dollars for additional compliance review and verification.
   自动化系统将标记任何超过一万美元的交易，以进行额外的合规审查和核实。

2. After running the marathon for three hours, the athlete's stamina began to **flag** noticeably during the final stretch.
   在跑了三个小时的马拉松后，这位运动员的体力在最后一段明显开始衰退。

3. The national **flag** was raised ceremoniously at dawn to commemorate the anniversary of the country's independence declaration.
   国旗在黎明时分隆重升起，以纪念国家独立宣言的周年纪念日。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a flag drooping on a windless day — just as a flag hangs limp without wind, energy and enthusiasm can flag and droop over time.
想象一面旗帜在无风的日子里耷拉着——就像旗帜在没有风时无力地垂下，精力和热情也会随着时间衰退和消沉。
