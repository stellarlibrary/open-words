# 💖 philanthropy

🔊 /fɪˈlænθrəpi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The desire to promote the welfare of others, expressed especially by the generous donation of money, time, or resources to good causes
**中文：** n. 博爱，慈善；慈善事业

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- charity（慈善）
- benevolence（仁慈）
- altruism（利他主义）
- generosity（慷慨）
- humanitarianism（人道主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **corporate philanthropy / engage in philanthropy / a spirit of philanthropy / philanthropy and public service**

👉 Example: Her lifelong commitment to philanthropy resulted in the creation of dozens of community programs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Corporate **philanthropy** has become an essential component of modern business strategy, enhancing both social welfare and brand reputation.
   企业慈善已成为现代商业战略的重要组成部分，同时提升了社会福祉和品牌声誉。

2. The industrialist devoted the latter decades of his life to **philanthropy**, funding libraries and research institutions across the country.
   这位实业家将后半生献给了慈善事业，在全国资助了图书馆和研究机构。

3. Effective **philanthropy** requires careful planning and evaluation to ensure that donated resources truly benefit the intended communities.
   有效的慈善事业需要仔细规划和评估，以确保捐赠的资源真正惠及目标社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "phil-anthropy" as "phil (love) + anthropy (humanity)" — philanthropy is literally the love of humankind through generous action.
把"philanthropy"想成"phil（爱）+ anthropy（人类）"——慈善就是通过慷慨行为表达对人类的热爱。
