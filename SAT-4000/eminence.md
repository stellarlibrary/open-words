# 🏔️ eminence

🔊 /ˈemɪnəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Fame or recognized superiority within a particular sphere; a piece of rising ground or elevated position
**中文：** n. 显赫；卓越；高处

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prominence（显著）
- distinction（卓越）
- prestige（声望）
- renown（声誉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **achieve eminence / a position of eminence / eminence in a field / His Eminence**

👉 Example: Through decades of groundbreaking research, the scientist achieved eminence in the field of molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor rose to **eminence** in the academic world through her pioneering contributions to computational linguistics and artificial intelligence.
   这位教授通过她在计算语言学和人工智能领域的开创性贡献在学术界声名显赫。

2. Few artists in the twentieth century attained such **eminence** that their names became synonymous with entire movements in modern art.
   二十世纪很少有艺术家达到如此显赫的地位，以至于他们的名字成为整个现代艺术运动的代名词。

3. The castle was strategically built upon a rocky **eminence** that provided commanding views of the surrounding valley and approaching roads.
   这座城堡战略性地建在一处岩石高地上，可以俯瞰周围的山谷和通往此处的道路。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eminence" as containing "eminent" — someone standing on a high place, towering above others in reputation.
把"eminence"想成包含"eminent（杰出的）"——某人站在高处，声望凌驾于他人之上。
