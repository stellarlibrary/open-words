# 💪 dogged

🔊 /ˈdɔːɡɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing tenacious and determined persistence; stubbornly unyielding
**中文：** adj. 顽强的；顽固的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tenacious（坚韧的）
- persistent（执着的）
- stubborn（顽固的）
- resolute（坚决的）
- relentless（不屈不挠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dogged determination / dogged persistence / dogged pursuit / dogged effort**

👉 Example: Her dogged determination to succeed eventually earned her a position at the prestigious research institute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective's **dogged** pursuit of the truth ultimately led to the resolution of the decades-old criminal case.
   侦探对真相的顽强追查最终使这桩几十年前的刑事案件得以侦破。

2. Through **dogged** persistence and unwavering discipline, the young scholar completed her doctoral dissertation ahead of schedule.
   凭借顽强的毅力和坚定的自律，这位年轻学者提前完成了她的博士论文。

3. The organization's **dogged** commitment to environmental protection has inspired numerous policy reforms across the region.
   该组织对环境保护的执着承诺在整个地区激发了众多政策改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a dog that won't let go of a bone — a "dogged" person holds on to their goal with the same stubborn grip.
想象一只紧咬骨头不放的狗——一个"dogged"的人就像这样顽强地坚守自己的目标不放手。
