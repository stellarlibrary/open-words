# 🧔 hirsute

🔊 /ˈhɜːrsuːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a lot of hair, especially on the body or face; hairy or bristly
**中文：** adj. 多毛的；有粗毛的；有鬃毛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hairy（多毛的）
- shaggy（蓬乱的）
- bushy（浓密的）
- bristly（有刚毛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hirsute appearance / hirsute individual / hirsute features / hirsute limbs**

👉 Example: The biologist noted the hirsute characteristics of the newly discovered primate species.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologist described the ancient figurine as depicting a **hirsute** warrior with a thick beard and bushy eyebrows.
   考古学家描述这座古代雕像刻画了一位蓄着浓密胡须和浓眉的多毛武士。

2. Certain genetic conditions can cause an unusually **hirsute** appearance that may require medical evaluation and treatment.
   某些遗传疾病会导致异常多毛的外观，可能需要医学评估和治疗。

3. The **hirsute** caterpillar's dense covering of fine bristles serves as a natural defense mechanism against predators.
   这只多毛毛虫身上浓密的细刚毛是一种对抗捕食者的天然防御机制。
