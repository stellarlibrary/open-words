# 👤 plebeian

🔊 /plɪˈbiːən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to the common people; lacking refinement; coarse or vulgar. (n.) A commoner; an ordinary person
**中文：** adj. 平民的；普通的；粗俗的 n. 平民；庶民

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- common（普通的）
- vulgar（粗俗的）
- lowborn（出身低微的）
- proletarian（无产阶级的）
- unrefined（未经雕琢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plebeian tastes / plebeian origins / plebeian class / dismiss as plebeian**

👉 Example: The aristocrat dismissed the local cuisine as plebeian and unworthy of his refined palate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's critics accused him of harboring **plebeian** tastes that were unbecoming of someone in such high office.
   参议员的批评者指责他有着与其高位不相称的粗俗品味。

2. In ancient Rome, a **plebeian** could rarely aspire to the political influence that patrician families wielded with ease.
   在古罗马，一个平民很少能够奢望获得贵族家庭轻易掌握的政治影响力。

3. The art critic regarded the exhibition as **plebeian** entertainment, lacking the sophistication expected of a major cultural institution.
   这位艺术评论家认为该展览是粗俗的娱乐，缺乏一个重要文化机构应有的精致水准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plebeian" as "pleb-being" — a pleb (common person) just being themselves, ordinary and unpretentious.
把"plebeian"想成"pleb-being"——一个普通人（pleb）做他们自己，平凡而不矫饰。
