# 😢 bemoan

🔊 /bɪˈmoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To express deep sorrow or regret over something; to lament or grieve about a loss or misfortune
**中文：** v. 惋惜；哀叹；为……恸哭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lament（哀叹）
- bewail（悲叹）
- deplore（哀叹）
- grieve（悲伤）
- mourn（哀悼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bemoan the loss of / bemoan the fact that / bemoan the state of / bemoan one's fate**

👉 Example: Many scholars bemoan the decline of critical thinking skills among contemporary university students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental activists **bemoan** the rapid destruction of ancient forests that have stood for thousands of years.
   环保人士为存在了数千年的古老森林遭到迅速破坏而痛心疾首。

2. The economist publicly **bemoaned** the widening gap between the wealthy elite and the increasingly impoverished working class.
   这位经济学家公开哀叹富裕精英与日益贫困的工薪阶层之间不断扩大的差距。

3. Rather than **bemoan** their misfortune, the resilient community devoted its energy to rebuilding after the catastrophic flood.
   这个坚韧的社区没有为不幸而哀叹，而是集中精力在灾难性洪水后重建家园。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-moan" — to "be moaning," that is, to moan or cry out in sorrow over something you have lost.
把"bemoan"拆成"be + moan（呻吟）"——不停地呻吟哀叹，即为某事悲伤惋惜。
