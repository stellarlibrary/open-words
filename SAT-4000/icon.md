# 🌟 icon

🔊 /ˈaɪkɑːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A symbol or representative image; a person or thing regarded as a representative symbol or worthy of admiration; a small pictorial symbol on a computer screen
**中文：** n. 图标；偶像；肖像；雕像

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- symbol（象征）
- idol（偶像）
- emblem（标志）
- image（图像）
- figurehead（代表人物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cultural icon / fashion icon / an icon of something / click on the icon / pop icon**

👉 Example: She became a cultural icon whose influence extended far beyond the boundaries of the entertainment industry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Martin Luther King Jr. remains an enduring **icon** of the civil rights movement and a symbol of nonviolent resistance worldwide.
   马丁·路德·金至今仍是民权运动的不朽偶像，也是全世界非暴力抵抗的象征。

2. The art historian explained how Byzantine religious **icons** served both as devotional objects and as powerful tools of political authority.
   艺术史学家解释了拜占庭宗教圣像如何既是宗教膜拜的对象，又是强大的政治权威工具。

3. The software designer created an intuitive **icon** that users could immediately recognize as the application's primary navigation button.
   软件设计师创建了一个直观的图标，用户可以立即将其识别为应用程序的主要导航按钮。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "I-con" — "I" am connected to this image or person, they represent something I admire. An icon is someone or something that stands as a recognizable symbol.
把"icon"想成"I-con"——"我"（I）与这个形象或人物相连，他们代表了我所崇拜的东西。偶像就是某个可被识别的象征。
