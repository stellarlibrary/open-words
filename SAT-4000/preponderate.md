# ⚖️ preponderate

🔊 /prɪˈpɑːndəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To exceed in weight, power, importance, or number; to be greater or superior in influence or quantity
**中文：** v. 占优势；超过；胜过；在重量、数量或力量方面压倒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- predominate（占主导）
- outweigh（超过）
- prevail（占上风）
- surpass（胜过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preponderate over / preponderate in number / evidence preponderates / advantages preponderate**

👉 Example: In the final analysis, the benefits of the proposed reform clearly preponderate over its potential drawbacks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The evidence favoring the defendant's account clearly **preponderates** over the circumstantial claims presented by the prosecution team.
   支持被告陈述的证据明显超过控方提出的间接证据。

2. In many developing nations, the agricultural sector continues to **preponderate** in terms of total employment and economic output.
   在许多发展中国家，农业部门在总就业和经济产出方面继续占据主导地位。

3. Historians noted that conservative voices **preponderated** in the assembly, effectively blocking all attempts at progressive legislative reform.
   历史学家指出，保守派的声音在议会中占了上风，有效地阻止了所有渐进式立法改革的尝试。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-ponder-ate" — "ponder" relates to weighing (from Latin "pondus" = weight), so to preponderate means to outweigh.
把"preponderate"想成"pre（超过）+ ponder（称量）+ ate"——在称量中超过对方，即占优势、胜过。
