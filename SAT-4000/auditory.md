# 🎧 auditory

🔊 /ˈɔːdɪtɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Relating to the sense of hearing or the organs involved in hearing.

**中文：** 听觉的；耳朵的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- aural（听觉的）
- acoustic（听觉的；声学的）
- hearing-related（与听力相关的）
- auricular（耳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Auditory" is commonly used in scientific and academic contexts. Frequent collocations include "auditory cortex," "auditory nerve," "auditory processing," and "auditory learner." It is interchangeable with "aural" in many contexts, though "auditory" is more technical.

"auditory" 常用于科学和学术语境。常见搭配包括 auditory cortex（听觉皮层）、auditory nerve（听觉神经）、auditory processing（听觉处理）和 auditory learner（听觉型学习者）。在许多语境中可与 "aural" 互换，但 "auditory" 更具专业性。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The neurologist determined that damage to the **auditory** nerve caused the patient's hearing loss.
   神经科医生确定**听觉**神经损伤导致了该患者的听力丧失。

2. Children who are **auditory** learners often benefit from lectures and recorded study materials.
   **听觉**型学习者的儿童通常能从讲座和录音学习材料中受益。

3. The research team mapped the **auditory** cortex to understand how the brain processes sound.
   研究团队绘制了**听觉**皮层图谱，以了解大脑如何处理声音。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Auditory" = "audit-" (hear, from Latin "audire") + "-ory" (relating to). Think of an "audio story" — it relates entirely to hearing. Anything "auditory" is about what your ears do.

"auditory" = "audit-"（听，源自拉丁语 audire）+ "-ory"（与……有关的）。想象一个"有声故事"（audio story）——它完全与听觉有关。任何 "auditory" 的事物都和耳朵的功能有关。
