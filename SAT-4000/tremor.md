# 🌋 tremor

🔊 /ˈtremər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An involuntary quivering movement; a slight earthquake; a sudden feeling of fear or excitement
**中文：** n. 震颤；颤动；小地震；（恐惧或兴奋引起的）颤抖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quiver（颤抖）
- shudder（战栗）
- vibration（振动）
- shaking（震动）
- quake（地震）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **earth tremor / a tremor of fear / tremor in one's voice / send tremors through**

👉 Example: A slight tremor in his voice betrayed the anxiety he was trying to conceal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Seismologists detected a series of minor **tremors** in the region, raising concerns about a potentially larger seismic event.
   地震学家在该地区探测到一系列轻微的震颤，引发了对可能发生更大地震的担忧。

2. A **tremor** of excitement swept through the audience when the renowned physicist announced her groundbreaking discovery.
   当这位著名物理学家宣布她的突破性发现时，一阵兴奋的颤动席卷了全场观众。

3. The progressive neurological condition caused persistent **tremors** in the patient's hands, impairing fine motor function significantly.
   这种进行性神经疾病导致患者双手持续震颤，严重损害了精细运动功能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tremor" as "trem + or" — "trem" sounds like "tremble," and a tremor is indeed a trembling or shaking motion.
把"tremor"想成"trem + or"——"trem"听起来像"tremble（颤抖）"，震颤确实就是一种颤抖或摇晃的运动。
