# 👗 skirt

🔊 /skɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A garment worn from the waist down; an edge or border. (v.) To go around the edge of; to avoid or evade
**中文：** n. 边缘

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- border（边缘）
- circumvent（绕过）
- evade（回避）
- bypass（绕过）
- fringe（边缘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **skirt the issue / skirt the edge / skirt around / skirt the boundary**

👉 Example: The politician skillfully skirted the controversial question during the live televised debate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hiking trail **skirted** the edge of the steep cliff, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the valley below.
   这条远足小径沿着陡峭悬崖的边缘延伸，提供了壮观的山谷全景。

2. The diplomat repeatedly **skirted** the most sensitive topics during negotiations, preferring to focus on areas of mutual agreement.
   这位外交官在谈判中反复回避最敏感的话题，倾向于关注双方一致的领域。

3. A dense forest **skirted** the northern boundary of the estate, providing a natural barrier against the harsh winter winds.
   一片茂密的森林环绕在庄园的北部边界，形成了抵御严冬寒风的天然屏障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a "skirt" going around the waist — just as the garment circles the body, to "skirt" something means to go around it.
想想"skirt（裙子）"围绕腰部——就像这件衣服环绕身体一样，"skirt"某事意味着绕过它。
