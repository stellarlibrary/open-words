# 🕊️ release

🔊 /rɪˈliːs/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To set free from confinement or restraint; to make available to the public. (n.) The act of freeing or the state of being freed; a public distribution
**中文：** v. 释放；发射；让与；发布 n. 释放；发布

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- liberate（解放）
- discharge（释放）
- free（释放）
- publish（发布）
- relinquish（让与）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **release a statement / release prisoners / release an album / press release**

👉 Example: The government decided to release the classified documents after decades of secrecy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee voted to **release** the comprehensive annual report detailing environmental violations across the region.
   委员会投票决定发布详细记载该地区环境违规行为的年度综合报告。

2. After serving twenty years in prison, the court finally ordered authorities to **release** the wrongfully convicted man.
   在服刑二十年后，法院最终下令释放这名被错误定罪的男子。

3. The pharmaceutical company plans to **release** a new medication following successful clinical trials and regulatory approval.
   该制药公司计划在临床试验成功并获得监管批准后发布一种新药。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-lease" as ending a lease — when the lease is over, you release what was held and let it go free.
把"release"想成"re-lease"——当租约结束时，你释放被持有的东西，让它自由离去。
