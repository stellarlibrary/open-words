# 🌅 sanguine

🔊 /ˈsæŋɡwɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb, noun | 形容词, 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Optimistic or positive, especially in a difficult situation; blood-red in color. (v.) To stain with blood. (n.) A blood-red color
**中文：** adj. 血红的；红润的；乐观的，怀有希望的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- optimistic（乐观的）
- hopeful（充满希望的）
- confident（自信的）
- buoyant（振奋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sanguine about / sanguine outlook / sanguine disposition / sanguine expectations**

👉 Example: Despite the economic downturn, the investors remained sanguine about the company's long-term growth prospects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chief economist maintained a **sanguine** outlook on the nation's recovery despite the persistent rise in unemployment figures.
   尽管失业率持续上升，首席经济学家对国家的经济复苏仍持乐观态度。

2. Few analysts were **sanguine** about the proposed treaty, citing historical precedents of diplomatic failure in the region.
   很少有分析人士对拟议的条约持乐观态度，并援引了该地区外交失败的历史先例。

3. Her **sanguine** temperament enabled her to persevere through professional setbacks that would have discouraged most of her peers.
   她乐观的性格使她能够坚持度过职业上的挫折，而这些挫折会让大多数同行灰心丧气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sanguine" from Latin "sanguis" meaning "blood" — a person with good blood flow has rosy cheeks and a healthy, optimistic disposition.
把"sanguine"和拉丁语"sanguis（血液）"联系起来——一个血液循环好的人面色红润，性格健康乐观。
