# 👔 colleague

🔊 /ˈkɒliːɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person with whom one works in a profession or business; a fellow worker or associate
**中文：** n. 同事；同僚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coworker（同事）
- associate（同事）
- peer（同辈）
- fellow（同伴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a close colleague / former colleague / colleague at work / respected colleague**

👉 Example: She consulted a trusted colleague before presenting her findings to the executive board.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Dr. Chen and her **colleagues** at the research institute published a landmark paper on gene therapy techniques last year.
   陈博士和她在研究所的同事去年发表了一篇关于基因治疗技术的里程碑式论文。

2. A respected **colleague** from the engineering department offered to review the proposal before the final submission deadline.
   工程部一位受人尊敬的同事主动提出在最终提交截止日期前审查该提案。

3. Maintaining professional relationships with **colleagues** is essential for fostering a productive and harmonious workplace environment.
   与同事保持专业关系对于营造高效和谐的工作环境至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "col-league" — people in the same "league" working together ("col-" means together), forming a team of colleagues.
把"colleague"想成"col-league"——在同一个"联盟"("league")中一起("col-")工作的人，构成一群同事。
