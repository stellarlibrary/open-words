# 🔋 stimulation

🔊 /ˌstɪmjəˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of stimulating; encouragement; invigoration
**中文：** n. 激励，鼓舞；刺激

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- encouragement（激励）
- motivation（动机）
- arousal（唤起）
- impetus（推动力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intellectual stimulation / sensory stimulation / economic stimulation / provide stimulation**

👉 Example: Young children require constant intellectual stimulation to support healthy cognitive development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Neuroscientists discovered that targeted electrical **stimulation** of specific brain regions could significantly improve memory recall in patients.
   神经科学家发现，对特定大脑区域进行有针对性的电刺激可以显著改善患者的记忆回忆能力。

2. The lack of intellectual **stimulation** in the workplace led many talented employees to seek more challenging positions elsewhere.
   工作场所缺乏智力刺激，导致许多有才华的员工到别处寻找更具挑战性的职位。

3. Government officials proposed a comprehensive package of fiscal **stimulation** measures to revive the stagnant national economy.
   政府官员提出了一套全面的财政刺激措施方案，以重振停滞不前的国民经济。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stimulation" as "stimulate + tion (action)" — it is the action or result of stimulating something into activity.
把"stimulation"想成"stimulate（刺激）+ tion（行为）"——它是刺激某物使其活跃起来的行为或结果。
