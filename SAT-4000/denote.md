# 📍 denote

🔊 /dɪˈnoʊt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To indicate, signify, or represent; to be a sign or symbol of something
**中文：** v. 表示；指示；意味着

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indicate（表示）
- signify（意味着）
- represent（代表）
- symbolize（象征）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **denote a meaning / denote a change / symbols that denote / colors denoting**

👉 Example: In this diagram, the red arrows denote the direction of blood flow through the heart.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In formal logic, the symbol "∀" is used to **denote** universal quantification across all elements within a given domain.
   在形式逻辑中，符号"∀"用于表示给定域内所有元素的全称量化。

2. The rising unemployment figures **denote** a significant economic downturn that policymakers must urgently address with new reforms.
   不断上升的失业数据表明了一场严重的经济衰退，决策者必须紧急采取新改革来应对。

3. On this historical map, the shaded regions **denote** territories that were contested during the prolonged border negotiations of 1848.
   在这张历史地图上，阴影区域表示1848年漫长的边界谈判中存在争议的领土。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-note" as "to make a note of" — when something denotes a meaning, it notes or points out what it signifies.
把"denote"想成"de-note（做笔记）"——当某物denote一个含义时，它就是在标注或指出其含义。
