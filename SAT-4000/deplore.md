# 😔 deplore

🔊 /dɪˈplɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To feel or express strong disapproval of; to regard as deeply regrettable or lamentable
**中文：** v. 谴责；悲悼；哀叹；表示悲痛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lament（哀叹）
- bemoan（悲叹）
- condemn（谴责）
- abhor（憎恶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deplore the violence / deplore the loss / deplore the conditions / deeply deplore**

👉 Example: Human rights organizations deplore the continued use of child labor in unregulated industries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador issued a formal statement to **deplore** the senseless destruction of irreplaceable cultural heritage sites during the armed conflict.
   大使发表正式声明，对武装冲突中无谓破坏不可替代的文化遗产地表示悲痛。

2. Many educators **deplore** the decline in critical thinking skills among students, attributing it to an overreliance on standardized testing methods.
   许多教育工作者哀叹学生批判性思维能力的下降，将其归因于对标准化测试方法的过度依赖。

3. While we **deplore** the tragic circumstances that led to this crisis, we must now focus our collective efforts on recovery.
   虽然我们对导致这场危机的悲惨情况深表惋惜，但现在必须集中力量进行恢复。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-plore" as "deeply implore with sorrow" — when you deplore something, you express deep sadness and disapproval, as if pleading for it to stop.
把"deplore"想成"deeply implore with sorrow（带着悲伤深切恳求）"——当你deplore某事时，你在表达深深的悲伤和不满，仿佛在恳求它停止。
