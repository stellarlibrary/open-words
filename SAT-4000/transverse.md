# ↔️ transverse

🔊 /trænzˈvɜːrs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Situated or extending across something; lying or being across or in a crosswise direction. (n.) A transverse part or section
**中文：** adj. 横向的；横断的 n. 横断面；横轴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crosswise（横向的）
- lateral（侧面的）
- horizontal（水平的）
- perpendicular（垂直的）
- oblique（斜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transverse section / transverse axis / transverse wave / transverse beam**

👉 Example: The engineer examined the transverse section of the bridge to assess structural integrity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist made a **transverse** cut through the rock formation to reveal the distinct layers of sedimentary deposits.
   地质学家对岩层进行了横切，以揭示不同的沉积层。

2. In physics, light is classified as a **transverse** wave because its oscillations occur perpendicular to the direction of propagation.
   在物理学中，光被归类为横波，因为其振荡方向与传播方向垂直。

3. The architect incorporated **transverse** beams into the design to provide additional lateral support for the expansive roof structure.
   建筑师在设计中加入了横梁，为宽阔的屋顶结构提供额外的横向支撑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-verse" as "turning (verse) across (trans)" — something that cuts or lies across another thing, like a crossbar.
把"transverse"想成"trans（横穿）+ verse（转向）"——横穿或横跨某物的东西，就像一根横杆。
