# 📋 usage

🔊 /ˈjuːsɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The way in which something is used; the customary manner of employing language; habitual or accepted practice
**中文：** n. 使用；用法；惯例

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- use（使用）
- application（应用）
- practice（惯例）
- convention（习俗）
- employment（运用）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **language usage / common usage / data usage / usage patterns / proper usage**

👉 Example: The style guide provides detailed instructions on the proper usage of academic citations and references.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The linguist published an extensive study examining changes in English **usage** across different social media platforms over the past decade.
   这位语言学家发表了一项广泛研究，考察了过去十年英语在不同社交媒体平台上用法的变化。

2. Excessive water **usage** during the drought prompted municipal authorities to impose strict conservation measures on all residents.
   干旱期间过度用水促使市政当局对所有居民实施严格的节水措施。

3. The software company monitored **usage** patterns to identify features that customers valued most and those requiring improvement.
   该软件公司监测使用模式，以确定客户最看重的功能以及需要改进的功能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "usage" as "use + age" — how something has been used over an age of time becomes its established usage or custom.
把"usage"想成"use + age"——某事物在长时间内的使用方式就成了其既定的用法或惯例。
