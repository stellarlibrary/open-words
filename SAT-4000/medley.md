# 🎭 medley

🔊 /ˈmɛdli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A diverse mixture or assortment of things; a musical composition combining several songs or melodies. (adj.) Mixed; made up of a variety of elements
**中文：** n. 混合，混杂；集成曲 adj. 混合的，拼凑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mixture（混合物）
- assortment（各种各样）
- potpourri（混合物）
- miscellany（杂集）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a medley of / medley relay / musical medley / a colorful medley**

👉 Example: The orchestra performed a delightful medley of classical and contemporary pieces at the gala.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The opening ceremony featured a vibrant **medley** of traditional songs representing each of the participating nations.
   开幕式上演奏了一段充满活力的传统歌曲集成曲，代表每一个参赛国家。

2. The marketplace offered a remarkable **medley** of spices, fabrics, and handcrafted goods from across the region.
   这个市场提供了来自各地的各种香料、织物和手工制品，种类繁多。

3. Her essay presented a compelling **medley** of historical evidence and modern analysis to support the central thesis.
   她的文章呈现了令人信服的历史证据和现代分析的组合，以支持中心论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "medley" as "med(mix) + ley(lay)" — laying out a mix of different things together, like a DJ mixing songs.
把"medley"想成"med（混合）+ ley（排列）"——把不同的东西混合排列在一起，就像DJ混音歌曲一样。
