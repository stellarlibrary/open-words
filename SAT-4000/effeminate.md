# 🌸 effeminate

🔊 /ɪˈfɛmɪnət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with women; lacking masculine strength or vigor
**中文：** adj. 柔弱的；女人气的；缺乏阳刚之气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unmanly（不男子汉的）
- delicate（柔弱的）
- soft（柔和的）
- womanish（女人气的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effeminate manner / effeminate appearance / effeminate voice / effeminate behavior**

👉 Example: Critics in the eighteenth century often described the luxurious tastes of the aristocracy as effeminate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Spartan culture regarded any display of **effeminate** behavior as a serious threat to military discipline and civic virtue.
   斯巴达文化认为任何柔弱行为的表现都是对军事纪律和公民美德的严重威胁。

2. Historians have noted that Roman moralists frequently condemned what they perceived as **effeminate** indulgence among the ruling elite.
   历史学家注意到，罗马道德家经常谴责他们所认为的统治精英阶层中的柔弱放纵行为。

3. The character's **effeminate** mannerisms were deliberately exaggerated by the playwright to challenge conventional notions of masculinity.
   剧作家故意夸大了该角色的女性化举止，以挑战关于男子气概的传统观念。

Think of "ef-femin-ate" as containing "feminine" — effeminate describes someone displaying traits traditionally seen as feminine.
把"effeminate"想成包含"feminine（女性的）"——effeminate描述的是展现传统上被视为女性特质的人。
