# 🐝 teem

🔊 /tiːm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To be full of or swarming with; to be present in large numbers
**中文：** v. 大量出现；充满

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- swarm（蜂拥）
- abound（充满）
- overflow（溢出）
- brim（充满）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **teem with life / teem with activity / teem with ideas / teeming streets**

👉 Example: The tropical rainforest teems with biodiversity that scientists have only begun to catalog.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coastal waters **teem** with marine organisms that form the foundation of a remarkably complex ecological food web.
   沿海水域充满了海洋生物，它们构成了一个极其复杂的生态食物网的基础。

2. During the festival season, the ancient city streets **teem** with tourists eager to experience local culture and traditions.
   在节日期间，古城街道上挤满了渴望体验当地文化和传统的游客。

3. Her latest novel **teems** with richly drawn characters whose interwoven stories illuminate the complexities of modern urban life.
   她最新的小说充满了刻画丰满的人物，他们交织的故事揭示了现代城市生活的复杂性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "teem" as sounding like "team" — imagine a huge team of ants swarming everywhere, filling every space.
把"teem"想成听起来像"team"（团队）——想象一大群蚂蚁到处涌动，填满每一个空间。
