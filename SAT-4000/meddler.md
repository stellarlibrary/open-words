# 👃 meddler

🔊 /ˈmedlər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who interferes in or busies themselves with things that are not their concern; a busybody
**中文：** n. 干涉者；爱管闲事的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- busybody（爱管闲事的人）
- interloper（干涉者）
- intruder（闯入者）
- snoop（窥探者）
- interferer（干预者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **notorious meddler / political meddler / a meddler in other people's affairs**

👉 Example: The neighbors considered him a meddler who could never resist offering his unsolicited opinions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat was widely regarded as a **meddler** whose constant interference in domestic affairs strained international relations.
   这位外交官被普遍视为爱管闲事的人，其对国内事务的不断干涉损害了国际关系。

2. History often portrays the advisor as a well-intentioned **meddler** whose interventions ultimately caused more harm than good.
   历史常将这位顾问描绘成一个好心的干涉者，其干预最终弊大于利。

3. The committee chairman warned members against becoming **meddlers** in matters that fell outside their designated areas of responsibility.
   委员会主席警告成员不要成为干涉者，插手其指定职责范围之外的事务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meddler" as "meddle + r" — a person who meddles, or sticks their nose into everyone else's business uninvited.
把"meddler"想成"meddle + r"——一个爱管闲事的人，不请自来地把鼻子伸进别人的事务中。
