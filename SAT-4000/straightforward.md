# 🎯 straightforward

🔊 /ˌstreɪtˈfɔːrwərd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Easy to understand or do; uncomplicated. (adj.) Honest and direct in manner or speech
**中文：** adj. 直截了当的，简单明了的；正直的，诚实的，坦率的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- candid（坦率的）
- forthright（直率的）
- uncomplicated（简单的）
- honest（诚实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **straightforward answer / straightforward approach / straightforward question / fairly straightforward**

👉 Example: The professor gave a straightforward explanation that even beginners could easily understand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The instructions for assembling the device were **straightforward**, allowing most users to complete the task within thirty minutes.
   组装该设备的说明简单明了，大多数用户能在三十分钟内完成任务。

2. Her **straightforward** manner of communicating earned the trust and respect of colleagues who valued transparency above all.
   她直截了当的沟通方式赢得了那些最看重透明度的同事们的信任和尊重。

3. What initially appeared to be a **straightforward** legal case soon became entangled in a web of unforeseen complications.
   最初看起来简单明了的法律案件很快就陷入了一系列意想不到的复杂纠葛之中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "straight + forward" literally — going straight ahead without twists or turns, just as a straightforward person speaks directly without deception.
从字面理解"straight + forward"——笔直向前没有弯路，就像一个straightforward的人说话直来直去，毫不虚伪。
