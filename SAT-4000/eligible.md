# ✅ eligible

🔊 /ˈɛlɪdʒəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Qualified or entitled to be chosen; meeting the necessary conditions or requirements for something
**中文：** adj. 合格的；符合条件的；合适的；有资格的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- qualified（有资格的）
- suitable（合适的）
- entitled（有权利的）
- fit（适合的）
- worthy（值得的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eligible for / eligible to vote / eligible candidate / eligible bachelor / eligible to apply**

👉 Example: Only students with a minimum grade point average of 3.5 are eligible for the scholarship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Citizens who have reached the age of eighteen are **eligible** to vote in federal and state elections throughout the country.
   年满十八岁的公民有资格在全国联邦和州选举中投票。

2. The foundation announced that only nonprofit organizations with established track records would be **eligible** for the annual grant.
   该基金会宣布只有具有良好记录的非营利组织才有资格申请年度拨款。

3. After completing the required training program, all participants became **eligible** to sit for the professional certification examination.
   完成规定的培训计划后，所有参与者都有资格参加专业认证考试。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eligible" as "e-lig-ible" — "lig" sounds like "league," and to be in the league, you must be eligible and meet the standards.
把"eligible"想成"e-lig-ible"——"lig"听起来像"league（联盟）"，要进入联盟，你必须合格并达到标准。
