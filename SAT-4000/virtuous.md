# 🌟 virtuous

🔊 /ˈvɜːrtʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing high moral standards; morally excellent, righteous, and upright in character
**中文：** 有道德的；善良的；正直的；贞洁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- righteous（正直的）
- moral（有道德的）
- upright（正直的）
- honorable（高尚的）
- ethical（合乎道德的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **virtuous life / virtuous circle / virtuous conduct / virtuous character / virtuous person**

👉 Example: The philosopher argued that leading a virtuous life requires constant self-reflection and discipline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biography portrayed the civil rights leader as a deeply **virtuous** individual who sacrificed personal comfort for justice.
   这本传记将这位民权领袖描绘成一个品德高尚的人，为正义牺牲了个人安逸。

2. Economists describe a **virtuous** circle in which increased investment leads to higher productivity and subsequently greater prosperity.
   经济学家描述了一个良性循环，其中增加的投资带来更高的生产力，进而带来更大的繁荣。

3. Ancient philosophers believed that **virtuous** conduct was the foundation of a harmonious society and lasting civic order.
   古代哲学家认为道德行为是和谐社会和持久公民秩序的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virtuous" as "virtue + ous" — full of virtue; someone who is virtuous overflows with moral goodness and integrity.
把"virtuous"想成"virtue + ous"——充满美德的；一个virtuous的人充满了道德善良和正直。
