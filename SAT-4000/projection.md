# 📽️ projection

🔊 /prəˈdʒɛkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of projecting an image or light onto a surface; a forecast or estimate; something that protrudes; the attribution of one's feelings to others
**中文：** n. 投射；规划；突出；发射；推测

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- forecast（预测）
- estimate（估计）
- protrusion（突出物）
- prediction（预言）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **projection of / financial projection / map projection / projection screen / sales projection**

👉 Example: The company's revenue projection for the next quarter exceeded the expectations of all analysts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The financial analyst presented a conservative **projection** indicating steady economic growth over the next five fiscal years.
   财务分析师提出了一项保守的预测，表明未来五个财政年度经济将稳步增长。

2. The Mercator **projection** distorts the relative size of landmasses near the poles, making Greenland appear disproportionately large.
   墨卡托投影扭曲了极地附近陆地的相对大小，使格陵兰岛显得不成比例地大。

3. Psychologists describe **projection** as a defense mechanism in which individuals attribute their own unacceptable thoughts to others.
   心理学家将投射描述为一种防御机制，个体将自己不可接受的想法归因于他人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "project-ion" — the act of "projecting" something outward, whether it is light onto a screen or a prediction into the future.
把"projection"想成"project（投射）的名词形式"——将某物向外"投射"的行为，无论是将光投射到屏幕上还是对未来进行预测。
