# 🏡 quaint

🔊 /kweɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Attractively old-fashioned or unusual in a charming way; pleasingly or strangely curious
**中文：** adj. 古雅的；奇怪的；离奇有趣的；做得很精巧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- charming（迷人的）
- picturesque（如画的）
- old-fashioned（古老的）
- queer（古怪的）
- whimsical（奇特的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quaint village / quaint customs / quaint little shop / quaint charm**

👉 Example: Tourists flocked to the quaint seaside village for its cobblestone streets and historic cottages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologist documented the **quaint** customs of the remote community that had preserved centuries-old traditions remarkably well.
   人类学家记录了这个偏远社区的古雅风俗，该社区极好地保存了数百年的传统。

2. What once seemed like a **quaint** notion of handwritten correspondence has experienced a surprising revival among younger generations.
   曾经看似古怪的手写书信概念在年轻一代中经历了令人惊讶的复兴。

3. The professor described the **quaint** architecture of medieval European towns as evidence of sophisticated urban planning principles.
   教授将中世纪欧洲城镇的精巧建筑描述为先进城市规划原则的证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quaint" as "queen +aint (paint)" — a queen's painting of an old village, charming and beautifully old-fashioned.
把"quaint"想成"queen（女王）+ aint（paint，画）"——女王画的一幅古老村庄图，迷人而古雅。
