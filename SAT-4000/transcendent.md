# ✨ transcendent

🔊 /trænˈsɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Surpassing the ordinary; exceptional or supreme; beyond the limits of normal experience
**中文：** 卓越的；超常的；超凡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supreme（至高的）
- extraordinary（非凡的）
- sublime（崇高的）
- unparalleled（无与伦比的）
- preeminent（杰出的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transcendent beauty / transcendent experience / transcendent achievement / transcendent power**

👉 Example: The audience was moved by the transcendent beauty of the soprano's final aria.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that moral principles possess a **transcendent** authority that no earthly government can legitimately override.
   这位哲学家主张，道德原则拥有超越性的权威，任何世俗政府都不能合法地凌驾其上。

2. Critics hailed the artist's latest exhibition as a **transcendent** achievement that elevated contemporary sculpture to new heights.
   评论家称赞这位艺术家的最新展览是一项卓越的成就，将当代雕塑提升到了新的高度。

3. The orchestra delivered a **transcendent** performance that left the entire audience in a state of profound emotional awe.
   管弦乐团呈献了一场超凡的演出，令全场观众沉浸在深深的情感震撼之中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transcend-ent" as "one who transcends" — something transcendent has already climbed beyond all ordinary limits.
把"transcendent"想成"transcend（超越）+ ent（……的）"——具有超越性的事物已经攀越了所有平凡的界限。
