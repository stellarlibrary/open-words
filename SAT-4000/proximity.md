# 📍 proximity

🔊 /prɑːkˈsɪməti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Nearness in space, time, or relationship; the state of being close to something or someone
**中文：** n. 接近，邻近；接近度，距离；亲近；近似

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- closeness（接近）
- nearness（邻近）
- vicinity（附近）
- adjacency（毗邻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in proximity to / close proximity / proximity to something / geographic proximity**

👉 Example: The hotel was chosen for its proximity to the conference center and major transportation hubs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **proximity** of the research facility to the university campus encourages frequent collaboration between scientists and scholars.
   研究设施与大学校园的邻近促进了科学家和学者之间的频繁合作。

2. Investigators determined that the suspect's **proximity** to the crime scene at the estimated time raised significant concerns.
   调查人员确定嫌疑人在估计时间内与犯罪现场的接近程度引起了重大关注。

3. Urban planners emphasized the importance of residential **proximity** to public transit in reducing commute times and carbon emissions.
   城市规划者强调住宅区靠近公共交通对于减少通勤时间和碳排放的重要性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proxim-ity" as "approximate" — both share the root "proxim," meaning near. Proximity is all about being approximately close.
把"proximity"想成"approximate"——两者共享词根"proxim"，意为接近。proximity就是关于大致接近的距离。
