# 🔌 lead

🔊 /liːd/ or /lɛd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) /liːd/ To guide, direct, or be in charge of; to result in. (n.) /liːd/ A position of leadership or advantage. (n.) /lɛd/ A soft, heavy metallic element; an electrical conductor wire
**中文：** n. 领导；铅；导线 v. 领导；致使；引导

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guide（引导）
- direct（指挥）
- conduct（带领）
- head（领导）
- wire（导线）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lead a team / lead to results / take the lead / lead wire / lead poisoning**

👉 Example: The investigation led to the discovery of significant financial irregularities within the corporation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The principal's visionary approach helped **lead** the struggling school to achieve the highest academic rankings in the district.
   校长富有远见的方法帮助这所困难学校达到了学区内最高的学术排名。

2. Prolonged exposure to **lead** in contaminated water sources can cause severe neurological damage, especially in young children.
   长期接触受污染水源中的铅可能导致严重的神经损伤，尤其是对幼儿。

3. The electrician carefully connected each **lead** to the circuit board, ensuring that every terminal was properly secured.
   电工仔细地将每根导线连接到电路板上，确保每个端子都正确固定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lead" with two pronunciations: /liːd/ like a "leader" who goes first, and /lɛd/ like the heavy metal that weighs you down.
把"lead"想成两种发音：/liːd/ 像"leader（领导者）"走在最前面，/lɛd/ 像沉重的金属铅压着你。
