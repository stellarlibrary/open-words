# 🔬 sensitivity

🔊 /ˌsɛnsɪˈtɪvɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or condition of being sensitive; the degree to which an instrument or organism responds to stimulation or external conditions
**中文：** n. 灵敏度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- responsiveness（灵敏性）
- susceptibility（敏感性）
- delicacy（精细）
- perceptiveness（感知力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sensitivity to [factor] / cultural sensitivity / high sensitivity / sensitivity analysis / light sensitivity**

👉 Example: The laboratory evaluated the sensitivity of the detector to ensure it met the required performance standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **sensitivity** of the diagnostic test was crucial, as even a minor failure to detect the pathogen could result in a serious public health crisis.
   该诊断测试的灵敏度至关重要，因为即使是微小的病原体检测失败也可能导致严重的公共卫生危机。

2. Training programs that emphasize cultural **sensitivity** enable healthcare professionals to provide more equitable and compassionate care to diverse patient populations.
   强调文化敏感性的培训项目使医疗专业人员能够为多元化患者群体提供更公平、更有同情心的护理。

3. The researcher conducted a **sensitivity** analysis to determine how variations in input parameters would affect the overall reliability of the model's predictions.
   研究人员进行了灵敏度分析，以确定输入参数的变化将如何影响模型预测的整体可靠性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sensitivity" as the noun form of "sensitive" with "-ity" — it measures how much something can sense, like a thermometer's sensitivity determining how small a temperature change it can detect.
把"sensitivity"想成"sensitive"加"-ity"的名词形式——它衡量某物能感知多少，就像温度计的灵敏度决定了它能检测到多小的温度变化。
