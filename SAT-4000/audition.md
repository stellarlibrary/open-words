# 🎭 audition

🔊 /ɔːˈdɪʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词，动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A trial performance by an actor, singer, or musician to demonstrate suitability for a role; the power or sense of hearing. (v.) To perform in a trial to be assessed for a role or position.

**中文：** (n.) 听力；试听 (v.) 试听

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- tryout（选拔赛）
- trial（试演）
- screening（筛选）
- hearing（听证；听力）
- test（测试）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

As a verb, "audition" can be used transitively ("The director auditioned fifty candidates") or intransitively ("She auditioned for the lead role"). In formal or medical contexts, it can also refer to the faculty of hearing itself.

作动词时，"audition" 可用作及物动词（"导演面试了五十位候选人"）或不及物动词（"她试演了主角"）。在正式或医学语境中，它也可指听觉能力本身。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. More than two hundred performers **auditioned** for the principal role in the new production.
   超过两百名表演者为新作品的主角进行了**试演**。

2. The casting director scheduled a private **audition** for the highly recommended young actress.
   选角导演为这位备受推荐的年轻女演员安排了一场私人**试镜**。

3. Medical researchers studied how prolonged noise exposure affects the faculty of **audition**.
   医学研究人员研究了长期噪音暴露如何影响**听觉**功能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Audition" = "aud-" (hear) + "-ition" (process/action). An audition is the process of being "heard" — whether you are listening to someone perform or testing your own hearing ability.

"audition" = "aud-"（听）+ "-ition"（过程/行为）。audition 就是"被听到"的过程——无论是听某人表演还是测试自己的听力。
