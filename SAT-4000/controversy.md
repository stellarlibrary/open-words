# 🔥 controversy

🔊 /ˈkɒntrəvɜːrsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A prolonged public disagreement or heated debate, often involving strong opposing viewpoints on a particular issue
**中文：** n. 争论；论战；辩论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- debate（辩论）
- dispute（争端）
- contention（争论）
- disagreement（分歧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spark controversy / stir up controversy / cause controversy / amid controversy**

👉 Example: The new policy sparked widespread controversy among educators and parents alike.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proposed regulation ignited significant **controversy** among environmental scientists and industrial leaders across the country.
   这项拟议的法规在全国范围内的环境科学家和工业领袖之间引发了重大争论。

2. Despite the **controversy** surrounding her appointment, the new director implemented several progressive reforms within months.
   尽管她的任命引发了争议，新任主任还是在几个月内实施了几项进步的改革。

3. The academic paper generated intense **controversy** because its findings directly contradicted decades of established scientific research.
   这篇学术论文引发了激烈的论战，因为其发现直接与数十年来公认的科学研究相矛盾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "contro-versy" as "contra-versus" — people standing "against versus" each other, engaged in a heated argument.
把"controversy"想成"contra-versus"——人们"对立相争"，参与激烈的辩论。
