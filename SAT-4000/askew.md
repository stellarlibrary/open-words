# ↗️ askew

🔊 /əˈskjuː/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not in a straight or level position; crooked. (adv.) In a crooked or slanted manner
**中文：** adj. 歪斜的 adv. 歪斜地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crooked（歪的）
- lopsided（不平衡的）
- awry（歪斜的）
- tilted（倾斜的）
- slanted（倾斜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hang askew / sit askew / knocked askew / slightly askew**

👉 Example: The painting on the wall hung slightly askew, irritating the perfectionist homeowner immensely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the earthquake, every picture frame in the gallery hung **askew**, creating an eerie and unsettling atmosphere.
   地震过后，画廊里每一幅画框都歪斜着，营造出一种诡异而令人不安的氛围。

2. His tie was noticeably **askew** throughout the entire presentation, which distracted several members of the audience.
   他的领带在整个演讲过程中明显歪斜，分散了几位听众的注意力。

3. The old fence posts had been knocked **askew** by years of relentless wind and harsh weather conditions.
   那些旧围栏柱子经过多年无情的风吹和恶劣天气已经歪斜不正。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-SKEW" — something "skewed" is off-center and crooked, exactly what askew describes.
把"askew"想成"skew（倾斜）"——歪斜偏离中心的东西，正是askew所描述的。
