# 🏝️ isolate

🔊 /ˈaɪsəleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To set apart from others; to place alone or in quarantine. (n.) A person, organism, or strain that has been isolated
**中文：** v. 使隔离；使孤立；使绝缘 n. 隔离种群

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- separate（分离）
- segregate（隔离）
- seclude（使隔绝）
- quarantine（检疫隔离）
- detach（使脱离）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **isolate a variable / isolate the problem / isolate from others / socially isolated**

👉 Example: Researchers needed to isolate the compound before they could accurately determine its chemical properties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public health officials moved quickly to **isolate** infected individuals in order to prevent the further spread of the contagious disease.
   公共卫生官员迅速采取行动隔离感染者，以防止传染病的进一步传播。

2. The laboratory team managed to **isolate** the specific gene responsible for the hereditary condition after years of painstaking research.
   实验室团队经过多年的艰苦研究，成功分离出了导致该遗传疾病的特定基因。

3. Geographic barriers effectively **isolate** island populations, leading to the development of unique species found nowhere else on Earth.
   地理屏障有效地隔离了岛屿种群，导致了地球上其他地方找不到的独特物种的形成。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "isolate" as "island-ate" — to make something into an island, surrounded and cut off from everything else.
把"isolate"想成"island（岛）+ -ate"——让某物变成一座孤岛，被周围的一切隔绝开来。
