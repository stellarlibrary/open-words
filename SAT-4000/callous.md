# 💎 callous

🔊 /ˈkæləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Emotionally hardened; showing no sympathy or sensitivity. (v.) To make or become insensitive or hardened
**中文：** adj. 无情的；冷淡的；硬结的；起老茧的 v. 使冷酷无情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- heartless（无情的）
- unfeeling（冷漠的）
- insensitive（麻木的）
- ruthless（残忍的）
- cold-blooded（冷血的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **callous disregard / callous indifference / callous attitude / callous treatment**

👉 Example: The manager displayed a callous disregard for the workers' safety concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation's **callous** treatment of displaced communities drew severe criticism from international human rights organizations.
   该公司对流离失所社区的冷酷对待招致了国际人权组织的严厉批评。

2. Critics described the government's response to the humanitarian crisis as shockingly **callous** and woefully inadequate.
   批评者称政府对人道主义危机的回应冷酷得令人震惊且严重不足。

3. Years of working in the emergency ward had **calloused** her to the sight of suffering and grief.
   多年在急诊室工作使她对痛苦和悲伤的景象变得麻木不仁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "callous" as related to "callus" — just as a callus makes skin hard and numb, a callous person has a hardened, numb heart.
把"callous"联想到"callus"（老茧）——就像老茧使皮肤变硬变麻木一样，callous的人有一颗冷硬麻木的心。
