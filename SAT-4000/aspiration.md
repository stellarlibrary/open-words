# 🌟 aspiration

🔊 /ˌæspɪˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A strong hope or ambition to achieve something; the act of breathing or drawing in
**中文：** 渴望；抱负；志向

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ambition（志向）
- dream（梦想）
- goal（目标）
- desire（渴望）
- yearning（向往）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aspiration for / aspiration to / career aspiration / personal aspiration / lofty aspirations**

👉 Example: Her aspiration to become a renowned surgeon motivated her through years of demanding medical training.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship program was designed to support students whose **aspirations** exceeded the financial resources available to them.
   该奖学金项目旨在支持那些抱负超越其可用经济资源的学生。

2. His lifelong **aspiration** to serve the public good ultimately led him to pursue a distinguished career in diplomacy.
   他毕生为公共利益服务的志向最终引导他追求一段杰出的外交生涯。

3. The commencement speaker urged graduates to pursue their **aspirations** with determination, regardless of obstacles they might encounter.
   毕业典礼演讲者敦促毕业生们以坚定的决心追求自己的抱负，不论可能遇到什么障碍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aspiration" sounds like "a-SPIRE-ation" — think of a spire (tall tower) reaching upward, just like your goals reaching for the sky.
"Aspiration"听起来像"a-SPIRE-ation"——想象一座尖塔（spire）向上延伸，就像你的目标直冲云霄。
