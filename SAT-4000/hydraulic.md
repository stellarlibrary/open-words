# 🔧 hydraulic

🔊 /haɪˈdrɔːlɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or operated by the pressure of water or other liquids; pertaining to hydraulics as a branch of engineering
**中文：** adj. 液压的；水力的；水压的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- water-powered（水力驱动的）
- fluid-driven（流体驱动的）
- hydrostatic（静水压的）
- pressurized（加压的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hydraulic pressure / hydraulic system / hydraulic lift / hydraulic engineering / hydraulic brake**

👉 Example: The construction crew relied on hydraulic machinery to lift the massive steel beams into position.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The factory installed a new **hydraulic** press capable of exerting thousands of pounds of force per square inch.
   工厂安装了一台新的液压机，能够产生每平方英寸数千磅的压力。

2. Engineers designed an advanced **hydraulic** braking system to improve vehicle safety under extreme driving conditions.
   工程师们设计了一套先进的液压制动系统，以提高在极端驾驶条件下的车辆安全性。

3. The ancient Romans constructed elaborate **hydraulic** networks to transport fresh water across vast distances to their cities.
   古罗马人建造了精密的水力管网，将淡水跨越远距离输送到他们的城市。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydraul-ic" as "hydra" (water) + "aulos" (pipe) — water flowing through pipes under pressure.
把"hydraulic"想成"hydra"（水）加"aulos"（管道）——水在管道中在压力下流动。
