# 💫 charming

🔊 /ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Very pleasant or attractive; delightful in a way that wins the affection of others
**中文：** adj. 迷人的；可爱的；令人高兴的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- delightful（令人愉快的）
- captivating（迷人的）
- enchanting（迷人的）
- appealing（吸引人的）
- engaging（有魅力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **charming personality / charming smile / charming village / utterly charming**

👉 Example: The diplomat's charming demeanor helped ease the tension during the negotiation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **charming** personality enabled her to build strong alliances with foreign delegates at the international summit.
   大使迷人的个性使她能够在国际峰会上与外国代表建立牢固的联盟。

2. Visitors were captivated by the **charming** medieval village nestled among rolling hills and ancient vineyards in southern France.
   游客们被坐落在法国南部连绵丘陵和古老葡萄园中的迷人中世纪村庄所吸引。

3. His **charming** manner concealed a shrewd intellect that consistently outmaneuvered his rivals in corporate negotiations.
   他迷人的举止掩盖了一种精明的智慧，使他在商业谈判中总能胜过对手。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "charm + ing" — someone casting a charm or spell that makes everything around them pleasant and attractive.
把"charming"想成"charm（魅力）+ ing"——某人施展魅力，使周围一切变得令人愉悦和有吸引力。
