# 🪨 intransigent

🔊 /ɪnˈtrænzɪdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unwilling or refusing to change one's views or to agree about something; stubbornly uncompromising. (n.) An intransigent person
**中文：** adj. 不妥协的；不让步的 n. 不妥协的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unyielding（不屈服的）
- uncompromising（不妥协的）
- inflexible（不灵活的）
- obstinate（固执的）
- adamant（坚定不移的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intransigent attitude / intransigent position / remain intransigent / intransigent opposition**

👉 Example: The union's intransigent stance during negotiations led to a prolonged and costly strike.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The peace talks collapsed because both sides adopted **intransigent** positions and refused to make even the smallest concessions to each other.
   和平谈判破裂了，因为双方都采取了不妥协的立场，拒绝对彼此作出哪怕最小的让步。

2. The senator's **intransigent** opposition to the bipartisan bill frustrated colleagues who had worked tirelessly to reach a compromise agreement.
   这位参议员对两党法案的不妥协反对让那些为达成妥协协议而不懈努力的同事们感到沮丧。

3. Historians often attribute the failure of diplomatic efforts to the **intransigent** leadership that prioritized ideology over practical resolution.
   历史学家常常将外交努力的失败归因于那些将意识形态置于务实解决方案之上的不妥协领导层。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-TRANS-igent" — "in" (not) + "transigent" (willing to come to agreement). An intransigent person will not transit or move from their position.
把"intransigent"想成"in"（不）+ "transigent"（愿意达成协议的）——一个不妥协的人不会从自己的立场移动或转变。
