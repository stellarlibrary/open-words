# 🌊 levee

🔊 /ˈlɛvi/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An embankment built to prevent a river from overflowing; a formal reception or gathering. (v.) To build a levee along a waterway
**中文：** n. 堤坝；防洪堤，大堤；早晨接见 vt. 为……筑堤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- embankment（堤岸）
- dike（堤坝）
- dam（水坝）
- floodwall（防洪墙）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **breach the levee / reinforce the levee / levee system / along the levee**

👉 Example: Engineers worked around the clock to reinforce the levee before the floodwaters arrived.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The catastrophic breach of the **levee** resulted in widespread flooding that displaced thousands of residents.
   堤坝的灾难性溃决导致大范围洪水，使数千居民流离失所。

2. Federal authorities allocated substantial funding to repair and strengthen the aging **levee** system along the river.
   联邦当局拨出大量资金来修复和加固沿河老化的堤坝系统。

3. Historical records indicate that the colonial governor held a formal **levee** each morning to receive distinguished visitors.
   历史记录表明，殖民地总督每天早晨举行正式接见仪式，接待贵宾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "levee" as "lev-ee" from "lever/elevate" — a levee is an elevated bank of earth that holds back water.
把"levee"想成来自"elevate（升高）"——堤坝是一道升高的土堤，用来挡住洪水。
