# 🛡️ immune

🔊 /ɪˈmjuːn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Protected against a particular disease or infection by the body's natural defenses. Also, not affected or influenced by something; exempt from an obligation or penalty.
**中文：** adj. 免疫的；有免疫力的；不受影响的；免除的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resistant（有抵抗力的）
- protected（受保护的）
- exempt（免除的）
- invulnerable（不受伤害的）
- unaffected（不受影响的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **immune to disease / immune from prosecution / immune response / immune to criticism**

👉 Example: After recovering from the infection, the patient became immune to that particular strain of the virus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vaccination rendered the majority of the population **immune** to the virus that had previously caused devastating epidemics.
   疫苗接种使大多数人口对此前引发毁灭性流行病的病毒产生了免疫力。

2. No political leader should consider themselves **immune** from accountability to the citizens they have sworn to serve.
   任何政治领导人都不应认为自己可以免于对其宣誓服务的公民承担责任。

3. Prolonged exposure to media sensationalism has rendered many viewers effectively **immune** to the emotional impact of tragic news reports.
   长期接触媒体的煽情报道使许多观众对悲剧性新闻报道的情感冲击变得麻木不仁。

把"immune"想成穿了隐形盔甲——你的身体拥有内在的防护，使你免受疾病或伤害。
