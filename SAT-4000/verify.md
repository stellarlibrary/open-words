# ✅ verify

🔊 /ˈvɛrɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To confirm the truth, accuracy, or validity of something through examination or evidence
**中文：** v. 核实；查证；证实；证明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confirm（确认）
- validate（验证）
- authenticate（鉴定）
- corroborate（证实）
- substantiate（证明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verify the accuracy / verify a claim / verify identity / verify information**

👉 Example: The journalist contacted three independent sources to verify the accuracy of the report.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The auditor was hired to **verify** the financial statements before the company proceeded with its public offering.
   审计师受聘在公司进行公开发行之前核实财务报表。

2. Scientists must **verify** their experimental results through repeated trials before publishing any conclusions in peer-reviewed journals.
   科学家必须通过反复试验来验证实验结果，然后才能在同行评审期刊上发表结论。

3. The embassy required applicants to **verify** their identity by presenting original documents along with certified translations.
   大使馆要求申请人出示原始文件及经认证的翻译件来核实身份。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veri-fy" as "very + identify" — you identify something so thoroughly that it becomes very certain.
把"verify"想成"very + identify"——你非常彻底地辨认某事，使其变得非常确定。
