# 🧵 spindle

🔊 /ˈspɪndl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A shaft or axis; a spool used in spinning thread; something tall and thin. (adj.) Of or resembling a spindle; slender. (v.) To furnish with a spindle; to grow tall and thin
**中文：** n. 纺锤体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shaft（轴）
- axis（轴心）
- rod（杆）
- spool（线轴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spindle fiber / spindle cell / spindle-shaped / muscle spindle**

👉 Example: The mitotic spindle plays a critical role in separating chromosomes during cell division.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During mitosis, the **spindle** fibers attach to chromosomes and pull them toward opposite poles of the cell.
   在有丝分裂过程中，纺锤丝附着在染色体上，将它们拉向细胞的两极。

2. The artisan carefully wound the raw cotton around the wooden **spindle** to produce fine, even thread.
   工匠小心地将生棉缠绕在木质纺锤上，以纺出细而均匀的线。

3. The biologist observed that the **spindle** apparatus had failed to form properly, resulting in abnormal cell division.
   生物学家观察到纺锤体结构未能正常形成，导致细胞分裂异常。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spindle" as "spin-dle" — something that spins, like a spinning wheel's rod that twists fiber into thread.
把"spindle"想成"spin-dle"——旋转的东西，就像纺车上将纤维拧成线的杆子。
