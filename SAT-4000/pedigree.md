# 🐾 pedigree

🔊 /ˈpɛdɪɡriː/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The recorded ancestry or lineage of a person or animal; a distinguished background. (adj.) Of pure breed
**中文：** n. 血统；家谱；门第；纯种 adj. 纯种的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lineage（血统）
- ancestry（祖先）
- heritage（遗产）
- genealogy（家谱学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pedigree dog / impressive pedigree / pedigree of excellence / trace one's pedigree**

👉 Example: The candidate's academic pedigree included degrees from two of the world's most prestigious universities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The breeder provided a detailed **pedigree** chart tracing the dog's ancestry back through five generations of champion bloodlines.
   饲养员提供了一份详细的血统表，追溯了这只狗五代冠军血统的祖先。

2. Despite her impressive academic **pedigree**, she remained humble and committed to mentoring first-generation college students effectively.
   尽管拥有令人印象深刻的学术背景，她仍然保持谦逊，致力于有效地指导第一代大学生。

3. The institution's long **pedigree** of scientific innovation has attracted leading researchers from around the globe for decades.
   该机构在科学创新方面悠久的传统几十年来吸引了来自世界各地的顶尖研究人员。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pedi-gree" as "ped (foot) + gree (degree)" — tracing the footsteps and degrees of your ancestors through the family tree.
把"pedigree"想成"ped（脚步）+gree（级别）"——追溯祖先在家族树上的脚步和等级。
