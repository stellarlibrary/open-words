# 🔍 characteristic

🔊 /ˌkærɪktəˈrɪstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Typical of a particular person, place, or thing; distinctive. (n.) A distinguishing feature or quality
**中文：** adj. 典型的；特有的；表示特性的 n. 特征；特性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- typical（典型的）
- distinctive（独特的）
- trait（特征）
- attribute（属性）
- hallmark（标志）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **characteristic of / characteristic feature / defining characteristic / key characteristic**

👉 Example: Patience is a characteristic of great leaders who inspire lasting change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bright plumage is **characteristic** of tropical birds that inhabit dense rainforest canopies throughout the region.
   鲜艳的羽毛是该地区热带雨林树冠中栖息的热带鸟类的典型特征。

2. Resilience emerged as the most important **characteristic** shared by all successful entrepreneurs in the longitudinal study.
   在这项纵向研究中，韧性被认为是所有成功企业家最重要的共同特征。

3. The **characteristic** aroma of freshly roasted coffee permeated the entire laboratory during the sensory evaluation experiment.
   在感官评价实验中，新鲜烘焙咖啡的特有香气弥漫了整个实验室。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "character + istic" — a quality related to one's character, something that defines who they are.
把"characteristic"想成"character（性格）+ istic"——与性格相关的品质，定义一个人是谁的特征。
