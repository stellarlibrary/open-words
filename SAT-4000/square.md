# 🔲 square

🔊 /skwɛr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb, noun, adverb | 形容词, 动词, 名词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having four equal sides and right angles; honest and direct. (v.) To make square or rectangular; to bring into agreement. (n.) A plane figure with four equal sides; an open area in a town. (adv.) At right angles
**中文：** n. 正方形；广场；平方 adj. 平方的；正方形的；直角的；正直的 v. 使成方形；与…一致 adv. 成直角地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plaza（广场）
- rectangle（矩形）
- align（使一致）
- honest（正直的）
- straightforward（直截了当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **square deal / square with the facts / town square / square root / fair and square**

👉 Example: The candidate promised a square deal for every citizen regardless of social background.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect designed the building around a central **square** that served as a communal gathering space for residents.
   建筑师围绕一个中央广场设计了这座建筑，广场作为居民的公共聚集空间。

2. The committee struggled to **square** the proposed budget with the actual financial constraints facing the department.
   委员会努力使拟议预算与该部门面临的实际财务限制保持一致。

3. Her testimony did not **square** with the physical evidence presented by the forensic investigators during the trial.
   她的证词与法庭上法医调查人员提供的物证不一致。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a town "square" — it is a perfectly shaped open area where everything is orderly and lines up at right angles, just like the shape.
想象一个城镇"广场"——它是一个形状规整的开放区域，一切都井然有序，线条成直角，就像正方形一样。
