# 🌱 nurture

🔊 /ˈnɜːrtʃər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To care for and encourage the growth or development of; to nourish. (n.) The process of nurturing; upbringing, nourishment
**中文：** vt. 教养，抚育；给…营养物 n. 营养物，食物；养育

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cultivate（培养）
- nourish（滋养）
- foster（培育）
- rear（抚养）
- sustain（维持）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nurture talent / nurture a child / nurture growth / nature versus nurture**

👉 Example: Good teachers nurture their students' curiosity by encouraging questions and exploration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's primary mission is to **nurture** young entrepreneurs by providing mentorship, funding, and professional development resources.
   该组织的主要使命是通过提供指导、资金和专业发展资源来培养年轻企业家。

2. The debate over nature versus **nurture** has shaped decades of psychological research into human behavior and personality development.
   关于先天与后天的辩论影响了数十年来对人类行为和性格发展的心理学研究。

3. A supportive academic environment can effectively **nurture** critical thinking skills that students will carry throughout their professional careers.
   一个支持性的学术环境可以有效培养学生在整个职业生涯中都会运用的批判性思维能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nurture" as "nurse + nature" — like a nurse caring for nature, helping things grow with attention and nourishment.
把"nurture"想成"nurse + nature"——像护士照料自然一样，用关注和营养帮助事物成长。
