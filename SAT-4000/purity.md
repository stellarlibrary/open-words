# ✨ purity

🔊 /ˈpjʊrəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being pure; freedom from contamination, adulteration, or moral corruption
**中文：** n. 纯度；纯洁；纯净；纯粹

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cleanliness（洁净）
- innocence（纯真）
- chastity（贞洁）
- wholesomeness（健全）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **purity of intention / racial purity / chemical purity / moral purity / purity test**

👉 Example: The laboratory technician verified the purity of the chemical sample before proceeding with the experiment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **purity** of the water supply must be rigorously tested to ensure it meets federal safety standards.
   必须严格检测供水的纯度，以确保其符合联邦安全标准。

2. Critics argued that demands for ideological **purity** within the party were driving away moderate voters.
   批评者认为，党内对意识形态纯洁性的要求正在赶走温和派选民。

3. The gemologist assessed the **purity** of the diamond using specialized instruments and precise grading criteria.
   宝石学家使用专业仪器和精确的分级标准评估了钻石的纯度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "purity" as "pure + ity" — the suffix "-ity" turns the adjective "pure" into a noun describing the state of being completely clean and unmixed.
把"purity"想成"pure + ity"——后缀"-ity"将形容词"pure（纯的）"变成描述完全洁净、未混合状态的名词。
