# ⚖️ warrant

🔊 /ˈwɔːrənt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To justify or make necessary; to guarantee or assure. (n.) A document authorizing an action; a justification or reason
**中文：** v. 保证；担保；使有正当理由 n. 正当理由；许可证；委任状

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- justify（使有正当理由）
- authorize（授权）
- guarantee（保证）
- sanction（批准）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **warrant attention / warrant investigation / arrest warrant / search warrant**

👉 Example: The severity of the allegations certainly warrants a thorough and independent investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unusual findings in the preliminary study **warrant** further investigation before any definitive conclusions can be drawn.
   初步研究中的异常发现值得在得出任何明确结论之前进行进一步调查。

2. The judge issued a **warrant** for the suspect's arrest after reviewing compelling evidence presented by the prosecution team.
   法官在审查了检察团队提交的有力证据后签发了逮捕令。

3. The magnitude of the public health crisis **warrants** an immediate and coordinated response from all levels of government.
   公共卫生危机的严重程度使得各级政府必须立即采取协调行动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "warrant" as "war-rant" — in a war, you need official authorization (a warrant) and good reason to act.
把"warrant"想成"war-rant"——在战争中，你需要官方授权（许可证）和正当理由才能行动。
