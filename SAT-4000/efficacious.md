# 💊 efficacious

🔊 /ˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Successful in producing a desired or intended result; having the power to achieve an effect
**中文：** adj. 有效的；灵验的；有功效的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- effective（有效的）
- potent（有力的）
- productive（有成效的）
- effectual（奏效的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **efficacious treatment / efficacious remedy / efficacious method / efficacious drug**

👉 Example: Clinical trials demonstrated that the new vaccine was highly efficacious against all known variants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The herbal remedy, long dismissed by Western medicine, proved surprisingly **efficacious** in treating chronic inflammation in controlled trials.
   这种长期被西医忽视的草药疗法在对照试验中证明对治疗慢性炎症出人意料地有效。

2. Policymakers must evaluate whether proposed interventions are truly **efficacious** before committing substantial public resources to their implementation.
   政策制定者必须在投入大量公共资源之前评估拟议干预措施是否真正有效。

3. The most **efficacious** approach to reducing poverty combines immediate financial assistance with long-term investments in education and training.
   减少贫困最有效的方法是将即时财政援助与对教育和培训的长期投资相结合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "effi-cacious" as sharing its root with "efficiency" — something efficacious works efficiently to produce the desired result.
把"efficacious"想成和"efficiency（效率）"有相同词根——efficacious的事物能高效地产生预期效果。
