# 📜 license

🔊 /ˈlaɪsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An official permit or document granting authorization to do something. (v.) To grant a license or official permission to someone
**中文：** n. 许可；执照 v. 许可，发许可证给

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permit（许可证）
- authorization（授权）
- certification（认证）
- warrant（许可）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **driver's license / license to (do something) / license agreement / under license**

👉 Example: You must obtain a valid license before operating any commercial vehicle on public roads.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The restaurant owner applied for a **license** to serve alcoholic beverages after completing all required health and safety inspections.
   餐厅老板在完成所有必要的健康和安全检查后，申请了提供含酒精饮料的许可证。

2. The government agency decided to **license** the new pharmaceutical company after rigorous evaluation of its manufacturing and quality standards.
   政府机构在对其生产和质量标准进行严格评估后，决定向这家新制药公司发放许可证。

3. Artistic **license** allows writers and filmmakers to alter historical facts for the purpose of creating more compelling narratives.
   艺术许可允许作家和电影制作人为了创作更引人入胜的叙事而改变历史事实。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "license" as "li-cense" — you need official "sense" (approval) to legally do something, like a license plate on your car.
把"license"想成需要官方的"准许"才能合法做某事——就像你车上的车牌（license plate）一样。
