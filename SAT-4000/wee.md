# 🐜 wee

🔊 /wiː/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely small; tiny. (n.) A small amount or a short time
**中文：** adj. 极小的；很早的；很少的；微小的 n. 一点点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tiny（微小的）
- minute（极小的）
- diminutive（小型的）
- minuscule（微不足道的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a wee bit / wee hours / wee child / a wee amount**

👉 Example: They stayed up talking until the wee hours of the morning about their research findings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher worked diligently until the **wee** hours of the morning to complete her analysis before the conference deadline.
   这位研究人员勤奋工作到凌晨，以便在会议截止日期前完成她的分析。

2. Even a **wee** adjustment to the experimental parameters produced remarkably different outcomes in the final laboratory results.
   即使对实验参数做出微小的调整，也会在最终的实验结果中产生显著不同的结果。

3. The committee required only a **wee** portion of the allocated budget to implement the most critical phase of reform.
   委员会只需要分配预算中的一小部分就能实施最关键的改革阶段。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wee" as the sound a tiny mouse might make — small and barely noticeable, just like the word's meaning.
把"wee"想成一只小老鼠可能发出的声音——微小而几乎不引人注意，就像这个词的含义一样。
