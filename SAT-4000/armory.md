# 🏰 armory

🔊 /ˈɑːrməri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A place where weapons and military equipment are stored; a factory where arms are manufactured
**中文：** n. 军械库；兵工厂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arsenal（军火库）
- depot（仓库）
- magazine（弹药库）
- storehouse（储藏室）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **national armory / armory of weapons / the armory building**

👉 Example: The regiment's armory was heavily guarded around the clock by armed sentries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The soldiers were instructed to return all issued firearms to the **armory** before the end of their shift.
   士兵们被要求在换班前将所有配发的枪支归还军械库。

2. During the inspection, officials discovered that the **armory** contained several unregistered weapons and outdated munitions.
   在检查中，官员们发现军械库中有数件未登记的武器和过期弹药。

3. The historic **armory** building has been converted into a cultural center that hosts exhibitions and community events.
   这座历史悠久的兵工厂大楼已被改建为举办展览和社区活动的文化中心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arm-ory" as a place full of "arms" (weapons) — an armory is where arms are stored.
把"armory"拆成"arm（武器）+ ory（场所）"——存放武器的地方就是军械库。
