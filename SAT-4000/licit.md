# ✅ licit

🔊 /ˈlɪsɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not forbidden by law; permitted; lawful and legitimate
**中文：** adj. 合法的，正当的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lawful（合法的）
- legitimate（正当的）
- legal（法律许可的）
- permissible（允许的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **licit activities / licit trade / licit substances / perfectly licit**

👉 Example: The transaction was entirely licit, conducted in full accordance with international trade regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government established clear regulations to distinguish between **licit** commerce and illegal trafficking across national borders.
   政府制定了明确的法规来区分跨国界的合法商业和非法贩运。

2. Although the practice was technically **licit**, many scholars questioned whether it was ethically justifiable under the circumstances.
   虽然这种做法从技术上讲是合法的，但许多学者质疑在这种情况下它在伦理上是否站得住脚。

3. The pharmaceutical company ensured that all ingredients used in production were derived from **licit** and fully documented sources.
   该制药公司确保生产中使用的所有原料均来自合法且有完整记录的来源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "licit" as the opposite of "illicit" — remove the "il-" (meaning "not") and you get something that is lawful and allowed.
把"licit"想成"illicit"（非法的）的反义词——去掉"il-"（表示"不"），你就得到了合法和允许的意思。
