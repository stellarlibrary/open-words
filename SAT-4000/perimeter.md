# 📐 perimeter

🔊 /pəˈrɪmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The continuous boundary line enclosing an area; the total length of such a boundary; the outer edge or border of an enclosed area
**中文：** n. 周长；周界；边界；外缘

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boundary（边界）
- border（边缘）
- circumference（周长）
- periphery（外围）
- edge（边沿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perimeter of a shape / perimeter fence / security perimeter / perimeter defense**

👉 Example: The guards patrolled the entire perimeter of the military compound throughout the night.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect calculated the **perimeter** of the proposed building site before drafting the initial blueprints for the construction project.
   建筑师在为建设项目起草初始蓝图之前，计算了拟建工地的周长。

2. Security personnel established a strict **perimeter** around the embassy following the credible threat received from intelligence agencies that morning.
   安全人员在当天早上收到情报机构的可靠威胁后，在大使馆周围建立了严格的警戒线。

3. Students learned to compute the **perimeter** of irregular polygons by measuring and summing the lengths of all individual sides.
   学生们学会了通过测量和求和所有各边的长度来计算不规则多边形的周长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peri-METER" as "measure around" — "peri" means around (as in periscope), and "meter" means measure.
把"perimeter"想成"peri（周围）+ meter（测量）"——即"围绕测量"，测量周围一圈的长度。
