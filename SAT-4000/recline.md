# 🛋️ recline

🔊 /rɪˈklaɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To lean or lie back in a relaxed position; to rest or repose in a leaning posture
**中文：** v. 向后靠；斜倚；靠；依赖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lean back（向后靠）
- lounge（懒散地靠）
- repose（休息）
- sprawl（伸展四肢）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recline in a chair / recline on a sofa / recline the seat / recline against**

👉 Example: Passengers are advised not to recline their seats during takeoff and landing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient was instructed to **recline** comfortably on the examination table while the physician performed the diagnostic procedure.
   患者被要求舒适地斜倚在检查台上，同时医生进行诊断程序。

2. She preferred to **recline** in the leather armchair by the fireplace while reading her favorite literary journals.
   她喜欢斜倚在壁炉旁的皮扶手椅上阅读她最喜爱的文学期刊。

3. Ancient Roman aristocrats would **recline** on ornate couches during elaborate banquets that lasted well into the evening.
   古罗马贵族在持续到深夜的奢华宴会上会斜倚在华丽的长椅上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-cline" as related to "incline" — when you incline backward, you recline, leaning back to rest.
把"recline"和"incline（倾斜）"联系起来——当你向后倾斜时，你就是在recline，向后靠着休息。
