# 🧊 crystallization

🔊 /ˌkrɪstəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process by which a substance forms a crystalline structure; the act of making an idea or plan definite and concrete
**中文：** n. 结晶化；具体化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- solidification（凝固）
- formation（形成）
- concretization（具体化）
- materialization（实现）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crystallization of ideas / crystallization process / crystallization point / undergo crystallization**

👉 Example: The crystallization of the salt solution was carefully monitored throughout the laboratory experiment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **crystallization** of public opinion against the policy ultimately forced the legislature to abandon the controversial proposal.
   公众舆论对该政策的明确反对最终迫使立法机构放弃了这项有争议的提案。

2. Chemists studied the **crystallization** process of the protein to better understand its three-dimensional molecular structure.
   化学家研究了蛋白质的结晶过程，以更好地了解其三维分子结构。

3. The philosopher's later works represent the **crystallization** of decades of thought into a coherent and unified theoretical framework.
   这位哲学家后期的作品代表了数十年思考结晶为一个连贯统一的理论框架。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crystal-ization" — the process (-ization) of becoming a crystal. Just as liquid becomes solid crystal, vague ideas become concrete through crystallization.
把"crystallization"想成"crystal（晶体）+ ization（过程）"——变成晶体的过程。正如液体变成固体晶体，模糊的想法通过crystallization变得具体。
