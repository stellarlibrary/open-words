# 💪 morale

🔊 /məˈræl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The confidence, enthusiasm, and discipline of a person or group at a particular time
**中文：** n. 士气；斗志；民心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spirit（精神）
- confidence（信心）
- enthusiasm（热情）
- resolve（决心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **boost morale / low morale / high morale / team morale / morale of the troops**

👉 Example: The manager organized a celebration to boost the morale of the exhausted team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unexpected victory on the battlefield significantly raised the **morale** of the weary soldiers and their commanding officers.
   战场上意想不到的胜利大大提升了疲惫的士兵及其指挥官的士气。

2. Employee **morale** declined sharply after the company announced widespread layoffs without any prior consultation or warning.
   公司在未经事先协商或警告的情况下宣布大规模裁员后，员工士气急剧下降。

3. Strong leadership and transparent communication are essential for maintaining high **morale** during periods of organizational change.
   在组织变革期间，强有力的领导和透明的沟通对于保持高昂的士气至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "morale" as "moral + e" — when people have strong morals and purpose, their morale stays high.
把"morale"想成"moral + e"——当人们有坚定的信念和目标时，他们的士气就会高涨。
