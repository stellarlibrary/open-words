# 🧪 nitrogen

🔊 /ˈnaɪtrədʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A chemical element (symbol N), a colorless, odorless gas that makes up about 78% of the Earth's atmosphere, essential to all living organisms
**中文：** n. 氮（一种化学元素，无色无味的气体）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- N₂（氮气分子）
- azote（氮的旧称）
- element 7（第7号元素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nitrogen cycle / nitrogen fixation / liquid nitrogen / nitrogen-rich**

👉 Example: The nitrogen cycle is a critical biogeochemical process that sustains ecosystems worldwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Atmospheric **nitrogen** constitutes approximately seventy-eight percent of the air we breathe on Earth every day.
   大气中的氮约占我们每天呼吸的空气的百分之七十八。

2. Scientists used liquid **nitrogen** to rapidly freeze the biological samples and preserve their cellular integrity for analysis.
   科学家们使用液氮快速冷冻生物样本，以保持其细胞完整性供分析使用。

3. Excessive **nitrogen** in agricultural runoff can lead to harmful algal blooms that devastate aquatic ecosystems.
   农业径流中过量的氮会导致有害藻华，严重破坏水生生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nitro-gen" as "niter generator" — niter (saltpeter) is a nitrogen compound, so nitrogen is the element that generates niter.
把"nitrogen"想成"niter（硝石）的生成者"——硝石是一种含氮化合物，所以氮就是生成硝石的元素。
