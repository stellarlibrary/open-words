# ⚛️ hydrogen

🔊 /ˈhaɪdrədʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A colorless, odorless, highly flammable chemical element with the atomic number 1, the lightest and most abundant element in the universe
**中文：** n. 氢

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- H（氢元素符号）
- lightest element（最轻元素）
- protium（氕，轻氢）
- dihydrogen（双原子氢）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hydrogen atom / hydrogen bond / hydrogen fuel / hydrogen peroxide / liquid hydrogen**

👉 Example: Scientists are exploring hydrogen as a clean fuel alternative to reduce dependence on fossil fuels.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist demonstrated that water molecules consist of two **hydrogen** atoms covalently bonded to one oxygen atom.
   化学家演示了水分子由两个氢原子与一个氧原子通过共价键结合而成。

2. Engineers are developing efficient **hydrogen** fuel cells that could revolutionize the transportation industry within the coming decades.
   工程师们正在开发高效的氢燃料电池，可能在未来几十年内彻底改变交通运输行业。

3. Astronomers confirmed that **hydrogen** constitutes approximately seventy-five percent of all ordinary matter in the observable universe.
   天文学家证实，氢约占可观测宇宙中所有普通物质的百分之七十五。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydro-gen" as "water-generator" — hydrogen is the element that generates water when it combines with oxygen.
把"hydrogen"想成"hydro"（水）+"gen"（产生）——氢是与氧结合时生成水的元素。
