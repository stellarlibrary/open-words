# 😤 oppressive

🔊 /əˈprɛsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unjustly harsh or authoritarian; weighing heavily on the mind or spirits; unbearably hot and humid
**中文：** adj. 压迫的；沉重的；压制性的；难以忍受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tyrannical（暴虐的）
- burdensome（沉重的）
- stifling（令人窒息的）
- repressive（压制的）
- suffocating（令人透不过气的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oppressive regime / oppressive heat / oppressive laws / oppressive atmosphere**

👉 Example: The oppressive summer heat made outdoor activities virtually impossible for the entire population of the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Citizens staged massive protests against the **oppressive** regime that had systematically stripped away their fundamental civil liberties.
   公民发起了大规模抗议，反对这个系统性剥夺他们基本公民自由的压迫性政权。

2. The **oppressive** humidity of the tropical climate made it exceedingly difficult for the expedition members to maintain their pace.
   热带气候令人窒息的潮湿使探险队员极难保持行进速度。

3. Workers endured **oppressive** conditions in the factories throughout the nineteenth century before labor reform movements gained significant momentum.
   在劳工改革运动获得显著动力之前，工人们在整个十九世纪都忍受着工厂中令人难以忍受的条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "op-PRESS-ive" — it literally contains "PRESS," as if something is pressing down on you heavily, crushing your freedom or spirit.
把"oppressive"想成"op-PRESS-ive"——它字面上包含"PRESS（压）"，就好像某物沉重地压在你身上，压迫你的自由或精神。
