# 🔀 variant

🔊 /ˈvɛəriənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Differing from a standard or type. (n.) A form or version that differs from other forms of the same thing
**中文：** adj. 不同的；变异的 n. 变体；变种

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alternative（替代的）
- deviation（偏差）
- modification（修改）
- version（版本）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **variant form / variant strain / variant spelling / genetic variant**

👉 Example: Scientists identified a new variant of the virus that appeared to spread more rapidly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly discovered **variant** of the pathogen exhibited increased resistance to conventional antiviral treatments and therapies.
   新发现的病原体变种对常规抗病毒治疗和疗法表现出更强的耐药性。

2. Several **variant** spellings of the word have persisted in academic literature across different historical periods and regions.
   该词的几种不同拼写形式在不同历史时期和地区的学术文献中一直存在。

3. Researchers compared the original manuscript with a **variant** text to determine which version more accurately reflected the author's intent.
   研究人员将原始手稿与一个变体文本进行比较，以确定哪个版本更准确地反映了作者的意图。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vari-ant" as "vary + ant" — an ant that varies from the rest of the colony, standing out as different.
把"variant"想成"vary + ant"——一只与蚁群中其他蚂蚁不同的蚂蚁，显得与众不同。
