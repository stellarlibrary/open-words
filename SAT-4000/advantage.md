# 🏆 advantage

🔊 /ədˈvæntɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A favorable condition or circumstance that gives superiority; (v.) to benefit or profit
**中文：** n. 优势；利益；有利条件 v. 获利；有利于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- benefit（好处）
- edge（优势）
- superiority（优越性）
- leverage（有利地位）
- asset（有利因素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **take advantage of / have an advantage over / competitive advantage / to one's advantage**

👉 Example: Bilingual candidates often have a distinct advantage over monolingual applicants in the global job market.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company's early investment in renewable technology gave it a significant **advantage** over its competitors in the emerging market.
   该公司在可再生技术上的早期投资使其在新兴市场上比竞争对手拥有显著优势。

2. Students who develop strong critical thinking skills gain a lasting **advantage** in both academic pursuits and professional endeavors.
   培养出较强批判性思维能力的学生在学业和职业发展中都会获得持久的优势。

3. The diplomat skillfully used the economic data to **advantage** her nation's position during the bilateral trade negotiations.
   这位外交官巧妙地利用经济数据在双边贸易谈判中为本国争取有利地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "advantage" as "ADVANCE-age" — having an advantage means you are in an age of advancement, always one step ahead.
把"advantage"想成"ADVANCE-age"——拥有优势就像处于领先的时代(age)，总是比别人先进(advance)一步。
