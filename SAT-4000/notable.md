# 🌟 notable

🔊 /ˈnoʊtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Worthy of attention or notice; remarkable; famous. (n.) A famous or important person
**中文：** adj. 值得注意的；显著的；著名的 n. 名人，显要人物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- remarkable（显著的）
- prominent（杰出的）
- distinguished（卓越的）
- noteworthy（值得注意的）
- eminent（著名的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a notable achievement / notable for something / notable exception / notable figure**

👉 Example: The researcher made a notable contribution to the field of molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The most **notable** achievement of the conference was the unanimous agreement on international climate reduction targets.
   这次会议最显著的成就是就国际减排目标达成了一致同意。

2. Several **notable** scholars have challenged the traditional interpretation of this ancient philosophical text.
   几位著名学者对这部古代哲学文本的传统解读提出了质疑。

3. With a few **notable** exceptions, most participants in the study reported significant improvements in cognitive performance.
   除了少数几个值得注意的例外，研究中大多数参与者报告认知能力有了显著提升。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "note-able" — something so important that you are able to take note of it; it deserves to be noted.
把"notable"想成"note(注意) + able(值得的)"——值得你记下来的、引人注目的事物。
