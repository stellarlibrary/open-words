# 🔍 debunk

🔊 /dɪˈbʌŋk/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To expose the falseness or hollowness of; to prove that something is false or exaggerated
**中文：** v. 揭穿；拆穿假面具；驳斥

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expose（揭露）
- disprove（反驳）
- refute（驳斥）
- discredit（使不可信）
- unmask（揭露真面目）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **debunk a myth / debunk a theory / debunk claims / debunk misconceptions**

👉 Example: The scientific study effectively debunked the widely held myth about the health benefits of that supplement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigative journalist worked tirelessly to **debunk** the fraudulent claims made by the pharmaceutical company regarding its miracle drug.
   这位调查记者不懈努力揭穿了那家制药公司关于其神奇药物的虚假宣传。

2. Modern archaeological evidence has helped **debunk** several long-standing myths about the construction methods used for ancient pyramids.
   现代考古证据帮助揭穿了关于古代金字塔建造方法的几个长期流传的谣言。

3. The professor devoted an entire lecture to **debunk** the popular misconception that humans only use ten percent of their brains.
   教授用了整整一节课来驳斥人类只使用了百分之十大脑的流行误解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-bunk" as removing the "bunk" (nonsense) — you clear away the lies and reveal the truth underneath.
把"debunk"想成"去掉bunk（废话）"——你清除谎言，揭示下面的真相。
