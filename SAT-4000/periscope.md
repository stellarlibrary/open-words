# 🔭 periscope

🔊 /ˈperɪskoʊp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An optical instrument that uses mirrors or prisms to allow observation of objects above or around obstacles
**中文：** n. 潜望镜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- viewing instrument（观察仪器）
- optical device（光学装置）
- scope（观测镜）
- sighting tube（瞄准管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **raise the periscope / periscope depth / look through a periscope / submarine periscope**

👉 Example: The captain ordered the crew to raise the periscope to survey the surface before emerging.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The submarine commander peered through the **periscope** to confirm that no enemy vessels were patrolling the surrounding waters.
   潜艇指挥官通过潜望镜观察，确认周围水域没有敌舰巡逻。

2. Engineers developed a miniature **periscope** that allowed soldiers to observe hostile zones without exposing themselves to gunfire.
   工程师们开发了一种微型潜望镜，使士兵能够在不暴露于枪火下的情况下观察敌区。

3. The physics teacher demonstrated how a simple **periscope** works by arranging two mirrors at precise forty-five-degree angles.
   物理老师通过将两面镜子精确地以四十五度角排列，演示了简易潜望镜的工作原理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peri-scope" — "peri" means "around" and "scope" means "to look." A periscope lets you look around obstacles without being seen.
把"periscope"想成"peri（周围）+ scope（看）"——潜望镜让你绕过障碍物观察周围环境。
