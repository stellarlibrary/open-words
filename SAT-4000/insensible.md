# 😶 insensible

🔊 /ɪnˈsensəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unconscious; lacking awareness or sensation; indifferent or unresponsive to something
**中文：** 昏迷的；无知觉的；麻木不仁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unconscious（昏迷的）
- oblivious（不知道的）
- unaware（未察觉的）
- indifferent（漠不关心的）
- numb（麻木的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insensible to pain / insensible to danger / rendered insensible / insensible of the risk**

👉 Example: The patient was rendered insensible by the anesthesia before the surgical procedure began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The injured soldier lay **insensible** on the battlefield until the medical team arrived to administer critical first aid.
   受伤的士兵昏迷在战场上，直到医疗队赶到进行紧急急救。

2. The committee appeared entirely **insensible** to the devastating consequences their decision would have on vulnerable populations.
   委员会似乎完全没有意识到他们的决定将对弱势群体产生的毁灭性后果。

3. Prolonged exposure to extreme cold rendered the hiker **insensible**, and rescuers found him unresponsive near the summit trail.
   长时间暴露在极度寒冷中使这位徒步者失去了知觉，救援人员在山顶小径附近发现他毫无反应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-sens-ible" — "in" (not) + "sens" (sense) + "ible" (able). Not able to sense — either physically unconscious or emotionally unaware.
把"insensible"想成"in（不）+ sens（感觉）+ ible（能够）"——不能感知的，要么身体失去知觉，要么情感上麻木不仁。
