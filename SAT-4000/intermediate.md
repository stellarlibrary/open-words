# 🔀 intermediate

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈmiːdiət/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective, noun | 动词, 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To act as a mediator or go-between. (adj.) Coming between two things in time, place, or level. (n.) Something in between; a mediating agent
**中文：** v. 起媒介作用 adj. 中间的，中级的 n. 中间物；媒介

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- middle（中间的）
- mediator（调解者）
- transitional（过渡的）
- midway（中途的）
- in-between（居间的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intermediate level / intermediate stage / intermediate step / chemical intermediate**

👉 Example: Students at the intermediate level should be able to construct complex sentences with relative clauses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **intermediate** course in organic chemistry bridges the gap between introductory concepts and advanced research methodologies.
   有机化学的中级课程弥合了入门概念与高级研究方法之间的差距。

2. The lawyer agreed to **intermediate** between the disputing parties to facilitate a settlement before the case went to trial.
   律师同意在争议各方之间进行调解，以便在案件开庭审理前促成和解。

3. Several **intermediate** compounds were identified during the analysis of the complex biochemical reaction pathway in the laboratory.
   在实验室分析复杂的生化反应途径时，鉴定出了几种中间化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-MEDI-ate" — "medi" means middle (like "median"), so intermediate is something that sits in the middle between two extremes.
把"intermediate"想成"inter-MEDI-ate"——"medi"意为中间（如"median"），所以intermediate是处于两个极端之间的事物。
