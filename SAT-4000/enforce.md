# ⚖️ enforce

🔊 /ɪnˈfɔːrs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To compel observance of a law, rule, or obligation; to impose something by force or authority
**中文：** v. 实施，执行；强迫；强制执行

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- implement（实施）
- impose（强加）
- compel（强迫）
- execute（执行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enforce the law / enforce regulations / enforce a policy / enforce compliance**

👉 Example: The regulatory agency pledged to enforce stricter environmental standards across all industries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The municipal government hired additional officers to **enforce** newly enacted traffic regulations in the congested downtown district.
   市政府雇用了额外的执法人员在拥挤的市中心区域执行新颁布的交通法规。

2. International organizations struggle to **enforce** humanitarian agreements when member nations prioritize their own political and economic interests.
   当成员国优先考虑自身政治和经济利益时，国际组织难以执行人道主义协议。

3. The school administration decided to **enforce** a strict code of academic integrity to discourage plagiarism among students.
   学校管理层决定执行严格的学术诚信准则，以阻止学生的抄袭行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "enforce" as "en-FORCE" — to put force into action, making sure rules are followed by applying authority.
把"enforce"想成"en-FORCE"——把力量付诸行动，通过施加权威确保规则得到遵守。
