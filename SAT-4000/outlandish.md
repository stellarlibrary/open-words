# 👽 outlandish

🔊 /aʊtˈlændɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Strikingly bizarre, strange, or unfamiliar; looking or sounding foreign; extremely unconventional
**中文：** adj. 古怪的；奇异的；异国风格的；偏僻的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bizarre（古怪的）
- eccentric（怪异的）
- peculiar（奇特的）
- exotic（异域的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outlandish idea / outlandish appearance / outlandish claim / outlandish behavior**

👉 Example: The designer's outlandish costumes attracted both admiration and bewilderment from the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics dismissed the scientist's theory as **outlandish**, but subsequent experiments proved its remarkable validity.
   批评者认为这位科学家的理论荒诞不经，但随后的实验证明了其非凡的正确性。

2. The diplomat arrived wearing an **outlandish** ensemble that reflected the vibrant cultural traditions of her homeland.
   这位外交官穿着一套奇异的服装到场，体现了她祖国充满活力的文化传统。

3. His **outlandish** proposal to restructure the entire department was met with skepticism by the senior management team.
   他关于重组整个部门的怪异提议遭到了高级管理团队的质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out + land + ish" — something from an outer land seems foreign, strange, and bizarre to locals.
把"outlandish"拆成"out（外面的）+ land（土地）+ ish（像…的）"——来自外地的东西对本地人来说显得陌生、奇怪和古怪。
