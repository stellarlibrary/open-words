# 🧊 phlegmatic

🔊 /fleɡˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having an unemotional and stolidly calm disposition; not easily excited or moved to action or display of emotion
**中文：** adj. 冷淡的；迟钝的；冷漠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impassive（无动于衷的）
- stoic（淡漠的）
- apathetic（冷漠的）
- unflappable（镇定的）
- indifferent（冷淡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **phlegmatic temperament / phlegmatic response / phlegmatic attitude / remain phlegmatic**

👉 Example: The surgeon maintained a phlegmatic demeanor even during the most critical moments of the operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the chaos erupting around him, the commander remained remarkably **phlegmatic** and issued orders with steady composure.
   尽管周围一片混乱，指挥官仍然非常冷静，镇定自若地发出命令。

2. Her **phlegmatic** response to the devastating news surprised everyone who expected an emotional and immediate reaction from her.
   她对这一毁灭性消息的冷淡回应让所有期待她做出情绪化反应的人感到惊讶。

3. Critics described the diplomat as excessively **phlegmatic**, arguing that his lack of visible passion undermined public confidence in negotiations.
   批评者形容这位外交官过于冷漠，认为他缺乏明显的激情削弱了公众对谈判的信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "phlegm-atic" — someone full of phlegm (mucus) seems sluggish and unemotional, like a person who is too congested to react.
把"phlegmatic"想成"phlegm（痰）+ atic"——一个满是痰的人显得迟缓且无动于衷，就像一个太拥堵而无法做出反应的人。
