# 📊 median

🔊 /ˈmiːdiən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The middle value in a sorted series of numbers; the dividing line in the center of a road. (adj.) Situated in the middle; relating to the median
**中文：** n. 中值；中位数；中线 adj. 中值的；中央的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- midpoint（中点）
- middle（中间）
- center（中心）
- average（平均值）
- intermediate（中间的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **median income / median value / median age / median strip / median line**

👉 Example: The median household income in the region has risen steadily over the past five years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Statisticians prefer the **median** over the mean when analyzing income data because it is less affected by extreme outliers.
   统计学家在分析收入数据时更倾向于使用中位数，因为它受极端异常值的影响较小。

2. The **median** age of the country's population has increased significantly, reflecting broader demographic shifts toward an aging society.
   该国人口的中位年龄显著增加，反映了向老龄化社会更广泛的人口结构转变。

3. Urban planners installed a landscaped **median** along the boulevard to separate traffic lanes and enhance pedestrian safety.
   城市规划者在林荫大道上设置了绿化隔离带，以分隔车道并提高行人安全。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "median" as "medi-an" — "medi" means middle (like "medieval" = middle ages), so the median is always the middle value.
把"median"想成"medi-an"——"medi"意为中间（如"medieval"=中世纪），所以median总是中间的值。
