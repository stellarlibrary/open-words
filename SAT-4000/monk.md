# 🙏 monk

🔊 /mʌŋk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A member of a religious community of men who live under vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience; a man devoted to monastic life
**中文：** n. 僧侣；修道士；和尚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- friar（修道士）
- cleric（神职人员）
- ascetic（苦行者）
- hermit（隐士）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Buddhist monk / Benedictine monk / live like a monk / monastery of monks**

👉 Example: The monk spent decades in solitary contemplation within the ancient mountain monastery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **monk** devoted his entire life to transcribing sacred manuscripts in the dimly lit scriptorium of the medieval abbey.
   这位修道士毕生致力于在中世纪修道院昏暗的缮写室中抄录神圣手稿。

2. A Buddhist **monk** from the remote highland temple traveled to the capital to deliver a series of philosophical lectures.
   一位来自偏远高地寺庙的佛教僧侣前往首都发表一系列哲学讲座。

3. The young **monk** embraced a life of austere discipline, renouncing all worldly possessions and personal ambitions entirely.
   这位年轻的僧侣过上了严格自律的生活，完全放弃了所有世俗财产和个人抱负。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "monk" sounding like "mono" (alone) — a monk often lives alone or in seclusion, dedicating himself to spiritual practice.
"monk"听起来像"mono"（单独）——僧侣通常独自或隐居生活，致力于精神修行。
