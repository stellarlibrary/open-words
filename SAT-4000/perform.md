# 🎭 perform

🔊 /pərˈfɔːrm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To carry out, accomplish, or execute a task or action; to present entertainment before an audience; to function or operate
**中文：** v. 履行；执行；做；表演；演出；运转

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- execute（执行）
- accomplish（完成）
- carry out（实施）
- enact（表演）
- fulfill（履行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perform a task / perform surgery / perform well / perform a role / perform an experiment**

👉 Example: The research team was asked to perform a series of controlled experiments over the semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon will **perform** a delicate operation on the patient's spinal cord early tomorrow morning at the university hospital.
   外科医生明天一早将在大学医院为患者进行一项精细的脊髓手术。

2. Students are expected to **perform** at a high academic level if they wish to maintain their competitive scholarships.
   如果学生希望保持竞争性奖学金，他们应在高学术水平上表现出色。

3. The orchestra will **perform** a challenging symphony composed by Mahler at the annual international music festival this weekend.
   管弦乐队将在本周末的年度国际音乐节上演奏一首由马勒创作的高难度交响曲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-FORM" as doing something "through the form" — you perform by carrying an action through to its completed form.
把"perform"想成"per（通过）+ form（形式）"——通过完整的形式将行动贯彻到底。
