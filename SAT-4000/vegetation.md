# 🌲 vegetation

🔊 /ˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Plants considered collectively, especially those found in a particular area or habitat; the process of plant growth
**中文：** n. 植被；植物；（总称）草木

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flora（植物群）
- greenery（绿色植物）
- foliage（树叶）
- undergrowth（灌木丛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dense vegetation / natural vegetation / tropical vegetation / vegetation cover**

👉 Example: The dense vegetation along the riverbank provided essential habitat for numerous wildlife species.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Satellite imagery revealed that the region's **vegetation** cover had declined by nearly thirty percent over the past two decades.
   卫星图像显示，该地区的植被覆盖率在过去二十年中下降了近百分之三十。

2. The ecologist conducted a comprehensive survey of the native **vegetation** to assess the impact of invasive species on local biodiversity.
   生态学家对当地植被进行了全面调查，以评估入侵物种对当地生物多样性的影响。

3. After the wildfire, new **vegetation** slowly began to emerge from the scorched earth, signaling the gradual recovery of the ecosystem.
   野火过后，新的植被慢慢开始从焦土中长出，标志着生态系统的逐步恢复。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veget-ation" as "the state (-ation) of vegetating" — plants collectively in their natural state of growing.
把"vegetation"想成"vegetating的状态（-ation）"——植物在其自然生长状态下的总称。
