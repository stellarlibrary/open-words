# 🧬 artificial fertilization

🔊 /ˌɑːrtɪˈfɪʃəl ˌfɜːrtɪlɪˈzeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The process of achieving fertilization by introducing sperm or pollen artificially rather than through natural mating or pollination.

**中文：** 人工受精；人工授精

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- artificial insemination 人工授精
- assisted reproduction 辅助生殖
- in vitro fertilization 体外受精

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Artificial fertilization" is a broad term used in both animal husbandry and human reproductive medicine. It encompasses techniques like artificial insemination and in vitro fertilization (IVF).

"artificial fertilization" 是一个广泛的术语，用于畜牧业和人类生殖医学。它涵盖了人工授精和体外受精（IVF）等技术。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The veterinary team used **artificial fertilization** to improve the genetic quality of the livestock herd.
   兽医团队使用人工授精来改善牲畜群体的基因质量。

2. Advances in **artificial fertilization** have given countless couples renewed hope of starting a family.
   人工受精技术的进步让无数夫妇重新燃起了组建家庭的希望。

3. The biologist explained how **artificial fertilization** differs from natural reproduction in several key aspects.
   这位生物学家解释了人工受精与自然繁殖在几个关键方面的不同。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Artificial" (人造的) + "fertilization" (受精) — fertilization achieved through human intervention, not nature.

"Artificial"（人造的）+ "fertilization"（受精）——通过人为干预而非自然方式实现的受精。
