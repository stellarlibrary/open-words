# ⚔️ rebut

🔊 /rɪˈbʌt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To claim or prove that evidence or an accusation is false; to refute or disprove
**中文：** v. 反驳；揭穿；拒绝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refute（反驳）
- disprove（驳斥）
- contradict（反驳）
- counter（反击）
- negate（否定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rebut an argument / rebut a claim / rebut allegations / rebut evidence**

👉 Example: The defense attorney attempted to rebut every piece of evidence presented by the prosecution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist published a detailed paper to **rebut** the controversial claims made by opponents of the prevailing climate theory.
   这位科学家发表了一篇详细论文，以反驳主流气候理论反对者提出的争议性说法。

2. During the debate, the candidate skillfully **rebutted** her opponent's accusations with verifiable facts and statistical evidence.
   在辩论中，这位候选人用可查证的事实和统计数据巧妙地反驳了对手的指控。

3. The company issued an official statement to **rebut** allegations of financial misconduct that had surfaced in recent media reports.
   公司发表了一份官方声明，以反驳近期媒体报道中出现的财务不当行为的指控。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-but" as saying "but" again — when you rebut, you keep saying "but that's not true" to counter an argument.
把"rebut"想成反复说"but"——当你rebut时，你不断说"但这不是事实"来反驳论点。
