# 💥 demolish

🔊 /dɪˈmɒlɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To tear down or destroy completely; to put an end to; to ruin or defeat decisively
**中文：** v. 拆除；破坏；毁坏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destroy（摧毁）
- raze（夷为平地）
- dismantle（拆卸）
- wreck（破坏）
- obliterate（消灭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **demolish a building / demolish an argument / demolish the opposition / demolish barriers**

👉 Example: The defense attorney systematically demolished every argument presented by the prosecution during the trial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. City officials voted to **demolish** the condemned warehouse district to make way for a modern affordable housing development.
   市政官员投票决定拆除被判定为危险建筑的仓库区，为现代化经济适用房建设让路。

2. The scholar's meticulous research effectively **demolished** the widely held assumption that the manuscript was authentic and historically accurate.
   这位学者细致的研究有效地推翻了该手稿为真实且具有历史准确性的普遍假设。

3. The underdog team managed to **demolish** the defending champions with a stunning display of strategic coordination and skill.
   这支处于劣势的队伍以令人惊叹的战略协调和技巧成功击溃了卫冕冠军。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-MOLISH" — to "molish" sounds like "abolish," and adding "de-" intensifies it: to completely abolish a structure.
把"demolish"想成"de-MOLISH"——"molish"听起来像"abolish"（废除），加上"de-"加强语气：彻底废除一个结构。
