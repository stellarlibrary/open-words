# 📖 bibliomania

🔊 /ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An obsessive or passionate desire to collect and possess books, especially rare or valuable editions
**中文：** 藏书癖；（尤指珍本书）收藏狂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- book obsession（藏书痴迷）
- bibliophilism（爱书癖）
- book hoarding（囤书癖）
- book collecting mania（书籍收藏狂热）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffer from bibliomania / a case of bibliomania / bibliomania for rare editions / driven by bibliomania**

👉 Example: His bibliomania led him to spend his entire fortune acquiring first editions of medieval manuscripts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The collector's **bibliomania** was so extreme that every room in his mansion overflowed with rare and antique volumes.
   这位收藏家的藏书癖如此极端，以至于他豪宅的每个房间都堆满了珍稀古籍。

2. Scholars have documented cases of **bibliomania** in which individuals resorted to theft to obtain coveted literary treasures.
   学者们记录了一些藏书癖案例，其中有人不惜偷窃来获取梦寐以求的文学珍品。

3. Her **bibliomania** began innocently with weekend visits to bookshops but gradually evolved into an uncontrollable compulsion.
   她的藏书癖始于无害的周末书店之行，但逐渐演变为一种无法控制的强迫行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "biblio" (book) + "mania" (madness, obsession) — bibliomania is literally a madness or obsession for books.
把"bibliomania"拆分为"biblio"（书）+"mania"（狂热、痴迷）——bibliomania字面意思就是对书的疯狂痴迷。
