# 😤 rankle

🔊 /ˈræŋkl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause persistent annoyance, irritation, or resentment; to fester and continue to be painful or bitter
**中文：** v. 激怒；使怨恨；化脓；发炎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritate（激怒）
- fester（溃烂）
- gall（使恼怒）
- embitter（使痛苦）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rankle with / still rankles / rankle someone / continue to rankle**

👉 Example: The unfair dismissal continued to rankle him for years, fueling his desire for professional vindication.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee's decision to reject the proposal without explanation continued to **rankle** the researchers long after the verdict was announced.
   委员会未作解释就拒绝提案的决定，在裁决宣布后很久仍令研究人员愤愤不平。

2. It **rankled** the senior faculty that newly hired professors received substantially higher salaries with far fewer years of experience.
   新聘教授以少得多的工作经验获得明显更高的薪水，这让资深教员深感不满。

3. The perceived injustice of the court ruling **rankled** the entire community, eventually becoming a catalyst for widespread civic reform.
   法院裁决的不公正感激怒了整个社区，最终成为广泛公民改革的催化剂。

---

Think of "rankle" as "rank + ulcer" — a rank, festering ulcer that keeps irritating you and never fully heals.
把"rankle"想成"rank + ulcer"——一个恶臭的、溃烂的伤口，持续刺激你，永远无法完全愈合。
