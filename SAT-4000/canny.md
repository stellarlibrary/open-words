# 🧠 canny

🔊 /ˈkæni/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing shrewdness and good judgment, especially in money or business matters; cautious and prudent.
**中文：** adj. 精明的；谨慎的；节约的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shrewd（精明的）
- astute（机敏的）
- prudent（谨慎的）
- sagacious（睿智的）
- judicious（明智的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **canny investor / canny businessman / canny decision / canny observer / canny negotiator**

👉 Example: The canny investor diversified her portfolio well before the market downturn began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **canny** entrepreneur recognized the emerging market trend months before her competitors and positioned her company accordingly.
   那位精明的企业家比竞争对手早数月发现了新兴市场趋势，并据此调整了公司的定位。

2. His **canny** negotiation tactics allowed the small firm to secure a remarkably favorable contract with the multinational corporation.
   他精明的谈判策略使这家小公司与那家跨国公司签订了一份极为有利的合同。

3. Political analysts praised the senator's **canny** ability to build coalitions across ideological lines during the legislative session.
   政治分析人士称赞那位参议员在立法会议期间跨越意识形态界限建立联盟的精明能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "canny" as "can + any" — a canny person CAN handle ANY situation wisely because they are shrewd and cautious in their judgments.
把"canny"想成"can（能）+ any（任何）"——一个精明的人能够明智地处理任何情况，因为他们在判断中既精明又谨慎。
