# ⚖️ equality

🔊 /ɪˈkwɒləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being equal in status, rights, or opportunities; a mathematical statement expressing equivalence
**中文：** n. 平等；相等；等式

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- parity（平等）
- equivalence（等值）
- fairness（公正）
- uniformity（一致性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equality of opportunity / gender equality / racial equality / promote equality**

👉 Example: The new legislation was designed to promote equality of opportunity in the workplace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The civil rights movement fought tirelessly to establish **equality** under the law for all citizens regardless of race.
   民权运动不懈地为所有公民争取不分种族的法律面前人人平等。

2. Achieving true **equality** in education requires addressing systemic barriers that disproportionately affect disadvantaged communities.
   实现教育方面的真正平等需要解决对弱势群体产生不成比例影响的系统性障碍。

3. The mathematical concept of **equality** forms the foundation upon which algebraic equations and proofs are constructed.
   数学中等式的概念构成了代数方程和证明的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equality" as "equal + ity" — the state of being equal. Picture two sides of a scale perfectly balanced.
把"equality"想成"equal + ity"——平等的状态。想象天平两端完美平衡的画面。
