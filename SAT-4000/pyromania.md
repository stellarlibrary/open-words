# 🔥 pyromania

🔊 /ˌpaɪroʊˈmeɪniə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An obsessive, uncontrollable impulse to start fires; a psychological disorder characterized by a compulsive urge to set things ablaze
**中文：** n. 放火狂；纵火癖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arson compulsion（纵火冲动）
- fire obsession（纵火痴迷）
- incendiarism（纵火行为）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffer from pyromania / diagnosed with pyromania / a case of pyromania / pyromania disorder**

👉 Example: The forensic psychologist testified that the defendant suffered from pyromania and required treatment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The psychiatrist diagnosed the patient with **pyromania** after documenting a persistent pattern of deliberate fire-setting behavior.
   精神科医生在记录了持续的故意纵火行为模式后，将该患者诊断为放火狂。

2. Researchers studying **pyromania** emphasize that it is a rare impulse-control disorder distinct from criminal arson motivated by revenge.
   研究放火癖的研究人员强调，它是一种罕见的冲动控制障碍，不同于出于报复动机的犯罪纵火。

3. The defense attorney argued that **pyromania** diminished the accused's capacity to control his actions at the time of the incident.
   辩护律师辩称，放火癖削弱了被告在事件发生时控制自己行为的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pyro + mania" — "pyro" means fire (as in pyrotechnics) and "mania" means obsession, so pyromania is an obsessive compulsion to start fires.
把"pyromania"想成"pyro（火）+ mania（狂热）"——"pyro"意为火（如烟火技术），"mania"意为痴迷，所以pyromania就是对放火的强迫性冲动。
