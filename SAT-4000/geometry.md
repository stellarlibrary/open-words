# 📐 geometry

🔊 /dʒiˈɒmətri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of mathematics concerned with the properties, measurement, and relationships of points, lines, angles, surfaces, and solids
**中文：** n. 几何学；几何形状

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mathematics（数学）
- spatial analysis（空间分析）
- trigonometry（三角学）
- topology（拓扑学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Euclidean geometry / the geometry of something / study geometry / plane geometry**

👉 Example: The architect relied on precise geometry to design the building's complex facade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students must master basic **geometry** before advancing to more sophisticated branches of higher mathematics.
   学生在进入更高深的数学分支之前，必须掌握基础几何学。

2. The intricate **geometry** of the crystal structure revealed important information about the compound's molecular properties.
   晶体结构的复杂几何形状揭示了该化合物分子特性的重要信息。

3. Renaissance painters employed principles of **geometry** to achieve realistic perspective and spatial depth in their compositions.
   文艺复兴时期的画家运用几何学原理在作品中实现了逼真的透视和空间深度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "geo-metry" as "geo" (earth) + "metry" (measure) — it literally means "earth measurement," which is how ancient Greeks measured land.
把"geometry"想成"geo"（地球）+"metry"（测量）——字面意思是"测量大地"，这正是古希腊人丈量土地的方式。
