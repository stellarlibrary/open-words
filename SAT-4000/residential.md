# 🏘️ residential

🔊 /ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or designed for people to live in; suitable for or occupied by private houses
**中文：** adj. 住宅的，适合居住的，做住家用的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- domestic（家庭的）
- suburban（郊区的）
- dwelling（居住的）
- inhabited（有人居住的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **residential area / residential neighborhood / residential property / residential district**

👉 Example: The city council approved the conversion of the commercial zone into a residential district for young families.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Local zoning regulations strictly prohibit industrial development within **residential** neighborhoods to preserve quality of life for inhabitants.
   当地分区法规严格禁止在住宅区内进行工业开发，以保护居民的生活质量。

2. The rapid expansion of **residential** construction in suburban areas has raised concerns about environmental sustainability and resource depletion.
   郊区住宅建设的快速扩张引发了人们对环境可持续性和资源枯竭的担忧。

3. Architects proposed an innovative **residential** complex that integrates green spaces, renewable energy systems, and communal gathering areas.
   建筑师提出了一个创新的住宅综合体方案，融合了绿色空间、可再生能源系统和公共聚集区域。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "residential" as "resident-ial" — anything related to where residents live, a residential place is where people reside.
把"residential"想成"resident-ial"——任何与居民居住地有关的，住宅区就是人们居住的地方。
