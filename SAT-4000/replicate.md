# 🔬 replicate

🔊 /ˈrɛplɪkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make an exact copy of; to reproduce or duplicate. (adj.) Folded back on itself; copied exactly
**中文：** v. 复制；折叠 adj. 复制的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- duplicate（复制）
- reproduce（再现）
- copy（拷贝）
- imitate（模仿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **replicate results / replicate an experiment / replicate a process / replicate findings**

👉 Example: Scientists must be able to replicate experimental results before they can be accepted as valid.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team attempted to **replicate** the groundbreaking findings published by their colleagues at the European laboratory.
   研究团队试图复制其欧洲实验室同事发表的突破性发现。

2. Modern technology enables manufacturers to **replicate** intricate designs with remarkable precision and minimal cost.
   现代技术使制造商能够以惊人的精度和极低的成本复制复杂的设计。

3. No subsequent study has managed to **replicate** the original results, casting serious doubt on the initial conclusions.
   此后没有任何研究能够复制原始结果，这对最初的结论产生了严重质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-plic-ate" as "re" (again) + "plic" (fold, from Latin plicare) — folding something again to make an identical copy.
把"replicate"想成"re"（再次）+"plic"（折叠）——再次折叠，制造一份完全相同的副本。
