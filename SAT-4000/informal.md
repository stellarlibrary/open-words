# 🤝 informal

🔊 /ɪnˈfɔːrməl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not formal; relaxed and friendly in tone or style; not following official rules or conventions
**中文：** adj. 非正式的；不拘礼节的；随便的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- casual（随意的）
- relaxed（轻松的）
- unofficial（非官方的）
- colloquial（口语的）
- easygoing（随和的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **informal meeting / informal tone / informal setting / informal language / informal attire**

👉 Example: The professor held an informal discussion with students after the lecture to address their concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador hosted an **informal** gathering to foster dialogue between diplomats from the two nations outside official negotiations.
   大使举办了一次非正式聚会，以促进两国外交官在正式谈判之外的对话。

2. Academic papers require formal language, so students must learn to distinguish between **informal** and scholarly writing conventions.
   学术论文需要使用正式语言，因此学生必须学会区分非正式和学术写作规范。

3. The committee reached an **informal** agreement during the recess, which was later ratified through the proper legislative channels.
   委员会在休会期间达成了一项非正式协议，随后通过适当的立法渠道予以批准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-" (not) + "formal" — literally "not formal," like wearing jeans instead of a suit to a meeting.
把"informal"想成"in-（不）" + "formal（正式的）"——字面意思是"不正式的"，就像穿牛仔裤而不是西装去开会。
