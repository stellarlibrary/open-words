# 📦 cache

🔊 /kæʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A hidden store of provisions or valuables; a high-speed data storage mechanism in computing; (v.) to hide or store away secretly
**中文：** n. 隐藏处所；贮藏物；高速缓冲存储器 v. 隐藏；窖藏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hoard（贮藏）
- stash（隐藏物）
- stockpile（储备）
- store（储存）
- conceal（隐藏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a cache of weapons / cache of supplies / hidden cache / clear the cache**

👉 Example: Authorities discovered a hidden cache of stolen artifacts beneath the abandoned warehouse floor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists uncovered a remarkable **cache** of ancient gold coins buried beneath the ruins of a medieval settlement.
   考古学家在一处中世纪聚落遗址下发现了一批埋藏的珍贵古代金币。

2. Investigators discovered a **cache** of classified documents that had been concealed within the diplomat's private residence for years.
   调查人员发现了一批机密文件，这些文件多年来一直藏在该外交官的私人住所内。

3. The survival expedition leader instructed the team to **cache** emergency provisions along the planned route through the mountain pass.
   探险队长指示团队沿着计划穿越山口的路线隐藏储存应急物资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cache" sounding like "cash" — you hide your cash in a secret place, just like a cache is a hidden store of valuable things.
想想"cache"听起来像"cash"（现金）——你把现金藏在秘密的地方，就像cache是贵重物品的隐藏储存处一样。
