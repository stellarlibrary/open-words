# 🧬 anaerobic respiration

🔊 /ˌænəˈroʊbɪk ˌrɛspɪˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A type of cellular respiration that occurs without oxygen, producing energy through the breakdown of glucose via fermentation or other metabolic pathways
**中文：** 无氧呼吸；厌氧呼吸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fermentation（发酵）
- anaerobic metabolism（厌氧代谢）
- oxygen-free respiration（无氧呼吸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undergo anaerobic respiration / anaerobic respiration occurs / process of anaerobic respiration / anaerobic respiration in muscles**

👉 Example: During intense exercise, muscle cells switch to anaerobic respiration when oxygen supply becomes insufficient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Yeast cells perform **anaerobic respiration** during the fermentation process, converting glucose into ethanol and carbon dioxide efficiently.
   酵母细胞在发酵过程中进行无氧呼吸，将葡萄糖高效地转化为乙醇和二氧化碳。

2. The biology professor explained that **anaerobic respiration** produces significantly less ATP than its aerobic counterpart per glucose molecule.
   生物学教授解释说，无氧呼吸每分子葡萄糖产生的ATP远少于有氧呼吸。

3. Certain bacteria thrive in deep-sea environments by relying exclusively on **anaerobic respiration** to generate their cellular energy.
   某些细菌通过完全依赖厌氧呼吸来产生细胞能量，在深海环境中茁壮生长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "an- (without) + aerobic (oxygen) + respiration (breathing)" — it is literally breathing without oxygen at the cellular level.
拆分记忆："an-（无）+ aerobic（有氧的）+ respiration（呼吸）"——字面意思就是细胞层面的无氧呼吸。
