# 📺 advertiser

🔊 /ˈæd.vər.taɪ.zər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person or company that pays to promote products, services, or ideas through advertisements.
**中文：** 广告客户；刊登广告的人；广告商

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- promoter（推广者）
- marketer（营销者）
- sponsor（赞助商）
- publicist（宣传人员）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **major / online / digital advertiser; advertiser + spending / revenue / demand**

👉 Example: The major advertiser allocated a substantial budget to digital campaigns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **advertiser** launched a multimillion-dollar campaign to promote its new product line nationwide.
   该广告客户发起了一场数百万美元的全国性新产品推广活动。

2. Online **advertisers** increasingly rely on data analytics to target specific consumer demographics.
   在线广告商越来越依赖数据分析来锁定特定的消费群体。

3. Regulatory agencies require every **advertiser** to substantiate claims made in commercial promotions.
   监管机构要求每个广告客户对商业推广中的宣传内容提供证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Advertiser" = "advertise" + "-er" (person who does). The one who advertises is the advertiser.
联想记忆：advertiser = advertise（做广告）+ -er（做……的人），即"做广告的人"。
