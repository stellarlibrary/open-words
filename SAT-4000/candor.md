# 🪞 candor

🔊 /ˈkændər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being open, honest, and sincere in speech or expression; frankness and straightforwardness.
**中文：** n. 坦白；直率；坦诚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frankness（坦率）
- honesty（诚实）
- forthrightness（直率）
- sincerity（真诚）
- openness（开放）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with candor / refreshing candor / intellectual candor / speak with candor / appreciate someone's candor**

👉 Example: The professor was known for his intellectual candor when discussing controversial theories in class.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The executive's **candor** during the press conference earned widespread respect from journalists and shareholders alike.
   那位高管在新闻发布会上的坦诚赢得了记者和股东们的广泛尊敬。

2. Her memoir is celebrated for its remarkable **candor** in revealing the personal sacrifices behind her professional achievements.
   她的回忆录因坦率揭示其职业成就背后的个人牺牲而广受赞誉。

3. The committee valued the witness's **candor**, as her forthright testimony proved essential to establishing the facts of the case.
   委员会重视证人的坦白，因为她直率的证词对确定案件事实至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "candor" as sharing the root "cand-" with "candle" and "candid" — all related to light and clarity, because candor means shining the light of truth without concealment.
把"candor"想成与"candle（蜡烛）"和"candid（坦率的）"共享词根"cand-"——都与光明和清晰有关，因为坦白意味着毫不隐瞒地照亮真相。
