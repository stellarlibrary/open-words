# 🌅 nostalgia

🔊 /nɒˈstældʒə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sentimental longing or wistful affection for a period in the past; homesickness
**中文：** n. 乡愁；怀旧之情；怀乡病；思家病；向往过去

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- homesickness（思乡）
- longing（渴望）
- wistfulness（惆怅）
- reminiscence（回忆）
- sentimentality（多愁善感）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel nostalgia / a wave of nostalgia / nostalgia for something / overcome with nostalgia**

👉 Example: Looking at old photographs filled her with a deep nostalgia for her childhood summers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary evoked a strong sense of **nostalgia** among viewers who remembered the cultural movements of that era.
   这部纪录片在那些记得那个时代文化运动的观众中唤起了强烈的怀旧之情。

2. Many immigrants experience intense **nostalgia** for their homeland during the first years of living in a foreign country.
   许多移民在旅居国外的头几年会对故乡产生强烈的思乡之情。

3. The professor argued that **nostalgia** can serve as a psychological mechanism for maintaining emotional continuity with the past.
   这位教授认为怀旧可以作为一种心理机制来维持与过去的情感连续性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nost-algia" — "nost" sounds like "lost" and "algia" means pain, so nostalgia is the pain of something lost in the past.
把"nostalgia"想成"nost(失去的) + algia(痛)"——怀旧就是对失去的过去感到的隐痛。
