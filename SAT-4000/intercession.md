# 🙏 intercession

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈsɛʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of intervening or mediating on behalf of another; a plea or prayer offered for someone else
**中文：** n. 调解；说情；代为求情；仲裁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mediation（调解）
- intervention（干预）
- arbitration（仲裁）
- advocacy（倡导）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **divine intercession / intercession on behalf of / make an intercession / intercession of a third party**

👉 Example: The prisoner's family sought the intercession of a prominent lawyer to appeal the harsh sentence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Through the timely **intercession** of the neutral ambassador, the two warring factions agreed to a ceasefire and began formal negotiations.
   通过中立大使的及时调解，交战双方同意停火并开始正式谈判。

2. The **intercession** of experienced mediators proved essential in resolving the prolonged labor dispute between the union and management.
   经验丰富的调解员的介入对解决工会与管理层之间旷日持久的劳资纠纷至关重要。

3. Many historical accounts describe the **intercession** of religious leaders who pleaded with rulers to show mercy toward the condemned.
   许多历史记载描述了宗教领袖为被判刑者向统治者求情的事迹。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-cess-ion" — "inter" means between, and "cess" relates to going (from Latin "cessio"), so intercession is the act of going between parties to plead or mediate.
把"intercession"想成"inter(之间) + cession(走动)"——在各方之间走动，即代为说情、调解。
