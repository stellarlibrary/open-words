# 💎 jade

🔊 /dʒeɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A hard, typically green gemstone; an old or worn-out horse. (adj.) Made of jade. (v.) To become weary or exhausted
**中文：** n. 翡翠；碧玉；老马 adj. 玉制的 v. 疲倦

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nephrite（软玉）
- jadeite（翡翠）
- fatigue（疲倦）
- exhaust（使疲惫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jade bracelet / jade carving / jade green / jaded by experience**

👉 Example: The museum displayed an exquisite jade figurine dating back to the Han Dynasty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists discovered an intricately carved **jade** pendant buried within the ancient tomb alongside other ceremonial artifacts.
   考古学家在古墓中发现了一枚精雕细琢的玉石吊坠，旁边还有其他礼仪器物。

2. In traditional Chinese culture, **jade** symbolizes virtue, grace, and moral integrity, making it highly prized among collectors.
   在中国传统文化中，玉象征着美德、优雅和道德操守，因此深受收藏家珍视。

3. After years of relentless campaigning, the long hours and constant travel began to **jade** even the most enthusiastic volunteers.
   经过多年不懈的竞选活动，漫长的工作时间和不断的奔波开始令即使是最热情的志愿者也感到疲倦。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jade" as a precious green stone — and just as overuse can wear down even the hardest stone, it also means to become worn out or tired.
把"jade"想成一块珍贵的绿色宝石——正如过度使用可以磨损最坚硬的石头，它也意味着变得疲倦不堪。
