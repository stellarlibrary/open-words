# 🎓 collegian

🔊 /kəˈliːdʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A student or recent graduate of a college or university
**中文：** n. 大学生；学院的学生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- undergraduate（本科生）
- university student（大学生）
- scholar（学者）
- student（学生）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **young collegian / fellow collegian / ambitious collegian / a group of collegians**

👉 Example: As a dedicated collegian, she balanced her academic coursework with meaningful extracurricular activities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious **collegian** secured a prestigious internship at a leading investment bank during her sophomore summer break.
   这位雄心勃勃的大学生在大二暑假期间获得了一家顶尖投行的名额有限的实习机会。

2. Many **collegians** today face the difficult challenge of managing substantial student loan debt while pursuing their academic goals.
   如今许多大学生面临着在追求学业目标的同时管理大量学生贷款债务的艰难挑战。

3. The campus newspaper, written entirely by **collegians**, won a national award for excellence in investigative journalism.
   这份完全由大学生撰写的校园报纸获得了调查新闻卓越成就的全国性奖项。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "college" + "-ian" (a person) — a collegian is simply a person who attends college.
把"collegian"想成"college"（大学）加上"-ian"（表示人的后缀）——collegian就是上大学的人。
