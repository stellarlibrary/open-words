# 🥗 vegetarian

🔊 /ˌvɛdʒɪˈtɛəriən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who does not eat meat or fish. (adj.) Relating to or suitable for vegetarians; consisting of only plant-based foods
**中文：** n. 素食者 adj. 素食的；素食主义的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- herbivore（食草者）
- plant-based（植物性的）
- meatless（无肉的）
- vegan（纯素食者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vegetarian diet / vegetarian restaurant / strict vegetarian / vegetarian option**

👉 Example: The conference organizers ensured that vegetarian meals were available for all attendees who requested them.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A growing body of research suggests that a well-balanced **vegetarian** diet can provide all essential nutrients required for optimal health.
   越来越多的研究表明，均衡的素食饮食可以提供最佳健康所需的所有必需营养素。

2. The university dining hall expanded its **vegetarian** menu in response to increasing student demand for plant-based meal alternatives.
   大学食堂扩大了素食菜单，以回应学生对植物性餐食替代品日益增长的需求。

3. She became a committed **vegetarian** after studying the environmental impact of industrial livestock production on global ecosystems.
   在研究了工业化畜牧业对全球生态系统的环境影响后，她成为了一名坚定的素食者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vegetar-ian" as "a person (-ian) who eats only vegetables" — the suffix "-ian" marks someone who follows a practice.
把"vegetarian"想成"一个只吃蔬菜的人（-ian）"——后缀"-ian"表示遵循某种做法的人。
