# 😌 sedate

🔊 /sɪˈdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Calm; composed. (v.) To administer a sedative to
**中文：** adj. 安静的；稳重的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- placid（平静的）
- tranquil（安宁的）
- composed（沉着的）
- dignified（庄重的）
- unruffled（冷静的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sedate pace / sedate manner / sedate the patient / sedate atmosphere**

👉 Example: The physician decided to sedate the patient before performing the uncomfortable diagnostic procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The elderly professor maintained a **sedate** demeanor throughout the heated debate, never raising his voice or losing composure.
   这位年长的教授在激烈的辩论中始终保持沉着的态度，从未提高嗓门或失去镇定。

2. Veterinarians were required to **sedate** the injured animal before they could safely examine and treat its wounds.
   兽医需要先给受伤的动物注射镇静剂，然后才能安全地检查和治疗伤口。

3. The village offered a **sedate** lifestyle that appealed to those weary of the relentless pace of urban existence.
   这个村庄提供了一种安静的生活方式，吸引了那些厌倦城市生活无情节奏的人。
