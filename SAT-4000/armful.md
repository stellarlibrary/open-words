# 🤗 armful

🔊 /ˈɑːrmfʊl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** As much as a person's arm or arms can hold; a large quantity carried in one's arms
**中文：** 一抱之量；满怀；一臂之量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- handful（一把）
- bundle（一捆）
- load（一堆）
- heap（大量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an armful of flowers / an armful of books / carry an armful / armfuls of**

👉 Example: She arrived at the party carrying an armful of beautifully wrapped gifts for the host.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The librarian struggled to carry an **armful** of dusty reference volumes from the storage room to the main reading hall.
   图书管理员费力地将一**抱**满是灰尘的参考书从储藏室搬到主阅览大厅。

2. He gathered an **armful** of freshly picked wildflowers from the meadow to decorate the dining table for the celebration.
   他从草地上采了满满一**怀**新鲜的野花，用来装饰庆典的餐桌。

3. The child ran toward her grandmother with an **armful** of handmade drawings, eager to display her latest artistic creations.
   孩子抱着满满一**怀**手绘画跑向祖母，急切地想展示她最新的艺术作品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "armful" as "arm + full" — your arms are completely full, holding as much as they possibly can.
把"armful"想成"arm + full"——你的手臂完全满了，抱着尽可能多的东西。
