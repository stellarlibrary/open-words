# 🔥 ardent

🔊 /ˈɑːrdənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing strong feelings of enthusiasm, passion, or devotion; intensely eager or zealous
**中文：** adj. 热情的；热心的；激烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- passionate（热情的）
- fervent（热烈的）
- zealous（热心的）
- enthusiastic（热忱的）
- impassioned（充满激情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ardent supporter / ardent admirer / ardent desire / ardent advocate / ardent believer**

👉 Example: She has been an ardent supporter of environmental conservation throughout her entire career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator was an **ardent** advocate for educational reform and tirelessly campaigned for increased funding to public schools.
   这位参议员是教育改革的热心倡导者，不知疲倦地为增加公立学校经费而奔走。

2. His **ardent** devotion to scientific research inspired an entire generation of young scholars to pursue academic careers.
   他对科学研究的热情投入激励了整整一代年轻学者投身学术事业。

3. The **ardent** fans waited outside the concert hall for hours despite the freezing rain and bitter cold.
   狂热的粉丝们不顾冰冷的雨和刺骨的寒风，在音乐厅外等了数小时。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ard-ent" — it sounds like "ard" from "ardor" (burning heat). Someone ardent burns with passion, like a fire that cannot be extinguished.
把"ardent"和"ardor（热情）"联系起来——"ard"来自拉丁语"燃烧"，一个ardent的人内心燃烧着无法熄灭的热情之火。
