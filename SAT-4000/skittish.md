# 🐴 skittish

🔊 /ˈskɪtɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easily frightened or nervous; excitable and unpredictable in behavior
**中文：** adj. 易惊的，羞怯的，胆小的；多变的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nervous（紧张的）
- jittery（神经过敏的）
- restive（不安的）
- jumpy（易受惊的）
- flighty（轻浮多变的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **skittish investors / skittish horse / skittish about / become skittish**

👉 Example: The skittish horse bolted at the sudden crack of thunder during the afternoon ride.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Skittish** investors rapidly withdrew their capital from emerging markets following the unexpected announcement of new tariff regulations.
   易受惊的投资者在意外宣布新关税法规后迅速从新兴市场撤出资金。

2. The rescue dog remained **skittish** around strangers for months, a lasting consequence of its prolonged exposure to neglect and abuse.
   这只被救助的狗几个月来在陌生人周围仍然胆怯不安，这是长期遭受忽视和虐待的持久后果。

3. Analysts noted that consumers had grown increasingly **skittish** about major purchases amid rising inflation and economic uncertainty.
   分析师指出，在通货膨胀上升和经济不确定性加剧的情况下，消费者对大额购买越来越谨慎不安。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "skit-tish" as "skit + ish" — like a small skit or sudden movement that startles; a skittish creature reacts to the slightest skit of motion.
把"skittish"想成"skit + ish"——像一个突然的小动作让人吃惊；一个胆小的生物会对最轻微的动静做出反应。
