# ⚔️ dissension

🔊 /dɪˈsɛnʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Strong disagreement or discord, especially within a group; conflict arising from differing opinions
**中文：** n. 纠纷；意见分歧；冲突

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- discord（不和）
- conflict（冲突）
- disagreement（分歧）
- strife（纷争）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sow dissension / cause dissension / internal dissension / dissension among members**

👉 Example: Internal dissension among party members threatened to derail the entire legislative agenda.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Growing **dissension** within the board of directors ultimately led to the resignation of the chief executive officer.
   董事会内部日益加剧的分歧最终导致了首席执行官的辞职。

2. The controversial policy created widespread **dissension** among faculty members who held fundamentally opposing views on academic reform.
   这项有争议的政策在持有根本对立学术改革观点的教职员工中引发了广泛的纷争。

3. Historians argue that **dissension** among the allied generals significantly contributed to their defeat in the decisive campaign.
   历史学家认为，盟军将领之间的意见不合对他们在这场决定性战役中的失败起了重大作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-sens-ion" as people having different "senses" or opinions — leading to tension and conflict.
把"dissension"想成人们有不同的"senses"（感受/意见）——导致紧张和冲突。
