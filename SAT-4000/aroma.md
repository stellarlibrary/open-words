# 🌿 aroma

🔊 /əˈroʊmə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A pleasant, distinctive smell, especially one associated with food, drink, or spices
**中文：** n. 芳香，香气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fragrance（芬芳）
- scent（香味）
- bouquet（芳香）
- perfume（香气）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aroma of coffee / rich aroma / delightful aroma / aroma therapy**

👉 Example: The aroma of freshly baked bread filled the entire kitchen with warmth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rich **aroma** of roasted coffee beans permeated the cafe and attracted passersby from the busy street.
   烘焙咖啡豆的浓郁香气弥漫整个咖啡馆，吸引了繁忙街道上的路人。

2. Connoisseurs appreciate the complex **aroma** of aged wine, which reveals subtle notes of oak and dark fruit.
   鉴赏家们欣赏陈年葡萄酒的复杂香气，其中透出橡木和黑色水果的微妙气息。

3. The distinctive **aroma** of lavender has long been valued for its calming and therapeutic properties.
   薰衣草独特的芳香因其镇静和治疗功效而长期受到人们的推崇。

---

把"aroma"想成"a Roma"——想象你去罗马旅行，到处都能闻到意大利美食的芳香。
