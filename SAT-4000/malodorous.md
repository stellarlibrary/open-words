# 🤢 malodorous

🔊 /ˌmælˈoʊdərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a very unpleasant smell; morally offensive or deeply repugnant
**中文：** adj. 有恶臭的；令人极为反感的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fetid（恶臭的）
- putrid（腐臭的）
- stinking（发臭的）
- noisome（令人作呕的）
- rank（难闻的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **malodorous odor / malodorous substance / malodorous swamp / malodorous reputation**

👉 Example: The malodorous fumes from the chemical plant prompted residents to file formal complaints.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inspector discovered a **malodorous** substance leaking from the abandoned industrial facility near the riverbank.
   检查员发现一种恶臭的物质正从河岸附近废弃的工业设施中泄漏出来。

2. Critics described the politician's **malodorous** dealings as a profound betrayal of the public trust he once enjoyed.
   批评人士将这位政客令人反感的交易描述为对他曾享有的公众信任的严重背叛。

3. The **malodorous** garbage that had accumulated in the alley created an unbearable environment for nearby shop owners.
   小巷中堆积的恶臭垃圾给附近的店主们造成了难以忍受的环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "mal-" means bad (as in malfunction) + "odorous" means having an odor — so malodorous literally means "having a bad odor."
拆解记忆："mal-"表示坏的（如malfunction故障）+ "odorous"表示有气味的——所以malodorous字面意思就是"有坏气味的"。
