# 🏆 contest

🔊 /ˈkɒntɛst (n.), kənˈtɛst (v.)/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To dispute, challenge, or argue against; to compete for something. (n.) A competition or struggle for superiority
**中文：** v. 争辩；质疑；竞赛 n. 竞赛；争夺；比赛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- competition（竞赛）
- dispute（争论）
- challenge（挑战）
- tournament（锦标赛）
- contention（争夺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contest a decision / contest an election / enter a contest / a close contest / contest the validity of**

👉 Example: The defendant's legal team plans to contest the ruling in a higher appellate court.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The losing candidate chose to **contest** the election results, citing numerous irregularities in the ballot counting process.
   落选的候选人选择质疑选举结果，指出计票过程中存在大量违规行为。

2. The international science **contest** attracted over five hundred participants from forty-seven countries across six continents.
   这项国际科学竞赛吸引了来自六大洲四十七个国家的五百多名参赛者。

3. Several legal scholars have publicly **contested** the constitutional validity of the newly enacted surveillance legislation.
   几位法学学者公开质疑了新颁布的监控立法的合宪性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-TEST" — a "test" done "con" (together with) others, like a competition where everyone is being tested at once.
把"contest"想成"一起（con）考试（test）"——大家一起参加考验，就是竞赛和争夺。
