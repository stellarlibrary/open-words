# 🧬 macrophage

🔊 /ˈmækrəˌfeɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large white blood cell that engulfs and digests foreign particles, bacteria, and dead cells as part of the immune response.
**中文：** n. 巨噬细胞（一种吞噬并消化外来颗粒、细菌和死细胞的大型白细胞）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- phagocyte（吞噬细胞）
- white blood cell（白细胞）
- immune cell（免疫细胞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **macrophage activation / macrophage response / tissue macrophage / macrophage function**

👉 Example: The macrophage engulfed the invading bacteria before they could spread to surrounding tissues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **macrophage** plays a critical role in the body's innate immune defense against bacterial infections.
   巨噬细胞在机体对抗细菌感染的先天免疫防御中发挥着关键作用。

2. Researchers observed that the **macrophage** rapidly engulfed the foreign particles introduced into the tissue sample.
   研究人员观察到巨噬细胞迅速吞噬了引入组织样本中的外来颗粒。

3. Chronic inflammation occurs when **macrophages** continuously release signaling molecules in response to persistent tissue damage.
   当巨噬细胞对持续性组织损伤不断释放信号分子时，就会发生慢性炎症。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "macro" (large) + "phage" (eater) — a macrophage is literally a "big eater" that devours harmful invaders in your body.
把"macrophage"拆成"macro"（大的）+"phage"（吃的）——巨噬细胞就是一个"大胃王"，专门吞噬体内的有害入侵者。
