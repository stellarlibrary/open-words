# 🤝 complaisance

🔊 /kəmˈpleɪzəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A disposition to please or comply; courteous willingness to oblige others; polite agreeableness
**中文：** n. 殷勤；彬彬有礼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- courtesy（礼貌）
- obligingness（乐于助人）
- deference（恭敬）
- affability（和蔼）
- agreeableness（随和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show complaisance / with complaisance / an air of complaisance / excessive complaisance**

👉 Example: The ambassador's natural complaisance made diplomatic negotiations considerably smoother.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her unfailing **complaisance** toward guests earned her a sterling reputation as the most gracious hostess in the district.
   她对客人始终如一的殷勤为她赢得了该地区最优雅女主人的美誉。

2. The courtier's **complaisance** concealed a shrewd political mind that carefully calculated every gesture of apparent generosity.
   这位朝臣的彬彬有礼掩盖了一个精明的政治头脑，他精心计算着每一个看似慷慨的举动。

3. Critics argued that excessive **complaisance** toward authority figures could undermine the independence of the judicial system entirely.
   批评者认为，对权威人物的过度殷勤可能会完全破坏司法系统的独立性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-PLAIS-ance" as "please-ance" — the quality of being eager to please others with politeness and courtesy.
把"complaisance"想成"please-ance"——渴望以礼貌和殷勤取悦他人的品质。
