# 🏗️ base

🔊 /beɪs/<br>
🏷️ *n. & adj. & v. | 名词 & 形容词 & 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) The bottom support or foundation of something; (adj.) morally low, without principles, or despicable; (v.) to use as a foundation or starting point for something.

**中文：** (n.) 基础；底部；(adj.) 卑鄙的；低劣的；(v.) 以……作基础。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- foundation （基础） [n.]
- despicable （卑鄙的） [adj.]
- ignoble （不光彩的） [adj.]
- ground （根据；以……为基础） [v.]

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

As an adjective meaning "morally low," "base" is a formal and somewhat literary word, often found in older texts. Do not confuse the adjective "base" (despicable) with "bass" (low-pitched sound). The verb form is commonly used in the phrase "based on" or "based upon," meaning founded or established upon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect designed a reinforced concrete **base** to support the towering steel structure above.
   中文：建筑师设计了一个钢筋混凝土底座来支撑上方高耸的钢结构。

2. The committee condemned his **base** betrayal of the trust placed in him by the public.
   中文：委员会谴责了他对公众信任的卑鄙背叛。

3. The researchers decided to **base** their conclusions on data collected from controlled experiments.
   中文：研究人员决定以受控实验收集的数据作为他们结论的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "base" as the "bottom" — both literally (the base of a building) and figuratively (base behavior = the lowest form of conduct). A person with base motives has sunk to the bottom of morality.

联想记忆：把"base"想成"底部"——既是字面意义（建筑的底部），也是比喻意义（base behavior = 最低劣的行为）。一个动机卑鄙的人沉到了道德的最底层。
