# 🏅 proficient

🔊 /prəˈfɪʃənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Highly skilled or competent through training or practice. (n.) An expert or skilled person
**中文：** adj. 熟练的，精通的 n. 精通者；专家，能手

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- skilled（熟练的）
- adept（内行的）
- competent（胜任的）
- accomplished（有造诣的）
- expert（专家级的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proficient in / proficient at / proficient with / become proficient / highly proficient**

👉 Example: She became proficient in Python programming after completing an intensive twelve-week course.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new recruits must become **proficient** in emergency procedures before they are permitted to work independently in the field.
   新员工必须在紧急程序方面变得熟练，才被允许独立在现场工作。

2. A **proficient** writer can convey complex arguments with clarity and precision, making sophisticated ideas accessible to all readers.
   一位精通写作的人能够清晰准确地传达复杂论点，使深奥的思想让所有读者都能理解。

3. After years of immersive study abroad, he became remarkably **proficient** in both spoken and written Mandarin Chinese.
   经过多年的沉浸式海外学习，他在中文口语和书面语方面都变得非常熟练。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proficient" as "pro-fish-ent" — imagine a professional fisherman who is so skilled he catches fish effortlessly every time.
把"proficient"想成"pro（专业的）+ fish + ent"——想象一位专业渔夫，技术如此熟练，每次都能轻松捕到鱼。
