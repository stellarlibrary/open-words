# 🌳 botany

🔊 /ˈbɒtəni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The scientific study of plants, including their structure, growth, classification, and distribution; the plant life of a particular region
**中文：** n. 植物学；地区植物总称

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plant science（植物科学）
- phytology（植物学）
- plant biology（植物生物学）
- herbalism（草本学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study botany / professor of botany / department of botany / marine botany**

👉 Example: She decided to pursue a degree in botany after developing a fascination with tropical plant ecosystems.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's department of **botany** has produced groundbreaking research on plant genetics and sustainable agricultural practices over the decades.
   该大学植物学系数十年来在植物遗传学和可持续农业实践方面产出了开创性研究。

2. A thorough understanding of **botany** is essential for conservation biologists working to preserve endangered plant species worldwide.
   对植物学的透彻理解对于致力于保护全球濒危植物物种的保护生物学家来说至关重要。

3. The **botany** of the Mediterranean region is remarkably diverse, encompassing thousands of endemic species adapted to its unique climate.
   地中海地区的植物种类极为丰富，涵盖了数千种适应其独特气候的特有物种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "botany" coming from the Greek "botane" meaning "plant" — botany is simply the study of all things plant-related.
把"botany"想成源自希腊语"botane"（意为"植物"）——植物学就是对一切与植物相关事物的研究。
