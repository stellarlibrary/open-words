# 🧹 unkempt

🔊 /ʌnˈkɛmpt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having an untidy or disheveled appearance; not properly maintained or cared for
**中文：** 蓬乱的；不整洁的；未经打理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disheveled（凌乱的）
- messy（邋遢的）
- scruffy（不整洁的）
- slovenly（邋遢的）
- tousled（蓬乱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unkempt hair / unkempt appearance / unkempt garden / unkempt beard**

👉 Example: His unkempt appearance at the formal interview immediately created an unfavorable first impression.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **unkempt** hair and wrinkled clothing belied the brilliant and meticulously organized mind that lay beneath the surface.
   教授蓬乱的头发和褶皱的衣服掩盖了其表面之下那颗出色而条理清晰的头脑。

2. The once-beautiful estate had become **unkempt** after years of neglect, with overgrown hedges and crumbling stone pathways throughout.
   这座曾经美丽的庄园在多年的疏于打理后变得杂乱不堪，到处是疯长的树篱和破碎的石头小路。

3. Job candidates should avoid presenting an **unkempt** appearance, as first impressions significantly influence hiring decisions in professional settings.
   求职者应避免仪容不整，因为在职业场合中，第一印象会显著影响招聘决定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-kempt" as "un-combed" — "kempt" comes from an old word meaning combed, so unkempt literally means uncombed or untidy.
把"unkempt"想成"un（未）+ kempt（梳理过的）"——"kempt"源自古英语，意为梳理过的，所以unkempt就是未梳理的、蓬乱的。
