# 💪 conviction

🔊 /kənˈvɪkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A firmly held belief or opinion; the formal declaration that someone is guilty of a criminal offense by the verdict of a jury or judge
**中文：** n. 定罪；确信；坚定的信仰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- belief（信念）
- certainty（确定）
- assurance（确信）
- verdict（裁决）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strong conviction / deep conviction / criminal conviction / speak with conviction / personal conviction**

👉 Example: She spoke with such conviction that the entire audience was persuaded by her argument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The civil rights leader spoke with unwavering **conviction** about the necessity of equality and justice for all citizens.
   这位民权领袖以坚定不移的信念谈论了所有公民享有平等和正义的必要性。

2. The jury's **conviction** of the defendant was based on overwhelming forensic evidence presented during the lengthy criminal trial.
   陪审团对被告的定罪是基于漫长刑事审判中提出的大量法医证据。

3. Her deep **conviction** that education transforms communities motivated her to establish scholarship programs in underserved rural areas.
   她深信教育能改变社区，这促使她在服务不足的农村地区设立了奖学金项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-vict-ion" as "completely victorious in belief" — when you have conviction, your belief has completely won over any doubt.
把"conviction"想成"completely victorious in belief"——当你有信念时，你的信仰已经完全战胜了任何疑虑。
