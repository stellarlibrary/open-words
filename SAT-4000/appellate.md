# ⚖️ appellate

🔊 /əˈpɛlɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or dealing with appeals, especially in a court of law that has the authority to review and overturn decisions of lower courts
**中文：** 上诉的；受理上诉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- judicial（司法的）
- reviewing（复审的）
- revisory（修正的）
- appellatory（上诉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **appellate court / appellate jurisdiction / appellate review / appellate judge**

👉 Example: The defense team filed a motion with the appellate court to overturn the original verdict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **appellate** court reversed the lower court's ruling after discovering significant procedural errors in the trial.
   上诉法院在发现审判中存在重大程序错误后，推翻了下级法院的裁决。

2. She was appointed as an **appellate** judge following her distinguished career in constitutional law and civil litigation.
   凭借在宪法法和民事诉讼方面的杰出职业生涯，她被任命为上诉法官。

3. The defendant exercised his right to seek **appellate** review of the harsh sentence imposed by the district court.
   被告行使了对地方法院所施加的严厉判决寻求上诉审查的权利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "appellate" as "appeal-ate" — a court that has "eaten" or taken in an appeal for further review.
把"appellate"想成"appeal-ate"——一个"吞下"或接受上诉进行进一步审查的法院。
