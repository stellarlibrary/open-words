# 🏔️ range

🔊 /reɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The extent or scope of variation; a series of mountains; a row or line. (v.) To vary within specified limits; to extend or stretch in a particular direction; to roam freely
**中文：** n. 范围；幅度；排；山脉；量程 v. 变动；平行；延伸；漫游

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scope（范围）
- spectrum（光谱）
- extent（程度）
- span（跨度）
- array（排列）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a wide range of / range from...to / within the range / mountain range**

👉 Example: The study examined a wide range of factors that contribute to academic achievement among university students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university library offers a broad **range** of academic databases, covering disciplines from molecular biology to comparative literature.
   该大学图书馆提供了广泛的学术数据库，涵盖从分子生物学到比较文学的各个学科。

2. Global temperatures are projected to **range** from two to five degrees above pre-industrial levels by the end of this century.
   预计到本世纪末，全球气温将比工业化前水平高出二至五度。

3. The snow-capped mountain **range** extended for hundreds of kilometers, forming a natural barrier between the two neighboring countries.
   白雪皑皑的山脉绵延数百公里，在两个邻国之间形成了天然屏障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "range" as a mountain range stretching far and wide — it covers a vast area, just like a range of possibilities or topics.
把"range"想成一条绵延广阔的山脉——它覆盖了广大的区域，就像一系列的可能性或话题。
