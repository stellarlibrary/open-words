# 💥 overthrow

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈθroʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To remove forcibly from power; to defeat or topple. (n.) The act of removing someone or something from power; a downfall
**中文：** v. 推翻；打倒；倾覆 n. 推翻；倾覆；瓦解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- topple（推翻）
- oust（驱逐）
- dethrone（废黜）
- subvert（颠覆）
- depose（罢免）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overthrow a government / overthrow a regime / overthrow a dictator / attempt to overthrow**

👉 Example: The revolutionary forces sought to overthrow the authoritarian regime that had ruled for decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rebel coalition successfully **overthrew** the corrupt government after a prolonged campaign of resistance and strategic military operations.
   叛军联盟在长期的抵抗运动和战略军事行动之后成功推翻了腐败政府。

2. Historians continue to debate whether the **overthrow** of the monarchy ultimately benefited the nation's long-term political development.
   历史学家们仍在争论推翻君主制是否最终有利于该国的长期政治发展。

3. The foreign intelligence agency was accused of plotting to **overthrow** the democratically elected leader through covert operations.
   该外国情报机构被指控策划通过秘密行动推翻民主选举产生的领导人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-throw" as literally "throwing someone over" — you throw the ruler over and off their throne of power.
把"overthrow"想成"over（翻过去）+throw（扔）"——把统治者从权力宝座上扔翻过去，就是推翻。
