# ⚠️ bigamist

🔊 /ˈbɪɡəmɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who commits bigamy by marrying someone while still legally married to another person
**中文：** 重婚者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- polygamist（一夫多妻者）
- two-timer（脚踏两条船的人）
- adulterer（通奸者）
- bigamy offender（重婚犯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convicted bigamist / accused bigamist / known bigamist / prosecuted as a bigamist**

👉 Example: The court convicted him as a bigamist after discovering he had married a second wife without divorcing the first.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bigamist** maintained two separate households in different cities, concealing each marriage from the other spouse.
   这个重婚者在不同城市维持着两个独立的家庭，向每位配偶隐瞒了另一段婚姻。

2. Under most modern legal systems, a **bigamist** faces serious criminal charges including fraud and violation of matrimonial law.
   在大多数现代法律体系下，重婚者面临严重的刑事指控，包括欺诈和违反婚姻法。

3. The detective discovered that the suspected **bigamist** had obtained marriage licenses in three separate jurisdictions over five years.
   侦探发现这名涉嫌重婚者在五年内在三个不同的司法管辖区获得了结婚证。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi" (two) + "gamist" (from Greek gamos, marriage) — a bigamist is someone with two marriages at the same time.
把"bigamist"拆分为"bi"（二）+"gamist"（源自希腊语gamos，婚姻）——bigamist就是同时拥有两段婚姻的人。
