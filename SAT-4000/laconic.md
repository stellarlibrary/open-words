# 🗣️ laconic

🔊 /ləˈkɒnɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Using very few words; brief and concise in speech or expression; terse
**中文：** adj. 简洁的，简明的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- terse（简洁的）
- succinct（简明的）
- concise（精炼的）
- pithy（精辟的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a laconic reply / laconic style / laconic wit / laconic remark**

👉 Example: The general was famous for his laconic responses, often conveying complex orders in just a few words.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **laconic** response to the lengthy accusation consisted of only three words, yet it silenced the entire assembly.
   这位外交官对冗长指控的简洁回应仅有三个词，却让整个会场鸦雀无声。

2. Ernest Hemingway's **laconic** prose style revolutionized modern literature by demonstrating the power of restrained expression.
   欧内斯特·海明威简洁的散文风格通过展示克制表达的力量，彻底改变了现代文学。

3. The surgeon delivered a **laconic** briefing to the anxious family, stating only that the operation had been successful.
   外科医生对焦急的家属做了简短的说明，只说手术很成功。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laconic" as coming from Laconia, the region of ancient Sparta — Spartans were famously brief in speech, using as few words as possible.
把"laconic"想成源自Laconia（拉科尼亚），即古代斯巴达所在地区——斯巴达人以言简意赅著称，用尽可能少的词说话。
