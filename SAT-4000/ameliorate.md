# 🌱 ameliorate

🔊 /əˈmiːliəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make something bad or unsatisfactory better or less severe.
**中文：** 改善；减轻；改良

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- improve（改善）
- alleviate（减轻）
- mitigate（缓和）
- enhance（提升）
- remedy（补救）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conditions, situations, suffering, problems, effects**
👉 Example: Governments should ameliorate the living conditions of impoverished communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new policy was designed to **ameliorate** the suffering of displaced refugees across the region.
   这项新政策旨在改善该地区流离失所难民的苦难。

2. Urban planners proposed several initiatives to **ameliorate** traffic congestion in the metropolitan area.
   城市规划者提出了几项举措来改善都市区的交通拥堵状况。

3. The administration introduced reforms to **ameliorate** the deteriorating public healthcare system nationwide.
   政府推行了改革，以改善全国日益恶化的公共医疗卫生体系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-MELIO-rate" — "melio" comes from Latin "melior" meaning "better," so ameliorate means to make better.
联想"ameliorate"中的"melio"来自拉丁语"melior"（更好），所以这个词的意思就是使变得更好。
