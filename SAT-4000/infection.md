# 🦠 infection

🔊 /ɪnˈfɛkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of infecting or the state of being infected; a disease caused by the invasion of microorganisms; a harmful influence that spreads
**中文：** n. 感染；传染；影响；传染病

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contagion（传染）
- contamination（污染）
- disease（疾病）
- affliction（折磨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infection spreads / bacterial infection / risk of infection / prevent infection**

👉 Example: The doctor prescribed antibiotics to treat the bacterial infection in the patient's wound.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rapid spread of **infection** throughout the hospital prompted administrators to implement strict quarantine protocols immediately.
   感染在整个医院的迅速蔓延促使管理层立即实施了严格的隔离措施。

2. Researchers identified a novel strain of viral **infection** that demonstrated unusual resistance to all conventional treatments.
   研究人员发现了一种新型病毒感染，表现出对所有常规治疗方法的异常抗药性。

3. Proper hand hygiene remains the most effective method for reducing the risk of **infection** in clinical settings.
   正确的手部卫生仍然是降低临床环境中感染风险的最有效方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-fect-ion" as something that "affects you from the inside" — a harmful agent that gets IN and aFFECTs your body.
把"infection"想成"从内部影响你的东西"——有害物质进入（in）体内并影响（fect = affect）你的身体。
