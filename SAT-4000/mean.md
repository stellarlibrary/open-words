# 🎯 mean

🔊 /miːn/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective, noun | 动词, 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To intend; to signify or indicate. (adj.) Unkind or spiteful; average; inferior in quality. (n.) The average value of a set of numbers
**中文：** v. 意味；意指；打算 adj. 卑鄙的；吝啬的；平均的；低劣的 n. 平均值；中数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- signify（意味着）
- intend（打算）
- average（平均的）
- malicious（恶意的）
- miserly（吝啬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mean to do / mean by / mean well / by all means / the golden mean**

👉 Example: The statistical mean of the data set revealed a significant upward trend over the past decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor asked the students to calculate the arithmetic **mean** of the experimental data collected throughout the semester.
   教授要求学生计算整个学期收集的实验数据的算术平均值。

2. His **mean** temperament and unwillingness to compromise made it exceedingly difficult for colleagues to collaborate with him.
   他卑劣的性情和不愿妥协的态度使同事们极难与他合作。

3. The new regulations **mean** that corporations must now disclose their environmental impact in comprehensive annual reports.
   新法规意味着企业现在必须在综合年度报告中披露其环境影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mean" as the word in the middle — it has many meanings, and "the mean" is the middle value, just as a "mean" person is one who acts below the middle standard of decency.
把"mean"想成中间的词——它有很多含义，"the mean"是中间值，而"mean"的人是行为低于基本礼仪标准的人。
