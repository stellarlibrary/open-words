# 🪟 translucent

🔊 /trænzˈluːsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Allowing light to pass through partially; semi-transparent, so that objects on the other side appear blurred or indistinct.
**中文：** adj. 半透明的；透光的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- semi-transparent（半透明的）
- see-through（透明的）
- diaphanous（极薄透明的）
- pellucid（清澈的）
- limpid（透明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **translucent glass / translucent material / translucent skin / translucent fabric / translucent surface**

👉 Example: The architect specified translucent glass panels to allow natural light while maintaining privacy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **translucent** curtains diffused the harsh afternoon sunlight, casting a soft and gentle glow throughout the entire room.
   半透明的窗帘漫射了刺眼的午后阳光，在整个房间投下柔和温暖的光芒。

2. Certain deep-sea organisms possess **translucent** bodies that render them nearly invisible to predators in the dark ocean depths.
   某些深海生物拥有半透明的身体，使它们在黑暗的海洋深处对捕食者几乎不可见。

3. The geologist examined the **translucent** mineral specimen under polarized light to determine its crystallographic properties and composition.
   地质学家在偏振光下检查了这块半透明的矿物样本，以确定其晶体学性质和成分。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "translucent" as "trans (through) + luc (light) + ent" — light passes through, but not completely, creating a semi-transparent effect.
把"translucent"想成"穿过（trans）+ 光（luc）+ ent"——光能穿过，但不完全透明，产生半透明效果。
