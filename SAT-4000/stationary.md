# 🧱 stationary

🔊 /ˈsteɪʃəneri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not moving or not intended to be moved; fixed in one place; remaining unchanged. (n.) A person or military force that remains in a fixed position
**中文：** adj. 静止的，固定的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immobile（不动的）
- fixed（固定的）
- static（静态的）
- motionless（静止的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remain stationary / stationary object / stationary position / stationary bike**

👉 Example: Do not confuse "stationary" (not moving) with "stationery" (writing materials).

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surveillance camera recorded the suspect's vehicle remaining **stationary** outside the building for approximately three hours.
   监控摄像头记录到嫌疑人的车辆在大楼外静止不动约三个小时。

2. The professor explained that a **stationary** object will not accelerate unless acted upon by an external unbalanced force.
   教授解释说，一个静止的物体除非受到外部不平衡力的作用，否则不会加速。

3. Despite rapid technological progress in neighboring cities, the town's economic development had remained largely **stationary** for a decade.
   尽管周边城市技术进步迅速，该镇的经济发展在十年间基本保持不变。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "station-ary" as "staying at a station" — something stationary stays at its station and does not move.
把"stationary"想成"station + ary"——像待在车站一样，静止不动，固定在原位。
