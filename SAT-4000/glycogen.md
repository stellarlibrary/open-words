# 🔬 glycogen

🔊 /ˈɡlaɪkədʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A polysaccharide that serves as the principal storage form of glucose in animal cells, primarily in the liver and muscles
**中文：** n. 糖原；动物淀粉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- animal starch（动物淀粉）
- stored glucose（储存葡萄糖）
- polysaccharide reserve（多糖储备）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glycogen reserves / glycogen storage / glycogen depletion / glycogen synthesis**

👉 Example: Marathon runners must carefully manage their glycogen reserves to avoid hitting the wall during a race.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During prolonged exercise, the body depletes its **glycogen** stores and begins to rely more heavily on fat oxidation for energy.
   在长时间运动中，身体耗尽糖原储备，开始更多地依赖脂肪氧化来获取能量。

2. The liver plays a critical role in **glycogen** metabolism by converting excess blood sugar into this storage polysaccharide.
   肝脏在糖原代谢中起关键作用，将多余的血糖转化为这种储能多糖。

3. Patients with **glycogen** storage disorders often require specialized diets to maintain stable blood sugar levels throughout the day.
   患有糖原储存障碍的患者通常需要特殊饮食来维持全天血糖水平的稳定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glycogen" as "glyco + gen" — "glyco" relates to sugar and "gen" means to produce, so glycogen is the substance that generates sugar when the body needs it.
把"glycogen"想成"glyco（糖）+ gen（产生）"——与糖相关并能产生能量，即身体需要时能释放糖分的物质。
