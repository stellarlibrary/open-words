# 🎨 palette

🔊 /ˈpælɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A thin board or slab on which an artist mixes colors; the range of colors used in a particular painting or by a particular artist; a range or selection of qualities or elements.
**中文：** n. 调色板；颜料；色彩范围

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- color range（色彩范围）
- spectrum（色谱）
- array（系列）
- selection（选择）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **color palette / artist's palette / limited palette / warm palette / palette of flavors**

👉 Example: The interior designer chose a neutral palette of soft grays and whites for the modern living space.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Impressionist painter was renowned for employing a vibrant **palette** of blues, greens, and golds to capture the essence of natural light.
   这位印象派画家以运用蓝色、绿色和金色的鲜艳**调色板**来捕捉自然光的本质而闻名。

2. The graphic designer carefully selected a digital **palette** that would convey professionalism while remaining visually appealing to younger audiences.
   平面设计师精心挑选了一套数字**色彩方案**，既能传达专业感，又能在视觉上吸引年轻受众。

3. The chef's culinary **palette** drew heavily from Mediterranean traditions, blending aromatic herbs with fresh seasonal ingredients from local farms.
   这位厨师的烹饪**风格**深受地中海传统的影响，将芳香草本与当地农场的新鲜时令食材融为一体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "palette" as the flat board a "pal" (friend) who is an artist holds — it is the surface where all the beautiful colors come together.
把"palette"想成一个当画家的"pal"（朋友）手持的平板——它是所有美丽色彩汇聚的地方。
