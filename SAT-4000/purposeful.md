# 🎯 purposeful

🔊 /ˈpɜːrpəsfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing determination and resolve; done with a clear aim or intention; meaningful
**中文：** adj. 有目的的；有决心的；有意义的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deliberate（故意的）
- determined（坚定的）
- resolute（果断的）
- intentional（有意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **purposeful action / purposeful stride / purposeful approach / purposeful effort**

👉 Example: She walked with a purposeful stride toward the podium, ready to deliver her address.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The director's **purposeful** restructuring of the department resulted in a measurable increase in overall productivity.
   主任有目的的部门重组带来了整体生产力的可衡量增长。

2. Every element in the architect's design appeared highly **purposeful**, serving both aesthetic and functional objectives.
   建筑师设计中的每一个元素都显得非常有目的性，兼顾美学和功能目标。

3. The committee adopted a more **purposeful** approach to policy reform after years of indecisive deliberation.
   经过多年犹豫不决的审议后，委员会对政策改革采取了更有目的的方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "purpose + ful" — someone who is "full of purpose" acts with clear intention and determination, never aimless.
把"purposeful"想成"purpose（目的）+ ful（充满的）"——一个"充满目的"的人行动时有明确的意图和决心，绝不漫无目的。
