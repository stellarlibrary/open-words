# 👁️ optic

🔊 /ˈɑːptɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to sight, the eye, or optics. (n.) An eye or a lens used in an optical instrument
**中文：** adj. 光学的；视觉的；眼睛的 n. 眼睛；镜片

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- visual（视觉的）
- ocular（眼睛的）
- optical（光学的）
- lenticular（透镜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **optic nerve / optic fiber / optic lens / optic pathway**

👉 Example: The optic nerve transmits visual information from the retina directly to the brain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Damage to the **optic** nerve can result in permanent vision loss that no corrective surgery can fully restore.
   视神经损伤可能导致永久性视力丧失，任何矫正手术都无法完全恢复。

2. The scientist calibrated the **optic** instruments before conducting the laser diffraction experiment in the laboratory.
   科学家在实验室进行激光衍射实验之前校准了光学仪器。

3. Advanced **optic** technologies have revolutionized telecommunications by enabling faster data transmission through fiber cables.
   先进的光学技术通过光纤电缆实现更快的数据传输，彻底改变了电信行业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "optic" as sharing a root with "optician" — the person who helps you see better with lenses for your eyes.
把"optic"想成和"optician（配镜师）"同根——就是帮你用镜片看得更清楚的人。
