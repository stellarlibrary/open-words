# 🤔 demur

🔊 /dɪˈmɜːr/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To raise objections or show reluctance; to hesitate or express disagreement. (n.) An objection or protest
**中文：** v. 反对；抗辩 n. 异议

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- object（反对）
- protest（抗议）
- hesitate（犹豫）
- balk（畏缩不前）
- dissent（异议）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **demur at a suggestion / without demur / demur to a proposal / politely demur**

👉 Example: When asked to work overtime without compensation, several employees chose to demur respectfully but firmly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board member **demurred** at the proposed merger, arguing that the financial risks far outweighed any potential strategic benefits.
   这位董事会成员对提议的合并表示反对，认为财务风险远远超过了任何潜在的战略利益。

2. Although her colleagues accepted the new policy without **demur**, she felt compelled to voice her concerns about its implementation.
   尽管她的同事毫无异议地接受了新政策，她仍感到有必要表达她对其实施的担忧。

3. The diplomat tactfully **demurred** when pressured to sign the agreement, requesting additional time for thorough legal review and analysis.
   这位外交官在被施压签署协议时巧妙地提出了异议，要求更多时间进行彻底的法律审查和分析。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-MUR" — to "murmur" in disagreement. When you demur, you murmur your objections quietly but firmly.
把"demur"想成"de-MUR"——不满时"murmur"（低声说）。当你demur时，你在安静而坚定地低声提出反对意见。
