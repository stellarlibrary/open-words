# 🧬 endocrine

🔊 /ˈɛndəkrɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The internal secretion of a gland. (adj.) Relating to or denoting glands that secrete hormones directly into the blood
**中文：** n. 内分泌 adj. 内分泌的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hormonal（荷尔蒙的）
- glandular（腺体的）
- secretory（分泌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endocrine system / endocrine glands / endocrine disorders / endocrine function**

👉 Example: The endocrine system regulates metabolism, growth, and mood through the release of hormones.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **endocrine** system plays a critical role in regulating metabolism, reproduction, and the body's response to environmental stress.
   内分泌系统在调节新陈代谢、生殖和身体对环境压力的反应方面起着关键作用。

2. Researchers discovered that prolonged exposure to certain chemicals can severely disrupt normal **endocrine** function in aquatic organisms.
   研究人员发现，长期接触某些化学物质会严重干扰水生生物的正常内分泌功能。

3. The physician referred the patient to a specialist after detecting an abnormality in the **endocrine** glands during a routine examination.
   医生在常规检查中发现内分泌腺异常后，将患者转诊给了专科医生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "endo-crine" as "endo (inside) + crine (secrete)" — glands that secrete hormones inside the body directly into the bloodstream.
把"endocrine"想成"endo（内部）+ crine（分泌）"——在体内将激素直接分泌到血液中的腺体。
