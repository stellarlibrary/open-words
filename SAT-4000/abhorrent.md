# 🚫 abhorrent

🔊 /əbˈhɔːrənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing or deserving strong dislike or hatred; utterly detestable; fundamentally incompatible
**中文：** adj. 可恶的，令人厌恶的；格格不入的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detestable（可憎的）
- repugnant（令人反感的）
- loathsome（令人厌恶的）
- revolting（令人作呕的）
- odious（可恨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **morally abhorrent / abhorrent behavior / abhorrent to someone / find something abhorrent**

👉 Example: The tribunal declared the regime's treatment of political prisoners morally abhorrent and indefensible.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international community condemned the use of chemical weapons as an **abhorrent** violation of human rights.
   国际社会谴责使用化学武器是对人权的可恶侵犯。

2. Such discriminatory practices are **abhorrent** to the fundamental principles enshrined in the national constitution.
   这种歧视性做法与国家宪法中所确立的基本原则格格不入。

3. Many citizens found the politician's racist remarks deeply **abhorrent** and called for his immediate resignation.
   许多公民认为该政客的种族主义言论极其可恶，并要求他立即辞职。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abhorrent" contains "HORROR" — something abhorrent fills you with horror and disgust.
"Abhorrent"中包含"HORROR"（恐惧）——令人abhorrent的东西让你充满恐惧和厌恶。
