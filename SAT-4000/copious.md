# 📚 copious

🔊 /ˈkoʊpiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Abundant in supply or quantity; yielding an abundant supply; large in number or amount
**中文：** adj. 丰富的；大量的；多产的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- plentiful（充足的）
- profuse（大量的）
- ample（充裕的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **copious notes / copious amounts / copious evidence / copious tears**

👉 Example: The researcher took copious notes during the three-hour seminar on molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian produced **copious** documentation to support her controversial thesis about the origins of the ancient civilization.
   这位历史学家提供了大量文献来支持她关于古代文明起源的争议性论点。

2. Students who take **copious** notes during lectures tend to perform significantly better on comprehensive final examinations.
   在讲座中做大量笔记的学生往往在综合期末考试中表现明显更好。

3. The tropical region receives **copious** rainfall throughout the monsoon season, sustaining its remarkably diverse ecosystem.
   该热带地区在整个季风季节接收到大量降雨，维持着其极为多样的生态系统。

---

Think of "copious" as "copy-us" — imagine making so many copies that you end up with an abundant, overflowing pile.
把"copious"想成"copy-us"——想象复制了太多份，最后堆成了丰富的、溢出来的一大堆。
