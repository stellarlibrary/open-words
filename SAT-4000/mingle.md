# 🤝 mingle

🔊 /ˈmɪŋɡl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To mix or combine together; to move around and socialize with others at a gathering.
**中文：** vi. 混合起来；相往来 vt. 使混合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blend（混合）
- mix（混合）
- socialize（社交）
- intermingle（混合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mingle with / mingle freely / mingle among / mingle together**

👉 Example: At the reception, diplomats from various countries were encouraged to mingle with one another.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scent of fresh flowers **mingled** with the aroma of baking bread, creating a pleasant atmosphere throughout the house.
   鲜花的香味与烤面包的香气混合在一起，在整个房子里营造出宜人的氛围。

2. Students from different departments were encouraged to **mingle** at the orientation event to build interdisciplinary connections and friendships.
   来自不同院系的学生被鼓励在迎新活动中相互交流，以建立跨学科的联系和友谊。

3. Feelings of excitement and anxiety **mingled** in her mind as she prepared to deliver her first keynote address.
   当她准备发表第一次主题演讲时，兴奋和焦虑的情绪交织在她的脑海中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mingle" as "min-gle" — people at a party "gleefully" meeting and "mingling" in a minimum of time, blending into each group.
把"mingle"想成"min-gle"——派对上的人们在最短时间内欢快地相互认识和交往，融入每个群体。
