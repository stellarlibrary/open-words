# ✅ conscientious

🔊 /ˌkɒnʃiˈenʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Wishing to do what is right; thorough and careful in one's work or duty; governed by conscience.
**中文：** adj. 认真的；尽责的；本着良心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diligent（勤勉的）
- meticulous（一丝不苟的）
- scrupulous（尽职尽责的）
- painstaking（煞费苦心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conscientious worker / conscientious effort / conscientious objector / conscientious about**

👉 Example: She is a conscientious employee who always double-checks her reports before submitting them to management.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **conscientious** researcher verified every source and citation before publishing her findings in the academic journal.
   这位认真的研究人员在学术期刊发表研究成果之前，核实了每一个来源和引用。

2. As a **conscientious** objector, he refused military service on moral grounds but volunteered for community service instead.
   作为一名凭良心拒服兵役者，他基于道德立场拒绝服兵役，但转而自愿参加社区服务。

3. The most **conscientious** students consistently review their notes and seek clarification whenever they encounter unfamiliar material.
   最认真的学生总是坚持复习笔记，并在遇到不熟悉的内容时寻求解答。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "conscientious" as "conscience + tious" — someone with a strong conscience who is thorough and careful in everything they do.
把"conscientious"想成"conscience(良心) + tious"——一个良心很强的人，做任何事都认真仔细。
