# 📚 pedagogy

🔊 /ˈpedəˌɡɑːdʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The method, practice, and profession of teaching; the study of educational theory and instructional approaches
**中文：** n. 教育；教育学；教授法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- education（教育）
- instruction（教学）
- didactics（教学法）
- teaching methodology（教学方法论）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **modern pedagogy / critical pedagogy / effective pedagogy / pedagogy of the oppressed**

👉 Example: The university invested heavily in training faculty members to adopt innovative and evidence-based pedagogy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Progressive **pedagogy** emphasizes student-centered learning and encourages critical thinking over passive absorption of information.
   进步教育学强调以学生为中心的学习，鼓励批判性思维而非被动吸收信息。

2. The conference brought together scholars from diverse disciplines to discuss the future of digital **pedagogy** in higher education.
   此次会议汇聚了来自不同学科的学者，共同探讨高等教育中数字教学法的未来。

3. Effective **pedagogy** requires teachers to adapt their instructional strategies to the unique needs and abilities of each student.
   有效的教学法要求教师根据每位学生独特的需求和能力调整其教学策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pedagogy" as "ped" (child) + "agogy" (leading) — the art and science of leading children through the process of learning.
把"pedagogy"想成"ped"（孩子）+"agogy"（引导）——引导孩子完成学习过程的艺术与科学。
