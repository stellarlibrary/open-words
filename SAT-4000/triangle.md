# 🔺 triangle

🔊 /ˈtraɪæŋɡl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A plane figure with three straight sides and three angles; a situation involving three people or elements.
**中文：** n. 三角形；三角关系；三角地带

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trigon（三角形）
- triad（三元组）
- three-sided figure（三边形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **love triangle / Bermuda Triangle / right triangle / triangle of power**

👉 Example: The architect incorporated a series of triangles into the building's design to enhance structural stability.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician demonstrated that the sum of interior angles in any **triangle** is always exactly one hundred eighty degrees.
   数学家证明了任何三角形的内角之和始终恰好为一百八十度。

2. The geopolitical **triangle** among the three neighboring nations has shaped regional diplomacy for more than five decades.
   三个邻国之间的地缘政治三角关系已影响区域外交超过五十年。

3. Students were asked to calculate the area of a scalene **triangle** using the measurements provided on the examination paper.
   学生们被要求使用试卷上提供的数据计算一个不等边三角形的面积。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tri-angle" — "tri" means three, and "angle" means corner. A triangle literally has three angles.
把"triangle"拆分为"tri-angle"——"tri"表示三，"angle"表示角，三角形就是有三个角的图形。
