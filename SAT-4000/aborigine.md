# 🏔️ aborigine

🔊 /ˌæbəˈrɪdʒɪniː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An original or earliest known inhabitant of a region; a member of an indigenous people
**中文：** n. 土著；土著居民；原住民

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- native（本地人）
- indigenous person（原住民）
- autochthon（土著）
- original inhabitant（原始居民）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the aborigines of (a region) / Australian Aborigines / rights of aborigines / culture of aborigines**

👉 Example: Anthropologists have studied the social structures of the aborigines of this island for over fifty years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **aborigines** of this remote island have preserved unique cultural traditions unchanged for countless generations.
   这个偏远岛屿的土著居民保存了无数代人未曾改变的独特文化传统。

2. A new federal policy aims to protect the ancestral lands of **aborigines** from commercial mining operations.
   一项新的联邦政策旨在保护土著居民的祖传土地免受商业采矿作业的侵害。

3. Scholars emphasize that **aborigines** possess sophisticated knowledge systems developed through millennia of environmental observation.
   学者们强调，土著居民拥有通过数千年的环境观察发展起来的精密知识体系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aborigine" comes from Latin "ab origine," meaning "from the beginning" — the people who have been there from the start.
"Aborigine"源自拉丁语"ab origine"，意为"从一开始"——从最初就在那里的人。
