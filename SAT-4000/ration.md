# 🍞 ration

🔊 /ˈræʃən/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To distribute or allot in fixed, limited amounts. (n.) A fixed allowance of provisions or food; an allotted amount
**中文：** v. 配给；定量供应 n. 定量；口粮；配给量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- allot（分配）
- allocate（拨给）
- portion（一份）
- allowance（配给量）
- quota（定额）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ration food / ration supplies / daily ration / ration out / on short rations**

👉 Example: During the drought, authorities decided to ration water to ensure every household received a fair share.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military commander ordered soldiers to carefully **ration** their remaining supplies until the next shipment arrived at base.
   军事指挥官命令士兵仔细分配剩余物资，直到下一批补给到达基地。

2. During wartime, the government imposed strict policies to **ration** essential commodities such as fuel, sugar, and butter.
   战时，政府实施了严格的政策来定量配给燃料、糖和黄油等基本物资。

3. Each refugee received a modest daily **ration** of bread and clean water from the humanitarian relief organization's distribution center.
   每位难民从人道主义救援组织的分发中心领取少量的每日面包和清洁饮水配给。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ration" as "ratio + n" — a ration divides supplies into ratios so everyone gets their fair, measured portion.
把"ration"想成"ratio（比率）+ n"——配给将物资按比例分配，使每个人都得到公平的、有计量的份额。
