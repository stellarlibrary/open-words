# 🗣️ vernacular

🔊 /vərˈnækjələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the native language or dialect of a specific region or country. (n.) The everyday spoken language of ordinary people in a particular locality
**中文：** adj. 本国的；地方的；用本地语写成的 n. 本地话；方言；动植物的俗名

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dialect（方言）
- colloquial language（口语）
- common speech（日常用语）
- native tongue（母语）
- patois（土语）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in the vernacular / vernacular architecture / vernacular language / local vernacular**

👉 Example: The author chose to write in the vernacular so that ordinary readers could understand the philosophical arguments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet's decision to compose in the **vernacular** rather than Latin marked a revolutionary shift in European literary tradition.
   这位诗人决定用方言而非拉丁语写作，标志着欧洲文学传统的一次革命性转变。

2. Linguists traveled to remote villages to document the **vernacular** before rapid modernization could erase these endangered regional dialects.
   语言学家前往偏远村庄记录方言，以免快速现代化抹去这些濒危的地方方言。

3. The architect drew inspiration from **vernacular** building traditions, incorporating locally sourced materials and time-tested structural techniques.
   这位建筑师从乡土建筑传统中汲取灵感，采用当地材料和久经考验的结构技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vern-acular" — "verna" in Latin meant a home-born slave, hence something domestic or local. The vernacular is the language born at home, the local tongue.
把"vernacular"想成"vern-acular"——拉丁语"verna"意为家生奴隶，引申为本地的。vernacular就是在家中诞生的语言，即本地话。
