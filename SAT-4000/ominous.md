# 🌑 ominous

🔊 /ˈɑːmɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Giving the impression that something bad or unpleasant is about to happen; threatening or inauspicious
**中文：** adj. 预兆的；不吉利的；不祥的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- threatening（威胁性的）
- foreboding（预感不祥的）
- sinister（险恶的）
- menacing（有威胁的）
- portentous（不祥的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ominous sign / ominous warning / ominous clouds / ominous silence / ominous tone**

👉 Example: The ominous silence that fell over the courtroom suggested the verdict would not be favorable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ominous** clouds rolling across the sky forced the organizers to cancel the afternoon's outdoor graduation ceremony.
   天空中翻滚的不祥乌云迫使组织者取消了下午的户外毕业典礼。

2. An **ominous** tone pervaded the diplomat's speech, suggesting that negotiations between the two nations had collapsed entirely.
   外交官的讲话中弥漫着不祥的语气，暗示两国之间的谈判已完全破裂。

3. The sudden departure of senior executives was viewed as an **ominous** indicator of the corporation's declining financial health.
   高管的突然离职被视为公司财务状况恶化的不祥信号。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ominous" as coming from "omen" + "-ous" — full of bad omens, like dark signs warning you of trouble ahead.
把"ominous"想成来自"omen（预兆）"加"-ous"——充满不祥预兆，就像黑暗的征兆警告你前方有麻烦。
