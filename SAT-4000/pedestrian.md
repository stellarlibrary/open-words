# 🚶 pedestrian

🔊 /pəˈdɛstriən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking imagination or excitement; dull and ordinary. (n.) A person walking rather than traveling in a vehicle
**中文：** adj. 徒步的；呆板的；平庸的；缺乏想象力的 n. 步行者；行人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mundane（平凡的）
- prosaic（平淡的）
- unimaginative（缺乏想象力的）
- commonplace（平庸的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pedestrian prose / pedestrian approach / pedestrian crossing / pedestrian traffic**

👉 Example: The critic described the novel's plot as pedestrian, lacking any originality or creative spark.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the author's reputation, the new novel was criticized for its **pedestrian** prose and predictable narrative structure.
   尽管这位作者声名显赫，新小说还是因其平庸的文笔和可预见的叙事结构而受到批评。

2. City planners proposed widening the **pedestrian** walkways to improve safety and reduce conflicts between walkers and cyclists.
   城市规划者提议拓宽人行道，以提高安全性并减少步行者与骑行者之间的冲突。

3. The professor urged students to move beyond **pedestrian** interpretations and develop more nuanced analytical perspectives on the text.
   教授敦促学生超越平庸的解读，对文本发展出更加细致入微的分析视角。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pedestrian" as "ped (foot) + estrian" — just walking on foot, nothing exciting. A pedestrian idea is one that just plods along.
把"pedestrian"想成"ped（脚）+estrian"——只是步行，没什么激动人心的。一个pedestrian的想法就是平淡无奇地走着。
