# 💊 tonic

🔊 /ˈtɒnɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A medicinal drink taken to give a feeling of vigor or well-being; something that restores energy. (adj.) Having a restorative or invigorating effect; relating to musical tone
**中文：** n. 补药；滋补品；奎宁水；主音 adj. 滋补的；使精神振奋的；声调的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restorative（恢复剂）
- stimulant（兴奋剂）
- elixir（万灵药）
- invigorant（补药）
- boost（提振）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a tonic for / gin and tonic / tonic effect / tonic note / much-needed tonic**

👉 Example: The weekend retreat in the countryside proved to be a welcome tonic for the exhausted team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The warm spring weather acted as a natural **tonic** for residents weary from the long and unforgiving winter months.
   温暖的春天天气对经历了漫长而严酷的冬季的居民来说是一剂天然的补药。

2. Physicians in the nineteenth century frequently prescribed herbal **tonics** to patients suffering from chronic fatigue and malaise.
   十九世纪的医生经常给患有慢性疲劳和不适的病人开草药补剂。

3. The conductor emphasized the importance of resolving the harmonic progression to the **tonic** chord at the conclusion of the piece.
   指挥强调了在乐曲结尾将和声进行解决到主和弦的重要性。
