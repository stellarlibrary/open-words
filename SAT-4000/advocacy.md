# 📣 advocacy

🔊 /ˈæd.və.kə.si/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Public support or recommendation of a particular cause, policy, or course of action.
**中文：** 主张；拥护；辩护；倡导

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- support（支持）
- promotion（推动）
- endorsement（背书）
- championship（拥护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **advocacy for / of + [cause/policy]; advocacy + group / campaign / efforts**

👉 Example: Her advocacy for environmental protection inspired widespread public action.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's **advocacy** for human rights has influenced legislation in several countries.
   该组织对人权的倡导已经影响了多个国家的立法。

2. Effective **advocacy** requires a combination of research, persuasion, and sustained public engagement.
   有效的倡导需要研究、说服和持续的公众参与相结合。

3. His lifelong **advocacy** of educational reform earned him recognition from numerous academic institutions.
   他毕生对教育改革的主张为他赢得了众多学术机构的认可。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Advocacy" shares its root with "advocate" — think of advocacy as the act of being an advocate, speaking up for a cause.
联想记忆：advocacy 与 advocate（倡导者）同根，advocacy 就是"倡导"这一行为本身，即为某项事业发声。
