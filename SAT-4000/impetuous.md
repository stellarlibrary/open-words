# 🌊 impetuous

🔊 /ɪmˈpɛtʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Acting or done quickly without thought or care; rash and impulsive; moving with great force or violence
**中文：** adj. 冲动的；鲁莽的；猛烈的；激烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impulsive（冲动的）
- rash（鲁莽的）
- hasty（仓促的）
- reckless（不顾后果的）
- headstrong（固执冲动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impetuous decision / impetuous youth / impetuous behavior / impetuous rush**

👉 Example: His impetuous decision to resign without a backup plan left him in a difficult financial situation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young officer's **impetuous** charge across open terrain resulted in unnecessary casualties that could have been entirely avoided.
   这位年轻军官冲动地穿越开阔地带发起冲锋，导致了本可完全避免的不必要伤亡。

2. Critics warned that the senator's **impetuous** legislative proposal lacked sufficient analysis and would produce severe unintended economic consequences.
   批评人士警告说，这位参议员冲动的立法提案缺乏充分分析，将产生严重的意想不到的经济后果。

3. The **impetuous** floodwaters surged through the valley with devastating force, destroying bridges and displacing thousands of vulnerable residents.
   猛烈的洪水以毁灭性的力量涌过山谷，摧毁了桥梁，使数千名脆弱的居民流离失所。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impetuous" as sharing its root with "impetus" — both come from Latin "impetere" meaning to rush at, so impetuous describes someone who rushes into action without thinking.
把"impetuous"和"impetus（冲力）"联系起来——两者都源自拉丁语"impetere（冲向）"，所以impetuous描述的是不假思索就冲动行事的人。
