# 📏 stature

🔊 /ˈstætʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person's natural height; the level of respect, importance, or reputation gained by achievement or ability
**中文：** n. 身高，身材

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- height（身高）
- standing（地位）
- prestige（声望）
- eminence（卓越）
- repute（名声）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grow in stature / of great stature / moral stature / international stature**

👉 Example: The scientist's stature in the field of genetics continued to grow after her groundbreaking discovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite his modest physical **stature**, the general commanded immense respect from every officer and soldier under his authority.
   尽管身材矮小，这位将军仍赢得了麾下每一位军官和士兵的极大尊重。

2. The university's academic **stature** has risen considerably since it began attracting Nobel laureates to its distinguished research faculty.
   自从该大学开始吸引诺贝尔奖得主加入其杰出的研究团队以来，其学术声望大幅提升。

3. Her intellectual **stature** and leadership qualities positioned her as the most qualified candidate for the chief executive role.
   她的学识高度和领导才能使她成为首席执行官一职最合格的候选人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stature" as "stat + ure" — your "stats" (statistics) of height and standing; it measures both physical height and moral height.
把"stature"想成"stat + ure"——关于身高和地位的"数据"，既衡量身体的高度，也衡量精神的高度。
