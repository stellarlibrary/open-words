# 🌀 chimerical

🔊 /kaɪˈmɛrɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Existing only in imagination; wildly fanciful or unrealistic; absurdly improbable
**中文：** adj. 空想的；妄想的；荒唐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fanciful（空想的）
- illusory（虚幻的）
- utopian（空想的）
- fantastical（荒诞的）
- quixotic（不切实际的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chimerical dream / chimerical scheme / chimerical vision / chimerical notion**

👉 Example: The investors dismissed the proposal as a chimerical scheme with no basis in economic reality.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that the pursuit of absolute perfection in governance is ultimately a **chimerical** endeavor.
   这位哲学家认为，追求治理中的绝对完美最终是一种空想的努力。

2. Critics regarded the architect's plan for a self-sustaining underwater city as a **chimerical** fantasy.
   批评家们将这位建筑师关于自给自足水下城市的计划视为荒唐的幻想。

3. The committee rejected the **chimerical** proposal, noting that its projected outcomes defied all established scientific principles.
   委员会否决了这项空想的提案，指出其预测结果违背了所有既定的科学原理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chimera" — in Greek mythology, the Chimera was an impossible creature (lion, goat, serpent combined), so chimerical means wildly imaginary.
联想"chimera（奇美拉）"——在希腊神话中，奇美拉是一种不可能存在的怪物（狮头、羊身、蛇尾），所以chimerical意为空想的、荒唐的。
