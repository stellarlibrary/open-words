# 💍 bigamy

🔊 /ˈbɪɡəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The offense of marrying someone while already legally married to another person
**中文：** 重婚罪；重婚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- polygamy（一夫多妻/一妻多夫）
- plural marriage（多重婚姻）
- unlawful marriage（非法婚姻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commit bigamy / charge with bigamy / convicted of bigamy / bigamy laws**

👉 Example: He was charged with bigamy after investigators discovered his secret second marriage in another state.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court found the defendant guilty of **bigamy** after evidence confirmed he had married twice without obtaining a divorce.
   法院在证据证实被告未离婚便再次结婚后，判定其犯有重婚罪。

2. In most Western legal systems, **bigamy** is classified as a criminal offense punishable by imprisonment or substantial fines.
   在大多数西方法律体系中，重婚被归类为可处监禁或巨额罚款的刑事犯罪。

3. The prosecution presented marriage certificates from two jurisdictions to substantiate the **bigamy** charge against the accused.
   控方出示了来自两个司法管辖区的结婚证书，以证实对被告的重婚指控。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "-gamy" (marriage) — bigamy literally means having two marriages at the same time.
把"bigamy"拆分为"bi-"（二）+"gamy"（婚姻）——字面意思就是同时拥有两段婚姻。
