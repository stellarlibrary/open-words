# 🌀 chaos

🔊 /ˈkeɪɑːs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Complete disorder and confusion; a state in which everything is out of order and unpredictable
**中文：** n. 混乱；混沌；无序状态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disorder（无序）
- turmoil（混乱）
- pandemonium（大混乱）
- anarchy（无政府状态）
- bedlam（喧嚣混乱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **descend into chaos / cause chaos / utter chaos / chaos theory / in chaos**

👉 Example: The sudden resignation of the prime minister plunged the entire government into political chaos.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unexpected power outage plunged the metropolitan financial district into complete **chaos**, disrupting billions of dollars in electronic transactions.
   突如其来的停电使大都市金融区陷入一片混乱，扰乱了数十亿美元的电子交易。

2. Mathematicians studying **chaos** theory have revealed that even deterministic systems can exhibit profoundly unpredictable and complex behavior.
   研究混沌理论的数学家揭示了即使是确定性系统也能表现出深刻的不可预测性和复杂行为。

3. In the aftermath of the natural disaster, relief organizations struggled to deliver essential supplies amid the **chaos** of collapsed infrastructure.
   在自然灾害之后，救援组织在基础设施崩溃的混乱中艰难地运送基本物资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chaos" from Greek mythology — Chaos was the primordial void before the universe was ordered. When things return to chaos, they return to that original state of total disorder.
"Chaos"源于希腊神话——混沌（Chaos）是宇宙有序之前的原始虚空。当事物回到chaos，就是回到了最初的完全无序状态。
