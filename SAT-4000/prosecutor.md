# 👨‍⚖️ prosecutor

🔊 /ˈprɑːsɪkjuːtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A legal official who conducts proceedings against a defendant in a criminal court; one who institutes a prosecution
**中文：** n. 检举人；检察官；公诉人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- district attorney（地方检察官）
- accuser（原告）
- plaintiff（起诉人）
- complainant（申诉人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chief prosecutor / public prosecutor / federal prosecutor / the prosecutor argued**

👉 Example: The prosecutor presented compelling evidence that convinced the jury to deliver a guilty verdict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The lead **prosecutor** delivered a powerful closing argument that systematically dismantled every claim made by the defense team.
   首席检察官发表了有力的结案陈词，系统地驳斥了辩护团队提出的每一项主张。

2. As a federal **prosecutor**, she spent over a decade building cases against organized crime networks operating across state lines.
   作为联邦检察官，她花了十多年时间建立针对跨州运作的有组织犯罪网络的案件。

3. The **prosecutor** requested that the judge impose the maximum sentence given the severity and premeditated nature of the offense.
   检察官请求法官鉴于犯罪的严重性和预谋性质判处最高刑罚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prosecutor" as the person who "prosecutes" — the one who professionally pursues justice in court by bringing charges.
把"prosecutor"想成"prosecute"的执行者——在法庭上专业地追求正义、提出指控的人。
