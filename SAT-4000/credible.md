# ✅ credible

🔊 /ˈkrɛdəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be believed; convincing; capable of persuading people that something will happen or be successful
**中文：** adj. 可靠的；可信的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- believable（可信的）
- plausible（合理的）
- convincing（令人信服的）
- trustworthy（值得信赖的）
- reliable（可靠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **credible evidence / credible source / credible threat / credible witness / credible explanation**

👉 Example: The investigation could not proceed without at least one credible witness willing to testify.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosecution must present **credible** evidence to convince the jury beyond a reasonable doubt of the defendant's guilt.
   控方必须出示可信的证据，以使陪审团毫无合理怀疑地确信被告有罪。

2. Only scholars who publish in peer-reviewed journals are considered **credible** authorities on matters of advanced scientific research.
   只有在同行评审期刊上发表文章的学者才被视为高级科学研究领域的可靠权威。

3. The diplomat warned that the intelligence agencies had identified a **credible** threat to national security requiring immediate action.
   这位外交官警告说，情报机构已经发现了一个需要立即采取行动的可信国家安全威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cred-ible" as "credit-able" — something worthy of credit or trust, therefore believable.
把"credible"想成"able to receive credit（值得信赖的）"——值得获得信用或信任的，因此是可信的。
