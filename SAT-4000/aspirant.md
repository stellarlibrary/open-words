# 🎯 aspirant

🔊 /ˈæspɪrənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who has ambitions to achieve something, typically a position or status. (adj.) Having ambitions; aspiring
**中文：** n. 有抱负的人；追求者 adj. 上进的；有志向的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- candidate（候选人）
- contender（竞争者）
- hopeful（有希望的人）
- seeker（追求者）
- applicant（申请者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political aspirant / aspirant to the throne / presidential aspirant / young aspirant**

👉 Example: Every aspirant to the scholarship must demonstrate exceptional academic achievement and leadership.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young **aspirant** dedicated years of rigorous training to earn a coveted position in the national orchestra.
   这位年轻的追求者投入了多年严格的训练，以赢得国家管弦乐团中令人垂涎的职位。

2. Several political **aspirants** gathered at the convention to outline their visions for the future of the country.
   几位有政治抱负的人在大会上齐聚一堂，阐述他们对国家未来的愿景。

3. As an **aspirant** to the medical profession, she volunteered tirelessly at clinics throughout her undergraduate studies.
   作为一名立志从医的人，她在整个本科学习期间不知疲倦地在诊所做志愿服务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aspirant" shares the root with "aspire" — an aspirant is someone who aspires to reach a higher goal or position.
"Aspirant"与"aspire"（渴望）同根——aspirant就是一个渴望达到更高目标或地位的人。
