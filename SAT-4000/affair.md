# 📋 affair

🔊 /əˈfɛr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A matter, event, or situation requiring attention; personal business or concerns.
**中文：** 事情；事务；私事。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- matter（事情）
- business（事务）
- concern（关切的事）
- issue（问题）
- event（事件）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **current, foreign, personal, state, private**
👉 Example: The minister was responsible for managing the country's foreign affairs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat was entrusted with handling a sensitive international **affair** that required considerable discretion and tact.
   这位外交官被委以处理一件需要相当谨慎和策略的敏感国际事务。

2. She preferred to keep her personal **affairs** entirely separate from her professional responsibilities at the firm.
   她更愿意将个人私事与她在公司的职业责任完全分开。

3. The controversial **affair** dominated newspaper headlines for several weeks and provoked widespread public debate.
   这一争议性事件连续数周占据了报纸头条，并引发了广泛的公众辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "affair" as "a fair" deal of things to handle — affairs are the matters you must attend to.
将"affair"想象成"a fair"（相当多的）要处理的事情——affairs就是你必须处理的事务。
