# 🌳 oak

🔊 /oʊk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large hardwood tree that produces acorns; the wood of this tree. (adj.) Made of or relating to oak wood.
**中文：** n. 橡树；橡木色；橡木家具 adj. 栎树的；栎木制的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hardwood（硬木）
- timber（木材）
- lumber（木料）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oak tree / oak table / oak forest / solid oak / oak barrel**

👉 Example: The dining room featured a magnificent table crafted from solid oak.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient **oak** in the courtyard had witnessed three centuries of history and still stood remarkably strong.
   庭院中那棵古老的橡树见证了三个世纪的历史，依然屹立不倒。

2. Architects specified **oak** flooring throughout the library to convey warmth and intellectual tradition.
   建筑师指定整个图书馆铺设橡木地板，以传达温馨和学术传统的氛围。

3. The naturalist observed that the mighty **oak** provides essential habitat for hundreds of insect species.
   博物学家观察到，这棵强壮的橡树为数百种昆虫提供了至关重要的栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oak" as "O-A-K" — "Old And Kingly" — oak trees are old, majestic, and stand like kings of the forest.
把"oak"想成"Old And Kingly"——橡树古老而威严，像森林之王一样矗立。
