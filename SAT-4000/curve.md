# 〰️ curve

🔊 /kɜːrv/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A line or outline that gradually deviates from being straight; a bend. (v.) To form or cause to form a curve; to bend
**中文：** n. 曲线；弯曲 v. 弯；使弯曲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bend（弯曲）
- arc（弧线）
- arch（拱形）
- contour（轮廓）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **learning curve / curve of the road / bell curve / curve upward / throw a curve**

👉 Example: The road curves sharply around the mountain, requiring drivers to reduce their speed significantly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economist presented a graph showing the **curve** of inflation rates rising steadily over the past two decades.
   经济学家展示了一张图表，显示过去二十年通货膨胀率曲线稳步上升。

2. The architect designed the museum entrance to **curve** gracefully inward, creating a welcoming and aesthetically striking impression.
   建筑师将博物馆入口设计成优雅地向内弯曲，营造出一种温馨且美观的印象。

3. The steep learning **curve** associated with advanced statistical methods often discourages students in their first year of study.
   与高级统计方法相关的陡峭学习曲线常常让第一年的学生望而却步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "curve" as the shape of the letter "C" — it bends and curves rather than going straight like the letter "I."
把"curve"想成字母"C"的形状——它弯曲而非像字母"I"那样笔直。
