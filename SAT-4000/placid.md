# 🕊️ placid

🔊 /ˈplæsɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Calm and peaceful; not easily upset or excited; having a quiet, undisturbed nature
**中文：** adj. 平静的；温和的；沉着的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- serene（宁静的）
- tranquil（平静的）
- calm（镇定的）
- composed（沉着的）
- unruffled（从容的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **placid temperament / placid expression / placid waters / placid demeanor**

👉 Example: The lake remained placid despite the strong winds blowing across the valley.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor maintained a **placid** demeanor throughout the heated academic debate, never raising her voice once.
   教授在激烈的学术辩论中始终保持平静的态度，从未提高过声音。

2. Beneath the **placid** surface of the river, powerful currents posed a significant danger to inexperienced swimmers.
   在平静的河面之下，强劲的水流对缺乏经验的游泳者构成了重大危险。

3. Her **placid** nature made her an ideal candidate for the high-pressure diplomatic negotiations between the two nations.
   她温和的性格使她成为两国之间高压外交谈判的理想人选。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "placid" as "place-id" — a place with a peaceful identity, like a calm, still lake where nothing disturbs the water.
把"placid"想成"place-id"——一个有着宁静特质的地方，就像一个平静无波的湖泊。
