# 🗣️ proponent

🔊 /prəˈpoʊnənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who advocates or supports a theory, proposal, or course of action
**中文：** n. 支持者；建议者；提倡者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advocate（倡导者）
- supporter（支持者）
- champion（拥护者）
- backer（后援者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proponent of reform / proponent of the theory / leading proponent / strong proponent**

👉 Example: She became a leading proponent of renewable energy policy after witnessing the effects of pollution firsthand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator has been a vocal **proponent** of universal healthcare reform throughout her distinguished three-decade political career.
   这位参议员在其杰出的三十年政治生涯中一直是全民医疗改革的积极倡导者。

2. As a devoted **proponent** of classical education, the professor argued that studying ancient texts develops essential critical thinking skills.
   作为古典教育的忠实支持者，这位教授认为研读古代文献能培养必要的批判性思维能力。

3. **Proponents** of the new trade agreement emphasized its potential to create thousands of jobs in economically disadvantaged communities.
   新贸易协定的支持者强调了它在经济弱势社区创造数千个就业机会的潜力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proponent" as "pro-ponent" — someone who is "pro" (for) something, standing in front to propose and support it.
把"proponent"想成"pro-ponent"——一个"pro"（支持）某事的人，站在前面提议和支持它。
