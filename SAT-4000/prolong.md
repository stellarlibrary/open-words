# ⏳ prolong

🔊 /prəˈlɔːŋ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To extend the duration of something; to make something last longer than usual or expected
**中文：** v. 延长；拖延；拉长

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extend（延伸）
- lengthen（延长）
- protract（拖延）
- sustain（维持）
- perpetuate（使持续）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prolong the life / prolong the process / prolong the suffering / prolong the debate**

👉 Example: The new treatment can significantly prolong the lives of patients diagnosed with the disease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medical advances have made it possible to **prolong** the lives of patients suffering from previously incurable chronic diseases.
   医学进步使得延长那些患有以前无法治愈的慢性病患者的生命成为可能。

2. The defense attorney sought to **prolong** the trial proceedings by introducing numerous motions and requesting additional witness testimony.
   辩护律师试图通过提出大量动议和要求额外证人证词来拖延审判程序。

3. Excessive bureaucratic procedures tend to **prolong** the approval process, causing frustration among applicants seeking timely decisions.
   过多的官僚程序往往会延长审批流程，令寻求及时决定的申请者感到沮丧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-LONG" — to make something go on for a "long" time; the word literally contains "long" within it.
把"prolong"想成"pro-LONG"——让某事持续"很长"时间；这个词本身就包含"long"（长）。
