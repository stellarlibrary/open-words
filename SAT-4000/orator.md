# 🗣️ orator

🔊 /ˈɔːrətər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A skilled and eloquent public speaker; one who delivers formal speeches with rhetorical power
**中文：** n. 演说者；演讲者；雄辩家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- speaker（演讲者）
- rhetorician（修辞学家）
- lecturer（讲师）
- declaimer（雄辩者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gifted orator / skilled orator / public orator / great orator**

👉 Example: Martin Luther King Jr. is widely regarded as one of the greatest orators in modern history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **orator** captivated the audience with her compelling arguments and masterful command of rhetorical techniques.
   这位著名的演说家以令人信服的论点和对修辞技巧的精湛掌握吸引了听众。

2. Ancient Greek culture placed great value on the skills of the **orator**, considering eloquence essential to civic participation.
   古希腊文化非常重视演说家的技能，认为雄辩对公民参与至关重要。

3. The young **orator** won the national debate championship by articulating her position with clarity, passion, and intellectual rigor.
   这位年轻的演讲者以清晰、热情和严谨的智识阐述自己的立场，赢得了全国辩论锦标赛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "orator" as "oral-ator" — a person who excels at "oral" communication, like a narrator who speaks powerfully.
把"orator"想成"oral-ator"——一个擅长"口头（oral）"交流的人，像一个有力量地讲述故事的叙述者。
