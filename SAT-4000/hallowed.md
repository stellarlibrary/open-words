# ⛪ hallowed

🔊 /ˈhæloʊd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Made holy; consecrated; greatly revered and honored
**中文：** adj. 神圣的；奉为神圣的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sacred（神圣的）
- consecrated（被祝圣的）
- sanctified（圣洁的）
- venerated（受崇敬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hallowed ground / hallowed halls / hallowed tradition / hallowed name**

👉 Example: The veterans gathered on the hallowed ground of the battlefield to honor their fallen comrades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ceremony took place on the **hallowed** ground where the nation's founders had signed the original declaration of independence.
   典礼在这片神圣的土地上举行，国家的创立者们曾在此签署了最初的独立宣言。

2. Students entering the **hallowed** halls of the ancient university felt a profound sense of reverence for centuries of scholarship.
   走进这所古老大学神圣殿堂的学生们对数百年的学术传统深感敬畏。

3. The committee voted to preserve the **hallowed** traditions that had defined the institution since its founding two centuries ago.
   委员会投票决定保留自两个世纪前建校以来定义该机构的神圣传统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hallowed" as "halo + wed" — something with a halo is holy, and when you are wedded to holiness, the place becomes hallowed.
把"hallowed"想成"halo + wed"——有光环的事物是神圣的，当你与神圣结合时，这个地方就变得神圣了。
