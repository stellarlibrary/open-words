# 🧠 perception

🔊 /pəˈsepʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ability to see, hear, or become aware of something through the senses; a way of regarding or understanding something; insight or intuition
**中文：** n. 理解，知觉；感受，体会；知觉作用，知觉过程；理解力，洞察力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- awareness（认知）
- insight（洞察力）
- understanding（理解）
- discernment（辨别力）
- cognition（认知）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public perception / perception of reality / visual perception / perception that / alter perception**

👉 Example: The marketing campaign was designed to change the public perception of the company's environmental policies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that human **perception** of reality is inevitably filtered through cultural and linguistic frameworks.
   这位哲学家认为，人类对现实的感知不可避免地受到文化和语言框架的过滤。

2. Advances in neuroscience have significantly deepened our understanding of how sensory **perception** operates in the brain.
   神经科学的进步极大地加深了我们对大脑感知觉运作方式的理解。

3. The widespread **perception** that standardized testing accurately measures intelligence has been challenged by numerous educational researchers.
   标准化测试能准确衡量智力这一普遍认知已被众多教育研究人员质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perception" as "percept-ion" — the process (-ion) of forming a percept, which is a mental impression of something observed.
把"perception"想成"percept-ion"——形成感知印象的过程（-ion），即对观察到的事物形成心理印象。
