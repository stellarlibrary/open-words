# 🎭 quip

🔊 /kwɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A witty remark or a clever, sarcastic comment. (v.) To make a witty or sarcastic observation
**中文：** n. 妙语；嘲弄；讽刺语；双关语 v. 嘲弄；讥讽

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- witticism（妙语）
- jest（玩笑）
- retort（反驳）
- repartee（机智的回答）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clever quip / witty quip / quip about / quip that**

👉 Example: The comedian delivered a sharp quip about modern politics that left the entire audience laughing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's well-timed **quip** about the complexity of quantum mechanics instantly lightened the mood of the tense lecture hall.
   教授关于量子力学复杂性的适时妙语立刻缓和了紧张的演讲厅氛围。

2. She could not resist **quipping** that the committee's lengthy deliberation had produced remarkably little of practical substance or value.
   她忍不住讽刺说，委员会漫长的审议几乎没有产生什么有实际价值的成果。

3. His sardonic **quip** about the budget proposal drew nervous laughter from colleagues who privately shared his critical assessment.
   他对预算提案的讽刺妙语引起了同事们紧张的笑声，他们私下里赞同他的批评性评价。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quip" as a "quick whip" of words — a fast, sharp remark that stings like a verbal whip crack.
把"quip"想成"quick whip"（快速鞭击）——一句快速而尖锐的话语，像口头上的鞭子一样有冲击力。
