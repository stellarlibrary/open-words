# 🚷 prohibition

🔊 /ˌproʊɪˈbɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of forbidding something by law or authority; a legal ban; historically, the era banning alcohol in the US
**中文：** n. 禁止；禁令；禁酒；诉讼中止令

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ban（禁令）
- embargo（禁运）
- restriction（限制）
- interdiction（禁止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prohibition on / prohibition against / impose a prohibition / lift the prohibition / during Prohibition**

👉 Example: The government imposed a strict prohibition on the sale of firearms to minors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **prohibition** on smoking in public buildings has significantly reduced secondhand smoke exposure among nonsmokers.
   公共建筑内的禁烟令大大减少了非吸烟者接触二手烟的机会。

2. During **Prohibition** in the 1920s, underground speakeasies flourished as citizens sought illegal access to alcohol.
   在20世纪20年代禁酒令期间，地下酒吧蓬勃发展，市民们寻求非法获取酒精的途径。

3. The constitutional **prohibition** against unreasonable searches protects individual privacy rights from government overreach.
   宪法中关于禁止不合理搜查的规定保护个人隐私权不受政府越权侵犯。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prohibit-ion" — it is the noun form of "prohibit," representing the official act or law that forbids something.
把"prohibition"想成"prohibit的名词形式"——代表正式禁止某事的行为或法律。
