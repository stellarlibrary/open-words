# 🏔️ chasm

🔊 /ˈkæzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A deep, gaping opening or fissure in the earth's surface; a profound difference or division between people, viewpoints, or situations
**中文：** n. 峡谷；裂口；深坑；分歧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abyss（深渊）
- gorge（峡谷）
- rift（裂缝）
- gulf（鸿沟）
- divide（分歧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deep chasm / yawning chasm / bridge the chasm / chasm between / widen the chasm**

👉 Example: A growing chasm between the wealthy and the poor threatens social stability.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geological survey revealed a massive **chasm** that had formed along the fault line after the devastating earthquake struck.
   地质调查显示，毁灭性地震发生后沿断层线形成了一个巨大的裂口。

2. The ideological **chasm** between the two political parties has widened considerably, making bipartisan cooperation increasingly difficult to achieve.
   两个政党之间的意识形态分歧已大幅加深，使跨党派合作越来越难以实现。

3. Explorers descended cautiously into the dark **chasm**, discovering an underground river flowing through ancient limestone formations below.
   探险者们小心翼翼地下到黑暗的深谷中，发现了一条穿过古老石灰岩地层的地下河。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chasm" sounding like "has-em" — the earth "has 'em" (has them), referring to the deep gaps it holds within.
把"chasm"的发音想成"has-em"——大地"拥有它们"，指的是地球内部深不可测的裂缝。
