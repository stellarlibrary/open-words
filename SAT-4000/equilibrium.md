# ⚖️ equilibrium

🔊 /ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of balance between opposing forces or influences; mental or emotional calmness
**中文：** n. 均衡；平衡；平静

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- balance（平衡）
- stability（稳定）
- equipoise（均势）
- steadiness（稳固）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach equilibrium / maintain equilibrium / disturb the equilibrium / chemical equilibrium**

👉 Example: The ecosystem eventually reached a natural equilibrium after the invasive species was controlled.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemical reaction will continue until the system reaches a state of dynamic **equilibrium** between reactants and products.
   化学反应将持续进行，直至系统在反应物和生成物之间达到动态平衡。

2. The sudden economic crisis severely disrupted the fragile **equilibrium** that had sustained the region's financial markets for decades.
   突发的经济危机严重破坏了维持该地区金融市场数十年的脆弱均衡。

3. Restoring her emotional **equilibrium** after the traumatic event required months of dedicated therapy and unwavering personal resolve.
   在创伤性事件后恢复她的情绪平静需要数月的专注治疗和坚定的个人决心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equilibrium" as "equi + librium" — "equi" means equal, "libra" means balance (like the zodiac sign Libra, the scales). Equal balance.
把"equilibrium"想成"equi + librium"——"equi"意为相等，"libra"意为天平（如同天秤座）。相等的平衡。
