# 😤 vindictive

🔊 /vɪnˈdɪktɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing a strong or unreasoning desire for revenge; intended to cause harm as retribution
**中文：** 怀恨的；有报仇心的；报复性的；惩罚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vengeful（报复的）
- spiteful（怀恨的）
- revengeful（复仇的）
- retaliatory（报复性的）
- malicious（恶意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vindictive behavior / vindictive person / vindictive streak / vindictive damages**

👉 Example: The court awarded vindictive damages to punish the defendant for particularly malicious conduct.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **vindictive** former employee deliberately leaked confidential documents to rival companies out of sheer resentment.
   那个怀恨在心的前员工出于纯粹的怨恨，蓄意将机密文件泄露给竞争对手公司。

2. Critics argued that the new regulations were **vindictive** measures designed to punish dissenting organizations rather than promote reform.
   批评人士认为这些新法规是报复性措施，旨在惩罚持异议的组织而非推动改革。

3. Her **vindictive** nature made it impossible to resolve the dispute through peaceful negotiation or diplomatic compromise.
   她报复心强的性格使得通过和平谈判或外交妥协来解决争端变得不可能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vindictive" as containing "vindict" — like "vindicate" but turned dark: someone who wants to vindicate themselves through revenge.
把"vindictive"想成包含"vindict"——像"vindicate"（证明正当）但变了味：一个想通过报复来为自己讨回公道的人。
