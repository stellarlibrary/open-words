# ⚖️ verdict

🔊 /ˈvɜːrdɪkt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A decision on a disputed issue in a civil or criminal case; a judgment or opinion formed after consideration
**中文：** n. 裁定；结论；定论；判断；意见

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- judgment（判决）
- ruling（裁决）
- decision（决定）
- finding（裁定）
- conclusion（结论）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach a verdict / deliver a verdict / guilty verdict / unanimous verdict / the final verdict**

👉 Example: After three days of deliberation, the jury finally reached a unanimous verdict of not guilty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The jury delivered a unanimous guilty **verdict** after carefully reviewing all the forensic evidence and witness testimonies.
   陪审团在仔细审查所有法医证据和证人证词后，一致作出了有罪裁定。

2. Literary critics gave a mixed **verdict** on the novelist's latest work, praising the prose but questioning the plot.
   文学评论家对这位小说家的最新作品给出了褒贬不一的评价，赞扬了散文风格但质疑了情节。

3. The public eagerly awaited the committee's **verdict** on whether the proposed infrastructure project should proceed as planned.
   公众热切等待委员会关于拟议基础设施项目是否应按计划推进的裁定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "verdict" as "vere-dict" — from Latin "vere" (truly) and "dictum" (said), meaning something truly spoken or declared as judgment.
把"verdict"想成"vere-dict"——来自拉丁语"vere"（真实地）和"dictum"（说），意为真实宣告的判决。
