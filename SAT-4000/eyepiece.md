# 🔬 eyepiece

🔊 /ˈaɪpiːs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The lens or group of lenses closest to the eye in an optical instrument such as a microscope or telescope
**中文：** n. 目镜，接目镜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ocular（目镜）
- viewing lens（观察镜片）
- ocular lens（接目透镜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **look through the eyepiece / adjust the eyepiece / telescope eyepiece / microscope eyepiece**

👉 Example: The astronomer carefully adjusted the eyepiece to bring the distant nebula into sharper focus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students were instructed to look through the **eyepiece** of the microscope to observe the cellular structures in detail.
   学生们被要求通过显微镜的目镜观察细胞结构的细节。

2. The researcher replaced the standard **eyepiece** with a high-magnification lens to better examine the mineral specimen.
   研究人员用高倍镜片替换了标准目镜，以便更好地检查矿物标本。

3. By rotating the **eyepiece**, the technician was able to correct the slight distortion that had impaired visibility.
   通过旋转目镜，技术人员得以纠正影响可见度的轻微失真。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eye-piece" literally — it is the piece of the instrument that your eye looks through, the eye's piece of the puzzle.
把"eyepiece"直接理解——它就是仪器上你的眼睛透过去看的那个部件，眼睛的那一"片"。
