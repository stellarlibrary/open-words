# ⚡ kinetic

🔊 /kɪˈnɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or resulting from motion; characterized by movement or dynamic energy
**中文：** adj. 运动的；动力的；活跃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dynamic（动态的）
- active（活跃的）
- energetic（充满活力的）
- mobile（运动的）
- motile（能动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **kinetic energy / kinetic art / kinetic theory / kinetic force / kinetic sculpture**

👉 Example: The kinetic energy of the moving object was converted into heat upon impact with the surface.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist demonstrated how **kinetic** energy increases proportionally with the square of an object's velocity.
   这位物理学家展示了动能如何与物体速度的平方成正比增加。

2. Modern museums frequently feature **kinetic** sculptures that captivate audiences through continuous and unpredictable movement.
   现代博物馆经常展出动态雕塑，通过持续且不可预测的运动吸引观众。

3. The engineer calculated the **kinetic** force required to propel the spacecraft beyond the gravitational pull of the planet.
   工程师计算了将航天器推出行星引力所需的动力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "kinetic" as related to "cinema" — both come from the Greek word "kinein" meaning "to move," and movies are all about movement.
把"kinetic"和"cinema"（电影）联系起来——两者都源自希腊语"kinein"（运动），而电影就是关于运动的。
