# 🚑 casualty

🔊 /ˈkæʒuəlti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person killed or injured in a war or accident; an unfortunate event or mishap; an emergency department in a hospital
**中文：** n. 意外事故；伤亡人员；急诊室

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- victim（受害者）
- fatality（死亡事故）
- loss（损失）
- injured party（伤者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **casualty report / civilian casualties / casualty rate / casualty ward / suffer casualties**

👉 Example: The conflict resulted in heavy casualties on both sides of the front line.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military spokesperson confirmed that the operation resulted in minimal **casualty** figures among allied forces stationed abroad.
   军方发言人证实，此次行动中驻外盟军的伤亡人数极少。

2. Independent journalism became the first **casualty** of the government's sweeping new censorship regulations and media restrictions.
   独立新闻业成为政府全面新审查法规和媒体限制的首个牺牲品。

3. Emergency workers rushed the **casualty** victims to nearby hospitals after the industrial explosion devastated the manufacturing district.
   工业爆炸摧毁了制造业区后，急救人员迅速将伤亡人员送往附近医院。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "casualty" as a "casual tea" gone wrong — what started as a casual gathering turned into an accident with casualties.
把"casualty"想成一场出了差错的"随意茶会"（casual tea）——原本是一场随意的聚会，结果变成了有伤亡的事故。
