# ☠️ bane

🔊 /beɪn/<br>
🏷️ *n. | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A cause of great distress, ruin, or death; something that causes ongoing annoyance or suffering; historically, a deadly poison.

**中文：** 毒药；祸害；克星；毁灭的根源；令人苦恼的事物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- scourge（灾祸）
- curse（诅咒）
- plague（瘟疫）
- nemesis（克星）
- affliction（折磨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Bane" is most commonly used in the phrase "the bane of one's existence," meaning a persistent source of annoyance or suffering. The original meaning of "poison" is now archaic but survives in compound words like "wolfsbane" and "henbane" (poisonous plants). It carries a strong negative connotation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Procrastination has long been the **bane** of students who struggle to complete their assignments before approaching deadlines.
   拖延症长期以来一直是那些难以在截止日期前完成作业的学生的**祸害**。

2. Invasive species have become the **bane** of conservationists working tirelessly to protect endangered native wildlife populations.
   入侵物种已成为那些为保护濒危本地野生动物种群而不懈努力的环保人士的**克星**。

3. Corruption remains the **bane** of economic development in many nations, undermining public trust and discouraging foreign investment.
   腐败仍然是许多国家经济发展的**祸害**，破坏了公众信任并阻碍了外国投资。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of the classic villain "Bane" from Batman — he is Batman's greatest curse and source of suffering. The word "bane" means exactly that: a source of ruin or torment.

想想蝙蝠侠中的经典反派"Bane"——他是蝙蝠侠最大的诅咒和痛苦之源。单词"bane"的意思正是如此：毁灭或折磨的根源。
