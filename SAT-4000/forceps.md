# 🏥 forceps

🔊 /ˈfɔːrsɛps/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A pair of pincers or tongs used in surgery or in a laboratory for gripping and holding objects
**中文：** n. 钳子；医用镊子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pincers（钳子）
- tongs（夹子）
- tweezers（镊子）
- clamp（夹钳）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a pair of forceps / surgical forceps / forceps delivery / grip with forceps**

👉 Example: The surgeon carefully gripped the fragment with forceps and removed it from the wound.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon used sterilized **forceps** to extract the small piece of shrapnel embedded deep within the patient's tissue.
   外科医生用消毒过的镊子取出深嵌在患者组织中的小块弹片。

2. Laboratory technicians must handle delicate specimens with **forceps** to avoid contamination from direct skin contact.
   实验室技术人员必须用镊子处理精细的标本，以避免直接皮肤接触造成的污染。

3. The obstetrician determined that a **forceps** delivery was necessary to assist the infant through the birth canal safely.
   产科医生判定需要进行产钳助产，以帮助婴儿安全通过产道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "force + ps (grip)" — forceps use force to grip and hold things tightly, like a powerful pinch.
把"forceps"想成"force + ps（夹住）"——镊子用力夹紧和握住东西，就像有力的一夹。
