# 🔄 regress

🔊 /rɪˈɡrɛs/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To return to a former or less developed state; to move backward. (n.) The act of going back; return or reversion.
**中文：** v. 逆行，倒退；复归 n. 回归；退回

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revert（回复）
- retrogress（倒退）
- deteriorate（恶化）
- relapse（复发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regress to / regress into / regress toward / emotional regress**

👉 Example: Without consistent practice, students may regress to earlier stages of language proficiency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient began to **regress** after discontinuing the prescribed medication, returning to previously observed behavioral patterns.
   患者在停用处方药物后开始倒退，回到了之前观察到的行为模式。

2. Economists warned that the nation's economy could **regress** significantly if the proposed fiscal reforms were not implemented promptly.
   经济学家警告说，如果不及时实施拟议的财政改革，该国经济可能会严重倒退。

3. The therapist noted that under extreme stress, individuals often **regress** to coping mechanisms developed during early childhood.
   治疗师指出，在极端压力下，个人往往会退回到童年早期形成的应对机制。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-gress" as "go back again" — "re" means back and "gress" (from Latin gradi) means to step, so regress means to step backward.
把"regress"想成"re（再次）+ gress（步行）"——即再次往回走，表示倒退、退步。
