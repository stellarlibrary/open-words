# 🌍 anthropology

🔊 /ˌænθrəˈpɑːlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The scientific study of human beings, including their origins, development, customs, beliefs, and social relationships.

**中文：** 人类学（对人类的起源、发展、风俗、信仰及社会关系的科学研究）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- ethnology 民族学
- social science 社会科学
- human science 人文科学
- ethnography 民族志学

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **cultural anthropology**, **physical anthropology**, **study anthropology**, **a degree in anthropology**, **department of anthropology**.

> Example: She decided to major in **anthropology** to understand diverse human cultures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university established a new department of **anthropology** to expand its humanities research programs.
   该大学成立了一个新的人类学系，以扩展其人文研究项目。

2. His coursework in **anthropology** provided valuable insights into the migration patterns of early civilizations.
   他在人类学方面的课程为了解早期文明的迁移模式提供了宝贵的见解。

3. Modern **anthropology** increasingly employs advanced genetic analysis to trace the origins of human populations.
   现代人类学越来越多地采用先进的基因分析来追溯人类群体的起源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anthropo-" (human, from Greek *anthropos*) + "-logy" (study of). **Anthropology** is simply "the study of humankind" in all its dimensions — biological, cultural, and social.

"anthropo-"（人类，源自希腊语 *anthropos*）+"-logy"（学科）。**Anthropology** 就是"关于人类的学问"——涵盖生物、文化和社会各个维度。
