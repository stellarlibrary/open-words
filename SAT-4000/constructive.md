# 🛠️ constructive

🔊 /kənˈstrʌktɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Serving a useful purpose; tending to build up or improve; having a positive and helpful effect
**中文：** adj. 建设性的；有助益的；推定的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- productive（有成效的）
- beneficial（有益的）
- positive（积极的）
- helpful（有帮助的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **constructive criticism / constructive feedback / constructive dialogue / constructive approach**

👉 Example: The mentor offered constructive feedback that helped the student significantly improve her thesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mediator encouraged both parties to engage in **constructive** dialogue rather than resorting to adversarial legal proceedings.
   调解员鼓励双方进行建设性对话，而不是诉诸对抗性的法律程序。

2. Peer review functions most effectively when scholars provide **constructive** criticism that identifies both strengths and areas for improvement.
   当学者们提供既指出优点又指出改进方面的建设性批评时，同行评审最为有效。

3. The committee proposed several **constructive** measures to address the persistent achievement gap in urban public school districts.
   委员会提出了几项建设性措施，以解决城市公立学区中持续存在的成绩差距问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "construct-ive" — something constructive helps to "construct" or build something better, as opposed to being destructive.
把"constructive"想成"construct（建造）+ ive"——建设性的东西有助于"建造"或改善事物，与破坏性的相反。
