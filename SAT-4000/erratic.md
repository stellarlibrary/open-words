# 🌀 erratic

🔊 /ɪˈrætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable and inconsistent in behavior or quality
**中文：** adj. 不稳定的；古怪的；不规律的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unpredictable（不可预测的）
- inconsistent（不一致的）
- irregular（不规律的）
- volatile（多变的）
- capricious（反复无常的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **erratic behavior / erratic performance / erratic weather / erratic heartbeat**

👉 Example: The employee's erratic performance led to a formal review by the management team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient's **erratic** heartbeat prompted the cardiologist to order a comprehensive series of diagnostic examinations immediately.
   患者不规律的心跳促使心脏病专家立即下令进行一系列全面的诊断检查。

2. Investors grew increasingly alarmed by the **erratic** fluctuations in the stock market throughout the volatile fiscal quarter.
   在动荡的财政季度中，投资者对股市不稳定的波动越来越感到担忧。

3. The scientist noted that the **erratic** migration patterns of the birds suggested significant environmental changes in the region.
   科学家注意到，鸟类不规律的迁徙模式表明该地区发生了重大的环境变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "erratic" as "err-atic" — full of errors and randomness, like something that keeps going wrong in unpredictable ways.
把"erratic"想成"err（错误）-atic"——充满错误和随机性，就像事情以不可预测的方式不断出错。
