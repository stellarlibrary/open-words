# 💎 bauble

🔊 /ˈbɔːbl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A showy but worthless or trivial ornament; a small, cheap piece of jewelry or decoration
**中文：** n. 小玩意；美观而无价值的饰物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trinket（小饰品）
- knickknack（小玩意）
- ornament（装饰品）
- gewgaw（华而不实的东西）
- trifle（琐碎之物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cheap baubles / glittering baubles / Christmas baubles / mere baubles**

👉 Example: The antique shop was filled with glittering baubles that attracted tourists but held little real value.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that material wealth often amounts to nothing more than **baubles** that distract from true fulfillment.
   这位哲学家认为，物质财富往往不过是分散人们对真正满足感注意力的小玩意。

2. Street vendors displayed colorful **baubles** along the market square, hoping to attract the attention of passing tourists.
   街头小贩在集市广场上展示着五颜六色的小饰品，希望吸引过路游客的注意。

3. Critics dismissed the extravagant decorations as mere **baubles**, arguing that the exhibition lacked genuine artistic substance.
   评论家们将那些奢华的装饰品斥为华而不实的小玩意，认为展览缺乏真正的艺术内涵。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bauble" as "bob + able" — something that just bobbles around, eye-catching but lightweight and worthless.
把"bauble"想成"bob + able"——一个能晃来晃去的东西，吸引眼球但轻飘飘且毫无价值。
