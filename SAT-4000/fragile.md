# 🏺 fragile

🔊 /ˈfrædʒəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easily broken, damaged, or destroyed; delicate and vulnerable
**中文：** 脆的；易碎的；脆弱的，易受伤害的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- delicate（精致的；脆弱的）
- brittle（易碎的）
- frail（虚弱的）
- vulnerable（易受伤害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fragile ecosystem / fragile economy / fragile health / handle with care (fragile)**

👉 Example: The fragile vase shattered into pieces the moment it slipped from her hands.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The country's **fragile** democracy was threatened by the growing influence of authoritarian movements within the government.
   这个国家脆弱的民主制度受到政府内部日益增长的威权运动的威胁。

2. Scientists warned that the **fragile** coral reef ecosystem could collapse entirely if ocean temperatures continued to rise.
   科学家警告说，如果海洋温度继续上升，脆弱的珊瑚礁生态系统可能会完全崩溃。

3. The patient remained in a **fragile** condition after surgery, requiring constant monitoring by the medical team.
   患者术后仍处于脆弱状态，需要医疗团队的持续监护。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fragile" as containing "frag" — like a fragment. Something fragile easily breaks into fragments.
把"fragile"想成包含"frag"——像fragment（碎片）。易碎的东西容易碎成碎片。
