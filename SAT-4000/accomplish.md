# ✅ accomplish

🔊 /əˈkɑːm.plɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To succeed in completing or achieving something.
**中文：** 完成；实现；达到

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- achieve（达成）
- fulfill（实现）
- attain（获得）
- complete（完成）
- execute（执行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accomplish a goal/task/mission/objective**

👉 Example: She accomplished her mission well ahead of the deadline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team **accomplished** a remarkable feat by developing the vaccine in record time.
   研究团队在创纪录的时间内研发出疫苗，完成了一项非凡的壮举。

2. Through persistent effort and discipline, he **accomplished** every objective outlined in the strategic plan.
   通过坚持不懈的努力和自律，他实现了战略计划中列出的每一项目标。

3. The diplomat **accomplished** what many considered impossible by brokering peace between the rival nations.
   这位外交官促成了敌对国家之间的和平，完成了许多人认为不可能的事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "ac-COMP-lish" — you complete something to the full, making it "complete" (com = fully + plish from "plere" = fill).
accomplish 中的 "compli" 类似 complete（完成），加上前缀 ac- 表示"朝向"，即"朝着完成的方向走"——实现目标。
