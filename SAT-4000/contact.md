# 🤝 contact

🔊 /ˈkɑːntækt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of physical touching; communication or a connection between people or organizations. (v.) To get in touch with; to communicate with
**中文：** n. 接触，联系；联络人 v. 使接触，联系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- connection（联系）
- communication（沟通）
- reach（联络）
- touch（接触）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make contact / come into contact / contact information / lose contact with / in contact with**

👉 Example: Please contact the admissions office directly if you have questions regarding your application status.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists were careful not to make direct **contact** with the fragile artifacts discovered in the excavation site.
   考古学家们小心翼翼地避免直接接触在发掘现场发现的脆弱文物。

2. The embassy urged all citizens abroad to **contact** their nearest consulate immediately in the event of a natural disaster.
   大使馆敦促所有海外公民在发生自然灾害时立即联系最近的领事馆。

3. Prolonged **contact** with certain industrial chemicals has been linked to an increased risk of respiratory illness among factory workers.
   长期接触某些工业化学品与工厂工人呼吸系统疾病风险增加有关。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-TACT" — "con" means together and "tact" relates to touch, so contact literally means "touching together" or making a connection.
把"contact"想成"con（共同）+ tact（触碰）"——字面意思是"一起触碰"，引申为建立联系或接触。
