# 🌺 exotic

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɒtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Originating in or characteristic of a distant foreign country; strikingly unusual or strange in appearance or effect
**中文：** adj. 异国情调的；外来的；奇异的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- foreign（外国的）
- unusual（不寻常的）
- striking（引人注目的）
- outlandish（古怪的）
- alien（异域的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exotic species / exotic destination / exotic beauty / exotic flavors / exotic plants**

👉 Example: The botanical garden features an impressive collection of exotic plants from tropical regions around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The introduction of **exotic** species into local ecosystems has caused significant disruption to native wildlife populations worldwide.
   外来物种引入当地生态系统已在全球范围内对本地野生动物种群造成了严重破坏。

2. Travelers are often drawn to **exotic** destinations that offer cultural experiences vastly different from their own familiar surroundings.
   旅行者常常被那些提供与自身熟悉环境截然不同的文化体验的异国目的地所吸引。

3. The chef's menu featured an array of **exotic** spices sourced from remote villages in Southeast Asia and northern Africa.
   主厨的菜单展示了一系列来自东南亚和北非偏远村庄的异国香料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exotic" as "exit-ic" — something so different it seems to have exited the ordinary world and arrived from a faraway land.
把"exotic"想成"exit-ic"——某样东西如此不同，仿佛它已离开了普通世界，从遥远的地方来到这里。
