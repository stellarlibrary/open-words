# 🧪 sebaceous

🔊 /sɪˈbeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or secreting sebum or fat; fatty or greasy in nature
**中文：** adj. 脂肪的，脂肪分泌的，脂肪过多的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oily（油性的）
- greasy（油腻的）
- fatty（脂肪的）
- oleaginous（含油的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sebaceous gland / sebaceous cyst / sebaceous secretion / sebaceous duct**

👉 Example: The dermatologist explained that overactive sebaceous glands were the primary cause of the patient's persistent acne.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **sebaceous** glands in human skin produce an oily substance called sebum that helps moisturize and protect the epidermis.
   人体皮肤中的皮脂腺分泌一种叫做皮脂的油性物质，帮助滋润和保护表皮。

2. A blocked **sebaceous** duct can lead to the formation of painful cysts that may require medical intervention to resolve.
   堵塞的皮脂管道可能导致形成疼痛的囊肿，可能需要医疗干预才能解决。

3. Researchers discovered that certain hormonal changes during adolescence dramatically increase **sebaceous** activity throughout the body.
   研究人员发现，青春期某些激素变化会显著增加全身的皮脂分泌活动。
