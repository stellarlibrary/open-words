# 🌟 beam

🔊 /biːm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A ray or shaft of light; a long, sturdy piece of timber or metal used in construction. (v.) To emit light or radiate; to smile radiantly
**中文：** n. 横梁；光线；光束；电波 v. 发送；照射；流露喜悦

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ray（光线）
- shaft（光柱）
- girder（大梁）
- radiate（辐射）
- gleam（闪光）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beam of light / laser beam / beam with pride / steel beam / beam a signal**

👉 Example: A narrow beam of sunlight pierced through the clouds and illuminated the ancient cathedral.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The lighthouse projected a powerful **beam** of light across the dark waters, guiding vessels safely through the treacherous strait.
   灯塔在黑暗的水面上投射出一道强烈的光束，引导船只安全穿越危险的海峡。

2. The architect specified reinforced steel **beams** to ensure the skyscraper could withstand seismic forces of considerable magnitude.
   建筑师指定使用加固钢梁，以确保摩天大楼能够承受相当大的地震力。

3. The proud grandmother **beamed** with joy as her granddaughter delivered a flawless valedictorian speech at the ceremony.
   骄傲的祖母在孙女于典礼上发表完美的毕业生代表致辞时满脸喜悦地微笑着。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "beam" as a "B-E-A-M" — Bright Energy Arriving Magnificently — whether it is a beam of light or a beaming smile.
把"beam"想成"B-E-A-M"——Bright Energy Arriving Magnificently（光辉能量华丽到来）——无论是一束光还是灿烂的微笑。
