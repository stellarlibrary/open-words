# 🔶 sector

🔊 /ˈsɛktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A department; a fan-shaped area; a quadrant instrument; a function ruler. (v.) To divide into fan-shaped parts
**中文：** n. 扇形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- domain（领域）
- division（部门）
- segment（部分）
- zone（区域）
- branch（分支）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public sector / private sector / financial sector / sector of the economy**

👉 Example: The technology sector has experienced unprecedented growth over the past decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government announced sweeping reforms aimed at revitalizing the manufacturing **sector** and reducing dependence on imports.
   政府宣布了旨在振兴制造业部门并减少进口依赖的全面改革。

2. Analysts predict that the energy **sector** will undergo significant transformation as renewable sources become increasingly cost-effective.
   分析师预测，随着可再生能源变得越来越具成本效益，能源部门将经历重大转型。

3. The private **sector** contributed substantially to the relief effort, donating resources and mobilizing volunteers throughout the region.
   私营部门为救济工作做出了重大贡献，在整个地区捐赠资源并动员志愿者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sect-or" — like a "section" of a circle or economy, a sector is a distinct slice or area within a larger whole.
把"sector"想成圆或经济体的一个"section（部分）"——扇区是更大整体中的一个独立部分。
