# 🤐 reserved

🔊 /rɪˈzɜːrvd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Slow to reveal emotions or opinions; restrained in manner; kept for a particular purpose or person
**中文：** adj. 保留的，预订的；缄默的，有节制的，冷淡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reticent（沉默的）
- aloof（冷淡的）
- restrained（克制的）
- taciturn（寡言的）
- demure（矜持的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reserved manner / reserved seat / naturally reserved / remain reserved**

👉 Example: Despite her reserved demeanor, she possessed a sharp intellect that emerged during academic discussions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat maintained a **reserved** posture throughout the negotiations, carefully concealing his delegation's true strategic priorities.
   这位外交官在整个谈判过程中保持着矜持的姿态，小心翼翼地隐藏着代表团的真正战略重点。

2. Her **reserved** nature often led acquaintances to mistake her thoughtfulness for indifference or social disinterest.
   她沉默寡言的性格常常使熟人误将她的深思熟虑当作冷漠或对社交的不感兴趣。

3. Several front-row seats were **reserved** exclusively for distinguished guests attending the international academic symposium that evening.
   几个前排座位专门为出席当晚国际学术研讨会的贵宾预留。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reserved" as "re-served" — someone who keeps serving themselves back, holding everything inside, staying quiet and restrained.
把"reserved"想成"re-served"——一个不断把自己收回来的人，把一切藏在心里，保持安静和克制。
