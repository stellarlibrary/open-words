# 🧪 adulterate

🔊 /əˈdʌltəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make impure by adding inferior or harmful substances; (adj.) contaminated or debased
**中文：** v. 掺假；掺杂劣质物 adj. 掺杂的；不纯的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contaminate（污染）
- taint（玷污）
- debase（降低品质）
- corrupt（使变质）
- dilute（稀释）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adulterate with / adulterated food / adulterated products / deliberately adulterated**

👉 Example: The merchant was prosecuted for attempting to adulterate premium olive oil with cheaper vegetable oils.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Unscrupulous vendors often **adulterate** milk with water and chemical additives to increase their profit margins.
   不法商贩经常在牛奶中掺水和化学添加剂以增加利润空间。

2. The investigation confirmed that the supplier had systematically **adulterated** the honey with high-fructose corn syrup.
   调查证实该供应商曾系统性地在蜂蜜中掺入高果糖玉米糖浆。

3. Governments worldwide have enacted strict legislation to prevent manufacturers from **adulterating** pharmaceutical products with untested compounds.
   世界各国政府已制定严格法律，防止制造商在药品中掺入未经检测的化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adulterate" as "a-DULL-ter-ATE" — someone ate something that was made dull (impure) by adding bad ingredients.
把"adulterate"想成"吃了(ate)被搞坏(dull)的东西"——掺假就是把纯净的东西变得"不纯"。
