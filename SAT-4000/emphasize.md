# 📢 emphasize

🔊 /ˈemfəsaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To give special importance or prominence to something in speaking or writing; to stress
**中文：** v. 强调；着重

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stress（强调）
- highlight（突出）
- underscore（强调）
- accentuate（着重）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emphasize the importance of / emphasize that / strongly emphasize / emphasize a point**

👉 Example: The report strongly emphasizes the need for immediate investment in renewable energy infrastructure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The director sought to **emphasize** the urgent necessity of adopting sustainable practices throughout every level of corporate operations.
   该主管试图强调在公司运营的各个层面采取可持续做法的紧迫必要性。

2. Health officials continue to **emphasize** that regular exercise and a balanced diet are essential for long-term cardiovascular wellness.
   卫生官员继续强调定期锻炼和均衡饮食对长期心血管健康至关重要。

3. The author chose to **emphasize** the moral complexities of the protagonist rather than presenting a simplistic portrayal of heroism.
   作者选择强调主人公的道德复杂性，而非呈现一种简单化的英雄主义描绘。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "emphasize" as "em-PHA-size" — you increase the "size" of an idea's importance so everyone notices it.
把"emphasize"想成"em-PHA-size（大小）"——你增大一个想法的重要性"尺寸"，让每个人都注意到它。
