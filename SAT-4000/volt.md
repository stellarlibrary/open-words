# ⚡ volt

🔊 /voʊlt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The SI unit of electromotive force, equal to the potential difference that would drive one ampere of current against one ohm of resistance
**中文：** n. 伏特（电压单位）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unit of voltage（电压单位）
- electromotive unit（电动势单位）
- potential difference unit（电位差单位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **measured in volts / high-volt / 120 volts / volt meter**

👉 Example: Standard household electrical outlets in the United States supply approximately 120 volts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer measured the circuit and confirmed that the battery was producing exactly twelve **volts** of output.
   工程师测量了电路，确认电池正好产生十二伏特的输出。

2. Electric vehicles typically operate on battery systems ranging from three hundred to eight hundred **volts** in capacity.
   电动汽车通常使用三百至八百伏特容量的电池系统运行。

3. Alessandro Volta's pioneering contributions to electrical science led to the naming of the **volt** in his honor.
   亚历山德罗·伏特对电学的开创性贡献使得"伏特"这一单位以他的名字命名。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "volt" as named after Alessandro Volta, the inventor of the electric battery — volt measures the electric "push" or pressure.
把"volt"联想到电池发明者亚历山德罗·伏特——伏特测量的是电的"推力"或压力。
