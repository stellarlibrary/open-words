# 💥 ravage

🔊 /ˈrævɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause severe and extensive damage to; to devastate or plunder. (n.) The destructive effects of something
**中文：** v. 毁坏；破坏；掠夺 n. 蹂躏，破坏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- devastate（毁灭）
- destroy（破坏）
- ruin（毁坏）
- plunder（掠夺）
- wreak havoc（造成浩劫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ravage the land / ravaged by war / ravages of time / ravaged by disease / ravages of nature**

👉 Example: The coastal village was ravaged by the hurricane, leaving thousands of residents without shelter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged civil war **ravaged** the nation's infrastructure, displacing millions of civilians and destroying entire communities.
   旷日持久的内战摧毁了国家的基础设施，使数百万平民流离失所，整个社区被摧毁。

2. Historians documented the **ravages** of the plague, which decimated nearly one-third of the continent's population in the fourteenth century.
   历史学家记录了瘟疫的蹂躏，它在十四世纪夺去了近三分之一大陆人口的生命。

3. Deforestation continues to **ravage** tropical ecosystems, threatening biodiversity and accelerating the pace of global climate change.
   森林砍伐继续破坏热带生态系统，威胁生物多样性并加速全球气候变化的步伐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rav-age" — like "rave" plus "age," as if a raging force has been destroying things for ages, leaving devastation behind.
把"ravage"想成"rave（狂暴）+ age（时代）"——狂暴的力量经过漫长岁月的破坏，留下满目疮痍。
