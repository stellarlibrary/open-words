# 💧 osmosis

🔊 /ɑːzˈmoʊsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process by which molecules pass through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution to a more concentrated one; gradual or unconscious absorption of knowledge or ideas.
**中文：** n. 渗透；渗透性；渗透作用；潜移默化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diffusion（扩散）
- permeation（渗透）
- absorption（吸收）
- assimilation（同化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **by osmosis / cultural osmosis / learn through osmosis / osmosis of ideas**

👉 Example: Children often acquire language skills through a kind of osmosis rather than formal instruction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biology students observed **osmosis** occurring as water molecules passed through the semipermeable membrane into the saline solution.
   生物学学生观察到水分子通过半透膜渗透到盐溶液中的渗透过程。

2. Growing up in a multilingual household, she seemed to learn three languages by **osmosis** rather than deliberate study.
   在一个多语言家庭中长大，她似乎通过潜移默化而非刻意学习掌握了三门语言。

3. The cultural values of the organization were transmitted through **osmosis** as new employees absorbed the norms of their colleagues.
   该组织的文化价值观通过潜移默化传递，新员工逐渐吸收了同事们的规范。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "os-MO-sis" — "MO" sounds like "more," and in osmosis, water moves toward the "more" concentrated side, seeping through slowly.
把"osmosis"中的"MO"联想成"more"（更多）——在渗透作用中，水向浓度"更高"的一侧移动，缓慢渗透过去。
