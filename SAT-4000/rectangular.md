# 📏 rectangular

🔊 /rekˈtæŋɡjələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the shape of a rectangle; placed or arranged at right angles
**中文：** adj. 矩形的；成直角的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oblong（长方形的）
- right-angled（直角的）
- orthogonal（正交的）
- quadrilateral（四边形的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rectangular shape / rectangular grid / rectangular coordinates / rectangular prism**

👉 Example: The engineer specified a rectangular cross-section for the structural steel beams in the design plans.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surveyor confirmed that the building lot was perfectly **rectangular**, measuring exactly fifty meters by thirty meters on each side.
   测量员确认该建筑用地是完美的矩形，每边精确地量为五十米乘三十米。

2. Scientists use a **rectangular** coordinate system to plot data points and analyze spatial relationships in three-dimensional experiments.
   科学家使用直角坐标系来绘制数据点并分析三维实验中的空间关系。

3. The landscape architect proposed a series of **rectangular** garden beds arranged symmetrically around the central courtyard fountain.
   景观建筑师提议在中央庭院喷泉周围对称排列一系列矩形花坛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rectangular" as "rectangle + -ar" — simply the adjective form of rectangle, describing anything shaped like or relating to a rectangle.
把"rectangular"想成"rectangle + -ar"——就是"矩形"的形容词形式，描述任何矩形状的或与矩形有关的事物。
