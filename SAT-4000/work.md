# ⚙️ work

🔊 /wɜːrk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Activity involving effort; a task or job; a product of effort; in physics, the transfer of energy. (v.) To perform labor; to operate or function; to bring about a result
**中文：** n. 工作；功；产品；著作；行为 v. 使工作；操作；经营

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- labor（劳动）
- toil（辛劳）
- endeavor（努力）
- operate（运作）
- function（运转）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **work on / work toward / at work / work of art / do work (physics) / body of work**

👉 Example: In physics, work is defined as the product of force and displacement in the direction of the force.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer calculated that the mechanical system would perform approximately five hundred joules of **work** per cycle.
   工程师计算出该机械系统每个循环大约做五百焦耳的功。

2. Her complete body of **work** spans four decades and includes over two hundred published scholarly articles and monographs.
   她的全部著作跨越四十年，包括两百多篇已发表的学术文章和专著。

3. The conservation team continued to **work** tirelessly to restore the damaged Renaissance fresco to its original splendor.
   修复团队继续不懈地工作，将受损的文艺复兴时期壁画恢复到原来的辉煌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "work" in physics as "W = F x d" — work equals force times distance, the fundamental formula that defines this everyday word scientifically.
把"work"与物理公式"W = F x d"联系起来——功等于力乘以距离，这个日常词汇在科学中有精确的定义。
