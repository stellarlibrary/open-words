# ⚖️ ethical

🔊 /ˈɛθɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to moral principles or the branch of knowledge dealing with these; morally correct and honorable
**中文：** adj. 与伦理有关的；道德的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- moral（道德的）
- principled（有原则的）
- virtuous（品德高尚的）
- righteous（正直的）
- honorable（光荣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ethical standards / ethical dilemma / ethical behavior / ethical responsibility / ethical considerations**

👉 Example: The committee raised serious ethical concerns about the proposed experiment on human subjects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation faced mounting pressure to adopt more **ethical** business practices in response to public scrutiny and regulatory demands.
   面对公众审视和监管要求，该公司面临越来越大的压力，需要采取更加道德的商业行为。

2. Medical professionals must navigate complex **ethical** dilemmas when patients' wishes conflict with established clinical guidelines and protocols.
   当患者的意愿与既定的临床指南和规程发生冲突时，医疗专业人员必须应对复杂的伦理困境。

3. The philosopher questioned whether it is **ethical** to prioritize economic growth at the expense of environmental sustainability and biodiversity.
   这位哲学家质疑以牺牲环境可持续性和生物多样性为代价来优先追求经济增长是否合乎道德。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ethical" as "ETHIC + al" — related to ethics, the study of what is morally right and wrong in human conduct.
把"ethical"想成"ETHIC + al"——与伦理学相关，即研究人类行为中何为道德上的对与错。
