# 🔥 ignition

🔊 /ɪɡˈnɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of setting something on fire or starting combustion; the mechanism in a vehicle that starts the engine
**中文：** n. 点火；点燃；灼烧；点火开关；点火装置

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- combustion（燃烧）
- kindling（点燃）
- inflammation（着火）
- firing（点火）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ignition switch / ignition key / ignition point / turn the ignition / ignition temperature**

👉 Example: The mechanic determined that the problem was caused by a faulty ignition system in the vehicle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ignition** of the experimental rocket fuel occurred precisely on schedule, propelling the spacecraft into its designated orbital trajectory.
   实验火箭燃料的点火按计划准时进行，将航天器推入其指定的轨道。

2. Safety inspectors determined that the warehouse fire was caused by the accidental **ignition** of improperly stored volatile chemical compounds.
   安全检查员确定仓库火灾是由不当存放的挥发性化合物意外点燃引起的。

3. The engineer redesigned the **ignition** system to improve fuel efficiency and reduce harmful emissions produced during the combustion process.
   工程师重新设计了点火系统，以提高燃油效率并减少燃烧过程中产生的有害排放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ignite + ion" — ignition is the noun form of ignite, the moment something catches fire or starts up.
把"ignition"想成"ignite（点燃）+ ion（名词后缀）"——就是点燃的那一刻，或事物启动的瞬间。
