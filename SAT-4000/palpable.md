# ✋ palpable

🔊 /ˈpælpəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to be touched or felt; so intense as to be almost physically perceived; readily noticeable
**中文：** adj. 明显的；可感知的；易觉察的；可触知的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tangible（可触知的）
- perceptible（可感知的）
- noticeable（显而易见的）
- unmistakable（明确的）
- evident（明显的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **palpable tension / palpable excitement / palpable sense of / palpable relief**

👉 Example: The palpable tension in the courtroom intensified as the jury prepared to deliver the verdict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. There was a **palpable** sense of anxiety among the delegates as negotiations reached their most critical phase.
   当谈判进入最关键阶段时，代表们之间弥漫着一种明显的焦虑感。

2. The relief in the emergency room became **palpable** when the surgeons announced that the operation had been successful.
   当外科医生宣布手术成功时，急诊室里的如释重负之感显而易见。

3. Her disdain for the proposed policy changes was so **palpable** that even her political allies felt uncomfortable.
   她对拟议政策变动的蔑视如此明显，连她的政治盟友都感到不自在。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "palp-able" — "palp" relates to touching (like palpation in medicine), so palpable means something you can almost physically feel.
想想"palp-able"——"palp"与触摸有关（如医学中的触诊），所以palpable意味着你几乎可以亲身感受到的东西。
