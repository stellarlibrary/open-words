# 🦶 biped

🔊 /ˈbaɪpɛd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A two-footed animal. (adj.) Having two feet
**中文：** n. 两足动物 adj. 有两足的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- two-legged creature（两足生物）
- bipedal organism（两足有机体）
- upright walker（直立行走者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bipedal locomotion / bipedal gait / a biped species / evolved as bipeds**

👉 Example: Humans are the most widespread bipeds on Earth, having adapted to nearly every environment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. As obligate **bipeds**, humans rely exclusively on two limbs for locomotion, unlike most other primates.
   作为专性两足动物，人类完全依赖两条腿行走，与大多数其他灵长类动物不同。

2. The paleontologist discovered fossilized tracks suggesting that the ancient **biped** walked with a remarkably modern stride.
   古生物学家发现的化石足迹表明，这种古代两足动物的步态与现代人惊人地相似。

3. Engineers designed the robot as a **biped** to navigate terrain originally built for human locomotion patterns.
   工程师们将这款机器人设计为两足行走的形态，以便在专为人类行走模式建造的地形中移动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "ped" (foot, as in pedal) — a creature that walks on two feet.
把"biped"拆成"bi-"（二）+"ped"（脚，如pedal踏板）——用两只脚走路的生物。
