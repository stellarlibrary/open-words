# 🛢️ crude

🔊 /kruːd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) In a natural or raw state; not yet processed or refined; lacking sophistication or subtlety. (n.) Crude oil; unrefined petroleum
**中文：** adj. 粗糙的；天然的，未加工的 n. 原油

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- raw（未加工的）
- unrefined（未精炼的）
- coarse（粗糙的）
- rough（粗略的）
- primitive（原始的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crude oil / crude method / crude estimate / crude humor / crude materials**

👉 Example: The factory processes crude materials into refined products suitable for industrial and consumer use.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The early prototype was a **crude** but functional device that demonstrated the fundamental principles of the invention.
   早期的原型机是一个粗糙但实用的装置，展示了该发明的基本原理。

2. Global markets reacted sharply when the price of **crude** oil surged beyond one hundred dollars per barrel.
   当原油价格飙升至每桶一百美元以上时，全球市场反应剧烈。

3. Scientists developed a **crude** approximation model before refining their calculations with more sophisticated computational methods.
   科学家们在用更精密的计算方法完善计算之前，先开发了一个粗略的近似模型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crude" as "cr-ude" — like "crew dug" raw materials straight from the earth, unprocessed and rough.
把"crude"想成"crew dug（工人挖掘）"——就像工人从地下直接挖出的原材料，未经加工且粗糙。
