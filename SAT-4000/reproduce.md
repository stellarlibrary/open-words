# 🔄 reproduce

🔊 /ˌriːprəˈdjuːs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To produce a copy or representation of; to generate offspring; to recreate or bring back into existence in the mind or reality
**中文：** v. 复制；再生；生殖；翻版；再现；使…在脑海中重现

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- duplicate（复制）
- replicate（复制）
- propagate（繁殖）
- recreate（再创造）
- copy（拷贝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reproduce results / reproduce offspring / reproduce an image / reproduce the conditions / reproduce faithfully**

👉 Example: High-quality printers can reproduce photographic images with exceptional clarity and color accuracy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist managed to **reproduce** the masterpiece with such precision that experts struggled to distinguish the copy from the original.
   这位艺术家复制名作的精度之高，连专家都难以区分副本和原作。

2. Certain species of bacteria can **reproduce** at astonishing rates, doubling their population within a matter of mere minutes.
   某些细菌能以惊人的速度繁殖，在短短几分钟内就能使种群数量翻倍。

3. Independent laboratories were unable to **reproduce** the controversial findings, raising significant questions about the methodology employed.
   独立实验室无法再现这些有争议的发现，引发了对所采用方法的重大质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-produce" as "produce again" — whether it is producing a copy, producing offspring, or producing an experience again in your mind.
把"reproduce"想成"re"（再次）+"produce"（产生）——无论是再次制造副本、产生后代，还是在脑海中再次重现一段经历。
