# 🌟 kismet

🔊 /ˈkɪzmɛt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Destiny or fate; the will of God or a predetermined course of events believed to be beyond human control
**中文：** n. 天命；命运

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fate（命运）
- destiny（天命）
- providence（天意）
- fortune（运气）
- predestination（宿命）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **believe in kismet / a matter of kismet / accept one's kismet / by kismet**

👉 Example: She attributed their chance meeting to kismet rather than mere coincidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novelist portrayed the lovers' reunion as an act of **kismet** that transcended the boundaries of time and distance.
   小说家将恋人的重逢描绘为一种超越时间和距离界限的天命。

2. Many philosophers have debated whether human agency can truly overcome **kismet** or whether all events are predetermined.
   许多哲学家一直在争论人类的能动性是否真能战胜命运，还是所有事件都已注定。

3. The explorer considered it **kismet** that a sudden storm had forced him onto the island where he discovered ancient ruins.
   这位探险家认为，一场突如其来的风暴迫使他登上发现古代遗迹的岛屿，这就是命运。

Think of "kismet" as "kiss-met" — imagine two people who were fated to meet, and when they finally did, they kissed. It was kismet.
把"kismet"想成"kiss-met"——想象两个注定要相遇的人，当他们终于相遇时亲吻了。这就是命运。
