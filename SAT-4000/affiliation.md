# 🤝 affiliation

🔊 /əˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A connection or association with a group, organization, or institution.
**中文：** 友好关系；加入；联盟；从属关系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- association（关联）
- alliance（联盟）
- connection（联系）
- partnership（合作关系）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political affiliation, religious affiliation, affiliation with an organization**
👉 Example: Her affiliation with the university opened many research opportunities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate refused to disclose his political **affiliation** during the press conference last week.
   这位候选人在上周的新闻发布会上拒绝透露自己的政治从属关系。

2. Her **affiliation** with the international research institute greatly enhanced her academic credentials.
   她与国际研究所的关系大大提升了她的学术资历。

3. The organization requires all members to declare any corporate **affiliation** before joining the board.
   该组织要求所有成员在加入董事会之前申报任何企业关联关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "affiliation" as "a-fill-iation" — you fill in your connection to a group, like filling out a form about which team you belong to.
把"affiliation"想象成"a-fill-iation"——你填写自己与某个团体的关系，就像填表说明你属于哪个团队。
