# ⏰ belated

🔊 /bɪˈleɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Coming later than expected or than should have been; delayed; overdue
**中文：** adj. 迟来的；误期的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overdue（逾期的）
- tardy（迟到的）
- delayed（延迟的）
- late（迟的）
- deferred（推迟的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **belated apology / belated birthday wishes / belated recognition / belated response**

👉 Example: She sent a belated birthday card along with a sincere note of apology for the delay.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The institution issued a **belated** apology to the families who had suffered decades of unjust discrimination.
   该机构向遭受了数十年不公正歧视的家庭发出了迟来的道歉。

2. The government's **belated** response to the environmental crisis drew sharp criticism from scientists and advocacy groups.
   政府对环境危机的迟缓回应招致了科学家和倡导团体的尖锐批评。

3. His **belated** recognition as a pioneer in the field came only after posthumous review of his groundbreaking research.
   他作为该领域先驱的迟来认可，是在其开创性研究经过身后审查之后才到来的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-late-d" — it literally contains "late," so belated means something that arrived late or after the proper time.
把"belated"想成"be-late-d"——里面就包含"late（迟到）"，所以belated就是迟来的、误期的。
