# 🛡️ obviate

🔊 /ˈɑːbvieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To remove or prevent (a difficulty or disadvantage); to make unnecessary
**中文：** v. 排除；避免；消除；预防

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eliminate（消除）
- prevent（预防）
- preclude（排除）
- avert（避免）
- forestall（预先阻止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obviate the need for / obviate the problem / obviate the risk / obviate difficulties**

👉 Example: The new software update will obviate the need for manual data entry across all departments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The installation of solar panels would **obviate** the community's dependence on increasingly expensive imported fossil fuels.
   太阳能电池板的安装将消除社区对日益昂贵的进口化石燃料的依赖。

2. Proper preventive maintenance can **obviate** many costly repairs that would otherwise disrupt operations for extended periods.
   适当的预防性维护可以避免许多代价高昂的维修，否则这些维修将长时间中断运营。

3. The revised treaty was designed to **obviate** potential conflicts between the two nations over disputed maritime boundaries.
   修订后的条约旨在消除两国之间关于争议海洋边界的潜在冲突。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "OBVI-ate" as making something "OBVIous" to avoid — you see the problem clearly and eliminate it before it happens.
把"obviate"想成让某事"obvious（明显）"以便避免——你清楚地看到问题并在它发生之前就消除它。
