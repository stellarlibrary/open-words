# 🔬 hydrolysis

🔊 /haɪˈdrɒlɪsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A chemical reaction in which a compound is broken down by its reaction with water, often resulting in the formation of two or more new substances
**中文：** n. 水解；水解作用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decomposition by water（水分解）
- aqueous cleavage（水裂解）
- water splitting reaction（水解反应）
- solvolysis（溶剂分解）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undergo hydrolysis / acid hydrolysis / base hydrolysis / enzymatic hydrolysis / hydrolysis reaction**

👉 Example: The ester underwent hydrolysis in the presence of a strong acid catalyst to yield an alcohol and a carboxylic acid.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biochemist explained that **hydrolysis** of adenosine triphosphate provides the primary energy source for most cellular processes.
   生物化学家解释说，三磷酸腺苷的水解为大多数细胞过程提供了主要能量来源。

2. During digestion, enzymes catalyze the **hydrolysis** of complex carbohydrates into simpler sugars that the body can readily absorb.
   在消化过程中，酶催化复杂碳水化合物的水解，将其分解为身体可以容易吸收的简单糖类。

3. The industrial process employs alkaline **hydrolysis** to break down waste proteins into amino acids for agricultural fertilizer production.
   该工业过程采用碱性水解将废弃蛋白质分解为氨基酸，用于农业肥料生产。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydro-lysis" as "water-breaking" — from Greek "hydor" (water) and "lysis" (loosening or splitting).
把"hydrolysis"拆成"hydro"（水）+"lysis"（分解）——用水来分解物质。
