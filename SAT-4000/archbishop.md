# ⛪ archbishop

🔊 /ˌɑːrtʃˈbɪʃəp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A senior bishop in charge of an archdiocese, holding the highest rank among bishops in a particular region
**中文：** n. 大主教；总教主

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prelate（高级教士）
- primate（首席主教）
- metropolitan（大都会主教）
- hierarch（宗教领袖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the Archbishop of Canterbury / appointed archbishop / archbishop's palace / serve as archbishop**

👉 Example: The Archbishop of Canterbury delivered a powerful sermon on social justice and compassion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **archbishop** convened a council of clergy to discuss pressing ethical issues facing the modern religious community.
   这位大主教召集了一次神职人员会议，讨论现代宗教界面临的紧迫伦理问题。

2. Thousands of faithful gathered in the cathedral to witness the ceremonial installation of the new **archbishop**.
   数千名信徒聚集在大教堂，见证新任大主教的就职典礼。

3. The medieval **archbishop** wielded considerable political influence and often served as a key advisor to the crown.
   这位中世纪的大主教拥有相当大的政治影响力，经常担任国王的重要顾问。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arch-bishop" — "arch" means chief or principal, so an archbishop is the chief bishop, the top leader among bishops.
把"archbishop"拆成"arch（首席的）+ bishop（主教）"——大主教就是主教中的首席领袖。
