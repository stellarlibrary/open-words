# 📦 accumulate

🔊 /əˈkjuː.mjə.leɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To gather or collect gradually over time; to increase in quantity.
**中文：** 累积；积聚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- amass（积聚）
- accrue（逐渐增加）
- hoard（囤积）
- stockpile（大量储备）
- aggregate（聚集）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accumulate wealth/knowledge/evidence/debt/experience**

👉 Example: He accumulated vast experience through decades of fieldwork.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Over several decades, the investor **accumulated** a substantial fortune through disciplined and strategic planning.
   经过几十年的时间，这位投资者通过严格的战略规划积累了大量财富。

2. Dust and debris **accumulate** rapidly in abandoned buildings, posing serious health risks to urban explorers.
   灰尘和碎屑在废弃建筑中迅速积聚，对城市探险者构成严重的健康威胁。

3. The prosecution must **accumulate** sufficient evidence before presenting a compelling case to the grand jury.
   检方必须积累足够的证据，才能向大陪审团提出有说服力的案件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "ac-CU-MU-late" — imagine a cup (cu) that keeps getting filled more and more (mulate), accumulating liquid.
accumulate 中有 cumul（堆积），想象不断往上"堆"东西，越堆越多——积累。
