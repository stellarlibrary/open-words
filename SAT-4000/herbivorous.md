# 🌱 herbivorous

🔊 /hɜːrˈbɪvərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or characteristic of animals that feed on plants and vegetation
**中文：** adj. 食草的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plant-eating（食草的）
- grass-feeding（以草为食的）
- vegetarian（素食的）
- phytophagous（植食性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **herbivorous animal / herbivorous diet / herbivorous species / herbivorous dinosaur**

👉 Example: Many herbivorous insects have evolved specialized mouthparts for consuming specific types of vegetation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **herbivorous** dinosaurs of the Jurassic period developed long necks to reach vegetation high in the forest canopy.
   侏罗纪时期的食草恐龙进化出长脖子以够到森林树冠高处的植被。

2. Scientists classified the newly discovered species as **herbivorous** based on the structure of its teeth and digestive tract.
   科学家根据其牙齿和消化道的结构将这一新发现的物种归类为食草动物。

3. The island's ecosystem depends heavily on **herbivorous** mammals that control the spread of invasive plant species.
   该岛的生态系统在很大程度上依赖于控制入侵植物蔓延的食草哺乳动物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "herbivorous" as "herb" (草药/植物) + "vorous" (贪吃的) — always devouring herbs and plants.
把"herbivorous"想成"herb"（草药/植物）+"vorous"（贪吃的）——总是贪吃草本植物。
