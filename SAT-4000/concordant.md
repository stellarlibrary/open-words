# 🤝 concordant

🔊 /kənˈkɔːrdənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In agreement or harmony; consistent and compatible with something
**中文：** adj. 和谐的；协调的；一致的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmonious（和谐的）
- consistent（一致的）
- compatible（兼容的）
- congruent（一致的）
- accordant（协调的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concordant with / concordant results / concordant findings / concordant views**

👉 Example: The experimental results were concordant with the predictions of the theoretical model.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The findings of the independent audit were entirely **concordant** with the conclusions reached by the internal review committee.
   独立审计的结果与内部审查委员会得出的结论完全一致。

2. Researchers from multiple institutions reported **concordant** data, strengthening the credibility of the original study's conclusions.
   来自多个机构的研究人员报告了一致的数据，增强了原始研究结论的可信度。

3. A successful diplomatic resolution requires the negotiating parties to establish **concordant** objectives before formal talks commence.
   成功的外交解决方案要求谈判各方在正式会谈开始前确立一致的目标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con (together) + cord (heart)" — when hearts are together, there is harmony and agreement, which is concordant.
把"concordant"想成"con（一起）+ cord（心）"——当心在一起时，就有了和谐与一致，即concordant。
