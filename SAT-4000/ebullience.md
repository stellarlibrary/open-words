# 🎉 ebullience

🔊 /ɪˈbʊliəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being cheerful and full of energy; enthusiastic excitement or exuberance
**中文：** n. 奔放；兴高采烈；热情洋溢

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exuberance（充沛）
- enthusiasm（热情）
- vivacity（活泼）
- effervescence（兴奋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natural ebullience / youthful ebullience / infectious ebullience / display ebullience**

👉 Example: Her natural ebullience made her the most popular and admired leader in the organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The speaker's infectious **ebullience** energized the entire audience and transformed a routine conference into a memorable event.
   演讲者极具感染力的热情激发了全场观众，把一场常规会议变成了一次难忘的活动。

2. Despite facing considerable adversity, she maintained an extraordinary **ebullience** that inspired her colleagues to persevere.
   尽管面临相当大的困难，她保持着非凡的热情，激励同事们坚持不懈。

3. The **ebullience** of the graduating class was palpable as students celebrated years of dedication and academic achievement.
   毕业班的兴高采烈之情溢于言表，学生们庆祝多年的奉献和学业成就。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ebullience" from Latin "ebullire" meaning "to bubble up" — like boiling water, emotions bubble over with excitement.
"ebullience"来自拉丁语"ebullire"，意思是"沸腾冒泡"——就像沸水一样，情感因兴奋而翻涌。
