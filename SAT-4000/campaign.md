# 📢 campaign

🔊 /kæmˈpeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An organized course of action to achieve a goal, such as a political election or military operation. (v.) To work in an organized way toward a particular goal, especially in politics or social causes.
**中文：** n. 运动；活动；战役；竞选运动 v. 作战；参加竞选；从事活动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crusade（运动）
- drive（推动活动）
- movement（运动）
- offensive（攻势）
- initiative（倡议）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **campaign for / campaign against / launch a campaign / election campaign / advertising campaign**

👉 Example: The organization launched a nationwide campaign against childhood hunger.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator decided to **campaign** vigorously across all fifty states to secure the presidential nomination.
   那位参议员决定在全部五十个州积极竞选，以争取总统提名。

2. Environmental activists organized a sustained **campaign** to raise public awareness about the dangers of deforestation.
   环保活动人士组织了一场持续的运动，以提高公众对森林砍伐危害的认识。

3. The military **campaign** in the northern region lasted several months before a ceasefire was finally negotiated.
   北部地区的军事战役持续了数月，最终才达成停火协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "camp-aign" as setting up "camp" for a cause — soldiers camp during a military campaign, and politicians camp out in states during election campaigns.
把"campaign"想成为某个目标搭"营地（camp）"——士兵在战役中驻扎营地，政客在竞选中驻扎各州。
