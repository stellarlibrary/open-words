# 🔨 severely

🔊 /sɪˈvɪrli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) To a great or serious degree; in a strict or harsh manner; in a plain and simple style
**中文：** adv. 严格地，激烈地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harshly（严厉地）
- gravely（严重地）
- sternly（严格地）
- acutely（剧烈地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **severely damaged / severely punished / severely limited / severely affected**

👉 Example: The region was severely affected by the prolonged drought that devastated local agriculture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The earthquake **severely** damaged the infrastructure of the coastal city, leaving thousands of residents without shelter.
   地震严重破坏了沿海城市的基础设施，使数千居民无家可归。

2. The professor **severely** criticized the student's thesis for lacking empirical evidence and rigorous methodology.
   教授严厉批评了该学生的论文，认为其缺乏实证依据和严谨的研究方法。

3. Government funding for scientific research has been **severely** curtailed due to the ongoing economic recession.
   由于持续的经济衰退，政府对科学研究的资助被大幅削减。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "severe-ly" — when something is done "severely," it is carried out in the most severe, intense way possible.
把"severely"想成"severe（严重的）+ ly"——以最严重、最强烈的方式进行。
