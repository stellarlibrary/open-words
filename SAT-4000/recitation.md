# 🎤 recitation

🔊 /ˌrɛsɪˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of reciting something from memory; a public reading or oral delivery of a prepared text; a detailed account or listing
**中文：** n. 背诵；朗诵；详述；叙述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recital（朗诵）
- narration（叙述）
- rendition（演绎）
- declamation（朗诵演说）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recitation of a poem / recitation from memory / recitation of facts / public recitation**

👉 Example: The student earned high praise for her flawless recitation of the Shakespearean sonnet.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor required a **recitation** of key historical dates and events as part of the final examination assessment.
   教授要求背诵关键的历史日期和事件，作为期末考试评估的一部分。

2. Her eloquent **recitation** of the classical poem moved the entire audience to silence and deep contemplation.
   她对古典诗歌的优美朗诵使全场观众陷入了沉默和深思。

3. The witness provided a thorough **recitation** of the events leading up to the incident during the formal court proceedings.
   证人在正式的法庭审理中详细叙述了事件发生前的经过。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "recitation" as "re-cite-ation" — to "cite" something again by speaking it aloud from memory, like reciting a favorite poem on stage.
把"recitation"拆成"re-（再次）+ cite（引用）+ -ation（名词后缀）"——再次引用，即从记忆中大声背诵出来。
