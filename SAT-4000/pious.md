# 🙏 pious

🔊 /ˈpaɪəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deeply religious and devoted; showing dutiful reverence; commendably dutiful or virtuous
**中文：** adj. 虔诚的；敬神的；可嘉的；尽责的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- devout（虔诚的）
- reverent（恭敬的）
- godly（敬神的）
- religious（宗教的）
- faithful（忠诚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pious devotion / pious hope / pious believer / pious fraud**

👉 Example: The congregation admired the priest for his pious dedication to serving the community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pious** monks devoted their entire lives to prayer, meditation, and the careful preservation of ancient manuscripts.
   这些虔诚的僧侣将毕生奉献于祈祷、冥想和精心保存古代手稿。

2. Critics dismissed the politician's speech as nothing more than **pious** rhetoric designed to conceal his true intentions.
   批评者认为这位政治家的演讲不过是虔诚的空话，旨在掩盖其真实意图。

3. Her **pious** observance of every religious holiday reflected a deeply sincere and unwavering commitment to her faith.
   她对每个宗教节日的虔诚遵守反映了她对信仰深切而坚定的承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pi-ous" as "pie for us" — a pious person might humbly bake a pie for others, showing devotion and selfless duty.
把"pious"想成"pie（派）for us"——一个虔诚的人会谦卑地为他人烤派，体现奉献和无私。
