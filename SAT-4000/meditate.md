# 🧘 meditate

🔊 /ˈmɛdɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To think deeply or carefully about something; to engage in contemplation or reflection; to plan mentally
**中文：** v. 沉思，冥想；反省；考虑；策划

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contemplate（沉思）
- reflect（反思）
- ponder（深思）
- deliberate（深思熟虑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **meditate on / meditate upon / meditate deeply / meditate daily**

👉 Example: The philosopher would meditate on the nature of existence during his long solitary walks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Before making any major decision, she preferred to **meditate** on all possible outcomes and their long-term consequences.
   在做任何重大决定之前，她更愿意沉思所有可能的结果及其长远影响。

2. The monks would **meditate** in silence for several hours each morning as part of their spiritual discipline.
   僧侣们每天早晨会静默冥想数小时，作为其修行的一部分。

3. The strategist spent weeks **meditating** a comprehensive plan to restructure the failing division of the corporation.
   这位战略家花了数周时间策划一项全面的方案，以重组公司陷入困境的部门。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meditate" as "medi(mind) + tate(state)" — putting your mind in a calm state to think deeply.
把"meditate"想成"medi（心灵）+ tate（状态）"——让心灵进入平静的状态来深入思考。
