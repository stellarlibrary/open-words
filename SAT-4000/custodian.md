# 🔑 custodian

🔊 /kʌˈstoʊdiən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who has responsibility for the care or protection of something; a guardian or caretaker
**中文：** n. 管理人；监护人；保管人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guardian（监护人）
- caretaker（看管人）
- steward（管理人）
- keeper（保管人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **custodian of records / custodian of the building / legal custodian / custodian of tradition**

👉 Example: The museum custodian ensured that every artifact was stored under optimal conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **custodian** of the historic library devoted decades to preserving its rare manuscripts and ancient texts.
   这座历史图书馆的管理人花了数十年时间保存其珍贵手稿和古代文献。

2. As the legal **custodian** of the estate, she bore full responsibility for managing its financial assets.
   作为遗产的合法监护人，她承担了管理其金融资产的全部责任。

3. The school **custodian** maintained the facility with remarkable diligence, ensuring a safe environment for students.
   学校管理员以非凡的勤勉维护设施，确保学生拥有安全的环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "custodian" as "custody + an" — the person who has custody of something, the one who guards and keeps it safe.
把"custodian"想成"custody（监管）+ an"——拥有监管权的人，负责守护和保管事物的人。
