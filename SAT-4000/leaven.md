# 🍞 leaven

🔊 /ˈlɛvən/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause dough to rise by adding a fermenting agent; to permeate and transform with a modifying influence. (n.) A substance such as yeast used to make dough rise; a pervasive influence that modifies something.
**中文：** v. 使发酵；影响，渗透 n. 酵母；酵素；影响力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ferment（发酵）
- permeate（渗透）
- infuse（注入）
- enliven（使活跃）
- yeast（酵母）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **leaven the dough / leaven with humor / leaven a speech / a leaven of wit**

👉 Example: A good professor will leaven rigorous academic content with occasional humor to maintain student engagement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The baker allowed the **leaven** to work overnight, producing a light and perfectly airy loaf of bread.
   面包师让酵母发酵了一整夜，做出了轻盈蓬松的面包。

2. Her dry wit served to **leaven** the otherwise somber atmosphere of the formal diplomatic reception.
   她的冷幽默为原本严肃的外交招待会增添了几分轻松气氛。

3. The professor managed to **leaven** his dense philosophical lectures with vivid anecdotes that captivated every student.
   这位教授设法用生动的轶事来调剂他深奥的哲学讲座，吸引了每一位学生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "leaven" as "leave-in" — you leave yeast in the dough to let it rise and transform, just as an influence is left in to change things.
把"leaven"想成"leave-in"——你把酵母留在面团里让它膨胀变化，就像一种影响力被留在其中改变事物一样。
