# 😠 irritate

🔊 /ˈɪrɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make someone annoyed or angry; to cause discomfort or inflammation in a body part
**中文：** 激怒；使恼火；使急躁；刺激（身体部位）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- annoy（惹恼）
- aggravate（激怒）
- provoke（激怒）
- exasperate（使恼怒）
- inflame（使发炎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irritate the skin / irritate someone / irritate the eyes / easily irritated**

👉 Example: The harsh chemical fumes began to irritate the workers' eyes and respiratory passages within minutes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Certain synthetic fabrics can **irritate** sensitive skin, causing persistent redness and discomfort that may require medical attention.
   某些合成面料会刺激敏感皮肤，导致持续发红和不适，可能需要就医。

2. The senator's dismissive remarks about climate change deeply **irritated** environmental advocates who had campaigned for decades.
   参议员对气候变化的轻蔑言论深深激怒了那些已经倡导了数十年的环保人士。

3. Prolonged exposure to airborne pollutants can **irritate** the lining of the lungs and significantly increase the risk of respiratory disease.
   长期接触空气中的污染物会刺激肺部内壁，并显著增加呼吸系统疾病的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irritate" as related to "itch" — when something irritates you, it is like an itch you cannot scratch, making you increasingly agitated.
把"irritate"和"itch（痒）"联系起来——当某事刺激你时，就像一个挠不到的痒，让你越来越烦躁。
