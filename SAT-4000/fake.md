# 🎭 fake

🔊 /feɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A counterfeit object or a person who deceives. (v.) To fabricate or pretend. (adj.) Not genuine; counterfeit or forged
**中文：** n. 假货；骗子 v. 伪造；假装 adj. 伪造的，假的，冒充的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- counterfeit（伪造的）
- fraudulent（欺诈的）
- bogus（假的）
- forged（伪造的）
- sham（虚假的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fake identity / fake news / fake documents / fake a smile**

👉 Example: The authorities confiscated thousands of fake passports from the criminal syndicate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Investigators discovered that the painting was a **fake**, expertly crafted to deceive even the most seasoned art collectors.
   调查人员发现这幅画是赝品，制作精良，甚至能骗过最有经验的艺术收藏家。

2. The defendant attempted to **fake** financial records in order to conceal evidence of widespread corporate embezzlement.
   被告试图伪造财务记录，以掩盖大规模公司挪用公款的证据。

3. The proliferation of **fake** news articles has significantly undermined public trust in credible journalism and media institutions.
   虚假新闻文章的泛滥严重削弱了公众对可信新闻业和媒体机构的信任。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fake" rhyming with "make" — someone who "makes" things up is creating fakes and fabrications.
把"fake"和"make"联想——"制造"出来的东西就是"假的"，伪造就是在"制造"虚假。
