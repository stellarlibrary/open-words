# 💭 idealist

🔊 /aɪˈdiːəlɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who is guided more by ideals than by practical considerations; one who envisions things in a perfect or unrealistically optimistic form
**中文：** n. 理想主义者；空想家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dreamer（梦想家）
- visionary（有远见的人）
- utopian（空想家）
- romantic（浪漫主义者）
- optimist（乐观主义者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a hopeless idealist / a naive idealist / an idealist at heart / idealist versus realist / political idealist**

👉 Example: Although often dismissed as a naive idealist, her proposals eventually reshaped the entire organization's approach to reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young senator was widely regarded as an **idealist** whose ambitious reform agenda inspired voters but alarmed pragmatic party leaders.
   这位年轻的参议员被广泛认为是一个理想主义者，其雄心勃勃的改革议程激励了选民，却令务实的党派领袖感到不安。

2. The philosopher described himself as an **idealist** who believed that moral principles should never be compromised for political convenience.
   这位哲学家自称是一个理想主义者，认为道德原则绝不应为政治便利而妥协。

3. Critics warned that only a reckless **idealist** would propose dismantling the entire system without offering a viable alternative framework.
   批评者警告说，只有鲁莽的空想家才会提议拆除整个体系而不提供可行的替代框架。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ideal" + "-ist" (a person) — an idealist is a person who chases the ideal, always reaching for a perfect world that may only exist in the mind.
把"idealist"拆分为"ideal"（理想）+"-ist"（人）——理想主义者就是追逐理想的人，总是追求一个可能只存在于脑海中的完美世界。
