# ✨ radiance

🔊 /ˈreɪdiəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Light or heat as emitted or reflected by something; a warm, glowing quality of appearance or manner; brilliance.
**中文：** n. 辐射；光辉；发光；容光焕发；闪烁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brilliance（光辉）
- luminosity（光度）
- glow（光芒）
- splendor（壮丽）
- effulgence（灿烂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **radiance of the sun / inner radiance / youthful radiance / a radiance about someone / emit radiance**

👉 Example: The artist captured the soft radiance of the morning light filtering through the cathedral windows.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **radiance** of the setting sun transformed the mountain landscape into a breathtaking tableau of gold and crimson hues.
   落日的光辉将山景变成了一幅令人叹为观止的金色与深红色画卷。

2. Her face possessed a natural **radiance** that no cosmetic could replicate, reflecting her genuine warmth and inner contentment.
   她的脸庞拥有一种任何化妆品都无法复制的自然光彩，反映出她真诚的温暖和内心的满足。

3. The physicist measured the **radiance** emitted by the experimental material to determine its potential applications in solar energy.
   物理学家测量了实验材料发出的辐射，以确定其在太阳能领域的潜在应用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "radiance" as "radi-ance" — from "radiate" plus "-ance," the quality of radiating light, like the sun's warm glow spreading outward.
把"radiance"想成"radi-ance"——来自"radiate（辐射）"加"-ance"，表示散发光芒的品质，就像太阳温暖的光芒向外扩散。
