# 🔀 supplant

🔊 /səˈplænt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To take the place of someone or something; to oust or push aside
**中文：** v. 排挤掉，代替

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- replace（替代）
- supersede（取代）
- displace（排挤）
- oust（驱逐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supplant a rival / supplant a tradition / supplant an old system / be supplanted by**

👉 Example: The new technology quickly supplanted the older methods that had been used for decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Streaming platforms have effectively **supplanted** traditional television networks as the dominant medium for entertainment content.
   流媒体平台已有效地取代了传统电视网络，成为娱乐内容的主导媒介。

2. The ambitious minister attempted to **supplant** the prime minister by secretly building alliances within the ruling party.
   这位野心勃勃的部长试图通过在执政党内秘密建立联盟来排挤首相。

3. Historians noted how the imported agricultural practices gradually **supplanted** indigenous farming techniques throughout the colonial period.
   历史学家指出，在整个殖民时期，引进的农业实践逐渐取代了本土的耕作技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sup-PLANT" — someone plants themselves in your spot, pushing you out and taking your place.
把"supplant"想成"sup-PLANT"——某人把自己"种植"在你的位置上，把你挤走并取代了你。
