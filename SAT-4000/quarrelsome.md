# 😤 quarrelsome

🔊 /ˈkwɒrəlsəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Given to or characterized by quarreling; inclined to argue or disagree frequently
**中文：** adj. 喜欢吵架的；好争论的；爱挑剔的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contentious（好争论的）
- belligerent（好斗的）
- argumentative（好辩的）
- combative（好战的）
- cantankerous（脾气暴躁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quarrelsome nature / quarrelsome neighbor / quarrelsome temperament / quarrelsome individual**

👉 Example: His quarrelsome temperament made it difficult for colleagues to collaborate with him on projects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **quarrelsome** tenant filed numerous complaints against every neighbor in the apartment building throughout the year.
   这位好争吵的租户一年到头对公寓楼里每一位邻居都提出了大量投诉。

2. Historians described the monarch as a **quarrelsome** ruler whose confrontational diplomacy led to several unnecessary wars.
   历史学家将这位君主描述为一位好争论的统治者，其对抗性外交导致了数场不必要的战争。

3. The committee struggled to reach consensus because of one particularly **quarrelsome** member who objected to every proposal.
   由于一位特别爱争吵的成员反对每一项提案，委员会难以达成共识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quarrel + some" — someone who always has "some quarrel" to pick with others is quarrelsome.
把"quarrelsome"想成"quarrel（争吵）+ some（一些）"——总是要跟别人"吵上几句"的人就是quarrelsome。
