# 🧪 vermicide

🔊 /ˈvɜːrmɪsaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A substance or agent used to kill parasitic worms; an anthelmintic drug
**中文：** n. 杀蠕虫药；驱虫剂；打虫药

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- anthelmintic（驱虫药）
- dewormer（驱虫剂）
- parasiticide（杀寄生虫剂）
- worm treatment（蠕虫治疗药）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **administer a vermicide / effective vermicide / vermicide treatment / prescribe a vermicide**

👉 Example: The veterinarian prescribed a vermicide to eliminate the intestinal parasites found in the livestock.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician administered a potent **vermicide** after laboratory tests confirmed the presence of parasitic worms in the patient.
   实验室检测确认患者体内有寄生虫后，医生使用了一种强效杀虫药。

2. Agricultural researchers developed a new **vermicide** that targets specific nematode species without harming beneficial soil organisms.
   农业研究人员开发了一种新型杀虫剂，能针对特定线虫物种而不伤害有益的土壤生物。

3. In tropical regions, regular application of **vermicide** is essential for maintaining the health and productivity of domestic animals.
   在热带地区，定期使用驱虫药对于维持家畜的健康和生产力至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vermi-cide" — "vermi" means worm (as in vermicelli, worm-shaped pasta) and "-cide" means to kill (as in homicide). So vermicide literally means "worm killer."
把"vermicide"想成"vermi-cide"——"vermi"意为蠕虫（如vermicelli，虫形意面），"-cide"意为杀死（如homicide）。所以vermicide字面意思就是"杀虫剂"。
