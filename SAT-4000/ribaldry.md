# 🤭 ribaldry

🔊 /ˈrɪbəldri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Obscene, indecent, or vulgar language; coarse and crude humor or speech
**中文：** n. 下流，下流的话

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vulgarity（粗俗）
- obscenity（淫秽）
- bawdiness（下流）
- coarseness（粗鲁）
- indecency（不雅）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **engage in ribaldry / full of ribaldry / a display of ribaldry / descend into ribaldry**

👉 Example: The play's ribaldry shocked audiences who expected a refined theatrical performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literary critic noted that the author balanced moments of **ribaldry** with passages of profound philosophical reflection throughout the work.
   文学评论家指出，作者在整部作品中将猥亵的话语与深刻的哲学反思段落相互平衡。

2. Medieval carnival traditions often included public **ribaldry** that would be considered entirely unacceptable by modern social standards.
   中世纪的狂欢节传统常常包含公开的粗俗言语，按照现代社会标准来看完全不可接受。

3. The committee condemned the speaker's **ribaldry** and demanded a formal apology for the offensive remarks made during the ceremony.
   委员会谴责了演讲者的下流言辞，并要求其为仪式上发表的冒犯性言论正式道歉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ribaldry" as "ribald" + "-ry" — the practice or product of being ribald, like vulgar jokes and crude speech collected together.
把"ribaldry"想成"ribald"加上"-ry"——即下流行为的集合，就像粗俗笑话和粗鲁言语的汇总。
