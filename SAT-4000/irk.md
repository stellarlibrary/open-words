# 😤 irk

🔊 /ɜːrk/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To annoy or irritate; to cause someone to feel weary or exasperated
**中文：** v. 使烦恼；使厌倦；使苦恼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- annoy（使烦恼）
- irritate（激怒）
- vex（使恼怒）
- bother（打扰）
- exasperate（使恼怒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irk someone / it irks me that... / irk someone to no end**

👉 Example: It irks me that some students refuse to participate in class discussions despite being well prepared.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. It **irked** the professor that several students consistently submitted their assignments well past the deadline.
   教授对几位学生总是远超截止日期才提交作业感到十分恼火。

2. The constant interruptions during the lecture began to **irk** even the most patient members of the audience.
   讲座期间不断的打断开始让即使是最有耐心的听众也感到厌烦。

3. Nothing **irks** a diligent researcher more than discovering that critical data has been carelessly misrecorded.
   没有什么比发现关键数据被粗心地错误记录更让勤奋的研究者感到恼火的了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irk" as the sound you make when something gets under your skin — a short, sharp grunt of annoyance.
把"irk"想象成某件事惹恼你时发出的声音——一声短促而尖锐的恼怒声。
