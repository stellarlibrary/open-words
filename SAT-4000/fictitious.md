# 📚 fictitious

🔊 /fɪkˈtɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not real or true; imaginary or fabricated; occurring in or invented for fiction
**中文：** adj. 虚构的；假想的；编造的；假装的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fabricated（捏造的）
- imaginary（虚构的）
- fictional（小说的）
- invented（发明的）
- bogus（伪造的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fictitious name / fictitious character / fictitious account / fictitious story / fictitious identity**

👉 Example: The author used a fictitious name to protect the privacy of the individuals involved in the case.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant created a **fictitious** company to funnel illegally obtained funds through a network of offshore accounts.
   被告创建了一家虚构的公司，通过离岸账户网络转移非法所得资金。

2. All characters depicted in this film are entirely **fictitious**, and any resemblance to actual persons is purely coincidental.
   本片中描绘的所有角色均为虚构，与真实人物的任何相似之处纯属巧合。

3. The journalist was dismissed after editors discovered that several sources in her award-winning article were completely **fictitious**.
   编辑发现她获奖文章中的几个消息来源完全是捏造的之后，这位记者被解雇了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fictitious" as "fiction + itious" — something that belongs to fiction is fictitious, meaning it is made up and not real.
把"fictitious"想成"fiction + itious"——属于小说（fiction）的东西就是fictitious，意味着它是编造的，不是真实的。
