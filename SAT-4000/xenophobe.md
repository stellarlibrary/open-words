# 😠 xenophobe

🔊 /ˈzɛnəˌfoʊb/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who fears or hates foreigners, strangers, or anything perceived as foreign or different
**中文：** 仇外者；害怕生人者；畏惧和憎恨外国人的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bigot（偏执者）
- chauvinist（沙文主义者）
- nationalist（民族主义者）
- racist（种族主义者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a xenophobe who / xenophobe toward / known xenophobe / outspoken xenophobe**

👉 Example: The politician was labeled a xenophobe after repeatedly calling for the expulsion of all foreign workers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial condemned the senator as a **xenophobe** whose rhetoric consistently targeted immigrant communities and their cultural traditions.
   社论谴责这位参议员是一个仇外者，其言论一贯针对移民社区及其文化传统。

2. Historians argue that the leader was fundamentally a **xenophobe** who enacted restrictive policies to isolate the nation from foreign influence.
   历史学家认为这位领导人本质上是一个排外者，制定了限制性政策以使国家与外国影响隔绝。

3. Being a **xenophobe** in an increasingly interconnected world can severely limit one's professional and intellectual opportunities.
   在一个日益互联的世界里，做一个仇外者会严重限制个人的职业和智识发展机会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "xeno-phobe" as "xeno" (foreign, stranger in Greek) + "phobe" (fear) — someone who fears foreigners.
把"xenophobe"拆分为"xeno"（希腊语中的外国人）+"phobe"（恐惧）——害怕外国人的人。
