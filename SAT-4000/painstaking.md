# 🔍 painstaking

🔊 /ˈpeɪnzˌteɪkɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done with or employing great care, thoroughness, and diligent effort. (n.) Careful and diligent work or effort.
**中文：** adj. 辛勤的；艰苦的；小心的 n. 辛苦；勤勉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- meticulous（一丝不苟的）
- thorough（彻底的）
- diligent（勤勉的）
- scrupulous（细致的）
- assiduous（刻苦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **painstaking effort / painstaking research / painstaking detail / painstaking process / painstaking work**

👉 Example: The restoration of the ancient manuscript required painstaking attention to every faded letter and damaged page.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years of **painstaking** research, the archaeologists finally uncovered evidence that confirmed the existence of the lost ancient civilization.
   经过多年**艰苦的**研究，考古学家终于发现了证实失落古文明存在的证据。

2. The artist devoted **painstaking** effort to reproducing every fine detail of the original masterpiece with extraordinary precision and care.
   这位艺术家付出了**辛勤的**努力，以非凡的精确度和细心再现了原始杰作的每一个细节。

3. The forensic team conducted a **painstaking** examination of the crime scene, cataloging every piece of physical evidence they discovered.
   法医团队对犯罪现场进行了**细致的**检查，将他们发现的每一件物证都编入目录。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pains-taking" literally — taking great pains (effort and care) to do something perfectly, enduring difficulty for excellence.
把"painstaking"按字面意思理解——费尽心思（付出努力和细心）来完美地做某事，为卓越而忍受艰辛。
