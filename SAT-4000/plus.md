# ➕ plus

🔊 /plʌs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, preposition | 名词, 形容词, 介词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A positive sign (+); an advantage or bonus. (adj.) Positive; additional. (prep.) With the addition of; increased by.
**中文：** n. 正号，加号；好处；附加额 adj. 正的；附加的 prep. 加，加上

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- addition（附加）
- advantage（优势）
- bonus（额外好处）
- positive（正面的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a big plus / plus side / plus sign / two plus three / plus or minus**

👉 Example: Her bilingual fluency is a definite plus when applying for international consulting positions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The salary package includes a base rate **plus** generous benefits such as health insurance and retirement contributions.
   薪酬方案包括基本工资**加上**慷慨的福利，如医疗保险和退休金缴纳。

2. Living near the university library is a significant **plus** for graduate students conducting extensive dissertation research.
   住在大学图书馆附近对进行大量论文研究的研究生来说是一个显著的**好处**。

3. The final examination score must be B **plus** or higher for students seeking admission into the advanced seminar course.
   期末考试成绩必须达到B**加**或以上，学生才能进入高级研讨课程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plus" as the "+" symbol itself — it always means adding something more, whether in math or in life's advantages.
把"plus"想成"+"符号本身——无论在数学还是生活的优势中，它总是意味着增加更多。
