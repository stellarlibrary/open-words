# 🔩 Iron

🔊 /ˈaɪərn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A strong, hard metallic element; a flat-bottomed heated device for pressing clothes. (v.) To press clothes with a heated iron. (adj.) Made of iron; strong and unyielding
**中文：** n. 铁；熨斗；烙铁；坚强 v. 熨；用铁铸成 adj. 铁的；残酷的；刚强的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- steel（钢铁）
- metal（金属）
- press（熨烫）
- firm（坚定的）
- unyielding（不屈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **iron will / iron out differences / iron curtain / strike while the iron is hot**

👉 Example: The negotiators worked tirelessly to iron out the remaining disagreements before the final summit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient blacksmith heated the **iron** in a roaring forge until it glowed bright orange and became malleable.
   古代铁匠在熊熊燃烧的锻炉中加热铁块，直到它发出明亮的橙色光芒并变得可锻造。

2. Her **iron** determination to succeed in academia inspired countless younger students to pursue their own scholarly ambitions.
   她在学术上取得成功的钢铁般的决心激励了无数年轻学生追求自己的学术抱负。

3. The committee convened an emergency session to **iron** out the inconsistencies discovered in the proposed legislation.
   委员会召开紧急会议，以消除在拟议立法中发现的不一致之处。
