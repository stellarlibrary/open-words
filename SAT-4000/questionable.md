# 🤨 questionable

🔊 /ˈkwestʃənəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Doubtful as regards truth or quality; open to doubt or suspicion; not clearly honest or morally acceptable
**中文：** adj. 可疑的；有问题的；不确定的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dubious（可疑的）
- suspicious（令人怀疑的）
- doubtful（不确定的）
- debatable（有争议的）
- suspect（可疑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **questionable behavior / questionable ethics / questionable motives / of questionable value**

👉 Example: The politician's questionable ethics became the subject of an extensive investigation by the press.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The auditors uncovered several **questionable** transactions that could not be adequately explained by the company's financial officers.
   审计人员发现了几笔可疑交易，公司财务人员无法对其作出充分解释。

2. Critics argued that the study's methodology was **questionable**, casting serious doubt on the validity of its conclusions.
   批评者认为该研究的方法论存在问题，严重质疑其结论的有效性。

3. His **questionable** decision to invest the organization's funds in speculative ventures ultimately resulted in significant losses.
   他将组织资金投入投机性项目的可疑决定最终导致了重大损失。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "question + able" — if something is "able to be questioned," it is questionable and therefore doubtful or suspicious.
把"questionable"想成"question（质疑）+ able（可以的）"——如果某事"可以被质疑"，那它就是questionable，即可疑的或令人怀疑的。
