# 🧠 mastermind

🔊 /ˈmæstərmaɪnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To plan and direct a complex scheme or enterprise. (n.) A person with outstanding intellect; the chief planner of a scheme
**中文：** v. 策划 n. 优秀策划者；具有极高才智的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- orchestrate（精心策划）
- engineer（策划；设计）
- architect（设计者）
- genius（天才）
- strategist（战略家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mastermind a plan / mastermind a robbery / the mastermind behind / criminal mastermind / evil mastermind**

👉 Example: Detectives eventually identified the mastermind behind the elaborate international smuggling operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The intelligence agency spent years trying to identify the individual who had **mastermind**ed the complex cyberattack on government systems.
   情报机构花了数年时间试图查明策划这次针对政府系统的复杂网络攻击的幕后人物。

2. She was widely recognized as the **mastermind** of the company's innovative marketing strategy that tripled quarterly revenue.
   她被广泛认为是该公司创新营销策略的总策划者，该策略使季度收入增长了两倍。

3. The documentary revealed how a single **mastermind** orchestrated the entire financial fraud scheme without arousing any suspicion.
   这部纪录片揭示了一个幕后策划者如何在不引起任何怀疑的情况下精心策划了整个金融欺诈案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "master + mind" — a person with a master-level mind, smart enough to plan and direct complex operations.
把"mastermind"想成"master + mind"——一个拥有大师级头脑的人，聪明到能够策划和指挥复杂的行动。
