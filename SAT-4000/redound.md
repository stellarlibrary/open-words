# 🔄 redound

🔊 /rɪˈdaʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To have a particular effect or result; to contribute or accrue to one's credit or advantage; to rebound or reflect upon
**中文：** v. 有助于；报偿，报应；被转移

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contribute（有助于）
- accrue（积累）
- rebound（回报）
- conduce（有益于）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **redound to one's credit / redound to the benefit of / redound upon / redound to one's advantage**

👉 Example: The charitable donation will redound to the company's reputation in the long term.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's tireless negotiations ultimately **redounded** to the benefit of both nations involved in the territorial dispute.
   这位外交官不懈的谈判最终有助于解决领土争端中涉及的两个国家的利益。

2. His years of dedicated public service **redounded** greatly to his credit when he sought the highest elected office.
   他多年的公共服务奉献在竞选最高公职时极大地增添了他的声望。

3. Poor fiscal decisions by the administration will inevitably **redound** upon the citizens who bear the resulting economic burden.
   政府糟糕的财政决策将不可避免地影响到承担经济负担的公民。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-round" — effects go "round" and come back to you, like a boomerang that returns benefit or consequence.
把"redound"想成"re-round"——效果绕了一圈又回到你身上，就像回旋镖一样带回利益或后果。
