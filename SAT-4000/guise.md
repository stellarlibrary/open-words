# 🎭 guise

🔊 /ɡaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An external form, appearance, or manner of presentation, typically concealing the true nature of something
**中文：** n. 外观；装束；伪装；假装

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disguise（伪装）
- appearance（外表）
- pretense（假装）
- facade（表面）
- semblance（外貌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **under the guise of / in the guise of / various guises / a new guise**

👉 Example: The spy entered the country under the guise of a traveling journalist to gather classified intelligence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The fraudulent scheme operated under the **guise** of a legitimate charitable organization collecting donations for disaster relief.
   该欺诈计划以合法慈善组织为伪装，打着为灾难救济募集捐款的旗号运作。

2. Authoritarianism often takes hold in the **guise** of national security measures that gradually erode democratic civil liberties.
   威权主义常常以国家安全措施为幌子逐渐侵蚀民主公民自由。

3. The ancient deity appeared in the **guise** of an ordinary traveler to test the hospitality of unsuspecting villagers.
   这位古老的神灵以普通旅人的装束出现，考验毫无戒备的村民们的好客之心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "guise" as sounding like "guys" in disguise — someone hiding their true identity behind an outward appearance.
把"guise"想成听起来像"guys in disguise"（伪装的人）——某人在外在形象背后隐藏真实身份。
