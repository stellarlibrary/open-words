# 🔦 accentuate

🔊 /əkˈsɛntʃuˌeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make more noticeable or prominent; to emphasize or stress something.
**中文：** 强调；着重指出；使突出；重读。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emphasize（强调）
- highlight（突出）
- underscore（强调）
- stress（着重）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accentuate the difference**, **accentuate the positive**, **accentuate the need for**

👉 Example: The report accentuates the need for comprehensive educational reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economic downturn **accentuated** the disparities between wealthy and impoverished communities.
   经济衰退加剧了富裕社区和贫困社区之间的差距。

2. Her elegant attire **accentuated** the formality and significance of the diplomatic occasion.
   她优雅的着装突显了这次外交场合的正式性和重要性。

3. The professor's analysis **accentuated** the critical role of innovation in sustaining economic growth.
   教授的分析着重强调了创新在维持经济增长中的关键作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accentuate" contains "accent" — when you put an accent on something, you accentuate it, making it stand out.
"Accentuate" 包含 "accent"（重音）——给某物加上重音，就是让它突出、强调它。
