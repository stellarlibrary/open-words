# 😱 aghast

🔊 /əˈɡæst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Struck with overwhelming shock or amazement; filled with sudden fright or horror.
**中文：** 吓呆的，惊骇的；吃惊的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- horrified（惊骇的）
- appalled（震惊的）
- stunned（目瞪口呆的）
- dismayed（沮丧的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stand aghast, look aghast, aghast at**
👉 Example: The audience stood aghast at the sudden collapse of the stage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members were **aghast** when they discovered the extent of the financial fraud.
   委员会成员在发现财务欺诈的严重程度时惊骇不已。

2. She stood **aghast** at the devastation caused by the unexpected natural disaster in her hometown.
   她对家乡突如其来的自然灾害所造成的破坏感到震惊。

3. The professor was **aghast** to learn that none of the students had completed the assigned reading.
   教授得知没有一个学生完成了指定的阅读任务时大为吃惊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aghast" sounds like "a ghost" — imagine seeing a ghost and being frozen in shock.
"Aghast"听起来像"a ghost"（一个鬼）——想象看到鬼被吓呆的样子。
