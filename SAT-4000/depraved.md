# 😈 depraved

🔊 /dɪˈpreɪvd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Morally corrupt or wicked; showing a complete lack of moral principles
**中文：** adj. 堕落的；腐化的；邪恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corrupt（腐败的）
- wicked（邪恶的）
- debased（堕落的）
- perverted（变态的）
- immoral（不道德的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **depraved behavior / depraved indifference / morally depraved / depraved mind**

👉 Example: The court found that the defendant had acted with depraved indifference to human life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary exposed the **depraved** conditions within the institution, revealing decades of systematic abuse and neglect.
   该纪录片揭露了该机构内部堕落的状况，揭示了数十年的系统性虐待和忽视。

2. Historians have characterized the emperor's reign as thoroughly **depraved**, marked by cruelty and unchecked indulgence.
   历史学家将这位皇帝的统治定性为彻底的堕落，以残暴和放纵无度为特征。

3. The prosecutor argued that only a **depraved** individual could have committed such a calculated and merciless act.
   检察官辩称，只有一个堕落的人才能犯下如此有预谋且残忍无情的行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-praved" as "de (down) + praved (from Latin pravus, meaning crooked)" — someone whose morals have gone crooked and downward.
把"depraved"想成"de（向下）+ praved（源自拉丁语pravus，意为歪曲的）"——道德扭曲堕落的人。
