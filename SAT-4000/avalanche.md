# 🏔️ avalanche

🔊 /ˈævəlɑːnʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A massive slide of snow, ice, and rock down a mountainside; a sudden overwhelming quantity. (v.) To descend in an avalanche.
**中文：** n. 雪崩；大量涌来的事物 v. 雪崩

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- landslide（山崩）
- deluge（洪水；大量）
- torrent（急流；大量）
- cascade（瀑布；一连串）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **avalanche of criticism / avalanche of letters / trigger an avalanche / avalanche warning**

👉 Example: The controversial announcement triggered an avalanche of complaints from concerned citizens nationwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sudden **avalanche** buried the mountain village under thirty feet of snow, trapping dozens of residents in their homes.
   突如其来的雪崩将山村掩埋在三十英尺的积雪之下，困住了数十名居民。

2. After the celebrity's apology went viral, an **avalanche** of public opinions flooded social media platforms within mere hours.
   在这位名人的道歉走红网络后，大量的公众意见在数小时内涌入社交媒体平台。

3. Experienced climbers carefully monitor weather conditions to avoid routes where warming temperatures might **avalanche** without any warning.
   有经验的登山者仔细监测天气状况，以避开气温升高可能导致无预警雪崩的路线。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Picture an "ava-LAUNCH" — snow launching off a mountain, crashing down in a massive, unstoppable wave.
想象一下"ava-LAUNCH"（发射）——雪从山上发射出去，以巨大的、不可阻挡的浪潮倾泻而下。
