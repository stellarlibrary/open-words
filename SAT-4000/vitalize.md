# 🌱 vitalize

🔊 /ˈvaɪtəlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give life, vigor, or energy to; to make more lively or animated; to endow with vitality
**中文：** v. 赋予……生命；激发；使有生气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- energize（激发活力）
- invigorate（使充满活力）
- animate（赋予生气）
- enliven（使活跃）
- revitalize（使复兴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vitalize the economy / vitalize a community / vitalize public discourse / vitalize education**

👉 Example: The government introduced new reforms designed to vitalize the stagnant rural economy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious infrastructure project was designed to **vitalize** the neglected neighborhoods and stimulate local economic development.
   这项雄心勃勃的基础设施项目旨在使被忽视的社区恢复生机并刺激当地经济发展。

2. The new curriculum aims to **vitalize** science education by incorporating hands-on experiments and real-world problem-solving activities.
   新课程旨在通过引入动手实验和解决现实问题的活动来激发科学教育的活力。

3. Community leaders launched several cultural initiatives to **vitalize** the historic town center and attract younger residents back.
   社区领导者发起了多项文化活动，以使历史悠久的城镇中心恢复活力并吸引年轻居民回归。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vitalize" as "vital" + "-ize" (to make) — to vitalize is to make something vital, to breathe life and energy into it.
把"vitalize"想成"vital"（有活力的）+ "-ize"（使……化）——vitalize就是使某物充满生机，向其注入生命和能量。
