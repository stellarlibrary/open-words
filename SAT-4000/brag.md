# 🗣️ brag

🔊 /bræɡ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To boast or speak with excessive pride about one's achievements. (n.) An act of boasting; a boastful statement
**中文：** v. 吹牛；自夸 n. 吹牛；自夸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boast（吹嘘）
- vaunt（夸耀）
- crow（得意洋洋地说）
- swagger（大摇大摆地炫耀）
- flaunt（炫耀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brag about / brag to someone / nothing to brag about / brag that...**

👉 Example: He could not resist the urge to brag about his promotion to every colleague in the office.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accomplished researcher chose not to **brag** about her groundbreaking discovery, preferring to let the results speak.
   这位杰出的研究者选择不炫耀她的突破性发现，而是让成果本身说话。

2. Politicians who constantly **brag** about their achievements often lose credibility among voters who demand substantive policy action.
   那些不断吹嘘自己成就的政客往往在要求实质性政策行动的选民中失去信誉。

3. Despite his tendency to **brag**, the young athlete demonstrated genuine talent that warranted recognition from professional scouts.
   尽管这位年轻运动员有吹牛的倾向，但他展现出的真正才华值得职业球探的认可。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brag" sounding like "drag" — a bragger drags on and on about themselves, boring everyone around them.
把"brag"想成听起来像"drag（拖拽）"——吹牛的人喋喋不休地说自己，让周围的人都感到厌烦。
