# 🛏️ abed

🔊 /əˈbɛd/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) In bed; on a bed; confined to bed
**中文：** adv. 在床上，卧床

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- in bed（在床上）
- resting（休息中）
- reclining（躺着的）
- bedridden（卧床不起的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lie abed / stay abed / remain abed / still abed**

👉 Example: The poet often lay abed until noon, composing verses in his mind before rising.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The elderly scholar remained **abed** for weeks, slowly recovering from a prolonged illness.
   这位年迈的学者卧床数周，慢慢从长期疾病中恢复。

2. She was still **abed** when the urgent telegram arrived at her country estate early that morning.
   那天清晨紧急电报送到她的乡间庄园时，她还在床上。

3. The wounded soldiers lay **abed** in the makeshift hospital, awaiting desperately needed medical supplies.
   受伤的士兵们躺在临时医院的床上，等待急需的医疗物资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abed" = "a-" (on/in) + "bed" — literally means "in bed." It is the simplest word to remember!
"Abed" = "a-"（在……上）+ "bed"（床）——字面意思就是"在床上"，最简单好记！
