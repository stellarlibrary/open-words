# ⛪ parochial

🔊 /pəˈroʊkiəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to a church parish; having a narrow or limited outlook; provincial
**中文：** adj. 教区的；地方范围的；狭小的；狭隘的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- provincial（狭隘的）
- insular（与世隔绝的）
- narrow-minded（心胸狭窄的）
- limited（有限的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **parochial school / parochial outlook / parochial interests / parochial attitudes**

👉 Example: Critics accused the senator of taking a parochial view that ignored broader national concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The council members were criticized for their **parochial** focus on local disputes while ignoring the larger regional economic challenges.
   委员会成员因狭隘地关注地方争端而忽视更大的区域经济挑战而受到批评。

2. A truly educated citizen must transcend **parochial** perspectives and develop an appreciation for diverse cultures and global issues.
   一个真正受过教育的公民必须超越狭隘的视角，培养对多元文化和全球问题的理解。

3. The university sought to broaden students' horizons beyond the **parochial** confines of their immediate communities and familiar surroundings.
   大学力图拓宽学生的视野，使其超越直接社区和熟悉环境的狭隘局限。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "parochial" as "parish-local" — like someone who never leaves their parish and has a narrow view of the world.
把"parochial"想成"parish（教区）+ local（本地的）"——像一个从不离开自己教区、对世界认识非常狭隘的人。
