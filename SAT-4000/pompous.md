# 🎭 pompous

🔊 /ˈpɑːmpəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Affectedly grand or self-important; excessively elevated or ornate in style or manner
**中文：** adj. 自大的；浮夸的；华而不实的；爱炫耀的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pretentious（自命不凡的）
- arrogant（傲慢的）
- grandiose（浮夸的）
- ostentatious（炫耀的）
- bombastic（夸大的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pompous speech / pompous manner / pompous tone / pompous official**

👉 Example: The professor's pompous lecture style alienated students who preferred a more approachable tone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator delivered a **pompous** address filled with grandiose promises that few in the audience believed.
   那位参议员发表了一篇自大的演讲，充满了鲜有听众相信的浮夸承诺。

2. His **pompous** demeanor at formal events made colleagues reluctant to engage him in casual conversation.
   他在正式场合中自命不凡的举止使同事们不愿与他随意交谈。

3. The critic dismissed the novel as **pompous** and overwrought, lacking the subtlety of the author's earlier works.
   评论家认为这部小说华而不实、过于做作，缺乏作者早期作品的细腻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pomp-ous" as "full of pomp" — someone who acts with exaggerated grandeur and self-importance, like a peacock strutting around.
把"pompous"想成"充满浮华的"——一个举止夸张、自以为是的人，就像孔雀四处炫耀。
