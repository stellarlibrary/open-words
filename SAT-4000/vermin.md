# 🐀 vermin

🔊 /ˈvɜːrmɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wild animals or insects that are harmful to crops, food, or livestock; parasites; also used figuratively to describe despicable people
**中文：** n. 害虫；害兽；寄生虫；歹徒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pests（害虫）
- parasites（寄生虫）
- rodents（啮齿动物）
- nuisance animals（有害动物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infested with vermin / exterminate vermin / vermin control / overrun by vermin**

👉 Example: The abandoned warehouse was infested with vermin that posed a serious public health risk.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The public health department dispatched inspectors after residents reported that the building was overrun by **vermin** and rodents.
   居民举报该建筑被害虫和啮齿动物侵占后，公共卫生部门派出了检查人员。

2. Medieval granaries employed cats specifically to control the **vermin** that threatened essential food supplies during harsh winters.
   中世纪的粮仓专门饲养猫来控制在严冬期间威胁重要食物供应的害兽。

3. The dictator's propaganda dehumanized political opponents by systematically referring to them as **vermin** unworthy of basic civil rights.
   独裁者的宣传将政治对手系统性地称为害虫，使其非人化，认为他们不配享有基本公民权利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vermin" — it sounds like "verm-in" where "verm" relates to worms (Latin "vermis"). Vermin are the unwanted creatures that worm their way into your home.
把"vermin"想成"verm-in"——"verm"与蠕虫有关（拉丁语"vermis"）。vermin就是那些钻进你家里的不速之客。
