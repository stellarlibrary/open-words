# 🔒 irreversible

🔊 /ˌɪrɪˈvɜːrsəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Impossible to reverse, undo, or restore to a previous condition; permanent and unalterable
**中文：** 不可逆的；不能取消的；不能翻转的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permanent（永久的）
- irrevocable（不可撤回的）
- unalterable（不可改变的）
- irreparable（不可修复的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irreversible damage / irreversible change / irreversible process / irreversible decline**

👉 Example: Scientists warned that the irreversible damage to the ecosystem could take centuries to manifest fully.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee concluded that prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants had caused **irreversible** harm to the surrounding wetland habitats.
   委员会得出结论，长期暴露于工业污染物已对周围的湿地栖息地造成了不可逆的损害。

2. Once the chemical reaction reaches a critical threshold, it becomes **irreversible** and cannot be halted by any known intervention.
   一旦化学反应达到临界阈值，它就变得不可逆，且无法通过任何已知手段阻止。

3. Historians argue that the technological revolution brought about **irreversible** transformations in the structure of modern society and labor.
   历史学家认为，技术革命给现代社会和劳动结构带来了不可逆转的变革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ir-reverse-ible" as "not able to reverse" — like a one-way street where you can never turn back.
把"irreversible"拆分为"ir-（不）+ reverse（逆转）+ -ible（能够）"——不能逆转的，就像一条永远无法回头的单行道。
