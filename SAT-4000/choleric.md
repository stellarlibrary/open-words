# 😤 choleric

🔊 /ˈkɒlərɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easily angered; bad-tempered; irascible; relating to the choleric temperament characterized by irritability
**中文：** adj. 易怒的；暴躁的；胆汁质的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irascible（易怒的）
- irritable（暴躁的）
- testy（急躁的）
- petulant（任性易怒的）
- cantankerous（脾气暴躁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **choleric temperament / choleric disposition / choleric outburst / choleric response**

👉 Example: His choleric temperament made it difficult for colleagues to collaborate with him on extended projects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **choleric** professor was known for delivering scathing rebukes to students who arrived unprepared for seminars.
   这位**暴躁的**教授以严厉斥责毫无准备来参加研讨会的学生而闻名。

2. Her **choleric** disposition worsened under the mounting pressures of the quarter-end financial audit and review.
   在季末财务审计和审查的重重压力下，她**易怒的**性情变得更加严重。

3. Medieval physicians believed that a **choleric** temperament resulted from an excess of yellow bile in the body.
   中世纪的医生认为**胆汁质**性情是由体内黄胆汁过多引起的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "choleric" as "choler + ic" — "choler" meant bile or anger in ancient medicine, so choleric means full of anger.
把"choleric"拆分为"choler + ic"——"choler"在古代医学中意为胆汁或愤怒，因此choleric意为充满愤怒的。
