# 🔥 concupiscence

🔊 /kɑːnˈkjuːpɪsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Strong sensual desire; lust; an intense longing for something, especially of a sexual nature
**中文：** n. 强烈的邪欲；强烈的性欲；色欲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lust（色欲）
- desire（欲望）
- lasciviousness（淫荡）
- lechery（好色）
- carnality（肉欲）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concupiscence of the flesh / overcome concupiscence / sinful concupiscence / unbridled concupiscence**

👉 Example: Medieval theologians frequently warned against the dangers of unchecked concupiscence in their sermons.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that **concupiscence** must be governed by reason if one hopes to achieve genuine moral virtue.
   这位哲学家认为，如果一个人希望获得真正的道德美德，就必须以理性来控制欲望。

2. In classical theology, **concupiscence** was regarded as an inherent human weakness that required constant spiritual vigilance to overcome.
   在古典神学中，情欲被视为人类固有的弱点，需要不断的精神警觉来克服。

3. The novel explored how unchecked **concupiscence** led to the protagonist's moral decline and eventual ruin in society.
   这部小说探讨了不加控制的邪欲如何导致主人公道德沦丧并最终在社会中走向毁灭。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con + cupid + escence" — Cupid is the god of desire, so concupiscence means being overwhelmed by intense desire.
把"concupiscence"想成"con + cupid（丘比特）+ escence"——丘比特是欲望之神，所以concupiscence意味着被强烈的欲望所支配。
