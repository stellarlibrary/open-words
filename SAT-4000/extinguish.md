# 🧯 extinguish

🔊 /ɪkˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To put out a fire or light; to suppress or destroy completely; to pay off a debt
**中文：** v. 熄灭；压制；偿清；消灭；扑灭；使黯然

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quench（扑灭）
- smother（闷熄）
- suppress（压制）
- douse（浇灭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extinguish a fire / extinguish hopes / extinguish a debt / extinguish all light**

👉 Example: Firefighters worked through the night to extinguish the blaze that engulfed the warehouse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The firefighters managed to **extinguish** the wildfire before it reached the residential neighborhoods surrounding the forest.
   消防员在野火蔓延到森林周围的居民区之前成功将其扑灭。

2. The authoritarian regime sought to **extinguish** all forms of political dissent through censorship and intimidation tactics.
   独裁政权试图通过审查和恐吓手段来压制一切形式的政治异见。

3. Years of repeated failures gradually **extinguished** her once passionate enthusiasm for pursuing a career in academic research.
   多年反复的失败逐渐熄灭了她曾经对从事学术研究事业的热情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extinguish" as "ex- (out) + tinguish (from Latin stinguere, to quench)" — to quench out, to put out completely.
把"extinguish"想成"ex-（出去）+ tinguish（来自拉丁语stinguere，熄灭）"——彻底浇灭、完全熄灭。
