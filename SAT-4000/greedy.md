# 🤑 greedy

🔊 /ˈɡriːdi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having an intense and selfish desire for something, especially wealth or food; eager to have more than one needs
**中文：** adj. 贪食的；贪婪的；渴望的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- avaricious（贪财的）
- covetous（贪婪的）
- rapacious（贪得无厌的）
- gluttonous（贪食的）
- acquisitive（贪求的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **greedy for power / greedy corporation / greedy eyes / greedy for knowledge**

👉 Example: The greedy landlord raised rents far beyond what tenants could reasonably afford.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigative report exposed how **greedy** executives had systematically exploited vulnerable workers to maximize their own corporate profits.
   调查报告揭露了贪婪的高管如何系统地剥削弱势工人以最大化他们自己的企业利润。

2. Critics argued that **greedy** speculation in the housing market was the primary catalyst behind the devastating financial crisis.
   评论人士认为，房地产市场的贪婪投机是那场毁灭性金融危机背后的主要催化剂。

3. Though often perceived as **greedy**, the young scholar's insatiable appetite for knowledge ultimately led to groundbreaking scientific discoveries.
   虽然这位年轻学者常被视为贪得无厌，但他对知识的强烈渴望最终带来了突破性的科学发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "greed-y" as full of "greed" — someone who is greedy always wants more, like a bottomless pit that can never be filled.
把"greedy"想成充满"greed（贪婪）"的——一个greedy的人总是想要更多，像一个永远填不满的无底洞。
