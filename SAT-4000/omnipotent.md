# 👑 omnipotent

🔊 /ɑːmˈnɪpətənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having unlimited or universal power; able to do anything; all-powerful
**中文：** adj. 无所不能的；全能的；有无限权力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- all-powerful（全能的）
- almighty（万能的）
- supreme（至高无上的）
- invincible（不可战胜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **omnipotent ruler / omnipotent deity / omnipotent force / omnipotent authority / seemingly omnipotent**

👉 Example: Ancient civilizations often worshipped omnipotent deities who were believed to control every aspect of nature.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher questioned whether an **omnipotent** being could create a stone so heavy that it could not be lifted.
   这位哲学家质疑一个全能的存在是否能创造一块连自己都举不起的石头。

2. The dictator cultivated an image of **omnipotent** authority, suppressing any dissent that threatened his absolute power.
   这位独裁者塑造了全能权威的形象，压制任何威胁其绝对权力的异议。

3. Modern technology, though remarkably advanced, is far from **omnipotent** in solving the complex challenges facing humanity.
   现代技术虽然非常先进，但在解决人类面临的复杂挑战方面远非全能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "omni-potent" as "omni" (all) + "potent" (powerful) — someone who is potent in all ways, all-powerful.
把"omnipotent"想成"omni（所有）"加"potent（强大的）"——在所有方面都强大的，即全能的。
