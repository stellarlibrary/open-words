# 🚗 bumper

🔊 /ˈbʌmpər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Exceptionally large or abundant. (n.) A protective bar at the front or rear of a vehicle; a buffer or shock absorber
**中文：** adj. 丰盛的，丰富的 n. 缓冲器，保险杆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- plentiful（充足的）
- bountiful（大量的）
- buffer（缓冲器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bumper crop / bumper harvest / bumper year / bumper sticker / front bumper**

👉 Example: Favorable weather conditions produced a bumper crop of wheat across the entire agricultural region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural ministry reported a **bumper** harvest this year, yielding thirty percent more grain than the previous annual average.
   农业部报告今年获得了丰收，粮食产量比往年平均水平高出百分之三十。

2. The car's front **bumper** absorbed most of the impact during the collision, thereby preventing serious structural damage to the vehicle.
   汽车的前保险杠在碰撞中吸收了大部分冲击力，从而防止了车辆严重的结构损坏。

3. It was a **bumper** year for the publishing industry, with book sales reaching unprecedented levels across all major categories.
   这是出版业丰收的一年，各主要类别的图书销售额均达到前所未有的水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bump" + "-er" — something that bumps into things (buffer), or so full it's bumping over the edges (abundant).
把"bumper"想成"bump（碰撞）+ -er"——碰撞时的缓冲器，或者满到溢出来的丰盛。
