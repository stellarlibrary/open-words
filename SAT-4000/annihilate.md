# 💥 annihilate

🔊 /əˈnaɪəleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *v. to destroy completely; to defeat utterly; to abolish | 动词：歼灭；战胜；废止*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To destroy utterly and completely; to reduce to nothing; to defeat decisively or render void.
**中文：** 彻底摧毁；化为乌有；决定性地击败或使之无效。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destroy（摧毁）
- obliterate（消灭）
- eradicate（根除）
- exterminate（灭绝）
- demolish（拆毁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **annihilate an army, annihilate the opposition, annihilate all traces, completely annihilated**
👉 Example: The invading forces threatened to annihilate every fortification along the contested border region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The superior fleet moved to **annihilate** the enemy's naval forces before they could regroup and mount a counterattack.
   优势舰队行动起来歼灭敌方海军力量，以免其重新集结并发动反攻。

2. The volcanic eruption had the power to **annihilate** entire villages, leaving nothing but ash and desolation in its wake.
   火山爆发有能力摧毁整个村庄，所到之处只留下灰烬和荒凉。

3. The court's landmark ruling effectively **annihilated** the outdated statute that had long restricted freedom of expression.
   法院的里程碑式裁决有效地废止了那项长期限制言论自由的过时法规。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Annihilate" contains "nihil" (Latin for nothing) — to annihilate is to reduce something to absolute nothingness, as if it never existed.
"Annihilate"包含"nihil"（拉丁语，意为"无"）——歼灭就是把某物化为绝对的虚无，仿佛它从未存在过。
