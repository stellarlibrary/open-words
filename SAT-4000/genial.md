# 😊 genial

🔊 /ˈdʒiːniəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Friendly, cheerful, and warm in manner or disposition; pleasantly kind and welcoming
**中文：** adj. 亲切的，友好的；和蔼的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- affable（和蔼的）
- cordial（热忱的）
- amiable（友善的）
- congenial（意气相投的）
- warm-hearted（热心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **genial host / genial smile / genial manner / genial personality / genial disposition**

👉 Example: The professor's genial manner immediately put nervous first-year students at ease during orientation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **genial** demeanor helped establish a positive atmosphere during the delicate and high-stakes diplomatic negotiations.
   大使亲切的态度有助于在微妙且高风险的外交谈判中营造积极的氛围。

2. Despite the pressure of the tournament, the chess grandmaster maintained a **genial** composure that earned widespread admiration from spectators.
   尽管面临锦标赛的压力，这位国际象棋大师保持了亲切的风度，赢得了观众的广泛钦佩。

3. Her **genial** approach to leadership fostered a collaborative work environment where employees felt valued and genuinely respected.
   她亲切的领导方式营造了一种协作的工作环境，让员工感到被重视和真正尊重。
