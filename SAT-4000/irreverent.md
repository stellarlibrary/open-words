# 😏 irreverent

🔊 /ɪˈrevərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing a lack of respect for people or things that are generally taken seriously; disrespectful in a bold or humorous way
**中文：** 不敬的；无礼的；不尊重的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disrespectful（不敬的）
- impudent（无礼的）
- flippant（轻率的）
- insolent（傲慢的）
- cheeky（放肆的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irreverent humor / irreverent attitude / irreverent tone / irreverent remarks**

👉 Example: His irreverent humor during the ceremony shocked the more traditional members of the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The columnist's **irreverent** commentary on political institutions attracted both admiration and fierce criticism from readers nationwide.
   该专栏作家对政治机构不敬的评论在全国范围内既赢得了赞赏，也招致了猛烈的批评。

2. Many scholars found his **irreverent** treatment of classical literature refreshing, though purists considered it deeply offensive.
   许多学者认为他对古典文学不敬的处理方式令人耳目一新，但纯粹主义者认为这极具冒犯性。

3. The artist became famous for her **irreverent** approach to religious iconography, challenging centuries of established artistic tradition.
   这位艺术家因其对宗教图像不敬的处理方式而闻名，挑战了数百年来的既有艺术传统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ir-reverent" as "not reverent" — the prefix "ir-" means "not," so irreverent literally means not showing reverence or respect.
把"irreverent"拆分为"ir-（不）+ reverent（尊敬的）"——不尊敬的，即不敬的、无礼的。
