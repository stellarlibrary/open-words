# 🛡️ immune system

🔊 /ɪˈmjuːn ˈsɪstəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The complex network of cells, tissues, and organs in the body that work together to defend against harmful pathogens, infections, and diseases.
**中文：** n. 免疫系统

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- body's defense system（身体防御系统）
- immunity（免疫力）
- natural defense mechanism（自然防御机制）
- biological defense（生物防御）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strengthen the immune system / weakened immune system / immune system response / boost the immune system**

👉 Example: A balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help strengthen the immune system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medical researchers have discovered that chronic stress can significantly compromise the **immune system** and increase vulnerability to infections.
   医学研究人员发现，长期压力会显著损害免疫系统，并增加对感染的易感性。

2. The autoimmune disorder causes the patient's **immune system** to mistakenly attack healthy cells and tissues within the body.
   这种自身免疫性疾病导致患者的免疫系统错误地攻击体内的健康细胞和组织。

3. Adequate sleep and regular exercise are essential for maintaining a robust **immune system** capable of resisting common illnesses.
   充足的睡眠和规律的运动对于维持一个能够抵抗常见疾病的强健免疫系统至关重要。

把"immune system"想成你身体的私人军队——一支由士兵（白细胞）组成的队伍，巡逻并保护你免受入侵者的攻击。
