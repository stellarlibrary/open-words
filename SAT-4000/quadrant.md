# 📐 quadrant

🔊 /ˈkwɒdrənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) One quarter of a circle or its area; one of four sections of a coordinate plane; an instrument for measuring angles
**中文：** n. 象限；象限仪；四分之一圆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quarter（四分之一）
- sector（扇区）
- segment（部分）
- section（区域）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **upper/lower quadrant / first quadrant / quadrant of a circle / quadrant analysis**

👉 Example: The data points were plotted in the first quadrant of the coordinate plane.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician instructed her students to plot the function exclusively within the first **quadrant** of the Cartesian plane.
   数学家指导她的学生将函数仅绘制在笛卡尔平面的第一象限内。

2. Surgeons identified the tumor in the upper right **quadrant** of the patient's abdomen during the diagnostic imaging procedure.
   外科医生在诊断成像过程中发现肿瘤位于患者腹部的右上象限。

3. Ancient navigators relied on the **quadrant** to measure the altitude of celestial bodies and determine their position at sea.
   古代航海家依靠象限仪测量天体的高度并确定他们在海上的位置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quad-rant" — "quad" means four, so a quadrant is one of four equal parts, like cutting a pizza into four slices.
把"quadrant"想成"quad（四）+ rant"——"quad"意为四，所以象限就是四等分之一，就像把比萨切成四块。
