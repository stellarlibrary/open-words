# 📋 tabulate

🔊 /ˈtæbjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a flat surface; broad and flat. (v.) To arrange data or figures in a table or systematic list
**中文：** v. 把…制成表格

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- categorize（分类）
- classify（归类）
- chart（制图）
- systematize（系统化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tabulate results / tabulate data / tabulate votes / tabulate figures**

👉 Example: The election officials worked through the night to tabulate all the votes from each district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research assistant was asked to **tabulate** all experimental results before the team meeting on Friday morning.
   研究助理被要求在周五上午的团队会议之前将所有实验结果制成表格。

2. Officials needed several days to **tabulate** the survey responses received from thousands of participants across the country.
   官员们需要几天时间才能将全国数千名参与者的调查回复制成表格。

3. The accountant used specialized software to **tabulate** quarterly earnings and present them in a clear, readable format.
   会计师使用专业软件将季度收益制成表格，并以清晰易读的格式呈现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tab-ulate" — like creating tabs in a spreadsheet to organize information into rows and columns of a table.
把"tabulate"想成"tab-ulate"——就像在电子表格中创建标签，将信息整理成行和列的表格。
