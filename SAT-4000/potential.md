# 🌱 potential

🔊 /pəˈtɛnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing the capacity to develop into something in the future; latent. (n.) Latent qualities or abilities that may be developed; possibility
**中文：** adj. 潜在的；可能的 n. 潜能；可能性；电势

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- possible（可能的）
- latent（潜在的）
- prospective（预期的）
- capability（能力）
- promise（前途）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **potential risk / potential candidate / reach one's potential / growth potential / full potential**

👉 Example: The young researcher showed tremendous potential in the field of molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee identified several **potential** risks associated with the proposed infrastructure project before granting final approval.
   委员会在给予最终批准之前确定了与拟议基础设施项目相关的几个潜在风险。

2. Educators believe that every student possesses untapped **potential** that can be unlocked through personalized instruction and consistent encouragement.
   教育工作者认为每个学生都拥有未被开发的潜能，可以通过个性化教学和持续鼓励来释放。

3. The startup demonstrated significant **potential** for disrupting the traditional market with its innovative technology and scalable business model.
   这家初创公司凭借其创新技术和可扩展的商业模式，展示了颠覆传统市场的巨大潜力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "potential" as "potent + ial" — something that is potentially potent, meaning it has the hidden power waiting to be unleashed.
把"potential"想成"potent（强有力的）+ ial"——某种潜在地强有力的事物，意味着它有等待释放的隐藏力量。
