# 🌟 aptitude

🔊 /ˈæptɪˌtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A natural ability or talent; an inherent capacity for learning or doing something well; suitability or fitness.

**中文：** 天资；天赋；自然倾向；适宜；才能。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- talent（才能）
- gift（天赋）
- flair（天分）
- inclination（倾向）
- proficiency（熟练）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** aptitude for, aptitude test, natural aptitude, show aptitude, demonstrate aptitude, academic aptitude

**Example:** She displayed a remarkable aptitude for mathematics from an early age.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship committee evaluates each candidate's **aptitude** for critical thinking and independent academic research.
   （奖学金委员会评估每位候选人在批判性思维和独立学术研究方面的天资。）

2. His natural **aptitude** for foreign languages enabled him to achieve fluency in three languages before adulthood.
   （他在外语方面的天赋使他在成年前就流利掌握了三门语言。）

3. The standardized **aptitude** test measures a student's potential for success in graduate-level academic programs.
   （标准化能力倾向测试衡量学生在研究生水平学术项目中取得成功的潜力。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Aptitude" starts with "apt" (fitting, naturally inclined). If you are "apt" at something, you have the "aptitude" — the natural talent for it.

"Aptitude" 以 "apt"（恰当的、有倾向的）开头。如果你 "apt" 于某事，那你就有这方面的 "aptitude"——天赋。
