# 🤝 intervene

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈviːn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To come between disputing parties; to take action to improve or prevent a situation; to occur between events
**中文：** v. 干涉；调停；插入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mediate（调解）
- intercede（斡旋）
- interfere（干预）
- step in（介入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intervene in a conflict / intervene on behalf of / intervene to prevent / military intervention**

👉 Example: The United Nations decided to intervene in the conflict to protect civilian populations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government chose to **intervene** in the financial crisis by implementing emergency measures to stabilize the collapsing banking sector.
   政府选择干预金融危机，通过实施紧急措施来稳定崩溃的银行业。

2. A bystander bravely decided to **intervene** when she witnessed the altercation escalating into a potentially dangerous physical confrontation.
   一位旁观者在目睹争吵升级为可能具有危险性的身体冲突时，勇敢地决定介入。

3. If diplomatic negotiations fail, the international community may be compelled to **intervene** militarily to restore regional peace and stability.
   如果外交谈判失败，国际社会可能被迫进行军事干预以恢复地区和平与稳定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-vene" as "inter" (between) + "vene" (come) — to come between two sides, stepping in to help resolve a dispute.
把"intervene"想成"inter"（之间）+ "vene"（来）——走到两方之间，介入以帮助解决争端。
