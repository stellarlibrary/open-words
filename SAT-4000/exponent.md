# 📐 exponent

🔊 /ɪkˈspoʊnənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who advocates or champions an idea or theory; a number indicating the power to which a base is raised in mathematics
**中文：** n. 指数；典型；说明者；倡导者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advocate（倡导者）
- proponent（支持者）
- champion（拥护者）
- representative（代表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a leading exponent of / an exponent of democracy / mathematical exponent / chief exponent**

👉 Example: She is widely regarded as the foremost exponent of minimalist architecture in contemporary design.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher was a prominent **exponent** of individual liberty, influencing generations of political thinkers across the Western world.
   这位哲学家是个人自由的杰出倡导者，影响了西方世界好几代政治思想家。

2. In mathematics, the **exponent** determines how many times the base number is multiplied by itself in a given expression.
   在数学中，指数决定了给定表达式中底数自乘的次数。

3. As the leading **exponent** of progressive education reform, she devoted her career to transforming public school curricula nationwide.
   作为进步教育改革的首要倡导者，她毕生致力于改革全国公立学校的课程体系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-pon-ent" as "one who puts out (expounds) ideas" — an exponent is someone who sets forth and champions a cause or concept.
把"exponent"想成"把想法展示出来（expound）的人"——exponent是阐述和倡导某一事业或概念的人。
