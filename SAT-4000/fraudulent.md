# 🎭 fraudulent

🔊 /ˈfrɔːdʒələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Obtained, done by, or involving deception, especially criminal deception; dishonest or deceitful
**中文：** 欺诈的，欺骗性的；不正的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deceitful（欺诈的）
- dishonest（不诚实的）
- deceptive（欺骗性的）
- counterfeit（伪造的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fraudulent activity / fraudulent claim / fraudulent scheme / fraudulent transaction**

👉 Example: The company was prosecuted for engaging in fraudulent accounting practices.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that the executive had been involved in **fraudulent** financial reporting for over a decade.
   调查显示，这位高管十多年来一直参与欺诈性财务报告。

2. Consumers are advised to remain vigilant against **fraudulent** websites that attempt to steal personal information and credentials.
   消费者被建议对试图窃取个人信息和凭证的欺诈网站保持警惕。

3. The court ruled that the contract was void because it had been obtained through **fraudulent** misrepresentation of material facts.
   法院裁定该合同无效，因为它是通过对重要事实的欺诈性虚假陈述而获得的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fraudulent" as "fraud" + "ulent" — it literally contains the word "fraud," meaning deception or trickery.
把"fraudulent"想成"fraud"（欺诈）+ "ulent"——它字面上包含"fraud"这个词，意为欺骗或诡计。
