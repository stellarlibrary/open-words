# 😔 pauper

🔊 /ˈpɔːpər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A very poor person; one who depends on public charity or relief. (adj.) Of or relating to paupers
**中文：** n. 乞丐；穷人；靠救济度日者 adj. 贫民的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beggar（乞丐）
- indigent（贫困者）
- destitute（赤贫者）
- mendicant（乞讨者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **live as a pauper / die a pauper / pauper's grave / reduced to a pauper**

👉 Example: After losing his fortune, the once-wealthy merchant was reduced to living as a pauper.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philanthropist dedicated her life to improving conditions for every **pauper** struggling to survive in the city.
   这位慈善家毕生致力于改善城市中每一个穷人的生存条件。

2. Historical records reveal that many a brilliant artist died a **pauper**, unrecognized and impoverished during their lifetime.
   历史记录显示，许多杰出的艺术家在世时默默无闻、穷困潦倒，最终以贫民身份去世。

3. The reform legislation sought to provide adequate shelter and sustenance for every **pauper** dependent on public relief.
   这项改革立法旨在为每一位依赖公共救济的穷人提供充足的住所和食物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pauper" as "poor-per" — a "poor person" who has nothing left and depends on charity.
把"pauper"想成"poor-per"——一个什么都没有、依赖施舍的"穷人"。
