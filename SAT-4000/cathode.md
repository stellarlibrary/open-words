# ⚡ cathode

🔊 /ˈkæθoʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The negatively charged electrode in an electrolytic cell or electron tube, toward which positively charged ions migrate
**中文：** n. 阴极（电解）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- negative electrode（负极）
- negative terminal（负端）
- negative pole（阴极）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cathode ray / cathode ray tube / cathode material / cathode reaction**

👉 Example: The cathode ray tube was the primary display technology in televisions for most of the twentieth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During electrolysis, positive ions migrate toward the **cathode**, where they gain electrons and undergo chemical reduction.
   在电解过程中，正离子向阴极迁移，在那里获得电子并发生化学还原反应。

2. Researchers developed a novel **cathode** material that significantly improved the energy density and lifespan of lithium-ion batteries.
   研究人员开发了一种新型阴极材料，显著提高了锂离子电池的能量密度和使用寿命。

3. The corrosion engineer explained that the **cathode** in the galvanic cell remains protected while the anode gradually deteriorates.
   腐蚀工程师解释说，在原电池中阴极受到保护，而阳极则逐渐腐蚀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cathode" as "cat-ode" — a cat sitting on the negative side, attracting all the positive charges (ions) toward it, just like cats attract attention.
把"cathode"想成"cat-ode"——一只猫坐在负极一侧，吸引所有正电荷（离子）向它靠近，就像猫总是吸引注意力一样。
