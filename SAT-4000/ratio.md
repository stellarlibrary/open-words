# 🔢 ratio

🔊 /ˈreɪʃioʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quantitative relation between two amounts showing how many times one value contains or is contained within the other
**中文：** n. 比；比率；比例

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proportion（比例）
- rate（比率）
- percentage（百分比）
- fraction（分数）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ratio of A to B / debt-to-equity ratio / in the ratio / ratio between**

👉 Example: The teacher maintained a student-to-instructor ratio of fifteen to one in every class.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ratio** of applicants to available positions exceeded twenty to one, reflecting the program's extraordinary competitive selectivity.
   申请人与可用职位的比例超过二十比一，反映了该项目极高的竞争性选拔标准。

2. Financial analysts examined the company's debt-to-equity **ratio** to determine whether the firm remained a sound long-term investment.
   金融分析师研究了该公司的债务与权益比率，以确定该公司是否仍然是一项稳健的长期投资。

3. Maintaining the proper **ratio** of ingredients is essential for producing consistent results in advanced chemical synthesis procedures.
   保持适当的成分比例对于在高级化学合成程序中产生一致的结果至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ratio" as "ration" without the "n" — both involve dividing things into measured parts; a ratio measures how parts relate.
把"ratio"想成"ration（配给）"去掉"n"——两者都涉及将事物分成可度量的部分；比率衡量各部分之间的关系。
