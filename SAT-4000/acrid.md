# 🔥 acrid

🔊 /ˈæk.rɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a strong, sharp, and unpleasant smell or taste; bitterly pungent; harsh or severe in manner.
**中文：** 辛辣的；苦的；刻薄的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pungent（刺鼻的）
- caustic（腐蚀性的）
- bitter（苦涩的）
- sharp（尖锐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acrid smell/odor/taste/smoke/fumes**, **acrid remark/tone**

👉 Example: The acrid smell of burning rubber filled the entire neighborhood after the factory fire.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. An **acrid** odor of chemical smoke drifted through the laboratory after the failed experiment.
   实验失败后，一股刺鼻的化学烟雾气味弥漫在整个实验室中。

2. The critic's **acrid** review of the novel left the young author feeling discouraged and disheartened.
   评论家对这部小说的尖刻评价让这位年轻作者感到沮丧和灰心。

3. Residents complained about the **acrid** fumes emanating from the industrial plant on the outskirts of town.
   居民们抱怨城镇郊区工厂散发出的刺鼻烟雾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ACRID" sounds like "acid" — both describe something sharp, biting, and unpleasant to the senses.
"ACRID"听起来像"acid（酸）"——两者都描述尖锐、刺激、令人不适的感觉，想象闻到酸液散发的刺鼻气味。
