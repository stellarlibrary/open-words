# 💡 intelligible

🔊 /ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be understood; clear and comprehensible in expression or meaning
**中文：** adj. 可理解的；明白易懂的；清楚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comprehensible（可理解的）
- understandable（易懂的）
- lucid（清晰的）
- coherent（连贯的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **barely intelligible / perfectly intelligible / intelligible to someone / intelligible speech**

👉 Example: The lecture notes were well-organized and perfectly intelligible to students at every level.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the heavy background noise, the speaker's words remained **intelligible** to those seated in the front rows of the auditorium.
   尽管背景噪音很大，坐在礼堂前排的人仍能听清演讲者的话。

2. The scientist made a deliberate effort to present her findings in a manner **intelligible** to the general public.
   这位科学家特意以公众能够理解的方式展示她的研究成果。

3. His handwriting was barely **intelligible**, forcing the committee to request a typed version of the submitted manuscript.
   他的字迹几乎无法辨认，迫使委员会要求提交打印版的手稿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intellig-ible" — if something is "able" to reach your "intelligence," it is intelligible, meaning you can understand it.
把"intelligible"想成"能(ible)进入你的智力(intellig)的"——如果某事能够被你的智力接收，它就是可理解的。
