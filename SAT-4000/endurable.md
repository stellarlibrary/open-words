# 💪 endurable

🔊 /ɪnˈdjʊərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being endured; bearable or tolerable
**中文：** adj. 能忍耐的；可忍受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bearable（可忍受的）
- tolerable（可容忍的）
- sufferable（可忍耐的）
- supportable（可承受的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **barely endurable / endurable pain / endurable conditions / make something endurable**

👉 Example: The harsh winter conditions were barely endurable, even for the most experienced explorers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The discomfort was **endurable** only because the patients knew the treatment would eventually lead to a complete recovery.
   这种不适之所以可以忍受，只是因为患者们知道治疗最终会带来完全康复。

2. With proper shelter and adequate supplies, the extreme cold of the arctic expedition became **endurable** for the research team.
   有了适当的住所和充足的补给，北极探险中的极端寒冷对研究团队来说变得可以忍受。

3. The long hours of repetitive labor were made **endurable** by the camaraderie and mutual support among the factory workers.
   工友之间的友谊和相互支持使长时间的重复劳动变得可以忍受。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "endur-able" as "endure + able" — if something is endurable, you are able to endure it, meaning you can bear it or put up with it.
把"endurable"想成"endure（忍耐）+ able（能够）"——如果某事是endurable的，你就能够忍受它。
