# 💰 parsimonious

🔊 /ˌpɑːrsɪˈmoʊniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely unwilling to spend money or use resources; excessively frugal or stingy
**中文：** adj. 吝啬的；过于节俭的；小气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stingy（吝啬的）
- miserly（守财奴般的）
- frugal（节俭的）
- tightfisted（吝啬的）
- penurious（一毛不拔的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **parsimonious with money / parsimonious approach / parsimonious spending / parsimonious employer**

👉 Example: The parsimonious landlord refused to repair the building despite collecting high rents from tenants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **parsimonious** executive refused to approve even modest expenditures for essential employee training and workplace safety improvements.
   这位吝啬的高管拒绝批准哪怕是适度的员工培训和工作场所安全改善支出。

2. His **parsimonious** habits, developed during years of poverty, persisted long after he had accumulated considerable personal wealth.
   他在多年贫困中养成的过度节俭习惯，在积累了可观的个人财富后仍然持续着。

3. Critics condemned the government's **parsimonious** allocation of funds to public education as short-sighted and detrimental to national progress.
   批评者谴责政府对公共教育的吝啬拨款是目光短浅且有损国家进步的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "parsi-MONEY-ous" — a parsimonious person is obsessed with holding onto their money and refuses to spend it.
把"parsimonious"想成"parsi-MONEY（钱）-ous"——一个吝啬的人痴迷于守住自己的钱，拒绝花钱。
