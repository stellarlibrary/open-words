# 📅 postdate

🔊 /ˌpoʊstˈdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To assign a date later than the actual date; to occur or exist later than something else
**中文：** v. 填迟……的日期；把……的日期填迟；继……之后

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- follow（在……之后）
- succeed（继……之后）
- come after（随后发生）
- antedate (opposite)（早于，反义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **postdate a check / postdate a document / postdate an event / postdate by several years**

👉 Example: The landlord asked the tenant to postdate the rent check to the first of the following month.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Archaeologists discovered that the pottery fragments **postdate** the original settlement by at least three centuries based on carbon analysis.
   考古学家发现，根据碳分析，这些陶器碎片比最初的定居点至少晚了三个世纪。

2. The attorney advised her client never to **postdate** legal documents, as doing so could raise serious questions of fraud.
   律师建议她的客户永远不要将法律文件的日期填迟，因为这样做可能引发严重的欺诈质疑。

3. Recent geological evidence suggests that the volcanic eruption may **postdate** the extinction event previously attributed to its impact.
   最新的地质证据表明，这次火山喷发可能晚于之前归因于其影响的灭绝事件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "post-date" — "post" means after, so to postdate is to put a date that comes after the actual date.
把"postdate"想成"post-date"——"post"意为之后，所以postdate就是把日期填为实际日期之后的日期。
