# ⚔️ accouter

🔊 /əˈkuː.tər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To equip or furnish with military equipment, clothing, or gear.
**中文：** 装备；供以军用品；供以服装

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equip（装备）
- outfit（配备）
- furnish（供给）
- arm（武装）
- provision（供给）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accouter someone with/in (gear/armor/uniform)**

👉 Example: The regiment was fully accoutered with modern combat equipment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The general ordered his officers to **accouter** every soldier with the latest standard-issue body armor.
   将军命令军官为每名士兵配备最新的制式防弹衣。

2. Medieval knights were lavishly **accoutered** in gleaming plate armor before riding into ceremonial tournaments.
   中世纪骑士在参加仪式性比武前，身上装备着闪亮华丽的板甲。

3. The expedition team was carefully **accoutered** with survival gear suitable for the harsh arctic conditions.
   探险队精心装备了适合严酷北极环境的生存器材。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "a-COOT-er" sounds like "a suit-er" — someone who puts a suit (of armor) on you, equipping you for battle.
accouter 发音像"额酷特"，想象穿上"很酷的特别"装备，就是"装备"的意思。
