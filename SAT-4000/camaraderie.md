# 🤝 camaraderie

🔊 /ˌkɑːməˈrɑːdəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Mutual trust and friendship among people who spend time together; a spirit of warm fellowship and loyalty
**中文：** n. 友情；同志之爱；战友情谊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fellowship（友谊）
- companionship（伙伴关系）
- solidarity（团结）
- brotherhood（兄弟情谊）
- esprit de corps（团队精神）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sense of camaraderie / build camaraderie / camaraderie among colleagues / spirit of camaraderie**

👉 Example: The team-building retreat fostered a strong sense of camaraderie among the new employees.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The soldiers developed a deep **camaraderie** during their months of training, forming bonds that would last a lifetime.
   士兵们在数月的训练中建立了深厚的战友情谊，形成了终生不渝的纽带。

2. A genuine sense of **camaraderie** among research colleagues often leads to greater innovation and more productive collaboration.
   研究同事之间真诚的友谊往往能带来更多创新和更高效的合作。

3. The annual conference served to strengthen the **camaraderie** within the professional community and encourage scholarly exchange.
   年度会议有助于加强专业社区内的友谊并促进学术交流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "camaraderie" as "comrade-rie" — it shares the root with "comrade," meaning a close companion or fellow soldier.
把"camaraderie"想成"comrade-rie"——它和"comrade"（同志、战友）同根，表示亲密伙伴间的情谊。
