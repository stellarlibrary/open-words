# 🎯 tact

🔊 /tækt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A keen sense of what to say or do to avoid giving offense; skill in dealing with others; diplomacy
**中文：** n. 机智，老练

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diplomacy（外交手腕）
- discretion（审慎）
- sensitivity（敏感度）
- finesse（策略）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with tact / show tact / exercise tact / lack of tact / diplomatic tact**

👉 Example: She handled the disagreement with remarkable tact, ensuring neither party felt disrespected.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador demonstrated exceptional **tact** when addressing the territorial dispute between the two neighboring nations.
   大使在处理两个邻国之间的领土争端时表现出了非凡的机智。

2. Managing a diverse team requires considerable **tact**, especially when mediating conflicts that arise from cultural differences.
   管理一个多元化的团队需要相当的老练，尤其是在调解因文化差异而产生的冲突时。

3. His complete lack of **tact** during the press conference alienated journalists and damaged the organization's public reputation.
   他在新闻发布会上完全缺乏机智，疏远了记者并损害了该组织的公众声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tact" as related to "contact" — when you make contact with people, you need tact to handle interactions smoothly.
把"tact"和"contact"（接触）联系起来——当你与人接触时，你需要机智来圆滑地处理互动。
