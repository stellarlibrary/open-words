# ♾️ eternal

🔊 /ɪˈtɜːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting forever; without end or beginning; perpetual and unchanging
**中文：** adj. 永恒的，永远的；不灭的；不朽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- everlasting（永恒的）
- perpetual（永久的）
- immortal（不朽的）
- timeless（永恒的）
- enduring（持久的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eternal life / eternal truth / eternal flame / eternal optimist / eternal gratitude**

👉 Example: The monument was erected as an eternal tribute to those who sacrificed their lives for freedom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Philosophers have long debated whether any moral principles can truly be considered **eternal** and universally applicable across all cultures.
   哲学家们长期以来一直在争论是否有任何道德原则可以被真正视为永恒的且普遍适用于所有文化。

2. The ancient text promised **eternal** wisdom to those who devoted themselves to the disciplined study of sacred knowledge.
   这部古老的典籍向那些致力于严谨研究神圣知识的人们许诺了永恒的智慧。

3. The **eternal** struggle between individual liberty and collective security remains a central challenge in modern governance.
   个人自由与集体安全之间的永恒斗争仍然是现代治理中的核心挑战。
