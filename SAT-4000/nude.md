# 🎭 nude

🔊 /njuːd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Wearing no clothes; bare; unadorned. (n.) A naked human figure as the subject of a painting, sculpture, or photograph
**中文：** adj. 裸体的；无装饰的；与生俱有的 n. 裸体；裸体画

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- naked（裸体的）
- bare（赤裸的）
- unclothed（未穿衣服的）
- undressed（未着装的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nude figure / in the nude / nude portrait / nude sculpture / nude color**
