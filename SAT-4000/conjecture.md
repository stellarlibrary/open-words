# 🤔 conjecture

🔊 /kənˈdʒɛktʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An opinion or conclusion formed on the basis of incomplete information; a guess or speculation. (v.) To form an opinion without sufficient evidence
**中文：** n. 推测，猜想 v. 推测，猜测

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- speculation（推测）
- hypothesis（假设）
- surmise（猜测）
- guess（猜想）
- supposition（假定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mere conjecture / a matter of conjecture / conjecture about / conjecture that**

👉 Example: Without concrete evidence, the detective's theory remained little more than conjecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In the absence of definitive archaeological evidence, the origin of the ancient structure remains a matter of scholarly **conjecture**.
   在缺乏确切考古证据的情况下，这座古代建筑的起源仍然是学术推测的问题。

2. Scientists **conjectured** that the newly discovered exoplanet might possess atmospheric conditions potentially suitable for sustaining life.
   科学家推测这颗新发现的系外行星可能拥有适合维持生命的大气条件。

3. The editorial was criticized for presenting unsubstantiated **conjecture** as though it were established and well-documented scientific fact.
   该社论因将未经证实的猜测当作已确立的、有充分记录的科学事实而受到批评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-JECT-ure" — "ject" means to throw (like "project"), so conjecture is throwing out an idea without proof.
把"conjecture"想成"con-JECT-ure"——"ject"意为抛出（如"project"），所以conjecture就是在没有证据的情况下抛出一个想法。
