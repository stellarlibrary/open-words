# 🐂 obstinate

🔊 /ˈɑːbstɪnət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action; difficult to manage, control, or treat
**中文：** adj. 顽固的；倔强的；难以控制的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stubborn（固执的）
- headstrong（任性的）
- intransigent（不妥协的）
- tenacious（顽强的）
- unyielding（不屈服的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obstinate refusal / obstinate resistance / obstinate child / remain obstinate**

👉 Example: The obstinate negotiator refused to compromise on any of the proposed terms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee faced an **obstinate** opposition that refused to accept any amendments to the original legislation.
   委员会面对顽固的反对派，他们拒绝接受对原始立法的任何修正。

2. Despite mounting evidence against his theory, the researcher remained **obstinate** in defending his original hypothesis.
   尽管越来越多的证据反驳了他的理论，这位研究员仍然固执地捍卫自己最初的假设。

3. The **obstinate** stain on the ancient manuscript proved impossible to remove without damaging the fragile parchment.
   古代手稿上那顽固的污渍被证明无法在不损坏脆弱羊皮纸的情况下去除。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-STIN-ate" as containing "STIN" like "stone" — someone obstinate is as hard and unmovable as a stone.
把"obstinate"想成包含"stin"像"stone（石头）"——一个obstinate的人就像石头一样坚硬、不可动摇。
