# ⚖️ guilty

🔊 /ˈɡɪlti/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Culpable of or responsible for a specified wrongdoing; having a feeling of guilt or remorse
**中文：** adj. 犯罪的；有罪的；自觉有罪的；内疚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- culpable（有罪的）
- remorseful（悔恨的）
- blameworthy（应受谴责的）
- ashamed（惭愧的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **guilty of / guilty conscience / guilty plea / guilty verdict / feel guilty about**

👉 Example: The defendant was found guilty of embezzlement after a prolonged and closely watched trial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The jury returned a **guilty** verdict after deliberating for three days over the complex financial fraud case.
   陪审团在对这起复杂的金融欺诈案审议三天后作出了有罪裁决。

2. She felt deeply **guilty** about neglecting her academic responsibilities while pursuing extracurricular activities throughout the semester.
   她对整个学期因参加课外活动而忽视学业责任感到深深的内疚。

3. The executive entered a **guilty** plea in exchange for a reduced sentence and full cooperation with federal investigators.
   该高管认罪以换取减刑，并全面配合联邦调查人员的工作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "guilty" as "guilt-y" — full of guilt, like a heavy weight on your conscience that you cannot easily shake off.
把"guilty"想成"guilt-y"——充满愧疚，就像良心上沉重的负担，难以轻易摆脱。
