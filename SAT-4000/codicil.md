# 📜 codicil

🔊 /ˈkɑːdɪsɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A supplement or addition to a will, modifying, explaining, or revoking parts of it; an appendix or addendum to a document
**中文：** n. 遗嘱的附录；附录；补充条款

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- addendum（附录）
- supplement（补充）
- amendment（修正案）
- appendix（附件）
- rider（附文）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **add a codicil / attach a codicil / codicil to a will / execute a codicil**

👉 Example: The attorney advised her client to add a codicil to the will rather than rewrite the entire document.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Shortly before his death, the philanthropist attached a **codicil** to his will bequeathing a substantial sum to the university library.
   这位慈善家在去世前不久在遗嘱上附加了一份附录，将一大笔款项遗赠给大学图书馆。

2. The **codicil** specifically revoked the earlier provision that had designated the eldest son as sole heir to the estate.
   该附录明确撤销了先前指定长子为遗产唯一继承人的条款。

3. Legal scholars debated whether the handwritten **codicil** met the formal requirements necessary for valid testamentary execution.
   法律学者就这份手写附录是否满足有效遗嘱执行所需的正式要求进行了辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "codicil" as "code" + "il" (a little code) — it is a small addition or extra piece attached to the main legal document.
把"codicil"想成"code"（法典）+ "il"（小的）——它是附在主要法律文件上的一小段补充。
