# 🎯 specialty

🔊 /ˈspeʃəlti/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular area of expertise or skill; a product for which a person or region is well known; a distinctive quality or characteristic. (adj.) Relating to a specialized field; distinctive or unique
**中文：** n. 专业

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expertise（专长）
- forte（强项）
- specialization（专业化）
- niche（特长领域）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **specialty of the house / medical specialty / specialty store / specialty coffee**

👉 Example: Her specialty in computational linguistics made her an invaluable asset to the research team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned surgeon's **specialty** in minimally invasive cardiac procedures attracted patients from hospitals across the entire continent.
   这位著名外科医生在微创心脏手术方面的专长吸引了来自整个大陆各医院的患者。

2. The region's culinary **specialty** is a slow-roasted lamb dish seasoned with herbs that have been cultivated locally for centuries.
   该地区的烹饪特产是一道用当地种植了数百年的香草调味的慢烤羊肉。

3. Graduate students are encouraged to identify their **specialty** early so they can focus their research efforts more effectively.
   鼓励研究生尽早确定自己的专业方向，以便更有效地集中研究精力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "specialty" as "special" + "ty" — it is that special thing you are particularly good at, your unique area of excellence.
把"specialty"想成"special"（特别的）+"ty"——它是你特别擅长的那件事，你独特的卓越领域。
