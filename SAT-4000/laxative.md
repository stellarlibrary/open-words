# 💊 laxative

🔊 /ˈlæksətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance that stimulates bowel movements and relieves constipation. (adj.) Having the effect of promoting bowel evacuation
**中文：** n. 泻药；缓泻药 adj. 通便的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- purgative（泻药）
- cathartic（通便剂）
- aperient（缓泻剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **take a laxative / mild laxative / natural laxative / laxative effect / laxative properties**

👉 Example: The physician recommended a mild laxative to alleviate the patient's chronic digestive discomfort.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor prescribed a gentle **laxative** after the patient reported persistent discomfort caused by several days of constipation.
   在患者报告因连续数天便秘引起的持续不适后，医生开了一种温和的泻药。

2. Certain high-fiber foods possess natural **laxative** properties that can help maintain healthy and regular digestive function.
   某些高纤维食物具有天然的通便特性，可以帮助维持健康和规律的消化功能。

3. Pharmacists caution against excessive use of **laxative** medications, as prolonged dependence may weaken natural bowel activity.
   药剂师警告不要过度使用泻药，因为长期依赖可能会削弱自然的肠道活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laxative" as "lax + ative" — it makes things "lax" or loose, relaxing the digestive tract to promote movement.
把"laxative"想成"lax（松弛的）+ ative"——它使肠道变得"松弛"，放松消化道以促进蠕动。
