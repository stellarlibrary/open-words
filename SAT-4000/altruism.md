# 💛 altruism

🔊 /ˈæltruːɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The selfless concern for the well-being of others; the practice of disinterested devotion to the welfare of others
**中文：** n. 利他；利他主义；无私

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- selflessness（无私）
- benevolence（仁慈）
- philanthropy（博爱）
- generosity（慷慨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pure altruism / act of altruism / altruism toward / practice altruism**

👉 Example: Her altruism was evident in the countless hours she devoted to charitable organizations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The billionaire's **altruism** was demonstrated through substantial donations to educational institutions serving underprivileged communities worldwide.
   这位亿万富翁的利他精神通过向服务全球贫困社区的教育机构进行大量捐赠得以体现。

2. Evolutionary biologists continue to debate whether genuine **altruism** exists or if all behavior ultimately serves self-interest.
   进化生物学家仍在争论真正的利他主义是否存在，还是所有行为最终都服务于自身利益。

3. The volunteers displayed remarkable **altruism** by dedicating their entire summer to rebuilding homes destroyed by the hurricane.
   志愿者们展现了非凡的无私精神，将整个夏天都用于重建被飓风摧毁的房屋。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "altru-" from Latin "alter" (other) + "-ism" (belief) — altruism is the belief in putting others first.
把"altruism"拆分为"altru-"（源自拉丁语"alter"，意为"他人"）和"-ism"（主义）——利他主义就是把他人放在首位的信念。
