# 👗 vogue

🔊 /voʊɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The prevailing fashion or style at a particular time; widespread popularity. (adj.) Fashionable; trendy
**中文：** n. 时尚；流行，时髦 adj. 时髦的，流行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trend（趋势）
- fashion（时尚）
- popularity（流行）
- craze（风靡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in vogue / come into vogue / go out of vogue / the vogue for something**

👉 Example: Minimalist architecture has been very much in vogue among contemporary designers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Sustainable fashion has come into **vogue** as consumers become increasingly aware of environmental consequences.
   随着消费者越来越意识到环境后果，可持续时尚已变得流行起来。

2. The pedagogical methods that were once in **vogue** have now been replaced by more evidence-based approaches.
   曾经流行的教学方法如今已被更多以证据为基础的方法所取代。

3. Classical architectural styles periodically return to **vogue**, reflecting society's enduring appreciation for traditional aesthetics.
   古典建筑风格周期性地重新流行，反映了社会对传统美学的持久欣赏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Vogue" the famous fashion magazine — whatever appears in Vogue is in vogue, meaning it is trendy and fashionable.
想想著名时尚杂志《Vogue》——凡是出现在《Vogue》上的都是流行的、时髦的。
