# ⛽ gasoline

🔊 /ˈɡæsəliːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A volatile, flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture used as fuel for internal-combustion engines
**中文：** n. 汽油

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- petrol（汽油）
- fuel（燃料）
- gas（汽油）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gasoline prices / gasoline engine / unleaded gasoline / gasoline consumption / gasoline tax**

👉 Example: Rising gasoline prices have compelled many commuters to consider alternative modes of transportation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rapid increase in **gasoline** prices has significantly affected household budgets and consumer spending patterns across the nation.
   汽油价格的快速上涨严重影响了全国家庭预算和消费支出模式。

2. Researchers are developing synthetic alternatives to **gasoline** that could reduce dependence on fossil fuels within the next decade.
   研究人员正在开发汽油的合成替代品，以期在未来十年内减少对化石燃料的依赖。

3. The environmental committee recommended a substantial increase in the federal **gasoline** tax to fund renewable energy infrastructure projects.
   环境委员会建议大幅提高联邦汽油税，以资助可再生能源基础设施项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gasoline" as "gas-o-line" — the gas that flows through the line (pipe) into your car's engine.
把"gasoline"想成"gas-o-line"——通过管线流入汽车发动机的气体燃料。
