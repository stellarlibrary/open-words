# 🌅 expanse

🔊 /ɪkˈspæns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A wide, continuous area of land, sea, or sky; a broad, open stretch of something
**中文：** n. 宽阔的区域；广阔的空间；苍天

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stretch（延伸区域）
- vastness（广阔）
- breadth（宽度）
- tract（大片地区）
- sweep（一大片）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vast expanse / expanse of ocean / expanse of desert / expanse of sky / endless expanse**

👉 Example: From the mountaintop, the hikers gazed across the vast expanse of forest stretching to the distant horizon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The astronauts marveled at the immense **expanse** of space visible through the observation window of the International Space Station.
   宇航员们对从国际空间站观察窗看到的广阔太空感到惊叹不已。

2. Farmers cultivated crops across the fertile **expanse** of the central plains, which provided sustenance for millions of people.
   农民们在中部平原肥沃的广阔土地上种植庄稼，为数百万人提供食物。

3. The documentary captured the breathtaking **expanse** of the Arctic tundra, revealing ecosystems rarely observed by human eyes.
   这部纪录片捕捉了北极苔原令人叹为观止的辽阔景象，揭示了人类极少观察到的生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "expanse" as "expand + se" — it is the noun form of expand, representing the wide area that results when something has expanded.
把"expanse"想成"expand + se"——它是expand的名词形式，代表事物扩展后形成的广阔区域。
