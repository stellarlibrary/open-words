# 🌲 arboretum

🔊 /ˌɑːrbəˈriːtəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A botanical garden or park devoted to the cultivation and display of a wide variety of trees and shrubs for scientific or educational purposes
**中文：** n. 植物园；树木园

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- botanical garden（植物园）
- tree garden（树木花园）
- conservatory（温室花园）
- nursery（苗圃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **visit an arboretum / national arboretum / university arboretum / arboretum collection / arboretum grounds**

👉 Example: The class took a field trip to the arboretum to study native and exotic tree species.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's **arboretum** contains over three hundred species of trees collected from temperate regions across the entire globe.
   该大学的树木园收藏了来自全球温带地区的三百多种树木。

2. Visitors to the national **arboretum** can explore winding paths through carefully curated displays of rare and endangered plant species.
   国家植物园的游客可以沿着蜿蜒的小路探索精心策划的珍稀濒危植物展览。

3. The city council voted to establish an **arboretum** in the abandoned lot to promote environmental education among local residents.
   市议会投票决定在废弃地块上建立一座树木园，以促进当地居民的环境教育。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Arboretum" = "arbor" (tree) + "-etum" (a place for) — literally a place for trees, just like an "auditorium" is a place for listening.
"Arboretum"="arbor"（树）+"-etum"（……的场所）——字面意思就是树的场所，就像"auditorium"是听讲的场所一样。
