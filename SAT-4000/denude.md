# 🍂 denude

🔊 /dɪˈnjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To strip of covering or possessions; to make bare or naked; to deprive of something essential
**中文：** v. 使裸露；剥夺；剥去覆盖物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strip（剥去）
- bare（使裸露）
- divest（剥夺）
- expose（暴露）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **denude the landscape / denude of vegetation / denude the hills / denuded terrain**

👉 Example: Decades of logging have denuded the mountainside of its once-dense forest canopy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Overgrazing by livestock has gradually **denuded** vast stretches of grassland, leaving the exposed soil vulnerable to severe wind erosion.
   牲畜的过度放牧逐渐剥去了大片草地的植被，使裸露的土壤容易受到严重的风蚀。

2. The volcanic eruption completely **denuded** the surrounding hillsides, turning what had been lush forests into barren wastelands of ash.
   火山喷发彻底剥光了周围山坡的植被，将曾经茂密的森林变成了荒芜的灰烬之地。

3. Economic sanctions effectively **denuded** the nation of its foreign reserves, severely limiting its capacity to import essential medical supplies.
   经济制裁有效地剥夺了该国的外汇储备，严重限制了其进口基本医疗用品的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-nude" as "to make nude" — denude means to strip something bare, just like making it nude.
把"denude"想成"de-nude（使…裸露）"——denude就是剥去覆盖物，使某物变得赤裸。
