# 📜 tenet

🔊 /ˈtɛnɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A principle, belief, or doctrine generally held to be true, especially one held in common by members of an organization or group.
**中文：** n. 原则；信条；教义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- principle（原则）
- doctrine（教义）
- creed（信条）
- precept（训诫）
- dogma（教条）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **central tenet / fundamental tenet / core tenet / tenet of (a belief system)**

👉 Example: Freedom of expression is a central tenet of democratic governance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor argued that the **tenet** of individual liberty remains foundational to modern constitutional law across Western democracies.
   教授认为个人自由的原则仍然是西方民主国家现代宪法的基础。

2. Many environmental organizations share the **tenet** that sustainable development must balance economic growth with ecological preservation.
   许多环保组织共同秉持可持续发展必须平衡经济增长与生态保护这一信条。

3. The researcher challenged a long-held **tenet** of evolutionary biology by presenting compelling evidence from newly discovered fossil records.
   该研究者通过展示新发现的化石记录中的有力证据，挑战了进化生物学中一个长期坚持的教义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tenet" as a palindrome — it reads the same forward and backward, just like a core principle that remains unchanged no matter how you examine it.
"tenet"是一个回文词——正读和反读都一样，就像核心原则无论怎样审视都不会改变。
