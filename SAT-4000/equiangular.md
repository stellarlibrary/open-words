# 📐 equiangular

🔊 /ˌiːkwɪˈæŋɡjʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having all angles equal in measure
**中文：** adj. 等角的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equal-angled（等角的）
- regular（正则的）
- symmetric（对称的）
- uniform（均匀的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equiangular triangle / equiangular polygon / equiangular spiral**

👉 Example: An equiangular triangle has three angles that each measure exactly sixty degrees.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In Euclidean geometry, an **equiangular** triangle is necessarily also equilateral, with all three sides of identical length.
   在欧几里得几何中，等角三角形必然也是等边三角形，三条边长度相同。

2. The architect incorporated **equiangular** polygons into the building's facade to achieve a visually harmonious and balanced design.
   建筑师将等角多边形融入建筑立面，以实现视觉上和谐均衡的设计。

3. The **equiangular** spiral appears frequently in nature, from the shells of nautiluses to the arrangement of sunflower seeds.
   等角螺线频繁出现在自然界中，从鹦鹉螺的外壳到向日葵种子的排列。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equiangular" as "equi + angular" — "equi" means equal and "angular" relates to angles. All angles are equal.
把"equiangular"想成"equi + angular"——"equi"意为相等，"angular"与角有关。所有的角都相等。
