# 🗣️ slander

🔊 /ˈslændər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make false and damaging statements about someone; to defame. (n.) A false spoken statement that harms someone's reputation
**中文：** n. 诽谤，诋毁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- defame（诽谤）
- libel（诽谤）
- malign（中伤）
- vilify（诋毁）
- calumniate（诬蔑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **slander someone's reputation / spread slander / slander campaign / guilty of slander**

👉 Example: The celebrity filed a lawsuit claiming the tabloid had deliberately slandered her professional reputation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The opposition candidate accused the ruling party of spreading **slander** to undermine his credibility before the upcoming general election.
   反对派候选人指责执政党在即将到来的大选前散布诽谤以削弱他的信誉。

2. The court ruled that the journalist had **slandered** the prominent businessman by broadcasting unverified and demonstrably false accusations on national television.
   法院裁定该记者在国家电视台播出未经证实且明显虚假的指控，诽谤了这位知名商人。

3. Medieval laws imposed severe penalties on those found guilty of **slander**, recognizing the profound damage that false statements could inflict.
   中世纪法律对被判犯有诽谤罪的人施以严厉惩罚，认识到虚假陈述可能造成的深远损害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "s-lander" as "s + land + er" — someone who lands a verbal blow on another person's reputation with false words.
把"slander"想成"s + land + er"——某人用虚假的言语对另一个人的名誉施以口头打击。
