# 🏅 contestant

🔊 /kənˈtɛstənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who takes part in a contest or competition; one who disputes or challenges something
**中文：** n. 竞争者；参赛者；争辩者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- competitor（竞争者）
- participant（参与者）
- contender（竞争者）
- rival（对手）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a contestant in / the winning contestant / fellow contestant / contestant on a show / each contestant**

👉 Example: Each contestant must submit their entry before the official deadline to be considered for the prize.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **contestant** who demonstrated the most innovative solution to the engineering problem was awarded the grand prize.
   展示了最具创新性工程问题解决方案的参赛者获得了大奖。

2. Every **contestant** in the national debate tournament had undergone months of rigorous preparation under experienced coaches.
   全国辩论锦标赛的每位参赛者都在经验丰富的教练指导下经历了数月的严格准备。

3. The panel of judges evaluated each **contestant** on criteria including technical proficiency, originality, and overall presentation quality.
   评审团根据技术熟练度、原创性和整体展示质量等标准对每位参赛者进行了评估。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "contest-ANT" — an ant entering a contest, competing tirelessly against all the other ants to win.
把"contestant"想成"参加contest（竞赛）的人"——像蚂蚁（ant）一样不知疲倦地在比赛中竞争。
