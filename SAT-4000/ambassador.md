# 🏛️ ambassador

🔊 /æmˈbæsədər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A diplomatic official of the highest rank sent by one country to represent it in another; a representative or promoter of a cause
**中文：** 大使；代表；使节

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diplomat（外交官）
- envoy（使者）
- emissary（特使）
- representative（代表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ambassador to / ambassador for / brand ambassador / goodwill ambassador**

👉 Example: The ambassador to France delivered a keynote address at the international peace summit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly appointed **ambassador** presented her credentials to the head of state during a formal ceremony.
   新任命的大使在正式仪式上向国家元首递交了国书。

2. He served as a goodwill **ambassador** for the organization, traveling extensively to raise awareness about global poverty.
   他担任该组织的亲善大使，广泛旅行以提高人们对全球贫困问题的认识。

3. The retiring **ambassador** reflected on three decades of diplomatic service devoted to strengthening bilateral relations.
   即将退休的大使回顾了三十年来致力于加强双边关系的外交生涯。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ambassador" as "amb-" (around) + "bassador" (one who goes) — someone who goes around the world representing their country.
把"ambassador"想成"amb-"（周围）+ "bassador"（行走者）——一个在世界各地代表自己国家的人。
