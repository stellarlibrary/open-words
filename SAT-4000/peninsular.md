# 🗺️ peninsular

🔊 /pəˈnɪnsjʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or resembling a peninsula; situated on a peninsula. (n.) An inhabitant of a peninsula
**中文：** adj. 半岛的；形成半岛的；半岛状的 n. 半岛居民

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coastal（沿海的）
- promontory（海角的）
- land-bound（陆地围绕的）
- jutting（突出的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peninsular region / peninsular war / peninsular climate / peninsular landform**

👉 Example: The peninsular geography of Italy has profoundly influenced its maritime history and culture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **peninsular** climate provides mild winters and warm summers, making the region ideal for year-round agricultural production.
   半岛气候提供了温和的冬季和温暖的夏季，使该地区适合全年农业生产。

2. Scholars have extensively studied the **peninsular** conflicts of the early nineteenth century and their lasting geopolitical consequences.
   学者们广泛研究了十九世纪初的半岛冲突及其持久的地缘政治影响。

3. The **peninsular** terrain shaped the development of unique trade routes that connected diverse civilizations across the Mediterranean Sea.
   半岛地形塑造了独特贸易路线的发展，连接了地中海沿岸的多种文明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "penin-sular" — "pen" (almost) + "insula" (Latin for island). A peninsula is almost an island, so peninsular describes something related to that landform.
把"peninsular"想成"pen（几乎）+ insula（拉丁语：岛）"——半岛就是几乎成为岛屿的陆地。
