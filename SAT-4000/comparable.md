# ⚖️ comparable

🔊 /ˈkɒmpərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be compared; of equivalent quality or standing; worthy of comparison
**中文：** adj. 可比较的；比得上的；类似的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equivalent（等同的）
- analogous（类似的）
- similar（相似的）
- corresponding（对应的）
- commensurate（相当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **comparable to / comparable in size / comparable quality / comparable with**

👉 Example: The new model offers performance comparable to its more expensive competitors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers found that results from the two experimental methods were **comparable** in both accuracy and reliability.
   研究人员发现两种实验方法的结果在准确性和可靠性方面都是可比较的。

2. No other ancient civilization produced architectural achievements **comparable** to those of the Roman Empire at its zenith.
   没有其他古代文明能创造出与罗马帝国鼎盛时期相比的建筑成就。

3. The salary offered by the smaller firm was entirely **comparable** to what major corporations in the industry were paying.
   这家小公司提供的薪资与该行业大公司支付的薪资完全不相上下。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comparable" as "compare + able" — able to be compared, meaning things are similar enough to measure side by side.
把"comparable"想成"compare（比较）+ able（能够）"——能够被比较的，意味着事物足够相似，可以并排衡量。
