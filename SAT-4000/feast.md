# 🍽️ feast

🔊 /fiːst/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large, elaborate meal or banquet; a religious festival or celebration. (v.) To eat and drink sumptuously; to entertain lavishly
**中文：** n. 节日；宴会；筵席 v. 享受；款待

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- banquet（宴会）
- celebration（庆典）
- gala（盛会）
- indulge（尽情享受）
- revel（狂欢）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feast on / feast for the eyes / feast day / a feast of / wedding feast**

👉 Example: The annual harvest celebration was a magnificent feast that brought the entire community together.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medieval **feast** featured dozens of elaborate dishes and entertained hundreds of guests from neighboring kingdoms and provinces.
   这场中世纪宴会呈上了数十道精致菜肴，款待了来自邻近王国和省份的数百位宾客。

2. Scholars **feast** on the rich archives of the university library, discovering manuscripts that had remained untouched for centuries.
   学者们尽情享用大学图书馆丰富的馆藏，发现了几个世纪以来无人触碰的手稿。

3. The photographic exhibition was a visual **feast** that showcased breathtaking landscapes from every inhabited continent on Earth.
   这场摄影展览是一场视觉盛宴，展示了地球上每个有人居住大洲的壮丽风景。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "feast" rhyming with "beast" — in fairy tales, even the beast enjoys a grand feast at the enchanted castle.
把"feast"与"beast（野兽）"押韵联想——在童话中，连野兽都在魔法城堡里享用盛大的宴会。
