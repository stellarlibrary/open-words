# 🎯 zero

🔊 /ˈzɪroʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, numeral | 名词, 数词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The point on a scale from which positive or negative quantities are reckoned; nothing. (num.) The cardinal number between negative one and one
**中文：** n. 零点；零度 num. 零

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nothing（无）
- nil（零）
- naught（零）
- cipher（零）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **zero tolerance / zero chance / below zero / zero in on / ground zero**

👉 Example: The temperature dropped to zero degrees overnight, forcing residents to seek adequate shelter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team reported **zero** significant side effects among participants during the entire clinical trial period.
   研究团队报告称，在整个临床试验期间，参与者中未出现任何显著的副作用。

2. Temperatures plunged well below **zero** across the northern provinces, disrupting transportation networks and power supply systems.
   北方各省气温骤降至零度以下，扰乱了交通网络和电力供应系统。

3. The detective decided to **zero** in on the financial records, believing they held the key to solving the case.
   侦探决定集中调查财务记录，认为其中隐藏着破案的关键线索。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "zero" as a circle — a shape with nothing inside, representing emptiness and the starting point.
把"zero"想成一个圆圈——一个内部空无一物的形状，代表着虚无与起点。
