# 📊 tendency

🔊 /ˈtɛndənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An inclination toward a particular characteristic or type of behavior; a general direction in which something develops or changes
**中文：** n. 倾向，趋势；癖好

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inclination（倾向）
- propensity（倾向）
- trend（趋势）
- predisposition（偏好）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tendency to / tendency toward / natural tendency / growing tendency / have a tendency**

👉 Example: There is a growing tendency among young professionals to prioritize work-life balance over salary.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study revealed a strong **tendency** among adolescents to conform to peer expectations rather than express individual opinions.
   研究揭示了青少年中存在一种强烈的倾向，即顺从同伴期望而非表达个人观点。

2. Economists noted an alarming **tendency** toward wealth concentration in the top one percent of the global population.
   经济学家注意到全球最富裕的百分之一人口中财富集中的趋势令人担忧。

3. The author's **tendency** to employ elaborate metaphors gives her prose a distinctly poetic and richly layered quality.
   这位作者善用精巧比喻的倾向赋予了她的散文一种独特的诗意和丰富的层次感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tend-ency" — what you "tend" to do repeatedly becomes your tendency, a habitual direction of behavior.
把"tendency"想成你反复"tend（倾向于）"做的事——一种习惯性的行为方向就是你的倾向。
