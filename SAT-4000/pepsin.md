# 🧪 pepsin

🔊 /ˈpepsɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An enzyme produced in the stomach that breaks down proteins during digestion
**中文：** n. 胃蛋白酶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- protease（蛋白酶）
- digestive enzyme（消化酶）
- gastric enzyme（胃酶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pepsin secretion / pepsin activity / pepsin digestion / gastric pepsin**

👉 Example: The stomach lining secretes pepsin to facilitate the breakdown of dietary proteins into smaller peptides.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biology textbook explained that **pepsin** functions optimally in the highly acidic environment of the stomach.
   生物教科书解释说，胃蛋白酶在胃的高酸性环境中功能最佳。

2. Researchers discovered that reduced **pepsin** activity may contribute to certain digestive disorders in elderly patients.
   研究人员发现，胃蛋白酶活性降低可能导致老年患者出现某些消化疾病。

3. The pharmaceutical company developed a synthetic form of **pepsin** to aid patients suffering from chronic protein malabsorption.
   这家制药公司开发了一种合成胃蛋白酶，以帮助患有慢性蛋白质吸收不良的患者。

把"pepsin"想成"Pepsi-n"——就像百事可乐在你胃里冒泡一样，胃蛋白酶在胃里工作来消化蛋白质。
