# ⚖️ law

🔊 /lɔː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A system of rules established by authority; a scientific principle describing a natural phenomenon; the legal profession or field of study
**中文：** n. 法律；规律；定律；法治；法学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regulation（法规）
- statute（法令）
- principle（原理）
- rule（规则）
- ordinance（条例）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **law of nature / break the law / enforce the law / law of gravity / by law**

👉 Example: Newton's law of universal gravitation fundamentally changed the way scientists understood the physical universe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legislature passed a new **law** requiring corporations to disclose their carbon emissions in annual sustainability reports.
   立法机关通过了一项新法律，要求企业在年度可持续发展报告中披露碳排放量。

2. The **law** of thermodynamics dictates that energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed from one form to another.
   热力学定律规定能量不能被创造或消灭，只能从一种形式转化为另一种形式。

3. Students pursuing careers in **law** must develop strong analytical reasoning and persuasive argumentation skills throughout their training.
   攻读法学专业的学生必须在整个培训过程中培养强大的分析推理和说服论证能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "law" as "L-A-W" — "Limits And Warnings" — laws set limits on behavior and warn of consequences for violations.
把"law"想成"L-A-W"——"Limits And Warnings（限制与警告）"——法律对行为设定限制并警告违反的后果。
