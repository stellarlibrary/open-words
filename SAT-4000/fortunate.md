# 🌟 fortunate

🔊 /ˈfɔːrtʃənət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Favored by or involving good luck; having unexpectedly good fortune; auspicious
**中文：** adj. 幸运的；侥幸的；吉祥的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lucky（幸运的）
- blessed（有福的）
- favored（受眷顾的）
- auspicious（吉祥的）
- propitious（有利的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fortunate enough to / fortunate that / less fortunate / fortunate outcome / fortunate circumstance**

👉 Example: We were fortunate enough to secure funding before the grant deadline passed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers were **fortunate** to obtain access to the rare archival materials that proved essential to their groundbreaking study.
   研究人员很幸运地获得了对珍贵档案材料的访问权，这些材料对他们的开创性研究至关重要。

2. Many **fortunate** individuals who survived the disaster credited their escape to the swift actions of emergency response teams.
   许多幸运地在灾难中幸存下来的人将他们的逃生归功于应急救援队的迅速行动。

3. The institution established a scholarship fund to provide educational opportunities for those from less **fortunate** socioeconomic backgrounds.
   该机构设立了奖学金基金，为来自较不富裕的社会经济背景的人提供教育机会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fortune-ate" — someone who has "eaten" fortune, meaning they have consumed good luck and are full of it.
把"fortunate"想成"fortune"（运气）+"ate"（吃了）——一个"吃了运气"的人，意味着他们充满了好运。
