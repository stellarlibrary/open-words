# 🌟 enrapture

🔊 /ɪnˈræptʃər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give intense pleasure or joy to; to fill with delight; to captivate completely
**中文：** v. 使狂喜；使著迷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- captivate（迷住）
- enchant（使陶醉）
- enthrall（使入迷）
- mesmerize（使著迷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enraptured by / enraptured with / enrapture the audience / completely enraptured**

👉 Example: The pianist's extraordinary performance enraptured the audience, leaving them in stunned silence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The breathtaking landscape of snow-capped mountains and crystal-clear lakes **enraptured** every visitor who arrived at the reserve.
   白雪皑皑的山峰和清澈见底的湖泊构成的壮丽景色令每位到达保护区的游客都为之陶醉。

2. Her eloquent and passionate recitation of classical poetry **enraptured** the literary society members throughout the evening event.
   她对古典诗歌雄辩而充满激情的朗诵令文学社成员在整个晚间活动中都如痴如醉。

3. The discovery of a previously unknown symphony by the celebrated composer **enraptured** musicologists around the entire world.
   发现了这位著名作曲家一部此前不为人知的交响曲，令全世界的音乐学家都为之狂喜。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-rapture" as being put "into rapture" — rapture means ecstatic joy, so enrapture means to cause that overwhelming delight.
把"enrapture"想成被送入"rapture（狂喜）"——rapture意为极度的喜悦，所以enrapture就是使人感到难以抑制的欣喜。
