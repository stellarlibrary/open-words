# ⬆️ ascendant

🔊 /əˈsɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A position of dominant power or influence; superiority. (adj.) Rising in power or influence; dominant; moving upward.

**中文：** (n.) 优势 (adj.) 上升的；优越的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- dominant 占主导地位的
- prevalent 盛行的
- supreme 至高的
- rising 上升的

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

The phrase "in the ascendant" means gaining power or becoming more influential. As a noun, it often appears in this fixed expression. As an adjective, it describes something growing in dominance.

短语 "in the ascendant" 意为正在获得权力或变得更有影响力。作为名词时常出现在这个固定搭配中。作为形容词时，描述正在增长的主导地位。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technology sector has been **ascendant** in the global economy for more than two decades now.
   科技行业在全球经济中已处于上升态势超过二十年。

2. With her party now in the **ascendant**, the senator seized the opportunity to advance bold reforms.
   随着她的政党现在占据优势，这位参议员抓住机会推动大胆的改革。

3. The **ascendant** influence of social media has fundamentally reshaped how people communicate and consume information.
   社交媒体日益上升的影响力从根本上重塑了人们沟通和获取信息的方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Ascend" (上升) + "-ant" (suffix) — something that is ascending, rising to a position of power or dominance.

"Ascend"（上升）+ "-ant"（后缀）——正在上升的事物，正升至权力或主导地位。
