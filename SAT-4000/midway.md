# 🛤️ midway

🔊 /ˈmɪdweɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, adverb | 名词, 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The middle point or area; an amusement area at a fair or carnival. (adj./adv.) In or toward the middle of something.
**中文：** n. 中途；娱乐场 adj. 中途的 adv. 中途

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- halfway（中途的）
- middle（中间的）
- intermediate（中间的）
- center（中心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **midway through / midway between / midway point / carnival midway**

👉 Example: The project stalled midway through the second phase due to unexpected funding shortages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee decided to reassess the proposal **midway** through the fiscal year after reviewing the quarterly results.
   委员会在审查了季度业绩后，决定在财年中途重新评估该提案。

2. A vibrant **midway** filled with games and rides attracted thousands of visitors to the annual county fair.
   一个充满游戏和游乐设施的娱乐场吸引了数千名游客参加年度县集市。

3. The research team discovered a significant anomaly **midway** between the two experimental control points in the dataset.
   研究团队在数据集的两个实验控制点中途发现了一个重大异常。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mid-way" as literally "the middle of the way" — you are halfway along your journey, neither at the start nor the end.
把"midway"想成"mid（中间）+ way（路）"——你正走在路的中间，既不在起点也不在终点。
