# 👁️ visibility

🔊 /ˌvɪzəˈbɪləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state or degree of being visible; the distance one can see under given weather conditions; the quality of being noticed or prominent
**中文：** n. 能见度，可见性；能见距离；明显性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clarity（清晰度）
- prominence（显著性）
- exposure（曝光度）
- perceptibility（可感知性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **low visibility / high visibility / visibility conditions / increase visibility**

👉 Example: The fog reduced visibility to fewer than fifty meters on the highway.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Poor **visibility** caused by dense fog forced aviation authorities to delay numerous flights at the international airport.
   浓雾导致的低能见度迫使航空当局推迟了国际机场的多个航班。

2. The marketing campaign significantly increased the company's **visibility** among younger demographics and online consumers.
   这场营销活动显著提高了公司在年轻群体和在线消费者中的知名度。

3. The committee emphasized the need for greater **visibility** of safety protocols in all manufacturing facilities across the region.
   委员会强调需要在该地区所有生产设施中提高安全规程的可见度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "visibility" as "visible" + "-ity" (quality of) — the quality of how well something can be seen or noticed.
把"visibility"想成"visible"（可见的）+ "-ity"（性质）——某物被看到或注意到的程度。
