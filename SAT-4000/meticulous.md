# 🔍 meticulous

🔊 /məˈtɪkjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise in one's work or actions.
**中文：** adj. 一丝不苟的；小心翼翼的；拘泥小节的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- painstaking（煞费苦心的）
- scrupulous（一丝不苟的）
- thorough（彻底的）
- fastidious（挑剔的）
- precise（精确的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **meticulous attention / meticulous planning / meticulous detail / meticulous record**

👉 Example: The architect was known for her meticulous attention to every structural detail in her designs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian's **meticulous** research uncovered previously unknown documents that fundamentally changed our understanding of the era.
   这位历史学家一丝不苟的研究发现了之前未知的文献，从根本上改变了我们对那个时代的认识。

2. Her **meticulous** approach to laboratory procedures ensured that every experiment produced reliable and reproducible results.
   她对实验室程序一丝不苟的态度确保了每个实验都产生可靠且可重复的结果。

3. The auditors performed a **meticulous** examination of the financial records to identify any discrepancies or irregularities.
   审计人员对财务记录进行了细致的审查，以发现任何差异或违规之处。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "me-TICK-ulous" — imagine someone so careful that they check every tiny "tick" mark on a checklist with extreme precision.
把"meticulous"想成"me-TICK-ulous"——想象一个人如此仔细，以至于他们以极高的精度检查清单上的每一个小"勾号"。
