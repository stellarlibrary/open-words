# 💍 polygamy

🔊 /pəˈlɪɡəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The practice or condition of having more than one spouse at the same time, including polygyny and polyandry
**中文：** n. 一夫多妻制；一妻多夫制；多配偶制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plural marriage（多配偶婚姻）
- polygyny（一夫多妻）
- polyandry（一妻多夫）
- bigamy（重婚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **practice polygamy / laws against polygamy / polygamy is prohibited / abolish polygamy**

👉 Example: Polygamy remains illegal in most Western nations, though it is permitted in certain cultures and legal systems.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologist studied communities where **polygamy** was traditionally practiced to understand its social and economic implications.
   这位人类学家研究了传统上实行多配偶制的社区，以了解其社会和经济影响。

2. Legislation explicitly prohibiting **polygamy** was enacted in the nineteenth century amid considerable public debate and moral controversy.
   在相当大的公众辩论和道德争议中，明确禁止多配偶制的法律于十九世纪颁布。

3. The documentary explored how **polygamy** affects family dynamics, inheritance rights, and gender equality in various contemporary societies.
   这部纪录片探讨了多配偶制如何影响当代各社会中的家庭关系、继承权和性别平等。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "poly" (many) + "gamy" (marriage) — polygamy literally means "many marriages," having multiple spouses at once.
把"polygamy"拆成"poly（多）"+"gamy（婚姻）"——字面意思就是"多重婚姻"，同时拥有多个配偶。
