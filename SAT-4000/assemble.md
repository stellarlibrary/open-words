# 🧩 assemble

🔊 /əˈsembl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To come together or bring together in one place; to fit together the parts or pieces of something.
**中文：** v. 集合，聚集；装配

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gather（聚集）
- convene（召集）
- congregate（集合）
- construct（组装）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assemble** for both gathering people and putting parts together. "Assemble a team" means to bring people together; "assemble a machine" means to put its parts together.

👉 Example: The engineers assembled the prototype in the workshop over the weekend.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The principal asked all students to **assemble** in the auditorium for an urgent announcement regarding campus safety.
   校长要求所有学生到礼堂集合，听取有关校园安全的紧急公告。

2. Factory workers **assemble** hundreds of electronic components each day following strict quality control guidelines and procedures.
   工厂工人每天按照严格的质量控制准则和程序装配数百个电子元件。

3. The committee will **assemble** next Monday to review the proposed amendments to the organization's founding charter.
   委员会将于下周一召集会议，审议对该组织创始章程的拟议修正案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assemble" as "a + sembl(e)" — "sembl" sounds like "similar," and when you assemble things, you bring similar parts together to form a whole.
把"assemble"想成"a + sembl(e)"——"sembl"听起来像"similar（相似的）"，装配就是把相关的部件组合在一起形成一个整体。
