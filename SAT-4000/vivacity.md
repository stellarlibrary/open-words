# 🌟 vivacity

🔊 /vɪˈvæsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being attractively lively, animated, or spirited; liveliness and enthusiasm
**中文：** n. 活泼；快活；精神充沛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- liveliness（活泼）
- animation（生气）
- exuberance（充沛）
- spiritedness（精神饱满）
- vitality（活力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vivacity of spirit / natural vivacity / infectious vivacity / vivacity and charm**

👉 Example: The performer's vivacity on stage was truly captivating and held the audience spellbound.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The lecturer's remarkable **vivacity** transformed an otherwise dull subject into an engaging and memorable academic experience.
   这位讲师非凡的活力将一个原本枯燥的科目变成了一段引人入胜且难忘的学术体验。

2. Her natural **vivacity** and intellectual curiosity made her an ideal candidate for the prestigious international fellowship program.
   她天生的活泼和求知欲使她成为这项享有盛誉的国际奖学金项目的理想候选人。

3. Age had done nothing to diminish the scholar's **vivacity**, and she continued to inspire students well into her eighties.
   岁月丝毫没有减弱这位学者的活力，她在八十多岁时仍继续激励着学生们。

---

Think of "vivacity" as "viva + city" — imagine a vibrant city full of life, energy, and excitement, just like a person with vivacity.
把"vivacity"想成"viva（生命）+ city（城市）"——想象一座充满生机、活力和兴奋的城市，就像一个活泼的人。
