# 📊 account for

🔊 /əˈkaʊnt fɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *phrasal verb | 短语动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To explain the cause of something; to be responsible for; to constitute a proportion of.
**中文：** 对…做出解释；说明…的原因；导致；对…负有责任；（比例）占

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- explain（解释）
- justify（证明…有理）
- constitute（构成）
- comprise（占…比例）
- elucidate（阐明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **account for + a percentage / a phenomenon / one's actions**

👉 Example: Exports account for nearly forty percent of the nation's revenue.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Renewable energy sources now **account for** approximately thirty percent of global electricity generation.
   可再生能源目前约占全球发电量的百分之三十。

2. The professor asked the student to **account for** the discrepancy between the predicted and actual results.
   教授要求学生解释预测结果与实际结果之间的差异。

3. Several socioeconomic factors **account for** the persistent achievement gap observed across urban school districts.
   多种社会经济因素导致了城市学区中持续存在的学业成绩差距。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "giving an account" — like a financial account that must balance, you must account for every item or reason.
把 account for 想象成"记账"——每一笔都要"说明原因"，就像会计必须解释每一项支出的去向。
