# 🤨 implausible

🔊 /ɪmˈplɔːzəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not seeming reasonable or probable; difficult to believe; unlikely to be true
**中文：** adj. 难以置信的；不像真实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unbelievable（难以置信的）
- improbable（不太可能的）
- far-fetched（牵强的）
- unconvincing（无说服力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **implausible explanation / implausible claim / implausible scenario / seem implausible**

👉 Example: The defendant's alibi seemed implausible to the jury given the overwhelming physical evidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher's findings were dismissed as **implausible** because they contradicted decades of well-established scientific evidence.
   该研究者的发现因与数十年来确立的科学证据相矛盾而被认为不可信。

2. Critics argued that the novel's plot was **implausible**, relying on too many unlikely coincidences to advance the narrative.
   评论家认为这部小说的情节不真实，依赖过多不太可能的巧合来推动叙事。

3. While the theory initially appeared **implausible**, subsequent experiments provided compelling data that supported its core predictions.
   虽然该理论最初看起来难以置信，但后续实验提供了支持其核心预测的有力数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-PLAUSIBLE" — the prefix "im-" means "not," so implausible literally means "not plausible," not worthy of applause or approval.
把"implausible"拆分为"im（不）+ plausible（似乎合理的）"——字面意思就是"不合理的"，不值得鼓掌认可的。
