# 🎯 specific

🔊 /spəˈsɪfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Particular; definite; clearly defined or identified. Having a special application or effect. (n.) A characteristic quality; a detail; a medicine effective for a particular disease.
**中文：** adj. 明确的，具体的；特定的；特有的，特殊的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- particular（特定的）
- precise（精确的）
- definite（明确的）
- explicit（清楚的）
- distinct（独特的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **specific details / specific requirements / specific purpose / be specific about**

👉 Example: The professor asked the students to be more specific about the evidence supporting their thesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research committee requested **specific** data to substantiate the claims made in the preliminary report.
   研究委员会要求提供具体数据来证实初步报告中提出的主张。

2. Each scholarship has **specific** eligibility criteria that applicants must satisfy before submitting their materials.
   每项奖学金都有申请人在提交材料前必须满足的特定资格标准。

3. The architect provided **specific** instructions regarding the dimensions and materials for the new auditorium.
   建筑师就新礼堂的尺寸和材料提供了明确的指示。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spec-ific" as related to "specify" — when something is specific, it has been specified and made clear, not vague.
把"specific"联想到"specify"（指定）——当某事物是specific时，它已经被具体指定、说清楚了，而非含糊不清。
