# 🥩 fat

🔊 /fæt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A natural oily substance found in animal bodies or certain foods. (adj.) Having a large amount of excess flesh; plump or overweight
**中文：** n. 脂肪；肥肉 adj. 肥的；胖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grease（油脂）
- lipid（脂质）
- obese（肥胖的）
- plump（丰满的）
- corpulent（肥胖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **body fat / fat content / saturated fat / fat chance / chew the fat**

👉 Example: Nutritionists recommend limiting saturated fat intake to maintain cardiovascular health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Excessive consumption of trans **fat** has been linked to a significantly increased risk of cardiovascular disease in adults.
   过量摄入反式脂肪与成年人心血管疾病风险的显著增加有关。

2. The research team analyzed the **fat** composition of various dietary oils to determine their nutritional value and health effects.
   研究团队分析了各种食用油的脂肪成分，以确定其营养价值和健康影响。

3. Public health campaigns have encouraged consumers to read labels carefully and choose foods lower in **fat** and sodium.
   公共卫生运动鼓励消费者仔细阅读标签，选择脂肪和钠含量较低的食品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fat" as something "flat and thick" — picture a flat layer of thick substance stored beneath the skin, which is body fat.
把"fat"想成"flat and thick（扁而厚）"——想象皮肤下一层扁而厚的物质，那就是体内脂肪。
