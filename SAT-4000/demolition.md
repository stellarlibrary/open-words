# 🏗️ demolition

🔊 /ˌdɛməˈlɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act or process of tearing down or destroying a structure; complete destruction or dismantling
**中文：** n. 拆除；破坏；毁坏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destruction（破坏）
- dismantling（拆除）
- razing（夷平）
- wrecking（拆毁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **demolition crew / demolition site / scheduled for demolition / demolition of a building / controlled demolition**

👉 Example: The controlled demolition of the obsolete power plant was carried out safely by a team of expert engineers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **demolition** of the historic theater sparked heated debate among preservationists and urban development advocates in the community.
   这座历史剧院的拆除在社区中的文物保护主义者和城市发展倡导者之间引发了激烈争论。

2. Environmental regulations require thorough hazardous material inspections before any large-scale **demolition** project can officially commence.
   环境法规要求在任何大规模拆除项目正式开始之前，必须进行彻底的危险材料检查。

3. The **demolition** of outdated trade barriers facilitated unprecedented economic growth and international cooperation across the region.
   过时贸易壁垒的拆除促进了该地区前所未有的经济增长和国际合作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "demolition" as the noun form of "demolish" — just add "-tion" to turn the action of destroying into the event itself.
把"demolition"想成"demolish"的名词形式——只需加上"-tion"就把摧毁的动作变成了事件本身。
