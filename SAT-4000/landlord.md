# 🏠 landlord

🔊 /ˈlændlɔːrd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who owns property and rents it to tenants; the owner of an inn or lodging house
**中文：** n. 房东，老板；地主

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- property owner（业主）
- lessor（出租人）
- proprietor（所有者）
- landowner（地主）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **landlord and tenant / absentee landlord / landlord responsibilities / contact the landlord**

👉 Example: The landlord agreed to reduce the monthly rent in exchange for the tenant maintaining the garden.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **landlord** was legally obligated to ensure that all rental units met the minimum safety and habitability standards.
   房东在法律上有义务确保所有出租单元符合最低安全和宜居标准。

2. Many tenants filed complaints against the negligent **landlord** who repeatedly ignored requests for essential building repairs.
   许多租户对这位疏忽的房东提出了投诉，因为他反复忽视对必要建筑维修的请求。

3. The absentee **landlord** hired a professional management company to oversee daily operations of the apartment complex.
   这位不在场的房东雇用了一家专业管理公司来监管公寓大楼的日常运营。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "land" + "lord" — the lord of the land, the person who rules over the property they own and rent out.
把"landlord"拆成"land（土地）"和"lord（领主）"——土地的领主，拥有并出租房产的人。
