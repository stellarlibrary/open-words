# 💰 liquidate

🔊 /ˈlɪkwɪdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To settle or pay off a debt; to wind up the affairs of a business by converting assets into cash; to eliminate or abolish
**中文：** v. 清算；偿付；消除；变卖资产

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- settle（清偿）
- dissolve（解散）
- eliminate（消除）
- pay off（偿清）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **liquidate assets / liquidate a company / liquidate debts / liquidate holdings**

👉 Example: The board voted to liquidate the failing subsidiary and distribute the proceeds to creditors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court ordered the bankrupt corporation to **liquidate** all remaining assets to satisfy its outstanding financial obligations.
   法院命令这家破产公司清算所有剩余资产，以清偿其未偿还的财务义务。

2. Investors rushed to **liquidate** their stock portfolios when the market showed unmistakable signs of an approaching downturn.
   当市场显示出明确的衰退迹象时，投资者纷纷急于清算其股票投资组合。

3. The executor was required to **liquidate** the estate's properties in accordance with the terms specified in the will.
   遗嘱执行人须按照遗嘱中规定的条款清算遗产中的房产。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liquidate" as "making things liquid" — turning solid assets into liquid cash, like melting gold into flowing currency.
把"liquidate"想成"使事物变为液态"——将固定资产变成流动的现金，就像把黄金融化成流动的货币。
