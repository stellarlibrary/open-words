# 🔬 metabolism

🔊 /məˈtæbəlɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The chemical processes that occur within a living organism in order to maintain life, including the breakdown of food for energy and the building of complex molecules.
**中文：** n. 新陈代谢；物质代谢

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- catabolism（分解代谢）
- anabolism（合成代谢）
- biochemical process（生化过程）
- metabolic rate（代谢率）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **boost metabolism / slow metabolism / metabolic rate / basal metabolism**

👉 Example: Regular exercise can help boost your metabolism and improve overall energy levels.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists discovered that prolonged fasting significantly alters the body's **metabolism** and affects hormonal regulation.
   科学家发现，长时间禁食会显著改变身体的新陈代谢并影响激素调节。

2. The physician explained that her slow **metabolism** was a contributing factor to persistent weight gain despite dieting.
   医生解释说，她缓慢的新陈代谢是尽管节食仍持续增重的一个促成因素。

3. Researchers have identified specific genes that regulate cellular **metabolism** and influence how organisms process nutrients efficiently.
   研究人员已经确定了调节细胞新陈代谢并影响生物体高效处理营养物质的特定基因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meta-bol-ism" as "meta" (change) + "bol" (throw) — your body is constantly "throwing changes" at the molecular level to keep you alive.
把"metabolism"想成"meta"（变化）+"bol"（投掷）——你的身体在分子层面不断"投掷变化"以维持生命。
