# 🔢 equation

🔊 /ɪˈkweɪʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A mathematical statement asserting the equality of two expressions; the process of equating or making equal
**中文：** n. 方程式；等式；相等；均衡

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- formula（公式）
- equivalence（等价）
- expression（表达式）
- calculation（计算）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solve an equation / balance the equation / part of the equation / a simple equation**

👉 Example: The physicist derived a new equation to describe the behavior of particles at quantum scales.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students must learn to solve quadratic **equations** before advancing to more complex areas of calculus and linear algebra.
   学生必须学会解二次方程才能进入更复杂的微积分和线性代数领域。

2. Environmental sustainability is a critical variable in the **equation** of long-term economic growth and national prosperity.
   环境可持续性是长期经济增长和国家繁荣等式中的一个关键变量。

3. Einstein's famous **equation** relating energy and mass fundamentally transformed our understanding of the physical universe.
   爱因斯坦关于能量与质量关系的著名方程式从根本上改变了我们对物理宇宙的理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equation" as "equa-tion" — from "equal" plus the noun suffix "-tion." An equation states that two things are equal.
把"equation"想成"equa-tion"——来自"equal"加上名词后缀"-tion"。方程式表示两个事物相等。
