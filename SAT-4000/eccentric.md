# 🎭 eccentric

🔊 /ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unconventional and slightly strange in behavior or appearance; deviating from the norm. (n.) A person of unconventional and peculiar habits
**中文：** adj. 古怪的；异乎寻常的 n. 古怪的人；怪人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- peculiar（奇特的）
- quirky（古怪的）
- unconventional（非传统的）
- idiosyncratic（特殊的）
- oddball（怪人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eccentric behavior / eccentric personality / eccentric billionaire / eccentric genius**

👉 Example: The eccentric inventor filled his workshop with bizarre contraptions that no one else could understand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **eccentric** philosopher was renowned for conducting lectures while pacing barefoot through the university gardens at dawn.
   这位古怪的哲学家以黎明时分赤脚在大学花园中边踱步边讲课而闻名。

2. Critics initially dismissed her **eccentric** artistic approach, but her unconventional methods eventually revolutionized contemporary sculpture.
   批评家最初否定了她古怪的艺术手法，但她非传统的方法最终革新了当代雕塑。

3. The town regarded the old professor as a harmless **eccentric** who collected antique clocks and conversed with his cats.
   小镇居民认为这位老教授是一个无害的怪人，喜欢收集古董钟表并和他的猫聊天。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ec-centric" as "ex-center" — someone who is off-center, away from the norm, behaving outside conventional boundaries.
把"eccentric"想成"ex-center（偏离中心）"——一个偏离中心的人，远离常规，行为超出传统界限。
