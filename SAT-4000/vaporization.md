# 💨 vaporization

🔊 /ˌveɪpəraɪˈzeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of converting a substance from a liquid or solid state into vapor or gas
**中文：** n. 蒸发；汽化；气化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- evaporation（蒸发）
- gasification（气化）
- sublimation（升华）
- volatilization（挥发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vaporization of water / heat of vaporization / vaporization point / vaporization process / rapid vaporization**

👉 Example: The heat of vaporization is a key concept in thermodynamics and phase transition studies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **vaporization** of liquid nitrogen occurs rapidly at room temperature, producing a dense white cloud of condensed moisture.
   液氮在室温下迅速蒸发，产生浓密的白色凝结水雾。

2. Engineers must account for the latent heat of **vaporization** when designing cooling systems for industrial chemical processing plants.
   工程师在设计工业化学加工厂的冷却系统时必须考虑汽化潜热。

3. The research team studied the **vaporization** rates of various metallic compounds when exposed to extremely high temperatures in vacuum conditions.
   研究团队研究了各种金属化合物在真空条件下暴露于极高温度时的蒸发速率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vaporization" as "vapor + ization" — the process of turning into vapor, like water disappearing into steam on a hot surface.
把"vaporization"想成"vapor（蒸气）+ ization（…化过程）"——变成蒸气的过程，就像水在热表面上变成蒸汽消失一样。
