# 🎯 veracity

🔊 /vəˈræsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Conformity to facts; accuracy; habitual truthfulness and honesty
**中文：** n. 诚实；精确性；真实

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- truthfulness（真实性）
- accuracy（准确性）
- honesty（诚实）
- integrity（正直）
- authenticity（真实性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **question the veracity / veracity of a claim / veracity of a statement / doubt the veracity**

👉 Example: Investigators questioned the veracity of the witness's account after finding several inconsistencies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist's reputation depended on the **veracity** of her sources and the accuracy of every published claim.
   这位记者的声誉取决于其消息来源的真实性和每一项已发表声明的准确性。

2. Scholars have long debated the **veracity** of the ancient manuscript, questioning whether it is genuine or forged.
   学者们长期以来一直在争论这份古代手稿的真实性，质疑它究竟是真品还是伪造。

3. The defendant's attorney attempted to undermine the **veracity** of the prosecution's key evidence during cross-examination.
   被告律师在交叉询问中试图质疑控方关键证据的真实性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veracity" as "vera-city" — a city built on "vera" (truth in Latin), where everything spoken is honest and accurate.
把"veracity"想成"vera-city"——一座建立在"vera"（拉丁语中的真实）上的城市，那里说的一切都是诚实准确的。
