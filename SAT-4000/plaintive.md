# 😢 plaintive

🔊 /ˈpleɪntɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Sounding sad and mournful; expressing sorrow or melancholy in a way that evokes sympathy
**中文：** adj. 哀伤的；悲哀的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mournful（悲哀的）
- sorrowful（忧愁的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- wistful（惆怅的）
- doleful（悲伤的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plaintive cry / plaintive melody / plaintive voice / plaintive tone**

👉 Example: The plaintive melody of the violin echoed through the empty concert hall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugee's **plaintive** account of displacement and loss moved the entire assembly to support the humanitarian aid resolution.
   难民对流离失所和失去一切的哀伤叙述打动了整个大会，使其支持人道主义援助决议。

2. A **plaintive** howl from the abandoned dog could be heard across the neighborhood, prompting several residents to contact animal services.
   被遗弃的狗发出的哀伤嚎叫声传遍了整个社区，促使几位居民联系动物服务部门。

3. The poet's **plaintive** verses captured the collective grief of a nation mourning the devastating consequences of prolonged civil conflict.
   诗人哀伤的诗句捕捉了一个国家为长期内战的毁灭性后果而哀悼的集体悲痛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plaintive" as "plain-tive" — the pain is so plain (obvious) that everyone can hear the sadness in the voice.
把"plaintive"想成"plain-tive"——痛苦如此明显（plain），每个人都能听出声音中的悲伤。
