# 🏔️ butte

🔊 /bjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An isolated hill or mountain with steep sides and a flat top, smaller than a mesa
**中文：** n. 孤峰；孤立的小山丘

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mesa（平顶山）
- knoll（小丘）
- hillock（小山丘）
- pinnacle（尖峰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a solitary butte / desert butte / sandstone butte / atop the butte**

👉 Example: The solitary butte rose dramatically from the flat, arid landscape of the southwestern desert.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist studied the ancient sandstone **butte** to understand the erosional forces that had shaped the surrounding terrain.
   地质学家研究了这座古老的砂岩孤峰，以了解塑造周围地形的侵蚀力量。

2. A single **butte** dominated the otherwise featureless plain, serving as a landmark for travelers crossing the region.
   一座孤峰矗立在原本平淡无奇的平原上，为穿越该地区的旅行者提供地标。

3. From the summit of the **butte**, researchers could observe the vast expanse of the valley stretching toward the horizon.
   从孤峰的山顶上，研究人员可以观察到延伸至地平线的广阔山谷全貌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "butte" sounding like "beauty" — a butte is a beautiful, solitary flat-topped hill standing alone in the desert landscape.
想想"butte"听起来像"beauty"（美丽）——butte就是沙漠景观中独自矗立的美丽平顶孤山。
