# 🧬 recombination

🔊 /ˌriːkɒmbɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of combining or rearranging elements again in a new way, especially the rearrangement of genetic material through crossing over during cell division
**中文：** n. 复合；再结合；重组

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rearrangement（重新排列）
- reshuffling（重新洗牌）
- reorganization（重组）
- reconfiguration（重新配置）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **genetic recombination / DNA recombination / recombination of elements / homologous recombination**

👉 Example: Genetic recombination is a fundamental mechanism that drives diversity within biological populations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The process of genetic **recombination** during meiosis produces offspring with unique combinations of traits inherited from both parents.
   减数分裂过程中的基因重组产生了具有从父母双方遗传的独特性状组合的后代。

2. Scientists utilized homologous **recombination** techniques to insert therapeutic genes into the defective chromosomes of affected patients.
   科学家利用同源重组技术将治疗基因插入受影响患者的缺陷染色体中。

3. The **recombination** of cultural influences in the cosmopolitan city created a vibrant and entirely unprecedented artistic movement.
   这座国际化城市中文化影响的重新组合创造了一场充满活力且前所未有的艺术运动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-combination" as "combining again" — elements that were separate are brought together in a new arrangement, like shuffling a deck of cards.
把"recombination"想成"re-combination（重新组合）"——原本分开的元素以新的排列方式重新结合，就像重新洗一副牌。
