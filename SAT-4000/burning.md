# 🔥 burning

🔊 /ˈbɜːrnɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) On fire; very hot; intense or passionate; urgent and compelling. (n.) The process of combustion or being consumed by fire
**中文：** adj. 燃烧的；强烈的；发热的 n. 燃烧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blazing（燃烧的）
- scorching（灼热的）
- intense（强烈的）
- fervent（热烈的）
- urgent（紧迫的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **burning desire / burning question / burning issue / burning sensation**

👉 Example: Climate change remains the most burning issue of our time, demanding immediate collective action.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee addressed the **burning** question of how to allocate limited resources among competing public health priorities.
   委员会讨论了如何在相互竞争的公共卫生优先事项之间分配有限资源这一紧迫问题。

2. A **burning** desire to achieve social justice motivated her to pursue a career in constitutional law and civil rights.
   对实现社会正义的强烈渴望促使她投身于宪法和民权领域的职业生涯。

3. The uncontrolled **burning** of fossil fuels has released unprecedented quantities of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere over centuries.
   几个世纪以来，化石燃料的不可控燃烧已向大气中释放了前所未有的温室气体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of something literally burning — the heat is intense, urgent, and impossible to ignore, just like a burning issue or desire.
想象某物在燃烧——热量是强烈的、紧迫的、无法忽视的，就像一个burning issue（紧迫问题）或burning desire（强烈渴望）。
