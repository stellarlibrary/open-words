# 😰 tremulous

🔊 /ˈtremjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Shaking or quivering slightly; timid or nervous; characterized by trembling
**中文：** adj. 胆小的；战栗的；震颤的；颤抖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quivering（颤抖的）
- shaky（摇晃的）
- timorous（胆怯的）
- faltering（踌躇的）
- wavering（动摇的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tremulous voice / tremulous smile / tremulous hands / tremulous with fear**

👉 Example: She offered a tremulous smile as she stepped onto the stage for the first time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness gave her testimony in a **tremulous** voice, visibly shaken by the gravity of the courtroom proceedings.
   证人以颤抖的声音提供了证词，明显被法庭诉讼的严肃性所震慑。

2. With **tremulous** fingers, the archaeologist carefully lifted the ancient artifact from its resting place in the excavation site.
   考古学家用颤抖的手指小心翼翼地从发掘现场将这件古代文物取出。

3. The young pianist's **tremulous** performance gradually steadied as her confidence grew throughout the demanding recital program.
   这位年轻钢琴家颤抖的演奏随着她在高难度音乐会中信心的增长而逐渐稳定下来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tremulous" as "tremble + ulous" — someone who is tremulous trembles constantly, full of nervousness.
把"tremulous"想成"tremble + ulous"——一个tremulous的人不停地颤抖，充满了紧张不安。
