# ✏️ modify

🔊 /ˈmɒdɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make partial changes to something; to alter or adjust without transforming entirely; in grammar, to qualify or describe another word
**中文：** v. 修改，修饰；更改，改造

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alter（改变）
- adjust（调整）
- revise（修订）
- amend（修正）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **modify a plan / modify behavior / modify the design / genetically modified**

👉 Example: The engineers decided to modify the original blueprint to accommodate the new safety regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee voted to **modify** the existing curriculum to incorporate more contemporary literature and critical theory.
   委员会投票决定修改现有课程，纳入更多当代文学和批评理论。

2. Researchers have successfully **modified** the gene responsible for the disease, opening new avenues for therapeutic intervention.
   研究人员已成功修改了导致该疾病的基因，为治疗干预开辟了新途径。

3. The architect was asked to **modify** the building's facade to harmonize with the historic character of the surrounding district.
   建筑师被要求修改建筑外立面，使其与周边历史街区的风貌协调一致。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mod-ify" as "mode + ify" — to put something into a different mode, meaning to change or adjust it.
把"modify"想成"mode + ify"——把某物变成不同的模式，即改变或调整它。
