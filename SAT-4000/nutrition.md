# 🍎 nutrition

🔊 /njuːˈtrɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth; the branch of science that deals with nutrients and nourishment
**中文：** n. 营养；营养学；营养品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nourishment（营养）
- sustenance（食物）
- diet（饮食）
- alimentation（营养供给）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **good nutrition / poor nutrition / nutrition education / nutrition label / nutrition expert**

👉 Example: The school launched a comprehensive nutrition program to promote healthier eating habits among students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advances in **nutrition** science have fundamentally changed our understanding of how dietary choices affect long-term health outcomes.
   营养科学的进步从根本上改变了我们对饮食选择如何影响长期健康结果的认识。

2. The government allocated additional funding to improve child **nutrition** programs in underserved rural communities across the country.
   政府拨出额外资金，以改善全国欠发达农村社区的儿童营养计划。

3. Experts in **nutrition** recommend a balanced diet rich in whole grains, lean proteins, and fresh vegetables for optimal health.
   营养专家建议摄入富含全谷物、瘦肉蛋白和新鲜蔬菜的均衡饮食，以达到最佳健康状态。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nutrition" as "nutri-tion" — the "tion" (process) of "nutri-" (nourishing), the entire science and practice of feeding your body well.
把"nutrition"想成"nutri-tion"——"nutri（滋养）"的"tion（过程）"，即喂养身体的整套科学和实践。
