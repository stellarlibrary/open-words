# 🦁 aggressive

🔊 /əˈɡrɛsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Ready or likely to attack or confront; pursuing one's aims forcefully; hostile or combative in manner
**中文：** 侵略性的；好斗的；有进取心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- belligerent（好战的）
- combative（好斗的）
- assertive（强势的）
- hostile（敌对的）
- pugnacious（好斗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aggressive behavior / aggressive strategy / aggressive campaign / aggressive stance / aggressive approach**

👉 Example: The company adopted an aggressive marketing strategy to capture a larger share of the emerging market.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board approved an **aggressive** expansion plan that would establish new regional offices in twelve countries within two years.
   董事会批准了一项激进的扩张计划，将在两年内在十二个国家设立新的区域办事处。

2. Wildlife researchers observed that the normally docile species became markedly **aggressive** when their nesting territory was threatened by intruders.
   野生动物研究人员观察到，这种通常温顺的物种在其筑巢领地受到入侵者威胁时变得明显具有攻击性。

3. The prosecutor pursued an **aggressive** line of questioning that ultimately compelled the reluctant witness to reveal crucial hidden evidence.
   检察官采用了咄咄逼人的质询方式，最终迫使不情愿的证人揭露了关键的隐藏证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ag-GRESS-ive" — from "aggress" meaning to attack, plus "-ive" making it an adjective describing someone who tends to attack.
想想"ag-GRESS-ive"——来自"aggress"（攻击），加上"-ive"变成形容词，描述倾向于攻击的人。
