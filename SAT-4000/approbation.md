# 👏 approbation

🔊 /ˌæprəˈbeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Approval or praise; an official or formal act of approving or sanctioning.

**中文：** 认可；赞许；批准；嘉许。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- approval（赞同）
- endorsement（认可）
- commendation（表彰）
- sanction（批准）
- acclaim（称赞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** win approbation, earn approbation, express approbation, public approbation, universal approbation

**Example:** The proposal received the approbation of the entire board of directors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher's groundbreaking study earned the **approbation** of the international scientific community last year.
   （这位研究者的开创性研究去年赢得了国际科学界的赞许。）

2. Without the **approbation** of the governing council, the new policy cannot be formally implemented.
   （没有管理委员会的批准，新政策无法正式实施。）

3. Her charitable efforts brought her widespread **approbation** from civic leaders and ordinary citizens alike.
   （她的慈善努力为她赢得了市政领导和普通市民的广泛认可。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Approbation" contains "approve" at its root — think of it as a formal, elevated way of saying "approval" or "praise."

"Approbation" 的词根含有 "approve"（赞同）——把它想成 "approval"（批准）的正式、高级说法。
