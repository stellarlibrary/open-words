# 🏝️ archipelago

🔊 /ˌɑːrkɪˈpeləɡoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A group or chain of islands clustered together in a body of water
**中文：** n. 群岛；列岛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- island chain（岛链）
- island group（岛群）
- cluster of islands（岛屿群）
- atoll chain（环礁链）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a volcanic archipelago / the Indonesian archipelago / scattered archipelago / across the archipelago**

👉 Example: The Indonesian archipelago consists of more than seventeen thousand islands spread across the equator.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The remote **archipelago** in the Pacific Ocean remained largely unexplored until European navigators arrived in the eighteenth century.
   这个太平洋上偏远的群岛在十八世纪欧洲航海家到来之前基本上未被探索过。

2. Marine biologists discovered several previously unknown species thriving in the coral reefs surrounding the tropical **archipelago**.
   海洋生物学家在这个热带群岛周围的珊瑚礁中发现了几种前所未知的物种。

3. The volcanic **archipelago** was formed over millions of years by tectonic activity along the boundary of two crustal plates.
   这个火山群岛是在数百万年间由两个地壳板块交界处的地质构造活动形成的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "archi-pelago" — imagine an "arch" of islands "pelagically" (in the sea) forming a chain across the ocean.
把"archipelago"想象成"arch（拱形）+ pelago（海洋）"——一串岛屿在海洋中排列成拱形，就是群岛。
