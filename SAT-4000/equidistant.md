# 📏 equidistant

🔊 /ˌiːkwɪˈdɪstənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) At equal distances from a particular point or from each other
**中文：** adj. 等距的；等距离的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equally spaced（等间距的）
- evenly distributed（均匀分布的）
- midway（居中的）
- symmetric（对称的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equidistant from / equidistant points / equidistant between / roughly equidistant**

👉 Example: The monitoring stations were placed equidistant from each other along the coastline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The three cities are approximately **equidistant** from the proposed site, making it an ideal location for the regional hub.
   三座城市与拟建地点的距离大致相等，使其成为区域枢纽的理想位置。

2. In cartography, an **equidistant** projection preserves accurate distances from the center point to all other locations on the map.
   在制图学中，等距投影保持从中心点到地图上所有其他位置的准确距离。

3. The engineer ensured that the support columns were **equidistant** from one another to distribute the structural load evenly.
   工程师确保各支撑柱之间等距排列，以均匀分配结构荷载。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equidistant" as "equi + distant" — "equi" means equal, "distant" means far away. Everything is equally far away.
把"equidistant"想成"equi + distant"——"equi"意为相等，"distant"意为远的。所有东西都一样远。
