# 💧 effuse

🔊 /ɪˈfjuːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To pour out or forth; to emit or radiate freely
**中文：** v. 流出；涌出；散发

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emanate（散发）
- exude（渗出）
- discharge（排出）
- radiate（辐射）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effuse light / effuse warmth / effuse gratitude / effuse from a source**

👉 Example: The volcanic vent continued to effuse molten lava across the barren landscape.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The deep-sea hydrothermal vents **effuse** mineral-rich water that sustains remarkable ecosystems on the ocean floor.
   深海热液喷口涌出富含矿物质的水，维持着海底非凡的生态系统。

2. The injured vessel began to **effuse** blood into the surrounding tissue, prompting the surgical team to intervene immediately.
   受伤的血管开始向周围组织渗出血液，促使外科团队立即进行干预。

3. Certain aromatic plants **effuse** volatile compounds that serve as natural deterrents against herbivorous insects.
   某些芳香植物散发出挥发性化合物，可作为抵御草食性昆虫的天然屏障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-FUSE" — like a fuse that blows and lets energy pour out; effuse means to flow or pour outward.
把"effuse"想成"ef-FUSE"——就像保险丝熔断后能量涌出一样，effuse意为向外流出或涌出。
