# 🌟 inspiring

🔊 /ɪnˈspaɪərɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the quality of motivating or encouraging someone; arousing feelings of enthusiasm and admiration
**中文：** adj. 鼓舞人心的；灌输的；启发灵感的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- uplifting（令人振奋的）
- motivating（激励的）
- stirring（激动人心的）
- encouraging（鼓舞的）
- exhilarating（令人兴奋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an inspiring speech / an inspiring leader / an inspiring story / truly inspiring**

👉 Example: Her inspiring speech at the conference moved the audience to take immediate action on educational reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The keynote speaker delivered an **inspiring** address that challenged the graduates to pursue meaningful careers in public service.
   主题演讲者发表了一篇鼓舞人心的演讲，激励毕业生追求有意义的公共服务事业。

2. The museum's **inspiring** exhibition on human resilience attracted visitors from across the globe throughout the entire season.
   博物馆关于人类韧性的鼓舞人心的展览在整个展期吸引了来自全球各地的参观者。

3. Many students cited the professor's **inspiring** teaching methods as the primary reason they chose to major in neuroscience.
   许多学生将这位教授鼓舞人心的教学方法视为他们选择神经科学专业的主要原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inspiring" as "in-spirit-ing" — something that is actively putting spirit and energy into you right now.
把"inspiring"想成"in-spirit-ing"——某事正在把精神和能量注入你体内。
