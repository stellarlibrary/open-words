# ⚖️ juridical

🔊 /dʒʊˈrɪdɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to the law, legal proceedings, or the administration of justice
**中文：** adj. 司法的；法院的；法律上的；裁判上的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- legal（法律的）
- judicial（司法的）
- forensic（法庭的）
- legislative（立法的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **juridical authority / juridical process / juridical framework / juridical review**

👉 Example: The committee examined the juridical implications of the proposed constitutional amendment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor devoted her entire career to analyzing the **juridical** foundations of international human rights law.
   这位教授将她的整个职业生涯都献给了分析国际人权法的法律基础。

2. Several **juridical** experts were summoned to testify before the congressional committee regarding the proposed reform legislation.
   数位法律专家被召集到国会委员会面前就拟议的改革立法作证。

3. The **juridical** framework governing intellectual property rights varies considerably across different national jurisdictions worldwide.
   管辖知识产权的法律框架在世界各国不同的司法管辖区之间差异相当大。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "juridical" as containing "jurid-" from Latin "juris" (law) — anything juridical is connected to the world of law and courts.
把"juridical"想成包含拉丁语"juris"（法律）——任何juridical的事物都与法律和法庭的世界相关。
