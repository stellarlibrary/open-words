# 🩺 amputate

🔊 /ˈæmpjʊteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cut off a limb or part of the body, typically by surgical operation; to remove or cut away entirely
**中文：** 截肢；切断；删除

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sever（切断）
- excise（切除）
- remove（移除）
- truncate（截断）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amputate a limb / amputate a leg / amputate below the knee / surgically amputate**

👉 Example: The surgeon had no choice but to amputate the infected limb to save the patient's life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The battlefield medic was forced to **amputate** the soldier's leg to prevent the gangrene from spreading further.
   战地医生被迫截断士兵的腿，以防止坏疽进一步扩散。

2. After the severe frostbite, doctors determined it was necessary to **amputate** three of the climber's fingers.
   在严重冻伤之后，医生确定有必要截除登山者的三根手指。

3. The editorial board decided to **amputate** several chapters from the manuscript to meet publication length requirements.
   编辑委员会决定删除手稿中的几个章节，以满足出版篇幅要求。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ampu-TATE" — imagine "tating" (taking) "amp" (a part) away, like cutting off a portion of something entirely.
把"amputate"拆分为"amp（一部分）+ tate（拿走）"——想象把身体的一部分完全拿走，就是截肢。
