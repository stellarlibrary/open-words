# 🔬 diagnosis

🔊 /ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The identification of the nature of an illness or other problem by examination of the symptoms; a conclusion reached through analysis
**中文：** n. 诊断；判断；分析结论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assessment（评估）
- evaluation（评价）
- identification（鉴定）
- determination（确定）
- analysis（分析）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a diagnosis / receive a diagnosis / early diagnosis / diagnosis of a disease**

👉 Example: Early diagnosis of the condition significantly improves the patient's long-term prognosis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician's **diagnosis** confirmed that the patient was suffering from a rare autoimmune disorder requiring immediate specialized treatment.
   医生的诊断证实该患者患有一种罕见的自身免疫性疾病，需要立即进行专业治疗。

2. Advances in medical technology have greatly improved the speed and accuracy of **diagnosis** for many previously undetectable conditions.
   医疗技术的进步极大地提高了对许多以前无法检测的疾病的诊断速度和准确性。

3. The mechanic's thorough **diagnosis** of the engine problem revealed that a faulty sensor had caused the repeated malfunctions.
   机修工对发动机问题的彻底诊断揭示了一个故障传感器导致了反复出现的故障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dia-gnosis" as "through knowing" (from Greek dia = through, gnosis = knowledge) — a diagnosis is reached through gaining knowledge of the problem.
把"diagnosis"想成"通过了解"（来自希腊语dia=通过，gnosis=知识）——诊断是通过了解问题而得出结论。
