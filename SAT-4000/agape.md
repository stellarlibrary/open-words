# 😲 agape

🔊 /əˈɡeɪp/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wide open, especially with surprise or wonder; gaping in astonishment
**中文：** 张口惊视的；目瞪口呆的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astonished（震惊的）
- awestruck（敬畏的）
- dumbfounded（目瞪口呆的）
- gaping（张嘴凝视的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mouth agape / stand agape / stare agape / eyes agape**

👉 Example: The audience sat with mouths agape as the magician performed the seemingly impossible illusion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The children stood **agape** before the towering dinosaur skeleton displayed in the grand hall of the museum.
   孩子们在博物馆大厅展出的高耸恐龙骨架前目瞪口呆地站着。

2. Spectators watched **agape** as the acrobat executed a flawless triple somersault high above the circus arena floor.
   当杂技演员在马戏场高空完美完成三重翻转时，观众们惊得张大了嘴。

3. The jury members sat **agape** when the defendant unexpectedly confessed to the crime during the dramatic court proceeding.
   当被告在戏剧性的庭审过程中出人意料地认罪时，陪审团成员们惊得目瞪口呆。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a gap" in your mouth — when you are agape, your mouth forms a gap because it is wide open in shock.
想象嘴巴里有"a gap"（一个缺口）——当你agape时，你的嘴因震惊而张大，形成一个缺口。
