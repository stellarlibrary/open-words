# 🛡️ invincible

🔊 /ɪnˈvɪnsəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Too powerful to be defeated or overcome; unconquerable
**中文：** adj. 不能征服的；无敌的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unconquerable（不可征服的）
- unbeatable（无敌的）
- indomitable（不屈不挠的）
- insurmountable（不可逾越的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **invincible army / invincible spirit / seem invincible / virtually invincible**

👉 Example: The team's undefeated record made them appear invincible to their rivals throughout the season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The empire's **invincible** navy dominated the seas for centuries, securing vital trade routes across the globe.
   该帝国无敌的海军统治了海洋数个世纪，确保了全球重要贸易航线的安全。

2. Although the champion appeared **invincible**, a determined young challenger ultimately exposed critical weaknesses in his technique.
   尽管这位冠军看起来不可战胜，但一位意志坚定的年轻挑战者最终暴露了他技术上的关键弱点。

3. The philosopher argued that an **invincible** commitment to truth remains the foundation of any just society.
   这位哲学家认为，对真理不可动摇的承诺仍然是任何公正社会的基石。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-VINCE-ible" — "vince" comes from Latin "vincere" meaning "to conquer," and "in-" means "not," so invincible means "not able to be conquered."
把"invincible"想成"in-VINCE-ible"——"vince"来自拉丁语"vincere"意为"征服"，而"in-"表示"不"，所以invincible就是"不能被征服的"。
