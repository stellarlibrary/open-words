# 📜 preamble

🔊 /ˈpriːæmbəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An introductory statement or preliminary explanation; the opening section of a document or speech. (v.) To make an introductory statement
**中文：** n. 序文；前言；先兆 v. 作序文

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- introduction（引言）
- preface（前言）
- foreword（序言）
- prologue（开场白）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preamble to / without preamble / lengthy preamble / constitutional preamble**

👉 Example: The preamble to the Constitution outlines the fundamental principles upon which the nation was founded.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **preamble** of the international treaty articulated the shared commitment of all signatory nations to preserving global peace.
   该国际条约的序言阐明了所有签署国对维护全球和平的共同承诺。

2. Without any **preamble**, the chairperson announced the immediate dissolution of the committee and the reassignment of all members.
   主席没有任何开场白，直接宣布了委员会的立即解散和所有成员的重新分配。

3. The professor devoted an extensive **preamble** to explaining the historical context before introducing the central thesis of the lecture.
   教授用了大量的引言来解释历史背景，然后才引入讲座的核心论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-amble" — "pre" means before, "amble" means to walk — a preamble is the walk you take before the main journey begins.
把"preamble"想成"pre（前）+amble（漫步）"——序言就是在正式旅程开始前的漫步。
