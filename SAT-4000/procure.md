# 🤝 procure

🔊 /prəˈkjʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To obtain or acquire something, especially with care or effort; to bring about; to cause
**中文：** v. 获得，取得；导致

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obtain（获得）
- acquire（取得）
- secure（确保获得）
- attain（达到）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **procure funding / procure supplies / procure a contract / procure services**

👉 Example: The organization worked diligently to procure the necessary funding for the humanitarian relief mission.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team managed to **procure** rare specimens from a remote island for their comprehensive biological study.
   研究团队设法从一个偏远岛屿获取了稀有标本，用于全面的生物学研究。

2. The logistics department was tasked with **procuring** essential supplies before the onset of the severe winter season.
   后勤部门的任务是在严冬来临之前采购必需的物资。

3. It took several months of negotiations to **procure** the necessary permits for the large-scale construction project.
   经过数月的谈判才为大型建设项目取得所需的许可证。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-cure" as "professionally cure a need" — you procure something when you professionally work to get what is needed.
把"procure"想成"pro（为了）+ cure（解决）"——为了解决需求而去获取所需之物。
