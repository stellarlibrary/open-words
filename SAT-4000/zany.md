# 🤡 zany

🔊 /ˈzeɪni/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Amusingly unconventional, comical, or absurd. (n.) A clown, buffoon, or foolish person
**中文：** adj. 滑稽的；古怪的；愚蠢的 n. 小丑；丑角；笨人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comical（滑稽的）
- eccentric（古怪的）
- wacky（疯疯癫癫的）
- absurd（荒谬的）
- buffoon（小丑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **zany humor / zany antics / zany character / zany comedy**

👉 Example: The film's zany humor and unpredictable plot twists made it a surprise hit at the box office.
