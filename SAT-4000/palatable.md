# 😋 palatable

🔊 /ˈpælətəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Pleasant or acceptable to the taste; agreeable or satisfactory to the mind or feelings; easy to accept or tolerate.
**中文：** adj. 美味的，可口的；愉快的；可接受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appetizing（开胃的）
- tasty（美味的）
- agreeable（令人愉快的）
- acceptable（可接受的）
- savory（可口的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **palatable food / palatable option / palatable to the public / more palatable / hardly palatable**

👉 Example: The committee worked to make the proposed budget cuts more palatable to members of the opposition party.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chef transformed simple and inexpensive ingredients into a remarkably **palatable** dish that impressed even the most discerning food critics.
   厨师将简单而廉价的食材变成了一道非常**可口的**菜肴，甚至令最挑剔的美食评论家也赞叹不已。

2. The administration struggled to present the new taxation policy in a manner that would be **palatable** to the general voting public.
   政府努力以一种让普通投票公众能**接受的**方式来呈现新的税收政策。

3. Without proper seasoning and careful preparation, even the finest cuts of meat can become barely **palatable** to most diners.
   如果没有适当的调味和精心准备，即使是最好的肉块对大多数食客来说也几乎不**可口**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "palat-able" as related to "palate" (the roof of your mouth, used for tasting) — if something is palatable, it pleases your palate.
把"palatable"和"palate"（上颚，用于品尝）联系起来——如果某物是palatable的，它就能取悦你的味觉。
