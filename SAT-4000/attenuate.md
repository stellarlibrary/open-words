# 🔬 attenuate

🔊 /əˈtenjuːeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To reduce the force, effect, or value of something; to make thin or slender. (adj.) Weakened or reduced in force
**中文：** v. 使减弱；使纤细 adj. 减弱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diminish（减少）
- weaken（削弱）
- dilute（稀释）
- lessen（减轻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **attenuate the effects / attenuate the signal / attenuate the risk / attenuated virus**

👉 Example: The thick walls of the building attenuate outside noise, creating a remarkably quiet interior.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers designed special materials to **attenuate** the dangerous radiation levels inside the nuclear facility.
   工程师们设计了特殊材料来减弱核设施内危险的辐射水平。

2. The vaccine uses an **attenuated** form of the virus to stimulate immunity without causing the actual disease.
   该疫苗使用减毒形式的病毒来激发免疫力，而不会引起实际疾病。

3. Distance and atmospheric conditions gradually **attenuate** the radio signal until it becomes undetectable by receivers.
   距离和大气条件会逐渐减弱无线电信号，直到接收器无法检测到为止。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "attenuate" as "a-THIN-uate" — to make something thinner or weaker, reducing its strength or intensity.
把"attenuate"想成"a-THIN-uate"——使某物变得更薄或更弱，减少其强度或力度。
