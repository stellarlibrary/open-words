# 🐒 anthropoid

🔊 /ˈænθrəpɔɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Resembling a human being in form; (n.) a higher primate, especially an ape or a member of the superfamily that includes humans.

**中文：** （形容词）类人猿的，似人的；（名词）类人猿。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- apelike 似猿的
- humanoid 类人的
- primate 灵长类动物
- hominoid 类人猿

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **anthropoid ape**, **anthropoid features**, **anthropoid primate**. As a noun, it refers specifically to great apes such as gorillas and chimpanzees.

> Example: The fossil displayed distinctly **anthropoid** characteristics in its skeletal structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers discovered a new **anthropoid** species in the dense tropical forests of Southeast Asia.
   研究人员在东南亚茂密的热带森林中发现了一种新的类人猿物种。

2. The museum exhibit featured an impressive collection of **anthropoid** skulls spanning several million years.
   博物馆的展览展出了一批跨越数百万年的令人印象深刻的类人猿头骨。

3. Certain **anthropoid** primates demonstrate remarkable cognitive abilities comparable to those of young children.
   某些类人猿灵长类动物展现出与幼童相当的非凡认知能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Split it: "anthropo-" (human, from Greek *anthropos*) + "-oid" (resembling). Something **anthropoid** looks like or resembles a human being.

拆分记忆："anthropo-"（人类，源自希腊语 *anthropos*）+"-oid"（类似的）。**Anthropoid** 就是看起来像人类的、与人类相似的。
