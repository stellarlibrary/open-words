# 🧪 formula

🔊 /ˈfɔːrmjələ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A mathematical relationship or rule expressed in symbols; a set method or procedure for achieving something; a list of ingredients or a recipe
**中文：** n. 公式；准则；配方

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equation（方程式）
- recipe（配方）
- method（方法）
- blueprint（蓝图）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mathematical formula / winning formula / secret formula / formula for success**

👉 Example: Scientists derived a formula to predict the rate of chemical reactions under varying conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician published an elegant **formula** that unified two previously unrelated branches of theoretical physics and number theory.
   这位数学家发表了一个优雅的公式，统一了理论物理和数论中两个先前无关的分支。

2. The pharmaceutical company guards the proprietary **formula** of its best-selling medication with the strictest confidentiality protocols available.
   这家制药公司以最严格的保密协议守护其最畅销药物的专有配方。

3. There is no simple **formula** for achieving lasting peace in regions that have endured decades of entrenched political conflict.
   对于经历了数十年根深蒂固政治冲突的地区，没有实现持久和平的简单准则。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "formula" as a "form" you follow — just as you fill out a form step by step, a formula is a fixed pattern or recipe you follow to get a result.
把"formula"想成一个你遵循的"form（表格）"——就像你一步步填写表格，formula是你遵循来获得结果的固定模式或配方。
