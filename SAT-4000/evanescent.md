# ✨ evanescent

🔊 /ˌɛvəˈnɛsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Soon passing out of sight, memory, or existence; quickly fading or disappearing
**中文：** adj. 容易消散的；逐渐消失的；会凋零的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fleeting（短暂的）
- transient（转瞬即逝的）
- ephemeral（短暂的）
- vanishing（消失的）
- momentary（瞬间的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **evanescent beauty / evanescent moment / evanescent glow / evanescent memory**

👉 Example: The evanescent morning mist dissolved as the sun rose above the mountain ridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that happiness is inherently **evanescent**, urging students to appreciate each moment before it fades away.
   这位哲学家认为幸福本质上是短暂易逝的，并敦促学生们在每个时刻消逝之前珍惜它。

2. Scientists captured the **evanescent** light waves that exist only at the boundary between two optical media of different densities.
   科学家们捕捉到了仅存在于两种不同密度光学介质边界处的消散光波。

3. The **evanescent** fragrance of cherry blossoms in spring reminds us that nature's most exquisite displays are often the briefest.
   春天樱花短暂的芬芳提醒我们，大自然最精美的展示往往也是最短暂的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "evan-escent" as "vanish-escent" — something that vanishes quickly, like steam evaporating into thin air.
把"evanescent"想成"vanish-escent"——像蒸汽消散在空气中一样，迅速消失的东西。
