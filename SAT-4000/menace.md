# ⚠️ menace

🔊 /ˈmɛnəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person or thing that poses a threat or danger; an atmosphere of threatening behavior. (v.) To threaten or intimidate; to pose a danger to.
**中文：** n. 威胁；危险的人 v. 恐吓，威胁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- threat（威胁）
- danger（危险）
- intimidate（恐吓）
- peril（危难）
- hazard（隐患）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a menace to society / pose a menace / menace the public / growing menace**

👉 Example: The rising crime rate was considered a serious menace to public safety in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Invasive species have become a significant **menace** to biodiversity in wetland ecosystems across the continent.
   入侵物种已成为整个大陆湿地生态系统生物多样性的重大威胁。

2. The dictator continued to **menace** neighboring countries with aggressive military exercises along their shared borders.
   独裁者继续在共有边境沿线进行具有攻击性的军事演习，威胁邻国。

3. Cybersecurity experts warned that ransomware attacks now **menace** critical infrastructure including hospitals and power grids.
   网络安全专家警告说，勒索软件攻击现在正威胁着包括医院和电网在内的关键基础设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "menace" sounding like "men + ace" — imagine a group of men holding aces as weapons, menacing everyone around them.
把"menace"想成"men（男人们）+ ace（王牌）"——想象一群人拿着王牌当武器，威胁周围的每个人。
