# 🔢 multiple

🔊 /ˈmʌltɪpl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or involving several parts, elements, or members; more than one. (n.) A number that may be divided by another a certain number of times without a remainder
**中文：** adj. 多重的；多样的；许多的；多倍的 n. 倍数；并联

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- numerous（许多的）
- several（若干的）
- manifold（多方面的）
- various（各种各样的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **multiple choice / multiple factors / multiple occasions / multiple sources**

👉 Example: The researcher consulted multiple sources before drawing any definitive conclusions about the phenomenon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that **multiple** factors contributed to the structural failure of the bridge during the storm.
   调查显示，多种因素导致了暴风雨期间桥梁的结构性损坏。

2. Students must demonstrate proficiency across **multiple** disciplines before they can be admitted to the advanced graduate program.
   学生必须在多个学科中展示熟练能力，才能被录取进入高级研究生项目。

3. The number twelve is a **multiple** of both three and four, making it particularly useful in mathematical applications.
   数字十二是三和四的倍数，这使得它在数学应用中特别有用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "multiple" as "MULTI-ple" — "multi" means many, so multiple simply means many or more than one of something.
把"multiple"想成"MULTI-ple"——"multi"意为"多"，所以multiple就是许多或不止一个的意思。
