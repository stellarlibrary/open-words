# 🌿 conservationist

🔊 /ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃənɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who advocates for the protection and preservation of the natural environment and its resources.
**中文：** n. 自然资源保护论者；环境保护主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- environmentalist（环保主义者）
- preservationist（保护主义者）
- ecologist（生态学家）
- naturalist（博物学家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wildlife conservationist / marine conservationist / leading conservationist / conservationist efforts**

👉 Example: The renowned conservationist dedicated decades of work to protecting endangered species across the African continent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **conservationist** campaigned tirelessly to establish new marine protected areas along the coast to safeguard declining fish populations.
   这位自然资源保护论者不懈地奔走呼吁，在沿海地区建立新的海洋保护区，以保护日益减少的鱼类种群。

2. As a prominent **conservationist**, she persuaded the government to enact stricter regulations against illegal logging in tropical rainforests.
   作为一位著名的环境保护主义者，她说服政府颁布了更严格的法规，禁止在热带雨林中非法伐木。

3. The young **conservationist** founded an organization dedicated to restoring wetland habitats that had been destroyed by urban development.
   这位年轻的自然资源保护论者创立了一个组织，致力于恢复被城市开发破坏的湿地栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "conserve + ation + ist" — a conservationist is a person (-ist) devoted to the conservation (conserve + ation) of nature.
把"conservationist"想成"conserve(保护) + ation(行为) + ist(人)"——保护主义者就是致力于保护自然的人。
