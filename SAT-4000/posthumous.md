# 🕊️ posthumous

🔊 /ˈpɑːstʃəməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring, awarded, or published after the death of the person concerned; born after the death of the father
**中文：** adj. 死后的；遗腹的；作者死后出版的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- postmortem（死后的）
- after-death（死后的）
- delayed（延迟的）
- belated（迟来的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **posthumous award / posthumous publication / posthumous recognition / posthumous honor / posthumously**

👉 Example: The distinguished scientist received a posthumous Nobel Prize for her groundbreaking contributions to physics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's **posthumous** novel was published two years after her death and quickly became an international literary sensation.
   这位作家的遗作在她去世两年后出版，并迅速成为国际文学轰动之作。

2. The soldier was awarded a **posthumous** medal of honor for his extraordinary bravery displayed during the final military engagement.
   这位士兵因在最后一次军事行动中表现出的非凡勇气而被追授荣誉勋章。

3. Scholars have debated whether the **posthumous** editing of the philosopher's manuscripts accurately reflects his original intellectual intentions.
   学者们一直在争论对这位哲学家手稿的遗著编辑是否准确反映了其原初的学术意图。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "post-humous" as "post-humus" — "post" means after and "humus" relates to earth (burial ground), so posthumous means after being laid in the earth.
把"posthumous"想成"post-humus"——"post"意为之后，"humus"与泥土（墓地）有关，所以posthumous意为入土之后的。
