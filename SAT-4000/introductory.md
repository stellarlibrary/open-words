# 📘 introductory

🔊 /ˌɪntrəˈdʌktəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Serving as an introduction; preliminary or basic, designed for beginners or as an opening
**中文：** adj. 引导的；介绍的；开端的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- preliminary（初步的）
- opening（开场的）
- elementary（基础的）
- prefatory（序言的）
- initial（最初的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **introductory course / introductory remarks / introductory chapter / introductory offer**

👉 Example: The professor's introductory remarks set the tone for the entire semester of advanced research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students must complete the **introductory** course in biology before enrolling in any advanced laboratory seminars.
   学生必须完成生物学入门课程，才能注册任何高级实验研讨课。

2. The author's **introductory** chapter provides essential context for understanding the complex theories discussed later.
   作者的引言章节为理解后文讨论的复杂理论提供了必要的背景。

3. The company offered an **introductory** discount to attract new customers and establish a loyal consumer base.
   公司提供了一个优惠的入门折扣，以吸引新客户并建立忠实的消费群体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "introductory" as "intro-duct-ory" — it "ducts" or leads you "into" a subject, like a door that opens to a new room.
把"introductory"想成"intro（进入）-duct（引导）-ory"——它把你"引导"进入一个主题，就像一扇通往新房间的门。
