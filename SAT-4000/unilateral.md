# ☝️ unilateral

🔊 /ˌjuːnɪˈlætərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Performed by or affecting only one side, party, or group; done without the agreement of others
**中文：** 单边的；单方面的；一方的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- one-sided（单方面的）
- independent（独立的）
- sole（单独的）
- unreciprocated（未回报的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unilateral decision / unilateral action / unilateral ceasefire / unilateral agreement**

👉 Example: The president's unilateral decision to withdraw troops sparked intense debate among congressional leaders.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The country's **unilateral** withdrawal from the treaty alarmed its allies and destabilized the entire diplomatic framework of the region.
   该国单方面退出条约使其盟友感到震惊，并破坏了整个地区的外交框架。

2. Critics argued that the CEO's **unilateral** restructuring of the company ignored valuable input from experienced department heads and advisors.
   批评者认为，首席执行官单方面重组公司忽视了经验丰富的部门主管和顾问的宝贵意见。

3. International law generally discourages **unilateral** military intervention, favoring multilateral cooperation through established diplomatic channels and institutions.
   国际法通常不鼓励单边军事干预，而是倾向于通过既定的外交渠道和机构进行多边合作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "uni-lateral" as "one side" — "uni" means one and "lateral" means side, so unilateral means involving only one side.
把"unilateral"想成"uni（一个）+ lateral（侧面的）"——只涉及一个方面，即单边的、单方面的。
