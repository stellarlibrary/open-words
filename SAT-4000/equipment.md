# 🔧 equipment

🔊 /ɪˈkwɪpmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The tools, machines, or other items needed for a particular purpose or activity
**中文：** n. 设备；装备；装置

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apparatus（器械）
- gear（装备）
- machinery（机械设备）
- instruments（仪器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laboratory equipment / heavy equipment / a piece of equipment / equipment failure**

👉 Example: The laboratory purchased new equipment to conduct advanced molecular research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital invested heavily in state-of-the-art medical **equipment** to improve patient diagnosis and treatment outcomes.
   医院大量投资最先进的医疗设备，以改善患者的诊断和治疗效果。

2. Researchers could not proceed with the experiment because essential **equipment** had been damaged during the recent power outage.
   由于最近的停电损坏了关键设备，研究人员无法继续进行实验。

3. The construction company regularly inspects all heavy **equipment** to ensure compliance with federal safety regulations and standards.
   建筑公司定期检查所有重型设备，以确保符合联邦安全法规和标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equip-ment" as "things that equip you" — the tools and gear that prepare you for a task.
把"equipment"想成"equip（装备）+ ment（名词后缀）"——让你做好准备的工具和装备。
