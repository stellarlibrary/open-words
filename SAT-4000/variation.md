# 🎭 variation

🔊 /ˌvɛəriˈeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A change or difference in condition, amount, or level; a different form of something
**中文：** n. 变化；变异；变种；变奏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fluctuation（波动）
- alteration（改变）
- deviation（偏差）
- diversity（多样性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **variation in / variation on a theme / genetic variation / seasonal variation**

👉 Example: There is considerable variation in test scores among students from different socioeconomic backgrounds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study revealed significant **variation** in rainfall patterns across the region over the past three decades of observation.
   该研究揭示了过去三十年观测期间该地区降雨模式的显著变化。

2. Genetic **variation** within a species provides the raw material upon which natural selection acts to drive evolutionary adaptation.
   物种内部的遗传变异为自然选择提供了驱动进化适应的原材料。

3. The composer wrote a brilliant set of **variations** on a simple folk melody that showcased extraordinary technical mastery.
   这位作曲家根据一首简单的民间旋律写了一组精彩的变奏曲，展示了非凡的技术造诣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vari-ation" as "vary + action" — the action of varying or changing from one state to another.
把"variation"想成"vary + action"——从一种状态变化到另一种状态的动作。
