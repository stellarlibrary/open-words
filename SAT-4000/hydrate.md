# 💧 hydrate

🔊 /ˈhaɪdreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A chemical compound formed by the union of water with another substance. (v.) To cause a substance to combine with water or to supply adequate water to the body
**中文：** n. 水合物 v. 使成水化合物；补充水分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- moisturize（保湿）
- saturate（浸透）
- soak（浸泡）
- replenish（补充）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hydrate the body / hydrate the skin / a crystalline hydrate / fully hydrated**

👉 Example: Athletes must consistently hydrate during intense training to maintain peak physical performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemical analysis revealed that the compound existed as a stable **hydrate** containing three molecules of water per unit.
   化学分析显示，该化合物以稳定的水合物形式存在，每单元含有三个水分子。

2. Medical professionals advise patients to **hydrate** thoroughly before undergoing any surgical procedure requiring general anesthesia.
   医学专业人员建议患者在接受任何需要全身麻醉的外科手术前充分补充水分。

3. The geologist identified the mineral sample as a naturally occurring **hydrate** formed under extreme pressure deep beneath the ocean floor.
   地质学家将该矿物样本鉴定为在海底深处极端压力下形成的天然水合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydra-" from Greek "hydor" meaning water — a hydrate is a substance bonded with water.
把"hydra-"想成希腊语"hydor"意为水——hydrate就是与水结合的物质。
