# 🌍 antipodes

🔊 /ænˈtɪpədiːz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The direct opposite side of the earth from a given point; by extension, two things that are diametrically opposite or contrary to each other.

**中文：** 对跖地（地球上的正对面）；正相反的事物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- opposite（对立面）
- antithesis（对立）
- contrary（相反）
- inverse（反面）
- counterpart（对应物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** the antipodes of, at the antipodes, cultural antipodes, ideological antipodes

**Example:** Their political philosophies were the **antipodes** of each other — one advocated liberty, the other control.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two scholars occupied intellectual **antipodes**, disagreeing on virtually every fundamental principle of governance.
   两位学者在思想上处于完全对立的位置，几乎在每一个治理的基本原则上都意见相左。

2. New Zealand and Spain are nearly exact geographic **antipodes**, situated on opposite sides of the globe.
   新西兰和西班牙几乎是精确的地理对跖地，分别位于地球的两侧。

3. His optimistic temperament stood at the **antipodes** of her characteristically cautious and skeptical worldview.
   他乐观的性格与她一贯谨慎多疑的世界观形成了鲜明的对比。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "anti-" (opposite) + "-podes" (feet). The antipodes are where people stand with their feet pointing directly opposite to yours — the other side of the earth.

联想："anti-"（相反）+ "-podes"（脚）。antipodes 就是脚与你的脚正对着的地方——地球的另一面，引申为正相反的事物。
