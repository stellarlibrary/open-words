# ⚖️ equipoise

🔊 /ˈɛkwɪpɔɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of balance or equilibrium between opposing forces or influences
**中文：** n. 平衡；均势

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equilibrium（平衡）
- balance（均衡）
- parity（均等）
- symmetry（对称）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain equipoise / achieve equipoise / a state of equipoise / clinical equipoise**

👉 Example: The negotiator sought to maintain equipoise between the competing interests of both parties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat skillfully maintained **equipoise** between the two rival nations, preventing tensions from escalating into open conflict.
   外交官巧妙地维持了两个对立国家之间的均势，防止紧张局势升级为公开冲突。

2. Clinical **equipoise** is essential in medical research, ensuring that no treatment arm is presumed superior before trial results emerge.
   临床均势在医学研究中至关重要，确保在试验结果出来之前不预设任何治疗组优于另一组。

3. The artist achieved a remarkable **equipoise** of light and shadow, creating a composition that conveyed both tranquility and depth.
   这位艺术家在光与影之间取得了非凡的平衡，创造出既宁静又有深度的构图。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equi-poise" as "equal poise" — standing with equal weight on both sides, perfectly balanced.
把"equipoise"想成"equi（相等）+ poise（姿态）"——两边均等的姿态，完美平衡。
