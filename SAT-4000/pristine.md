# ✨ pristine

🔊 /ˈprɪstiːn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) In its original condition; unspoiled; pure and clean, as if new
**中文：** adj. 原始的，古时的；纯朴的；清新的；纯洁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immaculate（完美无瑕的）
- unspoiled（未被破坏的）
- pure（纯净的）
- untouched（未受触碰的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pristine condition / pristine wilderness / pristine beaches / pristine environment**

👉 Example: The national park preserves vast stretches of pristine wilderness for future generations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists discovered the ancient tomb in **pristine** condition, with every artifact perfectly intact and undisturbed.
   考古学家发现这座古墓保存完好，每件文物都完整无损、未被扰动。

2. Environmental activists campaigned vigorously to protect the **pristine** coral reefs from industrial pollution and commercial development.
   环保活动人士积极倡导保护原始珊瑚礁免受工业污染和商业开发的侵害。

3. The rare manuscript remained in **pristine** condition despite being stored in the library for over three centuries.
   这份珍贵的手稿尽管在图书馆保存了三个多世纪，仍然保持着完好无损的状态。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pristine" as "priest-clean" — as clean and pure as a priest's white robes, untouched and spotless.
把"pristine"想成"priest-clean"——像牧师的白袍一样干净纯洁，未被触碰、一尘不染。
