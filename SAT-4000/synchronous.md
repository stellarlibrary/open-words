# ⏱️ synchronous

🔊 /ˈsɪŋkrənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Existing or occurring at the same time; operating in unison or at the same rate
**中文：** adj. 同时的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- simultaneous（同时的）
- concurrent（并发的）
- coincident（同时发生的）
- parallel（平行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **synchronous communication / synchronous learning / synchronous operation / synchronous with**

👉 Example: The online course offered both synchronous and asynchronous learning options for enrolled students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineers designed a **synchronous** motor that operates at a speed precisely matched to the frequency of the supply current.
   工程师们设计了一台同步电机，其运行速度与供电电流的频率精确匹配。

2. The university introduced **synchronous** online classes to ensure real-time interaction between instructors and remote students during lectures.
   该大学引入了同步在线课程，以确保讲座期间教师与远程学生之间的实时互动。

3. The two satellites maintained **synchronous** orbits, allowing them to relay continuous communication signals across the entire continent.
   两颗卫星保持同步轨道，使它们能够在整个大陆上中继连续的通信信号。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "syn-chron-ous" as "same-time-ous" — "syn" means together and "chronos" means time, so synchronous means happening at the same time.
把"synchronous"想成"syn（一起）+ chronos（时间）"——一起在同一时间发生，即同步的。
