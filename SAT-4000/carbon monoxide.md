# ☁️ carbon monoxide

🔊 /ˌkɑːrbən məˈnɒksaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, odorless, highly toxic gas (CO) produced by the incomplete combustion of carbon-containing fuels
**中文：** n. 一氧化碳

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- CO（一氧化碳）
- carbonous oxide（碳的氧化物）
- toxic gas（有毒气体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **carbon monoxide poisoning / carbon monoxide detector / carbon monoxide emissions / exposure to carbon monoxide**

👉 Example: Every household should install a carbon monoxide detector to prevent accidental poisoning.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Carbon monoxide** poisoning remains a leading cause of accidental death because the gas is virtually undetectable without specialized equipment.
   一氧化碳中毒仍然是意外死亡的主要原因之一，因为这种气体不借助专业设备几乎无法检测。

2. Automobile exhaust systems produce significant quantities of **carbon monoxide**, which poses serious risks to public health in urban areas.
   汽车排气系统产生大量一氧化碳，对城市地区的公共健康构成严重威胁。

3. Building codes now mandate the installation of **carbon monoxide** detectors in all residential properties to safeguard occupant welfare.
   建筑法规现在要求在所有住宅物业中安装一氧化碳探测器，以保障住户安全。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carbon monoxide" as "carbon + mono-oxide" — "mono" means one, so it is one carbon atom bonded with just one oxygen atom (CO), unlike dioxide which has two.
把"carbon monoxide"想成"carbon（碳）+ mono(一个)-oxide（氧化物）"——"mono"表示一，即一个碳原子仅与一个氧原子结合（CO），不像二氧化碳有两个。
