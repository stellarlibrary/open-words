# 🤝 assignee

🔊 /ˌæsaɪˈniː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person to whom a right, property, or responsibility is legally transferred; a designated agent or representative
**中文：** 代理人；受托人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agent（代理人）
- delegate（委托人）
- representative（代表）
- trustee（受托人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **legal assignee / assignee of property / assignee of a patent / the assignee shall**

👉 Example: In legal documents, the assignee is the party who receives transfer of rights or obligations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court recognized the **assignee** as the rightful holder of the intellectual property following the transfer agreement.
   法院在转让协议之后承认该受托人为知识产权的合法持有人。

2. As the **assignee** of the estate, she assumed full legal responsibility for managing all inherited assets.
   作为遗产的受托人，她承担了管理所有继承资产的全部法律责任。

3. The original patent holder designated a corporate **assignee** to commercialize the invention across international markets.
   原专利持有人指定了一家公司作为受让人，在国际市场上将该发明商业化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assign + ee" — the "-ee" suffix means the person who receives the action, so an assignee is the one who is assigned something.
把"assignee"拆成"assign + ee"——后缀"-ee"表示接受动作的人，所以assignee就是被指派某事的人。
