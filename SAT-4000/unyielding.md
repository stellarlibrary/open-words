# 💪 unyielding

🔊 /ʌnˈjiːldɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not giving way to pressure or persuasion; firm and resolute; physically rigid or stiff
**中文：** adj. 不屈的；坚强的；坚定的；不退让的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resolute（坚决的）
- steadfast（坚定不移的）
- inflexible（不可动摇的）
- tenacious（顽强的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unyielding determination / unyielding stance / unyielding spirit / unyielding resistance**

👉 Example: The negotiator maintained an unyielding stance on the core terms of the trade agreement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The civil rights leader demonstrated **unyielding** commitment to justice despite facing relentless opposition and personal threats.
   这位民权领袖展现了对正义不屈不挠的承诺，尽管面临着无情的反对和人身威胁。

2. The ancient fortress, built upon **unyielding** granite, had withstood centuries of warfare and natural disaster without collapse.
   这座建在坚硬花岗岩上的古老堡垒，历经数百年的战争和自然灾害而未倒塌。

3. Her **unyielding** pursuit of academic excellence inspired an entire generation of young scholars in the community.
   她对学术卓越不懈的追求激励了社区里整整一代年轻学者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-yield-ing" — someone who does not yield or surrender, standing firm like an immovable wall.
把"unyielding"想成"不屈服的"——un（不）+ yielding（屈服），就像一堵不可动摇的墙一样坚定。
