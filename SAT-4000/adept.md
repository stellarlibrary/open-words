# 🎯 adept

🔊 /əˈdɛpt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Very skilled or proficient at something (n.) A person who is skilled or proficient at a particular activity
**中文：** adj. 熟练的，擅长的 n. 内行，能手

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proficient（精通的）
- skilled（熟练的）
- dexterous（灵巧的）
- accomplished（有造诣的）
- expert（专家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adept at / adept in / adept with / become adept / particularly adept**

👉 Example: She proved herself adept at navigating the complex bureaucratic procedures of international diplomacy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon was remarkably **adept** at performing minimally invasive procedures on the most delicate tissues.
   这位外科医生在对最精细的组织进行微创手术方面极其娴熟。

2. A truly **adept** negotiator can find common ground even among the most adversarial parties in a dispute.
   一位真正的谈判能手即使在争端中最对立的各方之间也能找到共同点。

3. Over the years, she became increasingly **adept** at synthesizing vast quantities of data into coherent reports.
   多年来，她在将大量数据综合成条理清晰的报告方面变得越来越熟练。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adept" as "a-dept" — someone so skilled they could run A whole DEPTartment by themselves. From Latin "adeptus" meaning one who has attained mastery.
把"adept"想成"a-dept"——一个人熟练到可以独自管理一整个DEPT（部门）。源自拉丁语"adeptus"，意为已经达到精通的人。
