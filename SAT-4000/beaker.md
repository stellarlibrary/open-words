# 🧪 beaker

🔊 /ˈbiːkər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A cylindrical glass container with a flat bottom and a pouring lip, used in laboratories for mixing and measuring liquids; a large drinking cup with a wide mouth
**中文：** n. 烧杯；大口杯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flask（烧瓶）
- vessel（容器）
- container（容器）
- goblet（高脚杯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glass beaker / laboratory beaker / fill a beaker / pour into a beaker / measuring beaker**

👉 Example: The chemistry student carefully poured the reagent into a clean beaker before beginning the experiment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher heated the chemical solution in a glass **beaker** over a Bunsen burner until it reached the desired temperature.
   研究人员在本生灯上加热玻璃烧杯中的化学溶液，直到达到所需的温度。

2. Each laboratory station was equipped with graduated **beakers**, pipettes, and safety goggles for the advanced chemistry practicum.
   每个实验台都配备了刻度烧杯、移液器和安全护目镜，用于高级化学实践课程。

3. The student accidentally knocked over the **beaker**, spilling the carefully prepared solution across the entire laboratory bench.
   学生不小心打翻了烧杯，精心配制的溶液洒满了整个实验台。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "beaker" as "beak + er" — the beaker has a beak-like spout for pouring liquids, just like a bird's beak.
把"beaker"想成"beak + er"——烧杯有一个像鸟喙一样的倒液嘴，方便倾倒液体。
