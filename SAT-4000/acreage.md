# 🌾 acreage

🔊 /ˈeɪ.kər.ɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An area of land measured in acres; the total number of acres of a piece of land.
**中文：** 面积，英亩数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- land area（土地面积）
- expanse（广阔区域）
- tract（大片土地）
- estate（地产）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **total/vast/large acreage**, **acreage of farmland/forest**, **under cultivation**

👉 Example: The ranch boasts an impressive acreage of fertile land stretching across the valley.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government allocated a significant **acreage** of protected forest to prevent further commercial development.
   政府划拨了大面积的保护林区，以防止进一步的商业开发。

2. Farmers in the region reported a dramatic increase in cultivated **acreage** over the past decade.
   该地区的农民报告说，过去十年间耕地面积大幅增加。

3. The total **acreage** devoted to organic farming has expanded considerably in recent years worldwide.
   近年来，全球用于有机农业的总面积已大幅扩展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Acre" + "-age" — just like "mileage" measures miles, "acreage" measures how many acres of land you have.
"Acre（英亩）"加后缀"-age"——就像"mileage（里程）"表示多少英里，"acreage"表示多少英亩的土地面积。
