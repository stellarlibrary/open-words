# 😏 gibe

🔊 /dʒaɪb/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make taunting or mocking remarks; to jeer or ridicule. (n.) A taunting or sarcastic remark
**中文：** v. 嘲笑；愚弄；讥讽 n. 嘲讽话；讥刺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- taunt（嘲弄）
- mock（嘲笑）
- jeer（讥笑）
- ridicule（嘲讽）
- scoff（嘲笑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gibe at someone / a cruel gibe / hurl gibes / exchange gibes / a sharp gibe**

👉 Example: The opposition never missed an opportunity to gibe at the prime minister's policy failures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Political commentators continued to **gibe** at the administration's contradictory statements regarding the economic recovery plan.
   政治评论员们继续嘲讽政府在经济复苏计划上自相矛盾的声明。

2. The senator's sharp **gibe** about her opponent's lack of experience drew both laughter and criticism from the audience.
   这位参议员对其对手缺乏经验的尖锐嘲讽在观众中引发了笑声和批评。

3. Students who **gibe** at their peers for academic difficulties often undermine the collaborative learning environment of the classroom.
   嘲笑同学学业困难的学生往往会破坏课堂的合作学习氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gibe" as "jab" with an "e" — a gibe is a verbal jab, a sharp poke of mockery aimed at someone.
把"gibe"想成带"e"的"jab"（戳）——gibe是一种言语上的戳刺，是对某人尖锐的嘲讽。
