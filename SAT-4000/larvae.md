# 🐛 larvae

🔊 /ˈlɑːrviː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The immature, wingless, and often wormlike forms of insects or other organisms before metamorphosis (plural of larva)
**中文：** n. 幼虫；幼体（larva的复数形式）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grubs（蛴螬）
- caterpillars（毛虫）
- maggots（蛆虫）
- nymphs（若虫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mosquito larvae / insect larvae / larvae develop into / larvae hatch from eggs**

👉 Example: The biologist carefully observed the larvae as they progressed through each developmental stage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Mosquito **larvae** develop in stagnant water, making drainage management essential for effective public health intervention.
   蚊子幼虫在死水中发育，因此排水管理对于有效的公共卫生干预至关重要。

2. The research team discovered that the **larvae** of this particular beetle species feed exclusively on decaying organic matter.
   研究团队发现这种特定甲虫的幼虫仅以腐烂的有机物为食。

3. Environmental scientists monitored aquatic **larvae** populations as a reliable indicator of overall water quality in the region.
   环境科学家监测水生幼虫种群数量，将其作为该地区整体水质的可靠指标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "larvae" as "larva + e" — the "e" at the end makes it plural. Picture baby insects wearing tiny "veils" (sounds like "larv-ae") before they reveal their adult forms.
把"larvae"想成"larva加e"——末尾的"e"表示复数。想象小虫子们戴着小面纱（发音类似"larv-ae"），在变成成虫前遮住自己。
