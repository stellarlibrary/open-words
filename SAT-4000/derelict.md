# 🏚️ derelict

🔊 /ˈdɛrəlɪkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Neglectful of duty; abandoned and in a state of disrepair. (n.) A person without a home or means of support; an abandoned property or vessel
**中文：** adj. 玩忽职守的；被抛弃的；破旧的 n. 遗弃物；无家可归者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- negligent（疏忽的）
- abandoned（被遗弃的）
- dilapidated（破旧的）
- forsaken（被抛弃的）
- remiss（玩忽职守的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **derelict in duty / derelict building / derelict vessel / derelict of responsibility**

👉 Example: The inspector declared the officer derelict in his duty for failing to respond to repeated emergency calls.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city council voted to demolish the **derelict** warehouse that had become a persistent safety hazard for the neighborhood.
   市议会投票决定拆除这座废弃的仓库，因为它已成为该社区持续存在的安全隐患。

2. The commanding officer was found **derelict** in his duties for neglecting critical safety protocols during the military exercise.
   这名指挥官因在军事演习中忽视关键安全规程而被认定为玩忽职守。

3. Salvage crews discovered a **derelict** vessel adrift in the northern Atlantic, abandoned by its crew under mysterious circumstances.
   打捞队在北大西洋发现了一艘漂泊的废弃船只，其船员在神秘情况下弃船离去。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dere-lict" as "dare to neglect" — a derelict person dares to neglect their duties, and a derelict building has been neglected completely.
把"derelict"想成"dare（敢）to neglect（忽视）"——玩忽职守的人敢于忽视职责，废弃的建筑则被完全忽视了。
