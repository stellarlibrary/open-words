# 💎 facet

🔊 /ˈfæsɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) One side or aspect of something; a flat surface cut on a gemstone or crystal
**中文：** n. 方面；（宝石的）刻面，小平面

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aspect（方面）
- dimension（维度）
- side（侧面）
- element（要素）
- feature（特征）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **facet of a problem / every facet / facet of life / facet of a diamond / multiple facets**

👉 Example: The documentary explored every facet of the environmental crisis, from policy failures to industrial pollution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sociologist examined multiple **facets** of urban poverty, including housing insecurity, educational inequity, and healthcare access.
   这位社会学家研究了城市贫困的多个方面，包括住房不安全、教育不平等和医疗可及性。

2. A skilled jeweler carefully polishes each **facet** of the diamond to maximize its brilliance and light refraction.
   技艺精湛的珠宝匠仔细打磨钻石的每一个刻面，以最大限度地提高其光彩和折射效果。

3. The report addressed every **facet** of the proposed legislation, ensuring that lawmakers could make fully informed decisions.
   该报告涉及拟议立法的每个方面，确保立法者能够做出充分知情的决定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "facet" as a tiny "face" — a diamond has many small faces (facets), just as a complex issue has many faces or sides to consider.
把"facet"想成一个小小的"face（面）"——钻石有许多小面（刻面），就像一个复杂的问题有许多面需要考虑。
