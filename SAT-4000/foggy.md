# 🌫️ foggy

🔊 /ˈfɒɡi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Filled with or obscured by fog; unclear, confused, or vague in perception or understanding
**中文：** adj. 有雾的；不清楚的；模糊的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- misty（雾蒙蒙的）
- hazy（朦胧的）
- unclear（不清楚的）
- vague（模糊的）
- murky（阴暗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **foggy weather / foggy memory / foggy idea / not the foggiest (idiom: no idea at all)**

👉 Example: He had only a foggy recollection of what happened during the final hours of the conference.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **foggy** conditions along the coastline forced authorities to delay all commercial flights departing from the regional airport.
   沿海地区**大雾**天气迫使当局推迟了从该地区机场起飞的所有商业航班。

2. After the long illness, the patient reported a **foggy** mental state that made concentrating on simple daily tasks extremely difficult.
   长期患病后，患者报告了一种**模糊**的精神状态，使得集中精力完成简单的日常任务变得极为困难。

3. The witness could provide only a **foggy** account of the events, making it challenging for investigators to reconstruct the timeline.
   目击者只能提供一份**模糊**的事件描述，使得调查人员很难重建事件的时间线。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "foggy" as walking through thick fog — you cannot see clearly, just as foggy thinking means your mind is clouded and unclear.
把"foggy"想成在浓雾中行走——你看不清楚，就像思维模糊意味着你的头脑被蒙蔽、不清晰。
