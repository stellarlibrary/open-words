# 💥 collision

🔊 /kəˈlɪʒ.ən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A violent impact between two or more objects; a conflict or clash of opposing ideas, interests, or factions.

**中文：** 碰撞；冲突。指两个或多个物体之间的猛烈撞击，也指对立思想、利益或派系之间的冲突。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- crash（碰撞）
- impact（撞击）
- clash（冲突）
- conflict（矛盾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** "collision between/with" — used to describe physical crashes or metaphorical conflicts.

**Example:** A collision between traditional values and modern ideals is inevitable in a rapidly changing society.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **collision** between the two freight trains caused significant damage to the railway infrastructure and delayed operations for hours.
   两列货运列车之间的**碰撞**对铁路基础设施造成了严重破坏，并导致运营延误数小时。

2. The diplomatic negotiations revealed a fundamental **collision** of interests between the two nations regarding territorial boundaries.
   外交谈判揭示了两国在领土边界问题上根本性的利益**冲突**。

3. Scientists observed a **collision** of subatomic particles that produced data essential for advancing quantum physics research.
   科学家们观察到亚原子粒子的**碰撞**，产生了对推进量子物理研究至关重要的数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** Think of "col-" (together) + "lision" (from Latin *laedere*, to strike) — things coming together and striking each other.

**中文：** 联想"col-"（一起）+"lision"（源自拉丁语 *laedere*，打击）——东西聚到一起互相撞击，即"碰撞"。
