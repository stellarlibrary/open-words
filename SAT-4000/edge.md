# 🔪 edge

🔊 /ɛdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The outside limit of an object or area; a sharp side of a blade; a competitive advantage. (v.) To sharpen; to move gradually or carefully
**中文：** n. 边缘；刀刃；棱；优势 v. 使锐利；缓慢移动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- border（边界）
- margin（边缘）
- advantage（优势）
- brink（边缘）
- rim（边沿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **competitive edge / on the edge of / cutting edge / edge closer to / edge over**

👉 Example: The company's innovative technology gave it a decisive edge over its competitors in the market.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate's extensive international experience gave her a clear **edge** over the other applicants for the diplomatic position.
   这位候选人丰富的国际经验使她在竞争该外交职位时明显优于其他申请者。

2. Negotiations **edged** closer to a resolution after both parties agreed to make significant concessions on key issues.
   在双方同意在关键问题上做出重大让步后，谈判逐渐接近解决。

3. The explorer stood at the **edge** of the vast canyon, contemplating the geological forces that had shaped it over millennia.
   探险家站在广阔峡谷的边缘，思考着数千年来塑造它的地质力量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a knife's edge — it is both the sharp border of the blade and what gives it an advantage in cutting, connecting the meanings of "boundary" and "advantage."
想想刀刃（edge）——它既是刀片的锋利边界，也是赋予它切割优势的关键，将"边缘"和"优势"两个含义联系在一起。
