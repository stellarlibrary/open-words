# 🦥 sloth

🔊 /slɔːθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Reluctance to work or make effort; habitual laziness or indolence; a slow-moving tropical mammal
**中文：** n. 怠惰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- laziness（懒惰）
- indolence（怠惰）
- lethargy（倦怠）
- idleness（无所事事）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intellectual sloth / moral sloth / sink into sloth / overcome sloth**

👉 Example: The professor warned that intellectual sloth would inevitably lead to poor academic performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manager attributed the department's declining productivity to widespread **sloth** among employees who lacked proper motivation.
   经理将部门生产力下降归咎于缺乏适当激励的员工中普遍存在的怠惰。

2. Medieval theologians classified **sloth** as one of the seven deadly sins because it represented a failure of spiritual duty.
   中世纪神学家将怠惰列为七宗罪之一，因为它代表了精神责任的缺失。

3. The nation's economic decline was partly caused by bureaucratic **sloth** that delayed critical infrastructure projects for decades.
   国家经济衰退部分是由官僚体制的怠惰造成的，这使关键基础设施项目拖延了数十年。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the animal "sloth" — it moves incredibly slowly, which perfectly captures the meaning of laziness and reluctance to act.
想想动物"树懒"——它移动得极其缓慢，完美地诠释了懒惰和不愿行动的含义。
