# 🖼️ pictograph

🔊 /ˈpɪktəɡræf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A pictorial symbol or sign representing a word or idea; a chart or diagram using pictures or symbols to convey statistical data
**中文：** n. 象形文字；古代石壁画；统计图表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hieroglyph（象形文字）
- ideogram（表意文字）
- symbol（符号）
- glyph（字形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pictograph chart / ancient pictograph / pictograph writing / read a pictograph**

👉 Example: The teacher used a pictograph to help students visualize data about population growth across continents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Archaeologists discovered a series of ancient **pictographs** carved into the cave walls that depicted hunting scenes and rituals.
   考古学家发现了一系列刻在洞穴墙壁上的古代象形文字，描绘了狩猎场景和仪式。

2. The textbook included a **pictograph** that illustrated the distribution of natural resources across the major regions of the world.
   教科书中包含一幅统计图表，展示了世界主要地区的自然资源分布情况。

3. Scholars believe that **pictographs** represent one of the earliest forms of written communication developed by human civilizations.
   学者们认为象形文字代表了人类文明发展出的最早的书面交流形式之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "picto-graph" as "picture" + "graph" — a pictograph is a picture that writes or graphs information visually.
把"pictograph"拆成"picto"（图画）+"graph"（书写）——象形文字就是用图画来书写或图示信息。
