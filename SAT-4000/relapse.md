# 🔻 relapse

🔊 /rɪˈlæps/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fall back into a former state, especially illness or bad habits; to deteriorate after a period of improvement. (n.) A recurrence of symptoms or undesirable behavior after a period of improvement.
**中文：** v. 故态复萌；旧病复发 n. 复发；再发

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regress（倒退）
- revert（回复）
- recurrence（复发）
- deterioration（恶化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **relapse into / suffer a relapse / relapse rate / prevent relapse**

👉 Example: The doctor warned the patient that failing to complete the treatment could result in a serious relapse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After six months of steady recovery, the patient unexpectedly **relapsed** and required immediate readmission to the intensive care unit.
   经过六个月的稳定恢复之后，患者意外复发，需要立即重新进入重症监护室。

2. Addiction specialists emphasize that a single **relapse** does not signify failure but rather a common obstacle in the recovery process.
   成瘾专家强调，一次复发并不意味着失败，而是康复过程中常见的一个障碍。

3. Without ongoing support and structured intervention, individuals struggling with substance abuse may **relapse** into destructive behavioral patterns.
   如果没有持续的支持和有计划的干预，与药物滥用作斗争的人可能会重新陷入破坏性的行为模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-lapse" as "lapse again" — a lapse is a slip or fall, so a relapse is falling back into the same problem once more.
把"relapse"想成"re（再次）+ lapse（失误、滑落）"——lapse是滑落，relapse就是再次滑落回同一个问题。
