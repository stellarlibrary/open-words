# 🚧 obstruct

🔊 /əbˈstrʌkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To block or impede passage, progress, or action; to get in the way of something
**中文：** v. 妨碍；阻塞；截断；堵塞；阻止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- block（阻挡）
- impede（妨碍）
- hinder（阻碍）
- barricade（设路障阻挡）
- thwart（阻挠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obstruct justice / obstruct traffic / obstruct the view / obstruct progress**

👉 Example: Fallen trees obstructed the highway, causing severe delays for commuters during rush hour.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator was charged with attempting to **obstruct** the congressional investigation into the financial scandal.
   这位参议员被指控试图妨碍国会对金融丑闻的调查。

2. A massive landslide **obstructed** the only road leading to the remote mountain village, cutting off vital supply routes.
   一次大规模山体滑坡阻塞了通往偏远山村的唯一道路，切断了重要的补给线路。

3. The new skyscraper would **obstruct** the panoramic ocean views that residents had enjoyed for more than two decades.
   这座新摩天大楼将遮挡居民们二十多年来一直享有的全景海景。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-STRUCT" as "ob (against) + struct (structure)" — to put a structure against something, blocking the way.
把"obstruct"想成"ob（对抗）+ struct（建筑）"——在某物前面放置建筑物，挡住了去路。
