# 🎨 portray

🔊 /pɔːrˈtreɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To depict or describe someone or something in a particular way; to play a role in a film, play, or other performance
**中文：** v. 描绘；扮演；画像

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- depict（描绘）
- represent（表现）
- illustrate（阐明）
- characterize（描述特征）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **portray someone as / portray a character / accurately portray / portray an image**

👉 Example: The documentary sought to portray the daily struggles of refugees with honesty and compassion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novelist chose to **portray** the protagonist as a deeply conflicted individual torn between duty and personal desire.
   这位小说家选择将主人公描绘成一个在责任和个人欲望之间深感矛盾的人。

2. Critics argued that the film failed to accurately **portray** the historical events surrounding the civil rights movement.
   评论家认为这部电影未能准确描绘民权运动的相关历史事件。

3. The actress was widely praised for her ability to **portray** vulnerability and strength simultaneously on the stage.
   这位女演员因其在舞台上同时表现脆弱与刚强的能力而广受赞誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "por-TRAY" — an artist uses a tray of paints to portray someone in a portrait painting.
把"portray"想成"por-TRAY"——一位艺术家用一盘（tray）颜料来描绘（portray）某人的肖像画。
