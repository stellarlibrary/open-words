# 🎭 humbug

🔊 /ˈhʌmbʌɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who deceives others; deceptive or false talk or behavior; a fraud. (v.) To deceive or trick someone
**中文：** n. 骗子；欺骗；虚伪 v. 欺骗；哄骗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fraud（骗子）
- impostor（冒充者）
- deception（欺骗）
- sham（虚假）
- charlatan（江湖骗子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Bah, humbug! / a complete humbug / humbug someone into doing something**

👉 Example: The journalist exposed the self-proclaimed healer as a humbug who preyed on vulnerable patients.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigative report revealed the celebrated psychic to be nothing more than a **humbug** exploiting grieving families.
   调查报告揭露了这位著名通灵师不过是一个利用悲伤家庭的骗子。

2. Critics dismissed the politician's promises as mere **humbug** designed to win votes without any genuine intent to reform.
   批评者认为该政治家的承诺纯属欺骗，旨在赢得选票而没有任何真正的改革意图。

3. The cunning salesman managed to **humbug** dozens of investors into funding a project that never existed.
   这个狡猾的推销员成功地欺骗了数十名投资者为一个根本不存在的项目提供资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humbug" as "hum + bug" — a bug that hums pleasantly to lure you in, but it is really deceiving you.
把"humbug"想成"hum + bug"——一只发出悦耳嗡嗡声来引诱你的虫子，但它其实在欺骗你。
