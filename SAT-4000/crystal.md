# 💎 crystal

🔊 /ˈkrɪstəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A solid material whose atoms are arranged in a highly ordered, repeating pattern; clear, transparent glass of high quality. (adj.) Made of or resembling crystal; perfectly clear
**中文：** n. 结晶，晶体；水晶 adj. 水晶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gem（宝石）
- quartz（石英）
- transparent（透明的）
- crystalline（结晶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crystal clear / crystal structure / crystal formation / liquid crystal / crystal ball**

👉 Example: The mineral formed a perfect crystal structure that fascinated the geology students in the laboratory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist examined the **crystal** lattice under an electron microscope to identify structural defects at the atomic level.
   物理学家在电子显微镜下检查晶体晶格，以在原子层面识别结构缺陷。

2. The mountain spring water was **crystal** clear, reflecting the surrounding peaks with remarkable precision and brilliance.
   山泉水清澈如水晶，以惊人的精确度和光彩映射出周围的山峰。

3. Researchers discovered that the rare **crystal** formations in the cave had developed over millions of years of geological activity.
   研究人员发现洞穴中珍稀的晶体结构是经过数百万年的地质活动形成的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crystal" as something so pure and clear you can see right through it — crystal clear is the ultimate standard of transparency.
把"crystal"想成如此纯净透明以至于可以直接看透的东西——"crystal clear"是透明度的最高标准。
