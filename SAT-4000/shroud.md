# 🌫️ shroud

🔊 /ʃraʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A burial garment; a covering that conceals or obscures; the ropes supporting a ship's mast. (v.) To cover, envelop, or conceal
**中文：** v. 遮蔽；隐藏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conceal（隐藏）
- cloak（遮掩）
- veil（遮蔽）
- envelop（笼罩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shrouded in mystery / shrouded in fog / shrouded in secrecy / shroud of darkness**

👉 Example: The ancient ruins were shrouded in mist, lending the site an atmosphere of mystery and solemnity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government's decision-making process was **shrouded** in secrecy, prompting widespread demands for greater transparency.
   政府的决策过程笼罩在秘密之中，引发了社会各界对提高透明度的广泛呼吁。

2. Dense fog **shrouded** the harbor throughout the morning, forcing all incoming vessels to reduce speed and proceed cautiously.
   浓雾整个上午笼罩着港口，迫使所有进港船只减速谨慎行驶。

3. The true origins of the manuscript remain **shrouded** in uncertainty despite decades of scholarly investigation and debate.
   尽管经过数十年的学术调查和争论，这份手稿的真正起源仍笼罩在不确定性之中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a "shroud" as a cloth draped over something to hide it — just as fog shrouds a landscape, making everything invisible.
把"shroud"想象成盖在某物上遮蔽它的布——就像雾笼罩大地，让一切都看不见。
