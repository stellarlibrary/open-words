# 🍞 subsistence

🔊 /səbˈsɪstəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of maintaining or supporting oneself at a minimal level; the means of surviving
**中文：** n. 生存；生活

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- survival（生存）
- livelihood（生计）
- sustenance（食物和生计）
- existence（存在）
- maintenance（维持）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subsistence farming / subsistence level / subsistence wages / bare subsistence**

👉 Example: Millions of people around the world still depend on subsistence farming for their daily food supply.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The report revealed that nearly half the population was living at a **subsistence** level with inadequate nutrition and shelter.
   报告显示，近一半的人口生活在仅能糊口的水平，营养和住所都不足。

2. **Subsistence** farming remains the primary economic activity in many remote regions untouched by industrialization.
   自给农业仍然是许多未受工业化影响的偏远地区的主要经济活动。

3. The humanitarian organization provided emergency aid to ensure the **subsistence** of displaced families during the conflict.
   人道主义组织提供了紧急援助，以确保冲突期间流离失所家庭的基本生存。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "subsistence" as the noun form of "subsist" — it refers to the bare minimum existence needed to stay alive.
把"subsistence"想成"subsist"（勉强生存）的名词形式——指维持生命所需的最低限度的生存状态。
