# 🔄 variable

🔊 /ˈvɛriəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Liable to change; inconsistent or fluctuating. (n.) A factor or quantity that can vary or be varied in value
**中文：** adj. 可变的；易变的；不稳定的 n. 变量；可变因素

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- changeable（可变的）
- fluctuating（波动的）
- inconsistent（不一致的）
- mutable（易变的）
- unpredictable（不可预测的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **variable rate / independent variable / dependent variable / variable weather / control the variables**

👉 Example: Researchers must identify and control every variable to ensure the validity of their experimental results.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **variable** nature of consumer demand makes it challenging for manufacturers to maintain consistent inventory levels throughout the fiscal year.
   消费者需求的易变性使制造商难以在整个财年保持一致的库存水平。

2. In a controlled experiment, the independent **variable** is deliberately manipulated while the dependent variable is carefully measured and recorded.
   在对照实验中，自变量被刻意操控，而因变量则被仔细测量和记录。

3. The region's highly **variable** climate patterns have compelled agricultural scientists to develop more resilient crop varieties for local farmers.
   该地区高度不稳定的气候模式迫使农业科学家为当地农民开发更具韧性的作物品种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "variable" as "vary + able" — something that is able to vary or change, like the weather or a number in an equation.
把"variable"想成"vary（变化）+ able（能够）"——能够变化的东西，就像天气或方程中的数字。
