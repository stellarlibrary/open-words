# ✈️ bombardier

🔊 /ˌbɑːmbərˈdɪr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A member of a bomber crew responsible for aiming and releasing bombs; also, a non-commissioned artillery officer in certain armies.
**中文：** n. 投弹手（轰炸机上负责瞄准和投放炸弹的机组人员）；炮兵下士

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bomber（轰炸手）
- artilleryman（炮兵）
- gunner（炮手）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the bombardier released / bombardier beetle / served as bombardier / trained as a bombardier**

👉 Example: The bombardier calculated the precise trajectory before releasing the payload over the target zone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bombardier** carefully adjusted the bombsight to ensure maximum accuracy before releasing the ordnance over the strategic target.
   投弹手在向战略目标投放弹药前仔细调整了瞄准器，以确保最大精度。

2. During the Second World War, every **bombardier** underwent rigorous training in navigation, mathematics, and precision targeting techniques.
   在第二次世界大战期间，每位投弹手都接受了导航、数学和精确瞄准技术的严格训练。

3. The young **bombardier** demonstrated exceptional composure under heavy anti-aircraft fire throughout the dangerous night mission.
   这位年轻的投弹手在整个危险的夜间任务中，面对猛烈的防空火力表现出了非凡的沉着。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bombardier" as "bombard + ier (one who)" — the person who does the bombarding. A bombardier is literally the one who bombards targets from above.
把"bombardier"想成"bombard（轰炸）+ ier（做...的人）"——执行轰炸的人。投弹手就是从空中轰炸目标的人。
