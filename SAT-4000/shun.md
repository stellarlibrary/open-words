# 🚫 shun

🔊 /ʃʌn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To deliberately avoid or keep away from; to reject or refuse to associate with
**中文：** v. 避开，避免

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- avoid（避免）
- evade（逃避）
- eschew（回避）
- ostracize（排斥）
- reject（拒绝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shun publicity / shun contact / shun society / shun responsibility**

👉 Example: The reclusive author chose to shun public appearances and communicate only through written correspondence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many communities historically chose to **shun** individuals who violated deeply held social norms or traditions.
   许多社区在历史上选择排斥那些违反根深蒂固的社会规范或传统的人。

2. The distinguished professor decided to **shun** commercial ventures and dedicate herself entirely to academic research.
   这位杰出的教授决定避开商业活动，全身心投入学术研究。

3. Environmental activists urged consumers to **shun** products manufactured by companies with poor sustainability records.
   环保活动人士敦促消费者回避那些可持续发展记录不佳的公司生产的产品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shun" as sounding like "shoo + n" — when you shoo someone away, you are shunning them, pushing them out of your presence.
把"shun"想成发音类似"shoo（嘘走）+ n"——当你把某人嘘走时，你就是在避开他们，把他们赶出你的视线。
