# 🥶 remorseless

🔊 /rɪˈmɔːrsləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Without remorse or pity; merciless and cruel; relentless and unstoppable
**中文：** adj. 冷酷的；无恻隐心的；不知过错的；坚持不懈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merciless（无情的）
- ruthless（残忍的）
- relentless（不留情的）
- pitiless（冷酷的）
- unsparing（毫不留情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remorseless pursuit / remorseless criticism / remorseless efficiency / remorseless in doing something**

👉 Example: The prosecutor described the defendant as a remorseless individual who showed no regard for the suffering of others.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dictator's **remorseless** campaign of repression silenced all political opposition and dismantled every independent civic institution.
   独裁者冷酷无情的镇压运动压制了所有政治反对派，并摧毁了每一个独立的公民机构。

2. The investigative journalist pursued the story with **remorseless** determination, uncovering layers of corporate fraud spanning two decades.
   这位调查记者以坚持不懈的决心追踪报道，揭开了长达二十年的企业欺诈层层内幕。

3. Critics condemned the **remorseless** exploitation of natural resources that left entire ecosystems devastated beyond any hope of recovery.
   批评者谴责了对自然资源毫无顾忌的开发，使整个生态系统遭到毁灭性破坏，毫无恢复希望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remorseless" as "remorse + less" — someone with less (no) remorse, completely lacking guilt or compassion.
把"remorseless"想成"remorse + less"——一个没有懊悔的人，完全缺乏愧疚或同情心。
