# 😩 irksome

🔊 /ˈɜːrksəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing annoyance or weariness; tedious and irritating
**中文：** adj. 令人厌烦的；讨厌的；令人厌恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- annoying（令人烦恼的）
- tedious（乏味的）
- vexatious（令人恼怒的）
- tiresome（令人厌倦的）
- wearisome（令人疲倦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irksome task / irksome habit / irksome delay / find something irksome**

👉 Example: The irksome bureaucratic procedures delayed the approval of the research grant by several months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **irksome** repetition of administrative paperwork discouraged many talented scholars from pursuing federal research funding.
   令人厌烦的行政文书工作的重复使许多有才华的学者不愿申请联邦研究资金。

2. Students found the **irksome** requirement of daily attendance logs to be an unnecessary burden on their academic routine.
   学生们觉得每日考勤记录这一令人厌烦的要求是对他们学习生活的不必要负担。

3. Despite the **irksome** constraints of the outdated software, the engineering team managed to complete the project on schedule.
   尽管过时软件的限制令人厌烦，工程团队仍设法按时完成了项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irksome" as "irk + some" — something that delivers some irk, meaning it hands you a serving of annoyance.
把"irksome"想成"irk + some"——某种给你带来一些烦恼的东西，意味着它递给你一份恼怒。
