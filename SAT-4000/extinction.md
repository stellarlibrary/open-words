# 🌋 extinction

🔊 /ɪkˈstɪŋkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state or process of a species or group ceasing to exist; the act of extinguishing or being extinguished
**中文：** n. 灭绝；消失；消灭；废止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- annihilation（毁灭）
- eradication（根除）
- obliteration（消灭）
- disappearance（消失）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mass extinction / face extinction / on the brink of extinction / drive to extinction**

👉 Example: Many conservationists believe that preventing species extinction should be a global priority.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rapid rate of habitat destruction is driving countless species toward **extinction** at an unprecedented pace.
   栖息地的快速破坏正以前所未有的速度将无数物种推向灭绝。

2. Paleontologists have identified five major mass **extinction** events throughout Earth's geological history spanning millions of years.
   古生物学家已经确定了地球数百万年地质历史中的五次主要大规模灭绝事件。

3. Without immediate intervention, the critically endangered rhinoceros faces **extinction** within the next generation of wildlife.
   如果不立即采取干预措施，极度濒危的犀牛将在下一代野生动物中面临灭绝。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extinction" as "extinguish + tion" — like extinguishing a candle, an entire species' flame goes out permanently.
把"extinction"想成"extinguish + tion"——就像熄灭蜡烛一样，整个物种的火焰永久地熄灭了。
