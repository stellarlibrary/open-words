# 🦋 transfigure

🔊 /trænsˈfɪɡjər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To transform into something more beautiful or elevated; to change the outward form or appearance of something dramatically
**中文：** 使变形；美化；使改观

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transform（使变形）
- metamorphose（使变态）
- convert（转变）
- glorify（美化）
- exalt（使升华）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transfigure completely / transfigure into / transfigured by light / transfigured appearance**

👉 Example: The setting sun transfigured the ordinary landscape into a scene of breathtaking golden splendor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The restoration project managed to **transfigure** the dilapidated mansion into an elegant museum that attracted visitors from around the world.
   修复项目成功地将这座破旧的大宅改造成一座优雅的博物馆，吸引了来自世界各地的游客。

2. In religious tradition, the event known as the Transfiguration describes how divine light **transfigured** the appearance of the prophet.
   在宗教传统中，被称为"显圣容"的事件描述了神圣光芒如何使先知的外貌发生了神奇的改变。

3. The playwright's brilliant adaptation **transfigured** a simple folk tale into a profound meditation on mortality and human resilience.
   剧作家出色的改编将一个简单的民间故事升华为对死亡和人类韧性的深刻沉思。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-figure" as "changing the figure" — to transfigure is to change someone's figure or form into something more beautiful or radiant.
把"transfigure"想成"trans（改变）+ figure（形象）"——使某人的形象或外观变得更加美丽或光辉。
