# 📡 wavelength

🔊 /ˈweɪvlɛŋθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The distance between successive peaks of a wave, especially in electromagnetic radiation or sound; figuratively, a way of thinking or communicating
**中文：** n. 波长；（比喻）思维方式

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frequency（频率）
- spectrum（光谱）
- bandwidth（带宽）
- wave period（波周期）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on the same wavelength / wavelength of light / short wavelength / long wavelength**

👉 Example: The two researchers were on the same wavelength regarding the direction of the project.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The visible spectrum comprises light with a **wavelength** ranging approximately from four hundred to seven hundred nanometers.
   可见光谱包含波长大约从四百到七百纳米的光。

2. Different colors of light correspond to different **wavelengths**, with red possessing the longest and violet the shortest in the visible range.
   不同颜色的光对应不同的波长，红色在可见光范围内波长最长，紫色最短。

3. The communications satellite was designed to transmit signals at a specific **wavelength** optimized for penetrating atmospheric interference.
   这颗通信卫星被设计为在特定波长上传输信号，以优化穿透大气干扰的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wave" + "length" — it is literally the length of one complete wave cycle, from one crest to the next.
把"wavelength"想成"wave"加"length"——它字面意思就是一个完整波周期的长度，从一个波峰到下一个。
