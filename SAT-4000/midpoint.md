# 📍 midpoint

🔊 /ˈmɪdpɔɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The exact middle point of something; the point equidistant from two ends, extremes, or boundaries.
**中文：** n. 中点；正中央

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- center（中心）
- middle（中间）
- halfway point（中途点）
- median（中位数）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach the midpoint / at the midpoint / midpoint of the semester / midpoint between two extremes**

👉 Example: The hikers reached the midpoint of the trail and decided to rest before continuing their ascent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor announced that students had reached the **midpoint** of the semester and should begin preparing for final examinations.
   教授宣布学生们已到达学期的中点，应该开始准备期末考试。

2. Negotiators struggled to find a **midpoint** between the two opposing positions that would satisfy both parties involved.
   谈判者努力在两个对立立场之间找到一个能让双方满意的中间点。

3. In geometry, the **midpoint** of a line segment is calculated by averaging the coordinates of its two endpoints.
   在几何学中，线段的中点是通过对其两个端点的坐标取平均值来计算的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mid-point" — literally the "point in the middle." If you split any line or journey in half, the midpoint is exactly where the two halves meet.
把"midpoint"想成"mid"（中间的）+"point"（点）——字面意思就是"中间的点"。如果你把任何线或旅程一分为二，中点就是两半相遇的地方。
