# 🧘 rapt

🔊 /ræpt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Completely absorbed or fascinated; deeply engrossed in something
**中文：** adj. 全神贯注的；入迷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engrossed（全神贯注的）
- absorbed（专注的）
- captivated（着迷的）
- enthralled（入迷的）
- spellbound（被迷住的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rapt attention / rapt audience / rapt fascination / rapt silence**

👉 Example: The audience listened in rapt silence as the pianist performed the final movement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students sat in **rapt** attention as the renowned physicist described the implications of her groundbreaking discovery.
   学生们全神贯注地坐着，听那位著名物理学家描述她开创性发现的意义。

2. She gazed with **rapt** fascination at the ancient manuscript, tracing its faded calligraphy with careful reverence.
   她入迷地凝视着那份古老的手稿，怀着敬畏之心描摹其褪色的书法。

3. The lecturer held the entire auditorium in **rapt** concentration for two hours through compelling evidence and vivid storytelling.
   演讲者通过有力的证据和生动的叙述使整个礼堂的听众全神贯注了两个小时。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rapt" as "wrapped" — when you are rapt, your mind is completely wrapped around one thing with total focus.
把"rapt"想成"wrapped"（包裹）——当你全神贯注时，你的思想完全被一件事所包裹。
