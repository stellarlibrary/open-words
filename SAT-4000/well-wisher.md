# 🤝 well-wisher

🔊 /ˈwɛlˌwɪʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who desires happiness or success for another, or who expresses such a desire
**中文：** n. 祝福者；祝愿他人幸福的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supporter（支持者）
- sympathizer（同情者）
- admirer（仰慕者）
- advocate（拥护者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **well-wishers gathered / a crowd of well-wishers / well-wishers sent messages / well-wishers lined the streets**

👉 Example: Hundreds of well-wishers gathered outside the hospital to show their support for the ailing leader.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Dozens of **well-wishers** assembled at the airport to greet the delegation returning from the successful international peace negotiations.
   数十名祝福者聚集在机场，迎接从成功的国际和平谈判中归来的代表团。

2. The retiring professor received heartfelt letters from **well-wishers** across the globe who had benefited from her groundbreaking research.
   这位即将退休的教授收到了来自世界各地祝福者的真挚信件，他们都曾受益于她的开创性研究。

3. A large crowd of **well-wishers** lined the ceremonial route, expressing their gratitude for the ambassador's years of dedicated service.
   一大群祝福者沿着仪式路线排列，表达对这位大使多年尽职服务的感激之情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "well-wisher" literally — someone who "wishes you well," hoping for your happiness and success.
从字面上理解"well-wisher"——一个"祝你好"的人，希望你幸福和成功。
