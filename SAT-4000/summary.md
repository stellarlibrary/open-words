# 📋 summary

🔊 /ˈsʌməri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done or performed quickly and without formality; concise. (n.) A brief statement of the main points; an abstract or synopsis
**中文：** n. 摘要，概要

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- synopsis（概要）
- abstract（摘要）
- brief（简报）
- overview（概述）
- condensation（精简）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **summary of / in summary / summary report / summary judgment / executive summary**

👉 Example: The professor asked each student to write a brief summary of the assigned reading.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee published a comprehensive **summary** of its findings to inform the public about the investigation's conclusions.
   委员会发布了一份调查结果的综合概要，以告知公众调查的结论。

2. The judge issued a **summary** dismissal of the case after determining that the evidence was wholly insufficient.
   法官在确定证据完全不足后，对该案作出了简易驳回裁定。

3. Each chapter concludes with a concise **summary** that highlights the essential arguments and supporting evidence presented.
   每一章都以简明的摘要结尾，概述了所提出的核心论点和支持证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "summary" as "sum-mary" — Mary sums up everything into a short version, giving you just the main points.
把"summary"想成"sum-mary"——Mary把所有内容总结成简短版本，只给你要点。
