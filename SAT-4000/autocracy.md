# 👑 autocracy

🔊 /ɔːˈtɒkrəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A system of government in which one person has absolute power and authority over others.
**中文：** n. 独裁政治；专制政治

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- despotism（专制主义）
- tyranny（暴政）
- dictatorship（独裁统治）
- absolutism（绝对主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **descend into autocracy / rule by autocracy / oppose autocracy**

👉 Example: The nation gradually descended into autocracy after the leader dissolved the parliament.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that **autocracy** ultimately leads to corruption because unchecked power erodes moral judgment.
   这位哲学家认为，独裁政治最终会导致腐败，因为不受约束的权力会侵蚀道德判断。

2. Citizens organized peaceful protests to resist the growing **autocracy** that threatened their fundamental democratic rights.
   公民们组织和平抗议，以抵制日益增长的威胁其基本民主权利的专制政治。

3. Historians often study how republics transform into **autocracy** when institutions fail to protect individual freedoms.
   历史学家经常研究当制度未能保护个人自由时，共和国如何转变为独裁政治。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Auto" means self, "cracy" means rule — autocracy is government by one self, one person ruling alone.
"Auto"意为自己，"cracy"意为统治——autocracy就是一个人独自统治的政府形式。
