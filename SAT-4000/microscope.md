# 🔬 microscope

🔊 /ˈmaɪkrəskoʊp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An optical instrument used for viewing very small objects, such as cells or microorganisms, by producing a magnified image of them.
**中文：** n. 显微镜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- magnifier（放大镜）
- electron microscope（电子显微镜）
- optical instrument（光学仪器）
- magnifying device（放大设备）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **under a microscope / electron microscope / examine under a microscope / put something under the microscope**

👉 Example: The biologist examined the tissue sample under a microscope to identify any abnormal cellular structures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The invention of the **microscope** in the seventeenth century opened an entirely new world of scientific discovery and exploration.
   十七世纪显微镜的发明开启了一个全新的科学发现和探索世界。

2. Using a powerful electron **microscope**, researchers were able to observe the intricate structure of individual protein molecules.
   利用强大的电子显微镜，研究人员能够观察单个蛋白质分子的复杂结构。

3. The forensic team placed the fiber evidence under a **microscope** to determine its origin and link it to the suspect.
   法医团队将纤维证据放在显微镜下，以确定其来源并将其与嫌疑人联系起来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "micro-scope" — "micro" (small) + "scope" (to look). A microscope is literally an instrument for "looking at small things."
把"microscope"想成"micro"（微小的）+"scope"（观察）——显微镜字面意思就是"观察微小事物"的仪器。
