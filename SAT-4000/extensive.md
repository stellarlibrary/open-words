# 🌐 extensive

🔊 /ɪkˈstɛnsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Covering a large area; having great range or scope; thorough and far-reaching in effect or influence
**中文：** adj. 广泛的；大量的；广阔的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comprehensive（全面的）
- broad（广泛的）
- vast（广阔的）
- wide-ranging（范围广的）
- thorough（彻底的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extensive research / extensive damage / extensive experience / extensive knowledge / extensive use**

👉 Example: The team conducted extensive research before proposing their new environmental conservation strategy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hurricane caused **extensive** damage to the coastal infrastructure, leaving thousands of residents without shelter or electricity.
   飓风对沿海基础设施造成了大面积破坏，导致数千居民失去住所和电力。

2. After **extensive** deliberation, the committee concluded that the proposed legislation required significant amendments before ratification.
   经过广泛审议后，委员会得出结论，该拟议立法在批准前需要进行重大修改。

3. The scholar's **extensive** bibliography demonstrates a lifelong dedication to the study of Renaissance art and architecture.
   这位学者广泛的参考文献目录体现了其毕生致力于文艺复兴艺术和建筑研究的精神。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-tens-ive" as "extending outward in ten directions" — something that stretches out broadly and covers a great range.
把"extensive"想成"extend(延伸)+sive"——向外不断延伸的，覆盖范围广大的，即广泛的、广阔的。
