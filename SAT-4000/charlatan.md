# 🎭 charlatan

🔊 /ˈʃɑːrlətən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who falsely claims to have special knowledge or skill; a fraud or impostor
**中文：** n. 吹牛者；江湖医生；庸医；骗子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impostor（冒充者）
- fraud（骗子）
- quack（庸医）
- swindler（诈骗犯）
- pretender（伪装者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **expose a charlatan / unmask a charlatan / mere charlatan / charlatan who claims**

👉 Example: The medical board exposed him as a charlatan who had fabricated his credentials.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigative journalist exposed the self-proclaimed healer as a **charlatan** who had deceived thousands of desperate patients.
   调查记者揭露了这位自称治疗师的人是一个欺骗了数千名绝望患者的江湖骗子。

2. Many investors lost their savings after trusting the financial **charlatan** who promised extraordinary returns with no risk.
   许多投资者在信任了那个承诺零风险高回报的金融骗子后损失了积蓄。

3. The academic community quickly identified the researcher as a **charlatan** when his experimental data proved entirely fabricated.
   当其实验数据被证明完全是捏造的时，学术界迅速认定该研究人员是一个骗子。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "charlatan" as "char-la-tan" — a person with a charming tan exterior but nothing genuine underneath the surface.
把"charlatan"想成一个外表光鲜（charming tan）但内在空洞的人——华而不实的骗子。
