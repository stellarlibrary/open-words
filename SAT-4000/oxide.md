# 🧪 oxide

🔊 /ˈɑːksaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A chemical compound that contains at least one oxygen atom bonded to another element
**中文：** n. 氧化物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oxidized compound（氧化化合物）
- rust（铁锈，特指氧化铁）
- oxidation product（氧化产物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **iron oxide / carbon oxide / metal oxide / oxide layer / oxide coating**

👉 Example: Iron oxide, commonly known as rust, forms when iron is exposed to moisture and oxygen over time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The red coloration of Martian soil is primarily due to iron **oxide** deposits that cover much of the planet's surface.
   火星土壤的红色主要归因于覆盖行星表面大部分区域的氧化铁沉积物。

2. Engineers developed a thin aluminum **oxide** coating to protect the metal components from corrosion in harsh environmental conditions.
   工程师开发了一层薄的氧化铝涂层，以保护金属部件在恶劣环境条件下免受腐蚀。

3. Carbon **oxide** emissions from industrial facilities remain a major contributor to atmospheric pollution and global warming trends.
   工业设施排放的碳氧化物仍然是大气污染和全球变暖趋势的主要因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ox-ide" as "oxygen inside" — an oxide always has oxygen inside the compound bonded to another element.
把"oxide"想成"ox（氧）+ide（在里面）"——氧化物的化合物里面总是含有氧元素。
