# 😊 blithe

🔊 /blaɪð/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Happy, carefree, and lighthearted; showing a casual indifference or lack of proper concern.
**中文：** adj. 愉快的，高兴的；快乐无忧的；漫不经心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cheerful（愉快的）
- carefree（无忧无虑的）
- jovial（快乐的）
- lighthearted（轻松的）
- nonchalant（漫不经心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blithe spirit / blithe disregard / blithe indifference / blithe optimism**

👉 Example: His blithe disregard for the safety regulations alarmed every member of the inspection team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young poet maintained a **blithe** spirit despite the numerous rejections she received from prestigious literary journals.
   这位年轻诗人尽管收到许多著名文学期刊的拒稿，仍保持着**愉快的**心态。

2. Critics condemned the administration's **blithe** indifference to the escalating environmental crisis threatening coastal communities nationwide.
   批评者谴责政府对威胁全国沿海社区的不断升级的环境危机**漫不经心的**态度。

3. She walked through the garden with a **blithe** contentment that suggested she was entirely unburdened by worldly concerns.
   她带着一种**无忧无虑的**满足感漫步花园，仿佛完全没有世俗烦恼的负担。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "blithe" as "bliss + lithe" — a blissful and flexible spirit, moving through life with lightness and joy.
把"blithe"想成"bliss + lithe"——一种幸福而灵活的精神，轻盈愉悦地度过人生。
