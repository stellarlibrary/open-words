# 🪨 bauxite

🔊 /ˈbɔːksaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A reddish-brown ore that is the principal source of aluminum, composed mainly of hydrated aluminum oxides
**中文：** n. 矾土，铁铝氧石；铝土矿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aluminum ore（铝矿石）
- laterite（红土）
- mineral deposit（矿藏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bauxite mining / bauxite deposits / bauxite ore / rich in bauxite / bauxite reserves**

👉 Example: Australia and Guinea possess some of the world's largest bauxite reserves used for aluminum production.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologists discovered substantial **bauxite** deposits beneath the tropical forest, prompting a debate over environmental conservation.
   地质学家在热带森林下发现了大量铝土矿矿藏，引发了关于环境保护的争论。

2. Global aluminum production depends primarily on the extraction and refining of **bauxite** through an energy-intensive industrial process.
   全球铝产量主要依赖于通过高能耗工业过程对铝土矿的开采和提炼。

3. The government imposed strict regulations on **bauxite** mining operations to prevent further degradation of the surrounding ecosystem.
   政府对铝土矿开采作业实施了严格的规定，以防止周围生态系统进一步退化。

Think of "bauxite" as "box + ite" — imagine a box of rocks with "-ite" (a mineral suffix), reminding you it is an ore for making aluminum.
把"bauxite"想成"box + ite"——想象一箱岩石加上"-ite"（矿物后缀），提醒你这是一种用来制造铝的矿石。
