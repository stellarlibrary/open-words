# 🚀 missile

🔊 /ˈmɪsəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A weapon that is propelled toward a target; any object thrown or projected as a weapon
**中文：** n. 导弹；投射物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- projectile（抛射物）
- rocket（火箭）
- torpedo（鱼雷）
- munition（军火）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **launch a missile / ballistic missile / guided missile / missile defense / missile strike**

👉 Example: The military successfully tested a new guided missile capable of striking targets over a thousand miles away.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense ministry announced the deployment of advanced **missile** systems along the northern border to deter foreign aggression.
   国防部宣布在北部边境部署先进的导弹系统以威慑外国侵略。

2. International negotiations aimed to limit the proliferation of nuclear **missiles** and reduce the threat of catastrophic global conflict.
   国际谈判旨在限制核导弹的扩散并减少灾难性全球冲突的威胁。

3. Ancient warriors used stones and spears as **missiles**, hurling them at enemy fortifications during prolonged siege operations.
   古代战士使用石头和长矛作为投射物，在长期围攻行动中将其投向敌方防御工事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "missile" as coming from Latin "mittere" (to send) — a missile is something "sent" flying toward a target.
把"missile"与拉丁语"mittere（发送）"联系起来——导弹就是被"发射"飞向目标的东西。
