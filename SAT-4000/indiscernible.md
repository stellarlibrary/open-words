# 🔍 indiscernible

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Impossible or very difficult to discern, perceive, or distinguish; barely detectable
**中文：** adj. 难识别的；看不见的；难以察觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imperceptible（察觉不到的）
- undetectable（无法检测的）
- invisible（看不见的）
- indistinguishable（难以分辨的）
- inconspicuous（不显眼的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indiscernible to / indiscernible from / almost indiscernible / virtually indiscernible**

👉 Example: The difference between the original painting and the forgery was indiscernible to the untrained eye.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The subtle variations in the two chemical compounds were **indiscernible** without the use of advanced spectroscopic analysis equipment.
   这两种化合物之间的细微差异不借助先进的光谱分析设备是无法辨别的。

2. Over centuries of erosion, the ancient inscriptions on the temple walls had become virtually **indiscernible** to archaeologists.
   经过数百年的侵蚀，寺庙墙壁上的古代铭文对考古学家来说几乎已无法辨认。

3. The spacecraft's signal grew increasingly **indiscernible** as it traveled beyond the outer boundaries of the solar system.
   随着航天器飞越太阳系外部边界，其信号变得越来越难以识别。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-discern-ible" — "not able to discern" — if you cannot discern something, it is indiscernible and hidden from view.
把"indiscernible"想成"in-discern-ible"——"无法辨别的"——如果你无法辨别某物，它就是看不见的、隐藏的。
