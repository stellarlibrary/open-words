# ⚔️ confrontation

🔊 /ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A hostile or argumentative meeting or encounter between opposing parties; a direct clash or conflict
**中文：** n. 对抗，对峙；冲突，交锋

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clash（冲突）
- conflict（对抗）
- showdown（摊牌）
- standoff（僵持）
- dispute（争端）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **direct confrontation / avoid confrontation / military confrontation / confrontation between / confrontation with**

👉 Example: Diplomatic efforts are aimed at preventing a military confrontation between the two nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tense **confrontation** between the labor union and management lasted several weeks before a compromise was finally reached.
   工会与管理层之间紧张的对抗持续了数周，才最终达成妥协。

2. Many scholars argue that the Cold War was defined by ideological **confrontation** rather than direct military engagement.
   许多学者认为冷战的特征是意识形态对抗，而非直接的军事冲突。

3. The public **confrontation** between the senator and the journalist drew widespread media attention and sparked national debate.
   参议员与记者之间的公开对峙引起了广泛的媒体关注并引发了全国性辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "confront-ATION" — the act (ACTION) of confronting someone face to face, like two opponents meeting head-on.
把"confrontation"想成"confront-ATION"——面对面对抗的行为（ACTION），就像两个对手正面交锋。
