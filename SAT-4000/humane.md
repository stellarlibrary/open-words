# 💛 humane

🔊 /hjuːˈmeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing compassion, benevolence, and sympathy toward others, especially toward those who are suffering; marked by kindness and mercy
**中文：** adj. 仁慈的；人道的；有人性的；有同情心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compassionate（富有同情心的）
- merciful（仁慈的）
- benevolent（慈善的）
- kind-hearted（善良的）
- sympathetic（体恤的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **humane treatment / humane society / humane conditions / humane approach / humane slaughter**

👉 Example: The organization campaigns for the humane treatment of animals in agricultural settings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. International law requires that prisoners of war receive **humane** treatment regardless of the nature of the conflict.
   国际法要求战俘无论冲突性质如何都必须得到人道待遇。

2. The shelter adopted a **humane** policy of caring for stray animals rather than resorting to euthanasia as a first measure.
   该收容所采取了人道政策，照顾流浪动物，而非将安乐死作为首要手段。

3. Advocates argue that a more **humane** criminal justice system would focus on rehabilitation rather than purely punitive measures.
   倡导者认为，更加人道的刑事司法制度应注重改造而非纯粹的惩罚措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humane" as "human" + "e" — to be humane is to act in the best way a human can, with compassion and kindness.
把"humane"想成"human"（人类）+"e"——人道就是以人类最好的方式行事，充满同情和善意。
