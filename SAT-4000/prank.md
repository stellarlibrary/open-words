# 😜 prank

🔊 /præŋk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A trick or practical joke intended to amuse or embarrass someone. (v.) To play a trick on; to dress up or adorn
**中文：** n. 恶作剧；戏谑；开玩笑 v. 装饰；打扮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trick（恶作剧）
- jest（玩笑）
- hoax（骗局）
- antic（滑稽动作）
- caper（恶作剧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **play a prank on / pull a prank / harmless prank / childish prank**

👉 Example: The students decided to play an elaborate prank on their unsuspecting teacher during the last day of school.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mischievous student decided to play a **prank** on his classmates by hiding all the classroom chalk before the lecture.
   这个调皮的学生决定在课前把所有粉笔藏起来，对同学们搞恶作剧。

2. What began as a harmless **prank** quickly escalated into a serious disciplinary matter investigated by the school administration.
   原本无害的恶作剧迅速升级为一件由学校管理层调查的严重纪律事件。

3. The editorial condemned the dangerous **prank** as irresponsible, arguing that such reckless behavior could endanger innocent bystanders.
   社论谴责这一危险的恶作剧是不负责任的，认为这种鲁莽行为可能危及无辜的旁观者。

把"prank"和"rank（臭的）"联系起来——一个糟糕的恶作剧会让你在同伴中名声变臭。
