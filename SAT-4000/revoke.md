# ✋ revoke

🔊 /rɪˈvoʊk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To put an end to the validity or operation of a decree, decision, or promise; to officially cancel. (n.) In card games, a failure to follow suit when able
**中文：** v. 撤回；废除，宣告无效

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rescind（废除）
- annul（宣告无效）
- repeal（撤销）
- nullify（使无效）
- countermand（撤回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **revoke a license / revoke permission / revoke a decision / revoke access / revoke citizenship**

👉 Example: The board voted to revoke the company's operating license due to repeated safety violations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university administration decided to **revoke** the honorary degree after the recipient was convicted of financial fraud.
   大学管理层在获授者被判犯有金融欺诈罪后决定撤回荣誉学位。

2. Several nations threatened to **revoke** diplomatic recognition if the regime continued to violate fundamental human rights agreements.
   如果该政权继续违反基本人权协议，几个国家威胁要撤回外交承认。

3. The regulatory agency has the authority to **revoke** any permit that was obtained through misrepresentation or fraudulent documentation.
   监管机构有权撤销任何通过虚假陈述或欺诈性文件获得的许可证。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-" (back) + "voke" (call, as in "invoke") — to call back a decision, to take it away officially.
把"revoke"想成"re-（回）+ voke（叫，如invoke中的词根）"——把决定召回来，正式收回。
