# 🏛️ regime

🔊 /reɪˈʒiːm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A government, especially an authoritarian one; a system or planned way of doing things; an ordered way of managing affairs.
**中文：** n. 政权；政体；社会制度；管理体制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- government（政府）
- administration（行政当局）
- authority（当局）
- system（制度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **authoritarian regime / political regime / regime change / under the regime**

👉 Example: The oppressive regime restricted freedom of speech and suppressed all forms of political dissent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The totalitarian **regime** systematically suppressed civil liberties and imprisoned journalists who dared to criticize the government.
   这个极权政权系统性地压制公民自由，并监禁了那些敢于批评政府的记者。

2. After decades of authoritarian rule, the people finally succeeded in overthrowing the corrupt **regime** through peaceful protest.
   经过数十年的威权统治，人民终于通过和平抗议成功推翻了腐败的政权。

3. The new environmental **regime** established strict regulations on industrial emissions to combat the effects of climate change.
   新的环境管理体制对工业排放制定了严格的法规，以应对气候变化的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "regime" as sharing roots with "regal" and "reign" — it refers to those who reign or rule, a system of governance.
把"regime"想成与"regal"（王室的）和"reign"（统治）同源——指统治者或治理体系。
