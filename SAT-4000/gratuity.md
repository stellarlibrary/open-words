# 💰 gratuity

🔊 /ɡrəˈtuːɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A sum of money given voluntarily as a reward for services rendered; a tip or gift
**中文：** n. 赏钱，小费；赠物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tip（小费）
- bonus（奖金）
- reward（报酬）
- gift（赠物）
- donation（捐赠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **leave a gratuity / generous gratuity / gratuity included / gratuity for service**

👉 Example: The restaurant automatically added an eighteen percent gratuity to parties of six or more.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hotel policy required that a fifteen percent **gratuity** be added automatically to all room service orders placed by guests.
   酒店政策要求对客人的所有客房服务订单自动加收百分之十五的小费。

2. Upon his retirement from the military, the officer received a substantial **gratuity** in recognition of his distinguished service.
   这位军官退役时获得了一笔丰厚的赏金，以表彰他的卓越服务。

3. The debate over whether to include a mandatory **gratuity** on restaurant bills has intensified among industry professionals nationwide.
   关于是否在餐厅账单中包含强制性小费的争论在全国业内人士中愈演愈烈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gratuity" as coming from "gratis" (free) — a gratuity is money given freely and voluntarily, beyond what is required.
把"gratuity"和"gratis（免费的）"联系起来——gratuity是自愿额外给出的钱，即小费或赏金。
