# 😤 disagreeable

🔊 /ˌdɪsəˈɡriːəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unpleasant or causing discomfort; bad-tempered and difficult to get along with
**中文：** adj. 不愉快的；脾气坏的；令人不快的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unpleasant（令人不快的）
- offensive（讨厌的）
- irritable（易怒的）
- obnoxious（令人反感的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disagreeable odor / disagreeable person / disagreeable task / disagreeable weather**

👉 Example: The disagreeable smell from the chemical plant prompted residents to file formal complaints.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee found the proposal's terms highly **disagreeable** and voted unanimously to reject it without further deliberation.
   委员会认为该提案的条款极为令人不快，一致投票否决，不再进一步讨论。

2. His **disagreeable** temperament alienated most of his colleagues, leaving him isolated within the department.
   他脾气坏的性格疏远了大多数同事，使他在部门内被孤立。

3. The researchers were assigned the **disagreeable** task of cataloging thousands of contaminated specimens in a poorly ventilated laboratory.
   研究人员被分配了在通风不良的实验室中编目数千件受污染标本这一令人不快的任务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-agree-able" — someone or something you cannot "agree" with because it is unpleasant or difficult.
把"disagreeable"想成"不(dis)+同意(agree)+能够(able)"——让人无法认同的、令人不快的事物或脾气很差的人。
