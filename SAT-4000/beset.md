# 😰 beset

🔊 /bɪˈsɛt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To trouble or afflict persistently; to surround or hem in on all sides
**中文：** v. 困扰；镶嵌；围绕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plague（困扰）
- afflict（折磨）
- harass（骚扰）
- besiege（围困）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beset by / beset with / beset on all sides**

👉 Example: The nation was beset by economic turmoil and widespread political unrest throughout the decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious project was **beset** by unforeseen technical difficulties that delayed its completion by several months.
   这个雄心勃勃的项目被意想不到的技术困难所困扰，完工时间延迟了数月。

2. Scholars of that era were **beset** with contradictions between traditional doctrines and emerging scientific discoveries.
   那个时代的学者们被传统教义与新兴科学发现之间的矛盾所困扰。

3. The expedition was **beset** on all sides by hostile terrain, severe weather, and dwindling provisions.
   探险队四面受困于恶劣地形、极端天气和日益减少的给养。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-SET" — problems are SET all around you, trapping you. To be beset means to be surrounded by troubles.
想想"be-SET"——问题被SET（放置）在你周围，困住你。Beset就是被麻烦包围。
