# ⚠️ perilous

🔊 /ˈpɛrɪləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Full of danger or risk; extremely hazardous or threatening to safety and well-being
**中文：** adj. 危险的；冒险的；艰险的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hazardous（危险的）
- treacherous（险恶的）
- precarious（不稳定的）
- dangerous（危险的）
- risky（冒险的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perilous journey / perilous situation / perilous times / perilous path / perilous conditions**

👉 Example: The mountaineers faced a perilous descent through the icy ravine during the violent storm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugees undertook a **perilous** voyage across the open sea in overcrowded boats with limited supplies and no navigation equipment.
   难民们乘坐拥挤的小船，在物资有限且没有导航设备的情况下进行了危险的跨海航行。

2. Investing heavily in a single volatile stock can place an individual's entire financial future in a **perilous** position.
   大量投资于单一的波动性股票可能使个人的整个财务前途处于危险境地。

3. The documentary revealed the **perilous** working conditions endured by miners in remote regions lacking proper safety regulations.
   纪录片揭示了偏远地区缺乏适当安全法规的矿工所忍受的危险工作环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "PERIL-ous" — the word "peril" is right inside it, meaning danger. Add "-ous" and you get "full of peril."
把"perilous"想成"PERIL（危险）+ ous（充满）"——"peril"就在词中，加上"-ous"就是"充满危险的"。
