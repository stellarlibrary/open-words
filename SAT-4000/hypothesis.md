# 🔬 hypothesis

🔊 /haɪˈpɑːθəsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A proposed explanation or assumption made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation
**中文：** n. 假说；假设；前提

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- theory（理论）
- assumption（假定）
- conjecture（推测）
- premise（前提）
- supposition（假设）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **test a hypothesis / formulate a hypothesis / support a hypothesis / reject a hypothesis / working hypothesis**

👉 Example: The research team formulated a hypothesis before designing the experiment to test their predictions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist proposed a bold **hypothesis** suggesting that environmental factors play a more significant role in the disease than genetic predisposition.
   这位科学家提出了一个大胆的假设，认为环境因素在该疾病中的作用比遗传因素更为重要。

2. After months of rigorous experimentation, the data ultimately failed to support the original **hypothesis** about cellular regeneration.
   经过数月严格的实验，数据最终未能支持关于细胞再生的原始假设。

3. The professor emphasized that a strong **hypothesis** must be both testable and falsifiable to meet the standards of scientific inquiry.
   教授强调，一个有力的假设必须既可检验又可证伪，才能符合科学探究的标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hypo-" (under, below) + "thesis" (a position or argument) — a hypothesis is the foundational idea placed "under" a thesis, waiting to be proven.
把"hypothesis"想成"hypo-"（在下面）+"thesis"（论点）——假设是放在论点"下方"的基础想法，等待被证明。
