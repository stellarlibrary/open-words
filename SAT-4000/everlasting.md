# ♾️ everlasting

🔊 /ˌɛvərˈlæstɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting forever or for a very long time; seemingly without end
**中文：** adj. 永恒的；接连不断的；无止境的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eternal（永恒的）
- perpetual（永久的）
- enduring（持久的）
- immortal（不朽的）
- infinite（无限的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **everlasting love / everlasting peace / everlasting impact / everlasting gratitude**

👉 Example: The treaty was intended to establish everlasting peace between the two nations after centuries of conflict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher pondered whether humanity's quest for **everlasting** life was a noble pursuit or a fundamental misunderstanding of nature.
   哲学家思考人类对永恒生命的追求是崇高的追求还是对自然的根本误解。

2. Her **everlasting** dedication to civil rights advocacy inspired generations of activists to continue the struggle for social justice.
   她对民权事业永恒的奉献激励了一代又一代的活动家继续为社会正义而奋斗。

3. The ancient monument stands as an **everlasting** testament to the architectural ingenuity and artistic vision of its anonymous creators.
   这座古老的纪念碑是其匿名创造者建筑才华和艺术远见的永恒见证。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ever-lasting" — it literally means "lasting forever," something that continues for ever and ever without stopping.
把"everlasting"想成"ever"（永远）+ "lasting"（持续的）——字面意思就是"永远持续的"，永不停止。
