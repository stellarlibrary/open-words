# ✨ enchant

🔊 /ɪnˈtʃænt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fill with great delight or charm; to cast a spell upon someone using magic or sorcery
**中文：** v. 使迷惑；施魔法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bewitch（迷住）
- captivate（使着迷）
- mesmerize（使入迷）
- fascinate（使神魂颠倒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enchant an audience / enchanted by / enchanting performance / enchanted forest**

👉 Example: The storyteller's vivid descriptions enchanted the children, who listened in wide-eyed wonder.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medieval legend tells of a sorceress who could **enchant** entire kingdoms with her mysterious and powerful incantations.
   中世纪传说讲述了一位女巫能用她神秘而强大的咒语迷惑整个王国。

2. Visitors are consistently **enchanted** by the breathtaking natural beauty and serene atmosphere of the coastal mountain region.
   游客们总是被这个沿海山区令人叹为观止的自然美景和宁静氛围所迷住。

3. The composer's ability to **enchant** audiences with hauntingly beautiful melodies secured her reputation as a generational talent.
   这位作曲家以萦绕心头的优美旋律迷住观众的能力奠定了她作为一代天才的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-chant" as being put under a spell through a "chant" — magical chanting that enchants and captivates you.
把"enchant"想成通过"chant（吟唱）"被施了魔法——神奇的吟唱使你着迷和沉醉。
