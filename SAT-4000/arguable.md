# 💬 arguable

🔊 /ˈɑːrɡjuəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Open to disagreement or debate; capable of being supported by reasoning or evidence
**中文：** 可论证的；可议的；有争议的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- debatable（可争论的）
- disputable（有争议的）
- contestable（可质疑的）
- questionable（可疑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **it is arguable that / arguable point / arguably (adverb form)**

👉 Example: It is arguable that social media has done more harm than good to public discourse in democratic societies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. It is **arguable** that renewable energy sources will entirely replace fossil fuels within the next several decades.
   可再生能源在未来几十年内将完全取代化石燃料，这一点是**可以论证的**。

2. The professor raised an **arguable** point about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence in modern healthcare systems.
   教授提出了一个关于人工智能在现代医疗系统中伦理影响的**可议**观点。

3. Whether the new policy will reduce crime rates remains highly **arguable** among leading criminologists and social researchers.
   新政策是否能降低犯罪率，在顶尖犯罪学家和社会研究者中仍然**存在很大争议**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arguable" as "argue-able" — something you are able to argue about, meaning it is open to debate.
把"arguable"想成"argue-able"——你能够争论的东西，意味着它是可以辩论的。
