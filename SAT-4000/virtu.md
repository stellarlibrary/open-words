# 🏺 virtu

🔊 /vɜːrˈtuː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Objects of art collectively, especially fine antiques; a knowledge of or taste for fine art objects and curios
**中文：** 古董类；艺术品；对艺术品的鉴赏力或爱好

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- antiques（古董）
- curios（珍品）
- objets d'art（艺术品）
- collectibles（收藏品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **articles of virtu / objects of virtu / collection of virtu / taste for virtu**

👉 Example: The museum's gallery displayed remarkable articles of virtu from the Renaissance period.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The aristocrat's mansion was filled with articles of **virtu** collected during decades of extensive travel across Europe.
   这位贵族的宅邸里摆满了数十年间在欧洲广泛旅行时收集的古董艺术品。

2. Auction houses regularly attract wealthy collectors who compete fiercely for rare pieces of **virtu** from distinguished estates.
   拍卖行定期吸引富有的收藏家，他们为来自著名庄园的稀有古董艺术品展开激烈竞争。

3. Her refined appreciation for **virtu** enabled her to identify authentic Renaissance artifacts among numerous forgeries and replicas.
   她对古董艺术品的高雅鉴赏力使她能够在众多赝品和复制品中识别出真正的文艺复兴时期文物。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virtu" as related to "virtuoso" — both come from Italian roots meaning excellence; virtu refers to objects of artistic excellence.
把"virtu"想成与"virtuoso"相关——两者都源自意大利语中表示卓越的词根；virtu指的是具有艺术卓越性的物品。
