# 👔 uniform

🔊 /ˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Remaining the same in all cases; consistent, identical. (n.) A distinctive set of clothing worn by members of an organization
**中文：** adj. 统一的；一致的；始终如一的 n. 制服

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consistent（一致的）
- homogeneous（同质的）
- identical（相同的）
- standardized（标准化的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **uniform standards / uniform distribution / uniform appearance / school uniform / military uniform**

👉 Example: The school requires students to wear a uniform to promote equality and reduce distractions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manufacturer must ensure **uniform** quality across all products to maintain consumer trust and brand reputation.
   制造商必须确保所有产品质量统一，以维护消费者信任和品牌声誉。

2. Scientists observed a remarkably **uniform** distribution of particles throughout the experimental chamber during the controlled trial.
   科学家在受控试验中观察到实验室内粒子分布极为均匀。

3. The new policy established **uniform** regulations for all departments, eliminating the inconsistencies that had previously caused confusion.
   新政策为所有部门制定了统一的规章制度，消除了此前造成混乱的不一致之处。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "uni-form" as "one form" — everything takes one single form, making it all the same and consistent.
把"uniform"想成"uni（一个）+ form（形式）"——只有一种形式，即统一的、一致的。
