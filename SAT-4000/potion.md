# 🧪 potion

🔊 /ˈpoʊʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A liquid mixture intended as a medicine, poison, or magical preparation; a dose of liquid
**中文：** n. 一剂；一服；饮剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elixir（灵丹妙药）
- brew（调制饮料）
- concoction（调制品）
- tonic（补药）
- draught（饮剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **love potion / magic potion / healing potion / potent potion / poison potion**

👉 Example: The alchemist carefully prepared a potion believed to possess extraordinary curative properties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Ancient physicians documented elaborate recipes for herbal **potions** that were administered to patients suffering from chronic ailments.
   古代医生记录了复杂的草药饮剂配方，用于治疗患有慢性疾病的病人。

2. The researcher analyzed the chemical composition of the traditional **potion** to determine its actual medicinal effectiveness.
   研究人员分析了这种传统饮剂的化学成分，以确定其实际的药用效果。

3. Literary scholars note that the motif of a magical **potion** appears frequently in mythology as a catalyst for transformation.
   文学学者指出，魔法药剂的主题在神话中频繁出现，作为变化的催化剂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "potion" as "po-tion" sounding like "pour + tion" — you pour a special liquid mixture to create a potion.
把"potion"想成"pour（倒）+ tion"——你倒出一种特殊的液体混合物来制作一剂药水。
