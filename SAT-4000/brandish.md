# ⚔️ brandish

🔊 /ˈbrændɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To wave or flourish something, especially a weapon, in a menacing, excited, or triumphant way; to display ostentatiously. (n.) An act of waving or flourishing
**中文：** v. 挥舞；炫耀 n. 挥舞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flourish（挥动）
- wield（挥舞）
- wave（挥动）
- flaunt（炫耀）
- swing（挥舞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brandish a weapon / brandish a sword / brandish a knife / brandish evidence**

👉 Example: The knight raised his arm to brandish the gleaming sword before the assembled crowd of onlookers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The protester began to **brandish** a large banner while delivering an impassioned speech to the gathered crowd.
   抗议者一边向聚集的人群发表激昂的演讲，一边开始挥舞一面大横幅。

2. The attorney chose to **brandish** the newly discovered documents as irrefutable proof of the defendant's fraudulent scheme.
   律师选择展示新发现的文件，作为被告欺诈计划的确凿证据。

3. Security guards intervened when the agitated individual attempted to **brandish** a sharp object in the crowded public plaza.
   当那名情绪激动的人试图在拥挤的公共广场挥舞利器时，保安人员进行了干预。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brand-ish" — imagine branding a sword by waving it through the air, making it visible for all to see like a brand.
把"brandish"想成"brand（品牌/烙印）+ ish"——想象通过在空中挥舞一把剑来展示它，让所有人都能看到，就像展示品牌一样。
