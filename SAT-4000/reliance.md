# 🤝 reliance

🔊 /rɪˈlaɪəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of depending on or trusting in someone or something; confident trust or dependence
**中文：** n. 信赖，依靠；受信赖的人或物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dependence（依赖）
- trust（信任）
- confidence（信心）
- faith（信赖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reliance on / reliance upon / heavy reliance / place reliance on / self-reliance**

👉 Example: The nation's heavy reliance on imported fossil fuels has prompted a shift toward renewable energy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The country's excessive **reliance** on a single export commodity has made its economy vulnerable to global market fluctuations.
   该国对单一出口商品的过度依赖使其经济容易受到全球市场波动的影响。

2. Educators warn that students' growing **reliance** on digital tools may diminish their ability to think critically and independently.
   教育工作者警告说，学生对数字工具日益增长的依赖可能会削弱他们独立批判性思考的能力。

3. The success of the humanitarian mission depended on the **reliance** placed upon local volunteers who understood the community's needs.
   人道主义任务的成功取决于对了解社区需求的当地志愿者的信赖。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reliance" as "re-liance" from "rely" + "ance" — the state of relying on something again and again, building deep trust.
把"reliance"想成"rely"加"ance"——一次又一次依靠某物的状态，建立起深厚的信赖。
