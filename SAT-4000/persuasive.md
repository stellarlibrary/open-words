# 💬 persuasive

🔊 /pərˈsweɪsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the power or ability to convince or influence others; compelling in argument or reasoning
**中文：** adj. 有说服力的；劝诱的，劝说的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- convincing（令人信服的）
- compelling（引人注目的）
- cogent（有说服力的）
- influential（有影响力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **persuasive argument / persuasive evidence / persuasive speaker / persuasive essay / highly persuasive**

👉 Example: The defense attorney presented a persuasive argument that raised considerable doubt about the charges.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher delivered a highly **persuasive** presentation that convinced the funding committee to approve the ambitious project.
   这位研究人员做了一场极具说服力的报告，说服了资助委员会批准这个雄心勃勃的项目。

2. Critics acknowledged that the author's most **persuasive** essays combined rigorous logic with vivid, emotionally resonant examples.
   评论家们承认，该作者最具说服力的文章将严密的逻辑与生动且能引起情感共鸣的例子相结合。

3. A **persuasive** leader can inspire collective action even when the proposed course involves significant personal sacrifice.
   一位有说服力的领导者即使在所提议的方案涉及重大个人牺牲时也能激发集体行动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "persuasive" as "persuade + ive (having the quality)" — someone persuasive has the quality of being able to persuade.
把"persuasive"想成"persuade（说服）+ ive（具有……性质的）"——persuasive的人具有能够说服他人的特质。
