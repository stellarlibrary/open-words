# 📊 quantitative

🔊 /ˈkwɑːntɪteɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to, measuring, or measured by the quantity of something rather than its quality
**中文：** adj. 定量的；量的，数量的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- numerical（数值的）
- measurable（可测量的）
- statistical（统计的）
- empirical（经验的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quantitative analysis / quantitative data / quantitative research / quantitative methods**

👉 Example: The researchers relied on quantitative data to support their hypothesis about population growth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study employed **quantitative** methods to measure the correlation between income levels and educational attainment.
   该研究采用定量方法来衡量收入水平与教育程度之间的相关性。

2. Investors often rely on **quantitative** analysis to evaluate the financial performance of publicly traded companies.
   投资者通常依赖定量分析来评估上市公司的财务表现。

3. The professor emphasized that **quantitative** evidence is essential for drawing reliable conclusions in scientific research.
   教授强调定量证据对于在科学研究中得出可靠结论至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quantitative" as "quantity + ative" — it describes anything related to quantity, focusing on numbers and amounts.
把"quantitative"想成"quantity + ative"——描述与数量相关的事物，侧重于数字和数额。
