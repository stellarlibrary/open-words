# 🤫 tacit

🔊 /ˈtæsɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Understood or implied without being stated; silent, unspoken
**中文：** adj. 默许的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- implicit（含蓄的）
- unspoken（未说出口的）
- implied（暗示的）
- understood（心照不宣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tacit agreement / tacit approval / tacit understanding / tacit consent / tacit knowledge**

👉 Example: There was a tacit understanding among the colleagues that personal matters would not be discussed at work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manager gave her **tacit** approval by not objecting when the team proceeded with the new marketing strategy.
   经理没有反对团队推进新营销策略，从而给予了她默许的批准。

2. There existed a **tacit** agreement among the negotiators that certain sensitive topics would remain off the official agenda.
   谈判者之间存在一种默契，即某些敏感话题将不列入正式议程。

3. His **tacit** endorsement of the candidate was evident from his refusal to support any of the opposing nominees.
   他对该候选人的默许支持从他拒绝支持任何对手提名者中显而易见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tacit" sounding like "take it" — you just take it as understood, no words needed.
把"tacit"听起来像"take it"——你只需默默接受，无需言语。
