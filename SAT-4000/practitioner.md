# 🩺 practitioner

🔊 /prækˈtɪʃənər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person actively engaged in a profession, art, or discipline; especially a medical doctor or someone who practices a particular occupation
**中文：** n. 开业者，从业者，执业医生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- professional（专业人士）
- specialist（专家）
- clinician（临床医生）
- expert（行家）
- practiser（从业者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **medical practitioner / general practitioner / legal practitioner / experienced practitioner / skilled practitioner**

👉 Example: The general practitioner referred the patient to a specialist for further diagnostic evaluation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The experienced **practitioner** emphasized the importance of evidence-based approaches when treating patients with complex chronic conditions.
   这位经验丰富的执业医生强调了在治疗复杂慢性病患者时采用循证方法的重要性。

2. As a legal **practitioner** with decades of experience, she was well-equipped to navigate the intricacies of international trade law.
   作为一名拥有数十年经验的法律从业者，她完全有能力处理国际贸易法的复杂细节。

3. The conference brought together **practitioners** from diverse fields to discuss innovative strategies for sustainable urban development.
   会议汇集了来自不同领域的从业者，讨论可持续城市发展的创新战略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "practitioner" as "practice + tion + er" — a person (er) who does the action (tion) of practice, meaning someone who practices a profession.
把"practitioner"想成"practice（实践）+ tion + er（人）"——一个从事实践活动的人，即从业者。
