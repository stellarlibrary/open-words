# 💝 solicitous

🔊 /səˈlɪsɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing eager concern or attention; anxiously caring; earnestly desirous
**中文：** adj. 热切的；期望的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attentive（关心的）
- concerned（挂念的）
- anxious（焦虑的）
- caring（关怀的）
- eager（热切的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solicitous about / solicitous for / solicitous of / solicitous attention**

👉 Example: The nurse was solicitous about the patient's comfort during the lengthy recovery process.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador was **solicitous** of the delegates' needs, ensuring that every participant had adequate resources and accommodation.
   大使对代表们的需求十分关切，确保每位参与者都有充足的资源和住宿。

2. Her **solicitous** inquiries about the research team's progress demonstrated genuine investment in the project's ultimate success.
   她对研究团队进展的热切询问表明了她对项目最终成功的真诚投入。

3. The committee remained **solicitous** regarding public safety concerns and promptly addressed every complaint submitted by residents.
   委员会对公共安全问题始终保持关切，并及时处理了居民提交的每一项投诉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solicitous" as "solicit + us" — someone who solicits our well-being is eagerly caring about us.
把"solicitous"想成"solicit + us"——一个主动关心我们福祉的人是热切关怀我们的。
