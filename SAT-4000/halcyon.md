# 🕊️ halcyon

🔊 /ˈhælsiən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Denoting a period of time in the past that was idyllically happy and peaceful. (n.) A mythical bird; a kingfisher
**中文：** adj. 宁静的；平稳的；太平的 n. 翡翠鸟

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- serene（宁静的）
- tranquil（安宁的）
- peaceful（和平的）
- idyllic（田园般的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **halcyon days / halcyon period / halcyon times / halcyon era**

👉 Example: The elderly professor often reminisced about the halcyon days of his youth spent at the university.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many historians regard the postwar decade as a **halcyon** period of unprecedented economic growth and social stability.
   许多历史学家将战后十年视为经济空前增长和社会稳定的太平时期。

2. She longed to return to the **halcyon** summers of her childhood, when every afternoon was spent beside the lake.
   她渴望回到童年那些宁静的夏天，那时每个下午都在湖边度过。

3. The diplomat warned that the current **halcyon** atmosphere could dissolve rapidly if negotiations failed to produce an agreement.
   这位外交官警告说，如果谈判未能达成协议，当前的平静氛围可能迅速消散。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "halcyon" as "happy + calm + eon" — a long, calm, happy period of time, like the legendary halcyon bird calming the seas.
把"halcyon"想成"happy + calm + eon"——一段漫长、平静、快乐的时光，就像传说中的翡翠鸟能使大海平静一样。
