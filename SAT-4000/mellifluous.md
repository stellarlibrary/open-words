# 🍯 mellifluous

🔊 /məˈlɪfluəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (of a voice or words) Sweet or musical; pleasant to hear; flowing smoothly like honey
**中文：** adj. 如蜜般甜美的；流畅的；悦耳的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dulcet（悦耳的）
- euphonious（悦耳的）
- honeyed（甜蜜的）
- melodious（旋律优美的）
- smooth（流畅的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mellifluous voice / mellifluous tones / mellifluous prose / mellifluous speech**

👉 Example: The narrator's mellifluous voice captivated the audience throughout the entire performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **mellifluous** speech eased tensions in the room and persuaded delegates to resume productive negotiations.
   这位外交官甜美流畅的演讲缓解了室内的紧张气氛，说服代表们重新开始富有成效的谈判。

2. Audiences were entranced by the singer's **mellifluous** tones, which seemed to fill every corner of the concert hall.
   观众们被歌手悦耳的嗓音所陶醉，歌声似乎充满了音乐厅的每一个角落。

3. The author's **mellifluous** prose style transforms even mundane subjects into passages of remarkable literary beauty.
   这位作者流畅优美的散文风格将平凡的主题也转化为具有非凡文学美感的段落。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mellifluous" as "melli(honey) + flu(flow)" — words that flow like honey, sweet and smooth to the ear.
把"mellifluous"想成"melli（蜂蜜）+ flu（流动）"——像蜂蜜一样流淌的话语，甜美而顺滑。
