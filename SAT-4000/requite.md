# 💝 requite

🔊 /rɪˈkwaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To repay, reward, or return in kind; to reciprocate a feeling or action, especially love or kindness
**中文：** v. 报答，回报；酬谢

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reciprocate（回报）
- repay（偿还）
- recompense（补偿）
- reward（奖赏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **requite love / requite a favor / requite kindness / unrequited love**

👉 Example: He longed to requite her devotion but found himself unable to express his feelings adequately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The protagonist struggled to **requite** the affection of a loyal companion whose devotion remained steadfast throughout the narrative.
   主人公努力回报一位忠诚伙伴的感情，而这位伙伴在整个故事中始终忠贞不渝。

2. In many classical tragedies, the hero's failure to **requite** genuine love leads to devastating and irreversible consequences.
   在许多古典悲剧中，主人公未能回报真挚的爱情导致了毁灭性且不可逆转的后果。

3. The foundation was established to **requite** the generosity of donors who supported medical research for decades.
   该基金会的成立是为了回报几十年来支持医学研究的捐助者的慷慨。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "requite" as "re-quite" — you do something quite nice again in return, requiting a good deed.
把"requite"想成"re-quite"——你再次做一件相当好的事来回报，即报答善行。
