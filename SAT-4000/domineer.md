# 👊 domineer

🔊 /ˌdɒmɪˈnɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To assert one's authority in an arrogant or overbearing way; to bully or tyrannize others
**中文：** v. 跋扈；作威作福；盛气凌人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tyrannize（暴虐统治）
- bully（恃强凌弱）
- intimidate（恐吓）
- browbeat（威逼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **domineer over someone / domineer in a household / domineering personality**

👉 Example: The manager would domineer over the junior staff, creating a hostile work environment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The autocratic leader sought to **domineer** over every aspect of the organization's daily operations and decisions.
   这位专制的领导人试图对组织日常运作和决策的方方面面作威作福。

2. Parents who **domineer** their children often stifle creativity and undermine the development of independent thinking.
   对子女作威作福的父母往往会扼杀创造力并阻碍独立思考能力的发展。

3. The committee chair was criticized for attempting to **domineer** the proceedings rather than facilitating open discussion.
   委员会主席因试图在会议中独断专行而非促进公开讨论而受到批评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "domineer" as "dominate + engineer" — someone who engineers dominance over others like a tyrant.
把"domineer"想成"dominate + engineer"——一个像暴君一样"策划统治"他人的人。
