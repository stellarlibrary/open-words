# 🦅 percipient

🔊 /pəˈsɪpiənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having keen perception or discernment; quick to notice and understand. (n.) A person who perceives or is capable of perceiving
**中文：** adj. 感知的；有觉察力的；目光敏锐的 n. 有感知力的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perceptive（有洞察力的）
- discerning（有辨别力的）
- astute（精明的）
- observant（善于观察的）
- sagacious（睿智的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **percipient observer / percipient mind / percipient analysis / percipient reader**

👉 Example: The percipient journalist immediately recognized the inconsistencies in the official government statement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Only the most **percipient** members of the audience detected the subtle irony embedded in the speaker's closing remarks.
   只有观众中最敏锐的人才察觉到演讲者结束语中蕴含的微妙讽刺。

2. As a **percipient** critic, she was able to identify structural weaknesses in the manuscript that others had failed to notice.
   作为一位目光敏锐的评论家，她能够发现手稿中别人未注意到的结构缺陷。

3. The detective proved to be remarkably **percipient**, noticing minute details at the crime scene that ultimately solved the case.
   这位侦探被证明非常有洞察力，注意到犯罪现场的细微细节，最终破了案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "percipient" as "perceive + ient (patient)" — a patient perceiver who carefully notices everything around them.
把"percipient"想成"perceive + ient (patient)"——一个耐心的感知者，仔细注意周围的一切。
