# 📚 sedulous

🔊 /ˈsɛdʒʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing dedication and diligence; persevering and industrious in one's efforts
**中文：** adj. 勤勉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diligent（勤勉的）
- assiduous（刻苦的）
- industrious（勤劳的）
- painstaking（煞费苦心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sedulous effort / sedulous attention / sedulous pursuit / sedulous scholar**

👉 Example: The sedulous researcher spent years compiling data before publishing her groundbreaking findings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Through **sedulous** preparation and unwavering commitment, the student earned the highest score on the national examination.
   通过勤勉的准备和坚定不移的决心，这名学生在全国考试中取得了最高分。

2. The **sedulous** craftsman devoted decades to perfecting his technique, producing works of extraordinary precision and beauty.
   这位勤勉的工匠花了数十年时间完善自己的技艺，创作出精确而美丽的作品。

3. Her **sedulous** attention to every detail of the contract prevented the company from entering a disadvantageous agreement.
   她对合同每个细节的勤勉关注使公司免于签订不利的协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sed-u-lous" as "said you're diligent" — someone who is sedulous is always said to be diligent and hardworking.
把"sedulous"想成"said you're diligent"——一个勤勉的人总是被称赞为勤奋和努力工作的。
