# 💰 economical

🔊 /ˌɛkəˈnɑːmɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Giving good value or return in relation to the resources used; thrifty and avoiding waste
**中文：** adj. 节约的；节俭的；经济实惠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thrifty（节俭的）
- frugal（节约的）
- cost-effective（划算的）
- efficient（高效的）
- sparing（节省的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **economical use of / economical with resources / more economical than / economical solution**

👉 Example: Hybrid vehicles are considered more economical than traditional gasoline-powered cars over their lifetime.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new manufacturing process proved far more **economical** than previous methods, reducing production costs by nearly thirty percent.
   新的制造工艺比以前的方法经济得多，将生产成本降低了近百分之三十。

2. She was remarkably **economical** with her language, conveying complex arguments in concise and accessible prose.
   她用词极为精练，用简洁易懂的文字传达了复杂的论点。

3. Urban planners proposed an **economical** design for the public transit system that maximized coverage while minimizing taxpayer expenditure.
   城市规划者为公共交通系统提出了一个经济实惠的设计方案，在最大化覆盖范围的同时减少纳税人支出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Remember that "economical" ends in "-al" like "practical" — it describes a practical, money-saving quality, unlike "economic" which relates to the economy itself.
记住"economical"以"-al"结尾，就像"practical"（实用的）——它描述的是节俭、省钱的特质，不同于描述经济本身的"economic"。
