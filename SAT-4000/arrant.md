# 😈 arrant

🔊 /ˈærənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Complete and utter; thoroughly bad or objectionable; notorious
**中文：** adj. 极恶的；声名狼藉的；彻头彻尾的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- notorious（臭名昭著的）
- flagrant（公然的）
- utter（彻底的）
- egregious（极其恶劣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arrant nonsense / arrant fool / arrant liar / arrant coward**

👉 Example: The critic dismissed the proposal as arrant nonsense unworthy of serious consideration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor condemned the student's plagiarized thesis as a piece of **arrant** dishonesty that violated all academic standards.
   教授谴责该学生抄袭的论文是彻头彻尾的学术不端行为，违反了所有学术规范。

2. Only an **arrant** fool would invest an entire fortune in such a speculative and poorly researched venture.
   只有十足的蠢人才会将全部财产投入如此投机且缺乏研究的项目。

3. The diplomat described the accusations against her nation as **arrant** fabrications intended to undermine diplomatic relations.
   这位外交官将针对其国家的指控描述为旨在破坏外交关系的彻头彻尾的捏造。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arrant" as "errant" taken to the extreme — an errant person goes astray, but an arrant person is thoroughly wicked.
把"arrant"和"errant（犯错的）"联系起来——errant是走入歧途，arrant则是彻底堕落、极其恶劣。
