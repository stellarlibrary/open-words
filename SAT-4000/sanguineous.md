# 🩸 sanguineous

🔊 /sæŋˈɡwɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or containing blood; blood-red in color; having a sanguine temperament
**中文：** adj. 血的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bloody（血的）
- hemorrhagic（出血的）
- sanguine（血红的）
- hematic（血液的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sanguineous fluid / sanguineous discharge / sanguineous exudate / sanguineous drainage**

👉 Example: The physician noted sanguineous drainage from the surgical wound during the postoperative examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pathologist identified a **sanguineous** exudate in the tissue sample, indicating active inflammation within the affected organ.
   病理学家在组织样本中发现了血性渗出物，表明受影响器官内存在活跃的炎症。

2. Medieval physicians classified individuals with a **sanguineous** temperament as cheerful, sociable, and inclined toward optimism.
   中世纪的医生将具有多血质气质的人归类为开朗、善于社交和倾向于乐观的人。

3. The nurse documented the presence of **sanguineous** fluid in the drainage bag and promptly reported the finding to the surgeon.
   护士记录了引流袋中存在血性液体，并立即将发现报告给了外科医生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sanguineous" as "sanguin (blood) + eous (full of)" — literally meaning full of blood or relating to blood, sharing the same Latin root as "sanguine."
把"sanguineous"想成"sanguin（血液）+ eous（充满的）"——字面意思是充满血的或与血有关的，和"sanguine"共享相同的拉丁语词根。
