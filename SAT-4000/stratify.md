# 🧱 stratify

🔊 /ˈstrætɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To form or arrange into layers; to divide into social classes or levels
**中文：** v. 使成层，使分层；使形成阶层

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- layer（分层）
- classify（分类）
- categorize（归类）
- grade（分等级）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stratify into layers / stratify by income / stratify a population / socially stratified**

👉 Example: The researcher decided to stratify the sample population by age and educational background.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Geologists observed that centuries of sediment deposits had **stratified** into distinct and clearly visible layers of rock.
   地质学家观察到数百年的沉积物已经分成了明显可见的不同岩层。

2. The study aimed to **stratify** participants according to socioeconomic status in order to identify meaningful correlations.
   该研究旨在按社会经济地位对参与者进行分层，以找出有意义的相关性。

3. Colonial policies effectively **stratified** the population into rigid social classes that persisted for generations after independence.
   殖民政策有效地将人口分成了僵化的社会阶层，这种阶层在独立后持续了几代人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "strat-ify" as "stratum + ify" — to make into strata or layers, like a layered cake with distinct horizontal sections.
把"stratify"想成"stratum + ify"——变成层状，就像一个有着清晰水平分层的千层蛋糕。
