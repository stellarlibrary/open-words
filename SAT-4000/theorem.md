# 📐 theorem

🔊 /ˈθɪərəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A general proposition that has been proved or is assumed to be true on the basis of reasoning; a rule in mathematics or logic.
**中文：** n. 定理；原理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proposition（命题）
- principle（原理）
- axiom（公理）
- formula（公式）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prove a theorem / mathematical theorem / fundamental theorem / apply a theorem**

👉 Example: The students spent the entire class period attempting to prove the theorem on the blackboard.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor demonstrated how the Pythagorean **theorem** can be applied to solve complex architectural design problems.
   教授演示了如何将勾股定理应用于解决复杂的建筑设计问题。

2. Establishing a rigorous proof for this **theorem** required mathematicians to develop entirely new analytical frameworks and methods.
   为这个定理建立严格的证明需要数学家开发全新的分析框架和方法。

3. The fundamental **theorem** of calculus elegantly connects the concepts of differentiation and integration in mathematical analysis.
   微积分基本定理优雅地将微分和积分的概念在数学分析中联系起来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "theo-rem" as "the-o-rem" — "the original reminder" of a proven truth in mathematics.
把"theorem"想成"the original reminder"——数学中已被证明的真理的"原始提醒"。
