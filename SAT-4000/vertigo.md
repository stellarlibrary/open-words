# 🌀 vertigo

🔊 /ˈvɜːrtɪɡoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A sensation of dizziness or spinning; a feeling of disorientation often caused by looking down from a great height
**中文：** n. 眩晕；晕头转向

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dizziness（头晕）
- giddiness（眩晕）
- lightheadedness（头昏）
- disorientation（迷失方向）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffer from vertigo / a bout of vertigo / experience vertigo / induce vertigo**

👉 Example: She experienced a sudden bout of vertigo while standing at the edge of the observation deck.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient reported persistent **vertigo** and nausea, prompting the physician to order a comprehensive neurological examination.
   患者报告持续性**眩晕**和恶心，促使医生下令进行全面的神经系统检查。

2. Looking down from the fortieth floor of the skyscraper induced an overwhelming sense of **vertigo** in the visitors.
   从摩天大楼的第四十层往下看，使来访者产生了一种难以抗拒的**眩晕**感。

3. Chronic **vertigo** can severely impair a person's ability to perform routine daily tasks and maintain proper balance.
   慢性**眩晕**会严重损害一个人执行日常事务和保持平衡的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vertigo" as "vert-i-go" — when the world "verts" (turns), you want to "go" but cannot keep your balance.
把"vertigo"想成"vert-i-go"——当世界"verts（旋转）"时，你想"go（走）"却无法保持平衡。
