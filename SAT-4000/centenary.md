# 🎂 centenary

🔊 /sɛnˈtɛnəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The hundredth anniversary of a significant event; a period of one hundred years (adj.) Of or relating to a hundred-year period
**中文：** n. 百年纪念；一百年 adj. 百年的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- centennial（百年纪念的）
- hundredth anniversary（一百周年）
- century（世纪）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **centenary celebration / centenary of the founding / mark the centenary / centenary edition**

👉 Example: The university organized a grand centenary celebration to honor its one hundred years of academic excellence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **centenary** of the nation's independence was commemorated with elaborate ceremonies attended by dignitaries from around the world.
   该国独立一百周年纪念活动举行了隆重的仪式，来自世界各地的政要出席了活动。

2. A special **centenary** edition of the author's most celebrated novel was published with previously unseen illustrations and annotations.
   作者最著名小说的百年纪念版出版，收录了此前未公开的插图和注释。

3. Scholars gathered at the conference to reflect on the **centenary** of the treaty that reshaped international diplomatic relations.
   学者们齐聚会议，反思重塑国际外交关系的条约签署一百周年。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cent-" (hundred, as in century or cent) + "-enary" — a celebration marking one hundred years.
想想"cent-（百，如century世纪或cent分）"加"-enary"——标志着一百年的庆典。
