# 👑 peerless

🔊 /ˈpɪrləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unequaled; having no match or rival; incomparably superior
**中文：** adj. 无与伦比的；出类拔萃的；无比的；绝世的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unrivaled（无敌的）
- matchless（无比的）
- incomparable（无与伦比的）
- unparalleled（无可比拟的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peerless talent / peerless beauty / peerless achievement / peerless performance**

👉 Example: The orchestra delivered a peerless performance that left the audience in stunned silence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's collection of Renaissance paintings is considered **peerless**, attracting scholars and art enthusiasts from every continent.
   该博物馆的文艺复兴绘画收藏被认为是无与伦比的，吸引了来自各大洲的学者和艺术爱好者。

2. Her **peerless** dedication to scientific research over four decades resulted in groundbreaking discoveries that transformed modern medicine.
   她四十年来对科学研究无与伦比的奉献带来了改变现代医学的突破性发现。

3. Critics hailed the young pianist's technique as **peerless**, comparing her interpretive depth to that of the greatest virtuosos in history.
   评论家称赞这位年轻钢琴家的技巧无可匹敌，将她的诠释深度与历史上最伟大的演奏家相提并论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peer-less" as "without peers" — no one is at your level, so you have no peers. You stand alone at the top.
把"peerless"想成"没有同等的人"——没有人能与你并列，你独自站在巅峰。
