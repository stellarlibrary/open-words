# 🧬 gene

🔊 /dʒiːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A unit of heredity that is transferred from parent to offspring and determines some characteristic of the offspring; a segment of DNA
**中文：** n. 基因；遗传因子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hereditary unit（遗传单位）
- DNA segment（DNA片段）
- allele（等位基因）
- genetic factor（遗传因子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gene expression / gene mutation / gene therapy / dominant gene / recessive gene**

👉 Example: Scientists identified the specific gene responsible for the hereditary condition affecting multiple generations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team successfully isolated the **gene** associated with increased susceptibility to cardiovascular disease in certain populations.
   研究团队成功分离出与某些人群心血管疾病易感性增加相关的基因。

2. Advances in **gene** therapy have raised both tremendous hope and significant ethical questions within the medical community worldwide.
   基因治疗的进步在全球医学界引发了巨大的希望和重大的伦理问题。

3. The biologist explained that a single **gene** mutation can have profound and far-reaching effects on an organism's development and survival.
   这位生物学家解释说，单个基因突变可以对生物体的发育和生存产生深远的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gene" as "generate" shortened — genes are the biological units that generate and pass on your traits to the next generation.
把"gene"想成"generate"的缩写——基因是生成并将你的特征传递给下一代的生物单位。
