# 🏔️ upheave

🔊 /ʌpˈhiːv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To heave or lift upward; to cause to rise or swell, especially by geological forces; to displace forcefully
**中文：** v. 鼓起；举起；使隆起；使上升

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- uplift（抬起）
- elevate（升高）
- heave（举起）
- raise（升起）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **upheave the ground / upheave the surface / upheaved rock / upheave foundations**

👉 Example: The volcanic eruption upheaved massive sections of the ocean floor, creating new geological formations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Powerful tectonic forces **upheaved** the ancient seabed, transforming it into the towering mountain range visible across the horizon today.
   强大的构造力使古老的海床隆起，将其变成了今天地平线上可见的高耸山脉。

2. The earthquake **upheaved** several kilometers of roadway, rendering the primary transportation corridor completely impassable for months.
   地震使数公里的道路隆起，使主要交通走廊数月内完全无法通行。

3. Frost cycles gradually **upheaved** the buried foundation stones, causing visible structural damage to the centuries-old cathedral.
   冻融循环逐渐将埋在地下的地基石块鼓起，对这座百年古老教堂造成了明显的结构损坏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "up-heave" — literally heaving something upward, like the earth pushing rocks up from below the surface.
把"upheave"想成"向上用力推"——up（向上）+ heave（用力举），就像大地从地下将岩石向上推起。
