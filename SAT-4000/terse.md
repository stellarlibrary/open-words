# ✂️ terse

🔊 /tɜːrs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Using few words; devoid of superfluity; concise and to the point, sometimes to the degree of seeming rude or abrupt
**中文：** 简洁的，精练的；（有时显得）简短生硬的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concise（简洁的）
- succinct（简明的）
- laconic（简洁的）
- brief（简短的）
- pithy（精练的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **terse reply / terse statement / terse prose / terse tone**

👉 Example: The manager issued a terse response to the complaint, addressing only the essential facts without elaboration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **terse** statement offered no further explanation, leaving journalists to speculate about the reasons behind the withdrawal.
   这位外交官简洁的声明没有提供进一步的解释，让记者们猜测撤出背后的原因。

2. Her writing style is notably **terse**, conveying complex scientific ideas in remarkably few but carefully chosen words.
   她的写作风格极为精练，用极少但精心挑选的词语传达复杂的科学概念。

3. The professor's **terse** feedback on the dissertation surprised the student, who had expected detailed comments and suggestions.
   教授对论文简短的反馈让学生感到惊讶，他原本期待详细的评论和建议。

---

把"terse"和"verse（诗句）"联系起来——好的诗句简短精练，每个词都很重要。
