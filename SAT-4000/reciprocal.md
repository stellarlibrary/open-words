# 🤝 reciprocal

🔊 /rɪˈsɪprəkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Given, felt, or done in return; mutual and complementary. (n.) In mathematics, the quantity obtained by dividing one by a given number
**中文：** adj. 互惠的；相互的；倒数的 n. 倒数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mutual（相互的）
- complementary（互补的）
- corresponding（对应的）
- equivalent（等价的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reciprocal agreement / reciprocal relationship / reciprocal respect / reciprocal trade**

👉 Example: The two nations signed a reciprocal trade agreement that eliminated tariffs on key exports.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The alliance was built on a **reciprocal** understanding that each nation would defend the other in times of military conflict.
   该联盟建立在相互理解的基础上，即每个国家都将在军事冲突时期保卫对方。

2. Healthy friendships depend on **reciprocal** trust, where both parties invest equal effort in maintaining the relationship.
   健康的友谊取决于相互信任，双方在维持关系方面付出同等的努力。

3. In mathematics, the **reciprocal** of five is one-fifth, a concept fundamental to understanding division and fractions.
   在数学中，五的倒数是五分之一，这是理解除法和分数的基本概念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reciprocal" as "re-circle" — what goes around comes around in a circle, reflecting the mutual, give-and-take nature of the word.
把"reciprocal"想成"re-circle（再循环）"——来回循环，体现了这个词互惠互利、有来有往的含义。
