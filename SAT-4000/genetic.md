# 🧬 genetic

🔊 /dʒəˈnɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to genes or heredity; concerning the transmission of characteristics from parents to offspring through DNA
**中文：** adj. 遗传的；基因的；起源的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hereditary（遗传的）
- inherited（继承的）
- genomic（基因组的）
- inborn（天生的）
- congenital（先天的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **genetic mutation / genetic engineering / genetic code / genetic predisposition / genetic disorder**

👉 Example: Advances in genetic research have opened new possibilities for treating previously incurable diseases.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists discovered that the rare disease was caused by a **genetic** mutation affecting a single protein in the immune system.
   科学家发现这种罕见疾病是由影响免疫系统中单一蛋白质的基因突变引起的。

2. The debate over **genetic** modification of crops continues to divide agricultural experts and environmental advocacy groups worldwide.
   关于农作物基因改造的争论继续在世界范围内分裂农业专家和环保倡导团体。

3. Researchers identified a **genetic** predisposition that significantly increases the likelihood of developing cardiovascular disease in certain populations.
   研究人员发现了一种基因易感性，显著增加了某些人群患心血管疾病的可能性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "genetic" as "gene-tic" — directly related to "gene," the basic unit of heredity that parents pass to their children.
把"genetic"拆成"gene-tic"——直接与"gene"（基因）相关，基因是父母传给孩子的基本遗传单位。
