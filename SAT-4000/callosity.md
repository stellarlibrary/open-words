# 🪨 callosity

🔊 /kæˈlɒsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A hardened, thickened area of skin; emotional insensitivity or lack of feeling
**中文：** n. 老茧皮；硬结；无情；冷酷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- callus（老茧）
- hardness（硬度）
- insensitivity（麻木）
- indifference（冷漠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **callosity of skin / callosity of heart / emotional callosity / develop a callosity**

👉 Example: Years of manual labor had produced a thick callosity on the palms of his hands.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician examined the **callosity** on the patient's foot and recommended a specialized treatment regimen.
   医生检查了患者脚上的老茧，并推荐了一套专门的治疗方案。

2. His emotional **callosity** prevented him from empathizing with the suffering of those around him.
   他情感上的冷酷使他无法对周围人的痛苦产生同理心。

3. Repeated exposure to human tragedy can produce a dangerous **callosity** in even the most compassionate professionals.
   反复接触人间悲剧即使在最富有同情心的专业人士身上也会产生危险的麻木感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "callosity" as "call-os-ity" — like a "callous city" where people are hardened and unfeeling, just like thick skin.
把"callosity"想成"callous city"——一座冷酷的城市，人们像老茧一样坚硬而无情。
