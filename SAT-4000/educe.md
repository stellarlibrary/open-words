# 🧠 educe

🔊 /ɪˈdjuːs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To bring out or draw forth something latent or hidden; to deduce or infer from given information
**中文：** v. 引出；演绎；得出；导出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elicit（引出）
- extract（提取）
- derive（推导）
- deduce（演绎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **educe a principle / educe meaning / educe a conclusion from**

👉 Example: The philosopher sought to educe universal truths from the particular observations of daily life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The skilled interviewer was able to **educe** candid responses from the otherwise reticent and guarded witness.
   这位熟练的采访者能够从这位沉默寡言、戒备森严的证人口中引出坦率的回答。

2. Through careful analysis of the data, the researcher managed to **educe** a coherent pattern from seemingly random results.
   通过对数据的仔细分析，研究人员设法从看似随机的结果中得出了一个连贯的模式。

3. The professor challenged students to **educe** the underlying assumptions that governed the author's controversial argument.
   教授要求学生们推导出支配该作者争议性论点的潜在假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-duce" as "e(out) + duce(lead)" — to lead out hidden knowledge, like a detective drawing out the truth.
把"educe"想成"e(出) + duce(引导)"——把隐藏的知识引导出来，就像侦探引出真相一样。
