# ⚓ armada

🔊 /ɑːrˈmɑːdə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large fleet of warships; by extension, any large group of vehicles or vessels moving together
**中文：** 舰队；大批船只（或车辆）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fleet（舰队）
- flotilla（小型舰队）
- squadron（中队）
- navy（海军）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Spanish Armada / an armada of ships / a vast armada / invincible armada**

👉 Example: The Spanish Armada of 1588 was famously defeated by the English navy in a pivotal naval engagement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The emperor assembled a formidable **armada** of warships to defend the coastal territories against the impending foreign invasion.
   皇帝集结了一支强大的战舰**舰队**，以保卫沿海领土免受即将到来的外国入侵。

2. An **armada** of fishing vessels set sail at dawn, heading toward the rich waters of the continental shelf for the seasonal harvest.
   一支捕鱼船**舰队**在黎明时分启航，驶向大陆架丰富的水域进行季节性捕捞。

3. The documentary chronicled the defeat of the great **armada** and its lasting impact on the balance of European maritime power.
   这部纪录片记录了这支庞大**舰队**的失败及其对欧洲海上力量平衡的深远影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "armada" as "armed" + "a" — an armada is a group of "armed" ships ready for battle on the sea.
把"armada"想成"armed"+"a"——舰队就是一群"武装的"船只，准备在海上作战。
