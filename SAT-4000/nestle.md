# 🏡 nestle

🔊 /ˈnɛsəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To settle comfortably and snugly; to cuddle or press closely; to be situated in a sheltered or partly hidden position
**中文：** v. 舒适地坐定；偎依；半隐半现地处于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- snuggle（依偎）
- cuddle（拥抱）
- settle（安顿）
- nuzzle（依偎）
- tuck（藏入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nestle among / nestle against / nestle into / nestle in the hills / nestle close to**

👉 Example: The quaint village nestles in a valley surrounded by towering snow-capped mountain peaks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient monastery **nestles** among densely forested hillsides, almost invisible to travelers passing along the winding road below.
   这座古老的修道院坐落在茂密的山林之间，路过下方蜿蜒道路的旅行者几乎看不到它。

2. The child **nestled** against her grandmother's shoulder, finding comfort and warmth during the long winter evening by the fire.
   孩子依偎在祖母的肩膀上，在漫长冬夜的炉火旁找到了舒适和温暖。

3. Several charming cottages **nestle** along the riverbank, their thatched roofs barely visible through the surrounding ancient oak trees.
   几座迷人的小屋半隐半现地坐落在河岸边，茅草屋顶在周围古老橡树丛中若隐若现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nestle" as coming from "nest" — just as a bird settles snugly into its nest, you nestle into a comfortable position.
把"nestle"联想到"nest"(巢)——就像鸟儿舒适地窝进巢里一样，你也舒适地安顿下来。
