# 🫁 bronchus

🔊 /ˈbrɒŋkəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Either of the two main branches of the trachea that lead to the lungs, serving as air passages
**中文：** n. 支气管

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- airway（气道）
- air passage（气管通道）
- bronchial tube（支气管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **main bronchus / left/right bronchus / bronchus obstruction / plural: bronchi**

👉 Example: The surgeon carefully examined the right bronchus before proceeding with the minimally invasive procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The radiologist identified a small obstruction in the left **bronchus** that was restricting airflow to the lower lung lobe.
   放射科医生发现左支气管中有一个小阻塞物，限制了通往下肺叶的气流。

2. During the anatomy lecture, the professor explained how the **bronchus** divides into progressively smaller bronchioles within each lung.
   在解剖学课上，教授讲解了支气管如何在每个肺内逐渐分成越来越小的细支气管。

3. Foreign objects that accidentally enter the **bronchus** can cause severe respiratory distress and require immediate medical intervention.
   意外进入支气管的异物可能引起严重的呼吸窘迫，需要立即进行医疗干预。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bronchus" as the "branch" of your windpipe — both start with "br" and a bronchus branches off the trachea into your lungs.
把"bronchus"想成气管的"分支（branch）"——两者都以"br"开头，支气管从气管分出进入肺部。
