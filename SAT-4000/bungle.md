# 🤦 bungle

🔊 /ˈbʌŋɡl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To carry out a task clumsily or incompetently; to botch or ruin something. (n.) A clumsy or botched attempt; a failure
**中文：** v. 笨手笨脚地做；把…搞糟 n. 粗劣；失败

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- botch（搞砸）
- fumble（笨拙地处理）
- blunder（犯大错）
- mishandle（处理不当）
- mess up（弄糟）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bungle an operation / bungle the job / bungle an opportunity / a complete bungle**

👉 Example: The inexperienced surgeon nearly bungled the routine procedure due to a lack of adequate preparation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The contractor managed to **bungle** every phase of the renovation, resulting in significant delays and cost overruns.
   承包商在翻修的每个阶段都搞砸了，导致了严重的延误和成本超支。

2. Investigators concluded that the agents had **bungled** the surveillance operation by failing to secure their communications properly.
   调查人员得出结论，特工们因未能妥善保护通讯而搞砸了监视行动。

3. The diplomatic **bungle** caused an international incident that took months of careful negotiation to resolve satisfactorily.
   这次外交失误引发了一起国际事件，花了数月的谨慎谈判才得以圆满解决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bungle" as rhyming with "jungle" — imagine stumbling clumsily through a dense jungle, making a mess of everything.
把"bungle"和"jungle（丛林）"联系起来——想象在茂密的丛林中笨手笨脚地跌跌撞撞，把一切搞得一团糟。
