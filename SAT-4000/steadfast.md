# 🛡️ steadfast

🔊 /ˈstɛdfæst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Resolutely or dutifully firm and unwavering; loyal, faithful, and unchanging in one's commitments
**中文：** adj. 稳定的；坚定的，忠实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unwavering（坚定不移的）
- resolute（坚决的）
- loyal（忠诚的）
- devoted（忠实的）
- constant（始终如一的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **steadfast commitment / steadfast ally / steadfast in one's belief / remain steadfast**

👉 Example: She remained steadfast in her conviction despite enormous pressure to change her position.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Throughout the prolonged negotiations, the ambassador remained **steadfast** in her commitment to achieving a fair and equitable agreement.
   在漫长的谈判过程中，这位大使始终坚定地致力于达成一项公平公正的协议。

2. The organization's **steadfast** dedication to environmental conservation has earned it widespread recognition and numerous international awards.
   该组织对环境保护的坚定奉献为其赢得了广泛的认可和众多国际奖项。

3. His **steadfast** loyalty to the institution never wavered, even when colleagues and former allies chose to pursue other opportunities.
   即使当同事和昔日盟友选择追求其他机会时，他对该机构的忠诚也从未动摇。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stead-fast" as "steady and fast (firmly held)" — someone steadfast holds steady and stands fast, never wavering from their position.
把"steadfast"想成"steady + fast（牢固的）"——坚定的人站稳脚跟，牢牢坚守，从不动摇自己的立场。
