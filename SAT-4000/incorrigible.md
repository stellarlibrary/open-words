# 🚫 incorrigible

🔊 /ɪnˈkɒrɪdʒɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not able to be corrected, improved, or reformed (n.) A person who cannot be reformed
**中文：** adj. 不可救药的；积习难改的 n. 不可救药的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irredeemable（不可挽回的）
- hopeless（无药可救的）
- inveterate（根深蒂固的）
- unreformable（不可改造的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incorrigible liar / incorrigible optimist / incorrigible behavior / incorrigible habit**

👉 Example: Despite numerous warnings, the incorrigible student continued to disrupt the class with his antics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge described the repeat offender as **incorrigible**, noting that previous rehabilitation programs had entirely failed.
   法官形容这名惯犯不可救药，指出之前的改造项目完全失败了。

2. My grandmother is an **incorrigible** gossip who simply cannot resist sharing every bit of neighborhood news she hears.
   我的祖母是一个积习难改的八卦者，她就是忍不住分享她听到的每一条邻里消息。

3. Critics called the corporation **incorrigible** after it repeatedly violated environmental regulations despite mounting penalties and fines.
   在该公司不断违反环保法规、罚款不断增加之后，批评人士称其不可救药。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-corrig-ible" as "not able to correct" (corrigere = to correct in Latin) — an incorrigible person is beyond correction.
把"incorrigible"拆分为"in（不能）+ corrig（纠正）+ ible（能够）"——不能被纠正的，就是不可救药的。
