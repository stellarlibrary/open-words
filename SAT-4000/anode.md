# ⚡ anode

🔊 /ˈænoʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The positively charged electrode through which electric current flows into a device; the terminal where oxidation occurs
**中文：** 阳极；正极

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- positive electrode（正电极）
- positive terminal（正极端）
- cathode（阴极，反义参考）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **anode material / anode terminal / sacrificial anode / anode and cathode / anode corrosion**

👉 Example: In electrochemistry, oxidation always occurs at the anode while reduction takes place at the cathode.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer replaced the corroded **anode** in the battery to restore the device's optimal electrical performance and efficiency.
   工程师更换了电池中腐蚀的阳极，以恢复设备的最佳电气性能和效率。

2. In a lithium-ion battery, the **anode** is typically composed of graphite, which stores lithium ions during the charging process.
   在锂离子电池中，阳极通常由石墨制成，在充电过程中储存锂离子。

3. Ship hulls are fitted with sacrificial **anode** blocks made of zinc to prevent seawater corrosion of the steel structure.
   船体上安装了由锌制成的牺牲阳极块，以防止钢结构被海水腐蚀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "anode" as "a node that is positive" — the "a" stands for the ascending direction where current flows up and out.
把"anode"想成"一个正的节点"——"a"代表电流上升流出的方向，即阳极。
