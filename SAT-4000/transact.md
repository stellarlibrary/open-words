# 🤝 transact

🔊 /trænˈzækt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To conduct or carry out business, negotiations, or other dealings
**中文：** 交易；办理；谈判

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- negotiate（谈判）
- conduct（办理）
- execute（执行）
- carry out（完成）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transact business / transact a deal / transact affairs / transact with someone**

👉 Example: The two corporations agreed to transact all future business through a neutral intermediary.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. International banks must comply with strict regulations when they **transact** business across national borders and jurisdictions.
   国际银行在跨越国界和司法管辖区办理业务时必须遵守严格的法规。

2. The committee was authorized to **transact** all necessary affairs on behalf of the organization during the chairman's absence.
   委员会被授权在主席缺席期间代表组织处理一切必要事务。

3. Modern technology enables consumers to **transact** purchases instantly without ever visiting a physical retail establishment.
   现代技术使消费者能够即时完成交易，无需亲自前往实体零售店。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-act" as "acting across" — when you transact, you act across the table with another party to complete a deal.
把"transact"想成"trans（穿越）+ act（行动）"——交易就是双方跨越桌面互相行动，达成协议。
