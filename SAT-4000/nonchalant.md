# 😎 nonchalant

🔊 /ˌnɒnʃəˈlɑːnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety, interest, or enthusiasm; coolly indifferent
**中文：** adj. 冷淡的；漠不关心的；若无其事的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indifferent（冷漠的）
- unconcerned（不在意的）
- casual（随意的）
- blasé（漫不经心的）
- unperturbed（泰然自若的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nonchalant attitude / nonchalant manner / nonchalant shrug / appear nonchalant**

👉 Example: Despite the mounting pressure, she maintained a nonchalant demeanor that impressed her colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. He gave a **nonchalant** shrug when asked about the exam results, though he had actually studied for weeks beforehand.
   当被问到考试成绩时，他漫不经心地耸了耸肩，尽管他实际上已经提前复习了好几周。

2. Her **nonchalant** response to the crisis alarmed the board members, who expected a more urgent and decisive reaction.
   她对这场危机漫不经心的回应让董事会成员感到震惊，他们期望看到更紧迫果断的反应。

3. The detective remained deliberately **nonchalant** during the interrogation to avoid alerting the suspect to the mounting evidence.
   侦探在审讯中刻意保持若无其事的态度，以免让嫌疑人对不断增加的证据产生警觉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "non-chalant" as "non-caring" — the French root "chaloir" means "to care," so nonchalant literally means "not caring."
把"nonchalant"想成"non（不）+ chalant（关心）"——法语词根"chaloir"意为"关心"，所以nonchalant就是"不关心的"。
