# 🌍 terrestrial

🔊 /təˈrestriəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to the earth or its inhabitants; living on or relating to land as opposed to water or air
**中文：** 地球的；陆地的；陆生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- earthly（地球的）
- land-based（陆地的）
- worldly（世俗的）
- ground-dwelling（地栖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **terrestrial planet / terrestrial ecosystem / terrestrial animal / terrestrial habitat**

👉 Example: Scientists have discovered several terrestrial species that exhibit remarkable adaptations to extreme desert environments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Mars and Venus are classified as **terrestrial** planets because they possess rocky surfaces similar to that of Earth.
   火星和金星被归类为类地行星，因为它们拥有与地球相似的岩石表面。

2. The documentary explored how **terrestrial** ecosystems are increasingly threatened by rising global temperatures and deforestation practices.
   这部纪录片探讨了陆地生态系统如何日益受到全球气温上升和砍伐森林的威胁。

3. Unlike their aquatic ancestors, modern **terrestrial** vertebrates have developed lungs and limbs suited for survival on dry land.
   与它们的水生祖先不同，现代陆生脊椎动物已发育出适合在陆地上生存的肺和四肢。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "terra" (Latin for "earth") inside "terrestrial" — anything terrestrial belongs to the earth or land.
把"terrestrial"中的"terra"想成拉丁语中的"地球"——任何terrestrial的事物都属于地球或陆地。
