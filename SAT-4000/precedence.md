# 🥇 precedence

🔊 /ˈprɛsɪdəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The condition of being considered more important than someone or something else; priority in importance, order, or rank
**中文：** n. 优先；居先；优先权

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- priority（优先）
- preeminence（卓越）
- superiority（优越）
- primacy（首位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **take precedence over / give precedence to / have precedence / in order of precedence**

👉 Example: In emergency situations, patient safety must always take precedence over administrative procedures and protocols.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Supreme Court ruled that constitutional rights take **precedence** over state legislation when the two are in direct conflict.
   最高法院裁定，当宪法权利与州立法直接冲突时，宪法权利优先于州立法。

2. In academic publishing, the originality of research findings often takes **precedence** over the sheer volume of data presented.
   在学术出版中，研究发现的原创性往往优先于所呈现的数据数量。

3. The committee established a clear order of **precedence** to determine which infrastructure projects would receive funding first.
   委员会建立了明确的优先顺序，以确定哪些基础设施项目将首先获得资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "preced-ence" from "precede" — what precedes (comes first) has precedence (higher priority) over what follows.
把"precedence"和"precede（先于）"联系起来——先到的（precede）就有优先权（precedence）。
