# 🤐 taciturn

🔊 /ˈtæsɪtɜːrn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Reserved or uncommunicative in speech; saying little; habitually silent
**中文：** adj. 沉默寡言的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reticent（寡言的）
- reserved（内敛的）
- uncommunicative（不爱说话的）
- laconic（简洁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **taciturn man / taciturn demeanor / taciturn by nature / remain taciturn**

👉 Example: The taciturn professor rarely spoke outside of lectures, preferring solitude and quiet reflection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **taciturn** witness provided only brief, reluctant answers during the lengthy cross-examination in the courtroom.
   这位沉默寡言的证人在法庭上漫长的交叉询问中只提供了简短而勉强的回答。

2. Despite his **taciturn** nature, the scientist's written publications were remarkably eloquent and profoundly insightful.
   尽管性格沉默寡言，这位科学家的书面出版物却非常雄辩且极具深刻见解。

3. She found her new colleague surprisingly **taciturn**, rarely contributing to discussions during departmental meetings or social gatherings.
   她发现新同事出奇地沉默寡言，在部门会议或社交聚会中很少参与讨论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tacit-urn" — like a "tacit urn," an urn that sits silently and never speaks, just as a taciturn person rarely talks.
把"taciturn"想成"tacit-urn"——像一个沉默的骨灰瓮，静静地放在那里从不说话，就像沉默寡言的人很少讲话一样。
