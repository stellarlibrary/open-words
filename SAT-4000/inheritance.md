# 🏛️ inheritance

🔊 /ɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Property, money, or titles received from someone who has died; the act of inheriting; traits passed genetically from parent to offspring
**中文：** n. 继承；遗传；遗产

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- legacy（遗产）
- bequest（遗赠）
- heritage（传承）
- birthright（与生俱来的权利）
- patrimony（祖产）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **receive an inheritance / leave an inheritance / cultural inheritance / genetic inheritance / inheritance tax**

👉 Example: She received a substantial inheritance from her late grandmother's estate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dispute over the family **inheritance** lasted several years and ultimately required mediation by a professional legal arbitrator.
   这场家族遗产纠纷持续了数年，最终需要一位专业法律仲裁员来调解。

2. Genetic **inheritance** plays a significant role in determining an individual's susceptibility to certain hereditary diseases and conditions.
   遗传在决定个体对某些遗传性疾病的易感性方面起着重要作用。

3. The nation's rich cultural **inheritance** includes centuries of literary, artistic, and philosophical achievements passed through successive generations.
   该国丰富的文化遗产包括代代相传的数百年文学、艺术和哲学成就。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-HERIT-ance" — it comes from "inherit," meaning what you HERIT (receive) from those who came before you.
把"inheritance"想成"in + herit（继承）+ ance"——你从前人那里继承（herit）而来的东西。
