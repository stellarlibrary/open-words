# 🏡 seclude

🔊 /sɪˈkluːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To isolate or shut off from others; to keep apart or withdraw into solitude
**中文：** v. 隐居，与世隔绝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- isolate（隔离）
- sequester（使隔绝）
- withdraw（退隐）
- cloistered（隐居的）
- shelter（庇护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seclude oneself / secluded location / secluded from society / secluded retreat**

👉 Example: The author chose to seclude herself in a remote mountain cabin to complete her novel without distractions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned philosopher decided to **seclude** himself in a rural monastery to focus entirely on completing his magnum opus.
   这位著名的哲学家决定隐居在一座乡村修道院中，专心完成他的代表作。

2. Wealthy aristocrats would often **seclude** themselves in private country estates far removed from the noise and chaos of urban life.
   富裕的贵族通常会隐居在远离城市喧嚣的私人乡间庄园中。

3. The medical team chose to **seclude** the infected patients in a separate ward to prevent the rapid spread of the contagion.
   医疗团队选择将感染患者隔离到单独的病房中，以防止传染病的快速传播。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "seclude" as "se-clude" where "clude" comes from Latin "claudere" (to close) — to close yourself off, to shut away from others.
把"seclude"想成"se（分开）+ clude（关闭）"——把自己关起来，与他人隔绝，隐居起来。
