# 🚨 intruder

🔊 /ɪnˈtruːdər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who enters a place or situation without permission or invitation; one who intrudes
**中文：** n. 侵入者；入侵者；干扰者；妨碍者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trespasser（擅入者）
- invader（入侵者）
- infiltrator（潜入者）
- prowler（徘徊者）
- encroacher（侵犯者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **detect an intruder / intruder alert / unwelcome intruder / intruder in the system**

👉 Example: The security system immediately triggered an alarm when it detected an intruder on the premises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The security cameras captured footage of an **intruder** attempting to bypass the electronic locks at the facility entrance.
   安全摄像头拍到了一名入侵者试图绕过设施入口电子锁的画面。

2. Cybersecurity experts worked around the clock to identify the **intruder** who had breached the company's database.
   网络安全专家夜以继日地工作，以识别入侵公司数据库的侵入者。

3. The immune system treats foreign bacteria as an **intruder** and rapidly mobilizes defensive cells to neutralize the threat.
   免疫系统将外来细菌视为入侵者，并迅速调动防御细胞来消除威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intruder" as "in-truder" — someone who "intrudes" by forcing their way IN where they do not belong.
把"intruder"想成"in（进入）-truder（闯入者）"——一个强行闯入不属于自己地方的人。
