# 🕳️ orifice

🔊 /ˈɔːrɪfɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An opening or hole, especially one in the body or in a mechanical device that serves as an entry or exit point
**中文：** n. 孔口；孔，口

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- opening（开口）
- aperture（孔径）
- hole（孔洞）
- vent（通风口）
- mouth（口）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **orifice plate / nasal orifice / orifice diameter / body orifice**

👉 Example: The engineer measured the diameter of the orifice to ensure proper fluid flow through the pipe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon carefully examined the **orifice** of the wound to determine the extent of internal tissue damage sustained by the patient.
   外科医生仔细检查了伤口的孔口，以确定患者所遭受的内部组织损伤程度。

2. An **orifice** plate was installed in the pipeline to regulate the flow rate of the pressurized industrial fluid system.
   管道中安装了一个孔板来调节加压工业流体系统的流速。

3. The biologist described how the **orifice** of certain marine organisms functions as both an intake and expulsion mechanism.
   生物学家描述了某些海洋生物的孔口如何同时作为吸入和排出机制发挥作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "orifice" as "oral-fice" — the "oral" part reminds you of the mouth, which is a natural opening or hole in the body.
把"orifice"想成"oral-fice"——"oral"部分让你联想到嘴巴，这是身体上一个天然的开口或孔洞。
