# 🎩 aristocrat

🔊 /əˈrɪstəkræt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A member of the aristocracy; a person of noble birth or high social standing
**中文：** 贵族；出身高贵的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nobleman（贵族）
- patrician（贵族人士）
- peer（贵族）
- blue blood（名门望族）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wealthy aristocrat / a true aristocrat / born an aristocrat / aristocrat by birth**

👉 Example: The aristocrat donated a significant portion of his inherited fortune to charitable causes across the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **aristocrat** maintained a lavish estate in the countryside, complete with meticulously landscaped gardens and a private library.
   这位**贵族**在乡间拥有一座豪华庄园，配有精心修剪的花园和私人图书馆。

2. Despite being an **aristocrat** by birth, she dedicated her entire career to advocating for the rights of the underprivileged.
   尽管她生来就是**贵族**，她却将整个职业生涯都献给了为弱势群体争取权利的事业。

3. The celebrated **aristocrat** played a pivotal diplomatic role in negotiating the treaty that ended the prolonged border conflict.
   这位著名的**贵族**在谈判终结旷日持久的边境冲突的条约中发挥了关键的外交作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "aristocrat" as "aristo-crat" — from Greek "aristos" (best), so an aristocrat is someone from the "best" or highest ranking family.
把"aristocrat"想成"aristo-crat"——源自希腊语"aristos"（最优秀的），所以贵族就是来自"最优秀"或最高等级家族的人。
