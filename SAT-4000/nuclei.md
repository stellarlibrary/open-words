# ⚛️ nuclei

🔊 /ˈnjuːkliaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Plural of nucleus; the central and most important parts of objects, movements, or groups; the positively charged cores of atoms
**中文：** n. 核心；核子；原子核（nucleus的复数形式）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cores（核心）
- centers（中心）
- kernels（核）
- hearts（核心部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **atomic nuclei / cell nuclei / the nuclei of atoms / nuclei of organizations**

👉 Example: The physicist studied how atomic nuclei behave under extreme conditions of temperature and pressure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team observed that the **nuclei** of hydrogen atoms fuse together under extreme temperatures to release tremendous energy.
   研究团队观察到氢原子核在极端温度下融合在一起，释放出巨大的能量。

2. In advanced biology courses, students learn how cell **nuclei** regulate gene expression and control cellular reproduction processes.
   在高级生物课程中，学生学习细胞核如何调控基因表达和控制细胞繁殖过程。

3. The **nuclei** of these rural communities served as vital centers for organizing social and economic activities in the region.
   这些农村社区的核心充当了组织该地区社会和经济活动的重要中心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nuclei" as "new-clay-eye" — imagine shaping new clay around tiny eyes (cores); nuclei are the central cores of atoms or cells.
把"nuclei"想成"nucleus的复数"——就像一颗颗小核心，是原子和细胞最中心的部分。
