# ⚡ stimulus

🔊 /ˈstɪmjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A thing or event that evokes a specific reaction; something that encourages activity or development
**中文：** n. 刺激（物）；促进因素

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incentive（激励）
- impetus（推动力）
- spur（刺激）
- catalyst（催化剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **economic stimulus / stimulus package / stimulus for growth / provide a stimulus**

👉 Example: The government introduced a massive stimulus package to revive the struggling economy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new tax policy served as a powerful **stimulus** for small business development and entrepreneurial innovation across the region.
   新的税收政策成为该地区小企业发展和创业创新的强大刺激因素。

2. Researchers discovered that visual **stimulus** can significantly alter neural pathways in the developing brains of young children.
   研究人员发现，视觉刺激可以显著改变幼儿发育中大脑的神经通路。

3. The professor's provocative lecture provided an intellectual **stimulus** that inspired many students to pursue further research.
   教授那场发人深省的讲座提供了智力上的激励，激发了许多学生进行深入研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stimul-us" as "stimulate us" — a stimulus is something that stimulates us into action or reaction.
把"stimulus"想成"stimulate us"——刺激物就是能刺激我们产生行动或反应的东西。
