# 🧠 rational

🔊 /ˈræʃənl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Based on or in accordance with reason or logic; sensible. (n.) A rational number
**中文：** adj. 合理的；理性的 n. 有理数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- logical（合乎逻辑的）
- reasonable（合理的）
- sensible（明智的）
- sound（合理的）
- lucid（清醒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rational decision / rational thinking / rational approach / rational explanation**

👉 Example: The committee made a rational decision based on the available evidence and data.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **rational** analysis of the economic data suggests that inflation will gradually decline over the next fiscal quarter.
   对经济数据的理性分析表明，通胀将在下一财季逐步下降。

2. The philosopher argued that only **rational** beings possess the capacity for moral reasoning and ethical judgment.
   这位哲学家认为，只有理性的存在才具备道德推理和伦理判断的能力。

3. Despite the emotional turmoil, she maintained a **rational** perspective and evaluated each option with careful deliberation.
   尽管情绪波动，她仍保持理性的视角，仔细审慎地评估每一个选项。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ratio-nal" — someone who thinks in ratios and proportions is calculating and logical, hence rational.
把"rational"想成"ratio（比例）+ nal"——一个用比例和比率思考的人是理性的、有逻辑的。
