# 📐 regularity

🔊 /ˌrɛɡjʊˈlærɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being regular; consistency in pattern, timing, or arrangement; uniformity or symmetry.
**中文：** n. 规则性；规律性；整齐；一致性；匀称

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consistency（一致性）
- uniformity（统一性）
- periodicity（周期性）
- symmetry（对称性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with regularity / striking regularity / clockwork regularity / maintain regularity**

👉 Example: The researcher observed the phenomenon occurring with striking regularity across all experimental trials.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The astronomers documented the comet's orbit with remarkable **regularity**, predicting its return to within days of accuracy.
   天文学家以惊人的规律性记录了这颗彗星的轨道，将其回归预测精确到了几天之内。

2. Maintaining **regularity** in sleeping and eating habits is essential for sustaining long-term physical and psychological health.
   保持睡眠和饮食习惯的规律性对于维持长期的身心健康至关重要。

3. The architect admired the geometric **regularity** of the classical facade, noting its perfectly balanced proportions and symmetry.
   建筑师欣赏古典立面的几何规整性，注意到其完美平衡的比例和对称性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "regularity" as "regular + ity" — the state of being regular, like a clock ticking at perfectly even intervals.
把"regularity"想成"regular（规则的）+ ity（名词后缀）"——即规则的状态，如同时钟以完美均匀的间隔滴答作响。
