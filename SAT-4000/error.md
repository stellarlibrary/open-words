# 🚫 error

🔊 /ˈɛrər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A mistake or inaccuracy; the state of being wrong in conduct or judgment; the difference between a measured value and the true value
**中文：** n. 误差；错误；过失

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mistake（错误）
- blunder（大错）
- inaccuracy（不准确）
- oversight（疏忽）
- deviation（偏差）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **margin of error / human error / error in judgment / trial and error**

👉 Example: The margin of error in the survey results was less than three percent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer discovered a critical **error** in the structural calculations that could have compromised the entire building's integrity.
   工程师发现了结构计算中的一个严重误差，可能会危及整栋建筑的完整性。

2. Through a process of trial and **error**, the research team eventually identified the optimal conditions for the experiment.
   通过反复试验，研究团队最终确定了实验的最佳条件。

3. The statistical **error** in the published dataset prompted the journal to issue an immediate retraction of the article.
   已发表数据集中的统计误差促使该期刊立即撤回了该文章。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "error" as related to "err" — to err is human, and an error is the result of that human tendency to make mistakes.
把"error"想成与"err（犯错）"相关——人非圣贤孰能无过，error就是人类容易犯错这一倾向的结果。
