# 👑 vainglorious

🔊 /ˌveɪnˈɡlɔːriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Excessively proud of oneself or one's achievements; showing excessive vanity and boastfulness
**中文：** adj. 虚荣心强的；非常自负的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boastful（自夸的）
- conceited（自负的）
- pompous（自大的）
- arrogant（傲慢的）
- egotistical（自我中心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vainglorious boast / vainglorious leader / vainglorious display / vainglorious ambition**

👉 Example: The general's vainglorious proclamation of victory proved premature when the enemy launched a counterattack.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **vainglorious** emperor commissioned an enormous monument to celebrate achievements that historians would later regard as insignificant.
   这位虚荣自负的皇帝委托建造了一座巨大的纪念碑，以颂扬后来被历史学家视为微不足道的成就。

2. His **vainglorious** speeches alienated many former allies who found his constant self-praise both tiresome and unwarranted.
   他自吹自擂的演讲疏远了许多前盟友，他们觉得他不断的自我吹捧既令人厌倦又毫无根据。

3. The biographer revealed the **vainglorious** nature of the celebrated author, who privately demanded flattery from every acquaintance.
   传记作者揭示了这位著名作家虚荣自负的本性，他私下里要求每个熟人对他阿谀奉承。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vainglorious" as "vain + glorious" — someone who vainly believes they are glorious and cannot stop boasting about it.
把"vainglorious"想成"vain（虚荣）+ glorious（光荣）"——一个虚妄地认为自己光荣无比、并不停吹嘘的人。
