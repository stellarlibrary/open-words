# 😠 petulant

🔊 /ˈpetʃələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Childishly sulky or bad-tempered; irritable and impatient over trivial matters
**中文：** adj. 脾气坏的；使性子的；暴躁的；任性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritable（暴躁的）
- peevish（易怒的）
- querulous（爱抱怨的）
- petty（小气的）
- testy（急躁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **petulant child / petulant tone / petulant outburst / petulant behavior**

👉 Example: The executive's petulant response to mild criticism surprised everyone in the boardroom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **petulant** refusal to compromise on minor procedural issues threatened to derail the entire negotiation.
   这位外交官对次要程序问题任性地拒绝妥协，几乎使整个谈判脱轨。

2. Critics described the author's public response to the unfavorable review as unnecessarily **petulant** and unprofessional.
   批评者认为这位作者对差评的公开回应是不必要的暴躁和不专业的。

3. Rather than engaging in constructive dialogue, the senator delivered a **petulant** tirade against his political opponents.
   这位参议员没有进行建设性对话，而是对他的政治对手发起了一场任性的长篇抨击。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pet-ulant" — imagine a spoiled pet that whines and snaps when it does not get what it wants, just like a petulant person.
把"petulant"想成"pet"（宠物）+"ulant"——想象一只被宠坏的宠物，得不到想要的东西就哀嚎和发脾气，就像一个任性的人。
