# 😠 menacing

🔊 /ˈmɛnəsɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Suggesting the presence of danger; threatening or intimidating in appearance or manner.
**中文：** adj. 威胁的；险恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- threatening（威胁的）
- ominous（不祥的）
- intimidating（令人生畏的）
- sinister（险恶的）
- foreboding（预兆不祥的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **menacing tone / menacing figure / menacing look / menacing presence**

👉 Example: The guard fixed a menacing stare on the trespassers as they approached the restricted area.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Dark clouds gathered in a **menacing** formation over the coastline, signaling the approach of a severe tropical storm.
   乌云以险恶之势在海岸线上空聚集，预示着一场严重的热带风暴即将来临。

2. The diplomat described the neighboring regime's nuclear program as the most **menacing** threat to regional stability in decades.
   这位外交官将邻国政权的核计划描述为数十年来对地区稳定最具威胁的因素。

3. The stray dog bared its teeth in a **menacing** display that caused pedestrians to cross to the opposite sidewalk.
   流浪狗露出牙齿，做出威胁的姿态，导致行人纷纷走到对面的人行道上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "menacing" as "menace + ing" — something that is actively being a menace, like a dark figure looming in a doorway.
把"menacing"想成"menace（威胁）+ ing"——某事物正在进行威胁，就像一个黑影在门口若隐若现。
