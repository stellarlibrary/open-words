# 🐦 finch

🔊 /fɪntʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small songbird of the family Fringillidae, typically having a short stout bill adapted for crushing seeds
**中文：** n. 雀科鸣鸟；雀类

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- songbird（鸣禽）
- sparrow（麻雀）
- warbler（莺）
- bunting（鹀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Darwin's finches / goldfinch / house finch / flock of finches / finch species**

👉 Example: Darwin's finches on the Galapagos Islands provided critical evidence for the theory of natural selection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ornithologist documented fourteen distinct **finch** species on the archipelago, each adapted to a unique ecological niche.
   鸟类学家在该群岛上记录了十四种不同的雀类，每种都适应了独特的生态位。

2. A brightly colored **finch** perched on the branch outside the window, singing a melodious tune at dawn.
   一只色彩鲜艳的雀鸟栖息在窗外的树枝上，在黎明时分唱着悦耳的曲调。

3. Researchers studying **finch** beak morphology discovered that variations correlated directly with available food sources in each habitat.
   研究雀类喙部形态的科学家发现，其变异与每个栖息地中可用食物来源直接相关。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "finch" and "pinch" — a finch uses its beak to pinch and crack open seeds, which is its defining characteristic.
把"finch"和"pinch（捏）"联系起来——雀鸟用它的喙捏开并咬碎种子，这是它的标志性特征。
