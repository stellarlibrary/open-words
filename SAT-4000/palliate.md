# 💊 palliate

🔊 /ˈpælieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make a disease or its symptoms less severe without eliminating the underlying cause; to disguise the seriousness of an offense by excuses or apologies; to alleviate or mitigate.
**中文：** v. 减轻；掩饰；辩解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alleviate（减轻）
- mitigate（缓和）
- relieve（缓解）
- extenuate（减轻…的严重性）
- temper（调和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **palliate the symptoms / palliate the pain / palliate the effects / palliate suffering**

👉 Example: The medication was intended to palliate the patient's chronic pain rather than to cure the underlying condition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician prescribed stronger medication to **palliate** the severe symptoms, though he acknowledged that no permanent cure currently existed.
   医生开了更强的药物来**减轻**严重症状，尽管他承认目前没有永久性的治疗方法。

2. The spokesperson's attempt to **palliate** the corporation's environmental violations only intensified public outrage and media scrutiny of the company.
   发言人试图**掩饰**公司环境违规行为的做法只会加剧公众的愤怒和媒体对该公司的审查。

3. Humanitarian organizations distributed emergency supplies to **palliate** the suffering of thousands of refugees displaced by the prolonged armed conflict.
   人道主义组织分发了紧急物资，以**减轻**数千名因长期武装冲突而流离失所的难民的苦难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "palli-ate" as related to "pall" (a cloth draped over a coffin) — to palliate is to cover up or cloak the severity of something, like draping a cloth over pain.
把"palliate"和"pall"（棺材上的覆盖布）联系起来——palliate就是掩盖或遮蔽某事的严重性，就像在痛苦上披一层布。
