# 🎻 cadenza

🔊 /kəˈdɛnzə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An elaborate, virtuosic solo passage inserted into a concerto or other musical work, typically near the end of a movement
**中文：** 装饰乐段；华彩乐段（通常在协奏曲乐章末尾由独奏者演奏的炫技段落）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- solo passage（独奏乐段）
- flourish（华丽演奏）
- improvisation（即兴演奏）
- virtuoso passage（炫技乐段）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brilliant cadenza / improvised cadenza / cadenza in a concerto / perform a cadenza**

👉 Example: The violinist's improvised cadenza stunned the audience with its technical brilliance and emotional depth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pianist performed a breathtaking **cadenza** that showcased extraordinary technical skill and profound musical sensitivity.
   钢琴家演奏了一段令人叹为观止的华彩乐段，展示了非凡的技术水平和深刻的音乐感悟。

2. Mozart's concertos often include written-out **cadenza** passages, though performers historically preferred their own improvisations.
   莫扎特的协奏曲通常包含写好的华彩乐段，尽管演奏者历来更喜欢自己的即兴创作。

3. The audience held its breath during the soloist's extended **cadenza**, which lasted nearly three spellbinding minutes.
   在独奏者长达近三分钟的华彩乐段中，观众屏住了呼吸，如痴如醉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cadenza" as "cadence + za (pizzazz)" — it is the flashy, showy cadence moment where the soloist dazzles everyone.
把"cadenza"想成"cadence（节奏）+ za（pizzazz的缩写，意为华丽）"——这是独奏者炫技闪耀的华丽节奏时刻。
