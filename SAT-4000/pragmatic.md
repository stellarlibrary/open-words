# 🎯 pragmatic

🔊 /præɡˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Dealing with things sensibly and realistically; focused on practical results rather than theory or ideals
**中文：** adj. 实际的；实用主义的；注重实效的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- practical（实际的）
- realistic（现实的）
- sensible（明智的）
- utilitarian（功利的）
- down-to-earth（脚踏实地的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pragmatic approach / pragmatic solution / pragmatic decision / pragmatic leader**

👉 Example: The committee adopted a pragmatic approach to solving the budget crisis rather than pursuing idealistic reforms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The director took a **pragmatic** approach to resource allocation, prioritizing projects with the highest likelihood of measurable success.
   这位主管对资源分配采取了务实的方法，优先考虑最有可能取得可衡量成功的项目。

2. Rather than debating abstract theories, the **pragmatic** engineer focused on designing solutions that could be implemented immediately.
   这位务实的工程师没有讨论抽象理论，而是专注于设计可以立即实施的解决方案。

3. Her **pragmatic** leadership style earned widespread respect because she consistently delivered tangible results under difficult circumstances.
   她务实的领导风格赢得了广泛尊重，因为她在困难环境下始终能够取得切实的成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pragma-tic" — "pragma" sounds like "program" — a good program is practical and gets things done efficiently.
把"pragmatic"想成"pragma-tic"——"pragma"听起来像"program（程序）"——好的程序是实用的，能高效完成任务。
