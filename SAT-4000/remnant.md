# 🧩 remnant

🔊 /ˈrɛmnənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small remaining quantity of something; a surviving trace or fragment. (adj.) Remaining; left over
**中文：** n. 残余部分，剩余部分 adj. 剩余的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- residue（残余）
- remainder（剩余物）
- vestige（遗迹）
- fragment（碎片）
- relic（遗物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remnants of / a remnant of the past / remnant population / fabric remnant**

👉 Example: The archaeologists discovered remnants of an ancient civilization buried beneath the desert sands.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The crumbling walls are the last **remnants** of a once-magnificent fortress that dominated the surrounding countryside for centuries.
   这些摇摇欲坠的城墙是一座曾经宏伟壮观、统治周围乡村数百年的堡垒的最后遗迹。

2. Scientists analyzed the **remnant** radiation from the early universe to better understand the origins of cosmic structure.
   科学家分析了早期宇宙的残余辐射，以更好地理解宇宙结构的起源。

3. Only a small **remnant** of the original forest ecosystem survives after decades of aggressive deforestation and urban expansion.
   经过数十年的大规模砍伐和城市扩张，原始森林生态系统仅剩下一小部分残余。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remnant" as "remain + ant" — imagine a tiny ant that remains behind after everyone else has left, the last survivor.
把"remnant"想成"remain + ant"——想象一只小蚂蚁在所有人离开后仍然留下，是最后的幸存者。
