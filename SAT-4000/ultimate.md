# 🏆 ultimate

🔊 /ˈʌltɪmət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Being the last or final element; the greatest or most extreme. (n.) The best achievable or most fundamental thing
**中文：** adj. 最终的；极限的；根本的 n. 终极；顶点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- final（最终的）
- supreme（至高的）
- paramount（最重要的）
- definitive（决定性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ultimate goal / ultimate authority / ultimate responsibility / the ultimate in**

👉 Example: The research team's ultimate objective was to develop a vaccine effective against all known variants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor argued that the **ultimate** purpose of education is to cultivate independent and critical thinking.
   这位教授认为，教育的终极目的在于培养独立的批判性思维。

2. After decades of research, scientists reached the **ultimate** conclusion that the theory was fundamentally flawed.
   经过数十年的研究，科学家们最终得出结论，认为该理论在根本上是有缺陷的。

3. This luxury resort is considered the **ultimate** in modern hospitality, offering amenities that surpass all competitors.
   这家豪华度假村被认为是现代酒店业的极致，提供超越所有竞争对手的设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ultimate" as "ulti-MATE" — your final and best mate, the one who stands at the very end.
把"ultimate"想成"ulti-MATE"（终极伙伴）——你最后也是最好的伙伴，站在最终点的那个人。
