# 🔄 equivalent

🔊 /ɪˈkwɪvələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Equal in value, function, or meaning. (n.) A person or thing that is equal to or corresponds with another
**中文：** adj. 等价的；相等的 n. 等价物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equal（相等的）
- comparable（可比较的）
- counterpart（对应物）
- tantamount（等同的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equivalent to / the equivalent of / roughly equivalent / moral equivalent**

👉 Example: One mile is roughly equivalent to 1.6 kilometers in the metric system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor explained that the foreign degree was fully **equivalent** to a domestic master's qualification for employment purposes.
   教授解释说，就就业目的而言，该外国学位完全等同于国内硕士学位。

2. Scientists discovered that the energy released by the eruption was **equivalent** to several hundred nuclear detonations occurring simultaneously.
   科学家发现，火山爆发释放的能量相当于数百次核爆同时发生。

3. The committee struggled to find an **equivalent** term in the target language that captured the full nuance of the original expression.
   委员会努力在目标语言中寻找一个能完全捕捉原始表达全部细微差别的等价词。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equi-valent" as "equal in value" — two things that hold the same worth or meaning.
把"equivalent"想成"equi（相等）+ val（价值）+ ent"——两个具有相同价值或意义的事物。
