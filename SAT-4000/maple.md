# 🍁 maple

🔊 /ˈmeɪpəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A deciduous tree of the genus Acer, known for its distinctive lobed leaves and valued for its timber and syrup; its pale brownish color
**中文：** n. 枫树；淡棕色

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sycamore（悬铃木）
- acer（槭属植物）
- hardwood（硬木）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maple syrup / maple leaf / maple wood / sugar maple / maple tree**

👉 Example: The maple trees along the avenue turned brilliant shades of red and gold every autumn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Canada's national flag prominently features a red **maple** leaf, symbolizing the country's vast northern forests and natural heritage.
   加拿大国旗上醒目地绘有一片红色枫叶，象征着该国广袤的北方森林和自然遗产。

2. The artisan crafted an elegant dining table from **maple** wood, prized for its fine grain and exceptional durability.
   工匠用枫木制作了一张精美的餐桌，枫木因其细密的纹理和卓越的耐用性而备受推崇。

3. Farmers in Vermont tap **maple** trees each spring to collect sap, which is then boiled down into premium syrup.
   佛蒙特州的农民每年春天都会采集枫树的树液，然后将其煮制成优质枫糖浆。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "maple" and picture the distinctive leaf on Canada's flag — the word even starts with "map," and the maple leaf is literally on the map of Canada's identity.
想到"maple"就想象加拿大国旗上那片独特的叶子——这个词甚至以"map"开头，而枫叶确实标注在加拿大身份的"地图"上。
