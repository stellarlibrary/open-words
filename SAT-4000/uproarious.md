# 📢 uproarious

🔊 /ʌpˈrɔːriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Characterized by or causing loud noise, commotion, or unrestrained laughter; extremely noisy or tumultuous
**中文：** adj. 骚动的；喧嚣的；令人捧腹大笑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tumultuous（喧闹的）
- boisterous（喧嚣的）
- riotous（骚乱的）
- clamorous（吵闹的）
- hilarious（令人捧腹的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **uproarious laughter / uproarious applause / uproarious crowd / uproarious comedy**

👉 Example: The comedian's satirical performance drew uproarious laughter from the entire audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The parliamentary debate descended into **uproarious** disorder when opposing factions began shouting accusations across the chamber floor.
   当对立派系开始隔着议会大厅互相高声指责时，议会辩论陷入了喧嚣的混乱。

2. The satirical novel received widespread acclaim for its **uproarious** humor and incisive commentary on contemporary political culture.
   这部讽刺小说因其令人捧腹的幽默和对当代政治文化的犀利评论而广受好评。

3. Witnesses described the protest as an **uproarious** demonstration that disrupted traffic and commerce for several consecutive hours.
   目击者将这场抗议描述为一场喧嚣的示威活动，连续数小时扰乱了交通和商业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "uproar-ious" — full of uproar. Where there is uproar, there is noise, chaos, and commotion everywhere.
把"uproarious"想成"充满喧嚣的"——uproar（喧嚣）+ ious（充满的），到处都是噪音、混乱和骚动。
