# 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 generation

🔊 /ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** All the people born and living at about the same time; the act or process of producing or creating something; a single stage in the descent of a family
**中文：** n. 一代人；世代；产生；发生；生殖；生育

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- age group（年龄组）
- cohort（同辈群体）
- production（产生）
- creation（创造）
- era（时代）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **younger generation / generation gap / power generation / next generation / generation of ideas**

👉 Example: The younger generation is increasingly concerned about climate change and environmental sustainability issues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each **generation** inherits both the achievements and the unresolved problems of those who came before, shaping society anew.
   每一代人都继承了前人的成就和未解决的问题，从而重新塑造社会。

2. The **generation** of renewable energy from solar and wind sources has increased dramatically over the past two decades worldwide.
   过去二十年来，全球太阳能和风能等可再生能源的发电量大幅增长。

3. Sociologists have noted a significant **generation** gap in attitudes toward technology adoption between older and younger demographic groups.
   社会学家注意到，在技术采用态度方面，年龄较大和较年轻的人口群体之间存在显著的代沟。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "generation" as "generate + tion" — the act of generating, whether generating offspring (a new generation of people) or generating energy and ideas.
把"generation"想成"generate + tion"——生成的行为，无论是生育后代（新一代人）还是产生能量和想法。
