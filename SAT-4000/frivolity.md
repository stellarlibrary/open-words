# 🎭 frivolity

🔊 /frɪˈvɒlɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lack of seriousness; lighthearted or trivial behavior; a frivolous act or thing
**中文：** 轻浮；轻率；轻薄；无聊的举动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- levity（轻率）
- flippancy（轻浮）
- triviality（琐碎）
- silliness（愚蠢）
- lightheartedness（无忧无虑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **engage in frivolity / moment of frivolity / dismiss as frivolity / youthful frivolity**

👉 Example: The professor had no patience for frivolity and expected students to maintain scholarly focus at all times.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board criticized the executive team for their **frivolity** in spending company funds on lavish and unnecessary retreats.
   董事会批评管理团队在奢华且不必要的度假活动上轻率地花费公司资金。

2. In times of national crisis, the public has little tolerance for **frivolity** among their elected political leaders.
   在国家危机时期，公众对其当选政治领导人的轻浮行为几乎毫无容忍。

3. She looked back on the **frivolity** of her youth with a mixture of fond nostalgia and mild embarrassment.
   她带着一种温馨怀旧和些许尴尬的复杂心情回顾自己年轻时的轻率行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frivolity" as the noun form of "frivolous" — it describes the state of being silly and not serious, like a party with no purpose.
把"frivolity"看作"frivolous（轻浮的）"的名词形式——它描述的是一种不严肃、无聊的状态，就像一场没有目的的派对。
