# 💢 acrimony

🔊 /ˈæk.rɪ.moʊ.ni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Bitterness or harshness of temper, manner, or speech; sharp hostility in words or behavior.
**中文：** 辛辣；尖刻；严厉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bitterness（苦涩）
- hostility（敌意）
- rancor（怨恨）
- animosity（仇恨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with acrimony**, **acrimony between (parties)**, **full of acrimony**, **bitter acrimony**

👉 Example: The divorce proceedings were conducted with considerable acrimony on both sides.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political debate was marked by intense **acrimony** that left both candidates unwilling to cooperate.
   这场政治辩论充满了激烈的尖刻言辞，使得双方候选人都不愿合作。

2. Years of unresolved **acrimony** between the two nations finally erupted into a formal diplomatic dispute.
   两国之间多年来悬而未决的积怨最终升级为正式的外交争端。

3. The board meeting dissolved into bitter **acrimony** when members disagreed sharply over the proposed budget cuts.
   当成员们对拟议的预算削减产生严重分歧时，董事会会议陷入了激烈的争吵。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ACRI-MONY" — "acri" resembles "acrid" (sharp, bitter), so acrimony is bitterness in human interactions.
"ACRI-MONY"——"acri"类似"acrid（尖刻的）"，所以acrimony就是人际交往中的尖酸刻薄，想象两人争吵时言辞如利刃般尖锐。
