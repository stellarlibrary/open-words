# 👁️ cataract

🔊 /ˈkætərækt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A medical condition in which the lens of the eye becomes cloudy, causing impaired vision; a large waterfall; a sudden downpour of rain
**中文：** n. 白内障；大瀑布；暴雨

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- waterfall（瀑布）
- cascade（瀑布）
- torrent（急流）
- deluge（暴雨）
- downpour（倾盆大雨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **develop a cataract / cataract surgery / a cataract of water / thundering cataract**

👉 Example: The physician recommended cataract surgery to restore the patient's deteriorating vision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ophthalmologist diagnosed a severe **cataract** in her left eye, recommending immediate surgical intervention to preserve her sight.
   眼科医生诊断出她左眼有严重的白内障，建议立即进行手术以保护视力。

2. Explorers stood in awe before the magnificent **cataract** that plunged three hundred feet into the ravine below.
   探险者们敬畏地站在壮观的大瀑布前，瀑布从三百英尺高处倾泻而下落入下方的峡谷。

3. A sudden **cataract** of rain overwhelmed the drainage system and caused widespread flooding throughout the district.
   突如其来的暴雨使排水系统不堪重负，导致整个地区发生大面积洪水。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cat-a-ract" as a cat reacting to a massive waterfall — water crashes down so hard it clouds everything, just like a cataract clouds the eye.
把"cataract"想成一只猫对着大瀑布反应——水猛烈冲下遮蔽了一切，就像白内障遮蔽了眼睛。
