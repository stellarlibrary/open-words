# 🌿 photosynthesis

🔊 /ˌfoʊtoʊˈsɪnθəsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The biological process by which green plants and certain organisms convert light energy, water, and carbon dioxide into glucose and oxygen
**中文：** n. 光合作用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carbon fixation（碳固定）
- light reaction（光反应）
- carbon assimilation（碳同化）
- autotrophic nutrition（自养营养）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rate of photosynthesis / photosynthesis process / photosynthesis occurs / undergo photosynthesis**

👉 Example: Without photosynthesis, most life on Earth would cease to exist within a matter of years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rate of **photosynthesis** increases significantly when plants are exposed to higher concentrations of carbon dioxide.
   当植物暴露在较高浓度的二氧化碳中时，光合作用的速率会显著增加。

2. Researchers discovered that certain deep-sea organisms can perform **photosynthesis** using geothermal light rather than sunlight.
   研究人员发现某些深海生物可以利用地热光而非阳光进行光合作用。

3. The biology textbook explained how **photosynthesis** converts solar energy into chemical energy stored within glucose molecules.
   这本生物学教科书解释了光合作用如何将太阳能转化为储存在葡萄糖分子中的化学能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "photo-synthesis" as "photo" (light) + "synthesis" (putting together) — plants put things together using light to make food.
把"photosynthesis"拆成"photo"（光）+"synthesis"（合成）——植物利用光把物质合成为食物。
