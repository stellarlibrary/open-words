# 🌅 reminiscent

🔊 /ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Tending to remind one of something; suggestive of a past experience; absorbed in recollections
**中文：** adj. 怀旧的，回忆往事的；令人联想的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- evocative（引起回忆的）
- suggestive（暗示的）
- nostalgic（怀旧的）
- redolent（令人想起的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reminiscent of / be reminiscent of a time / reminiscent tone / reminiscent mood**

👉 Example: The architecture of the old town is reminiscent of a bygone era of elegance and grandeur.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The melody of the symphony was **reminiscent** of the folk songs that villagers once sang during harvest celebrations.
   这首交响曲的旋律让人联想起村民们曾在丰收庆典上演唱的民歌。

2. Her writing style is distinctly **reminiscent** of the literary traditions prevalent in nineteenth-century European fiction.
   她的写作风格明显让人联想到十九世纪欧洲小说中流行的文学传统。

3. The warm amber light in the restaurant created an atmosphere **reminiscent** of intimate family gatherings from childhood.
   餐厅里温暖的琥珀色灯光营造出一种让人想起童年温馨家庭聚会的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reminiscent" as "remind + scent" — a scent that reminds you of the past, bringing old memories flooding back.
把"reminiscent"想成"remind + scent"——一种让你想起过去的气味，使旧日回忆涌上心头。
