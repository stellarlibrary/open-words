# 🔬 genotype

🔊 /ˈdʒɛnətaɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The genetic constitution of an individual organism, as distinguished from its physical appearance (phenotype)
**中文：** n. 基因型；遗传型

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genetic makeup（基因构成）
- genetic constitution（遗传组成）
- genetic profile（基因谱）
- hereditary composition（遗传成分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **genotype and phenotype / determine the genotype / genotype analysis / genotype frequency**

👉 Example: The researchers analyzed the genotype of each participant to identify markers associated with disease susceptibility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **genotype** of the plant determined its resistance to drought, even though its outward appearance closely resembled susceptible varieties.
   该植物的基因型决定了其抗旱性，尽管其外观与易感品种非常相似。

2. Advanced laboratory techniques now allow scientists to identify an organism's **genotype** with remarkable speed and unprecedented accuracy.
   先进的实验室技术现在使科学家能够以惊人的速度和前所未有的准确性识别生物体的基因型。

3. Understanding the relationship between **genotype** and phenotype remains one of the central challenges in contemporary molecular biology research.
   理解基因型和表现型之间的关系仍然是当代分子生物学研究的核心挑战之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "genotype" as "geno-type" — the "type" written in your "genes," the hidden genetic blueprint behind what you look like.
把"genotype"拆成"geno-type"——写在你"基因"里的"类型"，是你外貌背后隐藏的遗传蓝图。
