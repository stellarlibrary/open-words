# 📅 antedate

🔊 /ˌæn.tɪˈdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (v.) To precede in time; to assign a date earlier than the actual date. (n.) A date that is earlier than the actual date; a backdated date.

**中文：** (v.) 先于；把日期提前填写。(n.) 比实际提前的日期。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- predate（早于）
- precede（先于）
- backdate（倒填日期）
- foredate（提前标注日期）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antedate a document, antedate a check, antedate by several years, antedate the founding

**Example:** The archaeological findings suggest that this settlement **antedates** the Roman conquest by at least two centuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent excavations revealed that the fortress **antedates** the earliest written records of the region by several hundred years.
   最近的发掘表明，这座堡垒比该地区最早的文字记录还早几百年。

2. The accountant was dismissed for attempting to **antedate** financial documents to conceal irregularities in the ledger.
   那位会计因试图提前填写财务文件日期以掩盖账本中的违规行为而被解雇。

3. Scholars have confirmed that this manuscript **antedates** all other known copies of the philosophical treatise.
   学者们已确认这份手稿早于该哲学论著所有其他已知的副本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Ante-" (before) + "date" = a date placed **before** the real one — or something that comes before another date in time. 前缀 ante-（之前）+ date（日期）= 把日期写在实际日期**之前**，或在时间上早于某个日期。
