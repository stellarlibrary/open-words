# 🔍 perspicacity

🔊 /ˌpɜːrspɪˈkæsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of having a ready insight into things; shrewdness and keen mental perception
**中文：** n. 洞察力；聪颖；睿智

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acumen（敏锐）
- shrewdness（精明）
- discernment（辨别力）
- insight（洞察力）
- sagacity（睿智）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intellectual perspicacity / remarkable perspicacity / demonstrate perspicacity / lack of perspicacity**

👉 Example: The diplomat's perspicacity allowed her to navigate complex negotiations with exceptional skill.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **perspicacity** in identifying emerging research trends attracted talented graduate students from around the world.
   这位教授在识别新兴研究趋势方面的洞察力吸引了来自世界各地的优秀研究生。

2. With remarkable **perspicacity**, the analyst predicted the economic downturn several quarters before conventional indicators signaled any warning.
   凭借非凡的洞察力，这位分析师在常规指标发出任何警告之前几个季度就预测到了经济衰退。

3. The novel's protagonist relies on her innate **perspicacity** to unravel a conspiracy that has deceived an entire nation.
   小说的主人公依靠她天生的洞察力揭开了一个欺骗了整个国家的阴谋。

---

Think of "perspi-CACITY" — the "capacity" to see through (per-spic) things, a mental sharpness that penetrates to the core of any matter.
把"perspicacity"想成"perspi-CACITY"——"看穿"(per-spic)事物的"能力"，一种能穿透任何事物核心的精神敏锐度。
