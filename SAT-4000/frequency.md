# 📊 frequency

🔊 /ˈfriːkwənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The rate at which something occurs or is repeated over a particular period of time; the number of cycles per unit of time
**中文：** 频率；频繁；出现次数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regularity（规律性）
- recurrence（重复出现）
- rate（速率）
- prevalence（普遍性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **high frequency / low frequency / frequency of occurrence / radio frequency**

👉 Example: The frequency of extreme weather events has increased dramatically over the past two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers noted a significant increase in the **frequency** of cyberattacks targeting critical infrastructure across multiple nations.
   研究人员注意到针对多个国家关键基础设施的网络攻击频率显著增加。

2. The **frequency** of the radio waves determines whether they can penetrate solid barriers or reflect off atmospheric layers.
   无线电波的频率决定了它们是否能穿透固体障碍物或从大气层反射回来。

3. Public health officials monitored the **frequency** of reported cases to determine whether the outbreak was accelerating or subsiding.
   公共卫生官员监测了报告病例的频率，以确定疫情是在加速还是在减缓。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frequency" as "frequent" + "-cy" — it measures how frequent something happens, turning the adjective into a countable concept.
把"frequency"想成"frequent"（频繁的）+ "-cy"——它衡量某事发生的频率，将形容词变成可计量的概念。
