# 🧪 anhydrous

🔊 /ænˈhaɪdrəs/<br>
🏷️ *adj. containing no water | 形容词：无水的*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Containing no water, especially water of crystallization; completely free of moisture in a chemical context.
**中文：** 不含水的，尤其指不含结晶水的；在化学语境中完全无水分的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dehydrated（脱水的）
- desiccated（干燥的）
- waterless（无水的）
- dry（干的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **anhydrous compound, anhydrous form, anhydrous solvent, anhydrous conditions**
👉 Example: The experiment required anhydrous ethanol to prevent any unwanted side reactions with water.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory protocol strictly specified that only **anhydrous** solvents should be used throughout the synthesis procedure.
   实验室规程严格规定在整个合成过程中只能使用无水溶剂。

2. Researchers stored the **anhydrous** compound in sealed containers to prevent it from absorbing atmospheric moisture.
   研究人员将无水化合物存放在密封容器中，以防止其吸收大气中的水分。

3. Converting the hydrated salt into its **anhydrous** form required prolonged heating at precisely controlled temperatures.
   将含水盐转化为无水形式需要在精确控制的温度下长时间加热。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "an-" (without) + "hydr-" (water, as in hydrate) + "-ous" (full of) — literally "without water."
拆解记忆："an-"（无）+ "hydr-"（水，如hydrate水合物）+ "-ous"（充满的）——字面意思就是"无水的"。
