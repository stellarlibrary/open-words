# ⚠️ reprehensible

🔊 /ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deserving of censure or condemnation; morally wrong and blameworthy
**中文：** adj. 应斥责的；应该谴责的；应受责难的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deplorable（可悲的）
- disgraceful（可耻的）
- blameworthy（该受责备的）
- inexcusable（不可原谅的）
- contemptible（可鄙的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reprehensible behavior / reprehensible conduct / morally reprehensible / reprehensible act**

👉 Example: The court ruled that the defendant's conduct was morally reprehensible and warranted severe punishment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist exposed the **reprehensible** practices of corporations that deliberately concealed toxic waste from regulatory authorities.
   记者揭露了企业故意向监管机构隐瞒有毒废物的应受谴责的行为。

2. Society must hold individuals accountable for **reprehensible** actions that cause widespread suffering among vulnerable populations.
   社会必须追究那些给弱势群体造成广泛痛苦的应受谴责行为的责任。

3. The ambassador condemned the **reprehensible** treatment of political prisoners, demanding their immediate and unconditional release.
   大使谴责了对政治犯应受谴责的对待方式，要求立即无条件释放他们。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reprehens-ible" as something that can be "reprehended" (scolded) — if behavior is reprehensible, it deserves to be called out and condemned.
把"reprehensible"想成可以被"reprehend"（斥责）的事物——如果行为是reprehensible的，它就应该被指出和谴责。
