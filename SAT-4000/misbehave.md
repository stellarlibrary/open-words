# 😈 misbehave

🔊 /ˌmɪsbɪˈheɪv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To behave badly or improperly; to act in a way that is rude, inappropriate, or contrary to accepted standards
**中文：** v. 行为不端；行为不礼貌；作弊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- act up（捣乱）
- transgress（违反）
- offend（冒犯）
- disobey（不服从）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **misbehave in class / misbehave badly / children misbehave / tend to misbehave**

👉 Example: Students who consistently misbehave in class may face disciplinary action from school authorities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The teacher warned that any student who continued to **misbehave** during the examination would be immediately removed from the room.
   老师警告说，任何在考试期间继续行为不端的学生将立即被带离考场。

2. Children are more likely to **misbehave** when they feel neglected or lack consistent boundaries and parental guidance at home.
   当孩子感到被忽视或在家中缺乏一致的行为规范和父母引导时，更容易行为不端。

3. The diplomat was recalled after reports surfaced that he had **misbehaved** at an official state reception hosted by the embassy.
   在有报道称他在大使馆举办的官方国宴上行为失礼后，这位外交官被召回。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-behave" — "mis" means wrongly, and "behave" means to act. So misbehave literally means to act wrongly or badly.
把"misbehave"拆成"mis（错误地）+ behave（行为）"——字面意思就是行为不当、举止失礼。
