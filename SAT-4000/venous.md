# 🩸 venous

🔊 /ˈviːnəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or contained in veins; having prominent veins or vein-like markings
**中文：** adj. 静脉的；有脉纹的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vascular（血管的）
- veined（有脉纹的）
- phlebotic（静脉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **venous blood / venous system / venous return / venous pressure**

👉 Example: The physician measured the patient's venous pressure to assess cardiac function.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon carefully ligated the damaged **venous** vessel to prevent further internal hemorrhaging during the operation.
   外科医生仔细结扎了受损的静脉血管，以防止手术中进一步的内部出血。

2. Prolonged immobility during long flights can impair **venous** return and significantly increase the risk of thrombosis.
   长途飞行中长时间不动会影响静脉回流，并显著增加血栓形成的风险。

3. The biologist noted the intricate **venous** patterns on the leaf, which facilitated efficient nutrient distribution throughout.
   生物学家注意到叶片上复杂的脉纹图案，这促进了养分在整个叶片中的高效分配。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "venous" as coming from "vein" plus "-ous" — simply meaning "related to veins," just like "nervous" relates to nerves.
把"venous"想成"vein"（静脉）加"-ous"后缀——就像"nervous"与nerve有关一样，"venous"就是与静脉有关的。
