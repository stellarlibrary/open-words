# 🎀 velvety

🔊 /ˈvelvɪti/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having the soft, smooth texture of velvet; pleasantly smooth to the taste or senses
**中文：** adj. 天鹅绒般柔软的；醇和的，可口的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- silky（丝般柔滑的）
- smooth（光滑的）
- soft（柔软的）
- plush（毛绒般的）
- sleek（光亮柔滑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **velvety texture / velvety skin / velvety voice / velvety smooth / velvety petals**

👉 Example: The sommelier described the aged wine as having a velvety texture with rich undertones of dark fruit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanist noted that the plant's leaves had a distinctly **velvety** surface, which helped reduce water loss in arid environments.
   植物学家注意到该植物的叶子有明显的天鹅绒般的表面，这有助于在干旱环境中减少水分流失。

2. The chef prepared a **velvety** bisque from roasted butternut squash, balancing its natural sweetness with subtle aromatic spices.
   厨师用烤奶油南瓜制作了一道口感醇滑的浓汤，用微妙的香料平衡了其天然甜味。

3. The singer's **velvety** voice captivated the audience throughout the evening performance at the grand concert hall.
   这位歌手天鹅绒般柔美的嗓音在整晚的音乐大厅演出中令观众着迷。
