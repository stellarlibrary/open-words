# 🌿 artless

🔊 /ˈɑːrtləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Without guile or deception; naturally simple and sincere; lacking artificiality or pretense.

**中文：** 天真的；朴实的；无虚饰的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- naive 天真的
- ingenuous 单纯的
- guileless 坦率的
- unaffected 不做作的
- sincere 真诚的

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Artless" is often a compliment meaning naturally honest and unpretentious. However, it can occasionally imply a lack of sophistication. It is the opposite of "artful," which suggests cunning or skillful deception.

"artless" 通常是一个褒义词，意为自然诚实、不矫揉造作。但有时也可暗示缺乏老练。它是 "artful"（狡猾的）的反义词。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The child gave an **artless** account of the incident, describing everything with pure honesty and innocence.
   这个孩子对事件做了天真的叙述，以纯粹的诚实和天真描述了一切。

2. Her **artless** charm won the hearts of everyone at the gathering without any deliberate effort.
   她朴实无华的魅力不费任何刻意努力便赢得了聚会上每个人的心。

3. The poet's **artless** style conveyed deep emotion through simple words that resonated with ordinary readers.
   这位诗人朴素的风格通过简单的词语传达了深厚的情感，引起了普通读者的共鸣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Art" + "-less" (without) — without art or artifice, meaning natural and sincere, without any tricks.

"Art"（技巧/伪装）+ "-less"（没有）——没有技巧或伪装，即天真自然、毫无诡计。
