# 🌙 nocturnal

🔊 /nɒkˈtɜːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or occurring at night; active during the nighttime rather than during the day
**中文：** adj. 夜的；夜间发生的；夜间活动的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nighttime（夜间的）
- nightly（每夜的）
- night-active（夜行性的）
- crepuscular（黄昏活动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nocturnal animals / nocturnal habits / nocturnal activities / nocturnal emissions**

👉 Example: Owls are nocturnal predators that rely heavily on their exceptional hearing to hunt in darkness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many **nocturnal** creatures have evolved highly sensitive eyes that allow them to navigate in near-total darkness.
   许多夜行动物进化出了高度敏感的眼睛，使它们能在几乎完全黑暗的环境中活动。

2. The researcher documented the **nocturnal** behavior of bats using infrared cameras positioned throughout the cave system.
   研究人员使用布置在整个洞穴系统中的红外摄像机记录了蝙蝠的夜间行为。

3. His **nocturnal** study habits concerned his parents, who worried that insufficient sleep would impair his academic performance.
   他夜间学习的习惯让父母担忧，他们担心睡眠不足会影响他的学业表现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "noct-urnal" — "noct" sounds like "night," so nocturnal means everything related to the night.
把"nocturnal"中的"noct"联想到"night（夜晚）"——nocturnal就是与夜晚有关的一切。
