# 🎵 melodious

🔊 /məˈloʊdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a pleasant, tuneful quality; producing or characterized by melody; sweet-sounding
**中文：** adj. 旋律优美的；悦耳的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tuneful（悦耳的）
- harmonious（和谐的）
- musical（音乐般的）
- mellifluous（甜美流畅的）
- dulcet（柔和悦耳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **melodious voice / melodious sound / melodious tune / melodious singing**

👉 Example: The melodious chanting of the choir echoed through the ancient stone cathedral.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bird's **melodious** song at dawn served as a natural alarm clock for the residents of the rural village.
   这只鸟在黎明时悦耳的歌声成了这个乡村居民的天然闹钟。

2. Her **melodious** reading of the poem transformed the simple words into an unforgettable auditory experience for all present.
   她对这首诗悦耳动听的朗读将简单的文字变成了在场所有人难忘的听觉体验。

3. The ensemble produced a rich, **melodious** arrangement that blended traditional instruments with modern electronic effects.
   这个乐团演奏了一段丰富而旋律优美的编曲，将传统乐器与现代电子音效融为一体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "melodious" as "melody + ous(full of)" — full of melody, overflowing with pleasant musical sounds.
把"melodious"想成"melody（旋律）+ ous（充满）"——充满旋律的，溢满悦耳乐声的。
