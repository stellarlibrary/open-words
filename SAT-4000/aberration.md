# 🔀 aberration

🔊 /ˌæbəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A departure from what is normal or expected; a deviation from the proper course; a mental or moral lapse
**中文：** n. 失常，反常；偏差；越轨，脱轨

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- anomaly（异常）
- deviation（偏差）
- irregularity（不规则）
- oddity（怪异）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mental aberration / statistical aberration / a temporary aberration / chromosomal aberration**

👉 Example: The committee dismissed the incident as a temporary aberration rather than a systemic failure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists attributed the unusual temperature readings to a statistical **aberration** rather than actual climate change.
   科学家将异常的温度读数归因于统计偏差，而非实际的气候变化。

2. His violent outburst was considered a momentary **aberration** in an otherwise calm and rational temperament.
   他的暴力爆发被认为是其一贯冷静理性性格中的一时失常。

3. The election result was widely regarded as a political **aberration** that defied all forecasts and predictions.
   这一选举结果被普遍视为一种违背所有预测和预期的政治反常现象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aberration" contains "err" — when something goes into aberration, it errs from the normal path.
"Aberration"中包含"err"（犯错）——当事物出现aberration时，就是偏离了正常轨道。
