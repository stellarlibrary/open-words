# 🤝 sociable

🔊 /ˈsoʊʃəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Fond of socializing; friendly and pleasant in company. (n.) A social gathering
**中文：** adj. 好交际的，友善的；喜欢群居的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gregarious（爱交际的）
- affable（和蔼可亲的）
- convivial（欢乐的）
- outgoing（外向的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sociable person / sociable atmosphere / sociable nature / sociable gathering**

👉 Example: Her sociable nature made her the ideal candidate to lead the community outreach program.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new employee proved to be highly **sociable**, quickly forming productive relationships with colleagues across departments.
   这位新员工非常善于交际，很快就与各部门的同事建立了高效的工作关系。

2. Researchers found that individuals who are naturally **sociable** tend to report higher levels of personal well-being.
   研究人员发现，天生好交际的人往往报告更高水平的个人幸福感。

3. The conference created a **sociable** environment that encouraged scholars to exchange ideas and collaborate on future projects.
   会议营造了一种友好社交的氛围，鼓励学者们交流思想并合作未来的项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sociable" as "social-able" — someone who is able and willing to be social, friendly with others.
把"sociable"想成"social-able"——一个有能力且愿意社交的人，对他人友善。
