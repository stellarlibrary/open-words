# ⚡ speed

🔊 /spiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To move quickly; to exceed the legal speed limit; to accelerate. (n.) The rate at which something moves or operates; rapidity; prosperity.
**中文：** n. 速率

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- velocity（速度）
- pace（步伐）
- swiftness（迅速）
- rapidity（快速）
- haste（急速）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **at high speed / speed up / speed limit / pick up speed / full speed ahead**

👉 Example: The train gradually picked up speed as it left the station and entered the open countryside.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The advancement of technology has dramatically increased the **speed** at which information travels across global networks.
   技术的进步极大地提高了信息在全球网络中传播的速率。

2. Emergency responders must **speed** to the scene of an accident without compromising the safety of other motorists.
   急救人员必须迅速赶到事故现场，同时不危及其他驾驶者的安全。

3. The research team worked at remarkable **speed** to develop a viable vaccine before the epidemic spread further.
   研究团队以惊人的速度工作，力求在疫情进一步扩散前开发出可行的疫苗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "speed" as the needle on a speedometer — the higher it goes, the faster you move through space and time.
把"speed"想象成速度表上的指针——指针越高，你在时空中移动得越快。
