# 🏠 accommodate

🔊 /əˈkɒmədeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To provide space or lodging for; to adapt or adjust to; to supply or fulfill a need; to reconcile differences.
**中文：** 容纳；使适应；供应，提供；调解，调和。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- house（容纳）
- adapt（适应）
- oblige（迁就）
- cater to（迎合）
- reconcile（调和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accommodate + guests/needs/requests/differences**

👉 Example: The hotel can accommodate up to five hundred guests at a time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new auditorium was designed to **accommodate** over two thousand attendees for major conferences.
   新的礼堂设计可容纳两千多名与会者参加大型会议。

2. The university revised its policies to **accommodate** the diverse learning needs of all enrolled students.
   大学修订了政策，以适应所有在校学生的多样化学习需求。

3. Skilled diplomats must learn to **accommodate** conflicting viewpoints to reach a workable consensus.
   熟练的外交官必须学会调和相互冲突的观点，以达成可行的共识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accommodate" has double C and double M — it is so spacious it accommodates extra letters!
accommodate 有两个 c 和两个 m——它如此"宽敞"，连额外的字母都能容纳！
