# 📢 reproof

🔊 /rɪˈpruːf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An expression of blame or disapproval; a formal or official censure directed at someone for a fault or offense
**中文：** 责备；谴责；非难

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rebuke（斥责）
- reprimand（训斥）
- censure（谴责）
- admonition（告诫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a stern reproof / a gentle reproof / words of reproof / administer a reproof**

👉 Example: The teacher delivered a stern reproof to the student who had disrupted the examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge issued a sharp **reproof** to the attorney for repeatedly interrupting the witness during cross-examination.
   法官对那位在交叉询问中反复打断证人的律师发出了严厉的谴责。

2. Her quiet **reproof** carried more weight than any loud rebuke could have, silencing the entire committee room.
   她平静的责备比任何大声斥责都更有分量，让整个委员会会议室鸦雀无声。

3. The editorial served as a public **reproof** of the administration's failure to address the growing environmental crisis.
   这篇社论是对政府未能应对日益严重的环境危机的公开谴责。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reproof" as "re-proof" — someone demands proof again because they disapprove of what you did, giving you a scolding.
把"reproof"想成"re-proof"——有人因为不满你的所作所为而要求你再次证明，对你进行责备。
