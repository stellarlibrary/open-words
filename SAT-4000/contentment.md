# 😊 contentment

🔊 /kənˈtɛntmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of happiness and satisfaction; peaceful ease of mind derived from having one's desires fulfilled
**中文：** n. 满足；满意

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- satisfaction（满意）
- fulfillment（充实感）
- serenity（宁静）
- gratification（满足）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a sense of contentment / deep contentment / find contentment / contentment with / quiet contentment**

👉 Example: She found lasting contentment not in material wealth but in meaningful relationships.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The survey revealed that **contentment** among employees increased significantly after the company introduced flexible working arrangements.
   调查显示，公司引入灵活工作安排后，员工的满意度显著提高。

2. Philosophers have long debated whether true **contentment** arises from the fulfillment of desires or from their elimination.
   哲学家们长期以来一直在争论真正的满足感是来自欲望的实现还是消除。

3. After decades of relentless ambition, he finally discovered a profound sense of **contentment** in the simplicity of rural life.
   经过数十年不懈的追求，他终于在乡村生活的简朴中发现了深深的满足感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "CONTENT-ment" — when you feel "content" with what you have, that state of being is your contentment.
把"contentment"想成"content（满意的）+ ment（状态）"——对自己所拥有的感到满意的状态就是满足。
