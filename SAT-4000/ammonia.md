# 🧪 ammonia

🔊 /əˈmoʊniə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, pungent gas (NH₃) that is highly soluble in water, used in fertilizers and cleaning products.
**中文：** 氨，阿摩尼亚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- azane（氮烷）
- NH₃（氨分子式）
- ammonium hydroxide（氨水，溶液形式）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **levels, solution, gas, exposure, production, fumes, compounds**
👉 Example: The laboratory detected elevated ammonia levels in the water samples.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Industrial plants produce vast quantities of **ammonia** annually for use in agricultural fertilizers worldwide.
   工业工厂每年生产大量氨，用于全球的农业化肥。

2. Prolonged exposure to **ammonia** fumes can cause severe irritation to the respiratory tract and mucous membranes.
   长时间接触氨气可导致呼吸道和黏膜严重刺激。

3. The chemist carefully diluted the concentrated **ammonia** solution before applying it to the experimental apparatus.
   化学家在将浓缩氨溶液应用于实验装置之前，仔细地进行了稀释。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ammonia" is named after the Temple of Ammon in Egypt, where ammonium salts were once collected from camel dung nearby.
"Ammonia"得名于埃及的阿蒙神庙，那里曾从附近的骆驼粪便中收集铵盐。
