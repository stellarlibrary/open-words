# 📢 clamorous

🔊 /ˈklæmərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Making a loud and confused noise; expressing vehement protests or demands
**中文：** adj. 吵闹的；大声要求的；喧哗的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vociferous（大声叫喊的）
- boisterous（喧闹的）
- uproarious（喧嚣的）
- strident（刺耳的）
- tumultuous（吵闹的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clamorous crowd / clamorous demands / clamorous protest / clamorous voices**

👉 Example: The clamorous demands of the shareholders forced the board to reconsider the merger proposal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **clamorous** crowd outside the courthouse demanded immediate justice for the victims of corporate negligence.
   法院外喧闹的人群要求对企业疏忽的受害者立即伸张正义。

2. Despite the **clamorous** objections raised by opposition members, the legislature proceeded to pass the controversial bill.
   尽管反对派成员提出了强烈抗议，立法机关仍继续通过了这项有争议的法案。

3. The **clamorous** reception that greeted the ambassador revealed the depth of public discontent with foreign policy decisions.
   迎接大使时的喧哗反应揭示了公众对外交政策决定的深层不满。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clamor-ous" — full of clamor, like a noisy clam snapping its shell loudly and repeatedly.
想象"clamorous"——充满喧嚣，就像一只蛤蜊大声反复地啪啪合壳一样吵闹。
