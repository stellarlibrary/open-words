# 🍰 portion

🔊 /ˈpɔːrʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A part of a whole; an amount of food served to one person; one's fate or destiny. (v.) To divide or distribute something into shares
**中文：** n. 部分；一份；命运 v. 分配；给…嫁妆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- segment（部分）
- share（份额）
- allotment（分配量）
- fraction（一小部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a portion of / portion out / generous portion / equal portions**

👉 Example: The committee decided to portion out the remaining funds equally among all eligible departments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A significant **portion** of the annual budget was allocated to scientific research and technological development initiatives.
   年度预算的很大一部分被分配给了科学研究和技术开发项目。

2. The professor asked each student to analyze a different **portion** of the extensive historical document for the seminar.
   教授要求每位学生为研讨会分析这份大量历史文献的不同部分。

3. The organization chose to **portion** the donated supplies among the communities most severely affected by the disaster.
   该组织选择将捐赠物资分配给受灾最严重的社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "portion" as "port-ion" — at a port, goods are divided into portions for different ships heading to different destinations.
把"portion"想成"port-ion"——在港口，货物被分成不同的份额装上不同的船驶向不同的目的地。
