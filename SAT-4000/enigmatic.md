# 🔮 enigmatic

🔊 /ˌenɪɡˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Difficult to interpret or understand; mysterious and puzzling
**中文：** adj. 神秘的；高深莫测的；谜一般的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mysterious（神秘的）
- cryptic（隐晦的）
- inscrutable（高深莫测的）
- puzzling（令人困惑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enigmatic smile / enigmatic figure / enigmatic expression / enigmatic personality**

👉 Example: The professor's enigmatic remarks left the students pondering their deeper significance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **enigmatic** manuscript discovered in the ancient library has baffled scholars and linguists for decades.
   在古老图书馆中发现的这份神秘手稿令学者和语言学家困惑了数十年。

2. Her **enigmatic** smile during the diplomatic negotiations suggested she possessed crucial information others lacked.
   她在外交谈判中神秘的微笑暗示她掌握着他人所缺乏的关键信息。

3. The artist cultivated an **enigmatic** public persona that fueled intense speculation about his private life.
   这位艺术家塑造了一个高深莫测的公众形象，引发了人们对其私生活的强烈猜测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "enigmatic" as containing "enigma" — an enigma is a riddle or puzzle, so enigmatic means mysterious like an unsolved riddle.
把"enigmatic"想成包含"enigma（谜）"——谜是未解的难题，所以enigmatic就是像未解之谜一样神秘莫测。
