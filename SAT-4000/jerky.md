# 🤸 jerky

🔊 /ˈdʒɜːrki/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Characterized by abrupt, sudden movements; not smooth or steady. (n.) Lean meat that has been dried and preserved
**中文：** adj. 急动的；不平稳的；抽筋的 n. 牛肉干

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spasmodic（痉挛性的）
- jolting（颠簸的）
- uneven（不平稳的）
- fitful（断断续续的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jerky movements / jerky motion / jerky ride / beef jerky**

👉 Example: The old elevator ascended with jerky, unsettling movements that alarmed every passenger inside.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The robot's **jerky** movements revealed significant flaws in its motor calibration that the engineering team needed to address.
   机器人急动不稳的动作暴露了其电机校准中的重大缺陷，工程团队需要加以解决。

2. Passengers on the aging bus endured a **jerky** ride along the unpaved mountain road, clutching their seats for stability.
   老旧公共汽车上的乘客在未铺设的山路上忍受着颠簸不平的行程，紧紧抓住座位以保持稳定。

3. The amateur filmmaker's footage appeared **jerky** and disorienting because he had not used any form of camera stabilization equipment.
   这位业余电影制作人的镜头显得急动且令人眩晕，因为他没有使用任何相机稳定设备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jerky" as coming from "jerk" — a sudden, sharp pull. Something jerky moves in quick, abrupt jerks rather than flowing smoothly.
把"jerky"和"jerk"（猛拉）联系起来——jerky的东西以急促、突然的抽动方式移动，而不是流畅地运动。
