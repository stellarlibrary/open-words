# 💧 perspire

🔊 /pərˈspaɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To secrete sweat through the pores of the skin; to exude moisture
**中文：** v. 流汗；分泌；渗出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sweat（出汗）
- exude（渗出）
- secrete（分泌）
- transpire（蒸发排出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perspire heavily / perspire profusely / perspire under pressure / begin to perspire**

👉 Example: The nervous candidate began to perspire visibly during the intense cross-examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The athletes began to **perspire** profusely within the first few minutes of the grueling endurance training session.
   运动员们在艰苦的耐力训练开始最初几分钟内就开始大量出汗。

2. Witnesses noted that the defendant started to **perspire** noticeably when the prosecutor presented the critical evidence.
   证人注意到，当检察官出示关键证据时，被告开始明显地流汗。

3. Working in tropical climates causes laboratory technicians to **perspire** constantly, which can compromise sterile procedures.
   在热带气候中工作会使实验室技术人员不断出汗，这可能会影响无菌操作规程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-spire" as "per (through) + spire (breathe)" — your skin breathes through its pores and releases sweat.
把"perspire"想成"per（通过）+ spire（呼吸）"——你的皮肤通过毛孔呼吸并释放汗水。
