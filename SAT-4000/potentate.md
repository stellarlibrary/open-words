# 👑 potentate

🔊 /ˈpoʊtənˌteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A monarch or ruler, especially an autocratic one; a person who possesses great power or authority
**中文：** n. 统治者，君主；有权势的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- monarch（君主）
- sovereign（统治者）
- ruler（统治者）
- despot（专制君主）
- autocrat（独裁者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **foreign potentate / powerful potentate / ancient potentate / local potentate**

👉 Example: The foreign potentate arrived with an elaborate entourage of advisors and diplomats.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medieval **potentate** exercised absolute authority over the vast territories under his dominion without any legislative oversight.
   这位中世纪的统治者对其管辖下的广大领土行使绝对权力，没有任何立法监督。

2. Historians debate whether the ancient **potentate** was a benevolent leader or a ruthless tyrant who suppressed all dissent.
   历史学家争论这位古代君主是一位仁慈的领导者还是一位镇压一切异议的残暴暴君。

3. The visiting **potentate** signed a landmark treaty that would reshape diplomatic relations between the two neighboring nations.
   来访的君主签署了一项具有里程碑意义的条约，将重塑两个邻国之间的外交关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "potentate" as "potent + ate" — a person who has "eaten" or consumed all the power, becoming the supreme ruler.
把"potentate"想成"potent（强有力的）+ ate（吃掉）"——一个"吃掉"了所有权力的人，成为最高统治者。
