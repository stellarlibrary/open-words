# 🔒 resistant

🔊 /rɪˈzɪstənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Offering resistance to something; not affected or harmed by something; unwilling to accept or be changed by something
**中文：** adj. 抵抗的，有抵抗力的；顽固的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impervious（不受影响的）
- immune（免疫的）
- defiant（抵抗的）
- unyielding（不屈的）
- obstinate（顽固的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resistant to change / heat-resistant / water-resistant / resistant to treatment / drug-resistant**

👉 Example: The new coating makes the surface resistant to scratches and chemical corrosion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Several strains of the virus have become increasingly **resistant** to conventional treatments, prompting researchers to develop alternative therapeutic approaches.
   该病毒的几种毒株对传统治疗方法的抵抗力越来越强，促使研究人员开发替代治疗方案。

2. The management team remained **resistant** to implementing organizational reforms despite compelling evidence that the current structure was inefficient.
   尽管有充分证据表明现有结构效率低下，管理团队仍然抵制实施组织改革。

3. Scientists engineered a crop variety that is naturally **resistant** to common pests, thereby reducing the need for chemical pesticides.
   科学家培育出一种对常见害虫具有天然抵抗力的作物品种，从而减少了对化学农药的需求。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "resistant" as "resist-ant" — picture an ant that resists being stepped on, always surviving and fighting back.
把"resistant"想成"resist-ant"——想象一只抵抗被踩踏的蚂蚁，总是顽强生存、奋力反抗。
