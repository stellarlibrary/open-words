# 😤 discountenance

🔊 /dɪsˈkaʊntənəns/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To show disapproval of; to refuse to approve or support; to cause embarrassment or abashment
**中文：** v. 使丢脸；不赞成

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disapprove（不赞成）
- discourage（阻止）
- frown upon（皱眉反对）
- disfavor（不支持）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **discountenance a proposal / discountenance such behavior / discountenance the idea / officially discountenance**

👉 Example: The board chose to discountenance the proposal, citing its potential to undermine the organization's core mission.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dean publicly **discountenanced** the students' disruptive protest, stating that such conduct violated the university's code of behavior.
   院长公开表示不赞成学生的破坏性抗议，称这种行为违反了大学的行为准则。

2. Religious authorities moved to **discountenance** the heretical teachings that had begun spreading rapidly among the congregation's younger members.
   宗教当局采取行动反对那些在会众年轻成员中迅速传播的异端学说。

3. The committee voted unanimously to **discountenance** the use of unverified data in official government reports and policy recommendations.
   委员会一致投票反对在官方政府报告和政策建议中使用未经核实的数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-countenance" — "countenance" means face or approval, so "discountenance" means to take away one's face (approval), to show disapproval.
把"discountenance"拆成"dis-countenance"——"countenance"意为面容或赞同，加上"dis"就是不给面子、不赞成。
