# 😶 stupor

🔊 /ˈstjuːpər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of near-unconsciousness or daze; numbness or lethargy
**中文：** n. 昏迷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- daze（恍惚）
- torpor（麻木）
- lethargy（昏睡）
- oblivion（无意识状态）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in a stupor / drunken stupor / fall into a stupor / emerge from a stupor**

👉 Example: After the accident, the patient lay in a stupor for several hours before regaining full consciousness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The intense heat of the afternoon left the entire audience in a drowsy **stupor** during the lengthy outdoor ceremony.
   午后的酷热使全体观众在漫长的户外仪式中陷入昏昏欲睡的恍惚状态。

2. He emerged from his grief-induced **stupor** only after his closest friends intervened and offered their unwavering support.
   在最亲密的朋友介入并给予坚定支持后，他才从悲痛引起的恍惚中走出来。

3. The medication induced a temporary **stupor** that prevented the patient from responding coherently to any medical inquiries.
   药物引起了暂时的昏迷状态，使患者无法对任何医学询问做出清晰的回应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stupor" as "stupid + or" — when you are in a stupor, your mind feels so dull that you cannot think or react properly.
把"stupor"想成"stupid + or"——当你处于昏迷状态时，大脑迟钝得无法正常思考或反应。
