# 🚫 censor

🔊 /ˈsensər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To examine and suppress or delete content considered objectionable, harmful, or politically unacceptable. (n.) An official who examines material and removes unacceptable parts
**中文：** v. 审查，检查（并删除不当内容） n. 审查员，检查员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- suppress（压制）
- expurgate（删除不当内容）
- redact（编辑删除）
- bowdlerize（删改）
- ban（禁止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **censor the press / censor information / government censor / censor content / heavily censored**

👉 Example: The authoritarian regime attempted to censor all media reports that criticized government policies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government moved swiftly to **censor** online publications that contained information deemed threatening to national security interests.
   政府迅速采取行动审查包含被认为威胁国家安全利益信息的在线出版物。

2. During wartime, military **censors** routinely examined soldiers' letters to ensure that no sensitive strategic details were inadvertently disclosed.
   战时，军事审查员定期检查士兵的信件，以确保不会无意中泄露敏感的战略细节。

3. Many intellectuals argued that the decision to **censor** the controversial documentary represented a dangerous precedent for freedom of expression.
   许多知识分子认为，审查这部有争议纪录片的决定为言论自由开创了一个危险的先例。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "censor" as "cen-SOR" — a "sore" spot for free speech, because censors cut out anything that might be a sore topic for those in power.
把"censor"想成"cen-SOR"——言论自由的"sore（痛处）"，因为审查员会删除任何可能是当权者痛处的话题。
