# 🔬 concentration

🔊 /ˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The amount of a substance in a given volume; the act of focusing one's attention or mental effort; the process of making something more concentrated
**中文：** n. 浓度；集中；浓缩；专注

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- focus（专注）
- density（浓度）
- intensity（强度）
- attentiveness（注意力）
- convergence（集中）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concentration of / high concentration / lose concentration / concentration level / require concentration**

👉 Example: Laboratory analysis revealed a dangerously high concentration of lead in the water supply.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **concentration** of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere has risen significantly over the past century due to industrial emissions.
   由于工业排放，过去一个世纪大气中二氧化碳的浓度显著上升。

2. Maintaining deep **concentration** during lengthy examinations is essential for achieving optimal academic performance.
   在漫长的考试中保持高度专注对于取得最佳学业成绩至关重要。

3. Scientists measured the **concentration** of dissolved minerals in the sample to determine the water's suitability for irrigation.
   科学家测量了样本中溶解矿物质的浓度，以确定该水是否适合灌溉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con (together) + centr (center) + ation" — bringing everything together to one center point, whether it's attention or chemical particles.
把"concentration"想成"con（一起）+ centr（中心）+ ation"——把一切汇聚到一个中心点，无论是注意力还是化学粒子。
