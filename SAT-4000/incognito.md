# 🕵️ incognito

🔊 /ˌɪnkɒɡˈniːtoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb, noun | 形容词, 副词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj./adv.) Having one's true identity concealed; under an assumed name or in disguise. (n.) A person whose identity is disguised or concealed.
**中文：** adj. 隐姓埋名的 adv. 隐姓埋名地 n. 匿名者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- anonymous（匿名的）
- disguised（伪装的）
- undercover（秘密的）
- unidentified（身份不明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **travel incognito / remain incognito / go incognito / incognito mode**

👉 Example: The celebrity traveled incognito to avoid being recognized by fans at the airport.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned author attended the literary festival **incognito**, wearing a hat and sunglasses to avoid attracting public attention.
   这位著名作家隐姓埋名地参加了文学节，戴着帽子和太阳镜以避免引起公众注意。

2. Government inspectors sometimes visit regulated facilities **incognito** to obtain a more accurate assessment of compliance standards.
   政府检查员有时会隐姓埋名地访问受监管设施，以更准确地评估合规标准。

3. The diplomat chose to remain **incognito** during the sensitive negotiations to prevent media interference with the proceedings.
   这位外交官在敏感的谈判期间选择保持匿名，以防止媒体干扰谈判进程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-COG-nito" — "cog" relates to "cognition" (knowing). Incognito means "not known" — your identity is hidden from recognition.
把"incognito"想成"in-COG-nito"——"cog"与"cognition（认知）"有关。Incognito意为"不被知道"——你的身份被隐藏，不被认出。
