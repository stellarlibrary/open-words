# 😨 recreant

🔊 /ˈrekrɪənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A coward or traitor. (adj.) Cowardly; unfaithful or disloyal
**中文：** n. 懦夫；叛徒 adj. 胆小的；怯懦的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coward（懦夫）
- traitor（叛徒）
- deserter（逃兵）
- craven（胆小鬼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recreant knight / recreant behavior / a recreant soldier / prove recreant**

👉 Example: The recreant soldier who deserted his comrades was dishonorably discharged from the military.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. History remembers the **recreant** official who betrayed his country's secrets to the enemy during the prolonged conflict.
   历史铭记了那位在漫长冲突中将国家机密出卖给敌人的叛徒官员。

2. The once-celebrated general proved **recreant** when he abandoned his troops at the critical moment of the decisive battle.
   那位曾经备受赞誉的将军在决战的关键时刻抛弃了部队，暴露了其怯懦本性。

3. Medieval literature often contrasts the virtuous hero with the **recreant** knight who forsakes his sacred oath of loyalty.
   中世纪文学经常将品德高尚的英雄与背弃神圣忠诚誓言的懦弱骑士形成对比。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "recreant" as "retreat-ant" — someone who is always ready to retreat like an ant scurrying away from danger.
把"recreant"联想为"retreat-ant"——一个总是准备像蚂蚁一样仓皇逃跑的人，即懦夫。
