# 📐 ordinate

🔊 /ˈɔːrdɪnət/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The second coordinate in a system of coordinates, representing the vertical distance of a point from the horizontal axis (the y-value in a Cartesian plane)
**中文：** n. 纵坐标

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- y-coordinate（y坐标）
- vertical coordinate（纵向坐标）
- y-value（y值）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ordinate axis / ordinate value / abscissa and ordinate / plot the ordinate**

👉 Example: In the Cartesian coordinate system, the ordinate represents the vertical position of a point.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student carefully plotted each **ordinate** on the graph to accurately represent the experimental data collected in the laboratory.
   学生仔细地在图表上标出每个纵坐标，以准确表示实验室中收集的实验数据。

2. When analyzing the function, the mathematician noted that the **ordinate** increased exponentially as the abscissa advanced linearly.
   在分析该函数时，数学家注意到随着横坐标线性增长，纵坐标呈指数增长。

3. The research paper required all graphs to clearly label both the abscissa and the **ordinate** for proper scientific interpretation.
   该研究论文要求所有图表清楚地标注横坐标和纵坐标，以便进行正确的科学解读。

把"ordinate"想成"or-din-ate"——"ordinate"将事物沿着垂直的餐桌按"顺序"排列，上下排列。
