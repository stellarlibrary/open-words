# 💣 undermine

🔊 /ˌʌndərˈmaɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To weaken or damage gradually; to erode the base or foundation of something; to sabotage or subvert
**中文：** 破坏；渐渐破坏；挖掘地基；暗中损害

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sabotage（蓄意破坏）
- subvert（颠覆）
- weaken（削弱）
- erode（侵蚀）
- compromise（危害）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undermine authority / undermine confidence / undermine efforts / undermine the foundation**

👉 Example: Constant criticism from a manager can seriously undermine an employee's confidence and productivity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The spread of misinformation on social media continues to **undermine** public trust in established scientific institutions and their findings.
   社交媒体上虚假信息的传播继续破坏公众对已建立的科学机构及其研究成果的信任。

2. Persistent budget cuts have gradually **undermined** the university's ability to attract and retain world-class researchers in critical fields.
   持续的预算削减逐渐削弱了该大学在关键领域吸引和留住世界级研究人员的能力。

3. The opposition party accused the government of attempting to **undermine** the independence of the judiciary through controversial legislative reforms.
   反对党指责政府试图通过有争议的立法改革来破坏司法的独立性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "under-mine" — digging a MINE UNDER a building's foundation; the structure above slowly collapses as the ground beneath is hollowed out.
把"undermine"想成"under（在下面）+ mine（挖矿）"——在建筑地基下面挖矿，上面的结构随着地基被掏空而慢慢坍塌。
