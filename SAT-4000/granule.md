# 🔬 granule

🔊 /ˈɡrænjuːl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A small, compact particle or grain of a substance
**中文：** n. 颗粒；细粒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grain（谷粒）
- particle（微粒）
- pellet（小球）
- speck（斑点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **granule of sugar / granule of sand / fine granules / coffee granules**

👉 Example: The scientist examined the granules under a high-powered microscope to determine their composition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company compressed the active ingredient into tiny **granules** for easier oral administration to patients.
   该制药公司将活性成分压缩成微小颗粒，以便患者更容易口服。

2. Each **granule** of volcanic ash contained trace minerals that significantly enriched the surrounding agricultural soil.
   每一粒火山灰颗粒都含有微量矿物质，显著地丰富了周围的农业土壤。

3. Researchers discovered that the **granules** within the cell played a crucial role in regulating protein synthesis.
   研究人员发现细胞内的颗粒在调节蛋白质合成方面起着至关重要的作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gran-ule" as a "grain" that is so small it needs the diminutive suffix "-ule" — a tiny grain is a granule.
把"granule"想成"grain（谷粒）"加上表示"小"的后缀"-ule"——一个极小的颗粒就是granule。
