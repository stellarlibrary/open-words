# 🤝 rendezvous

🔊 /ˈrɒndɪvuː/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A prearranged meeting at a specific time and place; a popular gathering spot. (v.) To meet at an agreed time and place
**中文：** n. 约会；约会地点；集结地 v. 会合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- meeting（会面）
- appointment（约会）
- gathering（聚会）
- assembly point（集合点）
- tryst（幽会）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rendezvous with someone / rendezvous point / secret rendezvous / a rendezvous at a place**

👉 Example: The intelligence agents arranged a clandestine rendezvous at the abandoned warehouse near the harbor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two spacecraft successfully completed their orbital **rendezvous** after days of precise navigational adjustments and calculations.
   两艘航天器在经过数天精确的导航调整和计算后成功完成了轨道会合。

2. The diplomats agreed to **rendezvous** at a neutral location to discuss the terms of the proposed ceasefire agreement.
   外交官们同意在中立地点会面，讨论拟议的停火协议条款。

3. The old cafe by the river had long served as a favorite **rendezvous** for writers, artists, and intellectuals.
   河边那家老咖啡馆长期以来一直是作家、艺术家和知识分子最喜欢的聚会场所。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "RENDE-vous" as a French phrase meaning "present yourselves" — people presenting themselves at a meeting point.
把"rendezvous"想成法语短语"present yourselves"——人们在约定地点相互现身。
