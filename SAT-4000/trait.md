# 🧬 trait

🔊 /treɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A distinguishing quality or characteristic, especially of a person's character; a genetically determined feature
**中文：** 特性；特点；特征（尤指性格特征或遗传特征）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- characteristic（特征）
- attribute（属性）
- quality（品质）
- feature（特点）
- property（性质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **personality trait / character trait / genetic trait / desirable trait / dominant trait**

👉 Example: Resilience is widely regarded as one of the most valuable traits a leader can possess.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers identified several genetic **traits** that appear to increase an individual's susceptibility to certain autoimmune disorders.
   研究人员发现了几个似乎会增加个体对某些自身免疫疾病易感性的遗传**特征**。

2. Intellectual curiosity is a fundamental **trait** that distinguishes exceptional scholars from their merely competent academic peers.
   求知欲是区分杰出学者与仅仅称职的学术同行的基本**特质**。

3. The study examined how specific personality **traits** influence decision-making behavior in high-pressure professional environments.
   该研究考察了特定的性格**特征**如何影响高压职业环境中的决策行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trait" as "trace it" — a trait is something you can trace back to identify what makes a person unique.
把"trait"想成"trace it（追溯它）"——特质是你可以追溯回去识别一个人独特之处的东西。
