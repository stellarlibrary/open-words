# 🍲 comestibles

🔊 /kəˈmes.tɪ.bəlz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Items of food; provisions or edible goods, especially those of fine or varied quality
**中文：** n. 食物；食品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- provisions（给养）
- foodstuffs（食品）
- victuals（食物）
- eatables（可吃的东西）
- fare（饭菜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fine comestibles / various comestibles / local comestibles / stock comestibles**

👉 Example: The expedition party ensured they had sufficient comestibles for the long journey ahead.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The merchant ship carried an abundant supply of **comestibles** to sustain the crew during their transatlantic voyage.
   商船载有充足的食物供给，以维持船员在横渡大西洋航行期间的生活所需。

2. Visitors to the annual harvest festival enjoyed sampling **comestibles** prepared by renowned chefs from across the region.
   一年一度丰收节的游客们享受着品尝由该地区知名厨师准备的各种美食。

3. The cellar was stocked with preserved **comestibles** that would remain edible throughout the harsh winter months ahead.
   地窖里储备了腌制的食品，这些食品在即将到来的严冬数月里仍可食用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comestibles" as things that "come to the table" — food items that are served and ready to eat.
把"comestibles"想成"来到餐桌上(come-to-table)的东西"——被端上来准备食用的食品。
