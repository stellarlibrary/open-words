# ⚙️ metal

🔊 /ˈmɛtl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A solid material that is typically hard, shiny, and a good conductor of heat and electricity. (v.) To cover or furnish with metal. (adj.) Made of metal.
**中文：** n. 金属；合金 v. 以金属覆盖 adj. 金属制的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alloy（合金）
- ore（矿石）
- mineral（矿物）
- metallic substance（金属物质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heavy metal / precious metal / metal detector / sheet metal / base metal**

👉 Example: The bridge was constructed from reinforced metal beams designed to withstand extreme weather conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The industrial revolution depended heavily on the ability to smelt and forge **metal** into machinery and infrastructure.
   工业革命在很大程度上依赖于将金属冶炼和锻造成机械和基础设施的能力。

2. Archaeologists unearthed ancient **metal** artifacts that provided crucial evidence of early trade routes across the continent.
   考古学家出土了古代金属文物，这些文物提供了早期大陆贸易路线的关键证据。

3. Engineers developed a new lightweight **metal** alloy that combines exceptional strength with remarkable resistance to corrosion.
   工程师们开发了一种新型轻质金属合金，兼具卓越的强度和出色的耐腐蚀性。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "metal" as related to "mettle" (courage) — both words share the same origin, as metals were tested for strength just like a person's mettle is tested.
把"metal"和"mettle"（勇气）联系起来——这两个词有相同的词源，因为金属要经过强度测试，就像一个人的勇气要经受考验一样。
