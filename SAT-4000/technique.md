# 🛠️ technique

🔊 /tekˈniːk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A way of carrying out a particular task, especially the execution of an artistic work or a scientific procedure; skill or ability in a particular field
**中文：** n. 技术，技巧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- method（方法）
- approach（途径）
- procedure（程序）
- skill（技能）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **technique for / surgical technique / painting technique / master a technique / innovative technique**

👉 Example: The surgeon employed an advanced laparoscopic technique to minimize recovery time for the patient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher developed a novel statistical **technique** that significantly improved the accuracy of predictive models in climate science.
   研究人员开发了一种新颖的统计技术，显著提高了气候科学中预测模型的准确性。

2. Mastering the **technique** of persuasive writing requires years of deliberate practice and careful study of rhetorical principles.
   掌握说服性写作技巧需要多年的刻意练习和对修辞原则的仔细研究。

3. The artist's distinctive brushwork **technique** has been widely imitated but never successfully replicated by contemporary painters.
   这位艺术家独特的笔触技法被广泛模仿，但从未被当代画家成功复制。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "technique" as "tech-nique" — "tech" relates to technology and skill, so a technique is the skilled way of doing something technical.
把"technique"想成"tech-nique"——"tech"与技术和技能有关，所以technique就是做技术性工作的熟练方式。
