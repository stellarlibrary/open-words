# 🌀 vortex

🔊 /ˈvɔːrteks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A whirling mass of fluid or air, especially a whirlpool or whirlwind; a situation regarded as drawing into its center all that surrounds it
**中文：** n. 旋涡；涡流；旋风；（动乱、争论等的）中心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- whirlpool（漩涡）
- maelstrom（大旋涡）
- eddy（涡流）
- cyclone（气旋）
- turbulence（动荡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vortex of chaos / vortex of emotion / polar vortex / caught in a vortex**

👉 Example: The small boat was caught in a powerful vortex that threatened to pull it beneath the surface.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political scandal created a **vortex** of controversy that drew in journalists, politicians, and the public alike.
   这场政治丑闻制造了一个争议的漩涡，将记者、政客和公众都卷入其中。

2. Scientists studied the atmospheric **vortex** over the Arctic to better understand its effects on global weather patterns.
   科学家研究了北极上空的大气涡流，以更好地了解其对全球天气模式的影响。

3. The nation found itself trapped in a **vortex** of economic decline, with rising unemployment and falling consumer confidence.
   这个国家发现自己陷入了经济衰退的漩涡，失业率上升，消费者信心下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vortex" as a spinning force that pulls everything toward its center — picture water spiraling down a drain.
把"vortex"想成一种把一切拉向中心的旋转力量——想象水沿着排水口螺旋而下的画面。
