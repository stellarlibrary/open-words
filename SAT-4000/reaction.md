# ⚡ reaction

🔊 /riˈækʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A response to a stimulus, event, or situation; a process in which substances interact to form new compounds; opposition to progress or reform
**中文：** n. 反应，感应；反动，复古；反作用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- response（回应）
- reply（答复）
- feedback（反馈）
- backlash（反弹）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reaction to / chain reaction / chemical reaction / emotional reaction / gut reaction**

👉 Example: The public reaction to the new policy was swift and overwhelmingly negative.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor observed the students' **reaction** to the unexpected examination results with considerable interest and concern.
   教授饶有兴趣且带着关切地观察了学生们对出乎意料的考试成绩的反应。

2. A violent chemical **reaction** occurred when the two volatile substances were inadvertently combined in the laboratory flask.
   当两种易挥发物质在实验室烧瓶中被意外混合时，发生了剧烈的化学反应。

3. Her immediate **reaction** to the controversial proposal was one of skepticism, though she later reconsidered her initial position.
   她对这项有争议的提案的第一反应是怀疑，尽管后来她重新考虑了自己最初的立场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-action" as "acting back" — a reaction is an action that comes back in response to something that happened first.
把"reaction"拆成"re（再次）+ action（行动）"——反应就是对先前发生的事情做出的回应行动。
