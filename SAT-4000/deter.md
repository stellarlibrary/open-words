# 🛑 deter

🔊 /dɪˈtɜːr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To discourage or prevent someone from doing something through fear, doubt, or difficulty
**中文：** v. 阻止；制止；威慑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- discourage（劝阻）
- dissuade（劝止）
- prevent（阻止）
- inhibit（抑制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deter someone from / deter crime / deter aggression / serve as a deterrent**

👉 Example: The harsh penalties were designed to deter citizens from engaging in fraudulent activities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The presence of security cameras may **deter** potential criminals from committing acts of theft in public spaces.
   安全摄像头的存在可能会阻止潜在的罪犯在公共场所实施盗窃行为。

2. Economic sanctions are often employed to **deter** nations from pursuing aggressive military policies against their neighbors.
   经济制裁通常被用来阻止国家对其邻国推行侵略性军事政策。

3. The complexity of the application process should not **deter** qualified candidates from seeking admission to the program.
   申请流程的复杂性不应阻止合格的候选人申请该项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deter" as "de-terror" — using terror or fear to turn someone away from doing something bad.
把"deter"想成"de-terror"——用恐惧来使某人不敢做某事。
