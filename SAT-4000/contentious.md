# 🔥 contentious

🔊 /kənˈtɛnʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing or likely to cause disagreement or controversy; inclined to argue or quarrel
**中文：** adj. 有异议的；引起争论的；爱争论的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- controversial（有争议的）
- disputatious（好争辩的）
- quarrelsome（爱争吵的）
- divisive（引起分裂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contentious issue / contentious debate / highly contentious / contentious topic / contentious claim**

👉 Example: The allocation of federal funding remains a highly contentious issue among state legislators.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proposed amendment proved deeply **contentious**, sparking weeks of heated debate on the floor of the legislative assembly.
   拟议的修正案极具争议性，在立法议会上引发了数周的激烈辩论。

2. Immigration policy has long been one of the most **contentious** topics in national discourse, dividing opinion across political lines.
   移民政策长期以来一直是国家话语中最具争议的话题之一，在政治立场上造成了分歧。

3. The **contentious** nature of the committee member made collaborative decision-making difficult and prolonged every meeting unnecessarily.
   该委员会成员好争论的性格使合作决策变得困难，并不必要地延长了每次会议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "conTENTION-ous" — full of "contention" and tension, where people argue and disagree with each other.
把"contentious"想成"充满contention（争端）的"——到处都是紧张和争议的场面。
