# ☀️ solar

🔊 /ˈsoʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or determined by the sun; utilizing energy from the sun. (n.) A sun room
**中文：** adj. 太阳的，日光的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stellar（恒星的）
- heliacal（太阳的）
- sunlit（阳光照耀的）
- radiant（辐射的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solar energy / solar system / solar panel / solar eclipse / solar power**

👉 Example: The university installed solar panels across the campus to reduce its dependence on fossil fuels.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government announced a substantial investment in **solar** energy infrastructure to accelerate the transition to renewables.
   政府宣布对太阳能基础设施进行大规模投资，以加速向可再生能源的转型。

2. Scientists observed a rare **solar** eclipse that provided valuable data about the composition of the sun's outer corona.
   科学家们观测到一次罕见的日食，为了解太阳外层日冕的组成提供了宝贵数据。

3. The **solar** farm generates enough electricity to power approximately five thousand residential homes throughout the region.
   该太阳能发电场产生的电力足以为该地区大约五千户住宅供电。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solar" as coming from "sol," the Latin word for sun — anything solar relates directly to the sun.
把"solar"想成来自"sol"，拉丁语中太阳的意思——任何solar的东西都与太阳直接相关。
