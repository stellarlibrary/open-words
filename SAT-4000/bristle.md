# 🦔 bristle

🔊 /ˈbrɪsl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A short, stiff hair on an animal or plant; a similar structure on a brush. (v.) To stand stiffly on end; to react with anger or indignation
**中文：** n. 猪鬃；刚毛 v. 竖起；发怒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prickle（刺毛）
- quill（羽毛管）
- spine（刺）
- stiffen（使僵硬）
- bridle（发怒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bristle with anger / bristle at a remark / bristle with weapons / hair bristled**

👉 Example: The cat's fur bristled when it sensed the presence of an unknown intruder nearby.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator visibly **bristled** at the accusation of corruption leveled against her during the heated congressional hearing.
   这位参议员在激烈的国会听证会上被指控腐败时明显表现出愤怒。

2. The old paintbrush had lost most of its **bristles**, rendering it virtually useless for any detailed artistic work.
   这把旧画笔已经掉了大部分刷毛，使其几乎无法用于任何精细的艺术创作。

3. The defensive fortifications **bristled** with artillery cannons and sharpened stakes designed to repel approaching enemy forces.
   防御工事上布满了大炮和削尖的木桩，旨在击退逼近的敌军。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bristle" as "br-istle" — like a thistle plant with sharp, stiff spines that bristle outward.
把"bristle"想成"br-istle"——像蓟草（thistle）一样，有尖硬的刺向外竖起。
