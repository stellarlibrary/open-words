# 😔 needy

🔊 /ˈniːdi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lacking the necessities of life; extremely poor; requiring a great deal of attention, affection, or emotional support
**中文：** adj. 贫困的；贫穷的；生活艰苦的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impoverished（贫穷的）
- destitute（赤贫的）
- indigent（穷困的）
- underprivileged（弱势的）
- disadvantaged（处于不利地位的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **needy families / needy children / the needy / needy communities**

👉 Example: Several charitable organizations provide food and shelter to the needy during harsh winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The foundation distributes essential supplies to **needy** families affected by natural disasters throughout the region.
   该基金会向该地区受自然灾害影响的贫困家庭分发基本物资。

2. Government programs designed to assist the **needy** have faced significant budget reductions in recent fiscal years.
   旨在帮助贫困人群的政府项目近年来面临大幅预算削减。

3. Volunteers organized a fundraising campaign to provide **needy** students with textbooks and educational materials for school.
   志愿者们组织了一场筹款活动，为贫困学生提供教科书和学习资料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "need" + "y" — someone who is "needy" is full of needs, lacking basic things required for a decent life.
想想"need（需要）"+"y"——"needy"的人充满了需求，缺乏体面生活所需的基本东西。
