# 📊 chart

🔊 /tʃɑːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A visual representation of data in the form of a graph, table, or diagram; a navigational map. (v.) To make a map or detailed plan of; to record progress systematically
**中文：** n. 图表；海图 v. 绘制……的图表；记录

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- graph（图表）
- diagram（图解）
- map（地图）
- plot（绘制）
- outline（概述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chart a course / bar chart / pie chart / chart progress / chart the development of**

👉 Example: The research team used a chart to display the correlation between variables.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economist presented a detailed **chart** illustrating the dramatic decline in unemployment rates over the past two decades.
   经济学家展示了一张详细的图表，说明了过去二十年失业率的大幅下降。

2. The explorers used nautical **chart**s to navigate safely through the treacherous straits during their maritime expedition.
   探险者们在航海探险中使用海图安全地穿越了危险的海峡。

3. The professor asked students to **chart** the progression of democratic movements across three continents throughout the twentieth century.
   教授要求学生绘制二十世纪三大洲民主运动发展进程的图表。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chart" as "ch-art" — the art of organizing information visually, turning numbers into pictures.
把"chart"想成"ch-art（艺术）"——将信息以视觉方式呈现的艺术，把数字变成图画。
