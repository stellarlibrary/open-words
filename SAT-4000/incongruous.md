# 🧩 incongruous

🔊 /ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something; out of place
**中文：** adj. 不协调的；不一致的；不和谐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incompatible（不相容的）
- discordant（不和谐的）
- inconsistent（不一致的）
- mismatched（不匹配的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incongruous with / seem incongruous / incongruous combination / incongruous element**

👉 Example: The modern glass tower appeared utterly incongruous with the surrounding medieval architecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The casual attire of the guest seemed **incongruous** at the formal diplomatic reception held at the embassy.
   该客人的休闲着装在大使馆举办的正式外交招待会上显得格格不入。

2. Critics noted the **incongruous** juxtaposition of lighthearted comedy and deeply tragic themes throughout the novel.
   评论家指出，整部小说中轻松喜剧与深刻悲剧主题的并置显得不协调。

3. A flourishing garden in the middle of the barren desert landscape struck travelers as deeply **incongruous**.
   在荒芜沙漠景观中间出现一座繁茂的花园，令旅行者感到极为不和谐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-congru-ous" as "not congruent" — in geometry, congruent shapes match perfectly, so incongruous means things that do not match.
把"incongruous"想成"in（不）+ congru（一致）+ ous"——不一致的，就像几何中不全等的图形，彼此不匹配。
