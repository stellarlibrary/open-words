# 🏔️ utmost

🔊 /ˈʌtmoʊst/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The greatest or most extreme extent or amount. (adj.) Most extreme; greatest in degree, quantity, or intensity
**中文：** n. 极限 adj. 极度的；最远的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- maximum（最大的）
- supreme（至高的）
- paramount（最重要的）
- greatest（最大的）
- extreme（极端的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **of the utmost importance / utmost care / utmost respect / do one's utmost / to the utmost**

👉 Example: The surgeon performed the procedure with the utmost precision to avoid any complications.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomatic negotiations required the **utmost** discretion, as any information leak could jeopardize the entire peace agreement.
   外交谈判需要极度谨慎，因为任何信息泄露都可能危及整个和平协议。

2. Scientists emphasized that it is of the **utmost** importance to address climate change before irreversible tipping points are reached.
   科学家们强调，在不可逆转的临界点到来之前应对气候变化是至关重要的。

3. The organization pledged to do its **utmost** to ensure equitable access to educational resources for all communities.
   该组织承诺尽最大努力确保所有社区公平获得教育资源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ut-MOST" — it literally means "the most out there," the outermost or greatest extreme you can reach.
想想"ut-MOST"——它字面意思就是"最外面的、最极致的"，你能达到的最大极限。
