# 🔥 incinerate

🔊 /ɪnˈsɪnəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To burn something completely until it is reduced to ashes; to destroy by fire.
**中文：** v. 把……烧成灰；烧弃；火化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- burn（焚烧）
- cremate（火化）
- combust（燃烧）
- char（烧焦）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incinerate waste / incinerate documents / incinerate completely / incinerated beyond recognition**

👉 Example: The factory was designed to incinerate hazardous waste materials at extremely high temperatures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The municipality chose to **incinerate** the contaminated materials rather than risk further environmental pollution in the area.
   市政府选择焚烧受污染的材料，而不是冒着进一步污染该地区环境的风险。

2. Classified documents were **incinerated** in a secure facility to prevent any possibility of unauthorized information disclosure.
   机密文件在安全设施中被焚毁，以防止任何未经授权的信息泄露。

3. The volcanic eruption **incinerated** vast stretches of forest, leaving only barren ash-covered terrain in its aftermath.
   火山爆发烧毁了大片森林，只留下一片覆盖着灰烬的荒凉地带。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CINER-ate" — "ciner" comes from Latin "cinis" meaning "ashes." To incinerate is to turn something into cinders and ashes.
把"incinerate"拆开看——"ciner"来自拉丁语"cinis"，意为"灰烬"。Incinerate就是把东西变成灰烬。
