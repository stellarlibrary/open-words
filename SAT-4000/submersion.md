# 💧 submersion

🔊 /səbˈmɜːrʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act or state of being submerged; the process of sinking or plunging beneath the surface of a liquid
**中文：** n. 淹没

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immersion（浸没）
- inundation（淹没）
- flooding（洪涝）
- submergence（沉入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **submersion in water / submersion of land / complete submersion / prolonged submersion**

👉 Example: The submersion of the coastal village was caused by unprecedented storm surges that overwhelmed the existing flood defenses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged **submersion** of the ancient ruins beneath the reservoir made archaeological excavation extraordinarily difficult and prohibitively expensive.
   古代遗迹长期淹没在水库之下，使得考古发掘异常困难且费用高得令人望而却步。

2. Medical researchers studied the physiological effects of cold-water **submersion** on the human body to improve emergency rescue protocols.
   医学研究人员研究了冷水浸没对人体的生理影响，以改进紧急救援方案。

3. The gradual **submersion** of the island's northern coastline has prompted the government to implement urgent climate adaptation strategies.
   该岛北部海岸线的逐渐淹没促使政府实施紧急的气候适应策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "submersion" as "submerge" + "-sion" (the state of) — the noun form describing the state of being plunged underwater, like a city lost beneath rising tides.
把"submersion"想成"submerge（淹没）"+"-sion（状态）"——描述被浸入水下的状态的名词形式，就像一座城市消失在上涨的潮水之下。
