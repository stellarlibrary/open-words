# 🤝 benefactor

🔊 /ˈbɛnɪfæktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who gives money or other help to a person or cause; a patron or donor who provides generous support
**中文：** n. 恩人；捐助者；施主；赞助人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- patron（赞助人）
- donor（捐赠者）
- philanthropist（慈善家）
- sponsor（资助者）
- supporter（支持者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a generous benefactor / an anonymous benefactor / the chief benefactor / benefactor of the arts**

👉 Example: The anonymous benefactor donated ten million dollars to establish a scholarship fund for disadvantaged students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's wealthiest **benefactor** funded the construction of a state-of-the-art research laboratory on campus.
   该大学最慷慨的捐助者资助了校园内一座先进研究实验室的建设。

2. Without the intervention of a generous **benefactor**, the struggling orphanage would have been forced to close its doors permanently.
   如果没有一位慷慨恩人的介入，这家难以为继的孤儿院将被迫永久关闭。

3. The museum honored its principal **benefactor** by naming the newly renovated exhibition hall after her distinguished family.
   博物馆以其主要捐助者的著名家族命名了新翻修的展览厅，以示敬意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bene-" (good) + "-factor" (one who does) — a benefactor is literally "one who does good" for others.
"Bene-"（好的）+"factor"（做事的人）——benefactor字面意思就是"做好事的人"，即恩人、捐助者。
