# ✅ justifiable

🔊 /ˌdʒʌstɪˈfaɪəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be shown as reasonable, right, or warranted; defensible with valid reasons
**中文：** adj. 可辩解的，有道理的；可证明为正当的；有理由的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- defensible（可辩护的）
- warranted（有正当理由的）
- legitimate（合理的）
- reasonable（合情合理的）
- excusable（可原谅的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **justifiable reason / justifiable anger / justifiable use of force / justifiable concern**

👉 Example: The committee concluded that the use of emergency funds was entirely justifiable under the circumstances.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board determined that the employee's termination was **justifiable** given the repeated violations of company policy.
   董事会认定，鉴于该员工多次违反公司政策，解雇是正当合理的。

2. Critics argued that the government's surveillance program was not **justifiable** even in the face of heightened security threats.
   批评者认为，即使面对日益严峻的安全威胁，政府的监控项目也不具有正当性。

3. The researcher presented **justifiable** evidence to support her controversial hypothesis about the origins of the ancient civilization.
   研究人员提出了有力的证据来支持她关于古代文明起源的争议性假说。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "justifi-able" as "able to be justified" — if something is justifiable, you can provide a just reason for it.
把"justifiable"想成"able to be justified"（能被证明正当的）——如果某事是justifiable的，你就能为它提供正当的理由。
