# 💢 besmirch

🔊 /bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To damage or soil the reputation of; to make dirty; to defame or slander
**中文：** v. 弄污；损害；诽谤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tarnish（玷污）
- defame（诽谤）
- slander（中伤）
- sully（损害名誉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **besmirch someone's name / besmirch someone's reputation / besmirch someone's honor**

👉 Example: The rival candidate attempted to besmirch her opponent's reputation through a series of misleading advertisements.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tabloid newspapers sought to **besmirch** the senator's character with sensational headlines based on unverified sources.
   小报试图用基于未经证实来源的耸人听闻的标题来损害参议员的声誉。

2. No amount of political maneuvering could **besmirch** the legacy of the celebrated humanitarian and Nobel laureate.
   任何政治手段都无法玷污这位著名人道主义者和诺贝尔奖获得者的遗产。

3. He accused his former colleagues of conspiring to **besmirch** his professional reputation through a coordinated disinformation campaign.
   他指控前同事们通过协调的虚假信息运动来共谋损害他的职业声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-SMIRCH" — like "smear" + "birch." Imagine smearing dark mud on a white birch tree, staining it. Besmirch means to stain someone's reputation.
想想"be-SMIRCH"——像"smear"（涂抹）+"birch"（白桦树）。想象在白色的桦树上涂黑泥，弄脏它。Besmirch就是玷污名誉。
