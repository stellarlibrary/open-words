# 🚫 atheism

🔊 /ˈeɪ.θi.ɪ.zəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The disbelief in or denial of the existence of God or gods; the doctrine that there is no deity.
**中文：** 不信神；无神论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disbelief（不信）
- nonbelief（不信仰）
- godlessness（无神论）
- skepticism（怀疑主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **profess/embrace/advocate atheism**, **atheism vs. agnosticism**

👉 Example: The philosopher's writings on atheism sparked heated debates in both academic and religious communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rise of **atheism** in modern society has prompted extensive scholarly debate about the role of religion.
   现代社会中无神论的兴起引发了关于宗教角色的广泛学术辩论。

2. His open declaration of **atheism** was considered remarkably bold in such a deeply conservative community.
   他公开宣布无神论在如此保守的社区中被认为是非常大胆的。

3. The professor explored the historical roots of **atheism** from ancient Greek philosophy to contemporary thought.
   这位教授探讨了无神论从古希腊哲学到当代思想的历史根源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ATHEISM" = "A-" (without) + "THE-" (god, from Greek "theos") — literally "without god," the belief that no god exists.
"ATHEISM"中"A-"表示"无"，"THE-"来自希腊语"theos（神）"——字面意思是"无神"，即认为没有神存在。
