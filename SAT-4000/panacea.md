# 💊 panacea

🔊 /ˌpænəˈsiːə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases; a universal cure
**中文：** n. 灵丹妙药；万能药

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cure-all（万灵药）
- elixir（灵丹妙药）
- remedy（疗法）
- silver bullet（妙方）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **not a panacea / panacea for all ills / universal panacea / seek a panacea**

👉 Example: Technology alone is not a panacea for the deeply rooted social inequalities affecting modern societies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many economists cautioned that deregulation should not be viewed as a **panacea** for the nation's persistent economic challenges.
   许多经济学家警告说，不应将放松管制视为解决国家长期经济挑战的万能药。

2. The newly proposed educational reform was heralded by supporters as a **panacea** that would resolve all systemic failures.
   新提出的教育改革被支持者誉为能解决所有系统性问题的灵丹妙药。

3. While the medication proved effective for some patients, physicians stressed that it was by no means a **panacea** for the disease.
   虽然这种药物对一些患者有效，但医生强调它绝不是治疗该疾病的万能药。
