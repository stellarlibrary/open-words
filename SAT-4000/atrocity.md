# 💀 atrocity

🔊 /əˈtrɑːsəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An extremely cruel or wicked act, especially one involving physical violence or injury; extreme cruelty
**中文：** n. 暴行；凶恶，残暴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outrage（暴行）
- barbarity（残暴）
- brutality（野蛮行为）
- savagery（凶残）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commit an atrocity / war atrocities / atrocity against / the atrocity of war**

👉 Example: The international community called for an investigation into the atrocities committed during the civil war.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians have documented every **atrocity** committed during the occupation to ensure future generations never forget.
   历史学家记录了占领期间犯下的每一起暴行，以确保后代永远不会忘记。

2. The United Nations condemned the **atrocity** and demanded immediate accountability from the responsible military commanders.
   联合国谴责了这一暴行，并要求相关军事指挥官立即承担责任。

3. Witnesses testified in court about the **atrocity** they had endured, leaving the audience in stunned silence.
   目击者在法庭上讲述了他们所遭受的暴行，令旁听者震惊得鸦雀无声。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Connect "atrocity" to "atrocious" — an atrocity is the noun form of something atrocious, an act of extreme cruelty.
将"atrocity"与"atrocious"联系起来——atrocity是atrocious的名词形式，指极其残暴的行为。
