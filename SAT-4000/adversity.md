# 🏔️ adversity

🔊 /ədˈvɜːr.sə.ti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A difficult or unfortunate situation; hardship or misfortune.
**中文：** 逆境；不幸；灾难

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hardship（困苦）
- misfortune（不幸）
- tribulation（磨难）
- affliction（苦难）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in the face of adversity / overcome adversity / times of adversity**

👉 Example: She demonstrated remarkable courage in the face of adversity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Great leaders are often forged through periods of profound **adversity** and personal struggle.
   伟大的领导者往往是在深重的逆境和个人挣扎中锤炼而成的。

2. The community showed extraordinary resilience when confronted with economic **adversity** during the recession.
   在经济衰退带来的逆境面前，这个社区展现了非凡的韧性。

3. Overcoming **adversity** builds character and strengthens one's capacity for empathy and perseverance.
   克服逆境能塑造品格，增强一个人的共情能力和毅力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Adversity" = "adverse" + "-ity" (noun suffix). When life turns adverse, you are living in adversity.
联想记忆：adversity 由 adverse（不利的）加名词后缀 -ity 构成，生活变得不利时，你就身处逆境。
