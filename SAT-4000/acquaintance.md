# 👥 acquaintance

🔊 /əˈkweɪntəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person one knows slightly but who is not a close friend; knowledge or experience of something
**中文：** 熟人；相识；了解；知道

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- associate（同事）
- contact（联系人）
- colleague（同僚）
- familiarity（熟悉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make the acquaintance of / a passing acquaintance / have some acquaintance with**

👉 Example: She had only a passing acquaintance with the subject before enrolling in the advanced course.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. He was merely a casual **acquaintance** from work, not someone she would confide in about personal matters.
   他只是工作中的一个泛泛之交，不是她会倾诉私事的人。

2. The diplomat made the **acquaintance** of several influential foreign dignitaries during the international summit in Geneva.
   这位外交官在日内瓦的国际峰会上结识了几位有影响力的外国政要。

3. A thorough **acquaintance** with classical literature is considered essential for students pursuing degrees in the humanities.
   对古典文学的深入了解被认为是攻读人文学科学位的学生所必备的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "acquaintance" as "a-quaint-dance" — you share a brief, quaint dance with someone at a party, but they remain just a casual contact.
把"acquaintance"想成"a-quaint-dance"——你在聚会上和某人跳了一支简短的舞，但他们仍只是泛泛之交。
