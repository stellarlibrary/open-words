# 😢 piteous

🔊 /ˈpɪtiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Arousing or deserving pity; evoking compassion through sadness or wretchedness
**中文：** adj. 可怜的；哀怨的；慈悲的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pathetic（可怜的）
- pitiable（令人同情的）
- plaintive（哀怨的）
- woeful（悲哀的）
- sorrowful（悲伤的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **piteous cry / piteous sight / piteous appeal / piteous expression / piteous condition**

👉 Example: The refugees made a piteous appeal for food and shelter to the humanitarian workers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The abandoned kitten let out a **piteous** cry that moved every passerby to stop and offer assistance.
   那只被遗弃的小猫发出了哀怨的叫声，使每个路人都停下来伸出援手。

2. The documentary captured the **piteous** conditions of displaced families struggling to survive in overcrowded temporary shelters.
   这部纪录片记录了流离失所的家庭在拥挤的临时避难所中艰难求生的可怜境况。

3. Her **piteous** expression during the testimony deeply affected the jury and ultimately influenced the outcome of the trial.
   她在作证时的哀怜神情深深打动了陪审团，并最终影响了审判结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pite-ous" as "full of pity" — something piteous is so sad that it fills you with pity when you see or hear it.
把"piteous"想成"充满pity（怜悯）的"——可怜的事物是如此悲伤，以至于你看到或听到时会充满同情。
