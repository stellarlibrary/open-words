# ⚔️ litigious

🔊 /lɪˈtɪdʒəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inclined to engage in lawsuits; argumentative or contentious by nature
**中文：** adj. 好诉讼的；好争论的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contentious（好争论的）
- quarrelsome（爱争吵的）
- argumentative（好辩的）
- disputatious（好争辩的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a litigious society / litigious individuals / increasingly litigious / litigious culture**

👉 Example: The company hired extra lawyers because it operated in an increasingly litigious industry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **litigious** tenant threatened to sue the landlord over every minor maintenance issue that arose in the building.
   这位好打官司的租户威胁要就楼里出现的每一个小维修问题起诉房东。

2. Critics argue that America has become an excessively **litigious** society where lawsuits replace reasonable negotiation.
   批评者认为美国已经变成了一个过度诉讼的社会，诉讼取代了合理的协商。

3. Her **litigious** nature made business partners reluctant to enter into contracts or agreements with her firm.
   她好打官司的性格使商业伙伴不愿与她的公司签订合同或协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "litigious" as "litigation + ous" — full of litigation, someone who loves to sue at every opportunity.
把"litigious"想成"litigation（诉讼）+ ous（充满的）"——充满诉讼欲望，动不动就想打官司的人。
