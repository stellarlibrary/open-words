# 🔮 cryptic

🔊 /ˈkrɪptɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure; deliberately vague or ambiguous in expression
**中文：** adj. 神秘的，含义模糊的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enigmatic（神秘的）
- mysterious（神秘莫测的）
- obscure（晦涩的）
- ambiguous（模棱两可的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cryptic message / cryptic remark / cryptic clue / cryptic warning / remain cryptic**

👉 Example: The professor left a cryptic note on the board that puzzled students for the entire semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient manuscript contained **cryptic** symbols that archaeologists struggled to decipher for more than two decades.
   这份古代手稿包含着神秘的符号，考古学家花了二十多年才努力破译。

2. Her **cryptic** response to the journalist's direct question only intensified public speculation about the company's future.
   她对记者直接提问的含糊回答只加剧了公众对公司未来的猜测。

3. Intelligence analysts intercepted a **cryptic** communication that appeared to reference coordinates of an undisclosed military installation.
   情报分析人员截获了一条含义模糊的通讯，似乎涉及一个未公开的军事设施的坐标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crypt-ic" — coming from "crypt," a hidden underground chamber. Something cryptic is hidden and hard to understand.
把"cryptic"想成来自"crypt（地下墓穴）"——一个隐藏的地下密室。cryptic的事物是隐秘的、难以理解的。
