# 🛒 peddle

🔊 /ˈpɛdl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To sell goods by going from place to place; to promote or spread an idea or viewpoint
**中文：** v. 叫卖；兜售；散播；沿街叫卖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hawk（沿街叫卖）
- vend（售卖）
- market（推销）
- promote（宣传）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peddle goods / peddle influence / peddle lies / peddle wares**

👉 Example: Street vendors continued to peddle their wares despite the new municipal regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization was accused of attempting to **peddle** misinformation through social media platforms and fabricated news articles.
   该组织被指控试图通过社交媒体平台和捏造的新闻文章散播虚假信息。

2. Local merchants would **peddle** handcrafted goods at the weekly market, attracting tourists from neighboring towns and cities.
   当地商人每周在集市上兜售手工制品，吸引了来自周边城镇的游客。

3. Politicians who **peddle** false promises ultimately erode public trust in democratic institutions and governance structures.
   兜售虚假承诺的政客最终会侵蚀公众对民主制度和治理结构的信任。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peddle" as "pedal" — a peddler pedals around on foot or bicycle, going door to door selling things.
把"peddle"想成"pedal（踩踏板）"——小贩骑着自行车或步行挨家挨户地叫卖东西。
