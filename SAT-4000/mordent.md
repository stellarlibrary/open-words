# 🎵 mordent

🔊 /ˈmɔːrdənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A musical ornament consisting of a rapid alternation of a note with the note immediately below or above it
**中文：** n. 涟音；波音

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trill（颤音）
- ornament（装饰音）
- embellishment（修饰音）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **play a mordent / upper mordent / lower mordent / add a mordent / mordent notation**

👉 Example: The harpsichordist added a delicate mordent to the opening phrase of the Baroque sonata.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pianist executed each **mordent** with remarkable precision, bringing an authentic Baroque quality to the entire performance.
   这位钢琴家以非凡的精确度演奏每一个波音，为整场演出赋予了地道的巴洛克风格。

2. Students of music theory must learn to distinguish a **mordent** from a trill, as each ornament serves a different expressive purpose.
   音乐理论专业的学生必须学会区分波音和颤音，因为每种装饰音都有不同的表达功能。

3. The composer indicated a **mordent** above the note to instruct performers to add a brief, rapid alternation in pitch.
   作曲家在音符上方标注了波音记号，指示演奏者加入一个短暂而快速的音高交替。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mordent" as "mord" (bite) + "ent" — a mordent is a quick musical "bite" that adds a brief ornamental flourish to a note.
把"mordent"想成"mord"（咬）+ "ent"——波音就是给音符加上一个快速的音乐"咬合"，形成短暂的装饰效果。
