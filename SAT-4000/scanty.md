# 🏜️ scanty

🔊 /ˈskænti/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking in quantity or quality; meager; barely sufficient; sparse
**中文：** adj. 缺乏的，不足的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- meager（贫乏的）
- sparse（稀疏的）
- insufficient（不足的）
- scant（不足的）
- inadequate（不够的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scanty evidence / scanty resources / scanty clothing / scanty information / scanty rainfall**

👉 Example: The detective had only scanty evidence to work with, making the investigation exceptionally challenging.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee criticized the proposal for providing only **scanty** details about the projected costs and anticipated environmental impact.
   委员会批评该提案仅提供了关于预计成本和预期环境影响的粗略细节。

2. Due to **scanty** rainfall throughout the growing season, farmers in the region suffered devastating crop losses and financial hardship.
   由于整个生长季节降雨不足，该地区的农民遭受了毁灭性的作物损失和经济困难。

3. Historians found only **scanty** records of the ancient civilization, leaving many questions about their customs and beliefs unanswered.
   历史学家仅发现了有关该古代文明的少量记录，留下了许多关于其习俗和信仰的未解之谜。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scanty" as "scan + ty" — when you scan a nearly empty shelf, you see how scanty the supplies are. Barely anything there!
把"scanty"想成"scan + ty"——当你扫视一个几乎空空的架子时，你就能看到物资是多么匮乏。几乎什么都没有！
