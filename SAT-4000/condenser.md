# ⚙️ condenser

🔊 /kənˈdɛnsər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A device used to condense vapor into liquid; a capacitor in electrical engineering; a lens or mirror that concentrates light.

**中文：** 冷凝器；电容器；聚光器。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- capacitor（电容器）
- cooler（冷却器）
- concentrator（聚光器）
- heat exchanger（热交换器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** steam condenser / condenser unit / condenser lens

**Example:** The power plant relies on a large **condenser** to convert exhaust steam back into water.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The industrial **condenser** efficiently converts steam into purified water for reuse in the cooling system.
   工业冷凝器有效地将蒸汽转化为纯净水，以便在冷却系统中重复使用。

2. A faulty **condenser** in the refrigeration unit caused the entire system to overheat and shut down.
   制冷装置中一个有故障的冷凝器导致整个系统过热并关闭。

3. The microscope's **condenser** focuses light onto the specimen to enhance clarity and resolution of the image.
   显微镜的聚光器将光线聚焦到样本上，以增强图像的清晰度和分辨率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** A condenser is the device that does the condensing — it turns vapor into liquid or concentrates energy.

**中文：** condenser 就是执行 "condense"（凝结）动作的装置——它将蒸汽变成液体或集中能量，即"冷凝器、聚光器"。
