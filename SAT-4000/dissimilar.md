# 🔀 dissimilar

🔊 /dɪˈsɪmɪlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not alike; different in nature, form, or quality
**中文：** adj. 不同的；不相似的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- different（不同的）
- unlike（不像的）
- distinct（有区别的）
- divergent（不同的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dissimilar to / dissimilar from / not dissimilar / dissimilar characteristics**

👉 Example: The two proposals were fundamentally dissimilar in both their scope and their underlying methodology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Although the twins appeared identical, their personalities were remarkably **dissimilar** in virtually every measurable respect.
   尽管这对双胞胎外表看起来一模一样，但他们的性格在几乎每个可衡量的方面都截然不同。

2. The committee compared two **dissimilar** approaches to urban planning before selecting the one most suitable for the community.
   委员会比较了两种不同的城市规划方法，然后选择了最适合该社区的一种。

3. Researchers discovered that **dissimilar** species can develop analogous traits through convergent evolution in comparable environmental conditions.
   研究人员发现，不同的物种可以在类似的环境条件下通过趋同进化发展出相似的特征。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-similar" — the prefix "dis" negates "similar," so it literally means "not similar."
把"dissimilar"想成"dis-similar"——前缀"dis"否定了"similar"（相似的），所以字面意思就是"不相似的"。
