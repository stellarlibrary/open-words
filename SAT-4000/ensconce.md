# 🛋️ ensconce

🔊 /ɪnˈskɒns/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To settle comfortably and securely in a place; to conceal or shelter safely
**中文：** v. 安置；安顿下来；使隐藏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nestle（安顿）
- shelter（庇护）
- settle（安置）
- conceal（隐藏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ensconce oneself in / ensconced in a chair / ensconced behind / comfortably ensconced**

👉 Example: After a long journey, she ensconced herself in the armchair beside the fireplace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The retired professor **ensconced** himself in his private study, surrounded by towering shelves of rare first-edition volumes.
   这位退休教授安顿在自己的私人书房中，四周环绕着一排排珍贵的初版书籍。

2. The fugitive managed to **ensconce** himself within a remote mountain village where authorities were unlikely to discover him.
   逃犯设法将自己隐藏在一个偏远的山村中，当局不太可能发现他。

3. The diplomat remained comfortably **ensconced** in the embassy compound while political turmoil engulfed the streets of the capital.
   当政治动乱席卷首都街头时，该外交官仍安然待在大使馆院内。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-sconce" as placing yourself in a "sconce" (a wall fixture) — tucked safely and snugly into a secure, sheltered spot.
把"ensconce"想成把自己放进"sconce（壁灯架）"里——安全而舒适地蜷缩在一个安隐之处。
