# 🗣️ garrulous

🔊 /ˈɡærələs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters; given to rambling speech
**中文：** adj. 唠叨的；喋喋不休的；多嘴的；饶舌的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- loquacious（健谈的）
- verbose（冗长的）
- talkative（多话的）
- chatty（爱聊天的）
- voluble（滔滔不绝的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **garrulous neighbor / garrulous old man / become garrulous / garrulous storyteller**

👉 Example: The garrulous passenger talked nonstop throughout the entire flight without pausing for breath.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **garrulous** committee member prolonged every meeting with lengthy digressions that had little relevance to the agenda.
   那位喋喋不休的委员会成员用与议程无关的冗长题外话拖延了每次会议。

2. Although brilliant in her research, the professor was known to be **garrulous** during lectures, often straying from the topic.
   尽管这位教授在研究方面才华横溢，但她在讲课时以唠叨著称，经常偏离主题。

3. The otherwise reserved diplomat became surprisingly **garrulous** after the formal proceedings had concluded for the evening.
   这位平时沉默寡言的外交官在当晚正式会议结束后变得出人意料地健谈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "garrulous" as "gargle + ous" — someone who keeps gargling words out of their mouth nonstop, always talking.
把"garrulous"想成"gargle + ous"——一个人不停地从嘴里"漱"出话来，总是说个不停。
