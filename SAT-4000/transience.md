# 🍂 transience

🔊 /ˈtrænziəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state or quality of being transient; the condition of lasting only for a short time; impermanence.
**中文：** n. 短暂；无常；转瞬即逝。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impermanence（无常）
- brevity（短暂）
- ephemerality（短暂性）
- fleetingness（稍纵即逝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the transience of life / the transience of fame / reflect on transience / embrace transience**

👉 Example: The poet meditated on the transience of beauty and the inevitability of change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher's essay explored the **transience** of human existence and urged readers to find meaning in fleeting moments.
   这位哲学家的文章探讨了人类存在的短暂性，并敦促读者在稍纵即逝的时刻中寻找意义。

2. Cherry blossoms serve as a powerful symbol of **transience** in Japanese culture, reminding observers that beauty is temporary.
   樱花在日本文化中是无常的有力象征，提醒观者美是暂时的。

3. The **transience** of political power became evident as the once-dominant regime collapsed within a matter of weeks.
   政治权力的短暂性变得显而易见，因为曾经占主导地位的政权在数周之内就瓦解了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transience" as "transit + ence" — like something in transit, always passing through and never staying permanently.
把"transience"想成"transit（过境）+ ence"——就像过境中的事物，总是经过而从不永久停留。
