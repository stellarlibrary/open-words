# 🎭 mimic

🔊 /ˈmɪmɪk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective | 动词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To imitate or copy the appearance, behavior, or speech of someone or something. (n.) A person who imitates others. (adj.) Imitative or simulated.
**中文：** v. 模仿；模拟 n. 效颦者，模仿者 adj. 模仿的，模拟的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imitate（模仿）
- emulate（效仿）
- simulate（模拟）
- copy（复制）
- impersonate（扮演）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mimic the behavior of / mimic the style / mimic the sound / closely mimic**

👉 Example: Certain insects have evolved to mimic the appearance of more dangerous species for protection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artificial intelligence system was designed to **mimic** human conversational patterns with remarkable accuracy and fluency.
   该人工智能系统旨在以出色的准确性和流畅度模仿人类的对话模式。

2. Several species of butterflies **mimic** the coloring of toxic varieties to deter potential predators in their natural habitat.
   几种蝴蝶模仿有毒品种的颜色，以在其自然栖息地中吓阻潜在的捕食者。

3. The talented **mimic** entertained the audience by perfectly reproducing the distinct voices of several well-known political leaders.
   这位才华横溢的模仿者通过完美再现几位知名政治领导人的声音来娱乐观众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mimic" as "me-me-copy" — someone who keeps saying "me too" by copying everything others do, like a mirror reflection.
把"mimic"想成"me-me-copy"——一个不断模仿别人做的一切说"我也是"的人，就像镜中的倒影。
