# 🎪 gypsy

🔊 /ˈdʒɪpsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A member of the Romani people; a person who leads an itinerant or unconventional lifestyle
**中文：** n. 吉卜赛人；吉普赛人；流浪者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nomad（游牧民）
- wanderer（流浪者）
- rover（漫游者）
- traveler（旅行者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gypsy lifestyle / gypsy caravan / gypsy music / gypsy soul**

👉 Example: The artist adopted a gypsy lifestyle, traveling from city to city in search of inspiration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary explored the rich cultural traditions of the **gypsy** communities scattered throughout Eastern Europe.
   这部纪录片探讨了散布在东欧各地的吉普赛社区丰富的文化传统。

2. She lived a **gypsy** existence for years, never staying in one place long enough to establish permanent roots.
   她过了多年的流浪生活，从未在一个地方待得足够久以扎下永久的根。

3. The **gypsy** musicians performed a mesmerizing concert that captivated the entire audience with passionate melodies.
   吉普赛音乐家们演奏了一场令人着迷的音乐会，用充满激情的旋律征服了全场观众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gypsy" as linked to "Egypt" — the Romani people were once mistakenly believed to have come from Egypt, hence the name.
把"gypsy"与"Egypt（埃及）"联系起来——罗姆人曾被误认为来自埃及，因此得名。
