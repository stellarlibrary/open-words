# 🔥 brazier

🔊 /ˈbreɪʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A portable container or open pan used for holding burning coals or charcoal; a craftsman who works with brass
**中文：** n. 火盆；黄铜匠

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fire pit（火坑）
- hearth（炉膛）
- grate（炉栅）
- censer（香炉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **charcoal brazier / iron brazier / gather around a brazier / burning brazier**

👉 Example: The soldiers huddled around the brazier to warm themselves during the freezing winter night.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The workers gathered around the glowing **brazier** in the courtyard, seeking warmth during the bitterly cold December evening.
   工人们围聚在庭院里发光的火盆旁，在寒冷刺骨的十二月夜晚寻求温暖。

2. Archaeological excavations revealed an ornate bronze **brazier** that had been used in ancient religious ceremonies and ritual offerings.
   考古发掘出一个精美的青铜火盆，曾被用于古代宗教仪式和祭祀活动中。

3. The medieval **brazier** crafted finely detailed candlesticks and decorative vessels from brass for the wealthy merchant families.
   这位中世纪的黄铜匠为富有的商人家族用黄铜制作了精细的烛台和装饰器皿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brazier" as "blaze + er" — a container where a blaze burns, or a person who works with brass (braze + er).
把"brazier"想成"blaze + er"——一个燃烧着火焰的容器，或者从事黄铜加工的人（braze + er）。
