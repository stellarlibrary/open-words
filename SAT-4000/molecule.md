# 🔬 molecule

🔊 /ˈmɒlɪkjuːl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The smallest unit of a chemical compound that retains its chemical properties, consisting of two or more atoms; a very small particle
**中文：** n. 分子；微小颗粒，微粒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- particle（粒子）
- atom（原子）
- compound（化合物）
- element（元素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **water molecule / molecule of DNA / organic molecule / molecule structure**

👉 Example: Each water molecule consists of two hydrogen atoms bonded to a single oxygen atom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers identified a novel **molecule** that could potentially inhibit the growth of cancer cells in laboratory conditions.
   研究人员发现了一种可能在实验室条件下抑制癌细胞生长的新型分子。

2. Understanding how a single **molecule** interacts with its neighbors is fundamental to advances in materials science and nanotechnology.
   理解单个分子如何与其相邻分子相互作用是材料科学和纳米技术进步的基础。

3. The pharmaceutical company invested heavily in designing a synthetic **molecule** capable of crossing the blood-brain barrier effectively.
   这家制药公司大力投资设计一种能有效穿越血脑屏障的合成分子。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mole-cule" as "mole + tiny (cule)" — a mole is a unit in chemistry, and "-cule" means small, so a molecule is a tiny chemical unit.
把"molecule"想成"mole + cule（小）"——mole是化学单位，-cule表示小，所以molecule就是微小的化学单位。
