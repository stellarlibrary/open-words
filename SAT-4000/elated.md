# 😄 elated

🔊 /ɪˈleɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely happy and excited, often as a result of a success or achievement; in high spirits
**中文：** adj. 兴高采烈的；得意洋洋的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ecstatic（狂喜的）
- jubilant（欢欣的）
- exhilarated（兴奋的）
- overjoyed（欣喜若狂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elated at / elated by / elated about / feel elated**

👉 Example: The students felt elated after receiving their acceptance letters from prestigious universities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team was **elated** when the clinical trial results confirmed the effectiveness of the new pharmaceutical treatment.
   当临床试验结果证实了新药物治疗的有效性时，研究团队欣喜若狂。

2. She was visibly **elated** upon learning that her doctoral dissertation had been accepted without any required revisions.
   得知她的博士论文无需修改即被接受时，她明显地兴高采烈。

3. The entire community felt **elated** after the conservation project successfully restored the endangered species to its native habitat.
   保护项目成功地将濒危物种恢复到其原始栖息地后，整个社区都感到欢欣鼓舞。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-late-d" as "elevated" — when you are elated, your spirits are elevated to the highest point.
把"elated"想成"elevated（被提升的）"——当你兴高采烈时，你的精神被提升到了最高点。
