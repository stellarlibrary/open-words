# 🧲 adherent

🔊 /ədˈhɪr.ənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who supports or follows a particular leader, cause, or belief (adj.) Sticking fast to an object or surface
**中文：** n. 信徒，追随者，拥护者 adj. 附着的，粘着的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- follower（追随者）
- supporter（支持者）
- disciple（门徒）
- devotee（信奉者）
- advocate（拥护者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adherent of / adherent to / loyal adherent / devoted adherent / political adherent**

👉 Example: The movement attracted adherents from diverse social and economic backgrounds across the nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher gained numerous **adherents** who championed his theories in academic circles throughout Europe.
   这位哲学家赢得了众多追随者，他们在整个欧洲的学术圈中宣扬他的理论。

2. As a devoted **adherent** of constitutional originalism, the judge interpreted every clause according to its historical meaning.
   作为宪法原旨主义的忠实信奉者，这位法官按照历史含义解释每一个条款。

3. The **adherent** surface of the new bandage ensures it remains firmly in place even during vigorous activity.
   这种新绷带的粘着表面确保它即使在剧烈运动中也能牢固地保持在原位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adherent" as someone who "adheres" to a cause — they stick to it like glue. An adherent is a person who clings faithfully to a leader or a belief system.
把"adherent"想成一个"adhere"（附着）于某个事业的人——他们像胶水一样紧紧粘着。Adherent就是忠心地追随领袖或信仰体系的人。
