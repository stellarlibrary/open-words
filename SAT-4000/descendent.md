# 🧬 descendent

🔊 /dɪˈsɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Proceeding from an ancestor or source; derived. (n.) A person, animal, or plant descended from a particular ancestor; an offspring
**中文：** adj. 派生的；下降的 n. 后裔；后代；子孙

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- offspring（后代）
- progeny（子孙）
- heir（继承人）
- successor（继承者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **direct descendent / descendent of / lineal descendent / descendent population**

👉 Example: She is a direct descendent of one of the nation's founding political leaders.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Genetic analysis confirmed that the modern population is **descendent** from a small group of settlers who arrived centuries ago.
   基因分析证实，现代人口是几百年前到达的一小群定居者的后裔。

2. As a **descendent** of distinguished scholars, she felt a profound obligation to uphold the family's longstanding academic tradition.
   作为杰出学者的后代，她感到有深切的责任去维护家族长久以来的学术传统。

3. The **descendent** species exhibit remarkable adaptations that differentiate them significantly from their common evolutionary ancestor.
   这些派生物种展现出显著的适应性特征，使它们与共同的进化祖先有明显区别。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "descend-ent" — one who descends from a family tree; a descendent climbs down the branches of ancestry.
把"descendent"想成"descend-ent（向下延伸的人）"——从家谱上往下延伸的人；后裔沿着血统的枝干向下攀延。
