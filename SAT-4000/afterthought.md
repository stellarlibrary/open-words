# 💭 afterthought

🔊 /ˈæftərˌθɔːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An idea, thought, or plan that comes to mind after the original decision or event; something added later
**中文：** 事后的想法；后来添加的东西

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reconsideration（再考虑）
- addendum（附录）
- supplement（补充）
- postscript（附言）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **as an afterthought / add as an afterthought / mere afterthought / almost an afterthought**

👉 Example: The safety clause was added to the contract as an afterthought before the final signing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect included a small balcony as an **afterthought**, yet it became the most cherished feature of the residence.
   建筑师将一个小阳台作为事后添加的设计，但它却成了住宅中最受喜爱的特色。

2. Environmental concerns were treated as a mere **afterthought** during the initial planning stages of the industrial complex.
   在工业园区的初期规划阶段，环境问题仅被当作事后才考虑的事情。

3. She mentioned her own remarkable accomplishments almost as an **afterthought**, preferring instead to praise her dedicated colleagues.
   她几乎是在事后才顺带提到自己的杰出成就，更愿意赞扬她敬业的同事们。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it into "after" + "thought" — a thought that comes after the main event, like remembering something you forgot to say.
将其拆分为"after"（之后）+"thought"（想法）——在事情发生之后才产生的想法，就像想起了忘记说的话。
