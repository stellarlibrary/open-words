# 🕵️ collusion

🔊 /kəˈluː.ʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A secret or illegal agreement between two or more parties to deceive, cheat, or defraud others; conspiracy.

**中文：** 勾结；共谋。指两方或多方之间为欺骗、欺诈他人而达成的秘密或非法协议。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- conspiracy（阴谋）
- complicity（共犯）
- connivance（默许）
- scheming（密谋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** "in collusion with / collusion between / act in collusion" — implies secrecy and illegal or unethical cooperation.

**Example:** The executives acted in collusion with government officials to secure contracts through fraudulent means.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that several corporations had engaged in **collusion** to fix prices and eliminate fair market competition.
   调查揭示，几家公司曾进行**共谋**以操纵价格并消除公平的市场竞争。

2. The court determined that the defendant had acted in **collusion** with foreign agents to undermine the integrity of the election.
   法院认定被告与外国代理人进行了**勾结**，以破坏选举的公正性。

3. Regulatory agencies were established specifically to detect and prevent **collusion** among firms that might harm consumer interests.
   监管机构的设立专门用于侦查和防止可能损害消费者利益的企业间**串谋**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Col-" (together) + "lusion" (from Latin *ludere*, to play) — people "playing together" in secret, scheming behind the scenes.

**中文：** "Col-"（一起）+ "lusion"（源自拉丁语 *ludere*，玩耍）——人们在暗中"一起玩"，即在幕后密谋，也就是"勾结、共谋"。
