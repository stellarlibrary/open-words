# 💡 inventive

🔊 /ɪnˈvɛntɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the ability to create or design new things or to think originally; showing creativity and originality
**中文：** adj. 善于创造的；有发明才能的；独出心裁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- creative（有创造力的）
- innovative（创新的）
- ingenious（巧妙的）
- resourceful（足智多谋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inventive mind / inventive approach / inventive solution / highly inventive / inventive spirit**

👉 Example: The engineer's inventive approach to the problem saved the company both time and resources.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect's **inventive** use of recycled materials earned international recognition and several prestigious design awards last year.
   这位建筑师对回收材料的创造性使用赢得了国际认可，并获得了几项享有盛誉的设计奖。

2. Students who demonstrate an **inventive** approach to problem-solving are more likely to succeed in rapidly evolving technological fields.
   在解决问题方面展现出创造力的学生更有可能在快速发展的技术领域取得成功。

3. The novelist is widely celebrated for her **inventive** narrative structures that challenge traditional storytelling conventions in contemporary fiction.
   这位小说家因其独出心裁的叙事结构而广受赞誉，这些结构挑战了当代小说中的传统叙事惯例。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inventive" as "invent + ive" — someone who is inventive has the quality of an inventor, always creating something new.
把"inventive"想成"invent（发明）+ ive（具有…性质的）"——有inventive特质的人就像发明家一样，总能创造新事物。
