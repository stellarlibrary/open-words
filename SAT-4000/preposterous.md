# 🤡 preposterous

🔊 /prɪˈpɒstərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Completely contrary to reason or common sense; utterly absurd or ridiculous
**中文：** adj. 荒谬的；可笑的；荒唐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absurd（荒谬的）
- ridiculous（可笑的）
- ludicrous（荒唐的）
- nonsensical（无意义的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a preposterous idea / preposterous claim / preposterous notion / utterly preposterous**

👉 Example: The defendant's explanation was so preposterous that even his own attorney appeared unconvinced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The notion that a single individual could have constructed the entire monument alone is utterly **preposterous** and historically unfounded.
   认为一个人能独自建造整座纪念碑的想法完全是荒谬的，在历史上毫无根据。

2. Critics dismissed the economic forecast as **preposterous**, arguing that no credible data supported such wildly optimistic projections.
   批评者认为这份经济预测荒唐可笑，辩称没有任何可靠数据支持如此过于乐观的预期。

3. It would be **preposterous** to allocate the entire research budget to a single untested hypothesis without peer review.
   在没有同行评审的情况下将整个研究预算分配给一个未经检验的假设是荒谬的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-poster-ous" as putting the "poster before the event" — everything is backwards and absurd, like advertising after it's over.
把"preposterous"想成"pre-poster-ous"——把海报贴在活动之前（前后颠倒），一切都反了，所以荒谬至极。
