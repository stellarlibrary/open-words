# ⚡ stun

🔊 /stʌn/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To shock or astonish greatly; to render unconscious or dazed by a blow. (n.) A state of daze or bewilderment
**中文：** vt. 使震惊，使目瞪口呆；把…打昏，使昏迷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astonish（使惊讶）
- daze（使发呆）
- bewilder（使困惑）
- stupefy（使惊呆）
- shock（使震惊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stun the audience / stun the world / stunned into silence / stun with a blow**

👉 Example: The unexpected verdict stunned the courtroom into complete and utter silence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The revelations contained in the investigative report **stunned** the political establishment and prompted immediate calls for reform.
   调查报告中的揭露震惊了政界，并立即引发了改革呼声。

2. The boxer delivered a powerful uppercut that **stunned** his opponent, leaving him visibly disoriented for several critical seconds.
   拳击手打出一记有力的上勾拳，将对手打得昏头转向，使其在关键的几秒钟内明显失去方向感。

3. Scientists were **stunned** to discover a thriving ecosystem deep beneath the Antarctic ice sheet, challenging longstanding biological assumptions.
   科学家们惊讶地发现南极冰盖深处有一个繁荣的生态系统，这挑战了长期以来的生物学假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stun" as a "stun gun" — it delivers a shock that leaves you dazed and unable to react, just like stunning news.
把"stun"想成"电击枪"——它发出的冲击让你目瞪口呆、无法反应，就像令人震惊的消息一样。
