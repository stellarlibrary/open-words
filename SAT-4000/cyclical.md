# 🔄 cyclical

🔊 /ˈsɪklɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Occurring in cycles; recurring at regular intervals in a repeated pattern
**中文：** adj. 周期的；循环的；轮转的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- periodic（周期性的）
- recurring（反复出现的）
- repetitive（重复的）
- rotational（轮转的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cyclical pattern / cyclical downturn / cyclical nature / cyclical fluctuations**

👉 Example: The economy experienced cyclical fluctuations that affected employment rates every decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Economists have long observed the **cyclical** nature of financial markets, which alternate between periods of growth and recession.
   经济学家长期观察到金融市场的周期性本质，其在增长和衰退之间交替变化。

2. The **cyclical** migration patterns of these birds have been documented by ornithologists for over a century.
   这些鸟类的周期性迁徙模式已被鸟类学家记录了一个多世纪。

3. Rising unemployment proved to be a **cyclical** phenomenon rather than an indication of permanent structural decline.
   失业率上升被证明是一种周期性现象，而非永久性结构衰退的迹象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cyclical" as coming from "cycle" — like a bicycle wheel going round and round, cyclical events repeat in circles.
把"cyclical"想成来自"cycle（循环）"——就像自行车轮不断转动一样，周期性事件循环往复。
