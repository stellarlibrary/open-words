# 🌊 tranquil

🔊 /ˈtræŋkwɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Free from disturbance; calm, serene, and peacefully quiet
**中文：** 安静的；平静的；宁静的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- serene（宁静的）
- placid（平和的）
- peaceful（和平的）
- calm（镇定的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tranquil waters / tranquil setting / tranquil atmosphere / tranquil life**

👉 Example: The tranquil lake reflected the surrounding mountains like a flawless mirror.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **tranquil** village nestled in the valley offered a welcome respite from the noise and chaos of urban life.
   坐落在山谷中的宁静村庄为人们提供了远离城市喧嚣和混乱的可喜休憩之所。

2. Scientists discovered that exposure to **tranquil** natural environments significantly reduces cortisol levels and promotes mental well-being.
   科学家发现，接触宁静的自然环境能显著降低皮质醇水平，促进心理健康。

3. Despite the political upheaval surrounding the election, the diplomat maintained a **tranquil** composure throughout the tense negotiations.
   尽管选举期间政治动荡不安，这位外交官在紧张的谈判中始终保持平静的镇定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tranquil" as "tranquil-izer" — a tranquilizer calms you down, and something tranquil is naturally calm and peaceful.
把"tranquil"联想到"tranquilizer（镇静剂）"——镇静剂让人平静下来，而tranquil就是自然地平静、安宁。
