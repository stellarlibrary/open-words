# 🚫 exclude

🔊 /ɪkˈskluːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To deny access to; to shut out; to bar from participation, consideration, or inclusion
**中文：** 把……排除在外；排斥；拒绝接纳；逐出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bar（阻止）
- prohibit（禁止）
- banish（驱逐）
- omit（省略）
- eliminate（排除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exclude from / exclude the possibility / exclude someone from participation**

👉 Example: The committee voted to exclude the controversial proposal from the final agenda.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The admissions policy was revised to ensure it did not unfairly **exclude** applicants from underrepresented socioeconomic backgrounds.
   招生政策经过修订，以确保不会不公正地将来自弱势社会经济背景的申请者排除在外。

2. Scientists could not **exclude** the possibility that environmental factors played a significant role in the observed population decline.
   科学家们无法排除环境因素在观察到的种群数量下降中起重要作用的可能性。

3. The new regulation effectively **excludes** foreign investors from acquiring majority stakes in domestic telecommunications companies.
   新法规实际上将外国投资者排除在收购国内电信公司多数股权之外。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-clude" as "ex-close" — you close the door on someone, shutting them out and keeping them external.
把"exclude"想成"ex-close"——你对某人关上门，把他们关在外面，使其处于外部。
