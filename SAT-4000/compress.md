# 🗜️ compress

🔊 /kəmˈpres/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To squeeze or press together; to make more compact by reducing size, volume, or duration
**中文：** v. 压缩；浓缩；受压缩小

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- condense（浓缩）
- compact（压紧）
- squeeze（挤压）
- constrict（压缩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **compress data / compress air / compress into / compress a file**

👉 Example: Engineers developed a new algorithm to compress large video files without sacrificing visual quality.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The software is designed to **compress** digital files so they occupy significantly less storage space on the server.
   该软件旨在压缩数字文件，使其在服务器上占用更少的存储空间。

2. Geologists explain that immense tectonic forces **compress** layers of sedimentary rock over millions of years.
   地质学家解释说，巨大的构造力在数百万年间压缩沉积岩层。

3. The editor asked the author to **compress** the lengthy manuscript into a more concise and readable version.
   编辑要求作者将冗长的手稿浓缩为更简洁、更易读的版本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-press" — press things together. When you press things together, you compress them into a smaller space.
把"compress"拆成"com（一起）+ press（按压）"——把东西压在一起，就是压缩。
