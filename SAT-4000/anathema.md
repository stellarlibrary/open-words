# ⚡ anathema

🔊 /əˈnæθəmə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Something or someone that is intensely disliked or loathed; a formal curse by a pope or council of the Church
**中文：** 诅咒；革出教门；被诅咒者；令人厌恶的人或事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abomination（令人厌恶之物）
- curse（诅咒）
- bane（祸根）
- pariah（被排斥者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **anathema to someone / declare anathema / regarded as anathema / absolute anathema**

👉 Example: The idea of censoring academic research is anathema to most university scholars.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Any form of government surveillance is **anathema** to civil liberties advocates who champion individual privacy rights.
   任何形式的政府监控对于捍卫个人隐私权的公民自由倡导者来说都是令人厌恶的。

2. The medieval church pronounced **anathema** upon those who dared to challenge its supreme doctrinal authority.
   中世纪教会对那些胆敢挑战其最高教义权威的人施以逐出教门的惩罚。

3. Compromise was **anathema** to the radical faction, which demanded complete and unconditional acceptance of its platform.
   妥协对于激进派来说是不可接受的，他们要求对其纲领给予完全和无条件的接受。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "anathema" as "a-nathe-ma" — sounds like "I'm not the ma (mother)" — someone rejected and cursed by the family.
把"anathema"想成"a-nathe-ma"——听起来像"我不是妈妈"——被家庭诅咒和抛弃的人。
