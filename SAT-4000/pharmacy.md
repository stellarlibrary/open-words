# 💊 pharmacy

🔊 /ˈfɑːrməsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A store where medicinal drugs are dispensed and sold; the science or practice of preparing and dispensing drugs
**中文：** n. 药房；药剂学；配药业；制药业

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- drugstore（药店）
- apothecary（药剂师；药房）
- dispensary（药房）
- chemist（药店）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pharmacy department / pharmacy school / community pharmacy / pharmacy technician**

👉 Example: She decided to study pharmacy because of her deep interest in medicinal chemistry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The local **pharmacy** implemented an innovative system to ensure patients received accurate dosages of their prescribed medications.
   当地药房实施了一套创新系统，以确保患者获得准确剂量的处方药物。

2. Advances in **pharmacy** have enabled the development of targeted therapies that significantly improve patient treatment outcomes.
   药剂学的进步使得靶向疗法的开发成为可能，显著改善了患者的治疗效果。

3. The university's college of **pharmacy** attracts students from around the world who aspire to careers in pharmaceutical research.
   该大学的药学院吸引了来自世界各地立志从事药学研究的学生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pharm-acy" — "pharm" relates to "pharma" (drugs/medicine), so a pharmacy is the place where medicine is prepared and given out.
把"pharmacy"想成"pharm"（与"pharma"药物相关）+"acy"（场所后缀）——药房就是准备和分发药物的地方。
