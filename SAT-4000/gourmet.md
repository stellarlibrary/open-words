# 🍽️ gourmet

🔊 /ɡʊərˈmeɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A connoisseur of fine food and drink; a person with refined taste in cuisine. (adj.) Of or relating to high-quality or expertly prepared food
**中文：** n. 美食家 adj. 菜肴精美的；高档的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- connoisseur（鉴赏家）
- epicure（美食家）
- gastronome（美食鉴赏家）
- foodie（美食爱好者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gourmet cuisine / gourmet restaurant / gourmet chef / gourmet meal**

👉 Example: The gourmet restaurant on the waterfront earned three Michelin stars for its innovative tasting menu.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. As a dedicated **gourmet**, she traveled extensively across Europe to sample authentic regional dishes and rare local ingredients.
   作为一位专注的美食家，她广泛游历欧洲，品尝正宗的地方菜肴和稀有的本地食材。

2. The university's new dining hall offers **gourmet** options that rival many fine restaurants in the surrounding metropolitan area.
   该大学新建的餐厅提供的精美菜肴可与周边都市区许多高档餐厅媲美。

3. Critics praised the **gourmet** cookbook for its meticulous attention to technique, elevating home cooking to a professional standard.
   评论家称赞这本美食烹饪书对技巧的细致关注，将家庭烹饪提升到了专业水准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gourmet" as "go-ur-may" — a person who says "go, you may" try only the finest dishes, because they accept nothing less than excellence.
把"gourmet"想成"go-ur-may"——一个会说"去吧，你可以"只品尝最精致菜肴的人，因为他们只接受卓越。
