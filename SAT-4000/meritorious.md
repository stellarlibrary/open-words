# 🏅 meritorious

🔊 /ˌmɛrɪˈtɔːriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Deserving reward, praise, or recognition; having merit or value; worthy of commendation for outstanding achievement or service.
**中文：** adj. 有功绩的；有价值的；值得称赞的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commendable（值得表扬的）
- praiseworthy（值得赞美的）
- laudable（值得赞赏的）
- admirable（令人钦佩的）
- exemplary（模范的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **meritorious service / meritorious conduct / meritorious achievement / meritorious action**

👉 Example: The soldier received a medal for meritorious service during the humanitarian relief operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was honored with a lifetime achievement award for her **meritorious** contributions to the field of molecular biology.
   这位教授因其在分子生物学领域的卓越贡献而获得终身成就奖。

2. The court acknowledged the officer's **meritorious** conduct during the rescue operation that saved dozens of civilian lives.
   法院认可了这位军官在拯救数十名平民生命的救援行动中的有功表现。

3. Only candidates with a demonstrated record of **meritorious** academic research will be considered for the prestigious fellowship.
   只有有据可查的卓越学术研究记录的候选人才会被考虑授予这项著名的奖学金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "merit-orious" as "full of merit" — like "glorious" but for merit, describing someone whose achievements shine brightly.
把"meritorious"想成"充满merit（功绩）的"——就像"glorious（光荣的）"但针对功绩，形容一个成就闪耀的人。
