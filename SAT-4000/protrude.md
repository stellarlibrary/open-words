# 👉 protrude

🔊 /proʊˈtruːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To extend beyond or above a surface; to stick out or project outward
**中文：** v. 突出；伸出；使突出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- project（突出）
- jut（伸出）
- stick out（伸出来）
- bulge（鼓起）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **protrude from / protrude through / protruding eyes / protruding rock**

👉 Example: A jagged piece of metal protruded from the wreckage, posing a serious hazard to rescue workers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Several steel reinforcement bars **protruded** dangerously from the demolished structure, requiring immediate attention from the safety inspectors.
   几根钢筋危险地从被拆除的建筑中突出，需要安全检查人员立即处理。

2. The geologist noted how layers of ancient sedimentary rock **protruded** from the eroded hillside, revealing millions of years of history.
   地质学家注意到古老的沉积岩层从被侵蚀的山坡中突出，揭示了数百万年的历史。

3. A narrow peninsula **protrudes** far into the ocean, providing a natural harbor that has sheltered fishing vessels for centuries.
   一个狭窄的半岛远远伸入大海，形成了一个数百年来为渔船提供庇护的天然港湾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-trude" as "push rudely" forward — something that protrudes pushes itself out where it does not belong.
把"protrude"想成"pro（向前）+ trude（推）"——某物向前推出，突出到不属于它的地方。
