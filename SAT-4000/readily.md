# ✅ readily

🔊 /ˈrɛdɪli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Without hesitation or reluctance; willingly; easily and without difficulty
**中文：** adv. 容易地；乐意地；无困难地；欣然地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- willingly（乐意地）
- easily（容易地）
- promptly（迅速地）
- effortlessly（毫不费力地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **readily available / readily apparent / readily accessible / readily agree / readily admit**

👉 Example: The information is readily available on the university's official website for all prospective students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Fresh produce is not **readily** available in many remote rural communities, contributing to significant nutritional deficiencies among residents.
   在许多偏远的农村社区，新鲜农产品并不容易获得，这导致居民出现严重的营养缺乏。

2. The distinguished scholar **readily** acknowledged the limitations of her research and welcomed constructive criticism from her academic peers.
   这位杰出学者欣然承认了自己研究的局限性，并欢迎学术同行的建设性批评。

3. The solution to the complex mathematical problem was not **readily** apparent, requiring several hours of careful analysis and computation.
   这道复杂数学问题的解答并不显而易见，需要数小时的仔细分析和计算。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "readily" as "ready + ly" — when you are ready, you can do things easily and willingly without delay.
把"readily"想成"ready（准备好的）+ ly"——当你准备好了，就能轻松、乐意地做事，毫不拖延。
