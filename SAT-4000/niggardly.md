# 💰 niggardly

🔊 /ˈnɪɡərdli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not generous; stingy; giving or spending as little as possible. (adv.) In a mean or miserly manner
**中文：** adj. 吝啬的；小气的；很少量的 adv. 小气地；很少量地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stingy（吝啬的）
- miserly（守财的）
- parsimonious（过分节俭的）
- penny-pinching（一毛不拔的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **niggardly sum / niggardly attitude / niggardly portion / niggardly spending**

👉 Example: The foundation offered only a niggardly donation despite its considerable financial resources.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation's **niggardly** contribution to the disaster relief fund drew sharp criticism from humanitarian organizations worldwide.
   该公司对救灾基金的吝啬捐款遭到了全球人道主义组织的严厉批评。

2. Despite enormous profits, the landlord provided only **niggardly** maintenance for the aging apartment building and its deteriorating facilities.
   尽管利润丰厚，房东对老化的公寓大楼及其恶化的设施只进行了极少的维护。

3. The committee allocated a **niggardly** budget for scientific research, severely limiting the scope of the proposed investigation.
   委员会为科学研究分配了微薄的预算，严重限制了拟议调查的范围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "niggard" (a miser) + "-ly" — someone who is niggardly holds onto every penny like a miser guarding a treasure chest.
把"niggardly"想成"niggard（吝啬鬼）"加"-ly"——一个吝啬的人紧紧抓住每一分钱，像守财奴守护宝箱一样。
