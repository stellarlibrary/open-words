# 💡 intermittent

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈmɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring at irregular intervals; not continuous or steady; stopping and starting repeatedly
**中文：** adj. 间歇的；断断续续的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sporadic（零星的）
- periodic（周期性的）
- irregular（不规则的）
- fitful（断断续续的）
- occasional（偶尔的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intermittent rain / intermittent fasting / intermittent signal / intermittent problems**

👉 Example: The region experienced intermittent power outages throughout the winter storm season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **intermittent** nature of the Wi-Fi signal made it nearly impossible to conduct the video conference without repeated disruptions.
   无线网络信号断断续续的特性使得在没有反复中断的情况下进行视频会议几乎不可能。

2. Researchers observed **intermittent** volcanic activity over the course of several decades before the major eruption finally occurred.
   研究人员在大规模喷发最终发生之前的数十年间观察到了间歇性的火山活动。

3. The patient reported **intermittent** chest pain that appeared without warning and subsided within minutes of each episode.
   患者报告了间歇性胸痛，这种疼痛毫无预兆地出现，并在每次发作后数分钟内消退。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intermittent" as "inter-MITT-ent" — like a baseball mitt that catches the ball on and off, intermittent means something that comes and goes.
把"intermittent"想成"inter-MITT-ent"——就像棒球手套时接时不接球一样，intermittent意为时断时续的。
