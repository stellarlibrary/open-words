# 😶 pallid

🔊 /ˈpælɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Pale, especially because of poor health; lacking in vitality or interest; dull
**中文：** adj. 苍白的；暗淡的；无生气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wan（苍白的）
- ashen（灰白的）
- pasty（苍白无血色的）
- lackluster（无光泽的）
- sallow（蜡黄的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pallid complexion / pallid face / pallid light / pallid performance**

👉 Example: The patient's pallid complexion alarmed the attending physician during the morning examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prisoner emerged from months of solitary confinement with a **pallid** face that revealed his prolonged suffering.
   囚犯在数月的单独监禁后露出苍白的面容，显露出他长期的痛苦。

2. Critics dismissed the director's latest film as a **pallid** imitation of his earlier, more groundbreaking works.
   评论家们认为该导演的最新电影不过是其早期更具开创性作品的苍白模仿。

3. Under the **pallid** glow of the fluorescent lights, the researchers continued their painstaking analysis of the specimens.
   在荧光灯暗淡的光线下，研究人员继续对标本进行细致的分析。
