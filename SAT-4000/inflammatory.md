# 💢 inflammatory

🔊 /ɪnˈflæmətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Tending to arouse strong feelings, especially anger or hostility; relating to or causing inflammation of a body part
**中文：** adj. 煽动性的；炎症性的；激动的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- provocative（挑衅的）
- incendiary（煽动性的）
- seditious（煽动叛乱的）
- rabble-rousing（煽动民心的）
- inciting（煽动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inflammatory remarks / inflammatory rhetoric / inflammatory response / inflammatory language**

👉 Example: The politician's inflammatory speech incited protests across the country.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist was criticized for publishing **inflammatory** articles that deliberately distorted facts to provoke public outrage and division.
   这位记者因发表煽动性文章而受到批评，这些文章故意歪曲事实以激起公众愤怒和分裂。

2. The physician diagnosed the patient with an **inflammatory** bowel condition that required immediate treatment and dietary modifications.
   医生诊断该患者患有炎症性肠病，需要立即治疗并调整饮食。

3. University administrators condemned the **inflammatory** posters distributed on campus as harmful to the spirit of civil academic discourse.
   大学管理层谴责了在校园分发的煽动性海报，认为其有害于文明学术讨论的精神。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inflame" + "atory" — inflammatory words "inflame" people's emotions, setting their passions on fire like a match to kindling.
把"inflammatory"想成"inflame（使燃烧、激怒）" + "atory"——煽动性的言辞"点燃"人们的情绪，如同火柴点燃柴火。
