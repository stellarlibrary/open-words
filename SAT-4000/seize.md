# ✊ seize

🔊 /siːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To take hold of suddenly and forcibly; to capture or take possession of; to comprehend; to arrest
**中文：** v. 抓住，逮住；夺取

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grab（抓住）
- capture（夺取）
- confiscate（没收）
- apprehend（逮捕）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seize the opportunity / seize power / seize control / seize assets**

👉 Example: The revolutionary forces moved swiftly to seize control of all government buildings in the capital.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Authorities **seized** approximately two million dollars worth of counterfeit merchandise from warehouses operating along the border.
   当局从边境沿线运营的仓库中没收了价值约两百万美元的假冒商品。

2. The ambitious entrepreneur understood that she must **seize** every opportunity before her competitors could react to market changes.
   这位雄心勃勃的企业家明白，她必须在竞争对手对市场变化作出反应之前抓住每一个机会。

3. The military commanders planned to **seize** the strategic mountain pass before enemy reinforcements could arrive at dawn.
   军事指挥官们计划在敌方增援部队黎明到达之前夺取这一战略性山口。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "seize" sounding like "sees" — when you see something you want, you reach out and seize it immediately.
把"seize"想成发音类似"sees"——当你看到想要的东西时，你立刻伸手去抓住它。
