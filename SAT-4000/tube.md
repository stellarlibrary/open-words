# 🧪 tube

🔊 /tjuːb/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A hollow cylinder used for conveying fluids; an electron tube; a test tube. (v.) To convey or transport through a tube
**中文：** n. 管；电子管；试管 v. 用管输送

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pipe（管道）
- cylinder（圆筒）
- conduit（导管）
- channel（通道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **test tube / tube feeding / vacuum tube / go down the tubes**

👉 Example: The chemist carefully heated the solution in a glass test tube over the Bunsen burner.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician carefully inserted the sample into a sterile **tube** before placing it in the centrifuge.
   实验室技术员小心翼翼地将样本放入无菌试管中，然后将其放入离心机。

2. Early computers relied on vacuum **tubes** for processing, which consumed enormous amounts of electricity and generated excessive heat.
   早期计算机依靠电子管进行处理，这消耗了大量电力并产生了过多的热量。

3. Engineers designed a reinforced titanium **tube** to transport pressurized gas safely across the hazardous industrial complex.
   工程师设计了一种加固钛管，以安全地在危险的工业综合体中输送加压气体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tube" as the shape itself — a long, hollow cylinder like a straw, which is the most basic form of a tube.
把"tube"想成它本身的形状——一个像吸管一样的长空心圆柱体，这是管子的最基本形态。
