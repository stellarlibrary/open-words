# 🧠 plasticity

🔊 /plæˈstɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being easily shaped or molded; the ability of an organism or material to adapt and change in response to external conditions
**中文：** n. 塑性，可塑性；适应性；柔软性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- malleability（可塑性）
- flexibility（灵活性）
- adaptability（适应性）
- pliability（柔韧性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **neural plasticity / brain plasticity / plasticity of materials / synaptic plasticity**

👉 Example: The remarkable plasticity of the developing brain allows children to recover from injuries more effectively than adults.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent studies on neural **plasticity** have revolutionized our understanding of how the brain adapts following traumatic injury or neurological disease.
   最近关于神经可塑性的研究彻底改变了我们对大脑在创伤性损伤或神经疾病后如何适应的理解。

2. The **plasticity** of certain advanced polymers enables engineers to design structures that flex under stress without sustaining permanent structural damage.
   某些先进聚合物的可塑性使工程师能够设计在应力下弯曲而不会造成永久性结构损坏的结构。

3. Cultural **plasticity** allows societies to evolve their traditions and institutions in response to shifting economic and environmental circumstances.
   文化可塑性使社会能够根据不断变化的经济和环境状况来演变其传统和制度。
