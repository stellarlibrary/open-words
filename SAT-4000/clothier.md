# 👔 clothier

🔊 /ˈkloʊðiər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person or company that manufactures or sells clothes or cloth; a dealer in garments or fine fabrics
**中文：** n. 衣商；呢绒商；服装制造商；布商

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outfitter（服装商）
- haberdasher（男装商）
- tailor（裁缝）
- draper（布商）
- garment dealer（服装经销商）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **local clothier / fine clothier / master clothier / visit the clothier / renowned clothier**

👉 Example: The distinguished clothier on Main Street has been dressing the city's elite for over fifty years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosperous **clothier** supplied fine wool garments to the aristocracy throughout the eighteenth century.
   这位富有的衣商在整个十八世纪向贵族阶层供应精美的羊毛服装。

2. As a respected **clothier**, he prided himself on sourcing only the highest quality fabrics from renowned mills.
   作为一名受人尊敬的布商，他以只从知名工厂采购最高品质的面料而自豪。

3. The family-owned **clothier** adapted to modern trends while preserving its tradition of expert craftsmanship and elegance.
   这家家族经营的服装店在保持精湛工艺和优雅传统的同时适应了现代潮流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clothier" as "cloth + ier (one who deals in)" — a clothier is simply one who deals in cloth and clothing.
把"clothier"拆解为"cloth（布料）+ ier（从事……的人）"——clothier就是从事布料和服装生意的人。
