# 📡 antenna

🔊 /ænˈten.ə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A rod, wire, or other device used to transmit or receive radio or television signals; in biology, one of a pair of long, thin sensory appendages on the heads of insects, crustaceans, and similar organisms.

**中文：** 天线（用于发射或接收无线电或电视信号的装置）；触角（昆虫、甲壳类动物等头部的细长感觉器官）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- aerial（天线）
- receiver（接收器）
- feeler（触角）
- sensor（传感器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** television antenna, radio antenna, satellite antenna, insect antenna, extend one's antennae

**Example:** The butterfly uses its **antennae** to detect subtle chemical signals in its surrounding environment.

**Note:** The plural form is "antennae" (biology) or "antennas" (electronics).

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team installed a high-frequency **antenna** on the rooftop to capture signals from the distant satellite.
   研究团队在屋顶安装了一根高频天线，以接收来自远方卫星的信号。

2. The entomologist observed that the moth's **antennae** were remarkably sensitive to pheromones released by potential mates.
   昆虫学家观察到这种蛾的触角对潜在配偶释放的信息素极为敏感。

3. A skilled diplomat must keep her political **antennae** finely tuned to shifts in public opinion and policy.
   一位熟练的外交官必须保持其政治触觉高度敏锐，以感知公众舆论和政策的变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Picture an ant with long feelers on its head — "ant" + "enna." Both an insect's feelers and a TV antenna stick up to detect signals from the surroundings. 想象一只蚂蚁（ant）头上长着长长的触角——ant + enna。无论是昆虫的触角还是电视天线，都是竖起来接收周围信号的。
