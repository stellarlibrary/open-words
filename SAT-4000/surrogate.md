# 🔄 surrogate

🔊 /ˈsɜːrəɡət/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substitute; a deputy; a probate judge. (v.) To act as a substitute. (adj.) Serving as a replacement or substitute.
**中文：** n. 代理者；代用品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- substitute（替代品）
- proxy（代理）
- deputy（代表）
- stand-in（替身）
- replacement（替换者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surrogate mother / surrogate parent / surrogate for / act as a surrogate**

👉 Example: In the absence of the director, the assistant served as a surrogate for all major decisions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orphaned child found a **surrogate** family through the community adoption program established by local authorities.
   这个孤儿通过当地政府设立的社区收养计划找到了一个替代家庭。

2. Virtual reality has become a **surrogate** for real-world experiences in many educational and therapeutic settings.
   虚拟现实在许多教育和治疗环境中已成为真实体验的替代品。

3. The diplomat acted as a **surrogate** for the president during the international negotiations held in Geneva.
   在日内瓦举行的国际谈判中，这位外交官作为总统的代理人出席。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surrogate" as "sur-ro-gate" — someone who goes through the gate on your behalf, standing in as your substitute.
把"surrogate"想成"sur-ro-gate"——某人代替你穿过大门，充当你的替身。
