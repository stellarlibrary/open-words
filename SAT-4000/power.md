# ⚡ power

🔊 /ˈpaʊər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability or capacity to do something; physical strength or force; political authority or control; (math) an exponent. (v.) To supply with energy; to move with great force
**中文：** n. 力量，能力；电力，功率；政权，势力；幂 v. 激励；供以动力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- authority（权力）
- strength（力量）
- influence（影响力）
- energy（能量）
- dominion（统治权）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **power struggle / purchasing power / power of attorney / to the power of / power grid**

👉 Example: The engine is powered by a revolutionary new battery that lasts significantly longer than its predecessors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The constitutional amendment sought to limit the executive branch's **power** and establish more effective checks and balances.
   这项宪法修正案旨在限制行政部门的权力并建立更有效的制衡机制。

2. In mathematics, two raised to the **power** of ten equals one thousand and twenty-four, a fundamental concept in computing.
   在数学中，二的十次幂等于一千零二十四，这是计算机科学中的一个基本概念。

3. The renewable energy initiative aimed to **power** the entire metropolitan district using only solar and wind resources.
   该可再生能源计划旨在仅使用太阳能和风能为整个大都市区供电。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "power" as the force that "empowers" everything — from electrical power running machines to political power running nations.
把"power"想成"赋能"一切的力量——从驱动机器的电力到治理国家的政权。
