# 🚨 emergency

🔊 /ɪˈmɜːrdʒənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A serious, unexpected, and often dangerous situation requiring immediate action
**中文：** n. 紧急情况；突发事件

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crisis（危机）
- urgency（紧迫）
- catastrophe（灾难）
- predicament（困境）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **declare an emergency / emergency room / emergency response / in case of emergency**

👉 Example: The hospital activated its emergency protocols when dozens of injured passengers arrived simultaneously.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The governor declared a state of **emergency** after the unprecedented flooding devastated several coastal communities.
   州长在史无前例的洪水摧毁了数个沿海社区后宣布进入紧急状态。

2. Medical professionals must remain composed during an **emergency** to provide effective and timely treatment to patients.
   医疗专业人员在紧急情况下必须保持冷静，以便为患者提供有效及时的治疗。

3. The university established a comprehensive **emergency** preparedness plan to safeguard students and faculty during natural disasters.
   该大学制定了一项全面的应急准备计划，以在自然灾害期间保护学生和教职员工。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "emerge-ncy" — something that suddenly "emerges" and demands your immediate attention.
把"emergency"想成"emerge（浮现）+ ncy"——某件事突然浮现出来，需要你立即关注。
