# 🏛️ rightful

🔊 /ˈraɪtfʊl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Legitimate, lawful, or having a just and legal claim; morally right and fair
**中文：** adj. 公正的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- legitimate（合法的）
- lawful（合法的）
- just（公正的）
- proper（正当的）
- legal（合法的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rightful owner / rightful heir / rightful place / rightful claim / rightful share**

👉 Example: The court ruled that the artifact must be returned to its rightful owner immediately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years of legal dispute, the museum finally returned the stolen paintings to their **rightful** owners in accordance with the settlement.
   经过多年法律纠纷，博物馆终于根据和解协议将被盗画作归还给其合法所有者。

2. The prince was denied his **rightful** claim to the throne through a series of political manipulations orchestrated by rival factions.
   王子因敌对派系策划的一系列政治操纵而被剥夺了对王位的合法继承权。

3. Environmental advocates argue that future generations have a **rightful** expectation of inheriting a planet with sustainable natural resources.
   环境倡导者认为，后代有正当的期望继承一个拥有可持续自然资源的星球。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rightful" as "right" + "-ful" — full of right, meaning something that is completely justified and legally proper.
把"rightful"想成"right"加上"-ful"——充满正当性，意味着某事完全合理且合法。
