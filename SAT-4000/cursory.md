# 👀 cursory

🔊 /ˈkɜːrsəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Hasty and therefore not thorough or detailed; performed with little attention to detail
**中文：** adj. 粗略的；草率的；匆忙的；仓促的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- superficial（表面的）
- hasty（匆忙的）
- perfunctory（敷衍的）
- brief（简短的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cursory glance / cursory examination / cursory review / cursory inspection**

👉 Example: A cursory glance at the report revealed several obvious errors in the financial calculations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inspector conducted only a **cursory** examination of the building, failing to identify critical structural deficiencies.
   检查员只对建筑物进行了粗略的检查，未能发现关键的结构缺陷。

2. Even a **cursory** review of the historical evidence suggests that the traditional narrative requires significant revision.
   即使对历史证据进行粗略的审查，也表明传统叙述需要重大修正。

3. The committee gave the proposal merely a **cursory** reading before voting to approve it without substantive debate.
   委员会只是草草浏览了提案，就在没有实质性辩论的情况下投票批准了它。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cursory" as "cursor" — a cursor moves quickly across the screen, just as a cursory review moves quickly over details.
把"cursory"联想到"cursor（光标）"——光标在屏幕上快速移动，就像草率的审查快速掠过细节一样。
