# :cloud: repine

:speaker: /rɪˈpaɪn/<br>
:label: *verb | 动词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To feel discontent or dissatisfaction; to complain or fret about one's circumstances
**中文：** v. 抱怨；不满；闷闷不乐

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- lament（哀叹）
- grumble（抱怨）
- brood（忧思）
- fret（烦恼）
- complain（抱怨）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **repine at fate / repine over losses / repine about circumstances / cease to repine**

:point_right: Example: Rather than repine over missed opportunities, successful individuals channel their energy into future endeavors.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher urged his students not to **repine** at the hardships of life but to embrace them as necessary lessons.
   这位哲学家敦促学生们不要为生活的艰辛而抱怨，而应将其视为必要的人生课堂。

2. She refused to **repine** over her modest upbringing and instead worked tirelessly to build a distinguished academic career.
   她拒绝为自己简朴的成长环境而怨天尤人，反而不懈努力建立了一段杰出的学术生涯。

3. The exiled poet continued to **repine** about the loss of his homeland in verses filled with sorrow and deep longing.
   流亡的诗人在充满悲伤和深切思念的诗篇中继续为失去祖国而悲叹。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "repine" as "re-PINE" — like a pine tree that sways and moans in the wind, you pine and complain repeatedly about your troubles.
把"repine"想成"re-pine（反复思念）"——就像松树在风中摇曳呻吟，你反复为烦恼哀叹抱怨。
