# 🔍 validate

🔊 /ˈvælɪdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To confirm or establish the truth, accuracy, or legitimacy of something; to make legally valid
**中文：** v. 证实，验证；使合法化；确认

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- verify（验证）
- confirm（确认）
- substantiate（证实）
- authenticate（鉴定）
- corroborate（佐证）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **validate a theory / validate results / validate a claim / validate a ticket / validate someone's feelings**

👉 Example: The experiment was designed to validate the hypothesis proposed by the research team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Independent laboratories were commissioned to **validate** the pharmaceutical company's clinical trial results before regulatory approval was granted.
   在获得监管批准之前，独立实验室受委托验证该制药公司的临床试验结果。

2. The archaeologist sought additional evidence to **validate** her theory regarding the ancient civilization's trade routes across the Mediterranean.
   这位考古学家寻找更多证据来证实她关于古代文明跨地中海贸易路线的理论。

3. Peer review serves as a critical mechanism to **validate** scholarly research before publication in reputable academic journals.
   同行评审是在知名学术期刊发表论文之前验证学术研究的关键机制。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "validate" as "valid + ate" — to make something valid by eating away all doubt through verification.
把"validate"想成"valid（有效的）+ ate（使…）"——通过验证消除所有疑虑，使某事变得有效。
