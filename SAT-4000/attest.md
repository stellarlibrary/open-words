# ✅ attest

🔊 /əˈtest/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To provide evidence or proof of something; to declare or certify something to be true or genuine
**中文：** v. 证明；证实

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- certify（证明）
- verify（核实）
- corroborate（证实）
- substantiate（佐证）
- affirm（确认）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **attest to the fact / attest to the truth / attest to the quality / as many witnesses can attest**

👉 Example: Several witnesses came forward to attest to the defendant's presence at the charity event that evening.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient inscriptions **attest** to the existence of a thriving civilization in this region thousands of years ago.
   这些古代铭文证明了数千年前该地区存在着一个繁荣的文明。

2. Her impressive portfolio and stellar references **attest** to her exceptional competence as an architectural designer.
   她令人印象深刻的作品集和出色的推荐信证明了她作为建筑设计师的卓越能力。

3. The notary was required to **attest** that all signatures on the legal document were authentic and voluntary.
   公证人被要求证明法律文件上的所有签名都是真实且自愿的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "attest" as "a-TEST" — like putting something to a test to prove it is true or genuine.
把"attest"想成"a-TEST"——就像对某事进行测试以证明其真实性或可靠性。
