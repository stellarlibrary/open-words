# 🏫 polytechnic

🔊 /ˌpɑːliˈteknɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or involving instruction in many technical subjects or applied sciences. (n.) An institution of higher education offering courses in various technical and vocational subjects
**中文：** adj. 各种工艺的；综合技术的 n. 工艺学校；理工专科学校

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- technical institute（技术学院）
- vocational school（职业学校）
- trade school（工业学校）
- institute of technology（理工学院）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **polytechnic institute / polytechnic university / attend a polytechnic / polytechnic education**

👉 Example: She enrolled at the polytechnic to study engineering before transferring to a research university.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government invested heavily in **polytechnic** education to address the growing demand for skilled workers in advanced manufacturing.
   政府大力投资综合技术教育，以应对先进制造业对技术工人日益增长的需求。

2. Many distinguished engineers and scientists received their foundational training at **polytechnic** institutions rather than traditional liberal arts universities.
   许多杰出的工程师和科学家在理工专科学校而非传统文理大学接受了基础培训。

3. The **polytechnic** curriculum emphasized practical laboratory experience alongside theoretical coursework in applied sciences and engineering disciplines.
   理工学校的课程强调实际的实验室经验，同时配合应用科学和工程学科的理论课程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "poly" (many) + "technic" (skills/arts) — a polytechnic teaches many technical skills, covering a wide range of practical disciplines.
把"polytechnic"拆成"poly（多）"+"technic（技术）"——理工学校教授多种技术技能，涵盖广泛的实用学科。
