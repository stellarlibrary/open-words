# ✋ relinquish

🔊 /rɪˈlɪŋkwɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To voluntarily give up, surrender, or let go of a right, possession, or claim
**中文：** v. 放弃；放手；让渡

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surrender（交出）
- abandon（放弃）
- forfeit（丧失）
- renounce（放弃）
- cede（让与）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **relinquish control / relinquish power / relinquish a claim / relinquish custody / relinquish one's hold**

👉 Example: The aging monarch was reluctant to relinquish the throne to the younger generation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dictator refused to **relinquish** power despite mounting international pressure and calls for democratic elections across the country.
   尽管面临日益增加的国际压力和全国范围内的民主选举呼声，这位独裁者仍拒绝放弃权力。

2. Under the terms of the settlement, the company was required to **relinquish** all intellectual property rights to the disputed patent.
   根据和解条款，该公司被要求放弃对争议专利的所有知识产权。

3. The scientist chose to **relinquish** her prestigious position at the university in order to pursue independent humanitarian research.
   这位科学家选择放弃她在大学的显赫职位，以从事独立的人道主义研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "relinquish" as "re-link-wish" — you wish to unlink yourself from something, so you let it go and give it up.
把"relinquish"想成"re-link-wish"——你希望把自己从某物上解开链接，所以你放手并放弃它。
