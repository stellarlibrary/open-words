# 😴 somnolent

🔊 /ˈsɑːmnələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inducing or tending to induce sleep; drowsy or sleepy
**中文：** adj. 想睡的；催眠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- drowsy（昏昏欲睡的）
- sleepy（困倦的）
- lethargic（无精打采的）
- torpid（迟钝的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **somnolent state / somnolent atmosphere / somnolent afternoon / somnolent effect**

👉 Example: The somnolent warmth of the afternoon sun made it nearly impossible to concentrate on the lecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **somnolent** village seemed frozen in time, with its quiet streets and gently swaying trees in the midday heat.
   这个令人昏昏欲睡的村庄似乎被时间凝固了，正午的街道寂静无声，树木轻轻摇曳。

2. After the heavy meal, the students fell into a **somnolent** state and struggled to remain attentive during the seminar.
   饱餐之后，学生们陷入了昏昏欲睡的状态，在研讨会上难以保持专注。

3. The professor's monotonous delivery had a distinctly **somnolent** effect on even the most dedicated members of the audience.
   教授单调的演讲方式对即使是最专注的听众也产生了明显的催眠效果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "somn-" from Latin "somnus" (sleep) — "somnolent" literally means "full of sleep," just like an insomniac cannot sleep.
把"somn-"联想到拉丁语"somnus"（睡眠）——"somnolent"字面意思是"充满睡意的"，和insomnia（失眠）共用同一词根。
