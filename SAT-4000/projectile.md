# 🎯 projectile

🔊 /prəˈdʒɛktaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An object propelled through the air, especially a weapon or missile. (adj.) Relating to or designed for propulsion
**中文：** n. 射弹；抛射体 adj. 抛射的；抛掷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- missile（导弹）
- bullet（子弹）
- shell（炮弹）
- rocket（火箭弹）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **projectile motion / launch a projectile / projectile weapon / projectile trajectory**

👉 Example: In physics class, students calculated the trajectory of a projectile launched at a forty-five degree angle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient catapult could hurl a heavy stone **projectile** over two hundred meters toward the enemy fortifications.
   这台古代投石机可以将沉重的石制抛射体投掷到两百多米外的敌方防御工事。

2. Engineers analyzed the **projectile** motion of the rocket to determine its optimal launch angle and velocity.
   工程师们分析了火箭的抛体运动，以确定其最佳发射角度和速度。

3. The forensic team examined the **projectile** recovered from the scene to identify the type of firearm used.
   法医团队检查了从现场回收的弹丸，以确定所使用的枪支类型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "project-ile" — something that is "projected" or thrown forward through the air, like a project launched into motion.
把"projectile"想成"project（投射）+ ile"——某物被"投射"或抛向空中，就像一个被发射出去的物体。
