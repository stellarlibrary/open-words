# 🎨 eclectic

🔊 /ɪˈklɛktɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources. (n.) A person who adopts an eclectic approach
**中文：** adj. 折中的；博采众长的；兼收并蓄的 n. 折衷派的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diverse（多样的）
- varied（各种各样的）
- wide-ranging（广泛的）
- multifaceted（多方面的）
- heterogeneous（异质的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eclectic taste / eclectic mix / eclectic collection / eclectic approach**

👉 Example: The museum boasts an eclectic collection that spans multiple centuries and artistic traditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect was known for her **eclectic** style, seamlessly blending modernist geometry with traditional Eastern motifs.
   这位建筑师以其兼收并蓄的风格著称，将现代主义几何与东方传统图案巧妙融合。

2. His **eclectic** reading habits encompassed everything from quantum physics to medieval poetry to contemporary philosophy.
   他博采众长的阅读习惯涵盖了从量子物理到中世纪诗歌再到当代哲学的各个领域。

3. The committee adopted an **eclectic** approach to curriculum reform, drawing from educational models across several different countries.
   委员会采用折中的方法进行课程改革，借鉴了多个不同国家的教育模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ec-lect-ic" as "elect to pick" from many sources — someone who elects the best ideas from everywhere is eclectic.
把"eclectic"想成"elect to pick"（选择去挑选）——一个从各处挑选最佳想法的人就是博采众长的。
