# ✂️ truncated

🔊 /ˈtrʌŋkeɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Shortened by having a part cut off; reduced in length or scope; abbreviated or curtailed
**中文：** 缩短了的；被删节的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abbreviated（缩写的）
- shortened（缩短的）
- curtailed（削减的）
- abridged（删节的）
- condensed（精简的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **truncated version / truncated form / truncated data / truncated text**

👉 Example: The editor published a truncated version of the manuscript due to space constraints.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journal published a **truncated** version of the original research paper, omitting several critical appendices and data tables.
   该期刊发表了原始研究论文的删节版，省略了几个关键的附录和数据表。

2. Budget constraints forced the committee to approve only a **truncated** timeline for the ambitious infrastructure project.
   预算限制迫使委员会只批准了这一宏大基础设施项目的缩短时间表。

3. The **truncated** dataset yielded incomplete results, compelling the researchers to repeat the entire analysis from scratch.
   被删节的数据集产生了不完整的结果，迫使研究人员从头重新进行整个分析。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "truncated" as related to "trunk" — like a tree trunk that has been cut short, losing its branches and full height.
把"truncated"联想到"trunk"（树干）——就像一棵树干被砍短了，失去了枝条和完整的高度。
