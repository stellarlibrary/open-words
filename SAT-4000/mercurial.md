# 🌡️ mercurial

🔊 /mɜːrˈkjʊəriəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Subject to sudden or unpredictable changes in mood or behavior; lively and quick-witted; of or relating to the planet Mercury or the element mercury. (n.) A preparation containing mercury.
**中文：** adj. 易变的；精明的；水银的；水星的 n. 汞制剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- volatile（易变的）
- capricious（反复无常的）
- fickle（善变的）
- erratic（不稳定的）
- temperamental（喜怒无常的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mercurial temperament / mercurial personality / mercurial mood / mercurial nature**

👉 Example: The artist's mercurial temperament made collaboration with her both thrilling and unpredictable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The CEO's **mercurial** leadership style left employees uncertain about which strategic direction the company would take next.
   这位首席执行官善变的领导风格让员工们对公司下一步的战略方向感到不确定。

2. Financial analysts attributed the stock market's **mercurial** behavior to geopolitical tensions and conflicting economic indicators.
   金融分析师将股市的多变表现归因于地缘政治紧张局势和相互矛盾的经济指标。

3. The novelist's **mercurial** protagonist shifts between euphoria and despair across the chapters, reflecting the human condition.
   小说家笔下善变的主人公在各章节间在欢欣与绝望之间切换，反映了人类的生存状态。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mercurial" as related to "Mercury" — the planet with extreme temperature swings, just like a mercurial person swings between moods.
把"mercurial"想成与"Mercury（水星）"有关——这颗行星温度变化极端，就像一个mercurial的人在不同情绪之间剧烈波动。
