# ⚡ alacrity

🔊 /əˈlækrɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Brisk and cheerful readiness; eager willingness combined with quickness
**中文：** 敏捷；轻快；乐意

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eagerness（热切）
- promptness（迅速）
- enthusiasm（热情）
- willingness（乐意）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with alacrity / accept with alacrity / respond with alacrity**
👉 Example: She accepted the challenging assignment with alacrity, eager to prove her capabilities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The volunteers responded with remarkable **alacrity** when the organization issued an urgent call for assistance.
   当该组织发出紧急援助号召时，志愿者们以非凡的敏捷做出了回应。

2. He completed every task assigned to him with **alacrity**, quickly earning the respect of his supervisors.
   他敏捷地完成了分配给他的每一项任务，很快赢得了上级的尊重。

3. The students embraced the new curriculum with surprising **alacrity**, demonstrating genuine enthusiasm for the revised approach.
   学生们以令人惊讶的热忱接受了新课程，展现出对改革方案的真正热情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alacrity" as "a lack of delay" — someone with alacrity acts quickly and willingly without hesitation.
把"alacrity"想成"a lack of delay"（没有拖延）——有alacrity的人行动迅速且乐意，毫不犹豫。
