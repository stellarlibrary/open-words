# 🎁 donate

🔊 /doʊˈneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give money, goods, or resources voluntarily to a charitable cause or organization
**中文：** v. 捐赠；捐献；赠送

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contribute（贡献）
- bestow（赐予）
- grant（授予）
- give（给予）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **donate to a charity / donate blood / donate money / donate organs**

👉 Example: Many alumni donate generously to the university's scholarship fund each year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philanthropist decided to **donate** a substantial portion of her fortune to medical research institutions worldwide.
   这位慈善家决定将她大部分财产捐赠给世界各地的医学研究机构。

2. Citizens were encouraged to **donate** blood during the national emergency following the devastating earthquake.
   在毁灭性地震后的全国紧急状态期间，公民被鼓励献血。

3. The corporation pledged to **donate** ten percent of its annual profits to environmental conservation programs.
   该公司承诺将其年利润的百分之十捐赠给环境保护项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "donate" as "do + nate (nation)" — you do something good for the nation by giving generously.
把"donate"想成"do + nate (nation)"——你通过慷慨给予为国家做了一件好事。
