# 🌱 renascent

🔊 /rɪˈnæsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Being reborn; showing renewed growth, vigor, or activity after a period of dormancy
**中文：** adj. 新生的，复活的，复兴的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reviving（复苏的）
- resurgent（复兴的）
- reborn（重生的）
- rejuvenated（焕发活力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **renascent spirit / renascent culture / renascent movement / renascent interest**

👉 Example: The renascent enthusiasm for environmental conservation inspired a new generation of activists.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **renascent** democratic movement gained momentum as citizens organized peaceful demonstrations across the country.
   新生的民主运动随着公民在全国各地组织和平示威而不断壮大。

2. Scholars have observed a **renascent** interest in ancient philosophical traditions among contemporary university students.
   学者们观察到当代大学生对古代哲学传统产生了复兴的兴趣。

3. The **renascent** manufacturing sector brought thousands of new employment opportunities to the previously declining industrial region.
   复兴的制造业为此前衰落的工业区带来了数千个新的就业机会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-NASCENT" — "nascent" means newly born, and "re-" means again, so renascent means being born again or reviving.
把"renascent"拆开——"nascent"意为新生的，"re-"意为再次，所以renascent就是重新诞生、复兴的意思。
