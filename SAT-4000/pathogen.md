# 🦠 pathogen

🔊 /ˈpæθədʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A microorganism, such as a bacterium or virus, that can cause disease in a host
**中文：** n. 病原体；病菌

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- microbe（微生物）
- germ（病菌）
- bacterium（细菌）
- virus（病毒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deadly pathogen / airborne pathogen / pathogen transmission / pathogen detection / plant pathogen**

👉 Example: The laboratory identified the pathogen responsible for the outbreak within forty-eight hours.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers developed a rapid diagnostic test capable of identifying the **pathogen** in blood samples within less than thirty minutes.
   研究人员开发了一种快速诊断测试，能够在不到三十分钟内识别血液样本中的病原体。

2. The emergence of antibiotic-resistant **pathogen** strains has become one of the most pressing challenges in modern public health.
   耐抗生素病原体菌株的出现已成为现代公共卫生中最紧迫的挑战之一。

3. Environmental scientists investigated how agricultural runoff may facilitate the spread of waterborne **pathogen** populations in rural communities.
   环境科学家研究了农业径流如何可能促进水源性病原体在农村社区的传播。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "patho-gen" as "patho (suffering) + gen (creator)" — a pathogen is something that generates suffering or disease.
把"pathogen"想成"patho（痛苦）+ gen（产生者）"——病原体是产生痛苦或疾病的东西。
