# 👸 belle

🔊 /bɛl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A beautiful woman, especially the most beautiful woman at a social gathering or in a particular group
**中文：** n. 美女；美人（尤指社交场合中最美的女子）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beauty（美人）
- goddess（女神）
- enchantress（迷人的女子）
- stunner（绝色佳人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the belle of the ball / the belle of the town / a Southern belle**

👉 Example: She was widely regarded as the belle of the ball at the annual charity gala.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. As the undisputed **belle** of the ball, she captivated every guest with her grace and elegant demeanor.
   作为舞会上公认的最美女子，她以优雅的风度吸引了在场的每一位宾客。

2. The novel's protagonist, a charming Southern **belle**, navigated the complexities of antebellum society with remarkable poise.
   小说的主人公是一位迷人的南方美女，她以非凡的沉着应对南北战争前社会的复杂局面。

3. Historians often described her as the **belle** of the imperial court, admired equally for her intellect and beauty.
   历史学家常称她为宫廷中的第一美人，她的才智与美貌同样受人赞赏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "belle" as related to the French word for "beautiful" — a belle is a beautiful woman, like Belle in the fairy tale.
"Belle"源自法语中"美丽"一词——就像童话中的贝儿公主，belle指的就是美女。
