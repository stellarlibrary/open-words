# 🌊 numerous

🔊 /ˈnjuːmərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Great in number; many; consisting of a large quantity
**中文：** adj. 众多的；许多的；大批的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- many（许多的）
- countless（无数的）
- abundant（丰富的）
- manifold（多种多样的）
- plentiful（大量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **numerous occasions / numerous studies / numerous examples / numerous attempts / too numerous to mention**

👉 Example: The committee received numerous complaints from residents about the proposed zoning changes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Numerous** studies have demonstrated a strong correlation between regular physical exercise and improved cognitive function in older adults.
   众多研究表明，经常进行体育锻炼与老年人认知功能的改善之间存在密切关联。

2. The manuscript contained **numerous** factual errors that the editorial team had to correct before the article could be published.
   手稿包含众多事实错误，编辑团队必须在文章发表前予以更正。

3. Despite **numerous** attempts to negotiate a peaceful resolution, the diplomatic talks ultimately collapsed without an agreement.
   尽管多次尝试通过谈判达成和平解决方案，外交会谈最终在没有达成协议的情况下破裂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "numerous" as "number-ous" — so full of numbers that there are too many to count, like a crowd too large to enumerate.
把"numerous"想成"number-ous"——充满数字的，多到数不过来，就像一个大到无法计数的人群。
