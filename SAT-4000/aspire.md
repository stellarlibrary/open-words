# 🚀 aspire

🔊 /əˈspaɪər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To direct one's hopes or ambitions toward achieving something; to long for or seek ambitiously
**中文：** 渴望；立志；追求

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strive（努力）
- yearn（渴望）
- aim（目标在于）
- desire（渴求）
- pursue（追求）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aspire to / aspire toward / aspire to greatness / aspire to become**

👉 Example: Many young athletes aspire to compete at the highest levels of international competition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. She **aspired** to become a leading researcher in the field of renewable energy and sustainable development practices.
   她立志成为可再生能源和可持续发展实践领域的顶尖研究人员。

2. The ambitious young writer **aspired** to produce works of literature that would influence generations of future readers.
   这位雄心勃勃的年轻作家渴望创作出能够影响未来几代读者的文学作品。

3. Nations that **aspire** to lasting prosperity must invest heavily in education, infrastructure, and scientific innovation alike.
   渴望持久繁荣的国家必须在教育、基础设施和科学创新方面同样大力投资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aspire" contains "spire" — a spire is the pointed top of a building that reaches upward, just like aspirations reach toward lofty goals.
"Aspire"包含"spire"（尖顶）——尖顶是建筑物向上延伸的尖端，就像抱负朝着崇高目标奋进。
