# 📉 deficient

🔊 /dɪˈfɪʃənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking in some necessary quality or element; insufficient in amount, degree, or quality
**中文：** adj. 不足的，缺乏的；有缺陷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inadequate（不充分的）
- insufficient（不足的）
- lacking（缺乏的）
- wanting（缺少的）
- incomplete（不完整的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deficient in something / nutritionally deficient / mentally deficient / deficient diet**

👉 Example: The report concluded that the patient's diet was severely deficient in essential vitamins and minerals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audit revealed that the company's safety protocols were seriously **deficient** and required comprehensive and immediate reform.
   审计显示该公司的安全协议严重不足，需要全面而紧急的改革。

2. Students who are **deficient** in basic mathematical skills often struggle with advanced coursework in the sciences.
   基础数学技能不足的学生在高级理科课程中往往会遇到困难。

3. The soil in this region is **deficient** in nitrogen, making it unsuitable for cultivating most commercial grain crops.
   该地区的土壤缺乏氮元素，不适合种植大多数商业粮食作物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-ficient" as the opposite of "sufficient" — the prefix "de-" suggests removal, so deficient means not having enough.
把"deficient"想成"sufficient（充分的）"的反面——前缀"de-"表示去除，所以deficient就是不充分的、缺乏的。
