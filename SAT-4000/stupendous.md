# 🤯 stupendous

🔊 /stjuːˈpɛndəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Astonishing; extraordinarily great or large
**中文：** adj. 惊人的；巨大的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astounding（令人震惊的）
- prodigious（巨大的）
- colossal（庞大的）
- remarkable（非凡的）
- tremendous（惊人的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stupendous achievement / stupendous effort / stupendous amount / stupendous feat**

👉 Example: The engineering team accomplished a stupendous feat by completing the bridge ahead of schedule.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists made a **stupendous** discovery that fundamentally altered our understanding of ancient Mediterranean civilizations.
   考古学家们做出了一项惊人的发现，从根本上改变了我们对古代地中海文明的理解。

2. The nation invested a **stupendous** amount of resources into developing its renewable energy infrastructure over the decade.
   这个国家在十年间投入了巨大的资源来发展可再生能源基础设施。

3. Her **stupendous** performance at the international competition earned widespread acclaim from critics and audiences alike.
   她在国际比赛中惊人的表现赢得了评论家和观众的广泛赞誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stupendous" as "stupid + enormous" — something so enormously impressive that it leaves you stupefied.
把"stupendous"想成"stupid + enormous"——某事大到令人目瞪口呆，惊得你说不出话。
