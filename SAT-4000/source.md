# 💧 source

🔊 /sɔːrs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A place, person, or thing from which something originates; a spring or fountainhead of water; original documents or materials used for research
**中文：** n. 电源

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- origin（起源）
- root（根源）
- fountainhead（源头）
- wellspring（源泉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **source of information / source material / primary source / open source**

👉 Example: The journalist refused to reveal the source of her confidential information to the authorities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scholars must always verify the credibility of each **source** before citing it in formal academic publications or dissertations.
   学者在正式学术出版物或论文中引用每个来源之前，必须始终核实其可信度。

2. The river's **source** was traced to a remote glacial spring hidden deep within the northern mountain range.
   这条河流的源头被追溯到隐藏在北部山脉深处的一处偏远冰川泉水。

3. Renewable energy has become a vital **source** of electricity for nations striving to reduce their carbon emissions significantly.
   可再生能源已成为那些努力大幅减少碳排放的国家的重要电源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "source" as "s-our-ce" — "our" center, the place where everything begins and flows outward from.
把"source"想成"s-our-ce"——"我们的"中心，一切开始并向外流出的地方。
