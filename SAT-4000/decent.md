# 👔 decent

🔊 /ˈdiːsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Conforming to standards of propriety and morality; respectable; of an acceptable quality or level
**中文：** adj. 正派的；得体的；体面的；相当好的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- respectable（体面的）
- proper（得当的）
- adequate（足够的）
- honorable（正直的）
- appropriate（合适的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **decent person / decent living / decent salary / decent education / decent standard**

👉 Example: Every citizen deserves access to decent housing and a reasonable standard of living.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nonprofit organization works tirelessly to ensure that every family in the community has access to **decent** and affordable housing.
   这家非营利组织不懈努力，确保社区中的每个家庭都能获得体面且负担得起的住房。

2. Despite lacking formal qualifications, she proved herself to be a **decent** and highly competent employee who earned the respect of colleagues.
   尽管缺乏正式资格证书，她证明了自己是一位称职且非常能干的员工，赢得了同事的尊重。

3. The professor argued that a **decent** society must protect the fundamental rights and dignities of all citizens regardless of background.
   教授认为，一个正派的社会必须保护所有公民的基本权利和尊严，无论其背景如何。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "decent" as "de-cent" — worth every cent, meaning something or someone that meets a good, respectable standard.
把"decent"想成"de-cent"——值得每一分钱，意味着某事或某人达到了良好、体面的标准。
