# 🤝 inclusive

🔊 /ɪnˈkluːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Including all the services, items, or people concerned; not excluding any section of society or any party.
**中文：** adj. 包括的，包含的；包容的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comprehensive（综合的）
- all-encompassing（包罗万象的）
- embracing（包容的）
- broad（广泛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inclusive of / inclusive environment / inclusive education / all-inclusive**

👉 Example: The university strives to create an inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds can thrive.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new policy aims to foster a more **inclusive** workplace by accommodating employees with diverse needs and perspectives.
   新政策旨在通过照顾具有不同需求和观点的员工来营造一个更具包容性的工作环境。

2. An **inclusive** curriculum ensures that the contributions of historically marginalized groups are properly represented in academic study.
   包容性的课程确保历史上被边缘化群体的贡献在学术研究中得到适当体现。

3. The conference organizers adopted an **inclusive** approach, providing translation services and accessibility features for all attendees.
   会议组织者采用了包容的方式，为所有与会者提供翻译服务和无障碍设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CLUS-ive" — "clus" from "include" means to shut in. Inclusive means everyone is shut IN, nobody is shut out.
把"inclusive"想成"in-CLUS-ive"——"clus"来自"include"，意为关入。Inclusive意味着每个人都被关在里面，没有人被排斥在外。
