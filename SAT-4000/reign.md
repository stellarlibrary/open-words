# 🏰 reign

🔊 /reɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To hold royal office; to rule as a monarch. To be predominant or prevalent. (n.) The period during which a sovereign rules; dominant influence.
**中文：** v. 统治；支配；盛行 n. 统治；统治时期；支配

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rule（统治）
- govern（治理）
- prevail（盛行）
- dominate（支配）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reign over / reign supreme / during the reign of / reign of terror**

👉 Example: Queen Victoria reigned over the British Empire for more than six decades of remarkable transformation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The emperor **reigned** for nearly four decades, during which the nation experienced unprecedented economic growth and cultural flourishing.
   这位皇帝统治了近四十年，在此期间国家经历了空前的经济增长和文化繁荣。

2. After the championship victory, an atmosphere of celebration and pride **reigned** throughout the entire city for several consecutive weeks.
   夺冠之后，庆祝和自豪的气氛在整座城市中持续弥漫了好几个星期。

3. Historians regard the **reign** of this particular dynasty as a pivotal era that fundamentally shaped the nation's legal institutions.
   历史学家认为这个特定王朝的统治时期是一个从根本上塑造了国家法律制度的关键时代。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reign" as sounding like "rain" — just as rain falls over everything, a ruler's reign covers and controls the entire kingdom.
把"reign"想成发音像"rain（雨）"——正如雨水覆盖一切，统治者的统治覆盖并控制着整个王国。
