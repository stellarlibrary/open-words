# 🎭 sensibility

🔊 /ˌsɛnsɪˈbɪlɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability to appreciate and respond to complex emotional or aesthetic influences; sensitivity or delicacy of feeling
**中文：** n. 敏感性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sensitivity（敏感）
- susceptibility（易感性）
- awareness（意识）
- perceptiveness（洞察力）
- receptiveness（感受力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **artistic sensibility / moral sensibility / offend sensibilities / cultural sensibility**

👉 Example: The novel reflects the refined sensibility of an author deeply attuned to human suffering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet's acute **sensibility** to the natural world allowed her to capture subtle emotional truths in remarkably vivid and precise language.
   这位诗人对自然界的敏锐感受力使她能用极其生动精确的语言捕捉微妙的情感真理。

2. Modern audiences possess a different **sensibility** than those of previous generations, particularly regarding issues of social justice and representation.
   现代观众与前几代人的感受力不同，尤其是在社会公正和代表性问题上。

3. The curator arranged the exhibition with great **sensibility**, ensuring that each artwork was presented in a context that enhanced its emotional impact.
   策展人以高度的鉴赏力布置了这次展览，确保每件艺术品都呈现在能增强其情感冲击力的语境中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sensibility" as the "ability to sense" — not just physical feeling, but the deeper capacity to perceive beauty, emotion, and subtle meaning.
把"sensibility"想成"感知的能力"——不仅是身体上的感觉，更是感受美、情感和微妙含义的深层能力。
