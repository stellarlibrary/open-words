# ♾️ constant

🔊 /ˈkɑːnstənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring continuously; unchanging or unwavering. (n.) A quantity or value that does not change
**中文：** adj. 不变的，恒定的；经常的 n. 常数，常量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- steady（稳定的）
- invariable（不变的）
- perpetual（持续不断的）
- unchanging（不变的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **constant pressure / constant temperature / remain constant / a mathematical constant**

👉 Example: The speed of light in a vacuum is a fundamental constant in physics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory maintained a **constant** temperature throughout the experiment to eliminate environmental variables from the results.
   实验室在整个实验过程中保持恒定温度，以排除环境变量对结果的影响。

2. The **constant** threat of cyberattacks has compelled organizations to invest heavily in advanced digital security infrastructure.
   持续不断的网络攻击威胁迫使各组织大量投资于先进的数字安全基础设施。

3. In mathematics, pi is a **constant** that represents the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter.
   在数学中，圆周率是一个常数，表示圆的周长与其直径的比值。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-STANT" — it "stands" firm and steady, never changing, like a person who constantly stands in the same position.
把"constant"想成"con-STANT（站立）"——它坚定不移地"站"着，从不改变，就像一个人一直站在同一位置。
