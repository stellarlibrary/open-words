# 🌫️ aerosol

🔊 /ˈɛərəˌsɒl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A suspension of fine particles or droplets in the air; a pressurized container that releases a substance as a spray.
**中文：** 气溶胶；气雾剂；喷雾器。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spray（喷雾）
- mist（薄雾）
- vapor（蒸汽）
- atomizer（雾化器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **can, spray, particles, atmospheric, emit**
👉 Example: The scientist measured the concentration of aerosol particles in the atmosphere.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Volcanic eruptions release massive quantities of **aerosol** particles that can significantly affect global climate patterns.
   火山喷发释放出大量气溶胶颗粒，能够显著影响全球气候模式。

2. Regulations have restricted the use of certain **aerosol** propellants that deplete the protective ozone layer.
   法规已限制使用某些会破坏保护性臭氧层的气雾剂推进剂。

3. The research team studied how **aerosol** concentrations in urban environments contribute to respiratory health problems.
   研究团队研究了城市环境中气溶胶浓度如何导致呼吸系统健康问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aero" (air) + "sol" (solution) — an aerosol is a solution suspended in the air.
"aero"（空气）+"sol"（溶液）——aerosol是悬浮在空气中的溶液。
