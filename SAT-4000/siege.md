# ⚔️ siege

🔊 /siːdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A military operation surrounding a place to force surrender; a prolonged effort or attack. (v.) To surround and blockade
**中文：** n. 包围，围困

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blockade（封锁）
- encirclement（包围）
- bombardment（轰击）
- onslaught（猛攻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lay siege to / under siege / lift the siege / state of siege / siege warfare**

👉 Example: The ancient fortress remained under siege for nearly three months before the defenders finally capitulated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged **siege** of the fortified city left its inhabitants desperately short of food and medical supplies.
   对这座设防城市的长期围困使其居民严重缺乏食物和医疗物资。

2. Activists laid **siege** to the corporate headquarters, demanding immediate action on environmental pollution and worker safety.
   活动人士包围了公司总部，要求立即就环境污染和工人安全问题采取行动。

3. Historians regard the **siege** of that strategic port as a pivotal turning point in the course of the war.
   历史学家认为对该战略港口的围困是战争进程中的关键转折点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "siege" as "sit + edge" — an army sits at the edge of a city, surrounding it and waiting for surrender.
把"siege"想成"sit（坐）+ edge（边缘）"——一支军队坐在城市边缘，包围并等待投降。
