# 📢 obstreperous

🔊 /əbˈstrɛpərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Noisy and difficult to control; resisting restraint in a loud or unruly manner
**中文：** adj. 吵闹的，喧嚣的；难驾驭的；任性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boisterous（喧闹的）
- unruly（难以控制的）
- clamorous（吵闹的）
- rowdy（粗暴的）
- vociferous（大声叫嚷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obstreperous crowd / obstreperous behavior / obstreperous child / become obstreperous**

👉 Example: The obstreperous protesters disrupted the hearing with their continuous shouting and chanting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor struggled to deliver her lecture as the **obstreperous** students in the back row continued their disruptive conversation.
   教授艰难地进行着讲座，因为后排喧闹的学生们一直在扰乱秩序地交谈。

2. Security personnel were called to escort the **obstreperous** spectator out of the courtroom after repeated warnings went unheeded.
   在多次警告无效后，安保人员被叫来将那名吵闹的旁听者带出法庭。

3. The once orderly town hall meeting became **obstreperous** when the mayor announced plans to raise property taxes significantly.
   当市长宣布大幅提高房产税的计划时，原本有序的市政厅会议变得喧嚣不堪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-STREP-erous" as containing "STREP" like a strep throat that makes you want to scream — obstreperous people are loud and uncontrollable.
把"obstreperous"想成包含"strep"像链球菌性喉炎（strep throat）让你想尖叫——obstreperous的人又吵又无法控制。
