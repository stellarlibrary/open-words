# 🕊️ benignant

🔊 /bɪˈnɪɡnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Kind and gracious in manner; having a beneficial effect; showing gentle goodwill
**中文：** adj. 仁慈的；和蔼的；良性的；有益的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- benevolent（仁慈的）
- gracious（和蔼的）
- kindly（善良的）
- benign（良性的）
- merciful（慈悲的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **benignant influence / benignant ruler / benignant expression / benignant smile**

👉 Example: The elderly matriarch cast a benignant gaze upon her grandchildren gathered around the table.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **benignant** influence of the elder statesman helped guide the young nation through its most turbulent formative years.
   这位年长政治家的仁慈影响帮助这个年轻国家度过了最动荡的早期岁月。

2. She possessed a **benignant** disposition that made everyone around her feel valued and genuinely welcomed.
   她拥有一种和蔼的性情，使周围的每个人都感到被重视和真心欢迎。

3. The **benignant** climate of the coastal region proved highly beneficial for patients recovering from respiratory ailments.
   沿海地区宜人的气候对正在康复的呼吸系统疾病患者非常有益。

把"benignant"想成"benign（良性的）+ ant（蚂蚁）"——像一只温和有益的巨蚁，不具威胁性。
