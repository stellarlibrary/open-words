# 🧬 species

🔊 /ˈspiːʃiːz/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A group of living organisms consisting of similar individuals capable of interbreeding; a kind or sort of something. (adj.) Of or relating to a biological species
**中文：** n. 种，种类

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- type（类型）
- kind（种类）
- breed（品种）
- genus（属）
- variety（品种）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endangered species / invasive species / species diversity / species of plant/animal**

👉 Example: Conservation biologists have identified several endangered species that require immediate habitat protection measures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly discovered **species** of deep-sea fish exhibits bioluminescent traits that have never been documented in marine biology literature.
   这种新发现的深海鱼种类展现出海洋生物学文献中从未记载过的生物发光特征。

2. Protecting endangered **species** requires coordinated international efforts involving habitat conservation, legal enforcement, and public education campaigns.
   保护濒危物种需要涉及栖息地保护、法律执行和公众教育活动的协调国际努力。

3. The botanist catalogued over two hundred **species** of flowering plants during her extensive three-year field expedition across the tropics.
   这位植物学家在长达三年的热带实地考察中编录了两百多种开花植物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "species" as "special sees" — each species is a special group that scientists "see" and classify as distinct from all others.
把"species"想成"special sees"——每个物种都是科学家们"看到"并归类为与其他所有物种不同的特殊群体。
