# 📢 summon

🔊 /ˈsʌmən/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To call upon to come; to convene; to muster or gather up (courage, strength)
**中文：** v. 召唤；召集；传讯，传唤；鼓起，振作

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- convene（召集）
- convoke（召集）
- muster（鼓起）
- beckon（召唤）
- invoke（祈求）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **summon courage / summon a meeting / summon someone to court / summon the strength**

👉 Example: The king summoned his advisors to discuss the imminent threat along the northern border.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court issued an order to **summon** the reluctant witness who had been evading the judicial proceedings.
   法院发出命令传唤那位一直逃避司法程序的不情愿的证人。

2. She had to **summon** all her courage before stepping onto the stage to deliver the commencement address.
   她不得不鼓起全部勇气，然后才走上舞台发表毕业典礼演讲。

3. The chairman decided to **summon** an emergency meeting after receiving alarming reports about the financial irregularities.
   主席在收到有关财务违规的警报后决定召集紧急会议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "summon" as "some-man" — you call some man to come to you, summoning him to appear.
把"summon"想成"some-man"——你叫某个人过来，召唤他出现。
