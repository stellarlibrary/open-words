# 💡 inspire

🔊 /ɪnˈspaɪər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fill someone with the urge or ability to do or feel something; to motivate, encourage, or stimulate creativity
**中文：** v. 激发；鼓舞；启示；产生；使生灵感；吸入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- motivate（激励）
- encourage（鼓励）
- stimulate（激发）
- uplift（振奋）
- galvanize（激起）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inspire confidence / inspire someone to do something / inspire creativity / inspire a generation**

👉 Example: The professor's passionate lectures never failed to inspire her students to pursue independent research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary about climate change **inspired** thousands of young people to participate in environmental conservation efforts.
   这部关于气候变化的纪录片激励了数千名年轻人参与环境保护工作。

2. Great leaders possess the remarkable ability to **inspire** confidence and unity even during periods of profound uncertainty.
   伟大的领导者拥有非凡的能力，即使在深刻的不确定时期也能激发信心和团结。

3. The architect's innovative designs were **inspired** by the organic forms and patterns found throughout the natural world.
   这位建筑师的创新设计灵感来源于自然界中随处可见的有机形态和图案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-spire" as "in-spirit" — to put spirit into someone, filling them with energy and motivation.
把"inspire"想成"in-spirit"——把精神注入某人体内，让他们充满活力和动力。
