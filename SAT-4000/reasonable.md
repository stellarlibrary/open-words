# 🧠 reasonable

🔊 /ˈriːzənəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Fair and sensible; based on good judgment; moderate and not excessive
**中文：** adj. 合理的，公道的；通情达理的；适度的，有道理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sensible（明智的）
- rational（合理的）
- fair（公正的）
- moderate（适度的）
- logical（合乎逻辑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reasonable price / reasonable explanation / reasonable doubt / reasonable expectation**

👉 Example: The judge instructed the jury to determine whether there was reasonable doubt regarding the defendant's guilt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee proposed a **reasonable** compromise that addressed the concerns of both parties involved in the dispute.
   委员会提出了一个合理的折中方案，解决了争议双方的关切。

2. It is **reasonable** to assume that increased funding for education will yield measurable improvements in student performance.
   有理由认为，增加教育经费将在学生成绩方面带来可衡量的提高。

3. The landlord offered a **reasonable** rent reduction to retain long-term tenants during the economic downturn.
   房东在经济低迷期间提出了合理的租金减免，以留住长期租户。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reason-able" as "able to use reason" — a reasonable person is someone capable of thinking logically and fairly.
把"reasonable"想成"able to use reason"——一个reasonable的人就是能够运用理性、公正思考的人。
