# ➕ sum

🔊 /sʌm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An amount of money; the total obtained by addition. (v.) To summarize or give a brief statement of the main points
**中文：** n. 和

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- total（总数）
- aggregate（合计）
- amount（金额）
- summation（总和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sum of money / sum total / sum up / in sum / a considerable sum**

👉 Example: The attorney asked the witness to sum up the key events that occurred on the evening in question.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation invested a substantial **sum** of capital into renewable energy research to diversify its long-term portfolio strategy.
   该公司将大量资金投入可再生能源研究，以实现其长期投资组合战略的多元化。

2. In **sum**, the committee concluded that the proposed legislation would produce more societal benefits than previously anticipated by analysts.
   总而言之，委员会得出结论，拟议的立法将产生比分析师先前预期的更多的社会效益。

3. The professor asked each student to **sum** up the principal arguments of the assigned readings in a concise written paragraph.
   教授要求每位学生用简洁的书面段落概括指定阅读材料的主要论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sum" as the result you get when you add numbers together — 2 + 3 = 5, where 5 is the sum.
把"sum"想成你把数字加在一起得到的结果——2 + 3 = 5，其中5就是和。
