# 📢 promulgate

🔊 /ˈprɒməlɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make widely known; to officially announce or put into effect a law, decree, or doctrine
**中文：** v. 公布；传播；发表；发布

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proclaim（宣布）
- announce（公告）
- disseminate（传播）
- decree（颁布）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **promulgate a law / promulgate a policy / promulgate a decree / promulgate regulations**

👉 Example: The president promulgated the new environmental protection regulations at a formal ceremony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government will **promulgate** new regulations designed to reduce carbon emissions from industrial facilities nationwide.
   政府将颁布新法规，旨在减少全国工业设施的碳排放。

2. Religious leaders traveled extensively to **promulgate** their doctrines among communities in remote and underserved regions.
   宗教领袖广泛游历，在偏远和服务不足的社区传播他们的教义。

3. The committee voted unanimously to **promulgate** the revised academic standards for all public universities in the state.
   委员会一致投票通过在该州所有公立大学发布修订后的学术标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-mul-gate" as "promote through the gate" — you push information through the gate so everyone can see it.
把"promulgate"想成"promote through the gate"——你把信息推过大门，让所有人都能看到。
