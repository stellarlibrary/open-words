# 🌌 nebula

🔊 /ˈnebjʊlə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A vast cloud of gas and dust in outer space, often visible as a luminous patch; in medicine, a cloudy spot on the cornea
**中文：** n. 星云；角膜云翳

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gas cloud（气体云）
- cosmic cloud（宇宙云）
- interstellar cloud（星际云）
- star nursery（恒星摇篮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **planetary nebula / emission nebula / nebula formation / Orion Nebula**

👉 Example: The Hubble Space Telescope captured a breathtaking image of the Eagle Nebula.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Orion **Nebula** is one of the brightest and most studied stellar nurseries visible to the naked eye from Earth.
   猎户座星云是从地球上肉眼可见的最明亮、研究最多的恒星摇篮之一。

2. Astronomers discovered that the collapsing **nebula** had begun forming new protostars within its dense molecular core regions.
   天文学家发现，正在坍缩的星云已在其致密的分子核心区域开始形成新的原恒星。

3. The planetary **nebula** surrounding the dying star displayed spectacular rings of ionized gas expanding outward at tremendous velocity.
   围绕垂死恒星的行星状星云展现出壮观的电离气体环，以巨大的速度向外膨胀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nebula" from Latin "nebula" meaning mist or cloud — a nebula is essentially a cosmic cloud floating among the stars.
"nebula"来自拉丁语"nebula"（雾或云）——星云本质上就是漂浮在群星之间的宇宙云团。
