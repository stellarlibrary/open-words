# ✅ aye

🔊 /aɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adverb | 名词，副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A vote in favor of a proposal or motion; an affirmative response meaning "yes," especially used in parliamentary or naval contexts.

**中文：** 赞成票；赞成。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- yes（是的）
- affirmative（肯定的）
- yea（赞成）
- agreement（同意）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Aye" is primarily used in formal voting contexts such as legislative bodies ("The ayes have it") and in naval traditions ("Aye, aye, captain"). It is not commonly used in everyday casual conversation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chairman announced that the **ayes** had prevailed, and the new amendment was officially passed into law.
   （主席宣布**赞成票**占多数，新修正案正式通过成为法律。）

2. When the captain gave the order, every sailor responded with a firm "**aye**" and immediately began preparations.
   （当船长下达命令时，每个水手都坚定地回答"**是**"，并立即开始准备。）

3. The council voted by a margin of twelve **ayes** to three nays, clearly favoring the proposed resolution.
   （委员会以十二票**赞成**、三票反对的结果投票，明显支持拟议的决议。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Aye" sounds exactly like "eye" — picture an eye winking at you as a sign of approval, meaning "yes, I agree!"

"Aye"发音和"eye（眼睛）"完全一样——想象一只眼睛向你眨眼表示认可，意思是"是的，我同意！"
