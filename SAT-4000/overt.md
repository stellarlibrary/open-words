# 🎯 overt

🔊 /oʊˈvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Done or shown openly; not secret or hidden; deliberate and unconcealed
**中文：** adj. 明显的；公然的；蓄意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obvious（明显的）
- blatant（公然的）
- explicit（明确的）
- undisguised（不加掩饰的）
- manifest（显然的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overt hostility / overt discrimination / overt act / overt aggression**

👉 Example: The manager's overt favoritism toward certain employees created widespread resentment in the office.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician's **overt** attempts to manipulate public opinion were quickly exposed by investigative journalists and media analysts.
   这位政客公然操纵舆论的企图很快被调查记者和媒体分析人士揭露。

2. Unlike subtle forms of prejudice, **overt** discrimination in hiring practices can be documented and legally challenged in court.
   与隐性偏见不同，招聘中的公然歧视可以被记录并在法庭上提出法律质疑。

3. The nation's **overt** military buildup along the border signaled its aggressive intentions to neighboring countries and international observers.
   该国在边境的公然军事集结向邻国和国际观察人士发出了其侵略意图的信号。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "overt" as "over" + "t" — something done "over the top" for everyone to see, nothing hidden underneath.
把"overt"想成"over（在上面）"+"t"——做事做在明面上，所有人都看得见，没有任何隐藏。
