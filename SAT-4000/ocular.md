# 👁️ ocular

🔊 /ˈɑːkjələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the eye or vision; perceived by sight. (n.) The eyepiece of an optical instrument
**中文：** adj. 眼睛的；视觉的；目击的 n. 目镜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- visual（视觉的）
- optic（眼睛的）
- optical（光学的）
- eye-related（与眼睛有关的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ocular examination / ocular disease / ocular evidence / ocular lens**

👉 Example: The ophthalmologist performed a thorough ocular examination to assess the patient's deteriorating vision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician recommended an immediate **ocular** examination after the patient reported persistent blurred vision and discomfort.
   患者报告持续视力模糊和不适后，医生建议立即进行眼科检查。

2. Advanced **ocular** implants have enabled many patients with severe corneal damage to regain functional sight successfully.
   先进的眼部植入物已使许多严重角膜损伤的患者成功恢复了功能性视力。

3. The detective relied on **ocular** evidence from three witnesses to reconstruct the sequence of events at the scene.
   侦探依靠三名目击者的目击证据来重建现场的事件经过。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ocular" as related to "oculus" — the Latin word for eye. An "ocular" exam is literally an eye exam.
把"ocular"与"oculus"联系起来——这是拉丁语中"眼睛"的意思。"ocular"检查就是眼科检查。
