# 🩹 scar

🔊 /skɑːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A mark left on the skin or other tissue after a wound has healed; lasting emotional damage. (v.) To mark with a wound; to leave a lasting adverse effect
**中文：** n. 疤，瘢痕；创伤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blemish（瑕疵）
- wound（伤口）
- mark（痕迹）
- trauma（创伤）
- disfigurement（毁容）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emotional scar / bear a scar / scar tissue / scarred for life / leave a scar**

👉 Example: The traumatic experience left a deep emotional scar that took years of therapy to heal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged civil conflict left deep **scars** on the nation's collective memory and profoundly altered its political landscape.
   旷日持久的内战给国家的集体记忆留下了深深的创伤，并深刻改变了其政治格局。

2. Environmentalists warned that unregulated mining operations would permanently **scar** the pristine landscape of the protected mountain region.
   环保人士警告说，无监管的采矿作业将永久性地破坏受保护山区的原始景观。

3. The psychological **scars** of childhood neglect can manifest in adulthood as difficulty forming trusting interpersonal relationships.
   童年被忽视的心理创伤可能在成年后表现为难以建立信任的人际关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scar" as a "star" missing its 't' — just as a star leaves a lasting impression in the sky, a scar leaves a lasting mark on the body or mind.
把"scar"想成少了"t"的"star"——就像星星在天空中留下持久的印象一样，伤疤在身体或心灵上留下持久的痕迹。
