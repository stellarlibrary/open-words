# ✨ fascinate

🔊 /ˈfæsɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To attract and hold the attention of someone intensely; to captivate or enchant
**中文：** v. 使着迷；使神魂颠倒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- captivate（迷住）
- enchant（使陶醉）
- enthrall（使着迷）
- mesmerize（使入迷）
- bewitch（使神魂颠倒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fascinate someone / be fascinated by / fascinate with details / never cease to fascinate**

👉 Example: The intricate patterns of the ancient mosaic never cease to fascinate visiting scholars.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's groundbreaking research on deep-sea organisms continued to **fascinate** the entire scientific community worldwide.
   这位教授对深海生物的开创性研究持续令全球科学界着迷。

2. Ancient civilizations **fascinate** historians because their architectural achievements still defy modern engineering explanations.
   古代文明令历史学家着迷，因为它们的建筑成就至今仍无法用现代工程学解释。

3. The documentary about quantum mechanics managed to **fascinate** even viewers with no prior background in physics.
   这部关于量子力学的纪录片甚至令没有物理学背景的观众也为之着迷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fascinate" as related to "fashion" — something so fashionable and attractive that it holds your gaze and captivates your mind.
把"fascinate"联想到"fashion（时尚）"——某样东西如此时尚迷人，以至于吸引了你的目光，令你心驰神往。
