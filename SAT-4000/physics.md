# ⚛️ physics

🔊 /ˈfɪzɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The branch of science concerned with the nature and properties of matter, energy, motion, and force; the physical properties and phenomena of a particular system
**中文：** n. 物理学；物理现象

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- natural science（自然科学）
- physical science（物理科学）
- mechanics（力学）
- dynamics（动力学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quantum physics / laws of physics / physics experiment / particle physics / applied physics**

👉 Example: The laws of physics govern the behavior of every object in the observable universe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her groundbreaking contributions to theoretical **physics** earned her the Nobel Prize and international recognition among scholars.
   她在理论物理学方面的开创性贡献为她赢得了诺贝尔奖和学者们的国际认可。

2. The professor explained that modern **physics** fundamentally challenged the assumptions that had dominated scientific thought for centuries.
   教授解释说，现代物理学从根本上挑战了统治科学思想数百年的假设。

3. Students enrolled in advanced **physics** courses are required to demonstrate proficiency in both calculus and linear algebra.
   选修高级物理学课程的学生必须展示出微积分和线性代数方面的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "physics" from Greek "physis" meaning "nature" — physics is the study of how nature works at its most fundamental level.
"Physics"源自希腊语"physis"，意为"自然"——物理学就是研究自然在最基本层面如何运作的学科。
