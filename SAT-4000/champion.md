# 🏆 champion

🔊 /ˈtʃæmpiən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who has won first place in a competition; a person who fights for or advocates a cause. (v.) To support, defend, or fight for a person or cause
**中文：** n. 冠军；拥护者；战士 v. 支持；拥护

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advocate（拥护者）
- defender（捍卫者）
- winner（胜利者）
- proponent（支持者）
- upholder（维护者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **champion a cause / champion the rights of / champion reform / world champion**

👉 Example: She has long championed the rights of marginalized communities through her legal advocacy work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator has consistently **championed** environmental legislation aimed at reducing industrial pollution and protecting endangered ecosystems nationwide.
   这位参议员一直拥护旨在减少工业污染和保护全国濒危生态系统的环境立法。

2. As the reigning world **champion**, she bore the immense pressure of defending her title before an audience of millions.
   作为卫冕世界冠军，她在数百万观众面前承受着保卫头衔的巨大压力。

3. Throughout his distinguished career, the civil rights lawyer **championed** the cause of equal justice for underrepresented minority populations.
   在其杰出的职业生涯中，这位民权律师始终为少数族裔争取平等正义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "champion" as "champ + ion" — a "champ" is a winner, and with the "-ion" suffix, a champion is someone who wins or fights for what they believe in.
把"champion"想成"champ（冠军）+ ion"——"champ"是赢家，加上"-ion"后缀，champion就是赢得胜利或为信念而战的人。
