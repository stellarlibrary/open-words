# 💡 original

🔊 /əˈrɪdʒɪnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Present or existing from the beginning; first or earliest; creative, inventive, and not copied. (n.) The earliest form of something from which copies are made; an eccentric person
**中文：** adj. 最初的，原始的，原文的；新颖的，有独创性的 n. 原物，原作，原文

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- authentic（真实的）
- initial（最初的）
- innovative（创新的）
- novel（新颖的）
- prototype（原型）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **original idea / original work / original version / original copy / original sin**

👉 Example: The curator confirmed that the painting was an original, not a reproduction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect presented an **original** design that incorporated sustainable materials and innovative structural engineering techniques throughout the building.
   建筑师展示了一个独创性设计，在整座建筑中融入了可持续材料和创新的结构工程技术。

2. Scholars traveled to the archive to examine the **original** manuscript, which had been preserved under carefully controlled conditions for centuries.
   学者们前往档案馆检查原始手稿，该手稿在精心控制的条件下保存了数百年。

3. The professor encouraged students to develop **original** arguments rather than merely summarizing the existing body of academic literature.
   教授鼓励学生发展原创论点，而不是仅仅总结现有的学术文献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "original" as "origin-al" — it relates to the "origin," the very first and authentic version of something before any copies were made.
把"original"想成"origin-al"——它与"origin"（起源）有关，是某物在任何复制之前的最初和真实版本。
