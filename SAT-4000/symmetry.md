# 🔲 symmetry

🔊 /ˈsɪmɪtri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being made up of exactly similar parts facing each other; balanced proportions
**中文：** n. 对称；匀称，整齐

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- balance（平衡）
- proportion（匀称）
- harmony（和谐）
- regularity（规则性）
- evenness（均匀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perfect symmetry / bilateral symmetry / line of symmetry / axis of symmetry**

👉 Example: The butterfly's wings display remarkable bilateral symmetry in both color and pattern.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor explained that bilateral **symmetry** is a fundamental characteristic observed across most animal species in nature.
   教授解释说，双侧对称是自然界中大多数动物物种的基本特征。

2. Renaissance painters carefully employed **symmetry** in their compositions to achieve visual harmony and aesthetic perfection.
   文艺复兴时期的画家在构图中精心运用对称来实现视觉和谐与美学完美。

3. The lack of **symmetry** in the experimental results suggested that an unknown variable was influencing the outcome.
   实验结果缺乏对称性，表明有一个未知变量在影响结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sym-metry" as "same-measure" — when both sides share the same measure, you have symmetry.
把"symmetry"想成"same-measure"——当两边具有相同的度量时，就是对称。
