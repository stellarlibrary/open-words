# 🔙 posterior

🔊 /pɑːˈstɪriər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Coming after in time or order; situated at the back or rear. (n.) The rear part of something; the buttocks
**中文：** adj. 其次的；较后的；后部的 n. 后部；臀部

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rear（后面的）
- subsequent（其后的）
- hind（后面的）
- later（较晚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **posterior to / posterior region / posterior view / posterior probability (in statistics)**

👉 Example: The surgeon carefully examined the posterior aspect of the knee joint before recommending a treatment plan.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anatomical textbook clearly illustrated that the **posterior** muscles of the thigh are essential for locomotion and postural stability.
   解剖学教科书清楚地说明了大腿后部肌肉对于运动和姿势稳定性至关重要。

2. In Bayesian statistics, the **posterior** distribution is updated from the prior distribution after incorporating newly observed data.
   在贝叶斯统计学中，后验分布是在纳入新观测数据后从先验分布更新而来的。

3. The archaeological layer containing iron tools is **posterior** to the bronze artifacts, confirming the expected chronological sequence.
   含有铁质工具的考古层晚于青铜器文物层，证实了预期的年代顺序。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "posterior" as "post + erior" — "post" means after, so posterior refers to what comes after or is at the back.
把"posterior"想成"post + erior"——"post"意为之后，所以posterior指的是在后面或之后的事物。
