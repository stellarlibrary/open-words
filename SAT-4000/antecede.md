# 🕰️ antecede

🔊 /ˌæn.tɪˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To go before in time, order, or rank; to precede or surpass something or someone.

**中文：** 在时间、顺序或等级上先于；胜过；居前。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- precede（先于）
- predate（早于）
- forerun（先行）
- antecede（领先）
- surpass（胜过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antecede in time, antecede a historical event, antecede by several centuries

**Example:** The invention of the printing press **anteceded** the widespread dissemination of scientific knowledge in Europe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosophical traditions of ancient Greece **anteceded** the development of modern Western thought by millennia.
   古希腊的哲学传统比现代西方思想的发展早了数千年。

2. Her remarkable achievements in the field of medicine **anteceded** those of many of her contemporaries.
   她在医学领域的杰出成就胜过了许多同时代人的成就。

3. The treaty negotiations **anteceded** the formal declaration of peace between the two neighboring nations.
   条约谈判先于两个邻国之间正式的和平宣言。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Ante-" means "before" and "-cede" means "to go" (as in "recede"). So antecede literally means "to go before." 前缀 ante-（在…之前）加上词根 -cede（走），字面意思就是"走在前面"，即"先于、居前"。
