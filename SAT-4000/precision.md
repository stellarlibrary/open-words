# 🔬 precision

🔊 /prɪˈsɪʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being exact and accurate; the degree of refinement in a measurement. (adj.) Designed for highly accurate performance.
**中文：** n. 精确；准确；精密度；精确度 adj. 精密的；精确的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accuracy（准确性）
- exactness（精确性）
- meticulousness（一丝不苟）
- rigor（严谨）
- correctness（正确性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with precision / precision instrument / precision engineering / surgical precision / precision medicine**

👉 Example: The watchmaker assembled the delicate components with remarkable precision, ensuring flawless timekeeping.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern telescopes operate with extraordinary **precision**, allowing astronomers to detect planets orbiting distant stars with great clarity.
   现代望远镜以非凡的精度运行，使天文学家能够清晰地探测到围绕遥远恒星运行的行星。

2. The military operation was executed with surgical **precision**, minimizing collateral damage while achieving all strategic objectives.
   这次军事行动以外科手术般的精确度执行，在实现所有战略目标的同时将附带损害降到最低。

3. The laboratory invested heavily in **precision** instruments to ensure that all experimental measurements met international standards.
   实验室大力投资精密仪器，以确保所有实验测量达到国际标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "precision" as "pre-cision" — making a "cision" (incision, cut) beforehand with exact planning, leaving no margin for error.
把"precision"想成"pre（预先）+ cision（切割）"——事先精确规划的切割，不留任何误差余地。
