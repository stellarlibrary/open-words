# 🌿 vegetative

🔊 /ˈvedʒɪˌteɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of or relating to plant life or growth; characterized by a lack of mental or physical activity; promoting or capable of growth
**中文：** adj. 植物的；植物人状态的，无所作为的；促使植物生长的；有生长力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dormant（休眠的）
- inert（惰性的）
- botanical（植物学的）
- passive（被动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vegetative state / vegetative growth / vegetative reproduction / vegetative stage**

👉 Example: After the severe brain injury, the patient remained in a vegetative state for several months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The neurologist confirmed that the patient had entered a **vegetative** state with no discernible signs of conscious awareness.
   神经科医生确认该患者已进入植物人状态，没有明显的意识迹象。

2. During the **vegetative** phase of development, the plant allocates most of its energy toward producing leaves and stems.
   在营养生长阶段，植物将大部分能量用于产生叶子和茎。

3. Critics described the protagonist's **vegetative** existence as a powerful metaphor for spiritual emptiness in modern society.
   评论家将主人公无所作为的生存状态描述为现代社会精神空虚的有力隐喻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vegetative" as related to "vegetable" — a person in a vegetative state is as still and unresponsive as a vegetable sitting on a shelf.
把"vegetative"和"vegetable（蔬菜）"联系起来——处于vegetative状态的人就像摆在架子上的蔬菜一样静止不动、毫无反应。
