# 💰 billion

🔊 /ˈbɪljən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, numeral, adjective | 名词, 数词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The number equal to one thousand million (1,000,000,000); an extremely large quantity or amount
**中文：** 十亿；大量（英式旧用法：万亿）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thousand million（十亿）
- milliard（十亿，英式旧称）
- multitude（大量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **billions of dollars / a billion people / billion-dollar industry / multibillion / net worth of billions**

👉 Example: The technology sector has generated billions of dollars in revenue over the past decade alone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government allocated over two **billion** dollars to infrastructure development aimed at modernizing the nation's transportation network.
   政府拨款超过二十亿美元用于基础设施建设，旨在实现国家交通网络的现代化。

2. Scientists estimate that the universe contains **billions** of galaxies, each harboring countless stars and planetary systems.
   科学家估计宇宙包含数十亿个星系，每个星系都拥有无数的恒星和行星系统。

3. The pharmaceutical company invested **billions** in research and development before the groundbreaking vaccine received regulatory approval.
   这家制药公司在突破性疫苗获得监管批准之前，在研发方面投入了数十亿美元。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "illion" — in the original French system, a billion was a million raised to the power of two (a million million). Now it simply means a thousand million.
把"billion"想成"bi-"（二）+"illion"——在原始法语体系中，billion是百万的二次方（一百万个百万）。如今它简单地表示十亿。
