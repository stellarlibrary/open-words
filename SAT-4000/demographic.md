# 📊 demographic

🔊 /ˌdɛməˈɡræfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the statistical study of human populations, including size, growth, density, and distribution
**中文：** adj. 人口统计学的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- population-based（基于人口的）
- statistical（统计的）
- census-related（人口普查相关的）
- populational（人口的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **demographic data / demographic shift / demographic trend / target demographic / demographic profile**

👉 Example: The company analyzed demographic data to identify the most promising markets for their new product line.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Significant **demographic** shifts in the region have compelled policymakers to reassess healthcare funding and infrastructure development priorities.
   该地区显著的人口结构变化迫使决策者重新评估医疗资金和基础设施建设的优先事项。

2. The marketing team conducted extensive **demographic** research to better understand the purchasing habits of their target consumer audience.
   营销团队进行了大量的人口统计研究，以更好地了解目标消费者群体的购买习惯。

3. Urban planners must consider **demographic** projections when designing transportation networks that will serve communities for decades to come.
   城市规划者在设计将服务社区数十年的交通网络时，必须考虑人口统计预测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "demo-graphic" as a "graph of the demos (people)" — demographic is about charting and studying populations.
把"demographic"想成"demo（人民）的graphic（图表）"——demographic就是用图表研究人口。
