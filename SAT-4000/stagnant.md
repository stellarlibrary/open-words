# 🚫 stagnant

🔊 /ˈstæɡnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not flowing or moving; showing no activity or development; sluggish or foul from lack of movement
**中文：** adj. 停滞的；迟钝的；不景气的；污浊的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- motionless（不动的）
- stale（陈腐的）
- dormant（休眠的）
- sluggish（迟缓的）
- static（静止的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stagnant water / stagnant economy / stagnant wages / stagnant growth / stagnant air**

👉 Example: The stagnant pond became a breeding ground for mosquitoes during the humid summer months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The country's **stagnant** economy prompted widespread calls for comprehensive reform of trade and fiscal policies.
   该国停滞的经济引发了对全面改革贸易和财政政策的广泛呼声。

2. Researchers discovered that **stagnant** water in abandoned containers served as the primary breeding site for disease-carrying insects.
   研究人员发现废弃容器中的死水是携带疾病昆虫的主要繁殖场所。

3. After years of **stagnant** progress, the committee decided to adopt an entirely new approach to the project.
   经过多年停滞不前的进展，委员会决定对该项目采用全新的方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stagnant" as "stag-nant" — a stag (deer) that refuses to move, standing perfectly still in a swamp of unmoving water.
把"stagnant"想成"stag-nant"——一只拒绝移动的雄鹿，静静地站在一潭死水的沼泽中。
