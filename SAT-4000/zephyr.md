# 🌬️ zephyr

🔊 /ˈzɛfər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A soft, gentle breeze; a light wind from the west; a fine, lightweight fabric
**中文：** n. 和风；西风；轻薄织物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- breeze（微风）
- gust（轻风）
- draft（气流）
- waft（飘送的气流）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a gentle zephyr / a warm zephyr / zephyr-like breeze / carried by a zephyr**

👉 Example: A soft zephyr rustled through the curtains on the warm summer evening.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The delicate **zephyr** swept across the meadow, carrying the fragrance of wildflowers to the nearby village.
   轻柔的和风吹过草地，将野花的芬芳带到了附近的村庄。

2. Poets of the Romantic era frequently invoked the **zephyr** as a symbol of gentle inspiration and renewal.
   浪漫主义时代的诗人常常以和风作为温柔灵感与新生的象征。

3. The sailors welcomed the mild **zephyr** after enduring weeks of violent storms on the open Atlantic Ocean.
   水手们在大西洋上经历了数周猛烈风暴后，欣然迎接这阵温和的微风。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Zephyrus," the Greek god of the west wind — a zephyr is the gentle breeze he brings.
想想希腊神话中的西风之神"Zephyrus"——zephyr就是他带来的那阵柔和微风。
