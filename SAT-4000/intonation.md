# 🎵 intonation

🔊 /ˌɪntəˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The rise and fall of the voice in speaking; the pattern of pitch changes in connected speech; the accuracy of musical pitch
**中文：** n. 声调；语调；语音的抑扬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inflection（语调变化）
- cadence（节奏）
- pitch（音高）
- modulation（调节）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rising intonation / falling intonation / intonation pattern / correct intonation**

👉 Example: In English, a rising intonation at the end of a sentence typically indicates a question.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The linguistics professor explained how **intonation** patterns vary significantly across different dialects and regional varieties of English.
   语言学教授解释了语调模式在不同方言和英语地区变体之间如何存在显著差异。

2. Mastering the correct **intonation** is essential for foreign language learners who wish to sound natural and be clearly understood.
   掌握正确的语调对于希望听起来自然且被清楚理解的外语学习者来说至关重要。

3. The choir director spent considerable time refining the singers' **intonation** to ensure every note was perfectly pitched for the concert.
   合唱指挥花了大量时间改进歌手们的音准，以确保音乐会上每个音符都完美无误。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-TONE-ation" — it contains the word "tone." Intonation is all about the tones and pitch patterns in your voice when you speak.
把"intonation"想成含有"tone"（音调）——语调就是你说话时声音中的音调和音高变化。
