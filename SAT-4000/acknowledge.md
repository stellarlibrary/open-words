# 🤝 acknowledge

🔊 /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To accept or admit the existence or truth of; to express gratitude for; to confirm receipt of.
**中文：** 承认；答谢；报偿；告知已收到

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recognize（承认）
- admit（承认）
- concede（让步承认）
- appreciate（感谢）
- confirm（确认）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acknowledge the fact / contribution / receipt / mistake / importance**

👉 Example: The committee acknowledged the significant contributions of the research team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The institution formally **acknowledged** the scientist's groundbreaking discoveries at the annual awards ceremony.
   该机构在年度颁奖典礼上正式表彰了这位科学家的开创性发现。

2. He reluctantly **acknowledged** that his initial hypothesis had been flawed and required substantial revision.
   他不情愿地承认自己最初的假设存在缺陷，需要大幅修改。

3. Please **acknowledge** receipt of this document by signing and returning the enclosed confirmation form.
   请签署并寄回随附的确认表，以告知已收到本文件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ac-KNOWLEDGE" — when you acknowledge something, you show that you have knowledge of it and accept it as true.
"ac-KNOWLEDGE"——当你acknowledge某事时，你表明你对此有knowledge（知识/了解）并接受它是真实的。
