# 🎭 artful

🔊 /ˈɑːrtfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Clever or skillful, especially in a crafty or cunning way; showing creative skill or taste
**中文：** adj. 巧妙的；狡猾的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cunning（狡猾的）
- crafty（诡计多端的）
- ingenious（巧妙的）
- shrewd（精明的）
- sly（狡诈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **artful dodger / artful manipulation / artful design / artful negotiation**

👉 Example: The politician gave an artful response that avoided the question entirely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The negotiator employed an **artful** strategy that persuaded both parties to accept the compromise without further dispute.
   谈判者运用了一种巧妙的策略，说服双方接受了妥协方案而没有进一步争议。

2. The con artist's **artful** deception was so convincing that even experienced investigators were initially taken in by it.
   这位骗子狡猾的欺骗手段如此令人信服，连经验丰富的调查员最初也上了当。

3. The architect's **artful** integration of modern materials with classical forms earned widespread acclaim from design critics.
   这位建筑师将现代材料与古典形式巧妙结合，赢得了设计评论界的广泛赞誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "art-ful" as "full of art" — someone artful is full of clever tricks, like an artist who skillfully manipulates the canvas.
把"artful"想成"full of art（充满技巧）"——artful的人充满巧妙的手段，就像艺术家巧妙地操控画布一样。
