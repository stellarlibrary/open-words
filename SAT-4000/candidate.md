# 🗳️ candidate

🔊 /ˈkændɪdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who applies for a job or is nominated for an election; a person taking an examination or being considered for a position.
**中文：** n. 候选人；候补者；应试者；报考者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- applicant（申请人）
- nominee（被提名人）
- contender（竞争者）
- aspirant（有抱负的人）
- prospect（有希望的人选）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **candidate for / presidential candidate / strong candidate / job candidate / candidate pool**

👉 Example: She emerged as the leading candidate for the position after a series of rigorous interviews.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The selection committee interviewed twelve **candidates** before narrowing the list down to three finalists for the position.
   遴选委员会面试了十二名候选人，最终将名单缩减至三名最终入选者。

2. As a doctoral **candidate**, she devoted years of meticulous research to analyzing patterns in global climate data.
   作为一名博士候选人，她投入多年细致的研究来分析全球气候数据中的模式。

3. The ideal **candidate** for this role must demonstrate exceptional leadership skills and extensive experience in public administration.
   这一职位的理想候选人必须展示出卓越的领导能力和丰富的公共管理经验。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "candidate" as coming from Latin "candidatus" meaning "dressed in white" — Roman candidates wore white togas to symbolize their purity and worthiness for office.
"Candidate"源自拉丁语"candidatus"，意为"穿白衣的人"——古罗马候选人穿白色长袍来象征他们的纯洁和担任公职的资格。
