# 🏙 alley

🔊 /ˈæli/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A narrow passageway between or behind buildings; a small lane or path
**中文：** 小巷；小路；小径

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lane（小巷）
- passage（通道）
- pathway（小路）
- backstreet（后街）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dark alley / narrow alley / back alley / bowling alley / right up one's alley**

👉 Example: The detective followed the suspect through a dimly lit alley behind the abandoned warehouse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The old city is famous for its winding **alleys** that lead visitors through centuries of architectural history and cultural heritage.
   这座老城以蜿蜒的小巷闻名，带领游客穿越数百年的建筑历史与文化遗产。

2. Local merchants set up colorful stalls along the narrow **alley**, transforming it into a vibrant open-air market every weekend.
   当地商贩沿着狭窄的小巷搭起了五颜六色的摊位，每个周末都将其变成一个充满活力的露天市场。

3. The cat disappeared into a dark **alley** between two abandoned buildings, evading the animal control officers with remarkable agility.
   那只猫消失在两栋废弃建筑之间的一条黑暗小巷中，以惊人的敏捷躲避了动物管控人员。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alley" as "all-ey" — a narrow path where "all" you can see is the "way" ahead between tight walls.
把"alley"想成"all-ey"——一条狭窄的路，在紧凑的墙壁之间你能看到的"全部"就是前方的"路"。
