# 😤 impenitent

🔊 /ɪmˈpɛnɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who is stubbornly unrepentant. (adj.) Not feeling shame or regret about one's actions; stubbornly unrepentant
**中文：** n. 顽固的人；不知悔改的人 adj. 顽固的；不知悔改的；不悔悟的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unrepentant（不悔改的）
- remorseless（无悔意的）
- unapologetic（不道歉的）
- obstinate（顽固的）
- incorrigible（不可救药的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impenitent sinner / impenitent attitude / remain impenitent / wholly impenitent**

👉 Example: The impenitent offender showed no remorse when confronted with evidence of his repeated misconduct.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge imposed a harsher sentence because the defendant remained thoroughly **impenitent** throughout the entire court proceedings.
   法官加重了判决，因为被告在整个庭审过程中始终毫无悔意。

2. Despite widespread condemnation from the academic community, the **impenitent** researcher refused to retract his fraudulent published findings.
   尽管遭到学术界的广泛谴责，这位不知悔改的研究人员拒绝撤回其伪造的已发表研究成果。

3. Her **impenitent** refusal to acknowledge the harm caused by her statements only deepened the rift among her colleagues.
   她顽固地拒绝承认其言论造成的伤害，这只加深了她与同事之间的裂痕。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-penitent" as "not penitent" — the prefix "im-" means "not," and "penitent" means feeling sorry, so impenitent means not sorry at all.
把"impenitent"拆成"im（不）+ penitent（忏悔的）"——不忏悔的，也就是不知悔改的。
