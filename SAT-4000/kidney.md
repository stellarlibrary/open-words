# 🫘 kidney

🔊 /ˈkɪdni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Either of a pair of organs in the body that filter waste from the blood and produce urine; temperament or nature (archaic)
**中文：** n. 肾脏；腰子；个性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- renal organ（肾脏器官）
- temperament（性情）
- disposition（性格）
- nature（本性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **kidney function / kidney failure / kidney transplant / kidney stone / of the same kidney**

👉 Example: The patient was referred to a specialist after routine tests revealed declining kidney function.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Chronic dehydration can significantly impair **kidney** function and lead to the formation of painful mineral deposits.
   慢性脱水会严重损害肾功能，并导致形成疼痛的矿物质沉积。

2. The medical team successfully performed a **kidney** transplant that restored the patient's health and quality of life.
   医疗团队成功完成了一次肾脏移植手术，恢复了患者的健康和生活质量。

3. Researchers discovered that a particular gene mutation increases the risk of developing **kidney** disease in later life.
   研究人员发现，一种特定的基因突变会增加晚年患肾脏疾病的风险。

Think of "kid-ney" as a "kid's knee" — picture a child pointing to their lower back where the kidneys are, just above the knee level.
把"kidney"想成"kid's knee"（小孩的膝盖）——想象一个孩子指着肾脏所在的腰部位置，就在膝盖水平之上。
