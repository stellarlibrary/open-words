# ⚖️ abet

🔊 /əˈbɛt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To encourage, assist, or support, especially in wrongdoing; to incite or instigate
**中文：** vt. 教唆，煽动；帮助，支持（尤指做坏事）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incite（煽动）
- instigate（唆使）
- encourage（鼓励）
- facilitate（促成）
- aid（帮助）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aid and abet / abet a crime / abet someone in doing something**

👉 Example: The court found that she had aided and abetted the fugitive in evading law enforcement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. He was charged with **abetting** the fraud by providing false financial documents to the investigators.
   他因向调查人员提供虚假财务文件而被指控教唆欺诈。

2. The corrupt official was accused of **abetting** illegal arms deals that destabilized the entire region.
   这名腐败官员被指控纵容非法武器交易，导致整个地区局势不稳。

3. Anyone found to have **abetted** this conspiracy will face severe criminal prosecution under federal law.
   任何被发现参与教唆此阴谋的人都将面临联邦法律的严厉刑事起诉。

---

Think: "a BET gone wrong" — when you abet someone, you are betting on their bad behavior by helping them.
想想："a BET gone wrong"——当你abet某人时，你就是在赌他们的恶行，帮助他们做坏事。
