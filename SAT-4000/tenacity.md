# 🔩 tenacity

🔊 /tɪˈnæsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being persistent and determined; firmness of hold or purpose; the quality of being cohesive
**中文：** n. 韧性；固执；不屈不挠；黏性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perseverance（毅力）
- determination（决心）
- persistence（坚持）
- resilience（韧性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show tenacity / remarkable tenacity / sheer tenacity / tenacity of purpose / with great tenacity**

👉 Example: It was her remarkable tenacity that allowed her to overcome every obstacle on the path to success.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The entrepreneur's **tenacity** in pursuing her vision ultimately transformed a modest startup into a multinational corporation.
   这位企业家追求愿景的坚韧最终将一家小型初创公司转变为一家跨国公司。

2. Historians often attribute the success of the civil rights movement to the extraordinary **tenacity** of its dedicated leaders.
   历史学家常常将民权运动的成功归功于其领导者非凡的不屈不挠精神。

3. The climbing team demonstrated remarkable **tenacity** by continuing their ascent despite severe weather conditions and dwindling supplies.
   登山队展现了非凡的韧性，尽管天气恶劣且补给减少，仍继续攀登。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tenac-ity" — the noun form of tenacious, like a city ("city" sounds like "-ity") that never falls, built on sheer determination.
把"tenacity"想成tenacious的名词形式——就像一座永不陷落的城市（city），建立在纯粹的决心之上。
