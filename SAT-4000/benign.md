# 🌿 benign

🔊 /bɪˈnaɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Gentle and kind in disposition; not harmful or dangerous; (medical) not malignant or cancerous
**中文：** adj. 良性的；和蔼的，亲切的；吉利的；良好的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmless（无害的）
- gentle（温和的）
- kindly（和蔼的）
- innocuous（无害的）
- favorable（有利的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **benign tumor / benign neglect / benign smile / benign influence / benign climate**

👉 Example: The doctor reassured the patient that the growth was entirely benign and required no further treatment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After extensive testing, the pathologist confirmed that the tumor was **benign** and posed no threat to the patient.
   经过广泛检测，病理学家确认该肿瘤为良性的，不会对患者构成威胁。

2. The professor's **benign** manner put even the most anxious students at ease during oral examinations.
   教授和蔼的态度使最焦虑的学生在口试时也能放松下来。

3. What appeared to be a **benign** policy change ultimately had profound and far-reaching consequences for the industry.
   看似无害的政策变化最终对该行业产生了深远的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "benign" as "be nice" rearranged — something benign is nice, gentle, and not harmful at all.
把"benign"想象成"be nice（友善）"的变形——良性的东西是温和的、无害的。
