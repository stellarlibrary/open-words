# 🍬 sugar

🔊 /ˈʃʊɡər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sweet crystalline substance; a term of endearment; flattery. (v.) To add sugar to; to make more palatable
**中文：** n. 糖类

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sucrose（蔗糖）
- sweetener（甜味剂）
- glucose（葡萄糖）
- saccharide（糖类）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sugar intake / sugar content / sugar-coat / blood sugar / refined sugar**

👉 Example: Nutritionists recommend reducing daily sugar intake to lower the risk of chronic metabolic diseases.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Excessive consumption of refined **sugar** has been conclusively linked to increased rates of obesity and cardiovascular disease worldwide.
   过量摄入精制糖已被确定地与全球肥胖和心血管疾病发病率的增加有关。

2. The pharmaceutical company decided to **sugar**-coat the tablets to improve patient compliance with the prescribed medication regimen.
   制药公司决定给药片包糖衣，以提高患者对处方用药方案的依从性。

3. Biochemists analyzed the molecular structure of various **sugar** compounds to better understand their metabolic pathways in human cells.
   生物化学家分析了各种糖类化合物的分子结构，以更好地了解其在人体细胞中的代谢途径。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sugar" — it looks and sounds sweet, just like the substance itself, making it one of the easiest words to remember.
想到"sugar"——它看起来和听起来都很甜，就像糖本身一样，是最容易记住的单词之一。
