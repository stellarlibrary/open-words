# 🔧 practicable

🔊 /ˈpræktɪkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be done or put into practice successfully; feasible and usable in real-world conditions
**中文：** adj. 可用的；行得通的；可实行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- feasible（可行的）
- viable（切实可行的）
- workable（行得通的）
- achievable（可实现的）
- attainable（可达到的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **practicable solution / as soon as practicable / practicable approach / where practicable / practicable alternative**

👉 Example: The engineers devised a practicable plan to reinforce the aging bridge within the allotted budget.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee recommended implementing the most **practicable** solution to address the city's growing infrastructure challenges within budget constraints.
   委员会建议在预算限制内实施最可行的方案来解决城市日益增长的基础设施挑战。

2. The regulations require that all emissions be reduced to the lowest **practicable** level using currently available technology.
   法规要求使用现有技术将所有排放降低到最低可行水平。

3. The diplomat urged both parties to reach an agreement as soon as **practicable** to prevent further escalation of the conflict.
   外交官敦促双方尽快达成协议，以防止冲突进一步升级。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "practicable" as "practice + able" — something you are able to put into practice, meaning it is feasible and workable.
把"practicable"想成"practice（实践）+ able（能够）"——你能够付诸实践的事物，意味着它是可行的。
