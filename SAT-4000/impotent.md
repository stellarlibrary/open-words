# 😞 impotent

🔊 /ˈɪmpətənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lacking power, strength, or ability to take effective action; helpless; unable to produce a desired effect
**中文：** adj. 无力的；无效的；虚弱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- powerless（无力的）
- helpless（无助的）
- ineffectual（无效的）
- feeble（虚弱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impotent rage / impotent fury / feel impotent / render impotent**

👉 Example: Citizens expressed impotent rage at the government's failure to address the ongoing crisis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The small nation's military forces were essentially **impotent** against the overwhelming technological superiority of the invading army.
   这个小国的军事力量在入侵军队压倒性的技术优势面前基本上无能为力。

2. Without adequate funding and proper enforcement mechanisms, the regulatory agency remained **impotent** in its efforts to curb corporate fraud.
   由于缺乏充足的资金和适当的执法机制，监管机构在遏制企业欺诈方面仍然无力。

3. The opposition party's **impotent** protests failed to prevent the passage of the controversial bill through the legislative chamber.
   反对党无力的抗议未能阻止争议法案在立法机构获得通过。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "IM-POTENT" — the prefix "im-" means "not," so impotent literally means "not potent," lacking power or strength.
把"impotent"拆分为"im（不）+ potent（有力的）"——字面意思就是"无力的"，缺乏力量。
