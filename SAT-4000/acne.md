# 😖 acne

🔊 /ˈækni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A skin condition characterized by inflamed or infected sebaceous glands, producing red pimples especially on the face
**中文：** 痤疮；粉刺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pimples（粉刺）
- blemishes（瑕疵）
- breakouts（爆痘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffer from acne / acne breakout / acne treatment / severe acne**

👉 Example: Many teenagers suffer from acne during puberty due to hormonal changes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Dermatologists recommend gentle cleansers and prescribed medications to effectively treat severe **acne** in adolescents.
   皮肤科医生推荐使用温和的洁面产品和处方药物来有效治疗青少年的严重痤疮。

2. The clinical study demonstrated that stress and dietary habits can significantly exacerbate **acne** in young adults.
   该临床研究表明，压力和饮食习惯会显著加重年轻人的痤疮。

3. Persistent **acne** may leave permanent scarring on the skin if left untreated for an extended period.
   持续的痤疮如果长期不治疗，可能会在皮肤上留下永久性疤痕。

---

Think of "acne" as "ac-knee" — imagine pimples so bad they spread from your face all the way down to your knee.
把"acne"想成"ac-knee"——想象粉刺严重到从脸上一直蔓延到膝盖。
