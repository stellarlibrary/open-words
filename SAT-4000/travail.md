# 😣 travail

🔊 /trəˈveɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Painful or laborious effort; the pains of childbirth. (v.) To engage in hard or agonizing labor; to toil strenuously
**中文：** n. 分娩的痛苦；艰苦劳动 v. 辛勤努力；艰苦奋斗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- toil（辛苦劳动）
- hardship（艰难）
- labor（劳作）
- ordeal（磨难）
- struggle（挣扎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **years of travail / travail of war / endure travail / travail through hardship**

👉 Example: The nation emerged stronger after decades of political travail and social upheaval.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years of **travail** in poverty and obscurity, the author finally achieved recognition with her critically acclaimed novel.
   在贫困和默默无闻中艰苦奋斗多年后，这位作家终于凭借其广受好评的小说获得了认可。

2. The documentary portrayed the **travail** of migrant workers who endure grueling conditions to support their families abroad.
   这部纪录片描绘了移民工人的艰辛，他们忍受着艰苦的条件来养活海外的家人。

3. Scientists continue to **travail** over the complexities of fusion energy, a challenge that has persisted for decades.
   科学家们继续为核聚变能源的复杂性艰苦努力，这一挑战已持续了数十年。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "travail" as "travel" through hardship — a difficult journey full of toil and suffering, like an exhausting trek.
把"travail"想成经历苦难的"travel（旅行）"——一段充满辛劳和苦难的艰难旅程，就像一次精疲力竭的跋涉。
