# 🎨 graphic

🔊 /ˈɡræfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Giving a vivid, detailed description; relating to visual art or drawing; of or in the form of a graph
**中文：** adj. 生动的，形象的；绘画的，图表的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vivid（生动的）
- detailed（详尽的）
- pictorial（图画的）
- descriptive（描述性的）
- illustrative（说明性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **graphic description / graphic detail / graphic design / graphic novel / graphic content**

👉 Example: The journalist provided a graphic account of the devastation caused by the earthquake.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary contained **graphic** footage of the disaster that left many viewers deeply disturbed and emotionally shaken.
   这部纪录片包含了灾难的生动画面，令许多观众深感不安和情感上的震撼。

2. Her **graphic** description of life in the refugee camp compelled the committee to approve additional humanitarian funding.
   她对难民营生活的形象描述促使委员会批准了额外的人道主义资金。

3. The museum's new exhibition featured **graphic** illustrations from medieval manuscripts that depicted everyday life in remarkable detail.
   博物馆的新展览展出了中世纪手稿中的绘画插图，以惊人的细节描绘了日常生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "graphic" as something so visually clear it could be drawn — a graphic description paints a picture in your mind.
把"graphic"想成画面感极强、仿佛可以画出来的东西——一段graphic的描述能在你脑海中画出一幅画。
