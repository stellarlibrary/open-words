# 💪 fortitude

🔊 /ˈfɔːrtɪtuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Courage and strength of mind in facing difficulty, adversity, danger, or temptation with resolve
**中文：** n. 刚毅；坚忍；不屈不挠；勇气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resilience（韧性）
- endurance（忍耐力）
- perseverance（毅力）
- tenacity（坚韧）
- grit（坚毅）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show fortitude / moral fortitude / mental fortitude / fortitude in the face of adversity**

👉 Example: The prisoners of war displayed remarkable fortitude during their long captivity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The community demonstrated extraordinary **fortitude** in rebuilding their town after the devastating earthquake destroyed nearly every structure.
   这个社区在毁灭性地震摧毁了几乎所有建筑后，展现了非凡的刚毅来重建家园。

2. Her **fortitude** throughout the grueling legal proceedings inspired other victims to come forward and share their own stories.
   她在艰苦的法律诉讼过程中表现出的坚韧激励了其他受害者站出来分享自己的经历。

3. Historians often celebrate the **fortitude** of early explorers who ventured into unknown territories with minimal resources and support.
   历史学家经常赞颂早期探险家的勇气，他们在资源和支持极少的情况下冒险进入未知领域。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fort-itude" — a "fort" is a stronghold, so fortitude is the inner "fort" of your mind, the strength that holds firm under pressure.
把"fortitude"想成内心的"fort"（堡垒）——堡垒是坚固的据点，所以fortitude就是你内心的堡垒，在压力下保持坚定的力量。
