# 🔬 mite

🔊 /maɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A very small amount or creature; a tiny arachnid; a small child; something extremely small or insignificant
**中文：** n. 极小量；小虫；小孩儿；微小的东西

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- speck（微粒）
- particle（微粒）
- morsel（少量）
- trace（微量）
- bit（一点点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a mite of / not a mite / dust mite / a poor little mite / a mite too small**

👉 Example: The child looked like a tiny mite standing alone in the enormous school auditorium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher discovered that the dust **mite** population in urban households was significantly higher than previously estimated.
   研究人员发现城市家庭中尘螨的数量远高于此前的估计。

2. Despite contributing only a **mite** to the overall budget, her donation carried enormous symbolic value for the charity.
   尽管她对总预算的贡献微乎其微，但她的捐赠对慈善机构具有巨大的象征意义。

3. The poor little **mite** had been wandering the streets alone before a compassionate stranger brought her to safety.
   这个可怜的小家伙独自在街上徘徊，后来一位好心的陌生人把她带到了安全的地方。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mite" as "mighty small" — a mite sounds like "might" but refers to something that has no might at all, something tiny.
把"mite"想成"mighty small"——mite听起来像"might"（力量），但它指的是毫无力量、极其微小的东西。
