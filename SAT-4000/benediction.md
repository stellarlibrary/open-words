# 🙏 benediction

🔊 /ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A spoken blessing, especially one given at the end of a religious service; an expression of good wishes or divine favor
**中文：** n. 祝福；赐福；恩赐；祈求上帝赐福的仪式

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blessing（祝福）
- invocation（祈祷）
- prayer（祈祷）
- grace（恩典）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pronounce a benediction / offer a benediction / the closing benediction / a benediction upon**

👉 Example: The priest offered a solemn benediction at the conclusion of the wedding ceremony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bishop pronounced a heartfelt **benediction** over the congregation, invoking divine protection for the year ahead.
   主教对会众宣读了一段真挚的祝福词，祈求神灵庇佑来年平安。

2. Her grandmother's parting words served as a quiet **benediction**, carrying warmth and wisdom that endured through the years.
   祖母临别的话语如同一份安静的祝福，其中的温暖与智慧历久弥新。

3. The commencement speaker concluded with a **benediction** that urged graduates to pursue justice and serve their communities faithfully.
   毕业典礼演讲者以一段祝福结束，勉励毕业生追求正义并忠诚地服务社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bene-" (good, from Latin) + "-diction" (speaking) — a benediction is literally "good speaking," a spoken blessing.
"Bene-"（好的）+"diction"（说话）——benediction字面意思就是"说好话"，即祝福。
