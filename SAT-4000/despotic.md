# 👑 despotic

🔊 /dɪˈspɒtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to a despot; exercising absolute power in a cruel or oppressive manner; tyrannical
**中文：** adj. 专制的；暴虐的；独裁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tyrannical（暴虐的）
- authoritarian（专制的）
- dictatorial（独裁的）
- oppressive（压迫的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **despotic ruler / despotic regime / despotic power / despotic rule**

👉 Example: The despotic regime suppressed all forms of political dissent through censorship and imprisonment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **despotic** monarch imposed crushing taxes on the peasantry while lavishing enormous sums on extravagant royal palaces.
   这位专制的君主对农民征收沉重的赋税，同时将巨额资金挥霍在奢华的皇家宫殿上。

2. Historians argue that **despotic** governments inevitably sow the seeds of their own destruction through systematic oppression of citizens.
   历史学家认为，专制政府通过系统性地压迫公民，不可避免地埋下了自我毁灭的种子。

3. The novel portrays a **despotic** corporate executive who manipulates employees through intimidation and relentless psychological control.
   这部小说描绘了一位专横的企业高管，通过恐吓和无情的心理控制来操纵员工。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "despot-ic" as relating to a "despot" — a ruler who "des-pots" people, smashing them like pots under absolute power.
把"despotic"想成与"despot（暴君）"相关——一个像砸碎罐子（pots）一样用绝对权力碾压人民的统治者。
