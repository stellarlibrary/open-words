# 🏅 motto

🔊 /ˈmɑːtoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A short phrase or sentence expressing a guiding principle, belief, or ideal adopted by an individual, family, or institution
**中文：** n. 座右铭，格言；箴言

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- maxim（格言）
- adage（谚语）
- slogan（标语）
- creed（信条）
- saying（箴言）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **live by a motto / adopt a motto / the school motto / personal motto**

👉 Example: The university's motto has inspired generations of students to pursue excellence in scholarship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation adopted the **motto** "Innovation Through Integrity" to reflect its commitment to ethical business practices.
   该公司采用了"以诚信促创新"的座右铭，以体现其对商业道德的承诺。

2. Her personal **motto** of perseverance guided her through years of rigorous academic training and professional setbacks.
   她坚持不懈的个人格言引导她度过了多年严格的学术训练和职业挫折。

3. The ancient family **motto** inscribed above the entrance hall served as a constant reminder of their noble heritage.
   刻在门厅上方的古老家族箴言时刻提醒着他们高贵的传承。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "motto" as "MOT-to" — the "MOT" (word) you speak "TO" yourself every day as your guiding principle.
把"motto"想成"MOT-to"——你每天对自己说的那句"话(mot)"，作为你的指导原则。
