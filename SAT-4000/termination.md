# 🔚 termination

🔊 /ˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of bringing something to an end; the conclusion or cessation of a process, agreement, or condition
**中文：** 结束，终止；终结

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cessation（停止）
- conclusion（结束）
- discontinuation（中断）
- expiration（到期）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **termination of a contract / termination of employment / early termination / grounds for termination**

👉 Example: The termination of the lease agreement required thirty days of written notice from either party.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board voted unanimously for the **termination** of the partnership due to repeated violations of contractual obligations.
   董事会因合同义务的反复违反而一致投票终止合作关系。

2. Upon **termination** of employment, all company-issued devices and identification badges must be returned immediately to the department.
   雇佣关系终止后，所有公司发放的设备和身份证件必须立即归还给相关部门。

3. The **termination** of the research program left dozens of scientists seeking new positions at universities across the country.
   该研究项目的终止使数十名科学家在全国各大学寻找新职位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "termin-ation" — "terminal" is the end of the line, so "termination" is the act of reaching that end.
把"termination"想成"terminal + ation"——"terminal"是终点，所以"termination"就是到达终点的行为。
