# ✒️ indelible

🔊 /ɪnˈdɛləbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Making marks that cannot be erased or removed; not able to be forgotten; permanently memorable
**中文：** adj. 难忘的；擦不掉的；不能拭除的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permanent（永久的）
- unforgettable（难忘的）
- enduring（持久的）
- lasting（持久的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indelible mark / indelible impression / indelible ink / indelible memory**

👉 Example: The civil rights movement left an indelible mark on the history of the nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The humanitarian crisis left an **indelible** impression on the young journalist, forever shaping her approach to investigative reporting.
   这场人道主义危机给这位年轻记者留下了难以磨灭的印象，永远改变了她的调查报道方式。

2. Voters used **indelible** ink on their fingers to prevent fraud, ensuring that each citizen could cast only one ballot.
   选民们在手指上使用不可擦除的墨水以防止欺诈，确保每位公民只能投一票。

3. The professor's passionate lectures on philosophy made an **indelible** impact on generations of students who studied under him.
   这位教授关于哲学的热情讲座对在他门下学习的几代学生产生了不可磨灭的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-delete-ible" — something that cannot be deleted, a mark so deep and permanent that no eraser can remove it.
把"indelible"想成"in-delete-ible"——不能被删除的东西，一个如此深刻和永久的印记，任何橡皮擦都无法抹去。
