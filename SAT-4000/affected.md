# 🪞 affected

🔊 /əˈfɛktɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Influenced or touched by an external factor; artificial or pretentious in behavior or manner.
**中文：** 受到影响的；做作的；假装的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pretentious（矫饰的）
- artificial（做作的）
- contrived（不自然的）
- influenced（受影响的）
- feigned（假装的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **manner, style, deeply, emotionally, areas**
👉 Example: The residents of the affected areas received immediate assistance from relief organizations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The communities most severely **affected** by the flooding required months of sustained recovery and rebuilding efforts.
   受洪水影响最严重的社区需要数月的持续恢复和重建工作。

2. His **affected** manner of speaking, filled with unnecessary flourishes, irritated his more straightforward colleagues considerably.
   他那矫揉造作的说话方式充满了不必要的修饰，让他更为直率的同事们相当恼火。

3. The new policy has **affected** thousands of workers across the manufacturing sector in significant and lasting ways.
   新政策以重大而持久的方式影响了制造业数以千计的工人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Affected" has two faces: something "affected by" change (influenced) or someone who is "affected" (fake) — both involve an outside force acting on the original.
"affected"有两面含义：被变化"影响的"或行为"做作的"——两者都涉及外力作用于原本的状态。
