# 🔬 assiduous

🔊 /əˈsɪdʒuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing great care, perseverance, and meticulous attention to detail; constant in application or effort
**中文：** 刻苦的，勤勉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diligent（勤勉的）
- industrious（勤奋的）
- sedulous（刻苦的）
- painstaking（煞费苦心的）
- tireless（不知疲倦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **assiduous effort / assiduous student / assiduous attention / assiduous in doing something**

👉 Example: An assiduous researcher double-checks every source before drawing conclusions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **assiduous** scholar spent countless hours in the library verifying every citation in her doctoral dissertation.
   这位勤勉的学者在图书馆花费了无数个小时核实她博士论文中的每一个引用。

2. Through **assiduous** practice and unwavering dedication, the young pianist mastered all of Chopin's most demanding etudes.
   通过刻苦的练习和坚定不移的奉献，这位年轻钢琴家掌握了肖邦所有最高难度的练习曲。

3. The detective's **assiduous** investigation of overlooked evidence ultimately revealed the true perpetrator of the crime.
   这位侦探对被忽视证据的勤勉调查最终揭露了犯罪的真正肇事者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assiduous" as "a-SIT-you-us" — picture someone who sits with you, us, working tirelessly until the job is done.
把"assiduous"想成"坐在那里陪你我一起干"——想象一个人坐在那里与你一起不知疲倦地工作直到完成。
