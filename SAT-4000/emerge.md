# 🌅 emerge

🔊 /ɪˈmɜːrdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To come into view or existence; to become apparent or known; to rise or come forth from a concealed or difficult situation
**中文：** v. 浮现；出现；摆脱；暴露

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appear（出现）
- surface（浮出）
- arise（产生）
- materialize（显现）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emerge from / emerge as a leader / emerge victorious / new evidence emerges**

👉 Example: After months of deliberation, a clear consensus finally emerged among the committee members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. New evidence **emerged** during the trial that fundamentally altered the jury's understanding of the defendant's role in the incident.
   审判期间浮现的新证据从根本上改变了陪审团对被告在事件中所扮演角色的理解。

2. The nation **emerged** from decades of economic stagnation to become one of the fastest-growing economies in the region.
   该国从数十年的经济停滞中摆脱出来，成为该地区增长最快的经济体之一。

3. Several promising candidates **emerged** from the highly competitive selection process for the prestigious research fellowship program.
   几位有前途的候选人从这一极具竞争力的知名研究奖学金遴选过程中脱颖而出。

---

把"emerge"想成"e-MERGE"——某物从黑暗中合并（MERGE）到光明中，第一次变得可见。
