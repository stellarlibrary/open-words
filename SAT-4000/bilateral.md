# 🤝 bilateral

🔊 /baɪˈlætərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or involving two sides; affecting or undertaken by two parties, especially countries
**中文：** 双边的；有两边的；双方的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- two-sided（双边的）
- mutual（相互的）
- reciprocal（互惠的）
- bipartite（由两部分组成的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bilateral agreement / bilateral talks / bilateral trade / bilateral relations / bilateral cooperation**

👉 Example: The two nations signed a bilateral trade agreement to reduce tariffs and promote economic growth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bilateral** negotiations between the two countries resulted in a comprehensive framework for security cooperation and trade.
   两国之间的双边谈判达成了一项关于安全合作与贸易的全面框架。

2. Surgeons performed a **bilateral** knee replacement to restore full mobility for the patient who had suffered from chronic arthritis.
   外科医生进行了双侧膝关节置换手术，以恢复长期患有慢性关节炎的患者的全部活动能力。

3. The **bilateral** partnership strengthened diplomatic ties and facilitated cultural exchange programs between the neighboring states.
   双边伙伴关系加强了外交纽带，并促进了邻国之间的文化交流项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "lateral" (side) — bilateral means involving two sides, like two countries shaking hands on a deal.
把"bilateral"拆分为"bi-"（二）+"lateral"（边、侧）——双边的，就像两个国家握手达成协议。
