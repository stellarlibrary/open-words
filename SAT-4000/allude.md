# 💬 allude

🔊 /əˈluːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To suggest or indirectly refer to something without explicitly mentioning it
**中文：** 暗指；间接提到

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hint（暗示）
- imply（暗示）
- suggest（暗示）
- refer（提及）
- intimate（暗示）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **allude to something / allude to the fact that / allude to a problem**

👉 Example: The speaker alluded to recent scandals without naming any specific individuals involved.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author frequently **alluded** to classical mythology throughout the novel to enrich the narrative with deeper symbolic significance.
   作者在整部小说中频繁暗指古典神话，以更深层的象征意义丰富叙事。

2. During the press conference, the senator carefully **alluded** to potential policy changes without committing to any specific legislative proposals.
   在新闻发布会上，参议员谨慎地暗指了潜在的政策变化，但没有承诺任何具体的立法提案。

3. The diplomat **alluded** to growing tensions between the two nations but deliberately refrained from making any provocative public statements.
   外交官暗指了两国之间日益紧张的关系，但刻意避免发表任何挑衅性的公开声明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "allude" as "a-LUDE" — like playing a subtle "prelude" that hints at the main theme without revealing it directly.
把"allude"想成"a-LUDE"——就像演奏一段微妙的"前奏"，暗示主旋律但不直接揭示。
