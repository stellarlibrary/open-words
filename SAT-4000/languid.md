# 😴 languid

🔊 /ˈlæŋɡwɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Displaying a lack of energy or vitality; weak, listless, or sluggish in movement or manner
**中文：** adj. 疲倦的；无力的；没精打采的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- listless（无精打采的）
- lethargic（昏昏欲睡的）
- sluggish（迟缓的）
- torpid（麻木的）
- languorous（慵懒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **languid pace / languid gesture / languid afternoon / languid expression**

👉 Example: The students adopted a languid pace during the unusually warm afternoon lecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The oppressive summer heat left the workers **languid** and unable to maintain their usual level of productivity.
   酷热的夏天使工人们变得无精打采，无法保持正常的工作效率。

2. She made a **languid** gesture toward the window, barely summoning the energy to point at the approaching storm.
   她有气无力地朝窗户比了个手势，勉强提起精力指向正在逼近的暴风雨。

3. The river moved at a **languid** pace through the valley, its slow current reflecting the tranquility of the surroundings.
   河水在山谷中缓缓流淌，它舒缓的水流映射出周围环境的宁静。
