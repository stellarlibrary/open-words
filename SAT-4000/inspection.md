# 🔎 inspection

🔊 /ɪnˈspɛkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A careful examination or scrutiny; an official review to ensure standards are met
**中文：** n. 视察；检查；检验；审视

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examination（检查）
- scrutiny（审查）
- review（审核）
- survey（调查）
- evaluation（评估）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **close inspection / routine inspection / on inspection / pass inspection / inspection of**

👉 Example: Upon closer inspection, the auditor discovered several discrepancies in the financial records.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The factory was temporarily shut down after a routine **inspection** revealed multiple violations of federal safety regulations.
   在一次例行检查发现多项违反联邦安全法规的行为后，该工厂被暂时关闭。

2. A thorough **inspection** of the ancient manuscript uncovered hidden text that had been obscured by centuries of accumulated grime.
   对这份古代手稿的彻底检验揭示了被数个世纪积累的污垢遮盖的隐藏文字。

3. The quality control team conducted a meticulous **inspection** of every component before the final product was approved for shipment.
   质量控制团队在最终产品获准发货前，对每个零部件进行了细致的检验。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in + spect (Latin for look)" — to look into something carefully, examining it closely and thoroughly.
把"inspection"想成"in + spect（拉丁语中'看'的意思）"——仔细地看进去，近距离彻底地检查。
