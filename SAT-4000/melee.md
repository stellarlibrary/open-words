# ⚔️ melee

🔊 /ˈmeɪleɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A confused, disorderly fight or struggle involving many people; a chaotic brawl or scuffle
**中文：** n. 混战；格斗；互殴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brawl（斗殴）
- skirmish（小冲突）
- scuffle（扭打）
- fray（争斗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in the melee / a wild melee / a violent melee / caught in the melee**

👉 Example: Several officers were injured in the melee that erupted outside the courthouse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A sudden **melee** broke out among the rival factions, forcing security personnel to intervene and restore order.
   敌对派系之间突然爆发了混战，迫使安保人员介入以恢复秩序。

2. During the chaotic **melee** in the marketplace, several valuable artifacts were damaged beyond any possibility of repair.
   在市场上的混乱格斗中，几件珍贵的文物遭到了无法修复的损坏。

3. The historian described the battle as a fierce **melee** in which disciplined formations quickly dissolved into hand-to-hand combat.
   历史学家将这场战役描述为一场激烈的混战，有序的阵型迅速瓦解为肉搏战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "melee" as "me + lee" — imagine "me" and "Lee" getting into a chaotic fight, with everyone joining in.
把"melee"想成"me（我）+ Lee（李）"——想象"我"和"李"卷入一场混乱的格斗，所有人都加入了。
