# 🫂 possessive

🔊 /pəˈzɛsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing desire to own or dominate; unwilling to share; relating to grammatical possession. (n.) The possessive case or form of a word
**中文：** adj. 占有的；所有的；所有格的；占有欲强的 n. 所有格

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- controlling（控制欲强的）
- jealous（嫉妒的）
- proprietary（所有权的）
- domineering（专横的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **possessive pronoun / possessive behavior / overly possessive / possessive case / possessive of**

👉 Example: The teacher explained that the possessive form of "it" is "its," written without an apostrophe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Psychologists warn that excessively **possessive** behavior in relationships often stems from deep-seated insecurity rather than genuine affection.
   心理学家警告说，关系中过度占有的行为往往源于根深蒂固的不安全感而非真正的感情。

2. Students must learn to distinguish between **possessive** pronouns and contractions, as this remains a common source of grammatical error.
   学生必须学会区分所有格代词和缩写形式，因为这仍然是语法错误的常见来源。

3. The documentary revealed how the collector became increasingly **possessive** of the rare artifacts, refusing to lend them to any institution.
   这部纪录片揭示了这位收藏家如何对珍稀文物变得越来越具有占有欲，拒绝将其借给任何机构。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "possessive" as "possess + ive" — someone who is possessive always wants to possess things and people around them.
把"possessive"想成"possess + ive"——一个占有欲强的人总是想要占有（possess）周围的东西和人。
