# 🚫 irrelevant

🔊 /ɪˈrɛləvənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not connected with or relevant to something; not applicable or pertinent to the matter at hand
**中文：** adj. 不相干的；不切题的；不相关的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unrelated（无关的）
- immaterial（不重要的）
- extraneous（无关的）
- inapplicable（不适用的）
- beside the point（离题的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irrelevant to / irrelevant details / irrelevant information / become irrelevant**

👉 Example: The defense attorney objected that the witness's testimony was entirely irrelevant to the charges being considered.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor dismissed the student's argument as **irrelevant** to the central thesis being debated in the seminar that afternoon.
   教授认为该学生的论点与当天下午研讨会上正在讨论的中心论题无关而予以驳回。

2. Advances in digital technology have rendered many traditional manufacturing techniques **irrelevant** in modern industrial production processes.
   数字技术的进步已使许多传统制造技术在现代工业生产过程中变得无关紧要。

3. The committee urged members to avoid raising **irrelevant** issues that would derail the discussion and delay the final decision.
   委员会敦促成员避免提出不相关的问题，以免使讨论偏离方向并延迟最终决定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irrelevant" as "ir-relevant" — the prefix "ir-" means "not," so it literally means "not relevant," like a puzzle piece that does not fit.
把"irrelevant"想成"ir-relevant"——前缀"ir-"意为"不"，所以它字面意思是"不相关的"，就像一块不合适的拼图。
