# 🔤 typography

🔊 /taɪˈpɒɡrəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The art, technique, or process of arranging type for printing; the style and appearance of printed matter
**中文：** n. 排印；活版印刷术；印刷排版

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- typesetting（排版）
- printing（印刷）
- font design（字体设计）
- lettering（刻字）
- type design（字体设计）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **typography and layout / elegant typography / digital typography / the art of typography**

👉 Example: The magazine is renowned for its striking typography and minimalist design aesthetic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **typography** of the Renaissance era reflected a profound shift from ornate manuscript scripts to cleaner, more legible typefaces.
   文艺复兴时期的排印反映了从华丽的手稿字体向更清晰易读的铅字字体的深刻转变。

2. Modern web designers must master **typography** to ensure that digital content remains accessible and visually appealing across devices.
   现代网页设计师必须掌握排版技术，以确保数字内容在各种设备上保持可访问性和视觉吸引力。

3. The professor delivered a lecture on the evolution of **typography** from Gutenberg's movable type to contemporary digital fonts.
   教授讲授了从古登堡活字印刷到当代数字字体的排印术演变。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "typo-graphy" as "type + writing" — the art of writing with type, arranging letters beautifully on a page.
把"typography"想成"typo（字体）+ graphy（书写）"——用字体书写的艺术，将文字优美地排列在页面上。
