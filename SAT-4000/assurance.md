# 🛡️ assurance

🔊 /əˈʃʊərəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A positive declaration intended to give confidence; a promise or guarantee; certainty or self-confidence
**中文：** 保证，担保；确信

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guarantee（保证）
- confidence（信心）
- pledge（承诺）
- certainty（确定性）
- conviction（确信）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **give assurance / assurance of quality / self-assurance / assurance that / with assurance**

👉 Example: The CEO gave shareholders a firm assurance that the company would return to profitability next quarter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite repeated **assurance** from the engineers, the residents remained skeptical about the structural safety of the bridge.
   尽管工程师们反复保证，居民们对这座桥的结构安全性仍持怀疑态度。

2. She spoke with quiet **assurance** during the board meeting, convincing every member to support her ambitious proposal.
   她在董事会会议上以沉稳的自信发言，说服了每位成员支持她雄心勃勃的提案。

3. The written **assurance** provided by the manufacturer guaranteed a full replacement if the product failed within two years.
   制造商提供的书面担保保证如果产品在两年内出现故障将提供全额更换。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assurance" as "a-SURE-ance" — it contains "sure," which is exactly what assurance gives you: the feeling of being sure and confident.
把"assurance"想成包含"sure（确定）"——这正是assurance给你的：一种确定和自信的感觉。
