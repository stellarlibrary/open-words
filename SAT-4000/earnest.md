# 🎯 earnest

🔊 /ˈɜːrnɪst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing sincere and intense conviction; serious in intention or effort. (n.) A thing intended or regarded as a sign of what is to come; seriousness
**中文：** adj. 认真的；热心的；诚挚的 n. 认真；诚意

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sincere（真诚的）
- fervent（热切的）
- solemn（庄重的）
- zealous（热心的）
- wholehearted（全心全意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in earnest / earnest effort / earnest desire / earnest appeal**

👉 Example: The negotiations began in earnest after both sides agreed to the preliminary conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scholar made an **earnest** appeal to the committee for additional funding to continue her groundbreaking research.
   这位年轻学者向委员会真诚地请求额外资金以继续她的开创性研究。

2. Relief efforts commenced in **earnest** once the full extent of the natural disaster became apparent to authorities.
   一旦当局充分了解自然灾害的全部范围，救援工作便认真地展开了。

3. His **earnest** commitment to social justice inspired an entire generation of activists to pursue meaningful reform.
   他对社会正义的真诚奉献激励了整整一代活动家去追求有意义的改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "earnest" as "earn-est" — the person who earns the most respect is the one who is most sincere and serious.
把"earnest"想成"earn-est"——赢得最多尊重的人就是最真诚、最认真的人。
