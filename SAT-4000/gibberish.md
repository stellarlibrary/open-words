# 🤪 gibberish

🔊 /ˈdʒɪbərɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unintelligible or meaningless speech or writing; nonsensical language that cannot be understood
**中文：** n. 快速而含混不清的言语；胡言乱语；莫名其妙的话

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nonsense（胡说）
- babble（胡言乱语）
- drivel（废话）
- jargon（行话，令人费解的话）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **talk gibberish / sound like gibberish / pure gibberish / technical gibberish**

👉 Example: The encrypted message appeared to be complete gibberish to anyone without the decryption key.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The complex legal document read like complete **gibberish** to those without formal training in jurisprudence.
   这份复杂的法律文件对于没有接受过法学正规训练的人来说如同天书。

2. Linguists determined that the ancient inscription was not **gibberish** but rather an undocumented dialect of a known language.
   语言学家确定这段古代铭文并非胡言乱语，而是一种已知语言中未被记录的方言。

3. The patient's speech deteriorated into incoherent **gibberish** as the fever intensified throughout the critical night.
   随着那个危急夜晚高烧加剧，病人的言语退化为语无伦次的呓语。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gibber-ish" — the suffix "-ish" means "like"; gibberish is speech that sounds like gibber (meaningless chatter).
把"gibberish"想成"gibber"（胡言）+"-ish"（像...的）——gibberish就是听起来像胡言乱语的话。
