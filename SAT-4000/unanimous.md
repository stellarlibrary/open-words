# 🗳️ unanimous

🔊 /juːˈnænɪməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Fully in agreement; with everyone holding the same opinion or making the same decision
**中文：** adj. 全体一致的；无异议的；一致同意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unified（统一的）
- concordant（一致的）
- harmonious（和谐的）
- undivided（一致的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unanimous decision / unanimous vote / unanimous agreement / unanimous support**

👉 Example: The committee reached a unanimous decision to fund the proposed public health research program.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The jury returned a **unanimous** verdict of not guilty after carefully reviewing all the evidence presented during the trial.
   陪审团在仔细审查审判中提交的所有证据后，一致裁定被告无罪。

2. The faculty senate gave **unanimous** approval to the revised curriculum that would take effect the following academic year.
   教授评议会一致批准了将于下一学年生效的修订课程方案。

3. It was a **unanimous** opinion among the analysts that the proposed merger would significantly benefit both organizations involved.
   分析师们一致认为，拟议的合并将使涉及的两个组织都显著受益。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "unanimous" as "uni (one) + animus (mind)" — having one mind, where everyone agrees without a single dissenting voice.
把"unanimous"拆分为"uni（一个）+ animus（心灵）"——众人一心，没有任何一个反对的声音。
