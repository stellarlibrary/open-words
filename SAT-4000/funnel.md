# 🔽 funnel

🔊 /ˈfʌnəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A cone-shaped utensil with a wide mouth and narrow stem, used for pouring liquids or powders into small openings; a smokestack on a ship or locomotive
**中文：** n. 漏斗；烟囱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- channel（通道）
- conduit（导管）
- chute（滑道）
- smokestack（烟囱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **funnel money into / funnel resources / sales funnel / through a funnel / funnel-shaped**

👉 Example: The organization used shell companies to funnel donations into unauthorized political campaigns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist carefully poured the volatile solution through a glass **funnel** to avoid spillage during the delicate transfer process.
   化学家小心翼翼地将挥发性溶液通过玻璃漏斗倾倒，以避免在精细的转移过程中发生溢出。

2. The marketing team redesigned their sales **funnel** to improve conversion rates at each stage of the customer acquisition journey.
   营销团队重新设计了他们的销售漏斗，以提高客户获取旅程中每个阶段的转化率。

3. Black smoke billowed from the ship's massive **funnel** as the aging vessel struggled to maintain speed against the current.
   当这艘老旧船只费力地顶着水流保持速度时，黑烟从船上巨大的烟囱中滚滚冒出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "funnel" as "fun + nel (channel)" — a fun little channel that guides everything from a wide opening down to a narrow point.
把"funnel"想成"fun（有趣的）+ nel（通道）"——一个有趣的小通道，将一切从宽阔的开口引导至狭窄的出口。
