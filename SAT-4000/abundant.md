# 🌿 abundant

🔊 /əˈbʌndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Existing or available in large quantities; more than enough; plentiful
**中文：** adj. 丰富的；充裕的；充分的；盛产的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plentiful（丰富的）
- copious（大量的）
- ample（充足的）
- profuse（丰富的）
- bountiful（慷慨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abundant resources / abundant evidence / abundant supply / abundant rainfall / abundant in**

👉 Example: The region is abundant in natural resources, making it a hub for industrial development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Fossil fuels were once considered **abundant**, but growing consumption has raised serious concerns about depletion.
   化石燃料曾被认为是充裕的，但不断增长的消费引发了对资源枯竭的严重担忧。

2. The researchers found **abundant** evidence that regular exercise significantly reduces the risk of chronic disease.
   研究人员发现了充分的证据表明定期锻炼能显著降低慢性病风险。

3. The valley's **abundant** rainfall supports a thriving ecosystem of diverse plant and animal species.
   山谷充沛的降雨维持着一个由多样化动植物物种构成的繁荣生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abundant" as "a-BUND-ant" — a BUNDLE so big that even an ANT could not carry it all, signifying excess.
把"abundant"想成"a-BUND-ant"——一捆(bundle)东西大到连蚂蚁(ant)都搬不完，表示数量极多。
