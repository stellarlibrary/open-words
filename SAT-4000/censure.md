# 👎 censure

🔊 /ˈsɛnʃər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To express severe disapproval of formally; to officially reprimand (n.) A formal expression of strong criticism or condemnation
**中文：** v. 严厉批评；正式谴责 n. 严厉批评；谴责

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- condemn（谴责）
- rebuke（斥责）
- reprimand（训斥）
- reproach（责备）
- admonish（告诫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **censure a politician / vote of censure / public censure / censure motion / censure for misconduct**

👉 Example: The senator faced formal censure from colleagues after violating the chamber's code of conduct.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ethics committee voted unanimously to **censure** the executive for his deliberate concealment of financial irregularities.
   伦理委员会一致投票谴责该高管故意隐瞒财务违规行为。

2. Public **censure** of the corporation intensified after evidence of environmental negligence was presented in court.
   在法庭上出示了环境疏忽的证据后，公众对该公司的谴责加剧了。

3. Historians note that the parliament's decision to **censure** the prime minister marked a significant turning point in legislative oversight.
   历史学家指出，议会谴责首相的决定标志着立法监督的一个重大转折点。

想想"censure"听起来像"sensor（传感器）"——传感器探测问题，而censure则正式发现并指出不当行为。
