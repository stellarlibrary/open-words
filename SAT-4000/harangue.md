# 📢 harangue

🔊 /həˈræŋ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A lengthy and aggressive speech or lecture. (v.) To lecture someone at length in an aggressive and critical manner
**中文：** n. 长篇大论；滔滔不绝的高声训斥 v. 滔滔不绝地演讲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tirade（长篇激烈演说）
- diatribe（抨击）
- lecture（训斥）
- rant（咆哮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliver a harangue / harangue the crowd / harangue about / a lengthy harangue**

👉 Example: The senator harangued his colleagues for nearly an hour about the failures of the proposed legislation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The activist delivered a passionate **harangue** against corporate negligence that captivated the entire audience for over an hour.
   这位活动家发表了一场长达一个多小时的激烈演说，抨击企业的疏忽，吸引了全场观众。

2. Rather than engage in constructive dialogue, the committee chair chose to **harangue** the witnesses about their perceived shortcomings.
   委员会主席没有进行建设性对话，而是选择对证人的缺点进行滔滔不绝的训斥。

3. The professor's tendency to **harangue** students about punctuality ultimately undermined his effectiveness as an inspiring educator.
   这位教授动辄就守时问题对学生进行长篇大论的倾向最终削弱了他作为鼓舞人心的教育者的效力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "harangue" as "her + rang" — she rang the bell and would not stop talking, delivering a long, loud speech that no one could escape.
把"harangue"想成"her + rang"——她敲响了铃，然后滔滔不绝地讲个不停，发表了一场谁也逃不掉的长篇大论。
