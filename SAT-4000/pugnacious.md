# 👊 pugnacious

🔊 /pʌɡˈneɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Eager or quick to argue, quarrel, or fight; combative and aggressive in nature
**中文：** adj. 好斗的，好战的；爱吵闹的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- combative（好斗的）
- belligerent（好战的）
- quarrelsome（爱争吵的）
- contentious（好争论的）
- bellicose（好战的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pugnacious attitude / pugnacious temperament / pugnacious spirit / pugnacious tone**

👉 Example: The senator's pugnacious demeanor during the hearing alienated many of his colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pugnacious** debater challenged every assertion made by the opposing team with relentless and sharp counterarguments.
   那位好斗的辩手以毫不留情的尖锐反驳挑战了对方团队提出的每一个论点。

2. His **pugnacious** temperament often led to unnecessary conflicts with colleagues who held differing professional opinions.
   他好斗的性格常常导致与持有不同职业观点的同事发生不必要的冲突。

3. The editorial criticized the governor for adopting a **pugnacious** stance rather than seeking diplomatic compromise on the issue.
   社论批评州长在这一问题上采取了好斗的立场，而非寻求外交妥协。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pug-nacious" — a pug dog may be small, but it has a feisty, aggressive spirit, always ready to pick a fight.
把"pugnacious"想成"pug（哈巴狗）+ nacious"——哈巴狗虽小，但斗志旺盛，随时准备战斗。
