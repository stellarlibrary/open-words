# 🏆 paragon

🔊 /ˈpærəɡɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A model of excellence or perfection; an outstanding example. (adj.) Perfect; flawless
**中文：** n. 模范；完美之物；优秀之人 adj. 完美的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exemplar（典范）
- ideal（理想）
- epitome（缩影）
- model（模范）
- archetype（原型）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **paragon of virtue / paragon of excellence / paragon of beauty / a true paragon**

👉 Example: She was widely regarded as a paragon of integrity in the legal profession.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university president was considered a **paragon** of academic leadership, consistently championing both scholarly rigor and student welfare.
   这位大学校长被认为是学术领导的典范，始终倡导学术严谨和学生福祉。

2. Critics praised the novel as a **paragon** of modern literary achievement, blending complex narrative structure with profound emotional depth.
   评论家称赞这部小说是现代文学成就的典范，将复杂的叙事结构与深刻的情感深度融为一体。

3. The ancient philosopher was celebrated as a **paragon** of wisdom whose teachings continued to influence ethical thought for centuries.
   这位古代哲学家被尊为智慧的典范，其教诲在数个世纪中持续影响着伦理思想。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "paragon" as "para" (beyond) + "gon" (gone) — someone who has gone beyond ordinary standards to become a perfect model.
把"paragon"想成"para"（超越）+ "gon"（gone，走过）——一个已经超越了普通标准、成为完美典范的人。
