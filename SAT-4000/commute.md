# 🚆 commute

🔊 /kəˈmjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To travel regularly between home and work; to exchange or substitute; to reduce a judicial sentence. (n.) A regular journey between home and work
**中文：** v. 通勤；交换；代偿；减刑 n. 通勤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- travel（旅行）
- exchange（交换）
- substitute（替代）
- mitigate（减轻）
- shuttle（穿梭往返）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commute to work / commute a sentence / daily commute / commute between A and B**

👉 Example: The governor decided to commute the prisoner's death sentence to life imprisonment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Thousands of employees **commute** by train from suburban areas to the financial district every weekday morning.
   每个工作日早晨，成千上万的员工乘火车从郊区通勤到金融区。

2. The judge agreed to **commute** the defendant's sentence from ten years of imprisonment to five years of probation.
   法官同意将被告的刑期从十年监禁减为五年缓刑。

3. Her daily **commute** across the metropolitan region takes approximately ninety minutes each way by public transportation.
   她每天乘坐公共交通穿越大都市区的通勤单程大约需要九十分钟。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "commute" as "com- (together) + mute (change)" — to change together, exchanging one thing for another, like exchanging home for office daily.
把"commute"想成"com-（一起）+ mute（改变）"——一起改变，用一样东西换另一样，就像每天在家和办公室之间来回切换。
