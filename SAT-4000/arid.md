# 🏜️ arid

🔊 /ˈærɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely dry, having little or no rainfall; lacking interest or excitement; barren
**中文：** 干旱的；不毛的；无趣的；枯燥乏味的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- parched（干燥的）
- barren（贫瘠的）
- desolate（荒凉的）
- dull（枯燥的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arid climate / arid landscape / arid region / arid discussion**

👉 Example: The arid terrain of the Sahara Desert stretches across vast portions of northern Africa.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Farmers in **arid** regions have developed innovative irrigation techniques to sustain crops despite the persistent lack of rainfall.
   **干旱**地区的农民开发了创新的灌溉技术，以在持续缺雨的情况下维持作物生长。

2. The professor's **arid** lecture on statistical methodology failed to engage even the most dedicated students in the auditorium.
   教授关于统计方法论的**枯燥**讲座甚至未能吸引礼堂中最专注的学生。

3. Climate scientists warn that previously temperate zones may become increasingly **arid** as global temperatures continue to rise.
   气候科学家警告说，随着全球气温持续上升，以前的温带地区可能会变得越来越**干旱**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arid" as "a rid" — the land has gotten "rid" of all its water, leaving it completely dry and barren.
把"arid"想成"a rid"——土地已经"去除"了所有的水分，变得完全干燥和贫瘠。
