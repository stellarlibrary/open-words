# 🏳️ concede

🔊 /kənˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To admit that something is true or valid after first resisting; to surrender or yield a right or privilege; to acknowledge defeat
**中文：** vt. 承认；承认失败；允许；退让；给予

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- admit（承认）
- acknowledge（认可）
- yield（让步）
- grant（准许）
- surrender（放弃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concede defeat / concede a point / concede that / concede the election**

👉 Example: After the final votes were counted, the candidate graciously conceded the election to her opponent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator was forced to **concede** that the proposed legislation lacked sufficient support to pass through the chamber.
   这位参议员被迫承认，拟议的立法缺乏足够的支持来通过议院。

2. After reviewing the compelling evidence, the defense attorney had to **concede** several critical points to the prosecution.
   在审查了令人信服的证据后，辩护律师不得不在几个关键点上向控方让步。

3. The champion refused to **concede** defeat even when trailing significantly in the final round of the competition.
   即使在比赛最后一轮大幅落后时，这位冠军仍拒绝认输。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-CEDE" where "cede" means to give way (like in "secede"). To concede is to give way and admit the truth.
把"concede"拆成"con + cede（让出）"——"cede"意为让步（如"secede"退出），所以concede就是承认并让步。
