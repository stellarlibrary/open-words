# 💛 compassionate

🔊 /kəmˈpæʃənət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing deep sympathy and concern for the suffering of others; merciful. (v.) To feel or show compassion for
**中文：** adj. 慈悲的；富于同情心的 v. 同情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sympathetic（同情的）
- empathetic（有同理心的）
- merciful（仁慈的）
- benevolent（慈善的）
- tender-hearted（心软的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **compassionate care / compassionate leave / compassionate toward / compassionate response**

👉 Example: The nurse provided compassionate care to every patient regardless of their background.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's **compassionate** approach to refugee resettlement has earned widespread recognition from international humanitarian agencies.
   该组织对难民安置的富有同情心的方式赢得了国际人道主义机构的广泛认可。

2. A truly **compassionate** leader considers the emotional well-being of team members alongside productivity and institutional goals.
   一位真正富有同情心的领导者会在考虑生产力和机构目标的同时关注团队成员的情感健康。

3. The court granted **compassionate** release to the elderly inmate after medical reports confirmed his rapidly deteriorating health condition.
   在医疗报告确认这位年迈囚犯的健康状况急剧恶化后，法院批准了人道主义释放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compassionate" as "com- (with) + passion (suffering) + ate" — to suffer with someone, feeling their pain as if it were your own.
把"compassionate"想成"com-（与…一起）+ passion（受苦）+ ate"——与他人一同受苦，感受他人的痛苦如同自己的一样。
