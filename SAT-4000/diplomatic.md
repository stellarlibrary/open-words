# 🕊️ diplomatic

🔊 /ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to diplomacy or international relations; tactful and skilled in dealing with sensitive matters
**中文：** adj. 外交的；老练的，圆滑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tactful（圆滑的）
- politic（策略的）
- discreet（谨慎的）
- judicious（明智的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diplomatic relations / diplomatic immunity / diplomatic approach / diplomatic corps**

👉 Example: She gave a diplomatic response to avoid escalating the tense situation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two nations restored **diplomatic** relations after nearly a decade of mutual hostility and economic sanctions.
   两国在近十年的相互敌对和经济制裁之后恢复了外交关系。

2. His **diplomatic** handling of the boardroom disagreement prevented a costly breakdown in the merger negotiations.
   他对董事会分歧的老练处理避免了合并谈判中代价高昂的破裂。

3. The professor offered a **diplomatic** critique of the student's thesis, balancing honest feedback with genuine encouragement.
   教授对学生的论文进行了委婉的评价，在诚实反馈与真诚鼓励之间取得了平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diplomatic" as "diploma + tic" — someone with a "diploma" in tact, whose every "tic" (habit) is smooth and careful.
把"diplomatic"想成"外交文凭(diploma)+习惯(tic)"——一个在圆滑方面拿了文凭的人，每个习惯都很稳妥周到。
