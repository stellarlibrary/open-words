# 🧱 insulator

🔊 /ˈɪnsjʊleɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A material or device that does not readily conduct heat, electricity, or sound; a person who installs insulation
**中文：** n. 绝缘体；从事绝缘工作的工人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nonconductor（非导体）
- barrier（屏障）
- shield（隔离物）
- separator（隔离器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **electrical insulator / thermal insulator / good insulator / act as an insulator**

👉 Example: Rubber is widely used as an insulator in electrical applications because it resists current flow.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Glass is an excellent **insulator** that prevents the transfer of electrical current in high-voltage transmission systems.
   玻璃是一种优良的绝缘体，可防止高压输电系统中电流的传递。

2. The research team discovered that the new polymer functions as a superior thermal **insulator** compared to traditional materials.
   研究团队发现这种新型聚合物与传统材料相比，是一种更优越的隔热绝缘体。

3. Air trapped between layers of fabric serves as an effective **insulator** against harsh winter temperatures.
   织物层之间的空气可以作为抵御严冬低温的有效绝缘体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insulator" as "insulate + or" — the "-or" suffix means "one who does," so an insulator is the thing that insulates, blocking heat or electricity.
把"insulator"想成"insulate（隔离）+ or（者）"——后缀"-or"表示"做某事的东西"，所以insulator就是进行隔离的物体，阻挡热量或电流。
