# 🔮 psychic

🔊 /ˈsaɪkɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the mind or soul; involving abilities that cannot be explained by natural laws, such as telepathy or clairvoyance. (n.) A person who claims to have supernatural mental powers
**中文：** adj. 精神的；心灵的；灵魂的；超自然的 n. 灵媒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clairvoyant（有洞察力的）
- telepathic（心灵感应的）
- supernatural（超自然的）
- medium（灵媒）
- extrasensory（超感觉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **psychic powers / psychic ability / psychic phenomenon / psychic energy / psychic medium**

👉 Example: Scientists remain skeptical of claims that certain individuals possess genuine psychic abilities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher investigated alleged **psychic** phenomena to determine whether any credible scientific evidence supported such extraordinary claims.
   研究人员调查了所谓的超自然现象，以确定是否有可信的科学证据支持这些非凡的说法。

2. Many cultures throughout history have revered individuals believed to possess **psychic** powers capable of predicting future events.
   纵观历史，许多文化都崇敬那些被认为拥有能够预测未来事件的超自然力量的人。

3. The therapist explored the **psychic** dimensions of the patient's trauma, focusing on deep emotional and subconscious patterns.
   治疗师探索了患者创伤的心灵层面，重点关注深层的情感和潜意识模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "psychic" as "psyche + ic" — from the Greek "psyche" meaning mind or soul. A psychic person taps into the unseen realm of the mind.
把"psychic"想成"psyche + ic"——来自希腊语"psyche"，意为心灵或灵魂。一个有超能力的人能触及心灵的无形领域。
