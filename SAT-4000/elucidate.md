# 💡 elucidate

🔊 /ɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make something clear; to explain or clarify a complex idea or statement
**中文：** v. 阐明；说明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clarify（阐明）
- illuminate（阐释）
- explicate（解释）
- expound（详述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elucidate a point / elucidate the meaning / elucidate the matter / elucidate on a topic**

👉 Example: The professor took great care to elucidate the theory for her students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher published a detailed paper to **elucidate** the underlying mechanisms of the newly discovered chemical reaction.
   研究人员发表了一篇详细论文，以阐明新发现的化学反应的潜在机制。

2. Could you please **elucidate** your position on the proposed amendment so that the committee can reach a decision?
   您能否阐明您对拟议修正案的立场，以便委员会做出决定？

3. The documentary aims to **elucidate** the complex historical factors that contributed to the collapse of the ancient empire.
   这部纪录片旨在阐明导致古代帝国崩溃的复杂历史因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-LUCID-ate" — it contains "lucid," meaning clear. To elucidate is to make something lucid and clear.
把"elucidate"想成"e-LUCID-ate"——其中包含"lucid"（清晰的）。阐明就是让事情变得清晰明了。
