# 🩸 lacerate

🔊 /ˈlæsəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To tear or cut roughly; to cause deep emotional pain or distress; to wound or mangle
**中文：** v. 割裂；撕碎；伤害，使…痛心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tear（撕裂）
- gash（割伤）
- rip（撕开）
- wound（伤害）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lacerate the skin / lacerate one's feelings / lacerated wound / deeply lacerated**

👉 Example: The sharp debris from the explosion lacerated the surface of the protective equipment beyond repair.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The broken glass severely **lacerated** the worker's hands, requiring immediate medical attention and multiple sutures.
   碎玻璃严重割伤了工人的双手，需要立即接受医疗救治和多处缝合。

2. The critic's harsh and personal review **lacerated** the young author's confidence, leaving her reluctant to publish again.
   评论家尖刻而带有人身攻击的评论伤害了年轻作家的信心，使她不愿再次出版。

3. Forensic analysis revealed that the victim's clothing had been **lacerated** by a sharp instrument consistent with industrial machinery.
   法医分析显示，受害者的衣物被一种与工业机械一致的锋利器具所割裂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lacerate" as containing "lace" — imagine lace fabric being torn apart, ripped and shredded into pieces.
把"lacerate"想成包含"lace"（蕾丝）——想象蕾丝布料被撕裂、扯碎成碎片。
