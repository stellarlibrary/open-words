# 🔊 cacophonous

🔊 /kəˈkɒfənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a harsh, discordant, unpleasant mixture of sounds; jarring to the ear
**中文：** adj. 发音不和谐的；刺耳的；粗腔横调的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- discordant（不和谐的）
- jarring（刺耳的）
- strident（尖锐的）
- dissonant（不协调的）
- raucous（沙哑刺耳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cacophonous noise / cacophonous sounds / cacophonous mixture / cacophonous chorus**

👉 Example: The cacophonous blare of car horns made it nearly impossible to concentrate in the downtown office.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **cacophonous** din of construction machinery outside the library disrupted the concentration of every student preparing for examinations.
   图书馆外建筑机械发出的刺耳噪音打断了每位备考学生的注意力。

2. What began as a harmonious rehearsal quickly devolved into a **cacophonous** clash of instruments playing entirely different melodies.
   原本和谐的排练很快变成了各种乐器演奏完全不同旋律的刺耳混乱。

3. The **cacophonous** protests echoing through the capital's streets signaled deep public dissatisfaction with the proposed economic reforms.
   回荡在首都街道上的嘈杂抗议声表明公众对拟议经济改革的深切不满。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "caco-phonous" — "caco" means bad (as in Greek "kakos") and "phon" means sound. Cacophonous literally means "bad-sounding."
想想"caco-phonous"——"caco"意为坏的（如希腊语kakos），"phon"意为声音。Cacophonous字面意思就是"听起来很糟的"。
