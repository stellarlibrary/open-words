# 🏘️ abut

🔊 /əˈbʌt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To border on; to be adjacent to; to share a common boundary with
**中文：** v. 邻接；毗邻；紧靠

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adjoin（毗邻）
- border（接壤）
- neighbor（邻近）
- touch（接触）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abut the property / abut the river / abut each other / abut against / abutting land**

👉 Example: The new shopping center abuts the residential neighborhood, raising concerns among local homeowners.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proposed development site **abuts** a protected wetland, prompting environmental groups to file formal objections.
   拟议的开发地块毗邻一片受保护的湿地，促使环保团体提交了正式反对意见。

2. Their property **abuts** the national park boundary, offering spectacular views of undisturbed wilderness every morning.
   他们的房产紧靠国家公园边界，每天早上都能欣赏到未受干扰的荒野壮景。

3. The two countries' territories **abut** along a mountainous frontier that has been disputed for generations.
   两国领土沿一条山脉边界毗邻，该边界已被争议了数代人之久。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abut" as "a BUTT" — two things that BUTT up against each other, sitting side by side with no gap.
把"abut"想成"a BUTT"——两个东西像臀部(butt)一样紧紧挨在一起，中间没有间隙。
