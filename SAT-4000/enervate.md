# 😩 enervate

🔊 /ˈenərveɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To weaken or drain of energy, vitality, or strength. (adj.) Lacking in energy or vitality; weakened
**中文：** v. 使衰弱；使无力 adj. 衰弱的；无力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- debilitate（使衰弱）
- weaken（削弱）
- exhaust（使精疲力竭）
- sap（消耗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enervate the body / enervate the spirit / enervated by heat / enervating effect**

👉 Example: The tropical humidity can enervate even the most resilient travelers within a few hours.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged illness gradually **enervated** the once-vigorous athlete, leaving him unable to complete even basic physical exercises.
   长期的疾病逐渐使这位曾经精力充沛的运动员变得虚弱，甚至无法完成基本的体育锻炼。

2. Critics argue that excessive bureaucracy can **enervate** institutional progress and stifle the creative potential of dedicated professionals.
   批评者认为过度的官僚主义会削弱机构的进步，并扼杀敬业专业人士的创造潜力。

3. The **enervating** effects of the extreme heat forced organizers to postpone the outdoor ceremony until cooler evening temperatures arrived.
   极端高温的消耗性影响迫使组织者将户外仪式推迟到较凉爽的傍晚时分。

把"enervate"想成"e-NERVE-ate"——它夺走了你的神经和精力，让你变得虚弱无力。
