# 😤 effrontery

🔊 /ɪˈfrʌntəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Insolent or impertinent behavior; shameless boldness or audacity
**中文：** n. 厚颜无耻；放肆；无礼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- audacity（大胆）
- impudence（无礼）
- insolence（傲慢）
- nerve（厚脸皮）
- gall（厚颜无耻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have the effrontery to / sheer effrontery / unbelievable effrontery / with brazen effrontery**

👉 Example: He had the effrontery to demand a raise after being caught falsifying his expense reports.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator had the **effrontery** to deny all allegations despite overwhelming evidence presented by the investigative committee.
   尽管调查委员会出示了压倒性的证据，这位参议员仍厚颜无耻地否认所有指控。

2. Guests were astonished by her sheer **effrontery** in arriving uninvited and then criticizing the quality of the host's hospitality.
   客人们对她不请自来然后批评主人招待质量的厚颜无耻感到震惊。

3. The defendant displayed remarkable **effrontery** by accusing the victims of fabricating their testimony throughout the lengthy and contentious trial.
   被告在漫长而激烈的审判中厚颜无耻地指控受害者捏造证词。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-front-ery" as "in your front (face)" — someone with effrontery confronts you shamelessly, right to your face.
把"effrontery"想成"in your front（当面）"——有effrontery的人厚颜无耻地当面冒犯你。
