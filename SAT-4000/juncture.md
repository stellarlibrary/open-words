# 🔗 juncture

🔊 /ˈdʒʌŋktʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A particular point in time, especially a critical one; a place where things join; the act or instance of joining
**中文：** n. 接缝；连接；接合；交界处；接合点；关键时刻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- moment（时刻）
- crossroads（关键时刻）
- turning point（转折点）
- conjunction（连接）
- seam（接缝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **at this juncture / critical juncture / important juncture / at the juncture of**

👉 Example: At this critical juncture, the board must decide whether to proceed with the merger or withdraw entirely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. At this pivotal **juncture** in the negotiations, any misstep by either delegation could permanently derail the peace process.
   在谈判的这个关键时刻，任何一方代表团的失误都可能永久性地破坏和平进程。

2. The historian argued that this particular **juncture** in colonial history marked the irreversible decline of imperial authority overseas.
   历史学家认为，殖民历史中的这一特定时刻标志着海外帝国权威不可逆转的衰落。

3. Engineers reinforced the **juncture** where the old bridge met the newly constructed extension to ensure structural safety.
   工程师们加固了旧桥与新建延伸段的接合处，以确保结构安全。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "juncture" as "junct (join) + ure" — the point where things join, whether physical seams or critical moments where paths converge.
把"juncture"想成"junct（连接）+ ure"——事物连接的点，无论是物理接缝还是路径汇聚的关键时刻。
