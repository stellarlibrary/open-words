# 🌐 encompass

🔊 /ɪnˈkʌmpəs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To surround or enclose completely; to include comprehensively within a scope or range
**中文：** v. 包含；包围

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- include（包括）
- comprise（包含）
- embrace（涵盖）
- surround（包围）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **encompass a wide range / encompass all aspects / encompass the entire / broadly encompass**

👉 Example: The new curriculum is designed to encompass a wide range of interdisciplinary subjects and practical skills.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The national park **encompasses** over three million acres of pristine wilderness, wetlands, and protected coastal habitats.
   该国家公园包含超过三百万英亩的原始荒野、湿地和受保护的沿海栖息地。

2. Her doctoral research **encompasses** multiple disciplines, drawing from psychology, neuroscience, and computational linguistics in equal measure.
   她的博士研究涵盖多个学科，同等程度地借鉴了心理学、神经科学和计算语言学。

3. The proposed legislation would **encompass** all digital transactions conducted by financial institutions operating within the jurisdiction.
   拟议的立法将包含管辖范围内金融机构进行的所有数字交易。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-compass" as drawing a circle with a "compass" around everything — a compass encompasses all directions within its arc.
把"encompass"想成用"compass（圆规）"画一个圈把所有东西圈进去——圆规在其弧线内涵盖所有方向。
