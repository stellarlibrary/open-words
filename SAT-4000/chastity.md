# 🕊️ chastity

🔊 /ˈtʃæstɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state or quality of being pure, virtuous, or abstaining from sexual activity; simplicity of style or expression
**中文：** n. 贞洁；纯洁；简洁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- purity（纯洁）
- virtue（美德）
- modesty（端庄）
- celibacy（禁欲）
- innocence（纯真）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vow of chastity / chastity of style / chastity and virtue / preserve chastity**

👉 Example: The monks took a strict vow of chastity upon entering the monastery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medieval literature frequently glorified **chastity** as the highest moral virtue a woman could possess in society.
   中世纪文学经常将贞洁颂扬为女性在社会中所能拥有的最高道德品质。

2. The architectural critic praised the **chastity** of the design, noting its elegant restraint and lack of ornamentation.
   建筑评论家赞扬了设计的简洁，指出其优雅的克制和没有过多装饰。

3. Religious orders throughout history have required vows of **chastity** as a fundamental condition of spiritual devotion.
   历史上各个宗教团体都要求将贞洁誓言作为精神奉献的基本条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chaste + ity" — "chaste" means pure, and "-ity" makes it a noun, so chastity is the state of being pure.
把"chastity"拆成"chaste（纯洁的）+ ity（名词后缀）"——纯洁的状态，就是贞洁、纯洁。
