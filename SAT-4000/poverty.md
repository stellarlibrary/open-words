# 😔 poverty

🔊 /ˈpɑːvərti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being extremely poor; the lack or scarcity of something; inferior quality or insufficiency
**中文：** n. 贫困；困难；缺少；低劣；贫瘠

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destitution（赤贫）
- deprivation（匮乏）
- indigence（贫穷）
- scarcity（缺乏）
- penury（贫困）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **poverty line / poverty rate / poverty alleviation / extreme poverty / poverty of imagination**

👉 Example: The government launched a comprehensive program aimed at reducing poverty in rural communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The United Nations report revealed that nearly one billion people worldwide continue to live in extreme **poverty** and deprivation.
   联合国报告显示，全球近十亿人继续生活在极端贫困和匮乏之中。

2. Critics argued that the novel suffered from a **poverty** of imagination, relying on predictable plot devices throughout its narrative.
   评论家认为这部小说想象力贫乏，在整个叙事中依赖于可预见的情节手法。

3. The philanthropic foundation dedicated its resources to combating **poverty** through sustainable education and vocational training initiatives.
   这个慈善基金会致力于通过可持续的教育和职业培训项目来消除贫困。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "poverty" as "pover-ty" — the root "pover" comes from Latin "pauper" meaning poor, so poverty is the state of being poor.
把"poverty"想成词根"pover"来自拉丁语"pauper（穷人）"——poverty就是贫穷的状态。
