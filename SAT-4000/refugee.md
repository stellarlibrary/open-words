# 🏃 refugee

🔊 /ˌrefjʊˈdʒiː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who has been forced to leave their country in order to escape war, persecution, or natural disaster.
**中文：** n. 难民；避难者；流亡者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exile（流亡者）
- displaced person（流离失所者）
- asylum seeker（寻求庇护者）
- emigrant（移民者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **refugee camp / refugee crisis / refugee status / political refugee**

👉 Example: The international community must work together to address the growing refugee crisis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Thousands of **refugee** families were relocated to temporary shelters after the devastating conflict destroyed their homeland.
   数千个难民家庭在毁灭性的冲突摧毁了他们的家园后被安置到临时避难所。

2. The organization provides legal assistance to help each **refugee** obtain official asylum status in the host country.
   该组织提供法律援助，帮助每位难民在东道国获得正式的庇护身份。

3. As a political **refugee**, she spent years advocating for human rights before finally being granted permanent residency.
   作为一名政治难民，她多年来一直倡导人权，最终获得了永久居留权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "refugee" as "refuge + ee" — a person (the -ee suffix) who seeks refuge or safety from danger.
把"refugee"想成"refuge + ee"——一个寻求避难或安全的人（-ee后缀表示人）。
