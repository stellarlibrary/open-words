# 🏆 kudos

🔊 /ˈkjuːdɒs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Praise and honor received for an achievement; glory or fame resulting from notable accomplishments
**中文：** n. 荣誉；名望；称赞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acclaim（赞誉）
- prestige（声望）
- recognition（认可）
- commendation（表彰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **earn kudos / receive kudos / give kudos to / kudos for**

👉 Example: The research team received well-deserved kudos for their groundbreaking discovery in quantum physics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young architect earned considerable **kudos** from the design community for her innovative and sustainable building concepts.
   这位年轻的建筑师因其创新和可持续的建筑理念赢得了设计界的高度赞誉。

2. The university bestowed **kudos** upon the retiring professor who had dedicated four decades to advancing scientific research.
   大学向这位致力于推动科学研究四十年的退休教授致以崇高的敬意。

3. International **kudos** followed the humanitarian organization after it successfully coordinated relief efforts across multiple disaster zones.
   在该人道主义组织成功协调了多个灾区的救援工作后，国际赞誉接踵而至。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "kudos" as coming from Greek "kydos" meaning glory — when someone says "kudos to you," they are giving you glory and praise.
把"kudos"想成源自希腊语"kydos"（荣耀）——当有人说"kudos to you"时，他们在给予你荣耀和赞美。
