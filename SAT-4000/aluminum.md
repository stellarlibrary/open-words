# 🔩 aluminum

🔊 /əˈluːmɪnəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A lightweight, silvery-white metallic element that is highly resistant to corrosion and widely used in industry
**中文：** n. 铝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aluminium（铝，英式拼写）
- Al（铝的化学符号）
- light metal（轻金属）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aluminum foil / aluminum alloy / aluminum can / recycled aluminum**

👉 Example: The aerospace industry relies heavily on aluminum alloys for constructing lightweight aircraft frames.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers selected **aluminum** as the primary material for the bridge structure due to its exceptional strength-to-weight ratio.
   工程师选择铝作为桥梁结构的主要材料，因为它具有优异的强度重量比。

2. The global demand for recycled **aluminum** has increased significantly as industries seek more sustainable manufacturing practices.
   随着各行业寻求更可持续的制造方式，全球对回收铝的需求显著增加。

3. Researchers developed a new **aluminum** composite that withstands extreme temperatures encountered during atmospheric reentry of spacecraft.
   研究人员开发了一种新型铝复合材料，能够承受航天器重返大气层时遇到的极端温度。
