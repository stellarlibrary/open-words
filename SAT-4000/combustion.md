# 🔥 combustion

🔊 /kəmˈbʌs.tʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of burning something, especially a chemical reaction in which a substance combines with oxygen to produce heat and light
**中文：** n. 燃烧；氧化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- burning（燃烧）
- ignition（点燃）
- incineration（焚烧）
- oxidation（氧化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **internal combustion / spontaneous combustion / combustion engine / combustion process**

👉 Example: The internal combustion engine revolutionized transportation in the twentieth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **combustion** of fossil fuels releases significant quantities of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere each year.
   化石燃料的燃烧每年向大气中释放大量的二氧化碳。

2. Scientists studied the rate of **combustion** to determine which materials posed the greatest fire hazard in buildings.
   科学家研究了燃烧速率，以确定哪些材料在建筑物中构成最大的火灾隐患。

3. Spontaneous **combustion** of improperly stored chemicals led to the devastating warehouse explosion last autumn.
   储存不当的化学品自燃导致了去年秋天毁灭性的仓库爆炸。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-BUST-ion" — something that "busts" into flames is undergoing combustion.
把"combustion"想成"com-BUST-ion"——某物"爆裂(bust)"成火焰，就是在经历燃烧。
