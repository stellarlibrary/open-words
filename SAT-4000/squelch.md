# 🤐 squelch

🔊 /skwɛltʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To suppress forcefully; to crush or silence completely. (n.) A squelching sound; a crushing retort or rebuke
**中文：** v. 压制；消除；镇压 n. 噪声控制；嘎吱声；压倒对方的反驳

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- suppress（压制）
- quash（镇压）
- stifle（扼杀）
- silence（使沉默）
- crush（压碎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **squelch a rumor / squelch dissent / squelch opposition / squelch a rebellion**

👉 Example: The authorities moved swiftly to squelch any form of organized dissent among the populace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The principal sought to **squelch** the unfounded rumors circulating among students before they caused further disruption.
   校长试图在不实谣言在学生中进一步传播造成更大干扰之前将其压制下去。

2. The regime employed censorship and intimidation to **squelch** all political opposition within the country.
   该政权利用审查和恐吓手段来镇压国内所有的政治反对力量。

3. Her sharp and well-reasoned rebuttal was enough to **squelch** the critic's objections during the debate.
   她犀利且条理清晰的反驳足以在辩论中压制住批评者的异议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "squelch" as the sound of stepping on something wet and crushing it flat — just as you crush something underfoot, you squelch opposition or rumors.
把"squelch"想成踩在湿东西上将其压扁的声音——就像你把东西踩在脚下碾碎一样，你压制反对意见或谣言。
