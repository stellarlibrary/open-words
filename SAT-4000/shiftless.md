# 😴 shiftless

🔊 /ˈʃɪftləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking ambition or motivation; lazy and inefficient; lacking resourcefulness
**中文：** adj. 无能的；不思上进的；偷懒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indolent（懒惰的）
- idle（闲散的）
- lackadaisical（无精打采的）
- unambitious（无雄心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shiftless individual / shiftless behavior / shiftless attitude / shiftless existence**

👉 Example: The manager dismissed the intern as shiftless after observing weeks of consistently poor performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial condemned the **shiftless** attitude of politicians who failed to address the worsening infrastructure crisis.
   这篇社论谴责了那些未能解决日益恶化的基础设施危机的政客们不思进取的态度。

2. Despite his considerable intelligence, he led a **shiftless** existence, never committing to any profession or long-term pursuit.
   尽管他相当聪明，却过着无所作为的生活，从不投身于任何职业或长期追求。

3. The community program aimed to transform **shiftless** youth into motivated citizens through mentorship and vocational training.
   该社区项目旨在通过指导和职业培训将懒散的年轻人转变为积极上进的公民。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shift-less" — someone without any "shift" or drive, just sitting still with no motivation to move forward.
把"shiftless"想成"没有shift（转变、行动）的人"——一个没有任何动力推动自己前进的人。
