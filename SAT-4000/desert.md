# 🏜️ desert

🔊 /ˈdɛzərt (n.) dɪˈzɜːrt (v.)/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A barren, arid landscape with little rainfall or vegetation. (v.) To abandon or leave without permission; to forsake
**中文：** n. 沙漠；荒漠 v. 遗弃；放弃；丢弃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abandon（遗弃）
- forsake（抛弃）
- wasteland（荒地）
- wilderness（荒野）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **desert a cause / desert one's post / desert island / vast desert**

👉 Example: The soldier was court-martialed for attempting to desert his post during the conflict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Several key supporters chose to **desert** the political coalition when its leadership adopted increasingly extreme and divisive policy positions.
   几位关键支持者在联盟领导层采取越来越极端和分裂的政策立场时选择放弃该政治联盟。

2. The expanding **desert** threatens to engulf fertile agricultural land, displacing thousands of families who depend on subsistence farming.
   不断扩张的沙漠威胁着吞没肥沃的农业用地，迫使数千依靠自给农业的家庭流离失所。

3. When funding was abruptly withdrawn, many collaborators **deserted** the research initiative, leaving only a small dedicated team behind.
   当资金突然撤回时，许多合作者放弃了该研究项目，只留下了一个小型的忠诚团队。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "desert" — a desert is a place deserted by water and life; to desert means to leave something empty and abandoned.
把"desert"想成——沙漠是被水和生命遗弃的地方；desert作动词意味着让某物变得空旷和被遗弃。
