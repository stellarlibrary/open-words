# 🧬 reproductive system

🔊 /ˌriːprəˈdʌktɪv ˈsɪstəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun phrase | 名词短语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The system of organs and structures in an organism that functions in reproduction; the biological mechanism by which organisms produce offspring
**中文：** 生殖系统；繁殖方式

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genital system（生殖系统）
- reproductive organs（生殖器官）
- breeding system（繁殖系统）
- procreative system（生育系统）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **male/female reproductive system / reproductive system disorders / reproductive system development**

👉 Example: The human reproductive system undergoes significant changes during puberty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biology textbook provides a detailed overview of the human **reproductive system** and its primary physiological functions.
   这本生物教科书详细介绍了人体生殖系统及其主要生理功能。

2. Environmental pollutants have been shown to adversely affect the **reproductive system** of many aquatic vertebrate species.
   研究表明，环境污染物会对许多水生脊椎动物的生殖系统产生不利影响。

3. Comparative anatomy reveals that the **reproductive system** of marsupials differs fundamentally from that of placental mammals.
   比较解剖学揭示了有袋类动物的生殖系统与胎盘类哺乳动物有根本性的不同。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reproductive system" as "re-produce system" — a system designed to produce again, creating new life from existing organisms.
把"reproductive system"想成"再生产的系统"——一个旨在重新生产的系统，从现有生物体中创造新生命。
