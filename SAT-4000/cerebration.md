# 🧠 cerebration

🔊 /ˌserəˈbreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act or process of thinking; mental activity or intellectual exercise
**中文：** n. 思考；用脑；精神活动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cogitation（深思）
- contemplation（沉思）
- deliberation（思虑）
- reflection（反思）
- rumination（反复思考）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intense cerebration / prolonged cerebration / cerebration process / unconscious cerebration**

👉 Example: The complex mathematical proof required hours of intense cerebration before a solution emerged.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After prolonged **cerebration**, the philosopher arrived at a groundbreaking theory that reconciled two seemingly contradictory schools of thought.
   经过长时间的深思，这位哲学家提出了一个开创性理论，调和了两个看似矛盾的学派。

2. The committee's collective **cerebration** over several weeks ultimately produced a comprehensive strategy for institutional reform.
   委员会经过数周的集体思考，最终制定了一套全面的制度改革战略。

3. Scientists have long debated whether unconscious **cerebration** contributes meaningfully to creative breakthroughs and artistic innovation.
   科学家们长期以来一直争论无意识的思维活动是否对创造性突破和艺术创新有实质性贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cerebration" as "cerebr- (brain) + -ation (action)" — it is literally the action of the brain, meaning the process of thinking.
把"cerebration"想成"cerebr-（脑）+ -ation（行为）"——字面意思是大脑的行为，即思考的过程。
