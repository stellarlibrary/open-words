# 🔷 congruent

🔊 /ˈkɒŋɡruənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In agreement or harmony; corresponding in character or form; (in geometry) having the same shape and size
**中文：** adj. 适合的，一致的；（几何）全等的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consistent（一致的）
- compatible（相容的）
- harmonious（和谐的）
- corresponding（相应的）
- identical（相同的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **congruent with / congruent triangles / congruent values / congruent to**

👉 Example: The candidate's stated beliefs must be congruent with her actions to maintain public trust.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher ensured that the experimental results were **congruent** with the theoretical predictions outlined in the original hypothesis.
   研究者确保实验结果与原始假设中提出的理论预测一致。

2. In geometry, two triangles are considered **congruent** when all corresponding sides and angles are exactly equal in measurement.
   在几何学中，当所有对应的边和角度完全相等时，两个三角形被视为全等的。

3. Effective leadership requires that one's actions remain **congruent** with the core values and principles one publicly advocates.
   有效的领导力要求一个人的行为与其公开倡导的核心价值观和原则保持一致。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-GRU-ent" — "con" means together and "gru" sounds like "glue," so congruent things are glued together in perfect alignment.
把"congruent"想成"con-GRU-ent"——"con"意为在一起，"gru"听起来像"glue"（胶水），所以congruent的东西像被胶水粘在一起般完美吻合。
