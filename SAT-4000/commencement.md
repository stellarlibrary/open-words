# 🎓 commencement

🔊 /kəˈmens.mənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The beginning or start of something; a ceremony at which academic degrees or diplomas are conferred
**中文：** n. 开始；毕业典礼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beginning（开始）
- onset（开端）
- inception（开始）
- graduation（毕业典礼）
- inauguration（开幕）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commencement ceremony / commencement address / commencement of operations / commencement day**

👉 Example: The renowned author delivered an inspiring commencement address to the graduating class.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **commencement** of construction on the new research facility is scheduled for the first quarter of next year.
   新研究设施的开工建设计划于明年第一季度开始。

2. Thousands of proud families attended the university **commencement** to watch their children receive their diplomas on stage.
   数千个自豪的家庭参加了大学毕业典礼，观看他们的孩子在台上领取毕业证书。

3. Prior to the **commencement** of the trial, both attorneys submitted their preliminary motions to the presiding judge.
   在审判开始之前，双方律师向主审法官提交了他们的初步动议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "commence-ment" — "commence" means to begin, so commencement is the beginning, and paradoxically, a graduation ceremony marks the "beginning" of a new chapter in life.
把"commencement"想成"commence-ment"——"commence"意为开始，所以commencement是开端；而毕业典礼恰恰标志着人生新篇章的"开始"。
