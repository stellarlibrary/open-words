# 🔄 incessant

🔊 /ɪnˈsɛsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Continuing without pause or interruption; never-ending and relentless
**中文：** adj. 不断的；不停的；连续的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unceasing（不间断的）
- relentless（不停的）
- perpetual（永久的）
- constant（持续的）
- unremitting（不懈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incessant rain / incessant noise / incessant demand / incessant chatter / incessant complaints**

👉 Example: The incessant noise from the construction site made it nearly impossible to concentrate on academic work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **incessant** rainfall throughout the monsoon season caused severe flooding in several low-lying agricultural regions.
   整个季风季节不间断的降雨在几个低洼农业地区造成了严重的洪水。

2. Critics noted that the **incessant** repetition of political slogans undermined the credibility of the candidate's substantive arguments.
   评论人士指出，政治口号的不断重复削弱了候选人实质性论点的可信度。

3. The patient's **incessant** coughing prompted the physician to order a comprehensive series of diagnostic pulmonary examinations.
   患者持续不断的咳嗽促使医生下令进行一系列全面的肺部诊断检查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CEASE-ant" — "cease" means to stop; with "in" (not), it means something that does not cease, it never stops.
把"incessant"想成"in（不）-CEASE（停止）-ant"——"cease"意为停止；加上"in"（不），就是不停止的，永不停歇的。
