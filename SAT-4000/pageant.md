# 🎭 pageant

🔊 /ˈpædʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An elaborate public spectacle, ceremony, or parade; a contest in which participants are judged on appearance and talent; a showy display or pretense.
**中文：** n. 盛会；庆典；游行；露天表演；虚饰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spectacle（壮观场面）
- ceremony（典礼）
- parade（游行）
- exhibition（展览）
- display（展示）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beauty pageant / Christmas pageant / historical pageant / pageant of history**

👉 Example: The annual beauty pageant attracted contestants from all fifty states to compete for the national title.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The town organized a magnificent historical **pageant** to commemorate the three hundredth anniversary of its founding by early settlers.
   小镇组织了一场盛大的历史**庆典**，以纪念早期定居者建镇三百周年。

2. Thousands of spectators lined the streets to witness the elaborate **pageant** celebrating the nation's independence with colorful floats and music.
   数千名观众沿街观看这场精心策划的**盛会**，用彩车和音乐庆祝国家的独立。

3. Critics argued that the election had become nothing more than a political **pageant** devoid of substantive debate on important policy issues.
   批评者认为，选举已变成一场缺乏重要政策问题实质性辩论的政治**表演**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "page-ant" as a "page" (a young attendant) performing "antics" at a grand royal event — a pageant is a grand public show.
把"pageant"想成"page"（侍从）在一场盛大的皇家活动中表演滑稽动作——pageant就是一场盛大的公开表演。
