# 📅 schedule

🔊 /ˈskɛdʒuːl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A timetable; a plan of events or activities; a list or catalog. (v.) To arrange or plan for something to take place at a particular time; to make an itinerary
**中文：** n. 目录，一览表，清单；程序表，计划表，时间表，课程表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- timetable（时间表）
- agenda（议程）
- itinerary（行程表）
- plan（计划）
- calendar（日程表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on schedule / behind schedule / ahead of schedule / schedule a meeting / busy schedule**

👉 Example: The project manager worked tirelessly to keep the construction project on schedule despite unexpected delays.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university released its revised course **schedule** for the upcoming semester, incorporating several newly approved interdisciplinary programs.
   大学发布了下学期修订后的课程表，纳入了几个新批准的跨学科项目。

2. The committee decided to **schedule** the public hearing for early March to allow sufficient time for community input and review.
   委员会决定将公众听证会安排在三月初，以留出充足的时间征求社区意见和进行审查。

3. Completing the infrastructure project ahead of **schedule** saved the city millions of dollars in projected labor and material costs.
   提前完成基础设施项目为该市节省了数百万美元的预计劳动力和材料成本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "schedule" as "school + duel" — at school, your schedule is a duel against time, battling to fit every class and activity into the day.
把"schedule"想成"school + duel"——在学校里，你的时间表就是一场与时间的决斗，努力把每节课和每项活动都塞进一天里。
