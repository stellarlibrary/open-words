# 🗡️ ruthless

🔊 /ˈruːθləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing no pity or compassion for others; merciless and cruel
**中文：** adj. 残酷的，无情的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merciless（无情的）
- pitiless（冷酷的）
- relentless（不留情的）
- callous（冷漠的）
- brutal（残忍的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ruthless dictator / ruthless ambition / ruthless efficiency / ruthless in pursuit of**

👉 Example: The ruthless corporate takeover left hundreds of loyal employees without jobs or severance packages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ruthless** dictator suppressed all forms of political dissent and imprisoned journalists who criticized the regime.
   这个残酷的独裁者镇压一切形式的政治异见，并监禁批评该政权的记者。

2. Her **ruthless** determination to succeed in the competitive industry earned her both admiration and widespread resentment.
   她在竞争激烈的行业中不择手段追求成功的决心既赢得了赞赏，也招致了广泛的不满。

3. The documentary exposed the **ruthless** exploitation of migrant workers by unscrupulous employers seeking maximum profit.
   这部纪录片揭露了不法雇主为追求最大利润而对移民工人进行的残酷剥削。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ruth-less" — "ruth" is an old English word meaning "pity," so ruthless literally means "without pity."
把"ruthless"拆解为"ruth（怜悯）+less（没有）"——ruth是古英语中"怜悯"的意思，所以ruthless就是"没有怜悯的"。
