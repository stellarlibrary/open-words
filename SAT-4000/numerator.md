# 🔢 numerator

🔊 /ˈnjuːməreɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The number above the line in a fraction, showing how many parts of the whole are being considered; one who calculates
**中文：** n. 分子；计算者；计算器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dividend（被除数）
- top number（上方数字）
- counter（计数者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **numerator of a fraction / common numerator / numerator and denominator**

👉 Example: To simplify the fraction, divide both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common factor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. When adding fractions with different denominators, students must first find a common denominator before combining the **numerators**.
   在加不同分母的分数时，学生必须先找到公分母，然后再合并分子。

2. The **numerator** in the probability formula represents the number of favorable outcomes in the given sample space.
   概率公式中的分子表示给定样本空间中有利结果的数量。

3. Increasing the **numerator** while keeping the denominator constant will always result in a larger fractional value.
   在保持分母不变的情况下增大分子，分数值必然增大。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "numerator" as the "number on top" — it tells you the number of parts you are counting out of the whole.
把"numerator"想成"上面的数字"——它告诉你在整体中你正在计算的部分数量。
