# 🔢 sequence

🔊 /ˈsiːkwəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular order in which related events, movements, or things follow each other; a series of related things. (v.) To arrange in a particular order
**中文：** n. 次序，顺序，序列；数列

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- series（系列）
- succession（连续）
- order（顺序）
- progression（递进）
- chain（链）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in sequence / sequence of events / DNA sequence / logical sequence / sequence the steps**

👉 Example: Understanding the sequence of historical events is essential for grasping the causes of major conflicts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists successfully mapped the entire **sequence** of the human genome, opening new frontiers in personalized medical treatment.
   科学家成功绘制了整个人类基因组序列，为个性化医疗开辟了新领域。

2. The detective reconstructed the precise **sequence** of events leading up to the crime by analyzing surveillance footage carefully.
   侦探通过仔细分析监控录像，重建了犯罪发生前的精确事件顺序。

3. The mathematician demonstrated that the Fibonacci **sequence** appears with remarkable frequency throughout patterns found in nature.
   这位数学家证明了斐波那契数列在自然界的各种模式中以惊人的频率出现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sequence" as "sequel + ence" — just like movie sequels follow one another, a sequence is things following in order.
把"sequence"想成"sequel + ence"——就像电影续集一部接一部，sequence就是事物按顺序排列。
