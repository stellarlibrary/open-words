# 🏛️ alderman

🔊 /ˈɔːldərmən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An elected member of a municipal council or legislative body, typically representing a specific ward or district
**中文：** 市议员；市府参事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- councilman（议员）
- councilor（顾问）
- legislator（立法者）
- representative（代表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **city alderman / elected alderman / alderman of a ward / the alderman voted**
👉 Example: The alderman proposed a new ordinance to improve public transportation in his district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **alderman** presented a comprehensive plan to revitalize the deteriorating neighborhood during the city council meeting.
   这位市议员在市议会会议上提出了一项全面计划，旨在振兴日益衰败的社区。

2. Residents of the third ward urged their **alderman** to address the persistent issue of inadequate street lighting.
   第三选区的居民敦促他们的市议员解决街道照明不足这一持续存在的问题。

3. The newly elected **alderman** pledged to increase funding for public schools and community health services in her district.
   新当选的市议员承诺增加对其选区公立学校和社区卫生服务的拨款。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it into "alder-man" — from Old English "ealdor" meaning "elder" plus "man"; an alderman is an elder person who governs.
拆解为"alder-man"——源自古英语"ealdor"（长者）加"man"（人）；alderman就是治理城市的长者。
