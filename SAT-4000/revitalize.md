# 🌱 revitalize

🔊 /riːˈvaɪtəlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To imbue with new life and vitality; to restore strength, energy, or activity to something
**中文：** v. 使苏醒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rejuvenate（使恢复活力）
- reinvigorate（使重新振作）
- restore（恢复）
- regenerate（再生）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **revitalize the economy / revitalize a neighborhood / revitalize a brand / revitalize efforts**

👉 Example: The city council approved a comprehensive plan to revitalize the deteriorating downtown district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly appointed director launched an ambitious campaign to **revitalize** the museum's declining attendance and public engagement.
   新任馆长发起了一项雄心勃勃的运动，以振兴博物馆日益下降的参观率和公众参与度。

2. Economists argue that significant infrastructure investment is essential to **revitalize** rural communities suffering from prolonged economic stagnation.
   经济学家认为，大规模基础设施投资对于振兴长期经济停滞的农村社区至关重要。

3. The government introduced tax incentives designed to **revitalize** the manufacturing sector and encourage domestic production of essential goods.
   政府推出了旨在振兴制造业并鼓励国内生产必需品的税收优惠政策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-" (again) + "vital" (life) + "-ize" (make) — to make something alive and vital again.
把"revitalize"想成"re-（再次）+ vital（有活力的）+ -ize（使）"——使某事物重新焕发生机。
