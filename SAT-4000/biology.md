# 🧬 biology

🔊 /baɪˈɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The scientific study of living organisms, their structure, function, growth, and evolution; the plant and animal life of a particular region
**中文：** 生物学；（一个地区全部的）生物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- life science（生命科学）
- natural science（自然科学）
- bioscience（生物科学）
- ecology（生态学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study biology / molecular biology / marine biology / biology department / evolutionary biology**

👉 Example: Advances in molecular biology have revolutionized our understanding of genetic diseases and their potential treatments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor of **biology** published a landmark paper demonstrating the role of epigenetics in hereditary disease transmission.
   这位生物学教授发表了一篇具有里程碑意义的论文，阐述了表观遗传学在遗传性疾病传播中的作用。

2. Marine **biology** researchers discovered several previously unknown species inhabiting the deep-sea thermal vents of the Pacific Ocean.
   海洋生物学研究人员发现了几种此前未知的物种栖息在太平洋深海热泉中。

3. Understanding the **biology** of invasive species is essential for developing effective strategies to protect native ecosystems from degradation.
   了解入侵物种的生物学特性对于制定有效策略以保护本地生态系统免受退化至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bio-" (life) + "-logy" (study of) — biology is simply the study of life in all its remarkable forms.
把"biology"拆分为"bio-"（生命）+"-logy"（学科）——生物学就是研究所有生命形式的学科。
