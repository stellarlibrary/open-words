# 🚫 lewd

🔊 /luːd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Crude and offensive in a sexual way; obscene or indecent in behavior or expression
**中文：** adj. 淫荡的；猥亵的；下流的；好色的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obscene（淫秽的）
- indecent（不雅的）
- vulgar（粗俗的）
- licentious（放荡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lewd behavior / lewd conduct / lewd remarks / lewd and lascivious**

👉 Example: The employee was terminated for making lewd remarks that violated the company's code of conduct.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court determined that the publication contained **lewd** material unsuitable for distribution to the general public.
   法院认定该出版物包含不适合向公众发行的猥亵内容。

2. Several witnesses testified that the defendant had engaged in **lewd** conduct in a public venue on multiple occasions.
   数名证人作证称被告曾多次在公共场所从事猥亵行为。

3. The broadcasting authority imposed heavy fines on the network for airing **lewd** content during prime-time programming hours.
   广播管理机构因该电视网在黄金时段播放下流内容而对其处以重罚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lewd" as rhyming with "crude" — both words describe something vulgar and offensive.
把"lewd"和"crude（粗鲁的）"联系起来——两个词都描述粗俗和令人反感的事物。
