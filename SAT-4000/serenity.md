# 🕊️ serenity

🔊 /səˈrenɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being calm, peaceful, and untroubled; clearness and brightness of weather
**中文：** n. 平静

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tranquility（宁静）
- calmness（平静）
- peacefulness（安宁）
- composure（沉着）
- placidity（平和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inner serenity / a sense of serenity / serenity of mind / achieve serenity**

👉 Example: The garden was designed to evoke a profound sense of serenity and contemplation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years of meditation practice, she finally achieved a lasting **serenity** that remained unshaken by external circumstances.
   经过多年的冥想练习，她终于获得了一种持久的平静，不受外部环境的动摇。

2. The **serenity** of the mountain lake at dawn provided the perfect setting for the philosophical retreat.
   黎明时分山间湖泊的宁静为这次哲学静修提供了完美的环境。

3. His remarkable **serenity** in the face of adversity demonstrated a maturity far beyond his years.
   他面对逆境时表现出的非凡平静展现了远超其年龄的成熟。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "serenity" as "serene + ity" — the noun form of serene, the quality of being perfectly calm like a still ocean.
把"serenity"想成"serene + ity"——serene的名词形式，拥有如静海般完美平静的品质。
