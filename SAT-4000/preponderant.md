# ⚖️ preponderant

🔊 /prɪˈpɑːndərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Superior in weight, force, influence, or numbers; predominant; having greater prevalence or importance
**中文：** adj. 占优势的；突出的；压倒性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- predominant（占主导地位的）
- dominant（支配的）
- prevailing（盛行的）
- paramount（至高无上的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preponderant influence / preponderant role / preponderant share / preponderant evidence**

👉 Example: The preponderant evidence strongly favored the prosecution's interpretation of the disputed events.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Economic factors played a **preponderant** role in shaping the government's foreign policy decisions throughout the turbulent decade.
   经济因素在整个动荡的十年中对政府外交政策决策的制定起了主导作用。

2. The study revealed that one ethnic group held a **preponderant** share of political power despite comprising a demographic minority.
   研究表明，一个族群尽管在人口上属于少数，却掌握着压倒性的政治权力份额。

3. Scholars acknowledge the **preponderant** influence of classical Greek philosophy on the development of Western intellectual traditions.
   学者们承认古希腊哲学对西方知识传统发展的主导性影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-ponder-ant" as something that "ponders" or "weighs" more than everything else — it tips the scale.
把"preponderant"想成"pre（超过）+ ponder（权衡）+ ant"——在权衡中超过其他一切的，即占优势的。
