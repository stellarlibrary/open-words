# 🌍 nationality

🔊 /ˌnæʃəˈnælɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The status of belonging to a particular nation; a group of people sharing the same culture, language, or history
**中文：** n. 国籍，国家；民族；部落

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- citizenship（公民身份）
- ethnicity（民族）
- nationality status（国籍身份）
- national origin（国家出身）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hold a nationality / dual nationality / acquire nationality / nationality of**

👉 Example: Applicants must declare their nationality on the official registration form.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The census required every resident to disclose their **nationality** in order to compile accurate demographic statistics.
   人口普查要求每位居民披露其国籍，以便编制准确的人口统计数据。

2. Scholars have long debated how **nationality** influences cultural identity and individual political participation in modern democracies.
   学者们长期以来一直在讨论国籍如何影响文化认同以及个人在现代民主国家中的政治参与。

3. The treaty granted dual **nationality** to citizens residing in the border regions of both neighboring countries.
   该条约授予居住在两个邻国边境地区的公民双重国籍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "national" + "-ity" — the quality of belonging to a nation, your national identity and legal status.
把"nationality"拆成"national"加"-ity"——属于某个国家的性质，即你的国家身份和法律地位。
