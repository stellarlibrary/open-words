# ⚖️ symmetrical

🔊 /sɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or involving symmetry; evenly proportioned or balanced on both sides
**中文：** adj. 对称的，均匀的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- balanced（平衡的）
- proportional（匀称的）
- even（均匀的）
- uniform（一致的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **symmetrical design / symmetrical pattern / symmetrical shape / symmetrical arrangement**

👉 Example: The architect created a perfectly symmetrical facade that impressed every visitor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers noted that the crystal structure exhibited a remarkably **symmetrical** arrangement of atoms at the molecular level.
   研究人员注意到该晶体结构在分子层面上呈现出非常对称的原子排列。

2. Classical architecture often features **symmetrical** facades to convey a sense of order, harmony, and visual balance.
   古典建筑常以对称的外观来传达秩序感、和谐感和视觉平衡。

3. The mathematician demonstrated that the equation produced a perfectly **symmetrical** curve when graphed on the coordinate plane.
   这位数学家证明了该方程在坐标平面上绘制时会产生一条完美对称的曲线。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sym-metric-al" as "same-measure-all" — both sides have the same measurements, making them symmetrical.
把"symmetrical"想成"same-measure-all"——两边有相同的度量，所以是对称的。
