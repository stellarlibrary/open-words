# 🚶 overstride

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈstraɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To stride over or across; to surpass or exceed by stepping beyond
**中文：** v. 跨过；超过；越过

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surpass（超越）
- transcend（超越）
- straddle（跨越）
- step over（跨过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overstride a boundary / overstride obstacles / overstride limitations / overstride a gap**

👉 Example: The determined explorer overstrode every obstacle the treacherous mountain terrain presented.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious reformer sought to **overstride** the bureaucratic barriers that had long impeded meaningful progress in educational policy.
   这位雄心勃勃的改革者试图跨越长期阻碍教育政策取得实质性进展的官僚壁垒。

2. With one powerful movement, the athlete **overstrode** the widening gap between the crumbling platforms during the obstacle course challenge.
   在障碍赛中，运动员以一个有力的动作跨过了崩塌平台之间不断扩大的缺口。

3. The empire's military commanders **overstrode** the natural boundaries of rivers and mountain ranges to expand their territorial dominion.
   帝国的军事指挥官们越过河流和山脉的天然边界，扩展他们的领土统治。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-stride" — to take a "stride over" something, stepping across it with one large, decisive step.
把"overstride"想成"跨步越过"某物——用一大步果断地跨过去。
