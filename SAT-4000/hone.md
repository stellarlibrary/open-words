# 🔪 hone

🔊 /hoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A fine-grained whetstone for sharpening tools. (v.) To sharpen on a whetstone; to refine or perfect over time
**中文：** n. 磨刀石 v. 用磨刀石磨；磨练；精炼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sharpen（磨快）
- refine（精炼）
- polish（打磨）
- perfect（完善）
- whet（磨砺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hone one's skills / hone a craft / hone a blade / hone an ability**

👉 Example: She spent years honing her writing skills before publishing her first novel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young attorney worked tirelessly to **hone** her arguments before presenting the case to the appellate court.
   这位年轻律师在向上诉法院陈述案件之前不懈地打磨自己的论点。

2. Musicians must **hone** their technique through daily practice if they wish to perform at a professional level.
   音乐家必须通过每日练习来磨练技巧，才能达到专业演奏水平。

3. The blacksmith used a fine **hone** to give the blade its final razor-sharp edge before delivering it to the client.
   铁匠用一块细磨刀石为刀刃做最后的精磨，使其锋利如剃刀，然后交付给客户。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hone" sounding like "home" — you go home to sharpen and perfect your skills, just as you hone a blade to perfection.
"hone"听起来像"home"——你回家磨练和完善技能，就像你磨砺刀刃至完美一样。
