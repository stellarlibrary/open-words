# 🫁 respiration

🔊 /ˌrɛspəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of breathing; the metabolic process by which organisms exchange gases with the environment, especially the intake of oxygen and release of carbon dioxide
**中文：** n. 呼吸；呼吸作用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- breathing（呼吸）
- inhalation（吸气）
- ventilation（通气）
- exhalation（呼气）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cellular respiration / aerobic respiration / artificial respiration / rate of respiration**

👉 Example: The doctor monitored the patient's respiration carefully throughout the surgical procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Cellular **respiration** converts glucose and oxygen into energy that sustains all vital functions within living organisms.
   细胞呼吸作用将葡萄糖和氧气转化为维持生物体所有重要功能的能量。

2. The medical team administered artificial **respiration** immediately after the patient experienced sudden cardiac arrest in the ward.
   医疗团队在患者在病房突发心脏骤停后立即实施了人工呼吸。

3. Elevated levels of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere can significantly impair **respiration** in both terrestrial and aquatic species.
   大气中二氧化碳含量的升高会显著损害陆生和水生物种的呼吸作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-spir-ation" — "spir" comes from Latin "spirare" meaning "to breathe," so respiration literally means "breathing again and again."
把"respiration"拆成"re-spir-ation"——"spir"源自拉丁语"spirare"，意为"呼吸"，所以respiration就是"一次又一次地呼吸"。
