# ✂️ succinct

🔊 /səkˈsɪŋkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Expressed clearly and in few words; brief and concise; compact and direct in expression
**中文：** adj. 简洁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concise（简明的）
- terse（简洁的）
- brief（简短的）
- pithy（精练的）
- laconic（简洁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **succinct summary / succinct explanation / succinct manner / succinct response / succinct and clear**

👉 Example: The professor's succinct explanation clarified a concept that had confused students for weeks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The attorney delivered a **succinct** closing argument that conveyed the essential points without unnecessary elaboration or repetition.
   律师发表了简洁的结案陈词，传达了要点而没有不必要的阐述或重复。

2. Effective academic writing requires **succinct** prose that communicates complex ideas with precision and economy of language.
   有效的学术写作需要简洁的文风，以精确和经济的语言传达复杂的思想。

3. The committee chairperson provided a **succinct** overview of the proposed budget revisions before opening the floor for discussion.
   委员会主席在开放讨论之前，对提议的预算修订提供了简明的概述。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "succinct" as "suck-in-ct" — sucking in all the extra words, compressing your message to be short and tight.
把"succinct"想成"suck-in-ct"——把所有多余的词吸进去，将你的信息压缩得简短紧凑。
