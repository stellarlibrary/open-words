# 👶 prattle

🔊 /ˈprætəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To talk at length in a childish or trivial way; to babble incoherently. (n.) Foolish or inconsequential talk; childish babbling
**中文：** v. 说孩子气的话；闲聊；胡说 n. 无聊话；咿咿呀呀声

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- babble（咿呀学语）
- chatter（喋喋不休）
- prate（唠叨）
- jabber（急促含混地说）
- blabber（胡扯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prattle on about / idle prattle / childish prattle / meaningless prattle**

👉 Example: The toddler would prattle on happily about imaginary friends while playing alone in the garden.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The guests grew visibly impatient as the host continued to **prattle** on about trivial anecdotes that had no relevance whatsoever.
   当主人继续喋喋不休地讲述毫不相关的琐碎轶事时，客人们明显变得不耐烦了。

2. Critics dismissed the lengthy report as mere **prattle**, arguing that it lacked the rigorous analysis required for informed policymaking.
   批评者将这份冗长的报告斥为空谈，认为它缺乏明智决策所需的严谨分析。

3. Rather than engaging in substantive debate, the candidate chose to **prattle** about personal stories that failed to address key issues.
   这位候选人没有进行实质性的辩论，而是选择闲聊个人故事，未能解决关键问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prattle" as "prat + little" — a little, silly talk, like a baby's meaningless babbling sounds.
把"prattle"想成"prat（傻瓜）+ little（小的）"——小而愚蠢的谈话，就像婴儿无意义的咿呀声。
