# 💰 usury

🔊 /ˈjuːʒəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The practice of lending money at unreasonably high or illegal rates of interest
**中文：** 高利贷；高利

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- loan-sharking（放高利贷）
- extortion（敲诈）
- profiteering（牟取暴利）
- exploitation（剥削）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **practice usury / laws against usury / accused of usury / usury rates**

👉 Example: Medieval laws strictly prohibited usury, reflecting moral concerns about exploiting borrowers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many ancient civilizations enacted strict laws against **usury** to protect vulnerable citizens from predatory lending practices.
   许多古代文明制定了严格的反高利贷法律，以保护弱势公民免受掠夺性贷款行为的伤害。

2. The investigation revealed that the financial institution had engaged in **usury** by charging interest rates far exceeding legal limits.
   调查显示，该金融机构通过收取远超法定上限的利率从事高利贷活动。

3. Religious scholars throughout history have debated the moral implications of **usury** and its effects on economic inequality.
   历史上的宗教学者一直在讨论高利贷的道德影响及其对经济不平等的作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "usury" as "use-you-ry" — they use you by charging outrageously high interest on loans.
把"usury"想成"use-you-ry（利用你）"——他们通过收取极高的贷款利息来利用你。
