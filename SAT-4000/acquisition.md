# 📦 acquisition

🔊 /ˌæk.wɪˈzɪʃ.ən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Something gained or obtained; the act of acquiring, especially a purchase or corporate takeover.
**中文：** 获得物，获得；收购

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- purchase（购买）
- procurement（采购）
- obtainment（获得）
- takeover（收购）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make an acquisition**, **acquisition of (something)**, **recent/major/strategic acquisition**

👉 Example: The company's latest acquisition strengthened its market position considerably.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's newest **acquisition** is a rare Renaissance painting valued at several million dollars.
   博物馆最新的藏品是一幅价值数百万美元的珍贵文艺复兴时期画作。

2. The **acquisition** of advanced language skills requires years of dedicated practice and immersion.
   掌握高级语言技能需要多年的刻苦练习和沉浸式学习。

3. Corporate leaders announced the **acquisition** of a rival firm to expand their global operations.
   企业领导人宣布收购一家竞争对手公司，以扩展其全球业务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "acquire" + "-tion" — the act or result of acquiring something, like collecting treasures.
联想"acquire（获取）"加上名词后缀"-tion"，就是"获取的东西"或"获取的行为"，想象收藏家不断获取珍宝。
