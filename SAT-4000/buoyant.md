# 🎈 buoyant

🔊 /ˈbɔɪənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to float; cheerful and optimistic; (of prices or business) tending to rise or remain at a high level
**中文：** adj. 有浮力的；轻快的，乐观的；上涨的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cheerful（愉快的）
- optimistic（乐观的）
- resilient（有弹性的）
- lighthearted（轻松的）
- ebullient（热情洋溢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **buoyant mood / buoyant economy / buoyant spirit / buoyant market**

👉 Example: The buoyant economy encouraged investors to increase their holdings in emerging sectors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the recent setbacks, the team maintained a **buoyant** spirit and continued to pursue their ambitious research goals.
   尽管最近遭遇挫折，团队仍然保持着乐观的精神，继续追求他们雄心勃勃的研究目标。

2. The **buoyant** housing market attracted numerous foreign investors seeking profitable opportunities in metropolitan areas.
   上涨的房地产市场吸引了众多寻求大都市地区盈利机会的外国投资者。

3. Cork is a naturally **buoyant** material, which makes it an ideal choice for manufacturing life preservers and flotation devices.
   软木是一种天然具有浮力的材料，这使它成为制造救生衣和漂浮装置的理想选择。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "buoy" inside "buoyant" — a buoy floats on water, so buoyant means able to float or stay uplifted.
想到"buoyant"中的"buoy"（浮标）——浮标漂浮在水面上，所以buoyant意味着有浮力的或保持高昂的。
