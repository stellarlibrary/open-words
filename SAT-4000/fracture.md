# 💔 fracture

🔊 /ˈfræktʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The cracking or breaking of a hard object or material; a break in a bone. (v.) To break or cause to break; to crack or split
**中文：** n. 破裂；断裂；骨折；裂缝 v. 破裂；折断

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- break（断裂）
- crack（裂缝）
- rupture（破裂）
- split（裂开）
- shatter（粉碎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fracture a bone / stress fracture / fracture lines / fracture a relationship / compound fracture**

👉 Example: The impact of the collision was severe enough to fracture the supporting beam of the bridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The athlete sustained a stress **fracture** in her tibia, which required several months of rehabilitation before she could return to competition.
   这位运动员胫骨出现了应力性骨折，需要几个月的康复治疗才能重返赛场。

2. Prolonged political disagreements threatened to **fracture** the alliance that had maintained regional stability for more than two decades.
   长期的政治分歧威胁着这个维持了二十多年地区稳定的联盟的分裂。

3. Geologists examined the **fracture** patterns in the rock formations to assess the likelihood of seismic activity in the surrounding area.
   地质学家检查了岩层中的断裂模式，以评估周边地区发生地震活动的可能性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fracture" as "fract-ure" — "fract" means "break" in Latin. Picture dropping a fragile object and hearing the crack as it fractures apart.
把"fracture"想成"fract"（打碎）+"ure"——"fract"在拉丁语中意思是"打碎"。想象摔落一个易碎物品，听到它裂开时的声响。
