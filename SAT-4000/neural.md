# 🧠 neural

🔊 /ˈnjʊərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to a nerve or the nervous system; of or pertaining to neurons or the central nervous system
**中文：** adj. 神经的；神经系统的；神经中枢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nervous（神经的）
- neurological（神经学的）
- cerebral（大脑的）
- neuronal（神经元的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **neural pathway / neural network / neural activity / neural development / neural processing**

👉 Example: Advances in neural imaging technology have revolutionized our understanding of how the human brain functions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers discovered that **neural** plasticity allows the brain to reorganize itself by forming new connections throughout an individual's lifetime.
   研究人员发现，神经可塑性使大脑能够在个体一生中通过形成新连接来自我重组。

2. The artificial **neural** network was trained on millions of data points to recognize complex patterns in medical diagnostic imaging.
   该人工神经网络通过数百万个数据点进行训练，以识别医学诊断影像中的复杂模式。

3. Chronic stress has been shown to cause significant damage to **neural** pathways associated with memory formation and emotional regulation.
   研究表明，长期压力会对与记忆形成和情绪调节相关的神经通路造成显著损害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neural" as "neuro" (nerve) + "al" (relating to) — anything "neural" relates to the nerves and the nervous system.
把"neural"想成"neuro"(神经) + "al"(与...相关的)——凡是"neural"的都与神经和神经系统有关。
