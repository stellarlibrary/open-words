# ✂️ bisect

🔊 /baɪˈsɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To divide into two equal parts; to cut or split in half
**中文：** v. 平分；二等分；把……一分为二

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- halve（对半分）
- divide（分割）
- split（劈开）
- partition（分隔）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bisect an angle / bisect a line / bisect a region / bisect the area**

👉 Example: In geometry class, students learn to bisect angles using only a compass and a straightedge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new highway will **bisect** the rural community, effectively dividing it into two isolated and unequal halves.
   新公路将把这个农村社区一分为二，使其分成两个孤立且不均等的部分。

2. The professor demonstrated how to **bisect** a line segment precisely using classical Euclidean construction methods.
   教授演示了如何使用经典的欧几里得作图方法精确地将线段平分。

3. A major river **bisects** the ancient city, creating distinct cultural districts on each bank with unique identities.
   一条大河将这座古城一分为二，在两岸形成了各具特色的文化区域。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "sect" (cut, as in section) — to cut something into two equal pieces.
把"bisect"拆成"bi-"（二）+"sect"（切，如section部分）——把东西切成两等份。
