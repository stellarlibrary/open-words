# 💎 debase

🔊 /dɪˈbeɪs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To lower in quality, value, or dignity; to degrade or adulterate
**中文：** v. 降低；使贬值；掺杂；贬损

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- degrade（降低）
- diminish（减少）
- cheapen（贬低）
- corrupt（使堕落）
- adulterate（掺杂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **debase the currency / debase oneself / debase the quality / debase standards**

👉 Example: Counterfeiters sought to debase the currency by mixing cheaper metals into the coins.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government's decision to print excessive money would inevitably **debase** the national currency and erode public confidence.
   政府过度印钞的决定势必会使国家货币贬值并侵蚀公众信心。

2. Critics argued that the sensationalist media coverage served only to **debase** the quality of public discourse on important issues.
   批评者认为，耸人听闻的媒体报道只会降低公众对重要议题讨论的质量。

3. A true scholar would never **debase** herself by fabricating data merely to secure publication in a prestigious journal.
   一位真正的学者绝不会为了在著名期刊上发表文章而伪造数据来贬损自己。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-base" as removing the "base" or foundation — when you debase something, you strip away its fundamental worth.
把"debase"想成"去掉base（基础）"——当你debase某物时，就是剥夺了它的根本价值。
