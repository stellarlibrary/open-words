# 🌱 emergent

🔊 /ɪˈmɜːrdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) In the process of coming into being or becoming prominent; arising unexpectedly and requiring urgent attention
**中文：** adj. 突现的；紧急的；浮现的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emerging（崛起的）
- nascent（新生的）
- budding（萌芽的）
- developing（发展中的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emergent properties / emergent behavior / emergent technology / emergent nations**

👉 Example: The researcher studied the emergent properties that arise when individual components interact within complex systems.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists have observed **emergent** behavior in flocks of birds that cannot be predicted from studying individual organisms alone.
   科学家观察到鸟群中的突现行为，这种行为无法仅通过研究单个个体来预测。

2. The conference focused on **emergent** technologies that promise to reshape global communication and information exchange systems.
   会议聚焦于有望重塑全球通信和信息交换系统的新兴技术。

3. Several **emergent** democracies in the region have made remarkable progress in establishing robust institutional frameworks since independence.
   该地区几个新兴民主国家自独立以来在建立健全的制度框架方面取得了显著进步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "emergent" as something "emerging" from beneath the surface — just beginning to appear and take shape.
把"emergent"想成从水面之下"浮现"出来的东西——刚刚开始出现并成形。
