# 💀 corpse

🔊 /kɔːrps/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A dead body, especially of a human being
**中文：** n. 尸体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cadaver（尸体）
- remains（遗体）
- body（遗体）
- carcass（躯体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **discover a corpse / examine a corpse / unidentified corpse / decomposing corpse**

👉 Example: The forensic team was called to examine the corpse found near the riverbank.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forensic pathologist carefully examined the **corpse** to determine the precise cause and estimated time of death.
   法医病理学家仔细检查了尸体，以确定确切的死因和估计的死亡时间。

2. Archaeologists discovered a remarkably well-preserved **corpse** in the peat bog, dating back approximately two thousand years.
   考古学家在泥炭沼泽中发现了一具保存极为完好的尸体，距今约两千年。

3. In anatomy courses, medical students learn essential surgical techniques by studying the structures of a donated **corpse**.
   在解剖课程中，医学生通过研究捐献的尸体结构来学习基本的外科技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "corpse" as "corps" (body) + "e" — the silent "e" at the end is like a body that has gone silent, no longer alive.
把"corpse"想成"corps"（身体）加上"e"——末尾无声的"e"就像一个已经沉默的身体，不再有生命。
