# 📊 actuary

🔊 /ˈæk.tʃu.ˌɛr.i/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A professional who analyzes financial risk using mathematics and statistics, especially in the insurance industry.
**中文：** 保险计算员；保险精算师

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- risk analyst（风险分析师）
- statistician（统计学家）
- underwriter（保险商）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **certified/qualified actuary**, **work as an actuary**, **actuary at (an insurance firm)**

👉 Example: The actuary calculated the long-term financial risks associated with the new insurance policy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **actuary** determined that rising life expectancy would significantly affect the pension fund's solvency.
   精算师确定，预期寿命的增长将显著影响养老基金的偿付能力。

2. Insurance companies rely heavily on the expertise of an **actuary** to price their policies accurately.
   保险公司在很大程度上依赖精算师的专业知识来准确定价其保单。

3. After passing a series of rigorous examinations, she began her career as a certified **actuary**.
   在通过一系列严格的考试后，她开始了自己的注册精算师职业生涯。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ACTU-ARY" — think "actual" numbers: an actuary deals with actual data and real calculations of risk.
"ACTU-ARY"——联想"actual（实际的）"数字：精算师处理的都是实际的数据和真实的风险计算。
