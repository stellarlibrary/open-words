# 📐 parallel lines

🔊 /ˈpærəˌlɛl laɪnz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Two or more lines in the same plane that never intersect, maintaining equal distance from each other at every point
**中文：** n. 平行线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equidistant lines（等距线）
- nonintersecting lines（不相交的线）
- concurrent lines（并行线）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **draw parallel lines / parallel lines never meet / cut by a transversal / parallel lines theorem**

👉 Example: In Euclidean geometry, parallel lines extend infinitely without ever crossing each other.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geometry teacher demonstrated that **parallel lines** cut by a transversal produce corresponding angles that are congruent.
   几何老师演示了被横截线切割的平行线会产生相等的对应角。

2. Engineers must ensure that the railroad tracks remain as **parallel lines** to guarantee the safe and smooth passage of trains.
   工程师必须确保铁轨保持平行线状态，以保证火车安全平稳地通行。

3. The concept of **parallel lines** never intersecting is a fundamental axiom in Euclidean geometry studied by all mathematics students.
   平行线永不相交的概念是欧几里得几何学中的基本公理，所有数学学生都要学习。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "parallel lines" as two friends walking side by side who agree to keep the same distance apart forever — they never meet.
把"parallel lines"想象成两个朋友并排行走，约定永远保持相同的距离——他们永远不会相遇。
