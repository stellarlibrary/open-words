# 🗺️ territorial

🔊 /ˌterɪˈtɔːriəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to the ownership or control of an area of land or sea; displaying behavior aimed at defending one's territory
**中文：** 领土的；区域的；有领地意识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regional（地区的）
- jurisdictional（管辖的）
- geographic（地理的）
- proprietary（所有权的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **territorial dispute / territorial waters / territorial behavior / territorial integrity**

👉 Example: The two nations have been locked in a territorial dispute over the contested islands for several decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The treaty was established to resolve longstanding **territorial** disputes between the neighboring countries along the contested border region.
   该条约的签订是为了解决邻国之间在争议边境地区长期存在的领土争端。

2. Many predatory animals exhibit strongly **territorial** behavior, aggressively defending their hunting grounds from rival members of the species.
   许多捕食性动物表现出强烈的领地行为，积极地保卫自己的猎场免受同类竞争者的侵犯。

3. International law protects a nation's **territorial** waters, extending sovereignty up to twelve nautical miles from the coastline.
   国际法保护一个国家的领海，将主权延伸至海岸线起十二海里。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "territory" inside "territorial" — if something is territorial, it relates to or defends a territory.
把"territorial"中的"territory（领地）"找出来——如果某事物是territorial的，它就与领地有关或在保卫领地。
