# 😕 misunderstand

🔊 /ˌmɪsʌndərˈstænd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fail to interpret or understand something correctly; to take in a wrong sense
**中文：** v. 误解；误会

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- misinterpret（曲解）
- misconstrue（误解）
- misread（误读）
- confuse（混淆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **misunderstand the point / misunderstand someone's intentions / easily misunderstood / commonly misunderstood**

👉 Example: It is easy to misunderstand the author's ironic tone if one reads the passage too quickly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics often **misunderstand** the novelist's use of satire, interpreting her sharp social commentary as personal hostility.
   评论家们常常误解这位小说家对讽刺手法的运用，将她尖锐的社会评论解读为个人敌意。

2. Students who **misunderstand** the fundamental principles of economics will struggle with more advanced coursework later on.
   误解经济学基本原理的学生在后续更高级的课程中将会遇到困难。

3. The ambassador's carefully worded statement was **misunderstood** by the press, leading to widespread diplomatic confusion.
   大使措辞谨慎的声明被媒体误解，导致了广泛的外交混乱。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-understand" as "understanding something in a missed way" — your understanding misses the mark entirely.
把"misunderstand"想成"以错失的方式理解"——你的理解完全偏离了目标。
