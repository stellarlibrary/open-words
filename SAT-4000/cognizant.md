# 👁️ cognizant

🔊 /ˈkɒɡnɪzənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having knowledge or awareness of something; fully informed and conscious of a fact or situation
**中文：** adj. 已认知的；审理的；有认识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aware（意识到的）
- conscious（有意识的）
- mindful（留心的）
- informed（知情的）
- sensible（察觉到的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cognizant of / fully cognizant / cognizant that**

👉 Example: The board members were fully cognizant of the financial risks involved in the proposed merger.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Effective leaders must remain **cognizant** of the evolving needs and concerns of their team members at all times.
   有效的领导者必须始终了解团队成员不断变化的需求和关切。

2. The researchers were **cognizant** of potential biases in their methodology and implemented rigorous controls to mitigate them.
   研究人员意识到其方法论中可能存在的偏差，并实施了严格的控制措施来减轻这些偏差。

3. Citizens should be **cognizant** of their legal rights before entering into any contractual agreements with corporate entities.
   公民在与企业签订任何合同协议之前，应当了解自己的合法权利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cognizant" as "recognize + ant" — like an ant that recognizes everything around it, being cognizant means being fully aware of your surroundings.
把"cognizant"想成"recognize（认识）+ ant（蚂蚁）"——就像一只能识别周围一切的蚂蚁，cognizant意味着完全了解你的周围环境。
