# 🦞 lobster

🔊 /ˈlɑːbstər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large marine crustacean with a cylindrical body, stalked eyes, and large pincers, valued as a seafood delicacy
**中文：** n. 龙虾

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crustacean（甲壳类动物）
- shellfish（贝类）
- crayfish（小龙虾）
- prawn（大虾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lobster tail / lobster pot / lobster bisque / lobster fishery / rock lobster**

👉 Example: The restaurant is famous for serving fresh lobster caught daily from the nearby coastal waters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The decline in **lobster** populations along the southern coast has prompted marine biologists to investigate environmental causes.
   南部海岸龙虾数量的下降促使海洋生物学家调查其环境原因。

2. Local fishermen depend heavily on the **lobster** trade, which constitutes the primary source of income for coastal communities.
   当地渔民严重依赖龙虾贸易，它是沿海社区的主要收入来源。

3. Researchers discovered that **lobster** shells contain compounds with potential applications in biodegradable packaging and medical materials.
   研究人员发现龙虾壳含有在可生物降解包装和医疗材料方面具有潜在应用的化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lobster" as "lob + ster" — imagine lobbing (throwing) a star-shaped creature from the sea onto your dinner plate.
把"lobster"想成"lob（扔）+ ster（星）"——想象从海里把一只星形生物扔到你的餐盘上。
