# 👇 abase

🔊 /əˈbeɪs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To lower in rank, prestige, or esteem; to degrade or humiliate
**中文：** v. 使谦卑；降低品格；贬低地位

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- degrade（贬低）
- humiliate（羞辱）
- demean（降低身份）
- belittle（贬损）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abase oneself / abase one's dignity / abase before someone**

👉 Example: He refused to abase himself before the corrupt officials who demanded his submission.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dictator forced political prisoners to **abase** themselves publicly in televised confessions before the entire nation.
   独裁者强迫政治犯在面向全国的电视认罪中公开自我贬低。

2. She would never **abase** her principles merely to gain favor with those in positions of power.
   她绝不会仅仅为了讨好当权者而降低自己的原则。

3. Critics argued that the new regulations would **abase** the dignity of workers throughout the industry.
   批评者认为新规定将贬损整个行业中工人的尊严。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-base" — to bring someone down to the base, the lowest level. Abase means to lower someone's status.
想想"a-base"——把某人拉到base（底部），最低的级别。Abase就是降低某人的地位。
