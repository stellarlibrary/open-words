# 🔧 plumb

🔊 /plʌm/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective, adverb | 动词, 名词, 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To explore or experience fully; to measure depth. (n.) A lead weight on a line for testing verticality. (adj.) Exactly vertical. (adv.) Exactly; precisely
**中文：** v. 使垂直；探测，探索 n. 铅锤；垂直 adj. 垂直的 adv. 恰恰，正；垂直地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fathom（探测）
- probe（探索）
- sound（测深）
- measure（测量）
- vertical（垂直的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plumb the depths / plumb line / plumb a mystery / plumb straight**

👉 Example: The philosopher sought to plumb the depths of human consciousness through rigorous logical inquiry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary attempted to **plumb** the depths of the political scandal that had shaken the nation's democratic foundations.
   这部纪录片试图探究那场动摇国家民主根基的政治丑闻的深层内幕。

2. The architect used a **plumb** line to ensure that every wall of the historic restoration project stood perfectly vertical.
   建筑师使用铅锤线来确保这个历史修复项目的每面墙都完全垂直。

3. Researchers continue to **plumb** the mysteries of deep ocean ecosystems that remain largely unexplored and poorly understood.
   研究人员继续探索深海生态系统的奥秘，这些系统在很大程度上仍未被探索和了解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plumb" from Latin "plumbum" (lead) — a lead weight drops straight down to plumb the depths, perfectly vertical.
把"plumb"和拉丁语"plumbum（铅）"联系起来——铅锤笔直下垂来探测深度，完全垂直。
