# ⛪ seminary

🔊 /ˈsɛmɪnɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A theological school for training clergy; an institution of learning; a place of origin or development
**中文：** n. 神学院

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- divinity school（神学院）
- theological college（神学学院）
- academy（学院）
- institute（学院）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **attend seminary / seminary education / seminary student / graduate from seminary**

👉 Example: After completing his undergraduate degree, he enrolled in a seminary to pursue theological studies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young minister spent four years at a prestigious **seminary** before being ordained and assigned to a rural congregation.
   这位年轻的牧师在一所知名神学院学习了四年后被授予圣职，并被派往一个乡村教会。

2. The **seminary** offered rigorous coursework in biblical languages, systematic theology, and pastoral counseling for aspiring clergy members.
   这所神学院为有志成为神职人员的学生提供圣经语言、系统神学和牧灵辅导方面的严格课程。

3. Founded in the eighteenth century, the **seminary** has served as an intellectual birthplace for many influential religious reformers and scholars.
   这所创建于十八世纪的神学院一直是许多有影响力的宗教改革者和学者的思想发源地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "seminary" as a "seed nursery" for future religious leaders — from Latin "seminarium" meaning seed plot, where spiritual knowledge is sown and grown.
把"seminary"想成未来宗教领袖的"育苗场"——源自拉丁语"seminarium"（苗圃），在这里播种和培育属灵知识。
