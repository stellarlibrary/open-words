# 🧹 slovenly

🔊 /ˈslʌvənli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Messy, untidy, and careless in appearance or habits; lacking neatness. (adv.) In an untidy or careless manner
**中文：** adj. 懒散的；不修边幅的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unkempt（不整洁的）
- sloppy（邋遢的）
- disheveled（凌乱的）
- untidy（不整齐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **slovenly appearance / slovenly habits / slovenly manner / slovenly work**

👉 Example: The professor's slovenly appearance belied his brilliant intellect and groundbreaking research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inspector cited the restaurant for its **slovenly** kitchen conditions, which posed a serious health risk to all patrons.
   检查员因餐厅厨房邋遢的卫生条件对所有顾客构成严重健康风险而对其进行了处罚。

2. His **slovenly** work habits eventually cost him the promotion that his colleagues had expected him to receive.
   他懒散的工作习惯最终使他失去了同事们预期他会获得的晋升机会。

3. The editor refused to publish the manuscript because the author's **slovenly** prose lacked coherence and scholarly precision.
   编辑拒绝出版这份手稿，因为作者不修边幅的行文缺乏连贯性和学术精确性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slovenly" as "slow + ven(ture) + ly" — someone who moves slowly and ventures through life carelessly, never tidying up.
把"slovenly"想成"slow（慢）+ ven(ture)（冒险）+ ly"——一个慢吞吞地、漫不经心地过日子的人，从不收拾整理。
