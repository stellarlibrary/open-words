# 🚫 inexcusable

🔊 /ˌɪnɪkˈskjuːzəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Too bad to be justified or tolerated; unforgivable; impossible to excuse or defend
**中文：** adj. 不可原谅的；没法辩解的；不可宽恕的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unforgivable（不可原谅的）
- unpardonable（不可饶恕的）
- indefensible（无法辩护的）
- unjustifiable（不合理的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inexcusable behavior / inexcusable negligence / inexcusable delay / absolutely inexcusable / morally inexcusable**

👉 Example: The supervisor's failure to report the safety violation was deemed inexcusable by the review board.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee declared the prolonged delay in emergency response to be **inexcusable** given the severity of the crisis.
   鉴于危机的严重性，委员会宣布紧急响应的长时间延误是**不可原谅**的。

2. Plagiarism in academic research is considered **inexcusable** and carries severe consequences including expulsion from the institution.
   学术研究中的抄袭被认为是**不可宽恕**的，会导致包括被开除在内的严重后果。

3. The diplomat called the deliberate targeting of civilian populations an **inexcusable** violation of international humanitarian law.
   这位外交官称蓄意针对平民的行为是对国际人道主义法的**不可原谅**的违反。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-excuse-able" as "not able to be excused" — an action so wrong that no excuse could possibly make it acceptable.
把"inexcusable"拆成"in-excuse-able"，即"不能被原谅的"——一个错得如此离谱的行为，没有任何借口能使其被接受。
