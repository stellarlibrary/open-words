# 🔧 reparable

🔊 /ˈrepərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being repaired, restored, or made good; able to be remedied or corrected
**中文：** adj. 可修缮的；可补偿的；可挽回的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fixable（可修理的）
- restorable（可恢复的）
- recoverable（可挽回的）
- rectifiable（可纠正的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reparable damage / reparable harm / reparable loss / easily reparable**

👉 Example: Fortunately, the damage to the manuscript was reparable and conservators were able to restore the pages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The structural engineer confirmed that the earthquake damage to the bridge was **reparable** with sufficient funding and time.
   结构工程师确认，只要有足够的资金和时间，桥梁的地震损坏是可以修复的。

2. Although the diplomatic incident was regrettable, officials on both sides believed the relationship was **reparable** through dialogue.
   尽管这次外交事件令人遗憾，但双方官员相信通过对话可以修复两国关系。

3. The technician determined that the water damage to the electronic equipment was **reparable** rather than requiring full replacement.
   技术人员确定电子设备的水损是可以修复的，而不需要全部更换。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "REPAIR-able" — reparable simply means something is able to be repaired or fixed.
把"reparable"想成"REPAIR-able"——reparable就是可以被修理或修复的意思。
