# 🚫 disavow

🔊 /ˌdɪsəˈvaʊ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To deny any responsibility for or connection with; to refuse to acknowledge or accept
**中文：** v. 否认，否定；不承认

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deny（否认）
- repudiate（否定）
- renounce（声明放弃）
- disclaim（否认）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disavow responsibility / disavow knowledge of / disavow any connection / publicly disavow**

👉 Example: The organization moved quickly to disavow the extremist remarks made by its former spokesperson.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government chose to **disavow** any involvement in the covert operation once the details became public knowledge.
   一旦细节被公众知晓，政府选择否认与该秘密行动有任何牵连。

2. The senator was pressured to **disavow** the controversial endorsement that had generated widespread criticism from constituents.
   这位参议员被迫否认那项引起选民广泛批评的有争议的背书。

3. The author publicly **disavowed** the unauthorized translation, claiming it grossly distorted the original meaning of her work.
   作者公开否认了那份未经授权的译本，声称它严重歪曲了她作品的原意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-avow" — to take back your "avow" (declaration), meaning you un-declare or deny what was previously stated.
把"disavow"想成"否定(dis)+声明(avow)"——撤回你的声明，即否认之前说过的话。
