# 🏆 acquire

🔊 /əˈkwaɪər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To obtain or gain possession of something through effort, ability, or purchase
**中文：** 获得；取得；学到；捕获

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obtain（获得）
- gain（取得）
- procure（获取）
- attain（达到）
- secure（获得）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acquire knowledge / acquire skills / acquire a taste for / acquire property**

👉 Example: Students acquire critical thinking skills through rigorous academic training and active intellectual engagement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The multinational corporation sought to **acquire** several promising technology startups to strengthen its competitive market position.
   这家跨国公司试图收购几家有前景的科技初创企业，以加强其市场竞争地位。

2. Children **acquire** language naturally through sustained exposure to speech and meaningful social interaction with caregivers.
   儿童通过持续接触语言和与照顾者进行有意义的社交互动来自然习得语言。

3. It takes years of dedicated practice to **acquire** the level of proficiency required for professional musicianship.
   要达到专业音乐家所需的熟练水平，需要多年的专注练习。

---

把"acquire"想成"a-choir"——要加入合唱团，你必须通过坚持不懈的练习和努力来获得歌唱技能。
