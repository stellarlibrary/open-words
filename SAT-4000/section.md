# 🧱 section

🔊 /ˈsɛkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A cross-section; a part; a department; a district; a chapter. (v.) To cut into pieces; to divide into parts
**中文：** n. 断面；一部分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- segment（部分）
- portion（部分）
- division（分区）
- chapter（章节）
- component（组成部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **section of / cross section / section off / in this section**

👉 Example: The final section of the report addresses the long-term implications of the proposed policy changes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor asked students to read the relevant **section** of the textbook before attending the seminar on Thursday.
   教授要求学生在周四参加研讨会之前阅读教科书中的相关部分。

2. Authorities decided to **section** off the contaminated area to prevent further exposure to hazardous chemical substances.
   当局决定将受污染区域隔离，以防止进一步接触有害化学物质。

3. Each **section** of the orchestra performed its passage with precision, contributing to a flawless symphonic interpretation.
   管弦乐团的每个声部都精确地演奏了各自的乐段，成就了一次完美的交响诠释。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sect-ion" — from Latin "sectio" meaning a cutting. A section is what you get when you cut something into parts.
把"section"想成来自拉丁语"sectio（切割）"——一个section就是把东西切开后得到的一部分。
