# 🫧 effervescent

🔊 /ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Giving off bubbles of gas; vivacious and enthusiastic in manner or temperament
**中文：** adj. 冒泡的；沸腾的；活泼的；热情洋溢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bubbly（冒泡的）
- vivacious（活泼的）
- sparkling（闪耀的）
- exuberant（热情洋溢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effervescent personality / effervescent drink / effervescent tablet / effervescent enthusiasm**

👉 Example: Her effervescent personality made her the perfect candidate for the role of public relations director.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **effervescent** reaction that occurs when baking soda meets vinegar is a classic demonstration used in chemistry classrooms.
   小苏打遇到醋时发生的冒泡反应是化学课堂上使用的经典演示实验。

2. Her **effervescent** charm and boundless energy captivated audiences throughout her remarkable three-decade career on the stage.
   她活泼的魅力和无穷的精力在她长达三十年的舞台生涯中吸引了无数观众。

3. The scientist observed that the **effervescent** liquid gradually lost its carbonation when exposed to warm temperatures for prolonged periods.
   科学家观察到，这种冒泡的液体在长时间暴露于温暖环境中后逐渐失去了碳酸。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "effer-vescent" as bubbles rising in a fizzy drink — both the liquid and a lively person can be effervescent.
把"effervescent"想成气泡在汽水中上升——无论是液体还是一个活泼的人都可以是effervescent的。
