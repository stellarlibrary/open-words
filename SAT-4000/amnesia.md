# 🧠 amnesia

🔊 /æmˈniːʒə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A partial or total loss of memory, especially caused by brain injury, shock, illness, or psychological trauma.
**中文：** 健忘症，记忆缺失

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- memory loss（记忆丧失）
- forgetfulness（健忘）
- blackout（暂时失忆）
- oblivion（遗忘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffer from, cause, retrograde, anterograde, temporary, selective, collective**
👉 Example: The patient suffered from amnesia after the traumatic head injury.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accident victim experienced severe **amnesia** and could not recall any events from the preceding week.
   事故受害者经历了严重的失忆症，无法回忆起前一周的任何事件。

2. Historians warned against collective **amnesia** regarding the atrocities committed during the prolonged civil conflict.
   历史学家警告人们不要对长期内战中犯下的暴行产生集体遗忘。

3. The neurologist diagnosed the elderly patient with progressive **amnesia** linked to early-stage neurodegenerative disease.
   神经科医生诊断该老年患者患有与早期神经退行性疾病相关的进行性失忆症。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amnesia" begins with "a-" (without) and "mnes-" (memory, from Greek "mnesis") — literally "without memory."
"Amnesia"以"a-"（没有）和"mnes-"（记忆，源自希腊语"mnesis"）开头——字面意思就是"没有记忆"。
