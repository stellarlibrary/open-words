# 🌊 submerge

🔊 /səbˈmɜːrdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To put or sink below the surface of water; to immerse or plunge into something completely
**中文：** v. 沉没，淹没；潜入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immerse（浸入）
- plunge（使沉入）
- inundate（淹没）
- engulf（吞没）
- drown（淹没）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **submerge in water / submerge oneself in work / submerge beneath the surface / completely submerged**

👉 Example: The floodwaters rose rapidly and submerged the entire lower district of the city within a matter of hours.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The divers had to **submerge** to a depth of thirty meters to inspect the damaged hull of the shipwreck on the ocean floor.
   潜水员不得不潜入三十米深处，以检查海底沉船受损的船体。

2. Rising sea levels threaten to **submerge** several low-lying island nations, displacing millions of residents within the coming decades.
   海平面上升有可能在未来几十年内淹没几个低洼岛国，使数百万居民流离失所。

3. She chose to **submerge** herself entirely in academic research, dedicating every available hour to completing her doctoral dissertation.
   她选择完全沉浸在学术研究中，将每一个可用的小时都用于完成博士论文。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-merge" as "sub" (under) + "merge" (to blend into) — to merge under the surface, disappearing beneath the water like a sinking object.
把"submerge"想成"sub"（在下面）+"merge"（融入）——融入水面以下，像沉没的物体一样消失在水下。
