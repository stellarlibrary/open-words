# 📦 compression

🔊 /kəmˈpreʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act or process of compressing; the state of being compressed; reduction in size, volume, or quantity
**中文：** n. 压缩；浓缩；压榨

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- condensation（浓缩）
- compaction（压实）
- contraction（收缩）
- squeezing（挤压）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **data compression / compression ratio / compression algorithm / under compression**

👉 Example: Modern streaming services rely on advanced compression technology to deliver high-quality video efficiently.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **compression** of digital audio files enables users to store thousands of songs on a single portable device.
   数字音频文件的压缩使用户能够在一台便携设备上存储数千首歌曲。

2. Excessive **compression** of the spinal discs can lead to chronic back pain and reduced mobility over time.
   脊椎间盘的过度压迫会随着时间推移导致慢性背痛和活动能力下降。

3. The engineer explained that the **compression** ratio of the engine directly affects its overall fuel efficiency.
   工程师解释说，发动机的压缩比直接影响其整体燃油效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compress" + "ion" (the action of) — compression is the act of pressing things together into less space.
把"compression"拆成"compress（压缩）+ ion（行为）"——压缩就是把东西挤到更小空间里的行为。
