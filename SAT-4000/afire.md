# 🔥 afire

🔊 /əˈfaɪər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Burning; on fire; in a state of flames. Also used figuratively to describe intense passion or excitement.
**中文：** 燃烧的；着火的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ablaze（燃烧的）
- burning（着火的）
- aflame（燃烧的）
- ignited（点燃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **set afire, afire with passion, afire with enthusiasm, the building was afire**
👉 Example: The old warehouse was afire before the firefighters could arrive at the scene.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The entire eastern wing of the historic mansion was **afire** before emergency services reached the remote estate.
   在紧急救援人员到达这座偏远庄园之前，这座历史悠久的大厦整个东翼已经着火。

2. Her imagination was **afire** with creative possibilities after attending the renowned artist's inspirational lecture.
   在参加了那位著名艺术家鼓舞人心的演讲后，她的想象力燃烧着创造的可能性。

3. Witnesses reported that the vessel was already **afire** when the coast guard dispatched its rescue boats.
   目击者报告说，当海岸警卫队派出救援船时，该船只已经着火。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Afire" is simply "a" + "fire" — something that is afire is literally in a state of fire, burning brightly.
"Afire"就是"a"+"fire"——处于afire状态的东西就是字面意义上着火了，熊熊燃烧。
