# 🚀 innovative

🔊 /ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Featuring new methods or ideas; original and creative in thinking or approach
**中文：** 革新的，创新的；新颖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- creative（创造性的）
- groundbreaking（开创性的）
- pioneering（先驱的）
- inventive（有创造力的）
- novel（新颖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **innovative approach / innovative solution / innovative design / highly innovative**

👉 Example: The company received an award for its innovative approach to renewable energy storage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor presented an **innovative** framework for analyzing the socioeconomic effects of climate change on developing nations.
   这位教授提出了一个创新的框架，用于分析气候变化对发展中国家的社会经济影响。

2. Several **innovative** policies were introduced to address the persistent achievement gap in public education systems.
   为解决公共教育系统中持续存在的成绩差距，引入了多项创新政策。

3. The research team developed an **innovative** technique that significantly reduces the time required for genetic sequencing.
   研究团队开发了一项创新技术，大幅缩短了基因测序所需的时间。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "innovative" as "inner + creative" — the creative energy comes from within to produce something entirely new.
把"innovative"想成"inner（内在的）+ creative（创造性的）"——创造性的能量源自内心，产生全新的事物。
