# 🎵 bass

🔊 /beɪs/ (music) or /bæs/ (fish)<br>
🏷️ *n. & adj. | 名词 & 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) The lowest adult male singing voice; a musical instrument that produces low-pitched sounds; a type of freshwater or saltwater fish; (adj.) low in pitch or tone.

**中文：** (n.) 男低音；低音乐器；鲈鱼；(adj.) 低音的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- baritone （男中音）
- low-pitched （低音调的）
- deep-toned （低沉的）
- perch （鲈鱼） [fish sense]

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Note the two different pronunciations: /beɪs/ when referring to music (rhymes with "face") and /bæs/ when referring to the fish (rhymes with "class"). In musical contexts, "bass" refers to the lowest range of notes, instruments, or voices. "Bass guitar," "bass clef," and "bass voice" are common collocations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bass** vocalist captivated the audience with his remarkably deep and resonant performance.
   中文：这位男低音歌手以其极为低沉而洪亮的表演征服了观众。

2. The angler caught a magnificent striped **bass** while fishing near the rocky coastal shoreline.
   中文：钓鱼者在岩石海岸线附近钓到了一条漂亮的条纹鲈鱼。

3. The composer wrote a striking **bass** line that provided a powerful foundation for the symphony.
   中文：作曲家写了一段引人注目的低音旋律，为交响乐奠定了有力的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

For the music meaning: "bass" looks like "base" — the bass voice is the base (foundation) of a choir, providing the lowest, deepest sound. For the fish meaning: picture a fish with a big mouth making a low "baaass" sound underwater.

音乐义记忆："bass"看起来像"base"（基础）——男低音是合唱团的基础，提供最低沉的声音。鱼义记忆：想象一条大嘴鱼在水中发出低沉的"baaass"声。
