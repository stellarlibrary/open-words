# 📜 scribe

🔊 /skraɪb/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A copyist; a writer; a marking tool. (v.) To write down or record; to mark with a scribing tool
**中文：** n. 抄写员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- copyist（抄写员）
- clerk（书记员）
- secretary（秘书）
- transcriber（誊写员）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **royal scribe / temple scribe / scribe a manuscript / work as a scribe**

👉 Example: In ancient Egypt, the scribe held an honored position within the royal court.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medieval **scribe** spent countless hours meticulously copying sacred texts by hand in the dimly lit monastery scriptorium.
   中世纪的抄写员在昏暗的修道院缮写室里花费了无数小时一丝不苟地手抄圣典。

2. Before the invention of the printing press, a trained **scribe** was essential for preserving and disseminating written knowledge.
   在印刷术发明之前，训练有素的抄写员对于保存和传播文字知识至关重要。

3. The carpenter used a sharp tool to **scribe** a precise line along the wooden panel before making the final cut.
   木匠在最终切割之前用锋利的工具在木板上划出了一条精确的线。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scribe" as sharing the root with "describe," "prescribe," and "inscribe" — all related to writing or marking.
把"scribe"联想到与"describe"（描述）、"prescribe"（开处方）和"inscribe"（铭刻）共有的词根——都与书写或标记有关。
