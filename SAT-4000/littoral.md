# 🏖️ littoral

🔊 /ˈlɪtərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or situated on the shore of a sea, lake, or ocean. (n.) A coastal region or zone
**中文：** adj. 沿海的；海滨的 n. 沿海地区

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coastal（沿海的）
- shoreline（海岸线）
- seaside（海滨的）
- waterfront（滨水的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **littoral zone / littoral region / littoral waters / littoral habitat**

👉 Example: Marine biologists conducted extensive research on organisms inhabiting the littoral zone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **littoral** zone of the lake supports a diverse ecosystem of plants, insects, and amphibian species.
   该湖泊的沿岸区域支撑着一个由植物、昆虫和两栖物种组成的多样化生态系统。

2. Naval forces patrolled the **littoral** waters to prevent unauthorized vessels from approaching the sovereign coastline.
   海军部队在沿海水域巡逻，以防止未经授权的船只接近主权海岸线。

3. Climate change has dramatically altered the **littoral** habitats along the Mediterranean, threatening numerous endangered species.
   气候变化极大地改变了地中海沿岸的滨海栖息地，威胁着众多濒危物种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "littoral" as "litt-oral" — sounds like "literal shore," the literal edge where land meets water.
把"littoral"想成"litt（岸）+ oral（口）"——在海岸边口述所见，联想到沿海地区。
