# 🌍 pluralism

🔊 /ˈplʊrəlɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A political or social system that embraces diversity and allows multiple groups, principles, or sources of authority to coexist; the practice of holding more than one office simultaneously.
**中文：** n. 多元主义；多元论；兼任

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diversity（多样性）
- multiculturalism（多元文化主义）
- inclusiveness（包容性）
- heterogeneity（异质性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cultural pluralism / political pluralism / religious pluralism / embrace pluralism**

👉 Example: The constitution enshrines pluralism as a core value to protect the rights of all citizens.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation's commitment to **pluralism** ensures that ethnic minorities can preserve their traditions while participating fully in civic life.
   该国对**多元主义**的承诺确保少数民族能够在充分参与公民生活的同时保留其传统。

2. Academic **pluralism** encourages scholars to approach complex problems from multiple theoretical frameworks rather than a single perspective.
   学术**多元化**鼓励学者从多种理论框架而非单一视角来研究复杂问题。

3. Critics argued that **pluralism** in the judiciary weakens efficiency, as judges holding multiple positions cannot devote full attention.
   批评者认为司法机构中的**兼任**削弱了效率，因为兼任多个职务的法官无法全身心投入。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pluralism" as "plural" + "ism" — the ideology of "plural," embracing many different groups and ideas at once.
把"pluralism"想成"plural"（复数的）+"ism"（主义）——"复数"的意识形态，同时拥抱许多不同的群体和思想。
