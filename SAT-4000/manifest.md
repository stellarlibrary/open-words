# 🔍 manifest

🔊 /ˈmænɪfɛst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb, noun | 形容词, 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Clear and obvious to the eye or mind. (v.) To show or demonstrate plainly; to prove. (n.) A list of cargo or passengers carried on a ship or aircraft
**中文：** adj. 明白的，显然的 v. 表明，证明，显示 n. 载货单

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- evident（明显的）
- apparent（显而易见的）
- demonstrate（证明）
- obvious（明白的）
- palpable（可感知的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **manifest destiny / manifest itself / manifest symptoms / cargo manifest**

👉 Example: The tension between the two nations began to manifest itself through increasingly hostile diplomatic exchanges.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The flaws in the proposed legislation became **manifest** once independent analysts reviewed the economic projections in detail.
   一旦独立分析师详细审查了经济预测，拟议立法中的缺陷就变得显而易见了。

2. Chronic stress can **manifest** as a range of physical symptoms, including headaches, fatigue, and elevated blood pressure levels.
   慢性压力可以表现为一系列身体症状，包括头痛、疲劳和血压升高。

3. The customs officer carefully examined the ship's **manifest** to verify that all declared cargo matched the actual contents aboard.
   海关官员仔细检查了船舶的载货单，以核实所有申报货物与船上实际物品是否一致。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mani-fest" as a "festival for your hands (mani)" — at a festival, everything is on display and clearly visible, just as something manifest is plain to see.
把"manifest"想成"手的节日（mani-fest）"——在节日上，一切都展示出来清晰可见，正如manifest的事物一目了然。
