# ➖ minus

🔊 /ˈmaɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *preposition, noun, adjective | 介词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (prep.) With the subtraction of; less. (n.) A minus sign; a disadvantage. (adj.) Involving or indicating subtraction; negative; slightly below a given grade.
**中文：** prep. 减，减去 n. 负号，减号；不足 adj. 表示减的，负的；阴性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- less（减去）
- negative（负的）
- without（没有）
- deficiency（不足）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **minus sign / on the minus side / minus ten degrees / a minus factor / plus or minus**

👉 Example: The temperature dropped to minus fifteen degrees Celsius during the unprecedented winter storm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The final budget calculation showed revenues of two million dollars **minus** operational expenses left a surprisingly narrow profit margin.
   最终预算计算显示，两百万美元的收入减去运营费用后，利润率出人意料地低。

2. On the **minus** side, the proposed merger would result in significant workforce reductions across multiple regional departments.
   不利的一面是，拟议的合并将导致多个区域部门大幅裁员。

3. The spacecraft must withstand temperatures of **minus** two hundred degrees in the harsh vacuum environment of outer space.
   航天器必须在外太空严酷的真空环境中承受零下两百度的温度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "minus" as "mine-us" — something taken from us, removed or subtracted, just like the minus sign takes away a number.
把"minus"想成"mine-us（从我们这里拿走）"——被拿走、被减去的东西，就像减号会减去一个数字。
