# 🔢 triple

🔊 /ˈtrɪpəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Consisting of three parts or three times as much. (v.) To become three times as great or numerous
**中文：** adj. 三倍的；三重的 v. 增至三倍

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- threefold（三倍的）
- treble（三倍的）
- triplicate（三重的）
- tripartite（三方的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **triple the amount / triple digits / triple threat / triple in size**

👉 Example: The company's revenue has tripled since the launch of its innovative product line.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university announced a plan to **triple** its scholarship funding for underprivileged students over the next decade.
   该大学宣布了一项计划，在未来十年内将贫困学生的奖学金资助增加两倍。

2. Scientists observed a **triple** increase in atmospheric carbon levels compared to preindustrial measurements from the eighteenth century.
   科学家观察到大气碳含量与十八世纪工业化前测量值相比增长了三倍。

3. The population of the metropolitan area has **tripled** since the construction of the new transportation infrastructure was completed.
   自新交通基础设施建成以来，该都市区的人口已增至三倍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tri-ple" — "tri" means three, so triple means three times as much, like a triangle has three sides.
把"triple"想成"tri（三）+ ple"——"tri"表示三，所以triple就是三倍，就像triangle（三角形）有三条边一样。
