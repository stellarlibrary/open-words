# 🔧 versatile

🔊 /ˈvɜːrsətaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to adapt or be adapted to many different functions, activities, or situations; having many skills or talents
**中文：** adj. 多才多艺的；通用的；万能的；多面手的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adaptable（适应性强的）
- flexible（灵活的）
- multitalented（多才多艺的）
- all-around（全面的）
- resourceful（足智多谋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **versatile performer / versatile tool / versatile material / remarkably versatile**

👉 Example: The versatile actress seamlessly transitioned between comedic and dramatic roles throughout her distinguished career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new software platform proved remarkably **versatile**, serving the needs of engineers, designers, and project managers simultaneously.
   这个新软件平台被证明是非常通用的，能同时满足工程师、设计师和项目经理的需求。

2. Leonardo da Vinci remains one of history's most **versatile** intellects, excelling equally in painting, engineering, anatomy, and mathematics.
   达芬奇仍然是历史上最多才多艺的智者之一，在绘画、工程、解剖学和数学方面同样出色。

3. Bamboo is a **versatile** natural resource used in construction, textile production, culinary applications, and sustainable environmental engineering.
   竹子是一种用途广泛的自然资源，用于建筑、纺织生产、烹饪应用和可持续环境工程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "versa-tile" — "versa" means to turn (as in "vice versa"). A versatile person can turn their hand to anything, adapting in every direction.
把"versatile"想成"versa-tile"——"versa"意为转动（如"vice versa"）。一个versatile的人能转向任何方向，适应各种情况。
