# 💧 condensation

🔊 /ˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process by which a gas or vapor changes into a liquid; water droplets formed on a surface; the act of making something more concise or compressed
**中文：** n. 冷凝；凝结；凝结物；压缩；浓缩

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- liquefaction（液化）
- precipitation（沉降）
- compression（压缩）
- distillation（蒸馏）
- abridgment（缩减）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **condensation on / condensation of / water condensation / condensation point / condensation reaction**

👉 Example: Tiny droplets of condensation formed on the cold glass surface as warm air came into contact with it.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The formation of **condensation** on the laboratory glass indicated that the ambient temperature had dropped below the dew point.
   实验室玻璃上凝结物的形成表明环境温度已降至露点以下。

2. In organic chemistry, a **condensation** reaction occurs when two molecules combine to form a larger molecule while releasing water.
   在有机化学中，缩合反应是指两个分子结合形成一个较大分子并同时释放出水的过程。

3. The editor produced a skillful **condensation** of the lengthy research report, preserving all essential findings in a concise format.
   编辑对这份冗长的研究报告进行了巧妙的压缩，以简洁的形式保留了所有重要发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "condense + ation" — when steam condenses on a cold window, those water droplets are condensation. It means making dense or compact.
把"condensation"想成"condense（凝结）+ ation"——当蒸汽在冷窗上凝结时，那些水滴就是condensation。它意味着变得浓密或紧凑。
