# 🍋 acerbity

🔊 /əˈsɜːrbɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Sharpness or severity of temper, manner, or tone; sourness or astringency of taste.
**中文：** 酸，涩；刻薄

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harshness（严厉）
- asperity（粗暴）
- acrimony（尖刻）
- tartness（酸涩）
- mordancy（辛辣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acerbity of tone / manner / speech / wit**

👉 Example: The acerbity of her remarks caught everyone off guard during the debate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **acerbity** in his voice revealed deep frustration with the committee's repeated delays and indecision.
   他声音中的刻薄透露出他对委员会一再拖延和优柔寡断的深深不满。

2. Literary critics noted the unusual **acerbity** of the novelist's satirical portrayal of aristocratic society.
   文学评论家注意到这位小说家对贵族社会讽刺描写中不同寻常的尖刻。

3. Despite the **acerbity** of the fruit, local farmers transformed it into a delicacy prized across the region.
   尽管这种水果很酸涩，当地农民还是将其制成了全地区闻名的美食。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Acerbity" is the noun form of "acerbic." Think: acerb- (sour) + -ity (quality) = the quality of being sharp or sour.
"acerbity"是"acerbic"的名词形式。记忆：acerb-（酸的）+ -ity（性质）= 酸涩或刻薄的性质。
