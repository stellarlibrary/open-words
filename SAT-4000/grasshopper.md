# 🦗 grasshopper

🔊 /ˈɡræsˌhɑːpər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A plant-eating insect with long hind legs used for jumping, typically producing a chirping sound
**中文：** n. 蚱蜢；蝗虫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- locust（蝗虫）
- cricket（蟋蟀）
- katydid（螽斯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **swarm of grasshoppers / grasshopper plague / grasshopper species / like a grasshopper**

👉 Example: The grasshopper leaped effortlessly from one blade of grass to another in the meadow.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A massive swarm of **grasshoppers** devastated the agricultural region, destroying crops that farmers had cultivated for an entire season.
   大批蝗虫摧毁了该农业区，毁掉了农民整个季节辛苦种植的庄稼。

2. The biologist studied the **grasshopper** population to understand how environmental changes influenced insect migration patterns across the plains.
   这位生物学家研究了蚱蜢种群，以了解环境变化如何影响昆虫在平原上的迁徙模式。

3. In the famous fable, the idle **grasshopper** spent the summer singing while the industrious ant diligently prepared for winter.
   在那则著名的寓言中，懒惰的蚱蜢整个夏天都在歌唱，而勤劳的蚂蚁则在勤勉地为冬天做准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grass-hopper" literally — it is an insect that hops through the grass. The name perfectly describes its behavior.
把"grasshopper"拆开来看——"grass（草）"加"hopper（跳跃者）"，就是在草丛中跳来跳去的虫子。
