# 💎 invaluable

🔊 /ɪnˈvæljuəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely useful or precious; so valuable that its worth cannot be measured
**中文：** adj. 非常宝贵的；无价的；极其有用的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- priceless（无价的）
- indispensable（不可或缺的）
- precious（珍贵的）
- irreplaceable（不可替代的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **invaluable resource / invaluable experience / invaluable contribution / prove invaluable**

👉 Example: Her expertise in data analysis proved invaluable to the success of the research project.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mentor's guidance was **invaluable** to the young entrepreneur, helping her navigate the complexities of launching a new business.
   导师的指导对这位年轻企业家来说是无价的，帮助她应对了创业的种种复杂问题。

2. Access to primary historical documents has proven **invaluable** for scholars seeking to reconstruct events of the medieval period.
   对于试图重建中世纪事件的学者来说，获取原始历史文献已被证明是极其宝贵的。

3. The volunteer translators provided an **invaluable** service during the international relief effort following the devastating earthquake.
   在毁灭性地震后的国际救援行动中，志愿翻译人员提供了极其宝贵的服务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-valu-able" — not as "not valuable" but as "impossible to value" because it is worth so much that no price can capture it.
把"invaluable"理解为"无法估价的"——不是"没有价值"，而是"价值高到无法衡量"。
