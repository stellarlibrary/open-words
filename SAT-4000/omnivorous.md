# 🍴 omnivorous

🔊 /ɑːmˈnɪvərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeding on food of both plant and animal origin; having very broad interests or tastes, especially in reading or learning
**中文：** adj. 杂食的；什么都读的；无所不吃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- all-consuming（无所不包的）
- indiscriminate（不加区分的）
- varied（多样的）
- eclectic（兼收并蓄的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **omnivorous diet / omnivorous reader / omnivorous appetite / omnivorous habits / omnivorous in taste**

👉 Example: She was an omnivorous reader who devoured everything from classical literature to modern scientific journals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **omnivorous** feeding habits of pigs make them remarkably adaptable to a wide range of agricultural environments.
   猪的杂食习性使它们能够非常好地适应各种农业环境。

2. As an **omnivorous** scholar, the professor consumed texts from philosophy, physics, art history, and mathematics with equal enthusiasm.
   作为一位博览群书的学者，这位教授以同样的热情阅读哲学、物理学、艺术史和数学方面的著作。

3. The research team studied the **omnivorous** tendencies of certain fish species to understand their impact on freshwater ecosystems.
   研究团队研究了某些鱼类的杂食性，以了解它们对淡水生态系统的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "omnivorous" as "omni" (all) + "vor" (eat) + "-ous" (full of) — full of eating everything, whether food or knowledge.
把"omnivorous"想成"omni（所有）"加"vor（吃）"加"-ous（充满）"——充满了吃一切东西的特性，无论是食物还是知识。
