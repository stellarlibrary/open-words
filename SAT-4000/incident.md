# 🚨 incident

🔊 /ˈɪnsɪdənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An event or occurrence, especially one that is notable or causes trouble. (adj.) Falling upon or striking; related or attached.
**中文：** n. 事件，事变；插曲 adj. 入射的；附带的；易发生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- occurrence（事件）
- event（事件）
- episode（插曲）
- happening（发生的事）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a serious incident / a minor incident / without incident / incident report**

👉 Example: The border incident nearly escalated into a full-scale diplomatic crisis between the two nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The security team filed a detailed report documenting every aspect of the **incident** that occurred on campus.
   安保团队提交了一份详细报告，记录了校园内发生的这起事件的各个方面。

2. Several minor **incidents** of academic dishonesty prompted the university to revise its examination policies significantly.
   几起轻微的学术不诚实事件促使该大学大幅修改了其考试政策。

3. The diplomatic **incident** at the embassy strained relations between the two countries for the remainder of the year.
   大使馆的外交事件使两国关系在该年余下时间内持续紧张。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-ci-dent" as "in" + "ci(ty)" + "dent" — something that makes a dent in a city is a notable incident.
把"incident"想成"in" + "ci(ty)" + "dent"——在城市中留下凹痕的事就是一件引人注目的事件。
