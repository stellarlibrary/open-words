# 🏃 agile

🔊 /ˈædʒ.aɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to move quickly and easily; mentally sharp and adaptive.
**中文：** 敏捷的；机敏的；活泼的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nimble（灵活的）
- deft（灵巧的）
- lithe（柔软的）
- sprightly（活泼的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **agile mind, agile movements, agile thinker, agile approach**
👉 Example: An agile mind is essential for solving complex problems under pressure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **agile** gymnast executed a flawless routine that captivated the entire audience in the arena.
   那位敏捷的体操运动员完成了一套完美的动作，吸引了整个场馆的观众。

2. Successful leaders must remain **agile** in their thinking to adapt to rapidly changing market conditions.
   成功的领导者必须保持思维敏捷，以适应快速变化的市场环境。

3. The **agile** cat leaped effortlessly from one rooftop to another during the quiet evening hours.
   那只灵活的猫在宁静的傍晚轻松地从一个屋顶跳到另一个屋顶。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "agile" as "a-JILE" — like a "jail" you can escape from quickly because you are so fast and flexible.
把"agile"想成能快速逃出"jail"（监狱）——因为你足够敏捷灵活。
