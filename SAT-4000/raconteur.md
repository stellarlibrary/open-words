# 🎤 raconteur

🔊 /ˌrækɑːnˈtɜːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who tells anecdotes in a skillful and amusing way; a gifted storyteller.
**中文：** n. 健谈者；善谈者；擅长讲故事的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- storyteller（讲故事的人）
- narrator（叙述者）
- anecdotalist（讲轶事的人）
- yarn-spinner（善编故事的人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a gifted raconteur / a brilliant raconteur / a famous raconteur / an accomplished raconteur**

👉 Example: The celebrated author proved to be a gifted raconteur who captivated audiences at every literary event.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished professor was a renowned **raconteur** whose lectures drew students from across the entire university campus.
   这位杰出的教授是一位著名的善谈者，他的讲座吸引了整个大学校园的学生。

2. As a skilled **raconteur**, the diplomat could defuse tense negotiations by weaving humorous anecdotes into the conversation.
   作为一位技巧娴熟的健谈者，这位外交官能够通过在对话中穿插幽默轶事来缓解紧张的谈判。

3. The memoir reveals the writer to be a natural **raconteur**, transforming ordinary childhood memories into compelling narratives.
   这部回忆录展示了作者是一位天生的故事高手，将普通的童年记忆转化为引人入胜的叙事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "raconteur" as "recount + er" — a person who recounts stories so well that everyone wants to listen.
把"raconteur"想成"recount + er"——一个把故事讲述得如此精彩、人人都想倾听的人。
