# 🔮 clairvoyance

🔊 /klɛrˈvɔɪəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The supposed faculty of perceiving things beyond the normal senses; exceptional insight or intuition
**中文：** n. 千里眼；透视；异常的洞察力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insight（洞察力）
- prescience（先见之明）
- foresight（远见）
- intuition（直觉）
- perception（感知力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **possess clairvoyance / demonstrate clairvoyance / a moment of clairvoyance / political clairvoyance**

👉 Example: Her remarkable clairvoyance about market trends astonished even the most seasoned investors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The analyst's apparent **clairvoyance** in predicting the financial crisis earned her considerable recognition among economists.
   这位分析师预测金融危机时表现出的超凡洞察力为她赢得了经济学家们的高度认可。

2. No amount of **clairvoyance** could have prepared the committee for the unprecedented challenges that followed the reform.
   再强的洞察力也无法让委员会为改革之后的空前挑战做好准备。

3. Some historians attribute a kind of **clairvoyance** to leaders who anticipated sweeping social transformations decades in advance.
   一些历史学家将一种洞察力归于那些提前数十年预见重大社会变革的领导者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clair-voyance" from French "clair" (clear) + "voir" (to see) — the ability to see things clearly beyond normal vision.
把"clairvoyance"拆成法语"clair"（清楚）+ "voir"（看见）——超越常人视觉的清晰洞察能力。
