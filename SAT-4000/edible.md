# 🍽️ edible

🔊 /ˈɛdɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Fit or suitable to be eaten; safe for consumption. (n.) An item of food
**中文：** adj. 可食用的；可以吃的 n. 食品；可食之物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consumable（可消费的）
- palatable（可口的）
- digestible（可消化的）
- wholesome（有益健康的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **edible plants / edible mushrooms / barely edible / edible portion / edible flowers**

👉 Example: Only a trained forager can reliably distinguish edible wild mushrooms from their toxic counterparts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The survival manual included detailed illustrations to help readers identify **edible** plants found commonly in temperate forest regions.
   这本生存手册包含了详细的插图，帮助读者识别温带森林地区常见的可食用植物。

2. Despite its unappetizing appearance, the fruit proved perfectly **edible** and was considered a delicacy in many tropical cultures.
   尽管这种水果外观不太诱人，但它完全可以食用，在许多热带文化中被视为美味佳肴。

3. Researchers developed an **edible** packaging film derived from seaweed that could significantly reduce plastic waste in the food industry.
   研究人员开发了一种源自海藻的可食用包装薄膜，可以显著减少食品行业的塑料废弃物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ed-ible" as "ed(eat)-ible(able)" — something that is able to be eaten, directly from the Latin "edere" meaning "to eat."
把"edible"想成"ed（吃）-ible（能够）"——能够被吃的东西，直接来自拉丁语"edere"，意为"吃"。
