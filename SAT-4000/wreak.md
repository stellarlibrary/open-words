# 💥 wreak

🔊 /riːk/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause a large amount of damage or harm; to inflict vengeance or punishment
**中文：** v. 发泄；报仇；造成（巨大的破坏或伤害）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inflict（施加）
- unleash（释放）
- cause（造成）
- bring about（导致）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wreak havoc / wreak destruction / wreak vengeance / wreak damage**

👉 Example: The hurricane wreaked havoc on coastal communities, leaving thousands of families displaced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The invasive species **wreaked** ecological havoc on the fragile island ecosystem within just a few short years.
   这种入侵物种在短短几年内就对脆弱的岛屿生态系统造成了生态浩劫。

2. Critics argued that the new economic policy would **wreak** lasting damage on small businesses and rural communities.
   批评人士认为，新的经济政策将对小企业和农村社区造成持久的损害。

3. The cyberattack **wreaked** unprecedented destruction on the nation's critical infrastructure, disrupting services for several weeks.
   这次网络攻击对该国关键基础设施造成了前所未有的破坏，导致服务中断数周。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wreak" as "wreck" without the "c" — to wreak havoc is to wreck everything in sight.
把"wreak"想成没有"c"的"wreck（破坏）"——wreak havoc就是把眼前的一切都摧毁。
