# 📁 archive

🔊 /ˈɑːrkaɪv/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A collection of historical documents or records; the place where they are kept. (v.) To store or preserve documents and records systematically
**中文：** n. 档案馆；档案 v. 存档；归档

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- repository（储存库）
- records（档案记录）
- chronicle（编年史）
- catalog（目录）
- store（储存）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **national archive / archive material / digital archive / archive documents / archived records**

👉 Example: The university decided to archive all historical manuscripts in a climate-controlled vault for preservation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers spent months examining the national **archive** to uncover classified documents related to the wartime intelligence operations.
   研究人员花了数月时间查阅国家档案馆，以发掘与战时情报行动相关的机密文件。

2. The library plans to **archive** its entire collection of rare photographs using state-of-the-art digital scanning technology.
   图书馆计划使用最先进的数字扫描技术将其全部珍贵照片收藏进行存档。

3. The newly discovered **archive** contained personal letters that revealed intimate details about the author's creative process.
   新发现的档案中包含私人信件，揭示了这位作家创作过程中的私密细节。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arc-hive" — imagine a "hive" (like a beehive) shaped in an "arc," buzzing with stored information and records, just like an archive stores documents.
把"archive"想成"arc（拱形）+ hive（蜂巢）"——想象一个拱形蜂巢里嗡嗡地存满了信息和记录，就像档案馆储存文件一样。
