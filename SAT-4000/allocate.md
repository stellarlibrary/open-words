# 📊 allocate

🔊 /ˈæləˌkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To distribute or apportion resources, money, or duties for a particular purpose
**中文：** 分配；拨出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assign（分配）
- distribute（分发）
- apportion（分摊）
- designate（指定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **allocate funds / allocate resources / allocate time / allocate budget to**

👉 Example: The committee voted to allocate additional funds to the public education initiative.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board of directors decided to **allocate** a substantial portion of the annual budget to research and technological development.
   董事会决定将年度预算的很大一部分分配给研究和技术开发。

2. The manager was tasked with determining how to **allocate** limited resources efficiently among the department's three competing project teams.
   经理的任务是确定如何在部门内三个相互竞争的项目团队之间有效分配有限的资源。

3. The federal government plans to **allocate** emergency relief funds to the communities most severely affected by the recent natural disaster.
   联邦政府计划向最近自然灾害中受灾最严重的社区拨出紧急救济资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "allocate" as "all-locate" — you locate where all the resources should go and place them accordingly.
把"allocate"想成"all-locate"——你找到所有资源应该去的地方，然后相应地分配它们。
