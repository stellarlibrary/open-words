# 🔲 medium

🔊 /ˈmiːdiəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Between two extremes; intermediate in size, amount, or degree. (n.) A means of communication or expression; a surrounding substance or environment; a middle state
**中文：** adj. 中间的，中等的 n. 方法；媒体；媒介；介质；中间物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intermediate（中间的）
- average（平均的）
- channel（渠道）
- vehicle（媒介）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **medium size / through the medium of / artistic medium / a happy medium**

👉 Example: Television became the dominant medium for political communication in the twentieth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist chose watercolor as her preferred **medium** because it allowed for subtle gradations of light and color.
   这位艺术家选择水彩作为她偏爱的媒介，因为它能表现光线和色彩的微妙变化。

2. Researchers discovered that bacteria thrive in a nutrient-rich **medium** maintained at a consistently warm temperature.
   研究人员发现细菌在保持恒定温暖温度的富营养培养基中大量繁殖。

3. The committee sought a happy **medium** between strict regulation and complete deregulation of the industry.
   委员会寻求在严格监管和完全放松管制之间找到一个折中方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "medium" as "medi(middle) + um" — something in the middle, not too big, not too small, or a middle channel for information.
把"medium"想成"medi（中间）+ um"——处于中间的事物，不大不小，或者是传递信息的中间渠道。
