# 🌱 sustainable

🔊 /səˈsteɪnəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being maintained at a steady level; able to be upheld or supported over the long term; environmentally responsible.
**中文：** adj. 可维持的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- maintainable（可维持的）
- viable（可行的）
- enduring（持久的）
- renewable（可再生的）
- tenable（站得住脚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sustainable development / sustainable energy / sustainable growth / environmentally sustainable / sustainable practices**

👉 Example: The company adopted sustainable manufacturing processes to reduce its carbon footprint significantly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The United Nations has established ambitious goals for **sustainable** development to address global poverty and environmental degradation.
   联合国制定了雄心勃勃的可持续发展目标，以应对全球贫困和环境退化问题。

2. Economists argue that the current rate of resource consumption is not **sustainable** without significant technological innovation.
   经济学家认为，如果没有重大技术创新，当前的资源消耗速度是不可持续的。

3. The city invested heavily in **sustainable** public transportation to reduce emissions and improve urban air quality.
   该市大力投资可持续的公共交通，以减少排放并改善城市空气质量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sustain-able" — something you are "able" to "sustain" or keep going without running out of resources.
把"sustainable"想成"sustain-able"——你"能够"（able）"维持"（sustain）的东西，不会耗尽资源。
