# 🧱 dullard

🔊 /ˈdʌlərd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A slow-witted or stupid person; someone who is mentally dull and lacking in intelligence
**中文：** n. 笨蛋；愚人；迟钝的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dunce（笨蛋）
- simpleton（傻瓜）
- blockhead（蠢人）
- dimwit（笨蛋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a complete dullard / no mere dullard / dismissed as a dullard / take someone for a dullard**

👉 Example: The professor refused to dismiss any student as a dullard, believing everyone had potential.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literary critic characterized the protagonist as a well-meaning **dullard** who stumbled through life without genuine self-awareness.
   文学评论家将主人公描述为一个善意的笨蛋，他在没有真正自我意识的情况下跌跌撞撞地过着生活。

2. Only a **dullard** would fail to recognize the obvious logical fallacies embedded within the politician's misleading rhetorical arguments.
   只有笨蛋才会无法识别出政客误导性修辞论点中明显的逻辑谬误。

3. His colleagues mistakenly regarded him as a **dullard**, unaware that his quiet demeanor concealed a remarkably sharp analytical mind.
   他的同事们错误地把他当成一个笨蛋，却不知道他安静的举止下隐藏着极其敏锐的分析头脑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dullard" as "dull + ard" — someone whose mind is "dull," with the suffix "-ard" indicating a person (like drunkard), so a dullard is a dull person.
把"dullard"想成"dull + ard"——头脑"迟钝的"人，后缀"-ard"表示人（如drunkard醉鬼），所以dullard就是迟钝的人。
