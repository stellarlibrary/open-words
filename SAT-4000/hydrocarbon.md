# 🛢️ hydrocarbon

🔊 /ˌhaɪdrəˈkɑːrbən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An organic chemical compound consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon atoms, commonly found in fossil fuels
**中文：** n. 碳氢化合物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- organic compound（有机化合物）
- fossil fuel component（化石燃料成分）
- petroleum derivative（石油衍生物）
- alkane（烷烃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hydrocarbon emissions / hydrocarbon fuel / aromatic hydrocarbon / hydrocarbon chain / unburned hydrocarbons**

👉 Example: The combustion of hydrocarbons releases both carbon dioxide and water vapor into the atmosphere.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental scientists measured elevated levels of **hydrocarbon** pollution in the coastal waters following the offshore drilling accident.
   环境科学家在近海钻探事故后测量到沿海水域碳氢化合物污染水平升高。

2. The organic chemistry course began with a thorough examination of simple **hydrocarbon** structures such as methane and ethane.
   有机化学课程从对甲烷和乙烷等简单碳氢化合物结构的全面研究开始。

3. Researchers are developing catalytic methods to convert **hydrocarbon** waste into commercially valuable chemical products and clean energy.
   研究人员正在开发催化方法，将碳氢化合物废料转化为有商业价值的化学产品和清洁能源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydro-carbon" as literally "hydrogen + carbon" — the two elements that make up these compounds.
把"hydrocarbon"直接拆开看——"hydro"（氢）加"carbon"（碳），就是由这两种元素组成的化合物。
