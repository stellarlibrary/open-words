# 🧠 ratiocination

🔊 /ˌrætiˌɒsɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of logical and methodical reasoning; the forming of judgments through a chain of deductive thought
**中文：** n. 推理；推论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reasoning（推理）
- deduction（推论）
- logic（逻辑）
- deliberation（深思熟虑）
- inference（推断）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **process of ratiocination / careful ratiocination / powers of ratiocination / through ratiocination**

👉 Example: The detective solved the case through meticulous ratiocination rather than relying on intuition alone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher's treatise demonstrated extraordinary powers of **ratiocination** in constructing arguments from first principles to conclusions.
   这位哲学家的论文展示了从第一原理到结论的非凡推理能力。

2. Edgar Allan Poe celebrated the art of **ratiocination** through his detective character who solved crimes using pure logical deduction.
   埃德加·爱伦·坡通过他的侦探角色颂扬了推理的艺术，该角色运用纯粹的逻辑推理来破案。

3. The legal scholar's **ratiocination** led her to an interpretation of the statute that no previous commentator had considered.
   这位法律学者的推理使她对该法规得出了此前没有评论者考虑过的解释。

把"ratiocination"想成"ratio（比率）+ nation（国度）"——一个建立在比率和逻辑上的国度；指系统地推理事物的过程。
