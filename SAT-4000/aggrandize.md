# 👑 aggrandize

🔊 /əˈɡrændaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To increase the power, status, or wealth of; to make something appear greater than it actually is; to exaggerate
**中文：** 增加；夸大；强化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exalt（提升）
- amplify（扩大）
- magnify（夸大）
- inflate（膨胀）
- elevate（提高）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **self-aggrandize / aggrandize power / aggrandize one's reputation / aggrandize wealth**

👉 Example: The dictator sought to aggrandize his authority by systematically eliminating all political opposition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious senator attempted to **aggrandize** her political influence by forging strategic alliances with several powerful industrial leaders.
   这位雄心勃勃的参议员试图通过与几位有权势的工业领袖结成战略联盟来扩大她的政治影响力。

2. Critics accused the biographer of attempting to **aggrandize** the minor historical figure into a national hero without sufficient evidence.
   批评人士指责传记作者试图在没有充分证据的情况下将这位次要历史人物夸大为民族英雄。

3. The corporation used aggressive marketing campaigns to **aggrandize** its brand image in the highly competitive international marketplace.
   该公司利用激进的营销活动在竞争激烈的国际市场上提升其品牌形象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ag-GRAND-ize" — the word "grand" is hidden inside, meaning to make something grander or more impressive.
想想"ag-GRAND-ize"——里面藏着"grand"（宏大的），意思是使某事物变得更宏大或更令人印象深刻。
