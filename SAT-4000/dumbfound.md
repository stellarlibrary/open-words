# 😲 dumbfound

🔊 /dʌmˈfaʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To strike speechless with amazement; to astonish or confound to the point of being unable to respond
**中文：** v. 使惊呆；使人哑然失声；使目瞪口呆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astound（使惊骇）
- stagger（使震惊）
- stupefy（使惊呆）
- flabbergast（使目瞪口呆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dumbfounded by / completely dumbfounded / dumbfound the audience / left dumbfounded**

👉 Example: The unexpected verdict dumbfounded everyone present in the courtroom that afternoon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The magician's final illusion **dumbfounded** the entire audience, leaving them in stunned silence for several prolonged seconds.
   魔术师最后的幻术使全场观众惊呆了，让他们陷入了好几秒钟的震惊沉默。

2. Scientists were completely **dumbfounded** when the experimental data contradicted every established theoretical prediction in the published literature.
   当实验数据与已发表文献中所有既定的理论预测相矛盾时，科学家们完全惊呆了。

3. The diplomat's sudden resignation **dumbfounded** senior officials who had considered her the most likely candidate for the ambassadorship.
   这位外交官的突然辞职使高级官员们目瞪口呆，他们一直认为她是大使职位最可能的人选。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dumbfound" as "dumb + found" — you are so shocked that you are "found dumb," rendered speechless and unable to utter a word.
把"dumbfound"想成"dumb + found"——你的震惊程度大到"被发现说不出话"，哑然失声，一个字也说不出来。
