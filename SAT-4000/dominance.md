# 👑 dominance

🔊 /ˈdɑːmɪnəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of having power, authority, or influence over others; the condition of being dominant or superior
**中文：** n. 优势；统治；支配地位

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supremacy（霸权）
- authority（权威）
- preeminence（卓越）
- ascendancy（优势）
- hegemony（霸权）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **market dominance / global dominance / political dominance / establish dominance / cultural dominance**

👉 Example: The corporation's dominance in the technology sector has raised concerns about fair competition and antitrust regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The empire's military **dominance** over the region lasted for centuries before internal conflicts gradually weakened its strategic position.
   该帝国对该地区的军事统治持续了数个世纪，之后内部冲突逐渐削弱了其战略地位。

2. Researchers studied the **dominance** hierarchies among primates to better understand the evolutionary roots of social organization.
   研究人员研究了灵长类动物中的等级优势关系，以更好地理解社会组织的进化根源。

3. The country's economic **dominance** was largely attributed to its investment in education, technology, and advanced manufacturing industries.
   该国的经济优势在很大程度上归功于其对教育、技术和先进制造业的投资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "domin" (from Latin "dominus," meaning lord or master) + "ance" — dominance is the state of being the master or ruler.
想象"domin"（来自拉丁语"dominus"，意为主人或统治者）+ "ance"——dominance就是成为主人或统治者的状态。
