# 😞 penury

🔊 /ˈpenjʊri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extreme poverty; destitution; severe lack of resources
**中文：** n. 贫困；贫穷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- poverty（贫穷）
- destitution（赤贫）
- indigence（贫困）
- privation（匮乏）
- impoverishment（贫困）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **live in penury / reduced to penury / years of penury / state of penury**

👉 Example: The once-prosperous family was reduced to penury after a series of devastating financial losses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the economic collapse, millions of citizens were plunged into **penury** and could not afford basic necessities.
   经济崩溃后，数百万市民陷入贫困，无法负担基本生活必需品。

2. The novelist vividly depicted the **penury** of rural communities during the prolonged drought of the 1930s.
   小说家生动地描绘了二十世纪三十年代长期干旱期间农村社区的贫困状况。

3. Despite years of **penury**, the scholar persisted in his research and eventually published a groundbreaking treatise.
   尽管多年贫困，这位学者仍坚持研究，最终发表了一篇开创性的论文。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "penury" as "pen + ury (injury)" — poverty is an injury to your pen (wallet), leaving you with nothing to write checks with.
把"penury"想成"pen + ury (injury)"——贫穷就像对你的钱包的伤害，让你连开支票的本钱都没有。
