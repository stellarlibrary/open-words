# 🗣️ indiscreet

🔊 /ˌɪndɪˈskriːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having, showing, or proceeding from too little discretion; imprudent; lacking caution in speech or action
**中文：** adj. 轻率的；不慎重的；不谨慎的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imprudent（轻率的）
- tactless（不得体的）
- injudicious（不明智的）
- reckless（鲁莽的）
- careless（粗心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indiscreet remark / indiscreet behavior / indiscreet about / prove indiscreet**

👉 Example: The ambassador's indiscreet comments about the trade negotiations were leaked to the international press.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The executive's **indiscreet** disclosure of confidential merger details led to a formal investigation by the securities commission.
   这位高管不慎泄露了机密的并购细节，导致证券委员会对此展开正式调查。

2. Several **indiscreet** remarks made during the closed-door meeting were later published verbatim in the national newspaper.
   在闭门会议上发表的几句轻率言论后来被逐字刊登在全国性报纸上。

3. Historians note that the general's **indiscreet** correspondence with foreign officials ultimately compromised the entire military campaign.
   历史学家指出，这位将军与外国官员之间不慎重的通信最终损害了整个军事行动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-discreet" — the opposite of "discreet" — someone who is not discreet blurts out secrets without thinking.
把"indiscreet"想成"in-discreet"——"discreet（谨慎）"的反义词——一个不谨慎的人会不假思索地泄露秘密。
