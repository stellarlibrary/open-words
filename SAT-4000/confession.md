# 🙏 confession

🔊 /kənˈfɛʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A formal statement admitting that one is guilty of a crime or wrongdoing; an acknowledgment of one's sins or faults, especially in a religious context; a declaration of one's feelings or beliefs.

**中文：** 忏悔；告解；供认；承认；招供。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- admission（承认）
- acknowledgment（认可）
- declaration（声明）
- disclosure（揭露）
- avowal（公开声明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** make a confession / forced confession / confession of guilt

**Example:** The suspect's **confession** provided key evidence that resolved the prolonged investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant's voluntary **confession** was presented as the most compelling piece of evidence during the trial.
   被告的自愿供认被作为审判中最有说服力的证据提交。

2. In many religious traditions, **confession** serves as a spiritual practice through which individuals seek forgiveness and redemption.
   在许多宗教传统中，忏悔是一种灵性修行，个人通过它寻求宽恕和救赎。

3. Her heartfelt **confession** of admiration for the author's work moved the audience at the literary symposium.
   她在文学研讨会上对这位作家作品的由衷赞赏打动了在场的观众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Con-" (fully) + "fess" (from Latin fateri, to acknowledge) — to fully acknowledge something, especially guilt or sin.

**中文：** "con-"（完全地）+ "fess"（源自拉丁语 fateri，承认）——完全地承认某事，尤其是罪行或过错，即"忏悔、供认"。
