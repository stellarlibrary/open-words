# 🛡️ bulwark

🔊 /ˈbʊlwərk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A strong defensive wall or barrier; a person or thing that acts as a protection. (v.) To protect or fortify with a defensive structure
**中文：** n. 壁垒；保障；防波堤 v. 保护；筑垒保卫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rampart（城墙）
- bastion（堡垒）
- fortress（要塞）
- safeguard（保障）
- defense（防御）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a bulwark against / serve as a bulwark / the last bulwark of / bulwark of democracy**

👉 Example: A free press serves as a vital bulwark against the abuse of governmental power.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The constitution was designed to serve as a **bulwark** against tyranny and the concentration of power in any single branch.
   宪法旨在作为抵御暴政和权力集中于任何单一机构的壁垒。

2. The massive stone **bulwark** along the coastline protected the harbor from the destructive force of seasonal storm surges.
   沿海岸线的巨大石砌防波堤保护了港口免受季节性风暴潮的破坏力。

3. Many scholars regard an independent judiciary as the strongest **bulwark** of civil liberties in any democratic society.
   许多学者认为独立的司法机构是任何民主社会中公民自由的最强保障。

Think of "bull" + "wark (work)" — a bull working to build a strong wall of defense.
把"bulwark"想成"bull（公牛）+ wark（工作）"——一头公牛在努力建造一堵坚固的防御墙。
