# ✈️ aeronautics

🔊 /ˌɛərəˈnɔːtɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The science or practice of building and flying aircraft.
**中文：** 航空学；飞行术。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aviation（航空）
- flight science（飞行科学）
- aerodynamics（空气动力学）
- avionics（航空电子学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study, advance, modern, civil, field of**
👉 Example: She devoted her career to the study of aeronautics at the national research center.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Substantial progress in **aeronautics** during the past century has transformed global transportation entirely.
   过去一个世纪航空学的巨大进步彻底改变了全球交通运输。

2. The university established a distinguished department of **aeronautics** to train the next generation of engineers.
   该大学设立了一个杰出的航空学系来培养下一代工程师。

3. Her groundbreaking research in **aeronautics** earned her the most prestigious award in the engineering community.
   她在航空学方面的开创性研究为她赢得了工程界最负盛名的奖项。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aero" (air) + "nautics" (navigation) — aeronautics is the art of navigating through the air.
"aero"（空气）+"nautics"（导航）——aeronautics就是在空中导航的艺术。
