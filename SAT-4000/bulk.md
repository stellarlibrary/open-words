# 📦 bulk

🔊 /bʌlk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Large size or mass; the greater part or majority of something. (v.) To expand, swell, or make something seem larger
**中文：** n. 体积，大小；大多数 vt. 使膨胀或延伸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mass（大量）
- volume（体积）
- majority（大多数）
- magnitude（巨大）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the bulk of / in bulk / bulk purchase / bulk up / sheer bulk**

👉 Example: The bulk of the research funding was allocated to the biomedical sciences division.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bulk** of the evidence presented at trial pointed conclusively toward the defendant's involvement in the financial scandal.
   审判中提出的大部分证据都明确指向被告参与了这起金融丑闻。

2. Purchasing office supplies in **bulk** allowed the university to reduce its annual procurement costs by nearly twenty percent.
   批量采购办公用品使该大学的年度采购成本降低了近百分之二十。

3. The sheer **bulk** of the ancient stone monument suggested that hundreds of laborers had worked together to construct it.
   这座古代石碑的巨大体积表明曾有数百名劳工共同参与建造。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bulk" as something so big and heavy it's like a "hulk" — massive in size and quantity.
把"bulk"联想成"hulk（巨人）"——体积庞大，数量众多。
