# 🧩 intricate

🔊 /ˈɪntrɪkət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very detailed and complicated in design or structure; difficult to follow or understand
**中文：** adj. 复杂的；错综的；缠结的；难以理解的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- complex（复杂的）
- elaborate（精细的）
- convoluted（错综复杂的）
- labyrinthine（迷宫般的）
- sophisticated（精密的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intricate design / intricate pattern / intricate details / intricate mechanism**

👉 Example: The watchmaker spent hours assembling the intricate mechanism inside the antique timepiece.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cathedral's ceiling featured an **intricate** mosaic that had taken artisans more than a decade to complete.
   大教堂的天花板上有一幅复杂的马赛克，工匠们花了十多年才完成。

2. Scientists are still working to understand the **intricate** relationship between genetics and environmental factors in disease.
   科学家们仍在努力理解遗传与环境因素在疾病中错综复杂的关系。

3. The novel's **intricate** plot weaves together multiple timelines and characters in a remarkably cohesive narrative.
   这部小说错综复杂的情节将多条时间线和人物交织成一个极为连贯的叙事。

---

把"intricate"想成"in-trick-ate"——充满了各种花招和曲折，复杂得像一个迷宫。
