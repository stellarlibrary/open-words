# 🌿 sap

🔊 /sæp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The fluid that circulates through a plant; vitality or energy. (v.) To gradually weaken or exhaust someone's strength or power
**中文：** v. 使衰竭 n. 树汁；体液

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- drain（耗尽）
- deplete（消耗）
- weaken（削弱）
- exhaust（使精疲力竭）
- undermine（逐渐损害）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sap one's strength / sap one's energy / sap morale / sap the will**

👉 Example: Months of relentless overtime work began to sap the employees' morale and productivity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged drought **sapped** the vitality of the crops, leaving farmers with significantly diminished yields that season.
   长期的干旱消耗了农作物的生命力，使得那个季节农民的产量大幅减少。

2. Chronic bureaucratic obstacles continued to **sap** the organization's energy and impede its ability to deliver aid effectively.
   长期的官僚障碍持续消耗着该组织的精力，阻碍了其有效提供援助的能力。

3. The maple **sap** collected in early spring is boiled down over many hours to produce pure and flavorful syrup.
   早春采集的枫树汁经过数小时的熬煮，制成纯正美味的糖浆。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sap" as the life fluid of a tree — just as sap flows out of a tree, something that saps your energy drains the life force right out of you.
把"sap"想成树木的生命液体——就像树液从树中流出一样，消耗你精力的东西会把你的生命力抽干。
