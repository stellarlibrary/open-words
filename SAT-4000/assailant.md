# ⚔️ assailant

🔊 /əˈseɪlənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who physically attacks another; an attacker
**中文：** 攻击者；行凶者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attacker（攻击者）
- aggressor（侵犯者）
- assaulter（袭击者）
- mugger（抢劫犯）
- perpetrator（肇事者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unknown assailant / identify the assailant / armed assailant / flee from an assailant**

👉 Example: The police launched a citywide search to identify and apprehend the unknown assailant.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The victim was unable to identify her **assailant** because the attack occurred in a dimly lit and isolated alleyway.
   受害者无法辨认她的攻击者，因为袭击发生在一条灯光昏暗的僻静小巷里。

2. Authorities released a composite sketch of the **assailant** in hopes that the public could assist with identification.
   当局公布了攻击者的拼图画像，希望公众能够协助辨认。

3. The brave shopkeeper confronted the armed **assailant** and managed to prevent the robbery until police officers arrived.
   勇敢的店主与持械攻击者对峙，并成功阻止了抢劫直到警察到来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Assailant" comes from "assail" (to attack). The "-ant" suffix means "one who does," so an assailant is "one who attacks."
"Assailant"来自"assail"（攻击）。后缀"-ant"表示"做某事的人"，所以assailant就是"攻击的人"。
