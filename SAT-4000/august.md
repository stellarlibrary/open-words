# 👑 august

🔊 /ɔːˈɡʌst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Inspiring reverence or admiration; dignified, grand, and majestic in manner or appearance.

**中文：** 威严的；令人敬畏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- majestic（庄严的）
- dignified（有尊严的）
- venerable（令人尊敬的）
- noble（高贵的）
- imposing（气势宏伟的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

When used as an adjective (pronounced /ɔːˈɡʌst/), "august" means majestic or venerable — do not confuse it with the month "August" (capitalized, pronounced /ˈɔːɡəst/). It is typically used to describe institutions, persons of high rank, or solemn occasions.

当用作形容词时（发音 /ɔːˈɡʌst/），"august" 意为威严的、令人尊敬的——不要与月份 "August"（首字母大写，发音 /ˈɔːɡəst/）混淆。通常用于描述机构、高层人士或庄严的场合。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **august** tribunal delivered its verdict with solemnity befitting the gravity of the case.
   这个**威严的**法庭以与案件严重性相称的庄重态度宣读了判决。

2. Scholars gathered in the **august** halls of the centuries-old university for the ceremony.
   学者们聚集在这所百年大学**庄严的**大厅里参加典礼。

3. The ambassador spoke before an **august** assembly of world leaders at the summit.
   大使在峰会上一个**令人敬畏的**世界领导人集会前发表了讲话。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

The month of August was named after Augustus Caesar, the first Roman emperor — a figure of supreme majesty and power. So "august" (lowercase) carries that same feeling of grandeur and reverence.

八月（August）以第一位罗马皇帝奥古斯都·凯撒（Augustus Caesar）命名——一位至高无上的威严与权力象征。因此小写的 "august" 也带有同样的宏伟与敬畏之感。
