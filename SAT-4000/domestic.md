# 🏠 domestic

🔊 /dəˈmɛstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the home or family; relating to one's own country rather than foreign nations. (n.) A product made within one's own country
**中文：** adj. 国内的；家庭的；本国的 n. 国货

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- household（家庭的）
- internal（国内的）
- native（本国的）
- homegrown（国产的）
- familial（家庭的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **domestic policy / domestic violence / domestic market / domestic affairs / domestic product**

👉 Example: The administration shifted its focus from international diplomacy to pressing domestic economic concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government implemented new **domestic** policies aimed at reducing unemployment and stimulating economic growth across all regions.
   政府实施了新的国内政策，旨在减少失业并刺激所有地区的经济增长。

2. Rising **domestic** demand for renewable energy has prompted significant investment in solar and wind power infrastructure nationwide.
   国内对可再生能源日益增长的需求促使全国范围内对太阳能和风能基础设施进行了大量投资。

3. The sociologist's research examined how **domestic** responsibilities are distributed between partners in contemporary dual-income households.
   这位社会学家的研究考察了当代双收入家庭中家庭责任在伴侣之间的分配方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dom" (home, as in Latin "domus") + "estic" — domestic literally relates to the home, whether your household or your homeland.
想象"dom"（家，如拉丁语"domus"）+ "estic"——domestic字面上与家有关，无论是你的家庭还是你的祖国。
