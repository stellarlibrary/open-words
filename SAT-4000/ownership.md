# 🏠 ownership

🔊 /ˈoʊnərʃɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act, state, or right of possessing something; legal right to hold, use, or dispose of property
**中文：** n. 所有权；物主身份；所有制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- possession（拥有）
- proprietorship（所有权）
- title（所有权）
- dominion（支配权）
- tenure（保有）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **take ownership / transfer ownership / home ownership / ownership rights / ownership stake**

👉 Example: The court ruled that ownership of the disputed property belonged to the original landowner's descendants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The transfer of **ownership** from the founder to the new management team was completed after months of careful legal negotiations.
   在数月的审慎法律谈判之后，所有权从创始人向新管理团队的转让得以完成。

2. Home **ownership** rates have declined significantly among younger generations due to rising property prices and stagnant wage growth.
   由于房价上涨和工资增长停滞，年轻一代的住房拥有率大幅下降。

3. Effective leaders encourage their team members to take **ownership** of projects, fostering accountability and a sense of personal responsibility.
   有效的领导者鼓励团队成员对项目承担主人翁意识，培养责任感和个人担当。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "owner-ship" as the "ship" that an "owner" sails — you captain your own ship when you have ownership of something.
把"ownership"想成"owner（主人）+ship（船）"——当你拥有所有权时，你就是驾驶自己那艘船的船长。
