# 🧊 face

🔊 /feɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The front of the head; any of the surfaces of a solid object; dignity or prestige. (v.) To confront; to be oriented toward
**中文：** n. 脸；表面；立体的面；面子 v. 面向；朝；面对

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- countenance（面容）
- surface（表面）
- confront（面对）
- encounter（遭遇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **face the facts / face a challenge / lose face / face value / face of a cube**

👉 Example: The researchers had to face the reality that their initial hypothesis was fundamentally flawed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each **face** of the crystal exhibited a distinct geometric pattern that revealed important information about its molecular structure.
   晶体的每个面都呈现出独特的几何图案，揭示了其分子结构的重要信息。

2. The committee must **face** the difficult decision of allocating limited resources among several equally deserving departments.
   委员会必须面对在几个同样值得资助的部门之间分配有限资源的艰难决定。

3. Diplomats warned that the controversial policy could cause the nation to lose **face** on the international stage.
   外交官们警告说，这项有争议的政策可能导致该国在国际舞台上丢面子。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a die (cube) — every side is a "face," just as every aspect of a problem is a face you must turn toward and confront.
想想一个骰子——每一面都是一个"face"，就像问题的每个方面都是你必须转向并面对的一面。
