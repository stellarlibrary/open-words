# 🍂 blight

🔊 /blaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A plant disease causing withering and decay; a condition of deterioration or ruin. (v.) To destroy, ruin, or cause to wither; to have a severely damaging effect on.
**中文：** n. 枯萎病；荒芜；破坏 v. 毁灭；使枯萎；破坏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ruin（毁灭）
- devastate（摧毁）
- wither（枯萎）
- plague（瘟疫）
- scourge（灾害）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blight on the landscape / blight one's hopes / urban blight / blight a career**

👉 Example: Years of industrial neglect had left a visible blight on the once-thriving waterfront district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The invasive fungal **blight** destroyed nearly forty percent of the region's staple crop within a single growing season.
   这种入侵性真菌**枯萎病**在一个生长季内摧毁了该地区近百分之四十的主要作物。

2. Corruption continued to **blight** the nation's institutions, eroding public trust and discouraging foreign investment significantly.
   腐败继续**侵蚀**着该国的机构，严重削弱了公众信任并大幅打击了外国投资。

3. The abandoned factories stood as grim monuments to the urban **blight** that had afflicted the city for decades.
   废弃的工厂矗立着，成为数十年来困扰这座城市的城市**衰败**的严峻纪念碑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "blight" as "b + light" — when the light is blocked (b-light), plants wither and die, just as blight destroys growth.
把"blight"想成"b + light"——当光线被阻挡时，植物就会枯萎死亡，正如blight摧毁生长一样。
