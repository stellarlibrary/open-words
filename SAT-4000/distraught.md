# 😰 distraught

🔊 /dɪˈstrɔːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely worried, nervous, or upset; deeply agitated with anxiety or emotional distress
**中文：** adj. 发狂的；心烦意乱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frantic（疯狂的）
- overwrought（过度紧张的）
- anguished（极度痛苦的）
- agitated（焦虑不安的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **distraught over / distraught by / distraught with grief / emotionally distraught**

👉 Example: The mother was utterly distraught when she learned that her child had gone missing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **distraught** parents pleaded with authorities to intensify the search for their missing daughter across the region.
   心烦意乱的父母恳求当局加大力度在整个地区搜寻他们失踪的女儿。

2. After receiving the devastating news, the **distraught** employee was unable to concentrate on any assigned tasks.
   在收到那个令人崩溃的消息后，心烦意乱的员工无法集中精力完成任何分配的任务。

3. The community remained **distraught** for weeks following the sudden closure of the beloved neighborhood school.
   在深受喜爱的社区学校突然关闭后，社区居民数周内都心烦意乱。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-traught" as "dis-thought" — your thoughts are so disrupted and scattered that you cannot think clearly.
把"distraught"想成"dis-thought"——你的思绪被打乱得七零八落，完全无法清楚地思考。
