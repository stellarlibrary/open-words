# 🎨 bedaub

🔊 /bɪˈdɔːb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To smear or daub over; to decorate in a tasteless or vulgar manner
**中文：** v. 涂污；俗气地装饰；过分地装饰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- smear（涂抹）
- daub（乱涂）
- besmear（涂污）
- plaster（粗涂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bedaub with paint / bedaub a wall / bedaubed in mud / bedaubed with ornaments**

👉 Example: The children bedaubed their faces with bright paint before the costume parade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vandals **bedaubed** the historic monument with garish spray paint, provoking outrage from the entire community.
   破坏者们在历史纪念碑上涂满了刺眼的喷漆，激怒了整个社区。

2. The interior decorator complained that the previous owner had **bedaubed** every wall with clashing, tasteless colors.
   室内装饰师抱怨说前任业主在每面墙上都涂了不协调且俗气的颜色。

3. After the muddy football match, the players emerged from the field thoroughly **bedaubed** with dirt and grass stains.
   泥泞的足球比赛结束后，球员们满身泥土和草渍地走出了球场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be + daub" — to bedaub is to daub something all over, smearing it messily or decorating it tastelessly.
把"bedaub"拆成"be + daub（涂抹）"——就是到处乱涂，把东西弄得很脏或装饰得很俗气。
