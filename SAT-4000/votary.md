# 🙏 votary

🔊 /ˈvoʊtəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who has dedicated themselves to a particular cause, religion, or way of life; a devoted follower or worshiper
**中文：** n. 信徒；崇拜者；出家人；热心追随者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- devotee（信徒）
- disciple（门徒）
- adherent（拥护者）
- zealot（狂热者）
- follower（追随者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **votary of art / votary of science / votary of truth / devoted votary**

👉 Example: As a lifelong votary of classical music, she attended every performance at the local symphony hall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher was a dedicated **votary** of rational inquiry who rejected all forms of superstition and unfounded belief.
   这位哲学家是理性探究的忠实信徒，拒绝一切形式的迷信和无根据的信念。

2. Each **votary** of the ancient temple took solemn vows of silence and devoted years to meditative spiritual practice.
   这座古庙的每位信徒都庄严地发下沉默的誓言，并将数年时间投入到冥想的灵修中。

3. As a **votary** of environmental conservation, he dedicated his career to protecting endangered habitats across the continent.
   作为环境保护的忠实拥护者，他将职业生涯致力于保护整个大陆濒临灭绝的栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "votary" as related to "vow" — a votary is someone who has made a vow of devotion to a cause or belief.
把"votary"联想到"vow"（誓言）——votary就是对某项事业或信仰发下忠诚誓言的人。
