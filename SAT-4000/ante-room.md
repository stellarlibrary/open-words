# 🚪 ante-room

🔊 /ˈæn.tiˌruːm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A small room leading to a main one, often used as a waiting area before being admitted to a larger room or office.

**中文：** 接待室；前厅；候见室，指通往主要房间的小房间，通常用作等候区。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- waiting room（候客室）
- vestibule（前厅）
- foyer（门厅）
- antechamber（前厅）
- lobby（大厅）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** wait in the ante-room, be shown to the ante-room, a spacious ante-room

**Example:** Visitors were asked to wait in the **ante-room** before the ambassador received them.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The delegates gathered in the **ante-room** while the committee finalized the agenda for the summit.
   委员们在前厅等候，而委员会正在敲定峰会议程。

2. A distinguished painting adorned the wall of the **ante-room**, lending the space an air of formality.
   一幅精美的画作装饰着接待室的墙壁，为这个空间增添了庄重的气氛。

3. The secretary escorted the visiting scholar through the **ante-room** and into the director's private office.
   秘书引导来访的学者穿过前厅，进入主任的私人办公室。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "ante-" meaning "before" and "room" — it is the room you enter **before** the main room. 把 ante-（前）和 room（房间）组合起来理解：进入主房间**之前**的那个房间，就是"前厅"或"接待室"。
