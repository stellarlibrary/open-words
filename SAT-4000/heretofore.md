# 📜 heretofore

🔊 /ˌhɪrtəˈfɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Up to this time; before now; previously
**中文：** adv. 直到此时，迄今为止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- previously（先前）
- hitherto（迄今）
- formerly（从前）
- until now（直到现在）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heretofore unknown / heretofore unseen / heretofore unprecedented / heretofore unrecognized**

👉 Example: The discovery revealed a heretofore unknown species of deep-sea organism living near hydrothermal vents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeological team uncovered a **heretofore** unknown civilization that flourished in the region over three thousand years ago.
   考古团队发现了一个迄今未知的文明，该文明在三千多年前曾在该地区繁荣发展。

2. The new legislation addressed **heretofore** unregulated aspects of digital privacy that had long concerned consumer advocacy groups.
   新立法涉及了迄今未受监管的数字隐私方面，这些问题长期以来一直困扰着消费者维权团体。

3. Scientists published findings about a **heretofore** unrecognized mechanism by which certain bacteria develop resistance to antibiotics.
   科学家发表了关于一种迄今未被认识到的某些细菌产生抗生素耐药性机制的研究结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heretofore" as "here-to-before" — referring to everything from here back to before, meaning up until now.
把"heretofore"想成"here-to-before"——指从这里回溯到之前的一切，即直到现在。
