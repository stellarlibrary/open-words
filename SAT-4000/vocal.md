# 🎤 vocal

🔊 /ˈvoʊkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the voice or speech; inclined to express opinions freely. (n.) A piece of music performed by voice; a vowel sound
**中文：** adj. 嗓音的，声音的，有声的 n. 声乐作品；元音

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outspoken（直言不讳的）
- articulate（善于表达的）
- oral（口头的）
- spoken（口语的）
- audible（听得见的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vocal critic / vocal cords / vocal supporter / vocal about / lead vocals**

👉 Example: She has been a vocal advocate for educational reform throughout her entire political career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator became an increasingly **vocal** opponent of the proposed legislation, delivering impassioned speeches on the chamber floor.
   这位参议员日益成为拟议立法的直言不讳的反对者，在议会大厅发表了慷慨激昂的演讲。

2. Professional singers must take exceptional care of their **vocal** cords to maintain the clarity and range of their performances.
   专业歌手必须格外保护声带，以保持演唱的清晰度和音域。

3. The **vocal** arrangements in the choral composition demonstrated the composer's masterful understanding of harmonic resonance and tonal balance.
   合唱作品中的声乐编排展示了作曲家对和声共鸣和音调平衡的精湛理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vocal" as sharing its root with "voice" — both come from Latin "vox," so vocal means anything related to the voice or speaking up.
把"vocal"想成与"voice（声音）"同根——两者都来自拉丁语"vox"，因此vocal指与声音有关的或敢于发声的。
