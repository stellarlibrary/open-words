# 🗺️ cartographer

🔊 /kɑːrˈtɒɡrəfər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who draws or produces maps; a specialist in the science and art of mapmaking
**中文：** n. 制图师；地图制作者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mapmaker（地图制作者）
- topographer（地形测量员）
- surveyor（测量员）
- geographer（地理学家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **skilled cartographer / royal cartographer / medieval cartographer / cartographer and explorer / digital cartographer**

👉 Example: The royal cartographer was commissioned to map the newly discovered territories.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The celebrated **cartographer** spent decades meticulously surveying coastlines to produce the most accurate maritime charts of the era.
   这位著名的制图师花了数十年时间仔细测量海岸线，绘制出那个时代最精确的航海图。

2. Modern **cartographers** rely heavily on satellite imagery and geographic information systems to create detailed digital representations of terrain.
   现代制图师大量依赖卫星图像和地理信息系统来创建详细的地形数字模型。

3. The medieval **cartographer** incorporated both empirical observations and mythological elements into his remarkably ambitious world map.
   这位中世纪制图师在他雄心勃勃的世界地图中融合了经验观察和神话元素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carto-" from Latin "carta" meaning "map/chart" and "-grapher" meaning "one who writes or draws" — a cartographer is literally someone who draws charts and maps.
把"carto-"想成拉丁语"carta"（地图/图表），"-grapher"想成"画/写的人"——cartographer字面意思就是"画地图的人"。
