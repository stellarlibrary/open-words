# 🤢 nausea

🔊 /ˈnɔːziə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A feeling of sickness in the stomach with an urge to vomit; intense disgust or loathing
**中文：** n. 恶心，晕船；极端的憎恶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- queasiness（恶心）
- sickness（不适）
- revulsion（厌恶）
- disgust（反感）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel nausea / wave of nausea / overcome with nausea / cause nausea**

👉 Example: A sudden wave of nausea swept over her during the turbulent flight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient reported persistent **nausea** and dizziness as primary side effects of the newly prescribed medication.
   患者报告持续的恶心和头晕是新处方药物的主要副作用。

2. A wave of **nausea** overcame the passengers when the vessel encountered unexpectedly rough seas during the crossing.
   当船只在横渡过程中遭遇意外的狂风巨浪时，一阵恶心袭来使乘客们难以忍受。

3. The graphic depiction of violence in the documentary induced a profound sense of **nausea** among the audience members.
   纪录片中对暴力的直观描述在观众中引起了强烈的恶心感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nausea" from the Greek "naus" meaning ship — seasickness was the original nausea, that queasy feeling on a rocking boat.
"nausea"源自希腊语"naus"（船）——晕船是最初的恶心感，即在摇晃的船上那种反胃的感觉。
