# 📏 approximately

🔊 /əˈprɑːksɪmətli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Close to an exact amount or number but not completely exact; roughly; about.

**中文：** 大约；近似地；大概。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- roughly（大致地）
- about（大约）
- nearly（几乎）
- around（左右）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** approximately equal, approximately half, approximately the same, approximately how many, takes approximately

**Example:** The project will take approximately six months to complete.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient ruins are **approximately** two thousand years old according to the latest archaeological findings.
   （根据最新的考古发现，这些古代遗址大约有两千年的历史。）

2. **Approximately** thirty percent of the annual budget was allocated to scientific research and development.
   （年度预算中大约百分之三十被拨给了科学研究与开发。）

3. The two cities are **approximately** five hundred kilometers apart when measured along the coastal highway.
   （沿海岸公路测量，这两座城市相距大约五百公里。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Approximately" comes from "proxim-" (near, close). Think: "approaching the exact number but not quite proximate enough to be precise."

"Approximately" 源自 "proxim-"（接近的）。联想：接近确切数字，但还不够精确——"近似地"。
