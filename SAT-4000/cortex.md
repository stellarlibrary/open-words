# 🧠 cortex

🔊 /ˈkɔːrtɛks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The outer layer of an organ, especially the brain; the bark of a tree; the outer layer of a fruit
**中文：** n. 皮质；树皮；果皮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bark（树皮）
- rind（外皮）
- outer layer（外层）
- integument（覆盖物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cerebral cortex / prefrontal cortex / cortex of the brain / renal cortex**

👉 Example: The cerebral cortex is responsible for higher-order cognitive functions such as reasoning and language.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Damage to the prefrontal **cortex** can significantly impair a person's ability to make rational decisions and plan ahead.
   前额叶皮质的损伤会严重影响一个人做出理性决定和提前规划的能力。

2. Neuroscientists discovered that the visual **cortex** processes information differently in individuals who have been blind since birth.
   神经科学家发现，先天失明者的视觉皮质处理信息的方式与常人不同。

3. The **cortex** of the adrenal gland produces essential hormones that regulate metabolism and immune system responses.
   肾上腺皮质产生调节新陈代谢和免疫系统反应的重要激素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cortex" as related to "cork" — just as cork is the outer bark of a tree, the cortex is the outer layer of an organ.
把"cortex"联想成"cork（软木）"——正如软木是树的外层树皮，皮质就是器官的外层。
