# 🍡 glutinous

🔊 /ˈɡluːtɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a sticky, glue-like texture; resembling or containing gluten; viscous and adhesive
**中文：** adj. 黏性的；胶状的；黏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sticky（黏的）
- viscous（黏稠的）
- adhesive（有黏性的）
- gummy（胶黏的）
- mucilaginous（黏液状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glutinous rice / glutinous texture / glutinous substance / glutinous consistency**

👉 Example: Glutinous rice is a staple ingredient in many traditional Asian desserts and ceremonial dishes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chef prepared traditional dumplings using **glutinous** rice flour, which gave them their characteristic chewy and sticky texture.
   厨师用糯米粉制作传统汤圆，赋予了它们标志性的软糯黏性口感。

2. The **glutinous** secretion produced by the plant's leaves effectively traps small insects that serve as a nutritional supplement.
   植物叶片分泌的黏性物质能有效捕捉小昆虫，作为营养补充。

3. Industrial engineers sought a **glutinous** compound that could bond metal surfaces together under extreme temperature conditions.
   工业工程师寻求一种能在极端温度条件下将金属表面黏合在一起的胶状化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glutinous" as "glue + tin + ous" — imagine something so sticky it could glue a tin can shut permanently.
把"glutinous"想成"glue（胶水）+ tin（锡罐）+ ous"——想象某种东西黏得能把锡罐永久粘住。
