# 🎈 aeronaut

🔊 /ˈɛərəˌnɔːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who operates or travels in an airship or balloon.
**中文：** 飞船或气球驾驶员。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aviator（飞行员）
- balloonist（气球驾驶员）
- pilot（飞行员）
- airman（飞行员）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **early, pioneering, daring, famous, skilled**
👉 Example: The pioneering aeronaut prepared for a transcontinental balloon voyage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **aeronaut** carefully inspected the balloon's envelope before ascending into the morning sky.
   那位飞船驾驶员在升入晨空之前仔细检查了气球的外罩。

2. As a celebrated **aeronaut**, she set numerous altitude records during the early twentieth century.
   作为一位著名的气球驾驶员，她在二十世纪初创下了多项高度纪录。

3. The museum honored the contributions of every **aeronaut** who advanced the science of lighter-than-air flight.
   博物馆表彰了每一位推动轻于空气飞行科学发展的飞船驾驶员。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aero" means air and "naut" means sailor — an aeronaut is literally a sailor of the air.
"aero"意为空气，"naut"意为水手——aeronaut字面意思就是"空中的水手"。
