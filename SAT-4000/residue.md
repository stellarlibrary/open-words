# 🔬 residue

🔊 /ˈrɛzɪduː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small amount of something that remains after the main part has gone or been taken; a substance left after evaporation, combustion, or filtration
**中文：** n. 残渣；剩余；滤渣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- remnant（残余）
- remainder（剩余物）
- dregs（渣滓）
- sediment（沉淀物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chemical residue / pesticide residue / leave a residue / sticky residue**

👉 Example: The laboratory analysis revealed traces of chemical residue on the surface of the contaminated samples.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Strict regulations require manufacturers to ensure that no harmful pesticide **residue** remains on produce sold to consumers.
   严格的法规要求制造商确保出售给消费者的农产品上不残留有害的农药残渣。

2. The forensic team carefully collected the powdery **residue** found at the scene to determine the precise chemical composition.
   法医团队仔细收集了现场发现的粉末状残留物，以确定其精确的化学成分。

3. After the solvent evaporated completely, a thin film of crystalline **residue** remained on the interior surface of the glass beaker.
   溶剂完全蒸发后，玻璃烧杯内表面残留了一层薄薄的结晶残渣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "residue" as "reside-ue" — the stuff that continues to reside on a surface after cleaning, the stubborn leftover.
把"residue"想成"reside-ue"——清洁后仍然残留在表面上的东西，顽固的残余物。
