# 🚷 eschew

🔊 /ɪsˈtʃuː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To deliberately avoid or keep away from something; to abstain from
**中文：** v. 避免；避开；回避

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- avoid（避免）
- shun（回避）
- abstain（戒绝）
- forgo（放弃）
- refrain（克制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eschew violence / eschew materialism / eschew publicity / eschew temptation**

👉 Example: The philosopher chose to eschew material wealth in favor of a life devoted to intellectual pursuits.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization publicly pledged to **eschew** all forms of violence and pursue only peaceful methods of political reform.
   该组织公开承诺避免一切形式的暴力，只采用和平方式进行政治改革。

2. Many contemporary minimalists **eschew** unnecessary consumer goods in order to lead simpler and more intentional daily lives.
   许多当代极简主义者避开不必要的消费品，以过上更简单、更有意识的日常生活。

3. The celebrated author was known to **eschew** public appearances and interviews, preferring complete solitude for creative work.
   这位著名作家以回避公开露面和采访而闻名，更喜欢在完全的独处中进行创作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eschew" as "es-chew" — you choose NOT to chew on something, meaning you avoid it entirely.
把"eschew"想成"es-chew（嚼）"——你选择不去嚼某样东西，意味着你完全回避它。
