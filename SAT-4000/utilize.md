# 🛠️ utilize

🔊 /ˈjuːtɪlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make practical and effective use of something; to employ for a specific purpose
**中文：** 利用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- employ（使用）
- harness（利用）
- exploit（开发利用）
- apply（应用）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **utilize resources / utilize technology / utilize skills / utilize fully / utilize effectively**

👉 Example: The team learned to utilize available data more efficiently to improve their research outcomes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation plans to **utilize** renewable energy sources to reduce its carbon footprint and meet new environmental regulations.
   该公司计划利用可再生能源来减少碳排放并满足新的环境法规要求。

2. Educators should **utilize** a variety of instructional strategies to accommodate the diverse learning styles of their students.
   教育工作者应利用多种教学策略来适应学生多样化的学习风格。

3. The research team was able to **utilize** advanced computational methods to analyze the vast dataset within a remarkably short period.
   研究团队能够利用先进的计算方法在极短的时间内分析庞大的数据集。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "utilize" as "u-TOOL-ize" — when you utilize something, you turn it into a tool for your purpose.
把"utilize"想成"u-TOOL-ize（工具化）"——当你利用某物时，你把它变成了达成目的的工具。
