# 🌱 neophyte

🔊 /ˈniːəfaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief; a beginner or novice; a new convert to a religion
**中文：** n. 新信徒；新入教者；初学者；新手

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- novice（新手）
- beginner（初学者）
- newcomer（新来者）
- apprentice（学徒）
- convert（新皈依者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political neophyte / complete neophyte / neophyte in the field / relative neophyte**

👉 Example: As a neophyte in the world of finance, she sought guidance from experienced mentors before making investments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seasoned professor patiently guided the **neophyte** researchers through the complexities of advanced statistical analysis methods.
   这位经验丰富的教授耐心地引导新手研究员理解高级统计分析方法的复杂性。

2. Despite being a **neophyte** in diplomatic affairs, the ambassador demonstrated remarkable composure during the tense international negotiations.
   尽管在外交事务中是新手，这位大使在紧张的国际谈判中表现出了非凡的镇定。

3. The monastery welcomed the **neophyte** with traditional ceremonies that marked the beginning of a lifelong spiritual commitment.
   修道院以传统仪式欢迎新入教者，标志着终身精神承诺的开始。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neo" (new) + "phyte" (plant, from Greek phyton) — a newly planted seedling, just starting to grow, like a beginner.
把"neophyte"想成"neo"(新) + "phyte"(植物)——一棵刚种下的幼苗，如同初学者刚刚起步。
