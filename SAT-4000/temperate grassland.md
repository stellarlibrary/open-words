# 🌿 temperate grassland

🔊 /ˈtɛmpərɪt ˈɡræslænd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A biome characterized by vast expanses of grass with few trees, found in regions with moderate temperatures and seasonal variation
**中文：** n. 温带草原，温带草地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prairie（草原）
- steppe（大草原）
- savanna（热带草原）
- pampas（南美草原）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **temperate grassland biome / temperate grassland ecosystem / temperate grassland region**

👉 Example: The temperate grassland supports a diverse community of herbivores and ground-nesting birds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **temperate grassland** biome is primarily characterized by deep-rooted grasses and a pronounced seasonal climate cycle.
   温带草原生物群落主要以深根草本植物和明显的季节性气候周期为特征。

2. Conservation efforts in the **temperate grassland** region have focused on protecting native species from agricultural encroachment.
   温带草原地区的保护工作重点在于保护本地物种免受农业侵蚀。

3. Scientists discovered that the **temperate grassland** ecosystem stores significant amounts of carbon beneath its extensive root systems.
   科学家发现，温带草原生态系统在其广泛的根系下储存了大量的碳。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temperate" as moderate weather and "grassland" as a land of grass — a vast green field under a mild, balanced climate.
把"temperate grassland"想成"温和的天气"加上"草的土地"——在温和均衡的气候下一片广阔的绿色原野。
