# 💬 socialize

🔊 /ˈsoʊʃəˌlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make social; to place under government or group ownership; to adapt to social life
**中文：** v. 交往、交际

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mingle（交际）
- interact（互动）
- fraternize（交往）
- associate（结交）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **socialize with friends / socialize at events / socialize children / socialize a policy**

👉 Example: The orientation week was designed to help freshmen socialize with their peers and faculty members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many psychologists emphasize the importance of allowing children to **socialize** with peers during their formative developmental years.
   许多心理学家强调在儿童成长发育关键期让他们与同龄人交往的重要性。

2. The company organized monthly events to encourage employees to **socialize** outside of the formal workplace setting.
   公司组织月度活动，鼓励员工在正式工作环境之外进行社交。

3. International students often find it challenging to **socialize** in a culture with unfamiliar customs and social norms.
   国际学生常常发现在一个有着陌生习俗和社会规范的文化中进行社交具有挑战性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "socialize" as "social-ize" — to make something social, or to engage in social activities with others.
把"socialize"想成"social-ize"——使某事变得社交化，或与他人进行社交活动。
