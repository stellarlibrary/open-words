# 🌊 lave

🔊 /leɪv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To wash or bathe; to flow along or against something, as water washing over a surface
**中文：** v. 洗；沐浴；冲刷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wash（洗涤）
- bathe（沐浴）
- rinse（冲洗）
- cleanse（清洗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lave the shore / lave the wounds / waves laving the rocks**

👉 Example: The gentle waves laved the sandy shore as the sun set over the tranquil bay.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The crystal-clear waters of the mountain stream **laved** the smooth stones along the riverbed in a soothing rhythm.
   山间清澈的溪水以舒缓的节奏冲刷着河床上光滑的石头。

2. Ancient Roman citizens would regularly **lave** themselves in the public baths as part of their daily social rituals.
   古罗马市民会定期在公共浴场沐浴，作为他们日常社交活动的一部分。

3. The warm tide rose steadily and **laved** the base of the weathered cliffs throughout the quiet summer evening.
   温暖的潮水稳步上升，在宁静的夏日傍晚冲刷着风化悬崖的底部。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lave" as the root of "lavatory" — a place where you wash, connecting lave to its meaning of washing and bathing.
把"lave"想成"lavatory（盥洗室）"的词根——一个洗漱的地方，将lave与洗涤和沐浴的含义联系起来。
