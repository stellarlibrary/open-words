# 🌬️ anemometer

🔊 /ˌænəˈmɑːmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An instrument used for measuring the speed or force of wind
**中文：** 风力计；风速计

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wind gauge（风量计）
- wind meter（风速仪）
- wind speed indicator（风速指示器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cup anemometer / digital anemometer / install an anemometer / anemometer reading**

👉 Example: The researchers installed an anemometer at the summit to record wind speeds throughout the winter season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The meteorological station uses a highly sensitive **anemometer** to provide accurate wind speed data for daily weather forecasts.
   气象站使用高灵敏度的风速计为每日天气预报提供准确的风速数据。

2. Before constructing the wind farm, engineers placed several **anemometers** across the valley to assess its energy potential.
   在建设风电场之前，工程师们在山谷各处放置了多个风速计以评估其能源潜力。

3. The ship's captain relied on the onboard **anemometer** to determine whether conditions were safe enough for departure.
   船长依靠船上的风速计来判断条件是否足够安全以便启航。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "anemo-meter" as "anemo" (wind, from Greek anemos) + "meter" (measure) — a device that measures the wind.
把"anemometer"拆成"anemo"（风，源自希腊语anemos）+"meter"（测量）——一种测量风的仪器。
