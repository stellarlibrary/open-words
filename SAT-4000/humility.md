# 🕊️ humility

🔊 /hjuːˈmɪləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of having a modest or low view of one's importance; a lack of arrogance or excessive pride
**中文：** n. 谦卑；谦逊；谦恭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modesty（谦虚）
- meekness（温顺）
- unpretentiousness（不矫饰）
- self-effacement（自谦）
- humbleness（谦逊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show humility / with humility / a sense of humility / intellectual humility**

👉 Example: True leadership requires humility — the willingness to listen and learn from others.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Nobel laureate accepted the prestigious award with genuine **humility**, crediting her colleagues for their indispensable contributions.
   这位诺贝尔奖获得者以真诚的谦逊接受了这一殊荣，感谢同事们不可或缺的贡献。

2. Practicing intellectual **humility** allows scholars to remain open to new evidence that may challenge their established theories.
   践行学术谦逊使学者们能够对可能挑战其既有理论的新证据保持开放态度。

3. The coach emphasized that **humility** in victory is just as important as resilience and composure in defeat.
   教练强调，胜利时的谦逊与失败时的坚韧和沉着同样重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humility" as "hum + ility" — someone with humility stays low to the ground (humus means earth in Latin), never rising above others.
把"humility"想成"hum + ility"——拥有谦逊的人始终贴近大地（拉丁语humus意为泥土），从不凌驾于他人之上。
