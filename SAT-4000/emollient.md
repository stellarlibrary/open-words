# 🧴 emollient

🔊 /ɪˈmɒliənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A preparation that softens or soothes the skin. (adj.) Having the quality of softening or soothing; attempting to avoid confrontation
**中文：** n. 润肤剂 adj. 柔软的；使柔软的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- moisturizer（保湿剂）
- soothing（舒缓的）
- softening（柔化的）
- balm（香膏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emollient cream / emollient properties / emollient tone / apply an emollient**

👉 Example: The dermatologist recommended an emollient to relieve the patient's dry and irritated skin condition.

---

1. The physician prescribed a specialized **emollient** to treat the chronic dryness and inflammation affecting the patient's sensitive skin.
   医生开了一种专门的润肤剂来治疗影响患者敏感皮肤的慢性干燥和炎症。

2. His **emollient** manner of speaking helped defuse the tension during the heated negotiations between the two opposing delegations.
   他温和的说话方式有助于在两个对立代表团之间激烈的谈判中缓和紧张局势。

   研究人员证实，这种新的润肤配方比传统的石油基护肤产品提供更出色的保湿效果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "emollient" as "e-MOLL-ient" — "moll" sounds like "mollify" (to soften), so an emollient softens and soothes.
把"emollient"想成"e-MOLL-ient"——"moll"听起来像"mollify（软化）"，所以emollient就是使皮肤柔软舒缓的东西。
