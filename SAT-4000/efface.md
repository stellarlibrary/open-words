# 🧽 efface

🔊 /ɪˈfeɪs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To wipe out, erase, or obliterate; to make oneself inconspicuous or withdraw from notice
**中文：** v. 抹去；抹掉；使不显眼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- erase（擦除）
- obliterate（消除）
- expunge（删除）
- delete（删去）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **efface a memory / efface an inscription / efface oneself / efface all traces**

👉 Example: Time and weather had gradually effaced the ancient inscriptions carved into the stone monument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The relentless erosion of wind and rain had **effaced** the names once proudly engraved upon the marble tombstones.
   无情的风雨侵蚀已经抹去了曾经骄傲地刻在大理石墓碑上的名字。

2. She tended to **efface** herself in social gatherings, preferring to observe quietly rather than attract any attention.
   她倾向于在社交场合中使自己不引人注目，宁愿安静地观察也不愿引起任何关注。

3. The authoritarian regime attempted to **efface** all historical records that contradicted its carefully constructed official narrative.
   该专制政权试图抹去所有与其精心构建的官方叙事相矛盾的历史记录。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-face" as "erase the face" — to efface means to wipe away, just like erasing someone's face from a photograph.
把"efface"想成"erase the face（擦掉脸）"——efface就是抹去，就像从照片上擦掉某人的脸一样。
