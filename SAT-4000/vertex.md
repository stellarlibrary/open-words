# 📐 vertex

🔊 /ˈvɜːrtɛks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The highest point or apex; the point where two lines meet to form an angle; the top of the head
**中文：** n. 顶点；头顶；天顶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apex（顶点）
- peak（顶峰）
- summit（最高点）
- pinnacle（巅峰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vertex of a triangle / vertex angle / at the vertex / opposite vertex**

👉 Example: The vertex of the triangle is the point where the two sides converge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In geometry class, students learned to calculate the angle formed at the **vertex** of each polygon with precision.
   在几何课上，学生们学习了如何精确计算每个多边形**顶点**处形成的角度。

2. The mountain climber finally reached the **vertex** of the peak after enduring several days of treacherous weather conditions.
   登山者在经历了数天恶劣的天气条件后，终于到达了山峰的**顶点**。

3. The architect positioned the decorative spire at the **vertex** of the cathedral to draw the observer's gaze upward.
   建筑师将装饰性尖塔放置在大教堂的**顶点**，以引导观者的目光向上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vertex" as "vertical + apex" — the vertical top point where everything converges upward.
把"vertex"想成"vertical（垂直的）+ apex（顶点）"——一切向上汇聚的垂直最高点。
