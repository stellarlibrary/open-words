# ⚡ momentary

🔊 /ˈmoʊmənteri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting for a very short time; brief and fleeting; liable to occur at any moment
**中文：** adj. 瞬间的；短暂的；随时会发生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fleeting（转瞬即逝的）
- transient（短暂的）
- brief（简短的）
- ephemeral（短暂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **momentary lapse / momentary hesitation / momentary pause / momentary silence**

👉 Example: After a momentary hesitation, the speaker regained her composure and delivered the remainder of her address flawlessly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **momentary** power outage disrupted the laboratory's sensitive instruments and compromised several hours of carefully collected data.
   瞬间的断电干扰了实验室的精密仪器，导致数小时精心收集的数据受损。

2. His **momentary** lapse in concentration during the examination cost him the highest score he had been consistently achieving.
   考试中一瞬间的注意力分散使他失去了一直以来保持的最高分。

3. The audience experienced a **momentary** silence before erupting into thunderous applause for the orchestra's stunning final movement.
   观众经历了片刻的沉默，随后爆发出雷鸣般的掌声，献给乐团精彩的终曲乐章。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "moment-ary" as "lasting only a moment" — the suffix "-ary" means "relating to," so momentary means relating to just one brief moment.
把"momentary"想成"只持续一个moment（瞬间）的"——后缀"-ary"表示"与…有关的"，所以momentary就是与短短一瞬有关的。
