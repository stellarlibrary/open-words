# 🏹 errant

🔊 /ˈɛrənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Traveling in search of adventure; straying from the proper course or standards; erring or mistaken
**中文：** adj. 不定的；周游的；错误的；迷途的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wayward（任性的）
- roaming（漫游的）
- stray（迷途的）
- wandering（游荡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **errant knight / errant behavior / errant ball / errant child**

👉 Example: The errant knight traveled across distant lands in search of glory and redemption.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor sternly corrected the **errant** student whose research methods deviated significantly from established academic protocols.
   教授严厉地纠正了那位研究方法严重偏离既定学术规范的迷途学生。

2. An **errant** missile veered off its intended trajectory and landed harmlessly in the uninhabited desert region nearby.
   一枚偏离预定轨道的导弹无害地落入了附近无人居住的沙漠地区。

3. Medieval literature frequently celebrates the **errant** knight who wanders the countryside seeking justice and noble quests.
   中世纪文学常常赞美那些在乡间漫游、寻求正义和崇高使命的游侠骑士。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "errant" as "err" + "ant" — an ant that errs and wanders off the trail, straying from the proper path.
把"errant"想成"err（犯错）"+"ant（蚂蚁）"——一只犯了错、偏离队列的蚂蚁，迷失了正确的路线。
