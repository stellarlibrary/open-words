# 🐴 thoroughbred

🔊 /ˈθʌrəbred/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An animal of pure breed, especially a horse bred for racing. (adj.) Of pure breed; outstanding or excellent in quality.
**中文：** n. 良种动物 adj. 良种的；优秀的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- purebred（纯种的）
- pedigree（血统优良的）
- elite（精英的）
- first-rate（一流的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **thoroughbred horse / thoroughbred racing / a real thoroughbred / thoroughbred champion**

👉 Example: The ranch is famous for breeding thoroughbred horses that compete in the most prestigious international races.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **thoroughbred** racehorse demonstrated extraordinary speed and stamina as it surged past its competitors in the final stretch.
   这匹纯种赛马在最后的冲刺阶段展现了非凡的速度和耐力，超越了竞争对手。

2. Only a genuine **thoroughbred** with impeccable lineage and documented ancestry is eligible to compete in the Kentucky Derby championship.
   只有血统无可挑剔、有文献记载家系的真正纯种马才有资格参加肯塔基德比锦标赛。

3. The diplomat was considered a **thoroughbred** professional whose refined expertise and composure earned admiration from colleagues worldwide.
   这位外交官被认为是一位优秀的专业人士，其精湛的专业知识和沉着赢得了全球同事的钦佩。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "thorough-bred" as "thoroughly bred" — an animal that has been thoroughly and carefully bred to ensure the purest quality.
把"thoroughbred"想成"thoroughly bred"——一种被彻底、精心培育以确保最纯品质的动物。
