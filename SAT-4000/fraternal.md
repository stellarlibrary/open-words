# 🤝 fraternal

🔊 /frəˈtɜːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of or like a brother or brothers; showing warmth and friendliness associated with brotherhood
**中文：** 兄弟般的；友好的；友爱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brotherly（兄弟般的）
- comradely（同志般的）
- amicable（友好的）
- cordial（热诚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fraternal bond / fraternal twins / fraternal organization / fraternal love**

👉 Example: The fraternal bond between the two soldiers grew stronger through years of shared hardship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two nations maintained a **fraternal** relationship, regularly cooperating on matters of trade, security, and cultural exchange.
   两国保持着兄弟般的关系，在贸易、安全和文化交流方面定期合作。

2. Unlike identical twins, **fraternal** twins develop from two separate fertilized eggs and may differ significantly in appearance.
   与同卵双胞胎不同，异卵双胞胎由两个不同的受精卵发育而来，外貌可能差异很大。

3. The university's **fraternal** organizations played an important role in fostering leadership skills and community service among students.
   该大学的兄弟会组织在培养学生的领导能力和社区服务方面发挥了重要作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fraternal" as coming from "frater," the Latin word for brother — a fraternity is a brotherhood, so fraternal means brotherly.
把"fraternal"想成来自拉丁语"frater"，意为兄弟——fraternity是兄弟会，所以fraternal意为兄弟般的。
