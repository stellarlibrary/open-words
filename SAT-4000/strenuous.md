# 💪 strenuous

🔊 /ˈstrɛnjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Requiring or involving great effort, energy, or exertion; vigorous and determined
**中文：** adj. 积极的，奋发的；费劲的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arduous（艰巨的）
- laborious（费力的）
- vigorous（精力充沛的）
- demanding（要求高的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strenuous exercise / strenuous effort / strenuous objection / strenuous activity**

👉 Example: The doctor advised against strenuous physical activity during the first two weeks of recovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mountaineers endured a **strenuous** climb through treacherous terrain before finally reaching the summit at dawn.
   登山者在险恶的地形中经过艰苦攀登，终于在黎明时分到达了山顶。

2. Despite **strenuous** opposition from several committee members, the proposal was ultimately approved by a narrow margin.
   尽管几位委员会成员强烈反对，该提案最终还是以微弱多数获得了通过。

3. The rehabilitation program involved weeks of **strenuous** physical therapy designed to restore full mobility to the injured joint.
   康复计划包括数周的艰苦物理治疗，旨在恢复受伤关节的全部活动能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stren-uous" as related to "strength" — strenuous activities require great strength and leave you exhausted.
把"strenuous"想成与"strength（力量）"相关——费力的活动需要很大的力气，让你精疲力竭。
