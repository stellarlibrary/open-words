# 🔬 anthropologist

🔊 /ˌænθrəˈpɑːlədʒɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A scholar who studies human societies, cultures, and their development, both past and present.

**中文：** 人类学家（研究人类社会、文化及其发展的学者）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- ethnographer 民族志学者
- social scientist 社会科学家
- ethnologist 民族学家
- cultural researcher 文化研究者

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **cultural anthropologist**, **forensic anthropologist**, **physical anthropologist**, **a team of anthropologists**. Often followed by "who studies/researches..."

> Example: The **anthropologist** spent three years living among the indigenous community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **anthropologist** published her groundbreaking findings on ancient burial rituals in the journal.
   这位著名的人类学家在该期刊上发表了她关于古代葬礼仪式的开创性发现。

2. A forensic **anthropologist** was called to the excavation site to examine the unidentified skeletal remains.
   一位法医人类学家被召到挖掘现场检查那些身份不明的骸骨遗骸。

3. The **anthropologist** conducted extensive fieldwork to document the traditions of the remote highland tribe.
   这位人类学家进行了广泛的田野调查，以记录偏远高地部落的传统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anthropo-" (human) + "-logist" (one who studies). An **anthropologist** is literally "one who studies humans" — their cultures, histories, and behaviors.

"anthropo-"（人类）+"-logist"（研究者）。**Anthropologist** 字面意思就是"研究人类的人"——研究人类的文化、历史和行为。
