# 🔬 infinitesimal

🔊 /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely small; immeasurably minute. (n.) An indefinitely small quantity; a value approaching zero
**中文：** adj. 无穷小的，极小的 n. 极小量，极微量，无限小

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- minuscule（极小的）
- microscopic（微观的）
- negligible（可忽略的）
- tiny（微小的）
- imperceptible（难以察觉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infinitesimal amount / infinitesimal difference / infinitesimal change / infinitesimal probability**

👉 Example: The probability of such an event occurring naturally is infinitesimal, approaching nearly zero.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **infinitesimal** variations in temperature were undetectable by standard instruments but proved significant in quantum-level experiments.
   温度中极其微小的变化是标准仪器无法检测到的，但在量子级别的实验中却具有重要意义。

2. Calculus relies on the concept of **infinitesimal** quantities to determine the slope of curves at any given point.
   微积分依赖于无穷小量的概念来确定曲线在任意给定点的斜率。

3. The chemical was present in such **infinitesimal** concentrations that it posed no measurable threat to human health or safety.
   该化学物质的浓度极其微小，对人类健康或安全不构成可衡量的威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "infinite" + "small" — something so small it approaches the infinitely tiny, almost nothing at all.
把"infinitesimal"想成"infinite（无限）" + "small（小）"——小到接近无限微小，几乎等于零。
