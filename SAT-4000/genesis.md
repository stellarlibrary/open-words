# 🌅 genesis

🔊 /ˈdʒɛnəsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The origin or coming into being of something; the beginning or start of a process or development
**中文：** n. 发生；起源

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- origin（起源）
- inception（开端）
- beginning（开始）
- emergence（出现）
- birth（诞生）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the genesis of an idea / the genesis of a movement / from its genesis / trace the genesis**

👉 Example: Scholars continue to debate the genesis of democratic governance in ancient civilizations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **genesis** of the scientific revolution can be traced to a period of intense intellectual curiosity in sixteenth-century Europe.
   科学革命的起源可以追溯到十六世纪欧洲一段强烈求知欲的时期。

2. Understanding the **genesis** of a conflict is essential for diplomats seeking to negotiate a lasting and meaningful peace.
   理解冲突的起源对于寻求谈判持久而有意义的和平的外交官来说至关重要。

3. The researcher devoted an entire chapter to exploring the **genesis** of modern environmental policy in industrialized nations.
   该研究者用了整整一章来探讨工业化国家现代环境政策的起源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "genesis" as the name of the first book of the Bible, which tells the story of the very beginning of the world — genesis means origin.
想到"genesis"是《圣经》第一卷《创世记》的名字，讲述了世界最初的故事——genesis意为起源。
