# 😔 rue

🔊 /ruː/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To feel regret or sorrow for something. (n.) An aromatic plant; regret or sorrow
**中文：** v. 后悔，懊悔

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regret（后悔）
- lament（悲叹）
- repent（悔悟）
- bemoan（惋惜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rue the day / rue a decision / rue the consequences / live to rue**

👉 Example: She will rue the day she dismissed such a promising opportunity without careful consideration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The executive came to **rue** his hasty decision to reject the merger proposal that would have saved the company.
   那位高管后来后悔自己仓促拒绝了本可以挽救公司的合并提案。

2. Many historians believe the general lived to **rue** his strategic blunder, which ultimately cost him the decisive battle.
   许多历史学家认为那位将军终生都在懊悔自己的战略失误，那最终让他输掉了关键战役。

3. Those who neglect their education in youth often **rue** the missed opportunities when they enter the competitive workforce.
   那些在年轻时忽视教育的人，在进入竞争激烈的职场时往往会后悔错失的机会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rue" as "R-U-E" — "aRe yoU rEgretting?" When you rue something, you ask yourself if you truly regret it.
把"rue"想成"R-U-E"——"你后悔了吗？"当你rue某件事时，就是在问自己是否真的后悔。
