# 🔀 transposition

🔊 /ˌtrænspəˈzɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of exchanging the positions or order of two or more things; in mathematics, the rearrangement of terms; in music, the shifting of a composition to a different key
**中文：** n. 移项；调换；变换位置

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rearrangement（重新排列）
- interchange（互换）
- reversal（颠倒）
- permutation（排列）
- switch（交换）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transposition of letters / transposition error / musical transposition / transposition of terms**

👉 Example: A simple transposition of two digits in the account number caused weeks of billing confusion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **transposition** of two critical digits in the financial report led to a significant accounting discrepancy that delayed the audit.
   财务报告中两个关键数字的调换导致了严重的会计差异，推迟了审计工作。

2. The pianist performed a skillful **transposition** of the original composition from a major key to a minor key.
   钢琴家将原曲从大调巧妙地移调到小调进行演奏。

3. Researchers discovered that a genetic **transposition** of nucleotide sequences was responsible for the rare hereditary condition.
   研究人员发现核苷酸序列的基因转位是导致这种罕见遗传疾病的原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-position" as "changing (trans) position" — things swap places, like shuffling cards in a deck.
把"transposition"想成"trans（改变）+ position（位置）"——事物交换位置，就像洗牌一样。
