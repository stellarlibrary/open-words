# 🎯 unequivocal

🔊 /ˌʌnɪˈkwɪvəkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Leaving no doubt; clear, unambiguous, and expressed in a way that cannot be misunderstood
**中文：** adj. 明确的；不含糊的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unambiguous（明确的）
- definitive（确定的）
- explicit（明白的）
- categorical（绝对的）
- unmistakable（不会弄错的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unequivocal support / unequivocal evidence / unequivocal statement / unequivocal denial / unequivocal terms**

👉 Example: The president issued an unequivocal statement condemning all forms of discrimination in the strongest possible terms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientific evidence in favor of climate change is **unequivocal**, leaving virtually no room for legitimate debate.
   支持气候变化的科学证据是明确的，几乎没有留下合理辩论的空间。

2. The board of directors issued an **unequivocal** denial of the allegations concerning financial misconduct within the organization.
   董事会对有关组织内部财务不当行为的指控发表了明确的否认声明。

3. Her **unequivocal** commitment to social justice inspired an entire generation of young activists to pursue meaningful reform.
   她对社会正义的明确承诺激励了整整一代年轻活动家去追求有意义的改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-equivocal" as the opposite of "equivocal" (ambiguous) — remove the ambiguity and you get something perfectly clear.
把"unequivocal"想成"equivocal"（含糊的）的反义——去掉含糊，你就得到了完全清楚的东西。
