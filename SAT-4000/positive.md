# ✅ positive

🔊 /ˈpɑːzɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Certain and definite; optimistic and constructive; greater than zero; showing the presence of a condition. (n.) A positive number; a photographic positive
**中文：** adj. 确实的，明确的；积极的，肯定的；正的，阳性的 n. 正数；正片

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- affirmative（肯定的）
- optimistic（乐观的）
- constructive（建设性的）
- definite（明确的）
- favorable（有利的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **positive attitude / positive outcome / positive feedback / test positive / positive impact**

👉 Example: Maintaining a positive outlook can significantly influence one's ability to overcome professional challenges.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The clinical trial yielded remarkably **positive** results, suggesting that the new treatment could benefit thousands of patients worldwide.
   临床试验产生了非常积极的结果，表明新疗法可能使全球数千名患者受益。

2. The committee provided **positive** feedback on the proposal, recommending only minor revisions before final approval was granted.
   委员会对该提案给予了肯定的反馈，建议在最终批准前仅作少量修改。

3. Researchers confirmed that the sample tested **positive** for the presence of previously undetected chemical compounds in the water supply.
   研究人员确认样本检测为阳性，发现了供水中此前未被检测到的化学化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "positive" as "posit + ive" — when you posit (assert) something firmly, you are being positive about it.
把"positive"想成"posit + ive"——当你坚定地断言（posit）某事时，你对此是确定的（positive）。
