# ✨ diaphanous

🔊 /daɪˈæfənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Light, delicate, and translucent; so fine as to be nearly transparent
**中文：** adj. 透明的；精致的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- translucent（半透明的）
- sheer（极薄的）
- gossamer（薄如蛛丝的）
- filmy（薄膜般的）
- gauzy（薄纱般的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diaphanous fabric / diaphanous veil / diaphanous curtain / diaphanous gown**

👉 Example: The bride wore a diaphanous veil that allowed her radiant smile to shine through.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dancers moved gracefully across the stage, their **diaphanous** costumes flowing like ethereal clouds in the wind.
   舞者们优雅地在舞台上移动，她们透明的服装像空灵的云朵般随风飘动。

2. Morning sunlight filtered through the **diaphanous** curtains, casting a gentle golden glow throughout the quiet bedroom.
   晨光透过轻薄的窗帘，在安静的卧室里投下柔和的金色光芒。

3. The butterfly's **diaphanous** wings revealed an intricate network of delicate veins visible against the bright sky.
   蝴蝶透明的翅膀在明亮的天空映衬下展现出精致的脉络网络。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diaphanous" as "dia-" (through) + "phan" (show) — something so thin that light shows through it, like a transparent fabric.
把"diaphanous"想成"dia-"（穿过）+"phan"（显现）——薄到光线能穿透的东西，就像透明的织物。
