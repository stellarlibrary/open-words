# 🎭 dissemble

🔊 /dɪˈsɛmbəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs; to disguise or pretend
**中文：** v. 掩饰；掩盖；假装

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disguise（伪装）
- conceal（隐藏）
- feign（假装）
- mask（掩饰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dissemble one's true feelings / dissemble intentions / dissemble about something**

👉 Example: The diplomat learned to dissemble his emotions during tense international negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accused politician attempted to **dissemble** his involvement in the scandal by offering contradictory and evasive statements.
   这位被指控的政客试图通过提供矛盾且含糊的陈述来掩饰自己在丑闻中的参与。

2. She could no longer **dissemble** her disappointment when the committee rejected her carefully prepared research proposal outright.
   当委员会断然拒绝她精心准备的研究提案时，她再也无法掩饰自己的失望。

3. Skilled negotiators often **dissemble** their true positions in order to gain a strategic advantage over their counterparts.
   老练的谈判者经常掩饰自己的真实立场，以便在对手面前获得战略优势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dissemble" as "dis-semble" — to dis-assemble your true self and present a false front.
把"dissemble"想成"dis-semble"——拆解真实的自己，呈现一个虚假的外表。
