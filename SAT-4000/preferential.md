# 🌟 preferential

🔊 /ˌprɛfəˈrɛnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Giving an advantage or special treatment to a particular person or group; relating to or involving preference
**中文：** adj. 优先的；选择的；特惠的；先取的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- favorable（优惠的）
- privileged（享有特权的）
- advantageous（有利的）
- priority（优先的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preferential treatment / preferential rates / preferential access / preferential tariff**

👉 Example: The trade agreement established preferential tariff rates for member nations to stimulate economic cooperation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argued that the admissions policy amounted to **preferential** treatment for applicants from wealthy and influential families.
   批评者认为，该招生政策实际上是对来自富裕和有影响力家庭的申请者的优惠待遇。

2. The international accord granted **preferential** trading terms to developing countries in an effort to reduce global economic inequality.
   该国际协议给予发展中国家优惠贸易条件，以努力减少全球经济不平等。

3. Employees who had served the company for over a decade received **preferential** access to the newly established retirement benefit program.
   在公司服务超过十年的员工获得了新设立的退休福利计划的优先参与权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "preference + ial" — if you have a "preference" for someone, you give them "preferential" treatment, like a VIP pass.
把"preferential"想成"preference（偏好）+ ial（形容词后缀）"——如果你对某人有偏好，你就给他们优先待遇，像VIP通行证一样。
