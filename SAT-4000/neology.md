# 🆕 neology

🔊 /niˈɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The practice of coining new words; the use of new words or new meanings of existing words
**中文：** n. 新词的使用；旧词新义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- word coinage（造词）
- neologism（新词）
- lexical innovation（词汇创新）
- word creation（词语创造）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **practice neology / linguistic neology / the art of neology / engage in neology**

👉 Example: The study of neology reveals how societies adapt their vocabulary to express emerging concepts and ideas.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The field of **neology** has expanded dramatically as digital communication generates new vocabulary at an unprecedented rate.
   随着数字通信以前所未有的速度产生新词汇，新词学领域已大幅扩展。

2. Scholars of **neology** examine the social and cultural forces that drive the creation of entirely new linguistic expressions.
   新词学学者研究推动全新语言表达方式产生的社会和文化力量。

3. Corporate marketing departments frequently engage in **neology**, inventing brand names and product terms that enter mainstream discourse.
   企业市场部门经常进行新词创造，发明品牌名称和产品术语并进入主流话语。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neo" (new) + "logy" (study of) — the study or practice of creating new words, just like "biology" is the study of life.
把"neology"想成"neo"(新) + "logy"(学科)——创造新词的学问，就像"biology"是生命的学问。
