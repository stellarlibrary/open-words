# 🪶 facile

🔊 /ˈfæsɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (Disapproving) Appearing neat and comprehensive but lacking depth; too simplistic. Also: easily achieved; effortless
**中文：** 轻率的；未经深思熟虑的；肤浅的；易做到的；流畅的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- superficial（肤浅的）
- simplistic（过于简单的）
- glib（油嘴滑舌的）
- effortless（轻松的）
- shallow（浅薄的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **facile argument / facile explanation / facile solution / facile assumption**

👉 Example: The critic dismissed the author's facile analysis of the complex geopolitical situation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator rejected the **facile** explanation offered by the spokesperson, demanding a more thorough investigation of the matter.
   参议员驳回了发言人给出的肤浅解释，要求对此事进行更彻底的调查。

2. Scholars have long criticized the **facile** assumption that economic growth alone can solve deep-rooted social inequality.
   学者们长期以来批评那种认为仅靠经济增长就能解决根深蒂固的社会不平等的轻率假设。

3. Her **facile** command of multiple languages impressed the international delegation gathered at the diplomatic conference.
   她对多种语言的娴熟掌握给在外交会议上聚集的国际代表团留下了深刻印象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "facile" as related to "facility" (ease) — something facile comes too easily, so it lacks depth and substance.
把"facile"和"facility"(容易)联系起来——facile的东西来得太容易了，所以缺乏深度和实质。
