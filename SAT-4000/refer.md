# 📚 refer

🔊 /rɪˈfɜːr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To direct attention to something; to mention or allude to; to consult for information; to submit for consideration
**中文：** v. 参考；涉及；提到；查阅；提交

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- allude（暗示）
- mention（提及）
- consult（查阅）
- cite（引用）
- direct（指引）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **refer to / refer back to / refer a case to / refer someone to**

👉 Example: The professor asked students to refer to the original source material before writing their analysis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author frequently **referred** to classical philosophical texts when constructing the theoretical framework for her groundbreaking dissertation.
   作者在构建其开创性论文的理论框架时经常参考古典哲学文本。

2. The physician decided to **refer** the patient to a specialist after preliminary examinations revealed an abnormal cardiac condition.
   初步检查发现异常心脏状况后，医生决定将患者转介给专科医生。

3. Please **refer** to the appendix for a comprehensive list of statistical data supporting the conclusions presented in this report.
   请参阅附录，了解支持本报告结论的统计数据的完整列表。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-fer" as "carry back" (from Latin re- + ferre) — you carry your attention back to a source for guidance.
把"refer"想成"re-fer"（拉丁语"带回"）——你把注意力带回到某个来源以获取指导。
