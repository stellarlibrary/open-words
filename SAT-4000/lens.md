# 🔍 lens

🔊 /lɛnz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A piece of transparent material with curved surfaces used to focus or disperse light rays; the transparent structure in the eye that focuses light onto the retina
**中文：** n. 透镜，镜头；眼睛中的水晶体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- optic（光学元件）
- glass（镜片）
- eyepiece（目镜）
- prism（棱镜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **through the lens of / camera lens / contact lens / lens of analysis**

👉 Example: Historians often examine past events through the lens of contemporary social theory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The photographer adjusted the **lens** carefully to capture the intricate details of the architectural facade.
   摄影师仔细调整镜头，以捕捉建筑外立面的精细细节。

2. Viewing the conflict through the **lens** of economic theory reveals underlying structural causes often overlooked.
   通过经济学理论的视角来审视这场冲突，可以揭示常被忽略的深层结构性原因。

3. The ophthalmologist detected a slight opacity in the patient's **lens** during the routine eye examination.
   眼科医生在例行眼部检查中发现患者的晶状体有轻微混浊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lens" as something that "lends" you a clearer view — a lens lends focus to what you see.
把"lens"想成"lend（借给）"的变体——透镜借给你更清晰的视野。
