# 🔍 penetrate

🔊 /ˈpenɪtreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To pass through or into something; to permeate; to succeed in understanding something complex or mysterious
**中文：** v. 穿过；渗入；看穿，洞察

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permeate（渗透）
- pierce（刺穿）
- infiltrate（渗入）
- discern（洞察）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **penetrate the surface / penetrate the market / penetrate a mystery / penetrate deeply**

👉 Example: The detective managed to penetrate the mystery that had baffled investigators for years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Sunlight could barely **penetrate** the dense canopy of the ancient forest, leaving the ground in perpetual shadow.
   阳光几乎无法穿透古老森林茂密的树冠，使地面长期处于阴影之中。

2. The company developed an innovative strategy to **penetrate** emerging markets in Southeast Asia and expand its global reach.
   该公司制定了一项创新战略，以渗透东南亚新兴市场并扩大其全球影响力。

3. Only a few scholars have managed to fully **penetrate** the complex symbolism embedded within the medieval manuscript.
   只有少数学者成功地完全洞察了嵌入这部中世纪手稿中的复杂象征意义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pene-trate" — "pen" (like a pen piercing paper) + "trate" (entering through). A pen penetrates paper just as ideas penetrate the mind.
把"penetrate"想成"pen（笔）+ etrate（穿入）"——笔尖刺穿纸张，就像思想穿透心灵一样。
