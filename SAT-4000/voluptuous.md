# 🌹 voluptuous

🔊 /vəˈlʌptʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or characterized by luxury and sensual pleasure; curvaceous and sexually attractive
**中文：** adj. 撩人的；性感的；骄奢淫逸的；沉溺于感官享受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sensuous（感官的）
- luscious（甘美的）
- hedonistic（享乐主义的）
- opulent（奢华的）
- sumptuous（豪华的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **voluptuous curves / voluptuous pleasure / voluptuous lifestyle / voluptuous figure**

👉 Example: The painting depicted a voluptuous feast filled with exotic fruits, silks, and flickering candlelight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Renaissance artist captured the **voluptuous** beauty of the natural landscape through rich colors and flowing forms.
   这位文艺复兴时期的艺术家通过丰富的色彩和流畅的形态捕捉了自然风景的感官之美。

2. Critics described the novel's prose as **voluptuous**, filled with elaborate imagery and deeply sensory descriptions throughout.
   评论家将这部小说的散文形容为感官丰富的，充满了精致的意象和深刻的感官描写。

3. The aristocratic court was known for its **voluptuous** excess, where lavish banquets and entertainment continued without restraint.
   这个贵族宫廷以其奢靡无度而闻名，奢华的宴会和娱乐活动不受节制地持续进行。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voluptuous" as coming from Latin "voluptas" meaning pleasure — it describes things that are full of sensual delight.
把"voluptuous"想成来自拉丁语"voluptas"，意为快乐——它描述充满感官愉悦的事物。
