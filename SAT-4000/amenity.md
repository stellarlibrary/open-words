# 🏨 amenity

🔊 /əˈmɛnɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A desirable or useful feature of a building, place, or facility; a pleasant quality or courtesy.
**中文：** 舒适；礼仪；便利设施

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- facility（设施）
- convenience（便利）
- comfort（舒适）
- luxury（奢华设施）
- courtesy（礼貌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public amenities, hotel amenities, modern amenities, basic amenities, social amenities**
👉 Example: The resort offers a wide range of amenities for its distinguished guests.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The residential complex boasts outstanding **amenities** including a gymnasium, swimming pool, and private library.
   该住宅小区拥有出色的便利设施，包括健身房、游泳池和私人图书馆。

2. Rural communities often lack basic **amenities** such as reliable internet access and adequate healthcare services.
   农村社区往往缺乏基本的便利设施，如可靠的互联网接入和充足的医疗服务。

3. The diplomatic reception was conducted with every **amenity** befitting a gathering of international dignitaries.
   这次外交招待会以符合国际政要聚会的一切礼仪来举办。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amenity" contains "amen" — think of saying "Amen!" in appreciation when you discover nice facilities and comforts.
"Amenity"包含"amen"——想象当你发现优质设施和舒适环境时赞叹说"Amen！"（阿门！太好了！）。
