# ✨ immaculate

🔊 /ɪˈmækjʊlət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Perfectly clean, tidy, or neat; free from any flaw, error, or blemish; morally pure
**中文：** adj. 纯洁无瑕的；完美的；洁净的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flawless（完美无瑕的）
- spotless（一尘不染的）
- pristine（原始的，崭新的）
- impeccable（无可挑剔的）
- unblemished（无瑕疵的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **immaculate condition / immaculate record / immaculate appearance / immaculate performance / keep immaculate**

👉 Example: The vintage automobile was maintained in immaculate condition despite being over fifty years old.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon's **immaculate** record of successful operations over two decades earned her widespread recognition throughout the medical community.
   这位外科医生二十年来完美的手术成功记录为她在医学界赢得了广泛赞誉。

2. The diplomat arrived at the summit wearing an **immaculate** suit that conveyed both authority and meticulous attention to detail.
   这位外交官身穿一套洁净无瑕的西装出席峰会，传递出权威和对细节的极致关注。

3. The research team presented an **immaculate** analysis of the data, leaving no room for criticism from the peer review committee.
   研究团队提交了一份完美无瑕的数据分析，使同行评审委员会无可挑剔。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "immaculate" as "im (not) + maculate (stained)" — literally "not stained," meaning perfectly clean and without any blemish.
把"immaculate"想成"im（不）+ maculate（有污点的）"——字面意思是"没有污点的"，即完美洁净、毫无瑕疵。
