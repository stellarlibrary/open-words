# 🎬 producer

🔊 /prəˈdjuːsər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person or entity that makes, creates, or manufactures something; a person responsible for the financial and administrative aspects of a film, broadcast, or stage production
**中文：** n. 制作人，制片人；生产者；发生器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- manufacturer（制造商）
- creator（创造者）
- maker（制作者）
- filmmaker（电影制作人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **film producer / music producer / leading producer / producer of goods**

👉 Example: The executive producer secured funding for the independent film that later won numerous awards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The country has become one of the world's leading **producers** of renewable energy through substantial investment in wind power.
   通过对风能的大量投资，该国已成为世界领先的可再生能源生产者之一。

2. The film **producer** carefully assembled a talented cast and crew to bring the acclaimed novel to the screen.
   电影制片人精心组建了一支才华横溢的演员和剧组团队，将这部广受赞誉的小说搬上银幕。

3. As the primary **producer** in the ecosystem, phytoplankton convert sunlight into energy that sustains marine food chains.
   作为生态系统中的主要生产者，浮游植物将阳光转化为维持海洋食物链的能量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "producer" as "produce + er" — a person who produces or brings something into existence, whether goods or creative works.
把"producer"想成"produce（生产）+ er（人）"——生产或创造某物的人。
