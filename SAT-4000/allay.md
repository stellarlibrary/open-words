# 🕊 allay

🔊 /əˈleɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To diminish or put to rest fears, suspicions, or worries; to relieve or alleviate pain or suffering.
**中文：** 减轻；使缓和；使平静。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alleviate（减轻）
- mitigate（缓和）
- soothe（抚慰）
- assuage（缓和）
- pacify（使平静）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **allay fears, allay concerns, allay suspicions, allay anxiety, allay doubts**
👉 Example: The official statement was issued to allay public fears about the outbreak.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government released detailed safety data in an effort to **allay** the mounting concerns of the general public.
   政府公布了详细的安全数据，以努力缓解公众日益增长的担忧。

2. The physician's calm and thorough explanation helped to **allay** the patient's anxiety before the surgical procedure.
   医生冷静而详尽的解释帮助缓解了病人在手术前的焦虑。

3. No amount of reassurance from the committee could fully **allay** the investors' deep-seated suspicions about the merger.
   委员会再多的保证也无法完全消除投资者对合并的根深蒂固的质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "allay" as "a-LAY" — to "lay down" your fears and worries, putting them to rest.
把"allay"想成"a-LAY（放下）"——把你的恐惧和忧虑"放下"，让它们平息。
