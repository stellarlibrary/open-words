# 🔬 rigorous

🔊 /ˈrɪɡərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely thorough and careful; strict, severe, or demanding
**中文：** adj. 严格的，严厉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stringent（严格的）
- meticulous（一丝不苟的）
- exacting（苛刻的）
- thorough（彻底的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rigorous standards / rigorous analysis / rigorous training / rigorous testing**

👉 Example: The university is known for its rigorous academic standards and demanding coursework.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company conducted **rigorous** clinical trials before submitting the new drug for regulatory approval.
   制药公司在提交新药审批之前进行了严格的临床试验。

2. Her **rigorous** approach to data analysis ensured that every conclusion was supported by verifiable empirical evidence.
   她严密的数据分析方法确保每个结论都有可验证的实证支持。

3. Military recruits must endure **rigorous** physical training designed to test their endurance and mental fortitude.
   军事新兵必须经受严格的体能训练，以考验他们的耐力和意志力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rigor" (strictness) + "-ous" — something full of rigor is rigorous, demanding the highest standards.
把"rigorous"想成"rigor（严格）+ -ous"——充满严格要求的，就是严格的、严密的。
