# 🌿 germination

🔊 /ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process by which a seed develops into a new plant; the beginning or coming into existence of something
**中文：** n. 发芽；萌芽；发生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sprouting（发芽）
- budding（萌芽）
- emergence（出现）
- inception（开端）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seed germination / the germination of an idea / germination period / germination rate**

👉 Example: The germination of wheat seeds requires consistent moisture and moderate temperatures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **germination** rate of the experimental crop exceeded expectations under the carefully controlled laboratory conditions.
   在精心控制的实验室条件下，实验作物的发芽率超出了预期。

2. Historians trace the **germination** of democratic ideals to philosophical movements that emerged during the Enlightenment era.
   历史学家将民主理想的萌芽追溯到启蒙时代出现的哲学运动。

3. Adequate soil temperature is essential for the successful **germination** of most commercially cultivated vegetable species.
   适宜的土壤温度对大多数商业种植蔬菜品种的成功发芽至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "germination" as "germ" + "nation" — a whole nation of new plants growing from tiny germs (seeds).
把"germination"想成"germ"（胚芽）+"nation"（国家）——一整个由微小胚芽（种子）生长出来的植物王国。
