# 💫 revive

🔊 /rɪˈvaɪv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To restore to life, consciousness, or strength; to bring back into use, activity, or existence
**中文：** v. 恢复；复苏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resuscitate（使复苏）
- rejuvenate（使恢复活力）
- restore（恢复）
- renew（更新）
- rekindle（重新点燃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **revive interest / revive a tradition / revive the economy / revive hopes / revive an old debate**

👉 Example: The discovery of the ancient manuscript revived scholarly interest in the forgotten civilization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Emergency responders worked tirelessly to **revive** the unconscious swimmer who had been pulled from the freezing lake.
   急救人员不懈努力地抢救从冰冷湖水中被拉出的昏迷游泳者。

2. The newly elected governor pledged to **revive** the region's struggling economy through targeted investment and comprehensive tax reform.
   新当选的州长承诺通过定向投资和全面税制改革来复兴该地区低迷的经济。

3. Several prominent universities have sought to **revive** classical liberal arts education as a counterbalance to narrow professional training.
   几所著名大学试图复兴古典人文教育，以此作为狭隘职业培训的一种平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-" (again) + "vive" (live, as in "vivid") — to make something live again, to bring it back to life.
把"revive"想成"re-（再次）+ vive（活，如vivid中的词根）"——使某物再次活过来，恢复生机。
