# 🌀 tortuous

🔊 /ˈtɔːrtʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Full of twists and turns; winding; excessively lengthy, complex, or indirect in expression or reasoning
**中文：** 扭曲的；弯曲的；迂回的；啰嗦的；复杂曲折的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- winding（蜿蜒的）
- convoluted（复杂的）
- serpentine（蛇形的）
- circuitous（迂回的）
- labyrinthine（迷宫般的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tortuous path / tortuous route / tortuous process / tortuous logic / tortuous negotiations**

👉 Example: The tortuous mountain road demanded extreme caution from even the most experienced drivers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **tortuous** negotiations between the two nations lasted over three years before a comprehensive peace agreement was finally reached.
   两国之间曲折的谈判持续了三年多，最终才达成全面和平协议。

2. Hikers navigated the **tortuous** trail through the dense forest, encountering sharp switchbacks and narrow cliff-side passages.
   徒步旅行者在茂密的森林中沿着蜿蜒的小路前行，遇到了急转弯和狭窄的悬崖边通道。

3. The author's **tortuous** prose style, filled with lengthy digressions and nested clauses, frustrated many impatient readers.
   这位作者啰嗦的行文风格充满了冗长的离题和嵌套从句，令许多缺乏耐心的读者感到沮丧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tortuous" as "tortoise-ous" — a tortoise walks in slow, winding paths, just as a tortuous road twists and turns endlessly.
把"tortuous"想成"tortoise（乌龟）-ous"——乌龟走缓慢而蜿蜒的路径，就像tortuous的道路不停地弯曲和扭转。
