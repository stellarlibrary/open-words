# 💣 subvert

🔊 /səbˈvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To undermine the power and authority of an established system or institution; to overthrow; to destroy or corrupt from within
**中文：** v. 推翻；搅乱，暗中破坏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- undermine（破坏）
- overthrow（推翻）
- destabilize（动摇）
- sabotage（蓄意破坏）
- corrupt（腐化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subvert the government / subvert authority / subvert expectations / subvert the system / subvert democracy**

👉 Example: Foreign agents attempted to subvert the democratic process through widespread disinformation campaigns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rebel faction sought to **subvert** the established government by infiltrating key military and intelligence institutions.
   叛乱派系试图通过渗透关键的军事和情报机构来颠覆现有政府。

2. Critics argued that the new legislation would effectively **subvert** the constitutional protections guaranteed to every citizen.
   批评者认为，新立法将有效地破坏宪法赋予每位公民的保护权利。

3. The filmmaker chose to **subvert** traditional narrative conventions, creating a work that challenged audiences' fundamental expectations.
   这位电影制作人选择颠覆传统的叙事惯例，创作了一部挑战观众基本期望的作品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-vert" as "sub" (under) + "vert" (turn) — to turn something from underneath, to overturn or overthrow it secretly.
把"subvert"想成"sub"（在下面）+ "vert"（转）——从下面翻转某物，即秘密地推翻或颠覆它。
