# 🌍 pandemic

🔊 /pænˈdemɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Prevalent over a whole country or the world. (n.) An outbreak of a disease that occurs over a wide geographic area and affects a large proportion of the population
**中文：** adj. （全国、全球）流行的；普遍的 n. 流行性疾病；大流行

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- epidemic（流行病）
- outbreak（爆发）
- plague（瘟疫）
- contagion（传染病）
- widespread（广泛传播的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **global pandemic / pandemic outbreak / pandemic response / declare a pandemic**

👉 Example: The World Health Organization officially declared the viral outbreak a pandemic after it spread to over one hundred countries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pandemic** forced governments worldwide to implement unprecedented lockdown measures that disrupted economies and daily life profoundly.
   这场大流行迫使世界各国政府实施前所未有的封锁措施，深刻扰乱了经济和日常生活。

2. Historians have noted that **pandemic** diseases throughout the centuries have reshaped political boundaries and accelerated social transformations.
   历史学家指出，几个世纪以来的流行性疾病重塑了政治版图并加速了社会变革。

3. The international community must develop coordinated strategies to ensure a more effective response to future **pandemic** threats.
   国际社会必须制定协调一致的战略，以确保对未来大流行威胁做出更有效的应对。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pan-demic" — "pan" means all and "demic" relates to people (demos); a pandemic affects ALL the people across wide regions.
想想"pan-demic"——"pan"意为"全部"，"demic"与人民（demos）有关；pandemic就是影响大范围所有人的疾病。
