# 🎯 subtlety

🔊 /ˈsʌtlti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being subtle; delicacy of perception, discernment, or distinction; a fine or slight difference
**中文：** n. 微妙，敏锐，细微的区别

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nuance（细微差别）
- delicacy（精妙）
- refinement（精细）
- finesse（巧妙）
- intricacy（复杂精细）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the subtlety of / lack subtlety / appreciate the subtlety / with great subtlety / subtleties of language**

👉 Example: The subtleties of the legal argument were lost on those without formal training in constitutional law.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **subtlety** of her argument impressed the panel of judges, who noted its remarkable depth and intellectual sophistication.
   她论点的微妙令评审团印象深刻，他们注意到了其非凡的深度和学术精妙。

2. Many readers fail to appreciate the **subtlety** of the author's satire, mistaking it for straightforward social commentary.
   许多读者未能领会作者讽刺的微妙之处，将其误认为直白的社会评论。

3. Mastering the **subtleties** of diplomatic protocol requires years of careful observation, practice, and cultural awareness.
   掌握外交礼仪的细微之处需要多年的仔细观察、实践和文化意识。

把"subtlety"想成"subtle"（微妙的）+ "ty"——subtle的名词形式。具有subtlety的事物有着你必须仔细观察才能发现的多层含义。
