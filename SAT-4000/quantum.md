# ⚛️ quantum

🔊 /ˈkwɑːntəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A discrete quantity of energy or the minimum amount of a physical entity. (adj.) Relating to quantum theory or quantum mechanics; a significant advance or sudden large change
**中文：** n. 量子；份额 adj. 量子的；巨大的（飞跃）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- particle（粒子）
- unit（单位）
- portion（份额）
- increment（增量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quantum leap / quantum mechanics / quantum physics / quantum computing**

👉 Example: The discovery represented a quantum leap in our understanding of subatomic particles.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The development of **quantum** computing promises to revolutionize data processing and cryptographic security worldwide.
   量子计算的发展有望彻底改变全球的数据处理和密码安全。

2. Scientists observed that energy is emitted in discrete **quantum** units rather than in a continuous stream.
   科学家观察到能量以离散的量子为单位发射，而非以连续流的形式释放。

3. The new treatment represented a **quantum** leap forward in the field of regenerative medicine and tissue repair.
   这种新疗法代表了再生医学和组织修复领域的一次巨大飞跃。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quantum" as the smallest "quantity" — a tiny, indivisible packet of energy that makes up the universe.
把"quantum"想成最小的"quantity（数量）"——组成宇宙的最小的、不可分割的能量包。
