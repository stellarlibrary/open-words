# 🐎 bestride

🔊 /bɪˈstraɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To sit or stand astride; to straddle; to mount or ride with one leg on each side
**中文：** v. 跨骑；跨越

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- straddle（跨坐）
- mount（骑上）
- span（横跨）
- ride（骑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bestride a horse / bestride the world / bestride the stage**

👉 Example: The legendary conqueror bestrode the ancient world like a colossus, commanding vast territories and armies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The fearless knight **bestrode** his warhorse with commanding authority as the regiment prepared for battle at dawn.
   无畏的骑士威严地跨坐在战马上，团队在黎明时分准备作战。

2. Shakespeare's Caesar was said to **bestride** the narrow world like a colossus, dwarfing all lesser men around him.
   莎士比亚笔下的凯撒据说像巨人一样跨越狭小的世界，使周围所有人都相形见绌。

3. The corporation now **bestrides** multiple industries, exerting enormous influence over global markets and trade policies alike.
   该公司现在横跨多个行业，对全球市场和贸易政策都有巨大的影响力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-STRIDE" — to take a big stride over something, with legs on both sides. Bestride means to sit astride or dominate.
想想"be-STRIDE"——迈一大步跨过某物，双腿在两边。Bestride就是跨坐或主宰。
