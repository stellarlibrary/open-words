# ⚖️ intercede

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To intervene on behalf of another person; to mediate or plead in a dispute between parties
**中文：** v. 调解；调停；求情；说项

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mediate（调解）
- intervene（干预）
- arbitrate（仲裁）
- advocate（倡导）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intercede on behalf of / intercede with someone / intercede in a dispute / intercede for someone**

👉 Example: The ambassador agreed to intercede on behalf of the detained citizens during diplomatic negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senior diplomat was asked to **intercede** in the territorial dispute before tensions between the two nations escalated further.
   这位资深外交官被要求在两国之间的领土争端进一步升级之前进行调停。

2. Several community leaders chose to **intercede** on behalf of the displaced families, urging the government to provide immediate assistance.
   几位社区领袖选择为流离失所的家庭说情，敦促政府提供紧急援助。

3. The professor decided to **intercede** with the administration when the scholarship committee unfairly rejected the qualified applicant's appeal.
   当奖学金委员会不公正地拒绝了这位合格申请人的申诉时，教授决定向管理层求情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-cede" — "inter" means between, and "cede" comes from Latin "cedere" meaning to go, so to intercede is to go between two parties to resolve a conflict.
把"intercede"想成"inter(之间) + cede(走)"——走到双方之间去，即居中调解。
