# 📝 perspicuity

🔊 /ˌpɜːrspɪˈkjuːɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being clear and easy to understand; lucidity and plainness in expression or style
**中文：** n. （语言、文章、表达等的）明晰；简明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clarity（清晰）
- lucidity（明了）
- intelligibility（可理解性）
- transparency（透明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perspicuity of expression / perspicuity of style / perspicuity of language / admirable perspicuity**

👉 Example: The professor was praised for the perspicuity of her lectures on otherwise dense philosophical topics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legal scholar was celebrated for the **perspicuity** of her writing, which made complex constitutional arguments accessible to all.
   这位法学学者因其写作的明晰而备受赞誉，使复杂的宪法论点对所有人来说都通俗易懂。

2. Scientific journals increasingly value **perspicuity** in submitted manuscripts to ensure that research findings reach the broadest audience.
   科学期刊越来越重视投稿论文的明晰性，以确保研究成果能传达给最广泛的读者。

3. The diplomat's **perspicuity** in drafting the resolution prevented the misinterpretations that had plagued previous international agreements.
   这位外交官在起草决议时的明晰性避免了困扰以往国际协议的误解。

把"perspicuity"想成"perspi-CU-ity"——就像"清晰地看穿"，文字如此清楚，你可以直接看到其含义。
