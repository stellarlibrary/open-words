# 💬 forthright

🔊 /ˈfɔːrθraɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Direct and outspoken; straightforward and honest in manner or speech
**中文：** adj. 直率的；直截了当的；径直的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- candid（坦率的）
- blunt（直言不讳的）
- frank（坦白的）
- outspoken（直言的）
- straightforward（直截了当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **forthright manner / forthright answer / forthright about / forthright in one's views**

👉 Example: The director was forthright about the challenges facing the organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's **forthright** acknowledgment of the policy failure earned her considerable respect from voters across the aisle.
   这位参议员对政策失败的坦率承认为她赢得了两党选民的极大尊重。

2. Researchers appreciated Dr. Huang's **forthright** assessment of the study's limitations, which strengthened the paper's overall credibility.
   研究人员赞赏黄博士对研究局限性的直率评估，这增强了论文的整体可信度。

3. In diplomatic negotiations, a **forthright** approach can sometimes achieve more progress than carefully guarded and evasive rhetoric.
   在外交谈判中，直截了当的方式有时比小心翼翼、闪烁其词的辞令能取得更大的进展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "forth-right" as speaking "forth" (forward) and "right" (correctly) — someone who speaks directly and rightly, without hesitation.
把"forthright"想成"forth"（向前）+"right"（正确地）——一个说话直接且正确的人，毫不犹豫。
