# 💰 avarice

🔊 /ˈævərɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Avarice is an extreme greed for wealth or material gain; an insatiable desire to accumulate money or possessions.

**中文：** 贪婪，贪财；对财富或物质利益的极度贪求，对金钱或财物的贪得无厌。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- greed （贪婪）
- cupidity （贪心）
- rapacity （贪婪，掠夺）
- covetousness （垂涎，贪求）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Avarice" is a formal and literary word often used in moral or philosophical contexts. It carries a stronger negative connotation than "greed" and is frequently associated with one of the seven deadly sins.

"avarice"是一个正式的文学用词，常用于道德或哲学语境。它比"greed"带有更强烈的贬义，常与七宗罪之一相关联。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The merchant's **avarice** drove him to exploit his workers and hoard every coin he earned.
   *那个商人的**贪婪**驱使他剥削工人，囤积他赚到的每一枚硬币。*

2. Throughout history, unchecked **avarice** has been the downfall of many powerful empires and rulers.
   *纵观历史，不加约束的**贪婪**导致了许多强大帝国和统治者的覆灭。*

3. The novel portrays **avarice** as a destructive force that corrupts even the most virtuous individuals.
   *这部小说将**贪婪**描绘为一种破坏性力量，甚至能腐蚀最正直的人。*

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Avarice" sounds like "I want rice" — imagine someone so greedy they want ALL the rice, never satisfied with what they have.

"avarice"听起来像"I want rice"（我要米）——想象一个人贪心到想要所有的米，永远不满足于自己所拥有的。
