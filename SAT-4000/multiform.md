# 🔷 multiform

🔊 /ˈmʌltɪfɔːrm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having many different forms, shapes, or kinds; occurring in great variety
**中文：** adj. 多样的；多种形式的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diverse（多样的）
- varied（各种各样的）
- manifold（多方面的）
- heterogeneous（异质的）
- multifaceted（多层面的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **multiform nature / multiform expressions / multiform challenges / multiform approach**

👉 Example: The multiform nature of the disease made it exceptionally difficult for researchers to develop a single treatment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **multiform** cultural traditions of the region reflect centuries of interaction among diverse ethnic and linguistic groups.
   该地区多样的文化传统反映了不同民族和语言群体之间数百年的交流。

2. Scientists discovered that the organism exhibited **multiform** characteristics depending on the specific environmental conditions it encountered.
   科学家发现该生物体根据所遇到的特定环境条件表现出多种形态特征。

3. The architect's **multiform** design philosophy incorporated elements from classical, modern, and indigenous building traditions seamlessly.
   这位建筑师多样化的设计理念将古典、现代和本土建筑传统的元素无缝融合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "multiform" as "MULTI + FORM" — literally many forms, just as a shape-shifter takes on multiple different forms.
把"multiform"想成"MULTI（多）+ FORM（形式）"——字面意思就是多种形式，就像变形者呈现出多种不同的形态。
