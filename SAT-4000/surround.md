# 🔵 surround

🔊 /səˈraʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective | 动词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To encircle; to enclose on all sides. (n.) Something that surrounds. (adj.) Relating to surround sound.
**中文：** v. 包围，环绕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- encircle（环绕）
- enclose（围住）
- encompass（包围）
- ring（围绕）
- besiege（围攻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surround oneself with / surrounded by / surround the area / surround sound**

👉 Example: The ancient castle was surrounded by a deep moat that served as its primary defense.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Dense forests **surround** the remote village, making it accessible only by a narrow mountain road.
   茂密的森林环绕着这个偏远的村庄，使其只能通过一条狭窄的山路进入。

2. The police moved quickly to **surround** the building after receiving reports of suspicious activity inside.
   警方在接到建筑物内有可疑活动的报告后迅速行动包围了该建筑。

3. Successful leaders often **surround** themselves with talented advisors who offer diverse perspectives and expertise.
   成功的领导者往往身边围绕着有才华的顾问，他们提供多元的视角和专业知识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sur-round" — something that goes "round" all around you, forming a complete circle.
把"surround"想成"sur-round"——某物围着你转了一"圈"（round），形成一个完整的环。
