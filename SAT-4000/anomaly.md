# 🔍 anomaly

🔊 /əˈnɑːməli/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Something that deviates from what is standard, normal, or expected; an irregularity or inconsistency
**中文：** 异常；不规则；反常事物；异常现象

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aberration（偏差）
- irregularity（不规则）
- deviation（偏离）
- oddity（怪异）
- exception（例外）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **statistical anomaly / detect an anomaly / anomaly in the data / climate anomaly / genetic anomaly**

👉 Example: The researcher identified a significant anomaly in the experimental data that warranted further investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sudden temperature **anomaly** detected in the Arctic region prompted climatologists to reevaluate their existing atmospheric models.
   在北极地区检测到的突然温度异常促使气候学家重新评估其现有的大气模型。

2. Auditors discovered a financial **anomaly** in the quarterly statements that suggested possible irregularities in the accounting procedures.
   审计人员在季度报表中发现了一个财务异常，表明会计程序中可能存在违规行为。

3. The physician noted that the patient's blood test results revealed an **anomaly** requiring additional diagnostic evaluation and monitoring.
   医生注意到患者的血液检测结果显示出一个异常，需要进一步的诊断评估和监测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "anomaly" as "a-normal-y" — something that is not normal, an abnormality that stands out from the pattern.
把"anomaly"想成"a-normal-y"（非正常的）——某种不正常的东西，从规律中突显出来的异常。
