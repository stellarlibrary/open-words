# 😤 crass

🔊 /kræs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing no sensitivity or refinement; grossly insensitive, crude, or lacking in subtlety
**中文：** adj. 愚钝的；粗鲁的；粗糙的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boorish（粗野的）
- vulgar（粗俗的）
- insensitive（迟钝的）
- obtuse（愚钝的）
- uncouth（粗鲁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crass behavior / crass remark / crass ignorance / crass materialism**

👉 Example: His crass remark about the tragedy offended everyone present at the memorial service.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **crass** comments about the host nation's customs created an international incident that took months to resolve.
   这位外交官对东道国风俗的粗鲁评论引发了一起需要数月才能解决的国际事件。

2. Critics condemned the film for its **crass** commercialism, arguing that it sacrificed artistic integrity for mass-market appeal.
   评论家谴责这部电影粗俗的商业主义，认为它为了迎合大众市场而牺牲了艺术完整性。

3. Such **crass** disregard for environmental consequences reveals a profound failure of corporate responsibility and moral judgment.
   如此愚钝地无视环境后果，暴露了企业责任和道德判断的严重缺失。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crass" as "crash" without the 'h' — someone whose behavior crashes through all boundaries of politeness and taste.
把"crass"想成没有'h'的"crash（冲撞）"——一个行为粗鲁、冲破所有礼貌和品味界限的人。
