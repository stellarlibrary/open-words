# 🎭 stratagem

🔊 /ˈstrætədʒəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A plan or scheme, especially one used to outwit an opponent or achieve a goal through cunning or deception
**中文：** n. 计策，计谋，策略

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ploy（策略）
- ruse（诡计）
- maneuver（计谋）
- tactic（战术）
- scheme（计划）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **military stratagem / clever stratagem / political stratagem / employ a stratagem**

👉 Example: The general devised a brilliant stratagem to draw the enemy forces into an inescapable ambush.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat employed a clever **stratagem** to bring the two hostile nations to the negotiating table without either losing face.
   那位外交官运用了一个巧妙的策略，使两个敌对国家在不丢面子的情况下坐到了谈判桌前。

2. Throughout history, military commanders have relied on elaborate **stratagems** to compensate for inferior numbers on the battlefield.
   纵观历史，军事指挥官一直依靠精心设计的计谋来弥补战场上兵力的不足。

3. Her courtroom **stratagem** of presenting the overlooked evidence last caught the opposing counsel completely off guard during trial.
   她在庭审中最后出示被忽视的证据这一诉讼策略令对方律师完全措手不及。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "strata-gem" — a gem hidden in layers (strata), just as a stratagem is a clever plan concealed beneath layers of deception.
把"stratagem"想成"strata-gem"——隐藏在层层（strata）之中的宝石（gem），就像计谋是隐藏在层层迷惑之下的巧妙计划。
