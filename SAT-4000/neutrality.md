# ⚖️ neutrality

🔊 /njuːˈtræləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of not supporting or helping either side in a conflict or disagreement; impartiality
**中文：** n. 中立；中性；中立立场

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impartiality（公正）
- objectivity（客观）
- detachment（超然）
- nonalignment（不结盟）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain neutrality / political neutrality / armed neutrality / strict neutrality**

👉 Example: The small nation declared its neutrality at the outset of the international conflict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Switzerland has historically maintained a policy of **neutrality** during major European conflicts and world wars.
   瑞士在重大欧洲冲突和世界大战期间一直保持中立政策。

2. The mediator's strict **neutrality** was essential for both parties to trust the outcome of the negotiations.
   调解人严格的中立立场对于双方信任谈判结果至关重要。

3. Critics argued that the journalist's apparent **neutrality** masked a subtle bias toward the governing party's agenda.
   批评者认为该记者表面上的中立掩盖了对执政党议程的微妙偏见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neutral" + "-ity" — the quality of being neutral, like a car in neutral gear that moves neither forward nor backward.
把"neutrality"想成"neutral（中立的）"加"-ity（状态）"——就像汽车挂在空挡，既不前进也不后退。
