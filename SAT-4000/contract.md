# 📄 contract

🔊 /ˈkɒntrækt (n.) kənˈtrækt (v.)/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A legally binding agreement between parties. (v.) To shrink or become smaller; to enter into a formal agreement; to acquire or catch a disease
**中文：** n. 合同；契约 v. 收缩；缩小；订约；感染

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agreement（协议）
- shrink（收缩）
- compress（压缩）
- acquire（感染；获得）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sign a contract / contract a disease / contract with / the muscles contract / breach of contract**

👉 Example: The steel beams contract significantly when temperatures drop below freezing during the winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Both parties reviewed the terms thoroughly before signing the **contract** that would govern their business partnership for five years.
   双方在签署将管辖其五年商业合作关系的合同之前，彻底审查了各项条款。

2. Metals **contract** when exposed to extremely low temperatures, a principle that engineers must account for in structural design.
   金属在极低温度下会收缩，这是工程师在结构设计中必须考虑的原理。

3. Several researchers **contracted** a rare tropical illness while conducting fieldwork in the dense equatorial rainforest region.
   几位研究人员在赤道热带雨林地区进行实地考察时感染了一种罕见的热带疾病。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "contract" as "con-tract" — people come together (con) to draw up (tract) an agreement, and things can also be drawn tighter, or contracted.
把"contract"想成"con（一起）+ tract（拉）"——人们聚在一起拉拢达成协议，事物也可以被拉紧，即收缩。
