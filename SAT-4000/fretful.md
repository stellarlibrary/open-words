# 😤 fretful

🔊 /ˈfrɛtfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Feeling or expressing distress or irritation; anxious and restless
**中文：** 焦躁的；烦躁的；易怒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritable（易怒的）
- restless（焦躁不安的）
- peevish（暴躁的）
- anxious（焦虑的）
- querulous（爱抱怨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fretful baby / fretful mood / fretful night / grow fretful**

👉 Example: The long delay at the airport made the passengers increasingly fretful and impatient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fretful** child refused to sleep, crying intermittently throughout the long and exhausting night.
   那个烦躁的孩子拒绝入睡，在漫长而疲惫的夜晚间歇性地哭泣。

2. Investors grew **fretful** as the stock market continued its unpredictable decline over several consecutive weeks.
   随着股市连续数周不可预测地下跌，投资者变得越来越焦躁不安。

3. Her **fretful** demeanor during the interview suggested she was unprepared for the rigorous questioning ahead.
   她在面试中焦躁的举止表明她对即将到来的严格提问准备不足。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fret + ful" — when you are full of fret (worry), you become fretful, anxious and irritable.
把"fretful"想成"fret（烦恼）+ ful（充满）"——当你充满烦恼时，你就变得焦躁不安。
