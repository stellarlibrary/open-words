# 🌾 pastoral

🔊 /ˈpæstərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the countryside; depicting rural life in an idealized way; relating to a pastor's duties. (n.) A work of art portraying rural life
**中文：** adj. 田园生活的；乡村的；牧师的；牧人的 n. 牧歌；田园诗；田园景色

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rural（乡村的）
- bucolic（田园的）
- rustic（乡野的）
- idyllic（田园诗般的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pastoral landscape / pastoral poetry / pastoral care / pastoral scene / pastoral life**

👉 Example: The painter captured a serene pastoral scene of sheep grazing beside a quiet stream.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nineteenth-century novel presented an idealized **pastoral** vision of English country life that contrasted sharply with urban industrialization.
   这部十九世纪的小说呈现了理想化的英国乡村田园景象，与城市工业化形成了鲜明对比。

2. The university appointed a new dean of students to oversee **pastoral** care and support the emotional well-being of undergraduates.
   大学任命了一位新的学生事务主任来负责教牧关怀，支持本科生的情感健康。

3. Beethoven's Sixth Symphony, known as the **Pastoral**, evokes the tranquil beauty of the countryside through its melodic compositions.
   贝多芬的第六交响曲，即《田园交响曲》，通过其旋律优美的乐曲唤起了乡村的宁静之美。

Think of "pastor-al" as relating to a "pasture" — green fields where animals graze in peaceful countryside settings.
把"pastoral"联想到"pasture（牧场）"——动物在宁静的乡间绿色田野上吃草的场景。
