# 🔮 esoteric

🔊 /ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with specialized knowledge or interest; obscure and difficult to comprehend
**中文：** adj. 秘传的；难懂的；深奥的；隐秘的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arcane（神秘的）
- obscure（晦涩的）
- abstruse（深奥的）
- cryptic（隐晦的）
- recondite（深奥的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **esoteric knowledge / esoteric subject / esoteric texts / esoteric traditions**

👉 Example: The professor's lecture on esoteric philosophy was fascinating but accessible to only a handful of specialists.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient manuscript contained **esoteric** teachings that were comprehensible only to scholars who had studied the tradition extensively.
   这份古代手稿包含的深奥教义只有深入研究过该传统的学者才能理解。

2. While quantum mechanics may seem **esoteric** to the general public, its principles underpin much of modern technological innovation.
   虽然量子力学对普通大众来说可能显得深奥难懂，但其原理支撑着许多现代技术创新。

3. The novelist incorporated **esoteric** references to medieval alchemy that only the most dedicated literary scholars would recognize and appreciate.
   这位小说家融入了对中世纪炼金术的隐秘引用，只有最专注的文学学者才能识别和欣赏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "esoteric" as "eso-terr-ic" — knowledge that is kept in an inner ("eso" from Greek "inner") territory, hidden from outsiders.
把"esoteric"想成"eso（内部）-terr（领域）-ic"——保存在内部领域的知识，对外人隐藏，所以是深奥难懂的。
