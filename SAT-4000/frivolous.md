# 🃏 frivolous

🔊 /ˈfrɪvələs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not having any serious purpose or value; carefree and superficial; (of a legal claim) lacking legal merit
**中文：** 无聊的；轻佻的；琐碎的；不严肃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trivial（琐碎的）
- flippant（轻率的）
- superficial（肤浅的）
- petty（无关紧要的）
- trifling（微不足道的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frivolous lawsuit / frivolous spending / frivolous remarks / frivolous pursuit**

👉 Example: The judge dismissed the frivolous lawsuit because it lacked any credible legal basis or evidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator condemned the **frivolous** spending of taxpayer money on projects that provided no measurable public benefit.
   参议员谴责将纳税人的钱轻率地花费在没有可衡量公共效益的项目上。

2. His **frivolous** attitude toward academic responsibilities ultimately led to his dismissal from the prestigious graduate program.
   他对学术责任的轻佻态度最终导致他被这个著名的研究生项目开除。

3. The court imposed sanctions on the attorney for repeatedly filing **frivolous** claims designed solely to harass the defendant.
   法院对该律师反复提起旨在骚扰被告的无聊诉讼进行了制裁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frivolous" as "free-vol-ous" — someone so free and carefree that they volunteer for nothing serious, only trivial things.
把"frivolous"想成"free（自由的）+ vol（意愿）"——一个如此自由随意的人，只愿意做琐碎无聊的事。
