# 💨 fumigate

🔊 /ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To disinfect or purify an area by applying chemical fumes or smoke to destroy pests, bacteria, or mold
**中文：** v. 熏制；香薰；用烟熏消毒；熏蒸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disinfect（消毒）
- sterilize（灭菌）
- smoke out（烟熏驱除）
- purify（净化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fumigate a building / fumigate for termites / fumigate the premises / fumigate with chemicals**

👉 Example: The health department ordered the warehouse fumigated after inspectors discovered a severe rodent infestation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The landlord was required to **fumigate** the entire apartment complex after tenants reported persistent infestations of cockroaches.
   在租户报告持续的蟑螂侵扰后，房东被要求对整个公寓楼进行熏蒸消毒。

2. Agricultural authorities decided to **fumigate** the imported grain shipment to eliminate any invasive insect species before distribution.
   农业部门决定对进口粮食进行熏蒸处理，以在分销前消灭任何入侵昆虫物种。

3. Museum conservators carefully **fumigated** the archival storage rooms to protect irreplaceable historical documents from mold and insects.
   博物馆文物保护人员仔细地对档案储藏室进行熏蒸，以保护不可替代的历史文献免受霉菌和昆虫侵害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fumigate" as "fume + gate" — opening the gate to let fumes in to clean and disinfect a space.
把"fumigate"想成"fume（烟雾）+ gate（门）"——打开门让烟雾进入，清洁和消毒一个空间。
