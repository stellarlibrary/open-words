# 🚫 disclaim

🔊 /dɪsˈkleɪm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To deny or renounce any claim to or connection with; to refuse to acknowledge or accept responsibility for
**中文：** v. 否认，拒绝；放弃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deny（否认）
- renounce（放弃）
- disavow（否认）
- repudiate（拒绝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disclaim responsibility / disclaim knowledge / disclaim ownership / disclaim any connection**

👉 Example: The company quickly disclaimed any responsibility for the defective products sold under its brand name.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician attempted to **disclaim** all knowledge of the scandal despite substantial evidence linking him to the affair.
   尽管有大量证据将其与丑闻联系起来，这位政客仍试图否认知情。

2. The manufacturer **disclaimed** liability for injuries resulting from improper use of the equipment as stated in the warranty.
   制造商在保修条款中声明，对因不当使用设备造成的伤害不承担责任。

3. She chose to **disclaim** her inheritance, allowing the entire estate to pass directly to her younger siblings instead.
   她选择放弃继承权，让整个遗产直接转给她的弟弟妹妹们。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-claim" as the opposite of "claim" — to disclaim is to push a claim away from yourself, to say "that is not mine."
把"disclaim"拆成"dis-claim"——"dis"表示相反，"claim"是声称，所以disclaim就是否认、不承认。
