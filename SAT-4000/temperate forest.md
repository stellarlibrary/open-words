# 🌲 temperate forest

🔊 /ˈtɛmpərɪt ˈfɔːrɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A forest biome found in temperate regions, characterized by moderate rainfall, distinct seasons, and deciduous or mixed tree species
**中文：** n. 温带森林

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- temperate woodland（温带林地）
- deciduous forest（落叶林）
- midlatitude forest（中纬度森林）
- broadleaf forest（阔叶林）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **temperate forest biome / temperate forest ecosystem / temperate forest region / temperate forest conservation**

👉 Example: Temperate forests support a wide variety of plant and animal species adapted to seasonal change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **temperate forest** biome covers vast areas of North America, Europe, and East Asia with rich biodiversity and seasonal foliage.
   温带森林生物群落覆盖了北美、欧洲和东亚的广阔地区，拥有丰富的生物多样性和季节性植被。

2. Researchers documented significant declines in **temperate forest** coverage due to decades of urbanization and unsustainable logging practices.
   研究人员记录了由于数十年的城市化和不可持续的伐木实践导致温带森林覆盖面积的显著减少。

3. Conservation programs aim to restore degraded **temperate forest** ecosystems by reintroducing native tree species and controlling invasive plants.
   保护项目旨在通过重新引入本地树种和控制入侵植物来恢复退化的温带森林生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temperate forest" as a forest with a mild "temper" — it experiences moderate conditions, neither tropical heat nor arctic cold, with gentle seasonal transitions.
把"temperate forest"想成一片有温和"脾气"的森林——它经历温和的条件，既不是热带的炎热也不是北极的寒冷，有着平缓的季节过渡。
