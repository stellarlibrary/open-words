# 🔬 specimen

🔊 /ˈspesɪmɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sample or example of something, used for examination or study; a representative part of a whole.
**中文：** n. 标本，样本

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sample（样品）
- example（例子）
- model（模型）
- instance（实例）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **specimen collection / specimen jar / blood specimen / fine specimen of**

👉 Example: The biologist carefully labeled each specimen before placing it under the microscope for analysis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory required a fresh **specimen** of tissue to conduct the diagnostic tests ordered by the physician.
   实验室需要一份新鲜的组织标本来进行医生要求的诊断测试。

2. This rare butterfly **specimen** was collected during an expedition to the remote highlands of South America.
   这个珍稀蝴蝶标本是在一次南美偏远高地考察期间采集的。

3. The museum displayed an impressive **specimen** of quartz crystal that weighed over two hundred kilograms.
   博物馆展出了一件重达两百多公斤的令人印象深刻的石英晶体标本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "speci-men" as "special men" — a specimen is a special individual picked out from a group to represent the whole.
把"specimen"想成"special men"（特别的人）——标本就是从一群中挑出来的特别个体，用来代表整体。
