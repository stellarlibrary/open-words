# 💡 diode

🔊 /ˈdaɪoʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A semiconductor device that allows electric current to flow in only one direction, commonly used in electronic circuits for rectification and signal processing
**中文：** n. 二极管；半导体二极管

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rectifier（整流器）
- semiconductor（半导体）
- vacuum tube（真空管）
- LED (light-emitting diode)（发光二极管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **light-emitting diode / silicon diode / diode bridge / diode circuit / Zener diode**

👉 Example: The engineer replaced the faulty diode in the circuit to restore proper current flow direction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The electrical engineer installed a **diode** in the circuit to prevent reverse current from damaging the sensitive electronic components downstream.
   电气工程师在电路中安装了一个二极管，以防止反向电流损坏下游的敏感电子元件。

2. Modern solar panels rely on specialized **diodes** to convert sunlight into usable electrical energy with increasing levels of efficiency.
   现代太阳能电池板依靠专用二极管将阳光转换为可用的电能，效率不断提高。

3. The invention of the light-emitting **diode** revolutionized the lighting industry by providing an energy-efficient alternative to traditional incandescent bulbs.
   发光二极管的发明通过提供节能的替代方案，彻底改变了照明行业对传统白炽灯泡的依赖。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "di-ode" as "two-road" — "di" means two, but a diode only lets traffic flow down one road, blocking the other direction.
把"diode"想成"双路"——"di"意为二，但二极管只允许电流沿一条路流动，阻断另一个方向。
