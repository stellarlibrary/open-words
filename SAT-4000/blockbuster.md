# 💥 blockbuster

🔊 /ˈblɒkˌbʌstər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Something remarkably successful or impressive, especially a film, book, or event that achieves enormous popularity; originally, a large bomb capable of destroying an entire block.
**中文：** n. 风靡一时之物；轰动的作品；一鸣惊人者；巨型炸弹

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sensation（轰动）
- hit（热门作品）
- phenomenon（现象级作品）
- smash（巨大成功）
- megahit（超级热门）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blockbuster film / blockbuster hit / summer blockbuster / blockbuster success / a real blockbuster**

👉 Example: The studio invested heavily in special effects, hoping to produce the summer's biggest blockbuster.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The director's latest **blockbuster** earned over one billion dollars worldwide within its first month of theatrical release.
   这位导演最新的**大片**在上映首月内全球票房收入超过十亿美元。

2. The pharmaceutical company's new drug proved to be a financial **blockbuster**, generating unprecedented revenue across global markets.
   该制药公司的新药被证明是一个财务上的**轰动之作**，在全球市场创造了前所未有的收入。

3. Critics initially dismissed the novel, yet it became the decade's most celebrated literary **blockbuster** among readers worldwide.
   评论家最初对这部小说不以为然，但它却成为全球读者心中十年来最受赞誉的文学**巨作**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "blockbuster" as "block + buster" — something so powerful it busts an entire block, just as a blockbuster hit takes the world by storm.
把"blockbuster"想成"block + buster"——强大到炸毁整个街区的东西，正如一部轰动作品席卷全球一样。
