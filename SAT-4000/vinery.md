# 🍇 vinery

🔊 /ˈvaɪnəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A greenhouse or enclosure for growing grapevines; a vineyard or area where vines are cultivated
**中文：** 葡萄园；葡萄温室；种植葡萄的场所

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vineyard（葡萄园）
- greenhouse（温室）
- conservatory（暖房）
- grapery（葡萄温室）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heated vinery / glass vinery / estate vinery / maintain a vinery**

👉 Example: The estate's vinery produced some of the finest table grapes in the entire region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Victorian estate featured an elaborate **vinery** where rare grape varieties were cultivated throughout the colder months.
   这座维多利亚时代的庄园拥有一座精致的葡萄温室，在寒冷的月份里培育稀有葡萄品种。

2. Skilled horticulturists managed the **vinery** with meticulous care, ensuring optimal temperature and humidity for each vine.
   技术熟练的园艺师精心管理着葡萄温室，确保每株葡萄藤都处于最佳温度和湿度条件下。

3. The university's botanical department restored the historic **vinery** to serve as a research facility for viticulture studies.
   大学植物学系修复了这座历史悠久的葡萄温室，使其成为葡萄栽培研究设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vinery" as "vine" + "-ery" (a place for) — a place specifically dedicated to growing vines, just like a bakery is for baking.
把"vinery"想成"vine"（葡萄藤）加上"-ery"（场所后缀）——一个专门种植葡萄藤的地方，就像bakery是烘焙的地方一样。
