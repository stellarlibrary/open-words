# 🔢 fraction

🔊 /ˈfrækʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A numerical quantity that is not a whole number; a small or tiny part, amount, or proportion of something
**中文：** n. 分数；部分；小部分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- portion（部分）
- fragment（碎片）
- segment（片段）
- sliver（小部分）
- percentage（百分比）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a fraction of / fraction of the cost / fraction of a second / small fraction / tiny fraction**

👉 Example: The new method achieves the same results at a fraction of the original cost.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Only a small **fraction** of the applicants were admitted to the highly competitive graduate program in molecular biology.
   只有一小部分申请者被录取进入竞争激烈的分子生物学研究生项目。

2. The experimental device can perform calculations in a **fraction** of the time required by conventional computing systems.
   这种实验设备能在传统计算系统所需时间的极小部分内完成计算。

3. Renewable energy currently accounts for a growing but still modest **fraction** of the world's total electricity generation capacity.
   可再生能源目前在全球总发电能力中所占比例虽在增长，但仍然较小。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fraction" as "fract-ion" — "fract" comes from Latin "frangere" meaning "to break." A fraction is something broken into smaller parts.
把"fraction"想成"fract"（打碎）+"ion"——"fract"来自拉丁语"frangere"，意思是"打碎"。分数就是被打碎成更小部分的东西。
