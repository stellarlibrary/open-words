# 😞 dejected

🔊 /dɪˈdʒektɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Sad and depressed; dispirited; feeling or showing discouragement and hopelessness
**中文：** adj. 沮丧的；灰心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- despondent（沮丧的）
- disheartened（灰心的）
- downcast（垂头丧气的）
- crestfallen（失望的）
- dispirited（意志消沉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel dejected / look dejected / dejected expression / dejected mood / utterly dejected**

👉 Example: The students looked dejected after learning that their research proposal had been rejected by the committee.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The athlete appeared visibly **dejected** after suffering an unexpected defeat in the championship semifinal round of the tournament.
   这位运动员在锦标赛半决赛中意外失利后显得明显沮丧。

2. Despite feeling **dejected** by the initial rejection, she revised her manuscript thoroughly and resubmitted it to the journal.
   尽管最初的拒稿让她感到灰心，她还是彻底修改了稿件并重新提交给了期刊。

3. The **dejected** workers filed out of the factory silently after management announced the closure and widespread layoffs.
   在管理层宣布关闭工厂和大规模裁员后，沮丧的工人们默默地走出了工厂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-ject-ed" as "de- (down) + ject (throw)" — someone who has been thrown down emotionally, feeling low and discouraged.
把"dejected"想成"de-（向下）+ ject（扔）"——一个在情感上被打倒的人，感到低落和沮丧。
