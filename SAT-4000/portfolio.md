# 💼 portfolio

🔊 /pɔːrtˈfoʊlioʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A flat case for carrying documents or artwork; a collection of investments; a range of responsibilities held by a government minister
**中文：** n. 公文包；文件夹；证券投资组合；部长职务

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dossier（档案）
- collection（合集）
- holdings（持有资产）
- briefcase（公文包）
- compilation（汇编）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **investment portfolio / portfolio of work / diversified portfolio / portfolio manager**

👉 Example: The financial advisor recommended diversifying the client's portfolio to reduce exposure to market volatility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist presented an impressive **portfolio** of watercolor paintings during her interview at the prestigious gallery.
   这位艺术家在著名画廊的面试中展示了一组令人印象深刻的水彩画作品集。

2. A well-diversified investment **portfolio** can help mitigate risks associated with fluctuations in any single market sector.
   多元化的投资组合可以帮助降低与任何单一市场板块波动相关的风险。

3. The newly appointed minister assumed the defense **portfolio** at a time of heightened regional security tensions.
   新任命的部长在地区安全局势紧张加剧之际接管了国防部长职务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "port-folio" as "portable folio" — a portable folder you carry, holding your important documents, artwork, or financial records.
把"portfolio"想成"portable(便携的) folio(对开纸)"——一个你随身携带的文件夹，装着你的重要文件、艺术品或财务记录。
