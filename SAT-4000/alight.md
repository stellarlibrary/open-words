# 🔥 alight

🔊 /əˈlaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词，形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To descend from a vehicle or come down from above; to settle or land. As an adjective, burning or illuminated.
**中文：** 下来；飞落，降落。烧着的；点亮着的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- descend（下降）
- dismount（下车，下马）
- land（着陆）
- ablaze（燃烧的）
- illuminated（照亮的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alight from, alight on/upon, set alight, eyes alight with**
👉 Example: The butterfly alighted on the branch with remarkable delicacy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The passengers **alighted** from the train and proceeded swiftly toward the station's main concourse.
   乘客们从火车上下来，迅速走向车站的主大厅。

2. A magnificent heron **alighted** upon the surface of the tranquil lake at the break of dawn.
   一只壮丽的苍鹭在黎明时分飞落在宁静的湖面上。

3. The entire building was **alight** within minutes, and firefighters struggled to contain the spreading blaze.
   整栋建筑在几分钟内就燃烧起来，消防员们奋力控制蔓延的火势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a + light" — a bird comes down "to light" on a branch, or something is "a-light" (on fire, glowing).
联想"a + light（光）"——鸟儿飞下来"落在"枝头如同"着陆点亮"，或某物"着了火，发着光"。
