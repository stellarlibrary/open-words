# 🎉 ecstatic

🔊 /ɪkˈstætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling or expressing overwhelming happiness or joyful excitement; in a state of rapture
**中文：** adj. 欣喜若狂的；入迷的；狂喜的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elated（兴高采烈的）
- euphoric（欣快的）
- rapturous（狂喜的）
- overjoyed（欣喜若狂的）
- exhilarated（兴奋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ecstatic about / ecstatic at the news / ecstatic reception / ecstatic with joy**

👉 Example: The team was ecstatic about their unexpected victory in the national championship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers were **ecstatic** when their decade-long experiment finally yielded the breakthrough results they had anticipated.
   当长达十年的实验终于产生了他们所期待的突破性成果时，研究人员欣喜若狂。

2. The audience gave an **ecstatic** reception to the symphony orchestra's flawless performance of the notoriously difficult composition.
   观众对交响乐团完美演绎这首出了名难的作品给予了狂热的反响。

3. She was absolutely **ecstatic** upon learning that her novel had been selected for the prestigious international literary award.
   当得知她的小说入选了这一著名的国际文学奖时，她欣喜若狂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ec-static" as "ex-static" — you are so happy that you cannot remain static; you jump out of your normal state with joy.
把"ecstatic"想成"ex-static"——你高兴得无法保持静止（static），喜悦让你跳出了正常状态。
