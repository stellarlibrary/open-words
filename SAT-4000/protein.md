# 🧬 protein

🔊 /ˈproʊtiːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large biomolecule consisting of amino acids, essential for the structure, function, and regulation of body tissues and organs. (adj.) Of or relating to protein
**中文：** n. 蛋白质 adj. 蛋白质的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- polypeptide（多肽）
- amino acid chain（氨基酸链）
- macromolecule（大分子）
- enzyme（酶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **protein synthesis / protein deficiency / high-protein diet / protein structure / rich in protein**

👉 Example: Nutritionists recommend consuming adequate protein daily to support muscle growth and tissue repair.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team identified a novel **protein** that plays a critical role in regulating cellular immune responses against viral infections.
   研究团队发现了一种新型蛋白质，在调节细胞对病毒感染的免疫反应中起着关键作用。

2. Athletes typically require higher daily **protein** intake to facilitate muscle recovery and sustain peak physical performance during competition.
   运动员通常需要更高的每日蛋白质摄入量，以促进肌肉恢复并在比赛中保持最佳体能表现。

3. Scientists used advanced imaging techniques to map the three-dimensional structure of the **protein** associated with neurodegenerative diseases.
   科学家使用先进的成像技术绘制了与神经退行性疾病相关的蛋白质的三维结构。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "protein" from Greek "proteios" meaning "primary" — protein is the PRIMARY building block your body needs for growth and repair.
把"protein"与希腊语"proteios"（意为"首要的"）联系起来——蛋白质是身体生长和修复所需的首要构建材料。
