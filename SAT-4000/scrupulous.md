# 🔍 scrupulous

🔊 /ˈskruːpjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Careful; cautious; thorough and attentive to detail; having moral integrity
**中文：** adj. 小心谨慎的，细心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- meticulous（一丝不苟的）
- conscientious（认真的）
- painstaking（煞费苦心的）
- diligent（勤勉的）
- thorough（彻底的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scrupulous attention / scrupulous honesty / scrupulous about details / scrupulously fair**

👉 Example: The auditor maintained scrupulous records of every financial transaction throughout the fiscal year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **scrupulous** researcher verified every data point independently before submitting her findings to the peer-reviewed scientific journal.
   这位细心的研究人员在向同行评审科学期刊提交研究结果之前，独立验证了每一个数据点。

2. His **scrupulous** adherence to ethical standards earned him the unwavering trust of both colleagues and clients throughout his career.
   他对道德标准一丝不苟的坚守为他赢得了同事和客户在整个职业生涯中始终如一的信任。

3. The restoration team took **scrupulous** care to preserve the original materials while repairing the damage to the historic cathedral.
   修复团队在修复这座历史大教堂的损坏时，细心地保护原始材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scrupulous" as related to "scruple" — someone full of scruples is extremely careful and attentive, never cutting corners.
把"scrupulous"联想到"scruple"（顾虑）——一个充满顾虑的人会极其小心谨慎，从不偷工减料。
