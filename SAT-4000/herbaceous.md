# 🌿 herbaceous

🔊 /hɜːrˈbeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or resembling herbs; denoting plants that have non-woody stems and die down to the ground after flowering
**中文：** adj. 草本的；绿色的；似草的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- herbal（草本的）
- leafy（多叶的）
- verdant（翠绿的）
- non-woody（非木质的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **herbaceous plant / herbaceous border / herbaceous perennial / herbaceous vegetation**

👉 Example: The garden featured a stunning herbaceous border filled with colorful perennials and seasonal blooms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanist classified the newly discovered species as an **herbaceous** perennial that thrives in temperate forest understories.
   植物学家将这一新发现的物种归类为在温带森林下层茁壮生长的草本多年生植物。

2. Unlike woody shrubs, **herbaceous** plants lack persistent above-ground stems and typically regenerate from their root systems each spring.
   与木本灌木不同，草本植物缺乏持久的地上茎，通常每年春天从根系重新生长。

3. The landscape architect recommended planting **herbaceous** borders along the pathways to create a naturalistic and visually appealing design.
   景观设计师建议沿小径种植草本花境，以营造自然且具有视觉吸引力的设计。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "herb" + "aceous" — if it relates to herbs (soft green plants), it is herbaceous, meaning green and non-woody.
把"herbaceous"想成"herb（草本植物）+ aceous（具有…性质的）"——如果与草本植物有关，就是herbaceous，即绿色的、非木质的。
