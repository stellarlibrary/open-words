# 😤 dour

🔊 /dʊr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Stern, gloomy, or stubbornly unyielding in manner or appearance
**中文：** adj. 严厉的；阴沉的；固执的；顽强的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stern（严厉的）
- grim（阴沉的）
- sullen（闷闷不乐的）
- austere（严肃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dour expression / dour demeanor / dour outlook / dour personality**

👉 Example: The professor's dour expression discouraged students from asking questions during the lecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The negotiator maintained a **dour** expression throughout the proceedings, revealing nothing of his strategic intentions.
   谈判代表在整个过程中保持着阴沉的表情，丝毫没有透露他的战略意图。

2. Despite his **dour** reputation, the critic occasionally offered generous praise for truly exceptional works of art.
   尽管以严厉著称，这位评论家偶尔也会对真正杰出的艺术作品给予慷慨的赞美。

3. The **dour** economic forecast prompted the government to implement a series of aggressive fiscal reform measures.
   阴沉的经济预测促使政府实施了一系列激进的财政改革措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dour" as rhyming with "sour" — a sour-faced person who always looks gloomy and stern.
把"dour"想成和"sour（酸的）"押韵——一个总是愁眉苦脸、表情阴沉严厉的人。
