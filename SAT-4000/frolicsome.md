# 🎉 frolicsome

🔊 /ˈfrɒlɪksəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lively and playful; full of fun and high spirits; inclined to frolic
**中文：** 嬉戏的；爱闹着玩的；快活的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- playful（爱玩耍的）
- sportive（嬉戏的）
- lively（活泼的）
- merry（快乐的）
- jovial（愉快的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frolicsome children / frolicsome puppy / frolicsome mood / frolicsome behavior**

👉 Example: The frolicsome kittens chased each other around the garden throughout the warm afternoon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **frolicsome** lambs leaping across the meadow provided a charming spectacle for visitors to the countryside farm.
   在草地上跳跃嬉戏的小羊为乡村农场的游客们提供了一道迷人的风景。

2. Despite the gravity of the occasion, the **frolicsome** children could not contain their energy during the lengthy ceremony.
   尽管场合庄重，嬉戏的孩子们在漫长的仪式中无法抑制他们的精力。

3. His **frolicsome** nature made him popular among colleagues, though it sometimes undermined his professional credibility in meetings.
   他爱闹着玩的性格使他在同事中很受欢迎，但有时会损害他在会议中的专业信誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frolic + some" — someone who has "some frolic" in them is always ready to play and have fun.
把"frolicsome"想成"frolic（嬉戏）+ some（一些）"——一个身上有"一些嬉戏"的人总是准备好玩耍和享乐。
