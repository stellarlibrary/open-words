# 👑 premier

🔊 /prɪˈmɪr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) First in importance, rank, or position; leading. (n.) A prime minister or head of government
**中文：** adj. 第一的；最初的；首要的 n. 总理；首相

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- foremost（最重要的）
- chief（首要的）
- leading（领先的）
- principal（主要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **premier institution / premier league / premier destination / the Premier of a country**

👉 Example: The university is widely regarded as the premier institution for biomedical research in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Oxford remains one of the world's **premier** academic institutions, attracting scholars from over one hundred and fifty countries.
   牛津大学仍然是世界顶级学术机构之一，吸引着来自一百五十多个国家的学者。

2. The **premier** addressed the nation in a televised speech, outlining comprehensive economic reforms for the coming fiscal year.
   总理在电视讲话中向全国发表讲话，概述了来年全面的经济改革计划。

3. This coastal city has established itself as the **premier** destination for international conferences on sustainable development.
   这座沿海城市已确立了自身作为可持续发展国际会议首选目的地的地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "premier" as sharing roots with "primary" and "prime" — all from Latin "primus" meaning "first."
把"premier"和"primary""prime"联系起来——它们都源自拉丁语"primus"，意为"第一"。
