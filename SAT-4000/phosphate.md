# 🧪 phosphate

🔊 /ˈfɒsfeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A salt or ester of phosphoric acid, widely used in fertilizers, detergents, and biological processes; a chemical compound containing the phosphate group
**中文：** n. 磷酸盐；皮膜化成

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- phosphoric salt（磷酸盐）
- orthophosphate（正磷酸盐）
- superphosphate（过磷酸盐）
- phosphate compound（磷酸化合物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **phosphate fertilizer / phosphate rock / calcium phosphate / phosphate levels**

👉 Example: Excessive phosphate runoff from agricultural fields can lead to harmful algal blooms in nearby waterways.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural industry relies heavily on **phosphate** fertilizers to replenish soil nutrients and sustain high crop yields annually.
   农业产业严重依赖磷酸盐肥料来补充土壤养分并维持每年的高产量。

2. Elevated **phosphate** concentrations in freshwater ecosystems can trigger eutrophication, leading to oxygen depletion and aquatic species decline.
   淡水生态系统中磷酸盐浓度的升高会引发富营养化，导致氧气耗竭和水生物种的减少。

3. Biochemists study how **phosphate** groups attach to proteins during phosphorylation, a process essential for cellular signaling pathways.
   生物化学家研究磷酸基团在磷酸化过程中如何附着到蛋白质上，这一过程对细胞信号通路至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "phosph-ate" as "phosphorus that you ate" — phosphate is a compound of phosphorus found in many foods and fertilizers.
把"phosphate"想成"phosphorus that you ate（你吃下的磷）"——磷酸盐是一种在许多食物和肥料中发现的磷化合物。
