# 😈 malevolent

🔊 /məˈlevələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing a wish to do evil to others; deeply malicious and spiteful
**中文：** adj. 恶毒的；有恶意的；坏心肠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- malicious（恶意的）
- spiteful（怀恨的）
- hostile（敌意的）
- venomous（恶毒的）
- pernicious（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **malevolent intent / malevolent gaze / malevolent force / malevolent spirit**

👉 Example: The villain's malevolent gaze made it clear that he had no intention of showing mercy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dictator's **malevolent** policies were deliberately designed to suppress dissent and terrorize the civilian population.
   这位独裁者的恶毒政策是蓄意制定的，旨在压制异议并恐吓平民。

2. Critics accused the corporation of **malevolent** negligence, arguing that executives knowingly exposed workers to hazardous conditions.
   批评者指控该公司恶意疏忽，认为高管们明知故犯地让工人暴露在危险环境中。

3. The researcher warned that artificial intelligence, if developed without ethical safeguards, could become a **malevolent** force in society.
   研究人员警告说，如果在没有伦理保障的情况下开发人工智能，它可能成为社会中一股恶意的力量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mal-evolent" as "mal" (bad) + "volent" (wishing, from Latin velle) — someone who wishes bad things upon others.
把"malevolent"想成"mal"（坏的）+"volent"（愿望，源自拉丁语velle）——一个希望别人遭殃的人。
