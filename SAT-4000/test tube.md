# 🧪 test tube

🔊 /ˈtest tjuːb/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A thin glass tube closed at one end, used to hold small amounts of material for laboratory testing or experiments
**中文：** 试管

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- culture tube（培养管）
- specimen tube（标本管）
- laboratory tube（实验管）
- glass vial（玻璃小瓶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **test tube experiment / test tube baby / heat a test tube / fill a test tube**

👉 Example: The chemist carefully poured the reagent into a test tube and observed the resulting color change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician labeled each **test tube** with the patient's identification number before sending the samples for analysis.
   实验室技术员在将样本送去分析之前，在每个试管上标注了患者的识别号码。

2. Students must wear protective goggles when heating a **test tube** over an open flame to prevent potential injury from splashing.
   学生在明火上加热试管时必须佩戴防护眼镜，以防止飞溅造成潜在伤害。

3. The breakthrough discovery occurred when researchers noticed an unexpected crystalline formation inside the **test tube** during routine experimentation.
   这一突破性发现发生在研究人员在常规实验中注意到试管内出现意外的结晶形成时。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "test tube" literally — it is a tube used for tests in the laboratory, simple and direct.
直接理解"test tube"——它就是实验室中用于测试的管子，简单明了。
