# 👥 minority

🔊 /maɪˈnɔːrəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The smaller number or part; a group differing from the majority in race, religion, or ethnicity; the state of being under legal age. (adj.) Relating to or belonging to a minority.
**中文：** n. 少数；少数派；少数民族；未成年 adj. 少数的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- few（少数）
- smaller group（较小群体）
- underrepresented group（弱势群体）
- fraction（一小部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **minority group / ethnic minority / minority opinion / in the minority / minority rights**

👉 Example: The committee found itself in the minority when voting against the proposed amendments to the charter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legislation was designed to protect the civil rights of **minority** communities that had historically faced systemic discrimination.
   该立法旨在保护历史上遭受系统性歧视的少数群体的公民权利。

2. Only a small **minority** of the survey respondents expressed opposition to the proposed environmental regulations and conservation measures.
   只有少数受访者对拟议的环境法规和保护措施表示反对。

3. The university established a scholarship fund specifically to support students from underrepresented **minority** backgrounds in pursuing advanced degrees.
   该大学专门设立了奖学金基金，以支持来自少数族裔背景的学生攻读高级学位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "minor-ity" as having the word "minor" inside — a minor part of the whole, the smaller portion of a larger group.
把"minority"想成里面有"minor（较小的）"——整体中的较小部分，较大群体中的少数。
