# 🧱 aggregate

🔊 /ˈæɡrɪɡət/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective | 动词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To collect or gather together into a whole. (n.) A total or combined amount; a collection of items. (adj.) Formed by combining several elements
**中文：** v. 集合；聚集 n. 合计；集合体 adj. 聚合的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accumulate（积累）
- compile（汇编）
- amass（聚集）
- composite（合成物）
- total（总计）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aggregate data / aggregate demand / in the aggregate / aggregate score / aggregate amount**

👉 Example: The research team aggregated survey responses from twelve countries to identify global trends.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The analyst was tasked with **aggregating** financial data from multiple departments to produce a comprehensive quarterly performance report.
   分析师的任务是汇总多个部门的财务数据，以编制一份全面的季度业绩报告。

2. In the **aggregate**, the combined economic output of these small nations rivals that of several major industrialized countries.
   从总量来看，这些小国的经济产出总和可与几个主要工业化国家相匹敌。

3. The platform uses sophisticated algorithms to **aggregate** news articles from thousands of verified sources around the entire world.
   该平台使用精密算法汇集来自全球数千个经过验证的来源的新闻文章。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ag-GREG-ate" — "greg" comes from Latin "grex" meaning flock, so aggregate means to flock or gather together.
想想"ag-GREG-ate"——"greg"来自拉丁语"grex"意为羊群，所以aggregate意为聚集在一起。
