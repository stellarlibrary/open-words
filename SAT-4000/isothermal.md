# 🌡️ isothermal

🔊 /ˌaɪsəˈθɜːrml/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or denoting a process that occurs at a constant temperature. (n.) A line on a map connecting points of equal temperature
**中文：** adj. 等温的；等温线的 n. 等温线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- constant-temperature（恒温的）
- thermally uniform（热均匀的）
- equal-temperature（等温的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **isothermal process / isothermal expansion / isothermal line / isothermal compression**

👉 Example: The gas underwent isothermal expansion, maintaining a constant temperature throughout the entire process.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In an **isothermal** process, the temperature of the system remains constant while heat is exchanged with the surrounding environment.
   在等温过程中，系统的温度保持恒定，同时与周围环境进行热量交换。

2. Meteorologists draw **isothermal** lines on weather maps to illustrate regions that share the same average annual temperature.
   气象学家在天气图上绘制等温线，以展示具有相同年平均温度的区域。

3. The engineer ensured that the chemical reaction occurred under **isothermal** conditions to maintain the accuracy of the experimental results.
   工程师确保化学反应在等温条件下进行，以保持实验结果的准确性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "iso-thermal" as "iso (equal) + thermal (heat)" — equal heat, meaning the temperature stays the same throughout.
把"isothermal"拆分为"iso-（相等的）+ thermal（热的）"——热量相等的，即温度始终保持不变。
