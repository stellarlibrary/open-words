# 🐛 bug

🔊 /bʌɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small insect; a defect or flaw; a hidden listening device. (v.) To annoy or bother; to install a hidden listening device
**中文：** n. 臭虫，小虫；故障；窃听器 v. 烦扰，打扰；装窃听器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insect（昆虫）
- glitch（故障）
- annoy（烦扰）
- pester（纠缠）
- defect（缺陷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bug in the system / bug someone about something / plant a bug / debug a program**

👉 Example: The engineers spent hours trying to find the bug that caused the entire system to crash.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A persistent **bug** in the software caused the application to crash whenever users attempted to save their documents.
   软件中一个持续存在的故障导致用户每次试图保存文档时应用程序都会崩溃。

2. The intelligence agency decided to **bug** the diplomat's office in order to monitor confidential communications.
   情报机构决定在外交官的办公室安装窃听器，以监听机密通讯。

3. It continued to **bug** the researcher that her experimental results could not be replicated under identical laboratory conditions.
   实验结果在相同的实验室条件下无法复制，这一直困扰着那位研究员。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a tiny bug crawling into a machine — it causes problems (glitch), it hides (listening device), and it irritates you (annoy).
想象一只小虫子爬进机器——它会造成问题（故障），它会隐藏（窃听器），它还会让你烦躁（打扰）。
