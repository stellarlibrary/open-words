# 💧 humid

🔊 /ˈhjuːmɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a high level of moisture or water vapor in the air; damp and warm
**中文：** adj. 潮湿的；湿润的；湿气重的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- damp（潮湿的）
- moist（湿润的）
- muggy（闷热潮湿的）
- sticky（黏糊糊的）
- sultry（闷热的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **humid climate / humid air / humid weather / hot and humid**

👉 Example: The tropical region is known for its extremely humid conditions throughout the summer months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers discovered that excessively **humid** environments significantly accelerate the deterioration of ancient manuscripts and artifacts.
   研究人员发现，过于潮湿的环境会显著加速古代手稿和文物的劣化。

2. Athletes competing in **humid** conditions must increase their fluid intake to prevent dangerous levels of dehydration.
   在潮湿条件下比赛的运动员必须增加液体摄入量，以防止危险程度的脱水。

3. The construction team installed advanced ventilation systems to combat the persistently **humid** atmosphere inside the warehouse.
   施工团队安装了先进的通风系统，以应对仓库内持续潮湿的空气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humid" as related to "humidifier" — a device that adds moisture to the air, making everything feel wet and damp.
把"humid"联想到"humidifier"（加湿器）——一种向空气中添加水分的设备，让一切感觉潮湿。
