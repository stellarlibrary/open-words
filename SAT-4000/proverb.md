# 📚 proverb

🔊 /ˈprɑːvɜːrb/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A short, well-known saying that expresses a general truth or piece of advice; a person or thing widely known for a particular quality
**中文：** n. 谚语；格言；众所周知的人或事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adage（格言）
- maxim（箴言）
- saying（俗语）
- aphorism（警句）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ancient proverb / as the proverb goes / proverbial wisdom / a proverb about**

👉 Example: As the old proverb goes, "A stitch in time saves nine."

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor illustrated her lecture on cultural values by analyzing **proverbs** from diverse societies spanning several continents and centuries.
   教授通过分析来自几大洲和几个世纪以来不同社会的谚语来阐释她关于文化价值观的讲座。

2. An ancient Chinese **proverb** reminds us that a journey of a thousand miles must begin with a single determined step.
   一句古老的中国谚语提醒我们，千里之行始于足下。

3. His remarkable generosity has made him a **proverb** in the community, frequently cited as a model of selfless philanthropy.
   他非凡的慷慨使他成为社区中众所周知的人物，经常被引为无私慈善的典范。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-verb" as a "professional word" — a proverb is a polished, time-tested saying that speaks like a professional advisor.
把"proverb"想成"pro（专业的）+ verb（话语）"——谚语是一句经过时间检验的精炼话语，如同专业顾问的建议。
