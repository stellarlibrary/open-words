# ➖ parallel

🔊 /ˈpærəˌlɛl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A line equidistant from another; a comparison. (v.) To be similar or correspond to. (adj.) Extending in the same direction and equidistant; similar or corresponding
**中文：** n. 平行线；平行面；类似；相似物 v. 使…与…平行 adj. 平行的；类似的；相同的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- analogous（类似的）
- corresponding（相应的）
- equivalent（等同的）
- comparable（可比较的）
- similar（相似的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **parallel to / run parallel / draw a parallel / parallel structure / in parallel**

👉 Example: The professor drew a parallel between the economic crisis of the 1930s and the current financial downturn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher drew a compelling **parallel** between the political upheavals of the twentieth century and contemporary global movements.
   研究人员在二十世纪的政治动荡与当代全球运动之间进行了令人信服的类比。

2. The two highways run **parallel** to each other for approximately one hundred miles before diverging near the mountain range.
   这两条高速公路平行延伸约一百英里，然后在山脉附近分开。

3. The development of artificial intelligence **parallels** the early evolution of the internet in both scope and transformative potential.
   人工智能的发展在范围和变革潜力方面都与互联网的早期演变相似。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the two "l"s in "parallel" as two parallel lines running side by side — the word itself shows you its meaning.
想想"parallel"中的两个"l"就像两条平行线并排延伸——这个词本身就展示了它的含义。
