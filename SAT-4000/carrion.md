# 🦅 carrion

🔊 /ˈkæriən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The decaying flesh of dead animals. (adj.) Relating to or feeding on dead and putrefying flesh
**中文：** n. 腐肉；死肉；臭尸 adj. 腐肉的；腐朽的；吃腐肉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carcass（动物尸体）
- remains（残骸）
- offal（内脏废肉）
- putrefying flesh（腐烂的肉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **carrion crow / carrion eater / feed on carrion / the stench of carrion / carrion beetle**

👉 Example: The vultures circled overhead, drawn by the scent of carrion on the open plain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vultures descended upon the **carrion** left by predators, fulfilling their essential ecological role as nature's efficient scavengers.
   秃鹫俯冲向掠食者留下的腐肉，履行着它们作为自然界高效清道夫的重要生态角色。

2. The forensic team discovered fragments of **carrion** near the excavation site that proved invaluable to reconstructing the ancient environment.
   法医团队在发掘现场附近发现了腐肉碎片，这对重建古代环境具有极高价值。

3. Certain beetle species are specifically adapted to locate and consume **carrion**, thereby accelerating the natural process of decomposition.
   某些甲虫物种专门适应于寻找和食用腐肉，从而加速自然分解过程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carrion" as "carry-on" — dead flesh that animals carry on eating. Also note the root "carn-" (flesh), linking it to "carnage" and "carnal."
把"carrion"想成"carry-on"（继续吃）——动物继续吃的死肉。还要注意词根"carn-"（肉），将其与"carnage"（屠杀）和"carnal"（肉体的）联系起来。
