# 💬 communicative

🔊 /kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Willing and eager to talk and share information; relating to the conveying of information; talkative and open in expression
**中文：** adj. 交际的；爱说话的，健谈的；善于沟通的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- talkative（健谈的）
- forthcoming（乐于提供信息的）
- expressive（善于表达的）
- garrulous（饶舌的）
- sociable（善交际的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **communicative skills / communicative approach / highly communicative / communicative competence**

👉 Example: Effective leaders are typically communicative individuals who articulate their vision with clarity and conviction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new department manager proved to be remarkably **communicative**, regularly updating the entire team on project developments and strategic decisions.
   新部门经理非常善于沟通，定期向整个团队通报项目进展和战略决策。

2. Children raised in linguistically rich environments tend to become more **communicative** and confident in expressing their thoughts and ideas.
   在语言丰富的环境中成长的孩子往往更善于交流，更自信地表达自己的想法和观点。

3. The therapist noted that the patient had become increasingly **communicative** over the course of several months of consistent treatment.
   治疗师注意到，经过几个月的持续治疗，患者变得越来越愿意交流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "communicative" as "communicate + ive" — someone who is naturally inclined to communicate, always ready to share and talk.
把"communicative"想成"communicate + ive"——天生倾向于交流的人，总是乐于分享和交谈。
