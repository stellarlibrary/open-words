# 🍺 guzzle

🔊 /ˈɡʌzl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To eat or drink something greedily or in large quantities; to consume rapidly and excessively
**中文：** v. 狂饮；暴食

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gulp（大口吞咽）
- devour（狼吞虎咽）
- gorge（暴食）
- swill（大口喝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **guzzle water / guzzle beer / guzzle fuel / guzzle down / gas-guzzler**

👉 Example: The athletes guzzled water during the halftime break after an exhausting first half in the scorching heat.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The SUV was criticized by environmental groups for its tendency to **guzzle** fuel at an alarming rate per mile.
   这款SUV因每英里惊人的油耗而遭到环保组织的批评。

2. After the grueling marathon, the exhausted runners **guzzled** electrolyte drinks to replenish their severely depleted energy reserves.
   在艰苦的马拉松赛后，精疲力竭的跑者狂饮电解质饮料以补充严重消耗的能量储备。

3. Financial analysts warned that the corporation continued to **guzzle** capital with little prospect of generating sustainable profits.
   金融分析师警告说，该公司继续大量消耗资本，却几乎没有产生可持续利润的前景。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "guzzle" as the sound of gulping — "guzz-guzz-guzz" — like someone noisily drinking something down without stopping.
把"guzzle"想成大口吞咽的声音——"guzz-guzz-guzz"——就像某人不停地大声喝东西。
