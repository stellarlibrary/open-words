# 🔀 analogous

🔊 /əˈnæləɡəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Comparable in certain respects; similar or equivalent in function, structure, or position, though not identical in origin
**中文：** 类似的；可比拟的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comparable（可比较的）
- similar（相似的）
- equivalent（等同的）
- parallel（平行的）
- corresponding（对应的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **analogous to / analogous structures / analogous situation / roughly analogous / closely analogous**

👉 Example: The wings of birds and the fins of fish are analogous structures that serve similar locomotive functions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The relationship between a CEO and a corporation is **analogous** to that between a captain and a ship in many respects.
   CEO与公司之间的关系在许多方面类似于船长与船只之间的关系。

2. Researchers found that the immune response observed in the experiment was **analogous** to reactions previously documented in human clinical trials.
   研究人员发现，实验中观察到的免疫反应与先前在人体临床试验中记录的反应类似。

3. The professor argued that the current economic crisis is **analogous** to the Great Depression in its underlying structural causes.
   教授认为，当前的经济危机在其深层结构性原因上与大萧条类似。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "analog" in "analogous" — just as an analog clock resembles a digital one in function but differs in form, analogous things are similar but not identical.
想到"analogous"中的"analog（模拟）"——就像模拟时钟和数字时钟功能相似但形式不同，analogous的事物相似但不完全相同。
