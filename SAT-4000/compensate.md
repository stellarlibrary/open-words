# 💰 compensate

🔊 /ˈkɒmpenseɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To give something, typically money, in recognition of loss, suffering, or injury; to make up for something unwelcome by exerting an opposite force or effect
**中文：** v. 补偿；赔偿；付报酬；抵消

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reimburse（偿还）
- indemnify（赔偿）
- offset（抵消）
- remunerate（酬劳）
- counterbalance（平衡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **compensate for / compensate someone for something / fully compensate / compensate losses**

👉 Example: The company agreed to compensate the affected residents for the environmental damage caused by the spill.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The insurance company was legally obligated to **compensate** policyholders for all documented losses resulting from the natural disaster.
   保险公司在法律上有义务赔偿保单持有人因自然灾害造成的所有记录在案的损失。

2. Many educators argue that no amount of technology can fully **compensate** for the absence of skilled and dedicated classroom teachers.
   许多教育工作者认为，再多的技术也无法完全弥补缺乏熟练且敬业的课堂教师。

3. The athlete trained rigorously to **compensate** for a chronic knee injury that had significantly limited her competitive performance.
   这位运动员刻苦训练以弥补严重限制她比赛表现的慢性膝伤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compensate" as "com- (together) + pensate (from pendere, to weigh)" — to weigh things together and balance the scales by paying back.
把"compensate"想成"com-（一起）+ pensate（来自pendere，称量）"——把事物放在一起称量，通过偿还来平衡天平。
