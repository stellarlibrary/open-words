# 🚀 weightlessness

🔊 /ˈweɪtləsnəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state or condition of having little or no weight, especially as experienced in outer space or free fall
**中文：** n. 失重；无重状态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- zero gravity（零重力）
- microgravity（微重力）
- buoyancy（浮力感）
- levitation（悬浮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **experience weightlessness / state of weightlessness / simulate weightlessness / feeling of weightlessness**

👉 Example: Astronauts must train extensively to adapt to the effects of weightlessness during orbital missions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The astronauts reported that prolonged **weightlessness** significantly affected their muscle mass and bone density during the mission.
   宇航员报告说，长时间的失重状态在执行任务期间显著影响了他们的肌肉质量和骨密度。

2. Researchers designed a specialized aircraft to simulate **weightlessness** so that scientists could conduct experiments in microgravity conditions.
   研究人员设计了一种专用飞机来模拟失重状态，以便科学家能够在微重力条件下进行实验。

3. The sensation of **weightlessness** during free fall provides valuable data for understanding human physiological responses to reduced gravity.
   自由落体时的失重感为理解人体对低重力的生理反应提供了宝贵的数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "weightlessness" as "weight-less-ness" — the state of being without weight, like floating freely in space.
把"weightlessness"想成"weight（重量）-less（没有）-ness（状态）"——没有重量的状态，就像在太空中自由漂浮一样。
