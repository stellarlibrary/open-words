# 💪 determination

🔊 /dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Firmness of purpose; resolute willpower; the process of establishing something exactly by calculation or research
**中文：** n. 决心；果断；测定

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resolve（决心）
- tenacity（坚韧）
- perseverance（毅力）
- firmness（坚定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show determination / with determination / fierce determination / determination to succeed**

👉 Example: Her fierce determination to succeed carried her through years of rigorous academic training.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The athlete's remarkable **determination** enabled her to recover from a career-threatening injury and compete at the international level.
   这位运动员非凡的决心使她从危及职业生涯的伤病中恢复过来，并在国际赛场上竞争。

2. The laboratory's precise **determination** of the compound's molecular weight confirmed the theoretical predictions published earlier that year.
   实验室对该化合物分子量的精确测定证实了当年早些时候发表的理论预测。

3. Despite encountering numerous obstacles, the team's collective **determination** ensured that the ambitious project was completed before the deadline.
   尽管遇到了许多障碍，团队的集体决心确保了这个宏大的项目在截止日期前完成。
