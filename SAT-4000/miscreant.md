# 👿 miscreant

🔊 /ˈmɪskriənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Villainous; wicked; heretical. (n.) A person who behaves badly or commits crimes; a villain or evildoer
**中文：** adj. 堕落的，卑劣的；异端的 n. 恶棍；罪大恶极之人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- villain（恶棍）
- scoundrel（无赖）
- rogue（流氓）
- wretch（卑鄙的人）
- criminal（罪犯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a band of miscreants / miscreant behavior / notorious miscreant / petty miscreant**

👉 Example: The judge showed no leniency toward the miscreant who had terrorized the community for months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The authorities finally apprehended the **miscreant** responsible for a series of brazen thefts that had plagued the district for years.
   当局终于逮捕了多年来在该地区猖獗盗窃的恶棍。

2. Historical records describe the outlaw as a notorious **miscreant** whose criminal exploits terrorized villages along the northern border.
   历史记录将这名不法之徒描述为一个臭名昭著的恶棍，其犯罪行径使北部边境的村庄人心惶惶。

3. The editorial condemned the **miscreant** officials who had systematically embezzled public funds intended for disaster relief and reconstruction.
   社论谴责了那些系统性挪用救灾和重建公共资金的卑劣官员。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-cre-ant" — "mis" means bad, and "creant" sounds like "creature." A miscreant is a bad creature, a villain.
把"miscreant"想成"mis（坏的）+ creant（像creature，生物）"——一个坏家伙，恶棍。
