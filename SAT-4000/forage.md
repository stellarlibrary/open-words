# 🌿 forage

🔊 /ˈfɔːrɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Food for horses or cattle; fodder. (v.) To search widely for food or provisions; to rummage
**中文：** n. 饲料；草料 v. 搜寻粮草；搜索

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- search（搜索）
- scavenge（翻找）
- rummage（翻寻）
- fodder（饲料）
- hunt（寻找）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **forage for food / forage for supplies / forage in the wild / forage crops**

👉 Example: The troops were ordered to forage for supplies in the surrounding countryside.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During the harsh winter, the deer were forced to **forage** for whatever edible vegetation remained beneath the snow.
   在严冬期间，鹿被迫在雪下搜寻仅存的可食用植物。

2. Researchers observed that the colony of ants could **forage** across a vast territory spanning several hundred meters.
   研究人员观察到，这个蚁群可以在跨越数百米的广阔区域内搜索食物。

3. The farmer stored enough **forage** in the barn to sustain the livestock through the entire winter season.
   农民在谷仓里储存了足够的草料，以维持牲畜度过整个冬季。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "forage" as "for + age" — for ages, humans have foraged in the wild to find food for survival.
把"forage"想成"for + age"——长久以来，人类一直在野外搜寻食物以求生存。
