# 😱 unspeakable

🔊 /ʌnˈspiːkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Too bad, shocking, or horrible to express in words; not able to be spoken or described; indescribably awful
**中文：** 无法形容的；坏透了的；说不出口的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indescribable（难以描述的）
- unutterable（说不出的）
- appalling（骇人的）
- dreadful（可怕的）
- abominable（令人憎恶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unspeakable horror / unspeakable suffering / unspeakable cruelty / unspeakable joy**

👉 Example: The survivors endured unspeakable hardships during their prolonged captivity in the remote region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tribunal's report documented the **unspeakable** atrocities committed against civilians during the prolonged armed conflict.
   法庭的报告记录了在长期武装冲突中对平民犯下的难以言喻的暴行。

2. Witnessing the aftermath of the natural disaster left the relief workers struggling with **unspeakable** grief and emotional exhaustion.
   目睹自然灾害的后果使救援人员陷入了难以形容的悲伤和情感上的疲惫。

3. The memoir recounts **unspeakable** acts of courage performed by ordinary individuals under extraordinary wartime circumstances.
   这部回忆录讲述了普通人在非凡的战时环境下做出的难以言表的勇敢行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-speak-able" as "not able to speak about" — something so terrible or extreme that words fail to capture it.
把"unspeakable"想成"un（不能）+ speak（说）+ able（能够）"——某事如此可怕或极端，以至于无法用言语来表达。
