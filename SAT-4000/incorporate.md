# 🔗 incorporate

🔊 /ɪnˈkɔːpəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To include or absorb something as part of a whole; to combine or unite into one body (adj.) Combined or united into one
**中文：** v. 合并；纳入；结合；包含 adj. 合并的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- integrate（整合）
- assimilate（吸收）
- merge（合并）
- embed（嵌入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incorporate into / incorporate elements of / incorporate feedback / incorporate changes**

👉 Example: The architect decided to incorporate sustainable materials into the design of the new building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The revised curriculum will **incorporate** recent advances in artificial intelligence and machine learning into core courses.
   修订后的课程将把人工智能和机器学习的最新进展纳入核心课程。

2. Successful leaders know how to **incorporate** diverse perspectives when formulating strategic decisions for their organizations.
   成功的领导者知道如何在为组织制定战略决策时吸纳多元化的观点。

3. The pharmaceutical company plans to **incorporate** the promising startup to gain access to its innovative drug delivery technology.
   该制药公司计划合并这家前景广阔的初创企业，以获取其创新的药物递送技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-corpor-ate" as "into the body" (corpus = body in Latin) — to incorporate is to bring something into the main body.
把"incorporate"想成"in（进入）+ corpor（身体）+ ate"——把东西纳入主体之中，就是合并、结合。
