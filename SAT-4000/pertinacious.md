# 🪨 pertinacious

🔊 /ˌpɜːrtɪˈneɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Stubbornly persistent or unyielding; holding firmly to a purpose, belief, or course of action
**中文：** adj. 顽固的；执拗的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tenacious（坚韧的）
- obstinate（固执的）
- dogged（顽强的）
- resolute（坚决的）
- unyielding（不屈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pertinacious advocate / pertinacious effort / pertinacious resistance / pertinacious pursuit**

👉 Example: The pertinacious journalist refused to abandon the investigation despite repeated threats.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pertinacious** researcher continued her experiments for over a decade before finally achieving the breakthrough discovery.
   这位执着的研究员坚持实验了十多年，最终取得了突破性发现。

2. His **pertinacious** refusal to compromise on fundamental principles earned him both admiration and criticism from political colleagues.
   他在基本原则上顽固的拒绝妥协为他赢得了政治同僚的钦佩和批评。

3. The **pertinacious** defense of civil liberties by advocacy organizations has shaped landmark judicial decisions throughout modern history.
   维权组织对公民自由的执着捍卫塑造了整个现代历史上的里程碑式司法判决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pertinacious" as "per (thoroughly) + tenacious" — someone who is thoroughly tenacious and never gives up.
把"pertinacious"想成"per（彻底地）+ tenacious（坚韧的）"——一个彻底坚韧、永不放弃的人。
