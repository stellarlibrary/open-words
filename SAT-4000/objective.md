# 🎯 objective

🔊 /əbˈdʒɛktɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not influenced by personal feelings or opinions; based on facts. (n.) A goal or aim that one works toward; a lens in an optical instrument.
**中文：** adj. 客观的，真实的 n. 目标，目的；物镜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impartial（公正的）
- unbiased（无偏见的）
- goal（目标）
- aim（目的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **objective analysis / objective evidence / primary objective / achieve an objective / objective perspective**

👉 Example: A good journalist strives to present an objective account of events without personal bias.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee demanded an **objective** review of the data before making any decisions regarding the proposed policy.
   委员会要求在就拟议政策做出任何决定之前，对数据进行客观审查。

2. The military commander outlined the strategic **objective** of securing the supply routes before the onset of winter.
   军事指挥官概述了在冬季来临前确保补给路线安全的战略目标。

3. Scientists must maintain an **objective** stance when interpreting experimental results to preserve the integrity of research.
   科学家在解释实验结果时必须保持客观立场，以维护研究的完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "objective" as "object + ive" — like viewing something as an object, from the outside, without personal feelings attached.
把"objective"想成"object + ive"——像看待一个物体一样从外部观察，不掺杂个人感情。
