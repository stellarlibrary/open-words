# ⚖️ disparity

🔊 /dɪˈspærəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A great difference or inequality between things
**中文：** n. 不同；不等；差距

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inequality（不平等）
- discrepancy（差异）
- imbalance（不平衡）
- divergence（分歧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disparity in wealth / disparity between groups / income disparity / racial disparity**

👉 Example: The growing disparity between rich and poor has become a central issue in political debates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study revealed a significant **disparity** in educational funding between urban school districts and rural communities.
   该研究揭示了城市学区与农村社区之间在教育经费上存在显著差距。

2. Addressing the **disparity** in healthcare access remains one of the most pressing challenges facing policymakers today.
   解决医疗服务获取方面的不平等仍然是当今决策者面临的最紧迫挑战之一。

3. The **disparity** between the projected revenue and actual earnings forced the company to revise its fiscal strategy.
   预期收入与实际收益之间的差距迫使公司修改其财务策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-parity" as "dis" (not) + "parity" (equality) — disparity literally means a lack of equality or balance.
把"disparity"想成"dis"（不）+"parity"（平等）——disparity字面意思就是缺乏平等或平衡。
