# 💔 vitiate

🔊 /ˈvɪʃieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To impair the quality or efficiency of; to make defective or invalid; to corrupt or contaminate
**中文：** v. 损害，弄坏；使无效；污染

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impair（损害）
- corrupt（败坏）
- invalidate（使无效）
- undermine（破坏）
- taint（污染）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vitiate a contract / vitiate the results / vitiate the atmosphere / vitiate an agreement**

👉 Example: The hidden clause was sufficient to vitiate the entire legal agreement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The procedural errors were serious enough to **vitiate** the outcome of the trial and necessitate a complete review.
   程序性错误严重到足以使审判结果无效，并需要进行全面复审。

2. Prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants can **vitiate** the quality of groundwater in surrounding residential communities.
   长期暴露于工业污染物会损害周围居民社区地下水的水质。

3. The researcher's failure to disclose a conflict of interest threatened to **vitiate** the credibility of the published study.
   研究人员未披露利益冲突，这可能会损害已发表研究的可信度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vitiate" as "vice + ate" — vice ate away the goodness, impairing and corrupting everything it touched.
把"vitiate"想成"vice（恶习）+ ate（吃掉）"——恶习侵蚀了一切美好，损害并败坏了它所触及的一切。
