# 🔋 electrode

🔊 /ɪˈlektroʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A conductor through which electricity enters or leaves an object, substance, or region; a terminal in an electrical device
**中文：** n. 电极

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- terminal（接线端）
- anode（阳极）
- cathode（阴极）
- conductor（导体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **positive electrode / negative electrode / electrode potential / attach an electrode**

👉 Example: The technician carefully attached the electrode to the patient's scalp to monitor brain activity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The positive **electrode** in the battery gradually corroded after prolonged exposure to the acidic electrolyte solution within the cell.
   电池中的正电极在长时间接触电池内的酸性电解液后逐渐腐蚀。

2. Scientists developed a new graphene-based **electrode** that dramatically increased the energy storage capacity of lithium-ion batteries.
   科学家开发了一种新型石墨烯基电极，大幅提高了锂离子电池的储能容量。

3. The medical team placed several **electrode** sensors on the patient's chest to continuously monitor cardiac rhythm during surgery.
   医疗团队在患者胸部放置了多个电极传感器，以在手术期间持续监测心律。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "electr-ode" as "electric road" — an electrode is the road through which electric current travels into or out of a device.
把"electrode"想成"electric road（电的道路）"——电极是电流进出设备的通道。
