# ✋ rebuff

🔊 /rɪˈbʌf/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A blunt or abrupt rejection. (v.) To reject someone or something in a sharp or ungracious manner
**中文：** n. 断然拒绝；回绝 v. 回绝；冷落

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reject（拒绝）
- snub（冷落）
- spurn（鄙弃）
- dismiss（驳回）
- repulse（拒绝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rebuff an offer / rebuff an advance / rebuff a proposal / suffer a rebuff**

👉 Example: The diplomat's peace overture was met with a sharp rebuff from the opposing delegation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company's merger proposal was swiftly **rebuffed** by the rival firm's board of directors, who cited unfavorable terms.
   该公司的合并提案被竞争对手的董事会迅速回绝，理由是条款不利。

2. Despite repeated **rebuffs** from publishers, the determined author continued to refine and resubmit her manuscript for consideration.
   尽管屡遭出版商拒绝，这位坚定的作者仍继续修改并重新提交她的书稿。

3. The senator **rebuffed** calls for resignation, insisting that the allegations were entirely unfounded and politically motivated.
   这位参议员断然拒绝了辞职的呼声，坚称指控完全是毫无根据且出于政治动机的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-buff" as getting "buffed away again" — like being pushed back or blocked, a rebuff is a forceful rejection.
把"rebuff"想成"再次被挡回去"——就像被推回或阻挡，rebuff就是一种强硬的拒绝。
