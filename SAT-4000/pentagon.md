# 📐 pentagon

🔊 /ˈpentəɡɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A plane figure with five straight sides and five angles; a five-sided polygon
**中文：** n. 五角形；五边形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- five-sided figure（五边形）
- polygon（多边形）
- quinquangle（五角形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regular pentagon / pentagon shape / sides of a pentagon / pentagon geometry**

👉 Example: The geometry teacher asked students to calculate the interior angles of a regular pentagon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematics instructor demonstrated how to construct a regular **pentagon** using only a compass and a straightedge on the whiteboard.
   数学老师演示了如何仅用圆规和直尺在白板上构造一个正五边形。

2. Ancient architects frequently incorporated the **pentagon** into their designs, believing its proportions possessed sacred geometric significance.
   古代建筑师经常将五角形融入他们的设计中，认为其比例具有神圣的几何意义。

3. Each interior angle of a regular **pentagon** measures exactly one hundred and eight degrees, a fundamental concept in Euclidean geometry.
   正五边形的每个内角恰好为一百零八度，这是欧几里得几何中的一个基本概念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "penta-gon" — "penta" means five in Greek, and "gon" means angle. A pentagon is simply a shape with five angles.
把"pentagon"想成"penta（五）+ gon（角）"——希腊语中"五个角"的意思，就是五边形。
