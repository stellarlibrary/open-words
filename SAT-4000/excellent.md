# 🌟 excellent

🔊 /ˈɛksələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely good; outstanding in quality, degree, or extent
**中文：** 卓越的；极好的；杰出的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outstanding（杰出的）
- superb（极好的）
- exceptional（卓越的）
- magnificent（壮丽的）
- splendid（辉煌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **excellent performance / excellent quality / excellent reputation / excellent results**

👉 Example: The research team produced excellent results that exceeded all initial expectations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor received an **excellent** evaluation from students who praised her clarity and depth of instruction.
   这位教授获得了学生们的优秀评价，他们称赞她讲课清晰且有深度。

2. Her **excellent** command of three languages made her an invaluable asset to the diplomatic negotiation team.
   她精通三种语言的卓越能力使她成为外交谈判团队中不可或缺的人才。

3. The university maintains an **excellent** reputation for producing graduates who excel in scientific research and innovation.
   这所大学因培养出在科学研究和创新方面表现出色的毕业生而享有卓越的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "excel-lent" — someone who excels is lent to the world as a gift, performing at the highest level.
把"excellent"想成"excel-lent"——一个出类拔萃的人如同被赐予世界的礼物，表现达到最高水平。
