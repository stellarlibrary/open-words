# 🔭 reconnoiter

🔊 /ˌrɛkəˈnɔɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make a military observation or survey of an area to gather information; to explore or inspect in order to gain strategic knowledge
**中文：** v. 侦察；勘查

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scout（侦察）
- survey（勘察）
- inspect（检查）
- explore（探索）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reconnoiter the area / reconnoiter the terrain / reconnoiter enemy positions / reconnoiter ahead**

👉 Example: The commander ordered a small unit to reconnoiter the valley before the main forces advanced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Special forces were deployed to **reconnoiter** the mountainous border region and report on enemy troop movements and fortifications.
   特种部队被派去侦察多山的边境地区，并报告敌军部队动向和防御工事。

2. The expedition team decided to **reconnoiter** the uncharted territory thoroughly before establishing a permanent research base camp.
   远征队决定在建立永久研究营地之前彻底勘查这片未知领域。

3. Intelligence officers used satellite imagery to **reconnoiter** the remote installation without risking the lives of field operatives.
   情报官员利用卫星图像侦察这处偏远设施，而无需让外勤特工冒生命危险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reconnoiter" as "recognize the territory" — you go out to recognize and understand the land before making your move.
把"reconnoiter"想成"recognize the territory（认识地形）"——你在行动之前先去了解和认识地形。
