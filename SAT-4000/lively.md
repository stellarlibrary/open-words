# 🎉 lively

🔊 /ˈlaɪvli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Full of life, energy, and enthusiasm; vivid and animated in character or expression
**中文：** adj. 活泼的；生动的；真实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vivacious（活泼的）
- animated（生动的）
- spirited（充满活力的）
- vibrant（充满生机的）
- energetic（精力充沛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lively debate / lively discussion / lively atmosphere / lively imagination**

👉 Example: The professor encouraged a lively debate among students on the ethical implications of the policy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **lively** discussion among the panelists kept the audience engaged throughout the entire three-hour conference session.
   小组成员之间活跃的讨论使观众在整个三小时的会议期间都保持了高度关注。

2. Her **lively** prose style captivated readers and transformed an otherwise mundane historical narrative into compelling literature.
   她生动的散文风格吸引了读者，将原本平淡的历史叙事转变为引人入胜的文学作品。

3. The marketplace was a **lively** center of commerce where merchants and customers bargained enthusiastically over exotic goods.
   这个市场是一个热闹的商业中心，商人和顾客热情地讨价还价交易异国商品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lively" as "live + ly" — being fully alive in manner, bursting with energy and vitality.
把"lively"想成"live（活）+ ly（地）"——以充满生命力的方式，洋溢着活力和生机。
