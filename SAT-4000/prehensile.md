# 🐒 prehensile

🔊 /prɪˈhɛnsaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of grasping or holding, especially by wrapping around an object; quick to understand or perceive
**中文：** adj. 适于抓握的；善于领会的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grasping（抓握的）
- gripping（紧握的）
- clinging（紧抓的）
- perceptive（敏锐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prehensile tail / prehensile limbs / prehensile feet / prehensile ability**

👉 Example: The spider monkey uses its prehensile tail as a fifth limb to navigate through the dense rainforest canopy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many species of New World monkeys possess **prehensile** tails that function as additional limbs for climbing and foraging.
   许多新世界猴种拥有适于抓握的尾巴，可作为攀爬和觅食的额外肢体。

2. The biologist observed that the chameleon's **prehensile** feet allowed it to maintain a secure grip on even the thinnest branches.
   生物学家观察到变色龙适于抓握的脚使其能在最细的树枝上也保持稳固的抓握。

3. Certain species of seahorses have evolved **prehensile** tails that enable them to anchor securely to coral and underwater vegetation.
   某些海马物种进化出了适于抓握的尾巴，使它们能牢固地锚定在珊瑚和水下植被上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-hens-ile" — imagine a hen (hens) grabbing seeds before (pre) others, using its claws to grasp tightly.
把"prehensile"想成"pre（之前）+ hens（母鸡）+ ile"——想象一只母鸡在其他鸡之前用爪子紧紧抓住种子。
