# 🔗 adjunct

🔊 /ˈædʒʌŋkt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词，形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Something added or attached in a supplementary capacity; an auxiliary or assistant; (adj.) attached in a subordinate or temporary role.
**中文：** 附属物；助手；修饰语；（形容词）附属的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supplement（补充物）
- appendage（附属物）
- auxiliary（辅助物）
- addition（附加物）
- accessory（附件）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adjunct to something**, **adjunct professor**, **adjunct member**

👉 Example: The lab serves as an adjunct to the main research facility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee appointed her as an **adjunct** member to provide expertise on environmental policy.
   委员会任命她为附属成员，以提供环境政策方面的专业知识。

2. Physical therapy is often used as an **adjunct** to surgical treatment for faster recovery.
   物理治疗常作为手术治疗的辅助手段，以加速康复。

3. He worked as an **adjunct** professor at the university while completing his doctoral dissertation.
   他在完成博士论文期间，在大学担任兼职教授。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ad-" (to) + "junct" (join) — something joined to another thing in a supporting role.
联想"ad-"（向）+"junct"（连接）——连接到另一事物上起辅助作用的东西。
