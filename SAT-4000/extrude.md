# 🔧 extrude

🔊 /ɪkˈstruːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To force or push out; to shape material by forcing it through a die or opening; to expel
**中文：** v. 挤出，压出；使突出；逐出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- squeeze out（挤出）
- expel（逐出）
- protrude（突出）
- eject（喷出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extrude material / extrude through a mold / extrude plastic / extrude metal**

👉 Example: The factory uses specialized machines to extrude aluminum into precise structural components.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers designed a new apparatus to **extrude** molten polymer through a narrow die at extremely high temperatures.
   工程师们设计了一种新装置，在极高温度下将熔融聚合物通过窄模具挤出。

2. The volcanic eruption caused magma to **extrude** from multiple fissures along the mountain's western slope.
   火山喷发使岩浆从山体西坡的多条裂缝中涌出。

3. Modern manufacturers commonly **extrude** plastic into various shapes for use in construction and packaging industries.
   现代制造商通常将塑料挤压成各种形状，用于建筑和包装行业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-trude" as "exit by thrusting" — material is thrust out through an opening, like toothpaste from a tube.
把"extrude"想成"通过推力exit"——材料被推出一个开口，就像牙膏从管子里挤出来一样。
