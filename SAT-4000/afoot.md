# 🚶 afoot

🔊 /əˈfʊt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In progress; being planned or happening; also, on foot or walking.
**中文：** 在进行中的；徒步的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- underway（进行中的）
- ongoing（持续的）
- brewing（酝酿中的）
- in progress（进行中的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plans afoot, something is afoot, changes afoot, mischief afoot**
👉 Example: There were rumors that major changes were afoot within the organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective suspected that a sinister conspiracy was **afoot** after discovering the concealed documents in the office.
   侦探在办公室发现隐藏文件后怀疑一个邪恶的阴谋正在进行中。

2. Significant reforms are **afoot** in the education sector that will reshape the national curriculum entirely.
   教育领域正在进行重大改革，将彻底重塑国家课程体系。

3. With new legislation **afoot**, industry leaders convened an urgent meeting to assess the potential regulatory impact.
   随着新立法的推进，行业领袖召开紧急会议评估潜在的监管影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Afoot" is "a" + "foot" — imagine plans walking toward you on foot, meaning something is actively moving forward and in progress.
"Afoot"是"a"+"foot"（脚）——想象计划正朝你走来，意味着某事正在积极推进中。
