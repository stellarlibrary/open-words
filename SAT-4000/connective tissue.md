# 🧬 connective tissue

🔊 /kəˈnektɪv ˈtɪʃuː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Tissue that connects, supports, binds, or separates other tissues or organs, including cartilage, bone, and blood.
**中文：** n. 结缔组织（如肌腱、韧带、软骨等）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supporting tissue（支持组织）
- stromal tissue（基质组织）
- fibrous tissue（纤维组织）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **connective tissue disorder / connective tissue disease / damage to connective tissue / types of connective tissue**

👉 Example: The surgeon carefully examined the connective tissue surrounding the joint before proceeding with the operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician diagnosed the patient with a rare **connective tissue** disorder that affects the elasticity of the skin.
   医生诊断该患者患有一种罕见的结缔组织疾病，影响皮肤的弹性。

2. Research has demonstrated that regular exercise strengthens **connective tissue** and significantly reduces the risk of joint injuries.
   研究表明，定期锻炼可以增强结缔组织，并显著降低关节受伤的风险。

3. The biology textbook describes how **connective tissue** provides structural support and binds together different organs within the body.
   生物学教科书描述了结缔组织如何提供结构支撑并将体内不同器官连接在一起。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "connective tissue" as the body's glue — it "connects" everything like tissue paper holding a gift together.
把"connective tissue"想成身体的胶水——它像包装纸把礼物包在一起一样，将身体的各个部分"连接"起来。
