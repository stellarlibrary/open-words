# 🧠 intelligence

🔊 /ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills; information gathered for strategic or military purposes; an organization engaged in gathering such information
**中文：** n. 智力；聪明；理解力；情报；消息

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intellect（智力）
- acumen（敏锐）
- sagacity（睿智）
- information（情报）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **artificial intelligence / intelligence agency / intelligence gathering / emotional intelligence**

👉 Example: The intelligence community analyzed the intercepted communications to assess the emerging threat.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her remarkable **intelligence** allowed her to grasp complex mathematical concepts that most students found extremely challenging.
   她非凡的智力使她能够掌握大多数学生觉得极其困难的复杂数学概念。

2. The defense ministry relied on accurate **intelligence** reports to formulate its strategic response to the growing regional crisis.
   国防部依赖准确的情报报告来制定应对日益严重的地区危机的战略对策。

3. Researchers continue to debate whether **intelligence** is primarily determined by genetic factors or shaped by environmental influences.
   研究人员继续争论智力主要由遗传因素决定还是受环境影响塑造。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intel-ligence" — "intel" sounds like "internal light," the inner brightness of the mind that helps you understand the world.
把"intelligence"想成"intel（内部的）+ ligence（光亮）"——内心的光芒，帮助你理解世界的智慧之光。
