# 💧 pervious

🔊 /ˈpɜːrviəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Allowing water or other substances to pass through; permeable; open to ideas or suggestions
**中文：** adj. 能被通过的；能接受的；可渗透的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permeable（可渗透的）
- porous（多孔的）
- penetrable（可穿透的）
- absorbent（吸收性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pervious to water / pervious surface / pervious to influence / pervious to reason**

👉 Example: The pervious pavement allowed rainwater to seep directly into the ground below.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers recommended installing **pervious** concrete to reduce surface runoff and minimize the risk of urban flooding.
   工程师建议安装透水混凝土，以减少地表径流并降低城市洪涝的风险。

2. The soil in this region is highly **pervious**, allowing groundwater to recharge rapidly during the rainy season.
   该地区的土壤渗透性很强，使得地下水在雨季能够迅速补给。

3. Unlike his predecessor, the new director proved **pervious** to constructive criticism and willingly adjusted organizational policies.
   与前任不同，新主任乐于接受建设性批评，并主动调整了组织政策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pervious" as the opposite of "impervious" — remove the "im-" (not) and you get something that can be passed through.
把"pervious"想成"impervious"（不可渗透的）的反义词——去掉"im-"（不），就得到可以通过的东西。
