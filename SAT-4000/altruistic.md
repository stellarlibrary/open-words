# 🕊 altruistic

🔊 /ˌæltruːˈɪstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing a selfless concern for the well-being of others; characterized by unselfish devotion to the needs and interests of others
**中文：** adj. 利他的；无私心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- selfless（无私的）
- benevolent（仁慈的）
- unselfish（不自私的）
- magnanimous（宽宏大量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **altruistic behavior / altruistic motives / purely altruistic / altruistic act**

👉 Example: Her decision to donate her entire bonus was motivated by purely altruistic intentions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist's **altruistic** decision to share her groundbreaking research freely enabled laboratories worldwide to accelerate their progress.
   这位科学家无私地分享其突破性研究的决定使全球实验室能够加速其研究进展。

2. While some questioned his motives, his consistent pattern of **altruistic** behavior suggested a genuinely selfless character beneath the surface.
   尽管有人质疑他的动机，但他一贯的利他行为模式表明他内心深处确实是无私的。

3. The organization recognized her **altruistic** contributions to public health by awarding her the prestigious humanitarian service medal.
   该组织授予她享有盛誉的人道主义服务奖章，以表彰她对公共卫生的无私贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "altruistic" as "all-true-istic" — a truly altruistic person is "all true" to others, putting their needs above personal gain.
把"altruistic"想成"all-true-istic"——一个真正利他的人对他人"完全真诚"，将他人的需求置于个人利益之上。
