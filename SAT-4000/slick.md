# 🧊 slick

🔊 /slɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, adverb, verb | 形容词, 名词, 副词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Smooth and glossy; superficially impressive but lacking substance; clever and skillful. (n.) A smooth tool; a popular magazine. (adv.) In a smooth manner. (v.) To make smooth or sleek
**中文：** adj. 光滑的；熟练的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- glossy（光滑的）
- smooth（圆滑的）
- polished（精致的）
- shrewd（精明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **slick surface / slick presentation / slick operator / oil slick**

👉 Example: The salesman gave a slick presentation, but the product failed to deliver on its promises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **slick** marketing campaign attracted thousands of investors who later discovered the venture was fraudulent.
   这场华而不实的营销活动吸引了数千名投资者，他们后来发现该项目是欺诈性的。

2. After the rainstorm, the **slick** roadway caused multiple vehicles to lose traction and collide at the intersection.
   暴雨过后，光滑的路面导致多辆车在十字路口失去抓地力并发生碰撞。

3. The diplomat was a **slick** negotiator who could defuse tensions with carefully chosen words and strategic concessions.
   这位外交官是一位熟练的谈判者，能用精心措辞和策略性让步化解紧张局势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slick" as something so smooth it makes you "slip and lick" the ground — polished on the surface but potentially dangerous.
把"slick"想成东西太光滑让你"滑倒舔地"——表面光鲜但可能暗藏危险。
