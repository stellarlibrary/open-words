# 🎭 expression

🔊 /ɪkˈsprɛʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of conveying thoughts, feelings, or ideas; a look on someone's face that reveals emotion; a mathematical symbol or formula; a word or phrase
**中文：** n. 表现，表示，表达；表情；式，符号；词句；表达式

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- utterance（表达）
- articulation（阐述）
- manifestation（表现）
- phrase（词句）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **facial expression / freedom of expression / mathematical expression / idiomatic expression**

👉 Example: The professor encouraged freedom of expression in all classroom discussions and debates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist's paintings serve as a profound **expression** of the emotional turmoil experienced during wartime conflict.
   这位艺术家的画作是对战时冲突中所经历的情感动荡的深刻表达。

2. Her facial **expression** betrayed a deep sense of unease despite her attempts to maintain composure during the interview.
   尽管她在面试中努力保持镇定，她的面部表情仍流露出深深的不安。

3. The algebraic **expression** presented in the examination required students to apply advanced principles of calculus and logic.
   考试中呈现的代数表达式要求学生运用高等微积分和逻辑学原理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-press-ion" as "pressing out" — you press your thoughts and feelings outward for others to see or hear.
把"expression"想成"ex(向外)+press(压)+ion"——把内心的想法和情感向外"压"出来，就是表达。
