# 🤔 deliberate

🔊 /dɪˈlɪbərət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done consciously and intentionally; carefully weighed or considered. (v.) To think about or discuss issues carefully before reaching a decision
**中文：** adj. 故意的；深思熟虑的 v. 仔细考虑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intentional（故意的）
- premeditated（预谋的）
- calculated（深思熟虑的）
- contemplate（沉思）
- ponder（仔细考虑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliberate attempt / deliberate action / deliberate decision / deliberate over / deliberate on a matter**

👉 Example: The jury was asked to deliberate carefully before reaching a verdict in this complex case.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation concluded that the fire was a **deliberate** act of arson rather than an accidental electrical malfunction.
   调查得出结论，这起火灾是蓄意纵火行为，而非意外的电气故障。

2. The senators will **deliberate** on the proposed legislation for several weeks before casting their final votes on the bill.
   参议员们将就拟议的立法进行数周的审议，然后对该法案进行最终投票。

3. Her **deliberate** approach to problem-solving ensured that every possible outcome was thoroughly analyzed before implementation began.
   她深思熟虑的问题解决方法确保了在开始实施之前对每一种可能的结果进行彻底分析。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-liber-ate" as "de- + liber (weigh/balance)" — to weigh something carefully on a scale before making a decision, like a judge deliberating.
把"deliberate"想成"de- + liber（称量/平衡）"——在做决定之前在天平上仔细称量，就像法官在审议一样。
