# 😤 embitter

🔊 /ɪmˈbɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause someone to feel bitter, resentful, or disillusioned; to make a situation more painful or unpleasant
**中文：** v. 使怨恨；使痛苦；使难受

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alienate（疏远）
- disillusion（使幻灭）
- sour（使变得不愉快）
- antagonize（使对立）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **embittered by failure / embittered relationship / embitter someone against / become embittered**

👉 Example: Years of unfair treatment had embittered the workers against the company's management.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged legal dispute **embittered** both families and destroyed what had once been a cordial neighborly relationship.
   旷日持久的法律纠纷使两个家庭心生怨恨，摧毁了曾经融洽的邻里关系。

2. Repeated failures to secure funding **embittered** the scientist, causing her to withdraw from academic collaboration entirely.
   屡次未能获得资助使这位科学家心生怨恨，导致她完全退出了学术合作。

3. The veterans returned home **embittered** by the government's broken promises regarding healthcare and employment support programs.
   退伍军人因政府在医疗和就业支持项目上的失信而心怀怨恨地回到家乡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "em-BITTER" — to make someone feel BITTER inside, as if they have tasted something sharp and unpleasant.
把"embitter"想成"em-BITTER"——让某人内心感到苦涩（BITTER），就像尝到了尖锐刺鼻的味道。
