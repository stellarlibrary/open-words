# 😤 upbraid

🔊 /ʌpˈbreɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To scold or criticize someone severely; to reproach harshly for a fault or wrongdoing
**中文：** v. 责骂；训斥；严厉谴责

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rebuke（斥责）
- reproach（责备）
- reprimand（训斥）
- admonish（告诫）
- chastise（严惩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **upbraid someone for something / upbraid harshly / upbraid publicly**

👉 Example: The professor upbraided the student for submitting a plagiarized research paper.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee chair **upbraided** the delegates for their failure to reach a consensus on the proposed environmental regulations.
   委员会主席训斥代表们未能就拟议的环境法规达成共识。

2. Historical accounts reveal that the emperor frequently **upbraided** his advisors for offering counsel that contradicted his ambitions.
   历史记载显示，这位皇帝经常训斥那些提出与其雄心相悖建议的顾问。

3. The editorial **upbraided** city officials for their negligent handling of the public health crisis throughout the summer.
   这篇社论严厉谴责了市政官员在整个夏季对公共卫生危机的疏忽处理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "up-braid" — imagine braiding someone's hair upward roughly while scolding them; the harsh pulling mirrors harsh words.
把"upbraid"想成"向上编辫子"——想象一边粗暴地向上编某人的头发一边责骂他们，粗暴的拉扯就像严厉的话语。
