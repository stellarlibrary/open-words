# 🔷 hexagon

🔊 /ˈhɛksəɡɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A plane figure with six straight sides and six angles; a six-sided polygon
**中文：** n. 六角形；六边形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- six-sided figure（六边形）
- polygon（多边形）
- hexagram（六角星形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regular hexagon / hexagon shape / hexagon pattern / hexagonal structure**

👉 Example: The architect incorporated hexagon patterns into the building's facade for both aesthetic and structural purposes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each cell within a honeycomb is a perfect **hexagon**, demonstrating nature's remarkable efficiency in structural design and space utilization.
   蜂巢中的每个蜂房都是完美的六边形，展示了自然界在结构设计和空间利用方面的卓越效率。

2. The mathematician proved that a regular **hexagon** can be divided into exactly six equilateral triangles sharing a common central vertex.
   数学家证明了正六边形可以被精确地分成六个共享一个公共中心顶点的等边三角形。

3. Engineers selected the **hexagon** as the optimal tile shape because it covers surfaces completely without leaving gaps or overlapping edges.
   工程师选择六边形作为最佳瓷砖形状，因为它可以完全覆盖表面而不留间隙或重叠边缘。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hex-" (six, like a hex bolt) + "-agon" (angle) — a shape with six angles, just like the familiar shape of a stop sign's cousin.
把"hexagon"拆成"hex-"（六，像六角螺栓）+"-agon"（角），一个有六个角的形状，就像蜂巢里的格子。
