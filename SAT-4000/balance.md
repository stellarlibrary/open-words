# ⚖️ balance

🔊 /ˈbæləns/<br>
🏷️ *n. v. | 名词 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A state of equilibrium; the amount remaining in an account. (v.) To bring into or maintain a state of equilibrium; to settle or reconcile accounts.

**中文：** （名词）平衡；余额。（动词）使平衡；结算，使收支相抵。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- equilibrium（平衡）
- stability（稳定）
- remainder（余额）
- poise（均衡）
- offset（抵消）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

As a noun, "balance" can refer to physical steadiness, mental composure, or a financial remainder. As a verb, it means to equalize weight or forces, or to reconcile financial accounts. The phrase "strike a balance" means to find a compromise between two extremes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accountant worked diligently to **balance** the company's financial statements before the annual audit commenced.
   会计在年度审计开始前努力**结算**公司的财务报表。

2. Maintaining a healthy **balance** between professional obligations and personal well-being is essential for sustained productivity.
   在职业责任和个人健康之间保持良好的**平衡**对持续的生产力至关重要。

3. The gymnast demonstrated remarkable poise and **balance** while performing her routine on the narrow beam.
   那位体操运动员在窄平衡木上表演时展示了非凡的沉着和**平衡**感。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Picture a classic balance scale with two pans — when both sides are equal, the scale is in "balance." The word itself feels balanced: "bal-ance."

想象一个经典的天平秤，两个托盘——当两边相等时，天平就处于"平衡"状态。这个词本身听起来也很平衡："bal-ance"。
