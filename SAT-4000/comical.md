# 😂 comical

🔊 /ˈkɒm.ɪ.kəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Amusing or funny, especially in a way that is absurd or incongruous
**中文：** adj. 滑稽的；好笑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- funny（好笑的）
- humorous（幽默的）
- amusing（有趣的）
- laughable（可笑的）
- ludicrous（滑稽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **comical situation / comical expression / comical effect / comical appearance**

👉 Example: The actor's comical expression elicited roaring laughter from the entire audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **comical** attempts to operate the new projector provided unexpected entertainment for the entire lecture hall.
   教授滑稽地试图操作新投影仪，为整个演讲厅提供了意想不到的娱乐。

2. There was something deeply **comical** about the dignified ambassador slipping on the polished marble floor during the ceremony.
   在典礼上，那位庄重的大使在光滑的大理石地板上滑倒，这件事有一种深深的滑稽感。

3. The playwright skillfully crafted a **comical** sequence of misunderstandings that kept audiences laughing throughout the performance.
   剧作家巧妙地设计了一连串滑稽的误会，让观众在整场演出中笑声不断。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comic-al" — directly related to "comic," like a comic strip that makes you laugh at absurd situations.
把"comical"想成"comic-al"——直接与"comic（漫画/喜剧）"相关，就像漫画让你对荒诞的情景发笑。
