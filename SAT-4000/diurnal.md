# 🌞 diurnal

🔊 /daɪˈɜːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or occurring during the daytime; happening every day or recurring daily
**中文：** adj. 白天的；每日的；日间的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- daytime（白天的）
- daily（每日的）
- quotidian（日常的）
- daylight（日间的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diurnal cycle / diurnal species / diurnal variation / diurnal rhythm**

👉 Example: Most birds of prey are diurnal, hunting actively during the hours of daylight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists observed that the **diurnal** temperature variation in desert regions can exceed thirty degrees within a single day.
   科学家观察到沙漠地区的昼夜温差在一天之内可超过三十度。

2. Unlike their nocturnal relatives, these **diurnal** primates forage for food exclusively during the sunlit hours of morning.
   与夜行性近亲不同，这些日行性灵长类动物只在早晨的日照时段觅食。

3. The research team documented the **diurnal** patterns of pollinator activity to better understand the ecosystem's reproductive cycles.
   研究团队记录了传粉者活动的昼间规律，以更好地了解生态系统的繁殖周期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "di-urnal" — "di" sounds like "day" and "urnal" like "journal," so a diurnal journal records what happens during the day.
把"diurnal"想成"di(day)-urnal(journal)"——一本白天的日记，记录白天发生的事情。
