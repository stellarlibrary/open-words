# 📦 stock

🔊 /stɒk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Shares of ownership in a company; a supply of goods kept for sale; lineage or ancestry. (adj.) Commonly kept in supply; ordinary. (v.) To keep a supply of goods available for sale
**中文：** n. 备料，库存，现货；股票，公债；砧木

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inventory（库存）
- supply（供应）
- shares（股份）
- reserve（储备）
- lineage（血统）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stock market / in stock / out of stock / stock up on / stock phrase / take stock of**

👉 Example: Investors should carefully evaluate a company's fundamentals before purchasing its stock.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company's **stock** surged by fifteen percent after the announcement of a groundbreaking partnership with a leading technology firm.
   该公司的股票在宣布与一家领先科技公司建立突破性合作关系后飙升了百分之十五。

2. The manager decided to **stock** the warehouse with additional supplies in anticipation of the upcoming holiday shopping season.
   经理决定在即将到来的假日购物季之前在仓库中储备额外的物资。

3. Before making any major decisions, the board of directors paused to take **stock** of the organization's current financial position.
   在做出任何重大决定之前，董事会暂停下来评估组织当前的财务状况。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stock" as a "store of goods stacked" — whether it is products on shelves or shares in a portfolio, stock means what you have stored up.
把"stock"想成"store of goods stacked（堆叠的货物储备）"——无论是货架上的产品还是投资组合中的股份，stock都是你储备的东西。
