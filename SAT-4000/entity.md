# 🔷 entity

🔊 /ˈentɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A thing with distinct and independent existence; a being or organization that possesses its own identity
**中文：** n. 实体；存在物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- being（存在物）
- organism（有机体）
- body（团体）
- organization（组织）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **legal entity / separate entity / corporate entity / political entity / independent entity**

👉 Example: Each subsidiary operates as a separate legal entity with its own financial obligations and governance structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly formed corporation was registered as a distinct legal **entity** with full authority to enter contracts independently.
   这家新成立的公司被注册为独立的法人实体，有权独立签订合同。

2. Philosophers have long debated whether consciousness constitutes a separate **entity** or merely emerges from complex neurological processes.
   哲学家们长期争论意识是否构成一个独立的存在物，还是仅仅从复杂的神经过程中产生。

3. The international treaty recognized the territory as a sovereign **entity** entitled to self-governance and diplomatic representation abroad.
   该国际条约承认该领土为拥有自治权和海外外交代表权的主权实体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ent-ity" — it contains "ent" like "enter," something that has entered existence and stands on its own as a real thing.
把"entity"想成"ent（存在）+ ity（名词后缀）"——一个独立存在的事物就是实体。
