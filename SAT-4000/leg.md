# 🦵 leg

🔊 /lɛɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A limb used for standing and walking; a supporting structure resembling a limb; in geometry, either of the two non-parallel sides of a trapezoid or either of the two sides forming the right angle in a right triangle.
**中文：** n. 腿；支柱；（梯形的）两条不平行的边；（直角三角形的）直角边

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- limb（肢体）
- support（支撑物）
- pillar（支柱）
- side（边）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **leg of a triangle / leg of a table / leg of a journey / leg of a trapezoid**

👉 Example: In geometry, students must identify the legs of a right triangle to apply the Pythagorean theorem correctly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician asked students to calculate the length of each **leg** of the trapezoid using the given coordinates.
   数学老师要求学生们用给定的坐标计算梯形每条腰的长度。

2. One **leg** of the right triangle measured five centimeters while the hypotenuse extended to thirteen centimeters exactly.
   直角三角形的一条直角边为五厘米，而斜边恰好为十三厘米。

3. The antique table wobbled dangerously because one of its ornately carved wooden **legs** had cracked near the base.
   那张古董桌子摇晃得很厉害，因为其中一条雕刻精美的木质桌腿在底部附近裂开了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a table's legs holding it up — just as your legs support your body, a leg in geometry supports the shape of a triangle or trapezoid.
想想桌腿支撑桌子——就像你的腿支撑身体一样，几何中的"leg"支撑着三角形或梯形的形状。
