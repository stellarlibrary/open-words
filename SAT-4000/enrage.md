# 😡 enrage

🔊 /ɪnˈreɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make extremely angry; to fill with rage or fury
**中文：** v. 激怒；使暴怒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- infuriate（激怒）
- incense（使愤怒）
- provoke（激起）
- exasperate（使恼怒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enrage the public / enraged by / enraged at / become enraged / utterly enraged**

👉 Example: The unjust verdict enraged citizens, prompting widespread demonstrations throughout the capital.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government's decision to raise taxes without public consultation **enraged** millions of working-class families across the country.
   政府未经公众咨询便决定加税，激怒了全国数百万工薪家庭。

2. Reports of corporate misconduct and environmental negligence **enraged** shareholders who demanded immediate accountability from executives.
   关于企业不端行为和环境疏忽的报道激怒了股东，他们要求高管立即承担责任。

3. The diplomat's inflammatory and dismissive remarks **enraged** the delegation, effectively derailing months of careful negotiations.
   该外交官带有煽动性和轻蔑态度的言论激怒了代表团，实际上破坏了数月的精心谈判。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-rage" as putting someone "into a rage" — the prefix "en-" means to cause, so enrage literally means to cause rage.
把"enrage"想成让某人"陷入rage（暴怒）"——前缀"en-"表示使之，所以enrage字面意思就是使人暴怒。
