# 🔄 periodicity

🔊 /ˌpɪəriəˈdɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality or character of being periodic; the tendency to recur at regular intervals
**中文：** n. 周期性；频率；定期性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recurrence（反复）
- regularity（规律性）
- cyclicity（循环性）
- rhythm（节律）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **periodicity of events / chemical periodicity / seasonal periodicity / exhibit periodicity**

👉 Example: The periodicity of the tides is governed by the gravitational pull of the moon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists have long studied the **periodicity** of sunspot activity to predict potential disruptions to satellite communications.
   科学家们长期研究太阳黑子活动的周期性，以预测对卫星通信的潜在干扰。

2. The **periodicity** of economic recessions suggests that market downturns follow identifiable and somewhat predictable cycles.
   经济衰退的周期性表明，市场低迷遵循可识别且在一定程度上可预测的周期。

3. Mendeleev's discovery of chemical **periodicity** revolutionized the organization and understanding of the known elements.
   门捷列夫对化学周期性的发现彻底改变了人们对已知元素的组织方式和理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "period-icity" — it contains "period," which means a span of time that repeats, plus "-icity" meaning a quality. So periodicity is the quality of repeating in periods.
把"periodicity"想成"period（周期）+ icity（特性）"——即按周期重复出现的特性。
