# ⛰️ brae

🔊 /breɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A steep bank or hillside; a slope, especially one leading down to a river or stream (chiefly Scottish)
**中文：** 斜坡；山坡（主要用于苏格兰英语）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hillside（山坡）
- slope（斜坡）
- incline（倾斜面）
- embankment（堤坡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **steep brae / grassy brae / climb the brae / down the brae**

👉 Example: The children raced down the brae toward the river, their laughter echoing across the valley.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient castle stood atop a rugged **brae** that overlooked the winding river and the pastoral valley below.
   这座古老的城堡矗立在一座崎岖的山坡上，俯瞰着蜿蜒的河流和下方的田园山谷。

2. Farmers in the Scottish Highlands often cultivated their crops on the gentler sections of the **brae** near the village.
   苏格兰高地的农民常常在村庄附近山坡较为平缓的地段耕种庄稼。

3. The poet described the wildflowers blooming along the **brae** as a vivid tapestry of color against the grey sky.
   诗人将沿着山坡盛开的野花描绘成灰色天空下一幅色彩鲜明的锦缎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brae" rhyming with "way" — a steep way up a hill in Scotland, the path climbing a hillside.
把"brae"想成与"way（路）"押韵——苏格兰山丘上一条陡峭的路，沿山坡攀升的小径。
