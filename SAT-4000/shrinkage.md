# 📉 shrinkage

🔊 /ˈʃrɪŋkɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process, degree, or amount of shrinking; a reduction in size, quantity, or value
**中文：** n. 收缩

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contraction（收缩）
- reduction（减少）
- diminution（缩小）
- decrease（减少）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shrinkage of fabric / inventory shrinkage / economic shrinkage / shrinkage rate**

👉 Example: The retailer attributed significant revenue losses to inventory shrinkage caused by theft and administrative errors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **shrinkage** of Arctic sea ice has accelerated dramatically over the past two decades, alarming climate scientists worldwide.
   过去二十年间，北极海冰的缩减急剧加速，令全球气候科学家深感忧虑。

2. Industrial engineers must account for material **shrinkage** during the cooling process when designing precision metal components.
   工业工程师在设计精密金属部件时必须考虑冷却过程中的材料收缩。

3. The **shrinkage** of the rural population has placed enormous economic pressure on agricultural communities throughout the region.
   农村人口的减少给整个地区的农业社区带来了巨大的经济压力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shrink-age" — the "age" or extent to which something has "shrunk," like a sweater getting smaller after washing.
把"shrinkage"想成"shrink（缩小）+ age（程度）"——某物缩小的程度，就像毛衣洗后变小一样。
