# 📜 laudation

🔊 /lɔːˈdeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of praising; a formal expression of praise; a eulogy or tribute
**中文：** n. 赞美；颂词；称赞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eulogy（颂词）
- tribute（赞辞）
- commendation（表彰）
- panegyric（颂文）
- acclaim（赞扬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laudation of someone / deliver a laudation / formal laudation / public laudation**

👉 Example: The professor delivered a moving laudation of the retiring dean's contributions to the university.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ceremony featured a heartfelt **laudation** of the Nobel laureate's decades of tireless humanitarian work worldwide.
   仪式上对这位诺贝尔奖获得者数十年来不懈的全球人道主义工作进行了衷心的赞美。

2. His **laudation** at the memorial service captured the profound impact the late author had on contemporary literature.
   他在追悼会上的颂词道出了这位已故作家对当代文学的深远影响。

3. The chairman's **laudation** acknowledged every team member's individual contribution to the project's remarkable success.
   主席的赞辞肯定了每位团队成员对项目显著成功所做的个人贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laudation" as "laud + ation" — the action or process of lauding someone, a formal act of giving praise.
把"laudation"想成"laud（赞美）+ ation（行为）"——赞美某人的行为或过程，一种正式的赞扬行为。
