# 📋 enumerate

🔊 /ɪˈnjuːməreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To name or list things one by one; to count or calculate
**中文：** v. 列举；枚举；计算

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- list（列举）
- itemize（逐项列出）
- catalog（编目）
- tally（计算）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enumerate the reasons / enumerate the steps / enumerate the advantages**

👉 Example: The professor asked students to enumerate the key factors contributing to the Industrial Revolution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The report carefully **enumerated** every violation discovered during the comprehensive audit of the corporation's financial records.
   该报告仔细列举了在对公司财务记录进行全面审计期间发现的每一项违规行为。

2. The constitution **enumerates** the fundamental rights and responsibilities granted to all citizens of the republic.
   宪法逐一列举了赋予共和国所有公民的基本权利和责任。

3. Researchers **enumerated** over three hundred distinct species of insects inhabiting the tropical rainforest canopy during their survey.
   研究人员在调查期间列举了栖息在热带雨林冠层中的三百多种不同昆虫。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-NUMBER-ate" — to enumerate is to go through things by number, listing them one by one.
把"enumerate"想成"e-数字(NUMBER)-ate"——列举就是按编号逐一过一遍事物。
