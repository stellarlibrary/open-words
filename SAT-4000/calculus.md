# 🧮 calculus

🔊 /ˈkælkjələs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (Mathematics) A branch of mathematics dealing with rates of change and accumulation (differential and integral calculus). (Medicine) An abnormal stony mass formed in the body, such as a kidney stone
**中文：** 微积分学；（医学）结石

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- computation（计算）
- analysis（分析）
- stone（结石）
- concretion（凝结物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **differential calculus / integral calculus / renal calculus / political calculus / moral calculus**

👉 Example: The political calculus behind the decision involved weighing electoral risks against long-term policy benefits.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students who master **calculus** gain essential analytical tools applicable to physics, engineering, economics, and numerous other quantitative disciplines.
   掌握微积分的学生将获得适用于物理学、工程学、经济学和众多其他定量学科的基本分析工具。

2. The physician diagnosed a renal **calculus** and recommended immediate intervention to prevent further complications and chronic kidney damage.
   医生诊断出肾结石，并建议立即进行干预以防止进一步并发症和慢性肾脏损伤。

3. The diplomat's strategic **calculus** required balancing national security interests against the imperative of maintaining fragile international alliances.
   外交官的战略考量需要在国家安全利益与维持脆弱国际联盟的紧迫性之间取得平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "calculus" from Latin "calculus" meaning "small stone" — ancient Romans used small stones for counting, which evolved into the mathematics meaning.
"Calculus"源自拉丁语"calculus"，意为"小石子"——古罗马人用小石子计数，后来演变为数学中的微积分含义。
