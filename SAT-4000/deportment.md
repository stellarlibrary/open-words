# 🎩 deportment

🔊 /dɪˈpɔːrtmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person's behavior or manners; the way one carries oneself physically and socially
**中文：** n. 举止；行为；态度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- demeanor（举止）
- bearing（风度）
- conduct（行为）
- comportment（举止）
- manner（态度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proper deportment / deportment in public / deportment and etiquette / graceful deportment**

👉 Example: The finishing school placed great emphasis on deportment and social etiquette.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's impeccable **deportment** during the summit earned widespread admiration from both allies and adversaries.
   这位外交官在峰会期间无可挑剔的举止赢得了盟友和对手的广泛赞赏。

2. Victorian society placed extraordinary value on proper **deportment**, expecting women to maintain rigid posture at all times.
   维多利亚时代的社会极其重视得体的举止，要求女性时刻保持端正的姿态。

3. The headmaster insisted that students' **deportment** reflected the institution's commitment to discipline and academic excellence.
   校长坚持认为学生的行为举止体现了学校对纪律和学术卓越的承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deportment" as "deport + ment" — the manner in which you "carry yourself out" into the world is your deportment.
把"deportment"想成"deport（表现）+ ment（名词后缀）"——你向世界展现自己的方式就是你的举止。
