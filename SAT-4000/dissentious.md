# 🗣️ dissentious

🔊 /dɪˈsɛnʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inclined to argue or quarrel; contentious and prone to causing disagreement
**中文：** adj. 争吵的；好争论的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contentious（好争论的）
- quarrelsome（爱争吵的）
- argumentative（好辩的）
- combative（好斗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dissentious nature / dissentious faction / dissentious debate / dissentious atmosphere**

👉 Example: The dissentious atmosphere in the committee made it nearly impossible to reach any consensus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **dissentious** faction within the organization repeatedly blocked efforts to implement meaningful and necessary institutional reforms.
   该组织内好争论的派系一再阻挠实施有意义且必要的制度改革。

2. Her **dissentious** temperament alienated many of her colleagues, who eventually refused to collaborate with her on projects.
   她好争论的性格疏远了许多同事，他们最终拒绝与她合作开展项目。

3. The chairman struggled to maintain order during the **dissentious** proceedings, as delegates constantly interrupted one another with objections.
   主席在这场充满争论的议程中艰难地维持秩序，代表们不断互相打断并提出反对意见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dissentious" as "dissent" + "-ious" — a person full of dissent, always ready to argue and disagree.
把"dissentious"想成"dissent"（异议）+"-ious"（充满的）——一个充满异议的人，总是准备争论和反对。
