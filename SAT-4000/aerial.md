# ✈️ aerial

🔊 /ˈɛr.i.əl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Existing, occurring, or operating in the air; relating to aircraft; (n.) an antenna for receiving or transmitting radio signals.
**中文：** (adj.) 空中的，航空的；(n.) 天线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- airborne（空中的）
- overhead（头顶上的）
- atmospheric（大气的）
- antenna（天线）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aerial + view / photograph / survey / bombardment / antenna**

👉 Example: The aerial photograph revealed the full extent of the flood damage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military conducted an **aerial** survey to assess infrastructure damage across the affected region.
   军方进行了一次空中勘察，以评估受灾地区的基础设施损毁情况。

2. **Aerial** photography has become an indispensable tool for modern urban planning and cartography.
   航空摄影已成为现代城市规划和制图学中不可或缺的工具。

3. The technician climbed the tower to repair the damaged **aerial** that disrupted local broadcast signals.
   技术人员爬上塔楼修理损坏的天线，该天线曾中断了当地的广播信号。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aerial" comes from "aer-" (air) + "-ial" (relating to) — anything aerial is related to the air above us.
联想记忆：aerial = aer-（空气）+ -ial（与……有关的），即"与空气有关的"，也就是空中的、航空的。
