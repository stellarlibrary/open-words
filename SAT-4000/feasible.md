# ✅ feasible

🔊 /ˈfiːzəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Possible and practical to accomplish; capable of being done or carried out successfully
**中文：** adj. 可行的；切实可行的；可实行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- practicable（可实行的）
- viable（可行的）
- achievable（可实现的）
- workable（可操作的）
- attainable（可达到的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feasible plan / feasible solution / economically feasible / technically feasible / feasible alternative**

👉 Example: The engineering team confirmed that the proposed bridge design was both structurally sound and financially feasible.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After months of analysis, the consultants concluded that expanding into the Asian market was both strategically and financially **feasible**.
   经过数月的分析，顾问们得出结论，进军亚洲市场在战略上和财务上都是可行的。

2. Advances in renewable energy technology have made large-scale solar power generation increasingly **feasible** in arid regions worldwide.
   可再生能源技术的进步使大规模太阳能发电在全球干旱地区变得越来越可行。

3. The committee evaluated whether it was **feasible** to implement the new curriculum within the existing budget and academic timeline.
   委员会评估了在现有预算和学术时间表内实施新课程是否可行。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "feas-ible" as "fees are able" — if the fees are manageable, the project is feasible and can move forward.
把"feasible"想成"fees are able（费用可以承受）"——如果费用可控，项目就是可行的，可以推进。
