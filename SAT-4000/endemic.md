# 🌍 endemic

🔊 /ɛnˈdɛmɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Native to or restricted to a certain region; regularly found among particular people or in a certain area. (n.) A disease or condition regularly found in a specific locality
**中文：** adj. 地方性的；风土的 n. 地方病

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indigenous（本土的）
- native（原产的）
- local（地方的）
- prevalent（流行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endemic to a region / endemic species / endemic disease / endemic corruption**

👉 Example: Malaria is endemic to many tropical regions where mosquito populations remain largely uncontrolled.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rare orchid is **endemic** to this remote island and cannot be found growing naturally anywhere else in the world.
   这种稀有兰花是该偏远岛屿的特有物种，在世界其他任何地方都无法自然生长。

2. Corruption has become so **endemic** in the institution that comprehensive structural reform is urgently required to restore public trust.
   腐败在该机构中已变得如此根深蒂固，迫切需要全面的结构性改革以恢复公众信任。

3. Scientists identified several **endemic** diseases in the region that had not been previously documented in medical literature.
   科学家在该地区发现了几种此前未在医学文献中记载过的地方病。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-demic" as "in the demo(graphic)" — something endemic belongs to a specific demographic area or population, like a local specialty.
把"endemic"想成"in the demo(graphic)（在特定人群中）"——endemic的事物属于特定的地区或人群，就像当地的特产一样。
