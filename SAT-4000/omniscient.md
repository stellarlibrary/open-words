# 🧠 omniscient

🔊 /ɑːmˈnɪʃənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Knowing everything; having infinite knowledge or awareness. (n.) An all-knowing being, especially God
**中文：** adj. 全知的；无所不知的 n. 上帝；无所不知者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- all-knowing（全知的）
- all-seeing（全视的）
- wise（睿智的）
- infinitely knowledgeable（知识无穷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **omniscient narrator / omniscient deity / omniscient perspective / omniscient point of view / seemingly omniscient**

👉 Example: The novel employs an omniscient narrator who reveals the inner thoughts of every character in the story.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author chose an **omniscient** narrative voice to provide readers with insight into each character's hidden motivations and fears.
   作者选择了全知的叙事视角，为读者提供了对每个角色隐藏动机和恐惧的洞察。

2. Theologians have long debated whether an **omniscient** creator can coexist with the concept of genuine human free will.
   神学家们长期以来一直在争论全知的造物主能否与真正的人类自由意志概念共存。

3. The intelligence agency's surveillance capabilities were so extensive that citizens perceived the government as nearly **omniscient**.
   情报机构的监控能力如此广泛，以至于公民认为政府几乎是无所不知的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "omni-scient" as "omni" (all) + "scient" (knowing, from Latin scientia) — one who possesses all knowledge.
把"omniscient"想成"omni（所有）"加"scient（知道，源自拉丁语scientia）"——拥有所有知识的人。
