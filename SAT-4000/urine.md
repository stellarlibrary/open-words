# 💧 urine

🔊 /ˈjʊrɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A pale yellow liquid waste product filtered from the blood by the kidneys, stored in the bladder, and discharged from the body
**中文：** n. 尿，小便

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pee（小便）
- micturition product（排尿产物）
- urinary excretion（泌尿排泄物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **urine sample / urine test / urine analysis / urine output / traces in urine**

👉 Example: The physician requested a urine sample to screen for potential metabolic abnormalities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician analyzed the **urine** sample to detect the presence of abnormal protein levels indicating kidney disease.
   实验室技术人员分析了尿液样本，以检测表明肾病的异常蛋白质水平。

2. Forensic investigators collected **urine** specimens from the scene as part of the comprehensive toxicological examination of evidence.
   法医调查人员从现场收集了尿液标本，作为全面毒理学证据检查的一部分。

3. Prolonged dehydration significantly reduces **urine** output and may lead to dangerous concentrations of waste products in the body.
   长期脱水会显著减少尿液排出量，并可能导致体内废物浓度达到危险水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "urine" as sharing a root with "urea" — both relate to the urinary system and the body's process of waste elimination.
把"urine"和"urea"（尿素）联系起来——两者都与泌尿系统和身体排泄废物的过程有关。
