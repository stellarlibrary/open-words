# ☀️ bask

🔊 /bæsk/<br>
🏷️ *v. | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To lie exposed to warmth and light, typically from the sun, for relaxation and pleasure; to take great pleasure or derive enjoyment from something.

**中文：** 晒太阳；取暖；沉浸于（愉悦之中）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- luxuriate （尽情享受）
- revel （陶醉）
- sunbathe （日光浴）
- delight （欣喜）
- relish （享受）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Bask" is often followed by "in": "bask in the sun," "bask in the glory," or "bask in the attention." The figurative usage is very common, meaning to enjoy and take pleasure in praise, success, or admiration. It always carries a positive, leisurely connotation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tourists paused to **bask** in the warm Mediterranean sunlight along the coastal promenade.
   中文：游客们在海滨长廊上驻足，沐浴在温暖的地中海阳光中。

2. The award-winning author continued to **bask** in the widespread acclaim following her latest novel.
   中文：这位获奖作家继续沉浸在她最新小说赢得的广泛赞誉之中。

3. After the remarkable victory, the entire team gathered to **bask** in their well-deserved glory.
   中文：在取得非凡胜利之后，整个团队聚在一起享受他们当之无愧的荣耀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Bask" sounds like "bath" + "ask" — imagine asking for a warm bath in the sun. You lie down, soak up the warmth, and relax. That is basking!

谐音记忆："bask"听起来像"bath"（沐浴）+ "ask"（请求）——想象你请求在阳光下洗个温暖的日光浴。你躺下来，吸收温暖，放松身心，这就是basking！
