# 😐 impassive

🔊 /ɪmˈpæsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not feeling or showing emotion; calm and unmoved in the face of provocation or distress
**中文：** adj. 冷漠的；无感觉的；无动于衷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stoic（坚忍的）
- indifferent（冷淡的）
- unemotional（不动感情的）
- apathetic（冷漠的）
- expressionless（面无表情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impassive expression / impassive face / remain impassive / impassive demeanor**

👉 Example: The judge remained impassive throughout the emotional testimony, revealing no hint of personal reaction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The negotiator maintained an **impassive** expression even as the opposing party launched a series of aggressive personal attacks.
   即使对方发起了一系列激烈的人身攻击，这位谈判者仍保持着冷漠的表情。

2. Despite the devastating news, she appeared remarkably **impassive**, betraying no visible signs of grief or emotional distress.
   尽管听到了毁灭性的消息，她看起来异常冷漠，没有表现出任何悲伤或情感痛苦的迹象。

3. The security guard stood **impassive** at the entrance, unmoved by the crowd's increasingly loud and desperate pleas for admission.
   保安冷漠地站在入口处，对人群越来越大声、越来越迫切的入场请求无动于衷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-passive" — "im" means not, and "passive" here relates to "passion," so impassive means without passion or emotion, completely unmoved.
把"impassive"想成"im-passive"——"im"表示"不"，这里的"passive"与"passion（激情）"相关，所以impassive就是"没有激情的、冷漠的"。
