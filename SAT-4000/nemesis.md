# ⚡ nemesis

🔊 /ˈnɛməsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A source of harm or ruin; an opponent that cannot be defeated; retributive justice or punishment
**中文：** n. 报应；给与惩罚的人；天罚；复仇女神

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- retribution（报应）
- adversary（对手）
- archenemy（大敌）
- downfall（毁灭）
- vengeance（复仇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **meet one's nemesis / prove to be someone's nemesis / ultimate nemesis**

👉 Example: Overconfidence proved to be his nemesis when the final examination revealed significant gaps in his preparation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corrupt official finally met his **nemesis** when investigative journalists exposed decades of financial misconduct.
   那位腐败官员最终遭到了报应，调查记者揭露了他数十年的财务不端行为。

2. Throughout literary history, every great hero has faced a formidable **nemesis** who challenges their moral convictions.
   纵观文学史，每位伟大的英雄都面对过一个强大的宿敌来考验他们的道德信念。

3. Climate change has become the **nemesis** of coastal communities, threatening their very existence with rising sea levels.
   气候变化已成为沿海社区的克星，海平面上升威胁着它们的存亡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Nemesis" as the Greek goddess of revenge — she ensures no wrongdoing goes unpunished, delivering divine retribution.
把"nemesis"联想到希腊复仇女神涅墨西斯——她确保没有恶行逃脱惩罚，降下天罚。
