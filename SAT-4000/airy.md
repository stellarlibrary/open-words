# 🌬️ airy

🔊 /ˈer.i/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Open to or having free circulation of air; light and breezy in manner; giving an impression of lightness.
**中文：** 空气的；通风的；轻快的；空中的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- breezy（通风的）
- ventilated（通风良好的）
- light（轻盈的）
- spacious（宽敞的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **airy room, airy atmosphere, airy manner, airy space**
👉 Example: The architect designed an airy atrium that flooded the entire building with natural light.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renovated gallery featured **airy** halls with large windows that allowed abundant sunlight to pour inside.
   翻新后的画廊拥有通风的大厅和大窗户，让充足的阳光倾泻进来。

2. She dismissed the serious concerns with an **airy** wave of her hand, refusing to acknowledge the problem.
   她轻飘飘地挥了挥手，驳回了那些严肃的担忧，拒绝承认问题的存在。

3. The mountain lodge offered an **airy** retreat where guests could enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.
   这座山间小屋提供了一个通风开阔的休憩之所，客人可以欣赏周围山谷的全景。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Airy" comes directly from "air" — picture a room full of fresh air flowing freely through open windows.
"Airy"直接来自"air"（空气）——想象一个新鲜空气通过敞开的窗户自由流通的房间。
