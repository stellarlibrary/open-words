# 📚 mythology

🔊 /mɪˈθɑːlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A collection of myths belonging to a particular culture or tradition; the study of myths
**中文：** n. 神话；神话学；神话集

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- folklore（民间传说）
- legend（传说）
- lore（知识，传说）
- tradition（传统）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Greek mythology / Norse mythology / comparative mythology / rich in mythology**

👉 Example: The professor has devoted her career to studying the comparative mythology of ancient Mediterranean civilizations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Greek **mythology** provides a rich tapestry of archetypal narratives that continue to influence Western literature and art.
   希腊**神话**提供了丰富的原型叙事，至今仍影响着西方文学和艺术。

2. The scholar argued that Norse **mythology** reflects the harsh environmental conditions endured by early Scandinavian societies.
   该学者认为北欧**神话**反映了早期斯堪的纳维亚社会所承受的恶劣环境条件。

3. Understanding a civilization's **mythology** offers profound insight into its collective values, fears, and aspirations.
   理解一个文明的**神话**能深刻洞察其集体价值观、恐惧和抱负。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mythology" as "myth + ology" — the "-ology" suffix means "the study of," so mythology is the study of myths.
把"mythology"想成"myth + ology"——后缀"-ology"意为"研究"，所以mythology就是对神话的研究。
