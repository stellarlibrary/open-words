# 🧬 transgenic animals

🔊 /trænzˈdʒɛnɪk ˈænɪməlz/<br>
🏷️ *noun phrase | 名词短语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Animals that have had foreign genes deliberately inserted into their genome through genetic engineering techniques.
**中文：** 转基因动物；通过基因工程技术将外源基因导入其基因组中的动物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genetically modified animals（基因改造动物）
- genetically engineered animals（基因工程动物）
- GM animals（转基因动物）
- bioengineered animals（生物工程动物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **produce transgenic animals / develop transgenic animals / study transgenic animals / use transgenic animals as models**

👉 Example: Scientists produce transgenic animals to study the function of specific human disease genes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers have developed **transgenic animals** that express human proteins, enabling more accurate pharmaceutical testing procedures.
   研究人员已开发出能表达人类蛋白质的转基因动物，从而实现更精确的药物测试程序。

2. The laboratory uses **transgenic animals** to investigate the genetic mechanisms underlying neurodegenerative diseases in human populations.
   该实验室利用转基因动物来研究人类群体中神经退行性疾病的遗传机制。

3. Ethical debates surrounding **transgenic animals** have intensified as biotechnology advances allow increasingly complex genetic modifications.
   随着生物技术进步允许越来越复杂的基因改造，围绕转基因动物的伦理争论日益加剧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-genic" as "transfer + gene" — genes are transferred across species into these animals.
把"transgenic"想成"transfer（转移）+ gene（基因）"——基因被跨物种转移到这些动物体内。
