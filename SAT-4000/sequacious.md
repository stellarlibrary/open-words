# 🐑 sequacious

🔊 /sɪˈkweɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking independence or originality of thought; inclined to follow others blindly; logically consistent
**中文：** adj. 盲从的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- subservient（顺从的）
- compliant（盲从的）
- obsequious（谄媚的）
- slavish（奴性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sequacious followers / sequacious obedience / sequacious mindset / sequacious adherence**

👉 Example: The professor criticized the sequacious students who accepted every theory without any critical examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial condemned the **sequacious** legislators who voted strictly along party lines without considering their constituents' needs.
   社论谴责了那些盲从的立法者，他们严格按照党派路线投票而不考虑选民的需求。

2. A truly innovative scholar must resist **sequacious** thinking and challenge established paradigms with independent intellectual inquiry.
   一位真正有创新精神的学者必须抵制盲从思维，以独立的智识探究挑战既有范式。

3. The cult's influence depended on maintaining a **sequacious** membership that would never question the pronouncements of its leader.
   该邪教的影响力依赖于维持一个盲从的成员群体，他们永远不会质疑领袖的声明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sequacious" as "sequel + acious" — always following like a sequel, never the original, blindly coming after what precedes.
把"sequacious"想成"sequel + acious"——总是像续集一样跟随，从来不是原创，盲目地跟在前面的事物后面。
