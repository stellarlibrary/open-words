# 😈 lecherous

🔊 /ˈlɛtʃərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing excessive or offensive sexual desire; lustful in a morally reprehensible way.
**中文：** adj. 好色的；淫荡的；色欲旺盛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lustful（好色的）
- lascivious（淫荡的）
- licentious（放荡的）
- salacious（色情的）
- lewd（下流的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lecherous behavior / lecherous gaze / lecherous intentions / lecherous old man**

👉 Example: The novel's antagonist was portrayed as a lecherous nobleman who exploited his position of power.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee condemned his **lecherous** conduct, which had created a hostile and intimidating environment for female colleagues.
   委员会谴责了他好色的行为，这给女同事们造成了充满敌意和恐吓的环境。

2. In medieval literature, the **lecherous** villain often served as a moral foil to the virtuous and noble protagonist.
   在中世纪文学中，好色的反派常常作为高尚主角的道德对照而存在。

3. The documentary exposed the **lecherous** practices of certain powerful figures who had long evaded public scrutiny.
   这部纪录片揭露了某些长期逃避公众审视的权贵人物的淫荡行径。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lecherous" as "letch-er-ous" — a "letch" is slang for a person with uncontrolled lustful desires, so lecherous describes that quality.
把"lecherous"想成"letch-er-ous"——"letch"是俚语，指欲望不受控制的好色之人，所以lecherous就是描述这种特质。
