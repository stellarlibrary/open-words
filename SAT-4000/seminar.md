# 🎓 seminar

🔊 /ˈsɛmɪnɑːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A discussion meeting or class for advanced study; a workshop or conference focused on a specific topic
**中文：** n. 研究班，研讨会

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- workshop（研讨会）
- symposium（座谈会）
- colloquium（学术讨论会）
- conference（会议）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **attend a seminar / seminar on [topic] / graduate seminar / lead a seminar**

👉 Example: The professor organized a seminar on contemporary approaches to environmental policy reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Graduate students are required to attend a weekly **seminar** in which they present and critique each other's research findings.
   研究生需要参加每周的研讨班，在会上展示和评论彼此的研究成果。

2. The department hosted an interdisciplinary **seminar** that brought together scholars from philosophy, linguistics, and cognitive science.
   该系举办了一场跨学科研讨会，汇集了哲学、语言学和认知科学领域的学者。

3. During the **seminar**, participants engaged in a rigorous debate about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence in modern society.
   在研讨会期间，与会者就人工智能在现代社会中的伦理影响展开了激烈辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "seminar" as related to "seminary" — both come from Latin "seminarium" (seed bed), a place where ideas are planted and cultivated through discussion.
把"seminar"想成和"seminary"相关——两者都源自拉丁语"seminarium"（苗圃），一个通过讨论播种和培养思想的地方。
