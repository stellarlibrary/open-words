# 🪨 agglomerate

🔊 /əˈɡlɒməreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective | 动词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To collect or gather into a mass or cluster. (n.) A mass or collection of things. (adj.) Gathered into a cluster or mass
**中文：** v. 使结块；使成团 n. 团块 adj. 凝聚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accumulate（积聚）
- cluster（聚集）
- conglomerate（聚合）
- amass（聚集）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **agglomerate into / agglomerate around / volcanic agglomerate / urban agglomerate**

👉 Example: Over centuries, volcanic debris agglomerated into massive rock formations along the coastal cliffs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Fine particles of dust and moisture gradually **agglomerated** into larger droplets within the controlled laboratory environment.
   细小的灰尘和水分颗粒在受控的实验室环境中逐渐凝结成更大的液滴。

2. The geologist studied the volcanic **agglomerate** carefully to determine the precise age and origin of the ancient eruption.
   地质学家仔细研究了火山团块，以确定古代火山喷发的确切年代和起源。

3. Small businesses tend to **agglomerate** in commercial districts where shared infrastructure reduces their overall operational costs significantly.
   小企业往往聚集在商业区，共享基础设施可以显著降低其整体运营成本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-glom-erate" — "glom" sounds like "clump," so agglomerate means to clump together into a mass.
想想"a-glom-erate"——"glom"听起来像"clump"（团块），所以agglomerate意为团结成块。
