# 💰 profiteer

🔊 /ˌprɒfɪˈtɪər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who makes an excessive or unfair profit, especially during times of crisis. (v.) To make an unreasonably large profit through exploitative means
**中文：** n. 奸商；牟取暴利的人 v. 赚大钱；牟取暴利

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exploiter（剥削者）
- racketeer（敲诈者）
- speculator（投机者）
- gouger（敲竹杠的人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **war profiteer / profiteer from / black-market profiteer / profiteering scheme**

👉 Example: During the crisis, unscrupulous merchants acted as profiteers by charging exorbitant prices for basic necessities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government imposed strict penalties on any **profiteer** caught hoarding essential medical supplies during the national health emergency.
   政府对在国家卫生紧急状态期间囤积基本医疗物资的奸商处以严厉惩罚。

2. Historians documented how certain industrialists chose to **profiteer** from wartime shortages rather than contribute to the relief effort.
   历史学家记录了某些实业家如何选择从战时短缺中牟取暴利，而非为救援工作做出贡献。

3. The investigative report exposed a network of **profiteers** who had been manipulating commodity prices for personal financial gain.
   调查报告揭露了一个为个人经济利益操纵商品价格的奸商网络。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "profit-eer" like "volunteer" but for profit — someone who eagerly volunteers themselves to grab unfair profits at others' expense.
把"profiteer"想成"profit（利润）+ eer（像volunteer中的人）"——一个积极主动以牺牲他人为代价攫取不当利润的人。
