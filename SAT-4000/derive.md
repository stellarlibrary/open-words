# 🌱 derive

🔊 /dɪˈraɪv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To obtain or receive from a source; to originate from; to deduce or infer
**中文：** v. 源于；得自；获得；导出；起源于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obtain（获得）
- originate（起源）
- deduce（推导）
- extract（提取）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **derive from / derive pleasure from / derive benefit from / derive a conclusion**

👉 Example: Many English words derive from Latin and ancient Greek roots.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher was able to **derive** a groundbreaking conclusion from the extensive dataset collected over several consecutive years.
   研究人员能够从连续几年收集的大量数据集中导出一个突破性的结论。

2. Much of the nation's economic prosperity **derives** from its abundant natural resources and its strategically advantageous geographic location.
   该国的大部分经济繁荣源于其丰富的自然资源和具有战略优势的地理位置。

3. Students who **derive** genuine satisfaction from intellectual inquiry tend to perform consistently well across all academic disciplines.
   从智力探究中获得真正满足感的学生往往在所有学科中都表现出色。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-rive(r)" — a river derives its flow from mountain springs; similarly, to derive means to draw something from a source.
把"derive"想成"de-river（河流）"——河流的水流源自山泉；同样，derive意味着从某个来源获取某物。
