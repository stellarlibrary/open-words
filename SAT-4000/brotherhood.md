# 🤝 brotherhood

🔊 /ˈbrʌðərhʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The relationship or bond between brothers; a feeling of fellowship and mutual support among a group of people; an association or community of people with common interests
**中文：** n. 兄弟关系；手足情谊；四海之内皆兄弟的信念；兄弟会

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fraternity（兄弟会）
- fellowship（情谊）
- camaraderie（同志情谊）
- solidarity（团结）
- kinship（亲属关系）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sense of brotherhood / brotherhood of man / sworn brotherhood / foster brotherhood**

👉 Example: The soldiers developed an unbreakable brotherhood forged through shared hardship and sacrifice on the battlefield.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The charitable organization was founded on the principle of universal **brotherhood** and the inherent dignity of every person.
   该慈善组织成立于普世兄弟情谊和每个人固有尊严的原则之上。

2. Years of training and competition together cultivated a profound sense of **brotherhood** among the Olympic team members.
   多年的共同训练和比赛在奥运队员之间培养了深厚的兄弟情谊。

3. The medieval **brotherhood** of scholars dedicated themselves to preserving ancient manuscripts and advancing scientific knowledge across Europe.
   中世纪的学者兄弟会致力于保存古代手稿和推动整个欧洲的科学知识发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brother + hood" — the "hood" (state or condition) of being brothers, representing mutual bonds and fellowship.
把"brotherhood"想成"brother（兄弟）+ hood（状态）"——处于兄弟般的状态，代表相互间的纽带和情谊。
