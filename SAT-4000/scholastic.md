# 🎓 scholastic

🔊 /skəˈlæstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to schools, education, or academic study; scholarly. (n.) A scholar; a student; a pedant; a scholastic philosopher
**中文：** adj. 学校的；学者的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- academic（学术的）
- educational（教育的）
- scholarly（学者的）
- intellectual（知识分子的）
- pedagogical（教学的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scholastic achievement / scholastic aptitude / scholastic performance / scholastic record / scholastic standards**

👉 Example: Her outstanding scholastic achievements earned her a full scholarship to a prestigious university.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The institution raised its **scholastic** standards significantly in an effort to attract more distinguished faculty and highly motivated students.
   该机构大幅提高了学术标准，以吸引更杰出的教师和更有上进心的学生。

2. Researchers examined the correlation between students' **scholastic** performance and their levels of physical activity throughout the academic year.
   研究人员考察了学生在整个学年中学业表现与体育活动水平之间的相关性。

3. The **scholastic** tradition of medieval Europe placed great emphasis on rigorous logical argumentation and systematic theological inquiry.
   中世纪欧洲的经院学术传统非常重视严谨的逻辑论证和系统的神学探究。

Think of "scholastic" as "school + astic" — anything scholastic is enthusiastically related to school and academic pursuits.
把"scholastic"想成"school + astic"——任何与scholastic相关的事物都热情地与学校和学术追求有关。
