# 💥 impulsive

🔊 /ɪmˈpʌlsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Acting or done without forethought; driven by sudden urges rather than careful consideration; prone to act on impulse
**中文：** adj. 冲动的；受感情驱使的；任性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rash（轻率的）
- spontaneous（自发的）
- hasty（仓促的）
- reckless（鲁莽的）
- impetuous（急躁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impulsive behavior / impulsive decision / impulsive buyer / impulsive nature / impulsive act**

👉 Example: His impulsive nature often led him to speak before fully considering the consequences of his words.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The psychologist's study revealed that **impulsive** spending habits are strongly correlated with heightened levels of emotional stress.
   心理学家的研究显示，冲动的消费习惯与情绪压力升高密切相关。

2. Critics argued that the president's **impulsive** response to the diplomatic crisis only served to escalate tensions between the two nations.
   批评者认为，总统对外交危机的冲动反应只会加剧两国之间的紧张关系。

3. Adolescents are generally more **impulsive** than adults because the prefrontal cortex has not yet fully developed at that stage.
   青少年通常比成年人更冲动，因为前额叶皮质在这一阶段尚未完全发育。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impulsive" as "impulse + -ive" — full of impulse, always acting on sudden urges without pausing to think.
把"impulsive"想成"impulse（冲动）+ -ive（充满……的）"——充满冲动的，总是不加思考就凭一时冲动行事。
