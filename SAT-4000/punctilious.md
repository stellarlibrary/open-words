# 🔍 punctilious

🔊 /pʌŋkˈtɪliəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing great attention to detail or correct behavior; extremely careful and precise about fine points
**中文：** adj. 一丝不苟的；谨小慎微的；拘泥细节的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- meticulous（一丝不苟的）
- scrupulous（严谨的）
- fastidious（挑剔的）
- painstaking（细心的）
- conscientious（尽职的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **punctilious attention / punctilious about details / punctilious observer / punctilious adherence**

👉 Example: The ambassador was punctilious about observing every protocol during the formal diplomatic reception.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **punctilious** editor reviewed every footnote and citation to ensure absolute accuracy before the manuscript went to press.
   这位一丝不苟的编辑在手稿付印前审查了每一个脚注和引用，以确保绝对准确。

2. Her **punctilious** adherence to laboratory safety protocols earned the respect of every researcher in the department.
   她对实验室安全规程的严格遵守赢得了部门每位研究人员的尊重。

3. The accountant was **punctilious** in verifying financial records, catching discrepancies that others had consistently overlooked for years.
   这位会计在核实财务记录方面一丝不苟，发现了其他人多年来一直忽视的差异。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "puncti-lious" — related to "punctual" and "point"; a punctilious person attends to every little point with precision.
把"punctilious"和"punctual（准时的）"及"point（要点）"联系起来——一个punctilious的人精确关注每一个细节要点。
