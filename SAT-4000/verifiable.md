# 🔍 verifiable

🔊 /ˌvɛrɪˈfaɪəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to be checked, confirmed, or substantiated as true or accurate
**中文：** adj. 可证实的；能作证的；可检验的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confirmable（可确认的）
- provable（可证明的）
- demonstrable（可论证的）
- substantiable（可证实的）
- testable（可测试的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verifiable evidence / verifiable facts / verifiable claim / verifiable data / independently verifiable**

👉 Example: The researcher insisted that every claim in the study must be supported by verifiable data.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientific hypotheses must generate **verifiable** predictions that can be tested through controlled experimentation and empirical observation.
   科学假设必须产生可通过对照实验和经验观察来检验的可证实的预测。

2. The auditors required **verifiable** documentation for every financial transaction recorded during the previous fiscal year.
   审计人员要求上一财年记录的每笔金融交易都有可核实的文件支持。

3. In the digital age, distinguishing **verifiable** information from misinformation has become an increasingly critical media literacy skill.
   在数字时代，区分可证实的信息和虚假信息已成为一项越来越关键的媒体素养技能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "verifiable" as "verify-able" — if you can verify it, then it is verifiable. Simple as splitting the word.
把"verifiable"想成"verify-able"——如果你能验证（verify）它，那它就是可验证的（verifiable）。拆词即可理解。
