# 🎭 verisimilar

🔊 /ˌvɛrɪˈsɪmɪlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having the appearance of being true or real; probable or likely
**中文：** adj. 好像是真的；好像真实的；貌似真实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plausible（似乎合理的）
- credible（可信的）
- probable（可能的）
- lifelike（逼真的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verisimilar account / verisimilar narrative / verisimilar portrayal / verisimilar detail**

👉 Example: The novelist crafted a verisimilar portrayal of wartime conditions that readers found deeply convincing.

---

1. The documentary relied on **verisimilar** reenactments to convey the emotional weight of historical events to modern audiences.
   这部纪录片依靠逼真的情景再现向现代观众传达历史事件的情感分量。

2. Critics praised the playwright for constructing a **verisimilar** dialogue that captured the authentic speech patterns of rural communities.
   评论家赞扬剧作家构建了逼真的对话，捕捉到了农村社区真实的语言模式。

3. A **verisimilar** hypothesis must be grounded in observable evidence rather than mere speculation or unfounded assumptions.
   一个貌似真实的假设必须建立在可观察的证据之上，而非仅凭猜测或毫无根据的假设。

把"verisimilar"想成"veri-similar"——某事与真相"非常相似"，所以看起来像真的，即使可能不是。
