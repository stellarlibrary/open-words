# 🏛️ attache

🔊 /ˌætəˈʃeɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person appointed to the staff of an ambassador, especially one with a specific area of responsibility
**中文：** n. 专员，使馆随员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diplomat（外交官）
- envoy（使节）
- emissary（特使）
- representative（代表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **military attaché / cultural attaché / press attaché / attaché case**

👉 Example: The military attaché was responsible for liaison between the two nations' armed forces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cultural **attaché** organized a series of exhibitions to promote artistic exchange between the two countries.
   文化专员组织了一系列展览，以促进两国之间的艺术交流。

2. As a senior **attaché** at the embassy, she handled sensitive negotiations with remarkable discretion and professionalism.
   作为大使馆的高级随员，她以非凡的审慎和专业精神处理敏感的谈判事务。

3. The press **attaché** issued an official statement clarifying the ambassador's position on the trade agreement.
   新闻专员发表了一份官方声明，澄清大使在贸易协定上的立场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "attaché" as someone "attached" to an embassy — they are staff members assigned to a diplomatic mission.
把"attaché"想成"attached"（附属的）——他们是被派驻到外交使团的工作人员。
