# 🎨 prolific

🔊 /prəˈlɪfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Producing much fruit, foliage, or many offspring; highly productive in creative work
**中文：** adj. 多产的；丰富的；富有创造力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- productive（多产的）
- fertile（富饶的）
- fruitful（丰硕的）
- abundant（丰富的）
- copious（大量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prolific writer / prolific author / prolific career / prolific output**

👉 Example: Shakespeare remains one of the most prolific playwrights in the history of English literature.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **prolific** novelist published over thirty critically acclaimed works during her remarkably productive literary career.
   这位多产的小说家在其非凡的文学生涯中出版了三十多部广受好评的作品。

2. Researchers discovered that the **prolific** growth of algae in the lake was directly linked to agricultural runoff.
   研究人员发现湖中藻类的大量繁殖与农业径流有直接关系。

3. As a **prolific** inventor, she held more than fifty patents spanning diverse fields of engineering and technology.
   作为一位多产的发明家，她拥有横跨工程和技术多个领域的五十多项专利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-LIFE-ic" — full of life and production, someone who creates abundantly as if overflowing with life force.
把"prolific"想成"pro-LIFE-ic"——充满生命力和产出，像是生命力溢出般地大量创作。
