# 🔵 circumference

🔊 /sərˈkʌmfərəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The distance around the outer edge of a circle or circular object; the perimeter of a circle.
**中文：** 圆周；周长；胸围；周围。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perimeter（周长）
- boundary（边界）
- periphery（外围）
- girth（周长）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the circumference of / measure the circumference / in circumference**

👉 Example: The circumference of the Earth at the equator is approximately 40,075 kilometers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers carefully calculated the **circumference** of the cylindrical tower before ordering the custom steel cladding panels.
   工程师们在订购定制钢板之前仔细计算了圆柱形塔楼的周长。

2. The ancient mathematician Archimedes devised an ingenious method to approximate the **circumference** of a circle using polygons.
   古代数学家阿基米德设计了一种巧妙的方法，用多边形来近似计算圆的周长。

3. Astronomers determined that the **circumference** of the newly discovered planet exceeded initial theoretical predictions significantly.
   天文学家确定这颗新发现的行星的周长大大超过了最初的理论预测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circum-" (around) + "ference" (carry) — it literally means "carrying around," referring to the distance carried around a circle.
把"circumference"拆分为"circum-（周围）"+"ference（携带）"——字面意思是"绕着带一圈"，指绕圆一周的距离。
