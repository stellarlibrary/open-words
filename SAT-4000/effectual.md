# 🎯 effectual

🔊 /ɪˈfɛktʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Producing or capable of producing the desired effect; having legal force or validity
**中文：** adj. 奏效的；有效的；有法律效力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- effective（有效的）
- efficacious（灵验的）
- productive（有成效的）
- potent（有力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effectual measures / effectual remedy / effectual leadership / effectual response**

👉 Example: The new regulations proved effectual in reducing industrial pollution along the river.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomatic negotiations were surprisingly **effectual**, resulting in a lasting ceasefire agreement between the two warring nations.
   外交谈判出人意料地奏效，促成了两个交战国之间的持久停火协议。

2. Without **effectual** enforcement mechanisms, even the most well-intentioned environmental policies will inevitably fail to achieve their goals.
   如果没有有效的执行机制，即使是最善意的环境政策也不可避免地无法实现其目标。

3. The committee proposed several **effectual** strategies to address the growing disparities in educational access across rural communities.
   委员会提出了几项有效的策略来解决农村社区之间日益扩大的教育机会差距。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "effect-ual" as "having a real effect" — if something is effectual, it actually produces the effect you want.
把"effectual"想成"有真正效果的"——如果某事是effectual的，它确实能产生你想要的效果。
