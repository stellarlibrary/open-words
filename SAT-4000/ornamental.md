# 🏺 ornamental

🔊 /ˌɔːrnəˈmɛntəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Serving or intended as an ornament; decorative rather than functional. (n.) A plant cultivated for its beauty; a decorative object
**中文：** adj. 装饰的，装饰性的 n. 观赏植物；装饰品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decorative（装饰性的）
- embellishing（点缀的）
- aesthetic（美学的）
- fancy（精美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ornamental garden / ornamental design / ornamental plant / purely ornamental / ornamental feature**

👉 Example: The ornamental ironwork on the balcony added an elegant touch to the historic building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The landscape designer selected several **ornamental** shrubs to enhance the visual appeal of the corporate headquarters entrance.
   景观设计师选择了几种观赏灌木来增强公司总部入口的视觉吸引力。

2. Critics argued that the elaborate **ornamental** details on the facade were purely aesthetic and served no structural purpose whatsoever.
   评论家认为外立面上精致的装饰细节纯粹是美学性的，完全没有任何结构用途。

3. The museum displayed a collection of **ornamental** artifacts from the medieval period, revealing the artisans' remarkable craftsmanship and skill.
   博物馆展出了一批中世纪时期的装饰工艺品，展现了工匠们非凡的手艺和技能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ornamental" as "ornament-al" — something related to an "ornament," a decorative item you hang on a tree or place on a shelf for beauty.
把"ornamental"想成"ornament-al"——与"ornament"（装饰品）有关，就是你挂在树上或放在架子上用来美化的装饰物。
