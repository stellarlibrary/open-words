# 🌿 vegetal

🔊 /ˈvɛdʒɪtəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or characteristic of plants; resembling or having the nature of plant life
**中文：** adj. 植物的；植物性的；植物般的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- botanical（植物学的）
- herbaceous（草本的）
- vegetative（植物生长的）
- organic（有机的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vegetal growth / vegetal pole / vegetal flavor / vegetal aroma**

👉 Example: The wine critic noted the distinctive vegetal notes of bell pepper in the young Cabernet Sauvignon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biologist examined the **vegetal** pole of the embryo to understand how cellular differentiation proceeds during early developmental stages.
   生物学家检查了胚胎的植物极，以了解早期发育阶段细胞分化是如何进行的。

2. The sommelier described the wine as having a pronounced **vegetal** character, with undertones of asparagus and freshly cut grass.
   侍酒师形容这款葡萄酒具有明显的植物性特征，带有芦笋和新割青草的底韵。

3. Ancient civilizations relied heavily on **vegetal** materials such as fibers, resins, and dyes for their daily manufacturing needs.
   古代文明在日常生产中大量依赖植物性材料，如纤维、树脂和染料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vegetal" as "veget-al" — the adjective form that means "pertaining to vegetation," just like "mental" pertains to the mind.
把"vegetal"想成"veget-al"——表示"与植被有关的"形容词形式，就像"mental"表示与心智有关一样。
