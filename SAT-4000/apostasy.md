# ⚔️ apostasy

🔊 /əˈpɑː.stə.si/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The abandonment or renunciation of a religious or political belief, principle, or cause; the act of defecting from a previously held allegiance.

**中文：** 变节；脱党；背教

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- defection（叛变）
- renunciation（放弃）
- desertion（背弃）
- betrayal（背叛）
- disavowal（否认）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **commit apostasy**, **accused of apostasy**, **act of apostasy**, **political/religious apostasy**, **apostasy from** (a belief or group).

> In certain theocratic states, **apostasy** from the official religion carries severe legal penalties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The theologian's **apostasy** from orthodox doctrine provoked fierce condemnation from the ecclesiastical authorities.
   这位神学家对正统教义的背离引发了教会当局的强烈谴责。

2. His **apostasy** from the ruling political party was viewed as an act of extraordinary courage by reformists.
   他脱离执政党的行为被改革派视为非凡勇气之举。

3. Throughout history, **apostasy** has been punished with penalties ranging from social ostracism to capital punishment.
   纵观历史，背教行为所受的惩罚从社会排斥到死刑不等。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apo-" (away from) + "-stasy" (standing, from Greek *stasis*). **Apostasy** means to **stand away from** what you once believed — to abandon your former position.

词根记忆：apo-（远离）+ -stasy（站立，源自希腊语 stasis）。apostasy 字面意思是"从原来的立场上走开"，即变节、背教。想象一个人从队伍中站出来（stand away），背离了原来的信仰。
