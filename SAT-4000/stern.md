# 😠 stern

🔊 /stɜːrn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The rear end of a ship. (adj.) Severe; strict; unyielding
**中文：** adj. 严厉的，苛刻的；严峻的，难对付的 n. 船尾，舟尾

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strict（严格的）
- austere（严厉的）
- severe（严峻的）
- uncompromising（不妥协的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stern warning / stern expression / stern discipline / stern measures**

👉 Example: The judge issued a stern warning to the defendant about the consequences of further misconduct.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The headmaster's **stern** demeanor commanded immediate respect and obedience from every student in the assembly hall.
   校长严厉的态度让礼堂里的每一个学生都立即产生了敬畏和服从。

2. Maritime engineers reinforced the **stern** of the vessel to withstand the extreme pressures encountered in arctic waters.
   海事工程师加固了船尾，以承受北极水域中遇到的极端压力。

3. The committee delivered a **stern** rebuke to officials who had neglected their regulatory obligations for several years.
   委员会对多年来疏忽监管职责的官员进行了严厉的斥责。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stern" as the back of a ship where the captain stands with a stern, serious face giving orders.
把"stern"想象成船尾，船长站在那里，板着严厉的面孔发号施令。
