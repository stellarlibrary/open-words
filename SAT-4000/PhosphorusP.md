# 💡 PhosphorusP

🔊 /ˈfɒsfərəs piː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Phosphorus (symbol P), a nonmetallic chemical element with atomic number 15; essential to all living organisms and widely used in industry and agriculture
**中文：** n. 磷（元素符号P）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- Phosphorus（磷）
- element 15（第15号元素）
- P element（磷元素）
- phosphoric element（含磷元素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **P concentration / P deficiency / total P / P cycling in ecosystems**

👉 Example: Soil tests revealed dangerously low levels of P, prompting farmers to apply phosphate-based fertilizers immediately.
