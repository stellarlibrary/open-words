# 🌟 renown

🔊 /rɪˈnaʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being widely known and highly acclaimed; fame and distinction earned through achievement
**中文：** n. 名望，声誉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fame（名声）
- prestige（威望）
- distinction（卓越）
- eminence（显赫）
- celebrity（名望）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **of great renown / international renown / achieve renown / a person of renown**

👉 Example: The surgeon gained international renown for her pioneering work in minimally invasive cardiac procedures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's department of physics has earned considerable **renown** for its groundbreaking research in quantum computing.
   该大学物理系因其在量子计算领域的开创性研究而享有盛誉。

2. Artists of great **renown** from around the world gathered at the prestigious exhibition to showcase their latest works.
   来自世界各地享有盛誉的艺术家齐聚这场著名展览，展示他们的最新作品。

3. The ancient city achieved lasting **renown** as a center of learning, commerce, and cultural exchange across civilizations.
   这座古城作为学术、商业和跨文明文化交流中心而享有持久的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-NOWN" — when people "know" you again and again, you have renown; your name is widely known.
把"renown"想成"re-KNOWN"——当人们一次又一次地认识你，你就有了名望；你的名字广为人知。
