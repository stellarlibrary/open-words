# 💓 vital

🔊 /ˈvaɪtl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Absolutely necessary or essential; relating to life; full of energy and vigor
**中文：** adj. 至关重要的；生死攸关的；有活力的；生命的，生机的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- essential（必不可少的）
- crucial（关键的）
- indispensable（不可或缺的）
- critical（至关重要的）
- vibrant（充满活力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vital role / vital importance / vital signs / vital information / vital organ**

👉 Example: Access to clean water is vital for the survival of communities in arid regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The preservation of biodiversity is **vital** to maintaining the ecological balance upon which all living organisms ultimately depend.
   保护生物多样性对于维持所有生物最终赖以生存的生态平衡至关重要。

2. The detective uncovered a **vital** piece of evidence that completely altered the direction of the criminal investigation.
   侦探发现了一条至关重要的证据，彻底改变了刑事调查的方向。

3. Despite her advanced age, the renowned professor remained remarkably **vital** and continued to inspire generations of new students.
   尽管年事已高，这位著名教授仍然精力充沛，继续激励着一代又一代的新学生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vital" as sharing the root "vit-" (life) with "vitamin" — something vital is as necessary to success as vitamins are to life.
把"vital"想成与"vitamin"（维生素）共享词根"vit-"（生命）——某事vital就如同维生素对生命那样不可或缺。
