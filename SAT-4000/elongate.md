# 📏 elongate

🔊 /ɪˈlɔːŋɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make or become longer; to stretch out or extend in length
**中文：** v. 拉长；使延长；延伸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stretch（拉伸）
- extend（延伸）
- lengthen（加长）
- prolong（延长）
- draw out（拉长）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elongate the shape / elongate the fibers / elongate over time / elongate the body / elongate the process**

👉 Example: Yoga practitioners learn to elongate their spine through a series of controlled stretching exercises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist chose to **elongate** the figures in her paintings to create a distinctive and ethereal visual aesthetic.
   这位艺术家选择拉长她画作中的人物，以创造一种独特而空灵的视觉美感。

2. Intense gravitational forces near a black hole can **elongate** objects through a phenomenon scientists informally call spaghettification.
   黑洞附近的强大引力可以拉长物体，科学家非正式地称这种现象为"面条化"。

3. The manufacturer developed a new technique to **elongate** synthetic fibers without compromising their structural integrity or tensile strength.
   制造商开发了一种新技术来拉长合成纤维，同时不损害其结构完整性或抗拉强度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elongate" as "e-long-ate" — the word literally contains "long," reminding you that to elongate means to make something longer.
把"elongate"想成"e-long-ate"——这个词本身就包含"long（长）"，提醒你elongate意味着把东西拉长。
