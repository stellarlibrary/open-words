# 🤝 accede

🔊 /əkˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To agree to a demand, request, or treaty; to assume an office or position of authority.
**中文：** 同意；加入（组织或条约）；就任；即位。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consent（同意）
- agree（同意）
- assent（赞成）
- comply（遵从）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accede to a request**, **accede to the throne**, **accede to a treaty**

👉 Example: The government finally acceded to the demands of the protesters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After prolonged negotiations, the board reluctantly **acceded** to the union's demands for higher wages.
   经过长时间的谈判，董事会不情愿地同意了工会提高工资的要求。

2. The young prince **acceded** to the throne following his father's unexpected abdication.
   年轻的王子在其父亲意外退位后继承了王位。

3. Several developing nations **acceded** to the international trade agreement last year.
   去年有几个发展中国家加入了该国际贸易协定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accede" sounds like "exceed" — when you accede, you go beyond resistance and say "yes."
"Accede" 听起来像 "exceed"（超越）——当你 accede 时，你超越了抵抗，表示同意。
