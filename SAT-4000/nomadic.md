# 🐫 nomadic

🔊 /noʊˈmædɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or characteristic of nomads; involving moving from place to place rather than settling permanently in one location
**中文：** adj. 游牧的；流浪的；游动的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- itinerant（巡回的）
- migratory（迁徙的）
- wandering（漫游的）
- roving（流浪的）
- peripatetic（巡游的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nomadic lifestyle / nomadic tribes / nomadic herders / nomadic existence**

👉 Example: The nomadic peoples of Mongolia have maintained their traditional way of life for millennia.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many ancient **nomadic** civilizations followed seasonal migration patterns dictated by the availability of grazing land and water.
   许多古代游牧文明遵循由牧场和水源决定的季节性迁徙模式。

2. The anthropologist spent three years studying the **nomadic** communities of the Sahara and documenting their cultural traditions.
   这位人类学家花了三年时间研究撒哈拉的游牧社区并记录他们的文化传统。

3. Rapid urbanization has increasingly threatened the **nomadic** way of life that once defined vast regions of the continent.
   快速城市化日益威胁着曾经定义这片大陆广大地区的游牧生活方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nomad-ic" as "nomad + ic" — simply the adjective form of nomad, describing anything related to the wandering lifestyle.
把"nomadic"想成"nomad（游牧民）+ ic（形容词后缀）"——就是描述与游牧生活相关的形容词。
