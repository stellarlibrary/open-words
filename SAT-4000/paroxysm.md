# 💥 paroxysm

🔊 /ˈpærəkˌsɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sudden, violent outburst of emotion or activity; a periodic recurrence or intensification of a disease
**中文：** n. （疾病周期性）发作；（情绪的）突发；爆发

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outburst（爆发）
- spasm（痉挛）
- convulsion（抽搐）
- eruption（爆发）
- fit（发作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a paroxysm of rage / paroxysm of coughing / paroxysm of laughter / paroxysm of grief**

👉 Example: The unexpected news sent her into a paroxysm of uncontrollable laughter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient experienced a sudden **paroxysm** of coughing that persisted for several minutes before the medication took effect.
   病人突然经历了一阵剧烈咳嗽，持续了几分钟药物才起效。

2. Upon hearing the unjust verdict, the defendant was seized by a **paroxysm** of rage that startled everyone present in the courtroom.
   听到不公正的判决后，被告突然爆发出一阵愤怒，惊吓了法庭上所有在场的人。

3. The political scandal triggered a **paroxysm** of public outrage, prompting thousands of citizens to demand immediate governmental reform.
   这场政治丑闻引发了一阵公众愤怒的爆发，促使数千名公民要求立即进行政府改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "par-OX-ysm" — imagine an ox suddenly going into a wild, uncontrollable spasm of rage.
把"paroxysm"想成"par + OX（公牛）+ ysm"——想象一头公牛突然陷入一阵狂暴的、不可控的痉挛。
