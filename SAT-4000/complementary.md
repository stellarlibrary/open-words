# 🔄 complementary

🔊 /ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Serving to complete or enhance something; combining in such a way as to form a whole or bring out the best qualities of each element
**中文：** adj. 补足的；补充的；互补的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supplementary（补充的）
- reciprocal（互补的）
- corresponding（对应的）
- completing（完善的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complementary colors / complementary skills / complementary roles / complementary medicine**

👉 Example: Their complementary expertise in finance and technology made them ideal business partners.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two researchers brought **complementary** skills to the project, with one excelling in data analysis and the other in fieldwork.
   两位研究人员为项目带来了互补的技能，一位擅长数据分析，另一位擅长实地调查。

2. In color theory, **complementary** colors are positioned opposite each other on the wheel and produce striking visual contrast together.
   在色彩理论中，互补色在色轮上位于彼此对面，搭配在一起会产生醒目的视觉对比。

3. The hospital integrated **complementary** therapies such as acupuncture alongside conventional treatments to improve patient recovery outcomes.
   医院将针灸等补充疗法与常规治疗相结合，以改善患者的康复效果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "complementary" as "complete-mentary" — two things that are complementary complete each other, like two puzzle pieces fitting together.
把"complementary"想成"complete-mentary"——两个互补的事物相互完善，就像两块拼图完美契合。
