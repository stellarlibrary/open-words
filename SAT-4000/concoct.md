# 🧪 concoct

🔊 /kənˈkɑːkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make by mixing various ingredients; to devise or fabricate a story, plan, or excuse
**中文：** v. 调制；混合而制；捏造；编造

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fabricate（捏造）
- devise（设计）
- brew（调制）
- invent（编造）
- formulate（配制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concoct a story / concoct a plan / concoct an excuse / concoct a mixture**

👉 Example: The journalist accused the politician of attempting to concoct a false narrative to mislead the public.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant was found guilty of attempting to **concoct** an elaborate alibi to conceal his involvement in the crime.
   被告被判犯有试图编造精心设计的不在场证明以掩盖其参与犯罪的罪行。

2. The chemist managed to **concoct** a potent remedy by combining several rare herbal extracts in precise proportions.
   这位化学家通过按精确比例混合几种稀有草药提取物，成功调制出一种强效药物。

3. Students were asked to **concoct** an original hypothesis and design a rigorous experiment to test its validity.
   学生们被要求构想一个原创假设，并设计一个严格的实验来验证其有效性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con + coct (cook)" — to concoct is to "cook up" something, whether a potion in a lab or a story in your mind.
把"concoct"想成"con + coct（烹饪）"——concoct就是"炮制"某物，无论是实验室里的药剂还是脑海中的故事。
