# ⚖️ inequality

🔊 /ˌɪnɪˈkwɑːləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being unequal in status, rights, or opportunities; a mathematical expression showing that two quantities are not equal
**中文：** n. 不平等；不同；不平均；不等式

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disparity（差距）
- imbalance（不平衡）
- inequity（不公平）
- discrepancy（差异）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social inequality / economic inequality / gender inequality / address inequality / reduce inequality**

👉 Example: The report highlighted growing economic inequality between urban and rural communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Persistent **inequality** in access to quality education continues to undermine social mobility in many developing nations.
   在许多发展中国家，获得优质教育机会的持续**不平等**继续削弱着社会流动性。

2. The economist presented compelling data illustrating the widening income **inequality** across all demographic groups.
   这位经济学家展示了令人信服的数据，说明所有人口群体之间收入**不平等**的不断扩大。

3. Scholars argue that systemic **inequality** is deeply rooted in historical structures of power and privilege.
   学者们认为，系统性的**不平等**深深植根于历史上的权力和特权结构。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-equal-ity" as "not equal" — the prefix "in-" negates "equality," giving you a state where things are not equal.
把"inequality"拆成"in-equal-ity"，即"不平等"——前缀"in-"否定了"equality（平等）"，表示事物不平等的状态。
