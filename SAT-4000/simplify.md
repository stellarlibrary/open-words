# 🧹 simplify

🔊 /ˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make something simpler or easier to understand; to reduce in complexity
**中文：** v. 单一化，简单化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- streamline（精简）
- clarify（阐明）
- reduce（减少）
- abridge（缩减）
- uncomplicate（简化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **simplify the process / simplify the equation / simplify matters / oversimplify / simplify regulations**

👉 Example: The new software was designed to simplify complex data analysis procedures for non-technical users.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government introduced reforms to **simplify** the tax filing process and reduce the administrative burden on citizens.
   政府推行改革以简化报税流程，减轻公民的行政负担。

2. The professor managed to **simplify** the advanced mathematical theorem so that undergraduate students could comprehend it.
   教授设法将高深的数学定理简化，使本科生也能理解。

3. Engineers worked diligently to **simplify** the device's interface without sacrificing any of its essential functionality.
   工程师们努力简化设备界面，同时不牺牲任何基本功能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "simplify" as "simple + fy (make)" — to simplify literally means to make something simple, stripping away unnecessary complexity.
把"simplify"想成"simple（简单的）+ fy（使…）"——simplify字面意思就是使事物变简单，去除不必要的复杂性。
