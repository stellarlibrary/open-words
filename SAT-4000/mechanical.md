# ⚙️ mechanical

🔊 /məˈkænɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or operated by machines or machinery; done as if by machine, lacking thought or spontaneity; relating to the science of mechanics
**中文：** adj. 机械的；机械制的；机械似的；呆板的；无意识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- automated（自动化的）
- robotic（机械般的）
- routine（例行的）
- automatic（自动的）
- perfunctory（敷衍的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mechanical device / mechanical failure / mechanical response / mechanical engineering**

👉 Example: His mechanical recitation of the poem lacked the emotional depth necessary to move the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The factory replaced manual labor with **mechanical** processes to increase production efficiency and reduce operational costs.
   工厂用机械化流程取代了人工劳动，以提高生产效率并降低运营成本。

2. Her **mechanical** repetition of the memorized answers revealed that she had not truly understood the underlying concepts.
   她机械地重复背诵的答案表明她并未真正理解其中的基本概念。

3. The investigators determined that **mechanical** failure in the engine's cooling system was the primary cause of the incident.
   调查人员确定发动机冷却系统的机械故障是该事故的主要原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mechanic-al" — like a mechanic who works on machines, "mechanical" describes anything that operates like a machine, including people who act without thinking.
把"mechanical"想成"mechanic-al"——就像修理机器的机械师，"mechanical"描述任何像机器一样运作的事物，包括不经思考行事的人。
