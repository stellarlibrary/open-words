# 🧭 inclination

🔊 /ˌɪnklɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A natural tendency or disposition to act or feel in a particular way; a slope or slant.
**中文：** n. 倾向，爱好；斜坡；倾斜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tendency（倾向）
- propensity（偏好）
- predisposition（倾向）
- preference（偏爱）
- leaning（倾向）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inclination to / inclination toward / natural inclination / have no inclination**

👉 Example: She showed a strong inclination toward the sciences from a very early age.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. His natural **inclination** toward philosophical inquiry led him to pursue a doctoral degree in ethics and political theory.
   他对哲学探究的天然倾向促使他攻读伦理学和政治理论的博士学位。

2. The committee noted a general **inclination** among faculty members to favor more rigorous standards for graduate admissions.
   委员会注意到教师们普遍倾向于支持更严格的研究生入学标准。

3. Despite her **inclination** to accept the prestigious offer, she ultimately chose the position that aligned with her values.
   尽管她倾向于接受这份有声望的邀请，但她最终选择了与自己价值观一致的职位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CLINE-ation" — "cline" means slope. Just as a hill inclines in one direction, your inclination is the direction your mind naturally leans.
把"inclination"想成"in-CLINE-ation"——"cline"意为坡度。就像山丘朝一个方向倾斜，你的inclination就是你的思想自然倾向的方向。
