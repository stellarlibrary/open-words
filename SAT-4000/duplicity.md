# 🎭 duplicity

🔊 /djuːˈplɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Deliberate deceptiveness in behavior or speech; the state of being deceitful or two-faced
**中文：** n. 口是心非；表里不一；奸诈；狡猾

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deceit（欺骗）
- treachery（背叛）
- deception（欺诈）
- guile（狡诈）
- dishonesty（不诚实）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political duplicity / accused of duplicity / duplicity of character / engage in duplicity**

👉 Example: The diplomat was accused of duplicity after secretly negotiating with both sides of the conflict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed the executive's **duplicity**, as he had been secretly funneling corporate funds into his personal offshore accounts.
   调查揭露了这位高管的欺诈行为，他一直在秘密地将公司资金转入其个人离岸账户。

2. Her apparent **duplicity** shocked longtime allies who had always trusted her public declarations of loyalty and unwavering commitment.
   她明显的口是心非震惊了长期以来一直信任她公开表示忠诚和坚定承诺的盟友们。

3. The novel explores themes of **duplicity** and moral ambiguity through a protagonist who leads a carefully constructed double life.
   这部小说通过一个过着精心构建的双重生活的主人公，探讨了口是心非和道德模糊的主题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "duplicity" as "duplic-ity" — related to "duplicate," meaning double. A person with duplicity has a "double" nature: one face shown publicly, another hidden beneath.
把"duplicity"想成"duplic-ity"——与"duplicate"（复制）有关，意为双重。有duplicity的人具有"双重"性格：一张面孔公开展示，另一张隐藏在下面。
