# 👊 reprehend

🔊 /ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To express sharp disapproval of; to rebuke or censure formally
**中文：** v. 申斥；指责；责备

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- censure（谴责）
- rebuke（斥责）
- reproach（责备）
- admonish（训诫）
- condemn（谴责）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reprehend someone for / reprehend misconduct / reprehend behavior / reprehend actions**

👉 Example: The committee convened to reprehend the official for his repeated violations of ethical standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial board chose to **reprehend** the politician publicly for making misleading statements during the campaign.
   编辑委员会选择公开斥责该政客在竞选期间发表误导性言论。

2. It is the duty of the judiciary to **reprehend** those whose actions undermine the fundamental principles of justice.
   司法机关有责任谴责那些破坏基本正义原则的行为者。

3. The professor did not hesitate to **reprehend** students who plagiarized material from published academic sources without attribution.
   教授毫不犹豫地斥责了那些未经注明便抄袭已发表学术资料的学生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "repre-hend" as "repre" (back) + "hend" (seize, like apprehend) — to seize someone back and scold them for wrongdoing.
把"reprehend"想成"repre"（回来）+"hend"（抓住）——把某人抓回来，斥责其不当行为。
