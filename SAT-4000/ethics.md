# 📜 ethics

🔊 /ˈɛθɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The branch of philosophy dealing with moral principles; a set of moral principles governing conduct
**中文：** n. 伦理，伦理学；道德标准

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- morality（道德）
- principles（原则）
- values（价值观）
- morals（道德准则）
- standards（标准）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **code of ethics / professional ethics / medical ethics / work ethics / ethics committee**

👉 Example: The university established a rigorous code of ethics to govern all faculty research activities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The course on **ethics** challenged students to examine the moral foundations underlying contemporary social and political institutions.
   这门伦理学课程促使学生审视当代社会和政治制度背后的道德基础。

2. Advances in artificial intelligence have raised unprecedented questions about **ethics** that existing legal frameworks are ill-equipped to address.
   人工智能的进步引发了前所未有的伦理问题，而现有的法律框架难以应对。

3. The journalist's strong professional **ethics** prevented her from publishing unverified claims despite intense pressure from her editors.
   这位记者坚定的职业道德使她在编辑的强大压力下仍拒绝发布未经核实的消息。
