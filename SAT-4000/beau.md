# 🎩 beau

🔊 /boʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A boyfriend or male admirer; a man who is excessively attentive to his appearance. (adj.) Fine; handsome
**中文：** n. 花花公子；喜修饰者；情郎 adj. 美的；好的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- suitor（求婚者）
- admirer（爱慕者）
- dandy（花花公子）
- sweetheart（恋人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **her beau / a dashing beau / beau monde (fashionable society)**

👉 Example: She introduced her new beau to the family at the annual holiday gathering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young woman's **beau** arrived at the doorstep carrying an elaborate bouquet of roses and a handwritten letter.
   这位年轻女子的情郎带着一束精美的玫瑰花和一封手写信来到门前。

2. In the nineteenth century, a well-dressed **beau** was expected to master the art of elegant conversation and social etiquette.
   在十九世纪，一位衣着讲究的花花公子被期望精通优雅的交谈艺术和社交礼仪。

3. The novel's protagonist was a charming **beau** who captivated every person he encountered at fashionable soirees.
   这部小说的主人公是一位迷人的花花公子，他在每场时髦的晚会上都令所有人着迷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "beau" sounding like "bow" — a gentleman bows to his lady because he is her beau, her admirer.
"beau"听起来像"bow（鞠躬）"——绅士向他的女士鞠躬，因为他是她的情郎和爱慕者。
