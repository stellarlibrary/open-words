# 🌾 agrarian

🔊 /əˈɡrer.i.ən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to cultivated land or the cultivation of land; pertaining to farming and agriculture.
**中文：** 土地的；耕地的；有关土地的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agricultural（农业的）
- pastoral（田园的）
- rural（乡村的）
- farming（耕作的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **agrarian reform, agrarian society, agrarian economy, agrarian land**
👉 Example: The government introduced agrarian reforms to redistribute land among rural communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation's transition from an **agrarian** economy to an industrial one transformed its social structure entirely.
   这个国家从农业经济向工业经济的转型彻底改变了其社会结构。

2. Many historians argue that **agrarian** reform was the most significant policy change of the twentieth century.
   许多历史学家认为土地改革是二十世纪最重要的政策变革。

3. The **agrarian** communities along the river valley depended heavily on seasonal floods for their annual harvests.
   河谷沿岸的农业社区严重依赖季节性洪水来保证年度收成。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Agrarian" starts with "agr-" like "agriculture" — both relate to land and farming.
"Agrarian"以"agr-"开头，就像"agriculture"（农业）——两者都与土地和耕作有关。
