# 🔧 solder

🔊 /ˈsɑːdər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To join or repair metal parts using a fusible alloy. (n.) A fusible metal alloy used for joining
**中文：** v. 焊接 n. 焊料

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- weld（焊接）
- fuse（熔合）
- braze（钎焊）
- bond（粘合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solder a joint / solder wires / solder connections / lead-free solder**

👉 Example: The technician carefully soldered the delicate wires onto the circuit board to complete the repair.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer instructed the apprentice to **solder** each connection precisely to ensure the circuit functioned without interruption.
   工程师指导学徒精确地焊接每个连接，以确保电路不间断地运行。

2. Modern electronics manufacturers prefer lead-free **solder** to comply with international environmental safety regulations and standards.
   现代电子产品制造商更喜欢使用无铅焊料，以符合国际环境安全法规和标准。

3. The artisan used silver **solder** to join the intricate metal components of the handcrafted jewelry piece together.
   工匠使用银焊料将手工珠宝的精细金属部件连接在一起。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solder" as "sold-er" — a substance that is "sold" to hold metal pieces together by melting and bonding them.
把"solder"想成"sold-er"——一种被"出售"（sold）来通过熔化和粘合将金属件固定在一起的物质。
