# 🧪 butane

🔊 /ˈbjuːteɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, flammable gaseous hydrocarbon (C₄H₁₀) obtained from petroleum, used as a fuel and in chemical synthesis
**中文：** n. 丁烷（一种无色可燃气态烃）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hydrocarbon（碳氢化合物）
- propellant（推进剂）
- alkane（烷烃）
- fuel gas（燃气）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **butane gas / butane lighter / butane torch / liquid butane**

👉 Example: The portable stove operates on butane cartridges, making it convenient for outdoor cooking.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemistry professor explained that **butane** is a four-carbon alkane commonly used as a fuel in portable lighters.
   化学教授解释说，丁烷是一种四碳烷烃，通常用作便携式打火机的燃料。

2. Industrial facilities must implement strict safety protocols when storing large quantities of **butane** due to its high flammability.
   工业设施在储存大量丁烷时必须实施严格的安全规程，因为它具有高度可燃性。

3. Researchers developed a more efficient method for converting **butane** into valuable chemical intermediates used in pharmaceutical manufacturing.
   研究人员开发了一种更高效的方法，将丁烷转化为药物生产中使用的有价值的化学中间体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "but-ane" — "but" sounds like "boot," and butane has four carbons (like four letters in "boot"). Remember: butane = 4-carbon gas.
想到"but-ane"——"but"有四个碳原子。记住：butane = 四碳气体，"丁"在中文里也表示第四位。
