# 💀 cadaver

🔊 /kəˈdævər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A dead body, especially one intended for medical dissection or scientific study
**中文：** n. 死尸；尸体（尤指供医学解剖用的）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corpse（尸体）
- remains（遗体）
- carcass（尸骸）
- body（遗体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cadaver dissection / cadaver lab / donate as a cadaver / examine a cadaver**

👉 Example: First-year medical students spend considerable time in the cadaver lab studying human anatomy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forensic pathologist carefully examined the **cadaver** to determine the precise cause and estimated time of death.
   法医病理学家仔细检查了尸体，以确定确切的死因和估计的死亡时间。

2. Medical students in the anatomy course performed their first **cadaver** dissection under the close supervision of experienced professors.
   解剖学课程中的医学生在经验丰富的教授密切指导下进行了首次尸体解剖。

3. The university established a program encouraging citizens to donate their bodies as **cadavers** for advancing medical research and education.
   该大学建立了一个项目，鼓励公民捐献遗体作为尸体标本，以推动医学研究和教育。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cadaver" containing "cave" — a dead body hidden in a cave. The Latin root "cadere" means "to fall," and a cadaver is one who has fallen permanently.
想想"cadaver"中包含"cave"（洞穴）——藏在洞穴中的尸体。拉丁词根"cadere"意为"倒下"，cadaver就是永远倒下的人。
