# 🤸 flexible

🔊 /ˈflɛksəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of bending without breaking; willing to change or compromise; adaptable to varying conditions or circumstances
**中文：** adj. 灵活的；柔韧的；易弯曲的；可变通的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adaptable（适应性强的）
- pliable（柔韧的）
- versatile（多才多艺的）
- supple（柔软的）
- elastic（有弹性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **flexible schedule / flexible approach / flexible working hours / flexible material / remain flexible**

👉 Example: A flexible approach to negotiation often yields better outcomes than rigid insistence on fixed terms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university adopted a more **flexible** curriculum that allowed students to customize their academic programs according to individual interests.
   该大学采用了更加灵活的课程设置，允许学生根据个人兴趣定制学术课程。

2. Engineers selected a **flexible** polymer for the bridge components because it could withstand seismic activity without fracturing.
   工程师为桥梁部件选择了一种柔韧的聚合物，因为它能承受地震活动而不会断裂。

3. Successful leaders must remain **flexible** in their strategies, adapting swiftly to rapidly changing economic and political conditions.
   成功的领导者必须在策略上保持灵活，迅速适应快速变化的经济和政治条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "flex-ible" — when you flex your muscles, they bend and stretch; something flexible can similarly bend without breaking.
把"flexible"想成"flex（弯曲）+ ible（能够的）"——能够弯曲的，就像肌肉弯曲伸展一样柔韧灵活。
