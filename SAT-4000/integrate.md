# 🔗 integrate

🔊 /ˈɪntɪɡreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To combine or bring together into a unified whole; to make part of a larger unit; to calculate the integral of a mathematical function. (adj.) Made up of integrated parts
**中文：** v. 使…完整；使…成整体；求…的积分 adj. 整合的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- combine（结合）
- merge（合并）
- unify（统一）
- incorporate（纳入）
- consolidate（整合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **integrate into / integrate with / fully integrated / integrate technology / integrate a function**

👉 Example: The school district worked to integrate students from diverse cultural backgrounds into a cohesive learning community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The software company plans to **integrate** artificial intelligence features into its existing product suite by next quarter.
   该软件公司计划在下一季度前将人工智能功能整合到其现有产品套件中。

2. Successful immigrants gradually **integrate** into the social and economic fabric of their adopted country over several years.
   成功的移民会在数年间逐渐融入其所在国家的社会和经济结构中。

3. In calculus, students learn to **integrate** complex functions using various analytical techniques and substitution methods.
   在微积分课程中，学生学习使用各种分析技巧和替换方法对复杂函数求积分。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "integrate" as "in-te-grate" — to make things "great" by bringing them "in" together as one unified whole.
把"integrate"想成"in（进入）+ (te)grate（伟大）"——把事物汇聚在一起使之成为一个统一的整体，从而变得更强大。
