# 🧴 disinfect

🔊 /ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cleanse or purify something by destroying harmful bacteria, viruses, or other microorganisms
**中文：** v. 将……消毒；杀菌消毒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sterilize（消毒）
- sanitize（清洁消毒）
- purify（净化）
- decontaminate（去污染）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disinfect a surface / disinfect a wound / disinfect with bleach / thoroughly disinfect**

👉 Example: Hospital staff must disinfect all surgical instruments before and after every procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public health authorities urged residents to **disinfect** all frequently touched surfaces to prevent the rapid spread of infection.
   公共卫生部门敦促居民对所有经常接触的表面进行消毒，以防止感染快速传播。

2. The laboratory protocol requires technicians to **disinfect** every workstation with approved chemical agents after each experiment.
   实验室规程要求技术人员在每次实验后用经批准的化学试剂对每个工作台进行消毒。

3. Researchers developed a novel ultraviolet technology capable of **disinfecting** large volumes of contaminated water within minutes.
   研究人员开发了一种新型紫外线技术，能够在几分钟内对大量受污染的水进行消毒。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-infect" as "dis (remove) + infect (infection)" — you remove the infection by cleaning and killing germs.
把"disinfect"想成"dis（去除）+ infect（感染）"——通过清洁杀菌来去除感染。
