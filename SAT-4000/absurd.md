# 🎭 absurd

🔊 /əbˈsɜːrd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Wildly unreasonable, illogical, or inappropriate; ridiculous. (n.) The quality or state of being absurd; the absurd
**中文：** adj. 荒谬的；可笑的；荒唐的 n. 荒诞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ridiculous（荒谬的）
- preposterous（荒唐的）
- ludicrous（可笑的）
- nonsensical（无意义的）
- farcical（滑稽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **absurd claim / absurd notion / absurd situation / patently absurd / theater of the absurd**

👉 Example: The defendant's explanation was so absurd that the jury dismissed it almost immediately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. It would be **absurd** to expect a single policy change to resolve decades of deeply rooted inequality.
   期望一项政策变化就能解决数十年根深蒂固的不平等是荒谬的。

2. The politician's **absurd** promise to eliminate all taxes drew widespread criticism from economic analysts.
   那位政客废除所有税收的荒唐承诺遭到了经济分析师的广泛批评。

3. The novel explores the **absurd** nature of modern bureaucracy through a series of darkly comic episodes.
   这部小说通过一系列黑色幽默情节探讨了现代官僚体制的荒诞本质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "absurd" as "ab-SURD" — something so unbelievable that it sounds SURD (deaf to reason), completely beyond logic.
把"absurd"想成"ab-SURD"——某件事荒唐到对理性"充耳不闻"(surd)，完全超出逻辑范畴。
