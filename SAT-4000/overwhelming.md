# 🌊 overwhelming

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈwɛlmɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** So great in amount, force, or degree as to be impossible to resist or overcome; overpowering
**中文：** adj. 压倒性的；势不可挡的；令人难以抗拒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overpowering（压倒性的）
- irresistible（不可抗拒的）
- staggering（令人震惊的）
- formidable（强大的）
- crushing（毁灭性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overwhelming majority / overwhelming evidence / overwhelming support / overwhelming response**

👉 Example: The overwhelming evidence presented by the prosecution left the jury with no reasonable doubt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proposal received **overwhelming** support from the committee, with ninety-five percent of members voting in its favor.
   该提案获得了委员会的压倒性支持，百分之九十五的成员投了赞成票。

2. The **overwhelming** volume of applications forced the admissions office to extend its review period by several additional weeks.
   压倒性的申请数量迫使招生办公室将审核期延长了数周。

3. Scientists presented **overwhelming** evidence that human activities have significantly accelerated the rate of global climate change.
   科学家们提出了压倒性的证据，表明人类活动显著加速了全球气候变化的速度。

---

把"overwhelming"想成一个巨浪从头顶"over（越过）"涌来把你"whelm（淹没）"——如此强大，完全将你吞没。
