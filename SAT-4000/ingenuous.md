# 🌿 ingenuous

🔊 /ɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Innocent and unsuspecting; showing childlike simplicity and candidness; free from deceit or cunning
**中文：** adj. 天真的；坦白的；正直的；朴实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- naive（天真的）
- candid（坦率的）
- guileless（不狡诈的）
- artless（朴实的）
- sincere（真诚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an ingenuous smile / ingenuous manner / ingenuous honesty / ingenuous nature**

👉 Example: Her ingenuous manner made everyone trust her immediately during the negotiation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness gave an **ingenuous** account of the events, clearly unaware of the legal implications of her candid testimony.
   证人对事件做了天真坦率的陈述，显然没有意识到她坦诚作证的法律后果。

2. His **ingenuous** belief that all people are fundamentally good sometimes left him vulnerable to deliberate manipulation.
   他天真地相信所有人本质上都是善良的，这有时使他容易受到蓄意操控。

3. The diplomat's **ingenuous** demeanor during the press conference surprised journalists who expected evasive and calculated responses.
   那位外交官在新闻发布会上的坦率态度令期待闪烁回答的记者们感到意外。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-GENU-ous" — "genu" sounds like "genuine," and an ingenuous person is genuinely open and without pretense.
把"ingenuous"想成"in + genu（像genuine真诚的）+ ous"——一个天真的人是真诚地坦率，毫无伪装。
