# 😈 heinous

🔊 /ˈheɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Utterly wicked, abominable, or shockingly evil; deserving of the strongest moral condemnation
**中文：** adj. 可憎的；极凶恶的；极恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- atrocious（残暴的）
- abominable（可恶的）
- monstrous（骇人的）
- villainous（恶棍般的）
- odious（可憎的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heinous crime / heinous act / heinous offense / heinous behavior**

👉 Example: The court imposed the maximum sentence for such a heinous crime against innocent civilians.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tribunal declared the war crimes to be among the most **heinous** offenses ever documented in modern international law.
   法庭宣布这些战争罪行是现代国际法中有记录以来最可憎的罪行之一。

2. Historians describe the ruler's persecution of minority groups as a **heinous** abuse of political power and authority.
   历史学家将该统治者对少数群体的迫害描述为对政治权力和权威的极恶滥用。

3. The community united in condemning the **heinous** act of vandalism that destroyed the century-old cultural heritage site.
   社区团结一致，谴责这一摧毁了百年文化遗产的可憎破坏行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heinous" as sounding like "hate-us" — a heinous act is so evil that it makes everyone hate the person who did it.
把"heinous"想成发音像"hate-us（恨我们）"——一个极恶的行为是如此邪恶，以至于所有人都会憎恨做这件事的人。
