# 🌾 bountiful

🔊 /ˈbaʊntɪfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Generously given or giving; abundant; plentiful in quantity or supply
**中文：** 丰富的；慷慨的；宽大的；大量的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- plentiful（充裕的）
- generous（慷慨的）
- copious（大量的）
- lavish（奢华的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bountiful harvest / bountiful supply / bountiful nature / bountiful generosity**

👉 Example: The region is celebrated for its bountiful harvests that sustain communities throughout the winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philanthropist's **bountiful** donations to the university enabled the construction of a new research facility.
   这位慈善家对大学的慷慨捐赠使得一座新的研究设施得以建成。

2. After years of drought, the farmers rejoiced at the **bountiful** rainfall that replenished their parched fields.
   经过多年干旱之后，农民们为充沛的降雨欢欣鼓舞，干涸的田地终于得到了滋润。

3. The region's **bountiful** natural resources have attracted significant foreign investment over the past decade.
   该地区丰富的自然资源在过去十年中吸引了大量外国投资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bounti-ful" as "bounty + full" — full of bounty, meaning overflowing with abundance and generosity.
把"bountiful"想成"bounty（赏金/丰饶）+ full（满的）"——满是丰饶，即丰富慷慨的。
