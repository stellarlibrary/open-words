# 🎛️ modulate

🔊 /ˈmɒdjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To regulate or adjust to a certain degree; to vary the pitch, tone, or intensity; in electronics, to alter a signal's characteristics
**中文：** v. 调节；调整；（信号）调制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regulate（调节）
- temper（缓和）
- adjust（调整）
- attenuate（减弱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **modulate one's voice / modulate the frequency / modulate the tone / modulate the response**

👉 Example: A skilled orator knows how to modulate her voice to maintain the audience's attention throughout the speech.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The central bank sought to **modulate** interest rates in order to stabilize the economy during a period of uncertainty.
   央行试图调节利率，以在不确定时期稳定经济。

2. The musician learned to **modulate** seamlessly between keys, creating a rich and emotionally compelling harmonic progression.
   这位音乐家学会了在不同调之间无缝转调，创造出丰富且令人动容的和声进行。

3. Scientists developed a novel device capable of **modulating** electromagnetic signals with unprecedented precision and efficiency.
   科学家们开发了一种能够以前所未有的精度和效率调制电磁信号的新型装置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "modul-ate" as related to "module" — you adjust individual modules or components, like tuning a dial to the right level.
把"modulate"想成与"module（模块）"相关——你调整各个模块或组件，就像调旋钮到合适的位置。
