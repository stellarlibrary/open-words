# 🎭 visage

🔊 /ˈvɪzɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person's face or facial expression; the outward appearance or aspect of something
**中文：** n. 面貌，容貌；外表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- countenance（面容）
- face（脸）
- appearance（外表）
- physiognomy（面相）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stern visage / solemn visage / pale visage / public visage**

👉 Example: The judge maintained a stern visage throughout the entire proceedings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat presented a calm **visage** despite the mounting tensions and hostile rhetoric exchanged during the negotiations.
   尽管谈判期间紧张局势加剧、充斥着敌意言辞，这位外交官仍保持镇定的面容。

2. Her **visage** betrayed no emotion as she delivered the devastating news to the assembled board of directors.
   当她向在座的董事会成员宣布这一毁灭性消息时，她的面容没有流露出任何情绪。

3. The ancient statue's weathered **visage** still conveyed a sense of dignity and authority after centuries of exposure.
   这座古老雕像饱经风霜的面容在经历了数百年的侵蚀后仍传递着尊严与威严。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "visage" as "vis" (see, from Latin "videre") + "age" — the face is what you see when you look at someone's age and expression.
把"visage"想成"vis"（看，源自拉丁语"videre"）+ "age"（年龄）——面容就是你看到一个人年龄和表情的地方。
