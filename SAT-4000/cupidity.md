# 💰 cupidity

🔊 /kjuːˈpɪdɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Excessive greed, especially for wealth or material possessions; avarice
**中文：** n. 贪心，贪婪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- greed（贪婪）
- avarice（贪财）
- rapacity（贪婪）
- covetousness（垂涎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **driven by cupidity / sheer cupidity / cupidity of the ruling class / cupidity and corruption**

👉 Example: The downfall of the empire was largely driven by the cupidity of its ruling elite.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novel depicts how unchecked **cupidity** among the merchant class ultimately led to the collapse of the colonial economy.
   这部小说描绘了商人阶层不受控制的贪婪最终如何导致殖民地经济的崩溃。

2. The senator's **cupidity** was exposed when investigators revealed millions of dollars hidden in offshore bank accounts.
   当调查人员揭露了隐藏在离岸银行账户中的数百万美元时，这位参议员的贪婪被曝光了。

3. Historians argue that **cupidity** rather than ideology was the primary motivation behind the conquest of the resource-rich territory.
   历史学家认为，贪婪而非意识形态是征服这片资源丰富领土的主要动机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Cupid" in "cupidity" — just as Cupid has an insatiable desire for love, cupidity describes an insatiable desire for wealth.
把"cupidity"中的"Cupid（丘比特）"联想起来——就像丘比特对爱情有无尽的渴望，cupidity描述的是对财富的无尽贪欲。
