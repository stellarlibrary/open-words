# 😤 insolent

🔊 /ˈɪnsələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing a rude and arrogant lack of respect; boldly disrespectful in speech or behavior
**中文：** adj. 傲慢的；无礼的；粗野的；无耻的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impudent（无耻的）
- disrespectful（无礼的）
- impertinent（放肆的）
- audacious（大胆无礼的）
- brazen（厚颜无耻的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insolent tone / insolent remark / insolent behavior / insolent attitude**

👉 Example: The student's insolent reply to the teacher shocked everyone in the classroom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **insolent** remarks during the international summit strained relations between the two neighboring countries for years.
   这位外交官在国际峰会上的无礼言论使两个邻国之间的关系紧张了多年。

2. The judge warned the defendant that further **insolent** outbursts in the courtroom would result in immediate contempt charges.
   法官警告被告，在法庭上再有傲慢无礼的爆发将立即导致藐视法庭的指控。

3. His **insolent** refusal to follow the established protocol demonstrated a complete disregard for institutional authority and tradition.
   他无礼地拒绝遵循既定协议，表现出对制度权威和传统的完全漠视。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insolent" as "in + solent" (sounds like "silent") — an insolent person is the opposite of silent, rudely speaking out with no respect.
把"insolent"想成"in + solent"（听起来像"silent"沉默的）——无礼的人与沉默相反，粗鲁地大声说话，毫无尊重。
