# 🔥 raze

🔊 /reɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To completely destroy a building, town, or other structure so that nothing remains; to demolish to the ground
**中文：** v. 夷为平地；破坏；消除；拆毁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- demolish（拆毁）
- destroy（破坏）
- level（夷平）
- obliterate（消除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **raze a building / raze to the ground / raze a city / raze a structure**

👉 Example: The old factory was razed to the ground to make way for a new residential complex.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The municipal council voted to **raze** the condemned apartment building after structural engineers declared it irreparably unsafe.
   市政委员会在结构工程师宣布该建筑无法修复后投票决定将这座危楼夷为平地。

2. Invading forces proceeded to **raze** the ancient fortress, leaving nothing but smoldering rubble and scorched earth behind.
   入侵军队将这座古老堡垒夷为平地，只留下冒烟的瓦砾和焦土。

3. Developers plan to **raze** the deteriorating warehouse district and construct a modern commercial center in its place.
   开发商计划拆除破旧的仓库区，在原址上建造一个现代化的商业中心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "raze" as rhyming with "erase" — when you raze a building, you erase it from the landscape completely.
把"raze"想成和"erase（擦除）"押韵——当你raze一栋建筑时，就是把它从地面上彻底擦除。
