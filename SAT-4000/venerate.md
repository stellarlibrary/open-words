# 🙏 venerate

🔊 /ˈvɛnəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To regard with great respect and reverence; to honor deeply
**中文：** v. 崇敬；尊敬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revere（崇敬）
- worship（崇拜）
- esteem（尊重）
- adore（敬慕）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **venerate a saint / venerate ancestors / venerate traditions / venerate a leader**

👉 Example: Many cultures venerate their ancestors through elaborate rituals and annual ceremonies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient civilization **venerated** the sun as a divine source of life, prosperity, and spiritual renewal.
   这个古老文明将太阳崇敬为生命、繁荣和精神更新的神圣源泉。

2. Scholars continue to **venerate** the philosopher's contributions to ethics, logic, and political theory.
   学者们继续崇敬这位哲学家在伦理学、逻辑学和政治理论方面的贡献。

3. The community **venerated** the elder for her decades of selfless service and unwavering moral integrity.
   社区崇敬这位长者数十年无私奉献和坚定的道德操守。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vener-ate" as related to "Venus," the goddess of love — you treat someone with the same awe and reverence as a goddess.
把"venerate"联想到"Venus"（维纳斯女神）——你以对待女神般的敬畏和崇敬来对待某人。
