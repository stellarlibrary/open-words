# 💡 suggestive

🔊 /səˈdʒɛstɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Tending to suggest or imply; indicative of; hinting at something
**中文：** adj. 提示的，暗示的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indicative（暗示的）
- evocative（引起联想的）
- reminiscent（令人联想的）
- implicative（暗含的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suggestive of / suggestive evidence / suggestive remarks / suggestive findings**

👉 Example: The archaeological findings were suggestive of a highly advanced civilization that once thrived in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The preliminary laboratory results were **suggestive** of a new viral strain, prompting the health authorities to initiate further investigations.
   初步的实验室结果暗示了一种新的病毒株，促使卫生当局启动了进一步调查。

2. Her ambiguous remarks were **suggestive** of deeper political tensions that remained concealed beneath the surface of diplomatic courtesy.
   她模棱两可的言论暗示了隐藏在外交礼仪表面之下的更深层政治紧张关系。

3. The unusual pattern of soil erosion was **suggestive** of significant climatic shifts occurring over the past several thousand years.
   不寻常的土壤侵蚀模式暗示着过去几千年中发生了重大的气候变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suggestive" as "suggest + ive" — something that has the quality of suggesting or hinting at a deeper meaning.
把"suggestive"想成"suggest + ive"——具有暗示或提示更深层含义的特质。
