# 〰️ vibration

🔊 /vaɪˈbreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A rapid oscillating movement back and forth; a quivering or trembling motion; an instinctive feeling about a place or situation
**中文：** n. 振动；颤动；心灵感应

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oscillation（振荡）
- tremor（颤动）
- resonance（共振）
- pulsation（脉动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vibration frequency / cause vibration / vibration of a string / mechanical vibration**

👉 Example: The engineers measured the vibration of the bridge to assess its structural integrity under heavy traffic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seismograph detected a subtle **vibration** in the earth's crust several hours before the major earthquake struck the region.
   地震仪在大地震袭击该地区前数小时检测到了地壳中细微的**振动**。

2. Excessive **vibration** from the industrial machinery gradually weakened the building's foundation over the course of several years.
   工业机械产生的过度**振动**在数年间逐渐削弱了建筑物的地基。

3. The physicist demonstrated how the **vibration** of a tuning fork produces sound waves that travel through the surrounding air.
   物理学家演示了音叉的**振动**如何产生通过周围空气传播的声波。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vibration" as "vibe-ration" — the "vibe" you feel is really a rapid back-and-forth ration of movement.
把"vibration"想成"vibe-ration"——你感受到的"vibe（氛围）"实际上是快速来回运动的配给。
