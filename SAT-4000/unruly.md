# 😤 unruly

🔊 /ʌnˈruːli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Disorderly and disruptive; not amenable to discipline or control; difficult to manage
**中文：** 不守规矩的；难驾驭的；难以管教的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unmanageable（难以管理的）
- disorderly（混乱的）
- rowdy（吵闹的）
- rebellious（叛逆的）
- wayward（任性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unruly behavior / unruly crowd / unruly hair / unruly children**

👉 Example: Security personnel were dispatched to manage the unruly crowd that had gathered outside the stadium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The teacher struggled to maintain order in the classroom, as several **unruly** students continually disrupted the lecture.
   老师努力维持课堂秩序，因为几个不守规矩的学生不断扰乱课堂。

2. Police officers formed a barricade to contain the **unruly** protesters who were blocking the main thoroughfare.
   警察组成了路障来控制那些堵塞主要通道的难以管控的抗议者。

3. The diplomat described the region as an **unruly** frontier where established laws held little authority over local factions.
   这位外交官将该地区描述为一个难以管辖的边疆，既定法律对当地派系几乎没有约束力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-rule-y" as "without rules" — someone who is unruly refuses to follow rules and cannot be controlled.
把"unruly"想成"un（没有）+ rule（规则）+ y"——不守规矩的人拒绝遵守规则，无法被管控。
