# 😴 lassitude

🔊 /ˈlæsɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of physical or mental weariness; lack of energy or enthusiasm; listlessness
**中文：** n. 疲乏；没精打采；厌倦

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lethargy（昏睡）
- fatigue（疲劳）
- weariness（倦怠）
- languor（无精打采）
- torpor（迟钝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overcome by lassitude / a feeling of lassitude / suffer from lassitude / intellectual lassitude**

👉 Example: After weeks of relentless work, a deep lassitude settled over the entire research team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged heatwave induced a pervasive **lassitude** among the workers, significantly reducing overall productivity on the construction site.
   持续的热浪在工人中引起了普遍的疲乏，显著降低了建筑工地的整体生产力。

2. Chronic **lassitude** is often an early symptom of underlying medical conditions that warrant thorough clinical investigation.
   慢性疲乏往往是潜在疾病的早期症状，需要进行全面的临床检查。

3. The nation's political **lassitude** allowed corruption to flourish unchecked throughout multiple levels of government administration.
   国家的政治倦怠使得腐败在多个政府管理层级中不受遏制地蔓延。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lassitude" as "LASS-i-tude" — imagine a "lass" (girl) with a tired "attitude." She is so weary she can barely move.
把"lassitude"想成"lass（少女）+ attitude（态度）"——想象一个少女带着疲惫的态度，累得几乎动不了。
