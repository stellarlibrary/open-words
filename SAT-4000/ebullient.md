# 🌟 ebullient

🔊 /ɪˈbʊliənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Overflowing with fervor, enthusiasm, or excitement; cheerfully energetic and lively
**中文：** adj. 热情洋溢的；沸腾的；兴高采烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exuberant（兴高采烈的）
- effervescent（热情洋溢的）
- buoyant（乐观的）
- vivacious（活泼的）
- enthusiastic（热情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ebullient personality / ebullient mood / ebullient spirit / ebullient crowd**

👉 Example: The ebullient crowd cheered wildly as the team scored the winning goal in the final minutes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ebullient** professor captivated students with her passionate lectures on Renaissance art and its cultural significance.
   这位热情洋溢的教授以她关于文艺复兴艺术及其文化意义的精彩讲座吸引了学生们。

2. Despite the disappointing quarterly results, the CEO remained **ebullient** about the company's long-term strategic prospects.
   尽管季度业绩令人失望，首席执行官对公司的长期战略前景仍然充满热情。

3. An **ebullient** atmosphere pervaded the research laboratory after the team successfully replicated their groundbreaking experimental findings.
   在团队成功复制了他们的突破性实验发现后，一种兴高采烈的气氛弥漫了整个研究实验室。

---

把"ebullient"想成"e-BULL-ient"——想象一头充满能量的公牛，带着无法阻挡的热情四处冲撞。
