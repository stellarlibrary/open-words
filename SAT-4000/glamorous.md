# 💎 glamorous

🔊 /ˈɡlæmərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having glamour; attractive in an exciting and special way; full of charm and allure
**中文：** adj. 迷人的，富有魅力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alluring（诱人的）
- captivating（迷人的）
- enchanting（迷人的）
- dazzling（令人眩目的）
- elegant（优雅的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glamorous lifestyle / glamorous appearance / glamorous career / glamorous event**

👉 Example: The actress made a glamorous entrance at the annual film awards ceremony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Behind the seemingly **glamorous** facade of the entertainment industry lies a reality of intense competition and personal sacrifice.
   在娱乐业看似迷人的外表背后，是激烈竞争和个人牺牲的现实。

2. The museum hosted a **glamorous** gala to celebrate the opening of its newly renovated contemporary art exhibition hall.
   博物馆举办了一场迷人的盛典，以庆祝其新翻修的当代艺术展厅的开幕。

3. Young professionals often discover that supposedly **glamorous** careers in fashion demand grueling hours and modest initial compensation.
   年轻的专业人士常常发现，时尚界所谓迷人的职业实际上要求高强度工作和微薄的初始薪酬。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glamorous" as "glamour + ous" — full of glamour, like a shining star that captures everyone's attention.
把"glamorous"想成"glamour（魅力）+ ous（充满）"——充满魅力，像一颗闪亮的星星吸引所有人的目光。
