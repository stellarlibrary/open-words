# 👤 profile

🔊 /ˈproʊfaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A side view or outline of something; a brief biographical description; public visibility. (v.) To describe the characteristics of; to present a summary of
**中文：** n. 侧面；轮廓，外形；人物简介；形象 v. 描……的轮廓；扼要描述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outline（轮廓）
- silhouette（剪影）
- biography（传记）
- portrayal（描绘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **high profile / low profile / profile of / keep a low profile / user profile / profile picture**

👉 Example: The magazine published a detailed profile of the award-winning scientist and her groundbreaking work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist wrote an insightful **profile** of the reclusive author whose novels had profoundly influenced contemporary literature.
   记者撰写了一篇关于这位隐居作家的深刻人物简介，其小说深刻影响了当代文学。

2. The architect carefully studied the **profile** of the hillside terrain before designing the foundation of the residential complex.
   建筑师在设计住宅小区地基之前仔细研究了山坡地形的轮廓。

3. The organization sought to raise its public **profile** by launching a series of charitable initiatives across several major cities.
   该组织试图通过在几个主要城市发起一系列慈善活动来提升其公众形象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-file" as "a filed record about a pro" — a profile is a summary that files away key information about someone or something.
把"profile"想成"pro（人物）+ file（档案）"——简介就是一份归档的关于某人或某物的关键信息记录。
