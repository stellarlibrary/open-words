# 🌫️ nebulous

🔊 /ˈnebjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Vague, unclear, or ill-defined; resembling or relating to a nebula or cloud
**中文：** adj. 朦胧的；模糊的；星云的，云雾状的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vague（模糊的）
- hazy（朦胧的）
- ambiguous（含糊的）
- indistinct（不清楚的）
- murky（晦涩的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nebulous concept / nebulous idea / nebulous plan / nebulous notion**

👉 Example: The professor warned students against relying on nebulous definitions in their thesis arguments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee presented only a **nebulous** outline of the proposed reforms, leaving many critical details unaddressed.
   委员会仅提出了拟议改革的模糊大纲，许多关键细节未得到解决。

2. Her understanding of quantum mechanics remained **nebulous** despite attending several advanced lectures on the subject.
   尽管参加了几次关于该主题的高级讲座，她对量子力学的理解仍然模糊不清。

3. The artist deliberately employed **nebulous** imagery to evoke a dreamlike atmosphere throughout the exhibition.
   这位艺术家刻意运用朦胧的意象，在整个展览中营造出梦幻般的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nebula" — a cloud of gas in space that looks hazy and unclear. "Nebulous" means cloudy and vague, just like a nebula.
想想"星云（nebula）"——太空中看起来朦胧模糊的气体云。"Nebulous"就像星云一样，表示模糊不清。
