# 🌅 suffuse

🔊 /səˈfjuːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To spread over or through; to fill or pervade with something
**中文：** v. 充满

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pervade（弥漫）
- permeate（渗透）
- imbue（充满）
- saturate（浸透）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffuse with light / suffuse with color / suffuse with warmth / suffuse with emotion**

👉 Example: A warm golden light suffused the entire room as the sun began to set over the horizon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A deep crimson blush **suffused** her cheeks when the professor publicly commended her outstanding research before the entire department.
   当教授在整个系面前公开表扬她的杰出研究时，一阵深红的红晕弥漫了她的双颊。

2. The fragrance of blooming jasmine **suffused** the evening air, creating an atmosphere of tranquility throughout the botanical garden.
   盛开的茉莉花香弥漫在傍晚的空气中，为整个植物园营造出一种宁静的氛围。

3. A sense of profound gratitude **suffused** the community after volunteers restored the historic landmark that had been damaged by flooding.
   在志愿者修复了被洪水破坏的历史地标后，一种深深的感激之情充满了整个社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suffuse" as "sub + fuse" — like pouring or fusing something underneath until it spreads everywhere.
把"suffuse"想成"sub + fuse"——就像在下面倾注或融合某物，直到它扩散到各处。
