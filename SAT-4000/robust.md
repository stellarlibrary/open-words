# 💪 robust

🔊 /roʊˈbʌst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Strong and healthy; vigorous; sturdy in construction or design
**中文：** adj. 强壮的，健康的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sturdy（结实的）
- vigorous（精力充沛的）
- resilient（有韧性的）
- hardy（强壮的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **robust health / robust economy / robust debate / robust system**

👉 Example: The country maintained a robust economy despite significant geopolitical challenges in the surrounding region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers developed a **robust** statistical framework capable of handling large datasets with numerous confounding variables.
   研究人员开发了一个强健的统计框架，能够处理包含大量混淆变量的大型数据集。

2. A **robust** immune system is essential for protecting the body against infectious diseases and environmental pathogens.
   强壮的免疫系统对于保护身体免受传染病和环境病原体侵害至关重要。

3. The engineering team designed a **robust** infrastructure that could withstand extreme weather conditions and seismic activity.
   工程团队设计了一个坚固的基础设施，能够承受极端天气条件和地震活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ro-bust" as "row + bust" — imagine someone so strong they can row a boat and bust through waves effortlessly.
把"robust"想成"row（划船）+ bust（冲破）"——想象一个人强壮到能划船并轻松冲破波浪。
