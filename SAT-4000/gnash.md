# 😤 gnash

🔊 /næʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To grind or strike the teeth together, especially in rage or anguish
**中文：** v. 咬牙切齿（尤指因愤怒或痛苦）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grind（磨牙）
- clench（咬紧）
- grit（咬紧牙关）
- gnaw（啃咬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gnash one's teeth / gnash in frustration / gnash with rage**

👉 Example: The defeated general gnashed his teeth upon learning of the catastrophic retreat.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prisoner **gnashed** his teeth in fury as the unjust verdict was read aloud by the presiding judge.
   囚犯在审判长宣读不公正的判决时愤怒地咬牙切齿。

2. Critics of the new regulation could only **gnash** their teeth, powerless to reverse the legislative decision already enacted.
   新法规的批评者只能咬牙切齿，无力推翻已经颁布的立法决定。

3. She **gnashed** her teeth in anguish when the scholarship committee formally rejected her meticulously prepared application.
   当奖学金委员会正式拒绝了她精心准备的申请时，她痛苦地咬牙切齿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gnash" as the sound of teeth grinding — "g-NASH" sounds like a violent clash, just like teeth smashing together in anger.
把"gnash"想象成牙齿磨擦的声音——"g-NASH"听起来像猛烈的碰撞，就像愤怒时牙齿咬在一起的样子。
