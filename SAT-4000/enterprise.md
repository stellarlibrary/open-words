# 🏢 enterprise

🔊 /ˈentərˌpraɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A business organization or company; a bold or difficult undertaking; initiative and resourcefulness
**中文：** n. 企业；事业；进取心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- venture（冒险事业）
- undertaking（事业）
- initiative（进取心）
- company（公司）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **free enterprise / private enterprise / business enterprise / spirit of enterprise**

👉 Example: The spirit of enterprise drove the young entrepreneur to launch three startups before turning thirty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government introduced new tax incentives to encourage private **enterprise** and stimulate economic growth in rural areas.
   政府推出了新的税收优惠政策，以鼓励私人企业发展并刺激农村地区的经济增长。

2. Establishing a colony on Mars remains one of the most ambitious **enterprises** humanity has ever contemplated undertaking.
   在火星上建立殖民地仍然是人类所设想过的最宏大的事业之一。

3. Her remarkable **enterprise** and determination enabled her to transform a modest idea into a multinational corporation.
   她非凡的进取心和决心使她将一个不起眼的想法发展成了一家跨国公司。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "enter-prize" — you enter a competition for a prize, which takes enterprise and bold initiative.
把"enterprise"想成"enter（进入）+ prize（奖品）"——为了赢得奖品而进入竞争，这需要进取心和勇气。
