# 🤔 ponder

🔊 /ˈpɑːndər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To think about something carefully and at length; to weigh mentally; to consider deeply
**中文：** v. 仔细考虑；思索；沉思；衡量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contemplate（沉思）
- deliberate（深思熟虑）
- reflect（反思）
- meditate（冥想）
- mull over（仔细考虑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ponder a question / ponder the meaning / ponder over / ponder deeply**

👉 Example: She sat quietly by the window to ponder the implications of the committee's unexpected decision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher spent decades **pondering** the fundamental nature of consciousness and human perception.
   这位哲学家花了数十年时间思索意识和人类感知的根本本质。

2. Before accepting the offer, she took several days to **ponder** the potential risks and rewards of relocation.
   在接受邀请之前，她花了几天时间仔细考虑搬迁的潜在风险和回报。

3. The committee members **pondered** the proposed legislation carefully before casting their votes on the measure.
   委员会成员在对该措施投票之前仔细斟酌了拟议的立法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pond-er" as someone sitting by a "pond," gazing at the still water and thinking deeply about life's big questions.
把"ponder"想成一个坐在"池塘(pond)"边的人，凝视着平静的水面，深思人生的重大问题。
