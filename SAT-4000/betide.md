# ⚡ betide

🔊 /bɪˈtaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To happen or come to pass; to befall; to portend or foretell
**中文：** v. 预示；降临于，发生于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- befall（降临）
- occur（发生）
- transpire（发生）
- portend（预示）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **woe betide / whatever may betide / come what may betide**

👉 Example: Woe betide anyone who dares to challenge the authority of the ruling council without proper standing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Woe **betide** the careless diplomat who neglects the established protocols of international negotiation and ceremony.
   不遵守既定国际谈判和礼仪规程的粗心外交官将会遭殃。

2. Whatever fortune may **betide** the nation in these uncertain times, the people shall remain resolute and united.
   无论在这些不确定的时期国家会遭遇什么命运，人民都将保持坚定和团结。

3. Few could have predicted the calamity that would **betide** the prosperous trading empire within a single decade.
   几乎没有人能预料到这个繁荣的贸易帝国会在短短十年内遭遇如此灾难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-TIDE" — like the tide of fate that comes to you. Whatever betides you is what the tides of fortune bring your way.
想想"be-TIDE"——像命运的潮水向你涌来。Betide你的就是命运之潮带给你的东西。
