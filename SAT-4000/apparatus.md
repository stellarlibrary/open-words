# 🔬 apparatus

🔊 /ˌæpəˈrætəs/<br>
🏷️ *n. 装置，设备；仪器*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The technical equipment or machinery needed for a particular activity or purpose; also, a complex system or organization.

**中文：** 装置；设备；仪器；也可指复杂的组织体系或机构。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- equipment 设备
- device 装置
- instrument 仪器
- mechanism 机制
- machinery 机械

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** scientific apparatus, laboratory apparatus, state apparatus, breathing apparatus, the apparatus of government

**Example:** "Apparatus" can refer to both physical equipment (lab apparatus) and abstract systems (the apparatus of power). The plural form can be either "apparatus" or "apparatuses."

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory was equipped with sophisticated **apparatus** designed for conducting advanced chemical experiments.
   实验室配备了用于进行高级化学实验的精密**仪器**。

2. The government's security **apparatus** was restructured to improve intelligence-gathering and threat assessment capabilities.
   政府的安全**机构**经过重组，以提高情报收集和威胁评估能力。

3. Firefighters donned their breathing **apparatus** before entering the smoke-filled building to search for survivors.
   消防员在进入浓烟弥漫的建筑物搜寻幸存者前穿戴好了呼吸**设备**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "apparatus" as "a-para-tus" — something "prepared" (from Latin "apparare," to prepare) for a task, i.e., equipment made ready for use.

联想记忆：apparatus 源自拉丁语"apparare"（准备）——为某项任务准备好的东西，即"设备、仪器"。
