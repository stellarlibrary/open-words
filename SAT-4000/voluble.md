# 🗣 voluble

🔊 /ˈvɑːljəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Speaking or spoken fluently, readily, and at length; characterized by a ready and continuous flow of words
**中文：** adj. 健谈的；流利的；易旋转的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- talkative（健谈的）
- loquacious（多话的）
- garrulous（唠叨的）
- eloquent（雄辩的）
- fluent（流利的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **voluble speaker / voluble conversation / voluble manner / voluble defense**

👉 Example: The voluble professor captivated students with her effortless and engaging lectures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **voluble** tour guide provided a continuous stream of fascinating historical anecdotes throughout the entire excursion.
   那位健谈的导游在整个游览过程中不断讲述引人入胜的历史轶事。

2. Although normally reserved, the diplomat became surprisingly **voluble** when discussing matters of international trade policy.
   尽管通常很矜持，这位外交官在讨论国际贸易政策时却出人意料地健谈。

3. Her **voluble** defense of the research methodology impressed the committee and silenced the most skeptical critics.
   她对研究方法论流利的辩护给委员会留下了深刻印象，也让最持怀疑态度的批评者哑口无言。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voluble" as "vol-" (to roll, from Latin "volvere") + "-uble" — words that roll off the tongue easily, describing someone who speaks fluently and at length.
把"voluble"想成"vol-"（滚动，来自拉丁语"volvere"）+"-uble"——话语轻松地从舌尖滚落，形容一个人说话流利且滔滔不绝。
