# 🧪 alkali

🔊 /ˈæl.kə.laɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词，形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A chemical substance that neutralizes acids, has a pH greater than 7, and forms hydroxide ions in solution; having the properties of a base.
**中文：** 碱；碱性物质。碱性的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- base（碱）
- caustic（苛性碱）
- antacid（抗酸剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alkali metal, alkali solution, strong alkali, alkali soil, alkali resistance**
👉 Example: The chemist added an alkali solution to neutralize the excess acid.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The industrial process requires a concentrated **alkali** to dissolve the organic compounds embedded in the raw material.
   该工业过程需要浓缩碱液来溶解嵌入原材料中的有机化合物。

2. Farmers in the arid region struggled with **alkali** soils that severely inhibited the growth of most crops.
   干旱地区的农民面临碱性土壤的困扰，这严重抑制了大多数作物的生长。

3. The laboratory technician carefully measured the **alkali** concentration before proceeding with the titration experiment.
   实验室技术员在进行滴定实验前仔细测量了碱的浓度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alkali" comes from Arabic "al-qaly" meaning "ashes of saltwort" — ancient people discovered alkali in plant ashes.
"alkali"来自阿拉伯语"al-qaly"，意为"盐蒿的灰烬"——古人从植物灰烬中发现了碱。
