# 🐦 quail

🔊 /kweɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To shrink back in fear; to cower or flinch. (n.) A small, short-tailed game bird
**中文：** v. 畏缩，胆怯；感到恐惧 n. 鹌鹑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cower（畏缩）
- flinch（退缩）
- recoil（退缩）
- shrink（畏缩）
- tremble（颤抖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quail at the thought / quail before someone / quail in fear / quail at the prospect**

👉 Example: Even the bravest soldiers quailed at the sight of the advancing enemy forces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young witness **quailed** under the relentless cross-examination of the defense attorney in the crowded courtroom.
   年轻的证人在拥挤的法庭上被辩护律师无情的盘问吓得畏缩不前。

2. Many experienced diplomats **quailed** at the prospect of negotiating with such an unpredictable and volatile foreign leader.
   许多经验丰富的外交官一想到要与如此不可预测且反复无常的外国领导人谈判就感到恐惧。

3. The researcher observed how the captive **quail** exhibited distinct behavioral patterns when exposed to various environmental stressors.
   研究人员观察了笼养鹌鹑在接受各种环境压力时表现出的独特行为模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quail" — a quail is a timid bird that trembles and hides, just like someone who quails in fear.
把"quail"想成鹌鹑——鹌鹑是一种胆小的鸟，总是颤抖和躲藏，就像一个因恐惧而畏缩的人。
