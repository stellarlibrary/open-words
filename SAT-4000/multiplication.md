# ✖️ multiplication

🔊 /ˌmʌltɪplɪˈkeɪʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The mathematical operation of multiplying one quantity by another; a substantial increase in number or quantity
**中文：** n. 乘法；增加

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proliferation（增殖）
- increase（增加）
- expansion（扩展）
- growth（增长）
- amplification（扩大）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **multiplication table / multiplication of cells / multiplication factor / rapid multiplication**

👉 Example: The rapid multiplication of bacteria in the contaminated water supply posed a serious public health threat.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Mastery of basic **multiplication** is considered an essential foundation for students advancing to higher levels of mathematics.
   掌握基本乘法被认为是学生进入更高数学阶段的重要基础。

2. The **multiplication** of social media platforms has fundamentally transformed how information is disseminated across global communities.
   社交媒体平台的大量增加从根本上改变了信息在全球社区中的传播方式。

3. Researchers observed the rapid **multiplication** of abnormal cells, prompting an urgent reassessment of the treatment protocol.
   研究人员观察到异常细胞的快速增殖，促使对治疗方案进行紧急重新评估。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "multiplication" as "MULTI-plic-ATION" — the action (ation) of making multiple (multi) copies, whether in math or in nature.
把"multiplication"想成"MULTI-plic-ATION"——制造多个(multi)副本的行为(ation)，无论是在数学中还是在自然界中。
