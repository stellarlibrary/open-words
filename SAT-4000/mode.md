# 🔄 mode

🔊 /moʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A way or manner in which something occurs or is done; a style or fashion; in statistics, the most frequently occurring value
**中文：** n. 模式；方式；风格；时尚；众数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- method（方法）
- manner（方式）
- style（风格）
- approach（方法）
- fashion（时尚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mode of transport / mode of operation / survival mode / in the mode of / statistical mode**

👉 Example: The researcher identified the mode of the data set as the most frequently occurring test score.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor explained that the **mode** is the value that appears most frequently in a given statistical data set.
   教授解释说，众数是在给定统计数据集中出现最频繁的值。

2. The company shifted its primary **mode** of communication from formal written memos to instant digital messaging platforms.
   该公司将其主要沟通方式从正式的书面备忘录转变为即时数字信息平台。

3. When the system detects a critical failure, it automatically switches into emergency **mode** to protect all stored data.
   当系统检测到严重故障时，它会自动切换到紧急模式以保护所有存储的数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mode" as "the MOst freqDE" — in statistics, the mode is the most frequent value, and "mode" rhymes with "code," a set way of doing things.
把"mode"想成"最常出现的方式"——在统计学中，众数是最常出现的值，而"mode"也意味着一种固定的做事方式。
