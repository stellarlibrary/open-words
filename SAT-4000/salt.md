# 🧂 salt

🔊 /sɔːlt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A crystalline compound used for seasoning and preserving food; wit or pungency. (adj.) Containing or tasting of salt. (v.) To season, preserve, or treat with salt
**中文：** n. 盐

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sodium chloride（氯化钠）
- seasoning（调味料）
- savor（风味）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salt of the earth / take with a grain of salt / salt water / worth one's salt**

👉 Example: Any experienced reader should take sensational claims with a grain of salt before accepting them as truth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Ancient civilizations considered **salt** so valuable that it was frequently used as a form of currency in commercial transactions.
   古代文明认为盐非常珍贵，经常将其用作商业交易中的一种货币形式。

2. The chef recommended using coarse sea **salt** rather than refined table salt to enhance the natural flavors of the dish.
   厨师建议使用粗海盐而不是精制食盐来增强菜肴的天然风味。

3. Workers were dispatched to **salt** the icy roads before dawn to ensure safe commuting conditions for morning travelers.
   工人们在黎明前被派去在结冰的道路上撒盐，以确保早晨通勤者的安全出行条件。

"Salt"是最基本的英语单词之一，就像盐对烹饪和保存食物必不可少一样，盐对生命也至关重要。
