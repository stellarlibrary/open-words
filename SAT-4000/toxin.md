# 🧪 toxin

🔊 /ˈtɑːksɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A poisonous substance produced by living organisms, especially one that is harmful to other organisms
**中文：** 毒素；毒质（尤指由生物体产生的有毒物质）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- poison（毒药）
- venom（毒液）
- contaminant（污染物）
- pollutant（污染物质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **release toxins / environmental toxin / bacterial toxin / remove toxins / exposure to toxins**

👉 Example: The research team identified a previously unknown toxin produced by the invasive species of algae.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists isolated a potent **toxin** from the deep-sea organism that could potentially be developed into a new antibiotic.
   科学家从深海生物中分离出一种强效**毒素**，有可能被开发成一种新型抗生素。

2. The liver plays a critical role in filtering **toxins** from the bloodstream and protecting vital organs from damage.
   肝脏在从血液中过滤**毒素**并保护重要器官免受损害方面发挥着关键作用。

3. Industrial facilities must adhere to strict regulations governing the disposal of materials that contain harmful **toxins**.
   工业设施必须遵守严格的法规来处置含有有害**毒素**的材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "toxin" as "tox-in" — the poison that gets "in" your body, a harmful substance produced by living things.
把"toxin"想成"tox（毒）-in（在里面）"——进入你体内的毒，是由生物体产生的有害物质。
