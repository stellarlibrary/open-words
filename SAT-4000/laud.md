# 🎖️ laud

🔊 /lɔːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To praise highly; to extol or glorify. (n.) Praise; a hymn or song of praise
**中文：** v. 赞美；称赞 n. 赞美；称赞；颂歌

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extol（颂扬）
- praise（赞美）
- commend（称赞）
- glorify（赞颂）
- acclaim（赞扬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laud someone for something / laud the achievements / laud the virtues**

👉 Example: Critics universally lauded the director for her innovative approach to storytelling.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee **lauded** the scientist for her groundbreaking research that advanced the field of renewable energy.
   委员会赞扬了这位科学家在推动可再生能源领域方面所做的开创性研究。

2. Historians continue to **laud** the diplomat's role in negotiating the landmark peace agreement between rival nations.
   历史学家们继续称赞这位外交官在敌对国家间谈判具有里程碑意义的和平协议中所发挥的作用。

3. The president's speech **lauded** the courage of first responders who risked their lives during the catastrophic flood.
   总统的演讲赞美了在灾难性洪水中冒着生命危险的急救人员的勇气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laud" as sounding like "lord" — you praise and glorify a lord, just as you laud someone's great achievements.
把"laud"想成发音类似"lord（领主）"——你会赞美和颂扬一位领主，正如你laud某人的伟大成就。
