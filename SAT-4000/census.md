# 📊 census

🔊 /ˈsɛnsəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An official count or survey of a population, recording various details of individuals (v.) To conduct such an official count or survey
**中文：** n. 人口普查 v. 实施统计调查

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- survey（调查）
- count（统计）
- enumeration（列举）
- tally（计数）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **national census / conduct a census / census data / census bureau / population census**

👉 Example: The national census is conducted every ten years to determine the distribution of congressional seats.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government relies on **census** data to allocate federal funding equitably among states and municipalities.
   政府依靠人口普查数据在各州和市政当局之间公平分配联邦资金。

2. Researchers discovered significant demographic shifts when comparing the latest **census** results with those from a decade earlier.
   研究人员在将最新的人口普查结果与十年前的结果进行比较时发现了显著的人口结构变化。

3. Local authorities plan to **census** the wildlife population in the reserve to inform conservation strategies.
   地方当局计划对保护区内的野生动物种群进行调查，以制定保护策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "census" as "sense us" — the government wants to "sense" (count and understand) all of us in the population.
想想"census"就像"sense us（感知我们）"——政府想要"感知"（统计和了解）人口中的每一个人。
