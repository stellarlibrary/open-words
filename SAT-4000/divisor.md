# 🔣 divisor

🔊 /dɪˈvaɪzər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A number by which another number is to be divided; a factor of an integer
**中文：** n. 除数；因子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- factor（因子）
- denominator（分母）
- quotient factor（商因子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **common divisor / greatest common divisor / divisor of / zero divisor**

👉 Example: In the equation twenty divided by five equals four, the number five serves as the divisor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The greatest common **divisor** of forty-eight and thirty-six is twelve, which simplifies the fraction to its lowest terms.
   四十八和三十六的最大公约数是十二，这可以将分数化简为最简分数。

2. A prime number has exactly two **divisors**: the number one and the prime number itself, with no others.
   质数恰好有两个因子：数字一和质数本身，没有其他因子。

3. The professor reminded students that a **divisor** of zero is undefined and produces an error in mathematical computation.
   教授提醒学生，除数为零是未定义的，在数学计算中会产生错误。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "divisor" as "the divider" — it is the number that does the dividing, splitting the dividend into equal parts.
把"divisor"想成"做除法的数"——它是执行除法的数，将被除数分成相等的部分。
