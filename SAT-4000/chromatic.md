# 🌈 chromatic

🔊 /krəˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or produced in color; (Music) relating to a scale that ascends or descends by semitones; easily stained or colored
**中文：** adj. 彩色的；色品的；半音的；易染色的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- colorful（色彩丰富的）
- polychromatic（多色的）
- prismatic（棱柱色的）
- variegated（杂色的）
- semitonal（半音的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chromatic scale / chromatic aberration / chromatic harmony / chromatic spectrum**

👉 Example: The chromatic scale in music includes all twelve pitches within a single octave, each separated by a semitone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist employed a bold **chromatic** palette to convey the emotional intensity of the turbulent landscape she depicted.
   这位艺术家运用了大胆的**彩色**调色板来传达她所描绘的动荡风景的情感张力。

2. Advanced telescopes are designed to minimize **chromatic** aberration, which causes colors to separate and blur the final image.
   先进的望远镜被设计为最大限度地减少**色**差，色差会导致颜色分离并模糊最终图像。

3. The composer's use of **chromatic** passages lent the orchestral piece a haunting and emotionally complex quality throughout.
   作曲家对**半音**段落的运用使管弦乐作品自始至终具有一种萦绕人心且情感复杂的品质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chromatic" as "chroma + tic" — "chroma" is Greek for color, so chromatic means related to color or the full spectrum.
把"chromatic"想成"chroma + tic"——"chroma"在希腊语中意为颜色，因此chromatic意为与颜色或完整光谱相关的。
