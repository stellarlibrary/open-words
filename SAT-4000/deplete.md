# 💧 deplete

🔊 /dɪˈpliːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To use up or exhaust the supply of a resource; to reduce severely in quantity, content, or strength
**中文：** v. 耗尽；用尽；使衰竭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exhaust（耗尽）
- drain（消耗）
- diminish（减少）
- consume（消耗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deplete resources / deplete reserves / deplete the ozone layer / severely depleted**

👉 Example: Industrial fishing practices continue to deplete marine populations at an unsustainable rate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Excessive irrigation practices have significantly **depleted** underground aquifers, threatening the long-term water security of the entire agricultural region.
   过度灌溉做法已严重耗尽了地下蓄水层，威胁着整个农业区的长期水资源安全。

2. The prolonged military campaign had **depleted** the nation's treasury, forcing the government to seek emergency loans from foreign creditors.
   旷日持久的军事行动耗尽了国库，迫使政府向外国债权人寻求紧急贷款。

3. If we continue to **deplete** nonrenewable fossil fuels at the current rate, future generations will face severe energy shortages.
   如果我们继续以目前的速度耗尽不可再生化石燃料，后代将面临严重的能源短缺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-plete" as "de-complete" — when you deplete something, you take it from complete to empty.
把"deplete"想成"de-complete（去完成）"——当你deplete某物时，你把它从满的状态变成空的。
