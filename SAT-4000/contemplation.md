# 🧘 contemplation

🔊 /ˌkɒntɛmˈpleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Deep reflective thought; the act of gazing at something steadily; the intention or expectation of a future event
**中文：** n. 沉思；注视；意图；预期

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- meditation（冥想）
- reflection（深思）
- deliberation（审慎考虑）
- rumination（沉思）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deep contemplation / in contemplation of / quiet contemplation / lost in contemplation**

👉 Example: The philosopher sat in quiet contemplation, pondering the meaning of existence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After hours of careful **contemplation**, the committee reached a unanimous decision regarding the proposed reforms.
   经过数小时的仔细沉思，委员会就拟议的改革达成了一致决定。

2. The scholar devoted her mornings to silent **contemplation** of the ancient manuscripts preserved in the archive.
   这位学者每天早晨都在静静地注视档案馆中保存的古代手稿。

3. The new policy was drafted in **contemplation** of the significant demographic changes anticipated over the next decade.
   这项新政策是在预期未来十年重大人口变化的情况下起草的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-TEMPLE-ation" — sitting inside a temple, deep in thought, gazing at the surroundings in peaceful reflection.
把"contemplation"想成"在寺庙（temple）中"——坐在寺庙里，深深地沉思，平静地注视着周围的一切。
