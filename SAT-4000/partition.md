# 🧱 partition

🔊 /pɑːrˈtɪʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A division into parts; a structure dividing a space. (v.) To divide into separate parts or sections
**中文：** n. 区分，隔开，分割；隔墙；隔离物 v. 分割；分隔；区分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- division（分割）
- separation（分隔）
- barrier（隔墙）
- segment（划分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **partition a room / partition a country / partition into sections / glass partition**

👉 Example: The office space was divided by a glass partition to create separate work areas.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architects proposed installing a movable **partition** to allow the conference hall to serve as two independent rooms.
   建筑师们提议安装一面可移动的隔墙，使会议厅可以用作两个独立的房间。

2. After the war, the allied powers agreed to **partition** the territory into distinct administrative zones under separate governance.
   战后，盟国同意将该领土分割为由不同政府管辖的独立行政区。

3. The database administrator decided to **partition** the massive table into smaller segments to improve query performance significantly.
   数据库管理员决定将庞大的数据表分割成更小的段，以显著提高查询性能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "part-ition" as "making PARTS" — a partition creates separate parts from a whole.
把"partition"想成"制造PARTS（部分）"——隔断将一个整体分成不同的部分。
