# 💧 condense

🔊 /kənˈdɛns/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To make something denser or more concentrated; to change from a gas or vapor to a liquid; to express in fewer words.

**中文：** 浓缩；凝结；压缩；精简。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- compress（压缩）
- concentrate（浓缩）
- abridge（缩减）
- consolidate（合并）
- distill（蒸馏；提炼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** condense something into something

**Example:** The editor asked the author to **condense** the lengthy report into a concise summary.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist observed water vapor **condense** on the cold surface of the laboratory glass apparatus.
   科学家观察到水蒸气在实验室玻璃器皿的冷表面上凝结。

2. She managed to **condense** three hundred pages of research findings into a single coherent chapter.
   她成功地将三百页的研究成果精简为一个连贯的章节。

3. As temperatures dropped overnight, moisture in the atmosphere began to **condense** into visible droplets.
   随着夜间气温下降，大气中的水分开始凝结成可见的水滴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** Think of "con-" (together) + "dense" — making things come together to become denser or more compact.

**中文：** 联想 "con-"（一起）+ "dense"（密集的）——把东西聚在一起变得更密集、更紧凑，即"浓缩、凝结"。
