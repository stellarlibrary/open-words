# 🎨 artistic

🔊 /ɑːrˈtɪstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Having or revealing natural creative skill; relating to or characteristic of art or artists; aesthetically pleasing.

**中文：** 艺术的；风雅的；有美感的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- creative 有创造力的
- aesthetic 美学的
- imaginative 富有想象力的
- tasteful 有品味的

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Artistic" describes both people who possess creative talent and things that display aesthetic beauty. It is often used to praise someone's refined sense of style or creative ability.

"artistic" 既可以形容拥有创造才华的人，也可以形容展现美感的事物。常用于赞扬某人精致的审美或创造能力。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her **artistic** talent became evident when she won the national painting competition at age fifteen.
   她在十五岁赢得全国绘画比赛时，其艺术才华便显露无遗。

2. The architect presented an **artistic** vision that seamlessly blended modern design with classical elegance.
   这位建筑师展示了一个将现代设计与古典优雅完美融合的艺术构想。

3. Growing up in a deeply **artistic** family, he naturally developed a profound appreciation for music and literature.
   在一个艺术氛围浓厚的家庭中长大，他自然而然地培养了对音乐和文学的深厚鉴赏力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Artist" + "-ic" (adjective suffix) — having the qualities of an artist, full of art and beauty.

"Artist"（艺术家）+ "-ic"（形容词后缀）——拥有艺术家的特质，充满艺术和美感。
