# ⛪ calvinism

🔊 /ˈkælvɪnɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The Protestant theological system of John Calvin, emphasizing predestination, the sovereignty of God, and the authority of Scripture
**中文：** n. 加尔文主义；加尔文教义；加尔文教派

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- Reformed theology（改革宗神学）
- Protestantism（新教）
- predestinarianism（预定论）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **principles of Calvinism / adherent of Calvinism / influence of Calvinism / Calvinism and predestination**

👉 Example: The doctrine of predestination is one of the central tenets of Calvinism.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Calvinism** profoundly influenced the political and social development of several European nations during the sixteenth and seventeenth centuries.
   加尔文主义深刻影响了十六和十七世纪几个欧洲国家的政治和社会发展。

2. The sociologist Max Weber argued that **Calvinism** played a significant role in fostering the spirit of modern capitalism.
   社会学家马克斯·韦伯认为加尔文主义在培养现代资本主义精神方面发挥了重要作用。

3. Many scholars continue to debate whether **Calvinism** encouraged individual autonomy or reinforced strict communal moral discipline.
   许多学者仍在争论加尔文主义究竟鼓励了个人自主还是强化了严格的社区道德纪律。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Calvinism" as "Calvin-ism" — the "-ism" (ideology) of John Calvin, the French reformer who emphasized that God has already chosen the saved.
把"Calvinism"想成"Calvin-ism"——法国改革家约翰·加尔文的"-ism"（主义），他强调上帝已经预先拣选了得救之人。
