# 🚫 thwart

🔊 /θwɔːrt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To prevent from accomplishing a purpose; to frustrate or defeat a plan or attempt
**中文：** v. 挫败；反对；阻碍；使受挫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frustrate（挫败）
- foil（阻挠）
- obstruct（阻碍）
- impede（妨碍）
- hinder（阻挡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **thwart a plan / thwart an attempt / thwart someone's efforts / thwart ambitions**

👉 Example: The detective managed to thwart the criminal's elaborate scheme just before it was executed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Security forces successfully **thwarted** a coordinated terrorist attack that had been planned for several months.
   安全部队成功挫败了一场筹划数月的协调恐怖袭击。

2. Bureaucratic regulations often **thwart** innovation by imposing excessive restrictions on emerging technologies and startups.
   官僚法规常常通过对新兴技术和初创企业施加过多限制来阻碍创新。

3. The opposition party vowed to **thwart** any legislative proposal that would undermine environmental protection standards.
   反对党誓言要阻止任何会削弱环境保护标准的立法提案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "thwart" as "throw + wart" — imagine throwing a wart at someone's plan to block it and make it ugly.
把"thwart"想成"throw（扔）+ wart（疣）"——想象向某人的计划扔一个疣来阻止它并使其变丑。
