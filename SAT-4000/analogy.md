# 🔗 analogy

🔊 /əˈnælədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A comparison between two things for the purpose of explanation or clarification; a correspondence or similarity in certain respects between otherwise different things
**中文：** 类比；类推；类似

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comparison（比较）
- parallel（类比）
- similarity（相似）
- correspondence（对应）
- likeness（相似性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **draw an analogy / by analogy / analogy between A and B / make an analogy / false analogy**

👉 Example: The teacher drew an analogy between the human heart and a mechanical pump to explain circulation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author used the **analogy** of a symphony orchestra to illustrate how different departments must coordinate within a large corporation.
   作者用交响乐团的类比来说明大公司中不同部门必须如何协调配合。

2. By **analogy** with the immune system, the cybersecurity team designed software that could detect and neutralize unfamiliar digital threats.
   通过与免疫系统的类比，网络安全团队设计了能够检测和消除陌生数字威胁的软件。

3. Critics dismissed the politician's **analogy** between climate regulation and economic tyranny as a misleading and fundamentally flawed comparison.
   批评者认为该政治家将气候监管与经济暴政相类比是一种误导性的、根本错误的比较。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "analogy" as "ana- (upon) + -logy (reasoning)" — reasoning upon similarities between two different things to explain one through the other.
把"analogy"理解为"ana-（基于）+ -logy（推理）"——基于两个不同事物之间的相似性进行推理，用一个来解释另一个。
