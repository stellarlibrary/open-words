# 🌬️ Oxygen

🔊 /ˈɒksɪdʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, odorless gaseous element that constitutes approximately 21 percent of the atmosphere and is essential for respiration and combustion.
**中文：** n. [化学] 氧气，氧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- O₂（氧气分子）
- air（空气）
- gas（气体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oxygen supply / oxygen level / oxygen mask / oxygen tank / lack of oxygen**

👉 Example: The climbers carried portable oxygen tanks to survive at extremely high altitudes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The concentration of **oxygen** in the atmosphere plays a critical role in sustaining all aerobic life forms on Earth.
   大气中**氧气**的浓度在维持地球上所有需氧生命形式方面起着关键作用。

2. Medical professionals administered supplemental **oxygen** to the patient whose respiratory function had severely deteriorated overnight.
   医疗专业人员为呼吸功能在夜间严重恶化的患者提供了补充**氧气**。

3. The research team discovered that dissolved **oxygen** levels in the lake had dropped below the threshold necessary for aquatic survival.
   研究团队发现湖中溶解**氧**的水平已降至水生生物生存所需的阈值以下。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oxy-gen" as "oxy" (sharp/acid) + "gen" (to produce) — oxygen was originally named because scientists believed it generated acids.
把"oxygen"拆分为"oxy"（酸）+"gen"（产生）——氧气最初被命名是因为科学家认为它能产生酸。
