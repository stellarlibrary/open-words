# 🪞 retrospect

🔊 /ˈrɛtrəˌspɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A review or contemplation of things in the past. (v.) To look back on or review past events or situations
**中文：** n. 回顾，追溯 v. 回顾，追溯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hindsight（后见之明）
- review（回顾）
- recollection（回忆）
- reflection（反思）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in retrospect / retrospect on / upon retrospect / looking back in retrospect**

👉 Example: In retrospect, the decision to expand operations overseas proved to be remarkably prescient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In **retrospect**, the ambassador acknowledged that earlier diplomatic intervention could have prevented the escalation of the conflict.
   回顾起来，大使承认更早的外交干预本可以防止冲突的升级。

2. The retiring professor used her farewell lecture to **retrospect** on four decades of groundbreaking discoveries in molecular biology.
   这位即将退休的教授在告别演讲中回顾了四十年来分子生物学领域的突破性发现。

3. In **retrospect**, the initial skepticism surrounding the experimental treatment was entirely unwarranted given its extraordinary clinical success.
   回顾来看，鉴于该实验性疗法取得了非凡的临床成功，最初的质疑完全是没有根据的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "retro-spect" as "spectacles looking retro" — wearing glasses that let you see backward into the past.
把"retrospect"想成"回顾的眼镜"——戴上一副让你能看到过去的眼镜。
