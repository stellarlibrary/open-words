# 🎶 cadent

🔊 /ˈkeɪdənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a rhythmic or measured flow; falling or descending in tone or movement
**中文：** 有节奏的；降落的；有韵律的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rhythmic（有节奏的）
- measured（有节制的）
- falling（下降的）
- melodious（悦耳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cadent tones / cadent rhythm / cadent voice / cadent melody**

👉 Example: The cadent tones of the evening hymn brought a profound sense of peace to the congregation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The speaker's **cadent** delivery held the audience spellbound as each phrase rose and fell with deliberate precision.
   演讲者抑扬顿挫的表达方式使听众如痴如醉，每个短语都精确地起伏跌宕。

2. Rain fell in a **cadent** pattern upon the ancient roof, creating a soothing and almost hypnotic natural percussion.
   雨水以有节奏的方式落在古老的屋顶上，形成了一种舒缓而近乎催眠的自然打击乐。

3. The **cadent** flow of the river over the terraced stones produced a gentle music that enchanted visiting travelers.
   河水沿阶梯状石头有节奏地流淌，发出轻柔的乐声，令来访的旅人陶醉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cadent" as the adjective form of "cadence" — something cadent has a cadence, a rhythmic, falling quality.
把"cadent"看作"cadence（节奏）"的形容词形式——有cadent特质的事物具有节奏感和起伏感。
