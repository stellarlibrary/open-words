# 💪 inure

🔊 /ɪˈnjʊr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To accustom or harden someone to something unpleasant through prolonged exposure
**中文：** v. 使习惯于；使适应；使坚强

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accustom（使习惯）
- habituate（使适应）
- harden（使坚强）
- desensitize（使麻木）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inure (someone) to hardship / inure to pain / inure to criticism / become inured to**

👉 Example: Years of working in emergency rooms had inured the surgeon to the sight of blood.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Growing up in extreme poverty had **inured** him to discomfort, making him remarkably resilient in adverse circumstances.
   在极度贫困中长大使他习惯了不适，使他在逆境中表现出非凡的韧性。

2. The soldiers became **inured** to the constant sound of artillery after spending several months on the front lines.
   在前线待了几个月后，士兵们已经习惯了持续不断的炮击声。

3. Critics argue that excessive media coverage of violence has **inured** the public to human suffering and tragedy.
   批评者认为，媒体对暴力的过度报道已使公众对人类的苦难和悲剧变得麻木。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-ure" as "in endure" — when you endure something long enough, you become inured to it.
把"inure"想成"in endure"——当你忍受某事足够久时，你就会习惯它。
