# 💥 explode

🔊 /ɪkˈsploʊd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To burst or shatter violently with a loud noise; to increase suddenly and dramatically; to express strong emotions abruptly
**中文：** v. 爆炸，爆发；激增

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detonate（引爆）
- erupt（爆发）
- burst（迸裂）
- surge（激增）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **explode with anger / explode in popularity / explode a myth / population explodes**

👉 Example: The demand for renewable energy has exploded in recent years due to growing environmental concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The population of the coastal city **exploded** during the economic boom, straining infrastructure and public services beyond capacity.
   这座沿海城市的人口在经济繁荣期间激增，使基础设施和公共服务不堪重负。

2. New research has effectively **exploded** the long-held myth that humans use only ten percent of their brain capacity.
   新的研究有效地推翻了人类只使用百分之十脑容量这一长期存在的误解。

3. The abandoned warehouse **exploded** without warning, sending debris across several city blocks and prompting an emergency evacuation.
   这座废弃仓库在毫无预警的情况下爆炸，碎片散落在好几个街区，引发了紧急疏散。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-plode" as "exit with a plod (loud sound)" — something bursts outward with great force and noise, like a sudden explosion.
把"explode"想成"exit with a plod（巨响）"——某物伴随着巨大的力量和声响向外迸裂，就像一场突然的爆炸。
