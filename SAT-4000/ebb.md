# 🌊 ebb

🔊 /ɛb/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The movement of the tide out to sea; a gradual decline or reduction. (v.) To recede or flow back; to gradually decrease or diminish
**中文：** n. 退潮；衰退 v. 退潮；减少；衰退

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recede（退去）
- wane（衰退）
- diminish（减少）
- subside（消退）
- decline（下降）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ebb and flow / ebb away / at a low ebb / ebb tide**

👉 Example: Public confidence in the institution continued to ebb as scandals were increasingly revealed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tide began to **ebb** just before sunset, revealing a vast expanse of glistening tidal pools along the shore.
   潮水在日落前开始退去，露出了海岸边大片闪闪发光的潮汐池。

2. Political support for the reform initiative **ebbed** significantly after economists published reports questioning its fiscal viability.
   在经济学家发表质疑其财政可行性的报告后，对改革倡议的政治支持大幅减退。

3. The **ebb** and flow of cultural trends reflects the ever-changing values and priorities of each successive generation.
   文化潮流的起伏反映了每一代人不断变化的价值观和优先事项。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ebb" as the opposite of "web" — a web grows and expands, but when you remove the "w," things shrink back and ebb away.
把"ebb"想成"web（网）"的反面——网会扩展变大，去掉"w"后，事物就会缩回、退去。
