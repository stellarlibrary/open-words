# 🌟 appreciate

🔊 /əˈpriːʃieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To recognize the full worth or significance of something; to be grateful for; to understand fully; to increase in value over time
**中文：** 欣赏；感激；领会；增值

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- value（重视）
- cherish（珍惜）
- acknowledge（认可）
- comprehend（领会）
- treasure（珍视）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **appreciate the effort / appreciate the significance / appreciate in value / greatly appreciate / fully appreciate**

👉 Example: I would greatly appreciate your assistance in reviewing the final draft of this report.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board members fully **appreciate** the complexity of the situation and have requested additional time to deliberate.
   董事会成员充分领会了局势的复杂性，并要求额外的时间进行审议。

2. Properties in the downtown district have **appreciated** significantly in value over the past five years due to urban renewal.
   由于城市更新，市中心地区的房产在过去五年中大幅增值。

3. We sincerely **appreciate** your generous contribution, which will fund scholarships for underprivileged students this academic year.
   我们衷心感谢您的慷慨捐助，这将为本学年贫困学生提供奖学金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "appreciate" as "a-PRECI-ate" — "preci" comes from Latin "pretium" meaning "price or value," so to appreciate is to recognize the true price or value of something.
把"appreciate"想成"a-PRECI-ate"——"preci"源自拉丁语"pretium"（价格或价值），所以appreciate就是认识到某物的真正价值。
