# ⚠️ abnormal

🔊 /æbˈnɔːrməl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deviating from what is normal, usual, or expected; not conforming to standard patterns or norms
**中文：** adj. 反常的，不规则的；不正常的；变态的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unusual（不寻常的）
- irregular（不规则的）
- anomalous（异常的）
- deviant（偏离的）
- atypical（非典型的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abnormal behavior / abnormal results / abnormal growth / abnormal psychology**

👉 Example: The doctor ordered further tests after detecting abnormal levels of white blood cells.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers identified **abnormal** patterns of brain activity in patients suffering from chronic sleep deprivation.
   研究人员在长期睡眠不足的患者中发现了异常的大脑活动模式。

2. The **abnormal** weather conditions this winter have caused widespread damage to agricultural crops across the region.
   今年冬天的异常天气状况对该地区的农作物造成了大面积损害。

3. Inspectors found **abnormal** stress fractures in the bridge's support beams during their routine safety assessment.
   检查人员在例行安全评估中发现桥梁支撑梁存在异常应力裂缝。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ab-" means "away from" + "normal" — literally "away from normal," something that deviates from the standard.
"Ab-"意为"远离"+ "normal"（正常的）——字面意思是"远离正常"，偏离标准的事物。
