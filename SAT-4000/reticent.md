# 🤐 reticent

🔊 /ˈretɪsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily; reserved, restrained, or reluctant to speak
**中文：** adj. 沉默的；沉默寡言的；有保留的；谨慎的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- taciturn（沉默寡言的）
- reserved（矜持的）
- uncommunicative（不爱说话的）
- tight-lipped（守口如瓶的）
- secretive（隐秘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reticent about / reticent to speak / remain reticent / naturally reticent**

👉 Example: The witness remained reticent about the details of the incident during the entire investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senior official remained remarkably **reticent** about the government's strategic plans during the nationally televised press conference.
   这位高级官员在全国转播的新闻发布会上对政府的战略计划保持了异常的沉默。

2. Many gifted students are **reticent** to participate in classroom discussions, preferring instead to express their ideas through writing.
   许多有天赋的学生不愿参加课堂讨论，而更倾向于通过写作来表达他们的想法。

3. The author was characteristically **reticent** about her personal life, rarely granting interviews or making public appearances.
   这位作家一贯对个人生活守口如瓶，很少接受采访或公开露面。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "RETI-cent" — "reti" sounds like "retreat" — a reticent person retreats into silence rather than speaking up.
把"reticent"想成"RETI-cent"——"reti"听起来像"retreat"（退缩）——沉默寡言的人退缩到沉默中而不是开口说话。
