# 💢 twinge

🔊 /twɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sudden, sharp localized pain; a brief pang of emotion such as guilt or regret. (v.) To cause a sharp, stinging pain
**中文：** n. 阵痛；刺痛；悔恨 v. 使刺痛；感到刺痛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pang（剧痛）
- stab（刺痛）
- sting（刺痛感）
- spasm（痉挛）
- prick（刺痛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a twinge of guilt / a twinge of pain / a twinge of regret / feel a twinge**

👉 Example: He felt a twinge of guilt after forgetting his friend's birthday.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. She experienced a sudden **twinge** of remorse upon realizing how her careless remarks had wounded her colleague.
   她意识到自己不经意的话语伤害了同事时，突然感到一阵悔恨的刺痛。

2. The athlete ignored a sharp **twinge** in his knee during the match, which later proved to be a serious ligament injury.
   运动员在比赛中忽略了膝盖的一阵剧痛，后来证明是严重的韧带损伤。

3. A **twinge** of anxiety passed through her as she approached the podium to deliver her first public address.
   当她走向讲台准备发表第一次公开演讲时，一阵焦虑掠过她的心头。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "twinge" as "twin + ge(t)" — like getting a twin stab of pain, a sharp double prick that makes you wince.
把"twinge"想成"twin（双重）+ ge(t)（得到）"——像是得到一次双重刺痛，让你不禁皱眉。
