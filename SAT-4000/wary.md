# 👁️ wary

🔊 /ˈwɛri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling or showing caution about possible dangers or problems; watchful and alert
**中文：** adj. 谨慎的；机警的；惟恐的；考虑周到的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cautious（谨慎的）
- vigilant（警惕的）
- circumspect（小心的）
- guarded（有戒心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wary of something / wary about doing something / wary eye / wary traveler**

👉 Example: Experienced investors remain wary of market trends that appear too favorable to be genuine.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Consumers have grown increasingly **wary** of sharing personal data online following a series of high-profile security breaches.
   在一系列引人关注的安全漏洞事件后，消费者对在网上分享个人数据变得越来越谨慎。

2. The diplomat maintained a **wary** stance throughout the negotiations, carefully evaluating each proposal before offering any response.
   这位外交官在整个谈判过程中保持谨慎立场，仔细评估每项提案后才作出回应。

3. Scientists are **wary** of drawing premature conclusions from a single experiment without sufficient replication and peer review.
   科学家们对从一次实验中草率得出结论持谨慎态度，认为需要充分的重复和同行评审。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wary" as "war-y" — in a war, you must always be watchful and cautious to stay safe.
把"wary"想成"war-y"——在战争中，你必须时刻保持警惕和谨慎才能安全。
