# 💭 absent-minded

🔊 /ˌæbsəntˈmaɪndɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Habitually forgetful or inattentive; lost in thought and unaware of one's surroundings
**中文：** adj. 心不在焉的，出神的；健忘的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- forgetful（健忘的）
- distracted（分心的）
- inattentive（不注意的）
- preoccupied（全神贯注于别事的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **absent-minded professor / absent-minded mistake / become absent-minded / seem absent-minded**

👉 Example: The absent-minded professor left his briefcase on the train for the third time this month.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The brilliant but **absent-minded** scientist frequently forgot appointments while absorbed in her research.
   这位才华横溢但心不在焉的科学家经常因沉浸于研究而忘记约会。

2. His **absent-minded** habit of misplacing important documents created constant frustration among his colleagues.
   他心不在焉地弄丢重要文件的习惯让同事们一直很恼火。

3. She gave an **absent-minded** nod during the meeting, clearly preoccupied with thoughts about another matter.
   她在会议中心不在焉地点了点头，显然在想着另一件事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
The word is self-explanatory: "absent" + "minded" — your mind is absent, somewhere else entirely. Your body is here but your thoughts have wandered away.
这个词不言自明："absent"（不在的）+"minded"（心智的）——你的心不在这里，完全在别处。身体在这里，但思绪已经飘走了。
