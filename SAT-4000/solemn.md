# 🎭 solemn

🔊 /ˈsɒləm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Dignified and serious in manner or character; formal and ceremonious; deeply earnest
**中文：** adj. 庄严的，隆重的；严肃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grave（严肃的）
- somber（庄重的）
- earnest（认真的）
- stately（庄严的）
- sober（严肃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solemn occasion / solemn oath / solemn promise / solemn expression**

👉 Example: The judge maintained a solemn expression throughout the entire sentencing proceeding.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomats exchanged **solemn** vows of mutual cooperation during the historic treaty-signing ceremony at the capital.
   外交官们在首都的历史性条约签署仪式上郑重交换了相互合作的誓言。

2. A **solemn** silence fell over the audience as the orchestra performed the memorial composition for the fallen soldiers.
   当管弦乐队演奏纪念阵亡将士的乐曲时，观众席上陷入了庄严的沉默。

3. The professor adopted a **solemn** tone when discussing the ethical implications of emerging biotechnological research methods.
   教授在讨论新兴生物技术研究方法的伦理影响时，采用了严肃的语气。
