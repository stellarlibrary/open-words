# ⚖️ argumentation

🔊 /ˌɑːrɡjuˌmɛnˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of forming reasons, making inductions, and drawing conclusions; systematic reasoning in support of a position
**中文：** 论证；争论；辩论；推理过程

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reasoning（推理）
- debate（辩论）
- discourse（论述）
- disputation（争辩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **line of argumentation / logical argumentation / legal argumentation / process of argumentation**

👉 Example: The lawyer's argumentation was so compelling that the jury reached a unanimous verdict within an hour.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher's **argumentation** relied on a series of carefully constructed logical premises leading to an irrefutable conclusion.
   这位哲学家的**论证**依赖于一系列精心构建的逻辑前提，最终得出了无可辩驳的结论。

2. Effective **argumentation** in academic writing requires credible evidence, clear reasoning, and anticipation of counterarguments.
   学术写作中有效的**论证**需要可靠的证据、清晰的推理以及对反驳观点的预判。

3. The students practiced formal **argumentation** techniques during the national debate competition held at the university campus.
   学生们在大学校园举行的全国辩论赛中练习了正式的**辩论**技巧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "argumentation" as "argument + ation" — the action or process of making arguments in a structured, formal way.
把"argumentation"想成"argument + ation"——以结构化、正式的方式进行论证的行为或过程。
