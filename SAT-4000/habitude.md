# 📜 habitude

🔊 /ˈhæbɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A typical condition, state, or appearance; a habitual tendency, custom, or way of behaving
**中文：** n. 习俗；习惯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- custom（习俗）
- habit（习惯）
- convention（惯例）
- tendency（倾向）
- disposition（性情）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **habitude of mind / habitude of thought / cultural habitude / ingrained habitude**

👉 Example: The philosopher examined the habitudes of mind that shape our perception of reality and moral judgment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologist studied the social **habitudes** of the remote tribal community to understand their collective decision-making processes.
   人类学家研究了偏远部落社区的社会习俗，以了解他们的集体决策过程。

2. Years of rigorous academic training had instilled in her a **habitude** of critical inquiry that permeated all aspects of life.
   多年严格的学术训练在她心中培养了一种批判性探究的习惯，渗透到生活的方方面面。

3. The colonial administration sought to replace indigenous **habitudes** with European customs, often disregarding centuries of cultural tradition.
   殖民政府试图用欧洲习俗取代土著习俗，往往无视数百年的文化传统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "habitude" as "habit + attitude" — your habitual attitude or customary way of doing things in daily life.
把"habitude"想成"habit（习惯）+ attitude（态度）"——你习惯性的态度或日常生活中惯常的做事方式。
