# 🗺️ area

🔊 /ˈɛəriə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A region or section of space; the extent of a surface; a particular subject or range of activity
**中文：** 区域，地区；面积；范围；领域

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- region（区域）
- domain（领域）
- zone（地带）
- territory（领土）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **area of expertise / residential area / surface area / area of study**

👉 Example: The professor specialized in the area of molecular biology and published extensively on genetic mutation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The municipal government designated a large **area** of the waterfront for commercial development and public recreation.
   市政府将滨水区的大片**区域**划定为商业开发和公共休闲用途。

2. Her **area** of expertise encompasses international trade law and the resolution of complex cross-border disputes.
   她的专业**领域**涵盖国际贸易法以及复杂跨境争端的解决。

3. Scientists calculated the total surface **area** of the newly discovered coral reef to assess its ecological significance.
   科学家们计算了新发现的珊瑚礁的总**面积**，以评估其生态意义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "area" as the space you "air out" — an open space or region where air flows freely.
把"area"想成你"通风"的地方——一个空气自由流通的开放空间或区域。
