# 🌅 resurrection

🔊 /ˌrezəˈrekʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of rising from the dead; a revival or restoration of something to use, activity, or prominence
**中文：** n. 复活；恢复；复兴；复苏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revival（复兴）
- rebirth（重生）
- resurgence（复苏）
- renewal（更新）
- restoration（恢复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resurrection of hopes / resurrection of a tradition / spiritual resurrection / political resurrection**

👉 Example: The resurrection of the ancient festival brought renewed pride to the local community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **resurrection** of the abandoned urban district required years of careful planning and substantial financial investment.
   这个废弃城区的复兴需要多年的精心规划和大量资金投入。

2. Scholars continue to debate the historical significance of the **resurrection** narrative in early theological literature.
   学者们继续争论复活叙事在早期神学文献中的历史意义。

3. The director's latest film marks the **resurrection** of a genre that critics had long considered obsolete and irrelevant.
   导演的最新影片标志着一个长期被评论界认为过时且无关紧要的类型的复兴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-sur-rection" as "re-" (again) + "surge" (rise) + "-tion" — something that surges up again, rising back to life.
把"resurrection"想成"re-"（再次）+ "surge"（涌起）+ "-tion"——某物再次涌起，重新恢复生机。
