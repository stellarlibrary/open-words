# 🔙 revert

🔊 /rɪˈvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To return to a former condition, practice, or belief; to bring up again. (n.) One who reverts to a previous state
**中文：** v. 回复，回返

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- return（返回）
- regress（退回）
- relapse（复发）
- backslide（退步）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **revert to / revert back to / revert to type / revert to the original**

👉 Example: After the experiment failed, the researchers decided to revert to the original methodology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Without consistent enforcement, communities often **revert** to old habits that undermine the progress achieved by reform initiatives.
   如果没有持续的执行力，社区往往会回复到破坏改革成果的旧习惯。

2. The board voted unanimously to **revert** to the previous policy framework after the new regulations proved ineffective.
   在新法规被证明无效后，董事会一致投票决定回复到之前的政策框架。

3. Scholars have observed that politically unstable nations tend to **revert** to authoritarian governance during periods of economic crisis.
   学者们观察到，政治不稳定的国家在经济危机期间往往会回复到威权治理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-" (back) + "vert" (turn, as in "vertical") — to turn back to where you started.
把"revert"想成"re-（回）+ vert（转，如vertical中的词根）"——转回到你开始的地方。
