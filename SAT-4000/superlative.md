# 🏆 superlative

🔊 /suːˈpɜːrlətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of the highest quality or degree; of or relating to the grammatical superlative. (n.) The highest degree; the best person; an exaggerated expression
**中文：** adj. 最高的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supreme（至高的）
- paramount（最重要的）
- unsurpassed（无与伦比的）
- preeminent（卓越的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **superlative performance / superlative quality / in the superlative / superlative degree**

👉 Example: The orchestra delivered a superlative performance that left the entire audience speechless.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee awarded the prize to the candidate who demonstrated **superlative** leadership skills throughout the rigorous selection process.
   委员会将奖项授予了在严格的遴选过程中展现出最高领导力的候选人。

2. Critics praised the novelist's **superlative** command of language, noting her ability to craft vivid and memorable prose.
   评论家们称赞这位小说家最高超的语言驾驭能力，指出她创作生动而令人难忘的散文的才华。

3. The research institution has earned a **superlative** reputation for producing groundbreaking studies in molecular biology.
   该研究机构因在分子生物学领域产出开创性研究而赢得了最高的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "super-lative" as "super" + "relative" — the superlative is the SUPER form when comparing relatives, the absolute highest.
把"superlative"想成"super"（超级）+ "relative"（相对的）——最高级就是比较中的超级形式，绝对的最高。
