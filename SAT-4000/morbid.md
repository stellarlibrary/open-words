# 😷 morbid

🔊 /ˈmɔːrbɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Characterized by an abnormal and unhealthy interest in disturbing subjects, especially death and disease; of the nature of or indicative of disease
**中文：** adj. 病态的；由病引起的；恐怖的；病变部位的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- macabre（恐怖的）
- ghoulish（令人毛骨悚然的）
- unwholesome（不健康的）
- pathological（病态的）
- grim（阴森的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **morbid curiosity / morbid fascination / morbid thoughts / morbid obsession**

👉 Example: She had a morbid fascination with abandoned hospitals and the histories of their former patients.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective's **morbid** attention to detail at crime scenes made him exceptionally effective but emotionally exhausted over time.
   这位侦探在犯罪现场病态般地关注细节，使他异常高效，但随着时间推移在情感上精疲力竭。

2. Critics argued that the documentary displayed a **morbid** preoccupation with suffering rather than offering constructive solutions to the crisis.
   批评人士认为这部纪录片表现出对苦难的病态关注，而没有为危机提供建设性的解决方案。

3. The physician noted that prolonged exposure to stress can trigger **morbid** changes in both cardiovascular function and mental health.
   医生指出，长期暴露在压力下会引起心血管功能和心理健康的病理性变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "morbid" as related to "morb" (Latin morbus = disease) — something morbid is connected to disease, death, or unhealthy obsession.
"morbid"与拉丁语"morbus"（疾病）有关——病态的事物与疾病、死亡或不健康的执念相关。
