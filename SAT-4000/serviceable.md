# 🔧 serviceable

🔊 /ˈsɜːrvɪsəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Useful and practical, though not particularly attractive; able to be used; durable and long-lasting
**中文：** adj. 耐用的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- functional（实用的）
- durable（耐用的）
- practical（实际的）
- usable（可用的）
- sturdy（结实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **serviceable condition / perfectly serviceable / serviceable equipment / serviceable clothing**

👉 Example: Although the coat was old, it was still perfectly serviceable for the harsh winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bridge, though constructed decades ago, remained entirely **serviceable** and continued to support heavy commercial traffic daily.
   这座桥虽然建于几十年前，但仍然完全可用，每天继续承载繁重的商业交通。

2. The committee concluded that the existing infrastructure was **serviceable** enough to meet the community's needs for another decade.
   委员会得出结论，现有的基础设施足够耐用，可以再满足社区十年的需求。

3. Her writing style, while not particularly elegant, was thoroughly **serviceable** for conveying complex scientific ideas clearly.
   她的写作风格虽然不够优雅，但对于清晰传达复杂的科学理念来说完全实用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "serviceable" as "service + able" — something able to provide service, still working and useful even if not brand new.
把"serviceable"想成"service + able"——能够提供服务的，即使不是全新的也仍然能用且实用。
