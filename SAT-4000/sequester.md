# 🔒 sequester

🔊 /sɪˈkwestər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To isolate or hide away; to withdraw into seclusion; to seize or confiscate by legal authority
**中文：** v. 使隐退；使隔绝；扣押

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- isolate（隔离）
- seclude（使隐退）
- confiscate（没收）
- segregate（隔开）
- impound（扣押）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sequester a jury / sequester carbon / sequester funds / sequester oneself**

👉 Example: The judge decided to sequester the jury to prevent media coverage from influencing their deliberations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court ordered officials to **sequester** the jury for the duration of the high-profile trial to ensure impartial deliberation.
   法庭下令在这场备受关注的审判期间将陪审团隔离，以确保公正的审议。

2. Environmental scientists are developing new methods to **sequester** carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and store it deep underground.
   环境科学家正在开发从大气中隔离二氧化碳并将其储存在地下深处的新方法。

3. The scholar chose to **sequester** herself in the university library for an entire semester to complete her doctoral dissertation.
   这位学者选择将自己隔绝在大学图书馆整整一个学期以完成她的博士论文。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sequester" as "se-quest-er" — you go on a quest alone, separating yourself from others, seeking seclusion.
把"sequester"想成"se-quest-er"——你独自踏上探索之旅，与他人隔离，寻求隐居。
