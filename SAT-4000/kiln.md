# 🔥 kiln

🔊 /kɪln/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A furnace or oven used for burning, baking, or drying materials such as pottery, bricks, or lime. (v.) To burn or fire in a kiln
**中文：** n. 窑；炉；干燥炉 v. 烧窑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- furnace（熔炉）
- oven（烤炉）
- forge（锻炉）
- hearth（炉膛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **kiln-dried / brick kiln / pottery kiln / fire in a kiln / lime kiln**

👉 Example: The artisan placed her ceramic vases into the kiln for the final stage of production.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient **kiln** discovered at the archaeological site provided crucial evidence of early industrial activity in the region.
   在考古遗址发现的古代窑炉为该地区早期工业活动提供了关键证据。

2. Craftsmen carefully monitored the temperature inside the **kiln** to ensure the pottery achieved the desired strength and glaze.
   工匠们仔细监控窑内温度，以确保陶器达到所需的强度和釉面效果。

3. The lumber company uses a large **kiln** to dry freshly cut timber before it is shipped to furniture manufacturers.
   木材公司使用大型干燥炉烘干新伐木材，之后再运送给家具制造商。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "kiln" as "kill-n" — the intense heat inside a kiln could "kill" any moisture or weakness, leaving only hardened material.
把"kiln"想成"kill-n"——窑内的高温能"杀灭"所有水分和脆弱性，只留下硬化的材料。
