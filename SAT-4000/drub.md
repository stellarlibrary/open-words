# 👊 drub

🔊 /drʌb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To beat with a stick or club; to criticize harshly; to defeat decisively
**中文：** v. 用棒打；猛烈批评；彻底击败

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thrash（痛打）
- berate（严厉批评）
- pummel（连续猛击）
- trounce（痛击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **drub an opponent / drub someone into submission / drub mercilessly**

👉 Example: The debate team was drubbed by their rivals in the final round of the competition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial columnist proceeded to **drub** the mayor's controversial housing proposal with a series of devastating arguments.
   专栏评论员以一系列毁灭性的论点猛烈批评了市长有争议的住房提案。

2. The incumbent political party was thoroughly **drubbed** in the general election, losing nearly every contested legislative seat.
   执政党在大选中被彻底击败，几乎失去了每一个有争议的立法席位。

3. Critics **drubbed** the author's latest novel for its shallow characterization and implausible narrative structure throughout.
   评论家们猛烈批评了这位作者最新小说中肤浅的人物刻画和不合理的叙事结构。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "drub" as "drum + club" — imagine someone beating a drum so hard with a club that it sounds like a harsh punishment.
把"drub"想成"drum + club"——想象有人用棍棒猛敲鼓，发出严厉惩罚般的声响。
