# 😌 composed

🔊 /kəmˈpoʊzd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Calm and in control of one's feelings; having a tranquil and self-possessed demeanor
**中文：** adj. 镇静的；沉着的；泰然自若的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- calm（冷静的）
- collected（镇定的）
- serene（平静的）
- unruffled（从容的）
- poised（沉着的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remain composed / stay composed / appear composed / calm and composed**

👉 Example: Despite the chaos around her, the surgeon remained perfectly composed throughout the emergency procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The CEO remained remarkably **composed** during the press conference despite facing aggressive questioning about the company's financial losses.
   尽管在新闻发布会上面临关于公司财务亏损的尖锐提问，首席执行官仍然保持着非凡的镇定。

2. A truly **composed** leader can inspire confidence among team members even during periods of extreme organizational uncertainty.
   一个真正沉着的领导者即使在组织极度不确定的时期也能激发团队成员的信心。

3. The defendant appeared remarkably **composed** in the courtroom, showing no visible signs of distress as the verdict was announced.
   被告在法庭上显得异常镇定，当宣布判决时没有表现出任何明显的焦虑迹象。

想想一位"作曲家"写音乐——他们必须在压力下保持冷静和"沉着"才能创作出和谐的作品。
