# 🌟 exemplary

🔊 /ɪɡˈzempləri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Serving as a desirable model; representing the best of its kind; serving as a warning or deterrent
**中文：** adj. 典范的；可仿效的；惩戒性的；典型的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- model（模范的）
- outstanding（杰出的）
- commendable（值得称赞的）
- praiseworthy（值得表扬的）
- ideal（理想的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exemplary behavior / exemplary conduct / exemplary performance / exemplary damages**

👉 Example: The court awarded exemplary damages to deter similar misconduct in the future.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor received a teaching award for her **exemplary** dedication to mentoring undergraduate students throughout her career.
   这位教授因其在整个职业生涯中对指导本科生的典范奉献而获得教学奖。

2. His **exemplary** conduct during the crisis earned him the respect and admiration of colleagues across the institution.
   他在危机期间的模范行为赢得了整个机构同事们的尊重和钦佩。

3. The organization published an **exemplary** report that set the standard for transparency in corporate governance practices.
   该组织发布了一份堪称典范的报告，为公司治理实践中的透明度树立了标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exemplary" as "example-worthy" — something so good it deserves to be used as an example for others.
把"exemplary"想成"example-worthy"——某事物优秀到值得成为他人的榜样。
