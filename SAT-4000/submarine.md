# 🚢 submarine

🔊 /ˈsʌbməˌriːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A watercraft designed to operate underwater. (adj.) Existing or occurring beneath the surface of the sea. (v.) To attack with a submarine
**中文：** adj. 水下的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- underwater（水下的）
- subaquatic（水下的）
- undersea（海底的）
- submerged（沉没的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **submarine cable / submarine warfare / submarine volcano / nuclear submarine**

👉 Example: The submarine cable network spanning the Atlantic Ocean carries the vast majority of intercontinental digital communications.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The naval fleet deployed a nuclear **submarine** to patrol the contested waters and deter potential adversaries from encroaching on territorial boundaries.
   海军舰队部署了一艘核潜水艇在争议水域巡逻，以阻止潜在对手侵犯领土边界。

2. Scientists discovered a previously unknown **submarine** volcanic ridge that extended for hundreds of kilometers beneath the Pacific Ocean floor.
   科学家们发现了一条此前未知的海底火山脊，在太平洋海底延伸了数百公里。

3. The installation of new **submarine** fiber-optic cables significantly improved data transmission speeds between the two continents for commercial users.
   新海底光缆的铺设显著提高了两大洲之间商业用户的数据传输速度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-marine" as "sub" (under) + "marine" (sea) — something that operates or exists under the sea, like a vessel diving beneath the waves.
把"submarine"想成"sub"（在下面）+"marine"（海洋的）——在海面以下运作或存在的东西，就像潜入波浪之下的船只。
