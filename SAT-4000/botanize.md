# 🌱 botanize

🔊 /ˈbɒtənaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To study plants scientifically; to collect and examine plant specimens in their natural habitat
**中文：** v. 研究植物；采集植物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- collect specimens（采集标本）
- study flora（研究植物群）
- herborize（采集植物）
- survey plants（调查植物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **botanize in the field / botanize along the river / go botanizing / botanize through the woods**

👉 Example: The professor encouraged her students to botanize along the mountain trails during the weekend field excursion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Charles Darwin would frequently **botanize** during his voyages, meticulously cataloging every unfamiliar plant species he encountered abroad.
   查尔斯·达尔文在航行期间经常采集植物，细致地编录他在海外遇到的每一种陌生植物。

2. The graduate students spent the entire summer **botanizing** in the remote highlands to gather specimens for their doctoral research.
   研究生们整个夏天都在偏远高地采集植物，为他们的博士研究收集标本。

3. Amateur naturalists often **botanize** in local meadows and wetlands, contributing valuable observational data to regional biodiversity databases.
   业余博物学家经常在当地的草地和湿地采集研究植物，为区域生物多样性数据库贡献有价值的观察数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "botanize" as "botany + ize" — to "do botany," meaning to go out and actively study or collect plants like a botanist.
把"botanize"想成"botany + ize"——去"做植物学"，意思是像植物学家一样出去主动研究或采集植物。
