# 🐦 aviary

🔊 /ˈeɪviˌɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A large enclosure, cage, or building designed for keeping and breeding birds; a place where birds are housed and displayed.

**中文：** 鸟类饲养场；大型鸟舍。专门为饲养和繁殖鸟类而设计的大型围栏、笼子或建筑物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- birdhouse （鸟舍）
- bird sanctuary （鸟类保护区）
- bird enclosure （鸟类围栏）
- volary （鸟笼，鸟舍）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Aviary" comes from the Latin "avis" meaning "bird." It specifically refers to an enclosed space large enough for birds to fly around in, distinguishing it from a simple birdcage. It is commonly found in zoos, botanical gardens, and conservation centers.

"aviary"源自拉丁语"avis"，意为"鸟"。它特指一个足够大、可以让鸟类在其中飞翔的封闭空间，与普通鸟笼不同。常见于动物园、植物园和保护中心。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanical garden recently constructed a magnificent **aviary** housing over two hundred species of tropical birds.
   *该植物园最近建造了一座壮观的**鸟舍**，容纳了两百多种热带鸟类。*

2. Visitors to the **aviary** were astonished by the vibrant plumage and melodious songs of the exotic birds.
   *参观**鸟舍**的游客对珍奇鸟类的鲜艳羽毛和悦耳歌声感到惊叹。*

3. The conservation program established a new **aviary** dedicated to breeding endangered parrots for eventual release.
   *该保护项目建立了一座新的**鸟类饲养场**，专门用于繁殖濒危鹦鹉以便日后放归野外。*

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Aviary" starts with "avi-" like "aviation" (flying). Both words come from "avis" (bird). An aviary is where birds do their own "aviation" — flying freely inside an enclosure.

"aviary"以"avi-"开头，就像"aviation"（航空）。这两个词都来自"avis"（鸟）。鸟舍就是鸟儿进行自己的"航空"——在围栏内自由飞翔的地方。
