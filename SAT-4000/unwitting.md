# 🙈 unwitting

🔊 /ʌnˈwɪtɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not aware of the full facts; done without knowledge or intention; unintentional
**中文：** adj. 不知情的；不知不觉的；无意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unaware（不知道的）
- inadvertent（无意的）
- unconscious（不自觉的）
- unsuspecting（未起疑心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unwitting accomplice / unwitting victim / unwitting participant / unwitting mistake**

👉 Example: The employee became an unwitting accomplice in the financial scheme orchestrated by senior executives.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many consumers were **unwitting** participants in the data collection practices employed by the social media platform.
   许多消费者不知不觉地参与了该社交媒体平台所采用的数据收集活动。

2. The journalist discovered that local residents had been **unwitting** victims of industrial contamination for over a decade.
   记者发现当地居民十多年来一直是工业污染的不知情受害者。

3. His **unwitting** disclosure of classified information during the interview prompted an immediate internal investigation by authorities.
   他在采访中无意泄露机密信息，促使当局立即展开内部调查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-wit-ting" — without wit or knowledge. If you lack wit about something, you are unaware of it.
把"unwitting"想成"没有知觉的"——un（没有）+ wit（知道）+ ting，不知道就是不知情的。
