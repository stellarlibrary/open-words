# 🤢 fetid

🔊 /ˈfetɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Smelling extremely unpleasant; having a stale and rotten stench
**中文：** adj. 恶臭的；腐臭的；有恶臭的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- putrid（腐臭的）
- foul（恶臭的）
- malodorous（有恶臭的）
- rank（难闻的）
- stinking（发臭的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fetid odor / fetid air / fetid water / fetid swamp / fetid smell**

👉 Example: The fetid swamp discouraged explorers from venturing further into the dense wetlands.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigators discovered a **fetid** pool of stagnant water in the basement that had caused widespread contamination.
   调查人员在地下室发现了一池发出恶臭的死水，已造成大范围污染。

2. The **fetid** conditions inside the overcrowded prison prompted humanitarian organizations to demand immediate governmental reform.
   监狱过度拥挤造成的恶臭环境促使人道主义组织要求政府立即进行改革。

3. A **fetid** smell emanating from the abandoned factory alerted residents to the illegal dumping of hazardous chemical waste.
   从废弃工厂散发出的恶臭使居民注意到了非法倾倒有害化学废物的行为。
