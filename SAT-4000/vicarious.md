# 🪞 vicarious

🔊 /vaɪˈkɛriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Experienced through imaginative participation in the experience of another; acting or serving in place of someone else
**中文：** adj. 代理的；间接感受到的；替代的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indirect（间接的）
- surrogate（替代的）
- secondhand（二手的）
- empathetic（感同身受的）
- delegated（委托的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vicarious pleasure / vicarious experience / live vicariously / vicarious thrill**

👉 Example: Many readers derive vicarious pleasure from the adventures described in travel memoirs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Parents often experience a **vicarious** sense of accomplishment when their children achieve significant academic or athletic milestones.
   当孩子取得重大学业或体育成就时，父母往往会体验到一种**间接的**成就感。

2. The documentary provided audiences with a **vicarious** glimpse into the daily struggles faced by refugees crossing dangerous borders.
   这部纪录片让观众**间接地**窥见了难民穿越危险边境所面临的日常挣扎。

3. She lived **vicariously** through her sister's letters, imagining the exotic landscapes and cultures described in vivid detail.
   她通过姐姐的信件**间接地**体验生活，想象着信中生动描述的异国风景和文化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vicarious" as "vicar-ious" — a vicar acts on behalf of someone else, so vicarious means experiencing something through another person.
把"vicarious"想成"vicar-ious"——vicar（代理人）代替他人行事，所以vicarious意为通过他人间接体验。
