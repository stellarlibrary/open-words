# 📜 voluminous

🔊 /vəˈluːmɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Very large in volume or extent; occupying much space; consisting of many volumes or written at great length
**中文：** adj. 大量的；多卷的；长篇的；容量大的；庞大的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- copious（大量的）
- extensive（广泛的）
- prolific（多产的）
- abundant（丰富的）
- capacious（宽敞的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **voluminous correspondence / voluminous notes / voluminous skirt / voluminous records**

👉 Example: The professor's voluminous notes on the subject filled an entire filing cabinet in her office.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian spent years sifting through the **voluminous** archives to reconstruct a comprehensive account of the era.
   这位历史学家花了数年时间筛选大量档案，以重建对那个时代的全面叙述。

2. Her **voluminous** research on climate change was published across multiple journals and academic anthologies.
   她关于气候变化的大量研究发表在多种期刊和学术文集上。

3. The defendant's attorney presented **voluminous** evidence during the trial to challenge the prosecution's central argument.
   被告律师在庭审中出示了大量证据，以质疑控方的核心论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voluminous" as "volume + ous" — something with great volume or size, like voluminous robes that billow with fabric.
把"voluminous"想成"volume + ous"——拥有巨大体积或规模的东西，就像飘逸着大量布料的宽大长袍。
