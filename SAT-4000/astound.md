# 😲 astound

🔊 /əˈstaʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To shock or greatly surprise someone; to overwhelm with amazement or disbelief.
**中文：** 使惊骇；使震惊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astonish（使惊讶）
- stagger（使震惊）
- stupefy（使目瞪口呆）
- amaze（使惊奇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **astound someone**, **astound the audience/world/critics**

👉 Example: The magician's final act never fails to astound even the most skeptical members of the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist's groundbreaking discovery **astounded** the entire academic community beyond all expectations.
   这位科学家的突破性发现震惊了整个学术界，超出了所有人的预期。

2. Her remarkable ability to solve complex equations **astounded** professors and fellow students alike.
   她解决复杂方程的非凡能力令教授和同学们都感到震惊。

3. The sheer scale of the ancient ruins **astounded** every visitor who entered the archaeological site.
   古代遗迹的宏大规模令每一位进入考古遗址的游客都感到惊骇。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ASTOUND" contains "STUN" rearranged — when you are astounded, you are essentially stunned by surprise.
"ASTOUND"中包含"STUN（击晕）"的变形——当你被astound时，就像被惊喜击晕了一样，目瞪口呆。
