# 📜 cession

🔊 /ˈseʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The formal giving up or yielding of rights, property, or territory by one party to another, especially by a state or government
**中文：** n. （权利的）转让，出让；（领土的）割让

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surrender（让渡）
- relinquishment（放弃）
- concession（让步）
- transfer（转让）
- yielding（让与）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cession of territory / cession of rights / land cession / treaty of cession**

👉 Example: The defeated nation was compelled to agree to the cession of its northern provinces under the terms of the treaty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **cession** of Louisiana from France to the United States in 1803 dramatically doubled the territorial extent of the young republic.
   1803年法国向美国割让路易斯安那，使这个年轻共和国的领土面积急剧翻倍。

2. International law scholars continue to debate the legality of forced territorial **cession** imposed upon defeated nations through coercive treaties.
   国际法学者继续争论通过强制性条约对战败国施加的强迫领土割让的合法性。

3. The **cession** of mineral rights to the foreign corporation sparked widespread protests among the indigenous communities of the region.
   将矿产权转让给外国公司在该地区的原住民社区中引发了广泛的抗议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cession" as related to "cede" (to yield or give up) — a cession is the act of ceding territory or rights. Do not confuse it with "session" (a meeting).
把"cession"想成与"cede"（让出、放弃）相关——cession就是割让领土或转让权利的行为。不要与"session"（会议）混淆。
