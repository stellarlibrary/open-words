# ⏳ onset

🔊 /ˈɒnset/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The beginning or start of something, especially something unpleasant; an attack or assault
**中文：** n. 开始，着手；发作；攻击，进攻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beginning（开始）
- commencement（开端）
- outbreak（爆发）
- attack（攻击）
- inception（起始）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **onset of disease / onset of winter / onset of symptoms / onset of war**

👉 Example: The onset of symptoms typically occurs within forty-eight hours of initial exposure to the virus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers are studying factors that may delay the **onset** of neurodegenerative diseases in genetically predisposed individuals.
   研究人员正在研究可能延缓遗传易感个体神经退行性疾病发作的因素。

2. The **onset** of the economic recession forced many corporations to implement drastic cost-cutting measures across all departments.
   经济衰退的到来迫使许多公司在所有部门实施大幅削减成本的措施。

3. With the **onset** of winter, migratory birds begin their long journey southward to warmer and more hospitable climates.
   随着冬天的来临，候鸟开始向南方温暖宜人的气候地区进行长途迁徙。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "on + set" — when something is SET ON, it begins, like a timer that has been set and starts counting.
把"onset"想成"on + set"——当某事被启动（set on），它就开始了，就像一个被设定好并开始计时的定时器。
