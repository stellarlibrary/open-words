# 🧹 scour

🔊 /skaʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To wash or scrub vigorously; to erode by flowing water; to cause diarrhea. (n.) The act of scrubbing; a cleaning agent; diarrhea in livestock
**中文：** v. 擦洗；冲刷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scrub（擦洗）
- cleanse（清洗）
- abrade（磨损）
- erode（侵蚀）
- polish（擦亮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scour the surface / scour the area / scour for clues / scour through records**

👉 Example: The detective decided to scour the crime scene for any overlooked evidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The relentless floodwaters continued to **scour** the riverbank, gradually undermining the foundation of the centuries-old stone bridge.
   无情的洪水不断冲刷河岸，逐渐侵蚀了这座百年石桥的地基。

2. Researchers had to **scour** through thousands of archived documents before they finally located the relevant historical manuscript.
   研究人员不得不翻遍数千份档案文件，才最终找到了相关的历史手稿。

3. The maintenance crew used industrial chemicals to **scour** the corroded pipes and restore proper water flow to the facility.
   维修人员使用工业化学品擦洗被腐蚀的管道，以恢复设施的正常水流。

---

Think of "scour" as "s-COUR" — imagine scouring a court floor by scrubbing it vigorously until it shines.
把"scour"想成"s-COUR"——想象使劲擦洗球场地板，直到它闪闪发光。
