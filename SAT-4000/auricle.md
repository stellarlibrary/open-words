# 🫀 auricle

🔊 /ˈɔːrɪkl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The external part of the ear (pinna); also, a small muscular pouch extending from the upper chambers (atria) of the heart, sometimes used to refer to the atrium itself.

**中文：** 耳廓；外耳；心耳

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- pinna（耳廓）
- outer ear（外耳）
- ear flap（耳翼）
- atrial appendage（心房附属物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Auricle" is primarily a medical and anatomical term. In otology (ear medicine), it refers to the visible outer ear. In cardiology, it refers to the ear-shaped appendages of the heart's atria. The adjective form is "auricular," which can describe anything related to the ear or heart auricle.

"auricle" 主要是医学和解剖学术语。在耳科学中，它指可见的外耳。在心脏病学中，它指心房的耳状附属结构。其形容词形式为 "auricular"，可描述与耳朵或心耳相关的任何事物。

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon carefully reconstructed the damaged **auricle** to restore the ear's natural shape.
   外科医生仔细重建了受损的**耳廓**，以恢复耳朵的自然形状。

2. The cardiologist identified a blood clot forming within the left **auricle** of the heart.
   心脏病专家发现左侧**心耳**内形成了血栓。

3. During the anatomy lecture, the professor explained how the **auricle** funnels sound waves inward.
   在解剖学讲座中，教授解释了**耳廓**如何将声波向内汇聚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Auricle" = "aur-" (ear, from Latin "auris") + "-icle" (small thing, like "particle"). It is literally the "little ear" — whether the outer flap of your ear or the ear-shaped pouch on the heart.

"auricle" = "aur-"（耳朵，源自拉丁语 auris）+ "-icle"（小东西，如 particle）。它字面意思就是"小耳朵"——无论是耳朵的外部翼片，还是心脏上耳状的小囊。
