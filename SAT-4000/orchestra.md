# 🎵 orchestra

🔊 /ˈɔːrkɪstrə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large group of musicians who play together on various instruments, typically including strings, woodwinds, brass, and percussion; the area in a theater where musicians sit
**中文：** n. 管弦乐队；乐队演奏处

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ensemble（合奏团）
- symphony（交响乐团）
- philharmonic（爱乐乐团）
- band（乐队）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **orchestra conductor / orchestra pit / symphony orchestra / chamber orchestra**

👉 Example: The symphony orchestra performed a magnificent rendition of Beethoven's Ninth Symphony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **orchestra** rehearsed for several weeks before delivering a flawless performance at the prestigious concert hall.
   管弦乐队排练了数周，随后在著名音乐厅奉献了一场完美的演出。

2. A distinguished conductor was appointed to lead the national **orchestra** through its ambitious new season of performances.
   一位杰出的指挥家被任命带领国家管弦乐队完成其雄心勃勃的新演出季。

3. The **orchestra** pit was positioned directly below the stage, allowing the musicians to accompany the opera singers seamlessly.
   乐队演奏处位于舞台正下方，使乐手们能够无缝地为歌剧演唱者伴奏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "orchestra" as "orche-stra" — imagine an "arch" of musicians "strung" together in a curved arrangement on stage.
把"orchestra"想成"orche-stra"——想象一群乐手像"拱门"一样排列在舞台上，弦乐器将他们连接在一起。
