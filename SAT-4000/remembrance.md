# 🕯️ remembrance

🔊 /rɪˈmɛmbrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of remembering; a memory or recollection; a memento or keepsake that serves as a reminder
**中文：** n. 回想，回忆；纪念品；记忆力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recollection（回忆）
- memory（记忆）
- commemoration（纪念）
- memento（纪念品）
- reminiscence（追忆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in remembrance of / a day of remembrance / fond remembrance / remembrance ceremony**

👉 Example: The monument was erected in remembrance of the soldiers who sacrificed their lives during the war.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The annual ceremony is held in **remembrance** of the victims who perished during the devastating natural disaster.
   每年举行的仪式是为了纪念在毁灭性自然灾害中遇难的受害者。

2. She kept the antique locket as a **remembrance** of her grandmother's enduring love and wisdom.
   她保留那枚古董吊坠作为对祖母深厚爱意和智慧的纪念。

3. The professor's vivid lectures left a lasting **remembrance** in the minds of every student who attended the course.
   教授生动的讲座在每一位上过这门课的学生心中留下了持久的记忆。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remembrance" as "remember + ance" — the state or act of remembering something, like holding a memory close.
把"remembrance"想成"remember + ance"——记住某事的状态或行为，就像将记忆紧紧握在手中。
