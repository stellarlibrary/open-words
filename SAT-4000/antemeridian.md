# 🌅 antemeridian

🔊 /ˌæn.tɪ.məˈrɪd.i.ən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Of, relating to, or occurring in the morning; before noon. This is the origin of the abbreviation "a.m." (ante meridiem).

**中文：** 上午的；午前的。这是缩写"a.m."（ante meridiem，午前）的词源。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- morning（早晨的）
- matutinal（早晨的）
- forenoon（上午的）
- dawn（黎明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antemeridian hours, antemeridian session, antemeridian schedule, antemeridian light

**Example:** The conference reserved its most important lectures for the **antemeridian** sessions, when attendees were most alert.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician recommended that patients complete their exercise regimen during the **antemeridian** hours for optimal health.
   医生建议患者在上午时段完成锻炼计划，以获得最佳健康效果。

2. All **antemeridian** appointments were fully booked, so the client was offered an afternoon slot instead.
   所有上午的预约都已满，因此客户被安排到了下午的时段。

3. The **antemeridian** sunlight streamed through the tall windows, illuminating the manuscripts on the scholar's desk.
   上午的阳光透过高窗倾泻而入，照亮了学者书桌上的手稿。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "a.m." on your clock — it stands for "ante meridiem" (before the meridian/noon). Antemeridian simply means "before midday." 想想时钟上的"a.m."——它代表"ante meridiem"（午前）。antemeridian 就是"中午之前的"意思。
