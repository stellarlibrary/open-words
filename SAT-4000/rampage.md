# 💥 rampage

🔊 /ˈræmpeɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A period of violent, uncontrollable behavior or rage. (v.) To rush around in a violent, uncontrolled manner; to rage furiously
**中文：** n. 暴怒；暴跳；乱闹 v. 狂暴；乱闹；发怒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rage（暴怒）
- frenzy（狂乱）
- fury（狂暴）
- riot（骚乱）
- uproar（喧嚣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **go on a rampage / rampage through / rampage across**

👉 Example: The hurricane rampaged through the coastal towns, leaving behind widespread destruction and displacement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The invasive species went on a **rampage** through the fragile ecosystem, devastating native plant populations within a single season.
   这种入侵物种在脆弱的生态系统中肆虐，在一个季节内摧毁了本地植物种群。

2. Protesters began to **rampage** through the city streets after the controversial court verdict was announced on national television.
   在有争议的法庭裁决在全国电视上宣布后，抗议者开始在城市街道上横冲直撞。

3. The flooding river **rampaged** across the lowland farms, destroying crops and infrastructure that had taken decades to develop.
   泛滥的河水在低地农田上肆虐，摧毁了数十年来发展起来的农作物和基础设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rampage" as "ram + page" — imagine an angry ram tearing through pages, destroying everything in a wild fury.
把"rampage"想成"ram + page"——想象一只愤怒的公羊撕碎书页，在狂怒中摧毁一切。
