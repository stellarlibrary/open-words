# 🫖 infuse

🔊 /ɪnˈfjuːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To fill or inspire with a quality or feeling; to soak or steep in liquid to extract flavor or properties
**中文：** v. 灌输；使充满；浸渍；注入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- instill（灌输）
- imbue（浸透）
- permeate（渗透）
- steep（浸泡）
- inspire（激发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infuse something with / infuse confidence into / infuse new energy / infuse tea**

👉 Example: The mentor's guidance helped infuse confidence into the young entrepreneur's ambitious plans.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new director sought to **infuse** the organization with a renewed sense of purpose and creative energy.
   新任主管力图为该组织注入新的使命感和创造力。

2. Great teachers have the ability to **infuse** even the most mundane subjects with enthusiasm and intellectual curiosity.
   优秀的教师有能力为最枯燥的学科注入热情和求知欲。

3. The chef decided to **infuse** the broth with aromatic herbs and spices to enhance the overall flavor profile.
   厨师决定在汤中注入芳香草本和香料，以提升整体风味。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-FUSE" — like fusing something IN, pouring a quality or liquid into something to blend and transform it.
把"infuse"想成"in + fuse（融合）"——像是把某种品质或液体融入其中，使之混合并转化。
