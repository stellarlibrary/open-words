# 🤝 interact

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈækt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To act upon or influence each other; to communicate or engage with others. (n.) A short performance between acts of a play
**中文：** vi. 互相作用；互相影响 n. 幕间剧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engage（交往）
- communicate（交流）
- collaborate（合作）
- interface（互动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **interact with someone / interact socially / interact effectively / interact directly**

👉 Example: Students are encouraged to interact with their peers during collaborative learning activities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new software allows users to **interact** with three-dimensional models by manipulating objects directly on the touchscreen display.
   这款新软件允许用户通过在触摸屏上直接操控物体来与三维模型进行互动。

2. Researchers observed how different chemical compounds **interact** when combined under controlled laboratory conditions at varying temperatures.
   研究人员观察了不同化学化合物在受控实验室条件下以不同温度混合时如何相互作用。

3. The ability to **interact** professionally with colleagues from diverse cultural backgrounds is essential in the modern global workplace.
   在现代全球化工作场所中，与来自不同文化背景的同事进行专业互动的能力至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-act" — "inter" means between, and "act" means to do something, so to interact is to act between or among each other.
把"interact"想成"inter(之间) + act(行动)"——在彼此之间进行行动，即互相作用、互动。
