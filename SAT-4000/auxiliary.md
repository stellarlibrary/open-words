# 🛠️ auxiliary

🔊 /ɔːɡˈzɪliəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Providing supplementary or additional help and support. (n.) A helper; an auxiliary verb in grammar.
**中文：** adj. 辅助的 n. 助动词；辅助者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supplementary（补充的）
- ancillary（辅助的）
- subsidiary（附属的）
- supportive（支持性的）
- additional（额外的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **auxiliary verb / auxiliary power / auxiliary forces / auxiliary equipment**

👉 Example: The hospital dispatched auxiliary medical staff to assist overwhelmed emergency departments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military deployed **auxiliary** troops to reinforce the front lines after the main battalion suffered unexpected heavy casualties.
   在主力营遭受意外重大伤亡后，军方部署了辅助部队来加强前线力量。

2. In English grammar, **auxiliary** verbs such as "have" and "will" help form tenses and express mood or voice.
   在英语语法中，"have"和"will"等助动词帮助构成时态并表达语气或语态。

3. The spacecraft carried an **auxiliary** power system designed to activate automatically if the primary generator malfunctioned during flight.
   该航天器携带了一个辅助电力系统，旨在主发电机在飞行中出现故障时自动启动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "auxiliary" as "aux" (like an AUX cable) — it plugs in to help and support the main system.
把"auxiliary"想成"aux"（就像AUX线缆）——它插入来帮助和支持主系统。
