# 💘 enamor

🔊 /ɪˈnæmər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fill with love or deep admiration; to charm or captivate someone completely
**中文：** v. 使迷恋，使倾心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- captivate（迷住）
- enchant（使着迷）
- infatuate（使迷恋）
- charm（使倾心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **be enamored of / be enamored with / become enamored / deeply enamored**

👉 Example: The audience was completely enamored of the young pianist's extraordinary talent and stage presence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics and audiences alike were thoroughly **enamored** of the director's bold and innovative approach to classical theater.
   评论家和观众都对这位导演大胆创新的古典戏剧手法深深着迷。

2. She became increasingly **enamored** with the culture and traditions of the region during her year of academic study abroad.
   在海外学术交流的一年中，她越来越被该地区的文化和传统所吸引。

3. The investors were **enamored** of the startup's revolutionary technology and its potential to transform the healthcare industry.
   投资者们被这家初创公司的革命性技术及其改变医疗行业的潜力所倾倒。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "enamor" as containing "amor" (love in Latin and Spanish) — to enamor someone is to fill them with love.
把"enamor"看作包含"amor"（拉丁语和西班牙语中的"爱"）——使某人enamor就是让他们充满爱意。
