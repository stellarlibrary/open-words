# 🧲 attraction

🔊 /əˈtrækʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The power or quality of evoking interest, pleasure, or liking; a force by which objects are drawn toward each other
**中文：** n. 吸引，吸引力；引力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- allure（魅力）
- appeal（吸引力）
- fascination（迷恋）
- magnetism（磁力）
- charm（魅力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tourist attraction / gravitational attraction / mutual attraction / main attraction / attraction to**

👉 Example: The cathedral remains the city's primary attraction, drawing millions of visitors annually.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **attraction** between the two magnetic poles demonstrates fundamental principles of physics studied in every classroom.
   两个磁极之间的引力展示了每间教室里都会学到的基本物理原理。

2. The museum's new interactive exhibit has become the main **attraction** for families visiting the capital this summer.
   博物馆新的互动展览已成为今年夏天到访首都的家庭的主要景点。

3. Scientists continue to investigate the mysterious gravitational **attraction** that holds galaxies together across vast cosmic distances.
   科学家们继续研究将星系在广阔宇宙距离中维系在一起的神秘引力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "attraction" as "at-TRACT-ion" — "tract" means to pull, so attraction is the force that pulls things toward each other.
把"attraction"想成"at-TRACT-ion"——"tract"意为拉，因此attraction就是将事物拉向彼此的力量。
