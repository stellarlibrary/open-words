# ⏸️ hiatus

🔊 /haɪˈeɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A pause or gap in a sequence, series, or process; a break or interruption in continuity
**中文：** n. 裂缝；空隙；脱漏部分；间断；中断

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pause（暂停）
- break（中断）
- interruption（中断）
- gap（间隙）
- intermission（幕间休息）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on hiatus / take a hiatus / after a hiatus / a brief hiatus / a long hiatus from**

👉 Example: The renowned author returned to publishing after a five-year hiatus from writing fiction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The television series went on an unexpected **hiatus** midseason, leaving millions of devoted viewers anxiously awaiting its eventual return.
   该电视剧在季中意外停播，让数百万忠实观众焦急地等待其最终回归。

2. After a prolonged **hiatus** from academic research, the scientist resumed her groundbreaking work on renewable energy storage technologies.
   在长期中断学术研究之后，这位科学家恢复了她在可再生能源存储技术方面的开创性工作。

3. The diplomatic negotiations experienced a significant **hiatus** when both delegations withdrew to reassess their respective strategic positions thoroughly.
   当双方代表团撤回以彻底重新评估各自的战略立场时，外交谈判经历了一次重大中断。

把"hiatus"想成"hi-ate-us"——想象有人说"嗨！"然后吃东西，在对话中造成了尴尬的停顿，一个流程中的间隙。
