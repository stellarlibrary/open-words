# ⚖️ judicature

🔊 /ˈdʒuːdɪkətʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The administration of justice; the authority or jurisdiction of a court of law; the office or function of a judge
**中文：** n. 司法；法官的职权；司法权；法院系统

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- judiciary（司法机构）
- jurisdiction（管辖权）
- magistracy（地方法官的职位）
- adjudication（裁决）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supreme judicature / act of judicature / system of judicature / court of judicature**

👉 Example: The Supreme Court of Judicature serves as the highest appellate body in the nation's legal system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The reform of the national **judicature** was essential to restoring public confidence in the fairness of the legal system.
   国家司法制度的改革对于恢复公众对法律体系公正性的信心至关重要。

2. Parliament passed a comprehensive act of **judicature** that restructured the courts and established clearer appellate procedures.
   议会通过了一项全面的司法法案，重组了法院并建立了更清晰的上诉程序。

3. Scholars have long debated whether an independent **judicature** is the most reliable safeguard against executive overreach and tyranny.
   学者们长期争论独立的司法机构是否是防止行政越权和暴政的最可靠保障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "judicature" as "judge + nature" — the nature and system of judging, which is the essence of the judicial system.
把"judicature"想成"judge（法官）+ nature（本质）"——审判的本质和体系，即司法制度的核心。
