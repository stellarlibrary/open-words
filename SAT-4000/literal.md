# 📝 literal

🔊 /ˈlɪtərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Taking words in their most basic sense without metaphor or exaggeration; exact and word-for-word
**中文：** adj. 文字的；逐字的；照字面上的；无夸张的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exact（精确的）
- verbatim（逐字的）
- word-for-word（逐字逐句的）
- precise（精准的）
- faithful（忠实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **literal meaning / literal translation / literal interpretation / in the literal sense**

👉 Example: A literal translation of the idiom would make no sense to a foreign reader.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor emphasized that students should distinguish between the **literal** and figurative meanings of language in literary analysis.
   教授强调学生在文学分析中应区分语言的字面意义和比喻意义。

2. A **literal** translation of the ancient manuscript failed to convey the cultural nuances embedded within the original text.
   对这份古代手稿的逐字翻译未能传达原文中蕴含的文化细微差别。

3. Critics argued that a **literal** interpretation of the constitutional amendment ignored the framers' broader legislative intent.
   批评者认为，对该宪法修正案的字面解读忽略了制定者更广泛的立法意图。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "literal" as "liter-al" — sticking to the "letters" exactly as written, with no added interpretation or embellishment.
把"literal"想成紧扣"letters（文字）"——完全按照书写的文字理解，不添加任何解读或修饰。
