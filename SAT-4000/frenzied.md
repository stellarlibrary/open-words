# 🔥 frenzied

🔊 /ˈfrɛnzid/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wildly excited or uncontrolled; marked by extreme agitation, urgency, or violent behavior
**中文：** 疯狂的；狂乱的；激怒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frantic（疯狂的）
- feverish（狂热的）
- manic（狂躁的）
- delirious（极度兴奋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frenzied attack / frenzied crowd / frenzied efforts / frenzied buying**

👉 Example: The announcement of the sale triggered a frenzied rush of shoppers toward the store entrance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The media coverage of the scandal created a **frenzied** atmosphere in which rumors spread far faster than verified facts.
   对丑闻的媒体报道制造了一种狂乱的气氛，谣言传播的速度远远快于经过核实的事实。

2. In a **frenzied** attempt to meet the publication deadline, the research team worked through three consecutive nights without rest.
   为了疯狂地赶上发表截止日期，研究团队连续三个晚上不休息地工作。

3. The **frenzied** bidding at the auction drove the painting's price to nearly ten times its original estimated market value.
   拍卖会上疯狂的竞价将这幅画的价格推高到其原始估计市场价值的近十倍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frenzied" as "frenzy" + "-ed" — when someone is in a frenzy, they become frenzied, acting wild and out of control.
把"frenzied"想成"frenzy"（狂乱）+ "-ed"——当某人处于狂乱状态时，他们变得frenzied，行为疯狂且失控。
