# 💸 indigence

🔊 /ˈɪndɪdʒəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A state of extreme poverty; destitution; severe lack of material resources
**中文：** n. 穷困；贫乏；贫穷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- poverty（贫穷）
- destitution（赤贫）
- penury（贫困）
- impoverishment（穷困）
- privation（匮乏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **live in indigence / fall into indigence / escape indigence / state of indigence**

👉 Example: Years of economic mismanagement drove thousands of families into a state of profound indigence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The charity was established to provide legal assistance to individuals whose **indigence** prevented them from hiring attorneys.
   该慈善机构成立的目的是为那些因贫困而无法聘请律师的人提供法律援助。

2. Historical records reveal that widespread **indigence** among the rural population contributed to the social upheaval of that era.
   历史记录显示，农村人口中普遍的贫困加剧了那个时代的社会动荡。

3. The government introduced substantial welfare reforms to address the growing **indigence** in post-industrial urban communities.
   政府推出了大规模福利改革，以解决后工业化城市社区日益严重的贫困问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "indi-gence" as "in dire gents" — people in dire, desperate financial circumstances with nothing to spare.
把"indigence"想成"in dire gents"——处于极度困难的经济状况中的人，一无所有。
