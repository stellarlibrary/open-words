# 🌀 bedlam

🔊 /ˈbɛdləm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A scene or state of wild uproar, chaos, and confusion; (archaic) an insane asylum or madhouse
**中文：** n. 混乱；骚乱；吵闹的地方；精神病院，疯人院

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chaos（混乱）
- pandemonium（大混乱）
- turmoil（骚动）
- uproar（喧嚣）
- mayhem（混乱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complete bedlam / utter bedlam / bedlam erupted / bedlam ensued**

👉 Example: When the fire alarm went off during the exam, absolute bedlam erupted in the auditorium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The announcement of sudden layoffs transformed the previously orderly office into a scene of complete **bedlam**.
   突然裁员的消息将原本井然有序的办公室变成了一片混乱的场面。

2. Utter **bedlam** erupted in the stadium when the referee made a controversial decision in the championship's final minute.
   当裁判在锦标赛最后一分钟做出了有争议的判罚时，体育场爆发了彻底的骚乱。

3. The historical novel depicted the horrors of **bedlam**, where patients suffered neglect and inhumane treatment for decades.
   这部历史小说描绘了疯人院的恐怖景象，病人在其中遭受了数十年的忽视和不人道的待遇。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Bedlam" comes from "Bethlehem," the name of a famous London asylum — imagine the chaotic noise of a madhouse to remember bedlam means chaos.
"Bedlam"源自"Bethlehem（伯利恒）"，即伦敦一家著名精神病院的名字——想象疯人院里混乱嘈杂的声音，就能记住bedlam的意思是混乱。
