# 👑 aristocracy

🔊 /ˌærɪˈstɒkrəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The highest social class, typically comprising people of noble birth; a form of government in which power is held by the nobility
**中文：** 贵族；贵族统治；上层社会；贵族阶层

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nobility（贵族）
- elite（精英阶层）
- gentry（绅士阶层）
- patriciate（贵族阶级）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **landed aristocracy / ruling aristocracy / members of the aristocracy / the old aristocracy**

👉 Example: The French Revolution fundamentally dismantled the aristocracy that had dominated the nation for centuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **aristocracy** of eighteenth-century Europe wielded enormous political influence over matters of state governance and foreign policy.
   十八世纪欧洲的**贵族**对国家治理和外交政策事务拥有巨大的政治影响力。

2. Many members of the landed **aristocracy** resisted the social reforms that threatened to redistribute their vast hereditary estates.
   许多地主**贵族**成员抵制了威胁重新分配其庞大世袭庄园的社会改革。

3. The novel vividly portrays the decline of the British **aristocracy** during the turbulent period following the First World War.
   这部小说生动地描绘了第一次世界大战后动荡时期英国**贵族**的衰落。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "aristocracy" from Greek "aristos" (best) + "kratos" (power) — rule by the "best" or most noble class of society.
"aristocracy"源自希腊语"aristos"（最优秀的）+"kratos"（权力）——由社会中"最优秀"或最高贵的阶层统治。
