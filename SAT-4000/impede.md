# 🚧 impede

🔊 /ɪmˈpiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To obstruct, hinder, or delay the progress of something or someone
**中文：** v. 阻碍；妨碍；阻止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hinder（阻碍）
- obstruct（妨碍）
- hamper（阻碍）
- thwart（阻挠）
- retard（延缓）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impede progress / impede growth / impede the flow / impede development**

👉 Example: Bureaucratic regulations often impede the progress of small businesses trying to expand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Heavy snowfall continued to **impede** rescue operations in the remote mountainous region throughout the entire week.
   大雪持续阻碍了偏远山区整整一周的救援行动。

2. The lack of adequate funding will significantly **impede** scientific research on emerging infectious diseases worldwide.
   资金不足将严重妨碍全球新发传染病的科学研究。

3. Outdated infrastructure continues to **impede** economic development in many rural communities across the developing world.
   落后的基础设施继续阻碍着发展中国家许多农村社区的经济发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-pede" as "in your pedals" — something stuck in your pedals impedes your ability to move forward on a bicycle.
把"impede"想成"im（在）+ pede（脚）"——脚被绊住了，自然就被阻碍了，走不动了。
