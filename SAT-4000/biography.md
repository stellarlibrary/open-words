# 📚 biography

🔊 /baɪˈɒɡrəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An account of someone's life written by another person; a genre of literature dealing with the lives of real individuals
**中文：** 传记；个人简介；档案

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- life story（生平故事）
- memoir（回忆录）
- life history（生活史）
- profile（人物简介）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **write a biography / authorized biography / biographical account / biography of someone / political biography**

👉 Example: The acclaimed biography of the scientist revealed previously unknown details about her early childhood and education.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Pulitzer Prize-winning **biography** provided an intimate and thoroughly researched account of the president's formative years in politics.
   这部获得普利策奖的传记对总统早期政治生涯进行了亲切而透彻的研究记述。

2. Scholars frequently consult primary sources and personal correspondence when composing an authoritative **biography** of a historical figure.
   学者们在撰写一位历史人物的权威传记时，经常查阅原始资料和私人信件。

3. The unauthorized **biography** generated considerable controversy because it included allegations that the celebrity had never publicly addressed.
   这部未经授权的传记引发了相当大的争议，因为其中包含了该名人从未公开回应过的指控。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bio-" (life) + "-graphy" (writing) — a biography is literally the writing of someone's life story.
把"biography"拆分为"bio-"（生命）+"-graphy"（书写）——传记字面意思就是书写某人的生命故事。
