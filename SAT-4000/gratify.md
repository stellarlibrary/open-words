# 😊 gratify

🔊 /ˈɡrætɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give pleasure or satisfaction to; to indulge or satisfy a desire or feeling
**中文：** v. 使满意，使高兴；纵容

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- satisfy（满足）
- please（使高兴）
- delight（使愉悦）
- indulge（纵容）
- fulfill（实现）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gratify a desire / gratify one's curiosity / gratified to learn / instantly gratified**

👉 Example: The professor was gratified to see her students excel in the national competition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee was deeply **gratified** by the overwhelming community support for the new public library construction project.
   委员会对社区民众对新公共图书馆建设项目的大力支持深感欣慰。

2. Rather than **gratifying** every impulsive desire, the therapist encouraged her patients to develop greater emotional self-regulation.
   治疗师鼓励患者培养更强的情绪自我调节能力，而不是纵容每一个冲动的欲望。

3. It **gratified** the researchers to discover that their decade-long study had produced findings with significant clinical applications.
   研究人员很高兴地发现，他们长达十年的研究产生了具有重要临床应用价值的成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grat-ify" as related to "grateful" — when you gratify someone, you make them feel grateful and pleased.
把"gratify"和"grateful（感激的）"联系起来——当你gratify某人时，你让他们感到满意和愉悦。
