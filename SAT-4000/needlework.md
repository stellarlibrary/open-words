# 🧵 needlework

🔊 /ˈniːdlwɜːrk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The art or practice of sewing, embroidery, or other crafts done with a needle; items produced by such work
**中文：** n. 刺绣；缝纫；女红的作品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- embroidery（刺绣）
- sewing（缝纫）
- stitching（针线活）
- needlepoint（针绣）
- lacework（花边制作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fine needlework / intricate needlework / needlework skills / needlework pattern**

👉 Example: The museum displayed exquisite needlework from the eighteenth century in its textile collection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The antique tapestry showcased remarkably detailed **needlework** that had survived centuries of exposure and handling.
   这幅古董挂毯展示了极其精细的刺绣工艺，历经数百年的暴露和使用仍保存完好。

2. Victorian women of the upper class often devoted considerable hours to perfecting their **needlework** as a refined accomplishment.
   维多利亚时代的上层阶级女性常常花费大量时间来完善她们的女红技艺，将其视为一种优雅的才能。

3. The artisan's extraordinary **needlework** earned international recognition at the annual textile arts competition last autumn.
   这位工匠非凡的刺绣作品在去年秋天的年度纺织艺术比赛上获得了国际认可。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "needle" + "work" — it is literally work done with a needle, such as sewing and embroidery.
想想"needle（针）"+"work（工作）"——字面意思就是用针做的工作，即缝纫和刺绣。
