# 🧬 differentiation

🔊 /ˌdɪfəˌrenʃiˈeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of becoming different or distinct; in biology, the process by which cells become specialized in structure and function
**中文：** n. 变异，分化；区别

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distinction（区别）
- specialization（专门化）
- variation（变异）
- separation（分离）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cell differentiation / product differentiation / differentiation between / differentiation of**

👉 Example: Product differentiation is a key strategy for companies seeking a competitive advantage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Stem cell **differentiation** is a fundamental process in developmental biology that determines tissue formation and organ growth.
   干细胞分化是发育生物学中决定组织形成和器官生长的基本过程。

2. The **differentiation** between genuine antiques and modern reproductions requires years of training and careful expertise.
   区分真正的古董和现代仿制品需要多年的训练和专业知识。

3. Market **differentiation** allows smaller firms to compete effectively against larger corporations by targeting specialized niches.
   市场差异化使小型公司能够通过瞄准专业细分市场与大型企业有效竞争。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "differentiation" as "the action (-ation) of making things different" — the noun form captures the process of becoming distinct.
把"differentiation"想成"使事物不同的行为（-ation）"——这个名词形式体现了变得不同的过程。
