# 🤝 accompany

🔊 /əˈkʌmpəni/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To go along with as a companion or escort; to occur together with; to play a musical accompaniment for.
**中文：** 陪伴，伴随；伴奏。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- escort（护送）
- attend（陪同）
- follow（跟随）
- supplement（补充）
- chaperon（陪护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accompany + someone/something**; **be accompanied by**

👉 Example: Heavy rainfall often accompanies tropical storms in coastal regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador was **accompanied** by a team of senior advisors during the high-level diplomatic negotiations.
   大使在高级别外交谈判中由一组高级顾问陪同。

2. Significant technological innovation often **accompanies** periods of substantial economic investment and growth.
   重大技术创新通常伴随着大量经济投资和增长时期。

3. The gifted violinist asked her colleague to **accompany** her on piano for the charity recital.
   这位才华横溢的小提琴家请她的同事在慈善独奏会上为她钢琴伴奏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accompany" contains "company" — you keep someone company when you accompany them.
accompany 中包含"company（陪伴）"——accompany 就是给某人作伴。
