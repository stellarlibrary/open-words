# ☠️ noxious

🔊 /ˈnɒkʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant; injurious to physical or moral health
**中文：** adj. 有害的；有毒的；败坏道德的；讨厌的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- toxic（有毒的）
- harmful（有害的）
- poisonous（有毒的）
- deleterious（有害的）
- pernicious（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **noxious fumes / noxious gas / noxious substances / noxious influence / noxious weeds**

👉 Example: The factory released noxious fumes that posed a serious threat to the surrounding community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Residents filed a complaint after **noxious** chemicals from the nearby industrial plant contaminated the local water supply.
   在附近工厂的有害化学物质污染了当地供水系统后，居民们提出了投诉。

2. The environmental agency issued strict guidelines to regulate the emission of **noxious** gases from manufacturing facilities.
   环保机构发布了严格的指导方针来规范制造设施排放有害气体。

3. Prolonged exposure to **noxious** substances in the workplace has been linked to increased rates of respiratory disease.
   长期接触工作场所中的有害物质已被证实与呼吸系统疾病发病率上升有关。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nox-ious" — "nox" sounds like "knocks" you out; noxious fumes are so harmful they can knock you unconscious.
把"noxious"想成"nox(击倒)"——有毒气体如此有害，能把你"击倒"昏迷。
