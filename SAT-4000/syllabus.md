# 📋 syllabus

🔊 /ˈsɪləbəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A course outline; a summary of topics to be covered in an educational program
**中文：** n. 课程大纲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- curriculum（课程）
- outline（大纲）
- program（课程安排）
- course plan（课程计划）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **course syllabus / follow the syllabus / design a syllabus / syllabus requirements / deviate from the syllabus**

👉 Example: The professor distributed the course syllabus on the first day of the semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The department revised its **syllabus** to incorporate more contemporary research methodologies and interdisciplinary perspectives into the program.
   该系修订了课程大纲，将更多当代研究方法和跨学科视角纳入课程。

2. Students are expected to review the **syllabus** thoroughly before the first lecture to understand all assignment deadlines and grading criteria.
   学生应在第一堂课前仔细阅读课程大纲，以了解所有作业截止日期和评分标准。

3. The accreditation committee examined every **syllabus** submitted by the university to ensure compliance with the newly established academic standards.
   认证委员会审查了大学提交的每份课程大纲，以确保符合新制定的学术标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "syllabus" as "silly bus" — imagine a bus route map that tells you every stop along the way, just as a syllabus maps out every topic in a course.
把"syllabus"想成"silly bus（傻巴士）"——想象一张巴士路线图告诉你沿途每一站，就像课程大纲列出课程中的每个主题一样。
