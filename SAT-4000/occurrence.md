# 📅 occurrence

🔊 /əˈkɜːrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An event or incident that happens; the fact or frequency of something taking place
**中文：** n. 发生；出现；事件；发生的事情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- event（事件）
- incident（事故）
- happening（发生的事）
- phenomenon（现象）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **common occurrence / rare occurrence / daily occurrence / the occurrence of**

👉 Example: The occurrence of severe weather events has increased significantly over the past two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sudden **occurrence** of seismic activity in the region prompted authorities to issue an immediate evacuation order.
   该地区突然发生的地震活动促使当局发出了立即疏散的命令。

2. Researchers documented every **occurrence** of the rare species during their three-year field study in the Amazon.
   研究人员在为期三年的亚马逊实地研究中记录了该稀有物种的每一次出现。

3. Such a remarkable **occurrence** had not been observed in the laboratory since the original experiment was conducted.
   自最初的实验进行以来，实验室中从未观察到如此不寻常的现象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "occur + rence" — when something "occurs," the "occurrence" is the event itself that took place.
把"occurrence"想成"occur + rence"——当某事"发生"时，"occurrence"就是所发生的事件本身。
