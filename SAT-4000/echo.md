# 🔊 echo

🔊 /ˈɛkoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A reflected sound that returns to the listener; a repetition or imitation. (v.) To reflect sound; to repeat or imitate closely
**中文：** n. 回声；回音；共鸣 v. 反射（声音）；重复；附和

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reverberate（回响）
- resound（回荡）
- repeat（重复）
- mirror（反映）
- imitate（模仿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **echo through / echo with / echo the sentiments / find an echo in**

👉 Example: Her words echoed through the empty cathedral, creating an almost ethereal atmosphere.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's warnings about climate change **echoed** the concerns previously raised by leading international environmental organizations.
   教授关于气候变化的警告与国际主要环保组织此前提出的担忧如出一辙。

2. The sound of the violin **echoed** through the grand concert hall, captivating every member of the audience.
   小提琴的声音在宏伟的音乐厅中回荡，吸引了每一位观众。

3. Critics noted that the new policy merely **echoed** outdated strategies rather than proposing genuinely innovative solutions.
   批评人士指出，新政策只是重复了过时的策略，而非提出真正创新的解决方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "echo" as the nymph Echo from Greek mythology, who could only repeat the last words spoken to her — a perfect image of reflected sound.
把"echo"想成希腊神话中的仙女厄科（Echo），她只能重复别人对她说的最后几个词——这正是回声的完美形象。
