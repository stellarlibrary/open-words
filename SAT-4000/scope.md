# 🔭 scope

🔊 /skoʊp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The range, extent, or opportunity; field of vision; missile range. (v.) To examine or assess
**中文：** n. 范围；机会，余地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- range（范围）
- extent（程度）
- breadth（广度）
- latitude（余地）
- purview（范围）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scope of work / beyond the scope / scope out / broaden the scope**

👉 Example: The researchers needed to broaden the scope of their investigation to include environmental factors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **scope** of the environmental legislation was deliberately expanded to address both air pollution and water contamination issues.
   该环境立法的范围被刻意扩大，以同时解决空气污染和水污染问题。

2. The committee urged the investigators to **scope** the feasibility of the proposed infrastructure project before allocating public funds.
   委员会敦促调查人员在拨款之前审视拟议基础设施项目的可行性。

3. The ambitious undertaking was clearly beyond the **scope** of any single department and required extensive inter-agency cooperation.
   这项雄心勃勃的任务显然超出了任何单一部门的范围，需要广泛的跨机构合作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scope" as related to "telescope" or "microscope" — instruments that define the range of what you can see.
把"scope"联想到"telescope"（望远镜）或"microscope"（显微镜）——这些仪器定义了你所能看到的范围。
