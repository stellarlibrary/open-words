# 🚀 elevate

🔊 /ˈɛləˌveɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To raise to a higher position or level; to lift up; to improve or uplift in mood or status
**中文：** v. 提升；举起；振奋；提高

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- raise（提高）
- uplift（振奋）
- promote（提升）
- boost（增强）
- heighten（加强）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elevate the status / elevate the mood / elevate to a position / elevate the discussion**

👉 Example: The new policy aims to elevate educational standards across the entire district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee voted to **elevate** the junior researcher to the position of senior director of the laboratory.
   委员会投票决定将这位初级研究员提升为实验室高级主任。

2. Classical music has the remarkable ability to **elevate** one's mood and inspire a sense of profound tranquility.
   古典音乐有着非凡的能力，能够振奋人的情绪并激发一种深刻的宁静感。

3. The organization seeks to **elevate** public awareness about climate change through targeted educational campaigns nationwide.
   该组织力求通过全国有针对性的教育活动来提高公众对气候变化的意识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elevate" as related to "elevator" — an elevator lifts you to a higher floor, just as "elevate" means to raise something higher.
把"elevate"和"elevator（电梯）"联系起来——电梯把你提升到更高的楼层，就像"elevate"意味着将事物提高。
