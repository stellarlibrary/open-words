# 🌤️ temperate

🔊 /ˈtɛmpərɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing moderation or self-restraint; (of climate) mild, without extremes of temperature
**中文：** adj. 温和的；适度的；有节制的；温带的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- moderate（适度的）
- mild（温和的）
- restrained（克制的）
- clement（温暖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **temperate climate / temperate zone / temperate region / temperate response**

👉 Example: Nations in temperate zones generally enjoy four distinct seasons throughout the year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **temperate** climate of the Mediterranean region has historically attracted settlers seeking favorable agricultural conditions and mild winters.
   地中海地区温和的气候历来吸引着寻求有利农业条件和温暖冬季的定居者。

2. The diplomat's **temperate** response to the provocation helped prevent the negotiations from collapsing into open hostility.
   外交官对挑衅的温和回应帮助防止了谈判演变为公开敌对。

3. Ecologists study how species in **temperate** forests adapt to the pronounced seasonal variations in temperature and daylight hours.
   生态学家研究温带森林中的物种如何适应显著的季节性温度和日照时间变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temperate" as sharing a root with "temperature" — a temperate place or person keeps an even temperature, never too hot or too cold.
把"temperate"想成与"temperature"（温度）同源——一个温和的地方或人保持平稳的温度，不会太热也不会太冷。
