# 💧 liquid

🔊 /ˈlɪkwɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) In the form of a liquid; clear and transparent. (n.) A substance that flows freely, such as water or oil
**中文：** adj. 液体的；清澈的 n. 液体，流体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fluid（流体的）
- flowing（流动的）
- molten（熔化的）
- aqueous（水状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **liquid assets / liquid state / liquid form / clear liquid**

👉 Example: The company needed to convert its holdings into liquid assets to cover operating expenses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist carefully poured the **liquid** reagent into the flask to initiate the catalytic reaction process.
   化学家小心地将液体试剂倒入烧瓶中以启动催化反应过程。

2. Investors prefer **liquid** assets because they can be converted into cash quickly without significant loss in value.
   投资者青睐流动资产，因为它们可以快速转换为现金而不会大幅贬值。

3. The mountain stream was so clear that its **liquid** surface reflected the surrounding peaks with mirror-like precision.
   山间溪流清澈见底，其水面如镜般精准地倒映出周围的山峰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liquid" as related to Latin "liquidus" meaning "fluid" — picture water flowing freely in its natural state.
把"liquid"联想到拉丁语"liquidus"，意为"流动的"——想象水在自然状态下自由流动的画面。
