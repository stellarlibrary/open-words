# 🏅 superior

🔊 /suːˈpɪəriər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Higher in rank, status, or quality; excellent; surpassing others. (n.) A person of higher rank or authority
**中文：** adj. 优良的，卓越的；较…多的，优于… n. 上级，长官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excellent（优秀的）
- preeminent（卓越的）
- paramount（至高的）
- surpassing（超越的）
- boss（上级）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **superior quality / superior to / superior officer / superior performance / report to a superior**

👉 Example: This material is clearly superior to the alternatives in terms of both durability and cost-effectiveness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Independent testing confirmed that the new alloy was markedly **superior** to conventional materials in resisting extreme temperatures.
   独立测试证实，这种新合金在抵抗极端温度方面明显优于传统材料。

2. The lieutenant reported directly to her **superior** before making any tactical decisions in the field during the operation.
   中尉在行动中做出任何战术决定之前，都直接向上级汇报。

3. Scholars have long debated whether one civilization's contributions to science were genuinely **superior** to those of another.
   学者们长期以来一直在争论一个文明对科学的贡献是否真的优于另一个文明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "superior" as "super-ior" — someone or something that is super compared to others, standing above the rest.
把"superior"想成"super-ior"——与他人相比超级出色的人或物，高于其他一切。
