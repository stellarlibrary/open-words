# 😤 misanthropy

🔊 /mɪˈsænθrəpi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A general hatred or distrust of humankind; a cynical and pessimistic view of human nature
**中文：** n. 厌恶人类；厌世，愤世嫉俗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cynicism（愤世嫉俗）
- misanthropism（厌世主义）
- pessimism（悲观主义）
- antisociality（反社会性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deep misanthropy / descend into misanthropy / misanthropy toward / born of misanthropy**

👉 Example: Years of political corruption fueled his growing misanthropy and withdrawal from society.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher's **misanthropy** deepened after witnessing the devastating consequences of war and widespread human cruelty firsthand.
   这位哲学家在亲眼目睹战争的毁灭性后果和普遍的人类残忍行为后，厌世情绪加深了。

2. Critics argue that the author's apparent **misanthropy** is actually a profound disappointment rooted in idealistic expectations of humanity.
   评论家认为，这位作者表面上的愤世嫉俗实际上是源于对人类理想化期望的深切失望。

3. His **misanthropy** led him to abandon urban life entirely and retreat to an isolated cabin in the remote mountains.
   他对人类的厌恶使他完全放弃了城市生活，隐退到偏远山区的一间小屋中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-anthropy" — "mis" means hatred, "anthro" means human (like anthropology). So misanthropy is "hatred of humans."
把"misanthropy"拆成"mis（厌恶）+ anthro（人类）+ py"——就像anthropology（人类学）中的"人类"，加上"厌恶"，即厌恶人类。
