# 📜 antiquarian

🔊 /ˌæntɪˈkweəriən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the study or collection of antiquities, old books, or historical artifacts. (n.) A person who studies, collects, or deals in antiquities and historical objects.

**中文：** (adj.) 古文物研究的；与古物收藏有关的。(n.) 古文物研究者；古物收藏家。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- collector（收藏家）
- archivist（档案管理员）
- historian（历史学家）
- antiquary（古物研究者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antiquarian bookshop, antiquarian collection, antiquarian interest, antiquarian society, dedicated antiquarian

**Example:** The **antiquarian** bookshop on the corner specialized in rare first editions from the eighteenth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished **antiquarian** devoted three decades to cataloguing medieval manuscripts preserved in European monasteries.
   这位杰出的古文物研究者用了三十年时间为保存在欧洲修道院中的中世纪手稿编目。

2. Her **antiquarian** interests led her to establish one of the finest private collections of Roman coins.
   她对古物的兴趣促使她建立了最精美的罗马钱币私人收藏之一。

3. The museum appointed a renowned **antiquarian** to authenticate the recently discovered artifacts from the excavation site.
   博物馆聘请了一位著名的古文物研究者来鉴定最近从发掘现场发现的文物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "antiqu-" (ancient, old) + "-arian" (a person related to). An antiquarian is someone connected to ancient things — a scholar or collector of antiquities.

联想："antiqu-"（古老的）+ "-arian"（与…相关的人）。antiquarian 就是与古老事物打交道的人——古文物研究者或收藏家。
