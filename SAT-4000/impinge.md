# 💥 impinge

🔊 /ɪmˈpɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To have a noticeable effect or impact on something; to encroach or infringe upon; to strike or collide with
**中文：** v. 撞击；侵犯；对……产生影响

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- encroach（侵犯）
- infringe（侵害）
- trespass（侵入）
- affect（影响）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impinge on / impinge upon** (rights, freedom, privacy, territory)

👉 Example: New regulations should not impinge on citizens' fundamental rights to free expression.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proposed construction project would significantly **impinge** on the natural habitat of several endangered species in the region.
   拟建的建设项目将严重侵犯该地区数种濒危物种的自然栖息地。

2. Excessive government surveillance can **impinge** upon individual privacy rights that are guaranteed by the constitution.
   过度的政府监控可能侵犯宪法所保障的个人隐私权。

3. The bright artificial lights from the new stadium **impinge** directly on the observatory's ability to conduct astronomical research.
   新体育场的强烈人造灯光直接影响了天文台进行天文研究的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-PINGE" as something that "pinches" into your space — when something impinges, it pushes into where it shouldn't be.
把"impinge"想成"pinch（捏）"——当某物impinge时，它就像捏入了你的空间，侵犯了你的领域。
