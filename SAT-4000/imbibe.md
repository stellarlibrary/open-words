# 🍷 imbibe

🔊 /ɪmˈbaɪb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To absorb or take in ideas, knowledge, or nutrients; to drink, especially alcoholic beverages
**中文：** v. 吸收，接受；喝，饮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absorb（吸收）
- assimilate（消化吸收）
- consume（消费）
- drink（饮）
- ingest（摄入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imbibe knowledge / imbibe ideas / imbibe culture / imbibe freely / imbibe wisdom**

👉 Example: Students who study abroad tend to imbibe the customs and values of their host country quite naturally.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scholar traveled extensively throughout Europe to **imbibe** the philosophical traditions of different intellectual movements.
   这位年轻学者在欧洲各地广泛游历，吸收不同思想运动的哲学传统。

2. Children inevitably **imbibe** the attitudes and beliefs of the adults who surround them during their formative developmental years.
   儿童在成长发育的关键时期不可避免地会吸收周围成年人的态度和信念。

3. The conference provided a rare opportunity for professionals to **imbibe** cutting-edge research findings from leading international experts.
   此次会议为专业人士提供了一个难得的机会，吸收来自国际顶尖专家的前沿研究成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imbibe" as "im (in) + bibe (drink)" from Latin "bibere" meaning to drink — you drink in knowledge or liquid.
把"imbibe"想成"im（进入）+ bibe（喝）"，来自拉丁语"bibere"意为喝——你把知识或液体喝进去。
