# 👁️ superintend

🔊 /ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To oversee and direct; to manage or supervise a process or activity
**中文：** v. 主管，指挥，监督

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oversee（监督）
- supervise（管理）
- manage（管理）
- direct（指挥）
- administer（管理）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **superintend operations / superintend the construction / superintend the work / superintend a project**

👉 Example: The chief engineer was appointed to superintend all phases of the bridge construction project.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dean was tasked to **superintend** the academic reform process and ensure that all departments complied with new standards.
   院长被指派监督学术改革进程，并确保所有院系遵守新标准。

2. A senior officer was dispatched to **superintend** the relief operations in the disaster-stricken region along the coast.
   一名高级官员被派往沿海灾区主管救援行动。

3. The board appointed an experienced manager to **superintend** the merger and address any regulatory complications that arose.
   董事会任命了一位经验丰富的经理来指挥合并事宜，并处理出现的任何监管问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "superintend" as "super-intend" — someone who super intends to watch over everything, making sure it all goes well.
把"superintend"想成"super-intend"——一个超级有意愿看管一切的人，确保万事顺利。
