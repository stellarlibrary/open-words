# ⚖️ moderate

🔊 /ˈmɒdərət/ (adj., n.) /ˈmɒdəreɪt/ (v.)<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Average in amount, intensity, or degree; not extreme. (v.) To make or become less intense or extreme; to preside over a discussion
**中文：** adj. 中等的；适度的；温和的；稳健的 v. 缓和；主持

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- temperate（温和的）
- restrained（克制的）
- reasonable（合理的）
- mild（温和的）
- alleviate（缓和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **moderate amount / moderate pace / moderate a debate / moderate climate / moderate views**

👉 Example: The physician recommended moderate exercise for patients recovering from minor surgical procedures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee adopted a **moderate** approach to fiscal reform, balancing economic growth with responsible government spending limits.
   委员会采取了温和的财政改革方式，在经济增长与负责任的政府支出限制之间取得平衡。

2. The experienced journalist was invited to **moderate** the televised debate between the two leading presidential candidates.
   这位经验丰富的记者受邀主持两位主要总统候选人之间的电视辩论。

3. Studies indicate that **moderate** consumption of certain nutrients can significantly reduce the risk of chronic cardiovascular disease.
   研究表明，适度摄入某些营养素可以显著降低慢性心血管疾病的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "moderate" as "mode-rate" — staying in the middle mode and rating, never going to extremes on either side.
把"moderate"想成"mode-rate"——保持在中间的模式和等级，绝不走向任何一个极端。
