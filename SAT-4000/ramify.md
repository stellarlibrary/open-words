# 🌿 ramify

🔊 /ˈræmɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To divide or spread out into branches or branchlike parts; to produce complex consequences or ramifications
**中文：** v. 分枝，分叉；成网状；产生复杂的后果

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- branch（分支）
- diverge（分叉）
- subdivide（细分）
- proliferate（扩散）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ramify into / ramify throughout / ramify from**

👉 Example: The consequences of the policy decision began to ramify into every sector of the national economy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The initial dispute between the two departments began to **ramify** into a complex institutional crisis affecting the entire organization.
   两个部门之间最初的争端开始分化为一场影响整个机构的复杂危机。

2. Blood vessels **ramify** extensively throughout the human body, forming an intricate network that sustains every living tissue.
   血管在人体内广泛分支，形成一个维持每个活组织的复杂网络。

3. The professor explained how a single philosophical idea can **ramify** into dozens of competing interpretations over several centuries.
   教授解释了一个哲学思想如何在几个世纪内分化为数十种相互竞争的解读。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ramify" as "rami-fy" — "rami" comes from Latin "ramus" meaning branch, so ramify means to branch out like a tree.
把"ramify"想成"rami-fy"——"rami"来自拉丁语"ramus"意为树枝，所以ramify就是像树一样分枝。
