# 📉 trough

🔊 /trɒf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A long, narrow container for animals to eat or drink from; a low point in a cycle or wave; a channel or depression
**中文：** n. 水槽；饲料槽；低谷期；波谷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- basin（盆地）
- channel（水道）
- depression（低洼处）
- nadir（最低点）
- valley（低谷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **economic trough / trough of a wave / feeding trough / peak and trough**

👉 Example: The economy reached its trough in the second quarter before beginning a gradual recovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Economists predicted that the market would reach its **trough** by midyear and subsequently enter a period of sustained growth.
   经济学家预测，市场将在年中触及低谷，随后进入持续增长期。

2. The farmer filled the wooden **trough** with fresh water each morning to ensure the livestock remained properly hydrated throughout the day.
   农夫每天早上在木槽中灌满淡水，以确保牲畜全天得到充足的水分。

3. Meteorologists identified a **trough** of low atmospheric pressure that was expected to bring severe weather to the coastal region.
   气象学家发现了一个低气压槽，预计将给沿海地区带来恶劣天气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trough" as "tr-ough" — the "ough" sounds rough, like the rough bottom of a valley or a feeding container that sits low on the ground.
把"trough"想成在最"rough（艰难的）"时期到达的最低点——低谷期就像地面上凹下去的槽。
