# 😏 contemptuous

🔊 /kənˈtɛmptʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing contempt; scornful; displaying an attitude that someone or something is unworthy of respect
**中文：** adj. 轻蔑的；侮辱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scornful（轻蔑的）
- disdainful（不屑的）
- derisive（嘲讽的）
- dismissive（轻视的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contemptuous of / contemptuous glance / contemptuous tone / contemptuous attitude / contemptuous remark**

👉 Example: She cast a contemptuous glance at the proposal before dismissing it entirely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The critic was openly **contemptuous** of the film, describing it as a shallow imitation of far superior earlier works.
   这位评论家公然蔑视这部电影，称其为远优于它的早期作品的肤浅模仿。

2. His **contemptuous** tone during the negotiations alienated potential allies and ultimately doomed the diplomatic effort.
   他在谈判中轻蔑的语气疏远了潜在的盟友，最终毁掉了外交努力。

3. The aristocracy remained **contemptuous** of the emerging merchant class despite its growing economic influence and political power.
   尽管新兴商人阶层的经济影响力和政治权力日益增长，贵族阶层仍对其持轻蔑态度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "contempt-U-OUS" — someone full of contempt, with the "-ous" ending meaning "full of," so they are full of scorn.
把"contemptuous"想成"充满蔑视的"——后缀"-ous"表示"充满……的"，即满脸轻蔑。
