# 🤢 obnoxious

🔊 /əbˈnɑːkʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely unpleasant or offensive; causing strong dislike or disgust
**中文：** adj. 讨厌的；可憎的；不愉快的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- offensive（令人反感的）
- repulsive（令人厌恶的）
- disagreeable（不愉快的）
- detestable（可憎的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obnoxious behavior / obnoxious smell / obnoxious person / obnoxious remarks**

👉 Example: His obnoxious behavior at the dinner party embarrassed everyone present.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee unanimously agreed that the candidate's **obnoxious** remarks during the interview disqualified him from further consideration.
   委员会一致认为该候选人在面试中令人讨厌的言论使他失去了进一步考虑的资格。

2. Residents filed formal complaints about the **obnoxious** fumes emanating from the nearby industrial plant throughout the summer months.
   居民们对附近工厂在整个夏季排放的令人不快的气味提出了正式投诉。

3. The professor found it increasingly difficult to tolerate the student's **obnoxious** interruptions during every lecture session.
   教授发现越来越难以忍受那个学生在每次课堂上令人讨厌的打断。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-NOXIOUS" — it contains "noxious," meaning harmful or poisonous, so obnoxious describes something so unpleasant it feels toxic.
把"obnoxious"拆开看——里面有"noxious"（有害的、有毒的），所以obnoxious形容某物令人讨厌得像有毒一样。
