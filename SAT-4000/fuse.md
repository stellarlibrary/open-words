# 💥 fuse

🔊 /fjuːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To join or blend together to form a single entity; to melt with intense heat. (n.) A safety device containing a strip of wire that melts to interrupt an electric circuit; a cord or tube for igniting an explosive
**中文：** v. 融合；熔化 n. 保险丝，熔线；导火线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merge（合并）
- blend（混合）
- meld（融合）
- amalgamate（混合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fuse together / blow a fuse / short fuse / fuse elements / light the fuse**

👉 Example: The artist fused traditional techniques with modern aesthetics to create a striking new style.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The intense volcanic heat was sufficient to **fuse** the disparate minerals into a single crystalline structure.
   强烈的火山热量足以将不同的矿物熔合成一种单一的晶体结构。

2. The architect sought to **fuse** classical design principles with contemporary materials to produce an innovative building.
   这位建筑师试图将古典设计原则与当代材料融合，以打造一座创新建筑。

3. A defective electrical **fuse** in the laboratory triggered the automatic shutdown of the entire ventilation system.
   实验室中一个有缺陷的保险丝触发了整个通风系统的自动关闭。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fuse" as two things becoming "fused" — like melted metal joining together, or a lit fuse bringing explosive energy together.
把"fuse"想成两个东西被"熔合"——就像熔化的金属连接在一起，或者点燃的导火线将爆炸能量汇聚起来。
