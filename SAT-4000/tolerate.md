# 🤝 tolerate

🔊 /ˈtɒləreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To allow the existence or occurrence of something without interference; to endure or put up with something unpleasant
**中文：** 忍受；容忍；默许；宽容对待

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- endure（忍耐）
- bear（忍受）
- permit（允许）
- condone（宽恕）
- brook（容忍）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tolerate behavior / tolerate pain / tolerate differences / refuse to tolerate**

👉 Example: A democratic society must tolerate diverse opinions while maintaining civil discourse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor refused to **tolerate** any form of academic dishonesty and imposed strict penalties on offenders.
   这位教授拒绝容忍任何形式的学术不端行为，并对违规者施以严厉处罚。

2. Patients who **tolerate** the medication well during the initial phase may continue treatment for an additional six months.
   在初始阶段对药物耐受良好的患者可以继续治疗六个月。

3. The administration could no longer **tolerate** the persistent violations of safety regulations at the manufacturing plant.
   管理层再也无法容忍制造厂持续违反安全法规的行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "toler-ate" — you "ate" something bitter but managed to swallow it, just as tolerating means enduring something unpleasant.
想想"tolerate"——你"ate"（吃下了）一些苦涩的东西但还是咽了下去，就像容忍意味着忍受不愉快的事情。
