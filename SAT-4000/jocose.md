# 😄 jocose

🔊 /dʒoʊˈkoʊs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Playful or humorous; fond of or characterized by joking
**中文：** adj. 诙谐的；开玩笑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- humorous（幽默的）
- witty（机智的）
- facetious（滑稽的）
- jocular（诙谐的）
- playful（爱开玩笑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jocose manner / jocose remark / jocose tone / jocose disposition**

👉 Example: His jocose remarks during the lecture helped students relax and engage more readily with the material.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The keynote speaker adopted a **jocose** tone to ease the audience's tension before addressing the more serious topics of the conference.
   主讲人采用了诙谐的语调，以缓和听众的紧张情绪，然后再讨论会议中更严肃的议题。

2. Despite the gravity of the courtroom proceedings, the lawyer's **jocose** aside elicited unexpected laughter from the jury members.
   尽管法庭诉讼气氛严肃，律师诙谐的旁白还是引起了陪审员出乎意料的笑声。

3. Her **jocose** personality made her the most popular colleague in the department, always lifting spirits during stressful deadlines.
   她诙谐的性格使她成为部门里最受欢迎的同事，总能在紧张的截止日期前振奋大家的精神。
