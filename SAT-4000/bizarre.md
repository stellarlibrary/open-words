# 🎭 bizarre

🔊 /bɪˈzɑːr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very strange or unusual, especially in a striking or shocking way
**中文：** adj. 奇异的；古怪可笑的；异乎寻常的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eccentric（古怪的）
- outlandish（稀奇古怪的）
- peculiar（奇特的）
- grotesque（荒诞的）
- surreal（超现实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bizarre behavior / bizarre appearance / bizarre coincidence / bizarre twist / truly bizarre**

👉 Example: The witnesses described a series of bizarre events that defied all logical explanation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation uncovered a **bizarre** series of coincidences that ultimately led detectives to the prime suspect.
   调查揭露了一系列离奇的巧合，最终将侦探们引向了主要嫌疑人。

2. Critics praised the artist's **bizarre** installations for challenging conventional perceptions of space and materiality.
   评论家赞扬了这位艺术家奇异的装置作品，因为它们挑战了人们对空间和物质的传统认知。

3. The professor recounted the **bizarre** historical episode in which an entire town fell victim to mass hysteria.
   这位教授讲述了一段离奇的历史事件，整个小镇的居民都陷入了集体歇斯底里。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bizarre" sounding like "bazaar" — imagine a strange, chaotic bazaar filled with the most unusual and weird items.
"bizarre"听起来像"bazaar"（集市）——想象一个充满各种奇异怪诞物品的混乱集市。
