# 🐢 longevity

🔊 /lɒnˈdʒevɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Long duration of life; the length or duration of life; long existence or service
**中文：** n. 长寿，长命；寿命；耐久性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- durability（耐久性）
- endurance（持久力）
- permanence（永久性）
- persistence（持续性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **longevity of life / career longevity / remarkable longevity / promote longevity**

👉 Example: Scientists continue to study the factors that contribute to human longevity in different cultures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **longevity** of the ancient Roman aqueducts demonstrates the extraordinary engineering capabilities of classical civilization.
   古罗马引水渠的持久性展示了古典文明非凡的工程能力。

2. Researchers attribute the remarkable **longevity** of residents in Okinawa to their balanced diet and active lifestyle.
   研究人员将冲绳居民的非凡长寿归因于他们均衡的饮食和积极的生活方式。

3. The company's **longevity** in the competitive technology market is largely due to its consistent commitment to innovation.
   该公司在竞争激烈的科技市场中的持久存在，主要归功于其对创新的始终如一的承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "long-evity" — the word starts with "long," reminding you of a long life or long-lasting existence.
把"longevity"想成"long（长）+ evity"——开头就是"长"，提醒你这是关于长寿、长久存在的意思。
