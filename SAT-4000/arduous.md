# 🏔️ arduous

🔊 /ˈɑːrdʒuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Requiring great effort, energy, or endurance; extremely difficult and tiring
**中文：** 费力的；艰巨的；艰难的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strenuous（费力的）
- grueling（令人精疲力竭的）
- laborious（辛苦的）
- demanding（要求高的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arduous task / arduous journey / arduous process / arduous climb**

👉 Example: The arduous training regimen prepared the recruits for the physical demands of military service.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The explorers embarked on an **arduous** trek through the dense jungle, battling heat and humidity for several weeks.
   探险者们踏上了穿越茂密丛林的艰苦跋涉，与酷热和潮湿搏斗了数周。

2. Completing a doctoral dissertation is an **arduous** endeavor that demands years of dedicated research and unwavering discipline.
   完成博士论文是一项艰巨的事业，需要多年的专注研究和坚定不移的自律。

3. The **arduous** negotiations between the two nations finally produced a comprehensive and lasting peace agreement.
   两国之间的艰难谈判终于达成了一项全面而持久的和平协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arduous" as "are-do-us" — "Are you going to do this to us?" because arduous tasks feel like a burden imposed on you.
把"arduous"想成"are-do-us"——"你要让我们做这个吗？"因为费力的任务让人觉得是强加于身的负担。
