# 🌀 coil

🔊 /kɔɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To wind or twist into rings or spirals; to arrange in a circular shape. (n.) A series of loops; a spiral or ring-shaped arrangement, especially of wire
**中文：** v. 盘绕；把…卷成圈 n. 线圈；卷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spiral（螺旋）
- loop（环绕）
- wind（缠绕）
- curl（卷曲）
- twist（扭转）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coil around / coil up / coil of rope / electromagnetic coil**

👉 Example: The electrician carefully wound the copper wire into a tight coil for the transformer assembly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The serpent **coiled** itself around the branch with remarkable precision, awaiting its unsuspecting prey in the dense foliage.
   蛇精确地盘绕在树枝上，在茂密的树叶中等待毫无防备的猎物。

2. Engineers designed a new electromagnetic **coil** that significantly improved the efficiency of wireless power transmission systems.
   工程师设计了一种新的电磁线圈，显著提高了无线电力传输系统的效率。

3. The sailor began to **coil** the heavy rope on the deck, preparing the vessel for its departure from the harbor.
   水手开始在甲板上盘绕沉重的绳索，为船只离港做准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "coil" and picture a snake coiling up — the word even looks like a spiral when you imagine the "o" as a loop and the "i" as the center of the coil.
想到"coil"就想象一条蛇盘绕起来——当你把"o"想象成一个环、"i"想象成线圈的中心时，这个词本身看起来就像一个螺旋。
