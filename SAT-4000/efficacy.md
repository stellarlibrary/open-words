# ⚗️ efficacy

🔊 /ˈɛfɪkəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ability to produce a desired or intended result; effectiveness, especially of a drug or treatment
**中文：** n. 功效；效力；效能

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- effectiveness（有效性）
- potency（效力）
- capability（能力）
- usefulness（有用性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **efficacy of a treatment / clinical efficacy / proven efficacy / efficacy and safety**

👉 Example: Researchers continue to study the long-term efficacy of the vaccine across diverse populations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company was required to demonstrate the **efficacy** of its new drug through rigorous double-blind clinical trials.
   这家制药公司被要求通过严格的双盲临床试验来证明其新药的功效。

2. Scholars have long debated the **efficacy** of economic sanctions as a tool for compelling authoritarian regimes to change their behavior.
   学者们长期以来一直在争论经济制裁作为迫使专制政权改变行为的工具的效力。

3. The **efficacy** of the new educational curriculum was measured by comparing standardized test scores before and after its implementation.
   新教育课程的效力是通过比较实施前后的标准化考试成绩来衡量的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "effi-cacy" as "efficiency capacity" — efficacy measures the capacity of something to work efficiently and produce results.
把"efficacy"想成"efficiency capacity（效率能力）"——efficacy衡量的是某物高效运作并产生结果的能力。
