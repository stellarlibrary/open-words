# 🏛️ body

🔊 /ˈbɑːdi/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The physical structure of a person or animal; the main or central part of something; a large amount or collection; an organized group of people. (v.) To give form or shape to something.
**中文：** n. 身体；物体；主体；大量；团体；主要部分 v. 赋以形体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- physique（体格）
- frame（躯体）
- corpus（主体）
- entity（实体）
- mass（大量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **body of work / body of evidence / governing body / body of water / body forth**

👉 Example: The committee reviewed the entire body of evidence before issuing its final ruling.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legislative **body** convened an emergency session to address the escalating humanitarian crisis along the southern border.
   该立法机构召开紧急会议，以应对南部边境日益加剧的人道主义危机。

2. A substantial **body** of research now supports the hypothesis that regular exercise significantly reduces the risk of cognitive decline.
   大量研究现在支持这一假说，即定期锻炼能显著降低认知衰退的风险。

3. The artist's **body** of work spans four decades and encompasses painting, sculpture, and multimedia installations alike.
   这位艺术家的全部作品跨越四十年，涵盖绘画、雕塑和多媒体装置艺术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "body" as the core container — just as your body holds your organs together, a "body of work" or "body of evidence" holds a collection of things together as one whole.
把"body"想成核心容器——就像你的身体将器官聚在一起，"body of work"或"body of evidence"将一系列事物汇聚成一个整体。
