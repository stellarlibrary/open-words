# 🤯 incomprehensible

🔊 /ˌɪnkɒmprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Impossible to understand or comprehend; beyond the limits of understanding
**中文：** adj. 费解的；不可思议的；无限的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unintelligible（难以理解的）
- unfathomable（深不可测的）
- impenetrable（费解的）
- inscrutable（高深莫测的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incomprehensible language / incomprehensible to someone / totally incomprehensible / find something incomprehensible**

👉 Example: The professor's lecture on quantum mechanics was entirely incomprehensible to the first-year students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legal document was written in such dense jargon that it remained **incomprehensible** to the average citizen.
   该法律文件措辞极其晦涩，对普通公民而言完全费解。

2. Scientists found the ancient manuscript's symbolic notation system virtually **incomprehensible** without a proper decryption key.
   科学家发现，如果没有合适的解密密钥，这份古代手稿的符号系统几乎无法理解。

3. The sheer scale of the national debt is **incomprehensible** to most people who lack financial expertise.
   对于大多数缺乏金融专业知识的人来说，国债的庞大规模简直不可思议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-comprehens-ible" as "not able to comprehend" — if you cannot comprehend something, it is incomprehensible.
把"incomprehensible"拆分为"in（不）+ comprehens（理解）+ ible（能够）"——不能够理解的，就是费解的。
