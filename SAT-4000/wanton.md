# 🎭 wanton

🔊 /ˈwɑːntən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, verb | 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deliberate and unprovoked; reckless or unrestrained. (n.) A person given to immoral or extravagant behavior. (v.) To act in a reckless or unrestrained manner
**中文：** adj. 荒唐的；放纵的；恣意的；无节制的 n. 放荡的人 v. 放肆；嬉戏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reckless（鲁莽的）
- gratuitous（无端的）
- unrestrained（无节制的）
- deliberate（故意的）
- indiscriminate（不加区分的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wanton destruction / wanton cruelty / wanton disregard / wanton violence**

👉 Example: The invading forces engaged in wanton destruction of cultural heritage sites throughout the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tribunal condemned the **wanton** destruction of civilian infrastructure as a clear violation of international humanitarian law.
   法庭谴责对民用基础设施的恣意破坏，认为这明显违反了国际人道主义法。

2. Critics accused the corporation of **wanton** disregard for environmental regulations in its pursuit of short-term profits.
   批评者指责该公司为追求短期利润而肆意无视环境法规。

3. The novel portrays a society in which **wanton** excess and moral decay have eroded the foundations of civic responsibility.
   这部小说描绘了一个放纵无度和道德沦丧侵蚀了公民责任基础的社会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "want-on" — someone who wants to go on and on without any limits, acting recklessly and without restraint.
把"wanton"想成"want-on"——一个想要无限制地继续下去的人，行为鲁莽且毫无节制。
