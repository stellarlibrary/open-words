# 🍞 aliment

🔊 /ˈæl.ɪ.mənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Food or nourishment; something that sustains or supports physical or mental well-being.
**中文：** 滋养品；食物；营养物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nourishment（营养）
- sustenance（食物，生计）
- nutriment（营养物）
- provisions（食物供应）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **provide aliment, source of aliment, basic aliment, intellectual aliment**
👉 Example: The relief agency distributed essential aliment to the displaced families.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician prescribed a regimen of wholesome **aliment** to restore the patient's depleted physical strength.
   医生开出了一套有益健康的食物方案，以恢复病人消耗殆尽的体力。

2. Libraries serve as vital intellectual **aliment** for communities that lack access to digital resources.
   图书馆为缺乏数字资源的社区提供重要的精神食粮。

3. The explorers carried sufficient **aliment** and provisions to sustain their expedition through the harsh winter months.
   探险者们携带了充足的食物和补给品，以支撑他们度过严酷的冬季。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aliment" sounds like "all-I-meant" to eat — it refers to all the food meant to nourish you. Also linked to "alimentary" (digestive).
"aliment"听起来像"all I meant（我所指的一切）"吃的东西——指所有用来滋养你的食物。也与"alimentary（消化的）"相关。
