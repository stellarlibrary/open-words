# 🔬 analysis

🔊 /əˈnæləsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The detailed examination or study of something in order to understand its nature, structure, or essential features; the process of breaking a complex topic into smaller parts
**中文：** 分析；分解；验定

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examination（检查）
- evaluation（评估）
- assessment（评定）
- scrutiny（审查）
- dissection（剖析）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conduct an analysis / in-depth analysis / data analysis / critical analysis / analysis of / statistical analysis**

👉 Example: The team conducted a thorough analysis of the market data before making their investment recommendation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A comprehensive **analysis** of the archaeological evidence revealed that the settlement was far older than researchers had originally estimated.
   对考古证据的全面分析表明，该定居点比研究人员最初估计的要古老得多。

2. The financial **analysis** indicated that the merger would generate substantial long-term savings despite significant initial restructuring costs.
   财务分析表明，尽管初始重组成本巨大，合并将产生可观的长期节约。

3. Her literary **analysis** of the novel uncovered subtle themes of social inequality that previous scholars had consistently overlooked.
   她对这部小说的文学分析揭示了以往学者一直忽略的关于社会不平等的微妙主题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "analysis" as "ana- (up/apart) + -lysis (loosening)" — loosening something apart into pieces to understand how it works.
把"analysis"理解为"ana-（分开）+ -lysis（松开）"——把事物分开松解成各个部分，以理解其运作方式。
