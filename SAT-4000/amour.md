# 💕 amour

🔊 /əˈmʊr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A secret or illicit love affair or romantic relationship.
**中文：** 恋情；偷情

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- affair（恋情）
- liaison（私通）
- romance（风流韵事）
- intrigue（密谋恋情）
- dalliance（调情）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **secret amour, brief amour, passionate amour, amour propre (self-esteem)**
👉 Example: The diplomat's secret amour became a scandal in the national press.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historical novel revealed the **amour** between the queen and a distinguished member of the court.
   这部历史小说揭示了女王与一位杰出朝臣之间的恋情。

2. His clandestine **amour** was eventually discovered, causing irreparable damage to his public reputation.
   他秘密的偷情最终被发现，对他的公众声誉造成了无法挽回的损害。

3. Literary critics have long debated the significance of the **amour** depicted in that celebrated French play.
   文学评论家们长期以来一直在争论那部著名法国戏剧中所描绘的恋情的意义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amour" is the French word for "love" — think of a secret, romantic French love affair.
"Amour"就是法语中的"爱"——想象一段秘密的、浪漫的法式恋情。
