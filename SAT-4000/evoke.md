# 💫 evoke

🔊 /ɪˈvoʊk/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To bring or recall to the conscious mind; to elicit or draw forth a response, memory, or feeling
**中文：** v. 引起，唤起；博得；使人想起

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elicit（引出）
- invoke（唤起）
- arouse（激起）
- summon（召唤）
- conjure（使人想起）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **evoke memories / evoke emotions / evoke a response / evoke a sense of**

👉 Example: The haunting melody evoked powerful memories of her childhood in the countryside.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist's painting managed to **evoke** a profound emotional response from audiences of all cultural backgrounds.
   这位艺术家的画作成功地在不同文化背景的观众中引起了深刻的情感共鸣。

2. The senator's eloquent speech **evoked** a strong sense of national unity during the period of political turmoil.
   这位参议员雄辩的演讲在政治动荡时期唤起了强烈的民族团结意识。

3. Researchers discovered that certain fragrances can **evoke** vivid autobiographical memories stored deep within the brain.
   研究人员发现，某些香气能够唤起储存在大脑深处的生动自传体记忆。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-voke" as "e-voice" — to evoke is to call forth a voice from the past, bringing memories and feelings to the surface.
把"evoke"想成"e-voice"——唤起就像召唤出过去的声音，将记忆和感受带到表面。
