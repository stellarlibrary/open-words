# 🎨 nuance

🔊 /ˈnjuːɑːns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A subtle difference in or shade of meaning, expression, or sound
**中文：** n. 细微差别

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- subtlety（微妙）
- refinement（细微之处）
- distinction（差别）
- shade（细微差异）
- gradation（层次变化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subtle nuance / nuance of meaning / cultural nuance / appreciate the nuances / every nuance**

👉 Example: A skilled translator must understand the nuances of both languages to convey the original tone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literary critic praised the author's ability to capture every **nuance** of human emotion through precise and evocative prose.
   这位文学评论家称赞了作者通过精确而富有感染力的散文捕捉人类情感每一丝细微差别的能力。

2. Understanding the **nuances** of diplomatic language is essential for negotiators who work in complex international relations.
   理解外交语言的微妙之处对于从事复杂国际关系工作的谈判者来说至关重要。

3. The musician's performance was remarkable for its sensitivity to the subtle **nuances** of rhythm and dynamic expression.
   这位音乐家的表演因其对节奏和动态表达的微妙之处的敏感而令人瞩目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nu-ance" — "new" + "ants" — even tiny ants notice the newest, smallest differences; nuance is about tiny differences.
把"nuance"想成"new(新的) + ants(蚂蚁)"——连小蚂蚁都能察觉最细微的新变化；nuance就是关于微小的差别。
