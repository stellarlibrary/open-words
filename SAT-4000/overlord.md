# 👑 overlord

🔊 /ˈoʊvərlɔːrd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A supreme ruler or master; a feudal lord who has authority over lesser lords
**中文：** n. 霸王；最高统治者；封建君主

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sovereign（君主）
- tyrant（暴君）
- ruler（统治者）
- suzerain（宗主）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feudal overlord / supreme overlord / military overlord / serve an overlord**

👉 Example: The feudal overlord demanded absolute loyalty from every vassal within his vast domain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medieval **overlord** controlled vast territories and extracted heavy taxes from the peasants who labored on his lands.
   这位中世纪的封建领主控制着广袤的领土，并向在其土地上劳作的农民征收沉重的赋税。

2. In the aftermath of the conquest, the victorious general installed himself as the **overlord** of the defeated provinces.
   在征服之后，胜利的将军自立为被征服省份的最高统治者。

3. The corporation functioned like a modern **overlord**, exerting enormous influence over the economic policies of several developing nations.
   该公司就像一个现代霸主，对几个发展中国家的经济政策施加了巨大的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-lord" — a lord who is "over" all other lords, the supreme master who stands above everyone else.
把"overlord"想成"在其他领主之上的领主"——一个凌驾于所有人之上的最高主宰。
