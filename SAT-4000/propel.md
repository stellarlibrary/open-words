# 🚀 propel

🔊 /prəˈpɛl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To drive or push something forward; to motivate or urge someone toward a particular course of action
**中文：** v. 推进；驱使；激励；驱策；推动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- drive（驱动）
- thrust（推进）
- impel（推动）
- motivate（激励）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **propel forward / propel into action / propel to fame / propel growth**

👉 Example: Her determination and talent propelled her to the top of her profession within a decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The strong tailwind helped **propel** the sailing vessel across the Atlantic Ocean in record-breaking time last summer.
   强劲的顺风帮助这艘帆船在去年夏天以破纪录的时间横渡了大西洋。

2. A growing sense of injustice **propelled** thousands of citizens to organize peaceful demonstrations throughout the capital city.
   日益增长的不公正感驱使数千名市民在首都各地组织和平示威活动。

3. Technological innovation continues to **propel** economic growth in developing nations that invest strategically in digital infrastructure.
   技术创新继续推动那些在数字基础设施方面进行战略投资的发展中国家的经济增长。

---

把"propel"想成"pro-pel"——一颗专业的弹丸，以巨大的力量推动事物向前。
