# 🎯 resolute

🔊 /ˈrɛzəˌluːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Admirably purposeful, determined, and unwavering in pursuit of a goal or belief
**中文：** adj. 坚决的，果断的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- determined（坚定的）
- steadfast（坚定不移的）
- unwavering（毫不动摇的）
- tenacious（顽强的）
- firm（坚定的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resolute in one's determination / resolute stance / resolute opposition / remain resolute / resolute leader**

👉 Example: The president remained resolute in her commitment to reforming the healthcare system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The activist remained **resolute** in her campaign for environmental justice despite facing repeated legal challenges and public criticism.
   这位活动家尽管面临反复的法律挑战和公众批评，仍坚定不移地推进她的环境正义运动。

2. A **resolute** leader must be willing to make difficult decisions even when popular opinion runs contrary to the chosen course.
   一位果断的领导者必须愿意做出艰难决定，即使民意与所选择的方向相悖。

3. The committee adopted a **resolute** stance against corruption, implementing strict oversight measures throughout every level of the organization.
   委员会对腐败采取了坚决立场，在组织的每个层级实施了严格的监督措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "resolute" as "re-solute" (solution) — someone who keeps finding solutions again and again, never giving up.
把"resolute"想成"re-solute"（解决方案）——一个不断寻找解决方案、永不放弃的人。
