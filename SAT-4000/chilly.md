# 🥶 chilly

🔊 /ˈtʃɪli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Uncomfortably cool or cold; unfriendly or unwelcoming in manner; lacking warmth of feeling
**中文：** adj. 寒冷的；怕冷的；冷淡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cold（寒冷的）
- frigid（冰冷的）
- cool（凉爽的）
- frosty（冷淡的）
- unwelcoming（不友好的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chilly weather / chilly reception / chilly atmosphere / chilly wind / feel chilly**

👉 Example: The ambassador received a chilly reception from the host government upon arrival.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The negotiations began with a **chilly** atmosphere as both delegations maintained formal and distant demeanors throughout.
   谈判在冷淡的气氛中开始，双方代表团自始至终都保持着正式而疏远的态度。

2. Despite the **chilly** autumn winds sweeping across the campus, hundreds of students gathered for the outdoor ceremony.
   尽管寒冷的秋风席卷整个校园，数百名学生仍聚集参加户外典礼。

3. The critic's **chilly** review of the novel discouraged the young author from submitting her subsequent manuscript.
   评论家对这部小说冷淡的评价使这位年轻作者不敢提交后续的手稿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chill-y" — "chill" already means cold, and "-y" makes it an adjective, so chilly simply means feeling cold.
把"chilly"拆成"chill（寒冷）+ y（形容词后缀）"——chill本身就是寒冷的意思，加上y就是"感觉冷的"。
