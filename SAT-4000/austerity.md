# 💎 austerity

🔊 /ɒˈstɛrɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality or state of being austere; enforced or extreme economy, especially reduction of government spending; severe simplicity; strict self-discipline or asceticism.
**中文：** 紧缩；朴素；苦行；严厉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frugality（节俭）
- severity（严酷）
- asceticism（禁欲主义）
- stringency（紧缩）
- plainness（朴素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **austerity measures/program/budget**, **period of austerity**, **fiscal austerity**, **wartime austerity**

👉 Example: The government's austerity measures sparked widespread protests across the nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The European debt crisis led several governments to implement severe **austerity** programs that reduced public services dramatically.
   欧洲债务危机导致多国政府实施了严厉的紧缩计划，大幅削减了公共服务。

2. The monk embraced a life of **austerity**, renouncing material possessions and dedicating himself entirely to spiritual contemplation.
   这位僧侣拥抱了苦行的生活，放弃物质财产，全身心投入精神冥想。

3. During wartime **austerity**, citizens accepted rigorous rationing of food and essential goods without significant complaint.
   在战时紧缩期间，公民们毫无怨言地接受了食品和必需品的严格配给。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Austerity" is the noun form of "austere." Picture a government tightening its belt — cutting spending, living plainly. The "-ity" suffix turns the adjective into the state of being austere.
"Austerity"是"austere"的名词形式。想象一个政府在勒紧裤腰带——削减开支，过着朴素的生活。"-ity"后缀将形容词变为"处于austere的状态"。
