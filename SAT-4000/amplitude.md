# 🌊 amplitude

🔊 /ˈæmplɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The maximum extent of a vibration or oscillation; breadth, range, or magnitude of something.
**中文：** 振幅；丰富，充足；广阔

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- magnitude（量级）
- extent（程度）
- breadth（广度）
- range（范围）
- vastness（广阔）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amplitude of a wave, signal amplitude, amplitude modulation, large/small amplitude**
👉 Example: The amplitude of the seismic waves indicated a powerful earthquake offshore.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist measured the **amplitude** of the electromagnetic wave to determine the intensity of the transmitted signal.
   物理学家测量了电磁波的振幅以确定传输信号的强度。

2. The **amplitude** of public discourse on climate policy has expanded remarkably over the past two decades.
   过去二十年间，关于气候政策的公共讨论的广度已经显著扩大。

3. Engineers adjusted the **amplitude** of the oscillations to ensure the bridge could withstand resonance under extreme conditions.
   工程师们调整了振荡的振幅，以确保桥梁能够在极端条件下承受共振。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ampl-" (large, full) + "-itude" (state of) = the state of being large, like how far a wave swings.
"Ampl-"（大的、满的）+ "-itude"（状态）= 大的状态，就像波浪摆动的幅度。
