# 🔢 plural

🔊 /ˈplʊrəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or denoting more than one in number; consisting of multiple elements. (n.) The form of a word used to refer to more than one.
**中文：** adj. 复数的 n. 复数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- multiple（多数的）
- numerous（许多的）
- several（若干的）
- manifold（多种多样的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plural form / plural noun / plural society / plural ending**

👉 Example: Students must learn to distinguish between singular and plural forms in both English and Latin.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **plural** form of "analysis" is "analyses," which follows the Greek pattern of noun declension.
   "analysis"的**复数**形式是"analyses"，遵循希腊语名词变格规则。

2. Modern democracies function best as **plural** societies that embrace cultural diversity and protect minority viewpoints.
   现代民主国家作为**多元**社会运作最佳，拥抱文化多样性并保护少数群体的观点。

3. The instructor reminded students to verify subject-verb agreement when using **plural** nouns in formal academic writing.
   教师提醒学生在正式学术写作中使用**复数**名词时要核实主谓一致。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plural" as "plus-ral" — the word starts with "plu" like "plus," meaning more than one, an addition.
把"plural"想成"plus-ral"——这个词以"plu"开头，像"plus"（加），意味着不止一个。
