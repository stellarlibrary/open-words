# 🫄 placenta

🔊 /pləˈsɛntə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A temporary organ in the uterus of pregnant mammals that nourishes and sustains the developing fetus; (botany) the part of a plant ovary to which ovules are attached
**中文：** n. 胎盘；（植物学）胎座

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- afterbirth（胞衣）
- fetal membrane（胎膜）
- chorion（绒毛膜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **placenta previa / placental barrier / deliver the placenta / placental function / placental tissue**

👉 Example: The physician monitored the position of the placenta throughout the pregnancy using ultrasound imaging.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **placenta** serves as the critical interface between mother and fetus, facilitating the exchange of nutrients and oxygen.
   胎盘是母体与胎儿之间的关键界面，促进营养物质和氧气的交换。

2. Researchers discovered that certain environmental toxins can cross the **placenta** and potentially affect the development of the unborn child.
   研究人员发现某些环境毒素可以穿过胎盘，并可能影响未出生婴儿的发育。

3. In botany, the **placenta** refers to the tissue within the ovary where ovules are attached and eventually develop into seeds.
   在植物学中，胎座是指子房内胚珠附着并最终发育成种子的组织。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plac-enta" as "place-enter" — the placenta is the place where nutrients enter the baby's body from the mother.
把"placenta"想成"place（地方）+ enter（进入）"——胎盘是营养从母体进入婴儿身体的地方。
