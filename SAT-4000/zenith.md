# ⭐ zenith

🔊 /ˈziːnɪθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The highest point reached by a celestial body; the peak or pinnacle of achievement, power, or success
**中文：** 天顶；最高点；顶峰；顶点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pinnacle（顶峰）
- apex（顶点）
- peak（巅峰）
- summit（最高点）
- acme（极点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach the zenith / at the zenith of / the zenith of one's career / past its zenith**

👉 Example: The empire reached its zenith during the eighteenth century before beginning its long, gradual decline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Renaissance represented the **zenith** of artistic and intellectual achievement in European history, producing masterworks that endure today.
   文艺复兴代表了欧洲历史上艺术和智识成就的顶峰，创造了至今流传的杰作。

2. At the **zenith** of her career, the acclaimed scientist held prestigious appointments at three of the world's leading universities.
   在事业的巅峰时期，这位著名科学家在世界三所顶尖大学担任重要职位。

3. Analysts observed that the technology sector had reached its **zenith** and predicted a period of market correction ahead.
   分析师观察到科技行业已达到顶峰，并预测前方将迎来一段市场调整期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "zenith" as the "Z" point — the very last letter, the ultimate top — just as the zenith is the highest point in the sky directly above you.
把"zenith"想成"Z"点——字母表的最后一个字母，终极的顶端——就像天顶是你头顶正上方天空的最高点。
