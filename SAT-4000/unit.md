# 🔢 unit

🔊 /ˈjuːnɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A single, distinct item or entity regarded as an elementary component; a standard quantity used for measurement
**中文：** 单位；单元；部件；组

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- component（组成部分）
- element（元素）
- module（模块）
- segment（部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unit of measurement / unit cost / military unit / housing unit / business unit**

👉 Example: The kilogram is the standard unit of mass in the International System of Units.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each **unit** of the apartment complex is equipped with modern appliances and energy-efficient heating and cooling systems.
   公寓综合体的每个单元都配备了现代化电器和节能供暖及制冷系统。

2. The biology course is divided into six **units**, each focusing on a distinct area of cellular structure and function.
   生物课程分为六个单元，每个单元侧重于细胞结构与功能的不同领域。

3. The military deployed a specialized **unit** to the region to conduct reconnaissance operations and assess the evolving security situation.
   军方向该地区部署了一支专业部队，以执行侦察行动并评估不断变化的安全局势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "unit" as coming from "unity" — it represents one single thing, the smallest whole piece that makes up a larger group.
把"unit"想成来自"unity（统一）"——它代表一个单独的事物，是构成更大整体的最小完整部分。
