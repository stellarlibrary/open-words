# 🦠 virus

🔊 /ˈvaɪrəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A microscopic infectious agent that replicates inside living cells; something that corrupts or poisons the mind or morals
**中文：** n. 病毒；恶毒；毒害

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pathogen（病原体）
- microbe（微生物）
- contagion（传染病）
- infection（感染）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **computer virus / virus outbreak / virus spread / virus mutation**

👉 Example: The newly identified virus spread rapidly through densely populated urban areas.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists worked around the clock to develop a vaccine capable of neutralizing the deadly **virus** before it spread further.
   科学家们夜以继日地工作，以开发一种能够在致命病毒进一步传播之前将其消灭的疫苗。

2. The computer **virus** infiltrated the corporate network, compromising thousands of sensitive files and financial records.
   计算机病毒渗透了公司网络，危及数千份敏感文件和财务记录。

3. Researchers discovered that the **virus** had undergone a significant genetic mutation, rendering existing treatments ineffective.
   研究人员发现该病毒已经历了重大基因突变，使现有治疗方法失效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virus" as containing "vir" (Latin for poison) — a virus is essentially a tiny poison that invades living things.
把"virus"想成含有"vir"（拉丁语意为毒药）——病毒本质上就是入侵生物体的微小毒素。
