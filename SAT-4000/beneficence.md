# 💝 beneficence

🔊 /bəˈnɛfɪsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being kind and charitable; an act of generosity or goodwill; a charitable gift or donation
**中文：** n. 慈善；善行；捐款；仁慈

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- charity（慈善）
- benevolence（仁慈）
- generosity（慷慨）
- philanthropy（博爱）
- altruism（利他主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an act of beneficence / the beneficence of / private beneficence / public beneficence**

👉 Example: The hospital was built entirely through the beneficence of local philanthropists and civic organizations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The foundation's extraordinary **beneficence** enabled thousands of underprivileged children to receive quality education and healthcare.
   该基金会非凡的善举使数以千计的贫困儿童获得了优质的教育和医疗服务。

2. Scholars have long debated whether true **beneficence** can exist without any expectation of personal recognition or reward.
   学者们长期争论，没有任何个人认可或回报期望的真正善行是否存在。

3. The principle of **beneficence** in medical ethics requires physicians to act in the best interest of their patients at all times.
   医学伦理中的善行原则要求医生在任何时候都以患者的最大利益为出发点行事。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bene-" (good) + "-ficence" (doing, from Latin "facere") — beneficence is the act of "doing good" for others.
"Bene-"（好的）+"ficence"（做，源自拉丁语"facere"）——beneficence就是"做好事"，即善行、慈善。
