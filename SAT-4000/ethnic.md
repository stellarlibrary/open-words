# 🌍 ethnic

🔊 /ˈɛθnɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to a population group sharing a common cultural heritage, nationality, language, or racial background
**中文：** adj. 人种的，种族的；民族的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- racial（种族的）
- cultural（文化的）
- tribal（部落的）
- indigenous（本土的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ethnic group / ethnic minority / ethnic diversity / ethnic background / ethnic identity**

👉 Example: The city's ethnic diversity is reflected in its wide variety of restaurants, festivals, and cultural institutions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sociologist's research focused on how **ethnic** identity influences political participation and civic engagement among immigrant communities.
   这位社会学家的研究聚焦于民族身份如何影响移民社区的政治参与和公民参与。

2. Preserving **ethnic** traditions and languages is essential for maintaining the cultural heritage of communities facing rapid modernization.
   保护民族传统和语言对于维护面临快速现代化的社区的文化遗产至关重要。

3. The census collected comprehensive data on **ethnic** composition to ensure equitable representation in government policies and public services.
   人口普查收集了全面的种族构成数据，以确保政府政策和公共服务中的公平代表性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ethnic" as "ETHNO + IC" — from Greek "ethnos" meaning nation or people, so ethnic relates to a distinct group of people.
把"ethnic"想成"ETHNO + IC"——源自希腊语"ethnos"，意为民族或人民，因此ethnic与特定人群相关。
