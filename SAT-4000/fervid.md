# 🔥 fervid

🔊 /ˈfɜːrvɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Intensely enthusiastic or passionate, often to an excessive degree; burning hot
**中文：** adj. 热烈的；热情的；激烈的；炽热的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fervent（热心的）
- ardent（热烈的）
- passionate（激情的）
- vehement（热切的）
- fiery（火热的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fervid imagination / fervid enthusiasm / fervid supporter / fervid devotion / fervid rhetoric**

👉 Example: His fervid imagination often produced ideas that were both brilliant and impractical.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The revolutionary leader's **fervid** rhetoric inspired thousands of citizens to join the movement for independence.
   这位革命领袖激烈的言辞激励了成千上万的公民加入独立运动。

2. Critics noted that the author's **fervid** prose style occasionally overwhelmed the subtlety of her narrative themes.
   评论家指出，这位作者热烈的散文风格有时掩盖了她叙事主题的微妙之处。

3. The scientist pursued her hypothesis with **fervid** determination, working through countless failed experiments without discouragement.
   这位科学家以炽热的决心追求她的假设，经历了无数次失败的实验而不气馁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fervid" as "ferv-id" — the "ferv" root means "to boil," so fervid means boiling over with intense passion.
把"fervid"想成"ferv-id"——词根"ferv"意为"沸腾"，所以fervid意味着热情沸腾到溢出。
