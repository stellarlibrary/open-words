# 🫧 acidify

🔊 /əˈsɪdɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make or become acid; to convert into an acid or increase acidity.
**中文：** 酸化；变酸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sour（使变酸）
- ferment（发酵）
- embitter（使苦涩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acidify the soil / water / ocean / solution**

👉 Example: Excessive carbon emissions acidify the oceans and threaten marine ecosystems.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Rising carbon dioxide levels continue to **acidify** ocean waters, endangering coral reefs and shellfish populations worldwide.
   不断上升的二氧化碳水平持续酸化海水，危及全球珊瑚礁和贝类种群。

2. Certain fertilizers can **acidify** the soil over time, reducing crop yields and degrading agricultural productivity.
   某些肥料会随着时间的推移使土壤酸化，降低作物产量和农业生产力。

3. The chemist added a reagent to **acidify** the solution and initiate the desired catalytic reaction.
   化学家添加了一种试剂来酸化溶液，以启动所需的催化反应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Acid" + "-ify" (to make) = to make something acidic. The suffix "-ify" always means "to cause to become."
"acid（酸）"+ "-ify（使……化）"= 使变酸。后缀"-ify"总是表示"使成为……"。
