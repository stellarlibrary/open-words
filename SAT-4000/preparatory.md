# 📋 preparatory

🔊 /prɪˈpærətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Serving as or carrying out preparation for a task or undertaking. (n.) A preparatory school
**中文：** adj. 预备的；准备的 n. 预科；预备学校

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- preliminary（初步的）
- introductory（入门的）
- precursory（先驱的）
- prefatory（序言的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preparatory work / preparatory course / preparatory school / preparatory to (doing something)**

👉 Example: The committee conducted several preparatory meetings before the formal negotiations commenced in January.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students enrolled in the **preparatory** program demonstrated measurably stronger performance in their subsequent university-level coursework.
   参加预备课程的学生在随后的大学课程中表现出明显更强的学业表现。

2. The engineers completed extensive **preparatory** testing of all structural components before the bridge construction was officially authorized.
   工程师们在桥梁建设正式获批之前，完成了所有结构部件的广泛预备测试。

3. **Preparatory** to launching the new initiative, the organization assembled a diverse task force of leading international experts.
   在启动新计划之前，该组织组建了一支由国际顶尖专家组成的多元化工作组。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "preparatory" as "prepare + atory" — the "-atory" suffix means "relating to," so it simply means "relating to preparation."
把"preparatory"想成"prepare（准备）+ atory（与…有关的）"——与准备有关的，即预备的。
