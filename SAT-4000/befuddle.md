# 😵 befuddle

🔊 /bɪˈfʌdəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To confuse or perplex; to stupefy, especially with alcohol; to make unable to think clearly
**中文：** vt. 使迷惑；使迷糊；使酒醉昏迷；使昏沉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confuse（使困惑）
- bewilder（使迷惑）
- perplex（使费解）
- stupefy（使昏沉）
- muddle（使混乱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **befuddled by the question / befuddled mind / befuddled with drink / completely befuddled**

👉 Example: The convoluted instructions left the students completely befuddled and unable to begin the assignment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's deliberately paradoxical lecture thoroughly **befuddled** even the most advanced students in the graduate seminar.
   教授故意设置悖论的讲座甚至让研究生研讨班上最优秀的学生都感到困惑不解。

2. After consuming several glasses of wine, the diplomat was visibly **befuddled** and struggled to articulate a coherent response.
   在喝了几杯葡萄酒后，这位外交官明显变得昏沉，难以表达出连贯的回应。

3. The sheer volume of contradictory evidence presented at the trial **befuddled** the jury and prolonged their deliberations significantly.
   审判中呈现的大量相互矛盾的证据使陪审团困惑不已，大大延长了他们的审议时间。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be + fuddle" — to fuddle means to intoxicate or confuse, so befuddle intensifies that into total confusion and bewilderment.
把"befuddle"拆成"be + fuddle（灌醉、迷惑）"——fuddle是使人醉或糊涂，befuddle则加强了这种效果，表示彻底的困惑。
