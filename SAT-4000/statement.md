# 📜 statement

🔊 /ˈsteɪtmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A definite or clear expression of something; a formal account of facts or opinions; a financial report or summary
**中文：** n. 声明，陈述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- declaration（声明）
- announcement（公告）
- assertion（断言）
- proclamation（宣告）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **issue a statement / make a statement / financial statement / opening statement**

👉 Example: The president issued a formal statement condemning the recent acts of violence in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company released an official **statement** clarifying its position on the proposed merger with its largest competitor.
   该公司发布了一份正式声明，阐明其对与最大竞争对手拟议合并的立场。

2. The witness provided a detailed **statement** to the investigators describing the sequence of events leading to the incident.
   证人向调查人员提供了一份详细的陈述，描述了导致事件发生的经过。

3. The quarterly financial **statement** revealed a significant increase in revenue compared to the same period last year.
   季度财务报表显示，与去年同期相比收入大幅增长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "state-ment" as "to state something meant seriously" — a statement is when you formally state what you mean.
把"statement"想成"state + ment"——正式地表述你的意思，就是一份声明或陈述。
