# 🚨 kleptomaniac

🔊 /ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniæk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who has a persistent, compulsive urge to steal, often without regard for need or profit
**中文：** n. 有窃盗癖的人；有盗窃癖的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thief（窃贼）
- pilferer（小偷）
- shoplifter（商店扒手）
- compulsive stealer（强迫性偷窃者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diagnosed as a kleptomaniac / compulsive kleptomaniac / a known kleptomaniac / recovering kleptomaniac**

👉 Example: The court ordered a psychological evaluation after the defendant was suspected of being a kleptomaniac.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The psychiatrist determined that the patient was a **kleptomaniac** whose compulsive stealing was driven by anxiety rather than need.
   精神科医生确定该患者是一个盗窃癖患者，其强迫性偷窃行为是由焦虑而非需要驱动的。

2. Although affluent and respected in her community, the woman was secretly a **kleptomaniac** who could not resist taking small items.
   尽管她在社区中富裕且受人尊敬，这位女士却暗地里是一个无法抗拒偷拿小物品的盗窃癖患者。

3. The defense attorney argued that the accused was a **kleptomaniac** and therefore required treatment rather than incarceration.
   辩护律师辩称被告是一个盗窃癖患者，因此需要治疗而非监禁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "klepto-maniac" as "kept-o-maniac" — a maniac who has secretly "kept" things that belong to others, unable to stop the compulsion.
把"kleptomaniac"想成"kept-o-maniac"——一个疯狂地偷偷"kept"（保留）别人东西的人，无法停止这种冲动。
