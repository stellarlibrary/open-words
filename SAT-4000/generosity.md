# 💝 generosity

🔊 /ˌdʒɛnəˈrɒsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being kind, understanding, and willing to give money, help, or time freely; largeness of spirit
**中文：** n. 慷慨，大方；宽宏大量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- benevolence（仁慈）
- magnanimity（宽宏大量）
- liberality（慷慨）
- philanthropy（慈善）
- munificence（慷慨大方）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **generosity of spirit / act of generosity / show generosity / remarkable generosity**

👉 Example: The community was deeply moved by the generosity of anonymous donors who funded the new library.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The foundation's extraordinary **generosity** enabled hundreds of underprivileged students to pursue higher education at prestigious universities.
   该基金会非凡的慷慨使数百名贫困学生能够在知名大学接受高等教育。

2. Historians have noted that the **generosity** of Renaissance patrons played a crucial role in fostering artistic innovation.
   历史学家指出，文艺复兴时期赞助人的慷慨在促进艺术创新方面发挥了关键作用。

3. Her **generosity** extended beyond financial contributions to include countless hours of volunteer work in the community.
   她的慷慨不仅限于经济捐助，还包括在社区中无数小时的志愿服务。

---
