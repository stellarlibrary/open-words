# 😞 crestfallen

🔊 /ˈkrɛstˌfɔːlən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Sad and disappointed; dejected or dispirited, especially after having one's hopes or expectations dashed
**中文：** adj. 垂头丧气的，气馁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dejected（沮丧的）
- disheartened（灰心的）
- despondent（意志消沉的）
- downcast（垂头丧气的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **look crestfallen / feel crestfallen / appear crestfallen / visibly crestfallen**

👉 Example: The student looked crestfallen after learning that her scholarship application had been rejected.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The team appeared visibly **crestfallen** after their unexpected defeat in the championship final round.
   球队在锦标赛决赛中意外失利后，显得垂头丧气。

2. He tried to conceal his **crestfallen** expression when the promotion was awarded to his younger colleague instead.
   当晋升机会被给予他更年轻的同事时，他试图掩饰自己沮丧的表情。

3. The scientist was **crestfallen** upon discovering that her pioneering research had already been published by another laboratory.
   这位科学家发现她的开创性研究已被另一个实验室发表后，感到非常气馁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crest-fallen" — the crest (top/crown) has fallen down, like a rooster whose proud comb droops in defeat.
把"crestfallen"想成"冠冕落下"——骄傲的冠顶掉落了，就像公鸡在失败后垂下了它骄傲的鸡冠。
