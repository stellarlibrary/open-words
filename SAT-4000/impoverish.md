# 💸 impoverish

🔊 /ɪmˈpɑːvərɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make poor; to reduce to poverty; to deplete the strength, richness, or vitality of something
**中文：** v. 使贫穷；使枯竭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pauperize（使赤贫）
- deplete（使枯竭）
- bankrupt（使破产）
- diminish（减少）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impoverish a nation / impoverish the soil / impoverished communities / culturally impoverished**

👉 Example: Decades of exploitative trade policies have impoverished many developing nations across the continent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged civil war served to **impoverish** the entire region, destroying infrastructure and displacing millions of vulnerable civilians.
   旷日持久的内战使整个地区陷入贫困，摧毁了基础设施并使数百万弱势平民流离失所。

2. Intensive monoculture farming practices gradually **impoverish** the soil by stripping it of essential nutrients and beneficial microorganisms.
   集约化单一种植方式逐渐使土壤枯竭，剥夺了其必需的养分和有益微生物。

3. Critics argue that excessive censorship will **impoverish** the nation's cultural and intellectual life by stifling creative expression and debate.
   批评者认为过度审查将通过压制创造性表达和辩论来使国家的文化和知识生活贫乏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "IM-POVER-ISH" — it contains "poverty" right in the middle, making someone move toward poverty.
把"impoverish"想成中间包含"poverty（贫穷）"——使某人走向贫穷。
