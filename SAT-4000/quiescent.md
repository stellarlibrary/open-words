# 🌙 quiescent

🔊 /kwiˈɛsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Being at rest; quiet; still; inactive; in a state of dormancy or inactivity
**中文：** adj. 静止的；不活动的；沉寂的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dormant（休眠的）
- inactive（不活跃的）
- latent（潜伏的）
- tranquil（平静的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quiescent state / quiescent period / remain quiescent / largely quiescent**

👉 Example: The volcano had been quiescent for decades before suddenly erupting with devastating force.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The disease remained **quiescent** for several years before reappearing with renewed severity in the patient's immune system.
   这种疾病在患者的免疫系统中沉寂了数年后，又以更强的严重程度重新出现。

2. During the winter months, the normally bustling marketplace became **quiescent**, with only a few vendors braving the cold.
   在冬季，通常繁忙的市场变得沉寂，只有少数商贩冒着严寒坚持营业。

3. Political opposition forces were **quiescent** throughout the decade, but social unrest gradually awakened widespread calls for reform.
   政治反对力量在整个十年间都处于沉寂状态，但社会动荡逐渐唤醒了广泛的改革呼声。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quiescent" as "quiet + escent" — something becoming quiet, entering a state of stillness and rest.
把"quiescent"想成"quiet + escent"——某物正在变得安静，进入静止和休息的状态。
