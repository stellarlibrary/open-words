# ⚖️ outweigh

🔊 /ˌaʊtˈweɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To be greater or more significant than; to exceed in weight, value, or importance
**中文：** v. 比…重要；比…有价值；在重量上超过

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surpass（超过）
- exceed（超出）
- overshadow（使相形见绌）
- predominate（占优势）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outweigh the benefits / outweigh the risks / outweigh the costs / outweigh the disadvantages**

👉 Example: The potential benefits of the new treatment far outweigh the associated risks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many economists argue that the long-term advantages of free trade agreements significantly **outweigh** the short-term disruptions.
   许多经济学家认为，自由贸易协定的长期优势远远超过短期干扰。

2. The environmental costs of continued deforestation clearly **outweigh** the temporary economic gains derived from logging activities.
   持续砍伐森林的环境代价明显超过伐木活动带来的暂时经济收益。

3. In the committee's final assessment, the evidence supporting the proposal **outweighed** the objections raised by opponents.
   在委员会的最终评估中，支持该提案的证据超过了反对者提出的异议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out-weigh" as "weighing more on the outside" — one side of the scale tips down because it outweighs the other.
把"outweigh"想成"在天平上称出更重"——天平的一端因为更重要而倾斜下去。
