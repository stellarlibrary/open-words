# ⚖️ onus

🔊 /ˈoʊnəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A duty or responsibility; a burden of proof or obligation that falls on someone
**中文：** n. 责任，义务；负担

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- burden（负担）
- responsibility（责任）
- obligation（义务）
- liability（法律责任）
- duty（职责）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **onus of proof / onus is on someone / onus falls on / bear the onus**

👉 Example: The onus is on the prosecution to prove the defendant's guilt beyond a reasonable doubt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In civil litigation, the **onus** of establishing a valid claim rests squarely upon the plaintiff who initiates the lawsuit.
   在民事诉讼中，证明索赔有效的责任完全由提起诉讼的原告承担。

2. The manager placed the **onus** on each team member to ensure that project deadlines were consistently met without exception.
   经理将责任放在每位团队成员身上，确保项目截止日期始终如期完成。

3. Environmental advocates argue that the **onus** for reducing carbon emissions should fall primarily on large industrial corporations.
   环保倡导者认为，减少碳排放的责任应主要由大型工业企业承担。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "onus" as "own us" — a responsibility that OWNS US, something we cannot escape and must carry.
把"onus"想成"own us"——一种拥有（own）我们（us）的责任，我们无法逃避且必须承担。
