# ⚔️ embattle

🔊 /ɪmˈbætl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To arrange troops in battle formation; to prepare for battle or conflict; to fortify against attack
**中文：** v. 布阵；整军备战

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fortify（加强防御）
- entrench（设防）
- marshal（整编）
- arm（武装）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **embattled leader / embattled position / embattled troops / embattled city**

👉 Example: The general ordered his forces to embattle along the ridge before the enemy advanced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The commander decided to **embattle** his forces along the northern frontier in anticipation of a large-scale enemy offensive.
   指挥官决定沿北部边境布阵，以防备大规模的敌军进攻。

2. The **embattled** governor faced mounting pressure from both political opponents and citizens demanding immediate institutional reforms.
   这位四面楚歌的州长面临着来自政治对手和要求立即进行制度改革的公民的双重压力。

3. Medieval architects designed castle walls with **embattled** parapets to provide defenders with effective cover during prolonged siege warfare.
   中世纪建筑师设计了带有城垛的城堡墙壁，以在持久的围城战中为防御者提供有效掩护。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "em-BATTLE" — it literally contains "battle." To embattle means to get ready for battle by arranging forces in formation.
把"embattle"想成"em-BATTLE"——它字面上就包含"battle"（战斗）。备战就是通过布阵来为战斗做好准备。
