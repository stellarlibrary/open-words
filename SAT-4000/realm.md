# 👑 realm

🔊 /rɛlm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A kingdom; a field or domain of activity, interest, or knowledge
**中文：** n. 领域，范围；王国；国土

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- domain（领域）
- kingdom（王国）
- sphere（范围）
- territory（领土）
- field（领域）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in the realm of / beyond the realm of / public realm / realm of possibility / realm of science**

👉 Example: Her groundbreaking research opened new possibilities in the realm of quantum computing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher's influential work explored the **realm** of metaphysics, challenging long-established assumptions about the nature of reality itself.
   这位哲学家的重要著作探索了形而上学领域，挑战了关于现实本质的长期既有假设。

2. Such extraordinary technological advancements were once considered entirely beyond the **realm** of possibility by even the most optimistic scientists.
   即使是最乐观的科学家也曾认为如此非凡的技术进步完全超出了可能的范围。

3. The medieval **realm** prospered under the enlightened monarch's rule, enjoying an unprecedented era of cultural achievement and economic growth.
   这个中世纪王国在开明君主的统治下繁荣兴旺，迎来了文化成就和经济增长的空前时代。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "realm" as "real + m (map)" — a realm is a real territory you can find on a map, whether it is a physical kingdom or an abstract domain of knowledge.
把"realm"想成"real（真实的）+ m（地图map）"——realm是你能在地图上找到的真实领土，无论是实际的王国还是抽象的知识领域。
