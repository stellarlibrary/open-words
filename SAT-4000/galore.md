# 🎁 galore

🔊 /ɡəˈlɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj./adv.) In abundance; in great numbers or amounts, usually placed after the noun it modifies
**中文：** adj. 丰盛的；丰富的 adv. 丰富地；大量地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- plentiful（充足的）
- copious（大量的）
- aplenty（大量地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **opportunities galore / bargains galore / prizes galore** — always placed AFTER the noun

👉 Example: The festival offered entertainment galore, from live music to theatrical performances.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research library contained primary sources **galore**, providing scholars with unprecedented access to historical documents.
   该研究图书馆拥有大量原始资料，为学者们提供了前所未有的历史文献获取途径。

2. The annual conference featured networking opportunities **galore**, attracting professionals from every major industry sector.
   年度会议提供了大量的交流机会，吸引了各主要行业的专业人士。

3. Critics noted that the manuscript had errors **galore**, suggesting that the author had rushed through the revision process.
   评论家指出该手稿错误百出，表明作者在修改过程中过于仓促。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "galore" as "ga-MORE" — when there is galore of something, there is always more and more of it.
把"galore"想成"ga-MORE"——当某物galore时，总是越来越多，源源不断。
