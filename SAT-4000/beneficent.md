# 🌟 beneficent

🔊 /bəˈnɛfɪsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Performing acts of kindness and charity; producing good results; having a helpful or beneficial effect
**中文：** adj. 慈善的；善行的；有益的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- benevolent（仁慈的）
- charitable（慈善的）
- generous（慷慨的）
- philanthropic（博爱的）
- altruistic（利他的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a beneficent ruler / a beneficent influence / a beneficent act / beneficent effects**

👉 Example: The beneficent queen devoted much of the royal treasury to improving the lives of her poorest subjects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **beneficent** organization provided clean drinking water and essential medical supplies to villages devastated by the drought.
   这个慈善组织为遭受旱灾的村庄提供了洁净饮用水和基本医疗物资。

2. History remembers her as a **beneficent** ruler whose progressive policies lifted millions of citizens out of poverty.
   历史铭记她是一位仁慈的统治者，其进步的政策使数百万公民摆脱了贫困。

3. The scientist's research had a profoundly **beneficent** impact on public health, reducing infectious disease mortality by half.
   这位科学家的研究对公共健康产生了深远的有益影响，使传染病死亡率降低了一半。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "beneficent" as "bene-" (good) + "-ficent" (making) — someone who is beneficent is "making good things happen."
"Bene-"（好的）+"ficent"（制造）——beneficent就是"制造好事的"，即慈善的、有益的。
