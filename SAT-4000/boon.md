# 🎁 boon

🔊 /buːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A blessing or benefit; a timely advantage; something to be thankful for. (adj.) Jovial; convivial; generous
**中文：** n. 恩惠；福利；利益 adj. 愉快的；慷慨的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blessing（祝福）
- benefit（利益）
- advantage（优势）
- godsend（天赐之物）
- windfall（意外收获）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a boon to / a boon for / prove a boon / boon companion**

👉 Example: The new public transportation system proved to be a tremendous boon to commuters throughout the metropolitan area.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship was a significant **boon** to students from disadvantaged backgrounds who aspired to pursue higher education.
   该奖学金对渴望接受高等教育的弱势背景学生而言是一项重大恩惠。

2. Advances in renewable energy technology have been a considerable **boon** for nations seeking to reduce their dependence on fossil fuels.
   可再生能源技术的进步对寻求减少化石燃料依赖的国家来说是一大福音。

3. Access to clean drinking water remains an invaluable **boon** that many communities in developing regions still struggle to obtain.
   获得清洁饮用水仍然是许多发展中地区社区难以获得的宝贵福利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "boon" rhyming with "moon" — like moonlight that illuminates the darkness, a boon brightens your situation with unexpected benefit.
把"boon"与"moon"押韵联想——就像月光照亮黑暗一样，恩惠以意想不到的好处点亮你的处境。
