# 🎯 explicit

🔊 /ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion or doubt; direct and outspoken
**中文：** adj. 明确的；清楚的；直率的；详述的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clear（清楚的）
- unambiguous（明确的）
- definite（确定的）
- straightforward（直截了当的）
- precise（精确的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **explicit instructions / explicit statement / explicit content / make something explicit**

👉 Example: The contract contained explicit terms regarding the responsibilities of each party involved.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The supervisor provided **explicit** instructions to ensure that every member of the team understood the project requirements.
   主管提供了明确的指示，以确保团队的每个成员都了解项目要求。

2. The author's **explicit** criticism of government corruption sparked widespread debate among scholars and political commentators.
   作者对政府腐败的直率批评在学者和政治评论家中引发了广泛的讨论。

3. The regulations require companies to provide **explicit** warnings about potential health risks associated with their products.
   法规要求公司对其产品可能带来的健康风险提供明确的警告。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "explicit" as "ex-plicit" where "plic" means fold — something explicit has been completely unfolded, with nothing hidden or ambiguous.
把"explicit"想成"ex-plicit"，其中"plic"表示折叠——某事是explicit的，意味着它被完全展开，没有任何隐藏或模糊之处。
