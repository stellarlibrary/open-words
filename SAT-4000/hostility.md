# ⚔️ hostility

🔊 /hɒˈstɪləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unfriendly or aggressive feelings or behavior; opposition or resistance; (plural) acts of warfare
**中文：** n. 敌意；敌视；敌对；反对；战争行动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- antagonism（对抗）
- animosity（仇恨）
- enmity（敌意）
- aggression（攻击性）
- opposition（反对）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hostility toward / open hostility / deep-seated hostility / cease hostilities / outbreak of hostilities**

👉 Example: Negotiations broke down amid growing hostility between the two delegations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat warned that continued **hostility** between the two nations could escalate into a full-scale military confrontation.
   外交官警告说，两国之间持续的敌对状态可能升级为全面军事对抗。

2. The new policy was met with considerable **hostility** from labor unions, who argued it would erode workers' fundamental rights.
   新政策遭到工会的强烈反对，他们认为该政策会侵蚀工人的基本权利。

3. After decades of **hostility**, the neighboring countries finally signed a comprehensive peace treaty ending all border disputes.
   经过几十年的敌对，邻国终于签署了一项全面和平条约，结束了所有边界争端。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hostility" from "hostile" — like an unwelcome "host" who treats guests with aggression instead of warmth.
把"hostility"联想为来自"hostile"——就像一个不友好的"host"（主人），用攻击性而非热情对待客人。
