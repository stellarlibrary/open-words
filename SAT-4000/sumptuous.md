# 👑 sumptuous

🔊 /ˈsʌmptʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Splendid and expensive-looking; lavish; magnificent in appearance
**中文：** adj. 奢侈的，豪华的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lavish（奢华的）
- luxurious（豪华的）
- opulent（富丽堂皇的）
- magnificent（宏伟的）
- extravagant（奢侈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sumptuous feast / sumptuous decor / sumptuous banquet / sumptuous surroundings**

👉 Example: The guests were treated to a sumptuous banquet featuring delicacies from around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador hosted a **sumptuous** dinner at the embassy, complete with crystal chandeliers and imported wines.
   大使在使馆举办了一场豪华的晚宴，配有水晶吊灯和进口葡萄酒。

2. The palace interior was decorated in a **sumptuous** style that reflected the monarch's considerable wealth and refined taste.
   宫殿内部以奢华的风格装饰，体现了君主的雄厚财富和高雅品味。

3. Art historians described the painting's **sumptuous** use of color and texture as characteristic of the late Baroque period.
   艺术史学家将这幅画对色彩和质感的华丽运用描述为晚期巴洛克时期的特征。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sumptuous" as "sump-too-us" — it costs a sum too much for us, because it is so lavish and expensive.
把"sumptuous"想成"sump-too-us"——对我们来说花费太多了，因为它太奢华昂贵了。
