# 😈 vicious

🔊 /ˈvɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deliberately cruel or violent; having the nature of vice; extremely severe or intense
**中文：** adj. 恶毒的，凶残的，邪恶的；剧烈的；品性不端的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cruel（残忍的）
- malicious（恶意的）
- savage（凶猛的）
- brutal（残暴的）
- ferocious（凶恶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vicious attack / vicious cycle / vicious rumor / vicious criticism / vicious circle**

👉 Example: The editorial launched a vicious attack on the administration's handling of the economic crisis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The critic's **vicious** review of the novel discouraged many potential readers from purchasing it at bookstores.
   评论家对这部小说恶毒的评价使许多潜在读者打消了购买的念头。

2. Poverty and lack of education often create a **vicious** cycle that is extremely difficult for families to escape.
   贫困和教育缺乏往往形成一个恶性循环，家庭极难从中摆脱。

3. The committee condemned the **vicious** propaganda campaign designed to undermine public confidence in democratic institutions.
   委员会谴责了旨在破坏公众对民主制度信心的恶毒宣传活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vicious" as coming from "vice" (evil) + "-ious" — full of vice, therefore cruel and wicked in nature.
把"vicious"想成来自"vice"（邪恶）+"-ious"——充满邪恶的，因此本性残忍而邪恶。
