# 📢 advert

🔊 /ədˈvɜːrt/ (v.) · /ˈæd.vɜːrt/ (n.)<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To refer to or call attention to something; (n.) an advertisement.
**中文：** (v.) 注意；谈到；提及；(n.) 广告

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refer（提及）
- mention（谈到）
- allude（暗指）
- notice（注意）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **advert to + [topic/issue/matter]** (formal verb usage)

👉 Example: The speaker adverted to the pressing issue of climate change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor briefly **adverted** to the ethical implications of genetic engineering during the lecture.
   教授在讲座中简要提及了基因工程的伦理问题。

2. Before concluding her remarks, the ambassador **adverted** to the need for stronger diplomatic relations.
   在结束发言之前，大使提到了加强外交关系的必要性。

3. The editorial **adverted** to growing public concern over data privacy in the digital age.
   这篇社论谈到了数字时代公众对数据隐私日益增长的担忧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Advert" = "ad- (toward) + vert (turn)" — to turn your attention toward something, or a notice that turns your attention (an ad).
联想记忆：advert = ad（朝向）+ vert（转），即"把注意力转向"某事，或一则让你转移注意力的广告。
