# ✖️ intersect

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈsɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cross or cut across each other; to meet and cross at a point; to overlap or share common elements
**中文：** v. 相交；交叉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cross（交叉）
- overlap（重叠）
- converge（汇聚）
- bisect（二等分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intersect at a point / intersect with each other / where two paths intersect / intersecting lines**

👉 Example: The two highways intersect just north of the city, forming a major transportation hub.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers discovered that several important trade routes **intersected** at this ancient city, explaining its historical significance.
   研究人员发现，几条重要的贸易路线在这座古城交汇，这解释了其历史重要性。

2. In advanced mathematics, students learn to calculate the precise coordinates where two geometric planes **intersect** in space.
   在高等数学中，学生学习计算两个几何平面在空间中相交的精确坐标。

3. The themes of social justice and economic inequality **intersect** throughout the author's critically acclaimed body of literary work.
   社会正义与经济不平等的主题贯穿于这位作者广受好评的文学作品中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-sect" as "inter" (between) + "sect" (cut) — cutting between each other, like two lines crossing at a point.
把"intersect"想成"inter"（之间）+ "sect"（切）——互相切入，就像两条线在一个点上交叉。
