# ✨ enthrall

🔊 /ɪnˈθrɔːl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To captivate or charm completely; to hold spellbound with fascination or delight
**中文：** v. 迷住；使着迷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- captivate（迷住）
- fascinate（使着迷）
- enchant（使陶醉）
- mesmerize（使入迷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enthrall an audience / enthralled by a performance / enthralling story / utterly enthralled**

👉 Example: The virtuoso pianist enthralled the audience with a breathtaking interpretation of the concerto.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary **enthralled** viewers worldwide with its stunning cinematography and compelling narrative about ocean conservation.
   这部纪录片以其惊人的摄影技术和关于海洋保护的引人入胜的叙事迷住了全球观众。

2. Students were completely **enthralled** by the professor's vivid account of ancient civilizations and their enduring cultural legacies.
   学生们被教授对古代文明及其持久文化遗产的生动描述完全迷住了。

3. The novel's intricate plot and richly developed characters **enthralled** critics and readers alike throughout the literary season.
   这部小说错综复杂的情节和丰满的人物形象在整个文学季都令评论家和读者着迷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-thrall" as being put under a "thrall" (a spell) — someone who enthralls you holds you captive with wonder.
把"enthrall"想成被置于"thrall（奴役，魔咒）"之下——令你着迷的人用魅力将你俘虏。
