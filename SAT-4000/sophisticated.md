# 💎 sophisticated

🔊 /səˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Highly developed and complex; having worldly knowledge and refinement; elegant and cultured
**中文：** adj. 尖端的，复杂的，先进的；老练的，老于世故的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refined（精致的）
- worldly（世故的）
- complex（复杂的）
- cultured（有教养的）
- advanced（先进的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sophisticated technology / sophisticated taste / sophisticated approach / sophisticated system / sophisticated audience**

👉 Example: The laboratory employed sophisticated instruments capable of detecting molecular changes at the atomic level.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team developed a **sophisticated** algorithm capable of analyzing vast datasets with unprecedented speed and remarkable accuracy.
   研究团队开发了一种复杂的算法，能够以前所未有的速度和惊人的准确性分析大量数据集。

2. Her **sophisticated** understanding of international diplomacy made her an invaluable asset during the multilateral trade negotiations.
   她对国际外交老练的理解使她在多边贸易谈判中成为不可或缺的人才。

3. The museum's newly installed **sophisticated** climate control system ensures optimal preservation conditions for its priceless collection of artifacts.
   博物馆新安装的先进气候控制系统确保了其珍贵文物收藏的最佳保存条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sophisticated" as someone who has been shaped by the wisdom of the Sophists — worldly, knowledgeable, and highly refined.
把"sophisticated"想成一个被智者派的智慧所塑造的人——见多识广、知识渊博、修养极高。
