# 🔊 acoustics

🔊 /əˈkuːstɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of physics concerned with the properties of sound; the qualities of a room or building that determine how sound is transmitted within it
**中文：** 声学；音响效果，音质

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sound quality（音质）
- resonance（共鸣）
- sonics（声学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **room acoustics / study acoustics / the acoustics of / improve the acoustics**

👉 Example: The acoustics of the newly renovated theater received universal praise from musicians and critics alike.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **acoustics** in the cathedral were so remarkable that even a whisper could be heard clearly across the nave.
   这座大教堂的音响效果非常出色，即使是低语也能在中殿清楚地听到。

2. The professor specialized in **acoustics** and published several influential papers on underwater sound propagation.
   这位教授专攻声学，发表了多篇关于水下声音传播的有影响力的论文。

3. Architects carefully considered the **acoustics** when designing the new concert venue to ensure optimal sound distribution.
   建筑师在设计新音乐厅时仔细考虑了音响效果，以确保最佳的声音分布。

把"acoustics"想成"a-coo-sticks"——想象鸽子一边咕咕叫一边敲棍子，测试房间的音质。
