# 🧮 accountant

🔊 /əˈkaʊn.tənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A professional whose job is to keep, inspect, and analyze financial records.
**中文：** 会计师；会计人员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bookkeeper（簿记员）
- auditor（审计员）
- comptroller（财务总监）
- treasurer（财务主管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **certified/chartered accountant; hire/consult an accountant**

👉 Example: We hired a certified accountant to prepare the annual tax returns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **accountant** discovered a significant discrepancy in the company's quarterly financial statements.
   会计师在公司的季度财务报表中发现了一个重大差异。

2. Before launching the venture, the entrepreneur consulted an experienced **accountant** for sound fiscal advice.
   在启动项目之前，这位企业家咨询了一位经验丰富的会计师以获取可靠的财务建议。

3. A skilled **accountant** can identify tax deductions that ordinary individuals might easily overlook.
   一位熟练的会计师能发现普通人可能容易忽略的税收减免项。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "account-ANT" — picture a tiny ant diligently counting coins, keeping perfect records of every penny.
accountant 就是"管 account（账目）的人"，想象一只蚂蚁（ant）在认真记账，就是"会计师"。
