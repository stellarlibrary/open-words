# 🔢 factor

🔊 /ˈfæktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A circumstance, fact, or influence that contributes to a result; a number that divides into another without a remainder. (v.) To resolve or be resolvable into factors
**中文：** n. 因素；要素；因数；代理人 v. 将…分解因子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- element（要素）
- component（组成部分）
- determinant（决定因素）
- ingredient（因素）
- contributor（促成因素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **key factor / contributing factor / decisive factor / factor in / factor out**

👉 Example: Cost remains a significant factor in determining whether the project will proceed as planned.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers identified socioeconomic inequality as the primary **factor** contributing to disparities in educational achievement across the region.
   研究人员确定社会经济不平等是导致该地区教育成就差异的主要因素。

2. The mathematician demonstrated how to **factor** the complex polynomial into simpler expressions that could be solved independently.
   这位数学家演示了如何将复杂的多项式分解为可以独立求解的更简单的表达式。

3. Environmental degradation was cited as a critical **factor** accelerating the decline of biodiversity in tropical rainforest ecosystems worldwide.
   环境退化被认为是加速全球热带雨林生态系统生物多样性减少的关键因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "factor" as coming from Latin "facere" (to make/do) — a factor is something that "makes" a result happen, a contributing maker.
把"factor"想成来自拉丁语"facere"(制造/做)——factor就是"制造"结果的东西，一个促成的要素。
