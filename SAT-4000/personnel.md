# 👥 personnel

🔊 /ˌpɜːrsəˈnɛl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The people employed in an organization or engaged in a service; a department dealing with employees. (adj.) Relating to the employees of an organization
**中文：** n. 人事部门；全体人员 adj. 人员的；有关人事的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- staff（员工）
- workforce（劳动力）
- employees（雇员）
- human resources（人力资源）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **military personnel / personnel department / key personnel / personnel management**

👉 Example: The company's personnel department is responsible for recruitment, training, and employee welfare.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital urgently deployed additional medical **personnel** to manage the sudden influx of patients during the crisis.
   医院在危机期间紧急调派了更多医疗人员来应对突然涌入的患者。

2. Senior **personnel** in the organization were required to undergo comprehensive security clearance before accessing classified information.
   该组织的高级人员在访问机密信息之前必须通过全面的安全审查。

3. The restructuring plan called for reducing administrative **personnel** while increasing investment in frontline research and development.
   重组计划要求减少行政人员，同时增加对一线研发的投资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "person-nel" — a group of "persons" working together in an organization, the collective "person-pool" of a company.
把"personnel"想成"person-nel"——一群在组织中共同工作的"人"，公司的集体"人员库"。
