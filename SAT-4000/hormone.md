# 💉 hormone

🔊 /ˈhɔːrmoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A chemical substance produced by glands in the body that regulates the activity of cells or organs; a regulatory compound in organisms
**中文：** n. 激素；荷尔蒙

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chemical messenger（化学信使）
- secretion（分泌物）
- endocrine substance（内分泌物质）
- regulatory compound（调节化合物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **growth hormone / hormone levels / hormone imbalance / release hormones / regulate hormones**

👉 Example: The endocrine system releases hormones directly into the bloodstream to regulate bodily functions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Elevated levels of the stress **hormone** cortisol can impair cognitive function and weaken the immune system over time.
   压力激素皮质醇水平升高会随着时间推移损害认知功能并削弱免疫系统。

2. Researchers discovered that a specific **hormone** produced by the thyroid gland plays a critical role in metabolic regulation.
   研究人员发现甲状腺产生的一种特定激素在代谢调节中起着关键作用。

3. During adolescence, fluctuations in **hormone** levels contribute to rapid physical growth and significant emotional changes.
   在青春期，激素水平的波动导致身体快速发育和显著的情绪变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hormone" from Greek "hormon" meaning "to set in motion" — hormones are the body's chemical signals that set biological processes in motion.
"hormone"源自希腊语"hormon"，意为"使运动起来"——激素是使身体生物过程运转起来的化学信号。
