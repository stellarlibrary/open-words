# 👍 approval

🔊 /əˈpruːvəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The act of officially agreeing to or accepting something; a favorable opinion or judgment; consent or sanction.

**中文：** 批准；认可；赞成；同意。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- endorsement（认可）
- consent（同意）
- ratification（批准）
- sanction（许可）
- approbation（赞许）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** win approval, gain approval, seek approval, give approval, stamp of approval, approval rating, meet with approval

**Example:** The project awaits final approval from the regulatory authorities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new legislation received unanimous **approval** from every member of the legislative assembly yesterday.
   （新立法昨天获得了立法议会每位成员的一致批准。）

2. She sought her mentor's **approval** before submitting her research proposal to the academic journal.
   （她在向学术期刊提交研究提案之前征求了导师的认可。）

3. Public **approval** of the environmental policy increased substantially after the government published detailed data.
   （在政府公布详细数据后，公众对环保政策的赞成度大幅上升。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Approval" = "approve" + "-al" (noun suffix). If you "approve" of something, you give it your "approval" — your official thumbs-up.

"Approval" = "approve"（赞同）+ "-al"（名词后缀）。如果你 "approve" 某事，你就给予了它你的 "approval"——你的正式认可。
