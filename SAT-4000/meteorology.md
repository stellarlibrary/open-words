# 🌦️ meteorology

🔊 /ˌmiːtiəˈrɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of science concerned with the processes and phenomena of the atmosphere, especially as a means of forecasting the weather.
**中文：** n. 气象学；气象状态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- climatology（气候学）
- weather science（天气科学）
- atmospheric science（大气科学）
- weather forecasting（天气预报学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study meteorology / meteorology department / applied meteorology / synoptic meteorology**

👉 Example: She pursued a degree in meteorology because she was fascinated by severe weather phenomena.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advances in **meteorology** have enabled scientists to predict hurricanes with significantly greater accuracy than ever before.
   气象学的进步使科学家能够以比以往更高的精度预测飓风。

2. The university's department of **meteorology** conducts groundbreaking research on climate change and atmospheric circulation patterns.
   该大学的气象学系在气候变化和大气环流模式方面开展了开创性研究。

3. Modern **meteorology** relies heavily on satellite imagery and computer models to generate reliable weather forecasts worldwide.
   现代气象学在很大程度上依赖卫星图像和计算机模型来生成全球可靠的天气预报。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meteor-ology" — the study of things "high in the air" (from Greek "meteoros," meaning elevated), which is exactly where weather happens.
把"meteorology"想成"meteor"（高空的）+"ology"（学科）——研究"高空中的事物"，而天气正是在高空发生的。
