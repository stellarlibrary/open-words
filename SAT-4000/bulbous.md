# 🧅 bulbous

🔊 /ˈbʌlbəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Resembling a bulb in shape; round, swollen, or protruding
**中文：** adj. 球根的；球根状的；球根长成的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- swollen（膨胀的）
- rounded（圆形的）
- protuberant（突出的）
- globular（球状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bulbous nose / bulbous shape / bulbous tip / bulbous roots**

👉 Example: The caricature exaggerated his already bulbous nose to comic proportions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanist carefully examined the **bulbous** roots of the rare plant species collected from the tropical rainforest.
   植物学家仔细检查了从热带雨林中采集的稀有植物的球根状根部。

2. The architect designed a distinctive tower with a **bulbous** dome that has since become an iconic landmark of the city.
   建筑师设计了一座带有球状穹顶的独特塔楼，此后成为该城市的标志性地标。

3. His **bulbous** eyes widened in astonishment when he realized the magnitude of the scientific discovery before him.
   当他意识到眼前科学发现的重大意义时，他那突出的双眼因惊讶而睁大。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bulb" + "-ous" — shaped like a light bulb, round and swollen at one end.
把"bulbous"想成"bulb（灯泡）+ -ous（像…的）"——像灯泡一样，一端圆鼓鼓的。
