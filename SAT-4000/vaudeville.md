# 🎪 vaudeville

🔊 /ˈvɔːdəvɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A type of theatrical entertainment featuring a mixture of specialty acts such as comedy, song, dance, and acrobatics
**中文：** n. 杂耍；轻歌舞剧；综艺演出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- variety show（综艺表演）
- burlesque（滑稽戏）
- revue（时事讽刺剧）
- cabaret（卡巴莱歌舞表演）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vaudeville act / vaudeville theater / vaudeville performer / vaudeville era**

👉 Example: Many legendary comedians began their careers performing in vaudeville theaters across the country.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **vaudeville** circuit of the early twentieth century served as a crucial training ground for aspiring entertainers and comedians.
   二十世纪初的杂耍巡回演出为有抱负的艺人和喜剧演员提供了重要的训练平台。

2. Historians argue that **vaudeville** played a significant role in shaping American popular culture and the modern entertainment industry.
   历史学家认为轻歌舞剧在塑造美国流行文化和现代娱乐产业方面发挥了重要作用。

3. The documentary explored how **vaudeville** traditions influenced the development of television comedy formats in subsequent decades.
   这部纪录片探讨了杂耍传统如何影响了随后几十年电视喜剧形式的发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vaude-ville" as "a ville (town) of variety" — a whole town dedicated to varied, entertaining performances.
把"vaudeville"想成"a ville (town) of variety"——一座专门呈现各种精彩表演的城镇。
