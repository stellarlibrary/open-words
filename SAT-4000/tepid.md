# 🌡️ tepid

🔊 /ˈtɛpɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Slightly warm; lukewarm; showing little enthusiasm or conviction.
**中文：** adj. 微温的；不太热烈的；缺乏热情的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lukewarm（微温的）
- halfhearted（不热心的）
- unenthusiastic（不热情的）
- indifferent（冷淡的）
- mild（温和的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tepid response / tepid reception / tepid water / tepid applause / tepid support**

👉 Example: The new policy received only a tepid response from both legislators and the general public.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audience gave a **tepid** applause after the keynote speaker concluded a presentation that lacked both originality and persuasive evidence.
   在主讲人结束了一场既缺乏原创性又缺少说服力证据的演讲后，观众报以冷淡的掌声。

2. Critics offered a **tepid** reception to the novelist's latest work, noting that it failed to match the brilliance of her earlier publications.
   评论家对这位小说家的最新作品反应平淡，指出它未能达到她早期出版物的精彩程度。

3. Investor confidence remained **tepid** throughout the quarter as economic indicators continued to signal persistent uncertainty in global markets.
   由于经济指标持续显示全球市场的不确定性，整个季度投资者信心依然低迷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tepid" as "tip-id" — just the tip of warmth, barely warm, like water that is neither hot nor cold.
把"tepid"想成"tip（尖端）-id"——只有一丁点的温暖，几乎不热，就像既不热也不冷的水。
