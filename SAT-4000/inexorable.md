# ⏳ inexorable

🔊 /ɪnˈeksərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Impossible to stop or prevent; relentless; unyielding to persuasion or entreaty
**中文：** adj. 无情的；不屈不挠的；不可阻挡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relentless（无情的）
- unyielding（不屈的）
- unstoppable（不可阻挡的）
- implacable（难以平息的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inexorable march / inexorable rise / inexorable decline / inexorable progress / inexorable force**

👉 Example: The inexorable march of technological progress has transformed every aspect of modern society.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **inexorable** rise of global temperatures demands immediate and coordinated action from governments around the world.
   全球气温的**不可阻挡**的上升要求世界各国政府立即采取协调行动。

2. Despite fierce opposition, the **inexorable** advance of urbanization continued to reshape the rural landscape of the region.
   尽管遭到激烈反对，城市化的**无情**推进继续重塑着该地区的乡村面貌。

3. The historian described the empire's **inexorable** decline as a gradual process driven by internal corruption and external threats.
   历史学家将帝国**不可逆转**的衰落描述为由内部腐败和外部威胁驱动的渐进过程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-exor-able" as "not able to be exorcised" — like a force so powerful that it cannot be driven away or stopped by any means.
把"inexorable"想成"in-exor-able"，即"无法被驱除的"——就像一股强大到无法用任何手段阻止的力量。
