# 🌧️ unfavorable

🔊 /ˌʌnˈfeɪvərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not favorable; adverse, disadvantageous, or unlikely to bring success
**中文：** 不宜的；不顺利的；不利的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adverse（不利的）
- disadvantageous（不利的）
- unfriendly（不友好的）
- inauspicious（不吉利的）
- detrimental（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unfavorable conditions / unfavorable outcome / unfavorable review / unfavorable impression**

👉 Example: The expedition was postponed due to unfavorable weather conditions in the mountainous region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee issued an **unfavorable** report on the proposed legislation, citing numerous economic and social concerns.
   委员会对拟议的立法发出了不利报告，列举了众多经济和社会方面的担忧。

2. Despite the **unfavorable** odds, the research team persevered and ultimately achieved a significant scientific breakthrough.
   尽管形势不利，研究团队坚持不懈，最终取得了重大科学突破。

3. An **unfavorable** exchange rate can substantially diminish the purchasing power of international travelers and foreign investors.
   不利的汇率会大幅削弱国际旅行者和外国投资者的购买力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-favor-able" as "not able to be favored" — something that cannot win your favor because conditions work against it.
把"unfavorable"想成"un（不）+ favor（喜爱）+ able（能够的）"——不能被喜爱的，即不利的、不顺利的。
