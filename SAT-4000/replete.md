# :gem: replete

:speaker: /rɪˈpliːt/<br>
:label: *adjective | 形容词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Filled or well-supplied with something; abundantly provided; having had one's fill of food and drink
**中文：** adj. 充满的；充分供应的；饱足的

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- brimming（满溢的）
- saturated（饱和的）
- abundant（丰富的）
- teeming（充满的）
- gorged（饱食的）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **replete with examples / replete with detail / replete with imagery / feel replete**

:point_right: Example: The historical archive is replete with documents that illuminate the political struggles of that turbulent era.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The professor's lecture was **replete** with illuminating anecdotes that brought the abstract theoretical concepts vividly to life.
   教授的演讲中充满了富有启发性的轶事，使抽象的理论概念变得栩栩如生。

2. The ancient manuscript is **replete** with marginal annotations that reveal the intellectual debates of medieval European scholars.
   这部古代手稿中充满了页边注释，揭示了中世纪欧洲学者们的思想辩论。

3. After the elaborate banquet, the guests sat **replete** and contented, reflecting on the extraordinary quality of every course.
   丰盛的宴会结束后，宾客们饱足而满意地坐着，回味每道菜肴的非凡品质。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "replete" as "re-PLETE" — "plete" sounds like "complete," so replete means completely filled, nothing more can fit inside.
把"replete"想成"re-plete"——"plete"听起来像"complete（完整的）"，所以replete意味着完全填满，再也装不下了。
