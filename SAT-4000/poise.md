# 🧘 poise

🔊 /pɔɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A state of balance or equilibrium; graceful composure and self-assurance; an elegant bearing or posture. (v.) To hold in a balanced or steady position; to be ready or prepared.
**中文：** n. 平衡；姿势；镇静 v. 使平衡；保持姿势

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- composure（镇定）
- grace（优雅）
- equilibrium（平衡）
- elegance（从容）
- balance（均衡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain poise / poise and grace / poised for action / poised to succeed**

👉 Example: Despite the unexpected criticism, the diplomat maintained her poise throughout the entire press conference.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ballerina demonstrated remarkable **poise** as she held the demanding position motionless for an extended period on stage.
   芭蕾舞演员展现了非凡的**姿态**，在舞台上长时间一动不动地保持着高难度动作。

2. His calm **poise** under pressure during the crisis earned him the respect and admiration of his colleagues.
   他在危机中承受压力时的冷静**镇定**为他赢得了同事们的尊重和钦佩。

3. The hawk was **poised** on the edge of the cliff, perfectly balanced and ready to dive toward its prey below.
   那只鹰**栖立**在悬崖边缘，完美地保持平衡，随时准备向下方的猎物俯冲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "poise" as "poi-se" like "poi" (a balancing poi ball) — spinning gracefully in perfect balance, calm and composed.
把"poise"想成"poi-se"——像旋转的poi球一样优雅地保持完美平衡，沉着从容。
