# 💡 insight

🔊 /ˈɪnˌsaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The capacity to gain an accurate and deep understanding of something; a penetrating observation or understanding
**中文：** n. 洞察力；洞悉；深刻的见解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perception（洞察）
- discernment（辨别力）
- acumen（敏锐）
- understanding（理解力）
- intuition（直觉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gain insight into / provide insight / valuable insight / deep insight**

👉 Example: The research provided valuable insight into the behavioral patterns of migratory birds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biography offers remarkable **insight** into the private struggles and triumphs of the celebrated author.
   这部传记对这位著名作家的私人挣扎与成就提供了非凡的洞察。

2. Her years of clinical experience gave her profound **insight** into the psychological factors underlying chronic anxiety disorders.
   她多年的临床经验使她对慢性焦虑症背后的心理因素有了深刻的洞悉。

3. The professor's lecture provided fresh **insight** into how economic inequality shapes educational outcomes across different communities.
   教授的讲座对经济不平等如何影响不同社区的教育成果提供了新颖的见解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in + sight" — the ability to see deeply into something, beyond the surface to the truth within.
把"insight"想成"in + sight"——能够深入看到事物内部，透过表面看到内在的真相。
