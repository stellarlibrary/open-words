# 🧩 component

🔊 /kəmˈpoʊnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A part or element of a larger whole. (adj.) Constituting part of a larger whole; composing
**中文：** n. 成分；组件；部件 adj. 组成的；构成的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- element（元素）
- constituent（组成部分）
- ingredient（成分）
- part（部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **key component / essential component / component parts / component of a system**

👉 Example: Trust is a fundamental component of any successful long-term business partnership.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team identified a critical **component** of the vaccine formula that significantly enhanced its effectiveness against viral mutations.
   研究团队发现了疫苗配方中一个关键成分，可显著增强其对病毒变异的有效性。

2. Each **component** of the spacecraft must undergo rigorous testing before the assembly process can proceed to the next stage.
   航天器的每个组件在组装过程进入下一阶段之前都必须经过严格测试。

3. Emotional intelligence has become an increasingly important **component** of leadership training programs in major corporate organizations worldwide.
   情商已成为全球主要企业组织领导力培训项目中日益重要的组成部分。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "component" as "com-PONENT" — things you "put together" (Latin ponere = to put) to compose a whole.
把"component"想成"放在一起的东西"——拉丁语词根ponere意为"放置"，组件就是放在一起组成整体的部分。
