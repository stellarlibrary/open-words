# ✨ photon

🔊 /ˈfoʊtɑːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A quantum of electromagnetic radiation; the basic unit of light and all other forms of electromagnetic energy
**中文：** n. 光子；辐射量子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- light quantum（光量子）
- light particle（光粒子）
- quantum（量子）
- radiation unit（辐射单位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **photon energy / photon emission / photon absorption / single photon**

👉 Example: Each photon carries a specific amount of energy determined by its frequency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detector was sensitive enough to register the arrival of a single **photon** from a distant celestial source.
   该探测器灵敏到足以记录来自遥远天体源的单个光子的到达。

2. Scientists demonstrated that a **photon** can behave simultaneously as both a particle and a wave under certain conditions.
   科学家们证明，在特定条件下，光子可以同时表现为粒子和波。

3. The research team measured the energy of each **photon** emitted during the chemical reaction with remarkable precision.
   研究团队以极高的精度测量了化学反应过程中释放的每个光子的能量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "photon" as "photo" (light) + "-on" (a particle suffix like electron, proton) — a photon is a tiny particle of light.
把"photon"想成"photo"（光）+"-on"（粒子后缀，如electron、proton）——光子就是一个微小的光粒子。
