# ⚖️ mandatory

🔊 /ˈmændətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Required by law or rules; compulsory and not optional
**中文：** adj. 强制的；命令的；托管的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compulsory（强制的）
- obligatory（必须的）
- required（必需的）
- imperative（必要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mandatory requirement / mandatory training / mandatory sentencing / mandatory attendance**

👉 Example: Attendance at the safety briefing is mandatory for all new employees starting this week.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government introduced **mandatory** vaccination requirements for healthcare workers to prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
   政府对医疗工作者实行了强制疫苗接种要求，以防止传染病的传播。

2. Completion of the ethics training module is **mandatory** before any researcher may begin conducting experiments with human subjects.
   在任何研究人员开始对人类受试者进行实验之前，完成伦理培训模块是强制性的。

3. The judge imposed a **mandatory** minimum sentence of five years, leaving no room for judicial discretion in the case.
   法官判处了五年的强制最低刑期，在该案中不留任何司法自由裁量的余地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Mandatory" shares its root with "mandate" (an official command) — if something is mandatory, it comes from a mandate and you must do it.
"Mandatory"与"mandate"（正式命令）同根——如果某事是mandatory的，它源自一道命令，你必须执行。
