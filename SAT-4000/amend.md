# ✏️ amend

🔊 /əˈmɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make changes to a text, law, or statement in order to improve or correct it.
**中文：** 修改；改善，改进

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revise（修订）
- modify（修改）
- alter（改变）
- correct（纠正）
- rectify（矫正）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laws, constitutions, contracts, legislation, documents, behavior**
👉 Example: The legislature voted to amend the outdated immigration statute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The parliament decided to **amend** the constitution to guarantee equal rights for all citizens.
   议会决定修改宪法，以保障所有公民的平等权利。

2. The board resolved to **amend** the corporate charter in response to shareholder concerns about governance.
   董事会决定修改公司章程，以回应股东对治理问题的关切。

3. Scholars urged the committee to **amend** the educational curriculum to reflect contemporary scientific discoveries.
   学者们敦促委员会修改教育课程，以反映当代科学发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amend" shares its root with "mend" — just as you mend torn clothing, you amend flawed documents.
"Amend"与"mend"（修补）同根——就像修补破损的衣物一样，你修改有缺陷的文件。
