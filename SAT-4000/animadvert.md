# ⚖️ animadvert

🔊 /ˌænɪmædˈvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *v. to criticize; to censure; to reprove | 动词：批判；非难；责备*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make a critical or censorious remark; to express strong disapproval or pass harsh judgment upon someone or something.
**中文：** 发表批评性或指责性的评论；对某人或某事表达强烈的不满或严厉的评断。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- criticize（批评）
- censure（谴责）
- condemn（谴责）
- denounce（抨击）
- reprove（责备）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **animadvert on/upon (conduct/behavior/policy)**
👉 Example: The judge did not hesitate to animadvert upon the defendant's repeated disregard for court orders.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator rose to **animadvert** upon the proposed legislation, calling it a grave threat to civil liberties.
   那位参议员站起来批判这项拟议的立法，称其对公民自由构成严重威胁。

2. Several distinguished scholars chose to **animadvert** on the methodology employed in the controversial research paper.
   几位杰出的学者选择对这篇有争议的研究论文所采用的方法论进行批评。

3. The editorial board felt compelled to **animadvert** upon the administration's handling of the public health crisis.
   编辑委员会觉得有必要对政府处理公共卫生危机的方式加以非难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Animadvert" shares its root with "animadversion" — think of "anima" (mind) + "advert" (to turn to) — turning your mind to something in order to criticize it.
"Animadvert"与"animadversion"同根——想象"anima"（心灵）+ "advert"（转向）——将心灵转向某事以批评它。
