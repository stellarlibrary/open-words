# 🔍 defect

🔊 /dɪˈfɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A flaw, imperfection, or shortcoming. (v.) To abandon a cause, country, or allegiance in favor of an opposing one
**中文：** n. 缺点，缺陷 v. 变节；叛变

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flaw（缺陷）
- imperfection（不完美）
- shortcoming（缺点）
- blemish（瑕疵）
- desert（叛离）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a defect in something / manufacturing defect / birth defect / defect to another country**

👉 Example: The quality inspector discovered a serious defect in the engine assembly before shipment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineering team identified a critical **defect** in the bridge's structural design that required immediate remediation.
   工程团队发现了桥梁结构设计中的一个严重缺陷，需要立即修复。

2. The diplomat chose to **defect** to the neighboring country after becoming disillusioned with his government's oppressive policies.
   这位外交官在对本国政府的压迫政策感到幻灭后，选择叛逃到邻国。

3. A minor **defect** in the pharmaceutical packaging led to a nationwide recall affecting thousands of consumers.
   药品包装上的一个小缺陷导致了一场影响数千名消费者的全国性召回。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-fect" as "de-" (away from) + "fect" (make) — something made away from perfection, hence a flaw or leaving one's side.
把"defect"想成"de-"（偏离）+ "fect"（制造）——偏离完美制造的东西，即缺陷；或偏离自己一方，即叛变。
