# 🌿 marsh

🔊 /mɑːrʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An area of low-lying land that is frequently flooded and waterlogged; a wetland dominated by grasses and reeds
**中文：** n. 沼泽；湿地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- swamp（沼泽）
- wetland（湿地）
- bog（泥沼）
- fen（沼泽地）
- mire（泥潭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salt marsh / marsh gas / marsh land / drain the marsh / coastal marsh**

👉 Example: The salt marsh along the coast provides a critical habitat for migratory birds and marine life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental scientists conducted extensive research in the **marsh** to assess the impact of industrial runoff on native species.
   环境科学家在沼泽地进行了广泛的研究，以评估工业径流对本地物种的影响。

2. The ancient **marsh** had been gradually drained over centuries to create fertile agricultural land for local communities.
   这片古老的沼泽经过数百年的逐步排水，变成了供当地社区使用的肥沃农田。

3. Construction of the new highway was delayed because engineers discovered a protected **marsh** directly in the proposed route.
   新公路的建设被推迟，因为工程师发现拟议路线上正好有一片受保护的湿地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "marsh" as "mar + sh" — the sea (mar) says "shh" as it quietly creeps onto land, creating a soggy wetland.
把"marsh"想成"mar + sh"——大海（mar）悄悄地"嘘"着爬上陆地，形成了湿漉漉的沼泽地。
