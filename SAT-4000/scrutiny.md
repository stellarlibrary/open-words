# 🔍 scrutiny

🔊 /ˈskruːtɪni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Detailed examination or inspection; surveillance; close observation; a recount of votes
**中文：** n. 详细审查

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examination（审查）
- inspection（检查）
- analysis（分析）
- surveillance（监视）
- review（审视）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **under scrutiny / close scrutiny / public scrutiny / subject to scrutiny**

👉 Example: The new legislation came under intense scrutiny from constitutional scholars and legal experts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The financial records of the corporation were placed under rigorous **scrutiny** by federal auditors investigating potential fraud.
   该公司的财务记录被联邦审计人员严格审查，以调查潜在的欺诈行为。

2. Every aspect of the candidate's personal history faced intense public **scrutiny** during the heated electoral campaign season.
   在激烈的竞选季节中，候选人个人历史的方方面面都面临着公众的严格审视。

3. The scientific community subjected the controversial research findings to meticulous **scrutiny** before accepting the conclusions as valid.
   科学界对这些有争议的研究结果进行了细致的审查，然后才接受其结论为有效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scrutiny" as "scru-tiny" — you examine something so closely that even the tiniest details are visible under your scru-tinizing gaze.
把"scrutiny"想成"scru-tiny"——你审查得如此仔细，连最微小的细节都在你审视的目光下无所遁形。
