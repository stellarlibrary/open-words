# 🏢 superintendent

🔊 /ˌsuːpərɪnˈtɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who manages or oversees an organization, department, or activity; a director or supervisor
**中文：** n. 主管，负责人，指挥者，管理者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supervisor（主管）
- director（负责人）
- manager（管理者）
- overseer（监督者）
- administrator（行政人员）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **superintendent of schools / police superintendent / building superintendent / superintendent of operations**

👉 Example: The superintendent of schools announced new policies to improve student performance across the district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **superintendent** of the factory implemented rigorous safety protocols to reduce workplace accidents and improve conditions.
   工厂主管实施了严格的安全规程，以减少工作场所事故并改善条件。

2. As district **superintendent**, she was responsible for overseeing curriculum development and teacher training across forty schools.
   作为学区负责人，她负责监督四十所学校的课程开发和教师培训。

3. The police **superintendent** held a press conference to address public concerns regarding the recent rise in criminal activity.
   警察局长举行了新闻发布会，以回应公众对近期犯罪活动上升的关切。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "superintendent" as "super-intend-ent" — the person who super intends, the one in charge who oversees everything.
把"superintendent"想成"super-intend-ent"——超级有意愿管事的人，就是负责监督一切的主管。
