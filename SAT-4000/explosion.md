# 🌋 explosion

🔊 /ɪkˈsploʊʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A violent and destructive shattering or blowing apart of something; a sudden, dramatic increase in amount or extent
**中文：** n. 爆炸；爆发；激增

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blast（爆炸）
- detonation（引爆）
- eruption（爆发）
- surge（激增）
- outburst（迸发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a massive explosion / population explosion / explosion of interest / explosion in growth**

👉 Example: The explosion of social media use has fundamentally transformed how people communicate worldwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **explosion** at the industrial plant forced the immediate evacuation of thousands of residents from surrounding neighborhoods.
   工厂的爆炸迫使周围社区的数千名居民立即撤离。

2. The twentieth century witnessed an unprecedented **explosion** of technological innovation that reshaped virtually every aspect of daily life.
   二十世纪见证了前所未有的技术创新爆发，几乎重塑了日常生活的方方面面。

3. Economists attribute the recent **explosion** in housing prices to historically low interest rates combined with severe supply shortages.
   经济学家将近期房价的激增归因于历史低利率与严重的供应短缺的共同作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "explosion" as "ex-plosion" — something bursting outward (ex-) with tremendous force, whether a physical blast or a rapid expansion of something.
把"explosion"想成"ex-plosion"——某物以巨大的力量向外（ex-）迸裂，无论是物理上的爆炸还是某事物的迅速扩张。
