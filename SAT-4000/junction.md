# 🔀 junction

🔊 /ˈdʒʌŋkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A point where two or more things are joined or meet; a place where roads, railways, or paths converge or intersect
**中文：** n. 连接；接合；交叉点；接合处；枢纽站

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intersection（交叉路口）
- convergence（汇合点）
- crossroads（十字路口）
- confluence（汇合处）
- nexus（连接点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **railway junction / junction point / at the junction of / junction box**

👉 Example: The accident occurred at the busy junction where three major highways converge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city planners proposed building a new overpass at the congested **junction** to alleviate the chronic traffic delays.
   城市规划者提议在拥堵的交叉路口建造一座新的立交桥，以缓解长期的交通延误。

2. At the **junction** of the two rivers, a thriving commercial district had developed over the course of several centuries.
   在两条河流的汇合处，经过几个世纪的发展，已形成了一个繁荣的商业区。

3. The electrical **junction** box was inspected thoroughly to determine the cause of the recurring power failures in the building.
   电气接线盒经过了彻底检查，以确定大楼反复停电的原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "junction" as "junct (join) + ion" — a place where things join together, like roads meeting at one point.
把"junction"想成"junct（连接）+ ion"——事物连接在一起的地方，就像道路在一个点汇合。
