# 💭 bethink

🔊 /bɪˈθɪŋk/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause oneself to consider or reflect; to recall or bring to mind; to remind oneself
**中文：** v. 想起，忆起；使……思考；使……考虑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recall（回忆）
- recollect（想起）
- reflect（反思）
- ponder（思考）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bethink oneself of / bethink oneself to do something**

👉 Example: She bethought herself of an earlier promise she had made and resolved to fulfill it without delay.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The elder statesman **bethought** himself of the constitutional principles upon which the young republic had been founded.
   这位资深政治家想起了这个年轻共和国赖以建立的宪法原则。

2. Only after the meeting had concluded did she **bethink** herself of the crucial objection she had intended to raise.
   会议结束后她才想起了自己原本打算提出的关键反对意见。

3. The scholar paused to **bethink** himself of the moral implications before publishing his controversial research findings.
   这位学者在发表其有争议的研究成果之前停下来思考了道德层面的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-THINK" — to actively engage in thinking, to make yourself think about or remember something. The "be-" prefix intensifies the act of thinking.
想想"be-THINK"——积极地去思考，让自己想起或记住某事。"be-"前缀加强了思考的行为。
