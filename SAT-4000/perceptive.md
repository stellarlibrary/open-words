# 🎯 perceptive

🔊 /pəˈseptɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing keen insight, understanding, or intuition; quick to notice or understand things
**中文：** adj. 知觉的；感觉的；有知觉力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astute（敏锐的）
- insightful（有洞察力的）
- discerning（有辨别力的）
- observant（善于观察的）
- shrewd（精明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perceptive observer / perceptive analysis / perceptive reader / perceptive comment / highly perceptive**

👉 Example: The editor made several perceptive comments that significantly improved the overall clarity of the manuscript.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **perceptive** reader will notice the subtle foreshadowing woven throughout the opening chapters of the novel.
   一个有洞察力的读者会注意到小说开头几章中巧妙穿插的伏笔。

2. The therapist's **perceptive** observations helped the patient understand the root causes of her persistent anxiety.
   治疗师敏锐的观察帮助患者理解了她持续焦虑的根本原因。

3. His **perceptive** analysis of the economic data revealed trends that other financial analysts had completely overlooked.
   他对经济数据的敏锐分析揭示了其他金融分析师完全忽视的趋势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perceptive" as "percept-ive" — having the quality (-ive) of keen perception, like someone who sees what others miss.
把"perceptive"想成"percept-ive"——具有敏锐感知力的品质（-ive），就像一个能看到别人忽略之处的人。
