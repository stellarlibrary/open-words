# 💉 insulin

🔊 /ˈɪnsjʊlɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A hormone produced by the pancreas that regulates blood sugar levels by facilitating glucose absorption into cells
**中文：** n. 胰岛素

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hormone（激素）
- peptide hormone（肽类激素）
- glucoregulator（血糖调节物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insulin injection / insulin resistance / insulin levels / produce insulin / administer insulin**

👉 Example: Patients with Type 1 diabetes require daily insulin injections to maintain stable blood sugar levels.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pancreas fails to produce sufficient **insulin** in patients diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes mellitus.
   被诊断为一型糖尿病的患者的胰腺无法产生足够的胰岛素。

2. Medical researchers are developing an oral form of **insulin** that could eliminate the need for painful daily injections.
   医学研究人员正在开发一种口服形式的胰岛素，有望消除每日痛苦注射的需要。

3. Chronic **insulin** resistance is a primary contributing factor to the development of metabolic syndrome and Type 2 diabetes.
   慢性胰岛素抵抗是代谢综合征和二型糖尿病发展的主要促成因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insulin" as coming from Latin "insula" meaning "island" — insulin is produced by the "islets of Langerhans," tiny island-like clusters in the pancreas.
把"insulin"想成来自拉丁语"insula（岛屿）"——胰岛素由胰腺中的"朗格汉斯岛"产生，那是一些微小的岛状细胞团。
