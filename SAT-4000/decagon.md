# 🔷 decagon

🔊 /ˈdekəɡɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A polygon with ten sides and ten angles
**中文：** n. 十角形；十边形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ten-sided polygon（十边多边形）
- decagonal shape（十边形状）
- ten-sided figure（十边图形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regular decagon / decagon shape / decagon pattern / interior angles of a decagon**

👉 Example: The architect incorporated a decagon into the building's floor plan to create a distinctive geometric design.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In geometry class, students learned that the interior angles of a regular **decagon** sum to exactly one thousand four hundred forty degrees.
   在几何课上，学生们学到正十边形的内角之和恰好是一千四百四十度。

2. The ancient mosaic featured an intricate **decagon** pattern surrounded by smaller triangles and squares in harmonious arrangement.
   这幅古代马赛克展示了一个精致的十边形图案，周围环绕着排列和谐的小三角形和正方形。

3. The mathematician demonstrated how to construct a perfect **decagon** using only a compass and a straightedge on the whiteboard.
   数学家在白板上演示了如何仅用圆规和直尺来构造一个完美的十边形。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deca-gon" — "deca" means ten (like decade = 10 years) and "gon" means angle, so a decagon is a ten-angled shape.
把"decagon"拆分为"deca-gon"——"deca"意为十（如decade=十年），"gon"意为角，所以decagon就是十角形。
