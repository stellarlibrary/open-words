# ⚡ circuit

🔊 /ˈsɜːrkɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A closed path through which electric current flows; a regular journey around a particular area; a complete loop. (v.) To travel in a circular route around something
**中文：** n. 电路；回路；巡回 v. 环行

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- loop（回路）
- route（路线）
- orbit（轨道）
- course（线路）
- tour（巡回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **electrical circuit / circuit board / circuit court / short circuit / lecture circuit**

👉 Example: The engineer carefully designed the circuit to prevent overheating in the new device.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The electrical **circuit** was meticulously designed to ensure that current flowed efficiently through every component of the device.
   该电路经过精心设计，确保电流高效地流过设备的每个组件。

2. The renowned author embarked on a national lecture **circuit** to promote her latest book on environmental conservation.
   这位著名作家开始了全国巡回演讲，以推广她关于环境保护的最新著作。

3. A faulty wire caused a short **circuit** that temporarily disrupted power to the entire research laboratory complex.
   一根故障电线导致了短路，暂时中断了整个研究实验室大楼的供电。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circuit" as "circ" (circle) + "uit" (way) — a circuit is a circular way or path, whether for electricity or for travel.
把"circuit"想成"circ"（圆）+"uit"（路径）——无论是电流还是旅行，circuit都是一条环形路径。
