# 👑 paramount

🔊 /ˈpærəˌmaʊnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) More important than anything else; supreme in rank or authority. (n.) A supreme ruler or chief
**中文：** adj. 最重要的，主要的；至高无上的 n. 最高统治者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supreme（至高无上的）
- preeminent（卓越的）
- foremost（首要的）
- predominant（主要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **of paramount importance / paramount concern / paramount to success**

👉 Example: The safety of passengers remains of paramount importance to the airline industry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The protection of civil liberties is **paramount** in any democratic society that values individual freedoms and human rights.
   在任何重视个人自由和人权的民主社会中，保护公民自由都是至关重要的。

2. Maintaining public trust was **paramount** to the institution's credibility throughout the lengthy and contentious reform process.
   在漫长而有争议的改革过程中，维护公众信任对该机构的公信力至关重要。

3. The committee emphasized that environmental sustainability should be **paramount** when evaluating proposals for new urban development projects.
   委员会强调，在评估新城市开发项目的提案时，环境可持续性应是首要考量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "para-mount" as "above the mountain" — standing at the peak, above everything else, the most important.
把"paramount"想成"para（在…之上）+ mount（山）"——站在山顶之上，高于一切，最重要的。
