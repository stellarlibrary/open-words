# ✂️ dissever

🔊 /dɪˈsɛvər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To divide or separate into parts; to sever or split apart completely
**中文：** v. 割裂；使分离；使分裂；分开

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sever（切断）
- separate（分离）
- divide（分割）
- split（分裂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dissever ties / dissever a bond / dissever from something / dissever into parts**

👉 Example: The civil war threatened to dissever the nation into two irreconcilable halves.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged territorial dispute threatened to **dissever** the once-unified kingdom into several hostile and competing factions.
   旷日持久的领土争端威胁要将曾经统一的王国分裂成几个敌对、相互竞争的派系。

2. The court ruling effectively **dissevered** the corporation from its founding partnership, creating two entirely independent entities.
   法院裁决实际上将该公司从其创始合伙关系中分离出来，创建了两个完全独立的实体。

3. Ideological differences gradually **dissevered** the alliance that had held the coalition together for more than two decades.
   意识形态上的分歧逐渐割裂了维系该联盟二十多年的纽带。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-sever" as intensely "severing" — the prefix "dis" emphasizes a complete and thorough cutting apart.
把"dissever"想成强烈地"sever"（切断）——前缀"dis"强调彻底而完全的切割分离。
