# 🕳️ subterranean

🔊 /ˌsʌbtəˈreɪniən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Existing, occurring, or done beneath the earth's surface; secret or hidden. (n.) A person who works underground
**中文：** adj. 地下的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- underground（地下的）
- subterrestrial（地下的）
- concealed（隐蔽的）
- clandestine（秘密的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subterranean passage / subterranean cave / subterranean world / subterranean activity**

👉 Example: The archaeologists discovered a vast network of subterranean tunnels beneath the ancient city.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers designed an extensive **subterranean** transportation system to alleviate the city's chronic traffic congestion problems.
   工程师们设计了一个广泛的地下交通系统，以缓解该市长期的交通拥堵问题。

2. The researchers explored a **subterranean** cavern that had remained sealed and untouched for thousands of years.
   研究人员探索了一个被封存了数千年、未曾被触及的地下洞穴。

3. Political dissidents maintained a **subterranean** network of communication to coordinate their resistance against the regime.
   政治异见人士维持着一个秘密的通信网络，以协调他们对政权的抵抗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-terra-nean" as "sub" (below) + "terra" (earth) — literally "below the earth," something underground or hidden.
把"subterranean"想成"sub"（在下面）+ "terra"（大地）——字面意思是"在大地之下"，即地下的或隐蔽的。
