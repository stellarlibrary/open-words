# 🍽️ surfeit

🔊 /ˈsɜːrfɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An excessive amount, especially of food or drink; overindulgence. (v.) To cause to overeat or overindulge; to sicken through excess
**中文：** v. 使饮食过度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excess（过量）
- glut（过剩）
- overabundance（过多）
- satiate（使饱足）
- overindulgence（过度放纵）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surfeit of / surfeited with / surfeit oneself on / a surfeit of information**

👉 Example: The holiday banquet left every guest surfeited with rich food and fine wine.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **surfeit** of conflicting information made it nearly impossible for the committee to reach a well-informed and timely decision.
   过多相互矛盾的信息使委员会几乎不可能做出明智而及时的决定。

2. The guests were **surfeited** with an elaborate seven-course meal that left them too full to contemplate even the lightest dessert.
   宾客们被一顿精心准备的七道菜大餐撑得饱饱的，连最清淡的甜点都无法考虑。

3. Critics argued that the **surfeit** of standardized testing in schools was undermining genuine intellectual curiosity and creative thinking among students.
   批评者认为学校中过多的标准化考试正在削弱学生真正的求知欲和创造性思维。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surfeit" as "surface + it" — when you have so much that it overflows the surface, you have a surfeit.
把"surfeit"想成"surface + it"——当你拥有的东西多到溢出表面时，就是surfeit（过量）。
