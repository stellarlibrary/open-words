# 🔥 temper

🔊 /ˈtɛmpər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person's state of mind or disposition; a tendency to become angry. (v.) To harden metal by heating and cooling; to moderate or mitigate
**中文：** n. 脾气；性情；回火；倾向 v. 使回火；锻炼；调和；使缓和

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disposition（性情）
- temperament（气质）
- moderate（使缓和）
- mitigate（减轻）
- restrain（抑制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lose one's temper / temper justice with mercy / bad temper / temper expectations**

👉 Example: A wise leader must temper ambition with prudence to achieve sustainable progress.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor urged students to **temper** their enthusiasm with careful analysis before drawing conclusions from preliminary data.
   教授敦促学生在从初步数据得出结论之前，用仔细的分析来调和他们的热情。

2. His notorious **temper** made negotiations extremely difficult, as colleagues feared provoking an unpredictable emotional outburst.
   他臭名昭著的脾气使谈判极为困难，因为同事们害怕引发不可预测的情绪爆发。

3. The blacksmith carefully **tempered** the steel blade, alternating between intense heat and rapid cooling to achieve optimal hardness.
   铁匠仔细地回火钢刀，在高温和快速冷却之间交替进行，以达到最佳硬度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temper" as "temperature" of emotions — just as you temper steel by controlling heat, you temper feelings by controlling intensity.
把"temper"想成情绪的"温度"——就像通过控制热量来回火钢铁一样，你通过控制强度来调和情感。
