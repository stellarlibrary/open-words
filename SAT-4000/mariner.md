# ⚓ mariner

🔊 /ˈmærɪnər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who navigates or assists in navigating a ship; a sailor or seaman
**中文：** n. 水手；船员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sailor（水手）
- seaman（海员）
- seafarer（航海者）
- navigator（领航员）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **experienced mariner / ancient mariner / master mariner / mariners at sea**

👉 Example: The seasoned mariner guided the vessel safely through the treacherous strait during the storm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **mariner** relied on celestial navigation techniques to chart a course across the vast and featureless expanse of open ocean.
   这位水手依靠天文导航技术在广阔无垠的大洋上规划航线。

2. Samuel Taylor Coleridge's poem "The Rime of the Ancient **Mariner**" remains one of the most celebrated works in English literature.
   塞缪尔·泰勒·柯勒律治的诗作《古舟子咏》至今仍是英国文学中最著名的作品之一。

3. Every aspiring **mariner** must complete rigorous training in seamanship, safety procedures, and maritime law before receiving certification.
   每位有志成为水手的人在获得认证之前，都必须完成航海技术、安全程序和海事法律的严格培训。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Mariner" contains "marine" (relating to the sea) + "-er" (a person who does something) — a mariner is simply a person of the sea.
"Mariner"包含"marine"（与海洋有关的）+ "-er"（做某事的人）——mariner就是一个海上的人。
