# 👻 apparition

🔊 /ˌæpəˈrɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *n. 幽灵；幻影；鬼怪*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A ghost or ghostlike image of a person; a supernatural appearance or phantom; something that appears suddenly or unexpectedly.

**中文：** 幽灵；鬼怪；幻影；突然出现的奇异景象。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- phantom 幽灵
- specter 鬼魂
- ghost 鬼
- wraith 幽灵
- vision 幻象

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** ghostly apparition, see an apparition, a strange apparition, the apparition appeared, spectral apparition

**Example:** "Apparition" is more literary and formal than "ghost." It emphasizes the visual aspect of something appearing suddenly and mysteriously.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The night watchman swore he witnessed a pale **apparition** gliding silently through the abandoned cathedral halls.
   夜间守卫发誓他看到一个苍白的**幽灵**无声地飘过废弃教堂的大厅。

2. A strange **apparition** emerged from the dense fog, startling the villagers who had gathered near the bridge.
   一个奇异的**幻影**从浓雾中出现，吓到了聚集在桥边的村民们。

3. Some historians believe the reported **apparition** was merely an optical illusion caused by atmospheric conditions.
   一些历史学家认为所报告的**鬼怪**不过是大气条件造成的光学幻觉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apparition" comes from "appear" — an apparition is something that appears out of nowhere, like a ghost materializing before your eyes.

词源联想：apparition 来自 appear（出现）——幽灵就是突然"出现"在你面前的东西。
