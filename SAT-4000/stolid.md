# 😐 stolid

🔊 /ˈstɒlɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Calm, dependable, and showing little emotion or interest; not easily excited or moved
**中文：** adj. 不易激动的，感觉迟钝的，神经麻木的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impassive（无动于衷的）
- phlegmatic（冷淡的）
- apathetic（冷漠的）
- unemotional（不动感情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stolid expression / stolid demeanor / stolid indifference / stolid temperament**

👉 Example: The guard maintained a stolid expression despite the chaos unfolding around him.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **stolid** bureaucrat processed each application with mechanical efficiency, showing no sympathy for the desperate applicants before him.
   那个迟钝的官僚以机械般的效率处理每份申请，对面前绝望的申请人毫无同情。

2. Despite the audience's enthusiastic applause, the **stolid** conductor merely nodded and returned his attention to the orchestra.
   尽管观众热烈鼓掌，那位不动声色的指挥家只是点了点头，便将注意力重新转向管弦乐队。

3. His **stolid** temperament made him an excellent emergency room physician, as he never panicked under extreme pressure.
   他不易激动的性情使他成为一名出色的急诊室医生，因为他在极端压力下从不慌张。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stolid" as "solid" with a "t" — a stolid person is solid and immovable in their emotions, like a block of wood.
把"stolid"想成在"solid（坚固的）"中加了个"t"——一个stolid的人在情感上像一块木头一样坚固不动。
