# 🎩 bowler

🔊 /ˈboʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A hard, round-topped hat; a person who bowls in cricket or bowling; one who plays a bowling game
**中文：** 圆顶礼帽；投球手；玩保龄球的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- derby hat（圆顶硬礼帽）
- pitcher（投手）
- player（选手）
- thrower（投掷者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bowler hat / fast bowler / spin bowler / skilled bowler**

👉 Example: The fast bowler delivered a devastating yorker that shattered the batsman's stumps in the final over.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The gentleman wore a traditional **bowler** hat as he strolled through the streets of Victorian London.
   这位绅士戴着一顶传统的圆顶礼帽，漫步在维多利亚时代的伦敦街头。

2. The cricket team's lead **bowler** consistently delivered accurate and powerful throws throughout the championship match.
   板球队的主力投球手在整场锦标赛中始终投出精准而有力的球。

3. As an experienced **bowler**, she competed in national tournaments and earned several prestigious awards over her career.
   作为一名经验丰富的保龄球手，她参加了全国锦标赛，并在职业生涯中赢得了多项殊荣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bowl-er" — a person who bowls, whether rolling a bowling ball down a lane or delivering a cricket ball.
把"bowler"想成"bowl（投球）+ er（人）"——投球的人，无论是打保龄球还是板球投球手。
