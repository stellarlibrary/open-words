# 😋 sapid

🔊 /ˈsæpɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a strong, pleasant taste; agreeable to the mind; interesting or engaging
**中文：** adj. 有味道的，滋味好的；有趣味的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flavorful（有风味的）
- palatable（可口的）
- savory（美味的）
- engaging（引人入胜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sapid cuisine / sapid prose / sapid conversation / sapid flavor**

👉 Example: The chef's sapid creations delighted diners who appreciated bold and distinctive flavors.

Think of "sapid" as related to "savor" — both come from Latin "sapere" meaning "to taste." Something sapid is full of savor, full of flavor or interest.
把"sapid"和"savor（品味）"联系起来——两者都来自拉丁语"sapere（品尝）"。sapid的东西充满了滋味，充满了风味或趣味。
