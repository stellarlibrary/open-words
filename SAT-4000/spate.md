# 🌊 spate

🔊 /speɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sudden flood or rush of water; a large number of similar things occurring in quick succession; a sudden outburst or outpouring
**中文：** n. 大水；大量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deluge（洪水）
- torrent（急流）
- surge（涌潮）
- flood（泛滥）
- barrage（连续猛击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a spate of robberies / a spate of complaints / a spate of attacks / in spate**

👉 Example: The city experienced a spate of burglaries during the holiday season, alarming residents and local authorities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **spate** of cyberattacks targeting financial institutions prompted regulators to impose stricter cybersecurity requirements across the banking sector.
   针对金融机构的一连串网络攻击促使监管机构对整个银行业实施更严格的网络安全要求。

2. The river was in **spate** after three consecutive days of torrential rain, flooding several low-lying villages along its banks.
   连续三天暴雨后河水暴涨，淹没了沿岸几个低洼村庄。

3. The publisher received a **spate** of manuscript submissions following the announcement of their prestigious annual literary competition.
   出版商在宣布其享有盛誉的年度文学竞赛后收到了大量的稿件投稿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spate" rhyming with "great" — a spate is a great flood or a great number of things all happening at once.
"spate"与"great"押韵——spate就是一场大洪水或一大堆事情同时发生。
