# 🎤 impromptu

🔊 /ɪmˈprɒmptjuː/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, adverb | 名词, 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An improvised performance or composition. (adj.) Done without preparation or rehearsal. (adv.) In an unplanned or spontaneous manner
**中文：** n. 即兴曲；即席演出 adj. 即席的；即兴的 adv. 即席地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spontaneous（自发的）
- improvised（即兴的）
- unrehearsed（未经排练的）
- extemporaneous（即席的）
- offhand（即兴的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impromptu speech / impromptu performance / impromptu meeting / impromptu visit**

👉 Example: The chairman delivered a brilliant impromptu speech that captivated the entire audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat gave an **impromptu** address at the press conference, responding eloquently to the unexpected line of questioning.
   这位外交官在新闻发布会上发表了即兴讲话，对意料之外的提问作出了雄辩的回应。

2. Students organized an **impromptu** celebration in the courtyard after learning that the university had received its prestigious accreditation.
   学生们在得知大学获得了著名认证后，在庭院里组织了一场即兴庆祝活动。

3. The pianist's **impromptu** performance at the charity gala demonstrated remarkable technical skill and emotional depth.
   这位钢琴家在慈善晚会上的即兴演出展现了非凡的技巧和情感深度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impromptu" as "I'm prompt to" do something — you are so prompt that you act immediately without preparation.
把"impromptu"想成"I'm prompt to（我迅速去做）"——你如此迅速以至于不加准备就立刻行动，即"即兴的"。
