# 🌿 indigenous

🔊 /ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; native; innate or inherent to a region
**中文：** adj. 本土的；土著的；国产的；固有的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- native（本土的）
- aboriginal（土著的）
- endemic（地方性的）
- autochthonous（原住的）
- homegrown（本地的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indigenous to / indigenous people / indigenous species / indigenous culture**

👉 Example: The researchers documented several plant species indigenous to the remote volcanic islands of the Pacific.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Conservation efforts must prioritize the protection of **indigenous** species that face extinction due to habitat destruction and climate change.
   保护工作必须优先保护那些因栖息地破坏和气候变化而面临灭绝的本土物种。

2. The university established a new department dedicated to preserving the languages and traditions of **indigenous** communities worldwide.
   该大学成立了一个新部门，致力于保护全球土著社区的语言和传统。

3. Many **indigenous** agricultural practices have proven more sustainable than modern industrial farming methods in tropical regions.
   许多本土农业实践在热带地区已被证明比现代工业化农业方法更具可持续性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "indi-gen-ous" as "generated in" a place — something generated in its original land, born and belonging there.
把"indigenous"想成"generated in"某个地方——在其原生土地上产生的，生于斯属于斯。
