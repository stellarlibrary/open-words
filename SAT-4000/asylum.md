# 🏛️ asylum

🔊 /əˈsaɪ.ləm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Protection or shelter granted by a nation to someone who has left their home country as a political refugee; an institution for the care of the mentally ill or destitute.
**中文：** 庇护；收容所；避难所

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refuge（庇护所）
- sanctuary（避难所）
- haven（安全港）
- shelter（庇护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seek/grant/deny/offer asylum**, **political asylum**, **asylum seeker**

👉 Example: The dissident sought political asylum at the embassy after fleeing persecution in his homeland.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Thousands of refugees arrived at the border, desperately hoping to be granted **asylum** by the neighboring country.
   数以千计的难民到达边境，迫切希望获得邻国的庇护。

2. The government announced new policies regarding the processing of **asylum** applications from war-torn regions.
   政府宣布了有关处理来自战乱地区庇护申请的新政策。

3. The old Victorian building once served as an **asylum** for those suffering from severe mental illness.
   这座古老的维多利亚式建筑曾经是严重精神病患者的收容所。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ASYLUM" comes from Greek "asylon" meaning "inviolable place" — think of a safe place where no one can harm you.
"ASYLUM"源自希腊语"asylon"，意为"不可侵犯之地"——想象一个没有人能伤害你的安全之所。
