# 🔶 rust

🔊 /rʌst/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A reddish-brown coating formed on iron or steel by oxidation; a plant disease. (v.) To become corroded; to deteriorate through disuse
**中文：** v. 生锈

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corrode（腐蚀）
- oxidize（氧化）
- deteriorate（恶化）
- decay（衰败）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rust away / rust through / rust belt / skills rust from disuse**

👉 Example: If you leave iron tools exposed to moisture, they will inevitably rust within a matter of weeks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Years of neglect had caused the once-magnificent bridge to **rust** beyond repair, forcing the city to plan demolition.
   多年的疏于维护导致这座曾经宏伟的桥梁锈蚀到无法修复，迫使该市计划拆除。

2. The archaeologists discovered that centuries of exposure had caused the ancient iron artifacts to **rust** almost completely.
   考古学家发现，数百年的暴露使这些古代铁器几乎完全生锈了。

3. Without regular practice, even the most talented musician's skills will gradually **rust** and diminish over time.
   如果不经常练习，即使是最有天赋的音乐家的技能也会随着时间的推移逐渐荒废。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rust" as what happens when you "rest" too long — both metals and skills deteriorate when left idle.
把"rust"想成"rest"（休息）太久的后果——金属和技能放置不用都会"生锈"退化。
