# 🌱 rejuvenate

🔊 /rɪˈdʒuːvɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make young or youthful again; to restore to an original or new condition; to give new energy or vigor to.
**中文：** v. 使年轻；使更新；使恢复精神；使复原

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revitalize（使恢复活力）
- refresh（使焕然一新）
- renew（更新）
- restore（恢复）
- reinvigorate（使重新振作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rejuvenate the skin / rejuvenate the economy / rejuvenate one's spirit / feel rejuvenated**

👉 Example: The weekend retreat was designed to rejuvenate employees who had been experiencing prolonged workplace stress.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city council approved a comprehensive plan to **rejuvenate** the neglected waterfront district with parks and modern public facilities.
   市议会批准了一项综合计划，用公园和现代公共设施来振兴被忽视的滨水区。

2. A balanced diet combined with regular exercise can significantly **rejuvenate** both physical stamina and overall mental well-being.
   均衡的饮食加上规律的运动可以显著恢复身体耐力和整体心理健康。

3. The new artistic director sought to **rejuvenate** the theater company by introducing contemporary works alongside the traditional classical repertoire.
   新任艺术总监试图通过在传统古典剧目之外引入当代作品来为剧团注入新活力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-juven-ate" as containing "juvenile" — to rejuvenate means to make juvenile (young) again, restoring youthful energy.
把"rejuvenate"想成包含"juvenile（年轻的）"——rejuvenate就是使再次年轻，恢复青春活力。
