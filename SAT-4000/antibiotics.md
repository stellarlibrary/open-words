# 💊 antibiotics

🔊 /ˌæntɪbaɪˈɑːtɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Medicines or chemical substances that are used to kill or inhibit the growth of bacteria and treat bacterial infections.

**中文：** 抗生素（用于杀灭或抑制细菌生长、治疗细菌感染的药物或化学物质）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- antimicrobials 抗微生物药
- bactericides 杀菌剂
- anti-infectives 抗感染药
- germicides 灭菌剂

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **prescribe antibiotics**, **a course of antibiotics**, **antibiotic resistance**, **take antibiotics**, **broad-spectrum antibiotics**. Note: the singular form "antibiotic" is also used as an adjective.

> Example: The physician prescribed a course of **antibiotics** to treat the patient's infection.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor prescribed strong **antibiotics** after confirming that the wound had developed a bacterial infection.
   医生在确认伤口发生细菌感染后开出了强效抗生素。

2. Overuse of **antibiotics** in agricultural livestock has contributed significantly to the rise of resistant bacteria.
   在畜牧业中过度使用抗生素在很大程度上导致了耐药细菌的增加。

3. Researchers are developing novel **antibiotics** to combat the growing threat of drug-resistant bacterial strains.
   研究人员正在开发新型抗生素，以应对耐药细菌菌株日益增长的威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anti-" (against) + "bio-" (life) + "-tics." **Antibiotics** are substances that work "against the life" of harmful bacteria, stopping them from growing or killing them outright.

"anti-"（对抗）+"bio-"（生命）+"-tics"。**Antibiotics** 就是"对抗有害细菌生命"的物质，阻止其生长或直接杀灭它们。
