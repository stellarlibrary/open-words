# 🔷 rhombus

🔊 /ˈrɒmbəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A parallelogram with four equal sides and oblique angles; a diamond-shaped figure
**中文：** n. 菱形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diamond（菱形）
- lozenge（菱形）
- parallelogram（平行四边形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rhombus shape / area of a rhombus / diagonals of a rhombus / rhombus pattern**

👉 Example: The geometry teacher asked students to calculate the area of a rhombus given its two diagonals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In Euclidean geometry, a **rhombus** is defined as a quadrilateral whose four sides all have equal length.
   在欧几里得几何中，菱形被定义为四条边长度全部相等的四边形。

2. The architect incorporated a repeating **rhombus** pattern into the facade to create a visually striking and modern exterior design.
   建筑师在立面中融入了重复的菱形图案，以创造视觉上引人注目的现代外部设计。

3. Students learned that the diagonals of a **rhombus** always bisect each other at right angles, a key geometric property.
   学生们了解到菱形的对角线总是互相垂直平分，这是一个关键的几何性质。

Think of "rhombus" sounding like "rom-bus" — imagine a bus shaped like a diamond (rhombus) driving down the road.
把"rhombus"想成发音像"rom-bus"——想象一辆形状像菱形的巴士在路上行驶。
