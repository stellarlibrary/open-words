# 💪 abdominal

🔊 /æbˈdɒmɪnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or situated in the abdomen; of or pertaining to the belly region
**中文：** adj. 腹部的；有腹鳍的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ventral（腹侧的）
- gastric（胃的）
- visceral（内脏的）
- belly-related（与腹部相关的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abdominal pain / abdominal muscles / abdominal surgery / abdominal cavity**

👉 Example: The patient was admitted to the hospital after experiencing severe abdominal pain overnight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician ordered an ultrasound to investigate the source of the patient's chronic **abdominal** discomfort.
   医生要求进行超声检查以调查患者慢性腹部不适的原因。

2. Regular **abdominal** exercises can strengthen core muscles and significantly reduce the risk of lower back injuries.
   定期的腹部锻炼可以增强核心肌群，并显著降低下背部受伤的风险。

3. The **abdominal** cavity houses vital organs including the liver, kidneys, and the entire digestive tract.
   腹腔容纳着重要器官，包括肝脏、肾脏和整个消化道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abdominal" is simply "abdomen" + "-al" (adjective suffix). If you know abdomen (belly), abdominal means "of the belly."
"Abdominal"就是"abdomen" + "-al"（形容词后缀）。如果你知道abdomen（腹部），abdominal就是"腹部的"。
