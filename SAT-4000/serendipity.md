# ✨ serendipity

🔊 /ˌserənˈdɪpɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The occurrence of finding pleasant or valuable things by chance; a talent for making fortunate discoveries accidentally
**中文：** n. 意外发现珍奇事物的本领

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- luck（运气）
- fortuity（偶然）
- happenstance（巧合）
- providence（天意）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pure serendipity / by serendipity / a moment of serendipity / serendipity played a role**

👉 Example: The discovery of penicillin was a classic case of serendipity in the history of science.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. It was through pure **serendipity** that the archaeologist stumbled upon the ancient tomb hidden beneath the village square.
   正是由于纯粹的机缘巧合，考古学家偶然发现了隐藏在村庄广场下方的古墓。

2. Many groundbreaking scientific discoveries throughout history have been attributed to **serendipity** rather than deliberate investigation.
   历史上许多开创性的科学发现都被归因于意外发现，而非刻意研究。

3. The researcher credited **serendipity** for leading her to the rare manuscript that transformed her entire doctoral thesis.
   这位研究者将引导她发现那份罕见手稿的功劳归于意外的机缘，这改变了她整篇博士论文。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "serendipity" as coming from "Serendip," an old name for Sri Lanka — the fairy tale heroes of Serendip always made lucky discoveries by accident.
把"serendipity"想成来自"Serendip"（斯里兰卡的旧称）——Serendip的童话英雄总是意外地发现珍贵事物。
