# 📊 systematic

🔊 /ˌsɪstəˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done or acting according to a fixed plan or system; methodical
**中文：** adj. 系统的，体系的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- methodical（有条理的）
- organized（有组织的）
- structured（有结构的）
- orderly（有序的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **systematic approach / systematic review / systematic analysis / systematic effort**

👉 Example: A systematic review of the literature revealed several gaps in the current understanding of the disease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigators conducted a **systematic** examination of all financial records to identify irregularities and potential fraud.
   调查人员对所有财务记录进行了系统的审查，以发现违规行为和潜在的欺诈。

2. Her **systematic** approach to studying allowed her to master complex subjects more efficiently than her peers.
   她系统的学习方法使她比同龄人更高效地掌握了复杂的学科。

3. The organization implemented a **systematic** process for evaluating employee performance on a quarterly basis across all departments.
   该组织实施了一个系统的流程，按季度评估所有部门的员工绩效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "system-atic" — having a system means everything is planned and organized in a logical order.
把"systematic"想成"system-atic"——拥有一个系统意味着一切都按照逻辑顺序进行计划和组织。
