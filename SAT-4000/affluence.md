# 💰 affluence

🔊 /ˈæfluəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of having great wealth and material abundance; a plentiful flow or supply.
**中文：** 富裕；丰富；流入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wealth（财富）
- prosperity（繁荣）
- opulence（奢华）
- richness（富足）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **material affluence, growing affluence, age of affluence, relative affluence**
👉 Example: The region's affluence was evident in its modern infrastructure and thriving businesses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The postwar economic boom brought unprecedented **affluence** to families across the entire nation.
   战后的经济繁荣给全国各地的家庭带来了前所未有的富裕。

2. Despite their considerable **affluence**, the family maintained a modest and understated lifestyle in the community.
   尽管相当富裕，这个家庭在社区中依然保持着低调朴素的生活方式。

3. Critics argue that the pursuit of **affluence** has often come at the expense of environmental sustainability.
   批评人士认为，对富裕的追求往往以牺牲环境可持续性为代价。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Affluence" contains "flu" — imagine wealth flowing toward you like a river, because the Latin root "fluere" means "to flow."
"Affluence"里有"flu"——想象财富像河流一样向你涌来，因为拉丁词根"fluere"意为"流动"。
