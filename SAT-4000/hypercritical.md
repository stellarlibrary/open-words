# 🔍 hypercritical

🔊 /ˌhaɪpərˈkrɪtɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Excessively or unreasonably critical; inclined to find fault with even trivial matters
**中文：** adj. 吹毛求疵的；过分苛刻的；苛评的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overcritical（过分挑剔的）
- fault-finding（吹毛求疵的）
- captious（挑剔的）
- censorious（苛评的）
- nitpicking（斤斤计较的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hypercritical of others / hypercritical attitude / hypercritical review / hypercritical judgment**

👉 Example: The editor's hypercritical approach to manuscripts often discouraged promising young writers from submitting their work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was so **hypercritical** of student essays that even the most talented writers received pages of detailed corrections.
   这位教授对学生论文如此吹毛求疵，以至于即使最有才华的作者也会收到满页的详细修改意见。

2. A **hypercritical** manager who constantly finds fault with minor details can severely damage team morale and overall productivity.
   一个不断在小细节上挑毛病的苛刻经理会严重损害团队士气和整体生产力。

3. Critics argued that the regulatory committee had become excessively **hypercritical**, rejecting proposals over insignificant procedural technicalities.
   批评者认为监管委员会变得过分苛刻，因为无关紧要的程序技术问题而拒绝提案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hyper-critical" as "overly critical" — the prefix "hyper" means excessive, so hypercritical means critical beyond reason.
把"hypercritical"想成"hyper"（过度的）+"critical"（批判的）——超出合理范围的批评，即吹毛求疵。
