# 📏 adherence

🔊 /ədˈhɪr.əns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of faithfully following or sticking to a rule, belief, or standard; the state of being firmly attached to something
**中文：** n. 坚持，遵守；依附，粘着；忠诚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compliance（遵从）
- fidelity（忠诚）
- observance（遵守）
- devotion（忠心）
- allegiance（效忠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adherence to rules / strict adherence / adherence to principles / adherence to protocol / ensure adherence**

👉 Example: The inspector praised the factory's strict adherence to all international safety regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court evaluated whether the company demonstrated sufficient **adherence** to the environmental regulations in question.
   法院评估了该公司是否充分遵守了相关的环境法规。

2. Strict **adherence** to the prescribed medication regimen is essential for the treatment to yield positive results.
   严格遵守规定的药物治疗方案对于治疗取得积极效果至关重要。

3. The organization's unwavering **adherence** to its founding principles has earned it widespread respect and credibility.
   该组织对其创始原则的坚定遵守为它赢得了广泛的尊重和信誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adherence" as "adhere + ence" — the noun form of sticking to something. If you adhere to rules, your adherence is the quality of doing so consistently and faithfully.
把"adherence"想成"adhere + ence"——粘着、坚持的名词形式。如果你adhere（遵守）规则，你的adherence就是持续而忠实地这样做的品质。
