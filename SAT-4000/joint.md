# 🤝 joint

🔊 /dʒɔɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, verb | 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Shared by or involving two or more parties. (n.) A point of connection between bones; a junction where things meet. (v.) To connect or fit together; to cut meat at the joints
**中文：** adj. 联合的；共同的 n. 关节；接缝；接合处 v. 连接；把肉切成大块

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- combined（联合的）
- mutual（共同的）
- collective（集体的）
- shared（共有的）
- collaborative（协作的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **joint effort / joint venture / joint statement / joint account / joint custody**

👉 Example: The two nations issued a joint statement condemning the use of chemical weapons.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two universities launched a **joint** research initiative to investigate the long-term effects of climate change on biodiversity.
   两所大学启动了一项联合研究计划，以调查气候变化对生物多样性的长期影响。

2. Through a **joint** venture between public and private sectors, the deteriorating infrastructure was finally modernized and expanded.
   通过公私部门的联合企业，破旧的基础设施终于得到了现代化改造和扩建。

3. The investigators submitted a **joint** report detailing their findings on the systemic failures that led to the financial crisis.
   调查人员提交了一份联合报告，详细说明了导致金融危机的系统性失败。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "joint" as the point where two things "join" — whether bones joining in your body or people joining forces together.
把"joint"想成两个事物"join（连接）"的地方——无论是身体中骨骼的连接，还是人们联合力量。
