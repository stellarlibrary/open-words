# ✉️ epistolary

🔊 /ɪˈpɪstəleri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or in the form of letters; written as a series of letters or correspondence
**中文：** adj. 书信的；书信体的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- letter-based（书信式的）
- correspondence-related（通信相关的）
- written（书面的）
- literary（文学的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **epistolary novel / epistolary form / epistolary tradition / epistolary style**

👉 Example: Samuel Richardson's "Pamela" is widely regarded as one of the earliest epistolary novels in English literature.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **epistolary** novel unfolds entirely through letters exchanged between the two protagonists separated by war and circumstance.
   这部**书信体**小说完全通过被战争和境遇分隔的两位主人公之间的书信往来展开。

2. Scholars value the **epistolary** archives of Renaissance thinkers for the intimate glimpses they provide into intellectual life.
   学者们珍视文艺复兴时期思想家的**书信**档案，因为它们提供了对知识分子生活的亲密一瞥。

3. The author adopted an **epistolary** format to create a sense of immediacy and personal connection between the narrator and reader.
   作者采用了**书信体**格式，以在叙述者和读者之间营造一种即时感和个人联系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EPISTLE" inside "epistolary" — an epistle is a letter, so epistolary means relating to letters.
把"epistolary"中的"epistle（书信）"提取出来——epistle就是信件，所以epistolary就是与书信相关的。
