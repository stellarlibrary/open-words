# 🎨 vivid

🔊 /ˈvɪvɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Producing powerful feelings or clear images in the mind; intensely bright or deep in color; strikingly lifelike
**中文：** adj. 鲜艳的；生动的，栩栩如生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brilliant（鲜艳的）
- graphic（生动的）
- striking（醒目的）
- lifelike（栩栩如生的）
- intense（强烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vivid description / vivid imagination / vivid colors / vivid memory / vivid dream**

👉 Example: The author provided a vivid description of the ancient city that transported readers back in time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist's use of **vivid** pigments created a stunning contrast that immediately drew the viewer's attention to the canvas.
   这位艺术家对鲜艳颜料的运用形成了令人惊叹的对比，立刻吸引了观者对画布的注意。

2. Her **vivid** account of the expedition through the Amazon rainforest captivated every member of the scientific committee.
   她对亚马逊雨林探险的生动描述吸引了科学委员会的每一位成员。

3. The documentary presented such **vivid** imagery of marine ecosystems that audiences felt completely immersed in the underwater world.
   这部纪录片展现了如此生动的海洋生态系统影像，使观众感到完全沉浸在水下世界中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vivid" as coming from Latin "vivere" (to live) — something vivid is so real and bright that it seems alive before your eyes.
把"vivid"想成来自拉丁语"vivere"（生活）——生动的事物如此真实明亮，仿佛活生生地呈现在你眼前。
