# 🎓 salutatory

🔊 /səˈluːtəˌtɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Expressing greeting or welcome. (n.) An opening address, especially at a graduation ceremony
**中文：** n. 大学毕业典礼的贺词

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- welcoming（欢迎的）
- greeting（致敬的）
- introductory（开场的）
- commendatory（赞美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salutatory address / salutatory speech / salutatory remarks**

👉 Example: The valedictorian and the salutatorian each delivered a salutatory address at the commencement ceremony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student who earned the second-highest grades delivered the **salutatory** address at the graduation ceremony.
   成绩排名第二的学生在毕业典礼上发表了致敬贺词。

2. Her **salutatory** remarks set a warm and optimistic tone for the entire academic conference that followed.
   她的欢迎致辞为随后的整个学术会议定下了温暖而乐观的基调。

3. The dean opened the convocation with a brief **salutatory** speech acknowledging the distinguished guests in attendance.
   院长以一段简短的欢迎词开始了会议，向出席的贵宾致意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "salutatory" as related to "salute" — a salute is a greeting or sign of respect, so a salutatory speech is one that greets and honors the audience.
把"salutatory"和"salute（敬礼）"联系起来——敬礼是一种致敬或表示尊重的方式，因此salutatory的演讲就是向观众致敬和欢迎的演讲。
