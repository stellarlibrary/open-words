# ⚠️ negligence

🔊 /ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Failure to take proper care or exercise reasonable caution; carelessness or inattention that results in harm or damage
**中文：** n. 疏忽；忽视；粗心大意

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carelessness（粗心）
- inattention（疏忽）
- oversight（失察）
- dereliction（玩忽职守）
- laxity（松懈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **criminal negligence / gross negligence / professional negligence / negligence claim**

👉 Example: The hospital faced a lawsuit alleging negligence in the treatment of a critically ill patient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court determined that the company's **negligence** directly contributed to the environmental contamination of the surrounding area.
   法院认定该公司的疏忽直接导致了周围地区的环境污染。

2. Due to apparent **negligence** by the maintenance crew, the critical safety equipment failed during the emergency drill.
   由于维护人员的明显疏忽，关键安全设备在应急演习中发生故障。

3. The inquiry revealed systematic **negligence** in the oversight of financial transactions within the regulatory agency.
   调查揭示了监管机构在金融交易监管方面的系统性疏忽。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neg-LIG-ence" as "neglecting" — negligence is what happens when someone neglects their duties or responsibilities.
把"negligence"想成"neglecting（忽视）"——疏忽就是某人忽视了自己的职责或责任时发生的事。
