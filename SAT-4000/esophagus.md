# 🫁 esophagus

🔊 /ɪˈsɒfəɡəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The muscular tube connecting the throat to the stomach through which food passes during swallowing
**中文：** n. 食管；食道

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gullet（食道）
- food pipe（食管）
- throat passage（咽喉通道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **esophagus lining / esophagus cancer / esophagus inflammation / damage to the esophagus**

👉 Example: Chronic acid reflux can cause significant damage to the lining of the esophagus over time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The gastroenterologist performed an endoscopy to examine the patient's **esophagus** for signs of chronic inflammation or disease.
   胃肠病学家进行了内窥镜检查，以检查患者的食管是否有慢性炎症或疾病的迹象。

2. Persistent acid reflux can erode the delicate mucosal lining of the **esophagus** and increase the risk of serious complications.
   持续的胃酸反流会侵蚀食管脆弱的黏膜内层，并增加严重并发症的风险。

3. The anatomy lecture focused on how the **esophagus** uses rhythmic muscular contractions called peristalsis to transport food downward.
   解剖学讲座重点讲述了食管如何利用称为蠕动的节律性肌肉收缩将食物向下输送。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "esophagus" as "eso-phag-us" — "phag" comes from Greek "phagein" meaning "to eat," so the esophagus is the tube through which we eat.
把"esophagus"想成"eso-phag（吃）-us"——"phag"来自希腊语"phagein"意为"吃"，所以esophagus就是我们进食的管道。
