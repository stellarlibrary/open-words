# 🌟 magnanimous

🔊 /mæɡˈnænɪməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or less powerful person; noble in spirit and free from pettiness.
**中文：** adj. 宽宏大量的；有雅量的；高尚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- generous（慷慨的）
- benevolent（仁慈的）
- noble（高尚的）
- forgiving（宽容的）
- gracious（有雅量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **magnanimous gesture / magnanimous in victory / magnanimous spirit / magnanimous leader**

👉 Example: The magnanimous leader forgave his former opponents and invited them to join his administration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In a **magnanimous** gesture, the winning candidate praised her opponent's dedication and commitment to public service.
   获胜候选人以宽宏大量的姿态赞扬了对手对公共服务的奉献和投入。

2. The professor was **magnanimous** enough to extend the deadline after learning about the students' extraordinary circumstances.
   教授十分宽厚，在了解到学生们的特殊情况后延长了截止日期。

3. History remembers those rulers who were **magnanimous** in victory rather than vengeful toward their defeated enemies.
   历史铭记那些在胜利中宽宏大量而非对战败敌人实施报复的统治者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "magna" (great) + "animus" (spirit) — a magnanimous person has a "great spirit" that rises above pettiness and grudges.
把"magnanimous"想成"magna"（伟大的）+"animus"（精神）——一个宽宏大量的人拥有"伟大的精神"，超越了小气和怨恨。
