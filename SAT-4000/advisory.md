# 🧭 advisory

🔊 /ədˈvaɪ.zər.i/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having the power or duty to advise; relating to or containing advice or recommendations.
**中文：** 咨询的；顾问的；劝告的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consultative（咨询的）
- counseling（顾问的）
- recommendatory（建议的）
- guiding（指导的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **advisory + board / committee / role / capacity / opinion**

👉 Example: She served on the advisory board of the research institute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government established an **advisory** committee to evaluate the long-term effects of the policy.
   政府成立了一个咨询委员会来评估该政策的长期影响。

2. The consultant served in an **advisory** capacity, offering strategic guidance without executive authority.
   该顾问以咨询身份任职，提供战略指导但不拥有执行权力。

3. The national weather service issued an **advisory** warning residents of potential flooding in coastal areas.
   国家气象局发布了一则劝告性通知，提醒居民注意沿海地区可能发生的洪水。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Advisory" = "advise" + "-ory" (relating to). If it is advisory, it relates to giving advice.
联想记忆：advisory = advise（建议）+ -ory（与……有关的），即"与建议有关的"，也就是咨询的、顾问性的。
