# 📋 compliant

🔊 /kəmˈplaɪənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Inclined to agree with others or obey rules; disposed to yield to the wishes or demands of others; conforming to requirements
**中文：** adj. 顺从的；服从的；适应的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obedient（服从的）
- submissive（顺从的）
- acquiescent（默许的）
- docile（温顺的）
- amenable（顺从的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fully compliant / compliant with regulations / compliant behavior / become compliant**

👉 Example: All manufacturers must ensure their products are fully compliant with the latest safety standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company invested heavily in new software systems to become fully **compliant** with the updated federal data protection regulations.
   公司大力投资新的软件系统，以完全符合更新后的联邦数据保护法规。

2. A workforce that is overly **compliant** may lack the creative independence necessary to drive genuine innovation within an organization.
   过于顺从的员工队伍可能缺乏推动组织内真正创新所必需的创造性独立精神。

3. The auditors confirmed that every department was fully **compliant** with the international standards governing financial reporting and transparency.
   审计人员确认每个部门都完全符合管理财务报告和透明度的国际标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compliant" as "com-PLY-ant" — someone who plies or bends to follow rules and comply with every demand made of them.
把"compliant"想成"com-PLY-ant"——一个顺从弯曲去遵守规则、服从每个要求的人。
