# 🌊 overflow

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈfloʊ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To flow over the brim or edge of a container; to be excessively full or abundant. (n.) The excess amount that spills over; a surplus
**中文：** v. 溢出；泛滥；充溢 n. 溢出；泛滥；超值；溢值

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flood（泛滥）
- spill over（溢出）
- brim over（满溢）
- surplus（过剩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overflow with / overflow into / buffer overflow / overflow capacity / overflow parking**

👉 Example: The river began to overflow its banks after three consecutive days of heavy rainfall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The stadium was filled to capacity, and the **overflow** crowd gathered in the adjacent plaza to watch on large screens.
   体育场已满员，溢出的人群聚集在相邻广场上通过大屏幕观看。

2. After the torrential rains, the reservoir began to **overflow**, prompting officials to issue urgent evacuation warnings.
   暴雨过后，水库开始溢出，促使官员发布紧急疏散警告。

3. The charity event was **overflowing** with generous donations from community members eager to support the relief effort.
   慈善活动中充满了社区成员慷慨的捐赠，他们热切地支持救援工作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-flow" as "flowing over the top" — when a cup is too full, the water flows over the edge.
把"overflow"想成"flow over"——当杯子太满时，水从边缘流出来。
