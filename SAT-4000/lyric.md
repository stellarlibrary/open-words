# 🎵 lyric

🔊 /ˈlɪrɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Expressing personal emotions in an imaginative and musical way. (n.) A lyric poem; the words of a song
**中文：** adj. 抒情的；吟唱的 n. 抒情诗；歌词

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- poetic（诗意的）
- melodic（旋律的）
- songlike（歌曲般的）
- expressive（富有表现力的）
- verse（诗歌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lyric poetry / lyric soprano / song lyrics / lyric verse / lyric beauty**

👉 Example: The composer is celebrated for writing lyric melodies that convey deep emotional resonance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Romantic poets elevated **lyric** poetry to new heights by emphasizing individual emotion and the beauty of nature.
   浪漫主义诗人通过强调个人情感和自然之美将抒情诗提升到了新的高度。

2. The songwriter spent months refining the **lyrics** to ensure every word perfectly complemented the composition's haunting melody.
   这位词曲作者花了数月时间打磨歌词，以确保每个词都与乐曲萦绕心头的旋律完美契合。

3. Her **lyric** soprano voice filled the concert hall with a warmth and clarity that left the audience profoundly moved.
   她抒情女高音的嗓音以温暖与清澈充满了整个音乐厅，令观众深受感动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lyric" from "lyre" — the ancient Greek stringed instrument poets played while singing their emotional verses.
把"lyric"想成来自"lyre（里拉琴）"——古希腊诗人弹奏弦乐器时吟唱情感诗篇的乐器。
