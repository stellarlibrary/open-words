# 🌐 pervasive

🔊 /pərˈveɪsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Spreading widely throughout an area or a group of people; prevalent and omnipresent
**中文：** adj. 普遍的；到处渗透的；流行的；弥漫的；广泛存在的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ubiquitous（无处不在的）
- prevalent（盛行的）
- omnipresent（无所不在的）
- widespread（广泛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pervasive influence / pervasive problem / pervasive culture / pervasive sense of**

👉 Example: The pervasive use of social media has fundamentally altered how people communicate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pervasive** influence of technology on modern education has transformed both teaching methods and student expectations.
   技术对现代教育的广泛影响已经改变了教学方法和学生的期望。

2. Researchers documented the **pervasive** effects of air pollution on public health across multiple urban regions.
   研究人员记录了空气污染对多个城市地区公共健康的普遍影响。

3. A **pervasive** atmosphere of distrust made it exceedingly difficult for the negotiating parties to reach consensus.
   一种弥漫的不信任氛围使得谈判各方极难达成共识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pervasive" as "pervade" + "-ive" — if something pervades, it is pervasive, meaning it is everywhere you look.
把"pervasive"想成"pervade"（弥漫）+"-ive"（形容词后缀）——如果某物弥漫开来，它就是pervasive，到处都能看到。
