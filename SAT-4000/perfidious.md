# 🗡️ perfidious

🔊 /pərˈfɪdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Deceitful and untrustworthy; deliberately faithless or treacherous in the betrayal of trust
**中文：** adj. 背信弃义的；不忠的；有叛逆心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- treacherous（背叛的）
- disloyal（不忠的）
- faithless（不忠实的）
- traitorous（叛变的）
- deceitful（欺诈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perfidious ally / perfidious act / perfidious betrayal / perfidious conduct**

👉 Example: History reveals many instances of perfidious allies who turned against their former partners.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **perfidious** advisor secretly conspired with rival factions while publicly professing unwavering loyalty to the sovereign.
   这位背信弃义的顾问暗中与敌对派系勾结，同时公开宣称对君主矢志不渝。

2. Scholars have long debated whether the diplomat's **perfidious** negotiations ultimately led to the collapse of the fragile alliance.
   学者们长期争论这位外交官背信弃义的谈判是否最终导致了脆弱联盟的瓦解。

3. The novel's protagonist discovers that her most trusted companion has been **perfidious** throughout their entire arduous journey together.
   小说的主人公发现她最信任的同伴在他们整个艰苦的旅程中一直是不忠的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-FID-ious" as containing "fid" from "fidelity" — perfidious means the opposite of fidelity, a complete lack of faithfulness.
把"perfidious"想成包含"fid"（来自fidelity，忠诚）——perfidious意味着忠诚的反面，完全缺乏信义。
