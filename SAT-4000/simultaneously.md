# ⏱️ simultaneously

🔊 /ˌsaɪməlˈteɪniəsli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** At the same time; occurring or done at the same moment
**中文：** adv. 同时地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concurrently（同时地）
- at once（立刻，同时）
- synchronously（同步地）
- in tandem（一起，同时）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **simultaneously with / occur simultaneously / happen simultaneously / work simultaneously**

👉 Example: The two events occurred simultaneously, making it difficult to determine which caused the disruption.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor managed to **simultaneously** lecture on quantum mechanics and demonstrate the principles with live experiments.
   这位教授设法同时讲授量子力学并通过现场实验演示其原理。

2. Several nations **simultaneously** announced new trade agreements, signaling a coordinated effort toward economic reform.
   几个国家同时宣布了新的贸易协定，标志着一项协调一致的经济改革努力。

3. The software can **simultaneously** process thousands of transactions without any noticeable decline in system performance.
   该软件可以同时处理数千笔交易，而系统性能不会出现明显下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "simul-" as "similar time" — things happening at a similar (the same) time are occurring simultaneously.
把"simul-"想成"similar time"——在相似（相同）时间发生的事情就是同时发生的。
