# 🔨 iconoclast

🔊 /aɪˈkɑːnəklæst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who attacks or criticizes cherished beliefs, traditional institutions, or established values; originally, a destroyer of religious images or icons
**中文：** n. 偶像破坏者；攻击传统观念和习俗的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rebel（反叛者）
- nonconformist（不墨守成规者）
- dissident（持不同意见者）
- heretic（异端者）
- radical（激进分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a true iconoclast / intellectual iconoclast / iconoclast in the field of / regarded as an iconoclast**

👉 Example: The philosopher was regarded as an iconoclast who dared to challenge the most deeply held assumptions of Western thought.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The celebrated **iconoclast** spent decades dismantling conventional wisdom about economics, earning both admiration and fierce opposition from peers.
   这位著名的反传统者花了数十年时间拆解关于经济学的传统观念，赢得了同行的钦佩和激烈反对。

2. As an **iconoclast** in the art world, she rejected established aesthetic standards and pioneered a radically new form of visual expression.
   作为艺术界的叛逆者，她拒绝了既定的审美标准，开创了一种全新的视觉表达形式。

3. The author's reputation as an **iconoclast** grew after he published a controversial book challenging the foundational myths of the nation.
   这位作者出版了一本挑战国家基础神话的争议性著作后，其反传统的名声日益增长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "icon" (image, idol) + "clast" (breaker, from Greek "klastes") — an iconoclast is literally an "idol-breaker," someone who smashes established beliefs.
把"iconoclast"拆分为"icon"（偶像）+"clast"（打破者，来自希腊语"klastes"）——字面意思就是"打碎偶像的人"，即挑战传统信仰的人。
