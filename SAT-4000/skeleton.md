# 💀 skeleton

🔊 /ˈskɛlɪtn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The internal framework of bones; an outline or basic structure; an emaciated person. (adj.) Of or relating to a skeleton; reduced to a minimum
**中文：** n. 骨骼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- framework（框架）
- bones（骨骼）
- outline（纲要）
- structure（结构）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **skeleton crew / skeleton key / skeleton in the closet / skeletal structure**

👉 Example: The company operated with a skeleton crew during the holiday season to keep essential functions running.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Archaeologists discovered a remarkably well-preserved **skeleton** dating back over three thousand years in the excavation site near the river.
   考古学家在河流附近的挖掘现场发现了一具保存极为完好的、距今三千多年的骨骼。

2. The professor presented only the **skeleton** of the research proposal, intending to flesh out the details after receiving initial feedback.
   教授仅展示了研究提案的纲要，打算在收到初步反馈后再充实细节。

3. The museum displayed the complete **skeleton** of a Tyrannosaurus rex, attracting thousands of visitors from around the world each year.
   博物馆展出了一具完整的霸王龙骨骼，每年吸引来自世界各地的数千名游客。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "skeleton" as the basic "shell" of a body — just as a building has a steel skeleton before walls are added, a body has a bone skeleton.
把"skeleton"想成身体的基本"外壳"——就像建筑在加墙之前有钢骨架一样，身体也有骨骼框架。
