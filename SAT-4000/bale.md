# 📦 bale

🔊 /beɪl/<br>
🏷️ *n. v. | 名词 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A large bundle of goods tightly bound for storage or transport; great sorrow or calamity. (v.) To make into bales; to compress and bind tightly.

**中文：** （名词）大包，大捆（紧密捆扎用于储存或运输）；灾祸，不幸。（动词）将……打包，捆扎。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- bundle（捆）
- package（包裹）
- parcel（包）
- woe（灾祸）
- calamity（灾难）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

In modern usage, "bale" most commonly refers to a compressed bundle of material such as hay, cotton, or straw. The archaic meaning of "evil" or "calamity" survives mainly in the adjective "baleful." Do not confuse "bale" (a bundle) with "bail" (security for release from custody).

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The farmers loaded enormous **bales** of hay onto the truck before the approaching storm could damage the harvest.
   农民们在即将到来的暴风雨损坏收成之前，将大捆大捆的**干草包**装上了卡车。

2. Workers at the recycling facility used hydraulic machines to **bale** cardboard into compact blocks for efficient shipping.
   回收设施的工人使用液压机将纸板**打包**成紧凑的块状以便高效运输。

3. The warehouse stored hundreds of **bales** of raw cotton awaiting inspection before export to overseas textile manufacturers.
   仓库储存了数百**包**原棉，等待在出口到海外纺织制造商之前接受检验。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Picture a big rectangular "bale" of hay on a farm — it rhymes with "tale," and a bale of hay could tell a tale of hard farm work. For the "calamity" meaning, think of "bale" as the root of "baleful" (evil).

想象农场上一个大的长方形"干草包"——它和"tale（故事）"押韵，一捆干草可以讲述辛苦农活的故事。对于"灾祸"的含义，可以把"bale"看作"baleful（恶意的）"的词根。
