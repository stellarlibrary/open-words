# ✨ inspiration

🔊 /ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of being mentally stimulated to do or feel something creative; a person or thing that inspires; the act of breathing in
**中文：** n. 灵感；鼓舞；激励；吸气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- motivation（动力）
- stimulus（刺激）
- muse（灵感来源）
- encouragement（鼓励）
- creativity（创造力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **source of inspiration / draw inspiration from / find inspiration / inspiration for / inspiration behind**

👉 Example: The composer drew inspiration from the dramatic landscapes of the Scottish Highlands.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist credited her childhood fascination with the natural world as the primary **inspiration** behind her groundbreaking research.
   这位科学家将她童年对自然界的迷恋归功为她开创性研究背后的主要灵感来源。

2. His courageous recovery from a devastating injury served as an **inspiration** to countless athletes struggling with similar setbacks.
   他从毁灭性伤病中勇敢恢复的经历成为无数面临类似挫折的运动员的鼓舞。

3. The architect found **inspiration** in classical Greek temples, blending ancient aesthetic principles with modern construction techniques seamlessly.
   这位建筑师从古希腊神庙中获得灵感，将古代美学原则与现代建筑技术无缝融合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in + spiration (from Latin spirare, to breathe)" — to breathe something in, like breathing in a creative idea that fills your mind.
把"inspiration"想成"in + spiration（源自拉丁语spirare，呼吸）"——吸入某种东西，就像吸入一个充满你头脑的创意想法。
