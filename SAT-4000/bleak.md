# 🌬️ bleak

🔊 /bliːk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Cold and dreary; barren and exposed to harsh weather; offering little hope or encouragement; grim and depressing; monotonous.
**中文：** adj. 阴冷的，寒冷的；荒凉的，无遮蔽的；黯淡的，无希望的；令人沮丧的；单调的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- desolate（荒凉的）
- dreary（沉闷的）
- grim（严酷的）
- barren（贫瘠的）
- dismal（阴沉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bleak outlook / bleak landscape / bleak future / bleak prospects / bleak winter**

👉 Example: The economic forecast painted a bleak picture for small businesses in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bleak** landscape of the northern tundra stretched endlessly, devoid of trees or any visible shelter.
   北方苔原**荒凉的**景象无尽延伸，看不到树木或任何遮蔽物。

2. Analysts presented a **bleak** assessment of the nation's fiscal health, warning of prolonged economic stagnation ahead.
   分析师们对该国的财政状况做出了**黯淡的**评估，警告前方将面临长期经济停滞。

3. The refugees faced a **bleak** winter without adequate housing, medical supplies, or reliable sources of nutrition.
   难民们面临着一个**严酷的**冬天，没有足够的住房、医疗物资或可靠的营养来源。
