# 😴 slothful

🔊 /ˈslɔːθfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lazy and unwilling to work or exert effort; characterized by habitual idleness; sluggish
**中文：** adj. 偷懒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lazy（懒惰的）
- indolent（怠惰的）
- idle（闲散的）
- lethargic（无精打采的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **slothful behavior / slothful attitude / slothful worker / slothful approach**

👉 Example: The supervisor reprimanded the slothful employee for repeatedly failing to meet project deadlines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee criticized the agency's **slothful** response to the environmental crisis that had endangered thousands of residents.
   委员会批评了该机构对危及数千居民的环境危机的怠惰回应。

2. A **slothful** approach to academic research will inevitably result in superficial analysis and unreliable conclusions.
   对学术研究采取懒散的态度必然会导致肤浅的分析和不可靠的结论。

3. The once-prosperous kingdom fell into decline because its **slothful** rulers neglected governance and military preparedness.
   这个曾经繁荣的王国走向衰落，因为其懒惰的统治者忽视了治理和军事准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slothful" as "sloth + full" — completely full of sloth, meaning entirely consumed by laziness.
把"slothful"想成"sloth（怠惰）+ full（充满的）"——充满了怠惰，意味着完全被懒惰所占据。
