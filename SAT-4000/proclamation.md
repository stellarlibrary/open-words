# 📜 proclamation

🔊 /ˌprɒkləˈmeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An official public announcement or declaration, especially one made by a person in authority
**中文：** n. 公告；宣布；宣告；公布

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- declaration（宣言）
- announcement（公告）
- decree（法令）
- edict（敕令）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **issue a proclamation / royal proclamation / official proclamation / proclamation of independence**

👉 Example: The king issued a proclamation granting amnesty to all political prisoners held in the kingdom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The president signed an official **proclamation** designating the first week of October as National Heritage Week.
   总统签署了一项正式公告，将十月的第一周定为国家文化遗产周。

2. The historic **proclamation** of emancipation fundamentally altered the social and legal landscape of the entire nation.
   这项历史性的解放宣言从根本上改变了整个国家的社会和法律格局。

3. Local authorities released a **proclamation** urging residents to conserve water resources during the prolonged summer drought.
   地方当局发布公告，敦促居民在漫长的夏季干旱期间节约用水资源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proclamation" as "proclaim + ation" — it is the noun form of proclaim, the formal act of publicly announcing something.
把"proclamation"想成"proclaim（宣告）+ ation（名词后缀）"——宣告的行为或文件。
