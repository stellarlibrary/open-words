# 🌵 desertification

🔊 /dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture
**中文：** n. 荒漠化；沙漠化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- land degradation（土地退化）
- aridification（干旱化）
- soil erosion（土壤侵蚀）
- desolation（荒芜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **combat desertification / prevent desertification / desertification process / threat of desertification**

👉 Example: International organizations have launched programs to combat desertification in sub-Saharan Africa.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Desertification** poses a severe threat to global food security, as millions of hectares of arable land are lost annually.
   荒漠化对全球粮食安全构成严重威胁，因为每年有数百万公顷的可耕地流失。

2. The government implemented large-scale reforestation programs specifically designed to reverse the accelerating trend of **desertification** in the region.
   政府实施了大规模的重新造林计划，专门旨在逆转该地区加速的荒漠化趋势。

3. Climate scientists have identified overgrazing and unsustainable irrigation practices as primary contributors to **desertification** in arid zones worldwide.
   气候科学家已确定过度放牧和不可持续的灌溉方式是全球干旱地区荒漠化的主要原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "desert-ification" — the making of a desert; adding "-ification" to "desert" means the process of turning land into a desert.
把"desertification"想成"desert-ification（沙漠化过程）"——在"desert"后加上"-ification"表示将土地变成沙漠的过程。
