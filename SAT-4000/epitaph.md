# 🪦 epitaph

🔊 /ˈepɪtæf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An inscription on a tombstone in memory of the person buried there; a brief statement commemorating a deceased person
**中文：** n. 碑文，墓志铭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inscription（铭文）
- memorial（纪念文）
- tribute（悼词）
- elegy（挽歌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **write an epitaph / epitaph on a tombstone / fitting epitaph / famous epitaph**

👉 Example: The simple epitaph on the soldier's grave read: "He gave his life so that others might live."

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet composed a moving **epitaph** for his beloved mentor that captured the essence of a life devoted to scholarship.
   诗人为他敬爱的导师撰写了一篇感人的**墓志铭**，概括了一生致力于学术的精髓。

2. Shakespeare's **epitaph** famously curses anyone who dares to disturb his bones, a warning that has been heeded for centuries.
   莎士比亚的**墓志铭**著名地诅咒任何敢于打扰他遗骨的人，这一警告已被遵守了数百年。

3. Critics suggested that the failed policy initiative would serve as a fitting **epitaph** for the outgoing administration's legacy.
   评论家们认为，这项失败的政策倡议将成为即将卸任的政府遗产的一个恰当**墓志铭**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EPI-TAPH" as "epi (upon) + taphos (tomb)" — words written upon a tomb to honor the deceased.
把"epitaph"拆分为"epi（在...之上）+ taphos（坟墓）"——写在坟墓上的文字，用以纪念逝者。
