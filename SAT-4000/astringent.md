# 🍋 astringent

🔊 /əˈstrɪn.dʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a sharp, harsh, or severe quality; causing contraction of body tissues and stopping bleeding; (n.) a substance that contracts tissues and stops bleeding.
**中文：** (adj.) 涩的；收敛性的；止血的；严厉的 (n.) 收敛剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acerbic（尖酸的）
- caustic（刻薄的）
- harsh（严厉的）
- styptic（止血的）
- tart（酸涩的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **astringent taste/quality/tone/criticism/lotion/solution**

👉 Example: The dermatologist recommended an astringent lotion to help reduce the oiliness of her skin.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unripe persimmon left an **astringent** sensation on her tongue that lingered for several minutes.
   未成熟的柿子在她舌头上留下了一种涩涩的感觉，持续了好几分钟。

2. His **astringent** remarks about the proposal discouraged the junior staff from offering further suggestions.
   他对该提案的严厉评价使初级员工不敢再提出更多建议。

3. The nurse applied an **astringent** solution to the wound to halt the bleeding before bandaging it.
   护士在包扎伤口前涂抹了一种收敛溶液来止血。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ASTRINGENT" contains "STRING" — imagine a string tightening and pulling tissue together, which is exactly what an astringent does.
"ASTRINGENT"中包含"STRING（绳子）"——想象一根绳子收紧并把组织拉在一起，这正是收敛剂的作用。
