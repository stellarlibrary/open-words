# 🌱 biennial

🔊 /baɪˈɛniəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring every two years; (adj./n.) of a plant that takes two years to complete its life cycle
**中文：** adj. 两年一次的；两年生的 n. 二年生植物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- biannual（两年一次的）
- two-yearly（每两年的）
- semiperennial（半多年生的）
- alternate-year（隔年的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **biennial event / biennial exhibition / biennial plant / biennial report / biennial conference**

👉 Example: The Venice Biennale is one of the most prestigious biennial art exhibitions in the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization holds a **biennial** conference where scholars from around the globe present their most recent research findings.
   该组织每两年举办一次会议，来自全球的学者在会上展示他们最新的研究成果。

2. Carrots and parsley are common **biennial** plants that produce foliage in the first year and flowers in the second.
   胡萝卜和欧芹是常见的二年生植物，第一年长叶，第二年开花。

3. The **biennial** audit revealed significant improvements in the institution's financial management and regulatory compliance practices.
   两年一次的审计揭示了该机构在财务管理和法规合规实践方面的显著改善。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi" (two) + "ennial" (from Latin annus, year) — biennial literally means "every two years," just as biweekly means every two weeks.
把"biennial"拆分为"bi"（二）+"ennial"（源自拉丁语annus，年）——biennial字面意思就是"每两年"，就像biweekly意为每两周一样。
