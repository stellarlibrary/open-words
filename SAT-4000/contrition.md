# 🙏 contrition

🔊 /kənˈtrɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of feeling deep remorse and penitence for one's wrongdoing; sincere regret for past actions
**中文：** n. 痛悔；悔悟；后悔

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- remorse（悔恨）
- penitence（忏悔）
- repentance（悔改）
- regret（遗憾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show contrition / express contrition / genuine contrition / an act of contrition / deep contrition**

👉 Example: The defendant showed no contrition for his actions, which angered both the judge and the jury.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The public expected a display of genuine **contrition** from the executive who had orchestrated the massive financial fraud scheme.
   公众期望策划大规模金融欺诈计划的高管表现出真诚的悔悟。

2. Her letter of **contrition** moved the entire committee, ultimately persuading them to grant her a second opportunity to participate.
   她的忏悔信感动了整个委员会，最终说服他们给予她第二次参与的机会。

3. Without sincere **contrition**, the diplomat's formal apology was widely regarded as a hollow and purely ceremonial political gesture.
   如果没有真诚的悔悟，这位外交官的正式道歉被普遍认为是空洞的、纯粹形式上的政治姿态。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "contrition" as "con-CRUSH-tion" — your heart is crushed with guilt when you feel true contrition for something you have done.
把"contrition"想成"心被压碎"——当你为所做之事感到真正的悔悟时，你的心因内疚而被压碎。
