# 🔬 filter

🔊 /ˈfɪltər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To pass through a filter to remove impurities; to seep or permeate gradually. (n.) A device or substance used to strain out unwanted material
**中文：** v. 滤过；渗入 n. 滤波器；过滤器；滤管

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strain（过滤）
- sieve（筛选）
- permeate（渗透）
- screen（筛选）
- purify（净化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **filter out / filter through / water filter / air filter / filter information**

👉 Example: Sunlight filtered through the canopy of leaves, casting dappled shadows on the forest floor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team used a specialized membrane to **filter** out microscopic contaminants from the water supply effectively.
   研究团队使用专用膜有效地过滤掉了供水中的微观污染物。

2. News of the unexpected policy change began to **filter** through the organization, generating widespread concern among employees.
   这项意外政策变化的消息开始在组织中渗透传开，引起了员工的广泛担忧。

3. Scientists developed an advanced optical **filter** capable of isolating specific wavelengths of light for precise spectral analysis.
   科学家开发了一种先进的光学滤波器，能够隔离特定波长的光以进行精确的光谱分析。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "filter" as "fill-ter" — it fills a barrier with tiny holes that only let certain things pass through, trapping the rest.
把"filter"想成"fill-ter"——它像一个充满小孔的屏障，只允许某些东西通过，其余的则被拦截。
