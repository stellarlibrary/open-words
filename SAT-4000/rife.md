# 🌊 rife

🔊 /raɪf/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Widespread, prevalent, or commonly occurring, especially of something undesirable
**中文：** adj. 普遍的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- widespread（广泛的）
- prevalent（盛行的）
- pervasive（普遍的）
- rampant（猖獗的）
- abundant（大量的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rife with corruption / rife with speculation / rife with problems / rumors are rife**

👉 Example: The region was rife with political unrest following the disputed election results.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's internal report revealed that the department was **rife** with corruption, nepotism, and gross financial mismanagement.
   该组织的内部报告揭示，该部门腐败、裙带关系和严重财务管理不善现象普遍存在。

2. Speculation was **rife** among analysts that the central bank would raise interest rates before the end of the fiscal quarter.
   分析师中普遍猜测央行将在本财季结束前加息。

3. The historical record shows that medieval European cities were **rife** with disease, largely due to inadequate sanitation and overcrowding.
   历史记录表明，中世纪欧洲城市疾病盛行，主要由于卫生条件不足和过度拥挤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rife" rhyming with "life" — when something is rife, it is so widespread that it has become a common part of everyday life.
把"rife"和"life"押韵联想——当某物是rife的，它就普遍到成为日常生活的一部分。
