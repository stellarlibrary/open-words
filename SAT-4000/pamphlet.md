# 📄 pamphlet

🔊 /ˈpæmflɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments about a single subject
**中文：** n. 小册子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- booklet（小册子）
- leaflet（传单）
- brochure（宣传册）
- flyer（传单）
- tract（小册子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **distribute a pamphlet / publish a pamphlet / informational pamphlet / political pamphlet**

👉 Example: Volunteers distributed pamphlets outlining the candidate's key policy positions to every household in the district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization published a **pamphlet** explaining the potential environmental consequences of the proposed industrial development project.
   该组织出版了一本小册子，解释拟议工业开发项目可能带来的环境后果。

2. During the eighteenth century, political **pamphlets** played a crucial role in shaping public opinion and fueling revolutionary movements.
   在十八世纪，政治小册子在塑造公众舆论和推动革命运动方面发挥了关键作用。

3. The health department issued a detailed **pamphlet** advising residents on preventive measures against the spread of infectious diseases.
   卫生部门发布了一份详细的小册子，指导居民采取预防传染病传播的措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pamphlet" as "palm + flat" — a small, flat booklet that fits in the palm of your hand.
把"pamphlet"想成"palm + flat"——一本小巧扁平、可以放在手掌中的小册子。
