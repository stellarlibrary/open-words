# ⚔️ reprisal

🔊 /rɪˈpraɪzl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An act of retaliation against an enemy or adversary; a retaliatory measure, especially in warfare or conflict
**中文：** n. 报复（行为）；报复性劫掠

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- retaliation（报复）
- revenge（复仇）
- vengeance（报仇）
- retribution（惩罚）
- counterattack（反击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fear of reprisal / in reprisal for / military reprisal / reprisal attack / threat of reprisal**

👉 Example: Civilians lived in constant fear of reprisal from the occupying forces after the resistance attack.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bombing was carried out in **reprisal** for the assassination of a high-ranking military commander the previous week.
   这次轰炸是对上周一名高级军事指挥官被暗杀的报复行动。

2. Many witnesses refused to testify against the criminal organization, fearing violent **reprisal** against themselves and their families.
   许多证人拒绝出庭指证该犯罪组织，担心自己和家人遭到暴力报复。

3. International law strictly prohibits **reprisal** attacks targeting civilian populations during periods of armed conflict between nations.
   国际法严格禁止在国家间武装冲突期间对平民进行报复性攻击。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-prisal" — "re" (back) + "prisal" (like "prize" or seize) — to seize back, to strike back as revenge for a wrong done.
把"reprisal"想成"re"（回来）+"prisal"（像"prize"，夺取）——夺回来，为所受的冤屈进行报复性回击。
