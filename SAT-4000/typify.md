# 🏛️ typify

🔊 /ˈtɪpɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To serve as a typical example of; to represent or symbolize a particular quality or characteristic
**中文：** v. 代表；作为……的典型；象征

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exemplify（例证）
- represent（代表）
- embody（体现）
- symbolize（象征）
- illustrate（说明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **typify an era / typify the spirit of / typify a trend / typify a generation**

👉 Example: Her bold leadership style typifies the new generation of corporate executives.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ornate cathedrals of medieval Europe **typify** the Gothic architectural style with their pointed arches and flying buttresses.
   欧洲中世纪华丽的大教堂以其尖拱和飞扶壁代表了哥特式建筑风格。

2. His reluctance to embrace new technology **typifies** the resistance that many traditional industries face during periods of innovation.
   他不愿接受新技术代表了许多传统行业在创新时期面临的抵制态度。

3. The candidate's populist rhetoric **typifies** a broader political trend that has emerged across several democratic nations.
   该候选人的民粹主义言论代表了在多个民主国家出现的更广泛的政治趋势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "typify" as "type + ify" — to make something into the type, to be the perfect representative of a category.
把"typify"想成"type（类型）+ ify（使成为）"——使之成为该类型的代表，即完美地代表某一类别。
