# 🤫 acquiescence

🔊 /ˌækwiˈɛsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The reluctant acceptance of something without protest; passive compliance or silent agreement
**中文：** 默许；默从

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compliance（顺从）
- consent（同意）
- submission（屈服）
- resignation（顺从）
- assent（赞同）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **silent acquiescence / acquiescence in / acquiescence to / passive acquiescence**

👉 Example: His silence was interpreted as acquiescence to the terms of the proposed agreement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The regime maintained power through the passive **acquiescence** of a population too fearful to voice open dissent.
   该政权通过民众因恐惧而不敢公开反对的消极默从来维持权力。

2. Her **acquiescence** to the unfair working conditions was motivated by a desperate need to support her family.
   她对不公平工作条件的默许是出于养家糊口的迫切需要。

3. Legal scholars argued that prolonged **acquiescence** could be construed as implicit approval of the contested regulatory policy.
   法律学者认为，长期的默许可能被解释为对有争议的监管政策的隐性认可。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "acquiescence" as "a-quiet-essence" — the essence of staying quiet, accepting things without making a fuss.
把"acquiescence"想成"a-quiet-essence"——保持安静的本质，不声不响地接受事物。
