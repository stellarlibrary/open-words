# 🏥 rehabilitate

🔊 /ˌriːhəˈbɪlɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To restore to a former state of health, good condition, or good standing; to restore one's reputation or rights; to bring back to normal functioning.
**中文：** v. 使康复；使恢复名誉；使恢复原状；使复原

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restore（恢复）
- recover（康复）
- reinstate（使复职）
- renovate（修复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rehabilitate a patient / rehabilitate one's image / rehabilitate offenders / rehabilitate a building**

👉 Example: The program was designed to rehabilitate former offenders and help them reintegrate into society.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physical therapy center worked diligently to **rehabilitate** patients recovering from severe spinal injuries sustained in accidents.
   物理治疗中心努力帮助在事故中遭受严重脊柱损伤的患者进行康复。

2. The politician launched a public campaign to **rehabilitate** his tarnished reputation after the corruption scandal became widely known.
   这位政治家在腐败丑闻广为人知后发起了一场公共运动以恢复其受损的名誉。

3. Conservation efforts aimed to **rehabilitate** the degraded wetlands, restoring the natural habitat for dozens of endangered aquatic species.
   保护工作旨在修复退化的湿地，为数十种濒危水生物种恢复自然栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-habilitate" as "re + ability + ate" — to give someone their abilities back again, restoring them to full capacity.
把"rehabilitate"想成"re（再次）+ ability（能力）+ ate"——再次赋予某人能力，使其恢复到完全状态。
