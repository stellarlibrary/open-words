# 🏢 annex

🔊 /əˈnɛks/ (v.), /ˈænɛks/ (n.)<br>
🏷️ *v. to append; to acquire; to seize | 动词：附加；获得；并吞 · n. addition; supplementary building | 名词：附加物；附属建筑物*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To add or attach, especially to something larger; to incorporate territory into an existing political unit. (n.) An addition to a building; a supplementary structure or document.
**中文：** （动词）附加或连接，尤指连接到更大的事物上；将领土并入现有政治实体。（名词）建筑物的附属部分；补充性建筑或文件。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- append（附加）
- seize（夺取）
- incorporate（合并）
- extension（扩展部分）
- supplement（补充物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **annex territory, annex a region, annex a document, building annex, hospital annex**
👉 Example: The empire sought to annex the neighboring province to expand its access to valuable natural resources.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The powerful nation moved swiftly to **annex** the resource-rich territory despite strong international condemnation and protests.
   那个强大的国家迅速行动并吞了这片资源丰富的领土，尽管遭到了强烈的国际谴责和抗议。

2. The university constructed a modern **annex** adjacent to the historic library to accommodate its growing collection of manuscripts.
   大学在历史悠久的图书馆旁边建造了一座现代化的附属建筑，以容纳其不断增长的手稿收藏。

3. The committee voted to **annex** several supplementary clauses to the original treaty before submitting it for ratification.
   委员会投票决定在提交批准之前为原始条约附加几项补充条款。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "annex" as "a next" addition — something added next to what already exists, whether it is land, a building, or a document.
把"annex"想成"a next"（下一个）附加物——添加到已有事物旁边的东西，无论是土地、建筑还是文件。
