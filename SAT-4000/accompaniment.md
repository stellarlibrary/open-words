# 🎵 accompaniment

🔊 /əˈkʌmpənɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A musical part that supports a leading melody; something that supplements or goes along with something else.
**中文：** 伴奏；伴随物，附属物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- backup（伴奏）
- complement（补充物）
- supplement（附加物）
- adjunct（附属物）
- escort（伴随）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **musical/piano accompaniment**; **to the accompaniment of**; **as an accompaniment to**

👉 Example: She sang the aria to the accompaniment of a full orchestra.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vocalist delivered a stunning performance with only a simple piano **accompaniment** in the background.
   这位歌手仅以简单的钢琴伴奏为背景，献上了一场令人惊叹的演出。

2. Economic growth in the region came with the inevitable **accompaniment** of rapid urbanization and social change.
   该地区的经济增长伴随着快速城市化和社会变革这一不可避免的附带现象。

3. The chef recommended a light white wine as the perfect **accompaniment** to the delicate seafood dish.
   厨师推荐了一款清淡的白葡萄酒作为这道精致海鲜菜肴的完美搭配。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accompaniment" = "accompany" + "-ment" — the thing that accompanies, whether music or anything else.
accompaniment 就是"accompany（陪伴）"加"-ment"——陪伴之物，无论是音乐伴奏还是其他伴随物。
