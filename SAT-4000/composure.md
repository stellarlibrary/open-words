# 🧘 composure

🔊 /kəmˈpoʊʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being calm, self-possessed, and in control of one's emotions, especially in a difficult situation
**中文：** n. 镇静；沉着；自若

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- calmness（冷静）
- equanimity（沉着）
- serenity（平静）
- poise（泰然）
- self-possession（自制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain composure / lose composure / regain composure / with composure**

👉 Example: The negotiator maintained her composure despite the increasingly hostile demands from the opposing side.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pilot's remarkable **composure** during the engine failure was instrumental in safely landing the aircraft with all passengers aboard.
   飞行员在发动机故障期间表现出的非凡镇定对安全着陆载有全部乘客的飞机至关重要。

2. She briefly lost her **composure** upon hearing the devastating news but quickly recovered to address the concerned audience.
   听到这个毁灭性消息时，她短暂地失去了镇定，但很快恢复过来向关切的观众发表讲话。

3. Maintaining one's **composure** under intense scrutiny is an essential quality for any public figure serving in high-stakes leadership roles.
   在严密审视下保持镇静是任何担任高风险领导角色的公众人物必备的素质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "composure" as "compose-SURE" — when you are sure you can compose yourself, you have composure.
把"composure"想成"确定能自我调整"——当你确信自己能控制情绪时，你就拥有了镇静。
