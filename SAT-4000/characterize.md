# 🖊️ characterize

🔊 /ˈkærɪktəˌraɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To describe the distinctive qualities of someone or something; to be a distinguishing feature of
**中文：** v. 描绘……的特性；具有……的特征；表现……的特色；刻画……的性格

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- describe（描述）
- depict（描绘）
- distinguish（区分）
- typify（代表）
- define（定义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **characterize as / characterize by / commonly characterized / broadly characterize**

👉 Example: Critics characterize the new policy as overly restrictive and counterproductive.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians often **characterize** the Renaissance as a period of unprecedented cultural and intellectual flourishing across Europe.
   历史学家通常将文艺复兴描述为欧洲文化和思想空前繁荣的时期。

2. Extreme drought and rising temperatures **characterize** the climate patterns that scientists have observed in this arid region.
   极端干旱和气温上升是科学家在这一干旱地区观察到的气候模式的典型特征。

3. The author skillfully **characterize**d the protagonist as a deeply conflicted individual torn between duty and personal desire.
   作者巧妙地将主人公刻画为一个在责任与个人欲望之间深感矛盾的人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "character + ize" — to "ize" means to make or describe, so characterize means to describe someone's character.
把"characterize"想成"character（性格）+ ize（使……化）"——描述某人的性格特征。
