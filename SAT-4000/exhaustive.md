# 🔍 exhaustive

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Including or considering all elements or aspects; fully comprehensive; leaving nothing out
**中文：** adj. 详尽的，无遗漏的，彻底的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comprehensive（全面的）
- thorough（彻底的）
- complete（完整的）
- extensive（广泛的）
- all-inclusive（包罗万象的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exhaustive study / exhaustive review / exhaustive analysis / exhaustive list / exhaustive research**

👉 Example: The committee conducted an exhaustive review of all submitted applications before making its decision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forensic team performed an **exhaustive** analysis of the crime scene, documenting every piece of physical evidence found.
   法医团队对犯罪现场进行了详尽的分析，记录了发现的每一件物证。

2. After an **exhaustive** search of the historical archives, the scholar finally located the missing correspondence from that era.
   在对历史档案进行彻底搜索之后，这位学者终于找到了那个时代遗失的信件。

3. The regulatory agency published an **exhaustive** report covering every aspect of environmental compliance within the industry sector.
   监管机构发布了一份详尽的报告，涵盖了该行业内环境合规的各个方面。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exhaustive" as "exhaust-ive" — you exhaust every possibility, leaving absolutely nothing uncovered.
把"exhaustive"想成"exhaust-ive"——你穷尽了每一种可能性，不留任何遗漏。
