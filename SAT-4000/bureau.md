# 🏢 bureau

🔊 /ˈbjʊəroʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A government department or agency; a writing desk with drawers; a chest of drawers
**中文：** n. 局，处，署；办公桌；衣柜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agency（机构）
- department（部门）
- office（办公室）
- division（处，科）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **federal bureau / bureau of investigation / bureau chief / travel bureau**

👉 Example: The Federal Bureau of Investigation launched a comprehensive inquiry into the financial fraud scheme.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **bureau** issued new regulations requiring all financial institutions to submit quarterly compliance reports to federal authorities.
   该局发布了新规定，要求所有金融机构向联邦当局提交季度合规报告。

2. She organized her correspondence neatly inside the antique **bureau** that had belonged to her grandmother for decades.
   她把信件整齐地放进那张属于祖母数十年的古董写字台里。

3. The census **bureau** released demographic data indicating significant population shifts toward urban centers across the nation.
   人口普查局发布的人口数据显示，全国范围内人口正大规模向城市中心转移。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bureau" as a French word for "desk" — from the desk where officials work, it came to mean the office or agency itself.
"bureau"是法语中"桌子"的意思——从官员们工作的桌子引申为办公室或机构本身。
