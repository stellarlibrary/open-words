# 🔬 diagnostic

🔊 /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the identification of an illness or problem by examination of symptoms. (n.) A procedure or test used to identify a disease or condition
**中文：** adj. 诊断的 n. 诊断法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- analytical（分析的）
- investigative（调查的）
- indicative（指示的）
- evaluative（评估的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diagnostic test / diagnostic tool / diagnostic criteria / diagnostic procedure**

👉 Example: The physician ordered a series of diagnostic tests to determine the cause of the patient's symptoms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medical team employed advanced **diagnostic** imaging techniques to identify the precise location of the tumor.
   医疗团队采用先进的诊断影像技术来确定肿瘤的精确位置。

2. Early **diagnostic** screening programs have significantly reduced mortality rates for several types of cancer.
   早期诊断筛查项目已显著降低了几种癌症的死亡率。

3. The technician ran a comprehensive **diagnostic** on the malfunctioning server to isolate the source of the error.
   技术人员对故障服务器进行了全面诊断，以找出错误的根源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diagnostic" as "dia-" (through) + "gnos-" (know) — to know through examination, to identify a problem by looking through the evidence.
把"diagnostic"想成"dia-"（通过）+"gnos-"（知道）——通过检查来了解，通过审视证据来识别问题。
