# 🔺 apex

🔊 /ˈeɪ.peks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The highest point, peak, or tip of something; the culmination or climax of an achievement, process, or development.

**中文：** 顶点；尖端

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- peak（顶峰）
- summit（顶点）
- pinnacle（巅峰）
- zenith（最高点）
- acme（极点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **at the apex of**, **reach the apex**, **the apex of one's career**, **apex predator**, **the very apex**.

> The empire reached the **apex** of its power during the second century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned architect reached the **apex** of her career upon receiving the prestigious international design award.
   这位著名建筑师在获得享有盛誉的国际设计奖时达到了职业巅峰。

2. The eagle soared gracefully toward the **apex** of the mountain before descending into the valley below.
   雄鹰优雅地飞向山的顶端，然后俯冲進入下方的山谷。

3. Historians regard this period as the **apex** of cultural achievement in the entire Renaissance movement.
   历史学家将这一时期视为整个文艺复兴运动中文化成就的顶峰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Picture a triangle: the **apex** is the very top point. The word itself looks like "A-peak-s" — the **A** shape of a triangle pointing to its peak.

形象记忆：想象一个三角形，apex 就是最顶上的那个尖。字母 A 本身就是一个三角形，A 的最上面那个尖就是 apex（顶点）。
