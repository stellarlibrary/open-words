# 🧠 cogitate

🔊 /ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To think deeply or carefully about something; to ponder or meditate
**中文：** v. 仔细考虑；谋划；思考

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ponder（沉思）
- contemplate（深思）
- deliberate（仔细考虑）
- meditate（冥想）
- ruminate（反复思考）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cogitate on / cogitate about / cogitate over a problem**

👉 Example: The philosopher would cogitate on the nature of existence for hours at a time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members needed several weeks to **cogitate** on the proposed amendments before reaching a final decision.
   委员会成员需要几周时间来仔细考虑提议的修正案，然后才能做出最终决定。

2. She preferred to **cogitate** in solitude, finding that quiet environments were most conducive to productive thought.
   她更喜欢独自思考，发现安静的环境最有利于高效的思维。

3. Before presenting his thesis, the scholar spent months **cogitating** over the philosophical implications of his research findings.
   在发表论文之前，这位学者花了数月时间仔细思考其研究成果的哲学含义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cogitate" as "cog-itate" — like the cogs (gears) in your brain turning as you think deeply about something.
把"cogitate"想成"cog（齿轮）-itate"——就像大脑中的齿轮在你深入思考时不停转动。
