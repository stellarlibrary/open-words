# 🌡️ acclimate

🔊 /ˈæklɪmeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To become accustomed to a new climate, environment, or set of conditions; to adapt.
**中文：** 服水土；适应新环境或新条件。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adapt（适应）
- adjust（调整）
- acclimatize（适应气候）
- acculturate（适应文化）
- habituate（使习惯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acclimate to + environment/altitude/conditions/climate**

👉 Example: It took several weeks for the new employees to acclimate to the corporate culture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Mountain climbers must gradually **acclimate** to higher altitudes to avoid potentially fatal altitude sickness.
   登山者必须逐步适应更高的海拔，以避免可能致命的高原反应。

2. International students often need several months to **acclimate** to the academic expectations of a new institution.
   国际学生通常需要几个月时间来适应新学校的学术要求。

3. The tropical species failed to **acclimate** to the temperate region despite extensive conservation efforts.
   尽管进行了大量保护工作，该热带物种仍未能适应温带地区的环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Acclimate" contains "climate" — to acclimate is to get used to a new climate or situation.
acclimate 中包含"climate（气候）"——acclimate 就是适应新的气候或环境。
