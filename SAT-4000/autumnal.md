# 🍂 autumnal

🔊 /ɔːˈtʌmnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or characteristic of autumn; past the prime of life, suggesting maturity or decline.
**中文：** adj. 秋天的；已过中年的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fall-like（似秋的）
- mature（成熟的）
- waning（衰退的）
- mellow（柔和的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **autumnal equinox / autumnal colors / autumnal mood / autumnal years**

👉 Example: The park was resplendent in autumnal colors of gold, amber, and deep crimson.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The landscape was transformed by **autumnal** hues as golden leaves carpeted the winding paths through the ancient forest.
   秋天的色调改变了景观，金色的落叶铺满了穿过古老森林的蜿蜒小路。

2. The poet captured the **autumnal** melancholy of aging, comparing life's later years to fading leaves and shortened days.
   诗人捕捉了衰老的秋日般的忧愁，将人生晚年比作凋零的树叶和缩短的白昼。

3. Scientists study the **autumnal** equinox carefully because it marks the precise moment when day and night are equal.
   科学家们仔细研究秋分，因为它标志着昼夜完全等长的精确时刻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Autumnal" simply adds "-al" to "autumn" — think of the golden, fading beauty of the autumn season of life.
"Autumnal"只是在"autumn"后加上"-al"——想象人生秋季金色而渐逝的美丽。
