# 🎉 gusto

🔊 /ˈɡʌstoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Enjoyment or vigorous enthusiasm in doing something; great relish or zest
**中文：** n. 爱好；由衷的高兴；嗜好；趣味；兴味

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enthusiasm（热情）
- zest（热忱）
- relish（享受）
- fervor（热忱）
- eagerness（渴望）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with gusto / eat with gusto / sing with gusto / perform with gusto**

👉 Example: The children attacked their meals with gusto after spending the entire afternoon playing outdoors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orchestra performed the symphony with remarkable **gusto**, captivating every member of the audience from beginning to end.
   管弦乐队以非凡的热情演奏了这部交响乐，自始至终吸引了每一位观众。

2. She embraced her new research position with genuine **gusto**, determined to make meaningful contributions to the field.
   她以真正的热忱投入到新的研究职位中，决心为该领域做出有意义的贡献。

3. The debate team argued their case with such **gusto** that even the opposing judges acknowledged their passionate delivery.
   辩论队以如此强烈的热情为自己的观点辩护，连对方评委也认可了他们充满激情的表达。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gusto" as related to "gust" — like a gust of wind, gusto is a burst of energy and enthusiasm that sweeps you up.
把"gusto"和"gust"（阵风）联系起来——就像一阵风，gusto是一股席卷你的能量和热情。
