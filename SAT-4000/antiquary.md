# 🏺 antiquary

🔊 /ˈæntɪkweri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A person who studies, collects, or deals in antiquities, ancient artifacts, and historical relics; an expert in old and rare objects.

**中文：** 古文物研究者；古董商人。研究、收藏或买卖古物的人。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- antiquarian（古物研究者）
- collector（收藏家）
- curator（馆长）
- historian（历史学家）
- connoisseur（鉴赏家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** distinguished antiquary, local antiquary, professional antiquary, antiquary of note

**Example:** The **antiquary** examined the vase with great care before confirming its origin as fifth-century Greek.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The celebrated **antiquary** published an authoritative treatise on the bronze weaponry of ancient Mesopotamia.
   这位著名的古文物研究者发表了一篇关于古代美索不达米亚青铜兵器的权威论著。

2. As a seasoned **antiquary**, he could readily distinguish authentic medieval tapestries from modern reproductions.
   作为一位经验丰富的古董商人，他能轻松区分真正的中世纪挂毯和现代仿制品。

3. The **antiquary** traveled extensively through the region, acquiring rare manuscripts that had survived for centuries.
   这位古文物研究者在该地区广泛游历，收购了存世数百年的珍稀手稿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "antiqu-" (ancient) + "-ary" (a person). An antiquary is simply a person of antiquities — someone whose profession revolves around old, rare, and historical objects.

联想："antiqu-"（古老的）+ "-ary"（人）。antiquary 就是与古物打交道的人——以研究和买卖古董为业的人。
