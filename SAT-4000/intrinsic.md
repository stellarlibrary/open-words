# 🔬 intrinsic

🔊 /ɪnˈtrɪnzɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Belonging naturally; inherent and essential, not dependent on external factors
**中文：** adj. 本质的；固有的；内在的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inherent（固有的）
- innate（天生的）
- essential（本质的）
- fundamental（根本的）
- integral（不可分割的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intrinsic value / intrinsic motivation / intrinsic quality / intrinsic part of**

👉 Example: The intrinsic value of education extends far beyond mere career advancement or financial gain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Curiosity is an **intrinsic** quality of human nature that drives scientific inquiry and technological innovation.
   好奇心是人类本性中固有的品质，推动着科学探究和技术创新。

2. The professor argued that **intrinsic** motivation produces far more sustainable results than external rewards alone.
   教授认为，内在动机比单纯的外部奖励能产生更加持久的效果。

3. Gold possesses **intrinsic** properties that make it uniquely resistant to corrosion and highly valuable in electronics.
   黄金具有固有的特性，使其能够独特地抵抗腐蚀，并在电子领域具有很高的价值。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intrinsic" as "interior" + "inside" — qualities that come from the inside, built into the very nature of something.
把"intrinsic"想成"interior（内部的）"——来自内部的品质，内建于事物的本质之中。
