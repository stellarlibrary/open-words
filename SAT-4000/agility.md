# ⚡ agility

🔊 /əˈdʒɪl.ə.ti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ability to move quickly and easily; quickness of mind or body.
**中文：** 敏捷；灵活；机敏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nimbleness（灵活）
- dexterity（灵巧）
- swiftness（迅速）
- quickness（敏捷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mental agility, physical agility, agility training, with agility**
👉 Example: The athlete's remarkable agility allowed her to outperform all competitors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dancer's extraordinary **agility** enabled her to perform the most demanding choreography with grace.
   这位舞者非凡的敏捷使她能够优雅地完成最高难度的编舞。

2. Mental **agility** is considered one of the most valuable assets in today's competitive professional environment.
   思维敏捷被认为是当今竞争激烈的职业环境中最宝贵的素质之一。

3. The rescue team demonstrated impressive **agility** while navigating the treacherous terrain during the mission.
   救援队在任务中穿越危险地形时展现了令人印象深刻的灵活性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Agility" shares its root with "agile" — picture an acrobat whose defining quality is agility in every movement.
"Agility"与"agile"同根——想象一位杂技演员，每个动作都展现出敏捷的特质。
