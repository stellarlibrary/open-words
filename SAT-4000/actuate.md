# ⚙️ actuate

🔊 /ˈæk.tʃu.eɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause a machine or device to operate; to motivate or drive someone to act.
**中文：** 开动（机器等）；促使，驱使；激励

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- activate（激活）
- motivate（激励）
- propel（推动）
- trigger（触发）
- impel（驱使）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **actuate a mechanism/device**, **actuated by (desire/greed/ambition)**

👉 Example: The engineer designed a sensor to actuate the emergency shutdown system automatically.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A small electric motor is used to **actuate** the valve that controls water flow in the system.
   一个小型电动机被用来驱动控制系统中水流的阀门。

2. She was **actuated** by a genuine desire to improve public education for underprivileged communities.
   她被改善弱势群体公共教育的真诚愿望所驱使。

3. The pressure sensor will automatically **actuate** the alarm if it detects any hazardous gas leaks.
   如果检测到任何危险气体泄漏，压力传感器将自动触发警报。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ACT-uate" — to make something ACT or start working; to put into ACTION.
"ACT-uate"——让某物"act（行动）"起来，使其开始运作；联想"把……付诸action（行动）"。
