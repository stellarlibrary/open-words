# 🌄 landscape

🔊 /ˈlændskeɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The visible features of an area of land; a painting or photograph depicting natural scenery. (v.) To improve the aesthetic appearance of an area by modifying its terrain and planting vegetation
**中文：** n. 风景；风景画；景色 v. 做景观美化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scenery（风景）
- terrain（地形）
- panorama（全景）
- vista（远景）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **landscape painting / political landscape / landscape architecture / transform the landscape**

👉 Example: The political landscape shifted dramatically after the unexpected results of the general election.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The breathtaking **landscape** of the mountain valley attracted thousands of hikers and nature photographers every autumn season.
   山谷令人叹为观止的风景每年秋天都吸引了数千名徒步旅行者和自然摄影师。

2. The digital revolution has fundamentally transformed the **landscape** of global commerce and interpersonal communication alike.
   数字革命从根本上改变了全球商业和人际交流的格局。

3. The city council hired specialists to **landscape** the neglected waterfront area into an inviting public park and recreation space.
   市议会聘请专家将被忽视的滨水区域美化为一个宜人的公共公园和休闲空间。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "land" + "scape" (from shape) — the shape of the land, what you see when you look across a stretch of terrain.
把"landscape"想成"land（土地）"和"scape（形状）"——土地的形态，你放眼望去所见的地形景色。
