# 📷 tripod

🔊 /ˈtraɪpɒd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A three-legged stand or support, especially one used to hold a camera or other apparatus steady
**中文：** n. 三脚架；三脚支撑物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stand（支架）
- mount（固定支架）
- trivet（三脚架）
- support（支撑物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **set up a tripod / mount on a tripod / camera tripod / tripod stand**

👉 Example: The photographer carefully positioned her camera on a tripod to capture the long-exposure night sky image.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The astronomer mounted the telescope on a sturdy **tripod** to ensure stable observation of the lunar eclipse that evening.
   天文学家将望远镜固定在一个坚固的三脚架上，以确保当晚对月食的稳定观测。

2. Archaeological excavations revealed an ancient bronze **tripod** that was likely used in ceremonial rituals during the classical period.
   考古发掘发现了一个古代青铜三脚架，可能在古典时期用于ceremonial仪式活动。

3. The surveyor placed the leveling instrument on a **tripod** before beginning precise measurements of the construction site terrain.
   测量员在开始对施工现场地形进行精确测量之前，将水准仪放在了三脚架上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tri-pod" — "tri" means three and "pod" means foot, so a tripod literally has three feet to stand on.
把"tripod"想成"tri（三）+ pod（脚）"——三只脚的支架，就像三脚架一样稳稳站立。
