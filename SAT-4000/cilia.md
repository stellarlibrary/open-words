# 🔬 cilia

🔊 /ˈsɪliə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (plural) Short, hair-like structures on the surface of cells that move in wave-like motions; also refers to eyelashes
**中文：** n. 纤毛；睫毛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flagella（鞭毛）
- filaments（细丝）
- hair-like projections（毛状突起）
- lashes（睫毛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **motile cilia / respiratory cilia / cilia and flagella / ciliary movement**

👉 Example: The respiratory cilia help sweep mucus and foreign particles out of the airways.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **cilia** lining the respiratory tract perform a vital function by removing inhaled particles and pathogens.
   呼吸道内壁的纤毛通过清除吸入的颗粒和病原体发挥着至关重要的功能。

2. Under the microscope, students observed how **cilia** beat in coordinated rhythmic waves to propel the organism forward.
   在显微镜下，学生们观察到纤毛如何以协调的节律性波动推动生物体前进。

3. Damage to the **cilia** of lung epithelial cells is a well-documented consequence of prolonged exposure to cigarette smoke.
   肺上皮细胞纤毛的损伤是长期接触香烟烟雾的一个有据可查的后果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cilia" sounding like "silly hair" — tiny silly hairs waving back and forth on the surface of cells.
"cilia"听起来像"silly hair"（傻傻的毛发）——细胞表面来回摆动的微小毛发。
