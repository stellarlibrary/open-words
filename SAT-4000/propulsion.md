# 🚀 propulsion

🔊 /prəˈpʌlʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The force that drives something forward; the action or process of pushing or driving forward
**中文：** n. 推进力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thrust（推力）
- drive（驱动力）
- impetus（推动力）
- momentum（动力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jet propulsion / rocket propulsion / propulsion system / means of propulsion**

👉 Example: The spacecraft relied on ion propulsion to travel efficiently across vast interstellar distances.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers developed a revolutionary **propulsion** system that significantly reduced fuel consumption in commercial aircraft.
   工程师们开发了一种革命性的推进系统，大大降低了商用飞机的燃油消耗。

2. The submarine's nuclear **propulsion** enabled it to remain submerged for extended periods without surfacing for refueling.
   潜艇的核推进装置使其能够长时间潜航而无需浮出水面补充燃料。

3. Researchers are investigating solar sail **propulsion** as a viable method for deep-space exploration missions beyond our solar system.
   研究人员正在研究太阳帆推进技术，作为超越太阳系的深空探测任务的可行方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-PULSE-ion" — a powerful PULSE of energy that pushes something forward, providing the force of propulsion.
把"propulsion"想成"pro-PULSE-ion"——一股强大的脉冲能量向前推动物体，提供推进力。
