# 🎯 upshot

🔊 /ˈʌpʃɒt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The final result or outcome of a situation; the main point or gist of a matter
**中文：** n. 结果，结局；要点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outcome（结果）
- result（结局）
- consequence（后果）
- culmination（终结）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the upshot of / the upshot is that / the upshot was**

👉 Example: The upshot of the negotiations was a mutually beneficial trade agreement between the two nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **upshot** of the lengthy committee deliberations was a revised policy that addressed most stakeholder concerns.
   冗长的委员会审议的最终结果是一项修订后的政策，回应了大多数利益相关者的关切。

2. After months of market analysis, the **upshot** was that the company decided to postpone its international expansion.
   经过数月的市场分析，最终结论是公司决定推迟其国际扩张计划。

3. The **upshot** of the scientific investigation confirmed that environmental factors played a decisive role in the decline.
   科学调查的结果证实，环境因素在这一衰退中起了决定性作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "up-shot" as the final shot that goes "up" — the last shot in a game that determines the outcome.
把"upshot"想成最后"向上"射出的一箭——比赛中决定胜负的最后一击，即最终结果。
