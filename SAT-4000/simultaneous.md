# ⏱️ simultaneous

🔊 /ˌsaɪməlˈteɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring, operating, or done at the same time. (n.) A simultaneous interpreter
**中文：** adj. 同时的，同时发生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concurrent（同时发生的）
- synchronous（同步的）
- coincident（同时的）
- parallel（并行的）
- contemporaneous（同时期的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **simultaneous translation / simultaneous events / simultaneous equations / simultaneous release / occur simultaneously**

👉 Example: The conference offered simultaneous translation in six languages to accommodate its international audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **simultaneous** occurrence of the earthquake and the tsunami created a catastrophic disaster across the coastal region.
   地震和海啸的同时发生在沿海地区造成了灾难性的破坏。

2. The broadcasting network arranged a **simultaneous** release of the documentary across twelve countries on the same evening.
   广播网络安排了这部纪录片在同一晚于十二个国家同步发行。

3. Solving three **simultaneous** equations required the students to apply advanced algebraic techniques and careful logical reasoning.
   求解三个联立方程要求学生运用高级代数技巧和严谨的逻辑推理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "simultaneous" as "simul (same) + taneous (time)" — from Latin "simul" meaning "at the same time," things happening simultaneously occur together.
把"simultaneous"想成"simul（相同）+ taneous（时间）"——源自拉丁语"simul"意为"同时"，同时发生的事情一起出现。
