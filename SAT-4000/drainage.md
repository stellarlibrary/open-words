# 🚰 drainage

🔊 /ˈdreɪnɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The system or process by which water or other liquids are drained away from an area
**中文：** n. 排水；排水系统；排水装置

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sewerage（排污系统）
- runoff（径流）
- outflow（流出）
- discharge（排放）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **drainage system / drainage ditch / poor drainage / drainage basin**

👉 Example: The city invested heavily in improving its drainage infrastructure to prevent flooding.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural engineers designed an advanced **drainage** system to prevent waterlogging and protect the fertile cropland.
   农业工程师设计了一套先进的排水系统，以防止内涝并保护肥沃的农田。

2. Poor **drainage** in the urban district contributed to severe flooding that displaced hundreds of residents last summer.
   城区排水不畅导致了去年夏天的严重洪灾，使数百名居民流离失所。

3. The geological survey examined the natural **drainage** patterns of the watershed before approving the new development project.
   地质调查在批准新开发项目之前检查了流域的自然排水模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "drainage" as "drain + age" — the age-old practice of draining water away to keep land dry and usable.
把"drainage"想成"drain + age"——将水排走以保持土地干燥和可用的古老做法。
