# 👀 ogle

🔊 /ˈoʊɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To stare at someone in a way that shows strong interest or desire; to look at flirtatiously. (n.) A flirtatious or amorous glance
**中文：** v. 向…抛媚眼；色迷迷地注视 n. 媚眼；色迷迷的目光

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stare（凝视）
- gawk（呆呆地看）
- leer（色迷迷地看）
- gaze（注视）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ogle at someone / ogle someone / catch someone ogling / stop ogling**

👉 Example: The manager warned the employee to stop ogling at colleagues, as it constituted inappropriate workplace behavior.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Several guests were caught **ogling** at the priceless art collection displayed throughout the grand ballroom of the estate.
   几位客人被发现目不转睛地盯着庄园大宴会厅里展出的无价艺术收藏品。

2. The critic accused the film director of encouraging audiences to **ogle** rather than engage thoughtfully with the narrative content.
   评论家指责这位电影导演鼓励观众色迷迷地观看，而非深入思考叙事内容。

3. She noticed a stranger **ogling** at her from across the crowded room and immediately felt uncomfortable and self-conscious.
   她注意到一个陌生人从拥挤的房间对面色迷迷地盯着她，立刻感到不安和局促。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ogle" as "oh + google eyes" — when you ogle someone, your eyes go wide like cartoon googly eyes staring with fascination.
把"ogle"想成"oh + google eyes"——当你ogle某人时，你的眼睛像卡通的大眼睛一样瞪得大大的，充满迷恋地盯着看。
