# 🎻 virtuoso

🔊 /ˌvɜːrtʃuˈoʊsoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person highly skilled in music or another artistic pursuit; a master of technique. (adj.) Displaying exceptional skill or brilliance
**中文：** n. 艺术大师；音乐名手；鉴赏家 adj. 技艺精湛的；大师级的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- maestro（大师）
- prodigy（天才）
- master（名家）
- expert（专家）
- genius（天才）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **virtuoso performance / virtuoso pianist / virtuoso technique / virtuoso display**

👉 Example: The audience was captivated by the pianist's virtuoso performance of the demanding concerto.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young **virtuoso** astonished the audience with a flawless interpretation of one of the most technically demanding violin concertos.
   这位年轻的音乐大师以对一部技术要求最高的小提琴协奏曲的完美演绎令观众惊叹不已。

2. Critics hailed her **virtuoso** performance as a landmark achievement that redefined contemporary standards of operatic excellence.
   评论家称赞她大师级的表演是一项里程碑式的成就，重新定义了当代歌剧卓越的标准。

3. His **virtuoso** command of multiple programming languages enabled him to architect elegant solutions for extraordinarily complex problems.
   他对多种编程语言的精湛掌握使他能够为极其复杂的问题设计优雅的解决方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virtuoso" as "virtue + oso" — someone with so much virtue (skill) that it overflows; the "-oso" ending sounds grand, like a solo performer.
把"virtuoso"想成"virtue + oso"——一个才华横溢到溢出来的人；"-oso"的结尾听起来很宏大，就像独奏表演者一样。
