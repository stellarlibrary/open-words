# 🗣️ fluent

🔊 /ˈfluːənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to express oneself easily and articulately; flowing smoothly and effortlessly
**中文：** adj. 流利的；流畅的；善辩的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- articulate（口齿伶俐的）
- eloquent（雄辩的）
- smooth（流畅的）
- effortless（毫不费力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fluent in a language / fluent speaker / fluent English / fluent conversation**

👉 Example: After years of practice, she became fluent in three languages and could switch between them seamlessly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat was **fluent** in five languages, which proved invaluable during complex multilateral negotiations at the summit.
   这位外交官**精通**五种语言，这在峰会上复杂的多边谈判中被证明极为宝贵。

2. Her **fluent** presentation of the research findings impressed the entire committee and secured additional funding for the project.
   她对研究成果**流畅**的陈述给整个委员会留下了深刻印象，并为项目争取到了额外资金。

3. To become truly **fluent** in academic writing, students must practice constructing well-organized arguments over many semesters.
   要真正**熟练**掌握学术写作，学生必须经过多个学期练习构建条理清晰的论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fluent" as related to "fluid" — when you speak fluently, your words flow like water, smooth and uninterrupted.
把"fluent"和"fluid（流体）"联系起来——当你说话流利时，你的话语像水一样流畅、不间断。
