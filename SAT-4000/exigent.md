# 🔥 exigent

🔊 /ˈeksɪdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Requiring immediate attention or action; urgent; making excessive demands; exacting
**中文：** adj. 迫切的；紧急的；苛求的；要求很高的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- urgent（紧急的）
- pressing（迫切的）
- demanding（苛求的）
- critical（关键的）
- imperative（必要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exigent circumstances / exigent demands / exigent situation / exigent needs**

👉 Example: The exigent circumstances forced the committee to convene an emergency session immediately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **exigent** demands of the humanitarian crisis required governments to mobilize resources without any further delay.
   人道主义危机的紧迫要求迫使各国政府毫不拖延地调动资源。

2. Under **exigent** circumstances, law enforcement officers may conduct searches without obtaining a warrant beforehand.
   在紧急情况下，执法人员可以在未事先获得搜查令的情况下进行搜查。

3. The professor's **exigent** standards compelled students to revise their research papers multiple times before final submission.
   教授苛刻的标准迫使学生在最终提交前多次修改他们的研究论文。

---

Think of "exigent" as "exit-gent" — a gentleman rushing to the exit because the situation is so urgent he must act immediately.
把"exigent"想成"exit-gent"——一位绅士急忙冲向出口，因为情况太紧急，他必须立刻行动。
