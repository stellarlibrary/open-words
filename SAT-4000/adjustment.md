# ⚙️ adjustment

🔊 /əˈdʒʌstmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of making changes or alterations to achieve a desired result; a modification or adaptation.
**中文：** 调整，调节；调节器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modification（修改）
- alteration（变更）
- adaptation（适应）
- calibration（校准）
- correction（修正）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make an adjustment**, **adjustment to/in something**, **minor/major adjustment**

👉 Example: A slight adjustment to the schedule resolved the conflict for all participants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineers made a critical **adjustment** to the satellite's trajectory before it entered orbit.
   工程师们在卫星进入轨道前对其轨迹进行了关键的调整。

2. Cultural **adjustment** can be challenging for international students during their first semester abroad.
   文化适应对留学生来说在第一学期可能颇具挑战性。

3. The monetary policy committee announced a significant **adjustment** to interest rates to curb inflation.
   货币政策委员会宣布对利率进行重大调整以抑制通货膨胀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adjust" + "-ment" — the result of adjusting, like turning a dial to get things just right.
联想"adjust"（调整）+"-ment"（名词后缀）——调整的结果，就像转动旋钮使一切恰到好处。
