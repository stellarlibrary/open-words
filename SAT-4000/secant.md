# 📐 secant

🔊 /ˈsiːkænt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Cutting or intersecting. (n.) A straight line that intersects a curve at two or more points; the trigonometric function equal to the reciprocal of the cosine
**中文：** n. 割线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intersecting line（交叉线）
- cutting line（割线）
- transversal（截线）
- chord（弦）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **secant line / secant function / secant of a circle / secant method**

👉 Example: In calculus, the slope of a secant line through two points on a curve approximates the derivative.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor demonstrated how the **secant** line through two points on a parabola approaches the tangent as the points converge.
   教授演示了抛物线上两点之间的割线如何在两点趋近时逼近切线。

2. In trigonometry, the **secant** function is defined as the reciprocal of cosine and is essential for solving advanced equations.
   在三角学中，正割函数被定义为余弦的倒数，对于求解高级方程至关重要。

3. Engineers applied the **secant** method to find numerical solutions for equations that could not be solved through purely analytical means.
   工程师们应用割线法来求解无法通过纯分析方法解决的方程的数值解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "secant" as "sec-ant" — imagine an ant cutting (sectioning) across a circle, creating a line that intersects the curve at two points.
把"secant"想成"sec（切割）-ant（蚂蚁）"——想象一只蚂蚁切过一个圆，形成一条在两点处与曲线相交的割线。
