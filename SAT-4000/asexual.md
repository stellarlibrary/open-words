# 🧬 asexual

🔊 /eɪˈsɛkʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Without sexual feelings or associations; relating to reproduction that does not involve the fusion of gametes
**中文：** 无性的；无性生殖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nonsexual（非性的）
- agamic（无性生殖的）
- vegetative（营养繁殖的）
- sexless（无性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **asexual reproduction / asexual organism / asexual identity**

👉 Example: Many plants rely on asexual reproduction to propagate efficiently in stable environments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Certain species of lizards are capable of **asexual** reproduction, producing offspring without requiring a mate.
   某些蜥蜴物种能够进行无性生殖，无需配偶即可产生后代。

2. The biology textbook devoted an entire chapter to explaining **asexual** methods of cellular division in organisms.
   这本生物学教科书用整整一章来解释有机体的无性细胞分裂方法。

3. Scientists discovered that the **asexual** propagation of coral reefs plays a vital role in marine ecosystem recovery.
   科学家发现珊瑚礁的无性繁殖在海洋生态系统恢复中起着至关重要的作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
The prefix "a-" means "without," so "asexual" literally means "without sex" — no sexual component involved.
前缀"a-"表示"没有"，所以"asexual"字面意思是"没有性的"——不涉及性的成分。
