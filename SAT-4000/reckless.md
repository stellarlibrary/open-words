# 🏎️ reckless

🔊 /ˈrɛkləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Acting without thinking about the consequences; heedless of danger; rash and irresponsible
**中文：** adj. 鲁莽的；不计后果的；粗心大意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rash（轻率的）
- careless（粗心的）
- imprudent（不谨慎的）
- heedless（不留心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reckless driving / reckless behavior / reckless abandon / reckless disregard**

👉 Example: The company's reckless disregard for environmental regulations resulted in severe legal penalties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that **reckless** financial decisions by senior executives had led to the corporation's dramatic collapse.
   调查显示，高管们鲁莽的财务决策导致了公司的急剧崩溃。

2. Critics condemned the government's **reckless** spending policies, warning that mounting debt would burden future generations significantly.
   批评者谴责政府不计后果的支出政策，警告不断增加的债务将严重拖累后代。

3. The driver was charged with **reckless** endangerment after weaving through traffic at dangerously excessive speeds on the highway.
   这名司机在高速公路上以危险的超高速穿梭于车流中，被指控鲁莽危害公共安全。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reck-less" as "reck (care) + less (without)" — literally without care or concern, acting as if consequences do not exist.
把"reckless"拆成"reck（顾虑）+ less（没有）"——字面意思就是没有顾虑，行事不计后果。
