# 🎉 merriment

🔊 /ˈmɛrɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Laughter, fun, and enjoyment; the state of being cheerful and full of gaiety; festive celebration and amusement.
**中文：** n. 欢乐；嬉戏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gaiety（欢乐）
- jollity（欢闹）
- mirth（欢笑）
- festivity（庆祝活动）
- revelry（狂欢）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **general merriment / air of merriment / sounds of merriment / holiday merriment**

👉 Example: The sounds of merriment drifted through the halls during the annual holiday celebration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The wedding reception was filled with **merriment** as guests danced and raised their glasses to toast the newlywed couple.
   婚礼宴会上充满了欢乐，宾客们翩翩起舞并举杯祝贺新婚夫妇。

2. Despite the somber economic forecast, the townspeople embraced the festival with genuine **merriment** and communal spirit.
   尽管经济前景黯淡，镇上的人们仍以真诚的欢乐和团结精神参加了节日庆典。

3. The professor's unexpected humor brought a welcome burst of **merriment** to the otherwise formal academic conference.
   教授出人意料的幽默给原本正式的学术会议带来了一阵令人愉快的欢笑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "merry-ment" as "the state of being merry" — just like "enjoyment" is the state of enjoying, merriment is the state of being merry and joyful.
把"merriment"想成"merry（快乐的）的状态"——就像"enjoyment"是享受的状态，merriment就是快乐和喜悦的状态。
