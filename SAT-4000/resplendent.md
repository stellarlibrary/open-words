# ✨ resplendent

🔊 /rɪˈsplɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Shining brilliantly; dazzling in appearance; impressively beautiful or magnificent
**中文：** adj. 光辉的；华丽的；灿烂的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- splendid（壮丽的）
- dazzling（耀眼的）
- magnificent（华丽的）
- radiant（光芒四射的）
- glorious（辉煌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resplendent in / resplendent beauty / resplendent colors / resplendent attire / resplendent glory**

👉 Example: The queen appeared resplendent in her ceremonial robes adorned with jewels and gold thread.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cathedral's interior was **resplendent** with gilded mosaics and stained glass windows that illuminated the sacred space.
   大教堂内部金色马赛克和彩色玻璃窗交相辉映，照亮了这片神圣的空间。

2. The autumn forest appeared **resplendent** in shades of crimson, amber, and gold as the afternoon sunlight filtered through.
   秋天的森林在午后阳光的照射下呈现出深红、琥珀和金色的华丽色调。

3. The ambassador arrived at the gala **resplendent** in formal diplomatic attire that reflected centuries of distinguished tradition.
   大使身着体现数百年尊贵传统的正式外交礼服，光彩照人地出席了晚会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-SPLEND-ent" — it contains "splend" like "splendid," so resplendent means something so splendid it keeps shining again and again.
把"resplendent"拆开看——里面有"splend"，和"splendid（壮丽的）"同源，所以resplendent就是光辉灿烂到不断闪耀的意思。
