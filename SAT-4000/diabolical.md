# 😈 diabolical

🔊 /ˌdaɪəˈbɒlɪkl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Belonging to or characteristic of the devil; extremely wicked, cruel, or fiendishly clever
**中文：** adj. 恶魔的；极其邪恶的；残忍的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fiendish（恶魔般的）
- wicked（邪恶的）
- demonic（恶魔的）
- nefarious（邪恶的）
- sinister（阴险的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diabolical plan / diabolical scheme / diabolical nature / diabolical cunning**

👉 Example: The detective uncovered a diabolical plot to sabotage the entire city's water supply.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The villain in the novel devised a **diabolical** plot to seize control of the government through blackmail and deception.
   小说中的反派设计了一个恶魔般的阴谋，企图通过勒索和欺骗夺取政府控制权。

2. Historians described the regime's treatment of political prisoners as **diabolical** in its systematic cruelty and disregard for life.
   历史学家将该政权对待政治犯的方式描述为极其邪恶，系统性地残忍且漠视生命。

3. The puzzle was so **diabolical** in its complexity that even the most experienced mathematicians struggled to find a solution.
   这道谜题在复杂性上如此刁钻，以至于即使最有经验的数学家也难以找到答案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diabolical" as containing "diablo" (Spanish for "devil") — something diabolical is literally devilish in nature.
把"diabolical"想成包含"diablo（西班牙语中的魔鬼）"——diabolical的事物本质上就是恶魔般的。
