# 🔧 implement

🔊 /ˈɪmplɪment/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To put into effect; to carry out or execute a plan, policy, or decision. (n.) A tool, utensil, or instrument used for a particular purpose
**中文：** v. 实施，执行；实现，使生效 n. 工具，器具；手段

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- execute（执行）
- enforce（实施）
- carry out（贯彻）
- instrument（工具）
- utensil（器具）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **implement a policy / implement a plan / implement changes / farming implements**

👉 Example: The school board voted unanimously to implement the new curriculum starting next semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government plans to **implement** sweeping economic reforms designed to reduce inflation and stimulate long-term industrial growth.
   政府计划实施全面的经济改革，旨在降低通胀并刺激长期工业增长。

2. Archaeological excavations at the site uncovered primitive stone **implements** that dated back approximately twelve thousand years.
   该遗址的考古发掘出土了距今约一万两千年的原始石器工具。

3. The committee recommended that the university **implement** stricter admission standards to maintain its academic reputation and rigor.
   委员会建议大学实施更严格的录取标准，以维护其学术声誉和严谨性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "IMPLEMENT" as "I'M-PLE-MEANT" — I'm plenty meant to do this, so I will carry it out and put it into action.
把"implement"想成"I'm meant（我注定要做）"——我注定要执行这件事，所以我会付诸行动。
