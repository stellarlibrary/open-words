# 🏜️ dearth

🔊 /dɜːrθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A scarcity or lack of something; an inadequate supply or amount
**中文：** n. 缺乏；不足；饥馑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scarcity（稀缺）
- shortage（短缺）
- deficiency（不足）
- paucity（少量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dearth of evidence / dearth of information / dearth of talent / dearth of resources**

👉 Example: The dearth of qualified candidates forced the company to extend its recruitment timeline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **dearth** of affordable housing in major urban centers has become a pressing social issue for policymakers.
   主要城市中心经济适用房的缺乏已成为政策制定者面临的紧迫社会问题。

2. Researchers lamented the **dearth** of longitudinal studies available on the long-term effects of the new medication.
   研究人员对缺乏关于新药长期效果的纵向研究感到遗憾。

3. A **dearth** of natural resources compelled the island nation to develop innovative strategies for sustainable development.
   自然资源的匮乏迫使这个岛国制定可持续发展的创新策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dearth" as "dear + th" — when something is dear (expensive), it usually means there is a dearth (shortage) of it.
把"dearth"想成"dear（昂贵的）+ th"——当某物昂贵时，通常意味着它很稀缺。
