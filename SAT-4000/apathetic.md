# 😶 apathetic

🔊 /ˌæp.əˈθet.ɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Showing or feeling no interest, enthusiasm, or concern; indifferent and unresponsive to matters that typically provoke emotion.

**中文：** 冷漠的；无动于衷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- indifferent（冷漠的）
- unconcerned（不关心的）
- uninterested（不感兴趣的）
- listless（无精打采的）
- impassive（无表情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **apathetic about/toward** (something), **apathetic response**, **apathetic voters**, **remain apathetic**, **seemingly apathetic**.

> Many young citizens appear **apathetic** toward local political affairs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board criticized the **apathetic** response of the public to the environmental crisis.
   董事会批评了公众对环境危机的冷漠反应。

2. Students became increasingly **apathetic** toward their studies after months of monotonous instruction.
   经过数月单调的教学后，学生们对学业变得越来越冷漠。

3. An **apathetic** electorate poses a significant threat to the foundations of democratic governance.
   冷漠的选民对民主治理的基础构成了重大威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Break it down: "a-" (without) + "pathetic" (from Greek *pathos*, meaning feeling). **Apathetic** literally means "without feeling."

拆解记忆：a-（没有）+ pathetic（来自希腊语 pathos，意为"感情"）。apathetic 字面意思就是"没有感情的"，即冷漠的。
