# 🧹 cathartic

🔊 /kəˈθɑːrtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; purgative. (n.) A medicine that causes evacuation of the bowels; a laxative
**中文：** adj. 宣泄情感的；导泻的；通便的 n. 泻药

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- purgative（泻药；净化的）
- cleansing（净化的）
- therapeutic（治疗的）
- liberating（解放的）
- laxative（泻药）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cathartic experience / cathartic effect / emotionally cathartic / cathartic release**

👉 Example: Writing in her journal each evening proved to be a deeply cathartic experience for her.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many patients found that discussing their traumatic experiences in group therapy sessions was profoundly **cathartic** and healing.
   许多患者发现，在团体治疗中讨论创伤经历具有深刻的情感宣泄和治愈作用。

2. The playwright crafted a **cathartic** final act that allowed audiences to confront their deepest fears through the protagonist's journey.
   剧作家精心创作了一个宣泄性的最后一幕，让观众通过主人公的旅程直面内心最深的恐惧。

3. In traditional medicine, physicians frequently prescribed a **cathartic** to purge the body of what they believed were harmful toxins.
   在传统医学中，医生经常开泻药来清除他们认为体内的有害毒素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cathartic" as "cat-heart-ic" — imagine pouring your heart out like a crying cat, releasing all pent-up emotions for a thorough cleansing.
把"cathartic"想成"cat-heart-ic"——想象像一只哭泣的猫一样倾吐心声，释放所有压抑的情感，进行彻底的净化。
