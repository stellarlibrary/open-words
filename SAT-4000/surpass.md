# 🏆 surpass

🔊 /sərˈpæs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To exceed or go beyond in degree, quality, or achievement; to be greater or better than
**中文：** v. 超过，超越；胜过

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exceed（超过）
- outstrip（超越）
- transcend（超出）
- eclipse（使黯然失色）
- outdo（胜过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surpass expectations / surpass a record / surpass all others / surpass in quality**

👉 Example: The results of the experiment surpassed all expectations set by the research team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young violinist's remarkable talent was widely acknowledged to **surpass** that of many seasoned professionals who had performed for decades.
   这位年轻小提琴家的非凡才华被广泛认为超越了许多演奏了数十年的资深专业人士。

2. Annual revenue for the technology firm managed to **surpass** the projected targets by a substantial margin for the third consecutive quarter.
   该科技公司的年收入连续第三个季度大幅超过了预期目标。

3. The beauty of the alpine landscape **surpassed** anything the travelers had previously encountered during their extensive journeys through Europe.
   这片高山风景的美丽超越了旅行者在他们漫长的欧洲之旅中所见过的一切。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surpass" as "sur (over) + pass" — to pass over and beyond someone else, leaving them behind as you advance further.
把"surpass"想成"sur（超过）+ pass（通过）"——从别人上方通过并超越，在你继续前进时将他们甩在身后。
