# ⚖️ probate

🔊 /ˈproʊbeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The legal process of validating a will; a verified copy of a will. (v.) To establish the validity of a will through legal proceedings
**中文：** n. 遗嘱；遗嘱检验 v. 检验遗嘱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- validation（验证）
- authentication（认证）
- verification（核实）
- certification（证明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **probate court / probate process / grant of probate / go through probate**

👉 Example: The estate must go through probate before any assets can be distributed to the heirs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The family hired an experienced attorney to guide them through the **probate** process after their grandmother passed away unexpectedly.
   祖母意外去世后，家人聘请了一位经验丰富的律师指导他们完成遗嘱检验程序。

2. The **probate** court determined that the handwritten will was legally valid and should be enforced according to its stated terms.
   遗嘱检验法院裁定该手写遗嘱在法律上有效，应按其所述条款执行。

3. Several months were required to **probate** the complex estate, which included properties in multiple states and overseas accounts.
   对这笔复杂遗产进行遗嘱检验花了数月时间，因其包括多个州的房产和海外账户。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "probate" as "probe-it" — you must probe and examine a will to prove it is authentic before distributing the estate.
把"probate"想成"probe-it"——你必须探查和审验遗嘱，证明其真实性后才能分配遗产。
