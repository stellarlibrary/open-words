# ⚔️ apostate

🔊 /əˈpɒsteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adj. 变节的 n. 脱党者；变节者*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Abandoning a formerly held belief, cause, or faith; (n.) a person who renounces a religious or political belief or principle.

**中文：** （形容词）变节的，背叛信仰的；（名词）叛教者，脱党者，变节者。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- renegade 叛徒
- defector 背叛者
- turncoat 变节者
- dissident 持不同政见者
- heretic 异端者

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** apostate leader, apostate member, branded as an apostate, become an apostate

**Example:** The term "apostate" often carries a strongly negative connotation, suggesting betrayal of a sacred or deeply held commitment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **apostate** general was condemned by the regime for his public defection to the opposition.
   该**变节的**将军因公开倒向反对派而受到政权的谴责。

2. Throughout history, many **apostates** have faced severe punishment for abandoning the dominant faith.
   历史上，许多**叛教者**因放弃主流信仰而遭受严厉惩罚。

3. She was denounced as an **apostate** after she publicly criticized the organization's core principles.
   她在公开批评该组织的核心原则后被斥为**变节者**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "apo-" (away from) + "state" (standing/position) — an apostate is someone who stands away from their former position or belief.

联想记忆：apo-（远离）+ state（立场）——apostate 就是远离原来立场的人，即"变节者"。
