# 🌐 omnipresent

🔊 /ˌɑːmnɪˈprezənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Present everywhere at the same time; widespread and constantly encountered
**中文：** adj. 无所不在的；普遍存在的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ubiquitous（无处不在的）
- pervasive（普遍的）
- ever-present（始终存在的）
- universal（普遍的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **omnipresent threat / omnipresent influence / omnipresent surveillance / seemingly omnipresent / omnipresent in modern life**

👉 Example: The omnipresent security cameras in the city center made residents feel both protected and watched.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Social media has become an **omnipresent** force in contemporary society, shaping opinions and influencing behavior across all demographics.
   社交媒体已成为当代社会中无所不在的力量，塑造着各个群体的观点并影响着行为。

2. The **omnipresent** hum of machinery in the factory served as a constant reminder of the relentless pace of production.
   工厂里无所不在的机器嗡嗡声不断提醒人们生产的不懈节奏。

3. Scholars have noted that the theme of mortality is **omnipresent** throughout the collected works of the renowned poet.
   学者们注意到，死亡的主题贯穿这位著名诗人的全部作品，无处不在。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "omni-present" as "omni" (all) + "present" (here) — present in all places at once, like air surrounding you everywhere.
把"omnipresent"想成"omni（所有）"加"present（存在的）"——同时存在于所有地方，就像空气包围着你一样。
