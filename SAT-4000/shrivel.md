# 🍂 shrivel

🔊 /ˈʃrɪvəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To wrinkle and contract, especially due to loss of moisture; to wither; to become reduced or diminished
**中文：** v. 起皱纹；失效

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wither（枯萎）
- wilt（凋谢）
- shrink（收缩）
- desiccate（干枯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shrivel up / shrivel in the heat / shrivel and die / shrivel with age**

👉 Example: The plants shriveled in the relentless summer heat after weeks without any rainfall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Without adequate irrigation, the crops began to **shrivel** under the scorching sun, threatening the entire harvest season.
   由于缺乏足够的灌溉，庄稼在烈日下开始枯萎，威胁到整个收获季节。

2. Public enthusiasm for the reform initiative **shriveled** as citizens grew disillusioned with the government's broken promises.
   随着公民对政府食言日益失望，公众对改革倡议的热情逐渐消退。

3. The leather manuscript had **shriveled** over centuries of exposure to fluctuating temperature and humidity conditions.
   这份皮革手稿经过数百年温度和湿度波动的侵蚀，已经皱缩变形。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shrivel" as "shrink + wrinkle" combined — something dries out, shrinks down, and wrinkles up all at once.
把"shrivel"想成"shrink（缩小）+ wrinkle（起皱）"的结合——某物干枯、缩小、起皱同时发生。
