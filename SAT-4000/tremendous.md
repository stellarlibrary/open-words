# 💥 tremendous

🔊 /trɪˈmendəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Very great in amount, scale, or intensity; extraordinarily large, powerful, or impressive
**中文：** adj. 极大的，巨大的；惊人的；了不起的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enormous（巨大的）
- immense（极大的）
- colossal（庞大的）
- prodigious（惊人的）
- formidable（令人敬畏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tremendous effort / tremendous impact / tremendous pressure / tremendous success**

👉 Example: The discovery had a tremendous impact on our understanding of molecular biology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's volunteers made a **tremendous** contribution to disaster relief efforts in the aftermath of the earthquake.
   该组织的志愿者在地震后的救灾工作中做出了巨大的贡献。

2. Advances in artificial intelligence have placed **tremendous** pressure on policymakers to develop new ethical regulatory frameworks.
   人工智能的进步给政策制定者带来了巨大的压力，要求他们制定新的伦理监管框架。

3. The athlete demonstrated **tremendous** resilience by returning to competition only months after a career-threatening injury.
   这位运动员在遭受可能终结职业生涯的伤病后仅几个月便重返赛场，展现了惊人的韧性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tremendous" as "tremend + ous" — from Latin "tremendus" meaning "to tremble," something so great it makes you tremble.
把"tremendous"想成"tremend + ous"——来自拉丁语"tremendus"，意为"令人颤抖的"，大到让你发抖。
