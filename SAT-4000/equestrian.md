# 🐴 equestrian

🔊 /ɪˈkwestriən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to horse riding or horseback. (n.) A person who rides horses; a skilled rider
**中文：** adj. 马的；骑马的 n. 骑手；马术运动员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- horseback（骑马的）
- mounted（骑着的）
- rider（骑手）
- jockey（骑师）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equestrian sports / equestrian competition / equestrian statue / equestrian center**

👉 Example: The Olympic equestrian events attract some of the most skilled riders from around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **equestrian** competition at the international games showcased extraordinary talent in both dressage and show jumping.
   国际运动会上的马术比赛展示了盛装舞步和障碍赛中的非凡才华。

2. An impressive **equestrian** statue of the legendary general stands prominently in the center of the historic square.
   一座雄伟的传奇将军骑马雕像醒目地矗立在历史广场中央。

3. The accomplished **equestrian** devoted decades to training young riders in the classical traditions of horsemanship.
   这位成就卓著的骑手数十年来致力于以古典马术传统训练年轻骑手。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equestrian" as coming from Latin "equus" meaning horse. "Equestrian" is one who is associated with horses.
把"equestrian"想成来自拉丁语"equus"，意为马。"Equestrian"指与马相关的人或事物。
