# 🎭 subterfuge

🔊 /ˈsʌbtərˌfjuːdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A deceptive device or stratagem; a pretext or excuse; a trick used to avoid blame or escape a difficult situation
**中文：** n. 托辞；借口

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ruse（诡计）
- stratagem（计谋）
- deception（欺骗）
- pretext（借口）
- artifice（诡计）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resort to subterfuge / employ subterfuge / use subterfuge / a subterfuge to conceal**

👉 Example: The diplomat resorted to subterfuge to conceal the true purpose of the negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation employed elaborate **subterfuge** to conceal its financial losses from investors and regulatory authorities.
   该公司使用精心设计的诡计来向投资者和监管机构隐瞒财务亏损。

2. Unable to obtain the documents through legitimate channels, the journalist resorted to **subterfuge** to gain access.
   由于无法通过合法渠道获取文件，记者诉诸于诡计来获得访问权限。

3. The professor warned students that any form of academic **subterfuge** would result in immediate expulsion from the program.
   教授警告学生，任何形式的学术欺骗行为都将导致立即被开除出该项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-ter-fuge" as "sub" (under) + "fuge" (flee) — fleeing under cover, using tricks to escape or deceive.
把"subterfuge"想成"sub"（在下面）+ "fuge"（逃跑）——在掩护下逃跑，用诡计来逃脱或欺骗。
