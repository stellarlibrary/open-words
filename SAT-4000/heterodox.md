# 🔀 heterodox

🔊 /ˈhetərədɑːks/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not conforming to accepted or orthodox standards or beliefs; holding unorthodox opinions
**中文：** adj. 异端的；非正统的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unorthodox（非正统的）
- heretical（异端的）
- nonconformist（不墨守成规的）
- dissident（持不同意见的）
- unconventional（非传统的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heterodox views / heterodox ideas / heterodox approach / heterodox economics**

👉 Example: The professor was known for her heterodox approach to economic theory, challenging mainstream assumptions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar's **heterodox** interpretation of constitutional law sparked intense debate among legal experts across the country.
   这位学者对宪法的非正统解读在全国法律专家之间引发了激烈的辩论。

2. Throughout history, **heterodox** thinkers have often been marginalized before their revolutionary ideas gained widespread acceptance.
   纵观历史，非正统的思想家在其革命性观点获得广泛接受之前往往遭到边缘化。

3. The journal published a collection of **heterodox** economic theories that challenged the fundamental assumptions of neoclassical models.
   该期刊发表了一系列非正统的经济理论，这些理论挑战了新古典主义模型的基本假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heterodox" as "hetero" (different) + "dox" (opinion/belief) — having different opinions from the mainstream.
把"heterodox"拆成"hetero"（不同的）+"dox"（观点/信条）——持有与主流不同的观点。
