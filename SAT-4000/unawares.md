# 💨 unawares

🔊 /ˌʌnəˈwɛrz/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Without being aware; unexpectedly; by surprise; without prior knowledge or preparation
**中文：** adv. 不知不觉地；出其不意地；意外地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unexpectedly（意外地）
- inadvertently（无意中）
- unwittingly（不知不觉地）
- by surprise（出其不意地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **catch someone unawares / take someone unawares / come upon unawares / creep up unawares**

👉 Example: The sudden policy reversal caught the entire diplomatic community completely unawares.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The severity of the financial crisis caught regulators **unawares**, exposing critical weaknesses in their oversight mechanisms.
   金融危机的严重程度出乎监管机构的意料，暴露了其监督机制中的关键弱点。

2. The predator crept through the tall grass and struck its prey **unawares** before any escape was possible.
   捕食者在高草丛中潜行，在猎物毫无察觉之时发动了攻击，使其无法逃脱。

3. Technological disruptions often arrive **unawares**, transforming entire industries before established firms can formulate an adequate response.
   技术颠覆往往在不知不觉中到来，在老牌企业能够制定充分应对策略之前就改变了整个行业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-awares" — the opposite of being aware, so you are caught completely off guard, with no awareness at all.
把"unawares"想成"un（没有）+ awares（意识）"——完全没有意识，因此被打了个措手不及。
