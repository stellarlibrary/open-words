# 🚫 censorship

🔊 /ˈsɛnsərʃɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The practice of examining and suppressing or deleting material considered objectionable; the system or institution that enforces such control
**中文：** n. 审查；审查制度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- suppression（压制）
- restriction（限制）
- control（管控）
- prohibition（禁止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **government censorship / media censorship / impose censorship / censorship laws / self-censorship**

👉 Example: The government imposed strict censorship on all media outlets during the period of civil unrest.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scholars argue that **censorship** of academic publications undermines the fundamental principles of intellectual freedom and open inquiry.
   学者们认为，对学术出版物的审查破坏了知识自由和开放探究的基本原则。

2. The committee voted to relax **censorship** regulations, allowing greater transparency in the reporting of governmental affairs.
   委员会投票放宽了审查规定，使政府事务的报道更加透明。

3. Digital **censorship** has become a significant concern as authoritarian regimes increasingly restrict access to online information.
   随着威权政权日益限制网络信息的获取，数字审查已成为一个重大问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "censor" + "-ship" — the ship that carries away forbidden content, controlling what people can see or read.
想想"censor（审查者）"加"-ship（制度）"——一艘带走违禁内容的船，控制人们能看到或读到的东西。
