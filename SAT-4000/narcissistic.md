# 🪞 narcissistic

🔊 /ˌnɑːrsɪˈsɪstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having an excessive interest in or admiration of oneself; characterized by selfishness, vanity, and a sense of entitlement
**中文：** adj. 自恋的；自我陶醉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vain（虚荣的）
- egotistical（自我中心的）
- self-centered（以自我为中心的）
- conceited（自负的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **narcissistic personality / narcissistic behavior / narcissistic tendencies / narcissistic personality disorder**

👉 Example: The therapist identified several narcissistic traits that were undermining the patient's personal relationships.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study found that individuals with **narcissistic** tendencies often struggle to maintain long-term professional and personal relationships.
   研究发现，具有**自恋**倾向的人往往难以维持长期的职业和人际关系。

2. Critics described the executive's management style as profoundly **narcissistic**, prioritizing personal glory over collective achievement.
   批评者将这位高管的管理风格描述为极其**自恋**的，将个人荣耀置于集体成就之上。

3. The **narcissistic** leader consistently dismissed constructive criticism and surrounded himself exclusively with compliant subordinates.
   这位**自恋的**领导者一贯无视建设性批评，身边只围着顺从的下属。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "narcissistic" as the adjective form of Narcissus — someone who acts like Narcissus, constantly admiring themselves.
把"narcissistic"想成纳西索斯的形容词形式——一个像纳西索斯一样不断自我欣赏的人。
