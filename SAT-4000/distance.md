# 📏 distance

🔊 /ˈdɪstəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The amount of space between two points; a far-off place. (v.) To make or become distant; to dissociate oneself
**中文：** n. 距离；远方 v. 疏远；拉开距离

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- interval（间隔）
- span（跨度）
- separation（分离）
- remoteness（遥远）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **keep one's distance / distance oneself from / in the distance / at a distance**

👉 Example: The politician sought to distance himself from the controversial remarks made by his former ally.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers measured the **distance** between the two celestial bodies using advanced telescopic equipment and precise calculations.
   研究人员使用先进的望远镜设备和精确计算测量了两个天体之间的距离。

2. Following the scandal, the corporation moved swiftly to **distance** itself from the implicated subsidiary and its leadership.
   丑闻发生后，该公司迅速采取行动与被牵涉的子公司及其领导层划清界限。

3. From a **distance**, the ancient ruins appeared remarkably well preserved despite centuries of exposure to harsh climatic conditions.
   从远处看，这些古代遗迹尽管历经数百年严酷气候的侵蚀，仍然保存得相当完好。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "di-stance" as "two stances" — two positions standing apart from each other, representing the space between them.
把"distance"想成"di（两个）+ stance（站立位置）"——两个位置彼此分开站立，代表它们之间的空间。
