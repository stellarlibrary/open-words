# ⏳ bide

🔊 /baɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To wait patiently; to remain or stay in a place; to endure or withstand
**中文：** 等待；居住，滞留；忍受

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wait（等待）
- remain（停留）
- endure（忍受）
- tarry（逗留）
- abide（忍受）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bide one's time / bide a while / bide patiently / bide and wait**

👉 Example: The strategist advised the general to bide his time until the enemy exposed a critical weakness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seasoned diplomat chose to **bide** his time rather than respond impulsively to the provocative accusations from abroad.
   这位经验丰富的外交官选择耐心等待，而不是冲动回应来自国外的挑衅性指控。

2. She resolved to **bide** patiently in the small village until conditions became favorable for her return to the capital.
   她决定在小村庄里耐心等待，直到条件有利于她返回首都。

3. The predator would **bide** silently among the tall grasses, waiting for the ideal moment to strike at unsuspecting prey.
   掠食者会在高草丛中静静等待，等候向毫无防备的猎物发起攻击的理想时机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the phrase "bide your time" — like hiding and waiting. "Bide" rhymes with "hide," and both involve staying still and being patient.
想想短语"bide your time"（等待时机）——就像隐藏和等待。"Bide"与"hide"（隐藏）押韵，两者都涉及静候和耐心。
