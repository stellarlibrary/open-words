# 🪞 narcissism

🔊 /ˈnɑːrsɪsɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Excessive interest in or admiration of oneself and one's physical appearance; extreme selfishness with a grandiose view of one's own importance
**中文：** n. 自恋；自我陶醉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vanity（虚荣）
- self-absorption（自我沉迷）
- egotism（自我中心）
- conceit（自负）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clinical narcissism / cultural narcissism / narcissism and entitlement / a form of narcissism**

👉 Example: Psychologists warn that social media platforms may foster narcissism by encouraging constant self-promotion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The psychologist's research explored how unchecked **narcissism** in leadership positions can erode organizational trust and cohesion.
   心理学家的研究探讨了领导岗位上不受约束的**自恋**如何侵蚀组织信任和凝聚力。

2. Cultural critics argue that the rise of social media has contributed to an epidemic of **narcissism** among younger generations.
   文化评论家认为社交媒体的兴起加剧了年轻一代中**自恋**的泛滥。

3. The memoir revealed a troubling pattern of **narcissism** that prevented the author from forming genuine emotional connections.
   这本回忆录揭示了一种令人不安的**自恋**模式，使作者无法建立真正的情感联系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of Narcissus from Greek mythology — the beautiful youth who fell in love with his own reflection in a pool of water.
想想希腊神话中的纳西索斯——那个爱上了自己在水中倒影的美少年。
