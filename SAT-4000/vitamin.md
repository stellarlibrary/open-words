# 💊 vitamin

🔊 /ˈvaɪtəmɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Any of a group of organic compounds essential in small quantities for normal metabolism and bodily function
**中文：** n. 维生素；维他命

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nutrient（营养素）
- micronutrient（微量营养素）
- supplement（补充剂）
- nourishment（营养物质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vitamin deficiency / vitamin supplement / vitamin intake / rich in vitamins**

👉 Example: Doctors recommend increasing your vitamin intake during the cold winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A severe **vitamin** deficiency can lead to chronic fatigue, weakened immunity, and various other health complications.
   严重的维生素缺乏会导致慢性疲劳、免疫力下降以及其他各种健康并发症。

2. The research team discovered that a particular **vitamin** compound could significantly reduce inflammation in damaged tissues.
   研究团队发现一种特定的维生素化合物可以显著减轻受损组织中的炎症。

3. Nutritionists emphasize that obtaining **vitamins** from whole foods is generally more beneficial than relying on synthetic supplements.
   营养学家强调，从天然食物中获取维生素通常比依赖合成补充剂更为有益。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vita-min" as "vital mineral" — vitamins are vital (essential for life) substances your body needs in minimal amounts.
把"vitamin"想成"vital + min"——维生素是身体所需的极少量的生命必需物质。
