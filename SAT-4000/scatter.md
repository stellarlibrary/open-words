# 🌬️ scatter

🔊 /ˈskætər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To throw or disperse in various random directions; to cause a group to separate and move apart; to deflect or diffuse. (n.) The act of scattering; a dispersal
**中文：** v. 分散，散开；撒开，驱散

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disperse（分散）
- disseminate（散布）
- spread（传播）
- strew（撒）
- diffuse（扩散）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scatter seeds / scatter in all directions / scatter to the winds / scattered across / scattered remains**

👉 Example: The strong gust of wind scattered the loose papers across the entire courtyard.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sudden arrival of the police caused the crowd of protesters to **scatter** in every direction along the narrow side streets.
   警察的突然到来导致抗议人群向狭窄小巷的各个方向四散奔逃。

2. Archaeological evidence suggests that ancient trade routes helped **scatter** technological innovations across vast and previously isolated regions.
   考古证据表明，古代贸易路线帮助将技术创新传播到广阔且此前与世隔绝的地区。

3. The farmer rose at dawn to **scatter** seeds evenly across the freshly plowed field before the spring rains arrived.
   农民在黎明时分起床，在春雨到来之前将种子均匀地撒在刚犁过的田地上。

把"scatter"想成"s + cat + ter"——想象一只受惊的猫把桌上的东西碰掉，慌乱中把物品撒得到处都是。
