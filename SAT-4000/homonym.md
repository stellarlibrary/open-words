# 🗣️ homonym

🔊 /ˈhɒmənɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A word that is spelled or pronounced the same as another word but has a different meaning and origin
**中文：** n. 同音异义词；同形异义词；同名异物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- homophone（同音词）
- homograph（同形词）
- polyseme（多义词）
- double meaning（双关义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a pair of homonyms / homonyms in English / distinguish between homonyms / common homonyms**

👉 Example: "Bank" is a classic homonym — it can mean the side of a river or a financial institution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students often confuse **homonyms** such as "bear" and "bare," which sound identical but carry entirely different meanings.
   学生经常混淆"bear"和"bare"等同音异义词，它们发音相同但含义完全不同。

2. The linguist devoted an entire chapter to **homonyms**, demonstrating how context determines the intended meaning of a word.
   这位语言学家用整整一章来讨论同音异义词，展示了语境如何决定一个词的预期含义。

3. Understanding **homonyms** is essential for language learners who must rely on context rather than spelling alone.
   理解同音异义词对于语言学习者至关重要，他们必须依靠语境而非仅凭拼写来区分词义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homo-" (same) + "-nym" (name) — words that share the same name (spelling or sound) but differ in meaning.
把"homonym"拆成"homo-"（相同）+"nym"（名字）——拥有相同"名字"（拼写或读音）但意义不同的词。
