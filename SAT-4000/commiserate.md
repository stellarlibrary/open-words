# 😢 commiserate

🔊 /kəˈmɪzəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To express sympathy or pity for someone; to feel or show sorrow for another's suffering or misfortune
**中文：** v. 同情，怜悯；表示遗憾

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sympathize（同情）
- empathize（感同身受）
- condole（慰问）
- console（安慰）
- compassionate（怜悯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commiserate with someone / commiserate over something / commiserate about a loss**

👉 Example: After the devastating defeat, the teammates gathered to commiserate with one another.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor took time to **commiserate** with the student who had failed the examination despite months of diligent preparation.
   教授花时间安慰那位尽管经过数月努力准备仍未通过考试的学生。

2. Colleagues gathered in the break room to **commiserate** over the sudden cancellation of the long-anticipated research project.
   同事们聚集在休息室里，对期待已久的研究项目突然取消表示惋惜。

3. Rather than merely **commiserate** with the displaced families, the organization provided tangible assistance and essential resources.
   该组织不仅仅是对流离失所的家庭表示同情，还提供了切实的帮助和必要的资源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "co-miserate" as "co-misery" — sharing in someone's misery together, feeling their pain alongside them.
把"commiserate"想成"co-misery"——一起分担别人的痛苦，与他们共同感受悲伤。
