# 🙇 homage

🔊 /ˈhɒmɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Special honor, respect, or tribute paid publicly to someone or something worthy of admiration
**中文：** n. 敬意；尊敬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tribute（致敬）
- reverence（敬畏）
- respect（尊敬）
- deference（恭敬）
- adulation（奉承）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pay homage to / in homage to / homage to a tradition / render homage**

👉 Example: The filmmaker paid homage to classic cinema by incorporating several iconic visual references.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The memorial was erected to pay **homage** to the soldiers who sacrificed their lives defending the nation's sovereignty.
   这座纪念碑的建造是为了向为捍卫国家主权而牺牲生命的士兵们致敬。

2. In her acceptance speech, the laureate paid **homage** to the mentors whose guidance had shaped her distinguished career.
   在获奖感言中，这位获奖者向那些指导塑造了她杰出职业生涯的导师们表达了敬意。

3. The composer's latest symphony is a deliberate **homage** to Beethoven, echoing his thematic structures and harmonic language.
   这位作曲家的最新交响曲是对贝多芬的刻意致敬，呼应了他的主题结构和和声语言。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homage" as "home + age" — at home, we show respect to our elders (age), just as homage means showing deep respect.
把"homage"想成"home + age"（家+年龄）——在家里我们尊敬长辈，正如homage表示对人深深的敬意。
