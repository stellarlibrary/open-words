# 🤲 altruist

🔊 /ˈæltruːɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who practices selfless concern for the welfare of others; one who acts out of generosity rather than self-interest
**中文：** n. 利他主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- philanthropist（慈善家）
- benefactor（恩人）
- humanitarian（人道主义者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **true altruist / natural altruist / regarded as an altruist / selfless altruist**

👉 Example: She was regarded as a genuine altruist who never expected anything in return for her kindness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **altruist** devoted her entire fortune to establishing hospitals and schools in impoverished regions across the globe.
   这位著名的利他主义者将全部财产用于在全球贫困地区建立医院和学校。

2. Critics questioned whether any political leader could truly be an **altruist** when personal ambition inevitably shapes their decisions.
   批评者质疑，当个人野心不可避免地影响决策时，是否有政治领导人能真正成为利他主义者。

3. History remembers him as an **altruist** whose tireless efforts to alleviate suffering transformed countless lives in his community.
   历史铭记他是一位利他主义者，他不懈地减轻苦难的努力改变了社区中无数人的生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "altruist" as "alter" (other) + "-ist" (person) — an altruist is a person who lives for others.
把"altruist"想成"alter"（他人）加"-ist"（人）——利他主义者就是为他人而活的人。
