# 💧 reservoir

🔊 /ˈrɛzərˌvwɑːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large natural or artificial lake used as a source of water supply; a place where a large quantity of something is stored or accumulated
**中文：** n. 水库；蓄水池

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- basin（水池）
- cistern（蓄水池）
- repository（储藏处）
- reserve（储备）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **water reservoir / reservoir of knowledge / natural reservoir / vast reservoir**

👉 Example: The city relies on a massive reservoir in the mountains to supply fresh water to its growing population.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly constructed **reservoir** provides sufficient water capacity to sustain the metropolitan area through prolonged periods of drought.
   新建的水库提供了足够的蓄水能力，可以在长期干旱期间维持大都市区的用水。

2. Universities serve as vast **reservoir**s of intellectual talent, producing graduates who drive innovation across multiple industries.
   大学是巨大的人才储备库，培养出在多个行业推动创新的毕业生。

3. Scientists discovered that the underground **reservoir** contained mineral-rich water dating back thousands of years to ancient geological periods.
   科学家发现该地下蓄水层含有可追溯到数千年前古代地质时期的富含矿物质的水。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reservoir" as "reserve-voir" — a place to "voir" (see, in French) your water reserves stored safely.
把"reservoir"想成"reserve-voir"——一个可以看到（法语voir）你安全储存的水资源的地方。
