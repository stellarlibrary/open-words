# 🔀 divergent

🔊 /daɪˈvɜːrdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Tending to differ or move apart from a common point; deviating from each other or from a standard
**中文：** adj. 相异的；分歧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- differing（不同的）
- deviating（偏离的）
- disparate（迥然不同的）
- contrasting（对比鲜明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **divergent views / divergent opinions / divergent thinking / divergent paths**

👉 Example: The committee members held divergent opinions on how to allocate the remaining budget funds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The two political parties offered **divergent** solutions to the nation's growing economic inequality and unemployment crisis.
   两个政党对国家日益加剧的经济不平等和失业危机提出了截然不同的解决方案。

2. **Divergent** thinking is widely regarded as an essential component of creativity and innovative problem-solving in educational research.
   发散性思维被广泛认为是教育研究中创造力和创新解决问题能力的重要组成部分。

3. The researchers discovered that the two species followed **divergent** evolutionary paths despite sharing a common ancestor millions of years ago.
   研究人员发现，尽管这两个物种在数百万年前拥有共同祖先，但它们走上了不同的进化道路。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "di-vergent" as "two directions" — like a fork in the road where paths diverge and go separate ways.
把"divergent"想成"di(两个)-vergent(方向)"——就像岔路口，道路分开朝不同方向延伸。
