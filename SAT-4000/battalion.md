# 🪖 battalion

🔊 /bəˈtæljən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large organized body of troops, typically consisting of several companies; a large group of people united for a common purpose
**中文：** n. 营，军营；军队，部队

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regiment（团）
- brigade（旅）
- legion（军团）
- contingent（分遣队）
- squadron（中队）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a battalion of soldiers / deploy a battalion / command a battalion / battalions of volunteers**

👉 Example: The commander ordered an entire battalion to advance toward the fortified position at dawn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The general dispatched a full **battalion** to secure the strategic bridge before enemy forces could arrive.
   将军派遣了一整个营去夺取战略桥梁，以抢在敌军到达之前。

2. A **battalion** of volunteers assembled at the disaster site, prepared to provide immediate humanitarian relief.
   一大批志愿者聚集在灾难现场，准备提供即时的人道主义救援。

3. The infantry **battalion** underwent rigorous training exercises for six months before its overseas deployment.
   该步兵营在海外部署前进行了六个月的严格训练演习。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "battalion" as "battle-ion" — a group of ions ready for battle, like a unit of soldiers prepared for combat.
把"battalion"想成"battle-ion"——一群准备战斗的离子，就像一支准备作战的士兵部队。
