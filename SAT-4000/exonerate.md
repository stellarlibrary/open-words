# ⚖️ exonerate

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɒnəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To absolve from blame or responsibility; to clear of accusation or guilt
**中文：** v. 使免罪；免除（责任）；证明无罪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absolve（赦免）
- acquit（宣判无罪）
- vindicate（证明清白）
- exculpate（开脱）
- clear（澄清）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exonerate someone of/from charges / exonerate the accused / fully exonerated**

👉 Example: The new DNA evidence was sufficient to exonerate the defendant of all criminal charges.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years of wrongful imprisonment, the court finally **exonerated** the man based on newly discovered forensic evidence.
   在多年的冤狱之后，法院终于根据新发现的法医证据为该男子平反。

2. The internal investigation **exonerated** the officer of misconduct, confirming that proper protocols had been followed throughout.
   内部调查免除了该官员的不当行为指控，确认整个过程遵循了正当程序。

3. Historians have sought to **exonerate** the scientist whose groundbreaking theories were initially dismissed as fraudulent by contemporaries.
   历史学家们试图为这位科学家平反，他开创性的理论最初被同时代的人斥为欺诈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-oner-ate" as "exit the onus" — to remove the burden of blame from someone, setting them free.
把"exonerate"想成"exit the onus"——把责任的重担从某人身上移除，让他们获得自由。
