# 🧪 corrosion

🔊 /kəˈroʊʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The gradual destruction or deterioration of a material, especially metal, by chemical reaction with its environment; decay or decline
**中文：** n. 腐蚀；侵蚀；衰败

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- erosion（侵蚀）
- deterioration（恶化）
- decay（腐化）
- oxidation（氧化）
- rusting（生锈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **corrosion resistance / corrosion damage / prevent corrosion / corrosion of metal**

👉 Example: Regular maintenance and protective coatings can significantly slow the corrosion of steel structures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bridge inspection revealed extensive **corrosion** of the steel support beams, prompting the city to authorize immediate structural repairs.
   桥梁检查发现钢支撑梁存在大面积腐蚀，促使市政府批准立即进行结构修复。

2. Scientists developed a revolutionary new alloy with exceptional **corrosion** resistance for use in deep-sea exploration equipment.
   科学家开发了一种具有卓越抗腐蚀性能的革命性新合金，用于深海探测设备。

3. The **corrosion** of public trust in governmental institutions has become a pressing concern for political analysts worldwide.
   公众对政府机构信任的侵蚀已成为全球政治分析人士关注的紧迫问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "corrosion" as "corrode" + "-sion" — picture rust slowly eating away at metal, turning a strong surface into crumbling flakes.
把"corrosion"想成"corrode"（腐蚀）加上"-sion"——想象铁锈慢慢侵蚀金属，把坚固的表面变成碎裂的薄片。
