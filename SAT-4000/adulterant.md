# ⚗️ adulterant

🔊 /əˈdʌltərənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance used to adulterate or contaminate another; (adj.) used for adulterating
**中文：** n. 掺杂物；掺假物质 adj. 掺杂用的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contaminant（污染物）
- impurity（杂质）
- additive（添加剂）
- pollutant（污染物质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chemical adulterant / food adulterant / adulterant substance / detect an adulterant**

👉 Example: Regulatory agencies conduct rigorous testing to detect any adulterant present in commercial food products.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Laboratory analysis revealed a harmful **adulterant** in the imported spice that posed a serious risk to consumer health.
   实验室分析在进口香料中发现了一种有害掺杂物，对消费者健康构成严重威胁。

2. The pharmaceutical company faced severe penalties after inspectors discovered an unauthorized **adulterant** in its medications.
   在检查人员发现其药品中含有未经授权的掺杂物后，该制药公司面临严厉处罚。

3. Advanced spectroscopy techniques enable scientists to identify even trace amounts of **adulterant** materials in food products.
   先进的光谱技术使科学家能够在食品中检测出即使是微量的掺杂物质。

---

Think of "adulterant" as "a-DULL-ter-ant" — an ant (adulterant) sneaks into your pure sugar, making it dull and impure.
把"adulterant"想成"一只蚂蚁(ant)偷偷混入纯糖中"——掺杂物就像潜入纯净物质中的不速之客。
