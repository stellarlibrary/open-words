# 🎭 semblance

🔊 /ˈsɛmbləns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The outward appearance or apparent form of something; a pretense or likeness
**中文：** n. 外表；伪装

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appearance（外貌）
- pretense（伪装）
- facade（表面）
- likeness（相似）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **semblance of order / semblance of normalcy / semblance of truth / without any semblance**

👉 Example: Despite the crisis, the manager maintained a semblance of calm to reassure the anxious staff members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The negotiators struggled to maintain a **semblance** of civility as fundamental disagreements threatened to derail the entire peace process.
   谈判者们在根本分歧威胁到整个和平进程时，努力维持着表面上的礼貌。

2. After the devastating earthquake, local authorities worked tirelessly to restore some **semblance** of normal life for displaced residents.
   毁灭性的地震过后，地方当局不懈努力为流离失所的居民恢复一些正常生活的样子。

3. The defendant's testimony bore no **semblance** of truth, as multiple witnesses directly contradicted every claim he presented.
   被告的证词毫无真实性可言，因为多名证人直接反驳了他提出的每一项声明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "semblance" as "seem-blance" — it is how things seem on the surface, which may differ from the reality beneath.
把"semblance"想成"seem-blance"——它是事物表面上看起来的样子，可能与内在的现实不同。
