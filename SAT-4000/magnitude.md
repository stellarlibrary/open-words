# 📏 magnitude

🔊 /ˈmæɡnɪˌtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The great size, extent, or importance of something; a numerical quantity or value representing size or degree; the brightness of a star or the strength of an earthquake.
**中文：** n. 大小；量级；震级；重要性；巨大；光度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scale（规模）
- extent（程度）
- significance（重要性）
- greatness（巨大）
- proportion（比例）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **of great magnitude / order of magnitude / magnitude of the problem / earthquake magnitude**

👉 Example: The magnitude of the humanitarian crisis required an unprecedented international response.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists measured the earthquake at a **magnitude** of 7.2, making it the strongest seismic event recorded that year.
   科学家测得这次地震震级为7.2级，使其成为当年记录到的最强地震事件。

2. Few people fully comprehend the **magnitude** of the challenges posed by global climate change and resource depletion.
   很少有人能完全理解全球气候变化和资源枯竭所带来的挑战的巨大程度。

3. The budget deficit has increased by an order of **magnitude** over the past decade, alarming economists worldwide.
   在过去十年中，预算赤字增加了一个数量级，令全世界的经济学家感到震惊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "magni" (great) + "tude" (state of) — magnitude is the "state of greatness," measuring how big or important something truly is.
把"magnitude"想成"magni"（大的）+"tude"（状态）——magnitude就是"巨大的状态"，衡量事物到底有多大或多重要。
