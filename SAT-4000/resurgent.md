# 🌅 resurgent

🔊 /rɪˈsɜːrdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Rising or tending to rise again; experiencing a revival or renewed vigor after a period of decline or inactivity
**中文：** adj. 复活的；复苏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reviving（复兴的）
- resurrected（复活的）
- renewed（更新的）
- rebounding（反弹的）
- reinvigorated（重新焕发活力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resurgent economy / resurgent nationalism / resurgent movement / resurgent interest / resurgent force**

👉 Example: The resurgent economy surprised analysts who had predicted a prolonged period of stagnation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **resurgent** nationalist movement posed significant challenges to the established framework of international cooperation and diplomacy.
   复苏的民族主义运动对既有的国际合作与外交框架构成了重大挑战。

2. After decades of urban decline, the **resurgent** city attracted new investments, businesses, and a growing influx of young professionals.
   经过数十年的城市衰落，这座复苏的城市吸引了新的投资、企业和越来越多的年轻专业人士。

3. Scholars have attributed the **resurgent** interest in classical philosophy to widespread disillusionment with contemporary materialist cultural values.
   学者们将对古典哲学重新兴起的兴趣归因于对当代物质主义文化价值观的普遍幻灭。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-SURGE-nt" — like a "surge" of energy coming back again — resurgent describes something surging back to life after a period of decline.
把"resurgent"想成"re-surge-nt"——就像一股能量再次涌来——resurgent描述的是在一段衰退期后重新焕发生机。
