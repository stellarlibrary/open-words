# 🔥 sulfur

🔊 /ˈsʌlfər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To treat with sulfur. (n.) A yellow nonmetallic chemical element; the color of sulfur
**中文：** n. 硫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brimstone（硫磺）
- sulphur（硫磺，英式拼写）
- element 16（第16号元素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sulfur dioxide / sulfur compound / sulfur deposit / sulfur content / sulfur emissions**

👉 Example: Volcanic eruptions release massive quantities of sulfur into the atmosphere, affecting global climate patterns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Geochemists detected elevated concentrations of **sulfur** in the groundwater samples collected near the dormant volcanic formation.
   地球化学家在休眠火山构造附近采集的地下水样本中检测到了升高的硫浓度。

2. Industrial facilities that burn fossil fuels must carefully monitor **sulfur** emissions to comply with environmental protection regulations.
   燃烧化石燃料的工业设施必须仔细监测硫排放量，以遵守环境保护法规。

3. Ancient civilizations used **sulfur** as a fumigant and medicinal agent long before its chemical properties were scientifically understood.
   远在硫的化学性质被科学认识之前，古代文明就已经将其用作熏蒸剂和药物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sulfur" as the yellow substance from volcanoes — picture a volcano spewing bright yellow smoke to remember this fiery element.
把"sulfur"想成来自火山的黄色物质——想象一座火山喷出明亮的黄色烟雾，来记住这个炽热的元素。
