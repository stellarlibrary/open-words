# 🥀 languish

🔊 /ˈlæŋɡwɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To lose vitality and grow weak; to suffer from neglect or unfavorable conditions; to pine with longing
**中文：** v. 憔悴；凋萎；失去活力；苦思

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wither（枯萎）
- deteriorate（衰退）
- decline（衰落）
- pine（憔悴）
- stagnate（停滞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **languish in prison / languish in obscurity / languish without attention / languish for years**

👉 Example: The reform bill continued to languish in committee without receiving a single vote or hearing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political dissident was forced to **languish** in a remote prison cell for over a decade without a formal trial.
   这位政治异见人士被迫在偏远的牢房中煎熬了十多年，没有经过正式审判。

2. Without adequate funding, the once-promising research initiative began to **languish** and eventually lost all institutional support.
   由于缺乏足够的资金，这项曾经前景光明的研究计划开始衰退，最终失去了所有机构的支持。

3. The neglected garden continued to **languish** throughout the drought, its flowers wilting and its soil cracking in the heat.
   在干旱期间，被忽视的花园持续凋萎，花朵枯萎，土壤在高温下龟裂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "languish" as "lang-" (long) + "-guish" (anguish) — to suffer a long anguish, slowly wasting away in misery.
把"languish"想成"lang（长）"加"guish（痛苦）"——经受漫长的痛苦，在苦难中慢慢消耗。
