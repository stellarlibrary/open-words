# 🗣️ interlocutor

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈlɒkjʊtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who takes part in a dialogue or conversation; a discussion partner
**中文：** n. 对话者；谈话者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conversant（交谈者）
- discussant（讨论者）
- speaker（发言者）
- dialogist（对话者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chief interlocutor / key interlocutor / diplomatic interlocutor / trusted interlocutor**

👉 Example: The ambassador served as the primary interlocutor between the two governments during the peace talks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat was regarded as a skilled **interlocutor** who could navigate complex negotiations between the conflicting parties.
   这位外交官被视为一位能够在冲突各方之间驾驭复杂谈判的出色对话者。

2. During the academic symposium, each panel member addressed their **interlocutor** with carefully reasoned arguments and mutual respect.
   在学术研讨会上，每位小组成员都以精心推理的论点和相互尊重回应他们的对话者。

3. The journalist proved to be a formidable **interlocutor**, pressing the politician with incisive questions about the proposed reforms.
   这位记者证明了自己是一位令人生畏的对话者，用尖锐的问题追问政治家有关拟议改革的事宜。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-locu-tor" — "inter" means between, "locu" relates to speaking (like "elocution"), so an interlocutor speaks between people in conversation.
把"interlocutor"想成"inter-locu-tor"——"inter"意为之间，"locu"与说话有关（如"elocution"），所以interlocutor是在人们之间交谈的人。
