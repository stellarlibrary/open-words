# 🌅 inception

🔊 /ɪnˈsɛpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The establishment or starting point of an institution, organization, or activity; the beginning or commencement
**中文：** n. 起初；开端；开始

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beginning（开始）
- commencement（开端）
- origin（起源）
- outset（开头）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **since its inception / from inception / at the inception of / since the inception of the program**

👉 Example: Since its inception in 1945, the United Nations has worked to promote international peace and cooperation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship program has awarded over ten thousand grants since its **inception** more than three decades ago.
   该奖学金项目自三十多年前创立以来已颁发了一万多笔资助。

2. From its **inception**, the research initiative was designed to foster interdisciplinary collaboration among leading international scientists.
   该研究计划从一开始就旨在促进国际顶尖科学家之间的跨学科合作。

3. The organization has undergone significant structural changes since its **inception**, adapting to evolving global economic conditions.
   该组织自成立以来经历了重大的结构变革，以适应不断变化的全球经济状况。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CEPT-ion" — "cept" relates to "capture" or "take"; inception is when you first "take" hold of an idea and begin something new.
把"inception"想成"in-CEPT（抓住）-ion"——"cept"与"抓住"有关；inception就是你第一次"抓住"一个想法并开始新事物的时刻。
