# 🔢 decimal

🔊 /ˈdesɪməl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or based on the number ten; expressed in a system of counting using ten as a base. (n.) A fraction expressed in a notation using the base ten
**中文：** adj. 小数的；十进位的 n. 小数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fractional（分数的）
- base-ten（十进制的）
- numeric（数字的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **decimal point / decimal system / decimal place / decimal fraction / convert to decimal**

👉 Example: Students must learn to convert ordinary fractions into decimal form before advancing to more complex mathematics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The metric system is based on **decimal** units, which makes conversion between different measurements remarkably straightforward and intuitive.
   公制以十进制单位为基础，这使得不同计量单位之间的换算非常简单直观。

2. The engineer calculated the result to four **decimal** places to ensure the highest possible degree of precision in the design.
   工程师将结果计算到小数点后四位，以确保设计中尽可能高的精度。

3. Many ancient civilizations independently developed **decimal** counting systems because humans naturally count using their ten fingers.
   许多古代文明独立发展了十进制计数系统，因为人类天生用十个手指来计数。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deci-mal" — "deci" means ten (like decade = 10 years, December was originally the 10th month), so decimal relates to the base-ten number system.
把"decimal"中的"deci"想成十——（如decade=十年，December原本是第十个月），所以decimal与十进制有关。
