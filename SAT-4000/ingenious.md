# 💡 ingenious

🔊 /ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Cleverly inventive or resourceful; showing remarkable originality, skill, or cleverness in design or execution
**中文：** adj. 有独创性的；机灵的；精制的；心灵手巧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inventive（有创造力的）
- clever（聪明的）
- resourceful（足智多谋的）
- innovative（创新的）
- creative（有创意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an ingenious solution / ingenious design / ingenious method / ingenious device**

👉 Example: The engineer devised an ingenious solution to the persistent overheating problem.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect proposed an **ingenious** design that maximized natural light while minimizing overall energy consumption costs.
   建筑师提出了一个巧妙的设计，在最大化自然采光的同时将总体能耗成本降到最低。

2. Through an **ingenious** combination of traditional techniques and modern technology, the researchers achieved unprecedented experimental results.
   通过巧妙地将传统技术与现代科技相结合，研究人员取得了前所未有的实验结果。

3. The student presented an **ingenious** argument that reframed the entire philosophical debate from a completely unexpected perspective.
   该学生提出了一个独具匠心的论点，从完全出人意料的角度重新构建了整个哲学辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-GENIUS" — an ingenious person has genius inside them, producing brilliantly clever ideas.
把"ingenious"想成"in + genius（天才）"——一个有独创性的人内心住着天才，能产生绝妙的想法。
