# 💝 philanthropic

🔊 /ˌfɪlənˈθrɒpɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or characterized by philanthropy; benevolent and generous in assisting those in need
**中文：** adj. 博爱的；仁慈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- charitable（慈善的）
- benevolent（仁慈的）
- altruistic（利他的）
- humanitarian（人道主义的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **philanthropic efforts / philanthropic organization / philanthropic donation / philanthropic mission**

👉 Example: The billionaire's philanthropic efforts have funded hospitals and schools across developing nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The foundation's **philanthropic** initiatives have provided clean drinking water to over three million people in rural communities.
   该基金会的慈善项目已为农村社区超过三百万人提供了清洁饮用水。

2. Several **philanthropic** organizations collaborated to establish scholarship programs for underprivileged students seeking higher education.
   几家慈善组织合作建立了为经济困难学生提供高等教育的奖学金项目。

3. Her **philanthropic** vision inspired a generation of wealthy entrepreneurs to dedicate significant portions of their fortunes to social causes.
   她博爱的愿景激励了一代富有的企业家将其大量财产投入社会事业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "phil-anthrop-ic" as "phil loves anthropos (people)" — someone who loves humanity is philanthropic.
把"philanthropic"想成"phil（爱）+ anthropos（人类）"——热爱人类的人就是博爱的。
