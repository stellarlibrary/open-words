# 💎 trove

🔊 /troʊv/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A store of valuable or interesting things; a treasure trove; a rich collection of discovered items
**中文：** n. 被发现的东西；收藏的东西；宝库

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- treasure（宝藏）
- hoard（储藏物）
- cache（隐藏物）
- collection（收藏品）
- repository（宝库）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **treasure trove / a trove of information / a trove of artifacts / rich trove**

👉 Example: The attic proved to be a trove of family heirlooms and forgotten historical documents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists unearthed a remarkable **trove** of ancient coins and pottery fragments dating back to the Roman imperial period.
   考古学家出土了一批可追溯到罗马帝国时期的珍贵古代硬币和陶器碎片。

2. The newly digitized archive represents a **trove** of primary source materials that will transform scholarly research on colonial history.
   新近数字化的档案是一座原始资料宝库，将彻底改变殖民史的学术研究。

3. Investigators discovered a **trove** of confidential documents in the abandoned office that revealed decades of corporate financial misconduct.
   调查人员在废弃的办公室中发现了一批机密文件，揭露了数十年的企业财务不当行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trove" as part of "treasure trove" — picture discovering a hidden chest full of valuable treasures waiting to be found.
把"trove"想成"treasure trove（宝藏）"的一部分——想象发现了一个装满珍贵宝物的隐藏箱子。
