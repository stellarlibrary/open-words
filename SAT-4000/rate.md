# 📊 rate

🔊 /reɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A measure or quantity measured against another; speed; price or charge. (v.) To assess the value or quality of; to consider or regard
**中文：** n. 比率；速度；价格；等级 v. 认为；估价；责骂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ratio（比率）
- pace（速度）
- evaluate（评估）
- assess（评定）
- rank（排列）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rate of change / interest rate / at any rate / rate highly / rate as**

👉 Example: Economists closely monitor the rate of inflation to predict future market conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The central bank decided to raise the interest **rate** by half a percentage point to combat persistent inflationary pressures.
   中央银行决定将利率提高半个百分点，以对抗持续的通胀压力。

2. Researchers found that the **rate** of deforestation in tropical regions has accelerated significantly over the past two decades.
   研究人员发现，热带地区的森林砍伐速度在过去二十年中显著加快。

3. Critics **rate** this novel as one of the most influential works of contemporary literary fiction published this century.
   评论家将这部小说评为本世纪出版的最具影响力的当代文学作品之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rate" as measuring "how much per unit" — a rate always compares one thing against another, like speed per hour.
把"rate"想成衡量"每单位多少"——比率总是将一个事物与另一个事物进行比较，如每小时的速度。
