# 🔈 phonic

🔊 /ˈfɒnɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or producing sound; of or relating to speech sounds or the science of acoustics
**中文：** adj. 有声的，浊音的；声音的，语音的；声学的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acoustic（声学的）
- auditory（听觉的）
- vocal（声音的）
- sonic（声波的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **phonic awareness / phonic skills / phonic method / phonic elements**

👉 Example: Early literacy programs emphasize phonic skills to help children decode written language through sound patterns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The teacher employed a **phonic** approach to reading instruction, systematically linking each letter to its corresponding sound.
   老师采用语音教学法进行阅读教学，系统地将每个字母与其对应的声音联系起来。

2. Researchers analyzed the **phonic** properties of various musical instruments to understand how they produce distinct tonal qualities.
   研究人员分析了各种乐器的声学特性，以了解它们如何产生独特的音质。

3. The linguistics course devoted an entire module to **phonic** analysis, exploring how speech sounds are articulated and perceived.
   语言学课程用了整个模块来进行语音分析，探索语音是如何发出和感知的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "phon-ic" as related to "phone" (sound) — a telephone transmits sound, and phonic means relating to sound.
把"phonic"想成与"phone（声音）"有关——电话传输声音，phonic就是与声音有关的意思。
