# 💉 vaccine

🔊 /vækˈsiːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A biological preparation that provides immunity against a specific disease. (adj.) Of or relating to vaccines or vaccination
**中文：** n. 疫苗 adj. 疫苗的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inoculation（接种）
- immunization（免疫接种）
- antiserum（抗血清）
- prophylactic（预防药物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **administer a vaccine / develop a vaccine / vaccine against / vaccine for / vaccine efficacy**

👉 Example: Scientists raced to develop a vaccine against the rapidly spreading virus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly developed **vaccine** demonstrated remarkable efficacy in clinical trials involving over ten thousand participants worldwide.
   新开发的疫苗在涉及全球一万多名参与者的临床试验中展现了显著的效果。

2. Public health authorities urged all citizens to receive the **vaccine** before the onset of the influenza season.
   公共卫生当局敦促所有公民在流感季节来临之前接种疫苗。

3. Researchers published a comprehensive study confirming that the **vaccine** provided lasting immunity for at least five years.
   研究人员发表了一项综合研究，证实该疫苗至少能提供五年的持久免疫力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vaccine" as coming from "vacca" (Latin for cow) — the first vaccine was derived from cowpox to prevent smallpox.
把"vaccine"想成来自拉丁语"vacca"（牛）——第一种疫苗是从牛痘中提取的，用来预防天花。
