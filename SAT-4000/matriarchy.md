# 👑 matriarchy

🔊 /ˈmeɪtriɑːrki/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A form of social organization in which the mother or the oldest female is the head of the family; a society governed or dominated by women
**中文：** n. 母权制；女家长制；母系社会

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- matriliny（母系制度）
- gynocracy（女权政治）
- female dominance（女性主导）
- matrilineal society（母系社会）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a matriarchy society / establish a matriarchy / matriarchy and patriarchy / under a matriarchy / ancient matriarchy**

👉 Example: Anthropologists have documented several indigenous cultures that operated under a matriarchy for centuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologist's research revealed that the ancient civilization functioned as a **matriarchy** where women held all political authority.
   人类学家的研究揭示，这个古代文明是一个母权制社会，女性掌握着所有政治权力。

2. In the **matriarchy** of the Mosuo people, property and family names are passed down through the maternal lineage.
   在摩梭人的母系社会中，财产和姓氏通过母系血统传承。

3. Scholars continue to debate whether any true **matriarchy** ever existed or if such societies were merely matrilineal in structure.
   学者们继续争论是否曾经存在过真正的母权制社会，还是这些社会仅仅在结构上是母系的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "matri + archy" — "matri" comes from Latin "mater" (mother), and "archy" means rule, so matriarchy means "mother's rule."
把"matriarchy"想成"matri + archy"——"matri"来自拉丁语"mater"（母亲），"archy"表示统治，所以matriarchy就是"母亲的统治"。
