# ⚖️ righteous

🔊 /ˈraɪtʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Morally right or justifiable; virtuous, upright, and acting in accordance with ethical principles
**中文：** adj. 正直的，正当的；公正的，正义的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- virtuous（有道德的）
- moral（正直的）
- upright（正派的）
- just（公正的）
- ethical（合乎伦理的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **righteous anger / righteous indignation / self-righteous / righteous cause / righteous person**

👉 Example: Her righteous indignation at the injustice inspired thousands to join the civil rights movement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The activist's **righteous** determination to combat inequality earned her widespread admiration from both political allies and former opponents alike.
   这位活动家打击不平等的正义决心赢得了政治盟友和昔日对手的广泛钦佩。

2. Scholars have long debated whether **righteous** warfare can ever truly be justified under the principles of international humanitarian law.
   学者们长期争论，在国际人道主义法原则下，正义战争是否真的能被证明是正当的。

3. The judge delivered a **righteous** verdict that restored public confidence in the integrity and fairness of the judicial system.
   法官作出了公正的判决，恢复了公众对司法体系的公正性和公平性的信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "righteous" as "right" + "-eous" — someone who is righteous always does what is right, guided by a strong moral compass.
把"righteous"想成"right"加上"-eous"——一个righteous的人总是做正确的事，以强烈的道德指南针为导向。
