# ⛔ discontinue

🔊 /ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cease doing or providing something; to put an end to a state or activity; to stop producing or making available
**中文：** v. 停止；使中止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cease（停止）
- halt（中止）
- terminate（终止）
- suspend（暂停）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **discontinue a product / discontinue treatment / discontinue service / discontinue the use of**

👉 Example: The pharmaceutical company chose to discontinue the drug after clinical trials revealed serious side effects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manufacturer announced its decision to **discontinue** the popular product line due to declining sales and rising production costs.
   制造商宣布因销量下降和生产成本上升而决定停产这一热门产品线。

2. Patients should not **discontinue** prescribed medication without first consulting their physician about potential withdrawal symptoms or risks.
   患者在未先咨询医生关于潜在戒断症状或风险的情况下，不应擅自停用处方药物。

3. The university was forced to **discontinue** several academic programs after the budget committee imposed significant funding reductions across all departments.
   在预算委员会对所有部门实施大幅削减资金后，该大学被迫停办了几个学术项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-continue" — the prefix "dis" negates "continue," so discontinue literally means to not continue, to stop.
把"discontinue"拆成"dis-continue"——前缀"dis"否定了"continue（继续）"，所以字面意思就是不再继续、停止。
