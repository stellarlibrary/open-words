# ✨ sensation

🔊 /sɛnˈseɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A physical feeling or perception resulting from stimulation of the senses; a widespread reaction of excitement or interest
**中文：** n. 感觉，知觉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- feeling（感觉）
- perception（知觉）
- impression（印象）
- excitement（轰动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cause a sensation / burning sensation / sensation of [feeling] / media sensation**

👉 Example: The discovery of the ancient artifact caused a sensation among archaeologists worldwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient described a tingling **sensation** in her extremities, which prompted the neurologist to order a comprehensive series of diagnostic tests.
   病人描述了四肢有刺痛感，这促使神经科医生下令进行一系列全面的诊断检查。

2. The young pianist became an overnight **sensation** after her virtuoso performance at the international competition captivated audiences and critics alike.
   这位年轻钢琴家在国际比赛上的精湛表演征服了观众和评论家，一夜之间引起轰动。

3. Researchers investigated the neural pathways responsible for the **sensation** of pain in order to develop more effective analgesic treatments.
   研究人员调查了负责痛觉的神经通路，以开发更有效的镇痛治疗方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sensation" as "sense + ation" — it is the action of sensing, whether a physical feeling on your skin or the thrill that sweeps through a crowd.
把"sensation"想成"sense + ation"——即感知的动作，无论是皮肤上的触感，还是席卷人群的兴奋之情。
