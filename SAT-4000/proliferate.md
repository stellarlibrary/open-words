# 🌱 proliferate

🔊 /prəˈlɪfəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To increase rapidly in number; to reproduce or spread at a fast rate
**中文：** v. 增殖；扩散；激增；繁殖；繁衍

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- multiply（繁殖）
- spread（扩散）
- burgeon（激增）
- escalate（升级）
- mushroom（迅速增长）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proliferate rapidly / proliferate unchecked / proliferate across / nuclear proliferate**

👉 Example: Misinformation tends to proliferate rapidly on social media platforms without proper moderation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Digital technologies continue to **proliferate** across developing nations, transforming economies and reshaping daily life for millions.
   数字技术继续在发展中国家扩散，改变了经济并重塑了数百万人的日常生活。

2. Invasive plant species **proliferated** throughout the wetland ecosystem after the natural predators were removed from the region.
   在天然捕食者被从该地区移除后，入侵植物物种在湿地生态系统中大量繁殖。

3. Concerns about nuclear weapons that **proliferate** beyond established powers have dominated international security discussions for decades.
   关于核武器向现有核大国之外扩散的担忧数十年来一直主导着国际安全讨论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-LIFE-rate" — things that are "pro life" reproduce and multiply at a great rate.
把"proliferate"想成"pro-LIFE-rate"——支持"生命"的事物会以极快的速率繁殖和增长。
