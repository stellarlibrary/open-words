# 👑 monarchy

🔊 /ˈmɒnərki/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A form of government in which a single person, usually a king or queen, holds supreme authority; a state ruled by a monarch
**中文：** n. 君主政体；君主国；君主政治

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- kingdom（王国）
- sovereignty（主权）
- realm（领域）
- dominion（统治权）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **constitutional monarchy / absolute monarchy / hereditary monarchy / establish a monarchy**

👉 Example: The country transitioned from an absolute monarchy to a constitutional monarchy in the nineteenth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **monarchy** in that nation has endured for over five centuries despite numerous political upheavals and revolutions.
   该国的君主政体历经五个多世纪，尽管经历了无数次政治动荡和革命，依然延续至今。

2. Many political theorists have debated whether a constitutional **monarchy** can coexist effectively with modern democratic institutions.
   许多政治理论家一直在争论君主立宪制能否与现代民主制度有效共存。

3. The revolution of 1789 ultimately dismantled the French **monarchy** and ushered in a new era of republican governance.
   1789年的革命最终推翻了法国君主制，开启了共和治理的新时代。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mono" (one) + "archy" (rule) — monarchy means "rule by one person," a single ruler governing the state.
把"monarchy"拆成"mono"（一个）+"archy"（统治）——君主制就是"一个人的统治"。
