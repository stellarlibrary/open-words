# 😊 amiable

🔊 /ˈeɪmiəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a friendly and pleasant manner; good-natured and likable.
**中文：** 和蔼可亲的，亲切的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- affable（和蔼的）
- genial（亲切的）
- cordial（热诚的）
- congenial（意气相投的）
- pleasant（令人愉快的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **personality, disposition, manner, character, host, colleague**
👉 Example: Her amiable disposition made her universally beloved among her peers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **amiable** demeanor encouraged students to participate actively in classroom discussions.
   教授和蔼可亲的态度鼓励学生积极参与课堂讨论。

2. Despite the intensity of the negotiations, the ambassador maintained an **amiable** tone throughout the proceedings.
   尽管谈判气氛紧张，大使在整个过程中始终保持着亲切的语调。

3. The **amiable** shopkeeper greeted every customer with a warm smile and genuine interest in their needs.
   那位和蔼可亲的店主以温暖的微笑和真诚的关心迎接每一位顾客。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amiable" starts with "ami" — from French "ami" meaning "friend." An amiable person is naturally friendly.
"Amiable"以"ami"开头——来自法语"ami"（朋友）。一个amiable的人天生就很友善。
