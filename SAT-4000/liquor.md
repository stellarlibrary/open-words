# 🍷 liquor

🔊 /ˈlɪkər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An alcoholic drink, especially distilled spirits; a liquid solution or substance
**中文：** n. 酒，含酒精饮料（尤指烈性酒）；溶液

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spirits（烈酒）
- alcohol（酒精）
- brew（酿造酒）
- beverage（饮料）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **liquor store / hard liquor / liquor license / malt liquor**

👉 Example: The restaurant applied for a liquor license to serve wine and cocktails to its patrons.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government imposed stricter regulations on the sale of **liquor** to reduce alcohol-related public health problems nationwide.
   政府对烈酒销售实施了更严格的法规，以减少全国范围内与酒精相关的公共健康问题。

2. During Prohibition, the illegal production and distribution of **liquor** gave rise to powerful organized crime networks.
   在禁酒令期间，非法生产和分销烈酒催生了强大的有组织犯罪网络。

3. The chef used a small amount of **liquor** to deglaze the pan and create a rich, flavorful sauce for the dish.
   厨师用少量烈酒来给锅去渣，为这道菜制作出浓郁可口的酱汁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liquor" as sharing the root "liqu-" with "liquid" — liquor is essentially a potent liquid that people drink.
把"liquor"想成与"liquid（液体）"共享词根"liqu-"——烈酒本质上就是人们饮用的一种烈性液体。
