# 🚲 pedal

🔊 /ˈpedəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun, adjective | 动词, 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To operate a pedal; to ride a bicycle. (n.) A foot-operated lever or control. (adj.) Of or relating to the foot
**中文：** v. 踩踏板；骑自行车 n. 踏板；脚蹬子 adj. 脚的；脚踏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lever（操纵杆）
- treadle（踏板）
- cycle（骑车）
- footboard（脚踏板）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pedal a bicycle / gas pedal / brake pedal / soft pedal / pedal to the metal**

👉 Example: She had to pedal hard uphill against the strong headwind to reach the summit before sunset.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pianist used the sustain **pedal** with remarkable subtlety to create a seamless and resonant musical performance.
   这位钢琴家以非凡的细腻运用延音踏板，创造出流畅而共鸣的音乐表演。

2. Environmental advocates encourage commuters to **pedal** to work instead of driving, thereby reducing urban carbon emissions significantly.
   环保倡导者鼓励通勤者骑车上班而非开车，从而大幅减少城市碳排放。

3. The engineer redesigned the brake **pedal** mechanism to improve response time and enhance overall vehicle safety standards.
   工程师重新设计了制动踏板机构，以缩短响应时间并提高整体车辆安全标准。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pedal" as coming from Latin "pes" (foot) — a pedal is something you push with your foot, whether on a bike or a piano.
把"pedal"想成来自拉丁语"pes"（脚）——踏板就是你用脚踩的东西，无论是自行车还是钢琴上的。
