# 🤝 succor

🔊 /ˈsʌkər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Assistance and support in times of hardship and distress; a person who gives such aid. (v.) To give aid, relief, or assistance to someone in need
**中文：** v. 援助，救援 n. 救援；援助者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aid（援助）
- relief（救济）
- assistance（帮助）
- comfort（安慰）
- rescue（救援）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bring succor / provide succor / succor the wounded / succor to the afflicted / in need of succor**

👉 Example: The humanitarian organization rushed to provide succor to the victims of the devastating earthquake.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. International relief agencies worked tirelessly to bring **succor** to the thousands of refugees displaced by the armed conflict.
   国际救援机构不知疲倦地工作，为数以千计因武装冲突而流离失所的难民提供救援。

2. The charitable foundation was established to **succor** impoverished communities lacking access to basic healthcare and educational resources.
   该慈善基金会的成立旨在援助缺乏基本医疗保健和教育资源的贫困社区。

3. During the siege, brave volunteers risked their own lives to deliver **succor** to the trapped civilians within the besieged city.
   在围困期间，勇敢的志愿者冒着生命危险向被围城市中被困的平民提供救援。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "succor" as similar to "sucker" — but instead of being fooled, you are being helped. A succor is someone who sucks away your suffering.
把"succor"想成类似于"sucker"——但不是被欺骗，而是被帮助。succor是一个吸走你痛苦的人。
