# 🍋 zest

🔊 /zɛst/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Great enthusiasm, excitement, or keen interest; a distinctive flavor or quality. (v.) To add flavor or zest to something
**中文：** n. 风味；热心；强烈的兴趣 v. 给……调味

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enthusiasm（热情）
- passion（激情）
- relish（享受）
- gusto（热忱）
- fervor（热烈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **zest for life / add zest to / with great zest / lemon zest**

👉 Example: Her boundless zest for learning inspired every student in the advanced seminar.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor lectured with remarkable **zest**, transforming even the driest topics into captivating intellectual discussions.
   这位教授讲课时充满热情，即使是最枯燥的话题也能变成引人入胜的学术讨论。

2. Traveling abroad added a new **zest** to her life, broadening her perspective on diverse cultures and traditions.
   出国旅行为她的生活增添了新的情趣，拓宽了她对多元文化和传统的视野。

3. The chef recommended using fresh lemon **zest** to enhance the flavor profile of the delicate seafood preparation.
   厨师建议使用新鲜柠檬皮来提升这道精致海鲜菜肴的风味层次。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "zest" as the outer peel of a lemon — it adds sharp, bright flavor, just as zest adds excitement to life.
把"zest"想成柠檬的外皮——它增添了鲜明的风味，正如热情为生活增添了色彩。
