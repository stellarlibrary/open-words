# 😠 berate

🔊 /bɪˈreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To scold or criticize someone angrily and at length; to reprimand harshly
**中文：** v. 严厉指责；申斥；痛斥

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scold（斥责）
- reproach（责备）
- castigate（严厉批评）
- admonish（告诫）
- rebuke（训斥）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **berate someone for / berate publicly / berate harshly / berate endlessly**

👉 Example: The manager berated the employee for repeatedly failing to meet the project deadlines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coach **berated** the players for their careless mistakes during the critical championship match last evening.
   教练因球员们在昨晚关键的冠军赛中犯下粗心错误而严厉训斥了他们。

2. It is unprofessional to **berate** subordinates in front of their colleagues, as it undermines morale and mutual respect.
   当着同事的面训斥下属是不专业的行为，因为这会损害士气和相互尊重。

3. The senator **berated** the committee for its failure to address the escalating environmental crisis with adequate urgency.
   参议员严厉指责委员会未能以足够的紧迫感应对不断升级的环境危机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "berate" as "be + rate" — when someone rates you badly, they berate you with harsh criticism.
把"berate"想成"be + rate（评价）"——当有人恶评你时，就是在严厉指责你。
