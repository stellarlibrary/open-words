# 💉 serum

🔊 /ˈsɪrəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The clear, yellowish fluid component of blood; a liquid used for medical purposes, especially to provide immunity against disease
**中文：** n. 血清

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- antiserum（抗血清）
- plasma（血浆）
- antitoxin（抗毒素）
- vaccine（疫苗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blood serum / serum level / antivenom serum / serum sample / administer serum**

👉 Example: The laboratory analyzed the patient's serum to determine the concentration of specific antibodies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicians administered a specialized **serum** to counteract the effects of the rare venomous snakebite on the patient.
   医生们注射了一种专用血清来抵消罕见毒蛇咬伤对患者造成的影响。

2. Elevated cholesterol levels in the blood **serum** are widely recognized as a significant risk factor for cardiovascular disease.
   血清中胆固醇水平升高被广泛认为是心血管疾病的重要风险因素。

3. Researchers developed a new diagnostic **serum** capable of detecting the pathogen within hours rather than days.
   研究人员开发了一种新型诊断血清，能够在数小时而非数天内检测出病原体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "serum" as the "serious" fluid — it is the serious, essential part of blood that carries antibodies and fights disease.
把"serum"想成"serious"的液体——它是血液中严肃、关键的部分，携带抗体并对抗疾病。
