# 📐 linear

🔊 /ˈlɪniər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Arranged in or extending along a straight line; progressing sequentially from one stage to another; of or relating to lines.
**中文：** adj. 线的，线型的；直线的；长度的；线性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- straight（直的）
- sequential（顺序的）
- successive（连续的）
- rectilinear（直线的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **linear progression / linear relationship / linear equation / linear thinking / linear growth**

👉 Example: The data revealed a clear linear relationship between hours of study and examination performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economist demonstrated that population growth in the region followed a remarkably **linear** pattern over the past three decades.
   经济学家证明，该地区过去三十年的人口增长呈现出非常明显的线性模式。

2. Critics argue that **linear** thinking fails to capture the complexity of interconnected social and environmental challenges we face.
   批评者认为，线性思维无法捕捉我们面临的相互关联的社会和环境挑战的复杂性。

3. The mathematics curriculum introduced **linear** algebra as a foundational course, essential for advanced study in engineering and physics.
   数学课程将线性代数作为基础课程引入，它对工程和物理学的高级学习至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "line + ar" — the adjective form of "line," describing anything that follows a straight, step-by-step path.
把"linear"拆分为"line（线）+ ar（形容词后缀）"——"线"的形容词形式，描述任何沿直线逐步推进的事物。
