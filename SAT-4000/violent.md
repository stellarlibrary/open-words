# 💥 violent

🔊 /ˈvaɪələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Using or involving physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill; very strong or powerful in effect or intensity
**中文：** 暴力的；猛烈的；激烈的；强暴的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fierce（猛烈的）
- brutal（残暴的）
- ferocious（凶猛的）
- vehement（激烈的）
- turbulent（动荡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **violent crime / violent storm / violent reaction / violent conflict / violent outburst**

👉 Example: The region experienced a violent storm that caused extensive damage to infrastructure and homes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary examined the root causes of **violent** crime in urban communities and proposed evidence-based intervention strategies.
   这部纪录片探讨了城市社区暴力犯罪的根本原因，并提出了基于证据的干预策略。

2. A **violent** eruption of the volcano forced the immediate evacuation of all residents within a twenty-kilometer radius.
   火山的猛烈喷发迫使方圆二十公里内的所有居民立即撤离。

3. The patient suffered a **violent** allergic reaction to the medication, requiring emergency medical treatment at the hospital.
   患者对药物产生了剧烈的过敏反应，需要在医院接受紧急治疗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "violent" as "viol-ent" — the "viol" sounds like "vile," and violent acts are indeed vile, forceful, and destructive.
把"violent"想成"viol-ent"——"viol"听起来像"vile"（卑鄙的），暴力行为确实是卑劣的、强制的和破坏性的。
