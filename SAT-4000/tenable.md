# 🏰 tenable

🔊 /ˈtɛnəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to be maintained or defended against attack or objection; capable of being held or occupied
**中文：** adj. 守得住的，可防守的；站得住脚的；有条理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- defensible（可辩护的）
- justifiable（有正当理由的）
- sustainable（可维持的）
- viable（可行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tenable position / tenable argument / no longer tenable / barely tenable / tenable hypothesis**

👉 Example: Without new evidence, the researcher's original hypothesis was no longer tenable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney argued that the prosecution's theory was not **tenable** given the newly discovered forensic evidence.
   辩护律师认为，鉴于新发现的法医证据，控方的理论站不住脚。

2. As public opinion shifted dramatically, the senator's moderate stance became increasingly difficult and barely **tenable**.
   随着公众舆论发生剧烈变化，这位参议员的温和立场变得越来越难以维持。

3. Historians have debated whether the fortress was truly **tenable** against the superior forces that besieged it for months.
   历史学家们一直在争论这座堡垒在数月围困中是否真的能抵御优势兵力的进攻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ten-able" — if you can hold something with "ten" fingers, you can maintain it; a tenable position is one you can hold onto.
把"tenable"想成"ten（十）+ able（能够）"——如果你能用十根手指抓住某物，就能维持它；站得住脚的立场就是你能坚守的。
