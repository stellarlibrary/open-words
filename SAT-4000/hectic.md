# 🌀 hectic

🔊 /ˈhɛktɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Full of incessant or frantic activity; very busy and fast-paced; feverishly excited
**中文：** adj. 忙碌的，繁忙的；兴奋的；发烧的，发热的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frantic（狂乱的）
- frenetic（狂热的）
- bustling（忙碌的）
- chaotic（混乱的）
- turbulent（动荡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hectic schedule / hectic pace / hectic day / hectic lifestyle / hectic week**

👉 Example: After a hectic week of final exams, the students welcomed the holiday break.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **hectic** pace of modern urban life has been linked to rising levels of stress, anxiety, and chronic fatigue.
   现代城市生活的忙碌节奏与压力、焦虑和慢性疲劳水平的上升有关。

2. Despite her **hectic** schedule, the executive always reserved time each morning for strategic planning and reflection.
   尽管日程繁忙，这位高管每天早上总会留出时间进行战略规划和反思。

3. The trading floor became increasingly **hectic** as investors reacted to the unexpected announcement from the central bank.
   随着投资者对央行意外公告的反应，交易大厅变得越来越忙碌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hectic" sounding like "heck-tick" — so many ticks on your clock, every second is filled with frantic activity.
把"hectic"想成"heck-tick（天哪，滴答）"——时钟的每一秒都被疯狂的活动填满。
