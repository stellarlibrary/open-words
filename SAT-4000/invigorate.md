# 💪 invigorate

🔊 /ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give strength or energy to; to fill with life and vitality; to encourage or inspire
**中文：** v. 鼓舞；使精力充沛；使充满活力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- energize（使充满活力）
- revitalize（使恢复活力）
- stimulate（激励）
- enliven（使活跃）
- animate（鼓舞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **invigorate the economy / invigorate the mind / invigorate the body / feel invigorated**

👉 Example: A brisk morning walk can invigorate both the body and the mind before a long day of work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new leadership team sought to **invigorate** the struggling company by introducing bold reforms and innovative strategies.
   新的领导团队试图通过引入大胆改革和创新策略来振兴这家陷入困境的公司。

2. The cool mountain air seemed to **invigorate** the exhausted hikers, restoring their energy for the final ascent.
   凉爽的山间空气似乎使疲惫的登山者精力充沛，恢复了他们最后攀登的体力。

3. The professor's passionate lecture managed to **invigorate** a class that had grown weary of routine academic exercises.
   教授充满激情的讲座成功地鼓舞了一个已对日常学术练习感到厌倦的班级。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-VIGOR-ate" — the word "vigor" (meaning energy and strength) is right in the middle, so to invigorate is to put vigor INTO someone.
把"invigorate"想成"in-VIGOR-ate"——单词中间就有"vigor"（活力、精力），所以invigorate就是把活力注入某人体内。
