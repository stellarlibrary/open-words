# 🏘️ burgher

🔊 /ˈbɜːrɡər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A citizen of a town or city, especially a member of the wealthy middle class; a prosperous solid citizen
**中文：** n. 市民；自治市居民；公民

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- citizen（公民）
- townsman（市民）
- resident（居民）
- denizen（居民）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prosperous burgher / wealthy burghers / burghers of the town / respectable burgher**

👉 Example: The wealthy burghers of the medieval town contributed generously to the construction of the new cathedral.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosperous **burghers** of Amsterdam commissioned renowned artists to paint portraits reflecting their elevated social status.
   阿姆斯特丹富裕的市民委托著名艺术家绘制肖像，以反映他们崇高的社会地位。

2. Local **burghers** convened at the town hall to deliberate on matters concerning trade regulations and municipal governance.
   当地市民在市政厅集会，商议有关贸易法规和市政管理的事务。

3. As a respected **burgher** of the community, he felt obligated to advocate for improvements in public education and infrastructure.
   作为社区中一位受尊敬的市民，他觉得有义务倡导改善公共教育和基础设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "burg" (town or fortress in German) + "her" — a burgher is someone who belongs to a burg, a respectable town citizen.
想到"burg"（德语中的城镇或堡垒）+ "her"——burgher就是属于城镇的人，即受人尊敬的市民。
