# 💀 lethal

🔊 /ˈliːθəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing or capable of causing death; extremely harmful or destructive. (n.) A lethal factor or agent
**中文：** adj. 致命的，致死的 n. 致死因子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deadly（致命的）
- fatal（致死的）
- mortal（致命的）
- toxic（有毒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lethal dose / lethal weapon / lethal injection / lethal combination**

👉 Example: The combination of extreme heat and dehydration proved lethal to many of the stranded hikers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Exposure to even small amounts of the chemical compound can be **lethal** to humans without proper protective equipment.
   在没有适当防护设备的情况下，即使接触少量该化合物也可能对人类致命。

2. The research team identified a **lethal** mutation in the gene that prevented normal embryonic development in mice.
   研究团队在基因中发现了一种致死突变，阻碍了小鼠的正常胚胎发育。

3. Critics argued that the policy's **lethal** consequences for vulnerable populations had been grossly underestimated by officials.
   批评人士认为，官员们严重低估了该政策对弱势群体造成的致命后果。

把"lethal"与希腊神话中的"忘川（Lethe）"联系起来——致命的东西会把你送往忘川。
