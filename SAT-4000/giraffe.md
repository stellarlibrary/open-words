# 🦒 giraffe

🔊 /dʒəˈræf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A tall African mammal with a very long neck, long legs, and a distinctive spotted coat
**中文：** n. 长颈鹿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- camelopard（长颈鹿的旧称）
- ruminant（反刍动物）
- ungulate（有蹄类动物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a herd of giraffes / giraffe population / giraffe habitat / giraffe conservation**

👉 Example: The wildlife biologist spent three years studying giraffe behavior in the Serengeti ecosystem.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **giraffe** population in sub-Saharan Africa has declined significantly due to habitat loss and illegal poaching activities.
   由于栖息地丧失和非法偷猎活动，撒哈拉以南非洲的长颈鹿数量大幅下降。

2. Researchers discovered that the **giraffe** uses its remarkably long neck not only for feeding but also for thermoregulation.
   研究人员发现，长颈鹿利用其极长的脖子不仅用于进食，还用于体温调节。

3. Conservation organizations have classified the **giraffe** as a vulnerable species requiring urgent international protective measures.
   保护组织已将长颈鹿列为需要紧急国际保护措施的易危物种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "giraffe" as "g-i-raff" — imagine a giraffe so tall it reaches the rafters of a building.
把"giraffe"想成"g-i-raff（屋椽）"——想象一只长颈鹿高到能够到建筑物的屋椽。
