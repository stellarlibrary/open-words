# 🏥 syndrome

🔊 /ˈsɪndroʊm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A group of symptoms that consistently occur together; a set of concurrent conditions characterizing a disease or disorder
**中文：** n. 综合症

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- condition（病症）
- disorder（障碍）
- complex（综合征）
- symptom cluster（症状群）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Down syndrome / metabolic syndrome / chronic fatigue syndrome / suffer from a syndrome / diagnose a syndrome**

👉 Example: The doctor suspected the patient was exhibiting signs of a rare genetic syndrome.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers identified a new **syndrome** characterized by chronic inflammation, joint pain, and persistent fatigue in affected individuals.
   研究人员发现了一种新的综合症，其特征是受影响个体出现慢性炎症、关节疼痛和持续疲劳。

2. Impostor **syndrome** affects many high-achieving professionals who doubt their accomplishments despite abundant evidence of their competence.
   冒充者综合症影响着许多高成就的专业人士，尽管有充分的能力证据，他们仍怀疑自己的成就。

3. The medical team developed a comprehensive treatment protocol specifically designed to address the symptoms of this complex **syndrome**.
   医疗团队制定了一项专门针对这一复杂综合症症状的综合治疗方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "syn-drome" as "together-run" — multiple symptoms that run together as a group form a syndrome.
把"syndrome"想成"syn（一起）+ drome（跑）"——多种症状一起跑出来，便构成了综合症。
