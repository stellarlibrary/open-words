# 🔗 combination

🔊 /ˌkɒm.bɪˈneɪ.ʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The act or process of joining or merging two or more things together; a mixture or union of different elements; a sequence of numbers or letters used to open a lock.

**中文：** 结合；组合；联合。指将两个或多个事物合在一起的行为或过程；不同元素的混合或联合；也指用于开锁的数字或字母序列。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- blend（混合）
- union（联合）
- mixture（混合物）
- amalgamation（合并）
- synthesis（综合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** "a combination of / in combination with / winning combination" — used to describe the merging of elements that together produce a particular effect.

**Example:** The success of the project was attributed to a combination of innovative design and rigorous testing procedures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company discovered that a **combination** of two existing compounds produced a significantly more effective treatment for the disease.
   这家制药公司发现，两种现有化合物的**组合**产生了一种对该疾病显著更有效的治疗方法。

2. The architect employed a striking **combination** of traditional materials and contemporary design principles to create the award-winning structure.
   建筑师运用了传统材料与现代设计原则的绝妙**结合**，创造出了这座获奖建筑。

3. Effective leadership requires a **combination** of strategic vision, interpersonal skill, and the capacity to adapt to unforeseen challenges.
   有效的领导力需要战略眼光、人际交往能力和适应突发挑战能力的**结合**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Combine" + "-ation" (the act of) — the act of combining things. Think of a combination lock where you need to bring the right numbers together.

**中文：** "Combine"（结合）+ "-ation"（行为）——结合事物的行为。想想密码锁（combination lock），你需要把正确的数字"组合"在一起才能打开。
