# 🧬 recessive

🔊 /rɪˈsɛsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Tending to recede or go back; in genetics, describing a trait that is expressed only when two copies of the gene are present. (n.) A recessive trait or gene
**中文：** adj. 隐性的；逆行的；后退的 n. 隐性性状

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- latent（潜在的）
- dormant（休眠的）
- hidden（隐藏的）
- retreating（后退的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recessive gene / recessive trait / recessive allele / recessive characteristic**

👉 Example: Blue eye color is typically a recessive trait that requires both parents to carry the gene.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The genetic disorder is caused by a **recessive** allele, meaning both parents must be carriers for the condition to manifest.
   这种遗传疾病由隐性等位基因引起，意味着父母双方都必须是携带者才会表现出来。

2. Researchers discovered that the **recessive** gene responsible for the rare condition had persisted in the population for centuries.
   研究人员发现，导致这种罕见疾病的隐性基因已在该人群中存在了数百年。

3. In Mendel's famous experiments, the **recessive** traits reappeared in the second generation after being masked by dominant ones.
   在孟德尔著名的实验中，隐性性状在被显性性状掩盖后，在第二代中重新出现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "recessive" as "receding into the background" — a recessive gene hides behind the dominant one, only showing itself when no dominant gene is present.
把"recessive"想成"退到背后的"——隐性基因隐藏在显性基因后面，只有在没有显性基因时才会显现。
