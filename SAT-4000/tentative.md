# 🤔 tentative

🔊 /ˈtɛntətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not certain or fixed; provisional; done without confidence; hesitant.
**中文：** adj. 试验性的，暂定的；踌躇的，犹豫不决的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- provisional（暂定的）
- preliminary（初步的）
- hesitant（犹豫的）
- uncertain（不确定的）
- exploratory（探索性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tentative agreement / tentative plan / tentative steps / tentative conclusion / tentative schedule**

👉 Example: The committee reached a tentative agreement, subject to final approval from the board of directors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers presented their **tentative** findings at the conference, noting that further experimentation was necessary to confirm the results.
   研究人员在会议上展示了他们的初步发现，并指出需要进一步实验来确认结果。

2. After months of negotiation, the two corporations announced a **tentative** merger plan pending regulatory approval from federal authorities.
   经过数月谈判，两家公司宣布了一项暂定的合并计划，等待联邦当局的监管批准。

3. The young diplomat took **tentative** steps into the world of international relations, carefully observing established protocols before speaking.
   这位年轻的外交官小心翼翼地踏入国际关系领域，在发言前仔细观察既定规程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tentative" as "tent-ative" — like living in a tent, which is a temporary, uncertain arrangement, not a permanent home.
把"tentative"想成"tent（帐篷）-ative"——就像住在帐篷里，是一种临时的、不确定的安排，而不是永久的家。
