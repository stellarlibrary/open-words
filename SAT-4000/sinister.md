# 👿 sinister

🔊 /ˈsɪnɪstər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Giving the impression that something harmful or evil is happening or will happen; ominous; of or on the left side
**中文：** adj. 左边的；险恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ominous（不祥的）
- menacing（威胁的）
- malevolent（恶意的）
- foreboding（预兆的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sinister motive / sinister plot / sinister figure / sinister implications**

👉 Example: The detective suspected a sinister motive behind the seemingly accidental disappearance of the documents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The abandoned mansion had a **sinister** atmosphere that discouraged even the most adventurous visitors from entering its premises.
   这座废弃的大宅有一种阴森的气氛，使得最大胆的访客也不敢进入。

2. Investigators uncovered a **sinister** conspiracy among senior officials to manipulate the results of the national election.
   调查人员发现了高级官员之间操纵全国选举结果的险恶阴谋。

3. The sudden policy reversal carried **sinister** implications for civil liberties, alarming constitutional scholars and human rights advocates.
   这一突然的政策逆转对公民自由具有险恶的影响，令宪法学者和人权倡导者感到震惊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sinister" as coming from Latin "sinister" meaning "left" — in ancient times, the left side was considered unlucky and evil.
把"sinister"想成来自拉丁语"sinister"，意为"左边"——在古代，左边被认为是不吉利和邪恶的。
