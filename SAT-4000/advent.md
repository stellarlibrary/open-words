# 🌅 advent

🔊 /ˈæd.vɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The arrival or coming of an important event, person, or invention.
**中文：** 到来；出现；（重要事物的）降临

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arrival（到来）
- emergence（出现）
- onset（开始）
- dawn（黎明，开端）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the advent of + [noun/noun phrase]**

👉 Example: The advent of the internet transformed global communication.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **advent** of artificial intelligence has revolutionized numerous industries across the globe.
   人工智能的到来已经彻底改变了全球众多行业。

2. With the **advent** of modern medicine, life expectancy has increased significantly over the past century.
   随着现代医学的出现，过去一个世纪里人均寿命显著提高。

3. Scholars regard the **advent** of the printing press as a pivotal moment in human history.
   学者们将印刷术的到来视为人类历史上的关键时刻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "advent" as "ad- (to) + vent (come)" — something coming toward you, like an adventure arriving.
联想记忆：advent = ad（向）+ vent（来），即"向你走来"，表示重要事物的到来。
