# ❌ negate

🔊 /nɪˈɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To nullify, deny, or make ineffective; to render something invalid or void
**中文：** v. 否定；取消；使无效

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nullify（使无效）
- invalidate（使作废）
- contradict（反驳）
- deny（否认）
- neutralize（抵消）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **negate the effect / negate the impact / negate the possibility / negate the argument**

👉 Example: The new evidence effectively negated the defendant's alibi in the criminal trial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unexpected side effects of the medication **negated** the therapeutic benefits observed during the initial clinical trials.
   该药物意想不到的副作用抵消了在初始临床试验中观察到的治疗效果。

2. Critics argued that the proposed amendments would **negate** years of progress achieved through comprehensive legislative reform.
   批评者认为拟议的修正案将否定多年来通过全面立法改革取得的进展。

3. A single factual error in the report was sufficient to **negate** the credibility of the entire research study.
   报告中的一个事实错误就足以否定整个研究的可信度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "negate" as containing "neg" like "negative" — to negate something is to turn it into a negative, canceling it out.
把"negate"想成包含"neg"，类似"negative（否定的）"——否定某事就是把它变成否定的，将其取消。
