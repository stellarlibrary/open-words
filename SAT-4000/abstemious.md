# 🍃 abstemious

🔊 /əbˈstiːmiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Moderate or sparing, especially in eating and drinking; self-restrained and temperate
**中文：** adj. 有节制的；节约的；节省的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- temperate（有节制的）
- frugal（节俭的）
- moderate（适度的）
- austere（朴素的）
- restrained（克制的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abstemious lifestyle / abstemious habits / abstemious diet / remarkably abstemious**

👉 Example: The monk led an abstemious life, consuming only the simplest of meals each day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite the lavish banquet, the diplomat remained **abstemious** and ate only a modest portion of food.
   尽管宴会奢华，这位外交官依然保持节制，只吃了少量食物。

2. Her **abstemious** approach to spending allowed her to save enough money for an early retirement.
   她节俭的消费方式使她存够了提前退休的资金。

3. The physician recommended an **abstemious** diet to help the patient manage his chronic health condition.
   医生建议采取有节制的饮食来帮助患者控制其慢性健康问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "abstemious" as "abs-STEM-ious" — you STEM (stop) your desires, keeping everything moderate and controlled.
把"abstemious"想成"abs-STEM-ious"——你阻止(stem)自己的欲望，保持一切适度和节制。
