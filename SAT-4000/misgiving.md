# 😟 misgiving

🔊 /ˌmɪsˈɡɪvɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A feeling of doubt, worry, or apprehension about a future event or course of action
**中文：** n. 疑惧；疑虑；不安

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apprehension（忧虑）
- reservation（保留意见）
- qualm（疑虑）
- unease（不安）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have misgivings about / express misgivings / deep misgivings / without misgiving**

👉 Example: Several board members expressed serious misgivings about the proposed merger with the rival company.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite her **misgivings** about the experimental treatment, the patient decided to proceed after consulting multiple specialists.
   尽管对这种实验性治疗心存疑虑，这位患者在咨询了多位专家后决定继续进行。

2. The diplomat harbored deep **misgivings** regarding the peace accord, fearing it would fail to address fundamental territorial disputes.
   这位外交官对和平协议心存深深的疑虑，担心它无法解决根本性的领土争端。

3. Public **misgivings** about the safety of nuclear energy intensified significantly following the catastrophic reactor incident abroad.
   在国外灾难性的反应堆事故发生后，公众对核能安全的疑虑显著加剧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-giving" as your mind "giving" you "mis-signals" — warning you that something might go wrong.
把"misgiving"想成你的内心在"给（giving）"你"错误信号（mis-signals）"——警告你某事可能会出问题。
