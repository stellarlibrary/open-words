# 🔍 probe

🔊 /proʊb/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A thorough investigation; a slender instrument used for exploration. (v.) To investigate thoroughly; to examine or explore with an instrument
**中文：** n. 探针；探测器；调查 v. 探查，穿刺，查究

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- investigate（调查）
- examine（检查）
- inquiry（询问）
- scrutinize（仔细审查）
- exploration（探索）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **probe into / federal probe / space probe / probe the depths of**

👉 Example: The committee launched a probe into the misuse of public funds by government officials.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Federal investigators launched a comprehensive **probe** into allegations of financial fraud within the multinational corporation's executive leadership.
   联邦调查人员对这家跨国公司高管层的金融欺诈指控展开了全面调查。

2. The space **probe** transmitted invaluable data about the composition of Saturn's rings back to mission control on Earth.
   太空探测器将关于土星环成分的宝贵数据传回了地球上的任务控制中心。

3. The journalist continued to **probe** the senator's financial records, uncovering several previously undisclosed conflicts of interest.
   记者继续深入调查这位参议员的财务记录，揭露了几项此前未公开的利益冲突。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "probe" as a tool you push into the unknown — like a doctor's probe or a space probe, it goes deep to find what is hidden.
把"probe"想成一个你推入未知领域的工具——就像医生的探针或太空探测器，深入挖掘隐藏的东西。
