# 🛡️ shield

🔊 /ʃiːld/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A broad piece of armor carried for defense; a protective device or barrier. (v.) To protect or conceal from danger or harm
**中文：** v. 保护，防护，遮蔽 n. 防护物，护罩，盾，盾状物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- protect（保护）
- safeguard（防护）
- shelter（庇护）
- defend（保卫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shield from harm / shield against attack / shield the truth / act as a shield**

👉 Example: The dense canopy of trees shielded the hikers from the intense midday sun.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new legislation was designed to **shield** consumers from predatory lending practices in the financial industry.
   这项新法规旨在保护消费者免受金融行业掠夺性贷款行为的侵害。

2. The attorney argued that the corporation had deliberately **shielded** critical documents from regulatory investigators.
   律师辩称该公司故意向监管调查人员隐瞒了关键文件。

3. Earth's magnetic field acts as a **shield** against harmful solar radiation that would otherwise devastate all living organisms.
   地球的磁场充当了一道屏障，抵御有害的太阳辐射，否则这些辐射将摧毁所有生物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a knight's "shield" — it stands between you and danger, blocking anything harmful from reaching you.
想象骑士的"盾牌"——它挡在你和危险之间，阻止一切有害的东西接近你。
