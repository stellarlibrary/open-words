# 🌾 productive

🔊 /prəˈdʌktɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of producing; yielding favorable results; fertile; achieving a significant amount
**中文：** adj. 能生产的；生产性的；多产的；富有成效的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prolific（多产的）
- fruitful（富有成果的）
- efficient（高效的）
- fertile（肥沃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **productive work / productive meeting / productive member / productive land / highly productive**

👉 Example: The team had a highly productive session that resolved several critical design issues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research laboratory had an exceptionally **productive** year, publishing over thirty peer-reviewed articles in prestigious journals.
   该研究实验室度过了异常高产的一年，在知名期刊上发表了三十多篇同行评审文章。

2. Implementing flexible working hours proved to be a **productive** strategy for improving employee morale and overall output.
   实行弹性工作时间被证明是一种提高员工士气和整体产出的有效策略。

3. The volcanic soil in this region is remarkably **productive**, enabling farmers to cultivate three harvests of crops annually.
   该地区的火山土壤非常肥沃，使农民能够每年种植三季庄稼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "productive" as "product + ive" — having the quality of making products, always bringing results forward.
把"productive"想成"product（产品）+ ive（有……性质的）"——具有生产产品的特性，总是能带来成果。
