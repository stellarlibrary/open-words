# 🏔️ tundra

🔊 /ˈtʌndrə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A vast, flat, treeless Arctic region where the subsoil is permanently frozen; characterized by low temperatures, short growing seasons, and sparse vegetation
**中文：** 苔原；冻土地带

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- permafrost region（永冻土地区）
- arctic plain（北极平原）
- frozen wasteland（冰冻荒原）
- barren land（不毛之地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arctic tundra / alpine tundra / tundra ecosystem / tundra biome / tundra landscape**

👉 Example: The expedition team crossed hundreds of miles of frozen tundra before reaching the research station.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Climate scientists warn that the melting of **tundra** permafrost could release vast quantities of trapped methane into the atmosphere.
   气候科学家警告说，苔原永冻层的融化可能会将大量被困甲烷释放到大气中。

2. The **tundra** ecosystem supports a surprisingly diverse array of species that have adapted to its extreme environmental conditions.
   苔原生态系统支撑着种类繁多且令人惊讶的物种，它们已适应了极端的环境条件。

3. Indigenous communities have inhabited the **tundra** for thousands of years, developing remarkable strategies for survival in harsh climates.
   原住民社区在苔原上生活了数千年，发展出了在恶劣气候中生存的非凡策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tundra" as "tun-dra" sounding like "ton of dry-ah" — a ton of dry, frozen, barren land stretching endlessly across the Arctic.
把"tundra"想成发音像"ton of dry-ah"——大量干燥、冰冻、贫瘠的土地在北极无尽延伸。
