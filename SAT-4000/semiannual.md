# 📅 semiannual

🔊 /ˌsɛmiˈænjuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring twice a year; happening every half year
**中文：** adj. 每半年的；半年期的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- biannual（一年两次的）
- half-yearly（半年一次的）
- twice-yearly（每年两次的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **semiannual report / semiannual review / semiannual meeting / semiannual payment**

👉 Example: The board of directors convenes for a semiannual meeting to evaluate the company's strategic direction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The financial institution publishes a **semiannual** report that provides investors with comprehensive updates on portfolio performance.
   该金融机构发布半年度报告，向投资者提供投资组合业绩的全面更新。

2. Employees undergo a **semiannual** performance evaluation to ensure alignment with organizational goals and professional development standards.
   员工接受半年一次的绩效评估，以确保与组织目标和职业发展标准保持一致。

3. The university schedules **semiannual** faculty conferences in January and July to discuss curriculum revisions and research initiatives.
   大学安排在一月和七月举行半年一次的教师会议，讨论课程修订和研究计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "semi-annual" as "semi" (half) + "annual" (yearly) — something that happens at the halfway point of each year, so twice per year.
把"semiannual"拆成"semi"（半）+"annual"（年度的）——在每年的中间点发生一次，因此每年两次。
