# 🏰 medieval

🔊 /ˌmɛdɪˈiːvəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to the Middle Ages (roughly 5th–15th century); resembling or characteristic of the Middle Ages; old-fashioned or primitive
**中文：** adj. 中世纪的；仿中世纪的；老式的，原始的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- archaic（古老的）
- antiquated（过时的）
- feudal（封建的）
- primitive（原始的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **medieval period / medieval times / medieval castle / medieval practices**

👉 Example: The professor specialized in medieval European history and its lasting cultural influence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architecture of the cathedral reflects a distinctly **medieval** style that has attracted scholars from around the world.
   这座大教堂的建筑风格反映了鲜明的中世纪特色，吸引了世界各地的学者。

2. Critics described the country's judicial system as **medieval** due to its harsh and outdated methods of punishment.
   批评者称该国的司法制度为中世纪式的，因为其惩罚方式严厉且过时。

3. The museum's collection of **medieval** manuscripts provides invaluable insight into the intellectual life of that era.
   博物馆收藏的中世纪手稿为了解那个时代的知识生活提供了宝贵的视角。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "medi-eval" as "middle ageeval(uation)" — evaluating something from the middle ages, a time of castles and knights.
把"medieval"想成"medi（中间）+eval（评估）"——对中世纪的事物进行评估，那个城堡与骑士的时代。
