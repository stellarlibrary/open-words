# 🌀 hurricane

🔊 /ˈhʌrɪkeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A severe tropical storm with violent winds exceeding 74 mph, typically forming over warm ocean waters; a violent windstorm
**中文：** n. 飓风；暴风；狂风

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- typhoon（台风）
- cyclone（气旋）
- tempest（暴风雨）
- gale（大风）
- storm（风暴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hurricane season / hurricane warning / the eye of the hurricane / Category 5 hurricane**

👉 Example: Residents along the coast were evacuated before the hurricane made landfall late Thursday evening.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The devastating **hurricane** caused billions of dollars in structural damage and displaced thousands of families across the region.
   这场毁灭性的飓风造成了数十亿美元的结构性损失，并使该地区数千个家庭流离失所。

2. Climate scientists warn that rising ocean temperatures will likely increase the frequency and intensity of **hurricane** activity.
   气候科学家警告说，海洋温度上升可能会增加飓风活动的频率和强度。

3. The emergency response team worked tirelessly to restore essential services in communities devastated by the powerful **hurricane**.
   应急救援队不知疲倦地工作，为遭受强飓风破坏的社区恢复基本服务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hurricane" as "hurry + cane" — the wind hurries so fast it could knock a cane out of your hand, like a violent spinning storm.
把"hurricane"想成"hurry + cane"——风急速地吹来，能把你手中的拐杖吹走，就像一场猛烈的旋转风暴。
