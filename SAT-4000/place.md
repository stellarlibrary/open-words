# 📍 place

🔊 /pleɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular position, location, or area; a seat or accommodation; a numerical position in a sequence. (v.) To put in a particular position; to appoint; to identify or classify
**中文：** n. 地方；住所；座位；数（位数） v. 放置；任命；寄予

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- location（位置）
- position（地点）
- site（场所）
- situate（安置）
- arrange（安排）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **place an order / place emphasis on / in place of / take place / place value / decimal place**

👉 Example: The mathematician asked the students to round the answer to the nearest decimal place.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee decided to **place** greater emphasis on environmental sustainability when evaluating proposals for the new development project.
   委员会决定在评估新开发项目提案时更加重视环境可持续性。

2. Archaeologists identified the ancient ruins as a sacred **place** of worship that had served the community for several centuries.
   考古学家确认这些古代遗迹是一个服务社区数百年的神圣礼拜场所。

3. The student calculated the answer to three decimal **places**, ensuring the precision required by the advanced physics assignment.
   这名学生将答案计算到小数点后三位，确保了高级物理作业所要求的精确度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "place" as one of the most versatile words in English — every thing has its place, whether it is a physical spot, a rank, or a decimal position.
把"place"想成英语中最通用的词之一——每样东西都有它的place，无论是物理位置、排名还是小数位。
