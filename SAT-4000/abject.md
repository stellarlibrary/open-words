# 💔 abject

🔊 /ˈæbdʒɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely bad, wretched, or degrading; showing utter hopelessness or resignation; contemptible and base
**中文：** adj. 凄惨的，绝望的；卑鄙的，卑贱的；可怜的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wretched（悲惨的）
- miserable（凄惨的）
- degrading（有辱人格的）
- contemptible（卑劣的）
- pitiful（可怜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abject poverty / abject failure / abject misery / abject apology / abject terror**

👉 Example: Millions of families continue to live in abject poverty despite decades of international aid programs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The humanitarian report revealed that over two million refugees were living in conditions of **abject** poverty.
   人道主义报告揭示，超过两百万难民生活在极度贫困的条件下。

2. The military campaign ended in **abject** failure, with the invading forces retreating in complete disarray.
   这场军事行动以彻底失败告终，入侵部队全面溃退，一片混乱。

3. He offered an **abject** apology before the board, acknowledging his serious errors in professional judgment.
   他在董事会面前做了深切的道歉，承认了自己在职业判断上的严重失误。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abject" = "ab-" (away from) + "ject" (throw, as in "reject"). Someone abject has been thrown down to the lowest state.
"Abject" = "ab-"（远离）+ "ject"（扔，如"reject"）。一个abject的人就是被抛到最低状态的人。
