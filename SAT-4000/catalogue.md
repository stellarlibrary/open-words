# 📖 catalogue

🔊 /ˈkætəlɒɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A complete, systematic list of items or entries; a university prospectus or directory. (v.) To enter items into an organized list or record
**中文：** n. 目录；目录册；大学情况一览 v. 把…编入目录

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inventory（库存清单）
- register（登记簿）
- directory（名录）
- index（索引）
- list（列表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **catalogue raisonné / exhibition catalogue / catalogue of errors / catalogue a library**

👉 Example: The gallery published a comprehensive catalogue for its annual contemporary art exhibition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archivist meticulously compiled a **catalogue** of every historical document stored within the national archives dating back centuries.
   档案管理员一丝不苟地编制了国家档案馆中每份可追溯至几个世纪前的历史文献目录。

2. The university's academic **catalogue** provides detailed descriptions of all courses, degree requirements, and institutional policies for enrollment.
   大学的学术目录提供了所有课程、学位要求和入学制度政策的详细说明。

3. Researchers undertook the ambitious project of creating a digital **catalogue** that would preserve the library's rare manuscript collection.
   研究人员开展了一项雄心勃勃的项目，创建一个数字目录来保存图书馆的珍稀手稿收藏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "catalogue" as "cat-a-logue" — a cat that keeps a dialogue (logue) about every item, listing them one by one in perfect order.
把"catalogue"想成"cat-a-logue"——一只猫对每件物品进行对话（logue），按完美顺序逐一列出它们。
