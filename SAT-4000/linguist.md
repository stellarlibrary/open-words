# 🧑‍🏫 linguist

🔊 /ˈlɪŋɡwɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who studies linguistics; a person skilled in or knowledgeable about languages.
**中文：** n. 语言学家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- philologist（语文学家）
- polyglot（通晓多种语言的人）
- etymologist（词源学家）
- grammarian（语法学家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a renowned linguist / computational linguist / applied linguist / trained linguist**

👉 Example: The renowned linguist delivered a lecture on the origins of Indo-European languages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The acclaimed **linguist** published a comprehensive analysis of syntactic structures found across unrelated language families worldwide.
   这位著名的语言学家发表了一篇关于全球无关语系中句法结构的综合分析。

2. As a trained **linguist**, she was able to decipher the ancient inscriptions that had puzzled scholars for over a century.
   作为一名受过训练的语言学家，她能够破译困扰学者们一个多世纪的古代铭文。

3. The computational **linguist** developed sophisticated algorithms that significantly improved machine translation accuracy between major world languages.
   这位计算语言学家开发了先进的算法，显著提高了主要世界语言之间机器翻译的准确性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lingua (tongue/language) + ist (person)" — a person who specializes in the study of language and tongues.
把"linguist"拆分为"lingua（语言）+ ist（从事……的人）"——专门研究语言的人。
