# 😭 bawl

🔊 /bɔːl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To shout or cry loudly and unrestrainedly; to weep noisily. (n.) A loud, unrestrained shout or cry
**中文：** v. 大叫；放声痛哭 n. 叫骂声

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wail（嚎啕大哭）
- howl（嚎叫）
- bellow（吼叫）
- sob（呜咽）
- shriek（尖叫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bawl out (someone) / bawl one's eyes out / bawl in frustration / bawl loudly**

👉 Example: The coach bawled out the players for their careless mistakes during the championship match.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The child began to **bawl** uncontrollably after dropping the ice cream cone on the hot pavement outside the shop.
   那个孩子把冰激凌掉在商店外炎热的人行道上后开始失控地放声大哭。

2. The drill sergeant would routinely **bawl** commands at the recruits during their exhausting pre-dawn training exercises.
   教官在令人疲惫的黎明前训练中经常对新兵大声吼叫命令。

3. Overwhelmed by the unexpected recognition, the award recipient could not help but **bawl** with tears of pure joy.
   获奖者被意想不到的认可所感动，忍不住喜极而泣，放声大哭。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bawl" as rhyming with "ball" — imagine a child bawling their eyes out after losing a ball, crying loudly.
把"bawl"想成和"ball"（球）押韵——想象一个孩子丢了球后放声大哭。
