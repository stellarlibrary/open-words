# 😤 importunate

🔊 /ɪmˈpɔːrtʃənət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Persistent and demanding in a way that is annoying or intrusive; making repeated and urgent requests
**中文：** adj. 强求的，纠缠不休的；急切的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- persistent（执着的）
- insistent（坚持的）
- relentless（不懈的）
- demanding（苛求的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **importunate requests / importunate demands / importunate creditors / importunate pleas**

👉 Example: The manager grew weary of the importunate salesman who called the office every single day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat found it difficult to concentrate on negotiations while surrounded by **importunate** journalists demanding exclusive statements and interviews.
   这位外交官发现很难集中精力进行谈判，因为周围都是纠缠不休、要求独家声明和采访的记者。

2. The charity received numerous **importunate** letters from applicants who desperately sought financial assistance for their educational pursuits.
   该慈善机构收到了大量来自申请者急切的信件，他们迫切寻求教育资助。

3. Despite the **importunate** appeals from lobbyists, the committee remained firm in its decision to reject the proposed legislation.
   尽管游说者不断纠缠恳求，委员会仍坚定地决定否决拟议的立法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "IMPORTUNATE" as "I'M-PORT-YOU-NATE" — imagine someone at a port who won't stop nagging you, urgently demanding passage on your ship.
把"importunate"想成一个人在港口（port）不停地纠缠你，急切地要求搭乘你的船——不断强求，令人烦恼。
