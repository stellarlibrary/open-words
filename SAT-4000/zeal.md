# 🔥 zeal

🔊 /ziːl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Great energy, enthusiasm, or devotion in pursuit of a cause, ideal, or goal
**中文：** 热情；热心；热诚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fervor（热情）
- enthusiasm（热忱）
- passion（激情）
- ardor（热心）
- devotion（奉献）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with great zeal / missionary zeal / reforming zeal / zeal for justice**

👉 Example: The young activist pursued environmental reform with remarkable zeal that inspired her entire community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new prosecutor attacked corruption cases with extraordinary **zeal**, earning widespread admiration from citizens and legal scholars alike.
   新任检察官以非凡的热情打击腐败案件，赢得了市民和法律学者的广泛赞誉。

2. Her **zeal** for scientific discovery drove her to spend countless hours in the laboratory refining her groundbreaking research.
   她对科学发现的热忱驱使她在实验室里花费无数小时来完善她的开创性研究。

3. While admirable in moderation, excessive **zeal** can sometimes blind individuals to the unintended consequences of their actions.
   虽然适度的热情值得赞赏，但过度的狂热有时会使人对其行为的意外后果视而不见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "zeal" as sounding like "seal" — imagine a seal clapping its flippers with great energy and enthusiasm.
把"zeal"想成听起来像"seal"（海豹）——想象一只海豹充满热情和活力地拍打鳍状肢。
