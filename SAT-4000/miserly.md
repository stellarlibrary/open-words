# 💸 miserly

🔊 /ˈmaɪzərli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unwilling to spend money; extremely stingy or greedy in the use of resources
**中文：** adj. 吝啬的；贪婪的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stingy（吝啬的）
- parsimonious（过分节俭的）
- niggardly（小气的）
- tightfisted（吝啬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **miserly attitude / miserly sum / miserly habits / miserly with resources**

👉 Example: The miserly landlord refused to install heating despite the freezing winter temperatures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board's **miserly** allocation of funds left the research department unable to pursue any meaningful scientific inquiry.
   董事会吝啬的资金分配使研究部门无法进行任何有意义的科学探究。

2. His **miserly** approach to philanthropy was widely criticized, given that his personal fortune exceeded several billion dollars.
   鉴于他的个人财富超过数十亿美元，他对慈善事业的吝啬态度受到广泛批评。

3. The government offered only a **miserly** increase in education spending, far below what teachers and administrators had requested.
   政府仅提供了微不足道的教育支出增长，远低于教师和行政人员的要求。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "miserly" as "miser" + "-ly" — behaving like a miser, someone who clings to every coin and dreads spending.
把"miserly"想成"miser（守财奴）"加"-ly"——像守财奴一样行事，紧抓每一分钱，害怕花钱。
