# 📐 parallelogram

🔊 /ˌpærəˈlɛləˌɡræm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A four-sided plane figure with opposite sides that are parallel and equal in length
**中文：** n. 平行四边形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quadrilateral（四边形）
- rhomboid（斜方形）
- rectangle（矩形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **area of a parallelogram / properties of a parallelogram / base and height of a parallelogram**

👉 Example: The area of a parallelogram is calculated by multiplying its base by its height.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematics instructor explained that every rectangle is technically a **parallelogram**, but not every parallelogram qualifies as a rectangle.
   数学老师解释说，每个矩形在技术上都是平行四边形，但并非每个平行四边形都是矩形。

2. Students were asked to calculate the area of a **parallelogram** with a base of twelve centimeters and a height of eight centimeters.
   学生们被要求计算一个底为十二厘米、高为八厘米的平行四边形的面积。

3. The architect incorporated **parallelogram** shapes into the building facade to create a dynamic and visually striking geometric pattern.
   建筑师将平行四边形形状融入建筑立面，创造出富有动感和视觉冲击力的几何图案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "parallel-o-gram" as a shape drawn ("gram") with parallel sides — the word literally tells you it is a figure made of parallel lines.
把"parallelogram"想成用平行边画出来（"gram"）的图形——这个词字面意思就告诉你它是由平行线构成的图形。
