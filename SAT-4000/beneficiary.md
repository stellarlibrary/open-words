# 🎁 beneficiary

🔊 /ˌbɛnɪˈfɪʃiˌɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who receives benefits, profits, or advantages, especially from a will, trust, or insurance policy. (adj.) Holding something in trust or by feudal grant
**中文：** n. 受益人；受惠者；封臣 adj. 拥有封地的；受圣俸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recipient（接受者）
- heir（继承人）
- legatee（遗产受赠人）
- inheritor（继承者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the primary beneficiary / a beneficiary of / named beneficiary / the chief beneficiary**

👉 Example: She was listed as the sole beneficiary of her late uncle's substantial estate and trust fund.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship's **beneficiary** expressed profound gratitude for the opportunity to pursue higher education without financial burden.
   该奖学金的受益人对能够无经济负担地接受高等教育的机会表示了深切的感激。

2. As the primary **beneficiary** of the new trade agreement, the developing nation experienced unprecedented economic growth.
   作为新贸易协定的主要受惠者，这个发展中国家经历了前所未有的经济增长。

3. The insurance company required the policyholder to designate at least one **beneficiary** before finalizing the coverage terms.
   保险公司要求投保人在最终确定保险条款之前指定至少一名受益人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bene-" (good) + "-ficiary" (one who receives) — a beneficiary is "one who receives good things" or benefits.
"Bene-"（好的）+"ficiary"（接受者）——beneficiary就是"接受好处的人"，即受益人。
