# 🫧 amorphous

🔊 /əˈmɔːrfəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Without a clearly defined shape, form, or structure; lacking organization.
**中文：** 无定形的；无组织的；非晶形的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shapeless（无形状的）
- formless（无定形的）
- unstructured（无结构的）
- nebulous（模糊的）
- vague（含糊的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mass, blob, shape, structure, substance, concept**
👉 Example: The committee presented an amorphous plan that lacked clear objectives.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist examined the **amorphous** substance under the microscope to determine its molecular composition.
   科学家在显微镜下检查了这种无定形物质以确定其分子组成。

2. His **amorphous** ideas about educational reform failed to gain support from the governing board.
   他关于教育改革的模糊想法未能获得管理委员会的支持。

3. The **amorphous** cloud of gas gradually condensed into a recognizable celestial structure over millennia.
   这团无定形的气体云在数千年间逐渐凝聚成可辨识的天体结构。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"A-" (without) + "morph" (form/shape) = without form, amorphous.
"A-"（没有）+ "morph"（形状）= 没有形状的，即无定形的。
