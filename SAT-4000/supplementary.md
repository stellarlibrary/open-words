# 📎 supplementary

🔊 /ˌsʌplɪˈmentəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Provided in addition to what is already present or available; serving to supplement. (n.) A person or thing that supplements
**中文：** adj. 增补的，补充的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- additional（额外的）
- complementary（补充的）
- auxiliary（辅助的）
- ancillary（附属的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supplementary materials / supplementary income / supplementary angle / supplementary information**

👉 Example: The professor distributed supplementary readings to help students prepare for the final examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee requested **supplementary** documentation before making a final decision on the proposed research funding.
   委员会在对拟议的研究经费做出最终决定之前要求提供补充文件。

2. In geometry, two angles are called **supplementary** when their measures combine to equal exactly one hundred eighty degrees.
   在几何学中，当两个角的度数之和恰好等于一百八十度时，它们被称为补角。

3. The government allocated **supplementary** funds to address the unexpected surge in demand for public healthcare services.
   政府拨出了补充资金，以应对公共医疗服务需求的意外激增。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "supplement-ary" as "supplement + carry" — it carries extra material to supplement what already exists.
把"supplementary"想成"supplement + carry"——它携带额外的材料来补充已有的内容。
