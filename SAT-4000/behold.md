# 👁 behold

🔊 /bɪˈhoʊld/<br>
🏷️ *verb, interjection | 动词, 感叹词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To see or observe with attention; to gaze upon. (int.) Look! See!
**中文：** v. 看；注视；目睹 int. 瞧；看呀

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- observe（观察）
- witness（目睹）
- gaze upon（注视）
- perceive（感知）
- contemplate（凝视）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **behold the spectacle / a sight to behold / lo and behold / beholden to**

👉 Example: Lo and behold, the missing manuscript was discovered hidden behind a false wall in the library.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Visitors to the ancient temple could **behold** intricate carvings that had endured for more than two millennia.
   参观古庙的游客可以目睹已经存续两千多年的精美雕刻。

2. The graduating class paused to **behold** the vast campus one final time before departing for their careers.
   毕业班的学生们在离校开始职业生涯前，驻足最后一次凝望辽阔的校园。

3. The astronomical phenomenon was truly a sight to **behold**, drawing thousands of enthusiastic observers worldwide.
   这一天文现象确实是一道壮观的景象，吸引了全球数千名热情的观测者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-hold" — to hold something in your vision, to keep your eyes fixed on it and truly see it.
把"behold"想成"be-hold"——用目光"抓住"某物，让眼睛紧紧盯着它，真正地看见它。
