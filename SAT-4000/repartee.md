# 💬 repartee

🔊 /ˌrepɑːˈtiː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Quick, witty, and clever conversation or reply; the skill of making sharp and humorous responses
**中文：** n. 机敏的应答；妙语；巧辩

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- banter（打趣）
- wit（机智）
- riposte（巧妙回击）
- quip（妙语）
- wordplay（文字游戏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **witty repartee / sharp repartee / engage in repartee / a gift for repartee**

👉 Example: The talk show host was famous for her sharp repartee with celebrity guests.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The lively **repartee** between the two debaters kept the audience thoroughly entertained throughout the entire evening session.
   两位辩手之间生动的巧辩让观众在整个晚间会议中都兴致盎然。

2. Oscar Wilde was celebrated for his brilliant **repartee**, and his witty remarks continue to be quoted extensively today.
   奥斯卡·王尔德以其出色的妙语著称，他的机智言论至今仍被广泛引用。

3. The diplomat's gift for **repartee** enabled her to defuse tense negotiations with well-timed humor and incisive observations.
   这位外交官的巧辩才能使她能够用恰到好处的幽默和敏锐的观察化解紧张的谈判。

把"repartee"想成"re-PARTY"——快速而巧妙的对话来回交锋，就像在热闹的派对上一样。
