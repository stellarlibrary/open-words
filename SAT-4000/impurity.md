# 🧪 impurity

🔊 /ɪmˈpjʊərɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being impure; a substance that contaminates or diminishes the purity of something; an unwanted foreign element
**中文：** n. 杂质；不纯；不洁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contaminant（污染物）
- adulterant（掺杂物）
- pollutant（污染物质）
- foreign matter（异物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remove impurities / trace impurities / chemical impurity / impurity in water / free from impurity**

👉 Example: The filtration system was designed to remove all impurities from the drinking water supply.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advanced semiconductor manufacturing requires the removal of even the smallest **impurity** from silicon wafers to ensure optimal performance.
   先进的半导体制造需要从硅片中去除即使是最微小的杂质，以确保最佳性能。

2. The presence of a single **impurity** in the chemical compound was sufficient to compromise the accuracy of the entire laboratory experiment.
   化合物中仅一种杂质的存在就足以影响整个实验室实验的准确性。

3. Water treatment facilities employ multiple filtration stages to eliminate harmful **impurities** before the water reaches residential consumers.
   水处理设施采用多级过滤来消除有害杂质，然后再将水输送到居民用户。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impurity" as "im + purity" — the opposite of purity, something that makes a substance no longer clean or pure.
把"impurity"想成"im（否定前缀）+ purity（纯净）"——纯净的反面，即杂质或不纯。
