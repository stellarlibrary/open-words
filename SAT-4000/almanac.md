# 📅 almanac

🔊 /ˈɔːlmənæk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An annual publication containing a calendar, weather forecasts, astronomical data, and other useful information.
**中文：** 年鉴；历书；年历

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- yearbook（年鉴）
- annual（年刊）
- calendar（日历）
- register（登记簿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **farmer's almanac, publish an almanac, consult the almanac, annual almanac**
👉 Example: The farmer consulted the almanac to determine the best planting dates for spring crops.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **almanac** predicted an unusually harsh winter, prompting residents to prepare additional fuel supplies.
   年历预测了一个异常寒冷的冬天，促使居民准备了额外的燃料储备。

2. Sailors in the eighteenth century relied upon the nautical **almanac** for celestial navigation across open seas.
   十八世纪的水手们依靠航海年历在公海上进行天文导航。

3. She discovered her grandfather's handwritten notes in the margins of a century-old **almanac** from the attic.
   她在阁楼上一本百年历书的页边发现了祖父的手写笔记。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "all + man + ac(k)" — a book that has ALL the information a MAN needs to ACKnowledge throughout the year.
把 almanac 拆分成 "all（所有）+ man（人）+ ac"——一本包含所有人全年所需信息的书，就是年鉴。
