# 🔥 enthusiast

🔊 /ɪnˈθuːziæst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who is intensely passionate or eager about a particular subject or activity; a devotee
**中文：** n. 狂热者；热心家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- devotee（热爱者）
- aficionado（狂热爱好者）
- fanatic（狂热者）
- zealot（狂热分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sports enthusiast / music enthusiast / an enthusiast of / keen enthusiast / outdoor enthusiast**

👉 Example: As a lifelong aviation enthusiast, he attended every air show within driving distance of his home.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technology **enthusiast** spent countless hours examining the latest innovations presented at the annual electronics exposition.
   这位技术狂热者花了无数小时研究年度电子博览会上展示的最新创新成果。

2. Art **enthusiasts** from across the continent gathered to witness the unveiling of the restored Renaissance masterpiece at the museum.
   来自整个大陆的艺术爱好者齐聚一堂，见证博物馆修复后的文艺复兴杰作的揭幕。

3. As a dedicated fitness **enthusiast**, she maintained a rigorous training schedule regardless of weather conditions or personal obligations.
   作为一名忠实的健身爱好者，无论天气状况或个人事务如何，她都坚持严格的训练计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "enthusi-ast" — the suffix "-ast" sounds like "fast," and an enthusiast moves fast toward what they love with burning passion.
把"enthusiast"想成"enthusiasm（热情）的人"——充满热情的人就是狂热者。
