# 💠 cameo

🔊 /ˈkæmioʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A small piece of jewelry with a carved raised design; a brief but notable appearance by a famous person in a film or play; a short descriptive literary sketch
**中文：** n. 刻有浮雕的宝石或贝壳；（影视剧中的）客串配角；小品文

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relief carving（浮雕）
- guest appearance（客串）
- vignette（小品文）
- miniature（微型作品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cameo appearance / cameo role / cameo brooch / make a cameo / cameo portrait**

👉 Example: The renowned director made a brief cameo appearance in the opening scene of his latest film.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The antique **cameo** brooch, carved from a single piece of shell, was appraised at several thousand dollars by experts.
   这枚由单片贝壳雕刻而成的古董浮雕胸针被专家估价数千美元。

2. The celebrated actress made a memorable **cameo** in the independent film, elevating its profile and critical reception significantly.
   这位著名女演员在独立电影中客串了一个令人难忘的角色，大大提升了影片的知名度和评价。

3. The author's literary **cameo** of nineteenth-century Parisian life captured the era's atmosphere with remarkable precision and elegance.
   作者关于十九世纪巴黎生活的小品文以惊人的精确和优雅捕捉了那个时代的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cameo" as "came-o" — a famous person "came, oh!" for just a brief moment, like a quick guest appearance carved into memory.
把"cameo"想成"came-o"——一个名人"来了，哦！"只有短暂的一瞬间，就像一个刻入记忆的短暂亮相。
