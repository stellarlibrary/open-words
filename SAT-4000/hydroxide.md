# 🧫 hydroxide

🔊 /haɪˈdrɒksaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A chemical compound or ion containing one oxygen atom bonded to one hydrogen atom (OH⁻); commonly found in bases and alkaline solutions
**中文：** n. 氢氧化物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alkali compound（碱性化合物）
- base（碱）
- OH group（羟基）
- caustic compound（苛性化合物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sodium hydroxide / calcium hydroxide / potassium hydroxide / hydroxide ion / metal hydroxide**

👉 Example: Sodium hydroxide is one of the most commonly used industrial chemicals for manufacturing soap and paper.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student carefully measured the concentration of **hydroxide** ions in the solution using a precise titration technique.
   学生使用精确的滴定技术仔细测量了溶液中氢氧化物离子的浓度。

2. Calcium **hydroxide** is widely employed in water treatment facilities to adjust acidity levels and remove harmful impurities.
   氢氧化钙被广泛用于水处理设施中，以调节酸度水平并去除有害杂质。

3. The reaction between an acid and a metal **hydroxide** produces a salt and water in a classic neutralization process.
   酸与金属氢氧化物之间的反应在经典的中和过程中生成盐和水。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydr-oxide" as "hydrogen-oxygen" — the hydroxide ion is simply one hydrogen atom bonded to one oxygen atom.
把"hydroxide"想成"hydr"（氢）+"oxide"（氧化物）——氢氧化物就是一个氢原子与一个氧原子结合而成。
