# 🧱 constituent

🔊 /kənˈstɪtʃuənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A component part or element; a voter in a particular electoral district. (adj.) Forming part of a whole; having the power to appoint or elect
**中文：** n. 成分，要素；选民 adj. 构成的，组成的；选举的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- component（组成部分）
- element（要素）
- ingredient（成分）
- voter（选民）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **constituent parts / constituent elements / constituent assembly / serve one's constituents**

👉 Example: The senator held a town hall meeting to address the concerns raised by her constituents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists identified the key **constituent** elements of the newly discovered compound through extensive spectrometric analysis.
   科学家们通过广泛的光谱分析确定了新发现化合物的关键组成元素。

2. The elected representative pledged to prioritize the healthcare concerns of her **constituents** above all partisan interests.
   这位民选代表承诺将选民的医疗保健问题置于所有党派利益之上。

3. Water is a fundamental **constituent** of all living organisms and plays a critical role in sustaining biological processes.
   水是所有生物体的基本组成部分，在维持生物过程中起着至关重要的作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "constitute-ent" — a constituent is something that helps "constitute" or make up a larger whole, like building blocks.
把"constituent"想成"constitute（构成）+ ent"——构成要素就是帮助"构成"更大整体的东西，就像积木一样。
