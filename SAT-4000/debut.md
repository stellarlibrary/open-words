# 🎭 debut

🔊 /deɪˈbjuː/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A first public appearance or performance. (v.) To make a first public appearance or to introduce for the first time
**中文：** n. 初次登台；开张 v. 初次登台；首次亮相

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- premiere（首映）
- introduction（引入）
- launch（发布）
- first appearance（首次亮相）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make one's debut / debut album / debut performance / debut novel**

👉 Example: The young soprano made her debut at the Metropolitan Opera to thunderous applause from the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The acclaimed novelist's **debut** work received widespread critical praise and earned several prestigious literary awards that year.
   这位备受赞誉的小说家的处女作获得了广泛的好评，并在当年赢得了多个著名文学奖项。

2. The technology company plans to **debut** its revolutionary artificial intelligence platform at the international conference next month.
   这家科技公司计划在下个月的国际会议上首次推出其革命性的人工智能平台。

3. After years of rigorous training, the ballet dancer finally made her professional **debut** on the world's most celebrated stage.
   经过多年严格训练，这位芭蕾舞演员终于在世界上最著名的舞台上完成了职业首秀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "debut" as coming from French "débuter" meaning to begin — it marks the very first time something or someone appears publicly.
"debut"来自法语"débuter"意为开始——它标志着某事或某人首次公开亮相。
