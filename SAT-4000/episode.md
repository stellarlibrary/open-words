# 🎬 episode

🔊 /ˈepɪsoʊd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular incident or event; a distinct segment of a narrative or series; a period of time marked by a notable occurrence
**中文：** n. 插曲；一段情节；一集

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incident（事件）
- occurrence（事件）
- installment（一集）
- chapter（章节）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a brief episode / a turbulent episode / episode of illness / episode in history**

👉 Example: The Cuban Missile Crisis remains one of the most dramatic episodes in Cold War history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomatic crisis proved to be a brief but consequential **episode** that permanently altered relations between the two nations.
   这场外交危机是一段短暂但影响深远的**插曲**，永久地改变了两国之间的关系。

2. The patient experienced a severe **episode** of cardiac arrhythmia that required immediate medical intervention at the hospital.
   患者经历了一次严重的心律不齐**发作**，需要在医院进行紧急医疗干预。

3. Each **episode** of the documentary series examines a different civilization that flourished and declined in the ancient world.
   纪录片系列的每一**集**都考察了一个在古代世界兴盛和衰落的不同文明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EPISODE" as "epi (upon) + sode (road)" — an event that happens upon the road of life, a notable stop along the way.
把"episode"想成旅途中的一站——在人生的道路上发生的一个值得注意的事件或片段。
