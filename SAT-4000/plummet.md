# 📉 plummet

🔊 /ˈplʌmɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A plumb bob or lead weight used for measuring depth. (v.) To fall or drop straight down rapidly; to decrease sharply in value or amount.
**中文：** n. 铅锤，坠子 v. 垂直落下；（价格、水平等）骤然下跌

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plunge（骤降）
- nosedive（急剧下降）
- tumble（暴跌）
- drop（下落）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plummet to the ground / prices plummet / temperatures plummet / plummet sharply**

👉 Example: Stock prices plummeted after the company announced unexpectedly poor quarterly earnings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Housing prices began to **plummet** across the region as the economic recession deepened throughout the fiscal year.
   随着经济衰退在整个财政年度加深，该地区房价开始骤降。

2. The aircraft lost engine power and **plummeted** toward the ocean before the pilot regained control at low altitude.
   飞机失去发动机动力后向海洋垂直坠落，飞行员在低空恢复了控制。

3. Public confidence in the administration **plummeted** following revelations of widespread corruption among senior government officials.
   在高级政府官员大范围腐败被揭露后，公众对政府的信心骤然下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plummet" as "plumb" + "met the ground" — a lead weight (plumb) falling straight down and meeting the ground with a crash.
把"plummet"想成"plumb"（铅锤）+"met the ground"（撞到地面）——铅锤笔直落下撞到地面。
