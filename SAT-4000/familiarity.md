# 🤝 familiarity

🔊 /fəˌmɪliˈærəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Close acquaintance with or thorough knowledge of something; the quality of being well known; relaxed friendliness or intimacy
**中文：** n. 熟悉，精通；亲密；随便

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acquaintance（了解）
- intimacy（亲密）
- closeness（亲近）
- awareness（认知）
- proficiency（精通）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **familiarity with / familiarity breeds contempt / gain familiarity / growing familiarity**

👉 Example: Her familiarity with the subject matter made her the ideal candidate for the position.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate's deep **familiarity** with international trade regulations distinguished her from the other applicants for the diplomatic post.
   这位候选人对国际贸易法规的深入了解使她在申请外交职位时脱颖而出。

2. As the proverb warns, excessive **familiarity** between colleagues can sometimes breed contempt and undermine professional boundaries.
   正如谚语所警告的那样，同事之间过度的亲密有时会滋生轻视并破坏职业界限。

3. Students must develop a thorough **familiarity** with primary source materials before attempting to construct original research arguments.
   学生在尝试构建原创研究论点之前，必须对原始资料建立全面的熟悉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "familiar + ity" — the state of being familiar with someone or something, like knowing your own family well.
把"familiarity"想成"familiar（熟悉的）+ ity（状态）"——就像了解自己的"family（家人）"一样熟悉的状态。
