# 💎 lucid

🔊 /ˈluːsɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Expressed clearly and easy to understand; bright or luminous; mentally clear and rational
**中文：** adj. 明晰的；透明的；易懂的；头脑清楚的；清澈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clear（清晰的）
- coherent（连贯的）
- transparent（透明的）
- intelligible（易理解的）
- pellucid（清澈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lucid explanation / lucid dream / lucid moment / lucid prose / lucid argument**

👉 Example: Despite her high fever, the patient had a brief lucid interval during which she recognized her family.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's **lucid** prose style made even the most abstract philosophical concepts accessible to a general readership.
   作者清晰的散文风格使得即使是最抽象的哲学概念也能为普通读者所理解。

2. During his **lucid** moments, the elderly scholar was still capable of offering profound insights into quantum mechanics.
   在头脑清醒的时刻，这位年迈的学者仍然能够对量子力学提出深刻的见解。

3. The professor delivered a remarkably **lucid** lecture on thermodynamics that left every student feeling confident about the material.
   教授做了一场关于热力学的极为清晰的讲座，让每个学生都对这些内容充满信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "luc-id" — "luc" comes from Latin "lux" meaning "light," so lucid means filled with light, clear and bright.
把"lucid"想成"luc（光）+ id"——"luc"来自拉丁语"lux（光）"，所以lucid就是充满光明的、清晰明亮的。
