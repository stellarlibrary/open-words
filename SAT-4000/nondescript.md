# 🫥 nondescript

🔊 /ˌnɒndɪˈskrɪpt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking distinctive or interesting features or characteristics; dull and unremarkable. (n.) A person or thing with no outstanding or remarkable qualities
**中文：** adj. 平凡的；没有特色的；难以描述的 n. 无特征的人或物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unremarkable（平凡的）
- ordinary（普通的）
- featureless（无特征的）
- nondistinctive（无特色的）
- bland（乏味的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nondescript building / nondescript appearance / nondescript clothing / nondescript vehicle**

👉 Example: The spy deliberately wore nondescript clothing to avoid drawing attention in the crowded marketplace.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The suspect fled into a **nondescript** sedan that blended seamlessly with hundreds of similar vehicles on the busy highway.
   嫌疑人逃进了一辆毫无特色的轿车，在繁忙的高速公路上与数百辆类似的车辆融为一体。

2. Behind the **nondescript** facade of the old warehouse lay a thriving art gallery filled with extraordinary contemporary works.
   在这座旧仓库毫不起眼的外墙后面，是一个充满非凡当代作品的繁荣艺术画廊。

3. Critics dismissed the novel as a **nondescript** work that failed to offer any fresh perspective on its well-worn theme.
   评论家们将这部小说贬为一部平庸之作，未能为其老生常谈的主题提供任何新鲜视角。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "non-de-script" as "not describable" — something so plain and ordinary that you cannot even describe it because nothing stands out.
把"nondescript"想成"non（不能）+ descript（描述）"——平凡到无法描述，因为没有任何突出之处。
