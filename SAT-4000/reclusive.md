# 🏔️ reclusive

🔊 /rɪˈkluːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Avoiding the company of other people; tending to live in seclusion and solitude
**中文：** adj. 隐遁的；隐居的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- solitary（孤独的）
- withdrawn（离群的）
- hermitic（隐士般的）
- secluded（隐蔽的）
- antisocial（不合群的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reclusive lifestyle / reclusive artist / reclusive billionaire / increasingly reclusive**

👉 Example: The reclusive author had not granted a single interview in over twenty years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **reclusive** billionaire rarely appeared in public and conducted all business transactions through a network of trusted intermediaries.
   这位隐居的亿万富翁很少公开露面，通过一群值得信赖的中间人处理所有商业交易。

2. Despite her **reclusive** nature, the painter's extraordinary works attracted international acclaim and widespread critical admiration.
   尽管她性格隐遁，这位画家的杰出作品仍赢得了国际赞誉和广泛的评论界推崇。

3. Researchers struggled to study the **reclusive** species of deep-sea creatures that inhabit the darkest ocean trenches.
   研究人员很难研究栖息在最黑暗海沟中的那些隐蔽的深海物种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reclusive" as "recluse + ive" — having the quality of a recluse, someone who actively chooses isolation.
把"reclusive"想成"recluse + ive"——具有隐士特质的，主动选择与世隔绝的。
