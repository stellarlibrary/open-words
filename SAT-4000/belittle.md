# 😤 belittle

🔊 /bɪˈlɪtl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make someone or something seem unimportant; to disparage or diminish the value of
**中文：** v. 轻视；贬低；使相形见小

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disparage（贬低）
- denigrate（诋毁）
- diminish（减损）
- depreciate（贬低）
- trivialize（轻视）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **belittle someone's efforts / belittle the achievements / belittle the importance of**

👉 Example: The manager's tendency to belittle his subordinates' contributions eroded team morale significantly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The critic sought to **belittle** the author's groundbreaking work by dismissing it as derivative and lacking originality.
   那位评论家试图贬低这位作者的开创性作品，将其斥为模仿之作，毫无创意。

2. It is unwise to **belittle** the contributions of volunteers, for their efforts sustain many vital community programs.
   轻视志愿者的贡献是不明智的，因为他们的努力维系着许多重要的社区项目。

3. The professor refused to **belittle** the student's hypothesis, choosing instead to offer constructive and thoughtful feedback.
   教授拒绝贬低学生的假设，而是选择提供建设性且深思熟虑的反馈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-little" — to make someone "be little," that is, to make them seem small and insignificant.
把"belittle"拆成"be + little"——让某人"变得渺小"，即贬低、轻视他人。
