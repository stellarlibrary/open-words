# 💨 methane

🔊 /ˈmɛθeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, odorless flammable gas (CH₄) that is the main constituent of natural gas; produced by decomposition of organic matter in marshes and swamps.
**中文：** n. 甲烷；沼气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- marsh gas（沼气）
- natural gas（天然气）
- CH₄（甲烷分子式）
- biogas（生物气体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **methane emissions / methane gas / release methane / methane concentration**

👉 Example: Agricultural practices contribute significantly to methane emissions in the atmosphere.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The environmental report revealed that **methane** emissions from livestock farming have increased dramatically over the past decade.
   环境报告显示，过去十年来畜牧业的甲烷排放量急剧增加。

2. Scientists developed innovative methods to capture **methane** from landfills and convert it into a renewable energy source.
   科学家们开发了从垃圾填埋场捕获甲烷并将其转化为可再生能源的创新方法。

3. Rising levels of atmospheric **methane** are considered a major contributing factor to accelerated global warming trends.
   大气中甲烷水平的上升被认为是加速全球变暖趋势的主要促成因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meth-ane" — "meth" sounds like "marsh," and methane is indeed "marsh gas" that bubbles up from swamps and wetlands.
把"methane"中的"meth"联想成"marsh"（沼泽），甲烷确实就是从沼泽和湿地中冒出来的"沼气"。
