# 💰 amass

🔊 /əˈmæs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To gather together or accumulate a large amount or quantity of something over a period of time
**中文：** v. 积聚，积累

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accumulate（积累）
- gather（收集）
- hoard（囤积）
- stockpile（储备）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amass wealth / amass a fortune / amass evidence / amass a collection**

👉 Example: The entrepreneur managed to amass a considerable fortune through decades of disciplined investment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigator worked tirelessly to **amass** sufficient evidence before presenting the case to the grand jury for indictment.
   调查人员不懈努力积累充分的证据，然后将案件提交给大陪审团进行起诉。

2. Over three decades, the museum curator **amassed** an extraordinary collection of rare manuscripts from civilizations throughout the ancient world.
   在三十年间，博物馆馆长积累了来自古代世界各文明的珍稀手稿的非凡收藏。

3. The corporation continued to **amass** unprecedented profits despite the challenging economic conditions that affected its smaller competitors adversely.
   尽管充满挑战的经济状况对其较小的竞争对手产生了不利影响，该公司仍继续积累空前的利润。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-mass" — to gather "a mass" of things together, piling them up into one large collection.
把"amass"想成"a mass"——把东西聚集成"一大堆"，将它们堆积成一个庞大的集合。
