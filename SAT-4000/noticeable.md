# 👁️ noticeable

🔊 /ˈnoʊtɪsəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Easily seen or noticed; clear or apparent; worthy of attention
**中文：** adj. 显而易见的；值得注意的；显著的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obvious（明显的）
- apparent（显而易见的）
- conspicuous（引人注目的）
- perceptible（可察觉的）
- evident（明显的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a noticeable difference / noticeable improvement / noticeable change / barely noticeable**

👉 Example: There was a noticeable improvement in air quality after the new regulations took effect.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **noticeable** decline in voter turnout has prompted political analysts to investigate the underlying causes of civic disengagement.
   投票率的明显下降促使政治分析人士调查公民参与度降低的深层原因。

2. After six months of rehabilitation, there was a **noticeable** improvement in the patient's motor skills and coordination.
   经过六个月的康复治疗，患者的运动技能和协调能力有了显著改善。

3. The economic disparity between urban and rural regions became increasingly **noticeable** during the period of rapid industrialization.
   在快速工业化时期，城乡地区之间的经济差距变得越来越明显。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "notice-able" — something you are able to notice right away because it stands out clearly.
把"noticeable"想成"notice(注意到) + able(能够)"——能够立刻注意到的，因为它非常突出。
