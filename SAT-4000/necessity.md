# 🔑 necessity

🔊 /nəˈsɛsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being required or indispensable; something essential or unavoidable; an imperative need
**中文：** n. 必要性；需要；必然性；必需品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- requirement（需求）
- essential（必需品）
- imperative（必要的事）
- prerequisite（先决条件）
- obligation（义务）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **out of necessity / bare necessity / absolute necessity / necessity of life**

👉 Example: Clean water is a basic necessity that millions of people around the world still lack.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **necessity** of implementing stricter environmental regulations became increasingly apparent after the industrial disaster.
   在工业灾难发生后，实施更严格的环境法规的必要性变得日益明显。

2. Many rural families purchase only the bare **necessities** due to limited financial resources and rising costs.
   由于经济资源有限和成本上升，许多农村家庭只购买最基本的必需品。

3. Out of sheer **necessity**, the researchers developed an innovative method to conserve their dwindling laboratory supplies.
   出于迫切需要，研究人员开发了一种创新方法来节约日益减少的实验室用品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ne-CESS-ity" as containing "cess" like "access" — you need access to necessities; they are things you cannot do without.
把"necessity"想成包含"cess"，类似"access（获取）"——你需要获取必需品，它们是你不可缺少的东西。
