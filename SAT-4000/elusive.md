# 🌫️ elusive

🔊 /ɪˈluːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Difficult to find, catch, or achieve; hard to define or understand; tending to escape perception or comprehension
**中文：** adj. 难懂的；易忘的；逃避的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- evasive（逃避的）
- intangible（难以捉摸的）
- fleeting（稍纵即逝的）
- slippery（难以把握的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elusive goal / elusive answer / elusive figure / elusive quality / prove elusive**

👉 Example: A lasting peace in the region has proven elusive despite years of diplomatic negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **elusive** snow leopard remains one of the most difficult large mammals for wildlife photographers to capture on film.
   难以捉摸的雪豹仍然是野生动物摄影师最难拍摄到的大型哺乳动物之一。

2. A comprehensive solution to global poverty has remained **elusive**, frustrating policymakers and humanitarian organizations for generations.
   全球贫困的全面解决方案仍然难以实现，令几代政策制定者和人道主义组织倍感沮丧。

3. The **elusive** meaning behind the poet's final work has inspired countless scholarly debates and competing interpretations.
   这位诗人最后作品背后难以捉摸的含义引发了无数学术争论和不同的诠释。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elusive" as related to "elude" — something elusive always eludes your grasp, like trying to catch fog with your hands.
把"elusive"和"elude"（逃避）联系起来——难以捉摸的东西总是从你手中溜走，就像试图用手抓住雾一样。
