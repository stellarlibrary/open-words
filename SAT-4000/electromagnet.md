# 🧲 electromagnet

🔊 /ɪˌlektroʊˈmæɡnɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A device consisting of a core of magnetic material surrounded by a coil of wire, through which an electric current is passed to magnetize the core
**中文：** n. 电磁体；电磁铁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- solenoid（螺线管）
- magnetic coil（磁线圈）
- electromagnetic device（电磁装置）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **build an electromagnet / powerful electromagnet / electromagnet coil / activate the electromagnet**

👉 Example: The industrial electromagnet lifted several tons of scrap metal with ease at the recycling facility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The massive **electromagnet** in the MRI machine generates a powerful magnetic field to produce detailed images of internal organs.
   核磁共振成像机中的巨大电磁铁产生强大的磁场，以生成内部器官的详细图像。

2. Students constructed a simple **electromagnet** by wrapping copper wire around an iron nail and connecting it to a battery.
   学生们通过将铜线缠绕在铁钉上并连接电池，制作了一个简单的电磁铁。

3. The crane operator activated the **electromagnet** to separate ferrous metals from non-ferrous materials at the industrial recycling plant.
   起重机操作员启动电磁铁，在工业回收厂将黑色金属与有色金属材料分离。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "electro-magnet" as "electricity makes a magnet" — an electromagnet is simply a magnet created and controlled by electric current.
把"electromagnet"想成"electricity makes a magnet（电制造磁铁）"——电磁铁就是由电流产生和控制的磁铁。
