# 🦠 cholera

🔊 /ˈkɒlərə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An acute infectious disease of the small intestine caused by the bacterium Vibrio cholerae, characterized by severe dehydration and diarrhea
**中文：** n. 霍乱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pestilence（瘟疫）
- epidemic（流行病）
- contagion（传染病）
- plague（瘟疫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cholera outbreak / cholera epidemic / cholera vaccine / contract cholera**

👉 Example: The cholera outbreak in the region prompted an immediate international humanitarian response.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **cholera** epidemic of 1854 in London led to groundbreaking discoveries in the field of epidemiology.
   1854年伦敦的**霍乱**疫情促成了流行病学领域的突破性发现。

2. International health organizations dispatched emergency supplies to combat the **cholera** crisis in the affected provinces.
   国际卫生组织紧急调配物资以应对受灾省份的**霍乱**危机。

3. Contaminated water sources remain the primary vector through which **cholera** spreads in densely populated urban areas.
   受污染的水源仍是**霍乱**在人口密集城市地区传播的主要途径。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cholera" as related to "choler" (bile) — the disease was historically associated with an excess of bile in the body.
把"cholera"与"choler"（胆汁）联系起来——这种疾病在历史上被认为与体内胆汁过多有关。
