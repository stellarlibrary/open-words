# 🌿 chloroplast

🔊 /ˈklɔːrəplæst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A plastid in green plant cells that contains chlorophyll and is the site of photosynthesis
**中文：** n. 叶绿体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plastid（质体）
- organelle（细胞器）
- photosynthetic body（光合体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chloroplast membrane / chloroplast DNA / chloroplast function / within the chloroplast**

👉 Example: The chloroplast converts light energy into chemical energy through the process of photosynthesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each **chloroplast** contains stacks of thylakoid membranes where the light-dependent reactions of photosynthesis occur.
   每个叶绿体都包含成叠的类囊体膜，光合作用的光反应就在此处进行。

2. Researchers discovered that **chloroplasts** possess their own DNA, supporting the endosymbiotic theory of cellular evolution.
   研究人员发现叶绿体拥有自己的DNA，这支持了细胞进化的内共生理论。

3. The density of **chloroplasts** in leaf cells directly correlates with the plant's capacity for photosynthetic energy production.
   叶细胞中叶绿体的密度与植物光合产能的能力直接相关。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chloro-plast" — "chloro" means green (as in chlorine's greenish color) and "plast" means formed body, so a chloroplast is the green body in cells.
把"chloroplast"拆成"chloro（绿色的）+ plast（成形体）"——细胞中的绿色成形体，就是叶绿体。
