# 🥗 nutriment

🔊 /ˈnjuːtrɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Nourishment; sustenance; any substance that provides essential nourishment for growth and the maintenance of life
**中文：** n. 营养物；促进生长的东西

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nourishment（营养）
- sustenance（食物）
- nutrition（营养）
- food（食物）
- aliment（养料）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **provide nutriment / essential nutriment / source of nutriment / derive nutriment from**

👉 Example: The soil must contain adequate nutriment to sustain healthy plant growth throughout the season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biologist explained how marine organisms derive essential **nutriment** from microscopic plankton suspended throughout the ocean waters.
   生物学家解释了海洋生物如何从悬浮在海水中的微小浮游生物中获取必需的营养物。

2. Proper **nutriment** during the early developmental stages is critical for ensuring healthy cognitive and physical growth in children.
   在早期发育阶段获得适当的营养对于确保儿童健康的认知和身体发育至关重要。

3. The research team analyzed the **nutriment** content of various indigenous crops to evaluate their potential for addressing regional food insecurity.
   研究团队分析了各种本土作物的营养成分，以评估其解决区域粮食安全问题的潜力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nutriment" as "nutri-ment" — the "ment" (substance) that "nutri-" (nourishes), literally the stuff that feeds and sustains life.
把"nutriment"想成"nutri-ment"——"nutri（滋养）"的"ment（物质）"，字面意思就是供养和维持生命的东西。
