# 🏅 laudatory

🔊 /ˈlɔːdətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Expressing or containing praise; serving to praise or commend
**中文：** adj. 赞美的；赞赏的；称赞的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- complimentary（赞美的）
- eulogistic（颂扬的）
- praising（赞扬的）
- adulatory（奉承的）
- approving（赞许的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laudatory remarks / laudatory review / laudatory speech / laudatory comments**

👉 Example: The book received uniformly laudatory reviews from major literary critics across the country.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **laudatory** editorial praised the governor's decisive leadership during the unprecedented public health emergency.
   这篇赞美性的社论称赞了州长在前所未有的公共卫生紧急事件中果断的领导力。

2. Several **laudatory** speeches were delivered at the banquet honoring the retiring professor's distinguished academic career.
   在宴会上发表了几篇赞美演讲，表彰这位即将退休的教授杰出的学术生涯。

3. Critics wrote **laudatory** assessments of the symphony, calling it the composer's most ambitious and accomplished work.
   评论家们撰写了对这部交响乐的赞赏性评价，称其为这位作曲家最具雄心和成就的作品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laudatory" as "laud + atory" — relating to lauding, like "congratulatory" relates to congratulating.
把"laudatory"想成"laud（赞美）+ atory（与…有关的）"——与赞美有关的，正如"congratulatory"与祝贺有关。
