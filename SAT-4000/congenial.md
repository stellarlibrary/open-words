# 😊 congenial

🔊 /kənˈdʒiːniəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Pleasant and agreeable in nature; having a similar temperament or interests; suited to one's disposition
**中文：** adj. 意气相投的，性格相似的；令人愉快的，适意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agreeable（令人愉快的）
- compatible（相容的）
- amiable（和蔼的）
- like-minded（志趣相投的）
- pleasant（愉快的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **congenial atmosphere / congenial company / congenial to / congenial surroundings**

👉 Example: The research team found the collaborative atmosphere congenial to productive scholarly work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The visiting professor found her new colleagues remarkably **congenial** and quickly adapted to the department's collaborative culture.
   这位访问教授发现她的新同事们非常意气相投，很快就适应了该系的合作文化。

2. A **congenial** work environment has been shown to significantly enhance employee creativity, satisfaction, and overall productivity.
   令人愉快的工作环境已被证明能显著提高员工的创造力、满意度和整体生产力。

3. The small coastal town provided a **congenial** setting for the writer, who produced her finest novels during that period.
   这座海滨小镇为这位作家提供了一个宜人的环境，她在那段时期创作了最出色的小说。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-GENIAL" — "genial" means friendly and warm, and "con" means together, so congenial people are friendly together.
把"congenial"想成"con-GENIAL"——"genial"意为友好温暖，"con"意为在一起，所以congenial就是在一起很友好。
