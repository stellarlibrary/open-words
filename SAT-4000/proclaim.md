# 📢 proclaim

🔊 /prəˈkleɪm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To announce officially or publicly; to declare; to indicate clearly; to praise
**中文：** v. 宣告，公布；声明；表明；显示；赞扬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- declare（宣布）
- announce（宣告）
- assert（断言）
- herald（宣扬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proclaim independence / proclaim a victory / proclaim something to be / publicly proclaim**

👉 Example: The newly elected leader stepped forward to proclaim a new era of reform and unity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The revolutionary leaders chose to **proclaim** independence from the colonial power in a bold public declaration.
   革命领袖们选择以大胆的公开声明宣告脱离殖民统治而独立。

2. The scientific community was eager to **proclaim** the discovery as one of the most significant breakthroughs of the decade.
   科学界急切地宣告这一发现是十年来最重大的突破之一。

3. Her confident demeanor and commanding presence seemed to **proclaim** her authority without the need for spoken words.
   她自信的举止和威严的风度似乎无需言语便彰显了她的权威。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-claim" as making a "professional claim" — when you proclaim something, you make a bold, public statement like a professional.
把"proclaim"想成"pro（公开地）+ claim（宣称）"——公开地、正式地宣告某事。
