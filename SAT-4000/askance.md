# 👀 askance

🔊 /əˈskæns/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** With an attitude or look of suspicion or disapproval; with a side glance
**中文：** 怀疑地；斜视地；不以为然地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- suspiciously（怀疑地）
- skeptically（怀疑地）
- distrustfully（不信任地）
- sideways（斜着）
- dubiously（可疑地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **look askance at / view askance / regard askance**

👉 Example: The professor looked askance at the student who claimed to have finished the exam in ten minutes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board members looked **askance** at the proposal, questioning whether the projected returns were genuinely achievable.
   董事会成员对这项提案持怀疑态度，质疑预计收益是否真的可以实现。

2. Neighbors viewed the eccentric artist **askance**, unsure what to make of his unusual nocturnal habits and routines.
   邻居们以怀疑的目光看待这位古怪的艺术家，不知该如何理解他不寻常的夜间习惯。

3. Traditional scholars regarded the new methodology **askance**, convinced that it lacked the rigor of established research practices.
   传统学者对这种新方法论持怀疑态度，认为它缺乏既有研究实践的严谨性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-SKANCE" as "a SKANned glance" — a sideways, scanning look full of doubt and suspicion.
把"askance"想成"扫视的一瞥"——充满怀疑和不信任的斜眼一看。
