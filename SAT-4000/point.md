# 📍 point

🔊 /pɔɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A main idea or essential element; a unit of scoring; a punctuation mark; a sharp tip or dot; a decimal point. (v.) To direct attention toward; to sharpen.
**中文：** n. 要点；得分；标点；尖端；小数点 v. 指向；弄尖；加标点于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tip（尖端）
- purpose（要点）
- indicate（指示）
- dot（点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a point / point out / decimal point / point of view / starting point**

👉 Example: The professor asked students to point out the key arguments in the author's thesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The main **point** of the researcher's argument was that environmental regulations must be strengthened to prevent further ecological damage.
   研究人员论点的主要**要点**是必须加强环境法规以防止进一步的生态破坏。

2. She used a laser **pointer** to **point** at the critical data on the graph during her formal conference presentation.
   她在正式会议报告中用激光笔**指向**图表上的关键数据。

3. The decimal **point** separates whole numbers from fractional values and is essential for precision in scientific calculations.
   小数**点**将整数与分数值分开，对科学计算的精确性至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "point" as a finger pointing — it directs your attention to the exact spot, the tip, or the main idea you should focus on.
把"point"想成一根指向的手指——它引导你的注意力到确切的位置、尖端或你应该关注的要点。
