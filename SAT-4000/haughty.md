# 👑 haughty

🔊 /ˈhɔːti/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Arrogantly superior and disdainful; having or showing an attitude of superiority and contempt for others
**中文：** adj. 傲慢的；自大的；目中无人的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arrogant（傲慢的）
- supercilious（高傲的）
- disdainful（轻蔑的）
- imperious（专横的）
- condescending（居高临下的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **haughty demeanor / haughty tone / haughty attitude / haughty expression**

👉 Example: Her haughty demeanor alienated many of her colleagues in the department.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The aristocrat's **haughty** manner offended the delegation and ultimately undermined the progress of diplomatic negotiations.
   那位贵族傲慢的态度冒犯了代表团，最终破坏了外交谈判的进展。

2. Despite her remarkable accomplishments, she remained humble and never adopted the **haughty** attitude of her peers.
   尽管她成就卓越，她始终保持谦逊，从未表现出同侪的傲慢态度。

3. The professor's **haughty** dismissal of the student's question discouraged further participation in the seminar discussion.
   教授傲慢地驳回了学生的问题，这打击了学生们进一步参与研讨会讨论的积极性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "haughty" as "high-ty" — someone who thinks they are higher or better than everyone else.
把"haughty"想成"high-ty（高高在上）"——一个认为自己比所有人都高贵的人。
