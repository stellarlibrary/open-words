# 📦 plethora

🔊 /ˈplɛθərə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An excessive amount or overabundance of something; a surplus beyond what is needed
**中文：** n. 过多；过剩；多血症

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excess（过剩）
- surplus（剩余）
- overabundance（过多）
- glut（过量）
- superfluity（多余）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a plethora of / plethora of choices / plethora of options / plethora of information**

👉 Example: The internet provides a plethora of information, but not all sources are equally reliable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee faced a **plethora** of competing proposals, making it exceedingly difficult to reach a consensus on priorities.
   委员会面临过多的竞争提案，使得在优先事项上达成共识极为困难。

2. A **plethora** of new regulations overwhelmed small business owners who lacked the resources to ensure full compliance.
   过多的新法规让缺乏资源确保完全合规的小企业主不堪重负。

3. The library offered a **plethora** of academic journals covering every discipline from molecular biology to comparative literature.
   图书馆提供了大量学术期刊，涵盖从分子生物学到比较文学的每个学科。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plethora" as "plenty-more-a" — so much of something that there is plenty and then even more on top of that.
把"plethora"想成"plenty-more-a"——某样东西多到已经够多了，上面还有更多。
