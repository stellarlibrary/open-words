# 🌟 extol

🔊 /ɪkˈstoʊl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To praise enthusiastically; to glorify or exalt someone or something with great admiration
**中文：** v. 颂扬；赞美；赞颂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- praise（赞扬）
- glorify（颂扬）
- exalt（赞美）
- commend（称赞）
- laud（赞颂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extol the virtues of / extol the merits of / extol the benefits of**

👉 Example: The professor loved to extol the virtues of classical literature to her students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The keynote speaker proceeded to **extol** the remarkable achievements of the research team throughout the annual conference.
   主旨演讲者在年度会议上颂扬了研究团队的杰出成就。

2. Health advocates consistently **extol** the benefits of regular exercise and a balanced diet for long-term wellness.
   健康倡导者一直赞扬定期锻炼和均衡饮食对长期健康的益处。

3. Critics universally **extol** the novel as a masterpiece of contemporary literature that redefines the boundaries of fiction.
   评论家普遍赞誉这部小说是当代文学的杰作，重新定义了小说的边界。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extol" as "ex- (up) + tol (from Latin tollere, to raise)" — to raise someone up with praise.
把"extol"想成"ex-（向上）+ tol（来自拉丁语tollere，举起）"——用赞美将某人高高举起。
