# 🪙 nickel

🔊 /ˈnɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A silvery-white metallic element (symbol Ni); a five-cent coin in the United States. (v.) To plate with nickel
**中文：** n. 镍；五分镍币 v. 镀镍于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- five-cent piece（五分硬币）
- coin（硬币）
- small change（零钱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nickel and dime / nickel alloy / nickel plating / not worth a nickel**

👉 Example: The company was accused of nickel-and-diming its employees by cutting small but meaningful benefits.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The child placed a shiny **nickel** into the old vending machine, hoping to receive a small toy or candy.
   孩子把一枚闪亮的五分硬币投入旧自动售货机，希望能得到一个小玩具或糖果。

2. **Nickel** is widely used in industrial manufacturing because of its exceptional resistance to corrosion and high temperatures.
   镍因其卓越的耐腐蚀性和耐高温性而被广泛应用于工业制造。

3. The factory decided to **nickel** plate the steel components to protect them from rust and extend their operational lifespan.
   工厂决定对钢制零件进行镀镍处理，以防锈并延长其使用寿命。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nickel" as a small but useful metal — just like the small five-cent coin, it may seem insignificant but adds up over time.
把"nickel"想成一种小而有用的金属——就像五分硬币，看似微不足道，但积少成多。
