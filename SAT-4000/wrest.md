# 💪 wrest

🔊 /rɛst/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To pull or force away by violent twisting; to seize or take by force or effort; to distort the meaning of. (n.) The act of wresting; a forceful twist
**中文：** v. 用力拧；抢夺；歪曲 n. 夺取，强夺；扭，拧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- seize（夺取）
- wrench（猛扭）
- extract（强取）
- usurp（篡夺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wrest control / wrest power / wrest away / wrest from**

👉 Example: The opposition party sought to wrest control of the parliament through a strategic coalition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rebel forces managed to **wrest** control of the northern provinces from the weakened central government.
   叛军成功地从衰弱的中央政府手中夺取了北方各省的控制权。

2. After years of litigation, the heirs finally **wrested** the valuable estate from the unscrupulous business partner.
   经过多年诉讼，继承人终于从不道德的商业伙伴手中夺回了宝贵的遗产。

3. Scholars accused the regime of attempting to **wrest** historical texts from their original meaning for political purposes.
   学者们指责该政权试图出于政治目的歪曲历史文本的原意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wrest" as "wrestle" shortened — you wrestle something away from someone when you wrest it from their grip.
把"wrest"想成"wrestle（摔跤）"的缩写——你从别人手中夺取东西时，就像在和他们摔跤。
