# 🍽️ comestible

🔊 /kəˈmes.tɪ.bəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An item of food. (adj.) Fit to be eaten; edible
**中文：** n. 食物 adj. 可吃的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- edible（可食用的）
- foodstuff（食品）
- provisions（食物供给）
- nourishment（食物）
- victuals（食物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **comestible items / comestible goods / fine comestibles / local comestibles**

👉 Example: The market offered an impressive array of comestible goods from local artisan producers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inspector confirmed that the products were perfectly **comestible** and met all federal food safety standards.
   检查员确认这些产品完全可以食用，并符合所有联邦食品安全标准。

2. Foragers must learn to distinguish **comestible** plants from toxic species that closely resemble them in appearance.
   采集者必须学会区分可食用植物和外观与之非常相似的有毒物种。

3. The gourmet shop specialized in rare **comestibles** imported from Mediterranean regions known for culinary excellence.
   这家美食店专门经营从以烹饪卓越著称的地中海地区进口的珍稀食品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "come-EST-ible" — it contains "est" like "ingest," reminding you it is something you can eat or ingest.
把"comestible"想成"come-EST-ible"——其中包含"est"，类似"ingest（摄入）"，提醒你这是可以吃或摄入的东西。
