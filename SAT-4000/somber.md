# 🌑 somber

🔊 /ˈsɑːmbər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Dark and gloomy; melancholy; grave or serious in manner or tone
**中文：** adj. 昏暗的；忧郁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gloomy（阴郁的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- grave（严肃的）
- dismal（阴沉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **somber mood / somber tone / somber occasion / somber expression / somber atmosphere**

👉 Example: The funeral was a somber occasion that reminded everyone of the fragility of life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The president delivered a **somber** address to the nation following the devastating earthquake that struck the coastal region.
   总统在毁灭性地震袭击沿海地区后向全国发表了一篇沉痛的讲话。

2. A **somber** atmosphere pervaded the courtroom as the judge prepared to announce the final verdict in the case.
   法官准备宣布案件最终判决时，法庭内弥漫着一种凝重的气氛。

3. The documentary adopted a **somber** tone to convey the harsh realities faced by refugees fleeing their homeland.
   这部纪录片采用了忧郁的基调来传达难民逃离家园所面临的严酷现实。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "somber" as "some + blur" — when things are blurred and dark, the mood feels somber and gloomy.
把"somber"想成"some + blur"——当事物变得模糊昏暗时，气氛就变得忧郁阴沉。
