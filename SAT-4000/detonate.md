# 💣 detonate

🔊 /ˈdɛtəˌneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause something to explode; to set off a bomb or explosive device with sudden and violent force
**中文：** v. 使爆炸；引爆；使突发大爆炸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- explode（爆炸）
- ignite（点燃）
- discharge（释放）
- trigger（触发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **detonate a bomb / detonate an explosive / remotely detonate / detonate on impact**

👉 Example: The demolition crew prepared to detonate the controlled explosives that would bring down the condemned building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Investigators confirmed that the device was designed to **detonate** remotely using a wireless signal transmitted from several miles away.
   调查人员确认该装置被设计为通过数英里外传输的无线信号进行远程引爆。

2. The construction firm hired specialists to safely **detonate** the remaining munitions discovered at the former military installation site.
   建筑公司聘请专家安全引爆在前军事设施遗址发现的剩余弹药。

3. Scientists studying volcanic activity noted that pressure buildup beneath the surface could effectively **detonate** a catastrophic eruption without warning.
   研究火山活动的科学家指出，地表下的压力积聚可能在毫无预警的情况下引发灾难性喷发。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "detonate" as "de-tone-ate" — the explosion is so loud it destroys all other tones, leaving only a deafening boom.
把"detonate"想成"de-tone-ate"——爆炸声如此之大，摧毁了所有其他声音，只留下震耳欲聋的轰鸣。
