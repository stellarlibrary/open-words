# 👁️ vigilance

🔊 /ˈvɪdʒɪləns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The action or state of keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties; alertness and watchfulness
**中文：** n. 警戒；警觉；警惕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alertness（警觉）
- watchfulness（警惕）
- attentiveness（专注）
- caution（谨慎）
- wariness（小心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eternal vigilance / maintain vigilance / heightened vigilance / exercise vigilance / constant vigilance**

👉 Example: Eternal vigilance is the price of liberty, as the famous political maxim reminds us.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cybersecurity team maintained constant **vigilance** against increasingly sophisticated threats targeting the organization's network infrastructure.
   网络安全团队对针对组织网络基础设施的日益复杂的威胁保持持续警惕。

2. Public health officials urged **vigilance** among travelers returning from regions where the infectious disease had reached epidemic levels.
   公共卫生官员敦促从疫情达到流行水平地区返回的旅行者保持警惕。

3. The success of the conservation program depends on the **vigilance** of local communities who monitor endangered wildlife habitats.
   保护计划的成功取决于监测濒危野生动物栖息地的当地社区的警觉性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vigil-ance" as keeping a "vigil" — staying awake and watchful through the night, always on guard against danger.
把"vigilance"想成保持"vigil"（守夜）——整夜保持清醒和警觉，时刻防范危险。
