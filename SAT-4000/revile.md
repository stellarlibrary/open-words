# 😡 revile

🔊 /rɪˈvaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To criticize in an abusive or angrily insulting manner; to subject to verbal abuse
**中文：** v. 辱骂，斥责

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vilify（诽谤）
- denounce（谴责）
- berate（痛斥）
- castigate（严厉批评）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **revile someone for / revile as / publicly reviled / widely reviled**

👉 Example: The corrupt official was publicly reviled for embezzling funds meant for disaster relief.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The disgraced politician was **reviled** by the press and the public alike for his repeated acts of dishonesty.
   这位名誉扫地的政客因反复的不诚实行为而遭到媒体和公众的一致辱骂。

2. Critics **reviled** the new legislation as a dangerous assault on civil liberties and constitutional rights.
   批评者斥责这项新立法是对公民自由和宪法权利的危险侵犯。

3. Throughout history, those who challenged established scientific doctrines were often **reviled** before being vindicated by evidence.
   纵观历史，那些挑战既定科学学说的人在被证据证明正确之前往往遭到辱骂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-" + "vile" — to call someone vile again and again, heaping insults upon them.
把"revile"想成"re-（反复）+ vile（卑鄙的）"——反复说某人卑鄙，对其大加辱骂。
