# 🎉 gaiety

🔊 /ˈɡeɪəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state or quality of being lighthearted and cheerful; festive activity or celebration; bright and showy appearance
**中文：** n. 快乐，兴高采烈；庆祝活动；欢庆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merriment（欢乐）
- cheerfulness（愉快）
- revelry（狂欢）
- jubilation（欢庆）
- mirth（欢笑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an air of gaiety / spirit of gaiety / gaiety of the occasion / infectious gaiety**

👉 Example: The gaiety of the holiday season brought the entire community together in joyful celebration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **gaiety** of the annual harvest festival filled the town square with music, laughter, and colorful decorations.
   一年一度的丰收节的欢乐气氛使城镇广场充满了音乐、笑声和五彩缤纷的装饰。

2. Despite the political turmoil, the citizens maintained an outward appearance of **gaiety** during the national celebration.
   尽管政治动荡，公民们在国庆活动期间保持着外表上的欢乐气氛。

3. Her infectious **gaiety** lightened the somber mood of the conference and encouraged more open dialogue among participants.
   她富有感染力的快乐气质缓解了会议的沉闷气氛，鼓励了与会者之间更开放的对话。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gaiety" as "gay + ety" — "gay" originally meant joyful and merry, so gaiety is the noun form meaning a state of joy.
把"gaiety"想成"gay + ety"——"gay"原意是快乐和愉快的，所以gaiety是其名词形式，意为快乐的状态。
