# 🍂 effete

🔊 /ɪˈfiːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** No longer capable of effective action; exhausted of vitality or strength; decadent and weak
**中文：** adj. 衰老的；疲惫的；衰弱的；颓废的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exhausted（精疲力竭的）
- decadent（颓废的）
- enfeebled（衰弱的）
- spent（耗尽的）
- worn-out（疲惫不堪的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effete aristocracy / effete civilization / effete society / effete intellectuals**

👉 Example: The historian described the late Roman Empire as an effete civilization unable to defend its own borders.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics dismissed the **effete** literary movement as lacking the vigor and originality needed to address contemporary social challenges.
   批评家们认为这一衰弱的文学运动缺乏应对当代社会挑战所需的活力和独创性。

2. The once-powerful dynasty had grown **effete** after centuries of unchallenged rule and excessive indulgence in luxury.
   这个曾经强大的王朝在几个世纪未受挑战的统治和过度奢侈之后已变得衰弱。

3. He argued that an **effete** ruling class, disconnected from the realities of ordinary citizens, would inevitably invite revolution.
   他认为，一个与普通公民的现实脱节的衰老统治阶级将不可避免地招致革命。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-fete" as "the party (fete) is over" — an effete person or society has spent all its energy, like guests after an exhausting celebration.
把"effete"想成"派对(fete)结束了"——一个effete的人或社会已经耗尽了所有精力，就像一场令人筋疲力尽的庆祝活动后的客人。
