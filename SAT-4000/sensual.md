# 🌹 sensual

🔊 /ˈsenʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or involving gratification of the senses and physical pleasure; worldly; of or pertaining to sensationalism
**中文：** adj. 感觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carnal（肉欲的）
- voluptuous（感官享受的）
- hedonistic（享乐主义的）
- worldly（世俗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sensual pleasure / sensual experience / sensual desire / sensual nature**

👉 Example: The poet's verse evoked a deeply sensual awareness of the natural world through vivid imagery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that a purely **sensual** existence, devoid of intellectual pursuit, leads to spiritual impoverishment.
   这位哲学家认为，纯粹感官的存在若缺乏智识追求，会导致精神上的贫乏。

2. Renaissance art often celebrated the **sensual** beauty of the human form while simultaneously conveying profound religious themes.
   文艺复兴时期的艺术常常在传达深刻宗教主题的同时赞美人体的感官之美。

3. The ascetic monks deliberately renounced all **sensual** pleasures in their commitment to a life of discipline and meditation.
   苦行僧侣在致力于纪律和冥想生活的过程中刻意摒弃了一切感官享乐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sensual" as "sense-you-all" — it appeals to all your senses, emphasizing physical and worldly pleasures.
把"sensual"想成"sense-you-all"——它诉诸你所有的感官，强调身体和世俗的愉悦。
