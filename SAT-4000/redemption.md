# 🕊️ redemption

🔊 /rɪˈdempʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of saving or being saved from sin, error, or evil; the act of buying back or repaying; fulfillment of a promise
**中文：** n. 赎回；拯救；偿还；实践；救赎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- salvation（拯救）
- atonement（赎罪）
- deliverance（解救）
- recovery（恢复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seek redemption / beyond redemption / redemption of debt / path to redemption**

👉 Example: The character's journey toward redemption provides the central narrative arc of the entire novel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The film explores the theme of **redemption** through the story of a convict who rebuilds his life after decades in prison.
   这部电影通过一名囚犯在入狱数十年后重建生活的故事探讨了救赎的主题。

2. The bond reached its **redemption** date, and investors received the full principal amount plus accumulated interest payments.
   债券到了赎回日期，投资者收到了全部本金和累积的利息。

3. Many theologians regard personal **redemption** as a transformative process rather than a single momentary event of divine grace.
   许多神学家认为个人的救赎是一个转变过程，而非神圣恩典的单一瞬间事件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "redemp-tion" — from "redeem" plus "-tion" (the noun form), so redemption is the act or result of redeeming.
把"redemption"想成"redeem(赎回/救赎) + tion(名词后缀)"——赎回或救赎的行为，即救赎、偿还。
