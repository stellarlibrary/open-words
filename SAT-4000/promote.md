# 📣 promote

🔊 /prəˈmoʊt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To advance in rank or position; to further the progress of something; to publicize or advertise
**中文：** v. 促进；发扬；提升；提拔；增进；助长；宣传；推销

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advance（推进）
- advocate（提倡）
- elevate（提升）
- encourage（鼓励）
- publicize（宣传）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **promote awareness / promote growth / promote equality / be promoted to / promote a product**

👉 Example: The organization launched a campaign to promote environmental awareness among young students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university established a new center dedicated to **promoting** cross-cultural dialogue and international academic collaboration among scholars.
   该大学建立了一个新中心，致力于促进学者之间的跨文化对话和国际学术合作。

2. After years of outstanding performance, she was **promoted** to the position of senior vice president within the corporation.
   经过多年的出色表现，她被提升为公司高级副总裁。

3. Government policies that **promote** renewable energy development are essential for achieving long-term environmental sustainability goals.
   促进可再生能源发展的政府政策对于实现长期环境可持续性目标至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-MOTE" — "pro" means forward, and "mote" sounds like "move"; to promote is to move something forward or upward.
把"promote"想成"pro-MOTE"——"pro"意为向前，"mote"听起来像"move"（移动）；promote就是把事物向前或向上推动。
