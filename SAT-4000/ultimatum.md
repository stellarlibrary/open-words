# ⚠️ ultimatum

🔊 /ˌʌltɪˈmeɪtəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A final demand or statement of terms, the rejection of which will result in retaliation or a breakdown of relations
**中文：** n. 最后通牒；最终条件

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- final demand（最后要求）
- final warning（最后警告）
- deadline（最后期限）
- last offer（最后提议）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **issue an ultimatum / deliver an ultimatum / give someone an ultimatum / face an ultimatum**

👉 Example: The union delivered an ultimatum to management, demanding better working conditions within forty-eight hours.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The president issued an **ultimatum** to the hostile nation, demanding the immediate withdrawal of all troops.
   总统向敌对国家发出了最后通牒，要求其立即撤出所有军队。

2. After months of fruitless negotiation, the board presented an **ultimatum** that left no room for further compromise.
   经过数月毫无成果的谈判，董事会提出了一份不留任何妥协余地的最后通牒。

3. She gave her employer an **ultimatum**: either approve the transfer request or accept her immediate resignation.
   她向雇主发出最后通牒：要么批准调动申请，要么接受她立即辞职。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ultimatum" as the "ultimateatum (item)" — the very last item on the negotiation table before everything falls apart.
把"ultimatum"想成"ultimate able item"——谈判桌上的最后一件事，之后一切将崩塌。
