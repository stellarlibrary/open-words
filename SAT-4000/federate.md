# 🤝 federate

🔊 /ˈfɛdəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To unite in a federation or alliance; to bring together under a central authority while retaining some autonomy. (adj.) Allied or united in a federation
**中文：** v. 使联合，使结成同盟 adj. 同盟的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unite（联合）
- ally（结盟）
- confederate（同盟）
- amalgamate（合并）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **federate states / federate organizations / federate into a union / federate under one government**

👉 Example: The small republics decided to federate in order to strengthen their collective bargaining power.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The independent colonies chose to **federate** under a single constitution to ensure mutual defense and economic prosperity.
   这些独立殖民地选择在一部宪法下联合起来，以确保共同防御和经济繁荣。

2. Several nonprofit organizations agreed to **federate** their efforts, creating a unified front against environmental degradation.
   几家非营利组织同意联合行动，形成一个对抗环境恶化的统一阵线。

3. The proposal to **federate** the regional universities into one system was met with both enthusiasm and skepticism.
   将区域大学联合为一个系统的提议既受到了热情支持，也遭到了质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "feder-ate" as related to "federal" — a federal system is one where states federate, joining together while keeping some independence.
把"federate"和"federal（联邦的）"联系起来——联邦制就是各州联合在一起，同时保留一定独立性的制度。
