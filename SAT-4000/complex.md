# 🌀 complex

🔊 /ˈkɒmpleks/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Consisting of many interconnected parts; intricate and not easy to understand. (n.) A group of related buildings, facilities, or psychological elements forming a unified whole
**中文：** adj. 复杂的 n. 复合体；联合体；综合建筑群

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intricate（错综复杂的）
- complicated（复杂的）
- elaborate（精密的）
- multifaceted（多方面的）
- compound（复合的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complex system / complex issue / apartment complex / inferiority complex / complex structure**

👉 Example: The complex interplay between genetics and environment determines many aspects of human development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist developed a **complex** algorithm capable of analyzing millions of data points to predict climate change patterns accurately.
   科学家开发了一种复杂的算法，能够分析数百万个数据点以准确预测气候变化模式。

2. The new residential **complex** will feature sustainable energy systems, rooftop gardens, and state-of-the-art recreational facilities for residents.
   新的住宅综合体将配备可持续能源系统、屋顶花园和最先进的居民休闲设施。

3. Navigating the **complex** regulatory framework requires specialized legal expertise that few practitioners in this field actually possess.
   驾驭复杂的监管框架需要专业的法律知识，而该领域中很少有从业者真正具备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "complex" as "com-PLEX" where "plex" means woven or braided — something complex has many strands woven together intricately.
把"complex"想成"com-PLEX"，其中"plex"意为编织——复杂的事物有许多线索错综交织在一起。
