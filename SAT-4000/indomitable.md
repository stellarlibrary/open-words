# 💪 indomitable

🔊 /ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Impossible to subdue or defeat; unyielding in spirit or determination
**中文：** adj. 不屈不挠的；不服输的；不气馁的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unconquerable（不可征服的）
- invincible（无敌的）
- unyielding（不屈的）
- resolute（坚定的）
- tenacious（顽强的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indomitable spirit / indomitable will / indomitable courage / indomitable determination**

👉 Example: Her indomitable spirit carried her through years of hardship and ultimately led to remarkable success.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The explorer's **indomitable** will enabled her to survive three weeks alone in the Arctic wilderness without adequate provisions.
   这位探险家不屈不挠的意志使她在没有充足补给的情况下独自在北极荒野中生存了三周。

2. Despite facing repeated legal setbacks, the activist maintained an **indomitable** resolve to secure civil rights for marginalized communities.
   尽管屡次遭遇法律挫折，这位活动家仍保持着不屈不挠的决心，为边缘化群体争取公民权利。

3. The regiment's **indomitable** courage under relentless bombardment earned them the highest military honors from the national government.
   该团在持续轰炸下表现出不屈不挠的勇气，为他们赢得了国家政府授予的最高军事荣誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-DOMIT-able" as "not able to be dominated" — someone indomitable cannot be tamed or conquered.
把"indomitable"想成"in(不)+domit(驯服)+able(能够)"——无法被驯服的人，就是不屈不挠的。
