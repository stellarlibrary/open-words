# 🎭 feign

🔊 /feɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To pretend or give a false appearance of; to fabricate or invent
**中文：** v. 假装；装作；捏造；想象

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pretend（假装）
- simulate（模拟）
- fabricate（捏造）
- counterfeit（伪造）
- dissemble（掩饰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feign illness / feign ignorance / feign surprise / feign interest / feign sleep**

👉 Example: The student tried to feign illness to avoid taking the final examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat chose to **feign** ignorance of the leaked documents rather than acknowledge their damaging contents publicly.
   这位外交官选择佯装不知泄露文件的存在，而非公开承认其破坏性内容。

2. She could not **feign** enthusiasm for the proposal when its flaws were so glaringly obvious to everyone present.
   当提案的缺陷对在场所有人来说都如此显而易见时，她无法假装热情。

3. The accused attempted to **feign** insanity during the trial, but the psychiatrist's testimony quickly discredited his performance.
   被告在审判期间试图假装精神失常，但精神科医生的证词很快揭穿了他的伪装。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "feign" as sounding like "feint" in fencing — a fake move. When you feign, you are faking something.
把"feign"想成击剑中的"feint（佯攻）"——一个假动作。当你feign时，你就是在假装某事。
