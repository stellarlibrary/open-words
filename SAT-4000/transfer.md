# 🔄 transfer

🔊 /ˈtrænsfɜːr/ (n.) /trænsˈfɜːr/ (v.)<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of moving something or someone to another place, position, or organization. (v.) To move from one place, person, or situation to another
**中文：** n. 转让；转移；调动 v. 转让；转学；转移

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relocate（调迁）
- convey（传送）
- transmit（传递）
- shift（转移）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transfer to / transfer from / transfer of power / transfer student / bank transfer**

👉 Example: The student decided to transfer to a university closer to her family after the first semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board approved the **transfer** of ownership from the founding family to a consortium of international investors.
   董事会批准将所有权从创始家族转让给一个国际投资者财团。

2. The laboratory technician must carefully **transfer** the biological samples to a sterile container to prevent any contamination.
   实验室技术人员必须小心地将生物样本转移到无菌容器中，以防止任何污染。

3. After completing two years at a community college, she plans to **transfer** to a prestigious research university this autumn.
   在社区学院完成两年学业后，她计划今年秋天转入一所著名的研究型大学。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-fer" as "carry across" — from Latin "trans" (across) + "ferre" (to carry), you carry something from one place to another.
把"transfer"想成"trans（穿越）+ fer（搬运）"——从拉丁语来看，就是把某物从一个地方搬运到另一个地方。
