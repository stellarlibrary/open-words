# 👋 salutation

🔊 /ˌsæljəˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A greeting; an expression of goodwill or courtesy upon meeting or in correspondence
**中文：** n. 招呼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- greeting（问候）
- welcome（欢迎）
- address（称呼）
- hello（你好）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **formal salutation / opening salutation / exchange salutations / proper salutation**

👉 Example: The letter began with the formal salutation "Dear Esteemed Members of the Committee."

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador exchanged warm **salutations** with the foreign delegates before the formal proceedings of the summit officially commenced.
   大使在峰会正式议程开始前与外国代表互致热情的问候。

2. In professional correspondence, choosing an appropriate **salutation** reflects the writer's awareness of etiquette and cultural sensitivity.
   在专业通信中，选择恰当的称呼体现了写信人对礼仪和文化敏感性的认知。

3. The ancient manuscript preserved a ceremonial **salutation** that scholars believe was used to honor visiting dignitaries in royal courts.
   这份古代手稿保存了一种仪式性的问候语，学者们认为它曾用于在王室中迎接到访的贵宾。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "salut-ation" — "salut" sounds like "salute," which is a form of greeting; a salutation is simply the act of greeting someone.
把"salutation"想成"salut（像salute，敬礼）+ ation"——敬礼就是一种打招呼的方式，salutation就是问候、招呼的意思。
