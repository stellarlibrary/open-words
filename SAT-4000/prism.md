# 🔮 prism

🔊 /ˈprɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A transparent optical element with flat, polished surfaces that refracts light; a solid geometric figure whose two ends are parallel and congruent polygons
**中文：** n. 棱镜；棱柱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lens（透镜）
- crystal（水晶体）
- spectrum（光谱）
- refractor（折射器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **through a prism / glass prism / prism of light / view through the prism of**

👉 Example: Scientists often examine social issues through the prism of historical context.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist demonstrated how a **prism** separates white light into its component colors during the optics lecture.
   物理学家在光学讲座中演示了棱镜如何将白光分解为各组成颜色。

2. When viewed through the **prism** of economic theory, the policy reveals significant structural flaws and contradictions.
   从经济学理论的角度来看，该政策暴露出重大的结构性缺陷和矛盾。

3. The crystal **prism** mounted on the laboratory bench refracted sunlight into a vivid rainbow across the wall.
   安装在实验台上的水晶棱镜将阳光折射成一道鲜艳的彩虹投射到墙上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prism" as "pri-sm" — "pri" sounds like "pretty," and a prism creates pretty rainbows by splitting light.
把"prism"想成"pri-sm"——"pri"听起来像"pretty（漂亮）"，棱镜通过分光制造出漂亮的彩虹。
