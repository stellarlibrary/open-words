# 🏈 spheroid

🔊 /ˈsfɪrɔɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A three-dimensional shape that is approximately spherical but not perfectly round; an ellipsoid of revolution.
**中文：** n. 球状体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ellipsoid（椭球体）
- ovoid（卵形体）
- globe（球体）
- orb（球状物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oblate spheroid / prolate spheroid / spheroid shape / spheroid geometry / approximate a spheroid**

👉 Example: Geodesists model the Earth as an oblate spheroid to account for the equatorial bulge caused by rotation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The planet Jupiter is classified as an oblate **spheroid** because its rapid rotation causes it to bulge at the equator.
   木星被归类为扁球状体，因为其快速自转导致赤道处膨胀。

2. In advanced mathematics, calculating the volume of a **spheroid** requires integrating along the axis of revolution.
   在高等数学中，计算球状体的体积需要沿旋转轴进行积分。

3. The ceramic artist skillfully shaped the clay into a smooth **spheroid** before placing it in the kiln for firing.
   陶艺家熟练地将黏土塑造成光滑的球状体，然后放入窑中烧制。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spher-oid" as "sphere + oid (resembling)" — a spheroid resembles a sphere but is not a perfect one, like a slightly squashed ball.
把"spheroid"想成"sphere（球）+ oid（类似）"——球状体类似球体但并不完美，就像一个被稍微压扁的球。
