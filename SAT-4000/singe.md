# 🔥 singe

🔊 /sɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To burn slightly on the surface; to scorch. (n.) A slight burn or scorch
**中文：** v. 烧焦，烤焦

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scorch（烧焦）
- char（烧成炭）
- sear（灼烧）
- burn（烧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **singe the edges / singe one's hair / singed by flames / a slight singe**

👉 Example: The candle flame singed the edge of the curtain before anyone noticed the danger.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The wildfire **singed** the outer bark of the ancient trees but fortunately did not destroy the forest entirely.
   野火烧焦了古树的外层树皮，但幸运的是没有完全摧毁森林。

2. The chef accidentally **singed** her eyebrows while igniting the flambé dish during the live cooking demonstration.
   厨师在现场烹饪演示中点燃火焰菜肴时不小心烤焦了眉毛。

3. Sparks from the welding torch **singed** the protective covering, reminding workers of the importance of proper safety equipment.
   焊接火炬的火花烧焦了保护罩，提醒工人们注意适当安全设备的重要性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "singe" as "sing + e" — if you sing too close to a fire, you might singe your hair.
把"singe"想成"sing + e"——如果你在火旁太近唱歌(sing)，你可能会烧焦头发。
