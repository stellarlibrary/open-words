# 🌳 ancestry

🔊 /ˈænsɛstri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** One's family or ethnic descent; the lineage of a person's ancestors or forebears
**中文：** 祖先；血统

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lineage（世系）
- heritage（遗产）
- pedigree（家谱）
- descent（血统）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **trace one's ancestry / of noble ancestry / common ancestry / ancestry and heritage**

👉 Example: She spent years tracing her ancestry back to a small village in southern Ireland.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Genetic testing has become a popular tool for individuals seeking to discover their **ancestry** and ethnic origins.
   基因检测已成为人们寻找其血统和民族起源的流行工具。

2. The royal family takes great pride in its distinguished **ancestry**, which can be traced back over eight centuries.
   皇室家族对其显赫的血统引以为豪，其历史可以追溯到八个多世纪以前。

3. Scientists confirmed that all modern humans share a common **ancestry** originating from the African continent thousands of years ago.
   科学家证实，所有现代人类都拥有源自数千年前非洲大陆的共同祖先。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ancestry" as "ancestor" + "y" — the quality or record of your ancestors, the family tree that stretches behind you.
把"ancestry"想成"ancestor"（祖先）+"y"——你祖先的记录，身后延伸的家族谱系。
