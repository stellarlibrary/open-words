# ⛈️ adverse

🔊 /ˈæd.vɜːrs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unfavorable, harmful, or acting against one's interests.
**中文：** 不利的；相反的；敌对的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unfavorable（不利的）
- detrimental（有害的）
- hostile（敌对的）
- harmful（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adverse + effects / conditions / impact / reaction**

👉 Example: The policy had adverse effects on small businesses.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged exposure to pollution can produce **adverse** effects on respiratory and cardiovascular health.
   长期暴露于污染环境会对呼吸和心血管健康产生不利影响。

2. Despite **adverse** weather conditions, the expedition team pressed forward toward the summit.
   尽管天气条件不利，探险队仍继续向山顶挺进。

3. The committee evaluated the **adverse** impact of the proposed regulation on economic growth.
   委员会评估了拟议法规对经济增长的不利影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Adverse" contains "advers-" like "adversary" (enemy) — if something is adverse, it works against you like an enemy.
联想记忆：adverse 与 adversary（敌人）同根，不利的事物就像敌人一样与你作对。
