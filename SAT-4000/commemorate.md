# 🏛️ commemorate

🔊 /kəˈmem.ə.reɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To recall and show respect for a person or event, especially with a ceremony or memorial
**中文：** v. 纪念；庆祝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- memorialize（纪念）
- honor（纪念）
- celebrate（庆祝）
- remember（缅怀）
- pay tribute to（致敬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commemorate an event / commemorate the anniversary / commemorate the fallen / commemorate a victory**

👉 Example: The nation gathered to commemorate the centennial anniversary of its independence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A bronze statue was erected in the town square to **commemorate** the soldiers who sacrificed their lives during the war.
   镇广场上竖立了一座铜像，以纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵们。

2. The university will **commemorate** its founding with a week-long series of academic lectures and cultural celebrations.
   该大学将通过为期一周的学术讲座和文化庆典来纪念其建校。

3. Citizens gathered at the memorial site to **commemorate** the victims of the earthquake on its tenth anniversary.
   市民们聚集在纪念场地，在地震十周年之际缅怀遇难者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-MEMOR-ate" — it contains "memor" like "memory," because to commemorate is to preserve a memory.
把"commemorate"想成"com-MEMOR-ate"——其中包含"memor"，类似"memory（记忆）"，因为纪念就是保存记忆。
