# 🛡️ prudential

🔊 /pruːˈdɛnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or involving prudence; characterized by practical wisdom and careful judgment in managing affairs
**中文：** adj. 谨慎的；明辨的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cautious（谨慎的）
- prudent（审慎的）
- discreet（慎重的）
- judicious（明智的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prudential regulation / prudential measures / prudential oversight / prudential considerations**

👉 Example: The central bank introduced new prudential regulations to safeguard the stability of the financial system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The regulatory agency established stricter **prudential** standards to prevent excessive risk-taking by commercial banking institutions.
   监管机构制定了更严格的审慎标准，以防止商业银行机构过度冒险。

2. Board members raised several **prudential** concerns regarding the proposed merger and its potential impact on shareholder value.
   董事会成员对拟议的合并及其对股东价值的潜在影响提出了几项审慎的关注。

3. The government's **prudential** oversight of the insurance sector has been credited with maintaining market confidence during turbulent periods.
   政府对保险行业的审慎监管被认为在动荡时期维持了市场信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prudential" as "prudent + ial" — it is simply the adjective form meaning "relating to prudence," like the insurance company name that implies careful management.
把"prudential"想成"prudent + ial"——它是"与谨慎有关的"形容词形式，就像那个暗示审慎管理的保险公司名称一样。
