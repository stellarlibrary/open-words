# 🎖️ brigadier

🔊 /ˌbrɪɡəˈdɪr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A military officer of high rank, typically commanding a brigade; a rank above colonel and below major general
**中文：** n. 准将；陆军准将；旅长

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- general（将军）
- commander（指挥官）
- officer（军官）
- colonel（上校）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brigadier general / promoted to brigadier / Brigadier Smith / the brigadier in command**

👉 Example: The brigadier was responsible for coordinating all military operations within the designated sector.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **brigadier** devised a comprehensive tactical plan that ultimately proved decisive in securing victory on the battlefield.
   该准将制定了一项全面的战术计划，最终在战场上取得胜利方面起到了决定性作用。

2. After decades of distinguished military service, Colonel Harris was finally promoted to the rank of **brigadier**.
   经过数十年的杰出军事服务，哈里斯上校终于被提升为准将。

3. The **brigadier** addressed the assembled troops with a solemn speech emphasizing discipline, unity, and unwavering commitment to duty.
   准将向集合的部队发表了庄严的讲话，强调纪律、团结和对职责的坚定承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brigadier" as "brigade" + "-ier" (one who leads) — the person who leads a brigade is a brigadier.
把"brigadier"想成"brigade"（旅）+ "-ier"（领导者）——领导一个旅的人就是准将。
