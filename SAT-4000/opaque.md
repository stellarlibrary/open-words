# 🌑 opaque

🔊 /oʊˈpeɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, verb | 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not transparent; difficult to understand; obscure. (n.) An opaque substance. (v.) To make opaque
**中文：** adj. 不透明的；不传热的；迟钝的；晦涩的 n. 不透明物 vt. 使不透明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obscure（晦涩的）
- murky（模糊的）
- impenetrable（难以理解的）
- nontransparent（不透明的）
- dense（难懂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **opaque glass / opaque language / opaque reasoning / opaque to outsiders**

👉 Example: The committee's opaque decision-making process frustrated journalists seeking transparency and accountability.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist used an **opaque** container to shield the light-sensitive chemicals from any exposure to ultraviolet radiation.
   科学家使用不透明的容器来保护感光化学品免受紫外线辐射的照射。

2. Critics condemned the government's **opaque** budgetary procedures, arguing that citizens deserved full access to financial records.
   批评人士谴责政府不透明的预算程序，认为公民应有权查阅所有财务记录。

3. The philosopher's prose was so **opaque** that even seasoned scholars struggled to decipher the intended meaning of the text.
   这位哲学家的文章如此晦涩，以至于连资深学者都难以解读文本的预期含义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "o-PAQUE" as "oh, PACK" — when something is packed so densely, light cannot pass through, making it opaque.
把"opaque"想成"oh, PACK"——当某物被包裹（pack）得如此紧密时，光线无法穿过，使其变得不透明。
