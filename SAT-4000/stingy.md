# 💰 stingy

🔊 /ˈstɪndʒi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unwilling to give or spend; miserly; lacking generosity
**中文：** adj. 吝啬的，小气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- miserly（吝啬的）
- parsimonious（极度节俭的）
- tightfisted（吝啬的）
- niggardly（小气的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stingy with money / stingy person / stingy about spending / too stingy to tip**

👉 Example: He was so stingy with his wealth that he refused to donate even a small amount to charity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation proved remarkably **stingy** in its charitable contributions despite reporting record-breaking annual profits last quarter.
   尽管上个季度报告了创纪录的年利润，该公司在慈善捐款方面却极为吝啬。

2. Critics accused the government of being **stingy** with foreign aid allocations during the unprecedented humanitarian crisis abroad.
   批评人士指责政府在这场史无前例的海外人道主义危机中对外援拨款过于小气。

3. The **stingy** landlord refused to repair the broken heating system even as winter temperatures plummeted dangerously below freezing.
   那个吝啬的房东拒绝修理坏掉的供暖系统，尽管冬季气温已危险地降至冰点以下。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stingy" as "sting-y" — a stingy person feels a painful sting every time they have to spend money.
把"stingy"想成"sting-y"——吝啬的人每次花钱都像被蜇了一样痛。
