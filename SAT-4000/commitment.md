# 🤝 commitment

🔊 /kəˈmɪtmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A pledge or promise to do something; the state of being dedicated to a cause or activity; an obligation that restricts freedom of action
**中文：** n. 承诺，保证；委任，委托；承担的义务

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dedication（奉献）
- devotion（忠诚）
- obligation（义务）
- pledge（誓言）
- allegiance（忠诚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a commitment / commitment to excellence / long-term commitment / fulfill a commitment**

👉 Example: Her unwavering commitment to social justice inspired an entire generation of activists.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new president demonstrated a strong **commitment** to reforming the education system by allocating unprecedented levels of funding.
   新总统通过拨付前所未有的资金，展示了对教育体制改革的坚定承诺。

2. Maintaining a serious **commitment** to physical fitness requires discipline, consistency, and a willingness to sacrifice leisure time.
   保持对身体健康的认真承诺需要自律、坚持和牺牲休闲时间的意愿。

3. The multinational corporation reaffirmed its **commitment** to sustainable practices by pledging to achieve carbon neutrality within five years.
   这家跨国公司通过承诺在五年内实现碳中和，重申了对可持续发展实践的承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "commitment" as "commit + meant" — what you commit to is what you truly meant to do, a firm promise you intend to keep.
把"commitment"想成"commit + meant"——你承诺的就是你真正打算做的，一个你打算信守的坚定承诺。
