# 🍷 intoxicate

🔊 /ɪnˈtɑːksɪˌkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause someone to lose control of their faculties through alcohol or drugs; to excite or elate someone beyond self-control; to poison
**中文：** v. 使陶醉；使喝醉；使中毒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inebriate（使醉）
- exhilarate（使兴奋）
- elate（使陶醉）
- stupefy（使昏迷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intoxicated by success / intoxicated with power / intoxicating aroma / become intoxicated**

👉 Example: The young entrepreneur became intoxicated by his early success and began taking reckless financial risks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The revolutionary leader appeared **intoxicated** by the adulation of the crowd, which only reinforced his increasingly authoritarian tendencies.
   这位革命领袖似乎被人群的奉承所陶醉，这只是加强了他日益专制的倾向。

2. Researchers discovered that certain chemical compounds found in common household products can **intoxicate** the nervous system if inhaled repeatedly.
   研究人员发现，常见家用产品中的某些化学化合物如果反复吸入会使神经系统中毒。

3. The **intoxicating** fragrance of the rare tropical flowers drew botanists from around the world to study the remote island's ecosystem.
   稀有热带花卉令人陶醉的芬芳吸引了世界各地的植物学家来研究这个偏远岛屿的生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-TOXIC-ate" — it contains the word "toxic" (poisonous). To intoxicate originally meant to poison, and now means to overwhelm with alcohol, drugs, or excitement.
把"intoxicate"想成含有"toxic"（有毒的）——原意是使中毒，现在指被酒精、药物或兴奋情绪所支配。
