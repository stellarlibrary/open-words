# 🌿 unaffected

🔊 /ˌʌnəˈfɛktɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not influenced or changed by something; genuine and natural in manner, without pretension
**中文：** adj. 不受影响的；自然的；真挚的；不做作的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- natural（自然的）
- genuine（真诚的）
- sincere（真挚的）
- unpretentious（不矫饰的）
- artless（朴实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unaffected by / unaffected manner / remain unaffected / seemingly unaffected**

👉 Example: Despite her sudden fame, she remained remarkably unaffected and true to her original character.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coastal villages remained **unaffected** by the economic downturn that devastated major urban centers across the region.
   沿海村庄未受到经济衰退的影响，而该地区的主要城市中心都遭到了严重打击。

2. Her **unaffected** warmth and sincerity instantly put the nervous interviewees at ease during the admissions process.
   她自然真挚的热情立刻让紧张的面试者在录取过程中放松了下来。

3. The scientist's **unaffected** demeanor during the press conference impressed journalists accustomed to sensationalized academic announcements.
   这位科学家在新闻发布会上不做作的举止给习惯了耸人听闻学术声明的记者们留下了深刻印象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-affected" — not putting on an "affect" (a false front), staying natural and genuine, or simply not being affected at all.
把"unaffected"想成"不装腔作势的"——不摆出虚假姿态，保持自然真实；或者就是"不受影响的"。
