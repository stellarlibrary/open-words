# 😞 despair

🔊 /dɪˈspɛr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A complete loss of hope; utter hopelessness. (v.) To lose all hope; to feel that a situation is beyond remedy
**中文：** n. 绝望；令人绝望的人或事 v. 对……绝望；丧失希望

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hopelessness（绝望）
- anguish（痛苦）
- despondency（沮丧）
- desperation（绝望）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **despair of / in despair / drive someone to despair / depths of despair**

👉 Example: After months of failed negotiations, the diplomat began to despair of ever reaching a peaceful resolution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugees expressed profound **despair** as they recounted the harrowing conditions they had endured during their perilous journey.
   难民们在讲述他们在危险旅途中所经历的可怕状况时表达了深深的绝望。

2. Many educators **despair** of adequately preparing students for an increasingly competitive global economy with limited resources.
   许多教育工作者对在资源有限的情况下充分培养学生适应日益激烈的全球经济竞争感到绝望。

3. Despite moments of **despair**, the research team persevered and ultimately achieved a significant breakthrough in renewable energy technology.
   尽管有过绝望的时刻，研究团队仍然坚持不懈，最终在可再生能源技术方面取得了重大突破。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-spair" as "de- (without) + spare (spare hope)" — when you have no spare hope left, you are in despair.
把"despair"想成"de-（没有）+ spare（多余的希望）"——当你没有多余的希望时，你就陷入了绝望。
