# 🔬 organelle

🔊 /ˌɔːrɡəˈnɛl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A specialized structure within a cell that performs a specific function, such as the nucleus, mitochondria, or ribosomes
**中文：** n. 细胞器；细胞器官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cell structure（细胞结构）
- subcellular component（亚细胞组分）
- cellular organ（细胞器官）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **membrane-bound organelle / cellular organelle / organelle function / organelle structure**

👉 Example: The mitochondrion is an organelle responsible for generating most of the cell's energy supply.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each **organelle** within the eukaryotic cell performs a distinct function essential to the organism's survival and growth.
   真核细胞中的每个细胞器都执行对生物体生存和生长至关重要的独特功能。

2. The biologist used an electron microscope to examine the detailed structure of each **organelle** within the tissue sample.
   生物学家使用电子显微镜检查组织样本中每个细胞器的详细结构。

3. Damage to critical **organelle** components, particularly the mitochondria, can lead to significant cellular dysfunction and disease.
   关键细胞器组件的损坏，尤其是线粒体，可能导致严重的细胞功能障碍和疾病。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "organelle" as "organ-elle" — a tiny "organ" (elle means small in French) inside a cell that does a specific job, just like organs in your body.
把"organelle"想成"organ-elle"——细胞内的小"器官"（elle在法语中表示小），执行特定功能，就像身体里的器官一样。
