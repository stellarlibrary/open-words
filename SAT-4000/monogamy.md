# 💍 monogamy

🔊 /məˈnɒɡəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The practice or state of being married to only one person at a time; the condition of having a single mate
**中文：** n. 一夫一妻制；单配偶制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- faithfulness（忠诚）
- fidelity（忠贞）
- exclusivity（排他性）
- devotion（专一）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **practice monogamy / serial monogamy / believe in monogamy / monogamy and fidelity**

👉 Example: Many societies around the world have adopted monogamy as the legally recognized form of marriage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Anthropologists have studied **monogamy** across various cultures to understand its origins and its role in social organization.
   人类学家研究了不同文化中的一夫一妻制，以了解其起源及其在社会组织中的作用。

2. The legal framework of the nation explicitly enforces **monogamy**, prohibiting any individual from entering multiple simultaneous marriages.
   该国的法律框架明确执行一夫一妻制，禁止任何人同时缔结多段婚姻。

3. Biologists discovered that certain species of birds exhibit strict **monogamy**, remaining with a single partner throughout their entire lives.
   生物学家发现某些鸟类表现出严格的单配偶制，终生只与一个伴侣相伴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mono" (one) + "gamy" (marriage) — monogamy means "one marriage," being committed to a single spouse at a time.
把"monogamy"拆成"mono"（一个）+"gamy"（婚姻）——一夫一妻制就是"一次只与一个人结婚"。
