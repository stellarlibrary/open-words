# 🔀 swerve

🔊 /swɜːrv/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To turn sharply; to change direction suddenly; to deviate. (n.) A sudden change in direction; degree of deviation
**中文：** v. 突然转向

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- veer（转向）
- deviate（偏离）
- diverge（偏离）
- dodge（闪避）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **swerve to avoid / swerve off course / swerve from one's principles / a sudden swerve**

👉 Example: The driver had to swerve sharply to avoid the fallen tree blocking the highway.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate refused to **swerve** from her original policy position despite mounting pressure from influential party members.
   尽管来自有影响力的党内成员施加了越来越大的压力，这位候选人仍拒绝偏离她最初的政策立场。

2. The cyclist was forced to **swerve** abruptly when a pedestrian stepped into the designated bicycle lane without warning.
   当一名行人毫无预警地走进自行车专用道时，骑车人被迫突然转向。

3. Critics noted a dramatic **swerve** in the author's literary style, moving from realism toward experimental narrative techniques.
   评论家注意到这位作家的文学风格发生了戏剧性的转变，从现实主义转向实验性叙事技巧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "swerve" as the "s-curve" you make when you suddenly turn your steering wheel to avoid an obstacle.
把"swerve"想成你突然转动方向盘避开障碍物时画出的"S形弯道"。
