# 💉 hypodermic

🔊 /ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A hypodermic syringe or injection. (adj.) Relating to the region immediately beneath the skin; administered beneath the skin
**中文：** n. 皮下注射 adj. 皮下的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- subcutaneous（皮下的）
- injection（注射）
- syringe（注射器）
- needle（针头）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hypodermic needle / hypodermic syringe / hypodermic injection / hypodermic layer**

👉 Example: The nurse prepared a hypodermic needle to administer the vaccine just beneath the patient's skin.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician used a **hypodermic** needle to deliver the medication directly into the subcutaneous tissue of the patient's arm.
   医生使用皮下注射针将药物直接注入患者手臂的皮下组织。

2. Advances in **hypodermic** technology have made modern injections significantly less painful than those administered a century ago.
   皮下注射技术的进步使现代注射比一百年前的注射疼痛感大大降低。

3. The biology textbook explained how **hypodermic** injections bypass the digestive system to deliver drugs more rapidly into the bloodstream.
   生物学教科书解释了皮下注射如何绕过消化系统，更快地将药物输送到血液中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hypo-" (under, beneath) + "dermic" (skin, from Greek "derma") — hypodermic literally means "under the skin."
把"hypodermic"拆分为"hypo-"（在下面）+"dermic"（皮肤，来自希腊语"derma"）——字面意思就是"皮肤之下"。
