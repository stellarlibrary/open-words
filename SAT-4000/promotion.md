# 📈 promotion

🔊 /prəˈmoʊʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of raising someone to a higher rank or position; activity that supports or encourages something; advertising or publicity
**中文：** n. 提升；晋级；宣传；推销；促进；发扬；振兴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advancement（晋升）
- elevation（提升）
- publicity（宣传）
- marketing（营销）
- furtherance（促进）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **earn a promotion / job promotion / sales promotion / promotion of health / promotion campaign**

👉 Example: Her consistent dedication and leadership skills earned her a well-deserved promotion to department director.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The employee's exceptional contributions to the project led directly to her **promotion** to regional manager within just two years.
   该员工对项目的杰出贡献直接促成她在短短两年内晋升为区域经理。

2. The government allocated additional funding for the **promotion** of public health initiatives targeting underserved rural communities nationwide.
   政府为促进针对全国服务不足的农村社区的公共健康倡议拨付了额外资金。

3. A strategic **promotion** campaign significantly boosted consumer awareness and increased quarterly sales figures beyond initial projections.
   一场战略性的促销活动显著提升了消费者认知度，并使季度销售额超出了最初的预期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "promote" + "-tion" — the noun form of promote; just as a motion moves physically, a promotion moves someone up in rank or moves a product into public view.
把"promotion"想成"promote"+"-tion"——promote的名词形式；就像motion是物理移动，promotion是把人往上推或把产品推向公众视野。
