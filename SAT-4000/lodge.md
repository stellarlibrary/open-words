# 🏡 lodge

🔊 /lɑːdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small house or cabin, especially in the countryside; a porter's quarters at the entrance of a building. (v.) To submit formally; to provide temporary accommodation; to become embedded
**中文：** n. 旅馆；门房；山林小屋 v. 提出；寄存；借住

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cabin（小屋）
- chalet（木屋）
- file（提交）
- accommodate（住宿）
- embed（嵌入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lodge a complaint / lodge an appeal / lodge with someone / hunting lodge / ski lodge**

👉 Example: The family decided to lodge a formal complaint with the authorities about the noise violations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The attorney advised her client to **lodge** a formal appeal within thirty days of the court's unfavorable ruling.
   律师建议她的客户在法院作出不利裁决后三十天内提出正式上诉。

2. The mountain **lodge** provided weary travelers with warm shelter, hearty meals, and breathtaking views of the valley below.
   山间小屋为疲惫的旅行者提供了温暖的住所、丰盛的餐食和山谷的壮丽景色。

3. A small bone fragment had **lodged** in the patient's throat, requiring immediate surgical intervention to prevent further complications.
   一小块骨碎片卡在了患者的喉咙里，需要立即进行手术干预以防止进一步并发症。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lodge" as a place where you "log" in for the night — a cozy cabin where travelers stop and stay.
把"lodge"想成你夜间"登记入住"的地方——一间旅客停留住宿的温馨小屋。
