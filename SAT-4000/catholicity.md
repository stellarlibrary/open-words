# 🌐 catholicity

🔊 /ˌkæθəˈlɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being universal in scope or applicability; broad-mindedness and tolerance, especially in tastes or interests
**中文：** n. 普遍性；宽容

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- universality（普遍性）
- broad-mindedness（心胸开阔）
- inclusiveness（包容性）
- tolerance（宽容）
- eclecticism（兼收并蓄）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **catholicity of taste / catholicity of interests / catholicity of spirit / remarkable catholicity**

👉 Example: Her catholicity of taste in literature ranged from classical poetry to contemporary science fiction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was admired for the extraordinary **catholicity** of her research interests, which spanned disciplines from neuroscience to philosophy.
   这位教授因其非凡的研究兴趣普遍性而受到钦佩，研究领域涵盖从神经科学到哲学。

2. His **catholicity** of spirit allowed him to appreciate artistic traditions from vastly different cultures without prejudice or favoritism.
   他的宽容精神使他能够不带偏见地欣赏来自截然不同文化的艺术传统。

3. The journal's editorial policy reflected a commendable **catholicity**, welcoming submissions from scholars of every theoretical persuasion.
   该期刊的编辑方针体现了令人称赞的包容性，欢迎各种理论流派的学者投稿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "catholicity" as rooted in "catholic" meaning "universal" — catholicity is the noun form describing the quality of being all-embracing and open to everything.
把"catholicity"想成源自"catholic"（意为"普遍的"）——catholicity是描述包罗万象、开放接纳一切品质的名词形式。
