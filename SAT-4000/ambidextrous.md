# 🤲 ambidextrous

🔊 /ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to use both hands equally well; unusually skillful or versatile; double-dealing or deceitful
**中文：** 双手灵巧的；非常灵活的；怀有二心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- versatile（多才多艺的）
- dexterous（灵巧的）
- adroit（熟练的）
- two-faced（两面派的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ambidextrous person / ambidextrous player / ambidextrous skills / naturally ambidextrous**

👉 Example: The ambidextrous surgeon could operate with remarkable precision using either hand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ambidextrous** basketball player had a distinct advantage, as defenders could not predict which hand he would use.
   这位双手灵巧的篮球运动员有明显的优势，因为防守者无法预测他会用哪只手。

2. Being naturally **ambidextrous** allowed the artist to continue painting even after injuring her dominant hand in an accident.
   天生双手灵巧使这位艺术家即使在事故中伤了惯用手后仍能继续绘画。

3. The **ambidextrous** politician was known for making contradictory promises to different groups of constituents.
   这位两面派的政治家以向不同选民群体做出相互矛盾的承诺而闻名。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ambi-" (both) + "dextrous" (skilled with hands) — skilled with BOTH hands, like having two right hands.
把"ambidextrous"拆成"ambi-"（两个）+ "dextrous"（灵巧的）——两只手都很灵巧，就像有两只右手一样。
