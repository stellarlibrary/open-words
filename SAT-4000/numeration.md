# 🔢 numeration

🔊 /ˌnjuːməˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The action or process of calculating or assigning a number to something; a method or system of numbering
**中文：** n. 计算；数字的读法；命数法；编号

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- counting（计数）
- enumeration（列举）
- calculation（计算）
- numbering（编号）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **numeration system / decimal numeration / binary numeration / numeration method**

👉 Example: Students must first master the basics of numeration before advancing to algebraic concepts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient Babylonians developed a base-sixty **numeration** system that continues to influence modern timekeeping and angular measurement.
   古巴比伦人发展了六十进制的命数法，至今仍影响着现代的计时和角度测量。

2. Accurate **numeration** of the collected specimens was essential for maintaining proper records throughout the research project.
   对收集的标本进行准确编号对于在整个研究项目中保持正确记录至关重要。

3. The professor devoted an entire lecture to comparing the Roman and Hindu-Arabic **numeration** systems and their historical significance.
   教授用了整整一堂课来比较罗马与印度-阿拉伯命数法及其历史意义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "numeration" as "number + ation" — the action of putting things into numbers, counting or labeling them systematically.
把"numeration"想成"number + ation"——将事物用数字表示的行为，即系统地计数或编号。
