# 👁️ overlook

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fail to notice; to look out over from above; to supervise; to tolerate or forgive. (n.) A scenic viewpoint
**中文：** v. 看漏，忽略；俯瞰，眺望；宽容，放任 n. 忽视；眺望

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- neglect（忽视）
- disregard（漠视）
- survey（俯瞰）
- excuse（宽容）
- miss（遗漏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overlook a mistake / overlook the valley / easily overlooked / overlook the fact that**

👉 Example: The hotel room overlooked a stunning valley, but the inspector overlooked several safety violations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hiring committee **overlooked** her lack of formal credentials because her extensive professional experience was truly exceptional.
   招聘委员会忽略了她缺乏正式资历这一事实，因为她丰富的专业经验确实非同凡响。

2. From the ancient fortress, one could **overlook** the entire coastal region stretching endlessly toward the distant horizon.
   从古老的堡垒上，可以俯瞰整个海岸地区，一直延伸到遥远的地平线。

3. It would be unwise to **overlook** the potential consequences of implementing such a radical reform without thorough deliberation.
   在没有经过深思熟虑的情况下忽视实施如此激进改革的潜在后果是不明智的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-look" — if you "look over" something too quickly, you miss details; if you "look over" from a height, you see a panoramic view.
把"overlook"想成"look over"——如果你太快地"看过"某事，你会遗漏细节；如果你从高处"看过去"，你会看到全景。
