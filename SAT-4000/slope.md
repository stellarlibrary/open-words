# ⛰️ slope

🔊 /sloʊp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A surface that has one end higher than another; an incline; the rate of rise or fall of a line. (v.) To incline or slant at an angle
**中文：** n. 斜率

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incline（斜面）
- gradient（坡度）
- slant（倾斜）
- pitch（坡度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **steep slope / gentle slope / slope of a line / ski slope / slippery slope**

👉 Example: The mathematician calculated the slope of the line to determine the rate of change in the data.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer measured the **slope** of the terrain before designing the drainage system for the new residential development.
   工程师在为新住宅开发项目设计排水系统之前测量了地形的坡度。

2. In calculus, the **slope** of a tangent line at any given point represents the instantaneous rate of change.
   在微积分中，任意给定点的切线斜率代表瞬时变化率。

3. The hiking trail **sloped** gradually upward through dense forest before reaching the exposed mountain summit above the treeline.
   徒步小径穿过茂密的森林逐渐向上倾斜，最终到达林线以上裸露的山顶。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slope" as "slow hope" — climbing a slope is like slowly hoping to reach the top, one step at a time.
把"slope"想成"slow hope（缓慢的希望）"——爬坡就像慢慢地希望到达山顶，一步一步来。
