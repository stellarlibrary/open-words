# 🧪 synthetic

🔊 /sɪnˈθɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Made by chemical synthesis to imitate a natural product; relating to or involving synthesis. (n.) A synthetic material or chemical
**中文：** adj. 合成的，人造的；综合的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- artificial（人造的）
- man-made（人工的）
- fabricated（制造的）
- imitation（仿造的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **synthetic materials / synthetic fibers / synthetic chemicals / synthetic approach**

👉 Example: Many modern fabrics are made from synthetic fibers rather than natural cotton or wool.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company invested heavily in developing **synthetic** alternatives to rare natural compounds found in plants.
   该制药公司大力投资开发植物中稀有天然化合物的合成替代品。

2. Critics argued that the **synthetic** flavors used in processed foods could pose long-term health risks to consumers.
   批评者认为，加工食品中使用的合成香料可能对消费者构成长期健康风险。

3. The researcher adopted a **synthetic** approach, combining insights from biology, chemistry, and engineering into one model.
   研究人员采用了一种综合方法，将生物学、化学和工程学的见解融合到一个模型中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "synthetic" as "synth-etic" — like a synthesizer that creates artificial sounds, synthetic things are artificially created.
把"synthetic"想成"synth-etic"——就像合成器创造人工声音一样，合成的东西是人工制造的。
