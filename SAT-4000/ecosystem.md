# 🌿 ecosystem

🔊 /ˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A biological community of interacting organisms and their physical environment; a complex network of interconnected systems
**中文：** n. 生态系统；（相互关联的）系统网络

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- biome（生物群落）
- habitat（栖息地）
- environment（环境）
- biosphere（生物圈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fragile ecosystem / marine ecosystem / digital ecosystem / ecosystem services / disrupt the ecosystem**

👉 Example: The coral reef ecosystem supports thousands of marine species and is vital to coastal communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Industrial pollution has severely disrupted the freshwater **ecosystem**, leading to a dramatic decline in native fish populations.
   工业污染严重破坏了淡水生态系统，导致本地鱼类数量急剧下降。

2. The technology company sought to build an integrated digital **ecosystem** connecting its hardware, software, and cloud services seamlessly.
   该科技公司试图建立一个集成的数字生态系统，将其硬件、软件和云服务无缝连接。

3. Conservation biologists emphasize that protecting an entire **ecosystem** is more effective than focusing on individual endangered species alone.
   保护生物学家强调，保护整个生态系统比仅关注单个濒危物种更为有效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eco-system" as "eco" (home, environment) + "system" — a complete system that makes up a living home for organisms.
把"ecosystem"想成"eco"（家、环境）+"system"（系统）——一个构成生物生存家园的完整系统。
