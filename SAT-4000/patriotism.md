# 🏳️ patriotism

🔊 /ˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Devotion to and vigorous support for one's country; love of one's homeland and willingness to defend it
**中文：** n. 爱国心，爱国精神；爱国主义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nationalism（民族主义）
- devotion（忠诚）
- allegiance（效忠）
- loyalty（忠心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sense of patriotism / patriotism and duty / display patriotism / fervent patriotism / blind patriotism**

👉 Example: The veterans' ceremony inspired a renewed sense of patriotism among the community members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian argued that genuine **patriotism** requires citizens to engage critically with their nation's policies rather than accept them unconditionally.
   这位历史学家认为，真正的爱国主义要求公民批判性地审视国家政策，而非无条件地接受。

2. During times of national crisis, expressions of **patriotism** often intensify as communities unite around shared values and collective purpose.
   在国家危机时期，爱国主义的表达往往会增强，因为社区围绕共同价值观和集体目标团结起来。

3. The philosopher distinguished between healthy **patriotism** rooted in civic responsibility and dangerous nationalism driven by ethnic exclusion.
   这位哲学家区分了根植于公民责任的健康爱国主义和由种族排斥驱动的危险民族主义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "patri-otism" as coming from "patria" (Latin for fatherland) — patriotism is love for your fatherland.
把"patriotism"想成来自"patria"（拉丁语中的"祖国"）——爱国主义就是对祖国的热爱。
