# 💃 sprightly

🔊 /ˈspraɪtli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lively, energetic, and full of spirit; cheerful and vivacious. (adv.) In a lively manner
**中文：** adj. 轻快的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lively（活泼的）
- vivacious（充满活力的）
- energetic（精力充沛的）
- animated（生气勃勃的）
- nimble（敏捷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sprightly pace / sprightly manner / sprightly dance / sprightly conversation**

👉 Example: Despite her advanced age, the professor maintained a sprightly demeanor that inspired all her students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **sprightly** octogenarian completed the morning hike with remarkable ease, outpacing many of the younger participants.
   这位精神矍铄的八旬老人轻松完成了晨间徒步，步伐超过了许多年轻参与者。

2. The orchestra performed a **sprightly** rendition of the concerto that captivated the audience from the very first note.
   管弦乐队演奏了一首轻快的协奏曲，从第一个音符起就吸引了观众。

3. Her **sprightly** wit and sharp intellect made her the most engaging participant in every academic symposium she attended.
   她轻快的机智和敏锐的才智使她成为每次学术研讨会上最引人入胜的参与者。

把"sprightly"想成"sprite-ly"——像精灵一样，轻盈而充满活力，在空中翩翩起舞。
