# ⚡ execute

🔊 /ˈeksɪkjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To carry out or put into effect a plan, order, or course of action; to perform an activity skillfully; to put to death as a legal punishment
**中文：** v. 实行，实施，执行，完成，实现；履行；处决

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- implement（实施）
- accomplish（完成）
- perform（执行）
- carry out（实行）
- fulfill（履行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **execute a plan / execute a strategy / execute an order / execute a task**

👉 Example: The team executed the project plan flawlessly, meeting every deadline without exception.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military commander ordered his troops to **execute** the carefully devised strategy before dawn.
   军事指挥官命令部队在黎明前执行精心策划的战略。

2. The architect was praised for her ability to **execute** complex designs with remarkable precision and artistry.
   这位建筑师因能以卓越的精确度和艺术性来实现复杂设计而受到赞扬。

3. The government must **execute** its fiscal policies effectively to maintain economic stability during uncertain times.
   政府必须有效执行其财政政策，以在不确定时期保持经济稳定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exe-cute" — an ".exe" file executes a program on your computer, just as you execute a plan in real life.
把"execute"想成"exe-cute"——电脑上的".exe"文件执行程序，就像你在现实中执行计划一样。
