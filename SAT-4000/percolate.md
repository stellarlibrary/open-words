# 💧 percolate

🔊 /ˈpɜːrkəleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To filter or seep through a porous substance; to spread gradually; to brew coffee by passing hot water through grounds. (n.) A filtered liquid
**中文：** v. 过滤；渗出；浸透 n. 滤过液；渗出液

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- seep（渗透）
- filter（过滤）
- permeate（弥漫）
- ooze（渗出）
- infiltrate（渗入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **percolate through / percolate down / percolate into / ideas percolate / let it percolate**

👉 Example: The innovative ideas from the research department began to percolate through the entire organization over several months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Rainwater slowly **percolated** through the layers of soil and rock before reaching the underground aquifer far below.
   雨水慢慢渗透过土壤和岩石层，最终到达深处的地下含水层。

2. The professor allowed the complex theory to **percolate** in her mind before presenting her refined interpretation to colleagues.
   教授让这个复杂的理论在她脑中慢慢酝酿，然后才向同事们展示她的精炼解读。

3. New cultural trends often **percolate** from urban centers to rural communities over the course of several years.
   新的文化趋势通常在几年时间内从城市中心逐渐渗透到农村社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "percolate" as a coffee percolator — water slowly filters through the coffee grounds, just as ideas or liquids percolate through barriers.
把"percolate"想成咖啡渗滤壶——水慢慢过滤通过咖啡粉，就像想法或液体渗透过屏障一样。
