# 🏅 competence

🔊 /ˈkɒmpɪtəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability to do something successfully or efficiently; the legal authority of a court or other body to deal with a particular matter
**中文：** n. 能力；胜任；权限

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proficiency（精通）
- capability（能力）
- aptitude（资质）
- expertise（专长）
- skill（技能）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **core competence / professional competence / demonstrate competence / within the competence of / linguistic competence**

👉 Example: The hiring committee assessed each candidate's professional competence through a series of practical examinations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board questioned the manager's **competence** after a series of costly operational failures undermined the department's annual performance.
   在一系列代价高昂的运营失误损害了部门年度业绩后，董事会质疑了该经理的能力。

2. Demonstrating technical **competence** in data analysis has become an essential requirement for advancement in most modern professional fields.
   展示数据分析方面的技术能力已成为大多数现代专业领域晋升的基本要求。

3. The tribunal first had to establish its own legal **competence** before it could proceed to hear the international dispute.
   法庭在审理这一国际争端之前，首先必须确定自身的法律管辖权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "competence" as "compete + ence" — the quality that allows you to compete effectively, meaning you have the ability and skill to succeed.
把"competence"想成"compete（竞争）+ ence（名词后缀）"——使你能够有效竞争的品质，意味着你有能力和技能取得成功。
