# 🔢 quotient

🔊 /ˈkwoʊʃənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The result obtained by dividing one quantity by another; a degree or amount of a specified quality or characteristic
**中文：** n. 商；系数；份额

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- result（结果）
- ratio（比率）
- proportion（比例）
- degree（程度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intelligence quotient / emotional quotient / quotient of / high quotient**

👉 Example: Researchers developed a new metric to measure the happiness quotient of citizens across different nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **quotient** obtained by dividing total revenue by the number of employees provides a useful measure of workforce productivity.
   用总收入除以员工人数所得的商提供了衡量劳动力生产率的有用指标。

2. Psychologists argue that emotional **quotient** is at least as important as intelligence quotient in determining long-term professional success.
   心理学家认为，情商在决定长期职业成功方面至少与智商同样重要。

3. The city's cultural **quotient** has risen dramatically since the opening of several internationally acclaimed museums and performance venues.
   自从几座享誉国际的博物馆和演出场所开放以来，这座城市的文化水平大幅提升。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quotient" as "quota + ient" — it measures what portion or quota you get when dividing something into equal parts.
把"quotient"想成"quota + ient"——它衡量的是把某物等分时你得到的配额或份额。
