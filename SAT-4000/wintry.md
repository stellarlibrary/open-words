# ❄️ wintry

🔊 /ˈwɪntri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Characteristic of winter; cold in weather or manner; lacking warmth or friendliness
**中文：** adj. 寒冷的，冬天的；冷淡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frigid（寒冷的）
- icy（冰冷的）
- bleak（萧瑟的）
- frosty（冷淡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wintry weather / wintry landscape / wintry smile / wintry reception / wintry blast**

👉 Example: The ambassador received a wintry reception from her counterparts, signaling deep diplomatic tensions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **wintry** landscape stretched endlessly before the expedition team, its frozen terrain presenting formidable challenges to their progress.
   冬日的荒凉景观在探险队面前无边无际地延伸，冰冻的地形对他们的前进构成了巨大的挑战。

2. The chairman's **wintry** demeanor during the board meeting discouraged junior executives from voicing their innovative but unconventional proposals.
   董事长在董事会议上冷淡的态度使初级管理人员不敢表达他们创新但非传统的提议。

3. Meteorologists predicted that **wintry** conditions would persist throughout the region, prompting authorities to issue severe weather advisories.
   气象学家预测寒冷的天气状况将在整个地区持续，促使当局发布恶劣天气预警。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wintry" as "winter" + "y" — just like winter itself, wintry describes anything cold, harsh, and unwelcoming.
把"wintry"想成"winter（冬天）+ y"——就像冬天本身一样，wintry描述任何寒冷、严酷和不友好的事物。
