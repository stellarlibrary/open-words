# 🐾 bestial

🔊 /ˈbɛstʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Resembling a beast; savagely cruel, brutal, or depraved; lacking human qualities
**中文：** adj. 兽性的，卑劣的；残忍的；野蛮的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brutal（残暴的）
- savage（野蛮的）
- barbarous（残忍的）
- inhuman（不人道的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bestial behavior / bestial cruelty / bestial instincts / bestial nature**

👉 Example: The tribunal documented the bestial treatment inflicted upon prisoners during the prolonged armed conflict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court condemned the **bestial** acts committed against civilians, calling them a grave violation of international law.
   法庭谴责了针对平民的兽性行为，称其严重违反了国际法。

2. Historians have long debated what societal conditions enabled such **bestial** cruelty to emerge during that dark period.
   历史学家长期争论是什么社会条件导致了那个黑暗时期如此兽性的残忍行为。

3. The philosopher argued that unchecked power inevitably awakens the **bestial** instincts lurking within human nature.
   这位哲学家认为，不受约束的权力必然会唤醒潜伏在人性中的兽性本能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "BEST-ial" — from "beast." Bestial describes behavior that belongs to a beast, not a civilized human being.
想想"BEST-ial"——来自"beast"（野兽）。Bestial描述的是属于野兽的行为，而非文明人类的行为。
