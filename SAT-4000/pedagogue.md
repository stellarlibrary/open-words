# 👨‍🏫 pedagogue

🔊 /ˈpedəˌɡɑːɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A teacher or educator, especially one who is strict, formal, or pedantic in teaching methods
**中文：** n. 教师，教员；卖弄学问者；喜欢卖弄学问的教师

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- instructor（讲师）
- educator（教育者）
- tutor（导师）
- schoolmaster（校长）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **strict pedagogue / old-fashioned pedagogue / a pedantic pedagogue / revered pedagogue**

👉 Example: The stern pedagogue insisted on absolute silence and rigid discipline in the classroom at all times.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **pedagogue** transformed the struggling school into one of the most prestigious academic institutions in the region.
   这位著名的教育家将一所处于困境中的学校转变为该地区最负盛名的学术机构之一。

2. Critics dismissed the professor as an insufferable **pedagogue** who prioritized rote memorization over genuine intellectual curiosity.
   批评者认为这位教授是一个令人难以忍受的学究，他重视死记硬背而非真正的求知欲。

3. The ancient Greek **pedagogue** was originally a slave who accompanied children to school and supervised their daily conduct.
   古希腊的"教仆"最初是一名陪伴孩子上学并监督其日常行为的奴隶。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peda-gogue" as "ped" (child) + "agogue" (leader) — a pedagogue literally "leads children" in their learning.
把"pedagogue"想成"ped"（孩子）+"agogue"（引导者）——pedagogue字面意思就是"引导孩子"学习的人。
