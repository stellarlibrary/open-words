# 🎭 presumption

🔊 /prɪˈzʌmpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An assumption that something is true based on probability; audacious or arrogant behavior that oversteps proper bounds
**中文：** n. 推测；假定；放肆；傲慢

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assumption（假设）
- supposition（推测）
- audacity（放肆）
- arrogance（傲慢）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **presumption of innocence / on the presumption that / have the presumption to**

👉 Example: The legal system operates on the presumption of innocence until guilt is proven beyond reasonable doubt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court upheld the **presumption** of innocence, ruling that insufficient evidence had been presented by the prosecution.
   法院维持了无罪推定原则，裁定控方提交的证据不足。

2. It was sheer **presumption** on his part to assume he would be appointed chairman without consulting the other board members.
   他未经咨询其他董事会成员就假设自己会被任命为主席，这纯属放肆。

3. The researcher proceeded on the **presumption** that the experimental conditions would remain stable throughout the entire trial period.
   研究人员在整个试验期间基于实验条件将保持稳定的假定来推进工作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-sump-tion" as "summing up before knowing" — you form a sum (conclusion) before you have all the facts.
把"presumption"想成"pre-sump-tion"——在了解全部事实之前就"sum（总结）"了，即提前下结论。
