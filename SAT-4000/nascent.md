# 🌅 nascent

🔊 /ˈnæsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Just beginning to develop; in the earliest stage of growth or formation; emerging
**中文：** adj. 初期的；初生的；开始存在的，发生中的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emerging（新兴的）
- budding（萌芽的）
- incipient（初期的）
- embryonic（萌芽期的）
- fledgling（初出茅庐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nascent industry / nascent democracy / nascent stage / nascent technology / nascent movement**

👉 Example: The nascent technology sector in the region has attracted significant venture capital investment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **nascent** democracy struggled to establish stable institutions amid persistent economic turmoil and political fragmentation.
   这个**新生的**民主国家在持续的经济动荡和政治分裂中艰难地建立稳定的制度。

2. Investors recognized the enormous potential of the **nascent** renewable energy industry and committed substantial long-term capital.
   投资者认识到**新兴**可再生能源产业的巨大潜力，投入了大量长期资金。

3. The **nascent** social movement quickly gained momentum as thousands of citizens rallied behind its call for institutional reform.
   这场**初生的**社会运动迅速获得动力，数千名公民响应其制度改革的号召。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nascent" as related to "natal" (birth) — something nascent is just being born, in its very first stage of existence.
把"nascent"想成和"natal"（出生）相关——nascent的事物刚刚诞生，处于存在的最初阶段。
