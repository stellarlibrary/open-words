# 💎 essence

🔊 /ˈɛsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The intrinsic nature or most important quality of something; a concentrated extract or perfume
**中文：** n. 本质，实质；精华；香精

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- core（核心）
- quintessence（精髓）
- substance（实质）
- crux（关键）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the essence of / in essence / capture the essence / essence of time**

👉 Example: In essence, the proposal seeks to reform the entire education system from the ground up.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that the **essence** of human existence lies in the relentless pursuit of knowledge and meaning.
   这位哲学家认为，人类存在的本质在于对知识和意义的不懈追求。

2. The perfumer carefully distilled the **essence** of rare jasmine flowers to create an exquisite and unforgettable fragrance.
   调香师精心蒸馏珍稀茉莉花的精华，创造出一种精美难忘的香氛。

3. Critics maintained that the adaptation failed to capture the **essence** of the original novel's complex moral themes.
   评论家认为这部改编作品未能捕捉到原著复杂道德主题的精髓。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "essence" as "essential sense" — the essential sense or core meaning at the heart of something.
把"essence"想成"essential sense"——某事物核心中最本质的含义。
