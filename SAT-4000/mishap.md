# ⚠ mishap

🔊 /ˈmɪshæp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An unlucky accident or unfortunate incident; a piece of bad luck causing minor damage or inconvenience
**中文：** n. 灾祸；不幸事故；晦气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accident（事故）
- misadventure（不幸遭遇）
- setback（挫折）
- calamity（灾难）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **without mishap / a minor mishap / a series of mishaps / suffer a mishap**

👉 Example: The expedition was completed without mishap despite the treacherous mountain terrain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A minor **mishap** in the laboratory resulted in the accidental discovery of a compound with remarkable antibacterial properties.
   实验室中的一次小事故意外地发现了一种具有显著抗菌特性的化合物。

2. The construction project suffered several costly **mishaps** due to inadequate safety protocols and poorly trained personnel.
   由于安全规程不完善和人员培训不足，该建设项目遭受了几次代价高昂的事故。

3. Despite a brief **mishap** during the opening ceremony, the international conference proceeded smoothly and achieved all of its objectives.
   尽管开幕式期间出现了一个小意外，这次国际会议仍然进展顺利并实现了所有目标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-hap" as "mis-happen" — something that happened wrongly or badly, an unfortunate happening.
把"mishap"想成"mis-happen（发生错误）"——某件事发生了差错，一次不幸的事件。
