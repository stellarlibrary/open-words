# 💥 impact

🔊 /ˈɪmpækt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A strong effect or influence; a collision or forceful contact. (v.) To have a strong effect on something or someone
**中文：** n. 影响；效果；碰撞 v. 对…产生影响

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- effect（效果）
- influence（影响）
- consequence（后果）
- impression（印象）
- collision（碰撞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have an impact on / make an impact / significant impact / environmental impact**

👉 Example: The new regulation will have a profound impact on the manufacturing industry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economic recession had a devastating **impact** on small businesses, forcing many to close permanently.
   经济衰退对小型企业产生了毁灭性的影响，迫使许多企业永久关闭。

2. Researchers are studying how climate change will **impact** agricultural productivity in developing nations over the next decade.
   研究人员正在研究气候变化在未来十年将如何影响发展中国家的农业生产力。

3. The meteorite struck the surface with tremendous **impact**, creating a crater several kilometers wide and incredibly deep.
   陨石以巨大的冲击力撞击地表，形成了一个数公里宽、极深的陨石坑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-pact" as "in-pack" — when something is packed into you forcefully, it makes a strong impact.
把"impact"想成"im-pact（紧紧压入）"——当某物用力压进来时，就会产生强烈的影响。
