# 🔒 indubitable

🔊 /ɪnˈdjuːbɪtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Impossible to doubt; unquestionable; clearly true or certain
**中文：** adj. 不容置疑的；确实的；明白的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unquestionable（无可置疑的）
- indisputable（不可争辩的）
- certain（确定的）
- undeniable（不可否认的）
- irrefutable（不可反驳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indubitable fact / indubitable evidence / indubitable proof / indubitable truth**

👉 Example: The forensic evidence provided indubitable proof that the suspect had been present at the crime scene.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeological discovery offered **indubitable** evidence that advanced civilizations had thrived in the region thousands of years earlier.
   这一考古发现提供了不容置疑的证据，证明数千年前该地区曾有先进文明繁荣发展。

2. Her **indubitable** talent for mathematics became apparent when she solved the complex theorem that had puzzled professors for decades.
   当她解决了困扰教授们数十年的复杂定理时，她毋庸置疑的数学天赋变得显而易见。

3. The senator presented **indubitable** data demonstrating that the proposed policy would significantly reduce carbon emissions within five years.
   这位参议员提供了确凿的数据，证明所提议的政策将在五年内显著减少碳排放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-DUBIT-able" as "not doubt-able" — the Latin root "dubitare" means "to doubt," so indubitable means beyond all doubt.
把"indubitable"想成"in(不)+dubit(怀疑)+able(能够)"——拉丁词根"dubitare"意为"怀疑"，所以indubitable就是不容怀疑的。
