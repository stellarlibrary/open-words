# 😰 desperate

🔊 /ˈdɛspərɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling or showing hopelessness; reckless because of despair; extremely urgent or critical
**中文：** adj. 令人绝望的；不顾一切的；极度渴望的；危急的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frantic（疯狂的）
- hopeless（绝望的）
- reckless（不顾一切的）
- dire（紧急的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **desperate attempt / desperate measures / desperate for / desperate situation**

👉 Example: The stranded hikers made a desperate attempt to signal rescue helicopters before nightfall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company took **desperate** measures to avoid bankruptcy, including massive layoffs and the liquidation of key assets.
   公司采取了不顾一切的措施来避免破产，包括大规模裁员和关键资产的清算。

2. Residents of the flood-ravaged town were **desperate** for clean drinking water and emergency medical supplies.
   遭受洪水蹂躏的城镇居民极度渴望获得洁净的饮用水和紧急医疗物资。

3. The surgeon performed a **desperate** operation that ultimately saved the critically injured patient against overwhelming odds.
   外科医生进行了一次孤注一掷的手术，最终在极大的困难下挽救了那位伤势严重的病人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "desperate" as "de-spare-ate" — you have eaten all your spare resources, leaving you desperate with nothing left.
把"desperate"想成"de-spare-ate"——你已经消耗了所有多余的资源，变得山穷水尽，不顾一切。
