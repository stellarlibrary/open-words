# 🚢 naval

🔊 /ˈneɪvəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to a navy or warships; connected with military operations at sea
**中文：** adj. 海军的；军舰的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- maritime（海事的）
- nautical（航海的）
- marine（海军陆战队的）
- seafaring（航海的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **naval base / naval officer / naval warfare / naval fleet / naval academy**

👉 Example: The naval fleet was deployed to patrol the contested waters in the South Pacific.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The country invested billions of dollars in modernizing its **naval** fleet to maintain strategic dominance across key maritime routes.
   该国投入数十亿美元对其海军舰队进行现代化改造，以维持对关键海上航线的战略主导地位。

2. The **naval** academy graduated its largest class in decades, reflecting the government's commitment to strengthening maritime defense.
   海军学院毕业了数十年来最大的一届学员，反映了政府加强海防的决心。

3. Historians regard the Battle of Midway as a decisive **naval** engagement that fundamentally altered the course of the war.
   历史学家将中途岛海战视为一场决定性的海军交战，它从根本上改变了战争的进程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "naval" as "navy" + "-al" — the adjective form of navy, describing anything related to military forces at sea.
把"naval"想成"navy"加"-al"——navy的形容词形式，描述与海上军事力量有关的一切。
