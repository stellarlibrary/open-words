# ⚖️ equitable

🔊 /ˈɛkwɪtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Fair and impartial; treating all parties with justice and equal consideration
**中文：** adj. 公平的；公正的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fair（公平的）
- just（公正的）
- impartial（不偏不倚的）
- unbiased（无偏见的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equitable distribution / equitable treatment / equitable solution / equitable access**

👉 Example: The judge sought an equitable resolution that would satisfy both parties in the dispute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee proposed an **equitable** distribution of resources to ensure that underprivileged communities received adequate support and funding.
   委员会提出了公平的资源分配方案，以确保弱势社区获得充分的支持和资金。

2. An **equitable** education system must provide every student with equal opportunities regardless of socioeconomic background or geographic location.
   公平的教育体系必须为每位学生提供平等的机会，无论其社会经济背景或地理位置如何。

3. The mediator worked tirelessly to craft an **equitable** agreement that addressed the legitimate concerns of all stakeholders involved.
   调解人不知疲倦地努力制定一项公平的协议，以解决所有相关利益方的合理关切。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equit-able" as "equity-able" — able to provide equity and fairness to everyone.
把"equitable"想成"equity（公平）+ able（能够的）"——能够为每个人提供公平。
