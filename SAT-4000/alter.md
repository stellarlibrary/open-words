# 🔄 alter

🔊 /ˈɔːltər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To change or cause to change in character, composition, or appearance; to modify or adjust.
**中文：** 改变；更改；修改

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modify（修改）
- change（改变）
- revise（修订）
- adjust（调整）
- amend（修正）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alter dramatically, alter significantly, alter the course of, alter one's appearance, alter a plan**
👉 Example: The discovery of new evidence compelled the investigators to alter their original hypothesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect was forced to **alter** the building's design to comply with newly enacted safety regulations.
   建筑师被迫修改建筑设计以符合新颁布的安全法规。

2. A single technological innovation can **alter** the trajectory of an entire industry within a few short years.
   一项技术创新可以在短短几年内改变整个行业的发展轨迹。

3. The tailor agreed to **alter** the suit before the ceremony so that it would fit the client perfectly.
   裁缝同意在典礼前修改西装，以便完美地合身。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alter" as sharing a root with "alternative" — when you alter something, you make it into an alternative version of itself.
把 alter 和 alternative（替代方案）联系起来——当你 alter 某物时，你就把它变成了另一个版本。
