# 😩 whine

🔊 /waɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To complain in a feeble or petulant way; to make a long, high-pitched sound. (n.) A complaint or whining sound
**中文：** v. 发牢骚；哭诉；发呜呜声 n. 抱怨；牢骚；哀鸣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- complain（抱怨）
- grumble（发牢骚）
- moan（呻吟）
- wail（哀号）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **whine about / whine and complain / stop whining / a constant whine**

👉 Example: The students continued to whine about the difficulty of the examination despite ample preparation time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator dismissed critics who merely **whine** about economic inequality without proposing constructive legislative solutions.
   这位参议员驳斥了那些只会对经济不平等发牢骚却不提出建设性立法方案的批评者。

2. A persistent **whine** from the malfunctioning engine alerted the engineers to a serious mechanical defect requiring immediate attention.
   发动机故障发出的持续呜呜声提醒工程师们注意一个需要立即处理的严重机械缺陷。

3. Effective leaders address legitimate concerns promptly rather than dismissing every objection as an employee's tendency to **whine**.
   高效的领导者会迅速处理合理的关切，而不是将每一个反对意见都视为员工的牢骚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "whine" sounding like "wine" — imagine someone whining because they spilled their wine, crying with a high-pitched voice.
把"whine"想成和"wine（葡萄酒）"同音——想象有人因为打翻了葡萄酒而哭诉抱怨，发出尖细的声音。
