# 🗯️ altercation

🔊 /ˌɔːltərˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A noisy argument or heated disagreement, especially a public one; a verbal dispute or quarrel.
**中文：** 争执；争论；口角

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quarrel（争吵）
- dispute（争论）
- argument（争论）
- confrontation（对抗）
- wrangle（争吵）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verbal altercation, physical altercation, heated altercation, involved in an altercation, altercation between**
👉 Example: The altercation between the two politicians drew widespread media attention across the nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A heated **altercation** erupted between the neighbors over the disputed boundary line separating their properties.
   邻居之间因分隔两家地产的有争议的边界线发生了激烈的争执。

2. The security guard intervened promptly to prevent the verbal **altercation** from escalating into a physical confrontation.
   保安及时介入，防止口头争执升级为肢体冲突。

3. Witnesses reported that the **altercation** began when one driver accused the other of recklessly changing lanes.
   目击者报告说，争执起因是一名司机指责另一名司机鲁莽变道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alter + cation" — when two people try to ALTER each other's opinions, it creates a heated altercation.
把 altercation 想象成两个人试图 alter（改变）对方的观点，互不相让，就变成了 altercation（争执）。
