# 🪨 obdurate

🔊 /ˈɑːbdʊrɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action; hard-hearted and unfeeling.
**中文：** adj. 顽固的，执拗的；冷酷无情的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stubborn（固执的）
- inflexible（不屈的）
- unyielding（不让步的）
- intransigent（不妥协的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obdurate refusal / obdurate stance / remain obdurate / obdurate resistance**

👉 Example: Despite the overwhelming evidence, the defendant remained obdurate in his denial of wrongdoing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **obdurate** negotiator refused every compromise, ultimately causing the diplomatic talks to collapse entirely.
   那位顽固的谈判代表拒绝了所有妥协方案，最终导致外交谈判彻底破裂。

2. Critics condemned the regime's **obdurate** indifference to the suffering of civilians displaced by the conflict.
   批评人士谴责该政权对冲突中流离失所的平民的苦难冷酷无情。

3. The professor's **obdurate** adherence to outdated methods frustrated students who sought more innovative approaches.
   教授对过时方法的执拗坚持让寻求更创新方法的学生们感到沮丧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-DUR-ate" — "durable" is hidden inside, meaning hard and lasting — an obdurate person is hard-hearted and won't budge.
想想"ob-DUR-ate"——里面藏着"durable"（持久的），意思是坚硬持久——obdurate的人铁石心肠，不会动摇。
