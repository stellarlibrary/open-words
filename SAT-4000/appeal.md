# 📢 appeal

🔊 /əˈpiːl/<br>
🏷️ *v. 呼吁；上诉；有吸引力 n. 呼吁；吸引力*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (v.) To make a serious or urgent request; to take a legal case to a higher court for review; to be attractive or interesting. (n.) A serious request for help or support; the quality of being attractive or interesting.

**中文：** （动词）呼吁；上诉；有吸引力。（名词）呼吁；恳求；吸引力；上诉。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- plea 恳求
- attraction 吸引力
- entreaty 恳求
- petition 请愿
- allure 魅力

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** appeal to someone, appeal for something, appeal a decision, broad appeal, mass appeal, lose its appeal

**Example:** "Appeal" has distinct usages: legal (appeal a verdict), emotional (appeal for mercy), and aesthetic (the painting's appeal). The preposition changes with meaning: "appeal to" (attract) vs. "appeal for" (request).

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The humanitarian organization issued an urgent **appeal** for donations to assist victims of the earthquake.
   该人道主义组织发出紧急**呼吁**，为地震灾民募集捐款。

2. The defense attorney decided to **appeal** the verdict, arguing that critical evidence had been improperly excluded.
   辩护律师决定**上诉**，认为关键证据被不当排除。

3. The quaint architecture and vibrant culture of the old town **appeal** strongly to international tourists.
   古镇迷人的建筑和充满活力的文化对国际游客极具**吸引力**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "a + peal" — a peal is a loud ringing of bells. An appeal is like ringing a bell loudly to get people's attention or attract them.

谐音联想：appeal 可拆为"a + peal"（钟声）——大声敲钟引起注意，就像"呼吁"或"吸引"别人。
