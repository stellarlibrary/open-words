# 🤣 hilarious

🔊 /hɪˈlɛəriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely funny; causing great amusement and uncontrollable laughter
**中文：** adj. 欢闹的；非常滑稽的；极其可笑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- uproarious（令人捧腹的）
- hysterical（极其滑稽的）
- amusing（有趣的）
- comical（滑稽的）
- side-splitting（令人笑破肚皮的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hilarious joke / hilarious comedy / hilarious story / absolutely hilarious / find something hilarious**

👉 Example: The audience found the comedian's improvised performance absolutely hilarious from start to finish.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The satirical novel was so **hilarious** that reviewers unanimously praised it as the funniest work of literary fiction published that entire decade.
   这部讽刺小说非常滑稽，评论家一致称赞它为那整个十年出版的最有趣的文学小说作品。

2. Students found the professor's **hilarious** anecdotes about laboratory disasters to be unexpectedly effective at illustrating fundamental principles of chemistry.
   学生们发现教授关于实验室灾难的滑稽轶事出人意料地有效说明了化学的基本原理。

3. The documentary included several **hilarious** behind-the-scenes moments that revealed the actors' genuine camaraderie and spontaneous wit during production.
   这部纪录片包含了几个非常搞笑的幕后时刻，揭示了演员们在制作期间真正的友情和即兴机智。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hilarity" — hilarious is full of hilarity; picture everyone on a hill laughing so hard they roll down, "hill-arious."
想到"hilarity"（欢闹）——hilarious就是充满欢笑；想象每个人在山丘（hill）上笑得滚下来，"hill-arious"。
