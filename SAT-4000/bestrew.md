# 🌸 bestrew

🔊 /bɪˈstruː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To scatter or strew things over a surface; to cover by strewing objects upon
**中文：** v. 布满；散布

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scatter（散布）
- strew（撒满）
- sprinkle（洒落）
- litter（散落）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bestrew with / bestrew something over / bestrewn with**

👉 Example: The battlefield was bestrewn with fragments of shattered equipment and abandoned supplies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the festival, the town square was **bestrewn** with flower petals, confetti, and remnants of celebratory decorations.
   节日过后，城镇广场上布满了花瓣、五彩纸屑和庆祝装饰品的残余物。

2. The autumn wind **bestrewed** the garden path with golden leaves, creating a picturesque scene of seasonal transition.
   秋风将金色的落叶散布在花园小径上，构成了一幅美丽的季节更替画面。

3. Ancient manuscripts lay **bestrewn** across the scholar's desk, evidence of months of intensive archival research.
   古老的手稿散布在学者的书桌上，是数月密集档案研究的证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-STREW" — it literally contains "strew," which means to scatter. Bestrew is simply the intensified form: to scatter things all over.
想想"be-STREW"——它字面包含"strew"（撒满）。Bestrew就是其加强形式：把东西撒得到处都是。
