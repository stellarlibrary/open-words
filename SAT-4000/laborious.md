# 💪 laborious

🔊 /ləˈbɔːriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Requiring considerable effort and time; industrious and hard-working; showing signs of great effort
**中文：** adj. 艰苦的，费力的；勤劳的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arduous（艰苦的）
- strenuous（费力的）
- painstaking（辛苦的）
- toilsome（繁重的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a laborious task / laborious process / laborious effort / laborious work**

👉 Example: The translation of the ancient manuscripts proved to be a far more laborious undertaking than anyone had anticipated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The restoration of the medieval cathedral was a **laborious** process that required the combined expertise of dozens of specialists.
   修复这座中世纪大教堂是一个艰苦的过程，需要数十位专家的共同努力。

2. Scientists undertook the **laborious** task of cataloguing every known species in the remote archipelago over a period of five years.
   科学家们花了五年时间完成了对偏远群岛上每个已知物种进行编目的艰巨任务。

3. The committee acknowledged that reviewing thousands of applications was **laborious** but essential for maintaining academic standards.
   委员会承认审查数千份申请虽然费力，但对于维持学术标准至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laborious" as "labor + ious" — full of labor, meaning something that requires a great deal of hard work and effort.
把"laborious"想成"labor（劳动）+ ious（充满的）"——充满劳动的，意味着需要大量的辛勤工作和努力。
