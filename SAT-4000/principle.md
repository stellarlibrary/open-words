# ⚖️ principle

🔊 /ˈprɪnsəpəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A fundamental truth or proposition that serves as the foundation for a system of belief or reasoning; a rule or standard of good behavior
**中文：** n. 原理，原则；主义，道义；本质，本义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tenet（信条）
- doctrine（教义）
- axiom（公理）
- precept（准则）
- canon（标准）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on principle / basic principle / guiding principle / a matter of principle / in principle**

👉 Example: She refused the offer on principle, believing that accepting it would compromise her professional integrity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientific **principle** of conservation of energy states that energy cannot be created or destroyed in an isolated system.
   能量守恒的科学原理指出，在孤立系统中能量不能被创造或毁灭。

2. He was a man of strong **principles** who consistently refused to compromise his ethical standards for personal financial gain.
   他是一个有坚定原则的人，始终拒绝为了个人经济利益而放弃自己的道德标准。

3. The committee agreed in **principle** to support the proposed reforms but requested additional data before making a final commitment.
   委员会原则上同意支持拟议的改革，但在做出最终承诺之前要求提供更多数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "principle" ending in "-ple" like "rule" — a principle is a fundamental rule or truth that guides behavior and reasoning.
把"principle"的结尾"-ple"联想到"rule（规则）"——原则是指导行为和推理的基本规则或真理。
