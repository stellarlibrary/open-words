# ✨ fabulous

🔊 /ˈfæbjələs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extraordinarily good; amazing; resembling or based on fable; mythical or legendary
**中文：** adj. 极好的，极妙的；寓言般的，神话般的；惊人的，难以置信的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- marvelous（非凡的）
- extraordinary（非同寻常的）
- legendary（传奇的）
- incredible（难以置信的）
- magnificent（壮丽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fabulous wealth / fabulous success / fabulous creature / fabulous opportunity**

👉 Example: The museum unveiled a fabulous collection of ancient artifacts discovered during the recent excavation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The expedition returned with tales of **fabulous** riches hidden deep within the unexplored mountain caverns of the region.
   探险队带回了关于该地区未探索的山洞深处隐藏着惊人财富的故事。

2. Ancient texts describe **fabulous** creatures such as griffins and phoenixes that symbolized power and immortality in mythology.
   古代文献描述了狮鹫和凤凰等神话般的生物，它们在神话中象征着力量和永生。

3. The architect presented a **fabulous** design for the new cultural center that impressed every member of the review panel.
   建筑师展示了新文化中心的极好设计方案，给评审小组的每位成员都留下了深刻印象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fabul-ous" as "fable-ous" — something so wonderful it seems to come straight out of a fable or fairy tale.
把"fabulous"想成"fable（寓言）+ ous"——某样东西好到仿佛直接从寓言或童话故事中走出来的。
