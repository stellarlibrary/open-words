# 🌾 furrow

🔊 /ˈfʌroʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A long, narrow trench made by a plow; a wrinkle or groove on the skin, especially the forehead. (v.) To plow; to make grooves or wrinkles in
**中文：** n. 皱纹；犁沟；车辙 v. 犁；耕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- groove（沟槽）
- wrinkle（皱纹）
- crease（褶痕）
- trench（沟渠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **furrow one's brow / plow a furrow / deep furrows / furrowed field**

👉 Example: She furrowed her brow in concentration as she reviewed the complicated legal document.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The farmer guided his plow steadily across the field, cutting deep **furrows** into the rich, dark soil.
   农夫稳稳地驾驶犁穿过田地，在肥沃的黑土上犁出深深的沟槽。

2. Years of anxiety had etched permanent **furrows** across his forehead, giving him an appearance of perpetual concern.
   多年的焦虑在他的额头上刻下了永久的皱纹，使他看起来总是忧心忡忡。

3. The professor **furrowed** her brow as she attempted to reconcile the contradictory findings within the research data.
   教授皱起眉头，试图调和研究数据中相互矛盾的发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "furrow" as "fur-row" — imagine rows of fur-like ridges in a plowed field, like wrinkles on a worried face.
把"furrow"想成"fur-row"——想象犁过的田地里一行行像毛皮一样的脊状隆起，就像忧虑面孔上的皱纹。
