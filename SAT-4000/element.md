# 🧱 element

🔊 /ˈelɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A fundamental substance that cannot be broken down into simpler substances by chemical means; a component or essential part of something
**中文：** n. 元素；要素；成分；组成部分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- component（组成部分）
- constituent（成分）
- ingredient（要素）
- factor（因素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **key element / chemical element / essential element / element of surprise / in one's element**

👉 Example: Carbon is a fundamental element found in all known forms of organic life on Earth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The periodic table organizes every known chemical **element** according to its atomic number and recurring chemical properties.
   元素周期表根据原子序数和重复出现的化学性质排列每一种已知的化学元素。

2. Trust is an essential **element** of any successful professional relationship, particularly in collaborative research environments.
   信任是任何成功专业关系的基本要素，尤其是在协作研究环境中。

3. The detective identified a crucial **element** of evidence that ultimately linked the suspect to the scene of the crime.
   侦探发现了一个关键的证据要素，最终将嫌疑人与犯罪现场联系起来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "element" as "elementary" — just as elementary means basic and fundamental, an element is the most basic building block of matter or ideas.
把"element"想成"elementary（基本的）"——正如elementary意味着基本和根本，元素是物质或想法的最基本构件。
