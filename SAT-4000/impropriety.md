# 🚫 impropriety

🔊 /ˌɪmprəˈpraɪəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being improper; an improper act or remark; a breach of accepted standards of behavior or etiquette
**中文：** n. 不适当；不正确；用词错误；不正当行为

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indecency（不体面）
- misconduct（不当行为）
- indiscretion（轻率行为）
- unseemliness（不得体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **financial impropriety / allegations of impropriety / moral impropriety / commit an impropriety**

👉 Example: The senator resigned after allegations of financial impropriety surfaced in the national press.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ethics committee launched a thorough investigation into the alleged **impropriety** involving misuse of institutional research funds.
   伦理委员会对涉嫌不当使用机构研究资金的行为展开了彻底调查。

2. Even the slightest appearance of **impropriety** can permanently damage a public official's reputation and political career.
   即使是最轻微的不当行为迹象也可能永久损害公职人员的声誉和政治生涯。

3. The court dismissed the case after finding no evidence of judicial **impropriety** in the lower tribunal's handling of proceedings.
   法院在未发现下级法庭处理诉讼程序中存在司法不当行为的证据后驳回了此案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impropriety" as "im + propriety" — the opposite of propriety (proper behavior), so it means improper behavior.
把"impropriety"想成"im（否定前缀）+ propriety（得体）"——得体的反面，即不当行为。
