# 🏆 exceptional

🔊 /ɪkˈsɛpʃənl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unusual; not typical; remarkably good or outstanding; forming an exception
**中文：** 异常的；例外的；特殊的；优越的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extraordinary（非凡的）
- remarkable（卓越的）
- anomalous（异常的）
- uncommon（不寻常的）
- outstanding（杰出的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exceptional talent / exceptional circumstances / exceptional ability / exceptional case**

👉 Example: Only under exceptional circumstances will the committee grant an extension for the submission deadline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young violinist demonstrated **exceptional** talent that captivated audiences and earned acclaim from seasoned critics worldwide.
   这位年轻的小提琴家展现了非凡的才华，令观众着迷，并赢得了全球资深评论家的赞誉。

2. Due to **exceptional** weather conditions, the scientific expedition was postponed until conditions became more favorable for research.
   由于异常的天气状况，科学考察被推迟，直到条件变得更有利于研究。

3. Her **exceptional** academic record distinguished her from hundreds of qualified applicants competing for the prestigious fellowship.
   她卓越的学术成绩使她从数百名竞争这项著名奖学金的合格申请者中脱颖而出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exception-al" — something so special it is the exception to the ordinary, standing apart from the rest.
把"exceptional"想成"exception-al"——如此特别以至于成为普通事物中的例外，与众不同。
