# 👶 pediatrics

🔊 /ˌpiːdiˈætrɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The branch of medicine dealing with children and their diseases
**中文：** n. 小儿科；儿科学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- child medicine（儿童医学）
- child healthcare（儿童保健）
- neonatology（新生儿学）
- pediatric medicine（儿科医学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **department of pediatrics / pediatrics residency / pediatrics specialist / practice pediatrics**

👉 Example: She decided to specialize in pediatrics because of her passion for improving children's health outcomes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital recently expanded its **pediatrics** department to accommodate the growing demand for specialized children's healthcare services.
   这家医院最近扩建了儿科部门，以满足对专业儿童医疗服务日益增长的需求。

2. Advances in **pediatrics** over the past century have dramatically reduced infant mortality rates across developed and developing nations.
   过去一个世纪儿科学的进步大幅降低了发达国家和发展中国家的婴儿死亡率。

3. Medical students pursuing **pediatrics** must develop exceptional communication skills to interact effectively with young patients and their families.
   攻读儿科的医学生必须培养出色的沟通技巧，以便与年幼的患者及其家属进行有效交流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ped-iatrics" as "ped (child) + iatrics (healing)" — the medical field dedicated to healing children.
把"pediatrics"想成"ped（儿童）+iatrics（治疗）"——专门治疗儿童的医学领域。
