# 🧠 conscious

🔊 /ˈkɒnʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Aware of and responding to one's surroundings; having knowledge of something; done deliberately and intentionally.
**中文：** adj. 意识到的；故意的；神志清醒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aware（意识到的）
- deliberate（故意的）
- mindful（留心的）
- intentional（有意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conscious effort / conscious decision / conscious of / self-conscious / environmentally conscious**

👉 Example: She made a conscious decision to pursue higher education despite the financial challenges she faced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee made a **conscious** effort to include diverse perspectives when drafting the new institutional policy document.
   委员会在起草新的机构政策文件时，有意识地努力纳入多元化的观点。

2. After the accident, the patient remained **conscious** and was able to describe the sequence of events to the medical team.
   事故发生后，患者保持清醒，能够向医疗团队描述事件的经过。

3. Many consumers are becoming increasingly **conscious** of the environmental impact their purchasing decisions have on the planet.
   许多消费者越来越意识到他们的购买决定对地球环境产生的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-sci-ous" as "with (con) science (sci)" — being conscious means having scientific awareness of what is happening around you.
把"conscious"想成"带有(con)科学认知(sci)"——有意识就是对周围发生的事情具有清醒的认知。
