# 📦 unused

🔊 /ʌnˈjuːzd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not being used or having never been used; not put to any purpose; not accustomed to something
**中文：** 不用的；从未用过的；不习惯的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- idle（闲置的）
- untouched（未动过的）
- dormant（休眠的）
- vacant（空闲的）
- surplus（多余的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unused resources / unused potential / unused land / unused to something**

👉 Example: The audit revealed millions of dollars in unused funds that could be redirected to essential public services.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city council proposed converting the **unused** industrial site into a public park with recreational facilities for residents.
   市议会提议将这块闲置的工业用地改建为供居民使用的带有娱乐设施的公园。

2. Researchers discovered that a significant percentage of the brain's **unused** capacity could be activated through targeted cognitive exercises.
   研究人员发现，通过有针对性的认知训练，大脑中很大比例的未开发能力可以被激活。

3. The newly appointed director was **unused** to the intense media scrutiny that accompanied every decision made by the institution.
   新任命的主任不习惯该机构每项决定所带来的激烈的媒体审视。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-used" as simply "not used" — something that sits idle without having been put to any purpose, still fresh and untouched.
把"unused"想成"un（没有）+ used（使用过的）"——闲置未被使用的东西，依然崭新且未被触碰。
