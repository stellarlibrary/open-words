# ✨ spontaneous

🔊 /spɒnˈteɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring without external cause; arising from natural impulse; done or produced freely and naturally without being forced or planned
**中文：** adj. 自发的，自然产生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impulsive（冲动的）
- instinctive（本能的）
- voluntary（自愿的）
- unplanned（未经计划的）
- natural（自然的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spontaneous combustion / spontaneous reaction / spontaneous generation / spontaneous decision**

👉 Example: The spontaneous applause from the audience demonstrated the profound impact of the speaker's words.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **spontaneous** combustion of the chemical compound prompted the laboratory to revise its safety protocols immediately.
   该化学化合物的自燃促使实验室立即修订了其安全规程。

2. Her **spontaneous** decision to volunteer abroad led to a transformative experience that shaped her entire career.
   她自发决定去海外做志愿者，这段经历改变了她的整个职业生涯。

3. The biologist observed a **spontaneous** mutation in the organism that conferred unexpected resistance to the antibiotic.
   生物学家观察到该生物体中发生了一种自发突变，赋予了它意想不到的抗生素耐药性。

---

Think of "spontaneous" as "spon-tan-eous" — like a sponge that naturally absorbs water on its own without anyone squeezing it.
把"spontaneous"想成"spon-tan-eous"——就像海绵自然而然地吸水，不需要任何人去挤压。
