# 👽 alien

🔊 /ˈeɪ.li.ən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Belonging to a foreign country or nation; unfamiliar or strange; a foreigner or extraterrestrial being.
**中文：** 外国的；相异的，陌生的；外国人；外星人。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- foreign（外国的）
- exotic（异国的）
- stranger（陌生人）
- outsider（局外人）
- extraterrestrial（外星的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alien to, alien culture, alien species, illegal alien, alien concept**
👉 Example: The concept of dishonesty was entirely alien to her upbringing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The customs and traditions of the remote tribe were completely **alien** to the visiting researchers.
   那个偏远部落的风俗和传统对来访的研究人员来说完全是陌生的。

2. As an **alien** residing in the country, she was required to register with the immigration authorities annually.
   作为居住在该国的外国人，她被要求每年向移民当局登记。

3. The biologist hypothesized that **alien** life forms might exist in the subsurface oceans of distant moons.
   这位生物学家假设外星生命体可能存在于遥远卫星的地下海洋中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alien" shares its root with "alienate" — both come from Latin "alienus" meaning "belonging to another."
"alien"与"alienate"同根，都来自拉丁语"alienus"，意为"属于他人的"。
