# ✈️ plane

🔊 /pleɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An aircraft; a flat surface; a level of existence or thought. (v.) To smooth with a tool; to glide or soar. (adj.) Flat; level
**中文：** n. 飞机；平面；程度，水平 v. 刨；乘飞机旅行；翱翔 adj. 平的；平面的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aircraft（飞机）
- surface（表面）
- level（水平）
- glide（滑翔）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plane of existence / plane geometry / on a higher plane / plane surface**

👉 Example: The discussion elevated the debate to an entirely different intellectual plane.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician demonstrated that two parallel lines on the same **plane** will never intersect regardless of how far they extend.
   数学家证明了同一平面上的两条平行线无论延伸多远都不会相交。

2. The philosopher argued that moral reasoning operates on a fundamentally different **plane** from purely empirical scientific inquiry.
   这位哲学家认为，道德推理在一个与纯粹经验科学探究根本不同的层面上运作。

3. The carpenter used a hand **plane** to smooth the rough surface of the oak board before applying the final coat of varnish.
   木匠用手刨将橡木板粗糙的表面刨平，然后涂上最后一层清漆。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plane" as something perfectly flat — whether it is a geometric plane, or an airplane gliding flat across the sky.
把"plane"想成完全平坦的东西——无论是几何平面，还是在天空中平稳滑行的飞机。
