# 📦 sufficiency

🔊 /səˈfɪʃənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An adequate amount or quantity; the state of being enough; self-satisfaction
**中文：** n. 充足，足量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adequacy（充足）
- abundance（丰富）
- plenty（充裕）
- competence（足够）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sufficiency of resources / sufficiency of evidence / self-sufficiency / a sufficiency of funds**

👉 Example: The committee questioned whether there was a sufficiency of evidence to proceed with the investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team confirmed the **sufficiency** of their data before submitting the final report to the academic review board.
   研究团队在向学术评审委员会提交最终报告之前确认了数据的充足性。

2. National leaders debated the **sufficiency** of current defense budgets to address emerging threats in the geopolitical landscape.
   国家领导人就当前国防预算是否足以应对地缘政治格局中的新兴威胁展开了辩论。

3. The agricultural minister assured citizens of the **sufficiency** of grain reserves to sustain the population through the winter months.
   农业部长向公民保证粮食储备充足，足以维持整个冬季的人口需求。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sufficiency" as "sufficient + cy" — it is the noun form of "sufficient," meaning the state of having enough.
把"sufficiency"想成"sufficient + cy"——它是"sufficient"的名词形式，表示拥有足够的状态。
