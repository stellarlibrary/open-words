# 📜 aforesaid

🔊 /əˈfɔːrsɛd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Previously mentioned or stated earlier in a document, speech, or discussion.
**中文：** 前述的，上述的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aforementioned（前面提到的）
- foregoing（前述的）
- preceding（先前的）
- above-mentioned（上述的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the aforesaid terms, the aforesaid parties, the aforesaid conditions, the aforesaid agreement**
👉 Example: The aforesaid regulations shall take effect at the beginning of the fiscal year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **aforesaid** conditions must be fully satisfied before the contract becomes legally binding and enforceable.
   上述条件必须完全满足后，合同才具有法律约束力和可执行性。

2. All **aforesaid** parties shall bear equal responsibility for any damages arising from the breach of agreement.
   所有前述各方应对因违反协议而产生的任何损害承担同等责任。

3. The judge referenced the **aforesaid** testimony as critical evidence in reaching the final verdict of the case.
   法官引用了前述证词作为得出案件最终裁决的关键证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aforesaid" breaks into "afore" (before) + "said" — it literally means "said before," referring to something already mentioned.
"Aforesaid"拆分为"afore"（之前）+"said"（说过的）——字面意思就是"之前说过的"，指已经提到的内容。
