# 🗣️ prate

🔊 /preɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To talk at length in a foolish or inconsequential way; to chatter idly. (n.) Idle or meaningless talk
**中文：** v. 唠叨；闲聊；瞎扯 n. 空谈；唠叨

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- babble（喋喋不休）
- chatter（闲聊）
- prattle（唠叨）
- blather（胡说）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prate about / prate on / endless prate / idle prate**

👉 Example: The critics dismissed his lengthy speech as nothing more than meaningless prate devoid of substance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator continued to **prate** about minor procedural issues while the committee awaited discussion of the urgent policy reform.
   这位参议员继续就次要的程序问题喋喋不休，而委员会正等待讨论紧迫的政策改革。

2. Scholars have little patience for those who **prate** endlessly without contributing any substantive insights to the academic discourse.
   学者们对那些喋喋不休却没有为学术讨论贡献任何实质性见解的人缺乏耐心。

3. The editorial accused the administration of choosing to **prate** about achievements rather than addressing the underlying systemic failures.
   社论指责政府选择空谈成就，而不是解决潜在的系统性问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prate" sounding like "pirate" — imagine a pirate who never stops talking about his old adventures, boring everyone on the ship.
把"prate"想成听起来像"pirate（海盗）"——想象一个海盗不停地唠叨他过去的冒险经历，让船上的每个人都无聊透顶。
