# 🚶 proceed

🔊 /prəˈsiːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To begin or continue a course of action; to move forward; to originate from
**中文：** v. 开始；继续进行；发生；行进；出发；出自

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- continue（继续）
- advance（前进）
- commence（开始）
- progress（进展）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proceed with / proceed to / proceed against / proceed from**

👉 Example: The committee voted to proceed with the new construction plan despite budgetary concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the brief interruption, the professor asked the students to **proceed** with their laboratory experiments as planned.
   短暂中断之后，教授要求学生们按计划继续进行实验。

2. The investigation will **proceed** cautiously to ensure that all evidence is properly documented and preserved.
   调查将谨慎进行，以确保所有证据得到妥善记录和保存。

3. The court granted permission for the prosecution to **proceed** against the defendant on all charges filed.
   法院批准检方就所提出的所有指控对被告提起诉讼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-ceed" as "professionally cede forward" — a professional moves forward and keeps going with purpose.
把"proceed"想成"pro（向前）+ ceed（走）"——向前走，继续前进。
