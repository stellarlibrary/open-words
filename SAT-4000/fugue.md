# 🎵 fugue

🔊 /fjuːɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A musical composition in which a theme is developed by interweaving voices; a state of psychological amnesia during which a person may travel and act purposefully
**中文：** n. 赋格曲；神游症

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- counterpoint（对位法）
- canon（卡农）
- polyphony（复调音乐）
- dissociative state（解离状态）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **play a fugue / compose a fugue / dissociative fugue / in a fugue state**

👉 Example: Bach's fugues remain among the most technically demanding works in the keyboard repertoire.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pianist performed a complex **fugue** by Bach, demonstrating remarkable precision in each interlocking melodic voice.
   钢琴家演奏了巴赫的一首复杂赋格曲，在每个交织的旋律声部中展现了非凡的精准。

2. During the dissociative **fugue**, the patient traveled across three states with no subsequent memory of the journey.
   在神游症发作期间，患者穿越了三个州，事后对这段旅程毫无记忆。

3. The composition professor assigned students to analyze the structural elements of a four-voice **fugue** for their final examination.
   作曲教授要求学生分析一首四声部赋格曲的结构要素，作为期末考试内容。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fugue" as related to "fugitive" — in music, the theme keeps running away and being chased by other voices.
把"fugue"和"fugitive（逃犯）"联系起来——在音乐中，主题不断逃跑，被其他声部追逐。
