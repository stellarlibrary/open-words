# 🎭 whimsical

🔊 /ˈwɪmzɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Playfully odd or fanciful; given to unpredictable changes of mind or behavior
**中文：** adj. 古怪的；异想天开的；反复无常的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- capricious（反复无常的）
- fanciful（幻想的）
- quirky（古怪的）
- eccentric（怪异的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **whimsical idea / whimsical charm / whimsical design / whimsical sense of humor**

👉 Example: The architect's whimsical design featured windows shaped like stars and doors that curved like waves.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's **whimsical** imagination transformed ordinary childhood experiences into fantastical adventures beloved by readers worldwide.
   这位作者异想天开的想象力将普通的童年经历变成了深受全球读者喜爱的奇幻冒险。

2. Critics praised the director's **whimsical** approach to storytelling, which blended surreal imagery with profound emotional depth.
   评论家们称赞导演异想天开的叙事方式，将超现实的意象与深刻的情感融为一体。

3. Her **whimsical** personality made it difficult for colleagues to predict whether she would support or oppose the proposal.
   她反复无常的性格让同事们难以预测她会支持还是反对这项提议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "whim" + "ical" — someone full of whims is whimsical, always changing their mind on a whim.
把"whimsical"想成"whim（突发奇想）+ ical"——一个充满奇思妙想的人就是whimsical，总是心血来潮地改变主意。
