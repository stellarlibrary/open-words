# 🧠 philosophy

🔊 /fɪˈlɒsəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The study of fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, values, reason, and reality; a particular system of thought or set of beliefs guiding one's life
**中文：** n. 哲学；哲理；人生观

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ideology（意识形态）
- doctrine（学说）
- worldview（世界观）
- metaphysics（形而上学）
- reasoning（推理）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **philosophy of life / political philosophy / moral philosophy / philosophy behind (something)**

👉 Example: His personal philosophy emphasized the importance of integrity and continuous self-improvement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's lectures on ancient Greek **philosophy** challenged students to examine their most fundamental assumptions about morality and truth.
   教授关于古希腊哲学的讲座促使学生审视他们对道德和真理最基本的假设。

2. The company's management **philosophy** prioritizes employee well-being and collaborative decision-making over rigid hierarchical control.
   该公司的管理哲学将员工福祉和协作决策置于僵化的等级控制之上。

3. Eastern **philosophy** has profoundly influenced Western approaches to meditation, mindfulness, and holistic health practices in recent decades.
   近几十年来，东方哲学深刻影响了西方在冥想、正念和整体健康实践方面的方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "philo-sophy" as "philo (love) + sophia (wisdom)" — philosophy is the love of wisdom, the pursuit of deep understanding.
把"philosophy"想成"philo（爱）+ sophia（智慧）"——哲学就是对智慧的热爱，是对深刻理解的追求。
