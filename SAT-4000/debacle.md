# 💥 debacle

🔊 /dɪˈbɑːkəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A sudden and complete disaster or fiasco; a great defeat or humiliating failure
**中文：** n. 崩溃；灾害；大败；溃败

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- catastrophe（灾难）
- fiasco（惨败）
- disaster（灾祸）
- calamity（大祸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **financial debacle / political debacle / complete debacle / debacle of the campaign**

👉 Example: The product launch turned into a debacle when critical software failures were discovered on release day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military campaign ended in a **debacle**, with thousands of troops retreating under chaotic and disorganized conditions.
   这场军事行动以惨败告终，数千名士兵在混乱无序的情况下撤退。

2. Investors lost millions in the financial **debacle** that resulted from years of unchecked corporate fraud and mismanagement.
   投资者在多年不受监管的企业欺诈和管理不善导致的金融崩溃中损失了数百万。

3. The diplomatic **debacle** severely damaged relations between the two nations and undermined years of careful negotiation.
   这场外交灾难严重损害了两国关系，破坏了多年来谨慎的谈判成果。

---

把"debacle"想成"扣环断裂"——想象桥的扣环断裂，导致完全崩溃和灾难。
