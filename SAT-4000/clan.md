# 🏰 clan

🔊 /klæn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A close-knit group of interrelated families; a large family group united by a common ancestor; an exclusive group of people with shared interests
**中文：** n. 宗族；部落；氏族；帮派；集团

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tribe（部落）
- faction（派系）
- kinship（亲属关系）
- lineage（宗族）
- sect（派别）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clan leader / clan rivalry / clan members / belong to a clan**

👉 Example: The ancient clan maintained strict traditions that governed every aspect of communal life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The powerful **clan** controlled vast territories and wielded significant political influence throughout the medieval period.
   这个强大的宗族控制着广阔的领地，并在整个中世纪时期拥有重要的政治影响力。

2. Anthropologists studied how the **clan** structure shaped social hierarchies and resource distribution among indigenous communities.
   人类学家研究了宗族结构如何影响原住民社区的社会等级和资源分配。

3. Bitter disputes between rival **clans** threatened to destabilize the fragile peace agreement negotiated by regional mediators.
   敌对宗族之间的激烈争端威胁到了地区调解人谈判达成的脆弱和平协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clan" as a "circle of linked ancestors and names" — a tight family group bound together by blood.
把"clan"想成"一圈相连的祖先和姓氏"——一个由血缘纽带紧密相连的家族群体。
