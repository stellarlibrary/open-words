# 😰 fainthearted

🔊 /ˌfeɪntˈhɑːrtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lacking courage or resolution; timid and lacking in spirit or determination
**中文：** adj. 懦弱的，胆小的；无精神的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- timid（胆小的）
- cowardly（懦弱的）
- spineless（没骨气的）
- pusillanimous（胆怯的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fainthearted attempt / not for the fainthearted / fainthearted response**

👉 Example: This challenging expedition is certainly not for the fainthearted.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fainthearted** soldiers retreated at the first sign of enemy fire, leaving their position completely undefended.
   懦弱的士兵在敌人开火的第一时间就撤退了，使他们的阵地完全无人防守。

2. Reform of the judicial system is not a task for the **fainthearted**, as it demands relentless perseverance.
   司法制度的改革不是胆小者能完成的任务，因为它需要不懈的毅力。

3. Her **fainthearted** approach to negotiation resulted in significant concessions that weakened the organization's standing.
   她在谈判中懦弱的态度导致了重大让步，削弱了该组织的地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "faint + hearted" — someone whose heart goes faint (weak) when facing danger, so they lack courage.
把"fainthearted"想成"faint（虚弱的）+ hearted（心）"——面对危险时心都发虚的人，自然就是胆小懦弱的。
