# 🔵 round

🔊 /raʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, adverb, verb, preposition | 名词, 形容词, 副词, 动词, 介词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A circle; a cycle; one bout or stage; a circular object. (adj.) Circular; complete; approximate; plump. (adv.) In a surrounding area; by a circuitous route; in the opposite direction. (v.) To complete; to gather; to go around; to make circular. (prep.) Near; past; approximately; surrounding
**中文：** v. 四舍五入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- circular（圆形的）
- cycle（循环）
- approximate（大约的）
- spherical（球形的）
- rotund（圆胖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **round number / round of applause / round up / round off / all year round**

👉 Example: The accountant decided to round the figures to the nearest hundred for the summary report.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The negotiators completed another **round** of talks without reaching a definitive agreement on the disputed territorial boundaries.
   谈判人员完成了又一轮会谈，但未能就有争议的领土边界达成明确协议。

2. The teacher asked students to **round** their final calculations to two decimal places for consistency in the report.
   老师要求学生将最终计算结果四舍五入到小数点后两位，以保持报告的一致性。

3. A **round** of enthusiastic applause erupted from the audience when the distinguished scholar concluded her keynote address.
   当那位杰出学者结束主旨演讲时，观众席上爆发出一阵热烈的掌声。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "round" as the shape of a clock face — it goes around in circles, has rounds of hours, and you round numbers up or down.
把"round"想成钟面的形状——它是圆的，有一圈圈的小时数，而你也可以对数字进行四舍五入。
