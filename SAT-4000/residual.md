# 🧪 residual

🔊 /rɪˈzɪdʒuəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A remainder or quantity left over after a process. (adj.) Remaining after the greater part has gone or been taken
**中文：** n. 剩余；残渣 adj. 剩余的；残留的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- remaining（剩余的）
- leftover（残余的）
- lingering（持续的）
- remnant（残留物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **residual effects / residual income / residual risk / residual traces**

👉 Example: The medication may produce residual drowsiness that persists for several hours after initial administration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental scientists detected **residual** traces of industrial pollutants in the river despite extensive remediation efforts over several years.
   尽管进行了数年的大规模治理工作，环境科学家仍在河流中检测到了工业污染物的残留痕迹。

2. The **residual** effects of the economic recession continued to influence employment rates well into the following decade.
   经济衰退的残余影响在随后的十年中继续影响着就业率。

3. After subtracting all known variables, researchers analyzed the **residual** data to identify previously unrecognized patterns in the results.
   在减去所有已知变量后，研究人员分析了残差数据，以识别结果中之前未被发现的模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "residual" as "reside-ual" — what still resides or stays behind after everything else has been removed.
把"residual"想成"reside-ual"——在其他一切被移走之后仍然留驻或残留下来的东西。
