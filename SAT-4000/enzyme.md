# 🧬 enzyme

🔊 /ˈenzaɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A protein produced by living organisms that acts as a biological catalyst to speed up chemical reactions
**中文：** n. 酶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- catalyst（催化剂）
- protein catalyst（蛋白质催化剂）
- biocatalyst（生物催化剂）
- ferment（酵素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **digestive enzyme / enzyme activity / enzyme inhibitor / enzyme reaction**

👉 Example: Digestive enzymes in the stomach break down complex food molecules into simpler nutrients.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **enzyme** amylase begins the process of starch digestion in the mouth before food reaches the stomach.
   淀粉酶这种**酶**在食物到达胃之前就在口腔中开始了淀粉的消化过程。

2. Scientists discovered a novel **enzyme** capable of breaking down plastic polymers into harmless organic compounds within hours.
   科学家们发现了一种新型**酶**，能够在数小时内将塑料聚合物分解为无害的有机化合物。

3. Without the proper **enzyme** to catalyze the reaction, the metabolic pathway would proceed far too slowly to sustain life.
   如果没有适当的**酶**来催化反应，代谢途径的进行将过于缓慢，无法维持生命。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-ZYME" as "energize me" — enzymes energize chemical reactions by making them happen faster.
把"enzyme"想成"energize me（给我能量）"——酶通过加速化学反应来为其提供动力。
