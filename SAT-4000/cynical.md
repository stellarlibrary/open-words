# 😏 cynical

🔊 /ˈsɪnɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Believing that people are motivated purely by self-interest; distrustful of human sincerity; scornfully mocking
**中文：** adj. 愤世嫉俗的；冷嘲热讽的；怀疑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- skeptical（怀疑的）
- distrustful（不信任的）
- sardonic（讥讽的）
- pessimistic（悲观的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cynical about / cynical view / cynical attitude / cynical remark**

👉 Example: His cynical attitude toward politics made him reluctant to participate in any elections.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist adopted a **cynical** perspective on corporate philanthropy, questioning whether donations served genuine altruism.
   这位记者对企业慈善持愤世嫉俗的观点，质疑捐赠是否出于真正的利他主义。

2. Voters grew increasingly **cynical** about campaign promises after years of witnessing unfulfilled pledges by elected officials.
   在多年目睹民选官员未兑现承诺后，选民对竞选承诺越来越持怀疑态度。

3. Her **cynical** remark about the charity gala offended several attendees who had donated generously to the cause.
   她对慈善晚会的冷嘲热讽冒犯了几位慷慨捐赠的与会者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cynical" as related to the ancient Greek "Cynics" — philosophers who distrusted societal norms and mocked conventional values.
把"cynical"联想到古希腊的"犬儒学派"——这些哲学家不信任社会规范，嘲讽传统价值观。
