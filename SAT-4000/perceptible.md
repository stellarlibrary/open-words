# 🔍 perceptible

🔊 /pəˈseptɪbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of being noticed or detected by the senses or the mind; noticeable
**中文：** adj. 可察觉的；可感知的；看得见的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- noticeable（显而易见的）
- detectable（可探测的）
- discernible（可辨别的）
- observable（可观察的）
- appreciable（可感知的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **barely perceptible / hardly perceptible / perceptible change / perceptible difference / perceptible to**

👉 Example: There was a barely perceptible tremor in her voice that revealed her underlying anxiety about the verdict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist noted a **perceptible** increase in temperature readings over the course of the three-year longitudinal study.
   科学家注意到在三年纵向研究过程中温度读数有了可察觉的上升。

2. A barely **perceptible** shift in public opinion began to emerge after the candidate delivered her compelling keynote address.
   在候选人发表了令人信服的主旨演讲后，公众舆论开始出现一种几乎难以察觉的转变。

3. The restoration experts ensured that their repairs were not **perceptible** to visitors examining the centuries-old masterpiece.
   修复专家确保他们的修补不会被参观这幅数百年杰作的游客察觉到。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perceptible" as "percept-ible (able)" — something that is able to be perceived or noticed by your senses.
把"perceptible"想成"percept-ible (able)"——能够被你的感官感知或注意到的东西。
