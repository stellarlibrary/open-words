# 😏 wry

🔊 /raɪ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Using or expressing dry, mocking humor; twisted or bent to one side; showing displeasure or distaste in a grimly humorous way
**中文：** adj. 歪斜的，扭歪的；用反语表达幽默的；揶揄的；挖苦的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sardonic（嘲讽的）
- ironic（讽刺的）
- dry（冷幽默的）
- twisted（扭曲的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wry smile / wry humor / wry observation / wry comment / wry wit**

👉 Example: She offered a wry smile when asked whether she enjoyed working overtime every weekend.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The columnist was renowned for her **wry** observations about the absurdities of modern political campaigns and rhetoric.
   这位专栏作家以其对现代政治竞选和言论荒谬之处的揶揄评论而闻名。

2. With a **wry** grin, the professor noted that the students who complained most about homework scored highest on exams.
   教授带着一丝揶揄的笑容指出，抱怨作业最多的学生考试成绩反而最高。

3. His **wry** memoir recounted decades of bureaucratic misadventures with a tone that was both bitter and deeply amusing.
   他那充满揶揄的回忆录以既苦涩又极为有趣的笔调讲述了数十年的官僚主义遭遇。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wry" as "awry" without the "a" — something awry is twisted, and a wry smile is a twisted, ironic grin.
把"wry"想成"awry（歪斜的）"去掉"a"——awry是歪斜的，而wry smile就是扭曲的、带有讽刺意味的微笑。
