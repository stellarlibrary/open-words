# 😋 relish

🔊 /ˈrelɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Great enjoyment or delight; a pleasing flavor or zest. (v.) To enjoy greatly; to look forward to with pleasure
**中文：** n. 享受，乐趣；滋味，风味 v. 盼望；享受；品味

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- savor（品味）
- delight（愉悦）
- enjoy（享受）
- zest（热情）
- appreciation（欣赏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **relish the opportunity / relish the challenge / with great relish / relish the thought of**

👉 Example: She did not relish the thought of delivering the keynote speech before such a large audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seasoned debater seemed to **relish** every opportunity to challenge her opponents with sharp, well-reasoned arguments.
   这位经验丰富的辩手似乎享受每一个用犀利、有理有据的论点挑战对手的机会。

2. He described his travels through Southeast Asia with such **relish** that everyone in the audience felt compelled to visit.
   他如此津津有味地描述了他在东南亚的旅行，以至于听众中的每个人都有了前往的冲动。

3. Few scholars **relish** the arduous process of peer review, yet it remains essential to maintaining academic integrity.
   很少有学者享受同行评审这一艰巨过程，但它对维护学术诚信仍然至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "relish" as something you put on a hot dog — it adds flavor and zest, just as relishing something adds enjoyment to life.
把"relish"想成你放在热狗上的调味酱——它增添了风味和滋味，就像品味某事为生活增添了乐趣。
