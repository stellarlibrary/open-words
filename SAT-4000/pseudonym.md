# 🎭 pseudonym

🔊 /ˈsuːdənɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A fictitious name, especially one used by an author or artist to conceal their true identity; a pen name
**中文：** n. 笔名；假名

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pen name（笔名）
- alias（化名）
- nom de plume（笔名）
- assumed name（假名）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **write under a pseudonym / publish under a pseudonym / adopt a pseudonym / use a pseudonym**

👉 Example: The celebrated author chose to publish her controversial essays under a pseudonym to protect her privacy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many nineteenth-century female writers adopted male **pseudonyms** to ensure their work would be taken seriously by publishers and critics.
   许多十九世纪女性作家采用男性笔名，以确保她们的作品被出版商和评论家认真对待。

2. The intelligence operative maintained several **pseudonyms** across different countries to avoid detection by foreign security agencies.
   该情报人员在不同国家使用多个假名，以避免被外国安全机构发现。

3. Scholars have long debated whether the **pseudonym** conceals a single author or a collaborative group working under one identity.
   学者们长期以来一直在争论这个笔名是隐藏了一位作者还是一个以同一身份工作的合作团队。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pseudo-nym" as "pseudo name" — "pseudo" means false and "nym" means name, so a pseudonym is literally a false name.
把"pseudonym"想成"pseudo name"——"pseudo"意为假的，"nym"意为名字，所以pseudonym字面意思就是假名。
