# 📕 bibliophile

🔊 /ˈbɪbliəˌfaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who loves books and reading; a collector of books, especially rare or fine editions
**中文：** 藏书家；爱书的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- book lover（爱书者）
- bookworm（书虫）
- book collector（藏书家）
- avid reader（热心读者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **devoted bibliophile / a true bibliophile / self-proclaimed bibliophile / bibliophile's collection**

👉 Example: As a devoted bibliophile, she could not pass a secondhand bookshop without stepping inside.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **bibliophile** donated his personal collection of over ten thousand volumes to the university's special archives.
   这位著名的藏书家将他个人收藏的一万多册藏书捐赠给了大学的特别档案馆。

2. Any serious **bibliophile** understands the importance of proper storage conditions to preserve the integrity of rare manuscripts.
   任何认真的爱书人都懂得适当保存条件对维护珍稀手稿完整性的重要性。

3. The annual antiquarian book fair attracted **bibliophiles** from around the world eager to acquire first-edition literary works.
   一年一度的古籍书展吸引了来自世界各地渴望获得初版文学作品的藏书爱好者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "biblio" (book) + "phile" (lover) — a bibliophile is literally a "book lover," someone who adores books.
把"bibliophile"拆分为"biblio"（书）+"phile"（爱好者）——bibliophile字面意思就是"爱书的人"。
