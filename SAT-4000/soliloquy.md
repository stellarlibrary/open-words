# 🎭 soliloquy

🔊 /səˈlɪləkwi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An act of speaking one's thoughts aloud when alone or regardless of hearers; a dramatic monologue
**中文：** n. 自言自语；独白

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- monologue（独白）
- aside（旁白）
- speech（演说）
- address（讲话）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliver a soliloquy / famous soliloquy / dramatic soliloquy / Hamlet's soliloquy**

👉 Example: The actor delivered a powerful soliloquy that captivated the entire audience in the theater.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Shakespeare's most celebrated **soliloquy** begins with the philosophical question "To be or not to be" in the third act.
   莎士比亚最著名的独白以第三幕中"生存还是毁灭"这一哲学问题开始。

2. The playwright crafted an introspective **soliloquy** that revealed the protagonist's deepest moral conflicts and hidden motivations.
   剧作家精心创作了一段内省的独白，揭示了主人公最深层的道德冲突和隐藏的动机。

3. During the conference, the keynote speaker's remarks resembled a **soliloquy** as she reflected aloud on decades of research experience.
   在会议期间，主旨演讲者的发言像是一段独白，她大声回顾了数十年的研究经历。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "soliloquy" as "solo + loquy (speak)" — speaking solo, talking to yourself alone on stage.
把"soliloquy"想成"solo + loquy（说话）"——独自说话，在舞台上自言自语。
