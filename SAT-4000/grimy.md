# 🏭 grimy

🔊 /ˈɡraɪmi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Covered with or characterized by grime; extremely dirty or filthy
**中文：** adj. 肮脏的；污秽的；满是污垢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- filthy（肮脏的）
- dirty（脏的）
- soiled（弄脏的）
- grubby（邋遢的）
- squalid（污秽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grimy hands / grimy windows / grimy streets / grimy walls / grimy surface**

👉 Example: The mechanic wiped his grimy hands on a cloth after spending hours repairing the engine.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists carefully brushed away layers of dust to reveal the **grimy** surface of the ancient artifact beneath.
   考古学家们小心地刷去层层灰尘，露出下面古代文物污秽的表面。

2. Years of industrial pollution had left the once-beautiful cathedral facade looking **grimy** and severely discolored.
   多年的工业污染使这座曾经美丽的大教堂外墙变得肮脏不堪，严重褪色。

3. The investigator peered through the **grimy** window of the abandoned warehouse, searching for evidence of illegal activity.
   调查员透过废弃仓库肮脏的窗户向里窥视，寻找违法活动的证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grimy" as "grime + y" — covered in grime, like the dark soot that collects on surfaces in old factories.
把"grimy"想成"grime + y"——沾满污垢的，就像旧工厂表面积聚的黑色煤烟。
