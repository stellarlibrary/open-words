# 📚 diligent

🔊 /ˈdɪlɪdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing care and conscientiousness in one's work or duties; hardworking and persistent in effort
**中文：** adj. 勤勉的；用功的；刻苦的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- industrious（勤劳的）
- assiduous（刻苦的）
- conscientious（尽责的）
- persevering（坚持不懈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diligent student / diligent worker / diligent effort / diligent research / diligent in one's duties**

👉 Example: Her diligent study habits enabled her to achieve the highest marks in every examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **diligent** researcher spent countless hours in the laboratory verifying each result before submitting her findings for peer review.
   这位勤勉的研究人员在实验室花费了无数小时核实每一个结果，然后才提交她的研究成果进行同行评审。

2. Through **diligent** practice and unwavering dedication, the young pianist transformed from a novice into an internationally acclaimed concert performer.
   通过刻苦练习和坚定不移的奉献精神，这位年轻钢琴家从一个新手成长为国际知名的音乐会演奏家。

3. The auditing team conducted a **diligent** examination of the company's financial records to identify any discrepancies or irregularities.
   审计团队对公司的财务记录进行了细致的审查，以发现任何差异或违规行为。
