# 🏔️ ravine

🔊 /rəˈviːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A deep, narrow gorge with steep sides, typically formed by the erosion of running water
**中文：** n. 沟壑，山涧；峡谷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gorge（峡谷）
- canyon（峡谷）
- chasm（深渊）
- gully（冲沟）
- gulch（峡谷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deep ravine / steep ravine / cross a ravine / at the bottom of a ravine / narrow ravine**

👉 Example: The hikers carefully navigated a narrow path along the edge of the steep ravine.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologists studied the deep **ravine** to understand how centuries of water erosion had carved through the limestone bedrock.
   地质学家研究了这条深邃的沟壑，以了解数百年的水蚀如何切穿了石灰岩基岩。

2. A wooden footbridge spanning the **ravine** provided the only safe passage between the two isolated mountain villages.
   一座横跨峡谷的木制人行桥是两个偏远山村之间唯一安全的通道。

3. Heavy rainfall transformed the shallow creek into a torrent that rapidly deepened the existing **ravine** along the hillside.
   暴雨将浅浅的溪流变成急流，迅速加深了山坡上现有的沟壑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ra-vine" — imagine vines growing down into a deep crack in the earth, hanging into the ravine below.
把"ravine"想成"ra + vine（藤蔓）"——想象藤蔓垂入大地深处的裂缝中，悬挂在下方的峡谷里。
