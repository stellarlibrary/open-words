# 😋 yummy

🔊 /ˈjʌmi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Highly attractive, pleasing, or delicious, especially in taste. (n.) Something delicious or delightful
**中文：** adj. 好吃的；美味的；愉快的 n. 美味的东西；令人喜爱的东西

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- delicious（美味的）
- scrumptious（极美味的）
- delectable（令人愉快的）
- appetizing（开胃的）
- luscious（甘美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **yummy food / yummy treats / looks yummy / something yummy**

👉 Example: The bakery displayed a row of yummy pastries that tempted every passerby on the busy street.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chef prepared an exceptionally **yummy** three-course meal that left every guest at the banquet thoroughly impressed.
   厨师准备了一顿极其美味的三道菜大餐，让宴会上的每位宾客都赞不绝口。

2. The food critic described the handcrafted chocolate truffles as absolutely **yummy** and worthy of international acclaim.
   美食评论家形容这些手工巧克力松露绝对美味，值得获得国际赞誉。

3. Children eagerly gathered around the table where a spread of **yummy** desserts awaited them after the ceremony.
   孩子们急切地聚集在桌旁，那里摆满了仪式后等待他们的美味甜点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "yummy" as the sound "yum!" you naturally say when tasting something delicious — just add "-my" to make it an adjective.
把"yummy"想成你品尝美食时自然发出的"yum！"——加上"-my"就变成了形容词。
