# 🏠 hut

🔊 /hʌt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small, simple dwelling or shelter, often made of crude or natural materials; a temporary camp structure
**中文：** n. 小屋；棚屋；临时营房

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shack（棚屋）
- cabin（小木屋）
- shanty（简陋小屋）
- shelter（避难所）
- hovel（茅舍）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a wooden hut / a mud hut / a mountain hut / a beach hut**

👉 Example: The explorers built a makeshift hut from branches and leaves to survive the night in the wilderness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team established a small **hut** at the base camp to store their scientific instruments and supplies.
   研究团队在大本营搭建了一间小屋来存放他们的科学仪器和物资。

2. Villagers constructed a communal **hut** from locally sourced timber and thatch to serve as a meeting place.
   村民们用当地采集的木材和茅草建造了一间公共小屋作为集会场所。

3. The anthropologist spent several months living in a traditional **hut** to better understand the indigenous community's daily life.
   这位人类学家在一间传统小屋中生活了数月，以更好地了解原住民社区的日常生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hut" as a short, small word for a short, small house — just three letters for a tiny dwelling.
把"hut"想成一个短小的词代表短小的房子——仅仅三个字母就描述了一间小小的住所。
