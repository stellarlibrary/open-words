# 💧 desiccant

🔊 /ˈdɛsɪkənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the property of drying; capable of absorbing moisture. (n.) A hygroscopic substance used as a drying agent
**中文：** adj. 去湿的；干燥的 n. 干燥剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- drying agent（干燥剂）
- dehydrator（脱水剂）
- absorbent（吸附剂）
- dryer（干燥物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **desiccant packet / silica gel desiccant / desiccant material / desiccant properties**

👉 Example: Small desiccant packets are commonly placed inside product packaging to prevent moisture damage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory uses a powerful **desiccant** to maintain extremely low humidity levels required for sensitive chemical storage procedures.
   实验室使用强效干燥剂来维持敏感化学品储存程序所需的极低湿度水平。

2. Pharmaceutical companies rely on **desiccant** materials within packaging to preserve the efficacy and shelf life of moisture-sensitive medications.
   制药公司在包装中使用干燥剂材料来保持对湿度敏感药物的功效和保质期。

3. Researchers developed a novel **desiccant** compound capable of absorbing three times more moisture than conventional silica gel alternatives.
   研究人员开发了一种新型干燥剂化合物，其吸湿能力是传统硅胶替代品的三倍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "desiccant" as "desi-can't" — moisture "can't" survive around a desiccant because it absorbs all the water.
把"desiccant"想成"desi-can't（不能）"——水分在干燥剂周围"不能"存活，因为它吸收了所有的水。
