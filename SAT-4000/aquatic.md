# 🐟 aquatic

🔊 /əˈkwætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (adj.) Relating to, living in, or growing in water. (n.) An aquatic plant or animal; (plural) water sports or exercises performed in or on water.

**中文：** (adj.) 水生的；水栖的；水中进行的。(n.) 水生动植物；水上运动。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- marine（海洋的）
- waterborne（水上的）
- freshwater（淡水的）
- amphibious（水陆两栖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** aquatic life, aquatic plants, aquatic ecosystem, aquatic environment, aquatic species, aquatic sports

**Example:** The pond supports a diverse range of aquatic organisms throughout the year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Conservation efforts have successfully restored the **aquatic** ecosystem in the river after decades of industrial pollution.
   （经过数十年的工业污染后，保护工作已成功恢复了该河流的水生生态系统。）

2. Researchers discovered several previously unknown **aquatic** species living in the deep trenches of the Pacific Ocean.
   （研究人员在太平洋深海沟中发现了几种此前未知的水生物种。）

3. The university's new recreation center features a modern **aquatic** facility designed for competitive swimming events.
   （该大学新建的休闲中心设有一个为竞技游泳赛事设计的现代水上运动设施。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Aquatic" comes from Latin "aqua" (water). Anything "aquatic" relates to water — just like an "aquarium" holds water creatures.

"Aquatic" 源自拉丁语 "aqua"（水）。任何 "aquatic" 的事物都与水有关——就像 "aquarium"（水族馆）养着水中生物。
