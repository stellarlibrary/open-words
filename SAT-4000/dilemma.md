# ⚖️ dilemma

🔊 /dɪˈlɛmə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A situation in which a difficult choice must be made between two or more equally undesirable alternatives; a state of perplexity or predicament
**中文：** n. 困境；进退两难；窘境

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- predicament（困境）
- quandary（窘境）
- plight（困境）
- impasse（僵局）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **face a dilemma / moral dilemma / ethical dilemma / resolve a dilemma / be in a dilemma**

👉 Example: The CEO faced a serious dilemma between maximizing short-term profits and investing in long-term sustainability.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher confronted an ethical **dilemma** when the experimental results could benefit society but required morally questionable testing methods.
   当实验结果可能造福社会但需要道德上有争议的测试方法时，研究人员面临了伦理困境。

2. Many graduates face the **dilemma** of choosing between pursuing advanced education and entering the workforce to gain practical experience.
   许多毕业生面临着在继续深造和进入职场获取实践经验之间进退两难的困境。

3. The government's **dilemma** intensified as economic growth demands increasingly conflicted with environmental protection commitments made at international summits.
   随着经济增长需求与在国际峰会上做出的环境保护承诺日益冲突，政府的困境加剧了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "di-lemma" as "two lemmas" — "di" means two, so a dilemma presents you with two equally difficult choices.
把"dilemma"想成"两个难题"——"di"意为二，所以dilemma就是面对两个同样困难的选择。
