# ⏪ preceding

🔊 /prɪˈsiːdɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Coming before something in time, order, or position; previous.
**中文：** adj. 在前的；先前的；前述的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- previous（先前的）
- foregoing（前述的）
- prior（之前的）
- antecedent（先行的）
- earlier（更早的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the preceding chapter / the preceding year / in the preceding paragraph / preceding events**

👉 Example: As discussed in the preceding section, the results confirm our initial hypothesis about climate patterns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The data collected during the **preceding** decade revealed a consistent trend of declining biodiversity across the region.
   前十年收集的数据揭示了该地区生物多样性持续下降的趋势。

2. The author references arguments made in the **preceding** chapters to build a comprehensive theoretical framework.
   作者引用了前几章中提出的论点，以构建一个全面的理论框架。

3. All **preceding** attempts to negotiate a ceasefire had failed due to deep mistrust between the two factions.
   此前所有停火谈判的尝试都因双方之间的深度不信任而失败。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-ceding" — "ceding" ground before you, like something that steps aside because it came first.
把"preceding"想成"pre（前）+ ceding（让步）"——在你之前让步的东西，因为它先来。
