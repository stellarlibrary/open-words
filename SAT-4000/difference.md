# 🔄 difference

🔊 /ˈdɪfərəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A point or way in which people or things are not alike; a disagreement or dispute; the remainder left after subtraction
**中文：** n. 差异；不同；争执；差额

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distinction（区别）
- disparity（差距）
- discrepancy（差异）
- variance（分歧）
- divergence（偏差）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a difference / tell the difference / difference between / split the difference / difference of opinion**

👉 Example: Understanding the subtle difference between correlation and causation is essential for scientific reasoning.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **difference** between the two candidates' policy platforms became the central issue during the final televised debate.
   两位候选人政策纲领之间的差异成为最后一次电视辩论的核心议题。

2. Small acts of kindness can make a profound **difference** in the lives of those who are struggling with adversity.
   小小的善举可以对那些在逆境中挣扎的人的生活产生深远的影响。

3. The auditor identified a significant **difference** between the reported revenue figures and the actual financial records on file.
   审计员发现报告的收入数字与存档的实际财务记录之间存在重大差异。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "difference" as "differ" + "-ence" — the state of differing, the quality that makes one thing unlike another.
把"difference"想成"differ"（不同）+"-ence"（状态）——不同的状态，使一件事物与另一件事物不同的特质。
