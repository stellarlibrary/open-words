# 🦋 gregarious

🔊 /ɡrɪˈɡɛriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Sociable; fond of the company of others. Also, (of animals) living in flocks or communities
**中文：** adj. 社交的；爱交际的；群居的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sociable（好交际的）
- outgoing（外向的）
- convivial（欢乐的）
- affable（和蔼的）
- extroverted（外向的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gregarious personality / gregarious nature / gregarious species / gregarious by nature**

👉 Example: Her gregarious personality made her the center of attention at every social gathering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **gregarious** student quickly formed friendships with classmates from diverse cultural and academic backgrounds.
   这位善于社交的学生很快与来自不同文化和学术背景的同学建立了友谊。

2. Wolves are **gregarious** animals that rely on complex social hierarchies to maintain order within the pack.
   狼是群居动物，依靠复杂的社会等级制度来维持族群内的秩序。

3. Despite his **gregarious** demeanor at public events, the professor preferred solitary research in his private study.
   尽管这位教授在公共场合表现得十分合群，他更喜欢在私人书房里独自做研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gregarious" as containing "greg" — like a "group" of friends named Greg who always want to hang out together.
把"gregarious"想成包含"greg"——就像一群叫Greg的朋友总是想聚在一起，代表爱社交、群居。
