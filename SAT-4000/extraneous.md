# 🚫 extraneous

🔊 /ɪkˈstreɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Irrelevant or unrelated to the subject being dealt with; of external origin; not belonging to or part of something
**中文：** adj. 外来的；没有关联的；无关的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irrelevant（无关的）
- unrelated（不相关的）
- immaterial（不重要的）
- superfluous（多余的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extraneous information / extraneous details / extraneous factors / extraneous material**

👉 Example: The editor removed all extraneous details that distracted from the main argument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor advised students to eliminate all **extraneous** information from their essays to maintain a clear argumentative focus.
   教授建议学生删除论文中所有无关的信息，以保持清晰的论证重点。

2. Laboratory researchers must control for **extraneous** variables that could potentially compromise the validity of experimental results.
   实验室研究人员必须控制可能影响实验结果有效性的无关变量。

3. The judge instructed the jury to disregard **extraneous** testimony that bore no direct relevance to the case being tried.
   法官指示陪审团忽略与所审案件没有直接关联的无关证词。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extraneous" as "extra + neous" — something extra that does not belong, like a stranger (Latin extraneus) in a group.
把"extraneous"想成"extra + neous"——多余的、不属于的东西，就像一群人中的陌生人（拉丁语extraneus）。
