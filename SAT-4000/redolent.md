# 🌹 redolent

🔊 /ˈredələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a pleasant smell; fragrant; strongly suggestive or reminiscent of something
**中文：** adj. 芬芳的；有…香味的；令人想起…的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fragrant（芬芳的）
- aromatic（芳香的）
- evocative（引起回忆的）
- reminiscent（令人联想的）
- suggestive（暗示的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **redolent of / redolent with / air redolent of spices / prose redolent of nostalgia**

👉 Example: The old library was redolent of aged leather and cedar wood, evoking a sense of timeless scholarship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The garden was **redolent** of jasmine and lavender, creating an atmosphere of serene tranquility for all visitors.
   花园里弥漫着茉莉和薰衣草的芬芳，为所有来访者营造了一种宁静祥和的氛围。

2. Her autobiographical prose is **redolent** of a bygone era, transporting readers to the rural landscapes of her youth.
   她的自传体散文令人想起一个逝去的时代，将读者带回她年轻时的乡村风景中。

3. The marketplace was **redolent** with the rich aroma of freshly ground spices imported from distant tropical regions.
   市场上弥漫着从遥远热带地区进口的新鲜研磨香料的浓郁芳香。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "red-olent" — imagine a "red rose" that is "olent" (fragrant), giving off a beautiful smell that reminds you of something.
把"redolent"想成"red(红色) + olent(气味)"——像红玫瑰散发的香气，芬芳且令人回忆。
