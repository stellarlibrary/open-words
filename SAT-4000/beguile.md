# ✨ beguile

🔊 /bɪˈɡaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To charm, enchant, or deceive by cunning means; to pass time pleasantly
**中文：** v. 欺骗；使着迷；轻松地消磨（时光）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enchant（使着迷）
- deceive（欺骗）
- captivate（迷住）
- charm（吸引）
- entice（诱惑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beguile someone into / beguile the audience / beguile the hours / beguiling smile**

👉 Example: The con artist could beguile even the most cautious investor with his eloquent promises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The speaker's eloquence and wit were sufficient to **beguile** the entire audience throughout the lengthy ceremony.
   演讲者的口才和机智足以让整个观众在漫长的仪式中都听得入迷。

2. Travelers often **beguile** the long hours of a transatlantic flight by reading novels or watching films.
   旅行者常常通过阅读小说或观看电影来消磨跨大西洋航班的漫长时光。

3. The fraudulent advisor **beguiled** inexperienced investors into committing their savings to a fundamentally worthless scheme.
   那位欺诈性的顾问诱骗缺乏经验的投资者将积蓄投入一个根本毫无价值的计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-guile" — full of guile (cunning), so to beguile is to use charm or trickery to deceive or enchant.
把"beguile"想成"be-guile"——充满诡计(guile)，因此beguile就是用魅力或诡计来欺骗或迷住别人。
