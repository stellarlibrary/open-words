# 🗣️ hoarse

🔊 /hɔːrs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a rough, harsh, or raspy quality of voice, typically caused by illness, strain, or overuse
**中文：** adj. 嘶哑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- raspy（沙哑的）
- husky（嘶哑的）
- gravelly（粗哑的）
- croaky（沙哑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hoarse voice / hoarse whisper / hoarse from shouting / go hoarse**

👉 Example: The coach's voice grew hoarse after shouting instructions throughout the entire championship match.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After delivering a three-hour lecture without pause, the professor's voice became noticeably **hoarse** and strained.
   在不间断地进行了三个小时的演讲后，教授的声音变得明显嘶哑而紧绷。

2. The patient spoke in a **hoarse** whisper, struggling to communicate the severity of her symptoms to the physician.
   病人用嘶哑的低语说话，努力向医生传达她症状的严重程度。

3. Protesters chanted slogans until they were **hoarse**, determined to make their grievances heard by government officials.
   抗议者高喊口号直到声音嘶哑，决心让政府官员听到他们的不满。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hoarse" as sounding like "horse" — imagine a horse trying to speak; its voice would be rough and raspy.
把"hoarse"想成听起来像"horse"（马）——想象一匹马试图说话，它的声音一定是粗糙嘶哑的。
