# 🤝 unanimity

🔊 /ˌjuːnəˈnɪmɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Complete agreement among all members of a group; the state of being unanimous
**中文：** n. 全体一致；意见一致；同意

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- consensus（共识）
- agreement（一致）
- accord（协议）
- harmony（和谐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **achieve unanimity / reach unanimity / lack of unanimity / remarkable unanimity**

👉 Example: The jury reached unanimity after twelve hours of careful and exhaustive deliberation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board achieved rare **unanimity** in voting to approve the new environmental sustainability initiative for the corporation.
   董事会在投票批准公司新的环境可持续发展计划时罕见地达成了全体一致。

2. Despite initial disagreements, the delegates eventually reached **unanimity** on the final wording of the international resolution.
   尽管最初存在分歧，代表们最终就国际决议的最终措辞达成了一致意见。

3. The lack of **unanimity** among the panel members revealed deep ideological divisions that would prove difficult to resolve.
   小组成员之间缺乏一致意见，暴露了难以解决的深层意识形态分歧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "unanimity" as "uni (one) + animity (mind)" — everyone sharing one single mind, in complete agreement.
把"unanimity"拆分为"uni（一个）+ animity（心意）"——所有人同一个想法，完全一致。
