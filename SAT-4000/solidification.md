# 🧊 solidification

🔊 /səˌlɪdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of becoming solid; the act of unifying or making firm; concentration or consolidation
**中文：** n. 凝固

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hardening（硬化）
- crystallization（结晶）
- consolidation（巩固）
- coagulation（凝结）
- congealing（凝固）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solidification process / solidification point / solidification of (a substance) / rapid solidification**

👉 Example: The solidification of molten metal requires carefully controlled temperature conditions in the foundry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **solidification** of volcanic lava over centuries produced the distinctive basalt formations visible along the coastline today.
   数百年来火山熔岩的凝固形成了如今海岸线上独特的玄武岩地貌。

2. Researchers observed that the **solidification** of the polymer occurred more rapidly under conditions of increased atmospheric pressure.
   研究人员观察到，在大气压力增大的条件下，聚合物的凝固速度更快。

3. The **solidification** of political alliances during the negotiations ultimately strengthened the coalition's bargaining position considerably.
   谈判期间政治联盟的巩固最终大大增强了联盟的谈判地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solidification" as "solid + ification" — the process (-ification) of becoming solid, like water freezing into ice.
把"solidification"想成"solid + ification"——变成固体的过程（-ification），就像水结成冰一样。
