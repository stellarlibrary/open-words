# 🎭 cant

🔊 /kænt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Hypocritical or sanctimonious talk; the jargon of a particular group; a slanted surface. (v.) To tilt or slant; to speak in jargon.
**中文：** n. 伪善之言；隐语，行话；斜面 v. 使倾斜；讲黑话

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hypocrisy（伪善）
- jargon（行话）
- slant（倾斜）
- pretense（假装）
- argot（黑话）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political cant / religious cant / cant phrases / cant to one side**

👉 Example: The editorial was filled with political cant that offered no genuine solutions to the crisis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's speech was dismissed by critics as mere **cant**, lacking any substantive proposals for reform.
   参议员的演讲被批评家斥为纯粹的伪善之言，缺乏任何实质性的改革提案。

2. The old ship began to **cant** dangerously to starboard as floodwater surged into the lower compartments.
   随着洪水涌入下层舱室，这艘老船开始危险地向右舷倾斜。

3. Outsiders found it difficult to penetrate the specialized **cant** used by traders on the exchange floor.
   外行人发现很难理解交易大厅交易员所使用的专业行话。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "can't be real" — cant is insincere talk that can't be taken at face value, just hollow or hypocritical words.
把"cant"联想为"can't be real（不可能是真的）"——伪善之言不能当真，都是空洞虚伪的话。
