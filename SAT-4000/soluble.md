# 🧪 soluble

🔊 /ˈsɒljʊbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being dissolved in a liquid; capable of being solved or resolved
**中文：** adj. 可溶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dissolvable（可溶解的）
- solvable（可解决的）
- resolvable（可解答的）
- meltable（可熔化的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **soluble in water / water-soluble / soluble fiber / soluble problem**

👉 Example: Most vitamins in the B complex group are soluble in water and are easily absorbed by the body.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist confirmed that the compound was readily **soluble** in ethanol but virtually insoluble in nonpolar organic solvents.
   化学家确认该化合物易溶于乙醇，但几乎不溶于非极性有机溶剂。

2. Researchers discovered that the environmental challenge was indeed **soluble** once sufficient funding and interdisciplinary collaboration were secured.
   研究人员发现，一旦获得充足的资金和跨学科合作，这一环境挑战确实是可以解决的。

3. The pharmacologist determined that the new drug formulation was highly **soluble** and could be administered effectively in liquid form.
   药理学家确定这种新药物配方具有高溶解性，可以有效地以液体形式给药。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "soluble" as "solution + able" — something able to form a solution by dissolving in liquid.
把"soluble"想成"solution + able"——能够溶解在液体中形成溶液的东西。
