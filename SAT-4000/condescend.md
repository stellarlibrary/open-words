# 👑 condescend

🔊 /ˌkɒndɪˈsɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To behave as though one is superior to others; to do something that one regards as beneath one's dignity; to stoop or deign.

**中文：** 屈尊；俯就；表现出高人一等的态度。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- deign（屈尊）
- patronize（以恩人态度对待）
- stoop（降低身份）
- lower oneself（自降身份）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** condescend to do something / condescend to someone

**Example:** The professor would never **condescend** to explain basic concepts in a dismissive manner.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The wealthy aristocrat refused to **condescend** to speak with the merchants at the public gathering.
   那位富有的贵族拒绝屈尊与公共集会上的商人交谈。

2. She felt that her supervisor would often **condescend** to junior staff by oversimplifying routine instructions.
   她觉得主管经常通过过度简化日常指示来对初级员工表现出居高临下的态度。

3. The distinguished diplomat did not **condescend** to acknowledge the criticism raised by less experienced delegates.
   这位杰出的外交官不屑于理会经验较少的代表提出的批评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Con-" (with) + "descend" (go down) — to go down to someone's level, implying you think you are above them.

**中文：** "con-"（一起）+ "descend"（下降）——降到别人的层次，暗示你觉得自己高人一等，即"屈尊、俯就"。
