# 💡 clarify

🔊 /ˈklærɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make a statement or situation less confused and more comprehensible; to remove impurities from a substance
**中文：** v. 澄清；阐明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elucidate（阐明）
- illuminate（阐释）
- explicate（详细解释）
- simplify（简化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clarify a point / clarify one's position / clarify the meaning / clarify matters**

👉 Example: The professor paused the lecture to clarify a concept that had confused several students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The minister held a press conference to **clarify** the government's official stance on the newly proposed trade agreement.
   部长召开新闻发布会，澄清政府对新提出的贸易协定的官方立场。

2. Researchers must **clarify** their methodology in sufficient detail so that other scholars can replicate their experimental findings.
   研究人员必须足够详细地阐明其方法论，以便其他学者能够重复其实验结果。

3. The editor asked the author to **clarify** several ambiguous passages that could be interpreted in contradictory ways.
   编辑要求作者澄清几段可能被矛盾解读的模糊文字。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clar-ify" — to make "clear" (clar-), like wiping fog off a window so you can finally see through it.
把"clarify"想成使其"清楚"（clar-）——就像擦掉窗户上的雾气，让你终于能看清窗外。
