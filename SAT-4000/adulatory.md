# 🪞 adulatory

🔊 /ˈædʒələtɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Excessively praising or flattering; expressing servile admiration
**中文：** adj. 阿谀的；奉承的；谄媚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flattering（奉承的）
- sycophantic（谄媚的）
- obsequious（卑躬屈膝的）
- fawning（讨好的）
- laudatory（赞美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adulatory remarks / adulatory tone / adulatory praise / adulatory review**

👉 Example: The biography was criticized for its adulatory tone, which overlooked the subject's well-documented flaws.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **adulatory** speech delivered by the chairman seemed insincere and designed merely to curry political favor.
   董事长那番阿谀奉承的讲话显得很不真诚，只不过是为了讨好政治人物。

2. Scholars dismissed the **adulatory** account of the dictator's reign as propaganda rather than legitimate historical analysis.
   学者们认为对该独裁者统治的谄媚描述不过是宣传，而非正当的历史分析。

3. The journalist refused to write an **adulatory** profile, choosing instead to present a balanced and thoroughly researched portrait.
   这位记者拒绝撰写阿谀奉承的人物特写，而是选择呈现一幅客观全面、深入研究的肖像。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adulatory" as "a-DULL-story" — an adulatory review is so full of empty praise that it becomes a dull, unbelievable story.
把"adulatory"想成"a-DULL-story"——一篇阿谀的评论充满空洞的赞美，变成了一个无聊(dull)且令人难以置信的故事。
