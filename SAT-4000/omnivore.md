# 🍽️ omnivore

🔊 /ˈɑːmnɪvɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An animal or person that eats food from both plant and animal sources; one that consumes a variety of foods indiscriminately
**中文：** n. 杂食动物；不偏食的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mixed feeder（混合食性动物）
- generalist feeder（广食性动物）
- non-selective eater（非选择性进食者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natural omnivore / true omnivore / omnivore diet / classified as an omnivore / omnivore species**

👉 Example: Bears are classic omnivores, feeding on berries, roots, fish, and small mammals depending on the season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Humans are considered **omnivores** because their digestive systems are adapted to process both plant-based and animal-based nutrition.
   人类被认为是杂食动物，因为他们的消化系统适应处理植物性和动物性营养。

2. The biologist explained that the raccoon is a highly successful **omnivore** capable of thriving in diverse habitats worldwide.
   生物学家解释说，浣熊是一种非常成功的杂食动物，能够在全球各种栖息地中繁衍生息。

3. As an **omnivore**, the wild boar plays a critical ecological role by dispersing seeds and controlling insect populations.
   作为杂食动物，野猪通过传播种子和控制昆虫数量发挥着关键的生态作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "omni-vore" as "omni" (all) + "vore" (to eat, from Latin vorare) — one that eats everything, both plants and meat.
把"omnivore"想成"omni（所有）"加"vore（吃，源自拉丁语vorare）"——什么都吃的动物，包括植物和肉类。
