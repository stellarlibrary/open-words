# 🔬 minuscule

🔊 /ˈmɪnəskjuːl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely small; tiny. (n.) A lowercase letter; a small cursive script developed in the seventh century.
**中文：** adj. 极小的 n. 小写字；草写小字

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tiny（微小的）
- minute（极小的）
- microscopic（微观的）
- negligible（微不足道的）
- infinitesimal（极微小的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **minuscule amount / minuscule difference / minuscule fraction / minuscule detail**

👉 Example: The researchers found that the minuscule variation in temperature had a profound effect on the chemical reaction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientists detected a **minuscule** trace of the rare element that was nearly invisible to standard laboratory instruments.
   科学家们检测到了一种稀有元素的极微量痕迹，标准实验室仪器几乎无法发现。

2. Only a **minuscule** percentage of applicants were admitted to the highly competitive doctoral program at the prestigious university.
   只有极少比例的申请者被这所名校竞争激烈的博士项目录取。

3. The **minuscule** handwriting in the ancient manuscript required scholars to use powerful magnification tools for accurate transcription.
   古代手稿中极小的字迹要求学者使用强力放大工具进行准确抄录。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "minus-cule" as "minus" plus "molecule" — something so small it has been reduced to the size of a molecule, practically invisible.
把"minuscule"想成"minus（减少）"加"cule（小分子）"——小到被缩减至分子大小，几乎看不见。
