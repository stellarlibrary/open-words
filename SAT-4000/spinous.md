# 🌵 spinous

🔊 /ˈspaɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Resembling a spine or thorn; covered with spines or thorns; sharp-pointed; difficult to handle
**中文：** adj. 多刺的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thorny（有刺的）
- prickly（多刺的）
- spiny（带刺的）
- barbed（有倒刺的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spinous process / spinous projection / spinous layer / spinous surface**

👉 Example: The spinous processes of the vertebrae are prominent landmarks used in medical examinations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **spinous** processes of the lumbar vertebrae serve as important attachment points for muscles and ligaments.
   腰椎的棘突是肌肉和韧带的重要附着点。

2. The botanist noted that the **spinous** leaves of the desert plant had evolved as a defense against herbivores.
   植物学家注意到，这种沙漠植物的多刺叶片是其抵御食草动物的进化结果。

3. Careful examination revealed a **spinous** growth on the specimen's outer surface that had not been previously documented.
   仔细检查发现标本外表面有一个此前未被记录的刺状突起。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spinous" as "spine-ous" — full of spines, like a cactus covered in sharp, pointed thorns.
把"spinous"想成"spine-ous"——满是刺，就像仙人掌上布满尖锐的刺。
