# ➡️ sequent

🔊 /ˈsiːkwənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Following in a sequence; successive; occurring as a result. (n.) Something that follows; a consequence or result
**中文：** adj. 接续而来的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- successive（连续的）
- subsequent（随后的）
- consecutive（接连的）
- consequent（随之发生的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sequent events / sequent stages / sequent development / sequent phases**

👉 Example: The historian traced the sequent stages of the empire's decline through detailed archival research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist examined the **sequent** layers of sedimentary rock to reconstruct the environmental conditions of each prehistoric era.
   地质学家检查了连续的沉积岩层，以重建每个史前时代的环境条件。

2. Each **sequent** phase of the clinical trial must receive regulatory approval before researchers can proceed to the next stage.
   临床试验的每个接续阶段都必须获得监管批准，研究人员才能进入下一阶段。

3. The author structured the novel around **sequent** chapters that each revealed a new dimension of the protagonist's character.
   作者围绕接连的章节构建小说，每一章都揭示了主人公性格的一个新维度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sequent" as "sequ-ent" — sharing the root with "sequence," it describes what comes next in line, one thing following another.
把"sequent"想成"sequ-ent"——与"sequence"共享词根，描述排列中接下来的事物，一个接着一个。
