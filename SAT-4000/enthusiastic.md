# 🌟 enthusiastic

🔊 /ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval; passionately devoted
**中文：** adj. 热情的；热心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- passionate（热情的）
- zealous（热忱的）
- fervent（热烈的）
- eager（渴望的）
- ardent（热切的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enthusiastic about / enthusiastic response / enthusiastic support / enthusiastic welcome**

👉 Example: The students were enthusiastic about the new research project and volunteered immediately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audience gave an **enthusiastic** standing ovation after the orchestra delivered a flawless performance of the symphony.
   管弦乐队完美演绎了这首交响曲后，观众报以热情的起立鼓掌。

2. Despite initial skepticism, the board members became **enthusiastic** supporters of the proposed environmental sustainability initiative.
   尽管最初持怀疑态度，董事会成员们后来成为了拟议的环境可持续发展倡议的热心支持者。

3. The professor received an **enthusiastic** reception from scholars who recognized the groundbreaking significance of her published findings.
   这位教授受到了学者们的热烈欢迎，他们认识到了她发表的研究成果的开创性意义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-thusi-astic" — rooted in Greek "entheos" meaning "inspired by god," so enthusiastic means divinely inspired with passion.
把"enthusiastic"想成来自希腊语"entheos（神灵附体的）"——如同被神灵激发了热情，充满激情和热心。
