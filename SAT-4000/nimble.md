# 🏃 nimble

🔊 /ˈnɪmbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Quick and light in movement or action; mentally agile and quick-thinking
**中文：** adj. 敏捷的；聪明的；灵巧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agile（敏捷的）
- deft（灵巧的）
- sprightly（活泼的）
- quick-witted（机智的）
- lithe（柔软的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nimble fingers / nimble mind / nimble feet / nimble response**

👉 Example: The nimble gymnast executed a flawless routine that captivated the entire audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **nimble** cat leaped effortlessly from rooftop to rooftop, navigating the narrow gaps with remarkable precision and grace.
   那只敏捷的猫毫不费力地在屋顶之间跳跃，以惊人的精准和优雅穿越狭窄的间隙。

2. A **nimble** mind is essential for success in competitive debate, where arguments must be countered within seconds.
   在竞技辩论中，敏捷的思维至关重要，因为必须在几秒钟内反驳论点。

3. The startup's **nimble** approach to product development allowed it to adapt quickly to shifting consumer demands and trends.
   这家初创公司灵活的产品开发方式使其能够迅速适应不断变化的消费者需求和趋势。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nimble" as "nimbly able" — someone who is nimbly able to move quickly and think fast, like a dancer on light feet.
把"nimble"想成"nimbly able（灵活有能力的）"——一个能够快速移动和快速思考的人，就像轻盈舞步的舞者。
