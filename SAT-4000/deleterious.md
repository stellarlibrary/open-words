# ☠️ deleterious

🔊 /ˌdelɪˈtɪəriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing harm or damage; injurious; having a harmful or toxic effect on something
**中文：** adj. 有毒的；有害的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmful（有害的）
- detrimental（不利的）
- injurious（伤害的）
- noxious（有毒的）
- pernicious（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deleterious effects / deleterious impact / deleterious to health / deleterious consequences / deleterious substances**

👉 Example: The study revealed the deleterious effects of prolonged screen exposure on adolescent sleep patterns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers have confirmed that prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants has **deleterious** effects on both human health and ecosystems.
   研究人员已经证实，长期接触工业污染物对人类健康和生态系统都有有害影响。

2. The committee warned that the proposed budget cuts would have a **deleterious** impact on public education and community services.
   委员会警告说，拟议的预算削减将对公共教育和社区服务产生有害影响。

3. Consuming excessive amounts of processed sugar is widely recognized as **deleterious** to cardiovascular health and metabolic function.
   过量摄入加工糖被广泛认为对心血管健康和代谢功能有害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "delete-rious" — something so harmful that you want to "delete" it from your life because it causes serious damage.
把"deleterious"想成"delete（删除）+ -rious"——某种东西有害到你想从生活中"删除"它，因为它造成严重损害。
