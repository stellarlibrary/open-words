# 🔀 alternative

🔊 /ɔːlˈtɜːrnətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Available as another possibility or choice. (n.) One of two or more available possibilities; a substitute or replacement option.
**中文：** (adj.) 供选择的；替代的 (n.) 选择；替代方案

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- option（选项）
- substitute（替代品）
- choice（选择）
- replacement（替代物）
- fallback（后备方案）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alternative to, viable alternative, alternative approach, alternative energy, no alternative but to**
👉 Example: The board explored several alternatives before selecting the most cost-effective solution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Renewable energy sources represent a sustainable **alternative** to fossil fuels in the global effort against climate change.
   可再生能源是全球应对气候变化中化石燃料的可持续替代方案。

2. The physician recommended an **alternative** treatment plan after the patient exhibited adverse reactions to the initial medication.
   在患者对初始药物出现不良反应后，医生推荐了一种替代治疗方案。

3. With no viable **alternative** available, the committee reluctantly approved the original proposal despite its known limitations.
   由于没有可行的替代方案，委员会不情愿地批准了原方案，尽管它有已知的局限性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alternate + ive" — when you have an alternative, you have something that can alternate with or replace the original.
把 alternative 想象成 "alternate（交替）+ ive"——当你有一个 alternative，你就有了可以替代原来选项的东西。
