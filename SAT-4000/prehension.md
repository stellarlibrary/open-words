# 🤲 prehension

🔊 /prɪˈhɛnʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of grasping or seizing; mental understanding or comprehension of an idea
**中文：** n. 抓住；理解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grasp（抓握；理解）
- comprehension（理解）
- apprehension（领悟）
- seizure（抓取）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prehension of objects / prehension ability / manual prehension / cognitive prehension**

👉 Example: The therapist designed exercises to improve the patient's prehension after the hand surgery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study examined how infants develop **prehension** skills during the first twelve months of life through systematic observation.
   该研究通过系统观察考察了婴儿在出生后头十二个月如何发展抓握技能。

2. Damage to the motor cortex can severely impair **prehension**, making it difficult for patients to grasp everyday objects.
   运动皮层的损伤会严重影响抓握能力，使患者难以抓住日常物品。

3. The philosopher distinguished between sensory **prehension** and intellectual understanding as two fundamentally different modes of cognition.
   这位哲学家将感知性理解和智力理解区分为两种根本不同的认知模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-hension" as related to "apprehension" — both come from Latin "prehendere" (to seize). To grasp something physically or mentally.
把"prehension"与"apprehension"联系起来——两者都源自拉丁语"prehendere"（抓住）。无论是物理上还是思维上的抓握。
