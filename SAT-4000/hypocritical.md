# 🎭 hypocritical

🔊 /ˌhɪpəˈkrɪtɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Behaving in a way that suggests one has higher standards or more noble beliefs than is actually the case; characterized by hypocrisy
**中文：** adj. 虚伪的；伪善的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disingenuous（不真诚的）
- insincere（虚伪的）
- duplicitous（口是心非的）
- two-faced（两面派的）
- sanctimonious（假装虔诚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hypocritical behavior / hypocritical stance / it would be hypocritical to / deeply hypocritical**

👉 Example: It would be hypocritical to criticize others for wasteful spending while indulging in extravagant luxuries yourself.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial described the government's stance on human rights as profoundly **hypocritical** given its own record of suppressing dissent.
   社论将政府在人权问题上的立场描述为极其虚伪的，因为其自身就有镇压异见的记录。

2. Students found it **hypocritical** that the university promoted sustainability while investing heavily in fossil fuel corporations.
   学生们认为大学一边推广可持续发展，一边大量投资化石燃料公司，这是虚伪的。

3. The philosopher cautioned that **hypocritical** moralizing ultimately undermines the credibility of even the most legitimate ethical arguments.
   这位哲学家警告说，虚伪的道德说教最终会损害即使是最正当的伦理论点的可信度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hypocritical" as "hypo-critical" — someone who is "under" (hypo) their own critical standards, failing to meet the very rules they impose on others.
把"hypocritical"想成"hypo-critical"——某人处于自己批判标准"之下"（hypo），无法达到他们对别人施加的标准。
