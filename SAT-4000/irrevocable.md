# ⛓️ irrevocable

🔊 /ɪˈrevəkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not able to be changed, reversed, or recovered; final and unalterable
**中文：** 不可改变的；不能取消的；不能挽回的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irreversible（不可逆的）
- unalterable（不可改变的）
- final（最终的）
- binding（有约束力的）
- permanent（永久的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irrevocable decision / irrevocable trust / irrevocable commitment / irrevocable damage**

👉 Example: The board made an irrevocable decision to close the factory, leaving no possibility for future reconsideration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The contract contained an **irrevocable** clause that prevented either party from withdrawing once the agreement had been formally signed.
   该合同包含一项不可撤销的条款，一旦正式签署，任何一方都不得撤出。

2. Her departure from the research team proved to be **irrevocable**, as the institution had already appointed a permanent replacement.
   她离开研究团队一事被证明是不可挽回的，因为该机构已经任命了一位永久替代者。

3. Legal scholars emphasize that establishing an **irrevocable** trust requires careful deliberation, since the terms cannot be modified afterward.
   法律学者强调，设立不可撤销的信托需要慎重考虑，因为条款事后无法修改。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ir-revoke-able" as "not able to revoke" — once done, you cannot call it back, like words you can never un-speak.
把"irrevocable"拆分为"ir-（不）+ revoke（撤回）+ -able（能够）"——不能撤回的，就像说出口的话永远无法收回。
