# 💛 sympathetic

🔊 /ˌsɪmpəˈθɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Feeling or showing sympathy; relating to the sympathetic nervous system; resonant or agreeable
**中文：** adj. 同情的；共鸣的，赞同的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compassionate（有同情心的）
- understanding（理解的）
- empathetic（共情的）
- supportive（支持的）
- commiserative（怜悯的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sympathetic to / sympathetic toward / sympathetic ear / sympathetic response / sympathetic nervous system**

👉 Example: The counselor provided a sympathetic ear to students struggling with academic pressure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members were largely **sympathetic** to the proposal and agreed to allocate additional funding for the project.
   委员会成员在很大程度上赞同该提案，并同意为该项目拨付额外资金。

2. Although the judge appeared **sympathetic** toward the defendant, she nonetheless imposed the sentence required by the statute.
   尽管法官看起来对被告表示同情，但她仍然依法判处了法定刑罚。

3. The activation of the **sympathetic** nervous system triggers an increase in heart rate and heightened alertness in response to danger.
   交感神经系统的激活会引起心率加快并提高警觉性，以应对危险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sym-pathetic" as "same-feeling" — you feel the same pain as another person, showing sympathy.
把"sympathetic"想成"same-feeling"——你感受到与他人相同的痛苦，即表示同情。
