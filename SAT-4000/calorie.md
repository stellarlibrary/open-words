# 🔥 calorie

🔊 /ˈkæləri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A unit of energy used to measure the amount of heat or energy that food provides when consumed
**中文：** n. 卡路里（热量单位）；大卡

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- kilocalorie（千卡）
- energy unit（能量单位）
- thermal unit（热量单位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **calorie intake / calorie deficit / burn calories / count calories / low-calorie**

👉 Example: Nutritionists recommend monitoring daily calorie intake to maintain a healthy weight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research study demonstrated that reducing **calorie** consumption by twenty percent significantly improved metabolic health markers.
   这项研究表明，减少百分之二十的卡路里摄入显著改善了代谢健康指标。

2. Athletes must carefully balance their **calorie** expenditure with adequate nutritional intake to sustain peak physical performance.
   运动员必须仔细平衡卡路里消耗与充足的营养摄入，以维持最佳体能表现。

3. The food label indicated that each serving contained approximately two hundred **calories**, primarily derived from carbohydrates.
   食品标签显示每份含有大约两百卡路里，主要来自碳水化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "calorie" as coming from Latin "calor" meaning "heat" — calories measure the heat energy stored in food.
把"calorie"联想到拉丁语"calor"（热量）——卡路里衡量食物中储存的热能。
