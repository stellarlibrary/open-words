# ☠️ virulent

🔊 /ˈvɪrjələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely severe or harmful in effect; bitterly hostile or malicious; (of a disease or poison) highly infectious or toxic
**中文：** 剧毒的；致命的；恶性的；恶毒的；充满敌意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- toxic（有毒的）
- venomous（恶毒的）
- malignant（恶性的）
- pernicious（有害的）
- hostile（敌对的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **virulent strain / virulent attack / virulent criticism / virulent disease / virulent hatred**

👉 Example: The virulent strain of influenza spread rapidly through the densely populated urban centers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists identified a **virulent** strain of the pathogen that proved resistant to all currently available antibiotic treatments.
   科学家们发现了一种剧毒的病原体菌株，它对目前所有可用的抗生素治疗都具有耐药性。

2. The editorial launched a **virulent** attack on the administration's policies, accusing officials of systematic corruption and negligence.
   该社论对政府的政策发起了恶毒的攻击，指控官员们系统性腐败和玩忽职守。

3. The **virulent** spread of misinformation on social media platforms poses a significant threat to public health and democratic institutions.
   虚假信息在社交媒体平台上的恶性传播对公共卫生和民主制度构成了重大威胁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virulent" as "virus + lent" — like a virus that has been lent deadly power; anything virulent is dangerously toxic or hostile.
把"virulent"想成"virus + lent"——就像一种被赋予了致命力量的病毒；任何virulent的东西都是危险地有毒或充满敌意的。
