# 🎈 distend

🔊 /dɪˈstɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To swell or cause to swell by pressure from within; to expand or enlarge abnormally
**中文：** v. 使膨胀；使扩张；扩大

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- swell（膨胀）
- inflate（充气）
- expand（扩张）
- bloat（膨胀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **distend with gas / distended abdomen / distended veins / visibly distended**

👉 Example: The patient's abdomen was visibly distended due to the accumulation of excess fluid.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician noted that the patient's stomach had begun to **distend** significantly as a result of the intestinal obstruction.
   医生注意到由于肠梗阻，患者的胃部已经开始明显膨胀。

2. Excessive internal pressure caused the walls of the container to **distend** beyond their designed capacity, risking structural failure.
   过大的内部压力使容器壁膨胀超出其设计承受能力，存在结构破坏的风险。

3. The balloon continued to **distend** as more helium was pumped into it, eventually reaching its maximum elastic tolerance.
   随着更多氦气被泵入，气球持续膨胀，最终达到其最大弹性极限。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-tend" as "dis- (apart) + tend (stretch)" — to stretch apart or outward, like something swelling.
把"distend"想成"dis-（向外）+ tend（伸展）"——向外伸展扩张，就像某物膨胀一样。
