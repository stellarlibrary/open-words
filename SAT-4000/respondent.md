# 🗣️ respondent

🔊 /rɪˈspɒndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Making answer; responsive. (n.) A person who replies to something, especially in a survey or legal proceeding; the defendant in a lawsuit
**中文：** adj. 回答的 n. 被告；应答者；回答问题的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- defendant（被告）
- participant（参与者）
- interviewee（受访者）
- answerer（回答者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **survey respondent / respondent in a case / the respondent argued / respondent data / respondent pool**

👉 Example: Each respondent in the survey was asked to provide detailed feedback on their experience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court ordered the **respondent** to appear before the tribunal and present a formal defense against the allegations.
   法院命令被告出庭并对指控提出正式辩护。

2. Over seventy percent of **respondent** participants indicated strong support for the proposed educational reform initiative.
   超过百分之七十的受访参与者表示强烈支持拟议的教育改革倡议。

3. The **respondent** in the divorce proceeding contested the division of assets as fundamentally inequitable and legally flawed.
   离婚诉讼中的被告对财产分配提出异议，认为其根本上不公平且存在法律缺陷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "respond-ent" — a respondent is simply someone who "responds," whether answering a survey or responding to legal charges.
把"respondent"想成"respond（回应）+ ent（人）"——respondent就是"回应的人"，无论是回答调查还是应对法律指控。
