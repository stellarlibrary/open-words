# 💀 exterminate

🔊 /ɪkˈstɜːrmɪˌneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To destroy completely; to eradicate or eliminate an entire group, population, or species
**中文：** v. 消灭；根除

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eradicate（根除）
- annihilate（消灭）
- eliminate（消除）
- destroy（摧毁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exterminate pests / exterminate a species / exterminate the enemy / exterminate vermin**

👉 Example: The city council approved a plan to exterminate the invasive insect population threatening local agriculture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The invasive species posed such a severe ecological threat that authorities launched a campaign to **exterminate** the entire population.
   这种入侵物种构成了严重的生态威胁，当局发起了一场消灭其全部种群的运动。

2. Historical accounts reveal the horrific attempts to **exterminate** entire ethnic groups during periods of widespread conflict and hatred.
   历史记载揭示了在大规模冲突和仇恨时期消灭整个族群的可怕企图。

3. The research team developed an environmentally safe method to **exterminate** harmful bacteria without damaging surrounding beneficial organisms.
   研究团队开发了一种环保安全的方法来消灭有害细菌，同时不损害周围的有益生物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-termin-ate" — "terminus" means end or boundary, so to exterminate is to bring something completely to its end.
把"exterminate"想成"ex(完全)+termin(终结)+ate"——使某物彻底终结，即消灭、根除。
