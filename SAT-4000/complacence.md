# 😌 complacence

🔊 /kəmˈpleɪsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A feeling of quiet satisfaction or self-satisfaction, often without awareness of potential dangers or deficiencies
**中文：** n. 满足；自满

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- self-satisfaction（自满）
- smugness（沾沾自喜）
- contentment（满足）
- complacency（自满）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **breed complacence / a sense of complacence / overcome complacence / slide into complacence**

👉 Example: Years of stable profits bred a dangerous complacence among the company's leadership team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The team's early success fostered a **complacence** that ultimately led to their defeat in the championship finals.
   球队早期的成功滋生了一种自满情绪，最终导致他们在冠军决赛中落败。

2. Experts warned that widespread **complacence** about cybersecurity threats could leave critical infrastructure dangerously vulnerable.
   专家警告说，对网络安全威胁的普遍自满可能使关键基础设施处于危险境地。

3. The professor challenged her students to resist intellectual **complacence** and to question every assumption they encountered.
   教授要求学生抵制智识上的自满，质疑他们遇到的每一个假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-PLACE-nce" as being "comfortable in place" — you feel so comfortable where you are that you become self-satisfied and stop trying.
把"complacence"想成"comfortable in place"——你在原地感到如此舒适，以至于变得自满，不再努力。
