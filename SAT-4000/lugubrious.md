# 😢 lugubrious

🔊 /luːˈɡuːbriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Looking or sounding sad and dismal; excessively mournful in manner or appearance
**中文：** 悲哀的；悲惨的；可怜的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mournful（悲伤的）
- doleful（忧郁的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- woeful（悲哀的）
- sorrowful（悲痛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lugubrious expression / lugubrious tone / lugubrious voice / lugubrious manner**

👉 Example: The old hound had a lugubrious expression that made everyone feel sympathetic toward him.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor delivered the disappointing results in a **lugubrious** tone that made the entire classroom fall silent.
   教授用悲哀的语气宣布了令人失望的结果，整个教室陷入了沉默。

2. His **lugubrious** demeanor at the ceremony suggested he was deeply affected by the loss of his longtime colleague.
   他在仪式上悲伤的举止表明他对失去多年同事深感痛惜。

3. The **lugubrious** melody of the cello echoed through the empty cathedral, evoking a profound sense of solemnity.
   大提琴悲哀的旋律在空旷的大教堂中回荡，唤起了一种深沉的庄严感。

把"lugubrious"想成"loo-goo-boo-hoo"——某人"呜呜"哭泣，一副悲伤泪流满面的样子。
