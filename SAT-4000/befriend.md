# 🤝 befriend

🔊 /bɪˈfrɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To act as a friend to someone; to help, assist, or support, especially in times of need
**中文：** v. 待人如友；帮助；扶助

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assist（帮助）
- support（支持）
- aid（援助）
- patronize（赞助）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **befriend a stranger / befriend a newcomer / befriend the lonely / befriend someone in need**

👉 Example: The kind neighbor befriended the immigrant family and helped them navigate their new surroundings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The experienced diplomat made it a point to **befriend** junior colleagues and offer them valuable guidance and mentorship.
   这位经验丰富的外交官特意与初级同事交好，为他们提供宝贵的指导和帮助。

2. The charitable organization was established to **befriend** disadvantaged youth and provide them with educational opportunities.
   这个慈善组织的成立旨在帮助弱势青少年，为他们提供受教育的机会。

3. During his first semester abroad, a local student **befriended** him and introduced him to the customs and traditions of the region.
   在他出国留学的第一个学期，一位当地学生与他交友，并向他介绍了该地区的风俗和传统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be + friend" — to befriend someone is simply to become their friend, to treat them with friendship and kindness.
把"befriend"拆成"be + friend（朋友）"——待人如友，以友善和关怀对待他人。
