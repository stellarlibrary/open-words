# 🏭 sulfur disoxide

🔊 /ˈsʌlfər daɪˈɒksaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A toxic gas with a pungent smell, composed of sulfur and oxygen, produced by burning fossil fuels and volcanic activity
**中文：** n. 二氧化硫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- SO₂（二氧化硫）
- sulfurous acid anhydride（亚硫酸酐）
- sulfur oxide（硫氧化物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sulfur disoxide emissions / sulfur disoxide pollution / sulfur disoxide levels / exposure to sulfur disoxide**

👉 Example: Regulatory agencies have imposed strict limits on sulfur disoxide emissions from coal-fired power plants.

   长期接触二氧化硫会导致严重的呼吸系统问题，尤其是对有肺部基础疾病的人群。

2. Environmental scientists measured elevated **sulfur disoxide** concentrations in the atmosphere following the volcanic eruption on the island.
   环境科学家在岛上火山喷发后测量到大气中二氧化硫浓度升高。

3. The new industrial regulations mandate a significant reduction in **sulfur disoxide** output to combat the growing threat of acid rain.
   新的工业法规要求大幅减少二氧化硫排放，以应对日益严重的酸雨威胁。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sulfur disoxide" as "sulfur + di + oxide" — "di" means two, so it is sulfur combined with two oxygen atoms, forming SO₂.
把"sulfur disoxide"想成"sulfur + di + oxide"——"di"表示二，即硫与两个氧原子结合，形成SO₂。
