# 📢 blatant

🔊 /ˈbleɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Done openly and unashamedly; completely obvious and conspicuous, often in an offensive way
**中文：** adj. 明显的；明目张胆的；厚颜无耻的；喧嚣的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flagrant（公然的）
- brazen（厚颜无耻的）
- glaring（明显的）
- overt（公开的）
- conspicuous（显眼的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blatant disregard / blatant lie / blatant violation / blatant attempt / blatant hypocrisy**

👉 Example: The company's blatant disregard for safety regulations resulted in a massive regulatory fine.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed a **blatant** violation of international trade agreements that had persisted for several years.
   调查揭露了一项持续数年的对国际贸易协定的公然违反。

2. Her **blatant** disregard for the established protocols shocked even the most lenient members of the oversight committee.
   她对既定规程的公然无视甚至让监督委员会中最宽容的成员也感到震惊。

3. The politician's **blatant** attempt to mislead the public was quickly exposed by investigative journalists and fact-checkers.
   这位政客明目张胆地误导公众的企图很快被调查记者和事实核查人员揭穿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "BLAT-ant" — imagine someone "blating" (blaring) a horn loudly and shamelessly for everyone to hear.
把"blatant"想成"blat"（大声喧嚷）——想象某人厚颜无耻地大声吹号，让所有人都能听到。
