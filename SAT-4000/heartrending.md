# 💔 heartrending

🔊 /ˈhɑːrtˌrɛndɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Causing overwhelming distress; extremely sad or pitiful; evoking deep sorrow
**中文：** adj. 悲惨的；令人心碎的；令人极度悲伤的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- heartbreaking（令人心碎的）
- agonizing（痛苦的）
- poignant（辛酸的）
- distressing（令人痛苦的）
- sorrowful（悲伤的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heartrending story / heartrending scene / heartrending account / heartrending plea**

👉 Example: The documentary included heartrending accounts from survivors of the disaster.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugee's **heartrending** testimony moved the entire assembly and prompted immediate calls for humanitarian action.
   难民令人心碎的证词打动了全体与会人员，并促使各方立即呼吁采取人道主义行动。

2. The novel offers a **heartrending** portrayal of families separated by war and the enduring bonds that sustain them.
   这部小说对被战争分离的家庭以及维系他们的持久纽带进行了令人心碎的描写。

3. Photographs from the aftermath of the earthquake presented a **heartrending** scene of devastation and human suffering.
   地震后的照片呈现出一幅令人心碎的破坏与人类苦难的景象。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heart + rending" — something so sad it rends (tears apart) your heart into pieces.
把"heartrending"想成"heart（心）+ rending（撕裂）"——悲伤到把你的心撕成碎片。
