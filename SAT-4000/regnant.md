# 👑 regnant

🔊 /ˈrɛɡnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Reigning; ruling; exercising authority. Dominant or prevalent; widespread.
**中文：** adj. 统治的；在位的；占优势的；流行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dominant（占优势的）
- prevailing（盛行的）
- reigning（统治的）
- sovereign（至高的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regnant queen / regnant ideology / regnant power / regnant influence**

👉 Example: The regnant philosophy of the era shaped every aspect of public discourse and policy.

---

1. The **regnant** monarch consolidated power by establishing new trade agreements with neighboring kingdoms throughout the decade.
   在位的君主通过与邻国建立新的贸易协议来巩固其在整个十年中的权力。

2. In the nineteenth century, the **regnant** ideology of imperialism drove European nations to expand their colonial territories.
   在十九世纪，占主导地位的帝国主义意识形态驱使欧洲国家扩张其殖民领土。

3. The professor argued that the **regnant** paradigm in the field was outdated and required fundamental theoretical revision.
   这位教授认为该领域中占优势的范式已经过时，需要进行根本性的理论修正。
