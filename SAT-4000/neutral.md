# ⚖️ neutral

🔊 /ˈnjuːtrəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not supporting either side in a conflict or dispute; impartial; having no strongly marked characteristics. (n.) A person or nation that remains impartial
**中文：** adj. 中立的；中性的；中和的 n. 中立国；中立者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impartial（公正的）
- unbiased（无偏见的）
- objective（客观的）
- nonpartisan（无党派的）
- disinterested（不偏不倚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remain neutral / neutral ground / neutral territory / neutral stance / gender-neutral**

👉 Example: The mediator must remain neutral throughout the dispute to ensure a fair resolution for both parties involved.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Switzerland has maintained a **neutral** position in international conflicts for centuries, becoming a symbol of diplomatic impartiality worldwide.
   瑞士几个世纪以来在国际冲突中保持中立立场，成为全球外交公正的象征。

2. The research committee selected a **neutral** venue for the debate to ensure that neither political faction felt disadvantaged or uncomfortable.
   研究委员会选择了一个中立的场地进行辩论，以确保双方政治派别都不会感到不利或不适。

3. Effective journalists strive to present information in a **neutral** manner, allowing readers to form their own informed opinions independently.
   优秀的记者努力以中立的方式呈现信息，让读者能够独立形成自己知情的观点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neutral" as "neither" — a neutral person takes neither side, standing perfectly balanced in the middle of any dispute.
把"neutral"联想到"neither"(两者都不)——中立的人不站任何一方，在争论中保持完美的平衡。
