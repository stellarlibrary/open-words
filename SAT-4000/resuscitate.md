# 💓 resuscitate

🔊 /rɪˈsʌsɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To revive from apparent death or unconsciousness; to restore to vigor, activity, or prominence
**中文：** v. 使复苏；使苏醒；使复兴；复活

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revive（使复活）
- resurgent（复兴的）
- reanimate（使复活）
- rejuvenate（使恢复活力）
- restore（恢复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resuscitate a patient / resuscitate the economy / resuscitate a failing project / resuscitate interest in**

👉 Example: The paramedics worked tirelessly to resuscitate the drowning victim at the scene.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Emergency physicians managed to **resuscitate** the patient after nearly four minutes of cardiac arrest in the operating room.
   急诊医生在手术室里经过近四分钟的心脏骤停后成功使病人复苏。

2. The new administration introduced sweeping reforms designed to **resuscitate** the nation's stagnating industrial economy.
   新政府推出了全面改革措施，旨在复兴国家停滞不前的工业经济。

3. Curators attempted to **resuscitate** public interest in classical sculpture through a series of innovative multimedia exhibitions.
   策展人试图通过一系列创新的多媒体展览来重新激发公众对古典雕塑的兴趣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-SUS-citate" — "re-" (again) + "sus" sounds like "sustain" — to sustain life again, to bring someone back.
把"resuscitate"想成"re-"（再次）+ "sus"（像sustain，维持）——再次维持生命，把人救回来。
