# 🔥 carnal

🔊 /ˈkɑːrnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to physical, especially sexual, needs and desires; of or relating to the body or flesh rather than the spirit
**中文：** adj. 肉体的；肉欲的；性欲的；淫荡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sensual（感官的）
- fleshly（肉体的）
- lustful（贪欲的）
- corporeal（肉体的）
- physical（身体的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **carnal desires / carnal knowledge / carnal pleasures / carnal instincts / carnal appetites**

👉 Example: The philosopher warned against the dangers of surrendering entirely to carnal desires.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The theologian argued that spiritual transcendence requires one to overcome **carnal** temptations and base physical urges.
   这位神学家认为，精神的超越需要人们克服肉体的诱惑和低级的生理冲动。

2. Medieval literature frequently explored the tension between **carnal** desire and the pursuit of divine grace and virtue.
   中世纪文学经常探讨肉欲与追求神圣恩典和美德之间的矛盾。

3. The ascetic monks renounced all **carnal** pleasures in their unwavering devotion to contemplation and spiritual purity.
   苦修僧侣们放弃了一切肉体享乐，坚定不移地致力于冥想和精神纯净。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carn-" from Latin "carnis" meaning "flesh" — carnal literally means "of the flesh," relating to bodily and physical desires rather than spiritual ones.
把"carn-"想成拉丁语"carnis"（肉体）——carnal字面意思就是"肉体的"，与身体和物质的欲望有关，而非精神层面的。
