# 🦎 camouflage

🔊 /ˈkæməflɑːʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The disguising of military equipment, personnel, or objects to blend with surroundings; any means of concealment or deception. (v.) To disguise or conceal by means of camouflage
**中文：** n. 伪装；掩饰 v. 伪装；掩饰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disguise（伪装）
- concealment（隐蔽）
- cover（掩护）
- mask（掩饰）
- cloak（遮掩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **camouflage pattern / camouflage uniform / camouflage one's intentions / serve as camouflage / natural camouflage**

👉 Example: The soldiers wore camouflage uniforms to blend seamlessly into the dense forest environment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chameleon's remarkable ability to **camouflage** itself against various backgrounds has long fascinated evolutionary biologists worldwide.
   变色龙在各种背景下伪装自己的非凡能力长期以来一直令全球进化生物学家着迷。

2. The company used charitable donations as **camouflage** for its aggressive tax avoidance strategies and questionable business practices.
   该公司利用慈善捐赠作为其激进避税策略和可疑商业行为的掩饰。

3. Military engineers developed advanced digital **camouflage** patterns designed to disrupt visual detection across multiple terrain types effectively.
   军事工程师开发了先进的数字伪装图案，旨在有效干扰多种地形中的视觉探测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "camouflage" as from French "camoufler" meaning "to disguise" — picture a soldier's face painted to "come-off-large" and disappear into the landscape.
把"camouflage"联想到法语"camoufler"（伪装）——想象一个士兵的脸上涂满迷彩，融入风景中消失不见。
