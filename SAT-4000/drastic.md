# ⚡ drastic

🔊 /ˈdræstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Likely to have a strong or far-reaching effect; extreme or severe in nature
**中文：** adj. 激烈的；猛烈的；极端的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extreme（极端的）
- severe（严重的）
- radical（激进的）
- sweeping（彻底的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **drastic measures / drastic action / drastic change / drastic reduction**

👉 Example: The government took drastic measures to contain the spread of the epidemic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board approved **drastic** budget cuts that would eliminate several departments and reduce the workforce significantly.
   董事会批准了激烈的预算削减，这将取消多个部门并大幅裁减员工。

2. Climate scientists warn that without **drastic** intervention, global temperatures could rise to catastrophic levels by mid-century.
   气候科学家警告说，如果没有猛烈的干预，全球气温可能在本世纪中叶上升到灾难性水平。

3. The city implemented **drastic** traffic regulations to address the severe air pollution crisis threatening public health.
   该市实施了激烈的交通管制措施，以应对威胁公众健康的严重空气污染危机。
