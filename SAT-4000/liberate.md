# 🗽 liberate

🔊 /ˈlɪbəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To set free from imprisonment, oppression, or foreign control; to release from a state of constraint
**中文：** vt. 解放，释放

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emancipate（解放）
- free（释放）
- release（释放）
- deliver（解救）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **liberate from / liberate a country / liberate prisoners / liberate oneself**

👉 Example: The allied forces fought to liberate the occupied territories during the war.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The revolutionary movement sought to **liberate** the oppressed citizens from decades of authoritarian rule and systemic injustice.
   这场革命运动旨在将受压迫的公民从数十年的专制统治和系统性不公正中解放出来。

2. Education has the profound power to **liberate** individuals from the constraints of ignorance and limited economic opportunity.
   教育具有将个人从无知和有限的经济机会的束缚中解放出来的深远力量。

3. Scientists developed a novel technique to **liberate** trapped gases from deep geological formations for sustainable energy production.
   科学家们开发了一种新技术，从深层地质构造中释放被困气体以进行可持续能源生产。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liberate" as "liberty" + "ate" — to create liberty, you liberate people by setting them free from bondage.
把"liberate"想成"liberty"（自由）+"ate"——要创造自由，就要通过解放人们使他们脱离束缚。
