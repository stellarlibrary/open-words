# 💪 virile

🔊 /ˈvɪrəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having strength, energy, and a strong drive traditionally associated with masculinity; robust and vigorous
**中文：** 男性的；有男子气概的；刚健的；强壮的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- masculine（男性的）
- robust（强健的）
- vigorous（精力充沛的）
- manly（有男子气概的）
- potent（强有力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **virile strength / virile energy / virile appearance / virile prose**

👉 Example: The author's virile prose style conveyed raw energy and uncompromising directness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sculptor captured the **virile** strength of the warrior figure through bold lines and exaggerated muscular proportions.
   雕塑家通过大胆的线条和夸张的肌肉比例捕捉到了战士形象的阳刚力量。

2. Literary critics praised the novel for its **virile** narrative voice, which combined forceful expression with emotional depth.
   文学评论家称赞这部小说具有刚健的叙事声音，将有力的表达与情感深度相结合。

3. The aging actor maintained a remarkably **virile** presence on stage, commanding the audience's attention with unwavering authority.
   这位年迈的演员在舞台上保持着令人瞩目的阳刚风采，以坚定不移的气场吸引着观众的注意力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virile" as coming from Latin "vir" meaning "man" — a virile person embodies the traditional qualities of manly strength and vigor.
把"virile"想成来自拉丁语"vir"（意为"男人"）——一个virile的人体现了传统的男性力量和活力。
