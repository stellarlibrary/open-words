# ⏩ anterior

🔊 /ænˈtɪr.i.ər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Located nearer to the front of the body or a structure; coming before in time or order; prior or preceding.

**中文：** 前面的（位于身体或结构的前部）；先前的；在前的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- front（前面的）
- preceding（先前的）
- prior（在前的）
- foregoing（前述的）
- forward（前方的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** anterior wall, anterior lobe, anterior position, anterior to, anterior surface

**Example:** The surgeon made a careful incision along the **anterior** surface of the patient's knee during the procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The MRI scan revealed a small lesion on the **anterior** portion of the patient's cerebral cortex.
   核磁共振扫描显示患者大脑皮层前部有一个小病灶。

2. The **anterior** section of the building houses the reception hall, while the offices occupy the rear wing.
   建筑物的前部是接待大厅，而办公室则位于后翼。

3. Events **anterior** to the signing of the accord were thoroughly examined by the international tribunal.
   国际法庭对协议签署之前的事件进行了彻底审查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anterior" shares the root "ante-" (before/in front of). Think of it as the opposite of "posterior" (behind). In anatomy, anterior = front, posterior = back. anterior 包含词根 ante-（在前面），是 posterior（后面的）的反义词。在解剖学中：anterior = 前面，posterior = 后面。
