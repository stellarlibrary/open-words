# 🗣️ persuade

🔊 /pərˈsweɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause someone to believe or do something through reasoning or argument; to convince or induce
**中文：** v. 说服，劝说；使某人相信；劝某人做（不做）某事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- convince（使信服）
- coax（哄劝）
- induce（诱导）
- sway（使动摇）
- prevail upon（说服）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **persuade someone to do / persuade someone of / persuade someone that / persuade someone into**

👉 Example: The lawyer managed to persuade the jury of her client's complete innocence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat worked tirelessly to **persuade** both nations to accept the terms of the proposed ceasefire agreement.
   这位外交官不懈努力，劝说两国接受拟议的停火协议条款。

2. No amount of empirical evidence could **persuade** the staunch critics to revise their deeply entrenched theoretical positions.
   再多的实证证据也无法说服这些顽固的批评者修正他们根深蒂固的理论立场。

3. The committee chair attempted to **persuade** reluctant members that the proposed reform would yield substantial long-term benefits.
   委员会主席试图说服持保留意见的成员们，所提议的改革将产生可观的长期效益。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-suade" as "per (thoroughly) + suade (sweet talk)" — you thoroughly sweet-talk someone until they agree.
把"persuade"想成"per（彻底地）+ suade（甜言蜜语）"——你彻底地用甜言蜜语说服别人直到他们同意。
