# 🌵 asperity

🔊 /æˈspɛrɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Harshness of tone or manner; roughness; severity of conditions or treatment
**中文：** 严酷；粗暴；刻薄

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harshness（严厉）
- severity（严酷）
- acrimony（尖刻）
- brusqueness（粗暴）
- roughness（粗糙）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with asperity / speak with asperity / tone of asperity / unexpected asperity**

👉 Example: She responded with surprising asperity when questioned about the delay in the project timeline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge addressed the defendant with notable **asperity**, making clear that such misconduct would not be tolerated.
   法官以明显的严厉口气对被告讲话，明确表示这种不当行为不会被容忍。

2. He spoke with sudden **asperity** when his colleagues challenged the validity of his carefully prepared research findings.
   当同事们质疑他精心准备的研究结果的有效性时，他突然严厉地说话。

3. The editorial criticized the administration's policies with considerable **asperity**, sparing no one from its sharp commentary.
   这篇社论以相当尖刻的口吻批评了政府的政策，尖锐的评论对谁都毫不留情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "asperity" as "a-SPER-ity" — like "rough as a spur." A spur is sharp and harsh, just like asperity in tone.
把"asperity"想成"像马刺一样粗糙"——马刺尖锐而刺人，就像语气中的严厉刻薄。
