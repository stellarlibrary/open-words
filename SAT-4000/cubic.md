# 📐 cubic

🔊 /ˈkjuːbɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the shape of a cube; relating to or involving the third power of a quantity; measured in units of volume
**中文：** adj. 立方体的，立方的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cube-shaped（立方形的）
- three-dimensional（三维的）
- volumetric（体积的）
- block-like（块状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cubic meter / cubic centimeter / cubic feet / cubic equation / cubic capacity**

👉 Example: The reservoir has a total capacity of approximately five million cubic meters of fresh water.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers calculated that the underground storage facility could hold over two thousand **cubic** meters of compressed natural gas.
   工程师们计算出地下储存设施可以容纳超过两千立方米的压缩天然气。

2. The mathematician demonstrated an elegant method for solving **cubic** equations that had challenged scholars for centuries.
   这位数学家展示了一种解决困扰学者数百年的三次方程的优雅方法。

3. The mineral specimen exhibited a perfectly **cubic** crystal structure that is characteristic of the halite mineral group.
   该矿物标本展现出完美的立方晶体结构，这是石盐矿物族的典型特征。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cub-ic" — relating to a cube. Just add "-ic" (meaning "of or relating to") to "cube" and you get cubic, describing anything cube-shaped or measured in three dimensions.
把"cubic"想成"cube（立方体）+ ic（……的）"——与立方体有关。只需在"cube"后加"-ic"（表示"关于"），就得到cubic，描述任何立方形的或三维测量的事物。
