# 🌾 unassuming

🔊 /ˌʌnəˈsjuːmɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not pretentious or arrogant; modest and unpretentious in nature or appearance
**中文：** adj. 谦逊的；不装腔作势的；不摆架子的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modest（谦虚的）
- humble（谦逊的）
- unpretentious（不做作的）
- self-effacing（低调的）
- retiring（内敛的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unassuming manner / unassuming personality / quiet and unassuming / remarkably unassuming**

👉 Example: Despite his extraordinary achievements, the professor maintained an unassuming and approachable demeanor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Nobel laureate was known for her **unassuming** nature, preferring quiet laboratory work over public appearances and lectures.
   这位诺贝尔奖得主以谦逊的品性著称，比起公开露面和演讲，她更喜欢安静的实验室工作。

2. Behind the **unassuming** exterior of the small bookshop lay one of the most impressive rare manuscript collections in the country.
   在这家小书店不起眼的外表背后，藏着全国最令人印象深刻的珍稀手稿收藏之一。

3. His **unassuming** leadership style earned him the genuine respect and loyalty of every member of his research team.
   他谦逊的领导风格为他赢得了研究团队每位成员的真诚尊重和忠诚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-assuming" — someone who does not assume importance or superiority, who never puts on airs.
把"unassuming"想成"不自以为是的"——一个不假装自己重要或优越的人，从不摆架子。
