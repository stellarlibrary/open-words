# 🗡️ impugn

🔊 /ɪmˈpjuːn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To dispute the truth, validity, or honesty of; to challenge or call into question; to attack as false or questionable
**中文：** v. 指责；非难；抨击；反驳；对……表示怀疑；质疑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- challenge（质疑）
- dispute（反驳）
- question（质问）
- contest（争辩）
- assail（抨击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impugn someone's motives / impugn one's integrity / impugn the credibility / impugn the validity**

👉 Example: The defense attorney attempted to impugn the reliability of the prosecution's key witness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The opposition leader sought to **impugn** the integrity of the electoral process by citing irregularities in voter registration data.
   反对党领袖试图通过引用选民登记数据中的违规行为来质疑选举过程的公正性。

2. Scientists warned that attempts to **impugn** peer-reviewed research without evidence could undermine public trust in academic institutions.
   科学家警告说，在没有证据的情况下试图质疑同行评审的研究可能会削弱公众对学术机构的信任。

3. The journalist refused to **impugn** the character of the accused without first conducting a thorough and impartial investigation.
   这位记者拒绝在未先进行彻底公正调查的情况下就对被告的品格进行抨击。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impugn" as "I'm a pug — n(o)!" — imagine a stubborn pug dog barking "no!" at everything, challenging and questioning all claims.
把"impugn"想成"im + pugn（来自拉丁语pugnare，战斗）"——用言语去"战斗"，即质疑和抨击。
