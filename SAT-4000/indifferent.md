# 😐 indifferent

🔊 /ɪnˈdɪfərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned; neither good nor bad; mediocre; unbiased or neutral
**中文：** adj. 漠不关心的；冷淡的；不感兴趣的；无偏袒的，中立的；无关紧要的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apathetic（冷漠的）
- unconcerned（不关心的）
- detached（超然的）
- impartial（公正的）
- nonchalant（漫不经心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indifferent to / indifferent about / remain indifferent / seem indifferent**

👉 Example: The committee appeared indifferent to the complaints raised by residents during the public hearing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator remained **indifferent** to the mounting criticism, refusing to alter her position on the proposed legislation.
   这位参议员对日益增长的批评依然漠不关心，拒绝改变她对拟议立法的立场。

2. Scholars argue that an **indifferent** electorate poses a greater threat to democracy than an actively opposed one.
   学者们认为，一个冷漠的选民群体比一个积极反对的群体对民主构成更大的威胁。

3. The judge must remain strictly **indifferent** to public opinion when deliberating on matters of constitutional law.
   法官在审议宪法问题时必须对公众舆论保持严格的中立态度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-different" — something that makes no difference to you, so you simply do not care about it.
把"indifferent"想成"in-different"——某件事对你来说没有不同（无所谓），所以你根本不在乎。
