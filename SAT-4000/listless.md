# 😴 listless

🔊 /ˈlɪstləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking energy, enthusiasm, or interest; feeling languid and indifferent
**中文：** adj. 倦怠的；无精打采的；百无聊赖的；冷漠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lethargic（昏昏欲睡的）
- languid（无力的）
- apathetic（冷漠的）
- sluggish（迟缓的）
- torpid（麻木的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel listless / listless attitude / listless performance / appear listless**

👉 Example: The prolonged heatwave left the students feeling listless and unable to concentrate during lectures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After weeks of intense preparation for the examination, the students became **listless** and struggled to maintain their usual focus.
   经过数周紧张的考试准备后，学生们变得无精打采，难以保持平时的专注力。

2. The patient appeared **listless** and unresponsive, prompting the physician to order a comprehensive series of diagnostic tests.
   患者表现得倦怠且反应迟钝，促使医生下令进行一系列全面的诊断检查。

3. A **listless** economy characterized by low consumer spending and minimal investment alarmed policymakers across the region.
   以低消费支出和极少投资为特征的疲软经济令该地区的政策制定者感到震惊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "list-less" as "without a list" — when you have no to-do list, you drift around with no energy or purpose.
把"listless"想成"没有清单（list）"——当你没有待办事项清单时，你就漫无目的地游荡，毫无精力和方向。
