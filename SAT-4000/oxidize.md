# ⚗️ oxidize

🔊 /ˈɑːksɪdaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To combine or cause to combine chemically with oxygen; to undergo or cause to undergo a reaction in which electrons are lost
**中文：** v. 使氧化；使生锈；发生氧化反应

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corrode（腐蚀）
- rust（生锈）
- tarnish（使失去光泽）
- deteriorate（变质）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oxidize metals / oxidize rapidly / oxidize in air / easily oxidized**

👉 Example: Copper will gradually oxidize when exposed to the atmosphere, forming a distinctive green patina.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. When iron is left exposed to moisture and air, it begins to **oxidize** and eventually forms a layer of reddish-brown rust.
   当铁暴露在潮湿和空气中时，它开始氧化，最终形成一层红棕色的铁锈。

2. The chemist demonstrated how certain metals **oxidize** more rapidly than others when placed in an acidic solution at elevated temperatures.
   化学家演示了某些金属在高温酸性溶液中比其他金属氧化得更快。

3. Antioxidants in fresh fruits help prevent cells from **oxidizing**, thereby reducing the risk of tissue damage and premature aging.
   新鲜水果中的抗氧化剂有助于防止细胞氧化，从而降低组织损伤和过早衰老的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oxid-ize" as "making something into an oxide" — the suffix "-ize" means "to make," so oxidize means to make into an oxide.
把"oxidize"想成"使某物变成oxide（氧化物）"——后缀"-ize"表示"使成为"，所以oxidize就是使之氧化。
