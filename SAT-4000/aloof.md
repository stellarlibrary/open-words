# 🧊 aloof

🔊 /əˈluːf/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant in manner; detached and uninvolved.
**中文：** 冷淡的；远离的；冷漠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detached（超然的）
- distant（疏远的）
- indifferent（冷漠的）
- reserved（矜持的）
- standoffish（冷淡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remain aloof, stand aloof from, seem aloof, aloof manner, aloof demeanor**
👉 Example: He remained aloof from the petty office disputes that consumed his colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new director maintained an **aloof** demeanor that discouraged employees from approaching her with concerns.
   新主管保持着一种冷淡的态度，使员工们不敢向她提出问题。

2. Critics accused the government of standing **aloof** while communities struggled with the aftermath of the disaster.
   批评者指责政府在社区艰难应对灾后局面时袖手旁观。

3. Despite her **aloof** exterior, she possessed a deeply compassionate nature that few people ever witnessed.
   尽管她外表冷漠，但她内心深处有着很少有人见过的深切同情心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a loof" — imagine someone standing on a high "roof" (loof sounds like roof), looking down at others, too far away to engage.
把 aloof 想象成站在高高的 "roof（屋顶）" 上——高高在上，远离人群，自然显得冷淡疏远。
