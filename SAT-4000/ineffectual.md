# 🚫 ineffectual

🔊 /ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not producing the desired effect; lacking the ability to achieve results; futile and ineffective
**中文：** adj. 无效的；不起作用的；徒劳无益的；不成功的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- futile（徒劳的）
- ineffective（无效的）
- useless（无用的）
- fruitless（无成果的）
- unavailing（无益的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ineffectual leader / ineffectual attempt / ineffectual measures / ineffectual protest**

👉 Example: The committee's ineffectual response to the crisis drew sharp criticism from both the public and the media.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government's **ineffectual** attempts to control inflation led to widespread economic instability and growing public discontent across the nation.
   政府控制通货膨胀的无效尝试导致了广泛的经济不稳定和全国日益增长的公众不满。

2. Despite his prestigious credentials, the newly appointed director proved remarkably **ineffectual** at managing the department's complex bureaucratic challenges.
   尽管拥有令人瞩目的资历，新任命的主任在管理该部门复杂的官僚挑战方面被证明是极其无效的。

3. Historians have characterized the monarch's reign as largely **ineffectual**, marked by indecision and an inability to implement meaningful reforms.
   历史学家将这位君主的统治描述为基本上毫无成效的，其特点是优柔寡断和无力实施有意义的改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-EFFECT-ual" as "not having an effect" — something ineffectual simply does not produce the intended result.
把"ineffectual"想成"in(没有)+effect(效果)+ual(的)"——无效的事情就是不能产生预期结果的。
