# 🕷️ spider

🔊 /ˈspaɪdər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small arachnid with eight legs that spins webs; one who sets traps; a tripod or trivet
**中文：** n. 蜘蛛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arachnid（蛛形纲动物）
- crawler（爬行者）
- spinner（吐丝者）
- weaver（织网者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spider web / spider silk / spider venom / spider species**

👉 Example: The garden spider constructed an elaborate web between the branches of the old oak tree.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **spider** had woven an intricate web that glistened with morning dew in the soft sunlight.
   蜘蛛织了一张精致的网，在柔和的阳光下闪烁着晨露的光芒。

2. Researchers discovered that the **spider** silk possesses a tensile strength exceeding that of most synthetic materials.
   研究人员发现蜘蛛丝的抗拉强度超过了大多数合成材料。

3. The venomous **spider** was carefully collected by the entomologist for further study in the university laboratory.
   这只有毒的蜘蛛被昆虫学家小心地收集起来，以便在大学实验室中进一步研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spider" as "spy-der" — a spy that lurks in corners and sets traps with its web to catch prey.
把"spider"想成"spy-der"——一个潜伏在角落里的间谍，用网设下陷阱来捕捉猎物。
