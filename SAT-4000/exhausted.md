# 😩 exhausted

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Completely drained of energy or resources; extremely tired; used up entirely
**中文：** adj. 疲惫的；耗尽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fatigued（疲倦的）
- depleted（耗尽的）
- drained（精疲力竭的）
- weary（疲惫的）
- spent（耗尽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel exhausted / completely exhausted / physically exhausted / mentally exhausted / exhausted resources**

👉 Example: After the twelve-hour surgery, the entire medical team felt completely exhausted.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **exhausted** refugees arrived at the border crossing after walking for several days through harsh terrain.
   筋疲力尽的难民在恶劣地形中步行数天后抵达了边境口岸。

2. Once the nation's petroleum reserves were **exhausted**, the government was compelled to invest heavily in renewable energy.
   一旦国家的石油储备耗尽，政府被迫大力投资可再生能源。

3. The **exhausted** students completed their final examinations and immediately collapsed into well-deserved rest at home.
   疲惫不堪的学生们完成了期末考试后，立即回家好好休息了一番。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exhausted" as "ex-hausted" — the energy has exited and you feel completely hauled down to nothing.
把"exhausted"想成"ex-hausted"——能量已经流出，你感觉整个人被拽空了。
