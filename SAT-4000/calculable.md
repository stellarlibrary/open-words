# 🔢 calculable

🔊 /ˈkælkjələbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Able to be measured, computed, or estimated; predictable and dependable in behavior or outcome
**中文：** 可计算的；能预测的；可靠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- computable（可计算的）
- measurable（可测量的）
- predictable（可预测的）
- estimable（可估计的）
- quantifiable（可量化的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **calculable risk / calculable outcome / calculable effect / easily calculable**

👉 Example: The financial losses, though severe, were at least calculable and could be addressed through systematic recovery measures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineers determined that the structural load was entirely **calculable** using standard formulas from established civil engineering principles.
   工程师们确定结构载荷完全可以使用既定土木工程原理中的标准公式来计算。

2. Unlike emotional damages, the economic losses from the breach of contract were readily **calculable** and presented clearly to the court.
   与精神损害不同，违约造成的经济损失是可以计算的，并被清楚地呈报给了法庭。

3. A responsible leader takes only **calculable** risks, ensuring that potential consequences are thoroughly assessed before any major commitment.
   一个负责任的领导者只承担可预测的风险，确保在做出重大承诺前彻底评估潜在后果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "calculable" as "calculate" + "-able" — if something is calculable, you are able to calculate or predict it with confidence.
把"calculable"想成"calculate（计算）+ -able（能够的）"——如果某物是calculable的，你就能有把握地计算或预测它。
