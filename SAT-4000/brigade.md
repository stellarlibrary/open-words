# 🎖️ brigade

🔊 /brɪˈɡeɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large military unit, typically consisting of several battalions; an organized group of people with a common purpose. (v.) To organize into a brigade
**中文：** n. 旅；大部队；队列 v. 把……编成旅

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- regiment（团）
- battalion（营）
- corps（军团）
- unit（部队）
- squad（小队）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fire brigade / infantry brigade / a brigade of volunteers / brigade commander**

👉 Example: The fire brigade responded swiftly to the emergency and prevented further structural damage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The commanding general dispatched an entire **brigade** to reinforce the defensive positions along the northern border region.
   指挥将军派遣了一整个旅去加强北部边境地区的防御阵地。

2. A volunteer **brigade** was assembled to assist with disaster relief efforts following the devastating earthquake in the province.
   一支志愿者队伍被组建起来，协助省内毁灭性地震后的救灾工作。

3. The infantry **brigade** conducted extensive training exercises to prepare for the complex peacekeeping mission overseas.
   该步兵旅进行了大规模训练演习，为海外复杂的维和任务做准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brigade" as "brave-aid" — a brave group that comes to aid, like a military unit or fire brigade.
把"brigade"想成"brave-aid"——一群勇敢的人前来援助，就像军队或消防队。
