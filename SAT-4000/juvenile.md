# 👦 juvenile

🔊 /ˈdʒuːvənaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or characteristic of young people; childishly immature. (n.) A young person, especially one below the legal age of adulthood
**中文：** adj. 青少年的，少年特有的；幼稚的 n. 青少年；未成年人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- youthful（年轻的）
- immature（不成熟的）
- adolescent（青少年的）
- childish（幼稚的）
- minor（未成年人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **juvenile delinquent / juvenile court / juvenile behavior / juvenile offender**

👉 Example: The judge recommended that the juvenile offender be placed in a rehabilitation program rather than prison.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court ruled that **juvenile** offenders should be afforded greater opportunities for rehabilitation rather than purely punitive sentences.
   法院裁定，少年犯应获得更多的改造机会，而非纯粹的惩罚性判决。

2. His **juvenile** remarks during the formal board meeting undermined his credibility and frustrated his more experienced colleagues.
   他在正式董事会会议上幼稚的言论损害了他的信誉，并让更有经验的同事感到不满。

3. Researchers studied how **juvenile** brain development influences decision-making processes and susceptibility to peer pressure among teenagers.
   研究人员研究了青少年大脑发育如何影响决策过程以及青少年对同伴压力的易感性。
