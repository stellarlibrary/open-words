# 🏔️ hoary

🔊 /ˈhɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Gray or white with age; very old or ancient, often implying something outdated or overused
**中文：** adj. 灰白的；久远的；古老的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ancient（古老的）
- venerable（古老的）
- grizzled（灰白的）
- antiquated（过时的）
- aged（年迈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hoary tradition / hoary tale / hoary head / hoary old saying**

👉 Example: The lecturer dismissed the argument as based on a hoary misconception that had long been disproved.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar challenged the **hoary** assumption that ancient civilizations lacked sophisticated knowledge of mathematics and engineering.
   这位学者质疑了那个古老的假设，即古代文明缺乏复杂的数学和工程知识。

2. A **hoary** oak tree stood at the center of the village, its gnarled branches bearing witness to centuries of change.
   一棵古老的橡树矗立在村庄中央，它那虬曲的枝干见证了几个世纪的变迁。

3. Critics dismissed the politician's speech as a collection of **hoary** platitudes that offered no substantive policy solutions.
   批评人士认为这位政治家的演讲不过是一堆陈旧的老生常谈，没有提供任何实质性的政策方案。

把"hoary"想成"hoar（灰白头发）+ y"——想象一个满头白发的人，年纪大到一切都显得古老。
