# 🔍 subtle

🔊 /ˈsʌtl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) So delicate or precise as to be difficult to detect or describe; making use of clever and indirect methods; capable of making fine distinctions
**中文：** adj. 敏锐的；精细的，微妙的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nuanced（微妙的）
- delicate（精细的）
- understated（含蓄的）
- refined（精炼的）
- elusive（难以捉摸的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subtle difference / subtle hint / subtle change / subtle distinction / subtle influence**

👉 Example: The subtle shift in tone revealed the speaker's underlying frustration with the proceedings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The painting contained **subtle** variations in color that only trained art critics could readily perceive and appreciate.
   这幅画包含微妙的色彩变化，只有受过训练的艺术评论家才能轻易察觉和欣赏。

2. A **subtle** change in the committee's language signaled a significant departure from its previously stated policy positions.
   委员会用语中的微妙变化标志着其与先前既定政策立场的重大偏离。

3. The diplomat's **subtle** approach to negotiation proved far more effective than direct confrontation with the opposing delegation.
   这位外交官微妙的谈判方式被证明远比与对方代表团的直接对抗更为有效。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "subtle" — the "b" is silent, just like something subtle is quiet and hard to notice. The word itself demonstrates its meaning.
想想"subtle"——字母"b"是不发音的，就像微妙的事物一样安静且难以察觉。这个词本身就展示了它的含义。
