# 📉 abate

🔊 /əˈbeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To reduce in intensity, amount, or degree; to diminish or put an end to
**中文：** v. 减轻；减少；废除

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diminish（减少）
- subside（消退）
- lessen（减轻）
- wane（衰退）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abate a nuisance / the storm abated / pain abated / noise abatement**

👉 Example: The fierce winds finally abated after three days of relentless battering along the coast.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public outrage over the scandal showed no signs of **abating** despite the government's repeated apologies and reforms.
   尽管政府多次道歉和改革，公众对丑闻的愤怒仍没有减弱的迹象。

2. The floodwaters began to **abate** slowly, allowing emergency crews to access the devastated neighborhoods at last.
   洪水开始慢慢消退，使救援人员终于能够进入受灾社区。

3. Economists predict that inflation will **abate** in the coming quarter as supply chain disruptions are resolved.
   经济学家预测随着供应链中断问题的解决，通胀将在下一季度有所缓解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-bait" — you pull back the bait, reducing it. Abate means to reduce or pull back in intensity.
想想"a-bait"——你收回鱼饵，减少它。Abate就是减少或减轻强度。
