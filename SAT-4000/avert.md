# 🛡️ avert

🔊 /əˈvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To prevent or ward off something undesirable from happening; to turn away one's eyes or thoughts from something.

**中文：** 避免，防止；转移（目光或注意力）。指阻止不好的事情发生，或将视线、注意力从某物上移开。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- prevent （预防）
- forestall （预先阻止）
- preclude （排除，防止）
- deflect （转移，偏转）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Avert" has two distinct uses: (1) to prevent something bad, as in "avert a disaster," and (2) to turn away, as in "avert your gaze." Both senses involve the idea of directing something away from its course.

"avert"有两个不同的用法：（1）避免坏事发生，如"avert a disaster"（避免灾难）；（2）转移目光，如"avert your gaze"（移开视线）。两种含义都涉及将某物从其方向上引开的概念。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The swift intervention of the negotiators helped **avert** a potentially catastrophic international conflict.
   *谈判者的迅速干预帮助**避免**了一场可能造成灾难性后果的国际冲突。*

2. She **averted** her eyes from the gruesome scene, unable to bear the sight any longer.
   *她把目光从那可怕的场景上**移开**，无法再忍受那景象。*

3. Proper maintenance of the infrastructure could have **averted** the bridge collapse that injured dozens of people.
   *对基础设施的适当维护本可以**防止**那次导致数十人受伤的桥梁坍塌。*

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Avert" comes from Latin "avertere" — "a-" (away) + "vertere" (to turn). To avert is to TURN AWAY from danger or to turn danger away from you.

"avert"源自拉丁语"avertere"——"a-"（远离）+"vertere"（转）。avert就是从危险中转身离开，或将危险从你身边转移走。
