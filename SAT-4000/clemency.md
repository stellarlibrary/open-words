# 🕊️ clemency

🔊 /ˈklɛmənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Mercy or leniency, especially toward an offender or enemy; mildness of weather or temperament
**中文：** n. 仁慈；温和；宽厚；和蔼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mercy（仁慈）
- leniency（宽大）
- compassion（同情）
- forbearance（克制）
- mildness（温和）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grant clemency / show clemency / plead for clemency / executive clemency / an act of clemency**

👉 Example: The governor granted clemency to the prisoner after reviewing compelling evidence of rehabilitation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney appealed for **clemency**, arguing that the defendant had demonstrated genuine remorse and significant personal transformation.
   辩护律师请求宽大处理，辩称被告已表现出真诚的悔意和显著的个人转变。

2. Throughout history, acts of **clemency** by monarchs have often served strategic political purposes beyond mere humanitarian compassion.
   纵观历史，君主的宽恕行为往往超越了纯粹的人道主义同情，具有战略政治目的。

3. The international tribunal considered whether **clemency** was appropriate given the extraordinary circumstances surrounding the case under review.
   国际法庭考虑鉴于所审理案件的特殊情况，宽大处理是否合适。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clem-ency" sounding like "calm-ency" — a calm, gentle disposition that shows mercy instead of harshness.
把"clemency"想象成"calm-ency"（平静）——一种以仁慈代替严厉的平和温柔性情。
