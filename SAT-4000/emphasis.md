# 🎯 emphasis

🔊 /ˈemfəsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Special importance, value, or prominence given to something; stress laid on a word or words to indicate particular significance
**中文：** n. 强调；重点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stress（强调）
- importance（重要性）
- prominence（突出）
- focus（焦点）
- accent（重音）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **place emphasis on / with emphasis / shift the emphasis / special emphasis**

👉 Example: The curriculum places particular emphasis on developing critical thinking skills alongside traditional academic subjects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new educational policy places greater **emphasis** on cultivating creativity and problem-solving skills rather than rote memorization techniques.
   新的教育政策更加强调培养创造力和解决问题的能力，而非死记硬背的技巧。

2. The professor spoke with considerable **emphasis** when explaining the fundamental principles that underpin modern democratic governance systems.
   教授在解释支撑现代民主治理体系的基本原则时语气格外强调。

3. Recent scholarship has shifted the **emphasis** from individual achievement to the collaborative processes that drive meaningful scientific discovery.
   近期的学术研究已将重点从个人成就转移到推动有意义科学发现的协作过程上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "emphasis" as "EM-PHA-sis" — when you emphasize, you PHASE into something, shining a spotlight on what matters most.
把"emphasis"想成"EM-PHA-sis"——当你强调时，你把聚光灯打在最重要的事情上。
