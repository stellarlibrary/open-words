# 🌀 frenetic

🔊 /frəˈnɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Fast and energetic in a rather wild and uncontrolled way; frenzied or feverishly active
**中文：** 狂热的；发狂的；疯狂忙乱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frenzied（疯狂的）
- hectic（忙乱的）
- feverish（狂热的）
- manic（狂躁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frenetic pace / frenetic activity / frenetic energy / frenetic lifestyle**

👉 Example: The frenetic pace of modern urban life leaves many people feeling exhausted and overwhelmed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The trading floor was a scene of **frenetic** activity as brokers scrambled to respond to the sudden market downturn.
   交易大厅一片疯狂忙乱，经纪人争先恐后地应对突然的市场下跌。

2. The director's **frenetic** editing style, with rapid cuts and shifting angles, perfectly captured the chaos of the battle scene.
   导演狂热的剪辑风格，快速切换和变换角度，完美地捕捉了战斗场面的混乱。

3. Despite the **frenetic** preparations backstage, the orchestra's performance appeared effortlessly polished and deeply moving to the audience.
   尽管后台的准备工作疯狂忙乱，但管弦乐团的演出在观众看来毫不费力地精致且深深动人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frenetic" as related to "frenzy" — both share the root "fren-" and describe wild, uncontrolled energy and activity.
把"frenetic"想成和"frenzy"（狂乱）相关——两者共享词根"fren-"，都描述疯狂、不受控制的能量和活动。
