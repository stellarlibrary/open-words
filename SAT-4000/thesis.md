# 📄 thesis

🔊 /ˈθiːsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A long essay or dissertation involving original research, submitted for a university degree; a statement or proposition put forward as a premise to be maintained or proved.
**中文：** n. 论文；论点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dissertation（学位论文）
- proposition（命题）
- argument（论点）
- premise（前提）
- treatise（论著）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **doctoral thesis / thesis statement / defend a thesis / central thesis / thesis advisor**

👉 Example: Every persuasive essay should begin with a clear thesis statement that outlines the writer's main argument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctoral candidate spent three years conducting fieldwork in remote villages to gather evidence supporting her **thesis** on rural migration.
   这位博士候选人花了三年时间在偏远村庄进行实地调查，以收集支持她关于农村人口迁移论文的证据。

2. The central **thesis** of the book argues that technological innovation has been the primary driver of economic growth throughout history.
   该书的中心论点认为，技术创新一直是历史上经济增长的主要驱动力。

3. Committee members challenged the student to defend her **thesis** by presenting counterarguments that tested the validity of her conclusions.
   委员会成员通过提出反驳论点来考验其结论的有效性，要求这位学生为她的论文进行答辩。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "thesis" as "the sis" — your "sister" idea, the one main idea you champion and defend throughout your entire paper.
把"thesis"想成"the sis"——你的"姐妹"想法，即你在整篇论文中倡导和捍卫的那一个主要观点。
