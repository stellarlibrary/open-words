# 📐 decimal point

🔊 /ˈdesɪməl pɔɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The dot placed in a number to separate the whole-number part from the fractional part
**中文：** n. 小数点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dot（点）
- radix point（基数点）
- period（句点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **place the decimal point / move the decimal point / decimal point error / to two decimal points**

👉 Example: Always double-check the position of the decimal point when converting between units of measurement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accountant discovered that a misplaced **decimal point** had caused a significant discrepancy in the quarterly financial report.
   会计发现一个错位的小数点导致了季度财务报告中的重大差异。

2. Students must learn to position the **decimal point** correctly when performing multiplication with fractional numbers.
   学生在进行小数乘法运算时必须学会正确放置小数点。

3. The scientist moved the **decimal point** three places to the right to convert the measurement from grams to milligrams.
   科学家将小数点向右移动三位，以将测量值从克转换为毫克。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "decimal point" as the "decision point" in a number — it decides where the whole part ends and the fraction begins.
把"decimal point"想成数字中的"决策点"——它决定了整数部分在哪里结束、小数部分从哪里开始。
