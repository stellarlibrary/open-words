# 😇 beatitude

🔊 /biˈætɪˌtuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A state of supreme happiness or blessedness; (Beatitudes) the declarations of blessedness made by Jesus in the Sermon on the Mount
**中文：** n. 至福；祝福；极大的幸福

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blessedness（至福）
- bliss（极乐）
- felicity（幸福）
- rapture（狂喜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a state of beatitude / eternal beatitude / achieve beatitude / the Beatitudes**

👉 Example: The monk's face radiated a calm beatitude that seemed to transcend all earthly concerns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher described **beatitude** as the ultimate goal of human existence, attainable only through virtuous living.
   这位哲学家将至福描述为人类存在的终极目标，只有通过美德生活才能实现。

2. Her expression conveyed a serene **beatitude** that suggested she had found profound inner peace and contentment.
   她的表情传达出一种宁静的至福，表明她已经找到了深层的内心平静与满足。

3. Theologians have long debated the precise meaning of each **beatitude** proclaimed in the Sermon on the Mount.
   神学家们长期以来一直在争论登山宝训中每条祝福的确切含义。

Think of "beat-it-ude" — when you beat all of life's troubles, you reach a state of supreme happiness: beatitude.
把"beatitude"想成"beat-it-ude"——当你战胜人生所有烦恼后，你便达到了至福的境界。
