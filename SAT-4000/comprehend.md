# 💡 comprehend

🔊 /ˌkɒmprɪˈhɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To understand fully; to grasp the meaning or nature of something; to include or encompass
**中文：** v. 理解；了解；包含；领悟

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- understand（理解）
- grasp（领会）
- fathom（彻底了解）
- perceive（感知）
- apprehend（领悟）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fully comprehend / fail to comprehend / difficult to comprehend / comprehend the significance**

👉 Example: It took the students several weeks to fully comprehend the implications of the quantum mechanics theorem.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many citizens struggle to **comprehend** the complex legal language embedded within government policy documents and legislative proposals.
   许多公民难以理解政府政策文件和立法提案中嵌入的复杂法律语言。

2. The sheer magnitude of the ecological disaster was almost impossible to **comprehend** without witnessing the devastation firsthand in the region.
   如果不亲眼目睹该地区的破坏，这场生态灾难的巨大规模几乎无法理解。

3. Advanced artificial intelligence systems can process vast amounts of data, yet they cannot truly **comprehend** human emotions or subjective experiences.
   先进的人工智能系统可以处理大量数据，但它们无法真正理解人类的情感或主观体验。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comprehend" as "com-PRE-HEND" — to "grab hold of" (Latin prehendere = to seize) an idea completely with your mind.
把"comprehend"想成"完全抓住"——拉丁语词根prehendere意为"抓住"，理解就是用你的头脑完全抓住一个想法。
