# 😊 felicity

🔊 /fɪˈlɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Intense happiness; the quality of being well-suited or appropriate; a pleasing manner or style, especially in speech or writing
**中文：** n. 幸福；快乐；幸运

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bliss（极乐）
- happiness（幸福）
- joy（快乐）
- blessedness（幸运）
- aptness（恰当）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **domestic felicity / felicity of expression / find felicity / marital felicity / great felicity**

👉 Example: The couple enjoyed years of domestic felicity in their quiet countryside home.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author wrote with such **felicity** that even the most complex philosophical ideas became accessible to ordinary readers.
   这位作者的文笔如此精妙，即使是最复杂的哲学思想也变得通俗易懂。

2. After decades of hardship, she finally achieved a state of lasting **felicity** surrounded by her loving family and close friends.
   经历了数十年的艰辛后，她终于在挚爱的家人和亲密朋友的陪伴下获得了持久的幸福。

3. The diplomat's **felicity** of expression enabled him to navigate sensitive negotiations without offending any of the parties involved.
   这位外交官精妙的表达能力使他能够在敏感的谈判中游刃有余，不冒犯任何一方。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "felicity" as containing "Felix" — a name meaning "happy" in Latin. Felicity is the state of being truly happy and fortunate.
把"felicity"想成包含"Felix"——一个在拉丁语中意为"幸福的"名字。Felicity就是真正幸福和幸运的状态。
