# 🌅 crepuscular

🔊 /krɪˈpʌskjələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, resembling, or relating to twilight; dim or indistinct; active during dawn and dusk
**中文：** adj. 黄昏的；朦胧的；拂晓的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- twilight（黄昏的）
- dim（朦胧的）
- dusky（昏暗的）
- shadowy（阴暗的）
- murky（昏暗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crepuscular light / crepuscular animals / crepuscular rays / crepuscular hours / crepuscular glow**

👉 Example: Many desert animals are crepuscular, emerging only during the cooler twilight hours to hunt.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **crepuscular** rays filtering through the forest canopy created an ethereal atmosphere that captivated every visiting photographer.
   透过森林树冠过滤的黄昏光线营造出一种空灵的氛围，吸引了每一位到访的摄影师。

2. Biologists classify rabbits as **crepuscular** creatures because they are most active during the dawn and dusk transition periods.
   生物学家将兔子归类为晨昏性动物，因为它们在黎明和黄昏的过渡时段最为活跃。

3. The painting masterfully captured the **crepuscular** quality of the landscape, bathing the entire scene in soft, dusky light.
   这幅画精妙地捕捉了风景的黄昏特质，将整个场景笼罩在柔和、朦胧的光线中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "crepuscular" containing "dusk" rearranged — it describes the dim, hazy quality of light at twilight.
把"crepuscular"和"dusk（黄昏）"联系起来——它描述的是黄昏时分那种朦胧、模糊的光线特质。
