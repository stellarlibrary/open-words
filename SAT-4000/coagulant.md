# 🧪 coagulant

🔊 /koʊˈæɡjʊlənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A substance that causes a liquid, especially blood, to change to a solid or semi-solid state; an agent that promotes coagulation or clotting
**中文：** n. 促凝剂；凝血剂；凝结剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clotting agent（凝血剂）
- thickener（增稠剂）
- coagulator（凝固剂）
- flocculant（絮凝剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chemical coagulant / blood coagulant / add a coagulant / natural coagulant / coagulant agent**

👉 Example: The water treatment facility uses aluminum sulfate as the primary coagulant to remove suspended impurities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon applied a topical **coagulant** to the wound to accelerate clotting and minimize excessive blood loss.
   外科医生在伤口处涂抹了局部凝血剂以加速凝血并减少过多失血。

2. Municipal water purification systems rely on chemical **coagulants** to aggregate and remove fine suspended particles effectively.
   市政水净化系统依靠化学凝结剂来有效地聚集和去除细小的悬浮颗粒。

3. Researchers developed a novel plant-based **coagulant** that proved more environmentally sustainable than conventional synthetic alternatives.
   研究人员开发了一种新型植物基凝结剂，证明比传统的合成替代品更具环境可持续性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "coagulant" as "co-ag-ulant" — something that causes particles to "come together and aggregate," turning liquid into solid.
把"coagulant"拆成"co（一起）+ ag（聚集）+ ulant"——一种使颗粒"聚集在一起"的物质，将液体变为固体。
