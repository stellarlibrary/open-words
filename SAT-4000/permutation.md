# 🔀 permutation

🔊 /ˌpɜːrmjuˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A way of arranging or ordering a set of items; each of several possible ways in which things can be ordered or combined
**中文：** n. 排列；置换；交换；变换

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- arrangement（排列）
- combination（组合）
- variation（变化）
- transformation（变换）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **permutation of elements / possible permutations / every permutation / random permutation**

👉 Example: The team explored every permutation of the variables before arriving at the optimal solution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician calculated that there were over one million possible **permutations** of the eight-character alphanumeric password.
   数学家计算出，八位字母数字密码有超过一百万种可能的排列方式。

2. The committee examined every **permutation** of the seating arrangement to ensure diplomatic protocols were properly observed.
   委员会检查了座位安排的每一种排列方式，以确保外交礼仪得到妥善遵守。

3. Genetic **permutations** within a population contribute significantly to the species' ability to adapt to changing environments.
   种群内的基因排列变化对物种适应不断变化的环境的能力有重大贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-mut-ation" — "mut" means "change" (as in mutate). A permutation is a change in the arrangement or order of things.
把"permutation"想成"per（完全）+ mut（改变）+ ation"——完全改变排列顺序，即排列、置换。
