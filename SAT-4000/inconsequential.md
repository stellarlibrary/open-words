# 🪶 inconsequential

🔊 /ɪnˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not important or significant; trivial; lacking logical sequence
**中文：** adj. 不重要的；不合理的；不合逻辑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trivial（琐碎的）
- insignificant（无关紧要的）
- negligible（可忽略的）
- trifling（微不足道的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seem inconsequential / relatively inconsequential / inconsequential detail / inconsequential matter**

👉 Example: What appeared to be an inconsequential detail in the contract later proved to be of critical importance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee dismissed the budgetary discrepancy as **inconsequential**, choosing to focus on more pressing fiscal concerns.
   委员会认为预算差异无关紧要，选择关注更紧迫的财政问题。

2. Though the error seemed **inconsequential** at first, it ultimately led to a cascading failure in the entire system.
   尽管这个错误起初看起来微不足道，但它最终导致了整个系统的连锁故障。

3. Historians often reveal that seemingly **inconsequential** events can profoundly alter the course of political history.
   历史学家常常揭示，看似无关紧要的事件可以深刻改变政治历史的进程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-consequential" as "not of consequence" — something without consequence has no real importance or significance.
把"inconsequential"拆分为"in（不）+ consequential（重要的）"——没有后果的事情，就是不重要的。
