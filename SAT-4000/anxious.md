# 😰 anxious

🔊 /ˈæŋk.ʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Feeling or showing worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome; eagerly wishing or wanting.

**中文：** 焦虑的；担忧的；渴望的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- worried（担心的）
- apprehensive（忧虑的）
- uneasy（不安的）
- eager（渴望的）
- nervous（紧张的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **anxious about** (something), **anxious for** (something desired), **anxious to** (do something), **grow anxious**, **feel anxious**.

> She was **anxious** about the results of her medical examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members were **anxious** about the potential consequences of the proposed legislation.
   委员会成员对拟议立法的潜在后果感到焦虑。

2. Parents remain **anxious** for the safe return of their children from the overseas program.
   父母们一直渴望孩子们能从海外项目安全归来。

3. The investors grew increasingly **anxious** as the market continued its unpredictable fluctuations.
   随着市场持续不可预测地波动，投资者们变得越来越焦虑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "anxious" as containing "anx-" which sounds like "angst" (a German word for fear/anxiety). When you feel **angst**, you are **anxious**.

联想记忆：anxious 中的 "anx-" 听起来像德语的 "angst"（恐惧/焦虑）。当你感到 angst 时，你就是 anxious（焦虑的）。
