# 📜 extant

🔊 /ˈɛkstənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Still in existence; surviving; not destroyed, lost, or extinct
**中文：** adj. 现存的；尚存的；显著的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surviving（幸存的）
- existing（现存的）
- remaining（剩余的）
- undestroyed（未毁灭的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extant manuscripts / extant species / the oldest extant / still extant**

👉 Example: The museum houses the only extant copy of the medieval manuscript in the entire world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **extant** records from the ancient civilization provide invaluable insight into their social and political structures.
   这一古代文明现存的记录为了解其社会和政治结构提供了宝贵的资料。

2. Scientists estimate that fewer than five thousand **extant** species of mammals inhabit the planet at the present time.
   科学家估计目前地球上现存的哺乳动物物种不到五千种。

3. The cathedral remains the oldest **extant** example of Gothic architecture in the region, dating back to the twelfth century.
   这座大教堂仍是该地区现存最古老的哥特式建筑典范，可追溯至十二世纪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extant" as "ex-stand" — something that still stands, that has not fallen or disappeared over time.
把"extant"想成"ex(仍然)+stant(站立)"——仍然站立着的，即现存的、尚未消失的。
