# 👥 populace

🔊 /ˈpɑːpjələs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The people living in a particular area or country; the general public; the common people
**中文：** n. 大众；平民；民众；人口

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- public（公众）
- citizenry（全体公民）
- masses（大众）
- inhabitants（居民）
- commoners（平民）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the general populace / the local populace / the educated populace / inform the populace**

👉 Example: The government launched a campaign to inform the populace about the new public health regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ruling party struggled to maintain the trust of the **populace** during the prolonged economic recession.
   在持续的经济衰退期间，执政党努力维持民众的信任。

2. Public libraries serve as essential institutions for educating the **populace** and promoting equal access to knowledge.
   公共图书馆是教育大众和促进知识平等获取的重要机构。

3. The **populace** demanded greater transparency from elected officials following the corruption scandal in the capital.
   在首都腐败丑闻曝光后，民众要求民选官员提高透明度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "popul-ace" as "people" with an "-ace" ending — the populace is simply the people of a place, the general population.
把"populace"想成"people(人们)"加上"-ace"后缀——populace就是一个地方的人们，即普通大众。
