# 🏛️ administrator

🔊 /ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person responsible for managing, directing, or overseeing the operations of an organization or system.
**中文：** 管理人；行政官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- manager（管理者）
- director（主管）
- executive（行政人员）
- superintendent（监督人）
- overseer（监管者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **school/hospital/system administrator**, **serve as administrator**, **chief administrator**

👉 Example: The administrator implemented new policies to improve organizational efficiency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university **administrator** introduced a comprehensive plan to enhance student support services.
   大学管理者推出了一项全面计划以加强学生支持服务。

2. As the estate **administrator**, she was responsible for distributing assets according to the will.
   作为遗产管理人，她负责按照遗嘱分配资产。

3. The network **administrator** detected a security breach and immediately restricted unauthorized access.
   网络管理员发现了安全漏洞并立即限制了未经授权的访问。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "admin" + "istrator" — the one who administers or manages things, like a minister who serves.
联想"admin"（管理）+"istrator"（执行者）——管理事务的人，就像"minister"（部长）一样为机构服务。
