# ⚖️ litigant

🔊 /ˈlɪtɪɡənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person involved in a lawsuit. (adj.) Relating to or engaged in litigation
**中文：** n. 诉讼当事人 adj. 诉讼的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plaintiff（原告）
- defendant（被告）
- claimant（索赔人）
- petitioner（申请人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a litigant in person / the litigant's rights / both litigants / civil litigant**

👉 Example: The litigant presented compelling evidence before the court to support her claim.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **litigant** filed a motion requesting that the judge recuse himself due to a potential conflict of interest.
   该诉讼当事人提交了一份动议，要求法官因潜在的利益冲突而回避。

2. As a self-represented **litigant**, she navigated the complex legal proceedings with remarkable composure and determination.
   作为一名自我代理的诉讼当事人，她以非凡的镇定和决心应对了复杂的法律程序。

3. The court ordered both **litigants** to attend mandatory mediation before the trial could proceed any further.
   法院命令双方诉讼当事人在审判进一步进行之前参加强制调解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "litig-ant" as "a person (ant) who litigates" — someone actively involved in a legal dispute.
把"litigant"想成"litig（诉讼）+ ant（人）"——一个参与法律纠纷的人。
