# 🕳️ lapse

🔊 /læps/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A temporary failure of concentration, memory, or judgment; a passage of time. (v.) To become invalid or expire; to fall into a particular state
**中文：** n. 失误，疏忽；流逝，间隔 v. 终止，失效；陷入...状态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- slip（疏忽）
- oversight（失察）
- expiration（失效）
- intermission（间隔）
- blunder（失误）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a lapse in judgment / a lapse of memory / lapse into silence / let something lapse**

👉 Example: After a brief lapse in concentration, the student realized she had missed an entire paragraph.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The security breach was attributed to a temporary **lapse** in the monitoring team's standard operating procedures.
   这次安全漏洞被归因于监控团队在标准操作流程上的一次暂时疏忽。

2. If the policyholder fails to pay the premium on time, the insurance coverage will **lapse** automatically.
   如果投保人未能按时缴纳保费，保险保障将自动失效。

3. After months of disciplined training, the athlete experienced an unfortunate **lapse** in focus during the final round.
   经过数月的严格训练，这位运动员在最后一轮中经历了一次不幸的注意力失误。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lapse" as "l-apse" — like "collapse" without the "col." When something collapses, it falls apart or fails, just like a lapse in judgment.
把"lapse"想成"collapse（崩塌）"去掉"col"——当事物崩塌时就会出错失效，就像判断上的失误一样。
