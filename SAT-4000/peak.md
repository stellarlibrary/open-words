# ⛰️ peak

🔊 /piːk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The pointed top of a mountain; the highest point or maximum level. (v.) To reach the highest point. (adj.) At the maximum level
**中文：** n. 山峰；最高点；顶点；帽舌 v. 使达到最高点；使竖起 adj. 最高的；最大值的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- summit（顶峰）
- apex（最高点）
- pinnacle（巅峰）
- zenith（顶点）
- acme（极点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach a peak / peak performance / peak season / at the peak of**

👉 Example: The athlete's career reached its peak when she won three consecutive gold medals at the Olympics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Global oil production is expected to **peak** within the next decade before gradually declining due to renewable energy adoption.
   全球石油产量预计将在未来十年内达到峰值，然后因可再生能源的普及而逐渐下降。

2. The mountaineers endured harsh conditions for several days before finally reaching the **peak** of the formidable summit.
   登山者们忍受了数天恶劣的环境，终于到达了这座险峻山峰的顶点。

3. Consumer spending typically reaches its **peak** during the holiday season, driving significant revenue growth for retail businesses.
   消费者支出通常在假日季节达到最高点，推动零售企业收入的显著增长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peak" as the sharp pointed tip of a mountain — the highest "P-point" you can reach before coming back down.
把"peak"想成山的尖顶——你能到达的最高"P点"，之后就只能往下走了。
