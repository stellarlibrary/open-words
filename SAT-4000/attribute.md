# 🏅 attribute

🔊 /ˈætrɪbjuːt (n.), əˈtrɪbjuːt (v.)/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A quality or characteristic inherent in or ascribed to someone or something. (v.) To regard something as being caused by or belonging to
**中文：** n. 属性；特质 v. 归属；把…归于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quality（品质）
- characteristic（特征）
- trait（特性）
- ascribe（归因于）
- property（属性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **attribute something to / key attribute / personal attribute / attribute success to**

👉 Example: She attributed her academic success to years of disciplined study and supportive mentors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians **attribute** the fall of the empire to a combination of internal corruption and relentless external invasions.
   历史学家将帝国的衰落归因于内部腐败和持续不断的外部入侵的共同作用。

2. Patience and resilience are essential **attributes** for anyone pursuing a career in scientific research and development.
   耐心和韧性是任何从事科学研究与开发事业的人所必备的特质。

3. The painting was initially **attributed** to a minor artist but later confirmed as a genuine masterwork by Vermeer.
   这幅画最初被归于一位不知名的画家，但后来被确认为维米尔的真迹。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "attribute" as "at-TRIBUTE" — you pay tribute to a cause by attributing credit to it; it also carries qualities worthy of tribute.
把"attribute"想成"at-TRIBUTE"——你向某个原因"致敬"（tribute），将功劳归于它；它也承载着值得致敬的品质。
