# ✨ quintessence

🔊 /kwɪnˈtɛsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The purest or most perfect embodiment of something; the most typical example; the essential essence of a thing
**中文：** n. 精华；典范；第五元素

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- essence（本质）
- epitome（典范）
- embodiment（化身）
- paragon（模范）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the quintessence of / quintessence of elegance / quintessence of style / embody the quintessence**

👉 Example: The ancient temple is widely regarded as the quintessence of classical architectural harmony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics hailed the novel as the **quintessence** of postmodern literature, combining irony and self-reference with remarkable sophistication.
   评论家们称赞这部小说是后现代文学的精华，以非凡的精妙将反讽和自我指涉相结合。

2. The ambassador's graceful diplomacy represented the **quintessence** of measured restraint during an extremely volatile international crisis.
   大使优雅的外交手段代表了在极度动荡的国际危机中克制有度的典范。

3. Her research distilled the **quintessence** of decades of scientific inquiry into a single groundbreaking and accessible publication.
   她的研究将数十年科学探索的精华提炼为一部开创性且通俗易懂的著作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quint-essence" as "fifth essence" — ancient philosophers believed the fifth element was the purest substance beyond earth, water, air, and fire.
把"quintessence"想成"第五本质"——古代哲学家认为第五元素是超越土、水、气、火的最纯净物质。
