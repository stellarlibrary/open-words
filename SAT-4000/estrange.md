# 💔 estrange

🔊 /ɪˈstreɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause someone to become unfriendly or distant; to alienate or drive apart
**中文：** v. 使疏远；离间

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alienate（疏远）
- distance（使疏远）
- disaffect（使不满）
- separate（分离）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **estrange from / become estranged / estranged spouse / estranged relationship**

👉 Example: His constant dishonesty gradually estranged him from his closest friends and family members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bitter inheritance dispute **estranged** the two brothers, who had once been inseparable throughout their entire childhood.
   那场激烈的遗产纠纷使两兄弟疏远了，他们曾在整个童年时代形影不离。

2. Radical policy changes can **estrange** loyal supporters and ultimately undermine the foundation of any political movement.
   激进的政策变化可能会疏远忠实的支持者，最终动摇任何政治运动的基础。

3. The author became **estranged** from the literary establishment after publishing a controversial memoir that challenged prevailing norms.
   这位作者在出版了一本挑战现行规范的争议性回忆录后，与文学界疏远了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "estrange" as "e-STRANGE" — when people become strange to each other, they grow apart and become estranged.
把"estrange"想成"e-STRANGE"——当人们彼此变得陌生(strange)时，他们就疏远了。
