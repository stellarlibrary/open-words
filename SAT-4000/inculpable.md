# 🕊️ inculpable

🔊 /ɪnˈkʌlpəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Free from guilt or blame; not deserving of censure; blameless and beyond reproach
**中文：** adj. 无辜的；无罪的；无可非议的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blameless（无可指责的）
- innocent（无辜的）
- irreproachable（无可非议的）
- guiltless（无罪的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inculpable conduct / inculpable behavior / deemed inculpable / entirely inculpable**

👉 Example: The investigation concluded that the officer's conduct was entirely inculpable under the circumstances.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After a thorough review of the evidence, the committee determined that the researcher's methodology was entirely **inculpable**.
   在彻底审查证据后，委员会认定该研究人员的方法论完全没有过错。

2. The defendant's legal team argued that his actions were **inculpable** because he had acted under duress and without malicious intent.
   被告的法律团队辩称他的行为是无罪的，因为他是在胁迫下行事且没有恶意。

3. Her **inculpable** reputation in the academic community made the plagiarism accusations seem entirely unfounded and implausible.
   她在学术界无可非议的声誉使得剽窃指控显得完全没有根据且不可信。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-culp-able" — "in" means "not" and "culp" comes from Latin "culpa" (fault), so inculpable literally means "not at fault."
把"inculpable"拆开——"in"表示"不"，"culp"来自拉丁语"culpa"（过错），所以字面意思就是"没有过错的"。
