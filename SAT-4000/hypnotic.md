# 🌀 hypnotic

🔊 /hɪpˈnɒtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or producing hypnosis; having a compelling or mesmerizing quality. (n.) A drug or agent that induces sleep
**中文：** adj. 催眠的；令人着迷的 n. 安眠药；催眠药

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mesmerizing（令人着迷的）
- soporific（催眠的）
- spellbinding（迷人的）
- sedative（镇静的）
- tranquilizing（使镇静的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hypnotic effect / hypnotic state / hypnotic trance / hypnotic rhythm / hypnotic drug**

👉 Example: The musician's hypnotic melody held the entire audience in a state of silent, captivated wonder.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The therapist used a calm, **hypnotic** voice to guide the patient into a deeply relaxed state of heightened suggestibility.
   治疗师用平静的催眠般的声音引导患者进入高度放松和易受暗示的状态。

2. The physician prescribed a mild **hypnotic** to help the patient overcome persistent insomnia that had lasted several months.
   医生开了一种温和的安眠药，帮助患者克服已持续数月的顽固性失眠。

3. The documentary featured **hypnotic** aerial footage of the northern lights dancing across the vast Arctic sky in vivid colors.
   这部纪录片展示了令人着迷的北极光在广阔的北极天空中以鲜艳色彩舞动的航拍画面。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hypnotic" from "Hypnos," the Greek god of sleep — anything hypnotic puts you into a sleep-like trance.
把"hypnotic"联想到希腊睡神"Hypnos"——任何催眠的东西都会让你进入类似睡眠的恍惚状态。
