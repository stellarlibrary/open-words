# 🎯 volitive

🔊 /ˈvɑːlɪtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or arising from the will; expressing wish, permission, or intention
**中文：** adj. 意志的；表示祈望或准许的；发自意志的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- volitional（意志的）
- willful（有意的）
- deliberate（故意的）
- intentional（有意图的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **volitive act / volitive expression / volitive mood / volitive faculty**

👉 Example: In linguistics, the volitive mood expresses the speaker's desire or wish for something to happen.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The psychologist examined whether the patient's behavior was **volitive** or merely a reflexive response to stimuli.
   心理学家检查了患者的行为是出于意志的还是仅仅是对刺激的反射性反应。

2. Certain languages employ a **volitive** grammatical mood to express wishes, hopes, and deliberate intentions.
   某些语言使用祈愿语法语气来表达愿望、希望和有意的意图。

3. Philosophers distinguish between **volitive** actions, which arise from conscious intent, and purely instinctive behaviors.
   哲学家区分了源于有意识意图的意志行为和纯粹本能的行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "volitive" as "volition" + "-ive" — if volition is the will, then volitive describes anything related to or produced by the will.
把"volitive"想成"volition"（意志）+"-ive"——如果volition是意志，那么volitive就是描述与意志相关的或由意志产生的。
