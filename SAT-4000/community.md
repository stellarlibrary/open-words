# 🏘️ community

🔊 /kəˈmjuːnəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A group of people living in the same area or sharing common interests, beliefs, or characteristics; the condition of sharing or having certain attitudes and interests in common
**中文：** n. 社区；群落；共同体；团体，社会

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- society（社会）
- neighborhood（邻里）
- collective（集体）
- populace（民众）
- fellowship（团体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **local community / community service / sense of community / the international community**

👉 Example: Building a strong sense of community requires active participation and mutual respect among all residents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The local **community** organized a volunteer program to provide meals and essential supplies to families affected by the recent floods.
   当地社区组织了一个志愿者项目，为受近期洪水影响的家庭提供餐食和基本物资。

2. The scientific **community** expressed widespread concern regarding the potential ethical implications of unregulated artificial intelligence development.
   科学界对不受监管的人工智能开发可能带来的伦理影响表示了广泛关注。

3. A thriving **community** depends upon the collective efforts of its members to uphold shared values and support one another consistently.
   一个繁荣的社区依赖于其成员的共同努力，以维护共同价值观并持续相互支持。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "community" as "common-unity" — a group united by something in common, sharing the same space, interests, or identity.
把"community"想成"common-unity"——因共同之处而团结在一起的群体，共享同一空间、兴趣或身份。
