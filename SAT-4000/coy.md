# 🙈 coy

🔊 /kɔɪ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Shy and reluctant to make a commitment or give information; pretending to be modest or bashful
**中文：** adj. 腼腆的；忸怩作态的；怕羞的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- demure（端庄的）
- bashful（害羞的）
- reticent（沉默的）
- coquettish（卖弄风情的）
- evasive（回避的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coy about something / coy smile / play coy / coy response**

👉 Example: The politician remained coy about her plans for the upcoming presidential campaign.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The actress gave a **coy** smile when reporters pressed her for details about the rumored collaboration.
   当记者追问有关传闻中合作的细节时，这位女演员露出了腼腆的微笑。

2. Despite repeated inquiries, the company remained deliberately **coy** about the timeline for its revolutionary product launch.
   尽管反复被询问，该公司对其革命性产品发布的时间表仍然刻意含糊其辞。

3. Her **coy** reluctance to discuss the award only intensified public curiosity about her remarkable achievements.
   她对讨论该奖项的忸怩不愿只是加剧了公众对她卓越成就的好奇心。

---

把"coy"想成"koi（锦鲤）"——一条害羞地藏在睡莲叶下、胆怯地探头张望的锦鲤。
