# 🔄 resumption

🔊 /rɪˈzʌmpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of beginning something again after a pause or interruption; the taking back of something that was given or lost
**中文：** n. 恢复；重新开始；取回

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- continuation（继续）
- renewal（重新开始）
- recommencement（重新开始）
- restart（重启）
- revival（恢复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resumption of / resumption of talks / resumption of hostilities / resumption of services / upon resumption**

👉 Example: The resumption of peace negotiations brought renewed hope to the war-torn region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **resumption** of diplomatic relations between the two nations followed years of careful and deliberate behind-the-scenes negotiation.
   两国外交关系的恢复经历了多年精心而审慎的幕后谈判。

2. Students eagerly anticipated the **resumption** of classes after the prolonged disruption caused by the unprecedented public health crisis.
   学生们热切期盼在前所未有的公共卫生危机造成的长期中断后恢复上课。

3. The **resumption** of international trade agreements was widely regarded as essential for stabilizing the faltering global economic recovery.
   国际贸易协定的重新启动被普遍认为对稳定步履蹒跚的全球经济复苏至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-SUMP-tion" — "resume" plus "-tion" — resumption is simply the noun form of "resume," meaning to start again after a pause.
把"resumption"想成"resume（恢复）+ tion（名词后缀）"——resumption就是"resume"的名词形式，意为暂停后重新开始。
