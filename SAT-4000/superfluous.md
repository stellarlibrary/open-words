# 🗑️ superfluous

🔊 /suːˈpɜːrfluəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unnecessary; exceeding what is sufficient or required; more than enough
**中文：** adj. 多余的，过剩的，过量的；不必要的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- redundant（多余的）
- excessive（过多的）
- surplus（剩余的）
- unnecessary（不必要的）
- extraneous（无关的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **superfluous detail / superfluous information / superfluous words / render something superfluous**

👉 Example: The editor removed all superfluous words to make the article more concise and impactful.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee determined that the additional regulations were entirely **superfluous** given the existing legal framework already in place.
   委员会认定，鉴于现有的法律框架，额外的规定完全是多余的。

2. Advances in automation have rendered many previously essential administrative positions increasingly **superfluous** in modern organizations.
   自动化的进步使许多以前必不可少的行政职位在现代组织中变得越来越多余。

3. The professor advised students to eliminate **superfluous** details from their essays and focus exclusively on the central argument.
   教授建议学生删除论文中不必要的细节，专注于核心论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "superfluous" as "super-flow-us" — when something super flows over us, there is too much of it; it is more than needed.
把"superfluous"想成"super-flow-us"——当某物大量涌向我们时，说明太多了，超出了需要。
