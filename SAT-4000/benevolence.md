# 💛 benevolence

🔊 /bəˈnɛvələns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An inclination to do good; an act of kindness or generosity; charitable goodwill toward others
**中文：** n. 仁慈；善行；仁爱心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- kindness（善良）
- generosity（慷慨）
- compassion（同情）
- altruism（利他主义）
- goodwill（善意）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show benevolence / act of benevolence / benevolence toward / with great benevolence**

👉 Example: The philanthropist's benevolence toward the community earned her widespread admiration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The wealthy patron's **benevolence** enabled the construction of a new hospital wing for underprivileged patients.
   这位富有赞助人的善行使得为贫困患者建造新的医院侧翼成为可能。

2. Throughout history, acts of **benevolence** have often shaped the moral fabric of civilized societies.
   纵观历史，善行往往塑造了文明社会的道德结构。

3. The foundation was established as a lasting monument to her extraordinary **benevolence** and humanitarian spirit.
   该基金会的建立是对她非凡仁慈和人道主义精神的永久纪念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bene-volence" — "bene" means "good" in Latin, and "vol" relates to "will," so benevolence is literally "good will" toward others.
把"benevolence"拆分为"bene（好）+ volence（意愿）"——拉丁语中意为"善意"，即对他人的美好心愿。
