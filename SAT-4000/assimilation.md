# 🔄 assimilation

🔊 /əˌsɪmɪˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of absorbing and integrating new information, ideas, nutrients, or people into a larger group or system
**中文：** 同化；吸收

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absorption（吸收）
- integration（融合）
- incorporation（合并）
- acculturation（文化适应）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cultural assimilation / assimilation of knowledge / assimilation process / assimilation into society**

👉 Example: The assimilation of diverse immigrant communities has shaped the nation's cultural identity over centuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **assimilation** of advanced technologies into traditional manufacturing processes has dramatically increased overall productivity.
   先进技术融入传统制造工艺的过程极大地提高了整体生产力。

2. Scholars have long debated whether cultural **assimilation** strengthens or diminishes the unique heritage of minority communities.
   学者们长期争论文化同化是加强还是削弱了少数族裔社区的独特遗产。

3. Effective **assimilation** of new employees requires structured orientation programs and ongoing mentorship from senior colleagues.
   新员工的有效融入需要结构化的入职培训项目以及资深同事的持续指导。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assimilation" as "a-SIMILAR-ation" — the process of making things similar, blending them into one unified whole.
把"assimilation"想成"变得相似的过程"——将不同的事物融为一体，使之趋于统一。
