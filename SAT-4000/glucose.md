# 🧪 glucose

🔊 /ˈɡluːkoʊs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A simple sugar that is the primary source of energy for the body's cells; found naturally in fruits and honey
**中文：** n. 葡萄糖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dextrose（右旋糖）
- blood sugar（血糖）
- grape sugar（葡萄糖）
- simple sugar（单糖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blood glucose / glucose level / glucose metabolism / glucose tolerance test**

👉 Example: Patients with diabetes must carefully monitor their blood glucose levels throughout the day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The body converts complex carbohydrates into **glucose**, which serves as the fundamental fuel for cellular respiration and metabolism.
   人体将复杂碳水化合物转化为葡萄糖，作为细胞呼吸和新陈代谢的基本燃料。

2. Elevated **glucose** concentrations in the bloodstream over prolonged periods can lead to serious cardiovascular and neurological complications.
   血液中葡萄糖浓度长期偏高可导致严重的心血管和神经系统并发症。

3. Researchers developed a non-invasive sensor capable of measuring **glucose** levels through the skin without requiring a blood sample.
   研究人员开发了一种无创传感器，能够通过皮肤测量葡萄糖水平而无需抽血。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glucose" as "glue + close" — glucose is the energy that keeps your body's cells glued closely together and functioning.
把"glucose"想成"glue（胶水）+ close（紧密）"——葡萄糖是让身体细胞紧密粘合并正常运作的能量。
