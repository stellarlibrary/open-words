# 🌿 botanical

🔊 /bəˈtænɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the scientific study of plants; derived from plants. (n.) A substance obtained from a plant and used in medicine or cosmetics
**中文：** adj. 植物学的 n. 植物性药材

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plant-based（植物性的）
- herbal（草本的）
- vegetative（植物的）
- floral（花卉的）
- phytological（植物学的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **botanical garden / botanical research / botanical illustration / botanical extract**

👉 Example: The university's botanical garden houses over three thousand species of plants from every continent on Earth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **botanical** expedition to the Amazon rainforest yielded several previously unclassified species of orchids and ferns.
   前往亚马逊雨林的植物学考察发现了几种此前未被分类的兰花和蕨类植物。

2. Researchers published a comprehensive **botanical** survey documenting the diverse flora found within the national park's protected boundaries.
   研究人员发表了一项全面的植物学调查，记录了国家公园保护区内发现的多样植物群。

3. Many traditional medicines are derived from **botanicals** that indigenous communities have cultivated and utilized for countless generations.
   许多传统药物源自土著社区世代培育和使用的植物性药材。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "botanical" as "botany + ical" — anything related to botany, the science of plants, is botanical in nature.
把"botanical"想成"botany + ical"——任何与植物学（botany）相关的事物在本质上都是botanical的。
