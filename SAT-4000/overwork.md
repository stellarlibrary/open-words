# 😩 overwork

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈwɜːrk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To work or cause someone to work too hard or too long. (n.) Excessive work; labor beyond one's capacity
**中文：** v. (使)过度工作，(使)过分劳累 n. 过于繁重的工作；过分劳累

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overexert（过度用力）
- exhaust（使精疲力竭）
- overtax（使过度劳累）
- strain（使紧张）
- burnout（精疲力竭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overwork employees / overwork oneself / die from overwork / suffer from overwork**

👉 Example: The company was criticized for consistently overworking its junior staff during peak production periods.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medical professionals warn that chronic **overwork** can lead to severe health consequences, including heart disease and clinical depression.
   医学专家警告，长期过度工作可能导致严重的健康后果，包括心脏病和临床抑郁症。

2. The factory management was found guilty of **overworking** its laborers, requiring fourteen-hour shifts with no adequate rest periods.
   工厂管理层被判犯有过度使用劳动力的罪行，要求工人连续工作十四小时且没有充足的休息时间。

3. Many ambitious young professionals tend to **overwork** themselves in pursuit of rapid career advancement and financial success.
   许多雄心勃勃的年轻专业人士往往为了快速晋升和经济成功而过度劳累自己。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-work" as working "over" and beyond your limits — you have gone past a healthy amount of work.
把"overwork"想成"over（超过）+work（工作）"——工作超过了你的极限，已经超出了健康的工作量。
