# 🏛️ pedestal

🔊 /ˈpɛdɪstl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A base or support on which a statue or column is mounted; a position of great admiration. (v.) To place on a pedestal; to support
**中文：** n. 基座；台座；柱脚；崇高的地位 v. 搁在台上；支持

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- base（底座）
- platform（平台）
- foundation（基础）
- stand（支架）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **put someone on a pedestal / knock off a pedestal / pedestal of a statue**

👉 Example: Society tends to put celebrities on a pedestal, ignoring their ordinary human flaws.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The marble **pedestal** supporting the ancient sculpture had begun to crack after centuries of exposure to the elements.
   支撑这座古代雕塑的大理石基座在经历了几个世纪的风吹日晒后开始出现裂缝。

2. Historians caution against placing political leaders on a **pedestal**, as doing so obscures their complex and flawed legacies.
   历史学家告诫人们不要将政治领袖供上神坛，因为这样做会掩盖他们复杂而有缺陷的遗产。

3. The new museum design features each artifact mounted on an illuminated **pedestal** to enhance visibility and aesthetic presentation.
   新博物馆的设计将每件文物安放在带灯光的展台上，以增强可见度和美学呈现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ped-estal" as "ped (foot) +estal (stand)" — a pedestal is literally a foot-stand, a base that something stands on.
把"pedestal"想成"ped（脚）+estal（站立）"——基座就是让事物站立的"脚"。
