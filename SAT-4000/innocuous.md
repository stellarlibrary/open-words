# 🕊️ innocuous

🔊 /ɪˈnɑːkjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not harmful or offensive; unlikely to cause any damage or provoke strong reaction
**中文：** 无害的；无伤大雅的；无毒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmless（无害的）
- benign（良性的）
- inoffensive（不冒犯的）
- unobjectionable（无可非议的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **innocuous remark / innocuous substance / seemingly innocuous / perfectly innocuous**

👉 Example: What appeared to be an innocuous comment quickly escalated into a heated debate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher confirmed that the chemical compound was entirely **innocuous** and posed no threat to human health.
   研究人员确认该化合物完全无害，不会对人体健康构成威胁。

2. His seemingly **innocuous** question about company finances revealed deeper concerns among the board members.
   他那个看似无伤大雅的关于公司财务的问题，暴露了董事会成员更深层的担忧。

3. The committee dismissed the proposal as **innocuous**, failing to recognize its potential long-term implications.
   委员会认为该提案无关紧要而予以驳回，未能认识到其潜在的长期影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-noc-uous" — "noc" sounds like "knock." Something innocuous won't even knock you down; it's completely harmless.
把"innocuous"想成"in-noc-uous"——"noc"听起来像"knock（敲击）"。无害的东西连你都敲不倒，完全没有伤害。
