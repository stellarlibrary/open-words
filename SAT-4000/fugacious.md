# 🌬️ fugacious

🔊 /fjuːˈɡeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lasting only a short time; fleeting; tending to disappear or escape
**中文：** adj. 无常的；短暂的；易逃逸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ephemeral（短暂的）
- transient（转瞬即逝的）
- fleeting（稍纵即逝的）
- evanescent（消散的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fugacious beauty / fugacious moments / fugacious nature / fugacious bloom**

👉 Example: The fugacious blossoms of the cherry tree reminded her that nothing beautiful lasts forever.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher noted that the **fugacious** compounds evaporated before accurate measurements could be recorded in the laboratory.
   研究人员注意到，这些易逃逸的化合物在实验室中尚未完成精确测量便已蒸发。

2. Critics described the artist's fame as **fugacious**, predicting that public interest would fade within a single season.
   评论家形容这位艺术家的名声是短暂的，预测公众的兴趣将在一个季度内消退。

3. The **fugacious** nature of youth compels philosophers to urge the deliberate pursuit of wisdom and lasting virtue.
   青春的无常本质促使哲学家们敦促人们刻意追求智慧和持久的美德。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fugacious" as "fugitive + gracious" — something gracious that flees like a fugitive, vanishing quickly.
把"fugacious"想成"fugitive（逃犯）+ gracious（优雅的）"——某种优雅的事物像逃犯一样迅速消失。
