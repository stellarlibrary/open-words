# 🌾 abundance

🔊 /əˈbʌndəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A very large quantity of something; the state of having more than enough; plentifulness
**中文：** n. 丰富；充裕；大量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plenty（充足）
- profusion（丰富）
- wealth（大量）
- surplus（过剩）
- copious amount（大量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an abundance of resources / in abundance / natural abundance / abundance of evidence / abundance of caution**

👉 Example: The tropical region boasts an abundance of wildlife that attracts researchers from around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeological site yielded an **abundance** of artifacts dating back to the early Bronze Age civilization.
   该考古遗址出土了大量可追溯到早期青铜时代文明的文物。

2. Clean water exists in **abundance** in some regions while remaining desperately scarce in many others.
   清洁水在某些地区非常充裕，而在许多其他地区却极度匮乏。

3. The report cited an **abundance** of scientific evidence supporting the need for immediate climate policy reform.
   该报告引用了大量科学证据，支持立即进行气候政策改革的必要性。

---

把"abundance"想成"a-BUN-dance"——想象有太多面包(bun)，多到它们开始跳舞(dance)，到处溢出来。
