# 📚 volume

🔊 /ˈvɑːljuːm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The amount of space occupied by a substance or object; the quantity or magnitude of something; a book forming part of a set or series; the degree of loudness of sound
**中文：** n. 体积；量；卷，册；音量；大量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- quantity（数量）
- capacity（容量）
- magnitude（规模）
- bulk（大量）
- tome（大部头书）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **volume of trade / volume of traffic / turn up the volume / speak volumes**

👉 Example: The sheer volume of applications received this year speaks volumes about the program's reputation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **volume** of international trade between the two nations has increased significantly over the past decade.
   两国之间的国际贸易量在过去十年中大幅增长。

2. Researchers measured the **volume** of liquid displaced by the irregular object to calculate its precise density.
   研究人员测量了不规则物体排出的液体体积，以计算其精确密度。

3. The first **volume** of the encyclopedia covers topics ranging from ancient civilizations to modern political theory.
   百科全书的第一卷涵盖了从古代文明到现代政治理论的各类主题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "volume" as related to "voluminous" — a large volume takes up a lot of space, like a voluminous book with many pages.
把"volume"联想到"voluminous"——大的体积占据很多空间，就像一本篇幅浩大的书有很多页。
