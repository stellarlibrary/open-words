# 📤 output

🔊 /ˈaʊtpʊt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The amount of something produced; the result of a process. (v.) To produce or supply data, information, or results
**中文：** n. 输出，输出量；产量；出产 v. 输出（信息）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- production（产量）
- yield（产出）
- result（结果）
- product（产品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **output data / output level / industrial output / total output**

👉 Example: The factory's annual output exceeded expectations due to improved manufacturing processes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation's industrial **output** increased by twelve percent following the implementation of new economic reforms.
   在实施新经济改革后，该国的工业产量增长了百分之十二。

2. The software program can **output** detailed statistical reports based on the raw data entered by researchers.
   该软件程序可以根据研究人员输入的原始数据输出详细的统计报告。

3. Agricultural **output** has declined significantly in regions affected by prolonged drought and soil degradation.
   在受长期干旱和土壤退化影响的地区，农业产量大幅下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out-put" as "putting something out" — what you put out into the world is your output, your production.
把"output"想成"put out"——你向外界"放出"的东西就是你的产出、输出。
