# 🔨 apprentice

🔊 /əˈprɛntɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词，动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A person who is learning a trade or skill from a skilled employer; a beginner. (v.) To place or employ someone as an apprentice.

**中文：** (n.) 学徒；生手；初学者。(v.) 使……当学徒。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- trainee（受训者）
- novice（新手）
- learner（学习者）
- beginner（初学者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** apprentice to, apprentice oneself to, serve as an apprentice, apprentice program, apprentice craftsman

**Example:** He was apprenticed to a master carpenter at the age of fourteen.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young **apprentice** devoted three years to mastering the traditional techniques of porcelain craftsmanship.
   （这位年轻的学徒花了三年时间来掌握传统的瓷器工艺技术。）

2. She was **apprenticed** to a renowned architect and gained invaluable experience designing public structures.
   （她被送去跟一位著名建筑师当学徒，获得了设计公共建筑的宝贵经验。）

3. Every skilled artisan was once an **apprentice** who patiently learned the fundamentals of the craft.
   （每位技艺精湛的工匠都曾是一名耐心学习基本功的学徒。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apprentice" comes from Old French "aprentis" (one who learns). Think: "a person who is APPROACHING expertise" — not there yet, still learning.

"Apprentice" 源自古法语 "aprentis"（学习者）。联想：一个正在"接近"（approach）专业水平的人——还没到，仍在学习。
