# 📚 vocabulary

🔊 /voʊˈkæbjəˌlɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The body of words known to or used by a particular person, language, or branch of knowledge; a list or collection of words
**中文：** n. 词汇，词汇量；词汇表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lexicon（词典）
- terminology（术语）
- diction（措辞）
- glossary（词汇表）
- vernacular（方言词汇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **expand one's vocabulary / limited vocabulary / technical vocabulary / vocabulary list / vocabulary building**

👉 Example: Reading extensively is one of the most effective methods for expanding your vocabulary.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students preparing for standardized examinations must develop a robust academic **vocabulary** to comprehend passages across diverse disciplines.
   准备标准化考试的学生必须积累扎实的学术词汇，以理解不同学科的文章。

2. The researcher noted that the indigenous community possessed a remarkably rich **vocabulary** for describing subtle variations in weather patterns.
   研究人员注意到，该土著社区拥有极为丰富的词汇来描述天气模式的细微变化。

3. Mastering the specialized **vocabulary** of a professional field is an essential prerequisite for effective scholarly communication and publication.
   掌握专业领域的专门词汇是进行有效学术交流和发表论文的基本前提。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vocabulary" as "vocal + bury" — all the words you can vocalize are buried in your mind, forming your vocabulary treasury.
把"vocabulary"想成"vocal（发声的）+ bury（埋藏）"——你能说出的所有单词都埋藏在脑海中，构成你的词汇宝库。
