# 📢 assertion

🔊 /əˈsɜːrʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A confident and forceful statement of fact or belief; the act of asserting or maintaining something.
**中文：** n. 断言，声明；主张

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- declaration（声明）
- claim（主张）
- statement（陈述）
- affirmation（肯定）
- contention（论点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assertion** for a strong, confident statement. It often implies that the claim may need proof. Common collocations: "make an assertion," "bold assertion," "unsubstantiated assertion."

👉 Example: His assertion that the project would succeed proved to be entirely correct.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **assertion** that climate change is accelerating was supported by decades of carefully collected scientific data.
   教授关于气候变化正在加速的断言得到了数十年精心收集的科学数据的支持。

2. Her bold **assertion** during the debate challenged the prevailing assumptions and forced the audience to reconsider their views.
   她在辩论中大胆的主张挑战了主流假设，迫使听众重新审视自己的观点。

3. The company dismissed the competitor's **assertion** of patent infringement as completely baseless and without factual merit.
   该公司认为竞争对手关于专利侵权的声明完全没有根据，毫无事实依据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Assertion" = "assert" + "-ion" (noun suffix). If you can "assert" (verb), the thing you produce is an "assertion" (noun). Think: a strong person making a strong statement.
"assertion"="assert"+"-ion"（名词后缀）。如果你能"assert"（动词），你产生的东西就是"assertion"（名词）。想象：一个强势的人发表强势的声明。
