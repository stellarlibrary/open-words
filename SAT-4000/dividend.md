# 💰 dividend

🔊 /ˈdɪvɪdɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sum of money paid regularly by a company to its shareholders; a number to be divided by another; an additional benefit
**中文：** n. 红利；股息；被除数；额外好处

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- profit（利润）
- yield（收益）
- bonus（红利）
- return（回报）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pay a dividend / dividend yield / declare a dividend / peace dividend**

👉 Example: The company announced a generous quarterly dividend to reward its long-term shareholders for their continued loyalty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Investors were pleased when the corporation declared a higher annual **dividend** than analysts had originally predicted.
   当公司宣布的年度股息高于分析师最初的预测时，投资者感到满意。

2. In mathematics, the **dividend** is the number that is being divided by the divisor to produce the quotient.
   在数学中，被除数是被除数除以除数以得出商的数。

3. Years of consistent investment in employee training have paid substantial **dividends** in terms of productivity and innovation.
   多年来对员工培训的持续投资在生产力和创新方面带来了丰厚的回报。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dividend" as "divide-end" — what you get at the end when profits are divided among shareholders.
把"dividend"想成"divide(分配)+end(结果)"——利润分配后你最终得到的份额。
