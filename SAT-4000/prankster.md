# 🃏 prankster

🔊 /ˈpræŋkstər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who plays pranks or practical jokes on others; a mischievous individual fond of tricks
**中文：** n. 爱开玩笑的人；顽皮的人；恶作剧的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- joker（爱开玩笑的人）
- trickster（恶作剧者）
- jester（滑稽人物）
- mischief-maker（捣蛋鬼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **notorious prankster / office prankster / known prankster / incorrigible prankster**

👉 Example: The notorious prankster was finally caught after weeks of elaborate hoaxes that bewildered the entire dormitory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The school's most notorious **prankster** was suspended after orchestrating an elaborate hoax that disrupted the annual ceremony.
   学校里最臭名昭著的恶作剧者在策划了一场扰乱年度典礼的精心骗局后被停学了。

2. Despite his reputation as a **prankster**, the young man demonstrated genuine compassion and reliability when serious situations arose.
   尽管他以恶作剧者闻名，但在严肃情况出现时，这个年轻人表现出了真正的同情心和可靠性。

3. The memoir fondly recounts the author's years as a childhood **prankster** who constantly tested the patience of teachers.
   这本回忆录深情地回顾了作者童年时期作为恶作剧者不断考验老师耐心的岁月。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prank-ster" — just add "-ster" (a person who does something) to "prank" — a prankster is simply a person who pranks.
把"prankster"想成"prank+ster"——在"prank（恶作剧）"后加上"-ster（做某事的人）"——恶作剧者就是搞恶作剧的人。
