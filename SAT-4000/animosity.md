# 😤 animosity

🔊 /ˌænɪˈmɑːsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *n. hatred; hostility; enmity | 名词：憎恶，仇恨，敌意*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Strong hostility or active hatred; a feeling of intense ill will or resentment toward someone or something.
**中文：** 强烈的敌意或积极的仇恨；对某人或某事怀有强烈的恶意或怨恨。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hostility（敌意）
- enmity（仇恨）
- antagonism（对抗）
- rancor（怨恨）
- antipathy（反感）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **animosity toward/against/between, deep animosity, personal animosity, bitter animosity**
👉 Example: The bitter animosity between the two rival factions threatened to undermine the entire peace negotiation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The longstanding **animosity** between the neighboring countries made diplomatic negotiations extremely difficult and protracted.
   两个邻国之间长期的敌意使外交谈判变得极其困难和旷日持久。

2. Despite years of personal **animosity**, the two former partners agreed to collaborate for the benefit of their community.
   尽管多年来个人之间存在仇恨，两位前合伙人同意为社区的利益而合作。

3. The witness testified that the defendant harbored deep **animosity** toward the victim long before the incident occurred.
   证人作证说被告在事件发生很久之前就对受害者怀有深深的敌意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Animosity" contains "animus" (Latin for hostile spirit) — picture an angry animal (anim-) full of ferocity, snarling with hatred.
"Animosity"包含"animus"（拉丁语，意为敌意的精神）——想象一只愤怒的动物（anim-）满怀凶猛，咆哮着仇恨。
