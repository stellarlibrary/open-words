# 🌊 deluge

🔊 /ˈdɛljuːdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A great flood; an overwhelming amount. (v.) To flood or overwhelm with a large quantity
**中文：** n. 洪水；泛滥 v. 使泛滥；压倒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flood（洪水）
- torrent（急流）
- inundation（淹没）
- avalanche（雪崩般的大量）
- barrage（连续猛烈攻击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a deluge of complaints / deluge of rain / deluged with requests / deluged by criticism**

👉 Example: The customer service department was deluged with complaints after the software update failed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unprecedented **deluge** of applications overwhelmed the admissions office, forcing them to extend the review deadline significantly.
   前所未有的大量申请使招生办公室不堪重负，迫使他们大幅延长审核截止日期。

2. After the celebrity endorsed the product, the company was **deluged** with orders that far exceeded their production capacity.
   在名人代言该产品后，公司被远超其生产能力的订单所淹没。

3. A sudden **deluge** transformed the dry riverbed into a raging torrent, compelling authorities to evacuate nearby residential areas.
   一场突如其来的暴雨将干涸的河床变成了汹涌的急流，迫使当局疏散附近居民区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deluge" as "de-luge" — imagine a "huge" flood that "de-luges" everything, washing it all away.
把"deluge"想成"de-luge"——想象一场"巨大的"洪水将一切冲走，淹没一切。
