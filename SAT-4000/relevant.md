# 🎯 relevant

🔊 /ˈreləvənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Closely connected or appropriate to the matter at hand; having a bearing on or importance to the subject
**中文：** adj. 相关的；切题的；中肯的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pertinent（相关的）
- applicable（适用的）
- germane（密切相关的）
- apposite（恰当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **relevant to / relevant information / relevant experience / highly relevant**

👉 Example: The attorney argued that the evidence was not relevant to the case and should be excluded.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor asked students to include only **relevant** sources that directly supported the central thesis of their research papers.
   教授要求学生只引用与其研究论文中心论点直接相关的文献。

2. Candidates must demonstrate **relevant** professional experience in order to be considered for the senior management position.
   候选人必须展示相关的专业经验，才能被考虑担任高级管理职位。

3. As technology evolves rapidly, workers must continually update their skills to remain **relevant** in a competitive job market.
   随着技术的快速发展，工人必须不断更新技能，以在竞争激烈的就业市场中保持竞争力。

---

Think of "relevant" as "relev-ant" — an ant that always finds the right path, staying connected to what matters most.
把"relevant"想成"relev-ant"——一只总能找到正确路径的蚂蚁，始终与最重要的事物保持联系。
