# 🙏 disciple

🔊 /dɪˈsaɪpəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A follower or student of a teacher, leader, or philosopher; one who accepts and helps to spread the doctrines of another
**中文：** n. 门徒，信徒；弟子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- follower（追随者）
- adherent（拥护者）
- devotee（信徒）
- apostle（使徒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disciple of (someone) / faithful disciple / devoted disciple / early disciples**

👉 Example: She was a devoted disciple of the renowned philosopher throughout her academic career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient text describes how each **disciple** faithfully recorded the teachings of the master for future generations.
   古文献描述了每位门徒如何忠实地为后世记录师父的教导。

2. As a committed **disciple** of modern architecture, she championed minimalist design principles in all her projects.
   作为现代建筑的忠实信徒，她在所有项目中倡导极简主义设计原则。

3. The professor attracted many **disciples** who went on to establish influential research programs at leading universities worldwide.
   这位教授吸引了许多弟子，他们后来在世界各地的顶尖大学建立了有影响力的研究项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "disciple" as sharing roots with "discipline" — a disciple is someone who follows the discipline of a teacher or leader.
把"disciple"和"discipline"联系起来——门徒就是遵循某位老师或领袖所传授之道的人。
