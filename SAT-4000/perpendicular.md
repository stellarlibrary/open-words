# 📐 perpendicular

🔊 /ˌpɜːrpənˈdɪkjʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) At an angle of 90 degrees to a given line, plane, or surface; exactly upright. (n.) A straight line at right angles to another
**中文：** adj. 垂直的；正交的；直立的；陡峭的 n. 垂线；垂直的位置

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vertical（垂直的）
- upright（直立的）
- orthogonal（正交的）
- plumb（铅直的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perpendicular to the surface / perpendicular lines / perpendicular angle / draw a perpendicular**

👉 Example: The architect ensured that the columns were perfectly perpendicular to the foundation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surveyor verified that the new wall was precisely **perpendicular** to the existing structure before construction could proceed.
   测量员在施工之前确认新墙与现有建筑物精确垂直。

2. In Euclidean geometry, two lines are considered **perpendicular** when they intersect to form four equal right angles.
   在欧几里得几何中，当两条直线相交形成四个相等的直角时，它们被认为是垂直的。

3. The cliff face rose nearly **perpendicular** from the ocean floor, presenting an imposing and seemingly insurmountable barrier.
   悬崖几乎从海底垂直升起，形成了一道令人生畏且看似不可逾越的屏障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-pend-icular" — "pend" means "hang" (as in pendulum). A pendulum hangs perfectly straight down, forming a perpendicular line to the ground.
把"perpendicular"想成"per + pend（悬挂）+ icular"——像钟摆一样笔直悬挂，与地面形成垂直线。
