# 🌟 advantageous

🔊 /ˌædvənˈteɪdʒəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Providing a favorable condition or benefit; creating a position of superiority
**中文：** adj. 有利的；有益的；有优势的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beneficial（有益的）
- favorable（有利的）
- profitable（有利可图的）
- expedient（合乎利益的）
- propitious（有利的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **advantageous position / advantageous to / mutually advantageous / advantageous terms**

👉 Example: The merger proved advantageous to both companies, resulting in increased revenue and market share.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Securing an internship at a prestigious firm is highly **advantageous** for students seeking competitive positions after graduation.
   在名企获得实习机会对毕业后寻求竞争性职位的学生非常有利。

2. The treaty established mutually **advantageous** trade conditions that stimulated economic growth in both nations for decades.
   该条约建立了互利的贸易条件，数十年来促进了两国的经济增长。

3. Researchers found that an **advantageous** genetic variation helped certain populations adapt more effectively to high-altitude environments.
   研究人员发现一种有利的基因变异帮助某些族群更有效地适应了高海拔环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "advantageous" as "advantage-OUS" — simply the adjective form of advantage, meaning full of advantages.
把"advantageous"想成"advantage加-ous"——就是advantage的形容词形式，"充满优势的"。
