# 👉 impute

🔊 /ɪmˈpjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To attribute or ascribe something, especially blame or a fault, to someone; to regard as being caused by or resulting from a particular source
**中文：** v. 归罪于；归咎于；嫁祸于；归因于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attribute（归因于）
- ascribe（归于）
- assign（指定）
- credit（归功于）
- charge（指控）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impute blame to / impute motives to / impute a crime to / impute responsibility to**

👉 Example: The investigation sought to impute responsibility for the disaster to the negligent construction firm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosecutor attempted to **impute** criminal intent to the defendant despite the absence of any direct incriminating evidence.
   检察官试图将犯罪意图归于被告，尽管缺乏任何直接的有罪证据。

2. It would be unjust to **impute** the failure of the entire project to a single team member without reviewing all contributing factors.
   在没有审查所有影响因素的情况下，将整个项目的失败归咎于一名团队成员是不公正的。

3. Scholars cautioned against the tendency to **impute** modern political ideologies to historical figures who lived in vastly different contexts.
   学者们告诫不要将现代政治意识形态归于生活在截然不同时代背景下的历史人物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impute" as "I'm put (blame on someone)" — I'm putting the blame onto another person, attributing fault to them.
把"impute"想成"im + pute（来自拉丁语putare，认为）"——把某事"认为"是某人造成的，即归罪于、归咎于。
