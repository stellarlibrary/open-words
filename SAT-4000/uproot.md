# 🌳 uproot

🔊 /ʌpˈruːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To pull a plant out by the roots; to displace someone from their home or familiar surroundings; to eradicate completely
**中文：** v. 根除；连根拔起；使背井离乡

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eradicate（根除）
- extirpate（灭绝）
- displace（使流离失所）
- eliminate（消除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **uproot a tree / uproot a family / uproot corruption / uproot traditions / uproot from home**

👉 Example: The hurricane uprooted hundreds of mature trees along the coastal highway overnight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The conflict **uprooted** millions of civilians, forcing them to seek refuge in neighboring countries under desperate conditions.
   这场冲突使数百万平民背井离乡，迫使他们在绝望的条件下到邻国寻求庇护。

2. The new administration vowed to **uproot** systemic corruption that had been entrenched in government institutions for decades.
   新政府誓言要根除数十年来根深蒂固于政府机构中的系统性腐败。

3. Botanists carefully **uprooted** the invasive species to prevent further ecological damage to the native wetland habitat.
   植物学家小心地将入侵物种连根拔起，以防止对原生湿地栖息地造成进一步的生态破坏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "up-root" — pulling the roots up out of the ground. Once the roots are gone, the plant (or problem) cannot survive.
把"uproot"想成"把根拔起来"——up（向上）+ root（根），一旦根被拔出，植物（或问题）就无法存活。
