# 🧪 hydrochloric acid

🔊 /ˌhaɪdrəˌklɔːrɪk ˈæsɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A strong, corrosive acid composed of hydrogen chloride dissolved in water, with the chemical formula HCl; widely used in industry and found naturally in gastric juice
**中文：** n. 氢氯酸；盐酸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- muriatic acid（盐酸）
- hydrogen chloride solution（氯化氢溶液）
- spirits of salt（盐精）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concentrated hydrochloric acid / dilute hydrochloric acid / hydrochloric acid solution / react with hydrochloric acid**

👉 Example: The laboratory protocol requires dilute hydrochloric acid for the neutralization reaction with sodium hydroxide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist carefully added **hydrochloric acid** to the beaker to dissolve the calcium carbonate sample for further analysis.
   化学家小心地将盐酸加入烧杯中，以溶解碳酸钙样品进行进一步分析。

2. Industrial manufacturers use **hydrochloric acid** extensively in the processing of steel and the production of organic compounds.
   工业制造商广泛使用盐酸来进行钢材加工和有机化合物的生产。

3. The human stomach produces **hydrochloric acid** naturally to facilitate the digestion of proteins and eliminate harmful bacteria.
   人体胃部自然分泌盐酸，以促进蛋白质的消化并消灭有害细菌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hydro-chlor-ic acid" as "hydrogen + chlorine + acid" — an acid made from hydrogen and chlorine.
把"hydrochloric acid"拆成"hydro"（氢）+"chlor"（氯）+"ic acid"（酸）——一种由氢和氯组成的酸。
