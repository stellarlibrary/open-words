# 🔀 dual

🔊 /ˈdjuːəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having two parts, aspects, or elements; twofold. (n.) The dual number in grammar
**中文：** adj. 双的；双重的 n. 双数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- twofold（双重的）
- double（双倍的）
- binary（二元的）
- paired（成对的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dual purpose / dual citizenship / dual role / dual nature**

👉 Example: The device serves a dual purpose as both a communication tool and a navigation system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor holds **dual** citizenship in France and Canada, which facilitates her frequent international research collaborations.
   这位教授持有法国和加拿大的双重国籍，这便利了她频繁的国际研究合作。

2. The new policy framework was designed to fulfill a **dual** mandate of promoting economic growth and environmental sustainability.
   新的政策框架旨在履行促进经济增长和环境可持续性的双重使命。

3. Philosophers have long debated the **dual** nature of human consciousness, examining both its physical and metaphysical dimensions.
   哲学家们长期以来一直在辩论人类意识的双重性质，探讨其物理和形而上学的维度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dual" as containing "du" — like "duo" meaning two. Anything "dual" involves a pair or two aspects working together.
把"dual"想成包含"du"——就像"duo"意思是二。任何"dual"的事物都涉及一对或两个方面的配合。
