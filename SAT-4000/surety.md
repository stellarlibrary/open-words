# 🤝 surety

🔊 /ˈʃʊrəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A guarantee or pledge; a person who assumes responsibility for another's obligations; the state of being sure or certain
**中文：** n. 担保，保证

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guarantee（保证）
- pledge（担保）
- bond（保证金）
- collateral（抵押品）
- assurance（保障）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stand surety for / surety bond / provide surety / act as surety**

👉 Example: His uncle agreed to stand surety for the loan so that he could purchase his first home.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court required the defendant to provide a **surety** of fifty thousand dollars before granting bail for the pending trial.
   法院要求被告在批准待审保释之前提供五万美元的担保。

2. She agreed to act as **surety** for her colleague's business loan, accepting full financial liability if repayment obligations were not met.
   她同意为同事的商业贷款做担保人，承担如果还款义务未履行的全部经济责任。

3. Without adequate **surety**, the bank refused to approve the construction project's financing despite the developer's strong track record.
   在没有充分担保的情况下，尽管开发商有良好的业绩记录，银行仍拒绝批准该建设项目的融资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surety" as "sure + ty" — it makes you sure that a promise will be kept, providing certainty through a guarantee.
把"surety"想成"sure + ty"——它使你确信承诺会被遵守，通过担保提供确定性。
