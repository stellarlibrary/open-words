# 💉 anesthetic

🔊 /ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or producing an insensibility to pain. (n.) A substance that induces insensitivity to pain, used during surgical procedures
**中文：** adj. 麻醉的 n. 麻醉剂；麻药

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sedative（镇静剂）
- analgesic（止痛药）
- numbing agent（麻木剂）
- painkiller（止痛剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **local anesthetic / general anesthetic / administer an anesthetic / under anesthetic / anesthetic effect**

👉 Example: The dentist applied a local anesthetic before extracting the severely decayed tooth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon confirmed that the patient was fully under **anesthetic** before making the first precise incision on the abdomen.
   外科医生在对腹部进行第一次精确切口之前，确认患者已完全处于麻醉状态。

2. Modern **anesthetic** techniques have dramatically reduced the risks associated with prolonged and intricate surgical procedures.
   现代麻醉技术大大降低了与长时间复杂外科手术相关的风险。

3. The veterinarian administered a mild **anesthetic** to calm the injured animal before carefully examining its fractured limb.
   兽医给受伤的动物注射了温和的麻醉剂，使其镇静下来，然后仔细检查其骨折的肢体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "an-esthetic" as "an" (without) + "esthetic" (feeling) — something that takes away your feeling, especially pain.
把"anesthetic"拆成"an"（没有）+"esthetic"（感觉）——让你失去感觉的东西，尤其是痛感。
