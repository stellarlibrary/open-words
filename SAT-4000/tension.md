# ⚡ tension

🔊 /ˈtɛnʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being stretched tight; mental or emotional strain; a strained political or social relationship between groups.
**中文：** n. 张力，拉力；紧张；紧张关系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strain（紧张）
- stress（压力）
- pressure（压迫）
- tautness（绷紧）
- friction（摩擦）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ease the tension / rising tension / racial tension / tension between / surface tension**

👉 Example: The mediator worked diligently to ease the tension between the two disputing parties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Political **tension** in the region escalated significantly after the government imposed strict new regulations on freedom of assembly.
   该地区的政治紧张局势在政府对集会自由实施严格新规定后显著升级。

2. The engineer calculated the **tension** in the suspension cables to ensure the bridge could withstand extreme weather conditions safely.
   工程师计算了悬索中的张力，以确保桥梁能够安全承受极端天气条件。

3. A palpable **tension** filled the examination hall as students awaited the distribution of their final assessment papers.
   一种明显的紧张气氛弥漫在考场中，学生们等待着期末考试试卷的分发。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tension" as "ten + sion (shun)" — imagine ten people pulling a rope in opposite directions, creating enormous tension.
把"tension"想成"ten（十）+ sion"——想象十个人朝相反方向拉一根绳子，产生巨大的张力。
