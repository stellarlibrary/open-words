# 🔢 remainder

🔊 /rɪˈmeɪndər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The part that is left over after other parts have been removed, used, or dealt with; in mathematics, the amount left after division
**中文：** n. 余数，残余；剩余物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- residue（残余物）
- remnant（剩余部分）
- surplus（剩余）
- balance（余额）
- leftover（剩余物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the remainder of / for the remainder of / remainder of one's life / divide with a remainder**

👉 Example: The remainder of the budget was allocated to infrastructure improvements in underserved communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the initial funding was distributed, the **remainder** of the grant was reserved for unexpected operational expenses throughout the year.
   初始资金分配后，拨款的剩余部分被预留用于全年意外运营费用。

2. The professor spent the **remainder** of the lecture analyzing the sociopolitical implications of the historical treaty in great detail.
   教授用讲座的剩余时间详细分析了这一历史条约的社会政治影响。

3. When seventeen is divided by five, the quotient is three and the **remainder** is two, a concept fundamental to modular arithmetic.
   当十七除以五时，商为三，余数为二，这是模运算的基本概念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remainder" as "remain-der" — it is what remains after everything else has been taken away or used up.
把"remainder"想成"remain-der"——它是其他一切被拿走或用完后剩下的东西。
