# 🎭 delude

🔊 /dɪˈluːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To impose a misleading belief upon someone; to deceive or trick; to cause someone to believe something that is not true
**中文：** v. 欺骗；哄骗；迷惑；蛊惑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deceive（欺骗）
- mislead（误导）
- beguile（诱骗）
- dupe（愚弄）
- hoodwink（蒙骗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **delude oneself / delude into believing / delude the public / be deluded by / delude someone into thinking**

👉 Example: He continued to delude himself into believing that his failing business would somehow recover on its own.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The fraudulent company managed to **delude** thousands of investors into believing that their returns were guaranteed and risk-free.
   这家欺诈公司成功地欺骗了数千名投资者，使他们相信回报是有保证且无风险的。

2. We must not **delude** ourselves into thinking that superficial reforms alone will resolve the deeply rooted systemic inequalities.
   我们不能自欺欺人地认为仅靠表面的改革就能解决根深蒂固的系统性不平等。

3. The dictator used state-controlled media to **delude** the population about the true state of the nation's collapsing economy.
   独裁者利用国家控制的媒体来蒙骗民众，掩盖国家经济崩溃的真实状况。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-lude" as "de- + lude (play)" — to play tricks on someone's mind, leading them away from the truth with a false performance.
把"delude"想成"de- + lude（玩耍）"——在某人的心智上玩把戏，用虚假的表演引导他们远离真相。
