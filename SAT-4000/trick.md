# 🎭 trick

🔊 /trɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A cunning act or scheme intended to deceive; a skillful technique or knack. (v.) To deceive or outwit someone through cunning.
**中文：** n. 诡计；窍门；把戏 v. 欺骗；哄骗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deception（欺骗）
- ruse（诡计）
- stratagem（策略）
- ploy（手段）
- deceive（欺骗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **play a trick on / trick someone into / do the trick / trick of the trade**

👉 Example: The magician performed an elaborate trick that left the entire audience astonished and bewildered.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The con artist used a sophisticated **trick** to defraud elderly investors of their retirement savings over several years.
   骗子使用了一个精心设计的诡计，在数年间骗取了老年投资者的退休储蓄。

2. The experienced negotiator **tricked** the opposing party into revealing their minimum acceptable terms before the formal discussions began.
   经验丰富的谈判者在正式讨论开始前哄骗对方透露了他们的最低可接受条件。

3. Learning the **tricks** of effective time management can significantly enhance both academic performance and personal productivity.
   学习有效时间管理的窍门可以显著提升学业表现和个人生产力。

---

把"trick"想成"tr-ick"——被骗后让你感到"恶心(ick)"的东西。
