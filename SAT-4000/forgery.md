# 🖋️ forgery

🔊 /ˈfɔːrdʒəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of producing a fraudulent imitation of a document, signature, or work of art; the object so produced
**中文：** n. 伪造；伪造罪；伪造物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- counterfeit（伪造品）
- fabrication（捏造）
- falsification（篡改）
- fraud（欺诈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commit forgery / detect a forgery / art forgery / forgery of documents**

👉 Example: The museum discovered that the Renaissance painting was actually a skilled forgery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advanced forensic techniques enabled investigators to confirm that the historical manuscript was a sophisticated **forgery** created in the twentieth century.
   先进的法医技术使调查人员确认这份历史手稿是二十世纪制造的精密伪造物。

2. The banker was sentenced to ten years in prison after being convicted of **forgery** and embezzlement on a massive scale.
   这位银行家因大规模伪造和贪污罪被判处十年监禁。

3. Experts in handwriting analysis were called upon to determine whether the contested signature was genuine or a **forgery**.
   笔迹分析专家被请来鉴定有争议的签名是真迹还是伪造的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "forge-ry" — a forge is where metals are shaped and molded, and forgery is when someone "forges" or molds a fake version of something real.
把"forgery"想成来自"forge（锻造）"——锻造是将金属塑形，而forgery就是有人"锻造"出真东西的假冒版本。
