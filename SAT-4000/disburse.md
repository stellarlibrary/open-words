# 💰 disburse

🔊 /dɪsˈbɜːrs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To pay out money, especially from a fund or account; to distribute funds for a specific purpose
**中文：** v. 支付；支出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pay out（支付）
- expend（花费）
- distribute（分配）
- allocate（拨款）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disburse funds / disburse payments / disburse money / disburse grants**

👉 Example: The foundation will disburse scholarship funds directly to qualifying students each semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The treasury department was authorized to **disburse** emergency relief funds to communities affected by the devastating earthquake.
   财政部门被授权向受毁灭性地震影响的社区支付紧急救济资金。

2. The university will **disburse** financial aid packages to eligible students within two weeks of the enrollment deadline.
   大学将在注册截止日期后两周内向符合条件的学生发放经济援助。

3. Auditors discovered that the agency had failed to properly **disburse** over three million dollars in allocated research grants.
   审计人员发现该机构未能妥善支付超过三百万美元的已拨研究经费。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-burse" — "burse" comes from "purse" (钱包); to disburse means to take money out of the purse and distribute it.
把"disburse"想成"分散(dis)+钱包(burse/purse)"——从钱包里掏出钱来分发，即支付。
