# ⭐ prominence

🔊 /ˈprɑːmɪnəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being important, famous, or noticeable; a thing that projects or stands out from something
**中文：** n. 突出；显著；突出物；卓越

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distinction（卓越）
- eminence（显赫）
- notability（显著）
- prestige（声望）
- renown（名望）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rise to prominence / gain prominence / achieve prominence / a position of prominence**

👉 Example: The young scientist rose to prominence after publishing groundbreaking research on gene therapy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The activist rose to **prominence** during the civil rights movement through her eloquent speeches and unwavering commitment to justice.
   这位活动家在民权运动期间凭借她雄辩的演讲和对正义的坚定承诺声名鹊起。

2. Environmental concerns have gained considerable **prominence** in recent political discourse as climate change accelerates across the globe.
   随着全球气候变化的加速，环境问题在近期政治讨论中获得了相当大的关注度。

3. The architectural **prominence** of the cathedral's spire made it a recognizable landmark visible from nearly every district in the city.
   大教堂尖塔在建筑上的突出使其成为城市几乎每个区域都能看到的标志性地标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "PROMINENT" without the "t" — prominence is the quality of sticking out, like a mountain peak that is prominent on the horizon.
把"prominence"想成"PROMINENT"去掉"t"——prominence是突出的特质，就像地平线上突出的山峰。
