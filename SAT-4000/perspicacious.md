# 🧠 perspicacious

🔊 /ˌpɜːrspɪˈkeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a ready insight into and understanding of things; shrewd and discerning
**中文：** adj. 有洞察力的；聪颖的；敏锐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astute（精明的）
- shrewd（敏锐的）
- discerning（有辨别力的）
- insightful（有洞察力的）
- perceptive（感知力强的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perspicacious observer / perspicacious analysis / perspicacious judgment / perspicacious mind**

👉 Example: The perspicacious investor recognized the market trend months before her competitors did.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **perspicacious** journalist uncovered the financial scandal by noticing subtle discrepancies in the corporation's quarterly reports.
   这位有洞察力的记者通过注意到公司季度报告中的细微差异揭露了这起金融丑闻。

2. Her **perspicacious** reading of the political landscape enabled the campaign to anticipate and counter every opposing strategy.
   她对政治格局的敏锐解读使竞选团队能够预见并反击每一项对手策略。

3. A **perspicacious** leader understands not only what employees say but also the unspoken concerns that influence workplace morale.
   一位有洞察力的领导者不仅理解员工所说的话，还能理解影响工作士气的未说出口的顾虑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perspi-CACIOUS" — someone with "capacity" to see through things clearly, like wearing special glasses that reveal hidden truths.
把"perspicacious"想成"perspi-CACIOUS"——一个有"能力"看穿事物的人，就像戴着能揭示隐藏真相的特殊眼镜。
