# 📜 stanza

🔊 /ˈstænzə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A group of lines forming the basic recurring unit in a poem; a verse; also used to refer to a period or phase in a performance or game
**中文：** n. 诗的一节；演出期；局；场

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- verse（诗节）
- canto（长诗的篇）
- strophe（诗节）
- passage（段落）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **opening stanza / final stanza / four-line stanza / stanza of a poem / stanza break**

👉 Example: The poet carefully crafted each stanza to build toward a powerful emotional climax.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor asked the students to analyze the rhyme scheme and imagery within the third **stanza** of the poem.
   教授要求学生分析这首诗第三节中的韵律和意象。

2. Each **stanza** of the ballad revealed a new chapter in the protagonist's long and sorrowful journey homeward.
   这首叙事诗的每一节都揭示了主人公漫长而悲伤的归途中的一个新篇章。

3. The critic praised the final **stanza** for its masterful use of metaphor and its deeply resonant emotional tone.
   评论家赞扬了最后一节对隐喻的巧妙运用及其深刻的情感共鸣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stanza" as "stand-za" — each stanza stands on its own as a distinct unit within a poem, like a room in a house.
把"stanza"想成"stand-za"——每个诗节作为诗中一个独立单元独立存在，就像房子里的一个房间。
