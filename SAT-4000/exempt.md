# 🛡️ exempt

🔊 /ɪɡˈzempt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To free from an obligation or liability imposed on others. (adj.) Free from an obligation or liability imposed on others
**中文：** v. 免除，豁免 adj. 被免除的，被豁免的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excuse（免除）
- release（释放）
- spare（赦免）
- immune（免疫的）
- excused（被免除的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exempt from taxes / exempt from duty / exempt from requirements / tax-exempt**

👉 Example: Nonprofit organizations are typically exempt from federal income tax obligations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholarship program **exempts** qualifying students from tuition fees for the entire duration of their studies.
   该奖学金计划免除符合条件的学生在整个学习期间的学费。

2. Diplomatic personnel are generally **exempt** from local jurisdiction and taxation under established international conventions.
   根据既定的国际公约，外交人员通常被豁免于当地的司法管辖和税收。

3. The committee voted to **exempt** small businesses from the new regulatory requirements due to financial constraints.
   委员会投票决定因财务限制而免除小型企业的新监管要求。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exempt" as "exit + empty" — you exit the obligation and your responsibility plate is empty.
把"exempt"想成"exit + empty"——你退出了义务，你的责任盘子空了。
