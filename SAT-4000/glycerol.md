# 🧴 glycerol

🔊 /ˈɡlɪsərɒl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless, odorless, viscous liquid used in pharmaceutical, food, and cosmetic products; also known as glycerin
**中文：** n. 甘油；丙三醇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- glycerin（甘油）
- glycerine（丙三醇）
- propanetriol（丙三醇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glycerol content / glycerol solution / glycerol molecule / glycerol-based**

👉 Example: The pharmaceutical company used purified glycerol as a key ingredient in its cough syrup formulation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Glycerol** is a fundamental component of triglycerides, which constitute the primary form of fat storage in the human body.
   甘油是甘油三酯的基本组成部分，而甘油三酯是人体脂肪储存的主要形式。

2. The cosmetics industry relies heavily on **glycerol** for its moisturizing properties that help retain water in the skin.
   化妆品行业非常依赖甘油的保湿特性，有助于保持皮肤水分。

3. During saponification, fats and oils are broken down into fatty acid salts and **glycerol** through an alkaline reaction.
   在皂化过程中，脂肪和油脂通过碱性反应被分解为脂肪酸盐和甘油。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glycerol" as "slippery oil" — glycerol is a slippery, viscous substance often found in lotions and soaps.
把"glycerol"想成"滑溜的油（slippery oil）"——甘油是一种滑溜的黏稠物质，常见于乳液和肥皂中。
