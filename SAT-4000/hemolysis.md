# 🔬 hemolysis

🔊 /hɪˈmɒlɪsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The destruction or dissolution of red blood cells, resulting in the release of hemoglobin into the surrounding fluid
**中文：** n. 溶血

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- erythrocyte destruction（红细胞破坏）
- blood cell lysis（血细胞溶解）
- haemolysis（溶血，英式拼写）
- erythrolysis（红细胞溶解）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cause hemolysis / hemolysis of red blood cells / intravascular hemolysis / hemolytic anemia**

👉 Example: Certain bacterial toxins can trigger hemolysis by directly attacking the membranes of red blood cells.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory report confirmed that **hemolysis** had occurred in the blood sample, rendering the test results unreliable.
   实验室报告确认血液样本中发生了溶血，导致检测结果不可靠。

2. Autoimmune disorders can provoke **hemolysis** when the immune system mistakenly targets and destroys healthy red blood cells.
   当免疫系统错误地攻击和破坏健康红细胞时，自身免疫性疾病可引发溶血。

3. The pharmacologist warned that the experimental drug could induce **hemolysis** as a potentially dangerous side effect in patients.
   药理学家警告说，该实验药物可能会引起溶血，这对患者来说是一种潜在的危险副作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "hemo" means blood and "lysis" means breaking apart — hemolysis is literally blood cells breaking apart.
拆分来看："hemo"表示血液，"lysis"表示分解——溶血字面意思就是血细胞的分解。
