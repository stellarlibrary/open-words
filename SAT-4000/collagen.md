# 🧬 collagen

🔊 /ˈkɒlədʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A structural protein found in skin, bone, cartilage, and connective tissue, providing strength and elasticity
**中文：** n. 胶原；胶原蛋白；胶原质

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- structural protein（结构蛋白）
- connective tissue protein（结缔组织蛋白）
- fibrous protein（纤维蛋白）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **collagen production / collagen supplements / collagen fibers / rich in collagen**

👉 Example: As we age, the body's natural collagen production gradually decreases, leading to wrinkles.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent clinical studies suggest that oral **collagen** supplements may improve skin elasticity and reduce visible signs of aging.
   最近的临床研究表明，口服胶原蛋白补充剂可能改善皮肤弹性并减少可见的衰老迹象。

2. The surgeon used a **collagen** scaffold to promote tissue regeneration in the patient's damaged knee cartilage.
   外科医生使用胶原蛋白支架来促进患者受损膝盖软骨的组织再生。

3. Vitamin C plays an essential role in **collagen** synthesis, which is critical for wound healing and maintaining healthy joints.
   维生素C在胶原蛋白合成中起着至关重要的作用，这对伤口愈合和维持关节健康至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "colla-gen" — "colla" sounds like "cola" (glue in Greek), and "gen" means to produce. Collagen is the protein that generates the "glue" holding your body together.
把"collagen"拆成"colla-gen"——"colla"源自希腊语的"胶水"，"gen"表示产生。胶原蛋白就是产生把身体粘合在一起的"胶水"的蛋白质。
