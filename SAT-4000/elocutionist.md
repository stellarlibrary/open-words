# 🎙️ elocutionist

🔊 /ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who is skilled in or teaches the art of public speaking and clear, expressive vocal delivery
**中文：** n. 演说家；雄辩家；朗诵家；演说术教师

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- orator（演说家）
- rhetorician（修辞学家）
- speaker（演讲者）
- declaimer（朗诵者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **renowned elocutionist / professional elocutionist / trained elocutionist / elocutionist and performer**

👉 Example: The famous elocutionist captivated audiences with her powerful and expressive recitation of classical poetry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The celebrated **elocutionist** traveled across the country delivering dramatic recitations of Shakespeare to enthralled theater audiences.
   这位著名的演说家在全国各地巡回，向着迷的剧院观众进行莎士比亚戏剧朗诵。

2. As a trained **elocutionist**, he was often invited to coach political candidates on their vocal delivery and stage presence.
   作为一名受过专业训练的演说家，他经常被邀请指导政治候选人的语言表达和舞台表现力。

3. The nineteenth-century **elocutionist** published several influential treatises on the principles of effective and persuasive public address.
   这位十九世纪的演说家出版了几部关于有效且有说服力的公共演讲原则的有影响力的论著。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elocutionist" as "elocution + ist" — just add "-ist" (a person who practices) to "elocution" (the art of speaking), giving you a master speaker.
把"elocutionist"想成"elocution + ist"——在"elocution（演说术）"后加上"-ist（从事者）"，就得到了一位演说大师。
