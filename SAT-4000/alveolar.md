# 🦷 alveolar

🔊 /ælˈviːələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the alveoli of the lungs or the bony ridge behind the upper teeth. (n.) A consonant sound articulated with the tongue against the alveolar ridge
**中文：** adj. 肺泡的；齿槽的 n. 齿槽音

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dental（齿音的）
- pulmonary（肺部的）
- alveolate（蜂窝状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alveolar ridge / alveolar consonant / alveolar bone / alveolar sac**

👉 Example: The phonetics professor demonstrated how alveolar consonants are produced by placing the tongue near the upper teeth ridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician identified damage to the **alveolar** tissue in the patient's lungs after reviewing the detailed imaging results.
   医生在查看详细的影像结果后，发现了患者肺部肺泡组织的损伤。

2. Linguistics students must learn to distinguish **alveolar** sounds from dental and palatal consonants in phonetic transcription exercises.
   语言学学生必须在语音转写练习中学会区分齿槽音与齿音和腭音。

3. The dental surgeon carefully examined the **alveolar** bone structure before proceeding with the implant placement procedure for the patient.
   牙科外科医生在为患者进行种植体植入手术之前，仔细检查了齿槽骨结构。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alveolar" as related to "alveoli" — tiny sacs in the lungs or sockets in the jaw, both are small cavities where important exchanges happen.
把"alveolar"与"alveoli"（肺泡）联系起来——无论是肺部的小囊还是颌骨中的齿槽，都是发生重要交换的小腔体。
