# 😠 browbeat

🔊 /ˈbraʊbiːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To intimidate or bully someone through stern, domineering, or threatening behavior
**中文：** v. 恫吓，吓唬；欺侮；以盛气凌人的态度对待

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intimidate（恐吓）
- bully（欺凌）
- coerce（胁迫）
- domineer（盛气凌人）
- badger（纠缠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **browbeat someone into doing something / browbeat a witness / browbeat subordinates**

👉 Example: The manager tried to browbeat the staff into accepting the unfavorable terms of the new contract.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney accused the prosecutor of attempting to **browbeat** the witness into changing her original testimony.
   辩护律师指控检察官试图恫吓证人更改她的原始证词。

2. Effective leaders inspire loyalty through respect rather than seeking to **browbeat** their subordinates into reluctant compliance.
   有效的领导者通过尊重来激发忠诚，而不是恫吓下属使其勉强服从。

3. The committee chairman was criticized for his tendency to **browbeat** dissenting members during heated parliamentary debates.
   该委员会主席因在激烈的议会辩论中恫吓持不同意见的成员而受到批评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brow + beat" — imagine someone beating their furrowed brow against yours, getting in your face to intimidate you.
把"browbeat"想成"brow（眉头）+ beat（打）"——想象某人皱着眉头逼近你的脸，用气势来吓唬你。
