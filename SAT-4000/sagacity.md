# 🧠 sagacity

🔊 /səˈɡæsəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being wise, shrewd, and far-sighted; keen discernment and sound judgment
**中文：** n. 睿智，聪敏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wisdom（智慧）
- acumen（敏锐）
- shrewdness（精明）
- discernment（洞察力）
- perspicacity（敏锐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political sagacity / remarkable sagacity / demonstrate sagacity / sagacity of the leader**

👉 Example: The director's sagacity in navigating corporate politics earned her widespread respect among colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **sagacity** was evident in her ability to foresee the implications of emerging technologies on society.
   这位教授的睿智体现在她预见新兴技术对社会影响的能力上。

2. Historical accounts frequently celebrate the **sagacity** of leaders who averted wars through skillful negotiation and diplomacy.
   历史记载经常赞颂那些通过巧妙谈判和外交手段避免战争的领导人的聪慧。

3. Investors who displayed **sagacity** during the financial crisis managed to protect their assets while others suffered devastating losses.
   在金融危机期间表现出远见的投资者成功保护了自己的资产，而其他人则遭受了毁灭性的损失。

---

把"sagacity"想成"saga（传奇）+ city（城市）"——一座建立在智慧传奇上的城市，每个决策都充满深刻的洞察力和远见。
