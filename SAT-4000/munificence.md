# 🎁 munificence

🔊 /mjuːˈnɪfɪsəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being extremely generous; lavish giving or great generosity, especially with money or gifts
**中文：** n. 慷慨给与；宽宏大量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- generosity（慷慨）
- liberality（大方）
- benevolence（仁慈）
- largesse（慷慨的赠予）
- philanthropy（慈善）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **munificence of a patron / royal munificence / display munificence / owe to the munificence of**

👉 Example: The museum's expansion was made possible by the munificence of several anonymous donors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university library was built entirely through the **munificence** of a wealthy alumnus who wished to remain anonymous.
   这座大学图书馆完全是由一位希望保持匿名的富有校友慷慨捐赠而建造的。

2. Historians often attribute the flourishing of Renaissance art to the **munificence** of powerful patrons such as the Medici family.
   历史学家常将文艺复兴时期艺术的繁荣归功于美第奇家族等权贵赞助人的慷慨资助。

3. The charity's annual report highlighted the remarkable **munificence** of donors who contributed over ten million dollars last year.
   该慈善机构的年度报告强调了去年捐款超过一千万美元的捐赠者们的卓越慷慨。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "muni-ficence" as "money-ficence" — someone with munificence is magnificent with their money, giving it away freely.
把"munificence"想成"money-ficence（金钱的华丽）"——拥有munificence的人在金钱上十分大方，慷慨解囊。
