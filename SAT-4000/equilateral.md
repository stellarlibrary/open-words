# 📊 equilateral

🔊 /ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having all sides of equal length. (n.) A figure in which all sides are equal
**中文：** adj. 等边的 n. 等边形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equal-sided（等边的）
- regular（正则的）
- symmetric（对称的）
- uniform（一致的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equilateral triangle / equilateral polygon / equilateral figure**

👉 Example: An equilateral triangle is one of the most fundamental shapes studied in geometry.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The teacher demonstrated that every **equilateral** triangle has three interior angles each measuring precisely sixty degrees.
   老师演示了每个等边三角形的三个内角各精确为六十度。

2. The **equilateral** design of the crystal lattice contributes to the remarkable structural strength observed in diamond formations.
   晶体点阵的等边设计促成了金刚石结构中观察到的显著结构强度。

3. Ancient architects frequently employed **equilateral** shapes in temple construction to symbolize harmony, balance, and divine perfection.
   古代建筑师经常在寺庙建造中使用等边形状来象征和谐、平衡和神圣的完美。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equilateral" as "equi + lateral" — "equi" means equal, "lateral" means side. All sides are equal in length.
把"equilateral"想成"equi + lateral"——"equi"意为相等，"lateral"意为边。所有的边长度相等。
