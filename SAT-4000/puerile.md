# 👶 puerile

🔊 /ˈpjʊərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Childishly silly and trivial; lacking maturity or seriousness; characteristic of a child rather than an adult
**中文：** adj. 幼稚的；孩子气的；未成熟的；天真的；不成熟的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- childish（幼稚的）
- immature（不成熟的）
- juvenile（少年的）
- infantile（婴儿般的）
- naive（天真的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **puerile behavior / puerile humor / puerile remarks / puerile attempt**

👉 Example: The professor dismissed the student's argument as puerile and lacking any substantive intellectual merit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **puerile** outburst during the summit undermined months of careful negotiation between the two allied nations.
   这位外交官在峰会上幼稚的爆发破坏了两个盟国之间数月的精心谈判。

2. Critics condemned the film for its **puerile** humor, arguing that it relied entirely on cheap gags rather than genuine wit.
   评论家谴责这部电影幼稚的幽默，认为它完全依赖廉价的噱头而非真正的机智。

3. The board found his **puerile** response to the serious audit findings both unprofessional and deeply concerning for stakeholders.
   董事会认为他对严肃审计结果的幼稚回应既不专业，也令利益相关者深感不安。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "puerile" as related to "puer," the Latin word for boy or child. Puerile behavior is literally acting like a little child.
把"puerile"想成与"puer"有关——这是拉丁语中"男孩"或"孩子"的意思。puerile的行为实际上就是像小孩子一样幼稚。
