# 🪦 malediction

🔊 /ˌmælɪˈdɪkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A curse; the utterance of a curse against someone; evil speech or slander
**中文：** n. 诅咒；咒骂；坏话

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- curse（诅咒）
- imprecation（咒骂）
- execration（咒骂）
- anathema（诅咒）
- denunciation（谴责）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **utter a malediction / hurl maledictions / malediction against someone**

👉 Example: The old woman hurled a malediction at those who had wronged her family for generations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient text contained a **malediction** that supposedly brought misfortune to anyone who disturbed the sacred tomb.
   这本古籍中载有一段诅咒，据说会给任何扰乱这座神圣陵墓的人带来厄运。

2. In his final speech, the deposed ruler pronounced a bitter **malediction** upon the conspirators who had overthrown his regime.
   在最后的演说中，被废黜的统治者对推翻其政权的阴谋者发出了痛苦的诅咒。

3. Scholars interpreted the inscription as a **malediction** designed to protect the burial site from grave robbers and intruders.
   学者们将这段铭文解读为一种诅咒，旨在保护墓地免遭盗墓者和入侵者的侵扰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "male-diction" as "bad words" — "mal" means bad (as in malfunction) and "diction" means speech, so malediction is literally "bad speech" or a curse.
把"malediction"想成"坏话"——"mal"意为坏的（如malfunction故障），"diction"意为言辞，所以malediction就是"坏的言辞"，即诅咒。
