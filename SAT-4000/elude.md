# 🏃 elude

🔊 /ɪˈluːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To escape from or avoid something or someone, typically in a skillful or cunning way; to fail to be remembered or understood
**中文：** v. 逃避，躲避；不记得

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- evade（逃避）
- dodge（躲避）
- escape（逃脱）
- circumvent（规避）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **elude capture / elude detection / elude someone's grasp / elude understanding**

👉 Example: The fugitive managed to elude the authorities for nearly three years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite extensive surveillance operations, the notorious criminal continued to **elude** law enforcement agencies across multiple jurisdictions.
   尽管进行了大规模的监视行动，这名臭名昭著的罪犯仍继续在多个辖区内逃避执法机构的追捕。

2. The precise cause of the mysterious illness **eluded** researchers for decades until advanced genetic sequencing tools became available.
   这种神秘疾病的确切原因在数十年间一直困扰着研究人员，直到先进的基因测序工具问世。

3. Success in the international competition had long **eluded** the young athlete, but her perseverance finally paid off this season.
   这位年轻运动员在国际比赛中长期与成功无缘，但她的坚持终于在本赛季得到了回报。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "elude" as "e-LUDE" — sounds like "a lewd escape." Picture someone slipping away craftily to elude their pursuer.
把"elude"想成"e-LUDE"——联想到"滑溜地逃走"。想象一个人狡猾地溜走以躲避追捕者。
