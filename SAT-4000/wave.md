# 🌊 wave

🔊 /weɪv/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A periodic disturbance that transfers energy through a medium; a surge or swell of water; a sudden increase. (v.) To move back and forth; to signal by moving the hand
**中文：** n. 波浪；波动；高潮；挥手示意 v. 波动；起伏；挥手示意；摇动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surge（浪涌）
- ripple（涟漪）
- swell（膨胀）
- undulation（波动）
- billow（巨浪）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wave of protests / wave goodbye / sound wave / wave pattern / heat wave**

👉 Example: A wave of enthusiasm swept through the audience when the keynote speaker took the stage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The earthquake triggered a massive tidal **wave** that devastated coastal communities and displaced thousands of residents from their homes.
   地震引发了一场巨大的海啸，摧毁了沿海社区并使数千名居民流离失所。

2. Researchers observed a new **wave** of technological innovation emerging from collaborative efforts between universities and private industry.
   研究人员观察到大学与私营企业的合作努力正在催生新一波技术创新浪潮。

3. The physicist demonstrated how a light **wave** behaves differently when passing through materials of varying optical density.
   这位物理学家演示了光波在通过不同光密度材料时表现出的不同行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of waving your hand like an ocean wave — both involve a back-and-forth, up-and-down motion that travels outward.
想想挥手就像海浪一样——两者都涉及来回、上下的运动向外传播。
