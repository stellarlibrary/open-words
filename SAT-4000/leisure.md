# 🌴 leisure

🔊 /ˈliːʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Free time when one is not working or occupied; unhurried ease or relaxation. (adj.) Relating to free time or recreational activities.
**中文：** n. 空闲；闲暇；悠闲；安逸 adj. 空闲的；休闲的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relaxation（放松）
- recreation（消遣）
- repose（休息）
- ease（安逸）
- downtime（闲暇时间）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **at leisure / leisure time / leisure activities / leisure class / leisure pursuit**

👉 Example: She preferred to read the lengthy report at her leisure rather than rush through it before the meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sociologist examined how different social classes allocate their **leisure** time and the cultural significance of those choices.
   这位社会学家研究了不同社会阶层如何分配闲暇时间以及这些选择的文化意义。

2. During his **leisure** hours, the distinguished professor devoted himself to watercolor painting and classical piano composition.
   在空闲时间里，这位杰出的教授致力于水彩画和古典钢琴作曲。

3. The resort offered a wide array of **leisure** activities designed specifically for guests seeking tranquility and cultural enrichment.
   该度假村提供了多种休闲活动，专门为寻求宁静和文化体验的客人设计。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "leisure" as "lee-sure" — when you are on the lee (sheltered) side, you feel sure and safe, free to relax without worry.
把"leisure"想成"lee-sure"——当你在"lee"（避风的）一侧时，你感到安全无忧，可以自由地放松。
