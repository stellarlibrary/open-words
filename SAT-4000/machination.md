# 🕵️ machination

🔊 /ˌmækɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A plot or scheme, especially one devised to do harm; crafty and underhanded planning intended to accomplish an evil end
**中文：** 阴谋；诡计

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scheme（阴谋）
- conspiracy（图谋）
- intrigue（密谋）
- plot（阴谋）
- stratagem（诡计）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political machinations / secret machinations / machinations of power / behind-the-scenes machinations**

👉 Example: The novel exposed the machinations of corrupt officials who manipulated legislation for personal gain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board members were completely unaware of the **machinations** taking place behind closed doors to force the chairman's resignation.
   董事会成员完全不知道在幕后正在进行迫使董事长辞职的阴谋。

2. Historical records reveal the complex **machinations** of rival courtiers who competed relentlessly for the monarch's favor and political influence.
   历史记录揭示了敌对朝臣之间复杂的阴谋诡计，他们为争夺君主的宠信和政治影响力而不懈竞争。

3. The journalist's investigation uncovered the financial **machinations** that had allowed the corporation to evade taxes for over a decade.
   记者的调查揭露了使该公司十多年来得以逃税的金融诡计。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "machin-ation" from "machine" — machinations are like hidden machines working behind the scenes to carry out secret plots.
把"machination"想成来自"machine（机器）"——machinations就像隐藏的机器在幕后运转，执行秘密阴谋。
