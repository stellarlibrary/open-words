# ☀️ optimism

🔊 /ˈɑːptɪmɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Hopefulness and confidence about the future or the success of something; a tendency to take a favorable view
**中文：** n. 乐观；乐观主义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hopefulness（希望）
- positivity（积极性）
- confidence（信心）
- sanguineness（乐观）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cautious optimism / sense of optimism / grounds for optimism / express optimism**

👉 Example: Despite the economic downturn, the CEO expressed cautious optimism about the company's future growth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her unwavering **optimism** inspired the entire team to persevere through the most challenging phase of the research project.
   她坚定不移的乐观精神激励整个团队坚持度过了研究项目中最具挑战性的阶段。

2. The diplomat conveyed cautious **optimism** regarding the peace negotiations, noting that both parties had made significant concessions.
   这位外交官对和平谈判表达了谨慎的乐观态度，指出双方都做出了重大让步。

3. Historical evidence suggests that **optimism** in financial markets often precedes periods of irrational exuberance and subsequent correction.
   历史证据表明，金融市场中的乐观情绪往往先于非理性繁荣和随后的修正时期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "optimism" as "opti-mism" — "opti" from "optimal" (the best), so optimism means believing the best will happen.
把"optimism"想成"opti-mism"——"opti"来自"optimal（最佳的）"，所以optimism就是相信最好的结果会发生。
