# 📝 context

🔊 /ˈkɒntɛkst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The circumstances or setting in which an event occurs; the parts of a text surrounding a word that clarify its meaning
**中文：** n. 环境；上下文；来龙去脉；文章前后关系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- background（背景）
- setting（环境）
- framework（框架）
- circumstance（情况）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in context / out of context / historical context / cultural context / within the context of**

👉 Example: The professor urged students to analyze the passage within its broader historical context.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Without proper **context**, the senator's remarks were widely misinterpreted by both the media and the public.
   没有适当的上下文，这位参议员的言论被媒体和公众广泛误解。

2. The archaeological findings can only be understood within the **context** of the region's ancient trade networks.
   这些考古发现只有在该地区古代贸易网络的背景下才能得到理解。

3. Scholars must examine literary works in their original cultural **context** to avoid imposing modern biases on the text.
   学者必须在作品原有的文化环境中研究文学作品，以避免将现代偏见强加于文本之上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-text" as "with the text" — context is the information that comes with the text to help you understand it.
把"context"想成"con（一起）+ text（文本）"——上下文就是与文本一起出现的、帮助你理解它的信息。
