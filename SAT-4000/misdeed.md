# ⚖️ misdeed

🔊 /ˌmɪsˈdiːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A wicked or illegal act; a crime or wrongful action
**中文：** n. 罪行；犯罪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- offense（罪行）
- transgression（违法行为）
- wrongdoing（不法行为）
- crime（犯罪）
- sin（罪过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **past misdeeds / punished for misdeeds / commit a misdeed / conceal one's misdeeds**

👉 Example: The politician's past misdeeds were exposed by investigative journalists during the election campaign.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed a pattern of corporate **misdeeds** that had gone undetected by regulatory agencies for over a decade.
   调查揭示了一种企业罪行模式，十多年来一直未被监管机构发现。

2. The memoir candidly addresses the author's youthful **misdeeds** and the long journey toward personal redemption and moral reform.
   这本回忆录坦诚地讲述了作者年轻时的过错以及走向个人救赎和道德改过的漫长旅程。

3. Citizens demanded that elected officials be held accountable for their **misdeeds** rather than shielded by institutional privilege and power.
   公民要求民选官员为其罪行承担责任，而不是受到制度特权和权力的庇护。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-deed" — "mis" means wrong, "deed" means an action. A misdeed is literally a wrong deed, a bad action.
把"misdeed"拆成"mis（错误的）+ deed（行为）"——字面意思就是错误的行为，即罪行。
