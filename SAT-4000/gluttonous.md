# 🍖 gluttonous

🔊 /ˈɡlʌtənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Excessively greedy in eating or consuming; having or showing a tendency toward overindulgence
**中文：** adj. 贪吃的，暴食的；饕餮的；贪婪的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- voracious（贪吃的）
- ravenous（饥饿的）
- insatiable（贪得无厌的）
- greedy（贪婪的）
- piggish（猪一般贪吃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gluttonous appetite / gluttonous behavior / gluttonous consumption / gluttonous feast**

👉 Example: The documentary criticized the gluttonous consumption habits of modern industrialized societies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Roman banquets were famously **gluttonous** affairs where guests consumed enormous quantities of food over many hours.
   罗马宴会以其饕餮盛况闻名，宾客们在数小时内消耗大量食物。

2. Critics described the corporation's **gluttonous** acquisition strategy as unsustainable and harmful to smaller competitors in the market.
   批评者称该公司贪婪的收购策略不可持续，并对市场上较小的竞争者造成了伤害。

3. The medieval tale warned that **gluttonous** behavior would inevitably lead to both physical illness and moral degradation.
   这个中世纪故事警告说，暴食行为将不可避免地导致身体疾病和道德堕落。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gluttonous" as "glutton + ous" — a glutton is someone who eats too much, so gluttonous describes that excessive, greedy quality.
把"gluttonous"想成"glutton（贪吃者）+ ous"——glutton是吃太多的人，所以gluttonous形容那种过度贪吃的特质。
