# :droplet: replenish

:speaker: /rɪˈplɛnɪʃ/<br>
:label: *verb | 动词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fill or make complete again; to restore a supply to its former level or condition
**中文：** v. 补充；再装满；重新供应

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- refill（重新装满）
- restock（补货）
- restore（恢复）
- reprovision（重新供给）
- reload（重新装载）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **replenish supplies / replenish stocks / replenish energy / replenish resources**

:point_right: Example: The relief organization worked around the clock to replenish food and medical supplies in the disaster zone.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The environmental agency launched a comprehensive initiative to **replenish** the region's depleted groundwater reserves through sustainable management.
   环境机构启动了一项综合计划，通过可持续管理来补充该地区枯竭的地下水储备。

2. After the prolonged drought, seasonal rains finally began to **replenish** the reservoirs that supplied water to surrounding agricultural communities.
   长期干旱之后，季节性降雨终于开始补充为周围农业社区供水的水库。

3. The company must **replenish** its workforce with skilled professionals to maintain competitive advantage in the rapidly evolving technology sector.
   公司必须补充技术熟练的专业人才，以在快速发展的科技行业中保持竞争优势。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "replenish" as "re-PLEN-ish" — "plen" comes from "plenty," so to replenish is to make plenty again, to fill back up.
把"replenish"想成"re-plen-ish"——"plen"来自"plenty（充足）"，所以replenish就是重新变得充足，再次填满。
