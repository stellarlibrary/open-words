# 🦠 ciliate

🔊 /ˈsɪlieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A single-celled organism bearing cilia used for locomotion and feeding. (adj.) Having cilia or hair-like projections
**中文：** n. 纤毛虫 adj. 有纤毛的；有睫毛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- protozoan（原生动物）
- microorganism（微生物）
- ciliated（有纤毛的）
- hair-covered（被毛发覆盖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ciliate organism / ciliate protozoan / ciliate species / ciliate surface**

👉 Example: Paramecium is a well-known ciliate found abundantly in freshwater environments.

---

1. The biology textbook devoted an entire chapter to the **ciliate**, highlighting Paramecium as a representative example.
   生物学教科书用整整一章介绍纤毛虫，以草履虫作为代表性例子。

2. Researchers discovered a previously unknown **ciliate** species thriving in the extreme conditions of deep-sea hydrothermal vents.
   研究人员发现了一种此前未知的纤毛虫物种，它在深海热液喷口的极端条件下繁衍生息。

3. The **ciliate** surface of the epithelial tissue enables efficient transport of mucus through the respiratory system.
   上皮组织的纤毛表面使黏液能够在呼吸系统中高效运输。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ciliate" as "cilia" + "-ate" (having) — a ciliate is an organism that has cilia, like a tiny creature covered in waving hairs.
把"ciliate"想成"cilia"（纤毛）+"-ate"（具有的）——纤毛虫就是拥有纤毛的生物，像一个浑身覆盖着摆动毛发的微小生物。
