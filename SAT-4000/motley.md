# 🎨 motley

🔊 /ˈmɑːtli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Made up of incongruously diverse elements; having varied colors. (n.) A heterogeneous mixture; a garment of mixed colors worn by a jester
**中文：** adj. 杂色的；混杂的；五颜六色的 n. 混杂；杂色衣服；小丑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assorted（各种各样的）
- heterogeneous（混杂的）
- varied（多样的）
- diverse（多种多样的）
- miscellaneous（混杂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a motley crew / a motley collection / a motley assortment / a motley group**

👉 Example: The expedition was led by a motley crew of adventurers from vastly different backgrounds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor addressed a **motley** assembly of students, journalists, and local activists gathered in the auditorium.
   教授向聚集在礼堂里的一群由学生、记者和当地活动人士组成的混杂听众发表讲话。

2. A **motley** collection of antique furniture and modern art pieces filled the eccentric collector's sprawling estate.
   各式各样的古董家具和现代艺术品填满了这位古怪收藏家的庞大庄园。

3. The marketplace offered a **motley** array of goods ranging from handcrafted textiles to imported electronic devices.
   市场上提供了从手工纺织品到进口电子设备的五花八门的商品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "motley" as "MOT-ley" — like a "MOTh" with colorful, patterned wings, representing a mix of many colors and types.
把"motley"想成"MOT-ley"——像一只翅膀五彩斑斓的"飞蛾(moth)"，代表多种颜色和类型的混合。
