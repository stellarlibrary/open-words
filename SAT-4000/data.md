# 📊 data

🔊 /ˈdeɪtə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Facts, statistics, or pieces of information collected for reference or analysis
**中文：** n. 数据；资料

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- information（信息）
- statistics（统计数据）
- evidence（证据）
- figures（数字）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **data analysis / data collection / raw data / data set / empirical data**

👉 Example: The research team spent months gathering data before drawing any preliminary conclusions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientists analyzed extensive **data** from satellite imagery to track deforestation rates across the Amazon basin.
   科学家们分析了来自卫星图像的大量数据，以追踪亚马逊流域的森林砍伐速度。

2. Reliable **data** is essential for policymakers who must make informed decisions about public health initiatives.
   可靠的数据对于必须就公共卫生举措做出明智决策的政策制定者至关重要。

3. The survey yielded compelling **data** that contradicted several long-standing assumptions within the field of behavioral psychology.
   该调查产生了令人信服的数据，反驳了行为心理学领域中几个长期存在的假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "data" as coming from the Latin "datum" meaning "something given" — data is what is given to you as facts to work with.
把"data"想成源自拉丁语"datum"，意为"给予之物"——数据就是作为事实提供给你的信息。
