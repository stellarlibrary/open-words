# 😴 idle

🔊 /ˈaɪdəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not active or in use; lazy or without purpose. (v.) To spend time doing nothing; to waste time
**中文：** adj. 空闲的；闲置的；懒散的 v. 空费；虚度；无所事事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inactive（不活跃的）
- dormant（休眠的）
- sluggish（懒散的）
- loaf（闲逛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **idle time / idle hands / idle chatter / sit idle / idle away the hours**

👉 Example: The factory machines sat idle for months during the prolonged economic downturn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee warned that allowing expensive laboratory equipment to sit **idle** represented a significant waste of institutional resources.
   委员会警告说，让昂贵的实验室设备闲置是对机构资源的重大浪费。

2. Rather than **idle** away the summer, the ambitious student enrolled in an intensive research program at the university.
   这位有抱负的学生没有虚度暑假，而是报名参加了大学的密集研究项目。

3. Critics dismissed the proposal as nothing more than **idle** speculation unsupported by empirical evidence or rigorous analysis.
   批评者认为该提议不过是缺乏实证支持和严格分析的空洞猜测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "idle" as an engine idling — running but going nowhere, doing nothing productive.
把"idle"想象成汽车怠速——引擎在转但车没有前进，什么有成效的事也没做。
