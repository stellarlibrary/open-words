# 🩺 ailment

🔊 /ˈeɪl.mənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A minor illness or chronic condition that causes discomfort; a slight but persistent disorder.
**中文：** 小病；不安

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- malady（疾病）
- affliction（痛苦）
- complaint（不适）
- disorder（失调）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **common ailment, minor ailment, chronic ailment, treat an ailment**
👉 Example: The physician recommended rest as the best remedy for such a minor ailment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many common **ailments** can be effectively prevented through regular exercise and a balanced nutritional diet.
   许多常见的小病可以通过定期锻炼和均衡的营养饮食来有效预防。

2. The persistent **ailment** forced the senator to withdraw from public engagements for the remainder of the term.
   持续的病痛迫使这位参议员在任期余下时间退出公共活动。

3. Traditional herbal remedies have been used for centuries to treat various **ailments** in rural communities worldwide.
   传统草药疗法几个世纪以来一直被用于治疗世界各地农村社区的各种疾病。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ailment" comes from "ail" (to suffer) + "-ment" — something that makes you ail, a small suffering or sickness.
"Ailment"来自"ail"（受苦）+"-ment"——让你不舒服的东西，即小病小痛。
