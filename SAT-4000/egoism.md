# 🏴 egoism

🔊 /ˈiːɡoʊɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The ethical theory or practice of placing one's own self-interest as the foundation of morality; excessive concern with oneself
**中文：** n. 利己主义；自我主义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- self-interest（利己）
- selfishness（自私）
- narcissism（自恋）
- self-regard（自我关注）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ethical egoism / psychological egoism / egoism versus altruism / rational egoism**

👉 Example: The philosopher distinguished between ethical egoism and mere selfishness in his treatise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's lecture on **egoism** explored whether rational self-interest could serve as a viable foundation for moral theory.
   教授关于利己主义的讲座探讨了理性自利是否可以作为道德理论的可行基础。

2. Critics of unchecked **egoism** argue that societies function best when individuals balance personal ambition with collective responsibility.
   不受约束的利己主义的批评者认为，当个人平衡个人抱负与集体责任时，社会运转得最好。

3. The novel portrays the protagonist's gradual transformation from ruthless **egoism** to a compassionate concern for others.
   这部小说描绘了主人公从无情的利己主义逐渐转变为对他人的同情关怀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EGO-ism" — it is the "-ism" or philosophy of the "ego"; egoism means making your ego the guiding principle.
把"egoism"想成"EGO-ism"——这是关于"ego"（自我）的"-ism"（主义）；egoism意为以自我为指导原则。
