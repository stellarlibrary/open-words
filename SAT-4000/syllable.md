# 🗣️ syllable

🔊 /ˈsɪləbl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A unit of pronunciation having one vowel sound. (v.) To divide into or pronounce by syllables
**中文：** n. 音节

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- phoneme（音素）
- unit of sound（语音单位）
- articulation（发音）
- utterance（话语）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stressed syllable / unstressed syllable / count the syllables / not a syllable of truth / in words of one syllable**

👉 Example: The teacher asked students to identify the stressed syllable in each multisyllabic word on the worksheet.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Linguists carefully analyze the distribution of stressed and unstressed **syllables** to determine the rhythmic patterns of a given language.
   语言学家仔细分析重读和非重读音节的分布，以确定某种语言的节奏模式。

2. The poet deliberately chose words with fewer **syllables** to create a sense of urgency and directness throughout the composition.
   诗人刻意选择音节较少的词语，以在整个作品中营造紧迫感和直接感。

3. Young learners often struggle to pronounce every **syllable** correctly when first encountering unfamiliar multisyllabic vocabulary terms.
   幼小的学习者在初次接触不熟悉的多音节词汇时，常常难以正确发出每个音节。
