# 🎭 alias

🔊 /ˈeɪ.li.əs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adverb | 名词，副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A false or assumed name used to conceal one's true identity; otherwise known as.
**中文：** 别名，化名；别名叫，又名。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pseudonym（笔名，假名）
- assumed name（化名）
- moniker（绰号）
- nom de guerre（化名）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **go by an alias, use an alias, known under an alias, also known as (a.k.a.)**
👉 Example: The suspect was operating under an alias to evade law enforcement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The fugitive adopted an **alias** and relocated to a small coastal town to avoid detection.
   那名逃犯使用了化名，搬到了一个海滨小镇以躲避追踪。

2. The author published her controversial novels under an **alias** to protect her professional reputation.
   这位作家用化名出版了她那些有争议的小说，以保护自己的职业声誉。

3. The intelligence agent was known by several **aliases** throughout his distinguished career in espionage.
   这位情报人员在其杰出的间谍生涯中使用过多个化名。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a lie + as" — an alias is like living "as a lie," pretending to be someone else.
联想"a lie（谎言）+ as（作为）"——化名就像"以谎言身份"生活，假装是另一个人。
