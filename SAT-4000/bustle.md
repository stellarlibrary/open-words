# 🏃 bustle

🔊 /ˈbʌsəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To move in a hurried and busy manner; to be full of energetic activity. (n.) Excited and noisy activity; a rush of movement
**中文：** vi. 匆匆忙忙；喧闹；忙乱 n. 忙乱；喧嚣；活跃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hustle（忙碌）
- hurry（匆忙）
- commotion（骚动）
- flurry（忙乱）
- stir（活动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bustle with activity / bustle about / hustle and bustle / bustle of the city**

👉 Example: The marketplace bustled with merchants and shoppers negotiating prices in the early morning hours.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The grand hotel lobby **bustled** with delegates arriving from around the world for the annual international trade summit.
   大酒店的大堂里挤满了从世界各地赶来参加年度国际贸易峰会的代表们。

2. She could barely concentrate on her research amid the constant **bustle** of the overcrowded university library during finals.
   在期末考试期间拥挤不堪的大学图书馆里，她几乎无法在持续不断的喧嚣中集中精力做研究。

3. Workers **bustled** about the construction site with remarkable efficiency, determined to complete the project ahead of the deadline.
   工人们在工地上忙碌不停，效率惊人，决心要在截止日期前完成项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bustle" as "busy + hustle" — when you bustle, you are busy hustling around in a hurried, energetic way.
把"bustle"想成"busy"（忙碌）+ "hustle"（奔忙）——当你bustle时，你正在忙碌地匆匆奔走。
