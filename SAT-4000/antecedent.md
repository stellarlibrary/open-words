# 🔙 antecedent

🔊 /ˌæn.tɪˈsiː.dənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A thing or event that existed before or logically precedes another; a predecessor or ancestor; in grammar, the word to which a pronoun refers. (adj.) Preceding in time or order; prior.

**中文：** (n.) 前情；先行词；祖先；前事。(adj.) 先行的；先前的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- predecessor（前任）
- forerunner（先驱）
- precursor（先兆）
- ancestor（祖先）
- prior（先前的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antecedent event, antecedent condition, grammatical antecedent, historical antecedent

**Example:** Understanding the **antecedent** causes of the conflict is essential for any meaningful peace negotiation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian traced the **antecedents** of the revolution to a series of economic crises in the preceding decade.
   历史学家将这场革命的前因追溯到前十年的一系列经济危机。

2. In the sentence "The scientist published her findings," the noun "scientist" serves as the **antecedent** of "her."
   在句子"The scientist published her findings"中，名词"scientist"是"her"的先行词。

3. The committee examined all **antecedent** agreements before drafting the terms of the new international treaty.
   委员会在起草新国际条约条款之前审查了所有先前的协议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Ante-" (before) + "-ced-" (go) + "-ent" (one who) = "the one that goes before." Picture an ancestor walking ahead on a timeline. 前缀 ante-（之前）+ 词根 -ced-（走）+ 后缀 -ent（…的人/物）= "走在前面的事物"。想象祖先在时间线上走在前面。
