# 🙏 entreaty

🔊 /ɪnˈtriːti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An earnest or humble request; a sincere and urgent plea for something
**中文：** n. 恳求；乞求

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plea（恳求）
- supplication（祈求）
- petition（请愿）
- appeal（呼吁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make an entreaty / heartfelt entreaty / ignore an entreaty / entreaty for mercy / desperate entreaty**

👉 Example: Despite repeated entreaties from the villagers, the authorities refused to reverse the demolition order.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's passionate **entreaty** for peace moved the delegates and prompted renewed negotiations between the warring factions.
   大使对和平的热切恳求感动了代表们，并促成了交战各方重新展开谈判。

2. She made a tearful **entreaty** to the judge, asking for leniency given the extraordinary circumstances surrounding her case.
   她含泪向法官恳求，请求鉴于其案件的特殊情况予以从轻处理。

3. The humanitarian organization issued a public **entreaty** for donations to assist communities devastated by the catastrophic earthquake.
   该人道主义组织发出了公开恳求，呼吁捐款以援助遭受灾难性地震破坏的社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-treaty" — like entering into a "treaty" by begging; an entreaty is a desperate plea to reach an agreement.
把"entreaty"想成"en（进入）+ treaty（条约）"——通过恳求来达成协议，即恳求、乞求。
