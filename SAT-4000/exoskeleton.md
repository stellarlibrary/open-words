# 🦀 exoskeleton

🔊 /ˌeksoʊˈskelɪtn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A rigid external covering or structure that provides support and protection for the body of an organism, such as insects or crustaceans
**中文：** n. 外骨骼（为昆虫或甲壳类动物等提供支撑和保护的坚硬外部结构）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outer shell（外壳）
- carapace（甲壳）
- cuticle（角质层）
- protective covering（保护层）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chitinous exoskeleton / shed an exoskeleton / robotic exoskeleton / exoskeleton of an insect**

👉 Example: The crab must periodically shed its exoskeleton in order to grow larger through a process called molting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Arthropods rely on a hardened **exoskeleton** composed primarily of chitin to shield their internal organs from environmental threats.
   节肢动物依靠主要由几丁质构成的坚硬外骨骼来保护内脏免受环境威胁。

2. Engineers have developed a wearable robotic **exoskeleton** that enables patients with spinal injuries to walk independently again.
   工程师们开发了一种可穿戴的机器人外骨骼，使脊柱损伤患者能够重新独立行走。

3. During the molting process, the organism discards its old **exoskeleton** and gradually forms a new, larger protective layer.
   在蜕皮过程中，生物体丢弃旧的外骨骼，并逐渐形成新的、更大的保护层。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exo-skeleton" — "exo" means outside, so an exoskeleton is literally a skeleton on the outside of the body.
把"exoskeleton"想成"exo-skeleton"——"exo"意为外部，所以exoskeleton就是长在身体外面的骨骼。
