# 🦁 dauntless

🔊 /ˈdɔːntləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing fearlessness and determination; impossible to intimidate or discourage
**中文：** adj. 无畏的；勇敢的；不屈不挠的；大胆的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fearless（无畏的）
- intrepid（勇敢的）
- undaunted（不屈不挠的）
- valiant（英勇的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dauntless spirit / dauntless courage / dauntless leader / dauntless determination**

👉 Example: Her dauntless spirit inspired the entire team to press forward despite overwhelming obstacles.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **dauntless** explorer ventured deep into uncharted territory, undeterred by the perils that awaited her expedition.
   这位无畏的探险家深入未知领域，不为等待着她探险队的危险所吓倒。

2. Throughout history, **dauntless** reformers have challenged oppressive systems even at great personal risk and sacrifice.
   纵观历史，不屈不挠的改革者们即使冒着巨大的个人风险和牺牲也要挑战压迫性制度。

3. The **dauntless** resolve of the rescue team saved dozens of lives during the catastrophic earthquake aftermath.
   救援队无畏的决心在灾难性地震后的救援中拯救了数十条生命。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dauntless" as "daunt + less" — having less fear, someone who cannot be daunted or intimidated by anything.
把"dauntless"想成"daunt（使畏缩）+ less（更少）"——更少恐惧的人，不会被任何事吓倒。
