# 📕 abridgement

🔊 /əˈbrɪdʒmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A shortened or condensed form of a book, speech, or other text; the act of reducing or curtailing
**中文：** n. 节略，缩写；删节本；减少

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- summary（摘要）
- condensation（压缩）
- synopsis（概要）
- digest（文摘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an abridgement of / publish an abridgement / read the abridgement / abridgement of rights**

👉 Example: The library carried both the original text and its popular abridgement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. This **abridgement** of the historical treatise retains all the essential arguments of the original work.
   这部历史论著的删节本保留了原作所有核心论点。

2. Scholars often debate whether an **abridgement** can truly represent the author's complete artistic vision.
   学者们经常争论删节本是否能真正代表作者完整的艺术构想。

3. The **abridgement** reduced the five-volume encyclopedia into a single comprehensive reference book.
   该节略本将五卷百科全书精简为一本综合参考书。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abridgement" is simply "abridge" + "-ment" (the result noun). The abridgement is what you get after you abridge something — a shorter version.
"Abridgement"就是"abridge"加上"-ment"（结果名词后缀）。Abridgement就是你abridge之后得到的东西——一个缩短版。
