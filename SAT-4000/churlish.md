# 😤 churlish

🔊 /ˈtʃɜːrlɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Rude, ill-mannered, or bad-tempered; lacking politeness or civility
**中文：** adj. 没有礼貌的；脾气暴躁的；粗野的；无礼的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boorish（粗鲁的）
- surly（无礼傲慢的）
- uncouth（粗俗的）
- ill-mannered（没有礼貌的）
- rude（粗鲁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **churlish behavior / churlish remark / churlish refusal / it would be churlish to...**

👉 Example: It would be churlish to refuse such a generous offer of help from a colleague.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **churlish** response to the invitation embarrassed the entire diplomatic delegation at the conference.
   大使对邀请的粗鲁回应让整个外交代表团在会议上感到尴尬。

2. It would be **churlish** to dismiss the volunteers' efforts, given the considerable time they devoted to the project.
   鉴于志愿者们为该项目投入了大量时间，忽视他们的努力未免太无礼了。

3. His **churlish** demeanor at the formal dinner alienated potential business partners who had traveled from abroad.
   他在正式晚宴上粗野的举止疏远了从国外远道而来的潜在商业伙伴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a "churl" — an old English word for a rude peasant. A churlish person behaves like a churl, lacking manners and grace.
"churl"是古英语中指粗鲁农夫的词。churlish的人就像一个没有教养的粗人，缺乏礼貌和风度。
