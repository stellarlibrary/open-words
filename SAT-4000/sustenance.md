# 🍞 sustenance

🔊 /ˈsʌstənəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Food; livelihood; support
**中文：** n. 食物，营养；支持，维持，扶持；生计

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nourishment（营养）
- sustentation（维持）
- livelihood（生计）
- provisions（食物供给）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **provide sustenance / derive sustenance from / sustenance for the body / means of sustenance**

👉 Example: The remote village relied on fishing as its primary source of sustenance throughout the harsh winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The humanitarian organization delivered essential **sustenance** to displaced families living in temporary shelters along the border.
   人道主义组织向居住在边境临时收容所的流离失所家庭提供了基本食物。

2. Scholars have long debated whether art provides genuine spiritual **sustenance** or merely serves as a pleasant diversion.
   学者们长期以来一直争论艺术是否提供真正的精神支持，还是仅仅作为一种愉快的消遣。

3. Without adequate **sustenance**, the explorers found their physical endurance rapidly diminishing as they crossed the arid desert.
   由于缺乏足够的食物，探险者们在穿越干旱沙漠时发现体力迅速下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sustenance" as "sustain + ance" — it is what sustains you, the food and support that keeps you going.
把"sustenance"想成"sustain（维持）+ ance"——它就是维持你生命的东西，即食物和支持。
