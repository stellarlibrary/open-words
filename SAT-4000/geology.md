# 🪨 geology

🔊 /dʒiˈɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The scientific study of the Earth's physical structure, substance, history, and the processes that act upon it
**中文：** n. 地质学；地质情况

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- earth science（地球科学）
- geoscience（地质科学）
- geomorphology（地貌学）
- mineralogy（矿物学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study geology / geology of a region / geological survey / geology department / marine geology**

👉 Example: The geology of the Grand Canyon reveals millions of years of Earth's complex and fascinating history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **geology** of the region indicates that massive volcanic activity shaped the landscape over several million years of transformation.
   该地区的地质情况表明，大规模火山活动在数百万年的变迁中塑造了这片地貌。

2. Students pursuing degrees in **geology** often conduct extensive fieldwork to collect rock samples and analyze sedimentary formations firsthand.
   攻读地质学学位的学生经常进行大量实地考察，亲自收集岩石样本并分析沉积构造。

3. Understanding the **geology** beneath urban areas is critical for engineers designing foundations for skyscrapers and underground infrastructure systems.
   了解城市地区地下的地质状况对于设计摩天大楼和地下基础设施系统的工程师至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "geology" as "geo" (earth) + "logy" (study of) — it is literally the study of the earth, its rocks, and its structure.
把"geology"拆成"geo"（地球）+ "logy"（研究）——它字面意思就是对地球、岩石和结构的研究。
