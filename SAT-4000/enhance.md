# ✨ enhance

🔊 /ɪnˈhæns/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To intensify, increase, or improve the quality, value, or extent of something
**中文：** v. 提高；加强；增强；改善

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- improve（改善）
- augment（增强）
- elevate（提升）
- amplify（放大）
- boost（促进）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enhance performance / enhance quality / enhance understanding / enhance the experience**

👉 Example: The new software features were designed to enhance user productivity and overall workflow efficiency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university invested in advanced laboratory equipment to **enhance** the quality of scientific research conducted by graduate students.
   大学投资了先进的实验室设备，以提高研究生进行的科学研究的质量。

2. Strategic partnerships with international firms significantly **enhanced** the company's ability to compete in the rapidly evolving global marketplace.
   与国际公司的战略合作伙伴关系显著增强了该公司在快速变化的全球市场中的竞争能力。

3. The restoration project aimed to **enhance** the historic building's structural integrity while preserving its original architectural character and charm.
   修复项目旨在增强这座历史建筑的结构完整性，同时保留其原有的建筑特色和魅力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "enhance" as "en-HANCE" — to advance or heighten something, making it better than it was before.
把"enhance"想成"en-HANCE"——推进或提升某事物，使其比以前更好。
