# 🐍 insidious

🔊 /ɪnˈsɪdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Proceeding in a gradual, subtle way, but with harmful effects; treacherous and cunning
**中文：** adj. 阴险的；隐伏的；暗中为害的；狡猾的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- treacherous（阴险的）
- cunning（狡猾的）
- stealthy（隐秘的）
- deceptive（欺骗性的）
- pernicious（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insidious threat / insidious disease / insidious influence / insidious effects**

👉 Example: The insidious spread of misinformation undermined public trust in credible institutions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **insidious** nature of the disease made early detection extremely difficult for even experienced physicians.
   这种疾病的隐伏特性使得即使是经验丰富的医生也极难进行早期诊断。

2. Critics warned that the **insidious** erosion of civil liberties could permanently alter the fabric of democratic governance.
   批评人士警告说，公民自由的暗中侵蚀可能会永久改变民主治理的结构。

3. The propaganda campaign had an **insidious** effect on public opinion, gradually shifting attitudes without people realizing it.
   这场宣传运动对公众舆论产生了潜移默化的影响，在人们不知不觉中逐渐改变了态度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insidious" as "inside + hideous" — something hideous hiding inside, working harm from within where you cannot see it.
把"insidious"想成"inside + hideous"——某种可怕的东西隐藏在内部，在你看不见的地方暗中为害。
