# 😨 pusillanimous

🔊 /ˌpjuːsɪˈlænɪməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing a lack of courage or determination; timid and cowardly; irresolute in the face of difficulty
**中文：** adj. 胆怯的；懦弱的；优柔寡断的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cowardly（懦弱的）
- timid（胆小的）
- fainthearted（怯懦的）
- spineless（没骨气的）
- craven（怯懦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pusillanimous behavior / pusillanimous leader / pusillanimous response / pusillanimous attitude**

👉 Example: The editorial condemned the legislature's pusillanimous refusal to confront the crisis head-on.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The general dismissed the negotiator's **pusillanimous** stance, arguing that appeasement would only embolden the adversary further.
   将军驳斥了谈判者懦弱的立场，认为妥协只会进一步助长对手的气焰。

2. Critics described the administration's response to the humanitarian crisis as **pusillanimous** and morally indefensible.
   批评者将政府对人道主义危机的回应描述为懦弱且在道德上站不住脚的。

3. A **pusillanimous** leader who avoids difficult decisions will inevitably lose the confidence and respect of the electorate.
   一个回避艰难决定的懦弱领导者将不可避免地失去选民的信心和尊重。
