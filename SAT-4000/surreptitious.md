# 🕵️ surreptitious

🔊 /ˌsɜːrəpˈtɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done secretly or stealthily; kept hidden from others; obtained or accomplished through clandestine means
**中文：** adj. 暗中的，秘密的，偷偷摸摸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clandestine（秘密的）
- covert（隐蔽的）
- furtive（鬼鬼祟祟的）
- stealthy（偷偷的）
- underhand（暗中的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surreptitious glance / surreptitious manner / surreptitious entry / surreptitious recording**

👉 Example: She cast a surreptitious glance at the confidential document lying open on her supervisor's desk.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist gained **surreptitious** access to the classified files, which ultimately exposed widespread corruption within the government agency.
   记者秘密获取了机密文件，最终揭露了该政府机构内部的大范围腐败。

2. He made a **surreptitious** attempt to leave the meeting early, hoping that no one would notice his quiet departure from the room.
   他偷偷地试图早退会议，希望没有人会注意到他悄悄离开房间。

3. The **surreptitious** surveillance operation conducted by the intelligence agency continued for months before any suspects were formally identified.
   情报机构进行的秘密监视行动持续了数月，之后才正式确认了任何嫌疑人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surreptitious" as containing "reptile" — like a reptile that creeps silently and stealthily, surreptitious actions are done in a sneaky, hidden way.
把"surreptitious"想成包含"reptile（爬行动物）"——就像爬行动物悄无声息地潜行一样，surreptitious的行为是偷偷摸摸、隐秘进行的。
