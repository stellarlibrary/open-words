# 🌊 bight

🔊 /baɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A wide, gentle curve in a coastline forming an open bay; a loop or slack part of a rope
**中文：** 海岸线的宽缓弯曲；海湾；绳圈；曲线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bay（海湾）
- cove（小湾）
- inlet（入口湾）
- loop（绳圈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a bight of rope / the Great Australian Bight / coastal bight / wide bight**

👉 Example: The fleet sought shelter in the bight during the violent storm that swept across the open sea.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Great Australian **Bight** stretches along the southern coastline and is renowned for its dramatic cliffs and marine biodiversity.
   大澳大利亚湾沿南部海岸线延伸，以其壮观的悬崖和海洋生物多样性而闻名。

2. The sailor carefully coiled a **bight** of rope on the deck to prevent it from tangling during the voyage.
   水手小心地将一圈绳索盘绕在甲板上，以防航行中缠结。

3. Geographers classified the shallow **bight** as a distinct marine habitat worthy of conservation and further scientific study.
   地理学家将这片浅海湾归类为值得保护和进一步科学研究的独特海洋栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bight" as a "bite" taken out of the coastline — a curved indentation where the land curves inward like a bay.
把"bight"想象成海岸线被"咬"了一口——陆地向内弯曲形成的弧形凹陷，就像一个海湾。
