# 🔥 zealot

🔊 /ˈzɛlət/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of religious, political, or ideological goals
**中文：** 狂热者；狂热信徒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fanatic（狂热者）
- extremist（极端分子）
- radical（激进分子）
- devotee（虔诚信徒）
- militant（激进者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **religious zealot / political zealot / zealot for a cause / a dangerous zealot**

👉 Example: The party's most vocal zealot refused to consider any form of compromise with the opposing faction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historical account described the rebel leader as a **zealot** whose uncompromising ideology ultimately led to devastating consequences.
   历史记载将这位叛军领袖描述为一个狂热者，其不妥协的意识形态最终导致了毁灭性的后果。

2. Critics warned that the movement had been hijacked by a **zealot** who prioritized ideological purity over practical reform.
   批评者警告说，该运动已被一个狂热者劫持，此人把意识形态纯洁性置于实际改革之上。

3. She was once a moderate advocate, but years of frustration gradually transformed her into an uncompromising **zealot**.
   她曾经是一位温和的倡导者，但多年的挫折逐渐将她变成了一个不妥协的狂热者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "zealot" as "zeal" + "-ot" (a person) — someone completely consumed by zeal, taking enthusiasm to an extreme.
把"zealot"想成"zeal"（热情）+"-ot"（表示人）——一个被热情完全吞噬的人，把热忱推向极端。
