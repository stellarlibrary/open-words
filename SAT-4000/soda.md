# 🥤 soda

🔊 /ˈsoʊdə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Sodium carbonate or bicarbonate; carbonated water or a carbonated soft drink
**中文：** n. 苏打

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carbonated water（碳酸水）
- soft drink（软饮料）
- pop（汽水）
- fizzy drink（起泡饮料）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **baking soda / soda water / soda fountain / soda pop**

👉 Example: The chemist dissolved baking soda in water to create a mild alkaline solution for the experiment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher used **soda** ash as a key reagent in the chemical process for manufacturing industrial glass products.
   研究人员在制造工业玻璃产品的化学过程中使用苏打灰作为关键试剂。

2. Public health officials have recommended reducing **soda** consumption to combat rising rates of obesity and diabetes.
   公共卫生官员建议减少苏打饮料的摄入，以对抗不断上升的肥胖和糖尿病发病率。

3. Baking **soda** serves as an effective and inexpensive cleaning agent for a wide variety of household surfaces.
   小苏打是一种有效且廉价的清洁剂，适用于各种家庭表面。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "soda" as the fizzy bubbles — sodium compounds create the effervescence in carbonated drinks, hence the name soda.
把"soda"想成冒泡的气泡——钠化合物在碳酸饮料中产生气泡，因此得名苏打。
