# 🦁 dominant

🔊 /ˈdɑːmɪnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having power, authority, or influence over others; most prominent or prevailing. (n.) A dominant trait or gene in genetics
**中文：** adj. 显性的；占优势的；支配的 n. 显性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prevailing（盛行的）
- predominant（占主导的）
- commanding（居高临下的）
- superior（优越的）
- paramount（至上的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dominant force / dominant position / dominant role / dominant gene / dominant culture**

👉 Example: English has become the dominant language in international business, diplomacy, and scientific research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **dominant** political party maintained control of the legislature for over two decades through broad coalition-building strategies.
   占主导地位的政党通过广泛的联盟建设策略，在超过二十年的时间里保持了对立法机构的控制。

2. In genetics, a **dominant** allele will express its trait even when paired with a recessive allele in the organism.
   在遗传学中，显性等位基因即使与隐性等位基因配对，也会在生物体中表达其性状。

3. The corporation emerged as the **dominant** player in the global market after a series of strategic acquisitions and mergers.
   该公司在一系列战略收购和合并之后，成为全球市场中占主导地位的参与者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "domin" (lord, master) + "ant" — like an ant that rules the colony, a dominant force is the one that leads and controls.
想象"domin"（主人、统治者）+ "ant"（蚂蚁）——就像统治蚁群的蚂蚁，dominant就是领导和控制一切的力量。
