# ⚖️ obesity

🔊 /oʊˈbiːsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The medical condition of being grossly overweight; excessive accumulation of body fat that poses health risks.
**中文：** n. 肥胖症，过胖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corpulence（肥胖）
- overweight（超重）
- fatness（肥胖）
- plumpness（丰满）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **childhood obesity / obesity epidemic / combat obesity / obesity rate / morbid obesity**

👉 Example: The government launched a national campaign to combat childhood obesity through healthier school meals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Obesity** has become one of the most pressing public health challenges in industrialized nations around the world.
   肥胖症已成为全球工业化国家最紧迫的公共卫生挑战之一。

2. Researchers have identified genetic factors that may predispose certain populations to higher rates of **obesity**.
   研究人员已经确定了可能使某些人群更容易患肥胖症的遗传因素。

3. The correlation between **obesity** and cardiovascular disease has been extensively documented in peer-reviewed medical literature.
   肥胖症与心血管疾病之间的相关性已在同行评审的医学文献中得到广泛记录。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "obesity" as "obese" + "-ity" (a noun suffix) — it is simply the noun form of being obese, the state of extreme overweight.
把"obesity"想成"obese"加上名词后缀"-ity"——它就是obese的名词形式，表示极度肥胖的状态。
