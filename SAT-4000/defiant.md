# 💪 defiant

🔊 /dɪˈfaɪənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Boldly resisting authority or an opposing force; showing open resistance or bold disobedience
**中文：** adj. 挑战的，挑衅的；目中无人的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rebellious（叛逆的）
- insubordinate（不服从的）
- disobedient（不顺从的）
- audacious（大胆的）
- confrontational（对抗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **defiant attitude / defiant stance / remain defiant / defiant gesture**

👉 Example: The protesters took a defiant stance against the government's proposed environmental regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **defiant** student refused to apologize for her remarks, insisting that her criticism of the policy was justified.
   这名挑衅的学生拒绝为自己的言论道歉，坚持认为她对该政策的批评是正当的。

2. Despite facing severe punishment, the activist remained **defiant** and continued to speak out against systemic corruption.
   尽管面临严厉惩罚，这位活动家仍然不屈不挠，继续公开抨击系统性腐败。

3. The general issued a **defiant** response to the enemy's ultimatum, declaring that his forces would never surrender.
   将军对敌方的最后通牒发出了挑战性的回应，宣布他的部队永远不会投降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "defi-ant" as "defy" + "ant" — someone who is like an ant that defy obstacles, boldly standing their ground no matter what.
把"defiant"想成"defy（违抗）+ ant（……的）"——像蚂蚁一样不惧障碍、勇敢对抗的，即挑衅的。
