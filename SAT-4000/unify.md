# 🤝 unify

🔊 /ˈjuːnɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make or become united, uniform, or whole; to bring together into a single entity
**中文：** 统一；使一致；使联合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unite（联合）
- consolidate（巩固）
- merge（合并）
- integrate（整合）
- harmonize（使和谐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unify a nation / unify efforts / unify theories / unify under a common goal**

👉 Example: The charismatic leader managed to unify the deeply divided factions into a single powerful coalition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prime minister sought to **unify** the country by implementing policies that addressed the concerns of every major region.
   首相试图通过实施顾及每个主要地区利益的政策来统一国家。

2. Physicists continue their quest to **unify** the fundamental forces of nature into one comprehensive and elegant theoretical framework.
   物理学家们继续寻求将自然界的基本力统一为一个全面而优雅的理论框架。

3. The new curriculum was designed to **unify** diverse educational standards across all fifty states into a coherent system.
   新课程旨在将五十个州各不相同的教育标准统一为一个连贯的体系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "uni-fy" as "make into one" — the prefix "uni" means one, and "-fy" means to make, so unify means to make things into one.
把"unify"想成"uni（一）+ fy（使成为）"——使成为一体，即统一、使一致。
