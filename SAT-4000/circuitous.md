# 🌀 circuitous

🔊 /sərˈkjuːɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Longer than the most direct way; roundabout; not following a straight or direct course
**中文：** adj. 迂曲的；绕行的；迂回线路的；不直接的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- roundabout（迂回的）
- indirect（间接的）
- meandering（蜿蜒的）
- tortuous（曲折的）
- winding（弯曲的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **circuitous route / circuitous path / circuitous journey / circuitous reasoning**

👉 Example: The politician gave a circuitous answer, carefully avoiding the journalist's direct question.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hikers chose a **circuitous** route through the mountain valley to avoid the dangerously steep cliffs along the ridge.
   徒步旅行者选择了一条穿过山谷的迂回路线，以避开山脊上危险的陡峭悬崖。

2. Her **circuitous** explanation of the policy change left the audience confused about the actual reasons behind the decision.
   她对政策变更的迂回解释让听众对该决定背后的实际原因感到困惑。

3. The river follows a remarkably **circuitous** course through the plains before eventually reaching the coast many miles away.
   这条河在平原上沿着一条极为曲折的路线流淌，最终到达数英里之外的海岸。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circuitous" as "circuit" + "-ous" — like going around a full circuit instead of taking a shortcut; the long way around.
把"circuitous"想成"circuit"（环路）+"-ous"——就像绕完整个环路而不走捷径；走了一条最远的路。
