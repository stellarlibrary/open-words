# 🚧 limitation

🔊 /ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A restriction or constraint; the act of limiting or the state of being limited; a shortcoming or deficiency.
**中文：** n. 限制；限度；极限；局限性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restriction（限制）
- constraint（约束）
- boundary（界限）
- shortcoming（缺陷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **limitation of / impose limitations / overcome limitations / within the limitations of**

👉 Example: Every scientific study must openly acknowledge the limitations of its methodology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers carefully outlined the **limitations** of their study, including the relatively small sample size and geographic scope.
   研究人员仔细阐述了其研究的局限性，包括相对较小的样本量和地理范围。

2. Budgetary **limitations** compelled the university to postpone construction of the new interdisciplinary research center until further funding arrived.
   预算限制迫使大学推迟新建跨学科研究中心的施工，直到获得更多资金。

3. Despite the inherent **limitations** of the current technology, engineers continue to develop innovative solutions for renewable energy storage.
   尽管当前技术存在固有的局限性，工程师们仍在继续开发可再生能源存储的创新解决方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "limit + ation" — the "-ation" turns the verb "limit" into the noun form, meaning the state of being held back.
把"limitation"拆分为"limit（限制）+ ation（名词后缀）"——后缀将动词变为名词，表示被限制的状态。
