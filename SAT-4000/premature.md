# ⏰ premature

🔊 /ˌprɛməˈtʃʊr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Occurring or done before the proper time; too early; born before the full term of gestation
**中文：** adj. 早产的；不成熟的；比预期早的；太早的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- untimely（不合时宜的）
- hasty（仓促的）
- precocious（早熟的）
- precipitate（急促的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **premature birth / premature conclusion / premature aging / premature decision**

👉 Example: It would be premature to draw any conclusions before all the data has been thoroughly analyzed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee warned that any **premature** announcement of the results could undermine public confidence in the investigation.
   委员会警告说，任何过早公布结果的行为都可能削弱公众对调查的信心。

2. Advances in neonatal medicine have significantly improved the survival rates of **premature** infants born before thirty-two weeks.
   新生儿医学的进步大大提高了三十二周前出生的早产婴儿的存活率。

3. Critics argued that the policy was **premature** and lacked sufficient empirical evidence to justify its nationwide implementation.
   批评人士认为该政策不成熟，缺乏足够的实证依据来证明其在全国范围内实施的合理性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-mature" as "before mature" — something that happens before it is fully mature or ready.
把"premature"想成"pre（之前）+ mature（成熟）"——在成熟之前就发生了，即过早的、不成熟的。
