# 🔍 comparison

🔊 /kəmˈpærɪsn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of examining similarities and differences between things; an analogy or illustration drawing parallels
**中文：** n. 比较；对照；比喻；比拟

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contrast（对比）
- analogy（类比）
- correlation（相关性）
- juxtaposition（并列）
- likeness（相似）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **by comparison / in comparison with / draw a comparison / beyond comparison / bear comparison**

👉 Example: The author drew a striking comparison between modern technology and ancient mythology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A careful **comparison** of the two manuscripts revealed significant differences in both language and thematic emphasis.
   对两份手稿的仔细比较揭示了在语言和主题重点方面的显著差异。

2. The critic drew an insightful **comparison** between the novelist's latest work and the existentialist tradition in European literature.
   评论家在这位小说家的最新作品与欧洲文学中的存在主义传统之间做了一个深刻的比较。

3. By **comparison** with previous economic downturns, the current recession has affected a far broader range of industries globally.
   与之前的经济衰退相比，当前的经济萧条在全球范围内影响了更广泛的行业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comparison" as "com- (together) + parison (from par, equal)" — bringing things together to see if they are equal or how they differ.
把"comparison"想成"com-（一起）+ parison（来自par，平等）"——把事物放在一起看它们是否相等或有何不同。
