# 🎭 disguise

🔊 /dɪsˈɡaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To conceal the true nature or appearance of; to pretend to be something else. (n.) A means of altering one's appearance to prevent recognition
**中文：** v. 掩饰；假装；装扮，假扮 n. 伪装；伪装物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conceal（隐藏）
- camouflage（伪装）
- mask（掩盖）
- cloak（遮掩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disguise oneself as / in disguise / disguise the fact that / a blessing in disguise**

👉 Example: The detective wore a clever disguise to infiltrate the criminal organization undetected.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist decided to **disguise** herself as a factory worker to investigate the unsafe labor conditions firsthand.
   这位记者决定伪装成工厂工人，以亲身调查不安全的劳动条件。

2. No amount of rhetoric could **disguise** the fundamental flaws in the proposed economic reform legislation.
   再多的辞藻也无法掩饰这项拟议经济改革法案中的根本缺陷。

3. The apparent setback proved to be a blessing in **disguise**, ultimately strengthening the organization's long-term strategy.
   这次表面上的挫折最终证明是因祸得福，从根本上巩固了该组织的长期战略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-guise" as "dis (remove) + guise (appearance)" — you remove your real appearance and put on a fake one.
把"disguise"想成"dis（去掉）+ guise（外观）"——去掉真实外观，换上伪装的样子。
