# 🦠 flagella

🔊 /fləˈdʒɛlə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Long, whip-like appendages extending from certain cells, used primarily for locomotion; plural of flagellum
**中文：** n. 鞭毛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cilia（纤毛）
- appendage（附肢）
- whip-like structures（鞭状结构）
- locomotor organelles（运动细胞器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bacterial flagella / flagella movement / rotate flagella / flagella propulsion**

👉 Example: The bacterium uses its flagella to propel itself through liquid environments toward nutrient sources.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Under the microscope, the **flagella** of the protozoan rotated rapidly, propelling the organism through the fluid medium.
   在显微镜下，原生动物的鞭毛快速旋转，推动该生物体在液体介质中运动。

2. Researchers discovered that mutations affecting **flagella** assembly significantly impaired the bacterium's ability to colonize host tissue.
   研究人员发现，影响鞭毛组装的突变显著削弱了细菌定殖宿主组织的能力。

3. The structure and function of **flagella** differ markedly between prokaryotic organisms and their eukaryotic counterparts.
   原核生物和真核生物的鞭毛在结构和功能上存在显著差异。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "flagella" as tiny "flags" that wave and whip around — like little flags flapping in the wind, flagella whip back and forth to move a cell.
把"flagella"想成微小的"flags（旗帜）"在挥动——就像小旗子在风中飘动一样，鞭毛来回摆动以推动细胞运动。
