# 😇 guileless

🔊 /ˈɡaɪlləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Devoid of guile; innocent and without deception; frank and honest
**中文：** adj. 诚实的；坦率的；不狡诈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- innocent（天真的）
- candid（坦诚的）
- artless（朴实的）
- sincere（真诚的）
- naive（天真的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **guileless smile / guileless expression / guileless nature / guileless charm**

👉 Example: Her guileless smile immediately put the nervous interviewees at ease during the selection process.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness gave a **guileless** account of the events, and the jury found her testimony entirely credible and trustworthy.
   证人对事件做了坦率的叙述，陪审团认为她的证词完全可信且值得信赖。

2. His **guileless** approach to business negotiations sometimes left him vulnerable to manipulation by more cunning counterparts.
   他在商业谈判中的坦率态度有时使他容易受到更狡猾的对手的操纵。

3. The author depicted the protagonist as a **guileless** youth whose honesty ultimately triumphed over the corruption surrounding him.
   作者将主人公描绘成一个坦率的青年，其诚实最终战胜了周围的腐败。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "guileless" as "guile-less" — without guile, meaning without any cunning or deceit, just pure honesty.
把"guileless"想成"guile-less"——没有狡猾，意味着没有任何诡计或欺骗，只有纯粹的诚实。
