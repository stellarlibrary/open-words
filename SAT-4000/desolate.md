# 🏚️ desolate

🔊 /ˈdɛsələt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deserted, barren, and bleak; feeling lonely and abandoned. (v.) To make a place empty and bleak; to devastate
**中文：** adj. 荒凉的；荒芜的；孤寂的 v. 使荒芜；使荒凉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- barren（荒芜的）
- forsaken（被遗弃的）
- bleak（荒凉的）
- devastate（使荒废）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **desolate landscape / desolate region / feel desolate / desolate wasteland**

👉 Example: The abandoned mining town presented a desolate scene of crumbling structures and empty streets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After the volcanic eruption, the once-thriving village was reduced to a **desolate** wasteland covered in layers of ash.
   火山爆发后，曾经繁荣的村庄变成了一片覆盖着层层火山灰的荒凉废墟。

2. The prolonged civil war had **desolated** entire provinces, displacing millions of civilians and destroying critical infrastructure.
   旷日持久的内战使整个省份变得荒芜，数百万平民流离失所，关键基础设施遭到严重破坏。

3. She felt utterly **desolate** upon learning that her scholarship application had been rejected by every institution.
   得知自己的奖学金申请被所有机构拒绝后，她感到无比孤寂和绝望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-sol-ate" as "the sun (sol) ate everything" — the sun consumed all life, leaving the land desolate.
把"desolate"想成"de-sol-ate"——太阳（sol）吞噬了一切，使大地变得荒芜。
