# 🌳 arbor

🔊 /ˈɑːrbər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A tree, especially as used in a garden or park; a shady garden structure or shelter formed by trees, climbing plants, or a lattice framework
**中文：** n. 乔木；凉亭；藤架

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pergola（藤架）
- bower（凉亭）
- trellis（棚架）
- gazebo（花园凉亭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **garden arbor / rose arbor / sit in an arbor / vine-covered arbor / Arbor Day**

👉 Example: The couple exchanged vows beneath a beautiful rose-covered arbor in the garden.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The children gathered beneath the vine-covered **arbor** to enjoy a peaceful afternoon of reading and quiet conversation.
   孩子们聚集在藤蔓覆盖的凉亭下，享受一个宁静的阅读和轻声交谈的下午。

2. A magnificent wooden **arbor** stood at the entrance to the botanical garden, welcoming visitors with cascading wisteria blossoms.
   一座华丽的木质藤架矗立在植物园入口处，层叠的紫藤花迎接着来访者。

3. The landscape architect designed an elegant **arbor** to provide shade and create a natural focal point in the courtyard.
   景观建筑师设计了一座优雅的凉亭，为庭院提供遮荫并营造一个自然的视觉焦点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Arbor" shares its root with "Arbor Day" — the holiday dedicated to planting trees. Picture a leafy tree forming a natural shelter.
"Arbor"与"Arbor Day"（植树节）共享词根——这是一个致力于植树的节日。想象一棵枝叶茂盛的树形成天然的遮蔽所。
