# ⚡ dominate

🔊 /ˈdɑːmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To have a commanding influence over; to control, govern, or rule; to be the most prominent or important element
**中文：** v. 控制；支配；占优势；统治

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- control（控制）
- govern（统治）
- prevail（占优势）
- overshadow（使黯然失色）
- command（指挥）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dominate the market / dominate the conversation / dominate the landscape / dominate the field**

👉 Example: A few multinational corporations continue to dominate the global pharmaceutical industry with remarkable influence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technology giant continues to **dominate** the smartphone market despite increasing competition from emerging international rivals.
   这家科技巨头尽管面临来自新兴国际竞争对手的日益激烈的竞争，仍然继续主导着智能手机市场。

2. The towering skyscraper **dominates** the city skyline and has become an iconic symbol of modern architectural achievement.
   这座高耸的摩天大楼主导着城市天际线，已成为现代建筑成就的标志性象征。

3. Throughout the debate, the experienced senator managed to **dominate** the discussion by presenting compelling evidence and logical arguments.
   在整个辩论过程中，这位经验丰富的参议员通过提出令人信服的证据和逻辑论点主导了讨论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "domin" (lord) + "ate" (to make) — to dominate is to "make yourself the lord" of a situation, taking full control.
想象"domin"（主人）+ "ate"（使成为）——dominate就是"使自己成为主人"，完全掌控局面。
