# 🌱 progeny

🔊 /ˈprɒdʒəni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The offspring or descendants of a person, animal, or plant; a result or product of something
**中文：** n. 子孙；后裔；成果

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- offspring（后代）
- descendants（子孙）
- posterity（后裔）
- issue（子嗣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **progeny of / intellectual progeny / literary progeny / produce progeny**

👉 Example: The scientist's intellectual progeny continued to advance her research long after her retirement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nobleman ensured that his vast estate would be inherited by his **progeny** for generations to come.
   这位贵族确保他的庞大产业将由他的后代世世代代继承下去。

2. Darwin observed that the **progeny** of organisms with favorable traits were more likely to survive and reproduce.
   达尔文观察到具有有利性状的生物体的后代更有可能存活和繁殖。

3. The philosophical movement produced numerous intellectual **progeny** that shaped modern political thought across the Western world.
   这一哲学运动产生了众多思想成果，塑造了整个西方世界的现代政治思想。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-geny" as "pro-gene" — the genes that move forward through your children and descendants.
把"progeny"想成"pro-gene"——通过你的子孙后代向前传递的基因。
