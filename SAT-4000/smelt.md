# 🔥 smelt

🔊 /smɛlt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To extract metal from ore by heating and melting; to refine. (n.) A small silvery fish
**中文：** v. 冶炼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- refine（精炼）
- forge（锻造）
- melt（熔化）
- extract（提取）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **smelt ore / smelt iron / smelt copper / smelting furnace**

👉 Example: The workers had to smelt the raw ore before it could be shaped into useful metal components.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Ancient civilizations learned to **smelt** copper and tin together, thereby creating bronze for tools and weapons.
   古代文明学会了将铜和锡一起冶炼，从而制造出用于工具和武器的青铜。

2. The factory was built specifically to **smelt** iron ore extracted from the nearby mountain deposits.
   这家工厂是专门为冶炼从附近山区矿床中开采的铁矿石而建造的。

3. Environmental regulations now require facilities that **smelt** metals to install advanced filtration and emission controls.
   环境法规现在要求冶炼金属的设施安装先进的过滤和排放控制装置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "smelt" as "s-melt" — you melt ore in a special process to extract metal, which is smelting.
把"smelt"想成"s-melt"——你在特殊过程中熔化（melt）矿石来提取金属，这就是冶炼。
