# 🕊️ soothe

🔊 /suːð/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To comfort or calm someone; to relieve or ease pain or discomfort
**中文：** v. 安慰，使平静；缓和，减轻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- calm（使平静）
- comfort（安慰）
- alleviate（减轻）
- pacify（使安宁）
- relieve（缓解）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **soothe anxiety / soothe pain / soothe nerves / soothe fears / soothe a child**

👉 Example: The nurse tried to soothe the patient's anxiety before the surgical procedure began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The therapist used calming techniques to **soothe** the patient's overwhelming anxiety before the critical evaluation session began.
   治疗师在关键评估开始前使用安抚技巧来缓解患者强烈的焦虑情绪。

2. Gentle classical music can effectively **soothe** frayed nerves and promote a sense of tranquility in stressful environments.
   轻柔的古典音乐能有效地安抚紧张的神经，并在压力环境中营造一种宁静感。

3. The government issued a statement designed to **soothe** public fears following the unexpected disruption in financial markets.
   政府发表了一份声明，旨在安抚金融市场意外动荡后公众的恐慌情绪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "soothe" as "smooth" — when you soothe someone, you smooth out their worries and make things feel calm again.
把"soothe"想成"smooth"（光滑的）——当你安慰某人时，你抚平了他们的忧虑，让一切恢复平静。
