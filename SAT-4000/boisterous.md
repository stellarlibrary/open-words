# 🎉 boisterous

🔊 /ˈbɔɪstərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Noisy, energetic, and cheerful; rowdy. Also describing rough or stormy weather or seas.
**中文：** adj. 喧闹的；活跃的；狂暴的；猛烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rowdy（喧闹的）
- rambunctious（难以控制的）
- uproarious（喧嚣的）
- tumultuous（骚乱的）
- exuberant（热情奔放的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **boisterous laughter / boisterous crowd / boisterous behavior / boisterous celebration / boisterous wind**

👉 Example: The boisterous crowd erupted in cheers when the team scored the winning goal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **boisterous** celebrations that followed the election victory could be heard reverberating throughout the entire city center.
   选举胜利后的喧闹庆祝活动在整个市中心都能听到回响。

2. Teachers struggled to maintain order in the classroom as the **boisterous** students grew increasingly restless before the holiday break.
   假期前学生们变得越来越躁动不安，老师们努力维持课堂秩序。

3. The **boisterous** winds battered the coastal village relentlessly, toppling fences and scattering debris across every roadway.
   猛烈的大风无情地袭击着沿海村庄，吹倒了栅栏，将碎片散落在每条道路上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "boisterous" as "boy-sterous" — picture a group of rowdy boys making noise and causing a stir everywhere they go, full of uncontainable energy.
把"boisterous"想成"boy-sterous"——想象一群吵闹的男孩到处制造噪音和骚动，精力旺盛到无法控制。
