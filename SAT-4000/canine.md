# 🐕 canine

🔊 /ˈkeɪnaɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or resembling dogs; of the dog family. (n.) A dog or other member of the dog family; a pointed tooth between the incisors and premolars.
**中文：** adj. 犬的；似犬的；犬齿的 n. 犬；犬科动物；犬齿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dog-like（似犬的）
- hound（猎犬）
- pooch（狗）
- lupine（狼似的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **canine companion / canine teeth / canine unit / canine behavior / canine species**

👉 Example: The police deployed a specially trained canine unit to search the building for concealed narcotics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Veterinary researchers have discovered that **canine** cognitive abilities are far more sophisticated than previously assumed by scientists.
   兽医研究人员发现，犬类的认知能力远比科学家先前假设的要复杂得多。

2. The forensic dentist identified the remains by examining the distinctive wear patterns on the upper **canine** teeth.
   法医牙科医生通过检查上犬齿上独特的磨损模式来识别遗骸。

3. Evolutionary biologists study **canine** social structures to understand the origins of cooperative behavior among pack animals.
   进化生物学家研究犬科动物的社会结构，以理解群居动物之间合作行为的起源。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "canine" as containing "can" + "nine" — imagine nine dogs in a can, which is absurd enough to remember that canine means anything related to dogs.
把"canine"想成"can（罐头）+ nine（九）"——想象九只狗在一个罐头里，这个荒谬的画面足以让你记住canine意为与犬有关的。
