# 💎 opulent

🔊 /ˈɑːpjʊlənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Ostentatiously rich and luxurious; wealthy; abundant or plentiful in supply
**中文：** adj. 富裕的；丰富的；大量的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- luxurious（奢华的）
- lavish（铺张的）
- affluent（富裕的）
- sumptuous（豪华的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **opulent lifestyle / opulent surroundings / opulent décor / opulent mansion**

👉 Example: The opulent ballroom featured crystal chandeliers, gilded mirrors, and marble floors throughout.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's **opulent** interior, adorned with Renaissance paintings and golden fixtures, attracted thousands of visitors annually.
   博物馆奢华的内部装饰着文艺复兴时期的画作和金色装置，每年吸引数千名游客。

2. Critics argued that the corporation's **opulent** headquarters stood in stark contrast to the poverty of surrounding communities.
   批评人士认为，该公司富丽堂皇的总部与周围社区的贫困形成了鲜明的对比。

3. The diplomat hosted an **opulent** reception featuring rare wines, exotic cuisine, and live orchestral performances for distinguished guests.
   这位外交官举办了一场奢华的招待会，为尊贵的宾客提供珍稀葡萄酒、异国美食和现场管弦乐表演。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "opulent" as "o-plenty" — when you have plenty of everything, your life is opulent and luxurious.
把"opulent"想成"o-plenty"——当你拥有大量的一切时，你的生活就是opulent（富裕奢华的）。
