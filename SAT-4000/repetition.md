# :repeat: repetition

:speaker: /ˌrɛpɪˈtɪʃən/<br>
:label: *noun | 名词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of repeating something; the recurrence of an action, event, or item; the practice of saying something again for emphasis or clarity
**中文：** n. 重复；反复；背诵；副本

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- recurrence（再现）
- reiteration（重申）
- duplication（复制）
- iteration（迭代）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **avoid repetition / through repetition / repetition of mistakes / constant repetition**

:point_right: Example: Mastery of a musical instrument requires countless hours of disciplined repetition and focused practice.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The professor emphasized that **repetition** of fundamental concepts is essential for deep comprehension and long-term retention of knowledge.
   教授强调，对基本概念的反复学习对于深入理解和长期记忆知识至关重要。

2. The **repetition** of the same structural failures in successive building projects prompted a thorough review of engineering standards.
   连续建筑项目中相同结构故障的重复出现促使人们对工程标准进行全面审查。

3. Critics observed that the author's excessive **repetition** of certain themes weakened the overall impact of the otherwise compelling novel.
   评论家注意到，作者对某些主题的过度重复削弱了这部原本引人入胜的小说的整体影响力。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "repetition" as "re-PETITION" — you petition again and again, repeating your request until it is heard.
把"repetition"想成"re-petition（再次请愿）"——你一再请愿，反复提出请求直到被听到。
