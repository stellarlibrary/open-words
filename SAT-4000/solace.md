# 🕊️ solace

🔊 /ˈsɑːləs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Comfort or consolation in a time of distress or sadness. (v.) To comfort; to soothe; to cheer
**中文：** v. 安慰；平息；抚慰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comfort（安慰）
- consolation（慰藉）
- relief（宽慰）
- succor（救助）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **find solace in / take solace from / offer solace / seek solace**

👉 Example: After the loss, she found solace in the supportive words of her closest friends and family.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many people find **solace** in literature and music during periods of personal grief and emotional hardship.
   许多人在个人悲伤和情感困难时期从文学和音乐中找到慰藉。

2. The counselor sought to **solace** the bereaved family by offering both practical assistance and compassionate guidance.
   咨询师试图通过提供实际帮助和富有同情心的指导来安慰这个失去亲人的家庭。

3. The ancient monastery provided **solace** to weary travelers who sought refuge from the dangers of the open road.
   这座古老的修道院为那些寻求躲避旅途危险的疲惫旅行者提供慰藉。
