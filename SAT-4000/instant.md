# ⚡ instant

🔊 /ˈɪnstənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Happening or produced immediately; urgent and pressing. (n.) A very brief moment in time
**中文：** adj. 立即的；直接的；紧迫的；速溶的 n. 瞬间；即刻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immediate（立即的）
- instantaneous（瞬间的）
- moment（瞬间）
- prompt（迅速的）
- swift（快速的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in an instant / instant access / instant gratification / at that instant / instant reaction**

👉 Example: The new policy took effect in an instant, transforming the entire regulatory landscape overnight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The discovery of the security breach demanded an **instant** response from the entire cybersecurity team to prevent further damage.
   安全漏洞的发现要求整个网络安全团队立即做出响应以防止进一步损害。

2. In that fateful **instant**, the scientist realized that her hypothesis had been confirmed by the experimental results before her.
   在那个决定性的瞬间，这位科学家意识到她的假设已被眼前的实验结果所证实。

3. Modern consumers have grown accustomed to **instant** access to information, fundamentally altering expectations of service delivery.
   现代消费者已经习惯了即时获取信息，从根本上改变了对服务交付的期望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "instant" as "in-an-st(ant)" — something that happens in the time it takes an ant to take a single step.
把"instant"想成"in-an-st(ant)"——在蚂蚁迈出一步的时间内就发生的事情。
