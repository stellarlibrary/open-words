# 🔩 joggle

🔊 /ˈdʒɒɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To shake or move with slight, repeated jerks; to fit together with a notch or joint. (n.) A slight shake; a notched joint
**中文：** v. 轻摇；摇动；啮合 n. 轻摇；啮合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- jiggle（轻轻摇动）
- jostle（推挤）
- shake（摇动）
- nudge（轻推）
- wobble（摇晃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **joggle something loose / joggle a handle / joggle joint / joggle into place**

👉 Example: She had to joggle the old door handle several times before the latch finally released.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer carefully **joggled** the interlocking stones into position to ensure the structural integrity of the ancient wall.
   工程师小心翼翼地将互锁的石块摇动到位，以确保古墙的结构完整性。

2. Passengers were **joggled** back and forth as the train lurched over the uneven sections of the deteriorating track.
   火车在破损轨道的不平路段上颠簸行驶，乘客们被前后摇晃。

3. The technician attempted to **joggle** the loose connector until it finally clicked securely into the designated slot.
   技术人员试图轻摇松动的连接器，直到它最终牢固地卡入指定的插槽。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "joggle" as "jog + wiggle" — a small jogging wiggle, like gently shaking something back and forth.
把"joggle"想成"jog + wiggle"——一种小幅度的慢跑式摆动，就像轻轻地来回摇晃某物。
