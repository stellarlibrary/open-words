# 🏔️ enormous

🔊 /ɪˈnɔːrməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very large in size, quantity, or extent; immense; exceedingly great
**中文：** adj. 庞大的；巨大的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immense（巨大的）
- colossal（庞大的）
- vast（广阔的）
- tremendous（极大的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enormous amount / enormous pressure / enormous impact / enormous potential / enormous challenge**

👉 Example: The project required an enormous investment of both financial resources and skilled personnel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The construction of the new research facility demanded an **enormous** allocation of funding from the federal budget.
   新研究设施的建设需要从联邦预算中拨出巨额资金。

2. Climate change poses an **enormous** threat to biodiversity, potentially driving thousands of vulnerable species toward extinction.
   气候变化对生物多样性构成巨大威胁，可能导致数千个脆弱物种走向灭绝。

3. The scholar's **enormous** contribution to the field of genetics earned her international recognition and numerous prestigious awards.
   这位学者对遗传学领域的巨大贡献为她赢得了国际赞誉和众多权威奖项。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-norm-ous" as "out of the norm" — something enormous goes far beyond what is normal in size or scale.
把"enormous"想成"e（超出）+ norm（正常）+ ous"——巨大的东西远远超出了正常的尺寸或规模。
