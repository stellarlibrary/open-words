# 🩹 abrasion

🔊 /əˈbreɪʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of scraping or wearing away by friction; an area of skin or surface damaged by scraping
**中文：** n. 磨损；磨耗；擦伤；磨蚀

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scrape（擦伤）
- scratch（抓痕）
- erosion（侵蚀）
- graze（擦伤）
- friction（摩擦）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **skin abrasion / surface abrasion / minor abrasion / abrasion resistance / signs of abrasion**

👉 Example: The patient arrived at the emergency room with multiple abrasions on her arms and knees.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist explained that the smooth canyon walls resulted from centuries of water **abrasion** against the rock.
   地质学家解释说，光滑的峡谷壁是几个世纪以来水流对岩石磨蚀的结果。

2. The cyclist sustained only minor **abrasions** on her elbows and knees after sliding across the pavement.
   这名骑行者在地面上滑行后，肘部和膝盖只受了轻微的擦伤。

3. Engineers selected materials with high **abrasion** resistance to ensure the flooring could withstand heavy industrial use.
   工程师选择了高耐磨材料，以确保地板能够承受高强度的工业使用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abrasion" is the noun form of "abrade" — just as "abrade" means to scrape, "abrasion" is the scrape itself.
"Abrasion"是"abrade"的名词形式——就像"abrade"意为刮擦，"abrasion"就是刮擦本身或其结果。
