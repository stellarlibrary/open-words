# 🌡️ thermal

🔊 /ˈθɜːrməl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to heat; designed to retain body heat. (n.) A rising current of warm air used by birds and glider pilots.
**中文：** adj. 热的；保暖的 n. 上升的热气流

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- heat-related（与热有关的）
- warm（温暖的）
- caloric（热量的）
- insulating（隔热的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **thermal energy / thermal insulation / thermal imaging / thermal underwear / ride a thermal**

👉 Example: Engineers installed thermal insulation throughout the building to reduce heat loss during the winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The satellite's **thermal** imaging sensors detected significant temperature variations across the surface of the volcanic region.
   卫星的热成像传感器检测到火山地区表面的显著温度变化。

2. Eagles soar effortlessly for hours by circling within rising **thermals** that carry them to remarkable altitudes without flapping.
   鹰通过在上升的热气流中盘旋，可以毫不费力地翱翔数小时，被带到惊人的高度而无需拍打翅膀。

3. The new **thermal** coating applied to spacecraft surfaces prevents critical components from overheating during atmospheric reentry procedures.
   应用于航天器表面的新型隔热涂层可防止关键部件在大气再入过程中过热。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "thermal" as related to "thermo-" (Greek for heat) — a thermos keeps drinks hot, and thermal means related to heat.
把"thermal"想成与"thermo-"（希腊语中的热）有关——热水瓶（thermos）保持饮料温热，thermal就是与热有关的。
