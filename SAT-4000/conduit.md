# 🔧 conduit

🔊 /ˈkɒndjuɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A channel or pipe for conveying water or other fluid; a tube or trough for protecting electric wires; a means by which something is transmitted or distributed.

**中文：** 导管；沟渠；传输渠道。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- channel（渠道）
- pipeline（管道）
- duct（管道）
- passage（通道）
- tube（管子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** conduit for something / serve as a conduit / electrical conduit

**Example:** The ambassador served as a diplomatic **conduit** between the two nations during the negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The underground **conduit** carries fresh water from the reservoir to residential areas across the entire district.
   地下导管将淡水从水库输送到整个地区的居民区。

2. Social media platforms have become a primary **conduit** for the rapid dissemination of information worldwide.
   社交媒体平台已成为全球快速传播信息的主要渠道。

3. Engineers installed a protective **conduit** to shield the electrical wiring from moisture and environmental damage.
   工程师安装了一条保护性导管，以保护电线免受潮湿和环境损害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Con-" (together) + "duit" (from Latin ducere, to lead) — a conduit leads things through, like water through a pipe.

**中文：** "con-"（一起）+ "duit"（源自拉丁语 ducere，引导）——conduit 就是引导东西通过的管道，即"导管、渠道"。
