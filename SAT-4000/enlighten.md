# 💡 enlighten

🔊 /ɪnˈlaɪtən/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give someone greater knowledge and understanding; to inform or instruct; to free from ignorance
**中文：** v. 启发；启蒙；教导

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- illuminate（启发）
- educate（教育）
- inform（告知）
- edify（启迪）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enlighten someone about / enlighten the public / enlighten the mind / enlighten oneself**

👉 Example: The documentary sought to enlighten viewers about the lasting consequences of climate change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished lecturer endeavored to **enlighten** the audience about the complex origins of the economic crisis.
   这位杰出的演讲者努力向观众阐明经济危机的复杂起因。

2. Extensive travel abroad can **enlighten** individuals by exposing them to diverse cultures, traditions, and worldviews.
   广泛的海外旅行能够通过接触不同的文化、传统和世界观来启发个人。

3. The committee published a comprehensive report to **enlighten** the public regarding the proposed legislative reforms.
   委员会发表了一份综合报告，以就拟议的立法改革向公众进行启蒙。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-light-en" as putting "light" into someone's mind — when you enlighten someone, you bring light to their understanding.
把"enlighten"想成往某人的头脑中注入"light（光）"——当你启发某人时，你为他们的理解带来了光明。
