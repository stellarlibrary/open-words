# 🧽 absorption

🔊 /əbˈzɔːrpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of absorbing or being absorbed; a state of intense concentration or engrossment
**中文：** n. 吸收；全神贯注，专心致志

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assimilation（吸收）
- immersion（沉浸）
- engrossment（全神贯注）
- preoccupation（专注）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **absorption of nutrients / absorption in study / absorption rate / complete absorption**

👉 Example: Her total absorption in the novel caused her to miss her bus stop entirely.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **absorption** of carbon dioxide by the ocean has increased significantly over the past century.
   海洋对二氧化碳的吸收在过去一个世纪显著增加。

2. His complete **absorption** in the research project left him unaware of the passing hours.
   他对研究项目的全神贯注使他完全忽略了时间的流逝。

3. Scientists measured the **absorption** rate of the new material to evaluate its potential industrial applications.
   科学家们测量了这种新材料的吸收率，以评估其潜在的工业应用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "absorption" as "ab-SORP-tion" — like a sponge that SORPs up water, absorbing everything completely.
把"absorption"想成"吸附"——就像海绵把水完全吸进去一样，无论是液体还是注意力都被完全吸收。
