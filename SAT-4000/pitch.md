# 🎵 pitch

🔊 /pɪtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of a sound determined by its frequency; a sticky dark substance; the degree of slope; a playing field; the act of throwing. (v.) To throw or hurl; to set up a tent; to tilt or slope
**中文：** n. 音高；音调；沥青；程度；倾斜；球场；投掷 v. 投掷；搭帐篷；倾斜；坠落

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tone（音调）
- throw（投掷）
- slope（倾斜）
- tar（沥青）
- level（程度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pitch a tent / pitch a ball / perfect pitch / sales pitch / pitch black / fever pitch**

👉 Example: The singer's ability to identify any musical pitch without a reference note amazed the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orchestra conductor asked the violinist to adjust her **pitch** slightly to achieve perfect harmony with the ensemble.
   管弦乐队指挥要求小提琴手稍微调整音高，以与乐团达到完美和谐。

2. The entrepreneur delivered a compelling **pitch** to the investors, outlining the innovative potential of her technology startup.
   这位企业家向投资者进行了一场引人注目的推介，概述了她科技初创公司的创新潜力。

3. The campers decided to **pitch** their tents near the river, choosing a flat clearing sheltered from the prevailing wind.
   露营者决定在河边搭帐篷，选择了一块避风的平坦空地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pitch" as a word that hits many targets — like pitching a ball, it can land in many different areas of meaning, from sound to slope to tar.
把"pitch"想成一个击中多个目标的词——就像投球一样，它可以落在从声音到斜坡到沥青等许多不同的意义领域。
