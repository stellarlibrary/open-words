# 🔗 akin

🔊 /əˈkɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Similar in nature, character, or quality; related by blood or origin
**中文：** 类似的；同类的；同族的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- similar（类似的）
- analogous（相似的）
- comparable（可比较的）
- kindred（同类的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **akin to something / closely akin / something akin to**
👉 Example: His experience was akin to that of many immigrants who arrived during the same period.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The feeling of accomplishment after completing the marathon was **akin** to the joy of earning a degree.
   完成马拉松后的成就感类似于获得学位时的喜悦。

2. Her management style is more **akin** to collaborative leadership than to traditional authoritarian command structures.
   她的管理风格更类似于协作式领导，而非传统的威权式指挥结构。

3. The architect designed a structure **akin** to ancient Roman aqueducts, blending classical form with modern engineering.
   建筑师设计了一座类似于古罗马水道的建筑，将古典形式与现代工程相结合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-kin" as "a kin" — your kin (relatives) are similar to you, so akin means similar or related.
把"akin"想成"a kin"——你的亲属（kin）与你相似，所以akin表示类似的或有关联的。
