# 💪 force

🔊 /fɔːrs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Strength or energy exerted; coercion or compulsion; a military body. (v.) To compel or drive someone to do something against their will
**中文：** n. 力量；武力；军队 v. 促使；强迫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compel（强迫）
- coerce（胁迫）
- power（力量）
- strength（力气）
- impel（推动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **force someone to / by force / driving force / force of habit / come into force**

👉 Example: The new regulations will come into force at the beginning of next year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government deployed a substantial military **force** to maintain order in the region following the political upheaval.
   政府在政治动荡后部署了一支庞大的军事力量以维持该地区的秩序。

2. Economic pressures **forced** many small businesses to close their doors permanently during the prolonged recession.
   经济压力迫使许多小企业在漫长的经济衰退期间永久关闭。

3. The **force** of the argument presented by the prosecution left the defense attorneys struggling to mount a credible rebuttal.
   控方提出的论点之有力使辩护律师难以提出可信的反驳。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "force" as related to "fort" (fortress) — a fort relies on force and strength to defend against enemies.
把"force"联想到"fort"（堡垒）——堡垒依靠力量和武力来抵御敌人。
