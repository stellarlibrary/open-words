# 🔮 provident

🔊 /ˈprɑːvɪdənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Making provision for future needs; prudent and thrifty; having or showing foresight
**中文：** adj. 有远见的；节俭的；顾及未来的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prudent（审慎的）
- thrifty（节俭的）
- foresighted（有远见的）
- frugal（节约的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **provident planning / provident measures / provident use of resources / provident fund**

👉 Example: Her provident management of household finances allowed the family to weather the economic downturn comfortably.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **provident** investor diversified her portfolio well before the market downturn, thereby preserving the majority of her assets.
   这位有远见的投资者在市场低迷之前就分散了投资组合，从而保全了大部分资产。

2. A **provident** government establishes fiscal reserves during periods of economic prosperity to safeguard against future recessions and crises.
   一个有远见的政府会在经济繁荣时期建立财政储备，以防范未来的衰退和危机。

3. His **provident** habit of storing surplus grain each harvest ultimately saved the entire village during the prolonged winter famine.
   他每次丰收时储存多余粮食的节俭习惯，最终在漫长的冬季饥荒中拯救了整个村庄。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "provident" as "provide + ent" — someone who is provident provides for the future by planning ahead wisely.
把"provident"想成"provide（提供）+ ent"——有远见的人会通过提前规划为未来做好准备。
