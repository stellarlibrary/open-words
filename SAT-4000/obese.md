# 🏋️ obese

🔊 /oʊˈbiːs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely overweight; having a body mass index significantly above the healthy range.
**中文：** adj. 肥胖的，过胖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overweight（超重的）
- corpulent（肥胖的）
- portly（肥大的）
- stout（粗壮的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clinically obese / morbidly obese / obese patients / obese individuals**

👉 Example: The physician warned the patient that being clinically obese increased the risk of heart disease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent studies indicate that **obese** individuals face a substantially higher risk of developing type two diabetes.
   最近的研究表明，肥胖人群患二型糖尿病的风险显著增加。

2. Public health campaigns aim to reduce the percentage of **obese** children through improved nutrition and exercise programs.
   公共卫生运动旨在通过改善营养和锻炼计划来降低肥胖儿童的比例。

3. The researcher found that **obese** mice responded differently to the experimental drug than their lean counterparts did.
   研究人员发现，肥胖小鼠对实验药物的反应与瘦的对照组小鼠不同。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "o-BESE" — sounds like "oh, beast!" — someone so large they might be mistaken for a beast in size.
把"obese"想成"oh, beast!"——一个人胖到体型可能被误认为是野兽。
