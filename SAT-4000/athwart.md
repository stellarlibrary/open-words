# ⛵ athwart

🔊 /əˈθwɔːrt/<br>
🏷️ *adverb, preposition | 副词，介词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) From side to side; crosswise; (prep.) across from one side to the other; in opposition to; counter to.
**中文：** (adv.) 横跨着；横过地 (prep.) 横过；逆；与……相反

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- across（横过）
- crosswise（交叉地）
- transversely（横向地）
- against（逆着）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **athwart the path/bow/deck/current/plans**

👉 Example: The fallen tree lay athwart the narrow road, blocking all traffic in both directions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A massive log had fallen **athwart** the hiking trail, forcing the group to find an alternate route.
   一根巨大的原木横倒在徒步小径上，迫使队伍寻找替代路线。

2. The stubborn senator stood **athwart** the proposed legislation, vowing to block its passage at every stage.
   这位固执的参议员阻挡拟议的立法，誓言在每个阶段都阻止其通过。

3. Dark clouds moved **athwart** the sky, casting long shadows over the entire valley below the mountain.
   乌云横过天空，在山下的整个山谷投下了长长的阴影。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ATHWART" contains "THWART" (to block or obstruct) — something lying athwart a path literally thwarts your progress.
"ATHWART"包含"THWART（阻挠）"——横在路上的东西确实会阻挠你的前进，两者含义紧密相连。
