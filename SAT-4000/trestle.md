# 🌉 trestle

🔊 /ˈtresəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A framework consisting of a horizontal beam supported by two pairs of sloping legs, used as a support; a bridge made of such frameworks
**中文：** n. 栈桥；支架；搁凳式桥梁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- support（支撑物）
- framework（框架）
- scaffold（脚手架）
- brace（支柱）
- sawhorse（锯木架）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **trestle bridge / trestle table / wooden trestle / railroad trestle**

👉 Example: The old railroad trestle spanning the gorge has become a popular landmark for hikers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers designed a massive steel **trestle** to carry the railway across the deep valley separating the two mountain towns.
   工程师们设计了一座巨大的钢制栈桥，将铁路架过分隔两个山城的深谷。

2. The exhibition hall featured long **trestle** tables arranged in rows, displaying hundreds of artifacts from the archaeological collection.
   展览大厅里摆放着一排排长条搁凳桌，展示了考古收藏中的数百件文物。

3. The historic wooden **trestle** was carefully restored to preserve its architectural significance and structural integrity for future generations.
   这座历史悠久的木制栈桥经过精心修复，以为后代保存其建筑意义和结构完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trestle" as "tres + tle" — "tres" means three in Spanish; imagine three legs forming a sturdy support structure.
把"trestle"想成"tres + tle"——"tres"在西班牙语中意为"三"；想象三条腿构成一个坚固的支撑结构。
