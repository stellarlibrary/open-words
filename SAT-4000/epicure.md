# 🍷 epicure

🔊 /ˈepɪkjʊr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who takes particular pleasure in fine food and drink; a connoisseur of good cuisine
**中文：** n. 老饕；美食家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gourmet（美食家）
- connoisseur（鉴赏家）
- gourmand（饕餮之徒）
- gastronome（美食学家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a true epicure / epicure's delight / discerning epicure / renowned epicure**

👉 Example: As a true epicure, she could distinguish the subtlest flavors in even the most complex dishes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **epicure** traveled across Europe sampling artisanal cheeses and rare wines from centuries-old vineyards.
   这位著名的**美食家**游遍欧洲，品尝手工奶酪和来自百年老酒庄的珍稀葡萄酒。

2. Only a seasoned **epicure** would appreciate the delicate balance of spices in this traditional Sichuan preparation.
   只有经验丰富的**美食家**才能欣赏这道传统川菜中香料的微妙平衡。

3. The magazine's food critic, a self-proclaimed **epicure**, published scathing reviews of restaurants that failed his exacting standards.
   该杂志的美食评论家，一位自称的**老饕**，对未达到其严格标准的餐厅发表了尖锐的评论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EPICURE" as coming from Epicurus, the Greek philosopher who valued pleasure — an epicure seeks the pleasure of fine food.
把"epicure"联想到古希腊哲学家伊壁鸠鲁(Epicurus)，他崇尚享乐——美食家追求美食带来的愉悦。
