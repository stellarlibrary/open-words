# ⏳ transient

🔊 /ˈtrænziənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting only for a short time; passing through a place quickly. (n.) A temporary or brief phenomenon, especially a sudden change in voltage or current.
**中文：** adj. 短暂的；路过的；临时的 n. 瞬变现象；短暂的事物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fleeting（短暂的）
- temporary（临时的）
- ephemeral（短暂的）
- passing（一时的）
- momentary（瞬间的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transient phenomenon / transient population / transient phase / transient response**

👉 Example: The transient nature of the economic boom left many investors unprepared for the downturn.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher noted that the **transient** spike in temperature was caused by a brief malfunction in the cooling system.
   研究人员注意到温度的短暂飙升是由冷却系统的短暂故障引起的。

2. Many **transient** workers migrate seasonally to agricultural regions where labor demand peaks during the harvest months.
   许多流动工人在收获季节劳动力需求高峰期季节性地迁移到农业地区。

3. The effects of the medication proved to be **transient**, as the patient's symptoms returned within forty-eight hours.
   该药物的效果被证明是短暂的，因为患者的症状在四十八小时内复发了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transient" as "transit + ent" — like a traveler in transit, only passing through briefly and never staying long.
把"transient"想成"transit（过境）+ ent"——就像一个过境中的旅客，只是短暂经过，从不久留。
