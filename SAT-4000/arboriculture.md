# 🪓 arboriculture

🔊 /ˈɑːrbərɪˌkʌltʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The science, art, and practice of cultivating, managing, and studying individual trees, shrubs, and other woody plants
**中文：** n. 树木的培植/栽培

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tree cultivation（树木栽培）
- silviculture（林业）
- forestry（森林学）
- horticulture（园艺学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **study arboriculture / practice arboriculture / arboriculture techniques / arboriculture industry / urban arboriculture**

👉 Example: She pursued a degree in arboriculture to learn how to manage urban tree populations effectively.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern **arboriculture** combines traditional pruning methods with advanced scientific research to improve the health of urban tree populations.
   现代树木栽培将传统修剪方法与先进的科学研究相结合，以改善城市树木的健康状况。

2. The city hired specialists in **arboriculture** to assess the structural integrity of aging trees along the main boulevard.
   该市聘请了树木栽培专家来评估主要林荫大道上老化树木的结构完整性。

3. Advances in **arboriculture** have enabled farmers to cultivate fruit-bearing trees in climates previously considered too harsh for growth.
   树木栽培技术的进步使农民能够在以前被认为气候过于恶劣的地区种植果树。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Split it into "arbori-" (tree) + "culture" (cultivation) — arboriculture is literally the culture or cultivation of trees, like agriculture is for fields.
把它拆成"arbori-"（树）+"culture"（培养）——arboriculture就是树木的培养，就像agriculture（农业）是田地的培养一样。
