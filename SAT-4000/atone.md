# 🙏 atone

🔊 /əˈtoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make amends for a wrongdoing or offense; to compensate or pay for a sin, crime, or mistake.
**中文：** 赎罪；弥补；补偿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expiate（赎罪）
- redeem（弥补）
- compensate（补偿）
- make amends（做出补偿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **atone for one's sins/crimes/mistakes/wrongdoings/past**

👉 Example: He spent years trying to atone for the harm he had caused to his family and closest friends.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The convicted man dedicated the rest of his life to charitable work in an effort to **atone** for his crimes.
   这名被定罪的男子将余生都奉献给了慈善事业，以弥补他的罪行。

2. She believed that sincere repentance and good deeds were the only true ways to **atone** for past mistakes.
   她相信真诚的悔改和善行是弥补过去错误的唯一真正途径。

3. The nation established a memorial fund to **atone** for the injustices committed against its indigenous population.
   该国设立了一个纪念基金，以弥补对其原住民所犯下的不公正行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ATONE" = "AT ONE" — when you atone, you try to become "at one" (reconciled) with the person you have wronged.
"ATONE"可以拆成"AT ONE（合为一体）"——当你赎罪时，你试图与你伤害过的人重新"合为一体"，即和解。
