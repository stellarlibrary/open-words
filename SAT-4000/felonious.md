# 🚨 felonious

🔊 /fəˈloʊniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or constituting a felony; wicked, villainous, or criminal in nature
**中文：** adj. 凶恶的；重罪的；极恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- criminal（犯罪的）
- villainous（恶毒的）
- wicked（邪恶的）
- nefarious（穷凶极恶的）
- unlawful（非法的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **felonious assault / felonious intent / felonious act / felonious conduct / felonious behavior**

👉 Example: The prosecutor argued that the defendant's felonious intent was evident from the planning stages of the crime.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court determined that the suspect's **felonious** conduct warranted a lengthy prison sentence and significant financial restitution.
   法院认定嫌疑人的重罪行为应判处长期监禁并承担巨额经济赔偿。

2. Investigators uncovered a **felonious** scheme in which corporate executives had systematically defrauded thousands of vulnerable investors.
   调查人员揭露了一个犯罪阴谋，公司高管系统性地欺诈了数千名弱势投资者。

3. The distinction between a misdemeanor and a **felonious** offense often depends on the severity of harm inflicted upon the victim.
   轻罪和重罪之间的区别通常取决于对受害者造成的伤害严重程度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "felonious" as "felon + ious" — a felon is a serious criminal, so felonious means having the nature of a felon's crime.
把"felonious"拆成"felon（重罪犯）+ ious"——felon是严重罪犯，因此felonious意味着具有重罪性质的。
