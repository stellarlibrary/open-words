# 🧬 evolution

🔊 /ˌɛvəˈluːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The gradual development or change of something over time; the biological process by which species develop and diversify
**中文：** n. 演变；进化论；进展；演化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- development（发展）
- progression（进展）
- transformation（转变）
- adaptation（适应）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the evolution of / theory of evolution / cultural evolution / evolution over time**

👉 Example: The evolution of digital technology has fundamentally transformed the way humans communicate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Darwin's theory of **evolution** revolutionized our understanding of biological diversity and the origins of species.
   达尔文的进化论彻底改变了我们对生物多样性和物种起源的认识。

2. The rapid **evolution** of artificial intelligence has raised significant ethical questions among policymakers and researchers worldwide.
   人工智能的快速演变在全球政策制定者和研究人员中引发了重大伦理问题。

3. Scholars have traced the **evolution** of democratic governance from ancient Greek city-states to modern representative systems.
   学者们追溯了民主治理从古希腊城邦到现代代议制的演变历程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "evolve-ution" — evolution is the result (ution) of things evolving, gradually changing and developing over long periods.
把"evolution"想成"evolve的结果"——进化是事物逐渐演变、经过漫长时间发展的结果。
