# 🚫 restrict

🔊 /rɪˈstrɪkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To put a limit on; to keep under control; to confine or keep within certain boundaries
**中文：** v. 限制；约束；限定

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- limit（限制）
- constrain（约束）
- confine（限制）
- curb（遏制）
- restrain（抑制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **restrict access / restrict movement / restrict to / severely restrict / restrict the use of**

👉 Example: The government moved to restrict access to classified documents following the security breach.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new legislation will **restrict** the sale of certain pharmaceutical products to licensed medical practitioners and authorized pharmacies only.
   新立法将把某些药品的销售限制在持证医疗从业者和授权药房范围内。

2. Budget constraints forced the university to **restrict** enrollment in several popular programs that had previously been open to all applicants.
   预算限制迫使大学对此前向所有申请者开放的几个热门项目进行招生限制。

3. International sanctions were imposed to **restrict** the nation's ability to trade in strategic materials and advanced military technology.
   实施国际制裁是为了限制该国进行战略物资和先进军事技术贸易的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-STRICT" — being "strict again and again" means putting tight limits on something, which is exactly what restrict means.
把"restrict"想成"re-strict"——"一再严格"就是对某事施加严格限制，这正是restrict的含义。
