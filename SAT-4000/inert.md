# 🪨 inert

🔊 /ɪˈnɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking the ability to move or act; chemically inactive; sluggish or slow to respond
**中文：** adj. 惰性的；呆滞的；迟缓的；无效的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inactive（不活跃的）
- dormant（休眠的）
- sluggish（迟缓的）
- lifeless（无生气的）
- static（静止的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inert gas / inert substance / chemically inert / remain inert / lie inert**

👉 Example: Noble gases such as helium and neon are chemically inert under normal conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient lay **inert** on the hospital bed, showing no visible response to external stimuli or verbal commands.
   病人**呆滞地**躺在病床上，对外界刺激和语言指令没有任何可见反应。

2. Nitrogen is a relatively **inert** gas that constitutes approximately seventy-eight percent of the Earth's atmosphere.
   氮气是一种相对**惰性**的气体，约占地球大气层的百分之七十八。

3. The committee remained **inert** despite mounting evidence that immediate action was necessary to prevent environmental damage.
   尽管越来越多的证据表明必须立即采取行动以防止环境破坏，委员会仍然**无动于衷**。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inert" as "in + ert (earth)" — like something buried in the earth, it just sits there doing nothing, completely inactive.
把"inert"想成"in + ert（earth，大地）"——就像埋在地下的东西一样，什么也不做，完全不活动。
