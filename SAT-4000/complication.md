# 🔄 complication

🔊 /ˌkɒmplɪˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A circumstance that makes a situation more difficult or complex; a secondary disease or condition that worsens an existing one
**中文：** n. 复杂化；复杂因素；并发症

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- difficulty（困难）
- obstacle（障碍）
- impediment（阻碍）
- setback（挫折）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **medical complication / added complication / unforeseen complication / serious complication**

👉 Example: The surgery proceeded well, but a postoperative complication delayed the patient's recovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unexpected **complication** in the supply chain forced the company to revise its entire production schedule for the quarter.
   供应链中意想不到的复杂因素迫使公司修改了整个季度的生产计划。

2. Diabetes can lead to serious **complications** if patients fail to manage their blood glucose levels consistently over time.
   如果患者未能长期稳定地控制血糖水平，糖尿病可能导致严重的并发症。

3. The diplomatic negotiations encountered a significant **complication** when both parties refused to compromise on territorial boundaries.
   当双方都拒绝在领土边界问题上妥协时，外交谈判遇到了重大的复杂因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "complication" as "compli-CATION" — a "complex situation" that tangles things up and makes them harder.
把"complication"想成"复杂的情况"——就像一团乱麻把事情搅得更难处理。
