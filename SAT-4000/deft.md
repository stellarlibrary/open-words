# 🎯 deft

🔊 /dɛft/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Demonstrating skill and cleverness; neatly skillful and quick in one's movements or actions
**中文：** adj. 巧妙的，熟练的；敏捷的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adroit（灵巧的）
- skillful（熟练的）
- nimble（敏捷的）
- dexterous（灵巧的）
- proficient（精通的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deft touch / deft handling / deft fingers / deft maneuver / deft at something**

👉 Example: The surgeon's deft handling of the delicate instruments impressed everyone in the operating room.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **deft** negotiation skills helped resolve the long-standing territorial dispute between the two nations peacefully.
   这位外交官巧妙的谈判技巧帮助和平解决了两国之间长期存在的领土争端。

2. With a few **deft** strokes of his brush, the artist transformed the blank canvas into a striking portrait.
   画家用几笔灵巧的笔触，将空白画布变成了一幅引人注目的肖像画。

3. The pianist's **deft** fingers moved across the keys with remarkable precision, captivating the entire concert audience.
   钢琴家灵巧的手指以惊人的精准度在琴键上移动，令整个音乐会观众着迷。
