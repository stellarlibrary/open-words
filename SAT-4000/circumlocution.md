# 🌀 circumlocution

🔊 /ˌsɜːrkəmləˈkjuːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The use of unnecessarily wordy and indirect language; an indirect or roundabout expression.
**中文：** 婉转曲折的说法；累赘的陈述；绕圈子的表达方式。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- periphrasis（迂回说法）
- verbosity（冗长）
- indirectness（拐弯抹角）
- wordiness（啰嗦）
- euphemism（委婉语）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resort to circumlocution / avoid circumlocution / a circumlocution for**

👉 Example: The politician's circumlocution made it impossible to discern his actual position on the issue.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat employed deliberate **circumlocution** to avoid offending either party during the sensitive treaty negotiations.
   外交官故意使用迂回的说法，以免在敏感的条约谈判中冒犯任何一方。

2. Academic writing that relies on excessive **circumlocution** often obscures rather than clarifies the author's intended argument.
   过度依赖冗长迂回表达的学术写作往往会模糊而非阐明作者的论点。

3. The witness used **circumlocution** throughout the hearing, compelling the judge to repeatedly request direct and concise answers.
   证人在整个听证会上都使用迂回的说法，迫使法官反复要求其作出直接简洁的回答。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circum-" (around) + "locution" (speaking) — speaking around the point instead of getting to it directly.
把"circumlocution"拆分为"circum-（周围）"+"locution（说话）"——绕着重点说话，而不是直奔主题。
