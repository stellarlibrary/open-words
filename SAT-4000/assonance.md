# 🎵 assonance

🔊 /ˈæsənəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The repetition of similar vowel sounds in nearby words, especially in poetry, to create a rhythmic or musical effect
**中文：** 类似的音，谐音；类韵

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vowel rhyme（元音韵）
- near rhyme（近韵）
- half rhyme（半韵）
- slant rhyme（斜韵）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **assonance in poetry / use of assonance / assonance and alliteration / create assonance**

👉 Example: The phrase "the rain in Spain stays mainly on the plain" is a classic demonstration of assonance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet employed subtle **assonance** throughout the stanza to evoke a melancholy mood without relying on full rhyme.
   诗人在整个诗节中运用了微妙的类韵来营造忧郁的氛围，而不依赖完全的押韵。

2. Literary critics noted the deliberate **assonance** of long vowel sounds that gave the passage its distinctive, haunting quality.
   文学评论家注意到长元音的刻意类韵赋予了这段文字独特而萦绕人心的品质。

3. Students of rhetoric must learn to distinguish **assonance** from consonance and alliteration in their analysis of texts.
   修辞学的学生必须学会在文本分析中区分类韵、辅音韵和头韵。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assonance" as "a-SON-ance" — like a son singing a song with repeating vowel sounds that echo melodically.
把"assonance"想成"像儿子（son）在唱歌"——重复的元音像歌声一样回荡，形成和谐的韵律。
