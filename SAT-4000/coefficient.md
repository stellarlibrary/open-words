# 🔢 coefficient

🔊 /ˌkoʊɪˈfɪʃənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A numerical or constant factor placed before and multiplying a variable in an algebraic expression; a measure of a specified property. (adj.) Cooperating; acting together
**中文：** n. 系数；率 adj. 合作的；协同的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- factor（因数）
- multiplier（乘数）
- constant（常数）
- parameter（参数）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coefficient of friction / correlation coefficient / coefficient of determination / drag coefficient**

👉 Example: The researcher calculated the correlation coefficient to determine the strength of the relationship between the two variables.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist measured the **coefficient** of friction between the two surfaces to predict the force required for movement.
   物理学家测量了两个表面之间的摩擦系数，以预测运动所需的力。

2. A high correlation **coefficient** indicated a strong positive relationship between educational attainment and lifetime earning potential.
   高相关系数表明教育程度与终身收入潜力之间存在很强的正相关关系。

3. Engineers adjusted the drag **coefficient** in their calculations to improve the aerodynamic efficiency of the new aircraft design.
   工程师们调整了计算中的阻力系数，以提高新飞机设计的空气动力学效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "co-" (together) + "efficient" — a coefficient works together with a variable to efficiently express a mathematical relationship.
把"coefficient"想成"co-"（一起）+ "efficient"（有效的）——系数与变量一起高效地表达数学关系。
