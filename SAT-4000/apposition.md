# 🧱 apposition

🔊 /ˌæpəˈzɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The placing of things side by side or next to each other; in grammar, a construction in which a noun or noun phrase is placed beside another to explain or identify it
**中文：** 并置，同格；同位语

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- juxtaposition（并列）
- adjacency（邻接）
- collocation（并置）
- conjunction（连接）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in apposition / placed in apposition / apposition to / a noun in apposition**

👉 Example: In the sentence "My friend, a talented musician, performed last night," the phrase "a talented musician" is in apposition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The phrase "the capital city" stands in **apposition** to "Beijing" in the sentence, providing additional identifying information.
   在这个句子中，"首都城市"与"北京"构成同位语关系，提供了额外的识别信息。

2. The scientist studied cellular growth through **apposition**, observing how new layers are deposited upon existing surfaces.
   这位科学家通过并置生长方式研究细胞生长，观察新层如何沉积在既有表面上。

3. Understanding grammatical **apposition** is essential for constructing sophisticated sentences in formal academic writing and discourse.
   理解语法中的同位语对于在正式学术写作和论述中构建复杂句子至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "apposition" as "a-POSITION beside" — placing one thing in a position right next to another to explain or identify it.
把"apposition"想成"a-POSITION beside"——把一个事物放在另一个旁边的位置来解释或识别它。
