# 📐 radius

🔊 /ˈreɪdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A straight line from the center to the circumference of a circle or sphere; the length of such a line; the area within a given distance from a central point; the thicker, shorter bone of the forearm.
**中文：** n. 半径；半径范围；桡骨；辐射光线

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- span（跨度）
- range（范围）
- reach（可及范围）
- scope（范围）
- extent（程度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **within a radius of / radius of a circle / turning radius / search radius / radius of influence**

👉 Example: The explosion caused damage within a radius of five kilometers from the epicenter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The emergency response team evacuated all residents within a ten-mile **radius** of the chemical plant after the spill.
   紧急响应小组在泄漏事故发生后疏散了化工厂十英里半径范围内的所有居民。

2. In geometry, the **radius** of a circle is exactly half the length of its diameter, a fundamental relationship.
   在几何学中，圆的半径恰好是其直径长度的一半，这是一个基本关系。

3. The orthopedic surgeon confirmed that the patient had fractured the **radius** in her left forearm during the fall.
   骨科医生确认患者在摔倒时左前臂的桡骨骨折了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "radius" as a ray shooting out from the center of a circle — "radi-us" shares the root with "ray," a line extending from a central point.
把"radius"想成从圆心射出的一条射线——"radi-us"和"ray（射线）"有共同的词根，都是从中心点延伸出去的线。
