# 💎 resource

🔊 /ˈriːsɔːrs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A stock or supply of materials, money, or other assets that can be drawn on when needed; a source of aid or support
**中文：** n. 资源，财力；办法；智谋

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- asset（资产）
- supply（供给）
- reserve（储备）
- means（手段）
- commodity（商品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natural resources / human resources / allocate resources / resource management / limited resources**

👉 Example: The organization must allocate its limited resources wisely to achieve maximum impact.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Developing nations often lack the financial **resource** base necessary to invest in critical infrastructure such as transportation and healthcare systems.
   发展中国家往往缺乏投资交通和医疗系统等关键基础设施所需的财力资源基础。

2. The library serves as an invaluable **resource** for students conducting research on historical events and primary source documents.
   图书馆是学生研究历史事件和原始文献资料时不可或缺的资源。

3. Effective **resource** management requires careful planning to ensure that both current and future generations can benefit equitably from available supplies.
   有效的资源管理需要周密规划，以确保当代和后代都能公平地从现有资源中获益。
