# 💎 bonanza

🔊 /bəˈnænzə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A rich vein of ore; a source of great wealth or prosperity; a stroke of good fortune
**中文：** n. 富矿带；致富之源；幸运

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- windfall（意外之财）
- jackpot（头奖）
- boon（恩惠）
- treasure trove（宝藏）
- goldmine（金矿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a bonanza of / bonanza year / economic bonanza / oil bonanza**

👉 Example: The discovery of natural gas reserves proved to be an economic bonanza for the small coastal nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unexpected **bonanza** of federal funding allowed the university to expand its research facilities significantly.
   意外获得的大量联邦资金使该大学得以大幅扩展其研究设施。

2. Prospectors who arrived early during the gold rush often stumbled upon a **bonanza** of precious minerals beneath the surface.
   淘金热期间早期到达的探矿者常常在地表下发现丰富的贵重矿藏。

3. The tech industry experienced a remarkable **bonanza** as consumer demand for digital services surged during the pandemic era.
   随着疫情时代消费者对数字服务需求的激增，科技行业经历了一次显著的繁荣。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bonanza" as "bonus +anza" — a massive bonus that brings extraordinary fortune and wealth.
把"bonanza"想成"bonus + anza"——一个带来非凡财富和好运的巨大奖金。
