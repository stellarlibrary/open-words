# 🙈 imperceptive

🔊 /ˌɪmpərˈsɛptɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking perception or awareness; unable to perceive or notice things; insensitive to subtle differences or details
**中文：** adj. 无感觉的；缺乏感知力的；无知觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unperceptive（迟钝的）
- insensitive（不敏感的）
- oblivious（浑然不觉的）
- obtuse（迟钝的）
- unaware（未察觉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imperceptive to (changes/nuances) / imperceptive observer / imperceptive of (feelings)**

👉 Example: The imperceptive manager failed to notice the growing dissatisfaction among his team members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The critic was remarkably **imperceptive** to the subtle thematic layers woven throughout the author's celebrated novel.
   这位评论家对作者名著中编织的微妙主题层次异常缺乏感知力。

2. An **imperceptive** leader who ignores early warning signs will inevitably fail to prevent organizational crises from escalating.
   一个缺乏洞察力、忽视早期预警信号的领导者将不可避免地无法阻止组织危机升级。

3. His **imperceptive** reading of the political landscape led him to make several costly and avoidable strategic miscalculations.
   他对政治格局缺乏感知的解读导致他犯了几个代价高昂且本可避免的战略失误。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-perceptive" as "not perceptive" — the prefix "im-" negates "perceptive," so someone imperceptive simply cannot perceive what is happening around them.
把"imperceptive"拆成"im（不）+ perceptive（有感知力的）"——没有感知力的，也就是迟钝的、无知觉的。
