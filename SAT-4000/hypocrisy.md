# 🎭 hypocrisy

🔊 /hɪˈpɑːkrəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The practice of claiming to have moral standards or beliefs to which one's own behavior does not conform; pretense of virtue
**中文：** n. 虚伪；伪善

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insincerity（不真诚）
- duplicity（口是心非）
- pretense（伪装）
- falseness（虚假）
- deceitfulness（欺骗性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sheer hypocrisy / political hypocrisy / expose hypocrisy / the hypocrisy of something**

👉 Example: Critics pointed out the hypocrisy of politicians who advocated for environmental reform while accepting donations from oil companies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's **hypocrisy** became evident when journalists revealed his private investments in the industries he publicly condemned.
   当记者揭露了这位参议员在他公开谴责的行业中的私人投资后，他的虚伪暴露无遗。

2. Many scholars have written about the **hypocrisy** embedded in colonial rhetoric that promoted freedom while perpetuating oppression.
   许多学者撰文论述了殖民主义修辞中固有的虚伪——一边宣扬自由，一边延续压迫。

3. The organization's **hypocrisy** in demanding transparency from others while concealing its own finances drew widespread criticism.
   该组织要求他人透明却隐瞒自身财务状况的虚伪行径招致了广泛批评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hypo-" (under, beneath) + "crisy" (from Greek "krisis," judgment) — a hypocrite judges others while hiding what lies beneath their own surface.
把"hypocrisy"想成"hypo-"（在下面）+"crisy"（来自希腊语"判断"）——伪善者评判别人，却隐藏自己表面之下的真相。
