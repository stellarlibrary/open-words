# 🧲 cohesion

🔊 /kəʊˈhiːʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The action or state of forming a united whole; the force that holds together molecules or parts of a substance
**中文：** n. 凝聚；结合；内聚力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unity（统一）
- solidarity（团结）
- adhesion（附着）
- bond（结合）
- integration（整合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social cohesion / team cohesion / group cohesion / lack of cohesion**

👉 Example: Strong social cohesion within a community can significantly reduce crime rates and promote collective well-being.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coach emphasized that team **cohesion** was far more important than individual talent in achieving consistent tournament victories.
   教练强调，团队凝聚力比个人才华更重要，是取得持续锦标赛胜利的关键。

2. Political analysts warned that growing economic inequality threatened the social **cohesion** necessary for a stable democratic society.
   政治分析家警告说，日益加剧的经济不平等威胁着稳定民主社会所必需的社会凝聚力。

3. The molecular **cohesion** of water allows insects to walk across its surface without breaking through the liquid membrane.
   水的分子内聚力使昆虫能够在其表面行走而不会穿透液体薄膜。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cohesion" as "co-hesion" — "co" means together and "hesion" sounds like "adhesion" (sticking), so cohesion is things sticking together as one.
把"cohesion"想成"co（共同）-hesion（粘合）"——凝聚就是事物粘合在一起成为一个整体。
