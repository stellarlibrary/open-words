# 🌸 comely

🔊 /ˈkʌm.li/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Pleasant to look at; attractive in appearance; suitable or proper
**中文：** adj. 清秀的；标致的；合适的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attractive（有吸引力的）
- fair（美丽的）
- handsome（英俊的）
- good-looking（好看的）
- becoming（得体的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **comely appearance / comely face / comely young woman / comely features**

👉 Example: The portrait captured the comely features of the noblewoman with remarkable precision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **comely** maiden in the classical painting embodied the era's prevailing standards of beauty and grace.
   古典画作中那位清秀的少女体现了那个时代盛行的美丽与优雅标准。

2. Despite her **comely** appearance, she was recognized primarily for her extraordinary intellect and scholarly achievements.
   尽管她容貌标致，但她主要因其非凡的智慧和学术成就而受到认可。

3. The author described the village as a **comely** settlement surrounded by rolling green hills and ancient forests.
   作者将这个村庄描述为一个秀丽的聚落，四周环绕着起伏的绿色山丘和古老的森林。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "come-ly" — someone so attractive that people "come" to look at them, a "lovely" sight to behold.
把"comely"想成"come-ly"——某人如此迷人，人们都"来(come)"看，是一道赏心悦目的风景。
