# 😶 passive

🔊 /ˈpæsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Accepting or submitting without resistance; not actively participating. (n.) The passive voice in grammar
**中文：** adj. 被动的，消极的；被动语态的 n. 被动语态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- submissive（顺从的）
- inactive（不活跃的）
- compliant（服从的）
- docile（温顺的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **passive voice / passive resistance / passive role / passive observer / passive income**

👉 Example: The committee members assumed a passive role and allowed the chairperson to make all decisions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argued that the administration adopted a dangerously **passive** approach to the escalating humanitarian crisis abroad.
   批评者认为，政府对不断升级的海外人道主义危机采取了危险的消极态度。

2. The researcher noted that students who remained **passive** during laboratory experiments showed lower retention of scientific concepts.
   研究者注意到，在实验室实验中保持被动的学生对科学概念的记忆保持率较低。

3. Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity through a completely **passive** process that requires no mechanical movement whatsoever.
   太阳能电池板通过完全被动的过程将阳光转化为电能，不需要任何机械运动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "passive" as related to "pass" — you just let things pass by without acting on them.
把"passive"联想到"pass（经过）"——你只是让事情从身边经过，而不采取行动。
