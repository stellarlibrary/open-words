# 🔋 battery

🔊 /ˈbætəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A device that stores electrical energy and converts it into power; a series or set of similar things; an artillery unit
**中文：** n. 电池，蓄电池；电池组；一系列；炮兵连

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cell（电池）
- array（阵列）
- series（系列）
- accumulator（蓄电池）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recharge a battery / battery of tests / battery life / battery-powered / assault and battery**

👉 Example: The research team conducted a battery of experiments to validate their hypothesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The electric vehicle's **battery** technology has improved significantly, now enabling travel distances exceeding four hundred miles.
   电动汽车的电池技术已经显著改善，现在续航里程可超过四百英里。

2. Candidates must complete a rigorous **battery** of assessments before being considered for the advanced research position.
   候选人必须完成一系列严格的评估，才能被考虑担任高级研究职位。

3. Engineers developed a new lithium-ion **battery** capable of retaining ninety percent of its charge after one thousand cycles.
   工程师们开发了一种新型锂离子电池，经过一千次循环后仍能保持百分之九十的电量。
