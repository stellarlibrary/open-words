# 🎯 allot

🔊 /əˈlɒt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give or distribute a share or portion of something; to set aside for a specific purpose
**中文：** 分配；拨给；分派

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assign（分配）
- allocate（分拨）
- apportion（分摊）
- designate（指定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **allot time / allot shares / allot a portion / allot something to someone**

👉 Example: Each participant was allotted fifteen minutes to present their research findings to the panel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor decided to **allot** equal time to each student for their oral presentations during the final examination week.
   教授决定在期末考试周为每位学生的口头报告分配相同的时间。

2. The organization will **allot** a fixed percentage of its annual revenue to charitable programs serving underprivileged communities nationwide.
   该组织将把其年收入的固定百分比拨给服务于全国弱势群体社区的慈善项目。

3. City planners have been asked to **allot** sufficient green space within the new urban development project for public recreation.
   城市规划者被要求在新的城市开发项目中分配足够的绿地用于公共休闲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "allot" as "a lot" — when you allot something, you divide "a lot" of things into smaller portions for distribution.
把"allot"想成"a lot"——当你分配某物时，你把"一大堆"东西分成更小的份额来分发。
