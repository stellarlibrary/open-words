# 🔧 compressible

🔊 /kəmˈpresəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of being compressed or squeezed into a smaller volume or space
**中文：** adj. 可压缩的；可压榨的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- squeezable（可挤压的）
- condensable（可浓缩的）
- reducible（可缩减的）
- contractible（可收缩的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **compressible fluid / compressible gas / compressible material / highly compressible**

👉 Example: Unlike liquids, gases are highly compressible and will decrease in volume under pressure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In aerodynamics, engineers must account for the fact that air becomes increasingly **compressible** at higher velocities.
   在空气动力学中，工程师必须考虑到空气在更高速度下变得越来越可压缩。

2. The new packaging material is lightweight and highly **compressible**, making it ideal for efficient shipping and storage.
   这种新型包装材料轻便且高度可压缩，非常适合高效运输和储存。

3. Scientists discovered that the **compressible** nature of certain polymers allows them to absorb significant impact force.
   科学家发现某些聚合物的可压缩特性使其能够吸收显著的冲击力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compress" + "ible" (able to) — something compressible is able to be compressed, like a sponge you can squeeze.
把"compressible"拆成"compress（压缩）+ ible（能够）"——可压缩的，就像你能挤压的海绵一样。
