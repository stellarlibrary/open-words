# 🚪 accessible

🔊 /əkˈsɛsɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Easy to approach, reach, enter, or use; able to be readily understood or appreciated.
**中文：** 易接近的；可进入的；可理解的；可获得的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- available（可获得的）
- approachable（可接近的）
- attainable（可达到的）
- comprehensible（可理解的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accessible to the public**, **easily accessible**, **accessible language**, **accessible by**

👉 Example: The museum strives to make art accessible to people of all backgrounds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's lectures were remarkably **accessible**, even to students with no prior knowledge of the subject.
   教授的讲座非常易懂，即使对没有相关基础知识的学生也是如此。

2. Public transportation has made the city center far more **accessible** to residents of outlying areas.
   公共交通使市中心对郊区居民来说更加便于到达。

3. The government pledged to make all public buildings **accessible** to individuals with physical disabilities.
   政府承诺让所有公共建筑对残障人士无障碍开放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accessible" = "access" + "-ible" (able to). If something is accessible, you are able to access it.
"Accessible" = "access"（进入）+ "-ible"（能够的），能够进入的就是 accessible。
