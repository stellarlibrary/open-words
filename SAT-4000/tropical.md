# 🌴 tropical

🔊 /ˈtrɒpɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or characteristic of the tropics; very hot and humid
**中文：** adj. 热带的；酷热潮湿的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equatorial（赤道的）
- sultry（闷热的）
- torrid（酷热的）
- humid（潮湿的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tropical climate / tropical region / tropical storm / tropical species**

👉 Example: The tropical climate of Southeast Asia supports an extraordinary diversity of plant and animal life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Deforestation in **tropical** regions has accelerated at an alarming rate, threatening biodiversity and contributing to global carbon emissions.
   热带地区的森林砍伐以惊人的速度加速，威胁着生物多样性并加剧了全球碳排放。

2. The research station was established on a remote **tropical** island to study coral reef ecosystems and their response to rising sea temperatures.
   该研究站设立在一个偏远的热带岛屿上，以研究珊瑚礁生态系统及其对海水温度上升的反应。

3. **Tropical** storms of increasing intensity have prompted meteorologists to reevaluate their predictive models for hurricane season forecasting.
   日益增强的热带风暴促使气象学家重新评估他们对飓风季节预测的预测模型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tropic-al" — it comes from "tropics," the warm zone around the equator, so tropical describes anything related to that hot region.
把"tropical"想成来自"tropics（热带）"——赤道附近的温暖地带，所以tropical描述与那个炎热地区有关的一切。
