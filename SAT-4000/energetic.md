# 💪 energetic

🔊 /ˌenərˈdʒetɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing great energy, vitality, or enthusiasm; vigorous and active in nature
**中文：** adj. 精力充沛的；积极的；有力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vigorous（精力旺盛的）
- dynamic（充满活力的）
- spirited（生气勃勃的）
- lively（活泼的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **energetic person / energetic approach / energetic support / energetic pursuit**

👉 Example: The energetic campaign volunteers canvassed every neighborhood before election day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **energetic** lectures consistently inspired students to engage more deeply with the complex subject matter.
   教授充满活力的讲座不断激励学生更深入地研究这一复杂课题。

2. An **energetic** reform movement swept through the institution, demanding transparency and accountability from senior officials.
   一场积极的改革运动席卷了该机构，要求高级官员保持透明和负责。

3. The committee praised the **energetic** efforts of the research team in completing the comprehensive study ahead of schedule.
   委员会赞扬了研究团队精力充沛的努力，使其提前完成了这项综合研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "energetic" as "energy + tic" — someone with so much energy they can't stop moving, like a tic of constant activity.
把"energetic"想成"energy + tic"——一个精力充沛到停不下来的人，像是不停活动的抽搐一样。
