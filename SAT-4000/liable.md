# ⚖️ liable

🔊 /ˈlaɪəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Legally responsible or obligated; likely or prone to do or experience something
**中文：** adj. 有责任的，有义务的；有…倾向的，易于…的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- responsible（负责的）
- accountable（有责任的）
- prone（易于…的）
- susceptible（易受影响的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **liable for / liable to (do something) / hold liable / legally liable**

👉 Example: The company was held liable for the damages caused by its defective products.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manufacturer is **liable** for any injuries resulting from defects in products sold directly to consumers nationwide.
   制造商对因直接销售给全国消费者的产品缺陷而导致的任何伤害承担责任。

2. Students who fail to submit assignments on time are **liable** to receive significantly lower grades for the semester.
   未能按时提交作业的学生很可能在该学期获得明显较低的成绩。

3. Without proper maintenance, the aging bridge is **liable** to collapse under the strain of heavy commercial vehicle traffic.
   如果没有适当的维护，这座老化的桥梁在重型商用车辆的压力下很可能会坍塌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liable" as "lie-able" — if you lie, you are able to be held responsible, making you liable for the consequences.
把"liable"想成"lie-able"——如果你撒谎，你就会被追究责任，使你对后果负有责任。
