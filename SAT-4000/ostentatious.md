# 💎 ostentatious

🔊 /ˌɑːstenˈteɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to attract notice or impress others; showy.
**中文：** adj. 招摇的；卖弄的；夸耀的；铺张的；惹人注目的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- showy（炫耀的）
- flamboyant（华丽的）
- pretentious（矫饰的）
- flashy（浮华的）
- gaudy（俗丽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ostentatious display / ostentatious wealth / ostentatious lifestyle / ostentatious manner**

👉 Example: The billionaire's ostentatious mansion featured gold-plated fixtures and a private helipad on the roof.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **ostentatious** display of wealth at the charity gala drew sharp criticism from the international press corps.
   这位外交官在慈善晚会上对财富的炫耀性展示招致了国际新闻界的尖锐批评。

2. Many scholars argue that **ostentatious** consumption reflects deeper insecurities about social status and personal identity.
   许多学者认为，炫耀性消费反映了对社会地位和个人身份更深层的不安全感。

3. The architecture of the palace was deliberately **ostentatious**, intended to project imperial power and intimidate foreign visitors.
   宫殿的建筑风格刻意铺张，旨在彰显帝国权力并震慑外国来访者。
