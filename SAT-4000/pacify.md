# 🕊️ pacify

🔊 /ˈpæsɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To bring to a state of peace or calm; to soothe or appease someone who is angry, agitated, or distressed; to restore order to a region or group.
**中文：** v. 使平静；安慰；抚慰；平定

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- appease（安抚）
- soothe（抚慰）
- placate（平息）
- mollify（缓和）
- calm（使平静）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pacify the crowd / pacify a crying child / pacify the region / pacify opponents**

👉 Example: The diplomat attempted to pacify the hostile delegation by offering significant concessions during negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government deployed additional peacekeeping forces in an effort to **pacify** the war-torn province and restore civilian order.
   政府部署了更多维和部队，努力**平定**饱受战争蹂躏的省份并恢复民间秩序。

2. Nothing the parents did could **pacify** the distressed infant, who continued to cry throughout the long overnight flight.
   父母做的一切都无法**安抚**那个不安的婴儿，他在漫长的夜间飞行中一直哭泣。

3. The chairman's reassuring speech was designed to **pacify** anxious shareholders who feared the company was heading toward bankruptcy.
   主席安抚性的讲话旨在**平息**那些担心公司走向破产的焦虑股东。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pac-ify" as coming from "pax" (Latin for peace) — to pacify means to make peaceful, just as the Pacific Ocean was named for its calm waters.
把"pacify"想成来自"pax"（拉丁语"和平"）——pacify就是使和平，正如太平洋（Pacific）因平静的海水而得名。
