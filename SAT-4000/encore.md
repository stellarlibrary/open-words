# 🎶 encore

🔊 /ˈɑːnkɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A demand by an audience for an additional performance; the additional performance itself. (v.) To call for a repeat performance
**中文：** n. 再演的要求 v. 要求再演

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- repeat（重演）
- curtain call（谢幕）
- reprise（再演）
- recall（再次登场）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perform an encore / demand an encore / encore performance / take an encore**

👉 Example: The enthusiastic crowd demanded an encore after the orchestra's magnificent final movement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audience rose to their feet and shouted for an **encore** after the soprano delivered a breathtaking final aria.
   女高音演唱完令人叹为观止的最后一首咏叹调后，观众起立高呼要求再演一曲。

2. The legendary guitarist performed a moving **encore** that left the entire concert hall in stunned and reverent silence.
   这位传奇吉他手表演了一段感人的加演，让整个音乐厅陷入震撼而敬畏的沉默。

3. Critics noted that the pianist's **encore** selection demonstrated remarkable artistic maturity and a deep understanding of the composer's intent.
   评论家指出，这位钢琴家选择的加演曲目展现了非凡的艺术成熟度和对作曲家意图的深刻理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "encore" as the French word meaning "again" — when the audience shouts "encore," they want to hear the performance once more.
把"encore"看作法语中"再来一次"的意思——当观众高喊"encore"时，他们想再听一次表演。
