# 😶‍🌫️ semiconscious

🔊 /ˌsɛmiˈkɒnʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Half-conscious; partially aware of one's surroundings or mental state
**中文：** adj. 半意识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- half-conscious（半清醒的）
- dazed（恍惚的）
- groggy（昏沉的）
- stuporous（昏迷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **semiconscious state / semiconscious patient / remain semiconscious / barely semiconscious**

👉 Example: The patient remained semiconscious for several hours following the surgical procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The injured hiker was found in a **semiconscious** state by the rescue team after spending the night on the mountainside.
   受伤的徒步旅行者在山坡上过了一夜后，被救援队发现时处于半清醒状态。

2. The doctor noted that the **semiconscious** patient could respond to verbal commands but was unable to maintain sustained awareness.
   医生注意到，这名半意识的病人能对语言指令做出反应，但无法保持持续的清醒。

3. After the severe concussion, the athlete lay **semiconscious** on the field while medical staff rushed to provide immediate assistance.
   严重脑震荡后，运动员半清醒地躺在场上，医护人员迅速赶来提供紧急救助。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "semi-conscious" as "semi" (half) + "conscious" (aware) — you are only halfway awake, like being stuck between sleep and wakefulness.
把"semiconscious"想成"semi"（半）+"conscious"（有意识的）——你只醒了一半，像是卡在睡眠和清醒之间。
