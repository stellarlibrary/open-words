# 🏡 rustic

🔊 /ˈrʌstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to the countryside; simple and charming; rough or unrefined. (n.) A rural person; an unsophisticated country dweller
**中文：** adj. 乡村的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pastoral（田园的）
- rural（农村的）
- bucolic（乡村的）
- homespun（朴素的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rustic charm / rustic furniture / rustic cabin / rustic simplicity**

👉 Example: The old farmhouse had a rustic charm that attracted visitors seeking an escape from modern urban life.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The interior designer incorporated **rustic** wooden beams and stone walls to create a warm, countryside atmosphere.
   室内设计师采用了质朴的木梁和石墙，营造出温馨的乡村氛围。

2. Despite its **rustic** appearance, the renovated barn concealed state-of-the-art technology and modern luxury amenities.
   尽管外表质朴，这座翻新的谷仓内隐藏着最先进的技术和现代化的豪华设施。

3. The artist deliberately adopted a **rustic** style in her paintings to evoke the simplicity of traditional village life.
   这位艺术家在绘画中刻意采用质朴的风格，以唤起传统乡村生活的淳朴之感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rustic" as "rust-ic" — like something aged and weathered with a layer of rust, giving it old-fashioned country character.
把"rustic"想成"rust（锈）+ic"——就像某物因岁月而覆上一层铁锈，散发出古朴的乡村韵味。
