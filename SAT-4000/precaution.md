# 🛡️ precaution

🔊 /prɪˈkɔːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A measure taken in advance to prevent something dangerous, unpleasant, or inconvenient from happening; caution exercised beforehand
**中文：** n. 预防；警惕；谨慎；预防措施

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- safeguard（保障措施）
- preventive measure（预防措施）
- caution（谨慎）
- provision（预备）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **take precautions / safety precaution / as a precaution / necessary precaution**

👉 Example: Residents were advised to take every precaution against flooding as the severe storm approached the coastal region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory enforced strict **precautions** to ensure that hazardous chemicals were handled safely by all research personnel at all times.
   实验室执行严格的预防措施，确保所有研究人员始终安全处理危险化学品。

2. As a **precaution**, the airline cancelled all departing flights until the weather conditions improved sufficiently for safe operations.
   作为预防措施，航空公司取消了所有出发航班，直到天气条件充分改善以确保安全运营。

3. The government issued a list of recommended **precautions** for citizens to follow during the anticipated outbreak of the infectious disease.
   政府发布了一份建议的预防措施清单，供公民在预期的传染病暴发期间遵循。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-caution" — caution taken "pre" (before) something bad happens — it is being careful in advance.
把"precaution"想成"pre（提前）+caution（谨慎）"——在坏事发生之前采取的谨慎措施——就是提前小心。
