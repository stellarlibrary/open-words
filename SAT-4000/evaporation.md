# 💧 evaporation

🔊 /ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of turning from liquid into vapor; the gradual disappearance of something
**中文：** n. 蒸发；消失

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vaporization（汽化）
- dissipation（消散）
- drying（干燥）
- dehydration（脱水）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rate of evaporation / evaporation process / prevent evaporation / natural evaporation**

👉 Example: The rate of evaporation increases significantly when water is exposed to direct sunlight and wind.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rapid **evaporation** of surface water in the drought-stricken region has led to severe agricultural losses and food shortages.
   干旱地区地表水的快速蒸发导致了严重的农业损失和粮食短缺。

2. Scientists measured the **evaporation** rate of various chemical solvents to determine which would be most suitable for the experiment.
   科学家们测量了各种化学溶剂的蒸发速率，以确定哪种最适合该实验。

3. The **evaporation** of public trust in governmental institutions represents one of the greatest challenges facing modern democratic societies.
   公众对政府机构信任的消失是现代民主社会面临的最大挑战之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "evaporation" as "evaporate" + "-tion" (noun ending) — the noun form of evaporate, the process of turning into vapor.
把"evaporation"想成"evaporate"（蒸发）+ "-tion"（名词后缀）——蒸发的名词形式，变成蒸汽的过程。
