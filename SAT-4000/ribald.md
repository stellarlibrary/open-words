# 😈 ribald

🔊 /ˈrɪbəld/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Crude, offensive, or humorously vulgar in language or behavior. (n.) A person who speaks in a vulgar or indecent manner
**中文：** adj. 下流的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vulgar（粗俗的）
- bawdy（下流的）
- coarse（粗鲁的）
- obscene（淫秽的）
- lewd（猥亵的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ribald humor / ribald jokes / ribald laughter / ribald remarks**

👉 Example: The comedian's ribald humor delighted some audience members but offended others.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor warned students that the medieval text contained **ribald** language unsuitable for those easily offended by vulgarity.
   教授警告学生，这篇中世纪文本包含下流的语言，不适合容易被粗俗内容冒犯的人。

2. His **ribald** commentary during the formal dinner drew disapproving glances from the distinguished guests seated nearby.
   他在正式晚宴上的粗俗评论引来了邻座尊贵客人们不赞成的目光。

3. Critics dismissed the novel as merely **ribald** entertainment, lacking the literary depth expected of serious contemporary fiction.
   评论家们将这部小说斥为仅仅是粗俗的消遣，缺乏严肃当代小说应有的文学深度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ribald" as "rib-bald" — imagine someone making bald, unfiltered jokes that hit your ribs from laughing, but in a crude way.
把"ribald"想成"rib-bald"——想象有人讲毫无遮掩的笑话，让你笑得肋骨疼，但方式很粗俗。
