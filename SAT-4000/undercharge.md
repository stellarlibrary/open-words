# 💰 undercharge

🔊 /ˌʌndərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To charge someone less than the proper or standard price; to put an insufficient charge into a battery or device
**中文：** 索价低于常价；充电不足；少收费

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- underprice（定价过低）
- discount（打折）
- undervalue（低估价值）
- shortchange（少找零钱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undercharge a customer / undercharge for services / undercharge a battery**

👉 Example: The store accidentally undercharged us by twenty dollars for the electronic equipment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The contractor consistently **undercharged** his clients for renovation work, which eventually led to severe financial difficulties for his business.
   承包商一直向客户收取低于正常水平的装修费用，最终导致他的企业陷入严重的财务困难。

2. If you habitually **undercharge** for your professional services, clients may begin to question the quality of the work you provide.
   如果你习惯性地对专业服务收费过低，客户可能会开始质疑你所提供工作的质量。

3. The technician warned that continuing to **undercharge** the battery would significantly reduce its overall lifespan and storage capacity.
   技术人员警告说，继续对电池充电不足将显著缩短其整体使用寿命和存储容量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "under-charge" — charging UNDER the proper amount, whether it is money (too little price) or electricity (too little power).
把"undercharge"想成"under（低于）+ charge（收费/充电）"——无论是钱还是电，都低于应有的量。
