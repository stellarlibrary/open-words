# 🎵 choir

🔊 /kwaɪər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An organized group of singers, especially one that performs in a church or concert hall; the part of a church where such a group sings. (v.) To sing together in a group
**中文：** n. 唱诗班；合唱团 v. 合唱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chorus（合唱团）
- ensemble（合奏团）
- glee club（合唱俱乐部）
- chorale（赞美诗合唱团）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **church choir / choir practice / choir director / join the choir / choir concert**

👉 Example: The cathedral choir performed a magnificent rendition of Handel's Messiah during the holiday season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university **choir** received international acclaim after winning the prestigious choral competition held in Vienna.
   这支大学合唱团在维也纳举办的著名合唱比赛中获胜后赢得了国际赞誉。

2. Members of the church **choir** devoted countless hours to rehearsal in preparation for the annual Christmas concert.
   教堂唱诗班成员投入了大量时间排练，为年度圣诞音乐会做准备。

3. The conductor assembled a **choir** of two hundred voices to perform the symphony's final movement with full orchestra.
   指挥组建了一支两百人的合唱队，与全编制管弦乐队共同演出交响曲的最后乐章。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "choir" — it looks like "chair" but with an "o," imagine a group of singers sitting in special chairs in a church, singing together.
"choir"看起来像"chair（椅子）"但多了一个"o"——想象一群歌手坐在教堂的特殊座椅上一起歌唱。
