# 🛡️ antibody

🔊 /ˈæntɪbɑːdi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A protein produced by the immune system in response to a foreign substance (antigen), which neutralizes or destroys toxins and disease-causing organisms.

**中文：** 抗体（免疫系统针对外来物质即抗原所产生的蛋白质，用于中和或消灭毒素和致病微生物）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- immunoglobulin 免疫球蛋白
- immune protein 免疫蛋白
- antitoxin 抗毒素

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **produce antibodies**, **antibody response**, **monoclonal antibody**, **antibody test**, **antibodies against (a virus)**. The plural "antibodies" is very frequently used.

> Example: The vaccine stimulates the body to produce **antibodies** against the virus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After vaccination, the immune system generates specific **antibodies** that provide lasting protection against the disease.
   接种疫苗后，免疫系统会产生特异性抗体，为身体提供对该疾病的持久保护。

2. Scientists developed a monoclonal **antibody** treatment that significantly reduces the severity of the infection.
   科学家们开发了一种单克隆抗体疗法，可显著降低感染的严重程度。

3. The blood test confirmed the presence of **antibodies**, indicating that the patient had previously encountered the pathogen.
   血液检测确认了抗体的存在，表明患者此前曾接触过该病原体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anti-" (against) + "body" (here referring to a foreign body/substance). An **antibody** is your body's defense weapon "against foreign bodies" that invade your system.

"anti-"（对抗）+"body"（此处指外来物质）。**Antibody** 就是你体内"对抗入侵外来物质"的防御武器。
