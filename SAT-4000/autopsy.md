# 🔬 autopsy

🔊 /ˈɔːtɒpsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A postmortem examination of a body to determine the cause of death; a critical analysis after an event.
**中文：** n. 验尸；尸体解剖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- postmortem（验尸）
- necropsy（尸检）
- dissection（解剖）
- examination（检查）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perform an autopsy / conduct an autopsy / autopsy report / autopsy results**

👉 Example: The medical examiner performed an autopsy to determine whether the death was accidental.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coroner ordered an **autopsy** after the unexpected death raised serious questions about potential foul play or negligence.
   验尸官在这起意外死亡引发关于潜在犯罪或疏忽的严重质疑后下令进行验尸。

2. The **autopsy** revealed that the victim had ingested a lethal quantity of poison several hours before being discovered.
   尸体解剖显示，受害者在被发现前数小时摄入了致命剂量的毒药。

3. After the project failed, the team conducted a figurative **autopsy** to identify which critical decisions led to its collapse.
   项目失败后，团队进行了一次比喻性的"验尸"，以确定哪些关键决策导致了项目的崩溃。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Auto" means self, "opsy" means seeing — an autopsy is seeing for yourself what caused the death.
"Auto"意为自己，"opsy"意为观看——autopsy就是亲眼查看死因。
