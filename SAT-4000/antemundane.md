# 🌌 antemundane

🔊 /ˌæn.tɪˈmʌn.deɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Existing or occurring before the creation of the world; pertaining to a time before the physical universe came into being.

**中文：** 世界形成前的；宇宙创造之前的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- precosmic（宇宙形成前的）
- primordial（原始的）
- preternatural（超自然的）
- preexistent（先在的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** antemundane existence, antemundane state, antemundane realm, antemundane period

**Example:** The theologian's lecture explored the concept of **antemundane** divine will and its implications for creation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medieval philosophers debated whether the soul had an **antemundane** existence before its incarnation in the physical body.
   中世纪哲学家们争论灵魂在进入肉体之前是否有一种世界形成前的存在。

2. The cosmologist's hypothesis posited certain **antemundane** conditions that may have given rise to the observable universe.
   这位宇宙学家的假说提出了某些可能产生可观测宇宙的世界形成前的条件。

3. In theological discourse, the concept of **antemundane** decrees refers to divine plans established before creation itself.
   在神学话语中，"世界形成前的旨意"概念指的是在创世之前就确立的神圣计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Ante-" (before) + "mundane" (from Latin *mundus*, meaning world) = before the world existed. 前缀 ante-（之前）+ mundane（来自拉丁语 mundus，世界）= 世界存在之前的。注意这里的 mundane 不是"平凡"的意思，而是指"世界"。
