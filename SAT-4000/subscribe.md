# 📬 subscribe

🔊 /səbˈskraɪb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To arrange to receive something regularly; to donate money; to purchase shares; to agree with; to sign a document
**中文：** v. 订购，订阅，预订

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enroll（注册）
- sign up（报名）
- pledge（承诺捐款）
- endorse（赞同）
- contribute（捐助）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subscribe to a magazine / subscribe to a theory / subscribe to a service / subscribe funds**

👉 Example: Many scholars subscribe to the view that environmental factors play a decisive role in development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students are encouraged to **subscribe** to reputable academic journals to stay current with research in their field.
   鼓励学生订阅有声誉的学术期刊，以跟上其领域的最新研究。

2. The philanthropist decided to **subscribe** a generous sum to the university's new scholarship endowment fund.
   这位慈善家决定向大学新的奖学金捐赠基金捐赠一笔可观的款项。

3. Not all historians **subscribe** to the conventional interpretation of the causes of the Industrial Revolution.
   并非所有历史学家都赞同关于工业革命原因的传统解读。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-scribe" as "under-write" — you write your name under a document to show agreement or commitment.
把"subscribe"想成"sub"（在下面）+"scribe"（写）——在文件下面写上你的名字，表示同意或承诺。
