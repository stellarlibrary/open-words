# 🔵 protuberate

🔊 /proʊˈtjuːbəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To swell or bulge outward; to form a rounded protuberance beyond the surrounding surface
**中文：** v. 隆起；凸出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bulge（鼓起）
- swell（膨胀）
- protrude（突出）
- distend（膨胀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **protuberate outward / protuberate from the surface / protuberating mass**

👉 Example: The tumor was found to protuberate from the tissue wall, requiring immediate surgical intervention.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The volcanic formation had begun to **protuberate** from the ocean floor, signaling the potential birth of a new island.
   火山构造已开始从海底隆起，预示着一个新岛屿可能诞生。

2. Examination revealed that the damaged pipe had started to **protuberate** under excessive internal pressure from the heated steam.
   检查发现受损管道已在加热蒸汽的过大内部压力下开始凸出。

3. The artist deliberately shaped the clay to **protuberate** in irregular patterns, creating a textured sculpture of striking dimensionality.
   艺术家故意将黏土塑造成不规则的凸起图案，创造出一件纹理鲜明、层次感突出的雕塑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-tuber-ate" as containing "tuber" — like a potato tuber that bulges out from the ground, protuberate means to swell outward.
把"protuberate"想成含有"tuber（块茎）"——就像土豆块茎从地面鼓出来一样，protuberate表示向外隆起。
