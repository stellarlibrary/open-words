# 💡 laser

🔊 /ˈleɪzər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A device that emits a narrow, intense beam of coherent light through stimulated emission of radiation; the beam itself
**中文：** n. 激光；激光器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beam（光束）
- ray（射线）
- light amplifier（光放大器）
- coherent light（相干光）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laser beam / laser surgery / laser pointer / laser-focused / laser technology**

👉 Example: The surgeon used a precision laser to correct the patient's deteriorating eyesight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advanced **laser** technology has revolutionized modern surgical procedures by enabling minimally invasive operations with remarkable precision.
   先进的激光技术通过实现具有卓越精度的微创手术，彻底改变了现代外科手术。

2. The physicist demonstrated how a **laser** beam could transmit data at unprecedented speeds through fiber-optic cable networks.
   这位物理学家演示了激光束如何通过光纤电缆网络以前所未有的速度传输数据。

3. Military researchers are developing high-powered **laser** systems capable of intercepting and neutralizing incoming aerial threats.
   军事研究人员正在开发能够拦截和消除来袭空中威胁的高功率激光系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"LASER" is actually an acronym: Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation. Remember it as a super-focused, amplified light beam.
"LASER"实际上是首字母缩写：Light Amplification by Stimulated Emission of Radiation（受激辐射光放大）。记住它是一种超级聚焦的放大光束。
