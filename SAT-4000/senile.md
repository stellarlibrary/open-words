# 👴 senile

🔊 /ˈsiːnaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or characteristic of old age; showing a decline in mental faculties associated with aging
**中文：** adj. 高龄的，衰老的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aged（年迈的）
- decrepit（老朽的）
- doddering（老态龙钟的）
- infirm（衰弱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **senile dementia / go senile / senile decay / become senile**

👉 Example: The physician diagnosed the elderly patient with senile dementia after observing significant cognitive decline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers studied the neurological mechanisms underlying **senile** cognitive deterioration in patients over the age of eighty-five.
   研究人员研究了八十五岁以上患者老年性认知退化背后的神经学机制。

2. Despite his advanced age, the retired professor showed no signs of **senile** decline and continued to publish academic papers regularly.
   尽管年事已高，这位退休教授没有表现出任何衰老迹象，并继续定期发表学术论文。

3. The family sought professional care when their grandmother began exhibiting **senile** behaviors, including memory loss and persistent confusion.
   当祖母开始出现记忆丧失和持续性意识混乱等老年症状时，家人寻求了专业护理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "senile" as sharing the root "sen-" with "senior" — both relate to old age, but "senile" specifically implies mental decline that comes with aging.
把"senile"想成和"senior"共享词根"sen-"——两者都与老年有关，但"senile"特指随年龄而来的心智衰退。
