# 🎖️ enlist

🔊 /ɪnˈlɪst/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To enroll or be enrolled in the armed forces; to engage someone's help or support for a cause
**中文：** v. 支持；从军；应募；征募

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recruit（招募）
- enroll（登记）
- mobilize（动员）
- engage（雇用）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **enlist in the army / enlist support / enlist the help of / enlist volunteers**

👉 Example: The organization sought to enlist the support of local businesses for its charitable initiative.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The campaign manager worked tirelessly to **enlist** volunteers from every district before the upcoming election season.
   竞选经理在即将到来的选举季前不懈努力地从每个选区征募志愿者。

2. At the age of eighteen, he chose to **enlist** in the navy rather than pursue a traditional university education.
   十八岁时，他选择参军加入海军，而非接受传统的大学教育。

3. Scientists sought to **enlist** the cooperation of international governments to address the global environmental crisis effectively.
   科学家们寻求获得各国政府的合作支持，以有效应对全球环境危机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-list" as putting someone's name "on a list" — when you enlist, your name goes on the official roster.
把"enlist"想成把某人的名字"放到名单（list）上"——当你入伍时，你的名字就被登记在正式花名册上了。
