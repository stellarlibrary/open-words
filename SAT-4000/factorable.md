# 🧮 factorable

🔊 /ˈfæktərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of being separated or resolved into mathematical factors; able to be decomposed into constituent elements
**中文：** 可分解因子的；可因式分解的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decomposable（可分解的）
- divisible（可除的）
- resolvable（可分解的）
- reducible（可化简的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **factorable expression / factorable polynomial / factorable equation / easily factorable**

👉 Example: The instructor asked students to determine whether each quadratic expression was factorable over the integers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The algebra textbook included exercises requiring students to identify which trinomial expressions were **factorable** using standard methods.
   代数教科书中包含练习，要求学生识别哪些三项式可以用标准方法进行因式分解。

2. Not every polynomial encountered in advanced mathematics is readily **factorable**, and some require numerical approximation techniques instead.
   高等数学中遇到的并非每个多项式都容易分解因子，有些需要用数值近似方法代替。

3. The professor explained that a **factorable** integer can always be expressed as a product of prime numbers according to fundamental theorem.
   教授解释说，根据基本定理，一个可分解的整数总能表示为质数的乘积。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "factor-able" as "able to be factored" — just add "-able" (capable of) to "factor" to get something that can be broken into parts.
把"factorable"想成"able to be factored"(能被分解的)——只需在"factor"后加上"-able"(能够)，就表示可以被分解成各部分。
