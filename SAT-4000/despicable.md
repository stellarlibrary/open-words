# 😤 despicable

🔊 /dɪˈspɪkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deserving hatred and contempt; morally reprehensible; utterly vile
**中文：** adj. 可鄙的；卑劣的；令人不齿的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contemptible（可鄙的）
- vile（卑鄙的）
- loathsome（令人厌恶的）
- abhorrent（可憎的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **despicable act / despicable behavior / despicable crime / truly despicable**

👉 Example: The court condemned the defendant's despicable conduct as a fundamental violation of human decency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist exposed the **despicable** practices of the corporation, which had knowingly contaminated the local water supply.
   记者揭露了该公司可鄙的行为——他们明知故犯地污染了当地的供水系统。

2. History regards the exploitation of vulnerable populations during wartime as one of the most **despicable** acts of aggression.
   历史将战时对弱势群体的剥削视为最卑劣的侵略行为之一。

3. The senator called the fraudulent scheme **despicable** and demanded an immediate investigation by federal regulatory authorities.
   参议员称这一欺诈计划卑劣可耻，并要求联邦监管机构立即展开调查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-spic-able" as "able to be spat upon (spic)" — something so vile that people want to spit on it.
把"despicable"想成"able to be spat upon（spic）"——某事物如此卑劣，以至于人们想往上吐口水。
