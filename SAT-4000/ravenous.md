# 🐺 ravenous

🔊 /ˈrævənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely hungry; having a voracious appetite; greedy or eager for something
**中文：** adj. 贪婪的；渴望的；狼吞虎咽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- voracious（贪婪的）
- famished（饥饿的）
- insatiable（贪得无厌的）
- starving（极度饥饿的）
- gluttonous（暴食的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ravenous appetite / ravenous hunger / ravenous for knowledge / ravenous wolves / absolutely ravenous**

👉 Example: After the twelve-hour hike, the exhausted climbers were absolutely ravenous and devoured their meals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students returned from the field expedition with **ravenous** appetites, consuming everything available in the dining hall.
   学生们从野外考察归来时饥肠辘辘，把餐厅里所有的食物都吃光了。

2. The young scholar possessed a **ravenous** thirst for knowledge that drove her to read extensively across multiple disciplines.
   这位年轻学者对知识有着贪婪的渴求，驱使她广泛阅读多个学科的书籍。

3. A pack of **ravenous** wolves descended upon the weakened herd, seizing the opportunity to hunt during the harsh winter months.
   一群饥饿的狼扑向虚弱的兽群，抓住严冬时节的机会进行捕猎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "raven-ous" — like a raven, the bird known for its greedy, scavenging appetite, always hungry and searching for food.
把"ravenous"想成"raven（渡鸦）+ ous"——渡鸦以贪婪的食欲闻名，总是饥肠辘辘地寻找食物。
