# 🎭 engrossing

🔊 /ɪnˈɡroʊsɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** So interesting or compelling that it absorbs all of one's attention; fascinating and captivating
**中文：** adj. 引人入胜的；全神贯注的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- captivating（迷人的）
- absorbing（吸引人的）
- fascinating（令人着迷的）
- riveting（引人入胜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **engrossing story / engrossing performance / engrossing narrative / engrossing documentary**

👉 Example: The documentary presented such an engrossing account of the expedition that viewers could scarcely look away.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian delivered an **engrossing** lecture on ancient civilizations that held the entire auditorium spellbound for over two hours.
   这位历史学家做了一场关于古代文明的引人入胜的讲座，让整个礼堂的听众入迷了两个多小时。

2. Critics praised the film as an **engrossing** portrayal of resilience, capturing the human spirit's triumph over extraordinary adversity.
   评论家称赞这部电影是对韧性的引人入胜的描绘，捕捉了人类精神战胜非凡逆境的力量。

3. The **engrossing** novel kept readers turning pages late into the night, eager to discover the resolution of its central mystery.
   这部引人入胜的小说让读者深夜仍在翻页，渴望发现核心悬念的结局。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "engrossing" as the adjective form of "engross" — something so engrossing pulls you in completely, like quicksand for your mind.
把"engrossing"想成"engross"的形容词形式——引人入胜的事物将你完全拉入其中，就像头脑的流沙一样。
