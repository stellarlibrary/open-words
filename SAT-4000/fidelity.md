# 🤝 fidelity

🔊 /fɪˈdelɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Faithfulness to a person, cause, or belief; the degree of exactness with which something is reproduced
**中文：** n. 忠诚；忠实；精确；保真度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- loyalty（忠诚）
- faithfulness（忠实）
- allegiance（效忠）
- devotion（奉献）
- accuracy（精确）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fidelity to / high fidelity / marital fidelity / fidelity of reproduction / with fidelity**

👉 Example: The translator was praised for the remarkable fidelity of her rendition to the original text.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The soldier's unwavering **fidelity** to his country was honored with the highest military distinction at the national ceremony.
   这位士兵对国家坚定不移的忠诚在国家典礼上被授予最高军事荣誉。

2. Audio engineers strive to achieve high **fidelity** in sound reproduction so that recordings faithfully capture live performances.
   音频工程师努力实现高保真音响再现，使录音忠实地捕捉现场演出。

3. The biographer approached her subject with strict **fidelity** to historical facts, refusing to embellish or dramatize events.
   这位传记作者严格忠实于历史事实来撰写其主题，拒绝对事件进行美化或戏剧化处理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fidelity" as "fidel-ity" — "fidel" comes from Latin "fidelis" meaning faithful, like a faithful dog that never leaves your side.
把"fidelity"想成"fidel-ity"——"fidel"来自拉丁语"fidelis"，意为忠诚的，就像一只忠实的狗永远不离开你身边。
