# 😤 nuisance

🔊 /ˈnjuːsns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person, thing, or circumstance causing inconvenience or annoyance; something harmful or offensive
**中文：** n. 讨厌的人；损害；麻烦事；讨厌的东西

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- annoyance（烦恼）
- pest（讨厌的人）
- irritation（恼怒的事）
- bother（麻烦）
- inconvenience（不便）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public nuisance / what a nuisance / nuisance value / make a nuisance of oneself**

👉 Example: The persistent noise from the construction site became a real nuisance to nearby residents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city council enacted new ordinances to address the growing **nuisance** of unauthorized dumping in residential neighborhoods.
   市议会颁布了新条例，以解决住宅区未经授权倾倒垃圾这一日益严重的麻烦问题。

2. Invasive species have become a significant ecological **nuisance**, threatening biodiversity and disrupting native plant communities.
   入侵物种已成为重大的生态危害，威胁生物多样性并破坏本地植物群落。

3. The attorney argued that the defendant's repeated violations constituted a public **nuisance** warranting immediate injunctive relief.
   律师辩称被告的反复违规行为构成公共妨害，应当立即获得禁令救济。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nuisance" as "new sense of annoyance" — every time a nuisance appears, you feel a new sense of irritation.
把"nuisance"想成"new sense of annoyance"——每当麻烦出现时，你就会感到一种新的烦躁。
