# 🧪 pipette

🔊 /pɪˈpɛt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A slender tube used in a laboratory to transfer or measure small quantities of liquid
**中文：** n. 移液管；吸移管；吸液管

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dropper（滴管）
- transfer tube（转移管）
- measuring tube（量管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **use a pipette / pipette the solution / graduated pipette / volumetric pipette**

👉 Example: The researcher carefully used a pipette to transfer exactly five milliliters of the reagent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician used a calibrated **pipette** to measure precise volumes of the chemical solution for the experiment.
   实验室技术员使用经过校准的移液管来精确测量实验所需的化学溶液体积。

2. Students must learn to handle a **pipette** correctly before they are permitted to conduct independent research in the lab.
   学生在被允许在实验室进行独立研究之前，必须学会正确操作移液管。

3. A contaminated **pipette** can compromise the integrity of an entire experiment, rendering all collected data unreliable.
   被污染的移液管可能会损害整个实验的完整性，使所有收集的数据变得不可靠。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pip-ette" as a "tiny pipe" — a pipette is essentially a small, precise pipe used to move liquids drop by drop.
把"pipette"想成"小pipe（管子）"——移液管本质上就是一根精密的小管，用来逐滴移动液体。
