# 💰 pension

🔊 /ˈpenʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A regular payment made to a retired person or to their surviving dependents; an allowance or subsidy. (v.) To grant a pension to someone
**中文：** n. 养老金，退休金；抚恤金；津贴 v. 发给养老金或抚恤金

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- retirement fund（退休基金）
- annuity（年金）
- allowance（津贴）
- stipend（定期津贴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pension plan / pension fund / receive a pension / pension off / retirement pension**

👉 Example: After thirty years of service, she finally began to receive her well-earned pension.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government announced significant reforms to the national **pension** system in order to address the growing demands of an aging population.
   政府宣布对国家养老金制度进行重大改革，以应对老龄化人口日益增长的需求。

2. Many employees are increasingly concerned that their **pension** funds may not provide adequate financial security during their retirement years.
   许多员工越来越担心他们的养老基金可能无法在退休期间提供足够的经济保障。

3. The corporation decided to **pension** off several senior executives as part of a comprehensive organizational restructuring initiative.
   该公司决定让几位高级管理人员领取退休金退休，作为全面组织重组计划的一部分。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pen-sion" — after years of signing documents with a "pen," you earn a "pension" for your long service.
把"pension"想成"pen（笔）+ sion"——用笔签了多年文件后，你终于赢得了退休金。
