# 📦 quantity

🔊 /ˈkwɑːntəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An amount or number of something; a considerable number or amount
**中文：** n. 量，数量；大量；总量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- amount（数量）
- volume（体积，量）
- number（数目）
- measure（度量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **large quantity / in quantity / quantity of / a vast quantity**

👉 Example: The factory produced a large quantity of goods to meet the rising consumer demand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers collected a significant **quantity** of data before drawing any conclusions about the experimental outcomes.
   研究人员在得出实验结论之前收集了大量数据。

2. When purchased in **quantity**, raw materials are considerably less expensive than buying them in small batches.
   大量购买原材料时，价格比小批量购买要便宜得多。

3. The sheer **quantity** of applications received this year overwhelmed the university admissions office and its staff.
   今年收到的申请数量之多令大学招生办公室及其工作人员应接不暇。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quantity" as "quant-ity" — "quant" sounds like "count," and quantity is something you can count or measure.
把"quantity"想成"quant-ity"——"quant"听起来像"count（计数）"，而quantity就是你可以计数或测量的东西。
