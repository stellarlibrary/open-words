# 💖 adoration

🔊 /ˌædəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Deep love, devotion, or worship; profound admiration and reverence
**中文：** n. 崇拜；爱慕；敬慕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- worship（崇拜）
- devotion（热爱）
- reverence（崇敬）
- veneration（尊崇）
- idolization（偶像化崇拜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adoration for / adoration of / mutual adoration / blind adoration**

👉 Example: The poet expressed his undying adoration for the beauty of the natural world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist's **adoration** of classical music was evident in every composition he produced throughout his career.
   这位艺术家对古典音乐的热爱体现在他整个职业生涯创作的每一部作品中。

2. Her **adoration** for her mentor grew stronger as she witnessed his unwavering commitment to scientific inquiry.
   随着她见证导师对科学探索坚定不移的投入，她对导师的崇敬之情愈发深厚。

3. The crowd gazed at the monument with an expression of silent **adoration** and profound national pride.
   人群以无声的崇敬和深切的民族自豪感注视着纪念碑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adoration" as "a-DOOR-ation" — you open the door of your heart wide for someone you deeply adore.
把"adoration"想成"a-DOOR-ation"——你为深爱的人大开心门(door)，表达无限崇拜和爱慕。
