# 🔬 solute

🔊 /ˈsɒljuːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance that is dissolved in another substance to form a solution. (adj.) Dissolved in a solution
**中文：** n. 溶质

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dissolved substance（溶解物）
- soluble matter（可溶物质）
- dissolute material（溶解材料）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **solute concentration / solute molecules / solute particles / dissolve a solute**

👉 Example: The solute must be thoroughly mixed into the solvent to achieve a uniform concentration.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher carefully measured the amount of **solute** required to prepare a precisely calibrated standard solution for analysis.
   研究人员仔细测量了制备精确校准标准溶液所需的溶质量。

2. As the temperature increased, the capacity of the solvent to accommodate additional **solute** molecules expanded significantly.
   随着温度升高，溶剂容纳额外溶质分子的能力显著增强。

3. The concentration of the **solute** in the aqueous mixture was determined through spectrophotometric methods in the laboratory.
   水溶液中溶质的浓度通过实验室中的分光光度法测定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "solute" as "solo + ute (small)" — the small solo substance that dissolves and disappears into the solvent.
把"solute"想成"solo + ute（小的）"——那个溶解并消失在溶剂中的小小独立物质。
