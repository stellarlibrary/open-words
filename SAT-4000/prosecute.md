# ⚖️ prosecute

🔊 /ˈprɑːsɪkjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To institute legal proceedings against someone; to carry out or pursue a course of action; to engage in or practice
**中文：** v. 实行，从事；告发，起诉；依法进行

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indict（起诉）
- charge（控告）
- pursue（追究）
- litigate（提起诉讼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prosecute a case / prosecute offenders / prosecute a war / prosecute to the fullest extent of the law**

👉 Example: The district attorney decided to prosecute the case despite limited physical evidence available.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government vowed to **prosecute** all individuals found guilty of engaging in insider trading and securities fraud.
   政府誓言将起诉所有被发现从事内幕交易和证券欺诈的个人。

2. Military commanders made the controversial decision to **prosecute** the campaign through the harsh winter months despite heavy casualties.
   军事指挥官做出了有争议的决定，在严冬时节继续推进战役，尽管伤亡惨重。

3. Environmental agencies now have broader authority to **prosecute** corporations that violate federal pollution and emissions standards.
   环境机构现在拥有更广泛的权力来起诉违反联邦污染和排放标准的企业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prosecute" as "pro-EXECUTE" — to professionally execute legal action against someone, bringing them to court.
把"prosecute"想成"pro-EXECUTE"——专业地执行法律行动，将某人告上法庭。
