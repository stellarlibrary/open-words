# ✂️ detach

🔊 /dɪˈtætʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To separate or disconnect from something; to send a group of soldiers or ships on a separate mission
**中文：** v. 分离；分开；派遣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- separate（分离）
- disconnect（断开）
- disengage（脱离）
- unfasten（解开）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **detach from / detach oneself / detach a unit / emotionally detach**

👉 Example: The therapist advised her to detach herself emotionally from the outcome of the decision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers were able to **detach** the damaged module from the spacecraft before it could compromise the entire mission.
   工程师们在损坏的模块危及整个任务之前成功地将其从航天器上分离了下来。

2. The general ordered his commander to **detach** a battalion and send it north to reinforce the vulnerable supply lines.
   将军命令他的指挥官派遣一个营向北增援脆弱的补给线。

3. Scholars must learn to **detach** personal bias from their analysis in order to produce rigorous and objective research.
   学者必须学会将个人偏见与分析分开，以产出严谨而客观的研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "detach" as "de-attach" — the prefix "de-" means to undo, so you are undoing an attachment, pulling things apart.
把"detach"想成"de-attach"——前缀"de-"表示撤销，所以就是撤销连接，把东西拉开。
