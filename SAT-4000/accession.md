# 🏛️ accession

🔊 /əkˈsɛʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of attaining or being elevated to a position of power or authority; an increase or addition.
**中文：** 就职，就任（尤指高位）；增加物，添加物。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- succession（继承）
- inauguration（就职）
- attainment（获得）
- acquisition（获取）
- addition（增加）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accession to + position/throne/power**

👉 Example: The prince's accession to the throne was celebrated throughout the kingdom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The queen's **accession** to power marked the beginning of a prosperous era for the nation.
   女王的登基标志着国家繁荣时代的开始。

2. The library's latest **accession** of rare manuscripts greatly enriched its archival collection.
   图书馆最新添加的珍稀手稿极大地丰富了其馆藏。

3. Upon his **accession** to the presidency, he immediately introduced sweeping economic reforms.
   他就任总统后，立即推行了大规模的经济改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "access" + "ion" — gaining access to a higher position or adding access to a collection.
把 accession 想成"获得通道（access）"——获得进入高位的通道，即"就职"；或增添新的藏品。
