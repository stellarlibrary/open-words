# ♟️ tactician

🔊 /tækˈtɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who is adept at planning tactics; a person skilled in maneuvering to achieve goals
**中文：** n. 有谋略的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- strategist（战略家）
- planner（规划者）
- schemer（谋划者）
- mastermind（策划者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brilliant tactician / political tactician / military tactician / skilled tactician**

👉 Example: The general was known as a brilliant tactician who could outmaneuver far larger opposing forces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political **tactician** devised a campaign strategy that successfully appealed to voters across multiple demographic groups.
   这位政治谋略家制定了一项竞选策略，成功地吸引了多个人口群体的选民。

2. As a seasoned **tactician**, the chess grandmaster anticipated her opponent's moves at least five turns in advance.
   作为一位经验丰富的谋略家，这位国际象棋大师至少提前五步预判对手的走法。

3. The CEO proved to be an astute **tactician**, navigating the company through a turbulent market with minimal financial losses.
   这位首席执行官证明自己是一位精明的谋略家，带领公司以最小的财务损失渡过了动荡的市场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tactic-ian" — a person who specializes in "tactics," like a musician specializes in music or a magician in magic.
把"tactician"想成"tactic-ian"——一个专门研究"tactics"（战术）的人，就像musician专攻音乐、magician专攻魔术一样。
