# 🌡️ thermometer

🔊 /θərˈmɒmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An instrument used for measuring and indicating temperature, typically consisting of a graduated glass tube containing mercury or alcohol.
**中文：** n. 温度计；体温计

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- temperature gauge（温度表）
- thermostat（恒温器）
- pyrometer（高温计）
- temperature indicator（温度指示器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clinical thermometer / digital thermometer / mercury thermometer / read a thermometer**

👉 Example: The nurse placed a digital thermometer under the patient's tongue to check for signs of fever.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **thermometer** outside the research station registered temperatures well below negative forty degrees during the Antarctic winter expedition.
   南极冬季考察期间，研究站外的温度计记录的温度远低于零下四十度。

2. Scientists use highly sensitive **thermometers** to detect minute temperature fluctuations in controlled laboratory environments with extreme precision.
   科学家使用高灵敏度温度计以极高的精度检测受控实验室环境中的微小温度波动。

3. The invention of the mercury **thermometer** in the eighteenth century marked a significant advancement in the history of scientific measurement.
   十八世纪水银温度计的发明标志着科学测量史上的一次重大进步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "thermo-meter" as "heat measurer" — "thermo" means heat and "meter" means to measure, so it literally measures heat.
把"thermometer"想成"热量测量器"——"thermo"表示热，"meter"表示测量，所以它字面意思就是测量热量。
