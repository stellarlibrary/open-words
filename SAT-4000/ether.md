# 🌌 ether

🔊 /ˈiːθər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A volatile organic compound used as an anesthetic; the hypothetical medium once believed to permeate space; the upper regions of the sky
**中文：** n. 乙醚；以太；苍天；天空

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- atmosphere（大气）
- firmament（苍穹）
- heavens（天空）
- sky（天空）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vanish into the ether / ether of space / diethyl ether / out of the ether**

👉 Example: The radio signal seemed to disappear into the ether without reaching any known receiver.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Early physicists believed that light waves propagated through a mysterious substance called the **ether** that filled all of space.
   早期物理学家认为光波通过一种充满整个空间的被称为以太的神秘物质传播。

2. The surgeon administered **ether** as an anesthetic before the procedure, which was a revolutionary practice in nineteenth-century medicine.
   外科医生在手术前施用乙醚作为麻醉剂，这在十九世纪的医学中是一项革命性的做法。

3. Her whispered words seemed to float away into the **ether**, lost forever among the vast silence of the empty hall.
   她低声说出的话语似乎飘散到了苍穹之中，永远消失在空旷大厅的沉寂里。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ether" as "E-THERE" — something ethereal that is out there in the vast, invisible space beyond our reach.
把"ether"想成"E-THERE"——某种飘渺的东西存在于我们触及不到的广阔无形空间之中。
