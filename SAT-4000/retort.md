# 💬 retort

🔊 /rɪˈtɔːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sharp, witty, or angry reply; a glass vessel with a long neck used for distillation. (v.) To reply sharply or angrily
**中文：** n. 反驳，顶嘴；曲颈瓶 v. 反驳，反击

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rejoinder（回答）
- riposte（机智的回击）
- comeback（反驳）
- rebuttal（反驳）
- counter（回击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sharp retort / witty retort / retort angrily / retort that...**

👉 Example: When accused of negligence, the manager fired back with a sharp retort that silenced the room.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator delivered a biting **retort** to the journalist's provocative question about her stance on immigration reform.
   这位参议员对记者关于其移民改革立场的挑衅性问题进行了尖锐的反驳。

2. He quickly **retorted** that the committee's findings were based on incomplete data and fundamentally flawed methodology.
   他迅速反驳说，委员会的调查结果基于不完整的数据和根本有缺陷的方法论。

3. In the chemistry laboratory, students learned to use a glass **retort** for the careful distillation of volatile compounds.
   在化学实验室里，学生们学习使用玻璃曲颈瓶来小心蒸馏挥发性化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-TORT" — "tort" sounds like "twist" (from Latin "torquere") — a retort twists someone's words back at them.
把"retort"想成"re-TORT"——"tort"听起来像"扭转"（源自拉丁语"torquere"）——反驳就是把别人的话扭转回去。
