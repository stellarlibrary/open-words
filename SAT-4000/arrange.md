# 📋 arrange

🔊 /əˈreɪndʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To put things in a particular order; to organize or make plans for something; to set in proper sequence
**中文：** v. 安排；排列；整理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- organize（组织）
- coordinate（协调）
- sort（整理）
- classify（分类）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arrange a meeting / arrange flowers / arrange in order / arrange for someone to do something**

👉 Example: She arranged the documents alphabetically to make retrieval more efficient.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee will **arrange** a formal reception for the visiting dignitaries at the grand hall next Thursday evening.
   委员会将于下周四晚在大厅为来访贵宾安排一场正式招待会。

2. The librarian carefully **arranged** the rare manuscripts in chronological order to facilitate academic research and study.
   图书馆员仔细地按时间顺序排列珍贵手稿，以便于学术研究和学习。

3. Please **arrange** for a qualified interpreter to be present during the international negotiations scheduled for tomorrow.
   请安排一名合格的口译员出席定于明天举行的国际谈判。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-range" — to arrange is to put items into a range or sequence, giving everything its proper place.
把"arrange"想成"a range（一个范围）"——安排就是把东西放入适当的范围和顺序中。
