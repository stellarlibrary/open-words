# 🗿 figurine

🔊 /ˌfɪɡjəˈriːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small carved or molded figure; a statuette, typically decorative or ornamental
**中文：** n. 小雕像；小塑像

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- statuette（小雕像）
- sculpture（雕塑）
- carving（雕刻品）
- ornament（装饰品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **porcelain figurine / collectible figurine / decorative figurine / clay figurine**

👉 Example: The antique porcelain figurine was displayed prominently in the museum's collection of decorative arts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Archaeologists unearthed a delicate bronze **figurine** dating back to the third century from beneath the ancient temple ruins.
   考古学家在古庙废墟下方出土了一件可追溯到三世纪的精美青铜小雕像。

2. The artist crafted each ceramic **figurine** by hand, meticulously painting every detail with remarkable skill and patience.
   艺术家手工制作每件陶瓷小雕像，以非凡的技巧和耐心精心绘制每一个细节。

3. A rare jade **figurine** from the Ming Dynasty was sold at auction for a record-breaking sum last month.
   上个月，一件明代稀有玉雕小像在拍卖会上以破纪录的价格售出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "figurine" as a small "figure" with the diminutive suffix "-ine" — it is literally a little figure or statuette.
把"figurine"想成一个小的"figure"加上表示"小"的后缀"-ine"——它就是一个小人像或小雕像。
