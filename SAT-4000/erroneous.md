# ❌ erroneous

🔊 /ɪˈroʊniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wrong; incorrect; based on or containing error
**中文：** adj. 错误的；不正确的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incorrect（不正确的）
- mistaken（错误的）
- fallacious（谬误的）
- inaccurate（不准确的）
- flawed（有缺陷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **erroneous assumption / erroneous conclusion / erroneous belief / erroneous data**

👉 Example: The report was retracted because it was based on erroneous data collected during the initial phase.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court overturned the conviction after discovering that the verdict had been based on **erroneous** forensic evidence.
   法院在发现判决基于错误的法医证据后撤销了定罪。

2. Several **erroneous** assumptions in the financial model led the corporation to severely underestimate its projected annual losses.
   财务模型中的几个错误假设导致该公司严重低估了其预计的年度亏损。

3. The journalist issued a public correction after publishing **erroneous** information about the senator's voting record in Congress.
   记者在发布了关于参议员国会投票记录的错误信息后发表了公开更正。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "erroneous" as "error-neous" — directly related to "error," meaning full of errors or wrong.
把"erroneous"想成"error（错误）-neous"——直接与"error"相关，意为充满错误的、不正确的。
