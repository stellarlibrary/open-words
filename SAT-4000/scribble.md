# ✏️ scribble

🔊 /ˈskrɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Something written hastily or carelessly; careless handwriting; a trivial composition. (v.) To write carelessly or hastily; to write poorly
**中文：** n. 潦草的笔迹

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scrawl（潦草书写）
- doodle（涂鸦）
- scratch（乱写）
- jot（草草记下）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **scribble a note / scribble down / illegible scribble / scribble in the margins**

👉 Example: He would often scribble notes in the margins of his textbook during lectures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician's hasty **scribble** on the prescription was nearly impossible for the pharmacist to decipher without considerable effort.
   医生在处方上潦草的笔迹让药剂师费了很大劲才勉强辨认出来。

2. She managed to **scribble** the essential details of the witness testimony before the court session officially adjourned for the afternoon.
   她在法庭下午正式休庭前设法草草记下了证人证词的关键细节。

3. The archaeologists discovered ancient **scribbles** on the cave walls that provided valuable insight into early human communication practices.
   考古学家在洞穴壁上发现了古代的涂写痕迹，为早期人类交流方式提供了宝贵的见解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scribble" as "scrib-ble" — "scrib" comes from the Latin "scribere" (to write), and the "-ble" ending suggests doing it sloppily or repeatedly.
把"scribble"想成"scrib-ble"——"scrib"来自拉丁语"scribere"（书写），"-ble"的结尾暗示潦草地或反复地书写。
