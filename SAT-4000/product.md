# 📦 product

🔊 /ˈprɒdʌkt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Something produced by a process; a result or consequence; a quantity obtained by multiplication; a work of creative effort
**中文：** n. 产品；结果；乘积；作品；生成物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commodity（商品）
- output（产出）
- result（结果）
- creation（创作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **product of / product line / product launch / by-product / end product**

👉 Example: The new smartphone is the product of three years of intensive research and development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The outstanding research paper was the **product** of years of meticulous data collection and rigorous scholarly analysis.
   这篇杰出的研究论文是多年细致数据收集和严格学术分析的结果。

2. The pharmaceutical company invested heavily to ensure each **product** met the stringent safety standards set by regulators.
   该制药公司大量投资以确保每一款产品都符合监管机构制定的严格安全标准。

3. In advanced mathematics, the **product** of two negative integers always yields a positive integer as the result.
   在高等数学中，两个负整数的乘积总是得出一个正整数。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-duct" as "something led forward (produced)" — from Latin "producere," meaning to lead forth or bring out.
把"product"想成"pro（向前）+ duct（引导）"——从拉丁语"producere"而来，意为引出、产出。
