# 🔢 multiply

🔊 /ˈmʌltɪplaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adverb, adjective | 动词, 副词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To increase in number or quantity; to perform mathematical multiplication; to breed or reproduce. (adv.) In multiple ways. (adj.) Having many layers or aspects
**中文：** v. 乘；使增加；使繁殖；使相乘 adv. 多样地；复合地 adj. 多层的；多样的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proliferate（激增）
- increase（增加）
- reproduce（繁殖）
- amplify（扩大）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **multiply by / multiply rapidly / multiply the effect / multiply opportunities**

👉 Example: Investments can multiply in value if managed wisely over a long period of time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher observed that the bacteria could **multiply** rapidly under warm and humid laboratory conditions.
   研究人员观察到细菌在温暖潮湿的实验室条件下能够迅速繁殖。

2. Government officials warned that unchecked spending would **multiply** the national debt to unsustainable levels within a decade.
   政府官员警告说，不加控制的开支将在十年内使国债增加到不可持续的水平。

3. Students must first learn to **multiply** fractions before advancing to more complex algebraic equations in the curriculum.
   学生必须先学会分数乘法，然后才能在课程中进入更复杂的代数方程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "multi-ply" as "many layers" — when you multiply, you add many layers or copies of something, making it grow.
把"multiply"想成"multi（多）+ ply（层）"——当你乘或增加时，就像叠加了许多层，使事物不断增长。
