# 🧬 electrolyte

🔊 /ɪˈlektrəlaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A substance that produces electrically conducting ions when dissolved in a solvent such as water; a liquid or gel containing ions used in batteries and biological systems
**中文：** n. 电解质；电解液

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ionic solution（离子溶液）
- conductor（导体）
- ionic compound（离子化合物）
- salt solution（盐溶液）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **electrolyte balance / electrolyte solution / strong electrolyte / electrolyte imbalance**

👉 Example: Athletes must replenish their electrolyte levels after intense physical exercise to maintain proper bodily function.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician recommended an **electrolyte** supplement to restore the patient's sodium and potassium levels after severe dehydration.
   医生建议补充电解质以恢复患者严重脱水后的钠和钾水平。

2. Solid-state **electrolyte** materials represent a promising advancement in battery technology due to their improved safety characteristics.
   固态电解质材料因其改进的安全特性而代表了电池技术中一项有前景的进步。

3. The chemistry students observed how the dissolved **electrolyte** enabled electrical current to flow through the aqueous solution efficiently.
   化学专业的学生观察了溶解的电解质如何使电流有效地通过水溶液。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "electro-lyte" as "electric light" — an electrolyte lights up the path for electricity to flow through a solution.
把"electrolyte"想成"electric light（电的灯光）"——电解质为电流在溶液中流动照亮了道路。
