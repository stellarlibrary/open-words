# 📜 charter

🔊 /ˈtʃɑːrtər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To grant a charter to; to hire or rent a vehicle or vessel for exclusive use. (n.) A written document granting rights, privileges, or authority; a constitution or fundamental law
**中文：** v. 特许；包租 n. 宪章；特权；豁免权；执照

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- constitution（宪章）
- license（许可证）
- hire（租用）
- commission（委托）
- franchise（特许权）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **charter a flight / charter school / royal charter / charter member / charter of rights**

👉 Example: The organization received a charter granting it authority to operate independently.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university received its royal **charter** in the eighteenth century, granting it the authority to confer academic degrees.
   该大学在十八世纪获得了皇家特许状，授予其颁授学位的权力。

2. The corporation decided to **charter** a private aircraft to transport executives to the overseas business conference efficiently.
   公司决定包租一架私人飞机将高管们高效地运送到海外商务会议。

3. The **charter** of fundamental rights established a clear framework for protecting individual freedoms within the new federation.
   基本权利宪章为保护新联邦内的个人自由建立了明确的框架。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "charter" as "chart + er" — a document that charts out the rules, rights, and privileges for an organization.
把"charter"想成"chart（图表）+ er"——一份为组织规划出规则、权利和特权的文件。
