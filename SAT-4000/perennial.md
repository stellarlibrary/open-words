# 🌿 perennial

🔊 /pəˈrɛniəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting for an indefinitely long time; enduring; recurring year after year. (n.) A plant that lives for more than two years
**中文：** adj. 四季不断的；终年的；常在的；反复的 n. 多年生植物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enduring（持久的）
- perpetual（永久的）
- everlasting（永恒的）
- persistent（持续的）
- recurring（反复出现的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perennial favorite / perennial problem / perennial plant / perennial issue**

👉 Example: The shortage of affordable housing remains a perennial challenge for urban planners worldwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The debate over standardized testing has become a **perennial** issue in educational policy discussions across the country.
   标准化考试的争论已成为全国教育政策讨论中一个长期存在的问题。

2. Lavender is a **perennial** plant that returns each spring, providing fragrant blossoms throughout the warm summer months.
   薰衣草是一种多年生植物，每年春天再生，在温暖的夏季提供芬芳的花朵。

3. Her **perennial** optimism inspired colleagues to persevere even during the most difficult and discouraging phases of the project.
   她持久的乐观精神激励同事们即使在项目最困难和最令人沮丧的阶段也坚持不懈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-ennial" as "per annum" (every year) — something perennial comes back every single year without fail.
把"perennial"想成"per annum"（每年）——多年生的事物每年都会回来，从不间断。
