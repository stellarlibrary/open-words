# 😒 trite

🔊 /traɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking originality or freshness; dull on account of overuse; hackneyed
**中文：** adj. 陈腐的；平庸的；老套的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hackneyed（陈腐的）
- banal（平庸的）
- clichéd（老套的）
- commonplace（平凡的）
- stale（陈旧的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **trite expression / trite remark / trite phrase / sound trite**

👉 Example: The critic dismissed the novel's dialogue as trite and lacking in genuine emotional depth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor advised her students to avoid **trite** expressions in their academic essays and strive for original analysis.
   教授建议学生在学术论文中避免使用陈腐的表达，努力进行原创分析。

2. Although the sentiment behind his speech was sincere, the **trite** phrases he employed failed to inspire the audience.
   尽管他演讲背后的情感是真诚的，但他使用的老套措辞未能打动听众。

3. What once seemed like a profound observation has become **trite** through decades of repetition in popular self-help literature.
   曾经看似深刻的见解在流行自助文学中经过数十年的重复后已变得陈腐不堪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trite" rhyming with "write" — if you write the same thing over and over, it becomes trite and boring.
把"trite"和"write"联想——如果你反复写同样的东西，它就变得陈腐无聊了。
