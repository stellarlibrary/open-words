# 🔄 oscillate

🔊 /ˈɑːsɪleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To swing back and forth with a steady rhythm; to waver between two states, opinions, or courses of action.
**中文：** v. 振荡；振动；动摇；犹豫不决

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fluctuate（波动）
- vacillate（犹豫）
- waver（动摇）
- alternate（交替）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oscillate between / oscillate rapidly / oscillate wildly / oscillate back and forth**

👉 Example: The pendulum continued to oscillate steadily, marking each second with precision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public opinion tends to **oscillate** between support and opposition depending on how the media frames the issue.
   公众舆论往往在支持和反对之间摇摆，取决于媒体如何报道这一问题。

2. The temperature readings **oscillated** dramatically throughout the experiment, making it difficult to draw reliable conclusions.
   整个实验过程中温度读数剧烈振荡，使得难以得出可靠的结论。

3. She continued to **oscillate** between accepting the prestigious fellowship and remaining at her current institution.
   她继续在接受这一著名奖学金和留在目前的机构之间犹豫不决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oscillate" sounding like "OS-cil-late" — picture an oscillating fan swinging back and forth, never staying in one direction.
把"oscillate"联想成一台摆头电风扇，来回摆动，从不停留在一个方向——振荡就是不断来回摆动。
