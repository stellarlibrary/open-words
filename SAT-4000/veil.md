# 🧣 veil

🔊 /veɪl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A piece of cloth used to cover the face or head; something that conceals or disguises. (v.) To cover or conceal with or as if with a veil; to disguise
**中文：** n. 面纱；遮蔽物；托词 v. 用面纱掩盖；掩饰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conceal（隐藏）
- shroud（遮蔽）
- mask（掩盖）
- cloak（掩饰）
- obscure（使模糊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **veil of secrecy / lift the veil / veil one's intentions / behind a veil of**

👉 Example: The organization operated behind a veil of secrecy that shielded its activities from public scrutiny.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat attempted to **veil** her true intentions behind carefully worded statements and diplomatic pleasantries.
   这位外交官试图用措辞谨慎的声明和外交辞令来掩饰她的真实意图。

2. A thin **veil** of mist descended over the valley, obscuring the ancient ruins from the view of approaching travelers.
   一层薄薄的雾幕笼罩在山谷上方，使古代遗迹在旅行者眼前变得模糊不清。

3. The investigative journalist worked tirelessly to lift the **veil** of corruption surrounding the government procurement process.
   这位调查记者不知疲倦地工作，揭开围绕政府采购过程的腐败面纱。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a bride's "veil" — it covers and hides her face, just as anything that veils something conceals or disguises it.
想想新娘的"面纱"——它遮住并隐藏了她的脸，就像任何veil都是在遮掩或伪装某物一样。
