# 🔄 overlap

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈlæp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A shared area or period; duplication between two things. (v.) To extend over and cover partly; to coincide partially
**中文：** n. 重叠；重复 v. 部分重叠；部分的同时发生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coincide（重合）
- intersect（交叉）
- converge（汇合）
- overlay（覆盖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overlap with / overlap between / considerable overlap / areas of overlap**

👉 Example: There is significant overlap between the responsibilities of the two departments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research interests of the two professors **overlap** considerably, leading to several productive collaborative publications.
   两位教授的研究兴趣有很大的重叠，因此产生了多项富有成效的合作出版物。

2. The scheduled events unfortunately **overlap**, forcing participants to choose between attending one session or the other.
   预定的活动不幸时间重叠，迫使参与者在两场会议之间做出选择。

3. There is a notable **overlap** between classical economic theory and modern behavioral approaches to understanding market dynamics.
   古典经济理论与现代行为学方法在理解市场动态方面存在显著的重叠。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-lap" as one thing "lapping over" another — like two sheets of paper placed so one partly covers the other.
把"overlap"想成一样东西"搭在"另一样东西上面——就像两张纸放置时一张部分盖住了另一张。
