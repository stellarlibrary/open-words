# 🌗 phase

🔊 /feɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A distinct stage in a process of change or development; the appearance of the moon at a particular point in its cycle
**中文：** n. 阶段；时期；月相；位相

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stage（阶段）
- period（时期）
- step（步骤）
- chapter（篇章）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **phase of development / phase of the moon / go through a phase / phase in / phase out**

👉 Example: The project has entered its final phase and should be completed within two months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team carefully documented each **phase** of the clinical trial to ensure full compliance with regulatory standards.
   研究团队仔细记录了临床试验的每个阶段，以确保完全符合监管标准。

2. Ancient astronomers meticulously tracked the **phases** of the moon to develop accurate calendars for agricultural planning.
   古代天文学家一丝不苟地追踪月相，以制定准确的农业规划日历。

3. Adolescence is often regarded as a turbulent **phase** during which individuals develop their sense of identity and autonomy.
   青春期通常被视为一个动荡的阶段，个人在此期间发展自己的身份认同和自主意识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the moon's phases — each phase is a different visible shape, representing a distinct stage in a continuous cycle.
想想月亮的月相——每个phase都是一个不同的可见形状，代表连续循环中的一个独特阶段。
