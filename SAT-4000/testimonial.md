# 📋 testimonial

🔊 /ˌtestɪˈmoʊniəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A formal written statement testifying to someone's character, qualifications, or the quality of a product; a recommendation or endorsement
**中文：** 证明书；推荐书；（对品质或能力的）褒奖

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recommendation（推荐信）
- endorsement（背书）
- reference（推荐）
- tribute（赞辞）
- commendation（嘉奖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **customer testimonial / glowing testimonial / written testimonial / testimonial dinner / provide a testimonial**

👉 Example: The company's website featured dozens of customer testimonials praising the reliability of its products.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university admissions committee carefully reviewed every **testimonial** submitted by the applicant's former professors and academic advisors.
   大学招生委员会仔细审查了申请人的前任教授和学术导师提交的每一份推荐书。

2. A heartfelt **testimonial** from a satisfied patient can be far more persuasive than any traditional advertising campaign.
   一位满意患者发自内心的证明书可能远比任何传统广告活动更具说服力。

3. The retiring director received numerous **testimonials** from colleagues acknowledging her decades of outstanding leadership and institutional service.
   即将退休的主任收到了同事们的大量褒奖，认可她数十年的杰出领导和机构服务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "testimoni-al" as "testimony + al (relating to)" — a testimonial is a written form of testimony about someone's worth.
把"testimonial"想成"testimony（证词）+ al（与……相关的）"——testimonial是关于某人价值的书面证词。
