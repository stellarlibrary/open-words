# ⚡ blaspheme

🔊 /blæsˈfiːm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To speak irreverently about God or sacred things; to curse, swear, or show contempt for something revered
**中文：** v. 亵渎；咒骂；辱骂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- profane（亵渎）
- desecrate（玷污）
- curse（咒骂）
- revile（辱骂）
- defile（亵渎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blaspheme against God / blaspheme the sacred / accused of blaspheming / dare to blaspheme**

👉 Example: In many ancient societies, to blaspheme against the gods was punishable by exile or death.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The accused was charged with having **blasphemed** against established religious doctrines during his public lectures at the university.
   被告被指控在大学的公开演讲中亵渎了既定的宗教教义。

2. Critics argued that the provocative artwork did not **blaspheme** but rather invited thoughtful reflection on spiritual traditions.
   批评者认为这件挑衅性的艺术品并非亵渎，而是引发了人们对精神传统的深思。

3. Throughout history, those who dared to **blaspheme** against dominant ideologies often faced severe persecution and social ostracism.
   纵观历史，那些敢于亵渎主流意识形态的人往往面临严厉的迫害和社会排斥。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "blast" + "flame" — blasting sacred things with flames of disrespectful words.
把"blaspheme"想成"blast"（轰炸）+"flame"（火焰）——用不敬的言语之火轰炸神圣之物。
