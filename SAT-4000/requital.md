# 🔁 requital

🔊 /rɪˈkwaɪtl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of repaying or rewarding; something given in return, compensation, or retaliation
**中文：** n. 报答；偿还；报仇；报酬；报复

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recompense（补偿）
- repayment（偿还）
- retribution（报应）
- retaliation（报复）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **requital of a debt / requital for kindness / in requital of / seek requital**

👉 Example: The knight sought requital for the injustice inflicted upon his family by the corrupt magistrate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The generous donation was offered in **requital** of the community's unwavering support during the prolonged economic crisis.
   这笔慷慨的捐赠是为了报答社区在长期经济危机中坚定不移的支持。

2. Scholars have long debated whether strict **requital** of wrongdoing serves justice better than rehabilitative approaches.
   学者们长期以来一直在讨论对不法行为的严格报复是否比教化手段更能伸张正义。

3. She expected no **requital** for her years of volunteer work, yet the award ceremony deeply moved her.
   她对自己多年的志愿工作不求回报，然而颁奖典礼深深打动了她。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "requital" as "re-quit-all" — you quit all debts by paying them back in full, achieving requital.
把"requital"想成"re-quit-all"——你通过全额偿还来了结所有债务，实现了报答。
