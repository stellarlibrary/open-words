# 🤫 discreet

🔊 /dɪˈskriːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Careful and prudent in one's speech or actions, especially to avoid causing offense or revealing confidential information
**中文：** adj. 言行谨慎的，慎重的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prudent（审慎的）
- cautious（谨慎的）
- tactful（圆滑的）
- circumspect（小心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **discreet inquiry / discreet manner / be discreet about something**

👉 Example: Please be discreet about this matter until the official announcement is made.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist promised to be **discreet** about the source of the leaked documents to protect the informant's identity.
   记者承诺对泄露文件的来源保持谨慎，以保护线人的身份。

2. A **discreet** investigation was conducted by internal affairs before any public statements were issued regarding the allegations.
   在就指控发表任何公开声明之前，内务部门进行了一项审慎的调查。

3. The manager made a **discreet** phone call to verify the applicant's credentials without alerting other members of the hiring committee.
   经理谨慎地打了一个电话核实申请人的资历，没有惊动招聘委员会的其他成员。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "discreet" as keeping things "secret" — a discreet person knows when to stay quiet and handle matters carefully.
把"discreet"和"secret"（秘密）联系起来——一个discreet的人知道何时保持沉默，谨慎处理事务。
