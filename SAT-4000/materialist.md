# 💎 materialist

🔊 /məˈtɪəriəlɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who believes that physical matter is the only reality; a person who is excessively concerned with material possessions and wealth
**中文：** n. 唯物主义者；实利主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pragmatist（实用主义者）
- consumerist（消费主义者）
- acquisitive person（贪婪的人）
- hedonist（享乐主义者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a confirmed materialist / philosophical materialist / dialectical materialist / materialist outlook**

👉 Example: Critics accused the billionaire of being a shallow materialist who valued possessions over personal relationships.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that a true **materialist** rejects all supernatural explanations and interprets the world through physical phenomena alone.
   这位哲学家认为，真正的唯物主义者拒绝一切超自然解释，仅通过物质现象来理解世界。

2. Modern consumer culture has produced a generation of **materialist**s who measure personal success primarily by wealth and acquisitions.
   现代消费文化培养了一代唯物质的人，他们主要以财富和所得来衡量个人成功。

3. As a devoted **materialist**, she insisted that consciousness itself could be fully explained by neurochemical processes in the brain.
   作为一名虔诚的唯物主义者，她坚持认为意识本身可以完全由大脑中的神经化学过程来解释。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "material + ist" — a person (-ist) focused on material things, whether physical matter in philosophy or material wealth in daily life.
把"materialist"想成"material + ist"——一个专注于物质（material）的人（-ist），无论是哲学中的物质还是日常生活中的物质财富。
