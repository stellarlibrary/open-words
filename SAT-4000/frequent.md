# 🔄 frequent

🔊 /ˈfriːkwənt/ (adj.) /frɪˈkwɛnt/ (v.)<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring or done on many occasions, at short intervals. (v.) To visit a place often or habitually
**中文：** adj. 频繁的；时常发生的；常见的 v. 常到，常去

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- common（常见的）
- regular（经常的）
- habitual（习惯性的）
- recurrent（反复发生的）
- patronize（经常光顾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frequent visitor / frequent flyer / frequent occurrence / frequent a place**

👉 Example: She was a frequent visitor to the museum, attending nearly every new exhibition throughout the year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor made **frequent** references to primary sources, encouraging students to engage directly with the original historical documents.
   教授频繁引用一手资料，鼓励学生直接研读原始历史文献。

2. Locals who **frequent** the cafe know that the best pastries are available only during the first hour after opening each morning.
   常去这家咖啡馆的当地人知道，最好的糕点只在每天早上开门后的第一个小时供应。

3. **Frequent** power outages disrupted daily operations at the hospital, prompting administrators to invest in a reliable backup generator.
   频繁的停电扰乱了医院的日常运营，促使管理层投资购买了一台可靠的备用发电机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frequent" as "free-quent" — something that happens so often it feels like it comes free, without waiting.
把"frequent"想成"free-quent"——某事发生得如此频繁，感觉像是免费的，不用等待就会出现。
