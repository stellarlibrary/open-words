# 🤝 acquaint

🔊 /əˈkweɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make someone aware of or familiar with something; to introduce or inform
**中文：** 使熟悉；使认识

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- familiarize（使熟悉）
- inform（告知）
- introduce（介绍）
- apprise（通知）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acquaint oneself with / acquaint someone with / become acquainted**

👉 Example: New employees should acquaint themselves with the company's safety protocols before starting work.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orientation program was designed to **acquaint** incoming students with the university's academic resources and campus facilities.
   迎新项目旨在让新生熟悉大学的学术资源和校园设施。

2. Before traveling abroad, it is advisable to **acquaint** yourself with the local customs and cultural expectations.
   出国旅行前，建议先了解当地的风俗习惯和文化期望。

3. The lawyer took considerable time to **acquaint** the jury with the complex details of the financial fraud case.
   律师花了大量时间让陪审团了解这起金融欺诈案的复杂细节。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "acquaint" as "a-quaint" — you meet someone in a quaint little village and get to know them, becoming familiar.
把"acquaint"想成"a-quaint"——你在一个古雅的小村庄遇到某人并逐渐认识他们，变得熟悉。
