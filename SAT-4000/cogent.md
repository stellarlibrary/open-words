# 🎯 cogent

🔊 /ˈkoʊdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Clear, logical, and convincing; powerfully persuasive in reasoning or argumentation
**中文：** adj. 强有力的；使人信服的；有说服力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compelling（令人信服的）
- persuasive（有说服力的）
- convincing（令人信服的）
- forceful（有力的）
- lucid（清晰的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cogent argument / cogent reasoning / cogent analysis / cogent case / cogent evidence**

👉 Example: The defense attorney presented a cogent argument that ultimately persuaded the jury to acquit the defendant.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher presented a **cogent** analysis of the data that convinced even the most skeptical members of the review committee.
   研究人员对数据进行了有力的分析，甚至说服了评审委员会中最持怀疑态度的成员。

2. Without a **cogent** justification for the proposed budget increase, the board of directors unanimously voted to reject the proposal.
   由于缺乏对拟议预算增加的有力论证，董事会一致投票否决了该提案。

3. Her **cogent** reasoning during the debate demonstrated a remarkable ability to synthesize complex information into clear, logical conclusions.
   她在辩论中有说服力的推理展示了将复杂信息综合为清晰、合乎逻辑的结论的卓越能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cogent" as "co-" + "gent" (gentleman) — a refined gentleman who speaks with clear, powerful logic that convinces everyone.
把"cogent"想成"co-" + "gent"（绅士）——一位谈吐清晰、逻辑有力、能说服所有人的优雅绅士。
