# 🕳️ nihilism

🔊 /ˈnaɪ.ɪlɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The philosophical belief that life is meaningless and that traditional values and institutions are unfounded; the rejection of all religious and moral principles
**中文：** n. 虚无主义；无政府主义；怀疑论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- skepticism（怀疑主义）
- cynicism（犬儒主义）
- pessimism（悲观主义）
- anarchism（无政府主义）
- negativism（消极主义）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **moral nihilism / existential nihilism / embrace nihilism / descent into nihilism**

👉 Example: The philosopher warned that unchecked nihilism could erode the moral foundations of society.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Friedrich Nietzsche explored **nihilism** extensively, arguing that the decline of religion would leave humanity searching for new meaning.
   弗里德里希·尼采广泛探讨了虚无主义，认为宗教的衰落将使人类寻找新的意义。

2. The novel's protagonist gradually descends into **nihilism** after experiencing a series of devastating personal losses and betrayals.
   小说主人公在经历了一系列毁灭性的个人损失和背叛后逐渐陷入虚无主义。

3. Some critics interpret the artist's provocative work as a deliberate expression of **nihilism** directed against established cultural norms.
   一些批评家将这位艺术家的挑衅性作品解读为对既定文化规范的虚无主义刻意表达。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nihil" (Latin for "nothing") + "-ism" — nihilism is the belief that nothing has real value or meaning.
把"nihilism"想成"nihil（拉丁语意为'无'）"加"-ism（主义）"——虚无主义就是认为一切都没有真正价值或意义的信仰。
