# 🧭 vector

🔊 /ˈvɛktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A quantity having direction and magnitude; an organism that transmits a pathogen. (v.) To guide or direct, especially by radio
**中文：** n. 矢量；向量；带菌者；传播媒介 v. 用无线电导航

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carrier（载体）
- agent（媒介）
- course（航向）
- direction（方向）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disease vector / vector quantity / attack vector / vector graphics**

👉 Example: Mosquitoes are the primary vector for transmitting malaria to human populations worldwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The epidemiologist identified the mosquito species that served as the principal **vector** for transmitting the disease to local communities.
   流行病学家确定了作为向当地社区传播该疾病的主要媒介的蚊子种类。

2. In physics, a **vector** is distinguished from a scalar by possessing both magnitude and direction in three-dimensional space.
   在物理学中，矢量与标量的区别在于它在三维空间中同时具有大小和方向。

3. Air traffic controllers **vectored** the aircraft to an alternative runway after severe crosswinds made the primary approach dangerously unstable.
   空中交通管制员在强烈侧风使主要进近变得极其不稳定后，引导飞机转向备用跑道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vector" as "vect-or" from Latin "vehere" meaning "to carry" — it carries direction in math and carries disease in biology.
把"vector"想成来自拉丁语"vehere"（意为"携带"）——在数学中它携带方向，在生物学中它携带疾病。
