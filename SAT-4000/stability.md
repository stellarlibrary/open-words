# ⚖️ stability

🔊 /stəˈbɪləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being stable, firm, or unlikely to change; steadfastness of character or purpose
**中文：** n. 稳定，安定；坚定，恒心

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- steadiness（稳定）
- firmness（坚固）
- permanence（持久）
- equilibrium（平衡）
- constancy（恒定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political stability / financial stability / emotional stability / stability of the economy / maintain stability**

👉 Example: The new policies were designed to promote long-term economic stability in the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation achieved unprecedented **stability** following decades of political turmoil and civil unrest throughout the region.
   经过数十年的政治动荡和地区内乱，该国实现了前所未有的稳定。

2. Engineers tested the structural **stability** of the bridge to ensure it could withstand extreme weather conditions safely.
   工程师们测试了桥梁的结构稳定性，以确保其能安全承受极端天气条件。

3. Emotional **stability** is considered a vital trait for leaders who must make critical decisions under intense pressure.
   情绪稳定被认为是领导者在巨大压力下做出关键决策时必备的重要特质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stability" as "stable-ability" — the ability to remain stable and unchanged, like a sturdy table that never wobbles.
把"stability"想成"stable-ability"——保持稳定不变的能力，就像一张从不摇晃的坚固桌子。
