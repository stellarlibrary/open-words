# 🌟 highlight

🔊 /ˈhaɪlaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To draw special attention to; to emphasize or make prominent. (n.) The most outstanding, memorable, or exciting part of something
**中文：** v. 使显著；强调；突出 n. 最精彩的部分；亮点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emphasize（强调）
- underscore（突出）
- accentuate（着重）
- feature（特写）
- climax（高潮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **highlight the importance / highlight a problem / highlight of the event / career highlight / highlight key points**

👉 Example: The keynote speaker sought to highlight the critical importance of sustainable development in modern economic policy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The annual report **highlighted** several critical challenges that the organization must address to remain competitive in the evolving global marketplace.
   年度报告突出了该组织必须应对的几个关键挑战，以在不断变化的全球市场中保持竞争力。

2. Winning the prestigious international award was undoubtedly the **highlight** of the young researcher's rapidly ascending academic career thus far.
   赢得这项享有盛誉的国际奖项无疑是这位年轻研究人员迅速上升的学术生涯中迄今为止最精彩的时刻。

3. The professor used bold annotations to **highlight** the most significant passages within the primary source document for her graduate students.
   教授用粗体标注来突出原始文献中最重要的段落，以供其研究生参考。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "high" + "light" — shining a bright light on something to make it stand out, like a spotlight on the most important point.
把"highlight"拆成"high"（高的）+"light"（光），就像把聚光灯打在最重要的地方，让它格外醒目。
