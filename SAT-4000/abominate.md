# 💢 abominate

🔊 /əˈbɒmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To detest utterly; to regard with intense loathing or hatred
**中文：** v. 痛恨；憎恶；厌恶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detest（憎恶）
- loathe（厌恶）
- abhor（憎恨）
- despise（鄙视）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abominate cruelty / abominate injustice / abominate violence / abominate hypocrisy**

👉 Example: She abominates all forms of discrimination and has dedicated her career to fighting inequality.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher openly **abominated** tyranny and devoted his writings to the defense of individual liberty.
   这位哲学家公开痛恨暴政，将其著作献给了捍卫个人自由的事业。

2. Many citizens **abominate** corruption in government, yet voter turnout remains discouragingly low each election cycle.
   许多公民痛恨政府中的腐败，然而每个选举周期的投票率仍然低得令人沮丧。

3. Environmental activists **abominate** the deliberate dumping of toxic industrial waste into rivers and coastal waters.
   环保活动人士痛恨将有毒工业废物故意倾倒入河流和沿海水域的行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abominate" shares its root with "abominable" — if something is abominable, you abominate it (feel extreme hatred for it).
"Abominate"与"abominable"同根——如果某事物是abominable的，你就会abominate它（对它感到极度憎恶）。
