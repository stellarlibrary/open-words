# 🎉 exultant

🔊 /ɪɡˈzʌltənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Triumphantly happy; filled with overwhelming joy or jubilation, especially after a success
**中文：** adj. 非常高兴的，欢跃的；狂喜的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- jubilant（欢欣鼓舞的）
- triumphant（得意洋洋的）
- elated（兴高采烈的）
- ecstatic（狂喜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exultant cry / exultant mood / exultant expression / exultant over a victory**

👉 Example: The team let out an exultant roar when the final results confirmed their championship victory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **exultant** crowd surged forward as the newly elected leader emerged to deliver her inaugural victory address.
   当新当选的领导人出来发表就职胜选演说时，狂喜的人群涌上前去。

2. After years of painstaking research, the scientist felt **exultant** upon confirming the validity of her groundbreaking hypothesis.
   经过多年艰苦的研究，这位科学家在证实其突破性假说的有效性后感到欣喜若狂。

3. The athletes wore **exultant** expressions as they stood on the podium receiving their well-deserved gold medals.
   运动员们站在领奖台上领取当之无愧的金牌时，脸上洋溢着欢欣的表情。

把"exultant"想成"从极度悲伤中exit"——当你exult时，你从悲伤中跳出来进入极度的喜悦。
