# 🧐 censorious

🔊 /sɛnˈsɔːriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Severely critical of others; inclined to find fault and express harsh judgment
**中文：** adj. 爱批判的；挑剔的；好挑毛病的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- critical（批判的）
- fault-finding（挑剔的）
- captious（吹毛求疵的）
- judgmental（爱评判的）
- hypercritical（过分挑剔的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **censorious tone / censorious attitude / censorious remarks / censorious critic**

👉 Example: The censorious reviewer dismissed every aspect of the performance without acknowledging any merit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her **censorious** comments about the research methodology undermined the confidence of the entire academic team.
   她对研究方法论的挑剔评论削弱了整个学术团队的信心。

2. The board adopted an increasingly **censorious** posture toward any proposal that deviated from established protocols.
   董事会对任何偏离既定程序的提案采取了越来越挑剔的态度。

3. Critics warned that a **censorious** approach to artistic expression could stifle creativity and discourage innovation.
   批评人士警告说，对艺术表达的苛责态度可能会扼杀创造力并阻碍创新。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

想想"censor（审查者）"加"-ious"——一个像审查者一样的人，总是挑毛病、严厉评判。
