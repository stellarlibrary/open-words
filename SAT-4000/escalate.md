# 📈 escalate

🔊 /ˈɛskəleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To increase rapidly in intensity, magnitude, or severity; to become or make something more serious or intense step by step
**中文：** v. 逐步增强；逐步升高；逐步升级

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intensify（加剧）
- amplify（增强）
- heighten（提高）
- aggravate（加重）
- surge（猛增）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **escalate a conflict / escalate tensions / escalate rapidly / escalate the situation**

👉 Example: The minor disagreement between the two nations quickly escalated into a full diplomatic crisis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The trade dispute between the two countries continued to **escalate** despite repeated diplomatic interventions from international mediators.
   尽管国际调解人反复进行外交干预，两国之间的贸易争端仍在不断升级。

2. Healthcare costs have **escalated** dramatically over the past decade, placing an enormous financial burden on working families.
   过去十年中，医疗费用急剧攀升，给工薪家庭带来了巨大的经济负担。

3. The manager decided to **escalate** the unresolved complaint to senior leadership after months of unsuccessful attempts at resolution.
   在数月未能成功解决后，经理决定将未解决的投诉升级至高层领导。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "escalate" as related to "escalator" — just as an escalator carries you higher step by step, to escalate means to rise gradually in intensity.
把"escalate"想成与"escalator（自动扶梯）"相关——就像自动扶梯一步步把你带到更高处，escalate意为逐步升级。
