# 🎀 adorn

🔊 /əˈdɔːrn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To decorate or add beauty to; to enhance the appearance of something
**中文：** v. 装饰；使生色；使更美观

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decorate（装饰）
- embellish（修饰）
- ornament（装点）
- beautify（美化）
- garnish（点缀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adorn with / adorn oneself / adorned in / richly adorned**

👉 Example: The cathedral walls were adorned with magnificent frescoes depicting biblical scenes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient temple was **adorned** with intricate carvings that reflected the extraordinary craftsmanship of its builders.
   这座古庙装饰着精美的雕刻，反映了建造者非凡的工艺水平。

2. She chose to **adorn** her academic thesis with carefully selected quotations from renowned philosophers and scholars.
   她选择用精心挑选的著名哲学家和学者的引文来为自己的学术论文增色。

3. Delicate wildflowers **adorned** the hillside each spring, transforming the barren landscape into a vibrant tapestry of color.
   每到春天，娇美的野花装点着山坡，将荒凉的景色变成一幅生机勃勃的彩色画卷。

---

Think of "adorn" as "a-DAWN" — at dawn, nature adorns the sky with beautiful colors of sunrise.
把"adorn"想成"a-DAWN"——黎明(dawn)时分，大自然用美丽的朝霞"装饰"天空。
