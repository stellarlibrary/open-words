# 📚 catalog

🔊 /ˈkætəlɔːɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A complete list of items arranged systematically; a publication containing such a list. (v.) To make a systematic list of; to classify or record
**中文：** n. 目录；目录册；登记 v. 登记；为…编目录

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inventory（库存清单）
- directory（名录）
- register（登记簿）
- index（索引）
- classify（分类）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **product catalog / catalog of works / library catalog / catalog items / catalog a collection**

👉 Example: The librarian spent weeks creating a comprehensive catalog of the newly acquired manuscripts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum published an extensive **catalog** documenting every artifact in its permanent collection of ancient Mediterranean pottery.
   博物馆出版了一份详尽的目录，记录了其古代地中海陶器永久收藏中的每件文物。

2. Scientists worked diligently to **catalog** the thousands of newly discovered species found in the deep ocean ecosystem.
   科学家们勤奋地为在深海生态系统中发现的数千个新物种编目录。

3. The retail company distributed its seasonal **catalog** to millions of households, showcasing products available for purchase online.
   这家零售公司向数百万家庭分发了季节性目录，展示可在线购买的产品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cat-a-log" — a cat logging every item it sees into a list. A catalog is a systematic log of items.
把"catalog"想成"cat-a-log"——一只猫把它看到的每样东西都记录（log）成一份清单。目录就是对物品的系统记录。
