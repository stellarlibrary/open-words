# ⏸️ suspend

🔊 /səˈspɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To delay or postpone; to cause to stop temporarily; to cause to float or hang in air or liquid.
**中文：** v. 吊，悬挂；推迟；暂停

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- postpone（推迟）
- halt（停止）
- defer（延缓）
- adjourn（休会）
- discontinue（中断）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suspend operations / suspend judgment / suspend disbelief / suspended from school**

👉 Example: The committee voted to suspend all funding until a thorough investigation could be completed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university decided to **suspend** the student for one semester following a serious violation of academic integrity.
   大学决定因严重违反学术诚信对该学生停学一个学期。

2. Authorities chose to **suspend** construction of the bridge after engineers discovered critical structural deficiencies.
   当局在工程师发现关键结构缺陷后决定暂停桥梁的建设。

3. Fine particles of dust remained **suspended** in the air for hours after the controlled demolition of the building.
   在建筑物的可控拆除之后，细小的灰尘颗粒在空气中悬浮了数小时。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sus-pend" — to "hang" (pend, like pendant) something up, leaving it dangling in an unresolved state.
把"suspend"想成"sus-pend"——把某物"挂"（pend，如pendant吊坠）起来，使其悬而不决。
