# 🎭 ingratiate

🔊 /ɪnˈɡreɪʃiˌeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To bring oneself into favor with someone by flattering or trying to please them, often in an insincere way
**中文：** v. 使迎合；使讨好；使逢迎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flatter（奉承）
- curry favor（讨好）
- fawn（巴结）
- toady（谄媚）
- grovel（卑躬屈膝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ingratiate oneself with / ingratiate himself into / ingratiating smile / ingratiating manner**

👉 Example: The new employee tried to ingratiate himself with the boss by constantly offering excessive compliments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious intern attempted to **ingratiate** herself with senior executives by volunteering for every high-profile project available.
   这位雄心勃勃的实习生试图通过主动参加每个高知名度项目来讨好高级管理层。

2. Politicians often **ingratiate** themselves with voters by making extravagant promises that they have no genuine intention of fulfilling.
   政客们常常通过做出他们根本无意兑现的夸大承诺来讨好选民。

3. His transparent attempts to **ingratiate** himself with the committee only succeeded in undermining his own professional credibility.
   他试图讨好委员会的拙劣伎俩只能适得其反，损害了自己的职业信誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-GRATI-ate" — "grati" resembles "gratify," so to ingratiate means to try to gratify someone to win their favor.
把"ingratiate"想成"in + grati（像gratify使满意）+ ate"——通过使别人满意来赢得好感。
