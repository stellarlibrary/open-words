# 🌕 apogee

🔊 /ˈæp.ə.dʒiː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The highest point or peak of something; the climax or culmination. In astronomy, the point in the orbit of the moon or a satellite at which it is farthest from the earth.

**中文：** 最高点，极点；远地点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- zenith（顶点）
- pinnacle（巅峰）
- summit（顶峰）
- apex（顶点）
- culmination（最高点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **reach the apogee**, **at the apogee of**, **the apogee of one's influence/power**, **apogee and perigee** (opposite pair in astronomy).

> The Byzantine Empire reached its **apogee** during the reign of Justinian the Great.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The satellite reaches its **apogee** at approximately thirty-six thousand kilometers above the earth's surface.
   该卫星在距地球表面约三万六千公里处到达远地点。

2. Many scholars consider the eighteenth century to be the **apogee** of Enlightenment philosophical thought.
   许多学者认为十八世纪是启蒙运动哲学思想的巅峰。

3. The artist's reputation reached its **apogee** when the national gallery acquired her masterwork permanently.
   当国家美术馆永久收藏她的杰作时，这位艺术家的声誉达到了顶峰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apo-" (away from) + "-gee" (earth, from Greek *ge*). The **apogee** is the point farthest **away from** the earth. Figuratively, it is the highest point you can reach — far above the ground.

词根记忆：apo-（远离）+ -gee（地球，源自希腊语 ge）。apogee 字面意思是"远离地球的点"，引申为"最高点"。反义词 perigee（近地点）：peri-（靠近）+ gee（地球）。
