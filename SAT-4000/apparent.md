# 👁️ apparent

🔊 /əˈpærənt/<br>
🏷️ *adj. 显然的；表面上的*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Clearly visible or understood; obvious. Also, seeming real or true but not necessarily so; ostensible.

**中文：** 显然的；明显的。也指表面上的，貌似的（但未必真实）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- obvious 明显的
- evident 显而易见的
- seeming 表面上的
- ostensible 表面的
- manifest 明白的

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** become apparent, immediately apparent, for no apparent reason, apparent contradiction, heir apparent

**Example:** Note the dual meaning: "apparent" can mean genuinely obvious ("The solution was apparent to everyone") or only seemingly true ("His apparent calm masked deep anxiety").

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **apparent** simplicity of the design concealed a remarkably complex engineering structure underneath the surface.
   该设计**表面上的**简洁掩盖了其下方极为复杂的工程结构。

2. It became **apparent** that the proposed policy would require significantly more funding than originally estimated.
   **显然**，拟议的政策将需要比最初估计多得多的资金。

3. Despite his **apparent** composure during the trial, the defendant was deeply troubled by the damning evidence.
   尽管被告在审判期间**看似**镇定，但那些不利证据令他深感不安。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apparent" shares its root with "appear" — what appears (shows itself) to you is apparent. But remember: appearances can be deceiving.

词根联想：apparent 与 appear（出现）同源——出现在眼前的就是"显然的"，但也可能只是"表面上的"。
