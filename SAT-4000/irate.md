# 😠 irate

🔊 /aɪˈreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Feeling or characterized by great anger; extremely angry or enraged
**中文：** adj. 生气的；发怒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- furious（狂怒的）
- enraged（暴怒的）
- incensed（激怒的）
- livid（大怒的）
- indignant（愤慨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irate customer / irate citizen / become irate / grow increasingly irate**

👉 Example: The irate passengers demanded a full refund after the airline canceled their flight without advance notice.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **irate** residents organized a public meeting to protest the city's decision to build a highway through their neighborhood.
   愤怒的居民组织了一次公开会议，抗议市政府在他们社区修建高速公路的决定。

2. An **irate** shareholder confronted the board of directors about the company's declining profits and questionable financial decisions.
   一位愤怒的股东就公司利润下滑和有疑问的财务决策质问了董事会。

3. The manager attempted to calm the **irate** client by offering a sincere apology and a comprehensive plan for resolution.
   经理试图通过真诚的道歉和全面的解决方案来安抚这位愤怒的客户。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "I-RATE" — when you are irate, you might say "I rate this experience as terrible!" because anger makes you want to criticize everything.
把"irate"想成"I-RATE"——当你生气时，你可能会说"I rate this experience as terrible!"（我给这次体验打最差分！），因为愤怒让你想批评一切。
