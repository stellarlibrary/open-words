# 🌸 odoriferous

🔊 /ˌoʊdəˈrɪfərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or giving off a smell, especially a fragrant or pleasant one; sometimes used ironically for unpleasant smells
**中文：** adj. 有香气的；散发气味的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fragrant（芬芳的）
- aromatic（芳香的）
- scented（有香味的）
- perfumed（散发香气的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **odoriferous flowers / odoriferous substances / odoriferous herbs / odoriferous compounds**

👉 Example: The garden was filled with odoriferous blossoms that attracted butterflies from across the meadow.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The botanist cataloged several **odoriferous** plant species whose essential oils have significant pharmaceutical and commercial applications.
   这位植物学家编录了几种芳香植物物种，其精油具有重要的药用和商业价值。

2. Ancient traders transported **odoriferous** spices along the Silk Road, generating enormous wealth for merchant families throughout the region.
   古代商人沿着丝绸之路运输芳香的香料，为整个地区的商人家族创造了巨大的财富。

3. The chemist identified the **odoriferous** compound responsible for the distinctive and immediately recognizable scent of freshly cut grass.
   化学家确认了使新割草地散发出独特且易于辨认气味的芳香化合物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "odor + iferous" — "odor" means smell and "-iferous" means bearing or carrying, so odoriferous literally means "smell-bearing."
把"odoriferous"想成"odor + iferous"——"odor"意为气味，"-iferous"意为携带的，所以odoriferous字面意思就是"携带气味的"。
