# 🎨 verisimilitude

🔊 /ˌvɛrɪsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The appearance of being true or real; the quality of seeming realistic in art, literature, or narrative
**中文：** n. 逼真；貌似真实；逼真的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- realism（现实主义）
- plausibility（可信性）
- authenticity（真实性）
- lifelikeness（逼真）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verisimilitude of the story / achieve verisimilitude / sense of verisimilitude / lack verisimilitude**

👉 Example: The film's remarkable verisimilitude made audiences feel as though they were witnessing actual events.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historical novel achieved extraordinary **verisimilitude** through meticulous research into the customs and language of the period.
   这部历史小说通过对该时期风俗和语言的细致研究达到了非凡的逼真度。

2. Directors of period dramas invest heavily in set design and costumes to enhance the **verisimilitude** of their productions.
   古装剧导演在布景设计和服装上投入大量资金，以增强其作品的逼真感。

3. The memoir's **verisimilitude** was later questioned when investigators discovered several fabricated episodes within the narrative.
   当调查人员发现叙事中有几个捏造的情节时，这部回忆录的真实性受到了质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veris-imilitude" as "very similar attitude to truth" — it describes something that has an attitude or quality very similar to reality.
把"verisimilitude"想成"very similar attitude to truth"——它描述的是某事物具有与现实非常相似的特质。
