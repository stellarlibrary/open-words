# 😢 wretch

🔊 /rɛtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A miserable or pitiable person; an unfortunate individual suffering from hardship; also, a despicable or contemptible person
**中文：** n. 可怜的人，不幸的人；卑鄙的人，无耻的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unfortunate（不幸的人）
- miserable soul（可怜的人）
- scoundrel（恶棍）
- villain（坏人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **poor wretch / miserable wretch / wretched creature / hapless wretch**

👉 Example: The poor wretch had been wandering the streets for days without food or shelter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novelist depicted the protagonist as a lonely **wretch** cast aside by society and stripped of all dignity.
   小说家将主人公描绘成一个被社会抛弃、尊严尽失的可怜人。

2. History remembers the tyrant as a power-hungry **wretch** who sacrificed his people's welfare for personal ambition.
   历史铭记这位暴君是一个贪权的卑鄙之人，为个人野心牺牲了人民的福祉。

3. The refugees, each a desperate **wretch** fleeing conflict, arrived at the border seeking asylum and basic humanitarian aid.
   这些难民，每一个都是逃离冲突的可怜人，抵达边境寻求庇护和基本人道主义援助。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wretch" as "wretched" without the "-ed" — a wretch is someone in a wretched, pitiful condition.
把"wretch"想成"wretched（悲惨的）"去掉"-ed"——wretch就是处于悲惨、可怜境况中的人。
