# 🎵 counterpoint

🔊 /ˈkaʊntərpɔɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The technique of combining two or more melodic lines in music; something that contrasts with or complements something else
**中文：** n. 复调；对位法；对应物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contrast（对比）
- complement（补充）
- antithesis（对立面）
- foil（衬托）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **as a counterpoint to / in counterpoint / serve as a counterpoint / provide a counterpoint**

👉 Example: The author's dry humor serves as an effective counterpoint to the otherwise somber tone of the novel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The composer masterfully employed **counterpoint** to weave together multiple independent melodies into a rich and harmonious composition.
   作曲家巧妙地运用对位法，将多条独立旋律编织成一首丰富和谐的乐曲。

2. Her optimistic worldview provided a welcome **counterpoint** to the pervasive cynicism that dominated the political discourse of that era.
   她乐观的世界观为那个时代政治话语中弥漫的犬儒主义提供了一种令人欣慰的对照。

3. The documentary presented rural traditions as a striking **counterpoint** to the rapid urbanization transforming the nation's cultural landscape.
   这部纪录片将乡村传统呈现为正在改变国家文化面貌的快速城市化的鲜明对照。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "counter-point" as "a point that counters" — a contrasting element that plays against another, just like two melodies running in parallel.
把"counterpoint"想成"counter（对立）+ point（观点/旋律）"——一个对立的元素与另一个并行，就像两条旋律交相辉映。
