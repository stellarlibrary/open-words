# 📅 chronological

🔊 /ˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Arranged in the order in which events occurred; relating to the establishment of dates and time sequences
**中文：** adj. 按年代顺序排列的；编年体的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sequential（按顺序的）
- temporal（时间的）
- ordered（有序的）
- successive（连续的）
- historical（按历史顺序的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chronological order / chronological sequence / chronological account / in chronological order**

👉 Example: The professor asked students to arrange the historical events in strict chronological order for their final essay.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biography presents the statesman's achievements in **chronological** order, from his early education to his final years in office.
   这部传记按**时间顺序**呈现了这位政治家的成就，从早期教育到最后任期。

2. Assembling a **chronological** timeline of the archaeological findings helped researchers establish when the ancient settlement was abandoned.
   编制考古发现的**年代顺序**时间线帮助研究人员确定了古代定居点被废弃的时间。

3. The museum curators organized the exhibition in **chronological** sequence to illustrate the evolution of artistic styles across centuries.
   博物馆策展人按**年代顺序**组织展览，以展示几个世纪以来艺术风格的演变。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chronological" as "chrono + logical" — it is the logical way to arrange things by "chrono" (time), putting events in their proper time order.
把"chronological"想成"chrono + logical"——按"chrono"（时间）进行逻辑排列，将事件按正确的时间顺序排列。
