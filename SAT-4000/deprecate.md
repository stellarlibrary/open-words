# 🚫 deprecate

🔊 /ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To express disapproval of; to protest against; to belittle or disparage
**中文：** v. 反对；抨击；不赞成；轻视

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disapprove（不赞成）
- denounce（谴责）
- disparage（贬低）
- belittle（轻视）
- criticize（批评）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deprecate a practice / deprecate violence / self-deprecating humor / deprecate the use of**

👉 Example: Environmental activists deprecate the continued reliance on fossil fuels for energy production.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee chose to **deprecate** the outdated regulation, recommending its immediate replacement with modern standards.
   委员会决定反对这项过时的法规，建议立即以现代标准取而代之。

2. She was known for her **self-deprecating** wit, frequently making humble jokes at her own expense during lectures.
   她以自嘲式的幽默著称，常在讲座中拿自己开玩笑以示谦逊。

3. Leading scholars have long **deprecated** the methodology as fundamentally flawed and unsuitable for rigorous academic research.
   知名学者长期以来一直抨击该方法论存在根本性缺陷，不适合严谨的学术研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deprecate" as "de (down) + prec (pray/value)" — to pray down or lower the value of something by expressing disapproval.
把"deprecate"想成"de（向下）+ prec（祈祷/价值）"——通过表达不赞成来降低某事物的价值。
