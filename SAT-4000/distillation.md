# 🔬 distillation

🔊 /ˌdɪstɪˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of purifying a liquid by successive evaporation and condensation; the extraction of the essential elements of something
**中文：** n. 蒸馏；精馏；提炼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- purification（净化）
- refinement（精炼）
- extraction（提取）
- concentration（浓缩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fractional distillation / distillation process / distillation of ideas / distillation column**

👉 Example: Fractional distillation is widely used in the petroleum industry to separate crude oil into its various components.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **distillation** of crude petroleum into usable fuels remains one of the most fundamental processes in the modern energy industry.
   将原油蒸馏成可用燃料仍然是现代能源产业中最基本的工艺之一。

2. Her thesis represents a remarkable **distillation** of complex philosophical arguments into a clear and logically coherent analytical framework.
   她的论文将复杂的哲学论证出色地提炼为一个清晰且逻辑连贯的分析框架。

3. The **distillation** apparatus in the laboratory was carefully calibrated to ensure precise temperature control throughout the entire experiment.
   实验室中的蒸馏装置经过仔细校准，以确保整个实验过程中温度的精确控制。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "distill-ation" as "the action of distilling" — the noun form of distill, describing the process of purifying or extracting essence.
把"distillation"想成"distill（蒸馏）的名词形式"——描述净化或提取精华的过程。
