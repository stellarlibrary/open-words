# 🫁 alimentary

🔊 /ˌæl.ɪˈmen.tə.ri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to food, nutrition, or the process of nourishing; pertaining to the organs and functions of digestion.
**中文：** 滋养的；食物的；消化的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nutritional（营养的）
- dietary（饮食的）
- digestive（消化的）
- nourishing（滋养的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alimentary canal, alimentary system, alimentary tract, alimentary needs**
👉 Example: The alimentary canal extends from the mouth to the anus in the human body.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor delivered a comprehensive lecture on the structure and function of the **alimentary** canal.
   教授就消化道的结构和功能进行了全面的讲解。

2. Several **alimentary** disorders can be prevented through the consistent adoption of a balanced and nutritious diet.
   通过坚持均衡营养的饮食，可以预防多种消化系统疾病。

3. The research team investigated how environmental pollutants affect the **alimentary** processes of marine organisms.
   研究团队调查了环境污染物如何影响海洋生物的消化过程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Alimentary" contains "aliment" (food) + "-ary" (relating to) — it literally means "relating to food and digestion."
"alimentary"包含"aliment（食物）"+ "-ary（与……有关的）"——字面意思就是"与食物和消化有关的"。
