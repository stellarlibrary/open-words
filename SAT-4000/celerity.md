# ⚡ celerity

🔊 /sɪˈlerɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Swiftness of movement or action; rapidity; speed
**中文：** n. 快速，敏捷，迅速

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- swiftness（迅速）
- rapidity（快速）
- alacrity（敏捷）
- velocity（速度）
- dispatch（迅速处理）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with celerity / remarkable celerity / act with celerity / surprising celerity**

👉 Example: The emergency team responded with remarkable celerity, arriving at the scene within minutes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomatic corps acted with unusual **celerity** to negotiate a ceasefire before the conflict could escalate into full-scale warfare.
   外交使团以不同寻常的迅速行动谈判停火，以防冲突升级为全面战争。

2. The **celerity** with which the new administration implemented sweeping policy reforms astonished both supporters and critics alike.
   新政府推行全面政策改革的速度之快令支持者和批评者都感到震惊。

3. Scientific breakthroughs in quantum computing are advancing with such **celerity** that predictions from a decade ago now seem remarkably conservative.
   量子计算领域的科学突破以如此惊人的速度推进，以至于十年前的预测现在看来极为保守。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "celerity" as related to "accelerate" — both share the Latin root "celer" meaning swift, so celerity is the quality of being fast.
把"celerity"联想到"accelerate（加速）"——两者都源于拉丁语词根"celer（快速的）"，所以celerity就是快速的品质。
