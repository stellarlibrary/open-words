# 😤 abominable

🔊 /əˈbɒmɪnəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing moral revulsion; extremely unpleasant or of very poor quality; utterly detestable
**中文：** adj. 讨厌的；令人憎恶的；糟透的；可恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detestable（可憎的）
- loathsome（令人厌恶的）
- repulsive（令人反感的）
- dreadful（可怕的）
- hateful（可恨的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abominable behavior / abominable conditions / abominable crime / the Abominable Snowman**

👉 Example: The refugees were forced to endure abominable living conditions in the overcrowded camp.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee condemned the **abominable** treatment of prisoners held in the underground detention facility.
   委员会谴责了地下拘留设施中对囚犯的恶劣对待。

2. Critics described the restaurant's hygiene standards as **abominable** after inspectors discovered rodents in the kitchen.
   在检查人员发现厨房有老鼠后，评论家称该餐厅的卫生标准糟糕透顶。

3. The report documented **abominable** working conditions that violated numerous international labor rights conventions.
   该报告记录了违反多项国际劳工权利公约的恶劣工作条件。

---

"Abominable" sounds like "a bomb in a bull" — imagine the horrible, detestable chaos that would cause.
"Abominable"听起来像"a bomb in a bull"（公牛里的炸弹）——想象那会造成多么可怕、令人憎恶的混乱。
