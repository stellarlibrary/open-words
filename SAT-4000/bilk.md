# 🎭 bilk

🔊 /bɪlk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cheat or defraud someone, especially of money; to evade paying a debt. (n.) A swindle or fraud; a person who cheats
**中文：** v. 欺骗；诈骗；赖账 n. 诈骗；骗子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- defraud（诈骗）
- swindle（诈骗）
- cheat（欺骗）
- fleece（敲竹杠）
- dupe（欺骗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bilk someone out of something / bilk investors / bilk the system / bilk creditors**

👉 Example: The fraudulent scheme was designed to bilk elderly investors out of their retirement savings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The confidence artist managed to **bilk** dozens of unsuspecting victims out of millions of dollars through an elaborate investment scheme.
   这名骗子通过精心策划的投资计划成功地从数十名毫无戒心的受害者那里骗取了数百万美元。

2. Federal prosecutors charged the corporation with attempting to **bilk** the government by submitting fraudulent claims for reimbursement.
   联邦检察官指控该公司试图通过提交虚假报销申请来欺诈政府。

3. Investigators uncovered a network of shell companies specifically established to **bilk** insurance providers through fabricated accident claims.
   调查人员发现了一个专门为通过伪造事故索赔欺诈保险公司而建立的空壳公司网络。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bilk" as "bill + k(ill)" — someone who kills the bill by refusing to pay, essentially cheating others out of money.
把"bilk"想成"bill（账单）+ k(ill)（消灭）"——一个消灭账单的人，即通过拒绝付款来欺骗他人钱财。
