# 📋 synopsis

🔊 /sɪˈnɒpsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A brief summary or general survey of something; a condensed outline of a narrative, treatise, or text
**中文：** n. 大纲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- summary（摘要）
- abstract（概要）
- outline（大纲）
- overview（概述）
- digest（文摘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brief synopsis / provide a synopsis / plot synopsis / synopsis of the book / write a synopsis**

👉 Example: The publisher asked the author to submit a detailed synopsis before reviewing the full manuscript.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The graduate student submitted a concise **synopsis** of her dissertation research to the admissions committee for their preliminary review.
   这位研究生向招生委员会提交了她论文研究的简要大纲，供初步审查。

2. Before attending the conference, each participant received a **synopsis** of the keynote presentations scheduled for the opening ceremony.
   在参加会议之前，每位与会者都收到了开幕式主旨演讲的概要。

3. The literary journal requires authors to include a **synopsis** of no more than three hundred words alongside every submitted manuscript.
   该文学期刊要求作者在每份提交的稿件旁附上不超过三百字的概要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "syn-opsis" as "together-view" — a synopsis gives you a combined view of all the main points in one brief summary.
把"synopsis"想成"syn（一起）+ opsis（看）"——大纲让你一起看到所有要点的概览。
