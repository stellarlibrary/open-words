# 📬 convey

🔊 /kənˈveɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To communicate or express an idea, feeling, or meaning; to transport or carry something from one place to another
**中文：** v. 传达；运输；让与

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- communicate（传达）
- express（表达）
- transport（运送）
- transmit（传递）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **convey a message / convey meaning / convey information / convey goods / convey emotions**

👉 Example: The ambassador sought to convey the urgency of the situation through a carefully worded diplomatic statement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author used vivid metaphors to **convey** the emotional complexity of the protagonist's struggle against social injustice and oppression.
   作者运用生动的隐喻来传达主人公与社会不公和压迫作斗争的复杂情感。

2. Modern logistics companies employ sophisticated tracking systems to **convey** goods efficiently across international borders and vast distances.
   现代物流公司采用先进的追踪系统，高效地跨越国际边界和远距离运送货物。

3. Statistical graphs and visual diagrams can **convey** complex research findings more effectively than lengthy written explanations alone.
   统计图表和可视化图形比单纯冗长的文字说明更能有效地传达复杂的研究结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-vey" as "carry on the way" — whether carrying a message or carrying goods, you bring something along the way.
把"convey"想成"carry on the way"——无论是传递信息还是运送货物，都是沿途携带某物。
