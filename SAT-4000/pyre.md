# 🔥 pyre

🔊 /paɪər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large pile of combustible material, especially wood, used for burning a corpse in a funeral ceremony
**中文：** n. 火葬用的柴堆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bonfire（篝火）
- funeral fire（葬火）
- cremation pile（火化柴堆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **funeral pyre / build a pyre / light a pyre / place on a pyre / the pyre burned**

👉 Example: The mourners gathered around the funeral pyre as the flames began to rise into the evening sky.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In many ancient civilizations, warriors of great distinction were honored with elaborate **pyres** adorned with precious offerings.
   在许多古代文明中，杰出的战士会被用装饰有珍贵祭品的精致柴堆来纪念。

2. The anthropologist documented the traditional **pyre** ceremony as part of her extensive study of indigenous funerary practices.
   这位人类学家将传统的火葬柴堆仪式记录为她对本土丧葬习俗广泛研究的一部分。

3. Villagers constructed a massive **pyre** on the riverbank to cremate the remains of their revered spiritual leader.
   村民们在河岸上搭建了一座巨大的柴堆，用来火化他们敬仰的精神领袖的遗体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pyre" as related to "pyro" (fire) — a pyre is a structure built specifically for fire, used in funeral cremation rituals.
把"pyre"和"pyro（火）"联系起来——pyre是专门为火而搭建的结构，用于葬礼火化仪式。
