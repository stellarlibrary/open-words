# 🧬 chromosome

🔊 /ˈkroʊməˌsoʊm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A thread-like structure of nucleic acids and protein found in the nucleus of living cells, carrying genetic information in the form of genes
**中文：** n. 染色体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gene carrier（基因载体）
- genetic material（遗传物质）
- DNA strand（DNA链）
- chromatin body（染色质体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chromosome pair / chromosome mutation / X chromosome / Y chromosome / chromosome abnormality**

👉 Example: Each human cell typically contains twenty-three pairs of chromosomes that encode the complete genetic blueprint.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists discovered that an abnormality in **chromosome** 21 is responsible for the genetic condition known as Down syndrome.
   科学家发现21号**染色体**的异常是导致唐氏综合征这一遗传疾病的原因。

2. During cell division, each **chromosome** replicates itself to ensure that both daughter cells receive identical genetic information.
   在细胞分裂过程中，每条**染色体**都会自我复制，以确保两个子细胞获得相同的遗传信息。

3. The research team mapped the entire sequence of genes on the human **chromosome** to identify markers for hereditary diseases.
   研究团队绘制了人类**染色体**上的全部基因序列图谱，以识别遗传疾病的标记。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chromosome" as "chromo + some" — "chromo" means color (because chromosomes absorb dye easily) and "some" means body, so it is a "colored body" in the cell.
把"chromosome"想成"chromo + some"——"chromo"意为颜色（因为染色体容易吸收染料），"some"意为体，所以它是细胞中的"有色体"，即"染色体"。
