# 📏 length

🔊 /lɛŋθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The measurement of something from end to end; the extent of time something lasts; the quality of a vowel or syllable being long
**中文：** n. 长度，长；时间的长短；音长

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extent（范围）
- duration（持续时间）
- span（跨度）
- measure（度量）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **at length / go to great lengths / the length of time / full length**

👉 Example: The professor discussed the topic at length during the three-hour seminar.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **length** of the research paper exceeded the maximum word count specified in the submission guidelines.
   研究论文的长度超过了投稿指南中规定的最大字数。

2. Scientists measured the **length** of the glacier to determine how rapidly it had receded over the decade.
   科学家测量了冰川的长度，以确定它在过去十年中消退的速度。

3. The committee went to great **lengths** to ensure that every candidate received a fair and impartial evaluation.
   委员会竭尽全力确保每位候选人都能获得公正公平的评估。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "length" as "long-th" — it describes how long something is, whether in space or time.
把"length"想成"long的名词形式"——它描述某物有多长，无论是空间还是时间上的。
