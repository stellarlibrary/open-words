# 🪨 stratum

🔊 /ˈstreɪtəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A layer or level of an organization; a geological layer of rock or soil; a social class or level
**中文：** n. 岩层；地层

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- layer（层）
- level（层次）
- tier（阶层）
- bed（岩层）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **geological stratum / social stratum / a stratum of society / upper stratum**

👉 Example: The archaeologists carefully excavated each stratum to preserve the artifacts buried within.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist identified a thin **stratum** of volcanic ash that marked the boundary between two distinct geological periods.
   地质学家发现了一层薄薄的火山灰层，标志着两个不同地质时期之间的界限。

2. Members of every **stratum** of society were invited to participate in the national dialogue on educational reform.
   社会各阶层的成员都被邀请参加关于教育改革的全国性对话。

3. Researchers discovered a previously unknown **stratum** of sedimentary rock containing remarkably well-preserved marine fossils.
   研究人员发现了一层此前未知的沉积岩层，其中含有保存极为完好的海洋化石。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stratum" as "street-um" — streets are laid down in flat layers, just like geological strata in the earth.
把"stratum"想成"street-um"——街道是一层层铺设的，就像地球中的地质层一样。
