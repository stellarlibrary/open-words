# 🚫 proscribe

🔊 /proʊˈskraɪb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To officially forbid or ban something; to condemn or denounce as harmful or dangerous
**中文：** v. 禁止；剥夺……的公权

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prohibit（禁止）
- ban（取缔）
- forbid（不许）
- outlaw（宣布非法）
- interdict（阻止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proscribe certain activities / proscribe by law / proscribed organizations / proscribed substances**

👉 Example: The government moved swiftly to proscribe the extremist group after evidence of illegal activities surfaced.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new legislation sought to **proscribe** discriminatory hiring practices across all federally funded institutions and agencies.
   新立法旨在禁止所有联邦资助机构的歧视性招聘行为。

2. Several nations have agreed to **proscribe** the development and stockpiling of chemical weapons under international treaty obligations.
   多国已同意根据国际条约义务禁止化学武器的开发和储存。

3. The university's academic integrity policy explicitly **proscribes** plagiarism and any form of unauthorized collaboration on individual assignments.
   该大学的学术诚信政策明确禁止抄袭以及在个人作业中进行任何形式的未经授权的合作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-SCRIBE" — to write (scribe) someone OUT of society, to officially ban them. Don't confuse with "prescribe" (to recommend).
把"proscribe"想成"pro-SCRIBE"——把某人从社会中除名（scribe写），即正式禁止。注意不要与"prescribe"（开处方，建议）混淆。
