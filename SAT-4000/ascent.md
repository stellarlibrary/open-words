# 🧗 ascent

🔊 /əˈsɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The act of rising or climbing upward; an upward slope or path; a rise to a higher position or level.

**中文：** 上升；上坡路；登高

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- climb 攀登
- rise 上升
- elevation 提升
- advancement 进步

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Ascent" can be used both literally (climbing a mountain) and figuratively (rising to fame or power). Do not confuse it with "assent," which means agreement or approval.

"ascent" 既可用于字面意义（攀登山峰），也可用于比喻意义（声名鹊起或权力上升）。不要与 "assent"（同意、赞成）混淆。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The team began their **ascent** of the mountain at dawn despite the harsh weather conditions ahead.
   尽管前方天气条件恶劣，团队还是在黎明时分开始了登山。

2. Her rapid **ascent** in the corporate world was attributed to both exceptional talent and relentless determination.
   她在企业界的快速上升归功于卓越的才华和不懈的决心。

3. The final **ascent** to the summit proved far more challenging than any of the climbers had anticipated.
   通往顶峰的最后一段攀登比任何攀登者预想的都要困难得多。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "a-scent" rising upward like a scent floating up into the air — "ascent" means going up.

想象 "a-scent"（一缕香气）像气味一样向上飘升——"ascent" 就是向上的意思。
