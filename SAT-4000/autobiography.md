# 📕 autobiography

🔊 /ˌɔːtəbaɪˈɒɡrəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An account of a person's life written by that person; a literary genre consisting of self-written life narratives.
**中文：** 自传；自传文学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- memoir（回忆录）
- life story（生平故事）
- personal narrative（个人叙事）
- self-portrait（自画像）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **write/publish an autobiography**, **autobiography of [person]**, **best-selling autobiography**. Distinguish from "biography" (written by someone else about another person).

👉 Example: The retired statesman spent three years writing his autobiography before its highly anticipated publication.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The celebrated author's **autobiography** revealed surprising personal struggles that her public persona had carefully concealed for decades.
   这位著名作家的自传揭示了她的公众形象数十年来精心隐藏的惊人个人挣扎。

2. Students were assigned to read the civil rights leader's **autobiography** and compose a reflective analytical essay afterward.
   学生们被要求阅读这位民权领袖的自传，并随后撰写一篇反思性分析文章。

3. The musician's candid **autobiography** became an international bestseller within weeks of its official release in bookstores.
   这位音乐家坦诚的自传在正式上架书店后数周内便成为国际畅销书。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it into three parts: "auto" (self) + "bio" (life) + "graphy" (writing). An autobiography is literally "self-life-writing" — you write the story of your own life.
拆分为三部分："auto"（自己）+"bio"（生命）+"graphy"（书写）。Autobiography字面意思是"自己-生命-书写"——你书写自己的人生故事。
