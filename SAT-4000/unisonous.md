# 🎵 unisonous

🔊 /juːˈnɪsənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Sounding together at the same pitch; being in unison; harmonious in sound or agreement
**中文：** 同音的；和谐的；一致的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmonious（和谐的）
- concordant（一致的）
- consonant（协调的）
- unanimous（一致同意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unisonous voices / unisonous chorus / unisonous response / unisonous agreement**

👉 Example: The choir delivered a unisonous rendition that resonated powerfully throughout the cathedral.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The congregation raised their **unisonous** voices in prayer, creating a deeply moving and spiritually uplifting atmosphere within the chapel.
   会众齐声祈祷，在教堂内营造出一种深深感人且令人精神振奋的氛围。

2. The committee reached a **unisonous** conclusion after extensive deliberation, agreeing that immediate reform was absolutely necessary for progress.
   委员会经过广泛讨论后达成了一致结论，认为立即改革对于进步是绝对必要的。

3. The orchestra achieved a remarkably **unisonous** performance, with every instrument blending seamlessly into a single magnificent sound.
   管弦乐团呈现了极为和谐的演出，每件乐器都完美融合为一个壮丽的声音。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "uni-son-ous" as "one sound" — "uni" means one and "son" comes from Latin "sonus" meaning sound, so unisonous means sounding as one.
把"unisonous"想成"uni（一个）+ son（声音）+ ous"——发出同一个声音，即同音的、和谐的。
