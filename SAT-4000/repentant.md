# :pray: repentant

:speaker: /rɪˈpɛntənt/<br>
:label: *adjective | 形容词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling or expressing sincere regret and remorse for one's wrongdoing or sins
**中文：** adj. 后悔的；悔改的；有后悔表现的

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- remorseful（懊悔的）
- contrite（悔恨的）
- penitent（忏悔的）
- apologetic（歉疚的）
- regretful（遗憾的）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a repentant sinner / repentant attitude / feel repentant / truly repentant**

:point_right: Example: The repentant offender wrote a heartfelt letter of apology to every person he had wronged.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The **repentant** executive publicly acknowledged the environmental damage caused by the corporation's negligent waste disposal practices.
   这位悔过的高管公开承认了公司疏忽的废物处理行为所造成的环境损害。

2. Although he appeared **repentant** during the sentencing hearing, the judge remained skeptical of his sincerity and imposed a strict penalty.
   尽管他在量刑听证会上表现出悔改的态度，法官仍对他的诚意持怀疑态度并施以严厉处罚。

3. The memoir portrayed a deeply **repentant** individual who devoted the remainder of his life to charitable service and atonement.
   这部回忆录描绘了一个深感悔悟的人，他将余生奉献给了慈善服务和赎罪。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "repentant" as "re-pent-ant" — you "pent up" guilt inside, and then you release it by repenting again and again.
把"repentant"想成"re-pent-ant"——你把内疚"压抑（pent up）"在心里，然后通过一再忏悔来释放它。
