# 😰 timidity

🔊 /tɪˈmɪdɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being easily frightened or lacking confidence; shyness or fearfulness in the face of challenge
**中文：** n. 胆怯；胆小；羞怯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shyness（害羞）
- diffidence（缺乏自信）
- cowardice（怯懦）
- apprehension（忧虑）
- bashfulness（腼腆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overcome timidity / display timidity / natural timidity / timidity in social situations**

👉 Example: Her timidity prevented her from speaking up during the important board meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student's **timidity** in classroom discussions concealed a brilliant intellect capable of extraordinary analytical reasoning.
   这个学生在课堂讨论中的胆怯掩盖了其卓越的分析推理能力。

2. Investors who exhibit excessive **timidity** during market downturns often miss valuable opportunities for long-term financial growth.
   在市场低迷时表现出过度胆怯的投资者往往会错过宝贵的长期财务增长机会。

3. The new employee gradually overcame her **timidity** and began contributing bold ideas to the team's strategic planning sessions.
   这位新员工逐渐克服了她的胆怯，开始为团队的战略规划会议贡献大胆的想法。

---

Think of "timidity" as "timid + city" — imagine a timid person lost in a big city, too scared to ask anyone for directions.
把"timidity"想成"timid（胆怯的）+ city（城市）"——想象一个胆小的人迷失在大城市里，太害怕而不敢向任何人问路。
