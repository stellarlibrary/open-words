# 😞 powerless

🔊 /ˈpaʊərləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Without ability, influence, or power; lacking the capacity to act or accomplish something; helpless
**中文：** adj. 无力的；无能力的，无权的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- helpless（无助的）
- impotent（无力的）
- defenseless（无防御的）
- incapable（无能力的）
- weak（虚弱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **powerless to prevent / powerless against / feel powerless / rendered powerless / powerless to act**

👉 Example: The citizens felt powerless to influence the outcome of the decision made behind closed doors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The local authorities were **powerless** to enforce the new environmental regulations without adequate funding and institutional support.
   地方当局在没有充足资金和制度支持的情况下无力执行新的环境法规。

2. Many residents felt completely **powerless** against the corporate interests that dominated the zoning decisions in their community.
   许多居民对主导其社区分区决策的企业利益感到完全无能为力。

3. The opposition party found itself **powerless** to block the legislative measure after losing its majority in the parliament.
   反对党在议会中失去多数席位后发现自己无力阻止这项立法措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "powerless" as "power + less" — literally having less power, or no power at all, leaving one unable to act.
把"powerless"想成"power（力量）+ less（没有）"——字面意思是没有力量，让人无法行动。
