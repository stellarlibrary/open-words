# ⚛️ proton

🔊 /ˈproʊtɑːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A stable subatomic particle with a positive electric charge, found in the nucleus of all atoms
**中文：** n. 质子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- positive particle（正粒子）
- nucleon（核子）
- subatomic particle（亚原子粒子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proton beam / proton therapy / proton mass / number of protons**

👉 Example: The number of protons in the nucleus determines the chemical identity of an element.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each hydrogen atom contains exactly one **proton** in its nucleus, making it the simplest element in the periodic table.
   每个氢原子的原子核中恰好含有一个质子，使其成为元素周期表中最简单的元素。

2. Scientists used a powerful accelerator to collide **protons** at nearly the speed of light to study fundamental forces.
   科学家使用强大的加速器以接近光速的速度碰撞质子，以研究基本力。

3. Advanced **proton** therapy offers a more targeted approach to cancer treatment by minimizing damage to surrounding healthy tissue.
   先进的质子疗法通过减少对周围健康组织的损伤，提供了一种更具针对性的癌症治疗方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proton" as "proto" (first) + "n" — it was among the first particles discovered and carries a positive charge.
把"proton"想成"proto（最初的）"——它是最早被发现的粒子之一，带有正电荷。
