# 📉 degradation

🔊 /ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of declining to a lower condition, quality, or level; a state of disgrace or humiliation; chemical or biological decomposition
**中文：** n. 退化；降格；降级；降解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deterioration（恶化）
- decline（衰退）
- debasement（贬低）
- decomposition（分解）
- humiliation（羞辱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **environmental degradation / degradation of quality / soil degradation / moral degradation**

👉 Example: The environmental degradation caused by industrial pollution has reached an alarming level in coastal regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists have documented the rapid **degradation** of coral reefs due to rising ocean temperatures and increasing acidification levels.
   科学家们记录了由于海洋温度上升和酸化水平增加导致的珊瑚礁快速退化。

2. The **degradation** of public discourse has been exacerbated by the prevalence of misinformation across social media platforms.
   公共话语的退化因社交媒体平台上错误信息的泛滥而加剧。

3. Chemical **degradation** of the polymer occurs when it is exposed to prolonged ultraviolet radiation and extreme temperatures.
   当聚合物长时间暴露在紫外线辐射和极端温度下时，会发生化学降解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-grad-ation" as "de- (down) + grade + -ation" — a downgrade in condition, like going from an A grade to an F.
把"degradation"想成"de-（向下）+ grade（等级）+ -ation"——条件的降级，就像从A级降到F级。
