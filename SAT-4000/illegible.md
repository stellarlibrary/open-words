# 🔍 illegible

🔊 /ɪˈlɛdʒəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not clear enough to be read; impossible or very difficult to decipher due to poor handwriting or print quality
**中文：** adj. 难辨认的；字迹模糊的；无法阅读的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unreadable（无法阅读的）
- indecipherable（难以辨认的）
- unintelligible（难以理解的）
- obscured（模糊不清的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **illegible handwriting / illegible script / almost illegible / rendered illegible / barely illegible**

👉 Example: The doctor's prescription was nearly illegible, forcing the pharmacist to call the office for clarification.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Years of exposure to moisture and sunlight had rendered the ancient inscription almost completely **illegible** to modern researchers.
   多年的潮湿和阳光侵蚀使得这段古代铭文对现代研究人员来说几乎完全无法辨认。

2. The professor returned the student's essay with a note explaining that several critical paragraphs were entirely **illegible** and required resubmission.
   教授退回了学生的论文，并附注说明几个关键段落完全无法辨认，需要重新提交。

3. Archivists employed advanced digital imaging techniques to recover text from the **illegible** manuscripts that had been damaged in the fire.
   档案管理员采用先进的数字成像技术来恢复在火灾中受损的难以辨认的手稿中的文字。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "il-legible" — the prefix "il-" means "not," and "legible" means readable, so illegible means "not readable."
把"illegible"拆成"il（不）+ legible（可读的）"——就是不可读的，无法辨认的。
