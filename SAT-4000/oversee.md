# 🔍 oversee

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈsiː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To watch over and direct; to supervise; to inspect or examine officially
**中文：** v. 监督；监视；检查；视察

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supervise（监督）
- manage（管理）
- administer（管理）
- monitor（监控）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oversee operations / oversee a project / oversee the work / oversee the implementation**

👉 Example: A senior engineer was appointed to oversee the implementation of the new safety protocols.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board of directors appointed a new committee to **oversee** the company's compliance with recently enacted environmental regulations.
   董事会任命了一个新委员会来监督公司遵守最近颁布的环境法规的情况。

2. As project manager, she was responsible for **overseeing** every phase of development from initial planning through final delivery.
   作为项目经理，她负责监督从初始规划到最终交付的每一个开发阶段。

3. International observers were dispatched to **oversee** the election process and ensure that all voting procedures remained fair and transparent.
   国际观察员被派遣去监督选举过程，确保所有投票程序保持公正透明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-see" — to "see over" everything from a higher position, like a supervisor watching all the work from above.
把"oversee"想成从更高的位置"看到一切"——就像一个监督者从上方观察所有工作。
