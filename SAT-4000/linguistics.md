# 📚 linguistics

🔊 /lɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The scientific study of language and its structure, including phonetics, phonology, morphology, syntax, semantics, and pragmatics.
**中文：** n. 语言学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- language science（语言科学）
- philology（语文学）
- language studies（语言研究）
- glossology（语言学）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **applied linguistics / computational linguistics / structural linguistics / a degree in linguistics / the field of linguistics**

👉 Example: She pursued a graduate degree in applied linguistics to improve second-language teaching methods.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern **linguistics** encompasses numerous subfields, including sociolinguistics, psycholinguistics, and computational approaches to language analysis.
   现代语言学包含许多子领域，包括社会语言学、心理语言学和语言分析的计算方法。

2. The department of **linguistics** at the university has gained international recognition for its pioneering research in language acquisition.
   该大学的语言学系因其在语言习得方面的开创性研究而获得了国际认可。

3. Advances in **linguistics** have enabled researchers to reconstruct aspects of languages that disappeared thousands of years before written records.
   语言学的进步使研究人员能够重建在文字记录出现之前数千年已消失的语言的某些方面。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "linguist + ics" — adding "-ics" (the study of) to "linguist" gives you the academic field dedicated to studying language.
把"linguistics"拆分为"linguist（语言学家）+ ics（学科后缀）"——加上"-ics"就变成了研究语言的学科。
