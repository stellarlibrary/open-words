# 🖨️ scanner

🔊 /ˈskænər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A device that examines or scans something, especially one used to convert documents or images into digital data; a photoelectric scanning apparatus
**中文：** n. 扫描器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detector（探测器）
- sensor（传感器）
- reader（读取器）
- monitor（监视器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **body scanner / barcode scanner / document scanner / CT scanner / run through a scanner**

👉 Example: The airport installed advanced body scanners to enhance passenger security screening procedures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital acquired a state-of-the-art CT **scanner** capable of producing highly detailed three-dimensional images of internal organs.
   医院购置了一台最先进的CT扫描仪，能够生成内脏器官的高精度三维图像。

2. Researchers used an infrared **scanner** to detect subtle temperature variations across the surface of the ancient manuscript.
   研究人员使用红外扫描仪来检测古代手稿表面的细微温度变化。

3. The library's digital preservation team employed a high-resolution **scanner** to archive thousands of fragile historical documents.
   图书馆的数字化保存团队使用高分辨率扫描仪来存档数千份脆弱的历史文献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "scanner" as "scan + er" — a device that scans. It sweeps across a surface like eyes scanning a page, capturing every detail.
把"scanner"想成"scan + er"——一个扫描的设备。它像眼睛扫视页面一样掠过表面，捕捉每一个细节。
