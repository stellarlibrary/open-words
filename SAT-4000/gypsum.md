# 🪨 gypsum

🔊 /ˈdʒɪpsəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A soft sulfate mineral (calcium sulfate dihydrate) used in construction, especially for making plaster and drywall
**中文：** n. 石膏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plaster（灰泥）
- calcium sulfate（硫酸钙）
- alabaster（雪花石膏）
- selenite（透石膏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gypsum board / gypsum plaster / gypsum deposit / gypsum mine / gypsum powder**

👉 Example: The construction crew used gypsum board to create smooth interior walls throughout the new office building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Geologists discovered a massive deposit of **gypsum** beneath the arid desert surface during their comprehensive mineral survey expedition.
   地质学家在全面的矿物勘探中发现了干旱沙漠地表下的大量石膏矿床。

2. The ancient Egyptians utilized **gypsum** as a key material in constructing the mortar joints of the Great Pyramids.
   古埃及人将石膏用作建造大金字塔砂浆接缝的关键材料。

3. Modern drywall manufacturing relies heavily on **gypsum** because of its excellent fire-resistant and sound-insulating properties.
   现代石膏板制造业严重依赖石膏，因为它具有出色的防火和隔音性能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gypsum" as "gyp-sum" — gyp sounds like "jip" (chip), and gypsum is chipped from rock to make the plaster on your walls.
把"gypsum"想成"gyp-sum"——gyp听起来像"chip"（碎片），石膏就是从岩石中开采出来制成墙壁灰泥的。
