# 😜 mischievous

🔊 /ˈmɪstʃɪvəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing or showing a fondness for causing trouble in a playful way; intended to harm or cause mischief
**中文：** adj. 淘气的；恶作剧的；有害的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- naughty（淘气的）
- impish（顽皮的）
- playful（爱玩的）
- roguish（捣蛋的）
- prankish（恶作剧的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mischievous grin / mischievous child / mischievous intent / mischievous behavior**

👉 Example: The mischievous boy hid his sister's homework behind the bookshelf as a prank.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student gave a **mischievous** grin before revealing the harmless prank he had carefully planned for the entire class.
   这个学生露出淘气的笑容，然后揭示了他为全班精心策划的无害恶作剧。

2. Spreading **mischievous** rumors about political opponents is a tactic frequently condemned by ethics committees and media watchdogs.
   散布关于政治对手的有害谣言是一种经常受到道德委员会和媒体监督机构谴责的策略。

3. The **mischievous** kitten knocked every item off the shelf while its owner was away attending an important meeting.
   这只淘气的小猫趁主人外出参加重要会议时把架子上的每样东西都打翻了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-chief-ous" — a "chief" of "mis"chief, the leader of all troublemakers who loves playing pranks.
把"mischievous"想成"mis-chief-ous"——恶作剧的"首领"（chief），所有捣蛋鬼的头头，最爱搞恶作剧。
