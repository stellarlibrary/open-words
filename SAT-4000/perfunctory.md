# 😒 perfunctory

🔊 /pərˈfʌŋktəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection; done merely as a routine duty without genuine care or interest
**中文：** adj. 敷衍的；马虎的；得过且过的；例行公事的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cursory（草率的）
- superficial（肤浅的）
- halfhearted（不热心的）
- mechanical（机械的）
- negligent（疏忽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perfunctory glance / perfunctory greeting / perfunctory inspection / perfunctory effort**

👉 Example: The inspector gave the building only a perfunctory examination before signing the approval form.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee conducted a **perfunctory** review of the budget proposal, approving it within minutes without raising any substantive questions.
   委员会对预算提案进行了敷衍的审查，几分钟内就批准了，没有提出任何实质性问题。

2. His **perfunctory** apology failed to convince anyone that he genuinely regretted the harm caused by his thoughtless remarks.
   他敷衍的道歉未能说服任何人他真正后悔自己轻率言论所造成的伤害。

3. The nurse gave the patient's chart only a **perfunctory** glance before administering the medication prescribed by the attending physician.
   护士在给药前只草草看了一眼病人的病历，就按照主治医生的处方给了药。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perfunctory" as "per-FUNK-tory" — doing something in a funk, without energy or enthusiasm, just going through the motions.
把"perfunctory"想成"per-FUNK-tory"——在低落的状态下做事，没有精力或热情，只是走走过场。
