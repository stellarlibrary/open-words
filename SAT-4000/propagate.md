# 🌱 propagate

🔊 /ˈprɒpəɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To spread and promote an idea, theory, or knowledge widely; to breed or reproduce organisms; to transmit signals or waves
**中文：** v. 传播；传送；繁殖；宣传；扩散

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disseminate（传播）
- spread（散布）
- proliferate（扩散）
- reproduce（繁殖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **propagate ideas / propagate a species / propagate information / propagate through a medium**

👉 Example: Scientists propagate rare plant species in controlled greenhouse environments to prevent extinction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Social media platforms have made it remarkably easy to **propagate** misinformation among millions of users within hours.
   社交媒体平台使得在数小时内向数百万用户传播错误信息变得极其容易。

2. Botanists developed innovative techniques to **propagate** endangered orchid species through careful tissue culture methods in laboratories.
   植物学家开发了创新技术，通过实验室中精心的组织培养方法来繁殖濒危兰花物种。

3. Sound waves **propagate** more efficiently through water than through air, enabling whales to communicate across vast oceanic distances.
   声波在水中比在空气中传播更有效，使鲸鱼能够跨越广阔的海洋距离进行交流。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "propagate" as "prop-a-gate" — you prop open a gate so things can spread through freely and multiply.
把"propagate"想成"prop-a-gate"——你撑开一扇门，让事物自由地传播和繁殖。
