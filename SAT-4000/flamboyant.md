# 🔥 flamboyant

🔊 /flæmˈbɔɪənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showy, bold, or audacious in style or manner; elaborately colorful and ornate; resembling flames
**中文：** adj. 艳丽的；火焰似的；炫耀的；华丽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ostentatious（炫耀的）
- extravagant（奢华的）
- showy（华丽的）
- ornate（华丽的）
- flamboyance（炫目）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **flamboyant personality / flamboyant style / flamboyant gesture / flamboyant display**

👉 Example: The designer's flamboyant creations attracted considerable attention at the international fashion exhibition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The opera singer was known for her **flamboyant** costumes that dazzled audiences at every major performance venue.
   这位歌剧演唱家以其在每个主要演出场所令观众目眩的华丽服装而闻名。

2. His **flamboyant** rhetoric and theatrical gestures made him one of the most memorable political figures of the era.
   他炫目的修辞和夸张的姿态使他成为那个时代最令人难忘的政治人物之一。

3. The architect rejected the **flamboyant** design proposals in favor of a more restrained and minimalist aesthetic approach.
   建筑师拒绝了华丽的设计方案，转而采用更加克制和简约的美学风格。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "flamboy-ant" as an ant near a flame — the flame is bright, colorful, and impossible to ignore, just like something flamboyant.
把"flamboyant"想成"flame"（火焰）+"boy"——一个像火焰一样耀眼、华丽、引人注目的男孩。
