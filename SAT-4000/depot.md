# 🏭 depot

🔊 /ˈdɛpoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A storage facility or warehouse; a bus or train station; a place for storing military supplies
**中文：** n. 仓库；储存处；停车场；车站

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- warehouse（仓库）
- storehouse（储藏室）
- terminal（终点站）
- repository（储存库）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bus depot / supply depot / military depot / freight depot**

👉 Example: The army established a supply depot near the front lines to ensure rapid distribution of provisions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The military constructed a massive **depot** in the desert to store ammunition and essential equipment for the campaign.
   军方在沙漠中建造了一座大型仓库，用于储存弹药和战役所需的重要装备。

2. Commuters gathered at the downtown bus **depot** each morning, waiting for transportation to the commercial district.
   通勤者每天早晨聚集在市中心的公交车站，等待前往商业区的交通工具。

3. The pharmaceutical company maintained a refrigerated **depot** to preserve temperature-sensitive vaccines and biological specimens.
   这家制药公司维护着一个冷藏仓库，用于保存对温度敏感的疫苗和生物标本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "depot" as related to "deposit" — a depot is where things are deposited and stored for later use.
把"depot"和"deposit（存放）"联系起来——depot就是存放物品以备后用的地方。
