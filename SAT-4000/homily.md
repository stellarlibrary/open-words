# 🎤 homily

🔊 /ˈhɒmɪli/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A religious discourse or sermon; a tedious moralizing lecture or piece of advice
**中文：** n. 布道；说教；训诫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sermon（布道）
- lecture（训诫）
- moralization（说教）
- preaching（传道）
- admonition（告诫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliver a homily / a long homily / a homily on virtue / a tiresome homily**

👉 Example: The priest delivered a brief homily on the importance of compassion and forgiveness during the service.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bishop's **homily** on social justice moved the congregation to take immediate action in support of local charities.
   主教关于社会公正的布道感动了会众，促使他们立即采取行动支持当地慈善机构。

2. Students grew restless as the headmaster launched into yet another lengthy **homily** about personal responsibility and discipline.
   当校长又开始了一轮关于个人责任和纪律的冗长说教时，学生们变得焦躁不安。

3. The editorial read less like objective journalism and more like a moralizing **homily** aimed at shaming political opponents.
   这篇社论读起来不像是客观的新闻报道，更像是旨在羞辱政治对手的说教。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homily" as "home + ily(family)" — like a family lecture at home where parents moralize about right and wrong.
把"homily"想成"home + ily（家人）"——就像在家里父母关于是非对错的说教一样。
