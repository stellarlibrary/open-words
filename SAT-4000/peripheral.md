# 🔲 peripheral

🔊 /pəˈrɪfərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to the outer edge; of secondary importance. (n.) An external device connected to a computer
**中文：** adj. 外围的；次要的；边缘的 n. 外部设备

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- marginal（边缘的）
- secondary（次要的）
- tangential（附带的）
- incidental（附属的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peripheral vision / peripheral issues / peripheral nervous system / peripheral device**

👉 Example: The committee considered the budget dispute to be a peripheral matter unrelated to the main agenda.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher argued that social media usage was merely **peripheral** to the central causes of declining academic performance.
   研究人员认为，社交媒体的使用只是学业成绩下降的次要原因。

2. Damage to the **peripheral** nervous system can result in numbness, tingling, and chronic pain in the extremities.
   周围神经系统的损伤可导致四肢麻木、刺痛和慢性疼痛。

3. The manager dismissed the complaints as **peripheral** concerns that should not delay the implementation of the new policy.
   经理将这些投诉视为不应推迟新政策实施的次要问题而予以驳回。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peri-pheral" — "peri" means "around" (as in perimeter). Something peripheral is around the edges, not at the center, hence secondary.
把"peripheral"想成"peri（周围）+ pheral"——"peri"表示"周围"（如perimeter周长），所以peripheral指的是边缘的、次要的。
