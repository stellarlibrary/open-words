# 😨 recoil

🔊 /rɪˈkɔɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To draw back instinctively in fear, disgust, or surprise; to spring back as a result of force or impact. (n.) The act of recoiling; a backward movement or reaction
**中文：** v. 畏缩；弹回；反冲；报应 n. 畏缩；弹回

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flinch（退缩）
- shrink（畏缩）
- wince（畏缩）
- rebound（弹回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recoil in horror / recoil from / recoil at the sight of / gun recoil**

👉 Example: She recoiled in horror when she discovered the extent of the corruption within the organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audience visibly **recoiled** when the documentary revealed the shocking conditions inside the overcrowded detention facilities.
   当纪录片揭露了拥挤的拘留设施内令人震惊的条件时，观众明显地畏缩了。

2. Many investors **recoil** from high-risk ventures that lack transparent financial reporting and adequate regulatory oversight mechanisms.
   许多投资者对缺乏透明财务报告和充分监管机制的高风险投资望而却步。

3. The powerful **recoil** of the experimental weapon forced engineers to redesign the entire stabilization system from the ground up.
   实验武器强大的反冲力迫使工程师从头开始重新设计整个稳定系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-coil" as "coiling back again" — like a spring that coils back when released, you recoil by pulling back suddenly.
把"recoil"想成"re-coil（重新卷缩）"——就像弹簧松开后卷缩回去一样，你突然向后退缩。
