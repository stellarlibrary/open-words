# 📚 synonym

🔊 /ˈsɪnənɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A word or phrase that means exactly or nearly the same as another word or phrase in the same language
**中文：** n. 同义词

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equivalent（等义词）
- counterpart（对应词）
- analog（类似词）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **synonym for / synonym of / close synonym / exact synonym / list of synonyms**

👉 Example: The word "happy" is a common synonym for "glad" in everyday English usage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students must learn to distinguish between exact **synonyms** and words that share only approximate or contextual similarities in meaning.
   学生必须学会区分精确的同义词和仅在近似或语境意义上相似的词汇。

2. The professor emphasized that no two **synonyms** are perfectly interchangeable because each carries subtle differences in connotation and register.
   教授强调没有两个同义词是完全可互换的，因为每个词在内涵和语域上都有微妙的差异。

3. In academic writing, using varied **synonyms** helps authors avoid repetition and maintain the reader's engagement throughout the text.
   在学术写作中，使用不同的同义词有助于作者避免重复并在全文中保持读者的参与度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "syn-onym" as "same-name" — two words that share the same name or meaning are synonyms.
把"synonym"想成"syn（相同）+ onym（名字）"——两个有相同含义的词就是同义词。
