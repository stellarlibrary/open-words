# 📚 comprehensive

🔊 /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Including all or nearly all elements or aspects; thorough and wide-ranging in scope
**中文：** adj. 综合的；广泛的；内容广泛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thorough（彻底的）
- exhaustive（详尽的）
- all-encompassing（包罗万象的）
- extensive（广泛的）
- inclusive（包容的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **comprehensive review / comprehensive plan / comprehensive analysis / comprehensive coverage**

👉 Example: The committee conducted a comprehensive review of all existing safety regulations before issuing new guidelines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university published a **comprehensive** guide to help incoming students navigate academic requirements and campus resources.
   大学出版了一本综合指南，帮助新生了解学术要求和校园资源。

2. Before launching the product, the team performed a **comprehensive** analysis of market trends and consumer preferences.
   在产品上市之前，团队对市场趋势和消费者偏好进行了全面分析。

3. The report provides a **comprehensive** overview of environmental challenges facing coastal communities throughout the region.
   该报告全面概述了该地区沿海社区面临的环境挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "comprehend" + "sive" — if you can comprehend everything, your understanding is comprehensive.
把"comprehensive"拆成"comprehend（理解）+ sive"——如果你能理解所有事情，那你的理解就是综合全面的。
