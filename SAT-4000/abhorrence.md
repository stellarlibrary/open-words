# 😤 abhorrence

🔊 /əbˈhɔːrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A feeling of extreme hatred or disgust; utter loathing; something that is detested
**中文：** n. 痛恨，憎恶；令人厌恶的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- loathing（憎恨）
- detestation（厌恶）
- revulsion（反感）
- repugnance（嫌恶）
- hatred（仇恨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abhorrence of / hold in abhorrence / express abhorrence / deep abhorrence**

👉 Example: She expressed her deep abhorrence of any form of discrimination in her powerful keynote address.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher wrote extensively about humanity's natural **abhorrence** of cruelty and unprovoked violence.
   这位哲学家大量论述了人类对残忍和无端暴力的天然憎恶。

2. Public **abhorrence** of corruption has intensified, driving millions of citizens to demand sweeping government reforms.
   公众对腐败的痛恨加剧，促使数百万公民要求进行大规模政府改革。

3. Her **abhorrence** of dishonesty shaped every decision she made throughout her distinguished judicial career.
   她对不诚实行为的深恶痛绝影响了她在杰出司法生涯中的每一个决定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abhorrence" comes from "abhor" + "-ence." Picture yourself recoiling in horror — "ab-HORROR-ence" equals extreme horror and disgust.
"Abhorrence"来自"abhor" + "-ence"。想象自己恐惧地退缩——"ab-HORROR-ence"就是极度的恐惧和厌恶。
