# 🤸 lithe

🔊 /laɪð/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Thin, supple, and graceful in movement; bending easily and flexibly
**中文：** adj. 轻盈的；柔软的；易弯曲的；优美灵活的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supple（柔韧的）
- flexible（灵活的）
- agile（敏捷的）
- graceful（优美的）
- nimble（轻快的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lithe body / lithe movements / lithe figure / lithe dancer / lithe and graceful**

👉 Example: The lithe gymnast executed a flawless routine that captivated the entire audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **lithe** dancer moved across the stage with effortless grace, captivating the audience with every precise, fluid motion.
   那位轻盈的舞者以毫不费力的优雅姿态穿过舞台，每一个精准流畅的动作都令观众着迷。

2. Years of dedicated yoga practice had given her a remarkably **lithe** physique that reflected both discipline and natural flexibility.
   多年坚持瑜伽练习使她拥有了非常柔韧的体态，体现了纪律性和天生的柔韧性。

3. The **lithe** branches of the willow tree swayed gently in the evening breeze, creating a tranquil and picturesque scene.
   柳树柔软的枝条在晚风中轻轻摇曳，营造出一幅宁静如画的景象。

把"lithe"与"writhe（扭动）"押韵联想——想象一条蛇顺畅地扭动，身体以柔软灵活的方式弯曲。
