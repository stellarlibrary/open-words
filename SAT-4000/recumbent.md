# 🛋️ recumbent

🔊 /rɪˈkʌmbənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lying down; reclining in a restful or resting position; leaning back
**中文：** adj. 斜倚的；靠着的；斜躺着的；休息的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reclining（斜倚的）
- supine（仰卧的）
- prostrate（俯卧的）
- prone（俯伏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recumbent position / recumbent figure / recumbent bicycle / recumbent statue**

👉 Example: The patient remained in a recumbent position for several hours following the surgical procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum displayed a magnificent **recumbent** marble statue of a Roman nobleman from the second century.
   博物馆展出了一尊宏伟的二世纪罗马贵族斜倚大理石雕像。

2. The physician advised the injured athlete to remain **recumbent** until the swelling in her lower spine fully subsided.
   医生建议受伤的运动员保持平躺，直到她下脊柱的肿胀完全消退。

3. Several **recumbent** figures were depicted in the Renaissance painting, suggesting an atmosphere of leisurely repose and tranquility.
   这幅文艺复兴时期的画作中描绘了几个斜倚的人物，营造出悠闲安宁的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-cumb-ent" — "cumb" sounds like "cumber" (as in encumber, to weigh down), so recumbent means weighed down into a lying position.
把"recumbent"想成"re(向后) + cumb(躺) + ent"——向后躺着的，即斜倚的。
