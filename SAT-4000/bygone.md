# ⏳ bygone

🔊 /ˈbaɪɡɒn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Belonging to or occurring in an earlier time; past; (n.) something that happened in the past
**中文：** adj. 过去的；以往的 n. 过去的事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- past（过去的）
- former（从前的）
- antiquated（过时的）
- defunct（已废弃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a bygone era / bygone days / let bygones be bygones / bygone age**

👉 Example: The museum exhibit transported visitors to a bygone era of elegance and aristocratic grandeur.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian's lecture vividly recreated the customs and traditions of a **bygone** era that few modern scholars understood.
   历史学家的讲座生动地重现了一个过去时代的习俗和传统，鲜有现代学者了解。

2. Both nations agreed to let **bygones** be bygones and embarked upon diplomatic negotiations to establish lasting peace.
   两国同意既往不咎，并着手开展外交谈判以建立持久和平。

3. The restored manor house stood as a magnificent testament to the architectural achievements of a **bygone** century.
   修复后的庄园大宅是对过去一个世纪建筑成就的壮丽见证。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "by-gone" — something that has "gone by," meaning it has already passed. Bygone literally describes what has gone by in time.
想想"by-gone"——已经"gone by"（经过/过去了）的东西。Bygone字面意思就是描述时间上已经过去的事物。
