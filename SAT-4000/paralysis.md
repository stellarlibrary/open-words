# 🧊 paralysis

🔊 /pəˈræləsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Loss of the ability to move part or all of the body; a state of powerlessness or inability to act
**中文：** n. 麻痹；瘫痪；无力；停顿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immobility（不动）
- incapacitation（丧失能力）
- numbness（麻木）
- stagnation（停滞）
- helplessness（无助）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **paralysis of / suffer paralysis / political paralysis / analysis paralysis / complete paralysis**

👉 Example: The sudden onset of paralysis left the patient unable to move his lower extremities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged political **paralysis** in the legislature prevented the passage of critical economic reform bills for over a year.
   立法机构长期的政治瘫痪导致关键的经济改革法案一年多未能获得通过。

2. The accident resulted in partial **paralysis** of her left arm, requiring extensive rehabilitation and physical therapy sessions.
   事故导致她左臂部分瘫痪，需要大量的康复治疗和物理治疗。

3. Many entrepreneurs suffer from analysis **paralysis**, overthinking decisions to the point where they are unable to take meaningful action.
   许多企业家患有"分析瘫痪"症，过度思考决策以至于无法采取有意义的行动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "para-lysis" as "para" (beside) + "lysis" (loosening) — your muscles loosen and go limp, leaving you unable to move.
把"paralysis"想成"para"（旁边）+ "lysis"（松弛）——肌肉松弛无力，让你无法动弹。
