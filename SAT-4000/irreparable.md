# 💔 irreparable

🔊 /ɪˈrɛpərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Impossible to repair, rectify, or make good; beyond the possibility of being restored to a former condition
**中文：** adj. 不能挽回的；不能修补的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irreversible（不可逆的）
- irremediable（无法补救的）
- permanent（永久的）
- unrecoverable（无法恢复的）
- beyond repair（无法修复的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irreparable damage / irreparable harm / irreparable loss / irreparable injury**

👉 Example: The oil spill caused irreparable damage to the coastal ecosystem that scientists say may never fully recover.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The leaked confidential documents caused **irreparable** harm to the diplomatic relations between the two formerly allied nations.
   泄露的机密文件对这两个曾经结盟的国家之间的外交关系造成了不可挽回的损害。

2. Historians warn that neglecting the preservation of ancient manuscripts will result in **irreparable** loss of cultural heritage.
   历史学家警告说，忽视古代手稿的保存将导致文化遗产不可挽回的损失。

3. The court ruled that the defendant's actions had inflicted **irreparable** injury upon the plaintiff's professional reputation and career.
   法院裁定被告的行为对原告的职业声誉和事业造成了不可弥补的伤害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "irreparable" as "ir-repair-able" — something that is not repairable, like a shattered mirror that can never reflect the same image again.
把"irreparable"想成"ir-repair-able"——不能修复的东西，就像一面碎裂的镜子永远无法再映出同样的影像。
