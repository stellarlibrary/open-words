# 😤 truculent

🔊 /ˈtrʌkjʊlənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant; harsh or cruel in speech
**中文：** 好斗的；野蛮的；言词刻毒的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- belligerent（好斗的）
- combative（好战的）
- pugnacious（爱争吵的）
- aggressive（挑衅的）
- contentious（好争论的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **truculent attitude / truculent tone / truculent response / truculent manner**

👉 Example: The diplomat's truculent remarks nearly derailed the peace negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members were taken aback by the witness's **truculent** demeanor during the congressional hearing.
   委员会成员对证人在国会听证会上好斗的态度感到震惊。

2. His **truculent** editorial provoked widespread condemnation from scholars and journalists across the political spectrum.
   他言词刻毒的社论引发了各政治派别学者和记者的广泛谴责。

3. The **truculent** negotiator refused every compromise, ultimately causing the trade agreement to collapse entirely.
   那位好斗的谈判代表拒绝一切妥协，最终导致贸易协定彻底破裂。

把"truculent"想成"truck-u-lent"——想象一个暴躁的卡车司机借给你东西后，凶巴巴地要你还回来。
