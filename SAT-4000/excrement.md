# 💩 excrement

🔊 /ˈɛkskrɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Waste matter discharged from the body, especially feces; bodily waste expelled through the digestive system
**中文：** 粪便；排泄物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- waste（废物）
- feces（粪便）
- dung（粪肥）
- droppings（动物粪便）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **animal excrement / human excrement / excrement samples / traces of excrement**

👉 Example: Archaeologists analyzed ancient excrement samples to determine the dietary habits of early civilizations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental scientists studied the **excrement** of migratory birds to track the spread of avian pathogens across continental boundaries.
   环境科学家研究了候鸟的排泄物，以追踪禽类病原体跨大陆的传播情况。

2. The accumulation of animal **excrement** in the waterway contributed to dangerously elevated levels of nitrogen and bacterial contamination.
   动物排泄物在水道中的积累导致氮含量和细菌污染达到了危险的高水平。

3. Forensic analysts collected **excrement** samples from the site to identify the species responsible for the ecological disturbance observed.
   法医分析人员从现场收集了排泄物样本，以确定造成所观察到的生态干扰的物种。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-cre-ment" — something that is "excreted" from the body, with "ment" indicating the substance or product of that action.
把"excrement"想成"ex-cre-ment"——从身体"排出"的东西，"ment"表示该动作的物质或产物。
