# ⚖️ reparation

🔊 /ˌrepəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of making amends for a wrong; compensation paid for damage or injury; (often plural) payments made by a defeated nation for war damages
**中文：** n. 赔偿，弥补，修理；赔款

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compensation（赔偿）
- restitution（归还）
- redress（补偿）
- amends（弥补）
- indemnity（赔偿金）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make reparation / war reparations / demand reparations / reparation for damages**

👉 Example: The court ordered the corporation to pay substantial reparations to the affected communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defeated nation was required to pay enormous **reparations** to the allied countries under the terms of the peace treaty.
   根据和平条约的条款，战败国被要求向同盟国支付巨额赔款。

2. Community leaders called for **reparation** to address the lasting economic inequalities caused by decades of discriminatory policies.
   社区领袖呼吁进行赔偿，以解决数十年歧视性政策造成的持久经济不平等。

3. The organization committed to making full **reparation** for the environmental damage resulting from the industrial chemical spill.
   该组织承诺对工业化学品泄漏造成的环境损害进行全面赔偿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "REPAIR-ation" — reparation is the act of repairing damage done, making things right again through compensation.
把"reparation"想成"REPAIR-ation"——reparation就是通过赔偿来修复所造成的损害，使事情恢复正常。
