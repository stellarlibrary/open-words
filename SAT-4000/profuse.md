# 🌸 profuse

🔊 /prəˈfjuːs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Abundantly plentiful; appearing in great quantity; given or offered freely and generously, sometimes excessively
**中文：** adj. 极其丰富的；很多的；慷慨的；奢侈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- copious（大量的）
- lavish（慷慨的）
- plentiful（充足的）
- excessive（过多的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **profuse apologies / profuse bleeding / profuse sweating / profuse thanks / profuse growth**

👉 Example: She offered profuse apologies for the misunderstanding that had caused so much inconvenience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador extended **profuse** thanks to the host nation for providing generous humanitarian assistance during the prolonged regional crisis.
   大使对东道国在长期地区危机中提供的慷慨人道主义援助表示衷心的感谢。

2. The tropical garden displayed **profuse** blooms of orchids and hibiscus that attracted researchers studying pollination patterns in equatorial climates.
   热带花园展示了繁茂的兰花和木槿花，吸引了研究赤道气候授粉模式的研究人员。

3. Despite his **profuse** assurances that the project would succeed, the board of directors remained cautious about approving additional funding.
   尽管他反复保证项目会成功，董事会在批准额外资金方面仍然保持谨慎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-fuse" as "pour-fuse" — imagine pouring so much that it overflows like a blown fuse, representing excessive abundance.
把"profuse"想成"pour（倾倒）+ fuse（保险丝）"——想象倒得太多以至于像保险丝烧断一样溢出，代表过度的丰富。
