# 😈 sardonic

🔊 /sɑːrˈdɒnɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Grimly mocking or cynical; disdainfully humorous; scornfully derisive
**中文：** adj. 嘲笑的，挖苦的，讥讽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cynical（愤世嫉俗的）
- mocking（嘲弄的）
- scornful（轻蔑的）
- derisive（嘲笑的）
- sarcastic（讽刺的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sardonic smile / sardonic humor / sardonic laugh / sardonic wit / sardonic remark**

👉 Example: He gave a sardonic smile when told the project would be completed ahead of schedule.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The critic offered a **sardonic** review of the film, praising its ability to waste both time and considerable financial resources.
   这位评论家对该电影进行了讽刺性的评论，赞扬了它浪费时间和大量财力的能力。

2. Her **sardonic** laughter echoed through the boardroom as the executives presented yet another overly optimistic quarterly forecast.
   当高管们又一次提出过于乐观的季度预测时，她嘲讽的笑声回荡在会议室里。

3. The novelist was known for his **sardonic** portrayal of upper-class society, exposing its vanity and moral emptiness.
   这位小说家以讥讽地描绘上流社会而闻名，揭露了其虚荣和道德空虚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sardonic" as related to "Sardinia" — ancient Greeks believed a plant from Sardinia caused facial convulsions resembling a bitter, mocking grin.
把"sardonic"与"撒丁岛"联系起来——古希腊人相信撒丁岛上的一种植物会引起面部抽搐，看起来像一种苦涩的嘲讽笑容。
