# 🚑 ambulance

🔊 /ˈæmbjələns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A specially equipped vehicle used to transport sick or injured people to a hospital for emergency medical treatment
**中文：** 救护车

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- emergency vehicle（急救车辆）
- rescue vehicle（救援车辆）
- paramedic unit（急救单元）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **call an ambulance / ambulance service / ambulance crew / air ambulance**

👉 Example: The bystanders immediately called an ambulance after witnessing the serious traffic accident.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ambulance** arrived within minutes, and the paramedics quickly stabilized the patient before transporting her to the hospital.
   救护车在几分钟内赶到，急救人员迅速稳定了病人的状况，然后将她送往医院。

2. The city invested heavily in upgrading its **ambulance** fleet to reduce emergency response times in rural districts.
   该市大力投资升级其救护车队，以缩短农村地区的急救响应时间。

3. An air **ambulance** was dispatched to the remote mountain area where the injured hiker could not be reached by road.
   一架空中救护机被派往偏远山区，因为受伤的登山者无法通过公路到达。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ambulance" from Latin "ambulare" (to walk) — originally a mobile hospital that "walked" to the battlefield to help the wounded.
把"ambulance"和拉丁语"ambulare"（行走）联系起来——最初是一种"走向"战场帮助伤员的移动医院。
