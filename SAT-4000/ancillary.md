# 🔧 ancillary

🔊 /ˈænsəˌlɛri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Providing necessary support to the primary activities or operation of an organization; supplementary or subordinate
**中文：** 辅助的；副的；从属的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supplementary（补充的）
- auxiliary（辅助的）
- subsidiary（附属的）
- subordinate（从属的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ancillary services / ancillary role / ancillary staff / ancillary costs / ancillary to**

👉 Example: The hospital relies heavily on ancillary staff to ensure that daily operations run smoothly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The report identified several **ancillary** costs that had not been included in the original project budget proposal.
   报告指出了几项未包含在原始项目预算提案中的附属费用。

2. While her primary role was research, she also undertook various **ancillary** duties such as mentoring junior colleagues.
   虽然她的主要职责是研究，但她还承担了各种辅助工作，例如指导初级同事。

3. The company expanded its **ancillary** services to include technical support, training, and comprehensive after-sales consultation.
   公司扩展了其辅助服务，涵盖技术支持、培训和全面的售后咨询。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ancillary" as related to "ancilla" (Latin for maidservant) — a helper who supports the main person in charge.
把"ancillary"联想到拉丁语"ancilla"（女仆）——一个辅助负责人的帮手。
