# 🌊 inundate

🔊 /ˈɪnʌndeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To flood or overwhelm with a great quantity; to submerge or cover completely with water
**中文：** v. 淹没；泛滥；浸水；使应接不暇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flood（淹没）
- overwhelm（使不知所措）
- deluge（使泛滥）
- submerge（淹没）
- swamp（使陷入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inundate with requests / inundate with information / inundated by floodwaters / inundate with emails**

👉 Example: After the announcement, the company was inundated with applications from qualified candidates nationwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The customer service department was **inundated** with complaints after the software update caused widespread system failures.
   软件更新导致大范围系统故障后，客服部门被投诉淹没了。

2. Rising sea levels threaten to **inundate** low-lying coastal communities, displacing millions of residents within the coming decades.
   海平面上升威胁着要淹没低洼沿海社区，在未来几十年内将迫使数百万居民流离失所。

3. The professor was **inundated** with research proposals and struggled to review each submission with the thoroughness it deserved.
   教授被大量研究提案淹没，难以对每一份提交材料进行应有的仔细审阅。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inundate" as "in-un-date" — imagine water rushing IN and making everything go UNDer, like a flood that covers a DATE on the calendar.
把"inundate"想成"in（进入）-und（下面）-ate"——想象水涌入，把一切都淹没在下面，就像洪水一样。
