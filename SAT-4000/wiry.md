# 💪 wiry

🔊 /ˈwaɪəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Resembling wire in form or texture; lean, tough, and sinewy in build
**中文：** adj. 金属丝般的；瘦长结实的；坚硬的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sinewy（肌肉发达的）
- lean（精瘦的）
- tough（结实的）
- wiry（刚毅的）
- stringy（纤维状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wiry build / wiry frame / wiry hair / wiry strength / wiry physique**

👉 Example: Despite his wiry frame, the marathon runner possessed remarkable endurance and surprising physical strength.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **wiry** mountain guide navigated treacherous terrain with an agility that belied his advanced age and modest stature.
   这位瘦长结实的登山向导以与他的高龄和普通身材不相称的敏捷性穿越了危险的地形。

2. Scientists noted that the specimen's **wiry** fur provided exceptional insulation, enabling the species to survive in harsh arctic conditions.
   科学家们注意到该标本粗硬的毛发提供了卓越的隔热性能，使该物种能够在严酷的北极条件下生存。

3. The veteran athlete maintained a **wiry** physique throughout his career, relying on speed and flexibility rather than brute force.
   这位资深运动员在整个职业生涯中保持着精瘦结实的体格，依靠速度和灵活性而非蛮力。

把"wiry"想成"wire（金属丝）+ y"——就像金属丝那样细却极其坚韧难以折断，wiry的人精瘦但结实。
