# 💍 conjugal

🔊 /ˈkɒndʒʊɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to marriage or the relationship between a married couple; pertaining to the duties and rights of spouses
**中文：** adj. 婚姻的，夫妇间的；结婚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- marital（婚姻的）
- matrimonial（婚姻的）
- nuptial（婚礼的）
- spousal（配偶的）
- connubial（婚姻的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conjugal rights / conjugal visit / conjugal life / conjugal bond / conjugal duties**

👉 Example: The court upheld the prisoner's right to receive conjugal visits from his spouse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legal reform sought to redefine **conjugal** rights and obligations to better reflect the principles of modern egalitarian partnerships.
   这项法律改革旨在重新界定婚姻权利和义务，以更好地体现现代平等伙伴关系的原则。

2. In many jurisdictions, **conjugal** visits are permitted as a means of preserving familial bonds during periods of incarceration.
   在许多司法管辖区，允许夫妻探视作为在监禁期间维系家庭纽带的一种方式。

3. The anthropological study examined how **conjugal** relationships and household structures varied significantly across different cultural traditions.
   这项人类学研究考察了婚姻关系和家庭结构在不同文化传统中的显著差异。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-JUG-al" — a married couple is like two people joined (JUG-gled) together, sharing a conjugal bond.
把"conjugal"想成"con-JUG-al"——"con"意为一起，"jug"联想到"join"（结合），婚姻就是两人结合在一起。
