# ⏱️ instantaneous

🔊 /ˌɪnstənˈteɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring or done in an instant; happening immediately without any perceptible delay
**中文：** adj. 瞬间的；即刻的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immediate（即刻的）
- instant（瞬间的）
- sudden（突然的）
- prompt（迅速的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **instantaneous reaction / instantaneous response / virtually instantaneous / instantaneous speed**

👉 Example: The chemical reaction was nearly instantaneous, producing visible results within a fraction of a second.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The communication satellite enabled **instantaneous** transmission of data across continents, revolutionizing global commerce and diplomacy.
   通信卫星实现了跨大陆的即时数据传输，彻底改变了全球商业和外交。

2. The physicist measured the **instantaneous** velocity of the particle at the precise moment it crossed the electromagnetic field boundary.
   物理学家测量了粒子在穿越电磁场边界那一精确瞬间的瞬时速度。

3. Public outrage was **instantaneous** when the leaked documents revealed the full extent of the corporation's environmental negligence.
   当泄露的文件揭示了该公司环境疏忽的全部程度时，公众的愤怒是即刻的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "instantaneous" as "instant-aneous" — so fast it happens in an instant, with "-aneous" emphasizing the quality of being that quick.
把"instantaneous"想成"instant-aneous"——快到发生在一瞬间，"-aneous"强调了这种快速的特质。
