# ⚖️ rectitude

🔊 /ˈrektɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Moral uprightness; righteousness and honesty in character and conduct
**中文：** n. 公正；诚实；清廉；正直

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- integrity（正直）
- righteousness（正义）
- uprightness（正直）
- probity（廉正）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **moral rectitude / personal rectitude / a person of rectitude / demonstrate rectitude**

👉 Example: The judge was widely admired for her unwavering moral rectitude throughout her distinguished career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee selected a candidate whose **rectitude** and professional integrity were beyond question or reproach.
   委员会选择了一位正直和职业操守无可置疑的候选人。

2. His reputation for **rectitude** made him the ideal choice to lead the independent ethics investigation.
   他以正直著称的声誉使他成为领导独立道德调查的理想人选。

3. The philosopher argued that personal **rectitude** must serve as the foundation for any just and equitable society.
   这位哲学家认为，个人的正直必须作为任何公正和公平社会的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rect-itude" — "rect" relates to "right" and "correct," so rectitude is the quality of being morally right.
把"rectitude"想成"rect(=right，正确) + itude(性质)"——道德上正确的品质，即正直。
