# 🔀 atypical

🔊 /eɪˈtɪpɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not representative of a type, group, or class; deviating from what is usual or expected
**中文：** adj. 非典型的；不合规则的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unusual（不寻常的）
- abnormal（异常的）
- anomalous（反常的）
- irregular（不规则的）
- unconventional（非常规的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **atypical behavior / atypical case / atypical symptoms / atypical for someone**

👉 Example: The warm weather in January was atypical for the region, which normally experiences heavy snowfall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor noted that the patient presented **atypical** symptoms that did not match any known diagnostic pattern.
   医生注意到该患者表现出的非典型症状与任何已知的诊断模式都不匹配。

2. Her calm and measured response to the crisis was **atypical** of someone so young and inexperienced.
   她对危机冷静而有分寸的反应，对于如此年轻且缺乏经验的人来说是非典型的。

3. The researcher discovered an **atypical** molecular structure that challenged previously established theories in organic chemistry.
   研究人员发现了一种非典型的分子结构，对有机化学中先前确立的理论提出了挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
The prefix "a-" means "not," so "atypical" literally means "not typical" — something that does not fit the usual pattern.
前缀"a-"表示"不"，所以"atypical"字面意思就是"不典型的"——不符合通常模式的事物。
