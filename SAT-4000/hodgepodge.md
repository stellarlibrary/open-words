# 🎨 hodgepodge

🔊 /ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A confused or disorderly mixture of various things; a jumble or medley
**中文：** n. 大杂烩；混合物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mishmash（大杂烩）
- medley（混合）
- jumble（杂乱的一堆）
- potpourri（混合物）
- assortment（各种各样）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a hodgepodge of styles / a hodgepodge of ideas / a hodgepodge of influences**

👉 Example: The exhibit was a hodgepodge of artistic styles spanning several centuries and cultural traditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proposed legislation was criticized as a **hodgepodge** of unrelated provisions that lacked coherent policy direction.
   这项拟议的立法被批评为缺乏连贯政策方向的、不相关条款的大杂烩。

2. The city's architecture is a fascinating **hodgepodge** of colonial, modernist, and traditional indigenous design elements.
   这座城市的建筑是殖民风格、现代主义和传统原住民设计元素的迷人大杂烩。

3. Her essay read like a **hodgepodge** of borrowed ideas rather than a carefully structured and original academic argument.
   她的文章读起来像是借来的观点的大杂烩，而非精心组织的原创学术论证。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hodgepodge" as "hotch + pot" — a pot where you throw in all sorts of random ingredients, creating a messy mixture.
把"hodgepodge"想成"hotch + pot"（乱炖锅）——一个什么都往里扔的锅，做出来了一锅大杂烩。
