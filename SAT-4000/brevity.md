# ✂️ brevity

🔊 /ˈbrɛvɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Conciseness and exactness of expression; shortness of time or duration
**中文：** n. 简洁，简短；短暂，短促

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conciseness（简洁）
- succinctness（简明）
- terseness（简短）
- shortness（短暂）
- briefness（简洁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brevity of expression / for the sake of brevity / brevity is the soul of wit / with brevity**

👉 Example: The editor praised the brevity of the report, noting that every sentence served a clear purpose.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor valued **brevity** in academic writing and encouraged students to express complex ideas in as few words as possible.
   这位教授重视学术写作中的简洁，鼓励学生用尽可能少的词语表达复杂的思想。

2. Given the **brevity** of the meeting, the committee was unable to address all the critical items on the agenda.
   鉴于会议时间短暂，委员会未能处理议程上所有的关键事项。

3. Shakespeare's famous observation that **brevity** is the soul of wit reminds writers to favor precision over unnecessary elaboration.
   莎士比亚关于简洁是智慧之灵魂的著名论断提醒作家们要重视精确而非多余的赘述。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brevity" sharing the root with "brief" — brevity means keeping things brief, short, and to the point.
把"brevity"和"brief"联系起来——它们有相同的词根，brevity就是保持简短、扼要、切中要点。
