# 📦 object

🔊 /ˈɑːbdʒɪkt/ (n.) /əbˈdʒɛkt/ (v.)<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A material thing that can be seen and touched; a goal or purpose; a grammatical complement. (v.) To express disapproval or opposition.
**中文：** n. 物体；目标；客体；宾语 v. 反对；提出异议

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- item（物品）
- thing（东西）
- article（物件）
- protest（反对）
- oppose（抵制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **object to something / foreign object / object of study / no object (money is no object)**

👉 Example: Several members of the committee object to the proposed changes in the budget allocation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists unearthed a mysterious **object** that appeared to date back to the early Bronze Age civilization.
   考古学家出土了一件神秘的物体，看起来可以追溯到早期青铜时代文明。

2. Citizens have every right to **object** to legislation that infringes upon their fundamental constitutional freedoms.
   公民完全有权反对侵犯其基本宪法自由的立法。

3. The primary **object** of the research initiative was to develop sustainable energy solutions for rural communities.
   该研究计划的主要目标是为农村社区开发可持续能源解决方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-JECT" — "ject" means "throw" in Latin — an object is something thrown before you, and to object is to throw your opinion against something.
想想"ob-JECT"——"ject"在拉丁语中意为"扔"——object作名词是扔在你面前的东西，作动词是把反对意见扔出去。
