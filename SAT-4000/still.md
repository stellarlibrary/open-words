# 🤫 still

🔊 /stɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adverb, adjective, noun, verb | 副词, 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) Yet; even more; motionlessly. (adj.) Motionless; quiet; calm. (n.) Silence; a photograph; a distillery. (v.) To distill; to make motionless
**中文：** n. 蒸馏釜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- motionless（静止的）
- calm（平静的）
- quiet（安静的）
- stationary（不动的）
- serene（宁静的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **still water / still life / stand still / still air / distillation still**

👉 Example: The painter preferred to work in the still hours of early morning before the household awoke.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The lake was perfectly **still** at dawn, its surface reflecting the surrounding mountains like an enormous polished mirror.
   黎明时分湖水完全静止，湖面如同一面巨大的抛光镜子般映照着周围的群山。

2. The artisan whiskey was produced in a traditional copper **still** that had been in continuous operation for over a century.
   这种手工威士忌是在一座持续运作了一个多世纪的传统铜制蒸馏釜中酿造的。

3. Despite mounting evidence against the initial hypothesis, the lead researcher **still** maintained her original position with conviction.
   尽管越来越多的证据反对最初的假设，首席研究员仍然坚定地维持她的原始立场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "still" as something that makes things settle — still water does not move, and a still (distillery) lets liquids settle and purify.
把"still"想成让事物沉淀的东西——静止的水不动，蒸馏釜让液体沉淀并纯化。
