# 🌾 ample

🔊 /ˈæmpəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Enough or more than enough; plentiful; large and spacious.
**中文：** 丰富的；足够的；宽敞的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- plentiful（充足的）
- sufficient（足够的）
- copious（大量的）
- spacious（宽敞的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ample time, ample space, ample evidence, ample opportunity, ample supply**
👉 Example: The library provides ample resources for students conducting advanced research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor allowed **ample** time for students to complete the examination without unnecessary pressure or haste.
   教授留出了充足的时间让学生在没有不必要的压力或匆忙的情况下完成考试。

2. There is **ample** evidence to suggest that regular exercise significantly reduces the risk of cardiovascular disease.
   有充分的证据表明，定期锻炼可以显著降低心血管疾病的风险。

3. The estate featured **ample** grounds with manicured gardens extending well beyond the principal residence and outbuildings.
   这座庄园拥有宽敞的场地，修剪整齐的花园远远延伸到主宅和附属建筑之外。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ample" sounds like "a maple" tree — imagine a large maple tree providing ample shade for everyone beneath it.
"Ample"听起来像"a maple"（一棵枫树）——想象一棵大枫树为树下的每个人提供充足的树荫。
