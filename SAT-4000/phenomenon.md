# 🌟 phenomenon

🔊 /fɪˈnɒmɪnən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A fact, occurrence, or circumstance observed or observable; something remarkable or extraordinary; a person of outstanding talent
**中文：** n. 现象；奇迹；杰出的人才

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- occurrence（事件）
- marvel（奇迹）
- spectacle（奇观）
- prodigy（天才）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natural phenomenon / cultural phenomenon / a phenomenon in (field)**

👉 Example: The aurora borealis is a breathtaking natural phenomenon visible in polar regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rapid spread of social media is a cultural **phenomenon** that has fundamentally transformed how people communicate worldwide.
   社交媒体的迅速传播是一种文化现象，从根本上改变了全世界人们的交流方式。

2. Scientists spent decades studying the mysterious **phenomenon** of bioluminescence in deep-sea marine organisms.
   科学家们花了数十年时间研究深海海洋生物中神秘的生物发光现象。

3. The young pianist was considered a true **phenomenon**, captivating audiences with her extraordinary technical skill and artistry.
   这位年轻的钢琴家被认为是真正的天才，以其非凡的技巧和艺术感征服了观众。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pheno-men-on" as "phenomenal men are on" — phenomenal people are remarkable phenomena in their fields.
把"phenomenon"想成"phenomenal men are on"——杰出的人在他们的领域中就是引人注目的现象。
