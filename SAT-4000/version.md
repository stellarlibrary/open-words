# 📄 version

🔊 /ˈvɜːrʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A particular form or variant of something; an account of an event from a specific perspective; a translation of a text
**中文：** n. 版本；说法，看法；译文

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- variant（变体）
- edition（版本）
- rendition（翻译）
- account（说法）
- interpretation（诠释）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **version of events / latest version / original version / revised version**

👉 Example: The updated version of the software includes several critical security patches.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The witness provided a dramatically different **version** of events than what the police report had originally documented.
   证人提供的事件**版本**与警方报告最初记录的内容截然不同。

2. Scholars have long debated which **version** of the ancient manuscript most accurately reflects the author's original intentions.
   学者们长期以来一直在争论哪个**版本**的古代手稿最准确地反映了作者的原意。

3. The publisher released a revised **version** of the textbook to incorporate the latest developments in molecular biology.
   出版社发布了教科书的修订**版本**，以纳入分子生物学的最新发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "version" as "ver-sion" — "ver" sounds like "vary," meaning each version varies slightly from others.
把"version"想成"ver-sion"——"ver"听起来像"vary（变化）"，意味着每个版本都与其他版本略有不同。
