# 🌍 population

🔊 /ˌpɑːpjəˈleɪʃn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The total number of people or organisms inhabiting a particular area; a group of individuals of the same species in a given area
**中文：** n. 人口；全体居民；种群；群体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inhabitants（居民）
- residents（住民）
- citizenry（全体市民）
- community（群体）
- demographic（人口群体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **population growth / population density / general population / population decline**

👉 Example: The population of the city has doubled over the past two decades due to rapid industrialization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers estimated that the **population** of endangered mountain gorillas has gradually increased thanks to conservation efforts.
   研究人员估计，由于保护工作的开展，濒危山地大猩猩的种群数量已逐步增加。

2. Rapid **population** growth in urban areas has placed enormous pressure on housing, transportation, and public services.
   城市地区人口的快速增长给住房、交通和公共服务带来了巨大压力。

3. The census revealed that the rural **population** had declined significantly as younger generations migrated to metropolitan centers.
   人口普查显示，随着年轻一代迁往大都市中心，农村人口大幅下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "popul-ation" as "people-ation" — the collective gathering of people in a place, forming the total count of inhabitants.
把"population"想成"people(人们)-ation(集合)"——一个地方人们的聚集，构成居民的总数。
