# 🔄 circular

🔊 /ˈsɜːrkjələr/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the shape of a circle; recurring in a cycle; (of an argument) using the conclusion as a premise. (n.) A printed notice or advertisement distributed to a large number of people
**中文：** adj. 圆形的；环形的；循环的 n. 通知；传单

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- round（圆的）
- cyclical（循环的）
- ring-shaped（环形的）
- flyer（传单）
- bulletin（通告）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **circular motion / circular argument / circular reasoning / circular letter / send out a circular**

👉 Example: The professor warned students to avoid circular reasoning in their thesis arguments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect incorporated a grand **circular** staircase into the building's design, creating an elegant and functional centerpiece.
   建筑师在建筑设计中融入了一座宏伟的圆形楼梯，打造出一个优雅且实用的核心亮点。

2. The company distributed a **circular** to all employees announcing the revised workplace safety protocols effective next month.
   公司向所有员工分发了一份通知，宣布下个月生效的修订版工作场所安全规程。

3. The philosopher identified a **circular** argument in the treatise, noting that the conclusion merely restated the initial premise.
   这位哲学家在论文中发现了一个循环论证，指出结论只是重述了最初的前提。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circular" as "circle" + "-ar" — shaped like a circle, or going around in circles like a circular argument that never reaches a real conclusion.
把"circular"想成"circle"（圆）+"-ar"——形状像圆的，或者像循环论证一样绕来绕去，永远得不出真正的结论。
