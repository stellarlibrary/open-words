# 🧠 circumvent

🔊 /ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To find a way around an obstacle or rule; to overcome or outwit by cleverness or stratagem; to surround or encircle.
**中文：** 智取，用计谋取胜；规避，绕行；包围；挫败。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bypass（绕过）
- evade（规避）
- outwit（智取）
- skirt（绕开）
- sidestep（回避）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **circumvent the law / circumvent regulations / circumvent restrictions / circumvent a problem**

👉 Example: The company devised a strategy to circumvent the new trade restrictions imposed by foreign governments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Hackers continually develop sophisticated methods to **circumvent** cybersecurity protocols and gain unauthorized access to sensitive databases.
   黑客不断开发复杂的方法来规避网络安全协议，非法获取敏感数据库的访问权限。

2. The general devised an ingenious flanking maneuver to **circumvent** the enemy's heavily fortified defensive positions along the river.
   将军设计了一个巧妙的侧翼机动战术，以绕过敌人沿河部署的坚固防御阵地。

3. Critics argued that the proposed amendment would enable corporations to **circumvent** environmental protections enacted by previous administrations.
   批评者认为，拟议的修正案将使企业能够规避前几届政府制定的环境保护法规。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circum-" (around) + "vent" (come) — to come around an obstacle, finding a clever way to get past it.
把"circumvent"拆分为"circum-（周围）"+"vent（来）"——绕过障碍找到巧妙的方法通过。
