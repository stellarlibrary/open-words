# 🏔️ remote

🔊 /rɪˈmoʊt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Far away in distance or time; situated far from the main centers of population; having very little connection or relationship
**中文：** adj. 偏远的，偏僻的；遥远的；关系疏远的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distant（遥远的）
- isolated（偏僻的）
- secluded（隐蔽的）
- far-flung（遥远的）
- aloof（疏远的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remote area / remote possibility / remote connection / remote access / remote from**

👉 Example: The research station was located in a remote region of the Arctic, accessible only by helicopter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologists traveled to a **remote** village in the highlands to document the oral traditions of an indigenous community.
   人类学家前往高地一个偏远的村庄，记录一个土著社区的口头传统。

2. The connection between the two historical events seemed **remote**, yet the scholar demonstrated a compelling causal link between them.
   这两个历史事件之间的联系看似疏远，但学者却证明了它们之间存在令人信服的因果关系。

3. There remains only a **remote** possibility that the endangered species will recover without immediate and sustained conservation intervention.
   如果没有立即和持续的保护干预，这种濒危物种恢复的可能性极为渺茫。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remote" as "re-mote" (like a remote control) — the TV is far away, so you need a device to reach it from a distance.
把"remote"想成遥控器（remote control）——电视在远处，所以你需要一个设备从远距离操控它。
