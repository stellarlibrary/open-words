# 🛡️ neutralize

🔊 /ˈnjuːtrəlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To render ineffective or harmless; to counterbalance or counteract; in chemistry, to make a solution neither acidic nor alkaline
**中文：** v. 抵消；使中和；使无效；使中立

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- counteract（抵消）
- negate（否定）
- nullify（使无效）
- offset（抵消）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **neutralize a threat / neutralize the effect / neutralize an acid / neutralize the opposition**

👉 Example: The antidote was administered to neutralize the venom before it could cause further damage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government deployed additional security forces to **neutralize** the threat posed by the insurgent groups near the border.
   政府部署了额外的安全部队来消除边境附近叛乱组织构成的威胁。

2. Adding a base to the solution will effectively **neutralize** the excess acid and restore a balanced pH level.
   向溶液中加入碱可以有效中和多余的酸，恢复平衡的pH值。

3. The diplomat's skillful negotiations helped **neutralize** tensions between the two rival nations before hostilities could escalate.
   外交官娴熟的谈判帮助化解了两个敌对国家之间的紧张局势，防止了冲突升级。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "neutral" + "-ize" — to make something neutral, like a referee who neutralizes conflict between opposing teams.
把"neutralize"想成"neutral（中立的）"加"-ize（使…）"——就像裁判化解对立队伍之间的冲突。
