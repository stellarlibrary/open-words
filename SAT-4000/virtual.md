# 🖥️ virtual

🔊 /ˈvɜːrtʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Almost or nearly as described, but not completely; created or existing by means of computer technology rather than physical form
**中文：** 虚拟的；实际上的；事实上的（但未在名义上或正式获承认）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- simulated（模拟的）
- digital（数字的）
- effective（实际的）
- practical（事实上的）
- near（近乎的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **virtual reality / virtual meeting / virtual monopoly / virtual certainty / virtual world**

👉 Example: Remote work has made virtual meetings an essential component of modern professional communication.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company achieved a **virtual** monopoly in the semiconductor market after acquiring its three largest competitors within two years.
   该公司在两年内收购了三个最大的竞争对手后，在半导体市场上实现了事实上的垄断。

2. Advances in **virtual** reality technology have transformed medical training by allowing surgeons to practice complex procedures safely.
   虚拟现实技术的进步通过让外科医生安全地练习复杂手术，改变了医学培训方式。

3. The prolonged drought brought **virtual** devastation to the agricultural sector, leaving countless farmers without viable harvests.
   长期干旱给农业部门带来了几近毁灭性的打击，使无数农民颗粒无收。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "virtual" as "virtue-al" — it has the virtue or essence of something real, but is not quite the real thing itself.
把"virtual"想成"virtue-al"——它拥有真实事物的本质或精髓，但又不完全是真实的东西本身。
