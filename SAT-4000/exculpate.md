# ⚖️ exculpate

🔊 /ˈɛkskʌlpeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To clear from alleged fault or guilt; to prove or declare innocent of wrongdoing
**中文：** 开脱；申明无罪；证明无辜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absolve（免除）
- exonerate（证明无罪）
- vindicate（证明清白）
- acquit（宣判无罪）
- pardon（赦免）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exculpate someone from / exculpate the defendant / exculpating evidence / fully exculpated**

👉 Example: The newly discovered evidence helped exculpate the defendant who had been wrongly accused for years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The DNA analysis conclusively **exculpated** the suspect, proving he could not have been present at the scene of the crime.
   DNA分析确证该嫌疑人无罪，证明他不可能出现在犯罪现场。

2. Defense attorneys presented compelling testimony that served to **exculpate** their client from all charges of financial misconduct.
   辩护律师提出了有力的证词，为其当事人洗脱了所有金融不当行为的指控。

3. The internal investigation **exculpated** the manager, revealing that the accounting errors were caused by a systematic software malfunction.
   内部调查为这位经理洗脱了嫌疑，揭示出会计错误是由系统性软件故障造成的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-culp-ate" — "ex" means out, "culp" comes from "culpa" (fault/blame), so it literally means to take the fault out, to free from blame.
把"exculpate"想成"ex-culp-ate"——"ex"意为出去，"culp"来自"culpa"（过错），字面意思就是把过错去除，免除罪责。
