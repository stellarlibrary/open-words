# 🆕 latest

🔊 /ˈleɪtɪst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb, noun | 形容词, 副词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Most recent or newest in time. (adv.) At the most recent time. (n.) The most recent news or development
**中文：** adj. 最新的，最近的 adv. 最迟地 n. 最新的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- newest（最新的）
- most recent（最近的）
- current（当前的）
- up-to-date（最新的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the latest news / the latest version / at the latest / latest findings / latest developments**

👉 Example: Please submit your application by Friday at the latest to be considered for the position.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **latest** research findings challenge several long-held assumptions about the relationship between diet and cardiovascular disease.
   最新的研究发现挑战了关于饮食与心血管疾病之间关系的几个长期假设。

2. The company announced that the **latest** version of its software includes significant improvements in both security and performance.
   该公司宣布其软件的最新版本在安全性和性能方面都有显著改进。

3. Delegates were briefed on the **latest** developments in international trade negotiations before the summit officially commenced.
   在峰会正式开始之前，代表们听取了关于国际贸易谈判最新进展的简报。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "latest" as the superlative of "late" — the "latest" thing is the one that came last in time, making it the most recent and newest.
把"latest"想成"late（晚的）"的最高级——"latest"的事物是时间上最后到来的，因此是最新最近的。
