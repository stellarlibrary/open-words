# 📡 transmit

🔊 /trænzˈmɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To send or convey something from one person, place, or thing to another; to broadcast signals; to pass on a disease or trait
**中文：** v. 传输；传播；发射；传送

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- convey（传达）
- broadcast（广播）
- relay（转播）
- disseminate（传播）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transmit data / transmit a signal / transmit disease / transmit information**

👉 Example: The satellite was designed to transmit high-resolution images back to the research station.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient Romans built an extensive network of roads to **transmit** goods and military intelligence across the empire.
   古罗马人修建了庞大的道路网络，以便在帝国各地传送货物和军事情报。

2. Fiber-optic cables can **transmit** vast quantities of data at speeds approaching the speed of light.
   光纤电缆能以接近光速的速度传输大量数据。

3. Mosquitoes are well known to **transmit** dangerous pathogens that cause diseases such as malaria and dengue fever.
   众所周知，蚊子会传播导致疟疾和登革热等疾病的危险病原体。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-mit" as "send (mit) across (trans)" — like a radio tower sending signals across great distances.
把"transmit"想成"trans（跨越）+ mit（发送）"——就像无线电塔将信号发送到远方。
