# 😴 lethargy

🔊 /ˈlɛθərdʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A state of sluggishness, inactivity, or apathy; abnormal drowsiness or lack of energy
**中文：** n. 昏睡；死气沉沉；嗜眠；无生气；懒洋洋

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- torpor（麻木）
- sluggishness（迟钝）
- languor（倦怠）
- inertia（惰性）
- apathy（冷漠）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overcome lethargy / sink into lethargy / a state of lethargy / mental lethargy**

👉 Example: The oppressive summer heat induced a profound lethargy among the workers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient's persistent **lethargy** prompted the physician to order a comprehensive series of blood tests.
   患者持续的昏睡促使医生下令进行一系列全面的血液检查。

2. A widespread **lethargy** gripped the organization, preventing any meaningful reform from being implemented effectively.
   一种普遍的死气沉沉笼罩了整个组织，使任何有意义的改革都无法有效实施。

3. Scholars attributed the empire's decline to a cultural **lethargy** that discouraged innovation and intellectual pursuit.
   学者们将帝国的衰落归因于一种抑制创新和学术追求的文化惰性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lethargy" as coming from "Lethe" (river of forgetfulness) + "argy" — so drowsy you forget to do anything.
把"lethargy"想成来自"Lethe（忘川）"+"argy"——困倦到忘记做任何事情。
