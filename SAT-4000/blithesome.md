# 🌸 blithesome

🔊 /ˈblaɪðsəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Cheerful, merry, and lighthearted in disposition; having or showing a happy, carefree nature.
**中文：** adj. 愉快的，高兴的；快乐的，无忧无虑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cheerful（愉快的）
- merry（欢乐的）
- buoyant（乐观的）
- joyous（欣喜的）
- gleeful（欢欣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blithesome manner / blithesome disposition / blithesome laughter / blithesome melody**

👉 Example: Her blithesome manner at the gathering lifted the spirits of everyone in attendance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The children's **blithesome** laughter echoed through the courtyard, bringing warmth to the otherwise solemn afternoon ceremony.
   孩子们**欢快的**笑声回荡在庭院中，为原本庄严的下午仪式带来了温暖。

2. Despite the mounting pressures of the academic term, she maintained a remarkably **blithesome** outlook on her studies.
   尽管学期压力不断增加，她对学业依然保持着异常**愉快的**态度。

3. The composer's **blithesome** melody captured the essence of spring, evoking images of renewal and unbounded natural vitality.
   作曲家**欢快的**旋律捕捉到了春天的精髓，唤起了万物更新和无限自然活力的意象。

把"blithesome"想成"blithe + some"——一个拥有额外愉快特质的人，洋溢着快乐和欢欣。
