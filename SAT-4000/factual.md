# 📋 factual

🔊 /ˈfæktʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Based on or concerned with facts; true and accurate rather than invented or imagined
**中文：** 事实的；真实的；确实的；实际的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accurate（准确的）
- objective（客观的）
- verifiable（可验证的）
- authentic（真实的）
- empirical（实证的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **factual information / factual accuracy / factual basis / factual account / factual error**

👉 Example: The journalist was committed to providing only factual reporting on the controversial incident.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court required all witnesses to provide strictly **factual** testimony, free from personal opinion or speculative interpretation of events.
   法庭要求所有证人提供严格基于事实的证词，不得掺杂个人意见或对事件的推测性解读。

2. A thorough **factual** analysis of the economic data revealed significant flaws in the administration's optimistic growth projections.
   对经济数据的全面事实分析揭示了政府乐观增长预测中的重大缺陷。

3. The editorial board emphasized that **factual** reporting must remain the cornerstone of responsible journalism in an era of misinformation.
   编辑委员会强调，在虚假信息泛滥的时代，基于事实的报道必须始终是负责任新闻业的基石。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fact-ual" as "full of facts" — something factual is grounded in facts, not fiction or opinion.
把"factual"想成"充满事实的"——factual的东西是建立在事实基础上的，而非虚构或观点。
