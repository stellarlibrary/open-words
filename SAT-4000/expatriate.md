# 🌍 expatriate

🔊 /ˌeksˈpeɪtriˌeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To send into exile; to cause someone to leave their native country. (n.) A person who lives outside their native country
**中文：** v. 逐出国外；使移居国外 n. 被流放者；侨民

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exile（流放）
- emigrant（移民）
- banish（放逐）
- deport（驱逐出境）
- displaced person（流离失所者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **expatriate community / expatriate workers / expatriate oneself / self-expatriate**

👉 Example: A thriving expatriate community in the capital offers newcomers a sense of belonging and cultural familiarity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many talented professionals choose to **expatriate** themselves in search of better career opportunities and improved quality of life.
   许多有才华的专业人士选择移居国外，寻找更好的职业机会和更高的生活质量。

2. The growing **expatriate** community in the city has significantly influenced the local culture, cuisine, and economic landscape.
   该城市日益壮大的侨民社区已对当地文化、美食和经济格局产生了显著影响。

3. During the political upheaval, the government threatened to **expatriate** dissidents who openly challenged the authority of the regime.
   在政治动荡期间，政府威胁要将公开挑战政权权威的异见人士逐出国外。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-patri-ate" — "ex" means out, "patri" relates to homeland (like patriot); an expatriate is someone sent out of their homeland.
把"expatriate"想成"ex-patri-ate"——"ex"意为外，"patri"与祖国有关（如patriot爱国者）；expatriate就是被送出祖国的人。
