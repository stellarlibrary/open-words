# 🏢 vocation

🔊 /voʊˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A strong feeling of suitability for a particular career or occupation; a person's employment or main calling
**中文：** n. 职业，业务；天职；天命；神召

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- calling（天职）
- profession（职业）
- occupation（职业）
- career（事业）
- métier（专长）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **find one's vocation / sense of vocation / vocation in life / vocational training / true vocation**

👉 Example: After years of searching, she finally discovered that teaching was her true vocation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many young graduates struggle to distinguish between a temporary job and a lifelong **vocation** that aligns with their deepest values.
   许多年轻毕业生难以区分临时工作和与其最深层价值观一致的终身职业。

2. The monastery offered spiritual retreats designed to help individuals discern whether religious life was their authentic **vocation**.
   修道院提供灵性静修，旨在帮助个人辨别宗教生活是否是他们真正的天职。

3. The counselor emphasized that choosing a **vocation** requires careful reflection on one's talents, interests, and long-term aspirations.
   咨询师强调，选择职业需要认真反思个人的才能、兴趣和长远抱负。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vocation" as coming from Latin "vocare" (to call) — your vocation is what you feel called to do, your life's true calling.
把"vocation"想成来自拉丁语"vocare"（呼唤）——你的天职就是你感到被召唤去做的事，你人生的真正使命。
