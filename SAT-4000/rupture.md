# 💥 rupture

🔊 /ˈrʌptʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A break or burst; a breach of relations; a hernia. (v.) To break or burst open; to develop a hernia
**中文：** v. 破裂，裂开；断绝

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fracture（断裂）
- breach（破裂）
- split（裂开）
- severance（断绝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rupture a blood vessel / rupture relations / rupture in the pipeline / rupture of trust**

👉 Example: The earthquake caused a major rupture in the underground water main, flooding several streets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged diplomatic crisis eventually led to a complete **rupture** of relations between the two neighboring countries.
   旷日持久的外交危机最终导致两个邻国之间的关系彻底破裂。

2. Excessive internal pressure can **rupture** the walls of aging industrial pipelines, causing hazardous chemical spills.
   过大的内部压力可能使老化的工业管道壁破裂，导致危险的化学品泄漏。

3. The sudden **rupture** of the dam released millions of gallons of water into the unsuspecting valley below.
   大坝的突然破裂将数百万加仑的水释放到下方毫无防备的山谷中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rupt-ure" — the root "rupt" means "break" (as in interrupt, erupt), so a rupture is something that breaks apart.
把"rupture"拆解为"rupt（破）+ure"——词根"rupt"表示"破裂"（如interrupt中断、erupt爆发），所以rupture就是破裂。
