# 📡 sensor

🔊 /ˈsensər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A device that detects or measures a physical property and records, indicates, or otherwise responds to it
**中文：** n. 传感器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detector（探测器）
- transducer（换能器）
- probe（探头）
- monitor（监测器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **motion sensor / temperature sensor / sensor data / sensor array / sensor technology**

👉 Example: The motion sensor automatically activated the security lights when it detected movement near the entrance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advanced **sensor** technology allows modern vehicles to detect obstacles and adjust speed accordingly for passenger safety.
   先进的传感器技术使现代车辆能够检测障碍物并相应调整速度以保障乘客安全。

2. The environmental research team deployed underwater **sensors** to monitor changes in ocean temperature and salinity levels.
   环境研究团队部署了水下传感器来监测海洋温度和盐度水平的变化。

3. A faulty **sensor** in the manufacturing plant triggered a false alarm that temporarily halted all production operations.
   制造工厂中一个有故障的传感器触发了错误警报，暂时中断了所有生产操作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sensor" as "sense-or" — it is a device that senses things for you, acting as an artificial organ of perception.
把"sensor"想成"sense-or"——它是一个替你感知事物的设备，充当一个人工感知器官。
