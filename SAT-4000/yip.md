# 🐕 yip

🔊 /jɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A short, sharp cry or bark. (v.) To bark shrilly or make a short, sharp cry
**中文：** n. 叫喊声；犬吠声 v. 犬吠；尖叫

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- yelp（尖叫）
- bark（吠叫）
- yap（狂吠）
- squeal（尖声叫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **yip with excitement / yip at someone / let out a yip / a sharp yip**

👉 Example: The small terrier began to yip excitedly the moment its owner reached for the leash.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coyotes began to **yip** across the darkened valley, their sharp cries echoing through the silent desert landscape.
   郊狼开始在昏暗的山谷中尖叫，它们尖锐的叫声在寂静的沙漠中回荡。

2. She let out an involuntary **yip** of surprise when the cold water from the garden hose splashed across her feet.
   当花园水管的冷水溅到她脚上时，她不由自主地发出一声惊叫。

3. The puppy would **yip** persistently at the back door, signaling its eager desire to play outside in the yard.
   小狗会在后门持续不断地吠叫，表达它急切地想去院子里玩耍的愿望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "yip" as a tiny, quick sound — it sounds exactly like the short, sharp bark a small dog makes.
把"yip"想成一个短促的声音——它听起来就像小狗发出的又短又尖的叫声。
