# 📀 album

🔊 /ˈælbəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A collection of recordings, photographs, or stamps kept together in a bound volume or similar format
**中文：** 相簿；唱片集；集邮簿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- collection（合集）
- compilation（汇编）
- anthology（选集）
- portfolio（作品集）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **photo album / music album / release an album / debut album**
👉 Example: The musician spent two years in the studio recording her highly anticipated third album.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned artist released a critically acclaimed **album** that explored themes of identity and cultural heritage.
   这位著名艺术家发行了一张广受好评的专辑，探讨了身份认同和文化遗产的主题。

2. She carefully arranged the family photographs in a leather-bound **album** to preserve decades of cherished memories.
   她小心翼翼地将家庭照片整理到一本皮面相簿中，以保存数十年的珍贵记忆。

3. The collector displayed his rare stamp **album** at the exhibition, attracting considerable admiration from fellow enthusiasts.
   收藏家在展览上展示了他珍贵的集邮簿，引起了同好们的极大赞赏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Album" comes from Latin "albus" (white) — originally a blank white tablet for records, now any collection kept together.
"Album"源自拉丁语"albus"（白色）——最初指用于记录的空白白板，现在指任何集合在一起的收藏。
