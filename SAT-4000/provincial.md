# 🏘️ provincial

🔊 /prəˈvɪnʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to a province; lacking sophistication or breadth of mind; narrow in outlook. (n.) A person from the provinces; an unsophisticated person
**中文：** adj. 省的；地方性的；偏狭的 n. 乡下人；粗野的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- parochial（偏狭的）
- narrow-minded（心胸狭窄的）
- insular（孤立的）
- rustic（乡村的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **provincial government / provincial attitude / provincial capital / provincial outlook**

👉 Example: His provincial attitude prevented him from appreciating the rich diversity of the cosmopolitan city.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **provincial** government allocated additional funds to improve rural infrastructure and expand access to quality healthcare services.
   省政府拨出额外资金以改善农村基础设施并扩大优质医疗服务的覆盖范围。

2. Critics dismissed his views as **provincial**, arguing that his narrow perspective failed to account for broader global economic trends.
   批评者斥其观点为偏狭，认为他狭隘的视角未能考虑到更广泛的全球经济趋势。

3. Moving from her **provincial** hometown to the capital exposed her to new ideas that fundamentally transformed her worldview.
   从偏远的家乡搬到首都使她接触到了从根本上改变其世界观的新思想。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "provincial" as "from the province" — someone who has never left their small province may have a limited, narrow view of the world.
把"provincial"想成"来自省份的"——一个从未离开过小省份的人可能对世界的看法有限而狭隘。
