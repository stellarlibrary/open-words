# 🔻 degenerate

🔊 /dɪˈdʒenəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective, noun | 动词, 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To decline or deteriorate physically, mentally, or morally. (adj.) Having declined from a former state; degraded. (n.) A morally corrupt person
**中文：** v. 使退化；恶化；衰退；堕落 adj. 退化的；堕落的 n. 堕落的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deteriorate（恶化）
- decline（衰退）
- degrade（降低）
- corrupt（堕落的）
- debased（卑劣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **degenerate into / degenerate behavior / degenerate condition / moral degenerate**

👉 Example: The peaceful protest quickly degenerated into a violent confrontation between opposing factions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Without proper maintenance, the historic building's structural integrity will **degenerate** rapidly over the coming decades.
   如果没有适当的维护，这座历史建筑的结构完整性将在未来几十年内迅速退化。

2. The scholarly debate **degenerated** into personal attacks, undermining the credibility of all participants involved in the discussion.
   这场学术辩论演变为人身攻击，损害了所有参与讨论者的公信力。

3. Critics argued that the once-vibrant cultural movement had become **degenerate** and devoid of its original artistic purpose.
   批评者认为这场曾经充满活力的文化运动已经堕落，失去了其最初的艺术目的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-generate" as "de- (down) + generate" — the opposite of generating something good; things go downward and worsen.
把"degenerate"想成"de-（向下）+ generate（产生）"——与产生好的东西相反，事物向下走、恶化。
