# ⚖️ integrity

🔊 /ɪnˈteɡrɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; the state of being whole, complete, and undivided
**中文：** n. 完整；正直；诚实；廉正

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- honesty（诚实）
- uprightness（正直）
- probity（廉正）
- wholeness（完整性）
- rectitude（正直）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **personal integrity / structural integrity / integrity of data / act with integrity / maintain integrity**

👉 Example: A leader who acts with integrity earns the lasting trust and respect of colleagues and subordinates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator was widely admired for her unwavering **integrity** and refusal to accept donations from questionable political sources.
   这位参议员因其坚定不移的正直品格和拒绝接受可疑政治来源的捐款而广受赞誉。

2. Engineers conducted thorough inspections to verify the structural **integrity** of the bridge before reopening it to public traffic.
   工程师进行了全面检查，以验证桥梁的结构完整性，然后才重新向公众开放通行。

3. The cybersecurity team implemented advanced encryption protocols to preserve the **integrity** of sensitive financial data stored online.
   网络安全团队实施了先进的加密协议，以保护在线存储的敏感财务数据的完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "integrity" as sharing the root with "integer" (whole number) — both come from Latin "integer" meaning "whole." A person of integrity is morally whole and undivided.
把"integrity"想成与"integer（整数）"同根——都来自拉丁语"integer"，意为"完整的"。一个有integrity的人在道德上是完整的、表里如一的。
