# 🗣️ utter

🔊 /ˈʌtər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To speak or say something aloud; to make a sound with one's voice. (adj.) Complete; absolute; total
**中文：** v. 发出声音；说出 adj. 完全的；彻底的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pronounce（说出）
- express（表达）
- absolute（绝对的）
- complete（完全的）
- total（彻底的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **utter a word / utter nonsense / utter silence / utter failure / utter disbelief**

👉 Example: The witness refused to utter a single word during the entire cross-examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant did not **utter** a single word in his own defense throughout the lengthy and contentious trial proceedings.
   被告在漫长而有争议的审判过程中没有为自己说过一个字。

2. The experiment was an **utter** failure, yielding no usable data despite months of meticulous preparation and substantial funding.
   这项实验彻底失败了，尽管经过数月的精心准备和大量资金投入，仍未产生任何可用数据。

3. Scholars were reluctant to **utter** criticisms of the prevailing theory for fear of professional ostracism from their peers.
   学者们不愿发表对主流理论的批评，因为害怕遭到同行的排斥。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "utter" as the sound "uh-ter" — when you open your mouth to say "uh," you utter a sound. For the adjective, think "utterly complete."
把"utter"想成发出"uh-ter"的声音——当你张嘴说"uh"时，你就发出了声音。对于形容词，想想"utterly complete（完完全全）"。
