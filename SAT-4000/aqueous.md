# 💧 aqueous

🔊 /ˈeɪkwiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or resembling water; dissolved in or made with water
**中文：** adj. 水的；水般的；含水的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- watery（含水的）
- liquid（液态的）
- hydrous（含水的）
- fluid（流动的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aqueous solution / aqueous humor / aqueous layer / aqueous environment**

👉 Example: The chemist prepared an aqueous solution of sodium chloride for the experiment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician carefully measured the concentration of the **aqueous** solution before proceeding with the analysis.
   实验室技术员在进行分析之前仔细测量了水溶液的浓度。

2. The **aqueous** humor inside the eye provides essential nutrients and maintains proper intraocular pressure for vision.
   眼睛内的水状液提供必要的营养物质，并维持适当的眼内压以保障视力。

3. Minerals dissolve more readily in an **aqueous** environment where temperature and acidity levels are sufficiently elevated.
   矿物质在温度和酸度足够高的水性环境中更容易溶解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "aqua" (water) + "-eous" (having the quality of) — aqueous means having the quality of water.
想到"aqua"（水）+"-eous"（具有……性质的）——aqueous就是具有水的性质的。
