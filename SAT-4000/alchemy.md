# ✨ alchemy

🔊 /ˈælkəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A medieval chemical philosophy aimed at transforming base metals into gold; a seemingly magical process of transformation
**中文：** 点金术；炼金术；魔力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sorcery（魔法）
- magic（魔力）
- transmutation（嬗变）
- wizardry（巫术）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the alchemy of / political alchemy / a kind of alchemy / emotional alchemy**
👉 Example: The chef's alchemy transformed simple ingredients into an extraordinary culinary masterpiece.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Through a remarkable form of **alchemy**, the director transformed an obscure novel into one of the year's finest films.
   通过一种非凡的魔力，导演将一部默默无闻的小说变成了年度最佳影片之一。

2. Medieval practitioners of **alchemy** devoted their lives to the elusive quest of turning lead into pure gold.
   中世纪的炼金术士毕生致力于将铅变为纯金这一难以实现的追求。

3. The **alchemy** of her leadership style lay in combining strict discipline with genuine compassion for every team member.
   她领导风格的魔力在于将严格纪律与对每位团队成员的真诚关怀相结合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "al-chemy" — "al" is Arabic for "the" and "chemy" relates to chemistry; alchemy is the magical ancestor of chemistry.
想想"al-chemy"——"al"是阿拉伯语中的"the"，"chemy"与化学有关；炼金术是化学的神奇前身。
