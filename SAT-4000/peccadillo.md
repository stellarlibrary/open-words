# 😅 peccadillo

🔊 /ˌpekəˈdɪloʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A minor sin or fault; a small, relatively unimportant offense or shortcoming
**中文：** n. 轻罪；小过失；小瑕疵

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- misdemeanor（轻罪）
- transgression（过失）
- indiscretion（不检点）
- foible（小缺点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **minor peccadillo / youthful peccadillo / forgive a peccadillo / dismiss as a peccadillo**

👉 Example: The committee chose to overlook the senator's minor peccadillo and focus on more pressing legislative matters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biographer treated the composer's youthful **peccadilloes** as trivial matters unworthy of overshadowing his artistic achievements.
   传记作者将这位作曲家年轻时的小过失视为无关紧要的事情，不值得掩盖他的艺术成就。

2. In an era of heightened media scrutiny, even the most minor **peccadillo** can derail a promising political career overnight.
   在媒体监督日益加强的时代，即使是最微小的过失也能在一夜之间毁掉一个有前途的政治生涯。

3. The professor argued that society should distinguish between genuine moral failings and harmless **peccadilloes** of everyday life.
   这位教授认为，社会应该区分真正的道德过错和日常生活中无害的小瑕疵。
