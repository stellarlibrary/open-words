# ⏳ lifetime

🔊 /ˈlaɪfˌtaɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The duration of a person's life; the period during which something exists or functions. (adj.) Lasting for the entirety of one's life.
**中文：** n. 一生；寿命；终生 adj. 一生的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lifespan（寿命）
- existence（存在期）
- duration（持续时间）
- life expectancy（预期寿命）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in a lifetime / once in a lifetime / lifetime achievement / lifetime opportunity**

👉 Example: Receiving the Nobel Prize was a once-in-a-lifetime honor for the distinguished researcher.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished professor dedicated an entire **lifetime** to advancing our understanding of molecular biology and genetics.
   这位杰出的教授毕生致力于推动我们对分子生物学和遗传学的认识。

2. A **lifetime** of disciplined research enabled the scientist to make groundbreaking contributions to the field of astrophysics.
   一生严谨的研究使这位科学家在天体物理学领域做出了开创性的贡献。

3. The foundation established a **lifetime** achievement award to recognize individuals who have profoundly influenced public education.
   该基金会设立了终身成就奖，以表彰对公共教育产生深远影响的个人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "life + time" — the total time of your life, from beginning to end.
把"lifetime"拆分为"life（生命）+ time（时间）"——从头到尾，你生命中的全部时间。
