# 📚 theory

🔊 /ˈθɪəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A systematic set of principles or ideas intended to explain something; a supposition or conjecture; a branch of knowledge or study.
**中文：** n. 理论；原理；学说

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hypothesis（假说）
- doctrine（学说）
- concept（概念）
- framework（框架）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in theory / theory of evolution / put forward a theory / theory and practice**

👉 Example: In theory, the plan should work perfectly, but practical challenges may arise during implementation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Einstein's **theory** of relativity fundamentally transformed our understanding of space, time, gravity, and the structure of the universe.
   爱因斯坦的相对论从根本上改变了我们对空间、时间、引力和宇宙结构的理解。

2. The professor encouraged students to question established **theory** rather than accept conventional scientific explanations without critical scrutiny.
   教授鼓励学生质疑已有理论，而不是不加批判地接受传统的科学解释。

3. While the **theory** appeared sound on paper, repeated experimental trials failed to produce the results that researchers had anticipated.
   虽然该理论在纸面上看起来合理，但反复的实验试验未能产生研究人员预期的结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "theory" as coming from Greek "theoria" meaning "contemplation" — a theory is something you contemplate deeply before accepting.
把"theory"想成来自希腊语"theoria"，意为"沉思"——理论是你在接受之前深入思考的东西。
