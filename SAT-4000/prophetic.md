# 🔮 prophetic

🔊 /prəˈfɛtɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Accurately predicting what will happen in the future; of, relating to, or characteristic of a prophet or prophecy
**中文：** adj. 预言的；预示的；先知的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prescient（有先见之明的）
- predictive（预测的）
- oracular（神谕的）
- visionary（有远见的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prophetic words / prophetic vision / prophetic warning / prove prophetic**

👉 Example: His prophetic warning about the financial crisis proved accurate when the markets collapsed the following year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's **prophetic** novel, written decades ago, eerily predicted many technological developments that exist in society today.
   这位作者几十年前写的预言性小说，令人毛骨悚然地预测了当今社会存在的许多技术发展。

2. Her **prophetic** remarks about climate change during the 1990s were dismissed at the time but have since been validated.
   她在二十世纪九十年代关于气候变化的预言性言论当时被忽视，但此后已被证实。

3. The economist's **prophetic** analysis of housing market trends enabled investors to avoid catastrophic losses during the recession.
   这位经济学家对房地产市场趋势的预见性分析使投资者在经济衰退期间避免了灾难性损失。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prophetic" as "prophet + ic" — relating to a prophet who can see the future before it happens.
把"prophetic"想成"prophet + ic"——与能在事情发生前预见未来的先知有关。
