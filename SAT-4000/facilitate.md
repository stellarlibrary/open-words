# 🤝 facilitate

🔊 /fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make an action or process easier or more achievable; to assist the progress of something
**中文：** 促进；帮助；使容易；使便利

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expedite（加速）
- promote（促进）
- enable（使能够）
- assist（协助）
- streamline（简化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **facilitate discussion / facilitate communication / facilitate the process / facilitate trade**

👉 Example: The mediator was brought in to facilitate negotiations between the two disputing parties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new software platform was designed to **facilitate** seamless collaboration among researchers working across different continents.
   这个新软件平台旨在促进不同大洲研究人员之间的无缝合作。

2. International organizations play a critical role in **facilitating** diplomatic dialogue between nations engaged in territorial disputes.
   国际组织在促进有领土争端的国家之间的外交对话方面发挥着至关重要的作用。

3. The university established a mentorship program to **facilitate** the academic and professional development of first-generation students.
   该大学建立了一个导师计划，以促进第一代大学生的学术和职业发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "facili-tate" as making things "facile" (easy) — to facilitate is to make a process easier for everyone involved.
把"facilitate"想成让事情变得"facile"(容易)——facilitate就是让一个过程对所有参与者来说更容易。
