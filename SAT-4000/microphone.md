# 🎤 microphone

🔊 /ˈmaɪkrəfoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An instrument for converting sound waves into electrical energy variations, which may then be amplified, transmitted, or recorded.
**中文：** n. 话筒；扩音器；麦克风

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mic（麦克）
- transmitter（传声器）
- recording device（录音设备）
- audio pickup（拾音器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wireless microphone / speak into a microphone / microphone stand / turn on the microphone**

👉 Example: The speaker adjusted the microphone before addressing the large audience in the auditorium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist held out her **microphone** to capture the diplomat's response during the tense international press conference.
   记者伸出麦克风，在紧张的国际新闻发布会上捕捉外交官的回应。

2. Advances in **microphone** technology have enabled studio engineers to record sound with unprecedented clarity and precision.
   麦克风技术的进步使录音室工程师能够以前所未有的清晰度和精度录制声音。

3. The professor requested a wireless **microphone** so she could move freely while delivering her lecture to the hall.
   教授要求使用无线麦克风，以便她在向大厅发表演讲时能够自由移动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "micro-phone" — "micro" (small) + "phone" (sound). It is a device that picks up small sounds and makes them louder for everyone to hear.
把"microphone"想成"micro"（微小的）+"phone"（声音）——一个捕捉微小声音并将其放大让所有人听到的设备。
