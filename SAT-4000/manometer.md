# 🔬 manometer

🔊 /məˈnɒmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An instrument for measuring the pressure of gases or liquids, including blood pressure
**中文：** n. 压力计；测压计；血压计

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pressure gauge（压力表）
- barometer（气压计）
- sphygmomanometer（血压计）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **manometer reading / mercury manometer / differential manometer / connect a manometer**

👉 Example: The technician attached a manometer to the pipeline to measure the internal gas pressure accurately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory technician used a **manometer** to determine whether the vacuum chamber had reached the required pressure level.
   实验室技术人员使用压力计来确定真空室是否达到了所需的压力水平。

2. A differential **manometer** was installed across the filter to monitor pressure drop and detect potential blockages in the system.
   在过滤器两端安装了一个差压计，以监测压降并检测系统中可能的堵塞。

3. Students learned to read a mercury **manometer** during the physics practicum, recording atmospheric pressure variations throughout the week.
   学生们在物理实践课上学习了如何读取水银压力计，并记录了整周的大气压变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "mano-" comes from a Greek root meaning thin or rare (related to pressure), and "-meter" means a measuring device — so a manometer measures pressure.
拆解记忆："mano-"来自希腊语词根，与压力有关，"-meter"表示测量工具——所以manometer就是测量压力的仪器。
