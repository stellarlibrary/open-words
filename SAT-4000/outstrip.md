# 🏃 outstrip

🔊 /ˌaʊtˈstrɪp/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To move faster than and overtake; to surpass or exceed in performance, achievement, or extent
**中文：** v. 超过；胜过；比…跑得快

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surpass（超越）
- exceed（超过）
- outpace（超过速度）
- overtake（赶超）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outstrip demand / outstrip supply / outstrip expectations / outstrip competitors**

👉 Example: Population growth continues to outstrip the available supply of affordable housing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The demand for renewable energy technology has begun to **outstrip** the current capacity of global manufacturing facilities.
   对可再生能源技术的需求已开始超过全球制造设施的现有产能。

2. Several emerging economies have managed to **outstrip** established nations in terms of annual growth rate.
   几个新兴经济体在年增长率方面已成功超越发达国家。

3. The university's enrollment figures **outstripped** all projections, forcing administrators to expand dormitory and classroom facilities.
   该大学的招生人数超出所有预期，迫使管理层扩建宿舍和教室设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out-strip" as "stripping past others on the outside" — like a runner who passes everyone on the outer lane.
把"outstrip"想成"从外侧strip past"——就像一个跑步者从外道超越所有人。
