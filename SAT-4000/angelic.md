# 👼 angelic

🔊 /ænˈdʒɛlɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Resembling or befitting an angel; exceptionally beautiful, innocent, or kind in nature
**中文：** 天使的；似天使的；天国的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- celestial（天上的）
- seraphic（六翼天使般的）
- divine（神圣的）
- heavenly（天堂般的）
- ethereal（飘逸的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **angelic face / angelic voice / angelic smile / angelic appearance / angelic patience**

👉 Example: The child's angelic smile charmed every guest who attended the formal reception.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The soprano's **angelic** voice resonated through the cathedral, moving the entire congregation to reverent silence and tears.
   女高音天使般的嗓音在大教堂中回荡，全体会众感动得肃静落泪。

2. Despite his **angelic** appearance, the young diplomat proved to be a shrewd and formidable negotiator at the summit.
   尽管外表看起来像天使一样纯真，这位年轻外交官在峰会上被证明是一位精明而强大的谈判者。

3. The painter captured an **angelic** quality in the portrait that seemed to radiate purity and otherworldly serenity.
   画家在肖像中捕捉到了一种天使般的气质，似乎散发着纯洁和超凡脱俗的宁静。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "angel-ic" as simply "angel" + "-ic" (like) — having the qualities of an angel: pure, beautiful, and heavenly.
把"angelic"想成"angel"（天使）+"-ic"（像……的）——拥有天使的品质：纯洁、美丽、如同天国一般。
