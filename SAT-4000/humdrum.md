# 😴 humdrum

🔊 /ˈhʌmdrʌm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking excitement or variety; dull, monotonous, and tediously repetitive
**中文：** adj. 单调的；无聊的；乏味的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- monotonous（单调的）
- tedious（乏味的）
- mundane（平凡的）
- dreary（沉闷的）
- dull（无聊的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **humdrum existence / humdrum routine / humdrum life / humdrum job**

👉 Example: She longed to escape her humdrum routine and experience something truly adventurous and new.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years of a **humdrum** existence in the suburbs, he decided to travel abroad and pursue his artistic ambitions.
   在郊区度过了多年单调的生活后，他决定出国旅行，追求自己的艺术抱负。

2. The professor's engaging lecture transformed what could have been a **humdrum** topic into a fascinating intellectual exploration.
   教授引人入胜的讲座将一个原本可能很无聊的话题变成了一次迷人的知识探索。

3. Many employees find their **humdrum** daily tasks unbearable without occasional creative projects to sustain their motivation.
   许多员工发现，如果没有偶尔的创意项目来维持动力，日常单调的工作令人难以忍受。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humdrum" as "hum + drum" — imagine someone boringly humming while tapping a drum in the same dull rhythm, over and over.
把"humdrum"想成"hum + drum"——想象一个人无聊地哼着歌，同时用同样沉闷的节奏一遍又一遍地敲着鼓。
