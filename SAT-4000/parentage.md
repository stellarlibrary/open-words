# 👪 parentage

🔊 /ˈpɛrəntɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The identity and origins of one's parents; lineage, ancestry, or birth origin
**中文：** n. 出身；亲子关系；门第；起源

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ancestry（祖先）
- lineage（血统）
- origin（起源）
- heritage（遗产）
- descent（血统）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **of humble parentage / of noble parentage / unknown parentage / mixed parentage**

👉 Example: The child of humble parentage rose to become one of the most influential leaders in history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite her modest **parentage**, she earned a prestigious scholarship and eventually became a distinguished professor at the university.
   尽管她出身平凡，她获得了一笔有声望的奖学金，最终成为大学里一位杰出的教授。

2. The biographer devoted an entire chapter to examining the **parentage** and early upbringing of the celebrated nineteenth-century novelist.
   传记作者用了整整一章来考察这位著名十九世纪小说家的出身和早年成长经历。

3. Geneticists can now determine **parentage** with remarkable accuracy through advanced DNA analysis and sophisticated molecular testing methods.
   遗传学家现在可以通过先进的DNA分析和精密的分子检测方法以惊人的准确性确定亲子关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "parent-age" as "the age and story of your parents" — your parentage tells the story of where you come from.
把"parentage"想成"parent（父母）+ age（时代）"——你的出身讲述了你来自哪里的故事。
