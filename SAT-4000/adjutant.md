# 🎖️ adjutant

🔊 /ˈædʒʊtənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词，形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A military officer who assists a commanding officer with administrative duties; a helper or assistant; (adj.) serving in an auxiliary capacity.
**中文：** 副官，助手；（形容词）辅助的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aide（助手）
- assistant（助理）
- deputy（副手）
- aide-de-camp（副官）
- subordinate（下属）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adjutant general**, **adjutant to the commander**, **serve as adjutant**

👉 Example: The colonel relied heavily on his adjutant for daily administrative operations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **adjutant** prepared all briefing materials before the general's morning strategy session.
   副官在将军早晨的战略会议之前准备了所有简报材料。

2. She served as **adjutant** to the division commander throughout the entire peacekeeping mission.
   她在整个维和任务期间担任师长的副官。

3. The newly appointed **adjutant** streamlined communication between headquarters and the field units.
   新任副官简化了总部与野战部队之间的通信流程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adjutant" sharing the root with "aid" — the person who aids a commanding officer.
联想"adjutant"与"aid"（帮助）同源——辅助指挥官的人。
