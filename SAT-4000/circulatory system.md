# 🫀 circulatory system

🔊 /ˈsɜːrkjələˌtɔːri ˈsɪstəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun phrase | 名词短语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The system of organs and tissues, including the heart, blood vessels, and lymphatic vessels, that circulates blood and lymph through the body.
**中文：** [解剖] 循环系统；由心脏、血管和淋巴管组成的在体内循环输送血液和淋巴的器官系统。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cardiovascular system（心血管系统）
- vascular system（脉管系统）
- blood circulation（血液循环）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the circulatory system / circulatory system function / disorders of the circulatory system**

👉 Example: A healthy diet and regular exercise are essential for maintaining a well-functioning circulatory system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **circulatory system** delivers oxygen and essential nutrients to every cell throughout the human body.
   循环系统将氧气和必需的营养物质输送到人体的每一个细胞。

2. Prolonged sedentary behavior can severely impair the efficiency of the **circulatory system** over time.
   长期久坐不动会随着时间推移严重损害循环系统的效率。

3. Medical researchers have developed innovative techniques to study the **circulatory system** at the microscopic level.
   医学研究人员开发了创新技术，从微观层面研究循环系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circulatory" from "circle" — blood travels in a circle through the body via this system, always returning to the heart.
把"circulatory"联想到"circle（圆圈）"——血液通过这个系统在体内循环流动，始终回到心脏。
