# 📋 commission

🔊 /kəˈmɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A group of people officially appointed to perform a function; a fee paid for a service; an order for a piece of work. (v.) To formally authorize or appoint; to order the production of something
**中文：** n. 委员会；佣金；委任，委托 v. 委任，任命；委托制作

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- committee（委员会）
- mandate（授权）
- authorize（批准）
- delegate（委派）
- fee（费用）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commission a study / commission a work of art / earn a commission / a royal commission**

👉 Example: The city council voted to commission a new public mural for the downtown district.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government decided to **commission** an independent investigation into the causes of the environmental disaster near the coast.
   政府决定委托一项针对沿海地区环境灾难原因的独立调查。

2. The wealthy patron agreed to **commission** a portrait from the renowned artist for the gallery's permanent collection.
   这位富有的赞助人同意委托知名艺术家为画廊的永久收藏创作一幅肖像画。

3. The sales representative earned a generous **commission** after successfully negotiating the largest contract in the company's history.
   该销售代表在成功谈成公司历史上最大的合同后获得了丰厚的佣金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "commission" as "co-mission" — a shared mission given to a group or person, like a committee assigned an official task.
把"commission"想成"co-mission"——交给一个团体或个人的共同使命，就像委员会被分配的官方任务。
