# 💨 volatile

🔊 /ˈvɑːlətl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Liable to change rapidly and unpredictably; easily evaporated at normal temperatures. (n.) A volatile substance
**中文：** adj. 挥发性的；易变的；不稳定的 n. 挥发物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unstable（不稳定的）
- unpredictable（不可预测的）
- erratic（反复无常的）
- mercurial（变化无常的）
- fickle（善变的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **volatile market / volatile situation / volatile substance / volatile personality**

👉 Example: Investors should exercise caution when dealing with volatile markets during periods of uncertainty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **volatile** political situation in the region deterred foreign corporations from making long-term investment commitments.
   该地区不稳定的政治局势使外国企业不愿做出长期投资承诺。

2. Chemists must store **volatile** compounds in sealed containers to prevent rapid evaporation and potential hazards.
   化学家必须将挥发性化合物存放在密封容器中，以防止快速蒸发和潜在危险。

3. Her **volatile** temperament made diplomatic negotiations challenging, as her positions shifted without warning or explanation.
   她反复无常的性格使外交谈判充满挑战，因为她的立场会毫无预警地改变。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "volatile" as "vol-a-tile" — something that can "fly away" (Latin "volare") like a tile blown off a roof, unpredictable and unstable.
把"volatile"想成"vol-a-tile"——像被风吹走的瓦片一样"飞走"（拉丁语"volare"），不可预测且不稳定。
