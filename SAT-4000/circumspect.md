# 🔍 circumspect

🔊 /ˈsɜːrkəmspɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Wary and unwilling to take risks; careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences before acting.
**中文：** 慎重的；周到的；细心的；谨慎小心的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cautious（谨慎的）
- prudent（审慎的）
- wary（小心的）
- discreet（慎重的）
- vigilant（警惕的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **circumspect approach / circumspect about / remain circumspect / circumspect in dealings**

👉 Example: A circumspect leader evaluates every option before committing resources to a new initiative.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The experienced diplomat remained **circumspect** in her public statements to avoid provoking an international incident.
   这位经验丰富的外交官在公开声明中保持审慎，以避免引发国际事件。

2. Investors should be **circumspect** when evaluating speculative ventures that promise extraordinarily high returns with minimal risk.
   投资者在评估那些承诺以极低风险获得超高回报的投机项目时应当谨慎。

3. The committee adopted a **circumspect** strategy, thoroughly analyzing potential consequences before implementing the proposed regulatory changes.
   委员会采取了审慎的策略，在实施拟议的监管变革之前彻底分析了潜在后果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circum-" (around) + "spect" (look) — to look around carefully before acting, like checking all directions at a crossroads.
把"circumspect"拆分为"circum-（周围）"+"spect（看）"——行动前四下张望仔细观察，就像在十字路口查看各个方向。
