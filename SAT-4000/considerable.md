# 📊 considerable

🔊 /kənˈsɪdərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Notably large in size, amount, or extent; worthy of consideration or attention
**中文：** adj. 相当大的，可观的；重要的，值得考虑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- substantial（大量的）
- significant（重要的）
- noteworthy（值得注意的）
- appreciable（可观的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **considerable amount / considerable effort / considerable influence / considerable attention**

👉 Example: The project required considerable investment before yielding any measurable returns.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company devoted **considerable** resources to developing a vaccine that could withstand extreme temperature variations.
   该制药公司投入了大量资源来开发一种能够承受极端温度变化的疫苗。

2. Her research made a **considerable** contribution to the field of renewable energy and sustainable development.
   她的研究对可再生能源和可持续发展领域做出了相当大的贡献。

3. The committee faced **considerable** opposition from stakeholders who questioned the feasibility of the proposed reforms.
   委员会遭到了利益相关者的强烈反对，他们质疑拟议改革的可行性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "consider-able" — something so large or important that it is "able" to make you stop and "consider" it carefully.
把"considerable"想成"able to make you consider"——大到或重要到让你不得不停下来认真"考虑"的程度。
