# ⚔️ feint

🔊 /feɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A deceptive or pretended action, especially in combat or sports; a mock attack. (v.) To make a deceptive movement to mislead an opponent
**中文：** n. 佯攻；伪装 v. 佯攻；假装

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ruse（诡计）
- bluff（虚张声势）
- diversion（牵制）
- stratagem（策略）
- pretense（伪装）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make a feint / feint to the left / a feint attack / feint and strike**

👉 Example: The boxer executed a feint with his left hand before delivering a powerful right hook.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The general ordered a **feint** on the eastern front to draw enemy forces away from the primary point of attack.
   将军下令在东线进行佯攻，以将敌军从主要进攻点引开。

2. The fencer made a quick **feint** toward the shoulder, then redirected her blade to score a decisive touch below.
   击剑手迅速向肩部做了一个假动作，然后将剑锋转向下方得到了决定性的一击。

3. His proposal was merely a diplomatic **feint** designed to buy time while the administration prepared its actual strategy.
   他的提议只不过是一个外交佯攻，旨在为政府准备实际战略争取时间。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "feint" as a "faint attack" — it looks like a real attack but is faint, weak, and designed only to deceive.
把"feint"想成"faint attack（微弱的攻击）"——它看起来像真正的攻击，但实际上很微弱，只是为了欺骗对手。
