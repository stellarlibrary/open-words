# 🧱 refractory

🔊 /rɪˈfræktəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Stubbornly resistant to authority or control; difficult to treat or manage; resistant to heat or other physical processes
**中文：** adj. 难治的；难熔的；不听话的；难控制的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obstinate（固执的）
- intractable（难处理的）
- recalcitrant（顽抗的）
- stubborn（顽固的）
- unmanageable（难管理的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **refractory patient / refractory material / refractory behavior / refractory period / refractory disease**

👉 Example: The refractory patient refused to follow the prescribed treatment plan despite repeated medical advice.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician noted that the infection had become **refractory** to standard antibiotics, necessitating a more aggressive treatment approach.
   医生注意到感染对标准抗生素已产生耐药性，需要采取更积极的治疗方法。

2. Engineers selected **refractory** ceramic materials capable of withstanding temperatures exceeding two thousand degrees in the industrial furnace lining.
   工程师选择了能够承受工业炉衬中超过两千度高温的耐火陶瓷材料。

3. The **refractory** students consistently defied classroom rules, prompting the administration to implement a comprehensive behavioral intervention program.
   这些不听话的学生持续违反课堂规则，促使管理层实施全面的行为干预计划。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-fract-ory" — something that "fractures" your attempts to control it, resisting all your efforts like a stubborn mule.
把"refractory"想成"re-fract-ory"——"打碎"你控制它的企图的东西，像一头倔驴一样抵抗你所有的努力。
