# 🏷 misnomer

🔊 /ˌmɪsˈnoʊmər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A wrong or inaccurate name or term; a misapplied or inappropriate designation for something
**中文：** n. 用词不当；误称

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mislabel（错误标注）
- misdesignation（错误命名）
- inaccuracy（不准确）
- wrong name（错误名称）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a bit of a misnomer / something of a misnomer / a complete misnomer / prove to be a misnomer**

👉 Example: Calling the device a "phone" is something of a misnomer, given that it functions primarily as a computer.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The term "dark ages" is widely considered a **misnomer**, as significant intellectual and cultural achievements occurred during that period.
   "黑暗时代"一词被普遍认为是一种误称，因为那个时期取得了重要的思想和文化成就。

2. Labeling the massive reptile a "dinosaur" proved to be a scientific **misnomer** once researchers reclassified it as a marine creature.
   一旦研究人员将这种巨大的爬行动物重新归类为海洋生物，将其称为"恐龙"就被证明是一个科学上的误称。

3. Critics argued that "affordable housing" was a **misnomer** for the project, since most units remained far beyond average residents' budgets.
   批评人士认为"经济适用房"对该项目来说是一种误称，因为大多数单元远超普通居民的预算。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-nomer" where "nomer" comes from Latin "nomen" (name) — a "mis-name," a wrong name applied to something.
把"misnomer"中的"nomer"与拉丁语"nomen（名字）"联系起来——一个"错误的名字"，即对事物的错误称呼。
