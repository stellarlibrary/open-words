# ⚙️ operation

🔊 /ˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of operating or functioning; a surgical procedure; a business activity; a mathematical process
**中文：** n. 操作；经营；手术；运算

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- procedure（程序）
- functioning（运作）
- execution（执行）
- undertaking（事业）
- maneuver（演习）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in operation / operation of a machine / surgical operation / mathematical operation**

👉 Example: The new factory will be in full operation by the end of the fiscal quarter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The successful **operation** of the power plant requires continuous monitoring by a team of highly trained engineers and technicians.
   发电厂的成功运作需要一支训练有素的工程师和技术人员团队进行持续监控。

2. Surgeons performed a delicate **operation** to remove the tumor, which lasted over seven hours and required extraordinary precision.
   外科医生进行了一场精细的手术以切除肿瘤，手术持续了七个多小时，需要极高的精准度。

3. Students must demonstrate mastery of each arithmetic **operation** before advancing to more complex algebraic concepts in the curriculum.
   学生必须展示对每种算术运算的掌握，才能在课程中进入更复杂的代数概念。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "opera-tion" as "opera + action" — like an opera performance, an operation involves a series of coordinated actions working together.
把"operation"想成"opera + action"——就像歌剧表演一样，一个操作涉及一系列协调配合的动作。
