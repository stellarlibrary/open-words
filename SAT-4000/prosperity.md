# 💰 prosperity

🔊 /prɑːˈspɛrəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being prosperous; success, especially in financial or economic terms; flourishing condition
**中文：** n. 繁荣，兴旺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- affluence（富裕）
- wealth（财富）
- flourishing（繁盛）
- success（成功）
- opulence（富足）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **economic prosperity / bring prosperity / period of prosperity / prosperity and growth**

👉 Example: The postwar era ushered in an unprecedented period of prosperity and technological advancement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation enjoyed decades of uninterrupted **prosperity** following the implementation of comprehensive economic reform policies.
   在实施全面经济改革政策后，该国享受了数十年不间断的繁荣。

2. True **prosperity** encompasses not merely material wealth but also the well-being and educational advancement of all citizens.
   真正的繁荣不仅包括物质财富，还包括全体公民的福祉和教育进步。

3. Historians attribute the region's remarkable **prosperity** to its strategic location along ancient trade routes connecting East and West.
   历史学家将该地区显著的繁荣归因于其位于连接东西方的古代贸易路线上的战略位置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prosper-ity" — when things PROSPER, they grow and thrive, leading to a state of prosperity and abundance.
把"prosperity"想成"prosper-ity"——当事物繁荣（prosper）时，它们不断成长壮大，达到繁荣兴旺的状态。
