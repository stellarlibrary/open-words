# 🌈 spectroscope

🔊 /ˈspektrəskoʊp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An optical instrument used to separate and observe the spectrum of light emitted or absorbed by a substance.
**中文：** n. 分光镜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spectrometer（光谱仪）
- spectrograph（摄谱仪）
- prism instrument（棱镜仪器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **use a spectroscope / spectroscope analysis / observe through a spectroscope / spectroscope readings**

👉 Example: The chemist used a spectroscope to identify the unknown elements present in the mineral sample.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. By examining starlight through a **spectroscope**, astronomers can determine the chemical composition of distant celestial bodies.
   通过分光镜观察星光，天文学家可以确定遥远天体的化学成分。

2. The physics professor demonstrated how a **spectroscope** separates white light into its component wavelengths during the lecture.
   物理教授在课堂上演示了分光镜如何将白光分离成各组成波长。

3. Early scientists relied on the **spectroscope** to discover previously unknown elements such as cesium and rubidium.
   早期科学家依靠分光镜发现了铯和铷等此前未知的元素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spectro-scope" as "spectrum + scope" — it is literally a scope (viewing device) for observing the spectrum of light.
把"spectroscope"想成"spectrum + scope"——它字面意思就是观察光谱（spectrum）的观察器（scope）。
