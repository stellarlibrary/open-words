# 🍽️ digestion

🔊 /daɪˈdʒɛstʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of breaking down food in the body so it can be absorbed; the mental process of understanding and absorbing information
**中文：** n. 消化；领悟

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absorption（吸收）
- assimilation（同化吸收）
- metabolism（新陈代谢）
- processing（处理）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aid digestion / impair digestion / digestion of food / slow digestion / proper digestion**

👉 Example: Regular physical exercise has been shown to aid digestion and improve overall metabolic health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **digestion** of complex carbohydrates begins in the mouth through the enzymatic action of salivary amylase on starches.
   复杂碳水化合物的消化从口腔中唾液淀粉酶对淀粉的酶促作用开始。

2. Prolonged stress can significantly impair **digestion** and lead to a range of chronic gastrointestinal health disorders.
   长期压力会严重损害消化功能，并导致一系列慢性胃肠道健康疾病。

3. The **digestion** of dense academic literature requires patience, critical thinking, and a willingness to revisit challenging passages repeatedly.
   消化晦涩的学术文献需要耐心、批判性思维以及反复重读困难段落的意愿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "digestion" as "digest + ion (action)" — the ongoing action of breaking things down, whether food in your stomach or ideas in your mind.
把"digestion"想成"digest + ion（动作）"——持续分解事物的过程，无论是胃里的食物还是脑中的思想。
