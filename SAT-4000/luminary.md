# 🌟 luminary

🔊 /ˈluːmɪnɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who inspires or influences others; a prominent figure in a particular field; also, a natural light-giving body
**中文：** 发光体；杰出人物；才智出众的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- luminary（杰出人物）
- visionary（有远见的人）
- notable（名人）
- dignitary（显要人物）
- icon（偶像）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a luminary in the field / literary luminary / scientific luminary / political luminary**

👉 Example: She became a luminary in the field of molecular biology after decades of pioneering research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The conference attracted **luminaries** from the worlds of science, technology, and international policy development.
   这次会议吸引了来自科学、技术和国际政策制定领域的杰出人物。

2. As a **luminary** of modern architecture, her innovative designs transformed the skylines of several major cities worldwide.
   作为现代建筑界的杰出人物，她的创新设计改变了全球多个主要城市的天际线。

3. The young scholar aspired to become a **luminary** in her discipline by publishing groundbreaking research in prestigious journals.
   这位年轻学者立志通过在知名期刊上发表开创性研究成为本学科的杰出人物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lumin-ary" from "luminous" — a luminary is someone who shines brightly, illuminating their entire field.
把"luminary"想成来自"luminous（发光的）"——luminary就是一个光芒四射、照亮整个领域的人。
