# 🌀 devious

🔊 /ˈdiːviəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing a skillful use of underhanded tactics to achieve goals; departing from the direct or usual course; roundabout
**中文：** adj. 偏僻的；弯曲的；不光明正大的；迂回的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cunning（狡猾的）
- deceitful（欺骗的）
- underhanded（不正当的）
- roundabout（迂回的）
- sly（狡诈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **devious plan / devious tactics / devious route / devious mind**

👉 Example: The spy employed devious methods to infiltrate the enemy organization undetected.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed a **devious** scheme designed to embezzle funds from the charitable organization over several years.
   调查揭露了一个旨在数年内从慈善组织挪用资金的不正当阴谋。

2. The hikers chose a **devious** path through the mountains, winding around cliffs and through narrow hidden valleys.
   徒步旅行者选择了一条迂回的山间小路，蜿蜒绕过悬崖，穿过狭窄的隐蔽山谷。

3. Political analysts criticized the candidate for using **devious** tactics to undermine the credibility of the opposing campaign.
   政治分析人士批评该候选人使用不光明正大的策略来削弱对方竞选活动的可信度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "devious" as "deviating from the obvious" — a devious person takes a crooked, indirect path instead of a straight one.
把"devious"想成"偏离(deviate)显而易见的(obvious)路线"——一个devious的人走的是弯曲、间接的路。
