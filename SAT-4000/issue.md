# 📰 issue

🔊 /ˈɪʃuː/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An important topic or problem for debate; a particular edition of a publication; the act of distributing or releasing. (v.) To supply or distribute officially; to publish or announce formally
**中文：** n. 问题；争论点；期号；发行物 v. 发行；发布；发给；流出，放出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- matter（事务）
- concern（问题）
- publish（发布）
- distribute（分发）
- release（发行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **address an issue / issue a statement / the latest issue / issue a warning**

👉 Example: The government decided to issue new guidelines regarding public health safety measures during the outbreak.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial board devoted the entire **issue** of the journal to examining the ethical implications of artificial intelligence in healthcare.
   编辑委员会将该期刊的整期都用于探讨人工智能在医疗保健中的伦理影响。

2. The central bank announced its plan to **issue** new currency notes designed with advanced security features to prevent counterfeiting.
   中央银行宣布计划发行采用先进安全特征设计的新钞票，以防止伪造。

3. Climate change remains the most pressing **issue** facing policymakers, demanding urgent international cooperation and sustainable long-term solutions.
   气候变化仍然是决策者面临的最紧迫的问题，需要紧迫的国际合作和可持续的长期解决方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "issue" as something that "flows out" — from its Latin root "exire" (to go out). Problems flow out into discussion, and publications flow out to readers.
把"issue"想成"流出"的东西——来自拉丁语"exire（出去）"。问题流入讨论，出版物流向读者。
