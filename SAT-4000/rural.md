# 🌾 rural

🔊 /ˈrʊrəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or characteristic of the countryside; having a pastoral or rustic quality
**中文：** adj. 农村的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pastoral（田园的）
- rustic（乡村的）
- bucolic（乡村的）
- agrarian（农业的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rural area / rural community / rural development / rural landscape**

👉 Example: Many young professionals are leaving rural communities in search of better career opportunities in cities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government allocated significant funding to improve healthcare infrastructure in underserved **rural** regions across the country.
   政府拨出大量资金，改善全国服务不足的农村地区的医疗基础设施。

2. Despite rapid urbanization, many citizens still maintain a deep attachment to their **rural** ancestral homelands.
   尽管城市化进程迅速，许多公民仍然对他们的农村祖籍地保持着深厚的感情。

3. The sociologist conducted extensive research on the economic disparities between **rural** and urban populations in developing nations.
   这位社会学家对发展中国家农村和城市人口之间的经济差距进行了广泛研究。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rural" as sounding like "rule of the land" — the countryside is where the land rules over the concrete jungle.
把"rural"想象成"rule of the land"（土地的统治）——乡村就是土地主宰一切、而非钢筋水泥丛林的地方。
