# 💀 succumb

🔊 /səˈkʌm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To yield to a superior force; to die; to be overwhelmed
**中文：** v. 屈服，屈从；死

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- yield（屈服）
- submit（顺从）
- capitulate（投降）
- perish（死亡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **succumb to pressure / succumb to temptation / succumb to illness / succumb to injuries**

👉 Example: The patient eventually succumbed to the disease after a prolonged and courageous struggle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite her strong resolve, she finally **succumbed** to the overwhelming pressure from corporate stakeholders and resigned from her position.
   尽管她意志坚定，她最终还是屈服于企业利益相关者的巨大压力，辞去了职务。

2. Several elderly residents **succumbed** to the extreme heat wave that persisted throughout the region for two consecutive weeks.
   数名老年居民在该地区持续两周的极端热浪中不幸去世。

3. The ancient fortress **succumbed** to the relentless siege after defenders exhausted their remaining supplies of food and ammunition.
   这座古老的堡垒在守军耗尽剩余的食物和弹药后，最终在无情的围攻下陷落。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suc-cumb" as "suck + come down" — when you are sucked down, you yield and fall under a greater force.
把"succumb"想成"suck + come down"——当你被吸下去时，你在更大的力量下屈服倒下。
