# 🤨 incredulous

🔊 /ɪnˈkrɛdjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unwilling or unable to believe something; showing disbelief or skepticism
**中文：** adj. 怀疑的；不轻信的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- skeptical（怀疑的）
- disbelieving（不相信的）
- doubtful（疑惑的）
- dubious（半信半疑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incredulous look / incredulous tone / incredulous at / remain incredulous**

👉 Example: She gave him an incredulous look when he claimed to have finished the entire project overnight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The audience was **incredulous** when the magician appeared to levitate three feet above the stage without any visible support.
   当魔术师似乎在舞台上方三英尺处悬浮且没有任何可见支撑时，观众们难以置信。

2. Investors remained **incredulous** about the startup's projected revenue, questioning whether such rapid growth was truly achievable.
   投资者对该初创公司预测的收入持怀疑态度，质疑如此快速的增长是否真正可行。

3. The detective cast an **incredulous** glance at the suspect whose alibi seemed far too convenient to be genuine.
   侦探向嫌疑人投去怀疑的目光，因为其不在场证明似乎过于巧合而难以令人信服。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-cred-ulous" as "not credulous" — credulous means gullible and believing, so incredulous means the opposite: skeptical and disbelieving.
把"incredulous"拆分为"in（不）+ cred（相信）+ ulous"——不轻信的，和"credulous（轻信的）"正好相反。
