# 💊 sedative

🔊 /ˈsɛdətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A tranquilizer; something that calms; a painkiller. (adj.) Causing calm; causing quiet
**中文：** adj. 镇静的，止痛的 n. 镇静剂，止痛药

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tranquilizer（镇静剂）
- calmative（镇定药）
- soporific（催眠的）
- anodyne（止痛药）
- relaxant（松弛剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prescribe a sedative / sedative effect / mild sedative / under sedative**

👉 Example: The nurse administered a mild sedative to help the anxious patient relax before the operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor prescribed a **sedative** to alleviate the patient's severe anxiety and allow her to rest peacefully overnight.
   医生开了一种镇静剂来缓解患者严重的焦虑，让她能安然入睡。

2. Certain herbal teas possess a gentle **sedative** effect that can promote relaxation without causing excessive drowsiness.
   某些草药茶具有温和的镇静效果，可以促进放松而不会导致过度嗜睡。

3. The veterinarian used a powerful **sedative** to calm the distressed horse before transporting it safely to the facility.
   兽医使用了一种强效镇静剂来安抚这匹焦躁的马，然后将其安全运送到设施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sedat-ive" — related to "sedate," something that makes you sedate (calm and still), like a calming medicine.
把"sedative"想成与"sedate（镇静的）"相关——让你变得安静平和的东西，比如镇静药物。
