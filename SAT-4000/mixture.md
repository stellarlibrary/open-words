# 🧪 mixture

🔊 /ˈmɪkstʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A combination of two or more different substances, elements, or qualities blended together
**中文：** n. 混合；混合物；混合剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blend（混合物）
- combination（组合）
- compound（化合物）
- medley（混杂）
- amalgamation（融合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a mixture of / strange mixture / mixture of emotions / pour the mixture / blend the mixture**

👉 Example: The novel presents a compelling mixture of historical fact and imaginative literary fiction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist carefully analyzed the chemical **mixture** to determine the precise ratio of each individual component.
   科学家仔细分析了这种化学混合物，以确定每种成分的精确比例。

2. Her expression revealed a complex **mixture** of relief and lingering anxiety after receiving the long-awaited examination results.
   收到期待已久的考试成绩后，她的表情显露出一种复杂的宽慰与残存焦虑的混合。

3. The city's cultural identity is a fascinating **mixture** of indigenous traditions and modern cosmopolitan influences from abroad.
   这座城市的文化特征是本土传统与来自海外的现代国际化影响的迷人融合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mixture" as "mix-ture" — the word literally contains "mix," reminding you that it is something mixed together.
把"mixture"想成"mix-ture"——这个词本身就包含"mix"（混合），提醒你它是混合在一起的东西。
