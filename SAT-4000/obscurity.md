# 🌒 obscurity

🔊 /əbˈskjʊrəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being unknown or forgotten; darkness or dimness; the quality of being difficult to understand
**中文：** n. 朦胧；阴暗；晦涩；身份低微；不分明

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- anonymity（无名）
- darkness（阴暗）
- ambiguity（模糊）
- oblivion（湮没）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fade into obscurity / live in obscurity / rise from obscurity / the obscurity of a passage**

👉 Example: The artist lived in total obscurity until a critic discovered her remarkable paintings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After decades of fame, the once-celebrated novelist gradually faded into **obscurity** and was forgotten by the literary world.
   在数十年的声名之后，这位曾经备受赞誉的小说家逐渐淡出人们的视野，被文学界所遗忘。

2. The **obscurity** of the ancient philosophical text made it nearly impossible for modern scholars to interpret accurately.
   这部古代哲学文本的晦涩难懂使得现代学者几乎不可能准确地解读它。

3. She rose from complete **obscurity** to international prominence after her groundbreaking research was published in a leading journal.
   她的开创性研究在一家顶级期刊发表后，从默默无闻一跃成为国际知名人物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "obscurity" as "obscure + city" — imagine a dark, hidden city where nobody knows your name, representing the state of being unknown.
把"obscurity"想成"obscure + city"——想象一座黑暗隐蔽的城市，没人知道你的名字，代表着默默无闻的状态。
