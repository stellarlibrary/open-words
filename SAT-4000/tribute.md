# 🎁 tribute

🔊 /ˈtrɪbjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An expression of respect or admiration; a gift or payment made as a sign of allegiance or gratitude.
**中文：** n. 礼物；贡物；颂词；致敬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- homage（敬意）
- accolade（赞誉）
- commendation（表彰）
- offering（贡品）
- eulogy（颂词）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pay tribute to / a tribute to / tribute band / floral tribute**

👉 Example: The ceremony was organized to pay tribute to the scientists who dedicated their lives to medical research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. World leaders gathered at the memorial to pay **tribute** to the brave soldiers who sacrificed their lives during the conflict.
   世界各国领导人齐聚纪念碑前，向在冲突中牺牲的英勇士兵致敬。

2. The new library was built as a **tribute** to the philanthropist whose generous donations transformed the entire community.
   新图书馆的建造是对这位慷慨捐赠、改变了整个社区的慈善家的致敬。

3. In ancient times, conquered nations were required to send annual **tribute** to the imperial court as acknowledgment of submission.
   在古代，被征服的国家须每年向朝廷进贡，以表示臣服。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tribute" as "tri-bute" where "tri" sounds like "try" — you try to give something to show respect or honor someone.
把"tribute"想成"tri-bute"——"tri"听起来像"try"，你努力（try）给予某物来表达敬意。
