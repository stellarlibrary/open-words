# 🔩 pertinacity

🔊 /ˌpɜːrtɪˈnæsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being stubbornly persistent; resolute adherence to a purpose or course of action
**中文：** n. 顽固；执拗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tenacity（坚韧）
- obstinacy（固执）
- doggedness（顽强）
- persistence（坚持不懈）
- stubbornness（倔强）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with pertinacity / display pertinacity / remarkable pertinacity / sheer pertinacity**

👉 Example: It was her sheer pertinacity that allowed her to overcome every obstacle placed in her path.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The explorer's extraordinary **pertinacity** enabled him to survive three harrowing months alone in the uncharted wilderness.
   这位探险家非凡的毅力使他在未知的荒野中独自度过了三个月的艰险岁月。

2. Historians attribute the movement's eventual success largely to the **pertinacity** of its founding members who refused to yield.
   历史学家将该运动最终的成功主要归功于其创始成员们拒绝屈服的顽强不屈。

3. The **pertinacity** with which the young scholar pursued her doctoral research impressed even her most demanding academic advisors.
   这位年轻学者追求博士研究的执着精神令她最严格的学术导师们也深感佩服。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pertinacity" as the noun form of "pertinacious" — it is the quality of clinging to something like a tenacious grip that never lets go.
把"pertinacity"想成"pertinacious"的名词形式——它是一种像紧握不放的坚韧抓力一样的品质。
