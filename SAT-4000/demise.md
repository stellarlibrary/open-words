# 💀 demise

🔊 /dɪˈmaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Death or the end of existence; the cessation or termination of something
**中文：** n. 死亡，终止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- death（死亡）
- passing（逝世）
- downfall（衰落）
- collapse（崩溃）
- extinction（灭绝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **untimely demise / lead to the demise of / the demise of an empire / hasten the demise**

👉 Example: The rapid expansion of digital media hastened the demise of many traditional print newspapers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **demise** of the ancient civilization remains a subject of considerable scholarly debate among archaeologists and historians worldwide.
   这个古代文明的消亡至今仍是全世界考古学家和历史学家激烈学术争论的话题。

2. Corporate mismanagement and mounting debt ultimately led to the **demise** of what was once the nation's largest retail chain.
   企业管理不善和不断增加的债务最终导致了这个曾经全国最大零售连锁企业的终结。

3. Following the monarch's unexpected **demise**, the kingdom entered a prolonged period of political instability and civil unrest.
   在君主意外去世后，王国进入了长期的政治动荡和社会骚乱时期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "demise" as "de-mise" (dismiss from life) — when someone meets their demise, they are dismissed from existence.
把"demise"想成"de-mise"（从生命中解除）——当某人遭遇demise时，他们就从存在中被解除了。
