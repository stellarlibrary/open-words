# 🔀 albeit

🔊 /ɔːlˈbiːɪt/<br>
🏷️ *conjunction | 连词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Although; even though; notwithstanding that
**中文：** 虽然；即使

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- although（虽然）
- though（尽管）
- even though（即使）
- notwithstanding（尽管）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **albeit + adjective / albeit + adverb / albeit + prepositional phrase**
👉 Example: The project was completed on time, albeit with significant difficulty along the way.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee reached a consensus, **albeit** a fragile one that could easily unravel under political pressure.
   委员会达成了共识，虽然是一个脆弱的共识，在政治压力下很容易瓦解。

2. The experiment yielded positive results, **albeit** modest, suggesting that further research in this direction is warranted.
   实验产生了积极的结果，虽然不大，但表明在这个方向上进一步研究是有必要的。

3. He accepted the position, **albeit** reluctantly, knowing that the opportunity might never present itself again.
   他接受了这个职位，虽然很不情愿，但他知道这个机会可能不会再有。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "al-be-it" comes from "although it be" — a shortened form of concession meaning "even though."
拆解来看："al-be-it"源自"although it be"——一种表示让步的缩略形式，意为"即使"。
