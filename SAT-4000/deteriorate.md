# 📉 deteriorate

🔊 /dɪˈtɪəriəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To become progressively worse in quality, condition, or value; to decline or degrade
**中文：** v. 恶化；变坏；退化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decline（衰退）
- degrade（退化）
- worsen（恶化）
- decay（腐朽）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deteriorate rapidly / deteriorate over time / health deteriorates / conditions deteriorate**

👉 Example: The patient's condition began to deteriorate rapidly after the experimental treatment was discontinued.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Diplomatic relations between the two nations continued to **deteriorate** despite repeated attempts at reconciliation through formal channels.
   尽管多次通过正式渠道尝试和解，两国之间的外交关系仍在持续恶化。

2. Without regular maintenance, the structural integrity of historic buildings will inevitably **deteriorate** and eventually become hazardous.
   如果没有定期维护，历史建筑的结构完整性将不可避免地恶化，最终变得危险。

3. Researchers observed that air quality in urban centers tends to **deteriorate** significantly during periods of extreme heat and stagnation.
   研究人员观察到，城市中心的空气质量在极端高温和停滞期间往往会显著恶化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deteriorate" as "de-terior-ate" — "terior" sounds like "interior," and when the interior falls apart, everything gets worse.
把"deteriorate"想成"de-terior-ate"——"terior"听起来像"interior"（内部），当内部崩溃时，一切都会变糟。
