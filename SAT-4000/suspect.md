# 🔍 suspect

🔊 /səˈspɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective, noun | 动词, 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To believe to be guilty or false; to have doubts about. (adj.) Questionable; not to be trusted. (n.) A person suspected of a crime.
**中文：** v. 怀疑，猜疑，疑心 adj. 令人怀疑的 n. 嫌疑犯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distrust（不信任）
- doubt（怀疑）
- mistrust（猜疑）
- conjecture（推测）
- questionable（可疑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suspect someone of / prime suspect / suspect motives / suspect that**

👉 Example: The detective began to suspect the witness after noticing several inconsistencies in his testimony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Investigators **suspect** that the fire was deliberately set based on traces of accelerant found at the scene.
   调查人员根据现场发现的助燃剂痕迹怀疑这场火灾是人为纵火。

2. The authenticity of the ancient manuscript is **suspect**, as several experts have identified anomalies in its composition.
   这份古代手稿的真实性令人怀疑，因为多位专家发现了其成分中的异常之处。

3. The primary **suspect** was released after new forensic evidence conclusively proved his innocence in the case.
   在新的法医证据确凿地证明了该嫌疑犯的清白后，主要嫌疑人被释放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sus-pect" — to "look" (spect, like spectacle) at someone with suspicion, examining them from below (sus = sub).
把"suspect"想成"sus-pect"——从下面（sus = sub）"看"（spect，如spectacle）某人，带着怀疑审视他们。
