# 🎯 opportune

🔊 /ˌɒpərˈtjuːn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring at a favorable or useful time; well-timed; suitable for a particular purpose
**中文：** adj. 适当的；恰好的；合时宜的；及时的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- timely（及时的）
- propitious（吉利的）
- auspicious（有利的）
- favorable（有利的）
- convenient（方便的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **opportune moment / opportune time / opportune occasion / at an opportune juncture**

👉 Example: The diplomat waited for an opportune moment to introduce the sensitive topic during the bilateral negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher seized the **opportune** moment to present her groundbreaking findings at the prestigious international scientific conference.
   研究人员抓住了这一恰当的时机，在享有盛誉的国际科学会议上展示了她的突破性发现。

2. An **opportune** change in government policy allowed the struggling company to secure vital funding for its expansion plans.
   政府政策的一次适时变化使这家困难重重的公司获得了扩张计划的关键资金。

3. The general considered the enemy's retreat an **opportune** occasion to advance and consolidate territorial gains along the frontier.
   将军认为敌人的撤退是推进并巩固边境领土收益的大好时机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "opportune" as related to "opportunity" — an opportune moment is one that opens a door of opportunity at just the right time.
把"opportune"联想到"opportunity（机会）"——一个opportune的时刻就是在恰当时间打开一扇机会之门。
