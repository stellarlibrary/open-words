# 🎵 acoustic

🔊 /əˈkuːstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to sound or the sense of hearing; not electronically amplified
**中文：** 声学的；音响的；听觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- auditory（听觉的）
- sonic（声音的）
- aural（耳的）
- unplugged（不插电的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acoustic guitar / acoustic panel / acoustic properties / acoustic signal**

👉 Example: The singer performed an acoustic version of the popular song without any electronic amplification.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The concert hall was designed with superior **acoustic** properties to enhance the natural resonance of orchestral performances.
   这座音乐厅设计时具有卓越的声学特性，以增强管弦乐演出的自然共鸣。

2. Engineers installed **acoustic** panels throughout the office to reduce noise pollution and improve workplace productivity.
   工程师们在办公室各处安装了隔音板，以减少噪音污染并提高工作效率。

3. The physicist conducted experiments to measure the **acoustic** velocity of sound waves traveling through various media.
   这位物理学家进行了实验，测量声波在不同介质中传播的声学速度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-cou-stic" — "a cow stick" — imagine a cow tapping a stick on the ground, producing a natural, unamplified sound.
把"acoustic"想成"a cow stick"——想象一头牛用木棍敲地，发出自然的、未经放大的声音。
