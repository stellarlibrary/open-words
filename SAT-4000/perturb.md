# 😰 perturb

🔊 /pərˈtɜːrb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To disturb greatly; to make anxious or uneasy; to throw into disorder or confusion
**中文：** v. 扰乱；使……混乱；使……心绪不宁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disturb（打扰）
- unsettle（使不安）
- agitate（使焦虑）
- disquiet（使忧虑）
- fluster（使慌乱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perturb the system / perturb someone greatly / deeply perturbed / perturbed by**

👉 Example: The unexpected findings deeply perturbed the research team and forced a complete reassessment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The sudden resignation of the chief executive served to **perturb** investors who had relied on his leadership for stability.
   首席执行官的突然辞职使依赖他的领导来维持稳定的投资者们感到不安。

2. External gravitational forces can **perturb** the orbits of comets, sending them on unpredictable trajectories through the solar system.
   外部引力可以扰乱彗星的轨道，使它们在太阳系中沿不可预测的轨迹运行。

3. The committee was visibly **perturbed** by the revelation that critical safety protocols had been systematically disregarded for months.
   委员会对关键安全协议已被系统性地忽视数月这一事实的揭露明显感到不安。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-turb" as "per (thoroughly) + turb (turbulence)" — to be thoroughly thrown into turbulence and unease.
把"perturb"想成"per（彻底地）+ turb（turbulence 动荡）"——被彻底地抛入动荡和不安之中。
