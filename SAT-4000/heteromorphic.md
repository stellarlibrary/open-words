# 🔬 heteromorphic

🔊 /ˌhɛtərəˈmɔːrfɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having different forms or shapes at different stages of development; deviating from the normal or standard form
**中文：** adj. 异形的；异态的；形态不同的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- polymorphic（多形态的）
- irregular（不规则的）
- variable（多变的）
- dissimilar（不相似的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heteromorphic forms / heteromorphic life cycle / heteromorphic chromosomes / heteromorphic alternation**

👉 Example: The biologist documented the heteromorphic stages of the organism throughout its complex life cycle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The species exhibits **heteromorphic** characteristics, with larvae and adults displaying remarkably different physical structures and body plans.
   该物种表现出异形特征，幼虫和成虫展示出明显不同的身体结构和体型。

2. Geneticists discovered that the **heteromorphic** sex chromosomes in certain reptiles determine gender through environmental temperature variations.
   遗传学家发现某些爬行动物中的异形性染色体通过环境温度变化来决定性别。

3. The botanist classified the plant as **heteromorphic** because its leaves changed shape dramatically between juvenile and mature growth phases.
   植物学家将该植物归类为异形的，因为其叶片在幼年和成熟生长阶段之间形状变化显著。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hetero-" (different) + "morphic" (form/shape) — literally "different shapes," like a caterpillar transforming into a butterfly.
把"heteromorphic"拆成"hetero-"（不同的）+"morphic"（形态），字面意思是"不同形态的"，就像毛毛虫变成蝴蝶。
