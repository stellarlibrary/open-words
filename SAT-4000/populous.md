# 🏙️ populous

🔊 /ˈpɑːpjələs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a large number of people; densely populated; thickly inhabited
**中文：** adj. 人口稠密的；人口多的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- densely populated（人口稠密的）
- crowded（拥挤的）
- teeming（众多的）
- packed（挤满的）
- thronged（拥挤的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **populous city / populous region / most populous / densely populous area**

👉 Example: China and India remain the most populous nations on Earth, each with over a billion residents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Tokyo is one of the most **populous** metropolitan areas in the world, with over thirty million residents.
   东京是世界上人口最稠密的都市区之一，拥有超过三千万居民。

2. The government allocated additional funding to **populous** districts that required expanded healthcare and educational infrastructure.
   政府向需要扩建医疗和教育基础设施的人口稠密地区拨付了额外资金。

3. As the once-quiet town became increasingly **populous**, local authorities struggled to manage traffic and urban development.
   随着这个曾经安静的小镇人口日益增多，地方当局难以管理交通和城市发展。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "popul-ous" as "full of people" — the suffix "-ous" means "full of," so populous literally means full of population.
把"populous"想成"充满人的"——后缀"-ous"表示"充满"，所以populous字面意思就是人口众多。
