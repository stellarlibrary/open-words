# ⏳ lasting

🔊 /ˈlæstɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Continuing or enduring for a long time; permanent or durable in effect or influence
**中文：** adj. 持久的，永久的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enduring（持久的）
- permanent（永久的）
- durable（耐久的）
- abiding（持续的）
- perpetual（永恒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lasting impression / lasting impact / lasting peace / lasting legacy / lasting effect**

👉 Example: The professor's mentorship left a lasting impression on generations of graduate students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The landmark treaty established a **lasting** peace between the two nations that had endured decades of intermittent armed conflict.
   这项具有里程碑意义的条约在经历了数十年间歇性武装冲突的两国之间建立了持久和平。

2. Childhood experiences often have a **lasting** influence on an individual's emotional development and interpersonal relationship patterns.
   童年经历往往对个人的情感发展和人际关系模式产生持久的影响。

3. The architect's innovative designs made a **lasting** contribution to the field of sustainable urban planning and development.
   这位建筑师的创新设计对可持续城市规划和发展领域做出了持久贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lasting" as "last-ing" — something that keeps on "lasting" through time. If it lasts, it is lasting.
把"lasting"想成"last（持续）+ ing"——一直持续下去的东西就是lasting（持久的）。
