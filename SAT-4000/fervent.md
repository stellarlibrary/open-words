# 🔥 fervent

🔊 /ˈfɜːrvənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or displaying a passionate intensity; extremely enthusiastic or devoted
**中文：** adj. 热心的；热烈的；强烈的；炽热的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ardent（热烈的）
- passionate（热情的）
- zealous（热忱的）
- impassioned（充满激情的）
- earnest（诚挚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fervent supporter / fervent belief / fervent hope / fervent prayer / fervent desire**

👉 Example: The activist delivered a fervent speech calling for immediate reform of environmental policies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The congregation offered **fervent** prayers for the recovery of their beloved community leader during the ceremony.
   会众在仪式上为他们敬爱的社区领袖的康复献上了热切的祈祷。

2. Despite repeated setbacks, she maintained a **fervent** belief that her research would eventually yield significant results.
   尽管屡遭挫折，她仍保持着坚定的信念，相信她的研究最终会取得重大成果。

3. The senator's **fervent** advocacy for educational reform won widespread support from teachers and parents across the state.
   这位参议员对教育改革的热烈倡导赢得了全州教师和家长的广泛支持。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fervent" as "ferv-ent" — "ferv" sounds like "fever," and when you have a fever, you burn hot with intensity.
把"fervent"想成"ferv-ent"——"ferv"听起来像"fever"（发烧），当你发烧时，你燃烧着强烈的热情。
