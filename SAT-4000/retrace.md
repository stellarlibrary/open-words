# 🔙 retrace

🔊 /rɪˈtreɪs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To go back over the same path or route; to trace something back to its origin; to redraw or outline again
**中文：** v. 追溯；折回；退回；重描

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- backtrack（原路返回）
- revisit（重访）
- trace back（追溯）
- return（返回）
- recapitulate（重述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retrace one's steps / retrace the route / retrace the origins of / retrace the path**

👉 Example: After realizing she had lost her keys, she decided to retrace her steps through the park.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective attempted to **retrace** the suspect's movements on the evening of the crime using surveillance footage.
   侦探试图利用监控录像追溯嫌疑人在案发当晚的行动轨迹。

2. Historians have painstakingly **retraced** the ancient trade routes that once connected distant civilizations across vast continents.
   历史学家们费尽心力地追溯了曾经连接遥远文明的古代贸易路线。

3. Unable to find the trailhead, the hikers were forced to **retrace** their path through dense forest back to camp.
   由于找不到步道起点，徒步者被迫穿过茂密的森林原路返回营地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-TRACE" — literally to "trace again" — to follow the same trace or path you took before, going back the way you came.
把"retrace"想成"re-TRACE"——字面意思是"再次追踪"——沿着之前走过的痕迹或路径再走一遍，原路折回。
