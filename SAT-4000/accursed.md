# 😈 accursed

🔊 /əˈkɜːr.sɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Under a curse; hateful or detestable; extremely annoying.
**中文：** 被诅咒的；讨厌的；可憎的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cursed（被诅咒的）
- damned（该死的）
- abominable（可憎的）
- detestable（令人厌恶的）
- wretched（可恶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accursed place/object/fate/creature**

👉 Example: Legend holds that the accursed tomb brings misfortune to all who enter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The villagers believed the ancient ruin was **accursed** and refused to venture anywhere near its crumbling walls.
   村民们认为那座古老的废墟是被诅咒的，拒绝靠近那些摇摇欲坠的墙壁。

2. The protagonist struggled to escape his **accursed** fate, which had been foretold by the oracle at birth.
   主人公挣扎着想要逃脱他那被诅咒的命运，这个命运在他出生时就被神谕预言了。

3. After weeks of torrential rain, the farmers lamented the **accursed** weather that had destroyed their harvest.
   经过数周的暴雨，农民们痛惜这该死的天气毁掉了他们的庄稼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "a-CURSED" — it literally contains the word "cursed," meaning something is under a curse or deeply hated.
accursed 里面就有 curse（诅咒），加上前缀 a- 和后缀 -ed，就是"被诅咒的"。
