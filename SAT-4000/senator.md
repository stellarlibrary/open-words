# 🏛️ senator

🔊 /ˈsɛnətər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A member of a senate; a legislator in the upper chamber of a national or state government
**中文：** n. 参议员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- legislator（立法者）
- lawmaker（立法者）
- representative（代表）
- statesman（政治家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Senator [Name] / junior senator / senior senator / state senator / elect a senator**

👉 Example: The senator introduced a bipartisan bill aimed at reforming the national healthcare system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **senator** delivered an impassioned speech on the floor of the chamber, urging her colleagues to support the education reform bill.
   这位参议员在议会大厅发表了一番慷慨激昂的演讲，敦促同事们支持教育改革法案。

2. As a newly elected **senator**, he vowed to prioritize legislation addressing climate change and renewable energy development.
   作为一名新当选的参议员，他誓言优先推动应对气候变化和可再生能源发展的立法。

3. The committee, chaired by a senior **senator**, convened to investigate allegations of financial misconduct within the federal agency.
   由一位资深参议员主持的委员会召开会议，调查联邦机构内部的财务违规指控。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "senator" as coming from Latin "senex" (old man) — senators were originally the "elders" of Rome who governed with wisdom and experience.
把"senator"想成来自拉丁语"senex"（老人）——参议员最初是罗马的"长者"，凭智慧和经验治国。
