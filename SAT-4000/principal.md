# 👔 principal

🔊 /ˈprɪnsəpəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) First in order of importance; main. (n.) The head of a school; a chief or senior figure; the original sum of money invested or lent
**中文：** adj. 主要的；资本的 n. 首长；校长；资本；当事人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chief（首要的）
- primary（主要的）
- foremost（最重要的）
- leading（领先的）
- main（主要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **principal reason / principal investigator / school principal / principal amount**

👉 Example: The principal reason for the project's failure was a lack of adequate funding from the outset.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **principal** investigator presented the research findings at the international conference, drawing praise from distinguished scholars worldwide.
   首席研究员在国际会议上展示了研究成果，赢得了世界各地杰出学者的赞誉。

2. Repaying only the interest without reducing the **principal** will significantly extend the total duration of the mortgage loan.
   只偿还利息而不减少本金将大大延长抵押贷款的总期限。

3. The school **principal** implemented a new curriculum designed to improve critical thinking skills among all enrolled students.
   校长实施了一项旨在提高所有在校学生批判性思维能力的新课程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "principal" ending in "-pal" — the principal is your pal (friend) who runs the school; it also means the main or primary thing.
把"principal"的结尾想成"-pal（朋友）"——校长（principal）是管理学校的你的"朋友"；它也表示主要的或首要的事物。
