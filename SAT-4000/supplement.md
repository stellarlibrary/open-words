# ➕ supplement

🔊 /ˈsʌplɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To add to something to enhance or complete it. (n.) Something added to complete or make up for a deficiency; an additional section of a publication
**中文：** n. 补角，指两角之和为180度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- addition（补充）
- complement（补足物）
- appendix（附录）
- augmentation（增加）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **supplement one's income / dietary supplement / supplement to a book / supplement with**

👉 Example: She took a part-time job to supplement her income while attending graduate school.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many students choose to **supplement** their classroom learning with online courses to gain a broader understanding of the subject.
   许多学生选择用在线课程来补充课堂学习，以获得对该学科更广泛的理解。

2. The publisher released a **supplement** to the encyclopedia that included all major scientific discoveries from the past decade.
   出版商发行了一份百科全书增刊，收录了过去十年所有重大科学发现。

3. Physicians often recommend that patients **supplement** their diets with vitamins when nutritional deficiencies are detected.
   当检测到营养缺乏时，医生通常建议患者用维生素来补充饮食。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "supple-ment" as making something "supple" again by adding what is missing — you supplement to fill the gap.
把"supplement"想成通过添加缺少的东西使某物再次"supple"（完整）——你补充以填补空缺。
