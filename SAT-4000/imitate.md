# 🪞 imitate

🔊 /ˈɪmɪteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To copy or reproduce the behavior, appearance, or actions of someone or something; to counterfeit or forge
**中文：** v. 模仿，仿效；仿造，伪造

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mimic（模仿）
- emulate（效仿）
- replicate（复制）
- copy（抄袭）
- simulate（模拟）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imitate a style / imitate behavior / imitate nature / imitate a model / closely imitate**

👉 Example: Young children naturally imitate the speech patterns and gestures of their parents and caregivers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many aspiring painters attempt to **imitate** the techniques of the great masters before developing their own distinctive artistic style.
   许多有抱负的画家在形成自己独特的艺术风格之前会尝试模仿大师的技法。

2. The manufacturer was fined for producing counterfeit goods designed to **imitate** the appearance of well-known luxury brands.
   该制造商因生产旨在仿造知名奢侈品牌外观的假冒商品而被罚款。

3. Robotics engineers strive to **imitate** the fluid movements of the human hand to create more versatile prosthetic devices.
   机器人工程师努力模仿人手的流畅运动，以创造更多功能的假肢装置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imitate" as "I + mi(rror) + tate" — like looking in a mirror and copying what you see, you reproduce someone else's actions.
把"imitate"想成"I + mi(rror)（镜子）+ tate"——就像照镜子并模仿你看到的，你复制别人的行为。
