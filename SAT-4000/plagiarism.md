# 📋 plagiarism

🔊 /ˈpleɪdʒərɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The practice of taking someone else's work or ideas and passing them off as one's own; the copied material itself
**中文：** n. 剽窃；抄袭；剽窃物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- copying（抄袭）
- piracy（盗用）
- infringement（侵权）
- theft（偷窃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commit plagiarism / accused of plagiarism / academic plagiarism / plagiarism detection**

👉 Example: The student was expelled from the university after committing plagiarism on her thesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university implemented strict **plagiarism** detection software to ensure the integrity of all submitted research papers.
   该大学实施了严格的剽窃检测软件，以确保所有提交的研究论文的完整性。

2. Accusations of **plagiarism** effectively ended the promising political career of the young senator from the opposition party.
   剽窃指控实际上终结了这位来自反对党的年轻参议员前途光明的政治生涯。

3. The journal retracted the article after discovering substantial **plagiarism** from previously published works in the same field.
   该期刊在发现大量抄袭同一领域已发表作品的内容后撤回了这篇文章。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plagiarism" as containing "plague" — plagiarism is a plague on academic integrity, spreading dishonesty wherever it goes.
把"plagiarism"想成含有"plague（瘟疫）"——剽窃是学术诚信的瘟疫，到处传播不诚实。
