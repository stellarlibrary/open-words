# 🌍 gravitation

🔊 /ˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The force of attraction between all masses in the universe; movement toward or tendency to approach something
**中文：** n. 重力；万有引力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gravity（重力）
- attraction（吸引力）
- pull（引力）
- magnetism（磁力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **law of gravitation / universal gravitation / gravitational force / gravitation toward**

👉 Example: Newton's law of universal gravitation explains the attractive force between any two objects with mass.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Newton's theory of **gravitation** fundamentally transformed humanity's understanding of the physical forces governing celestial bodies in the universe.
   牛顿的万有引力理论从根本上改变了人类对宇宙中支配天体的物理力量的认识。

2. The physicist demonstrated that **gravitation** between two objects increases proportionally with their masses and decreases with distance.
   这位物理学家证明了两个物体之间的引力与其质量成正比，与距离成反比。

3. Scholars have observed a cultural **gravitation** toward democratic governance in many developing nations throughout the past century.
   学者们观察到，在过去一个世纪中，许多发展中国家在文化上趋向于民主治理。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gravit-ation" as "gravity in action" — gravitation is the process or force by which gravity pulls objects together.
把"gravitation"想成"gravity in action（引力在起作用）"——gravitation就是引力吸引物体的过程或力量。
