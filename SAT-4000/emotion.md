# 💖 emotion

🔊 /ɪˈmoʊʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A natural instinctive state of mind deriving from one's circumstances, mood, or relationships with others; a strong feeling
**中文：** n. 情感；情绪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- feeling（感觉）
- sentiment（情感）
- passion（激情）
- affect（情感状态）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **express emotion / strong emotion / mixed emotions / overcome with emotion**

👉 Example: The speaker's voice trembled with emotion as she recounted her personal experience of overcoming adversity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary evoked powerful **emotion** in audiences by depicting the resilience of communities affected by the devastating earthquake.
   这部纪录片通过描绘受毁灭性地震影响的社区的韧性，在观众中引发了强烈的情感。

2. Psychologists emphasize that the ability to regulate **emotion** effectively is a critical component of healthy interpersonal relationships.
   心理学家强调，有效调节情绪的能力是健康人际关系的关键组成部分。

3. The novelist masterfully conveyed the raw **emotion** of loss and grief through her vivid and deeply resonant prose style.
   这位小说家通过生动且极具共鸣的散文风格巧妙地传达了失去和悲伤的真实情感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-motion" as "energy in motion" — emotions are inner energy that moves you to feel and act.
把"emotion"想成"e-motion（运动中的能量）"——情感是推动你去感受和行动的内在能量。
