# 🌿 shrubbery

🔊 /ˈʃrʌbəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A collection of shrubs; an area planted with shrubs or bushes
**中文：** n. 灌木林

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thicket（灌木丛）
- underbrush（林下灌木）
- bushes（灌木）
- hedgerow（树篱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dense shrubbery / ornamental shrubbery / trim the shrubbery / hide in the shrubbery**

👉 Example: The gardener spent the afternoon pruning the overgrown shrubbery along the estate's perimeter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The landscape architect recommended planting native **shrubbery** to create a natural boundary around the property.
   景观建筑师建议种植本地灌木林，在房产周围营造天然屏障。

2. Dense **shrubbery** along the riverbank provided essential habitat for several endangered bird species.
   河岸边茂密的灌木林为几种濒危鸟类提供了重要的栖息地。

3. The maintenance crew was hired to remove the overgrown **shrubbery** that obscured the building's entrance.
   维护人员受雇清除遮挡建筑入口的杂乱灌木丛。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shrub-bery" as a place full of "shrubs" — just like a bakery is full of baked goods, a shrubbery is full of shrubs.
把"shrubbery"想成"灌木的地方"——就像bakery（面包店）里满是烘焙食品，shrubbery里满是灌木。
