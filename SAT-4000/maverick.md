# 🐂 maverick

🔊 /ˈmævərɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An unbranded calf or yearling; an independent-minded person who refuses to conform to the views of a group. (adj.) Unconventional and independent
**中文：** n. 没打烙印的动物；持不同意见的人 adj. 特立独行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nonconformist（不墨守成规者）
- rebel（叛逆者）
- iconoclast（打破传统者）
- dissenter（持异议者）
- individualist（个人主义者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a political maverick / maverick thinker / maverick approach / maverick spirit / a maverick in the industry**

👉 Example: The senator earned a reputation as a maverick by consistently voting against her own party's leadership.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scientist was considered a **maverick** in her field because she challenged established theories that most researchers accepted without question.
   这位年轻科学家被认为是其领域的特立独行者，因为她挑战了大多数研究者不加质疑就接受的既定理论。

2. His **maverick** approach to business management alienated traditional executives but ultimately led to unprecedented growth and innovation.
   他特立独行的企业管理方式疏远了传统高管，但最终带来了前所未有的增长和创新。

3. Throughout history, many **maverick**s who defied conventional wisdom were initially ridiculed but later celebrated as visionary pioneers.
   纵观历史，许多挑战传统智慧的特立独行者最初被嘲笑，但后来被誉为有远见的先驱。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "maverick" as named after Samuel Maverick, a Texas rancher who refused to brand his cattle — just like a maverick person refuses to be "branded" by any group.
"Maverick"得名于塞缪尔·马维里克，一位拒绝给牛打烙印的得克萨斯牧场主——就像一个特立独行的人拒绝被任何群体"打上标签"。
