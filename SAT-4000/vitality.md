# ⚡ vitality

🔊 /vaɪˈtæləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being strong, active, and full of energy; the power giving continuance of life; liveliness
**中文：** n. 活力，生气；生命力，生动性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vigor（精力）
- energy（活力）
- liveliness（生动）
- dynamism（活力）
- exuberance（充沛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **full of vitality / sense of vitality / restore vitality / economic vitality**

👉 Example: The new leadership brought a renewed sense of vitality to the struggling organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The elderly artist displayed remarkable **vitality**, producing a prolific body of work well into his ninth decade of life.
   这位年迈的艺术家展现了非凡的活力，在他九十多岁时仍创作出了大量作品。

2. Urban planners sought to restore **vitality** to the downtown district by introducing green spaces and pedestrian-friendly infrastructure.
   城市规划者通过引入绿色空间和步行友好型基础设施来恢复市中心的活力。

3. The **vitality** of a democratic society depends upon the active participation and informed engagement of its citizens.
   一个民主社会的生命力取决于其公民的积极参与和知情投入。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vitality" as "vital" + "-ity" (quality of) — vitality is the quality of being vital, the life force that keeps you energized.
把"vitality"想成"vital"（有活力的）+ "-ity"（性质）——vitality就是充满生机的品质，是让你保持精力充沛的生命力。
