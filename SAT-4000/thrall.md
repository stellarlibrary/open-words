# ⛓️ thrall

🔊 /θrɔːl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A slave or captive; the state of being in bondage or servitude. (v.) To enslave or hold in bondage
**中文：** n. 奴隶；束缚；奴役状态 v. 使成为奴隶；奴役

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bondage（束缚）
- servitude（奴役）
- captivity（囚禁）
- enslavement（奴役）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in thrall to / hold in thrall / thrall of power / under the thrall of**

👉 Example: The audience was held in thrall by the speaker's passionate and eloquent address.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation remained in **thrall** to an authoritarian regime that suppressed all forms of political dissent.
   这个国家仍然受制于一个压制一切政治异见的专制政权。

2. Ancient civilizations often kept conquered peoples in **thrall**, forcing them to labor without compensation or freedom.
   古代文明经常将被征服的民族沦为奴隶，迫使他们无偿劳动且没有自由。

3. The philosopher argued that modern consumers are held in **thrall** by relentless advertising and materialistic impulses.
   这位哲学家认为，现代消费者被无休止的广告和物质主义冲动所束缚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "thrall" as rhyming with "crawl" — a slave crawls before their master, completely under their control.
把"thrall"想成和"crawl（爬行）"押韵——奴隶在主人面前匍匐爬行，完全受其控制。
