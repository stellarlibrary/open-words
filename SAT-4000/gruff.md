# 😤 gruff

🔊 /ɡrʌf/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Rough or stern in manner or appearance; hoarse or harsh in voice
**中文：** adj. 粗暴的；粗哑的；脾气坏的；生硬的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brusque（唐突的）
- curt（简短无礼的）
- hoarse（嘶哑的）
- surly（粗暴无礼的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gruff voice / gruff manner / gruff exterior / gruff reply**

👉 Example: Despite his gruff exterior, the old professor genuinely cared about his students' success.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The security guard issued a **gruff** warning to the trespassers, ordering them to vacate the premises immediately.
   保安粗声粗气地警告擅自闯入者，命令他们立即离开该场所。

2. Behind her **gruff** demeanor, the veteran surgeon concealed a profound compassion for every patient she treated.
   在粗暴的外表背后，这位资深外科医生对她治疗的每一位病人都怀有深切的同情。

3. The foreman answered in a **gruff** tone that discouraged further questions from the newly hired construction workers.
   工头用粗哑的语气回答，使新雇的建筑工人不敢再多问问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gruff" as the sound a grumpy dog makes — rough and harsh, just like a gruff person's voice or manner.
把"gruff"想成一只脾气暴躁的狗发出的声音——粗糙而刺耳，就像一个粗暴之人的嗓音或态度。
