# ⚖️ predominate

🔊 /prɪˈdɒmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To be the strongest or main element; to have controlling power or influence; to be most frequent or prominent
**中文：** v. 支配；主宰；在……中占优势

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dominate（支配）
- prevail（盛行）
- outweigh（超过）
- overshadow（使……相形见绌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **predominate over / predominate in / tend to predominate / continue to predominate**

👉 Example: In tropical forests, broad-leaved evergreen trees predominate over other plant species.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Conservative economic policies continued to **predominate** in the region throughout the latter half of the twentieth century.
   保守的经济政策在整个二十世纪下半叶继续主导该地区。

2. Although several theories exist, the evolutionary explanation tends to **predominate** in contemporary biological research.
   尽管存在多种理论，但进化论的解释往往在当代生物学研究中占主导地位。

3. Small family-owned farms still **predominate** in rural areas, despite the rapid expansion of large-scale commercial agriculture.
   尽管大规模商业农业迅速扩张，小型家庭农场在农村地区仍然占主导地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "predominate" as "pre-dominate" — to dominate ahead of everyone else, being the most powerful force in the room.
把"predominate"想成"pre（在前）+ dominate（支配）"——在所有人之前占据支配地位，成为最强大的力量。
