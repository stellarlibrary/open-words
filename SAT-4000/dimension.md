# 📐 dimension

🔊 /dɪˈmɛnʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A measurable extent of a particular kind, such as length, width, or height; an aspect or feature of a situation, problem, or thing
**中文：** n. 方面；维；尺寸；维度；层面

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aspect（方面）
- facet（层面）
- extent（范围）
- measurement（尺寸）
- scope（范围）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **add a new dimension / dimension of the problem / three dimensions / spatial dimension / cultural dimension**

👉 Example: The discovery of the manuscript added an entirely new dimension to the historical investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect carefully calculated every **dimension** of the structure to ensure it complied with local building codes and safety regulations.
   建筑师仔细计算了结构的每个尺寸，以确保其符合当地的建筑法规和安全条例。

2. The study explored the social **dimension** of climate change, examining how rising temperatures disproportionately affect vulnerable communities worldwide.
   该研究探讨了气候变化的社会层面，考察了气温上升如何不成比例地影响全球弱势群体。

3. Physicists theorize that additional hidden **dimensions** beyond the three we perceive may exist at subatomic scales within the universe.
   物理学家推测，在我们所感知的三个维度之外，宇宙中可能存在亚原子尺度上的额外隐藏维度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dimension" as "di-MENTION" — every dimension is worth mentioning because it adds a new way to measure or understand something.
把"dimension"想成"di-MENTION"——每个维度都值得提及，因为它增加了一种衡量或理解事物的新方式。
