# 😊 winsome

🔊 /ˈwɪnsəm/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Attractive or appealing in appearance or character; charming in a fresh, innocent way
**中文：** adj. 迷人的；可爱的；引人注目的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- charming（迷人的）
- endearing（讨人喜欢的）
- captivating（令人着迷的）
- appealing（有吸引力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **winsome smile / winsome personality / winsome charm / winsome manner**

👉 Example: Her winsome smile and genuine warmth immediately put the nervous interview candidates at ease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The actress delivered a **winsome** performance that captivated audiences and earned unanimous praise from even the harshest critics.
   这位女演员献上了一场迷人的表演，吸引了观众，甚至赢得了最严苛评论家的一致赞扬。

2. The diplomat's **winsome** manner and gracious demeanor proved invaluable in building trust among formerly hostile negotiating parties.
   这位外交官迷人的举止和优雅的风度在在formerly敌对的谈判各方之间建立信任方面被证明是无价的。

3. With a **winsome** blend of humor and sincerity, the keynote speaker transformed a routine conference into a memorable event.
   凭借幽默与真诚的迷人结合，主旨演讲者将一次例行会议变成了一场令人难忘的活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "win" + "some" — someone so charming they can win some hearts effortlessly with their winsome personality.
把"winsome"想成"win（赢得）+ some（一些）"——一个如此迷人的人能够轻松地用他迷人的个性赢得一些人心。
