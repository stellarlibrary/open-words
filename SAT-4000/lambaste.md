# 💥 lambaste

🔊 /læmˈbeɪst/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To criticize harshly or severely; to beat or thrash someone
**中文：** v. 痛打；严责；鞭打

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- berate（严厉斥责）
- castigate（严惩）
- censure（谴责）
- thrash（痛打）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lambaste someone for / lambaste a policy / publicly lambaste / lambaste the decision**

👉 Example: The editorial lambasted the administration for its failure to address the housing crisis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator **lambasted** the proposed budget for its drastic cuts to public education and healthcare funding.
   这位参议员严厉抨击了拟议预算中对公共教育和医疗拨款的大幅削减。

2. Critics **lambasted** the director's latest film, calling it a superficial and poorly executed adaptation of the novel.
   评论家们严厉批评了这位导演的最新电影，称其是对原著肤浅且拙劣的改编。

3. The coach **lambasted** the team after their embarrassing defeat, demanding greater discipline and commitment in practice.
   教练在球队遭遇令人尴尬的失败后严厉训斥了他们，要求在训练中更加自律和投入。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lamb" + "baste" — imagine basting a lamb so aggressively that it becomes a beating, linking to harsh criticism.
把"lambaste"想成"lamb（羊）"和"baste（涂油）"——想象猛烈地给羊涂油，就像在痛打和严厉批评。
