# ⚖️ condemn

🔊 /kənˈdɛm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To express strong disapproval of; to sentence someone to a particular punishment; to officially declare unfit for use
**中文：** v. 谴责；指责；判刑；定罪；宣告不适用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- denounce（谴责）
- censure（责难）
- sentence（判刑）
- reproach（指责）
- castigate（严厉批评）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **condemn someone for / condemn someone to / strongly condemn / condemn violence / condemn a building**

👉 Example: World leaders unanimously condemned the use of chemical weapons against civilian populations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international community swiftly moved to **condemn** the unprovoked military aggression as a flagrant violation of international law.
   国际社会迅速采取行动谴责这一无端军事侵略，视其为对国际法的公然违反。

2. The court **condemned** the convicted spy to life imprisonment without the possibility of parole for committing acts of treason.
   法院判处这名被定罪的间谍终身监禁且不得假释，因其犯有叛国罪。

3. City inspectors **condemned** the dilapidated apartment building after determining that its structural integrity posed a serious safety hazard.
   城市检查员在确定该破旧公寓楼的结构完整性构成严重安全隐患后，宣布其不宜居住。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con + demn (damn)" — to condemn someone is to damn them, expressing the strongest disapproval or punishment.
把"condemn"想成"con + demn（damn，诅咒）"——condemn某人就是谴责或惩罚他们，表达最强烈的不满。
