# ❤️ artery

🔊 /ˈɑːrtəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A blood vessel that carries blood from the heart to body tissues; a major road or route in a transportation network
**中文：** n. 动脉；干道

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vessel（血管）
- thoroughfare（大道）
- highway（公路）
- main road（主干道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **main artery / blocked artery / arterial road / artery of commerce**

👉 Example: The coronary artery supplies oxygen-rich blood directly to the heart muscle.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The surgeon performed a delicate procedure to clear the blocked **artery** and restore normal blood flow to the heart.
   外科医生进行了一项精细手术，以疏通堵塞的动脉并恢复心脏的正常血流。

2. The newly constructed expressway serves as a vital **artery** connecting the industrial zone to the international shipping port.
   新建的高速公路是连接工业区和国际航运港口的重要干道。

3. Researchers found that regular aerobic exercise significantly reduces the risk of **artery** disease in middle-aged adults.
   研究人员发现，定期有氧运动能显著降低中年人患动脉疾病的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "artery" as "art + ery" — arteries are the masterpiece (art) of the body's circulation, the essential pathways of life.
把"artery"想成"art（艺术）+ ery"——动脉是人体循环系统的杰作，是生命的重要通道。
