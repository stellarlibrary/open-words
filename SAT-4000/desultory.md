# 🌀 desultory

🔊 /ˈdɛsəlˌtɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking a plan, purpose, or enthusiasm; occurring randomly or intermittently
**中文：** adj. 散漫的；不连贯的；断断续续的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- haphazard（随意的）
- aimless（漫无目的的）
- random（随机的）
- sporadic（断断续续的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **desultory conversation / desultory attempt / desultory manner / desultory reading**

👉 Example: Their desultory conversation drifted from one trivial topic to another without any clear direction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor criticized the student's **desultory** approach to research, noting a complete lack of systematic methodology.
   教授批评了这位学生散漫的研究方法，指出完全缺乏系统的方法论。

2. After several **desultory** attempts to negotiate a ceasefire, both parties agreed to seek formal mediation from a neutral state.
   在数次断断续续的停火谈判尝试之后，双方同意寻求中立国的正式调解。

3. The audience grew restless during the **desultory** lecture, which meandered through unrelated topics without any coherent thesis.
   观众在这场散漫的演讲中变得焦躁不安，演讲在不相关的话题间游荡，没有连贯的论点。
