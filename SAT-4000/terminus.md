# 🚉 terminus

🔊 /ˈtɜːrmɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The final point or end of something; the last station on a railway or bus route
**中文：** 终点；终点站；末端

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- endpoint（终点）
- destination（目的地）
- terminal（终端站）
- conclusion（结尾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bus terminus / railway terminus / reach the terminus / northern terminus**

👉 Example: The train reached the terminus at midnight, and all passengers were required to disembark promptly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient Roman road stretched for hundreds of miles before reaching its **terminus** at the fortified coastal settlement.
   这条古罗马道路绵延数百英里，最终到达其终点——一个设防的沿海定居点。

2. Passengers arriving at the **terminus** must transfer to local bus services to continue their journey northward.
   到达终点站的旅客必须换乘当地公交服务继续向北行程。

3. The southern **terminus** of the newly constructed highway connects directly to the international shipping port and logistics hub.
   新建高速公路的南部终点直接连接国际航运港口和物流中心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "terminus" as the place where everything "terminates" — the very last stop on the line.
把"terminus"想成一切"terminate（终止）"的地方——线路上的最后一站。
