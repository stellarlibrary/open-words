# ✨ intriguing

🔊 /ɪnˈtriːɡɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Arousing great curiosity or interest; fascinating and captivating
**中文：** adj. 有趣的；迷人的；引起兴趣的；有迷惑力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fascinating（迷人的）
- captivating（吸引人的）
- compelling（引人注目的）
- engrossing（令人入迷的）
- alluring（诱人的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intriguing question / intriguing possibility / intriguing idea / intriguing discovery**

👉 Example: The researcher presented an intriguing hypothesis that challenged conventional thinking about memory formation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeologists made an **intriguing** discovery that could fundamentally reshape our understanding of ancient civilizations.
   考古学家们做出了一项迷人的发现，可能从根本上改变我们对古代文明的认识。

2. Her **intriguing** proposal attracted the attention of several prominent investors eager to fund the innovative project.
   她引人入胜的提案吸引了几位知名投资者的关注，他们渴望资助这一创新项目。

3. The documentary raised **intriguing** questions about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence in modern healthcare.
   这部纪录片提出了关于人工智能在现代医疗中伦理影响的引人深思的问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "intriguing" as related to "intrigue" — something that intrigues you pulls you in like a mystery, making you curious.
把"intriguing"与"intrigue（阴谋、激起好奇心）"联系起来——让你着迷的事物像谜团一样吸引你，令你充满好奇。
