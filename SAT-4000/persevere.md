# 💪 persevere

🔊 /ˌpɜːrsəˈvɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To continue in a course of action despite difficulty or lack of success; to persist steadfastly
**中文：** v. 坚持不懈；锲而不舍；固执己见（在辩论中）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- persist（坚持）
- endure（忍耐）
- press on（继续前进）
- carry on（坚持下去）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **persevere in / persevere with / persevere through / persevere despite obstacles**

👉 Example: She persevered with her doctoral research despite numerous setbacks and funding difficulties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scientist chose to **persevere** with her groundbreaking research even after three consecutive grant applications were rejected.
   这位年轻科学家在连续三次拨款申请被拒后仍选择坚持她的开创性研究。

2. Immigrants who **persevere** through the arduous naturalization process often develop a profound appreciation for their adopted country.
   坚持不懈地通过艰苦入籍程序的移民往往对他们的移居国产生深厚的感激之情。

3. The committee urged all participants to **persevere** in their efforts to reach a diplomatic consensus before the deadline.
   委员会敦促所有与会者在截止日期前坚持不懈地努力达成外交共识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-SEVERE" — going through severe hardship per (through) every challenge, yet never giving up.
把"persevere"想成"per-SEVERE"——经历每一个严峻的挑战，却永不放弃。
