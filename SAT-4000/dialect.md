# 🗣️ dialect

🔊 /ˈdaɪəlɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A particular form of a language spoken in a specific region or by a specific social group, distinguished by vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation
**中文：** n. 方言，土话

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vernacular（方言）
- patois（土语）
- idiom（语言特色）
- regional speech（地方语言）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **local dialect / regional dialect / speak in dialect / dialect of a language**

👉 Example: The linguist spent years documenting the endangered dialect spoken in remote mountain villages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novel's authentic use of **dialect** vividly captured the cultural identity of the rural Southern community.
   这部小说对方言的真实运用生动地捕捉了南方农村社区的文化特征。

2. Researchers discovered that the isolated island **dialect** preserved grammatical features lost in the standard language centuries ago.
   研究人员发现，这种孤立的岛屿方言保留了数百年前在标准语言中已消失的语法特征。

3. Students of linguistics must learn to analyze **dialect** variation without imposing value judgments on any speech community.
   语言学专业的学生必须学会分析方言差异，而不对任何言语社区施加价值判断。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dialect" as "dia-" (between) + "lect" (speak) — a way of speaking that varies between different groups or regions.
把"dialect"想成"dia-"（之间）+"lect"（说）——在不同群体或地区之间有所不同的说话方式。
