# 🌀 labyrinth

🔊 /ˈlæbərɪnθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A complicated network of winding passages or paths; a maze; any complex and confusing arrangement or situation
**中文：** n. 迷宫；迷路；难解的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- maze（迷宫）
- tangle（纠缠）
- web（网络）
- complexity（复杂事物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a labyrinth of corridors / labyrinth of regulations / navigate the labyrinth / lost in a labyrinth**

👉 Example: The ancient palace was designed as an elaborate labyrinth to confuse and trap unwelcome intruders.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new employee struggled to navigate the **labyrinth** of bureaucratic procedures required to obtain proper security clearance.
   新员工在为获得适当安全许可而必须通过的官僚程序迷宫中苦苦挣扎。

2. Archaeologists discovered a vast underground **labyrinth** beneath the ruins that may have served as a ceremonial temple complex.
   考古学家在废墟下方发现了一个巨大的地下迷宫，它可能曾用作仪式神殿建筑群。

3. The legal scholar published a comprehensive guide to help citizens through the **labyrinth** of international trade regulations.
   这位法学学者出版了一本综合指南，帮助公民理解国际贸易法规的复杂体系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the Greek myth of the Labyrinth — the maze built by Daedalus to contain the Minotaur, a place so complex that no one could find the way out.
想想希腊神话中的迷宫——代达罗斯为关住牛头怪建造的迷宫，复杂到没人能找到出路。
