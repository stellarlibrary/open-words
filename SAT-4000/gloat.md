# 😏 gloat

🔊 /ɡloʊt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To dwell on one's own success or another's misfortune with smugness or malicious pleasure
**中文：** v. 幸灾乐祸；心满意足地注视；沾沾自喜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exult（狂喜）
- revel（陶醉）
- crow（得意洋洋）
- triumph（得意扬扬）
- gloat over（对……幸灾乐祸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gloat over / gloat about / gloat at someone's expense**

👉 Example: It is considered poor sportsmanship to gloat over a defeated opponent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rival company began to **gloat** publicly after their competitor's product recall made national headlines.
   竞争对手公司在其对手的产品召回成为全国新闻后开始公开幸灾乐祸。

2. Rather than **gloat** about her perfect score, the student graciously offered to help classmates who had struggled.
   这位学生没有对自己的满分沾沾自喜，而是大方地提出帮助那些考得不好的同学。

3. The editorial warned that politicians who **gloat** over their opponents' failures often lose public sympathy in return.
   社论警告说，那些对对手失败幸灾乐祸的政客往往会因此失去公众的同情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gloat" as "glow + oat" — imagine someone glowing with satisfaction while eating oats they stole from someone else.
把"gloat"想成"glow（发光）+ oat（燕麦）"——想象一个人一边吃着从别人那里偷来的燕麦，一边满脸得意地发光。
