# 🪞 identical

🔊 /aɪˈdɛntɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Exactly alike; being the very same; showing no differences whatsoever
**中文：** adj. 同一的；完全相同的；相等的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indistinguishable（无法区分的）
- equivalent（等同的）
- matching（匹配的）
- uniform（一致的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **identical to / identical twins / identical copies / virtually identical**

👉 Example: The two documents were virtually identical, differing only in the date printed on the cover page.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Forensic analysts confirmed that the fingerprints found at the crime scene were **identical** to those already stored in the database.
   法医分析人员确认犯罪现场发现的指纹与数据库中已存储的指纹完全相同。

2. The architect produced two **identical** blueprints so that both the contractor and the client could review the design simultaneously.
   建筑师制作了两份完全相同的蓝图，以便承包商和客户能同时审查设计。

3. Although raised in different households, the **identical** twins displayed remarkably similar behavioral patterns throughout their adolescent years.
   尽管在不同家庭中长大，这对同卵双胞胎在整个青少年时期表现出了极为相似的行为模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "identity" — things that are identical share the same identity, as if they are one and the same.
想想"identity（身份）"——完全相同的事物共享同一个身份，仿佛它们就是同一个东西。
