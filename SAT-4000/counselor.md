# ⚖️ counselor

🔊 /ˈkaʊnsələr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who gives advice or guidance, especially professionally; a legal adviser or lawyer
**中文：** n. 顾问；法律顾问；律师

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adviser（顾问）
- consultant（咨询师）
- mentor（导师）
- attorney（律师）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **school counselor / legal counselor / guidance counselor / camp counselor**

👉 Example: The guidance counselor helped students select courses that aligned with their career aspirations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The academic **counselor** recommended that the student enroll in advanced placement courses to strengthen her college application.
   学术顾问建议该学生注册大学先修课程，以增强她的大学申请竞争力。

2. As a licensed **counselor**, he specialized in helping adolescents navigate the emotional challenges associated with family transitions.
   作为一名持证顾问，他专门帮助青少年应对与家庭变迁相关的情感挑战。

3. The defense **counselor** presented compelling evidence that ultimately persuaded the jury to acquit the defendant of all charges.
   辩护律师提供了令人信服的证据，最终说服陪审团宣判被告所有罪名不成立。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "counselor" as "counsel + or" — one who gives counsel (advice), like a wise guide helping you make important decisions.
把"counselor"想成"counsel（建议）+ or（人）"——给出建议的人，就像一位智慧的向导帮助你做重要决定。
