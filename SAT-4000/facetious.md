# 😄 facetious

🔊 /fəˈsiːʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not meant to be taken seriously; treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor or flippancy
**中文：** 诙谐的；爱开玩笑的；滑稽的；（不合时宜地）幽默的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- flippant（轻率的）
- jocular（诙谐的）
- witty（机智的）
- tongue-in-cheek（不认真的）
- humorous（幽默的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **facetious remark / facetious comment / facetious tone / being facetious**

👉 Example: His facetious remark about the budget deficit did not amuse the committee members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor warned students not to make **facetious** comments during the formal academic presentation on climate change.
   教授警告学生不要在关于气候变化的正式学术报告中发表诙谐的评论。

2. Although she was being **facetious**, her colleagues mistakenly interpreted the remark as a serious policy recommendation.
   虽然她只是在开玩笑，但同事们错误地将这番话理解为严肃的政策建议。

3. The committee chair dismissed his **facetious** suggestion and urged all members to focus on substantive legislative proposals.
   委员会主席驳回了他那不严肃的建议，并敦促所有成员关注实质性立法提案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "face-tious" as making a funny "face" — someone who is facetious puts on a humorous face even in serious situations.
把"facetious"想成做鬼"脸"(face)——一个诙谐的人即使在严肃场合也会做出幽默的表情。
