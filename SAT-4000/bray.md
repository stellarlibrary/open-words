# 🫏 bray

🔊 /breɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make the loud, harsh cry of a donkey; to crush or grind into small pieces. (n.) The loud, harsh cry of a donkey; a blaring sound like a trumpet
**中文：** v. 嘶叫；捣碎 n. 驴叫声；喇叭声

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- neigh（嘶鸣）
- blare（嘟嘟响）
- bellow（吼叫）
- honk（鸣叫）
- clamor（喧嚣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bray loudly / the bray of a donkey / bray like a mule / bray of trumpets**

👉 Example: The donkey began to bray at dawn, waking everyone in the small village.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distant **bray** of the donkey echoed through the valley, signaling the approach of the merchant caravan at sunrise.
   远处驴子的嘶叫声回荡在山谷中，预示着商队在日出时分的到来。

2. Medieval apothecaries would **bray** herbs and minerals in a stone mortar to produce the fine powders used in remedies.
   中世纪的药剂师会在石臼中捣碎草药和矿物，以制成药剂所用的细粉。

3. The triumphant **bray** of the brass instruments filled the concert hall, marking the dramatic climax of the symphony.
   铜管乐器高亢的嘟嘟声充满了音乐厅，标志着交响乐戏剧性高潮的到来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bray" rhyming with "hey" — a donkey saying "hey" in its loud, harsh voice is braying at you.
把"bray"和"hey"联想在一起——一头驴用它响亮刺耳的声音对你喊"hey"，就是在bray。
