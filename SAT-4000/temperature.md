# 🌡️ temperature

🔊 /ˈtɛmpərətʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The degree of heat or cold in a body or environment; a measure of bodily warmth above normal; fever
**中文：** n. 温度；体温；气温；发烧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- heat（热度）
- warmth（温暖）
- fever（发烧）
- climate（气候）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **room temperature / body temperature / temperature rises / temperature drops / take one's temperature**

👉 Example: The doctor took the patient's temperature to determine whether an infection was present.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Global average **temperature** has risen significantly over the past century due to increased greenhouse gas emissions.
   由于温室气体排放增加，过去一个世纪全球平均温度显著上升。

2. The nurse recorded the patient's **temperature** every four hours to monitor the progression of the viral infection.
   护士每四小时记录一次病人的体温，以监测病毒感染的发展情况。

3. Researchers found that even a slight increase in ocean **temperature** can disrupt fragile marine ecosystems and coral habitats.
   研究人员发现，即使海洋温度略有升高，也会破坏脆弱的海洋生态系统和珊瑚栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temper-ature" — the "temper" of the air, how hot or cold it feels, measured as a numerical "ature" (nature).
把"temperature"想成空气的"脾气"——空气有多热或多冷，就是温度的本质。
