# 🎯 exact

🔊 /ɪɡˈzækt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Precise, accurate, and without error. (v.) To demand and obtain something, especially a payment or penalty, by force or authority
**中文：** adj. 准确的，精密的；精确的 v. 强求；急需，要求

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- precise（精确的）
- accurate（准确的）
- demand（要求）
- extract（榨取）
- impose（强加）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exact revenge / exact a toll / exact a price / the exact moment / exact measurements**

👉 Example: The prolonged war exacted a devastating toll on the civilian population of the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective required **exact** measurements of the crime scene to reconstruct the sequence of events with scientific precision.
   侦探需要犯罪现场的精确测量数据，以科学的精度重建事件的经过。

2. History has shown that authoritarian regimes often **exact** severe punishments on those who dare to voice political dissent.
   历史表明，专制政权往往对那些敢于表达政治异议的人施以严厉惩罚。

3. The natural disaster **exacted** an enormous economic cost that would take the developing nation several decades to recover from.
   这场自然灾害造成了巨大的经济损失，使这个发展中国家需要数十年才能恢复。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exact" as "ex-act" — to act out precisely, or to act upon someone by demanding what is owed with no room for error.
把"exact"想成"ex-act"——精确地执行，或对某人采取行动，分毫不差地强求所欠之物。
