# 🔍 analyze

🔊 /ˈænəlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To examine something methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it; to break down into components for study
**中文：** 分析，分解

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examine（检查）
- evaluate（评估）
- dissect（剖析）
- scrutinize（审视）
- investigate（调查）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **analyze data / analyze the results / carefully analyze / analyze the impact / analyze a problem / critically analyze**

👉 Example: Scientists must carefully analyze the experimental data before drawing any definitive conclusions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team spent several months attempting to **analyze** the complex genetic sequences responsible for the inherited disorder.
   研究团队花了几个月试图分析导致该遗传性疾病的复杂基因序列。

2. Economists will **analyze** the impact of the new trade agreement on domestic manufacturing and international supply chain dynamics.
   经济学家将分析新贸易协定对国内制造业和国际供应链动态的影响。

3. The detective carefully **analyzed** the forensic evidence collected at the scene to reconstruct the precise sequence of events.
   侦探仔细分析了在现场收集的法医证据，以重建事件的精确顺序。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "analyze" as "ana- (apart) + -lyze (loosen)" — to loosen something apart, breaking it into smaller pieces to understand each component.
把"analyze"理解为"ana-（分开）+ -lyze（松开）"——把事物松解分开，将其拆分成更小的部分以理解每个组成部分。
