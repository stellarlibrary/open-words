# 📏 diameter

🔊 /daɪˈæmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A straight line passing through the center of a circle or sphere, connecting two points on the circumference; the length of such a line
**中文：** n. 直径

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- breadth（宽度）
- width（宽度）
- caliber（口径）
- span（跨度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in diameter / diameter of a circle / measure the diameter / inner diameter / outer diameter**

👉 Example: The telescope's mirror has a diameter of ten meters, making it one of the largest in the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient redwood tree measured over seven meters in **diameter** at the base of its massive trunk.
   这棵古老的红杉树在其巨大树干的底部直径超过七米。

2. Engineers calculated that the pipeline required a minimum **diameter** of thirty centimeters to maintain adequate flow rates.
   工程师计算出管道需要至少三十厘米的直径才能维持足够的流量。

3. The **diameter** of the newly discovered exoplanet is approximately twice that of Earth, according to recent observations.
   根据最新观测，这颗新发现的系外行星的直径大约是地球的两倍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "diameter" as "dia-" (across) + "meter" (measure) — a measurement that goes all the way across a circle through its center.
把"diameter"想成"dia-"（穿过）+"meter"（测量）——一条穿过圆心、横跨整个圆的测量线。
