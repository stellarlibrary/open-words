# 🚨 urgent

🔊 /ˈɜːrdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Requiring immediate action or attention; conveying a pressing or compelling sense of importance
**中文：** adj. 紧急的；急迫的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pressing（紧迫的）
- imperative（迫切的）
- critical（关键的）
- compelling（紧要的）
- acute（急迫的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **urgent matter / urgent need / urgent request / urgent attention / urgent care**

👉 Example: The committee issued an urgent call for additional funding to support disaster relief operations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital received an **urgent** request for blood donations after the earthquake devastated several communities in the region.
   地震摧毁了该地区多个社区后，医院收到了紧急的献血请求。

2. World leaders acknowledged the **urgent** necessity of establishing enforceable international standards for artificial intelligence governance.
   世界各国领导人承认建立可执行的人工智能治理国际标准的紧迫必要性。

3. The professor sent an **urgent** memorandum to the administration warning that laboratory safety protocols needed immediate revision.
   教授向行政部门发送了一份紧急备忘录，警告实验室安全规程需要立即修订。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "urgent" as containing "urge" — when something is urgent, it urges you to act without delay.
把"urgent"想成包含"urge"（催促）——当某事是紧急的，它催促你毫不拖延地行动。
