# 🧂 saline

🔊 /ˈseɪliːn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of, relating to, or containing salt. (n.) A salt lake; a saline solution or purgative
**中文：** adj. 盐的；含盐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- salty（咸的）
- brackish（微咸的）
- briny（含盐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **saline solution / saline water / saline drip / saline environment**

👉 Example: The nurse administered a saline solution intravenously to help the dehydrated patient recover fluids.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctor prescribed a **saline** nasal spray to alleviate the patient's chronic sinus congestion during the dry winter months.
   医生开了含盐鼻喷雾剂来缓解患者在干燥冬季的慢性鼻窦充血。

2. Researchers discovered that certain organisms thrive in extremely **saline** environments where most other life forms cannot survive.
   研究人员发现某些生物在大多数其他生命形式无法生存的极端含盐环境中茁壮成长。

3. The geologist analyzed the **saline** deposits found beneath the desert surface to determine the region's ancient hydrological history.
   地质学家分析了沙漠地表下发现的盐矿沉积物，以确定该地区古老的水文历史。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sal-ine" where "sal" is the Latin root for salt — saline simply means something related to or containing salt.
把"saline"想成"sal（拉丁语中的盐）+ ine"——saline就是与盐有关的、含盐的意思。
