# 😐 prosaic

🔊 /proʊˈzeɪɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking poetic beauty or imagination; dull and ordinary; of or resembling prose rather than poetry
**中文：** adj. 散文的，散文体的；乏味的，平凡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mundane（平凡的）
- pedestrian（平淡的）
- banal（陈腐的）
- commonplace（普通的）
- unimaginative（缺乏想象力的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prosaic reality / prosaic details / prosaic language / prosaic existence**

👉 Example: After years of adventurous travel, she found the prosaic routine of suburban life unbearably dull.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novelist's early work was criticized for its **prosaic** style, which lacked the vivid imagery readers had expected.
   这位小说家的早期作品因其平淡的风格而受到批评，缺乏读者所期望的生动意象。

2. Behind the company's glamorous marketing campaigns lay the **prosaic** reality of long hours and modest salaries.
   在公司光鲜的营销活动背后，是加班加点和微薄薪水的平凡现实。

3. The professor urged students to move beyond **prosaic** descriptions and develop more analytical and creative arguments in their essays.
   教授敦促学生在论文中超越平淡的描述，发展更具分析性和创造性的论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prosaic" as "prose-like" — prose is the ordinary form of writing, unlike poetry, so prosaic means dull and ordinary.
把"prosaic"想成"像散文一样的"——散文是普通的写作形式，不像诗歌，所以prosaic意为平凡乏味的。
