# 🔓 larceny

🔊 /ˈlɑːrsəni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The unlawful taking of personal property with the intent to permanently deprive the rightful owner; theft
**中文：** n. 盗窃；盗窃罪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- theft（偷盗）
- stealing（偷窃）
- robbery（抢劫）
- pilferage（小偷小摸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grand larceny / petty larceny / charged with larceny / commit larceny**

👉 Example: The suspect was charged with grand larceny after stealing merchandise valued at over ten thousand dollars.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The district attorney decided to prosecute the case as grand **larceny** because the stolen goods exceeded the statutory threshold.
   地区检察官决定以重大盗窃罪起诉此案，因为被盗物品的价值超过了法定标准。

2. Petty **larceny** remains one of the most frequently reported property crimes in densely populated urban areas.
   小额盗窃仍然是人口密集城市地区最常报告的财产犯罪之一。

3. The employee was terminated and charged with **larceny** after surveillance footage revealed systematic embezzlement of company funds.
   监控录像揭露了系统性挪用公司资金的行为后，该员工被解雇并被指控盗窃罪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "larceny" as "lar-SEEN-y" — imagine a thief who was "seen" stealing from a "lair." The crime of stealing from someone's lair is larceny.
把"larceny"想成"lair（巢穴）+ seen（被看见）"——想象一个小偷被人看见从巢穴里偷东西，这种偷窃行为就是larceny。
