# 😤 loathe

🔊 /loʊð/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To feel intense dislike or disgust for someone or something; to detest utterly
**中文：** v. 厌恶；憎恶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detest（憎恨）
- abhor（痛恨）
- despise（蔑视）
- abominate（厌恶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **loathe doing something / loathe the idea of / utterly loathe / loathe oneself**

👉 Example: She loathed the monotonous routine of her corporate job and longed for creative freedom.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned critic openly **loathed** the film, calling it a superficial and uninspired exercise in commercial filmmaking.
   这位知名评论家公开表示厌恶这部电影，称其为一次肤浅且缺乏灵感的商业电影制作。

2. Many citizens **loathe** the bureaucratic inefficiency that plagues government agencies and delays essential public services.
   许多公民厌恶困扰政府机构并延误基本公共服务的官僚低效问题。

3. He **loathed** public speaking so intensely that he would decline every invitation to present at professional conferences.
   他如此强烈地厌恶公开演讲，以至于拒绝了每一次在专业会议上发表演讲的邀请。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "loathe" as "load + the" — the emotional load of hatred is so heavy it weighs you down with disgust.
把"loathe"想成"load（负担）+ the"——厌恶的情感负担如此沉重，令人作呕不堪。
