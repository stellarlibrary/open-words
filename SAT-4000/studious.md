# 📚 studious

🔊 /ˈstjuːdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Devoted to study and learning; showing great care and attention; diligent in academic pursuits
**中文：** adj. 好学的；刻苦的，勤奋的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diligent（勤勉的）
- scholarly（学术的）
- industrious（勤奋的）
- assiduous（刻苦的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **studious effort / studious attention / studious silence / studious approach**

👉 Example: The studious attention she gave to every footnote earned her a reputation for thoroughness among her peers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **studious** young scholar spent countless hours in the university library examining primary sources from the medieval period.
   这位勤奋的年轻学者在大学图书馆花了无数个小时研究中世纪的原始资料。

2. Her **studious** approach to the complex legal case revealed critical inconsistencies that other attorneys had entirely overlooked.
   她对这个复杂法律案件的认真研究揭示了其他律师完全忽略的关键矛盾之处。

3. The committee maintained a **studious** silence throughout the hearing, carefully absorbing every detail of the testimony presented.
   委员会在整个听证会期间保持着刻意的沉默，仔细吸收所呈现证词的每一个细节。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "studi-ous" as "study + ous" — full of study, someone who is always studying and devoted to learning.
把"studious"想成"study + ous"——充满学习的，一个总是在学习、致力于求知的人。
