# ⚖️ egalitarian

🔊 /ɪˌɡælɪˈtɛəriən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or believing in the principle that all people are equal and deserve equal rights and opportunities. (n.) A person who advocates for equality
**中文：** adj. 平等主义的 n. 平等主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equitable（公平的）
- democratic（民主的）
- impartial（公正的）
- classless（无阶级的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **egalitarian society / egalitarian principles / egalitarian ideals / egalitarian approach**

👉 Example: The nation's constitution enshrines egalitarian principles that guarantee equal treatment under the law.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that a truly **egalitarian** society must ensure equal access to education and healthcare for all citizens.
   这位哲学家认为，一个真正平等主义的社会必须确保所有公民平等地获得教育和医疗。

2. Several Nordic countries are frequently cited as models of **egalitarian** governance due to their progressive social policies.
   几个北欧国家因其进步的社会政策而经常被引为平等主义治理的典范。

3. The organization adopted an **egalitarian** structure in which every member possessed an equal vote on institutional decisions.
   该组织采用了平等主义的结构，每位成员在机构决策上都拥有平等的投票权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "EQUAL-itarian" — an egalitarian believes in equality for all, just like the word "equal" hidden inside it.
把"egalitarian"想成"EQUAL-itarian"——一个egalitarian的人相信人人平等，就像里面藏着"equal"（平等）这个词。
