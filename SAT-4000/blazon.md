# 📜 blazon

🔊 /ˈbleɪzən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To proclaim widely; to display conspicuously; to adorn or embellish. (n.) A coat of arms; a detailed and accurate depiction or description.
**中文：** v. 广布，宣布；夸示；装饰 n. 纹章；细致准确的绘制或描述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proclaim（宣布）
- emblazon（装饰）
- herald（宣告）
- display（展示）
- publicize（公布）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blazon abroad / blazon across / blazon forth / blazon one's achievements**

👉 Example: The tabloid newspapers blazoned the scandal across their front pages for weeks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The medieval knight's shield was **blazoned** with elaborate heraldic symbols representing his noble ancestry and valor.
   这位中世纪骑士的盾牌上装饰着精致的纹章符号，代表着他高贵的血统和英勇。

2. The corporation **blazoned** its philanthropic contributions across every media platform to enhance its public reputation.
   该公司在每个媒体平台上大肆宣扬其慈善捐赠，以提升其公众声誉。

3. The artist's name was **blazoned** on banners throughout the city in anticipation of her landmark exhibition.
   在她具有里程碑意义的展览到来之前，这位艺术家的名字被醒目地展示在全城的横幅上。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "blazon" as "blaze on" — to blaze something on a banner or shield, making it visible for all to see.
把"blazon"想成"blaze on"——把某物光彩夺目地展示在旗帜或盾牌上，让所有人都能看到。
