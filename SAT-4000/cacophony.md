# 🥁 cacophony

🔊 /kəˈkɒfəni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A harsh, discordant mixture of sounds; an unpleasant combination of loud, jarring noises
**中文：** n. 刺耳的音调；不和谐音；杂音

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- discord（不和谐音）
- dissonance（不协调）
- din（喧嚣）
- clamor（喧闹）
- racket（噪音）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a cacophony of sounds / cacophony of voices / cacophony of horns / amid the cacophony**

👉 Example: The marketplace was filled with a cacophony of vendors shouting, engines rumbling, and dogs barking.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **cacophony** of competing voices erupted in the legislative chamber as delegates fiercely debated the controversial amendment.
   当代表们激烈辩论这项争议性修正案时，立法厅内爆发出一片嘈杂的争论声。

2. The urban neighborhood's constant **cacophony** of traffic, sirens, and construction drove many longtime residents to seek quieter surroundings.
   城市社区持续不断的交通噪音、警笛声和施工声驱使许多老住户去寻找更安静的环境。

3. The orchestra's warm-up produced a brief **cacophony** before the conductor raised the baton and silence fell across the hall.
   乐团的热身演奏制造了短暂的杂音，随后指挥举起指挥棒，大厅归于寂静。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "caco-phony" — "caco" means bad and "phony" sounds like "phone." Imagine a bad phone connection with terrible, garbled sounds — that is cacophony.
想想"caco-phony"——"caco"意为坏的，"phony"听起来像"phone"（电话）。想象一个信号很差的电话里传出可怕的杂乱声音——那就是cacophony。
