# 🌿 exuberant

🔊 /ɪɡˈzuːbərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Full of energy, excitement, and cheerfulness; growing luxuriantly or profusely
**中文：** adj. 繁茂的；生气勃勃的；兴高采烈的，热情洋溢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ebullient（热情洋溢的）
- exhilarated（兴奋的）
- lush（繁茂的）
- vivacious（活泼的）
- effervescent（兴高采烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exuberant growth / exuberant mood / exuberant celebration / exuberant personality**

👉 Example: The exuberant crowd filled the stadium with thunderous applause after the winning goal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tropical rainforest displayed **exuberant** vegetation, with towering canopies and dense undergrowth covering every surface.
   热带雨林展现出繁茂的植被，高耸的树冠和茂密的灌木覆盖了每一寸地面。

2. Her **exuberant** enthusiasm for the research project inspired the entire team to work with renewed dedication.
   她对研究项目热情洋溢的态度激励了整个团队以新的热忱投入工作。

3. The **exuberant** children celebrated the last day of school by running through the halls cheering and laughing.
   兴高采烈的孩子们在学校最后一天欢呼雀跃地跑过走廊庆祝。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exuberant" as containing "uber" (meaning super) — someone exuberant has super energy and enthusiasm overflowing.
把"exuberant"想成包含"uber"（超级的意思）——一个exuberant的人拥有超级多的能量和热情，满溢而出。
