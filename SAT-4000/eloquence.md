# 🗣️ eloquence

🔊 /ˈɛləkwəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Fluent, persuasive, and expressive speech or writing; the art or power of using language effectively and movingly
**中文：** n. 口才；雄辩；流利的表达能力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fluency（流利）
- articulateness（善于表达）
- rhetoric（修辞）
- expressiveness（表现力）
- oratory（演说术）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natural eloquence / great eloquence / speak with eloquence / eloquence of expression / eloquence and passion**

👉 Example: The diplomat's eloquence was instrumental in brokering the historic peace agreement between the two nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The civil rights leader's **eloquence** inspired millions of people to join the movement for justice and social equality.
   这位民权领袖的雄辩口才激励了数百万人加入争取正义和社会平等的运动。

2. Her natural **eloquence** made her the most effective advocate for educational reform that the committee had ever witnessed.
   她天生的口才使她成为委员会所见过的最有力的教育改革倡导者。

3. The **eloquence** of the closing argument persuaded the jury to reach a unanimous verdict in favor of the defendant.
   结案陈词的雄辩说服了陪审团一致做出有利于被告的裁决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eloquence" as "elo-quence" — "elo" sounds like "hello," and someone with eloquence says "hello" to an audience so beautifully that everyone is captivated.
把"eloquence"想成"elo-quence"——"elo"听起来像"hello"，有口才的人向观众说"hello"时如此优美，让每个人都为之着迷。
