# 😤 nettle

🔊 /ˈnɛtəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To irritate or annoy; to provoke or sting. (n.) A wild plant with stinging hairs on its leaves and stems
**中文：** v. 刺激；惹恼 n. 荨麻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- irritate（激怒）
- annoy（惹恼）
- provoke（挑衅）
- vex（使恼火）
- aggravate（加剧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **be nettled by / grasp the nettle / nettle someone into action / a nettled expression**

👉 Example: The senator was visibly nettled by the journalist's persistent questioning about the unresolved scandal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was clearly **nettled** by the student's dismissive attitude toward the extensive research that had taken years to complete.
   教授显然被学生对耗费多年完成的大量研究的轻蔑态度所激怒。

2. Rather than avoiding the difficult issue, the committee decided to grasp the **nettle** and address the budget crisis directly.
   委员会决定不回避困难问题，而是迎难而上，直接解决预算危机。

3. His condescending remarks during the meeting **nettled** his colleagues, creating an atmosphere of tension and mutual resentment.
   他在会议中居高临下的言论激怒了同事们，制造了紧张和相互不满的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of touching a stinging nettle plant — it pricks and irritates your skin, just as annoying comments "nettle" your emotions.
想象触碰荨麻——它刺痛并刺激你的皮肤，就像恼人的评论"刺激"你的情绪一样。
