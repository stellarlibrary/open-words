# 🔥 instigate

🔊 /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To bring about or initiate an action or event, often one that is controversial or harmful; to urge or incite someone to act
**中文：** v. 唆使；煽动；教唆；怂恿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- provoke（挑起）
- incite（煽动）
- foment（煽动）
- trigger（触发）
- spur（促使）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **instigate a rebellion / instigate change / instigate proceedings / instigate violence**

👉 Example: The opposition leader was accused of attempting to instigate unrest among the disenfranchised population.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians continue to debate which political factions were primarily responsible for **instigating** the revolution that toppled the monarchy.
   历史学家们继续争论哪些政治派系主要负责煽动了推翻君主制的革命。

2. The committee voted to **instigate** a formal investigation into allegations of financial misconduct within the executive department.
   委员会投票决定对行政部门内财务不当行为的指控发起正式调查。

3. Environmental activists **instigated** a series of peaceful demonstrations that ultimately led to the passage of stricter pollution regulations.
   环保活动人士发起了一系列和平示威，最终促使了更严格的污染法规的通过。

---

把"instigate"想成"in-sti(ck)-gate"——有人用棍子撬开大门，让麻烦涌入。
