# 💎 extravagance

🔊 /ɪkˈstrævəɡəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Excessive spending or lavishness; lack of restraint in spending money or using resources; an instance of something unreasonably costly
**中文：** n. 奢侈，浪费；过度；放肆的言行

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lavishness（奢华）
- excess（过度）
- prodigality（挥霍）
- indulgence（放纵）
- wastefulness（浪费）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sheer extravagance / unnecessary extravagance / an extravagance of / indulge in extravagance**

👉 Example: Critics condemned the extravagance of the royal banquet during a time of national austerity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **extravagance** of the wedding celebration shocked even the wealthiest guests who attended the lavish three-day event.
   婚礼庆典的奢华程度甚至震惊了参加这场为期三天豪华活动的最富有的宾客。

2. Financial advisors consistently warn against **extravagance**, urging clients to develop disciplined saving habits for long-term security.
   财务顾问一直警告不要奢侈浪费，敦促客户养成有纪律的储蓄习惯以保障长期安全。

3. The architectural **extravagance** of the palace reflected the monarch's desire to project absolute power and divine authority.
   宫殿在建筑上的奢华反映了君主展示绝对权力和神圣权威的渴望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extravagance" as "extra + vagance (wander)" — wandering extra far beyond what is reasonable in spending.
把"extravagance"想成"extra + vagance（漫游）"——在消费上远远超出合理范围地漫游。
