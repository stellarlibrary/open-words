# 🛡️ impunity

🔊 /ɪmˈpjuːnɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Exemption from punishment, harm, or unpleasant consequences; freedom from the injurious consequences of an action
**中文：** n. 不受惩罚；免罚；无患

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exemption（免除）
- immunity（豁免）
- freedom（自由）
- license（放任）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **with impunity / act with impunity / enjoy impunity / culture of impunity**

👉 Example: The corrupt officials acted with impunity, knowing that no one would hold them accountable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The warlord operated with complete **impunity** for decades, as no international tribunal had jurisdiction over his territory.
   这个军阀几十年来完全不受惩罚地活动，因为没有国际法庭对他的领地拥有管辖权。

2. Human rights organizations condemned the culture of **impunity** that allowed systematic abuses to persist without any legal consequences.
   人权组织谴责了这种不受惩罚的文化，它使系统性的侵权行为在没有任何法律后果的情况下持续存在。

3. Citizens cannot violate environmental regulations with **impunity**, as governmental agencies now enforce increasingly stringent penalties for pollution.
   公民不能不受惩罚地违反环境法规，因为政府机构现在对污染行为实施越来越严格的处罚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "impunity" as "im + punity (punish)" — without punishment, so you can act freely without facing consequences.
把"impunity"想成"im（无）+ punity（来自punish惩罚）"——没有惩罚，即不受惩罚。
