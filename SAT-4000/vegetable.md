# 🥬 vegetable

🔊 /ˈvɛdʒtəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A plant or part of a plant used as food; any member of the plant kingdom. (adj.) Of, relating to, or derived from plants
**中文：** n. 蔬菜；植物 adj. 蔬菜的；植物的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- produce（农产品）
- greens（绿叶菜）
- plant（植物）
- crop（庄稼）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vegetable garden / vegetable oil / root vegetable / vegetable matter**

👉 Example: The nutritionist recommended increasing daily vegetable intake to improve overall dietary health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural report indicated that **vegetable** production had increased substantially due to improved irrigation techniques and soil management.
   农业报告显示，由于灌溉技术和土壤管理的改善，蔬菜产量大幅增加。

2. Researchers discovered that certain **vegetable** oils contain beneficial fatty acids that may reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.
   研究人员发现某些植物油含有有益的脂肪酸，可能降低心血管疾病的风险。

3. The chef prepared an elaborate dish featuring seasonal **vegetables** sourced exclusively from local organic farms in the surrounding region.
   这位厨师用完全来自周边地区当地有机农场的时令蔬菜准备了一道精致的菜肴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veget-able" as "something able to vegetate" — a living plant that is able to grow and be eaten.
把"vegetable"想成"able to vegetate"——一种能够生长并可以食用的活的植物。
