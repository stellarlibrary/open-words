# 🗺️ itinerary

🔊 /aɪˈtɪnərɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A planned route or schedule of travel; a detailed plan for a journey
**中文：** n. 旅程，路线；旅行日程；行程表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- schedule（日程表）
- route（路线）
- agenda（议程）
- timetable（时间表）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **travel itinerary / plan an itinerary / follow an itinerary / detailed itinerary**

👉 Example: Before departing, she carefully reviewed the itinerary to ensure all reservations were confirmed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The travel agent prepared a comprehensive **itinerary** that included flights, hotel accommodations, and guided tours throughout Europe.
   旅行社准备了一份详尽的行程表，包括整个欧洲的航班、酒店住宿和导游服务。

2. According to the official **itinerary**, the diplomatic delegation would visit three capital cities within a single week.
   根据官方行程安排，外交代表团将在一周内访问三个首都城市。

3. The professor modified her research **itinerary** after learning that the archive in Vienna had recently been closed for renovation.
   教授在得知维也纳的档案馆最近因翻修关闭后，修改了她的研究行程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "itinerary" as "I-tin-er-ary" — imagine putting your travel plans "in a tin" container to keep them organized and ready.
把"itinerary"想成"I-tin-er-ary"——想象把你的旅行计划放进一个"铁罐（tin）"里，保持有条理随时备用。
