# 🔢 total

🔊 /ˈtoʊtl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb, noun | 形容词, 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Complete in extent or degree; absolute. (v.) To amount to; to add up. (n.) The entire amount or sum
**中文：** adj. 全部的；完全的 v. 总数达；合计 n. 总数；总额

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- complete（完全的）
- entire（全部的）
- aggregate（总计的）
- absolute（绝对的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **total amount / total cost / total failure / in total / total up**

👉 Example: The total expenditure for the research project exceeded the originally approved budget by fifteen percent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **total** number of participants in the clinical trial reached five thousand across twelve international sites.
   参加该临床试验的参与者**总**人数在十二个国际站点达到了五千人。

2. When investigators began to **total** the financial losses, they discovered the fraud amounted to several million dollars.
   当调查人员开始**合计**经济损失时，他们发现欺诈金额高达数百万美元。

3. The expedition ended in **total** failure after severe weather conditions forced the team to abandon their ascent.
   恶劣天气迫使团队放弃攀登后，这次探险以**完全**失败告终。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "total" as "to-tall" — imagine stacking everything together until it reaches the tallest point, representing the full sum.
把"total"想成"to-tall（到最高）"——想象把所有东西堆在一起直到最高点，代表全部总和。
