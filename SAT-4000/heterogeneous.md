# 🧬 heterogeneous

🔊 /ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Diverse in character or content; composed of parts of different kinds; not uniform in structure or composition
**中文：** adj. 多相的；异种的；不均匀的；不同种类的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diverse（多样的）
- varied（各种各样的）
- miscellaneous（混杂的）
- assorted（混合的）
- disparate（不同的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heterogeneous mixture / heterogeneous group / heterogeneous population / heterogeneous society**

👉 Example: The committee was composed of a heterogeneous group of experts from various academic disciplines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team analyzed a **heterogeneous** sample of materials to determine how varying compositions affected overall structural integrity.
   研究团队分析了一组异质材料样本，以确定不同成分如何影响整体结构完整性。

2. Modern metropolitan cities are remarkably **heterogeneous**, comprising residents from vastly different cultural and linguistic backgrounds.
   现代大都市具有显著的异质性，居民来自截然不同的文化和语言背景。

3. The professor emphasized that a **heterogeneous** classroom environment fosters more creative problem-solving among students of diverse abilities.
   教授强调，异质性的课堂环境能促进不同能力学生之间更具创造性的问题解决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hetero-" (different) + "geneous" (kind/type) — literally "of different kinds," like a mixed bag of unlike things.
把"heterogeneous"拆成"hetero-"（不同的）+"geneous"（种类），字面意思是"不同种类的"，就像一袋混装的不同东西。
