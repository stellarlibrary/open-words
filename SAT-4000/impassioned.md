# 🔥 impassioned

🔊 /ɪmˈpæʃənd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Filled with or showing great emotion, fervor, or intensity of feeling
**中文：** adj. 充满激情的；热情洋溢的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- passionate（热情的）
- fervent（热切的）
- ardent（热烈的）
- intense（强烈的）
- heartfelt（衷心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impassioned speech / impassioned plea / impassioned debate / impassioned appeal**

👉 Example: The activist delivered an impassioned speech urging world leaders to take immediate action on climate change.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney made an **impassioned** plea to the jury, arguing that her client had been wrongfully accused of the crime.
   辩护律师向陪审团发出了充满激情的恳求，辩称她的当事人被错误地指控犯罪。

2. The senator's **impassioned** address on education reform moved many colleagues to reconsider their positions on the proposed bill.
   这位参议员关于教育改革的激情演讲促使许多同僚重新考虑他们对该法案的立场。

3. Students organized an **impassioned** demonstration outside the administration building to demand greater transparency in university governance.
   学生们在行政大楼外组织了一场充满激情的示威，要求大学治理更加透明。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-passion-ed" — the word "passion" is right inside it, so impassioned simply means filled with passion and strong emotion.
把"impassioned"拆开看——里面就有"passion（激情）"，所以impassioned就是"充满激情的"。
