# 🐍 sinuous

🔊 /ˈsɪnjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having many curves and turns; winding; moving in a smooth, wavelike manner
**中文：** adj. 蜿蜒的；曲折的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- winding（蜿蜒的）
- serpentine（蛇形的）
- meandering（曲折的）
- tortuous（弯弯曲曲的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sinuous path / sinuous river / sinuous movements / sinuous curves**

👉 Example: The sinuous river wound gracefully through the lush green valley below the mountains.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **sinuous** mountain road tested the skill of even experienced drivers with its countless sharp turns and narrow passages.
   这条蜿蜒的山路以其无数的急转弯和狭窄通道考验着即使是经验丰富的司机的技术。

2. The dancer captivated the audience with her **sinuous** movements, each gesture flowing seamlessly into the next with effortless grace.
   这位舞者以她蜿蜒流畅的动作迷住了观众，每个手势都毫不费力地优雅过渡到下一个。

3. Geologists traced the **sinuous** course of the ancient riverbed using satellite imagery and detailed topographic surveys.
   地质学家利用卫星图像和详细的地形测量追踪了古河床的曲折走向。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sinu-ous" as related to "sine wave" — a sine wave is a smooth, curving line, just like something sinuous.
把"sinuous"想成与"sine wave（正弦波）"相关——正弦波是一条平滑的曲线，就像蜿蜒的东西一样。
