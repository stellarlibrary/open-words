# 🌟 compliment

🔊 /ˈkɒmplɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A polite expression of praise or admiration. (v.) To express praise or admiration for someone
**中文：** n. 恭维；称赞；问候 v. 恭维；称赞；褒扬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- praise（赞美）
- commend（称赞）
- flatter（奉承）
- acclaim（赞扬）
- tribute（致敬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pay a compliment / return a compliment / compliment someone on something / sincere compliment**

👉 Example: The professor complimented the student on her exceptionally well-researched thesis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador **complimented** the host nation on its remarkable progress in establishing sustainable energy infrastructure throughout the region.
   大使称赞东道国在整个地区建立可持续能源基础设施方面取得的显著进展。

2. Receiving a genuine **compliment** from a respected colleague can significantly boost one's confidence and professional motivation.
   收到一位受人尊敬的同事的真诚称赞可以显著提升一个人的信心和职业动力。

3. The architectural critic **complimented** the designer for seamlessly integrating modern aesthetics with traditional cultural elements in the building.
   建筑评论家称赞设计师在建筑中将现代美学与传统文化元素无缝融合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compliment" with an "i" — "I" like to give compliments. (Don't confuse with "complement" with an "e" meaning to complete.)
把"compliment"和字母"i"联系起来——"我"(I)喜欢给人称赞。（不要和用"e"拼写的"complement"混淆，那个意思是补充。）
