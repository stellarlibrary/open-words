# 🌿 tropical rain forest

🔊 /ˈtrɒpɪkəl reɪn ˈfɒrɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A dense, warm forest found near the equator with consistently heavy rainfall and extraordinary biodiversity
**中文：** n. 热带雨林

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rainforest（雨林）
- jungle（丛林）
- equatorial forest（赤道森林）
- tropical woodland（热带林地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tropical rain forest ecosystem / tropical rain forest destruction / tropical rain forest canopy / protect the tropical rain forest**

👉 Example: The tropical rain forest of the Amazon Basin harbors millions of species that remain undiscovered by science.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists estimate that the **tropical rain forest** contains more than half of the world's plant and animal species within its layered canopy.
   科学家估计，热带雨林在其分层的树冠中包含了世界上一半以上的动植物物种。

2. The rapid destruction of the **tropical rain forest** poses a significant threat to indigenous communities who depend on it for survival.
   热带雨林的快速破坏对依赖它生存的原住民社区构成了重大威胁。

3. Conservation organizations have launched international campaigns to preserve the remaining **tropical rain forest** before irreversible damage is inflicted.
   保护组织已发起国际运动，以在不可逆转的破坏发生之前保护剩余的热带雨林。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tropical rain forest" as three clues: tropical (near the equator), rain (constant rainfall), forest (dense trees) — together they describe Earth's wettest, most biodiverse forests.
把"tropical rain forest"想成三个线索：tropical（赤道附近）、rain（持续降雨）、forest（茂密树林）——组合起来就是地球上最潮湿、生物多样性最丰富的森林。
