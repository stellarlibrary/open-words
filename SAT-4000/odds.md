# 🎲 odds

🔊 /ɑːdz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The probability or likelihood of something happening; the ratio of chances in favor of or against an outcome
**中文：** n. 几率；胜算；不平等；差别；可能性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- probability（概率）
- likelihood（可能性）
- chances（机会）
- prospects（前景）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **against all odds / the odds are / odds of success / at odds with / defy the odds**

👉 Example: Against all odds, the small startup managed to secure funding from a major venture capital firm.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Statistical analysis revealed that the **odds** of a full economic recovery within one year were remarkably slim.
   统计分析显示，一年内经济完全复苏的几率非常渺茫。

2. The two researchers found themselves at **odds** with each other over the interpretation of the experimental data.
   两位研究人员在实验数据的解读上意见相左。

3. Despite overwhelming **odds** against her, the candidate won the election by a substantial and unexpected margin.
   尽管形势对她极为不利，这位候选人还是以出人意料的大幅优势赢得了选举。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "odds" as "odd numbers" — they stand out as different, just like odds measure how likely something unusual might happen.
把"odds"想成"odd numbers（奇数）"——它们因不同而突出，就像odds衡量不寻常之事发生的可能性。
