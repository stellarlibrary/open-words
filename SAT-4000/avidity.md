# 🔥 avidity

🔊 /əˈvɪdəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** An intense eagerness, enthusiasm, or desire for something; extreme greediness or voracity in pursuit of an interest or goal.

**中文：** 热望，贪欲；对某事物的强烈渴望、热情或贪求。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- eagerness （热切）
- fervor （热情）
- voracity （贪婪）
- zeal （热忱）
- keenness （热心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Avidity" can be used in both positive and negative senses. Positively, it describes passionate enthusiasm for learning or a pursuit. Negatively, it can imply excessive greed. It is related to the adjective "avid," which is more commonly used in everyday language.

"avidity"可用于正面和负面含义。正面用法描述对学习或某项追求的热切热情；负面用法可暗示过度贪婪。它与形容词"avid"相关，后者在日常语言中更常用。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scholar pursued her research with an **avidity** that impressed even the most seasoned professors.
   *这位年轻学者以一种令最资深教授都印象深刻的**热切**态度进行她的研究。*

2. His **avidity** for acquiring rare manuscripts led him to travel across three continents over the decade.
   *他对收集珍本手稿的**热望**促使他在十年间跨越了三个大洲。*

3. The investors' **avidity** for quick profits blinded them to the obvious risks of the volatile market.
   *投资者对快速利润的**贪欲**使他们忽视了波动市场中显而易见的风险。*

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of an "avid" reader — someone who reads with great passion. "Avidity" is the noun form of "avid," representing that intense hunger for more.

想想一个"avid"（热心的）读者——一个充满热情地阅读的人。"avidity"是"avid"的名词形式，代表那种对更多东西的强烈渴望。
