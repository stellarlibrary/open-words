# 🔁 recrudescent

🔊 /ˌriːkruːˈdesənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Breaking out again after a period of dormancy; recurring, especially of a disease, trouble, or undesirable condition
**中文：** adj. 复发的，再发作的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recurring（复发的）
- resurgent（再起的）
- relapsing（再发的）
- reappearing（重新出现的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recrudescent disease / recrudescent violence / recrudescent symptoms / recrudescent conflict**

👉 Example: The region faced recrudescent ethnic tensions that threatened the fragile peace agreement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public health authorities grew alarmed by the **recrudescent** outbreak of the infectious disease in the densely populated urban district.
   公共卫生当局对人口密集城区传染病的复发疫情感到震惊。

2. The **recrudescent** violence along the disputed border compelled the international community to deploy additional peacekeeping forces immediately.
   边境争议地区暴力冲突的再次爆发迫使国际社会立即部署额外的维和部队。

3. Scholars have observed that **recrudescent** nationalist movements tend to emerge during periods of widespread economic instability and uncertainty.
   学者们观察到，复发性的民族主义运动往往在经济普遍不稳定和充满不确定性的时期出现。

把"recrudescent"想成"re-crude-escent"——某种粗糙而不愉快的东西不断复发，就像伤口重新裂开。
