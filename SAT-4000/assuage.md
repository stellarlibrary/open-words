# 🕊️ assuage

🔊 /əˈsweɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make an unpleasant feeling less intense; to relieve, calm, or soothe pain, grief, anger, or thirst
**中文：** 平息；缓和；减轻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alleviate（减轻）
- mitigate（缓和）
- soothe（安慰）
- pacify（平息）
- mollify（安抚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **assuage fears / assuage guilt / assuage pain / assuage hunger / assuage concerns**

👉 Example: The manager's reassuring words helped assuage the team's anxiety about the upcoming restructuring.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's carefully chosen words did little to **assuage** the growing tensions between the two neighboring nations.
   这位外交官精心选择的措辞几乎没有缓和两个邻国之间日益加剧的紧张局势。

2. She volunteered at the shelter every weekend to **assuage** her guilt about the privileges she had always enjoyed.
   她每个周末在收容所做志愿者，以减轻她对自己一直享有的特权的内疚感。

3. No amount of rational explanation could fully **assuage** the public's deep-seated fears about the new nuclear facility.
   再多的理性解释也无法完全平息公众对新核设施根深蒂固的恐惧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assuage" as "a-SEWAGE" — imagine clearing away sewage to make a place clean and calm, just as you assuage pain to bring relief.
把"assuage"想成"清除污水（sewage）"——想象清除污水使地方洁净平静，就像你缓和痛苦以带来舒适一样。
