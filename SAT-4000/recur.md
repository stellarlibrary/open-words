# 🔄 recur

🔊 /rɪˈkɜːr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To occur again or repeatedly; to come back to one's mind; to happen at intervals
**中文：** v. 复发；重现；再发生；循环

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reoccur（再发生）
- repeat（重复）
- reappear（再现）
- return（返回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recur frequently / recur periodically / recur throughout / a recurring theme**

👉 Example: The same logistical problems continued to recur despite the committee's repeated attempts at reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The debilitating migraines began to **recur** with alarming frequency, prompting the patient to seek specialist consultation.
   使人衰弱的偏头痛开始以令人担忧的频率复发，促使患者寻求专科会诊。

2. Themes of social inequality **recur** throughout the author's literary works, reflecting her deep commitment to justice.
   社会不平等的主题在这位作家的文学作品中反复出现，反映了她对公正的深切承诺。

3. If structural deficiencies are not properly addressed, the same catastrophic failures will inevitably **recur** in future operations.
   如果结构缺陷得不到妥善解决，同样的灾难性故障将不可避免地在未来的运营中重现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-cur" — "re" means again and "cur" relates to "current" (running), so recur means to run or come back again.
把"recur"想成"re(再次) + cur(发生/跑)"——再次发生，即复发、重现。
