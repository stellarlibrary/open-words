# 🌐 domain

🔊 /doʊˈmeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An area of territory or knowledge controlled by a particular ruler or authority; a sphere of activity or expertise
**中文：** n. 领域；范围；域名；产业

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- realm（领域）
- territory（领地）
- sphere（范围）
- field（领域）
- province（范围）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public domain / domain of knowledge / domain name / domain expertise**

👉 Example: Artificial intelligence has rapidly expanded beyond the domain of computer science into numerous other disciplines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's research falls squarely within the **domain** of theoretical physics, focusing on quantum mechanics and particle behavior.
   这位教授的研究完全属于理论物理学领域，专注于量子力学和粒子行为。

2. Historically, foreign policy was considered the exclusive **domain** of the executive branch rather than the legislative body.
   从历史上看，外交政策被认为是行政部门的专属领域，而非立法机构的范畴。

3. Once a patent expires, the invention enters the public **domain** and becomes freely available for commercial or educational use.
   一旦专利到期，该发明就进入公共领域，可以自由用于商业或教育用途。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "domain" as "dome + ain" — like a dome covering a certain area, a domain is the area over which someone has authority.
将"domain"想成"dome + ain"——就像一个穹顶覆盖着某个区域，domain就是某人拥有权威的范围。
