# 🏔 altitude

🔊 /ˈæltɪˌtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The height of an object or point above sea level or ground level; a high area or region
**中文：** n. 高地；高度；海拔

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elevation（海拔）
- height（高度）
- loftiness（高耸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **high altitude / at an altitude of / altitude sickness / gain altitude**

👉 Example: The aircraft cruised at an altitude of thirty-five thousand feet above sea level.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Mountaineers must acclimatize gradually when ascending to extreme **altitude** to prevent serious physiological complications.
   登山者在攀登至极高海拔时必须逐步适应，以防止严重的生理并发症。

2. The research station was constructed at a remarkable **altitude** of four thousand meters in the Andes mountain range.
   该研究站建在安第斯山脉海拔四千米的显著高度上。

3. Pilots are trained to manage cabin pressure effectively when the aircraft reaches its cruising **altitude** during long flights.
   飞行员接受过训练，能够在长途飞行中飞机达到巡航高度时有效管理座舱压力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alt-" (high) + "-itude" (state) — altitude is the state of being high, like how "alt" in Latin means high.
把"altitude"拆分为"alt-"（高）和"-itude"（状态）——altitude就是处于高处的状态，拉丁语中"altus"意为"高"。
