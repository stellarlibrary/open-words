# 🌟 brightness

🔊 /ˈbraɪtnəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality or state of being bright; luminosity or brilliance; intelligence or cheerfulness
**中文：** n. 亮度；聪明；活泼；鲜艳；愉快

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- luminosity（光度）
- brilliance（光辉）
- radiance（光芒）
- vividness（鲜明）
- luster（光泽）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adjust the brightness / brightness level / brightness of the sun / brightness and contrast**

👉 Example: The brightness of the display can be adjusted to reduce eye strain during prolonged use.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The remarkable **brightness** of the supernova was visible to astronomers across multiple observatories for several consecutive weeks.
   这颗超新星非凡的亮度在连续几周内被多个天文台的天文学家观测到。

2. Researchers measured the **brightness** of various light sources to determine their suitability for controlled laboratory experiments.
   研究人员测量了各种光源的亮度，以确定它们是否适合受控实验室实验。

3. Her intellectual **brightness** and analytical acuity distinguished her from other candidates during the rigorous selection process.
   她的聪慧和敏锐的分析能力使她在严格的选拔过程中脱颖而出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brightness" as "bright" + "-ness" — the state of being bright, whether it refers to light, color, or intellect.
把"brightness"想成"bright"（明亮的）+ "-ness"（状态）——明亮的状态，无论指光线、色彩还是智力。
