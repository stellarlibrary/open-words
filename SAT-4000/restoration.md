# 🔧 restoration

🔊 /ˌrɛstəˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of returning something to its former condition; the process of repairing or renovating; the reinstatement of a previous practice, right, or situation
**中文：** n. 恢复；复位；修复；王政复辟；归还

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- renovation（翻新）
- rehabilitation（修复）
- reinstatement（恢复）
- recovery（恢复）
- repair（修缮）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **restoration of / restoration work / under restoration / restoration project / ecological restoration**

👉 Example: The restoration of the ancient temple took over a decade of meticulous and painstaking effort.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **restoration** of the Renaissance painting required years of careful analysis and delicate conservation techniques by expert specialists.
   这幅文艺复兴画作的修复需要专家数年的仔细分析和精细的保护技术。

2. Environmental activists have called for the immediate **restoration** of the wetlands that were destroyed by decades of industrial development.
   环保活动人士呼吁立即恢复因数十年工业开发而遭到破坏的湿地。

3. The **restoration** of democratic governance following the military regime was celebrated as a pivotal moment in the nation's history.
   军事政权结束后民主治理的恢复被视为该国历史上的关键时刻。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-STORE-ation" — it literally means "storing something back" to where or how it was before, restoring it to its original state.
把"restoration"想成"re-store-ation"——字面意思就是"把东西放回原处"，即恢复到原始状态。
