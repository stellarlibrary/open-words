# 🔔 sonorous

🔊 /ˈsɑːnərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Producing a deep, rich, resonant sound; impressively grand in sound
**中文：** adj. 洪亮的，响亮的；能发出响亮的声音的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- resonant（洪亮的）
- booming（轰鸣的）
- deep-toned（低沉的）
- reverberant（回响的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sonorous voice / sonorous tone / sonorous language / sonorous quality / sonorous prose**

👉 Example: The orator's sonorous voice echoed through the grand hall, captivating every listener in attendance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cathedral bells produced a **sonorous** chime that resonated across the entire valley during the evening ceremony.
   大教堂的钟声在晚间仪式中发出洪亮的鸣响，回荡在整个山谷。

2. His **sonorous** baritone voice lent an air of authority and gravitas to every speech he delivered before the assembly.
   他洪亮的男中音嗓音为他在大会上发表的每一次演讲增添了权威感和庄重感。

3. The poet crafted deliberately **sonorous** verses designed to evoke the majesty and power of the ancient natural landscape.
   诗人精心创作了响亮铿锵的诗句，旨在唤起古老自然景观的雄伟与力量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sonorous" sharing the root "son-" with "sonic" (sound) — something sonorous produces a powerful, impressive sound.
把"sonorous"和"sonic"（声音的）联系起来，它们共享词根"son-"——sonorous的东西能发出有力而令人印象深刻的声音。
