# 📅 quarterly

🔊 /ˈkwɔːrtərli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb, noun | 形容词, 副词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring or published once every quarter of a year. (adv.) Once every three months. (n.) A publication issued four times a year
**中文：** adj. 季度的，按季度的 adv. 按季度，每季一次 n. 季刊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- periodic（定期的）
- trimonthly（每三个月的）
- seasonal（季节性的）
- regular（定期的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **quarterly report / quarterly earnings / quarterly review / quarterly publication**

👉 Example: The company releases its quarterly earnings report to shareholders at the end of each fiscal quarter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board of directors convenes **quarterly** to review the organization's financial performance and strategic initiatives.
   董事会每季度召开一次会议，审查组织的财务表现和战略举措。

2. Analysts eagerly awaited the **quarterly** earnings report, which would reveal whether the corporation had met its projections.
   分析师热切期待季度收益报告，该报告将揭示公司是否达到了其预期目标。

3. The literary **quarterly** published essays by distinguished scholars and emerging voices in contemporary critical theory.
   这本文学季刊刊登了当代批评理论领域杰出学者和新兴学者的文章。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quarter + ly" — a quarter is one-fourth of a year, so quarterly means every one-fourth of a year, or every three months.
把"quarterly"想成"quarter（四分之一）+ ly"——一个quarter是一年的四分之一，所以quarterly就是每年的四分之一，即每三个月。
