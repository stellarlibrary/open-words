# 🔁 reiterate

🔊 /riːˈɪtəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To say or do something again or repeatedly, especially for emphasis or clarity; to restate or repeat.
**中文：** v. 重申；反复地说；重做

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- repeat（重复）
- restate（重新陈述）
- emphasize（强调）
- recapitulate（概括重述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reiterate a point / reiterate one's commitment / reiterate the importance of / reiterate that**

👉 Example: The spokesperson reiterated the organization's commitment to transparency in all financial dealings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor chose to **reiterate** the fundamental principles of the theory before introducing more advanced and nuanced concepts.
   教授选择在引入更高级和细致的概念之前重申该理论的基本原理。

2. During the press conference, the official **reiterated** that the government would not negotiate with parties who violated the terms.
   在新闻发布会上，该官员重申政府不会与违反条款的各方进行谈判。

3. The safety manual **reiterates** the critical importance of wearing protective equipment in all designated hazardous work areas.
   安全手册反复强调在所有指定危险工作区域佩戴防护设备的关键重要性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-iterate" as "iterate again" — to iterate means to go through something, so reiterate means to go through it once more.
把"reiterate"想成"re（再次）+ iterate（重复）"——iterate是经历某事，reiterate就是再经历一次，即重申。
