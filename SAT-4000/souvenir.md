# 🎁 souvenir

🔊 /ˌsuːvəˈnɪr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A thing that is kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event; a memento or gift. (v.) To keep as a memento
**中文：** n. 纪念品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- memento（纪念品）
- keepsake（纪念物）
- token（信物）
- relic（遗物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **souvenir shop / souvenir from / collect souvenirs / as a souvenir**

👉 Example: She brought back a beautiful handcrafted souvenir from her trip to the ancient coastal town.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Tourists often purchase a **souvenir** from the museum gift shop to commemorate their visit to the historic landmark.
   游客们常常在博物馆礼品店购买纪念品，以纪念他们对这一历史地标的参观。

2. The antique pocket watch served as a precious **souvenir** of her late grandfather's distinguished career in the navy.
   这块古董怀表是她已故祖父在海军杰出生涯的珍贵纪念品。

3. The delegation exchanged formal **souvenirs** at the conclusion of the diplomatic summit to symbolize mutual respect and goodwill.
   代表团在外交峰会结束时交换了正式纪念品，以象征相互尊重和善意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "souvenir" from French "souvenir" meaning "to remember" — it is something you keep to help you remember a special experience.
"souvenir"来自法语"souvenir"，意为"记住"——它是你保留的东西，帮助你记住一次特别的经历。
