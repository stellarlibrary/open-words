# 🔄 rotary

🔊 /ˈroʊtəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Revolving or turning around a center or axis. (n.) A rotary machine or device; a traffic circle
**中文：** adj. 旋转的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revolving（旋转的）
- spinning（转动的）
- circular（环形的）
- rotating（旋转的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rotary motion / rotary engine / rotary dial / rotary club**

👉 Example: The old telephone featured a rotary dial that required users to physically turn a numbered wheel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer explained how the **rotary** mechanism converts continuous circular motion into precise linear displacement within the device.
   工程师解释了旋转机构如何将连续的圆周运动转化为设备内精确的直线位移。

2. Early aircraft relied on **rotary** engines in which the crankshaft remained stationary while the cylinders spun around it.
   早期飞机依赖转缸式发动机，其中曲轴保持静止而气缸围绕其旋转。

3. The city council approved construction of a **rotary** intersection to improve traffic flow and reduce vehicle collisions at the junction.
   市议会批准建造一个环形交叉路口，以改善交通流量并减少该路口的车辆碰撞。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rotate" + "-ary" — rotary describes anything that rotates, like a rotary phone dial spinning in a circle.
把"rotary"想成"rotate（旋转）+ -ary"——rotary描述任何旋转的东西，就像旋转电话拨号盘转圈一样。
