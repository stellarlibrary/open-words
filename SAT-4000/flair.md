# 🌟 flair

🔊 /flɛr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A natural talent or aptitude; a distinctive elegance or style; an instinctive ability to discern or appreciate
**中文：** n. 天资；天分；鉴别力；才华

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- talent（才能）
- aptitude（天资）
- knack（诀窍）
- panache（派头）
- gift（天赋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have a flair for / natural flair / artistic flair / creative flair**

👉 Example: She has a remarkable flair for languages and can pick up new vocabulary with ease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young architect demonstrated an exceptional **flair** for combining modern aesthetics with traditional structural elements.
   这位年轻建筑师展现了将现代美学与传统结构元素相结合的非凡天赋。

2. Her natural **flair** for public speaking enabled her to captivate audiences throughout her distinguished academic career.
   她天生的演讲才能使她在杰出的学术生涯中始终能够吸引听众。

3. The editor recognized his **flair** for narrative prose and immediately offered him a position on the editorial team.
   编辑认可了他在叙事散文方面的天分，并立即向他提供了编辑团队的职位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "flair" sounding like "flare" — a flare lights up the sky brilliantly, just as someone with flair shines with natural talent.
把"flair"想成发音类似"flare"（照明弹）——照明弹点亮天空，就像有天赋的人闪耀着才华。
