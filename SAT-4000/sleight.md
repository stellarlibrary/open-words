# 🎭 sleight

🔊 /slaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A clever trick or stratagem; dexterity or skill, especially in deceiving
**中文：** n. 技巧，手法

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dexterity（灵巧）
- cunning（狡猾）
- artifice（诡计）
- adroitness（熟练）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sleight of hand / by sleight / verbal sleight**

👉 Example: The magician's sleight of hand left the entire audience utterly astonished.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The politician used rhetorical **sleight** to redirect the debate away from his controversial voting record.
   这位政客运用修辞技巧将辩论从他有争议的投票记录上转移开来。

2. Through sheer **sleight** of hand, the illusionist made the silver coin vanish before hundreds of spectators.
   凭借纯熟的手法，魔术师在数百名观众面前让银币消失了。

3. The accountant's financial **sleight** concealed millions in unauthorized expenditures from the auditing committee.
   这位会计的财务手法向审计委员会隐瞒了数百万未经授权的支出。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sleight" as "sly + light" — a sly, light-fingered trick, like a magician's quick hand movements.
把"sleight"想成"sly（狡猾的）+ light（轻巧的）"——一种狡猾而轻巧的手法，就像魔术师快速的手部动作。
