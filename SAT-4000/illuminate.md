# 💡 illuminate

🔊 /ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To clarify or make something easier to understand; to light up or brighten with light
**中文：** v. 阐明，说明；照亮，照明；启蒙

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clarify（阐明）
- elucidate（解释）
- enlighten（启发）
- brighten（照亮）
- explain（说明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **illuminate a topic / illuminate the path / illuminate the issue / illuminate understanding**

👉 Example: The professor's lecture helped illuminate the complex relationship between economics and social policy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new research findings **illuminate** the underlying mechanisms of cellular regeneration in damaged human tissue.
   这项新的研究发现阐明了受损人体组织中细胞再生的内在机制。

2. Strategically placed lanterns **illuminate** the ancient temple grounds, creating an atmosphere of solemn reverence each evening.
   精心放置的灯笼照亮了古老的寺庙庭院，每晚营造出庄严肃穆的氛围。

3. The documentary aims to **illuminate** the overlooked contributions of immigrant communities to the nation's industrial development.
   这部纪录片旨在阐明移民社区对国家工业发展中被忽视的贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "illuminate" as "I + lumin (light) + ate" — to bring light to something, either literally or figuratively by making it clear.
把"illuminate"想成"I + lumin（光）+ ate"——给某物带来光明，无论是字面上的照亮还是比喻上的阐明。
