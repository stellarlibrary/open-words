# 🧫 homologous chromosome

🔊 /həˈmɒləɡəs ˈkroʊməsoʊm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A pair of chromosomes, one from each parent, that have the same gene loci and structure and pair during meiosis
**中文：** n. [遗] 同源染色体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- homolog（同源物）
- paired chromosome（配对染色体）
- chromosome pair（染色体对）
- bivalent（二价体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **homologous chromosome pair / homologous chromosomes separate / exchange segments between homologous chromosomes**

👉 Example: During meiosis I, homologous chromosomes align at the cell's equatorial plate before separating.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Each **homologous chromosome** carries genes for the same traits but may contain different alleles inherited from each parent.
   每条同源染色体携带相同性状的基因，但可能含有从父母各自遗传的不同等位基因。

2. The failure of **homologous chromosomes** to separate properly during meiosis can result in conditions such as Down syndrome.
   同源染色体在减数分裂中未能正确分离可能导致唐氏综合征等疾病。

3. Crossing over between **homologous chromosomes** increases genetic variation by shuffling alleles between maternal and paternal strands.
   同源染色体之间的交叉互换通过在母本和父本链之间重排等位基因来增加遗传变异。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homologous" (same origin) + "chromosome" (color body) — two chromosomes from different parents that match up like dance partners.
把"homologous chromosome"想成"同源的"+"染色体"——来自不同父母的两条染色体像舞伴一样配对。
