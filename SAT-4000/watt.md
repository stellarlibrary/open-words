# ⚡ watt

🔊 /wɒt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A unit of power in the International System of Units, equal to one joule per second, used to measure the rate of energy transfer
**中文：** n. 瓦特（功率单位）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unit of power（功率单位）
- power measure（功率度量）
- kilowatt（千瓦）
- joule per second（焦耳每秒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **100-watt bulb / watts of power / kilowatt / wattage**

👉 Example: The new solar panel is capable of generating up to three hundred watts of electricity under optimal conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory equipment requires a continuous power supply of at least five hundred **watts** to operate reliably and safely.
   该实验室设备需要至少五百瓦的持续电力供应才能可靠安全地运行。

2. Engineers calculated that the proposed wind turbine could produce over two million **watts** of electricity during peak performance periods.
   工程师们计算出拟建的风力涡轮机在峰值性能期间可产生超过两百万瓦的电力。

3. Replacing traditional incandescent bulbs with LED alternatives reduced the building's lighting consumption from sixty to just ten **watts** per fixture.
   将传统白炽灯泡更换为LED替代品后，建筑物每个灯具的照明功耗从六十瓦降至仅十瓦。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of James Watt, the inventor who improved the steam engine — the unit "watt" is named after him to measure power.
想想詹姆斯·瓦特，这位改进了蒸汽机的发明家——"瓦特"这个单位就是以他的名字命名来度量功率的。
