# 🪞 baldness

🔊 /ˈbɔːldnəs/<br>
🏷️ *n. | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The state of having little or no hair on the head; figuratively, the quality of being blunt, direct, or unadorned in expression.

**中文：** 光秃，秃头；（比喻义）率直，直白，不加修饰。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- bareness（光秃）
- bluntness（直率）
- starkness（赤裸裸）
- frankness（坦率）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Baldness" has both a literal and figurative meaning. Literally, it refers to a lack of hair. Figuratively, it describes a plain, unvarnished quality in speech or writing — the "baldness" of a statement means it is stripped of any softening or ornamentation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **baldness** of his accusations startled the audience, as he offered no diplomatic phrasing whatsoever.
   他指控的**直白**令观众震惊，因为他没有使用任何外交辞令。

2. Researchers have made significant progress in understanding the genetic factors that contribute to premature **baldness**.
   研究人员在理解导致过早**秃顶**的遗传因素方面取得了重大进展。

3. The **baldness** of the winter landscape, stripped of all foliage, conveyed a haunting and austere beauty.
   冬日景观的**光秃**，褪去了所有枝叶，传达出一种令人难忘的素朴之美。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Bald" + "ness" — think of a bald head, smooth and bare with nothing to hide. A "bald" statement is equally bare and hides nothing.

"Bald（秃的）"+"ness（状态）"——想象一个光头，光滑而裸露，无所隐藏。一个"bald"的陈述同样赤裸直白，毫无隐瞒。
