# 🤡 fatuous

🔊 /ˈfætʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Silly and pointless; devoid of intelligence or substance; complacently foolish
**中文：** adj. 愚笨的；昏庸的；发呆的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- foolish（愚蠢的）
- inane（空洞的）
- asinine（愚蠢的）
- vacuous（空虚的）
- witless（无知的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fatuous remark / fatuous grin / fatuous suggestion / fatuous optimism**

👉 Example: The committee dismissed his fatuous suggestion as lacking any serious analytical foundation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's **fatuous** comments during the debate revealed a startling lack of understanding about the economic crisis.
   这位参议员在辩论中愚蠢的评论暴露了他对经济危机惊人的缺乏理解。

2. Critics condemned the **fatuous** optimism of the report, which ignored substantial evidence of environmental degradation and resource depletion.
   评论家谴责该报告愚昧的乐观态度，它忽视了环境退化和资源枯竭的大量证据。

3. His **fatuous** grin during the solemn ceremony offended the audience and undermined the gravity of the occasion entirely.
   他在庄严仪式上发呆般的笑容冒犯了观众，完全破坏了这一场合的严肃性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fat-u-ous" as "fat on foolishness" — someone so stuffed with foolishness that nothing intelligent comes out.
把"fatuous"想成"fat on foolishness（充满愚蠢）"——一个人满脑子愚蠢，说不出任何聪明的话。
