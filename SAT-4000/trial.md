# ⚖️ trial

🔊 /ˈtraɪəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A formal examination of evidence in court; an experiment or test; a difficult experience. (adj.) Relating to or used in a test or experiment.
**中文：** n. 试验；审讯；磨炼 adj. 试验的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hearing（审讯）
- experiment（试验）
- ordeal（磨炼）
- test（测试）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stand trial / trial period / trial and error / on trial / trial run**

👉 Example: The company offered a free trial period so customers could evaluate the software before purchasing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant will stand **trial** next month for the alleged financial fraud committed against several major investors.
   被告将于下月就涉嫌对多名主要投资者实施的金融欺诈接受审判。

2. After extensive **trial** and error, the research team finally developed a viable prototype for the renewable energy device.
   经过大量试验和摸索，研究团队终于为可再生能源设备开发出了可行的原型。

3. The prolonged drought proved to be a severe **trial** for the farming communities that depended entirely on seasonal rainfall.
   长期干旱对完全依赖季节性降雨的农业社区来说是一场严峻的磨炼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trial" as "try-all" — in a trial, you try all the evidence, try all the methods, or endure all the hardships.
把"trial"想成"try-all"——在审判中尝试所有证据，在试验中尝试所有方法，在磨炼中经历所有艰辛。
