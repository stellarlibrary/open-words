# 🏞️ vista

🔊 /ˈvɪstə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A pleasing view, especially one seen through a long narrow opening; a mental view of a series of events or possibilities
**中文：** n. 远景，狭长的街景；展望；回顾

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- panorama（全景）
- view（景色）
- prospect（展望）
- outlook（前景）
- landscape（风景）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sweeping vista / mountain vista / open up new vistas / vista of possibilities**

👉 Example: The hiking trail offered a breathtaking vista of the snow-capped mountain range.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. From the summit, hikers were rewarded with a magnificent **vista** of rolling hills and distant valleys bathed in sunlight.
   从山顶望去，徒步旅行者们欣赏到了连绵起伏的山丘和沐浴在阳光中的远处山谷的壮丽景色。

2. The professor's groundbreaking research opened up entirely new **vistas** of understanding in the field of molecular biology.
   这位教授的开创性研究在分子生物学领域开辟了全新的认识视野。

3. The renovation of the historic boulevard restored the elegant **vista** that city planners had originally envisioned centuries ago.
   这条历史悠久的大道经过翻修，恢复了几个世纪前城市规划者最初设想的优雅街景。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vista" as coming from Italian/Latin "vedere" (to see) — a vista is a beautiful scene you see stretching out before you.
把"vista"想成源自意大利语/拉丁语"vedere"（看）——vista就是你看到的在眼前展开的美丽景象。
