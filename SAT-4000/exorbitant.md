# 💰 exorbitant

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɔːrbɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Exceeding what is reasonable or appropriate; unreasonably high in price or amount
**中文：** adj. 过度的；过高的；昂贵的；不合理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excessive（过度的）
- extravagant（奢侈的）
- outrageous（离谱的）
- excessively high（过高的）
- unreasonable（不合理的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exorbitant price / exorbitant cost / exorbitant fees / exorbitant rate**

👉 Example: Many patients struggle to afford the exorbitant cost of prescription medications in the current healthcare system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **exorbitant** tuition fees at elite universities have sparked a national debate about accessibility and educational equity.
   顶尖大学过高的学费引发了一场关于入学机会和教育公平的全国性辩论。

2. Consumers filed a class-action lawsuit against the corporation for charging **exorbitant** prices during the natural disaster.
   消费者对该公司在自然灾害期间收取过高价格提起了集体诉讼。

3. The committee rejected the contractor's bid because the proposed budget was deemed **exorbitant** relative to comparable projects.
   委员会拒绝了承包商的投标，因为与类似项目相比，拟议预算被认为过于高昂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-orbit-ant" — something that has gone out of orbit, flying way beyond any reasonable range, just like an exorbitant price.
把"exorbitant"想成"ex-orbit-ant"——某样东西飞出了轨道，远远超出合理范围，就像过高的价格一样。
