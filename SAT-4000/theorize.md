# 🔬 theorize

🔊 /ˈθɪəraɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To form a theory or set of theories about something; to speculate or conjecture based on evidence or reasoning.
**中文：** v. 建立理论；推理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hypothesize（假设）
- speculate（推测）
- conjecture（推断）
- postulate（假定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **theorize about / theorize that / theorize on / widely theorized**

👉 Example: Scientists began to theorize about the origins of the mysterious signals detected from deep space.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers **theorize** that prolonged exposure to social media may significantly alter adolescent brain development and emotional regulation.
   研究人员推论，长期接触社交媒体可能会显著改变青少年的大脑发育和情绪调节。

2. The archaeologist **theorized** that the ancient ruins once served as a ceremonial center for the surrounding agricultural communities.
   考古学家推断，这些古代遗址曾经是周围农业社区的仪式中心。

3. It is unwise to **theorize** extensively without sufficient data, as premature conclusions can mislead subsequent lines of inquiry.
   在没有充分数据的情况下大量推论是不明智的，因为过早的结论可能会误导后续研究方向。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "theorize" as "theory + ize" — to "ize" means to make or do, so theorize means to make theories.
把"theorize"想成"theory + ize"——"ize"表示做或制造，所以theorize就是制造理论。
