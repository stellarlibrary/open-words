# 🧘 sober

🔊 /ˈsoʊbər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Calm and clear-headed; not intoxicated. (v.) To make serious; to become sober
**中文：** adj. 清醒的；认真的，冷静的，严肃的；颜色不鲜艳，暗淡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- serious（严肃的）
- solemn（庄重的）
- temperate（节制的）
- clearheaded（头脑清醒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sober assessment / sober reality / sober judgment / sober reflection**

👉 Example: The report offered a sober assessment of the challenges facing the healthcare system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economist provided a **sober** analysis of the fiscal crisis, warning that recovery would take several years.
   这位经济学家对财政危机进行了冷静的分析，警告复苏将需要数年时间。

2. After the initial excitement, the team adopted a more **sober** approach to evaluating the project's feasibility.
   在最初的兴奋之后，团队采取了更为冷静的方式来评估项目的可行性。

3. The judge delivered a **sober** reminder that all citizens must respect the rule of law in a democratic society.
   法官严肃地提醒，在民主社会中所有公民都必须尊重法治。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sober" as "so-bare" — when you are sober, your mind is bare and clear, free from confusion or intoxication.
把"sober"想成"so-bare"——当你清醒时，你的头脑是赤裸而清晰的，没有困惑或醉意。
