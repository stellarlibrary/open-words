# 🍄 spore

🔊 /spɔːr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A minute reproductive unit of fungi, algae, or plants, capable of developing into a new organism. (v.) To produce spores
**中文：** n. 孢子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- seed（种子）
- germ（胚芽）
- reproductive cell（生殖细胞）
- propagule（繁殖体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spore formation / spore dispersal / fungal spore / spore germination**

👉 Example: The fungal spores were carried by the wind across vast distances to colonize new habitats.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **spore** of the fern was dispersed by wind currents and eventually germinated in the moist forest soil.
   蕨类植物的孢子被气流传播，最终在潮湿的森林土壤中萌发。

2. Scientists discovered that bacterial **spores** can survive extreme temperatures, radiation, and even the vacuum of outer space.
   科学家发现细菌孢子能够在极端温度、辐射甚至外太空真空中存活。

3. The release of millions of **spores** from the mushroom cap ensures the species' continued survival and reproduction.
   蘑菇伞盖释放数百万孢子，确保了该物种的持续生存和繁殖。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spore" as "s-pour" — spores pour out of fungi in massive numbers, spreading everywhere to reproduce.
把"spore"想成"s-pour"——孢子从真菌中大量涌出，四处传播以繁殖。
