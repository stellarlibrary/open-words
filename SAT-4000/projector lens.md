# 🔍 projector lens

🔊 /prəˈdʒɛktər lɛnz/<br>
🏷️ *noun phrase | 名词短语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The optical lens component in a projector that focuses and directs light to produce a clear image on a surface
**中文：** n. 放映机镜头；投影仪镜头

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- projection lens（投影镜头）
- optical lens（光学镜头）
- focusing lens（聚焦镜头）
- condenser lens（聚光镜头）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clean the projector lens / replace the projector lens / projector lens focus / projector lens assembly**

👉 Example: A scratched projector lens can significantly degrade the quality of the displayed image.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technician carefully cleaned the **projector lens** to remove dust particles that were causing blurry images during presentations.
   技术人员仔细清洁了放映机镜头，以去除在演示过程中导致图像模糊的灰尘颗粒。

2. A high-quality **projector lens** with superior optical coatings can dramatically improve color accuracy and image sharpness.
   具有优质光学涂层的高品质投影仪镜头可以显著提高色彩准确性和图像清晰度。

3. The cinema upgraded its **projector lens** system to accommodate the new wide-screen format required for modern films.
   该影院升级了其放映机镜头系统，以适应现代电影所需的新宽屏格式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of it as two parts: "projector" (the device that throws light) + "lens" (the glass that focuses it) — the eye of the projector.
把它想成两部分："projector"（投射光线的设备）+ "lens"（聚焦光线的镜片）——投影仪的眼睛。
