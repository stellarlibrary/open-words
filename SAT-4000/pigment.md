# 🎨 pigment

🔊 /ˈpɪɡmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance that gives color to tissue, paint, ink, or other materials; a natural coloring matter in animal or plant cells. (v.) To color or add pigment to something
**中文：** n. 色素；颜料 v. 给……着色

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dye（染料）
- colorant（着色剂）
- tint（色彩）
- hue（色调）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **skin pigment / pigment production / pigment cells / natural pigment / synthetic pigment**

👉 Example: The artist carefully selected each pigment to achieve the perfect shade for the landscape painting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The absence of melanin **pigment** in the skin results in a condition known as albinism, which affects both humans and animals.
   皮肤中黑色素色素的缺失会导致一种称为白化病的疾病，影响人类和动物。

2. Ancient civilizations extracted natural **pigments** from minerals and plants to create vivid paints for their murals and pottery.
   古代文明从矿物和植物中提取天然颜料，为壁画和陶器制作鲜艳的涂料。

3. The biologist explained how **pigment** molecules in leaves absorb specific wavelengths of light during the process of photosynthesis.
   生物学家解释了叶片中的色素分子如何在光合作用过程中吸收特定波长的光。
