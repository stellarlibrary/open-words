# 🌱 regenerate

🔊 /rɪˈdʒenəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To bring new and more vigorous life to; to regrow or be renewed; to reform or revitalize. (adj.) Reformed or reborn.
**中文：** v. 使再生；革新；重建 adj. 再生的；革新的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revitalize（振兴）
- renew（更新）
- restore（恢复）
- rejuvenate（使恢复活力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regenerate tissue / regenerate the economy / regenerate urban areas / regenerate interest**

👉 Example: Scientists are exploring new methods to regenerate damaged nerve tissue in spinal cord injuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city launched an ambitious initiative to **regenerate** the neglected industrial district into a thriving cultural and residential hub.
   该市发起了一项雄心勃勃的计划，将被忽视的工业区重建为繁荣的文化和住宅中心。

2. Certain species of salamanders can **regenerate** entire limbs, a remarkable biological ability that fascinates medical researchers.
   某些蝾螈物种能够再生整个肢体，这种非凡的生物能力令医学研究人员着迷。

3. The new government promised to **regenerate** the struggling economy through comprehensive infrastructure investment and policy reform.
   新政府承诺通过全面的基础设施投资和政策改革来振兴低迷的经济。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "regenerate" as "re-GENERATE" — to generate or create something once more, bringing it back to life or making it new again.
把"regenerate"想成"re-GENERATE"——重新生成或创造某物，使其恢复生机或焕然一新。
