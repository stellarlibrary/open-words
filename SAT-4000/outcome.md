# 🎯 outcome

🔊 /ˈaʊtkʌm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The final result or consequence of an action, process, or situation
**中文：** n. 结果，结局；成果

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- result（结果）
- consequence（后果）
- conclusion（结论）
- upshot（结果）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outcome of the election / positive outcome / determine the outcome / outcome of the experiment**

👉 Example: The outcome of the negotiations will determine the future of trade relations between the two nations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **outcome** of the clinical trial exceeded expectations, demonstrating significant improvement in patient recovery rates.
   这项临床试验的结果超出预期，表明患者康复率有了显著提高。

2. Voters anxiously awaited the **outcome** of the election, which would shape national policy for the next decade.
   选民们焦急地等待选举结果，这将决定未来十年的国家政策走向。

3. The **outcome** of the merger negotiations remained uncertain until both boards reached a unanimous agreement.
   合并谈判的结果一直不确定，直到双方董事会达成一致意见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out + come" — what comes out at the end of a process is the outcome, the final result.
把"outcome"拆成"out（出来）+ come（来）"——在一个过程结束后所"出来"的东西，就是结果。
