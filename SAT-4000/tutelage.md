# 🎓 tutelage

🔊 /ˈtjuːtəlɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Protection or authority over someone or something; guardianship; instruction or teaching
**中文：** n. 监护；指导；保护

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- guidance（指导）
- mentorship（导师指导）
- guardianship（监护）
- instruction（教导）
- supervision（监督）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **under the tutelage of / tutelage and guidance / provide tutelage**

👉 Example: The young pianist flourished under the tutelage of a world-renowned maestro.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Under the **tutelage** of her distinguished professor, the graduate student developed a groundbreaking thesis on molecular biology.
   在她杰出教授的指导下，这位研究生撰写了一篇关于分子生物学的开创性论文。

2. The colonial territories gradually gained independence after decades of foreign **tutelage** and administrative oversight.
   经过数十年的外国监护和行政管理，这些殖民地逐渐获得了独立。

3. Many aspiring artists seek the **tutelage** of experienced mentors to refine their techniques and creative vision.
   许多有抱负的艺术家寻求经验丰富的导师指导，以完善他们的技巧和创作视野。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tutelage" as "tutor + age" — the age or period during which a tutor guides and protects you.
把"tutelage"想成"tutor（导师）+ age（时期）"——由导师指导和保护你的时期。
