# 🕵️ covert

🔊 /ˈkoʊvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A hiding place or shelter. (adj.) Not openly acknowledged or displayed; secret or concealed
**中文：** n. 隐藏处 adj. 隐蔽的；隐密的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clandestine（秘密的）
- surreptitious（偷偷摸摸的）
- concealed（隐藏的）
- stealthy（隐秘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **covert operation / covert surveillance / covert action / covert mission**

👉 Example: The intelligence agency launched a covert operation to gather critical information behind enemy lines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist uncovered evidence of a **covert** surveillance program that had been monitoring citizens without proper judicial authorization.
   记者揭露了一项未经适当司法授权就监控公民的秘密监视项目的证据。

2. Diplomatic negotiations often involve **covert** channels of communication that operate entirely outside the sphere of public awareness.
   外交谈判通常涉及完全在公众意识之外运作的秘密沟通渠道。

3. The committee launched an investigation into the **covert** funding arrangements that had allegedly influenced several key legislative decisions.
   委员会对据称影响了几项关键立法决定的秘密资金安排展开了调查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "covert" as "covered" — something that is covered up and hidden from plain sight, like a secret operation.
把"covert"联想成"covered（被遮盖的）"——被遮盖起来、隐藏在众人视线之外的东西，就像秘密行动一样。
