# 🏘️ borough

🔊 /ˈbɜːroʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A self-governing incorporated town or district; an administrative division of a large city
**中文：** n. 自治市镇；区

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- district（区）
- township（镇区）
- municipality（市政区）
- precinct（选区）
- ward（行政区）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **borough council / borough president / borough of / metropolitan borough**

👉 Example: Each borough of New York City maintains its own distinct cultural identity and administrative structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **borough** council approved funding for the construction of a new community center to serve the growing local population.
   区议会批准了为不断增长的当地人口建设新社区中心的资金。

2. Manhattan remains the most densely populated **borough** in New York City, attracting millions of visitors and commuters each year.
   曼哈顿仍然是纽约市人口最稠密的行政区，每年吸引数百万游客和通勤者。

3. Residents of the small **borough** voted overwhelmingly to preserve the historic town square rather than permit commercial redevelopment.
   这个小自治市镇的居民以压倒性多数投票决定保留历史悠久的城镇广场，而非允许商业重新开发。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "borough" as "burrow" — a small, self-contained area like a rabbit's burrow, representing a self-governing community within a larger region.
把"borough"想成"burrow"（洞穴）——一个小而自成一体的区域，就像兔子的洞穴，代表大区域中一个自治的社区。
