# 📚 lexicon

🔊 /ˈlɛksɪkɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A dictionary or the complete set of words and terms used in a particular language, subject, or by a specific person or group
**中文：** n. 词典，辞典；（某语言、学科或群体的）全部词汇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dictionary（词典）
- vocabulary（词汇）
- glossary（术语表）
- wordbook（词汇手册）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the lexicon of / enter the lexicon / expand one's lexicon / a rich lexicon**

👉 Example: The word "selfie" has only recently entered the popular lexicon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor compiled a comprehensive **lexicon** of technical terms used frequently in the field of molecular biology.
   教授编纂了一部分子生物学领域常用技术术语的综合词典。

2. New words continue to enter the English **lexicon** each year as technology and culture evolve rapidly around the world.
   随着技术和文化在全球范围内的快速发展，每年都有新词进入英语词汇。

3. Her extensive **lexicon** allowed her to articulate complex philosophical arguments with remarkable precision and clarity in writing.
   她丰富的词汇量使她能够在写作中以非凡的精确性和清晰度阐述复杂的哲学论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lexicon" as "lex" (law/word in Latin) + "icon" — an iconic collection of words, like a dictionary that is the icon of language.
把"lexicon"想成"lex"（拉丁语中的词/法）+"icon"（图标）——一个标志性的词汇集合，就像语言的图标一样的词典。
