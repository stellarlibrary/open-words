# 🏠 commodious

🔊 /kəˈmoʊdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Spacious and comfortable; roomy; conveniently arranged with ample space for use
**中文：** adj. 宽敞的；方便的；宽裕的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spacious（宽敞的）
- roomy（宽大的）
- capacious（容量大的）
- ample（充足的）
- expansive（广阔的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commodious quarters / commodious dwelling / commodious interior / a commodious hall**

👉 Example: The renovated library featured a commodious reading room that could seat over two hundred visitors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's residence was a **commodious** estate with numerous guest rooms, expansive gardens, and several elegant reception halls.
   大使的官邸是一座宽敞的庄园，拥有众多客房、广阔的花园和几个优雅的接待大厅。

2. Unlike the cramped apartments in the city center, the suburban homes offered **commodious** living spaces ideal for growing families.
   与市中心狭小的公寓不同，郊区的住宅提供了宽敞的生活空间，非常适合不断壮大的家庭。

3. The newly constructed conference center provided a **commodious** venue for hosting international symposiums and large-scale academic gatherings.
   新建的会议中心为举办国际研讨会和大型学术聚会提供了宽敞的场所。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "commodious" as related to "accommodate" — both share the root meaning of having enough room to fit comfortably.
把"commodious"和"accommodate"联系起来——两者都有足够空间、舒适容纳的意思。
