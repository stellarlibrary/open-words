# 🐺 carnivorous

🔊 /kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeding on other animals; relating to or characteristic of organisms that eat flesh or animal tissue
**中文：** adj. 食肉的；肉食性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- predatory（掠食的）
- flesh-eating（食肉的）
- meat-eating（吃肉的）
- zoophagous（食动物的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **carnivorous animal / carnivorous plant / carnivorous species / carnivorous diet / carnivorous dinosaur**

👉 Example: Venus flytraps are among the most well-known carnivorous plants in the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The expedition discovered a previously unknown **carnivorous** plant species capable of trapping and digesting small insects in the tropical canopy.
   探险队发现了一种此前未知的食肉植物物种，能够在热带树冠中捕获和消化小昆虫。

2. Paleontologists determined that the fossilized remains belonged to a large **carnivorous** dinosaur that once dominated the ancient ecosystem.
   古生物学家确定这些化石遗骸属于一种曾经主宰古代生态系统的大型食肉恐龙。

3. The documentary examined how **carnivorous** mammals have evolved specialized teeth and claws adapted for capturing and consuming prey.
   这部纪录片研究了食肉哺乳动物如何进化出适合捕获和食用猎物的特化牙齿和爪子。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carnivorous" as "carni-" (flesh) + "-vorous" (eating) — the suffix "-vorous" appears in many words like "herbivorous" and "omnivorous," all describing eating habits.
把"carnivorous"想成"carni-"（肉）+ "-vorous"（吃的）——后缀"-vorous"出现在许多词中，如"herbivorous"（食草的）和"omnivorous"（杂食的），都描述食性。
