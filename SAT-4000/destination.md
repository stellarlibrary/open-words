# 🗺️ destination

🔊 /ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The place to which someone or something is going or being sent; the ultimate goal or purpose
**中文：** n. 目的地；终点；目标

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- terminus（终点）
- goal（目标）
- endpoint（终点）
- target（目标）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **final destination / travel destination / reach a destination / popular destination**

👉 Example: The cargo shipment arrived at its intended destination three days behind the original schedule.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The remote mountain village has become an increasingly popular **destination** for adventurous travelers seeking unspoiled natural landscapes.
   这个偏远的山村已成为寻求原始自然风光的冒险旅行者越来越热门的目的地。

2. Scholars debate whether the ultimate **destination** of technological progress is human liberation or unprecedented societal dependence.
   学者们争论技术进步的最终目标究竟是人类解放还是前所未有的社会依赖。

3. The new railway line will connect major urban centers and reduce travel time to previously inaccessible **destinations** significantly.
   新铁路线将连接主要城市中心，并大幅缩短前往此前难以到达的目的地的旅行时间。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "destin-ation" as "destiny + nation" — your destination is the place destiny has determined for you to reach.
把"destination"想成"destiny（命运）+ nation（国度）"——你的目的地就是命运为你安排要到达的地方。
