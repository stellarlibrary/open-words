# 🕊️ reverent

🔊 /ˈrɛvərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing deep respect, devotion, or veneration; characterized by a feeling of profound awe and reverence
**中文：** adj. 虔诚的；恭敬的；尊敬的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- respectful（恭敬的）
- devout（虔诚的）
- deferential（恭顺的）
- worshipful（崇敬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reverent tone / reverent silence / reverent attitude / reverent admiration**

👉 Example: The audience maintained a reverent silence as the conductor raised the baton for the memorial performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The congregation adopted a **reverent** posture as the ancient hymn echoed through the vaulted cathedral during the solemn ceremony.
   当古老的赞美诗在庄严仪式中回荡在拱形大教堂时，会众们保持着虔诚的姿态。

2. The biographer's **reverent** treatment of the civil rights leader's legacy offered readers a deeply moving portrait of moral courage.
   传记作者对这位民权领袖遗产的恭敬描写为读者呈现了一幅关于道德勇气的感人画像。

3. Museum visitors spoke in **reverent** whispers as they stood before the original manuscript of the nation's founding constitutional document.
   博物馆参观者站在国家建国宪法文件的原稿前，以恭敬的低语交谈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reverent" as "revere" + "ent" — someone who reveres becomes reverent, full of deep respect and quiet admiration.
把"reverent"想成"revere"加上"ent"——一个崇敬他人的人变得虔诚恭敬，充满深深的尊重和安静的钦佩。
