# 🔑 access

🔊 /ˈæksɛs/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The means or right to approach, enter, or use something; a way of reaching a place. (v.) To obtain, retrieve, or make use of something.
**中文：** (n.) 进入；使用权；通路；途径。(v.) 使用；存取；接近。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- entry（进入）
- admission（准入）
- approach（途径）
- retrieve（存取）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **access to information**, **gain access**, **access a database**, **have access to**

👉 Example: Students must have access to reliable internet for online learning.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new legislation aims to guarantee equal **access** to healthcare for all citizens.
   新立法旨在保障所有公民平等获得医疗保健的权利。

2. Researchers can now **access** the university's digital archives from any location worldwide.
   研究人员现在可以从全球任何地点访问该大学的数字档案。

3. The narrow mountain pass provided the only **access** to the remote village beyond the ridge.
   狭窄的山口是通往山脊那边偏远村庄的唯一通道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "access" as "a key to success" — you need access (a way in) to reach success.
把 "access" 想成 "a key to success"——你需要 access（通道）才能通往成功。
