# 🌊 undulate

🔊 /ˈʌndjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To move with a smooth, wavelike motion; to rise and fall in a flowing pattern. (adj.) Having a wavy form or surface
**中文：** v. 起伏，波动 adj. 波动的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ripple（涟漪般波动）
- fluctuate（波动）
- surge（起伏）
- oscillate（振荡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **undulate across / undulate gently / undulating hills / undulating terrain / undulating waves**

👉 Example: The tall grass began to undulate gracefully as a warm breeze swept across the open meadow.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rolling countryside seemed to **undulate** endlessly beneath the afternoon sun, creating a mesmerizing pastoral landscape.
   起伏的乡村在午后的阳光下似乎无尽地波动，营造出迷人的田园风光。

2. The dancer's body appeared to **undulate** with fluid precision, captivating every member of the spellbound audience.
   舞者的身体似乎以流畅的精确度起伏波动，吸引了在场每一位入迷的观众。

3. Seismologists observed the ground **undulate** for several seconds before the primary tremor struck the densely populated coastal region.
   地震学家观测到地面起伏了数秒，随后主震袭击了人口密集的沿海地区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "und-ulate" as containing "unda" (Latin for wave) — something that undulates moves like a wave.
把"undulate"想成包含"unda"（拉丁语中"波浪"的意思）——起伏波动的事物像波浪一样运动。
