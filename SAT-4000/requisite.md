# 📋 requisite

🔊 /ˈrekwɪzɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Made necessary by particular circumstances or regulations; essential, indispensable. (n.) A thing that is necessary for the achievement of a specified end
**中文：** adj. 需要的；必不可少的；必备的 n. 必需品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- essential（必要的）
- necessary（必需的）
- indispensable（不可缺少的）
- required（必备的）
- prerequisite（前提条件）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **requisite skills / requisite qualifications / requisite knowledge / a requisite for success**

👉 Example: Candidates must demonstrate the requisite competence before being considered for the senior position.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The applicant lacked the **requisite** experience and professional certifications needed to qualify for the advanced engineering position.
   该申请人缺乏获得高级工程职位所需的必备经验和专业认证。

2. Adequate funding is a fundamental **requisite** for conducting large-scale clinical trials that meet international research standards.
   充足的资金是进行符合国际研究标准的大规模临床试验的基本必需条件。

3. The training program ensures that all participants acquire the **requisite** skills to operate the sophisticated laboratory equipment safely.
   该培训项目确保所有参与者获得安全操作精密实验室设备所需的必备技能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "requisite" as "require-site" — a site that requires certain things before you can enter, like essential qualifications or credentials.
把"requisite"想成"require-site"——一个在你进入之前需要某些东西的场所，就像必备的资格或证书一样。
