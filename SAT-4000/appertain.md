# 🔗 appertain

🔊 /ˌæpərˈteɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To belong to or be connected with something as a rightful part, attribute, or function; to relate or be relevant to
**中文：** 属于；和……有关；与……相关联

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pertain（关于）
- relate（涉及）
- belong（属于）
- concern（关系到）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **appertain to a subject / appertain to one's duties / rights appertaining to / privileges appertaining to**

👉 Example: The privileges appertaining to the office of the president are clearly outlined in the constitution.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. All rights and responsibilities **appertaining** to property ownership are clearly defined in the municipal legal code.
   所有与财产所有权相关的权利和责任在市政法律法规中都有明确界定。

2. The committee addressed only those matters that **appertain** to public safety and environmental protection in the region.
   委员会仅讨论了与该地区公共安全和环境保护有关的事项。

3. The benefits **appertaining** to senior faculty members include research funding and priority access to university facilities.
   资深教职人员所享有的福利包括科研经费和优先使用大学设施的权利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "appertain" as "a-PERTAIN" — it literally means to pertain or belong to something, with the prefix "ap-" reinforcing the connection.
把"appertain"想成"a-PERTAIN"——它的字面意思就是属于或关于某事物，前缀"ap-"加强了这种关联。
