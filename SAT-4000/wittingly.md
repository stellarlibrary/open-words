# 🎯 wittingly

🔊 /ˈwɪtɪŋli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) In a deliberate and intentional manner; with full awareness and knowledge of what one is doing
**中文：** adv. 有意地；故意地；存心地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deliberately（故意地）
- intentionally（有意地）
- knowingly（明知地）
- consciously（有意识地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wittingly or unwittingly / wittingly participated / wittingly deceived / wittingly chose to**

👉 Example: The official wittingly concealed the financial irregularities from the oversight committee.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that several executives had **wittingly** participated in the scheme to defraud unsuspecting shareholders.
   调查显示，几位高管故意参与了欺骗不知情股东的计划。

2. No reasonable person would **wittingly** enter an agreement that so clearly disadvantaged their own financial interests.
   没有理性的人会故意签订一份如此明显损害自身经济利益的协议。

3. The diplomat **wittingly** omitted certain compromising details from the official report submitted to the international tribunal.
   这位外交官故意在提交给国际法庭的官方报告中遗漏了某些有损形象的细节。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wittingly" as "with wit" — doing something with full mental awareness, knowing exactly what you are doing.
把"wittingly"想成"wit（知道）+ -ingly（地）"——在完全知情的状态下做某事，即故意地、有意地。
