# 🏔️ seclusion

🔊 /sɪˈkluːʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being isolated or shut off from others; a secluded or sheltered place; privacy and solitude
**中文：** n. 隔离

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- isolation（隔离）
- solitude（独处）
- privacy（隐私）
- retreat（隐退处）
- withdrawal（退隐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in seclusion / live in seclusion / seek seclusion / total seclusion**

👉 Example: After the scandal, the former official retreated into complete seclusion at his countryside residence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The celebrated poet spent the final decade of her life in voluntary **seclusion**, rarely receiving visitors or attending public events.
   这位著名诗人在她生命的最后十年中自愿隐居，很少接待访客或参加公共活动。

2. The remote island offered the perfect **seclusion** that the research team needed to conduct their sensitive experiments without interference.
   这座偏远的岛屿提供了研究团队所需的完美隔离环境，使他们能够不受干扰地进行敏感实验。

3. Many religious traditions emphasize periods of deliberate **seclusion** as essential for spiritual reflection, meditation, and personal renewal.
   许多宗教传统强调刻意的隐居时期对精神反思、冥想和个人更新至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "seclusion" as "se-clusion" from "seclude + ion" — the state of being closed off, like being in your own private enclosure away from the world.
把"seclusion"想成"seclude（隔离）+ ion（状态）"——被关闭隔离的状态，就像在自己的私人围场中远离世界。
