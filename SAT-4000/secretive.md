# 🤫 secretive

🔊 /ˈsiːkrətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inclined to secrecy; furtive; promoting secretion
**中文：** adj. 隐匿的，隐秘的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clandestine（秘密的）
- furtive（偷偷摸摸的）
- covert（隐蔽的）
- reticent（沉默寡言的）
- surreptitious（暗中的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **secretive about / secretive nature / secretive behavior / secretive manner**

👉 Example: The corporation remained remarkably secretive about its plans for the upcoming product launch.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat was highly **secretive** about the ongoing negotiations, refusing to disclose any details to the press.
   这位外交官对正在进行的谈判高度保密，拒绝向媒体透露任何细节。

2. Her **secretive** behavior aroused suspicion among colleagues who had previously considered her open and trustworthy.
   她隐秘的行为引起了同事们的怀疑，此前他们一直认为她坦诚可信。

3. The organization operated in a **secretive** manner, concealing its financial records from regulatory authorities and auditors.
   该组织以隐秘的方式运作，向监管机构和审计师隐瞒其财务记录。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "secret-ive" — someone who is full of "secrets" and always keeps things hidden from others.
把"secretive"想成充满"secret（秘密）"的人——总是把事情藏着掖着不让别人知道。
