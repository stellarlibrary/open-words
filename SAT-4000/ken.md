# 👁️ ken

🔊 /kɛn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The range of one's knowledge, understanding, or perception; scope of vision or comprehension
**中文：** n. 视野范围；见地；知识范围；知识领域

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- perception（感知）
- comprehension（理解力）
- awareness（认知）
- purview（范围）
- cognizance（认识）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beyond one's ken / within one's ken / outside the ken of**

👉 Example: The intricacies of quantum mechanics are beyond the ken of most casual observers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The complexities of international trade law remain well beyond the **ken** of the average citizen today.
   国际贸易法的复杂性至今仍远超普通公民的认知范围。

2. Advanced theoretical physics lies outside the **ken** of researchers who lack rigorous mathematical training.
   高等理论物理超出了缺乏严格数学训练的研究者的知识范围。

3. The professor argued that certain metaphysical questions are simply beyond the **ken** of empirical science alone.
   这位教授认为，某些形而上学问题完全超出了经验科学的认知范畴。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ken" as "can" — what you "can" see or know is within your ken; what you "can't" is beyond your ken.
把"ken"想成"can"——你"能"看见或知道的事物在你的ken（视野）之内；你"不能"的则超出你的ken。
