# 🤝 consensus

🔊 /kənˈsensəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A general agreement among a group of people; the collective opinion or position reached by most of those concerned.
**中文：** n. 一致；舆论；共识

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agreement（一致）
- unanimity（全体一致）
- accord（协议）
- harmony（和谐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach a consensus / general consensus / build consensus / consensus among / consensus on**

👉 Example: The board of directors reached a consensus on the proposed merger after several rounds of deliberation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After extensive deliberation, the committee finally reached a **consensus** on the allocation of funding for the upcoming research projects.
   经过广泛的审议，委员会终于就即将开展的研究项目的资金分配达成了共识。

2. The scientific **consensus** holds that human activities are the primary driver of accelerating climate change across the globe.
   科学界的共识认为，人类活动是全球气候变化加速的主要驱动因素。

3. Building **consensus** among stakeholders with competing interests remains one of the greatest challenges in public policy formation.
   在利益相互竞争的利益相关者之间建立共识，仍然是公共政策制定中最大的挑战之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-sensus" as "with (con) the same sense (sensus)" — consensus means everyone shares the same sense or opinion on a matter.
把"consensus"想成"拥有(con)相同的感觉(sensus)"——共识意味着每个人对某件事有相同的看法。
