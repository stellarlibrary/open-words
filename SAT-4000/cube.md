# 🎲 cube

🔊 /kjuːb/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A three-dimensional solid with six equal square faces; the product of a number multiplied by itself twice. (v.) To cut into cube shapes; to raise to the third power
**中文：** n. 立方；立方体 v. 使成立方形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- block（块）
- hexahedron（六面体）
- square solid（正方体）
- die（骰子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ice cube / cube root / cube the number / Rubik's cube / cube-shaped**

👉 Example: The mathematics instructor asked students to cube the variable and simplify the resulting expression.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect designed the modern art gallery as a massive glass **cube** suspended above a reflecting pool.
   建筑师将现代艺术画廊设计为悬浮在倒影池上方的巨大玻璃立方体。

2. To solve the equation, students must first **cube** the unknown variable and then isolate the remaining constant terms.
   要解这个方程，学生必须先将未知变量进行立方运算，然后分离剩余的常数项。

3. The laboratory technician carefully measured each ice **cube** to ensure uniform dimensions for the controlled thermal experiment.
   实验室技术人员仔细测量每个冰块，以确保受控热实验中尺寸均匀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a Rubik's Cube — a perfect cube with six colorful square faces, each side equal, representing the geometric and mathematical concept.
想想魔方（Rubik's Cube）——一个有六个彩色正方形面的完美立方体，每边相等，代表几何和数学概念。
