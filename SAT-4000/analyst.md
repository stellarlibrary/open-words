# 🧑‍💼 analyst

🔊 /ˈænəlɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who conducts detailed examination and interpretation of data or complex subjects; a specialist in psychoanalysis
**中文：** 分析者；精神分析医师

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examiner（审查员）
- evaluator（评估者）
- researcher（研究员）
- commentator（评论员）
- expert（专家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **financial analyst / data analyst / political analyst / market analyst / intelligence analyst / analyst predicts**

👉 Example: The financial analyst recommended diversifying the portfolio to reduce exposure to volatile market sectors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The intelligence **analyst** identified a pattern in the intercepted communications that proved critical to preventing the security breach.
   情报分析师在截获的通讯中识别出一个模式，对防止安全漏洞至关重要。

2. A prominent political **analyst** predicted that the upcoming election would fundamentally reshape the balance of power in the legislature.
   一位著名的政治分析师预测，即将到来的选举将从根本上重塑立法机构的权力平衡。

3. The data **analyst** presented her findings to the board, demonstrating a clear correlation between customer satisfaction and revenue growth.
   数据分析师向董事会展示了她的发现，证明了客户满意度与收入增长之间的明确关联。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "analyst" as "analysis + -ist (person)" — the person who performs analysis, someone whose job is to break things down and examine them.
把"analyst"理解为"analysis（分析）+ -ist（人）"——进行分析的人，其工作就是分解和审查事物。
