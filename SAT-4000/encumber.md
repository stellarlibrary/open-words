# 🏋️ encumber

🔊 /ɪnˈkʌmbər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To weigh down or burden; to hinder or impede the function or movement of
**中文：** v. 阻塞；妨害；拖累；阻碍

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hinder（阻碍）
- hamper（妨碍）
- burden（拖累）
- impede（阻挡）
- obstruct（阻塞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **encumber with debt / encumber progress / encumber movement / encumbered by regulations**

👉 Example: The heavy regulations encumber small businesses trying to enter the competitive market.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The outdated bureaucratic procedures continue to **encumber** the agency's ability to respond swiftly to urgent situations.
   过时的官僚程序继续妨碍该机构迅速应对紧急情况的能力。

2. Excessive debt will inevitably **encumber** the nation's economic growth and reduce opportunities for future generations.
   过多的债务将不可避免地拖累国家的经济增长，并减少子孙后代的机会。

3. The soldiers were heavily **encumbered** by their equipment, making the long march through mountainous terrain extremely arduous.
   士兵们被沉重的装备拖累，使得穿越山区的长途行军极为艰苦。

---

Think of "en-cumber" as carrying a heavy "cucumber" — imagine lugging a giant cucumber that weighs you down and slows your movement.
把"encumber"想成背着一根沉重的"cucumber（黄瓜）"——想象你扛着一根巨大的黄瓜，压得你走不动路。
