# 🔍 parse

🔊 /pɑːrs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To analyze a sentence or word grammatically; to examine or interpret something methodically and in detail
**中文：** v. 从语法上分析；解析；仔细审查

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- analyze（分析）
- dissect（剖析）
- deconstruct（解构）
- interpret（解释）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **parse a sentence / parse the data / parse the meaning / parse the language / difficult to parse**

👉 Example: The linguist carefully parsed the ancient inscription to determine its grammatical structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literature students were required to **parse** each stanza of the poem to identify its underlying syntactic and rhetorical patterns.
   文学系学生被要求对诗歌的每一节进行语法分析，以识别其潜在的句法和修辞模式。

2. Analysts struggled to **parse** the ambiguous language of the treaty, which had been deliberately crafted to permit multiple interpretations.
   分析人士难以解析该条约模糊的措辞，因为它被故意设计为允许多种解释。

3. The software engineer developed an algorithm capable of **parsing** vast quantities of unstructured textual data with remarkable speed and accuracy.
   软件工程师开发了一种算法，能够以惊人的速度和准确性解析大量非结构化文本数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "parse" as "parts" — to parse is to break something into its grammatical parts and analyze each piece.
把"parse"想成"parts（部分）"——解析就是把东西拆分成语法部分并逐一分析。
