# 🍼 coddle

🔊 /ˈkɑːdəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To treat with excessive care or kindness; to pamper or overprotect; to cook gently in water just below boiling
**中文：** v. 娇养；溺爱；悉心照料；用文火煮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pamper（娇惯）
- spoil（溺爱）
- indulge（纵容）
- mollycoddle（过分呵护）
- baby（娇养）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coddle a child / coddle employees / coddle eggs / stop coddling**

👉 Example: Critics accused the administration of coddling the industry by refusing to enforce stricter environmental standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Parents who **coddle** their children excessively may inadvertently hinder the development of resilience and independent thinking.
   过度溺爱孩子的父母可能会无意中阻碍其韧性和独立思考能力的发展。

2. The editorial argued that the government had been **coddling** wealthy corporations while ordinary citizens struggled with inflation.
   社论认为政府一直在纵容富裕企业，而普通公民却在与通货膨胀作斗争。

3. Rather than **coddling** underperforming students, the new policy emphasized accountability and structured academic intervention programs.
   新政策没有娇惯表现不佳的学生，而是强调责任制和有组织的学术干预项目。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "coddle" as "cuddle" — when you cuddle someone too much, you are coddling them with excessive warmth and care.
把"coddle"想成"cuddle"（拥抱）——当你过度拥抱某人时，你就是在用过多的温暖和关怀来溺爱他们。
