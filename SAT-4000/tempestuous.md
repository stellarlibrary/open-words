# 🌪️ tempestuous

🔊 /tɛmˈpɛstʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Characterized by strong and turbulent or conflicting emotion; stormy; violently agitated
**中文：** adj. 有暴风雨的；暴乱的；剧烈的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- turbulent（动荡的）
- stormy（暴风雨的）
- volatile（易变的）
- tumultuous（喧嚣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tempestuous relationship / tempestuous weather / tempestuous emotions / tempestuous debate**

👉 Example: Their tempestuous relationship was marked by passionate arguments and equally passionate reconciliations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee's **tempestuous** debate over the proposed budget lasted well into the early hours of the morning.
   委员会就拟议预算进行的激烈辩论一直持续到凌晨。

2. After a **tempestuous** period of political upheaval, the nation gradually restored democratic institutions and civil order.
   经历了一段动荡的政治变革之后，该国逐步恢复了民主制度和社会秩序。

3. The **tempestuous** seas forced the captain to abandon the original route and seek shelter in a nearby harbor.
   暴风雨般的海况迫使船长放弃原定航线，前往附近港口避难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tempest" (a violent storm) plus "-uous" — something full of tempests is tempestuous, wild and uncontrollable.
把"tempestuous"想成"tempest（暴风雨）"加上"-uous"——充满暴风雨的，狂野且无法控制。
