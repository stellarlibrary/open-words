# 🏛️ academician

🔊 /ˌækədəˈmɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A member of an academy or learned society, especially one devoted to the arts or sciences; a distinguished scholar.
**中文：** 院士；学会会员；学术界人士。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scholar（学者）
- fellow（院士）
- intellectual（知识分子）
- savant（博学之士）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a renowned academician**, **elected as an academician**, **academician of the academy**

👉 Example: She was elected as an academician of the National Academy of Sciences.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **academician** delivered a keynote lecture on the future of artificial intelligence.
   这位院士发表了关于人工智能未来的主题演讲。

2. As a respected **academician**, she contributed groundbreaking research to the field of genetics.
   作为一位受人尊敬的院士，她为遗传学领域做出了开创性的研究贡献。

3. The committee nominated several distinguished **academicians** to review the proposed curriculum.
   委员会提名了几位杰出的学者来审查拟议的课程方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Academician" = "academic" + "-ian" (a person). An academician is the person who excels in academia.
"Academician" = "academic"（学术的）+ "-ian"（表示人），院士就是学术领域中出类拔萃的人。
