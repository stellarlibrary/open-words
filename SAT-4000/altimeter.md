# 🛩️ altimeter

🔊 /ælˈtɪmɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An instrument used for measuring the altitude or height of an object above a fixed level, especially above sea level.
**中文：** 测高仪；高度计

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- altitude gauge（高度表）
- elevation meter（海拔计）
- hypsometer（测高仪）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **read the altimeter, barometric altimeter, radar altimeter, altimeter reading, calibrate the altimeter**
👉 Example: The pilot checked the altimeter before initiating the descent toward the runway.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pilot consulted the **altimeter** repeatedly as the aircraft navigated through dense cloud cover over the mountain range.
   飞行员在飞机穿越山脉上空浓密云层时反复查看高度计。

2. Modern digital **altimeters** provide considerably more accurate readings than their earlier mechanical counterparts did.
   现代数字高度计比早期的机械高度计提供更为精确的读数。

3. The mountaineer strapped an **altimeter** to her wrist to monitor elevation changes during the challenging summit attempt.
   登山者将高度计绑在手腕上，以便在挑战性的登顶过程中监测海拔变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alti(tude) + meter" — it literally measures altitude. The Latin root "altus" means high, and "meter" means to measure.
把 altimeter 拆分成 "alti（altitude 高度）+ meter（测量仪）"——字面意思就是测量高度的仪器。
