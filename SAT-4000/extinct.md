# 🦕 extinct

🔊 /ɪkˈstɪŋkt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** No longer in existence; having died out completely. (Of a volcano) no longer active; having ceased to erupt
**中文：** adj. 灭绝的，绝种的；熄灭的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vanished（消失的）
- defunct（已灭绝的）
- obsolete（废弃的）
- nonexistent（不存在的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **become extinct / go extinct / extinct species / extinct volcano**

👉 Example: The dodo bird went extinct in the seventeenth century due to human activity and habitat destruction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists warn that numerous marine species could become **extinct** within decades if ocean pollution continues unabated.
   科学家警告说，如果海洋污染持续不减，许多海洋物种可能在几十年内灭绝。

2. The woolly mammoth has been **extinct** for approximately ten thousand years, yet researchers still study its preserved remains.
   猛犸象已经灭绝了大约一万年，但研究人员仍在研究其保存下来的遗骸。

3. Several ancient languages are now essentially **extinct**, surviving only in written records and scholarly translations.
   一些古老的语言现在基本上已经消亡，仅存于书面记录和学术翻译中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-tinct" as "exit + tinct (tinge/trace)" — all traces have exited, nothing remains.
把"extinct"想成"exit + tinct（痕迹）"——所有痕迹都已消失，什么都不剩了。
