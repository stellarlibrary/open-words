# 🔬 bacterium

🔊 /bækˈtɪəriəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A single microscopic organism belonging to the group of prokaryotes; the singular form of "bacteria."

**中文：** 细菌（单数形式）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- microbe（微生物）
- germ（病菌）
- microorganism（微生物）
- pathogen（病原体）
- bacillus（杆菌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Bacterium" is the singular Latin form, while "bacteria" is the plural. In scientific writing, precision matters: use "bacterium" when referring to a single organism and "bacteria" when referring to multiple. Many English speakers mistakenly use "bacteria" as both singular and plural.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist isolated a single **bacterium** from the sample and examined its structure under an electron microscope.
   （科学家从样本中分离出一个**细菌**，并在电子显微镜下检查了它的结构。）

2. Each **bacterium** in the colony reproduced rapidly, doubling its population in less than twenty minutes under ideal conditions.
   （菌落中的每个**细菌**都快速繁殖，在理想条件下不到二十分钟就将其数量翻倍。）

3. The newly identified **bacterium** proved resistant to all conventional antibiotics, alarming the medical research community greatly.
   （新发现的这种**细菌**对所有常规抗生素都具有抗药性，这极大地震惊了医学研究界。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

The "-um" ending in "bacterium" signals it is singular in Latin (like "datum" vs. "data"). Remember: one bacterium, many bacteria.

"Bacterium"的"-um"词尾表示它是拉丁语的单数形式（就像"datum"对"data"）。记住：一个bacterium，许多bacteria。
