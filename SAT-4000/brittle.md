# 💔 brittle

🔊 /ˈbrɪtl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Hard but easily broken or shattered; lacking warmth or flexibility; fragile in character or temperament
**中文：** adj. 易碎的，脆弱的；易生气的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fragile（易碎的）
- breakable（易破的）
- crisp（脆的）
- delicate（脆弱的）
- frail（脆弱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **brittle bones / brittle material / brittle smile / brittle temper / peanut brittle**

👉 Example: The extremely cold temperatures made the metal components brittle and prone to sudden fracture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged exposure to extreme cold rendered the steel pipes dangerously **brittle**, increasing the risk of catastrophic structural failure.
   长期暴露在极度寒冷中使钢管变得危险地易碎，增加了灾难性结构故障的风险。

2. The archaeologist handled the **brittle** parchment with extraordinary care, knowing that even slight pressure could destroy the ancient text.
   考古学家极其小心地处理这份易碎的羊皮纸，因为即使轻微的压力也可能毁坏这份古代文献。

3. Her **brittle** composure finally shattered when she received the devastating news about the outcome of the medical examination.
   当她收到关于体检结果的坏消息时，她脆弱的镇定终于崩溃了。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "brittle" as "brit-tle" — like a "little bit" of force can break it, because brittle things shatter easily.
把"brittle"想成"brit-tle"——只需"一点点"（a little bit）力量就能打碎它，因为脆弱的东西很容易碎裂。
