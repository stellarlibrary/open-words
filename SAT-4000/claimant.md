# ⚖️ claimant

🔊 /ˈkleɪmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who makes a claim, especially in a legal context; a plaintiff or one who asserts a right to something.
**中文：** 原告；索赔人；提出要求者；声称拥有权利的人。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plaintiff（原告）
- petitioner（申请人）
- applicant（申请者）
- litigant（诉讼当事人）
- appellant（上诉人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the claimant argued / insurance claimant / claimant to the throne / legitimate claimant**

👉 Example: The claimant filed a formal complaint alleging negligence on the part of the manufacturer.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **claimant** presented compelling documentary evidence to support her demand for compensation in the civil proceedings.
   原告提出了有力的书面证据，以支持她在民事诉讼中的赔偿要求。

2. Multiple **claimants** to the disputed estate emerged after the billionaire passed away without leaving a valid written will.
   这位亿万富翁在没有留下有效书面遗嘱的情况下去世后，出现了多名遗产争夺者。

3. The insurance **claimant** was required to submit detailed medical records before the company would process the reimbursement request.
   保险索赔人被要求提交详细的医疗记录，然后保险公司才会处理报销申请。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "claim" + "-ant" (one who) — a claimant is simply "one who claims," someone asserting a right or demanding something owed.
把"claimant"拆分为"claim（要求）"+"-ant（……的人）"——claimant就是"提出要求的人"，即主张权利或索取应得之物的人。
