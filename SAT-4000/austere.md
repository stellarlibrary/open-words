# 🏔️ austere

🔊 /ɒˈstɪər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Severe or strict in manner, attitude, or appearance; having no comforts or luxuries; plain and simple in style; practicing rigorous self-discipline.
**中文：** 严峻的；简朴的；苦行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stern（严厉的）
- spartan（简朴的）
- ascetic（禁欲的）
- rigorous（严格的）
- frugal（节俭的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **austere lifestyle/manner/expression**, **austere surroundings/conditions**, **austere beauty/simplicity**

👉 Example: The monastery's austere interior reflected the monks' commitment to a life of simplicity and prayer.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor maintained an **austere** demeanor in the classroom that commanded immediate respect from every student.
   这位教授在课堂上保持着严峻的举止，令每位学生都肃然起敬。

2. After the financial crisis, the government imposed **austere** budgetary measures that eliminated many public welfare programs.
   金融危机后，政府实施了严格的预算措施，削减了许多公共福利项目。

3. The architect designed an **austere** building with clean geometric lines and no superfluous decorative ornamentation whatsoever.
   建筑师设计了一座简朴的建筑，线条几何而干净，没有任何多余的装饰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "austere" as "au-STEER" — someone who steers away from luxury and comfort, living a plain, strict life. Also sounds like "a steer" — a work animal that lives a tough, no-frills existence.
把"austere"想象成"au-STEER"——一个远离奢华和舒适的人，过着朴素严格的生活。也像"a steer（一头公牛）"——一种过着艰苦朴实生活的劳动动物。
