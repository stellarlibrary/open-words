# 💰 remunerate

🔊 /rɪˈmjuːnəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To pay or compensate someone for services rendered, work done, or losses incurred
**中文：** v. 酬劳；给与报酬；赔偿

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compensate（补偿）
- reimburse（偿还）
- reward（奖赏）
- pay（支付）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remunerate someone for something / remunerate fairly / adequately remunerated**

👉 Example: The company failed to remunerate its employees for the overtime hours they had worked.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization agreed to **remunerate** all volunteers generously for their dedicated service during the relief effort.
   该组织同意为所有志愿者在救援行动中的奉献给予丰厚报酬。

2. Many professionals feel they are not adequately **remunerated** for the specialized expertise they bring to their positions.
   许多专业人士认为他们的专业技能没有得到充分的报酬。

3. The contract stipulated that the consultant would be **remunerated** at a fixed hourly rate for all advisory work.
   合同规定该顾问的所有咨询工作将按固定时薪获得报酬。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-MUNE-rate" as "re-MONEY-rate" — to give money back, to pay someone for their efforts.
把"remunerate"想成"re-MONEY-rate"——把钱还回去，为某人的付出给予报酬。
