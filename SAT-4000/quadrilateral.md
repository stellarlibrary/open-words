# 🔷 quadrilateral

🔊 /ˌkwɒdrɪˈlætərəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A polygon with four sides and four angles. (adj.) Having four sides
**中文：** n. 四边形 adj. 四边形的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- polygon（多边形）
- rectangle（矩形）
- parallelogram（平行四边形）
- four-sided figure（四边图形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **irregular quadrilateral / convex quadrilateral / quadrilateral shape / properties of a quadrilateral**

👉 Example: Students learned to classify different types of quadrilaterals based on their geometric properties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geometry teacher asked students to calculate the interior angles of an irregular **quadrilateral** drawn on the board.
   几何老师要求学生计算画在黑板上的不规则四边形的内角。

2. Every square is a **quadrilateral**, but not every quadrilateral possesses equal sides or right angles.
   每个正方形都是四边形，但并非每个四边形都具有等边或直角。

3. The architect designed the courtyard as a **quadrilateral** enclosure with each side measuring a different length.
   建筑师将庭院设计为一个每边长度不同的四边形围合空间。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quadri-lateral" — "quadri" means four and "lateral" means side, so it literally means "four-sided."
把"quadrilateral"想成"quadri（四）+ lateral（边）"——字面意思就是"四边的"。
