# 😢 bereave

🔊 /bɪˈriːv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To deprive someone of a loved one through death; to rob or strip someone of something valued
**中文：** v. 使丧失；使失去（亲人）；剥夺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deprive（剥夺）
- dispossess（使丧失）
- rob（夺去）
- strip（剥除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bereave of / bereaved family / bereaved parents / recently bereaved**

👉 Example: The tragic accident bereaved the family of their eldest son, leaving them devastated with grief.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The devastating earthquake **bereaved** thousands of families, leaving entire communities to mourn their irreplaceable losses.
   毁灭性的地震使数千个家庭痛失亲人，整个社区都在哀悼无法弥补的损失。

2. War has the power to **bereave** a nation of its youngest and most promising generation of citizens.
   战争有能力使一个国家失去最年轻、最有前途的一代公民。

3. The sudden illness **bereaved** the children of their mother, fundamentally altering the course of their young lives.
   突如其来的疾病夺走了孩子们的母亲，从根本上改变了他们年幼生命的轨迹。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bereave" as "be + reave (rob)" — to bereave means to be robbed of someone you love, leaving emptiness behind.
把"bereave"想成"be + reave（掠夺）"——被夺走所爱之人，留下的只有空虚。
