# 🎭 imbue

🔊 /ɪmˈbjuː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To inspire or permeate with a feeling, quality, or idea; to saturate or soak thoroughly
**中文：** v. 灌输；使感染；使渗透，浸透

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- infuse（灌输）
- permeate（渗透）
- instill（逐渐灌输）
- saturate（浸透）
- inspire（激励）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imbue with meaning / imbue with spirit / imbue with values / imbue with a sense of**

👉 Example: The mentor sought to imbue her students with a strong sense of personal responsibility and integrity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The founding leaders sought to **imbue** the new constitution with principles of justice, equality, and democratic governance.
   建国领袖们力图在新宪法中灌输正义、平等和民主治理的原则。

2. Great literature has the power to **imbue** readers with empathy by exposing them to diverse human experiences and perspectives.
   伟大的文学作品能够通过让读者接触多样的人类经历和视角来使他们产生共情。

3. The artist managed to **imbue** the simple portrait with a profound sense of melancholy that captivated every gallery visitor.
   这位艺术家成功地在这幅简单的肖像中注入了深深的忧郁感，吸引了每一位参观者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imbue" as "im (in) + blue (dye)" — like dipping fabric into blue dye, you soak something thoroughly with a quality or feeling.
把"imbue"想成"im（进入）+ blue（蓝色染料）"——就像把布浸入蓝色染料中，你用某种品质或情感彻底浸透某物。
