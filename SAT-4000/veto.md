# 🚫 veto

🔊 /ˈviːtoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The constitutional right to reject a decision or proposal made by a lawmaking body. (v.) To reject or prohibit authoritatively
**中文：** n. 否决权 v. 否决（尤指他人或其他组织已赞同之事）；禁止

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reject（否决）
- prohibit（禁止）
- overrule（驳回）
- block（阻止）
- ban（禁止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **veto power / exercise a veto / veto a bill / presidential veto / pocket veto**

👉 Example: The president threatened to veto the bill if Congress did not amend the controversial provision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The governor exercised her **veto** to block the proposed legislation that would have weakened environmental protections statewide.
   州长行使了她的**否决权**，阻止了那项将削弱全州环保措施的拟议立法。

2. The United Nations Security Council resolution failed because a permanent member chose to **veto** the proposed sanctions.
   联合国安理会的决议未能通过，因为一个常任理事国选择**否决**了拟议的制裁措施。

3. The board of directors decided to **veto** the merger proposal, citing concerns about long-term financial sustainability.
   董事会决定**否决**合并提案，理由是对长期财务可持续性的担忧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veto" from Latin "I forbid" — a powerful single word that stops everything in its tracks.
"veto"来自拉丁语，意为"我禁止"——一个能让一切止步的强有力的词。
