# 🌟 extrovert

🔊 /ˈekstrəvɜːrt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An outgoing, socially confident person who thrives on interaction with others
**中文：** n. 外向的人；性格外向者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- socialite（社交名流）
- gregarious person（合群的人）
- outgoing person（外向的人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a natural extrovert / extrovert personality / born extrovert**

👉 Example: As a natural extrovert, she excelled in roles that required constant collaboration with colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor, a confirmed **extrovert**, regularly organized interdepartmental gatherings to foster academic collaboration and dialogue.
   这位教授是个十足的外向者，经常组织跨部门聚会以促进学术合作与交流。

2. Research suggests that **extroverts** tend to perform better in careers that demand frequent public speaking and leadership.
   研究表明，外向者往往在需要频繁公开演讲和领导力的职业中表现更出色。

3. Despite being labeled an **extrovert**, she valued quiet reflection as much as lively social engagement with peers.
   尽管被贴上外向者的标签，她同样珍视安静的反思和与同龄人活跃的社交互动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "extro-vert" as "extra outward turning" — an extrovert turns their energy outward toward other people.
把"extrovert"想成"extra向外转"——外向者将自己的精力向外转向他人。
