# 🏅 status

🔊 /ˈsteɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The relative social, professional, or other standing of someone or something; the current state or condition of affairs
**中文：** n. 地位，身份；情形，状况

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- standing（地位）
- position（身份）
- rank（等级）
- condition（状态）
- prestige（声望）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social status / status quo / status symbol / marital status / status update**

👉 Example: The committee requested a status report on the progress of the infrastructure project.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's elevated **status** within the international community granted her access to high-level negotiations between the rival nations.
   这位外交官在国际社会中的崇高地位使她得以参与敌对国家之间的高级别谈判。

2. The board of directors convened an emergency meeting to assess the current **status** of the company's financial obligations.
   董事会召开紧急会议，以评估公司财务义务的当前状况。

3. Maintaining the **status** quo proved increasingly difficult as public demand for institutional reform intensified across the country.
   随着全国各地公众对制度改革的要求日益强烈，维持现状变得越来越困难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "status" as related to "state" — your status is the state of your position or standing in society.
把"status"想成与"state"相关——你的status就是你在社会中的地位或状态。
