# 📃 legible

🔊 /ˈlɛdʒəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Clear enough to be read; easily decipherable in terms of handwriting or print.
**中文：** adj. 清晰的；易读的；易辨认的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- readable（可读的）
- decipherable（可辨认的）
- clear（清楚的）
- distinct（清晰的）
- intelligible（可理解的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **legible handwriting / barely legible / legible print / legible text / legible script**

👉 Example: The professor returned the essay, noting that the student's handwriting was barely legible.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ancient manuscript remained remarkably **legible** despite having been stored in damp conditions for several hundred years.
   这份古代手稿尽管在潮湿环境中保存了数百年，仍然清晰可读。

2. Applicants must ensure that all entries on the official form are **legible** to avoid delays in administrative processing.
   申请人必须确保官方表格上的所有填写内容清楚易读，以避免行政处理延误。

3. The archaeologist used advanced imaging technology to render the faded and weathered inscription fully **legible** once again.
   考古学家使用先进的成像技术使褪色风化的铭文再次变得完全可辨认。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "legible" as "leg-ible" — related to the Latin root "legere" meaning "to read," so legible literally means "able to be read."
把"legible"想成与拉丁词根"legere"（阅读）有关——legible字面意思就是"能够被阅读的"。
