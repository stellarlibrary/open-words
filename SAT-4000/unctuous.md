# 🎭 unctuous

🔊 /ˈʌŋktʃuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Excessively flattering or ingratiating; oily in texture or manner; having a greasy or soapy feel
**中文：** 油质的；虚情假意的；油腔滑调的；谄媚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sycophantic（谄媚的）
- obsequious（奉承的）
- smarmy（虚情假意的）
- ingratiating（讨好的）
- oleaginous（油滑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unctuous manner / unctuous smile / unctuous charm / unctuous flattery**

👉 Example: His unctuous compliments made everyone at the dinner table suspicious of his true motives.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The salesman's **unctuous** demeanor immediately put the experienced customers on guard against his exaggerated claims and false promises.
   推销员油腔滑调的态度立刻让有经验的顾客对他夸大的说辞和虚假承诺产生了警惕。

2. The diplomat delivered an **unctuous** speech praising the dictator, while privately expressing deep contempt for the authoritarian regime.
   这位外交官发表了一番谄媚的演讲赞美独裁者，私下却对这个专制政权深表蔑视。

3. Critics described the novel's villain as an **unctuous** aristocrat whose polished manners concealed a ruthless ambition for absolute power.
   评论家将这部小说的反派描述为一个虚情假意的贵族，优雅的举止下隐藏着对绝对权力的冷酷野心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "unctuous" from Latin "unctum" (ointment) — like someone smearing greasy oil on you with fake flattery; slippery and insincere.
把"unctuous"联想到拉丁语"unctum"（油膏）——就像有人用虚假的奉承往你身上抹油，油滑且不真诚。
