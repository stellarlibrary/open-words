# 🕰️ contemporary

🔊 /kənˈtɛmpərɛri/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person living at the same time as another. (adj.) Belonging to the present time; existing or occurring at the same period
**中文：** n. 同时代的人 adj. 当代的；现代的；同时代的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- modern（现代的）
- current（当前的）
- present-day（当今的）
- peer（同龄人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contemporary art / contemporary society / a contemporary of / contemporary literature**

👉 Example: The museum's collection features contemporary art from emerging artists around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Shakespeare and Cervantes were **contemporary** writers whose masterpieces continue to shape literary traditions across the globe.
   莎士比亚和塞万提斯是同时代的作家，他们的杰作至今仍在塑造全球文学传统。

2. The architect designed the building in a **contemporary** style that harmonized with the surrounding historical district.
   建筑师以当代风格设计了这座建筑，使其与周围的历史街区和谐相融。

3. Many of Einstein's **contemporaries** initially struggled to comprehend the revolutionary implications of his theory of relativity.
   爱因斯坦的许多同时代人起初难以理解他相对论的革命性意义。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-TEMPO-rary" — sharing the same "tempo" or time, like people dancing to the same rhythm of an era.
把"contemporary"想成"同一个tempo（节拍）"——共享同一个时代的节奏，像人们随同一个时代的旋律起舞。
