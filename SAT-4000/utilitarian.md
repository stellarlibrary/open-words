# ⚙️ utilitarian

🔊 /ˌjuːtɪlɪˈtɛːriən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Designed to be useful or practical rather than attractive; relating to utilitarianism. (n.) A person who advocates utilitarianism
**中文：** adj. 功利的；功利主义的；实用的 n. 功利主义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- practical（实用的）
- pragmatic（务实的）
- functional（功能性的）
- utilitarian（实用主义的）
- serviceable（耐用的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **utilitarian approach / utilitarian design / utilitarian philosophy / utilitarian purpose**

👉 Example: The architect favored a utilitarian design that prioritized functionality over aesthetic appeal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher advocated a strictly **utilitarian** approach to ethics, arguing that actions should maximize overall happiness and welfare.
   这位哲学家倡导严格的功利主义伦理方法，认为行为应最大化整体幸福和福祉。

2. The building's **utilitarian** design reflected a commitment to efficiency rather than ornamental beauty or architectural grandeur.
   这座建筑的实用设计体现了对效率而非装饰美感或建筑宏伟的重视。

3. Critics of the **utilitarian** framework contend that it fails to adequately protect the rights of minority populations.
   功利主义框架的批评者认为它未能充分保护少数群体的权利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "util-itarian" — it contains "utility," meaning usefulness. A utilitarian cares most about what is useful and practical.
想想"util-itarian"——它包含"utility（效用）"。功利主义者最关心的是什么有用、什么实际。
