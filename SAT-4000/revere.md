# 🙏 revere

🔊 /rɪˈvɪr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To feel deep respect or admiration for someone or something; to regard with awe and devotion
**中文：** v. 敬畏；尊敬；崇敬

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- venerate（敬仰）
- admire（钦佩）
- esteem（尊重）
- worship（崇拜）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **revere a leader / revere a tradition / revere as a hero / deeply revered**

👉 Example: Communities throughout the region continue to revere the philosopher whose teachings shaped their cultural identity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Citizens across the nation continue to **revere** the founding leaders whose vision and sacrifice established the principles of democratic governance.
   全国各地的公民继续敬仰那些以远见和牺牲奠定民主治理原则的建国领袖。

2. The indigenous community deeply **reveres** the ancient forest, regarding it as a sacred space intimately connected to their ancestral heritage.
   这个原住民社区深深敬畏这片古老的森林，将其视为与祖先遗产紧密相连的神圣之地。

3. Scholars worldwide **revere** the Nobel laureate for her transformative contributions to the field of theoretical quantum mechanics.
   世界各地的学者敬仰这位诺贝尔奖获得者对理论量子力学领域的变革性贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "revere" and Paul Revere — a historical figure so respected and admired that his name became synonymous with American patriotism.
把"revere"和保罗·里维尔联系起来——一位备受尊敬和钦佩的历史人物，他的名字已成为美国爱国主义的代名词。
