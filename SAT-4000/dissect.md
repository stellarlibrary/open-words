# 🔬 dissect

🔊 /dɪˈsɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cut apart methodically for examination; to analyze in great detail
**中文：** v. 解剖；切细；仔细分析；仔细研究

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- analyze（分析）
- examine（检查）
- scrutinize（仔细审查）
- deconstruct（解构）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dissect an argument / dissect a specimen / dissect the data / dissect a problem**

👉 Example: The professor asked students to dissect the author's argument and identify its logical flaws.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Biology students were required to **dissect** a preserved frog in order to study its internal anatomical structures.
   生物学学生被要求解剖一只保存的青蛙，以研究其内部解剖结构。

2. The legal team proceeded to **dissect** every clause of the contract before advising their client to sign it.
   法律团队在建议客户签署合同之前，逐条仔细分析了合同的每一项条款。

3. Critics eagerly **dissect** the filmmaker's latest work, searching for hidden symbolism and thematic connections throughout.
   评论家们热切地剖析这位电影制作人的最新作品，寻找其中隐藏的象征意义和主题关联。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-sect" as "dis" (apart) + "sect" (cut) — to cut something apart to examine every piece.
把"dissect"想成"dis"（分开）+"sect"（切割）——把东西切开来检查每一部分。
