# 🌋 seethe

🔊 /siːð/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To boil; to bubble; to be in a state of intense but suppressed agitation or anger. (n.) A state of boiling or seething
**中文：** v. 沸腾；激动，骚动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- boil（沸腾）
- simmer（酝酿）
- fume（发怒）
- rage（愤怒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seethe with anger / seethe with resentment / seethe with rage / seething crowd**

👉 Example: She was seething with frustration after discovering that her proposal had been rejected without review.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The population continued to **seethe** with indignation as the government repeatedly failed to address widespread corruption.
   随着政府一再未能解决普遍的腐败问题，民众持续激愤不已。

2. Beneath his calm exterior, the diplomat was **seething** over the deliberate insult delivered during the formal negotiations.
   在冷静的外表下，这位外交官对正式谈判中受到的蓄意侮辱怒不可遏。

3. The volcanic hot springs **seethed** and bubbled, releasing clouds of sulfurous steam into the cold mountain air.
   火山温泉沸腾翻滚，将含硫的蒸汽释放到寒冷的山间空气中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "seethe" as "see the" boiling water — when you see the bubbles, the water is seething, just like a person boiling with anger.
把"seethe"想成"see the"沸腾的水——当你看到气泡时，水在沸腾，就像一个人怒火中烧一样。
