# 👁️ descry

🔊 /dɪˈskraɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To catch sight of something difficult to see; to discover by careful observation
**中文：** v. 看见；发现；察看

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- discern（辨别）
- detect（发现）
- perceive（察觉）
- spot（看出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **descry in the distance / descry a pattern / descry a figure / barely descry**

👉 Example: The lookout was the first to descry the approaching vessel on the distant horizon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After hours of meticulous analysis, the scientist was finally able to **descry** a subtle pattern hidden within the data.
   经过数小时的细致分析，科学家终于能够在数据中发现一个隐藏的微妙模式。

2. From the summit of the mountain, the explorers could **descry** a narrow river winding through the densely forested valley below.
   从山顶上，探险者们可以看见一条狭窄的河流蜿蜒穿过下方茂密的森林山谷。

3. Only a trained observer could **descry** the faint inscriptions carved into the weathered surface of the ancient stone monument.
   只有训练有素的观察者才能辨认出刻在古老石碑风化表面上的模糊铭文。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-scry" like using a crystal ball to "scry" — descry means to see or discover something hidden or far away, as if peering through a mystical lens.
把"descry"想成"de-scry（用水晶球占卜）"——descry意味着看到或发现隐藏的或遥远的东西，就像透过神秘的镜片窥视一样。
