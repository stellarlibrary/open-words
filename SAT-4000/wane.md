# 🌘 wane

🔊 /weɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To decrease gradually in size, intensity, or power; to decline. (n.) A period of decline or diminishing
**中文：** v. 衰落；变小；亏缺；消逝 n. 衰退；月亏；衰退期

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diminish（减少）
- decline（衰退）
- ebb（退潮）
- dwindle（缩小）
- subside（消退）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wane in popularity / wane in influence / on the wane / wax and wane**

👉 Example: Public enthusiasm for the reform began to wane after repeated delays in implementation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The empire's military power began to **wane** significantly after decades of costly foreign campaigns and internal strife.
   经过数十年代价高昂的对外征战和内部纷争，该帝国的军事力量开始显著衰退。

2. As the afternoon wore on, the students' concentration started to **wane**, making the lecture increasingly difficult to follow.
   随着下午时间的推移，学生们的注意力开始减退，使得讲座越来越难以跟上。

3. Interest in the once-revolutionary technology has been on the **wane** as newer and more efficient alternatives have emerged.
   随着更新、更高效的替代方案出现，人们对这项曾经具有革命性的技术的兴趣日渐衰退。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the "waning moon" — the moon gets smaller each night, just as something that wanes gradually decreases.
想想"月亏"——月亮每晚都在变小，就像"wane"表示逐渐减少一样。
