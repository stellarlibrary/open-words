# 🎭 rationalization

🔊 /ˌræʃənələˈzeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of attempting to justify behavior or attitudes with logical reasons, even if these are not appropriate; the process of making something more efficient or logical
**中文：** n. 合理化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- justification（正当化）
- explanation（解释）
- excuse（借口）
- vindication（辩护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mere rationalization / rationalization of behavior / rationalization process / industrial rationalization**

👉 Example: His explanation for missing the deadline was nothing more than a convenient rationalization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The psychologist explained that **rationalization** is a common defense mechanism used to protect the ego from uncomfortable truths.
   心理学家解释说，合理化是一种常见的防御机制，用于保护自我免受不舒适的真相的冲击。

2. The company's **rationalization** of its manufacturing operations resulted in the closure of three underperforming regional factories.
   公司对制造业务的合理化导致了三家表现不佳的区域工厂的关闭。

3. Critics dismissed his argument as mere **rationalization** designed to obscure the true motives behind the controversial policy decision.
   批评者认为他的论点不过是合理化的借口，旨在掩盖这项有争议的政策决定背后的真正动机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rationalize-ation" — it is the act of rationalizing, making excuses sound rational even when they are not.
把"rationalization"想成"rationalize（合理化）+ ation（行为）"——把借口说得听起来合理的行为。
