# 🍷 hedonism

🔊 /ˈhiːdənɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The pursuit of pleasure as the highest good; the ethical theory that pleasure is the chief good and the proper aim of human life
**中文：** n. 享乐主义；快乐主义；快乐论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pleasure-seeking（追求享乐）
- self-indulgence（自我放纵）
- epicureanism（享乐主义）
- sensualism（感觉论）
- sybaritism（奢侈逸乐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **embrace hedonism / pure hedonism / culture of hedonism / critique of hedonism**

👉 Example: The philosopher's critique of hedonism argued that lasting fulfillment requires more than mere pleasure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Ancient Greek philosophers debated whether **hedonism** could serve as a viable ethical framework for a virtuous society.
   古希腊哲学家们争论享乐主义是否可以作为一个美德社会的可行伦理框架。

2. Critics argue that unchecked **hedonism** in consumer culture leads to environmental degradation and social inequality over time.
   批评人士认为，消费文化中不加约束的享乐主义会逐渐导致环境恶化和社会不平等。

3. The memoir chronicles her transition from a life of reckless **hedonism** to one devoted to meaningful service.
   这本回忆录记录了她从放纵享乐的生活转变为致力于有意义的服务的历程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hedon-ism" from the Greek "hedone" (pleasure) — hedonism is the "-ism" (belief system) centered entirely on pleasure.
把"hedonism"想成来自希腊语"hedone（快乐）"——hedonism就是以快乐为中心的"主义"。
