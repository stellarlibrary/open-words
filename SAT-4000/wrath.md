# 😡 wrath

🔊 /ræθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extreme anger; intense fury or indignation, often suggesting righteous or divine punishment
**中文：** n. 愤怒；大怒；激怒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fury（狂怒）
- rage（暴怒）
- indignation（愤慨）
- ire（愤怒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incur wrath / divine wrath / wrath of God / public wrath / provoke wrath**

👉 Example: The politician's remarks about education reform incurred the wrath of teachers' unions nationwide.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The emperor's **wrath** was so fierce that no courtier dared to speak against the proposed military campaign.
   皇帝的愤怒如此猛烈，以至于没有朝臣敢对提议的军事行动表示反对。

2. Environmental activists warned that humanity would face nature's **wrath** if deforestation continued at the current rate.
   环保活动人士警告说，如果砍伐森林继续以目前的速度进行，人类将面临自然的怒火。

3. The CEO's sudden resignation was widely attributed to the **wrath** of shareholders angered by declining quarterly profits.
   首席执行官的突然辞职被普遍归因于因季度利润下降而愤怒的股东们的怒火。

Think of "wrath" rhyming with "bath" — imagine anger so hot it could boil your bathwater into steam.
把"wrath"和"bath（洗澡）"联系起来——想象愤怒如此炽热，能把你的洗澡水煮得沸腾成蒸汽。
