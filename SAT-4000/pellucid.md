# 💎 pellucid

🔊 /pəˈluːsɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Translucently clear; easily understood; lucid in style or meaning
**中文：** adj. 透明的；澄清的；清晰的；易懂的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transparent（透明的）
- lucid（清晰的）
- crystalline（水晶般的）
- limpid（清澈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pellucid prose / pellucid water / pellucid explanation / pellucid clarity**

👉 Example: The philosopher's pellucid writing style made even the most abstract concepts accessible to general readers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mountain lake's **pellucid** waters allowed visitors to observe fish swimming several meters below the surface with remarkable clarity.
   山间湖泊清澈透明的水让游客能够清楚地观察到水面下数米处游动的鱼。

2. Her **pellucid** explanation of quantum mechanics was praised for making a notoriously difficult subject comprehensible to undergraduate students.
   她对量子力学清晰易懂的解释因使这一出了名难懂的学科变得本科生也能理解而受到赞誉。

3. The essayist's **pellucid** style, free from unnecessary jargon and ornamentation, has become a model for aspiring academic writers.
   这位散文家清澈透明的文风，没有不必要的术语和修饰，已成为有抱负的学术写作者的典范。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pel-lucid" as "purely lucid" — something so clear and transparent that you can see right through it, both literally and figuratively.
把"pellucid"想成"purely lucid（纯粹地清晰）"——如此清澈透明，无论字面还是比喻意义上都能一眼看穿。
