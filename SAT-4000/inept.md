# 🤦 inept

🔊 /ɪˈnɛpt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or showing no skill; clumsy or awkward; inappropriate or unsuitable in a given context
**中文：** adj. 笨拙的；不适当的；无能的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incompetent（无能的）
- clumsy（笨拙的）
- unskilled（不熟练的）
- bungling（笨手笨脚的）
- maladroit（不灵巧的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **socially inept / inept handling / inept at managing / inept leadership**

👉 Example: The manager's inept handling of the merger negotiations resulted in significant financial losses for the company.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **inept** remarks during the press conference inadvertently escalated tensions between the two nations to a dangerous level.
   大使在新闻发布会上不当的言论无意中将两国之间的紧张关系升级到了危险水平。

2. Despite years of formal training, he remained remarkably **inept** at public speaking and consistently failed to engage his audiences.
   尽管接受了多年的正式培训，他在公开演讲方面仍然非常笨拙，始终无法吸引听众。

3. The committee's **inept** management of the relief funds drew a formal investigation from the independent oversight board last quarter.
   委员会对救济资金的无能管理在上季度引发了独立监督委员会的正式调查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-EPT" as the opposite of "apt" — if someone is apt they are skilled and suitable, but someone inept is unskilled and unsuitable.
把"inept"想成"apt(恰当的、有能力的)"的反义词——apt的人有能力且得体，inept的人则笨拙且不得体。
