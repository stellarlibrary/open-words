# 🔗 coalescence

🔊 /ˌkoʊəˈlesəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of coming together to form one mass, group, or whole; the merging or fusion of separate elements into a unified entity
**中文：** n. 合并；联合；接合；融合

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merger（合并）
- fusion（融合）
- amalgamation（混合）
- convergence（汇聚）
- unification（统一）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **coalescence of ideas / political coalescence / coalescence of forces / gradual coalescence / cultural coalescence**

👉 Example: The coalescence of several activist movements created a powerful unified front for social reform.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **coalescence** of diverse political factions into a single party proved essential for winning the national election decisively.
   各种不同政治派别合并为一个政党，对于决定性地赢得全国大选至关重要。

2. Scientists observed the **coalescence** of water droplets on the surface, which eventually formed larger pools of condensation.
   科学家们观察到水滴在表面上的聚合，最终形成了更大的冷凝水池。

3. The **coalescence** of Eastern and Western philosophical traditions has produced remarkably innovative approaches to contemporary ethics.
   东西方哲学传统的融合产生了对当代伦理学极具创新性的研究方法。

---

Think of "coalescence" as "coal + essence" — imagine pieces of coal merging their essence together into one solid block, representing fusion and unity.
把"coalescence"想成"coal（煤炭）+ essence（本质）"——想象煤块将各自的本质融合在一起，形成一个整体，代表融合与统一。
