# 🏳️ submission

🔊 /səbˈmɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of yielding to authority or control; the act of presenting something for consideration; humble compliance
**中文：** n. 屈服，降服

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surrender（投降）
- capitulation（屈服）
- compliance（服从）
- yielding（顺从）
- obedience（顺从）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **submission to authority / submission of a proposal / forced into submission / submission deadline**

👉 Example: The deadline for the submission of research papers to the international conference has been extended by two additional weeks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defeated general signed the formal document of **submission**, effectively ending three years of devastating armed conflict in the region.
   战败的将军签署了正式的投降文件，有效地结束了该地区三年的毁灭性武装冲突。

2. The committee received over two hundred **submissions** for the annual literary prize, making the selection process exceptionally competitive this year.
   委员会收到了两百多份年度文学奖的提交作品，使今年的评选过程竞争异常激烈。

3. The professor emphasized that blind **submission** to authority without critical examination undermines the fundamental principles of democratic governance.
   教授强调，不经批判性审视就盲目屈服于权威会破坏民主治理的基本原则。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-mission" as being sent on a "mission" from "below" — you are placed in a lower position, yielding and submitting to those above you.
把"submission"想成从"下面"（sub）被派遣去执行"任务"（mission）——你被置于较低的位置，向上面的人屈服和顺从。
