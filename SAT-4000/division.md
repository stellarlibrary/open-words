# 🔢 division

🔊 /dɪˈvɪʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The mathematical operation of dividing one number by another; a section or department within an organization; separation or disagreement
**中文：** n. 除法；部门；分裂；分歧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- separation（分离）
- department（部门）
- partition（划分）
- section（部分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **long division / division of labor / division between / division headquarters**

👉 Example: The company restructured its marketing division to better respond to rapidly changing consumer trends.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students must master the fundamental operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication, and **division** before advancing to algebra.
   学生在进入代数学习之前，必须掌握加法、减法、乘法和除法的基本运算。

2. The research **division** of the pharmaceutical company allocated significant resources toward developing treatments for rare genetic diseases.
   该制药公司的研究部门投入了大量资源用于开发罕见遗传疾病的治疗方法。

3. Growing economic inequality has created a deep **division** within society that threatens the foundations of democratic governance.
   日益加剧的经济不平等在社会内部造成了深刻的分裂，威胁着民主治理的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "division" as "di-vision" — two visions looking in different directions, representing separation or splitting apart.
把"division"想成"di(分开)+vision(视野)"——两种视野看向不同方向，代表分裂或分开。
