# 🔋 exhaust

🔊 /ɪɡˈzɔːst/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To drain completely of resources or energy; to use up entirely; to discuss or treat thoroughly. (n.) Waste gases expelled from an engine; a system for expelling such gases
**中文：** v. 使筋疲力尽；耗尽；彻底探讨 n. 排气装置；废气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deplete（耗尽）
- drain（消耗）
- fatigue（使疲劳）
- consume（消耗）
- expend（花费）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exhaust resources / exhaust options / exhaust possibilities / exhaust oneself / exhaust fumes**

👉 Example: The researchers exhausted every possible avenue of inquiry before publishing their findings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged drought threatened to **exhaust** the region's freshwater reserves, prompting officials to impose strict rationing measures.
   长期干旱威胁着该地区淡水储备的耗尽，促使官员实施严格的配给措施。

2. Vehicle **exhaust** emissions remain a primary contributor to urban air pollution and associated respiratory health concerns.
   汽车尾气排放仍然是城市空气污染和相关呼吸健康问题的主要原因。

3. The attorney advised her client to **exhaust** all administrative remedies before pursuing litigation in federal court.
   律师建议她的当事人在向联邦法院提起诉讼之前用尽所有行政救济途径。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exhaust" as "ex-hausted" — once all energy has exited, you are completely hauled out and empty.
把"exhaust"想成"ex-hausted"——一旦所有能量都流出，你就被彻底掏空了。
