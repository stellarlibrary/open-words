# 🧪 plastic

🔊 /ˈplæstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Made of plastic material; capable of being shaped or molded; relating to surgical reshaping. (n.) A synthetic material that can be molded into shape; credit cards collectively
**中文：** adj. 塑料的；可塑的；造型的 n. 塑料制品；整形；可塑体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- synthetic（合成的）
- malleable（可塑的）
- pliable（柔韧的）
- flexible（灵活的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plastic surgery / plastic material / plastic arts / plastic deformation**

👉 Example: The young brain is remarkably plastic, capable of reorganizing neural pathways after injury.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Environmental scientists warned that **plastic** pollution in the world's oceans has reached catastrophic levels threatening countless marine species.
   环境科学家警告说，世界海洋中的塑料污染已达到灾难性水平，威胁着无数海洋物种。

2. The neuroscientist's research demonstrated that the adult brain remains far more **plastic** than previously believed by the medical community.
   这位神经科学家的研究表明，成人大脑比医学界此前认为的要具有更强的可塑性。

3. Ancient artisans shaped **plastic** clay into elaborate vessels that scholars now study to understand the cultural practices of early civilizations.
   古代工匠将可塑的黏土塑造成精美的器皿，学者们现在研究这些器皿以了解早期文明的文化习俗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plastic" as related to the Greek "plastikos" meaning "to mold" — anything plastic can be shaped and reshaped into new forms.
把"plastic"想成源自希腊语"plastikos"意为"塑造"——任何具有可塑性的东西都可以被塑造成新的形态。
