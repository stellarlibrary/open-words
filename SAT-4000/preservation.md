# 🛡️ preservation

🔊 /ˌprɛzərˈveɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of keeping something in its original state or in good condition; the protection of something from harm or decay
**中文：** n. 保存；保留；保护；维护

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conservation（保护）
- maintenance（维护）
- safeguarding（保卫）
- protection（保护）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preservation of / historic preservation / self-preservation / preservation efforts**

👉 Example: The preservation of ancient manuscripts requires careful climate control and handling procedures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum dedicated substantial resources to the **preservation** of rare artifacts dating back to the medieval period.
   博物馆投入了大量资源来保存可追溯到中世纪的珍贵文物。

2. Environmental organizations have advocated for the **preservation** of wetlands as critical habitats for endangered species.
   环保组织一直倡导保护湿地，因为湿地是濒危物种的重要栖息地。

3. The scholar devoted her career to the **preservation** of indigenous languages that were at risk of disappearing entirely.
   这位学者毕生致力于保存那些面临完全消亡风险的土著语言。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-serve-ation" as "serving something before it is lost" — you serve and protect things in advance to keep them safe.
把"preservation"想成"pre-serve-ation"——在事物消失之前去"服务"和保护它们，提前守护使之安全。
