# ✨ cleanliness

🔊 /ˈklɛnlɪnəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state or quality of being clean; the habitual practice of keeping oneself and one's surroundings free from dirt or contamination
**中文：** n. 清洁；洁净

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hygiene（卫生）
- tidiness（整洁）
- purity（纯净）
- sanitation（卫生设施）
- neatness（整齐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **personal cleanliness / standards of cleanliness / maintain cleanliness / cleanliness of the environment**

👉 Example: The hospital enforces strict cleanliness protocols to prevent the spread of infectious diseases.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Public health officials emphasized that **cleanliness** in food preparation areas is essential for preventing outbreaks of bacterial infection.
   公共卫生官员强调，食品加工区域的清洁对于预防细菌感染暴发至关重要。

2. The inspector evaluated the **cleanliness** of the facility and issued a comprehensive report detailing several areas requiring improvement.
   检查员对该设施的清洁度进行了评估，并发布了一份详细报告，列出了多个需要改进的方面。

3. Maintaining **cleanliness** in laboratory settings is not merely preferable but absolutely critical for obtaining reliable experimental results.
   保持实验室环境的清洁不仅仅是可取的，而且对于获得可靠的实验结果至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clean-li-ness" — the "-ness" of being "cleanly," a state where everything is spotless and orderly.
把"cleanliness"想成"clean"（干净）的"-ness"（状态）——一切都一尘不染、井然有序的状态。
