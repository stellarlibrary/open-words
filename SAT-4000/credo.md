# 📜 credo

🔊 /ˈkriːdoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A statement of the beliefs or aims that guide someone's actions; a set of fundamental convictions or guiding principles
**中文：** n. 信条；教义

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- creed（信条）
- doctrine（教义）
- tenet（信条）
- philosophy（哲学理念）
- ideology（意识形态）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **personal credo / company credo / live by a credo / artistic credo / political credo**

👉 Example: Her personal credo of integrity and perseverance guided every major decision throughout her career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's **credo** of transparency and accountability has earned it widespread trust among international donors and partners.
   该组织透明和负责的信条为其赢得了国际捐助者和合作伙伴的广泛信任。

2. His philosophical **credo** emphasized that rational inquiry must always take precedence over blind acceptance of inherited traditions.
   他的哲学信条强调，理性探究必须始终优先于对传统的盲目接受。

3. The scientist adopted a rigorous **credo** of empirical verification, refusing to accept any hypothesis without substantial experimental proof.
   这位科学家奉行严格的实证验证信条，拒绝接受任何没有充分实验证据的假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "credo" from Latin "I believe" — it is the core belief or creed that someone holds dear and lives by.
把"credo"与拉丁语"我相信"联系起来——它是一个人珍视并遵循的核心信念或信条。
