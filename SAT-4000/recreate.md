# 🎯 recreate

🔊 /ˈrekrɪeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To refresh oneself through leisure activity or amusement; to take recreation
**中文：** v. 娱乐；消遣；得到休养

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relax（放松）
- amuse（娱乐）
- refresh（恢复精力）
- unwind（放松身心）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **recreate oneself / recreate in the park / recreate after work / recreate through hobbies**

👉 Example: On weekends, the students recreate by hiking through the scenic trails near campus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After months of intensive academic research, the scholars chose to **recreate** at the countryside retreat to restore their energy.
   经过数月紧张的学术研究后，学者们选择在乡村度假地休闲娱乐以恢复精力。

2. The employees were encouraged to **recreate** during lunch breaks to enhance their overall productivity and well-being.
   公司鼓励员工在午休时间进行娱乐消遣，以提升整体工作效率和身心健康。

3. Many physicians recommend that patients **recreate** regularly through outdoor activities to maintain both physical and mental health.
   许多医生建议患者通过户外活动定期进行休闲娱乐，以保持身心健康。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-create" as "creating yourself anew" — when you recreate, you refresh and restore yourself through enjoyable activities.
把"recreate"想成"re-create"——重新创造自己，也就是通过娱乐活动恢复精力，焕然一新。
