# 🧪 flask

🔊 /flæsk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A container, typically of glass, with a narrow neck used in scientific laboratories; a portable bottle for carrying liquids
**中文：** n. 烧瓶；长颈瓶；便携式酒壶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bottle（瓶子）
- container（容器）
- vessel（器皿）
- vial（小瓶）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Erlenmeyer flask / round-bottom flask / hip flask / vacuum flask / conical flask**

👉 Example: The chemistry student carefully heated the solution in a glass flask over the Bunsen burner.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher poured the reagent into a sterile **flask** and placed it under controlled temperature conditions for observation.
   研究人员将试剂倒入无菌烧瓶中，并将其置于受控温度条件下进行观察。

2. Each laboratory station was equipped with an Erlenmeyer **flask**, graduated cylinders, and precision measuring instruments for the experiment.
   每个实验台都配备了锥形烧瓶、量筒和用于实验的精密测量仪器。

3. The mountaineer carried a metal **flask** filled with warm tea to sustain himself during the arduous climb to the summit.
   登山者携带了一个装满热茶的金属瓶，以在艰难的登顶过程中补充体力。

把"flask"想成"fl-ask"——想象一个科学家在实验室里手持烧瓶不断"ask"（提问）的画面。
