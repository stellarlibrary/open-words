# 🦁 voracious

🔊 /vəˈreɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a very eager approach to an activity; wanting or devouring great quantities of food or information
**中文：** adj. 贪婪的；贪吃的；狼吞虎咽的；求知欲强的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ravenous（贪婪的）
- insatiable（不知足的）
- gluttonous（暴食的）
- avid（渴望的）
- rapacious（掠夺的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **voracious appetite / voracious reader / voracious consumer / voracious learner**

👉 Example: She was a voracious reader who could finish an entire novel in a single afternoon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scholar's **voracious** appetite for knowledge led her to study multiple disciplines at the university simultaneously.
   这位年轻学者对知识的贪婪渴求使她同时在大学学习多个学科。

2. Invasive species often have **voracious** feeding habits that rapidly deplete the resources available to native wildlife populations.
   入侵物种通常有贪婪的进食习性，会迅速耗尽可供本地野生动物使用的资源。

3. The company's **voracious** expansion strategy involved acquiring dozens of smaller competitors across multiple international markets.
   该公司贪婪的扩张战略涉及在多个国际市场收购数十家较小的竞争对手。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voracious" as "devour-acious" — someone who devours everything, whether food or books, with unstoppable hunger.
把"voracious"想成"devour-acious"——一个以无法阻挡的饥饿狼吞虎咽一切的人，无论是食物还是书籍。
