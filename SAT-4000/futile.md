# 🚫 futile

🔊 /ˈfjuːtaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Incapable of producing any useful result; pointless; having no chance of success
**中文：** adj. 无用的；无效的；没有出息的；无希望的；无意义的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- useless（无用的）
- pointless（无意义的）
- vain（徒劳的）
- fruitless（无成果的）
- ineffectual（无效的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **futile attempt / futile effort / futile gesture / prove futile / utterly futile**

👉 Example: All efforts to negotiate a ceasefire proved futile as both sides refused to compromise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rescue team made a **futile** attempt to reach the trapped miners before the second collapse sealed the passage.
   救援队在第二次坍塌封堵通道之前做了一次徒劳的尝试去营救被困矿工。

2. Critics argued that imposing additional sanctions would be entirely **futile** without genuine diplomatic engagement from all parties.
   批评人士认为，如果没有各方的真正外交参与，额外的制裁将完全是无效的。

3. The defendant's **futile** efforts to conceal evidence only served to strengthen the prosecution's case against him.
   被告徒劳地试图隐藏证据，反而只是加强了控方对他的指控。

---

Think of "futile" as "few-tile" — imagine trying to tile an entire floor with just a few tiles; the effort would be completely futile.
把"futile"想成"few-tile"——想象只用几块瓷砖铺整层地板，这种努力完全是徒劳的。
