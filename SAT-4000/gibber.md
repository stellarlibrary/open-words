# 🗣️ gibber

🔊 /ˈdʒɪbər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To speak rapidly and unintelligibly, typically through fear, nervousness, or excitement; to chatter incoherently
**中文：** v. 喋喋而言；急促而含混不清地说话；胡扯

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- babble（胡言乱语）
- jabber（喋喋不休）
- prattle（唠叨）
- blather（胡扯）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gibber with fear / gibber incoherently / gibber nervously / gibber nonsense**

👉 Example: The terrified child began to gibber uncontrollably after witnessing the accident.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant began to **gibber** incoherently when confronted with the overwhelming evidence presented by the prosecution.
   被告在面对控方提出的压倒性证据时开始语无伦次地胡言乱语。

2. Witnesses reported that the survivor could only **gibber** in shock for several hours after being rescued.
   目击者称，幸存者获救后连续数小时只能在惊恐中语无伦次地说话。

3. The audience watched in disbelief as the normally eloquent speaker began to **gibber** under the intense scrutiny.
   观众难以置信地看着这位平时口才出众的演讲者在严格审视下开始语无伦次。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gibber" as the root of "gibberish" — when someone gibbers, all you hear is gibberish, meaningless noise.
把"gibber"想成"gibberish"（胡言乱语）的词根——当某人gibber时，你听到的就是gibberish，毫无意义的声音。
