# 🔀 promiscuous

🔊 /prəˈmɪskjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Consisting of a disorderly mixture of various elements; indiscriminate or lacking standards of selection
**中文：** adj. 混杂的；杂乱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indiscriminate（不加区分的）
- haphazard（随意的）
- miscellaneous（混杂的）
- random（随机的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **promiscuous mixture / promiscuous use / promiscuous blend / promiscuous assortment**

👉 Example: The report criticized the promiscuous use of technical jargon that made the document inaccessible to general readers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar criticized the **promiscuous** blending of unrelated theoretical frameworks, arguing it weakened the rigor of the analysis.
   这位学者批评了对不相关理论框架的杂乱混用，认为这削弱了分析的严谨性。

2. A **promiscuous** array of architectural styles characterized the district, reflecting centuries of diverse cultural influences and rebuilding.
   该地区以混杂的建筑风格为特色，反映了几个世纪以来多元文化影响和重建的历史。

3. The committee warned against the **promiscuous** application of new regulations without carefully assessing their impact on existing industries.
   委员会警告不要不加区分地实施新法规，而应仔细评估其对现有行业的影响。

把"promiscuous"想成"pro-MIX-cuous"——包含"mix"（混合），暗示许多不同事物杂乱地混在一起。
