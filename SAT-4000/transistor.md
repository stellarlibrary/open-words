# 💡 transistor

🔊 /trænˈzɪstər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A semiconductor device used to amplify or switch electronic signals and electrical power; a fundamental building block of modern electronic circuits.
**中文：** n. 晶体管；半导体器件，用于放大或切换电子信号和电功率。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- semiconductor device（半导体器件）
- solid-state device（固态器件）
- electronic switch（电子开关）
- amplifier component（放大器元件）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transistor circuit / transistor radio / bipolar transistor / field-effect transistor / transistor amplifier**

👉 Example: Modern microprocessors contain billions of transistors etched onto a single silicon chip.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The invention of the **transistor** in 1947 revolutionized electronics and paved the way for the modern computing era.
   1947年晶体管的发明彻底改变了电子学，并为现代计算时代铺平了道路。

2. Engineers continue to miniaturize **transistors** to increase processing power while reducing energy consumption in advanced microchips.
   工程师们继续缩小晶体管的尺寸，以在先进微芯片中提高处理能力的同时降低能耗。

3. Each **transistor** in the integrated circuit functions as a tiny switch that controls the flow of electrical current.
   集成电路中的每个晶体管都充当一个微小的开关，控制电流的流动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transistor" as "transfer + resistor" — it transfers and resists electrical signals, controlling them precisely.
把"transistor"想成"transfer（传递）+ resistor（电阻器）"——它传递并控制电信号，实现精确调控。
