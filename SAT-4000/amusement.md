# 🎡 amusement

🔊 /əˈmjuːzmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of finding something entertaining or humorous; a pastime or recreational activity that provides enjoyment
**中文：** 消遣，娱乐；乐趣

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- entertainment（娱乐）
- diversion（消遣）
- recreation（休闲）
- merriment（欢乐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **amusement park / source of amusement / to one's amusement / for amusement / with amusement**

👉 Example: She watched with quiet amusement as the children attempted to build a sandcastle in the rain.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's dry wit was a constant source of **amusement** for students throughout the entire semester.
   教授的冷幽默在整个学期中一直是学生们的乐趣来源。

2. To everyone's **amusement**, the distinguished diplomat accidentally mispronounced the host country's name during his formal address.
   令所有人感到好笑的是，那位杰出的外交官在正式致辞中不小心念错了东道国的名字。

3. The local council approved funding for new public **amusement** facilities designed to promote community engagement and wellness.
   地方议会批准了用于新建公共娱乐设施的资金，旨在促进社区参与和健康。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-MUSE-ment" — a muse brings inspiration and delight, so amusement is the state of being delighted and entertained.
把"amusement"拆分为"a + muse（缪斯）+ ment"——缪斯女神带来灵感和愉悦，所以amusement就是被娱乐和取悦的状态。
