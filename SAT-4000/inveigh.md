# 😤 inveigh

🔊 /ɪnˈveɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To speak or write about something with great hostility; to protest or complain bitterly and at length
**中文：** v. 猛烈抨击；痛骂；漫骂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- denounce（谴责）
- rail（痛斥）
- decry（公开反对）
- fulminate（猛烈抨击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inveigh against injustice / inveigh against corruption / inveigh against a policy / inveigh bitterly**

👉 Example: The senator inveighed against the proposed tax cuts, calling them a handout to the wealthy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editorial **inveighed** against government surveillance programs, arguing that they violated fundamental constitutional liberties of ordinary citizens.
   这篇社论猛烈抨击政府的监控计划，认为它们侵犯了普通公民的基本宪法自由。

2. Community leaders publicly **inveighed** against the proposed factory construction, citing potential environmental damage to the surrounding area.
   社区领袖公开抨击拟建工厂的计划，指出其对周围地区可能造成的环境破坏。

3. The philosopher spent decades **inveighing** against materialism, urging society to pursue intellectual and spiritual fulfillment instead of wealth.
   这位哲学家花了数十年时间抨击物质主义，敦促社会追求智识和精神上的满足而非财富。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inveigh" sounding like "in + weigh" — to weigh in heavily against something, crushing it with harsh words.
把"inveigh"想成"in + weigh（称重）"——用沉重的言辞压向某事物，以严厉的话语猛烈抨击。
