# 💧 fluid

🔊 /ˈfluːɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to flow freely; not fixed or rigid; smooth and graceful. (n.) A substance that flows and has no fixed shape, such as a liquid or gas
**中文：** adj. 流动的；液体的；流畅的 n. 流体；液体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- liquid（液体的）
- flowing（流动的）
- flexible（灵活的）
- changeable（可变的）
- smooth（流畅的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fluid motion / fluid dynamics / body fluid / fluid situation / fluid boundaries**

👉 Example: The political situation remained fluid as negotiations continued behind closed doors throughout the week.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The dancer's **fluid** movements across the stage captivated the audience and earned a standing ovation from every spectator.
   舞者在舞台上**流畅**的动作吸引了观众，赢得了每位观众的起立鼓掌。

2. Engineers must understand **fluid** dynamics thoroughly before designing efficient hydraulic systems for large-scale industrial applications.
   工程师必须充分理解**流体**动力学，才能为大规模工业应用设计高效的液压系统。

3. The boundaries between academic disciplines have become increasingly **fluid**, encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration in modern research universities.
   学科之间的界限变得越来越**模糊**，鼓励了现代研究型大学的跨学科合作。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fluid" as coming from Latin "fluere" meaning "to flow" — anything fluid flows freely, whether it is water or a graceful dance.
把"fluid"想成来自拉丁语"fluere"，意为"流动"——任何流体都自由流动，无论是水还是优美的舞蹈。
