# 🔧 maintenance

🔊 /ˈmeɪntənəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of keeping something in good condition through regular checking and repair; the act of preserving a state or situation; financial support provided for living expenses.
**中文：** n. 维修；保养；维持；保持；生活费用

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- upkeep（保养）
- preservation（维护）
- repair（修缮）
- conservation（保护）
- sustenance（维持）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **routine maintenance / maintenance costs / maintenance work / maintenance of (standards/order) / low maintenance**

👉 Example: Regular maintenance of the equipment significantly extends its operational lifespan and reduces costs.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital invested heavily in the **maintenance** of its medical equipment to ensure patient safety and diagnostic accuracy.
   医院大量投资于医疗设备的维护保养，以确保患者安全和诊断准确性。

2. Proper **maintenance** of historical buildings requires specialized knowledge of traditional construction materials and restoration techniques.
   历史建筑的妥善维护需要对传统建筑材料和修复技术的专业知识。

3. The court ordered the payment of monthly **maintenance** to support the children's education and basic living expenses.
   法院裁定每月支付抚养费以支持子女的教育和基本生活开支。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "maintain" + "-ance" (the act of) — maintenance is simply the ongoing act of maintaining, like keeping a car running smoothly with regular oil changes.
把"maintenance"想成"maintain"（维持）+"-ance"（行为）——maintenance就是持续维护的行为，就像定期换油让汽车保持平稳运行一样。
