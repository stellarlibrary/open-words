# 🛡️ gallant

🔊 /ˈɡælənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Brave and heroic; showing courage in the face of danger; splendid or grand in appearance; chivalrous and attentive, especially toward women
**中文：** adj. 英勇的，勇敢的；华丽的；豪侠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brave（勇敢的）
- valiant（英勇的）
- heroic（英雄的）
- courageous（有勇气的）
- chivalrous（骑士风度的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gallant effort / gallant soldier / gallant deed / gallant rescue / gallant manner**

👉 Example: The gallant firefighter rushed into the burning building to save the trapped residents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **gallant** soldiers held their position against overwhelming odds, allowing the civilians enough time to evacuate safely.
   英勇的士兵们在极其不利的情况下坚守阵地，为平民们争取了安全撤离的时间。

2. Despite the team's **gallant** effort in the final quarter, they were ultimately unable to overcome the formidable deficit.
   尽管球队在最后一节进行了英勇的努力，他们最终未能克服巨大的分差。

3. The captain made a **gallant** stand at the narrow bridge, single-handedly defending the passage against the advancing enemy forces.
   队长在狭窄的桥头英勇抵抗，独自一人抵御敌军的推进。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gallant" as "gal-lant" — a knight of Lancelot who was gallant toward every "gal" (lady), brave and chivalrous in all his deeds.
把"gallant"想成"gal-lant"——像兰斯洛特(Lancelot)一样的骑士，对每位女士(gal)都英勇有礼，在一切行为中表现出勇敢和骑士风度。
