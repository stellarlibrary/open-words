# 🕯️ votive

🔊 /ˈvoʊtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Offered or consecrated in fulfillment of a vow; expressing or symbolizing a wish, prayer, or act of devotion
**中文：** adj. 奉献的；献纳的；诚心祈求的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- devotional（虔诚的）
- consecrated（奉献的）
- dedicatory（奉献的）
- sacrificial（祭祀的）
- reverential（虔敬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **votive candle / votive offering / votive prayer / votive shrine**

👉 Example: Pilgrims lit votive candles at the altar as a symbol of their heartfelt prayers and devotion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Archaeologists discovered several **votive** offerings buried near the temple, including small statues and inscribed clay tablets.
   考古学家在寺庙附近发现了几件奉献祭品，包括小雕像和刻有铭文的泥板。

2. The congregation placed **votive** candles along the sanctuary walls to honor the memory of their departed loved ones.
   会众在圣殿墙壁上放置了奉献蜡烛，以纪念他们已故的亲人。

3. Ancient Romans frequently made **votive** deposits at sacred springs to express gratitude for recovery from serious illness.
   古罗马人经常在神圣的泉水处进行奉献供奉，以表达从严重疾病中康复的感恩之情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "votive" as coming from "vote" in its original Latin sense of "vow" — a votive offering fulfills a sacred vow.
把"votive"想成来自"vote"的拉丁语本义"誓言"——奉献之物是为了履行一个神圣的誓约。
