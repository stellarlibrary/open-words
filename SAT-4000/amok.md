# 🌀 amok

🔊 /əˈmʌk/<br>
🏷️ *adverb; adjective | 副词；形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In a frenzy of violent or uncontrollable behavior; wildly out of control.
**中文：** 狂乱地；杀人狂的；狂乱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- berserk（狂暴的）
- frenziedly（疯狂地）
- wildly（疯狂地）
- rampant（肆虐的）
- uncontrollably（不可控制地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **run amok (most common collocation), go amok, emotions running amok**
👉 Example: The protesters ran amok through the streets, overturning vehicles and breaking windows.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Without proper supervision, the unsupervised children ran **amok** throughout the museum, disturbing other visitors.
   在没有适当监管的情况下，无人看管的孩子们在博物馆里到处乱跑，打扰了其他游客。

2. Critics argued that deregulation had allowed corporate greed to run **amok** in the financial markets.
   批评人士认为，放松管制使企业贪婪在金融市场上肆意蔓延。

3. The invasive species ran **amok** across the fragile island ecosystem, threatening numerous native plant populations.
   入侵物种在脆弱的岛屿生态系统中肆虐，威胁着众多本地植物种群。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amok" comes from Malay "amuk" meaning a frenzied attack — picture someone running wildly, completely out of control.
"Amok"来自马来语"amuk"，意为疯狂的攻击——想象一个人疯狂奔跑、完全失控的画面。
