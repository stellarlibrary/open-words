# 💬 colloquialism

🔊 /kəˈloʊ.kwi.ə.lɪz.əm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A word, phrase, or expression characteristic of informal spoken language; the use of such informal language in speech or writing.

**中文：** 白话，口语；口语体。指非正式口头语言中特有的词语、短语或表达方式，也指在言语或写作中使用此类非正式语言。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- slang（俚语）
- informalism（非正式用语）
- vernacularism（方言用法）
- idiom（习语）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** "use a colloquialism / common colloquialism / regional colloquialism" — refers to a specific informal expression or the practice of using informal language.

**Example:** The phrase "gonna" is a colloquialism derived from "going to" that appears frequently in casual speech.
