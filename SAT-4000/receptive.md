# 🤲 receptive

🔊 /rɪˈsɛptɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Willing and able to receive new ideas, suggestions, or experiences; open-minded and responsive
**中文：** adj. 善于接受的；能容纳的；愿意倾听的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- open-minded（思想开放的）
- responsive（反应积极的）
- amenable（顺从的）
- susceptible（易受影响的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **receptive to ideas / receptive audience / receptive to change / receptive to criticism**

👉 Example: A good leader must be receptive to constructive feedback from team members at every level.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members were highly **receptive** to the proposal for expanding scholarship programs for underprivileged students.
   委员会成员非常乐于接受为贫困学生扩大奖学金项目的提案。

2. Young children are especially **receptive** to language acquisition, absorbing new vocabulary and grammar structures with remarkable ease.
   幼儿特别善于语言习得，能以惊人的速度吸收新词汇和语法结构。

3. The nation's political climate was not **receptive** to radical reform, causing legislators to adopt a more incremental approach.
   该国的政治氛围不利于激进改革，迫使立法者采取了更为渐进的方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "receptive" as related to "receive" — someone who is receptive has their arms open, ready to receive new ideas.
把"receptive"联想到"receive（接收）"——善于接受的人就像张开双臂，随时准备接纳新想法。
