# ⚙️ pulley

🔊 /ˈpʊli/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A wheel with a grooved rim around which a cord passes, used to raise heavy weights or change the direction of force
**中文：** n. 滑轮；皮带轮；滑车

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- block and tackle（滑轮组）
- hoist（起重装置）
- winch（绞盘）
- sheave（滑轮轮盘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pulley system / pulley mechanism / rope and pulley / compound pulley**

👉 Example: The construction crew used a pulley system to lift the steel beams to the upper floors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers designed an elaborate **pulley** system to transport materials efficiently across the vast construction site.
   工程师设计了一套精密的滑轮系统，以便在广阔的施工现场高效运输材料。

2. The physics professor demonstrated how a simple **pulley** reduces the amount of force needed to lift heavy objects.
   物理学教授演示了简单滑轮如何减少提升重物所需的力。

3. Ancient civilizations relied on the **pulley** as one of their most essential mechanical devices for building monumental structures.
   古代文明依赖滑轮作为建造宏伟建筑最重要的机械装置之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pull-ey" — you pull a rope through a pulley to lift something heavy with less effort.
把"pulley"想成"pull（拉）+ ey"——你通过滑轮拉绳子，用更少的力气提起重物。
