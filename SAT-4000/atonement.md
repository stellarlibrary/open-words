# 🕊️ atonement

🔊 /əˈtoʊnmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of making amends for a wrongdoing or sin; reparation for an offense or injury
**中文：** n. 赎罪；补偿，弥补

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reparation（补偿）
- redemption（赎回）
- expiation（赎罪）
- amends（弥补）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make atonement for / atonement for sins / seek atonement / an act of atonement**

👉 Example: He spent years performing charitable work as atonement for his past misdeeds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The convicted official devoted his remaining years to public service as **atonement** for his corruption.
   这位被定罪的官员将余生献给公共服务，以此作为对其腐败行为的赎罪。

2. She offered a heartfelt apology and generous donation as **atonement** for the harm she had caused.
   她真诚地道歉并慷慨捐赠，以此弥补她所造成的伤害。

3. The novel explores how personal **atonement** can restore broken relationships and heal deep emotional wounds.
   这部小说探讨了个人的补偿如何修复破裂的关系并治愈深层的情感创伤。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "at-one-ment" — to become "at one" again with someone, you must make amends and reconcile.
拆分记忆："at-one-ment"——要再次与某人"合一"，你必须做出弥补并和解。
