# 🎭 irony

🔊 /ˈaɪrəni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The expression of meaning through language that normally signifies the opposite; a state of affairs that seems contrary to what one expects
**中文：** n. 讽刺；反语；具有讽刺意味的事；出人意料的事情或情况

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sarcasm（讽刺）
- satire（讽刺文学）
- paradox（悖论）
- mockery（嘲弄）
- incongruity（不协调）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dramatic irony / situational irony / verbal irony / the irony of / a touch of irony**

👉 Example: The irony of the situation was that the locksmith had locked himself out of his own house.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cruel **irony** of the failed rescue mission was that the victims had already freed themselves hours before help arrived.
   这次失败的救援行动的残酷讽刺在于，受害者在救援到达前几个小时就已经自行脱困了。

2. Literary scholars frequently analyze the layers of **irony** embedded in satirical works to reveal deeper social commentary.
   文学学者经常分析讽刺作品中蕴含的多层反讽，以揭示更深层的社会评论。

3. There is a profound **irony** in the fact that advanced technology designed to connect people often leads to greater isolation.
   旨在连接人们的先进技术往往导致更大的孤立，这一事实具有深刻的讽刺意味。

---

Think of "irony" as "I-run-y" — imagine reality running in the opposite direction from what you expected, creating a surprising twist.
把"irony"想成"I-run-y"——想象现实朝着与你预期相反的方向奔跑，制造出一个出人意料的转折。
