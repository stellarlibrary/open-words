# 💎 adamant

🔊 /ˈæd.ə.mənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Refusing to be persuaded or to change one's mind; unyielding and inflexible in attitude or opinion
**中文：** adj. 固执的，坚定不移的；不可动摇的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unyielding（不屈的）
- resolute（坚决的）
- inflexible（不可动摇的）
- steadfast（坚定的）
- intransigent（不妥协的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adamant about / adamant that / adamant in one's belief / adamant refusal / remain adamant**

👉 Example: The professor was adamant that all students must submit their research papers by the deadline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite mounting pressure from shareholders, the board remained **adamant** about its restructuring plan.
   尽管股东施加了越来越大的压力，董事会仍然坚持其重组计划。

2. The senator was **adamant** that no amendments would be accepted to the proposed legislation.
   这位参议员坚决表示不会接受对拟议立法的任何修正案。

3. She was **adamant** in her refusal to compromise on the ethical standards of the organization.
   她在拒绝对组织的道德标准做出妥协这一点上毫不动摇。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adamant" as related to "diamond" — both come from Greek "adamas" meaning unconquerable. Someone adamant is as hard and unbreakable as a diamond.
把"adamant"和"钻石"联系起来——两者都源于希腊语"adamas"，意为不可征服的。一个adamant的人就像钻石一样坚硬、不可动摇。
