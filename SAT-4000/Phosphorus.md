# 💡 Phosphorus

🔊 /ˈfɒsfərəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A chemical element (symbol P, atomic number 15) that exists in several allotropic forms; essential for life, found in bones, teeth, DNA, and used in fertilizers and matches
**中文：** n. 磷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- element P（元素磷）
- white phosphorus（白磷）
- red phosphorus（红磷）
- phosphoric element（含磷元素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **phosphorus cycle / phosphorus deficiency / white phosphorus / phosphorus content**

👉 Example: Phosphorus is a critical nutrient in agriculture, essential for healthy root development in plants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Phosphorus** plays a vital role in cellular energy transfer, as it forms the backbone of adenosine triphosphate molecules.
   磷在细胞能量转移中起着至关重要的作用，因为它构成了三磷酸腺苷分子的骨架。

2. Environmental scientists warned that excessive **phosphorus** discharge into rivers was accelerating the degradation of aquatic habitats nationwide.
   环境科学家警告说，过量的磷排放到河流中正在加速全国水生栖息地的退化。

3. The discovery of **phosphorus** in the seventeenth century marked a pivotal moment in the development of modern chemistry and industry.
   十七世纪磷的发现标志着现代化学和工业发展的关键时刻。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Phosphor-us" as "light-bearer" from Greek "phosphoros" — phosphorus glows in the dark, literally bearing light.
把"Phosphorus"想成希腊语"phosphoros（光的承载者）"——磷在黑暗中会发光，字面意思就是承载光芒。
