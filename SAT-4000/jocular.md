# 😆 jocular

🔊 /ˈdʒɒkjʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Fond of or characterized by joking; humorous or playful in manner
**中文：** adj. 爱开玩笑的；打趣的；滑稽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- humorous（幽默的）
- witty（机智的）
- jovial（快活的）
- facetious（滑稽的）
- jocose（诙谐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jocular tone / jocular remark / jocular manner / in a jocular way**

👉 Example: The professor was known for his jocular manner, which made even the most difficult lectures enjoyable.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **jocular** exchange with the foreign minister helped ease tensions and set a more cooperative tone for negotiations.
   外交官与外交部长诙谐的交谈有助于缓和紧张局势，并为谈判定下了更加合作的基调。

2. Although his comment was intended to be **jocular**, several audience members interpreted the remark as insensitive and inappropriate.
   虽然他的评论本意是打趣的，但一些观众将这番话理解为不敏感且不恰当的。

3. The chairman opened the annual meeting with a **jocular** anecdote that immediately put shareholders at ease and drew warm applause.
   董事长以一则诙谐的趣闻开始年度会议，立刻让股东们放松下来并赢得热烈掌声。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jocular" as "joker" + "ular" — a jocular person is like a joker, always ready to make others laugh with humorous remarks.
把"jocular"想成"joker（小丑）"+"ular"——一个jocular的人就像一个爱搞笑的人，总是准备好用幽默的话让别人笑。
