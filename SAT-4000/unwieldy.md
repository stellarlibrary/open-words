# 🏋️ unwieldy

🔊 /ʌnˈwiːldi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Difficult to carry, move, or manage because of size, shape, or complexity; clumsy or awkward
**中文：** adj. 笨拙的；笨重的；难处理的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cumbersome（笨重的）
- bulky（庞大的）
- clumsy（笨拙的）
- awkward（不便的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unwieldy bureaucracy / unwieldy system / unwieldy object / unwieldy process**

👉 Example: The unwieldy filing system made it nearly impossible for staff to retrieve documents efficiently.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **unwieldy** bureaucratic procedures delayed the approval of critical infrastructure projects by several months.
   笨重的官僚程序使关键基础设施项目的审批推迟了数月。

2. Critics argued that the proposed legislation was too **unwieldy** to implement effectively within the existing regulatory framework.
   批评者认为拟议的立法过于繁琐，无法在现有监管框架内有效实施。

3. The researchers struggled to transport the **unwieldy** equipment through the narrow corridors of the aging laboratory building.
   研究人员费力地将笨重的设备搬过老旧实验楼的狭窄走廊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-wield-y" — something you cannot wield or handle easily, like trying to swing a giant, heavy sword.
把"unwieldy"想成"无法挥舞的"——就像试图挥动一把又大又重的剑，根本无法轻松驾驭。
