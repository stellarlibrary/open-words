# 🧠 visualize

🔊 /ˈvɪʒuəlaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To form a mental image of something; to make something visible or imaginable; to represent graphically
**中文：** v. 形象化；想像，设想

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imagine（想象）
- envision（设想）
- picture（描绘）
- conceive（构想）
- envisage（展望）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **visualize success / visualize the outcome / visualize data / hard to visualize**

👉 Example: Athletes often visualize their performance before a competition to enhance focus and confidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coach encouraged athletes to **visualize** their victory in detail before stepping onto the field for the championship game.
   教练鼓励运动员在踏上冠军赛场之前详细地想象自己的胜利。

2. Scientists used advanced software to **visualize** the complex molecular structures that were previously impossible to observe directly.
   科学家们使用先进的软件来将以前无法直接观察到的复杂分子结构可视化。

3. It is often difficult to **visualize** the scale of geological time without the aid of comparative diagrams and illustrations.
   如果没有对比图表和插图的辅助，通常很难想象地质时间的尺度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "visualize" as "visual" + "-ize" (to make) — to visualize is to make something visual in your mind, to see it without your eyes.
把"visualize"想成"visual"（视觉的）+ "-ize"（使……化）——visualize就是在脑海中使某事变得可见，用心眼去看。
