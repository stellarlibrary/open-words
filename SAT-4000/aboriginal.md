# 🌏 aboriginal

🔊 /ˌæbəˈrɪdʒɪnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inhabiting or existing in a land from the earliest times; indigenous; native. (n.) An original inhabitant of a region
**中文：** adj. 土著的；原始的 n. 土著居民；土生生物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indigenous（本土的）
- native（土生的）
- original（原始的）
- autochthonous（土著的）
- primeval（原始的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aboriginal people / aboriginal culture / aboriginal land / aboriginal rights / Aboriginal Australians**

👉 Example: The museum features an extensive collection of aboriginal art from communities across the continent.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government formally recognized **aboriginal** land rights after decades of legal battles and public advocacy campaigns.
   经过数十年的法律斗争和公众倡导活动，政府正式承认了土著土地权利。

2. Linguists are working to preserve **aboriginal** languages that face extinction due to declining numbers of native speakers.
   语言学家正在努力保护因母语使用者减少而面临灭绝的土著语言。

3. The documentary explores how **aboriginal** communities have maintained traditional ecological knowledge for thousands of years.
   这部纪录片探讨了土著社区如何在数千年间保持了传统的生态知识。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Ab-original" — "ab" (from the) + "original" — the original people from the very beginning of a land.
"Ab-original"——"ab"（从）+ "original"（最初的）——从一片土地最初就存在的人。
