# 🔥 abrade

🔊 /əˈbreɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To scrape or wear away by friction or erosion; to irritate or roughen by rubbing
**中文：** v. 磨损；摩擦；擦伤；折磨

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- erode（侵蚀）
- scrape（刮擦）
- wear away（磨损）
- chafe（擦伤）
- grind（研磨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abrade the surface / abraded skin / abraded by wind / abrade against something**

👉 Example: Years of harsh winds and sand had visibly abraded the surface of the ancient stone monument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coarse fabric of the uniform **abraded** his skin, leaving painful red marks along his shoulders.
   制服的粗糙面料磨伤了他的皮肤，在肩膀上留下了疼痛的红印。

2. Constant foot traffic over the centuries has **abraded** the marble steps of the cathedral to a smooth finish.
   几个世纪以来的持续踩踏已将大教堂的大理石台阶磨得光滑如镜。

3. Windblown sand particles gradually **abrade** exposed rock surfaces, sculpting dramatic formations in desert landscapes.
   被风吹动的沙粒逐渐磨蚀裸露的岩石表面，在沙漠景观中塑造出壮观的地貌。

---

"A-BRADE"——想象一个"blade"（刀片）通过摩擦刮擦和磨损物体，就像abrade一样。
