# 😈 incubus

🔊 /ˈɪŋkjʊbəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A cause of distress or anxiety; a nightmare; an oppressive burden that weighs heavily on the mind
**中文：** n. 梦魇；沉重的负担

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nightmare（噩梦）
- burden（负担）
- oppression（压迫）
- torment（折磨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an incubus of debt / an incubus on the mind / lift the incubus / shake off the incubus**

👉 Example: The mounting national debt became an incubus on future generations of taxpayers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The overwhelming student loan debt hung over her like an **incubus**, preventing her from pursuing long-term financial goals.
   沉重的学生贷款债务像梦魇一样笼罩着她，使她无法追求长期的财务目标。

2. For decades, the colonial legacy remained an **incubus** on the newly independent nation's political and economic development.
   几十年来，殖民遗产一直是这个新独立国家政治和经济发展的沉重负担。

3. The unresolved scandal proved to be an **incubus** that haunted the politician throughout his entire career in public office.
   这桩悬而未决的丑闻成了梦魇，在这位政治家的整个公职生涯中一直困扰着他。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "incubus" as "in-cube-us" — imagine being trapped inside a tiny cube, feeling the crushing weight pressing down like a nightmare.
把"incubus"想成"in-cube-us"——想象被困在一个小方块里，感受到像噩梦一样的沉重压迫感。
