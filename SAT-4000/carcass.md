# 🦴 carcass

🔊 /ˈkɑːrkəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The dead body of an animal, especially one prepared for cutting as meat; the remains or shell of something
**中文：** n. （人或动物的）尸体；残骸；畜体

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corpse（尸体）
- remains（遗骸）
- cadaver（尸体）
- body（躯体）
- shell（残骸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **animal carcass / carcass of a building / carcass meat / rotting carcass / stripped carcass**

👉 Example: The vultures circled above the carcass of the fallen animal on the open savanna.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Health inspectors examined the **carcass** of each animal to ensure compliance with federal food safety regulations and standards.
   卫生检查员对每只动物的畜体进行了检查，以确保符合联邦食品安全法规和标准。

2. The abandoned factory's **carcass** stood as a haunting monument to the region's once-thriving but now defunct industrial heritage.
   废弃工厂的残骸矗立着，成为该地区曾经繁荣但如今消亡的工业遗产的令人难忘的纪念碑。

3. Biologists studied the whale **carcass** that washed ashore to determine the probable cause of its untimely and mysterious death.
   生物学家研究了被冲上岸的鲸鱼尸体，以确定其过早和神秘死亡的可能原因。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carcass" as "car + cass" — imagine a wrecked car (car-cass) left on the roadside, like the empty shell of something that was once alive and moving.
把"carcass"想成"car + cass"——想象一辆被丢弃在路边的报废汽车（car-cass），就像曾经活着并运转的东西留下的空壳。
