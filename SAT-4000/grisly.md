# 💀 grisly

🔊 /ˈɡrɪzli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing horror or disgust; gruesome; shockingly repellent
**中文：** adj. 恐怖的；可怕的；令人毛骨悚然的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gruesome（可怕的）
- horrifying（骇人的）
- ghastly（恐怖的）
- macabre（恐怖的）
- hideous（丑恶的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grisly discovery / grisly scene / grisly murder / grisly details / grisly fate**

👉 Example: The detective warned his partner to prepare for the grisly scene that awaited them inside the building.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forensic team documented every **grisly** detail of the crime scene to ensure that no critical evidence was overlooked.
   法医团队记录了犯罪现场每一个恐怖的细节，以确保没有遗漏关键证据。

2. Ancient chronicles describe the **grisly** punishments inflicted upon traitors as a deterrent to those who might challenge royal authority.
   古代编年史描述了对叛徒施加的可怕惩罚，以此威慑那些可能挑战王权的人。

3. The documentary spared viewers none of the **grisly** realities of warfare, presenting an unflinching account of battlefield conditions.
   这部纪录片毫不避讳地展示了战争的恐怖现实，如实呈现了战场上的状况。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grisly" as "grizzly" without the second z — like encountering a grizzly bear, it is a terrifying and horrifying experience.
把"grisly"想成少了一个z的"grizzly"——就像遇到灰熊一样，是一种恐怖骇人的经历。
