# 🚨 abduction

🔊 /æbˈdʌkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of kidnapping or carrying someone away by force; the process of logical inference from observation
**中文：** n. 诱拐，绑架；诱导推理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- kidnapping（绑架）
- seizure（劫持）
- snatching（抢夺）
- capture（俘获）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **child abduction / abduction case / alien abduction / attempted abduction**

👉 Example: The police launched a nationwide search following the abduction of the diplomat's daughter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **abduction** of the journalist sparked international outrage and demands for her immediate release.
   这名记者被绑架引发了国际社会的愤怒以及要求立即释放她的呼声。

2. Authorities confirmed that the **abduction** was carefully planned by an organized criminal network operating across borders.
   当局确认此次绑架是由一个跨境有组织犯罪网络精心策划的。

3. Parents grew increasingly concerned about child **abduction** after several incidents were reported in the neighborhood.
   在社区报告了多起事件后，家长们对儿童绑架问题日益担忧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "ab-" (away) + "duct" (lead) + "-ion" — to lead someone away, which is essentially kidnapping.
拆解记忆："ab-"（离开）+ "duct"（引导）+ "-ion"——把人引走，就是绑架。
