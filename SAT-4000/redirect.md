# 🔀 redirect

🔊 /ˌriːdəˈrekt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To change the direction or course of something; to send to a different destination or address; to repurpose
**中文：** v. 使改方向；使改道；重新寄送；更改姓名地址

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reroute（改变路线）
- divert（转移）
- channel（引导）
- reallocate（重新分配）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **redirect traffic / redirect funds / redirect attention / redirect resources / redirect efforts**

👉 Example: The manager decided to redirect the team's efforts toward a more achievable set of quarterly objectives.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The municipal government voted to **redirect** transportation funds toward the construction of a new public transit system.
   市政府投票决定将交通资金重新用于建设新的公共交通系统。

2. The therapist helped the patient **redirect** negative thought patterns into more constructive and productive cognitive habits.
   治疗师帮助患者将消极的思维模式重新引导为更具建设性和生产力的认知习惯。

3. Emergency personnel were dispatched to **redirect** vehicular traffic away from the flooded highway during the severe storm.
   紧急人员被派去在暴风雨期间将车辆从被洪水淹没的高速公路上改道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-direct" — "re" means again and "direct" means to guide, so redirect means to guide something in a new direction.
把"redirect"想成"re(重新) + direct(引导)"——重新引导方向，即使改变方向。
