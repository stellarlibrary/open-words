# 🐣 rookie

🔊 /ˈrʊki/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A newcomer or beginner, especially in a profession or activity
**中文：** n. 新手

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- novice（新手）
- beginner（初学者）
- newcomer（新来者）
- neophyte（新入门者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rookie mistake / rookie season / rookie officer / rookie year**

👉 Example: The rookie officer quickly earned the respect of his colleagues through diligence and exceptional performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **rookie** journalist made the common error of relying on a single anonymous source for the entire story.
   这位新手记者犯了一个常见错误，整篇报道仅依赖一个匿名来源。

2. Despite being a **rookie** in the industry, she demonstrated remarkable insight and secured several major client contracts.
   尽管是行业新手，她展现了非凡的洞察力并拿下了几个主要客户合同。

3. The coach assigned a veteran mentor to guide each **rookie** through the demanding rigors of professional athletic competition.
   教练为每位新人指派了一位资深导师，引导他们度过职业体育竞赛的严格考验。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rookie" as sounding like "rook" in chess — the rook starts in the corner and must learn to move across the board, just like a rookie learning the ropes.
把"rookie"联想到国际象棋中的"rook（车）"——车从角落开始，必须学会在棋盘上行动，就像新手需要学习基本技能一样。
