# 🤝 rapprochement

🔊 /ˌræprɒʃˈmɑːn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An establishment or resumption of harmonious relations, especially between nations; reconciliation
**中文：** n. 友好关系的恢复；和解；亲善

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reconciliation（和解）
- detente（缓和）
- conciliation（调和）
- reunion（重聚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rapprochement between / rapprochement with / diplomatic rapprochement / seek rapprochement**

👉 Example: The two nations pursued a rapprochement after decades of mutual distrust and hostility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomatic **rapprochement** between the rival nations marked a significant turning point in the region's political stability.
   这两个对立国家之间的外交和解标志着该地区政治稳定的重要转折点。

2. Historians credit the cultural exchange program with facilitating a gradual **rapprochement** between the formerly hostile neighboring countries.
   历史学家认为文化交流项目促进了这两个曾经敌对的邻国之间的逐步和解。

3. The president's speech signaled a willingness to pursue **rapprochement** with adversaries through dialogue rather than confrontation.
   总统的讲话表明了通过对话而非对抗寻求与对手和解的意愿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rapprochement" from French "rapprocher" meaning "to bring together" — two sides approach each other to restore friendship.
"rapprochement"来自法语"rapprocher"，意为"使靠近"——双方彼此走近以恢复友好关系。
