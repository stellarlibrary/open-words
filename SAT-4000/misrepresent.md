# 🎭 misrepresent

🔊 /ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To give a false or misleading account of something; to distort or convey inaccurately
**中文：** v. 歪曲；误传；不合适地代表

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distort（歪曲）
- falsify（篡改）
- misstate（说错）
- mischaracterize（错误描述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **misrepresent the facts / misrepresent oneself / deliberately misrepresent / grossly misrepresent**

👉 Example: The defendant was charged with deliberately misrepresenting his financial assets to the court.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The advertisement was found to **misrepresent** the product's effectiveness, leading regulatory authorities to impose substantial financial penalties.
   该广告被发现歪曲了产品的功效，导致监管机构处以了巨额罚款。

2. Historians have argued that popular accounts often **misrepresent** the true complexity of events surrounding the revolution.
   历史学家认为，流行的叙述往往歪曲了围绕这场革命的事件的真正复杂性。

3. The lobbyist was accused of attempting to **misrepresent** the environmental impact of the proposed industrial development project.
   这位游说者被指控试图歪曲拟议工业开发项目的环境影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mis-represent" as "representing something in the wrong way" — presenting a false picture instead of the truth.
把"misrepresent"想成"以错误的方式呈现（represent）"——展示一幅虚假的画面而非真相。
