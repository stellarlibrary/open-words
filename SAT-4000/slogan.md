# 📢 slogan

🔊 /ˈsloʊɡən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A short, memorable phrase used in advertising or political campaigns; a rallying cry or motto
**中文：** n. 标语，口号

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- motto（格言）
- catchphrase（标语）
- rallying cry（口号）
- tagline（宣传语）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **campaign slogan / catchy slogan / advertising slogan / chant a slogan**

👉 Example: The company's new slogan quickly became recognizable to consumers across the entire country.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political candidate's **slogan** resonated with millions of voters and became a defining symbol of the entire campaign.
   这位政治候选人的口号引起了数百万选民的共鸣，成为整个竞选的标志性符号。

2. Marketing experts spent months crafting a memorable **slogan** that would distinguish the brand from its numerous competitors.
   营销专家花了数月时间打造一条令人难忘的标语，以使该品牌区别于众多竞争对手。

3. Protesters marched through the capital chanting **slogans** that demanded immediate reform of the nation's education system.
   抗议者在首都游行，高呼口号，要求立即改革国家的教育体系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "slogan" as "s-log-an" — you log an idea into people's minds with a short, catchy phrase.
把"slogan"想成"s-log-an"——用一句简短上口的话把想法"记录"进人们的脑海中。
