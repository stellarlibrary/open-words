# ⚠️ unduly

🔊 /ʌnˈdjuːli/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) To an excessive or unwarranted degree; more than is reasonable or appropriate
**中文：** adv. 过度地；不适当地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- excessively（过度地）
- disproportionately（不成比例地）
- unreasonably（不合理地）
- inordinately（过分地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unduly influenced / unduly concerned / unduly harsh / unduly optimistic / unduly burdened**

👉 Example: The teacher cautioned students not to be unduly alarmed by the difficulty of the upcoming examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics argued that the new legislation would **unduly** restrict freedom of expression and limit journalistic independence.
   批评者认为新的立法会过度限制言论自由并削弱新闻独立性。

2. The board was advised not to be **unduly** optimistic about projected revenues given the current economic uncertainty.
   鉴于当前的经济不确定性，董事会被建议不要对预期收入过度乐观。

3. The defendant claimed that the sentence was **unduly** severe and filed an immediate appeal with the higher court.
   被告声称判决过于严厉，并立即向上级法院提起上诉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "unduly" as the adverb form of "undue" — it describes doing something in an undue (excessive, unwarranted) manner.
把"unduly"想成"undue"的副词形式——它描述以一种过度的、不正当的方式做某事。
