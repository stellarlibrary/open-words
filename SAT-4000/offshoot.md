# 🌿 offshoot

🔊 /ˈɔːfʃuːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A branch or division that develops from a main body or source; a derivative or byproduct
**中文：** n. 分支；支流；衍生物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- branch（分支）
- derivative（衍生物）
- spinoff（派生物）
- outgrowth（自然结果）
- byproduct（副产品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **offshoot of a company / offshoot of a movement / offshoot of a plant / direct offshoot**

👉 Example: The new department was an offshoot of the original research division established a decade earlier.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technology startup began as an **offshoot** of a university research lab focused on artificial intelligence and machine learning.
   这家科技初创公司起初是一所大学人工智能和机器学习研究实验室的分支。

2. Several political factions emerged as **offshoots** of the original reform movement that had swept through the country in the 1990s.
   几个政治派系作为20世纪90年代席卷全国的改革运动的分支而出现。

3. The gardener carefully transplanted the **offshoot** from the mature plant to a separate pot to encourage independent growth.
   园丁小心地将成熟植株的分枝移植到单独的花盆中，以促进其独立生长。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "off + shoot" — a shoot that grows "off" the main plant, branching out in a new direction on its own.
把"offshoot"想成"off + shoot"——从主植物上"离开"生长的嫩枝，朝新方向自行发展。
