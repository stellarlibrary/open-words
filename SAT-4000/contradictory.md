# 🔄 contradictory

🔊 /ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Mutually opposed or inconsistent; containing elements that are in direct conflict. (n.) A proposition or statement that is the opposite of another
**中文：** adj. 矛盾的；反对的；对立的 n. 对立物；矛盾命题

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inconsistent（不一致的）
- conflicting（冲突的）
- incompatible（不相容的）
- paradoxical（矛盾的）
- opposed（对立的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contradictory statements / contradictory evidence / mutually contradictory / contradictory to**

👉 Example: The report contained several contradictory claims that undermined its overall credibility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee received **contradictory** reports from two independent agencies regarding the environmental impact of the proposed development.
   委员会收到了两个独立机构关于拟议开发项目环境影响的矛盾报告。

2. His public statements were entirely **contradictory** to the private assurances he had given to his closest political allies.
   他的公开声明与他私下向最亲密的政治盟友做出的保证完全矛盾。

3. The philosopher argued that holding **contradictory** beliefs simultaneously reveals a fundamental failure in logical reasoning and critical thought.
   这位哲学家认为，同时持有矛盾的信念揭示了逻辑推理和批判性思维的根本缺陷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "contradictory" as "contra-dictionary" — as if two dictionaries gave opposite definitions for the same word, creating a contradiction.
把"contradictory"想成"两本相反的词典"——好像两本词典对同一个词给出了相反的定义，形成了矛盾。
