# 😐 bland

🔊 /blænd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lacking strong features or qualities; mild and dull; showing no strong emotions or interest
**中文：** adj. 乏味的；温和的；柔和的；冷漠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insipid（淡而无味的）
- vapid（索然无味的）
- nondescript（平淡无奇的）
- tasteless（无味的）
- mild（温和的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bland diet / bland expression / bland food / bland personality / bland taste**

👉 Example: The doctor recommended a bland diet to help soothe the patient's irritated stomach lining.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee issued a deliberately **bland** statement that avoided taking any definitive position on the controversy.
   委员会发表了一份刻意平淡的声明，避免在争议问题上表明任何明确立场。

2. Despite the chef's reputation, the meal was disappointingly **bland** and lacked the complexity of flavor we expected.
   尽管这位厨师名声在外，这顿饭却令人失望地乏味，缺乏我们期待的丰富口感。

3. His **bland** demeanor during the interrogation made it impossible for investigators to determine whether he was lying.
   他在审讯中冷漠的态度使调查人员无法判断他是否在撒谎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bland" rhyming with "land" — picture a flat, featureless land with nothing interesting as far as the eye can see.
"bland"和"land"押韵——想象一片平坦无奇的土地，目之所及毫无趣味。
