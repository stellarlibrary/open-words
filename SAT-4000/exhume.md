# ⛏️ exhume

🔊 /ɪɡˈzjuːm/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To dig out something buried, especially a corpse, from the ground; to bring to light something that has been forgotten or concealed
**中文：** v. 掘出；发掘；挖掘（尤指尸体）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- disinter（掘出）
- unearth（发掘）
- excavate（挖掘）
- dig up（挖出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exhume a body / exhume remains / exhume evidence / exhume the past**

👉 Example: Authorities decided to exhume the remains for further forensic examination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court ordered officials to **exhume** the victim's remains in order to conduct a more thorough forensic investigation.
   法院命令官员掘出受害者的遗骸，以便进行更彻底的法医调查。

2. Archaeologists plan to **exhume** artifacts from the ancient burial site that may reveal significant details about the civilization.
   考古学家计划从古代墓葬遗址中发掘可能揭示该文明重要细节的文物。

3. The historian sought to **exhume** forgotten documents from the national archives that could reshape our understanding of the era.
   这位历史学家试图从国家档案馆中发掘被遗忘的文件，这些文件可能重塑我们对那个时代的理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-hume" — "ex" means out, and "hume" comes from Latin "humus" meaning earth or ground, so exhume means to take out of the earth.
把"exhume"想成"ex-hume"——"ex"意为向外，"hume"源自拉丁语"humus"意为泥土，所以exhume意为从土中取出。
