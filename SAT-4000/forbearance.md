# 🧘 forbearance

🔊 /fɔːrˈbɛrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Patient self-control; restraint and tolerance; the quality of being willing to forgive or show mercy
**中文：** n. 自制；忍耐；宽容

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- patience（耐心）
- tolerance（宽容）
- restraint（克制）
- leniency（宽大）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **show forbearance / exercise forbearance / with great forbearance / forbearance toward**

👉 Example: The judge showed remarkable forbearance toward the defendant despite repeated contempt of court.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat displayed extraordinary **forbearance** during the tense negotiations, refusing to respond to deliberate provocations.
   外交官在紧张的谈判中表现出非凡的忍耐力，拒绝回应蓄意的挑衅。

2. Her **forbearance** in the face of persistent criticism earned her the deep admiration of colleagues and supervisors alike.
   她面对持续批评时的克制为她赢得了同事和上级的深深敬佩。

3. The school's policy emphasizes **forbearance** and understanding rather than harsh punishment when addressing student misconduct.
   学校的政策在处理学生不当行为时强调宽容和理解，而非严厉惩罚。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "for + bear + ance" — you bear with something for a long time, showing patience and restraint.
把"forbearance"想成"for + bear + ance"——你长时间忍受某事，展现耐心和克制。
