# 🌫️ equivocal

🔊 /ɪˈkwɪvəkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Open to more than one interpretation; ambiguous or deliberately vague; questionable or suspicious
**中文：** adj. 模棱两可的；意义不明确的；可疑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ambiguous（含糊的）
- vague（模糊的）
- unclear（不清楚的）
- dubious（可疑的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **equivocal response / equivocal results / equivocal evidence / equivocal language**

👉 Example: The witness gave an equivocal answer that left the jury uncertain about the truth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The test results were **equivocal**, forcing the medical team to order additional diagnostic procedures before reaching a definitive conclusion.
   检测结果模棱两可，迫使医疗团队在得出明确结论之前要求进行额外的诊断程序。

2. The senator's **equivocal** stance on the proposed legislation frustrated both supporters and opponents who demanded a clear position.
   参议员对拟议立法模棱两可的立场令支持者和反对者都感到沮丧，他们要求其明确表态。

3. Historians remain divided because the **equivocal** nature of the ancient text permits multiple contradictory interpretations of the event.
   历史学家们仍然存在分歧，因为这篇古代文献意义不明确的特性允许对该事件进行多种相互矛盾的解读。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equi-vocal" as "equal voices" — when two voices say different things equally, the meaning becomes unclear.
把"equivocal"想成"equi（相等）+ vocal（声音）"——当两种声音同样响亮地说着不同的话，意思就变得不清楚了。
