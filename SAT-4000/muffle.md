# 🔇 muffle

🔊 /ˈmʌfl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To wrap or cover for warmth or protection; to deaden or suppress the sound of; to restrain or conceal. (n.) A muted or suppressed sound; something used to deaden sound
**中文：** v. 蒙住；裹住；抑制；发低沉的声音 n. 低沉的声音；消声器；包裹物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stifle（抑制）
- dampen（减弱）
- suppress（压制）
- smother（闷住）
- deaden（使减弱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **muffle a sound / muffle a cry / muffle one's voice / muffled footsteps**

👉 Example: She pressed the pillow against her face to muffle her sobs so that no one would hear.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The thick stone walls of the medieval library served to **muffle** the disruptive noises from the bustling market outside.
   中世纪图书馆的厚石墙有效地隔绝了外面热闹市场的嘈杂声。

2. He wrapped his scarf tightly around his face to **muffle** himself against the biting wind and freezing temperatures.
   他把围巾紧紧围在脸上，以抵御刺骨的寒风和冰冻的气温。

3. The diplomat attempted to **muffle** the controversy by issuing a carefully worded statement to the international press corps.
   这位外交官试图通过向国际记者团发表措辞谨慎的声明来压制争议。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "muffle" as "MUFF-le" — a muff is a warm covering for hands, and to muffle is to cover and suppress sound or warmth.
把"muffle"想成"MUFF-le"——muff是暖手筒，muffle就是包裹、蒙住，压制声音。
