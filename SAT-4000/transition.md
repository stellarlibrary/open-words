# 🔄 transition

🔊 /trænˈzɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process or period of changing from one state, condition, or stage to another. (v.) To undergo or cause a change from one state to another.
**中文：** n. 过渡；转变；变迁 v. 过渡；转变。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- change（变化）
- shift（转变）
- transformation（转型）
- conversion（转换）
- passage（过渡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transition from A to B / smooth transition / transition period / make a transition / transition phase**

👉 Example: The country experienced a difficult transition from authoritarian rule to democratic governance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **transition** from a manufacturing-based economy to a service-oriented one required substantial investment in workforce retraining programs.
   从制造业经济向服务业经济的转变需要在劳动力再培训项目上进行大量投资。

2. Adolescence represents a critical **transition** period during which individuals develop their sense of identity and social autonomy.
   青春期代表了一个关键的过渡时期，在此期间个体发展其身份认同和社会自主性。

3. The organization plans to **transition** entirely to renewable energy sources within the next decade to reduce carbon emissions.
   该组织计划在未来十年内完全过渡到可再生能源，以减少碳排放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transition" as "trans (across) + ition (going)" — going across from one state to another, like crossing a bridge between two stages.
把"transition"想成"跨越（trans）+ 行进（ition）"——从一个状态跨越到另一个状态，就像在两个阶段之间过桥。
