# 🤝 accomplice

🔊 /əˈkɑːm.plɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who helps another commit a crime or do something wrong.
**中文：** 同谋者；共犯；帮凶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confederate（同盟者）
- accessory（从犯）
- abettor（教唆者）
- collaborator（合作者）
- partner in crime（同谋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accomplice in/to (a crime/scheme)**

👉 Example: The detective identified her as an accomplice to the robbery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court found him guilty of serving as an **accomplice** in the elaborate fraud scheme.
   法院判定他在这个精心策划的欺诈案中充当了共犯。

2. Without a willing **accomplice**, the spy could never have smuggled the classified documents abroad.
   没有一个自愿的同谋，间谍绝不可能将机密文件偷运出境。

3. She was arrested as an **accomplice** to the embezzlement after investigators traced the funds.
   在调查人员追踪到资金去向后，她因涉嫌挪用公款的同谋罪被逮捕。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "ac-COMP-lice" — a companion in lies, someone who accompanies you in wrongdoing.
把 accomplice 拆成 ac + compli(同 comply 服从) + ce，想象一个"服从"犯罪计划的人，就是"共犯"。
