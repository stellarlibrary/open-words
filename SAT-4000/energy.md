# ⚡ energy

🔊 /ˈenərdʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The capacity for vigorous activity or exertion; the power derived from physical or chemical resources to do work
**中文：** n. 能量；精力；活力

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vitality（活力）
- vigor（精力）
- power（力量）
- stamina（耐力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **renewable energy / conserve energy / devote energy to / burst of energy**

👉 Example: The nation invested heavily in renewable energy sources to reduce its dependence on fossil fuels.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists are developing innovative methods to harness solar **energy** more efficiently for widespread commercial and residential use.
   科学家们正在开发创新方法，以更高效地利用太阳能，供广泛的商业和住宅使用。

2. She devoted all her **energy** to the research project, sacrificing weekends and holidays to ensure its timely completion.
   她把所有精力都投入到这个研究项目中，牺牲了周末和假期以确保按时完成。

3. The government allocated substantial resources to **energy** conservation programs aimed at reducing the nation's carbon footprint significantly.
   政府拨出大量资源用于能源节约计划，旨在大幅减少国家的碳足迹。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "energy" as "en-erg-y" — "erg" is an actual unit of energy in physics, so the word contains its own definition.
把"energy"想成"en-erg-y"——"erg"是物理学中能量的实际单位，所以这个词本身就包含了它的定义。
