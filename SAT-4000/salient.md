# 🎯 salient

🔊 /ˈseɪliənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Prominent, conspicuous, or highly noticeable; projecting outward. (n.) An outward bulge or projecting angle
**中文：** adj. 显著的，卓越的；凸出的，突起的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prominent（突出的）
- conspicuous（显著的）
- notable（值得注意的）
- striking（引人注目的）
- outstanding（卓越的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salient point / salient feature / salient fact / most salient aspect**

👉 Example: The most salient feature of the proposal was its innovative approach to renewable energy funding.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The report identified several **salient** issues that required immediate attention from the board of directors and senior management.
   报告指出了几个需要董事会和高级管理层立即关注的显著问题。

2. One **salient** difference between the two theories lies in their fundamental assumptions about human decision-making under uncertainty.
   两种理论之间的一个显著区别在于它们对人类在不确定性下决策的基本假设不同。

3. The professor urged students to focus on the **salient** arguments rather than becoming distracted by peripheral and trivial details.
   教授敦促学生关注突出的论点，而不是被边缘和琐碎的细节所分散注意力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sail-ient" — like a sail that stands out prominently above a ship, a salient point stands out above everything else.
把"salient"想成"sail（帆）+ ient"——就像帆在船上高高突起、格外醒目一样，salient的事物总是最显著、最突出的。
