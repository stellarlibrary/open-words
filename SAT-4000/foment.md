# 🔥 foment

🔊 /foʊˈmɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To instigate or stir up (trouble, rebellion, or discord); to apply warm liquids to the body for therapeutic purposes
**中文：** v. 煽动；挑起；热敷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- instigate（煽动）
- incite（激起）
- provoke（挑起）
- stir up（煽动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **foment rebellion / foment discord / foment unrest / foment trouble**

👉 Example: Radical pamphlets were distributed to foment rebellion among the oppressed citizens.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The opposition leader was accused of attempting to **foment** unrest among factory workers during the economic crisis.
   反对派领导人被指控在经济危机期间试图在工厂工人中煽动骚乱。

2. Historians argue that foreign agents deliberately **fomented** discord between the two neighboring nations for strategic advantage.
   历史学家认为，外国特工为了战略利益故意在两个邻国之间挑起不和。

3. The inflammatory speech was designed to **foment** division within the academic community over the proposed curriculum reforms.
   这篇煽动性的演讲旨在就拟议的课程改革在学术界内部挑起分裂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "foment" as "foam + meant" — when you stir a liquid it foams up, just as fomenting stirs up trouble.
把"foment"想成"foam + meant"——当你搅动液体时它会起泡，就像煽动会搅起麻烦一样。
