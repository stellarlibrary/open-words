# 🏛️ oratory

🔊 /ˈɔːrətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The art or practice of formal public speaking, especially in an eloquent and persuasive manner
**中文：** n. 雄辩；演讲术

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eloquence（雄辩）
- rhetoric（修辞学）
- public speaking（公共演讲）
- elocution（演说术）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the art of oratory / political oratory / masterful oratory / gift for oratory**

👉 Example: His gift for oratory enabled him to rally supporters and influence public opinion effectively.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study of **oratory** was considered a fundamental component of classical education in both ancient Greece and Rome.
   演讲术的研习在古希腊和罗马都被视为古典教育的基本组成部分。

2. Her exceptional **oratory** during the parliamentary debate persuaded several undecided legislators to support the proposed reform bill.
   她在议会辩论中出色的演讲术说服了几位犹豫不决的立法者支持拟议的改革法案。

3. The training program emphasized **oratory** skills to help young professionals communicate their ideas with confidence and precision.
   该培训项目强调演讲技巧，帮助年轻专业人士自信而精确地表达他们的想法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oratory" as "orator-y" — the skill or art that an "orator" practices when delivering powerful speeches.
把"oratory"想成"orator-y"——演说家（orator）在发表有力演讲时所运用的技艺（-y）。
