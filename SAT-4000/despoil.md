# ⚔️ despoil

🔊 /dɪˈspɔɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To rob or plunder by force; to strip of possessions, honor, or beauty; to ravage
**中文：** v. 掠夺；剥夺；蹂躏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plunder（掠夺）
- pillage（抢劫）
- ravage（蹂躏）
- strip（剥夺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **despoil the land / despoil of resources / despoil a nation / despoiled countryside**

👉 Example: Invading armies despoiled the ancient temples of their priceless artifacts and sacred relics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Industrial mining operations have systematically **despoiled** vast stretches of pristine wilderness across the developing world.
   工业采矿作业已经系统性地掠夺了发展中国家大片原始荒野。

2. The conquering forces **despoiled** the city of its cultural treasures, leaving museums and libraries completely ransacked.
   征服者掠夺了城市的文化珍宝，博物馆和图书馆被洗劫一空。

3. Environmental advocates warned that unregulated logging would **despoil** the ancient forest and destroy irreplaceable ecological habitats.
   环保倡导者警告说，不受管制的伐木将糟蹋这片古老森林，摧毁不可替代的生态栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-spoil" as "to completely spoil" — to despoil is to ruin and strip something bare, spoiling it entirely.
把"despoil"想成"彻底spoil（破坏）"——despoil就是彻底毁坏和剥夺某物，将其完全糟蹋。
