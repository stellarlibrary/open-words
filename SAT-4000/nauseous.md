# 😖 nauseous

🔊 /ˈnɔːʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Causing nausea; feeling inclined to vomit; extremely unpleasant or disgusting
**中文：** adj. 令人作呕的；厌恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sickening（令人恶心的）
- revolting（令人作呕的）
- repulsive（令人反感的）
- queasy（引起恶心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel nauseous / nauseous smell / become nauseous / slightly nauseous**

👉 Example: The passengers felt nauseous after the ship encountered severe turbulence at sea.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **nauseous** fumes released by the chemical spill forced authorities to evacuate residents within a three-mile radius.
   化学品泄漏释放的令人作呕的烟雾迫使当局疏散了三英里半径内的居民。

2. Several students reported feeling **nauseous** during the laboratory demonstration involving highly volatile organic compounds.
   几名学生在涉及高度挥发性有机化合物的实验室演示中报告感到恶心。

3. The **nauseous** stench of decay permeated the archaeological site where ancient organic remains had recently been unearthed.
   腐烂的令人作呕的恶臭弥漫在最近出土了古代有机遗骸的考古遗址中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nauseous" as "nausea" + "-ous" (full of) — full of nausea, either causing it in others or feeling it yourself.
把"nauseous"想成"nausea"加"-ous"（充满……的）——充满恶心感，可以是使别人恶心，也可以是自己感到恶心。
