# 🎻 instrument

🔊 /ˈɪnstrəmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A tool or implement used for a particular purpose; a device for producing music; a means by which something is achieved; a formal legal document
**中文：** n. 仪器；工具；乐器；手段；器械

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tool（工具）
- device（设备）
- apparatus（仪器）
- implement（器具）
- mechanism（机制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **musical instrument / scientific instrument / instrument of change / instrument of policy / precision instrument**

👉 Example: The telescope remains one of the most important instruments in the history of astronomical observation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laboratory acquired several advanced analytical **instruments** capable of detecting molecular changes at the subatomic level with unprecedented precision.
   实验室购置了几台先进的分析仪器，能够以前所未有的精度检测亚原子级别的分子变化。

2. Education has long been regarded as the most powerful **instrument** of social mobility and economic advancement in democratic societies.
   在民主社会中，教育长期以来被视为社会流动和经济进步最有力的手段。

3. The surgeon carefully selected the appropriate surgical **instruments** before commencing the delicate and highly specialized cardiac procedure.
   外科医生在开始这项精细且高度专业化的心脏手术前仔细选择了合适的手术器械。
