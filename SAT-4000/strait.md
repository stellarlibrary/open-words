# 🌊 strait

🔊 /streɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Narrow or restricted. (n.) A narrow passage of water connecting two larger bodies of water; a difficult or troubled situation
**中文：** n. 海峡；（狭窄的）水道

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- channel（海峡）
- narrows（狭窄水道）
- predicament（困境）
- passage（通道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dire straits / Strait of Gibraltar / narrow strait / in desperate straits**

👉 Example: The ship navigated carefully through the treacherous strait under the cover of darkness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Commercial vessels must exercise extreme caution when navigating the narrow **strait** due to its unpredictable currents and heavy maritime traffic.
   商船在通过这条狭窄海峡时必须格外谨慎，因为这里水流变化莫测且海上交通繁忙。

2. The country found itself in dire **straits** after the sudden economic collapse devastated its financial markets and banking sector.
   该国在突然的经济崩溃摧毁了其金融市场和银行业之后陷入了严峻的困境。

3. The geopolitical significance of the **strait** has made it a focal point of international diplomatic negotiations for decades.
   这条海峡的地缘政治重要性使其数十年来一直是国际外交谈判的焦点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "strait" as "straight and narrow" — a strait is a narrow, straight-like passage of water between two landmasses.
把"strait"想成"straight and narrow（笔直而狭窄）"——海峡就是两块陆地之间一条狭窄的、像直线一样的水道。
