# 😤 harass

🔊 /həˈræs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To subject someone to aggressive pressure, intimidation, or persistent unwanted attention; to make repeated small-scale attacks
**中文：** v. 使困扰；使烦恼；侵扰；反复袭击

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- persecute（迫害）
- torment（折磨）
- pester（纠缠）
- bother（烦扰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **harass someone into / harass with demands / sexually harass / continually harass**

👉 Example: The investigative journalist was harassed by anonymous callers who sought to suppress the publication of her findings.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Federal legislation strictly prohibits employers from **harassing** workers on the basis of race, gender, religion, or national origin.
   联邦法律严格禁止雇主基于种族、性别、宗教或国籍对员工进行骚扰。

2. The guerrilla forces adopted a strategy of **harassing** the larger army through swift, unpredictable raids on vulnerable supply lines.
   游击队采取了通过对脆弱的补给线进行快速、不可预测的袭击来骚扰大部队的策略。

3. Persistent creditors continued to **harass** the struggling business owner with daily phone calls demanding immediate payment of debts.
   执着的债权人继续每天打电话骚扰这位苦苦挣扎的企业主，要求立即偿还债务。

Think of "harass" as "hare +ass" — imagine an annoying hare constantly bothering a donkey, pestering it without end.
把"harass"想成"hare + ass"——想象一只烦人的兔子不停地骚扰一头驴，无休止地纠缠它。
