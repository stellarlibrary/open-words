# 🏛️ statuesque

🔊 /ˌstætʃuˈɛsk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Resembling a statue in dignity, grace, or beauty; tall and attractively proportioned; having a stately or imposing quality
**中文：** adj. 雕像般的；庄严而优雅的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stately（庄严的）
- majestic（雄伟的）
- imposing（壮观的）
- dignified（端庄的）
- regal（高贵的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **statuesque figure / statuesque beauty / statuesque pose / statuesque elegance**

👉 Example: The statuesque model commanded attention the moment she appeared on the runway.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **statuesque** ballerina captivated the entire audience with her graceful movements and commanding stage presence throughout the performance.
   那位雕像般优雅的芭蕾舞演员以优美的动作和强大的舞台感染力在整场演出中吸引了全场观众。

2. Her **statuesque** bearing and composed demeanor made her an ideal candidate for the role of ambassador to the foreign nation.
   她雕像般庄严的仪态和沉稳的举止使她成为驻外大使的理想人选。

3. The architect designed the building's columns to achieve a **statuesque** grandeur reminiscent of ancient Greek and Roman temples.
   建筑师设计了这座建筑的立柱，使其呈现出令人联想到古希腊和罗马神庙的雕像般的宏伟感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "statue + esque" — the suffix "-esque" means "in the style of," so statuesque literally means "in the style of a statue," tall and dignified.
把"statuesque"想成"statue + esque"——后缀"-esque"意为"具有…风格的"，所以statuesque就是"如雕像般的"，高大而庄重。
