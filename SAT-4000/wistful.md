# 🌅 wistful

🔊 /ˈwɪstfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing a feeling of vague or regretful longing; pensively sad with yearning
**中文：** adj. 渴望的；沉思的；伤感的；徒然神往的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nostalgic（怀旧的）
- yearning（渴望的）
- melancholic（忧郁的）
- pensive（沉思的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wistful expression / wistful glance / wistful longing / wistful smile / wistful tone**

👉 Example: She cast a wistful glance at the old photograph, remembering the carefree summers of her youth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The retired professor gazed with **wistful** eyes at the campus where she had spent four decades inspiring generations of students.
   这位退休教授带着伤感的目光凝视着她曾花了四十年激励一代又一代学生的校园。

2. His memoir adopted a **wistful** tone as he reflected upon the ambitious dreams that had gradually yielded to practical necessities.
   他的回忆录在反思那些逐渐让位于现实需要的雄心壮志时采用了一种惆怅的语调。

3. The documentary captured the **wistful** expressions of elderly residents returning to their homeland after decades of involuntary exile.
   这部纪录片捕捉到了在数十年被迫流亡后重返故土的老年居民们伤感的表情。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wist-ful" as "wish-ful" — full of wishes for something lost or unattainable, gazing back with gentle sadness.
把"wistful"想成"wish-ful（充满愿望的）"——对失去或无法企及之物充满渴望，带着淡淡的忧伤回望。
