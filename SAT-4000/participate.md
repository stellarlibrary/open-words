# 🤝 participate

🔊 /pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To take part in an activity or event; to share in something with others
**中文：** v. 参与，参加；分享，分担

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engage（参与）
- partake（分享）
- contribute（贡献）
- join（加入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **participate in a discussion / participate in an event / participate actively / participate fully**

👉 Example: All students are encouraged to participate in the annual science fair competition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee urged every citizen to **participate** in the upcoming town hall meeting regarding the proposed zoning changes.
   委员会敦促每位市民参加即将举行的关于拟议分区变更的市政厅会议。

2. Several prominent scholars agreed to **participate** in the international symposium on sustainable energy and environmental policy.
   几位著名学者同意参加关于可持续能源和环境政策的国际研讨会。

3. Students who actively **participate** in classroom discussions tend to develop stronger critical thinking and analytical skills.
   积极参与课堂讨论的学生往往能培养出更强的批判性思维和分析能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "part-icipate" as "take PART in" — you become part of something by participating.
把"participate"想成"take PART in"——你通过参与而成为某事的一部分。
