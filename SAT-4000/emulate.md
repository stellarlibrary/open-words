# 🏅 emulate

🔊 /ˈɛmjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To match or surpass by imitation; to strive to equal or excel someone admired
**中文：** v. 仿真；模仿；尽力赶上

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imitate（模仿）
- mirror（效仿）
- replicate（复制）
- rival（与…匹敌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **emulate a role model / emulate the success of / emulate the style of / seek to emulate**

👉 Example: Young athletes often seek to emulate the techniques and discipline of their professional idols.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many developing nations have attempted to **emulate** the economic policies that propelled East Asian countries to rapid industrialization.
   许多发展中国家试图效仿推动东亚国家快速工业化的经济政策。

2. The software was designed to **emulate** the processing capabilities of older hardware systems on modern computing platforms.
   该软件旨在在现代计算平台上仿真旧硬件系统的处理能力。

3. She worked tirelessly to **emulate** her mentor's scholarly rigor and unwavering commitment to academic excellence.
   她孜孜不倦地努力效仿导师的学术严谨性和对卓越学术的坚定承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "emulate" as "I'm-you-late" — you arrive late but try to copy and catch up to someone you admire.
把"emulate"想成"I'm-you-late"——你来晚了，但试图模仿并赶上你所钦佩的人。
