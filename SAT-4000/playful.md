# 🎈 playful

🔊 /ˈpleɪfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Full of fun and high spirits; humorous; lighthearted and fond of games or amusement
**中文：** adj. 开玩笑的；幽默的；爱嬉戏的；顽皮的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frisky（活泼的）
- lighthearted（轻松的）
- whimsical（异想天开的）
- mischievous（淘气的）
- jovial（快活的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **playful tone / playful banter / playful smile / playful manner**

👉 Example: The professor adopted a playful tone to make the complex subject more accessible to her students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's **playful** use of language throughout the novel delighted readers and critics alike with its clever wit.
   作者在整部小说中对语言的巧妙运用以其机智幽默让读者和评论家都感到愉悦。

2. Despite the gravity of the negotiations, the diplomat maintained a **playful** demeanor that helped ease mounting tensions.
   尽管谈判气氛严肃，这位外交官保持了一种轻松幽默的态度，有助于缓解日益加剧的紧张局势。

3. The kitten's **playful** antics provided welcome amusement to the elderly residents of the community care center.
   小猫嬉戏的滑稽动作给社区护理中心的老年居民带来了令人欣慰的欢乐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "playful" as "full of play" — someone who is always ready to play and have fun, like a child at recess.
把"playful"想成"full of play（充满玩乐）"——一个总是准备好玩耍和享乐的人，就像课间休息时的孩子。
