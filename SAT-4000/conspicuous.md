# 👁️ conspicuous

🔊 /kənˈspɪkjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Standing out so as to be clearly visible or attracting notice; easily seen or noticed
**中文：** adj. 显眼的，明显的；引人注目的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prominent（突出的）
- noticeable（显而易见的）
- striking（引人注目的）
- obvious（明显的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conspicuous absence / conspicuous consumption / conspicuous by one's absence / conspicuous place**

👉 Example: The diplomat's conspicuous absence from the summit raised serious questions among international observers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's **conspicuous** silence during the debate suggested a deliberate strategy to avoid committing to either side.
   那位参议员在辩论中引人注目的沉默暗示了一种刻意回避任何一方的策略。

2. Sociologists have long studied **conspicuous** consumption as a means by which individuals signal wealth and social status.
   社会学家长期以来研究炫耀性消费，将其视为个人展示财富和社会地位的手段。

3. The error in the published report was so **conspicuous** that several reviewers questioned how it had escaped editorial scrutiny.
   已发表报告中的错误如此明显，以至于几位审稿人质疑它是如何逃过编辑审查的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-SPIC-uous" — the "spic" sounds like "spy," and a spy who is conspicuous is too easily spotted and noticed.
把"conspicuous"想成"con-SPIC-uous"——"spic"听起来像"spy（间谍）"，一个显眼的间谍太容易被发现了。
