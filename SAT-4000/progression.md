# 🔄 progression

🔊 /prəˈɡrɛʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of developing or moving gradually towards a more advanced state; a continuous sequence
**中文：** n. 前进；连续；发展；级数

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advancement（进步）
- development（发展）
- sequence（连续）
- evolution（演变）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **natural progression / career progression / progression from... to... / steady progression**

👉 Example: The disease showed a rapid progression from mild symptoms to a critical condition.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The student's **progression** from basic arithmetic to advanced calculus demonstrated remarkable intellectual growth over three years.
   这位学生从基础算术到高等微积分的进步展示了三年来显著的智力成长。

2. Scientists documented the **progression** of climate change through decades of temperature data and atmospheric measurements.
   科学家通过数十年的温度数据和大气测量记录了气候变化的演进过程。

3. The logical **progression** of the author's argument made the complex philosophical thesis accessible to a general audience.
   作者论证的逻辑递进使这篇复杂的哲学论文对普通读者来说也通俗易懂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "progress-ion" — it is the noun form of "progress," meaning the action of moving forward step by step.
把"progression"想成"progress的名词形式"——表示一步一步向前推进的过程。
