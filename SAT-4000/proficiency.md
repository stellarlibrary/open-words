# 🎯 proficiency

🔊 /prəˈfɪʃənsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A high degree of skill or competence; expert ability gained through training or practice
**中文：** n. 精通，熟练

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expertise（专长）
- competence（能力）
- mastery（精通）
- skill（技能）
- adeptness（熟练）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proficiency in / proficiency test / language proficiency / level of proficiency / demonstrate proficiency**

👉 Example: Applicants must demonstrate proficiency in at least two foreign languages before admission.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her remarkable **proficiency** in statistical analysis enabled the research team to interpret complex datasets with greater accuracy.
   她在统计分析方面出色的精通使研究团队能够更准确地解读复杂数据集。

2. The certification program requires candidates to demonstrate **proficiency** in both theoretical knowledge and practical laboratory techniques.
   该认证项目要求候选人展示其在理论知识和实验室实践技术方面的熟练程度。

3. Years of disciplined practice helped the young pianist achieve a level of **proficiency** that impressed even the most seasoned critics.
   多年的刻苦练习帮助这位年轻钢琴家达到了令最资深评论家都印象深刻的精通水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proficiency" as "pro-efficiency" — when you are proficient, you work with professional-level efficiency.
把"proficiency"想成"pro（专业的）+ ficiency（效率）"——当你精通时，你以专业级别的效率工作。
