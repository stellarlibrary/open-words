# 🤔 hesitant

🔊 /ˈhezɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Reluctant to act or speak due to uncertainty, doubt, or indecision; tentative
**中文：** adj. 迟疑的；踌躇的；吞吞吐吐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reluctant（不情愿的）
- uncertain（不确定的）
- tentative（犹豫的）
- indecisive（优柔寡断的）
- wavering（动摇的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hesitant to speak / hesitant about / hesitant steps / feel hesitant**

👉 Example: Many investors remain hesitant about entering the market during periods of economic uncertainty.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee members were **hesitant** to approve the proposal without first conducting a thorough review of the potential risks.
   委员会成员在未对潜在风险进行全面审查之前，对批准该提案犹豫不决。

2. She took **hesitant** steps toward the podium, visibly overwhelmed by the size of the audience gathered in the auditorium.
   她迈着犹豫的步伐走向讲台，显然被聚集在礼堂中的庞大观众所震撼。

3. Although he was **hesitant** at first, the young researcher eventually presented his controversial findings to the academic panel.
   尽管他起初犹豫不决，这位年轻的研究员最终还是向学术评审团展示了他有争议的研究成果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hesitant" as "he-sit-ant" — he just sits there like an ant, too nervous to move or make a decision.
把"hesitant"想成"he-sit-ant"——他像蚂蚁一样坐在那里，太紧张而不敢动或做决定。
