# ⏰ punctual

🔊 /ˈpʌŋktʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Happening or doing something at the agreed or proper time; prompt and on schedule
**中文：** adj. 严守时刻的，准时的；精确的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prompt（准时的）
- timely（及时的）
- on time（按时的）
- precise（精确的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **punctual arrival / punctual delivery / always punctual / punctual to the minute**

👉 Example: Employers value candidates who are consistently punctual and reliable in meeting deadlines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was remarkably **punctual**, beginning every lecture at precisely the scheduled time without a single exception.
   这位教授非常守时，每次讲座都准时在预定时间开始，没有一次例外。

2. The transit authority implemented new measures to ensure that buses remained **punctual** even during periods of heavy traffic.
   交通管理局实施了新措施，确保公交车即使在交通拥堵期间也保持准时。

3. Being **punctual** for diplomatic engagements is considered essential, as tardiness may be interpreted as a deliberate sign of disrespect.
   在外交活动中准时被视为至关重要，因为迟到可能被解读为故意表示不尊重。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "punct-ual" — from Latin "punctum" meaning point; a punctual person arrives at the exact point in time.
把"punctual"中的"punct"和拉丁语"punctum（点）"联系起来——一个准时的人总是在精确的时间点到达。
