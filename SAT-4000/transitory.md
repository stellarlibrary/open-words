# 🌅 transitory

🔊 /ˈtrænzɪtɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not permanent; lasting only for a short time; temporary and soon passing away.
**中文：** adj. 短暂的；暂时的；转瞬即逝的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- temporary（暂时的）
- fleeting（短暂的）
- ephemeral（短暂的）
- short-lived（短暂的）
- impermanent（非永久的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transitory nature / transitory phase / transitory effect / transitory period / transitory pleasure**

👉 Example: The economic recovery proved to be transitory, as growth stalled again within six months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The central bank described the inflationary pressures as **transitory**, predicting that prices would stabilize by the following quarter.
   中央银行将通胀压力描述为暂时的，预测价格将在下一季度稳定下来。

2. Most childhood fears are **transitory** in nature and tend to diminish as cognitive development progresses with age.
   大多数童年恐惧本质上是短暂的，随着年龄增长和认知发展往往会减少。

3. The artist captured the **transitory** beauty of a sunset, reminding viewers that such magnificent moments cannot be preserved.
   这位艺术家捕捉了日落的短暂之美，提醒观者如此壮丽的时刻无法被留存。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transitory" as "transit + ory" — like something at a transit station, it is only there temporarily before moving on.
把"transitory"想成"transit（中转）+ ory"——就像在中转站的事物，只是暂时停留然后就会离开。
