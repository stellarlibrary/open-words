# 💥 obliterate

🔊 /əˈblɪtəreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To destroy utterly; to wipe out completely so that no trace remains; to erase or remove from memory.
**中文：** v. 消灭，冲刷；忘却；涂抹，遮蔽；擦去

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destroy（摧毁）
- annihilate（歼灭）
- eradicate（根除）
- erase（擦除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **obliterate all traces / obliterate the memory / obliterate the evidence / completely obliterated**

👉 Example: The volcanic eruption obliterated the village, leaving nothing but ash and rubble behind.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The massive flood waters **obliterated** entire neighborhoods, reducing once-thriving communities to unrecognizable ruins.
   大规模的洪水摧毁了整个社区，将曾经繁荣的地方化为面目全非的废墟。

2. The historian argued that revisionist narratives could **obliterate** the authentic record of marginalized communities.
   历史学家认为修正主义叙事可能会抹去边缘化社区的真实记录。

3. Years of weathering had **obliterated** the inscription on the ancient monument, making it completely illegible to scholars.
   多年的风化侵蚀已经磨灭了古代纪念碑上的铭文，使学者们完全无法辨认。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ob-LITER-ate" — imagine pouring liters of water over writing until every letter is washed away and obliterated.
把"obliterate"想成"ob-LITER-ate"——想象倒了好几升（liter）水在字迹上，直到每个字母都被冲走、消灭殆尽。
