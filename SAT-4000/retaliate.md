# ⚔️ retaliate

🔊 /rɪˈtælieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To return like for like, especially to repay an injury or wrong with a similar action; to take revenge
**中文：** v. 报复；回敬；以牙还牙

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- avenge（报仇）
- reciprocate（回报）
- revenge（报复）
- counterattack（反击）
- strike back（回击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retaliate against / retaliate with force / retaliate by doing something / retaliate for an attack**

👉 Example: The country threatened to retaliate against any further economic sanctions imposed by its rivals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation chose to **retaliate** against the whistleblower by terminating her employment and revoking all security clearances.
   该公司选择通过解雇举报人并撤销其所有安全许可来进行报复。

2. Historical records indicate that the empire would swiftly **retaliate** whenever its border territories came under foreign assault.
   历史记录表明，每当边境领土遭到外国攻击时，帝国都会迅速进行报复。

3. Rather than **retaliate** with hostility, the diplomat pursued a measured and strategic approach to resolving the conflict.
   这位外交官没有以敌对方式回击，而是采取了一种审慎而有策略的方式来解决冲突。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-TALI-ate" — "tali" comes from Latin "talis" meaning "such" or "of the same kind" — to give back the same kind of treatment.
把"retaliate"想成"re-TALI-ate"——"tali"源自拉丁语"talis"，意为"同样的"——以同样的方式回敬。
