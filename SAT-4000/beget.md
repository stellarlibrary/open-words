# 🌱 beget

🔊 /bɪˈɡɛt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To produce or give rise to; to cause; to father (a child)
**中文：** v. 产生；招致；引起；当…的父亲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engender（产生）
- generate（引起）
- produce（产生）
- sire（做父亲）
- spawn（引发）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **beget conflict / beget change / beget violence / beget controversy**

👉 Example: Ignorance often begets fear, which in turn begets hostility toward the unfamiliar.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historical evidence suggests that prolonged economic inequality can **beget** social unrest and widespread political upheaval.
   历史证据表明，长期的经济不平等会引发社会动荡和广泛的政治动乱。

2. The philosopher argued that unchecked power inevitably **begets** corruption in even the most virtuous leaders.
   这位哲学家认为，不受约束的权力即使在最有德行的领导者中也必然会滋生腐败。

3. Scientific breakthroughs frequently **beget** new fields of inquiry that were previously unimaginable to researchers.
   科学突破常常催生出研究人员此前难以想象的全新研究领域。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-get" as "to be the one who gets something started" — to bring something into existence.
把"beget"想成"be-get"——成为让某事物开始存在的人，即"产生、引起"。
