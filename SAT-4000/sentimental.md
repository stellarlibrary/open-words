# 💭 sentimental

🔊 /ˌsentɪˈmentl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or prompted by feelings of tenderness, sadness, or nostalgia; excessively prone to feelings of emotion
**中文：** adj. 情感上的；多愁善感的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nostalgic（怀旧的）
- emotional（情感的）
- maudlin（过度伤感的）
- romantic（浪漫多情的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sentimental value / sentimental journey / sentimental attachment / sentimental reasons**

👉 Example: The antique watch held great sentimental value because it had belonged to her late grandmother.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics dismissed the film as overly **sentimental**, arguing that its emotional scenes lacked subtlety and genuine artistic depth.
   评论家认为这部电影过于感伤，指出其情感场景缺乏细腻和真正的艺术深度。

2. She kept the faded photograph not for its aesthetic quality but for the **sentimental** attachment it represented to her youth.
   她保留那张褪色的照片不是因为其美学价值，而是因为它代表着对青春的情感寄托。

3. The historian cautioned against a **sentimental** view of the past that ignores the genuine hardships people endured in earlier eras.
   这位历史学家告诫人们不要对过去持多愁善感的看法，而忽视人们在早期时代所经历的真实苦难。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sentimental" as "sentiment + al" — full of sentiment, meaning overflowing with tender emotions and nostalgic feelings.
把"sentimental"想成"sentiment + al"——充满情感，意味着洋溢着柔情和怀旧的感受。
