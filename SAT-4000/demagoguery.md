# 📢 demagoguery

🔊 /ˈdɛməɡɒɡəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The practice of spreading rumors or making emotional appeals to exploit prejudices and manipulate the masses
**中文：** n. 散布谣言；煽动行为

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agitation（煽动）
- rabble-rousing（蛊惑人心）
- incitement（煽动）
- propaganda（宣传鼓动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **engage in demagoguery / resort to demagoguery / political demagoguery / pure demagoguery**

👉 Example: The editorial accused the candidate of resorting to demagoguery rather than presenting substantive policy proposals.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians argue that the rise of authoritarian regimes is frequently preceded by periods of unchecked **demagoguery** and public fear.
   历史学家认为，威权政权的崛起之前往往伴随着不受约束的煽动行为和公众恐慌。

2. The senator's speech was criticized as blatant **demagoguery** designed to inflame public anger rather than promote rational discourse.
   这位参议员的演讲被批评为公然的煽动行为，旨在激起公众愤怒而非促进理性讨论。

3. Responsible journalism serves as a crucial safeguard against **demagoguery** by ensuring citizens receive accurate and balanced information.
   负责任的新闻报道通过确保公民获得准确和均衡的信息，成为抵御煽动行为的重要保障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "demo-goguery" as a "demo" of "goading" — a demagogue gives a demonstration of goading the crowd into frenzy.
把"demagoguery"想成"demo"（演示）加"goading"（煽动）——煽动家向人群演示如何煽风点火。
