# ⏳ temporal

🔊 /ˈtɛmpərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to time; secular rather than spiritual; temporary. (n.) Worldly possessions or affairs
**中文：** adj. 暂时的；当时的；现世的 n. 世间万物；暂存的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- secular（世俗的）
- worldly（世间的）
- transient（短暂的）
- chronological（时间的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **temporal power / temporal order / temporal lobe / temporal dimension / temporal boundary**

👉 Example: The philosopher distinguished between temporal pleasures and eternal spiritual fulfillment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian examined the **temporal** context of the Renaissance to understand the social forces that shaped artistic innovation.
   历史学家研究了文艺复兴的时代背景，以理解塑造艺术创新的社会力量。

2. Neurologists discovered that damage to the **temporal** lobe can significantly impair a patient's ability to process language.
   神经学家发现，颞叶损伤会严重影响患者处理语言的能力。

3. Medieval kings often struggled to balance **temporal** authority with the spiritual influence wielded by the Catholic Church.
   中世纪国王常常难以在世俗权力与天主教会行使的精神影响力之间取得平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tempo-ral" — related to "tempo" (time in music), so temporal means related to time or the temporary world.
把"temporal"想成与"tempo（音乐中的节拍）"相关——temporal意味着与时间或暂时的世界有关。
