# 😣 torturous

🔊 /ˈtɔːrtʃərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Involving or causing great pain, suffering, or anguish; extremely unpleasant or agonizing
**中文：** 折磨人的；痛苦的；令人难以忍受的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agonizing（痛苦的）
- excruciating（极度痛苦的）
- harrowing（折磨人的）
- grueling（令人筋疲力尽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **torturous process / torturous experience / torturous journey / torturous ordeal**

👉 Example: The torturous negotiations lasted for weeks before both parties finally reached a compromise.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students endured a **torturous** three-hour examination that tested every aspect of their analytical abilities.
   学生们忍受了一场长达三小时的折磨人的考试，考查了他们分析能力的各个方面。

2. Recovery from the surgery proved to be a **torturous** process requiring months of intensive physical therapy.
   手术后的恢复过程极其痛苦，需要数月的高强度物理治疗。

3. The refugees described their **torturous** journey across the desert as the most harrowing experience of their lives.
   难民们形容他们穿越沙漠的痛苦旅程是一生中最令人煎熬的经历。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "torturous" as "torture + ous" — something full of torture is torturous, causing great suffering.
把"torturous"想成"torture（折磨）+ ous（充满的）"——充满折磨的就是torturous，令人极度痛苦。
