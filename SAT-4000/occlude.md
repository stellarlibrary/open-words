# 🔒 occlude

🔊 /əˈkluːd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To stop, close up, or obstruct an opening or passage; to shut in or out; to block from view
**中文：** v. 使闭塞；封闭；挡住

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- block（阻塞）
- seal（封闭）
- obstruct（阻挡）
- close off（关闭）
- plug（堵塞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **occlude a vessel / occlude an artery / occlude light / occluded front (meteorology)**

👉 Example: The buildup of plaque can gradually occlude the coronary arteries, leading to serious cardiac events.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Cholesterol deposits may gradually **occlude** the blood vessels, significantly increasing the patient's risk of heart attack or stroke.
   胆固醇沉积可能会逐渐阻塞血管，显著增加患者心脏病发作或中风的风险。

2. Dense fog began to **occlude** the mountain pass, forcing authorities to close the highway to all vehicular traffic.
   浓雾开始封锁山口，迫使当局关闭了该公路的所有车辆通行。

3. The surgeon worked carefully to remove the clot that had **occluded** the critical artery supplying blood to the brain.
   外科医生小心翼翼地移除了阻塞向大脑供血的关键动脉的血栓。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "oc-CLUDE" as containing "CLUDE" like "close" (from Latin claudere) — to occlude is to close off or shut something up completely.
把"occlude"想成包含"clude"像"close（关闭）"（来自拉丁语claudere）——occlude就是完全封闭或堵住某物。
