# 🏆 garner

🔊 /ˈɡɑːrnər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To gather or collect something, especially information, support, or approval; to store in a granary. (n.) A granary; a storehouse for grain
**中文：** v. 获得；取得；收藏；储藏 n. 谷仓；粮仓

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acquire（获得）
- accumulate（积累）
- amass（收集）
- gather（聚集）
- reap（收获）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **garner support / garner attention / garner praise / garner votes / garner recognition**

👉 Example: The young filmmaker garnered critical acclaim for her debut documentary at the international festival.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nonprofit organization **garnered** widespread support from corporate sponsors eager to demonstrate their commitment to social responsibility.
   该非营利组织获得了企业赞助商的广泛支持，他们渴望展示自己对社会责任的承诺。

2. Her meticulously researched thesis **garnered** praise from the academic committee and was subsequently published in a prestigious journal.
   她精心研究的论文获得了学术委员会的赞誉，随后发表在一份著名期刊上。

3. The opposition candidate managed to **garner** enough votes in rural districts to force an unexpected runoff election.
   反对派候选人设法在农村地区获得了足够的选票，迫使举行了一场出人意料的决选。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "garner" as related to "granary" (grain storehouse) — just as a granary gathers grain, to garner means to gather and collect things of value.
把"garner"和"granary"（粮仓）联系起来——就像粮仓收集谷物一样，garner意味着收集和获取有价值的东西。
