# 🎭 chicanery

🔊 /ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The use of trickery, deception, or subterfuge to achieve a purpose; crafty or underhanded dealing
**中文：** n. 狡辩；欺骗；强词夺理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trickery（诡计）
- deception（欺骗）
- subterfuge（诡计）
- duplicity（口是心非）
- fraud（欺诈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **legal chicanery / political chicanery / resort to chicanery / full of chicanery**

👉 Example: The opposition accused the ruling party of chicanery during the electoral process.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed extensive financial **chicanery** that had defrauded investors of millions of dollars.
   调查揭露了大规模的金融欺骗行为，投资者被骗取了数百万美元。

2. Through legal **chicanery** and procedural manipulation, the corporation managed to avoid paying substantial environmental fines.
   通过法律上的狡辩和程序操纵，该公司成功避免了支付大笔环境罚款。

3. The senator denounced the **chicanery** employed by lobbyists to influence the outcome of critical legislative votes.
   这位参议员谴责了游说者为影响关键立法投票结果而使用的欺骗手段。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chican-ery" as "chicken-ery" — a sneaky chicken running around, symbolizing someone using sly tricks to get what they want.
把"chicanery"想成"chicken（鸡）+ ery"——一只狡猾的鸡到处乱跑，象征着某人用诡计来达到目的。
