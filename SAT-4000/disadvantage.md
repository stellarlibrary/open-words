# ⚠️ disadvantage

🔊 /ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An unfavorable circumstance or condition that reduces the chances of success; a drawback or shortcoming
**中文：** n. 缺点；不利条件；不便

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- drawback（缺点）
- shortcoming（不足）
- handicap（障碍）
- liability（不利因素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **at a disadvantage / put someone at a disadvantage / major disadvantage / disadvantage of**

👉 Example: The lack of reliable internet access put rural students at a significant disadvantage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The primary **disadvantage** of the proposed system is its excessive cost, which exceeds the allocated budget by thirty percent.
   该拟议系统的主要缺点是成本过高，超出分配预算百分之三十。

2. Students from underfunded schools often face a considerable **disadvantage** when competing for admission to elite universities.
   来自资金不足学校的学生在竞争名校录取时往往面临相当大的不利条件。

3. The report concluded that geographic isolation remains a persistent **disadvantage** for communities seeking access to specialized medical care.
   报告得出结论，地理隔离仍然是社区获得专业医疗服务的持续不利因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-advantage" — the prefix "dis-" negates "advantage," so it literally means the opposite of an advantage.
把"disadvantage"想成"否定(dis)+优势(advantage)"——前缀"dis-"否定了"advantage"，字面意思就是优势的反面。
