# 🌊 effusion

🔊 /ɪˈfjuːʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The act of pouring forth; an unrestrained outpouring of feeling; the escape of fluid into a body cavity
**中文：** n. 渗出；泻出；溢出；（感情的）流露

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outpouring（涌出）
- discharge（排出）
- emission（排放）
- overflow（溢出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pleural effusion / effusion of gratitude / emotional effusion / pericardial effusion**

👉 Example: The chest X-ray revealed a significant pleural effusion requiring immediate drainage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician diagnosed a pleural **effusion** after reviewing the patient's imaging results and respiratory symptoms.
   医生在查看了患者的影像结果和呼吸系统症状后诊断出胸腔积液。

2. His lengthy **effusion** of gratitude during the acceptance speech moved the entire audience to sustained applause.
   他在获奖感言中大量倾诉感激之情，令全场观众报以持久的掌声。

3. The volcanic **effusion** deposited layers of basaltic material across the surrounding terrain over several consecutive weeks.
   火山的溢流在连续数周内在周围地形上沉积了多层玄武岩物质。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-FUSION" — a fusion of feelings or fluids that spills outward, an effusion is something pouring out freely.
把"effusion"想成"ef-FUSION"——感情或液体融合后向外溢出，effusion就是自由涌出的东西。
