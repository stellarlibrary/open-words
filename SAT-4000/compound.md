# 🔬 compound

🔊 /ˈkɒmpaʊnd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance formed from two or more elements chemically united; an enclosed area. (adj.) Made up of multiple parts; composite. (v.) To combine; to make worse or more intense
**中文：** n. 化合物；混合物；大院 adj. 复合的；混合的 v. 合成；混合；加重

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mixture（混合物）
- composite（复合物）
- blend（混合）
- amalgam（混合体）
- combine（结合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **chemical compound / compound interest / compound a problem / compound fracture**

👉 Example: The error was compounded by a series of poor decisions that ultimately led to the project's failure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists discovered a new chemical **compound** that demonstrates significant potential for treating antibiotic-resistant bacterial infections in clinical trials.
   科学家发现了一种新的化合物，在临床试验中显示出治疗耐抗生素细菌感染的巨大潜力。

2. The economic downturn was **compounded** by rising inflation and widespread unemployment, creating severe hardship for vulnerable populations throughout the nation.
   经济衰退因通货膨胀上升和大范围失业而加剧，给全国弱势群体造成了严重困难。

3. The research facility is located within a secure **compound** surrounded by multiple layers of fencing and advanced surveillance technology.
   该研究设施位于一个安全的大院内，周围有多层围栏和先进的监控技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "compound" as "com-POUND" — you "pound together" different things to create something new, like mixing elements into a compound.
把"compound"想成"捣在一起"——你把不同的东西"捣"在一起创造出新东西，就像把元素混合成化合物。
