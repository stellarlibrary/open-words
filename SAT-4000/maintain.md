# 🔧 maintain

🔊 /meɪnˈteɪn/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To keep in an existing state or condition; to continue or preserve; to provide upkeep for; to assert or declare as true.
**中文：** vt. 维修；保养；维持；保持；坚持；主张；供养

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- preserve（保持）
- sustain（维持）
- uphold（坚持）
- assert（主张）
- retain（保留）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain order / maintain a position / maintain standards / maintain that (+ clause) / maintain equipment**

👉 Example: The organization works tirelessly to maintain high standards of quality across all its operations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city allocated additional funds to **maintain** its aging infrastructure, including bridges, roads, and public buildings.
   该市拨出额外资金来维护其老化的基础设施，包括桥梁、道路和公共建筑。

2. The defendant continued to **maintain** his innocence throughout the lengthy trial despite overwhelming circumstantial evidence.
   尽管有大量间接证据，被告在漫长的审判过程中始终坚持自己是无辜的。

3. Researchers must **maintain** strict laboratory protocols to ensure the reliability and reproducibility of experimental results.
   研究人员必须保持严格的实验室规程以确保实验结果的可靠性和可重复性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "main" (hand, from Latin manus) + "tain" (hold, from Latin tenere) — to maintain is to "hold in hand," keeping something steady and in good condition.
把"maintain"想成"main"（手，源自拉丁语manus）+"tain"（握住，源自拉丁语tenere）——maintain就是"握在手中"，保持事物稳定和良好状态。
