# 💪 formidable

🔊 /ˈfɔːrmɪdəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Inspiring fear or respect through being impressively powerful, large, or capable; difficult to deal with or overcome
**中文：** adj. 强大的；可怕的；令人敬畏的；难以克服的；难对付的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- daunting（令人畏惧的）
- imposing（令人印象深刻的）
- intimidating（令人生畏的）
- redoubtable（令人敬畏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a formidable opponent / formidable challenge / formidable reputation / formidable obstacle**

👉 Example: The defending champion proved to be a formidable opponent in the final round of the tournament.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team faced **formidable** technical challenges in developing a vaccine within such an unprecedented and compressed timeline.
   研究团队在如此前所未有的紧迫时间内开发疫苗时面临着巨大的技术挑战。

2. Her **formidable** intellect and commanding presence in the courtroom earned her widespread respect among both colleagues and adversaries.
   她令人敬畏的才智和在法庭上的威严风范为她赢得了同事和对手的广泛尊重。

3. The mountain range presented a **formidable** barrier to the ancient traders who attempted to establish routes between the two civilizations.
   这座山脉对试图在两个文明之间建立路线的古代商人构成了难以克服的障碍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "formid-able" — it contains "fear" hidden in the Latin root "formido" (meaning fear). Something formidable is so powerful it is able to cause fear.
把"formidable"想成来自拉丁语"formido（恐惧）"——formidable的事物强大到能够让人产生恐惧。
