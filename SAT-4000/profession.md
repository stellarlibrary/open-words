# 👔 profession

🔊 /prəˈfeʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A paid occupation requiring prolonged training and formal qualification; a declaration or open statement of belief or feeling
**中文：** n. 职业，专业；声明，宣布，表白；公开表示

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vocation（职业）
- career（事业）
- occupation（职业）
- declaration（声明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **by profession / the medical profession / profession of faith / enter a profession / legal profession**

👉 Example: She is a surgeon by profession and has practiced medicine for over two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The teaching **profession** demands continuous development of skills to address the evolving needs of diverse student populations.
   教师职业要求不断发展技能，以应对多样化学生群体不断变化的需求。

2. His **profession** of loyalty to the organization was met with skepticism by colleagues who questioned his true motives.
   他对组织忠诚的表白遭到了同事们的怀疑，他们质疑他的真实动机。

3. Many young graduates aspire to enter the legal **profession** despite the rigorous examinations and years of training required.
   许多年轻毕业生渴望进入法律行业，尽管这需要严格的考试和多年的培训。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-fess-ion" as "publicly confessing" what you do — a profession is what you profess to the world as your expertise.
把"profession"想成"pro（公开）+ fess（声称）+ ion"——职业就是你向世界公开宣称的专长。
