# 🌾 fallow

🔊 /ˈfæloʊ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) (Of farmland) plowed but left unsown for a period to restore fertility; inactive or dormant. (n.) Land left unseeded after plowing
**中文：** adj. 休耕的；不活跃的 n. 休耕地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dormant（休眠的）
- idle（闲置的）
- uncultivated（未耕种的）
- inactive（不活跃的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lie fallow / fallow field / fallow period / fallow land**

👉 Example: The farmer decided to let the eastern field lie fallow for an entire growing season.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After decades of intensive agriculture, the government mandated that certain fields must lie **fallow** to replenish depleted soil nutrients.
   经过数十年的集约农业后，政府要求某些田地必须休耕以补充耗尽的土壤养分。

2. The artist experienced a **fallow** period lasting several years before producing her most celebrated and influential masterpiece.
   这位艺术家经历了长达数年的沉寂期，之后才创作出她最著名且最有影响力的杰作。

3. Economists argue that **fallow** intervals in industrial production can ultimately lead to greater innovation and efficiency.
   经济学家认为，工业生产中的休整期最终可以带来更大的创新和效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fallow" sounding like "follow" — the land follows a rest cycle, lying idle before the next planting season.
把"fallow"联想成"follow（跟随）"——土地跟随一个休息周期，在下一个种植季节之前闲置休耕。
