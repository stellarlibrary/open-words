# 🌍 ozone

🔊 /ˈoʊzoʊn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A form of oxygen (O₃) with three atoms per molecule, found in the upper atmosphere where it absorbs ultraviolet radiation; also refers to fresh, invigorating air.
**中文：** n. [化学] 臭氧；新鲜的空气

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trioxygen（三氧）
- fresh air（新鲜空气）
- O₃（臭氧分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ozone layer / ozone depletion / ozone hole / ozone levels / ozone pollution**

👉 Example: The depletion of the ozone layer has been a major environmental concern since the late twentieth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists have warned that the continued depletion of the **ozone** layer could lead to a dramatic increase in skin cancer rates worldwide.
   科学家警告说，**臭氧**层的持续消耗可能导致全球皮肤癌发病率急剧上升。

2. International agreements such as the Montreal Protocol have been instrumental in reducing emissions of chemicals that destroy atmospheric **ozone**.
   《蒙特利尔议定书》等国际协议在减少破坏大气**臭氧**的化学物质排放方面发挥了重要作用。

3. Ground-level **ozone** is a harmful pollutant formed when industrial emissions react with sunlight in densely populated urban areas.
   地面**臭氧**是一种有害污染物，由工业排放物在人口密集的城市地区与阳光发生反应而形成。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ozone" as "O-zone" — a zone made of O (oxygen) that protects us like a shield high above the Earth.
把"ozone"想成"O-zone"——一个由O（氧）构成的区域，像盾牌一样在高空保护我们。
