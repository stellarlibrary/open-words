# 🩸 anemia

🔊 /əˈniːmiə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A condition in which the blood lacks sufficient healthy red blood cells or hemoglobin, resulting in pallor and fatigue
**中文：** 贫血；贫血症

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bloodlessness（缺血）
- pallor（苍白）
- weakness（虚弱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **iron-deficiency anemia / suffer from anemia / severe anemia / diagnose anemia / treat anemia**

👉 Example: The physician diagnosed the patient with severe anemia caused by chronic iron deficiency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Chronic **anemia** left the young student perpetually exhausted, making it difficult to concentrate during demanding academic lectures.
   慢性贫血使这位年轻学生长期疲惫不堪，难以在紧张的学术讲座中集中注意力。

2. The humanitarian organization reported that widespread **anemia** among children in the region was directly linked to malnutrition.
   人道主义组织报告称，该地区儿童中普遍存在的贫血与营养不良直接相关。

3. Researchers developed a new screening method to detect **anemia** earlier, potentially improving treatment outcomes for at-risk populations.
   研究人员开发了一种新的筛查方法，可以更早地检测贫血，从而有望改善高危人群的治疗效果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-nemia" as "a" (without) + "emia" (blood) — literally without enough blood, leaving you pale and tired.
把"anemia"拆成"a"（没有）+"emia"（血液）——字面意思就是血液不足，让你面色苍白、疲惫不堪。
