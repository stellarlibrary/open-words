# 🧠 apprehensible

🔊 /ˌæprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** Capable of being understood, grasped, or comprehended by the mind.

**中文：** 可理解的；可了解的；能领悟的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- comprehensible（可理解的）
- intelligible（明了的）
- understandable（可懂的）
- graspable（可领会的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocations:** apprehensible concept, apprehensible form, readily apprehensible, easily apprehensible

**Example:** Abstract theories become apprehensible when illustrated with concrete examples.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor made the **apprehensible** argument that economic growth depends on institutional stability.
   （教授提出了一个可理解的论点，即经济增长取决于制度稳定。）

2. Scientific findings must be presented in an **apprehensible** manner for the general public to benefit.
   （科学发现必须以可理解的方式呈现，以便公众从中受益。）

3. The composer transformed complex emotions into **apprehensible** melodies that resonated with every listener.
   （作曲家将复杂的情感转化为可理解的旋律，引起每位听众的共鸣。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "apprehend" (to grasp mentally) + "-ible" (able to) = something that can be mentally grasped.

把 "apprehend"（领悟）加上 "-ible"（能够的）= 能够被领悟的、可理解的。
