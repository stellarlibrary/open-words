# 💭 conceivable

🔊 /kənˈsiːvəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Capable of being imagined or believed; possible to think of or understand
**中文：** adj. 可能的；想得到的；可想象的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- imaginable（可想象的）
- possible（可能的）
- plausible（合理的）
- thinkable（可想到的）
- feasible（可行的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **every conceivable / it is conceivable that / by any conceivable means / conceivable outcome**

👉 Example: The committee explored every conceivable option before reaching a final decision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team examined every **conceivable** explanation for the anomalous data before publishing their findings.
   研究团队在发表研究结果之前，审查了对异常数据的每一种可能的解释。

2. It is entirely **conceivable** that advances in artificial intelligence will reshape the global labor market within a decade.
   完全可以想象，人工智能的进步将在十年内重塑全球劳动力市场。

3. The defense attorney argued that no **conceivable** motive existed for the accused to commit such a crime.
   辩护律师辩称，被告没有任何可以想象的动机去犯下这样的罪行。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "conceive + able" — if you can conceive (imagine) it in your mind, then it is conceivable.
把"conceivable"拆成"conceive（想象）+ able（能够）"——如果你能在脑海中想象到，那它就是"可想象的"。
