# 🪨 stubborn

🔊 /ˈstʌbərn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unyielding and determined; difficult to manage, treat, or overcome; obstinately refusing to change
**中文：** adj. 顽固的，倔强的；难对付的，难以克服的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obstinate（固执的）
- tenacious（顽强的）
- unyielding（不屈的）
- headstrong（任性的）
- intractable（棘手的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stubborn resistance / stubborn refusal / stubborn stain / stubborn determination**

👉 Example: Her stubborn refusal to compromise ultimately derailed the entire negotiation process.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **stubborn** insistence on certain preconditions prevented the two nations from reaching a diplomatic resolution.
   大使对某些先决条件的顽固坚持阻碍了两国达成外交解决方案。

2. Despite months of intensive treatment, the **stubborn** infection persisted and required a more aggressive medical intervention.
   尽管经过数月的集中治疗，这种顽固的感染仍然持续存在，需要更积极的医疗干预。

3. The **stubborn** determination of the civil rights leaders ultimately brought about significant legislative changes throughout the nation.
   民权领袖们顽强的决心最终在全国范围内带来了重大的立法变革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stub-born" as "born as a stub" — a tree stump that refuses to be removed, firmly rooted and immovable.
把"stubborn"想成"born as a stub（生来就是树桩）"——一个拒绝被移除的树桩，牢牢扎根，不可动摇。
