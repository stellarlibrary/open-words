# ✂️ halve

🔊 /hæv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To divide into two equal parts; to reduce by half
**中文：** v. 把……平分为二；将……减半

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bisect（二等分）
- divide（分割）
- split（劈开）
- reduce（减少）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **halve the cost / halve the time / halve the distance / halve the portion**

👉 Example: The new manufacturing process will halve production costs while maintaining superior quality standards.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government announced a bold initiative to **halve** carbon emissions within the next decade through renewable energy investment.
   政府宣布了一项大胆计划，通过可再生能源投资在未来十年内将碳排放减半。

2. Advances in automation have enabled the company to **halve** its processing time without sacrificing accuracy or reliability.
   自动化的进步使该公司能够将处理时间减半，同时不牺牲准确性或可靠性。

3. The research team sought to **halve** the dosage required for treatment by developing a more potent pharmaceutical compound.
   研究团队试图通过开发更强效的药物化合物将治疗所需剂量减半。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "halve" as simply "half" turned into a verb — when you halve something, you cut it in half.
把"halve"想成"half"变成了动词——当你halve某物时，就是把它切成一半。
