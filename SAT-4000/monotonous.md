# 😴 monotonous

🔊 /məˈnɒtənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Dull, tedious, and repetitious; lacking in variety and interest; unchanging in tone or pitch
**中文：** adj. 单调的；无抑扬顿挫的；无变化的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tedious（乏味的）
- repetitive（重复的）
- dreary（沉闷的）
- humdrum（单调的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **monotonous routine / monotonous work / monotonous voice / monotonous task / monotonous landscape**

👉 Example: The factory workers endured monotonous labor on the assembly line for twelve hours every day.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **monotonous** rhythm of the rain against the windowpane gradually lulled every occupant of the room into a deep sleep.
   雨水拍打窗玻璃的单调节奏逐渐将房间里的每个人催入沉睡。

2. Many employees reported that the **monotonous** nature of their daily responsibilities led to decreased motivation and lower overall productivity.
   许多员工反映，日常工作的单调性质导致了积极性下降和整体生产力降低。

3. The vast, **monotonous** desert stretched endlessly in every direction, offering no visual relief to the exhausted travelers below.
   广阔而单调的沙漠向四面八方无尽延伸，给疲惫不堪的旅行者带来毫无视觉缓解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mono" (one) + "tonous" (tone) — monotonous means having only "one tone," so boring and repetitive that nothing ever changes.
把"monotonous"拆成"mono"（一个）+"tonous"（音调的）——单调的就是只有"一个音调"，无聊而重复，毫无变化。
