# 💎 bedeck

🔊 /bɪˈdɛk/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To adorn or decorate lavishly; to dress up or embellish
**中文：** v. 装饰；修饰；打扮

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adorn（装饰）
- embellish（修饰）
- ornament（装点）
- festoon（以花彩装饰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bedeck with flowers / bedecked in jewels / bedecked with ribbons / lavishly bedecked**

👉 Example: The grand ballroom was bedecked with crystal chandeliers and cascading floral arrangements.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cathedral was magnificently **bedecked** with garlands and candles for the elaborate Christmas Eve celebration.
   大教堂为盛大的平安夜庆典装饰了华丽的花环和蜡烛。

2. The bride was elegantly **bedecked** in a gown adorned with hand-sewn pearls and delicate lace embroidery.
   新娘优雅地穿着一件缀满手工缝制珍珠和精致蕾丝刺绣的礼服。

3. Every storefront along the avenue was **bedecked** with patriotic banners and bunting for the national independence day parade.
   大街上每家店铺的门面都为国庆日游行装饰了爱国旗帜和彩旗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be + deck" — to bedeck is to deck something out, covering it with beautiful decorations like decking the halls.
把"bedeck"想成"be + deck（装饰）"——就像"deck the halls（装点大厅）"一样，用华丽的装饰物覆盖某物。
