# 🔧 ductile

🔊 /ˈdʌktaɪl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being drawn into thin wire or easily shaped; flexible and pliable
**中文：** adj. 柔软的；易延展的；可塑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- malleable（可锻的）
- pliable（柔韧的）
- flexible（灵活的）
- pliant（易弯曲的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ductile metal / ductile material / highly ductile / ductile enough to bend**

👉 Example: Gold is one of the most ductile metals, capable of being drawn into extremely thin wire.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers selected copper for the wiring because it is highly **ductile** and can be stretched into fine strands without fracturing.
   工程师们选择铜作为线材，因为它具有高度延展性，可以被拉伸成细丝而不断裂。

2. The metallurgist explained that **ductile** materials deform plastically under tensile stress before ultimately reaching their breaking point.
   冶金学家解释说，延展性材料在拉伸应力下会发生塑性变形，最终才达到断裂点。

3. Unlike brittle ceramics, **ductile** alloys can absorb significant mechanical stress, making them ideal for structural engineering applications.
   与脆性陶瓷不同，延展性合金可以吸收显著的机械应力，使其成为结构工程应用的理想材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ductile" as related to "duct" — like a duct that channels water, a ductile material can be drawn out and channeled into new shapes.
把"ductile"想成与"duct"（管道）有关——就像引导水流的管道一样，延展性材料可以被拉伸和塑造成新形状。
