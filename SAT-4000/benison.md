# 🙏 benison

🔊 /ˈbɛnɪsən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A blessing; an expression of good wishes or divine favor, especially in a formal or liturgical context
**中文：** n. 祝福；祝祷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blessing（祝福）
- benediction（祝祷）
- grace（恩典）
- invocation（祈祷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bestow a benison / pronounce a benison / receive a benison / a benison upon**

👉 Example: The priest bestowed a benison upon the congregation before they departed the chapel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The abbot's **benison** echoed through the ancient stone monastery as the monks bowed their heads in silent reverence.
   修道院院长的祝福在古老的石砌修道院中回荡，修士们低头默默致敬。

2. In medieval literature, a traveler often sought a holy **benison** before embarking on a perilous and uncertain journey.
   在中世纪文学中，旅行者在踏上危险而未知的旅途前常常寻求神圣的祝福。

3. The poet described the gentle rainfall as nature's own **benison**, nourishing the parched earth after months of drought.
   诗人将温柔的降雨描述为大自然自身的祝福，滋润了干旱数月的大地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "benison" as "bene (good) + son" — like a good blessing bestowed upon a son, a benison is a heartfelt blessing.
把"benison"想成"bene（好的）+ son（儿子）"——像给儿子的美好祝福，benison就是真诚的祝祷。
