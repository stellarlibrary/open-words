# 🎯 pertinent

🔊 /ˈpɜːrtɪnənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relevant or applicable to a particular matter; directly related and appropriate to the subject at hand
**中文：** adj. 相关的，相干的；中肯的；切题的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relevant（相关的）
- applicable（适用的）
- germane（密切相关的）
- apropos（恰当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pertinent information / pertinent question / pertinent to / pertinent details / highly pertinent**

👉 Example: The witness provided testimony that was directly pertinent to the central issue of the case.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The auditor requested access to all documents **pertinent** to the company's financial transactions during the previous fiscal year.
   审计员要求查阅所有与该公司上一财年财务交易相关的文件。

2. The professor encouraged students to ask **pertinent** questions that would advance the class discussion beyond surface-level observations.
   教授鼓励学生提出中肯的问题，以便将课堂讨论推进到超越表面观察的深度。

3. Only the most **pertinent** evidence was admitted by the judge, who strictly excluded all irrelevant and prejudicial material.
   法官只采纳了最相关的证据，严格排除了所有不相关和有偏见的材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pertinent" as "per (relating to) + tinent (holding)" — information that holds directly to the topic at hand.
把"pertinent"想成"per（关于）+ tinent（持有）"——与当前话题直接相关的信息。
