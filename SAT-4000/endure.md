# 🛡️ endure

🔊 /ɪnˈdjʊər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To suffer through something painful or difficult patiently; to tolerate or bear without yielding
**中文：** v. 忍耐；容忍；忍受；持久

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tolerate（容忍）
- withstand（承受）
- bear（忍受）
- persevere（坚持）
- suffer（忍受）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endure hardship / endure pain / endure criticism / endure the test of time**

👉 Example: Great works of literature endure the test of time because they speak to universal human experiences.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugees were forced to **endure** months of severe deprivation before finally reaching safety across the border.
   难民们被迫忍受了数月的严重匮乏，才最终抵达边境的安全地带。

2. Classical philosophical texts continue to **endure** as foundational works because their insights remain profoundly relevant to modern society.
   古典哲学文本作为基础著作继续流传，因为它们的见解对现代社会仍然具有深远的意义。

3. The athlete demonstrated an extraordinary ability to **endure** physical pain while competing at the highest level of international sport.
   这位运动员在参加最高水平的国际体育比赛时展现出了非凡的忍受身体痛苦的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-dure" as being "in duration" — when you endure something, you last through the full duration of the hardship without giving up.
把"endure"想成"in duration（在持续期间）"——当你endure某事时，你坚持度过了整个艰难时期而没有放弃。
