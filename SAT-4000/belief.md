# 💡 belief

🔊 /bɪˈliːf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An acceptance that something is true or exists; trust, faith, or confidence in something; a religious conviction or tenet
**中文：** n. 相信，信赖；信仰；教义；信念

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conviction（信念）
- faith（信仰）
- confidence（信心）
- creed（信条）
- trust（信赖）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **belief in / strong belief / beyond belief / belief system / deeply held belief**

👉 Example: Her belief in the importance of education motivated her to fund scholarships for underprivileged students.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist maintained an unwavering **belief** in the validity of her hypothesis despite widespread skepticism from peers.
   这位科学家在同行普遍质疑的情况下，仍然对其假设的有效性保持坚定的信念。

2. Freedom of **belief** is enshrined as a fundamental right in the constitutions of most modern democratic nations.
   信仰自由作为一项基本权利被写入大多数现代民主国家的宪法中。

3. The evidence presented at trial strained **belief**, as witnesses offered contradictory accounts of the same pivotal event.
   审判中提出的证据令人难以置信，因为证人们对同一关键事件给出了相互矛盾的陈述。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "be-lief" — related to "believe" minus the "ve," it is the noun form representing what you hold to be true.
把"belief"想成"believe"去掉"ve"——它是"相信"的名词形式，代表你认为真实的东西，即"信念、信仰"。
