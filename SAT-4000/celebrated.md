# 🌟 celebrated

🔊 /ˈselɪbreɪtɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Widely known and praised; famous, especially for notable achievements or qualities
**中文：** adj. 著名的，闻名的，有名望的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- renowned（著名的）
- distinguished（杰出的）
- illustrious（卓越的）
- acclaimed（受赞誉的）
- eminent（显赫的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a celebrated author / celebrated artist / celebrated case / celebrated for something**

👉 Example: The celebrated physicist was invited to deliver the keynote address at the international symposium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **celebrated** novelist received the prestigious literary award in recognition of her extraordinary contributions to contemporary fiction.
   这位著名小说家因其对当代小说的杰出贡献而获得了享有盛誉的文学奖。

2. This **celebrated** university has produced numerous Nobel laureates and world leaders throughout its distinguished three-century history.
   这所著名大学在其杰出的三百年历史中培养了众多诺贝尔奖得主和世界领袖。

3. The museum's exhibition featured works by the most **celebrated** painters of the Renaissance, attracting visitors from across the globe.
   博物馆的展览展出了文艺复兴时期最著名画家的作品，吸引了来自世界各地的参观者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "celebrated" as someone who has been "celebrate-d" — a person so remarkable that people celebrate them, making them famous.
把"celebrated"想成被"celebrate（庆祝）"过的人——一个如此杰出以至于人们为之庆祝的人，因此成为名人。
