# 🎁 endue

🔊 /ɪnˈdjuː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To provide with a quality, ability, or asset; to endow or invest with certain characteristics
**中文：** v. 授予；赋予

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- endow（赋予）
- bestow（授予）
- invest（赋予）
- furnish（提供）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endue with power / endue with qualities / endue with authority / endue with wisdom**

👉 Example: The constitution endues the judiciary with the authority to interpret and uphold the law.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Nature has **endued** certain animals with remarkable instincts that enable them to survive in the harshest environments on earth.
   大自然赋予了某些动物非凡的本能，使它们能够在地球上最严酷的环境中生存。

2. The scholarship program aims to **endue** talented students with the resources and opportunities necessary for academic excellence.
   该奖学金项目旨在赋予有才华的学生实现学术卓越所需的资源和机会。

3. Great leaders are often **endued** with a combination of charisma, intelligence, and an unwavering commitment to ethical principles.
   伟大的领导者往往被赋予魅力、智慧以及对道德原则坚定不移的承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "endue" as "en + due" — what is due to someone is given to them; to endue is to give someone what they are due, like a gift of talent or power.
把"endue"想成"en + due（应得的）"——把应得的东西给予某人；endue就是赋予某人应得的，如天赋或权力。
