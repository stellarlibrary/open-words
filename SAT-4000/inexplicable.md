# ❓ inexplicable

🔊 /ˌɪnɪkˈsplɪkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Unable to be explained or accounted for; beyond comprehension or understanding
**中文：** adj. 费解的；无法说明的；不能解释的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unexplainable（无法解释的）
- incomprehensible（不可理解的）
- baffling（令人困惑的）
- mystifying（令人迷惑的）
- enigmatic（神秘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inexplicable phenomenon / inexplicable behavior / inexplicable disappearance / seemingly inexplicable / remain inexplicable**

👉 Example: The sudden and inexplicable disappearance of the flight remains one of aviation's greatest mysteries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist encountered an **inexplicable** anomaly in the data that contradicted every established theoretical prediction.
   这位科学家在数据中遇到了一个**无法解释**的异常现象，与所有既定的理论预测相矛盾。

2. Her **inexplicable** decision to resign at the height of her career left colleagues bewildered and searching for answers.
   她在事业巅峰时期做出的**费解**的辞职决定让同事们困惑不已，四处寻找答案。

3. The ancient ruins contained **inexplicable** symbols that have defied interpretation by even the most distinguished archaeologists.
   这些古代遗迹包含**无法解释**的符号，即使是最杰出的考古学家也无法破译。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-explic-able" as "not able to be explained" — the prefix "in-" negates "explicable," leaving you with something that defies all explanation.
把"inexplicable"拆成"in-explic-able"，即"无法被解释的"——前缀"in-"否定了"able to explain"，留下了一个无法解释的事物。
