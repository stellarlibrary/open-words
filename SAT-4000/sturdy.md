# 💪 sturdy

🔊 /ˈstɜːrdi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Firm and resolute; physically strong and well-built; sound. (n.) A disease in sheep causing giddiness
**中文：** adj. 强健的，结实的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- robust（强壮的）
- durable（耐用的）
- resilient（坚韧的）
- solid（坚固的）
- stout（结实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sturdy construction / sturdy frame / sturdy foundation / sturdy resistance**

👉 Example: The cabin was built with sturdy timber that could withstand the harshest winter storms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers selected a **sturdy** species of oak tree known for its ability to endure extreme weather conditions.
   研究人员选择了一种以能承受极端天气条件而闻名的强健橡树品种。

2. The organization maintained a **sturdy** commitment to ethical practices despite mounting financial pressure from external investors.
   尽管面临外部投资者不断增加的财务压力，该组织仍坚定地致力于道德实践。

3. Engineers designed the bridge with a **sturdy** steel framework capable of supporting thousands of vehicles each day.
   工程师们设计了一座坚固的钢结构桥梁，每天能够承载数千辆车辆通行。

把"sturdy"想成"study + hard"——努力学习的人建立了坚实的知识基础，就像坚固的材料建造出结实的结构。
