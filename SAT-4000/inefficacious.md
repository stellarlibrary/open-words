# 🚧 inefficacious

🔊 /ˌɪnˌɛfɪˈkeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not producing the desired effect; lacking the power to achieve the intended purpose or result
**中文：** adj. 无效力的；无用的；无效果的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ineffective（无效的）
- futile（徒劳的）
- impotent（无力的）
- unavailing（无益的）
- unproductive（无成效的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inefficacious remedy / inefficacious treatment / inefficacious policy / inefficacious measures**

👉 Example: The clinical trial revealed that the experimental drug was wholly inefficacious against the targeted strain of the virus.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The regulatory agency withdrew approval for the medication after multiple studies confirmed it was **inefficacious** in treating chronic pain conditions.
   监管机构在多项研究证实该药物对治疗慢性疼痛无效后撤回了批准。

2. Diplomatic negotiations proved entirely **inefficacious** when neither party demonstrated a genuine willingness to compromise on territorial disputes.
   当双方都没有表现出在领土争端上做出真正妥协的意愿时，外交谈判被证明完全无效。

3. The school board acknowledged that its **inefficacious** anti-bullying program required a comprehensive overhaul based on current behavioral research.
   校董事会承认其无效的反霸凌项目需要根据当前行为研究进行全面改革。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-EFFICAC-ious" as "not efficacious" — if something is efficacious it works well, so inefficacious means it does not work at all.
把"inefficacious"想成"in(不)+efficacious(有效的)"——如果某事是efficacious则效果好，inefficacious就是完全无效。
