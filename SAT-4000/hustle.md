# 🏃 hustle

🔊 /ˈhʌsəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To push or shove roughly; to hurry or rush; to work energetically and with determination. (n.) A state of great activity or busy movement; forceful pushing
**中文：** v. 催促；猛推；急速活动；奔忙 n. 推；忙碌；奔忙

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rush（匆忙）
- hasten（加速）
- bustle（忙碌）
- shove（推搡）
- scramble（争抢）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hustle and bustle / hustle through / side hustle / hustle someone out of**

👉 Example: The security guards had to hustle the protesters out of the building before the ceremony began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious entrepreneur would **hustle** tirelessly from dawn until midnight to establish her startup in the competitive market.
   这位雄心勃勃的企业家从黎明到午夜不知疲倦地奔忙，以在竞争激烈的市场中建立她的初创公司。

2. Bodyguards quickly moved to **hustle** the diplomat into an armored vehicle when suspicious activity was detected outside the venue.
   当场馆外发现可疑活动时，保镖迅速将外交官推入一辆装甲车中。

3. The **hustle** and bustle of metropolitan life can be overwhelming for individuals accustomed to the tranquility of rural areas.
   大都市生活的喧嚣忙碌对于习惯了乡村宁静的人来说可能是难以承受的。

---

把"hustle"想成"hus + tle"——想象一只哈士奇犬(hus-)精力充沛、飞速地拉着雪橇，总是忙碌地向前冲。
