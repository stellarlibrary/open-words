# 🎵 timbre

🔊 /ˈtæmbər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The distinctive quality or tone color of a sound, especially of a musical instrument or voice
**中文：** n. 音色；音质；音品

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tone（音调）
- tonality（音调性）
- sound quality（音质）
- resonance（共鸣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **timbre of a voice / rich timbre / warm timbre / distinctive timbre / tonal timbre**

👉 Example: The cello has a rich, warm timbre that distinguishes it from other string instruments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vocalist's deep and resonant **timbre** captivated the audience throughout the entire evening performance.
   这位歌手深沉而洪亮的音色在整个晚间演出中吸引了观众。

2. Musicians carefully select instruments based on **timbre**, as each produces a unique sonic character and texture.
   音乐家根据音色精心挑选乐器，因为每种乐器都能产生独特的声音特征和质感。

3. The composer deliberately blended contrasting **timbres** to create an innovative and emotionally stirring orchestral arrangement.
   作曲家特意融合了对比鲜明的音色，创作出一部创新且感人至深的管弦乐编曲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "timbre" as "timber" rearranged — different types of timber (wood) make different sounds when struck, each with its own timbre.
把"timbre"想成"timber（木材）"的变形——不同种类的木材被敲击时发出不同的声音，各有其独特的音色。
