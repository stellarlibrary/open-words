# 🔄 loop

🔊 /luːp/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A curved or circular shape; a circuit or cycle that repeats. (v.) To form into a loop; to move in a circular path
**中文：** n. 环；圈；弯曲部分；回路 v. 打环；翻筋斗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- circle（圆圈）
- cycle（循环）
- coil（线圈）
- ring（环）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feedback loop / loop around / in the loop / out of the loop / loop through**

👉 Example: The manager kept all team members in the loop regarding the project's latest developments.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The software engineer designed a feedback **loop** that automatically adjusts the system's performance based on real-time data.
   软件工程师设计了一个反馈回路，根据实时数据自动调整系统性能。

2. The pilot skillfully **looped** the aircraft over the airfield, thrilling spectators gathered below with an impressive aerial display.
   飞行员熟练地驾驶飞机在机场上空翻筋斗，令下方聚集的观众为精彩的空中表演而兴奋不已。

3. Economists warn that a dangerous **loop** of inflation and wage increases could destabilize the national economy within months.
   经济学家警告说，通货膨胀和工资增长的危险循环可能在数月内破坏国家经济的稳定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of the two "o"s in "loop" as two circles forming a loop — a shape that goes around and comes back.
把"loop"中的两个"o"想象成两个圆圈组成的环——一个绕行并回到原点的形状。
