# 🚶 ramble

🔊 /ˈræmbl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A leisurely walk or stroll; a discursive piece of writing. (v.) To walk for pleasure without a definite route; to talk or write at length in a confused way
**中文：** n. 漫步；漫游；随笔 v. 漫步于；漫游；漫谈，闲聊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- wander（漫游）
- roam（闲逛）
- stroll（散步）
- digress（离题）
- meander（蜿蜒而行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ramble on / ramble through / go for a ramble / ramble about**

👉 Example: The professor tended to ramble on about tangential topics during his lengthy afternoon lectures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The elderly scholar would often **ramble** through the countryside, finding inspiration in the natural landscape around him.
   这位年迈的学者经常在乡间漫步，从周围的自然风景中寻找灵感。

2. Critics noted that the author's essay began to **ramble** after the introduction, lacking a coherent argumentative structure.
   评论家指出，这位作者的文章在引言之后就开始漫无边际，缺乏连贯的论证结构。

3. We decided to take a leisurely **ramble** along the coastal path before the academic conference resumed in the afternoon.
   我们决定在下午学术会议恢复之前，沿着海岸小径悠闲地漫步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ramble" as "ram + amble" — a ram that ambles around aimlessly, wandering wherever it pleases without a plan.
把"ramble"想成"ram + amble"——一只公羊漫无目的地闲逛，随心所欲地到处游荡。
