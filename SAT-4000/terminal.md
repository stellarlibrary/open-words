# 🚉 terminal

🔊 /ˈtɜːrmɪnəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The end point of a transportation route; a device for entering data into a computer. (adj.) Relating to or situated at the end; (of a disease) predicted to lead to death.
**中文：** n. 末端；终点站；终端机 adj. 末端的；晚期的；致命的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- final（最终的）
- endpoint（终点）
- fatal（致命的）
- concluding（结束的）
- station（站点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **terminal illness / terminal station / airport terminal / computer terminal / terminal stage**

👉 Example: The patient was diagnosed with a terminal illness and chose to spend her remaining time with family.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new international **terminal** at the airport was designed to accommodate over fifty million passengers annually with modern amenities.
   机场新建的国际航站楼旨在以现代化设施每年容纳超过五千万名旅客。

2. The physician informed the family that the disease had reached its **terminal** stage and that palliative care would be the primary focus.
   医生告知家属，疾病已到晚期，姑息治疗将成为主要方向。

3. Engineers connected a dedicated **terminal** to the mainframe computer to monitor real-time data from the satellite tracking system continuously.
   工程师将一台专用终端机连接到大型计算机上，以持续监控来自卫星跟踪系统的实时数据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "terminal" as "terminate + al" — it describes something at the very end, like an airport terminal where your ground journey terminates and your flight begins.
把"terminal"想成"terminate（终止）+ al"——它描述的是最末端的事物，就像机场航站楼，你的地面旅程终止，飞行旅程开始。
