# 📜 testament

🔊 /ˈtestəmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person's will or the document containing it; something that serves as evidence or proof of a fact, quality, or belief
**中文：** 遗嘱；确实的证明；证据

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proof（证明）
- testimony（证据）
- witness（见证）
- tribute（致敬）
- will（遗嘱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a testament to / last will and testament / living testament / Old Testament / New Testament**

👉 Example: The thriving community garden stands as a testament to the residents' dedication and collaborative spirit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The restored cathedral stands as a **testament** to the extraordinary craftsmanship and artistic vision of medieval builders.
   修复后的大教堂是中世纪建造者非凡工艺和艺术远见的确实证明。

2. Her remarkable recovery from the accident was a **testament** to both modern medicine and her own unwavering determination.
   她从事故中的惊人恢复是现代医学和她自身坚定决心的有力证明。

3. The billionaire's **testament** allocated the majority of his vast fortune to educational foundations serving underprivileged communities nationwide.
   这位亿万富翁的遗嘱将其大部分巨额财产分配给了服务全国弱势社区的教育基金会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "test-ament" as a "test" of truth — a testament tests and proves that something is real or true.
把"testament"想成一个真相的"test（测试）"——testament测试并证明某事是真实的。
