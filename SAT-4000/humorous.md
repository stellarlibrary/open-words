# 😄 humorous

🔊 /ˈhjuːmərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing laughter and amusement; having or showing a sense of humor; comic and witty
**中文：** adj. 诙谐的；幽默的；滑稽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- funny（有趣的）
- witty（机智的）
- comical（滑稽的）
- amusing（逗乐的）
- droll（诙谐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **humorous tone / humorous anecdote / humorous remark / humorous writing**

👉 Example: The author is known for her humorous essays that cleverly satirize contemporary social norms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **humorous** opening remarks effectively eased the tension in the room before the serious negotiations commenced.
   外交官幽默的开场白有效地缓解了房间里的紧张气氛，随后严肃的谈判才开始。

2. Many researchers have found that a **humorous** teaching style significantly improves student engagement and long-term information retention.
   许多研究人员发现，幽默的教学风格能显著提高学生的参与度和长期信息记忆。

3. The novelist's **humorous** portrayal of bureaucratic absurdity resonated with readers who had endured similar frustrating experiences.
   这位小说家对官僚荒谬的幽默刻画引起了经历过类似挫折的读者的共鸣。

把"humorous"想成"humor + ous"——充满幽默，就像"famous"意为充满名气一样。一个humorous的人就是幽默感满溢的人。
