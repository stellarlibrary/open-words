# 🎯 felicitous

🔊 /fɪˈlɪsɪtəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Well-suited for the occasion; pleasantly apt or appropriate; skillful in expression; happy or fortunate
**中文：** adj. 恰当的；善于措辞的；幸福的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apt（恰当的）
- appropriate（合适的）
- fitting（适宜的）
- eloquent（雄辩的）
- fortunate（幸运的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a felicitous phrase / a felicitous choice of words / a felicitous remark / a felicitous occasion**

👉 Example: The ambassador's felicitous choice of words helped defuse a potentially volatile diplomatic situation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet's **felicitous** turn of phrase captured the essence of longing in a way that resonated with every reader.
   这位诗人恰到好处的措辞捕捉到了思念的本质，引起了每位读者的共鸣。

2. It was a **felicitous** coincidence that the two scholars arrived at the same conclusion through entirely independent research.
   两位学者通过完全独立的研究得出相同的结论，这是一个幸运的巧合。

3. Her **felicitous** response to the interviewer's provocative question demonstrated both intelligence and remarkable composure under pressure.
   她对采访者挑衅性问题的巧妙回答展示了她的智慧和在压力下非凡的沉着。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "felicitous" as sharing the root with "felicity" (happiness). A felicitous remark makes everyone happy because it is perfectly chosen.
把"felicitous"和"felicity（幸福）"联系起来——一句恰当的话让所有人都满意，因为它选得恰到好处。
