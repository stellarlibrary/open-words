# 🌟 laudable

🔊 /ˈlɔːdəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deserving praise and commendation; worthy of admiration or approval
**中文：** adj. 值得赞赏的；值得称赞的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commendable（值得表扬的）
- praiseworthy（值得赞美的）
- admirable（令人钦佩的）
- meritorious（有功的）
- creditable（值得称赞的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **laudable goal / laudable effort / laudable attempt / laudable ambition**

👉 Example: Their laudable effort to reduce carbon emissions earned widespread recognition from environmental groups.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization's **laudable** commitment to providing free education has transformed countless communities across developing nations.
   该组织为提供免费教育所做的值得赞赏的承诺已经改变了发展中国家无数的社区。

2. Although her **laudable** intentions were clear, the proposed reform lacked the practical framework necessary for implementation.
   尽管她值得称赞的意图很明确，但拟议的改革缺乏实施所需的实际框架。

3. The mayor pursued the **laudable** goal of eliminating homelessness, allocating substantial resources to affordable housing programs.
   市长追求消除无家可归现象这一值得赞赏的目标，为经济适用房项目拨付了大量资源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "laud-able" as "able to be lauded" — something so good it deserves to be praised and celebrated.
把"laudable"想成"able to be lauded（能被赞美的）"——好到值得被赞美和称颂的事物。
