# 🌿 organic

🔊 /ɔːrˈɡænɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or derived from living matter; produced without the use of chemical fertilizers or pesticides; relating to carbon-based compounds in chemistry; fundamental or inherent
**中文：** adj. 有机的；组织的；器官的；根本的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- natural（天然的）
- biological（生物的）
- unprocessed（未加工的）
- inherent（固有的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **organic chemistry / organic food / organic growth / organic compound / organic matter**

👉 Example: Consumers increasingly prefer organic produce because it is grown without synthetic pesticides.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural department promoted **organic** farming methods to reduce environmental contamination and improve soil health over time.
   农业部推广有机农业方法，以减少环境污染并随着时间推移改善土壤健康。

2. Students in the advanced chemistry course were required to synthesize several **organic** compounds during the laboratory practicum.
   高级化学课程的学生被要求在实验室实习期间合成几种有机化合物。

3. The company experienced **organic** growth through innovation and customer loyalty rather than through mergers or acquisitions.
   该公司通过创新和客户忠诚度实现了有机增长，而非通过合并或收购。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "organic" as "organ-ic" — just like your body's organs are natural and living, organic things come from natural, living sources.
把"organic"想成"organ-ic"——就像你身体的器官是天然的、有生命的一样，有机的事物来自天然的、有生命的来源。
