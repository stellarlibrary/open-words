# 🔬 intensive

🔊 /ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Concentrated and thorough; involving great effort or resources within a short period. (n.) An intensifying element or agent
**中文：** adj. 加强的；集中的；深入细致的 n. 加强器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concentrated（集中的）
- rigorous（严格的）
- thorough（彻底的）
- exhaustive（详尽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intensive care / intensive training / intensive study / labor-intensive / capital-intensive**

👉 Example: The athletes underwent intensive training for six months before competing in the national championship.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital's **intensive** care unit was fully equipped to handle patients with the most severe and life-threatening medical conditions.
   医院的重症监护室设备齐全，能够处理病情最严重和危及生命的患者。

2. Students enrolled in the **intensive** summer program completed two full semesters of coursework in just eight demanding weeks.
   参加暑期强化课程的学生在短短八周内完成了两个学期的课程。

3. The investigation required an **intensive** review of thousands of financial documents spanning the previous decade of operations.
   调查需要对过去十年运营中的数千份财务文件进行深入审查。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-tens-ive" — the tension is "in" you, pushed to "ten" out of ten, meaning maximum effort and concentration.
把"intensive"想成"in(内部) + tens(紧张) + ive"——内在的紧张达到十分之十，意味着最大程度的努力和集中。
