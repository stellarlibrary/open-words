# 🗣️ bilingual

🔊 /baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to speak two languages fluently. (n.) A person who speaks two languages fluently
**中文：** adj. 双语的；能说两种语言的 n. 通两种语言的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- multilingual（多语言的）
- polyglot（通晓多种语言的人）
- dual-language（双语的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bilingual education / bilingual dictionary / bilingual speaker / bilingual community / become bilingual**

👉 Example: Children raised in bilingual households often demonstrate enhanced cognitive flexibility and problem-solving skills.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company actively seeks **bilingual** candidates who can communicate effectively with clients in both English and Mandarin.
   该公司积极寻找能够用英语和普通话与客户进行有效沟通的双语候选人。

2. Research suggests that **bilingual** individuals may experience a delayed onset of cognitive decline compared to monolingual peers.
   研究表明，与单语者相比，双语者的认知衰退可能出现得更晚。

3. The government implemented a **bilingual** education policy to preserve indigenous languages while ensuring proficiency in the national language.
   政府实施了双语教育政策，以在确保国语熟练度的同时保护土著语言。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bi-" (two) + "lingual" (tongue/language) — a bilingual person has "two tongues," able to speak two languages with ease.
把"bilingual"拆分为"bi-"（二）+"lingual"（舌头/语言）——双语者拥有"两条舌头"，能轻松说两种语言。
