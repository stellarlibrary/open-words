# 🏚️ archaic

🔊 /ɑːrˈkeɪɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very old or old-fashioned; belonging to an earlier period; no longer in common use
**中文：** adj. 古代的；陈旧的；过时的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- antiquated（过时的）
- obsolete（废弃的）
- outmoded（不再流行的）
- ancient（古老的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **archaic language / archaic customs / archaic laws / archaic practices / seem archaic**

👉 Example: The legal system still contains several archaic laws that have not been enforced for centuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar struggled to translate the **archaic** text because its vocabulary had long fallen out of common usage.
   这位学者在翻译这篇古老的文本时遇到了困难，因为其词汇早已不再常用。

2. Many critics argue that the country's **archaic** electoral system fails to represent the will of modern voters.
   许多批评人士认为，该国陈旧的选举制度未能代表现代选民的意愿。

3. The museum displayed **archaic** tools and weapons that illustrated the daily lives of early Bronze Age communities.
   博物馆展示了古代的工具和武器，展现了青铜时代早期社区的日常生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arch-aic" — "arch" sounds like "arch" in architecture, something old and grand. Archaic things belong to an ancient "arch" of history.
把"archaic"想成"arch（拱门）+ aic"——拱门让人联想到古老宏伟的建筑，archaic就是指属于历史长河中古老时代的事物。
