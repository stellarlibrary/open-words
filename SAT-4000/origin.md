# 🌱 origin

🔊 /ˈɔːrɪdʒɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The point or place where something begins or arises; a person's social background or ancestry; the fixed point from which coordinates are measured in mathematics
**中文：** n. 起源，由来；出身，来历；原点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- source（来源）
- beginning（开端）
- root（根源）
- genesis（起源）
- derivation（起源）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **country of origin / origin of species / point of origin / origin story / humble origins**

👉 Example: The researchers traced the origin of the epidemic to a remote village in the southern province.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians have long debated the **origin** of democratic governance and its gradual evolution across ancient Mediterranean civilizations.
   历史学家长期以来一直在争论民主治理的起源及其在古代地中海文明中的逐步演变。

2. Despite her humble **origin**, the scholar achieved international recognition for her groundbreaking contributions to theoretical physics.
   尽管出身卑微，这位学者因其对理论物理学的开创性贡献而获得了国际认可。

3. In coordinate geometry, the **origin** is defined as the point where the horizontal and vertical axes intersect at zero.
   在坐标几何中，原点被定义为水平轴和垂直轴在零处相交的点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "origin" as "or-i-gin" — "original beginning" — everything starts somewhere, and the origin is that very first point.
把"origin"想成"or-i-gin"——"最初的开始"——一切都从某处开始，而origin就是那个最初的起点。
