# 🔧 torque

🔊 /tɔːrk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A twisting force that tends to cause rotation; a measure of rotational force. (v.) To apply a twisting or turning force to something
**中文：** n. 转矩；扭矩；扭转力 v. 施加扭动力；扭转

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- twist（扭转）
- rotation（旋转）
- torsion（扭力）
- leverage（杠杆力）
- moment of force（力矩）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **apply torque / torque wrench / high torque / engine torque / torque the bolts**

👉 Example: The mechanic used a calibrated wrench to torque the bolts to the manufacturer's specifications.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer calculated the precise amount of **torque** required to secure the turbine blades without damaging the assembly components.
   工程师计算了固定涡轮叶片所需的精确扭矩，以避免损坏装配组件。

2. Electric motors are prized in automotive design because they deliver maximum **torque** instantly from a complete standstill.
   电动机在汽车设计中备受推崇，因为它们能从完全静止状态瞬间输出最大扭矩。

3. The technician carefully **torqued** each fastener to the specified setting to ensure the structural integrity of the aircraft wing.
   技术人员仔细地将每个紧固件拧到规定的扭矩值，以确保机翼的结构完整性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "torque" as "to work" with a twist — when you torque something, you work a twisting force on it, like turning a wrench.
把"torque"想成带有旋转的"to work（工作）"——当你torque某物时，你对它施加了一个扭转的力，就像拧扳手一样。
