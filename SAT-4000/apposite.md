# 🎯 apposite

🔊 /ˈæpəzɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very appropriate and relevant to a particular situation or context; strikingly fitting and to the point
**中文：** 适当的；贴切的；恰如其分的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- apt（恰当的）
- pertinent（相关的）
- fitting（合适的）
- germane（密切相关的）
- relevant（切题的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an apposite remark / an apposite example / an apposite quotation / particularly apposite**

👉 Example: The professor offered an apposite quotation from Shakespeare to illustrate her argument.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **apposite** response to the crisis demonstrated both cultural sensitivity and exceptional political awareness.
   这位外交官对危机的贴切回应展示了文化敏感性和出色的政治意识。

2. Her **apposite** analogy comparing the economy to an ecosystem helped the audience grasp the complex interrelationships involved.
   她把经济比作生态系统的贴切类比帮助听众理解了其中复杂的相互关系。

3. The lawyer cited an especially **apposite** legal precedent that directly addressed the constitutional question raised by the defense.
   律师引用了一个特别贴切的法律先例，直接回应了辩方提出的宪法问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "apposite" as "a POSIT that fits" — a position or statement that is perfectly placed and fitting for the situation.
把"apposite"想成"a POSIT that fits"——一个完美契合情境的立场或陈述，恰到好处。
