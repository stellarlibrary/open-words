# 🎫 token

🔊 /ˈtoʊkən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A sign, symbol, or indication of something; a small piece used as a substitute for money. (adj.) Done as a gesture, especially to demonstrate minimal compliance
**中文：** n. 表征；代币；记号；象征物 adj. 象征性的；装样子的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- symbol（象征）
- sign（标志）
- emblem（徽章）
- nominal（名义上的）
- gesture（姿态）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **token of appreciation / token gesture / token resistance / by the same token / token effort**

👉 Example: The company made only a token effort to address the employees' legitimate concerns about safety.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador presented a **token** of friendship between the two nations in the form of a rare cultural artifact.
   大使以一件珍贵的文化文物作为两国友谊的象征进行了赠送。

2. Critics dismissed the reform as a merely **token** gesture that failed to address the systemic inequalities in the institution.
   批评人士将这项改革斥为仅仅是象征性的姿态，未能解决该机构的系统性不平等问题。

3. By the same **token**, a policy that benefits one demographic group should not deliberately disadvantage another vulnerable population.
   同样地，一项有利于某一人口群体的政策不应刻意损害另一个弱势群体的利益。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "token" as a subway token — it is a small object that stands in for real money, just as token actions stand in for real effort.
把"token"想成地铁代币——它是一个代替真钱的小物件，就像象征性的行动代替了真正的努力一样。
