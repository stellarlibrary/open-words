# ⚖️ restitution

🔊 /ˌrɛstɪˈtjuːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The restoration of something lost or stolen to its proper owner; compensation for injury, loss, or damage; the act of returning something to its original state
**中文：** n. 恢复；赔偿；归还

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compensation（赔偿）
- reparation（补偿）
- restoration（恢复）
- amends（弥补）
- indemnity（赔偿金）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **make restitution / restitution for / order restitution / full restitution / restitution of property**

👉 Example: The judge ordered full restitution to the victims of the financial fraud scheme.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court mandated that the convicted executive pay complete **restitution** to all investors defrauded by the elaborate scheme.
   法院要求被定罪的高管向所有被精心策划的骗局所欺诈的投资者支付全额赔偿。

2. The museum demanded the **restitution** of cultural artifacts that had been illegally removed from the country during colonial occupation.
   博物馆要求归还在殖民占领期间被非法从该国运走的文化文物。

3. International law increasingly recognizes the obligation of nations to make **restitution** for historical injustices committed against indigenous populations.
   国际法越来越认可各国对原住民所犯历史不公正行为进行赔偿的义务。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rest-i-TU-tion" — "restore it to you" — restitution means giving back or restoring what belongs to someone.
把"restitution"想成"restore it to you（把它还给你）"——restitution就是归还或恢复属于某人的东西。
