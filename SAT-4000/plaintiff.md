# ⚖️ plaintiff

🔊 /ˈpleɪntɪf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who brings a case against another in a court of law; the complaining party in a civil lawsuit
**中文：** n. 原告

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- complainant（原告）
- petitioner（请求人）
- claimant（索赔人）
- litigant（诉讼当事人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plaintiff in a lawsuit / plaintiff's attorney / plaintiff alleges / plaintiff seeks damages**

👉 Example: The plaintiff filed a complaint against the manufacturer for producing defective medical devices.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **plaintiff** presented compelling evidence that the pharmaceutical company had knowingly concealed the harmful side effects of its drug.
   原告提供了有力证据，证明该制药公司故意隐瞒了其药物的有害副作用。

2. After years of litigation, the **plaintiff** was awarded substantial financial compensation for injuries sustained in the workplace accident.
   经过多年诉讼，原告因在工作场所事故中所受的伤害获得了大量经济赔偿。

3. The judge dismissed the case because the **plaintiff** failed to demonstrate sufficient grounds for the claims made against the defendant.
   法官驳回了此案，因为原告未能证明对被告提出的索赔有充分的理由。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plaintiff" as "plain-tiff" — the person who makes a plain (clear) complaint in a tiff (dispute) brought to court.
把"plaintiff"想成"plain-tiff"——在争端（tiff）中提出明确（plain）申诉并将其诉诸法庭的人。
