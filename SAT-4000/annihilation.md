# 💥 annihilation

🔊 /əˌnaɪəˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The complete destruction or obliteration of something; total devastation
**中文：** 灭绝；消灭；彻底毁灭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destruction（毁灭）
- obliteration（消灭）
- extermination（灭绝）
- eradication（根除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **total annihilation / nuclear annihilation / annihilation of a species / threat of annihilation**

👉 Example: The general warned that any further provocation could lead to the complete annihilation of the opposing forces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian documented the near **annihilation** of the indigenous population during the colonial expansion of the sixteenth century.
   历史学家记录了十六世纪殖民扩张期间土著人口几近灭绝的过程。

2. Scientists have warned that unchecked asteroid impacts pose a genuine threat of **annihilation** to life on Earth.
   科学家警告说，不加控制的小行星撞击对地球上的生命构成真正的灭绝威胁。

3. The diplomat argued that nuclear **annihilation** remains the gravest existential risk confronting modern civilization today.
   这位外交官认为，核毁灭仍然是当今现代文明面临的最严重的生存风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "annihil-ation" as containing "nil" — meaning nothing. Annihilation reduces everything to nil, to nothing at all.
把"annihilation"想成包含"nil"（零、无）——灭绝就是把一切化为乌有。
