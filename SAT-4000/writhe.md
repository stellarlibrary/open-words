# 🐍 writhe

🔊 /raɪð/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To twist or squirm in pain or discomfort; to contort the body. (n.) An act of writhing; a twisting or contorted movement
**中文：** v. 翻滚；蠕动；扭曲；扭歪 n. 翻滚；扭动；苦恼

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- squirm（蠕动）
- contort（扭曲）
- thrash（翻滚）
- wriggle（扭动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **writhe in pain / writhe in agony / writhe with embarrassment / writhe on the ground**

👉 Example: The injured athlete writhed in agony on the field until medical staff arrived.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The patient **writhed** in pain on the hospital bed as the doctors struggled to identify the source of infection.
   病人在病床上痛苦地翻滚，医生们费力地寻找感染源。

2. Watching the documentary about famine, the audience could see emaciated children **writhing** in suffering and despair.
   观看关于饥荒的纪录录片时，观众可以看到骨瘦如柴的孩子们在痛苦和绝望中挣扎。

3. She **writhed** with embarrassment when the professor publicly corrected her fundamental misunderstanding of the core theorem.
   当教授当众纠正她对核心定理的根本性误解时，她尴尬得坐立不安。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "writhe" as "write" with an "h" — imagine your pen writing squiggly, twisting lines when you are in pain.
把"writhe"想成加了"h"的"write（写）"——想象你痛苦时笔下写出扭曲蜿蜒的线条。
