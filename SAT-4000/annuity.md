# 💰 annuity

🔊 /əˈnjuːəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A fixed sum of money paid to someone each year, typically for the rest of their life; a form of financial investment yielding regular payments
**中文：** 年金；养老金；年度津贴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pension（养老金）
- allowance（津贴）
- stipend（定期补助金）
- income（收入）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fixed annuity / life annuity / annuity payment / purchase an annuity / receive an annuity**

👉 Example: After retiring, she relied on her annuity to cover living expenses throughout her remaining years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The financial advisor recommended purchasing an **annuity** to guarantee a stable income stream during the client's retirement years.
   财务顾问建议购买年金，以确保客户退休期间有稳定的收入来源。

2. Upon reaching the age of sixty-five, he began receiving monthly **annuity** payments from his employer's pension fund.
   在年满六十五岁时，他开始从雇主的养老基金中领取每月的年金。

3. The insurance company offers several types of **annuity** products designed to protect policyholders against the risk of outliving savings.
   该保险公司提供多种年金产品，旨在保护投保人免受储蓄耗尽的风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "annuity" as "annu- (year) + -ity (thing)" — it is the thing you receive every year, your yearly payment.
把"annuity"想成"annu-（年）+ -ity（事物）"——它就是你每年收到的东西，即年金。
