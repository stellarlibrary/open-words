# 🏜️ barren

🔊 /ˈbærən/<br>
🏷️ *adj. | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (of land) Too poor to produce much vegetation; unable to bear children or fruit; lacking in useful results or productive quality.

**中文：** 贫瘠的；不生育的；无益的；无结果的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- sterile （不育的；贫瘠的）
- infertile （不肥沃的；不能生育的）
- desolate （荒凉的）
- unproductive （无成效的）
- arid （干旱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Barren" can describe land that cannot support crops, a person or animal unable to reproduce, or an endeavor that yields no results. It is often used figuratively to describe something devoid of interest, creativity, or value. Be cautious using it to describe people, as it can be considered insensitive.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The explorers crossed miles of **barren** wasteland before finally reaching the distant oasis.
   中文：探险者们穿越了数英里的贫瘠荒地，终于到达了远处的绿洲。

2. Despite years of intensive research, the investigation proved entirely **barren** of meaningful results.
   中文：尽管经过多年深入研究，这项调查最终毫无有意义的结果。

3. The once **barren** hillside was gradually transformed into a thriving vineyard through careful cultivation.
   中文：曾经贫瘠的山坡通过精心耕作逐渐变成了一片繁茂的葡萄园。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Barren" sounds like "bare" + "en" — think of land that is completely bare, with nothing growing on it. Bare land = barren land.

谐音记忆："barren"听起来像"bare"（光秃秃的）+ "en"——想象一片完全光秃的土地，什么都不长。光秃的土地 = 贫瘠的土地。
