# 🎓 academic

🔊 /ˌækəˈdɛmɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to education, scholarship, or scholarly pursuits; theoretical rather than practical. (n.) A scholar or university teacher.
**中文：** (adj.) 学术的；理论的；学院的。(n.) 大学教师；学者。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scholarly（学术的）
- intellectual（知识分子的）
- theoretical（理论的）
- educational（教育的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **academic research**, **academic performance**, **academic institution**, **purely academic**

👉 Example: The distinction between the two theories is purely academic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her **academic** achievements earned her a prestigious scholarship to study abroad.
   她的学术成就为她赢得了出国留学的优秀奖学金。

2. The debate over that policy remains largely **academic**, as no legislation has been proposed.
   关于那项政策的争论基本上还停留在理论层面，因为尚未提出立法。

3. As a distinguished **academic**, he published extensively in the field of molecular biology.
   作为一名杰出的学者，他在分子生物学领域发表了大量论文。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Academic" shares its root with "academy" — picture a scholar in an ancient Greek academy devoted to learning.
"Academic" 和 "academy"（学院）同源——想象一位学者在古希腊学院里潜心学习。
