# 🍷 carouse

🔊 /kəˈraʊz/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To drink plentifully and enjoy oneself with others in a noisy, lively way. (n.) A noisy, lively drinking party or celebration
**中文：** v. 畅饮；痛饮；狂饮欢闹 n. 喧闹的酒会或宴会

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revel（狂欢）
- wassail（痛饮）
- make merry（寻欢作乐）
- feast（宴饮）
- celebrate（庆祝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **carouse through the night / carouse until dawn / carouse with friends / a night of carousing**

👉 Example: The sailors caroused through the night upon their triumphant return to port.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The victorious soldiers chose to **carouse** throughout the evening, celebrating their hard-fought triumph with abundant drink and song.
   胜利的士兵们选择整晚狂饮欢庆，以丰盛的美酒和歌声庆祝他们艰苦赢得的胜利。

2. Historical accounts reveal that the aristocrats frequently **caroused** at lavish banquets lasting well into the early morning hours.
   历史记载显示，贵族们经常在一直持续到凌晨的奢华宴会上纵情欢饮。

3. The dean admonished students who spent their weekends **carousing** rather than devoting adequate time to their academic responsibilities.
   院长告诫那些把周末花在狂欢痛饮上而不是投入足够时间履行学业责任的学生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "carouse" as sounding like "car-house" — imagine a rowdy party in a car house (garage), with people drinking and making noise all night long.
把"carouse"想成听起来像"car-house"（车库）——想象一群人在车库里开喧闹的派对，整夜喝酒狂欢。
