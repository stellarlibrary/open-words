# 🌿 fertilization

🔊 /ˌfɜːrtəlɪˈzeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of applying fertilizer to soil; the biological process of fusion of male and female reproductive cells
**中文：** n. 施肥；受精；肥沃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insemination（授精）
- pollination（授粉）
- enrichment（富化）
- impregnation（受孕）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fertilization of the soil / in vitro fertilization / fertilization process / cross-fertilization**

👉 Example: The fertilization of crops with organic compost improved both yield and soil quality.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fertilization** of agricultural fields with nitrogen-based compounds has dramatically increased global food production over decades.
   用氮基化合物对农田进行施肥在数十年间大幅提高了全球粮食产量。

2. In vitro **fertilization** has provided hope for millions of couples who struggle with natural conception and infertility.
   体外受精为数百万在自然受孕方面遇到困难的夫妇带来了希望。

3. Cross-**fertilization** of ideas between different academic disciplines often leads to the most innovative breakthroughs in research.
   不同学科之间思想的交叉融合往往会带来最具创新性的研究突破。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fertilization" as "fertile + action" — the action that makes something fertile, whether it is soil or an egg.
把"fertilization"想成"fertile + action"——使某物变得肥沃的行为，无论是土壤还是卵子。
