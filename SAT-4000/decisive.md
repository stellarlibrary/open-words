# 🎯 decisive

🔊 /dɪˈsaɪsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the power to determine an outcome; characterized by firmness and resolution in making decisions
**中文：** adj. 决定性的；果断的，坚定的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- determined（坚定的）
- resolute（果断的）
- conclusive（决定性的）
- definitive（明确的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **decisive action / decisive factor / decisive role / decisive victory / decisive moment**

👉 Example: The general's decisive leadership during the crisis prevented further casualties and restored order swiftly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee recognized that **decisive** action was necessary to address the rapidly deteriorating environmental conditions in the region.
   委员会认识到，必须采取果断行动来应对该地区迅速恶化的环境状况。

2. Her **decisive** manner during negotiations impressed the board members and secured a favorable outcome for the organization.
   她在谈判中果断的态度给董事会成员留下了深刻印象，并为组织争取到了有利的结果。

3. The discovery of new evidence proved to be the **decisive** factor in overturning the defendant's previous conviction.
   新证据的发现被证明是推翻被告先前定罪的决定性因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "decisive" as "decide + -ive" — someone who is decisive has the power to decide quickly and firmly.
把"decisive"想成"decide + -ive"——一个果断的人有能力快速而坚定地做出决定。
