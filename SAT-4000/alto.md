# 🎵 alto

🔊 /ˈæltoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The lowest female singing voice; a musical instrument with a range in the alto register. (adj.) Of or relating to the alto vocal or instrumental range
**中文：** n. 女低音；中音乐器 adj. 中音部的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contralto（女低音）
- mezzo-soprano（女次高音）
- countertenor（男高音假声）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alto voice / alto saxophone / alto clef / sing alto**

👉 Example: The choir director asked the alto section to sing their part with greater resonance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Her rich **alto** voice captivated the audience during the evening performance at the prestigious concert hall.
   她浑厚的女低音在那场著名音乐厅的晚间演出中吸引了全场观众。

2. The composer specifically arranged the passage for an **alto** saxophone to convey the melancholy tone of the piece.
   作曲家特意为中音萨克斯编排了这一段，以传达作品的忧郁基调。

3. She transitioned from soprano to **alto** after her vocal range deepened considerably during her years of professional training.
   在多年的专业训练中，她的音域明显变低，因此从女高音转为女低音。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alto" from Italian meaning "high" — but in music, it refers to the high range of lower voices, sitting between soprano and tenor.
"Alto"源自意大利语"高"的意思——但在音乐中，它指的是较低声部中较高的音域，位于女高音和男高音之间。
