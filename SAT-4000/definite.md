# ✅ definite

🔊 /ˈdɛfɪnɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Clearly stated or decided; having exact and clear meaning; not vague or doubtful
**中文：** adj. 明确的，肯定的；一定的，确切的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- certain（确定的）
- explicit（明确的）
- precise（精确的）
- unambiguous（不含糊的）
- conclusive（结论性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a definite answer / definite plan / definite improvement / definite article**

👉 Example: We need a definite answer by Friday so we can finalize the project schedule.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team reported a **definite** correlation between sleep deprivation and decreased cognitive performance in adults.
   研究团队报告了成人睡眠不足与认知能力下降之间存在明确的相关性。

2. There has been a **definite** shift in public opinion regarding renewable energy policies over the past decade.
   在过去十年中，公众对可再生能源政策的看法发生了明显的转变。

3. The manager gave a **definite** commitment to increase funding for employee training programs in the coming quarter.
   经理明确承诺在下一季度增加员工培训项目的资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "definite" as "define" + "ite" — something that can be clearly defined has definite boundaries and leaves no room for doubt.
把"definite"想成"define（定义）+ ite（……的）"——能被清楚定义的事物就是明确的、确定的。
