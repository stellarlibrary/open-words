# 🛡️ resistance

🔊 /rɪˈzɪstəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of opposing or withstanding something; a force that slows or prevents movement; refusal to accept or comply
**中文：** n. 反抗，抵抗；抵抗力；阻力；电阻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- opposition（反对）
- defiance（抵抗）
- obstruction（阻碍）
- impedance（阻抗）
- reluctance（不情愿）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resistance to / meet with resistance / put up resistance / offer resistance / antibiotic resistance**

👉 Example: The proposed policy changes met with fierce resistance from several influential industry groups.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The bacteria developed **resistance** to multiple antibiotics, posing a significant challenge for physicians treating the severe infection.
   这种细菌对多种抗生素产生了耐药性，给治疗严重感染的医生带来了重大挑战。

2. Political **resistance** to the new immigration legislation intensified as opposition leaders organized nationwide demonstrations and rallies.
   随着反对派领导人组织全国性的示威和集会，对新移民立法的政治抵抗加剧了。

3. Engineers calculated the wind **resistance** of the bridge structure to ensure it could withstand sustained gusts exceeding one hundred miles per hour.
   工程师们计算了桥梁结构的风阻，以确保其能承受超过每小时一百英里的持续阵风。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "resistance" as "re-sist-ance" — you "sist" (stand) again and again, refusing to give in.
把"resistance"想成"re-sist-ance"——你一次又一次地"站立"，拒绝屈服。
