# ❌ invalidate

🔊 /ɪnˈvælɪdeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make something legally or officially unacceptable; to deprive of legal force; to prove wrong or unfounded
**中文：** v. 使无效；使作废；使站不住脚

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nullify（使无效）
- void（使作废）
- negate（否定）
- undermine（削弱）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **invalidate a claim / invalidate a contract / invalidate results / invalidate an argument**

👉 Example: The new evidence could invalidate the entire prosecution's case against the defendant.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The discovery of fabricated data was sufficient to **invalidate** the researcher's published findings and tarnish his reputation.
   伪造数据的发现足以使这位研究人员已发表的研究结果无效，并损害了他的声誉。

2. Failure to disclose prior medical conditions may **invalidate** your insurance policy and leave you without financial coverage.
   未能披露既往病史可能会使您的保险单失效，使您失去经济保障。

3. The appellate court moved to **invalidate** the lower court's ruling, citing significant procedural errors during the original trial.
   上诉法院以原审中存在重大程序错误为由，裁定下级法院的判决无效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "invalidate" as "in + valid + ate" — to eat away at something's validity until it is no longer valid.
把"invalidate"想成"in（不）+ valid（有效）+ ate（吃掉）"——把某物的有效性吞噬掉，使其不再有效。
