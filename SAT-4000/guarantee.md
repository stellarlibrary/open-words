# 🤝 guarantee

🔊 /ˌɡærənˈtiː/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A formal promise or assurance that certain conditions will be fulfilled. (v.) To provide a formal assurance or promise regarding something
**中文：** n. 保证；担保；保证书 v. 保证；担保

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- assurance（保证）
- warranty（保修）
- pledge（承诺）
- promise（许诺）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **guarantee success / guarantee safety / money-back guarantee / under guarantee**

👉 Example: The manufacturer provides a two-year guarantee on all electronic products sold through authorized retailers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The constitution aims to **guarantee** fundamental rights and freedoms for every citizen regardless of social standing.
   宪法旨在保障每位公民的基本权利和自由，不论其社会地位如何。

2. No amount of preparation can fully **guarantee** success in an unpredictable and rapidly changing global market.
   在不可预测且瞬息万变的全球市场中，再充分的准备也无法完全保证成功。

3. The scholarship program serves as a **guarantee** that talented students from disadvantaged backgrounds can access higher education.
   该奖学金项目为来自弱势背景的优秀学生提供了接受高等教育的保障。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "guarantee" as "guard-an-tee" — someone who guards a promise like a tee holds up a golf ball, keeping it secure and stable.
把"guarantee"想成"guard-an-tee"——像球座托住高尔夫球一样守护承诺，确保安全稳固。
