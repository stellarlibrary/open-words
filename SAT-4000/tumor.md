# 🏥 tumor

🔊 /ˈtjuːmər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An abnormal mass of tissue that forms when cells grow and divide more than they should or do not die when they should; may be benign or malignant
**中文：** 肿瘤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- neoplasm（新生物）
- growth（增生物）
- mass（肿块）
- lesion（病变）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **benign tumor / malignant tumor / brain tumor / tumor growth / tumor suppression**

👉 Example: The biopsy revealed that the tumor was benign and could be safely removed through minor surgery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advanced imaging techniques enabled the surgeons to locate the **tumor** with remarkable precision before the operation began.
   先进的成像技术使外科医生在手术开始前能够以惊人的精度定位肿瘤。

2. Researchers discovered that the experimental drug significantly inhibited **tumor** growth in laboratory trials conducted over two years.
   研究人员发现，这种实验药物在为期两年的实验室试验中显著抑制了肿瘤的生长。

3. The oncologist explained that early detection of a **tumor** dramatically increases the probability of successful treatment and recovery.
   肿瘤科医生解释说，肿瘤的早期发现大大增加了成功治疗和康复的概率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tumor" as "too more" — the cells multiply "too more" than normal, creating an abnormal growth in the body.
把"tumor"想成"too more"——细胞增殖得"太多了"，在体内形成了异常的增生。
