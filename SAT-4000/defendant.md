# ⚖️ defendant

🔊 /dɪˈfɛndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Serving in defense; defending. (n.) A person accused or sued in a court of law
**中文：** adj. 辩护的 n. 被告

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accused（被告人）
- respondent（应诉人）
- litigant（诉讼当事人）
- suspect（嫌疑人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the defendant in a case / defendant's rights / defendant pleads guilty / criminal defendant**

👉 Example: The defendant maintained his innocence throughout the entire duration of the trial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **defendant** was granted bail after the judge determined that he posed no significant flight risk.
   法官认定被告没有重大逃跑风险后，准予其保释。

2. Legal counsel advised the **defendant** to remain silent during questioning to avoid any self-incriminating statements.
   法律顾问建议被告在审讯中保持沉默，以避免任何自证其罪的陈述。

3. The jury found the **defendant** not guilty of all charges after carefully reviewing the presented evidence.
   陪审团在仔细审查所提交的证据后，裁定被告所有指控罪名不成立。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "defend-ant" as "the one who must defend" — the defendant is the person in court who must defend themselves against charges.
把"defendant"想成"defend（辩护）+ ant（……的人）"——被告就是在法庭上必须为自己辩护的人。
