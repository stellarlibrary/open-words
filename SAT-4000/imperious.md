# 👑 imperious

🔊 /ɪmˈpɪriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Assuming power or authority without justification; arrogant and domineering; urgent or pressing
**中文：** adj. 专横的；傲慢的；迫切的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- domineering（专横的）
- overbearing（傲慢的）
- authoritarian（独裁的）
- dictatorial（独断的）
- haughty（高傲的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imperious tone / imperious manner / imperious demand / imperious gesture**

👉 Example: The director's imperious manner alienated many talented employees who eventually chose to resign.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The manager's **imperious** tone during meetings discouraged open discussion and stifled creative thinking among team members entirely.
   经理在会议上专横的语气阻碍了开放讨论，完全扼杀了团队成员的创造性思维。

2. With an **imperious** wave of her hand, the chairwoman dismissed all objections and proceeded to implement her controversial reforms.
   女主席傲慢地挥了挥手，驳回了所有反对意见，继续推行她有争议的改革。

3. History demonstrates that **imperious** leaders who refuse counsel from advisors often bring about their own political downfall.
   历史表明，拒绝接受顾问建议的专横领导者往往会导致自己的政治垮台。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imperious" as related to "imperial" — like an emperor who rules with absolute authority and expects total obedience from everyone.
把"imperious"联想到"imperial（帝国的）"——像皇帝一样专横跋扈，期望所有人绝对服从。
