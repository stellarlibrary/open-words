# 🔁 alternate

🔊 /ˈɔːltərneɪt/ (v.) /ɔːlˈtɜːrnət/ (adj.)<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词，形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To occur or do in turn repeatedly; to switch back and forth between two things. (adj.) Occurring by turns; every other.
**中文：** (v.) 交替；轮流 (adj.) 交替的；每隔一个的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rotate（轮换）
- interchange（交换）
- oscillate（摆动）
- switch（切换）
- take turns（轮流）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **alternate between, alternate days, alternate shifts, alternate route, alternate with**
👉 Example: The two physicians alternate weekends at the clinic to ensure continuous patient care.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seasons **alternate** between periods of intense heat and bitter cold in this mountainous continental region.
   在这个多山的大陆性气候地区，季节在酷热和严寒之间交替变化。

2. Students are required to attend laboratory sessions on **alternate** Tuesdays throughout the duration of the semester.
   学生们需要在整个学期内每隔一个星期二参加实验课。

3. The committee decided to **alternate** the hosting responsibilities among the three participating departments each quarter.
   委员会决定每季度在三个参与部门之间轮流承担主办职责。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "alter + nate" — things keep ALTERing their NATURE, switching back and forth between two states.
把 alternate 想象成事物不断 alter（改变）其 nature（性质），在两种状态之间来回切换。
