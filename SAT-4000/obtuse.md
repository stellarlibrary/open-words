# 🪨 obtuse

🔊 /əbˈtjuːs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Slow to understand; lacking sharpness of intellect; annoyingly insensitive or unperceptive
**中文：** adj. 迟钝的；愚蠢的；圆头的；不锋利的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dense（愚钝的）
- dull（迟钝的）
- dim-witted（笨的）
- imperceptive（感觉迟钝的）
- insensible（无知觉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliberately obtuse / seem obtuse / obtuse remark / obtuse angle (geometry)**

👉 Example: The manager appeared deliberately obtuse when asked about the discrepancies in the quarterly report.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat was either genuinely **obtuse** or deliberately evasive when confronted with questions about the treaty violations.
   当面对有关违反条约的问题时，这位外交官要么真的迟钝，要么是故意回避。

2. It would be **obtuse** to ignore the overwhelming scientific evidence supporting the link between emissions and climate change.
   忽视支持排放与气候变化之间关联的大量科学证据将是极其愚钝的。

3. His **obtuse** response to her carefully articulated concerns suggested that he had not truly listened to her argument.
   他对她精心阐述的担忧所作出的迟钝回应表明他并没有真正倾听她的论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of an "obtuse angle" — it is wider than 90 degrees, meaning it is blunt, not sharp. An obtuse person is similarly not sharp-minded.
想想"钝角"（obtuse angle）——它大于90度，意味着它是钝的，不尖锐。一个obtuse的人同样头脑不敏锐。
