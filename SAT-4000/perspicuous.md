# 🔍 perspicuous

🔊 /pərˈspɪkjuəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Clearly expressed and easily understood; lucid and plain in meaning
**中文：** adj. 明了的；清晰明白的；易懂的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lucid（清晰的）
- clear（清楚的）
- transparent（透明易懂的）
- intelligible（易理解的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perspicuous explanation / perspicuous prose / perspicuous argument / perspicuous style**

👉 Example: The professor was praised for her perspicuous explanation of complex scientific theories.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author's **perspicuous** writing style made advanced philosophical concepts accessible to a general readership.
   这位作者清晰明了的写作风格使深奥的哲学概念对普通读者来说也通俗易懂。

2. A **perspicuous** legal document reduces the likelihood of misinterpretation and costly disputes between contracting parties.
   一份清晰明了的法律文件可以减少缔约双方之间误解和代价高昂的纠纷的可能性。

3. The committee requested a more **perspicuous** summary of the findings before proceeding with the final vote.
   委员会要求在进行最终投票之前提供一份更加清楚易懂的调查结果摘要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perspicuous" as "perspective + see through" — when something is perspicuous, you can see through it clearly without confusion.
把"perspicuous"想成"perspective（视角）+ see through（看穿）"——当某事物是perspicuous时，你可以清楚地看透它，毫无困惑。
