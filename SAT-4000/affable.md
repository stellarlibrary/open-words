# 😊 affable

🔊 /ˈæfəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Friendly, good-natured, and easy to talk to; warmly approachable.
**中文：** 和蔼可亲的；友善的。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- amiable（和蔼的）
- genial（亲切的）
- cordial（热诚的）
- approachable（平易近人的）
- congenial（意气相投的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **manner, personality, host, demeanor, tone**
👉 Example: His affable manner immediately put the nervous interviewees at ease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **affable** professor welcomed every student into her office and listened to their concerns with patience.
   那位和蔼可亲的教授欢迎每一位学生来她办公室，并耐心倾听他们的困扰。

2. Despite his prominent position in government, the senator maintained an **affable** demeanor with all constituents.
   尽管在政府中身居要职，这位参议员对所有选民都保持着和蔼可亲的态度。

3. Her **affable** disposition made her the ideal candidate to serve as the organization's public relations director.
   她友善的性格使她成为担任该组织公共关系主任的理想人选。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aff-" sounds like "a friend" — an affable person is as warm and welcoming as a good friend.
"aff-"听起来像"a friend"（一个朋友）——一个affable的人就像好朋友一样温暖而热情。
