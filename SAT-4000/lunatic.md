# 🌙 lunatic

🔊 /ˈluːnətɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely foolish or irrational; mentally deranged. (n.) A person who is mentally ill or extremely reckless
**中文：** adj. 精神错乱的；疯狂的；极端愚蠢的 n. 疯子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insane（疯狂的）
- deranged（精神错乱的）
- maniac（疯子）
- irrational（不理性的）
- absurd（荒谬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lunatic fringe / lunatic behavior / lunatic asylum / a complete lunatic**

👉 Example: Only a lunatic would attempt to cross the frozen river without proper equipment or safety precautions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics dismissed the proposal as a **lunatic** scheme that would inevitably bankrupt the organization within a single fiscal year.
   批评者斥这项提案为疯狂的计划，认为它将在一个财政年度内不可避免地使该组织破产。

2. The **lunatic** fringe of the political movement attracted disproportionate media attention despite representing a very small minority.
   该政治运动中的极端分子尽管只是极少数，却吸引了过多的媒体关注。

3. His **lunatic** decision to invest the entire family fortune in an untested venture shocked every financial advisor he consulted.
   他将全部家产投入一个未经验证的风险项目的疯狂决定震惊了他咨询过的每一位财务顾问。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "luna-tic" from "luna" (moon) — ancient people believed the full moon could drive a person mad, hence a lunatic.
把"lunatic"想成来自"luna（月亮）"——古人认为满月会使人发疯，因此称疯子为lunatic。
