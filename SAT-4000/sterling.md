# 💷 sterling

🔊 /ˈstɜːrlɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Genuine; of British currency; of pure silver. (n.) British currency; standard-quality silver
**中文：** adj. 纯正的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genuine（真正的）
- excellent（优秀的）
- first-rate（一流的）
- exemplary（模范的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sterling silver / sterling character / sterling reputation / sterling quality**

👉 Example: Her sterling character earned her the trust and admiration of every colleague.

1. The ambassador was recognized for her **sterling** contributions to international peacekeeping over the past two decades.
   这位大使因其过去二十年来对国际维和的卓越贡献而受到表彰。

2. The antique dealer confirmed that the ornate tea set was crafted from **sterling** silver dating back to the Victorian era.
   古董商确认这套华丽的茶具是用维多利亚时代的纯银制成的。

3. His **sterling** academic record and impeccable references made him the strongest candidate for the research fellowship.
   他纯正的学术记录和无可挑剔的推荐信使他成为研究奖学金的最有力候选人。
