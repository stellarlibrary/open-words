# 🗡️ assassin

🔊 /əˈsæsɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who murders someone, especially a political or public figure, usually for ideological or financial reasons.
**中文：** n. 刺客；暗杀者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- killer（杀手）
- hitman（职业杀手）
- murderer（谋杀者）
- slayer（杀戮者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assassin** specifically for someone who kills a prominent person deliberately and often for political motives. For general killing, use "murderer" or "killer" instead.

👉 Example: The assassin was hired to eliminate the political leader before the election.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **assassin** concealed a weapon beneath his cloak and waited patiently near the embassy entrance.
   刺客将武器藏在斗篷下，耐心地在大使馆入口附近等候。

2. Historians believe the **assassin** acted alone, driven by radical political ideology and personal grievances.
   历史学家认为这名刺客是单独行动的，受激进政治意识形态和个人怨恨驱使。

3. The renowned leader survived an attack by a hired **assassin** who had infiltrated the security perimeter.
   这位著名领导人在一名渗透了安全警戒线的雇佣刺客的袭击中幸存下来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
The word "assassin" comes from the Arabic "Hashashin," a secret order of medieval killers. Picture a shadowy figure lurking in the dark, ready to strike.
"assassin"源自阿拉伯语"Hashashin"，指中世纪的秘密杀手组织。想象一个潜伏在黑暗中、随时准备出击的阴影身影。
