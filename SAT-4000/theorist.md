# 💡 theorist

🔊 /ˈθɪərɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person who develops or studies the theoretical framework of a subject; one who forms theories or speculates.
**中文：** n. 理论家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- thinker（思想家）
- philosopher（哲学家）
- scholar（学者）
- intellectual（知识分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political theorist / social theorist / leading theorist / conspiracy theorist**

👉 Example: As a prominent social theorist, she challenged conventional assumptions about class and power.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned political **theorist** argued that democratic institutions require constant civic engagement to remain effective and legitimate.
   这位著名的政治理论家认为，民主制度需要持续的公民参与才能保持有效和合法。

2. Many consider Charles Darwin to be not merely a biologist but also a groundbreaking **theorist** of natural selection.
   许多人认为查尔斯·达尔文不仅是一位生物学家，还是自然选择的开创性理论家。

3. The economic **theorist** proposed a novel model that integrated behavioral psychology into traditional market analysis frameworks.
   这位经济理论家提出了一个将行为心理学融入传统市场分析框架的新模型。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "theorist" as "theory + ist" — a person (ist) who works with theories, just like an "artist" works with art.
把"theorist"想成"theory + ist"——一个从事理论工作的人（ist），就像"artist"从事艺术一样。
