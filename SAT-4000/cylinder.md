# 🛢️ cylinder

🔊 /ˈsɪlɪndər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A solid or hollow object with straight parallel sides and a circular cross-section; a chamber in an engine
**中文：** n. 圆筒；汽缸；圆柱体；柱面

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tube（管状物）
- barrel（桶）
- column（柱）
- drum（圆筒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cylinder head / gas cylinder / on all cylinders / cylinder block**

👉 Example: The mechanic discovered that one cylinder in the engine was misfiring consistently.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineer calculated the volume of the **cylinder** using precise measurements of its radius and height.
   工程师使用半径和高度的精确测量值计算了圆柱体的体积。

2. A cracked **cylinder** in the combustion engine caused a significant reduction in the vehicle's overall performance.
   燃烧发动机中的一个汽缸破裂，导致车辆整体性能显著下降。

3. The laboratory stored compressed gases in reinforced steel **cylinders** that met strict international safety standards.
   实验室将压缩气体存放在符合严格国际安全标准的加固钢瓶中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cylinder" as "cy-LINDER" — picture a can of soda, which is a perfect cylinder shape, lining up on a shelf.
把"cylinder"想成"圆柱罐"——想象一罐汽水，它就是完美的圆柱形，整齐排列在货架上。
