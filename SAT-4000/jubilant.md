# 🎉 jubilant

🔊 /ˈdʒuːbɪlənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Feeling or expressing great happiness and triumph; exultantly joyful, especially after a victory or success
**中文：** adj. 欢呼的；喜洋洋的；欢腾的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exultant（欢欣的）
- triumphant（胜利的）
- elated（兴高采烈的）
- overjoyed（欣喜若狂的）
- ecstatic（狂喜的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **jubilant crowd / jubilant celebration / jubilant mood / jubilant fans**

👉 Example: The jubilant fans flooded the streets after their team clinched the championship title.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **jubilant** researchers celebrated their breakthrough discovery, which promised to revolutionize the treatment of autoimmune diseases.
   欢欣鼓舞的研究人员庆祝了他们的突破性发现，该发现有望彻底改变自身免疫性疾病的治疗方式。

2. A **jubilant** atmosphere pervaded the auditorium as the dean announced record-high graduation rates for the entire university.
   当院长宣布全校毕业率创历史新高时，礼堂里弥漫着欢腾的气氛。

3. The citizens were **jubilant** upon learning that the long-disputed treaty had finally been ratified by both parliaments.
   市民们得知长期争议的条约终于被两国议会批准后欢呼雀跃。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jubilant" as related to "jubilee" — a grand celebration, so jubilant means being in a celebratory, joyful state.
把"jubilant"想成与"jubilee（盛大庆典）"相关——所以jubilant意味着处于庆祝的、欢乐的状态。
