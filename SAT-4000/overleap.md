# 🦘 overleap

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈliːp/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To leap over or across; to skip or pass over; to disregard or ignore
**中文：** v. 越过；跳过；忽略

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surpass（超越）
- skip（跳过）
- vault（跃过）
- bypass（绕过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overleap boundaries / overleap obstacles / overleap oneself / overleap conventions**

👉 Example: In his ambition, he overleaped the established procedures and alienated his colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious politician attempted to **overleap** the traditional hierarchy, seeking the highest office without prior governmental experience.
   这位雄心勃勃的政治家试图越过传统的等级制度，在没有政府经验的情况下谋求最高职位。

2. Shakespeare warned that those who **overleap** themselves often fall on the other side of their reckless ambitions.
   莎士比亚警告说，那些过于自负的人往往会因鲁莽的野心而跌倒。

3. The horse gracefully **overleaped** the stone wall and continued galloping across the sunlit meadow without breaking stride.
   那匹马优雅地越过了石墙，继续在阳光普照的草地上飞奔，步伐丝毫不乱。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-leap" literally — to "leap over" something, whether a physical barrier or a step in a process you skip entirely.
把"overleap"直接理解为"跳过去"——无论是跳过一道物理障碍还是跳过一个完整的步骤。
