# 🌱 pea

🔊 /piː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small, round green seed eaten as a vegetable; the climbing plant that produces these seeds
**中文：** n. 豌豆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- legume（豆类）
- pulse（豆子）
- chickpea（鹰嘴豆）
- green pea（青豆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pea soup / sweet peas / like two peas in a pod / pea-sized**

👉 Example: The twins were like two peas in a pod, virtually indistinguishable in both appearance and temperament.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The agricultural study concluded that the **pea** is among the most nutritious and versatile legumes cultivated worldwide.
   该农业研究得出结论，豌豆是全球种植的最有营养且用途最广泛的豆类之一。

2. Gregor Mendel's groundbreaking experiments with the common **pea** plant established the fundamental principles of modern genetics.
   格雷戈尔·孟德尔对普通豌豆植株的开创性实验奠定了现代遗传学的基本原理。

3. The chef prepared a refined **pea** purée garnished with fresh mint to accompany the pan-seared salmon fillet.
   厨师制作了一道精致的豌豆泥，配以新鲜薄荷，搭配香煎三文鱼柳。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pea" as the tiny green ball on your plate — small, round, and always rolling away from your fork.
把"pea"想成盘子里那颗绿色的小圆球——小巧、圆润，总是从你的叉子上滚走。
