# 🧘 equanimity

🔊 /ˌekwəˈnɪməti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Mental calmness and composure, especially in a difficult situation
**中文：** n. 平静；镇定；沉着

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- composure（镇静）
- serenity（平静）
- poise（沉着）
- tranquility（安宁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain equanimity / with equanimity / disturb one's equanimity / remarkable equanimity**

👉 Example: She accepted the unexpected setback with remarkable equanimity and composure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seasoned diplomat maintained her **equanimity** throughout the tense negotiations despite provocative remarks from the opposition.
   这位经验丰富的外交官在整个紧张谈判中保持镇定，尽管遭到对方的挑衅性言论。

2. Practicing meditation daily can help cultivate a deep sense of **equanimity** in the face of life's inevitable challenges.
   每天练习冥想有助于在面对生活中不可避免的挑战时培养深层的平静。

3. His ability to respond to criticism with **equanimity** earned him considerable respect among both colleagues and adversaries.
   他能够以沉着态度回应批评，这为他在同事和对手中赢得了相当的尊重。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "equanimity" as "equa-nimity" — "equa" like equal, "nimity" like animity (spirit). An equal, balanced spirit stays calm.
把"equanimity"想成"equa-nimity"——"equa"如同equal（平等），"nimity"如同animity（心灵）。一颗平衡的心灵保持平静。
