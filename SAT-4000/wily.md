# 🐺 wily

🔊 /ˈwaɪli/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Skilled at gaining an advantage, especially through deceit or cunning; crafty and shrewd
**中文：** adj. 狡猾的；诡计多端的；老谋深算的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cunning（狡猾的）
- crafty（诡计多端的）
- shrewd（精明的）
- sly（狡诈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wily politician / wily fox / wily opponent / wily old veteran**

👉 Example: The wily negotiator anticipated every objection and prepared compelling counterarguments in advance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **wily** merchant devised an intricate scheme to undercut competitors while maintaining an appearance of fair business practices.
   这个老谋深算的商人设计了一个复杂的方案来压低竞争对手的价格，同时保持公平经营的外表。

2. Historians describe the general as a **wily** strategist who consistently outmaneuvered larger and better-equipped opposing forces.
   历史学家将这位将军描述为一位老谋深算的战略家，他不断地以智取胜于规模更大、装备更好的敌军。

3. The **wily** defense attorney exploited procedural technicalities to cast reasonable doubt on the prosecution's seemingly airtight case.
   这位精明的辩护律师利用程序上的技术细节对控方看似无懈可击的案件提出了合理怀疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wily" as "wile" + "y" — full of wiles and tricks, like a wily fox that always outwits the hunter.
把"wily"想成"wile（诡计）+ y"——充满诡计和花招，就像一只狡猾的狐狸总能智胜猎人。
