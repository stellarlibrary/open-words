# 🔥 abandon

🔊 /əˈbændən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A feeling of wild enthusiasm or recklessness. (v.) To give up completely; to desert or leave behind
**中文：** n. 放任；狂热 v. 遗弃；放弃；放纵

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- forsake（抛弃）
- relinquish（放弃）
- desert（遗弃）
- recklessness（放纵）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abandon hope / abandon a plan / abandon ship / with reckless abandon**

👉 Example: The survivors were forced to abandon ship when the hull began taking on water.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government decided to **abandon** the controversial policy after widespread public protests erupted across the nation.
   政府在全国范围内爆发大规模公众抗议后决定放弃这项有争议的政策。

2. She danced with wild **abandon** at the festival, completely free from self-consciousness or restraint.
   她在节日上纵情起舞，完全没有任何拘束或顾虑。

3. Researchers were reluctant to **abandon** the experiment despite months of inconclusive and disappointing results.
   尽管数月来结果不确定且令人失望，研究人员仍不愿放弃这项实验。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-ban-don" as "a ban is done" — when a ban is done, you let go of all restrictions and abandon control.
把"abandon"想成"a ban is done"——当禁令结束时，你放开所有限制，放弃控制。
