# 💥 collapse

🔊 /kəˈlæps/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To fall down or cave in suddenly; to break down or fail completely; to drop sharply in value. (n.) A sudden falling down or failure; a breakdown
**中文：** v. 倒塌；崩溃；虚脱；暴跌 n. 倒塌；崩溃；失败

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crumble（崩塌）
- disintegrate（瓦解）
- implode（内爆）
- breakdown（崩溃）
- downfall（衰落）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **collapse under pressure / economic collapse / collapse of a building / collapse from exhaustion**

👉 Example: The aging bridge could collapse under the weight of heavy commercial traffic if repairs are not made soon.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The abandoned warehouse began to **collapse** after decades of structural neglect and exposure to severe weather conditions.
   这座废弃的仓库在几十年的结构疏于维护和恶劣天气侵蚀后开始倒塌。

2. The sudden **collapse** of the financial markets in 2008 triggered a devastating global recession that affected millions of workers.
   2008年金融市场的突然崩溃引发了一场毁灭性的全球经济衰退，影响了数百万工人。

3. The marathon runner **collapsed** from heat exhaustion just meters before the finish line and required immediate medical attention.
   这位马拉松选手在距终点线仅几米处因中暑虚脱而倒地，需要立即接受医疗救治。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "col-lapse" — when all the "laps" (layers) come together ("col-") and fall inward, everything collapses.
把"collapse"想成"col-lapse"——当所有的层次("laps")一起("col-")向内坍陷时，一切都崩塌了。
