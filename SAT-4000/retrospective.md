# 🖼️ retrospective

🔊 /ˌrɛtrəˈspɛktɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Looking back on or dealing with past events; applying to the past. (n.) An exhibition or compilation showing the development of an artist's work over time
**中文：** adj. 回顾的；怀旧的；可追溯的 n. 回顾展

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reflective（反思的）
- backward-looking（回顾的）
- review（回顾）
- retroactive（追溯的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retrospective exhibition / retrospective analysis / retrospective study / retrospective look**

👉 Example: The museum hosted a comprehensive retrospective of the photographer's most influential works spanning five decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The gallery organized a magnificent **retrospective** showcasing the evolution of the sculptor's artistic vision from early works to recent masterpieces.
   画廊举办了一场精彩的回顾展，展示了这位雕塑家从早期作品到近期杰作的艺术视野演变。

2. A thorough **retrospective** analysis of the clinical trial data revealed previously overlooked correlations between dosage and patient outcomes.
   对临床试验数据的全面回顾性分析揭示了此前被忽视的剂量与患者疗效之间的关联。

3. The director's **retrospective** commentary on his early films provided audiences with valuable insight into his creative development.
   导演对其早期电影的回顾性评论为观众提供了关于其创作发展的宝贵见解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "retrospective" as a "retro perspective" — a perspective that looks backward in time, like a museum exhibit of an artist's entire career.
把"retrospective"想成"复古的视角"——一个回望过去的视角，就像博物馆展出一位艺术家整个职业生涯的展览。
