# 💞 affinity

🔊 /əˈfɪnəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A natural liking, attraction, or feeling of kinship; a close relationship or resemblance.
**中文：** 密切关系；吸引力；姻亲关系；类同

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attraction（吸引力）
- kinship（亲缘关系）
- similarity（相似性）
- sympathy（共鸣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **affinity for, affinity with, affinity between, natural affinity**
👉 Example: She felt a strong affinity for classical music from an early age.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young scientist demonstrated a remarkable **affinity** for complex mathematical theories throughout her studies.
   这位年轻科学家在整个学习过程中展现出对复杂数学理论的非凡亲和力。

2. There is a clear **affinity** between the two literary traditions that scholars have long recognized.
   学者们早已认识到这两种文学传统之间存在明显的类同。

3. His natural **affinity** with animals made him an excellent candidate for the veterinary program.
   他与动物之间的天然亲近感使他成为兽医项目的优秀候选人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Affinity" sounds like "a-fine-ity" — when you have affinity for something, you think it is fine and wonderful, drawing you closer to it.
"Affinity"听起来像"a-fine-ity"——当你对某事物有亲近感时，你觉得它很好很棒，因此被吸引过去。
