# 💪 intrepid

🔊 /ɪnˈtrɛpɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Fearless and adventurous; showing no fear in the face of danger or difficulty
**中文：** adj. 无畏的；勇敢的；勇猛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fearless（无畏的）
- dauntless（勇敢的）
- valiant（英勇的）
- audacious（大胆的）
- courageous（勇敢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **intrepid explorer / intrepid adventurer / intrepid spirit / intrepid leader**

👉 Example: The intrepid explorer ventured deep into uncharted territory despite warnings of extreme danger.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **intrepid** journalist risked her life to report from the war zone and expose the hidden atrocities.
   这位无畏的记者冒着生命危险从战区发回报道，揭露了隐藏的暴行。

2. Only the most **intrepid** mountaineers attempt to summit the peak during the treacherous winter months.
   只有最勇敢的登山者才会在危险的冬季尝试登顶。

3. Her **intrepid** determination to challenge the established scientific consensus ultimately led to a groundbreaking discovery.
   她无畏地挑战既有科学共识的决心最终促成了一项突破性发现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-trepid" as "in" + "trepidation" (fear) — someone who is NOT in trepidation, meaning they are fearless.
把"intrepid"想成"in（不）+ trepidation（恐惧）"——一个没有恐惧的人，即无畏的人。
