# 💧 dilute

🔊 /daɪˈluːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Weakened or thinned by the addition of water or another solvent. (v.) To make a liquid thinner or weaker by adding water or another substance; to diminish the strength or effect of something
**中文：** adj. 稀释的；冲淡的 v. 稀释；冲淡；削弱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- weaken（削弱）
- thin（稀释）
- attenuate（减弱）
- water down（冲淡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dilute a solution / dilute the impact / dilute with water / diluted form / dilute the message**

👉 Example: Adding too many exceptions to the policy would dilute its overall effectiveness and enforcement.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist was instructed to **dilute** the concentrated acid with distilled water before conducting the series of laboratory experiments.
   化学家被要求在进行一系列实验室实验之前，用蒸馏水稀释浓酸。

2. Critics argued that the proposed amendments would **dilute** the original legislation and render its key provisions virtually meaningless.
   批评者认为，拟议的修正案将削弱原始立法，使其关键条款几乎毫无意义。

3. The company decided to **dilute** its shares through a secondary offering to raise additional capital for international expansion plans.
   公司决定通过二次发行稀释其股份，以筹集额外资金用于国际扩张计划。

---

把"dilute"想成"di-LUTE"——想象在雨中弹琵琶，雨水冲淡了声音，使其变得微弱而单薄。
