# 🔄 switch

🔊 /swɪtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To change over; to whip with a switch. (n.) A control device; a change; a flexible rod
**中文：** n. 开关

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- toggle（切换）
- change（改变）
- swap（交换）
- shift（转换）
- convert（转换）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **switch on/off / switch between / make the switch / switch roles / switch tactics**

👉 Example: The company decided to switch from traditional marketing to a digital advertising strategy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university chose to **switch** its entire curriculum to an online format in response to the rapidly changing educational landscape.
   大学选择将整个课程转换为在线形式，以应对快速变化的教育环境。

2. Analysts observed that many consumers began to **switch** their loyalty to brands that demonstrated genuine environmental responsibility.
   分析师观察到许多消费者开始转向那些展现出真正环保责任感的品牌。

3. The sudden **switch** in governmental policy left numerous businesses struggling to adapt to the new regulatory requirements.
   政策的突然转变使众多企业难以适应新的监管要求。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of flipping a light "switch" — one quick motion and everything changes from one state to another.
想想拨动灯的"开关"——一个快速的动作，一切就从一种状态变为另一种状态。
