# 🌐 network

🔊 /ˈnɛtwɜːrk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An interconnected system of things or people; a group of broadcasting stations; an arrangement of intersecting lines or channels
**中文：** n. 网络；广播网；电视网；网状物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- system（系统）
- web（网）
- grid（网格）
- matrix（矩阵）
- framework（框架）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social network / neural network / network of contacts / broadcast network / computer network**

👉 Example: Building a professional network requires consistent effort and genuine interest in maintaining meaningful relationships.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university has established an extensive **network** of research partnerships with leading academic institutions across three continents.
   该大学已与三大洲的顶尖学术机构建立了广泛的研究合作网络。

2. The national broadcasting **network** announced that it would restructure its programming schedule to accommodate growing viewer demand for content.
   该国家广播网宣布将调整节目时间表，以满足观众日益增长的内容需求。

3. Scientists have mapped the intricate **network** of neural connections in the human brain to better understand cognitive and behavioral processes.
   科学家们已绘制出人脑中复杂的神经连接网络，以更好地理解认知和行为过程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "net" + "work" — a net that works by connecting many points together, forming an interconnected web of links.
把"network"想成"net"(网) + "work"(运作)——一张通过连接许多点而运作的网，形成相互关联的链接之网。
