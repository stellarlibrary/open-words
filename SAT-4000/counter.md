# 🔄 counter

🔊 /ˈkaʊntər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, adjective | 名词, 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A flat surface for conducting business; a device for counting. (v.) To respond in opposition; to strike back. (adj.) Contrary or opposite in direction
**中文：** n. 柜台；计数器 v. 反击，还击 adj. 相反的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oppose（反对）
- rebut（反驳）
- offset（抵消）
- contrary（相反的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **counter an argument / run counter to / counter a threat / over the counter**

👉 Example: The senator attempted to counter the opposition's claims with a detailed statistical analysis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The spokesperson issued a statement to **counter** the misleading allegations that had been circulating widely in the media.
   发言人发表声明以反驳媒体上广泛流传的误导性指控。

2. His optimistic assessment of the economy ran **counter** to the cautious projections offered by most independent financial analysts.
   他对经济的乐观评估与大多数独立金融分析师给出的谨慎预测相反。

3. The military strategist devised an effective plan to **counter** the enemy's unexpected advance along the northern border.
   军事战略家制定了一项有效计划来反击敌人沿北部边境的意外推进。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "counter" as "count + er" — something that counts, or someone who goes against (counts against) another's position.
把"counter"想成"count（计数/对抗）+ er"——计数的东西，或者对抗别人立场的行为。
