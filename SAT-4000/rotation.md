# 🔄 rotation

🔊 /roʊˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The action of rotating around an axis or center; a cycle or regular succession of events
**中文：** n. 转动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revolution（旋转）
- turning（转动）
- cycle（循环）
- alternation（轮流）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crop rotation / rotation of duties / in rotation / axis of rotation**

👉 Example: The committee assigns leadership positions on a rotation basis to ensure fairness among members.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Earth completes one full **rotation** on its axis approximately every twenty-four hours, creating day and night.
   地球大约每二十四小时绕自身轴线完成一次自转，从而形成昼夜交替。

2. The hospital adopted a strict staff **rotation** policy to prevent burnout among its overworked medical professionals.
   医院采取了严格的员工轮换制度，以防止过度劳累的医务人员产生职业倦怠。

3. Farmers have long practiced crop **rotation** to maintain soil fertility and reduce the prevalence of plant diseases.
   农民长期以来实行轮作制度，以保持土壤肥力并减少植物病害的发生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rotation" as "rotate + ion" — picture a wheel rotating around and around in a continuous cycle.
把"rotation"想成"rotate（旋转）+ ion（名词后缀）"——想象一个轮子不断地转动循环。
