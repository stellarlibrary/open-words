# 🌀 subliminal

🔊 /sʌbˈlɪmɪnəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Below the threshold of conscious perception; not strong enough to be consciously noticed. (n.) A subconscious stimulus or message
**中文：** adj. 下意识的；潜在意识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- subconscious（潜意识的）
- unconscious（无意识的）
- imperceptible（难以察觉的）
- hidden（隐藏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subliminal message / subliminal advertising / subliminal influence / subliminal perception**

👉 Example: The documentary explored how subliminal messages embedded in advertisements can subtly influence consumer purchasing decisions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers conducted experiments to determine whether **subliminal** visual cues could measurably alter participants' decision-making behavior in controlled settings.
   研究人员进行了实验，以确定潜意识视觉线索是否能在受控环境中可观测地改变参与者的决策行为。

2. The regulatory commission banned the use of **subliminal** advertising techniques, arguing that they constitute an unethical manipulation of consumers.
   监管委员会禁止使用潜意识广告技术，认为它们构成对消费者的不道德操控。

3. Many psychologists remain skeptical about the effectiveness of **subliminal** persuasion, citing insufficient empirical evidence to support its claims.
   许多心理学家对潜意识说服的有效性持怀疑态度，认为缺乏足够的实证证据来支持其主张。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sub-liminal" as "sub" (below) + "limen" (threshold) — a message below the threshold of your awareness, sneaking past your conscious mind.
把"subliminal"想成"sub"（在下面）+"limen"（门槛）——一条低于你意识门槛的信息，悄悄绕过你的显意识。
