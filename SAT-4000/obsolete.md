# 🗑️ obsolete

🔊 /ˌɑːbsəˈliːt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb | 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) No longer in use or practice; outdated; replaced by something newer. (v.) To cause to become outdated or replace with something more current
**中文：** adj. 废弃的；老式的；过时的 v. 淘汰；废弃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outdated（过时的）
- antiquated（陈旧的）
- archaic（古老的）
- defunct（废止的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **become obsolete / render obsolete / obsolete technology / obsolete terms**

👉 Example: The rapid advancement of smartphones rendered many older devices completely obsolete.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The introduction of digital streaming services has rendered physical media formats such as DVDs increasingly **obsolete** worldwide.
   数字流媒体服务的引入使得DVD等实体媒体格式在全球范围内日益过时。

2. Military strategists acknowledged that several conventional defense systems had become **obsolete** in the era of advanced cyber warfare.
   军事战略家承认，在先进网络战争时代，几种传统防御系统已经过时。

3. The legislature voted to repeal the **obsolete** statute that had remained unenforced on the books for over half a century.
   立法机构投票废除了这项在法典上已有半个多世纪未被执行的过时法规。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "obsolete" as "OB-so-LETE" — "oh, so late!" — something that arrived so late to the party that everyone has already moved on to newer things.
把"obsolete"想成"OB-so-LETE"——"oh, so late!"（哦，太迟了！）——某物来得太晚了，大家都已经转向更新的东西了。
