# 🔍 vigilant

🔊 /ˈvɪdʒɪlənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties; alert and watchful
**中文：** adj. 警惕的；警醒的；注意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alert（警觉的）
- watchful（警惕的）
- attentive（注意的）
- wary（小心的）
- observant（观察力敏锐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **remain vigilant / stay vigilant / vigilant against threats / vigilant eye / ever vigilant**

👉 Example: Citizens must remain vigilant against attempts to undermine fundamental democratic principles.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The security personnel remained **vigilant** throughout the diplomatic summit, carefully screening every individual who entered the building.
   安保人员在整个外交峰会期间保持警惕，仔细检查每一位进入大楼的人员。

2. Parents should be **vigilant** about monitoring their children's online activities to protect them from potential exploitation.
   家长应对监控孩子的在线活动保持警惕，以保护他们免受潜在侵害。

3. A **vigilant** press corps serves as an essential check on governmental power by exposing corruption and malfeasance.
   警觉的新闻媒体通过揭露腐败和渎职行为，对政府权力起着至关重要的制衡作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vigilant" as a "vigil-ant" — an ant that keeps vigil, always alert and watching for threats to protect the colony.
把"vigilant"想成"vigil-ant"——一只守夜的蚂蚁，始终保持警觉，注视着威胁以保护蚁群。
