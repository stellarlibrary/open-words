# ⚖️ mass

🔊 /mæs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective, verb | 名词, 形容词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A large body of matter with no definite shape; the quantity of matter in an object; a large number or amount. (adj.) Relating to or involving a large number of people. (v.) To gather together in a mass or as a mass
**中文：** n. 块，团；群众；大量；质量 adj. 大规模的 v. 聚集

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bulk（大量）
- aggregate（集合体）
- multitude（大量；众多）
- accumulation（积累）
- throng（人群）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **mass production / mass media / critical mass / mass of evidence / the masses**

👉 Example: The factory shifted to mass production in order to meet the rapidly growing demand for consumer goods.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist calculated the **mass** of the particle using advanced instrumentation and confirmed the theoretical predictions of the model.
   物理学家使用先进仪器计算了粒子的质量，并确认了该模型的理论预测。

2. A **mass** of protesters gathered outside the parliament building to demand immediate reforms to the electoral system.
   大批抗议者聚集在议会大楼外，要求立即改革选举制度。

3. The corporation launched a **mass** marketing campaign designed to reach millions of consumers across multiple international markets.
   该公司发起了一场大规模营销活动，旨在覆盖多个国际市场的数百万消费者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mass" as a massive gathering — a large lump of matter or a huge crowd, everything coming together in one big bulk.
把"mass"想成一次大规模的聚集——一大块物质或一大群人，所有东西汇聚成一个大团。
