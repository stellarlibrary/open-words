# 🔢 algebra

🔊 /ˈældʒɪbrə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A branch of mathematics dealing with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols to solve equations
**中文：** 代数学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mathematics（数学）
- calculus（微积分）
- arithmetic（算术）
- computation（计算）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **linear algebra / algebra class / algebraic equation / study algebra / Boolean algebra**
👉 Example: A strong foundation in algebra is essential for students pursuing advanced studies in engineering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor emphasized that mastering **algebra** is a fundamental prerequisite for understanding higher-level mathematical concepts.
   教授强调掌握代数是理解高等数学概念的基本前提。

2. Many students find **algebra** challenging at first, yet it becomes intuitive with consistent practice and application.
   许多学生起初觉得代数很有挑战性，但通过持续的练习和应用，它会变得直观。

3. The development of **algebra** by medieval Islamic scholars laid the groundwork for modern scientific and technological advancement.
   中世纪伊斯兰学者对代数的发展为现代科学和技术进步奠定了基础。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Algebra" comes from Arabic "al-jabr" meaning "reunion of broken parts" — just as algebra reunites unknowns with their values.
"Algebra"源自阿拉伯语"al-jabr"，意为"破碎部分的重新组合"——正如代数将未知数与其值重新联系起来。
