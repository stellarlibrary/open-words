# ✨ myriad

🔊 /ˈmɪriəd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Countless or extremely great in number. (n.) An indefinitely large number of things; originally, ten thousand
**中文：** adj. 无数的；种种的 n. 无数，极大数量；无数的人或物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- countless（无数的）
- innumerable（数不清的）
- multitude（大量）
- plethora（过多）
- legion（大批）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a myriad of / myriad possibilities / myriad challenges / myriad forms**

👉 Example: The tropical rainforest supports a myriad of species, many of which remain undiscovered by scientists.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university offers **myriad** opportunities for students to engage in research, community service, and international exchange programs.
   该大学为学生提供了无数参与研究、社区服务和国际交流项目的机会。

2. Astronomers continue to discover a **myriad** of exoplanets orbiting distant stars, each with unique atmospheric compositions.
   天文学家不断发现围绕遥远恒星运行的无数系外行星，每颗都有独特的大气成分。

3. The city's cultural heritage is reflected in the **myriad** architectural styles that span centuries of diverse historical influence.
   这座城市的文化遗产体现在跨越数百年不同历史影响的无数建筑风格之中。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "myriad" as "my + r.i" (really infinite) — there are so many things it feels really infinite, like an uncountable number.
把"myriad"想成"my + r.i.（really infinite，真的无限）"——事物多得让人感觉真的无穷无尽，像是数不清的数字。
