# 🌍 tour

🔊 /tʊr/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A journey for pleasure or education visiting several places; a series of performances in different locations. (v.) To travel through or make a tour of a place
**中文：** n. 旅游；巡回演出；参观 v. 旅行；巡回

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- journey（旅程）
- excursion（远足）
- expedition（远征）
- voyage（航行）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **guided tour / tour of duty / concert tour / go on a tour / tour the facilities**

👉 Example: The ambassador was given a comprehensive tour of the newly constructed research facility.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished author embarked on a nationwide book **tour** to promote her critically acclaimed latest novel.
   这位著名作家开始了全国巡回签售**旅行**，以推广她广受好评的最新小说。

2. Foreign diplomats were invited to **tour** the advanced manufacturing plant and observe its innovative production methods.
   外国外交官受邀**参观**这座先进的制造工厂并观摩其创新生产方法。

3. The orchestra completed a successful European **tour**, performing in twelve prestigious concert halls over three weeks.
   管弦乐队完成了成功的欧洲**巡回演出**，在三周内于十二个著名音乐厅献艺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tour" as related to "turn" — you turn through many places, visiting one after another in a circular journey.
把"tour"和"turn（转）"联系起来——你在旅程中转遍各个地方，一个接一个地参观。
