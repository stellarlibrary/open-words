# 🔥 conflagration

🔊 /ˌkɒnfləˈɡreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large, destructive fire that spreads across a wide area; a sudden and violent outbreak or conflict
**中文：** n. 大火，大火灾；突发的冲突

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inferno（地狱般的大火）
- blaze（烈火）
- holocaust（大火灾）
- wildfire（野火）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a raging conflagration / massive conflagration / conflagration of war**

👉 Example: The conflagration consumed several city blocks before firefighters could bring it under control.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **conflagration** that erupted in the industrial district destroyed dozens of warehouses and displaced hundreds of residents.
   工业区爆发的大火摧毁了数十座仓库，使数百名居民流离失所。

2. Historians regard the conflict as a political **conflagration** that fundamentally reshaped the balance of power in Europe.
   历史学家将这场冲突视为从根本上重塑了欧洲权力格局的政治大爆发。

3. Without adequate firefighting resources, the small brushfire quickly escalated into an uncontrollable **conflagration** across the valley.
   由于缺乏足够的消防资源，小型灌木火迅速升级为蔓延整个山谷的不可控大火。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-FLAG-ration" — imagine flags catching fire, and the flames spreading into a massive conflagration.
把"conflagration"想成"con-FLAG-ration"——想象旗帜着火，火焰蔓延成一场大火。
