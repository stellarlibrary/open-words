# 🔄 diversify

🔊 /daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make or become more varied or diverse; to enlarge the range of products, operations, or investments
**中文：** v. 使多样化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vary（使变化）
- broaden（扩展）
- expand（扩大）
- branch out（拓展）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **diversify investments / diversify the economy / diversify a portfolio / diversify into**

👉 Example: Financial advisors consistently recommend that investors diversify their portfolios to minimize overall risk.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company decided to **diversify** its product line in order to reduce dependence on a single revenue stream.
   公司决定使其产品线多样化，以减少对单一收入来源的依赖。

2. Developing nations are urged to **diversify** their economies beyond natural resource extraction to achieve long-term sustainable growth.
   发展中国家被敦促使经济多样化，超越自然资源开采，以实现长期可持续增长。

3. The university sought to **diversify** its faculty by actively recruiting scholars from underrepresented backgrounds and academic disciplines.
   该大学通过积极招聘来自代表性不足背景和学术领域的学者来使其教职队伍多元化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "divers-ify" — to make things like "divers" (various kinds), adding the suffix "-ify" meaning "to make."
把"diversify"想成"divers(各种各样)-ify(使……化)"——使事物变得各种各样，即多样化。
