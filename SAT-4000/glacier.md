# 🏔️ glacier

🔊 /ˈɡleɪʃər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large, slow-moving mass of ice formed from compacted snow on mountains or near the poles
**中文：** n. 冰河，冰川

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ice sheet（冰盖）
- ice field（冰原）
- ice cap（冰帽）
- iceberg（冰山）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glacier retreat / glacier melt / glacier formation / alpine glacier**

👉 Example: Scientists have documented the rapid retreat of the glacier over the past two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **glacier** has retreated more than two kilometers in the past decade due to rising global temperatures.
   由于全球气温上升，该冰川在过去十年中后退了两公里以上。

2. Researchers extracted ice cores from the ancient **glacier** to analyze atmospheric composition spanning thousands of years.
   研究人员从古老的冰川中提取冰芯，以分析跨越数千年的大气成分。

3. The melting **glacier** has created a new freshwater lake that now sustains diverse aquatic ecosystems in the valley.
   融化的冰川形成了一个新的淡水湖，现在维持着山谷中多样的水生生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "glacier" as "glass + ear" — a glacier is like a giant ear of glass, smooth and frozen, listening to centuries of silence.
把"glacier"想成"glass（玻璃）+ ear（耳朵）"——冰川像一只巨大的玻璃耳朵，光滑而冰冻，聆听着数百年的沉寂。
