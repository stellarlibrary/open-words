# 🎨 creation

🔊 /kriˈeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act or process of bringing something into existence; a thing that has been made or invented, especially something showing artistic talent
**中文：** n. 创造；创作；产生；创作物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- invention（发明）
- production（产物）
- conception（构想）
- origination（创始）
- masterpiece（杰作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the creation of / artistic creation / job creation / creation of wealth / literary creation**

👉 Example: The creation of the national park system represented a landmark achievement in environmental conservation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **creation** of a unified legal framework was essential for fostering international cooperation on climate change mitigation.
   建立统一的法律框架对于促进应对气候变化的国际合作至关重要。

2. Her latest artistic **creation** drew widespread acclaim from critics who praised its bold originality and emotional depth.
   她最新的艺术创作赢得了评论家们的广泛赞誉，他们称赞其大胆的独创性和情感深度。

3. The **creation** of new educational institutions in underserved regions has significantly improved access to higher learning opportunities.
   在服务不足地区创建新的教育机构极大地改善了获得高等教育机会的途径。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "creation" as "create + ion" — the action (ion) of creating something new that did not exist before.
把"creation"想成"create + ion"——创造某种以前不存在的新事物的行为（ion）。
