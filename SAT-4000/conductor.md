# 🎵 conductor

🔊 /kənˈdʌktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A material or object that transmits heat, electricity, or sound; a person who directs an orchestra or choir; a person who collects fares on a bus or train.

**中文：** 导体；指挥（乐队）；售票员；领导者。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- transmitter（传导器）
- director（指挥者）
- leader（领导者）
- maestro（大师；指挥家）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** electrical conductor / orchestra conductor / conductor of heat

**Example:** Copper is an excellent **conductor** of electricity and is widely used in industrial wiring.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned **conductor** led the symphony orchestra through a masterful performance of the classical repertoire.
   这位著名的指挥家带领交响乐团演奏了一场精彩的古典曲目表演。

2. Metals such as copper and silver are highly efficient **conductors** of both heat and electrical current.
   铜和银等金属是高效的热和电流导体。

3. The train **conductor** verified each passenger's ticket before the locomotive departed from the central station.
   列车售票员在机车从中央车站出发前核实了每位乘客的车票。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Conduct" (to lead or carry) + "-or" (one who does) — a conductor is someone or something that leads, carries, or transmits.

**中文：** "conduct"（引导、传导）+ "-or"（做…的人/物）——conductor 就是引导、传导的人或物，即"导体、指挥、售票员"。
