# 🕊️ amicable

🔊 /ˈæmɪkəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Characterized by friendliness and goodwill; done in a peaceable and cooperative spirit.
**中文：** 友好的；友善的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cordial（友好的）
- harmonious（和谐的）
- peaceable（和平的）
- congenial（友善的）
- civil（文明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **settlement, agreement, divorce, resolution, relationship, parting**
👉 Example: The two nations reached an amicable agreement after months of diplomatic talks.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After extensive mediation, the disputing parties reached an **amicable** settlement without proceeding to litigation.
   经过广泛的调解，争议双方达成了友好和解，未进入诉讼程序。

2. The former business partners arranged an **amicable** dissolution of their company and divided assets fairly.
   前商业伙伴友好地安排了公司的解散，并公平地分配了资产。

3. Maintaining **amicable** relations between neighboring countries is essential for sustained regional stability and prosperity.
   维护邻国之间的友好关系对于持续的地区稳定与繁荣至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Amicable" comes from Latin "amicus" meaning "friend" — an amicable interaction is one between friends, not enemies.
"Amicable"源自拉丁语"amicus"（朋友）——友好的互动是朋友之间的，而非敌人之间的。
