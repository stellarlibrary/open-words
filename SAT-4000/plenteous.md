# 🌻 plenteous

🔊 /ˈplɛntɪəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Existing in great quantity; abundant; yielding or producing in large amounts
**中文：** adj. 许多的，丰饶的，充足的，丰富的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundant（丰富的）
- copious（大量的）
- bountiful（慷慨的）
- ample（充足的）
- plentiful（丰富的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plenteous supply / plenteous harvest / plenteous resources / plenteous bounty**

👉 Example: The farmers celebrated a plenteous harvest after months of favorable weather conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The expedition discovered a **plenteous** supply of fresh water, ensuring the survival of the entire team for weeks.
   探险队发现了充足的淡水供应，确保了整个团队数周的生存。

2. The nation's **plenteous** natural reserves of minerals and timber fueled its rapid industrial growth throughout the decade.
   该国丰饶的矿物和木材自然储备推动了其整个十年的快速工业增长。

3. Scholars in the medieval period had access to **plenteous** manuscripts that preserved the philosophical wisdom of antiquity.
   中世纪的学者们能够接触到大量保存了古代哲学智慧的手稿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plenteous" as "plenty-ous" — simply meaning full of plenty, overflowing with abundance like a cornucopia.
把"plenteous"想成"plenty-ous"——简单地说就是充满了"plenty（大量）"，像丰饶之角一样溢满富足。
