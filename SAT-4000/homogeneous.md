# 🔬 homogeneous

🔊 /ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of the same kind or nature; uniform in structure or composition throughout
**中文：** adj. 均匀的；齐次的；同种的；同性质的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- uniform（均匀的）
- consistent（一致的）
- identical（相同的）
- alike（相似的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **homogeneous mixture / homogeneous population / homogeneous group / culturally homogeneous**

👉 Example: The researcher prepared a homogeneous solution by stirring the compound until fully dissolved.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee sought a more **homogeneous** set of criteria to ensure fairness and consistency across all evaluations.
   委员会寻求一套更加统一的标准，以确保所有评估的公平性和一致性。

2. Critics argued that the neighborhood had become too **homogeneous**, lacking the cultural diversity that once defined it.
   批评者认为该社区已变得过于同质化，缺乏曾经赋予其特色的文化多样性。

3. A **homogeneous** mixture displays identical properties in every sample regardless of where it is taken from.
   均匀混合物在任何取样处都表现出相同的性质，无论从哪里取样。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homo-" (same) + "-geneous" (kind) — everything is of the same kind, uniform and identical throughout.
把"homogeneous"拆成"homo-"（相同）+"geneous"（种类）——所有东西都是同一种类，始终均匀一致。
