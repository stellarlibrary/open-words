# 🏰 chateau

🔊 /ʃæˈtoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large French country house or castle, often one that produces wine; a grand estate or mansion
**中文：** n. 城堡；别墅

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- castle（城堡）
- mansion（豪宅）
- estate（庄园）
- palace（宫殿）
- manor（庄园宅邸）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **French chateau / chateau wine / chateau estate / visit a chateau**

👉 Example: The wine was produced at a renowned chateau in the Bordeaux region of France.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The aristocratic family had maintained their ancestral **chateau** in the Loire Valley for over three centuries.
   这个贵族家庭在卢瓦尔河谷维护他们的祖传城堡已经超过三个世纪。

2. Tourists from around the world travel to France each year to visit the magnificent **chateaux** of the countryside.
   来自世界各地的游客每年都前往法国参观乡间宏伟的城堡。

3. The diplomat hosted the international summit at a secluded **chateau** surrounded by vineyards and rolling hills.
   外交官在一座被葡萄园和连绵山丘环绕的僻静别墅中主持了国际峰会。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chat-eau" as "chat in a castle" — imagine chatting with friends in a grand French castle over wine.
把"chateau"想成"在城堡里聊天（chat）"——想象在一座宏伟的法国城堡里与朋友边品酒边聊天。
