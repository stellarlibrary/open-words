# ⚖️ regulate

🔊 /ˈrɛɡjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To control or direct according to rule, principle, or law; to adjust for accuracy or proper functioning; to maintain order or consistency.
**中文：** v. 管理，控制，使遵守规章；调节，调整，校准

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- control（控制）
- govern（管理）
- adjust（调整）
- supervise（监管）
- moderate（调节）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regulate the market / regulate temperature / regulate behavior / strictly regulated**

👉 Example: The government introduced new legislation to regulate the rapidly expanding financial technology sector.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Federal agencies were established to **regulate** the pharmaceutical industry and ensure that all medications meet rigorous safety standards.
   联邦机构的成立旨在监管制药行业，确保所有药品符合严格的安全标准。

2. The thermostat was designed to **regulate** indoor temperature automatically, maintaining a comfortable environment throughout the year.
   恒温器被设计为自动调节室内温度，全年保持舒适的环境。

3. International organizations seek to **regulate** cross-border trade agreements to promote fairness and prevent exploitative economic practices.
   国际组织致力于规范跨境贸易协议，以促进公平并防止剥削性的经济行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "regulate" as "rule + gate" — like a gate that enforces rules, controlling what passes through and how.
把"regulate"想成"rule（规则）+ gate（门）"——就像一扇执行规则的门，控制着什么可以通过以及如何通过。
