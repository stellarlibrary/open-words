# 🚫 embargo

🔊 /ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country. (v.) To impose an official ban on trade or to prohibit the movement of ships in and out of ports
**中文：** n. 禁令；禁运 v. 禁止出入港口；禁止通商

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ban（禁令）
- prohibition（禁止）
- sanction（制裁）
- blockade（封锁）
- restriction（限制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impose an embargo / lift an embargo / trade embargo / arms embargo / embargo on exports**

👉 Example: The United Nations voted to impose an embargo on weapons shipments to the conflict zone.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The international community decided to impose a strict **embargo** on oil exports to pressure the regime into diplomatic negotiations.
   国际社会决定对石油出口实施严格禁运，以迫使该政权进行外交谈判。

2. The government chose to **embargo** all shipments of military equipment to the neighboring country until the border dispute was resolved.
   政府决定禁止向邻国运送所有军事装备，直到边界争端得到解决。

3. Economists warned that the prolonged trade **embargo** would devastate the nation's economy and harm millions of ordinary citizens.
   经济学家警告说，长期的贸易禁运将摧毁该国的经济，并损害数百万普通公民的利益。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "embargo" as "em-BAR-go" — imagine a bar blocking ships from going. An embargo bars goods from moving in or out.
把"embargo"想成"em-BAR-go"——想象一根横杆挡住船只出入。禁运就是阻止货物的进出。
