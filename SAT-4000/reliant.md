# 🔗 reliant

🔊 /rɪˈlaɪənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Dependent on someone or something for aid, support, or supply; having trust or confidence in
**中文：** adj. 依赖的；信赖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dependent（依赖的）
- contingent（取决于的）
- trusting（信赖的）
- counting on（依靠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reliant on / reliant upon / heavily reliant / self-reliant / overly reliant**

👉 Example: Many developing nations remain heavily reliant on foreign aid to fund essential public services.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern agriculture has become increasingly **reliant** on chemical fertilizers, raising serious concerns about long-term soil degradation.
   现代农业越来越依赖化肥，引发了对长期土壤退化的严重担忧。

2. The research team was entirely **reliant** on government grants, making their continued work uncertain during periods of budget cuts.
   该研究团队完全依赖政府拨款，使他们在预算削减期间的持续工作变得不确定。

3. Students who are overly **reliant** on memorization often struggle with analytical questions that require deeper conceptual understanding.
   过度依赖记忆的学生在需要更深层概念理解的分析性问题上往往表现不佳。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reliant" as "rely-ant" — an ant that relies on its colony, never working alone but always depending on the group.
把"reliant"想成"rely-ant"——一只依赖蚁群的蚂蚁，从不独自工作，总是依靠群体。
