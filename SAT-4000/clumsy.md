# 🤕 clumsy

🔊 /ˈklʌmzi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Awkward in movement or handling; lacking grace, coordination, or dexterity; done without skill or tact
**中文：** adj. 笨拙的；不灵巧的；愚笨的；不得体的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- awkward（笨拙的）
- ungainly（不雅的）
- maladroit（笨拙的）
- inept（无能的）
- bumbling（笨手笨脚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **clumsy attempt / clumsy fingers / clumsy handling / clumsy movement / clumsy apology**

👉 Example: His clumsy attempt to explain the discrepancy only deepened the committee's suspicion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's **clumsy** remarks during the press conference inadvertently escalated tensions between the two nations.
   这位外交官在新闻发布会上笨拙的言论无意中加剧了两国之间的紧张关系。

2. The intern made a **clumsy** effort to repair the database, which unfortunately resulted in further data corruption.
   实习生笨手笨脚地试图修复数据库，不幸导致了进一步的数据损坏。

3. Her **clumsy** handling of the fragile antique vase caused it to shatter into countless irreparable pieces.
   她笨拙地处理那个易碎的古董花瓶，导致它碎成了无数无法修复的碎片。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clumsy" as "clump-sy" — someone who moves in clumps, heavy and uncoordinated, stumbling through every task.
把"clumsy"想成"clump（笨重地行走）+ sy"——一个行动笨重、不协调、做什么都磕磕绊绊的人。
