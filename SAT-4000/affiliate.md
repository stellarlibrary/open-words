# 🤝 affiliate

🔊 /əˈfɪliˌeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词 · verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A subsidiary organization or branch associated with a larger body; (v.) to officially attach or connect to an organization.
**中文：** （名词）联号；隶属机构；（动词）使附属；接纳。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- associate（关联机构）
- subsidiary（子公司）
- branch（分支）
- partner（合作伙伴）
- ally（盟友）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **closely, with, local, network, program**
👉 Example: The local station operates as an affiliate of the national broadcasting network.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company decided to **affiliate** itself with a prestigious international organization to expand its global presence.
   该公司决定使自己隶属于一个声誉卓著的国际组织，以扩大其全球影响力。

2. Each regional **affiliate** operates independently while adhering to the standards established by the parent corporation.
   每个地区隶属机构独立运营，同时遵守母公司制定的标准。

3. The university chose to **affiliate** with several research institutions to strengthen its academic programs and funding.
   该大学选择与多家研究机构建立附属关系，以加强其学术项目和资金支持。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Af-" (to) + "filiate" from Latin "filius" (son) — to affiliate is to adopt as a son, bringing someone into the family.
"af-"（向）+"filiate"源自拉丁语"filius"（儿子）——affiliate就是收为儿子，将某人纳入家庭。
