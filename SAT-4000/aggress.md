# ⚔️ aggress

🔊 /əˈɡrɛs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To initiate an attack or act of hostility; to commit the first act of aggression against someone or something
**中文：** 攻击；侵略

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attack（攻击）
- assault（袭击）
- invade（入侵）
- provoke（挑衅）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aggress against / aggress upon / aggress toward / tendency to aggress**

👉 Example: International law prohibits any sovereign nation from aggressing against its peaceful neighboring states.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historical records confirm that the empire chose to **aggress** against the smaller kingdom without any legitimate provocation or cause.
   历史记录证实，该帝国在没有任何合理挑衅或理由的情况下选择了对这个小王国发动攻击。

2. Psychologists have studied the conditions under which individuals are most likely to **aggress** against perceived threats in social settings.
   心理学家研究了在社会环境中个人最有可能对感知到的威胁做出攻击行为的条件。

3. The security council unanimously condemned any nation that would **aggress** upon the territorial sovereignty of its neighboring states.
   安全理事会一致谴责任何侵犯邻国领土主权的国家。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ag-GRESS" — "gress" comes from Latin "gradi" meaning to step, so aggress means to step toward someone aggressively.
想想"ag-GRESS"——"gress"来自拉丁语"gradi"意为迈步，所以aggress意为带有攻击性地向某人迈进。
