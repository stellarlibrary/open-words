# 💰 usurious

🔊 /juːˈʒʊəriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to or characterized by usury; charging excessively high or unlawful rates of interest on loans
**中文：** adj. 高利贷的；收取高额利息的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- extortionate（勒索的）
- exorbitant（过高的）
- exploitative（剥削性的）
- predatory（掠夺性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **usurious rates / usurious interest / usurious lending / usurious practices**

👉 Example: Consumer protection agencies warned the public against financial institutions that charge usurious interest rates.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The regulatory commission imposed heavy fines on lenders found guilty of imposing **usurious** interest rates on vulnerable borrowers.
   监管委员会对被判定向弱势借款人收取高利贷利率的放贷人处以巨额罚款。

2. Medieval church law strictly prohibited **usurious** lending practices, considering the charging of excessive interest a grave moral sin.
   中世纪教会法严格禁止高利贷行为，认为收取过高利息是严重的道德罪过。

3. The investigative report exposed a network of **usurious** payday lenders who systematically exploited low-income communities across the region.
   调查报告揭露了一个高利贷发薪日贷款机构网络，它们系统性地剥削该地区的低收入社区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "usurious" as related to "usury" (高利贷) — a usurious lender practices usury by charging outrageously high interest.
把"usurious"和"usury"（高利贷）联系起来——高利贷放贷人通过收取极高利息来从事高利贷行为。
