# 🎶 ensemble

🔊 /ɑːnˈsɑːmbəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A group of musicians, actors, or dancers who perform together; the overall effect of something viewed as a whole
**中文：** n. 全体；总效果；合奏组；合唱团

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- troupe（剧团）
- group（团体）
- company（一团人）
- collective（集体）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ensemble cast / musical ensemble / ensemble performance / chamber ensemble / the whole ensemble**

👉 Example: The chamber ensemble delivered an outstanding performance of Mozart's string quartets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The distinguished **ensemble** of classically trained musicians performed a series of baroque concertos at the national concert hall.
   这支由受过古典训练的音乐家组成的杰出合奏团在国家音乐厅演奏了一系列巴洛克协奏曲。

2. Critics praised the film primarily for its remarkable **ensemble** cast, whose chemistry elevated the otherwise predictable screenplay.
   评论家主要称赞了这部电影出色的全体演员阵容，他们的默契提升了原本情节平淡的剧本。

3. The architect designed the building so that the **ensemble** of structures would create a harmonious and visually unified campus.
   建筑师对大楼进行了设计，使建筑群的整体效果形成一个和谐且视觉统一的校园。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ensemble" as coming from French meaning "together" — an ensemble is a group that comes together to create one unified performance.
把"ensemble"想成来自法语中"一起（together）"的意思——合奏组就是聚在一起创造统一表演的团体。
