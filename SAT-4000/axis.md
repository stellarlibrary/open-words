# 🌀 axis

🔊 /ˈæksɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** An imaginary line around which a body rotates; a central line or principle around which something is organized or arranged.

**中文：** 轴；轴线。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- pivot（枢轴）
- center line（中心线）
- spindle（纺锤；轴）
- shaft（轴杆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Axis" is used in mathematics, science, and politics. In math, it refers to reference lines on a graph (x-axis, y-axis). In science, the Earth rotates on its axis. The plural form is "axes" (pronounced /ˈæksiːz/).

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Earth completes one full rotation on its **axis** approximately every twenty-four hours without fail.
   （地球大约每二十四小时绕其**轴**完成一次完整的自转。）

2. The professor asked the students to plot the data points along the horizontal **axis** of the graph.
   （教授要求学生们沿着图表的水平**轴**标出数据点。）

3. The entire foreign policy of the alliance revolved around a central **axis** of mutual economic cooperation.
   （该联盟的整个外交政策围绕着互惠经济合作这一核心**轴线**展开。）

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "axis" as "ax is" — an axe handle is a straight shaft, just like an axis is a straight line that things rotate around.

联想"axis"为"ax is（斧头是）"——斧头的把手是一根直杆，就像轴是一条万物围绕旋转的直线。
