# 🏛️ dignity

🔊 /ˈdɪɡnɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being worthy of honor and respect; a composed and serious manner or style; inherent nobility of character
**中文：** n. 尊严；高贵；体面

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- honor（荣誉）
- nobility（高贵）
- grace（优雅）
- decorum（端庄）
- stateliness（庄严）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **human dignity / with dignity / beneath one's dignity / sense of dignity / dignity and respect**

👉 Example: Every individual deserves to be treated with dignity regardless of social status or background.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Universal Declaration of Human Rights affirms that all people are born with inherent **dignity** and equal fundamental rights.
   《世界人权宣言》申明所有人生而具有固有的尊严和平等的基本权利。

2. She faced the devastating diagnosis with remarkable **dignity** and composure, inspiring everyone who witnessed her extraordinary courage.
   她以非凡的尊严和镇定面对毁灭性的诊断，激励了所有见证她非凡勇气的人。

3. The senator considered it beneath his **dignity** to engage in petty political disputes that distracted from substantive policy debates.
   这位参议员认为参与琐碎的政治争端有失体面，因为那会分散对实质性政策辩论的注意力。

---

Think of "dignity" as "dig-nity" — you must dig deep within yourself to find true dignity, the inner nobility that commands respect.
把"dignity"想成"dig-nity"——你必须深入内心才能找到真正的尊严，那种令人尊敬的内在高贵。
