# 📢 avow

🔊 /əˈvaʊ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To declare or acknowledge openly, boldly, and without shame; to assert or confess frankly and publicly.

**中文：** 承认；公开宣称。公开地、大胆地、毫不羞愧地声明或承认某事；坦率而公开地断言或坦白。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- declare （声明）
- affirm （肯定）
- profess （公开表示）
- assert （断言）
- proclaim （宣告）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Avow" emphasizes openness and boldness in making a declaration. It is stronger than simply "saying" or "admitting" — it implies a willingness to stand by one's statement publicly. The related noun "avowal" and adjective "avowed" are also commonly used.

"avow"强调声明的公开性和大胆性。它比简单的"说"或"承认"更有力——暗示愿意公开为自己的声明负责。相关名词"avowal"和形容词"avowed"也很常用。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator **avowed** his unwavering commitment to educational reform during the nationally televised debate.
   *这位参议员在全国电视转播的辩论中**公开宣称**他对教育改革的坚定承诺。*

2. Despite facing severe criticism, she boldly **avowed** her belief in the controversial scientific theory.
   *尽管面临严厉的批评，她仍大胆地**公开声明**自己对这一有争议的科学理论的信念。*

3. The organization **avowed** its responsibility for the error and immediately implemented corrective measures to prevent recurrence.
   *该组织**承认**了对这一错误的责任，并立即实施了纠正措施以防止再次发生。*

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Avow" sounds like "a vow" — when you make a vow, you declare something openly and solemnly. To avow is to make a public, bold declaration, just like taking a vow.

"avow"听起来像"a vow"（一个誓言）——当你发誓时，你公开而庄严地宣告某事。avow就是做出公开、大胆的声明，就像发誓一样。
