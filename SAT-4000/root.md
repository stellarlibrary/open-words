# 🌱 root

🔊 /ruːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The underground part of a plant; the fundamental cause or origin of something; a mathematical value. (v.) To establish deeply; to search or rummage
**中文：** n. 方根；方程的根

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- origin（起源）
- source（根源）
- foundation（基础）
- basis（基础）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **root cause / root of the problem / take root / root out / square root**

👉 Example: Investigators worked diligently to identify the root cause of the catastrophic system failure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician demonstrated that the **root** of the polynomial equation could be determined using an elegant algebraic method.
   数学家证明了该多项式方程的根可以用一种优雅的代数方法求得。

2. Scholars traced the **root** of the conflict back to unresolved territorial disputes that had persisted for several decades.
   学者们将冲突的根源追溯到持续了几十年的未解决的领土争端。

3. The new environmental policy aims to **root** out corruption and ensure transparent allocation of conservation funding.
   新的环境政策旨在根除腐败，确保保护资金的透明分配。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of a tree's root buried underground — it is the hidden foundation from which everything grows, just as a root cause is the hidden origin of a problem.
把"root"想成埋在地下的树根——它是万物生长的隐藏基础，正如根本原因是问题的隐藏起源。
