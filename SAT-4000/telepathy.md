# 🧠 telepathy

🔊 /tɪˈlɛpəθi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The supposed transmission of thoughts or feelings between minds without using known sensory channels or physical interaction
**中文：** n. 心灵感应；传心术；感应

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mind-reading（读心术）
- clairvoyance（千里眼；超感力）
- extrasensory perception（超感官知觉）
- psychic communication（灵媒交流）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **telepathy between / mental telepathy / practice telepathy / a form of telepathy**

👉 Example: The twins seemed to share a kind of telepathy, often finishing each other's sentences effortlessly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite decades of investigation, no scientific study has produced reliable evidence confirming the existence of **telepathy**.
   尽管经过数十年的研究，没有任何科学研究产生了证实心灵感应存在的可靠证据。

2. The novelist explored **telepathy** as a central theme, imagining a society where private thoughts were no longer secure.
   这位小说家将心灵感应作为核心主题进行探讨，想象一个私人想法不再安全的社会。

3. Some researchers propose that apparent **telepathy** can be explained by subtle nonverbal cues and unconscious behavioral patterns.
   一些研究人员提出，表面上的心灵感应可以用微妙的非语言信号和无意识的行为模式来解释。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tele-pathy" as "tele" (far) + "pathy" (feeling) — feeling or sensing something from far away, mind to mind.
把"telepathy"想成"tele"（远）+ "pathy"（感觉）——从远处感知事物，心灵与心灵之间的感应。
