# ⚠️ endanger

🔊 /ɪnˈdeɪndʒər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To put someone or something at risk or in danger; to threaten with harm or destruction
**中文：** v. 危及；使遭到危险

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- jeopardize（危及）
- imperil（使陷入危险）
- threaten（威胁）
- compromise（危害）
- risk（冒险）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endanger lives / endanger a species / endanger public safety / endangered species**

👉 Example: Reckless industrial practices endanger both the environment and the health of local communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Deforestation and illegal poaching continue to **endanger** numerous species that are already on the brink of extinction.
   森林砍伐和非法偷猎继续危及许多已经濒临灭绝的物种。

2. The government warned that inadequate safety protocols could seriously **endanger** the lives of workers in the mining industry.
   政府警告说，不完善的安全规程可能严重危及采矿业工人的生命。

3. Releasing untreated chemical waste into rivers will inevitably **endanger** the drinking water supply for millions of residents.
   将未经处理的化学废物排入河流将不可避免地危及数百万居民的饮用水供应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "en-danger" as literally putting something "in danger" — the prefix "en-" means to cause to be in a state, so endanger means to cause danger.
把"endanger"想成字面上把某物置于"danger（危险）"之中——前缀"en-"表示使处于某种状态，因此endanger就是使陷入危险。
