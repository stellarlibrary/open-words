# 🐺 rabid

🔊 /ˈræbɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having or proceeding from an extreme or fanatical support of something; furiously violent; affected with rabies
**中文：** adj. 激烈的；狂暴的；偏激的；患狂犬病的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fanatical（狂热的）
- fervent（热烈的）
- extreme（极端的）
- frenzied（疯狂的）
- zealous（狂热的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rabid fan / rabid supporter / rabid opposition / rabid dog / rabid nationalism**

👉 Example: The politician's controversial remarks drew rabid opposition from advocacy groups across the political spectrum.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **rabid** supporters of the political movement refused to consider any compromise, demanding an absolute adherence to their ideology.
   该政治运动的狂热支持者拒绝考虑任何妥协，要求绝对遵循他们的意识形态。

2. Health officials issued urgent warnings after a **rabid** animal was discovered roaming through a densely populated residential neighborhood.
   卫生官员在一个人口密集的居民区发现一只患狂犬病的动物后，发出了紧急警告。

3. The debate grew increasingly **rabid** as both sides abandoned reason and resorted to personal attacks and inflammatory rhetoric.
   随着双方放弃理性转而进行人身攻击和煽动性言辞，辩论变得越来越激烈。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rabid" as related to "rabies" — just as a rabid animal is wild and uncontrollable, a rabid person is fanatically intense.
把"rabid"与"rabies"（狂犬病）联系起来——就像患狂犬病的动物狂野不可控一样，rabid的人也是狂热而偏激的。
