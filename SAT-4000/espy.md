# 🔍 espy

🔊 /ɪˈspaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To catch sight of something; to perceive or detect, especially something distant or hidden
**中文：** v. 看到；认出；发现；窥探

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spot（发现）
- discern（辨认）
- detect（察觉）
- glimpse（瞥见）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **espy a figure / espy something in the distance / espy a flaw**

👉 Example: From the hilltop, the scout was able to espy the enemy camp several miles away.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vigilant guard managed to **espy** a suspicious individual lurking near the restricted entrance of the building.
   警惕的守卫发现了一个可疑人物潜伏在大楼禁区入口附近。

2. After hours of careful observation, the ornithologist finally **espied** the rare migratory bird perched among the branches.
   经过数小时的仔细观察，鸟类学家终于发现了那只珍稀候鸟栖息在树枝间。

3. The detective **espied** a critical piece of evidence that all previous investigators had completely overlooked during their search.
   侦探发现了一条关键证据，而此前所有调查人员在搜查中都完全忽略了它。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "espy" as "eye + spy" — using your eye to spy on something, catching sight of it from a distance.
把"espy"想成"eye + spy"——用眼睛像间谍一样窥探，从远处发现某样东西。
