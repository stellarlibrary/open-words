# 📜 abidance

🔊 /əˈbaɪdəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of abiding; compliance or obedience to rules; continuance or permanence; residence
**中文：** n. 遵守，遵从；持续；居住

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- compliance（遵守）
- adherence（遵循）
- observance（奉行）
- conformity（符合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abidance by the rules / abidance with the law / strict abidance / abidance by regulations**

👉 Example: The organization emphasized strict abidance by all safety regulations at every operational level.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The treaty requires strict **abidance** by all signatory nations regarding the prohibition of nuclear testing.
   该条约要求所有签署国严格遵守关于禁止核试验的规定。

2. Their long **abidance** in the remote village gave them a deep understanding of the local customs.
   他们长期居住在偏远村庄，使他们对当地习俗有了深刻的理解。

3. The court emphasized that **abidance** by established legal procedures is essential for maintaining public trust.
   法院强调，遵守既定法律程序对于维护公众信任至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Abidance" comes from "abide" + "-ance." If you abide by the rules, your abidance is your obedience to them.
"Abidance"来自"abide"（遵守）+ "-ance"（名词后缀）。如果你abide by规则，你的abidance就是你对规则的遵从。
