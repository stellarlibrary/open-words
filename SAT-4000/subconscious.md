# 🧠 subconscious

🔊 /sʌbˈkɒnʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to mental processes occurring below the level of conscious awareness. (n.) The part of the mind not fully aware but influencing actions and feelings
**中文：** adj. 下意识的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unconscious（无意识的）
- subliminal（潜意识的）
- implicit（隐含的）
- instinctive（本能的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subconscious mind / subconscious desire / subconscious level / subconscious thought**

👉 Example: Many of our daily decisions are guided by subconscious biases that we rarely recognize or question.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Psychologists have demonstrated that **subconscious** biases can significantly influence hiring decisions even among well-intentioned employers.
   心理学家已证明，下意识的偏见即使在善意的雇主中也会显著影响招聘决策。

2. The artist believed that her most creative work emerged from **subconscious** processes rather than deliberate intellectual planning.
   这位艺术家认为她最有创意的作品来自下意识的过程，而非刻意的理性规划。

3. Repeated exposure to advertising messages can shape **subconscious** attitudes toward products without consumers ever realizing the influence.
   反复接触广告信息会在消费者毫无察觉的情况下塑造其对产品的下意识态度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "subconscious" as "sub + conscious" — "sub" means below, so subconscious is what lies below your conscious awareness, working silently in the background.
把"subconscious"想成"sub + conscious"——"sub"意为下方，所以subconscious就是在你意识之下默默运作的心理活动。
