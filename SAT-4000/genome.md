# 🧬 genome

🔊 /ˈdʒiːnoʊm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The complete set of genes or genetic material present in a cell or organism; the full DNA sequence of an organism
**中文：** n. 基因组；染色体组

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genetic code（遗传密码）
- DNA sequence（DNA序列）
- genetic blueprint（遗传蓝图）
- chromosomal set（染色体组）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **human genome / genome sequencing / genome editing / map the genome / genome project**

👉 Example: The Human Genome Project revolutionized our understanding of biology by mapping the entire human genetic code.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists successfully sequenced the entire **genome** of the endangered species to develop more effective conservation and breeding strategies.
   科学家成功测序了该濒危物种的整个基因组，以制定更有效的保护和繁殖策略。

2. Advances in **genome** editing technology have raised profound ethical questions about the boundaries of human intervention in nature.
   基因组编辑技术的进步引发了关于人类干预自然界限的深刻伦理问题。

3. Comparing the **genome** of ancient specimens with modern populations reveals significant evolutionary changes that occurred over millennia.
   将古代标本的基因组与现代种群进行比较，揭示了数千年来发生的重大进化变化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "genome" as "gene + -ome" (meaning "complete set") — the genome is the complete collection of all genes in an organism.
把"genome"拆成"gene + -ome"（意为"完整集合"）——genome是一个生物体中所有基因的完整集合。
