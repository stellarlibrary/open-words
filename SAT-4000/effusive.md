# 🎉 effusive

🔊 /ɪˈfjuːsɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or heartfelt manner; excessively demonstrative
**中文：** adj. 感情横溢的；过分热情的；流出的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gushing（热情洋溢的）
- exuberant（充满活力的）
- demonstrative（感情外露的）
- lavish（过分慷慨的）
- unreserved（毫无保留的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effusive praise / effusive gratitude / effusive welcome / effusive in one's thanks**

👉 Example: The critic was effusive in her praise of the novel, calling it a masterpiece of modern literature.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador received an **effusive** welcome from the delegation, complete with ceremonial honors and extended speeches.
   大使受到代表团热情洋溢的欢迎，包括礼仪致敬和冗长的演讲。

2. Despite her typically reserved demeanor, she became remarkably **effusive** when discussing her groundbreaking research findings.
   尽管她通常举止矜持，但在讨论她的突破性研究成果时变得异常热情。

3. The **effusive** endorsement from the renowned scientist significantly bolstered public confidence in the new vaccine.
   这位著名科学家的热情背书极大地增强了公众对新疫苗的信心。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-FUSE-ive" — emotions fusing and overflowing outward; an effusive person lets their feelings pour out without restraint.
把"effusive"想成情感"fuse"（融合）后向外溢出——一个effusive的人会毫无节制地倾泻感情。
