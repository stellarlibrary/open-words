# 💼 consultant

🔊 /kənˈsʌltənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who provides expert advice professionally; a senior specialist physician who gives professional consultations
**中文：** n. 顾问，咨询者；会诊医生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adviser（顾问）
- specialist（专家）
- expert（专家）
- counselor（咨询师）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **management consultant / hire a consultant / consult with a consultant / independent consultant**

👉 Example: The firm hired an independent consultant to evaluate its organizational efficiency.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government appointed a team of environmental **consultants** to assess the ecological impact of the proposed dam construction.
   政府任命了一组环境顾问来评估拟建大坝的生态影响。

2. As a leading management **consultant**, she advised multinational corporations on strategies for navigating complex regulatory environments.
   作为一名顶尖的管理顾问，她为跨国公司提供应对复杂监管环境的策略建议。

3. The hospital's chief **consultant** recommended a comprehensive treatment plan after reviewing the patient's complete medical history.
   医院的首席会诊医生在审查了患者的完整病史后，推荐了一项综合治疗方案。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "consult-ant" — a person (ant) whom you "consult" when you need professional expertise and specialized guidance.
把"consultant"想成"consult（咨询）+ ant（人）"——当你需要专业知识和专门指导时去"咨询"的那个人。
