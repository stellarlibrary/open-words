# 🔄 habitual

🔊 /həˈbɪtʃuəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done or occurring regularly as a habit; customary or usual
**中文：** adj. 习惯的；惯常的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- customary（惯例的）
- routine（常规的）
- accustomed（习惯的）
- regular（固定的）
- chronic（长期的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **habitual behavior / habitual offender / habitual liar / habitual routine / habitual residence**

👉 Example: The court imposed a harsher sentence on the defendant because he was classified as a habitual offender.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **habitual** tardiness to lectures became a source of frustration among the otherwise punctual student body.
   教授惯常的上课迟到成为那些守时学生们不满的根源。

2. Psychologists have demonstrated that **habitual** patterns of negative thinking can significantly impair long-term mental health.
   心理学家已证明，习惯性的消极思维模式可以严重损害长期心理健康。

3. Her **habitual** morning routine of meditation and exercise contributed greatly to her sustained productivity throughout the day.
   她惯常的晨间冥想和锻炼习惯极大地提高了她全天的持续工作效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "habitual" as "habit + ual" — something relating to a habit, done over and over as part of your regular routine.
把"habitual"想成"habit（习惯）+ ual（形容词后缀）"——与习惯有关的，作为日常惯例反复做的事。
