# 🌡️ cooling

🔊 /ˈkuːlɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Making or becoming less hot; having a refreshing effect. (n.) The process of becoming or making something less hot
**中文：** adj. 冷却的；使凉爽的 n. 冷却；降温

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chilling（使冷却的）
- refreshing（清凉的）
- soothing（舒缓的）
- refrigerating（制冷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cooling system / cooling effect / cooling period / cooling tower**

👉 Example: The engineer inspected the cooling system to ensure the machinery would not overheat.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **cooling** effect of the ocean breeze provided welcome relief during the sweltering summer afternoon.
   海风的冷却效果在炎热的夏日午后带来了令人欢迎的缓解。

2. Advanced **cooling** technology has become essential for maintaining optimal performance in modern data centers.
   先进的冷却技术已成为维持现代数据中心最佳性能的关键。

3. After the volcanic eruption, the gradual **cooling** of the lava created distinctive geological formations along the coastline.
   火山喷发后，熔岩的逐渐冷却在海岸线沿线形成了独特的地质构造。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cool" + "-ing" as the ongoing process of something becoming cool — like a hot cup of tea slowly losing its heat.
把"cooling"想成"cool"加上"-ing"——就像一杯热茶慢慢失去热量的持续过程。
