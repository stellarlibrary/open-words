# 🔬 demonstrate

🔊 /ˈdɛmənstreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To prove or make evident by reasoning or evidence; to show or exhibit clearly; to explain through practical examples
**中文：** v. 证明；展示；论证

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prove（证明）
- illustrate（说明）
- exhibit（展示）
- substantiate（证实）
- manifest（表明）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **demonstrate competence / demonstrate the ability to / clearly demonstrate / demonstrate a correlation**

👉 Example: The experiment was designed to demonstrate the direct correlation between sleep deprivation and cognitive decline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The longitudinal study convincingly **demonstrated** that early childhood education significantly improves long-term academic and professional outcomes.
   这项纵向研究令人信服地证明了早期儿童教育显著改善了长期的学业和职业成果。

2. Applicants must **demonstrate** proficiency in at least two foreign languages before being considered for the diplomatic service program.
   申请者在被考虑加入外交服务项目之前，必须展示至少两门外语的熟练程度。

3. The professor used a series of controlled experiments to **demonstrate** how environmental variables directly influence chemical reaction rates.
   教授通过一系列对照实验来论证环境变量如何直接影响化学反应速率。

---

把"demonstrate"想成"demon-strate"——想象一个"恶魔"直截了当地用不可否认的证据来证明某事。
