# 🧬 mutation

🔊 /mjuːˈteɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A change or alteration in form or nature; in biology, a permanent change in the DNA sequence of a gene that may produce new characteristics
**中文：** n. 突变；变化；元音变化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- alteration（变化）
- transformation（转变）
- variation（变异）
- modification（修改）
- metamorphosis（蜕变）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **genetic mutation / undergo mutation / random mutation / mutation rate**

👉 Example: Scientists discovered a rare genetic mutation that appeared to confer resistance to the disease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team identified a specific **mutation** in the gene that significantly increased the organism's resistance to environmental toxins.
   研究团队确定了基因中的一个特定突变，该突变显著增强了生物体对环境毒素的抗性。

2. Rapid **mutation** of the virus made it exceedingly difficult for pharmaceutical companies to develop an effective long-term vaccine.
   病毒的快速突变使得制药公司极难开发出有效的长期疫苗。

3. Cultural **mutation** occurs when traditional practices gradually evolve in response to technological advancement and social change.
   当传统习俗因技术进步和社会变革而逐渐演变时，文化变异就会发生。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mutation" as "mutate + tion" — to "mutate" is to change, and "-tion" makes it a noun, so a mutation is a change or transformation.
把"mutation"想成"mutate（突变）+ -tion（名词后缀）"——"mutate"是改变的意思，加上"-tion"变成名词，所以mutation就是变化或转变。
