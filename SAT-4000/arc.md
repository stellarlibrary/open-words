# 🌈 arc

🔊 /ɑːrk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A curved line or shape forming part of a circle; a luminous electrical discharge between two points. (v.) To move or form an arc or curved path
**中文：** n. 弧；弧光 v. 形成电弧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- curve（曲线）
- bow（弓形）
- crescent（新月形）
- arch（拱形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arc of a circle / electric arc / story arc / arc across the sky / arc welding**

👉 Example: The ball traced a perfect arc across the sky before landing in the outfield.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The meteor blazed a brilliant **arc** across the night sky, captivating thousands of spectators gathered at the observatory grounds.
   流星在夜空中划出一道璀璨的弧线，吸引了聚集在天文台场地上的数千名观众。

2. Electricity **arced** between the two exposed wires, producing dangerous sparks that threatened to ignite the surrounding dry materials.
   电流在两根裸露的电线之间形成电弧，产生的危险火花有可能点燃周围的干燥材料。

3. The narrative **arc** of the novel spans three generations, tracing the family's gradual rise from poverty to prominence.
   这部小说的叙事弧线跨越三代人，追溯了这个家族从贫穷到显赫的渐进崛起。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Picture a rainbow — it forms a perfect arc in the sky. The word "arc" even sounds like "ark" (Noah's Ark), which had a curved hull shape.
想象一道彩虹——它在天空中形成一道完美的弧线。"Arc"听起来像"ark"（诺亚方舟），方舟也有弯曲的船体形状。
