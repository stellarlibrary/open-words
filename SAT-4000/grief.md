# 😢 grief

🔊 /ɡriːf/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Deep sorrow or sadness, especially caused by loss or bereavement; a cause of such suffering
**中文：** n. 悲痛；忧伤；悲伤的事；不幸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sorrow（悲伤）
- anguish（痛苦）
- mourning（哀悼）
- heartache（心痛）
- bereavement（丧亲之痛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **grief over / grief at / come to grief / grief-stricken / overwhelming grief**

👉 Example: The entire community was overwhelmed with grief after the sudden loss of their beloved teacher.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation was united in **grief** following the tragic natural disaster that claimed thousands of innocent lives.
   这场夺走数千无辜生命的悲惨自然灾害发生后，举国上下沉浸在悲痛之中。

2. Her **grief** over the failed experiment was compounded by the realization that years of research had been wasted.
   实验失败带来的悲痛因意识到多年研究付诸东流而更加沉重。

3. The therapist helped patients process their **grief** through structured counseling sessions and supportive group discussions.
   治疗师通过系统的咨询和支持性小组讨论帮助患者处理他们的悲伤情绪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "grief" as "gr-ief" — the "gr" sounds like a groan, and groaning is what you do when you feel deep sorrow.
把"grief"想成"gr-ief"——"gr"听起来像呻吟声，而呻吟正是你深感悲痛时会做的事。
