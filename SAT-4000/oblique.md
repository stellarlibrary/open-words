# 📐 oblique

🔊 /əˈbliːk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun, verb | 形容词, 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Slanting or sloping; not straightforward or direct in manner. (n.) Something that is oblique. (v.) To slant or incline.
**中文：** adj. 倾斜的；间接的；不坦率的，无诚意的 n. 倾斜物 vi. 倾斜

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- indirect（间接的）
- slanting（倾斜的）
- evasive（闪避的）
- diagonal（对角线的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **oblique reference / oblique angle / oblique approach / oblique criticism / oblique glance**

👉 Example: She made an oblique reference to his past failures without mentioning any specifics directly.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat made an **oblique** reference to the trade dispute without directly naming the countries involved.
   外交官间接提到了贸易争端，但没有直接点出相关国家的名字。

2. Sunlight entered the cathedral at an **oblique** angle, casting long dramatic shadows across the stone floor.
   阳光以一个倾斜的角度射入大教堂，在石地板上投下了长长的戏剧性阴影。

3. His **oblique** manner of answering questions left the interviewer uncertain about his true position on the matter.
   他不坦率的回答方式让面试官无法确定他对此事的真正立场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "o-BLIQUE" — sounds like "o-BLEAK" — when someone is oblique, communication becomes bleak and unclear because nothing is said directly.
把"oblique"想成听起来像"o-BLEAK"（暗淡的）——当某人不坦率时，沟通变得暗淡不明，因为什么都不直说。
