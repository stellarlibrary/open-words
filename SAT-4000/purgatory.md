# 🔥 purgatory

🔊 /ˈpɜːrɡətɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A place or state of temporary suffering or expiation; in some religious traditions, a condition of purification after death
**中文：** n. 炼狱；涤罪；暂时的苦难

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- limbo（不确定状态）
- torment（折磨）
- ordeal（磨难）
- suffering（苦难）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a state of purgatory / living in purgatory / endure purgatory / go through purgatory**

👉 Example: Waiting for the medical test results felt like an endless purgatory for the anxious family.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The refugees described their time in the overcrowded camp as a prolonged **purgatory** of uncertainty and deprivation.
   难民们将他们在拥挤营地中的时光描述为一段漫长的不确定和匮乏的炼狱。

2. For many aspiring academics, the years spent as adjunct lecturers represent a kind of professional **purgatory**.
   对许多有抱负的学者来说，担任兼职讲师的岁月代表着一种职业上的炼狱。

3. The nation endured a **purgatory** of economic hardship before the reforms finally produced measurable improvements.
   这个国家经历了一段经济困难的炼狱，改革最终才产生了可衡量的改善。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "purg-atory" as related to "purge" — purgatory is a place where sins are purged through suffering before one can move on.
把"purgatory"和"purge（清除）"联系起来——炼狱是一个通过受苦来清除罪孽的地方，之后才能继续前行。
