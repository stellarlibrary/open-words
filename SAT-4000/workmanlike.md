# 🔧 workmanlike

🔊 /ˈwɜːrkmənlaɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing efficient competence and skill; done with practical proficiency and craftsmanship
**中文：** adj. 技术熟练的；精工细作的；精巧的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- skillful（熟练的）
- competent（能干的）
- proficient（精通的）
- craftsmanlike（精工细作的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **workmanlike manner / workmanlike performance / workmanlike effort / workmanlike approach**

👉 Example: The carpenter completed the renovation in a thoroughly workmanlike manner, paying attention to every detail.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect delivered a **workmanlike** design that balanced aesthetic appeal with structural integrity and cost efficiency.
   这位建筑师交出了一份技术精湛的设计，兼顾了美观、结构完整性和成本效益。

2. Despite the tight deadline, the research team produced a **workmanlike** report that met all the academic standards required.
   尽管截止日期很紧，研究团队仍完成了一份技术精良的报告，达到了所有要求的学术标准。

3. Her **workmanlike** approach to restoring the antique furniture earned widespread admiration from collectors and historians alike.
   她精工细作地修复古董家具的方法赢得了收藏家和历史学家的广泛赞赏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "workman-like" — like a skilled workman who does everything with practiced hands and professional precision.
把"workmanlike"想成"像工匠一样"——就像一位技术娴熟的工匠，用熟练的双手和专业的精确度完成每一件事。
