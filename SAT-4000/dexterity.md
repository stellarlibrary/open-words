# 🤹 dexterity

🔊 /dekˈsterɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Skill in performing tasks, especially with the hands; mental skill or quickness; adroitness
**中文：** n. 灵巧；敏捷；机敏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- agility（敏捷）
- adroitness（机敏）
- nimbleness（灵活）
- proficiency（熟练）
- finesse（技巧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **manual dexterity / mental dexterity / remarkable dexterity / with great dexterity**

👉 Example: The surgeon performed the delicate procedure with extraordinary dexterity and precision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pianist displayed remarkable **dexterity** as her fingers moved effortlessly across the keys during the complex concerto.
   这位钢琴家在演奏复杂的协奏曲时展现了非凡的灵巧，手指在琴键上毫不费力地移动。

2. The diplomat handled the sensitive negotiations with considerable **dexterity**, achieving consensus among all conflicting parties involved.
   这位外交官以相当的机敏处理了敏感的谈判，在所有冲突各方之间达成了共识。

3. Young children develop fine motor **dexterity** through activities such as drawing, building blocks, and manipulating small objects.
   幼儿通过绘画、搭积木和操作小物件等活动来发展精细运动的灵巧性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dexter" (Latin for "right hand") — dexterity originally meant right-hand skill, and the right hand was considered the skillful one.
把"dexterity"想成来自拉丁语"dexter（右手）"——灵巧最初指右手的技能，因为右手被认为是灵巧的那只手。
