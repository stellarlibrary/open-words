# 🧴 sealant

🔊 /ˈsiːlənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance used to seal a surface or joint, preventing the passage of fluid or air
**中文：** n. 密封剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adhesive（粘合剂）
- caulk（填缝剂）
- filler（填充剂）
- coating（涂层）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **apply sealant / silicone sealant / waterproof sealant / sealant layer**

👉 Example: The contractor applied a layer of waterproof sealant around the bathroom fixtures to prevent leakage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers recommended applying an industrial-grade **sealant** to the concrete joints to prevent moisture infiltration and structural degradation.
   工程师建议在混凝土接缝处涂抹工业级密封剂，以防止水分渗透和结构退化。

2. The dental hygienist carefully applied a protective **sealant** to the patient's molars to reduce the long-term risk of decay.
   牙科保健师仔细地在患者的磨牙上涂抹保护性密封剂，以降低长期蛀牙的风险。

3. Without a proper **sealant** on the exterior walls, the historic building suffered extensive water damage during the prolonged rainy season.
   由于外墙缺乏适当的密封剂，这座历史建筑在漫长的雨季中遭受了严重的水损害。

---

Think of "sealant" as "seal + ant" — imagine a tiny ant using glue to seal every crack it finds, just like a sealant seals gaps.
把"sealant"想成"seal（密封）+ ant（蚂蚁）"——想象一只小蚂蚁用胶水密封它发现的每一条裂缝，就像密封剂填补缝隙一样。
