# ⚔️ insurgent

🔊 /ɪnˈsɜːrdʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Rising in revolt against established authority; rebellious. (n.) A person who rises in forceful opposition to lawful authority; a rebel
**中文：** adj. 叛乱的；起义的 n. 叛乱者；起义者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rebel（叛乱者）
- revolutionary（革命者）
- mutineer（叛变者）
- dissident（异见者）
- insurrectionist（暴动者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insurgent forces / insurgent group / insurgent attack / armed insurgent / insurgent movement**

👉 Example: The government deployed additional troops to counter the insurgent forces in the northern provinces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **insurgent** faction launched a coordinated offensive against government military installations along the southern border region.
   叛乱派系对南部边境地区的政府军事设施发动了协调一致的进攻。

2. International observers documented how **insurgent** groups exploited political instability to recruit disillusioned youth into their ranks.
   国际观察人士记录了叛乱组织如何利用政治不稳定来招募幻想破灭的年轻人加入其队伍。

3. The historian analyzed the social conditions that transformed ordinary citizens into determined **insurgent** leaders during the revolution.
   历史学家分析了在革命期间将普通公民转变为坚定的起义领导者的社会条件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "insurgent" as "in-surge-nt" — people who "surge in" against authority, rising up like a powerful wave of opposition.
把"insurgent"想成"in（向内）+ surge（涌起）+ nt" ——像强大的浪潮一样涌向当局，奋起反抗的人。
