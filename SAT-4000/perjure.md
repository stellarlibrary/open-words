# ⚖️ perjure

🔊 /ˈpɜːrdʒər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To willfully make a false statement under oath; to commit perjury by lying in a legal proceeding
**中文：** v. 作伪证；发假誓；使破坏誓言

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lie under oath（在宣誓下撒谎）
- forswear（发假誓）
- bear false witness（作伪证）
- deceive（欺骗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perjure oneself / perjure testimony / convicted of perjuring / willing to perjure**

👉 Example: The witness chose to perjure himself rather than implicate his close associates in the crime.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defendant was charged with attempting to **perjure** himself during the cross-examination by providing deliberately false testimony.
   被告被指控在交叉询问期间故意提供虚假证词，试图作伪证。

2. Several witnesses were willing to **perjure** themselves in order to protect the organization's leadership from criminal prosecution.
   数名证人愿意作伪证，以保护该组织的领导层免受刑事起诉。

3. The attorney warned her client that choosing to **perjure** himself could result in additional felony charges and imprisonment.
   律师警告她的当事人，选择作伪证可能导致额外的重罪指控和监禁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-jure" — "per" sounds like "pair" and "jure" relates to "jury." Imagine lying in front of a pair of jurors — that is to perjure yourself.
把"perjure"想成"per（在…前）+ jure（发誓/法律）"——在法律面前发假誓，即作伪证。
