# 🌱 afresh

🔊 /əˈfrɛʃ/<br>
🏷️ *adverb | 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** In a new or different way; once more from the beginning
**中文：** 重新；再度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- anew（重新）
- again（再次）
- freshly（新近地）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **start afresh / begin afresh / think afresh**

👉 Example: After the project failed, the team decided to start afresh with a revised plan.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Following the devastating fire, the community resolved to build their neighborhood **afresh** with improved safety standards.
   在毁灭性的火灾之后，社区决心以更高的安全标准重新建设他们的街区。

2. The novelist discarded three hundred pages of manuscript and commenced writing the entire story **afresh** from scratch.
   这位小说家丢弃了三百页手稿，从头开始重新撰写整个故事。

3. The committee agreed to examine the controversial legislation **afresh** after new evidence surfaced during the investigation.
   在调查过程中出现新证据后，委员会同意重新审议这项有争议的立法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "a-fresh" as "a fresh start" — doing something afresh means beginning with a clean, fresh slate.
把"afresh"想成"a fresh start"——重新做某事就是从崭新的起点开始。
