# 🍷 bibulous

🔊 /ˈbɪbjʊləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Excessively fond of drinking alcohol; also, highly absorbent of liquid
**中文：** 嗜酒的；饮酒的；吸水的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intemperate（不节制的）
- drunken（酗酒的）
- absorbent（吸水的）
- tipsy（微醺的）
- sottish（嗜酒的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bibulous habits / bibulous tendencies / bibulous nature / bibulous material / bibulous companions**

👉 Example: His bibulous habits eventually cost him both his career and his reputation in the academic community.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novelist's **bibulous** lifestyle was well documented in numerous biographical accounts of his turbulent later years.
   这位小说家嗜酒的生活方式在许多关于他动荡晚年的传记记述中都有详细记录。

2. Scientists selected a **bibulous** paper for the experiment because it could rapidly absorb large quantities of the test solution.
   科学家们选择了一种吸水纸进行实验，因为它能快速吸收大量的测试溶液。

3. The **bibulous** patrons of the old tavern were legendary figures in the folklore of the small coastal village.
   这家老酒馆里嗜酒的常客是这个沿海小村庄民间传说中的传奇人物。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bib" — a baby's bib absorbs drool, and "bibulous" means absorbing liquids. Also, "bib" sounds like sipping a drink, hinting at the love of alcohol.
想想"bib"（围嘴）——婴儿的围嘴吸收口水，而"bibulous"就是吸收液体的意思。同时"bib"听起来像在啜饮，暗示对酒精的喜爱。
