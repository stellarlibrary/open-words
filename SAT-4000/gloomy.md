# 🌑 gloomy

🔊 /ˈɡluːmi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Dark or poorly lit; causing feelings of sadness or depression; pessimistic about the future
**中文：** adj. 阴暗的，阴沉的；令人沮丧的，抑郁的；悲观的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- somber（阴沉的）
- dismal（阴郁的）
- bleak（暗淡的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- dreary（沉闷的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gloomy forecast / gloomy atmosphere / gloomy outlook / gloomy expression**

👉 Example: The gloomy weather matched the somber mood of the memorial ceremony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Economists presented a **gloomy** forecast for the housing market, predicting continued decline throughout the fiscal year.
   经济学家对房地产市场作出了令人沮丧的预测，预计整个财政年度将持续下滑。

2. The **gloomy** corridors of the abandoned hospital created an unsettling atmosphere that discouraged even the bravest explorers.
   废弃医院阴暗的走廊营造出一种令人不安的氛围，连最勇敢的探险者也望而却步。

3. Despite the **gloomy** prognosis, the research team remained determined to find a viable treatment for the disease.
   尽管预后令人悲观，研究团队仍然决心为这种疾病找到可行的治疗方法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gloomy" as "gloom + y" — gloom means darkness and sadness, and the "-y" suffix makes it an adjective describing that dark, sad quality.
把"gloomy"想成"gloom（黑暗、忧郁）+ y"——gloom意为黑暗和忧伤，加上"-y"后缀变成形容词，描述那种阴暗、忧郁的特质。
