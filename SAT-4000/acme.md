# 🏔️ acme

🔊 /ˈækmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The point at which something is at its best or most highly developed; the peak or summit.
**中文：** 顶点，极点；最高点

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pinnacle（顶峰）
- zenith（巅峰）
- apex（顶点）
- summit（最高点）
- peak（高峰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the acme of perfection / achievement / success / one's career**

👉 Example: The Renaissance is often regarded as the acme of artistic achievement in Europe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Many historians consider the empire's territorial expansion to represent the **acme** of its political and military power.
   许多历史学家认为该帝国的领土扩张代表了其政治和军事实力的顶峰。

2. The architect's masterpiece was widely celebrated as the **acme** of modernist design and structural innovation.
   这位建筑师的杰作被广泛赞誉为现代主义设计和结构创新的巅峰之作。

3. She reached the **acme** of her academic career when she was appointed chair of the prestigious department.
   当她被任命为该著名系的系主任时，她达到了学术生涯的顶峰。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ACME" from cartoons — the brand that claims to be the best of everything. Acme literally means the highest point.
想想动画片中的"ACME"品牌——它自称是万物之最。Acme的字面意思就是最高点。
