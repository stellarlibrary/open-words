# ✨ effulgence

🔊 /ɪˈfʌldʒəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A brilliant radiance; a shining forth of light or splendor
**中文：** n. 灿烂；光辉；光芒四射

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- radiance（光辉）
- brilliance（灿烂）
- luminosity（光亮）
- splendor（壮丽）
- resplendence（辉煌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **effulgence of the sun / effulgence of light / radiant effulgence / dazzling effulgence**

👉 Example: The effulgence of the morning sun bathed the entire valley in golden warmth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **effulgence** of the chandelier illuminated the grand ballroom, casting prismatic reflections across the marble floor.
   枝形吊灯的光辉照亮了宏伟的舞厅，在大理石地板上投射出棱镜般的光影。

2. Scholars have long admired the **effulgence** of Renaissance art, which conveyed spiritual transcendence through luminous color.
   学者们长期以来赞赏文艺复兴艺术的灿烂光辉，它通过明亮的色彩传达出精神上的超越。

3. The **effulgence** radiating from the distant supernova was captured by the observatory's most sensitive telescopic instruments.
   远处超新星散发出的光辉被天文台最灵敏的望远镜设备捕捉到。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ef-FULG-ence" — "fulg" comes from Latin "fulgere" meaning "to shine," so effulgence is light shining outward in full force.
把"effulgence"中的"fulg"联想到拉丁语"fulgere"（发光），所以effulgence就是光芒向外全力绽放。
