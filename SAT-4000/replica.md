# :framed_picture: replica

:speaker: /ˈrɛplɪkə/<br>
:label: *noun | 名词*

---

## :books: Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An exact copy or reproduction of something, especially one made on a smaller scale or by the original artist
**中文：** n. 复制品；复制物；仿制品

---

## :link: Synonyms | 近义词
- reproduction（复制品）
- duplicate（副本）
- facsimile（摹本）
- imitation（仿制品）
- copy（拷贝）

---

## :pushpin: Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an exact replica / a faithful replica / a replica of the original / build a replica**

:point_right: Example: The museum displayed a meticulously crafted replica of the ancient Greek temple for educational purposes.

---

## :memo: Examples | 例句

1. The architect constructed a detailed scale **replica** of the cathedral to demonstrate the structural ingenuity of medieval Gothic design.
   建筑师建造了一个精细的大教堂缩比复制品，以展示中世纪哥特式设计的结构精妙之处。

2. Experts confirmed that the painting was merely a skillful **replica** and not the authentic masterpiece originally attributed to the renowned artist.
   专家证实这幅画只是一件技艺精湛的复制品，并非最初归于那位著名艺术家的真迹。

3. The historical society commissioned a full-scale **replica** of the colonial ship to commemorate the anniversary of the nation's founding voyage.
   历史学会委托建造了一艘殖民地船只的全尺寸复制品，以纪念国家开拓航行的周年纪念。

---

## :jigsaw: Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "replica" as "REPLI-ca" — it "replies" to the original by echoing it exactly, creating a perfect copy.
把"replica"想成"repli-ca"——它通过精确"回应（reply）"原作来创造一个完美的复制品。
