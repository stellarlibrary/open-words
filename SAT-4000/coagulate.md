# 🩸 coagulate

🔊 /koʊˈæɡjʊleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To change from a liquid to a solid or semi-solid state; to cause a substance, especially blood, to clot or thicken into a mass
**中文：** v. （使）凝结；（使）凝固；（使）凝血

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clot（凝结）
- congeal（凝固）
- solidify（固化）
- thicken（变稠）
- curdle（凝结）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **blood coagulates / coagulate rapidly / cause to coagulate / coagulate into / coagulate on the surface**

👉 Example: When exposed to air, the blood began to coagulate and form a protective scab over the wound.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The proteins in milk will **coagulate** when an acidic substance such as lemon juice or vinegar is added.
   当加入柠檬汁或醋等酸性物质时，牛奶中的蛋白质会凝结。

2. Certain genetic disorders prevent blood from **coagulating** properly, which poses serious risks during surgical procedures.
   某些遗传性疾病导致血液无法正常凝固，这在外科手术中构成严重风险。

3. The industrial wastewater was treated with chemicals that caused the dissolved contaminants to **coagulate** and settle out.
   工业废水经化学品处理后，溶解的污染物凝结并沉淀下来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "coagulate" as "co- (together) + agul (from Latin agere, to drive)" — particles are driven together to form a solid clot or mass.
把"coagulate"理解为"co-（一起）+ agul（源自拉丁语agere，驱动）"——颗粒被驱动到一起形成固体凝块。
