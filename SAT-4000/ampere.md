# ⚡ ampere

🔊 /ˈæmpɪr/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The SI base unit of electric current, equal to one coulomb per second.
**中文：** 安培（电流的国际单位制基本单位）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- amp（安培，缩写）
- unit of current（电流单位）
- electrical unit（电学单位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **measured in amperes, milliampere, ampere rating, ampere-hour**
👉 Example: The circuit requires a fuse rated at fifteen amperes for safe operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The electrical engineer calculated that the device would draw approximately twelve **amperes** during peak operation.
   电气工程师计算出该设备在峰值运行期间将消耗大约十二安培的电流。

2. A standard household outlet in North America typically provides a maximum of fifteen **amperes** of current.
   北美标准家用插座通常提供最大十五安培的电流。

3. The laboratory instrument measured the current flowing through the conductor as precisely two **amperes**.
   实验室仪器精确测量出流过导体的电流为两安培。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Named after Andre-Marie Ampere, a French physicist who pioneered electromagnetism — "Ampere" the man gave us the "ampere" the unit.
以法国物理学家安德烈-玛丽·安培命名，他是电磁学的先驱——"安培"这个人给了我们"安培"这个单位。
