# ⚛️ hadron

🔊 /ˈhædrɒn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A subatomic particle composed of quarks held together by the strong nuclear force, such as protons and neutrons
**中文：** n. 强子

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- baryon（重子）
- meson（介子）
- subatomic particle（亚原子粒子）
- nucleon（核子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **hadron collider / hadron physics / hadron therapy / hadron spectroscopy**

👉 Example: The Large Hadron Collider at CERN accelerates particles to nearly the speed of light to study fundamental physics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The discovery of the Higgs boson at the Large **Hadron** Collider confirmed a crucial prediction of the Standard Model of physics.
   在大型强子对撞机上发现希格斯玻色子证实了物理学标准模型的一个关键预测。

2. Physicists classify every **hadron** as either a baryon composed of three quarks or a meson composed of a quark-antiquark pair.
   物理学家将每个强子归类为由三个夸克组成的重子或由夸克-反夸克对组成的介子。

3. Advanced **hadron** therapy uses proton beams to target cancerous tumors with greater precision than conventional radiation treatment techniques.
   先进的强子疗法使用质子束来靶向癌性肿瘤，比传统辐射治疗技术具有更高的精确度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hadron" as related to the Greek "hadros" meaning thick or strong — hadrons are particles held together by the strong force.
把"hadron"与希腊语"hadros（厚的、强壮的）"联系起来——强子是由强力结合在一起的粒子。
