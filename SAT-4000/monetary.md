# 💰 monetary

🔊 /ˈmʌnɪtɛri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to money or currency; pertaining to the financial systems or policies of a government or institution
**中文：** adj. 货币的；财政的；金融的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- financial（财务的）
- fiscal（财政的）
- pecuniary（金钱的）
- economic（经济的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **monetary policy / monetary system / monetary value / monetary aid / monetary reform**

👉 Example: The central bank adjusted its monetary policy to combat rising inflation across the economy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government implemented strict **monetary** controls to stabilize the national currency during the economic crisis.
   政府实施了严格的货币管控措施，以在经济危机期间稳定本国货币。

2. International organizations provided substantial **monetary** assistance to developing nations affected by the devastating natural disaster.
   国际组织向受到毁灭性自然灾害影响的发展中国家提供了大量资金援助。

3. Scholars continue to debate the long-term effects of expansionary **monetary** policy on sustainable economic growth and stability.
   学者们继续就扩张性货币政策对可持续经济增长和稳定的长期影响展开辩论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "money" hiding inside "monetary" — monetary is simply the adjective form of money, describing anything related to currency.
"monetary"里藏着"money"（钱）——monetary就是money的形容词形式，描述与货币相关的一切。
