# ⚠️ detrimental

🔊 /ˌdetrɪˈmentl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Causing harm or damage; injurious to health, reputation, or well-being
**中文：** adj. 不利的；有害的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmful（有害的）
- damaging（破坏性的）
- injurious（有害的）
- deleterious（有害的）
- adverse（不利的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **detrimental to health / detrimental effects / detrimental impact / prove detrimental**

👉 Example: Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation is detrimental to human skin cells.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee concluded that excessive screen time is **detrimental** to the cognitive development of young children.
   委员会得出结论，过多的屏幕时间对幼儿的认知发展是有害的。

2. Deforestation has proven profoundly **detrimental** to biodiversity in tropical regions across the developing world.
   森林砍伐已被证明对发展中国家热带地区的生物多样性极为有害。

3. The economist warned that the proposed tariffs would be **detrimental** to international trade relations and domestic growth.
   经济学家警告说，拟议的关税将对国际贸易关系和国内增长产生不利影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "detriment-al" as related to "detriment" (damage) — something detrimental brings detriment, causing harm like a mental dent.
把"detrimental"想成与"detriment（损害）"相关——detrimental的事物带来损害，就像在精神上造成了一个凹痕。
