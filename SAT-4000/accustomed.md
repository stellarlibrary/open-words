# 🔄 accustomed

🔊 /əˈkʌstəmd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Familiar with something through habit or experience; usual or customary.
**中文：** 习惯的；通常的；独有的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- habituated（习惯的）
- familiar（熟悉的）
- customary（惯常的）
- used to（习惯于）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accustomed to (something / doing something)**

👉 Example: She was accustomed to working long hours in the research laboratory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After years abroad, he had become **accustomed** to the cultural norms and social etiquette of his host country.
   在国外生活多年后，他已经习惯了所在国的文化规范和社交礼仪。

2. The soldiers were **accustomed** to operating under extreme conditions with minimal resources and provisions.
   士兵们已经习惯了在资源和补给极少的极端条件下作战。

3. Residents grew **accustomed** to the noise of construction as the new infrastructure project progressed steadily.
   随着新基础设施项目稳步推进，居民们逐渐习惯了施工的噪音。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accustomed" contains "custom" — a custom is something done regularly, so being accustomed means being used to it.
"accustomed"包含"custom（习俗）"——习俗是经常做的事情，因此accustomed表示"习惯了的"。
