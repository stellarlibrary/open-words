# 🏅 accolade

🔊 /ˈækəleɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An award, honor, or expression of praise and admiration for outstanding achievement.
**中文：** 荣誉；荣誉称号；（对杰出成就的）称赞，赞美。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- honor（荣誉）
- award（奖项）
- tribute（致敬）
- commendation（嘉奖）
- distinction（殊荣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **receive/win/earn an accolade**; **highest/ultimate/prestigious accolade**

👉 Example: Winning the Nobel Prize is considered the highest accolade in science.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The director received numerous **accolades** for her thought-provoking documentary on climate change.
   这位导演因其发人深省的气候变化纪录片而获得了众多荣誉。

2. Among the many **accolades** he earned, the Presidential Medal of Freedom held the greatest significance.
   在他获得的众多荣誉中，总统自由勋章意义最为重大。

3. The university bestowed its highest **accolade** upon the researcher for decades of pioneering contributions.
   该大学将最高荣誉授予这位研究者，以表彰其数十年的开创性贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Picture a knight receiving an "accolade" — a tap on the shoulder with a sword during a ceremony of honor.
想象一位骑士接受"accolade"——在荣誉仪式上被剑轻触肩膀，即"授勋"。
