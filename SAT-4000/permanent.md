# 🏛️ permanent

🔊 /ˈpɜːrmənənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting or intended to last indefinitely; not temporary. (n.) A hairstyle produced by setting hair in waves or curls with chemicals
**中文：** adj. 永久的；持久的；不变的 n. 烫发

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lasting（持久的）
- enduring（长久的）
- perpetual（永恒的）
- everlasting（永远的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **permanent residence / permanent damage / permanent position / permanent marker**

👉 Example: The accident caused permanent damage to the bridge, requiring a complete reconstruction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee voted to establish a **permanent** fund dedicated to supporting underprivileged students pursuing higher education.
   委员会投票设立一项永久基金，专门资助贫困学生接受高等教育。

2. Exposure to excessive noise over prolonged periods can cause **permanent** hearing loss that no medical intervention can reverse.
   长期暴露于过度噪音中可能导致任何医疗手段都无法逆转的永久性听力损失。

3. The government granted her **permanent** residency after she demonstrated significant contributions to the nation's scientific research.
   在她展示了对国家科学研究的重大贡献后，政府授予她永久居留权。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-manent" — "per" means "through" and "manent" comes from "manere" (to remain). Something permanent remains through all time.
把"permanent"想成"per（贯穿）+ manent（停留）"——永久的东西贯穿时间始终存在。
