# 🤝 collaborate

🔊 /kəˈlæbəˌreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To work jointly with others on a shared project or task; to cooperate treasonably with an enemy
**中文：** v. 合作；协作；勾结，通敌

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cooperate（合作）
- partner（搭档合作）
- team up（联手）
- conspire（共谋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **collaborate with / collaborate on / collaborate closely / collaborate effectively**

👉 Example: The two departments decided to collaborate on a comprehensive marketing strategy for the new product.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Leading scientists from multiple countries agreed to **collaborate** on the groundbreaking research initiative addressing climate change.
   来自多个国家的顶尖科学家同意就应对气候变化的突破性研究计划进行合作。

2. The composer and the lyricist chose to **collaborate** closely, producing an opera that captivated audiences worldwide.
   这位作曲家和词作者选择密切合作，创作了一部令全球观众着迷的歌剧。

3. During the occupation, citizens who **collaborated** with the enemy forces were later subjected to severe legal consequences.
   在占领期间，与敌军勾结的公民后来受到了严厉的法律制裁。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "co-labor-ate" — to "labor together" with others, working side by side on a common goal.
把"collaborate"拆成"co-labor-ate"——一起劳动，与他人并肩工作以实现共同目标。
