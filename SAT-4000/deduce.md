# 🔍 deduce

🔊 /dɪˈdjuːs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To arrive at a conclusion by reasoning from evidence or general principles; to infer logically
**中文：** v. 推论，推断；演绎出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- infer（推断）
- conclude（得出结论）
- reason（推理）
- derive（推出）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deduce from evidence / deduce that / deduce the cause / deduce the meaning**

👉 Example: The detective was able to deduce the suspect's identity from a single strand of hair found at the scene.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. From the geological evidence collected during the expedition, scientists were able to **deduce** that the region had once been submerged underwater.
   根据探险期间收集的地质证据，科学家们能够推断出该地区曾经被淹没在水下。

2. The linguist carefully analyzed the ancient manuscript and managed to **deduce** the grammatical rules of the previously undocumented language.
   语言学家仔细分析了古代手稿，成功推断出这种此前未被记录的语言的语法规则。

3. By examining the patient's symptoms and medical history, the physician was able to **deduce** the underlying cause of the illness.
   通过检查患者的症状和病史，医生能够推断出疾病的根本原因。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deduce" as "de- + duce (lead)" — to lead yourself down from general facts to a specific conclusion, like a detective following clues.
把"deduce"想成"de- + duce（引导）"——从一般事实引导自己到具体结论，就像侦探追踪线索一样。
