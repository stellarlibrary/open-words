# 👁️ perceive

🔊 /pəˈsiːv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To become aware of through the senses; to understand or interpret; to regard in a particular way
**中文：** vt. 察觉，感知；理解，领悟

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- discern（辨别）
- detect（察觉）
- comprehend（理解）
- recognize（认知）
- apprehend（领会）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perceive a threat / perceive as / perceive a difference / perceive the world**

👉 Example: Many citizens perceive the new regulation as an unnecessary restriction on individual freedom and autonomy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The study revealed that consumers **perceive** organic products as healthier, even when scientific evidence remains inconclusive.
   研究表明，消费者认为有机产品更健康，即使科学证据尚无定论。

2. Children often **perceive** the world differently from adults because their cognitive frameworks are still developing rapidly.
   儿童对世界的感知往往与成人不同，因为他们的认知框架仍在快速发展。

3. The diplomat was quick to **perceive** the subtle shift in negotiations and adjusted her strategy accordingly.
   这位外交官迅速察觉到谈判中的微妙变化，并相应地调整了策略。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perceive" as "per-ceive (receive)" — you receive information through your senses and thus perceive the world around you.
把"perceive"想成"per-ceive (receive)"——你通过感官接收信息，从而感知周围的世界。
