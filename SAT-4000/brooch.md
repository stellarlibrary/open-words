# 💎 brooch

🔊 /broʊtʃ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An ornamental pin or clasp worn on clothing, typically at the chest or collar, often set with gems
**中文：** n. （女用的）胸针，领针

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pin（别针）
- clasp（扣针）
- ornament（饰品）
- badge（徽章）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wear a brooch / diamond brooch / antique brooch / pin a brooch**

👉 Example: She pinned an elegant pearl brooch to the lapel of her tailored jacket before attending the gala.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The antique **brooch** displayed at the museum was crafted in the eighteenth century and adorned with rare sapphires.
   博物馆展出的那枚古董胸针制作于十八世纪，镶嵌着稀有的蓝宝石。

2. Her grandmother's silver **brooch** had been passed down through four generations as a cherished family heirloom.
   她祖母的银胸针作为珍贵的家族传家宝已经传承了四代。

3. The jeweler appraised the diamond **brooch** at a substantial sum, confirming its exceptional craftsmanship and historical significance.
   珠宝商对这枚钻石胸针估出了一笔可观的价值，证实了其卓越的工艺和历史意义。
