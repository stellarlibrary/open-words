# ⚙️ Iron Fe

🔊 /ˈaɪərn ɛf iː/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A chemical element with the symbol Fe and atomic number 26; a silvery-grey, lustrous metal that is essential in biological systems and widely used in industry
**中文：** n. 铁（化学元素，符号Fe，原子序数26）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ferrum（铁，拉丁语）
- metallic iron（金属铁）
- ferrous metal（黑色金属）
- steel component（钢铁成分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **iron deficiency / iron ore / iron compound / cast iron / wrought iron**

👉 Example: Iron (Fe) is the most abundant element on Earth by mass and plays a critical role in oxygen transport.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Iron** (Fe) serves as the central atom in hemoglobin, enabling red blood cells to transport oxygen throughout the body.
   铁（Fe）作为血红蛋白中的中心原子，使红细胞能够在全身运输氧气。

2. The periodic table classifies **Iron** Fe as a transition metal with remarkable magnetic and catalytic properties.
   元素周期表将铁（Fe）归类为具有显著磁性和催化性质的过渡金属。

3. Geologists confirmed that the planet's core consists primarily of **Iron** Fe and nickel under extreme pressure conditions.
   地质学家证实，该行星的核心在极端压力条件下主要由铁（Fe）和镍组成。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "Fe" as coming from the Latin word "ferrum" — just as "Fe-rrum" starts with "Fe," the chemical symbol preserves its ancient origin.
把"Fe"想成来自拉丁语"ferrum"——正如"Fe-rrum"以"Fe"开头，这个化学符号保留了它的古老起源。
