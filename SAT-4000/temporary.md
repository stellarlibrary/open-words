# ⏱️ temporary

🔊 /ˈtɛmpərɛri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lasting for only a limited period of time; not permanent. (n.) A person employed on a short-term basis
**中文：** adj. 暂时的，临时的 n. 临时工，临时雇员

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transient（短暂的）
- provisional（临时的）
- interim（过渡的）
- fleeting（稍纵即逝的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **temporary solution / temporary housing / temporary worker / temporary measure / temporary shelter**

👉 Example: The city established temporary shelters for residents displaced by the devastating flood.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The administration implemented a **temporary** ban on imports to protect domestic industries from an unprecedented surge in competition.
   政府实施了临时进口禁令，以保护国内产业免受前所未有的竞争冲击。

2. Scientists set up a **temporary** research station on the glacier to collect data before the onset of winter storms.
   科学家们在冰川上建立了一个临时研究站，以便在冬季风暴来临之前收集数据。

3. The director reassured the staff that the budget cuts were only **temporary** and would be reversed in the next fiscal year.
   主任向员工保证，预算削减只是暂时的，将在下一个财政年度恢复。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "temp-orary" — a "temp" worker only stays for a short time, just like anything temporary does not last.
把"temporary"想成"temp（临时工）"——临时工只待很短时间，就像任何暂时的事物都不会持久。
