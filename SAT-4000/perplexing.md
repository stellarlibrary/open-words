# 🤔 perplexing

🔊 /pərˈplɛksɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Causing confusion or bewilderment; difficult to understand or explain
**中文：** adj. 复杂的，令人费解的；令人困惑的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- puzzling（令人困惑的）
- bewildering（令人迷惑的）
- confounding（令人混淆的）
- baffling（令人费解的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **perplexing question / perplexing problem / perplexing situation / perplexing mystery**

👉 Example: The detective encountered a perplexing case that defied all conventional methods of investigation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team faced a **perplexing** dilemma when their experimental results contradicted all established theoretical predictions.
   研究团队面临一个令人费解的困境，因为他们的实验结果与所有既定的理论预测相矛盾。

2. Historians find it **perplexing** that such an advanced civilization could vanish without leaving any written records behind.
   历史学家感到困惑的是，如此先进的文明竟然能在没有留下任何文字记录的情况下消失。

3. The senator's sudden reversal on the trade policy was **perplexing** to both colleagues and political analysts alike.
   这位参议员在贸易政策上的突然转变令同僚和政治分析人士都感到困惑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-PLEX-ing" — something so complex that it leaves you "plexed" (twisted up) in confusion, unable to find the answer.
把"perplexing"想成"per-PLEX-ing"——某事如此复杂，让你像被"扭结"一样困惑，找不到答案。
