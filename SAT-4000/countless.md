# ♾️ countless

🔊 /ˈkaʊntləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Too many to be counted; extremely numerous; innumerable
**中文：** adj. 无数的；数不尽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- innumerable（无数的）
- myriad（无数的）
- numerous（众多的）
- incalculable（不可计数的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **countless times / countless hours / countless opportunities / countless examples**

👉 Example: The researcher spent countless hours in the laboratory refining the experimental procedure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The archaeological site has yielded **countless** artifacts that provide invaluable insight into the daily life of ancient civilizations.
   该考古遗址出土了无数文物，为了解古代文明的日常生活提供了宝贵的见解。

2. **Countless** scholars have attempted to decipher the mysterious inscription, but its meaning continues to elude definitive interpretation.
   无数学者试图破译这段神秘铭文，但其含义至今仍未得到确切解读。

3. The nonprofit organization has improved the lives of **countless** families through its comprehensive educational and healthcare outreach programs.
   该非营利组织通过其全面的教育和医疗外展项目改善了无数家庭的生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "count-less" as "less able to count" — there are so many that you simply cannot count them all.
把"countless"想成"count（计数）+ less（不能）"——多到你根本数不过来。
