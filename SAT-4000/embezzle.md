# 💰 embezzle

🔊 /ɪmˈbɛzəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To steal or misappropriate money or property entrusted to one's care, typically from an employer or organization
**中文：** v. 盗用；挪用；贪污

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- misappropriate（挪用）
- pilfer（偷窃）
- defraud（诈取）
- swindle（诈骗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **embezzle funds / embezzle money / embezzle from a company / embezzle public resources**

👉 Example: The accountant was charged with embezzling over two million dollars from the charitable foundation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The former treasurer was convicted of **embezzling** substantial sums from the nonprofit organization over a period of several years.
   前财务主管因在数年间从该非营利组织挪用大量资金而被定罪。

2. An internal audit revealed that the manager had **embezzled** company funds to finance his extravagant personal lifestyle.
   内部审计揭示该经理盗用公司资金来资助其奢侈的个人生活方式。

3. Investigators discovered that the official had systematically **embezzled** public money designated for infrastructure development projects.
   调查人员发现该官员系统性地贪污了用于基础设施建设项目的公共资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "em-BEZZLE" — someone who "bezzles" away money, sneaking it into their own pocket like a weasel.
把"embezzle"想成"em-BEZZLE"——某人像黄鼠狼一样偷偷把钱"卷走"塞进自己口袋。
