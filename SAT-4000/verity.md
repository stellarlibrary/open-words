# 📜 verity

🔊 /ˈvɛrɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A fundamental truth or principle; the quality or state of being true or real
**中文：** n. 真实；真理；事实；真实性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- truth（真理）
- reality（现实）
- fact（事实）
- axiom（公理）
- certainty（确定性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eternal verity / fundamental verity / the verity of a statement / accept as verity**

👉 Example: The philosopher dedicated his life to exploring the eternal verities that govern human existence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor argued that certain moral **verities** transcend cultural boundaries and remain constant across all civilizations.
   教授认为某些道德真理超越文化界限，在所有文明中保持不变。

2. Skeptics questioned the **verity** of the witness's testimony after several inconsistencies were discovered in the official record.
   在官方记录中发现若干不一致之处后，怀疑论者对证人证词的真实性提出了质疑。

3. Great literature often illuminates fundamental **verities** about the human condition that empirical science alone cannot fully capture.
   伟大的文学作品常常揭示关于人类境况的基本真理，这些是单凭实证科学无法完全捕捉的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "verity" as "verify + -ty" — the state of being verified, which means truth. Latin "veritas" gives us both "verity" and "verify."
把"verity"想成"verify + -ty"——被证实的状态，即真理。拉丁语"veritas"同时给了我们"verity"和"verify"。
