# ✅ satisfy

🔊 /ˈsætɪsfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To meet the expectations, needs, or desires of; to fulfill a requirement or condition; to provide adequate resolution
**中文：** v. 使…成立

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fulfill（实现）
- gratify（使满足）
- appease（安抚）
- content（使满意）
- please（使高兴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **satisfy a requirement / satisfy a need / satisfy one's curiosity / satisfy a condition / satisfy a demand**

👉 Example: The student must satisfy all prerequisite requirements before enrolling in the advanced seminar.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The evidence presented was insufficient to **satisfy** the rigorous standards of proof demanded by the appellate court.
   提交的证据不足以满足上诉法院要求的严格证明标准。

2. The new policy was specifically designed to **satisfy** the growing demands of both environmental advocates and industrial stakeholders.
   新政策是专门为满足环保倡导者和工业利益相关者日益增长的需求而设计的。

3. No single theory can fully **satisfy** the complex questions raised by recent discoveries in quantum physics and cosmology.
   没有一个单一的理论能够完全满足量子物理学和宇宙学最新发现所提出的复杂问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "satis-fy" — "satis" is Latin for "enough." To satisfy means to do enough to make someone or something complete and content.
把"satisfy"想成"satis（足够）+ fy（使）"——satisfy就是做到足够多，使某人或某事完整而满意。
