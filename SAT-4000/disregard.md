# 🙈 disregard

🔊 /ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To pay no attention to; to ignore deliberately. (n.) Lack of attention or respect
**中文：** v. 不理；不顾；忽视 n. 忽视；漠视

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ignore（忽略）
- overlook（忽视）
- neglect（疏忽）
- dismiss（不予理会）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disregard the rules / disregard advice / in disregard of / blatant disregard for / disregard a warning**

👉 Example: The company showed a blatant disregard for environmental regulations throughout its operations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board chose to **disregard** the auditor's recommendations, a decision that ultimately led to severe financial consequences.
   董事会选择无视审计师的建议，这一决定最终导致了严重的财务后果。

2. His consistent **disregard** for established safety protocols placed both himself and his colleagues in unnecessary danger.
   他对既定安全规程一贯的漠视使他本人和同事都处于不必要的危险之中。

3. The court ruled that the defendant had acted with reckless **disregard** for the rights and welfare of the affected parties.
   法院裁定被告在行事时肆意漠视了受影响各方的权利和福祉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-regard" as "dis" (not) + "regard" (to look at or consider) — to not regard something means to ignore or pay no attention to it.
把"disregard"想成"dis"（不）+"regard"（关注、考虑）——不去关注某事就意味着忽视或不予理会。
