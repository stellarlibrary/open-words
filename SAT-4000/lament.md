# 😢 lament

🔊 /ləˈmɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A passionate expression of grief; a song or poem of mourning. (v.) To mourn deeply; to express sorrow or regret
**中文：** v. 哀悼；悲叹；悔恨 n. 悲伤，哀悼；挽诗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mourn（哀悼）
- bewail（痛哭）
- grieve（悲伤）
- elegy（挽歌）
- deplore（深感遗憾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lament the loss of / lament over / a funeral lament / lament that**

👉 Example: Environmentalists lament the loss of biodiversity caused by rapid deforestation in tropical regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scholars often **lament** the decline of critical thinking skills among students in contemporary educational institutions.
   学者们经常痛惜当代教育机构中学生批判性思维能力的下降。

2. The poet composed a moving **lament** for the fallen soldiers who had sacrificed their lives defending the nation.
   诗人为那些为保卫国家而牺牲生命的阵亡将士写了一首感人的挽歌。

3. Many residents **lament** the destruction of historic buildings that were demolished to make way for modern development.
   许多居民对为了现代开发而拆除的历史建筑的毁坏表示悲叹。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "la-ment" as "la" (a musical note) + "ment" (mental) — a mental song of sadness, a mournful melody in your mind.
把"lament"想成"la（音符）"加"ment（心理的）"——心中一首悲伤的歌，脑海中哀伤的旋律。
