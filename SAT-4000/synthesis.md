# 🧬 synthesis

🔊 /ˈsɪnθəsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The combination of ideas or things into a complex whole; the production of chemical compounds by reaction from simpler materials
**中文：** n. 综合；合成

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- combination（结合）
- integration（整合）
- amalgamation（合并）
- fusion（融合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **synthesis of ideas / chemical synthesis / a synthesis of approaches / in synthesis**

👉 Example: The professor presented a brilliant synthesis of Eastern and Western philosophical traditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher achieved a remarkable **synthesis** of disparate theories into a single, coherent framework for analysis.
   研究人员将各种不同的理论综合成一个单一、连贯的分析框架，取得了显著成果。

2. The **synthesis** of the new compound required precise temperature control and several hours of careful monitoring.
   这种新化合物的合成需要精确的温度控制和数小时的仔细监测。

3. Her essay offered an impressive **synthesis** of historical evidence and modern sociological perspectives on urbanization.
   她的论文对城市化的历史证据和现代社会学观点进行了令人印象深刻的综合。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "syn-thesis" as "syn" (together) + "thesis" (placing) — placing things together to form a unified whole.
把"synthesis"想成"syn"（一起）+"thesis"（放置）——把东西放在一起形成一个统一的整体。
