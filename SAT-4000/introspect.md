# 🪞 introspect

🔊 /ˌɪntrəˈspɛkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To examine one's own thoughts, feelings, and mental processes; to engage in self-reflection
**中文：** v. 内省；内观；反省

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reflect（反思）
- contemplate（沉思）
- self-examine（自我审视）
- meditate（冥想）
- ruminate（深思）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **introspect deeply / introspect about / introspect on one's behavior / take time to introspect**

👉 Example: After the failure, she took time to introspect and identify the personal habits that had contributed to it.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The therapist encouraged her patient to **introspect** carefully before making any major life-altering decisions.
   治疗师鼓励她的病人在做出任何重大的人生决定之前仔细进行内省。

2. Philosophers have long argued that the ability to **introspect** distinguishes human consciousness from mere animal instinct.
   哲学家们长期以来认为，内省的能力将人类意识与单纯的动物本能区分开来。

3. Leaders who regularly **introspect** tend to demonstrate greater emotional intelligence and more effective decision-making skills.
   定期进行内省的领导者往往表现出更高的情商和更有效的决策能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "introspect" as "intro-spect" — "intro" means inward and "spect" means to look, so it means looking inward at yourself.
把"introspect"想成"intro（向内）-spect（看）"——向内看自己，即内省。
