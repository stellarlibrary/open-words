# 🙈 heedless

🔊 /ˈhiːdləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing a reckless lack of care or attention; not paying attention to potential risks or consequences
**中文：** adj. 不注意的；不留心的；漫不经心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- careless（粗心的）
- negligent（疏忽的）
- reckless（鲁莽的）
- inattentive（不注意的）
- oblivious（不在意的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heedless of danger / heedless of warnings / heedless of the consequences / heedless of risks**

👉 Example: The young driver was heedless of the speed limit and narrowly avoided a serious collision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation remained **heedless** of environmental regulations and continued to discharge pollutants into the nearby river.
   该公司对环境法规置若罔闻，继续向附近的河流排放污染物。

2. **Heedless** of the mounting evidence against their hypothesis, the researchers persisted with their original experimental approach.
   尽管越来越多的证据否定了他们的假设，研究人员仍然坚持原来的实验方法。

3. The administration appeared **heedless** of public opinion when it enacted the controversial taxation policy without consultation.
   政府在未经协商的情况下实施了有争议的税收政策，似乎对公众舆论毫不在意。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heed" + "less" — having less heed, meaning you lack attention and ignore what matters.
把"heedless"想成"heed（注意）+ less（缺少的）"——缺少注意力，表示你忽视了重要的事情。
