# 😨 timorous

🔊 /ˈtɪmərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing or suffering from nervousness, fear, or a lack of courage; easily frightened
**中文：** adj. 胆怯的；胆小的；畏缩的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- timid（胆怯的）
- fearful（恐惧的）
- apprehensive（忧虑的）
- fainthearted（怯懦的）
- pusillanimous（胆小的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **timorous creature / timorous nature / timorous glance / timorous approach / timorous voice**

👉 Example: The timorous deer froze at the edge of the clearing, sensing potential danger nearby.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **timorous** witness struggled to testify before the courtroom, visibly trembling under the pressure of cross-examination.
   这位胆怯的证人在法庭上艰难地作证，在交叉询问的压力下明显发抖。

2. A **timorous** leadership style often fails to inspire confidence among employees during periods of organizational crisis.
   在组织危机时期，胆怯的领导风格往往无法激发员工的信心。

3. Robert Burns famously addressed a "wee, sleek, **timorous** beastie" in his celebrated poem about a field mouse.
   罗伯特·彭斯在他关于田鼠的著名诗歌中称其为"小小的、光滑的、胆怯的小动物"。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "timorous" as "Tim or us?" — imagine someone so scared they cannot decide if Tim or they themselves should face the danger.
把"timorous"想成"Tim or us?（Tim还是我们？）"——想象一个人害怕到无法决定让Tim还是自己去面对危险。
