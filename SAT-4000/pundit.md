# 🎓 pundit

🔊 /ˈpʌndɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An expert in a particular subject who is frequently called upon to give opinions to the public; a learned person
**中文：** n. 专家；博学者；权威人士

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- expert（专家）
- authority（权威）
- commentator（评论员）
- scholar（学者）
- savant（博学之士）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political pundit / media pundit / pundit analysis / self-proclaimed pundit**

👉 Example: Television pundits offered their predictions on the outcome of the presidential election.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The economic **pundit** warned that rising inflation would inevitably lead to significant adjustments in monetary policy this fiscal year.
   这位经济专家警告说，不断上升的通胀将不可避免地导致本财年货币政策的重大调整。

2. Several political **pundits** appeared on the evening broadcast to analyze the implications of the newly proposed legislation.
   几位政治评论家出现在晚间节目中，分析新提出的立法的影响。

3. The technology **pundit** accurately predicted the rapid adoption of artificial intelligence across multiple industries over the past decade.
   这位科技专家准确预测了过去十年中人工智能在多个行业的快速普及。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pundit" as coming from Hindi "pandit" meaning a wise, learned person — a pundit is someone whose expertise others seek.
"pundit"源自印地语"pandit"，意为智者、博学者——pundit是别人寻求其专业意见的人。
