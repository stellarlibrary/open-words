# 🙏 penitent

🔊 /ˈpenɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling or showing sorrow and regret for having done wrong; repentant. (n.) A person who repents their sins or wrongdoing
**中文：** adj. 忏悔的；悔过的 n. 悔罪者，忏悔者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- repentant（悔过的）
- contrite（痛悔的）
- remorseful（悔恨的）
- apologetic（歉疚的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **penitent sinner / penitent expression / truly penitent / penitent tone**

👉 Example: The defendant appeared genuinely penitent during the sentencing hearing and expressed deep remorse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **penitent** prisoner wrote a heartfelt letter to the victim's family, expressing profound regret for his criminal actions.
   这位忏悔的囚犯给受害者家属写了一封真挚的信，对自己的犯罪行为表达了深深的悔恨。

2. With a **penitent** expression on her face, the student acknowledged her act of plagiarism before the academic integrity board.
   学生脸上带着悔过的表情，在学术诚信委员会面前承认了自己的抄袭行为。

3. The novel portrays a **penitent** protagonist who spends decades seeking redemption for the injustices he committed in his youth.
   这部小说描绘了一位忏悔的主人公，他花了几十年时间为年轻时犯下的不公正行为寻求救赎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peni-tent" — a "tent" where you go to do "penance." A penitent person seeks a quiet place to reflect on their wrongdoing.
把"penitent"想成"peni（忏悔）+ tent（帐篷）"——去帐篷里做忏悔的人，就是悔过者。
