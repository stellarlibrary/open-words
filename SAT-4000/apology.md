# 🙏 apology

🔊 /əˈpɑː.lə.dʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A regretful acknowledgment of an offense or failure; a formal expression of remorse. Also, a reasoned argument or defense in justification of something.

**中文：** 道歉；谢罪；辩护

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- regret（遗憾）
- excuse（致歉）
- confession（承认）
- defense（辩护）
- justification（正当理由）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **offer/make an apology**, **accept an apology**, **owe someone an apology**, **a formal/public apology**, **apology for** (something).

> The corporation issued a formal **apology** for the data breach that affected millions of users.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator delivered a public **apology** for the inappropriate remarks made during the televised debate.
   参议员就电视辩论期间发表的不当言论公开道歉。

2. Plato's most celebrated work is the **Apology**, a philosophical defense of Socrates at his trial.
   柏拉图最著名的作品是《申辩篇》，这是苏格拉底受审时的哲学辩护。

3. A sincere **apology** requires not merely words of remorse but a genuine commitment to changed behavior.
   真诚的道歉不仅需要悔恨的言辞，还需要真正改变行为的承诺。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Apo-" (away) + "-logy" (speech/word). An **apology** is a speech to push blame **away** — originally a defense speech, now commonly an expression of regret.

词根记忆：apo-（离开）+ -logy（言语/话）。apology 原义是"为自己辩护的话"（如柏拉图的《申辩篇》），后来演变为"道歉"——把过错推开的话语。
