# 🫁 bronchitis

🔊 /brɒŋˈkaɪtɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Inflammation of the mucous membrane in the bronchial tubes, typically causing coughing and difficulty breathing
**中文：** n. 支气管炎

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chest infection（胸部感染）
- respiratory inflammation（呼吸道炎症）
- airway disease（气道疾病）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acute bronchitis / chronic bronchitis / suffer from bronchitis / develop bronchitis**

👉 Example: The physician diagnosed the patient with acute bronchitis after a thorough examination of the respiratory system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants significantly increases the risk of developing chronic **bronchitis** over time.
   长期接触工业污染物会显著增加随时间推移患慢性支气管炎的风险。

2. The research team published findings linking cigarette smoke to elevated rates of **bronchitis** among urban populations.
   研究团队发表了将香烟烟雾与城市人口中支气管炎高发率联系起来的研究成果。

3. After weeks of persistent coughing, the specialist confirmed that the patient was suffering from **bronchitis** requiring medication.
   经过数周持续咳嗽后，专科医生确认患者患有需要药物治疗的支气管炎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bronch- (bronchial tubes) + -itis (inflammation)" — inflammation of the bronchial tubes in your lungs.
把"bronchitis"想成"bronch-（支气管）+ -itis（炎症）"——肺部支气管的炎症。
