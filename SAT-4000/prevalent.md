# 🌍 prevalent

🔊 /ˈprɛvələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Widespread in a particular area or at a particular time; commonly occurring or existing
**中文：** adj. 流行的；普遍的；盛行的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- widespread（广泛的）
- common（常见的）
- pervasive（弥漫的）
- ubiquitous（无处不在的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prevalent in / prevalent among / most prevalent / increasingly prevalent**

👉 Example: Misinformation has become increasingly prevalent on social media platforms in recent years.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Respiratory illnesses are particularly **prevalent** during the winter months, when people spend more time in enclosed indoor spaces.
   呼吸系统疾病在冬季尤为普遍，因为人们在封闭的室内空间中待的时间更长。

2. The study revealed that anxiety disorders are far more **prevalent** among university students than previously estimated by researchers.
   研究表明，焦虑症在大学生中的发病率远高于研究人员此前的估计。

3. Corruption remained **prevalent** throughout the region despite multiple reform initiatives undertaken by the newly elected administration.
   尽管新当选的政府采取了多项改革举措，腐败现象在该地区仍然盛行。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "preval-ent" as "prevail + ent" — something that prevails everywhere, dominating and spreading across a wide area.
把"prevalent"想成"prevail + ent"——某种到处"盛行"的事物，在广泛区域内占据主导地位并蔓延开来。
