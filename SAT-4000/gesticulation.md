# 🤌 gesticulation

🔊 /dʒɛˌstɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A dramatic or animated gesture or movement of the body, especially the hands and arms, used to express or emphasize something
**中文：** n. 手势；姿势；做手势传达信息的动作

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gesture（手势）
- signal（信号）
- motion（动作）
- pantomime（哑剧动作）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **wild gesticulation / frantic gesticulation / gesticulation and speech / accompany with gesticulation**

👉 Example: His animated gesticulation made it clear he was passionate about the subject.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The diplomat's vigorous **gesticulation** during the press conference conveyed far more urgency than his measured words.
   这位外交官在新闻发布会上激烈的手势比他沉稳的话语传达了更多的紧迫感。

2. Researchers studying nonverbal communication found that **gesticulation** varies significantly across different cultural contexts.
   研究非语言交流的学者发现，手势动作在不同文化背景下差异显著。

3. The witness accompanied her testimony with frequent **gesticulation**, pointing and waving to illustrate the sequence of events.
   证人在作证时频繁地做手势，指指点点以说明事件的经过。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gesticulation" as "gesture" + "ulation" — it is an exaggerated, dramatic form of gesturing with your whole body.
把"gesticulation"想成"gesture"（手势）的加强版——用整个身体做出夸张、戏剧性的手势动作。
