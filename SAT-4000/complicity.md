# 🤝 complicity

🔊 /kəmˈplɪsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being involved with others in an unlawful or morally wrong activity; partnership in wrongdoing
**中文：** n. 共谋；串通；共犯关系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- collusion（勾结）
- connivance（纵容）
- conspiracy（阴谋）
- collaboration（合谋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **complicity in a crime / silent complicity / alleged complicity / complicity with someone**

👉 Example: The investigation revealed her complicity in the financial fraud that defrauded thousands of investors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosecutor argued that the executive's **complicity** in the embezzlement scheme warranted the maximum sentence under federal law.
   检察官认为，该高管在挪用公款计划中的共谋行为应依据联邦法律判处最高刑罚。

2. Through their silence and inaction, the board members demonstrated a troubling **complicity** in the corporation's unethical practices.
   通过沉默和不作为，董事会成员表现出对公司不道德行为令人不安的默许共谋。

3. Historians continue to debate the degree of public **complicity** in the authoritarian regime's systematic oppression of minority groups.
   历史学家继续争论公众在独裁政权对少数群体的系统性压迫中的共谋程度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "complicity" as "com-PLI-city" — an "accomplice in the city" who is secretly involved in wrongdoing.
把"complicity"想成"同伙在城市里"——一个秘密参与不法行为的共犯。
