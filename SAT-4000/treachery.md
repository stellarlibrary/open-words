# 🗡️ treachery

🔊 /ˈtretʃəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Betrayal of trust; deceptive action or nature; an act of deliberate disloyalty or treason
**中文：** n. 背叛；变节；背信弃义的行为

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- betrayal（背叛）
- perfidy（不忠）
- treason（叛国）
- duplicity（口是心非）
- disloyalty（不忠诚）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **an act of treachery / political treachery / expose treachery / guilty of treachery**

👉 Example: The general's treachery was discovered only after the battle had been lost.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novel explores themes of **treachery** and redemption as the protagonist uncovers a conspiracy within the royal court.
   这部小说探讨了背叛与救赎的主题，主人公揭露了皇室内部的阴谋。

2. History records numerous instances of political **treachery** in which trusted advisors secretly undermined their own sovereigns.
   历史记载了许多政治背叛的案例，其中受信任的顾问暗中破坏了自己的君主。

3. The soldier's **treachery** in revealing troop positions to the enemy resulted in a devastating and catastrophic defeat.
   这名士兵向敌人泄露部队位置的叛变行为导致了一场毁灭性的惨败。
