# 🌈 utopian

🔊 /juːˈtoʊpiən/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or resembling an imagined perfect society; idealistic and impractical. (n.) An idealist who believes in or pursues a perfect social order
**中文：** adj. 乌托邦的；空想的 n. 空想家

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- idealistic（理想主义的）
- visionary（空想的）
- quixotic（不切实际的）
- impractical（不现实的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **utopian vision / utopian society / utopian ideals / utopian dream / utopian scheme**

👉 Example: The philosopher's utopian vision of a classless society inspired generations of political reformers.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political theorist outlined a **utopian** society in which poverty, inequality, and conflict had been permanently eliminated.
   这位政治理论家描绘了一个永久消除了贫困、不平等和冲突的乌托邦社会。

2. Critics dismissed the proposed economic reforms as **utopian** fantasies that ignored the fundamental realities of human nature.
   批评者将拟议的经济改革斥为忽视人性基本现实的空想幻想。

3. The architect's **utopian** design for a self-sustaining city attracted both admiration from visionaries and skepticism from engineers.
   这位建筑师关于自给自足城市的乌托邦设计既赢得了空想家的赞赏，也招来了工程师的质疑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "utopian" from Thomas More's "Utopia" — "u-topia" means "no place" in Greek, because a perfect society exists nowhere.
想想"utopian"来自托马斯·莫尔的《乌托邦》——"u-topia"在希腊语中意为"没有的地方"，因为完美社会不存在于任何地方。
