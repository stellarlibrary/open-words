# 🌊 erode

🔊 /ɪˈroʊd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To gradually wear away or destroy by natural forces; to diminish or deteriorate over time
**中文：** v. 侵蚀；腐蚀；逐渐削弱

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corrode（腐蚀）
- deteriorate（恶化）
- wear away（磨损）
- diminish（减少）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **erode confidence / erode trust / erode the foundation / gradually erode**

👉 Example: Years of neglect had begun to erode the foundation of the historic bridge.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Persistent inflation can **erode** the purchasing power of consumers, disproportionately affecting lower-income households and retirement savings over time.
   持续的通货膨胀会侵蚀消费者的购买力，随着时间推移对低收入家庭和退休储蓄造成不成比例的影响。

2. The relentless waves continued to **erode** the coastal cliffs, threatening several residential properties situated dangerously close to the edge.
   无情的海浪持续侵蚀海岸悬崖，威胁着几处危险地靠近边缘的住宅物业。

3. Repeated scandals have severely **eroded** public trust in governmental institutions, making meaningful policy reform increasingly difficult to accomplish.
   一再的丑闻严重侵蚀了公众对政府机构的信任，使有意义的政策改革越来越难以实现。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-rode" as something that "rode away" — water rides over rock and slowly wears it down to nothing.
把"erode"想成某物"rode away（骑走了）"——水流经岩石，慢慢将其磨蚀殆尽。
