# 😱 shriek

🔊 /ʃriːk/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To utter a loud, sharp, piercing cry; to call attention urgently. (n.) A loud, sharp, high-pitched sound or cry
**中文：** n. 尖声叫喊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scream（尖叫）
- screech（尖叫声）
- howl（嚎叫）
- wail（哀号）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shriek with terror / shriek of laughter / let out a shriek / shriek in pain**

👉 Example: The audience let out a collective shriek when the unexpected twist was revealed on stage.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A piercing **shriek** echoed through the empty corridor, startling the security guard into immediate investigation.
   一声刺耳的尖叫在空荡荡的走廊里回荡，惊得保安立即前去调查。

2. The brakes of the locomotive **shrieked** as the engineer desperately attempted to halt the train before the obstruction.
   工程师拼命试图在障碍物前停住火车，机车的刹车发出刺耳的尖叫声。

3. The headline seemed to **shriek** its warning about the impending financial crisis that economists had long predicted.
   这则标题似乎在大声疾呼，警告经济学家们早已预测到的即将到来的金融危机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "shriek" as sounding like the noise itself — the "sh" and "eek" mimic a sharp, startled cry of alarm.
把"shriek"想成它本身的声音——"sh"和"eek"模仿了一声尖锐、惊慌的叫喊。
