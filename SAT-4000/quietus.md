# ⚰️ quietus

🔊 /kwaɪˈiːtəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Something that serves to suppress, check, or eliminate; death or a final settlement; release from life or obligation
**中文：** n. 解除；偿清；生命的终止；寂灭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- death（死亡）
- termination（终结）
- release（解脱）
- settlement（清偿）
- coup de grace（致命一击）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **give the quietus to / put the quietus on / receive one's quietus / final quietus**

👉 Example: The Supreme Court ruling put the quietus on any further attempts to challenge the legislation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The devastating economic report gave the **quietus** to the senator's ambitious plan for expanding federal infrastructure spending.
   这份毁灭性的经济报告终结了参议员扩大联邦基础设施支出的雄心勃勃的计划。

2. After years of prolonged suffering, the philosopher wrote poignantly about seeking a peaceful **quietus** from worldly burdens.
   在多年的长期痛苦之后，这位哲学家深情地写下了寻求从尘世负担中平静解脱的文字。

3. The merger effectively served as a **quietus** for the smaller company, ending its independent operations after three decades.
   这次合并实际上成了那家小公司的终结，结束了其三十年的独立运营。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quietus" as the ultimate "quiet" — the final silence of death or the settling of all debts, making everything quiet forever.
把"quietus"想成终极的"quiet"——死亡的最终沉默或所有债务的清偿，让一切永远安静。
