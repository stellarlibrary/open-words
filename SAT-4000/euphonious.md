# 🎵 euphonious

🔊 /juːˈfoʊniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Pleasing to the ear; having a pleasant or harmonious sound
**中文：** adj. 悦耳的；声音和谐的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- melodious（悦耳的）
- harmonious（和谐的）
- dulcet（甜美的）
- mellifluous（流畅动听的）
- tuneful（音调优美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **euphonious voice / euphonious melody / euphonious tone / euphonious language**

👉 Example: The poet deliberately chose euphonious words to create a soothing rhythm throughout the verse.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The composer arranged the instruments to produce a **euphonious** blend that captivated every listener in the concert hall.
   作曲家编排乐器以产生悦耳的和声，吸引了音乐厅里的每一位听众。

2. Her **euphonious** reading of the classical sonnet transformed the ordinary classroom into a space of quiet literary reverence.
   她对经典十四行诗悦耳的朗读将普通的教室变成了一个安静的文学敬仰之地。

3. Linguists note that certain languages are perceived as more **euphonious** than others due to their vowel-consonant distribution patterns.
   语言学家指出，由于元音和辅音的分布模式，某些语言被认为比其他语言更悦耳。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eu-phon-ious" as "eu" (good) + "phon" (sound) — literally "good sound," something pleasant to hear.
把"euphonious"想成"eu"（好的）+ "phon"（声音）——字面意思是"好的声音"，即悦耳动听的。
