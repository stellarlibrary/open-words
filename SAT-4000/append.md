# 📎 append

🔊 /əˈpɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To add something as a supplement or attachment to the end of a document, list, or other item
**中文：** 附加；贴上；增补

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attach（附上）
- affix（粘贴）
- supplement（补充）
- annex（附加）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **append a note / append data / append to a document / append a signature**

👉 Example: The author decided to append a glossary to the end of the manuscript for clarity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher chose to **append** a detailed bibliography to the final chapter of her doctoral dissertation.
   这位研究者选择在她的博士论文最后一章附加一份详细的参考书目。

2. Please **append** your digital signature to the contract before submitting it through the official online portal.
   请在通过官方在线门户提交合同之前附上您的电子签名。

3. The editor asked the journalist to **append** several clarifying footnotes to address potential reader confusion.
   编辑要求记者附加几个注释性脚注，以解决读者可能产生的困惑。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "append" as "ap-PEND" — "pend" sounds like "pending," something hanging at the end, waiting to be added on.
把"append"想成"ap-PEND"——"pend"听起来像"pending"（悬挂），就像某物挂在末尾，等待被加上去。
