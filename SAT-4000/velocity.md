# 🚀 velocity

🔊 /vəˈlɑːsɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The speed of something in a given direction; the rate at which an object changes its position
**中文：** n. 【物】速度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- speed（速度）
- rapidity（迅速）
- swiftness（敏捷）
- pace（步速）
- celerity（快速）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **high velocity / terminal velocity / velocity of light / escape velocity / at great velocity**

👉 Example: The physics professor explained how the velocity of a projectile determines the shape of its trajectory.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Engineers calculated that the spacecraft must reach an escape **velocity** of approximately 11.2 kilometers per second to leave Earth's gravity.
   工程师们计算出航天器必须达到大约每秒11.2公里的逃逸速度才能脱离地球引力。

2. The **velocity** of technological change in the twenty-first century has fundamentally transformed how societies communicate and share information.
   二十一世纪技术变革的速度从根本上改变了社会沟通和分享信息的方式。

3. Researchers measured the **velocity** of blood flow through the arterial system using advanced ultrasound imaging techniques.
   研究人员使用先进的超声成像技术测量了动脉系统中血液流动的速度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "velocity" as containing "veloc-" like "velociraptor" — the fast dinosaur. Both words share the Latin root "velox" meaning swift.
把"velocity"想成包含"veloc-"，就像"velociraptor（迅猛龙）"一样。两个词都源自拉丁语词根"velox"，意为迅速。
