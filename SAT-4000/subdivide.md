# ✂️ subdivide

🔊 /ˌsʌbdɪˈvaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To divide further into smaller parts or sections; to partition again
**中文：** v. 再分；细分

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- partition（分割）
- segment（划分）
- bisect（二等分）
- split（拆分）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subdivide land / subdivide into sections / subdivide a property / subdivide further**

👉 Example: The city council voted to subdivide the large parcel of land into smaller residential lots for development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The urban planner proposed to **subdivide** the industrial district into zones designated for commercial, residential, and recreational use.
   城市规划师提议将工业区细分为商业、住宅和休闲用途的区域。

2. Researchers chose to **subdivide** the experimental group into three smaller cohorts to better analyze the varying treatment outcomes.
   研究人员选择将实验组再分为三个较小的队列，以便更好地分析不同的治疗结果。

3. The estate was **subdivided** among the heirs according to the detailed provisions outlined in the original legal testament.
   该遗产根据原始法律遗嘱中列出的详细条款在继承人之间进行了细分。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "subdivide" as "sub + divide" — to divide something that has already been divided, breaking it into even smaller sub-parts.
把"subdivide"想成"sub + divide"——对已经分过的东西再进行划分，将其分成更小的部分。
