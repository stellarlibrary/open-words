# 🎐 aerostat

🔊 /ˈɛərəˌstæt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A lighter-than-air aircraft, such as a balloon or airship, that gains lift from a buoyant gas.
**中文：** 航空器；高空气球。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- airship（飞艇）
- balloon（气球）
- dirigible（飞艇）
- blimp（软式飞艇）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tethered, military, surveillance, high-altitude, deploy**
👉 Example: The military deployed a tethered aerostat for long-range surveillance operations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The tethered **aerostat** remained suspended above the base to provide continuous aerial surveillance of the region.
   系留的高空气球悬停在基地上方，为该地区提供持续的空中监视。

2. Engineers designed a new **aerostat** capable of withstanding extreme weather conditions at high altitudes.
   工程师们设计了一种能够承受高空极端天气条件的新型航空器。

3. Before the invention of the airplane, the **aerostat** represented humanity's most reliable means of aerial travel.
   在飞机发明之前，航空气球代表着人类最可靠的空中旅行方式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Aero" (air) + "stat" (standing still) — an aerostat stands still in the air, floating in place.
"aero"（空气）+"stat"（静止）——aerostat就是静止在空中、漂浮不动的装置。
