# 🏥 infirmary

🔊 /ɪnˈfɜːrməri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A place in a large institution, such as a school or prison, for the care of those who are ill; a small hospital or clinic
**中文：** n. 医务室；医院；养老院

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clinic（诊所）
- hospital（医院）
- sickbay（医务室）
- dispensary（药房）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **school infirmary / campus infirmary / sent to the infirmary / treated at the infirmary**

👉 Example: The injured student was escorted to the school infirmary for immediate medical attention.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After collapsing during physical training, the cadet was immediately transported to the military base **infirmary** for evaluation.
   在体能训练中晕倒后，这名学员被立即送往军事基地医务室接受检查。

2. The university expanded its campus **infirmary** to accommodate the growing number of students seeking mental health services.
   该大学扩建了校园医务室，以容纳越来越多寻求心理健康服务的学生。

3. During the epidemic, the prison **infirmary** was overwhelmed with patients and had to convert additional wards for treatment.
   在疫情期间，监狱医务室的病人不堪重负，不得不改建更多病房用于治疗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "infirm" + "ary" — an "infirm" person is weak or sick, and the "-ary" suffix means a place, so an infirmary is a place for the sick.
把"infirmary"想成"infirm（虚弱的）" + "ary（场所）"——虚弱的人待的地方，就是医务室。
