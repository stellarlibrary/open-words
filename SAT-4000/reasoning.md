# 🧠 reasoning

🔊 /ˈriːzənɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of thinking logically about something in order to form a conclusion or judgment; argumentation
**中文：** n. 推理；论证；评理

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- logic（逻辑）
- deduction（推论）
- rationale（理由）
- argumentation（论证）
- inference（推断）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **logical reasoning / line of reasoning / deductive reasoning / flawed reasoning**

👉 Example: The scientist's reasoning led to a groundbreaking hypothesis about the origins of the universe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor challenged students to examine the **reasoning** behind each philosophical argument presented in the seminal text.
   教授要求学生审视这部经典著作中每个哲学论点背后的推理。

2. Her **reasoning** was impeccable, and the board unanimously accepted the proposal after reviewing her thorough analysis.
   她的推理无懈可击，董事会在审阅了她的详尽分析后一致通过了提案。

3. Critics argued that the administration's **reasoning** for the policy change was based on incomplete data and faulty assumptions.
   批评者认为政府改变政策的论据是基于不完整的数据和错误的假设。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reason-ing" as "the act of using reason" — reasoning is your mind's engine working through a problem step by step.
把"reasoning"想成"运用理性的过程"——推理就是你的大脑一步步解决问题的过程。
