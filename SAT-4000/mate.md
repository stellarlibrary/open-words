# 🤝 mate

🔊 /meɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A companion or fellow worker; a spouse or partner; an assistant. (v.) To pair or bring together for breeding or partnership
**中文：** n. 助手；配偶；同事；伙伴 v. 使配对；结伴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- companion（同伴）
- partner（伙伴；配偶）
- colleague（同事）
- spouse（配偶）
- pair（配对）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **running mate / classmate / roommate / mate for life / first mate**

👉 Example: Many species of birds mate for life and share the responsibility of raising their young together.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biologist observed that the endangered eagles would carefully select a **mate** based on elaborate courtship displays and territorial calls.
   生物学家观察到，这种濒危鹰会根据精心的求偶展示和领地叫声来谨慎选择配偶。

2. The captain relied heavily on his first **mate** to manage the crew during the long and treacherous voyage across the Atlantic.
   船长在漫长而危险的横渡大西洋航行中，非常依赖大副来管理船员。

3. Conservationists developed a program to **mate** captive pandas in hopes of increasing the dwindling population of this beloved species.
   自然保护主义者制定了一个为圈养大熊猫配对的计划，希望增加这一珍贵物种日益减少的种群数量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "mate" as someone you meet and match with — your classmate, roommate, or life mate; they all share your space or journey.
把"mate"想成你遇见并匹配的人——你的同学（classmate）、室友（roommate）或伴侣（life mate）；他们都与你共享空间或旅程。
