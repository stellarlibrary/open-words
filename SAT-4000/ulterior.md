# 🎭 ulterior

🔊 /ʌlˈtɪriər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Existing beyond what is obvious or admitted; intentionally hidden; relating to the future
**中文：** 隐秘的；不可告人的；将来的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- hidden（隐藏的）
- covert（隐秘的）
- undisclosed（未公开的）
- concealed（隐蔽的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ulterior motive / ulterior purpose / ulterior design / ulterior intention**

👉 Example: The detective suspected that the witness had an ulterior motive for providing such detailed testimony.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee questioned whether the donor's generous contribution was driven by **ulterior** motives rather than genuine philanthropy.
   委员会质疑捐赠者的慷慨捐助是否出于不可告人的动机，而非真正的慈善之心。

2. Although he presented a reasonable argument, his colleagues sensed an **ulterior** purpose behind his sudden proposal.
   尽管他提出了合理的论点，同事们仍感觉到他突然提议背后有隐秘的目的。

3. The diplomat's **ulterior** agenda became apparent only after the treaty negotiations had completely collapsed.
   直到条约谈判彻底破裂后，那位外交官的隐秘意图才变得显而易见。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ulterior" as "ultra-interior" — something buried so deep inside that it is hidden from view.
把"ulterior"想成"ultra-interior"（极度内在的）——某种深藏在内部、不被人看见的东西。
