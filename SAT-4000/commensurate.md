# ⚖️ commensurate

🔊 /kəˈmen.sə.rət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Corresponding in size, extent, or degree; proportionate or adequate
**中文：** adj. 相称的；相当的；同量的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- proportionate（成比例的）
- corresponding（相应的）
- equivalent（等同的）
- comparable（可比较的）
- appropriate（适当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commensurate with experience / commensurate with effort / commensurate with the task / salary commensurate with**

👉 Example: The company offers a salary commensurate with the candidate's qualifications and experience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Employees expect compensation that is **commensurate** with the level of responsibility and expertise their positions demand.
   员工期望获得与其职位所要求的责任和专业水平相称的薪酬。

2. The punishment imposed by the court was deemed **commensurate** with the severity of the offense committed by the defendant.
   法院所施加的惩罚被认为与被告所犯罪行的严重程度相称。

3. The organization allocated resources **commensurate** with the scale of the humanitarian crisis unfolding in the affected region.
   该组织分配了与受灾地区人道主义危机规模相当的资源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-MENSUR-ate" — it contains "mensur" from Latin "mensura" (measure), meaning things are measured together and found to be equal.
把"commensurate"想成"com-MENSUR-ate"——其中包含"mensur"，来自拉丁语"mensura（测量）"，意味着事物被一起衡量并发现是相等的。
