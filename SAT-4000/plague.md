# 💀 plague

🔊 /pleɪɡ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A widespread infectious disease causing high mortality; a disaster or affliction. (v.) To cause continual trouble or distress to someone
**中文：** n. 瘟疫；灾祸；麻烦；讨厌的人 v. 折磨；使苦恼；使得灾祸

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pestilence（瘟疫）
- epidemic（流行病）
- affliction（折磨）
- torment（使痛苦）
- blight（损害）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **plague of locusts / plagued by doubts / plagued with problems / avoid like the plague**

👉 Example: The city was plagued by a series of infrastructure failures throughout the winter months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Financial scandals continued to **plague** the corporation, eroding investor confidence and driving stock prices to historic lows.
   财务丑闻持续困扰着该公司，侵蚀了投资者信心并将股价推至历史低点。

2. The bubonic **plague** devastated Europe in the fourteenth century, killing an estimated one-third of the entire continental population.
   腺鼠疫在十四世纪摧毁了欧洲，据估计夺走了整个大陆三分之一人口的生命。

3. Technical difficulties **plagued** the space mission from its inception, ultimately forcing engineers to delay the launch by several months.
   技术难题从一开始就困扰着这次太空任务，最终迫使工程师将发射推迟了数月。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plague" as "play + ugly" — when a plague plays out, things get ugly fast with disease and devastation everywhere.
把"plague"想成"play + ugly"——当瘟疫蔓延时，到处都是疾病和毁灭，情况迅速变得很糟糕。
