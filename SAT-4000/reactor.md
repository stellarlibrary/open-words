# ☢️ reactor

🔊 /riˈæktər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An apparatus or structure in which nuclear fission or fusion is initiated and controlled; a container in which chemical reactions take place
**中文：** n. 反应器；反应堆；核反应堆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- nuclear reactor（核反应堆）
- converter（转换器）
- generator（发生器）
- pile（原子反应堆）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nuclear reactor / reactor core / reactor vessel / build a reactor / shut down a reactor**

👉 Example: Engineers worked around the clock to safely shut down the reactor after detecting an anomaly in the cooling system.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government approved the construction of a new nuclear **reactor** to meet the country's growing demand for clean energy.
   政府批准建造一座新的核反应堆，以满足国家对清洁能源日益增长的需求。

2. Scientists at the research facility successfully tested an experimental fusion **reactor** that could revolutionize global energy production.
   研究设施的科学家成功测试了一座实验性聚变反应堆，这可能彻底改变全球能源生产。

3. The aging **reactor** was decommissioned after forty years of operation, and the site entered a lengthy remediation process.
   这座老化的反应堆在运行四十年后退役，该场地进入了漫长的修复过程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reactor" as "react + or (one who)" — a reactor is something that facilitates reactions, especially nuclear ones that generate enormous power.
把"reactor"拆成"react（反应）+ or（……的东西）"——反应堆就是促进反应的装置，尤其是产生巨大能量的核反应。
