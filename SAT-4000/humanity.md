# 🌍 humanity

🔊 /hjuːˈmænəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The human race collectively; the quality of being humane or benevolent; (plural) branches of learning concerned with human culture, such as literature, philosophy, and history
**中文：** n. 人类；人性；人情；仁慈；人文学科

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- humankind（人类）
- compassion（同情）
- benevolence（仁慈）
- civilization（文明）
- the human race（人类）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **crimes against humanity / for the benefit of humanity / the humanities / show humanity / common humanity**

👉 Example: The discovery was hailed as one of the greatest achievements in the history of humanity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that **humanity** must confront climate change collectively or face irreversible consequences for future generations.
   这位哲学家认为人类必须共同应对气候变化，否则将给后代带来不可逆转的后果。

2. The doctor showed great **humanity** by volunteering in conflict zones where medical resources were desperately scarce.
   这位医生通过在医疗资源极度匮乏的冲突地区做志愿者展现了伟大的人道精神。

3. Universities require students to study the **humanities** to develop critical thinking and a deeper understanding of cultural heritage.
   大学要求学生学习人文学科，以培养批判性思维和对文化遗产更深层的理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "humanity" as "human" + "-ity" (quality) — the collective quality of being human, encompassing both our species and our capacity for kindness.
把"humanity"拆成"human"（人类）+"-ity"（性质）——作为人类的集体特质，既包含我们的物种，也包含我们善良的能力。
