# 🧪 acid

🔊 /ˈæsɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A chemical substance with a pH below 7; (adj.) having a sharp, sour taste; bitterly critical.
**中文：** (n.) 酸；(adj.) 酸的；讽刺的；刻薄的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- caustic（腐蚀性的）
- sour（酸的）
- biting（尖刻的）
- acerbic（尖酸的）
- corrosive（腐蚀的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **acid rain / test / tongue / remark / reaction**

👉 Example: His acid remarks about the proposal surprised everyone at the meeting.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The industrial emissions contributed to **acid** rain that devastated forests and contaminated freshwater lakes.
   工业排放导致的酸雨摧毁了森林并污染了淡水湖泊。

2. The journalist's **acid** commentary on political corruption earned her both admiration and powerful enemies.
   这位记者对政治腐败的尖刻评论为她赢得了赞赏，也树立了强大的敌人。

3. Chemists carefully measured the concentration of the **acid** solution before proceeding with the controlled experiment.
   化学家们在进行对照实验之前，仔细测量了酸溶液的浓度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Acid" comes from Latin "acidus" (sour). Picture tasting a lemon — that sour, sharp sensation captures both the literal and figurative meanings.
"acid"源自拉丁语"acidus（酸的）"。想象品尝柠檬——那种酸涩、尖锐的感觉同时体现了字面和比喻含义。
