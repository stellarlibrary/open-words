# 🪨 engrave

🔊 /ɪnˈɡreɪv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cut or carve a design or text into a hard surface; to impress deeply on the mind or memory
**中文：** v. 雕刻；铭记；深深印入

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carve（雕刻）
- etch（蚀刻）
- inscribe（铭刻）
- imprint（印刻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **engrave on stone / engrave in memory / engraved invitation / deeply engraved**

👉 Example: The artisan carefully engraved an intricate floral pattern onto the surface of the silver pendant.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The craftsman meticulously **engraved** the family crest onto the antique gold ring that had been passed down through generations.
   工匠精心地将家族纹章雕刻在这枚世代相传的古董金戒指上。

2. The harrowing experiences of wartime were permanently **engraved** in the survivor's memory, shaping her worldview for life.
   战争时期的惨痛经历永久地铭刻在幸存者的记忆中，塑造了她一生的世界观。

3. The monument's base was **engraved** with the names of every soldier who had served and sacrificed during the conflict.
   纪念碑的底座上刻着每一位在冲突中服役和牺牲的士兵的名字。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "engrave" as "en-GRAVE" — carving words into a gravestone, making a permanent and deep impression that lasts forever.
把"engrave"想成"en-GRAVE"——在墓碑上刻字，留下永久而深刻的印记。
