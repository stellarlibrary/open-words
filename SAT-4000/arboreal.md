# 🐒 arboreal

🔊 /ɑːrˈbɔːriəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of, relating to, or resembling trees; living in or habitually found among trees
**中文：** adj. 树木的；栖息在树上的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tree-dwelling（栖树的）
- sylvan（森林的）
- wooded（树木繁茂的）
- dendritic（树状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **arboreal species / arboreal habitat / arboreal primates / arboreal lifestyle / arboreal creatures**

👉 Example: Many arboreal primates have evolved long limbs and prehensile tails for life in the treetops.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **arboreal** lifestyle of orangutans requires exceptionally strong arms for swinging between branches high above the forest floor.
   猩猩的树栖生活方式需要格外强壮的手臂，以便在高于地面的树枝之间摆荡。

2. Scientists discovered a new **arboreal** species of lizard that spends its entire life cycle in the canopy of tropical rainforests.
   科学家发现了一种新的树栖蜥蜴物种，其整个生命周期都在热带雨林的树冠中度过。

3. The region's **arboreal** landscapes provide essential habitats for countless bird species that depend on mature forest ecosystems.
   该地区的树木景观为无数依赖成熟森林生态系统的鸟类物种提供了重要的栖息地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Arboreal" comes from "arbor" (tree) + "-eal" (relating to). Picture a monkey living its arboreal life, leaping from arbor to arbor.
"Arboreal"来自"arbor"（树）+"-eal"（与……有关的）。想象一只猴子过着树栖生活，从一棵树跳到另一棵树。
