# ⏱️ isochronous

🔊 /aɪˈsɒkrənəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Occurring at equal time intervals; having a uniform period of vibration or oscillation
**中文：** 等时的；等步的；同步的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- synchronous（同步的）
- simultaneous（同时的）
- periodic（周期的）
- uniform（均匀的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **isochronous oscillation / isochronous transfer / isochronous signal / isochronous intervals**

👉 Example: The pendulum exhibits isochronous motion, completing each swing in exactly the same amount of time.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Galileo famously observed that the swinging of a chandelier appeared to be **isochronous**, regardless of the amplitude of each oscillation.
   伽利略曾著名地观察到吊灯的摆动似乎是等时的，与每次振荡的幅度无关。

2. USB technology relies on **isochronous** data transfer to ensure continuous streaming of audio and video without interruption.
   USB技术依赖等时数据传输来确保音频和视频的连续流式传输不中断。

3. The engineer designed the circuit to produce **isochronous** pulses, ensuring that each signal was transmitted at precisely equal intervals.
   工程师设计了该电路以产生等时脉冲，确保每个信号以精确相等的间隔传输。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "iso-chronous" as "iso (equal) + chronos (time)" — equal time, like a perfectly regular clock ticking at identical intervals.
把"isochronous"拆分为"iso-（相等的）+ chronos（时间）"——时间相等的，就像一个以完全相同间隔滴答作响的完美时钟。
