# 💡 incandescent

🔊 /ˌɪnkænˈdɛsənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Emitting light as a result of being heated; glowing with intense heat; extremely bright or passionate
**中文：** adj. 辉耀的；炽热的；发白热光的；热情的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- radiant（发光的）
- luminous（明亮的）
- glowing（灼热发光的）
- brilliant（光辉灿烂的）
- fervent（炽热的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incandescent light / incandescent bulb / incandescent rage / incandescent with fury / incandescent performance**

👉 Example: The incandescent filament in the bulb produced a warm, steady glow that illuminated the entire hall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **incandescent** glow of molten lava illuminated the volcanic landscape, creating an atmosphere both beautiful and terrifying.
   炽热的熔岩光芒照亮了火山地貌，营造出一种既美丽又令人恐惧的氛围。

2. The senator delivered an **incandescent** speech that galvanized supporters and fundamentally altered the trajectory of the campaign.
   这位参议员发表了一场激情澎湃的演讲，激励了支持者并从根本上改变了竞选的走向。

3. Early twentieth-century **incandescent** lighting revolutionized urban life by extending productive hours well beyond the natural daylight.
   二十世纪初期的白炽灯照明彻底改变了城市生活，将生产时间大大延伸至自然日光之后。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-CANDLE-scent" — picture a candle glowing so intensely that it becomes white-hot; that is what incandescent means.
把"incandescent"想成"in-CANDLE（蜡烛）-scent"——想象一支蜡烛发出如此强烈的光芒，变得白热化；这就是incandescent的含义。
