# ⚡ electrification

🔊 /ɪˌlektrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of supplying electrical power to something; the conversion of a system to operate by electricity; the state of being charged with electricity
**中文：** n. 电气化；带电；起电

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- electrifying（通电）
- energization（通电）
- charging（充电）
- powering（供电）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rural electrification / railway electrification / electrification of transport / electrification project**

👉 Example: The electrification of rural villages transformed daily life by enabling access to modern technology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **electrification** of the national railway system significantly reduced carbon emissions and improved overall transportation efficiency.
   国家铁路系统的电气化显著减少了碳排放，并提高了整体运输效率。

2. Rural **electrification** programs brought reliable power to remote communities that had previously relied on kerosene lamps and candles.
   农村电气化项目为之前依赖煤油灯和蜡烛的偏远社区带来了可靠的电力。

3. The government allocated substantial funding to accelerate the **electrification** of public transportation across all major metropolitan areas.
   政府拨出大量资金以加速所有主要大城市地区公共交通的电气化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "electri-fication" as "electric" + "making" — electrification is the process of making something electric or powered by electricity.
把"electrification"想成"electric（电的）"加"making（制造）"——电气化就是使某物通电或用电力驱动的过程。
