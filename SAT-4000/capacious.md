# 🏛️ capacious

🔊 /kəˈpeɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a lot of space inside; roomy; able to hold a large amount.
**中文：** adj. 容积大的；宽敞的；广阔的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spacious（宽敞的）
- roomy（宽大的）
- voluminous（容量大的）
- expansive（广阔的）
- ample（充裕的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **capacious room / capacious bag / capacious mind / capacious memory / capacious interior**

👉 Example: The researcher's capacious memory allowed her to recall obscure details from decades-old studies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The library's **capacious** reading hall could accommodate several hundred scholars working simultaneously at long oak tables.
   图书馆宽敞的阅览大厅可以容纳数百名学者同时在长橡木桌前工作。

2. She carried a **capacious** leather bag that held her laptop, research notes, and several reference volumes.
   她携带着一个容量很大的皮包，里面装着笔记本电脑、研究笔记和几本参考书。

3. The architect designed a **capacious** atrium that served as both a gathering space and a natural light source.
   建筑师设计了一个宽敞的中庭，既用作聚会空间，又是自然光源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "capacity" — something capacious has great capacity, meaning it can hold a lot inside.
"capacious"与"capacity（容量）"同根——容积大的东西有很大的容量，能装很多东西。
