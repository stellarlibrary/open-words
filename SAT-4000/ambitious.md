# 🎯 ambitious

🔊 /æmˈbɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a strong desire and determination to succeed; requiring considerable effort or resources to accomplish
**中文：** 野心勃勃的；有雄心的；费力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aspiring（有抱负的）
- driven（有进取心的）
- enterprising（有事业心的）
- determined（坚定的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ambitious plan / ambitious goal / ambitious project / overly ambitious**

👉 Example: The company unveiled an ambitious plan to expand operations into fifteen new international markets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ambitious** young lawyer worked tirelessly to earn a partnership at one of the city's most prestigious firms.
   这位雄心勃勃的年轻律师不知疲倦地工作，以求在该市最负盛名的律所之一获得合伙人资格。

2. The government announced an **ambitious** infrastructure project aimed at modernizing the nation's aging transportation network.
   政府宣布了一项雄心勃勃的基础设施项目，旨在使国家老化的交通网络现代化。

3. Critics warned that the **ambitious** timeline for the reform initiative was unrealistic given the available budget constraints.
   批评人士警告说，鉴于预算限制，这项改革计划的宏大时间表是不切实际的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ambitious" as related to "ambition" — picture someone climbing a mountain with a flag, always aiming for the summit.
把"ambitious"和"ambition"（雄心）联系起来——想象一个人拿着旗帜攀登高山，始终瞄准顶峰。
