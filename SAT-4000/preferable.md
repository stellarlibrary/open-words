# ✅ preferable

🔊 /ˈprɛfərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) More desirable or suitable; worthy of being chosen over another option
**中文：** adj. 更好的，更可取的；更合意的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- favorable（有利的）
- superior（更优的）
- desirable（合意的）
- advisable（可取的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preferable to / far preferable / greatly preferable / considered preferable**

👉 Example: A diplomatic resolution is always preferable to armed conflict in matters of international dispute.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Most educators agree that active learning is **preferable** to passive memorization when developing critical thinking skills in students.
   大多数教育者认为，在培养学生批判性思维技能时，主动学习比被动记忆更可取。

2. The committee concluded that renewable energy sources were **preferable** to fossil fuels for achieving long-term environmental sustainability.
   委员会得出结论，可再生能源在实现长期环境可持续性方面比化石燃料更为可取。

3. In formal academic writing, precise and concise language is generally **preferable** to elaborate and ornamental prose styles.
   在正式的学术写作中，精确简洁的语言通常比华丽繁复的散文风格更好。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prefer-able" — something you are "able to prefer," meaning it is the better choice among options.
把"preferable"想成"prefer（更喜欢）+ able（能够的）"——能够让你更喜欢的选择，即更可取的。
