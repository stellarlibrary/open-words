# 📜 canonical

🔊 /kəˈnɒnɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Relating to or conforming to established rules or standards; authoritative; relating to canon law. (n.) The official robes worn by clergy.
**中文：** adj. 依教规的；规范的；权威的 n. 牧师礼服

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- authoritative（权威的）
- orthodox（正统的）
- standard（标准的）
- accepted（公认的）
- normative（规范的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **canonical text / canonical form / canonical example / canonical works**

👉 Example: Shakespeare's plays are considered canonical works in the study of English literature.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor emphasized that Darwin's writings remain part of the **canonical** literature in evolutionary biology.
   教授强调，达尔文的著作仍然是进化生物学经典文献的一部分。

2. The committee sought to establish a **canonical** set of guidelines that all member institutions would follow uniformly.
   委员会力图建立一套所有成员机构都统一遵循的规范性准则。

3. In mathematics, expressing equations in **canonical** form simplifies analysis and allows for more systematic comparisons.
   在数学中，将方程表达为标准形式可以简化分析并使比较更加系统化。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "canon" as the official rulebook — something "canonical" follows the established canon, like a canonical text in a literary tradition.
把"canonical"想成"canon（经典/准则）"的形容词——符合经典准则的就是"权威的、规范的"。
