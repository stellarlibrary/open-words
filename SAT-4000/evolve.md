# 🌿 evolve

🔊 /ɪˈvɑːlv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To develop gradually over time; to undergo biological evolution; to derive or deduce by reasoning
**中文：** v. 发展，进化；演化；使逐步形成；推断出，引申出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- develop（发展）
- progress（进步）
- transform（转变）
- adapt（适应）
- mature（成熟）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **evolve into / evolve from / evolve over time / evolve a strategy**

👉 Example: The small startup gradually evolved into a multinational corporation over two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists believe that modern birds **evolved** from small theropod dinosaurs during the late Mesozoic era millions of years ago.
   科学家认为，现代鸟类是在数百万年前的中生代晚期由小型兽脚类恐龙进化而来的。

2. The educational curriculum must continually **evolve** to address the changing demands of an increasingly technology-driven global economy.
   教育课程必须不断发展，以应对日益以技术为驱动的全球经济不断变化的需求。

3. Over several centuries, the English language has **evolved** significantly through contact with numerous other languages and cultures.
   经过几个世纪，英语通过与众多其他语言和文化的接触发生了显著演变。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "e-volve" as "e-revolve" — things evolve by revolving through cycles of change, slowly turning into something new.
把"evolve"想成"e-revolve"——事物通过不断轮转变化的循环而进化，慢慢变成新的东西。
