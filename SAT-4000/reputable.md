# 🏅 reputable

🔊 /ˈrepjʊtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a good reputation; widely respected and trusted for integrity, quality, or reliability
**中文：** 声誉好的；受尊敬的；卓越的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- respectable（受尊敬的）
- esteemed（受敬重的）
- prestigious（有声望的）
- honorable（光荣的）
- trustworthy（值得信赖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a reputable company / reputable source / reputable institution / reputable dealer**

👉 Example: Always verify information by consulting reputable academic sources before citing them in your research.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation was conducted by a **reputable** independent firm with decades of experience in forensic financial analysis.
   该调查由一家在法务财务分析领域拥有数十年经验的声誉卓著的独立公司进行。

2. Consumers are advised to purchase medications only from **reputable** pharmacies that comply with all federal regulatory standards.
   建议消费者仅从符合所有联邦监管标准的信誉良好的药房购买药品。

3. The journal is considered one of the most **reputable** publications in the field of molecular biology and genetic research.
   该期刊被认为是分子生物学和遗传研究领域最具声誉的出版物之一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reputable" as "repute-able" — someone who is able to maintain a good repute, meaning they have earned a solid, trustworthy reputation.
把"reputable"想成"repute-able"——一个能够保持好名声的人，意味着他们赢得了可靠、值得信赖的声誉。
