# 🎤 extemporaneous

🔊 /ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Done, spoken, or performed without preparation; impromptu; carried out on the spur of the moment
**中文：** adj. 即席的，临时的；无准备的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impromptu（即兴的）
- spontaneous（自发的）
- unrehearsed（未排练的）
- improvised（即兴创作的）
- offhand（即席的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **extemporaneous speech / extemporaneous remarks / extemporaneous performance / extemporaneous debate**

👉 Example: The candidate delivered an extemporaneous speech that impressed the audience with its clarity and conviction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's **extemporaneous** lecture on quantum mechanics demonstrated her remarkable depth of knowledge and eloquence.
   这位教授关于量子力学的即席演讲展现了她非凡的知识深度和口才。

2. During the competition, each participant was required to deliver an **extemporaneous** address on a randomly assigned topic.
   在比赛期间，每位参赛者都被要求就一个随机指定的话题进行即席演讲。

3. His **extemporaneous** response to the journalist's provocative question revealed both his composure and his intellectual agility.
   他对记者挑衅性问题的即席回应展现了他的沉着和敏捷的思维。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-tempo-raneous" — outside of time to prepare, done "out of the tempo" or schedule, meaning without prior planning.
把"extemporaneous"想成"ex(在…之外)+tempor(时间)+aneous"——在准备时间之外的，即没有事先准备的、即席的。
