# 💥 devastate

🔊 /ˈdevəsteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To destroy or ruin something completely; to cause severe and overwhelming shock or grief
**中文：** v. 毁灭；毁坏；使震惊

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- destroy（摧毁）
- ravage（蹂躏）
- demolish（拆毁）
- ruin（毁坏）
- annihilate（消灭）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **devastate a region / devastate the economy / be devastated by / devastating consequences**

👉 Example: The earthquake devastated the entire coastal region, leaving thousands without shelter.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The unprecedented hurricane **devastated** the island nation, displacing over two hundred thousand residents from their homes.
   这场史无前例的飓风摧毁了该岛国，使二十多万居民流离失所。

2. The financial scandal **devastated** investor confidence and triggered a sharp decline in the stock market index.
   这起金融丑闻严重打击了投资者信心，并引发了股市指数的大幅下跌。

3. Prolonged drought has **devastated** agricultural output in the region, threatening the food supply for millions of people.
   长期干旱已经毁坏了该地区的农业产出，威胁着数百万人的粮食供应。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-vast-ate" as making a "vast" area empty — devastation leaves a vast wasteland of destruction behind.
把"devastate"想成"de + vast(广阔的) + ate"——毁灭过后留下一片广阔的废墟。
