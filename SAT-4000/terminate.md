# 🛑 terminate

🔊 /ˈtɜːrmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To bring to an end; to conclude; to dismiss from employment; to end the existence of something.
**中文：** v. 使终止；使结束；解雇

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- end（结束）
- conclude（终结）
- cease（停止）
- dismiss（解雇）
- abolish（废除）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **terminate a contract / terminate employment / terminate a lease / terminate a program / terminate immediately**

👉 Example: The board voted to terminate the partnership agreement effective at the end of the fiscal year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company decided to **terminate** the employee's contract after a thorough investigation confirmed repeated violations of workplace policy.
   公司在彻底调查确认多次违反工作场所政策后，决定终止该员工的合同。

2. Both governments agreed to **terminate** the bilateral trade agreement, citing irreconcilable differences in tariff and regulatory standards.
   两国政府以关税和监管标准上不可调和的分歧为由，同意终止双边贸易协定。

3. The software will automatically **terminate** any background process that consumes excessive memory and threatens overall system stability and performance.
   该软件将自动终止任何消耗过多内存并威胁整体系统稳定性和性能的后台进程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "terminate" as "the Terminator" — the famous movie character whose mission is to end things permanently and decisively.
把"terminate"想成"the Terminator（终结者）"——那个著名的电影角色，其使命就是永久而果断地终结一切。
