# 📘 primer

🔊 /ˈprɪmər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An elementary textbook that serves as an introduction to a subject; a book for teaching children to read
**中文：** n. 初级读本；识字课本；原始物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- introduction（入门书）
- handbook（手册）
- manual（指南）
- beginner's guide（初学者指南）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a primer on / serve as a primer / basic primer / political primer**

👉 Example: The professor recommended a well-known primer on economics for students with no prior background in the field.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The newly published **primer** on molecular biology quickly became the standard introductory text for undergraduate science students.
   这本新出版的分子生物学初级读本迅速成为本科理科学生的标准入门教材。

2. Her concise article served as an excellent **primer** for anyone seeking to understand the complexities of international trade law.
   她那篇简洁的文章为任何想要了解国际贸易法复杂性的人提供了一本极好的入门读物。

3. Before attending the advanced seminar, participants were advised to review the **primer** distributed during the orientation session.
   在参加高级研讨会之前，与会者被建议复习在迎新会上分发的初级读本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "primer" as "prime" + "er" — it is the prime (first) book you read when learning something, your very first reader.
把"primer"想成"prime（最初的）"+"er"——它是你学习某样东西时阅读的第一本书，你最初的读本。
