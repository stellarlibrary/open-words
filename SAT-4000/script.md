# 🎬 script

🔊 /skrɪpt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A screenplay or broadcast text; handwriting; a writing system. (v.) To adapt into a screenplay
**中文：** n. 剧本，广播稿；文字体系；笔迹，手迹

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- screenplay（剧本）
- manuscript（手稿）
- text（文本）
- handwriting（笔迹）
- notation（书写体系）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **follow the script / write a script / script a scene / in cursive script**

👉 Example: The director asked the screenwriter to script several alternative endings for the film.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The playwright revised the **script** extensively after the initial table read revealed several pacing issues in the second act.
   在首次围读剧本发现第二幕存在若干节奏问题后，剧作家对剧本进行了大幅修改。

2. Scholars devoted years to deciphering the ancient **script** inscribed on the stone tablets unearthed during the archaeological expedition.
   学者们花了数年时间破译考古发掘中出土的石碑上铭刻的古代文字。

3. The professor asked each student to **script** a short documentary segment addressing a pressing contemporary social issue.
   教授要求每位学生编写一段关于当代紧迫社会问题的短纪录片剧本。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "script" as coming from Latin "scriptum" (something written) — a script is simply something that has been written down for others to follow.
把"script"联想到拉丁语"scriptum"（被写下的东西）——剧本就是被写下来供他人遵循的内容。
