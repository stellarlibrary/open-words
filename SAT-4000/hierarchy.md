# 🏛️ hierarchy

🔊 /ˈhaɪərɑːrki/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority; a graded order
**中文：** n. 层级；等级制度；统治集团；等级体系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ranking（等级排列）
- pecking order（等级次序）
- stratification（阶层化）
- chain of command（指挥链）
- class system（等级制度）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social hierarchy / corporate hierarchy / hierarchy of needs / at the top of the hierarchy / hierarchical structure**

👉 Example: The corporate hierarchy determined which executives had the authority to approve major financial decisions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Maslow's **hierarchy** of needs remains one of the most widely referenced theoretical frameworks in contemporary psychology and organizational behavior studies.
   马斯洛的需求层次理论仍然是当代心理学和组织行为学研究中被引用最广泛的理论框架之一。

2. The rigid **hierarchy** within the feudal system prevented individuals of lower social standing from advancing beyond their designated inherited class.
   封建制度内严格的等级制度阻止了较低社会地位的个人超越其指定的世袭阶层。

3. Researchers observed that the **hierarchy** among members of the wolf pack was maintained through subtle behavioral cues rather than overt aggression.
   研究人员观察到，狼群成员之间的等级关系是通过微妙的行为暗示而非公开攻击来维持的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hier-" (high) + "-archy" (rule) — a system where those higher up rule over those below, like a pyramid of power.
把"hierarchy"拆成"hier-"（高的）+"-archy"（统治），意思是高层统治低层的制度，就像一个权力金字塔。
