# 🎭 knavery

🔊 /ˈneɪvəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Dishonest or fraudulent conduct; the actions or behavior characteristic of a knave
**中文：** n. 恶行，欺诈；无赖行为

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trickery（诡计）
- deceit（欺骗）
- villainy（恶行）
- fraud（欺诈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **petty knavery / political knavery / acts of knavery / expose knavery**

👉 Example: The auditor's report revealed years of financial knavery within the corporation's accounting department.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The detective uncovered a web of **knavery** that connected several prominent business leaders to an elaborate embezzlement scheme.
   侦探揭露了一张将数位知名商业领袖与精心策划的挪用公款计划联系起来的欺诈之网。

2. Renaissance literature often portrayed the **knavery** of corrupt officials who exploited their positions of authority.
   文艺复兴时期的文学作品经常描绘腐败官员利用其职权进行的无赖行为。

3. The scholar argued that political **knavery** was the primary cause of the empire's gradual decline and eventual collapse.
   这位学者认为，政治欺诈是帝国逐渐衰落并最终崩溃的主要原因。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "knavery" as "knave + ry" — it is what a knave does, just as "bravery" is what the brave do.
把"knavery"想成"knave（无赖）+ ry"——这是无赖做的事，就像"bravery"是勇敢的人做的事一样。
