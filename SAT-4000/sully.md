# 🖤 sully

🔊 /ˈsʌli/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To damage, spoil, or tarnish; to bring disgrace upon. (n.) A stain or blemish
**中文：** v. 玷污

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- tarnish（玷污）
- besmirch（诋毁）
- defile（亵渎）
- stain（污损）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sully one's reputation / sully one's name / sully the honor / sully the memory of**

👉 Example: The scandalous allegations threatened to sully the distinguished professor's reputation beyond repair.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The opposition party attempted to **sully** the candidate's reputation by circulating unverified allegations through national media outlets.
   反对党试图通过全国媒体传播未经证实的指控来玷污候选人的声誉。

2. Critics warned that accepting corporate donations would inevitably **sully** the charitable organization's image of political neutrality and independence.
   批评者警告说，接受企业捐款将不可避免地玷污该慈善组织政治中立和独立的形象。

3. The historian refused to **sully** the legacy of the national hero by omitting documented achievements from the official biographical account.
   这位历史学家拒绝通过在官方传记中删除有记录的成就来玷污这位民族英雄的遗产。
