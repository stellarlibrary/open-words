# 😤 remiss

🔊 /rɪˈmɪs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Lacking care or attention to duty; negligent; careless about fulfilling one's responsibilities
**中文：** adj. 怠慢的；玩忽职守的；不小心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- negligent（疏忽的）
- careless（粗心的）
- derelict（失职的）
- lax（松懈的）
- slack（懈怠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **be remiss in doing something / remiss in one's duties / it would be remiss not to**

👉 Example: It would be remiss of me not to mention the significant contributions of the entire research team.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The inspector was **remiss** in failing to identify the critical structural defects before the building was opened to tenants.
   检查员玩忽职守，没有在建筑物向租户开放之前发现关键的结构缺陷。

2. It would be **remiss** of the committee not to acknowledge the environmental consequences of the proposed industrial expansion.
   如果委员会不承认拟议工业扩张的环境后果，那将是失职的。

3. Several board members were found to be **remiss** in their fiduciary obligations to the shareholders of the corporation.
   数名董事会成员被发现在对公司股东的信托义务上存在失职行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "remiss" as "re-miss" — you keep missing your responsibilities again and again, being negligent in your duties.
把"remiss"想成"re-miss"——你一次又一次地错过自己的职责，在工作中疏忽大意。
