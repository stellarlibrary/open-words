# 📰 periodical

🔊 /ˌpɪriˈɒdɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring or appearing at regular intervals; recurring periodically. (n.) A magazine or journal published at regular intervals
**中文：** adj. 周期的；定期的 n. 期刊；杂志

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- journal（期刊）
- magazine（杂志）
- publication（出版物）
- recurring（周期性的）
- regular（定期的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **periodical publication / periodical review / academic periodical / periodical assessment**

👉 Example: The library subscribes to numerous academic periodicals covering a wide range of scientific disciplines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university library maintains an extensive collection of scholarly **periodicals** spanning decades of research in the natural sciences.
   大学图书馆保存着大量的学术期刊，涵盖了数十年的自然科学研究。

2. The committee recommended **periodical** evaluations of employee performance to ensure consistent standards across all departments within the organization.
   委员会建议对员工绩效进行定期评估，以确保组织内所有部门的标准一致。

3. Several respected **periodicals** published critical reviews of the landmark study, challenging its methodology and questioning its conclusions.
   几家受人尊敬的期刊发表了对这项里程碑式研究的批评性评论，质疑其方法论和结论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "period-ical" as "period + ical" — something that comes in periods, at regular time intervals, like a magazine subscription.
把"periodical"想成"period（周期）+ ical（的）"——按固定周期出现的事物，就像杂志订阅一样定期送达。
