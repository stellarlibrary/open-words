# 🌀 wavelet

🔊 /ˈweɪvlɪt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small wave; a brief oscillation or ripple, especially on the surface of water or in signal processing
**中文：** n. 微波；小浪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ripple（涟漪）
- small wave（小波浪）
- undulation（微波动）
- flutter（波动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **tiny wavelet / wavelet analysis / wavelet transform / wavelets on the surface**

👉 Example: The gentle breeze produced delicate wavelets that shimmered across the surface of the lake.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A series of gentle **wavelets** lapped against the shore as the morning breeze drifted softly across the tranquil lake.
   一阵阵轻柔的小浪拍打着岸边，晨风轻轻地吹过宁静的湖面。

2. The mathematician employed **wavelet** analysis to decompose the complex signal into distinct frequency components for further study.
   这位数学家运用小波分析将复杂信号分解为不同的频率成分以便进一步研究。

3. From the clifftop observatory, the researchers observed **wavelets** forming intricate interference patterns where two currents converged below.
   从悬崖顶部的观测站，研究人员观察到小浪在两股水流交汇处形成复杂的干涉图样。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wave" + "-let" (a diminutive suffix) — a wavelet is simply a little wave, just as a "booklet" is a small book.
把"wavelet"想成"wave"加"-let"（表示小的后缀）——wavelet就是小波浪，就像"booklet"是小册子一样。
