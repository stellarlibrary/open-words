# 😋 savor

🔊 /ˈseɪvər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To enjoy or appreciate something fully, especially by lingering over it; to give flavor to. (n.) A distinctive taste, smell, or quality
**中文：** v. 欣赏；使有味 n. 滋味；气味

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relish（享受）
- appreciate（欣赏）
- enjoy（享受）
- flavor（风味）
- taste（味道）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **savor the moment / savor the flavor / savor every bite / savor the experience / savor the victory**

👉 Example: She paused to savor the moment before stepping onto the stage for her final performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chef encouraged diners to **savor** each course slowly, appreciating the intricate balance of flavors in every carefully prepared dish.
   厨师鼓励用餐者慢慢品味每一道菜，欣赏每道精心准备的菜肴中精致的口味平衡。

2. After decades of dedicated research, the scientist took a moment to **savor** the long-awaited recognition from the international academic community.
   经过数十年的专注研究，这位科学家花了一刻时间来品味国际学术界期待已久的认可。

3. The wine's rich **savor** lingered on the palate, revealing subtle notes of oak, dark berries, and a hint of spice.
   这款葡萄酒浓郁的滋味在味蕾上久久不散，呈现出微妙的橡木、黑莓和一丝香料的味道。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "savor" as "save-or" — you want to save the flavor and experience, lingering over it rather than rushing through it.
把"savor"想成"save（保留）+ or"——你想保留那种味道和体验，细细品味而不是匆匆而过。
