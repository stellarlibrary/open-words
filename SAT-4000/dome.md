# 🏛️ dome

🔊 /doʊm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure, typically with a circular base
**中文：** n. 圆屋顶；穹顶

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cupola（圆顶）
- vault（拱顶）
- rotunda（圆形建筑）
- canopy（天篷）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **glass dome / dome of a cathedral / dome-shaped / under the dome**

👉 Example: The massive dome of the Capitol building is one of the most recognizable architectural landmarks in the nation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The cathedral's magnificent **dome** was designed by a renowned architect and took nearly two decades to complete.
   这座大教堂壮丽的穹顶由一位著名建筑师设计，历时近二十年才建成。

2. Visitors were awestruck by the painted frescoes that adorned the interior surface of the ancient **dome** above them.
   游客们被装饰在头顶古老穹顶内表面的壁画所深深震撼。

3. The geodesic **dome** structure provides exceptional strength and energy efficiency while using minimal building materials overall.
   测地线穹顶结构在使用最少建筑材料的同时，提供了卓越的强度和能源效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dome" sounding like "home" — a dome is the rounded roof that covers and protects a home or grand building.
"dome"听起来像"home"——穹顶就是覆盖和保护家园或宏伟建筑的圆形屋顶。
