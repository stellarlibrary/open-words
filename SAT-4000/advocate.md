# 🗣️ advocate

🔊 /ˈæd.və.keɪt/ (v.) · /ˈæd.və.kət/ (n.)<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To publicly support or recommend a cause or policy; (n.) a person who supports or speaks in favor of something; a lawyer.
**中文：** (v.) 提倡，主张；(n.) 提倡者；支持者；律师

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- champion（拥护）
- promote（推动）
- supporter（支持者）
- proponent（倡导者）
- endorse（支持）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **advocate for + [cause/person]; advocate + [noun/gerund]**

👉 Example: Many scientists advocate for stronger environmental regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator has consistently **advocated** for comprehensive healthcare reform throughout her career.
   这位参议员在其整个职业生涯中一直主张全面的医疗改革。

2. As a passionate **advocate** of civil liberties, he dedicated his life to defending individual freedoms.
   作为公民自由的热情倡导者，他毕生致力于捍卫个人自由。

3. Leading economists **advocate** adopting sustainable policies to address long-term environmental challenges.
   顶尖经济学家们提倡采用可持续政策来应对长期的环境挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Advocate" = "ad- (to) + voc (voice) + -ate" — to give voice to a cause, to speak up for something.
联想记忆：advocate = ad（朝向）+ voc（声音）+ -ate，即"为某事发出声音"，就是提倡和主张。
