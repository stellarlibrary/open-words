# 🖼️ caption

🔊 /ˈkæpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A title or brief explanation accompanying an illustration, photograph, or film; a subtitle. (v.) To provide with a caption or title
**中文：** n. 标题；字幕；说明 v. 加上说明；加上标题

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- title（标题）
- subtitle（字幕）
- heading（题目）
- legend（图例说明）
- inscription（题词）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **photo caption / caption a picture / closed caption / caption reads / write a caption**

👉 Example: The photograph's caption provided essential context that the image alone could not convey.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The editor asked the journalist to write a concise **caption** for each photograph accompanying the feature article.
   编辑要求记者为专题文章配图的每张照片撰写简洁的说明。

2. The museum decided to **caption** all exhibits in three languages to accommodate its diverse international visitors.
   博物馆决定用三种语言为所有展品加上说明，以方便其多元化的国际游客。

3. Without a proper **caption**, the historical image was frequently misinterpreted and cited out of its original context.
   由于缺少恰当的标题，这张历史图片经常被误解并脱离其原始语境被引用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "caption" as "cap + tion" — a "cap" sits on top, just like a caption sits on top of or below an image to explain it.
把"caption"想成"cap + tion"——"cap"（帽子）在顶部，就像标题说明位于图片上方或下方来解释它一样。
