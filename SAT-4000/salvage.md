# 🚢 salvage

🔊 /ˈsælvɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The rescue of property from shipwreck, fire, or other disaster; compensation for such rescue. (v.) To save or rescue from loss or destruction
**中文：** n. 抢救财物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rescue（救援）
- recover（恢复）
- retrieve（取回）
- reclaim（收回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salvage operation / salvage a relationship / salvage from wreckage / salvage what remains**

👉 Example: The crew worked tirelessly to salvage valuable cargo from the sunken vessel near the harbor.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Emergency teams managed to **salvage** critical documents and equipment from the building before the fire spread further.
   紧急救援队在大火蔓延之前设法从大楼中抢救出了重要的文件和设备。

2. The marine archaeologists undertook a complex **salvage** operation to recover artifacts from the ancient shipwreck.
   海洋考古学家进行了一次复杂的打捞作业，从古代沉船中打捞文物。

3. Despite the failed merger, the executive attempted to **salvage** the company's reputation through transparent public communication.
   尽管合并失败，这位高管试图通过透明的公开沟通来挽救公司的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "salvage" as "salv-age" where "salv" sounds like "save" — to salvage is to save something from destruction or loss.
把"salvage"想成"salv-age"，其中"salv"听起来像"save（拯救）"——salvage就是从毁灭或损失中拯救东西。
