# 🏛️ arcade

🔊 /ɑːrˈkeɪd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A covered passage with arches along one or both sides; a place containing coin-operated entertainment machines
**中文：** n. 拱廊；游乐场

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gallery（走廊）
- colonnade（柱廊）
- passageway（通道）
- plaza（广场）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shopping arcade / an arcade of shops / video arcade / arcade game**

👉 Example: The children spent the entire afternoon playing games at the video arcade near the mall.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historic **arcade** featured elegant stone arches and boutique shops that attracted tourists from around the world.
   这座历史悠久的拱廊以优雅的石拱和精品商店闻名，吸引了来自世界各地的游客。

2. City planners proposed constructing a new commercial **arcade** to revitalize the declining downtown shopping district.
   城市规划者提议建造一座新的商业拱廊，以振兴日益衰落的市中心商业区。

3. The renovated Victorian **arcade** now serves as a cultural center hosting art exhibitions and community gatherings.
   这座翻新的维多利亚时代拱廊如今用作文化中心，举办艺术展览和社区活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "arc-ade" — an arcade is full of "arcs" (arches), forming a covered walkway or entertainment hall.
把"arcade"拆成"arc（拱）+ ade"——拱廊就是由一个个拱形结构组成的走廊或娱乐场所。
