# 💉 antitoxin

🔊 /ˌæntɪˈtɒksɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** An antibody produced by the body or introduced through injection that counteracts a specific toxin; a serum containing such antibodies used to treat or prevent diseases caused by biological toxins.

**中文：** 抗毒素。由人体产生或通过注射引入的能够中和特定毒素的抗体；含有此类抗体的血清，用于治疗或预防由生物毒素引起的疾病。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- antiserum（抗血清）
- antibody（抗体）
- antidote（解毒剂）
- counteragent（对抗剂）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Common collocations:** administer antitoxin, produce antitoxin, diphtheria antitoxin, tetanus antitoxin, inject antitoxin

**Example:** The physician promptly administered a dose of **antitoxin** to neutralize the venom in the patient's bloodstream.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers developed a potent **antitoxin** capable of neutralizing the lethal effects of the bacterial infection.
   研究人员开发了一种能够中和细菌感染致命效果的强效抗毒素。

2. The discovery of diphtheria **antitoxin** in the late nineteenth century marked a revolutionary advance in medical science.
   十九世纪末白喉抗毒素的发现标志着医学科学的革命性进步。

3. Emergency responders carried vials of **antitoxin** as a precautionary measure during the outbreak investigation.
   在疫情调查期间，急救人员携带了数瓶抗毒素作为预防措施。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "anti-" (against) + "toxin" (poison). An antitoxin is literally something that works against poison — a substance that neutralizes harmful toxins.

联想："anti-"（对抗）+ "toxin"（毒素）。antitoxin 字面意思就是对抗毒素的东西——一种中和有害毒素的物质。
