# 🔥 anarchy

🔊 /ˈænərki/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A state of disorder due to the absence or nonrecognition of authority or government; complete lawlessness
**中文：** 无政府状态；混乱；无秩序

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chaos（混乱）
- disorder（无序）
- lawlessness（无法无天）
- turmoil（动荡）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **descend into anarchy / state of anarchy / political anarchy / verge of anarchy**

👉 Example: The nation descended into anarchy after the sudden collapse of the ruling government.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged civil war plunged the entire region into **anarchy**, leaving millions of citizens without basic services.
   旷日持久的内战使整个地区陷入无政府状态，数百万公民失去了基本服务。

2. Political theorists have long debated whether **anarchy** inevitably leads to tyranny or can sustain genuine freedom.
   政治理论家长期以来一直争论无政府状态是否必然导致暴政，还是能维持真正的自由。

3. The sudden resignation of every cabinet member threatened to reduce the administration to complete **anarchy**.
   所有内阁成员的突然辞职有可能使政府陷入彻底的混乱状态。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "an-archy" as "an" (without) + "archy" (ruler) — a society without any ruler leads to chaos.
把"anarchy"拆成"an"（没有）+"archy"（统治者）——一个没有统治者的社会必然走向混乱。
