# ✂️ divide

🔊 /dɪˈvaɪd/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To separate into parts or groups; to perform mathematical division. (n.) A ridge of land separating two drainage areas; a significant difference
**中文：** v. 划分；除；分开 n. 分水岭；分歧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- separate（分开）
- partition（划分）
- split（分割）
- divergence（分歧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **divide into / divide by / divide evenly / the great divide / bridging the divide**

👉 Example: The teacher asked students to divide the class into four groups for the collaborative research project.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Political analysts observed that the controversial policy continued to **divide** public opinion along ideological and regional lines.
   政治分析人士观察到，这项有争议的政策继续沿着意识形态和地区界线分裂公众舆论。

2. The Continental **Divide** stretches along the Rocky Mountains and determines the direction in which rivers flow across North America.
   大陆分水岭沿落基山脉延伸，决定了北美洲河流的流向。

3. The professor instructed students to **divide** the total revenue figure by the number of units sold to calculate the price.
   教授指示学生将总收入除以销售数量来计算单价。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "divide" as "di-vide" where "di" means two and "vide" sounds like "wide" — making two wide separate parts.
把"divide"想成"di(二)+vide(分开)"——把东西一分为二，分成两部分。
