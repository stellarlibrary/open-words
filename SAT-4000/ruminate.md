# 🤔 ruminate

🔊 /ˈruːmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To chew the cud as a ruminant animal; to think deeply and at length about something; to ponder repeatedly
**中文：** v. 反刍；沉思

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ponder（沉思）
- contemplate（深思）
- meditate（冥想）
- deliberate（仔细考虑）
- muse（沉思）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ruminate on / ruminate over / ruminate about / tend to ruminate**

👉 Example: The philosopher would ruminate on the nature of existence for hours in his quiet study.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scholar would often **ruminate** on the ethical implications of emerging technologies long before they reached mainstream adoption.
   这位学者经常在新兴技术被主流采用之前就深思其伦理影响。

2. After receiving the unexpected criticism, she spent several days **ruminating** over whether to revise her entire theoretical framework.
   在收到意外的批评后，她花了好几天反复思考是否要修改整个理论框架。

3. Cattle and other ruminant animals naturally **ruminate** by repeatedly chewing food that returns from the first compartment of the stomach.
   牛和其他反刍动物通过反复咀嚼从胃的第一个隔室返回的食物来自然地进行反刍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ruminate" as "room + in + ate" — picture sitting alone in a room, having eaten, with nothing to do but chew over your thoughts again and again.
把"ruminate"想成"room（房间）+ in（在里面）+ ate（吃过了）"——想象独自坐在房间里，吃过饭后无事可做，只能反复咀嚼自己的思绪。
