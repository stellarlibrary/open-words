# 💰 penurious

🔊 /pɪˈnjʊəriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Extremely stingy or miserly; lacking in resources; poverty-stricken
**中文：** adj. 吝啬的；缺乏的；贫困的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- miserly（吝啬的）
- parsimonious（小气的）
- stingy（吝啬的）
- impoverished（贫困的）
- niggardly（吝啬的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **penurious habits / penurious existence / penurious landlord / penurious conditions**

👉 Example: The penurious landlord refused to repair the building despite collecting substantial rent from every tenant.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **penurious** millionaire hoarded his wealth and refused to contribute even a small amount to charitable causes.
   这位吝啬的百万富翁囤积财富，拒绝为慈善事业捐出哪怕很少的金额。

2. Growing up in **penurious** circumstances taught her the value of frugality and careful financial planning.
   在贫困环境中长大使她懂得了节俭和谨慎理财规划的价值。

3. The professor described the **penurious** conditions of medieval peasants who struggled to survive harsh winters.
   教授描述了中世纪农民在严冬中挣扎求存的贫困状况。

---

Think of "penurious" as "penny-urious" — someone so obsessed with every penny that they become furious about spending.
把"penurious"想成"penny-urious"——一个对每一分钱都斤斤计较、花钱就抓狂的人。
