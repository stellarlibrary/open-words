# ⚔️ discord

🔊 /ˈdɪskɔːrd/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Disagreement or lack of harmony between people or things; a harsh mixture of sounds. (v.) To disagree or be at variance
**中文：** n. 不和；不调和 v. 不一致

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conflict（冲突）
- strife（纷争）
- dissension（意见不合）
- disharmony（不协调）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sow discord / cause discord / marital discord / discord among members / discord between nations**

👉 Example: The controversial policy decision sowed discord among the coalition partners and threatened the alliance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged **discord** between the two factions ultimately led to the collapse of the governing coalition and early elections.
   两个派系之间长期的不和最终导致了执政联盟的瓦解和提前选举。

2. Foreign agents sought to sow **discord** within the democratic institution by spreading disinformation through various social media platforms.
   外国特工试图通过各种社交媒体平台传播虚假信息，在民主机构内部制造不和。

3. The composer deliberately introduced moments of harsh **discord** into the symphony to evoke feelings of tension and unresolved conflict.
   作曲家故意在交响曲中引入刺耳的不和谐音，以唤起紧张感和未解决的冲突。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-cord" — a cord connects things, but "dis" breaks it apart. Discord is when the connection between people breaks down.
把"discord"拆成"dis-cord"——"cord"是绳索，连接事物，但"dis"把它断开了。Discord就是人与人之间的联系断裂。
