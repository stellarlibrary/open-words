# 🤝 cordial

🔊 /ˈkɔːrdʒəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Warm and friendly; sincerely heartfelt. (n.) A sweet, aromatic drink or a stimulating medicine
**中文：** adj. 热忱的；诚恳的；亲切的 n. 补品；兴奋剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- warm（热情的）
- genial（亲切的）
- affable（和蔼的）
- amiable（友善的）
- hearty（衷心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cordial relationship / cordial welcome / cordial invitation / cordial atmosphere**

👉 Example: The ambassador extended a cordial invitation to the visiting delegation from the neighboring country.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite their longstanding political differences, the two leaders maintained a **cordial** relationship throughout years of diplomatic negotiations.
   尽管长期存在政治分歧，两位领导人在多年的外交谈判中一直保持着亲切的关系。

2. The host offered a **cordial** greeting to each arriving guest, making everyone feel genuinely welcome at the reception.
   主人对每位到来的客人都致以诚恳的问候，让每个人在招待会上都感到由衷的欢迎。

3. The committee expressed **cordial** appreciation for the generous donation that would fund several community development projects.
   委员会对这笔将资助多个社区发展项目的慷慨捐款表达了热忱的感谢。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cord-ial" as related to "cor" (Latin for heart) — a cordial person speaks from the heart with warmth and sincerity.
把"cordial"想成和"cor"（拉丁语中的"心"）有关——一个cordial的人发自内心地带着温暖和真诚说话。
