# 😔 destitution

🔊 /ˌdɛstɪˈtjuːʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of extreme poverty or complete lack of resources; severe deprivation
**中文：** n. 穷困；缺乏；贫穷

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- poverty（贫穷）
- indigence（贫困）
- penury（赤贫）
- privation（匮乏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **live in destitution / fall into destitution / widespread destitution / abject destitution**

👉 Example: The prolonged drought drove entire communities into abject destitution across the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Years of civil conflict had reduced the once-prosperous nation to widespread **destitution** and economic collapse.
   多年的内战使这个曾经繁荣的国家陷入了普遍的穷困和经济崩溃。

2. The charitable organization was founded to alleviate **destitution** among displaced families in the aftermath of natural disasters.
   该慈善组织的成立旨在缓解自然灾害后流离失所家庭的贫困状况。

3. Historical records reveal that **destitution** was a primary catalyst for the mass migrations of the nineteenth century.
   历史记录表明，穷困是十九世纪大规模移民的主要推动力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "destitution" as "de-stitute-ion" — being "destitute" means having nothing, and the "-ion" makes it a state of having absolutely nothing.
把"destitution"想成"destitute"的名词形式——"destitute"意为一无所有，加上"-ion"就是一无所有的状态。
