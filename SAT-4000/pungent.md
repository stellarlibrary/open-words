# 🌶️ pungent

🔊 /ˈpʌndʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a sharply strong taste or smell; (of remarks) having a sharp, biting quality
**中文：** adj. 辛辣的；刺激性的；刺鼻的；尖刻的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- acrid（辛辣的）
- sharp（刺鼻的）
- biting（尖刻的）
- caustic（刻薄的）
- piquant（辛辣的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pungent aroma / pungent odor / pungent criticism / pungent flavor / pungent smell**

👉 Example: The pungent aroma of freshly ground spices filled the entire kitchen immediately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pungent** odor of industrial chemicals permeated the facility and forced workers to evacuate the building immediately.
   工业化学品的刺鼻气味弥漫了整个设施，迫使工人立即撤离大楼。

2. The critic delivered a **pungent** review of the novel, sparing no detail in her assessment of its numerous literary flaws.
   评论家对这部小说发表了尖刻的评论，对其众多文学缺陷的评价毫不留情。

3. Aged cheeses are often characterized by their **pungent** flavor, which develops gradually through the lengthy fermentation process.
   陈年奶酪通常以其浓烈的风味为特征，这种风味通过漫长的发酵过程逐渐形成。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pung-ent" — "pung" sounds like "punch"; a pungent smell punches your nose with its sharp intensity.
把"pungent"中的"pung"想成"punch（拳击）"——刺鼻的气味就像给你的鼻子一拳，冲击力十足。
