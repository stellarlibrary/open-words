# 🎉 regale

🔊 /rɪˈɡeɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To entertain or delight someone with conversation, stories, or fine food and drink; to feast. (n.) A lavish feast or entertainment.
**中文：** v. 取悦；盛情款待；使愉悦 n. 款待；盛宴

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- entertain（招待）
- delight（使高兴）
- amuse（使愉悦）
- feast（款待）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **regale someone with stories / regale guests with tales / regale the audience**

👉 Example: The host regaled his guests with fascinating stories from his travels abroad.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The retired professor would **regale** her students with captivating anecdotes from decades of fieldwork in remote regions.
   这位退休教授常常用她数十年来在偏远地区进行实地考察的精彩轶事来取悦学生。

2. At the annual banquet, the chairman **regaled** the distinguished guests with an elaborate seven-course meal and fine wine.
   在年度宴会上，主席用精致的七道菜和美酒盛情款待了尊贵的客人。

3. The documentary filmmaker **regaled** the audience with behind-the-scenes tales that revealed the extraordinary challenges of production.
   这位纪录片导演用幕后故事取悦了观众，揭示了制作过程中的非凡挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "regale" as "regal" — to treat someone in a regal (royal) manner, entertaining them like kings and queens.
把"regale"想成"regal"（王室的）——以王室般的方式款待某人，像招待国王和王后一样取悦他们。
