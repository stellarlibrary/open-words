# 🌡️ torrid

🔊 /ˈtɒrɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Very hot and dry; scorching; full of intense emotions, especially romantic passion; extremely ardent
**中文：** 晒热的；酷热的；热情的；炽热的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scorching（灼热的）
- sweltering（闷热的）
- passionate（热情的）
- blazing（炽热的）
- ardent（热烈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **torrid heat / torrid climate / torrid zone / torrid affair / torrid pace**

👉 Example: The explorers struggled to cross the torrid desert landscape under the relentless midday sun.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Agricultural yields declined dramatically as the **torrid** summer drought persisted well into the autumn harvest season.
   随着酷热的夏季干旱持续到秋季收获季节，农业产量急剧下降。

2. The novelist depicted a **torrid** romance between two protagonists set against the backdrop of wartime upheaval and uncertainty.
   小说家描绘了两位主人公在战时动荡和不确定的背景下的一段炽热的爱情。

3. Travelers venturing into the **torrid** regions near the equator must take rigorous precautions against heatstroke and dehydration.
   前往赤道附近酷热地区的旅行者必须严格预防中暑和脱水。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "torrid" as "torch-rid" — imagine the sun acting like a torch, making the land so hot it is rid of all moisture, leaving everything scorched.
把"torrid"想成"torch-rid"——想象太阳像火炬一样，让大地如此炎热以至于失去了所有水分，一切都被烤焦了。
