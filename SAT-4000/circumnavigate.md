# 🌍 circumnavigate

🔊 /ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To sail or travel all the way around something, especially the world; to go around or avoid an obstacle.
**中文：** 环航；环绕……航行；绕行。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- navigate around（绕行）
- orbit（环绕）
- sail around（环航）
- go around（绕过）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **circumnavigate the globe / circumnavigate the world / circumnavigate an obstacle**

👉 Example: Ferdinand Magellan's expedition was the first to circumnavigate the entire globe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The seasoned sailor spent fourteen months attempting to **circumnavigate** the globe solo in a thirty-foot vessel.
   这位经验丰富的水手花了十四个月试图独自驾驶一艘三十英尺的船环球航行。

2. Modern aircraft can **circumnavigate** the Earth in less than two days with only a few refueling stops required.
   现代飞机只需几次加油停留，就能在不到两天内环绕地球飞行。

3. The explorers had to **circumnavigate** a treacherous mountain range before reaching the isolated valley beyond it.
   探险者们不得不绕过一座险峻的山脉，才能到达山脉另一边那个与世隔绝的山谷。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circum-" (around) + "navigate" (to steer a ship) — to navigate around, especially around the entire world.
把"circumnavigate"拆分为"circum-（周围）"+"navigate（航行）"——绕着航行，尤其指环绕全球航行。
