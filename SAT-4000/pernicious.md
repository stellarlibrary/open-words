# ☠️ pernicious

🔊 /pərˈnɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way; highly destructive or deadly
**中文：** adj. 有害的；恶性的；致命的；险恶的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmful（有害的）
- detrimental（有损害的）
- noxious（有毒的）
- deleterious（有害的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pernicious influence / pernicious effects / pernicious anemia / pernicious habit**

👉 Example: The pernicious influence of misinformation has eroded public trust in scientific institutions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **pernicious** effects of prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants often remain undetected until irreversible damage has occurred.
   长期接触工业污染物的有害影响往往在造成不可逆转的损害之后才被发现。

2. Historians have documented the **pernicious** role that propaganda played in manipulating public opinion during wartime periods.
   历史学家记录了宣传在战争时期操纵公众舆论中所起的恶性作用。

3. The committee identified several **pernicious** policies that systematically disadvantaged minority communities over multiple decades.
   委员会确定了几项在数十年间系统性地使少数族裔社区处于不利地位的有害政策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "per-nicious" — it sounds like "per-vicious." Something pernicious is thoroughly vicious and harmful, often in a sneaky, gradual way.
把"pernicious"想成"per（非常）+ nicious（类似vicious，恶毒的）"——非常恶毒有害的，且往往以隐蔽渐进的方式造成伤害。
