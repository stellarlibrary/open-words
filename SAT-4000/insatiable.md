# 🔥 insatiable

🔊 /ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Impossible to satisfy; having a desire or appetite that cannot be fulfilled
**中文：** 贪得无厌的；不知足的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- voracious（贪婪的）
- unquenchable（无法满足的）
- ravenous（极度渴望的）
- greedy（贪心的）
- rapacious（贪婪的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **insatiable appetite / insatiable curiosity / insatiable desire / insatiable demand**

👉 Example: The young scholar had an insatiable curiosity that drove her to explore every branch of science.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The corporation's **insatiable** appetite for expansion ultimately led to unsustainable debt levels and financial collapse.
   该公司对扩张的贪得无厌最终导致了不可持续的债务水平和财务崩溃。

2. Her **insatiable** thirst for knowledge compelled her to pursue advanced degrees across three different academic disciplines.
   她对知识的无尽渴望促使她在三个不同的学术领域攻读高级学位。

3. The growing population created an **insatiable** demand for natural resources that far exceeded the region's sustainable capacity.
   不断增长的人口对自然资源产生了无法满足的需求，远远超出了该地区的可持续承载力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-sati-able" — "in" (not) + "sati" (satisfy) + "able." If you cannot be satisfied, your desire is insatiable.
把"insatiable"想成"in（不）+ sati（满足）+ able（能够）"——如果你无法被满足，你的欲望就是insatiable。
