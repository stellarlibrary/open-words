# 🧠 resourceful

🔊 /rɪˈsɔːrsfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having the ability to find quick and clever ways to overcome difficulties; skilled at devising practical solutions
**中文：** adj. 资源丰富的；机智的，多策略的；足智多谋的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ingenious（机灵的）
- inventive（有创造力的）
- clever（聪明的）
- adaptable（适应力强的）
- enterprising（有进取心的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **resourceful person / resourceful leader / highly resourceful / resourceful in finding solutions / resourceful approach**

👉 Example: A resourceful manager can accomplish ambitious goals even with a very limited budget.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **resourceful** engineer devised an innovative cooling system using only materials that were readily available in the remote research station.
   这位足智多谋的工程师仅利用偏远研究站中现有的材料设计出了一套创新的冷却系统。

2. Throughout history, **resourceful** leaders have turned apparent disadvantages into strategic opportunities during times of political uncertainty.
   纵观历史，足智多谋的领导者在政治不确定时期将明显的劣势转化为战略机遇。

3. The students proved remarkably **resourceful** when their laboratory equipment malfunctioned, improvising alternatives to complete the experiment successfully.
   当实验室设备出现故障时，学生们表现得非常机智，临时想出替代方案成功完成了实验。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "resourceful" as "resource-full" — a person who is full of resources and ideas, always finding a way out of any problem.
把"resourceful"想成"resource-full"——一个充满资源和想法的人，总能找到解决任何问题的办法。
