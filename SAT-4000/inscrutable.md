# 🔮 inscrutable

🔊 /ɪnˈskruːtəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Impossible to understand or interpret; mysterious and enigmatic
**中文：** 神秘的；不可理解的；高深莫测的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enigmatic（神秘的）
- impenetrable（难以理解的）
- unfathomable（深不可测的）
- cryptic（隐秘的）
- mysterious（神秘的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inscrutable expression / inscrutable smile / inscrutable motives / remain inscrutable**

👉 Example: The diplomat maintained an inscrutable expression throughout the entire negotiation session.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The judge's **inscrutable** demeanor made it impossible for the attorneys to predict the outcome of the landmark ruling.
   法官高深莫测的态度使律师们无法预测这一里程碑式裁决的结果。

2. The ancient text contained **inscrutable** symbols that even the most experienced archaeologists could not successfully decipher.
   这份古代文献包含了连最有经验的考古学家都无法成功破译的神秘符号。

3. Her **inscrutable** silence during the board meeting left her colleagues uncertain about her position on the merger.
   她在董事会会议上令人费解的沉默让同事们无法确定她对合并的立场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-scrut-able" — "in" (not) + "scrut" (scrutinize). Something inscrutable cannot be scrutinized or examined clearly.
把"inscrutable"想成"in（不）+ scrut（审视）+ able（能够）"——不可审视的，无法看透的。
