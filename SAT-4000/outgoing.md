# 🤝 outgoing

🔊 /ˈaʊtɡoʊɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Friendly and socially confident; leaving a position or office; going out or departing. (n.) An outflow or expenditure
**中文：** adj. 即将离职的；对人友好的，开朗的；外出的 n. 外出；流出；开支

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sociable（友好的）
- extroverted（外向的）
- departing（即将离任的）
- gregarious（合群的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outgoing personality / outgoing president / outgoing mail / outgoing tide**

👉 Example: Her outgoing personality made it easy for her to build rapport with clients and colleagues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **outgoing** president delivered a farewell address reflecting on the achievements and challenges of the past term.
   即将离任的总统发表了告别演讲，回顾了过去任期的成就与挑战。

2. Her **outgoing** nature and genuine warmth made her exceptionally popular among students and faculty members alike.
   她开朗的性格和真诚的热情使她在学生和教职员工中都极受欢迎。

3. The committee selected an **outgoing** candidate who could effectively represent the organization at international conferences.
   委员会选择了一位善于交际的候选人，能够在国际会议上有效地代表该组织。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out + going" — a person who is always going out to meet people is outgoing, friendly and sociable.
把"outgoing"拆成"out（外面）+ going（去）"——一个总是出去与人交往的人就是外向的、友好的、善于社交的。
