# 🧭 navigate

🔊 /ˈnævɪɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To plan and direct the route of a ship, aircraft, or vehicle; to steer through or find one's way across a difficult situation
**中文：** v. 驾驶，操纵；使通过；航行于；横渡；导航

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- steer（驾驶）
- pilot（领航）
- traverse（穿越）
- maneuver（操纵）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **navigate through / navigate a course / navigate the waters / navigate challenges**

👉 Example: The explorer had to navigate through dense jungle without any modern equipment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Early Polynesian sailors were able to **navigate** vast stretches of the Pacific Ocean using only the stars and ocean currents.
   早期的波利尼西亚水手仅凭星辰和洋流便能在太平洋的广阔海域中航行。

2. The new CEO must **navigate** a complex web of regulatory requirements before the proposed merger can receive final approval.
   新任首席执行官必须在拟议的合并获得最终批准之前应对复杂的监管要求。

3. Modern autonomous vehicles use advanced sensors and algorithms to **navigate** crowded urban streets safely and efficiently.
   现代自动驾驶汽车使用先进的传感器和算法来安全高效地在拥挤的城市街道上行驶。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "navi-" from Latin "navis" (ship) + "-gate" — to guide a ship through the gate of the harbor and out to sea.
"navi-"来自拉丁语"navis"（船）加"-gate"（门）——引导船只穿过港口的大门驶向大海。
