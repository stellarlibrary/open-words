# ⚡ heretical

🔊 /həˈretɪkəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Holding opinions or beliefs that contradict established religious doctrine or accepted standards
**中文：** adj. 异端的；异教的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unorthodox（非正统的）
- blasphemous（亵渎的）
- dissident（持不同意见的）
- iconoclastic（打破传统的）
- heterodox（异端的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heretical views / heretical beliefs / heretical ideas / deemed heretical**

👉 Example: The philosopher's heretical views on the nature of the soul drew fierce condemnation from religious authorities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During the medieval period, scholars who proposed **heretical** ideas about the universe often faced persecution from the church.
   在中世纪，提出关于宇宙的异端思想的学者经常遭到教会的迫害。

2. His **heretical** interpretation of the sacred texts was rejected by the theological council and formally condemned as dangerous.
   他对圣典的异端解读被神学委员会否决，并被正式谴责为危险言论。

3. What was once considered a **heretical** theory in physics has now become the foundation of modern quantum mechanics.
   曾经被认为是物理学中异端理论的观点，如今已成为现代量子力学的基础。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heretical" as "here-tickle" — ideas that tickle the establishment the wrong way, going against accepted beliefs.
把"heretical"想成"here-tickle"——这些想法以错误的方式刺激当权者，违背了公认的信条。
