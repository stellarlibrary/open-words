# 🛑 hindrance

🔊 /ˈhɪndrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A thing that provides resistance, delay, or obstruction to something or someone; the act or state of hindering
**中文：** n. 障碍；妨碍；阻碍物；干扰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obstacle（障碍）
- impediment（妨碍）
- obstruction（阻碍）
- barrier（屏障）
- encumbrance（累赘）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a hindrance to / more of a hindrance than a help / without hindrance / major hindrance / pose a hindrance**

👉 Example: The outdated regulations proved to be a significant hindrance to economic reform and modernization efforts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The language barrier proved to be the greatest **hindrance** to effective communication between the international research teams collaborating on the joint project.
   语言障碍被证明是参与联合项目合作的国际研究团队之间有效沟通的最大阻碍。

2. Rather than providing useful assistance, the newly implemented software became a considerable **hindrance** to daily workflow and overall office productivity.
   新实施的软件非但没有提供有用的帮助，反而成为日常工作流程和整体办公效率的巨大障碍。

3. The committee concluded that political corruption remained the primary **hindrance** to achieving sustainable economic development throughout the entire region.
   委员会得出结论，政治腐败仍然是整个地区实现可持续经济发展的主要障碍。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "hinder" + "-ance" — the noun form of hinder; a hindrance is the thing that hinders you, like a fence blocking your path.
把"hindrance"想成"hinder"（阻碍）+"-ance"（名词后缀）——hindrance就是阻碍你的那个东西，就像一道挡住去路的围栏。
