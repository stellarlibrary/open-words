# 🛢️ barrel

🔊 /ˈbærəl/<br>
🏷️ *n. & v. | 名词 & 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** (n.) A cylindrical container with flat ends, used for storing liquids; the tube of a gun through which a bullet travels; (v.) to put or pack into a barrel.

**中文：** (n.) 桶；枪管；(v.) 把……装入桶内。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- cask （木桶）
- keg （小桶）
- drum （圆桶）
- container （容器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Barrel" has multiple meanings depending on context. In commerce, it is a unit of measurement for oil (approximately 159 liters). The phrase "barrel through" means to move very fast and recklessly. "Over a barrel" is an idiom meaning to be in a helpless or vulnerable position.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The workers carefully rolled each **barrel** of aged wine into the underground cellar.
   中文：工人们小心翼翼地将每桶陈年葡萄酒滚进了地下酒窖。

2. The inspector examined the **barrel** of the rifle to ensure it was free of obstructions.
   中文：检查员检查了步枪的枪管以确保其中没有障碍物。

3. The merchants decided to **barrel** the freshly harvested olives for export to foreign markets.
   中文：商人们决定将新鲜采摘的橄榄装桶以出口到海外市场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Picture a large round "barrel" — it looks like a big belly. The word sounds like "bear-all," as if the barrel can bear (hold) all the things you put inside it.

形象记忆：想象一个大圆"桶"——它看起来像一个大肚子。这个词听起来像"bear-all"（承载一切），就好像桶可以承载你放进去的所有东西。
