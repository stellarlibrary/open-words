# 🏛️ exterior

🔊 /ɪkˈstɪriər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Situated on or relating to the outside of something; outward in appearance. (n.) The outer surface or structure of something
**中文：** adj. 外部的；表面的 n. 外部；表面；外观

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outer（外部的）
- outside（外面的）
- surface（表面的）
- outward（向外的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **exterior wall / exterior surface / exterior appearance / the building's exterior / calm exterior**

👉 Example: Despite her calm exterior, she was deeply troubled by the unexpected turn of events.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect designed an **exterior** facade that combined modern glass panels with traditional stone masonry in elegant proportion.
   建筑师设计了一个将现代玻璃面板与传统石砌结构优雅结合的外部立面。

2. Beneath his composed **exterior**, the diplomat harbored serious reservations about the proposed terms of the bilateral agreement.
   在沉着的外表之下，这位外交官对拟议的双边协议条款怀有严重的保留意见。

3. The restoration team spent several months repairing the **exterior** of the historic cathedral damaged by decades of environmental erosion.
   修复团队花了几个月时间修复这座历史悠久的大教堂因数十年环境侵蚀而受损的外部。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "exter-ior" — "exter" sounds like "external," pointing to what is on the outside, the outer shell of something.
把"exterior"想成"exter(外部的)+ior"——与"external"同源，指事物的外部、外层。
