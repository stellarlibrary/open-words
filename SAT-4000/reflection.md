# 🪞 reflection

🔊 /rɪˈflɛkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The throwing back of light, heat, or sound from a surface; an image seen in a mirror or reflective surface; serious thought or contemplation
**中文：** n. 反射；映象，倒影；反省，沉思

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- contemplation（沉思）
- meditation（冥想）
- image（映象）
- deliberation（深思熟虑）
- introspection（内省）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **on reflection / a reflection of / upon further reflection / deep reflection**

👉 Example: On reflection, the committee realized that their initial assessment had been overly optimistic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist studied the **reflection** of laser beams off various metallic surfaces to measure their precise optical properties.
   科学家研究了激光束在各种金属表面上的反射，以测量其精确的光学特性。

2. Upon deeper **reflection**, the philosopher concluded that moral truths cannot be derived solely from empirical observation of nature.
   经过更深入的反思，这位哲学家得出结论，道德真理不能仅从对自然的经验观察中得出。

3. The economic downturn was widely regarded as a **reflection** of structural weaknesses that had long plagued the financial sector.
   经济衰退被普遍认为是长期困扰金融部门的结构性弱点的反映。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reflect-ion" as looking into a mirror — you see your image (reflection) and then think deeply (reflection) about what you see.
把"reflection"想成照镜子——你看到自己的映象（reflection），然后深入思考（reflection）你所看到的。
