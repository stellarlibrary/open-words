# 🔖 ascribe

🔊 /əˈskraɪb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To attribute something to a cause, source, or origin; to regard something as being due to a particular person or thing.

**中文：** 归因于；归咎于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- attribute 归因于
- credit 归功于
- assign 归于
- impute 归咎于

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Ascribe" is typically followed by "to" — you ascribe something TO someone or something. It can be used in both positive contexts (ascribing credit) and negative contexts (ascribing blame).

"ascribe" 通常后接 "to"——你把某事 ascribe to 某人或某物。它既可用于正面语境（归功于），也可用于负面语境（归咎于）。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historians **ascribe** the fall of the empire to a combination of economic decline and military overexpansion.
   历史学家将帝国的衰落归因于经济衰退和军事过度扩张的共同作用。

2. She modestly refused to **ascribe** her remarkable success to personal genius, crediting her mentors instead.
   她谦虚地拒绝将自己非凡的成功归于个人天赋，而是归功于她的导师们。

3. Critics often **ascribe** deeper symbolic meaning to the author's seemingly simple and straightforward narrative choices.
   评论家常常将更深层的象征意义归于这位作者看似简单直白的叙事选择。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"A-scribe" — think of a scribe (writer) who writes down (assigns) credit or blame to someone in the records.

"A-scribe"——想象一个 scribe（抄写员）在记录中写下（分配）功劳或过错给某人。
