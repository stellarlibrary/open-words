# 🍔 corpulent

🔊 /ˈkɔːrpjʊlənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Having a large, bulky body; excessively fat or obese
**中文：** adj. 肥大的；肥胖的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obese（肥胖的）
- stout（粗壮的）
- portly（肥胖的）
- rotund（圆胖的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **corpulent figure / corpulent man / corpulent frame / corpulent appearance**

👉 Example: The portrait depicted a corpulent nobleman seated in an ornate velvet armchair.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician warned the **corpulent** patient that immediate dietary changes were necessary to reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.
   医生警告这位肥胖的患者，必须立即改变饮食以降低心血管疾病的风险。

2. Historical records describe King Henry VIII as increasingly **corpulent** in his later years, requiring specially constructed furniture and armor.
   历史记录描述亨利八世国王在晚年越来越肥胖，需要特制的家具和盔甲。

3. The novelist created a memorable **corpulent** character whose jovial personality and generous spirit endeared him to every reader.
   这位小说家塑造了一个令人难忘的肥胖角色，其快活的性格和慷慨的精神使每位读者都喜欢他。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "corp-ulent" as "corpus" (body) + "ulent" (full of) — literally full of body, meaning excessively large and heavy.
把"corpulent"想成"corpus"（身体）加上"ulent"（充满）——字面意思就是身体充盈，指过度肥大和沉重。
