# 🤖 automaton

🔊 /ɔːˈtɒmətən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A machine that operates on its own; a person who acts in a mechanical, routine manner without thinking.
**中文：** n. 自动机；机器人；行动呆板的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- robot（机器人）
- android（仿生人）
- machine（机器）
- drone（无人机；盲从者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **like an automaton / function as an automaton / become an automaton**

👉 Example: After years of repetitive work, he moved through his daily tasks like an automaton.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The factory workers performed their tasks like **automatons**, repeating the same precise movements for twelve consecutive hours daily.
   工厂工人们像自动机一样执行任务，每天连续十二个小时重复着同样精确的动作。

2. The eighteenth-century inventor astonished audiences by displaying a chess-playing **automaton** that appeared to think independently.
   这位十八世纪的发明家展示了一台似乎能独立思考的下棋自动机，令观众们惊叹不已。

3. Critics warned that excessive reliance on standardized testing could transform creative students into mindless **automatons** in classrooms.
   批评者警告说，过度依赖标准化考试可能会将有创造力的学生变成课堂上没有思想的机器人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Automaton" contains "auto" (self) + "maton" (acting) — it acts by itself, like a robot on autopilot.
"Automaton"包含"auto"（自己）+"maton"（行动）——它自己行动，就像一个处于自动驾驶模式的机器人。
