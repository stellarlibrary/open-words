# 🌀 gyrate

🔊 /ˈdʒaɪreɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To move in a circle or spiral; to revolve or rotate. (adj.) Having a coiled or spiral form
**中文：** v. 旋转；回旋 adj. 旋涡状的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rotate（旋转）
- revolve（转动）
- spin（旋转）
- whirl（急转）
- spiral（盘旋）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gyrate wildly / gyrate around / gyrate to the music / hips gyrate**

👉 Example: The tornado caused debris to gyrate violently through the air for several terrifying minutes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The electrons **gyrate** around the magnetic field lines at frequencies determined by the strength of the field.
   电子沿磁力线回旋，其频率由磁场强度决定。

2. Stock prices continued to **gyrate** unpredictably throughout the trading session, unsettling investors and market analysts alike.
   股票价格在整个交易时段中持续不可预测地波动，令投资者和市场分析师深感不安。

3. The dancer began to **gyrate** gracefully across the stage, captivating the audience with fluid circular movements.
   舞者开始在舞台上优雅地旋转，以流畅的回旋动作吸引了观众。

Think of "gyrate" as containing "gyro" — like a gyroscope that keeps spinning and rotating in circles.
把"gyrate"想成包含"gyro（陀螺仪）"——就像陀螺仪一样不停地旋转。
