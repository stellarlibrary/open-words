# 🪳 sordid

🔊 /ˈsɔːrdɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Filthy and degrading; morally ignoble or base; wretched and squalid
**中文：** adj. 肮脏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- squalid（肮脏的）
- vile（卑鄙的）
- disreputable（声名狼藉的）
- wretched（可悲的）
- ignoble（卑劣的）

---

Use with: **sordid affair / sordid details / sordid past / sordid history / sordid conditions**

👉 Example: The investigative journalist exposed the sordid details behind the financial scandal that had been concealed for years.
