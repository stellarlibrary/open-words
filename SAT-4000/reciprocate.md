# 🔄 reciprocate

🔊 /rɪˈsɪprəkeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To respond to a gesture or action by making a corresponding one in return; to give and receive mutually; to interchange
**中文：** v. 报答；互换；互给；回报

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- return（回报）
- repay（报答）
- requite（回报）
- exchange（交换）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reciprocate a favor / reciprocate kindness / reciprocate feelings / reciprocate the gesture**

👉 Example: She wanted to reciprocate his generosity by volunteering at the charity he supported.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's warm reception was **reciprocated** when the delegation visited the host country for formal diplomatic talks.
   当代表团前往东道国进行正式外交会谈时，大使的热情接待得到了回报。

2. True friendship requires the willingness to **reciprocate** acts of kindness without expecting material rewards in return.
   真正的友谊需要愿意回报善意之举，而不期望获得物质回报。

3. After the neighboring country offered disaster relief, the government was eager to **reciprocate** with financial and logistical assistance.
   在邻国提供灾难救援后，政府急于以财政和后勤援助作为回报。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reciprocate" as "recipe for cooperation" — you follow a recipe of giving back, creating a cycle of mutual generosity.
把"reciprocate"想成"recipe（食谱）for cooperation（合作）"——按照回报的食谱行事，形成互惠互助的循环。
