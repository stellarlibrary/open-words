# ⚡ transformer

🔊 /trænsˈfɔːrmər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A device that transfers electrical energy from one circuit to another, usually changing the voltage; also, something or someone that transforms
**中文：** 变压器；使变化的人或事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- converter（转换器）
- adapter（适配器）
- regulator（调节器）
- modifier（改变者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **electrical transformer / power transformer / step-up transformer / step-down transformer**

👉 Example: The electrical transformer outside the building converts high-voltage power into a level safe for residential use.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The utility company installed a new **transformer** to accommodate the increased electrical demand from the expanding residential neighborhood.
   公用事业公司安装了一台新的变压器，以满足不断扩大的住宅区日益增长的电力需求。

2. Engineers designed a more efficient **transformer** that reduces energy loss during the conversion of high-voltage current to lower voltages.
   工程师设计了一种更高效的变压器，可减少高压电流转换为低压时的能量损耗。

3. The damaged **transformer** caused a widespread power outage that affected thousands of homes and businesses throughout the metropolitan area.
   损坏的变压器导致大面积停电，影响了整个都市区的数千户家庭和商业场所。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "transform-er" as "one that transforms" — a transformer is a device that transforms voltage from one level to another.
把"transformer"想成"transform（转变）+ er（器具）"——变压器就是将电压从一个等级转变到另一个等级的装置。
