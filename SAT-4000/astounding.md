# 🤯 astounding

🔊 /əˈstaʊn.dɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Surprisingly impressive or notable; causing great astonishment or shock.
**中文：** 令人震惊的；令人惊骇的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- astonishing（令人惊讶的）
- staggering（令人震惊的）
- breathtaking（令人叹为观止的）
- stupefying（令人目瞪口呆的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **astounding discovery/achievement/success/speed/amount/fact**

👉 Example: The team achieved an astounding victory despite being the underdogs throughout the entire tournament.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers published an **astounding** report that challenged decades of established scientific theory.
   研究人员发表了一份令人震惊的报告，挑战了数十年来已确立的科学理论。

2. She demonstrated an **astounding** level of composure during the intense cross-examination in the courtroom.
   她在法庭上激烈的交叉询问中展现了令人震惊的镇定水平。

3. The company reported **astounding** growth figures that far exceeded every analyst's quarterly projections.
   该公司公布了令人震惊的增长数据，远远超出了每位分析师的季度预测。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ASTOUNDING" = "ASTOUND" + "-ING" — it describes something that has the power to astound or stun you completely.
"ASTOUNDING"就是"ASTOUND（震惊）"的形容词形式——描述某事物具有让你完全目瞪口呆的力量。
