# 🕊️ autonomous

🔊 /ɔːˈtɒnəməs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having the freedom to govern oneself or control one's own affairs; independent and self-directing.
**中文：** adj. 自治的；自主的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- independent（独立的）
- self-governing（自治的）
- sovereign（主权的）
- self-sufficient（自给自足的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **autonomous region / autonomous vehicle / autonomous decision / fully autonomous**

👉 Example: The university operates as an autonomous institution, free from direct government control.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The territory was granted **autonomous** status, allowing its residents to establish their own local laws and governance.
   该地区被授予自治地位，允许其居民制定自己的地方法律和治理制度。

2. Engineers are developing fully **autonomous** vehicles that can navigate complex urban environments without any human intervention whatsoever.
   工程师们正在开发完全自主的车辆，能够在没有任何人为干预的情况下导航复杂的城市环境。

3. The research team functioned as an **autonomous** unit within the corporation, setting their own priorities and timelines independently.
   该研究团队作为公司内部的一个自主单位运作，独立设定自己的优先事项和时间表。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Auto" means self, "nomos" means law — autonomous means making your own laws, governing yourself.
"Auto"意为自己，"nomos"意为法律——autonomous意味着制定自己的法律，自我管理。
