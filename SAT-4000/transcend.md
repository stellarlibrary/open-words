# 🚀 transcend

🔊 /trænˈsɛnd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To go beyond the limits of; to surpass or exceed in quality, degree, or scope
**中文：** 胜过；超越；超出……的范围

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- surpass（超越）
- exceed（超过）
- outstrip（胜过）
- eclipse（使黯然失色）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transcend boundaries / transcend limitations / transcend expectations / transcend differences**

👉 Example: Great works of literature transcend cultural boundaries and speak to universal human experiences.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The composer's final symphony was said to **transcend** all conventional musical forms and redefine orchestral composition entirely.
   这位作曲家的最后一部交响曲据说超越了所有传统音乐形式，彻底重新定义了管弦乐创作。

2. The humanitarian organization sought to **transcend** political divisions by providing aid to all affected communities without discrimination.
   该人道主义组织试图超越政治分歧，不加歧视地向所有受影响的社区提供援助。

3. Her remarkable athletic achievements **transcend** the realm of sport and serve as an inspiration to aspiring leaders everywhere.
   她卓越的体育成就超越了体育领域，成为各地有抱负的领导者的榜样。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-cend" as "climbing across and above" — from Latin "trans" (across) + "scandere" (to climb), literally climbing beyond limits.
把"transcend"想成"trans（穿越）+ scend（攀登）"——从拉丁语来看，就是攀越极限，超越一切。
