# 📅 triennial

🔊 /traɪˈɛniəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Occurring every three years; lasting for three years. (n.) A plant that lives for three years; an event held every three years.
**中文：** adj. 每三年的；持续三年的 n. 三年生植物；三年一届的活动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- three-yearly（每三年的）
- triyearly（三年一次的）
- periodic（周期性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **triennial exhibition / triennial review / triennial report / triennial cycle**

👉 Example: The museum hosts a triennial exhibition that showcases the most innovative contemporary art from around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The organization conducts a **triennial** review of its strategic plan to ensure alignment with evolving industry standards and goals.
   该组织每三年对其战略计划进行一次审查，以确保与不断变化的行业标准和目标保持一致。

2. The **triennial** art festival attracts thousands of visitors from across the globe, featuring works by both emerging and established artists.
   这个三年一届的艺术节吸引了来自全球各地数千名参观者，展出新兴和知名艺术家的作品。

3. Botanists classified the newly discovered species as a **triennial** because it completes its entire life cycle within precisely three years.
   植物学家将这一新发现的物种归类为三年生植物，因为它在恰好三年内完成整个生命周期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tri-ennial" — "tri" means three and "ennial" relates to years (like "annual" for one year, "biennial" for two). Triennial means every three years.
把"triennial"拆分为"tri-ennial"——"tri"表示三，"ennial"与年份有关（如annual一年，biennial两年），triennial就是每三年。
