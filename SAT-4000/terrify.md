# 😱 terrify

🔊 /ˈterɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause someone to feel extreme fear or dread; to frighten greatly
**中文：** 恐吓；使惊恐；使极度害怕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frighten（使恐惧）
- horrify（使恐惧）
- petrify（使吓呆）
- intimidate（威吓）
- alarm（使惊恐）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **terrify someone into doing something / terrify the audience / terrified of heights / absolutely terrified**

👉 Example: The sudden earthquake terrified the residents, sending thousands rushing into the streets in panic.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prospect of speaking before an audience of thousands continued to **terrify** even the most seasoned public officials.
   在数千人面前演讲的前景继续使最有经验的公务员都感到恐惧。

2. Reports of the approaching hurricane **terrified** coastal communities, prompting authorities to issue mandatory evacuation orders immediately.
   即将来袭的飓风报告使沿海社区感到恐惧，促使当局立即发布强制撤离命令。

3. The documentary's graphic depiction of environmental collapse was designed to **terrify** viewers into taking immediate and decisive action.
   这部纪录片对环境崩溃的生动描绘旨在恐吓观众采取立即而果断的行动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "terri-fy" as "terror + fy (make)" — to terrify is to make someone feel terror.
把"terrify"想成"terror（恐怖）+ fy（使）"——terrify就是使某人感到恐怖。
