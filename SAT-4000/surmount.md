# 🏔️ surmount

🔊 /sərˈmaʊnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To overcome a difficulty or obstacle; to climb over or get past something; to be placed on top of
**中文：** v. 战胜；超越；克服

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overcome（克服）
- conquer（征服）
- prevail over（战胜）
- triumph over（胜过）
- transcend（超越）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surmount obstacles / surmount difficulties / surmount challenges / surmount a barrier**

👉 Example: Through determination and hard work, she was able to surmount every obstacle in her path.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The engineering team worked tirelessly to **surmount** the technical challenges that had delayed the project for several consecutive months.
   工程团队不知疲倦地工作，以克服导致项目连续数月延迟的技术难题。

2. Despite facing severe financial hardship, the young entrepreneur managed to **surmount** every barrier and build a thriving international business.
   尽管面临严重的经济困难，这位年轻的企业家还是设法克服了每一个障碍，建立了一家蓬勃发展的国际企业。

3. The ancient fortress was **surmounted** by a massive stone tower that could be seen from miles away across the surrounding valley.
   这座古老的堡垒顶部矗立着一座巨大的石塔，从周围山谷数英里外都能看到。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surmount" as "sur (over) + mount (mountain)" — to surmount is to climb over the mountain, conquering whatever stands in your way.
把"surmount"想成"sur（超过）+ mount（山）"——surmount就是翻越高山，征服挡在你面前的一切。
