# 🐑 gambol

🔊 /ˈɡæmbəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To run or jump about playfully; to frolic. (n.) An act of running or jumping about playfully
**中文：** v. 雀跃；嬉戏；耍闹 n. 欢跳；雀跃；嬉戏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- frolic（嬉戏）
- cavort（欢跃）
- romp（嬉闹）
- prance（雀跃）
- caper（蹦跳）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gambol about / gambol across / gambol in the fields / gambol with delight**

👉 Example: The lambs gamboled joyfully across the meadow on the first warm day of spring.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Children **gamboled** freely across the sunlit courtyard while their parents gathered for the annual community celebration.
   孩子们在阳光明媚的庭院里自由地欢跳嬉戏，而他们的父母则聚在一起参加年度社区庆典。

2. The poet described woodland creatures that **gamboled** through enchanted forests, evoking a sense of pastoral innocence and wonder.
   诗人描写了在魔法森林中雀跃的林间生灵，唤起了一种田园般的纯真和奇妙感。

3. Young deer were observed **gamboling** along the riverbank, a scene that delighted the wildlife researchers conducting their survey.
   人们观察到小鹿沿着河岸欢跳嬉戏，这一场景令正在进行调查的野生动物研究人员感到欣喜。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gambol" as "game + bol(l)" — like a ball bouncing around in a game, gamboling means jumping and bouncing playfully.
把"gambol"想成"game + bol(l)"——就像游戏中弹跳的球一样，gamboling意味着欢快地蹦蹦跳跳。
