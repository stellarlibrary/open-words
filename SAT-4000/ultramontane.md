# 🏔️ ultramontane

🔊 /ˌʌltrəˈmɒnteɪn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person living beyond the mountains, especially south of the Alps. (adj.) Situated beyond the mountains; advocating supreme papal authority
**中文：** n. 山那边的人；教皇至上主义者 adj. 山那边的；主张教皇至上的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- transalpine（阿尔卑斯山那边的）
- papal（教皇的）
- foreign（外国的）
- overseas（海外的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ultramontane doctrine / ultramontane views / ultramontane party / ultramontane regions**

👉 Example: The ultramontane faction within the church insisted on absolute loyalty to papal decrees.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **ultramontane** movement in nineteenth-century Europe sought to concentrate all ecclesiastical authority in the papacy.
   十九世纪欧洲的教皇至上运动试图将所有教会权力集中于教皇手中。

2. Scholars debated whether the **ultramontane** territories had developed distinct cultural traditions compared to those north of the Alps.
   学者们争论山那边的领土是否相比阿尔卑斯山以北地区发展出了独特的文化传统。

3. His **ultramontane** sympathies put him at odds with those who favored national control over religious institutions.
   他的教皇至上主义倾向使他与那些主张国家控制宗教机构的人产生了分歧。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ultra-montane" as "ultra (beyond) + montane (mountain)" — literally, the person or thing beyond the mountain.
把"ultramontane"拆分为"ultra（超越）+ montane（山的）"——字面意思就是山那边的人或事物。
