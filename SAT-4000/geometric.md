# 📐 geometric

🔊 /ˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to geometry; characterized by regular lines, shapes, and forms; increasing or decreasing by a constant ratio
**中文：** adj. 几何学的；几何学图形的；几何级数的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mathematical（数学的）
- symmetrical（对称的）
- proportional（成比例的）
- angular（有角的）
- regular（规则的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **geometric pattern / geometric shape / geometric progression / geometric design / geometric growth**

👉 Example: The architect incorporated bold geometric patterns into the facade to create a striking visual effect.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artist's work featured intricate **geometric** patterns inspired by traditional Islamic architecture and ancient mosaic craftsmanship.
   这位艺术家的作品以受传统伊斯兰建筑和古代马赛克工艺启发的复杂几何图案为特色。

2. The population exhibited **geometric** growth over the decade, doubling approximately every three years before resources became critically scarce.
   该人口在十年间呈现几何级数增长，大约每三年翻一番，直到资源变得极度匮乏。

3. Modern computational tools enable engineers to model **geometric** relationships with extraordinary precision in structural design and analysis.
   现代计算工具使工程师能够在结构设计和分析中以非凡的精度建模几何关系。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "geometric" as "geo-metric" — measuring ("metric") the earth ("geo"), which is exactly what geometry originally meant in ancient Greece.
把"geometric"拆成"geo-metric"——测量（"metric"）地球（"geo"），这正是几何学在古希腊的最初含义。
