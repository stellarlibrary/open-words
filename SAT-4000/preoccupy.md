# 🧠 preoccupy

🔊 /priˈɑːkjəpaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To dominate or engross the mind of someone to the exclusion of other thoughts; to absorb one's attention entirely
**中文：** v. 使全神贯注；迷住；使心事重重

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- engross（使全神贯注）
- absorb（吸引）
- obsess（使着迷）
- consume（占据）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preoccupied with / preoccupied by / a preoccupying concern / deeply preoccupied**

👉 Example: The director was so preoccupied with budget constraints that she overlooked several critical staffing issues.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The looming threat of economic recession continued to **preoccupy** policymakers throughout the lengthy deliberations in parliament.
   经济衰退的迫在眉睫的威胁在议会漫长的审议过程中一直困扰着决策者们。

2. Scholars have long been **preoccupied** with the question of how ancient civilizations managed such sophisticated engineering feats.
   学者们长期以来一直专注于古代文明如何完成如此精密工程壮举的问题。

3. Environmental concerns increasingly **preoccupy** the minds of voters, significantly shaping electoral outcomes in recent national elections.
   环境问题日益占据选民的心思，显著影响了近年来全国选举的结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-occupy" as something that "occupies" your mind "before" anything else can get in.
把"preoccupy"想成"pre（预先）+ occupy（占据）"——某件事预先占据了你的心思，让其他事情无法进入。
