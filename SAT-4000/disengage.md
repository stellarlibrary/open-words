# 🔗 disengage

🔊 /ˌdɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To release or detach from something that holds fast or connects; to withdraw from involvement or commitment
**中文：** v. 脱离，松开；使脱离接触

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- detach（分离）
- release（释放）
- withdraw（撤出）
- disconnect（断开）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disengage from / disengage the clutch / disengage oneself / emotionally disengage**

👉 Example: The pilot had to disengage the autopilot and take manual control of the aircraft immediately.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The peacekeeping forces were ordered to **disengage** from the conflict zone after diplomatic negotiations reached a preliminary ceasefire agreement.
   在外交谈判达成初步停火协议后，维和部队奉命从冲突地区撤离。

2. Many employees tend to **disengage** from their work when they feel that their contributions are neither recognized nor valued by management.
   许多员工在感到自己的贡献既不被管理层认可也不被重视时，往往会从工作中脱离。

3. The mechanic carefully **disengaged** the faulty gear mechanism to prevent further damage to the engine's internal components during the repair.
   技工小心地松开了故障的齿轮机构，以防在维修过程中对发动机内部部件造成进一步损坏。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-engage" — the opposite of "engage" (参与/连接). When you disengage, you undo the engagement and pull away.
把"disengage"想成"dis-engage"——"engage"的反义（参与/连接）。当你disengage时，你解除了连接并抽身离开。
