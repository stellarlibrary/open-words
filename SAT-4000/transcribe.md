# 📝 transcribe

🔊 /trænˈskraɪb/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To put thoughts, speech, or data into written or printed form; to copy out from an original source
**中文：** 转录；抄写；把……记录下来

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- copy（抄写）
- record（记录）
- reproduce（复制）
- document（记载）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transcribe an interview / transcribe notes / transcribe music / transcribe a recording**

👉 Example: The research assistant was asked to transcribe the recorded interviews into a written document.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Court reporters must accurately **transcribe** every word spoken during legal proceedings to ensure a reliable official record.
   法庭记录员必须准确转录法律程序中所说的每一个字，以确保可靠的官方记录。

2. The musicologist spent years working to **transcribe** ancient melodies from fragmentary manuscripts into modern musical notation.
   这位音乐学家花了数年时间将古代旋律从残缺的手稿转录为现代乐谱。

3. Advanced software can now **transcribe** spoken language into text with remarkable accuracy across multiple languages simultaneously.
   先进的软件现在能够以惊人的准确度同时将多种语言的口语转录为文字。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-scribe" as "writing across" — you transfer words from one form (speech) across to another form (writing).
把"transcribe"想成"trans（转移）+ scribe（写）"——把文字从一种形式（语音）转移到另一种形式（书面）。
