# 🌱 reclamation

🔊 /ˌrɛkləˈmeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The process of reclaiming or recovering something, especially converting waste or uncultivated land into usable condition; the act of taking back or reforming
**中文：** n. 开垦；改造；收回；再利用；矫正

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restoration（恢复）
- recovery（收回）
- rehabilitation（修复）
- redemption（赎回）
- recoupment（收回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **land reclamation / reclamation of resources / reclamation project / water reclamation**

👉 Example: The reclamation of abandoned mining sites has become a priority for environmental agencies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The federal government invested billions of dollars in the **reclamation** of arid desert land for agricultural purposes.
   联邦政府投入数十亿美元用于开垦干旱沙漠土地以供农业使用。

2. Environmental engineers developed innovative techniques for the **reclamation** of polluted waterways in the industrial region.
   环境工程师开发了创新技术，用于修复工业区被污染的水道。

3. The **reclamation** of valuable metals from electronic waste has become an increasingly profitable and sustainable industry.
   从电子废弃物中回收贵重金属已成为一个日益盈利且可持续的行业。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-claim-ation" as "claiming again" — you are claiming back land, resources, or dignity that was lost or wasted.
把"reclamation"想成"re-claim-ation"——你在重新索回那些失去或浪费的土地、资源或尊严。
