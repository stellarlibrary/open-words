# 🏃 endurance

🔊 /ɪnˈdjʊərəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability to withstand hardship, fatigue, or stress; the power of enduring or bearing prolonged difficulty
**中文：** n. 忍耐力；忍耐；持久

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stamina（耐力）
- perseverance（毅力）
- resilience（韧性）
- fortitude（坚毅）
- tenacity（顽强）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **endurance test / endurance training / physical endurance / beyond endurance / test of endurance**

👉 Example: Marathon running is widely regarded as the ultimate test of physical endurance and mental determination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The astronauts underwent rigorous **endurance** training to prepare their bodies for the extreme conditions of prolonged space travel.
   宇航员接受了严格的耐力训练，以使身体为长期太空旅行的极端条件做好准备。

2. Her remarkable **endurance** throughout the lengthy legal battle earned the admiration of colleagues and opponents alike in the courtroom.
   她在漫长的法律战中展现出的非凡忍耐力赢得了法庭上同事和对手的一致钦佩。

3. The documentary chronicles the incredible **endurance** of polar explorers who survived months of isolation in subzero temperatures.
   这部纪录片记录了极地探险家令人难以置信的耐力，他们在零度以下的温度中孤立生存了数月。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "endur-ance" as the "endure + dance" — imagine a dancer who keeps performing through exhaustion; that is true endurance.
把"endurance"想成"endure（忍耐）+ dance（舞蹈）"——想象一个舞者在筋疲力尽时仍然坚持表演，这就是真正的耐力。
