# 💎 preference

🔊 /ˈprɛfərəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A greater liking for one alternative over another; priority or advantage given to one thing over others
**中文：** n. 偏爱，喜爱；优惠；优先选择；优先权

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- predilection（偏好）
- inclination（倾向）
- partiality（偏爱）
- favoritism（偏袒）
- priority（优先）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **preference for / give preference to / personal preference / state a preference**

👉 Example: Admissions committees may give preference to applicants with demonstrated leadership experience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The survey revealed a strong **preference** among consumers for locally sourced organic produce over imported conventional alternatives.
   调查显示，消费者强烈偏好本地来源的有机农产品，而非进口的传统替代品。

2. University hiring policies often give **preference** to candidates who have published extensively in peer-reviewed academic journals.
   大学的招聘政策通常优先考虑在同行评审学术期刊上发表大量论文的候选人。

3. Individual **preference** plays a significant role in determining which therapeutic approach a patient will respond to most effectively.
   个人偏好在决定患者对哪种治疗方法反应最有效方面起着重要作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "prefer-ence" — the state of "preferring" something, like putting one choice on a higher shelf where you can reach it first.
把"preference"想成"prefer（更喜欢）+ ence（状态）"——更喜欢某物的状态，就像把一个选择放在更高的架子上以便优先拿到。
