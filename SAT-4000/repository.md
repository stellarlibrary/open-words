# 🏛️ repository

🔊 /rɪˈpɒzɪtɔːri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A place where things are stored or deposited; a person regarded as a store of information or knowledge
**中文：** n. 贮藏室，仓库；知识库；学识渊博的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- storehouse（仓库）
- warehouse（库房）
- archive（档案馆）
- vault（保险库）
- depository（存放处）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **repository of knowledge / data repository / repository of information / central repository**

👉 Example: The national library serves as the primary repository of historical documents and rare manuscripts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university library functions as a vast **repository** of academic knowledge spanning several centuries of scholarship.
   大学图书馆作为一个庞大的学术知识库，涵盖了几个世纪的学术成果。

2. The newly constructed digital **repository** will preserve millions of endangered cultural artifacts for future generations worldwide.
   新建的数字资料库将为全世界后代保存数百万件濒危文化文物。

3. Professor Chen is widely regarded as a living **repository** of expertise on ancient Chinese philosophical traditions.
   陈教授被广泛认为是中国古代哲学传统方面的活知识库。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-posit-ory" as a place where things are "reposited" (put back) — a storage place where items rest safely.
把"repository"想成一个东西被"存放回去"的地方——一个物品安全存放的储藏室。
