# 📷 underexposure

🔊 /ˌʌndərɪkˈspoʊʒər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Insufficient exposure of photographic film or a digital sensor to light, resulting in a dark image; inadequate exposure or attention
**中文：** 曝光不足；（引申）关注不够；接触不足

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- insufficient exposure（曝光不足）
- underdevelopment（发展不足）
- inadequate lighting（光线不足）
- underrepresentation（代表不足）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **underexposure of film / underexposure to light / suffer from underexposure / correct underexposure**

👉 Example: The photographer adjusted her camera settings to prevent underexposure in the dimly lit gallery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **underexposure** of the photographs taken during the evening ceremony rendered most of the images too dark to use.
   晚间典礼期间拍摄的照片曝光不足，使得大多数图像因太暗而无法使用。

2. Media **underexposure** of rural health issues has contributed to a widespread lack of public awareness about critical disparities.
   媒体对农村健康问题关注不足，导致公众普遍缺乏对关键差距的认识。

3. The filmmaker deliberately used **underexposure** as an artistic technique to create a somber and oppressive atmosphere throughout the scene.
   电影制作人故意使用曝光不足作为一种艺术手法，在整个场景中营造出阴郁压抑的氛围。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "under-exposure" — the image is UNDER-exposed to light, so it comes out too dark, like hiding something in shadows.
把"underexposure"想成"under（不足）+ exposure（曝光）"——光照不够，图像太暗，就像把东西藏在阴影中。
