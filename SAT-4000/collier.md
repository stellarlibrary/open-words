# ⛏️ collier

🔊 /ˈkɒliər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A coal miner; a ship used for transporting coal
**中文：** n. 矿工；运煤船

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- coal miner（煤矿工人）
- miner（矿工）
- pitman（矿工）
- coal ship（运煤船）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a seasoned collier / collier ship / the colliers went on strike / life of a collier**

👉 Example: The old collier spent forty years working deep underground in the coal mines of northern England.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **collier** descended into the dark shaft each morning, enduring hazardous conditions to extract coal for the industrial economy.
   这位矿工每天早上都下到黑暗的矿井中，忍受危险的工作条件为工业经济开采煤炭。

2. A fleet of **colliers** transported thousands of tons of coal along the eastern coastline to fuel the nation's growing factories.
   一支运煤船队沿东海岸运送了数千吨煤炭，为这个国家不断增长的工厂提供燃料。

3. The museum exhibit documented the harsh daily lives of **colliers** during the nineteenth-century industrial revolution in Britain.
   博物馆的展览记录了十九世纪英国工业革命期间矿工们艰苦的日常生活。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "collier" as "coal" + "-ier" (one who works with) — a collier is a person who works with coal or a ship that carries it.
把"collier"想成"coal"（煤）加上"-ier"（从事...的人）——collier就是从事煤炭工作的人或运煤的船。
