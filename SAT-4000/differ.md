# 🔀 differ

🔊 /ˈdɪfər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To be unlike or distinct from something else; to disagree or hold a different opinion
**中文：** v. 不同，相异；意见不同

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vary（不同）
- diverge（分歧）
- contrast（对比）
- disagree（不同意）
- deviate（偏离）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **differ from / differ in / differ on / differ widely / beg to differ**

👉 Example: The two studies differ significantly in their methodology and conclusions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The twins may appear identical in appearance, but they **differ** considerably in temperament and intellectual interests.
   这对双胞胎外表可能看起来一模一样，但他们在性情和学术兴趣上差异很大。

2. Experts continue to **differ** on the most effective strategies for addressing the long-term consequences of climate change.
   专家们在应对气候变化长期后果的最有效策略上继续存在分歧。

3. The revised proposal **differs** from the original version in several critical aspects, including scope and projected budget.
   修订后的提案在几个关键方面与原始版本不同，包括范围和预计预算。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "differ" as "dif-" (apart) + "fer" (carry) — to carry apart, meaning things are carried in separate directions, making them unlike each other.
把"differ"想成"dif-"（分开）+"fer"（带）——分开带走，意味着事物被带向不同方向，彼此不同。
