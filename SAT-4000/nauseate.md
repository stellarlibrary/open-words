# 🤮 nauseate

🔊 /ˈnɔːzieɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause feelings of nausea or disgust; to feel sick or repelled
**中文：** v. 作呕；厌恶；产生恶感

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sicken（使恶心）
- repel（使反感）
- disgust（使厌恶）
- revolt（使作呕）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nauseate someone / be nauseated by / nauseating smell / nauseating experience**

👉 Example: The overwhelming stench from the factory was enough to nauseate anyone within a mile.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The putrid odor emanating from the abandoned warehouse was sufficient to **nauseate** even the most experienced investigators.
   从废弃仓库散发出的恶臭足以让即使是最有经验的调查人员也感到作呕。

2. Graphic footage of the environmental disaster **nauseated** viewers and prompted widespread calls for immediate governmental action.
   环境灾难的触目惊心的画面使观众感到恶心，并引发了要求政府立即采取行动的广泛呼声。

3. The defendant's callous testimony about the incident **nauseated** the jury members who sat in stunned and appalled silence.
   被告对事件冷酷无情的证词使陪审团成员感到厌恶，他们震惊而愤慨地沉默不语。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nauseate" as "nausea" + "-ate" (to cause) — to cause nausea in someone, making them feel sick or disgusted.
把"nauseate"想成"nausea"加"-ate"（使……）——使某人产生恶心感，让他们感到不适或厌恶。
