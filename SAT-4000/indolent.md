# 😴 indolent

🔊 /ˈɪndələnt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Habitually lazy or idle; disinclined to exertion; (medical) causing little or no pain
**中文：** adj. 懒惰的；无痛的；不痛的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lazy（懒惰的）
- slothful（怠惰的）
- lethargic（无精打采的）
- idle（闲散的）
- languid（倦怠的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **indolent lifestyle / indolent attitude / indolent tumor / indolent person**

👉 Example: The professor criticized the indolent student for failing to submit any assignments throughout the semester.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The once-promising scholar became increasingly **indolent**, neglecting research duties and refusing to attend faculty meetings.
   这位曾经前途光明的学者变得越来越懒惰，忽视研究职责并拒绝参加教职工会议。

2. Physicians determined that the **indolent** tumor posed minimal immediate risk and recommended a conservative monitoring approach.
   医生们确定这个无痛性肿瘤几乎没有直接风险，并建议采取保守的监测方案。

3. His **indolent** disposition prevented him from pursuing the ambitious career goals he had once articulated with enthusiasm.
   他懒散的性情使他无法追求他曾经满怀热情地阐述过的远大职业目标。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-DO-lent" as "not DO-ing" — an indolent person does not do anything; they are too lazy to act.
把"indolent"想成"in(不)+do(做)+lent(借出)"——一个懒惰的人什么都不做，连借给别人力气都不肯。
