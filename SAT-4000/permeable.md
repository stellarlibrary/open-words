# 💧 permeable

🔊 /ˈpɜːrmiəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Allowing liquids or gases to pass through; having pores or openings that permit the movement of substances
**中文：** adj. 能透过的；有渗透性的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- porous（多孔的）
- penetrable（可穿透的）
- absorbent（能吸收的）
- pervious（能透过的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **permeable membrane / permeable soil / permeable surface / highly permeable**

👉 Example: The permeable membrane allows water molecules to pass through while blocking larger contaminants.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geologist confirmed that the **permeable** rock formation would allow groundwater to flow freely into the underground reservoir.
   地质学家确认，这种可渗透的岩层将使地下水自由流入地下水库。

2. Urban planners advocated for **permeable** pavement materials to reduce surface runoff and prevent flooding in residential areas.
   城市规划者提倡使用可渗透的路面材料，以减少地表径流并防止住宅区洪水。

3. The cell's selectively **permeable** membrane regulates which substances may enter and exit to maintain internal equilibrium.
   细胞的选择性渗透膜调控哪些物质可以进出，以维持内部平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "perme-able" — "perme" comes from "permeate" (to pass through), and "-able" means capable of. Something permeable is able to be permeated.
把"permeable"想成"perme（渗透）+ able（能够）"——能够被渗透的，即有渗透性的。
