# 👂 auricular

🔊 /ɔːˈrɪkjʊlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of or relating to the ear or the sense of hearing; shaped like an ear; perceived by or spoken into the ear.
**中文：** 耳的；耳状的；听觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- aural（听觉的）
- auditory（听觉的）
- otic（耳的）
- ear-shaped（耳状的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **auricular confession**, **auricular nerve**, **auricular surface**, **auricular fibrillation**

👉 Example: The priest received the penitent's auricular confession in the dimly lit confessional booth.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physician carefully examined the **auricular** structures to determine the cause of the patient's hearing loss.
   医生仔细检查了耳部结构，以确定患者听力丧失的原因。

2. In medieval tradition, **auricular** confession was considered essential for the absolution of serious sins.
   在中世纪传统中，耳语式忏悔被认为是赦免严重罪行所必需的。

3. The anatomist identified the **auricular** surface of the ilium as a key landmark for skeletal analysis.
   解剖学家将髂骨的耳状面确定为骨骼分析的关键标志。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Break it down: "auri-" comes from the Latin "auris" meaning "ear," plus "-cular" meaning "related to." Think of an "auricle" — the outer part of the ear.
拆解记忆："auri-"来自拉丁语"auris"意为"耳朵"，加上"-cular"表示"与……有关"。想想"auricle（耳廓）"——耳朵的外部结构。
