# 🔥 salamander

🔊 /ˈsæləˌmændər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A type of amphibian resembling a lizard; mythologically, a creature able to endure fire; a broiling device or oven
**中文：** n. 蝾螈

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- newt（蝾螈）
- amphibian（两栖动物）
- eft（小蝾螈）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spotted salamander / fire salamander / salamander habitat / salamander species**

👉 Example: Biologists discovered a new species of salamander in the remote mountain streams of the region.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **salamander** population in the forest has declined sharply due to habitat destruction and increasing water pollution levels.
   由于栖息地被破坏和水污染日益加剧，森林中的蝾螈数量急剧下降。

2. In medieval mythology, the **salamander** was believed to possess the extraordinary ability to survive within flames unharmed.
   在中世纪神话中，人们相信蝾螈拥有在火焰中安然无恙的非凡能力。

3. Researchers tagged each **salamander** with a microchip to monitor migration patterns across the wetland ecosystem over several seasons.
   研究人员给每只蝾螈植入微芯片，以监测其在湿地生态系统中数个季节的迁徙模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sala-mander" as "sala-man-der" — a little man ("man") who wanders ("mander") through wet "sala" (halls), just like a salamander wanders through damp habitats.
把"salamander"想成"sala（厅堂）+ man（人）+ der"——一个在潮湿厅堂里游荡的小人，就像蝾螈在潮湿的栖息地中穿行。
