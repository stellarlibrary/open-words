# 😢 doleful

🔊 /ˈdoʊlfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Expressing sorrow; mournful; causing grief or sadness; gloomy
**中文：** adj. 寂寞的；悲哀的；阴沉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mournful（悲伤的）
- sorrowful（忧伤的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- somber（阴沉的）
- woeful（悲哀的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **doleful expression / doleful eyes / doleful tone / doleful cry**

👉 Example: The abandoned puppy gazed at passersby with doleful eyes that seemed to plead for compassion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pianist's **doleful** melody echoed through the concert hall, evoking profound emotions of loss and longing in the audience.
   钢琴家悲伤的旋律回荡在音乐厅中，唤起了观众内心深处对失去和渴望的情感。

2. After receiving the tragic news, she wore a **doleful** expression that conveyed the depth of her grief to everyone.
   收到悲痛的消息后，她面带悲哀的表情，向每个人传达了她内心深处的悲伤。

3. The **doleful** atmosphere of the abandoned village served as a stark reminder of the devastating effects of prolonged conflict.
   被遗弃村庄阴沉的氛围鲜明地提醒着人们长期冲突所带来的毁灭性影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dole" (sorrow) + "ful" (full of) — doleful means literally "full of sorrow," like someone in deep grief.
想象"dole"（悲伤）+ "ful"（充满）——doleful字面意思就是"充满悲伤的"，像一个人沉浸在深深的悲痛中。
