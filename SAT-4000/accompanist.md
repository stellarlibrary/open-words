# 🎹 accompanist

🔊 /əˈkʌmpənɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A musician who plays an instrumental accompaniment for a soloist, ensemble, or group performance.
**中文：** 伴奏者；伴随者。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- backing musician（伴奏乐手）
- collaborator（合作者）
- supporter（支持者）
- partner（搭档）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **piano/skilled/professional accompanist**; **serve as an accompanist**

👉 Example: A talented accompanist can elevate a soloist's performance considerably.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned pianist served as the **accompanist** for the celebrated soprano throughout her international tour.
   这位著名钢琴家在整个国际巡演中担任这位知名女高音的伴奏。

2. A skilled **accompanist** must be sensitive to the soloist's tempo and expressive nuances during performance.
   一位熟练的伴奏者必须在演出中对独奏者的节奏和表现细微之处保持敏感。

3. The choir director urgently sought a competent **accompanist** to prepare for the upcoming holiday concert.
   合唱团指挥急切地寻找一位称职的伴奏者，为即将到来的节日音乐会做准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accompanist" = "accompany" + "-ist" (person) — the person who accompanies a performer with music.
accompanist 就是"accompany（伴奏）"加"-ist（……的人）"——为表演者伴奏的人。
