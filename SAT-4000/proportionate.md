# ⚖️ proportionate

🔊 /prəˈpɔːrʃənət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Corresponding in size, degree, or intensity; being in proper proportion; neither too much nor too little relative to something
**中文：** adj. 成比例的；相称的；适当的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- commensurate（相称的）
- corresponding（对应的）
- equivalent（等同的）
- balanced（均衡的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **proportionate response / proportionate to the crime / proportionate share / proportionate representation**

👉 Example: The court ruled that the punishment must be proportionate to the severity of the offense committed.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. International law requires that any military response be **proportionate** to the threat posed by the aggressor nation.
   国际法要求任何军事回应都必须与侵略国构成的威胁相称。

2. Each department received funding **proportionate** to its size, ensuring equitable distribution of the organization's limited annual resources.
   每个部门获得与其规模成比例的资金，确保组织有限的年度资源得到公平分配。

3. The architect ensured that the windows were **proportionate** to the wall dimensions, creating an aesthetically pleasing and balanced exterior.
   建筑师确保窗户与墙壁尺寸成比例，创造出美观且平衡的外观。

---

Think of "proportionate" as "proportion + ate" — something that has eaten the right proportion, perfectly sized and balanced.
把"proportionate"想成"proportion + ate"——吃掉了正确比例的东西，大小完美且均衡。
