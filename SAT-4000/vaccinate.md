# 💉 vaccinate

🔊 /ˈvæksɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To administer a vaccine to a person or animal in order to produce immunity against a specific disease
**中文：** 接种疫苗

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- immunize（使免疫）
- inoculate（接种）
- inject（注射）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vaccinate against / vaccinate children / fully vaccinated / vaccinate the population / vaccinate widely**

👉 Example: Health authorities urged parents to vaccinate their children against measles before the school year began.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government launched a nationwide campaign to **vaccinate** the entire adult population against the rapidly spreading infectious disease.
   政府发起了一项全国性运动，为全体成年人接种疫苗以抵御迅速蔓延的传染病。

2. Researchers demonstrated that the initiative to **vaccinate** rural communities significantly reduced the incidence of preventable childhood illnesses.
   研究人员证明，为农村社区接种疫苗的举措显著降低了可预防儿童疾病的发病率。

3. The hospital established mobile clinics to **vaccinate** underserved populations who lacked convenient access to traditional healthcare facilities.
   医院设立了流动诊所，为缺乏便利医疗设施的弱势群体接种疫苗。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vaccinate" from Latin "vacca" meaning "cow" — the first vaccine was derived from cowpox to protect against smallpox.
想想"vaccinate"来自拉丁语"vacca（牛）"——第一种疫苗来源于牛痘，用于预防天花。
