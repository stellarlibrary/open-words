# 🏛️ probity

🔊 /ˈproʊbɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of having strong moral principles; complete honesty and uprightness of character
**中文：** n. 正直；诚实；廉洁

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- integrity（正直）
- honesty（诚实）
- rectitude（正直）
- uprightness（正派）
- virtue（美德）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **moral probity / financial probity / man of probity / question someone's probity**

👉 Example: A judge must be a person of unquestionable probity and impartiality in all matters.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nominee's **probity** was thoroughly examined by the committee before she was confirmed to serve on the federal appellate court.
   该提名人的正直品格在她被确认进入联邦上诉法院任职前受到了委员会的彻底审查。

2. Financial **probity** is essential for any public official entrusted with the management of taxpayer funds and government resources.
   对于任何受托管理纳税人资金和政府资源的公职人员来说，财务廉洁至关重要。

3. The organization's reputation for **probity** attracted donors who valued transparency and ethical governance in charitable institutions.
   该组织的正直声誉吸引了重视慈善机构透明度和道德治理的捐助者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "probity" as "probe-ity" — a person of probity can withstand any probe or investigation because they have nothing to hide.
把"probity"想成"probe-ity"——一个正直的人能经受住任何调查，因为他们没有什么需要隐瞒的。
