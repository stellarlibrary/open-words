# ⛓️ subjection

🔊 /səbˈdʒɛkʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of being under the control or dominion of another; submission or conquest
**中文：** n. 征服

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- domination（统治）
- subjugation（征服）
- servitude（奴役）
- subordination（隶属）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subjection to authority / subjection of a people / hold in subjection / complete subjection**

👉 Example: The colonial rulers maintained their subjection of the indigenous population through strict legal and military measures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged **subjection** of the colonized territories resulted in lasting cultural and economic consequences for the affected populations.
   对殖民地领土的长期征服给受影响的民众造成了持久的文化和经济后果。

2. The philosopher argued that voluntary **subjection** to just laws is essential for maintaining a stable and functioning civil society.
   这位哲学家认为，自愿服从公正的法律对于维持一个稳定运转的公民社会至关重要。

3. Throughout history, many nations have endured periods of foreign **subjection** before ultimately reclaiming their sovereignty and independence.
   纵观历史，许多国家在最终重新获得主权和独立之前，都经历了被外国征服的时期。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "subjection" as "subject" + "-ion" — being made a subject under someone's rule, like subjects of a king forced into obedience.
把"subjection"想成"subject"（臣民）+"-ion"（状态）——被迫成为某人统治下的臣民，就像被国王强迫服从的子民。
