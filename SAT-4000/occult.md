# 🌑 occult

🔊 /əˈkʌlt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, verb, noun | 形容词, 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Mystical, supernatural, or beyond ordinary understanding; hidden or concealed. (v.) To conceal or hide from view. (n.) Supernatural practices or phenomena
**中文：** adj. 神秘的，玄妙的；超自然的；隐蔽的 v. 掩蔽，隐藏 n. 神秘学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mystical（神秘的）
- supernatural（超自然的）
- esoteric（深奥的）
- arcane（晦涩的）
- hidden（隐藏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **occult practices / occult knowledge / the occult / occult forces / occult bleeding (medical)**

👉 Example: The ancient library contained texts devoted to the occult, covering alchemy, astrology, and divination.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scholars of the Renaissance period often studied **occult** philosophies alongside mainstream scientific disciplines without perceiving any contradiction.
   文艺复兴时期的学者经常在研究主流科学学科的同时研究神秘哲学，并不认为有任何矛盾。

2. The physician ordered additional tests to check for **occult** bleeding that standard examinations had failed to detect.
   医生下令进行额外检查，以排查常规检查未能发现的隐性出血。

3. The moon will briefly **occult** the distant star tonight, providing astronomers with a rare opportunity to study its atmosphere.
   今晚月球将短暂地遮掩那颗遥远的恒星，为天文学家提供了研究其大气层的罕见机会。

把"occult"想成一个秘密的"cult（邪教）"——occult涉及隐秘的、神秘的仪式，只有少数入门者才知道。
