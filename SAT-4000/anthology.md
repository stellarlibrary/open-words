# 📚 anthology

🔊 /ænˈθɑːlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A published collection of poems, stories, or other literary works chosen by a compiler.

**中文：** 选集；文选（由编者挑选的诗歌、故事或其他文学作品的合集）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- collection 合集
- compilation 汇编
- compendium 纲要
- treasury 宝库

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **an anthology of** (poems/essays/short stories), **publish an anthology**, **literary anthology**, **poetry anthology**.

> Example: The professor assigned a poetry **anthology** covering the Romantic period.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The publisher released a celebrated **anthology** of twentieth-century American short stories last autumn.
   该出版社去年秋天发行了一部备受赞誉的二十世纪美国短篇小说选集。

2. Her poem was selected for inclusion in a prestigious literary **anthology** of contemporary verse.
   她的诗被选入一部享有盛誉的当代诗歌文学选集。

3. Students were required to read the entire **anthology** before composing their comparative analysis essays.
   学生们被要求在撰写比较分析论文之前通读整部选集。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

Think of "antho-" (flower, from Greek *anthos*) + "-logy" (collection). An **anthology** is like gathering a bouquet of literary flowers — a curated collection of the finest works.

联想"antho-"（花，源自希腊语 *anthos*）+"--logy"（收集）。**Anthology** 就像采集一束文学之花——精心挑选的最佳作品合集。
