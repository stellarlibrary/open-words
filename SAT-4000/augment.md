# 📈 augment

🔊 /ɔːɡˈmɛnt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** To make something greater by adding to it; to increase in size, number, strength, or extent.

**中文：** 增加；增大

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- increase（增加）
- enlarge（扩大）
- supplement（补充）
- enhance（增强）
- amplify（扩充）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

"Augment" is more formal than "increase" and implies deliberately adding something to make a whole greater. It often collocates with "income," "forces," "resources," and "reality." The noun form is "augmentation."

"augment" 比 "increase" 更正式，暗示有意地添加某物以使整体更强大。常与 income、forces、resources、reality 等搭配。其名词形式为 "augmentation"。

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university decided to **augment** its scholarship fund to attract more talented students.
   该大学决定**增加**奖学金基金以吸引更多优秀学生。

2. She took a part-time tutoring position to **augment** her modest teaching salary.
   她找了一份兼职家教工作来**增加**自己微薄的教师工资。

3. The military commander chose to **augment** the ground forces with additional aerial support.
   军事指挥官决定用额外的空中支援来**增强**地面部队。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Augment" sounds like "aug-MEANT" — it was *meant* to grow bigger. Also, think of "augmented reality" (AR), where digital elements are *added* to the real world to make the experience greater.

"augment" 听起来像 "aug-MEANT"——它注定要变得更大。另外，想想"增强现实"（augmented reality, AR），数字元素被*添加*到现实世界中，使体验更丰富。
