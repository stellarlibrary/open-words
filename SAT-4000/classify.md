# 🗂️ classify

🔊 /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To arrange or organize into categories or groups according to shared qualities; to designate as officially secret
**中文：** v. 分类；分等

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- categorize（归类）
- sort（分类）
- catalog（编目）
- group（分组）
- rank（排列）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **classify information / classify species / classify data / classify documents as confidential**

👉 Example: Biologists classify organisms into hierarchical categories based on shared evolutionary characteristics.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists **classify** newly discovered species by examining morphological features, genetic sequences, and ecological behavior patterns.
   科学家通过检查形态特征、基因序列和生态行为模式来对新发现的物种进行分类。

2. The government decided to **classify** the intelligence report, restricting access to personnel with the highest security clearance.
   政府决定将该情报报告列为机密，仅限拥有最高安全许可的人员查阅。

3. Librarians **classify** books according to standardized systems so that patrons can locate relevant materials with minimal difficulty.
   图书馆员按照标准化系统对书籍进行分类，以便读者能轻松找到相关资料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "class-ify" — to put things into a "class" or category, like sorting students into different classrooms by grade level.
把"classify"想成把事物放入一个"class"（类别）——就像按年级把学生分到不同的教室一样。
