# 🚲 tricycle

🔊 /ˈtraɪsɪkl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A vehicle with three wheels, especially one driven by pedals, typically used by young children or for specialized transport.
**中文：** n. 三轮车；三轮脚踏车

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trike（三轮车）
- three-wheeler（三轮车）
- velocipede（脚踏车）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ride a tricycle / children's tricycle / motorized tricycle / tricycle rider**

👉 Example: The toddler proudly pedaled her new tricycle around the park while her parents watched with delight.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. In many Southeast Asian cities, the motorized **tricycle** serves as an essential and affordable mode of public transportation.
   在许多东南亚城市，机动三轮车是一种必不可少且经济实惠的公共交通方式。

2. The child development study found that learning to ride a **tricycle** improves coordination and spatial awareness in early childhood.
   儿童发展研究发现，学习骑三轮车可以提高幼儿的协调能力和空间意识。

3. Engineers designed an innovative solar-powered **tricycle** capable of traveling long distances without relying on conventional fossil fuels.
   工程师们设计了一种创新的太阳能三轮车，能够在不依赖传统化石燃料的情况下长途行驶。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tri-cycle" — "tri" means three and "cycle" means wheel or circle. A tricycle is simply a vehicle with three wheels.
把"tricycle"拆分为"tri-cycle"——"tri"表示三，"cycle"表示轮子或循环，三轮车就是有三个轮子的车。
