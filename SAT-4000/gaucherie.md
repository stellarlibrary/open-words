# 😬 gaucherie

🔊 /ˈɡoʊʃəri/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Awkward or tactless behavior; a socially clumsy or graceless act or remark
**中文：** n. 笨拙；无礼；粗鲁的言行

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- awkwardness（笨拙）
- clumsiness（不灵巧）
- tactlessness（不圆滑）
- gracelessness（不雅）
- ineptitude（不适当）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **social gaucherie / commit a gaucherie / display gaucherie / youthful gaucherie**

👉 Example: His gaucherie at the formal dinner embarrassed everyone seated at the ambassador's table.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The young diplomat's **gaucherie** at the state banquet was widely reported and caused considerable embarrassment to the delegation.
   这位年轻外交官在国宴上的失态被广泛报道，给代表团带来了相当大的尴尬。

2. Despite her intellectual brilliance, her occasional **gaucherie** in social settings made colleagues reluctant to invite her to formal events.
   尽管她才智出众，但她在社交场合偶尔的笨拙表现使同事们不愿邀请她参加正式活动。

3. The conduct manual was designed to help new employees avoid any **gaucherie** that might reflect poorly on the organization.
   这本行为手册旨在帮助新员工避免任何可能给组织带来不良影响的失礼行为。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gaucherie" from the French word "gauche" meaning left — historically, the left hand was considered clumsy, so gaucherie means social clumsiness.
把"gaucherie"与法语"gauche"（左边）联系起来——历史上左手被认为是笨拙的，所以gaucherie意为社交上的笨拙。
