# 💧 dissolve

🔊 /dɪˈzɒlv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause a solid substance to become incorporated into a liquid; to break down or decompose into parts
**中文：** v. 使溶解；使分解；融化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- melt（融化）
- liquefy（液化）
- disintegrate（分解）
- dissipate（消散）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dissolve in water / dissolve a partnership / dissolve into tears / dissolve parliament**

👉 Example: The sugar will dissolve more quickly if you stir the solution continuously.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist observed the compound **dissolve** gradually in the acidic solution over a period of several minutes.
   化学家观察到该化合物在酸性溶液中经过几分钟逐渐溶解。

2. The board voted unanimously to **dissolve** the committee after its recommendations had been fully implemented by the administration.
   在其建议被行政部门全面落实后，董事会一致投票决定解散该委员会。

3. Prolonged exposure to moisture caused the protective coating to **dissolve**, leaving the metal surface vulnerable to corrosion.
   长时间暴露于潮湿环境导致保护涂层溶解，使金属表面容易受到腐蚀。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-solve" as "dis- (apart) + solve (loosen)" — something loosens apart and breaks down into a liquid.
把"dissolve"想成"dis-（分开）+ solve（松开）"——某物松开分离，分解融入液体中。
