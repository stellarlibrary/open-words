# 🔬 cell

🔊 /sel/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The smallest structural and functional unit of an organism; a small room for a prisoner or monk; a device that converts chemical energy into electricity; a small compartment in a honeycomb
**中文：** n. 细胞；单人小室（牢房或修道院小房间）；电池；蜂房的巢室

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unit（单元）
- chamber（小室）
- compartment（隔间）
- cubicle（小隔间）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cell division / prison cell / fuel cell / cell membrane / cell biology**

👉 Example: The biologist examined the cell under a powerful microscope to study its internal structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Recent advances in **cell** biology have enabled scientists to understand the complex mechanisms governing cellular division and differentiation.
   细胞生物学的最新进展使科学家能够理解控制细胞分裂和分化的复杂机制。

2. The political prisoner spent over a decade confined to a narrow **cell**, yet he continued to write influential essays on human rights.
   这位政治犯在一间狭窄的牢房中被关押了十多年，但他继续撰写关于人权的有影响力的文章。

3. Engineers have developed a new hydrogen fuel **cell** that promises significantly greater energy efficiency than conventional battery-powered alternatives.
   工程师们开发了一种新型氢燃料电池，有望比传统的电池供电替代方案具有显著更高的能源效率。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cell" as the smallest "shell" of life — just like a tiny shell protects what is inside, a cell is the basic protective unit of all living organisms.
把"cell"想成生命最小的"shell（外壳）"——就像小小的外壳保护里面的东西一样，细胞是所有生物体的基本保护单元。
