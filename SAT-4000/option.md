# 🔀 option

🔊 /ˈɑːpʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A thing that is or may be chosen; the freedom or right to choose; a financial contract giving the right to buy or sell
**中文：** n. 选项；选择权；买卖的特权；选课

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- choice（选择）
- alternative（替代方案）
- selection（挑选）
- preference（偏好）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **have the option / viable option / stock option / keep one's options open**

👉 Example: Students have the option to select elective courses that align with their academic interests.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee evaluated every available **option** before reaching a consensus on the most sustainable development strategy.
   委员会在就最可持续的发展战略达成共识之前评估了每一个可用的选项。

2. Employees were granted stock **options** as part of their compensation package to incentivize long-term commitment to the firm.
   员工获得了股票期权作为薪酬方案的一部分，以激励他们对公司的长期承诺。

3. The physician presented several treatment **options** and encouraged the patient to make an informed decision after careful deliberation.
   医生提出了几种治疗方案，并鼓励患者在仔细考虑后做出明智的决定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "option" as "opt-ion" — to "opt" means to choose, so an option is something you can choose from.
把"option"想成"opt-ion"——"opt"意思是选择，所以option就是你可以从中做出选择的事物。
