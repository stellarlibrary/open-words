# 🕸️ gossamer

🔊 /ˈɡɑːsəmər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A fine, filmy substance consisting of cobwebs; sheer gauze fabric. (adj.) Extremely light, delicate, or tenuous
**中文：** n. 蛛丝；薄纱 adj. 轻飘飘的；薄如蝉翼的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- delicate（精致的）
- diaphanous（透明的）
- ethereal（轻灵的）
- filmy（薄膜般的）
- sheer（极薄的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gossamer threads / gossamer wings / gossamer fabric / gossamer veil**

👉 Example: The morning dew clung to gossamer threads strung between the garden hedges.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **gossamer** curtains in the ballroom allowed soft light to filter through, creating an ethereal atmosphere for guests.
   舞厅中薄纱般的窗帘让柔和的光线透入，为宾客营造出一种空灵的氛围。

2. Scientists discovered that the **gossamer** wings of the dragonfly possess remarkable structural strength despite their delicate appearance.
   科学家发现蜻蜓轻薄如纱的翅膀尽管外表纤细，却具有非凡的结构强度。

3. The poet wove **gossamer** imagery throughout the sonnet, evoking themes of fragility and the transience of human memory.
   诗人在十四行诗中编织了轻灵飘渺的意象，唤起了关于脆弱和人类记忆短暂性的主题。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gossamer" as "goose summer" — in old English, it referred to the warm autumn days when cobwebs float in the air like delicate threads.
把"gossamer"想成"goose summer"（鹅的夏天）——在古英语中，它指的是深秋温暖的日子里蛛丝像轻纱一样飘浮在空中。
