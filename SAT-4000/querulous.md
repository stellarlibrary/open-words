# 😒 querulous

🔊 /ˈkwerələs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Complaining in a petulant or whining manner; habitually finding fault or expressing dissatisfaction
**中文：** adj. 爱发牢骚的；易怒的；爱挑剔的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- petulant（暴躁的）
- peevish（易怒的）
- whiny（爱抱怨的）
- fretful（烦躁的）
- grumbling（嘟囔的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **querulous tone / querulous voice / querulous complaint / querulous manner**

👉 Example: The patient spoke in a querulous tone, complaining about every aspect of his hospital care.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **querulous** customer demanded to speak with the manager after finding minor imperfections in every dish served.
   这位爱挑剔的顾客在每道菜中都发现小瑕疵后，要求与经理对话。

2. Her **querulous** remarks during the meeting frustrated colleagues who were trying to advance the project constructively.
   她在会议期间爱发牢骚的言论令那些试图建设性推进项目的同事感到沮丧。

3. The elderly professor had become increasingly **querulous** over the years, criticizing every student's work with sharp disapproval.
   这位年迈的教授多年来变得越来越爱挑剔，对每位学生的作业都尖锐地批评。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "querulous" as "query + ulous" — someone who is always querying and questioning everything in a complaining way.
把"querulous"想成"query（质问）+ ulous"——一个总是以抱怨的方式质疑和挑剔一切的人。
