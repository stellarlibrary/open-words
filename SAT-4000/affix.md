# 📎 affix

🔊 /əˈfɪks/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词，名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To attach, fasten, or add something to; to append one's signature. (n.) A word element added to a root to modify meaning; an attachment.
**中文：** (v.) 粘上；署名 (n.) 词缀；附加物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attach（附上）
- fasten（固定）
- append（附加）
- stick（粘贴）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **affix a stamp, affix a signature, affix a label, prefix/suffix as types of affix**
👉 Example: Please affix your signature at the bottom of the document.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The notary instructed the client to **affix** her official seal beside the signature on the contract.
   公证人指示客户在合同签名旁边加盖她的官方印章。

2. Workers carefully **affixed** warning labels to every container before the shipment departed the warehouse.
   工人们在货物离开仓库前仔细地将警告标签粘贴在每个容器上。

3. In linguistics, an **affix** is a morpheme added to a word root to create a new meaning or grammatical form.
   在语言学中，词缀是添加在词根上以产生新含义或语法形式的语素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "affix" as "a fix" — you fix something onto another thing, like fixing a stamp onto a letter by sticking it on.
把"affix"想成"a fix"——你把某物固定到另一物上，就像把邮票贴固定在信封上一样。
