# 🪞 resemblance

🔊 /rɪˈzɛmbləns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of looking or being alike; a similarity or likeness between people or things
**中文：** n. 相似；相似之处；相似物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- similarity（相似）
- likeness（相像）
- semblance（外表）
- affinity（近似）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **bear a resemblance to / striking resemblance / close resemblance / family resemblance**

👉 Example: The twins bore a striking resemblance to each other that made it nearly impossible to tell them apart.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Critics noted a strong **resemblance** between the emerging author's prose style and that of the celebrated Victorian novelist.
   评论家注意到这位新兴作家的散文风格与那位著名维多利亚时代小说家之间有很强的相似之处。

2. The architectural design of the new museum bears a remarkable **resemblance** to ancient Greek temples in both proportion and symmetry.
   新博物馆的建筑设计在比例和对称性上与古希腊神庙有着惊人的相似之处。

3. Despite their geographical separation, the two languages share a curious **resemblance** that suggests a common ancestral origin.
   尽管地理上相隔甚远，这两种语言却有着奇特的相似之处，暗示着共同的祖先起源。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "resemblance" as "re-sembl-ance" — things that seem to assemble together again because they look so alike.
把"resemblance"想成"re-sembl-ance"——那些看起来如此相像以至于似乎可以重新组合在一起的事物。
