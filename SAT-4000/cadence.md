# 🎵 cadence

🔊 /ˈkeɪdəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A rhythmic flow of sounds or words; the rise and fall of the voice in speaking; a modulation or inflection
**中文：** 节奏；韵律；抑扬顿挫；（语调的）起伏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rhythm（节奏）
- tempo（节拍）
- inflection（声调变化）
- intonation（语调）
- meter（韵律）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **musical cadence / rhythmic cadence / cadence of speech / natural cadence**

👉 Example: The professor's measured cadence made even the most complex lectures accessible and engaging.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The poet carefully crafted each line to achieve a melodic **cadence** that echoed the gentle movement of ocean waves.
   诗人精心打磨每一行诗，以达到一种如海浪般柔和起伏的韵律。

2. Listeners found the orator's deliberate **cadence** and measured pauses remarkably persuasive during the formal parliamentary debate.
   在正式的议会辩论中，听众发现演说者刻意的抑扬顿挫和有节制的停顿极具说服力。

3. The **cadence** of the marching soldiers created a unified, powerful rhythm that resonated through the entire square.
   行进士兵的步伐节奏形成了一种统一而有力的韵律，回荡在整个广场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cadence" as "ca-DANCE" — when you dance, you follow a rhythm, just as cadence refers to rhythmic flow.
把"cadence"想成"ca-DANCE（舞蹈）"——跳舞时要跟随节奏，cadence正是指节奏和韵律。
