# 🚫 abnegate

🔊 /ˈæbnɪɡeɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To renounce or reject; to give up rights, claims, or responsibilities; to deny oneself something
**中文：** v. 放弃；舍弃；禁忌；克制

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- renounce（放弃）
- relinquish（放弃）
- forsake（舍弃）
- abstain（戒绝）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **abnegate responsibility / abnegate one's rights / abnegate power / self-abnegation**

👉 Example: The leader chose to abnegate his authority and transfer all power to the elected council.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The CEO was criticized for attempting to **abnegate** responsibility for the company's catastrophic financial losses.
   这位首席执行官因试图推卸公司灾难性财务损失的责任而受到批评。

2. Monks who join this order must **abnegate** all worldly possessions and live in complete simplicity.
   加入该修会的僧侣必须放弃所有世俗财产，过完全简朴的生活。

3. No political leader should **abnegate** the duty to protect the fundamental rights of every citizen.
   任何政治领导人都不应放弃保护每位公民基本权利的职责。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "ab-NEGATE" — to negate or deny something away from yourself, like negating your own desires.
想想"ab-NEGATE"——否定或拒绝某物，就像否定自己的欲望一样。
