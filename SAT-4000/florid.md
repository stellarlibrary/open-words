# 🌺 florid

🔊 /ˈflɔːrɪd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Elaborately or excessively ornate in style; having a red or flushed complexion; flowery in appearance or language
**中文：** adj. 绚丽的；华丽的；气色好的；红润的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ornate（华丽的）
- elaborate（精心制作的）
- flowery（辞藻华丽的）
- ruddy（红润的）
- flamboyant（炫目的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **florid prose / florid complexion / florid style / florid language / florid decoration**

👉 Example: The critic dismissed the novel's florid prose as unnecessarily verbose and lacking in narrative substance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Victorian mansion featured **florid** interior decorations with intricate carvings, gilded moldings, and elaborate ceiling frescoes.
   这座维多利亚式豪宅拥有绚丽的室内装饰，包括精致的雕刻、镀金线脚和精美的天花板壁画。

2. The professor's **florid** writing style, while undeniably elegant, often obscured the central argument of his scholarly papers.
   教授华丽的写作风格虽然无可否认地优雅，但常常模糊了其学术论文的核心论点。

3. The physician noted the patient's unusually **florid** complexion and recommended a thorough cardiovascular examination as a precaution.
   医生注意到患者异常红润的面色，并建议进行全面的心血管检查以防万一。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "florid" as coming from "flor-" (flower) — flowers are colorful and elaborate, and a florid style is similarly ornate and vivid.
把"florid"想成来自"flor"（花）——花朵色彩缤纷、精巧华丽，florid的风格同样绚丽夺目。
