# ⛪ sacrilege

🔊 /ˈsækrɪlɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Violation or misuse of something regarded as sacred; an outrageous or profane act against something holy
**中文：** n. 亵渎圣物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- blasphemy（亵渎）
- desecration（玷污）
- profanation（亵渎）
- impiety（不敬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **commit sacrilege / regarded as sacrilege / an act of sacrilege / tantamount to sacrilege**

👉 Example: Many traditionalists considered the modern renovation of the historic cathedral to be an act of sacrilege.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The vandalism of the ancient temple was condemned by religious leaders worldwide as an unforgivable act of **sacrilege**.
   对古庙的破坏行为被世界各地的宗教领袖谴责为不可饶恕的亵渎行为。

2. For devoted fans of classical music, performing the symphony with electronic instruments was considered utter **sacrilege**.
   对于古典音乐的忠实爱好者来说，用电子乐器演奏交响乐被视为彻底的亵渎。

3. The historian argued that destroying irreplaceable medieval manuscripts constituted intellectual **sacrilege** of the highest order.
   这位历史学家认为，销毁不可替代的中世纪手稿构成了最严重的知识亵渎。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sacri-lege" — "sacr" means "sacred" and "lege" sounds like "steal," so sacrilege is like stealing from the sacred.
把"sacrilege"拆解为"sacr（神圣的）+lege（偷取）"——从神圣之物中偷取，即亵渎圣物。
