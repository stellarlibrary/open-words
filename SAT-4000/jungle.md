# 🌿 jungle

🔊 /ˈdʒʌŋɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A dense, tropical forest with tangled vegetation; a dangerous or ruthless place or situation; a confused or disordered mass
**中文：** n. 丛林，密林；危险地带；乱七八糟的一堆

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- rainforest（热带雨林）
- wilderness（荒野）
- thicket（灌木丛）
- labyrinth（迷宫）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **concrete jungle / jungle of regulations / law of the jungle / urban jungle**

👉 Example: Navigating the jungle of bureaucratic procedures requires patience and persistence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The explorers spent several weeks traversing the dense **jungle** in search of previously undocumented plant species.
   探险者们花了数周时间穿越茂密的丛林，寻找此前未被记录的植物物种。

2. The corporate world can be a **jungle** where only the most resilient and strategic professionals manage to thrive.
   企业界可能是一片丛林，只有最坚韧、最有策略的专业人士才能蓬勃发展。

3. A **jungle** of tangled wires and outdated equipment made the server room nearly impossible to navigate efficiently.
   杂乱无章的电线和过时的设备使服务器机房几乎无法高效通行。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "jungle" as a place where everything is "jumbled" together — trees, vines, and animals all tangled in one chaotic mass.
把"jungle"想成一切都"混杂"在一起的地方——树木、藤蔓和动物都纠缠在一团混乱之中。
