# 👑 dictatorial

🔊 /ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or typical of a dictator; having or showing an autocratic manner; domineering and overbearing
**中文：** adj. 独裁的，专政的；专横的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- authoritarian（独裁的）
- tyrannical（暴虐的）
- despotic（专制的）
- domineering（专横的）
- imperious（傲慢的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **dictatorial regime / dictatorial power / dictatorial manner / dictatorial rule / dictatorial tone**

👉 Example: The manager's dictatorial style of leadership alienated nearly every member of the department.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **dictatorial** regime suppressed all forms of political dissent and maintained strict control over the national media.
   该独裁政权压制一切形式的政治异议，并对国家媒体实行严格管控。

2. Her **dictatorial** approach to managing the project left team members feeling undervalued and reluctant to contribute ideas.
   她对项目管理的专横方式让团队成员感到不被重视，不愿贡献想法。

3. Historians argue that **dictatorial** governments inevitably collapse when citizens demand transparency and democratic accountability.
   历史学家认为，当公民要求透明度和民主问责时，独裁政府必然会崩溃。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dictatorial" as "dictator" + "-ial" — behaving like a dictator, someone who dictates orders and tolerates no opposition.
把"dictatorial"想成"dictator"（独裁者）+"-ial"——像独裁者一样行事，发号施令、不容异议。
