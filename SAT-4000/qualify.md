# ✅ qualify

🔊 /ˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To meet the necessary requirements or conditions; to make competent or eligible; to modify or limit a statement
**中文：** v. 具有资格，证明合格；限制，限定；修饰

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- certify（证明）
- authorize（授权）
- modify（修饰）
- temper（缓和）
- restrict（限制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **qualify for a position / qualify a statement / qualify as an expert / qualify the remarks**

👉 Example: She needed to qualify her earlier assertion by acknowledging certain limitations of the study.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The doctoral candidate must complete a comprehensive examination to **qualify** for advancement to the dissertation stage of the program.
   博士候选人必须完成综合考试才有资格进入该项目的论文阶段。

2. The senator chose to **qualify** his endorsement by noting several reservations about the proposed legislation's long-term fiscal impact.
   这位参议员选择对他的支持加以限定，指出了对拟议立法长期财政影响的几点保留意见。

3. Only institutions that meet rigorous accreditation standards **qualify** to receive federal research funding under the new regulatory framework.
   在新的监管框架下，只有符合严格认证标准的机构才有资格获得联邦研究资金。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "quali-fy" — "quali" relates to "quality," so to qualify means to prove your quality meets the required standard.
把"qualify"想成"quali（质量）+ fy（使）"——使自己的质量达到要求的标准，即取得资格。
