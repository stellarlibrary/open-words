# 🏛️ palatial

🔊 /pəˈleɪʃəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Resembling a palace in size, grandeur, or magnificence; impressively spacious and luxurious.
**中文：** adj. 富丽堂皇的；宫殿般的；宏伟的；壮丽的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- magnificent（壮丽的）
- grand（宏伟的）
- luxurious（豪华的）
- opulent（富丽的）
- stately（庄严的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **palatial mansion / palatial residence / palatial estate / palatial surroundings / palatial home**

👉 Example: The billionaire entertained his guests in a palatial estate overlooking the Mediterranean coastline.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador's **palatial** residence featured marble floors, crystal chandeliers, and exquisite artwork collected from galleries across the continent.
   大使**富丽堂皇的**官邸铺设着大理石地板、水晶吊灯和从各地画廊收集的精美艺术品。

2. Critics questioned why public funds had been spent constructing such a **palatial** government building while citizens struggled with basic necessities.
   批评者质疑为何在公民为基本生活必需品发愁时，公共资金被用于建造如此**宏伟的**政府大楼。

3. The hotel's **palatial** ballroom could accommodate over a thousand guests and was frequently reserved for the city's most prestigious events.
   酒店**宫殿般的**宴会厅可容纳一千多位宾客，经常被预订用于该市最负盛名的活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "palat-ial" as directly derived from "palace" — something palatial is so grand and luxurious that it resembles a royal palace.
把"palatial"看作直接源自"palace"（宫殿）——palatial的事物如此宏伟豪华，以至于像皇家宫殿一样。
