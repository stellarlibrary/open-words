# 🔬 polymer

🔊 /ˈpɑːlɪmər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A large molecule composed of many repeated smaller units (monomers) bonded together, forming the basis of plastics, proteins, and many other materials
**中文：** n. 聚合物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- macromolecule（大分子）
- plastic（塑料）
- resin（树脂）
- compound（化合物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **synthetic polymer / natural polymer / polymer chain / polymer science / polymer chemistry**

👉 Example: Advances in polymer science have led to the development of lightweight materials used in aerospace engineering.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team synthesized a novel **polymer** that exhibited exceptional heat resistance and mechanical durability under extreme conditions.
   研究团队合成了一种新型聚合物，在极端条件下表现出卓越的耐热性和机械耐久性。

2. DNA is a natural **polymer** composed of nucleotide monomers that encode the genetic instructions for all living organisms.
   DNA是一种由核苷酸单体组成的天然聚合物，编码了所有生物体的遗传指令。

3. The environmental impact of synthetic **polymer** waste has prompted scientists to develop biodegradable alternatives for commercial packaging materials.
   合成聚合物废料的环境影响促使科学家为商业包装材料开发可生物降解的替代品。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "poly" (many) + "mer" (parts) — a polymer is made of many parts linked together in a long chain, like beads on a necklace.
把"polymer"拆成"poly（多）"+"mer（部分）"——聚合物由很多部分连接成长链，就像项链上的珠子。
