# 🔒 inalterable

🔊 /ɪnˈɔːltərəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not capable of being changed or modified; fixed and permanent in nature
**中文：** adj. 不变的；不能变更的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- unchangeable（不可改变的）
- immutable（永恒不变的）
- fixed（固定的）
- permanent（永久的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inalterable fact / inalterable truth / inalterable decision / inalterable law**

👉 Example: The committee declared that the terms of the agreement were inalterable and would not be renegotiated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The laws of thermodynamics represent **inalterable** principles that govern the behavior of energy in all physical systems.
   热力学定律代表着支配所有物理系统中能量行为的不可变更的原则。

2. The board announced that its decision regarding the merger was **inalterable**, regardless of shareholder objections or public criticism.
   董事会宣布其关于合并的决定是不可更改的，无论股东反对还是公众批评。

3. Historians regard the geographic constraints of ancient civilizations as **inalterable** factors that profoundly shaped their cultural development.
   历史学家将古代文明的地理限制视为深刻影响其文化发展的不可改变的因素。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-alter-able" — "in (not) + alter (change) + able" — simply not able to be altered; what is set remains set forever.
把"inalterable"拆成"in（不）+ alter（改变）+ able（能够）"——不能够被改变的；已经确定的就永远不变。
