# 🎁 keepsake

🔊 /ˈkiːpseɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A small item kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event; a memento or souvenir
**中文：** n. 纪念品；纪念物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- memento（纪念品）
- souvenir（纪念品）
- token（信物）
- remembrance（纪念物）
- relic（遗物）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **treasured keepsake / family keepsake / keepsake from / keep as a keepsake**

👉 Example: She kept the faded photograph as a precious keepsake from her childhood years abroad.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The antique locket served as a cherished **keepsake** that had been passed down through four generations of the family.
   这枚古董吊坠是一件珍贵的纪念品，已在家族中传承了四代。

2. Visitors to the historic site often purchase small **keepsakes** to commemorate their experience at the renowned archaeological destination.
   参观这一历史遗址的游客经常购买小纪念品来纪念他们在这一著名考古目的地的经历。

3. He carried a worn leather journal as a **keepsake** from his late mentor, a constant reminder of the lessons imparted.
   他随身携带一本磨损的皮革日记本，那是已故导师留给他的纪念品，时刻提醒着所传授的教诲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "keepsake" as "keep for the sake of memories" — you keep it for the sake of remembering someone or something special.
把"keepsake"想成"keep for the sake of memories"（为了记忆而保留）——你为了纪念某人或某件特别的事而保留它。
