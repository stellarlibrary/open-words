# 🥊 opponent

🔊 /əˈpoʊnənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who competes against or opposes another; an adversary. (adj.) Opposing; antagonistic
**中文：** n. 对手；反对者；敌手 adj. 对立的；敌对的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- adversary（对手）
- rival（竞争者）
- antagonist（敌手）
- competitor（竞争对手）
- foe（敌人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **political opponent / worthy opponent / opponent of a policy / formidable opponent**

👉 Example: The senator's most vocal opponent challenged every major proposal introduced during the legislative session.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chess grandmaster studied her **opponent's** strategy meticulously before executing a decisive move that secured her victory.
   这位国际象棋大师在执行决定性的一步之前仔细研究了对手的策略，从而确保了胜利。

2. Leading **opponents** of the proposed legislation organized a series of public demonstrations to express their strong dissatisfaction.
   拟议立法的主要反对者组织了一系列公众示威活动，以表达他们强烈的不满。

3. A skilled debater must anticipate the strongest arguments that an **opponent** is likely to present during the formal exchange.
   一位熟练的辩手必须预判对手在正式辩论中可能提出的最有力的论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "op-PONENT" as "opposite +ponent (one who poses)" — someone who poses themselves in an opposite position against you.
把"opponent"想成"opposite + ponent（提出者）"——一个站在你对立面提出反对意见的人。
