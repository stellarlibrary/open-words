# 🚗 congest

🔊 /kənˈdʒɛst/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To cause to become excessively full or blocked; to overcrowd; to cause an abnormal accumulation of blood in an organ or body part
**中文：** v. 使充满，使拥塞；使充血

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clog（堵塞）
- overcrowd（使过度拥挤）
- obstruct（阻塞）
- block（堵塞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **congest the roads / congested traffic / congested airways / congested area**

👉 Example: Heavy rainfall and construction work combined to congest the main arterial roads during rush hour.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rapid influx of commuters during peak hours tends to **congest** the city's already overburdened public transportation system.
   高峰时段通勤者的大量涌入往往使城市本已不堪重负的公共交通系统更加拥堵。

2. Allergic reactions can **congest** the nasal passages and sinuses, making it extremely difficult for patients to breathe normally.
   过敏反应会使鼻腔和鼻窦充血，令患者极难正常呼吸。

3. Urban planners warned that the proposed development would severely **congest** the surrounding neighborhoods and reduce overall livability.
   城市规划者警告说，拟议的开发项目将严重使周边社区拥堵，降低整体宜居性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-GEST" — like "gest" (a crowd gesture), when too many people gesture together in one place, things get congested.
把"congest"想成交通堵塞的画面——太多东西挤在一起（con-一起 + gest-堆积），形成拥堵。
