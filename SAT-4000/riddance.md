# 👋 riddance

🔊 /ˈrɪdəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of getting rid of something or someone undesirable; removal, deliverance, or expulsion
**中文：** n. 摆脱；解除

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- removal（移除）
- deliverance（解脱）
- elimination（消除）
- expulsion（驱逐）
- liberation（解放）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **good riddance / riddance of corruption / riddance from debt / a welcome riddance**

👉 Example: When the tyrannical manager resigned, the entire department whispered "good riddance."

1. The community celebrated the **riddance** of the invasive species that had threatened local wildlife for over a decade.
   社区庆祝摆脱了威胁当地野生动物长达十多年的入侵物种。

2. Many citizens viewed the **riddance** of outdated regulations as a necessary step toward meaningful economic reform and growth.
   许多市民将解除过时法规视为实现有意义的经济改革和增长的必要步骤。

3. The phrase "good **riddance**" expresses relief at being freed from an unpleasant person, situation, or burden in one's life.
   "good riddance"这个短语表达了从生活中一个令人不快的人、情况或负担中解脱后的如释重负。
