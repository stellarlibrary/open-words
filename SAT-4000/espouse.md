# 💍 espouse

🔊 /ɪˈspaʊz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To adopt or support a cause, belief, or way of life; to marry or become engaged to
**中文：** v. 支持；嫁娶；赞成

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- advocate（倡导）
- champion（拥护）
- support（支持）
- embrace（接受）
- endorse（赞同）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **espouse a cause / espouse a belief / espouse a philosophy / espouse the values of**

👉 Example: The activist has long espoused the principles of environmental sustainability and social justice.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The political leader passionately **espoused** democratic reform and urged citizens to participate actively in the electoral process.
   这位政治领袖热情地支持民主改革，并敦促公民积极参与选举进程。

2. The university proudly **espouses** the values of academic freedom, intellectual diversity, and rigorous pursuit of scientific truth.
   该大学自豪地倡导学术自由、思想多元和严谨追求科学真理的价值观。

3. Although few contemporaries initially **espoused** his radical economic theories, history ultimately vindicated his groundbreaking contributions to the field.
   尽管最初很少有同时代人赞成他激进的经济理论，但历史最终证明了他对该领域的开创性贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "espouse" as related to "spouse" — just as you commit to a spouse in marriage, you commit to a cause when you espouse it.
把"espouse"想成与"spouse（配偶）"相关——就像你在婚姻中对配偶做出承诺，当你espouse一项事业时，你也对它做出了承诺。
