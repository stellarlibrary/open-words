# 📚 bibliography

🔊 /ˌbɪbliˈɒɡrəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A list of books and articles referred to in a scholarly work; a systematic catalog of writings on a particular subject
**中文：** 参考书目；文献目录

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reference list（参考文献列表）
- catalog（目录）
- reading list（阅读书目）
- works cited（引用文献）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **annotated bibliography / comprehensive bibliography / compile a bibliography / include in the bibliography**

👉 Example: The professor required each student to submit an annotated bibliography before drafting the final paper.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher compiled an extensive **bibliography** of over two hundred sources spanning three decades of published scholarship.
   研究人员编纂了一份涵盖三十年已发表学术成果的两百多个来源的广泛参考书目。

2. An annotated **bibliography** requires the writer to summarize and evaluate each source rather than merely listing titles.
   注释性参考书目要求作者总结和评估每个来源，而不仅仅是列出标题。

3. The **bibliography** at the end of the dissertation revealed the extraordinary breadth of the candidate's interdisciplinary research.
   论文末尾的参考文献目录揭示了候选人跨学科研究的非凡广度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "biblio" (book, from Greek) + "graphy" (writing) — bibliography is literally "book writing," a written record of books and sources.
把"bibliography"拆分为"biblio"（书，源自希腊语）+"graphy"（书写）——bibliography字面意思是"书的记录"，即书籍和文献的书面记录。
