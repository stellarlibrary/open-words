# 👑 pomp

🔊 /pɑːmp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A splendid display of ceremony, magnificence, or grandeur; ostentatious show or display
**中文：** n. 盛况；浮华；壮丽；夸耀

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grandeur（壮丽）
- magnificence（辉煌）
- splendor（华丽）
- pageantry（盛典）
- ostentation（炫耀）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pomp and circumstance / with great pomp / full of pomp / regal pomp**

👉 Example: The royal wedding was celebrated with great pomp and ceremony befitting the occasion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coronation was conducted with extraordinary **pomp**, drawing spectators from every corner of the empire.
   加冕典礼以非凡的盛况举行，吸引了帝国各地的观众。

2. Critics argued that the festival's excessive **pomp** distracted from its original cultural and educational purpose.
   批评者认为节日过度的浮华分散了人们对其原本文化和教育目的的注意力。

3. Despite the **pomp** surrounding the diplomatic reception, the negotiations themselves remained tense and unresolved.
   尽管外交招待会场面壮观，谈判本身仍然紧张且悬而未决。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pomp" as the sound of trumpets — "pomp-pa-pa" — announcing a grand, magnificent ceremony full of splendor.
把"pomp"想成号角的声音——"pomp-pa-pa"——宣告一场盛大而辉煌的典礼。
