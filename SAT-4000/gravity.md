# ⚖️ gravity

🔊 /ˈɡrævɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The force that attracts objects toward the center of the earth; extreme importance or seriousness; solemn dignity
**中文：** n. 重力，地心引力；严重性；庄严，庄重

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- seriousness（严肃）
- solemnity（庄严）
- weightiness（重要性）
- gravitation（引力）
- severity（严重性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **the gravity of the situation / center of gravity / defy gravity / gravity of the offense**

👉 Example: The judge emphasized the gravity of the charges before delivering the verdict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambassador spoke with great **gravity** as she addressed the United Nations assembly regarding the escalating humanitarian crisis.
   大使在联合国大会上就不断升级的人道主义危机发言时语气十分庄重。

2. Scientists continue to investigate how **gravity** influences the formation and movement of galaxies across the vast expanse of space.
   科学家们继续研究重力如何影响星系在广袤太空中的形成和运动。

3. Few citizens fully appreciated the **gravity** of the economic crisis until major financial institutions began to collapse nationwide.
   直到主要金融机构开始在全国范围内倒闭，很少有公民充分认识到经济危机的严重性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gravity" as what keeps you grounded — both physically (the force pulling you down) and figuratively (a serious, weighty matter that demands attention).
把"gravity"想成让你"脚踏实地"的东西——既指物理上把你拉向地面的力，也比喻需要认真对待的严肃事务。
