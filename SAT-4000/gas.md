# 💨 gas

🔊 /ɡæs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A substance in a state in which it expands freely to fill any container; also informal for gasoline or fuel
**中文：** n. 气体；瓦斯；汽油

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- vapor（蒸气）
- fume（烟气）
- fuel（燃料）
- gasoline（汽油）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **gas station / natural gas / gas leak / run out of gas / gas pipeline**

👉 Example: The laboratory technician carefully monitored the concentration of the toxic gas in the sealed chamber.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemical plant released a dangerous **gas** into the atmosphere, prompting authorities to evacuate nearby residential areas immediately.
   化工厂向大气中释放了一种危险气体，促使当局立即疏散附近居民区。

2. Scientists discovered that the newly identified **gas** exhibited unique properties when subjected to extremely low temperatures.
   科学家们发现这种新发现的气体在极低温度下表现出独特的性质。

3. The city council approved a plan to transition from coal to natural **gas** in order to reduce carbon emissions significantly.
   市议会批准了一项从煤炭转向天然气的计划，以大幅减少碳排放。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "gas" as something that "goes all around in space" — it expands to fill whatever container holds it.
把"gas"想成"goes all around in space"——它会膨胀充满任何容器。
