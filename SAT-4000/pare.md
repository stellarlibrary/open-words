# 🔪 pare

🔊 /pɛr/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To peel or trim the outer layer of something; to reduce or diminish gradually
**中文：** v. 消减；削皮；剪；修掉（边等）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- peel（削皮）
- trim（修剪）
- reduce（减少）
- whittle（削减）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pare down / pare back / pare an apple / pare costs / pare to the bone**

👉 Example: The company decided to pare down its workforce to remain competitive in the market.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board of directors voted to **pare** the annual budget by fifteen percent in response to declining revenue streams.
   董事会投票决定将年度预算削减百分之十五，以应对收入来源的下降。

2. The editor worked diligently to **pare** the lengthy manuscript down to a concise and publishable three-hundred-page volume.
   编辑努力将冗长的手稿削减为一本简洁的、可出版的三百页著作。

3. To improve efficiency, the administration sought to **pare** away unnecessary bureaucratic procedures that had accumulated over the decades.
   为了提高效率，行政部门试图削减几十年来积累的不必要的官僚程序。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pare" as sounding like "pair" — to pare is to cut away until you pair things down to essentials, removing the excess.
把"pare"想成发音类似"pair（双）"——削减到只剩最基本的部分，去除多余。
