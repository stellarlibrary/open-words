# 🥄 dipper

🔊 /ˈdɪpər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A long-handled ladle or scoop used for dipping; a worker who dips objects into liquid for coating or dyeing
**中文：** n. 长柄勺；浸染工；药勺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ladle（长柄勺）
- scoop（勺子）
- spoon（匙）
- bailer（舀水器）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **Big Dipper / water dipper / dip into / a dipper of water**

👉 Example: She used a dipper to transfer soup from the large pot into individual bowls.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The astronomer pointed out the Big **Dipper** constellation, which served as a reliable guide to locating the North Star.
   天文学家指出了北斗七星，它是定位北极星的可靠向导。

2. The factory worker operated as a **dipper**, carefully submerging metal components into the protective coating solution.
   这名工厂工人担任浸染工，小心翼翼地将金属部件浸入保护涂层溶液中。

3. The pharmacist reached for a small **dipper** to measure the precise amount of liquid medicine required for the prescription.
   药剂师拿起一把小药勺，量取处方所需的精确药液用量。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dip-per" — a tool or person that "dips" things, just add "-er" to the action of dipping.
把"dipper"想成"蘸(dip)+者(er)"——用来蘸或舀东西的工具或做浸染工作的人。
