# 🤐 suppress

🔊 /səˈprɛs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To put an end to forcibly; to restrain or hold back; to prevent from being published or known
**中文：** v. 镇压，压制；抑制，忍住

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- repress（压抑）
- stifle（扼杀）
- quash（镇压）
- subdue（制服）
- inhibit（抑制）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suppress a rebellion / suppress evidence / suppress emotions / suppress a cough**

👉 Example: The government attempted to suppress all reports of the incident from reaching the public.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The authoritarian regime used military force to **suppress** the peaceful demonstrations that had spread throughout the capital city.
   该专制政权动用军事力量镇压了蔓延至首都各处的和平示威活动。

2. She struggled to **suppress** her anger during the heated debate, maintaining a calm and professional demeanor throughout the proceedings.
   她在激烈的辩论中努力抑制自己的愤怒，在整个过程中保持冷静和专业的态度。

3. The pharmaceutical company was accused of attempting to **suppress** research findings that revealed dangerous side effects of the medication.
   该制药公司被指控试图压制揭示药物危险副作用的研究结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "suppress" as "sub + press" — to press something down underneath so it cannot rise or be seen.
把"suppress"想成"sub + press"——把某物压到下面，使其无法升起或被看到。
