# 🧪 antidote

🔊 /ˈæntɪdoʊt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** A substance that counteracts the effects of a poison; figuratively, something that counteracts or prevents something unpleasant or harmful.

**中文：** 解毒剂；矫正方法（抵消毒物作用的物质；比喻义为对抗或防止不良事物的方法或手段）。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- remedy 补救措施
- cure 治愈方法
- counteragent 对抗剂
- corrective 矫正措施
- neutralizer 中和剂

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

Common collocations: **an antidote to/for (something)**, **administer an antidote**, **serve as an antidote**, **effective antidote**, **the perfect antidote**. Frequently used in both literal (medical) and figurative senses.

> Example: Humor can serve as a powerful **antidote** to stress and anxiety.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Emergency physicians administered the **antidote** within minutes, successfully neutralizing the effects of the toxic substance.
   急诊医生在数分钟内注射了解毒剂，成功中和了有毒物质的作用。

2. Many scholars regard a strong liberal arts education as the best **antidote** to ignorance and narrow thinking.
   许多学者认为扎实的文科教育是对抗无知和狭隘思维的最佳良方。

3. The comedian's lighthearted performance proved to be the perfect **antidote** to the audience's weary and somber mood.
   这位喜剧演员轻松愉快的表演成为化解观众疲惫和忧郁情绪的最佳良方。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

"Anti-" (against) + "dote" (from Greek *didonai*, to give). An **antidote** is literally "something given against" a poison — a counter-gift that cancels out harm.

"anti-"（对抗）+"dote"（源自希腊语 *didonai*，给予）。**Antidote** 字面意思是"给予用以对抗毒物的东西"——一种抵消伤害的反向馈赠。
