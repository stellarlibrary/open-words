# 😈 lascivious

🔊 /ləˈsɪviəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling or revealing an overt sexual interest or desire; inclined to lustfulness
**中文：** adj. 好色的，淫荡的；挑动情欲的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lewd（淫秽的）
- lustful（好色的）
- licentious（放荡的）
- salacious（猥亵的）
- prurient（好色的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lascivious behavior / lascivious conduct / lascivious intent / lascivious remarks**

👉 Example: The court found that the defendant's lascivious conduct constituted a clear violation of workplace harassment policies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The novel was banned in several countries because critics deemed its content excessively **lascivious** and morally corrupting.
   这部小说在多个国家被禁，因为评论家认为其内容过于淫秽且败坏道德。

2. The committee ruled that the advertisement's **lascivious** imagery was inappropriate for broadcast during family viewing hours.
   委员会裁定该广告中挑动情欲的图像不适合在家庭收视时段播出。

3. Historical records suggest that the emperor's **lascivious** reputation contributed significantly to the eventual decline of public trust.
   历史记录表明，这位皇帝好色的名声在很大程度上导致了公众信任的最终瓦解。

Think of "lascivious" as "la-SCIV-ious" — it contains "sciv" which sounds like "sieve." Imagine morals draining away through a sieve, leaving only lustful desires behind.
把"lascivious"想成含有"sciv"，听起来像"sieve（筛子）"——想象道德通过筛子流走，只留下好色的欲望。
