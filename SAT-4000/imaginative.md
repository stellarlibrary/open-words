# 🎨 imaginative

🔊 /ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing creativity and the ability to form new and original ideas or concepts
**中文：** adj. 富有想象力的，有创造力的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- creative（有创造力的）
- inventive（善于创新的）
- innovative（创新的）
- original（独创的）
- resourceful（足智多谋的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **imaginative approach / imaginative solution / imaginative writing / imaginative mind / highly imaginative**

👉 Example: The architect proposed an imaginative design that incorporated sustainable materials and natural lighting throughout.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The teacher encouraged her students to develop **imaginative** solutions to real-world environmental problems facing their local community.
   老师鼓励学生为当地社区面临的现实环境问题开发富有想象力的解决方案。

2. His **imaginative** use of color and texture in the painting earned him the highest honors at the national art exhibition.
   他在绘画中对色彩和质感的富有想象力的运用为他赢得了全国美术展的最高荣誉。

3. The company sought **imaginative** thinkers who could challenge conventional assumptions and propose entirely new business models.
   公司寻求富有想象力的思考者，能够挑战传统假设并提出全新的商业模式。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "imaginative" as "imagine + ative" — someone who is active at imagining, always creating new ideas in their mind.
把"imaginative"想成"imagine（想象）+ ative（积极的）"——一个积极想象的人，总是在脑海中创造新想法。
