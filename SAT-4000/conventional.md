# 📏 conventional

🔊 /kənˈvɛnʃənl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Based on or in accordance with what is generally done or believed; following traditional forms and practices
**中文：** adj. 符合习俗的，传统的；常见的；惯例的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- traditional（传统的）
- orthodox（正统的）
- customary（惯例的）
- mainstream（主流的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **conventional wisdom / conventional methods / conventional approach / conventional warfare**

👉 Example: The artist deliberately rejected conventional techniques in favor of a more experimental and abstract style.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. **Conventional** wisdom suggests that economic growth requires increased energy consumption, but recent evidence challenges that assumption.
   传统观念认为经济增长需要增加能源消耗，但最近的证据对这一假设提出了质疑。

2. The researcher abandoned **conventional** methods of data collection and developed an innovative algorithm to analyze complex patterns.
   该研究人员放弃了常规的数据收集方法，开发了一种创新算法来分析复杂模式。

3. Many architects have moved beyond **conventional** building designs to incorporate sustainable materials and energy-efficient technologies in their work.
   许多建筑师已经超越了传统的建筑设计，在作品中融入可持续材料和节能技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "convention-al" as "following the convention" — doing things the way everyone has agreed upon at the convention.
把"conventional"想成"following the convention"——按照大家在大会上达成一致的方式做事。
