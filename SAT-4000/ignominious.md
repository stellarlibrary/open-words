# 😖 ignominious

🔊 /ˌɪɡnəˈmɪniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Deserving or causing public disgrace or shame; deeply humiliating
**中文：** adj. 可耻的；不光彩的；丢脸的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- shameful（可耻的）
- disgraceful（不光彩的）
- humiliating（丢脸的）
- inglorious（不名誉的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ignominious defeat / ignominious failure / ignominious end / ignominious retreat / ignominious surrender**

👉 Example: The once-powerful empire met an ignominious end when its last ruler fled the capital under cover of darkness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The team's **ignominious** defeat in the championship final shocked analysts who had confidently predicted an overwhelming and decisive victory.
   该队在冠军决赛中的耻辱性失败震惊了那些曾自信预测他们将压倒性获胜的分析人士。

2. After the **ignominious** collapse of negotiations, the diplomat was recalled to the capital and permanently reassigned to a minor administrative post.
   在谈判不光彩地破裂后，这位外交官被召回首都并被永久调任到一个次要行政职位。

3. The professor suffered an **ignominious** fall from academic grace when investigators uncovered widespread fabrication in her published research.
   当调查人员发现她已发表研究中存在大量数据伪造时，这位教授遭受了学术声誉的耻辱性坠落。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ignominy + ous" — full of ignominy (public shame), like a defeat so bad everyone remembers it for the disgrace.
把"ignominious"想成"ignominy（耻辱）+ ous（充满……的）"——充满耻辱的，像一场让所有人都因丢脸而铭记的失败。
