# 🕵️ stealth

🔊 /stɛlθ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Secrecy; secret action; furtiveness
**中文：** n. 秘密行动

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- secrecy（秘密）
- furtiveness（鬼祟）
- covertness（隐蔽）
- slyness（狡猾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **by stealth / stealth mode / stealth attack / stealth technology**

👉 Example: The operative completed the mission by stealth, avoiding all detection systems.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The intelligence agency relied on **stealth** rather than brute force to infiltrate the foreign network undetected.
   情报机构依靠秘密行动而非蛮力来渗透外国网络而不被察觉。

2. Archaeological thieves removed the artifacts with remarkable **stealth**, leaving no trace of their nighttime intrusion.
   文物盗贼以惊人的隐秘手段取走了文物，没有留下任何夜间入侵的痕迹。

3. The committee advanced its agenda through political **stealth**, quietly securing votes before the opposition could organize resistance.
   该委员会通过政治上的秘密行动推进议程，在反对派组织抵抗之前悄悄获得了选票。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stealth" as "steal + th" — when you steal something, you must act in stealth to avoid getting caught.
把"stealth"想成"steal（偷）+ th"——当你偷东西时，必须秘密行动才能不被发现。
