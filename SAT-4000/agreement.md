# 🤝 agreement

🔊 /əˈɡriː.mənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A mutual arrangement or understanding between two or more parties; the state of being in harmony or accord.
**中文：** 协议；同意，一致

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accord（协议）
- consensus（共识）
- pact（契约）
- contract（合同）
- understanding（谅解）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reach an agreement, come to an agreement, in agreement with, sign an agreement**
👉 Example: Both nations reached an agreement on the terms of the bilateral trade deal.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After months of negotiation, the two corporations finally signed a comprehensive **agreement** on the joint venture.
   经过数月的谈判，两家公司终于就合资企业签署了一份全面的协议。

2. The committee members were in complete **agreement** regarding the proposed amendments to the existing policy.
   委员会成员对现有政策的修正提案完全达成了一致。

3. The international **agreement** on climate change required all signatories to reduce carbon emissions by thirty percent.
   这项关于气候变化的国际协议要求所有签署国将碳排放减少百分之三十。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Agreement" = "agree" + "-ment" — when people agree, they form an agreement, a shared understanding.
"Agreement"="agree"（同意）+"-ment"——当人们达成一致时，就形成了协议。
