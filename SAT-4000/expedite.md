# ⚡ expedite

🔊 /ˈekspɪdaɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb, adjective | 动词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make an action or process happen sooner or be accomplished more quickly; to accelerate. (adj.) Unimpeded; prompt
**中文：** v. 加速；促进 adj. 畅通的；迅速的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- accelerate（加速）
- hasten（催促）
- facilitate（促进）
- quicken（加快）
- speed up（加速）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **expedite the process / expedite delivery / expedite a request / expedite approval**

👉 Example: The manager offered to expedite the approval process so that the project could begin ahead of schedule.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government introduced new legislation designed to **expedite** the review and approval of essential infrastructure projects nationwide.
   政府出台了新立法，旨在加快全国范围内重要基础设施项目的审查和批准。

2. Digital automation tools have significantly helped organizations **expedite** routine administrative tasks that previously consumed substantial time and resources.
   数字自动化工具极大地帮助组织加速了以前消耗大量时间和资源的日常行政任务。

3. The humanitarian organization requested international support to **expedite** the distribution of emergency supplies to the affected communities.
   该人道主义组织请求国际支持，以加快向受灾社区分发紧急物资。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-ped-ite" — "ped" means foot (like pedestrian); to expedite is to free the feet from obstacles so things move faster.
把"expedite"想成"ex-ped-ite"——"ped"意为脚（如pedestrian行人）；expedite就是清除脚下的障碍，让事情进展得更快。
