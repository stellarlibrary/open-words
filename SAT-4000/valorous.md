# 🛡️ valorous

🔊 /ˈvælərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Showing great courage or bravery; heroic, especially in battle or dangerous situations
**中文：** adj. 勇敢的；勇武的；英勇的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- courageous（勇敢的）
- brave（英勇的）
- heroic（英雄的）
- gallant（勇敢的）
- intrepid（无畏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **valorous deeds / valorous effort / valorous warrior / valorous defense / valorous act**

👉 Example: The general praised the valorous soldiers who had held their ground against overwhelming forces.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **valorous** knights of the medieval era were celebrated in epic poems that recounted their daring exploits on the battlefield.
   中世纪英勇的骑士们在叙述他们战场上大胆功绩的史诗中备受赞颂。

2. Her **valorous** decision to speak out against corruption inspired an entire generation of activists to demand institutional reform.
   她勇敢地站出来反对腐败的决定激励了整整一代活动家要求体制改革。

3. The documentary honored the **valorous** contributions of medical workers who risked their lives during the devastating epidemic of the last century.
   这部纪录片向在上世纪毁灭性疫情中冒着生命危险的医务工作者的英勇贡献致敬。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "valorous" as "valor + ous" — full of valor, meaning full of courage and bravery like a legendary warrior.
把"valorous"想成"valor（英勇）+ ous（充满…的）"——充满勇气和英勇，像传奇战士一样。
