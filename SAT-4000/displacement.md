# 📐 displacement

🔊 /dɪsˈpleɪsmənt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of moving something from its place; the distance moved; the replacement of one thing by another
**中文：** n. 位移；取代；置换

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relocation（迁移）
- removal（移除）
- substitution（替代）
- shift（移动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **displacement of people / angular displacement / displacement activity / population displacement**

👉 Example: The displacement of rural populations into urban centers has accelerated dramatically in recent decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The forced **displacement** of civilian populations during the conflict drew widespread condemnation from international human rights organizations.
   冲突期间对平民的强制迁移引起了国际人权组织的广泛谴责。

2. The physicist calculated the total **displacement** of the object by measuring its initial and final positions in the experiment.
   物理学家通过测量实验中物体的初始位置和最终位置来计算总位移。

3. Cultural **displacement** occurs when immigrant communities gradually adopt the customs and language of their new homeland.
   当移民社区逐渐采纳新祖国的习俗和语言时，就会发生文化置换。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "displace-ment" as the noun form of "displace" — the -ment suffix turns the action of moving from a place into a measurable concept.
把"displacement"想成"displace"的名词形式——后缀"-ment"将从位置移开的动作转化为一个可衡量的概念。
