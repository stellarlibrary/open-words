# 😬 unbecoming

🔊 /ˌʌnbɪˈkʌmɪŋ/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Not fitting or appropriate; unsuitable, especially in terms of behavior or appearance
**中文：** 不适当的；不得体的；不相称的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inappropriate（不适当的）
- unseemly（不体面的）
- improper（不合适的）
- unflattering（不雅的）
- indecorous（不合礼仪的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unbecoming behavior / unbecoming conduct / unbecoming of someone**

👉 Example: The committee ruled that such language was unbecoming of a public official.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The senator's outburst during the hearing was deemed **unbecoming** of someone holding such a distinguished public office.
   参议员在听证会上的爆发被认为与担任如此显赫公职的人不相称。

2. Wearing casual attire to the formal ceremony was considered **unbecoming** and disrespectful to the honored guests in attendance.
   在正式典礼上穿着休闲服装被认为是不得体的，且对在场的贵宾不敬。

3. The judge cautioned the attorney that his aggressive questioning style was **unbecoming** of a professional in the courtroom.
   法官警告该律师，他咄咄逼人的询问方式不符合法庭专业人士的身份。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-becoming" — something that is NOT becoming (attractive or appropriate) to you; it does not suit you well.
把"unbecoming"想成"un（不）+ becoming（得体的）"——不得体的，不适合你的行为或外表。
