# 🌐 interpreter

🔊 /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who translates spoken language orally; one who explains or interprets the meaning of something; a program that executes instructions
**中文：** n. 解释者；口译者；注释器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- translator（翻译者）
- commentator（解说者）
- exponent（阐释者）
- mediator（中间人）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sign language interpreter / court interpreter / interpreter of the law / simultaneous interpreter**

👉 Example: The United Nations employs professional interpreters who provide real-time translation during multilateral negotiations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The court appointed a certified **interpreter** to ensure that the defendant fully understood every legal proceeding conducted in English.
   法院指定了一名认证口译员，以确保被告完全理解用英语进行的每一项法律程序。

2. As an **interpreter** of classical literature, the professor offered fresh perspectives on texts that had been studied for centuries.
   作为古典文学的阐释者，这位教授对研究了几个世纪的文本提供了全新的视角。

3. The Python **interpreter** executes code line by line, making it particularly suitable for rapid prototyping and iterative development.
   Python解释器逐行执行代码，使其特别适合快速原型设计和迭代开发。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-PRET-er" — a person who makes things "pretty" clear by explaining or translating between languages or concepts.
把"interpreter"想成"inter-PRET-er"——一个通过在语言或概念之间解释或翻译使事情变得"清晰明了"的人。
