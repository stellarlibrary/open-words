# 🧬 prokaryotic cell

🔊 /ˌproʊkæriˈɒtɪk sɛl/<br>
🏷️ *noun phrase | 名词短语*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A simple, single-celled organism that lacks a membrane-bound nucleus and other complex organelles, such as bacteria and archaea
**中文：** n. [细胞] 原核细胞

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prokaryote（原核生物）
- bacterial cell（细菌细胞）
- simple cell（简单细胞）
- non-nucleated cell（无核膜细胞）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prokaryotic cell structure / prokaryotic cell division / prokaryotic cell wall / compared with eukaryotic cell**

👉 Example: A prokaryotic cell lacks the membrane-bound organelles found in more complex eukaryotic organisms.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Unlike eukaryotic cells, a **prokaryotic cell** does not possess a defined nucleus enclosed by a nuclear membrane.
   与真核细胞不同，原核细胞不具备由核膜包裹的明确细胞核。

2. Biologists study the **prokaryotic cell** to understand the earliest forms of life that emerged on Earth billions of years ago.
   生物学家研究原核细胞，以了解数十亿年前地球上出现的最早生命形式。

3. The **prokaryotic cell** reproduces through binary fission, a simpler process than the mitosis observed in eukaryotic organisms.
   原核细胞通过二分裂进行繁殖，这比真核生物中观察到的有丝分裂过程更为简单。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-karyo-tic" as "before-kernel" — "pro" means before and "karyon" means kernel or nucleus, so it is a cell from before nuclei evolved.
把"prokaryotic"想成"pro（前）+ karyon（核）"——意为"核之前的"，即在细胞核进化之前的细胞。
