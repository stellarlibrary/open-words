# 🛡️ absolve

🔊 /əbˈzɒlv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To declare free from blame, guilt, or responsibility; to formally forgive or pardon
**中文：** v. 免除（责任）；赦免；宣告…无罪

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exonerate（免罪）
- pardon（赦免）
- acquit（宣判无罪）
- forgive（宽恕）
- vindicate（为…平反）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **absolve someone of / absolve someone from / absolve of blame / absolve of responsibility**

👉 Example: The jury's verdict absolved the defendant of all charges brought against him.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The independent investigation **absolved** the officer of any wrongdoing in the controversial arrest incident.
   独立调查免除了该警员在争议逮捕事件中的所有不当行为指控。

2. No amount of charitable work can **absolve** a corporation of its documented environmental violations.
   再多的慈善工作也不能免除一家公司已记录在案的环境违规之责。

3. The committee's final report **absolved** the director from responsibility for the project's catastrophic failure.
   委员会的最终报告免除了该主管对项目灾难性失败的责任。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think "ab-SOLVE" — to absolve is to solve the problem of guilt. Once you are absolved, the issue of blame is solved and removed.
想想"ab-SOLVE"——absolve就是解决（solve）罪责的问题。一旦你被absolve，责问就被解决和消除了。
