# 🔁 rotate

🔊 /ˈroʊteɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To move or turn around a central axis; to alternate or take turns in a recurring sequence
**中文：** v. 旋转

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- revolve（旋转）
- spin（转动）
- turn（转动）
- alternate（交替）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **rotate around / rotate crops / rotate shifts / rotate clockwise**

👉 Example: Farmers rotate their crops each season to maintain soil fertility and prevent nutrient depletion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The Earth continues to **rotate** on its axis once every twenty-four hours, producing the cycle of day and night.
   地球每二十四小时绕其轴自转一次，产生昼夜交替的循环。

2. The museum decided to **rotate** its permanent collection displays quarterly to encourage repeated visits from local patrons.
   博物馆决定每季度轮换其常设收藏展品，以鼓励当地观众反复参观。

3. Hospital administrators chose to **rotate** medical residents through multiple departments to ensure comprehensive clinical training and exposure.
   医院管理者选择让住院医生在多个科室轮转，以确保全面的临床培训和接触。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rota" (a list or roster) + "-te" — people on a rota rotate through different tasks or shifts in sequence.
把"rotate"想成"rota（值班表）+ -te"——值班表上的人按顺序轮流承担不同任务或班次。
