# 💻 technology

🔊 /tɛkˈnɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes; machinery and equipment developed from such knowledge; specialized terminology
**中文：** n. 技术；工艺；术语；科技

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- innovation（创新）
- engineering（工程）
- machinery（机械）
- technique（技巧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **information technology / cutting-edge technology / technology-driven / advances in technology**

👉 Example: Advances in technology have transformed how researchers collect and analyze experimental data.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Modern **technology** has fundamentally altered the way societies communicate, conduct commerce, and disseminate information globally.
   现代技术从根本上改变了社会沟通、商业运作和全球信息传播的方式。

2. The university invested heavily in new **technology** to enhance its research capabilities across multiple scientific disciplines.
   该大学大力投资新技术，以增强其在多个科学学科领域的研究能力。

3. Critics argue that an overreliance on **technology** may diminish critical thinking skills among younger generations of students.
   批评者认为过度依赖技术可能会削弱年轻一代学生的批判性思维能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "techno-logy" as "techno" (skill/craft) + "logy" (study of) — the study and application of practical skills and tools.
把"technology"想成"techno"（技艺）+ "logy"（学科）——对实用技能和工具的研究与应用。
