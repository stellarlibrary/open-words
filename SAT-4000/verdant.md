# 🌿 verdant

🔊 /ˈvɜːrdənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Green with grass or other rich vegetation; (of a person) inexperienced or unsophisticated
**中文：** adj. 青翠的；翠绿的；没有经验的；不老练的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- lush（茂盛的）
- green（翠绿的）
- leafy（多叶的）
- flourishing（繁茂的）
- naive（天真的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **verdant landscape / verdant hills / verdant meadow / verdant valley**

👉 Example: The hikers marveled at the verdant landscape that stretched endlessly across the mountain valley.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After weeks of spring rain, the once barren hillside transformed into a **verdant** expanse of wildflowers and tall grasses.
   经过数周的春雨，曾经荒芜的山坡变成了一片野花和高草的翠绿原野。

2. The **verdant** countryside surrounding the ancient monastery provided a serene retreat for scholars seeking quiet contemplation.
   围绕古老修道院的青翠乡村为寻求安静沉思的学者提供了宁静的隐居之所。

3. Despite his **verdant** inexperience in diplomacy, the young ambassador demonstrated remarkable composure during the tense negotiations.
   尽管这位年轻大使在外交方面经验尚浅，但在紧张的谈判中表现出了非凡的沉着。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "verdant" as related to "vert" (French for green) — verdant landscapes are vividly green and full of life.
把"verdant"联想到"vert"（法语中的绿色）——verdant的风景是生机勃勃、郁郁葱葱的绿色。
