# 🛠️ workmanship

🔊 /ˈwɜːrkmənʃɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The degree of skill with which a product is made or a job is executed; the art or skill of a craftsperson
**中文：** n. 手艺，工艺；技巧；技艺

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- craftsmanship（工艺）
- artistry（技艺）
- handiwork（手工制品）
- skill（技巧）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fine workmanship / exquisite workmanship / quality of workmanship / poor workmanship**

👉 Example: The fine workmanship of the handcrafted violin was evident in every polished curve and precise joint.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum curator praised the exquisite **workmanship** of the ancient bronze vessels unearthed from the archaeological site.
   博物馆馆长赞扬了从考古遗址出土的古代青铜器精湛的工艺。

2. Consumers increasingly value superior **workmanship** over mass-produced convenience when selecting furniture for their homes.
   消费者在为家中挑选家具时，越来越重视卓越的手工技艺而非大批量生产的便利。

3. The contractor was held liable for the defective **workmanship** that caused structural damage to the newly constructed building.
   承包商因低劣的工艺导致新建建筑结构性损坏而被追究责任。

把"workmanship"想成"工匠之船"——承载着工匠技艺和自豪感，航行于他建造的每一个项目之间。
