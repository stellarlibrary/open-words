# ➗ divisible

🔊 /dɪˈvɪzəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being divided, especially with no remainder in mathematical division; able to be separated into parts
**中文：** adj. 可分的；可分割的；可整除的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- separable（可分离的）
- dividable（可分的）
- partible（可分割的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **divisible by / evenly divisible / divisible into / not divisible**

👉 Example: The number twelve is divisible by one, two, three, four, six, and twelve itself.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A prime number is defined as a positive integer greater than one that is **divisible** only by itself and one.
   质数被定义为大于一的正整数，只能被自身和一整除。

2. The professor explained that any even number is always **divisible** by two, regardless of how large it may be.
   教授解释说，任何偶数都可以被二整除，无论它有多大。

3. The inheritance was structured so that the estate would be equally **divisible** among the three surviving beneficiaries.
   遗产的结构安排使其可以在三位尚存的受益人之间平均分配。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "divisible" as "division-able" — something that is able to undergo division cleanly without leftovers.
把"divisible"想成"division(除法)+able(能够)"——能够被整除、不留余数的。
