# 🎯 germane

🔊 /dʒɜːrˈmeɪn/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Closely related or relevant to the subject at hand; fitting and appropriate
**中文：** adj. 有密切关系的；贴切的；恰当的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- relevant（相关的）
- pertinent（切题的）
- applicable（适用的）
- apposite（恰当的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **germane to the discussion / germane to the issue / not germane / particularly germane**

👉 Example: The lawyer argued that the evidence was not germane to the case at hand.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor dismissed the student's question as not **germane** to the topic being examined in the lecture.
   教授认为学生的问题与讲座中正在讨论的主题无关，因而不予回答。

2. Her extensive research in neuroscience proved highly **germane** to the interdisciplinary committee's ongoing investigation.
   她在神经科学方面的广泛研究被证明与跨学科委员会正在进行的调查高度相关。

3. Only arguments that are directly **germane** to the constitutional question will be considered by the appellate court.
   上诉法院只会考虑与宪法问题直接相关的论点。

---

Think of "germane" as "German" — a German engineer is always precise and relevant, never off-topic.
把"germane"想成"German"（德国人）——德国工程师总是精确且切题的，从不跑题。
