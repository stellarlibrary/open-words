# 🔬 assay

🔊 /əˈseɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An analysis or examination, especially of metals or drugs. (v.) To analyze or examine something to determine its composition or quality.
**中文：** n. 化验；试验 v. 分析；化验

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- analyze（分析）
- examine（检验）
- evaluate（评估）
- test（测试）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assay** in scientific or technical contexts, especially when referring to chemical analysis of substances. It is commonly used in mining, pharmacology, and laboratory settings.

👉 Example: The laboratory will assay the ore samples to determine their gold content.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The chemist performed a rigorous **assay** of the mineral sample to determine its precise copper concentration.
   化学家对矿物样本进行了严格的化验，以确定其精确的铜含量。

2. Researchers must **assay** each batch of the experimental drug before it can proceed to clinical trials.
   研究人员必须对每一批实验药物进行化验，然后才能进入临床试验阶段。

3. The independent **assay** revealed that the precious metal content was significantly lower than the seller had claimed.
   独立化验显示，贵金属含量远低于卖方所声称的水平。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Assay" sounds like "essay" — just as you write an essay to examine a topic in depth, you perform an assay to examine a substance in depth.
"assay"听起来像"essay"——就像你写一篇文章来深入研究一个课题一样，你做一次化验来深入分析一种物质。
