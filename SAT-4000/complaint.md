# 😤 complaint

🔊 /kəmˈpleɪnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An expression of dissatisfaction or grievance; a formal accusation; a medical ailment or illness
**中文：** n. 抱怨；诉苦；疾病

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- grievance（不满）
- objection（异议）
- protest（抗议）
- ailment（疾病）
- grumble（抱怨）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **file a complaint / lodge a complaint / complaint about / a common complaint**

👉 Example: The customer filed a formal complaint with the regulatory agency regarding misleading advertising.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hospital received a formal **complaint** from a patient who alleged negligence during a routine surgical procedure.
   医院收到了一名患者的正式投诉，该患者指控在常规手术过程中存在疏忽。

2. A persistent skin **complaint** prompted the researcher to investigate potential environmental toxins present in the local water supply.
   一种持续的皮肤疾病促使研究人员调查当地供水中可能存在的环境毒素。

3. The most common **complaint** among employees was the lack of transparent communication from senior management regarding policy changes.
   员工中最常见的抱怨是高级管理层在政策变更方面缺乏透明的沟通。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "com-PLAINT" as containing "plain" — when you complain, you make your pain plain and clear for everyone to hear.
把"complaint"想成含有"plain"——当你抱怨时，你把你的痛苦明明白白地说出来让所有人听到。
