# 🌈 spectrum

🔊 /ˈspektrəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A band of colors produced by separation of light; the entire range or scope of related qualities, ideas, or activities.
**中文：** n. 谱，光谱，频谱；范围；幅度

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- range（范围）
- gamut（全部范围）
- scope（领域）
- continuum（连续体）
- array（系列）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **across the spectrum / broad spectrum / political spectrum / on the spectrum / spectrum of opinions**

👉 Example: The conference attracted scholars from across the spectrum of social science disciplines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee received feedback from across the political **spectrum**, ranging from conservative to progressive viewpoints.
   委员会收到了来自整个政治光谱的反馈，从保守派到进步派的观点都有。

2. A prism refracts sunlight into the visible **spectrum**, revealing the full range of colors from red to violet.
   棱镜将阳光折射成可见光谱，呈现出从红色到紫色的全部颜色范围。

3. The new antibiotic is effective against a broad **spectrum** of bacterial infections commonly found in hospital settings.
   这种新型抗生素对医院环境中常见的广谱细菌感染有效。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "spectrum" as "spect-rum" where "spect" means to look — a spectrum is what you see when light is spread out for viewing.
把"spectrum"想成"spect（看）+ rum"——光谱就是光被展开后你所看到的画面。
