# 🚫 prohibit

🔊 /prəˈhɪbɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To formally forbid something by law, rule, or authority; to prevent or make impossible
**中文：** v. 阻止；禁止；不准

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- forbid（禁止）
- ban（取缔）
- prevent（阻止）
- proscribe（禁止）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **prohibit someone from doing / strictly prohibit / prohibit the use of / law prohibits**

👉 Example: Federal law prohibits employers from discriminating against applicants on the basis of age.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's code of conduct strictly **prohibits** any form of academic dishonesty, including plagiarism and cheating.
   该大学的行为准则严格禁止任何形式的学术不端行为，包括剽窃和作弊。

2. Environmental regulations **prohibit** the dumping of industrial waste into rivers and other freshwater sources.
   环境法规禁止将工业废物倾倒入河流和其他淡水水源中。

3. The treaty **prohibits** signatory nations from developing or testing nuclear weapons within their territorial boundaries.
   该条约禁止签约国在其领土范围内开发或测试核武器。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pro-hibit" as "pro-inhibit" — to be in favor of inhibiting, which means to strongly stop or forbid something.
把"prohibit"想成"pro-inhibit"——支持抑制，即强烈地阻止或禁止某事。
