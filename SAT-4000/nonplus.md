# 😲 nonplus

🔊 /nɒnˈplʌs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cause someone to be so surprised and confused that they are unsure how to react. (n.) A state of being utterly perplexed or at a complete loss
**中文：** v. 使困惑；使不知所措 n. 迷惑；困惑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bewilder（使迷惑）
- perplex（使困惑）
- baffle（使为难）
- confound（使混乱）
- flummox（使困惑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nonplussed by / completely nonplussed / nonplus someone / at a nonplus**

👉 Example: The unexpected question from the audience completely nonplussed the seasoned speaker during the panel.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was visibly **nonplussed** when a student presented evidence that directly contradicted his published theory.
   当一名学生提出直接反驳他已发表理论的证据时，这位教授明显不知所措。

2. Her calm refusal to negotiate **nonplussed** the opposing attorneys, who had anticipated a much more cooperative response.
   她平静地拒绝谈判让对方律师大感困惑，他们原本预期会得到更合作的回应。

3. The magician's final illusion **nonplussed** even the most skeptical members of the audience, who could not explain the trick.
   魔术师最后的幻术让观众中最持怀疑态度的人也感到困惑不解，他们无法解释这个戏法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "non-plus" as "no more" (from Latin "non plus" meaning "no further") — you are so confused that your mind can go no further.
把"nonplus"想成拉丁语"non plus（不能再多了）"——你困惑到大脑无法再继续思考了。
