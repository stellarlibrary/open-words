# 🌍 widespread

🔊 /ˈwaɪdsprɛd/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Found or distributed over a large area or among many people; prevalent and extensive
**中文：** adj. 普遍的，广泛的；分布广的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- prevalent（流行的）
- pervasive（弥漫的）
- extensive（广泛的）
- ubiquitous（无处不在的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **widespread use / widespread concern / widespread belief / widespread support**

👉 Example: There is widespread agreement among scientists that climate change poses an urgent global threat.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **widespread** adoption of digital technology has fundamentally transformed how educational institutions deliver coursework to students.
   数字技术的广泛采用从根本上改变了教育机构向学生提供课程的方式。

2. **Widespread** public outrage over the environmental disaster prompted the government to enact stricter industrial pollution regulations.
   公众对这场环境灾难的普遍愤怒促使政府颁布了更严格的工业污染法规。

3. Researchers documented **widespread** evidence of cultural exchange between the two ancient civilizations through archaeological artifacts and inscriptions.
   研究人员通过考古文物和铭文记录了两个古代文明之间广泛存在的文化交流证据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "wide" + "spread" — something spread across a wide area is widespread, covering everywhere.
把"widespread"拆成"wide（宽广的）+ spread（传播）"——传播到很广的范围就是widespread，无处不在。
