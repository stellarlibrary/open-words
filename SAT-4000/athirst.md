# 💧 athirst

🔊 /əˈθɜːrst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having a strong desire or craving; eagerly desirous; (archaic) feeling thirsty.
**中文：** 渴望的；热切的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eager（渴望的）
- yearning（向往的）
- avid（热切的）
- desirous（渴望的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **athirst for knowledge/power/glory/adventure/truth**

👉 Example: The young scholar, athirst for wisdom, spent every evening immersed in the university library.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The ambitious explorer was **athirst** for discovery and spent years planning his expedition to uncharted territories.
   这位雄心勃勃的探险家渴望发现新事物，花了数年时间策划前往未知领域的探险。

2. A generation of students, **athirst** for knowledge, filled the lecture halls of the newly founded university.
   一代渴望知识的学生挤满了新建大学的演讲厅。

3. The revolutionaries were **athirst** for justice and refused to yield until their fundamental demands were met.
   革命者渴望正义，在基本诉求得到满足之前拒绝让步。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"ATHIRST" = "A" + "THIRST" — just like being physically thirsty for water, you are mentally thirsting for something you desire.
"ATHIRST"就是"A"加"THIRST（渴）"——就像身体渴望水一样，你在精神上渴望你想要的东西。
