# 💰 levy

🔊 /ˈlɛvi/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A tax or fee imposed by a government; the act of collecting a tax or conscripting troops. (v.) To impose or collect a tax, fee, or fine; to enlist troops for military service
**中文：** n. 征收；征兵，征税；税款 v. 征收（税等）；征集（兵等）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impose（征收）
- tax（税收）
- assess（评估征收）
- exact（强制征收）
- conscript（征召）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **levy a tax / levy a fine / levy troops / impose a levy**

👉 Example: The city council voted to levy a new tax on commercial properties to fund public infrastructure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The federal government plans to **levy** additional tariffs on imported goods to protect domestic manufacturing industries.
   联邦政府计划对进口商品征收额外关税，以保护国内制造业。

2. Parliament authorized the crown to **levy** troops from every county in preparation for the anticipated military campaign.
   议会授权国王从每个郡征集军队，为预期的军事行动做准备。

3. The substantial **levy** imposed on carbon emissions was designed to incentivize companies to adopt cleaner technologies.
   对碳排放征收的巨额税款旨在激励企业采用更清洁的技术。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "levy" as "leave-y" — the government makes you leave your money behind by levying taxes on you.
把"levy"想成"leave（留下）"的变体——政府通过征税让你把钱留下。
