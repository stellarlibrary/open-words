# ⏩ haste

🔊 /heɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) Excessive speed or urgency of movement or action; hurry. (v.) To move or act swiftly; to hurry
**中文：** n. 急速，匆忙 v. 赶快；匆忙

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- urgency（紧迫）
- hurry（匆忙）
- swiftness（迅速）
- rush（急忙）
- precipitation（仓促）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in haste / make haste / undue haste / haste to do something**

👉 Example: The report was compiled in haste and contained several factual errors.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The legislation was drafted in considerable **haste**, resulting in numerous ambiguities that courts later struggled to interpret.
   这项立法起草得相当仓促，导致了许多模糊之处，法院后来难以对其进行解释。

2. Critics argued that the administration acted with undue **haste** in approving the controversial development project.
   批评人士认为，政府在批准这一有争议的开发项目时过于仓促。

3. One should never make important financial decisions in **haste**, as the consequences may prove irreversible and costly.
   重要的财务决定绝不应仓促做出，因为后果可能不可挽回且代价高昂。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "haste makes waste" — the famous proverb reminds you that rushing leads to mistakes and wasted effort.
想想谚语"haste makes waste（欲速则不达）"——匆忙行事会导致错误和浪费。
