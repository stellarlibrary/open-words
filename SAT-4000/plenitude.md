# 🌾 plenitude

🔊 /ˈplɛnɪtjuːd/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An abundance or fullness; a large or sufficient amount of something
**中文：** n. 充分；丰富；大量

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- abundance（丰富）
- profusion（大量）
- plenty（充足）
- wealth（丰富）
- copiousness（充裕）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a plenitude of / in plenitude / plenitude of resources / plenitude of options**

👉 Example: The region enjoys a plenitude of natural resources that sustain its thriving economy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researcher had a **plenitude** of data at her disposal, allowing for a thorough and comprehensive statistical analysis.
   研究人员拥有大量可用数据，使她能够进行全面而详尽的统计分析。

2. The fertile valley offered a **plenitude** of agricultural opportunities that attracted settlers from across the entire continent.
   这片肥沃的山谷提供了丰富的农业机会，吸引了来自整个大陆的定居者。

3. Critics noted that the museum's collection displayed a remarkable **plenitude** of artistic styles spanning several centuries and cultures.
   评论家指出，该博物馆的藏品展现了跨越数个世纪和文化的丰富多样的艺术风格。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "plenitude" as "plenty-tude" — the state of having plenty, an attitude of abundance and fullness.
把"plenitude"想成"plenty-tude"——拥有充足的状态，一种丰富和充实的态度。
