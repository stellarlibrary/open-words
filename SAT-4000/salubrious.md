# 🌿 salubrious

🔊 /səˈluːbriəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Health-giving; pleasant and invigorating; conducive to good health or well-being
**中文：** adj. 有益健康的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- healthful（有益健康的）
- wholesome（有益的）
- invigorating（令人精神焕发的）
- salutary（有益的）
- beneficial（有益的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salubrious climate / salubrious environment / salubrious air / salubrious effect**

👉 Example: The doctor recommended relocating to a more salubrious climate to aid the patient's respiratory recovery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coastal town's **salubrious** climate attracted numerous retirees seeking a healthier environment for their later years.
   这座沿海小镇有益健康的气候吸引了众多寻求更健康晚年生活环境的退休人员。

2. Urban planners argued that creating more green spaces would produce a **salubrious** effect on residents' physical and mental health.
   城市规划者认为，创建更多绿色空间将对居民的身心健康产生有益的影响。

3. The mountain resort was renowned for its **salubrious** air, which physicians in the nineteenth century often prescribed for tuberculosis patients.
   这座山间度假胜地以其有益健康的空气闻名，十九世纪的医生经常向肺结核患者推荐此地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "salu-brious" where "salu" relates to "salute" (health greeting) — a salubrious place is one that salutes your health and makes you feel better.
把"salubrious"想成"salu（像salute，致敬健康）+ brious"——一个salubrious的地方就是向你的健康致敬、让你身体更好的地方。
