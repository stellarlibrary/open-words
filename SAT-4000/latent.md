# 🔍 latent

🔊 /ˈleɪtənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Existing but not yet developed, manifest, or active; hidden or concealed beneath the surface
**中文：** adj. 潜在的，潜伏的，不易察觉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dormant（休眠的）
- hidden（隐藏的）
- concealed（隐蔽的）
- underlying（潜在的）
- quiescent（静止的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **latent talent / latent potential / latent defect / latent heat / latent fingerprint**

👉 Example: The forensic team discovered latent fingerprints on the surface that were invisible to the naked eye.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The therapist helped the patient uncover **latent** anxieties that had been unconsciously influencing her behavioral patterns for years.
   治疗师帮助患者发现了多年来一直无意识地影响其行为模式的潜在焦虑。

2. A skilled educator can identify and cultivate **latent** abilities in students that might otherwise remain entirely undeveloped.
   一位技艺精湛的教育者能够发现并培养学生中可能完全未被开发的潜在能力。

3. The building inspector identified several **latent** structural defects that posed a serious risk to long-term occupant safety.
   建筑检查员发现了几处潜在的结构缺陷，这些缺陷对居住者的长期安全构成了严重风险。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "latent" as "LATE-nt" — like something that will show up "late" because it is hiding beneath the surface, waiting to emerge.
把"latent"想成"late（迟到的）+ nt"——就像某种隐藏在表面之下、等待出现的东西，它总是"迟到"才显现。
