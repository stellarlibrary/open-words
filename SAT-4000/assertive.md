# 🦁 assertive

🔊 /əˈsɜːrtɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing a confident and forceful personality; disposed to bold or confident assertion.
**中文：** adj. 肯定的；独断的；坚定而自信的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- confident（自信的）
- decisive（果断的）
- forceful（有说服力的）
- self-assured（自信的）
- emphatic（强调的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use **assertive** to describe someone who expresses opinions firmly and confidently without being aggressive. It is generally a positive trait. Contrast with "aggressive" (negative) and "passive" (opposite).

👉 Example: An assertive communicator states their needs clearly while respecting others.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new manager adopted a more **assertive** leadership style that earned immediate respect from her experienced colleagues.
   新经理采用了更加坚定自信的领导风格，赢得了资深同事们的即时尊重。

2. Students who are **assertive** in classroom discussions tend to develop stronger critical thinking and communication skills.
   在课堂讨论中表现得坚定自信的学生往往能培养出更强的批判性思维和沟通能力。

3. The diplomat maintained an **assertive** yet diplomatic tone throughout the tense negotiations between the two rival nations.
   在两个对立国家之间紧张的谈判中，这位外交官始终保持着坚定而不失外交风度的语气。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Assertive" = "assert" + "-ive" (adjective suffix meaning "tending to"). An assertive person tends to assert their views — like a lion confidently claiming its territory.
"assertive"="assert"+"-ive"（形容词后缀，意为"倾向于"）。一个assertive的人倾向于坚持自己的观点——就像一头狮子自信地宣示自己的领地。
