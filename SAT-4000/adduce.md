# 📑 adduce

🔊 /əˈdjuːs/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To cite as evidence or to bring forward as an example or reason in discussion or analysis
**中文：** v. 举出（理由）；引证（事实）；提出（证据）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cite（引用）
- invoke（援引）
- present（提出）
- allege（声称）
- reference（引证）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **adduce evidence / adduce reasons / adduce arguments / adduce proof / adduce examples**

👉 Example: The attorney adduced several precedents to bolster her argument before the appellate court.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prosecution **adduced** forensic evidence that conclusively linked the suspect to the scene of the crime.
   控方举出了将嫌疑人与犯罪现场确切联系起来的法医证据。

2. Scholars have **adduced** numerous historical documents to support the theory of early transatlantic contact.
   学者们引证了大量历史文献来支持早期跨大西洋接触的理论。

3. The report **adduces** compelling statistical data to demonstrate the correlation between poverty and poor health.
   该报告举出了令人信服的统计数据来证明贫困与健康不佳之间的相关性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "adduce" as "add-use" — you ADD evidence for USE in your argument. From Latin "adducere" meaning to lead toward, you are leading evidence toward your point.
把"adduce"想成"add-use"——你ADD（添加）证据以供USE（使用）在你的论点中。源自拉丁语"adducere"，意为引导、带向，你是在把证据引向你的观点。
