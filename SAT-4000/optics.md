# 🔬 optics

🔊 /ˈɑːptɪks/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The branch of physics dealing with the behavior and properties of light and its interaction with matter
**中文：** n. 光学

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- photonics（光子学）
- light science（光学科学）
- optical physics（光学物理）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **fiber optics / geometric optics / quantum optics / the optics of a situation**

👉 Example: The professor specialized in optics and published extensively on the refraction of light.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university expanded its **optics** laboratory to accommodate growing research in laser technology and photonic applications.
   该大学扩建了光学实验室，以适应激光技术和光子应用领域日益增长的研究需求。

2. Students enrolled in the **optics** course studied the fundamental principles of reflection, refraction, and diffraction of light.
   选修光学课程的学生学习了光的反射、折射和衍射的基本原理。

3. Modern fiber **optics** have transformed global communications by transmitting data at unprecedented speeds across vast distances.
   现代光纤技术通过以空前的速度在广阔距离间传输数据，改变了全球通信格局。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "optics" as "opt-ics" — the science (-ics) of how we "opt" to see things through light and lenses.
把"optics"想成"opt-ics"——关于我们如何通过光和透镜"选择（opt）"看事物的科学（-ics）。
