# 👁️ heedful

🔊 /ˈhiːdfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Paying careful attention; mindful and watchful of potential dangers or obligations
**中文：** adj. 注意的；深切注意的；留心的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- attentive（专注的）
- mindful（留心的）
- vigilant（警觉的）
- observant（善于观察的）
- cautious（谨慎的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **heedful of warnings / heedful of advice / heedful of danger / heedful of consequences**

👉 Example: A responsible leader must remain heedful of the long-term consequences of every decision.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The experienced mountaineer was always **heedful** of sudden weather changes that could endanger the entire expedition team.
   这位经验丰富的登山者始终留心可能危及整个探险队的突发天气变化。

2. Students who remain **heedful** of their professors' feedback tend to produce significantly stronger academic essays.
   那些留心教授反馈意见的学生往往能写出明显更优秀的学术论文。

3. The committee was **heedful** of public concerns and revised the proposal to address environmental protection issues.
   委员会留心了公众的关切，并修改了提案以解决环境保护问题。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heed" + "ful" — full of heed, meaning you are completely focused on paying attention to something important.
把"heedful"想成"heed（注意）+ ful（充满的）"——充满注意力，表示你全神贯注地关注重要事物。
