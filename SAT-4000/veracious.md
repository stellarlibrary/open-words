# ✅ veracious

🔊 /vəˈreɪʃəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Speaking or representing the truth; truthful, honest, and accurate
**中文：** adj. 诚实的；真实的；说真话的；可靠的；正确的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- truthful（真实的）
- honest（诚实的）
- accurate（准确的）
- candid（坦率的）
- forthright（直率的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **veracious account / veracious witness / veracious report / veracious testimony**

👉 Example: The journalist was known for her veracious reporting that never distorted the facts.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian provided a **veracious** account of the war, relying exclusively on primary sources and verified documents.
   这位历史学家提供了一份真实的战争记述，完全依赖于原始资料和经过核实的文件。

2. The court relied on the **veracious** testimony of the witness, whose credibility had been thoroughly established beforehand.
   法庭依赖了该证人真实的证词，其可信度已事先得到充分确认。

3. A **veracious** scientific report must present data transparently, without omission, manipulation, or selective interpretation of results.
   一份真实的科学报告必须透明地呈现数据，不得遗漏、操纵或选择性解读结果。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "veracious" as "vera-cious" — "vera" means "true" in Latin (as in "verify"), so veracious means truthful.
把"veracious"想成"vera-cious"——"vera"在拉丁语中意为"真实"（如"verify"验证），所以veracious就是诚实真实的。
