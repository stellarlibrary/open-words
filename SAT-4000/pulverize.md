# 💥 pulverize

🔊 /ˈpʌlvəraɪz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To reduce to fine particles by crushing or grinding; to demolish or destroy utterly
**中文：** v. 研磨成粉；粉碎；彻底毁灭

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- crush（压碎）
- grind（研磨）
- demolish（摧毁）
- obliterate（消灭）
- shatter（粉碎）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pulverize into dust / pulverize the opposition / pulverize rocks / finely pulverized**

👉 Example: The industrial machine can pulverize solid rock into a fine powder within minutes.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The pharmaceutical company used specialized equipment to **pulverize** raw minerals into the fine powder required for tablet production.
   该制药公司使用专业设备将原矿物研磨成生产药片所需的细粉。

2. The earthquake **pulverized** several buildings along the fault line, reducing concrete structures to rubble and dust.
   地震将断层线沿线的几栋建筑彻底摧毁，将混凝土结构化为瓦砾和灰尘。

3. The defending champion **pulverized** the competition, winning every event by a margin that stunned the athletic community.
   卫冕冠军彻底击溃了对手，每项赛事的获胜优势之大令体育界震惊。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pulver-ize" — "pulver" comes from Latin "pulvis" meaning dust; to pulverize is to turn something into dust.
把"pulverize"中的"pulver"和拉丁语"pulvis（灰尘）"联系起来——pulverize就是把东西变成灰尘。
