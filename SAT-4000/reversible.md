# 🔄 reversible

🔊 /rɪˈvɜːrsəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Capable of being reversed, undone, or turned inside out. (n.) A fabric or garment finished on both sides so as to be worn with either side out
**中文：** adj. 可逆的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- changeable（可改变的）
- alterable（可变更的）
- undoable（可撤消的）
- restorable（可恢复的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **reversible process / reversible reaction / reversible damage / reversible decision**

👉 Example: The scientists confirmed that the chemical reaction was entirely reversible under controlled conditions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The environmental damage caused by the oil spill may not be **reversible** despite extensive cleanup efforts by authorities.
   石油泄漏造成的环境破坏尽管当局进行了大规模清理，可能仍是不可逆的。

2. Fortunately, the side effects of the medication proved to be **reversible** once the patient discontinued the prescribed treatment.
   幸运的是，一旦患者停止处方治疗，药物的副作用被证明是可逆的。

3. The committee argued that no policy should be adopted unless its consequences are demonstrably **reversible** and clearly understood.
   委员会认为，除非政策的后果可以明确逆转并被充分理解，否则不应采纳任何政策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "reverse" + "-ible" — something that is able to be reversed, like flipping a jacket inside out.
把"reversible"想成"reverse（反转）+ -ible（能够的）"——能够被反转的东西，就像把夹克翻过来穿一样。
