# 🏜️ erosion

🔊 /ɪˈroʊʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The gradual wearing away or destruction of something by natural forces or persistent action
**中文：** n. 侵蚀；腐蚀；磨损

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corrosion（腐蚀）
- deterioration（恶化）
- degradation（退化）
- attrition（磨损）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **soil erosion / coastal erosion / erosion of trust / erosion of rights / prevent erosion**

👉 Example: Deforestation has dramatically accelerated soil erosion in the region over the past two decades.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **erosion** of democratic norms poses a significant threat to the stability and integrity of constitutional governance worldwide.
   民主规范的侵蚀对全球宪政治理的稳定性和完整性构成了重大威胁。

2. Geologists studied the patterns of glacial **erosion** to reconstruct the environmental conditions that prevailed during the last ice age.
   地质学家研究了冰川侵蚀的模式，以重建上一个冰河时期盛行的环境条件。

3. Coastal **erosion** has forced several communities to relocate inland as rising sea levels continue to consume vulnerable shoreline territories.
   海岸侵蚀迫使多个社区向内陆搬迁，因为不断上升的海平面继续吞噬脆弱的海岸线地区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "erosion" as "erode" + "ion" — the noun form of eroding, the slow process of something being eaten away bit by bit.
把"erosion"想成"erode（侵蚀）+ ion（名词后缀）"——侵蚀的名词形式，某物被一点一点蚕食的缓慢过程。
