# 😔 despondent

🔊 /dɪˈspɒndənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) In low spirits from loss of hope or courage; feeling dejected and disheartened
**中文：** adj. 垂头丧气的；沮丧的；意志消沉的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dejected（沮丧的）
- disheartened（灰心的）
- melancholy（忧郁的）
- dispirited（气馁的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel despondent / grow despondent / despondent about / despondent over**

👉 Example: The athlete grew despondent after learning that a severe injury would end his competitive career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The students became increasingly **despondent** as repeated funding cuts threatened to eliminate their graduate research programs entirely.
   随着反复的资金削减威胁到彻底取消他们的研究生研究项目，学生们变得越来越沮丧。

2. Although she felt **despondent** after the initial rejection, she resolved to revise her manuscript and resubmit it.
   尽管在最初被拒绝后她感到沮丧，但她决心修改稿件并重新提交。

3. The **despondent** tone of the ambassador's speech reflected the international community's frustration with the stalled peace negotiations.
   大使讲话中沮丧的语气反映了国际社会对陷入僵局的和平谈判的失望。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-spond-ent" as "de- (down) + respond (respond)" — someone so down that they can no longer respond with any enthusiasm.
把"despondent"想成"de-（向下）+ respond（回应）"——某人如此消沉，以至于无法再以任何热情回应。
