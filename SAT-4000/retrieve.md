# 🔍 retrieve

🔊 /rɪˈtriːv/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To get back or recover something; to restore or bring back to a former condition; to search for and find data in a database
**中文：** v. 检索；恢复；重新得到

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- recover（恢复）
- reclaim（取回）
- regain（重新获得）
- fetch（取回）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **retrieve data / retrieve information / retrieve a file / retrieve from memory**

👉 Example: The software enables users to retrieve archived documents in a matter of seconds.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Investigators worked tirelessly to **retrieve** the critical evidence that had been submerged at the bottom of the lake.
   调查人员不懈努力，从湖底找回了关键证据。

2. Advanced search algorithms allow researchers to **retrieve** relevant academic articles from vast digital databases with remarkable efficiency.
   先进的搜索算法使研究人员能够从庞大的数字数据库中高效检索相关学术文章。

3. The diplomat attempted to **retrieve** the nation's damaged reputation through a series of carefully orchestrated public addresses.
   这位外交官试图通过一系列精心策划的公开演讲来恢复国家受损的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-trieve" like a golden retriever — a dog that goes out and brings things back to you, retrieving what was lost.
把"retrieve"想成金毛寻回犬（retriever）——一只出去把东西叼回来给你的狗，帮你找回丢失的东西。
