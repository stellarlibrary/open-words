# 🛠️ prototype

🔊 /ˈproʊtəˌtaɪp/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An original model or first example from which other forms are developed or copied; a standard or typical example
**中文：** n. 原型；标准；模范

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- model（模型）
- archetype（原型）
- template（模板）
- standard（标准）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **build a prototype / prototype design / prototype model / serve as a prototype**

👉 Example: The engineering team built a working prototype before committing to full-scale production.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The company unveiled a **prototype** of its electric vehicle that demonstrated remarkable advances in battery efficiency and range.
   该公司展示了其电动汽车的原型，展现了在电池效率和续航方面的显著进步。

2. This ancient Greek temple has long served as the **prototype** for neoclassical architecture found throughout Western civilization.
   这座古希腊神庙长期以来一直是西方文明中新古典主义建筑的原型。

3. Engineers tested the **prototype** extensively under extreme conditions before approving the design for commercial manufacturing and distribution.
   工程师们在极端条件下对原型进行了广泛测试，然后才批准该设计进行商业生产和分销。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "proto-type" as "first type" — the very first version of something that sets the pattern for all that follow.
把"prototype"想成"proto（最初的）+ type（类型）"——某事物的第一个版本，为后续所有版本奠定模式。
