# ☀️ ray

🔊 /reɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A narrow beam of light or radiation; a slight indication of something positive; a flat-bodied marine fish. (v.) To emit rays; to radiate
**中文：** n. 射线；光线；鳐形目鱼 v. 放射；显出

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beam（光束）
- shaft（光柱）
- gleam（微光）
- streak（光线）
- radiance（光辉）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ray of light / ray of hope / ray of sunshine / ultraviolet rays / X-ray / cosmic rays**

👉 Example: A single ray of sunlight pierced through the dense clouds and illuminated the valley below.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The physicist demonstrated how a **ray** of light bends when it passes through a glass prism at a specific angle.
   物理学家演示了光线以特定角度穿过玻璃棱镜时如何发生弯折。

2. Her encouraging words provided a **ray** of hope to the discouraged team members who were on the verge of abandoning the project.
   她鼓励的话语为几乎要放弃项目的沮丧团队成员带来了一线希望。

3. Marine biologists observed a large manta **ray** gliding gracefully through the coral reef ecosystem during the deep-sea expedition.
   海洋生物学家在深海考察中观察到一只巨大的蝠鲼优雅地滑过珊瑚礁生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ray" as the sun's reach — each ray is the sun reaching out with a slim finger of light to touch the earth.
把"ray"想成太阳的触及——每一道射线都是太阳伸出的一根细长的光之手指，触摸大地。
