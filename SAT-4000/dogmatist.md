# 🧱 dogmatist

🔊 /ˈdɔːɡmətɪst/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person who asserts opinions in an arrogant and authoritative manner; one who rigidly adheres to doctrine
**中文：** n. 教条主义者；独断家；武断论者

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- doctrinaire（教条主义者）
- zealot（狂热者）
- ideologue（空想家）
- dogmatizer（武断者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a rigid dogmatist / an uncompromising dogmatist / political dogmatist**

👉 Example: The philosopher was dismissed as a mere dogmatist who refused to engage with opposing viewpoints.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The renowned critic described the senator as a **dogmatist** whose inflexible ideology prevented any meaningful legislative compromise.
   这位著名评论家将该参议员描述为一个教条主义者，其僵化的意识形态阻碍了任何有意义的立法妥协。

2. Unlike the open-minded researchers on her team, the lead scientist had become a **dogmatist** resistant to new evidence.
   与团队中思想开明的研究人员不同，这位首席科学家已经变成了一个抗拒新证据的教条主义者。

3. History has shown that a **dogmatist** in a position of authority can cause irreparable damage to intellectual freedom.
   历史表明，一个处于权威地位的武断论者可能对思想自由造成不可挽回的损害。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dogma + ist" — an "-ist" is a person, so a dogmatist is literally a "person of dogma" who clings to rigid beliefs.
想象"dogma + ist"——"-ist"表示人，所以dogmatist就是字面意义上的"教条之人"，紧抱僵化信念不放。
