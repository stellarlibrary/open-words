# 💔 disfigure

🔊 /dɪsˈfɪɡjər/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To spoil the appearance or shape of something; to mar or damage the external form, especially of a person or object
**中文：** v. 损伤外貌，使变丑；毁容

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- deface（损伤外观）
- mar（破坏）
- mutilate（毁伤）
- deform（使变形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disfigure the landscape / disfigure one's face / disfigured by fire / disfigured beyond recognition**

👉 Example: The explosion disfigured the historic building beyond recognition, leaving only charred walls standing.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The acid attack **disfigured** the young woman's face so severely that she required multiple reconstructive surgeries over the following years.
   这次酸液袭击严重损伤了这位年轻女性的面容，以至于她在之后的几年里需要多次进行整形手术。

2. Vandals **disfigured** the centuries-old marble statue in the public square by spraying paint across its intricately carved surface.
   破坏者在公共广场上向那座有数百年历史的大理石雕像精美雕刻的表面喷漆，损毁了它的外观。

3. Urban sprawl and poorly planned industrial zones have **disfigured** what was once a picturesque countryside admired by visitors from abroad.
   城市无序扩张和规划不当的工业区毁损了曾经令外国游客赞赏的风景如画的乡村。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-figure" — to ruin someone's "figure" (外形/容貌). The prefix "dis-" means to undo, so disfigure means to destroy the figure or appearance.
把"disfigure"想成"dis-figure"——毁坏某人的"figure"（外形/容貌）。前缀"dis-"表示破坏，所以disfigure就是破坏外形。
