# 🎬 stunt

🔊 /stʌnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A gimmick or trick; a feat of daring skill. (v.) To hinder the normal growth or development of
**中文：** v. 阻碍…发育，妨碍…生长 n. 发育迟缓；矮小的人

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- feat（绝技）
- trick（把戏）
- hinder（阻碍）
- impede（妨碍）
- retard（延缓）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stunt growth / publicity stunt / pull a stunt / stunt development**

👉 Example: Malnutrition during early childhood can permanently stunt a child's physical and cognitive development.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged drought and nutrient deficiency can severely **stunt** the growth of crops throughout the entire region.
   长期干旱和营养不足会严重阻碍整个地区农作物的生长。

2. The marketing team devised an elaborate publicity **stunt** to generate widespread attention for the new product launch.
   市场团队策划了一场精心设计的宣传噱头，以引起人们对新产品发布的广泛关注。

3. Critics argued that excessive regulation would **stunt** economic innovation and discourage entrepreneurial activity in the sector.
   批评者认为过度监管会阻碍经济创新并抑制该行业的创业活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stunt" as "s-tunt" — something that "stunts" your growth makes you stay short, like a "stunted" tree that never reaches full height.
把"stunt"想成"阻断生长"——就像一棵发育不良的树永远长不到应有的高度。
