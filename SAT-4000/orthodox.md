# 📏 orthodox

🔊 /ˈɔːrθədɑːks/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Conforming to established beliefs, customs, or doctrines; traditional; of or relating to the Eastern Orthodox Church. (n.) A person or thing that is orthodox.
**中文：** adj. 正统的；传统的；惯常的；东正教的 n. 正统的人；正统的事物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- conventional（传统的）
- traditional（惯常的）
- established（公认的）
- mainstream（主流的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **orthodox view / orthodox approach / orthodox belief / orthodox practice**

👉 Example: The professor challenged the orthodox interpretation of the historical event with compelling new evidence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee favored an **orthodox** approach to economic reform rather than adopting untested experimental strategies.
   委员会倾向于采用正统的经济改革方法，而非采纳未经检验的实验性策略。

2. His **orthodox** religious upbringing shaped his worldview and influenced his scholarly research on theology.
   他正统的宗教成长背景塑造了他的世界观，并影响了他在神学方面的学术研究。

3. Many scientists initially dismissed the theory because it deviated so sharply from **orthodox** scientific thinking.
   许多科学家最初否定了这一理论，因为它与正统的科学思想严重偏离。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ortho-" (straight, correct) + "dox" (belief) — orthodox means having the "straight" or correct belief, following the established path.
把"orthodox"拆成"ortho-"（正确的）+"dox"（信仰）——正统就是持有"正确的"信仰，遵循既定的道路。
