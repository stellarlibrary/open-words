# 📚 literacy

🔊 /ˈlɪtərəsi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The ability to read and write; competence or knowledge in a specified area
**中文：** n. 读写能力；识字；有文化；精通（某领域）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- education（教育）
- learning（学识）
- proficiency（精通）
- competence（能力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **literacy rate / digital literacy / financial literacy / literacy program / literacy skills**

👉 Example: The nonprofit organization launched a literacy program to help adults in rural communities learn to read.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Improving **literacy** rates in developing nations remains one of the most critical challenges facing international educational organizations today.
   提高发展中国家的识字率仍然是当今国际教育组织面临的最关键挑战之一。

2. Financial **literacy** courses should be incorporated into secondary school curricula to prepare students for responsible economic decision-making.
   金融素养课程应纳入中学课程，为学生负责任的经济决策做好准备。

3. The government allocated substantial funding to promote digital **literacy** among elderly citizens who lack experience with modern technology.
   政府拨出大量资金，以促进缺乏现代技术经验的老年公民的数字素养。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "liter-acy" as connected to "letters" — literacy is the mastery of letters, the foundation of reading and writing.
把"literacy"联想到"letters（字母）"——识字就是掌握字母，是阅读和写作的基础。
