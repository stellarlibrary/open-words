# 🎭 divers

🔊 /ˈdaɪvərz/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of varying types; several and of different kinds; sundry and miscellaneous
**中文：** adj. 不同种类的；各式各样的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- various（各种各样的）
- sundry（各式各样的）
- assorted（混合的）
- miscellaneous（混杂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **divers reasons / divers means / divers occasions / divers sources**

👉 Example: The scholar consulted divers historical manuscripts before drawing any definitive conclusions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's collection comprises **divers** artifacts gathered from ancient civilizations spanning several continents and historical periods.
   博物馆的藏品包括从横跨多个大洲和历史时期的古文明中收集的各种文物。

2. The committee examined **divers** proposals submitted by international organizations seeking to address the global water shortage crisis.
   委员会审查了国际组织提交的各种旨在解决全球水资源短缺危机的提案。

3. The author drew upon **divers** literary traditions to create a novel that blended Eastern and Western narrative techniques seamlessly.
   作者借鉴了各种文学传统，创作出一部将东西方叙事技巧无缝融合的小说。
