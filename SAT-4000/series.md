# 🔗 series

🔊 /ˈsɪriːz/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A number of things or events of the same kind coming one after another; a set of related programs or publications; an electrical connection in sequence
**中文：** n. 系列

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sequence（序列）
- succession（连续）
- chain（链）
- string（串）
- progression（进展）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a series of / in series / series of events / television series / lecture series**

👉 Example: The university hosted a series of lectures on contemporary political philosophy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A **series** of unprecedented diplomatic failures ultimately led to the collapse of the fragile international peace agreement.
   一系列史无前例的外交失败最终导致了脆弱的国际和平协议的瓦解。

2. The researcher published a **series** of influential papers that fundamentally challenged the prevailing theories in quantum mechanics.
   这位研究者发表了一系列有影响力的论文，从根本上挑战了量子力学中的主流理论。

3. When electrical components are connected in **series**, the same current flows through each element in the circuit sequentially.
   当电气元件以串联方式连接时，相同的电流依次流过电路中的每个元件。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "series" as related to "serial" — things arranged one after another in a row, like episodes in a TV serial.
把"series"想成与"serial"相关——事物一个接一个排列，就像电视连续剧的剧集一样。
