# 🎭 deceitful

🔊 /dɪˈsiːtfəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Guilty of or involving deception; misleading or fraudulent in nature
**中文：** adj. 欺骗的；欺诈的；虚伪的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dishonest（不诚实的）
- deceptive（欺骗性的）
- fraudulent（欺诈的）
- duplicitous（口是心非的）
- treacherous（奸诈的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deceitful behavior / deceitful person / deceitful practices / deceitful intentions**

👉 Example: The deceitful salesman misrepresented the product's features to convince unsuspecting customers to make a purchase.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The investigation revealed that the executive had engaged in **deceitful** accounting practices to conceal massive financial losses from shareholders.
   调查显示，这位高管从事欺诈性会计操作以向股东隐瞒巨额财务亏损。

2. Historians now regard the treaty as a **deceitful** maneuver designed to buy time while the aggressor nation secretly prepared for war.
   历史学家现在认为该条约是一个欺骗性的手段，旨在拖延时间，而侵略国秘密备战。

3. The court ruled that the company's **deceitful** advertising had deliberately misled consumers about the nutritional content of its products.
   法院裁定该公司的虚假广告故意误导了消费者对其产品营养成分的认知。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "deceit-ful" as "full of deceit" — someone who is deceitful is completely filled with dishonesty and trickery.
把"deceitful"想成"充满deceit（欺骗）"——一个deceitful的人满是不诚实和诡计。
