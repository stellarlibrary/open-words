# 🌍 global

🔊 /ˈɡloʊbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to the whole world; comprehensive or all-encompassing in scope
**中文：** adj. 全球的，世界的；总括的，普遍的；球形的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- worldwide（全世界的）
- universal（普遍的）
- international（国际的）
- comprehensive（全面的）
- planetary（全球性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **global warming / global economy / global perspective / on a global scale**

👉 Example: The pandemic demonstrated the need for a coordinated global response to health crises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **global** supply chain disruption affected manufacturers across every continent and raised consumer prices significantly.
   全球供应链中断影响了各大洲的制造商，并大幅推高了消费者价格。

2. Scientists have urged world leaders to address **global** climate change before its consequences become entirely irreversible.
   科学家们敦促世界各国领导人在全球气候变化的后果完全不可逆转之前加以应对。

3. The university adopted a more **global** curriculum to prepare students for careers in an interconnected world.
   该大学采用了更具全球视野的课程体系，以帮助学生为在互联互通的世界中就业做好准备。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "global" as coming from "globe" — a globe is a model of the entire Earth, so global means relating to the whole world.
把"global"想成来自"globe（地球仪）"——地球仪是整个地球的模型，所以global意味着与整个世界相关。
