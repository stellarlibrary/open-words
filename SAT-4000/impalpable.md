# 🌫️ impalpable

🔊 /ɪmˈpælpəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unable to be felt by touch; not easily understood or grasped by the mind
**中文：** adj. 感触不到的；摸不到的；难理解的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- intangible（无形的）
- imperceptible（感觉不到的）
- elusive（难以捉摸的）
- insubstantial（无实体的）
- subtle（微妙的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impalpable quality / impalpable difference / impalpable presence / impalpable distinction**

👉 Example: There was an impalpable tension in the room that everyone sensed but no one could articulate.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher explored the **impalpable** nature of consciousness, a phenomenon that cannot be physically measured or touched.
   这位哲学家探讨了意识的不可触知性质，这种现象无法被物理测量或触摸。

2. An **impalpable** sense of dread pervaded the abandoned building, making even the bravest investigators deeply uncomfortable.
   一种无形的恐惧感弥漫在这栋废弃的建筑中，让最勇敢的调查人员也深感不安。

3. The distinction between the two theories remained **impalpable** to most students despite the professor's thorough and patient explanation.
   尽管教授进行了详尽耐心的讲解，两种理论之间的区别对大多数学生来说仍然难以理解。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-palp-able" — "im" means not, "palp" relates to "palpate" (to feel by touching), so impalpable means unable to be felt or touched.
把"impalpable"拆成"im-palp-able"——"im"表示"不"，"palp"与"palpate（触诊）"有关，所以impalpable就是"摸不到的、感触不到的"。
