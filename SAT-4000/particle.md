# 🔬 particle

🔊 /ˈpɑːrtɪkl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An extremely small piece of matter; a subatomic unit of matter; a minor grammatical word
**中文：** n. 质点，粒子；颗粒；极小量；小品词

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- speck（微粒）
- fragment（碎片）
- grain（颗粒）
- molecule（分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **subatomic particle / particle physics / particle of dust / charged particle**

👉 Example: The physicist studied the behavior of charged particles in a magnetic field.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists at the research facility detected a previously unknown **particle** using the newly upgraded particle accelerator.
   研究设施的科学家们使用新升级的粒子加速器探测到了一种此前未知的粒子。

2. Even the smallest **particle** of contamination can compromise the integrity of a semiconductor manufacturing process.
   即使是最微小的污染颗粒也会损害半导体制造工艺的完整性。

3. The air quality report revealed that harmful **particle** concentrations had exceeded the acceptable threshold in several urban districts.
   空气质量报告显示，几个城区的有害颗粒物浓度已超过可接受的阈值。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "particle" as "part + icle (little)" — a tiny little part of something bigger.
把"particle"想成"part（部分）+ icle（小的）"——某个更大事物中极小的一部分。
