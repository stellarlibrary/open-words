# 🏚️ incommodious

🔊 /ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdiəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Causing inconvenience or discomfort; uncomfortably small or cramped.
**中文：** adj. 不便的；狭窄的；偏促的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cramped（狭窄的）
- uncomfortable（不舒适的）
- inconvenient（不便的）
- confined（局促的）
- restrictive（受限的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **incommodious quarters / incommodious space / incommodious lodgings / incommodious arrangement**

👉 Example: The researchers were assigned incommodious offices that barely accommodated their essential equipment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The graduate students were housed in **incommodious** dormitories that lacked adequate space for both living and academic work.
   研究生们被安排住在狭小的宿舍里，缺乏足够的生活和学习空间。

2. Despite the **incommodious** conditions aboard the vessel, the crew maintained exemplary discipline throughout the lengthy expedition.
   尽管船上条件狭窄不便，船员们在漫长的远征中始终保持着模范的纪律。

3. The architect proposed renovating the **incommodious** lecture hall to create a more functional and comfortable learning environment.
   建筑师提议翻修这个狭小的报告厅，以创造一个更加实用和舒适的学习环境。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-COMMOD-ious" — "commod" relates to "commodious" meaning spacious and comfortable. Adding "in-" negates it: incommodious means NOT spacious, cramped and inconvenient.
把"incommodious"想成"in-COMMOD-ious"——"commod"与"commodious（宽敞舒适的）"有关。加上"in-"就取反了：incommodious意为不宽敞的、狭窄不便的。
