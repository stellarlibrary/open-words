# 💡 clarification

🔊 /ˌklærɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The action of making a statement or situation less confused and more comprehensible; the process of purifying or filtering
**中文：** n. 澄清；说明；净化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- elucidation（阐明）
- explanation（解释）
- illumination（阐释）
- interpretation（解读）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **seek clarification / provide clarification / need clarification / issue a clarification**

👉 Example: The committee requested clarification on several ambiguous provisions within the proposed legislation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The spokesperson issued a formal **clarification** to address widespread misunderstandings that arose from the initial press release.
   发言人发布了一份正式声明，以澄清最初新闻稿引起的广泛误解。

2. Students who require further **clarification** on the assignment guidelines should consult the professor during designated office hours.
   需要进一步了解作业指导细则的学生应在指定的办公时间咨询教授。

3. The court demanded **clarification** of the contractual language before proceeding with the adjudication of the dispute.
   法院要求在继续裁决争端之前澄清合同用语。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clarific-ation" — the act of making something "clear" (clar-) like purifying muddy water until you can see through it.
把"clarification"想成使事物"清晰"（clar-）的过程——就像净化浑水直到你能看透它。
