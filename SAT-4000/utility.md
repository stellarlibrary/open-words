# 🔧 utility

🔊 /juːˈtɪləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The state of being useful or profitable; a public service such as electricity, gas, or water supply
**中文：** 实用；效用；公共设施

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- usefulness（有用性）
- practicality（实用性）
- functionality（功能性）
- service（服务设施）
- benefit（益处）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public utility / utility company / utility bill / of great utility / utility function**

👉 Example: The research demonstrated the utility of the new software in streamlining complex data analysis.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Economists often measure the **utility** of a product by assessing how effectively it satisfies consumer needs and preferences.
   经济学家通常通过评估产品满足消费者需求和偏好的有效程度来衡量其效用。

2. The municipal government invested heavily in upgrading public **utility** infrastructure to ensure reliable water and electricity service.
   市政府大力投资升级公共设施基础设施，以确保可靠的供水和供电服务。

3. Scholars questioned the practical **utility** of the proposed theoretical framework for addressing real-world environmental challenges.
   学者们质疑所提出的理论框架在应对现实环境挑战方面的实际效用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "utility" as "u-TILE-ity" — tiles are useful building materials, and utility is all about usefulness.
把"utility"想成"u-TILE-ity"——瓷砖是有用的建筑材料，而utility就是关于有用性。
