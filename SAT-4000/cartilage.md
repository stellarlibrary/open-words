# 🦴 cartilage

🔊 /ˈkɑːrtɪlɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A firm, flexible connective tissue found in various structures of the body, including the joints, outer ear, and nose; softer and more elastic than bone
**中文：** n. 软骨

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gristle（软骨）
- connective tissue（结缔组织）
- elastic tissue（弹性组织）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cartilage damage / torn cartilage / articular cartilage / cartilage repair / cartilage tissue**

👉 Example: The surgeon confirmed that the athlete had sustained significant cartilage damage in his knee.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orthopedic specialist determined that the persistent knee pain resulted from severely deteriorated **cartilage** between the femur and tibia.
   骨科专家确定持续的膝盖疼痛是由股骨和胫骨之间严重退化的软骨引起的。

2. Researchers have developed an innovative technique for regenerating damaged **cartilage** using stem cells harvested from the patient's own tissue.
   研究人员开发了一种利用从患者自身组织中提取的干细胞再生受损软骨的创新技术。

3. The **cartilage** in a shark's skeleton provides remarkable flexibility, enabling the creature to navigate ocean currents with extraordinary agility.
   鲨鱼骨骼中的软骨提供了非凡的柔韧性，使这种生物能够以惊人的敏捷度在洋流中游动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cart-i-lage" as "cart + age" — as a cart ages, its joints become stiff, just as cartilage in our bodies can wear down with age, causing joint problems.
把"cartilage"想成"cart + age"（车+年龄）——随着车子老化，其关节变得僵硬，就像我们体内的软骨随年龄磨损，导致关节问题。
