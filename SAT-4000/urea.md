# 🧪 urea

🔊 /jʊˈriːə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A colorless crystalline compound that is the main nitrogenous end product of protein metabolism in mammals, excreted in urine
**中文：** n. 尿素

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- carbamide（碳酰胺）
- carbonyl diamide（碳酰二胺）
- diaminomethanal（二氨基甲醛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **urea concentration / urea cycle / urea fertilizer / blood urea nitrogen**

👉 Example: The doctor ordered a blood test to measure the patient's urea levels as an indicator of kidney function.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Elevated **urea** levels in the bloodstream may indicate impaired kidney function and require further diagnostic evaluation.
   血液中尿素水平升高可能表明肾功能受损，需要进一步诊断评估。

2. Agricultural scientists have developed slow-release **urea** fertilizers that minimize nitrogen runoff into surrounding waterways.
   农业科学家开发了缓释尿素肥料，以减少氮素流失到周围水域。

3. The **urea** cycle is a critical metabolic pathway that converts toxic ammonia into a safer compound for excretion.
   尿素循环是一个关键的代谢途径，将有毒的氨转化为更安全的化合物以便排泄。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "urea" as containing "urine" — urea is the key waste compound found in urine, produced by protein breakdown.
把"urea"和"urine"（尿液）联系起来——尿素是尿液中的关键废物化合物，由蛋白质分解产生。
