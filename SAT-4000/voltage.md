# 🔌 voltage

🔊 /ˈvoʊltɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An electromotive force or potential difference expressed in volts; the electrical pressure that drives current through a circuit
**中文：** n. 电压

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- electric potential（电势）
- electromotive force（电动势）
- potential difference（电位差）
- tension（电压）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **high voltage / low voltage / voltage drop / voltage regulator / voltage source**

👉 Example: The power supply maintains a stable voltage to protect sensitive electronic components.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The technician used a multimeter to measure the **voltage** across the resistor and verify the circuit was functioning.
   技术人员使用万用表测量电阻器两端的电压，以验证电路是否正常运行。

2. High **voltage** transmission lines carry electricity over vast distances with minimal energy loss along the way.
   高压输电线路将电力传输到很远的距离，沿途能量损失极小。

3. Fluctuations in **voltage** can damage delicate laboratory equipment, making uninterruptible power supplies essential for research.
   电压波动可能损坏精密的实验室设备，因此不间断电源对研究至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "voltage" as "volt" + "-age" (a quantity of) — it is simply the quantity of electrical force measured in volts.
把"voltage"想成"volt"（伏特）+"-age"（量）——它就是以伏特为单位测量的电力量。
