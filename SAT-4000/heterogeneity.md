# 🎨 heterogeneity

🔊 /ˌhetərədʒəˈniːəti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality or state of being diverse in character or content; lack of uniformity or sameness
**中文：** n. 异质性；不均匀性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diversity（多样性）
- variety（多样）
- dissimilarity（不同）
- variability（可变性）
- nonuniformity（不均匀性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **genetic heterogeneity / cultural heterogeneity / heterogeneity of the sample / degree of heterogeneity**

👉 Example: The heterogeneity of the urban population makes it an ideal setting for cross-cultural research studies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **heterogeneity** of the patient population made it difficult to draw universal conclusions from the clinical trial results.
   患者群体的异质性使得从临床试验结果中得出普遍性结论变得困难。

2. Researchers emphasized that the **heterogeneity** within the dataset required more sophisticated statistical methods for accurate analysis.
   研究人员强调，数据集中的异质性需要更复杂的统计方法来进行准确分析。

3. The cultural **heterogeneity** of modern metropolitan cities presents both opportunities and challenges for urban policy planners.
   现代大都市的文化异质性为城市政策规划者带来了机遇和挑战。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "heterogeneity" as "hetero" (different) + "gene" (kind) + "ity" (state) — the state of being made up of different kinds.
把"heterogeneity"拆成"hetero"（不同的）+"gene"（种类）+"ity"（状态）——由不同种类组成的状态。
