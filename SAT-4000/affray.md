# ⚔️ affray

🔊 /əˈfreɪ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A public fight or disturbance; a noisy quarrel or brawl that disturbs the peace.
**中文：** 滋事；骚乱；争论

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- brawl（斗殴）
- skirmish（小冲突）
- fracas（吵闹）
- commotion（骚动）
- melee（混战）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **public affray, violent affray, charged with affray, involved in an affray**
👉 Example: Several individuals were arrested after the affray outside the stadium.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The police arrived promptly to disperse the **affray** that had erupted outside the downtown courthouse.
   警察迅速赶到，驱散了在市中心法院外爆发的骚乱。

2. Three men were formally charged with **affray** after the violent disturbance at the public gathering.
   三名男子在公共集会上发生暴力骚乱后被正式指控滋事。

3. Historical records describe a fierce **affray** between rival merchant guilds in the town square during the festival.
   历史记录描述了节日期间对立商人行会在城镇广场上的一场激烈冲突。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Affray" sounds like "afraid" — an affray is a public fight that makes bystanders afraid, causing fear and disorder.
"Affray"听起来像"afraid"（害怕）——一场骚乱是让旁人感到害怕的公开打斗，引起恐慌和混乱。
