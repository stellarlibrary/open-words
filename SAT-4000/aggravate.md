# 🔥 aggravate

🔊 /ˈæɡrəveɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To make a problem, injury, or situation worse or more serious; to irritate or annoy someone
**中文：** 加重；使恶化；激怒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- exacerbate（加剧）
- worsen（恶化）
- intensify（加强）
- irritate（激怒）
- provoke（激怒）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aggravate a condition / aggravate the situation / aggravate an injury / aggravate tensions**

👉 Example: His dismissive remarks only served to aggravate the already tense negotiations between the two parties.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The prolonged drought continued to **aggravate** the severe water shortage afflicting rural communities throughout the southern provinces.
   持续的干旱继续加剧了困扰南部各省农村社区的严重水资源短缺。

2. Ignoring the physician's advice and resuming strenuous exercise will certainly **aggravate** your existing knee injury beyond repair.
   忽视医生的建议并恢复剧烈运动肯定会使你现有的膝伤加重到无法修复的程度。

3. The new trade restrictions threatened to **aggravate** diplomatic tensions between the two nations at an already precarious moment.
   新的贸易限制在本已岌岌可危的时刻可能进一步加剧两国之间的外交紧张关系。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ag-GRAVE-ate" — the word "grave" is inside, meaning to make a situation more grave or serious.
想想"ag-GRAVE-ate"——里面有"grave"（严重的），意思是使情况变得更加严重。
