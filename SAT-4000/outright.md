# 🎯 outright

🔊 /ˈaʊtraɪt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, adverb | 形容词, 副词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Complete and total; open and direct. (adv.) Wholly and completely; directly and openly; immediately
**中文：** adj. 完全的，彻底的；直率的 adv. 全部地；立刻地；率直地；痛快地

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- completely（完全地）
- absolute（绝对的）
- total（彻底的）
- forthright（直率的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **outright ban / outright rejection / outright lie / win outright / buy outright**

👉 Example: The proposal was met with outright rejection by every member of the committee.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government imposed an **outright** ban on the import of certain chemicals deemed hazardous to public health.
   政府对被视为危害公共健康的某些化学品实施了全面禁止进口。

2. Rather than leasing the equipment, the university chose to purchase the laboratory instruments **outright** to reduce costs.
   大学选择直接买断实验室仪器而非租赁，以降低成本。

3. The candidate's claim was dismissed as an **outright** fabrication unsupported by any credible evidence or documentation.
   该候选人的说法被认为是彻头彻尾的捏造，没有任何可信证据或文件支持。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "out-right" as "right out in the open" — something outright is completely exposed, with nothing hidden.
把"outright"想成"right out"——完全摆在明面上，毫不隐藏，彻头彻尾。
