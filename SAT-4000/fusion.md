# ⚛️ fusion

🔊 /ˈfjuːʒən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity; the merging of different elements; nuclear fusion, in which atomic nuclei combine to release energy
**中文：** n. 融合；熔化；熔接；核聚变

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- merger（合并）
- amalgamation（混合）
- blend（混合）
- synthesis（综合）
- union（联合）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nuclear fusion / fusion cuisine / fusion of styles / cultural fusion / fusion reaction**

👉 Example: The restaurant is known for its fusion cuisine, blending Asian flavors with French cooking techniques.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Scientists continue to pursue controlled nuclear **fusion** as a potentially limitless and clean source of energy for humanity.
   科学家们继续探索可控核聚变，将其视为人类潜在的无限清洁能源。

2. The album represents a remarkable **fusion** of jazz improvisation and electronic music that captivated audiences worldwide.
   这张专辑代表了爵士即兴演奏与电子音乐的出色融合，吸引了全球听众。

3. The **fusion** of Eastern and Western philosophical traditions has produced many of the most influential intellectual movements.
   东西方哲学传统的融合产生了许多最具影响力的思想运动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fusion" as "fuse + ion" — ions fusing together in a nuclear reaction, just as ideas or cultures can fuse into something new.
把"fusion"想成"fuse + ion"——离子在核反应中熔合在一起，就像思想或文化可以融合成新事物一样。
