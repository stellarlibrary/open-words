# ⭐ stellar

🔊 /ˈstɛlər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to stars; principal; first-rate; outstanding
**中文：** adj. 星的；星形的；主要的；主角的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outstanding（杰出的）
- exceptional（卓越的）
- astral（星的）
- superb（极好的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **stellar performance / stellar reputation / stellar career / stellar achievement**

👉 Example: The young scientist delivered a stellar presentation at the international conference.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university's astronomy department has maintained a **stellar** reputation for groundbreaking research in astrophysics.
   该大学天文学系在天体物理学突破性研究方面一直保持着卓越的声誉。

2. Economists attributed the company's **stellar** growth to its innovative approach to sustainable technology development.
   经济学家将该公司的出色增长归功于其在可持续技术开发方面的创新方法。

3. The diplomat's **stellar** career spanned four decades of service across some of the most volatile regions.
   这位外交官辉煌的职业生涯横跨四十年，足迹遍及一些最动荡的地区。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "stellar" as related to "star" (Latin: stella) — stars shine brightly, so stellar means outstanding and brilliant.
把"stellar"联想到"star（星星）"（拉丁语：stella）——星星闪耀，所以stellar表示杰出和出色。
