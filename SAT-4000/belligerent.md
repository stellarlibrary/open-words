# 💥 belligerent

🔊 /bəˈlɪdʒərənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Hostile and aggressive; engaged in war or conflict. (n.) A nation or person engaged in war or conflict
**中文：** adj. 好战的；好斗的；交战的 n. 交战国；参加斗殴的人或集团

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bellicose（好战的）
- hostile（敌对的）
- aggressive（侵略性的）
- combative（好争斗的）
- pugnacious（好斗的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **belligerent nation / belligerent behavior / belligerent tone / belligerent parties**

👉 Example: The two belligerent nations finally agreed to a ceasefire after months of devastating conflict.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **belligerent** tone of the president's address signaled that diplomatic efforts had reached an impasse.
   总统讲话中好战的语气表明外交努力已陷入僵局。

2. International law imposes specific obligations on **belligerent** parties regarding the treatment of prisoners of war.
   国际法对交战方在战俘待遇方面规定了具体义务。

3. His **belligerent** attitude during the negotiation alienated potential allies and ultimately weakened his bargaining position.
   他在谈判中好斗的态度疏远了潜在的盟友，最终削弱了他的谈判地位。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "belli-" (war, from Latin "bellum") + "-gerent" (carrying) — a belligerent is someone who "carries war" with them.
"Belli-"（战争）+"gerent"（携带）——belligerent就是"携带战争的人"，即好战者。
