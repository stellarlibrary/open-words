# 🗺️ contour

🔊 /ˈkɒntʊər/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The outline or shape of something; a line on a map connecting points of equal elevation. (v.) To shape the outline of something
**中文：** n. 轮廓；等高线；周线 v. 画轮廓；按轮廓塑造

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- outline（轮廓）
- silhouette（剪影）
- profile（侧面轮廓）
- shape（形状）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **contour lines / contour map / the contours of the landscape / facial contours / contour the surface**

👉 Example: The architect carefully studied the contours of the hillside before designing the new structure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The geographer used **contour** lines on the topographic map to determine the steepness of the mountain terrain accurately.
   地理学家利用地形图上的等高线来准确确定山地地形的陡峭程度。

2. The sculptor meticulously shaped every **contour** of the marble figure to capture the natural grace of the human form.
   雕塑家精心雕琢大理石人像的每一处轮廓，以捕捉人体的自然优雅。

3. Satellite imagery revealed the dramatic **contours** of the canyon, which had been carved by millennia of erosion and flooding.
   卫星图像揭示了峡谷引人注目的轮廓，这些轮廓是由数千年的侵蚀和洪水雕刻而成的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "con-tour" as "touring around the outline" — a contour traces the outer boundary of a shape, like taking a tour around it.
把"contour"想成"沿着轮廓巡游"——轮廓线描绘了形状的外部边界，就像沿着它做一次巡游。
