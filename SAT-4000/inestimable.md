# 💎 inestimable

🔊 /ɪnˈestɪməbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Too great or precious to be measured or calculated; invaluable; beyond estimation
**中文：** adj. 无价的；难以估计的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- invaluable（无价的）
- priceless（极其珍贵的）
- immeasurable（不可估量的）
- incalculable（无法计算的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inestimable value / inestimable worth / inestimable contribution / inestimable damage / of inestimable importance**

👉 Example: The ancient manuscripts are of inestimable value to historians studying medieval Europe.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor's mentorship proved to be of **inestimable** value in shaping the careers of countless young researchers.
   这位教授的指导对塑造无数年轻研究人员的职业生涯具有**不可估量**的价值。

2. The destruction of the ancient library resulted in an **inestimable** loss of irreplaceable historical knowledge and artifacts.
   这座古老图书馆的毁坏造成了**无法估量**的不可替代的历史知识和文物损失。

3. Volunteers contribute **inestimable** service to their communities by dedicating time and effort to those most in need.
   志愿者通过将时间和精力奉献给最需要帮助的人，为社区做出了**无价**的贡献。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-estimate-able" as "not able to estimate" — something so valuable that you simply cannot put a price on it.
把"inestimable"拆成"in-estimate-able"，即"无法估计的"——某样东西太珍贵了，根本无法给它定价。
