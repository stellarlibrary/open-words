# 🪑 sedentary

🔊 /ˈsɛdənˌtɛri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Involving much sitting; accustomed to sitting; settled in one place; stationary
**中文：** adj. 久坐不动的；定居一处的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inactive（不活跃的）
- stationary（静止的）
- immobile（不动的）
- settled（定居的）
- sluggish（迟缓的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **sedentary lifestyle / sedentary work / sedentary habits / sedentary occupation**

👉 Example: Medical professionals warn that a sedentary lifestyle significantly increases the risk of cardiovascular disease.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged **sedentary** behavior has been linked to a higher incidence of obesity, diabetes, and chronic cardiovascular conditions.
   长时间久坐不动的行为与肥胖、糖尿病和慢性心血管疾病的高发病率有关。

2. The transition from nomadic hunting to **sedentary** agriculture marked a fundamental turning point in human civilization.
   从游牧狩猎向定居农业的转变标志着人类文明的一个根本转折点。

3. Office workers with **sedentary** occupations are strongly encouraged to take regular breaks and engage in physical activity.
   从事久坐职业的办公室工作人员被强烈建议定期休息并进行体育活动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sed-entary" — "sed" sounds like "sit" (from Latin "sedere," to sit). A sedentary person sits too much.
把"sedentary"想成"sed"听起来像"sit（坐）"——来自拉丁语"sedere（坐）"。久坐不动的人坐得太多了。
