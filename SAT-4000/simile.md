# ✍️ simile

🔊 /ˈsɪməli/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A figure of speech comparing two unlike things using "like" or "as"
**中文：** n. 明喻

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- comparison（比较）
- analogy（类比）
- likeness（相似）
- metaphor（隐喻）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **use a simile / draw a simile / extended simile / epic simile / simile comparing X to Y**

👉 Example: The poet employed a vivid simile, comparing the dancer's movements to leaves swirling in an autumn breeze.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The author crafted a striking **simile** comparing the politician's rhetoric to a storm that destroys everything in its path.
   作者构造了一个生动的明喻，将政客的言辞比作一场摧毁一切的风暴。

2. Students were asked to identify each **simile** in the passage and explain how it enhanced the overall literary meaning.
   学生们被要求找出文章中的每个明喻，并解释它如何增强整体文学意义。

3. The phrase "as cold as ice" is a commonly used **simile** that conveys emotional detachment or extreme low temperature.
   "冷若冰霜"是一个常用的明喻，用来传达情感上的冷漠或极低的温度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "simile" as "similar-e" — a simile shows how two things are similar by using "like" or "as" to draw a direct comparison.
把"simile"想成"similar（相似的）+ e"——明喻通过使用"像"或"如同"来展示两个事物的相似之处。
