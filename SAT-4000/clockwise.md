# 🕐 clockwise

🔊 /ˈklɒkwaɪz/<br>
🏷️ *adverb, adjective | 副词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adv.) In the direction in which the hands of a clock move, from top to right to bottom to left. (adj.) Moving or directed in the same rotational direction as clock hands
**中文：** adv. 顺时针方向地 adj. 顺时针方向的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dextrorotatory（右旋的）
- right-handed rotation（右旋）
- sunwise（顺日行方向的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **turn clockwise / rotate clockwise / in a clockwise direction / clockwise motion / clockwise rotation**

👉 Example: Tighten the bolt by turning the wrench in a clockwise direction until it is secure.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The instructor asked the students to rotate the dial **clockwise** until it reached the designated calibration point.
   教师要求学生们顺时针旋转刻度盘，直到达到指定的校准点。

2. Tropical cyclones in the Southern Hemisphere characteristically spin in a **clockwise** direction due to the Coriolis effect.
   由于科里奥利效应，南半球的热带气旋通常以顺时针方向旋转。

3. The assembly manual specified that each screw must be fastened by turning it **clockwise** with moderate pressure.
   装配手册规定每颗螺丝必须以适度压力顺时针旋转来固定。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clockwise" literally: it is the "wise" way of the "clock" — the direction the clock hands naturally travel, from twelve to three to six to nine.
直接从字面理解"clockwise"："clock（时钟）"的"wise（方式）"——即时钟指针自然行进的方向，从十二点到三点到六点到九点。
