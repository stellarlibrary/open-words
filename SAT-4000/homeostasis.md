# ⚖️ homeostasis

🔊 /ˌhoʊmiəˈsteɪsɪs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The tendency of a biological system to maintain relatively stable internal conditions despite external changes
**中文：** n. 体内平衡；内稳态

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- equilibrium（平衡）
- balance（平衡）
- stability（稳定性）
- self-regulation（自我调节）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **maintain homeostasis / disrupt homeostasis / achieve homeostasis / homeostasis of the body**

👉 Example: The human body relies on complex feedback mechanisms to maintain homeostasis at all times.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The endocrine system plays a critical role in maintaining **homeostasis** by regulating hormone levels throughout the entire body.
   内分泌系统通过调节全身的激素水平，在维持体内平衡方面发挥着关键作用。

2. Prolonged exposure to extreme temperatures can severely disrupt the body's **homeostasis** and lead to organ failure.
   长期暴露在极端温度下会严重破坏身体的内稳态，并导致器官衰竭。

3. Ecologists study how natural ecosystems achieve **homeostasis** through intricate networks of interdependent species and resources.
   生态学家研究自然生态系统如何通过复杂的相互依存的物种和资源网络来实现平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "homeostasis" as "homeo(same) + stasis(standing still)" — the body stays the same by standing still in balance.
把"homeostasis"想成"homeo（相同）+ stasis（静止）"——身体通过保持平衡不变来维持内部环境的稳定。
