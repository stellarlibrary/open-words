# 🕰️ chronology

🔊 /krəˈnɒlədʒi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The arrangement of events or dates in the order of their occurrence; the study of historical records to determine the dates of past events
**中文：** n. 年表；年代学；年代顺序

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- timeline（时间线）
- timetable（时间表）
- sequence（顺序）
- annals（编年史）
- dating（年代测定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **establish a chronology / chronology of events / detailed chronology / historical chronology**

👉 Example: The detective established a precise chronology of events leading up to the night of the incident.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The historian compiled a detailed **chronology** of the empire's rise and decline spanning more than five centuries of rule.
   历史学家编纂了一份详细的帝国兴衰**年表**，涵盖了五个多世纪的统治时期。

2. Advances in radiocarbon dating have significantly improved the accuracy of the **chronology** established for ancient civilizations.
   放射性碳定年技术的进步显著提高了为古代文明所确立的**年代学**的准确性。

3. The investigation required assembling a comprehensive **chronology** to determine the precise sequence of decisions that led to the failure.
   调查需要编制一份全面的**年代顺序**表，以确定导致失败的决策的精确先后次序。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "chronology" as "chrono + logy" — "chrono" means time and "logy" means study, so chronology is literally the study of time and the ordering of events.
把"chronology"想成"chrono + logy"——"chrono"意为时间，"logy"意为学科，因此chronology字面意思是对时间和事件排列的研究。
