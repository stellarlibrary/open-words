# ✨ refulgent

🔊 /rɪˈfʌldʒənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Shining brightly; radiant; gleaming with brilliant light or splendor.
**中文：** adj. 辉煌的；灿烂的；光芒四射的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- radiant（光辉的）
- resplendent（辉煌的）
- luminous（发光的）
- brilliant（灿烂的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **refulgent light / refulgent beauty / refulgent glow / refulgent splendor**

👉 Example: The refulgent sunrise cast golden hues across the entire mountain valley.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **refulgent** chandelier illuminated the grand ballroom, casting prismatic reflections across the marble walls and ceiling.
   辉煌的枝形吊灯照亮了宏伟的舞厅，在大理石墙壁和天花板上投射出棱镜般的光芒。

2. Critics praised the author's **refulgent** prose style, which brought vivid imagery and emotional depth to every passage.
   评论家赞扬了作者灿烂的散文风格，为每个段落带来了生动的意象和情感深度。

3. The queen appeared in a **refulgent** gown adorned with hundreds of diamonds that sparkled under the palace lights.
   女王身穿一件镶有数百颗钻石的华丽礼服出现，在宫殿灯光下闪闪发光。
