# 🔹 insignificant

🔊 /ˌɪnsɪɡˈnɪfɪkənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Too small or unimportant to be worth consideration; having little or no meaning or value
**中文：** adj. 无关紧要的；可忽略的；无意义的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- trivial（琐碎的）
- negligible（可忽略的）
- inconsequential（无关紧要的）
- trifling（微不足道的）
- petty（不重要的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **statistically insignificant / seemingly insignificant / insignificant detail / insignificant amount**

👉 Example: What appeared to be an insignificant detail turned out to be the key to solving the mystery.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The difference between the two experimental groups was statistically **insignificant**, leading researchers to reject the initial hypothesis.
   两个实验组之间的差异在统计上不显著，导致研究人员否定了最初的假设。

2. Many historians argue that seemingly **insignificant** events in the early twentieth century ultimately triggered profound geopolitical transformations.
   许多历史学家认为，二十世纪初看似无关紧要的事件最终引发了深刻的地缘政治变革。

3. The company dismissed the complaint as **insignificant**, but it later escalated into a major public relations crisis.
   公司将这一投诉视为无关紧要，但它后来升级为一场重大的公共关系危机。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in + significant" — the prefix "in-" negates "significant," so insignificant literally means not significant, not important enough to matter.
把"insignificant"想成"in + significant"——前缀"in-"否定了"significant"，所以字面意思就是不重要的、无关紧要的。
