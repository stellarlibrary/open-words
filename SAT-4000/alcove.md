# 🏛️ alcove

🔊 /ˈælkoʊv/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A recessed section of a room or a small, sheltered area set back from a larger space; a niche in a wall
**中文：** 凹室；壁龛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- niche（壁龛）
- recess（凹处）
- nook（角落）
- bay（凹窗）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a quiet alcove / window alcove / an alcove in the wall / reading alcove**
👉 Example: She discovered a peaceful alcove in the library where she could study without distraction.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The architect incorporated a charming **alcove** into the living room design, creating an intimate reading space by the window.
   建筑师在客厅设计中融入了一个迷人的凹室，在窗边营造了一个温馨的阅读空间。

2. A small marble statue was placed within the **alcove** of the cathedral wall, illuminated by soft candlelight.
   一尊小型大理石雕像被放置在大教堂墙壁的壁龛内，柔和的烛光将其照亮。

3. The restaurant featured several private **alcoves** where guests could enjoy their meals in seclusion and comfort.
   这家餐厅设有几个私密的凹室，客人可以在其中舒适且不受打扰地用餐。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "al-cove" as "a cove" — just as a cove is a sheltered inlet in a coastline, an alcove is a sheltered recess in a wall.
把"alcove"想成"a cove"——正如海湾是海岸线上的一个隐蔽入口，凹室是墙壁上的一个隐蔽凹处。
