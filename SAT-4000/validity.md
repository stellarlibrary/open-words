# 📐 validity

🔊 /vəˈlɪdɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The quality of being logically sound, well-grounded, or legally acceptable; the state of being valid
**中文：** n. 有效性；正确性；合法性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- soundness（合理性）
- legitimacy（合法性）
- cogency（说服力）
- authenticity（真实性）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **validity of an argument / question the validity / validity period / internal validity / external validity**

👉 Example: Scholars have long debated the validity of using standardized tests to measure intelligence.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The defense attorney challenged the **validity** of the evidence presented, arguing that it had been obtained through unlawful search procedures.
   辩护律师质疑所提交证据的有效性，辩称证据是通过非法搜查程序获得的。

2. Researchers must carefully assess the **validity** of their experimental design to ensure that conclusions accurately reflect the phenomena under study.
   研究人员必须仔细评估其实验设计的有效性，以确保结论准确反映所研究的现象。

3. The **validity** of the ancient manuscript was confirmed through carbon dating and meticulous analysis of the handwriting style.
   这份古代手稿的真实性通过碳年代测定和对笔迹风格的细致分析得到了确认。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "validity" as "valid + ity" — the quality or state of being valid, like how something passes the test of truth.
把"validity"想成"valid（有效的）+ ity（性质）"——即有效的状态或性质，就像某事通过了真实性的检验。
