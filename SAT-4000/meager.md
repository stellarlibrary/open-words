# 📉 meager

🔊 /ˈmiːɡər/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Lacking in quantity or quality; inadequate; thin or lean in body
**中文：** adj. 贫乏的；不足的；瘦弱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- scanty（不足的）
- sparse（稀疏的）
- paltry（微不足道的）
- insufficient（不充分的）
- scant（缺乏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **meager income / meager resources / meager rations / meager supply**

👉 Example: The refugees survived on meager rations distributed by the humanitarian aid organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Despite their **meager** resources, the research team managed to produce findings that significantly advanced the field of study.
   尽管资源匮乏，研究团队仍设法取得了显著推动该领域发展的研究成果。

2. The workers' **meager** wages were barely sufficient to cover basic necessities such as food, shelter, and clothing.
   工人们微薄的工资几乎不够支付食物、住所和衣物等基本生活需求。

3. The harvest yielded only a **meager** crop that season, forcing the community to seek external assistance for survival.
   那个季节的收成极为贫乏，迫使社区寻求外部援助以维持生存。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "meager" as "me + eager" — I am eager for more because what I have is so little and insufficient.
把"meager"想成"me + eager"——我渴望更多，因为我拥有的太少、太不够了。
