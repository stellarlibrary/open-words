# 💀 cadaverous

🔊 /kəˈdævərəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Resembling a corpse in being very pale, thin, or gaunt; having a deathly appearance
**中文：** 像尸体的；惨白的；憔悴的；骨瘦如柴的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- gaunt（憔悴的）
- emaciated（消瘦的）
- pallid（苍白的）
- haggard（形容枯槁的）
- ghastly（惨白的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cadaverous face / cadaverous appearance / cadaverous complexion / cadaverous figure**

👉 Example: The prisoner's cadaverous appearance shocked the visiting officials who had not seen him in months.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After weeks of severe illness, the patient presented a **cadaverous** complexion that alarmed the attending physicians deeply.
   经过数周严重疾病后，患者呈现出惨白的面色，令主治医师深感震惊。

2. The **cadaverous** figure emerging from the abandoned building appeared more like a specter than a living person.
   从废弃建筑中走出的骨瘦如柴的身影看起来更像幽灵而非活人。

3. His **cadaverous** features and hollow eyes revealed the devastating toll that prolonged famine had exacted upon him.
   他憔悴的面容和凹陷的双眼揭示了长期饥荒对他造成的毁灭性影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cadaver" (corpse) + "-ous" (like) — cadaverous literally means "corpse-like," pale and skeletal.
把"cadaverous"拆成"cadaver（尸体）+ -ous（像……的）"——字面意思就是"像尸体一样的"，苍白而骨瘦如柴。
