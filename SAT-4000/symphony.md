# 🎵 symphony

🔊 /ˈsɪmfəni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An elaborate musical composition for a full orchestra; a harmonious combination of sounds
**中文：** n. 交响乐，交响曲

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concerto（协奏曲）
- orchestral work（管弦乐作品）
- harmony（和声）
- composition（乐曲）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **symphony orchestra / symphony hall / perform a symphony / compose a symphony / a symphony of**

👉 Example: Beethoven's Ninth Symphony is widely regarded as one of the greatest musical achievements in history.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The conductor led the **symphony** orchestra through a flawless rendition of the composer's most demanding and celebrated work.
   指挥家带领交响乐团完美地演绎了这位作曲家最具挑战性和最负盛名的作品。

2. The autumn forest presented a **symphony** of colors, with golden leaves rustling in harmony against the crisp afternoon breeze.
   秋天的森林呈现出一幅色彩的交响曲，金色的树叶在清爽的午后微风中和谐地沙沙作响。

3. The university's music department will premiere a new **symphony** composed entirely by students during the annual spring concert.
   该大学音乐系将在年度春季音乐会上首演一部完全由学生创作的新交响曲。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "sym-phony" as "same-sound" — many instruments playing together in the same harmonious sound create a symphony.
把"symphony"想成"same-sound"——许多乐器一起演奏出相同的和谐声音，便构成了交响乐。
