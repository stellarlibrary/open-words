# 🌀 assimilate

🔊 /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To absorb and integrate information, ideas, or culture; to cause a person or group to become part of a larger group
**中文：** 吸收；使同化

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- absorb（吸收）
- integrate（融合）
- incorporate（纳入）
- acculturate（使文化同化）
- digest（消化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **assimilate into / assimilate knowledge / assimilate information / assimilate a culture**

👉 Example: Immigrants often face challenges as they assimilate into a new cultural environment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Students who actively engage in discussions can **assimilate** complex theoretical concepts far more effectively than passive learners.
   积极参与讨论的学生比被动学习者能更有效地吸收复杂的理论概念。

2. The newly arrived families worked diligently to **assimilate** into the local community while preserving their cultural heritage.
   新来的家庭努力融入当地社区，同时保留他们的文化遗产。

3. The body requires adequate time to **assimilate** essential nutrients from food before they enter the bloodstream.
   身体需要足够的时间从食物中吸收必需的营养成分，然后才能进入血液。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "assimilate" as "a-SIMILAR-ate" — to make similar, to absorb something until it becomes part of the whole.
把"assimilate"想成"使之相似"——吸收某物直到它成为整体的一部分，就像把不同的东西变得"similar（相似）"。
