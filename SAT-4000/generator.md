# ⚡ generator

🔊 /ˈdʒɛnəreɪtər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A machine or device that converts mechanical energy into electrical energy; a person or thing that generates or produces something
**中文：** n. 发电机；发生器；生成器

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- dynamo（发电机）
- alternator（交流发电机）
- power source（电源）
- producer（产生者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **electric generator / backup generator / power generator / generator capacity / portable generator**

👉 Example: The hospital relies on a backup generator to maintain critical operations during unexpected power outages.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The installation of a new wind-powered **generator** enabled the remote village to access reliable electricity for the first time.
   安装新的风力发电机使这个偏远村庄首次获得了可靠的电力供应。

2. During the hurricane, the emergency **generator** provided essential power to the shelter, keeping medical equipment fully operational.
   飓风期间，应急发电机为避难所提供了必要的电力，使医疗设备保持正常运转。

3. The engineer designed an innovative **generator** that converts tidal energy into electricity with significantly greater efficiency than previous models.
   这位工程师设计了一种创新型发电机，能以比以往型号明显更高的效率将潮汐能转化为电能。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "generator" as "generate + or" — the "-or" suffix means "one who does," so a generator is literally "one that generates" power or energy.
把"generator"想成"generate + or"——"-or"后缀意为"做……的人/物"，所以generator就是"产生电力或能量的东西"。
