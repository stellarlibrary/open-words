# 🌟 repute

🔊 /rɪˈpjuːt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The opinion generally held of someone or something; reputation or fame. (v.) To consider or regard as being; to be generally known or thought of as
**中文：** n. 名誉；名声；声望 v. 认为；把……称为

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reputation（名声）
- renown（声誉）
- standing（地位）
- esteem（尊重）
- regard（评价）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **of good repute / of ill repute / be reputed to / reputed expert**

👉 Example: The establishment is of high repute among scholars and visiting researchers from around the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university has long been held in high **repute** for its rigorous academic programs and distinguished faculty members.
   这所大学长期以来因其严谨的学术课程和杰出的教职人员而享有盛誉。

2. The ancient manuscript is **reputed** to contain formulas and techniques that were centuries ahead of their documented historical period.
   据称这份古代手稿包含比其有记载的历史时期早数百年的公式和技术。

3. A neighborhood of ill **repute** may deter prospective investors regardless of the actual economic potential the area possesses.
   一个名声不佳的社区可能会阻止潜在投资者，无论该地区实际拥有多大的经济潜力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "repute" as "re-put" — how people repeatedly put you in a certain category, shaping your public reputation over time.
把"repute"想成"re-put"——人们反复把你归入某个类别，随着时间推移形成你的公众声誉。
