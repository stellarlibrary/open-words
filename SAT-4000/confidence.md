# 💪 confidence

🔊 /ˈkɒnfɪdəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义

**EN:** The feeling or belief that one can rely on someone or something; a state of self-assurance arising from an appreciation of one's own abilities; a secret or private matter told to someone in trust.

**中文：** 信心；自信；信任；秘密。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词

- assurance（保证；信心）
- self-assurance（自信）
- trust（信任）
- conviction（确信）
- certainty（确定）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示

**Collocation:** have confidence in / with confidence / in confidence (secretly)

**Example:** The investors expressed strong **confidence** in the company's long-term strategic growth plan.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Years of rigorous academic training gave her the **confidence** to present her research before distinguished scholars.
   多年严格的学术训练给了她在杰出学者面前展示研究成果的信心。

2. The manager spoke in **confidence** when revealing the forthcoming organizational restructuring plans to senior staff.
   经理在向高级员工透露即将到来的组织重组计划时是秘密地说的。

3. Public **confidence** in the financial system was gradually restored after the government implemented comprehensive regulatory reforms.
   政府实施全面监管改革后，公众对金融体系的信心逐渐恢复。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧

**English:** "Con-" (with) + "fid" (faith, from Latin fidere) + "-ence" — having faith with or in something, trusting fully.

**中文：** "con-"（一起）+ "fid"（信念，源自拉丁语 fidere）+ "-ence"——对某事怀有信念，完全信任，即"信心、信任"。
