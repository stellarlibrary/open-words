# 💓 cardiac

🔊 /ˈkɑːrdiæk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to the heart. (n.) A medicine or drug that stimulates the heart
**中文：** n. 强心剂 adj. 心脏的；心脏病的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- cardiovascular（心血管的）
- coronary（冠状动脉的）
- heart-related（与心脏相关的）
- cardial（心脏的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **cardiac arrest / cardiac surgery / cardiac muscle / cardiac patient / cardiac care unit**

👉 Example: The patient was rushed to the cardiac care unit after experiencing severe chest pains.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Advances in **cardiac** surgery have dramatically improved survival rates for patients with congenital heart defects and coronary disease.
   心脏外科的进步大大提高了先天性心脏缺陷和冠心病患者的存活率。

2. The physician ordered a comprehensive **cardiac** evaluation to assess the patient's risk of future cardiovascular complications.
   医生要求进行全面的心脏评估，以评估患者未来心血管并发症的风险。

3. Regular aerobic exercise strengthens **cardiac** muscle and significantly reduces the likelihood of developing chronic heart conditions.
   定期有氧运动能增强心肌，并显著降低患慢性心脏病的可能性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cardiac" as coming from Greek "kardia" meaning heart — the root "cardi-" appears in many medical terms related to the heart, like cardiologist.
把"cardiac"想成来自希腊语"kardia"（心脏）——词根"cardi-"出现在许多与心脏相关的医学术语中，如cardiologist（心脏病学家）。
