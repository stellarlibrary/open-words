# 🏗️ renovate

🔊 /ˈrenəveɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To restore to good condition by repairing or remodeling; to refresh or revive
**中文：** v. 更新；修复；革新；刷新

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restore（修复）
- refurbish（翻新）
- remodel（改建）
- modernize（现代化）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **renovate a building / renovate a house / completely renovated / renovate the interior**

👉 Example: The university plans to renovate the century-old library to accommodate modern research facilities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The city council allocated substantial funds to **renovate** the historic theater and restore it to its original grandeur.
   市议会拨出大量资金来翻新这座历史悠久的剧院，恢复其原有的宏伟面貌。

2. The new owners decided to completely **renovate** the dilapidated mansion rather than demolish it and build anew.
   新业主决定彻底翻新这座破旧的豪宅，而不是拆除重建。

3. Several nonprofit organizations are working together to **renovate** abandoned properties and convert them into affordable housing.
   几家非营利组织正在合作翻新废弃房产，将其改造为经济适用住房。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "re-NOV-ate" as "re-NOVA-te" — "nova" means new, so renovate means to make something new again.
把"renovate"想成"re-NOVA-te"——"nova"意为新的，所以renovate就是重新翻新的意思。
