# 🚧 interference

🔊 /ˌɪntərˈfɪrəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The act of interfering or meddling in the affairs of others; obstruction or disruption of a process or signal
**中文：** n. 干扰，冲突；干涉

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- obstruction（阻碍）
- meddling（干预）
- intervention（介入）
- disruption（扰乱）
- intrusion（侵扰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **interference with / interference in / interference from / run interference**

👉 Example: The committee condemned political interference in the judicial process.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The constant **interference** from external radio signals severely disrupted the laboratory's sensitive measurement equipment.
   来自外部无线电信号的持续干扰严重破坏了实验室的精密测量设备。

2. Many citizens protested against government **interference** in the operations of independent media organizations across the country.
   许多公民抗议政府干涉全国各地独立媒体机构的运营。

3. The researcher discovered that electromagnetic **interference** had compromised the accuracy of the experimental data collected over several months.
   研究人员发现电磁干扰影响了数月来所收集实验数据的准确性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "inter-fear-ence" — when someone interferes, they create fear and disruption by getting in the way of your work.
把"interference"想成"inter-fear-ence"——当有人干涉时，他们通过妨碍你的工作制造恐惧和混乱。
