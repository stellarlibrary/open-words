# 🐜 infest

🔊 /ɪnˈfɛst/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To overrun or occupy in large numbers, especially so as to cause damage or disease; to be parasitic in or on
**中文：** v. 骚扰；寄生于；大批出没；大批滋生

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- overrun（泛滥）
- plague（困扰）
- swarm（蜂拥）
- invade（侵入）
- beset（困扰）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **infested with / infest an area / rats infest / termites infest**

👉 Example: The abandoned warehouse was infested with rodents and had to be professionally exterminated.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The neglected grain storage facility became **infested** with insects, resulting in the loss of several tons of wheat.
   被忽视的粮食储存设施遭到了虫害侵扰，导致数吨小麦损失。

2. Invasive species have begun to **infest** the lake, threatening the survival of native fish populations and aquatic ecosystems.
   入侵物种已经开始在湖中大量滋生，威胁着本地鱼类种群和水生生态系统的生存。

3. Health inspectors closed the restaurant after discovering that its kitchen was severely **infested** with cockroaches and vermin.
   卫生检查人员发现厨房严重遭到蟑螂和害虫侵扰后，关闭了这家餐厅。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-fest" as an unwanted "festival inside" — imagine pests throwing a wild party in your house, refusing to leave.
把"infest"想成"内部的节日（fest）"——想象害虫在你家里开了一场疯狂的派对，赖着不走。
