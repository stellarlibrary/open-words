# 👏 bravo

🔊 /ˈbrɑːvoʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb, interjection | 名词, 动词, 感叹词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A shout of approval; a hired assassin or desperado. (v.) To applaud by shouting "bravo." (int.) An exclamation of approval or admiration
**中文：** n. 喝彩；亡命徒 v. 喝彩 int. 好极了！妙！

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- applause（掌声）
- acclaim（喝彩）
- ovation（欢呼）
- cheer（欢呼）
- kudos（赞扬）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **shout bravo / cry bravo / bravo to someone / a chorus of bravos**

👉 Example: The audience shouted "Bravo!" as the soprano concluded her magnificent final aria.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The entire audience rose to their feet, shouting **bravo** after the pianist delivered an extraordinary and flawless performance.
   整个观众起立高喊"好极了"，因为钢琴家奉献了一场非凡而完美的演出。

2. In the historical novel, the mysterious **bravo** was hired by a powerful nobleman to eliminate his political rivals.
   在这部历史小说中，一个神秘的亡命徒被一位权贵雇来铲除他的政治对手。

3. Critics offered enthusiastic **bravos** for the director's bold reinterpretation of the classic Shakespearean tragedy on stage.
   评论家们对这位导演在舞台上大胆重新诠释莎士比亚经典悲剧报以热烈的喝彩。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "bravo" as the word you shout when someone is truly "brave" in their performance — brave + o equals bravo!
把"bravo"想成当某人在表演中真正"勇敢"时你喊出的词——brave加上o就是bravo！
