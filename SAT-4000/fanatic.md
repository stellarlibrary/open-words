# 🔥 fanatic

🔊 /fəˈnætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person filled with excessive and irrational devotion to a cause or belief. (adj.) Marked by extreme and uncritical enthusiasm or zeal
**中文：** n. 狂热者，盲信者，入迷者 adj. 狂热的，盲信的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- zealot（狂热者）
- extremist（极端分子）
- enthusiast（狂热爱好者）
- devotee（忠实信徒）
- radical（激进分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **religious fanatic / political fanatic / fanatic devotion / fanatic supporter**

👉 Example: The political fanatic refused to consider any viewpoint that contradicted his rigid ideology.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **fanatic** followers of the movement refused to engage in rational debate, dismissing all opposing evidence as propaganda.
   该运动的狂热追随者拒绝参与理性辩论，将所有反对证据都斥为宣传。

2. Historians have documented how **fanatic** ideologies led to some of the most devastating conflicts of the twentieth century.
   历史学家记录了狂热的意识形态如何导致了二十世纪一些最具破坏性的冲突。

3. She was a **fanatic** about environmental conservation, dedicating every aspect of her professional life to protecting endangered ecosystems.
   她是环保方面的狂热者，将职业生涯的方方面面都献给了保护濒危生态系统。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fan + atic" — a "fan" taken to the extreme becomes a fanatic, someone whose enthusiasm crosses into obsession.
把"fanatic"想成"fan（粉丝）+ atic"——一个走到极端的"粉丝"就变成了狂热者，热情过度变成了痴迷。
