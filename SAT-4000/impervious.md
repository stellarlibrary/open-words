# 🛡️ impervious

🔊 /ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Not allowing something to pass through; unable to be affected or influenced by something
**中文：** adj. 不受影响的；无动于衷的；不能渗透的；不可破坏的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impenetrable（不可渗透的）
- resistant（抵抗的）
- immune（免疫的）
- unaffected（不受影响的）
- invulnerable（无懈可击的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **impervious to (criticism/water/reason) / impervious surface / seemingly impervious**

👉 Example: The seasoned diplomat appeared impervious to the harsh criticism leveled at her during the press conference.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new coating renders the fabric completely **impervious** to water, making it ideal for high-performance outdoor athletic apparel.
   这种新涂层使织物完全不透水，非常适合制作高性能户外运动服装。

2. She remained **impervious** to flattery and manipulation, evaluating every business proposal solely on its objective factual merits.
   她对奉承和操纵无动于衷，完全根据客观事实来评估每一项商业提案。

3. The ancient fortress walls proved **impervious** to repeated bombardment, frustrating the invading army's siege for several grueling months.
   古堡城墙经受住了反复轰炸，使入侵军队的围攻在数月的苦战中屡屡受挫。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-pervious" as "impossible to pervade" — nothing can pervade or pass through something that is impervious, whether physical or emotional.
把"impervious"拆成"im（不能）+ pervious（可渗透的）"——不能渗透的，引申为不受影响的。
