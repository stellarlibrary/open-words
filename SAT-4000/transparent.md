# 🪟 transparent

🔊 /trænˈspærənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Allowing light to pass through so that objects behind can be distinctly seen; easy to perceive or detect; readily understood; obvious
**中文：** adj. 透明的；显然的；易懂的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- clear（清澈的）
- see-through（透明的）
- obvious（显然的）
- lucid（清晰的）
- pellucid（透明的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **transparent material / transparent process / transparent attempt / transparent government**

👉 Example: The organization adopted a more transparent approach to financial reporting after the audit.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The government pledged to make its decision-making process more **transparent** in order to rebuild public trust.
   政府承诺让其决策过程更加透明，以重建公众信任。

2. His **transparent** attempt to shift blame onto his colleagues only served to further damage his professional reputation.
   他明显试图将责任转嫁给同事的做法只会进一步损害他的职业声誉。

3. Scientists developed a new **transparent** polymer that combines remarkable strength with near-perfect optical clarity.
   科学家们开发出一种新型透明聚合物，兼具卓越的强度和近乎完美的光学透明度。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "trans-parent" as "appearing (parent) through (trans)" — you can see right through it, like glass.
把"transparent"想成"trans（穿过）+ parent（显现）"——你可以透过它看到另一面，就像玻璃一样。
