# 🤝 clientele

🔊 /ˌklaɪənˈtel/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The clients or customers of a business, professional practice, or public establishment considered collectively
**中文：** n. 客户（总称）；诉讼委托人；顾客群

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- customers（顾客）
- patrons（主顾）
- clients（客户）
- consumers（消费者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **loyal clientele / wealthy clientele / build a clientele / attract clientele / regular clientele**

👉 Example: The boutique hotel has cultivated a devoted clientele through its exceptional personalized service.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The law firm expanded its **clientele** by offering specialized services in international trade and intellectual property law.
   该律师事务所通过提供国际贸易和知识产权法方面的专业服务扩大了客户群。

2. The restaurant's upscale **clientele** expected nothing less than impeccable cuisine and attentive, refined service.
   这家餐厅的高端客户群期望的是无可挑剔的美食和细致周到的服务。

3. Building a diverse **clientele** requires consistent marketing efforts and a strong reputation for quality and reliability.
   建立多元化的客户群需要持续的营销努力以及对质量和可靠性的良好声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "clientele" as "client + tell" — your clients tell others about your business, and soon you have a whole clientele.
把"clientele"想成"client（客户）+ tell（告诉）"——你的客户告诉别人你的生意，很快你就有了一整群客户。
