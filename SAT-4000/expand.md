# 📐 expand

🔊 /ɪkˈspænd/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To increase in size, volume, or scope; to spread out or unfold; to elaborate on a topic in greater detail
**中文：** v. 膨胀；扩张；张开；展开；详述

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- enlarge（扩大）
- extend（延伸）
- broaden（拓宽）
- amplify（扩充）
- elaborate（详述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **expand a business / expand on an idea / expand rapidly / expand into new markets**

👉 Example: The company plans to expand its operations into several emerging markets across Southeast Asia next year.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university intends to **expand** its research facilities to accommodate the growing number of graduate students and faculty members.
   该大学打算扩建其研究设施，以容纳不断增长的研究生和教职人员。

2. Metals **expand** when heated and contract when cooled, a fundamental principle widely applied in engineering and construction.
   金属受热膨胀、遇冷收缩，这是工程和建筑中广泛应用的基本原理。

3. The professor asked the student to **expand** on her thesis argument by providing additional empirical evidence and scholarly references.
   教授要求该学生通过提供额外的实证证据和学术参考文献来详述她的论文论点。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ex-pand" — "pan" sounds like a pan spreading out; when you expand something, you spread it outward in all directions.
把"expand"想成"ex-pand"——"pan"听起来像平底锅铺展开来；当你expand某物时，你让它向四面八方延展。
