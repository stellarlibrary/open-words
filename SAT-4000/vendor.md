# 🏪 vendor

🔊 /ˈvendər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A person or company offering something for sale; a seller, especially one who sells goods in a public place or supplies products to businesses
**中文：** n. 小贩；卖主；供应商；自动售货机

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- seller（卖家）
- merchant（商人）
- supplier（供应商）
- dealer（经销商）
- trader（交易者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **street vendor / software vendor / vendor agreement / vendor management / third-party vendor**

👉 Example: The company evaluated several vendors before selecting one to provide cloud computing services for its operations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The procurement department negotiated favorable terms with the **vendor** to ensure a reliable supply of raw materials throughout the fiscal year.
   采购部门与供应商谈判了有利条件，以确保整个财年原材料的可靠供应。

2. Street **vendors** have been an integral part of the urban economy, providing affordable goods to residents in densely populated neighborhoods.
   街头小贩一直是城市经济的重要组成部分，为人口密集社区的居民提供价格实惠的商品。

3. The technology **vendor** released a comprehensive security update to address critical vulnerabilities discovered in its enterprise software platform.
   该技术供应商发布了一项全面的安全更新，以解决其企业软件平台中发现的关键漏洞。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vendor" as coming from "vend" (to sell) — a vendor is simply someone who vends, or sells, goods and services.
把"vendor"想成来自"vend（出售）"——vendor就是出售商品和服务的人。
