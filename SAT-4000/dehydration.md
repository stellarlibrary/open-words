# 💧 dehydration

🔊 /ˌdiːhaɪˈdreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The process of losing excessive amounts of water from the body or a substance; the condition resulting from such loss; the removal of water from a substance
**中文：** n. 脱水；干燥

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- desiccation（干燥）
- drying（干化）
- evaporation（蒸发）
- parching（烤干）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **suffer from dehydration / cause dehydration / prevent dehydration / severe dehydration / signs of dehydration**

👉 Example: Athletes must consume adequate fluids during competition to prevent dehydration and maintain performance.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged exposure to extreme heat without sufficient water intake can lead to severe **dehydration** and potentially fatal consequences.
   长时间暴露在极端高温下且水分摄入不足会导致严重脱水，并可能产生致命后果。

2. The **dehydration** process is widely used in food preservation to extend shelf life and reduce the weight of perishable goods.
   脱水工艺被广泛用于食品保存，以延长保质期并减轻易腐货物的重量。

3. Medical researchers found that chronic **dehydration** impairs cognitive function and significantly reduces concentration in academic settings.
   医学研究人员发现，慢性脱水会损害认知功能，并显著降低学术环境中的注意力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-hydr-ation" as "de- (remove) + hydra (water) + -ation" — the removal of water, like squeezing a sponge dry.
把"dehydration"想成"de-（去除）+ hydra（水）+ -ation"——去除水分，就像把海绵挤干一样。
