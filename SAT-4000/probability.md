# 🎲 probability

🔊 /ˌprɒbəˈbɪləti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The likelihood or chance that something will happen; in mathematics, the measure of how likely an event is to occur
**中文：** n. 可能性；概率；或然率

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- likelihood（可能性）
- chance（机会）
- odds（几率）
- prospect（前景）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **high/low probability / in all probability / probability of success / probability distribution**

👉 Example: In all probability, the experiment will yield statistically significant results.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The researchers calculated the **probability** of a major earthquake occurring along the fault line within the next fifty years.
   研究人员计算了该断层线在未来五十年内发生大地震的概率。

2. Statistical models indicate a high **probability** that global temperatures will continue rising without substantial reductions in emissions.
   统计模型表明，如果不大幅减少排放，全球气温继续上升的概率很高。

3. Students must understand **probability** theory before advancing to more complex topics in inferential statistics and data analysis.
   学生必须在学习推断统计和数据分析等更复杂的课题之前理解概率论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "probability" as "probe-ability" — the ability to probe or test how likely something is to happen.
把"probability"想成"probe-ability"——探测或检验某事发生可能性的能力。
