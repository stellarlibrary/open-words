# 📡 disseminate

🔊 /dɪˈsɛmɪneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To spread or scatter widely; to distribute information, ideas, or knowledge broadly
**中文：** vt. 散布；传播；宣传

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- spread（传播）
- distribute（分发）
- propagate（宣传）
- circulate（散布）
- broadcast（广播）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **disseminate information / disseminate knowledge / disseminate findings / disseminate widely**

👉 Example: The organization worked tirelessly to disseminate accurate health information to rural communities.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research institute sought to **disseminate** its findings through academic journals and international conference presentations.
   该研究机构力求通过学术期刊和国际会议报告来传播其研究成果。

2. Social media platforms have made it remarkably easy to **disseminate** both reliable information and dangerous misinformation quickly.
   社交媒体平台使得快速传播可靠信息和危险的错误信息都变得极为容易。

3. The government launched a nationwide campaign to **disseminate** public health guidelines during the emerging infectious disease outbreak.
   政府在新发传染病疫情期间发起了一场全国性运动来传播公共卫生指南。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dis-semin-ate" — "semin" comes from Latin "semen" (seed) — to scatter seeds of knowledge far and wide.
把"disseminate"想成"dis-semin-ate"——"semin"源自拉丁语"semen"（种子）——将知识的种子撒向四方。
