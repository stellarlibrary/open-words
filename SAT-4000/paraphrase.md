# 📝 paraphrase

🔊 /ˈpærəˌfreɪz/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A restatement of a text or passage in other words. (v.) To express the meaning of something using different words
**中文：** n. 释义；解释；意译 v. 释义；解释

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- restate（重新陈述）
- rephrase（改述）
- reword（改变措辞）
- interpret（解释）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **paraphrase a passage / paraphrase the author's words / to paraphrase someone**

👉 Example: Students must learn to paraphrase source material rather than copying it verbatim.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor asked each student to **paraphrase** the central argument of the essay without consulting the original text.
   教授要求每位学生在不查阅原文的情况下用自己的话复述文章的核心论点。

2. To avoid plagiarism, researchers must carefully **paraphrase** the findings of previous studies and provide proper citations.
   为避免抄袭，研究人员必须仔细地用自己的话解释先前研究的发现并提供正确的引用。

3. The diplomat offered a tactful **paraphrase** of the president's controversial remarks during the subsequent press conference.
   在随后的新闻发布会上，外交官对总统有争议的言论进行了委婉的意译。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "para-phrase" as "a parallel phrase" — saying the same thing in a parallel, different way using your own words.
把"paraphrase"想成"para（平行的）+ phrase（短语）"——用平行的、不同的方式说同样的意思。
