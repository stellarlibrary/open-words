# 🎯 optimal

🔊 /ˈɑːptɪməl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Best or most favorable; producing the most desirable outcome under given conditions
**中文：** adj. 最佳的；最理想的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ideal（理想的）
- best（最好的）
- prime（最优的）
- optimum（最佳的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **optimal conditions / optimal performance / optimal solution / optimal level**

👉 Example: The team worked to find the optimal balance between cost efficiency and product quality.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers determined that the **optimal** temperature for enzyme activity in this experiment was precisely thirty-seven degrees Celsius.
   研究人员确定该实验中酶活性的最佳温度恰好为三十七摄氏度。

2. Achieving **optimal** health requires a balanced combination of regular exercise, nutritious diet, and adequate rest each day.
   实现最佳健康状态需要每天规律运动、营养饮食和充足休息的均衡结合。

3. The engineer designed the bridge structure to ensure **optimal** load distribution across all supporting beams and foundations.
   工程师设计桥梁结构以确保所有支撑梁和基础上的最佳载荷分布。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "optimal" as "opti-mal" — "opti" sounds like "option," and the best option is always the optimal one.
把"optimal"想成"opti-mal"——"opti"听起来像"option（选择）"，最好的选择就是最佳的（optimal）。
