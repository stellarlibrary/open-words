# 💊 stimulant

🔊 /ˈstɪmjələnt/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A substance that increases physiological activity; something that arouses or excites. (adj.) Stimulating; exciting
**中文：** n. 刺激物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stimulus（刺激）
- tonic（兴奋剂）
- energizer（提神物）
- incentive（激励因素）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a powerful stimulant / act as a stimulant / stimulant drug / stimulant effect**

👉 Example: Caffeine is the most widely consumed stimulant in the world today.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Medical researchers are investigating whether certain natural **stimulants** can improve cognitive function without harmful side effects.
   医学研究人员正在调查某些天然兴奋剂是否能在无有害副作用的情况下改善认知功能。

2. The government's infrastructure investment served as a powerful economic **stimulant** during the prolonged period of recession.
   政府的基础设施投资在长期经济衰退期间起到了强有力的经济刺激作用。

3. The regulatory agency imposed strict controls on the distribution of pharmaceutical **stimulants** to prevent widespread misuse among students.
   监管机构对药物兴奋剂的销售实施了严格管控，以防止学生群体大范围滥用。

---

把"stimulant"想成"stimulate（刺激）+ ant（蚂蚁）"——想象一只小蚂蚁刺激你行动起来，像一股能量冲击。
