# 🏆 preeminence

🔊 /prɪˈemɪnəns/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The state of surpassing all others in quality, distinction, or achievement; supreme excellence or superiority
**中文：** n. 卓越；杰出；出类拔萃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- supremacy（至高无上）
- superiority（优越）
- distinction（卓越）
- eminence（杰出）
- dominance（支配地位）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **achieve preeminence / preeminence in / preeminence among / maintain preeminence**

👉 Example: The university achieved preeminence in biomedical research through decades of sustained investment.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The nation's **preeminence** in technological innovation has been sustained by substantial public and private research funding.
   这个国家在技术创新方面的卓越地位得益于大量公共和私人研究资金的支持。

2. Scholars have long debated the factors that contributed to the civilization's cultural **preeminence** during the classical period.
   学者们长期以来一直在讨论是哪些因素促成了该文明在古典时期的文化卓越地位。

3. Her **preeminence** among contemporary novelists was confirmed when she received the prestigious international literary award.
   当她获得享有盛誉的国际文学奖时，她在当代小说家中的杰出地位得到了确认。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pre-eminence" as "pre (before) + eminence (high rank)" — standing above the high-ranking, the very best before all others.
把"preeminence"想成"pre（在……之前）+ eminence（卓越）"——站在卓越之上，在所有人之前成为最优秀的。
