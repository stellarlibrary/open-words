# ⏳ duration

🔊 /djʊˈreɪʃən/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The length of time that something lasts or continues; the period during which something exists or persists
**中文：** n. 持续时间；期间；持久

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- span（跨度）
- period（时期）
- length（长度）
- extent（范围）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **for the duration of / short duration / long duration / duration of the experiment**

👉 Example: Participants were monitored for the duration of the clinical trial to track any side effects.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **duration** of the research project was extended by six months due to unforeseen complications in data collection.
   由于数据收集中出现意外问题，研究项目的持续时间延长了六个月。

2. Students must maintain consistent attendance for the entire **duration** of the academic semester to receive full credit.
   学生必须在整个学期期间保持持续出勤才能获得全部学分。

3. The **duration** of the solar eclipse was remarkably brief, lasting only three minutes over the observation site.
   此次日食的持续时间非常短暂，在观测点仅持续了三分钟。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "dur-ation" — "durable" things last a long time, and "duration" measures how long something endures.
把"duration"和"durable（耐久的）"联系起来——耐久的东西持续很久，duration就是衡量持续多久的词。
