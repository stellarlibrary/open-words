# 🔲 achromatic

🔊 /ˌækrəˈmætɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Without color; transmitting light without separating it into constituent colors.
**中文：** 消色差的；非染色质的；非彩色的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- colorless（无色的）
- uncolored（未着色的）
- neutral（中性的）
- monochrome（单色的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **achromatic lens / color / palette / design / light**

👉 Example: The photographer chose an achromatic palette to evoke a sense of timelessness.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist used an **achromatic** lens to eliminate color distortion in the microscopic observation.
   科学家使用消色差透镜来消除显微观察中的色彩失真。

2. The gallery's **achromatic** exhibition featured works rendered entirely in black, white, and shades of gray.
   画廊的非彩色展览展出了完全以黑、白和灰色调呈现的作品。

3. An **achromatic** design approach can create a sophisticated aesthetic that transcends fleeting color trends.
   非彩色的设计方法可以营造出超越短暂色彩潮流的精致美感。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"A-" (without) + "chromatic" (color) = without color. Think of "chrome" which relates to color — remove it and you get achromatic.
"a-"（无）+ "chromatic"（色彩的）= 无色的。想想"chrome（铬，与颜色有关）"——去掉颜色就是achromatic。
