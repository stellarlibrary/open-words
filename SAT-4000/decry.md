# 📢 decry

🔊 /dɪˈkraɪ/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To publicly denounce or express strong disapproval of; to condemn or criticize openly
**中文：** v. 责难，谴责；诽谤

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- denounce（谴责）
- condemn（责难）
- censure（指责）
- disparage（贬低）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **decry the decision / decry violence / decry injustice / decry the practice of**

👉 Example: Environmental groups continue to decry the government's reluctance to enforce stricter pollution regulations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Leading economists openly **decried** the proposed trade policy, warning that it would inevitably lead to higher consumer prices.
   知名经济学家公开谴责了拟议的贸易政策，警告它将不可避免地导致消费者价格上涨。

2. Human rights organizations **decried** the use of excessive force by authorities during the peaceful civilian demonstrations.
   人权组织谴责了当局在和平民众示威期间过度使用武力的行为。

3. The editorial board chose to **decry** the growing influence of misinformation on public discourse and democratic institutions.
   编辑委员会选择谴责虚假信息对公共话语和民主制度日益增长的影响。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "decry" as "de- + cry" — to cry out against something, raising your voice publicly to condemn it.
把"decry"想成"de- + cry（呼喊）"——大声反对某事，公开提高嗓音来谴责它。
