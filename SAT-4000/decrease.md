# 📉 decrease

🔊 /dɪˈkriːs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To make or become smaller or fewer in size, amount, or degree. (n.) A reduction in size, amount, or degree
**中文：** v. （使）变小，（使）减少 n. 减少

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- diminish（减少）
- decline（下降）
- reduce（减少）
- lessen（缩小）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **decrease in value / decrease by / decrease significantly / a steady decrease / a sharp decrease in**

👉 Example: The new regulations led to a significant decrease in industrial pollution levels over the past decade.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers observed a notable **decrease** in the population of migratory birds following the destruction of critical wetland habitats.
   研究人员观察到，在关键湿地栖息地遭到破坏后，候鸟数量显著减少。

2. The central bank decided to **decrease** interest rates in an effort to stimulate economic growth and consumer spending.
   中央银行决定降低利率，以刺激经济增长和消费支出。

3. Public health campaigns have contributed to a steady **decrease** in smoking rates among young adults over the past two decades.
   公共卫生运动在过去二十年中促使年轻人的吸烟率持续下降。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "decrease" as "de- + crease" — the prefix "de-" means down or away, so decrease means to bring the amount down, the opposite of increase.
把"decrease"想成"de- + crease"——前缀"de-"表示向下或离开，所以decrease意味着使数量下降，与increase相反。
