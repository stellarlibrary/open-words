# 📠 facsimile

🔊 /fækˈsɪmɪli/<br>
🏷️ *noun, adjective | 名词, 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An exact copy or reproduction of something; a fax transmission. (adj.) Relating to or being an exact copy
**中文：** n. 复制品；摹本；传真 adj. 复制的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- replica（复制品）
- reproduction（再版）
- duplicate（副本）
- copy（复印件）
- imitation（仿制品）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **facsimile edition / facsimile copy / exact facsimile / facsimile of the original**

👉 Example: The museum displayed a facsimile of the ancient manuscript to protect the fragile original.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The library acquired a remarkable **facsimile** of the Gutenberg Bible, allowing scholars to study it without risking the original.
   图书馆获得了一本精美的古登堡圣经复制品，使学者们可以在不损坏原件的情况下进行研究。

2. Art historians confirmed that the painting was merely a skillful **facsimile** and not the authentic Renaissance masterpiece originally attributed.
   艺术史学家确认这幅画只是一件技艺精湛的复制品，而非最初认定的真正的文艺复兴杰作。

3. The publisher released a limited **facsimile** edition of the author's handwritten notebooks to commemorate the centennial of her birth.
   出版社发行了限量版的作者手写笔记复制本，以纪念她诞辰一百周年。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "fac-simile" as "fac" (make, from Latin facere) + "simile" (similar) — to make something similar, an exact copy.
把"facsimile"拆成"fac"(制造，来自拉丁语facere) + "simile"(相似)——制造出相似的东西，即精确的复制品。
