# ✨ burnish

🔊 /ˈbɜːrnɪʃ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To polish something by rubbing; to enhance or improve the appearance of. (n.) A shine or luster produced by polishing
**中文：** v. 磨光；擦亮；使光亮 n. 光泽；抛光

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- polish（磨光）
- shine（使发光）
- buff（擦亮）
- enhance（提升）
- gloss（使光滑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **burnish one's reputation / burnish one's image / burnish credentials / burnish to a shine**

👉 Example: The senator sought to burnish her credentials by championing popular bipartisan legislation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The artisan carefully **burnished** the copper vessel until it gleamed with a warm, luminous surface worthy of display.
   工匠仔细地打磨铜器，直到它散发出温暖而明亮的光泽，值得展出。

2. The new CEO undertook several philanthropic initiatives to **burnish** the corporation's tarnished public image after the scandal.
   新任首席执行官开展了多项慈善活动，以在丑闻后修复公司受损的公众形象。

3. Years of dedicated scholarship helped **burnish** his reputation as one of the foremost authorities in comparative literature.
   多年的潜心学术研究帮助他树立了比较文学领域最权威学者之一的声誉。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "burn" + "ish" — when you burnish metal, you rub it so vigorously it almost seems to burn with brightness.
想到"burn"（燃烧）+ "ish"——当你burnish金属时，你用力擦拭，使它几乎像燃烧一样闪亮。
