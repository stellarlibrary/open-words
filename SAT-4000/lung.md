# 🫁 lung

🔊 /lʌŋ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Either of the two organs in the chest that are used for breathing; a respiratory organ
**中文：** 肺；呼吸器官

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- pulmonary organ（肺脏）
- respiratory organ（呼吸器官）
- air sac（气囊）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lung capacity / lung disease / lung cancer / at the top of one's lungs / iron lung**

👉 Example: Aerobic exercise is known to significantly improve lung capacity and overall cardiovascular health.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Prolonged exposure to industrial pollutants can cause irreversible damage to the **lungs** and other vital respiratory structures.
   长期接触工业污染物会对肺部和其他重要呼吸结构造成不可逆的损害。

2. The physician examined the patient's **lung** function using a spirometer to assess the severity of the chronic condition.
   医生使用肺活量计检查了患者的肺功能，以评估该慢性疾病的严重程度。

3. Researchers published a comprehensive study linking air quality deterioration to a marked increase in **lung** disease across urban areas.
   研究人员发表了一项综合研究，将空气质量恶化与城市地区肺部疾病的显著增加联系起来。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lung" sounding like "long" — you need your lungs to take a long, deep breath of fresh air.
把"lung"想成发音接近"long（长）"——你需要用肺来做一次又长又深的呼吸。
