# 🧪 Oxygen O

🔊 /ˈɒksɪdʒən əʊ/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The chemical element oxygen, represented by the symbol O on the periodic table, with an atomic number of 8; a highly reactive nonmetal essential for life.
**中文：** n. 氧（化学元素符号为O，原子序数8）

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oxygen（氧气）
- element 8（第八号元素）
- O₂（氧气分子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **element O / atomic oxygen / oxygen atom / symbol O / O in the periodic table**

👉 Example: In chemistry, the symbol O represents a single atom of oxygen in chemical equations.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The element **Oxygen O** occupies a prominent position in the periodic table due to its fundamental role in chemical reactions.
   元素**氧O**因其在化学反应中的基础作用而在元素周期表中占据重要位置。

2. Students must understand that **Oxygen O** readily forms covalent bonds with nearly all other elements in various compounds.
   学生必须理解**氧O**容易与几乎所有其他元素形成共价键，构成各种化合物。

3. The atomic mass of **Oxygen O** is approximately 16, making it one of the lighter elements essential for biological processes.
   **氧O**的原子质量约为16，使其成为对生物过程至关重要的较轻元素之一。

Think of "O" as a round shape like the mouth you open to breathe in oxygen — the circle reminds you of the element that keeps us alive.
把"O"想象成一个圆形，就像你张嘴呼吸氧气的样子——这个圆圈提醒你这是让我们活着的元素。
