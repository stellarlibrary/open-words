# 🔃 circulate

🔊 /ˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To move continuously through a closed system or area; to spread or distribute widely; to pass from person to person or place to place
**中文：** v. 循环；流通；传播；流传

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- distribute（分发）
- disseminate（传播）
- propagate（散布）
- spread（扩散）
- flow（流动）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **circulate a report / circulate rumors / circulate freely / blood circulates / circulate among guests**

👉 Example: The host made sure to circulate among all the guests at the reception.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Blood must **circulate** continuously through the body to deliver essential oxygen and nutrients to every organ and tissue.
   血液必须在体内不断循环，以将必需的氧气和营养物质输送到每个器官和组织。

2. The committee decided to **circulate** the revised policy document among all department heads before the final vote.
   委员会决定在最终投票前将修订后的政策文件在所有部门负责人之间传阅。

3. Unfounded rumors began to **circulate** rapidly through social media, causing unnecessary panic among the general population.
   毫无根据的谣言开始在社交媒体上迅速传播，在公众中引起了不必要的恐慌。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circulate" as "circle" + "-ate" (to do) — to circulate is to move in a circle, like blood circling through your body or news going around a community.
把"circulate"想成"circle"（圆）+"-ate"（做）——circulate就是做圆周运动，就像血液在体内循环或消息在社区中传播。
