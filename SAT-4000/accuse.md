# 👨‍⚖️ accuse

🔊 /əˈkjuːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To charge someone with an offense or crime; to blame or condemn.
**中文：** 控告，指控；谴责；归咎于

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- charge（指控）
- indict（起诉）
- blame（责备）
- denounce（谴责）
- impeach（弹劾）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **accuse someone of (doing) something**

👉 Example: They accused him of embezzling funds from the charity organization.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The whistleblower **accused** the corporation of deliberately concealing toxic waste disposal records.
   举报人指控该公司故意隐瞒有毒废物处理记录。

2. Several nations **accused** the regime of violating fundamental human rights and international treaties.
   几个国家谴责该政权违反基本人权和国际条约。

3. Critics **accused** the author of plagiarism after striking similarities were found in both texts.
   在两部作品中发现惊人的相似之处后，批评家们指控该作者抄袭。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Accuse" sounds like "a cause" — when you accuse someone, you claim they are the cause of something wrong.
"accuse"听起来像"a cause（一个原因）"——当你指控某人时，你声称他们是某件坏事的原因。
