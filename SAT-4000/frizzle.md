# 🌀 frizzle

🔊 /ˈfrɪzəl/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To form into small, tight curls; to curl or crisp with heat. (n.) A small, tight curl of hair
**中文：** v. 使卷缩；使卷曲 n. 卷发；小卷毛

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- curl（卷曲）
- crimp（使卷曲）
- frizz（使起卷）
- kink（使弯曲）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **frizzle hair / frizzle in the heat / frizzle up / frizzle bacon**

👉 Example: The extreme humidity caused her straightened hair to frizzle within minutes of stepping outside.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The intense tropical heat caused the delicate fabric to **frizzle** and shrink beyond any possibility of practical repair.
   强烈的热带高温使精致的织物卷缩收缩，已无任何修复的可能。

2. She used a curling iron to **frizzle** her hair into tight ringlets for the formal evening charity gala.
   她用卷发棒将头发卷成紧密的小卷，为正式的晚间慈善晚会做准备。

3. The chef demonstrated how to **frizzle** thin strips of prosciutto until they became perfectly crisp and golden brown.
   厨师演示了如何将薄片火腿卷曲煎至完美酥脆和金黄色。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "frizzle" as "frizz + sizzle" — hair that frizzes and sizzles in the heat, curling up into tight little spirals.
把"frizzle"想成"frizz（卷曲）+ sizzle（嘶嘶作响）"——头发在热气中卷曲嘶嘶作响，变成紧密的小螺旋。
