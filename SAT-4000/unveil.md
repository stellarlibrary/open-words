# 🎭 unveil

🔊 /ʌnˈveɪl/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** To reveal or make publicly known for the first time; to remove a covering from something
**中文：** v. 使公之于众；揭开；揭幕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- reveal（揭示）
- disclose（披露）
- expose（暴露）
- divulge（泄露）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unveil a plan / unveil a statue / unveil a secret / unveil a new product**

👉 Example: The company will unveil its latest innovation at the annual technology conference next month.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mayor **unveiled** a comprehensive urban renewal plan designed to revitalize the city's deteriorating downtown district.
   市长公布了一项全面的城市更新计划，旨在振兴该市日益衰败的市中心地区。

2. Scientists **unveiled** groundbreaking research findings that could fundamentally alter our understanding of cellular regeneration processes.
   科学家们公布了突破性的研究成果，这可能从根本上改变我们对细胞再生过程的理解。

3. The museum **unveiled** a previously hidden collection of Renaissance paintings that had been stored in vaults for decades.
   博物馆揭幕了一批此前隐藏的文艺复兴时期画作，这些画作已在地下室存放了数十年。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-veil" as removing a veil — when you lift the veil, what was hidden is now revealed to everyone.
把"unveil"想成"去掉面纱"——当你揭开面纱时，原本隐藏的事物就展现在所有人面前了。
