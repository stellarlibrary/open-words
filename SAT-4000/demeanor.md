# 🎭 demeanor

🔊 /dɪˈmiːnər/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A person's outward behavior, manner, or bearing; the way one presents oneself to others
**中文：** n. 风度；举止；行为

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- bearing（举止）
- comportment（态度）
- manner（方式）
- disposition（性情）
- conduct（行为）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **calm demeanor / professional demeanor / outward demeanor / change one's demeanor**

👉 Example: Despite the intense pressure, the surgeon maintained a calm and composed demeanor throughout the operation.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The candidate's confident **demeanor** during the interview impressed the hiring committee and distinguished her from other applicants.
   这位候选人在面试中自信的举止给招聘委员会留下了深刻印象，使她从其他申请者中脱颖而出。

2. His reserved **demeanor** often led colleagues to mistake his thoughtful introspection for indifference toward collaborative projects.
   他矜持的风度常常让同事们误以为他深思熟虑的内省是对合作项目的漠不关心。

3. The teacher's approachable **demeanor** encouraged students to ask questions freely and participate more actively in classroom discussions.
   这位老师平易近人的举止鼓励学生自由提问，更积极地参与课堂讨论。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "de-MEAN-or" — your demeanor is what you "mean" to show others, your outward behavior and manner.
把"demeanor"想成"de-MEAN-or"——你的demeanor就是你"有意"向他人展示的外在行为和态度。
