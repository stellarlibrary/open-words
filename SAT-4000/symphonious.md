# 🎶 symphonious

🔊 /sɪmˈfoʊniəs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Harmonious in sound; agreeable in tone or combination
**中文：** adj. 和谐的，调和的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- harmonious（和谐的）
- melodious（悦耳的）
- concordant（一致的）
- tuneful（曲调优美的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **symphonious blend / symphonious combination / symphonious tones / symphonious harmony**

👉 Example: The choir produced a symphonious blend of voices that resonated throughout the cathedral.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The orchestra delivered a **symphonious** performance that captivated the audience and earned a prolonged standing ovation afterward.
   管弦乐队奉献了一场和谐的演出，迷住了观众，并赢得了长时间的起立鼓掌。

2. The garden was designed to create a **symphonious** arrangement of colors, textures, and fragrances throughout every season.
   这座花园的设计旨在四季中营造出色彩、质感和芳香的和谐搭配。

3. Critics praised the novelist for achieving a **symphonious** balance between complex narrative structure and accessible prose style.
   评论家称赞这位小说家在复杂的叙事结构与通俗易懂的散文风格之间实现了和谐的平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "symphonious" as related to "symphony" — just as a symphony blends instruments into harmony, symphonious means harmonious.
把"symphonious"和"symphony（交响乐）"联系起来——就像交响乐将乐器融合成和声一样，symphonious意为和谐的。
