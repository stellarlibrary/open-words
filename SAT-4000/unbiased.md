# ⚖️ unbiased

🔊 /ʌnˈbaɪəst/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Showing no prejudice; fair and impartial in judgment or treatment
**中文：** 公正的；无偏见的；不偏不倚的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- impartial（公正的）
- objective（客观的）
- neutral（中立的）
- dispassionate（不带感情的）
- fair-minded（公平的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **unbiased opinion / unbiased assessment / unbiased reporting / unbiased sample**

👉 Example: The researchers sought an unbiased sample to ensure the validity of their study.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee appointed an **unbiased** mediator to resolve the longstanding dispute between the two departments fairly.
   委员会任命了一位公正的调解人来公平解决两个部门之间长期存在的争端。

2. Scientific journals require peer reviewers to provide **unbiased** evaluations of submitted manuscripts regardless of the authors' reputations.
   科学期刊要求同行评审人对提交的稿件提供公正的评价，不论作者的声誉如何。

3. An **unbiased** analysis of the economic data revealed that both political parties had overstated their claims about growth.
   对经济数据的客观分析表明，两个政党都夸大了各自关于增长的说法。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "un-biased" — the prefix "un" removes the "bias," leaving you with a perfectly balanced, fair perspective.
把"unbiased"想成"un（去除）+ biased（偏见的）"——去除偏见，就变得公正客观了。
