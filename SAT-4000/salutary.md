# 💡 salutary

🔊 /ˈsæljəˌteri/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Producing a beneficial or wholesome effect; conducive to health or improvement
**中文：** adj. 有益健康的；有益的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- beneficial（有益的）
- wholesome（有益的）
- remedial（补救的）
- salubrious（有益健康的）
- constructive（建设性的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **salutary effect / salutary lesson / salutary reminder / salutary experience**

👉 Example: The economic recession served as a salutary lesson about the dangers of excessive speculation in financial markets.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The new regulations had a **salutary** effect on workplace safety, significantly reducing the number of industrial accidents reported annually.
   新法规对工作场所安全产生了有益的影响，显著减少了每年报告的工业事故数量。

2. Losing the championship proved to be a **salutary** experience that motivated the team to train harder for the following season.
   输掉冠军赛被证明是一次有益的经历，激励了球队在下个赛季更加努力地训练。

3. The professor offered a **salutary** reminder that academic integrity must never be compromised regardless of external pressures or deadlines.
   教授发出了有益的提醒，即无论外部压力或截止日期如何，学术诚信绝不能妥协。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "salut-ary" from Latin "salus" meaning health — a salutary experience is one that ultimately benefits your health or well-being, like a lesson learned.
把"salutary"想成来自拉丁语"salus（健康）"——一个salutary的经历最终有益于你的健康或福祉，就像一个汲取的教训。
