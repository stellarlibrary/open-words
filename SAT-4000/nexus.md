# 🔗 nexus

🔊 /ˈnɛksəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A connection or series of connections linking two or more things; a central or focal point
**中文：** n. 关系；连结，连系

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- connection（连接）
- link（纽带）
- junction（交汇点）
- hub（中心）
- intersection（交叉点）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **nexus of power / nexus between A and B / cultural nexus / nexus point**

👉 Example: The city served as a nexus of trade routes connecting the eastern and western regions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Researchers identified a complex **nexus** between poverty, lack of education, and rising crime rates in urban areas.
   研究人员发现了城市地区贫困、教育缺乏与犯罪率上升之间的复杂关联。

2. The university library became the **nexus** of intellectual activity, attracting scholars from diverse academic disciplines.
   大学图书馆成为学术活动的中心，吸引了来自不同学科的学者。

3. Understanding the **nexus** between climate change and food security is critical for developing effective agricultural policies.
   了解气候变化与粮食安全之间的关联对于制定有效的农业政策至关重要。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "nexus" as related to "next" and "connect" — it is the point where things come next to each other and connect.
把"nexus"联想到"next（紧邻）"和"connect（连接）"——它是事物彼此紧密相连的交汇点。
