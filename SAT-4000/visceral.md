# 🫀 visceral

🔊 /ˈvɪsərəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Relating to the internal organs; arising from instinct or deep emotion rather than intellect; profoundly felt
**中文：** adj. 内脏的；出于本能的；发自肺腑的；粗俗的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- instinctive（本能的）
- gut-level（直觉的）
- intuitive（直觉的）
- primal（原始的）
- deep-seated（根深蒂固的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **visceral reaction / visceral response / visceral hatred / visceral experience**

👉 Example: The audience had a visceral reaction to the graphic scenes depicted in the documentary.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The documentary evoked a **visceral** response from viewers, many of whom were moved to tears by its raw portrayal.
   这部纪录片引发了观众发自肺腑的反应，许多人被其真实的描绘感动得流下了眼泪。

2. She felt a **visceral** fear when confronting the evidence that contradicted her long-held beliefs about the institution.
   当面对与她长期以来对该机构信念相矛盾的证据时，她感到了一种本能的恐惧。

3. The artist's work captures the **visceral** intensity of human suffering through bold strokes and unflinching imagery.
   这位艺术家的作品通过大胆的笔触和毫不退缩的意象捕捉了人类痛苦发自肺腑的强烈感受。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "visceral" as related to "viscera" (internal organs) — a visceral feeling comes from deep inside your gut, not your head.
把"visceral"想成与"viscera"（内脏）相关——一种visceral的感觉来自你内心深处，而非头脑。
