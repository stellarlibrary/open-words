# 🎭 pantomime

🔊 /ˈpæntəmaɪm/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A dramatic entertainment in which performers express meaning through gestures and body movement without words; a type of theatrical performance. (v.) To express or represent by exaggerated gestures without speech
**中文：** n. 哑剧；舞剧；手势 v. 打手势；演哑剧

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- mime（哑剧）
- charade（哑谜表演）
- dumb show（哑剧）
- gesticulation（手势表达）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pantomime a scene / pantomime performance / Christmas pantomime / play pantomime**

👉 Example: The actor pantomimed the act of opening an invisible door, captivating the audience with his expressive gestures.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The street performer's brilliant **pantomime** attracted a large crowd who watched in fascination as he portrayed an entire story silently.
   街头表演者精彩的哑剧吸引了一大群人，他们着迷地看着他无声地演绎了一个完整的故事。

2. Unable to speak the local language, the traveler had to **pantomime** her request for directions to the nearest train station.
   由于不会说当地语言，这位旅行者不得不用手势比划着询问去最近火车站的方向。

3. The annual Christmas **pantomime** has become a beloved tradition in British theater, blending comedy, music, and audience participation.
   一年一度的圣诞哑剧已成为英国戏剧中深受喜爱的传统，融合了喜剧、音乐和观众互动。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "panto-mime" — "panto" means all and "mime" means to imitate; pantomime is imitating ALL actions through gestures without words.
把"pantomime"想成"panto-mime"——"panto"意为"全部"，"mime"意为"模仿"；pantomime就是通过手势无声地模仿所有动作。
