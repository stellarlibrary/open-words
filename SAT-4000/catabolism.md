# 🧬 catabolism

🔊 /kəˈtæbəlɪzəm/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The set of metabolic pathways that break down complex molecules into simpler ones, releasing energy in the process
**中文：** n. 分解代谢

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- decomposition（分解）
- degradation（降解）
- breakdown（分解）
- dissimilation（异化作用）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **catabolism of proteins / catabolism and anabolism / catabolic pathway / catabolic process**

👉 Example: Catabolism provides the energy that cells need to carry out essential biological functions.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. During prolonged fasting, the body increases **catabolism** of stored fat reserves to supply the energy required for survival.
   在长时间禁食期间，身体增加对储存脂肪的分解代谢，以提供生存所需的能量。

2. The biochemistry lecture focused on the role of **catabolism** in converting glucose into adenosine triphosphate within cellular respiration.
   生物化学讲座重点介绍了分解代谢在细胞呼吸中将葡萄糖转化为三磷酸腺苷的作用。

3. Researchers discovered that impaired **catabolism** of certain amino acids can lead to the accumulation of toxic metabolic byproducts.
   研究人员发现，某些氨基酸的分解代谢受损会导致有毒代谢副产物的积累。

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "cat-abolism" — a cat abolishes its food by breaking it down. Catabolism is the body's way of breaking down complex molecules.
把"catabolism"想成"cat-abolism"——一只猫（cat）通过分解来废除（abolish）食物。分解代谢就是身体分解复杂分子的方式。
