# 😴 drowsy

🔊 /ˈdraʊzi/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Feeling sleepy or half-asleep; tending to induce sleepiness
**中文：** adj. 昏昏欲睡的；催眠的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- sleepy（困倦的）
- lethargic（昏沉的）
- somnolent（嗜睡的）
- torpid（迟钝的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **feel drowsy / drowsy afternoon / drowsy from medication / drowsy state**

👉 Example: The warm afternoon sun made the students feel increasingly drowsy during the lecture.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The antihistamine medication made the patient extremely **drowsy**, so the physician advised against operating heavy machinery.
   抗组胺药物使患者极度昏昏欲睡，因此医生建议不要操作重型机械。

2. A **drowsy** atmosphere pervaded the lecture hall as the professor continued his monotonous presentation without pause.
   当教授不间断地进行单调的演讲时，一种令人昏昏欲睡的气氛弥漫了整个报告厅。

3. The researchers discovered that prolonged exposure to the chemical compound rendered laboratory subjects noticeably **drowsy** and disoriented.
   研究人员发现，长时间接触该化合物会使实验对象明显昏昏欲睡且方向感混乱。

---

把"drowsy"想成"drow-sy"——想象你正在"睡眠中溺水"，慢慢沉入梦乡之海。
