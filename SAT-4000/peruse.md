# 📖 peruse

🔊 /pəˈruːz/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To read or examine something carefully and thoroughly; to study with close attention to detail
**中文：** v. 详细考察；精读

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- examine（审查）
- scrutinize（仔细检查）
- study（研究）
- inspect（检视）
- review（审阅）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **peruse a document / peruse the report / peruse the records / peruse at leisure / peruse carefully**

👉 Example: The scholar spent the entire afternoon perusing rare manuscripts in the university library.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board members were asked to **peruse** the financial statements thoroughly before casting their votes on the merger.
   董事会成员被要求在就合并事项投票之前仔细阅读财务报表。

2. Historians who **peruse** primary source documents often uncover nuances that secondary accounts fail to capture accurately.
   精读原始资料的历史学家常常能发现二手文献未能准确捕捉到的细微差别。

3. The attorney needed several hours to **peruse** the extensive collection of depositions submitted by opposing counsel.
   律师需要数小时来仔细审阅对方律师提交的大量证词材料。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "peruse" as "per (thoroughly) + use (your eyes)" — to thoroughly use your eyes to read every detail of a text.
把"peruse"想成"per（彻底地）+ use（使用你的眼睛）"——彻底地用你的眼睛阅读文本的每一个细节。
