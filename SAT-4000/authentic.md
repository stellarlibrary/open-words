# ✅ authentic

🔊 /ɔːˈθɛntɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Of undisputed origin; genuine and not counterfeit; made or done in a traditional way that faithfully resembles the original; reliable and trustworthy.
**中文：** 真正的，真实的；可信的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- genuine（真正的）
- real（真实的）
- legitimate（合法的）
- veritable（确实的）
- bona fide（真正的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **authentic document/artifact/experience**, **authentic cuisine/style**, **authentic voice/self**

👉 Example: The museum acquired an authentic manuscript dating back to the fifteenth century.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Art experts confirmed that the recently discovered painting was an **authentic** work by the renowned Dutch master.
   艺术专家确认这幅新近发现的画作是这位著名荷兰大师的真迹。

2. The restaurant prides itself on serving **authentic** regional cuisine prepared with locally sourced traditional ingredients only.
   这家餐厅以提供仅用当地传统食材制作的正宗地方菜肴为荣。

3. Scholars rely on **authentic** primary sources to reconstruct accurate narratives of significant events in world history.
   学者们依靠真实的一手资料来重建世界历史上重大事件的准确叙述。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Authentic" contains "author" — the original author or creator. Something authentic comes from its true author or origin, not a copycat. If it has the "author's stamp," it is authentic.
"Authentic"包含"author（作者）"——原始的作者或创造者。真正的东西来自其真正的作者或源头，而非模仿者。如果有"作者的印记"，那就是authentic的。
