# 🤴 autocrat

🔊 /ˈɔːtəkræt/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A ruler who has absolute power; a domineering person who insists on complete obedience from others.
**中文：** n. 独裁者，专制君主

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- despot（暴君）
- tyrant（专制者）
- dictator（独裁者）
- oppressor（压迫者）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **a ruthless autocrat / act like an autocrat / the autocrat of...**

👉 Example: The CEO managed the company like an autocrat, tolerating no dissent from employees.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **autocrat** suppressed all forms of political opposition and silenced journalists who dared to criticize his regime.
   这位独裁者压制了一切形式的政治反对派，并让敢于批评其政权的记者噤声。

2. History reveals that every **autocrat** eventually faces resistance from the very people they seek to control completely.
   历史表明，每一位专制君主最终都会遭到他们试图完全控制的人民的反抗。

3. The board members accused the chairman of behaving like an **autocrat** by making all major decisions without consultation.
   董事会成员指责主席在未经协商的情况下做出所有重大决定，行事如同独裁者。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "auto" (self) + "crat" (ruler) — an autocrat is a self-appointed ruler who answers to no one.
想想"auto"（自己）+"crat"（统治者）——autocrat就是一个自封的、不对任何人负责的统治者。
