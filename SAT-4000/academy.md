# 🏫 academy

🔊 /əˈkædəmi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An institution of higher learning, research, or specialized training; a society of distinguished scholars or artists.
**中文：** 学院；研究院；学会；专科院校。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- institute（研究所）
- school（学校）
- college（学院）
- institution（机构）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **military academy**, **academy of sciences**, **academy award**, **admitted to the academy**

👉 Example: He was admitted to the naval academy at the age of eighteen.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **academy** of sciences convened to discuss emerging challenges in climate research.
   科学院召开会议，讨论气候研究中新出现的挑战。

2. She graduated from a prestigious military **academy** and was commissioned as an officer.
   她毕业于一所著名的军事学院，并被授予军官军衔。

3. The art **academy** offers rigorous programs in painting, sculpture, and contemporary design.
   该艺术学院提供绘画、雕塑和当代设计方面的严格课程。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Academy" comes from Plato's school in ancient Athens, called "Akademeia" — the original place of learning.
"Academy" 源自柏拉图在古雅典创办的学园 "Akademeia"——最初的学术殿堂。
