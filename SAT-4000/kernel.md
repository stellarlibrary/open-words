# 🌰 kernel

🔊 /ˈkɜːrnl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The central or most important part of something; the core or essence; the softer inner part of a nut or seed; a grain of cereal
**中文：** n. 核心；要点；内核；仁；麦粒

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- core（核心）
- essence（本质）
- nucleus（中心）
- gist（要点）
- seed（种子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **kernel of truth / kernel of an idea / kernel of wisdom / corn kernel**

👉 Example: There is a kernel of truth in every myth that has endured for centuries.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Beneath the elaborate rhetoric, the speech contained a valuable **kernel** of wisdom about civic responsibility and duty.
   在精心修饰的言辞之下，这篇演讲包含了关于公民责任与义务的宝贵真知灼见。

2. The researcher identified the **kernel** of the problem after months of careful investigation and systematic analysis.
   经过数月的仔细调查和系统分析，研究人员找到了问题的核心所在。

3. Every enduring legend carries a **kernel** of historical truth that scholars endeavor to uncover and interpret.
   每个流传久远的传说都包含着学者们努力发掘和解读的历史真相内核。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "kernel" as the "colonel" of a nut — the leader hidden inside, the most important part at the center.
把"kernel"想成坚果里的"将军"——隐藏在内部、位于中心的最重要部分。
