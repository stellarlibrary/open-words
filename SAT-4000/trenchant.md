# 🔪 trenchant

🔊 /ˈtrentʃənt/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Vigorous or incisive in expression or style; sharply perceptive; keen and effective
**中文：** adj. 尖刻的；锐利的；一针见血的；犀利的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- incisive（敏锐的）
- caustic（尖刻的）
- penetrating（深刻的）
- acerbic（尖酸的）
- keen（敏锐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **trenchant criticism / trenchant analysis / trenchant wit / trenchant observation**

👉 Example: The editorial offered a trenchant critique of the administration's economic policies.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The journalist's **trenchant** commentary on corporate corruption exposed systemic failures that regulators had long overlooked.
   这位记者对企业腐败的犀利评论揭露了监管机构长期忽视的系统性缺陷。

2. Her **trenchant** analysis of the novel revealed layers of meaning that even seasoned literary scholars had failed to identify.
   她对这部小说的深刻分析揭示了连资深文学学者都未能发现的多层含义。

3. The senator delivered a **trenchant** rebuttal during the debate, dismantling the opposition's arguments with precision and clarity.
   这位参议员在辩论中进行了犀利的反驳，精准而清晰地瓦解了对方的论点。
