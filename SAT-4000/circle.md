# ⭕ circle

🔊 /ˈsɜːrkəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A round plane figure; a group of people with shared interests; a cycle or series. (v.) To move in a circular path; to revolve around
**中文：** n. 圆；圈子；循环，周期 v. 盘旋；旋转；环绕

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- ring（环）
- loop（圈）
- orbit（轨道）
- cycle（循环）
- coterie（小圈子）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inner circle / vicious circle / circle of friends / come full circle / circle around**

👉 Example: The discussion seemed to go in circles, never arriving at a practical conclusion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The hawk continued to **circle** high above the open meadow, patiently scanning the ground for unsuspecting prey.
   老鹰继续在开阔的草地上空高高盘旋，耐心地扫视地面寻找毫无察觉的猎物。

2. She moved within an elite intellectual **circle** that included renowned philosophers, scientists, and celebrated literary figures.
   她活跃在一个精英知识分子圈子中，其中包括著名的哲学家、科学家和知名文学人物。

3. The debate about educational reform has come full **circle**, returning to the very principles that were originally proposed.
   关于教育改革的讨论已经走了一个完整的循环，回到了最初提出的原则。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "circle" as "circ" (round, as in "circus") + "le" — a circus ring is round, just like a circle, and ideas can go round and round in circles.
把"circle"想成"circ"（圆的，如"circus"马戏团）+"le"——马戏团的表演场是圆的，正如圆圈一样，想法也可以在圈子里绕来绕去。
