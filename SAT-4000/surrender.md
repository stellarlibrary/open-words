# 🏳️ surrender

🔊 /səˈrɛndər/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To give up possession or control of something; to yield to the power of another; to submit. (n.) The act of giving up or yielding; capitulation
**中文：** v. 投降，交出，放弃

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- capitulate（投降）
- yield（屈服）
- relinquish（放弃）
- submit（服从）
- concede（让步）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **surrender to / surrender oneself / unconditional surrender / surrender control**

👉 Example: The besieged garrison had no choice but to surrender after weeks without supplies or reinforcements.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. After months of intense negotiation, the company was forced to **surrender** its controlling stake in the overseas subsidiary to foreign investors.
   经过数月的激烈谈判，该公司被迫将其在海外子公司的控股权交给外国投资者。

2. The general refused to **surrender** despite being vastly outnumbered, insisting that his troops would continue to defend the strategic position.
   尽管寡不敌众，将军拒绝投降，坚持其部队将继续守卫这一战略阵地。

3. The unconditional **surrender** of the opposing forces marked the definitive conclusion of a prolonged and devastating military conflict.
   对方军队的无条件投降标志着一场旷日持久且破坏性极大的军事冲突的最终结束。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "surrender" as "sur (under) + render (give)" — to render yourself under someone else's power by giving up the fight.
把"surrender"想成"sur（在下面）+ render（给予）"——将自己置于他人的权力之下，放弃抵抗。
