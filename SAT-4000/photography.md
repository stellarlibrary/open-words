# 📷 photography

🔊 /fəˈtɑːɡrəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The art, practice, or process of producing images by recording light on a sensitive surface; the profession or field of taking photographs
**中文：** n. 摄影；摄影术；照相术

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- picture-taking（拍照）
- imaging（成像）
- cinematography（电影摄影）
- photocomposition（照相排版）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **aerial photography / digital photography / photography exhibition / photography studio**

👉 Example: Her passion for photography led her to document wildlife in remote regions of the world.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The museum's latest exhibition features stunning **photography** captured by artists who traveled through conflict zones worldwide.
   博物馆最新的展览展出了由穿越世界各地冲突地区的艺术家拍摄的令人惊叹的摄影作品。

2. Advances in digital **photography** have fundamentally transformed the way journalists document and report current events.
   数字摄影技术的进步从根本上改变了记者记录和报道时事的方式。

3. The professor argued that **photography** played a crucial role in shaping public perception during the civil rights movement.
   这位教授认为摄影在民权运动期间对塑造公众认知发挥了至关重要的作用。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "photo-graphy" as "photo" (light) + "graphy" (writing) — photography is literally "writing with light."
把"photography"拆成"photo"（光）+"graphy"（书写）——摄影就是"用光书写"。
