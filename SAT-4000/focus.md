# 🎯 focus

🔊 /ˈfoʊkəs/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) The center of interest or activity; the point at which rays converge. (v.) To concentrate attention or effort on a particular subject or task
**中文：** n. 焦点；中心；重点 v. 使集中；使聚焦

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- concentrate（集中）
- center（中心）
- emphasis（重点）
- attention（注意力）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **focus on / focus attention / bring into focus / lose focus / sharp focus**

👉 Example: The researcher decided to focus on a single variable to ensure the experiment yielded reliable results.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee decided to **focus** its efforts on improving public transportation infrastructure in underserved rural communities across the state.
   委员会决定将精力**集中**在改善全州服务不足的农村社区的公共交通基础设施上。

2. The primary **focus** of the new curriculum is developing critical thinking skills that prepare students for higher education.
   新课程的主要**重点**是培养为学生升入高等教育做准备的批判性思维能力。

3. Without a clear **focus**, the research proposal lacked direction and failed to convince the review panel of its merit.
   由于缺乏明确的**焦点**，该研究提案缺乏方向，未能说服评审委员会认可其价值。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "focus" as a camera lens — when you focus, everything blurry becomes sharp and clear, just like concentrating your mind.
把"focus"想成照相机镜头——当你聚焦时，一切模糊的东西都变得清晰锐利，就像集中你的注意力一样。
