# 🗑️ eliminate

🔊 /ɪˈlɪməˌneɪt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To completely remove or get rid of something; to exclude from consideration; to rule out
**中文：** v. 消除；排除；淘汰；根除

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- remove（移除）
- eradicate（根除）
- exclude（排除）
- abolish（废除）
- discard（丢弃）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **eliminate the possibility / eliminate waste / eliminate a threat / eliminate from competition**

👉 Example: The new filtration system was designed to eliminate harmful contaminants from the water supply.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The research team worked diligently to **eliminate** all potential sources of error before publishing their experimental findings.
   研究团队在发表实验结论之前努力消除所有潜在的误差来源。

2. The government introduced strict regulations to **eliminate** industrial pollution from rivers and other vital freshwater ecosystems.
   政府出台了严格的法规以消除河流和其他重要淡水生态系统中的工业污染。

3. Through a systematic process of deduction, the detective was able to **eliminate** several suspects from the investigation.
   通过系统的演绎推理过程，侦探成功地从调查中排除了几名嫌疑人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "eliminate" as "e-limin-ate" — "limin" comes from Latin "limen" meaning threshold; to eliminate is to push something beyond the threshold and out.
把"eliminate"想成"e-limin-ate"——"limin"源自拉丁语"limen"意为门槛；消除就是把东西推过门槛扔出去。
