# ✨ ethereal

🔊 /ɪˈθɪriəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Extremely delicate and light; heavenly or celestial; seeming too perfect for this world
**中文：** adj. 飘逸的；优雅的；轻飘的；缥缈的；超凡的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- celestial（天上的）
- delicate（精致的）
- otherworldly（超凡的）
- sublime（崇高的）
- gossamer（轻薄的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **ethereal beauty / ethereal glow / ethereal quality / ethereal music / ethereal grace**

👉 Example: The ballerina possessed an ethereal grace that captivated every member of the audience.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The morning mist gave the ancient forest an **ethereal** quality, as though it belonged to a realm beyond ordinary experience.
   晨雾赋予古老森林一种缥缈的气质，仿佛它属于超越寻常体验的另一个领域。

2. The composer's final symphony was praised for its **ethereal** melody that seemed to transcend the boundaries of human emotion.
   这位作曲家的最后一部交响曲因其超凡的旋律而备受赞誉，仿佛超越了人类情感的边界。

3. Visitors to the cathedral were struck by the **ethereal** light filtering through the centuries-old stained glass windows above.
   大教堂的参观者被透过头顶百年彩色玻璃窗过滤而入的空灵光线所震撼。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "ethereal" as "ether + real" — something from the ether (heavens) made real, yet still seeming impossibly light and delicate.
把"ethereal"想成"ether + real"——来自以太（天空）的东西变为现实，却仍然显得不可思议般轻盈飘逸。
