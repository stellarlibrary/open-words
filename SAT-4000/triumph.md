# 🏆 triumph

🔊 /ˈtraɪʌmf/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A great victory or achievement; a feeling of joy from success. (v.) To achieve a decisive victory; to prevail
**中文：** n. 胜利；巨大成就；欢欣 v. 获得胜利；成功

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- victory（胜利）
- conquest（征服）
- prevail（获胜）
- succeed（成功）
- exultation（欢欣）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **triumph over / a triumph of / personal triumph / triumph in**

👉 Example: The team's triumph over their longtime rivals was celebrated throughout the entire city.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The scientist's discovery of the new vaccine was hailed as a **triumph** of modern medical research and international collaboration.
   这位科学家发现的新疫苗被誉为现代医学研究和国际合作的巨大胜利。

2. After years of persistent effort, the civil rights movement **triumphed** over systemic discrimination through peaceful protest and legal action.
   经过多年坚持不懈的努力，民权运动通过和平抗议和法律行动战胜了系统性歧视。

3. Her autobiography chronicles her personal **triumph** over adversity, from childhood poverty to becoming a distinguished university professor.
   她的自传记录了她个人战胜逆境的历程，从童年的贫困到成为一名杰出的大学教授。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "tri-umph" — imagine trying three (tri) times before you finally succeed and shout "umph!" in victory.
把"triumph"想成"tri（三次）+ umph（欢呼声）"——想象尝试了三次终于成功，发出胜利的欢呼。
