# 📚 instruct

🔊 /ɪnˈstrʌkt/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To teach someone a subject or skill; to direct or command someone to do something; to inform or notify officially
**中文：** vt. 教；教授；命令；指示

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- teach（教授）
- direct（指示）
- command（命令）
- educate（教育）
- train（训练）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **instruct someone to do something / instruct a jury / instruct students / as instructed**

👉 Example: The judge instructed the jury to disregard the inadmissible testimony presented by the defense attorney.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor was **instructed** by the department to revise the curriculum in accordance with the newly adopted academic standards.
   该教授被系里指示按照新采纳的学术标准修订课程。

2. Senior engineers **instruct** junior colleagues in the proper application of safety protocols during all construction site operations.
   高级工程师在所有建筑工地操作中指导初级同事正确应用安全规程。

3. The court **instructed** both parties to submit their final written arguments no later than the end of the fiscal quarter.
   法院命令双方在本财政季度结束前提交最终书面论据。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "instruct" as "in-struct(ure)" — to build structure into someone's mind by teaching them knowledge and skills.
把"instruct"想成"in-struct(ure)"——通过教授知识和技能在某人的头脑中构建架构。
