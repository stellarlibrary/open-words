# 🏆 panegyric

🔊 /ˌpænəˈdʒɪrɪk/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A public speech or published text in praise of someone or something; a formal or elaborate eulogy or tribute
**中文：** n. 颂词；赞颂；颂文

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- eulogy（颂词）
- tribute（赞辞）
- encomium（颂扬）
- accolade（赞美）
- paean（赞歌）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **deliver a panegyric / write a panegyric / panegyric to / panegyric in praise of**

👉 Example: The senator delivered an eloquent panegyric in honor of the retiring Supreme Court justice.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The biographer's latest work reads more like a **panegyric** than an objective account of the statesman's controversial political career.
   这位传记作者的最新作品读起来更像一篇颂词，而非对这位政治家争议性政治生涯的客观叙述。

2. At the memorial ceremony, several distinguished scholars delivered heartfelt **panegyrics** celebrating the professor's contributions to modern science.
   在纪念仪式上，几位杰出学者发表了真挚的颂词，赞扬这位教授对现代科学的贡献。

3. Ancient Roman orators were renowned for composing elaborate **panegyrics** that extolled the virtues and achievements of their emperors.
   古罗马演说家以撰写精心赞颂皇帝美德和功绩的华丽颂词而闻名。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pane-gyric" as "pan + a + lyric" — a lyric song for ALL to hear, praising someone's greatness publicly.
把"panegyric"想成"pan + a + lyric"——一首让所有人都能听到的赞美之歌，公开颂扬某人的伟大。
