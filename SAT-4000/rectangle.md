# 📐 rectangle

🔊 /ˈrektæŋɡəl/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) A plane figure with four straight sides and four right angles, especially one with unequal adjacent sides
**中文：** n. 矩形；长方形

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- oblong（长方形）
- quadrilateral（四边形）
- parallelogram（平行四边形）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **draw a rectangle / rectangle shape / area of a rectangle / rectangle dimensions**

👉 Example: The architect designed the floor plan around a central rectangle to maximize usable interior space.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The mathematician demonstrated that the area of a **rectangle** is calculated by multiplying its length by its width.
   数学家演示了矩形的面积是通过将其长度乘以宽度来计算的。

2. Urban planners arranged the new residential blocks in a precise **rectangle** formation to optimize traffic flow and green space.
   城市规划者将新住宅区按精确的矩形布局排列，以优化交通流量和绿化空间。

3. The artist framed her abstract composition within a golden **rectangle** to achieve a visually harmonious and balanced aesthetic.
   这位艺术家将她的抽象构图置于黄金矩形之中，以达到视觉上的和谐与平衡。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "rect-angle" as "right angle" — a rectangle is defined by having four right angles, and "rect" comes from Latin "rectus" meaning straight or right.
把"rectangle"拆成"rect-angle"——"rect"来自拉丁语"rectus"意为"直的"，所以矩形就是有四个直角的图形。
