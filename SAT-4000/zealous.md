# 🔥 zealous

🔊 /ˈzɛləs/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Having or showing great energy, enthusiasm, and devotion to a cause, ideal, or purpose
**中文：** 热心的；热情的；积极的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- fervent（热切的）
- passionate（充满激情的）
- ardent（热心的）
- enthusiastic（热情的）
- dedicated（专注的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **zealous advocate / zealous supporter / zealous in one's efforts / overly zealous**

👉 Example: The zealous attorney worked tirelessly to ensure that her client received a fair and impartial trial.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **zealous** reformer campaigned relentlessly for educational equity, delivering impassioned speeches at every public forum she attended.
   这位热心的改革者不遗余力地为教育公平奔走，在她参加的每个公共论坛上发表激情演讲。

2. His **zealous** commitment to environmental conservation inspired an entire generation of young scientists to pursue sustainable research.
   他对环境保护的热忱投入激励了整整一代年轻科学家投身于可持续研究。

3. The manager's overly **zealous** enforcement of minor workplace regulations ultimately diminished employee morale and overall productivity.
   经理对次要工作制度过于热心的执行最终降低了员工士气和整体生产力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "zealous" as "zeal" + "-ous" (full of) — someone who is full of zeal, brimming with passionate energy.
把"zealous"想成"zeal"（热情）+"-ous"（充满）——充满热情的人，洋溢着激情和活力。
