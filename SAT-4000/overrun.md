# 🐜 overrun

🔊 /ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/<br>
🏷️ *noun, verb | 名词, 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (n.) An instance of spreading beyond limits; exceeding expected costs or time. (v.) To spread over and occupy; to exceed a limit; to infest
**中文：** n. 泛滥成灾；超出限度 v. 超过；泛滥；蹂躏

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- infest（侵扰）
- invade（入侵）
- exceed（超过）
- swarm（蜂拥）
- overwhelm（淹没）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **overrun with weeds / overrun by insects / cost overrun / overrun a deadline / overrun the budget**

👉 Example: The abandoned garden was completely overrun with invasive weeds and tangled vines.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The construction project suffered a massive cost **overrun**, exceeding the original budget by nearly forty percent due to unforeseen complications.
   该建设项目出现了巨大的成本超支，由于不可预见的复杂情况，超出原始预算近百分之四十。

2. Without proper maintenance, the once-elegant estate became **overrun** with rodents and thick vegetation that obscured every pathway.
   由于缺乏适当的维护，这座曾经优雅的庄园被啮齿动物和茂密的植被所侵占，遮蔽了每一条小路。

3. The invading forces quickly **overran** the border defenses and advanced deep into the interior of the besieged territory.
   入侵部队迅速突破了边境防御，深入被围困领土的腹地。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "over-run" — something that "runs over" its boundaries, like pests running all over a garden or costs running over the budget.
把"overrun"想成某物"跑过"了它的边界——就像害虫跑遍了整个花园，或者费用超出了预算。
