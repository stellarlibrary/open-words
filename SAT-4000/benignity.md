# 🌸 benignity

🔊 /bɪˈnɪɡnɪti/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The quality of being kind, gentle, and well-disposed; an act of kindness or generosity
**中文：** n. 仁慈；善举；良性

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- kindness（善良）
- gentleness（温和）
- graciousness（和蔼）
- benevolence（仁慈）
- clemency（宽仁）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **innate benignity / benignity of character / show benignity / act of benignity**

👉 Example: The queen's benignity was evident in her merciful treatment of political prisoners.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The **benignity** of the village elders created a harmonious atmosphere that visitors immediately recognized and appreciated.
   村中长者的仁慈营造了一种和谐的氛围，来访者立刻就能感受到并心生感激。

2. His remarkable **benignity** toward former adversaries demonstrated a rare capacity for forgiveness and reconciliation.
   他对昔日对手表现出的非凡仁慈展示了一种罕见的宽恕与和解能力。

3. The doctor spoke with a **benignity** that immediately calmed the anxious family awaiting the diagnosis in the corridor.
   医生以一种仁慈的语气说话，立刻安抚了在走廊里焦急等待诊断结果的家人。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "benignity" as "benign + ity" — the state of being benign, which means gentle and harmless, thus kindness.
把"benignity"拆分为"benign（和善的）+ ity（名词后缀）"——即和善的状态，也就是仁慈。
