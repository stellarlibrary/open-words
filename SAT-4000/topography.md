# 🗺️ topography

🔊 /təˈpɒɡrəfi/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** The arrangement of the natural and artificial physical features of an area; the detailed study or mapping of the surface features of a region
**中文：** 地势；地形；地形学；地貌

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- terrain（地形）
- landscape（地貌）
- geography（地理）
- relief（地势起伏）
- contour（轮廓）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **local topography / rugged topography / topography of the region / topography map**

👉 Example: The engineers carefully studied the topography of the valley before designing the new dam.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The rugged **topography** of the mountainous region presented significant challenges for the construction of the new railway line.
   该山区崎岖的地形为新铁路线的建设带来了重大挑战。

2. Advances in satellite imaging have revolutionized the way scientists map and analyze the **topography** of remote landscapes.
   卫星成像技术的进步彻底改变了科学家绘制和分析偏远地区地形的方式。

3. Military strategists must thoroughly understand the **topography** of a battlefield to position their forces for maximum tactical advantage.
   军事战略家必须充分了解战场的地形，以便将部队部署在最有利的战术位置。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "topo-graphy" as "top of the ground writing" — topo means "place" in Greek, and graphy means "writing," so topography literally means writing about the features of a place.
把"topography"想成"地表的书写"——topo在希腊语中意为"地方"，graphy意为"书写"，所以topography字面意思是描绘一个地方的特征。
