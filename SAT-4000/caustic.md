# 🧪 caustic

🔊 /ˈkɔːstɪk/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Able to burn or corrode organic tissue by chemical action; sarcastic or severely critical in a biting way. (n.) A caustic substance or agent
**中文：** adj. 腐蚀性的；苛性的；刻薄的 n. 腐蚀剂

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- corrosive（腐蚀性的）
- acerbic（尖刻的）
- mordant（辛辣的）
- scathing（严厉批评的）
- biting（尖锐的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **caustic remark / caustic wit / caustic soda / caustic humor / caustic criticism**

👉 Example: His caustic remarks about the proposal left several committee members visibly offended.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The literary critic was renowned for her **caustic** reviews, which spared no author regardless of reputation or commercial success.
   这位文学评论家以其刻薄的评论而闻名，无论作者的声誉或商业成就如何都不留情面。

2. Laboratory safety protocols require that all **caustic** chemicals be stored in properly labeled, corrosion-resistant containers at all times.
   实验室安全规程要求所有腐蚀性化学品必须始终存放在正确标记的耐腐蚀容器中。

3. The senator's **caustic** response to the reporter's question revealed deep frustration with the media's coverage of the scandal.
   这位参议员对记者提问的刻薄回应揭示了他对媒体报道该丑闻方式的深深不满。
