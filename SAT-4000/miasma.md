# 🌫️ miasma

🔊 /maɪˈæzmə/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** An unpleasant or unhealthy smell or vapor; an oppressive or unpleasant atmosphere that surrounds or emanates from something.
**中文：** n. 瘴气；臭气；不良影响

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- stench（恶臭）
- effluvium（臭气）
- vapor（蒸气）
- noxious atmosphere（有毒气氛）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **miasma of corruption / miasma of fear / toxic miasma / a miasma hung over**

👉 Example: A miasma of distrust hung over the organization after the scandal was revealed to the public.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. A thick **miasma** of industrial pollution settled over the valley, forcing residents to remain indoors for several days.
   浓厚的工业污染瘴气笼罩着山谷，迫使居民在室内待了好几天。

2. The **miasma** of corruption that pervaded the government institution ultimately led to sweeping legislative reforms nationwide.
   弥漫在政府机构中的腐败气氛最终导致了全国范围内的大规模立法改革。

3. Medieval physicians believed that disease was caused by a **miasma** rising from decaying matter in swamps and sewers.
   中世纪的医生认为，疾病是由沼泽和下水道中腐烂物质散发的瘴气引起的。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "my-ASTHMA" — a miasma is such bad air that it could trigger "my asthma," helping you remember it means a foul or noxious atmosphere.
把"miasma"想成"my asthma"（我的哮喘）——瘴气的空气如此恶劣，足以引发"我的哮喘"，帮助你记住它意味着恶臭或有害的气氛。
