# ⚖️ inalienable

🔊 /ɪnˈeɪliənəbəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Unable to be taken away from or given away by the possessor; not subject to transfer or forfeiture
**中文：** adj. 不可分割的；不可剥夺的；不能让与的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- inviolable（不可侵犯的）
- absolute（绝对的）
- inherent（固有的）
- unassailable（不可剥夺的）
- sacrosanct（神圣不可侵犯的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **inalienable right / inalienable freedom / inalienable dignity / inalienable principle**

👉 Example: The constitution affirms that every citizen possesses certain inalienable rights that no government may revoke.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The philosopher argued that the pursuit of happiness is an **inalienable** right bestowed upon every human being at birth.
   这位哲学家认为，追求幸福是每个人与生俱来的不可剥夺的权利。

2. International law recognizes that personal liberty is an **inalienable** entitlement that transcends national borders and cultural boundaries.
   国际法承认个人自由是一种超越国界和文化界限的不可剥夺的权利。

3. The delegation insisted that sovereignty over natural resources constitutes an **inalienable** right of every independent nation.
   代表团坚持认为，对自然资源的主权构成每个独立国家不可剥夺的权利。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "in-alien-able" — these rights cannot be made "alien" (foreign) to you; they are permanently yours and can never be separated from you.
把"inalienable"想成"不能被'alien'（疏远）的"——这些权利不能被从你身上疏离，它们永远属于你，不可分离。
