# 🎭 calumny

🔊 /ˈkæləmni/<br>
🏷️ *noun | 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A false statement maliciously made to damage someone's reputation; the act of making such statements
**中文：** n. 诽谤；中伤；诬蔑

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- slander（诽谤）
- defamation（中伤）
- libel（诽谤）
- vilification（诋毁）
- aspersion（诬蔑）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **spread calumny / victim of calumny / base calumny / subject to calumny**

👉 Example: The senator dismissed the accusations as nothing more than calumny designed to destroy his political career.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The professor endured years of **calumny** from jealous colleagues before her groundbreaking research was finally vindicated.
   这位教授忍受了嫉妒的同事多年的诽谤，她的开创性研究最终得到了证实。

2. In an era of social media, **calumny** can spread across the globe within minutes, causing irreparable reputational damage.
   在社交媒体时代，诽谤可以在几分钟内传遍全球，造成无法弥补的名誉损害。

3. The court determined that the published article constituted deliberate **calumny** and awarded substantial damages to the plaintiff.
   法院认定该发表的文章构成蓄意诽谤，并判决给予原告大额赔偿。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "calumny" as "call-um-knee" — imagine someone calling you out and bringing you to your knees with false, damaging words.
把"calumny"想成"call-um-knee"——想象有人用虚假、恶毒的话语攻击你，把你批得跪倒在地。
