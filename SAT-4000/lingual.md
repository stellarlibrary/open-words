# 🗣️ lingual

🔊 /ˈlɪŋɡwəl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Of or relating to language; of or relating to the tongue. (n.) A sound articulated with the tongue.
**中文：** adj. 语言的；舌的 n. 舌音

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- linguistic（语言学的）
- verbal（言语的）
- oral（口头的）
- glossal（舌的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **lingual diversity / lingual nerve / bilingual / multilingual / lingual surface**

👉 Example: The dentist examined the lingual surface of the patient's lower teeth for signs of erosion.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The anthropologist studied the **lingual** diversity of indigenous communities to understand how geographic isolation affects language evolution.
   人类学家研究了土著社区的语言多样性，以了解地理隔离如何影响语言演变。

2. During the oral surgery, the surgeon took great care to avoid damaging the **lingual** nerve that runs along the lower jaw.
   在口腔手术中，外科医生非常小心，避免损伤沿下颌延伸的舌神经。

3. The university's **lingual** training program prepares diplomats to communicate effectively across diverse cultural and linguistic boundaries.
   该大学的语言培训项目旨在培养外交官跨越不同文化和语言障碍进行有效沟通的能力。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "lingua" (Latin for "tongue") — your tongue is the organ of language, so "lingual" relates to both.
想想"lingua"（拉丁语中的"舌头"）——舌头是语言的器官，因此"lingual"既与舌头有关，也与语言有关。
