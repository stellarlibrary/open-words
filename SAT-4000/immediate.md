# ⚡ immediate

🔊 /ɪˈmiːdiət/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** Occurring or done at once; without delay. Also, nearest in time, space, or relationship; direct.
**中文：** adj. 立即的；直接的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- instant（即刻的）
- prompt（迅速的）
- direct（直接的）
- urgent（紧迫的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **immediate action / immediate response / immediate vicinity / immediate family**

👉 Example: The principal took immediate action to address the safety concerns raised by parents.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The board of directors demanded an **immediate** investigation into the financial irregularities discovered during the annual audit.
   董事会要求立即调查年度审计中发现的财务违规行为。

2. The hospital is located in the **immediate** vicinity of the university, making it convenient for medical students.
   医院位于大学的紧邻区域，为医学生提供了极大的便利。

3. The government's **immediate** priority was to provide emergency relief to the communities devastated by the earthquake.
   政府的首要任务是向受地震重创的社区提供紧急救助。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "im-medi-ate" as "in the medium of now" — something that happens in the present moment without any waiting.
把"immediate"想成"im（无）+ mediate（中间的）"——没有中间过程，所以是立即的、直接的。
