# 📚 annals

🔊 /ˈænəlz/<br>
🏷️ *n. yearly records; chronicles; historical accounts | 名词：年报；编年史；年鉴*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** A record of events arranged in yearly sequence; historical records or chronicles; the accumulated history of a particular field or organization.
**中文：** 按年份顺序排列的事件记录；历史记录或编年史；某一特定领域或组织的历史汇编。

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chronicles（编年史）
- records（记录）
- archives（档案）
- history（历史）
- accounts（记述）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **in the annals of (history/science/medicine), preserved in the annals, recorded in the annals**
👉 Example: Her groundbreaking discovery will forever be recorded in the annals of modern science.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The conquest of that ancient empire remains one of the most dramatic episodes in the **annals** of military history.
   那个古老帝国的征服仍然是军事史编年史中最戏剧性的事件之一。

2. Scholars spent decades examining the monastery's **annals** to reconstruct the region's forgotten medieval political alliances.
   学者们花了几十年时间研究修道院的年鉴，以重建该地区被遗忘的中世纪政治联盟。

3. The institution published its official **annals** each year, documenting every significant achievement and administrative decision made.
   该机构每年出版其官方年报，记录每一项重大成就和行政决策。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
"Annals" comes from Latin "annalis" (yearly) — think of "annual" reports stacked year after year, forming a complete historical record.
"Annals"源自拉丁语"annalis"（每年的）——想象"annual"（年度）报告年复一年地叠放，形成完整的历史记录。
