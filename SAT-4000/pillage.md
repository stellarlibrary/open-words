# ⚔️ pillage

🔊 /ˈpɪlɪdʒ/<br>
🏷️ *verb, noun | 动词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To rob a place using violence, especially in wartime; to plunder or loot. (n.) The act of pillaging; goods taken by force
**中文：** v. 掠夺；抢劫 n. 掠夺；掠夺物

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- plunder（掠夺）
- loot（抢劫）
- ransack（洗劫）
- ravage（蹂躏）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pillage and plunder / pillage a village / pillage resources / acts of pillage**

👉 Example: Invading armies would routinely pillage the countryside, leaving devastation in their wake.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. Historical records indicate that the invading forces proceeded to **pillage** every settlement along the northern coastal region.
   历史记录表明，入侵军队对北部沿海地区的每一个定居点进行了掠夺。

2. The documentary examined how colonial powers systematically **pillaged** the natural resources of the territories they occupied.
   这部纪录片审视了殖民列强如何系统性地掠夺其占领领土的自然资源。

3. International law now classifies the **pillage** of cultural heritage sites during armed conflict as a serious war crime.
   国际法现在将武装冲突期间对文化遗产遗址的掠夺定性为严重的战争罪。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pill-age" as an age when people "pill" (steal like taking pills from a bottle) everything — pillage means to plunder and rob violently.
把"pillage"想成一个人们"抢夺一切"的时代——pillage就是暴力掠夺和抢劫。
