# 🗣️ pejorative

🔊 /pɪˈdʒɒrətɪv/<br>
🏷️ *adjective, noun | 形容词, 名词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Expressing contempt or disapproval; having a disparaging or derogatory effect. (n.) A word or expression that is derogatory
**中文：** adj. 轻蔑的；贬抑的；贬义的 n. 轻蔑语；贬义词

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- derogatory（贬损的）
- disparaging（轻蔑的）
- demeaning（贬低的）
- deprecatory（不以为然的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pejorative term / pejorative connotation / pejorative sense / used in a pejorative way**

👉 Example: The word "bureaucrat" is often used in a pejorative sense to imply inefficiency and rigidity.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The committee objected to the use of **pejorative** language in the report, arguing that it undermined the document's scholarly credibility.
   委员会反对报告中使用贬义语言，认为这损害了文件的学术可信度。

2. What began as a neutral descriptor gradually acquired **pejorative** connotations through decades of negative cultural associations and media usage.
   最初作为中性描述词的术语，经过几十年的负面文化联想和媒体使用，逐渐获得了贬义色彩。

3. Linguists study how certain words shift from neutral to **pejorative** meanings as social attitudes and cultural norms evolve over time.
   语言学家研究某些词语如何随着社会态度和文化规范的演变，从中性含义转变为贬义含义。

把"pejorative"想成来自拉丁语"pejor（更差）"——pejorative的词让事物听起来更差或更低劣。
