# 🏃 pursue

🔊 /pərˈsuː/<br>
🏷️ *verb | 动词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (v.) To follow or chase someone or something; to continue or proceed along a path or course of action; to engage in an activity
**中文：** v. 追赶；追踪；继续；从事

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- chase（追逐）
- follow（跟随）
- seek（寻求）
- strive for（努力争取）
- engage in（从事）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **pursue a career / pursue a goal / pursue justice / pursue happiness / pursue a degree**

👉 Example: After graduating, she decided to pursue a career in international diplomacy and public policy.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The university encourages students to **pursue** independent research projects that align with their academic interests.
   该大学鼓励学生从事与其学术兴趣相一致的独立研究项目。

2. Law enforcement agencies continued to **pursue** the suspect across three states before finally making an arrest.
   执法机构在三个州持续追踪嫌疑人，最终将其逮捕。

3. The corporation announced plans to **pursue** aggressive expansion into emerging markets throughout Southeast Asia.
   该公司宣布计划积极拓展东南亚新兴市场。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "pur-sue" as "pure suit" — when you pursue something, you are in pure suit (full chase) after it, giving it your complete effort.
把"pursue"想成"pure suit（纯粹的追求）"——当你pursue某事时，你在全力追逐它，付出全部努力。
