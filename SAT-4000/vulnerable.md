# 🛡️ vulnerable

🔊 /ˈvʌlnərəbl/<br>
🏷️ *adjective | 形容词*

---

## 📖 Meaning | 释义
**EN:** (adj.) Susceptible to physical or emotional attack or harm; exposed to the possibility of being damaged or hurt
**中文：** adj. 易受伤害的；易受攻击的；有弱点的；脆弱的

---

## 🔗 Synonyms | 近义词
- susceptible（易受影响的）
- exposed（暴露的）
- defenseless（无防御的）
- unprotected（无保护的）
- fragile（脆弱的）

---

## 📌 Usage Tip | 用法提示
Use with: **vulnerable to attack / vulnerable population / vulnerable position / feel vulnerable**

👉 Example: Children and the elderly are among the most vulnerable populations during natural disasters and public health crises.

---

## 📝 Examples | 例句

1. The coastal communities remain **vulnerable** to severe flooding and storm surges caused by increasingly powerful tropical hurricanes.
   沿海社区仍然容易受到日益强大的热带飓风造成的严重洪水和风暴潮的影响。

2. The company's outdated cybersecurity infrastructure left its customer database **vulnerable** to sophisticated hacking attempts and data breaches.
   该公司过时的网络安全基础设施使其客户数据库容易受到复杂的黑客攻击和数据泄露。

3. Opening herself to criticism made the artist feel **vulnerable**, yet it ultimately strengthened her creative vision and resilience.
   向批评敞开心扉让这位艺术家感到脆弱，但最终增强了她的创作视野和韧性。

---

## 🧩 Memory Trick | 记忆技巧
Think of "vulnerable" as from Latin "vulnus" meaning "wound" — to be vulnerable is to be open to being wounded.
把"vulnerable"想成来自拉丁语"vulnus"，意为"伤口"——脆弱就是容易受到伤害。
